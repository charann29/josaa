# 04 — Reachability Methodology

## Which ranks/quotas decide a seat
For each (institute, branch) we check Vinay's two winning pools against the **correct quota**:
- **OBC-NCL(PwD)** rank **388**
- **General PwD** rank **940**
- Quota: **HS** for NIT Warangal, **OS** for other NITs, **AI** for IIITs/GFTIs.

A seat **clears in year Y** if:
`(OBC-PwD closing[Y] ≥ 388) OR (General-PwD closing[Y] ≥ 940)`
(He qualifies when his rank is ≤ the closing rank.)

## Reachability classes (across 2023–2025, per-year)
Count how many of the 3 years the seat clears:
| Class | Clears in | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **SAFE** | 3 / 3 | clears every year comfortably |
| **LIKELY** | 2 / 3 | clears most years |
| **BORDERLINE** | 1 / 3 | cleared once; upside bet |
| **OUT** | 0 / 3 | data exists but never clears (reach) |
| **No-data** | — | no closing-rank data for his pools |

> ⚠️ **Averages mislead.** Always classify per-year, not on the mean. e.g. Warangal M&C average looked ~396 but recent years were 225/283 (below 388) → it's really BORDERLINE, not safe.
