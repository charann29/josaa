# 02 — Vinay Profile & Constraints

## Candidate
**BOORA VINAY** — Male, home state **Telangana**.

## Ranks (JEE Main 2026)
| Pool | Rank | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| CRL (common rank) | 4,48,918 | too weak for NITs alone |
| OBC-NCL | 1,64,298 | too weak alone |

> The two ranks that actually win seats: **OBC-PwD 388** and **Common-PwD 940**. His non-PwD ranks are too weak for NITs/IIITs. **PwD status is the enabler** — without it, nothing reachable.

## Disability
**Locomotor (leg / walking) disability.** This is both:
- a **legal eligibility factor** — JoSAA bars his condition from certain institutes (see [[06 — Filters & Rules]]), and
- a **practical fit factor** — desk/software roles suit him best; field/plant/mine work does not.

## Eligibility
JEE-**Main** (B.E./B.Tech) seats only. **Not** eligible for JEE-Advanced/IIT seats, **not** B.Arch/B.Plan.

## Quotas that apply
- **Home State (HS)** at **NIT Warangal** only (Telangana home NIT).
- **Other State (OS)** at all other NITs.
- **All India (AI)** at IIITs and GFTIs.
- Gender-Neutral seats (male → no female-only).

## Stated family goals / preferences (accumulated over the session)
- Genuinely good **campus & culture**; strong **placements** (~₹12 LPA target).
- Branch/role **suited to a leg disability** (desk/software-friendly).
- **Close to home** (Telangana) — comfort + it's why HS-quota Warangal is reachable.
- **Good colleges only** — no random weak NITs/IIITs.
- **Warangal first** (home NIT), all its branches, weak ones lower.
- Brand order **NIT > IIIT > GFTI**, with home-city **University of Hyderabad** considered.
- **Cold-weather avoidance** (his comfort) — standing preference.

## Hard rules (final)
- **Exclude orthopedic/locomotor-restricted** institutes/branches (portal bars his condition). #critical
- **Remove Northeast** (NITs + IIITs + GFTIs).
- **Remove coldest North** — J&K, Himachal, Uttarakhand.
- **Bihar last.**
- **Demote unsuitable branches** (Mining, pure-science MSc, Ceramic, Industrial Design, Food, Bio-Medical) below real engineering.
