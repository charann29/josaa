# 01 — Session Overview

## Goal
Help Charan's cousin **BOORA VINAY** fill his **JoSAA 2026** choice-filling options — described as the single most important step in his admission. Produce a deeply-reasoned, optimal, priority-ordered list of *college + branch* choices, then stage automation to enter them on the JoSAA portal (`josaa.admissions.nic.in`) for Vinay to review and lock.

## What "optimal" meant here
A list that maximises Vinay's **realistic outcome** given his ranks and disability — not the most prestigious-sounding line on paper. Concretely: best reachable campus culture + placements (~₹12 LPA target), branch suited to his condition, close to home (Telangana), and **zero restricted seats** (critical for his disability — see [[06 — Filters & Rules]]).

## How JoSAA allocation works (the core mechanic)
JoSAA allots the **single highest choice in your list that you qualify for**. Nothing lower can override something higher. Consequences:
- Listing **dream/reach** picks on top costs nothing — if a miracle happens you take it, else you fall through.
- The **order among reachable options** is what actually determines the outcome.
- So the list must read **dream → realistic → safe → last-resort**, top to bottom.

- A final **464-choice** list (see [[07 — Final List & Predicted Outcome]]).
- A points/percentile scoring engine (see [[05 — Scoring System (Points)]]).
- A restriction-safe browser automation kit, staged for the next morning (see [[08 — Automation Engine]]).
- Data-verified prediction: Vinay lands a **strong NIT Warangal home branch** every modelled year.
