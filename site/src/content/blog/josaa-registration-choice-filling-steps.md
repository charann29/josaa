Registration is the easy part; doing it calmly, with the right things ready, is what saves you stress later. This is the step-by-step for JoSAA 2026 on the official portal, **josaa.nic.in**. Exact dates and fees change each year, so always confirm them on the portal — the *process* below stays the same.

> You always register, fill and lock your own choices on josaa.nic.in. No third party should ever ask for your login or OTP — see [counselling scams](/blog/guaranteed-seat-scams-josaa).

## Before you start: what to keep ready

- Your **JEE Main** (and **JEE Advanced**, if qualified) application number and password.
- Personal and academic details that match your documents exactly (name, date of birth, category).
- A clear idea of your **rank, category, home state and preferences** — and ideally a draft choice list already prepared. (Build it with our [choice-filling order guide](/blog/josaa-choice-filling-order-2026).)
- A stable laptop/desktop and internet connection. Avoid doing it on a phone if you can.

## Step-by-step: registration

1. **Open josaa.nic.in** and find the candidate login for the current year.
2. **Log in** using your JEE Main / JEE Advanced credentials.
3. **Verify your details** — personal information, category, and the ranks JoSAA has pulled in. Check these carefully.
4. **Confirm and proceed** to the choice-filling section.

That's it — registration itself is short and free.

## Step-by-step: choice filling

1. **Find your eligible choices.** The portal lets you filter by institute type (IIT / NIT / IIIT / GFTI), institute, branch and program.
2. **Add the choices you want.** Add generously — there is no cap, and you should add every option you'd genuinely join. (See [how many choices to fill](/blog/how-many-choices-to-fill-josaa).)
3. **Arrange them in the right order.** This is the part that decides your seat. Order strictly by **preference**, top to bottom: Dream → Likely → Safe. JoSAA gives you the highest choice you qualify for, so a wrong order can cost a seat. Full method: [JoSAA choice filling order](/blog/josaa-choice-filling-order-2026).
4. **Save regularly** as you build and reorder.
5. **Use mock allotment** to preview what your list would get, then fix it. See [mock allotment explained](/blog/josaa-mock-seat-allotment-explained).
6. **Lock your choices** before the deadline.

| Stage | What you do | Free? |
|---|---|---|
| Registration | Log in, verify details | Yes |
| Choice filling | Add, arrange, save | Yes |
| Mock allotment | Preview, adjust list | Yes |
| Lock | Confirm final list | Yes |
| Accept a seat | Pay Seat Acceptance Fee, report | Fee applies |

## Portal tips that prevent disasters

- **Single active session.** JoSAA generally allows one active login at a time. Logging in elsewhere can break your session — don't share credentials or open it in two places.
- **Beware timeouts.** Sessions can expire after a period of inactivity. **Save frequently.**
- **Fill in chunks.** If your list is long, add and save in batches rather than all at once.
- **Never share OTP/password.** Not with a "counsellor," not with anyone.
- **Lock early.** Don't wait for the last hour — portals are slowest under deadline load.

## After you lock

Once allotments are out, you'll accept your seat by choosing **Float, Freeze or Slide**, paying the Seat Acceptance Fee, and reporting with documents. Get those ready in advance using the [documents & reporting checklist](/blog/josaa-documents-reporting-checklist), and understand the round options in the [float/freeze/slide guide](/blog/float-freeze-slide-josaa).

Registration takes minutes. The order of your choices takes thought — that's where your real college is decided, so give that step the time it deserves.
