St. Aloysius Institute of Management and Information Technology (AIMIT) is a well-regarded institution located in Mangalore. It offers a range of postgraduate programs designed to equip students for careers in science, IT, and management.

This page provides a clear overview of the courses available, their fees, the admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision about your higher education journey.

## Programs Offered at AIMIT

AIMIT focuses on postgraduate education, providing both degree and diploma courses. These full-time programs are structured to offer in-depth knowledge and practical skills across various disciplines. The primary fields of study include Science, Information Technology & Software, and Business & Management Studies.

### Specializations Available

The institute offers specialized learning tracks within its main programs:

*   **M.Sc. Programs:** Students can specialize in cutting-edge areas such as Computer Science, Biochemistry, and Bioinformatics.
*   **PG Diploma Programs:** These diplomas offer focused learning in fields like Finance and Biology.
*   **MBA/PGDM Programs:** For those pursuing management, specializations are available in areas like Sales & Marketing.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to AIMIT's programs typically involves meeting specific academic criteria and, for some courses, appearing for entrance examinations. While specific cutoff details can vary by program and year, candidates are generally expected to have a relevant bachelor's degree from a recognized university.

Prospective students are encouraged to check the official AIMIT website for the most current information regarding application deadlines, eligibility criteria, and the selection process for their chosen program.

The fee structure for programs at AIMIT is competitive and varies depending on the course. AIMIT is known for its dedicated and experienced faculty members.
