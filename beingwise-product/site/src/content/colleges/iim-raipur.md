IIM Raipur is a well-regarded institution for management education, offering various programs designed to shape future leaders. If you're considering a postgraduate management degree, understanding what IIM Raipur has to offer in terms of courses, fees, admissions, and career prospects is crucial.

## Programs Offered

IIM Raipur provides a range of management programs catering to different career stages and aspirations. The flagship program is the Post Graduate Programme (PGP), equivalent to an MBA, designed for fresh graduates and those with limited work experience. For experienced professionals, the institute offers an Executive Post Graduate Programme in Management (ePGP), which is similar to an executive MBA, focusing on enhancing skills relevant to current industry needs.

## Admission Process

Admission to IIM Raipur is highly competitive and typically involves several stages. The primary requirement for most programs is a strong score in the Common Admission Test (CAT). After the CAT results, candidates are usually shortlisted based on a combination of factors, including their CAT score, academic performance in Class 10, Class 12, and graduation, work experience (if any), and academic diversity.

Shortlisted candidates then participate in a Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) round. Performance in these rounds is critical, as it allows the admissions committee to assess communication skills, general awareness, and suitability for a management career. Final admission offers are made considering all these components.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for programs at IIM Raipur can vary. For the flagship PGP program, the total fees typically cover tuition, library access, IT services, and other academic expenses. It's advisable to check the official IIM Raipur website for the most current and detailed fee information for each specific program you are interested in.

## CAT Cutoff Trends

IIM Raipur, like other IIMs, has specific CAT cutoff percentages that candidates need to meet to be considered for shortlisting. These cutoffs can vary each year based on the applicant pool and the institute's requirements. While a high overall CAT percentile is essential, candidates also need to clear sectional cutoffs. It's important to remember that meeting the cutoff doesn't guarantee admission but makes you eligible for the next stage of the selection process.

## Placements at IIM Raipur

IIM Raipur has a strong track record of placements, attracting a diverse range of recruiters from various sectors. The institute consistently strives to provide excellent career opportunities for its graduates.

### Summer Placements for MBA (2022-24 Batch)

Summer internships are an integral part of the MBA experience, providing students with practical exposure. For the 2022-24 batch, the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) and Fast-Moving Consumer Durables (FMCD) sectors were the top recruiters during summer placements.

Here's a breakdown of sector-wise placements during the summer internship drive for the 2022-24 batch:

*   FMCG/FMCD: 23%
*   BFSI (Banking, Financial Services & Insurance): 18%
*   IT/ITES: 16%
*   Manufacturing: 13%
*   Strategy & Consulting: 11%
*   Others: 19%
