# IBMR Group of Institutions, Gurgaon: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

The IBMR Group of Institutions in Gurgaon offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, focusing on industry-aligned education. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities to assist prospective students and their families.

## Key takeaways

*   IBMR Gurgaon offers both undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including BCA and MBA, with industry-synergized components.
*   Applications for various courses are currently open and can be submitted online through the institution's official website.
*   The institution reported a strong placement record in 2023, with 86% national and 4% international placements.
*   The highest package offered during the 2024 placements reached INR 21 LPA for PGDM students.

## Courses and Fees

IBMR Group of Institutions provides diverse programs at both the undergraduate and postgraduate levels, designed to equip students with practical skills for the professional world. Many courses incorporate industry-synergized components alongside their university affiliations.

### Undergraduate Courses

One prominent undergraduate offering is the BCA (affiliated with MDU, Rohtak) + UGPM (Industry Synergized) course. This three-year program is ideal for students aiming for a career in computer applications.

| Course | Total Fees (3 Years) | Registration Fee | Annual Fee | Semester Fee |
| :----- | :------------------- | :--------------- | :--------- | :----------- |
| BCA + UGPM | INR 3.35 Lakhs | INR 30,000 | INR 1.03 Lakhs | INR 50,833 |

### Postgraduate Courses

At the postgraduate level, IBMR offers an MBA (Dual Specialization, affiliated with MDU, Rohtak) + PGPM (Industry Synergized) program. This full-time course allows students to specialize in various fields, enhancing their career prospects. Specializations include:

*   Human Resources
*   Marketing
*   Finance
*   International Business
*   Information Technology
*   Production and Operation Management
*   Agri Business Management
*   Business Analytics
*   Public Policy Management

## Admission Process

Admissions for various courses at IBMR Group of Institutions are currently open. The application process is entirely online, and interested candidates can submit their forms through the institution's official website.

Candidates are typically selected based on their scores in relevant entrance examinations. A non-refundable application fee of INR 1,500 is required to complete the application process for any program.

## Placements

IBMR Group of Institutions has a notable track record in placements, with details for the academic years 2022-2023 and 2024 being released. The institution focuses on connecting students with leading companies across various sectors.

### Placement Statistics (2023)

According to the latest reports, IBMR Gurgaon achieved strong placement rates in the 2022-2023 academic year:

*   **National Placement Rate:** 86%
*   **International Placement Rate:** 4%
*   **Lowest Package Offered:** INR 5 LPA
