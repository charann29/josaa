Amity University Patna is a well-known institution offering a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide from BeingWise provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Amity University Patna: Courses and Eligibility

Amity University Patna provides a diverse range of programs across various fields. The general requirement for most undergraduate courses is a pass in Class 12 with a minimum aggregate score, typically between 50% and 55%. For postgraduate programs, a bachelor's degree in a relevant discipline is usually required.

### Popular Undergraduate Programs

*   **Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)**: This program is ideal for students interested in computer science and software development.
*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)**: Focuses on management principles and business operations.
*   **Integrated Law Programs (BA LLB Hons, BBA LLB Hons)**: Designed for students aspiring to a career in law, combining a bachelor's degree with a law degree.

### Popular Postgraduate Programs

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA)**: A comprehensive program for developing advanced management and leadership skills.

## Amity University Patna: Admission Process

Admission to Amity University Patna can be secured through various routes, including entrance exams and direct admission criteria.

### General Admission

For most programs, admission is based on academic merit and performance in accepted entrance examinations. The university generally accepts CUET scores for various programs.

### Direct Admission Opportunities

Amity University Patna offers direct admission pathways for eligible candidates. These include:

*   **Class 12 Performance**: Students securing over 80% aggregate in Class 12 may be eligible for direct admission to certain programs. This option is not available for Architecture, Design, Education, Fine Arts, Fashion, Law, and Physical Education programs.
*   **Integrated Law Programs**: Direct admission for BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons) is available based on current year CLAT scores of 70 or higher, provided other eligibility criteria are met.
*   **MBA Program**: For MBA, candidates need at least 60% in Class 12 and 50% in their graduation, along with a valid score from entrance exams like MAT, CAT, XAT, or GMAT.
*   **Amity Alumni**: Graduates from Amity University are eligible for direct admission to all Master's programs, provided they meet the minimum eligibility requirements.

### Documents Required for Admission

When applying, ensure you have the following documents ready:

*   Scanned copies of Class 10 and Class 12 mark sheets.
*   Scanned copy of your bachelor's degree mark sheet (if applying for postgraduate programs).
*   A recent colored passport-size photograph.

Specific cutoff scores for admission to various programs at Amity University Patna depend on the entrance exams accepted (like CUET for some courses) and the overall applicant pool.
