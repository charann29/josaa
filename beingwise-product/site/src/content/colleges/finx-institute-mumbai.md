FinX Institute, Mumbai, stands as a notable institution for higher education in the financial capital of India. It offers a range of programs designed to equip students with practical skills and knowledge relevant to the finance and data science sectors.

## Courses Offered at FinX Institute

FinX Institute provides diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs. These include Bachelor of Science (BSc) degrees, Bachelor of Commerce (BCom), and various postgraduate options like the Master of Management Studies (MMS) and Post Graduate Diplomas.

### Undergraduate Programs

*   **BSc in Data Science:** This full-time program focuses on the rapidly growing field of data analytics and its applications in finance. It has an intake capacity of 60 students.
*   **BSc in Finance:** A comprehensive program covering core financial concepts and market operations.
*   **BCom:** This traditional commerce degree prepares students for various roles in business and finance, including opportunities in the government sector.

### Postgraduate Programs

*   **Master of Management Studies (MMS):** The institute offers a total of 180 seats for its MMS program, distributed across two specializations:
    *   **MMS (Financial Technology):** 60 seats, focusing on the intersection of finance and technology.
    *   **MMS (Global Financial Markets):** 120 seats, designed for students interested in international financial markets.
*   **Post Graduate Diplomas (PGD):** The institute also offers specialized PGD courses, such as PGD-CS, PGD-FT, and PGD-GFM, catering to specific industry demands.

## Admission Process

Securing a seat at FinX Institute involves a structured process, ensuring that eligible and capable candidates are admitted. Here’s a general overview:

1.  **Eligibility Check:** Prospective students must first meet the specific eligibility criteria for their chosen course, which typically involves academic qualifications.
2.  **Application:** Eligible candidates can obtain and submit their application form either online through the institute's official website or by visiting the campus.
3.  **Aptitude Test:** Applicants are required to appear for the FinX Institute Entrance Exam, an aptitude test conducted by the institute.
4.  **Interview:** Shortlisted candidates from the aptitude test proceed to a panel interview round.
5.  **Admission Offer:** Based on performance in the entrance exam and interview, along with eligibility and seat availability, selected candidates receive an admission offer.
6.  **Confirmation:** To finalize admission, candidates must accept the offer and submit the required fees.
