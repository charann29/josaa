The Symbiosis School of Banking and Finance (SSBF), located in Pune, is a respected institution under Symbiosis International University. Established in 2010, SSBF focuses on providing specialized education in banking and finance.

## Programs Offered at SSBF Pune

SSBF Pune offers a range of programs designed to equip students and working professionals with the necessary skills for the financial sector. The flagship program is a full-time MBA, alongside various other specialized courses.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Banking and Finance

This is a comprehensive two-year, residential program tailored for those aspiring to leadership roles in banking and finance. It covers a wide array of topics crucial for understanding the complexities of the financial industry.

### Other Programs

Beyond the MBA, SSBF provides several shorter programs for professionals looking to enhance specific skills or gain new expertise:

*   **Executive Diploma in Fintech:** Focuses on financial technology and its applications.
*   **Certificate Program in Banking, Finance & Insurance:** A concise program covering core concepts in these areas.
*   **Corporate Training Programs:** Customized training solutions for organizations.
*   **40-day Certificate Program:** Specifically designed for working professionals seeking a focused learning experience.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to SSBF programs typically requires a strong academic background. For the MBA and other diploma/certificate courses, candidates generally need to have completed their graduation with a minimum aggregate score.

*   **MBA Program:** A graduation degree with at least 50% to 65% aggregate marks is usually required. Specific entrance exams might also be a prerequisite.
*   **Other Programs:** Eligibility criteria vary by program, but a bachelor's degree is a common requirement.

## Fee Structure

Investing in quality education is an important decision. For the MBA program at SSBF Pune, the total fee is approximately INR 17.8 lakhs. Prospective students should check the official SSBF website for the most current and detailed fee information for all programs.

## Placement Highlights

SSBF Pune has a strong track record of successful placements, attracting leading companies from the banking and financial services industry. The institute consistently strives to provide excellent career opportunities for its graduates.
