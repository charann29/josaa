Ethiraj College for Women, located in Chennai, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of courses for female students. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of its academic programs, admission process, fees, and career opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Courses Offered

Ethiraj College provides diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various streams, catering to different academic interests and career aspirations. These include degrees in Arts, Science, Commerce, Business Administration, Computer Applications, and Management.

### Undergraduate Programs

For students completing their 10+2 education, Ethiraj College offers popular Bachelor's degrees:

*   **B.A. (Bachelor of Arts):** Available in various specializations within Humanities and Social Sciences.
*   **B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science):** Covers a range of science disciplines.
*   **B.Com. (Bachelor of Commerce):** Focuses on accounting and commerce principles.
*   **BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration):** Aims to develop management and business skills.
*   **BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications):** Geared towards computer science and IT skills.

### Postgraduate Programs

For graduates seeking advanced studies, the college offers Master's degrees and other higher programs:

*   **M.A. (Master of Arts):** Advanced studies in specific humanities and social science fields.
*   **M.Sc. (Master of Science):** Specialized learning in various science disciplines.
*   **M.Com. (Master of Commerce):** Deep dive into advanced commerce and accounting.
*   **MCA (Master of Computer Applications):** A professional program for a career in IT.
*   **MBA/PGDM (Master of Business Administration / Post Graduate Diploma in Management):** For those aspiring to leadership roles in business.
*   **M.Phil and Ph.D.:** Research-oriented programs for academic and research careers.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to Ethiraj College is based on specific eligibility criteria for each course. Prospective students must meet these requirements to be considered for enrollment.

### Undergraduate Course Eligibility

For all Bachelor's degree programs (B.A., B.Sc., B.Com., BBA, BCA), candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 or an equivalent examination from a recognized board.

### Postgraduate Course Eligibility

*   **M.A.:** Requires a B.A. in Economics, B.Com., B.A. Corporate Secretaryship, BBA, B.Sc. Mathematics, or B.Sc. Statistics from a recognized university.
*   **M.Sc.:** Applicants need a B.Sc. in Botany/Plant Biology and Plant Biotechnology with a specified aggregate percentage from a recognized university.
*   **MCA:** Eligibility includes a BCA, BES, B.Sc. (Computer Science, Mathematics, Applied Sciences, Physics, Statistics), B.Com., B.B.M., B.B.A., B.L.M., B.A. Corporate Secretaryship, B.A. Economics, or any other degree with Business Mathematics and Statistics or Mathematics/Statistics at the Main/Allied level. B.Sc. Chemistry with Mathematics and Physics as allied subjects, B.E./B.Tech., M.B.A., or a Bachelor's degree in any discipline with Mathematics at the Higher Secondary level are also accepted.
*   **MBA/PGDM:** A Bachelor's degree in any subject from the University of Madras or an equivalent recognized university is required.
