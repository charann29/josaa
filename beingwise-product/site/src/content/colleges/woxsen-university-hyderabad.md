## Woxsen University, Hyderabad: An Overview

Woxsen University, located in Hyderabad, is recognized for its strong focus on management and technology programs. It aims to provide a comprehensive learning environment with modern facilities and industry-aligned curricula.

### Notable Achievements and Rankings

Woxsen University has received international recognition for its programs. For the 2025-26 academic year, it was ranked 6th in the Asia Pacific region by Bloomberg's Best B-School Ranking. Additionally, it secured the 30th position in Asia for its Full-time MBA program in the QS Full-time MBA Rankings 2026. The university is particularly known for its robust infrastructure, strong placement record, and acclaimed Management programs.

## Admission Process

Admission to Woxsen University is primarily based on entrance examinations. The university conducts its own set of tests, and also accepts scores from national-level exams. Admissions for all UG, PG, and Ph.D. courses for the upcoming 2026 intake are currently open.

### Steps for Admission:

1.  **Application:** Candidates must apply online through the university's official website. This involves registering, filling out the application form, uploading necessary documents, and paying a non-refundable application fee (typically around INR 2,000).
2.  **Entrance Examination:** Depending on the course, applicants need to appear for one of the following:
    *   **Woxsen Aptitude Test (WAT):** A one-hour online test covering Quantitative Aptitude, English, Reasoning, Reading Comprehension, and General Knowledge.
    *   **Woxsen Design Test (WDT)**
    *   **Woxsen Joint Entrance Exam Test (W-JEET)**
    *   **Woxsen Research Entrance Test (WRET)**
    *   Alternatively, scores from national exams like SAT or CUET may be accepted for some undergraduate programs.
3.  **Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates will undergo a Personal Interview.
4.  **Selection and Merit List:** Based on entrance exam scores, interview performance, and academic record, a merit list is prepared, and selected candidates are offered admission.

### Applying for MBA:

To apply for the MBA program, visit the official Woxsen University website, click 'Apply Now', register, complete the online application form, pay the INR 2,000 fee, upload documents, and submit.
