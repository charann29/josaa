The Delhi School of Management (DSM) at Delhi Technological University (DTU) stands as a prominent institution for management education in the capital. It offers a range of postgraduate management programs designed to equip students with essential business skills and knowledge. This guide provides a clear overview of DSM's offerings, including courses, fees, the admissions process, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered at DSM, DTU

DSM primarily focuses on its flagship Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Executive MBA programs. The full-time MBA is a two-year course, while the Executive MBA caters to working professionals seeking to enhance their careers.

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** This is a full-time, two-year program covering various specializations. Admissions are typically entrance exam-based.
*   **Executive MBA (EMBA):** Designed for experienced professionals, this program helps individuals advance their management careers without interrupting their work.
*   **PGDM (Data Science and Analytics):** DSM also offers a PGDM program focusing on the rapidly growing fields of Data Science and Analytics, though specific details regarding its admission cycle are often aligned with the EMBA.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Securing a spot at DSM, DTU requires meeting specific eligibility criteria and successfully navigating the selection process. The approach differs slightly between the full-time MBA and the Executive MBA.

### MBA Admission

Admission to the full-time MBA program is primarily entrance-based:

*   **Eligibility:** Candidates must hold a Bachelor's Degree with a minimum of 50% marks (or an equivalent CGPA) from a recognized university or institution.
*   **Selection Criteria:** Applicants need a valid score in national-level management entrance exams such as CAT or MAT. This is typically followed by a counseling session, Group Discussion (GD), and Personal Interview (PI) round.

### Executive MBA Admission

For the Executive MBA, the process is tailored for working professionals:

*   **Eligibility:** A Bachelor's Degree with at least 50% marks (or equivalent CGPA) from a recognized university is required.
*   **Application:** Eligible candidates must complete the online application form available on the official DTU website.
*   **Selection Criteria:** The selection process is comprehensive, focusing on two main aspects:
    *   **Academic Performance (50% weightage):** Evaluates the candidate's past academic record.
    *   **Personal Interview (50% weightage):** This interview assesses the quality of work experience, the candidate's suitability for the program, and overall fitness.

### Key Admission Dates

Admission dates, especially for the Executive MBA, are usually announced around April or May each year. For instance, in the previous cycle, the application deadline for the Executive MBA/PGDM (Data Science and Analytics) was around July 23.

## Fees Structure

The MBA program at Delhi School of Management is not free, and students are required to pay tuition fees as per the university's structure. For the full-time MBA, the total fee is approximately INR 2.9 lakh. Specific fee details for other programs like the Executive MBA can be found on the official DTU website when admissions open.

## Scholarships

DSM, DTU does offer some scholarships to support its students. These are generally based on academic merit and overall performance. Available scholarships include:

*   Merit-based scholarships.
*   Post-matric scholarships for SC students.
*   Merit scholarships for students from specific categories: SC, ST, OBC, and Minority.

Details on how to avail these scholarships are usually provided during the admission process or on the university's scholarship portal.
