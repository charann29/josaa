XLRI Xavier School of Management, located in Jamshedpur, is a highly respected institution known for its excellence in management education. For those considering a career in management, understanding its offerings, admission process, and career prospects is key. This page provides a clear summary of XLRI's courses, fee structure, admission criteria, cutoff scores, and placement records.

## Programs Offered at XLRI Jamshedpur

XLRI provides a diverse range of management programs designed for various career stages and aspirations. These include full-time postgraduate diplomas, executive programs, and doctoral studies.

### Postgraduate Diploma Programs

XLRI's flagship programs are its two-year, full-time Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) specializations:

*   **PGDM in Business Management (BM)**
*   **PGDM in Human Resource Management (HRM)**

These programs are highly sought after and form the core of XLRI's residential offerings.

### Executive and Online Programs

For working professionals, XLRI offers several specialized programs:

*   **Executive Diploma in Human Resource Management:** This program requires candidates to hold a bachelor's degree of at least three years and possess a minimum of five years of work experience. Admission is based on merit and an interview, without an entrance exam.
*   **Post Graduate Certificate in Human Resource Management (PGCHRM):** A one-year program for graduates from any discipline with at least 24 months of post-graduation work experience.
*   **Executive Fellow Programme in Management (EFPM):** This program is for experienced professionals aiming for doctoral-level research. Eligibility includes an MBA/PGDM or equivalent with at least 60% marks, or a one-year full-time residential program in Management, plus a minimum of five years of full-time executive/managerial/teaching/research experience.
*   **Online Learning (XOL) Programs:** Through its XOL platform, XLRI offers AICTE-approved three-year online postgraduate diploma courses. These include the Post Graduate Diploma in Human Resource Management (PGDHRM), Post Graduate Diploma in Business Management (PGDBM), and Post Graduate Diploma in Finance (PGDF).

## Fees Structure

The tuition fees for XLRI Jamshedpur's postgraduate diploma (PGDM) programs vary depending on the specific specialization chosen. Generally, these fees range from approximately INR 4 Lakh to INR 58.8 Lakh. It's important to note that these figures are indicative and subject to change by the institute.

## Admission Process and Key Dates

Admission to XLRI's full-time PGDM programs (BM and HRM) primarily relies on scores from the XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test). GMAT scores are also accepted for certain programs or profiles. The selection process extends beyond test scores to include a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round. Factors like work experience and extracurricular activities are also considered.

## Cutoff Scores

To be eligible for the GD and PI rounds for the PGDM in Human Resource Management, candidates need to meet specific XAT percentile cutoffs. These cutoffs vary based on gender and whether the candidate has an engineering or non-engineering background. For instance, male engineers typically need a 95 percentile overall, while female engineers require 90 percentile.

### XAT Sectional Cutoffs for HRM Program (Indicative):

| Stream                 | QA    | VALR  | DM    | Total |
| :--------------------- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Engineer (Male)        | >=83  | >=90  | >=87  | >=95  |
| Engineer (Female)      | >=73  | >=85  | >=80  | >=90  |
| Non-Engineer (Male)    | >=76  | >=90  | >=87  | >=93  |
| Non-Engineer (Female)  | >=68  | >=85  | >=80  | >=87  |

*QA: Quantitative Ability, VALR: Verbal & Logical Reasoning, DM: Decision Making*

## Placements

XLRI consistently reports strong placement outcomes, attracting top recruiters from various industries. While specific placement statistics can vary year to year, the institute is known for achieving 100% placements for its flagship PGDM programs. Graduates secure roles across sectors like consulting, finance, general management, sales & marketing, and human resources, often with competitive salary packages.
