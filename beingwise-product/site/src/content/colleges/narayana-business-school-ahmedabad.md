Narayana Business School (NBS) in Ahmedabad is a well-regarded institution for management education. This guide provides a clear overview of its programs, admission criteria, fee structure, placement outcomes, and more, to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at NBS Ahmedabad

NBS Ahmedabad offers a variety of postgraduate management programs designed to equip students with practical and theoretical knowledge. These include MBA and various PGDM specializations.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA) + Post Graduate Program in Corporate Excellence (PGPCE)

This two-year program is affiliated with Gujarat Technological University (GTU) and combines an MBA with a PGPCE. The curriculum is structured to provide a blend of academic theory and practical, field-specific expertise. Students also gain valuable real-world experience through a three-month internship opportunity at the end of their first year.

### Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)

NBS offers several PGDM programs approved by AICTE, including dual specialization options and focused programs in emerging fields.

**Program Intake Capacity:**

| Program                        | Intake Capacity |
| :----------------------------- | :-------------- |
| MBA + PGPCE                    | 180             |
| PGDM Dual Specialization       | 240             |
| PGDM In Data Science           | 30              |
| PGDM In Quantitative Finance   | 30              |

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to Narayana Business School is a multi-stage process that assesses a candidate's academic background and aptitude. To be eligible, applicants generally need to meet specific academic requirements and perform well in entrance examinations.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   Candidates must have secured a minimum aggregate of 60% in both their Class 12 examinations and their undergraduate degree.
*   Applicants need to achieve a valid score in the NBSAT, which is an aptitude test conducted by Narayana Business School itself.
*   The institution also considers scores from national-level entrance exams such as CMAT.

### Selection Process

Shortlisted candidates based on academic records and entrance exam scores will proceed to further rounds, which typically include:

*   **Group Discussion (GD):** To assess communication skills, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** To evaluate overall personality, motivation, and suitability for the program.

A final merit list is then released based on the cumulative performance in all selection stages.
