St Xavier's Catholic College of Engineering provides a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. These include Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) / Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech), Master of Engineering (M.E.) / Master of Technology (M.Tech), Master of Business Administration (MBA) / Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM), and Master of Computer Applications (MCA). All these programs are officially approved by the AICTE.
