T A Pai Management Institute (TAPMI), located in Manipal and part of MAHE, is a well-regarded institution for higher education. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make informed decisions.

## Programs Offered at TAPMI

TAPMI provides a range of programs, including BBA, MBA, and PhD courses, designed to equip students with essential skills and knowledge for their chosen careers.

### BBA Program

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program at TAPMI is a popular choice for students aiming for a strong foundation in management. To be eligible, candidates must have successfully completed Class 12 with a minimum of 60% marks, with English as a core subject. Admission also requires scores from entrance exams such as IPMAT, JIPMAT, JEE Main, SAT, CLAT, or MET.

### MBA Programs

TAPMI offers various MBA specializations, including a notable MBA in International Business (IB). The institute has strong ties with international universities like the University of Dubai and Macquarie University, enriching the learning experience for its MBA students.

### PhD Program

For those interested in advanced research, TAPMI offers a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) program. This program is designed for individuals looking to contribute to academic and industrial knowledge through rigorous research.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to TAPMI programs is competitive and involves several stages, ensuring that deserving candidates are selected.

### BBA Admission Steps

1.  **Eligibility Check:** Ensure you meet the academic criteria (60% in Class 12 with English) and have valid entrance exam scores.
2.  **Application:** Submit an online application through the official TAPMI website.
3.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are shortlisted based on their Class 12 academic performance and entrance exam scores.
4.  **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates participate in a Micro Presentation and Personal Interview (PI) round.
5.  **Final Selection:** A comprehensive review of the student's overall profile leads to final selection.
6.  **Fee Payment:** Selected candidates must pay the prescribed fees within the specified timeframe to confirm their admission.

### MBA Admission

The application process for MBA programs is entirely online. Candidates need to register on the official website, fill out the application form, upload necessary documents, and pay the application fee. The fee varies depending on the number of programs applied for:

*   **Single Course:** INR 1,500
*   **Two Programs:** INR 2,000
*   **Three Programs:** INR 2,750
*   **Four Programs:** INR 3,500

### PhD Admission

Applying for the PhD program at TAPMI is done via email. Candidates must:

1.  Download the application form from the official TAPMI website.
2.  Fill in all required details.
3.  Scan the completed form and attach it to an email.
4.  Include scanned copies of educational certificates from your bachelor's degree onwards, work experience details, an updated CV, and a research proposal.
5.  The research proposal should be 5-7 pages (approximately 2500-3000 words), outlining your research interest, the problem you intend to address, its significance, and your proposed methodology. It should be formatted using Arial 11-point font, 1.5 line spacing, and 1-inch margins.

## Campus Life and Facilities

TAPMI, established in 1981, boasts a modern campus in the heart of Manipal. It offers state-of-the-art facilities like smart classrooms and libraries. The campus is well-connected by road and public transport, ensuring accessibility. For student safety, CCTV cameras are installed throughout the premises, creating a secure environment for both male and female students.
