Lady Shri Ram College for Women (LSR), a constituent college of the University of Delhi, is consistently ranked among India's top educational institutions for women. Known for its academic excellence, vibrant campus life, and strong placement record, LSR attracts students from across the country seeking a high-quality education.

## Courses Offered at LSR

LSR provides a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. The college is particularly renowned for its arts, commerce, and social science programs. Here's a look at some of the popular courses:

### Undergraduate Programs (UG)

*   **Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) Hons.:** Offered in subjects like Economics, English, History, Journalism, Philosophy, Political Science, Psychology, Sanskrit, and Sociology.
*   **Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) Hons.:** A highly sought-after program focusing on business and finance.
*   **Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) Hons.:** Available in Mathematics and Statistics.

### Postgraduate Programs (PG)

*   **Master of Arts (M.A.):** Offered in English, Hindi, History, Mathematics, Philosophy, Political Science, Psychology, Sanskrit, and Sociology.
*   **Master of Commerce (M.Com):** An advanced program in commerce.

## Fee Structure

The fee structure at LSR is generally affordable, aligning with other Delhi University colleges. Fees vary slightly depending on the specific course chosen. Typically, the annual fees for undergraduate programs range from approximately INR 19,000 to INR 20,000. Postgraduate program fees are also within a similar range.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to LSR is highly competitive. For undergraduate courses, admission is primarily based on the Common University Entrance Test (CUET) scores. Candidates must appear for CUET and meet the specific eligibility criteria for their chosen program.

### Cutoffs

Historically, LSR has had some of the highest cutoff scores among all Delhi University colleges. While exact cutoffs vary each year based on applicant performance and seat availability, prospective students should aim for very high scores in CUET. For popular courses like B.A. (Hons.) Economics or B.Com (Hons.), cutoffs often exceed 98% (or equivalent CUET percentile).

For postgraduate admissions, candidates typically need to clear the Delhi University Entrance Test (DUET) or CUET (PG), depending on the specific program and university guidelines, along with meeting academic percentage requirements.

### Internship Opportunities

LSR also offers robust internship opportunities. In the 2025 internship session, 14 recruiters offered 66 full-time internships. The highest stipend recorded was INR 4.4 lakhs for an 8-week period, with an average stipend of INR 1.2 lakhs for 2 months. Leading companies like McKinsey & Co., BCN, and Accenture are frequent internship providers.

## Campus Facilities

Spread across 15 acres, LSR offers a comprehensive range of facilities to support student life and academics. These include a well-stocked library, modern laboratories, a sports complex, a gymnasium, a cafeteria, an auditorium, medical facilities, a shuttle service, and Wi-Fi connectivity across the campus. The college also provides hostel accommodation for female students.

## Faculty

LSR boasts a highly qualified and experienced faculty. Many professors hold advanced degrees and are actively involved in research and academic discourse. The college also regularly invites guest lecturers and experts for conferences, enriching the learning experience for students.

## Rankings

Lady Shri Ram College for Women consistently features among the top colleges in national rankings. As per the NIRF 2022 rankings, LSR was ranked 5th among colleges in India, underscoring its reputation for academic excellence.
