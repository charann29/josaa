The MET Institute of Management, located in Mumbai, is a well-regarded institution for higher education in business. If you're considering a management program, understanding its courses, fees, admission process, cutoff scores, and placement opportunities is key.

## Programs Offered

MET Institute of Management primarily focuses on postgraduate management education. Their flagship programs include:

*   **Master of Management Studies (MMS):** This is a two-year, full-time program affiliated with the University of Mumbai, designed to equip students with comprehensive management knowledge and skills.
*   **Doctor of Philosophy (PhD):** For those aspiring to deep academic research and contribution to the field of management, MET offers a PhD program.

## Admission Process

Admission to MET Institute of Management is competitive and based on entrance exam scores and academic merit. Direct admissions are generally not offered for either the MMS or PhD programs.

### For MMS Program

Admission to the MMS program is primarily based on scores from the **MAH CET** (Maharashtra Common Entrance Test).

Here's a general outline of the application steps:

1.  **Appear for MAH CET:** Candidates must first register for and take the MAH CET exam.
2.  **Visit MET Website:** After obtaining valid MAH CET scores, visit the official MET Institute of Management website.
3.  **Complete Application Form:** Select the MMS course and fill out the online application form. Carefully provide your educational qualifications, email ID, and marks obtained. Ensure you select "MMS" as the "course type" and "management" in the "discipline" section.
4.  **Submit Application:** Click "apply" to confirm your submission.
5.  **Verification and Offer:** MET Institute of Management reviews submitted applications. After initial verification of details, document verification is performed. A final admission offer is extended to shortlisted candidates once all information is cross-checked.

### For PhD Program

Applying for the PhD program involves a structured process:

1.  **Online Registration:** Begin by clicking the "Apply" link on the MET website. Register with your name, surname, email, mobile number, and qualification details.
2.  **Application Fee Payment:** Make a non-refundable online payment of INR 100 using a credit/debit card or Net Banking. Upon successful transaction, you will receive an email containing a PDF form download link.
3.  **Selection Procedure:** You will be notified via email or SMS about the scheduled date and time for an online or offline selection procedure.
4.  **University Admission Form:** If selected, you will need to fill out the University of Mumbai admission form, and your documents will be submitted to the University for further processing.
5.  **Fee Payment:** Once confirmed by the University of Mumbai, you will proceed to pay the course fees at MET.

## Fees Structure

The fee for the MMS program at MET Institute of Management is approximately **INR 2,20,345**. It's important to note that fees can be subject to change as per the directives of the Fees Regulatory Authority (FRA). Prospective students should always verify the latest fee structure on the college's official website.

## Cutoff Scores

For MMS admissions, the cutoff data is released by the Government of Maharashtra, as MAH CET is a state-level entrance examination. This data is applicable to all colleges participating in MBA/MMS admissions across Maharashtra. Candidates should aim for competitive scores in MAH CET to secure a seat at MET Institute of Management.

## Placements

MET Institute of Management has a commendable track record for placements. According to recent reports, the highest package offered during the 2025 placement drive at MET Mumbai was **INR 20 LPA**. This highlights the institute's strong industry connections and the career opportunities available to its graduates.

## Faculty

The faculty at MET Institute of Management comprises a balanced mix of young and experienced professors. Many faculty members are highly qualified and serve as visiting faculty, bringing valuable corporate insights from their roles in prominent corporate groups. They are known for being dedicated to student development, providing individual attention, and always being available for consultation and support.
