Jindal Global Business School (JGBS), part of O.P. Jindal Global University in Sonepat, offers a range of management and business programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and career prospects to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs and Fees at JGBS

JGBS provides a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The fee structure for these programs includes several components such as tuition and one-time payments. It's important to note that fees can change annually, typically with an increase of around 10%.

### MBA Programs

JGBS offers a full-time, two-year MBA and a one-year Global MBA in Business Analytics. For the Academic Year 2024, the two-year MBA has 120 seats, while the Global MBA in Business Analytics has 10 seats. Admission focuses heavily on interview performance.

| Program                        | Estimated Tuition Fee (2 years) | One-time Payment |
|--------------------------------|---------------------------------|------------------|
| MBA (2-year)                   | INR 1.6 Lakh - INR 22.5 Lakh    | INR 50,100       |

### BBA (H) and Integrated Courses

JGBS also offers a BBA (Honours) program and an integrated BBA (H) + MBA program.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to JGBS is based on entrance examination scores, followed by an interview. The application process is entirely online.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

For undergraduate courses, JGBS accepts scores from exams like SAT, ACT, and UGAT. They also conduct their own Jindal Scholastic Aptitude Test (JSAT). For MBA programs, accepted scores include CAT, MAT, and XAT.

### How to Apply

To apply, candidates must complete an online application form, providing personal and academic details, and uploading necessary documents. The application fees are:

*   **MBA:** INR 1,000
*   **BBA (H) and Integrated Courses:** INR 2,500

### Required Documents

Applicants will need to provide the following documents:

*   Academic mark sheets/transcripts
*   Leaving Certificate
*   Aadhaar Card
*   Passport-size photograph
*   Signature

## Placement Opportunities

Jindal Global Business School has a strong track record for placements across its BBA and MBA programs, with a high placement rate. Many students also opt to pursue further studies abroad.

JGBS maintains connections with leading companies across various sectors, including banking, consulting, and retail. Recruiters have included Mercedes Benz India, CJ Darcl, Reliance Retail, ICICI Bank, BOD Consulting, Regalo Kitchens, Asian Development Bank, ANZ Banking, and HDFC Bank.

## Why Choose Jindal Global Business School?

JGBS is a UGC-approved institution, accredited by NAAC, and is one of the twelve schools under O.P. Jindal Global University. It has received recognition, including an award from Fairtrade India. The institution was also ranked 8th in the MBA (Private) category by ICARE Ranking 2024.

The school emphasizes industry exposure through guest lectures from experienced professionals, offering students valuable insights and career guidance. This, combined with its strong industry connections, contributes to a beneficial learning environment for aspiring business leaders.
