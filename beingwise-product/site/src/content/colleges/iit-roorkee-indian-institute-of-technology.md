IIT Roorkee, a prestigious institution in the picturesque town of Roorkee, stands as a beacon of technical education in India. Renowned for its rich history and academic excellence, it offers a wide array of programs designed to shape future leaders and innovators.

## About IIT Roorkee

Established in 1847 and later designated an Indian Institute of Technology in 2001, IIT Roorkee is a public technical university of national importance, fully funded and governed by the central government. Its main campus spans 365 acres in Roorkee, complemented by a 25-acre campus in Saharanpur and a 10-acre extension center in Greater Noida. The institute boasts a vibrant community of over 8,000 students, supported by extensive residential facilities including ten boys' hostels, three girls' hostels, one co-ed hostel, and six hostels for married couples.

## Academic Programs and Departments

IIT Roorkee offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. The institute is structured into numerous departments, ensuring a comprehensive educational experience. Some key departments include:

*   Department of Architecture and Planning
*   Department of Applied Mathematics and Scientific Computing
*   Department of Civil Engineering
*   Department of Computer Science and Engineering
*   Department of Electrical Engineering
*   Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering
*   Department of Mechanical Engineering
*   Department of Chemical Engineering
*   Department of Biosciences and Bioengineering
*   Department of Physics
*   Department of Chemistry
*   Department of Earth Sciences
*   Department of Management Studies

The Department of Architecture and Planning, for instance, provides high-quality education in professional fields, offering Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) and Master of Architecture (M.Arch) degrees. It features state-of-the-art facilities like a Climatology Lab, Computer Labs, Computerized Design Studios, an Art Lab, and a Fabrication Lab, along with a prestigious Net Zero Emissions lab under an Indo-UK program.

## Admission Process

Admission to IIT Roorkee is highly competitive and primarily entrance-based. Prospective students must first meet the specific eligibility criteria for their chosen course. The general steps involve:

1.  **Eligibility Check**: Verify the academic requirements and other criteria for the desired program.
2.  **Application**: Submit an online application through the official portal.
3.  **Entrance Examination**: Appear for relevant national-level entrance exams such as JEE Advanced for B.Tech, IIT JAM for M.Sc, or GATE for M.Tech programs.
4.  **Counselling**: Participate in the subsequent counseling process based on entrance exam scores.
5.  **Fee Payment**: Upon selection, pay the admission fees to finalize the enrollment.

For M.Sc. programs, IIT JAM scores are accepted. For example, in 2023, a candidate with an IIT JAM rank of 200 in Mathematics, under the EWS category, would have been eligible for admission, as the closing ranks for Round 1 and the Last Round were 247 and 272 respectively.

## Faculty and Facilities

IIT Roorkee prides itself on its highly qualified and experienced faculty members, many of whom hold PhDs and are recognized experts in their fields, some even ranking among the top 2% globally. Students consistently rate the faculty highly for their knowledge and teaching skills.

The institute's extensive facilities enhance the learning and living experience. The central library, a treasure trove of knowledge, houses over 3.20 lakh documents, including online databases, CD-ROMs, standards, audio-video materials, theses, and reports. Beyond academics, IIT Roorkee offers numerous clubs, sports facilities, and guest houses, fostering a vibrant campus life for all its students.
