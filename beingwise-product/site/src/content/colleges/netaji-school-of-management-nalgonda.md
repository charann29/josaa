## Programs Offered

Netaji School of Management specializes in postgraduate management education. The primary program available is the Master of Business Administration (MBA).

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

This two-year full-time program is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of various business functions and management principles. It covers core areas such as finance, marketing, human resources, operations, and strategy, preparing students for leadership roles in diverse industries. The curriculum is structured to blend theoretical knowledge with practical application, often through case studies, projects, and internships.

NSM has an overall intake capacity of 120 students across its management programs. This ensures a focused learning environment. The courses offered at Netaji School of Management are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), a key government body that regulates technical education in India. This approval signifies that the program meets national standards for quality and curriculum.
