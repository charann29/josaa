Prestige Institute of Management and Research (PIMR) in Indore is a well-regarded institution for higher education. This page offers a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## About Prestige Institute of Management and Research, Indore

PIMR Indore is a private institution that has earned an 'A+' Grade accreditation from NAAC, reflecting its commitment to quality education. It is an ISO-certified college, approved by both UGC and AICTE, and is affiliated with Devi Ahilya University. The institute is known for its experienced faculty, many of whom hold advanced degrees and certifications, and it also facilitates faculty and student exchange programs.

## Courses Offered and Fees

PIMR offers a variety of undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) programs, primarily in the management stream. While specific fee details for all courses vary, the institute is known for its comprehensive curriculum designed by experts.

### Popular Courses and Eligibility

*   **MBA (Master of Business Administration):** To be eligible for MBA programs, candidates must appear for the CMAT entrance examination. Admission is merit-based, considering CMAT scores and other academic qualifications.
*   **Other UG Courses (e.g., BCA):** The institute has been recognized for its BCA program, ranking well nationally. Admission for many UG courses is typically merit-based.
*   **Law Courses:** PIMR also offers programs in Law, which have received national recognition.

## Admission Process

Admissions for various programs at PIMR Indore are currently open. Here’s a general step-by-step guide to the application process:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official website of Prestige Institute of Management and Research.
2.  **Register:** Locate the 'Admissions' section on the homepage and register yourself to create login credentials.
3.  **Log In:** Use your newly created login details to access the application portal.
4.  **Fill the Application Form:** Complete the application form with all the required personal and academic information.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Attach scanned copies of all necessary documents as specified.
6.  **Submit:** Review your application and submit it.

For MBA courses, appearing in the CMAT exam is a crucial eligibility step. After shortlisting based on merit and entrance exam scores, candidates will need to pay a confirmation fee to secure their seat.

## CMAT 2025 Schedule

The Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is a key entrance exam for MBA admissions at PIMR. For 2025, the CMAT exam was held earlier than in previous years:

*   **Registration Start:** November 14, 2024
*   **Registration End:** December 13, 2024
*   **Admit Card Release:** January 20, 2025
*   **Exam Date:** January 25, 2025

This schedule marked a return to an earlier examination period, contrasting with the May dates seen in 2023 and 2024.
