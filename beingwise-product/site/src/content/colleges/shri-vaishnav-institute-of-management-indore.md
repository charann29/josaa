Shri Vaishnav Institute of Management (SVIM), located in Indore, is a well-regarded institution offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. If you're considering SVIM for your higher education, this guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered and Eligibility

SVIM provides a diverse selection of courses across Science, Business & Management, and IT & Software fields. Here's a look at the main programs and their general eligibility requirements:

*   **B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science):** To be eligible, candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 examination from a recognized board.
*   **BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration):** Applicants need to have passed their 10+2 examination from a recognized board.
*   **BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications):** Prospective students must have completed their 10+2 examination from a recognized board.
*   **MBA/PGDM (Master of Business Administration/Post Graduate Diploma in Management):** A graduate degree or its equivalent from a recognized university is required.
*   **MCA (Master of Computer Applications):** Candidates must hold a graduate degree or its equivalent from a recognized university, with Mathematics as a core subject during their undergraduate studies.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to Shri Vaishnav Institute of Management can be either merit-based or entrance-based, depending on the specific program you're applying for.

*   **MBA Program:** For the Master of Business Administration (MBA) program, candidates are required to appear for the Common Management Admission Test (CMAT).
*   **Other Programs (B.Sc., BBA, BCA, MCA):** Admissions for most other programs are primarily merit-based, considering your academic performance in previous qualifying examinations.

Specific cutoffs for merit-based admissions can vary each year based on the number of applicants and their academic profiles. It's always advisable to check the official SVIM website or contact their admissions office for the most current cutoff information.

## Scholarships

Shri Vaishnav Institute of Management offers various scholarship opportunities to support eligible students. These scholarships aim to make quality education accessible to a wider range of candidates.

**Available Scholarships:**

*   **Post Metric Scholarship:** This scholarship is available for students belonging to SC/ST/OBC/Minority categories.
    *   **Eligibility Criteria:**
        *   **SC/ST Candidates:** Parental income should not exceed INR 6.00 lakhs per annum.
        *   **OBC Candidates:** Parental income should not exceed INR 3.00 lakhs per annum.
        *   **Minority & Central Sector Scheme Candidates:** Specific income criteria apply.
*   **Meritorious Scholarship:** This scholarship is specifically for high-achieving students from the Shri Vaishnav Group's various institutions.

Prospective applicants should check the detailed eligibility criteria for each scholarship with the institute's administration to confirm their suitability and application process.
