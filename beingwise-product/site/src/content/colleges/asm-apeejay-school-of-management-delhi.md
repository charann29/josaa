Welcome to BeingWise! If you're exploring options for higher education in management, the Apeejay School of Management (ASM) in Delhi is a name you might have come across. This page offers a clear overview of their programs, costs, admission steps, eligibility criteria, and career prospects.

## ASM - Apeejay School of Management, Delhi: An Overview

Located in the heart of Delhi, ASM is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management education. It aims to prepare students for the corporate world through its structured programs and industry-focused curriculum.

## Programs Offered and Fees

ASM primarily offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program, which is highly sought after by students aiming for leadership roles. There are two main PGDM options:

*   **General PGDM:** This comprehensive program covers various aspects of business management.
*   **PGDM in Marketing Management:** A specialized track for those passionate about marketing.

The total fee for the PGDM program at ASM is approximately **INR 9.70 Lakhs**. This amount includes a one-time payment of INR 35,000.

## Specializations within PGDM

During the second year of the PGDM program, students at ASM Dwarka can choose from a range of specializations to deepen their expertise. These include:

*   **Finance:** Focuses on investment analysis, financial markets, derivatives, and international finance.
*   **Marketing:** Covers brand management, consumer behavior, retail, sales, and digital marketing.
*   **Business Analytics:** Explores business forecasting, data visualization, big data, machine learning, and predictive tools.
*   **International Business:** Includes export-import documentation, international business law, strategy, marketing, and HR.
*   **Operations Management:** Deals with supply chain, logistics, quality tools, operations strategy, research, and analytics.
*   **Human Resource Management (HRM):** Involves employee relations, international HRM, compensation, performance management, and strategic HRM.

Each specialization offers a specific set of elective subjects, allowing students to tailor their learning to their career aspirations.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to ASM's PGDM programs is based on national-level entrance exam scores. Here's what you need to know:

*   **CAT (Common Admission Test):** A minimum overall percentile of 25 is generally required.
*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test):** For the 2023 academic year, the cutoff for CMAT was 85 percentile.

Prospective students should visit the official ASM website for the most current information regarding cutoffs, eligibility criteria, and the detailed admission process.

## Scholarships at ASM

ASM understands the importance of financial support for students. The institution offers various scholarship schemes to eligible candidates, helping to make quality management education more accessible. Interested students are encouraged to explore these options during the admission process.

## Campus Facilities

ASM provides a conducive environment for learning and personal growth. The campus offers various facilities to ensure a well-rounded student experience, including:

*   **Sports Facilities:** Both indoor and outdoor sports options are available to help students stay active and energized. This includes badminton, table tennis, and other recreational equipment.
*   **Gymnasium:** A 24/7 gym is accessible for students, along with facilities for indoor activities like yoga.

These facilities contribute to the overall well-being of students, promoting a healthy balance between academics and physical activity.

ASM aims to equip its students with the knowledge, skills, and industry exposure needed to thrive in the competitive business world. With its diverse programs, focus on practical learning, and dedicated placement support, it stands as a viable option for aspiring management professionals.
