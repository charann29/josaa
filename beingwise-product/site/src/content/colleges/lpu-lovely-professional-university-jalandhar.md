Lovely Professional University (LPU) in Jalandhar, Punjab, is a well-known private university offering a wide array of programs across various disciplines. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fees, admission process, and placement opportunities to help prospective students and parents make informed decisions.

## LPU: An Overview

LPU has established itself as a significant institution in the Indian higher education landscape. It caters to a diverse student body with a focus on practical learning and career readiness across its numerous schools and departments.

## Popular Courses and Eligibility

LPU offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Some of its most sought-after courses include B.Tech and MBA, alongside offerings in Design, Arts, Animation, Nursing, and Computer Applications. Here's a look at the eligibility and selection criteria for key programs:

### Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

*   **Eligibility:** Candidates must have completed Class 12 with at least 60% aggregate marks, with Physics, Mathematics, and English as compulsory subjects. For B.Tech in Biotechnology, Food Technology, and Biomedical Engineering, Class 12 with Physics, Mathematics/Biology/Biotechnology, and English is required, also with a minimum of 60% aggregate.
*   **Selection:** Admission is based on scores in LPUNEST, JEE Main, or CUET.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

*   **Eligibility:** A bachelor's degree with a minimum of 55% aggregate marks, or completion of professional qualifications like Company Secretary (CS) from ICSI, Chartered Accountancy (CA) from ICAI, or Cost & Management Accountancy (CMA) from ICAI.
*   **Selection:** Admission requires a valid score in LPUNEST, CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, or NMAT, followed by a Group Discussion and Personal Interview (GDPI).

### Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)/BBA (Hons)

*   **Eligibility:** Completion of Class 12 from a recognized board with 50%-60% aggregate marks, including English.
*   **Selection:** Based on scores in LPUNEST or CUET UG. Some BBA specializations may also consider merit.

### Master of Computer Applications (MCA)/MCA (Hons)

*   **Eligibility:** A minimum of 60% aggregate in BCA, B.Sc (CS/IT), or any other undergraduate degree where Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Business Maths, Business Statistics, or Quantitative Techniques was a subject at either the undergraduate or Class 12 level.
*   **Selection:** Admission is determined by performance in LPUNEST.

## Admission Process

LPU's admission process generally involves an entrance examination (LPUNEST being their proprietary test) and meeting specific academic eligibility criteria for each course. For certain programs, national-level entrance exam scores (like JEE Main for B.Tech or CAT/MAT for MBA) are also accepted. Some programs may also include an interview or group discussion round.

## Faculty and Learning Environment

LPU prides itself on its experienced and qualified faculty, many of whom hold PhDs or advanced degrees. The university encourages an advanced learning environment, often inviting guest lecturers and organizing workshops and seminars to enrich student knowledge and skills. This approach aims to provide students with comprehensive academic and practical exposure.
