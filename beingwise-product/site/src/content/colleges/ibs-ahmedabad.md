IBS Ahmedabad is a respected institution located in Gujarat, offering quality management education. If you're considering a postgraduate program in management, understanding its offerings, admission process, and career prospects is key.

## Programs Offered at IBS Ahmedabad

IBS Ahmedabad primarily focuses on its flagship Postgraduate Program in Management (PGPM). This program is designed to equip students with comprehensive business knowledge and practical skills for a successful career in various industries.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Securing a spot in the PGPM program at IBS Ahmedabad involves a structured process. Here’s what you need to know:

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission, candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum aggregate score of 50%.

### Application and Entrance Exams

Admission to the PGPM program is primarily through the IBS Aptitude Test (IBSAT). This exam is conducted by ICFAI Business School (IBS). Alternatively, IBS Ahmedabad also accepts scores from other national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, GMAT, and NMAT.

### Selection Rounds

Based on their performance in any of the accepted entrance exams, shortlisted candidates are invited for subsequent rounds, which include:

*   **Group Discussion (GD):** An assessment of your communication, teamwork, and analytical skills.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** An opportunity for the admissions committee to understand your aspirations, strengths, and suitability for the program.

Successful candidates in these rounds then proceed to document verification and fee payment to finalize their admission.

## IBSAT Key Dates (2023-2024 Cycle)

For those targeting admission through IBSAT, here are the important dates:

*   **Application Period:** Applications for IBSAT typically remain open until the last week of December.
*   **Exam Dates:** The IBSAT exam is usually held in the fourth week of December.
*   **Result Announcement:** Results for IBSAT are generally declared in the first week of January.

## Program Fees

While specific fee details are best confirmed directly with the institution, the investment in a PGPM from IBS Ahmedabad covers tuition, study materials, and other academic services. It's advisable to check the official website for the most current fee structure.

### Average Salary Trends

The average salary packages have shown a positive trend, reflecting the growing demand for IBS Ahmedabad graduates. For the 2025 batch, the overall average package was INR 7.81 LPA. Notably, the average package for the top 10% of students reached INR 9.97 LPA, and for the top 25%, it was INR 9.84 LPA.
