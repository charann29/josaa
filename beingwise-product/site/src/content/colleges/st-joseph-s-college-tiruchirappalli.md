## Courses Offered at St. Joseph's College

St. Joseph's College provides a diverse portfolio of full-time degree courses designed to equip students for various professional fields. These include undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) options across several disciplines.

**Undergraduate Programs (UG)**

The college offers five main UG programs:

*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** Focuses on core business principles and management skills.
*   **Bachelor of Arts (B.A.):** Includes specializations such as English, fostering critical thinking and communication.
*   **Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com):** Covers accounting, finance, and general commerce knowledge.
*   **Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.):** Features specializations like Computer Science, preparing students for technology-driven careers.
*   **Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA):** Aims to build strong foundations in computer applications and software development.

**Postgraduate Programs (PG)**

For those looking to advance their studies, the college offers:

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA)/Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** These programs are designed for aspiring business leaders, covering advanced management theories and practices.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

For BBA, B.A., B.Com, B.Sc., and BCA programs, applicants must have successfully passed their Higher Secondary examination (10+2) or an equivalent qualification from a recognized educational board.

**Postgraduate Course Eligibility:**
