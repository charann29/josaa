IIM Kozhikode, nestled in God's Own Country, stands as a beacon of management education in India. Renowned for its picturesque campus and innovative programs, it offers a transformative learning experience for aspiring leaders. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, admission process, fees, placements, and other key details to help you make an informed decision.

## About IIM Kozhikode

Established in 1996, IIM Kozhikode is one of the premier management institutes in India, consistently recognized for its academic excellence and contributions to management thought. The institute prides itself on a diverse and vibrant campus environment, fostering holistic development alongside rigorous academic training. With a focus on innovation and social responsibility, IIM Kozhikode prepares students to tackle complex global challenges.

## Programs Offered and Fees

IIM Kozhikode offers a range of programs designed to cater to different career stages and aspirations. The primary offerings include:

*   **Post Graduate Program (PGP):** This flagship program is a two-year residential course equivalent to an MBA. It equips students with a strong foundation in various management disciplines.
*   **Post Graduate Program in Finance (PGP-F):** A specialized program focusing on the intricacies of finance.
*   **Post Graduate Program in Liberal Studies & Management (PGP-LSM):** This unique program integrates liberal arts with management principles.
*   **Post Graduate Program in Business Leadership (PGP-BL):** A one-year intensive program for experienced professionals.
*   **Doctoral Programme in Management (DPM):** A research-oriented program for those aspiring to careers in academia or advanced research.
*   **BBA in Management Studies (BMS):** An undergraduate program for students completing their higher secondary education.

The fee structure varies significantly across these programs. For the PGP, PGP-F, and PGP-LSM, the total program fee is approximately INR 20.50 Lakhs. The PGP-BL has a fee of about INR 22.50 Lakhs. For the BMS program, the fee for the first year is around INR 5.50 Lakhs, with subsequent years' fees to be announced. It's important to note that these figures are subject to change, and prospective students should always verify the latest fee details on the official IIM Kozhikode website.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to IIM Kozhikode's programs is highly competitive, requiring candidates to meet specific eligibility criteria and successfully navigate a multi-stage selection process.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **Undergraduate Programs (BMS):** Candidates must have completed their Class 12 with relevant subjects from a recognized board.
*   **Postgraduate Programs (PGP, PGP-F, PGP-LSM, PGP-BL):** A Bachelor's degree in a relevant field from a recognized university is generally required. For PGP-BL, significant work experience is also a prerequisite.
*   **Doctoral Programme (DPM):** A Master's degree in a relevant field is essential.

### Selection Process

The selection process typically involves:

1.  **Entrance Exam:** For PGP and related programs, a valid CAT score is mandatory. For BMS, an entrance test conducted by IIM Kozhikode is required.
2.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are shortlisted based on their entrance exam scores, academic record, work experience (if applicable), and diversity factors.
3.  **Written Ability Test (WAT) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates undergo a WAT to assess their writing skills and a PI to evaluate their communication abilities, leadership potential, and general awareness.

## Cutoff

The cutoff for admission to IIM Kozhikode's PGP and related programs is generally high, reflecting the institute's prestige and the intense competition. While the exact CAT percentile varies each year based on the applicant pool and selection criteria, candidates typically need a composite score well above the 90th percentile to be considered for shortlisting. Specific sectional cutoffs also apply. For the BMS program, the cutoff for the institute's entrance exam will be determined annually.

## Campus Life and Facilities

Life at IIM Kozhikode is dynamic and enriching. The campus offers a comprehensive environment for learning and personal growth.

*   **Residential Facilities:** Most postgraduate students are required to live on campus. The institute provides 13 student hostels and faculty guest houses, fostering a close-knit community.
*   **Student Activities:** The Students' Council plays a vital role in campus life, coordinating various student bodies, clubs, and committees. Major annual events include:
    *   **Backwaters:** The annual management fest.
    *   **Echoes:** The annual intercollegiate cultural event.
    *   **E-Summit:** The annual entrepreneurship meet.
    *   **Horizons:** The annual management conclave.
    *   **Roobaroo:** A cultural event for new batches to integrate into IIMK culture.
    *   **Nostalgia:** The annual alumni meet, held since 2004.
    *   **Sangram:** An annual sports meet involving IIM Bangalore, IIM Trichy, and IIM Visakhapatnam.
*   **Innovative Ventures:** The campus is home to unique student-led initiatives, such as 'Bhooklagi,' a student-run startup operating within the campus, a first of its kind in any IIM.
