SRM Arts and Science College primarily offers a postgraduate degree in Business & Management Studies, specifically an MBA/PGDM program. This full-time course is designed to equip students with essential management skills and knowledge.

### MBA/PGDM Program

*   **Duration:** Typically two years.

*   **Total Seats:** The college has an intake capacity of 60 students for its MBA/PGDM program.

Admission to SRM Arts and Science College follows a structured process, with specific eligibility criteria for its programs.
