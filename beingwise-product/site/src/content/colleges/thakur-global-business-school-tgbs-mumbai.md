Thakur Global Business School (TGBS) in Mumbai is a well-regarded institution offering management education. If you're considering a postgraduate management program, understanding its offerings, costs, and career prospects is key. This guide will walk you through TGBS's courses, fees, admission process, and placement record.

## Programs Offered at TGBS

TGBS primarily focuses on its flagship Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This two-year, full-time course is designed to equip students with comprehensive business knowledge and practical skills, preparing them for leadership roles in various industries.

## Fees Structure for PGDM

Investing in a PGDM program is a significant decision, and understanding the fee structure is crucial. For the two-year PGDM program at Thakur Global Business School, the total tuition fee is approximately INR 11 Lakhs. This breaks down to INR 5.5 Lakhs per year. It's important to note that this figure covers tuition and does not include additional expenses like hostel accommodation, if you choose to avail it.

## Admission Process

Admission to the PGDM program at TGBS involves a structured process. Prospective students typically need to meet certain eligibility criteria, which usually include a bachelor's degree from a recognized university and a valid score in common management entrance exams. The selection process often involves reviewing academic performance, entrance exam scores, and performance in subsequent rounds like group discussions and personal interviews.

## Cutoff Information

While specific cutoff scores can vary each year based on the applicant pool and program demand, a strong performance in national-level management entrance exams is generally expected for admission to TGBS. Candidates are advised to check the official TGBS website or prospectus for the most current cutoff details and eligibility requirements.

## Faculty at TGBS

TGBS boasts a mix of experienced core faculty members and industry experts who serve as guest faculty. The institution has 13 core faculty members, with five holding PhDs and four having MMS qualifications, ensuring a strong academic foundation. Additionally, 12 guest faculty members, often industry professionals, bring real-world insights and perspectives to the classroom through workshops, seminars, and training sessions. This blend of academic rigor and practical industry exposure aims to provide students with a well-rounded learning experience.
