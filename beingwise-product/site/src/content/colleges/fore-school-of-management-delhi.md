FORE School of Management in Delhi is a well-regarded institution offering various postgraduate management programs. If you're considering a career in management, understanding its offerings, admission process, and career prospects is essential.

## Programs Offered at FORE School of Management

FORE School of Management focuses on postgraduate diplomas in management, designed to equip students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge for the corporate world. Their core offerings include:

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** This is a flagship program covering general management principles and practices.
*   **PGDM in International Business (IB):** Geared towards students interested in global markets and cross-border business operations.
*   **PGDM in Financial Management (FM):** For those aiming for careers in finance, banking, and investment sectors.
*   **PGDM in Big Data Analytics (BDA):** A specialized program for individuals keen on leveraging data for strategic decision-making.
*   **Fellow Programme in Management (FPM):** A doctoral-level program for aspiring researchers and academicians.

These programs are structured to provide a comprehensive learning experience, supported by experienced faculty and modern facilities.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the PGDM programs at FORE School of Management is highly competitive and primarily entrance-exam based. Here's a breakdown of the key steps:

### Eligibility Criteria

Candidates generally need a bachelor's degree with a minimum percentage (often 50%), though specific requirements might vary slightly by program. Appearing for a recognized national-level management entrance exam is mandatory.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

FORE School of Management accepts scores from the following national-level management entrance examinations:

*   **CAT (Common Admission Test)**
*   **XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)**
*   **GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)**

### Application Procedure

Applying to FORE School of Management is an online process. Here are the steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the admissions section.
2.  **Select Program:** Choose the PGDM program(s) you wish to apply for.
3.  **Register:** Provide your mobile number and other details to create login credentials.
4.  **Fill Application Form:** Complete the online application with your personal, academic, and professional details.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Submit scanned copies of required documents, such as mark sheets, certificates, and photographs.
6.  **Pay Application Fee:** The fee varies depending on the number of programs you apply to:

| Number of Courses | Application Fee (INR) |
| :---------------- | :-------------------- |
| One               | 1,770                 |
| Two               | 2,360                 |
| Three             | 2,950                 |
| Four              | 3,540                 |

7.  **Submit and Print:** Submit the form and keep a printout for your records.

### Selection Rounds

Shortlisted candidates, based on their entrance exam scores, will be invited for further selection rounds. These typically include:

*   **Extempore:** A spontaneous speaking task to assess communication and thought process.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** An interaction to evaluate personality, motivation, and suitability for the program.

Work experience is also given significant weightage during the final selection process.
