GL Bajaj Institute of Management and Research (GLBIMR), located in Greater Noida, is a well-known institution for management education. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fees, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities to help prospective students and their parents make informed decisions.

## Programs Offered: PGDM

GLBIMR primarily offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This is a full-time, two-year course designed to equip students with essential management skills and knowledge for the corporate world.

### Eligibility for PGDM

To be considered for the PGDM program, applicants must hold a graduate degree from a recognized university with at least 50% aggregate marks in their respective subjects.

### Selection Process

Admission to the PGDM program at GLBIMR involves a multi-stage selection process. Candidates are evaluated based on their scores in national-level management entrance exams, followed by a Group Discussion (GD) and a Personal Interview (PI).

The accepted entrance exams include:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   MAT (Management Aptitude Test)
*   CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   ATMA (AIMS Test for Management Admissions)

## Course Fees

The total tuition fee for the PGDM program at GL Bajaj Institute of Management and Research ranges from approximately INR 1.3 Lakhs to INR 7.8 Lakhs. It's always advisable to check the most current fee structure directly with the institution.

## Placements at GLBIMR

GLBIMR has a dedicated system to help students transition smoothly from academics to their professional careers. The institute focuses on making students industry-ready through various corporate readiness programs that run alongside their academic studies.

These programs include:

*   **Industry Visits:** Exposure to real-world corporate environments.
*   **Mentorship Activities:** Guidance from experienced professionals.
*   **Field Research Work:** Practical application of theoretical knowledge.
*   **Corporate Interface:** Opportunities to interact with industry leaders.
*   **Faculty-Monitored Corporate Internship Programs:** Hands-on experience under expert supervision.

GLBIMR has consistently reported 100% placements across various sectors. The institute attracts recruiters from diverse industries such as FMCG, Retail, Banking & Finance, Human Resources, Information Technology, Marketing, International Business, Hospitality, and other service sectors.

### Placement Highlights (2023)

As per the latest reports, the placements at GLBIMR for 2023 saw impressive figures:

*   **Highest Package:** INR 12 LPA (Lakhs Per Annum)
*   **Average Package:** INR 8.50 LPA (Lakhs Per Annum)

## Hostel Facilities

GL Bajaj Institute of Management and Research offers hostel accommodation for male students on its campus. While on-campus hostel facilities are available for boys, female students might need to explore options outside the campus. The rooms are subject to availability.

The hostel provides a range of amenities, including:

*   Hygienic food prepared according to a set menu.
*   Accommodation in two-seater and three-seater sharing rooms.
*   Both air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned room options.
*   Complimentary Wi-Fi and laundry services.
*   Spacious and airy rooms, often with balconies.
*   Each room is furnished with a bed, chair, wardrobe, and study table for each student.
*   Facilities for indoor games.
*   Geysers for hot water in winters and cold drinking water in summers.
*   Uninterrupted water supply and power backup.
*   Entertainment options like TV and newspapers.
*   First aid facilities and 24-hour security.

The approximate annual hostel fee for a three-seater room is around INR 95,000. Students also have the flexibility to arrange their accommodation off-campus if they prefer.
