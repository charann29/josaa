Jagan Institute of Management Studies (JIMS) in Rohini, Delhi, is a well-known institution established in 1993, offering various management programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

JIMS Rohini primarily focuses on postgraduate management education. The flagship offering is a two-year Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM).

*   **PGDM**: This program is designed to equip students with comprehensive management skills. It is considered equivalent to an MBA degree, a recognition that comes from its approval by AICTE and AIU.

## Fees Structure

The fee for the PGDM program at JIMS Rohini generally covers tuition, study materials, and other academic expenses. For the most current and detailed fee breakdown, including any installment options or additional charges, it is always best to refer to the official JIMS Rohini website or contact their admissions office directly.

## Admission Process

Admission to the PGDM program at JIMS Rohini is a multi-stage process, primarily based on entrance exam scores, followed by an evaluation of the candidate's overall profile.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the PGDM program, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university. There are no specific marks criteria mentioned, but a strong academic background is always beneficial.

### Application Steps

1.  **Online Application**: Candidates must visit the official JIMS Rohini website to complete and submit the online application form.
2.  **Entrance Exams**: Applicants need to appear for and achieve a valid score in one of the following national-level management entrance examinations:
    *   CAT (Common Admission Test)
    *   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
    *   GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)
    *   CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)
    *   ATMA (AIMS Test for Management Admissions)
3.  **Selection Rounds**: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for further selection rounds, which typically include:
    *   A Written Ability Test (WAT)
    *   Group Discussion (GD)
    *   Personal Interview (PI)

### Important Documents for Admission

When applying and during the admission process, candidates should be ready with the following documents:

*   Class 10th Marksheet
*   Class 12th Marksheet
*   Graduation Marksheets (all years/semesters)
*   Scorecard of the chosen entrance exam (CAT/XAT/GMAT/CMAT/ATMA)
*   Proof of work experience, if applicable
*   Certificates of extracurricular activities, if any

## Cutoff

JIMS Rohini considers scores from various entrance exams. While a specific cutoff percentage or score might vary each year based on the applicant pool and exam difficulty, a competitive score in any of the accepted entrance exams (CAT, XAT, GMAT, CMAT, ATMA) is crucial for shortlisting. The final selection also heavily depends on performance in the GD, PI, and WAT rounds.
