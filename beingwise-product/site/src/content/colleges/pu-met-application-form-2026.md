Planning your higher education journey in India often involves navigating various entrance exams and application processes. The PU MET (Panjab University Management Entrance Test) is a key examination for those aspiring to pursue management programs at Panjab University. This guide covers everything you need to know about the PU MET 2026 application, including courses offered, fees, the admission process, expected cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Understanding PU MET 2026

The PU MET is an annual entrance examination conducted by Panjab University, Chandigarh, for admission to its Master of Business Administration (MBA) and other related management programs. It's a paper-based test (PBT) designed to assess a candidate's aptitude for management studies. The exam is held once a year, and your performance in it is crucial for securing a seat in the university's esteemed departments.

### Key Dates for PU MET 2026 Application

While specific dates can shift slightly each year, based on previous trends, here are the expected timelines for PU MET 2026:

*   **Application Form Release:** Early December 2025
*   **Last Date for Online Registration:** Early April 2026
*   **Last Date for Fee Payment (Online/Offline):** Mid-April 2026
*   **Application Form Submission Deadline:** Mid-April 2026

It's always wise to check the official Panjab University website for the most accurate and up-to-date schedule.

## Courses Offered Through PU MET

The PU MET score is primarily used for admission to various MBA specializations and other management-related postgraduate programs at Panjab University. These programs are designed to equip students with comprehensive business knowledge and leadership skills.

## Eligibility Criteria for PU MET 2026

To be eligible for the PU MET, candidates generally need to meet specific academic requirements. While there might be minor variations for different courses, the fundamental criteria include:

*   A Bachelor's degree from a recognized university in any discipline, with a minimum aggregate of 50% marks.
*   Alternatively, candidates who have cleared the final examination of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India or England, the Institute of Cost and Works Accountants of India or England, or the Institute of Company Secretaries of India are also eligible.
*   Candidates who have passed the AMIE examination with at least 50% aggregate or a Diploma examination with a minimum of 60% aggregate and have at least five years of professional experience are also considered.

## PU MET Application Process 2026

The application process for PU MET 2026 is straightforward and can be completed online. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Go to the designated admission portal of Panjab University (often referred to as UAIMS).
2.  **Register:** Click on the registration link and provide your personal details such as your name, father's name, mother's name, date of birth, a valid mobile number, and an active email ID. Make sure all information is accurate.
3.  **Note Login Credentials:** After successful registration, you will receive an application number, password, and login ID. Keep these safe for future reference.
4.  **Download Fee Slip (for offline payment):** If you plan to pay the fee offline, download and print the SBI bank deposit slip.
5.  **Pay Application Fee:**
    *   **Online:** Pay using a debit card, credit card, internet banking, or UPI facility.
    *   **Offline:** Deposit the required fee at any State Bank of India (SBI) branch. Ensure you do this at least one day after printing the bank slip.
6.  **Complete Application Form:** Log in with your credentials and fill in the remaining parts of the application form, including academic details and choice of courses.
7.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of your recent passport-size colored photograph and signature as per the specifications.
8.  **Review and Submit:** Carefully review all the information entered before final submission. Print a copy of the completed application form for your records.

### Application Fee

The application fee for PU MET 2026 varies by category:

| Category         | Application Fee (INR) |
| :--------------- | :-------------------- |
| General Category | 2,710                 |
| SC/ST/OBC        | 1,355                 |

The fee can be paid online or offline through an e-challan at an SBI bank branch.

## Required Documents for Application

When applying for PU MET, ensure you have the following documents ready for upload or reference:

*   Scanned recent colored passport-size photograph
*   Class 10th mark sheet
*   Class 12th mark sheet
*   Bachelor's degree mark sheets/certificates (if applicable)
*   Date of birth certificate
*   Photo ID proof (Aadhar Card, Passport, Driving License, etc.)
*   Caste certificate (if applicable, for SC/ST/OBC candidates)
*   Any other relevant certificates (e.g., PwD certificate)
