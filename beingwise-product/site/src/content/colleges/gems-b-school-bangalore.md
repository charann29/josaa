GEMS B School in Bangalore is a well-regarded institution offering a range of business and management programs. It aims to develop future leaders through a strong, industry-focused curriculum and practical learning experiences.

## Programs and Courses Offered

GEMS B School provides diverse undergraduate and postgraduate degrees across various business disciplines, including management, hospitality, aviation, and mass communication. These programs are designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills essential for the corporate world.

## Admission Process

Admission to GEMS B School typically involves a structured evaluation process. Aspiring students need to meet specific eligibility criteria for their chosen program and successfully navigate the selection stages.

### Eligibility and Entrance Test

For most programs, including the Corporate Integrated BBA+MBA, candidates generally need to have completed their 12th standard. A key requirement for admission is also a minimum age of 21 for certain integrated programs. All applicants are required to take the GEMS Online Admission Test. This entrance exam assesses a candidate's aptitude, analytical abilities, and general knowledge relevant to the management programs offered. Shortlisting of candidates is based on their performance in this test.
