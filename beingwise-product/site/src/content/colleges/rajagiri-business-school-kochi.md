Rajagiri Business School (RBS) in Kochi is a well-regarded institution offering management education. This guide provides a clear overview of its programs, fees, admission process, and career opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

Rajagiri Business School primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs, including a flagship Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) and a Fellow Programme in Management (FPM).

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** This is a full-time, two-year program designed to equip students with comprehensive business knowledge and leadership skills.
*   **Fellow Programme in Management (FPM):** An advanced research-oriented program for those aspiring to careers in academia or advanced research.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for programs at Rajagiri Business School is competitive and aligns with similar institutions offering quality management education. For the PGDM program, prospective students should expect to invest approximately INR 8.25 Lakhs for the entire course duration.

## Admission Process

The admission process at Rajagiri Business School is structured to identify candidates with strong academic backgrounds and leadership potential. Here’s a breakdown for each program:

### PGDM Admission

1.  **Eligibility:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university with at least 50% aggregate marks.
2.  **Entrance Exams:** Candidates need to take one of the accepted management entrance exams: CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, or KMAT. A valid score from any of these is essential.
3.  **Application:** Submit your application form along with your entrance exam scores.
4.  **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a Group Discussion (GD) and a Personal Interview (PI). RBS also conducts a written test as part of the final selection.

### FPM Admission

1.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are initially shortlisted based on their scores in UGC NET, CSIR JRF, or the institute's own Rajagiri Research Aptitude Test (RRAT).
2.  **Interview and Presentation:** Shortlisted applicants will then undergo a personal interview and are required to deliver a presentation on a research topic.

## Cutoff Scores

Rajagiri Business School considers scores from various national and state-level management entrance exams for PGDM admissions. While specific cutoffs can vary each year, a strong performance in any of the following exams is crucial:

*   CAT
*   GMAT
*   KMAT Kerala
*   MAT
*   XAT
*   CMAT

Meeting the minimum eligibility and securing a competitive score in one of these exams will significantly enhance your chances of admission.
