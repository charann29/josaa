## DPU AIET 2026: Your Gateway to Management & Business Programs

DPU AIET serves as the entrance examination for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, primarily focusing on management and business administration. For the 2026 academic year, aspiring students can look forward to applying for programs like the Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) through this test. Understanding the application procedure, eligibility, and exam structure is crucial for a successful admission.

## Programs Offered and Eligibility Criteria

DPU offers sought-after programs through the AIET, with specific eligibility requirements for each. Here's what you need to know:

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

To be eligible for the MBA program, candidates must have completed their graduation in any discipline from a recognized university. A minimum aggregate score of 50% is required for candidates from the General category, while those belonging to Reserved categories need a minimum of 45%. Students appearing for their final year graduation examinations are also welcome to apply.

### Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

While specific details for BBA eligibility weren't provided in the source, typically, BBA programs require candidates to have successfully completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination from a recognized board.

## Understanding the AIET Application Process 2026

The application process for DPU AIET 2026 is entirely online, designed for convenience. It's important to be prepared with all necessary information and documents before you begin.

### Essential Documents for Application

When filling out your application form, ensure you have the following ready:

*   A valid email address and mobile phone number for communication.
*   Certificates and mark sheets from your qualifying examinations.
*   A scanned copy of your signature.
*   A recent passport-size photograph in digital format.
*   Details for online payment, such as a debit/credit card, UPI account, or net banking information.

### Application Fee Structure

The application fee is a crucial part of the process and is non-refundable. You can pay the fee online using a debit card, credit card, UPI, or net banking.

*   **For MBA Programs:** The application fee, including the test and processing fee, is INR 1,300.
*   **For BBA Programs:** The application fee, including the test and processing fee, is INR 1,050.
*   **Late Submission:** If you submit your application form after the official deadline, an additional late fee of INR 250 will be applicable.

### Important Note on Form Correction

It is vital to fill out the DPU AIET application form with utmost care and accuracy. Once the form is submitted, there is no provision for making corrections or edits. If an error is made after submission, candidates would need to fill out a completely new application form and pay the fee again. Therefore, double-check all information before final submission.

## DPU AIET Exam Pattern 2026

The DPU AIET is designed as an objective, multiple-choice question (MCQ) based examination. Here's a quick look at its structure:

| Aspect                 | Details                                  |
| :--------------------- | :--------------------------------------- |
| **Medium of Exam**     | English                                  |
| **Mode of Exam**       | Online Proctor Based Test                |
| **Duration**           | 150 minutes                              |
| **Type of Questions**  | Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)         |
| **Marking Scheme**     | 1 mark for each correct answer           |
| **Negative Marking**   | No negative marking                      |
| **Sections (MBA)**     | 5 sections                               |
| **Sections (BBA)**     | 4 sections                               |
| **Total Marks (MBA)**  | 150 marks                                |
| **Total Marks (BBA)**  | 100 marks                                |

### Taking the Online Proctored Exam

As an online proctored test, you can take the DPU AIET from any convenient location. However, you must ensure you have:

*   A reliable laptop or desktop computer with a functioning camera.
*   A stable and high-speed internet connection to avoid interruptions.

## Preparing for DPU AIET: Tips for Success

The DPU AIET is generally considered an accessible examination. With the right preparation, you can confidently aim for a good score.

*   **Understand the Syllabus:** Thoroughly cover all topics outlined in the AIET syllabus. This forms the foundation of your preparation.
*   **Practice Mock Tests:** Engage in regular practice sessions using mock tests. This helps in improving your speed, accuracy, and familiarity with the MCQ format.
*   **Time Management:** Since there's no negative marking, focus on attempting all questions while managing your time effectively across sections.
*   **Start Early:** If AIET is your primary entrance exam, consider starting your preparation at least six months in advance. If you've already appeared for other national-level entrance exams, focusing on AIET-specific sample papers should suffice.
