IIM Sirmaur, nestled in the picturesque state of Himachal Pradesh, is one of the newer Indian Institutes of Management, known for its focus on management education. This page offers a clear overview of its academic programs, fee structure, admission criteria, CAT cutoffs, and placement outcomes to assist prospective students and their families.

## Programs Offered at IIM Sirmaur

IIM Sirmaur primarily offers two full-time postgraduate programs designed to equip students with strong management skills:

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** This flagship two-year residential program provides a comprehensive understanding of various business functions and management principles.
*   **MBA in Tourism & Hospitality Management (MBA THM):** A specialized two-year residential program focusing on the unique management challenges and opportunities within the dynamic tourism and hospitality sectors.

## Fee Structure

The fees for IIM Sirmaur's programs cover tuition, library access, IT services, and other academic facilities. It's important to note that these figures are approximate and can change. Additional costs for accommodation, food, and personal expenses are separate.

## Admission Process

Admission to IIM Sirmaur's MBA and MBA THM programs is a multi-stage process, primarily based on CAT scores, followed by personal interviews and an assessment of academic and professional profiles.

1.  **CAT Examination:** Candidates must first appear for the Common Admission Test (CAT) and achieve a competitive score.
2.  **Shortlisting for Personal Interview (PI):** Based on CAT scores and other criteria (academic profile, work experience), candidates are shortlisted for the PI round.
3.  **Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates participate in a personal interview to assess their communication skills, leadership potential, and motivation.
4.  **Final Selection:** The final merit list is prepared considering the CAT score, PI performance, academic record, work experience, and diversity factors.

## CAT Cutoff

While the official cutoffs can vary each year based on the applicant pool and program demand, here are the typical CAT percentile cutoffs for IIM Sirmaur for general category candidates:

*   **Overall CAT Percentile:** 92+ percentile
*   **Sectional CAT Percentile:** 70+ percentile in each section (Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension, Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning, Quantitative Ability)

It's crucial to remember that achieving the minimum cutoff does not guarantee a call for the PI, as higher scores are often preferred, especially for the final selection.
