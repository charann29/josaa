The Suryadatta Institute of Business Management and Technology (SIBMT) in Pune is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate programs. It's a popular choice for students aiming for careers in business management and information technology.

## Programs Offered at SIBMT Pune

SIBMT primarily focuses on postgraduate studies, providing specialized programs designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills. The institute offers courses in:

*   **MBA/PGDM (Master of Business Administration/Post Graduate Diploma in Management):** This program is for those aspiring to leadership roles in various business sectors. It covers core management principles and offers specializations to help students carve out their niche.
*   **MCA (Master of Computer Applications):** Designed for students keen on a career in the IT and software industry, this course focuses on advanced computing concepts and application development.

Both the MBA/PGDM and MCA programs are approved by the AICTE, a key government body for technical education in India. The total intake capacity across all courses at SIBMT is 300 students.

## Admission Criteria for SIBMT Pune

To be considered for admission to SIBMT's postgraduate programs, candidates must meet specific academic requirements:

### MBA/PGDM Eligibility

*   Applicants must hold a Bachelor's Degree of at least three years duration in any discipline from a university recognized by the UGC or AIU.
*   A minimum of 50% aggregate marks is required. For candidates belonging to backward class categories and Persons with Disability from Maharashtra State, the minimum mark is 45%.
*   Students in their final year of a qualifying examination are also eligible to apply.

### MCA Eligibility

*   Candidates should have completed a BCA, B.Sc. (Computer Science), B.Sc. (IT), B.E. (CSE), B.Tech (CSE), B.E. (IT), B.Tech. (IT), or an equivalent degree from a recognized university or institute.
*   A minimum of 50% aggregate marks is necessary. For backward class categories and Persons with Disability from Maharashtra State, the requirement is 45%.
*   Graduates with any degree (B.E., B.Tech, B.Sc., B.Com, B.A., B.Voc) who have studied Mathematics at the 10+2 level or at the graduation level may also apply. They might need to complete additional bridge courses as per university norms.
*   Students appearing for their final year of the qualifying examination are eligible.
