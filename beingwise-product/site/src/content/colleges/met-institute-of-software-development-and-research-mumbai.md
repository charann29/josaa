The MET Institute of Software Development and Research (ISDR) in Mumbai is a notable institution for those looking to advance their skills in the software and IT sector. This guide offers a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and career opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at MET ISDR

MET ISDR primarily focuses on professional postgraduate programs designed to equip students with industry-relevant software development skills. The flagship offering is the Post Graduate Program (PGP) in Software Development.

### Post Graduate Program (PGP) in Software Development

This comprehensive program is tailored for individuals aiming to build a strong career in software development. It covers various aspects of software engineering, programming languages, and project management, preparing graduates for diverse roles in the IT industry.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

Admission to MET ISDR is competitive and follows a structured selection process. Understanding the eligibility criteria and application steps is crucial for prospective students.

### Eligibility Criteria for PGP

To be considered for the Post Graduate Program, applicants must meet one of the following requirements:

*   A Bachelor's degree (BE, BCom, BSc IT, BCA, or Science) from a recognized university.
*   A Post Graduate degree or a Management Graduate qualification from a recognized university.
*   Working professionals with relevant experience are also encouraged to apply.

### Selection Process

MET ISDR employs a two-stage selection process to identify suitable candidates:

1.  **Online Entrance Test:** This test assesses your aptitude and foundational knowledge relevant to software development.
2.  **Online Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates will undergo an online interview to evaluate their communication skills, motivation, and suitability for the program.

Both stages are mandatory, and successful completion of both is required for admission.

## Fee Structure

The fee for the Post Graduate Program (PGP) in Software Development at MET ISDR is ₹1.2 Lakhs. This amount is subject to change, and it's always advisable to confirm the latest fee details directly with the institute's admissions team.
