Chitkara University, located near Chandigarh, has built a strong reputation in Indian higher education since its establishment in 2010. Known for its wide array of programs and industry-focused approach, it's a popular choice for students across various disciplines.

## Overview of Chitkara University

Chitkara University, Chandigarh, is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and holds accreditations from various professional bodies like COA, PCI, NCTE, and the Indian Nursing Council. It also boasts an A+ Grade accreditation from NAAC (National Assessment & Accreditation Council), reflecting its commitment to quality education. The university is well-regarded for its infrastructure and its extensive offerings across undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels.

## Programs and Specializations

Chitkara University provides a comprehensive range of over 100 courses through its 16 colleges. These programs span diverse fields including Management, Humanities, Arts, Science, Commerce, Technology, and Health Sciences. The university also offers global and online courses, catering to a broad spectrum of student needs. Programs are available in full-time and part-time formats, with specializations like Business Analytics, Chemistry, and Civil Engineering.

### Popular Course Eligibility Criteria

Admission to various programs at Chitkara University relies on specific eligibility criteria and entrance exam scores. Here's a look at some key programs:

| Course Name | Common Eligibility |
| :---------- | :----------------- |
| BE          | Class 12 with 60% aggregate, with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) as compulsory subjects |
| BA LLB (Hons) | Class 12 with 50% aggregate |
| BBA         | Class 12 with 50% aggregate in any stream |
| BCA         | Class 12 in any stream with a minimum 50% aggregate |
| MCA         | Graduate with 55% aggregate and Mathematics as a compulsory subject in Class 12 |
| MBA         | Graduate with 50% aggregate in any stream |

## Admission Process and Entrance Exams

Chitkara University accepts scores from national-level entrance exams for admission to specific courses. For engineering programs like BE, a valid JEE Main score is essential. For other programs, tests like SAT and PSAT are also considered. For instance, the Integrated BBA-MBA (IPM) program accepts SAT scores, and the BDes program considers UCEED and SAT scores.

### Engineering Admissions (BE)

Prospective BE students must first apply for the course after appearing for JEE Main. Admission is granted based on the JEE Main score, followed by a counseling process. It's crucial for applicants to regularly check the official university website for updates on the counseling schedule, especially after JEE Main results are declared.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for courses at Chitkara University varies depending on the program. Specific fee details are typically provided on the university's official website or during the admission process. Students are required to pay the course fee to confirm their seat once selected.

## Placement Opportunities

Chitkara University has a strong track record for placements, with a significant number of students securing job offers across various sectors. The university's dedicated placement cell works to connect students with leading companies.

### 2023 Placement Highlights

*   **BTech Placements:** Over 1,150 BTech students were placed in 2023. More than 670 companies recruited engineering graduates, offering roles in IT, Assurance, Education, Operations, and Sales & Marketing.
*   **MBA Placements:** The highest package for MBA students in 2023 reached INR 44 LPA, with an average package of INR 9 LPA.

These figures demonstrate Chitkara University's commitment to ensuring its graduates are well-prepared for successful careers in their chosen fields.
