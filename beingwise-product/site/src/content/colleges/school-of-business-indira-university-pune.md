The School of Business at Indira University, Pune, is a well-regarded institution offering various management programs designed to equip students with essential business skills. If you're considering a career in business and management, understanding the courses, fees, admission process, and placement opportunities here is crucial.

## Programs Offered and Fees

Indira University's School of Business provides comprehensive programs for aspiring business leaders. The flagship program is the Master of Business Administration (MBA), alongside a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in management for those pursuing advanced research.

### MBA Program

The MBA program is structured to provide a strong foundation in business principles and practices, preparing students for diverse roles in the corporate world. The total fees for the MBA program generally range from INR 4,75,000 to INR 5,20,000.

### PhD Program

The PhD in Management is for candidates interested in advanced academic research and contributing to management theory and practice. Specific fee details for the PhD program are usually available upon inquiry to the university admissions office.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the School of Business is primarily based on entrance examinations, followed by further selection rounds. It's not solely merit-based on academic scores, emphasizing a holistic assessment of candidates.

### MBA Admission

**Eligibility:**
*   A bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 50% in graduation. For candidates from Maharashtra belonging to Backward Classes (BC) or Persons with Disabilities (PWD) categories, the minimum aggregate is 45%.

**Selection Process:**
Candidates must appear for one of the following national or state-level entrance examinations:
*   MAH CET
*   CAT
*   MAT
*   CMAT
*   XAT
*   ATMA
*   GMAT

Following a valid score in one of these exams, candidates will participate in a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round as part of the final selection process.

### PhD Admission

**Eligibility:**
*   A postgraduate degree in a relevant field from a recognized university.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 55% in the postgraduate degree.

**Selection Process:**
Admission to the PhD program involves an entrance examination conducted by the university, followed by a Personal Interview (PI) round.
