The Department of Management Studies (DMS) at IIT Delhi, located in Hauz Khas, offers a premier management education within a renowned technological institute. Choosing the right MBA program is a significant decision, and understanding all aspects from courses to career prospects is key.

## Programs Offered

DMS IIT Delhi primarily offers a full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) program and an Executive MBA (EMBA).

### MBA Program

The flagship two-year MBA program is designed for graduates seeking to develop strong management and leadership skills. It integrates a robust curriculum with practical exposure, leveraging IIT Delhi's strong industry connections and research environment.

### Executive MBA (EMBA) Program

The Executive MBA is a three-year program tailored for working professionals. To be eligible, candidates typically need a minimum of 60% marks in their undergraduate degree, along with relevant work experience. This program allows professionals to enhance their management capabilities without interrupting their careers.

## Fees Structure

Understanding the financial commitment is crucial for prospective students.

*   **Executive MBA:** The fee for the three-year Executive MBA program is approximately INR 12.6 lakhs. Please note that this figure is indicative and sourced from unofficial channels; it does not cover additional expenses like hostel accommodation or mess charges. For the most accurate and up-to-date fee information, candidates should always refer to the official DMS IIT Delhi website.

Fees for the full-time MBA program are also available on the official institute website and may vary slightly each academic year.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to DMS IIT Delhi's MBA programs is highly competitive, attracting bright minds from across the country. The selection process typically involves a valid CAT score, followed by a Written Ability Test (WAT) and a Personal Interview (PI).

*   **CAT Score:** A high score in the Common Admission Test (CAT) is a primary requirement for shortlisting candidates for the MBA program.
*   **Academic Record:** Candidates' academic performance throughout their undergraduate studies also plays a significant role.
*   **Work Experience:** For the Executive MBA, and often for the full-time MBA as well, relevant work experience is a crucial factor.

Specific cutoff percentages for CAT vary each year based on the applicant pool and institute policy. It is advisable to check the official DMS IIT Delhi admissions brochure for the precise cutoff criteria for the current admission cycle.

DMS IIT Delhi has a strong track record of successful placements, attracting top companies from various sectors. The institute consistently achieves high placement rates for its MBA graduates.

### Key Trends:

*   **Placement Rate:** In 2025, 98% of MBA students were placed, continuing a strong trend where the placement rate was 100% in the preceding two years.
*   **Highest Salary:** The highest package offered in 2025 reached INR 43.55 LPA, showing an upward trend from previous years.
*   **Average Salary:** The average package for 2025 stood at INR 22.52 LPA.
*   **Median Salary:** The median package in 2025 was INR 22.5 LPA.

### Top Recruiting Domains

Strategy & Consulting emerged as the leading recruiting domain in 2025, accounting for over 30% of placements. Other significant domains include IT, Operations, BFSI (Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance), and General Management.

### Major Recruiters

DMS IIT Delhi attracts a diverse range of prominent companies for placements. Some of the top recruiters in 2025 included Johnson & Johnson, Bain & Company, JSW, Accenture, Paytm, EY, PwC, and McKinsey & Company. These companies represent sectors such as consulting, FMCG, technology, and finance.

### Alumni Network

Alumni from DMS IIT Delhi are widely placed across various industries. Data suggests that the Business Development domain has absorbed a significant number of MBA graduates, followed by roles in Marketing, Consulting, Education, Product Management, and Finance, among others. The strong alumni network provides valuable connections and opportunities for current students.
