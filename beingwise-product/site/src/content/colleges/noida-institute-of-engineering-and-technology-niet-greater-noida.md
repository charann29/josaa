The Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology (NIET), located in Greater Noida, is a well-regarded private institution offering a range of programs in engineering, management, and other fields. Affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and approved by AICTE, NIET holds a NAAC 'A' grade accreditation and is recognized by the UGC.

## Courses Offered at NIET

NIET provides over 15 undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma courses across various disciplines. These include popular choices like B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA, alongside other specialized programs.

### Popular Courses and Fees (Approximate Annual)

| Course | Duration | Entrance Exam | Approximate Annual Fees (INR) |
|---|---|---|---|
| B.Tech | 4 Years | JEE Main, CUET | 1.5 - 1.7 Lakhs |
| M.Tech | 2 Years | CUET PG | 1.2 - 1.4 Lakhs |
| MBA | 2 Years | CUET PG, CAT (for UPTAC) | 1.3 - 1.5 Lakhs |
| Diploma | 3 Years | Merit-based | 60,000 - 80,000 |

*Please note: Fees are indicative and subject to change. Always check the official NIET website for the most current fee structure.* 

## Admission Process at NIET

Admissions at NIET are open for various courses. The process generally involves applying online, appearing for relevant entrance exams, and participating in counselling or selection rounds.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **For Undergraduate (UG) courses (e.g., B.Tech):** Candidates must have completed Class 12 from a recognized board.
*   **For Postgraduate (PG) courses (e.g., M.Tech, MBA):** Candidates need to hold a bachelor's degree of at least three years' duration from a recognized university.

### Application Steps

1.  **Online Application:** Visit the official NIET website and complete the online application form. A non-refundable application fee is required to finalize the submission.
2.  **Entrance Exams & Selection Rounds:** For most courses, admission is based on national or state-level entrance exam scores. Accepted exams include JEE Main and CUET for B.Tech, and CUET PG for M.Tech and MBA. Candidates will then participate in UPTAC counselling or internal selection processes. For direct admissions (including management quota), NIET may conduct its own admission tests or consider merit.
3.  **Merit List & Seat Confirmation:** Following the selection rounds, NIET releases a provisional merit list. Selected candidates must pay the course fees to confirm their admission and secure their seat.

### Direct Admission and Management Quota

NIET also offers direct admission under the Management/NRI quota, filling up to 15% of the approved intake for a course. This is done as per the guidelines set by the Government of Uttar Pradesh and AKTU.

## Cutoff Information

Cutoffs for admission to NIET's B.Tech and other programs are determined by the scores in entrance exams like JEE Main and CUET, and are managed through UPTAC counselling. These cutoffs vary each year based on factors such as the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. Prospective students should refer to the UPTAC counselling website and NIET's official site for specific cutoff ranks and scores from previous years.

## Placements at NIET

NIET has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with employment opportunities. The institute boasts a strong track record of placements, with students securing positions in leading national and multinational companies. Some of the prominent recruiters include TCS, Cognizant, HCL, Wipro, and Accenture.

NIET aims to provide quality education and prepare students for successful careers through its comprehensive academic programs and robust placement support.
