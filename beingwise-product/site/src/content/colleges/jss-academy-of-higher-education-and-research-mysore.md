JSS Academy of Higher Education and Research (JSS AHER) in Mysore is a prominent institution recognized for its strong focus on medical and health-related studies. It offers a wide array of courses, attracting students from across India and internationally.

## Courses Offered at JSS AHER

JSS AHER provides diverse programs ranging from undergraduate to doctoral levels, primarily in health sciences. Some of the key courses include:

*   **Undergraduate Programs:** MBBS, BDS, B.Pharm, Pharm.D, BSc (various specializations like Medical Genetics, Nutrition & Dietetics, Medical Laboratory Technology), BBA.
*   **Postgraduate Programs:** MD, MS, MDS, M.Pharm, MSc (numerous specializations), MBA.
*   **Doctoral Programs:** PhD across various disciplines.

## Fee Structure

The fee structure at JSS AHER varies significantly depending on the course. For instance, professional medical courses like MBBS and BDS have different fee components compared to science-based undergraduate or postgraduate programs. Generally, fees include tuition, a security deposit, and other charges. Specific details for each program are available on the official JSS AHER website.

## Admission Process

Admission to JSS AHER is a structured process that considers both academic merit and entrance examination scores for different programs.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **Undergraduate Courses (e.g., BSc, BBA):** Candidates generally need to have passed their Class 12 or equivalent examination from a recognized board, often with a minimum aggregate percentage as specified for the chosen specialization. For many BSc programs, a science background in Class 12 is required, but Mathematics may not be compulsory for all streams.
*   **Professional Medical/Dental Courses (MBBS, BDS):** Admission is primarily based on performance in national-level entrance examinations.
*   **Postgraduate Courses (MD, MS, MDS, M.Pharm, MSc, MBA):** Eligibility typically requires a relevant undergraduate degree and may involve either an entrance exam or merit-based selection from the qualifying examination.

### Application Procedure

Prospective students must complete an online application form available on the JSS AHER official website. This involves submitting required documents and paying the application fee online.

### Selection Process

*   **Merit-Based:** For courses like BSc, MSc, and BBA, selection is often based on the candidate's academic performance in their last qualifying examination.
*   **Entrance Exam Based:** For highly competitive courses such as MBBS, BDS, MD, MS, and MDS, admission is determined by scores in relevant entrance examinations.

### International Student Admissions

JSS AHER welcomes international students, including those from Foreign, PIO, and NRI categories, for a wide range of courses. These students typically apply online and are admitted based on their academic results in prerequisite examinations, without the need for an entrance test.

## Cutoff Information

Cutoff marks are particularly relevant for entrance exam-based courses like MBBS and BDS, where admission depends on national-level examination scores. These cutoffs can vary each year based on factors such as the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. For merit-based courses, the cutoff essentially refers to the minimum percentage or grade required in the qualifying examination for eligibility.
