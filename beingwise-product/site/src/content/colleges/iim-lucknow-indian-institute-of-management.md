# IIM Lucknow - Indian Institute of Management: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

IIM Lucknow, a premier institution located in Uttar Pradesh, is widely recognized for its high-quality management education. This page provides a clear overview of its academic programs, fee structures, admission procedures, expected cutoffs, and career opportunities, helping prospective students and parents make informed decisions.

## Key takeaways

*   IIM Lucknow offers a diverse range of postgraduate and doctoral programs in management, with its flagship PGP course being highly sought after.
*   Admission to most programs is primarily based on competitive entrance exams like CAT, followed by further selection rounds.
*   The institute consistently reports strong placement outcomes, with attractive salary packages for its graduates.
*   While highly competitive, a strategic approach to entrance exams and selection rounds can significantly improve admission chances.

## Programs Offered and Fees

IIM Lucknow provides various full-time and executive management programs tailored to different career stages and aspirations. The flagship Post Graduate Program (PGP) is a comprehensive two-year residential course, alongside specialized programs like PGP-ABM (Agri-Business Management) and PGP-SM (Sustainable Management), and the International Program in Management for Executives (IPMX).

For those interested in research, the Fellow Programme in Management (FPM) leads to a doctoral degree. Executive programs are also available for working professionals seeking to enhance their leadership skills.

While specific fee details for each program are best confirmed on the official IIM Lucknow website, the PGP program typically involves a substantial investment, covering tuition, accommodation, and other academic expenses. Prospective applicants should review the latest fee structure available on the institute's official portal.

## Admission Process and Selection Criteria

Admission to IIM Lucknow is a multi-stage process designed to identify candidates with strong academic potential and leadership qualities. For the flagship PGP program, the primary gateway is the Common Admission Test (CAT).

### PGP Admission Steps:

1.  **CAT Examination:** Candidates must register for and appear in the CAT exam, administered by the IIMs.
2.  **Shortlisting:** Based on CAT scores and other criteria (academic profile, work experience, diversity factors), candidates are shortlisted for the next stage.
3.  **Written Ability Test (WAT) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates participate in a Written Ability Test and a Personal Interview. These rounds assess communication skills, general awareness, subject knowledge, and suitability for a management career.
4.  **Final Selection:** A composite score, considering CAT performance, WAT, PI, and academic/diversity points, determines the final merit list.
5.  **Seat Confirmation:** Selected candidates confirm their admission by paying the required fees.

For other programs like the Executive MBA or FPM, different entrance exams such as GMAT, XAT, GATE, GRE, or JRF/SRF (UGC/CSIR/ICAR) might be accepted, followed by similar selection rounds.

## CAT Cutoff Trends

IIM Lucknow is one of the top-tier IIMs, and as such, the CAT cutoff scores are highly competitive. While the exact minimum percentile for shortlisting can vary each year based on the applicant pool and selection criteria, candidates typically need a very high overall percentile, often above the 90th percentile, with strong sectional percentiles, to receive an interview call for the general category. For reserved categories, the cutoffs are comparatively lower, as per government regulations.

It's important to remember that meeting the cutoff is just the first step; performance in the WAT and PI rounds is crucial for final selection.
