Chetana's Ramprasad Khandelwal Institute of Management and Research (CRKIMR) in Mumbai is a well-regarded institution for management studies. If you're considering a Master of Management Studies (MMS) degree, understanding its offerings, admission criteria, and career prospects is key.

## Master of Management Studies (MMS) at CRKIMR

CRKIMR primarily offers a comprehensive Master of Management Studies (MMS) program. This postgraduate degree is designed to equip students with essential management skills and knowledge, preparing them for diverse roles in the corporate world.

### Admission Process

Admission to the MMS program at CRKIMR is primarily based on performance in the MAH CET entrance examination. Following the entrance exam, candidates participate in a centralized admission process (CAP) conducted by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Maharashtra.

Key steps include:

*   **Entrance Exam:** Appearing for and scoring well in the MAH CET.
*   **Application:** Submitting an application to CRKIMR.
*   **Counselling:** Participating in the DTE CAP rounds.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the MMS program, candidates must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university with a minimum aggregate score (usually 50% for general category and 45% for reserved categories). Additionally, a valid MAH CET score is mandatory.

### Fees Structure

The approximate fee for the MMS program at CRKIMR is INR 5,50,000. It's always advisable to check the latest fee structure directly with the institute or on their official website, as figures can be subject to change.

## Cutoff Information

Admission to CRKIMR is competitive, with cutoffs varying each year based on the MAH CET scores. The institute releases category-wise cutoffs for the MMS program across multiple rounds of admission.

## Placements and Career Opportunities

CRKIMR has a decent track record for placements, with a dedicated placement cell that assists students in securing internships and final job placements. The institute focuses on preparing students for industry requirements through training and development programs.

### Placement Highlights:

*   **Median Salary:** The reported median salary for MMS graduates is approximately INR 5.50 Lakhs per annum.
*   **Placement Assistance:** The institute provides support for resume building, interview preparation, and connecting students with potential employers.

Students have rated CRKIMR's placements positively, indicating a satisfactory outcome for many graduates.
