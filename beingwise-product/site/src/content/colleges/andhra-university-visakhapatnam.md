Andhra University, located in the vibrant city of Visakhapatnam, stands as a prominent institution for higher education. Established with a vision to foster academic excellence, it offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines.

## Courses Offered at Andhra University

Andhra University provides a wide array of courses to cater to different academic interests and career aspirations. These programs are designed to offer comprehensive knowledge and practical skills.

### Undergraduate Programs

For students seeking bachelor's degrees, Andhra University offers programs like:

*   **Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech):** Specializations include Computer Science and Engineering, Naval Architecture and Ship Building, and Biotechnology, among others.
*   **Bachelor of Arts (B.A.):** This is a three-year program with numerous specializations, often structured over six or eight semesters. Some popular combinations include:
    *   History, Politics, Special Telugu
    *   History, Politics, Special English
    *   Public Administration, Politics, Sociology
    *   History, Economics, Politics
    *   History, Public Administration, Politics
*   **Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com):** This program is primarily offered through the university's affiliated colleges.

### Postgraduate Programs

The university also provides extensive postgraduate opportunities for advanced studies.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Securing admission to Andhra University involves specific eligibility criteria and entrance examinations, depending on the chosen course.

### B.Tech Admission

For B.Tech courses, candidates must qualify in either the Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test (AP EAMCET) or the Andhra Pradesh Engineering Common Entrance Test (AP ECET). It's worth noting that some affiliated engineering colleges may also consider JEE Main scores for admissions.

### Other Undergraduate Admissions

For many other undergraduate programs, a competitive state-wise rank, generally below 300 in relevant entrance exams, is often required for direct admission to the university's main campus programs. Specific entrance exams like INI CET might be relevant for certain courses.

### B.Com Admission

Admission to B.Com programs is managed by the university's affiliated colleges. Prospective students need to meet the specific eligibility requirements of the chosen affiliated college, which commonly includes clearing the higher secondary examination from a recognized board. The process typically involves registration, application submission, and fee payment directly with the affiliated college.

## Fee Structure

The fee structure for various courses at Andhra University can vary. Detailed fee information is usually available on the official university website or through the respective department/affiliated college brochures.

## Cutoff Information

Andhra University does not publicly release specific cutoff ranks for all B.Tech courses. Admission for these programs is primarily based on qualifying scores in AP EAMCET or AP ECET. For other undergraduate courses, a good state-level rank is essential for securing a seat.

## Placements at Andhra University

Andhra University has a commendable track record for student placements, with a dedicated focus on preparing students for successful careers. The university provides robust placement support, connecting students with leading companies.

## Faculty and Infrastructure

Andhra University is home to a distinguished faculty known for their expertise and contributions to research. The university employs over 350 professors, 190 associate professors, and 115 assistant professors, supported by more than 2,500 staff members. Many faculty members have been recognized nationally and internationally, with some holding key positions in state higher education bodies. Student reviews, particularly for the College of Engineering, often rate the faculty highly, indicating experienced and qualified educators.
