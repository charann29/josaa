Dibrugarh University, located in Dibrugarh, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of courses. This guide provides a clear overview of its academic programs, fee structures, admission procedures, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities to assist prospective students and their families.

## Dibrugarh University: An Overview

Dibrugarh University holds recognition from the University Grants Commission (UGC), affirming its standing in the Indian higher education landscape. It serves as a central hub for learning in the region, attracting students from various backgrounds.

## Courses and Fees

The university offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Here's a look at some popular choices:

*   **Bachelor of Arts (BA):** This undergraduate program is a cornerstone of the university, with a substantial intake capacity distributed across its affiliated colleges. Admission is primarily based on Class 12 merit.
*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** A sought-after postgraduate degree, the MBA program prepares students for leadership roles in various industries.
*   **Bachelor of Education (B.Ed):** This program is designed for aspiring teachers, focusing on pedagogical skills and educational theory.
*   **Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharma) & Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharma):** These programs cater to students interested in pharmaceutical sciences, covering drug discovery, development, and patient care.

Specific fee details for each course can vary and are best confirmed on the university's official website during the application period.

## Admission Process

### General Steps for Admission

1.  **Online Registration:** Candidates typically begin by registering on the official university website.
2.  **Application Form Submission:** After registration, applicants fill out the detailed application form and submit it along with the required application fee.
3.  **Merit/Entrance Exam:** Depending on the course, admission may be based on academic merit (e.g., Class 12 scores for BA) or performance in specific entrance examinations.
4.  **Selection and Enrollment:** Shortlisted candidates complete the admission process by submitting relevant documents and paying the course fees.

### Course-Specific Admission Details

*   **BA Admission:** Selection for the Bachelor of Arts program is based on the marks obtained in the Class 12 examination from a recognized board. The university also adheres to the state's reservation policies for seat allocation.
*   **MBA Admission:** For the MBA program, candidates are usually shortlisted based on their scores in national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, CMAT, or MAT. This is often followed by a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) conducted by the university.
*   **B.Ed Admission:** Admission to the Bachelor of Education course is determined by scores in the Common Entrance Test (CET) administered by Dibrugarh University itself.

## Seat Intake

Dibrugarh University, along with its affiliated institutions, has a substantial capacity for various programs. Please note that these figures are indicative and subject to change.

*   **BA:** The total intake for the BA program, including seats across all affiliated colleges, is approximately 82,365.
