Admission to Jain University typically involves several steps, including an entrance examination for many courses, while some engineering programs offer direct admission.

### Jain Entrance Test (JET)

For many programs, admission is based on the Jain Entrance Test (JET). This test usually comprises a written examination, followed by a Group Discussion (GD) and a Personal Interview (PI).

To apply for JET:

1.  **Register:** Complete the initial registration for the exam.
2.  **Application Form:** Fill out the online application form with all required details.
3.  **Test Centre Selection:** Choose your preferred test center.
4.  **Document Upload:** Submit necessary documents online.
5.  **Fee Payment:** Pay the application fee.

### Direct Admission (Lateral Entry)

For certain B.Tech programs, Jain University offers direct admission (lateral entry) based on academic merit from previous qualifying examinations. There is no entrance exam required for these lateral entry admissions. For example, admission to B.Tech Lateral Entry is granted based on a valid percentage in a Diploma course. Candidates who meet the eligibility criteria and selection process are then invited for a counseling session.
