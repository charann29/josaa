The SPJAT (SP Jain Aptitude Test) is a crucial entrance examination for those aspiring to pursue postgraduate management programs at the SP Jain School of Global Management. This guide provides a clear overview of the courses, fees, admission process, expected cutoffs, and placement opportunities for the 2026 intake, helping you make informed decisions about your higher education journey.

## Understanding SPJAT 2026

SPJAT is a computer-based test conducted multiple times throughout the year, designed to assess candidates' aptitude for management studies. It's a key step for admission to SP Jain's distinctive global programs.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for SPJAT and SP Jain's postgraduate programs, candidates generally need to hold a valid bachelor's degree from a recognized university. Students awaiting their final graduation results are also welcome to apply. While there's no age limit, applicants must have completed their Class 12 education from a recognized board or institute. For the Master of Global Business (MGB) program, fresh graduates without prior work experience are eligible. However, for the Global MBA (GMBA) program, relevant work experience is a prerequisite.

### Application Process and Fees

The application fee for SPJAT is INR 3500. This payment must be made online using methods like credit card, debit card, or internet banking, as offline payment options are not available.

## SPJAT Exam Pattern

The SPJAT question paper is structured into five distinct sections, comprising a total of 34 questions and a maximum score of 100 marks. The distribution of questions and marks per section is as follows:

| Section                 | Number of Questions | Marks per Question | Total Marks per Section |
| :---------------------- | :------------------ | :----------------- | :---------------------- |
| Diagrammatic Reasoning  | 7                   | 3                  | 21                      |
| Logic                   | 6                   | 2                  | 12                      |
| IQ                      | 6                   | 2                  | 12                      |
| Numeracy                | 10                  | 4                  | 40                      |
| Reading Comprehension   | 5                   | 3                  | 15                      |
| **Total**               | **34**              |                    | **100**                 |

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

After taking the SPJAT, the institute will announce a cutoff score to shortlist candidates. Only those who meet this cutoff will be invited to participate in the subsequent selection rounds. These typically include an essay writing component, case study analysis, and a personal interview. Occasionally, a group discussion might also be part of the process. These evaluation rounds are conducted at various selection centers, including those in India, Dubai, Singapore, and Sydney.

## Programs Offered and Fees

SP Jain offers prestigious postgraduate programs, primarily the Master of Global Business (MGB) and the Global MBA (GMBA). While specific fee details for 2026 are usually updated closer to the admission cycle, these programs are known for their global exposure and comprehensive curriculum. Prospective students should check the official SP Jain website for the most current fee structure.

## Placements

SP Jain School of Global Management has a strong reputation for impressive placements, largely due to its multi-campus model and global industry connections. Graduates from both the MGB and GMBA programs typically secure roles in diverse sectors across various countries, reflecting the international nature of their education. The career services team actively assists students with internships and final placements, preparing them for global careers.

For the most precise and up-to-date information regarding SPJAT 2026, including specific dates, detailed course structures, and placement reports, candidates are always advised to refer to the official SP Jain School of Global Management website.
