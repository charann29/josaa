Vivekanand Business School (VBS) in Mumbai is a well-regarded institution for management education. If you're considering a postgraduate management program, understanding VBS's offerings, admission process, and career prospects is key to making an informed decision.

## Courses and Eligibility

VBS primarily offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This is a comprehensive program designed to equip students with essential business skills and knowledge.

### PGDM Eligibility

To be considered for the PGDM program at VBS, you need to meet the following criteria:

*   **Academic Qualification:** A Bachelor's degree from any recognized university with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
*   **Entrance Exam:** A valid score from one of the accepted national-level management entrance examinations.

## Admission Process

Admission to the PGDM program at VBS is based on a structured, entrance-based process. The institute considers multiple factors to select suitable candidates.

### Selection Criteria for PGDM

1.  **Entrance Exam Scores:** You must have a valid score from one of these exams: CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, ATMA, MAH CET, or GMAT.
2.  **Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a Group Discussion and Personal Interview round. These stages assess your communication skills, analytical abilities, and overall suitability for a management program.

### Application Deadlines

For the PGDM program, the Phase 1 application deadline is typically around March 10. It's advisable to apply well in advance, as VBS offers a limited number of seats. Keep an eye on the application deadlines for the entrance exams you plan to take as well.

## Fees Structure

The fee details for the PGDM program at VBS are best confirmed directly with the admissions office, as they can vary slightly each academic year. It's always recommended to check the official VBS website or contact their admissions team for the most current and accurate fee information.
