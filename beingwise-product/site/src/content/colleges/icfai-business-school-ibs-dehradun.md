IBS Dehradun, part of the ICFAI Business School network, is a well-regarded institution for management education in Uttarakhand. This page offers a clear overview of its academic programs, fee structure, admissions process, and career opportunities, helping you make an informed decision for your higher education journey.

## Programs Offered and Eligibility

IBS Dehradun provides a range of business and management programs designed to equip students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge. Each course has specific academic requirements for admission.

*   **MBA (Master of Business Administration)**
    *   **Eligibility:** A bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. The medium of instruction during graduation must have been English.

*   **BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)**
    *   **Eligibility:** Completion of Class 12 or an equivalent examination from a recognized board, with at least 50% aggregate marks.

*   **BCom (Bachelor of Commerce)**
    *   **Eligibility:** Completion of Class 12 or an equivalent examination from a recognized board, with at least 50% aggregate marks.

*   **PhD (Doctor of Philosophy)**
    *   **Eligibility:** A bachelor's degree in any discipline with at least 50% aggregate marks, where English was the medium of instruction. Additionally, a postgraduate degree in Management (with specializations like Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, or Operations) with at least 55% aggregate marks is required.

## Admission Process and Entrance Exam

Admission to IBS Dehradun's programs typically involves an aptitude test and subsequent selection rounds.

### IBS Aptitude Test

IBS Dehradun conducts its own online aptitude test, known as the IBS Aptitude Test. This examination is administered by The ICFAI Foundation for Higher Education (IFHE), Hyderabad, which is a Deemed University. The test evaluates candidates on several key areas:

*   Quantitative Techniques
*   Data Interpretation
*   Data Adequacy
*   Vocabulary
*   Analytical Reasoning
*   Reading Comprehension

For detailed information on the application process and test dates, prospective students should visit the official website.

## Fee Structure

The approximate fee for the 2-year MBA program at IBS Dehradun is around INR 6.03 Lakhs. It's important to note that fees can vary and it's always best to check the most current figures directly with the institution.

## Campus Facilities

IBS Dehradun offers a conducive learning environment with several facilities:

*   **Library:** A comprehensive library stocked with a wide array of textbooks and reference materials covering management and IT subjects.
*   **Classrooms:** Modern classrooms equipped with LCD projectors, computers, and overhead projection systems.
*   **Computer Lab:** A computer lab offering 24-hour internet access.
*   **Auditorium:** An auditorium with a seating capacity for 120 individuals.
*   **Cafeteria:** A spacious rooftop cafeteria for students and staff.
