## Bahra University, Solan: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

Bahra University, located in Solan, Himachal Pradesh, is a private institution offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the university to help students and parents make informed decisions.

## About Bahra University

Established under a state legislature in Himachal Pradesh, Bahra University is a private university recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the State Government of Himachal Pradesh. It also holds recognition from bodies like the Bar Council of India (BCI) and the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), and is a proud member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU).

## Programs and Schools of Study

Bahra University offers diverse programs across various disciplines through its dedicated schools. Some of these include:

*   School of Engineering & Technology
*   School of Management & Commerce
*   School of Law
*   School of Pharmaceutical Sciences
*   School of Hospitality & Tourism
*   School of Basic & Applied Sciences

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admissions at Bahra University are generally conducted online. Prospective students can visit the official university website to apply. The process typically involves submitting an online application form along with the required documents.

### General Eligibility Criteria:

*   **Undergraduate Courses:** Candidates usually need to have completed Class 12 from a recognized board, meeting specific percentage requirements for their chosen program.
*   **Postgraduate Courses:** A relevant undergraduate degree from a recognized university is typically required.

### Specific Program Example: B.Pharmacy Admission

For the B.Pharmacy program, candidates must have passed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology or Mathematics, achieving a minimum of 45% aggregate marks (40% for reserved categories) from a recognized state or national board. Admission is primarily merit-based, followed by an interaction with an admissions counsellor.

### Required Documents for Admission:

When applying, students generally need to provide:

*   Class 10 Marksheet and Certificate
*   Class 12 Marksheet and Certificate
*   Undergraduate Degree Marksheet and Certificate (if applicable)
*   Entrance Test Scorecard (if applicable)
*   Passport-sized Photographs
*   Domicile/Residential Certificate
*   Aadhaar Card/ID Proof
*   Caste Validity Certificate (if applicable)
