Lloyd Business School, located in Greater Noida, is a well-regarded institution offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. If you're considering a future in business management or commerce, understanding its offerings, admissions process, and career prospects is key.

## Programs Offered

Lloyd Business School provides diverse programs tailored to different career aspirations:

*   **Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)**: This flagship program includes specializations like Business Analytics.
*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA)**
*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)**
*   **Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)**

## Admission Process

Securing a spot at Lloyd Business School involves a clear process, varying slightly by program. Admissions are currently open, and prospective students can apply online through the college's official website or by visiting the admissions office for offline forms.

### Selection Criteria

Admission to most programs is entrance-based, followed by further evaluation. Here's a breakdown:

| Course | Selection Criteria |
| :----- | :------------------------------------------------------ |
| PGDM/MBA | Valid scores in CAT/MAT/XAT/CMAT/GMAT + Group Discussion & Personal Interview (GDPI) |
| BBA    | Merit-based (Class 12 scores) + Personal Interview (PI) |
| B.Com  | Merit-based (Class 12 scores) + Personal Interview (PI) |

For final selection, the school considers not only entrance exam scores but also a candidate's past academic record, involvement in extracurricular activities, and overall personality.

### Eligibility for BBA

For the BBA program, candidates need a minimum of 45% marks in their Class 12 examinations from any stream. Students from SC/ST categories require a minimum of 40% in their qualifying examination.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure varies by program. Here’s a look at the PGDM with Analytics Specialization fee breakdown:

**PGDM with Analytics Specialization Fee (Approximate Total: INR 9 Lakhs)**

*   **At Admission**: INR 75,000 (Non-refundable registration)
*   **First Installment**: INR 1.4 Lakhs (within 15 days of admission)
*   **Subsequent Installments**: Five further installments of INR 1.4 Lakhs each, followed by a final installment of INR 1.25 Lakhs. These are typically spread across the duration of the program.

*Please note: These figures are based on official information but are subject to change. For the most current fee details for all programs, it's always best to check the official Lloyd Business School website.*

## Placements

Lloyd Business School has a consistent record of conducting annual placement drives for its postgraduate programs, attracting a wide array of companies across various sectors.

### PGDM Placements (Class of 2025 Highlights)

The PGDM program, including specializations like Business Analytics, is particularly noted for its strong placement outcomes.

*   **Highest International Package**: INR 25 LPA
*   **Highest Domestic Package**: INR 24.8 LPA
*   **Average Package**: INR 7.22 LPA
*   **Number of Recruiters**: 240+
*   **Total Offers & Internships**: 400+
*   **Highest Internship Stipend**: INR 57,000 per month

Sectors that frequently recruit from the PGDM batch include BFSI (Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance), Consulting, IT/ITeS, Manufacturing, and E-commerce.

### MBA Placements (Class of 2024 Highlights)

*   **Highest Package**: INR 15.5 LPA
*   **Average Package**: INR 5.25 LPA
*   **Lowest Package**: INR 3 LPA
*   **Total Recruiters**: 220+

These statistics highlight Lloyd Business School's commitment to connecting its students with valuable career opportunities upon graduation.
