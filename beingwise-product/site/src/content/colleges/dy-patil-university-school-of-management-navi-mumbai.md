Navi Mumbai's DY Patil University School of Management (DYPUSM) is a well-regarded institution offering various management programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, eligibility criteria, and placement opportunities to help prospective students and their families make informed decisions.

## Programs Offered and Fees

DYPUSM provides both undergraduate and postgraduate management programs, designed to equip students with essential business skills and knowledge.

### Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The BBA is a three-year undergraduate degree with five specialisation options. The total tuition fee for the BBA program ranges from INR 4.5 Lakhs to INR 8.3 Lakhs.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The MBA is a two-year postgraduate program offering a wide array of specialisations, with 13 options available. The total tuition fee for the MBA program is between INR 10 Lakhs and INR 12.6 Lakhs.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

The admission process at DYPUSM involves specific academic qualifications and, for some programs, entrance examinations and interviews.

### BBA Admission

*   **Eligibility:** Candidates must have passed their Class 12 examination from any recognised board (CBSE, ISC, State Boards, etc.) with a minimum aggregate score of 50%.
*   **Selection Process:** Admission is primarily based on the merit score achieved in the Class 12 board exams. Shortlisted candidates will also need to participate in a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round conducted by the university. The final admission decision considers the Class 12 performance, GD-PI rounds, and the overall student profile.

### MBA Admission

*   **Eligibility:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognised university with a minimum aggregate score of 50%.
*   **Selection Process:** Candidates need to possess a valid score from a recognised management entrance examination such as CAT, MAT, CMAT, ATMA, XAT, or the university's own DYPU-CET.

## Required Documents for Admission

When applying for admission to DYPUSM, candidates must provide several important documents. It's crucial to ensure all documents are valid and accurate to avoid any issues during the admission process. The required documents include:

*   Class 10 Marksheet
*   Class 12 Marksheet
*   Graduation Marksheets (for MBA applicants)
*   Aadhaar card copies (student and parents)
*   Original Migration Certificate
*   Original Transfer Certificate
*   Category Certificate (if applicable for SC/ST/OBC/EWS)
*   Three coloured passport-size photographs
*   Original Domicile Certificate
*   Character Certificate
*   Physical Fitness Certificate from a registered medical practitioner

## Placements

DY Patil University School of Management has a strong track record of placements for its graduates across various programs, including MBA, BBA, and BA. The institution focuses on ensuring students secure internships and employment opportunities.

Here are the key placement highlights for the MBA Class of 2023:

| Particulars        | Placement Statistics (2023) |
| :----------------- | :-------------------------- |
| Highest Package    | INR 10.04 LPA               |
| Average Package    | INR 6 LPA to INR 7 LPA      |
| Minimum Package    | INR 3 LPA                   |
| Top Recruiters     | Accenture, Deloitte, Airtel, Sony |

It's worth noting that 100% of MBA students from the 2023 batch secured internships.

## Recognition and Rankings

DY Patil University holds significant recognition and has achieved notable rankings. It is approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education). In 2023, the university was ranked 93rd out of 272 institutions in India and 26th in Maharashtra. Additionally, it secured a ranking of 120 out of 200 colleges nationally in 2023. The university also offers UGC-entitled online degree courses for both bachelor's and master's programs.
