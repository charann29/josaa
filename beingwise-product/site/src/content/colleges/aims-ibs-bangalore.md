AIMS IBS, located in Bengaluru, is a well-regarded institution offering various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, eligibility criteria, and placement opportunities, helping you make an informed decision for your higher education journey.

## Programs Offered at AIMS IBS

AIMS IBS provides a range of programs designed to equip students with the skills needed for today's dynamic job market. Their offerings include popular undergraduate degrees like BBA and BCA, as well as a comprehensive MBA program.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The MBA at AIMS IBS is a two-year, full-time program. It is affiliated with Bangalore University and holds approval from AICTE. A notable feature of this MBA is the flexibility to choose two specialisations, allowing students to tailor their studies to their career aspirations. Available specialisations include:

*   Marketing
*   Finance
*   Human Resource Management
*   Healthcare Management
*   Banking and Finance

### Other Programs

Beyond the MBA, AIMS IBS also offers popular undergraduate courses such as the Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) and Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA). These programs are designed to provide a strong foundation in their respective fields.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to AIMS IBS programs typically involves meeting specific academic requirements and, for certain courses, appearing for entrance examinations. The institute aims to admit students with a strong academic background and a keen interest in their chosen field.

While specific cut-off details for individual programs are generally communicated during the admission cycle, candidates are encouraged to maintain a good academic record and prepare for any required entrance tests.

## Fee Structure

The fee structure for programs at AIMS IBS varies by course. Prospective students should check the official AIMS IBS website for the most current and detailed fee information for their desired program.

## Placement Opportunities

AIMS IBS has a commendable track record when it comes to student placements, offering 100% placement assistance to its students across MBA, BCA, and BBA programs. The institute actively works to connect students with leading companies, both nationally and internationally.

### Placement Highlights for the Class of 2024

The placement drive for the Class of 2024 saw strong outcomes, with a significant percentage of students securing positions in reputable organisations. A small percentage also opted for further studies or entrepreneurship.

These recruiters reflect the diverse opportunities available to AIMS IBS graduates, highlighting the institute's strong industry connections and the quality of its programs.

## Institute Recognition

AIMS Institutes is generally considered to be a strong private institution and business school. AIMS-IBS has also received international recognition, being named Asia's best emerging business school by the World Education Congress and CMO Asia, underscoring its commitment to quality education and leadership development.
