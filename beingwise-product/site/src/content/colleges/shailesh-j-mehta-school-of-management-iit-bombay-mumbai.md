The Shailesh J. Mehta School of Management (SJMSOM) at IIT Bombay is a distinguished institution in Mumbai, offering a premier management education. This guide provides a clear overview of its programs, admission process, fees, placement outcomes, and more, to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

SJMSOM primarily offers a Master of Business Administration (MBA) program, designed to equip students with comprehensive management skills and a strong foundation in business principles. The curriculum is regularly updated to reflect current industry trends and challenges.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to SJMSOM's MBA program is highly competitive and primarily based on performance in the Common Admission Test (CAT). Candidates must meet specific academic and test score criteria to be considered.

### Eligibility Criteria for MBA

To be eligible for the MBA program, applicants generally need:

*   A four-year Bachelor's degree or a Master's degree in any field with a minimum of 60% aggregate marks. (For SC/ST candidates, the minimum aggregate is 55%).
*   Professionals with qualifications like CA, ICWA, CFA, CS, or FRM, along with a first-class Bachelor's degree (60% aggregate, or 55% for SC/ST), are also eligible.
*   Students in their final year of a degree program may apply, but their final admission is contingent on fulfilling all eligibility requirements by the time of admission.

### Selection Process

SJMSOM uses a multi-stage selection process:

1.  **CAT Score:** A valid CAT score is mandatory. Applicants must achieve minimum overall and sectional percentiles as determined by SJMSOM.
2.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates meeting the CAT cutoff are shortlisted for a Personal Interview (PI) round.
3.  **Final Merit List:** The final selection is based on a composite score, considering various parameters:

    | Parameter                  | Weightage |
    | :------------------------- | :-------- |
    | CAT Overall Score          | 70%       |
    | Gender Diversity           | 10%       |
    | Academic Profile           | 10%       |
    | Work Experience            | 5%        |
    | Post Graduate/Professionals | 5%        |

## CAT Cutoff Percentiles

To be considered for the Personal Interview stage, candidates typically need to meet the following minimum CAT percentile cutoffs:

| Category         | Quant | VARC | DILR | Overall |
| :--------------- | :---- | :--- | :--- | :------ |
| General / EWS    | 75    | 75   | 75   | 90      |
| NC-OBC           | 67.5  | 67.5 | 67.5 | 81      |
| SC               | 50    | 50   | 50   | 60      |
| ST               | 50    | 50   | 50   | 60      |
| PWD              | 50    | 50   | 50   | 60      |

These cutoffs are subject to change each admission cycle.

## Campus Life and Facilities

SJMSOM, located within the expansive IIT Bombay campus, offers a vibrant and well-equipped environment for learning and personal growth.

### Faculty

The school boasts a dedicated and highly qualified faculty, many of whom are alumni of top national institutions. With a student-to-core faculty ratio of approximately 10:1, students benefit from personalized attention and mentorship.

### Hostels

On-campus hostel facilities are available for both male and female students, offering single and shared occupancy options. Hostels are well-equipped with amenities such as reading rooms, TV rooms, gyms, and computer rooms. Regular movie screenings are held at the IIT Bombay Auditorium, and indoor games like table tennis, chess, and carrom are available for recreation.

### Medical Facilities

The campus includes a hospital providing general and specialized healthcare services to students, staff, and faculty, ensuring comprehensive medical support.

### Other Facilities

The SJMSOM campus provides modern amenities, including air-conditioned classrooms, laboratories, a cafeteria, gymnasium, auditorium, dance room, and a convenience store. The campus is fully equipped with high-speed internet, sports centers, and a well-stocked library with thousands of books and theses.
