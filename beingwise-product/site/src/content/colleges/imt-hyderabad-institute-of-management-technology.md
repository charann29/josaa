IMT Hyderabad, the Institute of Management Technology, is a well-regarded institution for management education located in Hyderabad. This page provides a clear overview of its postgraduate programs, fee structure, admission process, expected cutoffs, and placement records to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

IMT Hyderabad primarily offers the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program, which is equivalent to an MBA. This program is designed to equip students with essential management skills and knowledge, covering a wide range of specializations relevant to today's industries.

### PGDM Specializations
The PGDM program at IMT Hyderabad allows students to specialize in various areas, aligning their studies with their career aspirations. While specific specializations can vary, common options include Finance, Marketing, Human Resources, Operations, and Business Analytics.

## Fees Structure

The fee for the PGDM program at IMT Hyderabad generally covers tuition, academic resources, and other institutional charges. For the most current and detailed fee breakdown, it's always best to check the official IMT Hyderabad website.

## Admission Process

Admission to IMT Hyderabad's PGDM program is entrance exam-based. Prospective students need to demonstrate strong academic performance and achieve competitive scores in accepted management entrance tests.

### Eligibility Criteria for PGDM
To be considered for the PGDM program, applicants must meet the following requirements:

*   A Bachelor's degree of at least three years duration in any discipline, with a minimum aggregate score of 50%.
*   Valid scores from one of the accepted entrance exams: CAT (2023), XAT (2024), CMAT (2024), or GMAT (scores from January 1, 2020, to February 28, 2024, or GMAT Focus Edition).
*   Students in their final year of a Bachelor's degree program are also eligible to apply, provided they submit all necessary completion documents and mark sheets upon graduation.

### Application Procedure
Interested candidates must complete the IMT Hyderabad Application Form. The application fee for admission in 2023 was Rs. 2500.

## Cutoffs

While specific cutoffs can vary each year based on the applicant pool and exam difficulty, IMT Hyderabad considers scores from CAT, XAT, CMAT, and GMAT for shortlisting candidates. A higher score in these exams generally increases the chances of admission. The institute does not publicly release exact cutoff percentages, but a strong performance is crucial for progressing in the admission process.

## Placements

IMT Hyderabad has a consistent track record of good placements for its PGDM graduates, attracting a mix of national and multinational companies across various sectors.

### Placement Highlights

The placement statistics for recent years demonstrate a positive trend in both the highest and average salary packages offered to students.

### Average Package Trends
The average package has shown steady growth over the past three years, indicating improving opportunities for graduates.

*   **2022:** INR 9.65 LPA
*   **2023:** INR 12 LPA
*   **2024:** INR 12.4 LPA

For the 2023 batch, the average packages for different performance tiers were also impressive:

*   Overall Average Package: INR 12 LPA
*   Average of Top 10 Offers: INR 20.20 LPA
*   Average of Top 20 Offers: INR 18.10 LPA
*   Average of Top 50 Offers: INR 15.75 LPA
*   Average of Top 100 Offers: INR 13.51 LPA

### Top Recruiters
IMT Hyderabad attracts a diverse set of companies for its placement drives. Some of the prominent recruiters in 2024 included leading names such as Deloitte, EY, ICICI Bank, SBI Bank, Accenture, HCL, and L&T, among others.

## Rankings and Accreditation

IMT Hyderabad holds a respectable position in national rankings and is recognized by various prestigious bodies:

*   **NIRF Ranking 2024:** Ranked 97th among Management Institutes.
*   **Accreditations:** AIU, NBA, and SAQS.
*   **Approvals:** Approved by AICTE.
*   **Memberships:** Member of AACSB and EFMD.
