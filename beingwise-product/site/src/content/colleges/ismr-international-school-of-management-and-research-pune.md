The International School of Management & Research (ISMR) in Pune offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs for students aiming for careers in management, business administration, and computer applications. This guide provides a clear overview of ISMR Pune's courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered at ISMR Pune

ISMR Pune provides full-time programs at both the undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) levels. The main courses available include:

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** A two-year postgraduate degree.
*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** A three-year undergraduate degree.
*   **Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA):** A three-year undergraduate degree.

The MBA program stands out with eight specializations to choose from, allowing students to focus on specific areas of interest:

*   Financial Management
*   Marketing Management
*   HR Management
*   Operations and Supply Chain Management
*   Business Analytics
*   Agribusiness Management
*   Tourism and Hospitality Management
*   International Business

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to ISMR Pune's programs is based on specific eligibility criteria and entrance exam scores. The application process is primarily conducted online.

### For MBA Admissions

Prospective MBA students must possess a valid score from any of the following national-level entrance examinations:

*   MAH-MBA CET
*   CMAT
*   CAT
*   MAT
*   XAT
*   ATMA
*   GMAT

Meeting the eligibility criteria and achieving a good score in one of these exams allows candidates to proceed to the selection process, which typically includes a personal interview.

### For BBA and BCA Admissions

Admission to the undergraduate BBA and BCA courses is determined by scores obtained in the MAH-BCA/BBA/BMS/BBM-CET-2024.

### How to Apply

Applications for ISMR Pune are currently open. Interested candidates can apply online by visiting the official ISMR Pune website and looking for the 'Apply Online' section.
