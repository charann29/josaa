Universal AI University in Mumbai is gaining recognition for its focus on modern education, especially in the fields of business and technology. This page offers a clear overview of its programs, costs, admissions, and career prospects to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

Universal AI University provides a range of postgraduate management programs designed to equip students with skills relevant to today's industries. The flagship offerings include a comprehensive Master of Business Administration (MBA) with various specializations, and an Executive MBA tailored for experienced professionals.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

This two-year full-time program offers a deep dive into business management, with specializations allowing students to focus on areas of interest. The curriculum is crafted to blend theoretical knowledge with practical application, preparing graduates for leadership roles.

### Executive MBA/PGDM

Designed for working professionals, the Executive MBA/PGDM program at Universal AI University aims to enhance existing skills and foster new ones without requiring a career break. It caters to individuals who already possess significant work experience and are looking to advance into higher management positions. The program structure integrates their professional background, making the learning experience highly relevant to their career trajectory.

## Fees and Scholarships

While specific fee structures for each program are best confirmed directly with the university, Universal AI University is committed to supporting students through various financial aid options. These include scholarships and potential loan facilities, aiming to make quality education accessible.

### Scholarship Opportunities

The university offers several scholarships, potentially covering up to 50% of tuition fees. These are categorized to support a diverse group of students:

*   **Early Bird Scholarship:** An award of INR 50,000 for students who secure admission in the early rounds, typically by December 31st of the admission year.
*   **Special Scholarship (INR 50,000):** Available for specific groups, including:
    *   Children of armed forces personnel.
    *   Students from certain Indian states and Union Territories like Arunachal Pradesh, Ladakh, Goa, and Tamil Nadu.
    *   National or International sports achievers.
    *   Students who have lost a parent due to Covid-19.
*   **Merit Scholarship:** Awarded based on academic performance in previous qualifications. The scholarship amount ranges from INR 50,000 to INR 1,50,000, depending on the percentage or grade achieved (e.g., 80-84% for INR 50,000, 95% and above for INR 1,50,000 for Indian boards; similar tiers for international boards).
*   **School Achievement Scholarship (INR 50,000):** For students with a strong recommendation from their school principal or counsellor.
*   **Means Scholarship (up to INR 1,00,000):** For families with an annual household income below INR 6,00,000.

**Important Scholarship Notes:**

*   Students can typically avail only one type of special scholarship.
*   Merit scholarships cannot be combined with special scholarships.
*   The maximum scholarship amount a student can receive is INR 2,00,000.
*   Scholarship applications must be submitted before May to be considered.
*   All scholarship decisions are at the discretion of Universal AI University.
