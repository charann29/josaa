XLRI Xavier School of Management, located in Jamshedpur, is a highly respected institution for management education in India. Established with a vision to develop leaders with a strong sense of social responsibility, XLRI offers a range of postgraduate management programs designed to equip students for the challenges of the corporate world. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered and Their Capacity

XLRI Jamshedpur provides several Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) programs. Each specialization has a specific number of seats, ensuring a focused learning environment. While these figures are based on official information, they are indicative and can be subject to revision.

| Course                                        | Seats |
| :-------------------------------------------- | :---- |
| PGDM in Business Management                   | 300   |
| PGDM in Human Resource Management             | 200   |
| PGDM in General Management                    | 120   |
| PGDM in Innovation, Entrepreneurship & Venture Creation | 30    |
| PGDM in Logistic and Supply Chain Management  | 30    |

## Admission Process and Key Dates

Admission to XLRI's PGDM programs primarily requires a valid XAT score. For the PGDM (General Management) program, GMAT scores are also accepted. The selection process extends beyond entrance exam scores, incorporating a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round. Factors like prior work experience and participation in extracurricular activities are also considered during the final selection.

Here's a typical timeline for PGDM admissions:

*   **Registration Opens:** July 15, 2023
*   **Registration Ends:** November 30, 2023
*   **Admit Card Download:** December 20, 2023
*   **XAT Exam Date:** January 7, 2024
*   **Shortlist for Interviews:** Third week of January
*   **Interviews:** Mid-February to Mid-March
*   **Offers and Waitlist:** April
*   **Session Commencement:** Mid-June

### Application Fee

For candidates applying to the PGDM (General Management) program via GMAT, the application fee is INR 2,500. This payment can be made online using a credit card, debit card, or net banking. It's crucial to complete the payment by the deadline to ensure your application is successfully submitted.

## Placement Highlights

XLRI boasts a strong track record of placements, with students consistently securing positions in leading organizations. The institute strives for excellent placement outcomes across its programs.

### PGDM (Business Management) and (Human Resource Management) Placements

Historically, the PGDM BM and HRM programs have achieved 100% placements. The latest reports indicate a robust placement season, with students receiving numerous domestic and international offers.

### PGDM (General Management) Placements

The PGDM (GM) program also demonstrates strong placement figures. For the Class of 2025, key statistics include:

*   **Highest Package:** INR 75 LPA
*   **Average Package:** INR 27.71 LPA
*   **Median Package:** INR 27 LPA
*   **Lowest Package:** INR 11.47 LPA
*   **Total Students:** 112
*   **Students Eligible for Placements:** 111
*   **Students Seeking Placement Through Institute:** 104
*   **Offers Accepted:** 102

## Why XLRI is a Premier Choice

XLRI, established in 1949, is a private institution consistently ranked among the top management schools in India. It holds prestigious accreditations from AACSB, AMBA, and NBA, and is approved by AICTE, AIU, and the Government of India. These accreditations signify the high quality and global recognition of its programs.

While XLRI's fees may seem substantial due to its private status and comprehensive offerings, the institute understands this concern. To support deserving students, XLRI provides various scholarships. These financial aid options can significantly help manage the cost of education, ensuring that financial constraints do not deter aspiring students from pursuing their management dreams at XLRI.
