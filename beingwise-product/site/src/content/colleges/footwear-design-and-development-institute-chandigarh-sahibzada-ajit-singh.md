The Footwear Design and Development Institute (FDDI) in Chandigarh, located in Banur, Sahibzada Ajit Singh Nagar, is a well-regarded institution offering specialized programs in design and business management. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fees, admission process, and placement opportunities.

## Courses Offered at FDDI Chandigarh

FDDI Chandigarh provides a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to prepare students for careers in the footwear, leather goods, retail, and fashion industries. The institute focuses on practical, industry-relevant education.

Here's a look at the main programs:

*   **Bachelor of Design (B.Des):** This undergraduate program is for students interested in the creative and technical aspects of design.
*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** A bachelor's degree focused on management principles, applicable across various industries.
*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** A postgraduate program for developing advanced business management and leadership skills.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to FDDI Chandigarh is primarily entrance-based, ensuring that students meet specific academic and aptitude criteria. The institute considers both its own entrance exam and scores from national-level tests.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission, candidates must meet the following basic requirements:

*   **For B.Des and BBA:** Applicants must have successfully completed Class 12 in any academic stream.
*   **For MBA:** Candidates need a bachelor's degree in any discipline. Provisional admission may be granted to those awaiting final results, provided they submit their degree by a specified deadline.

### Selection Process

The selection process involves an entrance examination followed by counselling. Here’s how it works:

*   **FDDI All India Selection Test (AIST):** This is the institute's internal entrance exam, a key component for admission to all programs.
*   **National Level Entrance Exams:** FDDI also accepts scores from several recognized national entrance tests. For undergraduate programs, UCEED scores are considered. For postgraduate MBA courses, scores from CUET-PG, MAT, or CAT are accepted.
*   **Counselling:** After the entrance exam, eligible candidates participate in a counselling session, which is the final step in the admission process.

### Application Procedure

All applications for admission to FDDI Chandigarh are processed online. Prospective students should visit the institute's official website to access the application form and submit necessary documents.

## Fee Structure

Specific fee details are generally updated annually by the institute. It is recommended for prospective students and their parents to check the official FDDI Chandigarh website or admission brochure for the most current and accurate fee information for their chosen program.

## Cutoff Information

Cutoff scores for admission to FDDI Chandigarh depend on various factors, including the number of applicants, their performance in the entrance exams, and the availability of seats. The institute considers scores from both its own AIST and accepted national-level exams. Meeting the cutoff is crucial for progressing to the counselling stage.

## Placements at FDDI Chandigarh

FDDI Chandigarh has a strong focus on industry relevance, which translates into a good placement record for its graduates. The institute aims to equip students with skills highly sought after in the footwear, leather goods, retail, and fashion sectors.

### Key Highlights:

*   **Industry Connections:** FDDI maintains robust ties with leading companies in relevant sectors.
*   **Recruiting Companies:** Top brands frequently visit the campus for recruitment drives. Some of the prominent companies that have hired FDDI graduates include Bata, Hide Sign, Da Milano, Carlton London, Home Centre, Puma, Shoppers Stop, and Tommy Hilfiger.
*   **Career Opportunities:** Graduates find opportunities in various roles, including footwear design, product development, merchandising, retail management, and marketing.
