IFMR Graduate School of Business (GSB), part of KREA University in Sricity, Andhra Pradesh, is a well-regarded institution for management education. It offers various programs designed to equip students with strong business acumen and practical skills.

## Programs Offered at IFMR GSB

IFMR GSB provides a range of business-focused programs. Their flagship offering is a two-year, full-time MBA, which covers several key specializations. For students looking for an undergraduate pathway into business, an Integrated MBA program is also available.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The MBA program at IFMR GSB is a comprehensive two-year course. Students can choose to specialize in areas critical to today's business world, including:

*   Finance
*   Marketing
*   Data Science
*   Operations
*   Organizational Behavior & Human Resources

### Integrated MBA

This program is designed for students who have completed their Class 12 and wish to pursue a combined undergraduate and postgraduate management degree.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Applying to IFMR GSB is a straightforward online process. The institution considers entrance exam scores and may also include interview rounds for certain programs.

### Application Steps

1.  Visit the official IFMR GSB website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Apply Now' button.
3.  Register by providing your name, email address, mobile number, and residential address.
4.  After registering, check your email for a verification link and click it to proceed.
5.  Complete the detailed online application form.
6.  Pay the application fee of INR 1,500 using a debit card, credit card, or net banking.

### Required Documents

When applying, ensure you have scanned copies of the following documents ready:

*   Class 10 mark sheet
*   Class 12 mark sheet
*   Graduation mark sheets (if applicable)
*   Leaving Certificate
*   Aadhaar Card
*   Passport-size photograph
*   Signature

### Eligibility Criteria

For the Integrated MBA program, candidates typically need to have completed Class 12 with a minimum aggregate score of 60%. Meeting this percentage, along with other subject and selection requirements, makes you eligible to apply.

## Fees Structure

The application fee for all programs at IFMR GSB is INR 1,500.

## Placement Highlights
