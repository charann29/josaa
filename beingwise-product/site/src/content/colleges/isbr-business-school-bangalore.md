ISBR Business School, located in Bangalore, is a well-regarded private institution for management education. This page offers a clear overview of its programs, fees, admissions process, cutoff scores, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs and Courses

ISBR Business School primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs. Their flagship offering is the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM).

### PGDM Program

The PGDM is a two-year full-time program designed to equip students with comprehensive business knowledge and practical skills. It is approved by AICTE and AIU, and accredited by the NBA, signifying its adherence to national educational standards.

## Admission Process

Admission to the PGDM program at ISBR Business School is a multi-stage process, evaluating candidates on various parameters.

### Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university. A valid score in one of the accepted management entrance exams is also mandatory.

### Accepted Entrance Exams and Cutoffs

ISBR Bangalore accepts scores from several national-level management entrance tests. For the PGDM program, candidates typically need a competitive score in:

*   **MAT**: A minimum score of 650 out of 800 is generally expected.
*   **CAT, XAT, GMAT, NMAT, CMAT**: Valid scores from these examinations are also accepted.

### Selection Rounds

Beyond the entrance exam score, candidates must perform well in the subsequent selection rounds, which include:

*   **Written Ability Test (WAT)**: To assess writing and analytical skills.
*   **Micro Presentation**: To evaluate presentation and communication abilities.
*   **Personal Interview (PI)**: To gauge personality, aptitude, and suitability for the program.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for the PGDM program at ISBR Business School covers tuition and other academic expenses. Specific details are usually provided during the application process or on the official website. It's advisable to check the latest fee schedule directly from the institution.

## Placements

ISBR Business School has a strong track record of placements, particularly for its PGDM program, making it an attractive option for students seeking good career opportunities.

## Campus and Infrastructure

ISBR Business School offers a modern and well-equipped campus designed to support a conducive learning environment. The facilities include:

*   **Academic Spaces**: Air-conditioned classrooms, computer labs, multimedia-enabled lecture halls, seminar halls, and boardrooms.
*   **Knowledge Center**: A comprehensive library and audio-video media room.
*   **Student Amenities**: Cafeterias, food courts, coffee lounges, and discussion rooms.
*   **Hostel Facilities**: Separate hostel accommodations for boys and girls with 24/7 security, Wi-Fi, power backup, housekeeping, laundry services, and recreational options like indoor games and a gym.
*   **Health and Wellness**: Sports and medical facilities.

## Reputation and Recognition

ISBR Business School is generally regarded as a Tier 2 business school. It holds approvals from AICTE, Ministry of HRD, Government of India, and AIU, along with NBA accreditation. Its strong industry connections and consistent recognition in various surveys contribute to its standing as a reputable private institution for management education in India.
