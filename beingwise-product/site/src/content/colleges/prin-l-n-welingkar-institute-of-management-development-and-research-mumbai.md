Prin. L. N. Welingkar Institute of Management Development and Research, often called WeSchool, is a well-regarded institution in Mumbai. It offers a range of postgraduate management programs designed to prepare students for the corporate world. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fees, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Courses Offered

WeSchool Mumbai provides diverse Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) programs, each tailored to specific industry needs. These include:

*   PGDM (Core)
*   PGDM E-Business
*   PGDM Business Design
*   PGDM Human Capital Management (HCM)
*   PGDM Retail Management
*   PGDM Rural Management
*   PGDM Media & Entertainment
*   PGDM Research and Business Analytics
*   Post Graduate Program in Foreign Trade Management (FTM)

Additionally, the institute offers an MMS (Master of Management Studies) program.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

To be considered for admission to WeSchool Mumbai's PGDM or MMS programs, candidates must have completed their graduation with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. The institute accepts scores from several national-level entrance examinations:

*   CAT
*   CMAT
*   MH-CET
*   MAT
*   ATMA
*   XAT
*   GMAT

Having a valid score in any of these tests is crucial for progressing to further admission rounds.

## Cutoff Information

Admission to WeSchool's PGDM programs is competitive. For the 2023 intake, the cutoff percentiles for various exams were:

*   **MAH CET:** 99.97 percentile (Round 1 for PGDM and FTM)
*   **CAT:** 85 percentile (for General All India category)
*   **CMAT:** 85 percentile
*   **ATMA:** 90 percentile

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for WeSchool Mumbai's programs varies by course. Prospective students should check the official website or admission brochure for the most current and detailed fee information for their chosen program.

## Campus and Facilities

WeSchool operates two campuses, one in Mumbai and another in Bengaluru. The Mumbai campus, located near Matunga, Central Railway, offers a broader range of programs. Both campuses boast modern infrastructure and amenities designed to support a holistic learning environment.

Key facilities include:

*   **Fully Wi-Fi enabled campus:** Ensuring seamless connectivity for students.
*   **Hostel accommodation:** Providing convenient living options.
*   **Recreational facilities:** Indoor and outdoor games, and a recreation center.
*   **Academic resources:** A well-stocked library, innovative labs, and an amphitheater.
*   **Corporate exposure:** Regular guest lectures, international guest sessions, and visiting faculty from India and abroad.
*   **Internship opportunities:** An 8-week summer internship program to gain practical experience.

The faculty at WeSchool Mumbai is known for being supportive and helpful, contributing significantly to the students' learning journey.
