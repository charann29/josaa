The Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT) in Delhi is a highly regarded institution for management education, especially known for its focus on international business. If you're considering a career in global trade or business analytics, IIFT Delhi offers a strong foundation and excellent opportunities.

## Courses Offered

IIFT Delhi primarily offers postgraduate programs designed to equip students for leadership roles in various sectors. The main courses available are:

*   **MBA in International Business (IB):** This flagship program is tailored for students aiming for careers in international trade, export-import management, and global marketing. It provides a comprehensive understanding of international business practices.
*   **MBA in Business Analytics (BA):** This program focuses on developing skills in data analysis, big data management, and strategic decision-making using analytical tools, preparing students for the data-driven business world.
*   **MA in Economics:** A postgraduate course for those interested in advanced economic theory and its applications, often leading to roles in research, policy analysis, or academia.

## Admission Process

Admission to IIFT Delhi's programs is competitive and merit-based, primarily relying on entrance exam scores.

### For MBA Programs (IB & BA)

*   **Entrance Exam:** For the 2024-2026 batch, IIFT Delhi is accepting scores from the Common Admission Test (CAT). In previous years, IIFT conducted its own entrance exam.
*   **Application:** Prospective students must apply online through the official IIFT website. The application window typically opens in October and closes by the second week of November, based on past trends.
*   **Selection Criteria:** Shortlisting is based on CAT scores. Selected candidates then usually proceed to further rounds, which may include Group Discussions, Written Ability Tests, and Personal Interviews.

### For MA Economics

*   **Entrance Exam:** Admission to the MA Economics program requires candidates to clear an institute-specific entrance examination, which includes both objective aptitude and descriptive components.
*   **Selection:** Candidates are shortlisted and selected based on their performance in this entrance exam, along with meeting the program's eligibility criteria.

## Fees Structure (Approximate Annual)

The fee structure for IIFT Delhi programs can vary slightly each year. Here's an approximate overview:

*   **MBA (International Business):** Around INR 10.85 Lakhs per year.
*   **MBA (Business Analytics):** Around INR 9.75 Lakhs per year.
*   **MA Economics:** Around INR 1.5 Lakhs per year.

*Note: These figures are indicative and subject to change. Always refer to the official IIFT Delhi website for the most current fee information.* 

## Rankings

IIFT Delhi consistently ranks among the top management institutions in India. In the NIRF 2024 rankings, it secured the **15th position** in the Management category, reflecting its strong academic standing and reputation.

## Cutoff

IIFT Delhi's cutoff for MBA programs is competitive, as it accepts CAT scores. While specific cutoff percentages are not officially released by IIFT, candidates generally need a high percentile in CAT to be shortlisted for further admission rounds. The exact cutoff can vary each year based on the difficulty of the exam and the applicant pool. It is advisable to aim for a very strong CAT score to increase your chances of admission.

IIFT Delhi stands out as an excellent choice for those seeking a specialized management education with a strong international focus or a robust foundation in business analytics. Its consistent rankings, strong placements, and dedicated curriculum make it a premier institution in India.
