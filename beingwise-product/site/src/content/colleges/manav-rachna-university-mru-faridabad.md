Manav Rachna University (MRU), located in Faridabad, is a well-regarded private institution offering a diverse range of academic programs. Recognized by the UGC and with many courses approved by AICTE, MRU focuses on providing quality education across various disciplines.

## About Manav Rachna University

MRU began its journey as Manav Rachna College of Engineering and has since grown into a comprehensive university. It houses multiple schools dedicated to fields like Law, Management, Technology, and Sciences, offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. The university is committed to student welfare, offering scholarship opportunities to support deserving candidates.

## Courses Offered at MRU

Manav Rachna University provides a wide array of programs designed to meet diverse academic and career aspirations. Some of the key offerings include:

*   **Undergraduate Programs:** B.Tech, BBA, B.Sc, BA-LLB, BBA-LLB, B.Com (Hons), BCA
*   **Postgraduate Programs:** M.Tech, MBA, MCA, LLM, M.Sc
*   **Doctoral Programs:** PhD across various disciplines

## Admission Process at MRU

Admissions at Manav Rachna University are based on a combination of merit and entrance exam scores. The application process is straightforward and typically involves the following steps:

1.  **Online Application:** Candidates must complete the online application form available on the university's official website. A non-refundable application fee is required.
2.  **Entrance Examination:** Depending on the program, eligible candidates may need to appear for the Manav Rachna National Aptitude Test (MRNAT). For certain courses, national-level entrance exams like JEE Main, CAT, MAT, CLAT, or SAT India are also accepted.
3.  **Selection and Counseling:** Based on eligibility criteria and entrance exam scores, program-wise merit lists are prepared. Shortlisted candidates are then invited for physical counseling sessions, where they need to bring all necessary documents and pay the initial fees.
4.  **Admission Confirmation:** Upon successful verification of documents and payment of fees, candidates receive an admission letter and a temporary identity card.

### Specifics for BBA Admission

For BBA programs, candidates must meet the minimum educational qualifications from a recognized board. Selection is primarily through either the MRNAT or the SAT India entrance exam. Additionally, candidates might be required to undertake a Pearson Test conducted at the university level. A final merit list is prepared considering all qualifying exam scores and academic records.

## MRU Rankings and Recognition

Manav Rachna University has consistently been recognized for its academic quality. For instance, in the NIRF 2025 rankings, the university was placed in the 201-300 band for its B.Tech programs. Its BBA program also received a notable ranking of 51 in 2025.

## Campus Life and Safety

MRU boasts a safe and welcoming campus environment for all students. Located centrally in Faridabad, it benefits from excellent connectivity via road and public transport. The campus is equipped with CCTV cameras to ensure student safety and security.
