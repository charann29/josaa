Tribhuvan Sahkari University in Anand is a well-regarded institution offering specialized programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, expected cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered and Fees

Tribhuvan Sahkari University primarily focuses on management education, with a flagship MBA program in Rural Management. This program is designed to equip students with skills relevant to the unique challenges and opportunities in rural sectors.

### Application Fees for MBA (Rural Management)

The application fee varies based on the candidate's category:

*   **General/GEN-EWS/OBC:** INR 2,000
*   **SC/ST/PwD:** INR 1,000

## Admission Process

Admission to Tribhuvan Sahkari University is a multi-stage process, emphasizing both academic performance and personal attributes. Direct admissions are not available; candidates must apply through the prescribed channels.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the MBA (Rural Management) program, applicants must hold a graduate degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks from a recognized university.

### Selection Procedure

The university's selection process is comprehensive and includes several components:

1.  **Entrance Exam Score:** Candidates must achieve a valid score in common management entrance exams such as CAT or XAT.
2.  **Written Ability Test (WAT):** Shortlisted candidates will participate in a Written Ability Test, assessing their communication and analytical skills.
3.  **Personal Interview (PI):** Following the WAT, candidates will undergo a Personal Interview to evaluate their suitability for the program and their overall personality.

### Weightage for Final Selection

The final selection for the MBA program is based on a weighted average of different components:

*   **CAT/XAT Percentiles:** 35%
*   **Personal Interviews (PI):** 35%
*   **Tribhuvan Sahkari University Written Ability Test:** 5%
*   **Academic Performance:** 5%

## Faculty and Campus Life

Tribhuvan Sahkari University boasts a dedicated faculty team, comprising 43 core members from diverse academic backgrounds. This rich pool of experience contributes significantly to the learning environment and student development.
