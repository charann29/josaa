Krupanidhi College of Management (KCM), located in Bangalore, is a well-regarded institution for those looking to pursue management studies. This page offers a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at KCM

KCM primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs. The flagship offering is the Master of Business Administration (MBA).

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The MBA program at Krupanidhi College of Management is affiliated with North Bangalore University, approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and recognized by the Government of Karnataka. It is designed to equip students with comprehensive business knowledge and leadership skills.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

Admission to KCM's management programs requires meeting specific academic criteria and performing well in entrance examinations.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the MBA program, candidates must:

*   Hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   Have secured a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in their undergraduate studies.
*   Applicants who are in their final year of graduation are also welcome to apply.

### Admission Steps

The admission process typically involves:

1.  **Application:** Submitting the application form along with required documents.
2.  **Entrance Exam:** Qualifying in national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, ATMA, or Karnataka PGCET.
3.  **Selection Rounds:** Candidates may need to participate in group discussions and personal interviews.

## Cutoff Information

While KCM considers scores from various national entrance exams, specific cutoffs can vary each year based on the applicant pool and program demand. Historically, a CAT percentile of around 76 has been considered for admission, though the highest cutoffs can go up to 90 percentile for certain profiles or specializations.

## Fee Structure

Exact fee details for the academic year are best confirmed directly with the college. However, it's important to budget for both tuition and hostel expenses if you plan to reside on campus.

### Hostel Fees (Per Semester)

KCM offers separate hostel facilities for male and female students, with options for both air-conditioned (AC) and non-air-conditioned rooms. The fees vary accordingly:

*   **AC Rooms:** Approximately INR 52,000 per semester.
*   **Non-AC Rooms:** Approximately INR 40,000 per semester.

## Campus Life and Facilities

Krupanidhi College of Management aims to provide a conducive and enriching environment for its students. The campus is well-maintained, offering various amenities to support academic and personal growth.

### Key Facilities:

*   **Hostels:** Separate, well-maintained hostels for boys and girls with AC and non-AC room options. Student reviews often highlight the excellent condition of the hostels and quality of food in the mess.
*   **Cafeteria:** A well-regarded cafeteria providing good food options.
*   **Libraries:** Each academic department has its own library, supplementing the central library resources.
*   **Overall Environment:** The college strives to offer a balanced academic and social life, with a focus on student well-being and a positive learning atmosphere.
