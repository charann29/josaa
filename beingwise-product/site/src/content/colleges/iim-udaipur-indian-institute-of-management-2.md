IIM Udaipur, nestled in the scenic city of Udaipur, is a prominent institution for management education in India. If you're considering a future in business and leadership, understanding its programs, admission process, and career prospects is key.

## IIM Udaipur: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

This guide provides a clear overview to help prospective students and their families make informed decisions about pursuing an education at IIM Udaipur.

### Programs and Eligibility

IIM Udaipur offers a range of postgraduate and doctoral programs designed to cater to diverse career aspirations. Here's a look at some of its popular offerings and their general admission requirements:

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** Candidates need a Bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks from a recognized university and a valid CAT score.
*   **Certificate Programs:** These typically require a Class 12 qualification or equivalent, along with a Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% marks. Applicants must also provide scores from CAT, GMAT, or GRE.
*   **Executive MBA/PGDM:** This program is for experienced professionals. Eligibility includes a graduation in any stream with at least 50% marks or an equivalent CGPA, plus a minimum of three years of post-qualification work experience. A valid CAT score is also required.
*   **PhD:** Aspiring researchers need a Master's degree in any discipline with at least 55% marks, and a Bachelor's degree (minimum three years) with at least 50% marks. Alternatively, a completed professional qualification like CA, ICWA, or CS with a Bachelor's degree is accepted. Applicants must also present scores from CAT, UGC NET, or CSIR NET.

### The Admission Journey

Admission to IIM Udaipur is primarily entrance-based, followed by a rigorous selection process. While specific criteria vary by program, the general steps involve:

1.  **Entrance Exam:** Appearing for and securing a valid score in the relevant entrance examination (e.g., CAT for MBA, CAT/GMAT/GRE for Certificate programs).
2.  **Meeting Eligibility:** Ensuring all minimum educational qualifications are met.
3.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are shortlisted based on their entrance exam scores and academic profile.
4.  **Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates participate in an online Personal Interview round.
5.  **Final Merit List:** The final selection is based on a composite score derived from the entrance exam score, PI performance, and the candidate's overall profile.

### MBA Selection Process at IIM Udaipur

For the flagship MBA program, IIM Udaipur considers a combination of factors. After meeting the basic eligibility and achieving a valid CAT score, candidates are invited for a Personal Interview. The final merit list is compiled using scores from the CAT, the Personal Interview, and an assessment of the candidate's academic and professional profile.

### Application for Executive MBA

To apply for the Executive MBA at IIM Udaipur, candidates must submit scores from CAT, GMAT, GRE, or the IIMU Test. After clearing the entrance exam, the application process involves:

1.  **Online Application:** Filling out the application form on the institute’s official website.
2.  **Document Submission:** Uploading all necessary documents.
3.  **Personal Interview:** Attending the interview round.
4.  **Admission Offer:** Upon receiving an offer, submitting the admission fee to secure the spot.

### CAT Cutoff Trends

Historically, the CAT percentile cutoff for admission to IIM Udaipur has been around 92. While the official cutoff for the upcoming year will be released after the CAT results, this figure provides a good benchmark for aspiring candidates.

### Placements at IIM Udaipur

IIM Udaipur has a strong track record of placements. In the 2025 placement cycle, all 302 students participating from the MBA program received offers. Similarly, for the MBA in Global Supply Chain Management (GSCM) and MBA in Digital Enterprise Management (DEM) programs, all 48 and 53 students, respectively, who sought placement through the institute secured positions in 2025.

This demonstrates IIM Udaipur's commitment to ensuring its graduates are well-prepared for successful careers in their chosen fields.
