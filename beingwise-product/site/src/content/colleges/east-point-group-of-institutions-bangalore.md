East Point Group of Institutions (EPGI) in Bangalore is a well-regarded educational hub offering a diverse range of programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structures, admission procedures, cutoff criteria, and placement opportunities to assist prospective students and their families.

## Programs Offered

EPGI provides undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma programs across various fields. The institution is known for its strong offerings in Engineering, Health Sciences, and other academic streams. Some of its flagship programs include Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) and Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.).

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to East Point Group of Institutions can be secured either through entrance examinations or on a merit basis, depending on the specific course. Generally, candidates need to have achieved at least 50% marks in their 10+2 (Higher Secondary) examinations to be eligible for most programs.

### B.Tech Admissions

For the Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program, applicants must have completed their 10+2 with a minimum aggregate of 45% marks. Selection is primarily based on scores from entrance exams such as KCET or COMED-K UGET. Other prominent entrance exams considered for various programs include GATE and JEE Main.

### BCA Admissions

Admission to the three-year Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) course at EPGI is entirely merit-based. Candidates need to have passed Class 12 in any discipline. Selection is determined by the marks obtained in Class 12. Prospective students must fill out an online application form available on the official website.

### Direct Admissions

Yes, East Point Group of Institutions does offer direct admissions for certain courses. Interested candidates should apply promptly to ensure a smooth and timely admission process.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure varies by program. For instance, the total fee for the entire four-year B.Tech program at East Point Group of Institutions is approximately INR 1,057,488. The application fee for the B.Tech program is not explicitly stated on their website.

## Campus and Facilities

Established in 1997, East Point Group of Institutions has grown significantly, thanks to its experienced faculty and comprehensive infrastructure. The institution provides value-added programs under its “PROFESSIONAL TRACK” initiatives, which are designed to align with current market demands and emerging technological advancements. Students have rated the faculty highly, with an average rating of 4.1, indicating a qualified and experienced teaching staff.

EPGI also offers scholarships to deserving students, providing financial assistance to support their educational journey. The institution is led by Dr. S.M. Venkatpathi, its Founder Chairman, and Mrs. B.L. Ramadevi Venkatpathi, the Chairperson.
