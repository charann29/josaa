Sister Nivedita University (SNU) in Kolkata is a prominent institution offering a wide array of academic programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, entrance exam cutoffs, and placement opportunities, designed to help you make an informed decision.

## Courses and Eligibility at SNU

SNU offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. Each course has specific eligibility criteria that prospective students must meet, typically involving academic qualifications and performance in relevant entrance examinations.

For most undergraduate programs, candidates need to have completed their 10+2 education from a recognized board, often with a minimum aggregate percentage, especially for science and technical streams. Postgraduate courses usually require a relevant bachelor's degree.

### B.Tech Programs

One of the popular offerings at SNU is its Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program. For specializations like Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), the eligibility generally requires:

*   A Science stream in Class 12.
*   A minimum aggregate of 60% in Class 12.

Admission to B.Tech programs primarily considers scores from entrance exams like WBJEE or the university's own SNUET.

## Admission Process and Fees

Admissions at Sister Nivedita University are currently open. Interested candidates can apply online through the university's official website. The application process involves registering and submitting the application form along with a fee of INR 1,000.

### Entrance Exams Accepted

SNU accepts scores from a variety of entrance tests for admission to its various programs. While the university conducts its own SNUET (Sister Nivedita University Entrance Test) for both undergraduate and postgraduate courses, it also recognizes several national and state-level examinations. These include:

*   **For Engineering:** JEE Main, WBJEE
*   **For Management:** CAT, MAT, JEMAT
*   **General Admissions:** SNUET, GATE (for specific postgraduate programs)

Meeting the basic eligibility criteria for your chosen course and performing well in the prescribed entrance exam are crucial steps to secure admission.

## Placement Opportunities

Sister Nivedita University has a strong track record in placements, demonstrating excellent outcomes for its graduates. In the academic year 2022-2023, the university reported an impressive 97% placement rate.

### Placement Highlights (2023)

The key statistics from SNU's 2023 placement drive are as follows:

| Particulars        | Placement Statistics (2023) |
| :----------------- | :-------------------------- |
| Highest Package    | INR 51 LPA                  |
| Average Package    | INR 6.50 LPA                |
| Placement Rate     | 97%                         |
| Total Recruiters   | 270+                        |

Top companies that actively recruited from SNU include Amazon, Deloitte, EY, Infosys, and Wipro, among many others.

### Median Salaries (2024)

According to the NIRF report for 2025, the median salaries offered to students in specific programs are:

*   **B.Pharma:** INR 2.86 LPA
*   **LLB:** INR 3.60 LPA
*   **BA LLB / BBA LLB / B.Com LLB:** INR 3.60 LPA

## Campus Facilities

Sister Nivedita University provides a modern and well-equipped campus environment to support student learning and well-being. The facilities are designed to cater to academic, residential, and recreational needs.

*   **Medical Centre:** The university has a dedicated medical center staffed with physicians, pharmacists, and emergency medical officers, ensuring round-the-clock medical care for students.
*   **Library:** A comprehensive library offers access to a vast collection of books, journals, magazines, and e-resources from global publishers, available both in print and electronic formats across the campus.
*   **Hostels:** Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls are available, featuring 24-hour security and professional housekeeping and maintenance services.
*   **Other Facilities:** The campus also includes well-equipped laboratories, an auditorium, and a cafeteria, contributing to a holistic student experience.
