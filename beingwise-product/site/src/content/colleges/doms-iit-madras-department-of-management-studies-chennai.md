The Department of Management Studies (DoMS) at IIT Madras is a well-regarded institution in Chennai, offering a range of management programs. It provides a strong academic foundation, preparing students for successful careers in various industries.

## Programs Offered at DoMS IIT Madras

DoMS IIT Madras provides several management programs designed for different career stages and aspirations. These include a flagship MBA program, an Executive MBA for working professionals, and a robust PhD program for those interested in research and academia.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The full-time MBA program at DoMS IIT Madras is a two-year course focusing on a comprehensive understanding of business management. It aims to develop future leaders with strong analytical and decision-making skills.

### Executive MBA (EMBA)

For experienced professionals looking to enhance their management skills without interrupting their careers, the Executive MBA program is an ideal choice. This program is structured to accommodate working executives, offering a blend of rigorous academics and practical application.

### Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)

The PhD program at DoMS IIT Madras is designed for individuals passionate about contributing to management research and theory. It involves in-depth study and original research in various management disciplines, guided by experienced faculty.

## Admission Process and Key Dates

Admission to DoMS IIT Madras programs is highly competitive, based on academic merit, entrance exam scores, and personal interviews. Understanding the application timeline and requirements is crucial for prospective students.

### MBA Admissions

The application window for the MBA program typically opens after the Common Admission Test (CAT) results are announced. This usually happens in December and continues until the end of January. The selection process, which includes interviews, generally begins in March.

### PhD Admissions

Admission to the PhD program involves a thorough evaluation of academic performance, the quality of the Statement of Purpose (SOP) and research proposal. Shortlisted candidates are invited for an interview, which may also include a Research Aptitude Test.

### EMBA Admissions

Candidates for the Executive MBA program are typically assessed based on their bachelor's degree performance and relevant work experience.

## Eligibility Criteria

Meeting the specific eligibility requirements is essential before applying to any program at DoMS IIT Madras.

### MBA Eligibility

Applicants must possess a bachelor's degree with a good academic record and a valid CAT score.

### EMBA Eligibility

To be eligible for the EMBA, candidates generally need a bachelor's degree with at least 60% marks and a minimum of three years of work experience as of December 31st of the admission year.

### PhD Eligibility

PhD applicants usually need a master's degree or a five-year dual degree. For part-time and external candidates, five years of managerial experience is also required. Additionally, candidates must have qualified in national-level exams such as GATE, CAT, UGC-JRF, UGC NET, CSIR-JRF, or the Research Aptitude Test, along with an SOP and a personal interview.

### International Exposure

DoMS IIT Madras actively promotes international exposure for its students. Many scholars have secured summer internships and research fellowships at prestigious international universities and research institutes, including Nanyang Technological University (Singapore), University of Tokyo (Japan), and the National Institute of Informatics (Japan). They have also received renowned scholarships like the Deutscher Akademischer Akademischer Austausch Dienst (DAAD) and Endeavour Scholarship for exchange programs.

## Faculty at DoMS IIT Madras

DoMS IIT Madras boasts a dedicated faculty team of 25 members, specializing in diverse areas such as Integrative Management, Marketing, Information Systems, Finance, and Operations. These experienced educators bring a wealth of academic and industry knowledge to the classroom, guiding students through their learning journey.
