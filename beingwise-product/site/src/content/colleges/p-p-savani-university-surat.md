P. P. Savani University, located in Surat, Gujarat, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of academic programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities, designed to help students and parents make informed decisions.

## P. P. Savani University: An Overview

Established with a vision to provide quality education, P. P. Savani University is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and holds an 'A+' grade accreditation from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The university operates through nine distinct schools of study, catering to diverse academic interests from undergraduate to doctoral levels.

### Schools of Study

The university's academic structure is organized into nine specialized schools:

*   School of Sciences
*   School of Engineering
*   School of Agriculture
*   School of Homeopathy
*   School of Liberal Arts & Management Studies
*   School of Nursing
*   School of Physiotherapy
*   School of Universal School of Design
*   School of Architecture & Planning

All programs offered under these schools are UGC-approved, ensuring their academic validity and recognition.

## Admission Process

Admission to P. P. Savani University programs is based on a mix of merit and entrance examinations, depending on the chosen course. Here's a general outline:

*   **Eligibility:** Candidates must meet the specific academic requirements for their desired program, typically involving a minimum percentage in their previous qualifying examination (e.g., Class 12 for UG courses).
*   **Application:** Prospective students need to complete the application process and submit all necessary documents.
*   **Selection:** Admission depends on factors like academic performance, performance in any required entrance exams, and the overall competition for seats. Even students with average academic scores have a chance of admission if they meet the basic eligibility criteria.

## Cutoff Information

While specific cutoff ranks can vary each year based on applicant pool and program demand, the university generally sets clear minimum eligibility percentages for admission. ## Campus Facilities

P. P. Savani University boasts a modern, expansive campus designed to provide a conducive learning and living environment. Key facilities include:

*   **Campus:** A sprawling 100-acre green and secure campus.
*   **Connectivity:** 24x7 Wi-Fi access across the campus.
*   **Food Services:** Multiple options including a dedicated cafeteria and canteen offering healthy, home-like meals.
*   **Health & Wellness:** An in-campus hospital, ambulance services, and an indoor gym.
*   **Sports:** International standard sports facilities, including a jogging track, cricket ground, football turf, and volleyball courts.
*   **Accommodation:** Both AC and non-AC hostel facilities for boys and girls, equipped with essential amenities like mess services, water purifiers, and electricity. The hostels are secured with CCTV surveillance. Additionally, rental and paying guest options are available nearby.
*   **Transport:** Dedicated transport facilities for students and staff.

These facilities contribute to a holistic student experience, supporting academic pursuits and personal development.
