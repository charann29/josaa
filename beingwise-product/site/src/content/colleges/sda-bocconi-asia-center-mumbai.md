SDA Bocconi Asia Center in Mumbai offers a unique blend of international management education with a focus on the Asian market. It provides a global learning environment for students aspiring to excel in various business domains. This page offers a comprehensive overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, selection criteria, and career opportunities.

## Programs Offered and Fees

SDA Bocconi Asia Center primarily offers postgraduate management programs designed to equip students with essential business acumen and leadership skills. Their flagship program is the International Master in Business (IMB).

While specific fee details can vary by academic year, prospective students should anticipate fees that reflect the international standard of education provided. It's always best to check the official website for the most current fee structure for your chosen program.

## Admission Process and Key Dates

Admission to SDA Bocconi Asia Center is highly competitive and does not involve direct entry. Candidates must go through a structured application and selection process. Here are the crucial aspects and deadlines for the International Master in Business (IMB) program:

### Application Deadlines for IMB Program 2025

| Round | Submission Deadline |
| :---- | :------------------ |
| 1     | November 20, 2024   |
| 2     | January 21, 2025    |
| 3     | March 21, 2025      |
| 4     | To Be Determined    |

To apply, candidates need to submit an online application along with valid scores from accepted entrance exams. The admissions committee reviews applications in rounds, so applying early can be beneficial.

## Entrance Exams and Selection Criteria

SDA Bocconi Asia Center accepts scores from several well-known management entrance examinations. These include:

*   **CAT**
*   **GMAT**
*   **NMAT**
*   **GRE**

In addition to these, SDA Bocconi also conducts its own **Bocconi Test**. This online test is mandatory for applicants who do not have a valid score from any of the above exams, or if their submitted score does not meet the admissions committee's expectations. The Bocconi Test helps the institution evaluate a candidate's aptitude and suitability for their programs, including the IMB and Executive International Master in Business (IEMB) courses.
