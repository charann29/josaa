Thiagarajar School of Management (TSM) in Madurai is a respected institution known for its management programs. This page offers a comprehensive overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities to help prospective students and their families make informed decisions.

## Programs Offered

TSM Madurai primarily offers a two-year Master of Business Administration (MBA) program, which is its flagship offering. Beyond the MBA, the institution also provides Post Graduate Diploma, Certificate, and PhD courses.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to TSM Madurai is primarily based on entrance exam scores, followed by a rigorous selection process. The institute is currently accepting applications for its 2025 intake.

To be eligible for the MBA/PGDM program, candidates must hold a graduate degree in any discipline with a minimum aggregate of 60%.

### Selection Criteria for MBA/PGDM

Candidates must submit scores from one of the following national-level management entrance exams:

*   CAT
*   XAT
*   MAT
*   CMAT
*   GMAT
*   ATMA
*   TANCET (for MBA)

Following the entrance exam, the selection process includes a Group Discussion (GD), Personal Interview (PI), and a Written Ability Test (WAT). Additionally, the institution considers academic performance, relevant work experience, and participation in sports, extra-curricular, or co-curricular activities.

### Weightage for MBA Selection

The final selection for the MBA program is determined by a composite score, with different components carrying specific weightages:

| Selection Criteria                       | Weightage (%) |
| :--------------------------------------- | :------------ |
| CAT/MAT/XAT/CMAT/GMAT Entrance Score     | 35            |
| Academics                                | 20            |
| Personal Interview (PI)                  | 20            |
| Group Discussion (GD)                    | 10            |
| Written Ability Test (WAT)               | 5             |
| Extra/Co-Curricular Activities           | 5             |
| Work Experience                          | 5             |
| **Total**                                | **100**       |

### Application Procedure

The application process for TSM Madurai is conducted entirely online. Interested candidates should visit the official website to complete their application.

## Seat Intake

The MBA program at Thiagarajar School of Management has a total intake capacity of 120 students.

## Placements

TSM Madurai has a strong record of placements for its MBA and PGDM graduates. The institution consistently achieves a high placement rate, attracting reputable companies from various sectors. The Class of 2024 saw impressive outcomes:

*   **Placement Rate:** 96%
*   **Highest International Package:** INR 21.95 LPA
*   **Highest Domestic Package:** INR 16.22 LPA
*   **Average Package:** INR 8.41 LPA
*   **Median Package:** INR 7.5 LPA
*   **Top Domain:** Marketing (with the highest package of INR 21.95 LPA)

Leading companies such as ICICI Bank, TCS, and Colgate are among the regular recruiters visiting the campus for placements. Many students also secure internships through the college's network.

## Campus Environment

TSM Madurai is known for its serene and green campus environment, which fosters a peaceful atmosphere conducive to learning. The institution is dedicated to developing individuals who are not only technically proficient and managerially competent but also ethically sensitive, preparing them for successful careers and responsible leadership.
