Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), with its main campus at Takshashila in Indore, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide range of academic programs. For students and parents considering higher education options, understanding its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities is key. This guide aims to provide a clear overview to help you make an informed choice.

## Programs Offered and Total Seats

DAVV Takshashila Campus provides numerous undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. These courses are recognized by prominent government bodies like AICTE and PCI, ensuring their quality and relevance. The university has a substantial capacity, with a combined total of **5185 seats** available across all its approved programs.

For specific programs like MBA/PGDM, the campus offers **60 seats**.

## Admission Process

Admission to Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya is primarily merit-based and often requires candidates to sit for entrance examinations. The university does not offer direct admissions; instead, it follows a structured process to ensure fairness and academic standards.

### Common University Entrance Test (CUET)

For most undergraduate and postgraduate programs, admission is determined by scores obtained in the Common University Entrance Test (CUET), conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). Successfully clearing the CUET is a crucial step towards securing a provisional seat.

### Other Admission Pathways

Beyond CUET, DAVV also provides other routes for admission:

*   **Type 1: Through CUET** scores.
*   **Type 2: Through Department-level Tests** for specific programs.
*   **Type 3: Through Merit** in the qualifying examination.

### Application Details

The application fee for all types of admissions is **INR 1,550**.

## Fees Structure

While specific course fees can vary, the application fee for all admission types at Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya is **INR 1,550**. It's important to note that this application fee is generally non-refundable once paid.

## Cutoff Information

After the application process concludes and entrance exam results (like CUET) are available, the university releases cutoff lists. Candidates whose scores meet or exceed these cutoffs are then eligible to proceed with the subsequent stages of the admission process, which may include counselling or document verification.

## Placements

Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya has a commendable placement record, particularly for its professional courses. The campus strives to connect students with leading companies, offering promising career opportunities.

### MCA Placements

For students pursuing the Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program, the placement statistics are encouraging:

*   **Highest Package:** INR 12-15 lakhs per annum
*   **Average Package:** INR 4-6 lakhs per annum

The overall placement rate for the institution is generally around **80%**, though this can fluctuate slightly each year based on market conditions and student performance.

### Top Recruiting Companies

Some of the prominent companies that recruit from DAVV Takshashila Campus include:

*   Infosys
*   Wipro
*   TCS
*   HCL
*   IBM
*   Accenture

This robust list of recruiters reflects the university's strong industry connections and the quality of its graduates.

Choosing a university is a significant decision. DAVV Takshashila Campus offers a wide array of programs, a structured admission process, and a strong track record in placements, making it a viable option for many aspiring students.
