Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) is a renowned institution in Manipal, Karnataka, offering a vast array of courses. As an independent admissions guidance brand, BeingWise aims to provide clear, helpful insights into MAHE's offerings, admission process, fees, and career prospects.

This page summarises key information to help students and parents make informed decisions about pursuing higher education at MAHE.

## About Manipal Academy of Higher Education

MAHE is a private university that has earned the esteemed status of an 'Institution of Excellence' from the Government of India. It holds an A+ accreditation from NAAC and is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC). Beyond its main campus in Manipal, MAHE also has off-campuses in Mangaluru, Bengaluru, and Jamshedpur, along with an international campus in Dubai.

## Courses Offered at MAHE

MAHE provides over 300 academic programs across various disciplines, catering to diverse student interests and career aspirations. These include:

*   **Undergraduate Programs:** Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech), Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm), Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS), Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com), and many more.
*   **Postgraduate Programs:** Master of Arts (MA), Master of Technology (M.Tech), Master of Business Administration (MBA), and various other specialisations.
*   **Doctoral Programs:** Ph.D. opportunities in a wide range of fields.

## Admission Process

Admission to most programs at MAHE is primarily entrance-based. The main entrance examination is the Manipal Entrance Test (MET). After qualifying in MET, candidates often proceed to further selection rounds, which may include group discussions or personal interviews, depending on the specific course.

For certain courses, admissions might be based on merit from the last qualifying examination or through department-specific tests. For professional courses like MBBS, national-level entrance exams such as NEET UG are mandatory.

## Cutoff Information

MAHE releases cutoffs for its various programs based on entrance exam scores, such as MET or NEET UG. These cutoffs vary by course, category, and domicile (All India vs. Home State). For instance, in a recent year, the NEET UG cutoff for the General All India category for MBBS admission had a closing rank around 5766.

Students are advised to check the official MAHE admission portal for detailed and up-to-date cutoff information for their desired program and category.

## Placements at MAHE

Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT), a constituent unit of MAHE, has a commendable track record for student placements. The university consistently attracts a large number of recruiters, providing excellent career opportunities for its graduates. 

Here's a glimpse into recent placement highlights for the Class of 2025 at MIT Manipal:

*   **Highest International Package:** INR 2.19 Crore per annum
*   **Highest Domestic Package:** INR 57 LPA
*   **Average Package:** INR 12.31 LPA
*   **Median Package:** INR 10.05 LPA
*   **Total Recruiters:** Over 287
*   **Placement Rate:** 77.4%

These statistics demonstrate MAHE's commitment to preparing students for successful careers and its strong connections with the industry.

## Campus Life and Infrastructure

MAHE boasts a modern and well-equipped campus designed to support a holistic student experience. Key facilities include:

*   Air-conditioned classrooms and state-of-the-art laboratories.
*   A comprehensive library with extensive resources.
*   Auditoriums for events and seminars.
*   High-speed internet connectivity across the campus.
*   Dedicated sports facilities.
*   Separate hostel accommodation for boys and girls.

MAHE is committed to student safety, with CCTV surveillance throughout the campus and a strong anti-ragging policy enforced by an Anti-Ragging Cell. First-year B.Com students are required to stay in campus hostels, with exceptions only granted through a formal application process approved by parents and university authorities.

## Faculty at MAHE

MAHE prides itself on its highly qualified faculty, with a significant number holding PhDs and Master's degrees. The university fosters an environment of academic excellence by inviting guest lecturers and providing students with opportunities to interact with leading personalities in various fields. Currently, there are over 200 faculty members, many of whom are doctorates, ensuring quality education and mentorship.

MAHE stands out as a top choice for higher education in India, offering a blend of academic rigor, robust infrastructure, and promising career opportunities.
