The Xavier Institute of Management and Entrepreneurship (XIME) in Bangalore is a well-regarded institution for management education. If you're considering XIME for your postgraduate studies, this guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff scores, and placement records.

## Programs Offered at XIME Bangalore
XIME Bangalore primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs, designed to equip students with essential business skills and leadership qualities. The institute offers two key programs:

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** This is a flagship program covering various aspects of business administration.
*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management in Business Analytics (PGDM BA):** This specialized program focuses on data-driven decision-making and analytical tools relevant to modern business.

For those interested in advanced research, XIME also has a Research Center that offers a Ph.D. program, affiliated with the University of Mysore.

### Seat Intake

*   **PGDM:** 180 students
*   **PGDM Business Analytics:** 60 students

## Admission Process and Eligibility
Admission to XIME's PGDM programs is entirely entrance-exam based, ensuring a merit-driven selection process. There is no provision for direct admission.

### Eligibility Criteria for PGDM Programs

*   Applicants must hold a Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 50% is required in the Bachelor's degree (45% for candidates from SC/ST categories).
*   Candidates who are in their final year of graduation and awaiting results are also eligible to apply.

### Selection Process

1.  **Entrance Exam Scores:** Candidates are shortlisted based on valid scores obtained in national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, ATMA, CMAT, or GMAT.
2.  **Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates proceed to the GD and PI rounds.
3.  **Document Verification & Fee Payment:** Successful candidates in the GD and PI rounds are then invited for document verification and to complete the fee payment to secure their admission.

### Ph.D. Admission
Admission to the Ph.D. program at XIME Research Center follows the rules set by the University of Mysore. Eligibility typically requires a Master's degree, Diploma, or M.Phil. with at least 55% aggregate (50% for SC/ST). Selection considers UGC NET/CSIR NET/SLET/GATE scores, academic performance, and work experience.

XIME Bangalore has a strong track record of placements for its PGDM and PGDM (Business Analytics) programs, attracting a diverse range of recruiters.
