# Footwear Design and Development Institute, Chhindwara: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

The Footwear Design and Development Institute (FDDI) in Chhindwara is a dedicated institution focusing on the footwear and fashion industries. If you're considering a career in these creative fields, understanding its programs, admission process, and career prospects is essential.

## Programs Offered

FDDI Chhindwara provides a range of full-time degree programs at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels, designed to equip students with specialized skills for the industry. The main offerings include:

*   **Undergraduate (B.Des):** This four-year Bachelor of Design program offers two distinct specializations:
    *   Footwear Design & Production
    *   Fashion Design
*   **Postgraduate (MBA):** This two-year Master of Business Administration focuses on:
    *   Retail & Fashion Merchandise

## Admission Process

Securing a spot at FDDI Chhindwara involves a structured admission process. Here's a general overview of what prospective students can expect:

1.  **Online Application:** The first step is to apply through the official institute website. You'll need to complete the application form and pay the required application fee.
2.  **Entrance Examination:** Registered candidates are required to take the All India Selection Test (AIST) conducted by the institute. This test assesses your aptitude for the chosen field.
3.  **Selection and Confirmation:** Candidates who perform well in the AIST will be shortlisted. To finalize admission and secure your place, you'll need to pay the course fees within the stipulated timeframe.
