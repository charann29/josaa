Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna, established in 2008, has quickly become a prominent institution for engineering and technology education. Located in Bihar's capital city, it offers a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. This guide provides a clear overview of the courses, fee structures, admission processes, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities at IIT Patna.

## Courses Offered

IIT Patna provides a diverse array of programs across various disciplines:

*   **Undergraduate (B.Tech & B.S.):** These four-year programs cover core engineering fields.
*   **Postgraduate (M.Tech & M.Sc):** Two-year master's programs specializing in advanced topics.
*   **Doctoral (Ph.D.):** Research-focused programs for in-depth study and innovation.

## Fee Structure

The fees at IIT Patna vary by course and category. Here's an approximate range for tuition fees:

*   **B.Sc. Courses:** Tuition fees typically range from INR 2.4 Lakhs to INR 10 Lakhs. It's important to note that this is a tuition fee estimate, and other charges like hostel fees, caution deposits, examination fees, and institutional fees will also apply. For a detailed breakdown, candidates should refer to the official IIT Patna website.

## Admission Process

Admission to IIT Patna is highly competitive and strictly based on merit through national-level entrance examinations. Direct admission is not available for any course.

### Undergraduate Admissions (B.Tech & B.S.)

Admission to B.Tech and B.S. programs is determined by performance in the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main and JEE Advanced. Successful candidates then participate in the JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority) counseling process for seat allocation.

### Postgraduate Admissions (M.Tech & M.Sc)

*   **M.Tech:** Admission to M.Tech programs requires a valid GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) score, followed by counseling through COAP (Common Offer Acceptance Portal).
*   **M.Sc:** Admission to M.Sc programs is typically through IIT JAM (Joint Admission Test for M.Sc.), followed by CCMN (Centralized Counselling for M.Sc./M.Sc. (Tech.)) counseling.

### Doctoral Admissions (Ph.D.)

For Ph.D. programs, candidates must appear for an admission test conducted by IIT Patna. Following the test, a merit list of selected students is published.
