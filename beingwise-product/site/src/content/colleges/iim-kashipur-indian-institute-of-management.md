The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Kashipur, nestled in the serene foothills of Uttarakhand, is a prominent institution offering world-class management education. Established with a vision to nurture future business leaders, IIM Kashipur provides a stimulating academic environment and a vibrant campus life.

## IIM Kashipur: Courses Offered

IIM Kashipur primarily focuses on its flagship two-year MBA program, which is highly regarded for its comprehensive curriculum and industry relevance. This program is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in various management roles. The institute also offers specialized programs catering to specific career aspirations.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to IIM Kashipur's MBA program is highly competitive and does not offer direct entry. Prospective students must meet stringent eligibility criteria and achieve a commendable score in the Common Admission Test (CAT).

**Eligibility Criteria for MBA:**

*   A Bachelor's degree from a UGC/AIU recognized university with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks (or equivalent CGPA) for General category candidates.
*   For certain programs, candidates might also need a minimum of three years of post-graduation managerial, entrepreneurial, or professional work experience.

After fulfilling the eligibility requirements and clearing the CAT exam, candidates typically undergo a rigorous selection process that may include a Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI).

## IIM Kashipur Fees Structure

The fee structure for the MBA program at IIM Kashipur generally ranges from INR 12 lakh to INR 17.5 lakh. This figure covers tuition and other academic expenses for the entire two-year duration. It's advisable to check the official IIM Kashipur website for the most current and detailed fee information, as these figures can be subject to change.

## CAT Cutoff for IIM Kashipur

IIM Kashipur accepts CAT scores for admission to its MBA program. While the exact cutoff for the upcoming academic session is released annually, historical data provides a good indication. For the General All India category, the overall CAT percentile cutoff has consistently been around 93.

Candidates aiming for IIM Kashipur should strive to score well above this percentile, not just overall but also in each section of the CAT exam, as sectional cutoffs are also applied.

Here's a look at the historical sectional CAT cutoffs:

| Section | 2020 | 2021 | 2022 |
| :------ | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Overall | 94   | 93   | 93   |
| Quants  | 80   | 93   | 93   |
| VA-RC   | 80   | 93   | 93   |
| DI-LR   | 80   | 93   | 93   |

*Please note: These are previous year cutoffs and the actual cutoffs for future admissions may vary.*

## Placements at IIM Kashipur

IIM Kashipur has a strong track record of placements, attracting leading companies from various sectors. The institute consistently achieves impressive placement statistics, reflecting the quality of its graduates.

For the batch of 2023, the average package stood at INR 18.11 LPA, with the highest domestic package reaching INR 37 LPA.

**Sector-wise Placement Distribution (2025 data):**

*   IT: 26%
*   BFSI (Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance): 20%
*   Manufacturing: 17%
*   Consulting: 16%
*   FinTech: 8%
*   Others: 13%

This diverse distribution highlights the wide range of career opportunities available to IIM Kashipur graduates across different industries.

## Campus Life and Student Experience

Life at IIM Kashipur is vibrant and entirely student-driven. From managing academic and non-academic activities to organizing major annual events like "Agnitraya" (the annual fest) and various management conferences, students are at the forefront. This hands-on involvement provides invaluable experience in time management, teamwork, problem-solving, and administration.

The campus offers a supportive environment with facilities like student-run canteens that cater to late-night study sessions, in addition to the regular mess services. Located in the picturesque mountains of Uttarakhand, IIM Kashipur provides a peaceful and inspiring backdrop for academic pursuits, with students often enjoying views of the Himalayas from their rooms on clear days.

## Faculty at IIM Kashipur

IIM Kashipur boasts an experienced and dedicated faculty team. The professors hold advanced degrees, including PhDs, from reputed institutions. The institute also invites visiting faculty members and industry experts to share their insights and provide students with diverse perspectives and real-world exposure.

The faculty members are actively involved in research, consulting, and curriculum development, ensuring that the education provided is current, relevant, and of the highest standard.
