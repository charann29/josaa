Jamia Hamdard, located in Delhi, is a well-regarded institution offering a diverse range of programs. This guide from BeingWise provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Exploring Programs and Fees at Jamia Hamdard

Jamia Hamdard offers an extensive selection of undergraduate and postgraduate courses across various disciplines. The total tuition fees for these programs can vary significantly based on the specific course you choose. For instance, the tuition fees for Bachelor of Arts (BA) courses generally range from approximately INR 1 Lakh to INR 8.8 Lakh. It's important to remember that these figures are indicative and can change, as they often include components like tuition, library deposits, and other charges, some of which are one-time payments while others are annual or semester-based.

### Popular Courses and Their Fee Ranges (Indicative Tuition Fees)

*   **BA Courses:** INR 1 Lakh - INR 8.8 Lakh
*   **B.Tech:** Details available in the official prospectus.
*   **B.Pharm:** Details available in the official prospectus.
*   **MBA:** Details available in the official prospectus.

## Admission Process and Key Entrance Exams

Admission to Jamia Hamdard is based on a combination of merit and entrance examination scores, depending on the specific program. Each academic year, the university publishes detailed admission guidelines and prospectuses on its official website, which prospective students should carefully review.

### Entrance Exam Requirements

*   **B.Tech:** Admission is primarily based on scores from the JEE Main examination.
*   **MBBS:** Selection for the MBBS program is through the NEET UG examination.
*   **BSc, BSc (Hons), BA (Hons), BA, BJMC, BCom, BBA, BMS:** For these undergraduate programs, the university considers scores from the CUET UG (Common University Entrance Test - Undergraduate) conducted by NTA. Alongside CUET scores, applicants must also meet specific program eligibility criteria, and the final selection often considers both CUET and qualifying examination marks.
*   **BCA:** Admission to the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program is typically based on merit in Class 12, specifically the best of five subjects.

### Seat Intake Example: B.Pharm

For the Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) program, Jamia Hamdard has an intake of 120 seats. Admissions are granted based on NEET/JEE scores, the sanctioned seat intake, and the university's reservation policy, which includes provisions for Indian Muslim candidates.
