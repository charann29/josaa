The Department of Management Studies (DOMS) at IIT Roorkee offers a strong foundation in management education, blending technical expertise with business acumen. This page provides a comprehensive overview of its programs, admission process, fee structure, typical cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered

DOMS IIT Roorkee primarily focuses on postgraduate management education, with its flagship MBA program attracting students from diverse backgrounds. The department also offers a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) program for those interested in advanced research.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

This is a full-time, two-year program designed to equip students with essential management skills and knowledge. The curriculum covers various aspects of business administration, preparing graduates for leadership roles in industry.

### Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)

The PhD program at DOMS IIT Roorkee is for individuals seeking to contribute to academic research and scholarship in management. It involves rigorous coursework and independent research culminating in a dissertation.

## Fees Structure

The fees for programs at DOMS IIT Roorkee are structured to be competitive while reflecting the quality of education provided. Here's a general overview:

*   **MBA Program:** The application fee for the MBA course is INR 1,600. However, candidates from SC/ST/PD categories pay a reduced fee of INR 800. Notably, female candidates are exempt from paying any application fee.
*   **PhD Program:** The application fee for the PhD program is INR 200, with a concession for SC/ST/PD categories, who pay INR 100.

Detailed tuition and other charges are typically updated annually and can be found on the official IIT Roorkee website.

## Admission Process

Admission to DOMS IIT Roorkee programs is highly competitive and follows a structured selection process.

### MBA Admission

The MBA admission process involves several stages:

1.  **CAT Application:** Candidates must first apply for the Common Admission Test (CAT) by the specified deadline.
2.  **Shortlisting:** Based on the CAT scores, candidates are shortlisted for the next round. A strong performance in CAT is crucial.
3.  **Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates are invited for a Personal Interview. This round assesses communication skills, general awareness, and suitability for the program.
4.  **Final Selection:** The final selection is based on a cumulative score that considers the CAT score, performance in the Personal Interview, academic record, and relevant work experience (if any).

### PhD Admission

For PhD admissions, candidates are primarily shortlisted based on their scores in national-level entrance exams such as GATE, UGC NET, or CSIR NET. The final selection also includes an evaluation of their past academic performance and a Personal Interview.

## Cutoff Information

While specific official CAT percentile cutoffs for admission are generally not made public, historical trends provide an estimate. For the General AI category, a CAT percentile around **95 overall**, with **50 percentile in each sectional component**, has typically been considered for shortlisting for the MBA program. Cutoffs for other categories are usually lower.
