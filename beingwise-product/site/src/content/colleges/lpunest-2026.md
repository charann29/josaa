Choosing the right higher education path is a big decision, and Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a popular choice for many. The LPUNEST (Lovely Professional University National Entrance and Scholarship Test) is your gateway to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs offered by the university. Let's explore what LPUNEST 2026 entails, from available courses and fees to the admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Understanding LPUNEST 2026

LPUNEST is a crucial online entrance examination that not only determines your eligibility for admission to LPU but also qualifies you for scholarship opportunities. It's designed to assess your aptitude and knowledge, ensuring that students admitted are well-prepared for their chosen fields of study. The exam is conducted in multiple phases throughout the year, offering flexibility to applicants.

### Examination Mode and Schedule

LPUNEST 2026 is conducted entirely online. You have two convenient options for taking the test: either from the comfort of your home as a remote proctored examination or at designated test centers across the country. For Phase 2 of LPUNEST 2026, remote proctored exams began on April 4, 2026, and continued until April 20, 2026. Test center examinations for this phase were held from April 4, 2026, until April 30, 2026.

## Courses Offered Through LPUNEST

LPU offers a wide array of programs across various disciplines. LPUNEST is the entrance test for many of these, including:

*   **Engineering (B.Tech):** Specializations like Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electronics, and more.
*   **Management (BBA, MBA):** Programs focusing on business administration, finance, marketing, and human resources.
*   **Pharmacy (B.Pharm, M.Pharm):** Courses in pharmaceutical sciences.
*   **Law (BA LLB, BBA LLB, LLB, LLM):** Integrated and postgraduate law programs.

*   **Sciences (B.Sc, M.Sc):** Various science disciplines.
*   **Arts (BA, MA):** Humanities and social science programs.

The specific programs requiring LPUNEST and their eligibility criteria can be found in the university's official information brochure.

## Admission Process and Key Dates

The admission process for LPU through LPUNEST is straightforward:

1.  **Application Submission:** Fill out the application form online on the official LPU website. The application dates are the same whether you choose a remote proctored exam or a test center-based exam.
2.  **Slot Booking:** After applying, you will need to book your preferred slot for the LPUNEST exam. You can indicate your preferred test city during registration, but the final slot and center allocation depend on availability.
3.  **Appear for LPUNEST:** Take the online examination on your scheduled date and time.
4.  **Result Declaration:** Results are typically announced shortly after the exam. They will be available on the official website and also shared via email.
5.  **Counselling and Seat Allocation:** Based on your LPUNEST score, you will be invited for online counseling. Seat allocation is strictly based on merit, with higher scores receiving preference.

LPUNEST is conducted in multiple phases from January to August each year. If you miss an earlier phase or are not satisfied with your score, you can reapply and appear for a later phase to improve your chances. Each phase is considered independently, and you will receive a new score and rank for each attempt.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure at LPU varies significantly depending on the program you choose. LPUNEST scores can also qualify you for scholarships, which can substantially reduce the payable fees. Detailed fee information for each course is available on the university's official website. It's advisable to check the specific program you are interested in for accurate and up-to-date fee details.

## Cutoff Scores

LPU does not publicly release specific cutoff marks for LPUNEST. Admission and scholarship eligibility are determined based on a merit list derived from the LPUNEST scores. Candidates with higher scores naturally have a better chance of securing admission to their desired programs and availing better scholarship brackets. The cutoff essentially depends on the overall performance of all candidates in a particular phase and the number of available seats.
