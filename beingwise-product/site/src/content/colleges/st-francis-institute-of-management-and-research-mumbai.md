St. Francis Institute of Management and Research (SFIMR) in Mumbai is a well-regarded institution for management education. If you're considering a career in business and management, understanding SFIMR's offerings, admission criteria, and career prospects is key. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about their courses, fees, admission process, cutoffs, and placements.

## Courses Offered at SFIMR

SFIMR primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs, approved by AICTE. These courses are designed to equip students with essential business acumen and leadership skills.

### Master of Management Studies (MMS)

The MMS program is a comprehensive two-year full-time course. It covers various aspects of management, preparing students for diverse roles in the corporate world.

### Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)

The PGDM program is another popular offering, providing a robust curriculum in management. It often includes specializations that allow students to focus on specific areas of interest.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

To secure a seat at SFIMR, candidates need to meet specific eligibility criteria and successfully navigate the admission process.

### Eligibility Criteria

For postgraduate management programs like MMS and PGDM, candidates typically need a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. For certain programs like the MBA/PGDM, a minimum of two years of work experience at an executive or supervisory level after graduation is required.

### Entrance Exams and Cutoffs

Admission to the MMS course at SFIMR is primarily based on MAH CET scores. The cutoff for the General All India category has seen variations. For instance, a recent cutoff score for this category was 97.85 percentile, which was notably higher than previous years. This indicates a competitive admission landscape.

## Fee Structure

Understanding the financial commitment is crucial. The fee range for the courses offered at St. Francis Institute of Management and Research typically falls between INR 1,45,770 and INR 5,48,000. It's advisable to check the exact fees for your chosen program directly with the institute.

## Campus Life and Infrastructure

SFIMR boasts a well-supported campus environment designed to enhance the student experience. The institute has received positive ratings for its infrastructure and campus life.

### Facilities Available

The campus provides a range of facilities including air-conditioned classrooms, an auditorium, a cafeteria, a library, and Wi-Fi connectivity. For student accommodation, separate boys' and girls' hostels are available. The institute also features a gym, laboratories, shuttle services, and a sports complex, contributing to a vibrant campus life.

## Placements at SFIMR

Placements are a significant aspect for any management aspirant, and SFIMR has a dedicated placement assistance program. The institute has consistently placed its students in various reputable companies.

### Median Salary and Top Recruiters

The median package offered for the MBA/PGDM course at SFIMR is around INR 4,00,000 per annum. Students have been recruited by well-known organizations across different sectors. Some of the companies that have hired from SFIMR include:

Based on verified student reviews, the institute has received a rating of 3.24 out of 5 for its placements, indicating a generally satisfactory outcome for students seeking employment.

## Faculty and Learning Experience

The quality of faculty plays a vital role in shaping a student's learning journey. SFIMR has been rated positively for its faculty members.

Student reviews indicate that the institute has qualified and experienced faculty, contributing to a decent learning environment. The faculty rating stands at 4.3 out of 5, reflecting student satisfaction with their educators.

## Overall Intake Capacity

St. Francis Institute of Management and Research has an overall intake capacity of up to 390 students across its various approved courses. This allows for a diverse student body and a dynamic learning environment.
