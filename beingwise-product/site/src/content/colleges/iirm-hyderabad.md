The Institute of Insurance and Risk Management (IIRM) in Hyderabad is a well-known institution focusing on specialized management education. It aims to prepare students for careers in finance, particularly in insurance, risk management, actuarial science, and business analytics.

## Programs Offered

IIRM Hyderabad primarily offers postgraduate diploma programs that are approved by AICTE and recognized by IRDAI. These full-time programs are designed to be current with industry needs.

*   **PGDM (Post Graduate Diploma in Management)**: This two-year program comes with a selection of nine specializations, allowing students to focus on areas like finance, marketing, human resources, and, critically, insurance and risk management.
*   **PG Diploma in Actuarial Science**: A specialized program for those interested in the analytical and statistical aspects of risk, particularly within the insurance sector.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to IIRM Hyderabad's programs is primarily entrance-based, considering various national-level management aptitude tests.

### Eligibility Criteria

For the PGDM program, candidates generally need to have a bachelor's degree from a recognized university with at least 50% aggregate marks. A valid score from an accepted entrance exam is also essential.

### Selection Criteria

The selection process involves multiple stages:

1.  **Entrance Exam Score**: Candidates must have a valid score from one of the following exams: CAT, MAT, CMAT, ATMA, XAT, GMAT, or IIRM CET.
2.  **Actuarial Proficiency Test**: For the PG Diploma in Actuarial Science, in addition to the management entrance exam score, candidates must also pass an Actuarial Proficiency Test conducted by IIRM.
3.  **Personal Interview (PI)**: Shortlisted candidates will undergo a personal interview.
4.  **Academic Performance**: The candidate's past academic record is also taken into consideration.

Interested applicants can find detailed application procedures on the institute's official website.

## Fees Structure (Approximate)
