The Punjab Institute of Technology (PIT), GTB Garh, is a constituent college of Maharaja Ranjit Singh Punjab Technical University (MRSPTU), located in Moga. This page offers a clear overview of its academic programs, fee structures, admission procedures, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities.

## Courses Offered at PIT GTB Garh

PIT GTB Garh provides a diverse range of programs across various academic streams, catering to students seeking undergraduate, postgraduate, and certificate qualifications. The primary areas of study include Business & Management, Information Technology, Engineering, and Commerce.

### Undergraduate Programs

*   **Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech):** This four-year engineering degree is available with specializations in:
    *   Civil Engineering
    *   Computer Science Engineering
    *   Electrical Engineering
    *   Mechanical Engineering
*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** A three-year program focused on management and business principles.
*   **Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA):** A three-year program for students interested in computer science and software development.
*   **Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com):** A three-year program covering accounting, finance, and business studies.

### Postgraduate Programs

*   **Master of Engineering (M.E.) / Master of Technology (M.Tech):** A two-year postgraduate engineering program with a specialization in Electrical Engineering.

### Certificate Programs

*   The institute also offers certificate courses with specializations in Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering, providing focused skills training.

## Admission Process and Cutoff

Admission to programs at PIT GTB Garh is primarily based on merit and entrance examination scores, depending on the course.

*   **B.Tech Admissions:** Candidates are generally admitted based on their performance in state-level or national-level engineering entrance examinations, such as JEE Main, followed by counseling conducted by MRSPTU or a designated authority. Cutoff ranks will vary each year based on the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats.
*   **Other Undergraduate and Postgraduate Admissions:** For programs like BBA, BCA, B.Com, and M.E./M.Tech, admissions are typically based on academic performance in qualifying examinations and may include an institute-level entrance test or counseling process. Specific cutoff criteria are released by the university annually.

Prospective students should regularly check the MRSPTU admission portal for application deadlines, eligibility criteria, and detailed admission guidelines.

## Placements

PIT GTB Garh focuses on preparing its students for successful careers through a dedicated placement cell. The institute strives to connect students with various industries and companies for internships and final job placements.

While specific placement statistics can fluctuate year to year, the college aims to provide opportunities in relevant sectors. Graduates from engineering disciplines often find roles in manufacturing, IT services, infrastructure, and core engineering companies. Management and commerce graduates are sought after in areas like finance, marketing, human resources, and business development across various industries.

Students are encouraged to actively participate in training programs, workshops, and placement drives organized by the college to enhance their employability skills and secure desirable job offers.
