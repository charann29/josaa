Symbiosis Institute of Health Sciences (SIHS), part of Symbiosis International University in Pune, is a well-regarded institution focused on healthcare education. It offers a range of programs designed to equip students for various roles within the health sector.

## Programs Offered at SIHS Pune

SIHS provides diverse undergraduate and postgraduate courses, catering to different aspects of health sciences. These include specialized BSc degrees and an MBA in Hospital and Healthcare Management.

### Undergraduate Courses

The institute offers Bachelor of Science (BSc) programs with distinct specializations:

*   **BSc Medical Technology:** A comprehensive program preparing students for technical roles in healthcare diagnostics and treatment.
*   **BSc Radiotherapy:** Focuses on the principles and practice of using radiation for medical treatment.
*   **BSc Mental Health:** This unique program delves into mental health principles and care, preparing students for roles in this vital area.

### Postgraduate Courses

For those seeking advanced management roles in healthcare, SIHS offers:

*   **MBA Hospital and Healthcare Management (HHM):** This program is designed to develop leaders for hospitals, healthcare organizations, and related industries.

## Admission Process

Admission to SIHS Pune is competitive and typically involves a multi-stage process, ensuring that deserving candidates are selected. The specific steps vary slightly depending on the course.

### BSc Admission Process

For most BSc programs, the admission journey involves:

1.  **Online Application:** Candidates must register and complete the application form on the official SIHS Pune website, paying the required application fee.
2.  **Entrance Examination:** For BSc Medical Technology and Radiotherapy, applicants need to appear for the Symbiosis Entrance Test (SET) conducted by the university. For the BSc Mental Health program, a Statement of Purpose (SOP) is required instead of an entrance test.
3.  **Written Ability Test (WAT) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates for most BSc programs (except BSc Mental Health, which only has a PI) are invited for a WAT and PI round.
4.  **Provisional Admission:** Successful candidates receive provisional admission offers via email. Final admission is confirmed after document verification and payment of the initial fees.

### MBA (HHM) Admission Process

Admission to the MBA program typically requires:

1.  **SNAP Test:** Applicants must take the Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP).
2.  **Application to SIHS:** After SNAP, candidates apply specifically to SIHS for the MBA (HHM) program.
3.  **WAT & PI:** Shortlisted candidates from SNAP are called for a Written Ability Test and Personal Interview.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for programs at SIHS Pune is determined by Symbiosis International University. While specific figures can change, here's a general idea:

*   **BSc Programs:** The annual tuition fees for BSc courses typically range from approximately INR 2.30 Lakhs to INR 2.50 Lakhs.
*   **MSc Programs:** For postgraduate science programs, the fees are generally around INR 2.10 Lakhs per year.
*   **MBA (HHM):** The total fees for the MBA program usually fall in the range of INR 9.00 Lakhs to INR 9.50 Lakhs for the entire course duration.

*Note: These figures are indicative and it's always best to check the latest fee schedule on the official SIHS Pune website.*
