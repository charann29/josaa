The International Management Institute (IMI) in Kolkata stands as a prominent institution for management education in Eastern India. If you're considering a postgraduate management program, understanding IMI Kolkata's offerings, admission process, and career prospects is crucial.

## Programs Offered

IMI Kolkata primarily focuses on its flagship two-year Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This comprehensive course is structured into six trimesters, with each trimester lasting approximately 10-12 weeks, providing a rigorous and in-depth learning experience.

## Admission Process

Admissions for the upcoming academic year are currently open at IMI Kolkata. Prospective students are encouraged to visit the institute's official website to complete the application form. The selection process is multi-faceted and considers various parameters:

*   **Entrance Exam Scores:** Applicants must submit valid scores from one of the accepted national-level management entrance examinations: CAT, XAT, GMAT, or CMAT.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates will undergo a personal interview round, which is a critical component of the selection process.

## Fees Structure

The tuition fee for the PGDM program at IMI Kolkata is ₹9.80 lakhs. This fee covers the academic aspects of the program, but it's always advisable to check the official website for a complete breakdown of all associated costs, including hostel, mess, and other charges.

## Placement Highlights

IMI Kolkata has consistently demonstrated strong placement records for its PGDM graduates, reflecting the quality of its education and industry connections. The institute attracts a diverse range of recruiters across various sectors.
