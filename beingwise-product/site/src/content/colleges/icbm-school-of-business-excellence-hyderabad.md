ICBM-School of Business Excellence in Hyderabad is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at ICBM-SBE

ICBM-SBE primarily focuses on management education, offering a comprehensive Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This is a full-time, two-year course approved by the AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education).

### PGDM Specializations

The PGDM program at ICBM-SBE allows students to specialize in multiple areas, equipping them with diverse skills for the business world. Some of the key specializations include:

*   Marketing
*   Human Resources
*   Finance
*   Operations
*   International Business
*   Entrepreneurship
*   Information Technology

The curriculum is designed to foster essential business acumen, covering areas like communication, presentation skills, leadership, professional ethics, team building, and business analytics.

## Fees Structure

For the two-year, full-time PGDM program at ICBM-SBE, the total tuition fee is INR 7 Lakhs. Additionally, there is a one-time fee of INR 45,000, bringing the total program cost to INR 7.45 Lakhs.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the PGDM program at ICBM-SBE is based on a combination of academic merit and entrance examination scores.

### Eligibility Criteria

Prospective students must hold a bachelor's or master's degree with a minimum aggregate score. This foundational academic qualification is crucial for consideration.

### Entrance Exams

Applicants are required to achieve a valid score in one of the recognized national-level management entrance examinations. These include:

*   CAT
*   MAT
*   XAT
*   CMAT
*   ATMA
*   GMAT

## Scholarships

ICBM-SBE offers scholarships to deserving PGDM students through the AB-KBC Scholarship test. The scholarship amount is directly linked to the score achieved in this test, providing financial assistance based on merit.

| Test Score Secured | Scholarship Amount |
| :----------------- | :----------------- |
| Above 90           | INR 1 Lakh         |
| 81 to 90           | INR 75,000         |
| 71 to 80           | INR 50,000         |
| 61 to 70           | INR 25,000         |

## Seat Intake

The PGDM program at ICBM-SBE has an annual intake capacity of 300 students. This number covers all the available specializations within the single PGDM course.

## Cutoff Information

The cutoff scores for admission to the MBA/PGDM program at ICBM-SBE for the upcoming academic year (2025) have not yet been released. Historically, the institute considers minimum percentiles from previous years' entrance exam scores for admissions.

## Placements at ICBM-SBE

ICBM-SBE has a dedicated placement cell that actively prepares students for their careers. They conduct extensive training sessions focusing on various aspects of professional development to enhance employability.

While achieving a 100% placement rate can be challenging for any institution due to various student choices (like entrepreneurship or further studies) and attendance requirements, ICBM-SBE aims to provide placement assistance to every PGDM student who maintains at least 85% attendance during their course.
