The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Ranchi is a respected institution offering quality management education. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff scores, and placement opportunities to help you make informed decisions.

## IIM Ranchi: Courses and Programmes

IIM Ranchi offers a range of management programmes tailored for different academic and professional stages.

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** This is the flagship programme, covering General Management. IIM Ranchi also offers specialized MBAs in Human Resources (MBA-HR) and Business Analytics.
*   **Executive MBA (EMBA):** Designed for working professionals, this programme requires a minimum of five years of full-time work experience.
*   **Integrated Programme in Management (IPM):** A five-year integrated course for students after Class 12, combining undergraduate (BBA) and postgraduate (MBA) management studies. Upon completion, students receive an MBA degree.

The IPM curriculum is broad, drawing from humanities, social sciences, arts, and sciences, and builds a strong foundation in business core subjects like management, marketing, accounting, finance, economics, information systems, data science, and strategic management.

## IIM Ranchi: Fee Structure

The fees at IIM Ranchi vary by programme. Here's an estimated breakdown:

*   **Integrated Programme in Management (IPM):** The total fees for the first three years of the programme are approximately INR 16 Lakh. For the subsequent two years, the fees will be equivalent to the MBA programme fees set for those specific years.

For current and detailed fee information for all programmes, it's always best to check the official IIM Ranchi website.

## IIM Ranchi: Admission Process and Selection Criteria

Admission to IIM Ranchi is highly competitive and primarily entrance-based, followed by personal interviews.

### For MBA Programmes (General, HR, Business Analytics)

1.  **Common Admission Test (CAT):** Candidates must appear for the CAT exam. This exam has three sections: Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC), Data Interpretation and Logical Reasoning (DILR), and Quantitative Aptitude (QUANT).
2.  **Cutoff Scores:** Applicants must meet both the sectional and overall CAT percentile cutoffs to be eligible for further rounds. For MBA 2024 admissions, the expected qualifying CAT cutoff was around 93 percentile overall, with a minimum of 75 percentile in each section.
3.  **Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates based on CAT scores are invited for a Personal Interview. In some cases, a Written Ability Test (WAT) and Group Discussion (GD) might also be part of the selection process.
4.  **Final Selection:** Final admission offers are made based on a composite score, typically weighted as follows:
    *   CAT Score: 40%
    *   Personal Interview (PI) Score: 30%
    *   Candidate Profile (academic record, work experience, diversity): 30%

### For Executive MBA

1.  **Work Experience:** Candidates must have at least five years of full-time work experience.
2.  **Personal Interview (PI):** Selection is primarily based on a Personal Interview process.

### For Integrated Programme in Management (IPM)

1.  **IPMAT Examination:** Candidates aspiring for the IPM programme after Class 12 must appear for the IPMAT examination.
2.  **Personal Interview (PI):** Following the IPMAT, shortlisted candidates undergo a virtual Personal Interview round conducted by IIM Ranchi.

## IIM Ranchi: Cutoff Scores

The CAT cutoff for IIM Ranchi is an important benchmark for MBA admissions. While cutoffs can vary each year, the final CAT cutoff for the General AI category has historically been around the 94 percentile, both overall and section-wise. Candidates with a percentile between 90 and 95 (General category) may be eligible, provided they also perform well in the subsequent selection rounds like WAT, GD, and PI.

## IIM Ranchi: Placements

IIM Ranchi has a consistent record of strong placements, attracting a diverse range of companies across various sectors. The institute facilitates campus recruitment drives, providing students with opportunities for summer internships and final placements. Detailed placement reports, including average and highest salary packages and top recruiting companies, are usually released annually by the institute.
