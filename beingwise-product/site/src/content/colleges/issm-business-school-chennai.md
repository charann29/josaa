ISSM Business School, located in Chennai, offers a range of management programs designed to prepare students for the corporate world. If you're considering a career in business, understanding their offerings, admission process, and career prospects is key.

## Programs Offered

ISSM Business School primarily focuses on postgraduate management education. Their flagship offering is a comprehensive two-year MBA program. They also provide various certificate courses that can enhance specific skills and career readiness.

### MBA Program

This full-time, two-year postgraduate program is designed to provide a strong foundation in business administration. It covers various aspects of management, preparing students for leadership roles across industries.

### Certificate Courses

In addition to the MBA, ISSM offers certificate programs that cater to specific areas of business, providing focused training and skill development.

## Fees Structure

For precise fee details for the MBA and certificate programs, it's always best to check the official ISSM Business School website or contact their admissions office directly, as these figures can be updated periodically.

## Admission Process

Admission to ISSM Business School, particularly for the MBA program, is based on a combination of academic merit and entrance exam scores. Here's a general overview of how to apply:

### Eligibility Criteria

For the MBA program, candidates typically need a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in their undergraduate degree. This academic performance is a crucial initial step.

### Entrance Exams Accepted

ISSM Business School accepts scores from several national-level management entrance examinations for MBA admissions. These include:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   MAT (Management Aptitude Test)
*   TANCET (Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test)

Meeting the cutoff scores in one of these exams is essential for consideration.

### Application Procedure

The application process at ISSM Business School is conducted offline. Prospective students need to:

1.  **Obtain the Application Form:** Download or collect the application form from the institute.
2.  **Fill the Form:** Complete the application form with all required personal and academic details.
3.  **Prepare Supporting Documents:** Gather all necessary documents, such as mark sheets, certificates, and entrance exam scorecards.
4.  **Application Fee:** Include a Demand Draft (DD) of INR 1000, payable to "Indian School of Science & Management" in Chennai.
5.  **Submission:** Send the filled form, supporting documents, and the DD to the ISSM Business School admissions office.

After successful submission, candidates will proceed to the selection rounds, which may include interviews or group discussions.

## Placement Opportunities

ISSM Business School has a consistent record of placing its MBA and certificate course students. The institute emphasizes practical exposure, including summer internships, to prepare students for corporate roles.

### Internship Program

Students at ISSM participate in summer internships, typically in July and August. These internships offer valuable job training, providing real-time corporate experience and often leading to pre-placement offers from companies.
