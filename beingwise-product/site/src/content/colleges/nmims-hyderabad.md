# NMIMS Hyderabad: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

NMIMS Hyderabad is a well-regarded institution offering a range of programs in management and other disciplines. This page provides a clear overview of its offerings, admission process, fee structure, and career prospects to help you make informed decisions.

## Key Takeaways

*   Admission to most undergraduate programs like BBA and B.Com (Hons) requires appearing for the NMIMS NPAT entrance exam.
*   Certain specialized programs, such as B.Sc. Applied Statistics and Analytics, consider merit-based admission alongside other criteria.
*   NMIMS Hyderabad has a strong placement record, with MBA programs consistently achieving good median and average packages.
*   The institution strictly prohibits ragging and maintains a supportive learning environment.

## Admission Process

Admission to NMIMS Hyderabad is primarily entrance-based for many programs. Here's a general outline:

### Undergraduate Admissions

*   **BBA, B.Com (Hons):** Candidates must appear for the NMIMS Programs After Twelfth (NPAT) entrance examination. This is followed by a counselling process.
*   **B.A. L.L.B (Hons), B.B.A. L.L.B (Hons):** Admission typically requires a valid score in entrance exams like CLAT or LSAT, followed by an interview.
*   **B.Sc. Applied Statistics and Analytics:** Admission for this program is merit-based, considering academic performance and other relevant achievements.

### Postgraduate Admissions

*   **MBA:** Prospective students need to take the NMAT by GMAC™ entrance exam. Shortlisted candidates then proceed to a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round.
*   **MBA (Pharma Tech), MBA Applied Statistics and Analytics:** These programs also consider merit, alongside academic records and other achievements.

It's important to keep an eye on the official NMIMS website for specific application timelines, as they are usually announced in conjunction with the respective entrance exam registration dates, such as the NPAT dates expected in January.
