## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admissions at SRMU Lucknow are generally based on both academic merit and entrance examination scores, depending on the program. The university aims to select deserving candidates through a transparent process.

### Application Procedure

*   **Online Application:** Most admissions begin with filling out an online application form available on the SRMU official website.
*   **Application Fee:** There is typically a non-refundable application fee, which can be paid through various digital methods like credit/debit cards, net banking, or UPI.
*   **Seat Booking:** For some programs, candidates might have the option to book a seat by paying an initial amount, which is often adjusted against the first-year fees.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **Undergraduate Programs:** For courses like B.Tech, B.Pharma, BCA, and B.Com, candidates usually need to have completed 10+2 with a minimum percentage in relevant subjects, as specified for each program.
*   **Postgraduate Programs:** For MBA, M.Tech, and other master's courses, a relevant bachelor's degree from a recognized university is generally required, often with a minimum aggregate score.
*   **Lateral Entry:** Students who have completed a diploma (e.g., D.Pharma) can often apply for lateral entry into relevant bachelor's degree programs (e.g., B.Pharma), allowing them to join in the second year.

### Important Dates

Admission forms for various programs, including D.Pharma, are usually released on the university's official website. It's advisable to regularly check the site for current admission cycles and deadlines.
