The Institute for Future Education, Entrepreneurship and Leadership (iFEEL) in Pune stands out as a dedicated institution for management education. Located in Lonavala, it focuses on equipping students with the skills needed for the modern business world.

## Institute for Future Education, Entrepreneurship and Leadership, Pune: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

iFEEL Pune is an AICTE-approved residential college, recognized by the Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra. It also holds international accreditations from the International Association for Quality Assurance in Pre-Tertiary & Higher Education (QAHE), and is a member of the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB) and the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP). The institute is also affiliated with the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII).

### Popular Courses and Fees

iFEEL is particularly well-regarded for its Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This two-year, full-time course is designed to provide a strong foundation in management principles and techniques, aiming to develop future leaders across various industries. The curriculum emphasizes practical experience and industry relevance.

**PGDM Program Fees:**

| Particulars        | Amount       |
| :----------------- | :----------- |
| Total Course Fee   | INR 8.90 Lakh |
| Annual Hostel Fee  | INR 1.80 Lakh |

### Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to iFEEL's PGDM program is a multi-stage process, ensuring that deserving candidates are selected. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree in any stream with a minimum aggregate score of 50%.

**Selection Criteria:**

*   **Entrance Exam Scores:** Candidates must have valid scores from national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, ATMA, or GMAT.
*   **Aptitude Test:** An online aptitude test is conducted by the institute.
*   **Written Ability Test (WAT):** This assesses a candidate's writing and analytical skills.
*   **Personal Interview Rounds:** Typically, two rounds of personal interviews are conducted to evaluate communication skills, leadership potential, and overall suitability for the program.

**Application Fee:**
The application fee for the PGDM program is INR 880. It's important to complete the application and pay the fee promptly to proceed with the admission process.

### Scholarships

iFEEL understands that quality education can be an investment and offers scholarships to support deserving students. One notable scholarship is:

*   **Scholarship Amount:** INR 1 Lakh
*   **Eligibility:** Open to female candidates who have scored 85% or higher in their MBA/PGDM entrance exams for admission to the 2024-26 batch.
*   **Availability:** Offered to a maximum of 25 eligible students.
*   **Application:** The scholarship is applied as a direct fee waiver to the first year's fees.

### Placements

iFEEL has a strong track record of placements, making it an attractive option for students seeking good career opportunities after their PGDM. The institute focuses on preparing students for successful careers through its industry-aligned curriculum.
