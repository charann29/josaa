# IMI Delhi: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

The International Management Institute (IMI) Delhi is a well-regarded institution offering various postgraduate management programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, admission criteria, fee structure, and placement opportunities to help prospective students and parents make informed decisions.

## Key takeaways

*   Admission to most IMI Delhi programs relies on national-level entrance exam scores like CAT or GMAT.
*   The flagship PGDM program requires a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks.
*   IMI Delhi consistently achieves strong placements, with a high average package for its graduates.
*   The institute offers a diverse range of programs, including full-time PGDM, Executive PGDM, and a Fellow Programme in Management (FPM).

## Programs Offered and Eligibility

IMI Delhi provides a selection of management programs tailored for different career stages and aspirations. Here’s a look at the main offerings and their basic eligibility requirements:

*   **PGDM (Post Graduate Diploma in Management):** This is the flagship two-year full-time program. Candidates need a bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
*   **EPGDM (Executive Post Graduate Diploma in Management):** Designed for working professionals, this program requires a bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks and a minimum of three years of post-qualification executive or self-employed professional experience.
*   **FPM (Fellow Programme in Management):** This doctoral-level program is for those interested in academic and research careers. Eligibility typically involves a master's degree or equivalent with at least 60% aggregate marks, or a bachelor's degree with a high percentage and significant work experience.

## Admission Process and Selection Criteria

Admission to IMI Delhi is primarily entrance-based, with specific criteria varying by program. There is no direct admission for most courses.

### For PGDM

Selection is based on a combination of factors:

*   Valid scores from national-level entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, or GMAT.
*   Performance in a video assessment and personal interview (PI).
*   Evaluation of past academic records.

### For EPGDM

Admission considers:

*   Scores from entrance exams like CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, ATMA, or GMAT.
*   An IMI Admission Test score.
*   Past academic performance and work experience.
*   A personal interview.

### For FPM

The selection process for the Fellow Programme in Management is comprehensive:

*   Scores from CAT, GMAT, XAT, GRE, NET, or the IMI Research Aptitude Test.
*   An admission interview.
*   Submission of a research abstract.
*   Review of past academic records.
*   Work experience is also considered, particularly for part-time candidates.

## Fee Structure

The fees for IMI Delhi programs reflect the quality of education and facilities provided. As per official information, the total fee for the two-year PGDM program is approximately INR 23.54 lakh. This amount includes tuition, a refundable security deposit, an admission fee, and other one-time charges. It's important for selected candidates to pay the first installment of fees to confirm their admission. Prospective students should always verify the latest fee structure on the institute's official website, as these figures are subject to change.

## Placements

IMI Delhi has a strong track record of placements, attracting a wide range of companies across various sectors. While specific placement statistics for the current year are often released later, historical data indicates impressive outcomes.

For the Class of 2025, the placement insights, as per official data, were:

*   **Highest Package:** INR 40.31 LPA
*   **Average Package:** INR 17.91 LPA
*   **Median Package:** INR 16.61 LPA
*   **Participating Companies:** Over 100+ companies recruited from the campus.

These figures demonstrate the institute's ability to connect its graduates with excellent career opportunities. The placement percentage is typically very high, often nearing 100% once officially declared.

IMI Delhi continues to be a top choice for management education, preparing students for successful careers in a dynamic global environment.
