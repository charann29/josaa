GM University, located in Davangere, is a well-known institution offering a wide array of programs across various disciplines. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, entrance exam cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Courses Offered at GM University

GM University boasts over 60 courses, catering to diverse academic interests. These span popular fields like Engineering, Science, Commerce, Business Management, and Information Technology. At the undergraduate level, some of the highly sought-after programs include Bachelor of Technology (BTech), Bachelor of Commerce (BCom), Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), and Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA). For postgraduate studies, options like Master of Science (MSc), Master of Commerce (MCom), and Master of Business Administration (MBA) are available.

### Popular Courses and Eligibility Criteria

To apply for any program at GM University, candidates must meet specific eligibility requirements. For certain courses, passing an entrance examination is also a prerequisite. Here's a look at the common eligibility for some key programs:

| Course | Common Eligibility Criteria |
| :----------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **BTech** | Passed Class 12 or pre-university exam with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology / Computer Science / Electronics as optional subjects. Minimum 50% aggregate (45% for SC/ST/CAT-1) and English as one language. |
| **MBA** | Graduation with at least 50% aggregate (45% for SC/ST/CAT-1). |
| **BCom** | Passed Class 12 or pre-university exam (PUC-II) in Science, Arts, or Commerce with at least 35% aggregate. |
| **BBA** | Passed Class 12 or pre-university exam (PUC-II) in Science, Arts, or Commerce with at least 35% aggregate. |
| **MCA** | BCA/BSc/BCom/BBM with at least 50% aggregate (45% for SC/ST/CAT-1). |

## Admission Process and Entrance Exams

Admission to GM University programs is based on either merit in qualifying examinations or scores in relevant entrance tests. The university conducts its own entrance exam, GMUSET, for specific programs like MBA, BCom LLB, BA LLB, LLB, and DPharm.

Additionally, GM University accepts scores from various other prominent entrance exams:

*   **KCET:** Considered for BTech, BPharm, and DPharm programs.
*   **Karnataka PGCET:** Accepted for MBA and MCA programs.
*   **GATE and PGCET:** Considered for M.Tech courses.

For programs like BBA, BCom, and BCA, admission is typically merit-based, relying on your scores in the last qualifying examination. It's also possible to gain direct admission to the second year of BTech and BPharm programs through lateral entry, provided you meet the specific course eligibility criteria.
