Embarking on an MBA journey is a significant decision, and Shiv Nadar University Chennai (SNUC) offers a compelling program for aspiring business leaders. Located in the vibrant city of Chennai, SNUC's MBA program is designed to equip students with essential management skills and a strong foundation for their careers.

## Shiv Nadar University Chennai MBA Program Overview

SNUC's MBA is a full-time, two-year residential program. The curriculum is crafted to provide a comprehensive understanding of business principles and practices, preparing graduates for diverse roles in the corporate world.

## Admission Process for SNUC MBA

Admission to the MBA program at SNUC is primarily based on entrance examination scores. Candidates are evaluated on their performance in nationally recognized management entrance tests or state-level exams.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

SNUC accepts scores from a range of popular entrance examinations, including:

*   CAT
*   XAT
*   CMAT
*   MAT
*   ATMA
*   NMAT
*   TANCET (Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test)

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the MBA program, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree with a minimum aggregate of 60% marks from a recognized university.

### How to Apply

The application process for SNUC's MBA program is straightforward and conducted online:

1.  Visit the official Shiv Nadar University Chennai website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Apply Now' option, typically found on the homepage.
3.  Register by providing basic details to create your unique login credentials.
4.  Log in using your new credentials and proceed to fill out the application form carefully.
5.  Upload all required documents, such as academic transcripts and entrance exam scorecards.
6.  Pay the application fee as specified.
7.  Review all entered information and submit the application form.

## SNUC MBA Cutoff

For the 2024 admissions cycle, the CAT cutoff percentile for the General AI quota at Shiv Nadar University Chennai was 60 overall. The sectional cutoffs were as follows:

| Section    | 2024 Cutoff Percentile |
| :--------- | :--------------------- |
| Overall    | 60                     |
| Quants     | 40                     |
| VA-RC      | 40                     |
| DI-LR      | 40                     |

## Program Fees

The total tuition fee for the two-year full-time MBA program at SNUC is INR 6 lakhs. It's important to note that this is a compulsory residential program, and living expenses will be additional to the tuition fees.

## Placement Highlights

Shiv Nadar University Chennai has a strong track record of placements for its MBA graduates. The placement statistics for 2025 demonstrate competitive salary packages.

### MBA Placement Statistics (2025)

| Particulars             | Placement Statistics (2025) |
| :---------------------- | :-------------------------- |
| Overall Average Package | INR 7.60 LPA                |
| Top 10% Average Package | INR 13.50 LPA               |
| Top 25% Average Package | INR 10.90 LPA               |

## Why Choose SNUC for your MBA?

SNUC stands out as a reputable institution for MBA studies in Chennai. The university boasts a large campus equipped with modern infrastructure, creating a conducive learning environment. Beyond academics, SNUC supports its students through various scholarship programs, including Merit, Merit-cum-means, and Sports scholarships, designed to encourage and assist deserving candidates.

## Campus Safety and Facilities

Student safety is a priority at SNUC. The campus, situated on Old Mahabalipuram Road, Chennai, is well-connected by road and public transport. Extensive CCTV surveillance is in place across the campus to ensure a secure environment for all students, both male and female.
