Jagdish Sheth School of Management (JAGSoM) in Bangalore is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admissions process, cutoff scores, and placement statistics to help prospective students and their families make informed decisions.

## Programs Offered at JAGSoM

JAGSoM primarily offers two-year Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) programs, which are equivalent to an MBA. These programs are designed to provide comprehensive management education across various specialisations. The total intake for all PGDM specialisations is 420 students.

Here's a breakdown of the PGDM specialisations and their respective seat intakes:

| PGDM Specialisation         | Seat Intake |
| :-------------------------- | :---------- |
| General                     | 180         |
| Marketing                   | 60          |
| Finance                     | 60          |
| Data Science and Business Analytics | 60          |
| International Business      | 60          |

JAGSoM also offers unique dual international PGDM programs in International Business. In these programs, students spend one year studying at JAGSoM and the second year at partner universities located in the USA and France, gaining valuable global exposure.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

To be eligible for JAGSoM's PGDM programs, candidates must hold a bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks. The selection process considers valid scores from national-level entrance examinations. JAGSoM accepts scores from:

*   CAT
*   XAT
*   MAT
*   CMAT
*   JAGMAT

## Cutoff Scores

Based on past trends, the cutoff for admission into JAGSoM's PGDM programs typically ranges as follows:

*   **XAT:** Around 70 percentile
*   **CAT:** Around 75 percentile

It's important to note that cutoff scores can vary each year based on the number of applicants and their performance.

## Fee Structure

While specific fee details are best confirmed directly with the institution, JAGSoM is known for its comprehensive program fees. The institution also provides scholarship opportunities, which can be significant, with some scholarships reaching up to INR 14 lakh for students from both General and Reserved categories.

## Placements at JAGSoM

JAGSoM has consistently demonstrated strong placement records for its PGDM graduates, both domestically and internationally. The placement cell actively facilitates interactions between students and a wide range of recruiters.

### Placement Highlights (2024-2025 Data):

*   **Highest Domestic Package:** INR 43 LPA
*   **Average Package:** INR 17.2 LPA
*   **Highest International Package:** INR 85 LPA (achieved by 3 students)
*   **Highest Package (PGDM 2025 Batch):** INR 20 LPA

### Recruiter Participation:

The number of companies participating in placements has been substantial:

*   **2024:** Over 70 inbound recruiters and 113 outbound recruiters
*   **2023:** 100 companies
*   **2022:** 123 companies

## Rankings and Recognition

JAGSoM's PGDM courses have received international recognition. QS Masters Global has ranked JAGSoM for its specialised PGDM programs:

*   **Marketing:** 101+
*   **Analytics:** 101+
*   **Finance:** 151+

## Campus Facilities and Faculty

JAGSoM provides excellent infrastructure to support a conducive learning environment. Facilities include modern classrooms, well-equipped libraries, an auditorium, and provisions for medical and sports activities.

The faculty at JAGSoM is highly qualified, with a significant number holding PhDs in their respective fields. The remaining faculty members possess extensive expertise and experience in their domains, ensuring high-quality education and mentorship for students.
