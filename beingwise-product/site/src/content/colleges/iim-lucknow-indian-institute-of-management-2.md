The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Lucknow stands as a beacon of management education in India, renowned for its academic rigor and commitment to nurturing future leaders. Located in the vibrant city of Lucknow, this institution offers a diverse range of postgraduate and doctoral programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the global business landscape.

## Programs Offered at IIM Lucknow

IIM Lucknow provides a comprehensive portfolio of management programs catering to various career stages and aspirations. These include flagship postgraduate programs, specialized courses, and doctoral research opportunities.

*   **Post Graduate Programme (PGP):** This is the flagship MBA-equivalent program, designed for fresh graduates and those with limited work experience, offering a broad-based management education.
*   **Post Graduate Programme in Agri-Business Management (PGP-ABM):** A specialized program focusing on management principles within the agricultural and food sectors.
*   **Post Graduate Programme in Sustainable Management (PGP-SM):** This program emphasizes sustainable business practices and responsible leadership.
*   **International Programme in Management for Executives (IPMX):** Tailored for experienced professionals, this one-year full-time residential program is designed to enhance leadership capabilities.
*   **Executive Post Graduate Programme (Executive PGP):** A part-time program for working professionals, allowing them to pursue management education without interrupting their careers.
*   **Fellow Programme in Management (FPM):** A doctoral-level program for those aspiring to careers in academia or research.

## Admission Process and Selection Criteria

Admission to IIM Lucknow is highly competitive, based on a multi-stage selection process that evaluates academic performance, entrance exam scores, and personal attributes. The specific criteria vary slightly by program.

### General Admission Steps

1.  **Entrance Exam:** Candidates must appear for the relevant entrance examination (e.g., CAT for PGP, GMAT/CAT/XAT/GATE for Executive PGP, FPM).
2.  **Application:** Submit an online application through the official IIM Lucknow website or, for PGP, through the CAT application process.
3.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are shortlisted based on their entrance exam scores, academic record, work experience (where applicable), and diversity factors.
4.  **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates participate in Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds.
5.  **Final Offer:** Successful candidates receive an admission offer, which is confirmed upon payment of the admission fees.

### Program-Specific Selection Criteria

| Program                      | Selection Criteria                                                                        |
| :--------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| PGP / PGP-ABM / PGP-SM       | CAT Score + Written Ability Test (WAT) + Personal Interview (PI)                          |
| Executive PGP                | GMAT/CAT/XAT/GATE/PGP-WE Entrance Exam Score + WAT + PI                                   |
| FPM                          | CAT/GATE/GRE/GMAT/JRF/SRF (UGC/CSIR/ICAR) Score + Research Proposal + Interview           |
| IPMX                         | GMAT Score + Work Experience + Essay + Interview                                          |

## Fees Structure

The fee structure at IIM Lucknow is comprehensive and covers tuition, study material, library access, and other academic expenses. It's important to note that these figures can be subject to revision by the institute. For the most up-to-date information, always refer to the official IIM Lucknow website.

*   **Post Graduate Programme (PGP):** Approximately INR 20.8 Lakhs (for the entire duration)
*   **International Programme in Management for Executives (IPMX):** Approximately INR 26 Lakhs (for the entire duration)

*Note: Fees for other programs, including Executive PGP, PGP-ABM, PGP-SM, and FPM, are available on the institute's official website and vary based on program length and structure.* 

## Placement Opportunities

IIM Lucknow boasts an impressive track record for placements, with students securing roles in top companies across various sectors like consulting, finance, sales & marketing, IT & analytics, and general management. The institute consistently achieves high placement rates, reflecting the strong industry demand for its graduates.

The placement statistics for the 2025 batch highlight the excellent career prospects:

*   **Highest Domestic Package:** INR 75 LPA
*   **Highest International Package:** INR 65 LPA
