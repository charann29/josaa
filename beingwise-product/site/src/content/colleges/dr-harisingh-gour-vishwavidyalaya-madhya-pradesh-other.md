## Hostel Facilities and Fees

Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya provides comfortable hostel accommodation for both male and female students. The hostel fees comprise several components.

### Girls' Hostel Options

The university offers various girls' hostels with different room capacities:

*   **Nivedita Hostel I:** 133 rooms, accommodating 266 students (double occupancy)
*   **Nivedita Hostel II:** 72 rooms, accommodating 144 students (double occupancy)
*   **Rani Lakshmi Bai Kanya Hostel:** 193 rooms, accommodating 193 students (single occupancy)
*   **Saraswati Girls Hostel:** 48 rooms, accommodating 96 students (double occupancy)

### Hostel Fee Components for B.Sc. Students

For B.Sc. students, the hostel fees include:

*   **Registration Fee:** A one-time, non-refundable charge of INR 2000, payable during admission.
*   **Hostel Rent:** Approximately INR 5000 per month.
*   **Mess Fees:** Monthly charges covering breakfast, lunch, evening tea with snacks, and dinner. For the first month of admission and the last month of the semester, mess fees are calculated based on actual usage.

*Note: These fees are subject to change and are provided as an estimate based on information from official sources.*

### Government Job Prospects after B.Pharm

Graduates with a B.Pharm degree from Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya have several avenues in government sector jobs related to pharmaceuticals. These include:

*   **Drug Inspector:** Qualify for state or central government examinations.
*   **Pharmacist:** Work in government hospitals, clinics, and healthcare centers.
*   **Lecturer:** Pursue teaching roles in government colleges after clearing exams like NET.
*   **Research Officer:** Opportunities in government research institutions such as CSIR or ICMR.
