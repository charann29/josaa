Taxila Business School, located in Jaipur, offers a comprehensive Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) with a focus on Business Analytics and multiple specializations. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, entrance exam cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## PGDM Program Overview

Taxila Business School primarily offers a full-time PGDM program in Business Analytics with Triple Specialization. This program is designed to equip students with analytical skills and management expertise across various domains.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the PGDM program at Taxila Business School, applicants need to meet the following requirements:

*   **Educational Qualification:** A bachelor's degree in any discipline is mandatory. General category candidates must have scored at least 50% marks in their graduation, while reserved category candidates require a minimum of 45%.
*   **Final Year Students:** Those appearing for their final year bachelor's degree examinations are also eligible to apply.
*   **Entrance Exam Scores:** Applicants must have valid scores from any of the recognized management entrance exams such as CAT, CMAT, XAT, GMAT, MAT, or ATMA.
*   **Interview Performance:** Performance in a telephonic interview and a personal interview is also a crucial part of the selection process.

## Admission Process

Admission to the PGDM program at Taxila Business School involves several steps:

1.  **Application Submission:** Candidates must fill out the application form, providing their academic details and entrance exam scores.
2.  **Shortlisting:** Based on academic performance and entrance exam scores, candidates are shortlisted for the next stage.
3.  **Interview Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates will undergo a telephonic interview followed by a personal interview.
4.  **Final Selection:** The final selection is based on a cumulative assessment of academic record, entrance exam scores, and performance in the interview rounds.

## Fees Structure

The fee for the PGDM program at Taxila Business School is structured to cover tuition and other academic expenses. For specific and up-to-date fee details, it is always recommended to check the official website or contact the admissions office directly.

## Internships

The PGDM program at Taxila Business School includes two mandatory internships, providing valuable practical exposure:

*   **Winter Internship:** This typically takes place during January and February.
*   **Summer Internship:** This internship usually runs from July to August.
