Welcome to our guide on KL Business School (KLBS) in Guntur! This page will help you understand everything about its courses, fees, admission process, cutoff information, and placement opportunities, so you can make an informed decision for your management education.

## Overview of KL Business School

KL Business School is a part of KL University, a well-regarded institution in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh. The university holds an A+ grade accreditation from NAAC and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC). KLBS itself is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), ensuring its programs meet national standards. In 2024, KLBS was ranked 125th in the MBA category, reflecting its standing in the field of management education.

## Programs Offered and Fees

KLBS offers a range of management programs designed to prepare students for the dynamic business world. Here's a look at some of the key offerings and their associated fees:

### Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The BBA program at KLBS is comprehensive, with specializations in areas like Business Analytics, Global Finance with CMA, and Logistics and Supply Chain Management. This program is a strong foundation for a career in business.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The MBA program at KLBS offers various specializations, including a Global Program and Fintech. The total tuition fee for the MBA program generally ranges from INR 6.7 lakh to INR 11 lakh. This fee covers various components such as tuition, examination fees, and other charges. A portion of this fee is typically required at the time of admission to secure your seat.

## Admission Process and Cutoff

Admission to KL Business School is primarily based on entrance examination scores, ensuring a merit-based selection process. There is no direct admission available.

### For BBA and BCom (Hons)

Candidates applying for BBA and BCom (Hons) programs are required to take a written test conducted by the school.

### For MBA

For MBA admissions, KLBS accepts scores from national-level entrance exams such as CAT, MAT, XAT, or ICET. Alternatively, candidates can also appear for the KLMAT, an entrance exam conducted by KL University, for MBA admission.

## Placements at KL Business School

KL Business School has a commendable record for student placements, particularly for its MBA and BBA programs, often achieving a 100% placement rate. The school focuses on connecting students with leading companies across various sectors.
