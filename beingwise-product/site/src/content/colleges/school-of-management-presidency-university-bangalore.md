The School of Management at Presidency University, Bangalore, offers a range of management programs designed to prepare students for leadership roles in various industries. Situated in a vibrant city, the school focuses on providing quality education with practical insights.

## Programs Offered at Presidency University School of Management

The School of Management primarily offers a robust Master of Business Administration (MBA) program. This postgraduate degree is designed to equip students with essential business acumen, strategic thinking, and leadership capabilities.

### MBA Specializations

The MBA program at Presidency University's School of Management offers six distinct specializations, allowing students to focus on areas that align with their career aspirations. These specializations provide in-depth knowledge and skills relevant to specific industry sectors:

*   **Finance:** For those aspiring to careers in financial management, investment banking, or corporate finance.
*   **Marketing:** Ideal for students interested in branding, sales, market research, and consumer behavior.
*   **Human Resources:** Focuses on talent management, organizational development, and employee relations.
*   **Operations Management:** Covers supply chain, logistics, production, and quality management.
*   **Business Analytics:** Emphasizes data-driven decision-making, statistical modeling, and predictive analytics.
*   **Aviation Management:** A specialized stream for careers within the growing aviation industry, covering airline operations, airport management, and aviation business strategies.

## Fees Structure

The tuition fees for the MBA program at Presidency University's School of Management are competitively set. For the full program, the total tuition fee generally ranges from INR 5.3 Lakhs to INR 7 Lakhs. It's important to note that this figure typically covers tuition and may not include other expenses such as application fees, examination fees, or hostel charges.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the MBA program is based on a structured process that evaluates a candidate's academic background and aptitude. Prospective students need to fulfill specific eligibility criteria:

*   **Academic Qualification:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university with a minimum aggregate score, typically 50% (45% for reserved categories).
*   **Entrance Exams:** Candidates are required to appear for and secure a valid score in national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, ATMA, or Karnataka PGCET. The university also conducts its own entrance test, the Presidency University Aptitude Test (PUAT), for those who haven't taken other exams.
*   **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates will typically undergo a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round, which assesses their communication skills, general awareness, and suitability for the program.

## Cutoff Information

The cutoff for admission to the MBA program varies each year based on factors like the number of applicants, the difficulty of the entrance exams, and the overall performance of candidates. While specific cutoff scores are released annually, a strong performance in any of the accepted entrance exams significantly improves admission chances. Generally, competitive scores in CAT or MAT are considered for admission.

## Placement Opportunities

Presidency University's School of Management has a dedicated career services cell that assists students with placements. The school aims to provide good career opportunities across various sectors. MBA graduates from the School of Management are sought after for roles in different industries, including:

*   Banking and Financial Services
*   Consulting
*   Information Technology
*   Marketing and Sales
*   Human Resources
*   Logistics and Supply Chain
*   Aviation

Common job profiles that graduates secure include Brand Manager, Account Manager, International Business Manager, Marketing Executive, and Business Technical Consultant. The school maintains relationships with various companies to facilitate internships and final placements for its students.

## Why Choose Presidency University School of Management?

Choosing the School of Management at Presidency University offers several advantages, including:

*   **Diverse Specializations:** A wide array of MBA specializations, including a unique Aviation Management program.
*   **Affordable Education:** Compared to some other institutions, the fee structure is more accessible while maintaining quality.
*   **High Seat Intake:** With a substantial number of seats available for the MBA program, there are generally more opportunities for admission.
*   **Industry Focus:** Programs are designed to be industry-relevant, preparing students for real-world challenges.

The School of Management at Presidency University, Bangalore, strives to be a preferred destination for management education, offering a blend of academic rigor and practical exposure to shape future business leaders.
