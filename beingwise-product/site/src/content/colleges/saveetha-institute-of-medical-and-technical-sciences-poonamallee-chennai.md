Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences (SIMATS), located in Poonamallee, Chennai, is a well-known institution offering a variety of programs in medical, dental, and technical fields. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, admission process, fees, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Courses Offered at SIMATS

SIMATS focuses primarily on health sciences and engineering disciplines. Here are some of its prominent offerings:

*   **MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery):** A highly regarded program known for its comprehensive curriculum and extensive clinical exposure through its affiliated hospital. It employs a credit-based and problem-based learning approach.
*   **BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery):** Considered one of the top dental programs in India, emphasizing practical knowledge and training in advanced dental procedures with experienced faculty.
*   **MDS (Master of Dental Surgery):** A respected postgraduate dental program, featuring modern dentistry equipment and specialized instrumentation labs.
*   **B.Tech in Biotechnology:** A well-regarded engineering program focusing on genetic engineering and industrial process engineering.
*   **B.Tech in Computer Science:** Another key engineering offering.
*   **B.Sc. Nursing:** A program designed to train future nursing professionals.
*   **BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy):** A program in the allied health sciences.

It's important to note that while some sources might mention an MBA, the institute's primary focus and strengths lie in its medical, dental, and core engineering programs. Information regarding an MBA program and its placements is not readily available or highlighted as a core offering by the institution.

## Admission Process

Admission to SIMATS Chennai is a blend of entrance examination performance and academic merit. Here’s a general outline:

*   **Undergraduate Courses:** For most UG programs, candidates must have completed Class 12 or an equivalent examination from a recognized board with at least 50% aggregate marks.
*   **NEET-UG:** For MBBS and BDS courses, appearing for and clearing the NEET-UG examination conducted by NTA is mandatory.
*   **Engineering (B.Tech):** Admissions are typically based on scores from exams like TNEA.
*   **Postgraduate Engineering (M.Tech):** Admissions often consider GATE scores.
*   **Postgraduate Management (if applicable):** While not a primary focus, if an MBA program were offered, TANCET scores would likely be considered.

Cutoff marks, especially for medical and dental programs (MBBS, BDS), are determined by NEET-UG scores and vary each year based on factors like the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. ## Placements

SIMATS has a strong reputation for placements within its core disciplines, particularly medicine and dentistry. Graduates from MBBS and BDS programs are well-prepared for clinical practice and further specialization. The institution's affiliated hospital provides excellent opportunities for practical experience, which aids in career progression.

For engineering programs like B.Tech in Biotechnology and Computer Science, students are trained with relevant industry skills.
