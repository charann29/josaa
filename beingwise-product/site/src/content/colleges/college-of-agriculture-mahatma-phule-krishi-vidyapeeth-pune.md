The College of Agriculture, a part of Mahatma Phule Krishi Vidyapeeth in Pune, is a well-regarded institution for those looking to build a career in agricultural sciences and related fields. This guide provides essential information about its programs, admission process, and campus life.

## Courses Offered

The college provides a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in agriculture and allied sectors. The main streams available are Science and Business & Management Studies.

### Undergraduate Program

*   **B.Sc. (Agriculture)**: This four-year program focuses on various aspects of agricultural science, preparing students for diverse roles in the industry.

### Postgraduate Programs

*   **M.Sc. (Agriculture)**: Specializations are available in areas like Horticulture and other agricultural disciplines, offering advanced study and research opportunities.
*   **MBA/PGDM (Agriculture & Food Business)**: This program is tailored for students interested in the business side of agriculture, food processing, and related management roles.

## Eligibility Criteria

Admission to the College of Agriculture, MPKV, Pune, is based on specific academic qualifications for each program.

### For B.Sc. (Agriculture)

*   Candidates must have passed their 12th standard or an equivalent examination from the Maharashtra State Board of Higher Secondary Education.
*   Required subjects include Physics, Chemistry, English, and either Mathematics or Biology. Students who have studied Inter Agriculture are also eligible.
*   Those who haven't studied Biology or Mathematics in their qualifying exam may need to complete deficiency courses.
*   Physically handicapped candidates are eligible with a minimum aggregate of 40% marks.

### For M.Sc. Programs

*   Applicants typically need a Bachelor's degree in a relevant agricultural field. For admissions through ICAR AIEEA (PG), a four-year Bachelor's degree is generally required, though some exceptions exist for specific courses at certain ICAR-Deemed Universities.
*   Candidates must be at least 19 years old as of August 31st of the admission year.

### For MBA/PGDM (Agriculture & Food Business)

*   Eligibility extends to graduates with a four-year degree in various agricultural and allied fields, including B.Sc. (Agri./Hons. Agri.), B.Sc. (Hort./Hons. Hort.), B.Sc. (Forestry/Hons. Forestry), B.Sc. (Agri. Bio-Tech.), B.Tech. (Biotechnology), B.B.M. (Agri.), B.Sc. (A.B.M.), B.Sc. (Hons.) Agri. Business Management, B.B.A. (Agriculture), B.Tech. (Agril. Engg.), B.Tech. (Food Tech./Food Technology/Food Science), B.Sc. (Hons.) Home Science/Community Science, or B.F.Sc.
*   Graduates from other states must hold a degree from an ICAR-accredited Agricultural University.
