The CII School of Logistics, part of Amity University Kolkata, specializes in preparing students for careers in the dynamic logistics and supply chain sector. This page provides a clear overview of its postgraduate programs, admission criteria, fee structure, and career prospects.

## Courses Offered

The CII School of Logistics focuses on a single, specialized postgraduate management program: the Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Logistics and Supply Chain Management. This program is designed to equip students with comprehensive knowledge and practical skills essential for managing complex supply chains.

## Admission Process

Admission to the MBA program at CII School of Logistics is competitive and based on entrance examination scores. Here’s a breakdown of the eligibility and selection process:

### Eligibility Criteria

*   Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in their Class 10, Class 12, and graduation examinations.

### Selection Process

*   The institute accepts scores from several national-level management entrance exams, including CAT, MAT, GMAT, and XAT.
*   Alternatively, candidates can also take the entrance examination conducted directly at the Amity Kolkata Campus.

## Fees Structure

Specific fee details for the MBA program are best confirmed directly with the admissions office. However, Amity University's fee structure generally reflects its comprehensive curriculum and facilities.

## Scholarships

CII School of Logistics, through Amity University, offers scholarships to deserving students based on their academic performance in qualifying examinations. These scholarships can significantly reduce the financial burden of higher education.

Scholarship percentages are awarded based on a combination of Class 12 board results and graduation marks:

*   **100% Scholarship:** Awarded to students with an aggregate of 95% or above in CBSE/ISC Class 12 and 80% in Graduation.
*   **50% Scholarship:** Awarded to students with an aggregate between 90% and 94.9% in CBSE/ISC Class 12 and 75% in Graduation.
*   **20% Scholarship:** Awarded to students with an aggregate of 90% or above in State Board Class 12 and 75% in Graduation.
