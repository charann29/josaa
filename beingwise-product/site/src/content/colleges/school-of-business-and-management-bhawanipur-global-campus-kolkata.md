The School of Business and Management at Bhawanipur Global Campus, Kolkata, offers a range of programs for students aspiring to build careers in business and management. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities.

## Courses Offered

Bhawanipur Global Campus provides various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Business and Management Studies. These courses are designed to equip students with essential skills and knowledge for the corporate world.

Some of the prominent programs include:

*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)**
*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA)**
*   **Master of Hospital Administration (MHA)**
*   **Executive MBA (E-MBA)**

The campus emphasizes practical learning and industry relevance, with its courses approved by AICTE. It also boasts international recognition and collaborations with various organizations like IBM, NISM Partnership, and the National Entrepreneurship Network.

## Eligibility Criteria

Admission requirements vary depending on the specific program you're interested in. Generally, candidates need to meet the following criteria:

*   **For Undergraduate Programs (e.g., BBA):** Applicants must have successfully completed Class 12 from a recognized educational board.
*   **For Postgraduate Programs (e.g., MBA, MHA):** A bachelor's degree from a recognized university is mandatory.
*   **For Executive MBA (E-MBA):** In addition to a graduate degree, a minimum of two years of relevant work experience is required.

Beyond these academic qualifications, candidates must also successfully clear the selection rounds for final admission.

## Admission Process

The application process for the School of Business and Management, Bhawanipur Global Campus, is straightforward and primarily conducted online. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official website of the School of Business and Management, Bhawanipur Global Campus.
2.  **Locate 'Apply Now':** Click on the 'Apply Now' tab, usually found prominently on the homepage, and select the appropriate campus.
3.  **Register Online:** Provide the necessary details to create an online account.
4.  **Complete Application Form:** Log in using your registered credentials and meticulously fill out the application form with all required information.
5.  **Pay Application Fees:** Submit the prescribed application fee through the available online payment options.
6.  **Submit Form:** Review all details carefully and then submit your application form.

For MBA admissions, candidates are also required to appear for a management entrance examination such as MAT, XAT, CAT, CMAT, or JEMAT. A minimum aggregate of 50% in graduation is generally expected for MBA applicants.

## Fee Structure

While specific fee details for each course are best obtained directly from the institution's official channels, the School of Business and Management aims to provide quality education with a competitive fee structure. Prospective students are encouraged to contact the admissions office or visit the official website for the most current fee information for their chosen program.
