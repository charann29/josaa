Embarking on a postgraduate journey requires careful planning, and the University of Rajasthan Admission Test for Postgraduate (URAT PG) is your gateway to various master's programs at the university. This guide provides a clear overview of the URAT PG 2026 examination, covering essential details like courses offered, fee structure, admission procedure, expected cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Understanding URAT PG 2026

The URAT PG is an annual entrance examination conducted by the University of Rajasthan itself. It serves as the primary selection tool for admission to a wide array of postgraduate courses across different faculties. The university's official website, uniraj.ac.in, is the definitive source for all important announcements and updates regarding the exam.

### Courses Offered Through URAT PG

The URAT PG exam facilitates admissions to numerous postgraduate programs. While specific course offerings can vary slightly year-to-year, candidates typically find options in fields such as Arts, Science, Commerce, Law, and Management. Prospective students should check the official university prospectus for the complete list of available master's degrees for the 2026 academic session.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for URAT PG 2026, candidates must meet specific academic requirements:

*   **General Category:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree with a minimum aggregate of 55% marks.
*   **Reserved Categories (SC/ST/PH):** Candidates belonging to Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), or Physically Handicapped (PH) categories require at least 50% aggregate marks in their qualifying undergraduate degree.
*   Beyond these general criteria, individual courses may have additional specific prerequisites. It is crucial to review the detailed eligibility for your chosen program on the university website.

### Application Process for URAT PG 2026

The application process for URAT PG is entirely online. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Navigate to uniraj.ac.in.
2.  **Registration:** Locate the online application registration link. You'll need to sign up by providing details such as your name, email ID, and creating a password.
3.  **Declaration and Submission:** Read and agree to the candidate and parent declarations, then click 'Submit'.
4.  **Receive Login Credentials:** Upon successful registration, you will receive a unique login ID and password.
5.  **Log In and Fill Form:** Use these credentials to log in to the application portal. Carefully fill in all required personal and academic details.
6.  **Fee Payment:** Pay the application fee using an online method (Credit/Debit card or Internet Banking).
7.  **Document Upload:** You will need to upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and other necessary documents. Ensure these are correctly sized and formatted as per the instructions.

It is strongly advised to complete the application using a desktop computer or laptop, as resizing and uploading documents can be challenging on a mobile phone.

### Examination Mode

The URAT PG examination is conducted in an **offline, pen-and-paper mode**. Candidates will need to mark their answers on an OMR sheet. The exam is held across multiple cities within Rajasthan.

Cutoff scores for URAT PG admissions are influenced by several factors, including the total number of applicants, the difficulty level of the exam, and the number of available seats for each program.
