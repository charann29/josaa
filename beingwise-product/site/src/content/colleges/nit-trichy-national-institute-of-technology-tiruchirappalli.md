NIT Trichy, officially known as the National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli, is a highly respected engineering institution located in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu. It's a popular choice for students aiming for quality technical education in India.

## Courses Offered at NIT Trichy

NIT Trichy provides a wide array of undergraduate (B.Tech), postgraduate (M.Tech, MCA, MBA, M.Sc), and doctoral programs. The B.Tech specializations are particularly sought after, covering various engineering disciplines.

### B.Tech Programs and Seat Intake

For students aspiring to pursue a Bachelor of Technology, NIT Trichy offers diverse branches. The total intake for all B.Tech programs is 942 students. Here’s a breakdown of seats per specialization:

*   **Computer Science and Engineering (CSE):** 119 seats
*   **Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE):** 117 seats
*   **Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE):** 116 seats
*   **Mechanical Engineering:** 118 seats
*   **Civil Engineering:** 118 seats
*   **Production Engineering:** 118 seats
*   **Instrumentation and Control Engineering:** 117 seats
*   **Metallurgical and Materials Engineering:** 79 seats
*   **Chemical Engineering:** 40 seats

### MCA Program

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a three-year postgraduate program with an intake of 115 students. Admission to this course is based on the NIMCET examination and subsequent centralized counseling.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to B.Tech programs at NIT Trichy primarily relies on performance in the JEE Main examination, followed by JoSAA counseling. Securing a competitive rank is crucial for admission.

## Placement Highlights at NIT Trichy

NIT Trichy has a strong placement record, attracting numerous top companies across various sectors. The institute facilitates robust career opportunities for its students.

### Overall Placement Packages (2024-2025)

*   **MCA (2025):** Highest package recorded was INR 64.3 LPA, with an average package of INR 13.16 LPA.
*   **B.Tech (2024):** The highest package offered was INR 64 LPA, while the lowest was INR 5 LPA.
*   **M.Tech (2024):** Highest package reached INR 52.89 LPA, and the lowest was INR 3 LPA.
*   **MBA (2024):** The highest package stood at INR 19 LPA.
*   **M.Sc (2024):** The highest package was INR 52.89 LPA.

## Campus Facilities and Transport

NIT Trichy's campus is well-equipped with various facilities, including a dedicated transport committee to ensure smooth movement for students and staff.
