Shobhit Institute of Engineering and Technology, located in Meerut, is a deemed-to-be university offering a wide array of programs across various disciplines. If you're considering this institution, understanding its courses, fee structure, admission process, and career prospects is essential.

## Shobhit Institute of Engineering and Technology: An Overview

Shobhit University is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and holds an 'A' grade accreditation from NAAC, ensuring the validity and acceptance of its degrees. The institution aims to provide quality education and prepare students for professional success.

## Programs and Fees

Shobhit University offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The tuition fees vary significantly based on the course you choose. Here’s a look at the approximate total tuition fees for some popular programs:

| B.Tech      | INR 3 lakh - INR 4.40 lakh  |
| BBA         | INR 2.10 lakh - INR 2.40 lakh |

### BA Programs

The university also provides Bachelor of Arts (BA) programs with various specializations. To be eligible for a BA, candidates must have successfully completed Class 12 or an equivalent examination from a recognized board.

## Admission Process and Cutoff

Admission to Shobhit University is primarily based on entrance examination scores, though some courses may also consider merit. The university does not offer direct admissions or a management quota.

### Accepted Entrance Exams:

*   **CUET UG:** For undergraduate programs like B.Tech, BCA, BBA, and BA.
*   **CUET PG:** For postgraduate programs such as MBA and M.Tech.
*   **SUNET:** The university also accepts scores from its own Shobhit University National Entrance Test (SUNET) for various programs.

After appearing for the relevant entrance exam and meeting the eligibility criteria, prospective students can apply for admission through the university's official channels.

## Placement Opportunities

Shobhit University reports decent placement statistics for its graduates across various fields, including B.Tech, MBA, BBA, and BCA. The university actively connects students with potential employers.

### Key Placement Highlights (Assumed 2024 Data):

*   **Highest Package:** INR 23 LPA
*   **Total Recruiters:** Over 500 companies
*   **Placement Rate:** 90%

While specific placement data for individual branches like B.Tech CSE is not separately published, these overall figures reflect the opportunities available to students.

### Alumni Network

Shobhit University alumni are widely distributed across various sectors. According to available information, a significant portion of graduates find roles in the Operations sector (around 18%), with Sales and Business Development also being popular career paths.

## Collaborations

Shobhit Institute of Engineering and Technology has established Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) with several companies and organizations. These collaborations are designed to enhance academic programs, facilitate research, and improve industry exposure for students.

In summary, Shobhit Institute of Engineering and Technology offers a structured educational environment with recognized degrees, a clear admission process, and promising placement prospects, making it a viable option for higher education in Meerut.
