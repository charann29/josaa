The Vijay Patil School of Management (VPSM) in Navi Mumbai is a well-known institution offering a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. If you're considering higher education in management or related fields, understanding their courses, fees, admission process, and career prospects is essential.

## Courses Offered at VPSM

VPSM provides a range of programs designed to equip students with the skills needed for today's dynamic business world. Their offerings include popular choices like the Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), alongside specialized Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) degrees.

### Undergraduate Programs

*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** A three-year program focusing on foundational business knowledge and management skills.
*   **Bachelor of Science (B.Sc):** Specific specializations within the B.Sc program are available, designed to provide in-depth knowledge in particular fields.

### Postgraduate Programs

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** A flagship program that delves into advanced management concepts and leadership development.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admissions for various courses at VPSM are currently open, with selection criteria varying by program. Prospective students can apply online through the university's official website.

### MBA Admission

For the MBA program, admission is primarily based on scores obtained in relevant entrance examinations. Candidates must meet the specific eligibility requirements for the course.

### BBA Admission

To be eligible for the BBA program, applicants must have completed Class 12 with a minimum aggregate of 75%. A concession is provided for reserved categories, requiring a minimum of 70% aggregate. Selection for BBA is merit-based, evaluating academic performance in Class 12.

### B.Sc Admission

Admission to B.Sc courses at VPSM is merit-based. Students need to have completed Class 12 with a minimum aggregate of 50%. The application process involves visiting the official website, completing the online form, and ensuring eligibility. Upon selection, a fee payment is required to confirm the seat.

## Fees Structure

Specific fee details for each course are typically available on the official VPSM website or during the application process. It's advisable to check the latest fee structure directly from the institution for the most accurate information.
