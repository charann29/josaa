IIM Ranchi, established in 2010, is a prominent management institution located in Jharkhand. It offers a range of postgraduate and integrated programs designed to cultivate future business leaders. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fees, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## IIM Ranchi: Programs Offered

IIM Ranchi provides several sought-after management programs, catering to different academic and professional backgrounds. These include full-time MBA programs, an Executive MBA for experienced professionals, and an Integrated Program in Management (IPM) for students right after Class 12.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

This is the flagship postgraduate program, preparing students for diverse roles in general management. IIM Ranchi also offers specialized MBA programs in Human Resources (MBA-HR) and Business Analytics, reflecting the evolving demands of the industry.

### Executive MBA

Designed for working professionals, this program helps enhance leadership and management skills without interrupting their careers. It focuses on practical applications and strategic thinking.

### Integrated Program in Management (IPM)

This is a unique five-year program for students who have completed Class 12. It combines undergraduate studies (BBA) with postgraduate management education (MBA), culminating in an MBA degree. The curriculum is broad, covering humanities, social sciences, arts, sciences, and a core business foundation in areas like marketing, finance, economics, and data science.

## IIM Ranchi: Admission Process

Admission to IIM Ranchi's programs is competitive and typically involves entrance examinations followed by personal interviews.

### MBA Admission

Prospective students for the MBA, MBA-HR, and MBA in Business Analytics programs must take the Common Admission Test (CAT). Candidates need to meet both the overall and sectional cutoff scores in the CAT to be considered for the next stage. Shortlisted candidates are then invited for Personal Interviews (PI).

### Executive MBA Admission

Admission to the Executive MBA program is based solely on a Personal Interview. Applicants must have a minimum of five years of full-time work experience to be eligible.

### IPM Admission

For the Integrated Program in Management, candidates need to appear for the IPMAT examination. Following this, shortlisted candidates participate in a virtual Personal Interview round conducted by IIM Ranchi.

## IIM Ranchi: Selection Criteria

The final selection for most programs is a multi-stage process, weighing various components:

| Course             | Selection Criteria                               |
| :----------------- | :----------------------------------------------- |
| MBA                | CAT Score + Personal Interview                   |
| Executive MBA      | Personal Interview                               |
| IPM                | IPMAT Score + Personal Interview                 |

For the MBA program, the final admission offer is based on a composite score:

*   **CAT Score:** 40%
*   **Personal Interview (PI) Score:** 30%
*   **Profile (Academic record, work experience, diversity):** 30%

## IIM Ranchi: Cutoff

Meeting the cutoff scores in the respective entrance exams is a crucial step for admission.

### CAT Cutoff for MBA

For MBA programs, candidates must achieve a minimum sectional percentile of 75% in each of the three CAT sections: Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC), Data Interpretation and Logical Reasoning (DILR), and Quantitative Aptitude (QUANT). The expected overall qualifying CAT percentile for IIM Ranchi MBA admissions is typically around 93 percentile for the general category. Historically, the final CAT cutoff for the General AI category has been around 94 percentile overall and sectionally.

## IIM Ranchi: Fees

The fee structure varies by program. It's important to note that fees can be subject to revision.

### Integrated Program in Management (IPM) Fees

The total fees for the first three years of the IPM program are approximately INR 16 Lakh. The fees for the subsequent two years (the MBA component) will be equivalent to the MBA program fees set for that academic year.

## IIM Ranchi: Placements

IIM Ranchi has a strong track record of placements, attracting leading companies across various sectors. The institute's dedicated placement cell works to ensure students secure desirable roles, reflecting the quality of education and industry relevance of its programs.

## Life at IIM Ranchi

Beyond academics, IIM Ranchi offers a vibrant campus life with numerous student clubs, committees, and events. These activities provide opportunities for holistic development, networking, and leadership experience, complementing the rigorous academic curriculum and preparing students for the corporate world.
