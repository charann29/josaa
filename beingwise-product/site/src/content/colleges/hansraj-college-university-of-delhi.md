Hansraj College, a prominent institution affiliated with the University of Delhi, is a highly sought-after destination for higher education in India. This guide offers a comprehensive overview of its academic programs, fee structure, admission procedures, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities.

## Courses Offered at Hansraj College

Hansraj College provides a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. Students can pursue degrees in Arts, Science, and Commerce streams, tailored to meet evolving academic and professional demands. Some popular courses include:

*   B.A. (Hons.) in Economics, English, History, Political Science, Sanskrit
*   B.Sc. (Hons.) in Chemistry, Computer Science, Electronics, Mathematics, Physics, Zoology, Botany
*   B.Com. (Hons.)
*   M.A. in English, Hindi, History, Sanskrit
*   M.Sc. in Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics, Zoology, Botany

## Fee Structure

The fees at Hansraj College vary depending on the course and category of admission. Generally, the annual fees for undergraduate programs are quite affordable, making quality education accessible to a broad spectrum of students. Postgraduate course fees are also structured to be reasonable. For precise figures, it's always best to consult the official college prospectus or website as they can be subject to minor adjustments each academic year.

## Admission Process

Admission to Hansraj College for most undergraduate courses is primarily based on scores obtained in the Common University Entrance Test (CUET). For postgraduate programs, admission often involves a combination of entrance exam scores (like CUET PG or DUET, depending on the course) and academic merit. The process typically involves:

1.  **CUET Examination:** Appearing for the CUET exam is mandatory for undergraduate admissions.
2.  **Online Application:** Candidates need to register and apply online through the University of Delhi's admission portal, indicating Hansraj College as their preferred choice.
3.  **Merit List & Allotment:** Based on CUET scores and other eligibility criteria, merit lists are released, followed by seat allocation.
4.  **Document Verification:** Selected candidates must complete the document verification process and pay the admission fees to secure their seat.

### CUET Registration

Registration for the CUET exam typically opens early in the year on the official NTA website (cuet.nta.nic.in). Prospective students should regularly check the NTA website for notification releases and application deadlines. It's crucial to meet the eligibility criteria before applying.

## Cutoff Scores

Hansraj College is renowned for its academic excellence, leading to competitive cutoff scores for admission. These cutoffs are determined by factors such as the number of applicants, seat availability, and the overall performance of students in the CUET exam. Historically, the cutoffs for popular courses like B.Com (Hons.), B.A. (Hons.) Economics, and B.Sc. (Hons.) Physics have been quite high, often exceeding 95% in initial rounds. Aspiring students should aim for exceptionally strong CUET scores to enhance their chances of admission.
