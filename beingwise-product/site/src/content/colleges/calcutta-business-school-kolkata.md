Calcutta Business School (CBS) is a well-known institution in Kolkata, offering various management programs designed to equip students for the corporate world. If you're considering a career in business and management, understanding CBS's offerings is a great first step.

## Courses Offered at Calcutta Business School

CBS provides a range of programs tailored for different career stages and aspirations. Their flagship offerings include:

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** This is a comprehensive program equivalent to an MBA, focusing on practical skills and industry relevance.
*   **Executive MBA/PGDM:** Designed for working professionals looking to enhance their management skills without interrupting their careers.
*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** A foundational undergraduate program for students aiming for an early start in business management.

## Fees Structure

Exact fee details can vary by academic year and program. It's always best to check the official CBS website for the most current information. Generally, fees for PGDM programs at institutions like CBS cover tuition, study materials, and access to campus facilities. Compared to other top-tier business schools, CBS aims to offer competitive fee structures.

## Admission Process

Admission to Calcutta Business School is typically a multi-stage process, ensuring they select candidates with strong academic backgrounds and leadership potential.

1.  **Application:** Candidates must complete an online application form, providing academic details, work experience (if any), and other relevant information.
2.  **Entrance Exam:** For PGDM programs, applicants usually need to have a valid score from national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, or ATMA.
3.  **Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates are invited for a Group Discussion to assess their communication and teamwork skills, followed by a Personal Interview to evaluate their aptitude, motivation, and personality.
4.  **Final Selection:** The final selection is based on a composite score, considering academic performance, entrance exam scores, work experience, and performance in the GD/PI rounds.

## Cutoff Information

While specific cutoffs can fluctuate each year based on the applicant pool and exam difficulty, CBS generally considers scores from prominent management entrance tests. Candidates with competitive scores in CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, or ATMA will have a better chance of being shortlisted for the GD/PI rounds. Aiming for a strong percentile in any of these exams is advisable for aspiring students.

## Placements at Calcutta Business School

Calcutta Business School has a strong track record of placing its graduates in reputable companies across various sectors. The placement cell at CBS works diligently to connect students with leading recruiters, providing career guidance and training.

CBS reported impressive placement statistics for its 2023 graduating batch, including both domestic and international opportunities.

Over 1000 job offers were extended to students in 2023, reflecting strong industry demand for CBS graduates.

### Top Recruiters

Leading companies from various sectors actively recruit from Calcutta Business School. Some of the prominent recruiters in 2023 included:

*   Property Pistol
*   Flipkart

*   TCS

These organizations offer roles in diverse domains such as marketing, finance, human resources, and operations.

### Alumni Network and Sector-wise Placements

CBS alumni are well-distributed across various industries. A significant portion of graduates find roles in:

*   Sales (36%)
*   Business Development (22%)
*   Human Resources (17%)

This distribution indicates the versatility of the skills gained at CBS and the wide range of career paths available to its graduates.

### Summer Internships

Calcutta Business School also facilitates robust summer internship programs, providing students with practical industry exposure. In 2022, the highest stipend offered during summer internships was INR 20,000 per month, with opportunities available in sectors like Marketing, Finance, and Operations.
