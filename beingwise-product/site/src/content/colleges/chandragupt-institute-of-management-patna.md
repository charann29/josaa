Chandragupt Institute of Management, Patna (CIMP) is a well-regarded institution in Bihar, dedicated to fostering management talent. Established with a vision to provide quality education, CIMP offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) and a Fellow Programme in Management (FPM), both approved by the AICTE.

## Programs Offered at CIMP

CIMP primarily focuses on its flagship Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program, which is highly sought after. They also offer a Fellow Programme in Management (FPM) for those interested in deeper research and academia.

### PGDM Program

The PGDM is a full-time, two-year management program designed to equip students with essential business knowledge and leadership skills. It's considered the institute's core offering and has a strong reputation, particularly for its placement outcomes.

### FPM Program

The Fellow Programme in Management (FPM) is a doctoral-level program for individuals aspiring to excel in management research and teaching. It prepares scholars for roles in academia and industry research.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to CIMP's programs is competitive and based on a combination of entrance exam scores, academic performance, and an interview process. The institute aims to select candidates with strong analytical abilities and a passion for management.

### PGDM Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the PGDM program, candidates must have a bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for reserved categories). Final-year graduation students are also welcome to apply. A valid score from one of the following management entrance exams is mandatory:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)

Following the entrance exam, shortlisted candidates will typically undergo a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round.

### Total Intake

CIMP has an approved intake of 140 students across all its programs, ensuring a focused learning environment.

## Fee Structure

The fee structure for CIMP's programs is designed to be accessible while covering the costs of quality education and infrastructure. While specific figures can vary, the median salary for the PGDM course has been reported around INR 5,00,000 per annum.

CIMP offers a vibrant campus environment with modern facilities to support a holistic learning experience. Students have rated the infrastructure highly, reflecting the quality of amenities available.

*   **Auditorium:** For events, seminars, and guest lectures.
*   **Hostels:** Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls, with mandatory hostel accommodation for students.
*   **Cafeteria:** Providing nutritious meals and snacks.
*   **Gym:** For fitness and well-being.
*   **Library:** A rich collection of books, journals, and digital resources.
*   **Labs:** Equipped with necessary software and hardware for practical learning.
