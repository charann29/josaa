IIEBM Indus Business School in Pune is a well-regarded institution offering management programs. For students and parents considering higher education in business, understanding the various aspects of a college is crucial. This page provides a clear overview of IIEBM's courses, fee structure, admission process, selection cutoffs, and placement records.

## About IIEBM Indus Business School Pune

Established in 2000 by Col. Vinod Marwaha and Shrimant Vijay Sinha Naik Nimbalkar (Shivajiraje), IIEBM is a private institution. Over the years, it has built a reputation for its management education and has forged alliances with several prominent national and international organizations and institutions, including the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

## Courses Offered and Fees

IIEBM primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs. The flagship offering is the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM). They also offer a specialized Post Graduate Program in Enterprise Resource Planning (PGP-ERP).

While specific fee details for all courses are best confirmed directly with the institution, it's important to budget for tuition and other expenses. For students opting for on-campus accommodation, the hostel charges for the PGDM course are approximately INR 3.4 lakhs. Please note that fee structures can be revised by the institute.

## Admission Process

Admissions at IIEBM are primarily conducted online and are based on entrance exam scores followed by interview rounds. Here's a general guide to the application process:

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission to most courses, candidates generally need a graduate degree from a recognized university with an aggregate score of 55-60%. For the PGP-ERP program, a graduation degree with an IT background and at least 55% aggregate marks is typically required.

### Application Steps

1.  **Visit the Website:** Go to the official IIEBM Pune website.
2.  **Register:** Create an account by providing your basic personal details.
3.  **Login:** Use your new credentials to log in.
4.  **Apply Now:** Click on the 'Apply Now' section.
5.  **Fill and Submit:** Complete the application form with all necessary information and submit it online. While online application is preferred, you can contact the institute for information on offline application methods.

### Required Documents

When applying, have scanned copies of the following documents ready:

*   Class 10th mark sheet
*   Class 12th mark sheet
*   Graduation mark sheets
*   Leaving Certificate
*   Aadhaar Card
*   Passport-size photograph
*   Signature

### Selection Process

IIEBM accepts scores from various national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, MAT, XAT, ATMA, and GMAT for its PGDM program. After meeting the eligibility criteria and submitting exam scores, candidates must participate in an interview and assessment rounds conducted by the college. Final admission is granted based on performance in these rounds.

## Cutoffs

Specific cutoff scores for entrance exams vary each year based on the applicant pool and program demand. Candidates are advised to perform well in their chosen entrance exams to maximize their chances of selection. The interview and assessment rounds also play a significant role in the final selection.
