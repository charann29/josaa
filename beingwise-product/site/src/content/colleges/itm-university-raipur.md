ITM University, located in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide range of academic programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fees, admission process, and placement opportunities to help prospective students and their families make informed decisions.

ITM University provides diverse undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) courses across various disciplines. Here's a look at the approximate fee structure for some popular undergraduate programs:

## Admission Process

ITM University, Raipur offers both direct admission and admission based on entrance exam scores for its UG and PG programs. 

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **For Undergraduate (UG) Courses:** Candidates must have completed Class 12 from a recognized board.
*   **For Postgraduate (PG) Courses:** A bachelor's degree from a recognized university is required.

### Application Steps

1.  **Check Eligibility:** Ensure you meet the specific eligibility requirements for your chosen course.
2.  **Apply Online:** Visit the official ITM University, Raipur website and complete the online application form for your preferred program.
3.  **Selection:** Admission can be based on merit in the qualifying examination or through entrance exam scores, depending on the course.

## MBA Program at ITM University, Raipur

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is one of the flagship programs at ITM University, Raipur. It is a two-year, four-semester program with specializations available in:

*   Marketing
*   Finance
*   Human Resource

### MBA Eligibility and Selection

*   **Eligibility:** A bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks from an AIU-recognized university.
*   **Selection:** Admission is based on scores from national-level entrance exams or the university's own entrance exam.

### Accepted Entrance Exams for MBA

ITM University, Raipur accepts scores from the following management entrance exams:

*   CAT
*   XAT
*   MAT
*   CMAT
*   State CETs
*   ITM University Entrance Exam (for candidates without valid CET scores)

## Placements at ITM University, Raipur

ITM University has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with potential employers. The university has a commendable track record for placements, especially for its MBA program.
