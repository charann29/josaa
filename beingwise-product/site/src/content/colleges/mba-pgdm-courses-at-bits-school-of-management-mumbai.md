Embarking on an MBA or PGDM journey is a significant step, and the BITS School of Management (BITSoM) in Mumbai offers a program designed to prepare you for leadership roles. This page provides a clear overview of their courses, fee structure, admission process, selection criteria, and career opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

BITSoM primarily offers a full-time MBA program, carefully crafted to equip students with essential business acumen and leadership skills. For students currently pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering (BE) at BITS Pilani, there's a unique opportunity to combine their undergraduate studies with an MBA. This integrated five-year BE+MBA dual degree program allows second-year BE students from BITS Pilani to transition into the BITSoM MBA curriculum.

## Fee Structure

The total fee for the full-time MBA program at BITSoM for the academic year 2026 is approximately INR 28.55 Lakhs. This comprehensive amount covers various components, including tuition fees, a one-time admission fee, and a refundable accommodation security deposit. It's important to note that the first year's installment will be slightly higher due to the inclusion of these one-time payments. For the academic year 2024, the total program fee was INR 27.2 Lakhs.

Should you need to withdraw your admission, BITSoM follows University Grants Commission (UGC) guidelines regarding refunds. Generally, refunds are processed for fees paid if the admission cancellation occurs within 30 days of the course commencement.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the MBA program at BITSoM is a competitive process and direct admissions are not available. Prospective students must clear specific entrance examinations and subsequent interview rounds.

### Entrance Exams

To be considered for the MBA program, applicants must submit a valid score from one of the following standardized tests:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)
*   GMAT Focus Edition
*   GRE (Graduate Record Examinations)

Having a score from one of these exams is a mandatory requirement for application.

### Work Experience

Unlike some MBA programs, BITSoM does not require prior work experience for admission. Candidates with or without work experience are equally eligible to apply and neither group receives preferential treatment during the selection process. This means you can apply right after receiving your entrance exam scores.

### Personal Interview

Shortlisted candidates, based on their application and entrance exam scores, will be invited for a personal interview round. This interview is a crucial part of the selection process, allowing the admissions committee to assess your suitability for the program.

## Scholarships

BITSoM offers Merit-cum-Need Scholarships to support deserving students. To be eligible, the combined annual income of the applicant and their family (parents if unmarried, or spouse if married) should not exceed INR 18 Lakhs.
