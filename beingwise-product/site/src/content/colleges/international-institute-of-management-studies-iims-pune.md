The International Institute of Management Studies (IIMS) in Pune is a well-known institution offering postgraduate management programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities, helping you make an informed decision for your higher education.

## Programs and Fees at IIMS Pune

IIMS Pune primarily offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. The total program fee for the PGDM course is ₹9.63 Lakhs. This amount covers various components like admission fees, tuition, and a security deposit. Keep in mind that additional costs, such as hostel and mess charges, or an optional international study tour, would be separate.

### Hostel and Mess Charges

For students opting for on-campus accommodation, the hostel fee is ₹57,700, and mess charges are ₹48,000 per year. These are separate from the main course fee.

## Admission Process for IIMS Pune

Applying to IIMS Pune is a straightforward online process. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1.  **Visit the College Website:** Navigate to the official IIMS Pune admissions page.
2.  **Initiate Application:** Click on the 'Apply Now' button.
3.  **Register:** Create a student account using your personal details.
4.  **Log In and Fill Form:** Use your new credentials to log in and complete the application form thoroughly.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Attach all required documents digitally.
6.  **Pay Application Fees:** Complete the payment for the application fee.
7.  **Submit:** Finalize and submit your application.

### Required Documents for Admission

Ensure you have the following documents ready for your application:

*   Copies of your 10th, 12th, and Graduation mark sheets.
*   A scorecard from a valid management entrance exam (MAT/CAT/CMAT/ATMA).
*   An experience certificate, if applicable.

It's crucial that all submitted documents are authentic, as any discrepancies could lead to the rejection of your application.

## Placements at IIMS Pune

IIMS Pune has a commendable record for placements for its PGDM graduates. The institute focuses on preparing students for successful careers, and its placement statistics reflect this commitment.

For the PGDM Class of 2025, the placement highlights were:

| Highest Package       | ₹26.10 LPA                  |
| Average Package       | ₹7.50 LPA                   |
| Total Students Placed | 233                         |

These figures indicate strong outcomes for students seeking employment after their PGDM program.

## Faculty at IIMS Pune

IIMS Pune prides itself on its experienced and qualified faculty members. The institute has a diverse team, including over 30 faculty members holding PhDs. They bring a wealth of knowledge and practical experience to the classroom. In addition to core faculty, IIMS Pune also arranges for visiting faculty and guest lecturers to provide students with broader perspectives and industry insights.

## Campus Life and Facilities

IIMS Pune offers a supportive and well-equipped campus environment for its students.

### Hostel Facilities

The institute provides separate hostel buildings for boys and girls, totaling four blocks. These hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi and LAN connectivity across the campus, ensuring students have access to necessary resources.

### Mess and Food

The campus features a subsidized cafeteria that caters to both hostel residents and day scholars. The mess provides three nutritious meals daily, along with evening tea and snacks. Emphasis is placed on hygiene, and fresh fruit is also part of the morning offerings. Students also have various dining options available near the campus.

### Sports Facilities

IIMS Pune encourages a balanced lifestyle and provides sports facilities to support students' physical and mental well-being.
