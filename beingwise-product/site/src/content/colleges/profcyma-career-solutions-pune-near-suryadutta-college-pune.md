## Admission Process and Eligibility

The admission criteria for programs facilitated by Profcyma Career Solutions are set by their partner universities. Generally, the eligibility requirements are as follows:

*   **For Undergraduate Programs (BBA, BCA, B.Com, BA):** Candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognized board.
*   **For Postgraduate Programs (Online MBA, MCA, M.Com, MA):** Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
*   **For Online MBA Programs:** A bachelor's degree from any recognized university is typically required, with specific additional criteria determined by the individual partner university offering the MBA.
*   **For Advanced or Doctoral Programs:** Eligibility is defined by the respective partner university, usually requiring a master's degree from a recognized institution along with any other specific university requirements.
