Marri Laxman Reddy Institute of Technology and Management (MLRITM), located in Hyderabad, is a well-regarded institution for engineering and management studies. This page offers a comprehensive overview of its academic programs, fee structure, admission procedures, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities, helping you make an informed decision for your higher education journey.

## Programs Offered at MLRITM

MLRITM provides a range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses, primarily focusing on technology and management disciplines. These programs are designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge for their chosen careers.

*   **Undergraduate (UG) Programs:** Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
*   **Postgraduate (PG) Programs:** Master of Technology (M.Tech) and Master of Business Administration (MBA)

## Admission Process

Admission to MLRITM is primarily based on entrance examination scores, followed by a counseling process. Aspiring students must meet specific eligibility criteria and successfully clear the relevant entrance exams.

### Eligibility Requirements

*   **For B.Tech:** Candidates must have completed Class 10 and Class 12 with a minimum aggregate of 60% marks.
*   **For M.Tech and MBA:** Applicants need to possess a relevant graduation degree.

### Entrance Exams Accepted

*   **For B.Tech:** Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture & Medical Common Entrance Test (TS EAMCET).
*   **For M.Tech:** Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) or Postgraduate Engineering Common Entrance Test (PGECET).
*   **For MBA:** Telangana State Integrated Common Entrance Test (TS ICET).

To secure admission, it is mandatory to apply for and appear in the respective entrance examination for your desired course and fulfill the academic eligibility criteria.

## Placement Opportunities

MLRITM places a strong emphasis on preparing its students for successful careers. The institute has a dedicated training and placement cell that works tirelessly throughout the year to connect graduates with prospective employers.

### Placement Cell Activities

The placement cell at MLRITM offers comprehensive support to students, including career guidance, skill development workshops, and interview preparation. Their primary goal is to equip students with the necessary information and abilities to navigate the job market effectively.

### Placement Highlights (2024)

While a definitive placement rate for 2024 is yet to be released, the institute has shared promising statistics regarding the number of students placed:

*   **Total students placed (overall):** 983
*   **B.Tech students placed:** 215

Historically, MLRITM has shown consistent placement results. For instance, in the 2018-19 academic year, out of 451 B.Tech graduates, 311 secured placements with a median salary of INR 3.2 Lakhs, and 48 students opted for higher education.
