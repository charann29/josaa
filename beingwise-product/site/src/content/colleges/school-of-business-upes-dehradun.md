The School of Business at UPES, Dehradun, offers a range of management and commerce programs designed to prepare students for the professional world. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered

UPES School of Business provides diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs. A key offering is the Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) with specializations, alongside various Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) and Master of Business Administration (MBA) options.

### Undergraduate Courses

*   **Bachelor of Commerce (Hons.) in Banking & Insurance:** This specialized program has a limited intake of 50 seats.
*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** Available in core and various specializations.

### Postgraduate Courses

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** A comprehensive program for management aspirants.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to programs at UPES School of Business typically involves meeting academic criteria and a selection round.

### B.Com Admission

*   **Eligibility:** Candidates must have achieved a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in both Class 10 and Class 12.
*   **Selection:** Admission is based on Class 12 scores, followed by a personal interview (PI).
*   **Entrance Exam:** No specific entrance examination is required for B.Com admissions; Class 12 performance is key.

### BBA Admission

*   **Eligibility:** A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10 and Class 12 is required.
*   **Selection:** While Class 12 scores are considered, admission largely depends on performance in the UPESMET-UG exam or other accepted entrance tests, along with subsequent selection rounds.

### Application Process

*   The admission portal is currently open for applications. Interested students can register on the official UPES website, complete the application form, and pay the required fees.

## Fee Structure

Specific fee details for each course can be found on the official UPES website. It is advisable to check the latest fee schedule directly from the university.
