Jaipuria Institute of Management in Jaipur is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help prospective students and parents make informed decisions.

## Programs Offered

Jaipuria Jaipur primarily offers two main programs designed for aspiring business professionals and researchers:

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** This is a two-year, full-time program approved by AICTE, accredited by NBA, and recognized by AIU. It aims to equip students with comprehensive management skills.
*   **Fellowship Program in Management (FPM):** This is a doctoral-level program for those interested in management research and academics. It's offered across various specializations.

### PGDM Specializations

The PGDM program provides a broad foundation in management, allowing students to specialize in areas like:

*   Marketing
*   Finance
*   Human Resources Management
*   Operations Management
*   Information Technology
*   Decision Sciences and Business Analytics

### FPM Specializations

The FPM program offers an in-depth research opportunity across eight key areas:

*   Marketing
*   Finance
*   Information Technology
*   Decision Sciences and Business Analytics
*   General Management and Strategy
*   Economics and Public Policy
*   Human Resources Management and Organizational Behavior
*   Operations Management

## Fees Structure

Understanding the financial commitment is crucial for any higher education. Here's a breakdown of the tuition fees at Jaipuria Institute of Management, Jaipur:

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** The total tuition fee for the two-year PGDM program is approximately INR 12.75 Lakhs.
*   **PGDM in Service Management:** Similar to the general PGDM, the tuition fee for this specialized program is also around INR 12.75 Lakhs.
*   **Fellowship Program in Management (FPM):** The FPM program at Jaipuria Jaipur is offered at a competitive fee, making it a valuable option for doctoral studies in management.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to Jaipuria Institute of Management is competitive and based on a multi-stage selection process.

### PGDM Admission

**Eligibility:**

*   Candidates must hold a Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% marks.
*   Final year students can also apply, provided they meet the eligibility criteria by a specified date.

**Selection Criteria:**

1.  **Entrance Exam Scores:** Applicants must have valid scores from any of the following national-level management entrance exams:
    *   CAT
    *   MAT
    *   XAT
    *   CMAT
2.  **Case-Based Group Discussion (GD):** Shortlisted candidates participate in a group discussion to assess their analytical and communication skills.
3.  **Personal Interview (PI):** A personal interview is conducted to evaluate the candidate's personality, motivation, and suitability for the program.
4.  **Academic Performance and Work Experience:** The institute also considers academic records and any relevant work experience during the final selection.

**Seat Intake:** The PGDM program at Jaipuria Institute of Management, Jaipur, has a total intake capacity of 360 students.

### FPM Admission

**Eligibility:**

*   Candidates should possess a Master's degree or equivalent in fields such as Engineering, Economics, Psychology, Commerce, Humanities, Technology, Management, CA, or ICWA.
*   Final year students of Master's degree programs are eligible to apply provisionally. If selected, they must submit their final marksheet by December 31st of the admission year, with all degree requirements completed by September 30th.

**Selection Criteria:**

1.  **Aptitude Test:** The institute conducts its own aptitude test for FPM applicants.
2.  **Interview Round:** Shortlisted candidates undergo a personal interview to assess their research aptitude and academic suitability.
