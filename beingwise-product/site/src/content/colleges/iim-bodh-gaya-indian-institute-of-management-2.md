The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Bodh Gaya, located in Gaya, is a prominent institution for management education. This page offers a clear overview of its academic programs, fee structure, admission process, cutoff scores, and placement outcomes, helping prospective students and parents make informed decisions.

## Programs Offered

IIM Bodh Gaya primarily offers a flagship Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM), equivalent to an MBA, designed to equip students with comprehensive business knowledge and leadership skills. The curriculum is rigorous, employing diverse teaching methods to foster industry-relevant competencies.

## Fee Structure

The total tuition fee for the MBA program at IIM Bodh Gaya is approximately INR 9.6 lakhs. It's important to note that this figure is indicative and candidates should always refer to the official institute website for the most current and precise fee details.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to IIM Bodh Gaya's MBA program is highly competitive, typically requiring a strong score in the Common Admission Test (CAT). Candidates are shortlisted based on their CAT score, academic record, work experience, and other parameters, followed by a Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) round. Specific cutoff scores vary each year based on the applicant pool and seat availability.

## Placements at IIM Bodh Gaya

IIM Bodh Gaya has consistently demonstrated strong placement records, with 100% of participating students securing placements. The institute attracts a growing number of recruiters from diverse sectors, reflecting the quality of its graduates.

### Placement Highlights (2023)

The 2023 placement season saw impressive statistics for IIM Bodh Gaya:

*   **Total Students:** 207
*   **Students Participated in Drive:** 204
*   **Placement Rate:** 100%
*   **Number of Offers:** 204
*   **Number of Recruiters:** 84
*   **Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs):** 13%

The number of companies visiting the campus for placements has steadily increased. In 2023, 84 companies participated, a significant rise from over 60 in 2022 and more than 50 in 2021, indicating growing industry interest.

## Campus Life

Life at IIM Bodh Gaya is designed to be enriching and challenging. The campus offers essential facilities including a well-equipped library, modern classrooms, computer labs, and comfortable hostels. Beyond academics, the institute fosters a vibrant student community through various clubs and committees. These groups organize a wide array of events, from sports competitions and cultural programs to social gatherings, ensuring a holistic development experience. The academic environment is rigorous, with professors employing diverse pedagogical methods to cultivate industry-required competencies. Students are expected to come prepared for lectures, work diligently on projects, participate in late-night group activities, and be ready for impromptu quizzes and examinations, all contributing to a dynamic learning experience.
