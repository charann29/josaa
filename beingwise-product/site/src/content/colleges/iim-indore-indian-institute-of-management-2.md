IIM Indore, or the Indian Institute of Management Indore, is a premier institution renowned for its management education. Located in the vibrant city of Indore, it offers a range of programs designed to cultivate future leaders and skilled professionals. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered at IIM Indore

IIM Indore provides several distinguished programs catering to various career stages and aspirations. These include:

*   **Post Graduate Program in Management (PGP):** This is the flagship two-year residential MBA program for graduates.
*   **Integrated Program in Management (IPM):** A unique five-year program combining undergraduate and postgraduate management education for students after Class 12.
*   **Executive Post Graduate Program in Management (EPGP):** Designed for experienced working professionals, offering a one-year intensive MBA.
*   **Post Graduate Program in Human Resource Management (PGP HRM):** A specialized two-year program focusing on human resource management.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to IIM Indore's programs is highly competitive. For the PGP and IPM programs, the selection process typically involves:

1.  **Entrance Exam:** Candidates must clear national-level entrance exams. For PGP, this is primarily the Common Admission Test (CAT). For IPM, it's the Integrated Program in Management Aptitude Test (IPMAT).
2.  **Shortlisting:** Based on exam scores, academic record, and other criteria, candidates are shortlisted for the next stage.
3.  **Written Ability Test (WAT) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates undergo a WAT and PI round. The WAT assesses writing skills, while the PI evaluates communication, leadership potential, and overall suitability.
4.  **Final Selection:** A composite score, considering performance in the entrance exam, WAT, PI, and academic profile, determines the final selection.

The cutoffs for CAT scores are generally high, often ranging in the 90+ percentile for general category candidates, though they can vary each year based on the applicant pool and selection criteria.

## Placements at IIM Indore

IIM Indore has a strong track record of achieving 100% placements for its flagship programs. The institute consistently attracts a diverse range of recruiters from various sectors. Here are some highlights from the 2025 placements for PGP and IPM batches:

*   **Highest Package:** INR 70 LPA
*   **Average Package:** INR 29.57 LPA
*   **Median Package:** INR 27 LPA
*   **Total Recruiters:** Over 225 companies participated

For specialized programs:

*   **PGP HRM (2025):** Highest package INR 40 LPA, Average package INR 24 LPA, Over 30 recruiters.
*   **EPGP (2025):** Highest package INR 36 LPA, Average package INR 23.5 LPA, Over 65 recruiters. (For comparison, the EPGP highest package in 2024 was INR 47 LPA).

Graduates find opportunities in various roles, including Brand Manager, Account Manager, International Business Manager, Marketing Executive, and Business Technical Consultant across sectors like consulting, finance, IT, and manufacturing.

## Campus Life and Facilities

IIM Indore offers a vibrant campus environment with excellent facilities to support student learning and well-being.

### Faculty

The institute boasts a highly qualified and experienced faculty, many of whom come from prestigious institutions and industry backgrounds. Students consistently rate the faculty highly (around 4.4 out of 5), appreciating their dedication, knowledge, and approachable nature, which helps bridge the gap between academic theory and industry practice.

### Hostel and Mess Facilities

IIM Indore provides comfortable accommodation options, including single-seated and twin-sharing rooms. The mess facilities offer a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian food options round the clock. Beyond regular meal times, a night canteen operates from 11 PM to 5 AM, offering snacks and light meals. Additionally, student-led initiatives like 'JAM' provide fresh fruits and juices, and the 'PI Shop' caters to daily needs, from stationery to personal care items.
