The GITAM School of Business in Visakhapatnam is a well-regarded institution for management education in India. If you're considering a business degree, understanding its programs, fees, admissions, and career prospects is key to making an informed choice.

## Programs Offered

GITAM School of Business provides a range of undergraduate and postgraduate business programs, including popular choices like BBA, B.Com, and MBA. These programs are designed to equip students with essential business knowledge and practical skills.

### Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The BBA program at GITAM is open to students from any academic stream. To be eligible, you need to have completed your Class 12 (or an equivalent qualification) with at least 50% marks in aggregate. For those interested in the BBA in Business Analytics specialization, a minimum of 50% in Class 12 with Mathematics or Statistics as a subject is required.

Admission to the BBA program involves two steps:

*   **GITAM Admission Test (GAT-UGTP)**: This is the university's own entrance exam.
*   **Interview**: Shortlisted candidates will participate in a personal interview.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The MBA program at GITAM School of Business has a total of 240 seats. To be considered for admission, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree with a minimum of 60% aggregate marks.

Admission to the MBA program is entrance-based. The school accepts scores from several national-level management entrance exams, including:

*   **CAT (Common Admission Test)**
*   **XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)**
*   **MAT (Management Aptitude Test)**
*   **GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)**
*   **ATMA (AIMS Test for Management Admissions)**
*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)**
*   **NMAT (Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies Management Aptitude Test)**

For candidates who haven't appeared for these national exams or wish to take another route, GITAM also conducts its own entrance test, the **GITAM Business Admission Test (GBAT)**. Meeting the cutoff scores in any of these accepted exams is crucial for progressing to the next stages of the admission process.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

The admission process generally involves appearing for an accepted entrance exam, followed by an interview and potentially a group discussion. Cutoffs for exams like MAT are usually released after the results are declared. It's important to keep an eye on the official GITAM website for specific dates and cutoff details for each admission cycle.

## Faculty and Recognition

GITAM School of Business is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and holds an A+ Grade accreditation from NAAC. The institution boasts a strong faculty team, comprising over 90 members, including full-time, part-time, and visiting faculty from international locations such as the USA, UK, Canada, and Papua New Guinea. This diverse faculty brings a global perspective to the learning experience.
