UPES, or the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, located in Dehradun, is a well-known institution offering a diverse range of programs. It's particularly recognized for its industry-focused approach across various fields like engineering, management, and law. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities.

## UPES, Dehradun: An Overview

UPES Dehradun is a private university that emphasizes practical, industry-aligned education. It aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their chosen careers. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines.

For postgraduate MBA programs, UPES offers specializations such as International Business, Operations Management, Aviation Management, Power Management, Oil and Gas Management, Business Analytics, Logistics and Supply Chain Management, and Marketing Management.

## Admission Process

Admission to UPES courses generally involves meeting eligibility criteria and performing well in relevant entrance examinations. The application process is straightforward:

1.  **Visit the Official Website**: Navigate to the UPES admissions portal.
2.  **Apply Online**: Click on the 'Apply Now' option, usually found on the homepage.
3.  **Register**: Provide the required details to create an account.
4.  **Fill Application Form**: Complete the application with your personal and academic information.
5.  **Upload Documents**: Submit scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and academic mark sheets (typically Class 10 and 12).
6.  **Pay Application Fee**: The application fee for programs like BBA is INR 1,350.
7.  **Submit**: Finalize and submit your application.

### MBA Admission Details

For MBA programs, candidates must have a graduation degree in any stream, along with at least 50% aggregate marks in Class 10, Class 12, and their undergraduate degree. Admission is based on scores from national-level tests like CAT, CUET PG, or XAT. If a candidate doesn't meet the minimum percentile for these exams, they might need to appear for UPESMET, the university's own online entrance exam. UPESMET covers English, General Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude, and Analytical Reasoning, lasting 2.5 hours.

For the Global MBA, a personal interview (PI) round is the primary selection criterion, followed by document verification and fee payment to secure the seat.

## Cutoff Information

Cutoffs for admission vary by course and entrance exam. For instance, in MBA admissions based on XAT 2023 scores, candidates generally needed a minimum of 50 percentile for the General category (All India Quota) to be considered for admission.

## Placements

UPES has a strong track record for placements, especially in its engineering programs. The university is known for attracting market leaders for recruitment drives. For example, its B.Tech Computer Science program is highly regarded, partly due to the excellent placement opportunities provided to its graduates. The university was ranked 54th in the Engineering category by NIRF 2023, reflecting its standing and commitment to student career outcomes. UPES also offers scholarships to deserving students across various courses.

## Why Choose UPES Dehradun?

Choosing UPES means opting for a university that focuses on industry relevance and holistic development. Its strong academic framework, diverse course offerings, robust placement support, and scenic Dehradun location make it an attractive option for many students pursuing higher education in India.
