Welcome to our guide on Vivekananda Global University (VGU), Jaipur! If you're considering higher education in Rajasthan, VGU offers a diverse range of programs. This page will break down everything you need to know about their courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities.

## Programs and Fees at VGU

VGU provides a wide array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. Here's a general overview of some popular courses and their approximate annual fees. Please note that these figures can vary, and it's always best to check the official VGU website for the most current information.

| Course | Eligibility (General) | Approximate Annual Fees (INR) |
|---|---|---|
| B.Tech | 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Maths (min. 50%) | 1,00,000 - 1,50,000 |
| B.Sc (Forensic Science) | 10+2 with Science stream (min. 50%) | 70,000 - 90,000 |
| MBA | Bachelor's Degree (min. 50%) | 1,20,000 - 1,80,000 |
| B.Des | 10+2 (min. 50%) | 1,00,000 - 1,30,000 |
| LLB | Bachelor's Degree (min. 45%) | 60,000 - 80,000 |

### B.Sc Forensic Science Admission

Securing a spot in the B.Sc Forensic Science program at VGU is achievable, provided you meet the eligibility criteria. You'll need to have passed your 10+2 exams with at least 50% marks in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biotechnology, or Biology. VGU conducts its own entrance exam for this program, and they also accept scores from national tests like AIFSET. A strong performance in either your 10+2 exams or these entrance tests will significantly boost your chances.

## Admission Process at VGU

The admission process at Vivekananda Global University is designed to be straightforward. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1.  **Online Application:** Start by filling out the application form on VGU's official admission portal. You'll need to pay a non-refundable registration fee.
2.  **Eligibility Criteria:** Ensure you meet the minimum academic requirements for your chosen program. Generally, a minimum of 50% marks in 12th standard for general category students (and 45% for SC/ST categories) is required for many courses.
3.  **Entrance Exams:** Admission to specific courses depends on various entrance exams:
    *   **Engineering:** Scores from JEE Main or VGU's own entrance test (VGUCET) are considered.
    *   **Medical & Allied:** NEET and CUET scores are taken into account.
    *   **Design:** Candidates need to clear an aptitude and creativity test called DE-Code.
    *   **Other Courses:** VGU may conduct its own entrance exam (VGUCET) or accept other relevant national-level exam scores.
4.  **Selection and Verification:** After qualifying, candidates undergo a selection procedure, followed by document verification by the admissions team.
5.  **Fee Payment:** Upon successful verification, pay the course fees to finalize your admission.

## Cutoff Information

VGU considers scores from various national and university-specific entrance exams for admission. While specific cutoffs can vary each year based on the number of applicants and exam difficulty, a strong performance in the relevant entrance test (JEE Main, NEET, CUET, VGUCET, AIFSET, or DE-Code) is crucial. For programs without specific entrance exams, your 10+2 marks play a significant role.

## Placements at VGU

Vivekananda Global University has a commendable track record when it comes to student placements, demonstrating strong opportunities for its graduates.

### Overall Placement Highlights (2025)

*   **Highest Package:** INR 46 LPA
*   **Average Package:** Approximately INR 4.6 LPA
*   **Placement Rate:** VGU has reported an impressive placement rate of 90%.
*   **Total Graduates Placed:** Over 15,000 graduates have been placed in various companies across different sectors.

### MBA Placements

While the overall placements are strong, MBA placements at VGU are slightly below the university's general average. Recruiters for MBA graduates have included companies like IndusInd Bank, Mercedes Benz (India) Ltd., A3Logics, and TAFE.

### B.Sc Forensic Science Placements

For B.Sc Forensic Science graduates, VGU offers moderate placement opportunities, given that it is a relatively newer private university. Career paths for these graduates include roles such as Scientific Officer, Lab Expert, and Criminal Investigation Officer. The university makes its placement statistics publicly available on its official website, providing transparency for prospective students.

## Campus Life and Facilities

VGU provides on-campus hostel facilities for both male and female students, ensuring a comfortable living environment. Additionally, various paying guest (PG) accommodations are available in the vicinity of the university, offering convenient access and a good environment with easy transportation options.
