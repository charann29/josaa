The Symbiosis Institute of Digital and Telecom Management (SIDTM), a part of Symbiosis International University (SIU) in Pune, stands out as a pioneering institution in telecom management within the SAARC region. It offers specialized MBA programs designed to prepare students for the evolving digital and telecom landscape.

## Courses Offered

SIDTM focuses on a two-year, full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) program with distinct specializations to cater to various industry needs. These specializations are:

*   **Systems & Finance:** This track blends technological understanding with financial management principles, ideal for roles in tech finance.
*   **Marketing & Finance:** Combining marketing strategies with financial acumen, this specialization prepares students for roles in product management, business development, and financial services within the digital sector.
*   **Analytics & Finance:** This specialization equips students with data analytics skills alongside financial knowledge, crucial for data-driven decision-making in the modern business world.

## Fees Structure

The total fee for the MBA program at SIDTM Pune is approximately INR 16.1 lakhs. This covers the tuition and other academic charges for the entire two-year duration of the course.

## Admission Process

Admission to the MBA program at SIDTM Pune is a multi-stage process, primarily based on the Symbiosis National Aptitude (SNAP) test, followed by an institute-specific selection round.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the MBA program, candidates must hold a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university or institution of national importance. A minimum aggregate of 50% marks is required for general category candidates, while SC/ST category candidates need a minimum of 45%.

### Selection Procedure

1.  **SNAP Exam:** Applicants must first register for and appear in the SNAP exam. This is a mandatory entrance test for all Symbiosis MBA programs.
2.  **Application to SIDTM:** After taking the SNAP exam, candidates need to fill out the application form specifically for SIDTM Pune.
3.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are then shortlisted based on their SNAP scores.
4.  **GE-PIWAT Round:** Shortlisted candidates proceed to the Group Exercise (GE), Personal Interview (PI), and Written Ability Test (WAT) round. Performance in this round is crucial for final selection.
5.  **Merit List:** A final merit list is prepared based on a combination of SNAP scores and performance in the GE-PIWAT round.

## Cutoff

While specific cutoff percentages can vary each year, admission is highly competitive. Candidates with strong SNAP scores and impressive performance in the GE-PIWAT rounds have a better chance of securing a seat. Meeting the minimum graduation percentage of 50% (45% for SC/ST) is a prerequisite.
