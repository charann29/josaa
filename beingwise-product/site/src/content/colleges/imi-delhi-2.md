The International Management Institute (IMI) in Delhi is a well-regarded institution offering various management programs. If you're considering a career in business, understanding what IMI Delhi offers in terms of courses, fees, admission criteria, selection process, and career prospects is crucial. This guide provides a clear overview to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at IMI Delhi

IMI Delhi provides a range of postgraduate and doctoral programs designed to equip students with essential management skills. These include:

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** A flagship two-year program.
*   **Executive Post Graduate Diploma in Management (EPGDM):** Designed for working professionals.
*   **Fellow Programme in Management (FPM):** A doctoral-level program for those interested in research and academia.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to IMI Delhi's programs is competitive and primarily based on national-level entrance exam scores. Direct admission is not available for most courses.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission, applicants must meet specific academic requirements:

*   **PGDM & EPGDM:** A Bachelor's degree (or equivalent) in any discipline from a recognized university with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
*   **FPM:** A Bachelor's or Master's degree with at least 60% aggregate marks. For the EPGDM, a minimum of three years of post-qualification executive or self-employed professional experience is also required.

### Selection Criteria

The selection process for IMI Delhi programs involves multiple stages:

*   **PGDM:** Candidates are evaluated based on their CAT/XAT/GMAT scores, past academic performance, a video assessment, and a personal interview.
*   **EPGDM:** Selection considers scores from CAT/XAT/CMAT/MAT/ATMA/GMAT, an IMI Admission Test score, past academic record, and an interview.
*   **FPM:** The selection process is comprehensive, including scores from CAT/GMAT/XAT/GRE/NET or the IMI Research Aptitude Test, an admission interview, a research abstract, academic history, and relevant work experience (for part-time applicants).

## Fees Structure

The fee for the two-year PGDM program at IMI Delhi is approximately INR 23.54 lakhs. This amount covers the tuition and other associated costs. When confirming admission, students are typically required to pay a first installment, which includes a refundable security deposit, an admission fee, and other one-time charges. It's important to note that fees are subject to change, so always refer to the official IMI Delhi website for the most current information.

## Accepted Entrance Exams

IMI Delhi accepts scores from several prominent management entrance examinations:

*   **For PGDM:** CAT, XAT, GMAT
*   **For EPGDM:** CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, ATMA, GMAT
*   **For FPM:** CAT, XAT, GMAT, GRE, NET, or the IMI Research Aptitude Test
