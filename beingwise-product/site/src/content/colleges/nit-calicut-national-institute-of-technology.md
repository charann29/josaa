The National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) is a well-regarded institution offering a range of engineering and architecture programs. Located in Kozhikode, Kerala, it provides a strong academic environment for students pursuing higher education in technology.

## Programs Offered

NIT Calicut primarily focuses on Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) and Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) degrees. These programs cover various engineering disciplines, preparing students for diverse career paths.

## Admission Process

Admission to B.Tech and B.Arch programs at NIT Calicut is primarily based on scores from the JEE Main examination. Candidates need to perform well in this national-level entrance test to be eligible for consideration. The institute releases cutoffs based on both All India and Home State quotas, and further category-wise cutoffs are also published.

## Cutoff Ranks for Admission

To secure a seat at NIT Calicut, prospective students need to achieve competitive ranks in JEE Main. For general category candidates under the All India quota, the cutoff ranks for B.Tech programs typically range from around 564 to 41,289. For those applying under the Home State general category, the cutoff ranks usually fall between 241 and 47,061. These figures can vary each year depending on factors like the number of applicants and exam difficulty.
