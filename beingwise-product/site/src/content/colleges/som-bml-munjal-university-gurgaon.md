The School of Management (SOM) at BML Munjal University, located in Gurgaon, offers a range of business programs designed to equip students for the professional world. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, academic cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered at SOM, BML Munjal University

SOM provides both undergraduate and postgraduate management programs. These courses are structured to offer a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, preparing students for various roles in the business landscape.

### Undergraduate Programs

*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** This program focuses on building a strong foundation in business fundamentals, covering areas like marketing, finance, human resources, and operations. It aims to develop managerial skills and an entrepreneurial mindset.

### Postgraduate Programs

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** The MBA program at SOM offers specializations in key areas, allowing students to deepen their expertise. Some of the available specializations include:
    *   Marketing
    *   Finance
    *   Operations and Supply Chain Management
    *   Human Resources
    *   Business Analytics

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for programs at SOM, BML Munjal University varies by course. Prospective students should check the official university website for the most current and detailed fee breakdown, including tuition, hostel, and other charges.

## Admission Process

Admission to SOM programs is based on a combination of academic performance, entrance exam scores, and personal interaction.

### BBA Admission

For the BBA program, candidates typically need to meet minimum eligibility criteria in their Class 12 examinations. The selection process often involves:

*   A valid score from standardized tests like SAT India or ACT.
*   A Personal Interview (PI).

### MBA Admission

To be considered for the MBA program, applicants must generally hold an undergraduate degree with a minimum of 50% marks. The selection process for MBA includes:

*   A valid score from management aptitude tests such as CAT, NMAT, GMAT, CMAT, XAT, GRE, or the university's own BMU-MAT (BMU's Management Aptitude Test).
*   A Personal Interview (PI).

## Cutoff Information

While specific cutoffs can vary each year based on the applicant pool and program demand, candidates are generally expected to achieve competitive scores in their respective entrance examinations to be considered for admission to SOM, BML Munjal University.
