JECRC University, located in Jaipur, is a well-known institution offering a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Courses Offered at JECRC University

JECRC University provides a diverse range of programs across various disciplines, catering to different academic interests and career aspirations. These include engineering, management, science, arts, and computer applications.

### Undergraduate Programs

For students completing their Class 12, JECRC University offers numerous UG courses. These typically span three to five years, depending on the program. Popular choices include:

*   **B.Tech:** A comprehensive engineering program.
*   **BBA:** Bachelor of Business Administration, focusing on management principles.
*   **BSc (Hons):** Science programs with specializations.
*   **BA (Hons):** Arts programs with various specializations.
*   **BCom:** Bachelor of Commerce.
*   **BCA:** Bachelor of Computer Applications.

### Postgraduate Programs

Graduates seeking to further their education can choose from a selection of two-year postgraduate courses. These include:

*   **M.Tech:** Master of Technology.
*   **MBA:** Master of Business Administration.
*   **MSc (Hons):** Advanced science programs.
*   **MCA:** Master of Computer Applications.

## JECRC University Fees and Application Process

Understanding the financial commitment and application steps is crucial for prospective students.

### Application Fees

An application fee is required when submitting your form for admission. This fee can be paid conveniently through various online payment methods.

| Course Type | Application Fee |
| :---------- | :-------------- |
| UG/PG       | INR 850         |
| PhD         | INR 1,500       |

### Course Fees Comparison

JECRC University aims to provide accessible education. For instance, its BA program is generally more affordable compared to some other universities. While specific figures can change, the BA course at JECRC University typically ranges from INR 1.6 Lakhs to INR 2.3 Lakhs.

For B.Tech programs, the tuition fees at JECRC University generally range from INR 3.6 Lakhs to INR 7 Lakhs.

## Admission Criteria and Eligibility

Admission to JECRC University is based on a combination of merit and entrance exam scores for many programs, with some courses offering direct admission based on academic performance.

### Eligibility Requirements

*   **For UG Courses:** Candidates must have successfully passed Class 12 from a recognized board, typically with a minimum of 50% marks in relevant subjects. For some programs, preference is given to candidates with CUET UG scores.
*   **For PG Courses:** Applicants need to hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university.

### Application Process

The application process for all programs at JECRC University is conducted online. Interested candidates can visit the university's official website to fill out the application form.

### Entrance Exams and Merit-Based Admissions

*   **Entrance-Based Admission:** For programs like B.Tech, admissions are in progress and often consider entrance exam scores. The university also gives preference to CUET UG scores for various undergraduate courses.
*   **Merit-Based Admission:** For certain programs such as BA (Hons), BCom, BCA, and MCA, admission can be secured based on a candidate's academic performance in their last qualifying examination, without the need for an entrance exam.

## JECRC University Placements

JECRC University has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with various companies. While specific placement percentages are not always publicly disclosed, the university shares positive placement statistics regarding offers and packages.

### Placement Highlights (Batch of 2026, *so far*)

*   **Companies Visited:** 80+
*   **Total Offers Made:** 1030+
*   **Highest Package Offered:** INR 33 LPA
*   **Average Package Offered:** INR 6.67 LPA

These figures indicate a strong industry connection and opportunities for students across various fields.

## Seat Intake Information

The number of available seats for each program at JECRC University varies. For instance, the BSc programs collectively offer around 60 seats. The B.Tech program has a significantly larger intake, with approximately 550 seats. These numbers are indicative and can be subject to change based on university policies and regulatory guidelines.

JECRC University strives to provide quality education and career opportunities, making it a viable option for students looking to pursue higher education in Jaipur.
