## Admission Process

Admission to Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute typically involves an application process, meeting eligibility criteria, and often an entrance examination.

### General Steps for Admission:

1.  **Online Application:** Candidates must complete and submit the application form available on the institute's official website.
2.  **Eligibility Check:** Ensure you meet the specific academic requirements for your chosen program.
3.  **Entrance Exam:** Many programs require candidates to appear for an entrance examination.
4.  **Counselling/Interview:** Shortlisted candidates may be invited for counselling or an interview.

### Specific Program Requirements:

*   **BCA:** Applicants need to have completed Class 12. Selection is based on the MGR Engineering Entrance Examination (MGREEE) followed by counselling.
*   **BSc:** Candidates must have passed Class 12 from a recognized board with at least 45% aggregate marks in relevant subjects.
*   **BA LLB (Hons.):** Requires completion of Class 12. Admission is based on scores from the All India Common Entrance Examination or MGREEE.
*   **M.Tech:** Admission is generally straightforward for eligible candidates who achieve satisfactory scores in relevant entrance exams. The program offers both regular and part-time options with a flexible credit system.
*   **M.Com:** This is a two-year, full-time postgraduate degree offered in two specialisations. Admission is typically merit-based.
