Starting your higher education journey can feel like a big step, and understanding all the details about entrance exams and admissions is key. Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), often known as IPU, conducts its own Common Entrance Test (CET) for various programs. This guide will help you navigate the IPU CET 2026, covering everything from available courses and fees to the admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Understanding IPU CET 2026

IPU CET is an annual entrance examination for admission to a range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses offered by GGSIPU and its affiliated colleges. While many programs accept national-level test scores like JEE Main, some specific courses require you to appear for the IPU CET.

### Courses Offered Through IPU CET

IPU CET covers admissions for various programs, particularly for lateral entry options and specialized courses. For example, B.Tech Lateral Entry (for Diploma holders or B.Sc. graduates) and B.Tech (Bio-Technology) are typically covered under IPU CET. It's important to note that regular B.Tech admissions are based on JEE Main scores, not a separate IPU CET.

**Common IPU CET Course Codes:**

*   **B.Tech LE (Diploma):** 128
*   **B.Tech LE (B.Sc.):** 129
*   **B.Tech (Biotechnology):** 130
*   **B.Tech (for regular admission, based on JEE Main):** 131

### Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility varies significantly by program. Here are some general guidelines:

*   **For B.Tech (regular admission):** You must have passed Class 12 or an equivalent exam with at least 55% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Admission is strictly based on JEE Main scores.
*   **For B.Tech Lateral Entry (Diploma Holders):** You need a three-year diploma in a relevant engineering discipline with a minimum of 60% marks. You must also have studied Mathematics at the Class 12 level or in the first year of your diploma. Appearing in IPU CET and being on the merit list is mandatory.
*   **For B.Tech (Biotechnology):** Similar to other B.Tech courses, Class 12 with 55% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (or Biology/Biotechnology) is usually required. Admission is through IPU CET.

### Application and Admission Process

1.  **Application Form:** Fill out the online application form on the GGSIPU website. Ensure all details, especially any national-level test application numbers, are accurate.
2.  **Appear for IPU CET:** If your chosen course requires it, take the IPU CET on the scheduled date. The exam is usually conducted once a year between March and May for different courses.
3.  **Merit List:** Based on your performance in the IPU CET or national-level tests, GGSIPU will release merit lists.
4.  **Counselling:** Qualified candidates will be invited for online counselling. This involves registration, choice filling, and seat allotment.
5.  **Fee Payment:** After seat allotment, you'll need to pay an academic fee to confirm your admission.

### IPU CET Exam Pattern and Marking Scheme

For programs that require the IPU CET, the exam typically consists of 100 multiple-choice questions. The total marks for the paper are 400, and the exam duration is usually 2 hours and 30 minutes.

| Aspect            | Details                                          |
| :---------------- | :----------------------------------------------- |
| **Total Questions** | 100                                              |
| **Total Marks**   | 400                                              |
| **Marking Scheme**| +4 marks for each correct answer                 |
|                   | -1 mark for each incorrect answer                |
| **Negative Marking**| Yes, 1 mark deducted for wrong answers           |
| **Unanswered Questions**| No marks deducted or awarded                   |
| **Exam Duration** | 2 hours 30 minutes                               |

### Counselling Fees

There is a non-refundable counselling fee, typically around INR 1,000, which must be paid to participate in the counselling process. This fee is not returned, even if you don't get a seat or decide to withdraw. However, if you pay the part academic fee (e.g., INR 40,000) after seat allotment, a portion of this might be refundable if you withdraw within specific university deadlines. Always consult the official GGSIPU counselling guidelines for the exact refund policy.

## Cutoffs and Placements

Specific cutoffs for IPU CET courses vary each year based on factors like the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. These are usually released during the counselling rounds. GGSIPU and its affiliated colleges have a dedicated placement cell that assists students with career opportunities. Many reputable companies recruit from GGSIPU, with placement statistics varying by program and college. Information on average packages and top recruiters is generally available on the university's official website or college brochures.

Always refer to the official Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University website for the most current and accurate information regarding IPU CET 2026 dates, syllabi, and admission procedures.
