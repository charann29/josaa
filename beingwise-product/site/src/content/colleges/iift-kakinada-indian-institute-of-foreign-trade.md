The Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT) Kakinada is a notable institution dedicated to shaping future leaders in global trade and business analytics. If you're considering a career in these dynamic fields, understanding its offerings, admission process, and career opportunities is key.

## Courses Offered at IIFT Kakinada

IIFT Kakinada primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs designed to equip students with specialized knowledge in international business and business analytics. These programs are rigorous and industry-aligned.

*   **MBA (International Business)**: This flagship program is tailored for aspiring professionals looking to excel in global trade, export-import management, and international marketing. It covers a wide array of subjects crucial for navigating the complexities of the international business landscape.
*   **MBA (Business Analytics)**: Designed for those with a flair for data and strategic decision-making, this program focuses on leveraging analytical tools and techniques to solve business challenges and drive growth.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to IIFT Kakinada's MBA programs is highly competitive and follows a structured selection process. From the academic year 2024-25, IIFT Kakinada accepts CAT scores for MBA admissions.

### Eligibility Criteria

For the MBA (International Business) and MBA (Business Analytics) programs, candidates generally need a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. Specific percentage requirements may apply, with relaxations for reserved categories.

### Selection Procedure

1.  **CAT Score**: Candidates must appear for the Common Admission Test (CAT) and achieve a competitive score.
2.  **Shortlisting**: Based on CAT scores and other criteria, candidates are shortlisted for the next stages.
3.  **Further Rounds**: Shortlisted candidates participate in Group Discussions (GD), Written Ability Tests (WAT), and Personal Interviews (PI).
4.  **Final Selection**: A final merit list is prepared considering CAT scores, performance in GD/WAT/PI, academic record, and work experience.

## Fee Structure

The fee structure for the MBA programs at IIFT Kakinada is determined by the institute and is subject to change. Prospective students should refer to the official IIFT Kakinada website for the most current and detailed fee information for their desired program.

## IIFT Kakinada Cutoff

The cutoff for admission to IIFT's MBA programs is a crucial factor. Historically, the cutoff scores, especially for the General category, have been competitive. For the MBA (IB) and MBA (Business Analytics) courses, the cutoff is now based on CAT scores. These cutoffs are usually announced in percentiles and can vary each year depending on the applicant pool and exam difficulty. The institute typically releases cutoff details around February, with selection rounds taking place between April and May.
