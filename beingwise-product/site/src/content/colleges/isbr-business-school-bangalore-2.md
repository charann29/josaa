ISBR Business School in Bangalore is a well-known institution for management education. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fees, admission process, selection criteria, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered and Fees

ISBR Business School primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs. Their flagship offering is the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM).

While specific fee structures for all courses are best confirmed directly with the institution, generally, the PGDM program involves tuition and other charges. It's advisable to check the official ISBR website or prospectus for the most current fee details, including any variations for specializations or additional components.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to ISBR's PGDM program is a multi-stage process designed to assess a candidate's academic background and potential for success in management. Here's a general outline:

### Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must typically hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university. A valid score in one of the accepted management entrance exams is also a prerequisite.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

ISBR Bangalore accepts scores from a range of national-level management aptitude tests. These include:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   MAT (Management Aptitude Test)
*   GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)
*   NMAT (NMAT by GMAC)
*   CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)

### Selection Rounds

Candidates who meet the eligibility criteria and possess a valid entrance exam score will proceed to the subsequent selection rounds. These typically include:

*   **Written Ability Test (WAT):** To evaluate written communication skills.
*   **Micro Presentation:** To assess presentation skills and spontaneous thinking.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** To gauge overall personality, communication, and aptitude for management.

## Cutoff Information

For the MAT exam, candidates aspiring for the PGDM program at ISBR Bangalore should aim for a score of at least 650 out of 800. While this is a key benchmark, strong performance in the WAT, Micro Presentation, and Personal Interview rounds is equally vital for final selection. Cutoffs for other entrance exams like CAT, XAT, GMAT, NMAT, and CMAT would also apply, and these are generally competitive.

## Campus Facilities and Infrastructure

ISBR Bangalore offers a modern and well-equipped campus environment designed to support student learning and well-being. Key facilities include:

*   **Academic Spaces:** Air-conditioned classrooms with multimedia capabilities, seminar halls, board rooms, discussion rooms, and computer labs.
*   **Knowledge Hub:** A comprehensive library and knowledge center, along with an audio-video media room.
*   **Residential Options:** Separate hostel facilities for both male and female students, providing secure and comfortable accommodation.
*   **Recreational and Support Services:** A food court, coffee lounge, indoor games, gym, laundry services, 24/7 security, power backup, and medical facilities.

## Reputation and Accreditations

ISBR Business School is recognized as a well-regarded private institution in the management education space. It holds approvals from the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). Additionally, it is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and is listed among the top business schools in the country by the Ministry of HRD, Government of India. While it is often considered a Tier 2 business school, its strong industry links and consistent performance contribute to its positive standing.
