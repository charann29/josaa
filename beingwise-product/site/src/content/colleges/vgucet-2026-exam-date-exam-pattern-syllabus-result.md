Planning your higher education journey for 2026? If Vivekananda Global University (VGU) in Jaipur is on your radar, understanding the VGUCET entrance exam is key. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about VGUCET 2026, including important dates, the exam structure, syllabus, and how to apply, along with details on VGU's courses, fees, admissions, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## VGUCET 2026: An Overview

VGUCET (Vivekananda Global University Common Entrance Test) is the pathway to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs at VGU. It's crucial to stay updated with the latest information to ensure a smooth application process.

### Application Process and Important Dates

The application form for VGUCET 2026 will be released online on the official Vivekananda Global University website. The entire process is conducted digitally, involving registration, fee payment, and document submission. Keep an eye on the official VGU website for the exact release date and the last date to apply, which will be announced later.

**Steps to Apply:**

1.  Visit the official VGU website.
2.  Locate and click on the direct link for the VGUCET 2026 application form.
3.  Fill in your personal details accurately, including your name, mobile number, email ID, parent's name, and gender.
4.  Pay the application fee online using a debit card, credit card, or net banking.
5.  Upload all necessary documents as specified.

### Application Fee

The application fee for VGUCET is INR 2000. This fee is non-refundable and must be paid online to complete your registration.

### Required Documents for Application

Before you start filling out the application form, make sure you have the following documents ready:

*   A valid email address
*   Your mobile number
*   Scanned photograph
*   10th-class mark sheet
*   12th-class mark sheet (if available)
*   Details for online payment (debit card, credit card, or net banking)

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for VGUCET 2026:

*   **For Undergraduate Courses:** Candidates must have completed 10+2 with a minimum of 50% marks.
*   **For Postgraduate Courses:** Candidates need a bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% marks.

### Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The VGUCET exam pattern will be detailed in the official notification. It's important to note that there is **no negative marking** for incorrect answers. Candidates are awarded one mark for each correct response. This means you can attempt as many questions as possible without fear of losing marks for wrong answers.

Specific details about the syllabus for different courses will be available on the official VGU website. It is advisable to refer to these official resources for comprehensive preparation.

## Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff, and Placements at VGU

Vivekananda Global University offers a wide array of courses across various disciplines. The admission process is primarily based on the VGUCET scores, followed by counseling.

### Scholarships

VGU provides several scholarship opportunities to support its students. These include:

*   Merit-based scholarships
*   Scholarships for VGU students and alumni
*   Scholarships for children of VGU employees
*   Scholarships for excellence in co-curricular activities such as sports, theatre, dance, performing arts, NCC, and NSS.

To apply for scholarships, candidates must submit the required documents during the admission process. Detailed information on each scholarship type will be available on the university's official portal.

### Placements

VGU has a dedicated placement cell that assists students in securing internships and job opportunities. The university focuses on preparing students for the industry through skill development programs and career counseling. Specific placement statistics and top recruiting companies are usually updated annually on the university's official website.

To get the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding course-specific fees, admission cutoffs, and placement records, always refer to the official Vivekananda Global University website.
