Apex University, located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, is a private institution offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Established under the Rajasthan State Legislature Act No. 27 and recognized by the UGC, it aims to provide quality education across various disciplines. This guide will walk you through its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities.

## About Apex University, Jaipur

Apex University operates as a private university, recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and established under the Rajasthan State Legislature Act No. 27. It holds approvals from several key regulatory bodies including AICTE, BCI, PCI, NCTE, RCI, INC, RNC, and RSAHC, ensuring its programs meet national standards. Additionally, the university is a member of prestigious organizations like the Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU) and the Association of Indian Universities (AIU).

## Admission Process

Admissions at Apex University are primarily entrance-based, with options available for both national-level entrance exam scores and the university's own entrance test. The application process is entirely online.

### How to Apply:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Go to the Apex University official website.
2.  **Register:** Click on 'Apply Now' and complete the registration with your basic details.
3.  **Log In:** Use your registered credentials to log in to the applicant portal.
4.  **Fill Application:** Complete the application form, providing all necessary academic and personal information.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of required documents (mark sheets, certificates, photographs, etc.).
6.  **Pay Application Fee:** Submit the non-refundable application fee online to finalize your application.

### Selection Criteria:

For most programs, Apex University follows a dual admission path:

*   **CUET Merit:** 50% of the total seats are filled based on scores from the Common University Entrance Test (CUET).
*   **Apex Merit (ACET/AUCET):** For the remaining 50% of seats, the university considers performance in its own entrance exams, such as the Apex Common Entrance Test (ACET) or Apex University Common Entrance Test (AUCET), depending on the program. Candidates applying under this category may need to appear for the university's test.

After shortlisting, eligible candidates who meet the criteria are offered admission. To confirm their seat, selected candidates must pay the initial installment of the course fee.

### Important Dates:

Admission dates can vary each year. While there isn't a fixed last date for all applications, it's crucial to check the university's official academic calendar for specific deadlines. For instance, in 2024, classes for various undergraduate courses commenced on September 2, and enrollment form filling was scheduled between September 25 and October 10. Spot Round Admission for CUET UG counseling was open until August 25, 2024.

## Cutoff Information

Apex University's cutoff scores depend on the program and the entrance exam considered (CUET or ACET/AUCET). Since 50% of seats are allocated via CUET, applicants must meet the minimum CUET scores for their desired course. For the remaining 50% of seats, the university's internal entrance test scores determine eligibility. Specific cutoff ranks or scores are usually released post-entrance exams and counseling rounds. It is advisable to refer to the university's admission notifications for detailed cutoff information each academic year.
