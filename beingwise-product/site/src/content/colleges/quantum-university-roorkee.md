## Admission Process

Quantum University generally follows a structured admission process. Prospective students can apply through both online and offline modes.

### Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility requirements vary by program. For instance, for the BBA LLB course, candidates must have passed Class 12 with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.

### Application Steps

**Online Application:**
*   Visit the official website of Quantum University.
*   Locate the admissions section and fill out the application form.
*   Pay the non-refundable application fee online.

**Offline Application:**
*   Candidates can visit the Quantum University campus or any of its admissions offices.
*   Register for admission in person.
*   Submit a bank Demand Draft (DD) of INR 1,200, payable to "Quantum University" at Roorkee, as the application fee.

### Application Fee

The application fee for most programs at Quantum University is INR 1,200. This fee is non-refundable.

### Important Dates

Application forms for the BBA LLB course for the academic batch of 2025 are currently open. It is advisable to check the university's website for application deadlines for other courses.

### CUET and Scholarships

While the official website doesn't explicitly state CUET UG scores are mandatory for BBA LLB admission, Quantum University does offer scholarships based on CUET performance for various undergraduate and integrated programs, including BBA LLB. This suggests that performing well in CUET could be beneficial for scholarship opportunities.
