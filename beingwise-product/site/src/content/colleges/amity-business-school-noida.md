Amity Business School (ABS) in Noida is a well-known institution for management education, offering a variety of programs for aspiring business professionals. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fees, admission process, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

ABS offers a range of management programs designed to cater to diverse career aspirations. The flagship program is the Master of Business Administration (MBA), available with numerous specializations. They also provide research opportunities through their Ph.D. program in Management.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Amity Business School's MBA program is highly comprehensive, featuring a remarkable 27 specializations. This allows students to tailor their studies to specific industry interests, from finance and marketing to human resources and international business. The program also includes unique international exposure opportunities, such as a five-week Study Abroad Program at Amity campuses in global cities like London, Dubai, New York, or Singapore.

### Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Management

For those interested in advanced research and academia, ABS offers a full-time Ph.D. in Management. This program typically spans two years, providing a structured environment for in-depth study and dissertation work.

## Fee Structure

The fees at Amity Business School vary depending on the program and whether a student is sponsored or non-sponsored. Here's a breakdown for the MBA and Ph.D. programs:

### MBA Program Fees (Per Semester)

| MBA Program Type           | Non-Sponsored Students | Sponsored Students |
| :------------------------- | :--------------------- | :----------------- |
| General MBA & MBA Finance  | INR 3.95 Lakhs         | INR 5.925 Lakhs    |
| Other Specialised MBA      | INR 3.45 Lakhs         | INR 5.175 Lakhs    |

Additionally, there is a one-time registration fee of INR 1,500 required when obtaining the application form for any desired course.

### Ph.D. in Management Fees

The total tuition fee for the two-year, full-time Ph.D. in Management program is INR 2.00 Lakhs.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

To apply for programs at Amity Business School, prospective students generally need to meet specific academic criteria and often participate in an entrance examination, group discussion, and personal interview. Detailed eligibility requirements and the application process can be found on the official Amity University website.

## Placements and Career Opportunities

Amity Business School has a strong track record of placements, attracting leading companies from various sectors. The university's strong industry connections contribute to favorable career outcomes for its graduates.

### MBA Placements

In 2023, the highest package offered during Amity MBA placements reached INR 15 LPA. The placement statistics for different sectors are as follows:

| Sector             | Highest Package (2023) |
| :----------------- | :--------------------- |
| Finance            | INR 15 LPA             |
| Marketing & Sales  | INR 15 LPA             |
| Human Resources    | INR 10 LPA             |
| IT & Operations    | INR 14.30 LPA          |

Overall, the highest package across all university placements in 2022 was INR 58 LPA. Top recruiters at ABS have included major companies like Cisco, MTX, Airtel, and many others.

### Ph.D. Placements

For Ph.D. in Management graduates, the salary packages in recent years ranged from INR 1.5 LPA to INR 5 LPA. Companies like Adobe and Dabur have been among the recruiters for Ph.D. holders from ABS.

## Campus Facilities

Amity Business School is part of the larger Amity University campus in Sector-125, Noida. The campus is extensive and well-connected, making it accessible via various transport modes. The nearest metro station, Okhla Bird Sanctuary, is a 15-20 minute drive away. For students requiring accommodation, the university provides hostel facilities for both male and female students. These include both air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned residential apartment suites, typically comprising four single rooms with an attached bathroom and a common sitting lounge equipped with amenities like a sofa, cable TV, and refrigerator.

## Rankings and Recognition

Amity University Noida has consistently received recognition for its management programs from reputable ranking bodies like NIRF (National Institutional Ranking Framework), underscoring its standing in the field of management education.
