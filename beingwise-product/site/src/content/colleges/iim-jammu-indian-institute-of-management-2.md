The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Jammu is a prominent management institution in India, known for its focus on developing future business leaders. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff scores, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## IIM Jammu: An Overview

Established as one of the newer IIMs, IIM Jammu has quickly made a mark in the Indian higher education landscape. It aims to provide high-quality management education and foster an environment of academic excellence and practical learning.

### Campus and Facilities

IIM Jammu's campus spans 200 acres, offering a modern learning environment. Key facilities include:

*   Contemporary classrooms equipped with the latest technology
*   A comprehensive library with extensive resources
*   Medical and sports facilities for student well-being
*   An auditorium for events and conferences
*   On-campus mess and banking services

Residential facilities are available for students, with separate hostels for boys and girls, ensuring a safe and secure living environment.

## Programs Offered and Fees

IIM Jammu offers a range of postgraduate management programs designed to cater to diverse career aspirations. The primary full-time programs are the Master of Business Administration (MBA) and the MBA in Hospital Administration & Health Care Management (HA & HM).

### MBA Program

*   **Duration:** 2 years
*   **Eligibility:** Bachelor's degree from a recognized university with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
*   **Selection Criteria:** CAT score, Personal Interview, and a review of the candidate's overall profile.
*   **Total Seat Intake:** 240 students
*   **Total Tuition Fee:** Approximately INR 10.5 lakhs

### MBA in Hospital Administration & Health Care Management (HA & HM)

*   **Duration:** 2 years
*   **Eligibility:** Bachelor's degree from a recognized university with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
*   **Selection Criteria:** CAT score, Personal Interview, and a review of the candidate's overall profile.
*   **Total Seat Intake:** 60 students
*   **Total Tuition Fee:** Approximately INR 10.5 lakhs

### Executive MBA Program

IIM Jammu also offers an Executive MBA, a two-year blended-mode program tailored for working professionals. It is designed for mid to senior-level executives who wish to enhance their management skills without significant disruption to their careers.

*   **Duration:** 2 years
*   **Mode:** Blended (combining online and in-person sessions)
*   **Eligibility:** Bachelor's degree recognized by UGC/AIU with a minimum of 50% aggregate (47% for NC-OBC, 45% for SC/ST/DAP), plus a minimum of three years of full-time work experience.

The tuition fees mentioned are for the program's core components and do not include other charges such as examination fees, application fees, or hostel charges. These figures are subject to change, so always check the official IIM Jammu website for the most current information.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to IIM Jammu's MBA and MBA HA & HM programs primarily relies on the Common Admission Test (CAT) score, followed by a Personal Interview (PI) and an evaluation of the candidate's academic and professional profile. Specific cutoff percentiles for CAT vary each year based on the applicant pool and seat availability. Candidates meeting the minimum CAT percentile are shortlisted for the PI round.

## Placements at IIM Jammu

IIM Jammu has a strong track record of placements, attracting a wide array of recruiters from various sectors. The institute's dedicated placement cell works to connect students with leading companies.

## Faculty and Infrastructure

IIM Jammu boasts a highly qualified and experienced faculty. Most faculty members hold PhDs in their respective fields, bringing a wealth of academic knowledge and industry insights to the classroom. The remaining faculty members are also well-educated and possess expertise relevant to their courses.

The institute's infrastructure supports a holistic learning experience, including modern classrooms, a well-stocked library, and various facilities for student development and welfare.
