SRM Easwari Engineering College, located in Chennai, is a well-regarded institution offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

SRM Easwari Engineering College primarily offers Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) and Master of Technology (M.Tech) degrees, affiliated with Anna University.

### B.Tech Programs

The college offers a four-year B.Tech program across 15 specializations. These programs are designed to provide a strong foundation in various engineering disciplines.

### M.Tech Programs

For those looking to specialize further, the college provides a two-year M.Tech program. This postgraduate course allows students to deepen their knowledge in specific engineering fields.

## Admission Process

Admission to both B.Tech and M.Tech programs at SRM Easwari Engineering College follows a structured process involving eligibility checks, entrance exams (where applicable), and counselling.

### B.Tech Admission

*   **Eligibility**: Candidates must have completed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics from a recognized board.
*   **Selection**: Admission is merit-based, primarily considering marks obtained in the Class 12 qualifying examination.
*   **Counselling**: Final seat allocation for B.Tech programs is done through the Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) counselling process, conducted online.

### M.Tech Admission

*   **Eligibility**: Applicants need a bachelor's degree in any engineering stream.
*   **Entrance Exams**: Candidates must qualify for either the state-level TANCET exam (conducted by Anna University) or the national-level GATE exam. Scores from one of these exams are essential.
*   **Application**: After qualifying the entrance exam, candidates submit their scores along with an online application to the college.

### Application Procedure

For B.Tech admissions, prospective students need to apply through the official TNEA Counselling website. For M.Tech, applicants should visit the SRM Easwari Engineering College's official website to fill out the online application form. Additionally, M.Tech aspirants must apply and appear for their chosen entrance exam (TANCET or GATE) through their respective official websites.

## Fee Structure

Understanding the financial commitment is crucial for prospective students and their families.

### B.Tech Fees

The tuition fee for the four-year B.Tech program at SRM Easwari Engineering College generally ranges between INR 1.5 Lakhs and INR 2 Lakhs. This range applies across all B.Tech specializations. Please note that these figures are based on official information and are subject to change.

### M.Tech Fees

Specific fee details for M.Tech programs can be obtained directly from the college's official website or admission office.
