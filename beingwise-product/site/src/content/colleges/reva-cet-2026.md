REVA University Common Entrance Test (REVA CET) is an important pathway for students aspiring to join various undergraduate and postgraduate programs at REVA University. This guide offers a comprehensive look at the courses, fees, admission process, expected cutoffs, and placement opportunities for the 2026 academic year.

## REVA CET 2026: Key Information

The REVA CET is designed to assess candidates' aptitude for their chosen fields of study. It's crucial for prospective students to understand the requirements and timelines to ensure a smooth application process.

### Eligibility Criteria for B.Tech Programs

To be considered for B.Tech admissions through REVA CET, candidates must meet specific academic qualifications:

*   **Academic Qualification:** You must have successfully completed your Pre-University Course (PUC) or 12th standard examination.
*   **Minimum Marks:** A minimum aggregate of 45% marks is required in the qualifying examination. For candidates belonging to the SC/ST categories, this requirement is relaxed to 40%.
*   **Compulsory Subjects:** In your 12th standard or equivalent exam, Physics and Mathematics are mandatory subjects. Additionally, you must have studied at least one of the following: Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, Computer Science, Electronics, or any Technical Vocational subject.

### Application Process

The application form for REVA CET 2026 is typically released online. Here's what you need to know:

*   **Online Submission:** Applications must be submitted online through the official REVA University website.
*   **Required Documents:** You will need to upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature. Ensure these are clear and correctly formatted to avoid issues during upload.
*   **No Offline Submission:** Hard copies of the application form are generally not accepted, nor are printouts required to be sent to the university.

### REVA CET Syllabus and Preparation

Understanding the syllabus is vital for effective preparation. The B.Tech REVA CET typically covers subjects aligned with the 12th standard curriculum, along with an aptitude component.

*   **Core Subjects:** The main subjects for the B.Tech exam are Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
*   **Aptitude Section:** In addition to the core subjects, candidates should prepare for Verbal Ability and Reasoning sections. Reviewing previous years' papers can be particularly helpful for understanding the types of questions asked in this section.
*   **Study Plan:** Familiarizing yourself with the detailed syllabus allows you to create a structured study timetable and focus on key topics.

### Admit Card and Examination Day Guidelines

*   **Admit Card Access:** Your REVA CET admit card will be available online. You'll need your application number, date of birth, and password to download it.
*   **Mandatory Documents at Centre:** On the day of the exam, you must carry your REVA CET admit card and a valid photo identity proof. Acceptable IDs include Aadhaar Card, Passport, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driver's License, or any other government-approved photo ID.
*   **Identity Verification:** Without these documents, you will not be permitted to enter the examination hall or attempt the test.
*   **Admit Card Discrepancies:** If you find any errors on your admit card, contact the university authorities immediately for correction.

## Admission Process

Admission to REVA University programs is primarily through the REVA CET, though other national-level entrance exams might also be considered for specific courses. The general steps involve:

1.  **Application:** Submit the online application form for REVA CET.
2.  **Entrance Exam:** Appear for the REVA CET on the scheduled date.
3.  **Merit List:** A merit list will be prepared based on REVA CET scores.
4.  **Counselling/Interview:** Shortlisted candidates may be called for counselling or an interview, depending on the program.
5.  **Document Verification & Fee Payment:** Final admission is confirmed after successful document verification and payment of the first installment of fees.
