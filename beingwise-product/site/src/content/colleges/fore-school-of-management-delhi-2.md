FORE School of Management, located in Delhi, is a well-regarded institution for postgraduate management education. This guide provides a clear overview of its programs, costs, application process, and career outcomes to help prospective students make informed choices.

## Programs Offered at FORE School of Management

FORE School of Management offers a range of full-time postgraduate diploma programs designed to equip students with essential management skills. These include specialized options alongside a general management track.

*   **PGDM (Post Graduate Diploma in Management):** This is a flagship two-year program covering various aspects of business administration.
*   **PGDM (International Business - IB):** Focuses on global trade, international finance, and cross-cultural management.
*   **PGDM (Financial Management - FM):** Specializes in financial markets, investment banking, corporate finance, and risk management.
*   **PGDM (Business Analytics - BDA):** Concentrates on data analysis, business intelligence, and decision-making using analytical tools.
*   **FPM (Fellow Programme in Management):** A doctoral-level program for those interested in research and academia.

## Fees and Costs

Understanding the financial commitment is crucial. Here's a breakdown of the fees for key programs:

### PGDM Programs (General, IB, FM, BDA)

For the two-year full-time PGDM programs, the total tuition fee is approximately **INR 23.2 Lakhs**. This covers the academic costs for the entire duration of the course.

### FPM Program

For the Fellow Programme in Management, the tuition fee is around **INR 3.1 Lakhs**. Additionally, there are one-time charges, such as a library security deposit and an institute security deposit, totaling **INR 64,000**, which are payable at the time of admission. It's important to remember that these figures are based on recent information and can be subject to change.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to FORE School of Management's programs is competitive and involves several stages.

### Eligibility Criteria

For PGDM programs, candidates typically need a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university or institute with a minimum of 50% marks. Specific eligibility for the FPM program may vary and usually requires a Master's degree or equivalent.

### Application Procedure

1.  **Entrance Exam:** Applicants must appear for a valid management entrance exam such as CAT, XAT, GMAT, or CMAT.
2.  **Application Form:** Submit an online application form through the FORE School of Management website.
3.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are shortlisted based on their entrance exam scores, academic record, and work experience (if any).
4.  **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates participate in Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds.
5.  **Final Offer:** Admission offers are extended based on a composite score derived from entrance exam performance, GD/PI, academic profile, and work experience.

## Cutoff Scores

FORE School of Management considers scores from various national-level management entrance exams. While the exact cutoffs can vary each year based on the applicant pool and program demand, here are typical ranges for the CAT percentile, which is a widely accepted exam:

*   **CAT Percentile:** Generally, a CAT percentile in the **85th to 90th range** and above is considered competitive for admission to the PGDM programs. However, candidates should aim for the highest possible score as other factors also play a significant role in the final selection.

## Placements

FORE School of Management has a strong track record of placements, attracting leading companies from various sectors. The institute's dedicated placement cell works to connect students with suitable career opportunities.

### Key Placement Highlights

*   **Average Salary:** The average salary package offered to students is consistently competitive, reflecting the quality of education and industry relevance of the programs.
*   **Highest Salary:** Students often secure impressive highest salary packages, demonstrating the potential for high-earning careers.
*   **Recruiting Sectors:** Top recruiters come from diverse sectors including Consulting, BFSI (Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance), IT & ITES, E-commerce, Manufacturing, FMCG, and Analytics.
*   **Top Recruiters:** Many well-known national and multinational companies participate in the placement drives, offering roles across various functional areas like marketing, finance, operations, HR, and analytics.

## Scholarships at FORE School of Management

FORE School of Management is committed to supporting deserving students through various scholarship schemes. These scholarships aim to ease the financial burden and encourage academic excellence.

### Types of Scholarships Offered

*   **Means-cum-Merit Scholarship:** Awarded to students who demonstrate both academic merit and financial need.
*   **Merit Scholarship:** Recognizes students with outstanding academic achievements.
*   **Scholarship for Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh and North-Eastern States:** Special scholarships to promote education for students from these regions.

### How to Apply for Scholarships

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Scholarship application forms are available online.
2.  **Check Eligibility:** Carefully review the specific eligibility criteria for each scholarship.
3.  **Fill Details Accurately:** Ensure all information provided matches official documents.
4.  **Submit Required Documents:** Provide necessary supporting documents as requested.
5.  **Apply Online:** Complete and submit the scholarship application form through the online portal.

These scholarships are available to eligible first and second-year students across the PGDM, PGDM (IB), PGDM (FM), and PGDM (BDA) programs.
