## SPSAT 2026: An Overview

SPSAT (presumably the Sri Padmavati School Admission Test, though the source does not explicitly state the full form) is an important entrance examination for students aspiring to join various programs. Understanding the structure and requirements of this test is crucial for effective preparation.

### Exam Format and Key Details

*   **Duration:** The SPSAT exam lasts for 60 minutes.
*   **Questions:** You will face 40 objective-type Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).
*   **Marking Scheme:** One mark is awarded for each correct answer. There is **no negative marking** for incorrect responses, so it's advisable to attempt all questions.
*   **Medium:** The examination is conducted entirely in English.
*   **Syllabus:** The question paper is based on the official SPSAT syllabus, and candidates should cover all prescribed topics thoroughly.

## Admission Process

The admission process for SPSAT 2026 generally involves several stages:

1.  **Application:** Candidates must first register and complete the online application form within the specified deadlines.
2.  **Admit Card:** Eligible candidates will receive their admit cards online. This document is essential for appearing in the exam and contains vital information like your name, exam center, date, time, and important guidelines.
3.  **Entrance Exam (SPSAT):** Appear for the online examination as per the schedule mentioned on your admit card.
4.  **Result Declaration:** The SPSAT results will be published online, detailing your marks and qualifying status.
5.  **Counselling and Allotment:** Based on your SPSAT score and rank, you will be invited for counselling sessions where you can choose your preferred course and institution. Final admission is subject to meeting eligibility criteria and document verification.
