The KIIT School of Rural Management (KSRM) in Bhubaneswar is a well-regarded institution dedicated to rural and agri-business management education. This guide provides a clear overview of its programs, costs, application process, and career opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at KSRM

KSRM specializes in management programs tailored for the rural sector and agribusiness. The institute offers two distinct MBA specializations:

*   **MBA in Rural Management:** This program focuses on developing professionals equipped to manage rural development initiatives, non-profit organizations, and businesses operating in rural economies.
*   **MBA in Agri-Business Management:** Designed for those interested in the agricultural sector, this program covers the business aspects of farming, food processing, supply chain management, and agricultural marketing.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admissions to the MBA programs at KSRM are currently open. Prospective students can apply online through the institute's official portal.

### Eligibility Criteria

Candidates generally need a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university. A crucial part of the application is a valid score from one of the following national-level management entrance exams:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   MAT (Management Aptitude Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)

There is no specified age limit for applicants to KSRM's programs.

### Application Fee

The application fee for the KIITEE Management exam, which is part of the admission process for KSRM, is INR 1,500. This fee must be paid online using a debit card, credit card, or net banking. It's important to note that the application form will not be considered submitted until the fee is paid, and this fee is non-refundable.

## Fees Structure

Specific detailed program fees for the MBA courses are typically updated annually on the KSRM website. However, the application fee for the KIITEE Management exam is INR 1,500.

## Campus Life and Facilities

KSRM offers a vibrant campus environment spread over approximately one acre. The infrastructure is modern, featuring technologically equipped classrooms, dedicated faculty offices, common areas, a conference hall, a cafeteria, a computer lab, and a well-stocked library. The institute also provides separate hostel facilities for both male and female students.

## Faculty Expertise

KSRM prides itself on its experienced and supportive faculty members. Many instructors hold advanced degrees such as PhDs, Master's, and Diplomas, and come from esteemed institutions like IRMA, XUB, IITs, and IIMs. Their extensive ground experience and academic backgrounds contribute significantly to the quality of education and student mentorship.

## Why Choose KSRM?

KSRM has established itself as a leading institution in rural management over the past 13 years. Its specific focus on rural and agri-business management, coupled with a dedicated faculty and strong industry connections, makes it an excellent choice for students aspiring to make a difference in these sectors.
