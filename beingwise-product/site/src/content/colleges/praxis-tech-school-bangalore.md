Praxis Tech School, located in Agara, Bangalore, is a well-regarded institution specializing in advanced technology education. It aims to equip students with cutting-edge skills for the evolving tech industry, particularly in data science and related fields.

## Courses Offered at Praxis Tech School

Praxis Tech School focuses on postgraduate and professional development programs designed to meet current industry demands. Their flagship offerings include:

*   **Post-Graduate Program in Data Science with Generative AI & Machine Learning:** This is a comprehensive 9-month full-time program. It's designed to be a pioneering course in data science education in India, integrating advanced concepts like Generative AI and Machine Learning.
*   **Post-Graduate Program in Data Engineering:** Available as a 9-month weekend online program, this course is tailored for working professionals looking to upskill or transition into data engineering roles.
*   **Business Analytics for Working Professionals:** A shorter, 4-month program delivered through a blend of online and classroom sessions, focusing on practical business analytics skills.

## Praxis Tech School Fees

While specific fee structures for each program are best confirmed directly on the official Praxis Tech School website, it's important to note that these are postgraduate and professional development programs, and fees typically reflect the specialized nature and industry relevance of the curriculum. Prospective students should check the official admissions portal for the most current fee details for their chosen course.

## Admission Process

Admission to Praxis Tech School's Post-Graduate Diploma programs is a structured, merit-based process, ensuring that admitted students are well-prepared for the rigorous curriculum. Certification programs, however, may offer direct admission.

The key steps for PG Diploma admissions are:

1.  **Application Process:** Candidates must register and complete the application form on the official Praxis Tech School admissions website (admissions.praxis.ac.in). Ensure all details are accurate as per official documents and submit the required application fee.
2.  **Entrance Exam:** For PG Diploma programs, admission is primarily based on performance in the **Praxis Aptitude Test**. This test assesses a candidate's readiness for the program.
3.  **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates, based on their entrance exam scores, proceed to further evaluation. This typically includes an **Essay Writing** component and a **Personal Interview (PI)** round. These stages help assess communication skills, critical thinking, and motivation.
4.  **Final Selection & Confirmation:** Successful candidates will be notified via email or phone. To secure their place, selected students must pay the specified admission fee by the given deadline.

There is no direct admission for the PG Diploma programs; all candidates must go through the entrance exam and subsequent selection rounds.

## Cutoff Information

Praxis Tech School does not publicly release specific cutoff scores for its entrance exams. Admission decisions are made holistically, considering a candidate's performance in the Praxis Aptitude Test, essay writing, and personal interview. A strong performance across all these selection rounds is crucial for securing a spot in their competitive programs.
