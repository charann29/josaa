Praxis Business School in Kolkata is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. If you're considering a career in business, understanding their offerings, admission process, and career support is key to making an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

Praxis Business School primarily focuses on postgraduate diploma programs designed to equip students with essential management skills. Their flagship offerings include:

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** This comprehensive program provides a broad understanding of business principles and practices.
*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (Marketing Major):** A specialized PGDM program for those aiming for a career in marketing, sales, and branding.

Both PGDM programs have a total intake capacity of 360 students, with 180 seats allocated to each specialization.

## Admission Process

Securing a spot at Praxis Business School involves a clear, multi-stage process. Here's what prospective students need to know:

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the PGDM programs, candidates must have:

*   A bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 50% in their undergraduate studies.
*   Students in their final year of graduation are also welcome to apply, provided they meet the other criteria upon completion of their degree.

### Entrance Exams

A valid score in one of the following national-level management entrance exams is mandatory:

*   CAT
*   XAT
*   MAT
*   CMAT
*   ATMA
*   GMAT

### Application Steps

The application process is primarily online:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the admissions section on the Praxis Business School official website.
2.  **Registration:** Complete the initial registration using a valid email ID and phone number.
3.  **Fill the Application Form:** Provide all necessary personal and academic details accurately.
4.  **Upload Documents:** Submit scanned copies of required documents.
5.  **Pay Application Fee:** Complete the payment for the application fee online and save the payment receipt.
6.  **Submission:** While the primary application is online, candidates may be instructed to download, print, and physically submit the form along with the payment receipt and bank challans to the admissions department.

### Selection Rounds

Shortlisted candidates, based on their entrance exam scores, will proceed to the following rounds:

*   **Essay Writing Test:** To assess written communication and analytical skills.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** To evaluate communication skills, leadership potential, and overall suitability for the program.

Successful candidates will then be invited for document verification and fee payment to finalize their admission.

### Required Documents

When applying and during the final admission stages, keep the following documents ready:

*   Class 10 mark sheet and passing certificate
*   Class 12 mark sheet and passing certificate
*   Graduation or Post-graduation mark sheets and passing certificates (if applicable)
*   Bachelor's degree certificate (if applicable)
*   Passport-sized photograph
*   Aadhar Card
*   Caste Certificate (if applicable)

## Fees Structure
