The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Jammu is a prominent management institution offering a range of programs for aspiring business leaders and professionals. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities.

## IIM Jammu: An Overview

IIM Jammu, established as one of the newer IIMs, has quickly built a reputation for its management education. Located in the picturesque region of Jammu, the institute aims to nurture talent with a strong curriculum and experienced faculty.

### Campus and Facilities

Spanning across 200 acres, the IIM Jammu campus provides a modern learning environment. Key facilities include state-of-the-art classrooms, a comprehensive library, medical services, sports amenities, an auditorium, and banking facilities. The institute also offers safe and secure separate hostel accommodations for male and female students.

## Programs Offered

IIM Jammu offers several postgraduate and executive programs tailored to different career stages.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

This flagship two-year full-time residential program is designed for graduates seeking a comprehensive management education.

*   **Eligibility:** Bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks from a recognized university.
*   **Selection Criteria:** CAT score, performance in Personal Interview, and profile evaluation.
*   **Total Seat Intake:** 240
*   **Total Tuition Fee:** Approximately INR 10.5 Lakhs (subject to change).

### MBA in Hospital Administration & Healthcare Management (MBA HA & HM)

A specialized two-year program focusing on the unique challenges and opportunities within the healthcare sector.

*   **Eligibility:** Bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks from a recognized university.
*   **Selection Criteria:** CAT score, performance in Personal Interview, and profile evaluation.
*   **Total Seat Intake:** 60
*   **Total Tuition Fee:** Approximately INR 10.5 Lakhs (subject to change).

### Executive MBA

Designed for working professionals, this two-year blended-mode program allows mid to senior-level executives to enhance their management skills without significant career disruption.

*   **Eligibility:** Bachelor's degree from a UGC/AIU recognized university with a minimum of 50% aggregate (47% for NC-OBC, 45% for SC/ST/DAP) and a minimum of three years of full-time work experience.
*   **Mode:** Blended (combining online and in-person sessions).

## Fees Structure

The tuition fees for the MBA and MBA HA & HM programs are around INR 10.5 Lakhs each. The overall fee range for programs at IIM Jammu can be between INR 8.4 Lakhs and INR 18 Lakhs. It's important to note that these figures primarily cover tuition and do not include other expenses like application fees, examination fees, hostel charges, or security deposits. Fees are subject to revision by the institute.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to IIM Jammu's MBA programs primarily relies on the Common Admission Test (CAT) scores, followed by a Personal Interview (PI) round and an assessment of the candidate's academic and professional profile. Specific CAT cutoffs vary each year based on the applicant pool and seat availability.

## Faculty

IIM Jammu prides itself on a highly qualified and experienced faculty.
