The University College of Commerce and Business Management (UCCBM) at Osmania University, Hyderabad, stands as a prominent institution for higher education in commerce and business. It offers a range of postgraduate programs designed to equip students with essential skills for the professional world.

## Programs Offered

UCCBM provides diverse postgraduate courses focusing on various aspects of commerce and business management. These programs are structured to offer in-depth knowledge and practical exposure.

*   **Master of Commerce (M.Com):** This program offers specializations, allowing students to delve deeper into specific areas of commerce.
*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** The MBA program includes general management and specialized tracks like Tourism and Travel Management, catering to different career aspirations.

## Fee Structure

The tuition fees for programs at UCCBM vary depending on the specific course and its duration. It's important for prospective students to be aware of these costs when planning their education.

For the two-year M.Com program, fees typically range from INR 41,600 to INR 67,600. MBA program fees also fall within a similar range, with variations based on the specialization chosen. These figures are indicative and are subject to change as per university policies.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to UCCBM's postgraduate programs is competitive and based on entrance examination scores, followed by counselling.

### MBA Admissions

For MBA programs, candidates need to appear for the **TSICET (Telangana State Integrated Common Entrance Test)**. The university releases cutoff ranks based on TSICET scores, which determine eligibility for admission through counselling.

Here's a look at the Round 1 TSICET cutoff ranks for the General Home State Category for recent years:

| Course                               | 2022 Rank | 2023 Rank | 2024 Rank |
| :----------------------------------- | :-------- | :-------- | :-------- |
| MBA in Tourism and Travel Management | 1208      | 1329      | 610       |
| Master of Business Administration    | 345       | 110       | 1372      |

### M.Com Admissions

For M.Com programs, admission is typically based on the **CPGET (Common Post Graduate Entrance Tests)** scores. Osmania University releases CPGET cutoffs, which are the minimum marks or ranks required for candidates to proceed to the next stages of the admission process. These cutoffs are influenced by factors such as the exam's difficulty, the number of applicants, and previous year's trends.
