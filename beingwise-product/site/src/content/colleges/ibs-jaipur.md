# IBS Jaipur: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

IBS Jaipur, established in 2003, is a well-regarded institution recognized by the UGC, focusing on business education. It offers a curriculum designed to meet current industry demands, providing students with strong industry exposure through internships and live projects. If you're considering a management career, here's a detailed look at what IBS Jaipur offers.

## Programs Offered

IBS Jaipur primarily focuses on management education, with its flagship program being the MBA/PGDM. This program allows students to specialize in key business areas.

### MBA/PGDM Specializations

Students pursuing the MBA/PGDM at IBS Jaipur can choose from various specializations to align with their career aspirations:

*   Marketing
*   Finance
*   Operations
*   Human Resources

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Securing a spot at IBS Jaipur involves meeting specific academic and entrance exam requirements. The process is straightforward, beginning with national-level entrance exams and followed by institute-specific selection rounds.

### Eligibility Criteria

For most programs, particularly the MBA/PGDM, candidates need to fulfill the following:

*   A Bachelor's degree with a minimum aggregate of 50% marks.
*   Candidates appearing in their final year of a qualifying degree are also eligible to apply.

### Entrance Exams Accepted

Admission to the MBA/PGDM program at IBS Jaipur is based on scores from recognized management entrance tests. These include:

*   XAT
*   CAT
*   NMAT
*   IBSAT

## How to Apply for IBSAT

The IBSAT examination is a primary pathway for admission to IBS campuses, including Jaipur. The application process is entirely online.

### Key Dates (Tentative for IBSAT 2026)

*   **Registration Start Date:** July 1st
*   **Application Deadline:** Third week of December

### Application Steps

To apply for IBSAT, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official IBS website.
2.  Click on the "Apply now" tab to create your IBSAT login credentials.
3.  Complete the online application form, providing all necessary personal and academic details.
4.  Review your application thoroughly for any errors before proceeding.
5.  Pay the application fee of INR 1800. Payment can be made using Credit/Debit cards, Net Banking, or UPI.

*Note: Ensure you meet all eligibility criteria before applying, as incomplete or ineligible applications will be cancelled.*

## Placements at IBS Jaipur

IBS Jaipur has a strong track record of placements for its MBA and BBA students, with a high percentage of students securing offers each year. The institute emphasizes preparing students for the corporate world, reflected in its placement statistics.

### Placement Highlights (Class of 2025)

The most recent placement drive for the Class of 2025 showcased impressive figures:

*   **Placement Percentage:** 95%
*   **Average Package:** INR 7.14 LPA
*   **Highest Domestic Package:** INR 12.76 LPA
*   **Highest International Package:** INR 41.40 LPA
