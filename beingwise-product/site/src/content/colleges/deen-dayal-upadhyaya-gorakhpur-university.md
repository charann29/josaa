Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Gorakhpur University, located in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. ## Courses and Fees

DDU Gorakhpur University offers diverse programs across various disciplines, including arts, science, commerce, law, and more. The fee structure can vary significantly depending on the specific course and whether it's offered directly by the university or an affiliated college. ## Admission Process

Admission to DDU Gorakhpur University programs typically involves an application process followed by merit-based selection or an entrance examination, depending on the course. Here's a general outline of the steps:

1.  **Application:** Candidates must complete and submit the application form for their desired course.
2.  **Document Submission:** After successful registration, applicants need to provide several documents for verification. For a B.Com course, for example, these include:
    *   A completed application form.
    *   Passport-sized photographs.
    *   Two proofs of current residence (e.g., Aadhar card, Voter ID).
    *   Self-attested copies of relevant academic mark sheets (qualifying exam).
    *   Category certificate (if applicable).
    *   College admission fee slip.
3.  **Selection:** Admissions are generally based on academic merit or performance in an entrance test conducted by the university.

## Faculty and Infrastructure

DDU Gorakhpur University boasts a dedicated team of over 250 teaching staff and more than 300 non-teaching staff. The teaching faculty are highly experienced, with many holding master's or PhD degrees in diverse fields such as Law, Engineering, Sociology, Biology, Commerce, Botany, Economics, and Physics.

The university provides a modern and well-equipped infrastructure to support its students' academic and personal growth. Key facilities include:

*   **Central Library:** A comprehensive resource hub for students and faculty.
*   **Hostel Facilities:** Accommodation is available for both male and female students. There are eight hostels in total, including Sant Kabir Hostel, Gautam Buddha Hostel, Swami Vivekananda Hostel, Nath Chandrawat Hostel, Late R. P. Shukla Hostel for boys, and Maharani Laxmibai Hostel, Alaknanda Hostel for girls, alongside an International Hostel.
*   **Other Amenities:** State-of-the-art facilities designed to enhance the overall learning experience.
