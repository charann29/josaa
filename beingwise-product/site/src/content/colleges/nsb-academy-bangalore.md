Deciding on the right college is a big step, and NSB Academy in Bangalore offers a range of management and commerce programs that might interest you. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about their courses, fees, how to get in, and what kind of career opportunities await.

## Programs and Fees at NSB Academy

NSB Academy provides full-time undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Here’s a breakdown of the courses and their approximate total fees:

*   **BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration):** This is a three-year program designed to build a strong foundation in business principles. The total fee for the BBA program is around INR 3 Lakhs.
*   **B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce):** A three-year program focusing on commerce and accounting. The total fee for B.Com is approximately INR 2.6 Lakhs.
*   **MBA/PGDM (Master of Business Administration / Post Graduate Diploma in Management):** This is a two-year, full-time postgraduate program. The total fee for the MBA/PGDM program is around INR 8.3 Lakhs.

Additionally, NSB Academy offers an MBA+MIP program, which is a two-year, full-time postgraduate course spread across four semesters, providing dual certification with a UGC-recognized MBA degree.

Hostel facilities are available, with an annual fee of approximately INR 1,32,000, which covers both accommodation and food.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Getting into NSB Academy involves meeting specific eligibility criteria and successfully completing the selection rounds.

### For BBA and B.Com Programs

*   **Eligibility:** Candidates must have successfully completed Class 12 from a recognized board with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. A relaxed criterion of 45% aggregate is applicable for reserved categories.
*   **Selection Process:** Admission for these undergraduate programs is primarily merit-based, evaluated through a Telephonic Interview or Personal Interview.

### For MBA/PGDM Programs

*   **Eligibility:** Applicants need a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   **Selection Process:** The admission procedure for MBA/PGDM is as follows:
    1.  **Entrance Exam Score:** You must have a valid score from one of the common management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, ATMA, KMAT, or GMAT.
    2.  **Shortlisting:** Based on your entrance exam scores, candidates will be shortlisted for the subsequent rounds.
    3.  **Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates will participate in a Group Discussion, followed by a Personal Interview (which might be telephonic).
    4.  **Final Selection:** Successful candidates from the GD and PI rounds will be offered admission. To confirm your seat for the MBA program, an admission fee of INR 30,000 is required.

After qualifying, you will need to report to the admissions cell at NSB Academy to complete formalities like document verification and fee payment, which finalizes your seat allotment.
