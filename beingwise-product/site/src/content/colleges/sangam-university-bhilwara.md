Sangam University, located in Bhilwara, Rajasthan, is a well-regarded institution established in 2012. It offers a wide array of programs from undergraduate to doctoral levels, aiming to provide quality education and prepare students for various professional fields.

## Courses Offered at Sangam University

Sangam University provides a diverse range of programs across multiple disciplines, catering to students with different academic interests and career aspirations. These include:

*   **Undergraduate (UG) Programs:** Bachelor's degrees in various fields.
*   **Postgraduate (PG) Programs:** Master's degrees in specialized areas.
*   **Doctoral (PhD) Programs:** Research-focused studies for advanced learners.
*   **Polytechnic and Diploma Courses:** Vocational and skill-based training.

### Popular M.Tech Specializations

The university offers several M.Tech specializations, allowing students to delve deep into specific engineering domains. Some of these include:

*   Power Systems & Power Electronics
*   Computer Science & Engineering
* Information Technology
*   Digital Communication
*   VLSI (Very Large Scale Integration)
*   Electronics
*   Production Engineering

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to Sangam University programs is based on specific eligibility criteria and, for some courses, entrance examination scores. Here's a general overview:

### B.Tech Admissions

For admission to the Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program, candidates typically need to qualify either:

*   **SUAT (Sangam University Admission Test)**
*   **JEE Main**

### PhD Admissions

Prospective PhD candidates must hold a Master's degree in a relevant subject with a minimum aggregate score of 55%.
