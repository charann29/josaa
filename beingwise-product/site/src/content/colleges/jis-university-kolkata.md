JIS University in Kolkata is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of programs for students looking to pursue higher education. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structures, admission procedures, eligibility criteria, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered at JIS University

JIS University provides diverse courses across undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG), and doctoral (PhD) levels. Some of the popular programs include B.Tech, B.Pharma, MBA, and PhD. Additionally, the university offers specialized diploma courses such as a Diploma in Hotel Operation and a Diploma in Cruise Line Operation.

### Current Admissions Status

Admissions for various programs at JIS University are currently open. Prospective students are encouraged to visit the official university website to register and apply. While there isn't a stated application deadline for all programs, it's advisable to complete your application as soon as possible to secure your spot.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Selection for courses at JIS University primarily depends on scores obtained in relevant entrance examinations. The university conducts its own entrance test, the JIS University Entrance Test (JET), for several programs. This is often followed by group discussions (GD) and personal interviews (PI) for certain postgraduate courses.

### MBA Admissions

For the two-year, full-time MBA program, candidates must meet specific eligibility requirements. This includes holding a bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks. Admission to the MBA program is based on performance in the university-level JET, followed by a GD-PI round.

### D.Pharm Admissions

Admission to the Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm) program also requires candidates to appear for the JIS University Entrance Test (JET). To be eligible, applicants must have completed Class 12 with a minimum of 60% aggregate, with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects, along with Mathematics or Biology.

## Fee Structure Overview

JIS University's fee structure includes various components like tuition fees and caution money. It's important to note that fees can be revised by the university for each academic year. A portion of the total fee is typically required upon selection to confirm your admission.

Here's an indicative look at the tuition fees for a couple of programs:

| Course        | Total Tuition Fee (Indicative) |

| D.Pharm       | INR 1.7 lakh                   |
| BMLT (3-year) | INR 2.46 lakh                  |

*Please remember that these figures are based on previous information and are subject to change. Always verify the latest fee details on the official university website.*

## Placement Opportunities at JIS University

JIS University has a strong track record of placements, attracting numerous recruiters annually across diverse sectors. The university consistently achieves good placement rates for its students across various courses like B.Tech, MBA, and M.Tech.

The 2025 placement season saw over 152 recruiters participating, leading to a high placement rate for the graduating batch. Students secured roles in sectors such as BFSI, IT, Education, FMCG, Consulting, and E-Commerce.
