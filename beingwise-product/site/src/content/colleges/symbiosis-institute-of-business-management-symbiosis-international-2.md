The Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM) Hyderabad, located in Nandigama, is a well-regarded institution offering a comprehensive MBA program. This page provides essential information on its courses, fees, admission process, selection criteria, and placement records to help prospective students make informed decisions.

## Programs and Fees at SIBM Hyderabad

SIBM Hyderabad primarily focuses on its flagship Master of Business Administration (MBA) program. The institute aims to provide a robust management education that prepares students for the corporate world.

### MBA Program Fees

The application fee for the MBA program at SIBM Hyderabad is INR 1,000. Additionally, candidates must pay a fee of INR 2,250 for the Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP). Both these payments can be made conveniently through online payment methods.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the MBA program at SIBM Hyderabad is exclusively through the Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP). Meeting the eligibility criteria and performing well in SNAP are crucial steps for prospective students.

### Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have a valid bachelor's degree from a recognized university with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for SC/ST candidates). Final year degree students are also eligible to apply, provided they complete their degree requirements within the stipulated time.

### Application Steps

The application process for the MBA program at SIBM Hyderabad involves these key steps:

*   **SNAP Registration:** Register for the Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP) online and pay the application fee.
*   **Appear for SNAP:** Take the SNAP examination on the scheduled date.
*   **SIBM Hyderabad Application:** After receiving your SNAP scores, visit the official SIBM Hyderabad website.
*   **Apply for MBA:** Locate the 'Apply for MBA' option, register or log in using your SNAP credentials, and complete the institute-specific application form.
*   **Fee Payment:** Pay the SIBM Hyderabad application fee online.

It's important to note that direct admission without appearing for the SNAP exam is not possible for the MBA program.

## Selection Criteria and Cutoff

Admission decisions at SIBM Hyderabad are based on a holistic evaluation, with SNAP scores being a primary factor. While specific cutoff scores can vary each year depending on the applicant pool and the difficulty of the exam, candidates generally need a strong SNAP percentile to be considered for further rounds. The selection process typically includes a Group Exercise (GE), Personal Interview (PI), and Written Ability Test (WAT) for shortlisted candidates.

## Campus Life and Facilities

SIBM Hyderabad offers a comfortable living and learning environment for its students. The institute provides hostel facilities with various seating options (2, 3, and 4-seater rooms), all equipped with attached bathrooms and essential amenities. The mess food is generally well-regarded, with a student team actively involved in menu planning to ensure variety and quality. Beyond academics, the campus fosters a vibrant community where students can engage in various co-curricular activities and personal development.
