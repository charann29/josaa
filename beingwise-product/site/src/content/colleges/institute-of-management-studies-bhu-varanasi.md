The Institute of Management Studies (IMS) at Banaras Hindu University (BHU) in Varanasi is a well-regarded institution for management education in India. It offers various postgraduate and doctoral programs, attracting students from across the country. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, admission process, fees, and career opportunities.

## Courses Offered at IMS BHU

IMS BHU provides a range of management programs designed to equip students with essential business skills and knowledge. The primary offerings include:

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** This flagship program offers specializations across various management functions.
*   **MBA in International Business (MBA IB):** Focused on global business practices and international trade.
*   **MBA in Agri-Business (MBA AB):** Tailored for careers in the agricultural and food sectors.
*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Business Analytics (PGDBA):** A specialized program for those interested in data-driven decision-making.
*   **Doctor of Philosophy (PhD):** For students pursuing advanced research in management.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admissions at IMS BHU are primarily entrance-based, ensuring a competitive selection process. The institute emphasizes a holistic evaluation of candidates.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for MBA programs, candidates must meet the following requirements:

*   A Bachelor's Degree (10+2+3 pattern) from a recognized university.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 50% in their undergraduate studies. (For SC/ST candidates, this is relaxed to 45%).

### Selection Process

The selection for MBA and MBA IB programs involves multiple stages:

1.  **Entrance Exam:** Performance in the Common Admission Test (CAT) conducted by the IIMs holds significant weightage (50%).
2.  **Academic Records:** Your past academic performance (20% weightage) is also considered.
3.  **Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI):** Candidates are shortlisted for GD and PI rounds, which collectively account for 30% of the selection criteria.

For PhD admissions, candidates are evaluated based on their scores in the Common Research Entrance Test (CRET) or Non-CRET criteria, followed by further selection processes.

## IMS BHU Cutoff

While specific cutoffs can vary each year, historical data suggests that a strong CAT percentile is crucial for admission to IMS BHU's MBA programs. In recent years, an overall CAT percentile of around 85 has been a general benchmark for qualifying for the admission process.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for various programs at IMS BHU is determined by the university and is subject to revision. It's always advisable to check the official IMS BHU website for the most current and detailed fee information for your chosen program.

## Placements at IMS BHU

IMS BHU has a dedicated placement cell that works diligently to connect students with leading companies. The institute has a strong track record of successful placements, with students securing roles across diverse industries. The placement cell focuses on preparing students through case studies, real-life projects, group discussions, and interactions with industry experts and alumni.

### Placement Highlights (Based on 2025 data)

*   **Highest Package:** INR 19.8 LPA
