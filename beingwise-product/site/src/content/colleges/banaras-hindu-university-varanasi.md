Banaras Hindu University (BHU), located in the ancient city of Varanasi, is a renowned institution offering a wide array of courses. For students and parents considering higher education, understanding BHU's offerings, admission process, fees, and career prospects is crucial. This guide provides a clear overview to help you make an informed decision.

## Courses and Admission Process

BHU provides a diverse selection of undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) programs across various disciplines. Admission to most UG courses, including the popular B.Tech program, is primarily based on scores from the Common University Entrance Test (CUET) UG. For postgraduate studies, like the MBA, while the CAT entrance test is generally considered for the regular MBA, some specialized MBA courses may accept CUET-PG scores.

### B.Tech Programs

BHU offers a four-year Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) course, structured into eight semesters. Prospective students must meet the specific eligibility criteria for their chosen specialization and apply through the CUET website.

### MBA Programs

The university offers a two-year full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) at the postgraduate level. Admission typically involves a valid CAT score, followed by Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds. However, for certain specialized MBA programs, a valid CUET-PG score can also be considered. Applicants should check the official BHU website for detailed eligibility requirements and application procedures.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure at BHU varies significantly depending on the course and category. For precise and up-to-date fee information, it is always recommended to visit the official Banaras Hindu University website.

## Cutoff Information

BHU releases cutoffs for admission to its UG and PG courses based on entrance exam scores. These cutoffs can fluctuate each year based on factors like the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. For example, in a recent admission cycle (Round 4 of CUET UG), cutoff scores for various courses ranged widely. A score of 200 in CUET could be sufficient for admission to some courses, while others like B.Sc. (Hons) in Medical Radiology and Imaging Technology required scores up to 628.46 for the General category.

## Hostel Facilities

BHU prioritizes hostel accommodation for students coming from outside Varanasi. During the allocation process, preference is given to outstation students to ensure they have suitable living arrangements. Local students are considered for hostel allotment once the needs of outstation students have been met.
