Calcutta University, situated in Kolkata, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of programs. For many students and parents, understanding the academic offerings, financial aspects, and career prospects is key to making an informed decision. This guide provides a clear overview of the university's courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff scores, and placement records.

## Why Consider Calcutta University?

Choosing a university is a significant decision, and Calcutta University presents several compelling advantages:

*   **Affordable Education:** As a government-funded institution, Calcutta University maintains significantly lower tuition fees compared to many private universities. For instance, the B.Tech program typically ranges from INR 30,800 to INR 40,500.
*   **Strong Return on Investment (ROI):** The university boasts a commendable placement record. In 2024, for its two-year postgraduate programs, 1,800 students secured placements with a median salary of INR 11 LPA. Similarly, six students from the three-year postgraduate programs in 2024 achieved a median salary of INR 9 LPA.
*   **Extensive Scholarship Opportunities:** To promote equitable access to education, Calcutta University provides over 80 different scholarships. These scholarships cater to various academic fields and student needs. Some examples include:
    *   **Anil Chandra Gupta Memorial Studentship:** For students in Bengali, Philosophy.
    *   **Bhupendranath Memorial Scholarship:** For Sanskrit scholars.
    *   **Nalinakhya Dutta Stipend:** For Pali studies.
    *   **Ganada Sundari Sarkar Memorial Scholarship:** For Bengali.
    *   **Hasimohon Kr. And Tulsi Mani Kr. Scholarship:** For B.A. students.
    *   **Arati Bakshi Memorial Scholarship:** For Economics.
    *   **Dr. Hensi Stephen Scholarship:** For English.
*   **Robust Alumni Network:** The university prides itself on a strong and active alumni community, which can be a valuable resource for current students and graduates.

### Median Salary Packages (2023)

While the highest package details for 2024 are still awaited, the median packages for 2023, as per the NIRF 2024 report, provide a good indication of salary trends. The highest median package in 2023 was INR 10 LPA, observed for both two-year postgraduate programs and B.Tech students. Here's a breakdown by course:

*   **PG (3-year):** INR 9.90 LPA
*   **B.Tech:** INR 10 LPA
*   **PG (2-year):** INR 10 LPA
*   **UG (3-year):** INR 9.20 LPA
*   **PG (5-year):** INR 8.80 LPA
*   **BA LLB:** INR 8 LPA

## Admission and Cutoffs

For admission to its B.Tech programs, including various specializations, Calcutta University considers scores from the WBJEE examination. This is followed by the WBJEE Counselling process.

### B.Tech CSE Cutoff

For Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), the cutoff ranks vary based on categories and quotas (All India and Home State). For the General All India category, the overall B.Tech CSE cutoff in 2025 was 545 in the first round and 980 in the final round. It's important for prospective students to remember that cutoffs for other categories and quotas will differ.

## Application Process

Calcutta University primarily facilitates its application process online. Prospective students should visit the university's official website to access and complete the application forms. Currently, there is no stated provision for offline applications for most programs, including those offered by the Faculty of Law.
