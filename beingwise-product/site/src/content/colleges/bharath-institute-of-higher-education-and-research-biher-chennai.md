Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research (BIHER), located in Chennai, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of academic programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, academic requirements, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered and Fees

BIHER provides diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. The fee structure varies significantly depending on the course chosen. For instance, the total fee for a D.Pharma program is approximately INR 4 Lakhs. It's always advisable to check the official BIHER website for the most current and detailed fee information for your specific program of interest.

### Eligibility Criteria for Key Undergraduate Courses

To help you understand the requirements, here's a summary of the eligibility for some popular undergraduate courses:

*   **BA in Agriculture:** Candidates need to have successfully completed their 10+2 or an equivalent examination with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology.
*   **BSc in Catering Science and Hotel Management:** Applicants should have passed their 10+2 (Higher Secondary) in any stream, or possess a 3-year Diploma, with an aggregate score of over 50%.
*   **BSc in Computer Science with a specialization in Data Analytics (IOA):** A pass in 10+2 or an equivalent qualification with Mathematics or Computer Science as a subject is required.

### Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharma) Eligibility

For the D.Pharma program, candidates must have completed their Class 12th or an equivalent examination from a recognized board or institute, with a minimum aggregate of 45%. Students who are appearing for their final year examinations can also apply for provisional admission.

## Admission Process

The admission process at BIHER is primarily conducted online through the university's official website. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the Bharath University official admissions portal.
2.  **Registration:** Register on the portal using a valid email address and mobile number.
3.  **Application Form:** Fill out the online application form with all the necessary personal and academic details.
4.  **Document Upload:** Upload scanned copies of all required documents, such as academic transcripts and identity proofs.
5.  **Application Fee:** Pay the non-refundable application fee through the available online payment options.
6.  **Submission:** Review all the entered information carefully and submit your application.

After submitting the application, the university will review and shortlist candidates based on their qualifications and eligibility. Shortlisted applicants will receive an admission offer and will need to pay a confirmation fee to secure their seat.

## Placement Opportunities

BIHER has a strong track record of placements, with a significant number of students securing positions in various industries. The placement cell actively works to connect students with leading recruiters.

In recent placement drives, BIHER has seen impressive figures. In 2022, a total of 1,616 students were successfully placed. Many top companies, including Infosys, TCS, Accenture, Cognizant, HCL, Amazon, and Tech Mahindra, are regular recruiters at BIHER.

## Rankings and Reputation

Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research holds a respectable position in national rankings. In 2023, the university was ranked 66th in the University Category by NIRF (National Institutional Ranking Framework), highlighting its standing among Indian universities.

BIHER offers a comprehensive educational experience with a focus on academic rigor and career readiness. Its strong placements and recognized rankings make it an attractive option for students seeking higher education in Chennai.
