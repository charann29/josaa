The Delhi School of Business (DSB) is a well-regarded institution in the capital city, offering various postgraduate programs. If you're considering a management education, understanding its offerings, admission process, and career prospects is key. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about DSB.

## Programs Offered

DSB primarily focuses on postgraduate management education. Their flagship program is the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM), which is a two-year, full-time course. This program is designed to equip students with comprehensive business knowledge and practical skills across various specializations.

For those interested in research and academia, DSB also offers a Fellow Programme in Management (FPM).

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to DSB's programs is competitive and involves several stages. Here's a general overview:

### PGDM Admission

To be eligible for the PGDM program, candidates must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. The method of calculating marks or converting grades/CGPA to percentages will follow the university's own guidelines.

The selection process typically includes:

*   **Entrance Exam Scores:** DSB accepts scores from major national-level management entrance exams.
*   **Group Discussion (GD):** Shortlisted candidates participate in a group discussion round.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** A personal interview is conducted to assess communication skills, aptitude, and motivation.

### FPM Admission

The FPM program also requires a strong academic background. The application process can be completed both online and offline. For offline applications, candidates need to visit the campus with physical copies of required documents and a research proposal.

DSB accepts scores from various entrance exams for FPM admissions, including CAT, MAT, GMAT, CMAT, and UGC-NET. UGC-NET is not compulsory; other accepted scores can also qualify you.

## Fees Structure

Specific fee details for each program are typically available on the official DSB website. It's advisable to check the most current information directly from their admissions portal, as fees can be subject to change.

## Cutoff Scores

For PGDM admissions, DSB considers scores from national-level entrance exams. For the 2024 academic year, the CAT cutoff for the PGDM program was 80 percentile for the general category. Meeting the cutoff is a crucial first step, followed by the GD and PI rounds.

## Placements

DSB has a strong track record for student placements, consistently achieving high placement rates. The institution focuses on preparing students for successful careers in diverse industries.

Here are some key highlights from the 2024 placements:

| Particulars                 | Placement Statistics (2024) |
| :-------------------------- | :-------------------------- |
| Highest Package             | INR 23.9 LPA                |
| Average Package             | INR 9 LPA                   |
| Top 20% Average Package     | INR 11.3 LPA                |
| Placement Rate              | 100%                        |

Top companies from various sectors visit the campus for recruitment. Some of the prominent recruiters include Wipro, Asian Paints, and Deloitte, offering diverse roles to graduating students.

## Campus Life

Life at the Delhi School of Business is described as vibrant and engaging. The campus encourages holistic development through a variety of activities. Students can participate in insightful seminars, cultural events, and community initiatives. There are opportunities for industry visits, club events, and entrepreneurship competitions, all designed to enhance personal and professional growth.
