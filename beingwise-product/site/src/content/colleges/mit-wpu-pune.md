MIT-WPU, or MIT World Peace University, in Pune is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, entrance exam cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Admissions and Entrance Exams

MIT-WPU follows a multi-step admission process for both its undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) courses. The university primarily conducts its own entrance examination, the MIT-WPU CET, for many of its programs. However, it also recognizes scores from various national-level entrance tests, providing multiple pathways for aspiring students.

For B.Tech admissions, for example, candidates can apply with scores from JEE Main, MHT CET, PERA CET, or NEET UG. Some specialized B.Tech programs might also require a Statement of Purpose (SOP) as part of the application. Similarly, for M.Pharm courses, a valid GPAT score is essential, followed by a Personal Interaction (PI) round to assess the candidate's subject knowledge and interest.

Beyond entrance exams, MIT-WPU also offers merit-based admissions for certain UG and PG courses. Prospective students should visit the university's official website to register and understand the specific selection criteria for their chosen program.

## Courses and Fees

MIT-WPU offers a diverse range of programs across various disciplines. Here's a look at some popular offerings:

*   **B.Tech:** A flagship program with multiple specializations, accepting scores from JEE Main, MHT CET, PERA CET, or NEET UG.
*   **MCA:** Offered in both Science and Management streams, this program is designed to provide comprehensive computer application skills.
*   **M.Pharm:** The Faculty of Pharmacy at MIT-WPU offers a two-year M.Pharm program across six specializations. Eligibility typically requires a Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy with a minimum of 60% aggregate (50% for reserved categories and persons with disability from Maharashtra state only). Admission is based on GPAT scores and a subsequent Personal Interaction.

**Application Fee:** A non-refundable application fee of INR 1,500 is generally required for each course.

**M.Pharm Tuition Fees:** The total tuition fee for the M.Pharm program at MIT-WPU's Faculty of Pharmacy typically ranges between INR 4.6 Lakhs and INR 5.1 Lakhs. This fee is subject to change and should be confirmed at the time of admission.

## Scholarships

MIT-WPU is committed to supporting meritorious students. The university offers various scholarships, often based on scores in entrance exams like MAH-CET or MIT-WPU CET.

## Placement Opportunities

MIT-WPU has a strong track record of placements, offering good opportunities to its B.Tech, MBA, BBA, BCA, and B.Des graduates. The university consistently attracts a large number of recruiters, providing students with a wide range of career options.
