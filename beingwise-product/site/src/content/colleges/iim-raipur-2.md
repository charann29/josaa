IIM Raipur is a well-regarded institution for management education in India. If you're considering a postgraduate management program, understanding its offerings, admissions process, and career prospects is crucial. This page provides a clear overview to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at IIM Raipur

IIM Raipur provides a range of management programs designed for different career stages and aspirations. The flagship offering is the Post Graduate Program in Management (PGP), equivalent to an MBA, for fresh graduates and early career professionals. For experienced individuals looking to enhance their skills without pausing their careers, the Executive Post Graduate Program in Management (ePGP) is available, structured like an Executive MBA.

## Admission Process

Entry into IIM Raipur is highly competitive and involves several stages. The primary requirement is a strong score in the Common Admission Test (CAT). After the CAT results, candidates are shortlisted based on their CAT score, academic record (10th, 12th, and graduation marks), work experience, and diversity factors (both academic and gender).

Shortlisted candidates then participate in a Written Ability Test (WAT) and a Personal Interview (PI). Your performance in these rounds is critical, as they offer a fresh opportunity to demonstrate your communication skills, subject knowledge, and suitability for a management career. The final selection is based on a composite score that considers all these elements.

## Fees Structure

The fees for IIM Raipur's programs typically cover tuition, library, and computer facilities, among other academic expenses. For the Post Graduate Program (PGP), the total fee is approximately INR 16.10 Lakhs. It's always advisable to check the official IIM Raipur website for the most current and detailed fee structure, including any additional charges or payment schedules.

## CAT Cutoffs

Meeting the CAT cutoff is the first hurdle for IIM Raipur admissions. While the exact cutoffs can vary slightly each year based on the applicant pool and paper difficulty, generally, candidates need a high percentile to be considered. For the General category, the overall CAT percentile usually needs to be above 92-94%. Sectional cutoffs are also important, meaning you need to perform well across all parts of the CAT exam. Reserved categories typically have lower cutoff requirements.

## Placements at IIM Raipur

IIM Raipur has a strong track record of placements, attracting a diverse range of recruiters across various sectors. The institute consistently achieves good placement statistics for its graduating batches.
