IBS Kolkata, a well-regarded institution in Kolkata, offers a strong foundation for a career in management. This page provides a clear overview of its Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM) course, fee structure, admission process, previous year cutoffs, and placement outcomes, helping you make an informed decision.

## IBS Kolkata: An Overview

Established in 1995, IBS Kolkata operates as a private, independent professional institution. It is recognized by the UGC and affiliated with the ICFAI Foundation for Higher Education, Hyderabad. The college aims to provide quality management education and has consistently maintained a high placement record.

### Programs Offered & Fees

IBS Kolkata primarily focuses on its Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM). This program is designed to equip students with essential business skills and knowledge.

| Program | Duration | Total Tuition Fee |
| :------ | :------- | :---------------- |
| PGPM    | 2 Years  | INR 9.11 Lakh     |

*Note: The fee mentioned is for tuition only and does not include other potential expenses like accommodation or living costs.*

### Admission Process

Admission to the PGPM program at IBS Kolkata is based on a comprehensive evaluation process. Candidates typically need to meet specific eligibility criteria and go through a selection procedure.

1.  **Eligibility:** Applicants generally need a bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum percentage (often 50%) and should have completed at least 15 years of education.
2.  **Entrance Exams:** Candidates are usually required to have valid scores from national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, GMAT, NMAT, or IBSAT (the institution's own aptitude test).
3.  **Application:** Interested candidates must fill out the application form available on the official IBS Kolkata website.
4.  **Selection Briefing:** Shortlisted candidates are invited for a selection briefing, which includes a Group Discussion (GD) and a Personal Interview (PI).
5.  **Final Selection:** The final admission offer is based on a combination of academic record, entrance exam scores, performance in GD and PI, and work experience (if any).

### Cutoff Information

While specific cutoff scores for entrance exams can vary each year based on the applicant pool and seat availability, IBS Kolkata generally considers competitive scores from major management entrance tests. Candidates with strong academic backgrounds and good scores in CAT, XAT, NMAT, or IBSAT have a better chance of admission.

IBS Kolkata provides a conducive learning environment with modern facilities designed to support student development.
