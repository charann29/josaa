Anurag University, located in Hyderabad, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, entrance exam cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make informed decisions.

## Courses Offered at Anurag University

Anurag University provides over 12 full-time courses across various specializations. These programs span engineering, pharmacy, business administration, and more, catering to diverse academic interests.

### Popular Programs and Their Duration

*   **Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech):** A four-year undergraduate engineering program.
*   **Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharma):** A four-year undergraduate program in pharmaceutical sciences.
*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** A three-year undergraduate management program.
*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** A two-year postgraduate management program.
*   **Master of Technology (M.Tech):** A two-year postgraduate engineering program.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admissions at Anurag University are primarily based on entrance examination scores, combined with academic merit, depending on the chosen course. Candidates must meet specific eligibility criteria for each program.

### General Eligibility Requirements

*   **For Undergraduate (UG) courses:** Candidates must have completed Class 12 with a minimum aggregate percentage (e.g., 50% for B.Pharma).
*   **For Postgraduate (PG) courses:** Candidates must hold a relevant Bachelor's degree.
*   **For Ph.D. courses:** Candidates typically require a Master's degree in a related field.

### Selection Criteria by Course

| Course    | Selection Criteria                               |
| :-------- | :----------------------------------------------- |
| B.Tech    | TS EAMCET                                        |
| B.Pharma  | TS EAMCET                                        |
| BBA       | Merit-Based (based on qualifying examination)    |
| MBA       | TSICET                                           |
| M.Tech    | TS PGECET                                        |

### Accepted Entrance Exams

Anurag University accepts scores from various state and national-level entrance examinations for its diverse programs. These include:

*   TS EAMCET (for B.Tech, B.Pharma)
*   TSICET (for MBA)
*   TS PGECET (for M.Tech)
*   JEE Main (for B.Tech)
*   GATE (for M.Tech)
*   NET
*   AnuragCET (university's own entrance exam, if applicable)

Prospective students should ensure they appear for the correct entrance exam relevant to their desired course.

## Cutoff Information

Specific cutoff ranks for admission to various programs at Anurag University are determined annually based on the performance in relevant entrance exams like TS EAMCET, TSICET, and TS PGECET. These cutoffs can vary each year depending on factors such as the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. Candidates are advised to refer to the official university admission notifications for the latest cutoff details.
