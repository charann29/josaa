## Admission Process

Admission to Sardar Patel College is based on a structured process that often includes entrance examinations, especially for postgraduate programs like MBA and MCA. Here's a general overview:

### MBA and MCA Admissions

Admission to the MBA and MCA programs at Sardar Patel College primarily relies on scores from the Telangana State Integrated Common Entrance Test (TSICET). This exam is conducted annually by Kakatiya University, Warangal, on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TGCHE).

**Application Steps for TSICET:**

1.  **Visit the Official TSICET Website:** Access the official portal for TSICET.
2.  **Registration:** Provide personal details such as your name, exam hall ticket number, date of birth, mobile number, email ID, and category.
3.  **Application Fee Payment:** Pay the required TSICET application fee. Payment options typically include Debit/Credit card, Net banking, or through TS Online/AP Online/meeSeva (e-Seva) centers.
4.  **Form Completion:** Fill out the detailed application form.
5.  **Document Upload:** Upload scanned copies of necessary documents.
6.  **Review and Submit:** Carefully review all entered information before final submission.

It's important to complete the entire TSICET process to be considered for admission.

## Important Dates for MBA Admission (TSICET)

Keeping track of key dates is crucial for a smooth application process. Here are some typical timelines for TSICET based on recent academic years:

*   **TSICET Registration Window:** Usually opens in March.
*   **TSICET Exam Dates:** Typically held in early June (e.g., June 5-6).
*   **Result Announcement:** Generally released a week or so after the exam (e.g., June 14).
*   **Counselling and Spot Admissions:** Institutional spot admissions for MBA courses are often scheduled in mid-October (e.g., October 15-16).

Candidates are advised to regularly check the official TSICET website for the most current schedule and any updates.
