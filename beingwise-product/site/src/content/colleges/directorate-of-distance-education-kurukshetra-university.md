The Directorate of Distance Education (DDE) at Kurukshetra University offers a flexible way to pursue higher education from Kurukshetra. It provides a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed for students who prefer or require a distance learning format.

## Courses Offered

DDE Kurukshetra University provides a diverse selection of courses at both the undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) levels. These programs cater to various academic interests and career goals.

### Undergraduate Programs

At the UG level, students can explore options such as:

*   **Bachelor of Arts (BA)**
*   **Bachelor of Commerce (BCom)**
*   **Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)**

### Postgraduate Programs

For those looking to advance their studies, DDE offers several PG courses, including:

*   **Master of Arts (MA)**: Available in various specializations.
*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA)**
*   **Master of Laws (LLM)**
*   **Bachelor of Education (B.Ed)**: A professional course for aspiring teachers.

## Admission Process

Admission to DDE Kurukshetra University programs is primarily merit-based or through an entrance examination, depending on the specific course. There is no direct admission without meeting the stipulated criteria.

### Eligibility Criteria

For most programs, applicants need to have a Bachelor's or Master's degree with marks typically ranging from 45% to 55%. Specific programs may have additional requirements.

### Application Procedure

Students can apply for admission through both online and offline modes:

*   **Online Application**: Access the university's admissions portal, fill out the application form, and submit it with the required documents.
*   **Offline Application**: Download the Admission-cum-Examination Form (Form-A to C) from the DDE website, complete it, and submit it. The offline application fee for the general category is INR 600 (registration fee INR 800), and for reserved categories, it is INR 150 (registration fee INR 200).

### Mandatory Documents for Admission

When applying, ensure you have the following documents ready:

*   Duly filled online admission form
*   Provisional degree or degree certificate of graduation
*   All semester/year/consolidated mark sheets of graduation

## Important Admission Dates (January 2025 Session)

Prospective students should be aware of the following deadlines for the January 2025 session:

*   **Without late fee**: April 30, 2025 (Wednesday)
*   **With a late fee of INR 500**: May 30, 2025 (Friday)
*   **With a late fee of INR 1000**: June 30, 2025 (Monday)
*   **With a late fee of INR 2000**: July 7, 2025
*   **With a late fee of INR 3000**: August 29, 2025
*   **With a late fee of INR 5000**: September 30, 2025

These dates are crucial for timely application submission. It is always advisable to check the official DDE Kurukshetra University website for the most current admission calendar.

## Cutoff & Placements

As a distance education directorate, specific cutoff marks for admission are typically merit-based on previous academic qualifications rather than competitive entrance exam cutoffs like traditional on-campus programs. Detailed placement statistics specifically for DDE programs are generally not as extensively published as for regular university departments. However, a degree from Kurukshetra University, even through distance mode, holds value and can enhance career prospects in various sectors.
