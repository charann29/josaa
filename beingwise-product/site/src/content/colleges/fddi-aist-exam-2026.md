The Footwear Design & Development Institute (FDDI) offers a range of specialized programs in design, management, and retail, focusing on the footwear, leather goods, and fashion sectors. The FDDI All India Selection Test (AIST) is your gateway to these exciting courses. This guide provides a clear overview of the AIST 2026 exam, including available programs, fee structures, the admission process, expected cutoffs, and career opportunities.

## FDDI AIST 2026: Key Information

The FDDI AIST is a computer-based entrance examination conducted for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs offered across FDDI campuses. Understanding the exam structure and important dates is the first step toward securing your spot.

### Anticipated Exam Schedule

While the official schedule for FDDI AIST 2026 will be released on the institute's admission website, based on previous trends, the entrance exam is likely to take place in the **second week of May 2026**.

### Application Process

Applying for FDDI AIST 2026 involves a few straightforward steps:

1.  **Registration:** Begin by registering on the official admission portal using a valid email address and mobile number.
2.  **Form Filling:** Complete the application form with your personal, academic, and contact details.
3.  **Document Upload:** Upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature as per the specified guidelines.
4.  **Fee Payment:** Pay the application fee online.
5.  **Submission:** Review all details carefully and submit the form.

### Application Fee

The application fee for FDDI AIST 2026 varies by category:

*   **General and OBC candidates:** INR 600
*   **SC, ST, and PwD candidates:** INR 300

Payment must be made online using net banking, credit card, or debit card.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to FDDI programs through AIST 2026 is competitive. Meeting the eligibility criteria is crucial for all applicants.

### Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must satisfy specific academic qualifications for their chosen program. Generally, for undergraduate courses, a 10+2 qualification from a recognized board is required. For postgraduate courses, a relevant bachelor's degree is necessary. Detailed eligibility criteria for each program will be outlined in the official information brochure.

### Selection Procedure

1.  **AIST Exam:** Candidates must appear for the FDDI AIST, which assesses general awareness, quantitative aptitude, English comprehension, and analytical ability.
2.  **Merit List:** Based on the scores obtained in the AIST, a merit list will be prepared.
3.  **Counseling:** Shortlisted candidates will be invited for counseling, where document verification and seat allocation will take place.

## Cutoff and Results

### Understanding Cutoffs

The cutoff scores for FDDI AIST vary each year based on factors like the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. A higher score generally increases your chances of admission to your preferred course and campus. FDDI usually releases cutoffs after the results are declared.

### Result Declaration

The FDDI AIST 2026 results will be published online on the admission portal. Your scorecard will contain essential details such as your name, roll number, sectional and overall scores, and your merit rank.

## Placements and Career Opportunities

FDDI has a strong track record of placing its graduates in reputable companies within the footwear, leather, fashion, and retail industries. The institute's focus on practical skills and industry relevance prepares students for successful careers.

### Top Recruiters

Graduates find opportunities with leading brands and manufacturers. Some of the sectors and types of companies that recruit from FDDI include:

*   Footwear manufacturing companies
*   Leather goods and accessories brands
*   Fashion design houses
*   Retail chains and e-commerce platforms
*   Merchandising and buying agencies
*   Quality control and assurance firms

### Career Paths

Depending on their specialization, FDDI graduates can pursue roles such as Footwear Designer, Product Developer, Production Manager, Merchandiser, Retail Manager, Fashion Designer, Leather Goods Designer, and Brand Manager, among others.

Preparing well for the FDDI AIST 2026 can open doors to a dynamic and creative career path. Stay updated with the official FDDI website for the latest announcements and detailed information.
