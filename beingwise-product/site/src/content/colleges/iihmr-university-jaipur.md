IIHMR University, located in Jaipur, is a well-regarded institution focusing on health management and research. It offers a range of postgraduate programs designed to equip students for leadership roles in the healthcare sector. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities.

## Programs and Fees

IIHMR University primarily offers postgraduate diplomas and master's degrees tailored for the healthcare industry. While specific program fees vary, they generally reflect the specialized nature of the education provided. Prospective students should check the official university website for the most current and detailed fee breakdown for each course.

Some of the key programs include:

*   **MBA Hospital and Health Management (MBA-HHM):** This program focuses on the operational and strategic management of hospitals and other healthcare organizations.
*   **MBA Pharmaceutical Management (MBA-PM):** Designed for those looking to build careers in the pharmaceutical industry, covering areas from marketing to regulatory affairs.
*   **Master of Public Health (MPH):** An interdisciplinary program preparing students for public health roles in government, NGOs, and international organizations.
*   **MBA Development Management (MBA-DM):** This program focuses on management principles applied to the development sector.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to IIHMR University's MBA programs typically requires candidates to hold a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks. The selection process usually involves a combination of academic performance, entrance exam scores, and a personal interview. The university accepts scores from various national-level management entrance exams.

**General Admission Steps:**

1.  **Application:** Submit an online application form through the university's official portal.
2.  **Entrance Exam:** Appear for a recognized management entrance exam (e.g., CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, GMAT) or IIHMR's own research aptitude test.
3.  **Group Discussion/Personal Interview:** Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a group discussion and/or a personal interview.
4.  **Final Selection:** Selection is based on a comprehensive evaluation of academic records, entrance exam scores, and performance in the interview rounds.

## Cutoff Information

IIHMR University's cutoffs for MBA admissions can fluctuate each year, influenced by factors such as the number of applicants, their overall performance, and the difficulty level of the entrance exams. While the university generally requires a minimum of 50% in a bachelor's degree, specific cutoff scores for entrance exams are determined annually. For the most precise and up-to-date cutoff information, candidates are advised to refer to the university's official admissions section.

## Placement Opportunities

IIHMR University has a strong track record of placements, connecting its graduates with prominent organizations across the healthcare, pharmaceutical, and development sectors. The university's dedicated placement cell works closely with students to facilitate internships and final placements.

### Placement Highlights

Recent placement data indicates robust opportunities for IIHMR graduates. The highest package offered in 2024 reached an impressive INR 35.60 LPA. In 2023, the highest domestic package was INR 13.10 LPA, with an average package of INR 7.20 LPA. A total of 188 students were placed in 2023, with over 130 companies participating in the recruitment drive.
