# IES's Management College and Research Centre, Mumbai: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

IES's Management College and Research Centre (IES MCRC) in Mumbai is a well-regarded institution offering various postgraduate management programs. This guide provides a clear summary of its courses, fees, admission process, cutoffs, and placement outcomes to help you make an informed decision.

## Key Takeaways

*   IES MCRC offers PGDM programs with diverse specializations and an MMS program.
*   Admission is merit-based, considering scores from multiple national-level entrance exams.
*   The institute has consistently reported strong placement rates for its PGDM programs.
*   A bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks is a general eligibility requirement.

## Programs Offered

IES MCRC provides a range of postgraduate management programs designed to equip students for various industry roles. These include:

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** Specializations available include Pharmaceutical Management, Healthcare and Hospital Management, Business Analytics, Entrepreneurship, Marketing, and Finance.
*   **Master of Management Studies (MMS):** This program offers specializations in System Management, Marketing, Operations, Finance, and Human Resource.

For the PGDM (Pharmaceutical Management) program specifically, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree in B. Pharm, B. Sc., or MBBS with a minimum of 50% marks.

## Admission Process

Admission to IES MCRC's management programs is competitive and merit-based. The process typically involves several stages:

1.  **Online Application:** Prospective students must submit their application forms via the institute's official website.
2.  **Entrance Exam Scores:** Applicants need to achieve valid scores in recognized national-level entrance examinations. IES MCRC accepts scores from CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, ATMA, MH-CET, and GMAT.
3.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are shortlisted based on their performance in these qualifying examinations and fulfillment of eligibility criteria.
4.  **Personal Interview:** Shortlisted candidates are invited for a personal interview as part of the selection process.
5.  **Document Verification and Fee Payment:** Successful applicants receive admission offers and are required to report to the campus for document verification and payment of the prescribed fees.

All applicants must hold a graduate degree from a recognized university.

## Cutoff Scores

IES MCRC considers scores from various entrance exams for shortlisting candidates for its PGDM courses. Based on recent data:

*   **CAT:** The overall cutoff percentile for CAT has been 25.
*   **CMAT:** The cutoff for CMAT is approximately 75 for the General AI quota.
*   **MAT:** For MAT, the cutoff is around 70 for the General AI quota.
*   **XAT:** The XAT cutoff stands at roughly 50 for the General AI quota.

These cutoffs are subject to change each year based on the applicant pool and institute policies.
