The Footwear Design and Development Institute (FDDI) in Noida stands as a key institution for aspiring professionals in the footwear, leather, and fashion sectors. It offers a range of specialized programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed for a dynamic career.

## Courses Offered at FDDI Noida

FDDI Noida provides diverse programs at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels, focusing on design, production, and management within its specialized fields. These full-time courses are structured to offer comprehensive industry exposure.

### Undergraduate Programs

**Bachelor of Design (B.Des):** This four-year program is a cornerstone of FDDI Noida, with a total intake of approximately 220 students. It offers three distinct specializations:

*   **Footwear Design & Production:** Focuses on the creation and manufacturing processes of footwear.
*   **Leather, Lifestyle & Product Design:** Explores design principles for a broader range of leather and lifestyle products.
*   **Fashion Design:** Covers the fundamentals and advanced concepts of fashion apparel design.

### Postgraduate Programs

**Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Retail and Fashion Merchandise:** This two-year, full-time MBA program is designed to develop critical thinking and reasoning skills essential for the retail industry. It focuses on comprehensive retail management concepts, particularly within the fashion sector.

**Master of Design (M.Des):** This program delves deeper into advanced design principles and applications, building on undergraduate design knowledge.

## Admission Process and Key Dates

Admission to all programs at FDDI Noida is primarily through the FDDI All India Selection Test (AIST). Candidates must appear for this exam to be considered for any course.

### Application Fee for AIST

The application fee for the AIST exam varies by category:

*   **General/OBC:** INR 600
*   **SC/ST/PwD:** INR 300

*Note: These fees are based on official information but are subject to change.*

### Important Dates for M.Des Admission 2025

*   **AIST Application Window:** Open until the second week of November.
*   **AIST Examination:** Scheduled for January.

For MBA admissions, specific dates will be announced on the institute's official website. Eligibility for the MBA program requires a Bachelor’s Degree in any discipline from a recognized Indian university, in addition to AIST performance.

## Placement Opportunities

FDDI Noida has a strong track record in placements, with an overall placement rate of 86% across all FDDI campuses in 2022. This indicates good career prospects for graduates.

### MBA Placements

For MBA students, the institute reported impressive figures in 2022:

*   **Average Package:** INR 3.50 Lakhs per annum
*   **Highest Package:** INR 12.00 Lakhs per annum
