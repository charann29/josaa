Choosing the right university is a big decision, and CMR University in Bangalore offers a range of programs that might be a great fit for you. This guide provides a clear overview of the courses, fees, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities at CMR University to help you make an informed choice.

## Programs Offered at CMR University

CMR University provides diverse academic options across various fields, including engineering, law, design, and computer applications. They focus on delivering relevant and up-to-date curricula, often through industry collaborations.

### School of Engineering and Technology

The School of Engineering and Technology at CMR University offers a variety of engineering and technology programs. They emphasize practical learning and industry relevance, maintaining strong partnerships with academic institutions, foreign universities, and industry bodies like NASSCOM. This ensures that their curriculum stays current with industry demands.

### School of Legal Studies

The School of Legal Studies offers comprehensive law programs, including LLB, BA LLB, BBA LLB, and LLM. The school is committed to preparing students for successful legal careers, as reflected in their placement trends.

### School of Design

For students interested in creative fields, the School of Design provides programs with a focus on both theoretical understanding and practical skills. The faculty includes experienced professionals with PhDs, Master's, and Diploma qualifications, and the school also organizes international programs and student exchange initiatives.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure varies significantly across different courses and specializations. Prospective students should refer to the university's official website or admission brochure for the most accurate and current fee details for their chosen program.

## Placement Opportunities

CMR University has a track record of good placements across its various schools and programs. Here's a glimpse into some of the placement highlights:

### Overall Placements (2022 Data)

In 2022, CMR University reported significant placement activity:

*   **Total Offers:** 630
*   **Highest Package:** INR 30 LPA
*   **Average Package:** INR 6.5 LPA

Offers were distributed across various salary brackets:

*   INR 3 LPA – INR 4 LPA: 91 offers
*   INR 4 LPA – INR 7 LPA: 345 offers
*   INR 7 LPA – INR 10 LPA: 138 offers
*   INR 10 LPA and above: 56 offers

### School of Legal Studies Placements

The School of Legal Studies has shown a positive trend in median salaries for its graduates. The data below reflects the median packages offered in recent years:

| Course           | Median Package 2022 | Median Package 2023 | Median Package 2024 |
| :--------------- | :------------------ | :------------------ | :------------------ |
| LLB              | INR 3.24 LPA        | INR 4.70 LPA        | INR 5.20 LPA        |
| BA LLB/ BBA LLB  | INR 4.75 LPA        | INR 5.00 LPA        | INR 5.50 LPA        |
| LLM              | INR 2.16 LPA        | INR 3.00 LPA        | INR 5.00 LPA        |

### B.Tech Placements

For B.Tech programs, students have secured competitive packages. Recent placements saw a highest package of INR 18 LPA and an average package of INR 4 LPA.

## Campus Life and Facilities

CMR University provides a modern campus environment with a range of facilities to support student learning and well-being:

*   **Infrastructure:** State-of-the-art labs, a well-stocked library, auditoriums, and a cafeteria.
*   **Connectivity:** Wi-fi internet access across the campus.
*   **Sports & Fitness:** A dedicated sports complex and a gym.
*   **Health:** Medical facilities available for students.
*   **Accommodation:** Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls.

## Faculty and Anti-Ragging Policy

CMR University prides itself on its experienced and well-trained faculty members who focus on providing both theoretical and practical knowledge. They engage students with innovative teaching methods and encourage quality research.

The university maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy against ragging. Security cameras are installed across the campus, and severe action is taken against anyone found involved in ragging activities, ensuring a safe and respectful environment for all students.
