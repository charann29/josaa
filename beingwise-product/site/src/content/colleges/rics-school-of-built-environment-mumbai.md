The RICS School of Built Environment in Mumbai is a specialized institution focusing on the dynamic fields of real estate, construction, and infrastructure. It offers a range of professional programs designed to equip students with the skills needed for careers in this vital sector. This guide provides an overview of its courses, fee structure, admissions process, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered

RICS SBE Mumbai provides both undergraduate and postgraduate programs tailored for the built environment industry. These courses are designed to meet the evolving demands of the sector.

### Postgraduate Programs (MBA/PGDM)

The school offers MBA/PGDM programs with specific specializations that are highly relevant to the built environment. These include:

*   **Project Management**: Focuses on the principles and practices of managing complex projects from conception to completion within the built environment.
*   **Infrastructure**: Concentrates on the planning, development, and management of large-scale infrastructure projects.

To be eligible for these MBA/PGDM programs, candidates typically need to have completed their graduation with a specified minimum percentage. Preference is often given to graduates from engineering and architecture backgrounds.

### Undergraduate Programs (BBA)

For students looking to start their careers in the built environment after schooling, a BBA program is available with a specialization in:

*   **Infrastructure**: This program introduces students to the business aspects and management principles within the infrastructure sector.

Admission to the BBA program requires candidates to have successfully passed their 10+2 examinations from a recognized board with a minimum percentage.

## Fee Structure

The specific fee details for each program are best confirmed directly with the institution, as they can vary. However, investing in a specialized education like that offered by RICS SBE generally reflects the value and industry relevance of the curriculum.

## Admission Process

Admissions to RICS SBE Mumbai are based on a structured process that evaluates academic qualifications and potential. Prospective students should review the specific eligibility criteria for their chosen program.

**Key Eligibility Points:**

*   **For MBA/PGDM**: A bachelor's degree with a minimum required percentage. Engineers and architects may receive preference.
*   **For BBA**: Completion of 10+2 with a minimum required percentage from a recognized board.

Applicants are advised to check the official website for detailed application procedures, important dates, and any entrance examination requirements.

## Placements and Career Opportunities

RICS SBE Mumbai has a dedicated placement assistance program that helps students transition into professional roles. The institution has a track record of connecting students with reputable companies in the built environment sector.

### Placement Highlights

*   **Median Package (MBA/PGDM)**: Students pursuing MBA/PGDM courses have secured a median salary package of approximately INR 5.80 Lakhs per annum.
*   **Median Package (BBA)**: For BBA graduates, the median salary package recorded is around INR 3.89 Lakhs per annum.
*   **Overall Median Package**: The overall median placement package offered across various programs stands at approximately INR 5.16 Lakhs per annum.
