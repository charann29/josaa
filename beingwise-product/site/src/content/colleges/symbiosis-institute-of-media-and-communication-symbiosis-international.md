The Symbiosis Institute of Media and Communication (SIMC), part of Symbiosis International University in Pune, stands out as a premier institution for students aspiring to build careers in the dynamic media and communication sector. It offers a range of postgraduate programs designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills and knowledge.

## Programs Offered and Seat Intake

SIMC Pune provides specialized postgraduate programs, focusing on different facets of media and communication. The institute carefully structures its intake to ensure a focused learning environment.

*   **MBA (Communication Management):** This program has a total of 120 seats.
*   **MA (Journalism):** This specialization offers 30 seats.
*   **MA (Audio Visual):** This program also has an intake of 30 seats.

Admission to these programs is based on performance in entrance examinations and subsequent selection rounds.

### Unique Aspects of the MBA (Communication Management) Curriculum

The MBA (Communication Management) program at SIMC is meticulously crafted to meet the evolving demands of the media and communication industry. It focuses on developing the necessary knowledge, skills, and professional attitude required for entry-level roles and initial career progression within agencies or media organizations.

The curriculum is structured around seven specialization subjects, spread across three terms, contributing 18 credits out of the program's total of 100 credits. Additionally, each term includes courses in five key areas to provide a holistic understanding of industry roles and business contexts:

*   Business Perspective
*   Marketing & Branding
*   Creativity & Innovation
*   Research
*   Social Sciences & Communications
*   Digital
