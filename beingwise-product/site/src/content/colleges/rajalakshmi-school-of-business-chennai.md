Rajalakshmi School of Business (RSB) in Chennai is a well-regarded institution for management education. This guide offers a clear overview of its programs, fees, admission process, and career opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

RSB primarily offers a two-year Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This diploma is designed to be equivalent to an MBA, providing a comprehensive curriculum that blends core business knowledge with essential soft skills, mirroring the breadth of offerings found at top management institutes in India.

## Admission Process

Securing admission to RSB involves several steps, focusing on both academic achievements and aptitude. Here's how to apply:

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission, you must hold an undergraduate degree (such as B.E., B.Tech, B.Sc, BBA, B.Com, or an equivalent qualification recognized by UGC and approved by AICTE) with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks or an equivalent CGPA.

### Application Procedure

1.  **Application Form:** You can apply online by visiting the official RSB Chennai website. The application fee is INR 1500, payable via credit/debit card or net banking. Offline applications are also accepted at the campus and head office.
2.  **Entrance Exams:** All applicants must appear for one of the national-level management entrance exams, including CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, ATMA, or TANCET.
3.  **Selection Process:** Final admission is based on a composite score that considers multiple factors:
    *   Your score in the chosen entrance exam.
    *   Any relevant work experience.
    *   A compelling Statement of Purpose (SOP).
    *   Your academic performance in previous degrees.
    *   Participation in extracurricular activities and demonstrated interests.
    *   Significant achievements and accomplishments.
    *   Group Discussion (GD), Personal Interview (PI), and Written Ability Test (WAT).

### Required Documents

When applying, ensure you have the following documents ready:

*   Class 10th board examination certificate.
*   Original mark sheet for Class 12th.
*   Bachelor's degree or provisional degree certificate.
*   Community certificate (if applicable: SC/ST/OBC).
*   Transfer certificate.
*   Migration certificate.
*   Scorecard of your entrance exam.
*   Graduation certificate and mark sheets.

## Fees Structure

For specific details on the fee structure for the PGDM program, it is best to refer to the official Rajalakshmi School of Business website or contact their admissions office directly. Fees can vary, so getting the most current information is always recommended.
