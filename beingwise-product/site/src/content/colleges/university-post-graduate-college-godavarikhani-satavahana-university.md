## Courses Offered

The college provides a range of postgraduate programs designed to equip students with specialized knowledge and skills for various professional fields. These include:

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** A professional management program focusing on leadership, business strategy, and functional areas like marketing, finance, and human resources.
*   **Master of Commerce (M.Com):** This program deepens understanding in commerce, accounting, finance, and business management, preparing students for roles in academia, research, or corporate finance.
*   **Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Physics:** A specialized science program for those with a strong foundation in physics, covering advanced concepts and research methodologies.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the postgraduate programs at University Post Graduate College, Godavarikhani, requires meeting specific eligibility criteria and often involves an entrance examination.

### General Eligibility Requirements

*   **Minimum Age:** Candidates must be at least 19 years old as of the notification date (e.g., born on or before March 30, 2003, for a specific admission cycle). There is no upper age limit.
*   **Qualifying Examination:** Applicants must have completed a relevant bachelor's degree from a recognized university.

### Course-Specific Eligibility

*   **MBA/PGDM:**
    *   A bachelor's degree of at least three years' duration (e.g., BA, B.Com, B.Sc, BBA, BBM, BCA, BE, B.Tech, B.Pharmacy, or any other non-oriental language degree).
    *   A minimum of 50% marks in the qualifying examination (45% for reserved categories).
    *   Students in their final year of a bachelor's degree can apply provisionally.
*   **M.Com:**
    *   Any bachelor's degree from a recognized institution or university.
*   **M.Sc. Physics:**
    *   A B.Sc. degree with Physics and Mathematics as optional subjects.
    *   Must have studied Physics for a minimum of 24 credits.
    *   The B.Sc. degree should include Physics as an optional subject, studied in at least four papers, each carrying 100 marks, in a year-wise program.
    *   Candidates who passed their qualifying examination compartmentally and secured the required percentage are also eligible.
    *   For SC/ST candidates, simply passing the qualifying examination is sufficient.
