Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT), located in Hyderabad, is a well-regarded institution offering a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at CBIT Hyderabad

CBIT offers a range of engineering and management programs designed to equip students with relevant skills and knowledge. The primary offerings include Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) and Master of Technology (M.Tech) degrees, alongside MBA and MCA programs. The institute also provides B.E. (Lateral Entry) for diploma holders.

### B.Tech Programs

The B.Tech curriculum at CBIT covers various engineering disciplines. Admission to these programs is primarily based on performance in state-level entrance examinations.

### M.Tech Programs

For postgraduate engineering studies, CBIT offers M.Tech degrees in several specializations. Entry to these programs typically requires a valid score in national or state-level postgraduate entrance exams.

### Management Programs

CBIT also provides Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Master of Computer Applications (MCA) programs, focusing on developing management and IT expertise.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to CBIT programs is primarily merit-based, determined by scores in relevant entrance examinations. A certain percentage of marks in qualifying exams is also a prerequisite.

### B.Tech Admission

Prospective B.Tech students must appear for the TS EAMCET (Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture & Medical Common Entrance Test). A minimum of 45% marks in the intermediate (10+2) examination is generally required.

### M.Tech Admission

For M.Tech programs, candidates are typically required to have a valid score in the GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) or TS PGECET (Telangana State Post Graduate Engineering Common Entrance Test). A B.E. or B.Tech degree from a recognized college, with a minimum of 45% marks, is essential.

### MBA/MCA Admission

Admission to MBA and MCA programs usually requires a valid score in the TS ICET (Telangana State Integrated Common Entrance Test). For MBA, a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks is needed. For B.E. (Lateral Entry), a diploma with a minimum of 70% passing percentage is typically required, along with a TS ICET score of 50%.

### Application Fees for Entrance Exams

*   **TS EAMCET:** Approximately INR 300
*   **TS PGECET:** Approximately INR 600

## Fee Structure

The fee structure for programs at CBIT is competitive. For instance, the approximate first-year fee for a B.Tech program is around INR 1.4 Lakhs.

## Cutoff Information

Cutoff ranks for admission to CBIT vary each year based on the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. These cutoffs are determined by performance in the respective entrance exams.

## Placements at CBIT Hyderabad

CBIT has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with potential employers. The institute aims to provide good career opportunities for its graduates.

### MBA/PGDM Placements

For MBA/PGDM students, the median salary package offered on campus is approximately INR 4 Lakhs per annum.

## How to Reach CBIT Hyderabad

CBIT is conveniently located in Hyderabad, making it accessible via various transport options:

*   **From Rajiv Gandhi International Airport:** A drive of approximately 30 minutes will take you to the campus.
*   **From Hyderabad Junction Railway Station:** The campus is about a 45-minute drive from the railway station.
