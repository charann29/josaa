IBS Gurgaon, part of the ICFAI Business School network, is a well-known institution for management education. Located in the bustling city of Gurgaon, it focuses on providing practical, industry-relevant knowledge to help students build successful careers.

## Programs Offered

IBS Gurgaon primarily offers a Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM), which is equivalent to an MBA. This two-year full-time program is designed to equip students with essential business skills and a strong understanding of management principles.

## Fees Structure

The total program fee for the two-year PGPM course at IBS Gurgaon is INR 9.1 Lakhs. This fee covers tuition and other academic expenses. Additionally, for students opting for on-campus accommodation, the hostel fees can range up to INR 1.75 Lakhs per year.

### Application Fee

The application fee for the PGPM program is INR 1,800. This fee also covers the cost of the IBSAT exam, which is a key component of the admission process. The fee remains the same whether you apply online or offline.

## Admission Process

Admission to the PGPM program at IBS Gurgaon involves a multi-step process, typically starting with an application and followed by an entrance exam and selection rounds.

### How to Apply

Candidates have two convenient ways to apply for admission:

*   **Online Application:** Visit the official admissions page for ICFAI Business School, Gurgaon. You will need to register or log in, fill out the application form with your details, and then pay the INR 1,800 application fee online before submitting.
*   **Offline Application:** You can visit any IBS campus directly to purchase the application form or the IBS Bulletin. The application fee of INR 1,800 must be paid in cash at the time of purchase.

### Selection Criteria

Admission is primarily based on a candidate's performance in the IBSAT exam, followed by group discussions and personal interviews. Academic records and work experience (if any) are also considered.

## Cutoff

While specific cutoff scores can vary each year, candidates generally need a competitive score in the IBSAT exam to be shortlisted for the subsequent selection rounds. The institute looks for well-rounded profiles with strong academic backgrounds.

## Placements

IBS Gurgaon has a strong track record in placements, consistently achieving high placement rates. The institute emphasizes practical learning, which prepares students well for corporate roles.

### Top Recruiting Companies

IBS Gurgaon attracts a wide range of companies from various sectors. Notable recruiters include major consulting firms like the Big 4 (PwC, KPMG, Deloitte, E&Y), along with companies such as Oyo Rooms, Expedia, CBRE, Exponential, Copal Amba, Protiviti, and Thomas International.

## Campus and Facilities

IBS Gurgaon offers a modern and well-equipped campus designed to support a conducive learning environment.

*   **Infrastructure:** The campus is fully Wi-Fi enabled, with lush green spaces. Students have access to a comprehensive library housing over 30,000 books, research databases, journals, and other academic resources.
*   **Hostel Facilities:** On-campus hostel accommodation is available for both male and female students. These hostels provide essential amenities including 24/7 electricity, internet connectivity, clean living spaces, quality mess services, and dedicated reading areas. Surveillance cameras are installed for security.

## Faculty

The faculty at IBS Gurgaon comprises experienced professionals and academics, many holding PhDs and other advanced degrees. The institute also regularly hosts guest lecturers, providing students with opportunities to interact with industry leaders and specialists, enhancing their learning experience with diverse perspectives.
