Bhavan's Vivekananda College of Science, Humanities and Commerce, located in Secunderabad, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff information, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

The college provides diverse courses across science, humanities, and commerce streams. Some of the main programs include:

*   **Undergraduate (UG) Programs:**
    *   Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)
    *   Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)
    *   Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
    *   Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
    *   Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
*   **Postgraduate (PG) Programs:**
    *   Master of Business Administration (MBA/PGDM)
    *   Master of Science (M.Sc.)
    *   Master of Commerce (M.Com)

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission, applicants must meet specific eligibility requirements for their chosen program:

*   **For B.Sc., B.A., B.Com, BBA, BCA:**
    *   Candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 examination from any recognized educational board.
*   **For M.Sc.:**
    *   Applicants should hold a B.Sc. degree with Mathematics and Computer Science (or Computer Applications) as optional subjects, having studied a minimum of 30 credits in each.
*   **For M.Com:**
    *   Candidates need a B.Com degree from a recognized university or institute. A passing grade is sufficient for SC/ST candidates.
*   **For MBA/PGDM:**
    *   A Bachelor's Degree (such as B.A., B.Com, B.Sc., BBA, BBM, BCA, BE, B.Tech, B. Pharmacy, or any other three or four-year degree, excluding Oriental Languages) is required.
    *   Candidates must have secured at least 50% marks in their qualifying examination (45% for reserved categories).
    *   Students appearing for their final year examinations can also apply provisionally.
    *   Applicants must be a minimum of 19 years old as of March 30, 2003, with no upper age limit.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to most undergraduate courses is typically based on merit from qualifying examinations. For postgraduate programs like the MBA, entrance exam scores are crucial.

### MBA Cutoff (TSICET)

For the MBA program, admission is often based on the TSICET (Telangana State Integrated Common Entrance Test) scores. The college releases seat allotment lists based on these scores.

Here's a look at the TSICET closing ranks for the MBA program (General Home State Category):

| Program                        | Category          | 2023 Overall Rank | 2024 Overall Rank |
| :----------------------------- | :---------------- | :---------------- | :---------------- |
| Master of Business Administration | General-Home State | 3128              | 3108              |

Please note that the 2024 cutoff for the General All India category for TSICET Round 1 Seat Allotment has not been released. The above figures are for Home State candidates.
