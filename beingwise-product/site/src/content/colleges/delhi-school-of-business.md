The Delhi School of Business (DSB) offers a Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) and a Fellow Programme in Management (FPM), designed to equip students with essential business skills and knowledge. This guide from BeingWise provides a clear overview of DSB's programs, admission process, fees, and career prospects.

## Programs Offered at Delhi School of Business

DSB primarily focuses on postgraduate management education. Their flagship offering is the PGDM, a two-year full-time program. They also offer a Fellow Programme in Management (FPM) for those interested in research and academia.

### Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)

The PGDM is a comprehensive program designed to prepare students for leadership roles in various industries. It covers a wide range of management disciplines and offers specializations to help students tailor their studies to their career aspirations. The program emphasizes practical learning and industry exposure.

### Fellow Programme in Management (FPM)

The FPM is a doctoral-level program for individuals aspiring to careers in teaching, research, and consulting. It involves rigorous academic study and the completion of a research thesis.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to DSB's programs involves meeting specific eligibility criteria and performing well in entrance examinations and subsequent selection rounds.

### PGDM Admission

To be eligible for the PGDM program, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. The institute considers the percentage of marks or CGPA obtained in the undergraduate degree. Selection for the PGDM program typically involves a valid score from a recognized management entrance exam, followed by a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round.

### FPM Admission

For FPM admissions, candidates generally need a master's degree or an equivalent qualification with a strong academic record. Applicants must also appear for one of the accepted entrance exams. The admission process may also include an assessment of a research proposal and an interview.

## Entrance Exams and Cutoffs

DSB accepts scores from several national-level management entrance examinations for its PGDM program. For the FPM, a wider range of exams may be considered.

### PGDM Cutoffs

For the 2024 academic year, the CAT cutoff percentile for the PGDM program (Gender-Neutral category) was 80 percentile. Besides CAT, DSB also accepts scores from other exams like MAT, XAT, CMAT, and GMAT.

### FPM Entrance Exams

For FPM admissions, candidates can submit scores from exams such as CAT, MAT, GMAT, CMAT, or UGC-NET. While UGC-NET is an option, it is not a mandatory requirement.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for programs at the Delhi School of Business varies by course. For specific and up-to-date fee details, it is always best to check the official DSB website or contact their admissions office directly.

## Placements at Delhi School of Business

DSB has a strong track record of placements for its PGDM graduates, with a high placement rate and competitive salary packages. The institute focuses on preparing students for successful careers through industry-relevant curriculum and placement support.

### Placement Highlights 2024

The placement statistics for the 2024 batch reflect positive outcomes for DSB students:

| Particulars                 | Statistics (2024) |
| :-------------------------- | :---------------- |
| Highest Package             | INR 23.9 LPA      |
| Average Package             | INR 9 LPA         |
| Top 20% Average Package     | INR 11.3 LPA      |
| Placement Rate (Students Placed) | 100%              |

The institute has achieved a 100% placement rate for its PGDM batch in 2024, indicating strong industry acceptance of its graduates. The average package offered was INR 9 LPA, with the top 20% of students securing an average package of INR 11.3 LPA.

### Top Recruiters

Leading companies from various sectors participate in DSB's placement drives. Some of the prominent recruiters include Wipro, Asian Paints, and Deloitte, offering diverse roles to graduates.

## Campus Life at Delhi School of Business

DSB offers a vibrant and engaging campus environment that goes beyond academics. Students have opportunities to participate in a variety of activities, fostering holistic development. The campus life is enriched by:

*   **Seminars and Workshops:** Regular events with industry experts and thought leaders.
*   **Cultural Fiestas:** Celebrations and events that showcase diverse talents and traditions.
*   **Community Initiatives:** Opportunities for students to engage in social responsibility.
*   **Club Activities:** A range of student-led clubs focusing on different interests, from entrepreneurship to cultural pursuits.
*   **Industry Visits:** Exposure to real-world business environments through organized trips.

These activities aim to develop students' personalities, leadership skills, and teamwork abilities, complementing their academic learning.
