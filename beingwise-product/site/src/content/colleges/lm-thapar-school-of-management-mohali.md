LM Thapar School of Management (LMTSM) in Mohali is a well-regarded institution for those looking to pursue management education. It offers a range of programs designed to equip students with the skills needed for today's dynamic business world.

## Courses Offered at LMTSM

LMTSM provides both postgraduate and doctoral-level programs. The primary offerings include a Master of Business Administration (MBA) and PhD programs, available in both residential and executive formats.

## Understanding the Fee Structure

While specific fee details can vary, it's always best to check the official LMTSM website for the most current information. Generally, fees cover tuition, study materials, and access to campus facilities. Prospective students should also factor in living expenses, especially if considering the residential programs.

## Admission Process for LMTSM Programs

Admission to LM Thapar School of Management is a structured process, ensuring that deserving candidates are selected. Here's a breakdown:

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the MBA program, applicants must hold a graduate or postgraduate degree in any discipline from a recognized institution, with a minimum aggregate score of 50%.

### Application Steps

1.  **Online Registration:** Begin by registering online on the LMTSM portal and paying the application fee of INR 1200.
2.  **Aptitude Test Scores:** Candidates must submit valid scores from one of the accepted management aptitude tests. These include CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, GMAT, NMAT by GMAC, GRE, SNAP, or the Thapar Management Aptitude Test (TMAT).
3.  **Shortlisting:** Based on eligibility and test scores, candidates are shortlisted for the next stages.
4.  **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates participate in further selection rounds, which typically include a Video Statement of Purpose and a Personal Interview (conducted online).

### Selection Weightage for MBA Admission

The final selection for the MBA program considers multiple aspects of a candidate's profile, weighted as follows:

*   **Professional Readiness (45%):** This evaluates your Video Statement of Purpose and performance in the Personal Interview.
*   **Academic Readiness (40%):** Your scores in aptitude tests like GRE, MAT, XAT, CMAT, GMAT, SNAP, or TMAT are assessed here.
*   **Academic History (15%):** Your performance in Class 10, Class 12, and at the graduation level contributes to this component.

## Campus Facilities at LMTSM

LMTSM offers a comprehensive range of facilities to support student learning and well-being. These include:

*   **Academic Infrastructure:** Air-conditioned classrooms, seminar halls, video conferencing facilities, a board room, and dedicated computer, language, and research labs.
*   **Student Support:** A well-stocked library, student multipurpose rooms, discussion rooms, and interview rooms.
*   **Residential & Dining:** Hostel accommodation, a cafeteria, and a convenience store.
*   **Health & Wellness:** A gym and access to hospital/medical facilities.
*   **Connectivity & Services:** Wi-Fi enabled campus, ATM, banking counter, and shuttle service.
