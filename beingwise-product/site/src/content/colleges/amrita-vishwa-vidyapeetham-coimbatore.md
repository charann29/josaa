Admissions to Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham are generally based on entrance examinations and merit. ## Campus Life and Facilities

The Coimbatore campus of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham offers a comprehensive and vibrant campus experience. The university provides extensive facilities to support both academic and personal growth.

*   **Hostels:** Seven hostel complexes are available on campus, with separate accommodations for boys and girls. All hostels are Wi-Fi enabled and equipped with essential amenities.
*   **Academic Resources:** The campus boasts well-equipped laboratories, a high-performance computing facility (PARAM 10000 supercomputer), and stable, well-connected campus networks.
*   **Sports and Recreation:** Students have access to a modern gymnasium, well-maintained courts for tennis, badminton, and basketball, and an Olympic-size swimming pool.
*   **Anti-Ragging Policy:** The university maintains a strict anti-ragging policy with a dedicated anti-ragging cell to ensure a safe and supportive environment for all students. Any incidents are encouraged to be reported to the designated authorities.

## Faculty and Learning Experience

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham prides itself on its experienced faculty, many of whom hold Ph.D. and other advanced degrees. The university also arranges guest lectures, providing students with opportunities to interact with leading personalities and industry experts, enriching their learning experience.

For programs like B.Sc., the university follows a semester-based system. A B.Sc. degree is typically spread over three to four years, divided into six to eight semesters, depending on the specialization, ensuring a structured and progressive learning path.
