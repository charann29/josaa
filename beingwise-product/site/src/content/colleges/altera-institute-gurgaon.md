Altera Institute, located in Gurgaon, is gaining recognition for its career-focused management programs. If you're considering a postgraduate degree in marketing, understanding their offerings, admission process, and career support is key. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about Altera Institute.

## Programs Offered at Altera Institute

Altera Institute primarily focuses on a specialized postgraduate program designed to equip students for the demands of the modern marketing landscape.

### Postgraduate Program (PGP) in Applied Marketing

This flagship program is a full-time, intensive course crafted for individuals aiming for leadership roles in marketing. It covers contemporary marketing strategies, digital tools, and practical application through case studies and real-world projects.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Altera Institute also offers a two-year Master of Business Administration (MBA) program with various specializations. This comprehensive program is designed to provide a broad understanding of business management principles across different functions.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission to Altera Institute's programs, candidates must meet specific academic requirements.

### For PGP in Applied Marketing & MBA

*   **Academic Qualification:** A bachelor's degree (graduation) in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   **Minimum Marks:** Applicants should have secured at least 50% marks in their undergraduate studies.
*   **Entrance Exams:** Candidates must provide valid scores from competitive exams like CAT, NMAT, XAT, or GMAT. If you haven't taken any of these, you will need to appear for the Altera National Aptitude Test (ALNAT).

## Admission Process

Altera Institute follows a structured, three-step admission process to evaluate candidates comprehensively.

### Step 1: Application Form

*   **Online Submission:** Prospective students need to complete an online application form available on the institute's official website.
*   **Required Information:** This form requires detailed information about your academic background, professional experience (if any), and a Statement of Purpose (SOP).
*   **Application Fee:** A non-refundable application fee is required. For early applicants, the fee is INR 1,499. For later application rounds, the fee increases to INR 1,999.

### Step 2: Assessment and Competitive Exams

*   **Entrance Exam Scores:** You must submit scores from CAT, NMAT, XAT, or GMAT. These scores are crucial for the initial screening.
*   **Altera National Aptitude Test (ALNAT):** If you do not have scores from the aforementioned national-level entrance exams, taking the ALNAT is mandatory. This test assesses your aptitude for the program.

### Step 3: Interview Rounds

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview, which typically involves two distinct interactions:

*   **Case Study Round:** In this round, you will be presented with a business case and asked to discuss it with a mentor from the admissions committee. This evaluates your critical thinking, analytical abilities, and business acumen.
*   **Behavioral/Direct Interaction Round:** This is a more conversational session, often with the founder or founding members. It focuses on understanding your past experiences, interests, career ambitions, and overall suitability for the program's rigor and culture.

### Admission Decision

The Admissions Committee will notify candidates of their final decision based on a holistic review of their application and interview performance. Successful candidates will receive an offer letter, which includes details about any potential scholarships.
