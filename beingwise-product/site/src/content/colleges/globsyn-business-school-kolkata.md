*   CAT
*   CMAT

After meeting the academic and entrance exam requirements, shortlisted candidates participate in:

*   **Personal Interview (PI)**

The final selection is based on a comprehensive assessment, including your academic record, entrance exam scores, and performance in the GD and PI rounds.
