IIM Rohtak, established in 2010, is one of the younger Indian Institutes of Management, known for its focus on management education and research. Located in Rohtak, Haryana, it aims to develop leaders and entrepreneurs through a range of well-structured programs.

## Programs Offered at IIM Rohtak

IIM Rohtak provides diverse management programs tailored for various career stages and interests. The flagship offering is the Post Graduate Programme in Management (PGP), equivalent to an MBA. Additionally, there are specialized courses and executive programs.

*   **Post Graduate Programme in Management (PGP):** This is a full-time, two-year residential program designed for graduates seeking a comprehensive understanding of business management.
*   **Integrated Programme in Management (IPM):** A five-year integrated program for students after Class 12, combining undergraduate studies with a Master's in Management.
*   **Executive Post Graduate Programme in Sports Management (EPGDSM):** A two-year specialized program for professionals keen on a career in the rapidly expanding global sports industry. Completing this program can even open doors to an MSc at Ulster University in the UK. The institute offers scholarships to students who have participated in national or international level sports competitions.
*   **Doctoral Programme in Management (DPM):** A Ph.D.-level program for those aspiring to careers in academia or research.

## Fee Structure

Understanding the financial commitment is crucial for prospective students. The fees for the flagship PGP program at IIM Rohtak are approximately ₹17.90 Lakhs. It's important to note that these figures can vary and are subject to change. Prospective students should always verify the latest fee structure on the official IIM Rohtak website.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to IIM Rohtak's PGP program is highly competitive and primarily based on the Common Admission Test (CAT) scores, followed by a Personal Interview (PI) and Written Ability Test (WAT) round. For the IPM program, admission is through the IPM Aptitude Test (IPMAT) conducted by IIM Rohtak.

### CAT Cutoff for PGP (Indicative)

While the overall CAT percentile is a significant factor, IIMs also consider sectional cutoffs. For the General category, a high CAT percentile, often above 95, is typically required for shortlisting. Reserved categories (SC, ST, OBC, EWS, PwD) have lower cutoffs, reflecting the government's reservation policies.

### Factors Beyond CAT Score

IIMs employ a holistic admission process. Beyond CAT scores, several other elements are considered:

*   **Academic Profile:** Performance in Class 10, Class 12, and graduation plays a role. While strong academic records are beneficial, a slightly lower academic score can sometimes be offset by an exceptional CAT performance.
*   **Work Experience:** Relevant work experience is often valued, especially for the PGP program.
*   **Diversity:** IIMs encourage academic and gender diversity. Candidates from non-engineering backgrounds or women candidates may receive additional weightage during the shortlisting process.
*   **Performance in Selection Rounds:** The Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) are critical. These rounds assess communication skills, general awareness, subject knowledge, and leadership potential. Strong performance here can significantly improve admission chances, irrespective of past academic averages.

## Placements at IIM Rohtak

IIM Rohtak consistently achieves excellent placement records for its PGP program. The institute has a strong track record of 100% placements for its PGP batches, including the recent 2025, 2024, and 2023 placements.

### Placement Highlights (2025)

The placements for the 2025 batch saw participation from numerous companies across diverse sectors. The highest salary package offered was ₹48.68 LPA, while the average package stood at ₹19.27 LPA.

### Sector-wise Placement Distribution (2025)

The marketing and strategy sector emerged as the leading recruiter, attracting a significant portion of the batch. The distribution across various sectors was as follows:

## Campus Life and Facilities

IIM Rohtak offers a vibrant campus environment with modern facilities to support academic and personal growth.

*   **Hostel Accommodation:** The institute provides separate hostel facilities for male and female students pursuing residential programs like the PGP. There are 12 hostel buildings in total, equipped with essential amenities such as beds, study tables, chairs, fans, and wardrobes.
*   **Knowledge Resource Centre (KRC):** The KRC functions as the central library and is open 24/7. It houses extensive learning resources in management and related fields, catering to the needs of students and faculty. Students can also access digital resources remotely through the RemoteXs service.
*   **Faculty:** IIM Rohtak boasts a dedicated faculty team of over 35 members across various management disciplines, including Economics and Public Policy, Finance and Accounting, Operations, Organizational Behaviour and HRM, Management Information Systems, and Marketing Strategy. The institute also benefits from the expertise of 21 international visiting faculty members and 9 adjunct faculty.

IIM Rohtak strives to create a conducive learning environment that prepares its students for successful careers in the dynamic business world.
