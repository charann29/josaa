Heritage Business School (HBS) in Kolkata is a well-regarded institution for management education. Established in 2003, it's part of the broader Heritage Group of Institutions, offering a focused approach to business learning.

## Courses Offered at Heritage Business School

HBS concentrates on a single, comprehensive postgraduate program: the Master of Business Administration (MBA). This is a full-time, two-year course designed to equip students with essential business acumen and leadership skills.

### MBA Specialisations

The MBA program at Heritage Business School allows students to specialise in key areas, enabling them to tailor their education to their career aspirations. The available specialisations include:

*   **Marketing Management:** For those interested in branding, sales, and market strategy.
*   **Finance Management:** Focusing on financial markets, investments, and corporate finance.
*   **Human Resource Management:** Dealing with talent acquisition, employee relations, and organisational development.
*   **Management Information Systems (MIS):** Combining business strategy with information technology.

## Fees Structure

The total fee for the two-year full-time MBA program at Heritage Business School is ₹6.92 lakh. This covers the entire course duration.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the MBA program at HBS, candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognised university.

### Selection Process

Admission to the MBA program is competitive and based on entrance exam scores. Applicants need a valid score from one of the following national or state-level management entrance examinations:

*   MAT (Management Aptitude Test)
*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   WBJEMAT (West Bengal Joint Entrance Management Aptitude Test)

## Cutoff

Heritage Business School considers scores from various management entrance exams as part of its admission process. While specific cutoff percentages can vary each year based on the applicant pool and exam difficulty, a strong performance in any of the accepted exams (MAT, CAT, XAT, WBJEMAT) is crucial for admission consideration.

## Placements at Heritage Business School

HBS has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with industry opportunities. While specific placement statistics like average or highest packages are not detailed in the source, the institution aims to prepare students for successful careers through its industry-aligned curriculum and faculty expertise.

## Campus Life and Facilities

Heritage Business School offers a vibrant campus environment with various facilities to support student life and learning:

*   **Hostel Facilities:** The campus provides well-equipped hostel accommodation for students, including amenities like public telephones, TV, and common rooms, ensuring a comfortable living experience.
*   **Sports and Recreation:** HBS encourages physical activity and sports. It offers excellent facilities for outdoor games such as cricket, football, volleyball, basketball, and even swimming. These activities are managed by a dedicated sports officer and physical instructor.
*   **Faculty:** The faculty members at Heritage Business School are highly qualified, with most holding PhDs in their respective fields. They also bring rich professional and industry experience from both private and public sectors, offering students a blend of academic knowledge and practical insights.

## Recognition and Affiliations

*   **AICTE Approval:** The MBA program at Heritage Business School is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), ensuring adherence to national standards for technical education.
*   **University Affiliation:** HBS is affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), formerly known as West Bengal University of Technology. This affiliation ensures that the degrees awarded are recognised and valid.
*   **Awards and Rankings:** Heritage Business School has received notable recognition, including being selected by ET Edge (an Economic Times initiative) as one of the 'Best Education Brands' in 2020. It was also honoured as the 'Most Preferred B-School' in 2020 at the 13th Assocham event.

Heritage Business School strives to provide a holistic educational experience, combining rigorous academics with practical exposure and a supportive campus environment, preparing students for the challenges of the business world.
