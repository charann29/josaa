Seeking to advance your career in business? Asian Business School (ABS) in Noida offers a range of management programs designed to equip you with the skills needed for today's dynamic corporate world. This guide provides a clear overview of their courses, fees, admission process, selection criteria, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered at Asian Business School Noida

ABS Noida primarily focuses on postgraduate management education. Their flagship offering is the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM), which is equivalent to an MBA. This program is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of various business functions and industry practices.

## Admission Process and Eligibility for ABS Noida

Admission to Asian Business School is based on a rigorous, entrance-exam-driven selection process. There is no provision for direct admission; all applicants must meet the specified eligibility and selection criteria.

### Eligibility Criteria for PGDM

To be considered for the PGDM program, candidates must:

*   Hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
*   Have secured a minimum of 50% marks in their undergraduate studies.
*   Possess a valid score from one of the national-level management entrance examinations.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

ABS Noida accepts scores from the following entrance tests:

*   MAT
*   XAT
*   CAT
*   CMAT
*   ATMA
*   GMAT
*   ABSAT (Asian Business School Admission Test)

### Selection Process

The selection process for the PGDM program at ABS Noida involves several stages, with different weightages assigned to each component:

*   **Entrance Examination Score:** Approximately 35% weightage.
*   **Group Discussion (GD):** A crucial stage to assess communication and teamwork skills.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** An opportunity for candidates to demonstrate their aptitude and motivation.
*   **Academic Performance:** Evaluation of past academic records.
*   **Extracurricular Activities:** Recognition of achievements beyond academics.
*   **Academic & Gender Diversity:** Consideration for a diverse student body.

### How to Apply

The application process is conducted online through the official Asian Business School website. Here are the steps:

1.  **Create an Account:** Register on the ABS Noida admission portal.
2.  **Access Application Form:** Log in to find the detailed application form.
3.  **Fill Details:** Carefully enter all personal and educational information.
4.  **Submit:** Complete and submit the application form.

## Fees Structure

The selection process considers the entrance exam score as one significant factor among others like GD, PI, and academic background.
