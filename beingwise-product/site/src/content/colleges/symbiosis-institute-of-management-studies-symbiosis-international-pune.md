The Symbiosis Institute of Management Studies (SIMS) in Pune, part of Symbiosis International University, is a well-regarded institution for management education. It offers various postgraduate programs designed to equip students for successful careers in the business world.

## Programs Offered and Fees

SIMS Pune primarily focuses on its Master of Business Administration (MBA) program. The curriculum is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of management principles and practices, preparing students for diverse roles across industries. The fee for the MBA program at SIMS Pune is approximately INR 17.8 lakhs.

## Admission Process

Admission to the MBA program at SIMS Pune is a multi-stage process, beginning with a mandatory entrance examination. Candidates must appear for the Symbiosis National Aptitude (SNAP) Test. This test is specifically designed for admission to MBA programs offered by Symbiosis International University's constituent institutes, including SIMS.

Following a successful SNAP score, shortlisted candidates proceed to the Group Exercise (GE), Personal Interaction (PI), and Writing Ability Test (WAT) rounds. Performance in all these stages, combined with academic records, determines final admission.

## Placement Highlights

SIMS Pune has a consistent track record of good placements for its MBA graduates. The institute attracts a range of companies across various sectors for recruitment.

For the 2023 batch, the average package for the top 50 students stood at INR 15 LPA, while the top 100 students received an average of INR 14 LPA. Over 82% of students secured packages exceeding INR 10 LPA. Prominent recruiters who have hired from SIMS Pune include Accenture, Amazon, Cognizant, Dell, HCL, ICICI Bank, and Infosys.

## Faculty and Infrastructure

SIMS Pune prides itself on its experienced and dedicated faculty members. Many faculty hold PhDs and have extensive academic and industry backgrounds. They are committed to providing both theoretical knowledge and practical insights, encouraging students to engage in current industry trends and research.

Located in the Khadki area of Pune, the SIMS campus offers a Wi-Fi enabled environment and is equipped with modern facilities to support student learning and development.
