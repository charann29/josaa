Pendekanti Institute of Management (PIM) in Hyderabad offers a focused MBA program designed to equip students for the corporate world. If you're considering a postgraduate management degree, understanding PIM's offerings, faculty, and career prospects is key.

## Pendekanti Institute of Management: An Overview

PIM is situated in Hyderabad and is recognized for its Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) programs. These programs are structured to provide a strong foundation in business and management studies, with a particular emphasis on practical application.

### Approved Programs and Intake

The management courses at Pendekanti Institute are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), ensuring they meet national standards for technical education. The total student intake capacity for their management programs, including the MBA/PGDM, is 120 seats.

## Courses and Specializations

PIM primarily offers postgraduate programs in business and management. The flagship offering is the MBA/PGDM, which is designed to be comprehensive and career-oriented.

### MBA/PGDM Program

This program covers various aspects of business administration. While the curriculum provides a broad understanding of management principles, students can also specialize in areas that align with their career ambitions.

**Specialization Offered:**

*   **Sales & Marketing:** This specialization focuses on developing skills essential for roles in sales, marketing strategy, brand management, and consumer relations.

## Fee Structure

The approximate total fee for the MBA/PGDM program at Pendekanti Institute of Management is around INR 2,00,000. It's always advisable to confirm the exact fee details, including any additional charges, directly with the institute's admissions office.

## Admission Process

Admission to the MBA/PGDM program at PIM typically involves a common entrance test followed by counseling. Candidates generally need to meet the eligibility criteria set by the university and the institute.

### Eligibility

Applicants usually need a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university. Specific percentage requirements may apply, along with qualifying in a state-level or national-level management entrance examination.

## Faculty Expertise

Students have consistently rated the faculty at Pendekanti Institute of Management highly. Based on verified student feedback, the institute's faculty members have received an average rating of 4.18 out of 5. This indicates that the teaching staff are considered knowledgeable, experienced, and effective in their roles, contributing positively to the learning environment.

## Placement Opportunities

Placements are a crucial aspect of any management program. Pendekanti Institute of Management has a decent track record regarding student placements. Based on feedback from a group of 11 students, the placement facility has an overall review rating of 3.55 out of 5.

While specific placement statistics such as average salary packages are not detailed, this rating suggests that the institute actively supports students in securing employment opportunities after graduation. The focus on Sales & Marketing as a specialization aligns with common industry demands, potentially opening doors in relevant sectors.
