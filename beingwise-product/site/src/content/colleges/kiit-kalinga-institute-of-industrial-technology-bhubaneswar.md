The Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) in Bhubaneswar is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of programs across various disciplines. If you're considering KIIT for your higher education, understanding its courses, fees, admission process, and placement opportunities is key. This guide will walk you through the essential details to help you make an informed decision.

## Courses Offered at KIIT

KIIT provides a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. Here's a look at some of the popular choices:

*   **Undergraduate Programs:**
    *   **B.Tech:** A flagship program with specialisations in various engineering fields. KIIT also offers lateral entry into the second year of B.Tech for eligible diploma holders.
    *   **B.Arch:** A five-year full-time program offered through the School of Architecture and Planning.
    *   **B.Com:** A foundational commerce degree.
    *   **UG Diploma:** Specialized diplomas, including one for Theater/Drama/Acting enthusiasts.

*   **Postgraduate Programs:**
    *   **M.Tech/M.E.:** Advanced engineering degrees, with preference for GATE-qualified candidates.
    *   **MBA:** A comprehensive business administration program.
    *   **M.Arch:** A master's in architecture.
    *   **M.Sc.:** Science programs in various disciplines.
    *   **MCA:** Master of Computer Applications.

*   **Doctoral Programs:**
    *   **Ph.D.:** Research opportunities across multiple faculties.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to most programs at KIIT is based on entrance examination scores, often combined with academic performance. The university conducts its own entrance exam, KIITEE (KIIT Entrance Examination), for many courses. Additionally, national-level scores are accepted for certain programs.

### General Eligibility Criteria

*   **For B.Tech:** Typically requires passing 10+2 with a minimum percentage in relevant subjects. For lateral entry, a three-year engineering diploma with at least 60% marks is needed.
*   **For B.Arch:** Requires 50% in 10+2 and a valid score in NATA and/or KIITEE.
*   **For B.Com:** Candidates born on or after July 1, 1998, who have passed 10+2 or an equivalent qualification like an IB Diploma.
*   **For MBA:** A bachelor's degree in any discipline with at least 50% marks. Scores from CAT, MAT, CMAT, or KIITEE Management are considered.
*   **For M.Tech/M.E.:** A bachelor's degree in the relevant engineering field with specified marks. M.Sc. (Electronics) first-class graduates are also eligible for certain M.Tech programs.
*   **For M.Sc.:** A B.Sc./B.E./B.Tech. or equivalent degree with specified marks. Students in their final year of graduation can also apply.
*   **For Ph.D.:** Requires an M.Tech/ME/MCA/MBA or equivalent degree with a minimum of 60% marks, or an M.Sc./MA/M.Com/LLM or equivalent with at least 55% marks.

### Required Documents for Admission

If selected, you'll need to present the following documents for verification:

*   KIITEE Admit Card and Rank Card
*   Class 10 Pass Certificate
*   Class 12 Marksheet and Pass Certificate
*   Graduation Marksheet and Pass Certificate (for postgraduate programs)
*   Diploma Pass Certificate and three-year marksheets (for lateral entry B.Tech)
*   B.Tech/B.E./MCA/M.Sc. or equivalent Degree Certificate (for M.Tech/LLM/Ph.D. candidates)
*   Relevant Reservation Category Certificate (if applicable)

## Fees Structure

KIIT's fee structure varies significantly by program. For example, the total tuition fees for the five-year B.Arch program are approximately INR 19 Lakhs. Detailed fee information for each course is available on the official KIIT website.

## Placements

KIIT has a strong record of placements, attracting numerous companies across various sectors. The university focuses on providing its students with good career opportunities.

*   **Average Placement Package:** During recent placement drives, the average package offered to KIIT graduates, including those from B.Arch, was around INR 10.5 Lakhs per annum.
*   **Highest Placement Package:** KIIT consistently strives to achieve high placement packages for its students, with specific figures varying each year based on industry trends and student performance.

KIIT aims to provide a holistic educational experience, preparing students not just academically but also for successful careers in their chosen fields. For the most current and specific details, always refer to the official KIIT website or prospectus.
