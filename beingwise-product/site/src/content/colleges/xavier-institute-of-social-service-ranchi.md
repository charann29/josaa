The Xavier Institute of Social Service (XISS) in Ranchi is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff trends, and placement details to help prospective students make informed decisions.

## Programs Offered

XISS Ranchi primarily offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) with various specializations, designed to equip students with essential management skills for diverse industries. The PGDM is available in the following key areas:

*   **Human Resource Management (HRM):** Focuses on talent acquisition, development, and organizational behavior.
*   **Rural Management (RM):** Concentrates on management principles applied to rural development and agribusiness.
*   **Marketing Management (MM):** Covers strategic marketing, consumer behavior, and sales management.
*   **Financial Management (FM):** Deals with corporate finance, investment, and financial markets.

## Fee Structure

For the 2024 academic year, the expected total fee for the two-year PGDM program at XISS Ranchi is approximately INR 8.90 Lakhs. This amount typically includes tuition fees, admission charges, and a security deposit. It's important for applicants to verify the exact breakdown and any additional costs during the admission process.

## Admission Process

Admission to the PGDM programs at XISS Ranchi involves a multi-stage process:

1.  **Application:** Candidates must complete the online application form within the specified deadlines.
2.  **Entrance Exam:** Applicants are required to appear for a valid management entrance examination such as CAT, XAT, CMAT, or ATMA. A strong score in one of these exams is crucial.
3.  **Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates based on their entrance exam scores will be invited for a Group Discussion and Personal Interview round.
4.  **Final Selection:** The final selection is based on a composite score, considering the entrance exam performance, GD/PI results, academic record, and work experience (if any).

## Cutoff Information

XISS Ranchi considers scores from various national-level management entrance exams. While specific cutoff percentiles can vary each year based on the applicant pool and exam difficulty, candidates generally need competitive scores to be considered for the GD/PI rounds. Prospective students should aim for high percentiles in CAT, XAT, CMAT, or ATMA to maximize their chances of admission.
