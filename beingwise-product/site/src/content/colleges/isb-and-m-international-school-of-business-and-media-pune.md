The International School of Business and Media (ISB&M) in Pune is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. It's known for its industry-focused curriculum and efforts to prepare students for the corporate world.

## Programs Offered at ISB&M Pune

ISB&M Pune primarily offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program, which is recognized by the AICTE and considered equivalent to an MBA. This flagship program allows students to specialize in various sought-after fields.

### PGDM Specializations

Students pursuing the PGDM at ISB&M Pune can choose from a range of specializations designed to meet current industry demands. These include:

*   Logistics & Supply Chain Management
*   Media Management
*   Business Analytics
*   Marketing
*   Finance
*   Human Resources

The PGDM in Finance Management, for example, focuses on providing a deep understanding of financial systems, markets, and control mechanisms. Graduates develop expertise in applying financial theories, using reporting methods for decision-making, and considering global financial impacts.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the PGDM program at ISB&M Pune is based on a comprehensive evaluation of a candidate's profile. The key eligibility criteria are:

*   A bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 50% in the bachelor's degree (45% for candidates from reserved categories).
*   Students in their final year of a bachelor's degree are also welcome to apply, provided they successfully complete all degree requirements before the program commences.
