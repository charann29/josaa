The Rizvi Institute of Management Studies and Research, located in Mumbai, is a well-regarded institution for aspiring management professionals. It offers various programs designed to equip students with essential business skills and knowledge.

This guide from BeingWise provides a clear overview of the institute's courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff scores, and placement opportunities, helping you make an informed decision about your higher education journey.

## Courses and Specializations

Rizvi Institute of Management Studies and Research provides a selection of management programs, including Certificate courses, Executive MBA/PGDM, and the flagship Master of Management Studies (MMS). These programs are taught by experienced faculty members dedicated to student success.

For those pursuing management studies, the institute offers key specializations that are highly relevant in today's business world:

*   **Digital Marketing:** Focuses on strategies and tools for online promotion and brand building.
*   **Business Analytics:** Teaches how to use data to make informed business decisions.
*   **Operations Management:** Covers the efficient planning and management of production and delivery of goods and services.

All courses offered at Rizvi Institute of Management Studies and Research are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), ensuring their quality and recognition. The institute has an intake capacity of up to 120 students across its various programs.

## Fee Structure

The tuition fees at Rizvi Institute of Management Studies and Research vary depending on the specific course chosen. Generally, the fees for programs offered at the institute range from approximately INR 75,000 to INR 4,30,000.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the management programs at Rizvi Institute of Management Studies and Research, particularly the MMS course, is primarily based on performance in the MAH CET (Maharashtra Common Entrance Test).

To be considered for admission, candidates must achieve a competitive percentile in the MAH CET. The institute evaluates applications based on these scores, among other criteria.

## Cutoff Scores

The cutoff percentiles for admission to the MMS program at Rizvi Institute of Management Studies and Research are released for various categories under the All India quota. For candidates belonging to the General All India category, the cutoff percentile has historically been quite high.

In recent admissions cycles, the cutoff percentile for the General All India category closed at 99.49. This indicates a highly competitive admission process, and prospective students should aim for a very strong score in the MAH CET.

## Placements

Rizvi Institute of Management Studies and Research has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with career opportunities. The institute has received positive feedback regarding its placement support from past students.

For students pursuing the MMS program, the institute generally offers in-campus placement salaries that typically range around INR 4.5 Lakhs Per Annum (LPA). While specific placement statistics can vary each year, the institute strives to secure good career starts for its graduates.
