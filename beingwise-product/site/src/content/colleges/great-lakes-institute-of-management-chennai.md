The Great Lakes Institute of Management (GLIM) in Chennai is a well-regarded institution for those looking to pursue management education. It consistently earns high marks in national rankings and holds prestigious accreditations, making it a strong choice for aspiring business leaders.

## Programs Offered

GLIM Chennai offers a focused selection of postgraduate management programs designed to equip students with practical skills and strategic insights. The main full-time programs are:

*   **Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM)**: This is a one-year fast-track program ideal for professionals with prior work experience.
*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)**: This is a two-year program suitable for fresh graduates or those with limited work experience.

### Specializations

Both programs offer opportunities to specialize in various areas of management, allowing students to tailor their education to their career goals.

## Fees Structure

The investment in a management degree from Great Lakes Chennai includes tuition and other associated costs. While exact figures can vary by academic year, here's an approximate overview of the program fees:

*   **PGPM**: The total program fee is typically around INR 17.15 Lakhs.
*   **PGDM**: The total program fee is generally around INR 17.15 Lakhs.

*Note: These figures are indicative and subject to change. It's always best to check the official GLIM Chennai website for the most current fee information.*

## Admission Process

Admission to Great Lakes Chennai is competitive and based on a holistic evaluation of a candidate's profile, including academic performance, entrance exam scores, work experience (for PGPM), and performance in the selection rounds.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **PGPM**: Applicants typically need a bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum percentage (often 50%) and significant work experience (usually 2+ years).
*   **PGDM**: Applicants generally need a bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum percentage (often 50%). Fresh graduates are welcome.

### Entrance Exams Accepted

Candidates must appear for one of the following management entrance exams:

*   CAT
*   XAT
*   GMAT
*   CMAT (for PGDM only)

### Selection Rounds

Shortlisted candidates will typically undergo an Analytical Writing Ability Test (AWA) followed by a Personal Interview (PI).

## Cutoff Scores

GLIM Chennai does not publish official cutoff percentages or scores for entrance exams. Admissions are based on a comprehensive profile evaluation. However, candidates with strong scores in the accepted exams generally have a better chance. Aiming for high percentiles in CAT, XAT, GMAT, or CMAT is advisable.
