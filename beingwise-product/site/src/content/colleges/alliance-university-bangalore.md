Alliance University, located in Bangalore, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, typical cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at Alliance University

Alliance University provides diverse academic programs across various disciplines, catering to students with different interests and career aspirations. From engineering and business to arts and sciences, there's a broad selection to choose from.

### Bachelor of Science (B.Sc. Hons)

The university offers a four-year B.Sc. (Honours) program, structured into eight semesters. This program is available across ten specializations through the Alliance School of Liberal Arts and Humanities, the School of Films and Media Studies, and the School of Science. Eligibility typically requires completion of Class 12.

Some of the specializations include:

*   Applied Mathematics
*   Physics
*   Chemistry
*   Film & Television
*   Computer Science
*   Data Science
*   Psychology
*   Statistics

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The MBA program at Alliance University is a popular choice for those looking to advance their careers in business. While specific placement data for the MBA program is not always immediately released, the university's overall placement record is strong, attracting numerous recruiters.

## Fee Structure

Understanding the financial commitment is crucial for prospective students. The fees vary depending on the program chosen.

### B.Sc. (Honours) Fees

For the B.Sc. (Honours) program, the total tuition fee generally ranges from INR 8 Lakhs to INR 14 Lakhs. An application fee of INR 1,000 is also required when applying for the course.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to Alliance University programs involves specific criteria and a selection process. While exact cutoff details can vary by year and program, they are determined based on entrance exam scores and academic performance.

Prospective students are encouraged to check the university's official website for the most current admission guidelines, application deadlines, and any entrance examinations required for their chosen course.

## Campus Facilities and Infrastructure

Alliance University boasts a sprawling 55-acre campus equipped with modern facilities designed to enhance the student learning and living experience. The infrastructure supports academic excellence and provides a comfortable environment.

Key facilities include:

*   Well-equipped laboratories
*   Extensive library resources
*   Hostel accommodation for both male and female students

*   Amphitheater-style classrooms
*   Modern fitness center

*   Healthcare center

*   Top-notch auditorium and conference halls

## Faculty and Learning Environment

Alliance University prides itself on its experienced and highly qualified faculty members. Many hold PhDs and higher degrees, bringing a wealth of knowledge and practical insight to the classroom. The faculty is categorized into:

*   **Core Faculty:** Permanent academic staff.
*   **Oxford Alliance Faculty:** Faculty associated through collaborations.
*   **International Faculty:** Academics from global institutions.

With over 420 skilled faculty members and a strong alumni network of over 32,000, students benefit from expert guidance and a supportive academic community.

## Rankings and Recognition

Alliance University has received recognition from various ranking bodies, highlighting its commitment to quality education. In 2026, it was ranked 274th in the 'Southern Asia' category by QS World University Rankings. For Management programs, NIRF ranked the university 71st in 2025.

These rankings underscore the university's standing as a reputable institution offering quality education under the guidance of experienced professionals.
