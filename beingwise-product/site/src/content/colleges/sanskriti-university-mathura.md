Sanskriti University, located in Mathura, Uttar Pradesh, is a multidisciplinary institution offering a wide range of academic programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities, helping you make an informed decision for your higher education journey.

## Sanskriti University: Courses and Eligibility

Sanskriti University offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various fields, including management, commerce, engineering, education, and science. The general eligibility for most undergraduate courses requires candidates to have passed Class 12 from a recognized board with a minimum aggregate percentage, typically 50%.

### Popular Programs and Their Specifics

*   **B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology):** For admission to B.Tech, candidates generally need to have a valid score in the JEE Main entrance examination. Alternatively, admission can be based on marks obtained in the Sanskriti University Engineering Entrance Exam (SEEE) or the merit of their qualifying examination (Class 12).
*   **B.Tech Lateral Entry:** Students holding a three-year diploma in a relevant engineering discipline with at least 55% marks are eligible for direct admission into the second year of the B.Tech program. Specializations available for lateral entry include Electrical, Mechanical, Computer Science & Engineering, and Civil Engineering. This program spans three years.
*   **MBA (Master of Business Administration):** Prospective MBA students must have completed their Class 12 and graduation with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. The university accepts scores from national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, MAT, XAT, or NMAT. Applicants with valid scores in these exams may be exempt from the university's written test.
*   **B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce):** Eligibility for the B.Com program requires candidates to have passed Class 12 in the Commerce stream with at least 50% aggregate marks. Admission is granted based on merit or scores from the CUET UG entrance examination.

## Admission Process and Entrance Exams

Admission to Sanskriti University programs typically involves an entrance examination or consideration of national-level test scores, followed by counseling or an interview. The university accepts scores from several prominent entrance exams:

*   **CUET (Common University Entrance Test):** Accepted for various undergraduate programs, including B.Com.
*   **JEE Main:** Essential for B.Tech admissions.
*   **NEET UG:** Relevant for health science programs (if offered).
*   **XAT, NMAT, CAT, MAT:** Accepted for MBA admissions.

While the university generally follows a structured admission process, specific details regarding direct admission for certain courses might not always be explicitly mentioned on their official website. For any queries about direct admissions, it's always advisable to contact the university's admission department directly.

Cutoff marks for admission to Sanskriti University programs depend on several factors, including the number of applicants, the difficulty of the entrance exam, and the availability of seats.
