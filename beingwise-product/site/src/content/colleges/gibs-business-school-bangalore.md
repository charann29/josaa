GIBS Business School in Bangalore is a well-regarded institution offering various management and business programs. ## GIBS Business School, Bangalore: Courses and Specialisations

GIBS offers a range of postgraduate and undergraduate programs designed to equip students for the business world. Many courses, except for the PGDM, include an international study tour, adding a global perspective to your education.

**Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)**

This flagship program offers a comprehensive curriculum with numerous specialisation options, allowing students to tailor their studies to their career aspirations. Key specialisations include:

*   Finance Management
*   Marketing Management
*   Human Resource Management (HRM)
*   Business Analytics & Artificial Intelligence
*   Digital Marketing
*   Information Technology
*   International Business
*   Agri-Business Management
*   Healthcare Management
*   FinTech Management
*   Product Management
*   Operations & Supply Chain Management

**Master of Business Administration (MBA)**

The MBA program at GIBS also provides diverse specialisation tracks, focusing on contemporary business needs. These include:

*   CMA
*   Digital Marketing & E-Commerce
*   Business Analytics & Data Science
*   Project Management
*   FinTech
*   CFA / NISM
*   AI & ML
*   HR Analytics

**Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)**

For undergraduate students, the BBA program offers foundational business knowledge with practical applications. It also includes specialisation choices similar to the MBA program, such as Digital Marketing & E-Commerce and Business Analytics & Data Science.

## Fees Structure at GIBS Business School

Understanding the financial commitment is a key part of choosing a college. Here's an overview of the fees at GIBS Business School:

*   **BBA Program:** The total fee for the three-year BBA program is approximately INR 5.2 Lakhs. This comprehensive fee covers tuition, a laptop, value-added programs, uniforms, industrial visits, convocation expenses, library access, and expert sessions (SMEs). No additional fees are typically applicable for these inclusions.

_Please note that fee details are based on available information and actual amounts may vary. It's always best to confirm the latest fee structure directly with the institution._

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to GIBS Business School involves both objective and subjective assessments. The application process is entirely online.

1.  **Online Application:** Visit the GIBS Business School website and click 'Apply Now'.
2.  **Form Submission:** Log in, fill out the application form with your details, and upload all required documents.
3.  **Application Fee:** Pay the institute's application fee: INR 950 for PGDM/MBA programs and INR 750 for the BBA program.
4.  **Entrance Exams:** For PGDM and MBA courses, applicants must have valid scores from national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, ATMA, or KMAT. For BBA, specific eligibility criteria apply.
5.  **Selection Criteria:** Beyond exam scores, the final selection process includes a Statement of Purpose (SOP) and a Personal Interview (PI).

_Remember that you will need to pay separate registration fees for the entrance exams directly to the respective exam conducting bodies._

## Cutoff Information

While GIBS accepts scores from various national-level entrance exams, there isn't a single published cutoff score. Admission is a holistic process that considers your entrance exam performance alongside your academic record, SOP, and personal interview.

## Rankings and Recognition

GIBS Business School enjoys significant recognition and affiliations, underscoring its commitment to quality education:

*   **National Recognition:** Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
*   **Accreditation:** Accredited by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) and the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).
*   **Affiliation:** Affiliated with Bangalore University.
*   **Memberships:** A proud member of prestigious international and national bodies like the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB) and the Association of Indian Management Schools (AIMS).

This extensive recognition ensures that degrees and diplomas from GIBS Business School hold significant value in the job market and academic circles.
