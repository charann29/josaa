FOSTIIMA Business School, located in Delhi, offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program designed to equip students for the corporate world. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, entrance exam cutoffs, and placement records to help prospective students and their families make informed decisions.

## FOSTIIMA Business School: PGDM Program

FOSTIIMA Business School's flagship offering is its two-year, full-time Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM). This program is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of business principles and practices, blending theoretical knowledge with practical application.

### PGDM Specialisations

Students pursuing the PGDM at FOSTIIMA can choose to specialise in several key areas, allowing them to tailor their education to their career aspirations. These specialisations include:

*   Human Resources Management
*   Marketing Management
*   Financial Management
*   Artificial Intelligence, Analytics & Operation Management
*   International Business

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the PGDM program at FOSTIIMA Business School is competitive and based on merit. The institute does not offer direct admissions; candidates must demonstrate their aptitude through national-level entrance examinations.

### Eligibility Criteria

Applicants typically need a bachelor's degree from a recognised university with a minimum percentage of marks, as specified by the institute. Final-year graduation students may also apply, subject to fulfilling all degree requirements before the PGDM program commences.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

To be considered for admission, candidates must have a valid score from one of the following management entrance exams:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   NMAT (NMAT by GMAC)
*   SNAP (Symbiosis National Aptitude Test)
*   FAT (FOSTIIMA Aptitude Test)
*   CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)

### Application Procedure

Currently, admissions for FOSTIIMA Business School are open. Interested candidates can apply online through the institute's official website. The application process typically involves submitting a completed application form along with the required documents and an application fee.

## Fee Structure

The total fee for the PGDM program at FOSTIIMA Business School is approximately INR 9.95 Lakhs. This amount covers tuition and other academic charges for the entire two-year program. It is advisable to verify the latest fee details directly with the institute's admissions office.

## Faculty and Learning Experience

FOSTIIMA Business School prides itself on its experienced and highly qualified faculty. Many faculty members are alumni of prestigious IIMs and bring extensive corporate experience to the classroom. They focus on delivering both theoretical knowledge and practical, experiential learning, preparing students for real-world business challenges.
