CGC University, located in Mohali, Punjab, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, eligibility criteria, and placement opportunities, designed to help prospective students and their families make informed decisions.

## Courses Offered at CGC University

CGC University provides diverse programs across various disciplines, including engineering, management, computer applications, pharmacy, and more. Some popular courses include:

*   **Undergraduate Programs:**
    *   B.Tech (various specializations)
    *   B.Sc (multiple streams)
    *   B.Com (Hons)
    *   BBA
    *   BCA
    *   B.Pharma
    *   D.Pharma
*   **Postgraduate Programs:**
    *   M.Tech
    *   MBA
    *   MCA

## Fee Structure

The fees at CGC University vary significantly based on the program and any additional services like hostel accommodation. Here's an approximate breakdown for some popular courses and facilities:

*   **B.Com (Hons):** The total tuition fee for the B.Com (Hons) program is approximately INR 93,000. Additionally, there's a one-time payment of INR 1,000.
*   **Hostel Fees:** For students opting for on-campus accommodation, hostel charges depend on the room type (AC/Non-AC and number of occupants). These can range from approximately INR 1.15 lakh to INR 1.98 lakh per annum.

It's important to remember that these figures are indicative and subject to change. For the most precise and current fee details, always refer to the official university website or admission brochure.

## Admission Process

Admissions at CGC University are typically based on a combination of academic merit and, for some programs, an entrance examination. The university accepts applications both online and offline. Here’s a general step-by-step guide:

1.  **Application Submission:** Candidates must apply for their desired program by filling out the application form, available on the university's official website or at the campus.
2.  **Selection Criteria:** Admission to most undergraduate courses is based on Class 12 academic performance, while postgraduate admissions consider graduation scores. For certain programs, candidates might need to appear for the CGCUET (CGC University Entrance Test).
3.  **Admission Offer:** Shortlisted candidates receive admission offers, often via their registered email addresses.
4.  **Fee Payment & Document Verification:** To secure a seat, selected students must pay the required admission fees and complete the document verification process within the stipulated timeframe.

### Eligibility Requirements

Specific eligibility criteria vary by program. Generally, for undergraduate courses, a strong academic record in Class 12 is essential. For postgraduate programs, a relevant undergraduate degree with a minimum percentage is usually required.

## Cutoff Information

CGC University does not typically publish a fixed cutoff list in the traditional sense for all programs. Admissions are often rolling and depend on the number of applications, available seats, and the academic merit of the applicants. For programs requiring the CGCUET, the cutoff will be based on performance in that examination.

## Placements at CGC University

CGC University has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with potential employers. The university strives to provide good placement opportunities across various sectors. Graduates from CGC University find roles in both the private and government sectors.

*   **Private Sector:** Students are placed in diverse industries, including IT, finance, manufacturing, and services, depending on their chosen field of study.
*   **Government Sector:** Graduates, particularly from programs like D.Pharma, are eligible for various government positions. These might include roles such as SBI Pharmacist, ESIC Pharmacist, MPSC Pharmacist, OPSC Drug Inspector, and Railway (RRB) Pharmacist. Some government roles may require candidates to clear competitive examinations conducted by bodies like UPSC or SSC.

The university regularly invites companies for campus recruitment drives, offering students a platform to showcase their skills and secure employment before graduation.

## Campus Facilities

CGC University offers a range of facilities to support student life and academics:

*   **Hostel Accommodation:** Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls are available with various room options (AC/Non-AC, different seating capacities).
*   **Library:** The university library is well-stocked and accessible on most days, including Sundays and holidays, providing a conducive environment for study and research.

CGC University aims to provide a comprehensive educational experience, preparing students for successful careers in their chosen fields.
