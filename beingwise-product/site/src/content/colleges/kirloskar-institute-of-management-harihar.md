Kirloskar Institute of Management (KIM) in Harihar is a well-known institution for management education. If you're considering a postgraduate management program, understanding its offerings, admission process, and career prospects is key. This page provides a clear overview to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

KIM Harihar primarily focuses on its flagship Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This full-time program is designed to equip students with essential business knowledge and practical skills for a successful career in various industries.

## Fees Structure

The financial commitment for the PGDM program at KIM Harihar includes tuition and other associated charges. The tuition fee for the PGDM program is approximately INR 7,16,500. When you include other necessary costs like administrative fees, alumni fund contributions, food and accommodation, and a refundable caution deposit, the total expenses amount to around INR 11,93,500. Please note that these figures are based on official information and can be subject to change.

## Admission Process

Admission to the PGDM program at KIM Harihar involves a structured process:

### Application Modes

You have two convenient ways to apply:

*   **Online:** Visit the institute's official website, register an account, and upload the required documents.
*   **Offline:** Submit a physical application form directly at the admissions office.

### Application Fee

A non-refundable application fee of INR 1,000 is required. Remember, you will also need to pay the separate application fees for any entrance exams you plan to take, as these scores are crucial for eligibility.

### Seat Intake

KIM Harihar offers a limited number of seats for its PGDM program, with an intake capacity of 120 students. Due to the competitive nature, it's advisable to apply early.

### Eligibility Criteria

Prospective students are typically required to hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university and achieve a valid score in one of the accepted management entrance examinations.

## Campus Life and Facilities

KIM Harihar provides a conducive learning and living environment spread across a green 6.3-acre campus. The institute emphasizes holistic development with modern amenities.

### Infrastructure

The campus boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, including:

*   Smart classrooms
*   Modern auditorium
*   Well-equipped computer labs
*   Campus-wide Wi-Fi connectivity
*   Extensive library resources
*   24/7 security with CCTV surveillance

### Sports and Recreation

To promote physical and mental well-being, KIM Harihar offers a range of sports facilities without additional charges. These include a swimming pool, football grounds, basketball and volleyball courts, and a gymnasium.

KIM Harihar prides itself on its experienced and knowledgeable faculty members.
