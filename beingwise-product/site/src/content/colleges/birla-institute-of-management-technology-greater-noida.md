Deciding on the right business school is a big step, and the Birla Institute of Management Technology (BIMTECH) in Greater Noida is a popular choice for many. This guide provides a clear overview of its programs, costs, how to get in, required scores, and career prospects to help you make an informed decision.

## About BIMTECH, Greater Noida

Established in 1988, BIMTECH is a private institution located in Greater Noida, known for its focus on management education. It holds 'academic autonomy' status from the UGC, making it one of the few institutions in the region with this distinction. The campus is centrally located and well-connected, with safety measures like CCTV surveillance in place for all students.

## Programs Offered and Fees

BIMTECH offers several Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) programs, each designed to equip students with specialized skills. Here's a look at the main offerings:

*   **PGDM (Core)**
*   **PGDM - International Business (IB)**
*   **PGDM - Retail Management (RM)**
*   **PGDM - Insurance Business Management (IBM)**

For specific program fees, it's best to check the latest information directly on the BIMTECH official website, as these can be updated regularly.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to BIMTECH's PGDM programs is competitive and based on a combination of factors, including entrance exam scores, academic record, and performance in selection rounds.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission, candidates generally need to have:

*   A bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
*   Final year graduation students (awaiting results) can also apply, provided they submit their final marksheet with 50% or more by October 31st of the admission year.

### Application Steps

Applying to BIMTECH is an online process. Here are the general steps:

1.  Visit the official BIMTECH admissions page.
2.  Navigate to the 'Admission' section.
3.  Click on 'Apply for Admission'.
4.  Register with your basic details to create an application account.
5.  Log in and meticulously fill out the application form.
6.  Pay the application fee, which is INR 2,250.
7.  Complete and submit the form.

### Required Documents

When applying, you'll need to upload several documents:

*   Class 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates
*   Graduation mark sheet
*   Valid score card from an accepted entrance exam (CAT/XAT/CMAT/MAT)
*   Work experience certificate (if applicable, for a minimum of one year as of January 31, 2024)
*   Appointment letter from current/last employer and last drawn salary slip (if applicable)
*   Recent photograph
*   Scanned signature
*   Domicile Certificate (if applicable)
*   Copy of passport or Aadhar card (as an additional identity proof)

## Entrance Exams and Cutoffs

BIMTECH accepts scores from various national-level management entrance exams for its PGDM programs.

*   **For PGDM & PGDM (IB):** CAT, XAT, CMAT scores are accepted.
*   **For PGDM (RM) & PGDM (IBM):** CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT scores are accepted.

Based on previous years' trends, the estimated cutoffs for general category (All India) applicants for PGDM admissions are typically around:

*   **CAT:** 75 percentile
*   **XAT:** 75 percentile
*   **CMAT:** 95 percentile

Meeting these cutoffs is crucial for being considered for further selection rounds.

## Placements

BIMTECH has a strong track record of placements, with a high percentage of students securing job offers. The institute conducts annual placement drives on campus.

For the PGDM Class of 2025, the institute reported an impressive 98.04% placement rate. Over 160 recruiters participated in the drive.

### Salary Packages (Batch 2025)

| Program                        | Highest Package (INR) | Average Package (INR) |
| :----------------------------- | :-------------------- | :-------------------- |
| PGDM (Core)                    | 22 LPA                | 10.90 LPA             |
| PGDM - International Business  | 16.42 LPA             | 10.61 LPA             |
| PGDM - Insurance Business Mgmt | 14.50 LPA             | 10.59 LPA             |
| PGDM - Retail Management       | 16.42 LPA             | 10.13 LPA             |

The overall highest package for the 2025 batch was INR 22 LPA, with an average package of INR 10.74 LPA and a median package of INR 10 LPA.

BIMTECH offers comprehensive residential facilities for both male and female students, providing a conducive environment for learning and living. Details regarding hostel fees and specific amenities are usually available on the institute's website or during the admission process.
