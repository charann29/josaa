Navi Mumbai's Dr. D.Y. Patil Vidyapeeth is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of programs across various disciplines. If you're considering this university, understanding its courses, fee structure, admission process, and career prospects is essential for making an informed choice.

## Programs Offered and Eligibility

D.Y. Patil Vidyapeeth provides numerous undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) courses. The eligibility criteria differ based on the program you're interested in. Generally, for UG courses, candidates must have successfully completed Class 12, while PG programs require a bachelor's degree.

Here’s a look at the general eligibility for some popular courses:

*   **B.Tech:** Class 12 with an aggregate score typically ranging from 45% to 50%.
*   **MBA:** A bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
*   **BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery):** Class 12 with at least 50% aggregate marks.
*   **BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery):** Class 12 with at least 50% aggregate marks.
*   **BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration):** Class 12 with an aggregate score between 35% to 75%, depending on the specific program.

## Admission Process and Application

Applying to D.Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Navi Mumbai, is typically an online process. Prospective students who meet the specific eligibility criteria for their chosen course can complete the application form and pay the required fee online.

For most undergraduate (UG) programs, the application fee is approximately INR 1,000.

## Hostel Facilities

For students requiring accommodation, D.Y. Patil University, Navi Mumbai, offers separate hostel facilities for male and female students. These hostels are equipped with essential amenities to ensure a comfortable stay, including water supply, recreational areas, and a mess facility. Hostel fees can vary widely, with reported ranges from INR 1.7 Lakhs to INR 12 Lakhs, depending on the type of accommodation and facilities.

D.Y. Patil University, Navi Mumbai, supports students in accessing various scholarship opportunities for both undergraduate and postgraduate studies.
