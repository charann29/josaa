IIM Ahmedabad stands as a premier institution for management education in India. If you're considering a postgraduate management degree, understanding the faculty, courses, fees, admission process, and career prospects at IIM Ahmedabad is crucial.

## IIM Ahmedabad: A Quick Overview

Located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, IIM Ahmedabad is a public, autonomous business school renowned for its academic excellence and research contributions. It consistently ranks among the top management institutes in India, having secured the #1 position in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) multiple times in recent years. The institute also holds the prestigious EQUIS accreditation from the European Foundation for Management Development (EFMD).

## Campus Life and Facilities

IIM Ahmedabad provides a comprehensive living and learning environment. The campus features 33 fully-furnished dormitories, offering single-room accommodation. For married students, dedicated married candidates' hostels are available. The dorms are well-equipped with amenities such as Wi-Fi, air conditioners, washing machines, private bathrooms, almirahs, televisions, and refrigerators.
