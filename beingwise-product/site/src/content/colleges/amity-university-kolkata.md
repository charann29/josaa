Amity University, Kolkata, is a well-known institution offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, eligibility criteria, and placement opportunities to help you make informed decisions.

## Courses and Fees at Amity University, Kolkata

Amity University, Kolkata, provides a diverse selection of programs across various disciplines. Here's a look at some popular courses and their approximate total tuition fees:

*   **Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA):** Around INR 3.63 Lakhs
*   **Integrated BCA + MCA:** Approximately INR 8 Lakhs
*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)**
*   **BBA LLB (Hons)**
*   **Master of Arts (MA)**

For most undergraduate courses, the basic eligibility requires a minimum of 50% marks in your Class 12 examinations. Postgraduate programs generally require a bachelor's degree.

## Amity University, Kolkata Admission Process

Amity University, Kolkata, typically opens its application window around November/December each year. The application process is primarily online and straightforward.

### How to Apply Online

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to Amity Kolkata's official admissions portal.
2.  **Select Program Level:** Under the 'Admission' section, choose 'UG' for undergraduate or 'PG' for postgraduate programs, then click 'Apply Now'.
3.  **Complete the Application Form:** Fill in all required personal and academic details accurately.
4.  **Upload Documents:** You'll need to upload scanned copies of your Class 12 mark sheet, a recent photograph, and your signature.
5.  **Pay Application Fee:** A non-refundable application fee of INR 1,500 is required to complete your submission.

### Direct Admission Opportunities

Amity University, Kolkata, offers a direct admission pathway for several courses, meaning you might not need to take an entrance exam or attend a personal interview. This option is typically available for candidates who have scored 80% or higher in their Class 12 examinations. For direct admission, your performance in your last qualifying examination is a key factor. Courses like BCA/BCA (Hons), BBA, BBA LLB (Hons), and MA are often considered under this merit-based direct admission scheme.

### Entrance Exams

While direct admission is an option for eligible students, many courses at Amity University, Kolkata, require you to appear for either the university's own entrance test or submit scores from national-level exams like JEE Main, CAT, or CUET, depending on the program. The selection process for these programs often includes both an entrance test and a personal interview.
