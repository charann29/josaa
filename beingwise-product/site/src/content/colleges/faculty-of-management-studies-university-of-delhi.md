The Faculty of Management Studies (FMS) at the University of Delhi is a highly respected institution for management education in India. Known for its rigorous programs and strong industry connections, FMS attracts a large number of aspiring business leaders each year. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

FMS Delhi primarily offers postgraduate management programs designed to equip students with essential business acumen and leadership skills. Their flagship offering is the full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) program.

### MBA Full-Time

This two-year, full-time program is the most sought-after at FMS. It provides comprehensive training across various management disciplines, preparing students for diverse roles in the corporate world.

### MBA Executive (MBA EXE)

Designed for working professionals, the MBA Executive program is a two-year, part-time course. Classes are typically held in the evenings, from 6:15 PM to 8:45 PM on weekdays (Monday to Friday), and from 3:00 PM to 8:45 PM on Saturdays. While physical attendance is compulsory for residents of Delhi-NCR, non-residents may have alternative modes of attendance.

### MBA Executive HCA (Healthcare Administration)

Similar to the MBA Executive, this two-year program is tailored for professionals in the healthcare sector. The class timings are identical to the MBA Executive program, focusing on the unique management challenges within healthcare.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission to the full-time MBA program at FMS, candidates must meet specific academic requirements:

*   **Bachelor's Degree:** A bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   **Minimum Marks:**
    *   **General Category:** A minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
    *   **OBC (NCL), EWS, PWD, CW Categories:** A minimum of 45% aggregate marks.
    *   **SC/ST Categories:** A passing grade in their bachelor's degree is sufficient.
*   **Final Year Students:** Candidates in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply.

## Admission Process

Admission to FMS Delhi's MBA programs is highly competitive and involves multiple stages:

1.  **CAT Exam:** Candidates must first appear for and achieve a competitive score in the Common Admission Test (CAT).
2.  **Application to FMS:** After the CAT results, applicants need to register on the official FMS Delhi website.
3.  **Shortlisting:** Based on CAT scores and other criteria, candidates are shortlisted for the subsequent rounds.
4.  **Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates participate in a Group Discussion and a Personal Interview. The Personal Interview round often includes an Extempore speech and a discussion on the Statement of Purpose (SOP).
5.  **Final Selection:** The final admission decision for the MBA full-time program is based on a composite score that considers various factors, including:
    *   CAT score
    *   Class 10th and 12th marks
    *   Performance in the Group Discussion
    *   Performance in the Extempore
    *   Performance in the Personal Interview
    *   Discussion on Statement of Purpose

## Cutoff & Placements

FMS Delhi does not publicly release official CAT cutoff percentiles. However, based on past trends, it is generally understood that a CAT percentile of 98 or above for the General category is often required to be considered for shortlisting. The high number of applicants, including many top CAT scorers, makes admission exceptionally competitive.

FMS is renowned for its excellent placement record. While specific figures can vary year to year, the institute consistently achieves high average and top salary packages, attracting leading companies from various sectors for recruitment.

## Fees

While FMS is a top-tier management institution, its fee structure is notably more affordable compared to many other leading business schools in India, being part of the University of Delhi. Prospective students should check the official FMS website for the most current and detailed fee information for each program.
