The Entrepreneurship Development Institute of India (EDII), located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, stands out as a unique institution dedicated to fostering entrepreneurial talent. If you're considering a career path that involves innovation and starting your own venture, EDII offers specialized programs designed to equip you with the necessary skills and mindset.

## Programs Offered at EDII

EDII focuses on management programs specifically tailored for entrepreneurship. They offer postgraduate and doctoral-level courses:

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) in Entrepreneurship:** This is a two-year, full-time program designed for aspiring entrepreneurs and those looking to lead entrepreneurial initiatives within existing organizations.
*   **PGDM Online:** An online version of their popular PGDM program, offering flexibility for students who cannot attend on-campus.
*   **Fellowship Programme in Management (FPM):** This is a doctoral-level program for individuals interested in in-depth research and academic careers in entrepreneurship.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to EDII's programs is competitive and primarily entrance-exam based, ensuring that deserving candidates are selected. There is no provision for direct admission.

### Eligibility Criteria

| Course Name      | Eligibility                                                                                              | Selection Criteria                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| :--------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| PGDM             | Bachelor's Degree in any discipline with at least 50% aggregate marks (10th, 12th & Graduation) from a recognized university. | Valid scores from CAT, MAT, XAT, ATMA, CMAT, GMAT, or CUET (Common University Entrance Test). Followed by a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round.                                                                |
| PGDM Online      | Bachelor's Degree in any discipline with at least 50% aggregate marks (10th, 12th & Graduation) from a recognized university. | Merit-based selection.                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| FPM              | Master's Degree or equivalent from a recognized university.                                            | Entrepreneurship Research Aptitude Test (ERAT) or Entrepreneurial Aptitude Test (EAT).                                                                                                                                                   |

### Application Fee

The application fee for EDII programs is INR 1,500. A reduced fee of INR 1,000 applies to female candidates and those from reserved categories.

### Important Dates

Admissions for the upcoming academic year are typically open on the official EDII website. Prospective students can usually find an admission inquiry form to get more detailed information about specific course admissions.

## Fee Structure

While specific fee amounts can vary slightly each year, EDII's programs are structured to provide comprehensive education. For the most accurate and up-to-date fee details, it's always best to check the official EDII website or their admission brochure.

## Placements and Career Opportunities

EDII has a strong track record of placing its graduates in various roles, with a focus on entrepreneurial ventures and management positions within companies that value innovation.

*   **Average Salary:** For PGDM students, the average on-campus placement salary is around INR 6.50 LPA. The median salary for the PGDM in Entrepreneurship in 2023 was INR 7.89 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters:** Leading companies from various sectors actively recruit from EDII. Some prominent names include Capgemini, TCS, Infosys, Nielsen, Cornerstone Technologies, BNP Paribas, Morgan Stanley, HSBC, Deloitte, and Siemens Information Systems.

Many graduates also choose to launch their own startups or join family businesses, leveraging the entrepreneurial skills gained at EDII.

## Faculty and Learning Environment

EDII is highly regarded for its experienced and dedicated faculty. The teaching staff comprises a blend of seasoned academic scholars, industry experts, and successful entrepreneurs. This diverse faculty brings a unique combination of theoretical knowledge and practical insights into the classroom, creating a dynamic learning environment that is ideal for nurturing future business leaders and innovators.
