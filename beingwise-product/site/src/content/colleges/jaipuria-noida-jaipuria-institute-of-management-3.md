Jaipuria Institute of Management in Noida is a well-regarded institution for management education. ## Programs Offered

Jaipuria Noida primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs designed to prepare students for leadership roles in various industries. The flagship program is the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM), which is equivalent to an MBA.

### Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)

The PGDM program at Jaipuria Noida is a comprehensive course covering essential business functions and contemporary management practices. It aims to develop strong analytical, decision-making, and leadership skills.

### Fellow Programme in Management (FPM)

For those interested in advanced research and academia, Jaipuria Noida offers the Fellow Programme in Management (FPM). This doctoral-level program is designed for individuals aspiring to contribute to management theory and practice through rigorous research.

## Admission Process

Admission to Jaipuria Noida's programs is competitive and based on a combination of academic merit, entrance exam scores, and personal assessment.

### PGDM Admission

To be eligible for the PGDM program, candidates must hold a bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks. The selection process involves:

*   **Entrance Exam Scores:** A valid score from one of the national-level management entrance exams is mandatory. Accepted exams include CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, and GMAT.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates will undergo a Personal Interview to assess their communication skills, general awareness, and suitability for the program.

There is no direct admission to the PGDM program; entrance exam scores and a personal interview are compulsory.

### FPM Admission

Admission to the FPM program is a structured process, typically conducted once a year. Interested candidates need to apply in a prescribed format through the institute's website, along with a fee of INR 1050. A crucial component of the application is a research proposal, usually 500-1000 words, which will be discussed during the interview.

The selection for the FPM program is based on multiple criteria with assigned weightages:

*   **Written Test:** 25%
*   **Work Experience:** 15%
*   **Academic Achievements & Prior Research Experience:** 20%
*   **Research Proposal & Interview:** 40%

The number of scholars admitted depends on the institute's available resources and discretion.
