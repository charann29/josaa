CMS Business School, part of Jain (Deemed-to-be University) in Bangalore, is a well-regarded institution for management education. If you're considering a career in business, understanding its programs, fees, admission process, expected cutoffs, and placement opportunities is key to making an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

CMS Business School provides a variety of management programs, primarily focusing on its Master of Business Administration (MBA) offerings. These include both single and dual specializations, allowing students to tailor their studies to specific career interests. There are also specialized MBA programs designed for working professionals, offering flexibility for those already in their careers.

## Admission Process

Admission to CMS Business School is based on a combination of merit and performance in entrance examinations. Here’s a general overview of how to apply:

*   **Online Application:** The first step is to visit the official website of Jain (Deemed-to-be University) and locate the application portal for undergraduate or postgraduate courses. Fill out the application form thoroughly and submit it.
*   **Entrance Exams:** Candidates must qualify through an accepted entrance exam. CMS Business School conducts its own Jain Entrance Test (JET) for certain programs. Additionally, it accepts scores from national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, MAT, CMAT, XAT, and NMAT.
*   **Eligibility:** Ensure you meet the specific eligibility criteria set by the institute for your chosen program before applying.

Admissions for the 2026 academic year are currently open, and prospective students are encouraged to visit the university's website for the most up-to-date information and to complete their applications.

## Fee Structure

The fee structure for programs at CMS Business School varies depending on the specific course and specialization. While exact figures should always be confirmed on the official website, management programs typically involve tuition fees, examination fees, and other administrative charges. It's advisable to check the detailed fee breakdown for your chosen MBA program directly from the university's admissions section.

## Cutoff Scores

CMS Business School releases cutoff scores for various entrance exams annually. For MBA admissions in 2024, the CAT cutoff was 50 percentile. This represents a trend where the CAT cutoff has shown a decrease over the past few years (it was 70 percentile in both 2023 and 2022). Students should monitor the official website for the latest cutoff information for different exams like XAT, NMAT, and others, as these can vary each year.

## Placements

CMS Business School has a strong track record of campus placements, attracting a significant number of recruiters annually. The institute facilitates on-campus placement drives for its eligible students across various programs, including MBA, BBA, and MA.

Here’s a snapshot of the placement highlights for the MBA batch in 2024:

| Particulars                 | MBA Placement Statistics (2024) |
| :-------------------------- | :------------------------------ |
| Highest Package             | INR 36.86 LPA                   |
| Overall Average Package     | INR 8.61 LPA                    |
| Average Package (Top 100 Offers) | INR 12.89 LPA                   |
| Companies Participated      | 300+                            |

Comparing 2024 with 2023, there has been a positive trend in placement statistics:

| Particulars                 | MBA Placement Statistics (2023) | MBA Placement Statistics (2024) |
| :-------------------------- | :------------------------------ | :------------------------------ |
| Highest Package             | INR 30 LPA                      | INR 36.86 LPA                   |
| Overall Average Package     | INR 8.42 LPA                    | INR 8.61 LPA                    |
| Average Package (Top 100 Offers) | INR 12.35 LPA                   | INR 12.89 LPA                   |
| Companies Participated      | 300+                            | 300+                            |

The average package for MBA students has shown consistent growth over the last three years:

*   **2022:** INR 8.27 LPA
*   **2023:** INR 8.42 LPA
*   **2024:** INR 8.61 LPA

With over 600 recruiters visiting the campus and placing students across diverse sectors, the placement scenario at CMS Business School is robust, offering good opportunities for graduates.

## Faculty and Learning Environment

The faculty at CMS Business School comprises experienced and well-trained educators, many holding PhDs, Master's degrees, and diplomas. They focus on a balanced approach, combining theoretical knowledge with practical training. The university also organizes international programs and student exchange initiatives, enriching the learning experience for students.
