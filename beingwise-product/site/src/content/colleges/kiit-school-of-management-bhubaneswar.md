The KIIT School of Management (KSOM), located in Bhubaneswar, is a well-regarded institution for higher education in business. It offers a range of programs designed to equip students with the skills needed for the corporate world. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff scores, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered and Fees

KSOM provides full-time programs at undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels. The main offerings include a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), a Master of Business Administration (MBA), and a Ph.D. in Management.

Here’s a look at the approximate fee structure for their popular programs:

| Program | Approximate Total Fees (INR) |
| :------ | :--------------------------- |
| BBA     | 8,00,000                     |
| MBA     | 16,00,000                    |

*Please note that these fees are estimates and can change. It's always best to check the official KSOM website for the most current information.*

## Admission Process

Admission to KSOM programs is competitive and typically involves an entrance examination followed by further evaluation rounds.

### For BBA Admissions

To join the BBA program, candidates must take the KIIT Entrance Examination (KIITEE). This is an online test conducted by KIIT. After the exam, eligible candidates participate in a counseling process to finalize their admission.

### For MBA Admissions

For the MBA program, KSOM considers scores from national-level entrance exams. These include CAT, MAT, XAT, and CMAT. Based on these scores, shortlisted candidates are invited for Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds. Successful candidates in these rounds receive an admission offer, which can be confirmed by paying the program fees.

## Cutoff Scores

While specific cutoffs can vary each year, generally, for MBA admissions, KSOM accepts scores from various management entrance exams. For the 2024 admissions, typical percentile cutoffs for the open category were around:

*   **CMAT:** 80 percentile
*   **MAT:** 80 percentile
*   **XAT:** 70 percentile

It is important to remember that achieving these scores makes you eligible for the next stages of admission, but does not guarantee a seat.

## Placements at KIIT School of Management

KSOM has a strong track record when it comes to student placements and internships. The institution actively facilitates both domestic and international placement opportunities. The placement rate usually falls between 80-82%.

### Placement Highlights:

*   **Highest Domestic Package:** INR 20 LPA
*   **Average Domestic Package:** INR 9 LPA
*   **Domestic Package Range:** INR 12-13 LPA
*   **International Package Range:** INR 25-26 LPA

Many prominent companies recruit from KSOM. Some of the top recruiters include ITC Hotels, Dell, HDFC Life, Yes Bank, Deloitte, TCS, Samsung, Tech Mahindra, Amazon, Berger Paint, Blue Star, Capital First Ltd, Capital IQ, Care Ratings, Dun & Bradstreet, and Dupont. KSOM also maintains partnerships with industry leaders like Ericsson, ITC Infotech, High Radius, Schneider Electric, Tech Mahindra, L&T Financial, Microsoft, and Oracle to enhance placement prospects for its students.

## Why Choose KIIT School of Management?

KSOM has been recognized for its academic excellence, including being awarded the "Best Business School of Odisha, 2023" by Times Business Awards. As a part of the Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology, it holds a reputable position among management schools in the region. The school's comprehensive curriculum, industry connections, and dedicated placement cell make it a noteworthy option for students aspiring to build a career in management.
