Management Development Institute (MDI) Gurgaon is a highly respected institution for management education in India. Located in the bustling city of Gurgaon, it offers a range of postgraduate programs designed to equip students with essential business skills and leadership qualities.

## Programs Offered and Fees

MDI Gurgaon provides several postgraduate diploma programs tailored to different career aspirations. The fees for these programs vary, covering tuition, hostel charges, and other academic expenses.

### Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) Programs

Here’s an overview of the approximate total tuition fees for key PGDM programs. Please note these figures are indicative and it's always best to confirm the latest fee structure directly with the institute's admissions department.

| Program                        | Approximate Total Tuition Fee |
| :----------------------------- | :---------------------------- |
| PGDM-Business Management (BM)  | INR 23.54 lakh                |
| PGDM-Business Analytics (BA)   | INR 8.25 lakh                 |
| PGDM-Public Policy Management (PPM) | INR 4.84 lakh                 |

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to MDI Gurgaon's flagship programs is highly competitive and involves a multi-stage selection process. The institute does not publish specific cutoff lists, but focuses on a holistic assessment of candidates.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission, applicants must meet the following general requirements:

*   A Bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks or an equivalent Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA).
*   A minimum of 50% marks in both Class 10 and Class 12 examinations.
*   A valid score from either the Common Admission Test (CAT) or the Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT).

### Selection Process

Beyond the entrance exam scores, candidates must perform well in the subsequent rounds:

*   **Group Discussion (GD):** Applicants participate in group discussions to assess their communication, leadership, and problem-solving abilities.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** A personal interview evaluates the candidate's academic background, work experience (if any), career aspirations, and overall suitability for the program.

It's important to note that MDI Gurgaon does not have any management quota seats; admissions are purely merit-based.

## Campus Life and Facilities

MDI Gurgaon offers a vibrant campus environment with excellent facilities, including well-maintained hostels for both male and female students.

### Hostel Accommodation

Separate hostel blocks are available for boys and girls. Hostels like Chaitanya, Change Masters, Takshashila, and Kshitij offer twin-sharing rooms, with Kshitij also providing four-sharing options. The girls' hostel block is known as 'Odyssey'.

Rooms are furnished with essential amenities such as a bed, study table, chair, and cupboard. Students might bring their own mattresses, linen, and toiletries, though these are also available at the student-run cooperative store, 'MART'.

Executive rooms are air-conditioned and fully furnished with modern conveniences, including internet access, to support extended study hours for participants.

### Overall Facilities

The hostel rooms are designed to be airy and spacious, providing a comfortable living and studying environment. The campus aims to foster a conducive atmosphere for academic growth and personal development.
