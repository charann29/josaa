Sri Krishna College of Engineering and Technology (SKCET) in Coimbatore is a well-regarded institution offering a range of engineering and technology programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, admission process, fee structure, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at SKCET

SKCET provides a diverse selection of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, primarily focusing on engineering and technology disciplines. Students can choose from various B.E. and B.Tech. specialisations, along with M.E. programs.

*   **Undergraduate (B.E./B.Tech.)**: Popular choices include Computer Science and Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and more. The B.Tech. in CSE and B.E. in AI & DS are both strong programs; your choice might depend on your interest in core programming versus the rapidly evolving fields of AI and data analytics.
*   **Postgraduate (M.E.)**: The college also offers master's level programs for advanced studies in specific engineering fields.

## Admission Process

Admissions to SKCET are primarily based on academic merit and counselling processes. The college has two main admission channels:

*   **General Quota (65% of seats)**: Admissions under this quota are managed through the Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) counselling process. Eligibility is determined by your aggregate marks in Class 12.
*   **Management Quota (35% of seats)**: For these seats, admissions are conducted through institutional counselling directly by the college.

Aspiring students should perform well in their Class 12 examinations and be prepared for the TNEA counselling if applying through the general quota.

## Fee Structure

While specific fee details can vary by program and year, SKCET is known for offering quality education. It is advisable to check the official SKCET website or contact the admissions office directly for the most current and detailed fee information for your chosen course.

## Cutoff Information

Admission cutoffs for SKCET are determined by the TNEA counselling process and vary each year based on factors like the number of applicants, their academic performance, and the availability of seats in each program. These cutoffs reflect the minimum marks or ranks required for admission to different engineering branches. Historically, popular branches like Computer Science and Electronics and Communication Engineering tend to have higher cutoffs.

## Campus Life and Facilities

SKCET provides a conducive learning environment with modern facilities and a focus on overall student development.

### Faculty

The college boasts a highly qualified and experienced faculty, many holding PhDs. Students often rate the faculty positively, appreciating their expertise and teaching methodologies.

### Infrastructure and Campus Safety

SKCET offers a well-maintained campus with good infrastructure. The college prioritizes student safety with round-the-clock security, including surveillance cameras in academic blocks and hostels. Wardens and staff are available 24/7 in hostels to assist students.

### Hostel Facilities

SKCET provides comfortable hostel accommodation for both boys and girls. The boys' hostel is located within the campus, featuring multiple blocks with a substantial capacity. The girls' hostel is situated about 3 km from the college, with a dedicated bus service for transport. Both hostels offer:

*   Four-person sharing rooms.
*   24x7 Wi-Fi connectivity.
*   Mess facilities with good quality food and structured meal timings.
*   Regular cleaning and maintenance services.
*   24x7 ambulance service for emergencies.

SKCET strives to offer a holistic educational experience, combining strong academics with robust infrastructure and student support services.
