The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Shillong, nestled in the picturesque hills of Meghalaya, is a premier institution for management education in India. Known for its focus on sustainability and a unique blend of traditional wisdom with modern business practices, IIM Shillong offers a vibrant academic environment.

## Programs Offered at IIM Shillong

IIM Shillong provides a range of postgraduate and doctoral programs designed to develop future business leaders. The flagship program is the Post Graduate Programme in Management (PGPM), equivalent to an MBA. Additionally, the institute offers specialized programs and executive education courses.

### Post Graduate Programme in Management (PGPM)

This two-year, full-time residential program aims to equip students with comprehensive management knowledge and skills. It covers various aspects of business administration, including finance, marketing, operations, human resources, and strategy, with a strong emphasis on practical application and critical thinking.

### Doctoral Programmes

For those interested in advanced research and academia, IIM Shillong offers doctoral programs. These programs are rigorous and focus on generating original research in various management disciplines.

## Fee Structure

The fees for the Post Graduate Programme in Management at IIM Shillong cover tuition, accommodation, and other academic expenses. For the 2025 academic year, the total fee for the PGPM program is approximately INR 17.50 Lakhs. This amount is subject to change, and students are advised to check the official IIM Shillong website for the most current information and detailed breakdowns.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to IIM Shillong's PGPM program is highly competitive and follows a multi-stage process. Candidates are evaluated based on their performance in the Common Admission Test (CAT), academic record, work experience, and performance in the Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI).

### CAT Score

The CAT score is a primary shortlisting criterion. While the exact cutoff varies each year based on the applicant pool and seat availability, candidates generally need a high percentile in CAT to be considered. For the General category, the overall CAT cutoff typically ranges in the 90+ percentile, with sectional cutoffs also playing a crucial role.

### Academic Profile and Work Experience

IIM Shillong considers a candidate's academic performance in Class 10, Class 12, and graduation. Work experience, if any, also adds significant weight to the application. Diversity, both academic and gender, is often given consideration during the shortlisting process.

### Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI)

Shortlisted candidates participate in the WAT and PI rounds. These rounds assess a candidate's communication skills, general awareness, subject knowledge, and suitability for a management career. Performance in these stages is critical for final selection.

## Placements at IIM Shillong

IIM Shillong has a strong track record of successful placements, attracting leading companies from various sectors. The institute consistently achieves impressive placement statistics, reflecting the quality of its graduates and the robust industry connections it maintains.

### Placement Highlights (2025 Batch)

| Category         | Statistics         |
| :--------------- | :----------------- |
| Highest Package  | INR 71.5 LPA       |
| Average Package  | INR 26.44 LPA      |
| Median Package   | INR 25 LPA         |

Top recruiting sectors include Consulting and Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI), alongside roles in General Management, Sales & Marketing, and Operations. Graduates secure positions in renowned national and international firms, underscoring the value of an IIM Shillong degree.

## Campus Life and Infrastructure

Spread across 120 acres, the IIM Shillong campus offers a serene and conducive environment for learning. The institute boasts modern infrastructure designed to support academic and extracurricular activities.

### Facilities

The campus includes state-of-the-art classrooms, a well-equipped computer center, and a dynamic library with access to over 17,000 e-journals and 40+ databases, including DELNET and EBSCO Business Source Ultimate. The entire campus is supported by a robust wired and wireless LAN infrastructure with enhanced security.

### Extracurricular Activities

IIM Shillong encourages a holistic development approach, offering numerous opportunities for students to engage in extracurricular activities. The institute hosts various management and cultural festivals, such as 'Khlur Thma', its annual management festival, and 'Nirvana', the cultural fest featuring music, dance, and theatre events. These activities foster interaction, teamwork, and leadership skills among students.
