Centurion University of Technology and Management (CUTM), Bhubaneswar Campus, offers a wide array of programs for students aspiring to build careers in technology and management. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structures, admission procedures, and placement opportunities.

## Courses Offered at Centurion University Bhubaneswar

CUTM Bhubaneswar provides various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. A key focus is on technology and management disciplines, catering to diverse academic interests and career goals.

### B.Tech Programs

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is a flagship offering, covering multiple engineering specializations. The program typically spans four years.

### B.Com Admissions

For students interested in commerce, the Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) program is available. Admission to this course, like many others, is entrance-based.

### MBA Programs

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a two-year postgraduate program designed for aspiring business leaders. It has a limited number of seats each academic year.

### MCA Programs

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is another two-year postgraduate course, ideal for those seeking advanced skills in computer applications.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admissions at CUTM Bhubaneswar are primarily merit-based, determined by performance in entrance examinations and prior academic records.

### Entrance Examinations

Centurion University conducts its own entrance test, the Centurion University Entrance Examination (CUEE), which is a multiple-choice question (MCQ) based exam. This exam is mandatory for admission to most programs, including B.Com, B.Tech, and MBA. For MCA, scores from OJEE or JEE Main are also considered.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **B.Tech:** Candidates generally need to have completed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, securing at least 50% aggregate marks (with a 5% relaxation for reserved categories). A valid CUEE score/rank is also required.
*   **B.Com:** Admissions are entrance-based, requiring a CUEE score.
*   **MBA:** Admission is solely based on the CUEE score. Scores from other national-level exams like CAT are not accepted for direct admission.
*   **MCA:** Eligibility typically requires a graduation degree with 50% marks, along with valid scores from OJEE, JEE Main, or CUEE.

### Application Procedure

Applying to CUTM Bhubaneswar is an online process:

1.  Visit the university's online admission portal.
2.  Complete the registration to create a student account.
3.  Log in to your new account and fill out the application form.
4.  Upload all required scanned documents.
5.  Pay the application fees online.
6.  Submit the application.

### Counselling Process

After the entrance exam results are announced, eligible candidates must participate in a counselling process conducted by the university on its campus.

## Placements

Centurion University Bhubaneswar focuses on providing good placement opportunities for its graduates. The university has a dedicated system to support students in securing jobs. For B.Tech graduates, the median salary is approximately INR 3.50 Lakh per annum, based on verified reviews.

While specific placement statistics can vary by program and year, the university consistently strives to connect students with reputable companies, helping them launch their professional careers.
