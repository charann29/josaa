The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Udaipur is a well-regarded institution offering a range of management programs. If you're considering IIM Udaipur for your higher education, understanding its courses, fees, admission process, and career prospects is crucial.

## IIM Udaipur: Programs and Eligibility

IIM Udaipur provides several programs designed for different career stages and aspirations. The primary admission criteria for most courses revolve around entrance exam scores combined with academic qualifications.

### MBA Program

This flagship program requires candidates to hold a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university with at least 50% marks. A valid score in the Common Admission Test (CAT) is also mandatory.

### Certificate Programs

For specialized certificate courses, applicants typically need to have completed Class 12 or equivalent and possess a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university with a minimum of 50% marks. Accepted entrance exams include CAT, GMAT, or GRE.

### Executive MBA/PGDM

Designed for working professionals, this program requires a Bachelor's degree in any discipline with at least 50% marks or an equivalent CGPA. Additionally, candidates must have a minimum of three years of post-qualification work experience. Admission is based on CAT scores.

### PhD Program

Prospective PhD candidates should have a Master's degree in any discipline with at least 55% marks, along with a Bachelor's degree (minimum three years duration after higher secondary) with at least 50% marks. Alternatively, a completed professional qualification like CA, ICWA, or CS with a Bachelor's degree is accepted. Applicants must also provide scores from CAT, UGC NET, or CSIR NET.

## Admission and Selection Process

Admission to IIM Udaipur is competitive and multi-faceted, primarily focusing on entrance exam performance and personal interviews.

### MBA Selection Process

For the MBA program, candidates are initially shortlisted based on their CAT scores, provided they meet the minimum sectional and overall cutoffs. Shortlisted candidates then proceed to an online Personal Interview (PI) round. The final merit list is compiled by considering a combination of the CAT score, PI score, and the candidate's overall profile.

### Executive MBA Application

For the Executive MBA, applicants must take an accepted entrance exam (CAT, GMAT, GRE, or the IIMU Test). After securing a valid score, candidates apply through the institute's official website, submitting the application form and required documents. The process includes a personal interview and profile evaluation, followed by an admission offer and fee submission.

## Placements at IIM Udaipur

IIM Udaipur has a strong track record of placements across its programs. The institute consistently strives to ensure that its students find suitable career opportunities.

For the MBA batch of 2025, a total of 302 students participated in the placement process, and all of them received offers. Similarly, in the MBA in Global Supply Chain Management (GSCM) and MBA in Digital Enterprise Management (DEM) programs, all 48 and 53 students, respectively, who sought placement through the institute in 2025 were successfully placed.
