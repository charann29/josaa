The New Delhi Institute of Management (NDIM) is a well-regarded institution located in India's capital, offering postgraduate management programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, important cutoffs, and placement records to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at NDIM

NDIM primarily focuses on its Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program, which is equivalent to an MBA. This comprehensive program is designed to equip students with essential management skills and knowledge across various specializations. All programs offered by NDIM hold approval from the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and have received accreditation from the Accreditation Service for International Schools, Colleges and Universities (ASIC) UK.

## Admission Process for PGDM

Admission to NDIM's PGDM program involves several steps, from application to final enrollment. The process is entirely online for convenience.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the PGDM program, candidates typically need a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university. A key part of the selection process is performance in national-level management entrance examinations.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

NDIM considers scores from a range of reputable entrance tests for admission to its PGDM program. These include:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   MAT (Management Aptitude Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)
*   ATMA (AIMS Test for Management Admissions)
*   GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)

### Application Procedure

Applying to NDIM is a straightforward online process. Here's how to do it:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the 'Apply Now' section on the NDIM official website.
2.  **Register:** Provide your basic details such as name, email address, and mobile number to create your registration.
3.  **Complete the Form:** Fill in all the required information accurately in the online application form.
4.  **Pay Application Fee:** You will be redirected to a secure payment gateway to pay the non-refundable application fee of INR 1,000. Payment can be made using a credit card, debit card, UPI, or net banking.
5.  **Receive Confirmation:** Upon successful payment, you will receive an application number. NDIM will also send a hard copy of their prospectus and communicate details about your Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) schedule.

### Selection Rounds

Shortlisted candidates, based on their entrance exam scores, will be invited for a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round. Beyond exam scores, NDIM also evaluates a candidate's academic history, achievements, and any relevant work experience.

### Merit List and Counseling

Candidates who perform well in the selection rounds will find their names on the merit list. These individuals must then attend counseling sessions with all necessary documents for verification. To confirm admission, shortlisted candidates are required to pay the admission fees.

## Fees Structure

While specific fee details for each specialization can vary, the application fee for the PGDM program is INR 1,000, which is non-refundable. For detailed course fees, it's best to refer to the official NDIM prospectus or website.
