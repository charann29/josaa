The Amrita School of Business (ASB) in Bengaluru, part of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, stands as a prominent institution for management education. This page offers a clear overview of its MBA program, including details on fees, the admissions process, cutoff scores, and career opportunities, to assist you in making an informed choice.

## Programs Offered

ASB Bengaluru primarily focuses on its Master of Business Administration (MBA) program. For students seeking an international experience, the institution also provides an integrated MBA + MS program in collaboration with the University at Buffalo, USA.

### MBA Course Structure

The MBA curriculum at ASB Bengaluru is designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing students for the dynamic business world. The program emphasizes a blend of academic rigor and real-world application, guided by experienced faculty.

## Admissions Process and Eligibility

Admission to the MBA program at ASB Bengaluru is competitive and based on various factors, including entrance exam scores, academic performance, and interview rounds. Applications are currently open for the upcoming MBA batch.

### Eligibility Criteria

Prospective candidates are required to meet specific eligibility criteria, typically involving a bachelor's degree from a recognized university and valid scores in management entrance exams.

### Application Procedure

Interested candidates who meet the eligibility requirements can apply online through the official Amrita School of Business admissions portal. It's advisable to check the official website for the latest application deadlines and detailed instructions.

## Faculty and Learning Environment

ASB Bengaluru prides itself on its experienced faculty, comprising 25 professors and visiting faculty members. Many hold PhD degrees and bring significant teaching and industry experience. The institution also benefits from international faculty visits, enriching the learning experience with global perspectives. The curriculum is designed to offer a balanced mix of theoretical understanding and practical application, fostering a comprehensive learning environment for students.

## Is an MBA from ASB Bengaluru Worth It?

Pursuing an MBA from Amrita School of Business, Bengaluru, is widely considered a valuable investment. The institute is known for its quality education, expert guidance, and a curriculum that balances academic theory with practical, real-world applications. Graduates consistently secure positions in leading companies, affirming the program's effectiveness in preparing students for successful careers in business.
