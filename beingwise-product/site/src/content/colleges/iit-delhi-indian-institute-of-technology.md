IIT Delhi, officially known as the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, stands as a premier institution for higher education in India. Located in the bustling capital, it offers a wide array of programs designed to shape future leaders and innovators across various fields. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structures, admission procedures, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered at IIT Delhi

IIT Delhi provides a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. Its offerings span engineering, science, humanities, and management, catering to a broad spectrum of academic interests.

### Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is the flagship program, with specializations including:

*   Computer Science and Engineering
*   Electrical Engineering
*   Mechanical Engineering
*   Chemical Engineering
*   Civil Engineering
*   Engineering Physics
*   Materials Engineering
*   Energy Science and Engineering
*   Biochemical Engineering and Biotechnology
*   Textile Technology
*   Production and Industrial Engineering
*   Engineering and Computational Mechanics
*   Mathematics and Computing Engineering
*   Electrical Engineering (Power and Automation)

IIT Delhi also offers integrated B.Tech + M.Tech programs in select disciplines like Computer Science and Engineering, Mathematics and Computing, and Chemical Engineering.

### Postgraduate Programs

For those seeking advanced studies, IIT Delhi offers Master of Technology (M.Tech), Master of Science (M.Sc), Master of Design (M.Des), and Master of Business Administration (MBA) programs. The Department of Management Studies (DMS) at IIT Delhi is particularly noted for its two-year full-time MBA program.

## Fee Structure

Understanding the financial commitment is crucial for prospective students. While specific fees can vary by program and year, here are some general indications:

*   **B.Tech Programs:** Tuition fees for B.Tech courses are set by the institute and are subject to change. Detailed breakdowns, including hostel and other charges, are available on the official admissions portal.
*   **M.Des Program:** The estimated total tuition fee for the two-year M.Des course is approximately INR 3 Lakhs. It's always advisable to verify the latest fee structure directly with the admissions department.
*   **MBA Program:** Fees for the MBA program at DMS IIT Delhi are also subject to revision. Candidates should consult the official DMS IIT Delhi website for the most current information.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to IIT Delhi's various programs is highly competitive, based on national-level entrance examinations.

### B.Tech Admissions

Admission to B.Tech and integrated B.Tech + M.Tech programs is primarily through the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Advanced. Candidates must first qualify JEE Main and then appear for JEE Advanced. The cutoff ranks for admission vary each year based on factors like the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. For the General AI category, B.Tech cutoff ranks in a recent year ranged from 126 to 5733.

### MBA Admissions

Admission to the MBA program at DMS IIT Delhi typically requires a valid CAT score, followed by group discussions and personal interviews.

## Placements at IIT Delhi

IIT Delhi boasts an impressive placement record, attracting top companies from India and abroad. The institute consistently achieves high placement rates across its programs.

### B.Tech Placements

During the 2023 placement drive, a significant number of B.Tech students secured lucrative offers. Notably, 50 students received domestic packages exceeding INR 1 crore per annum. The highest international package offered reached INR 2.5 crore per annum.

### MBA Placements (DMS IIT Delhi)

DMS IIT Delhi also demonstrates strong placement outcomes. In 2023, the MBA program achieved a 100% placement rate. The average package offered was INR 25.82 LPA, with the highest package reaching INR 41.13 LPA.

## Campus and Facilities

Spread across 325 acres, the IIT Delhi campus is well-planned, featuring distinct zones for academics, administration, residential facilities, and hostels. The infrastructure team continually works to enhance the campus facilities. Some hostels and common areas are equipped with air conditioning, providing a comfortable living and learning environment for students.

## Faculty at IIT Delhi

IIT Delhi prides itself on its highly qualified and experienced faculty members. Most hold PhDs from reputable institutions and bring extensive industry expertise to their teaching and research. They are dedicated to providing quality education and mentorship to students.
