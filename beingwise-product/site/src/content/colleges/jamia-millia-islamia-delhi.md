Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI), located in Delhi, is a respected public university known for its vibrant academic environment and rich history. It offers a wide array of courses, attracting students from across India.

## Why Choose Jamia Millia Islamia?

JMI stands out as a state public university with a strong emphasis on liberal arts and intellectual discourse. It has nurtured many prominent personalities, including actors like Shah Rukh Khan and Mouni Roy, and journalists such as Barkha Dutt and Arfa Khanum Sherwani. This commitment to fostering free thinkers is reflected in its curriculum, which is regularly updated to incorporate the latest developments in various fields.

Beyond its academic reputation, JMI holds approvals from numerous national regulatory bodies. These include the AICTE, BCI, CoA, Directorate General of Civil Aviation, Indian Council of Agricultural Research, INC, NCTE, National Medical Commission, PCI, and Rehabilitation Council of India, ensuring the quality and recognition of its diverse programs.

## Academic Programs and Fees

Jamia Millia Islamia offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses across various disciplines. Here's a look at some popular programs and their associated fees and eligibility criteria:

### Architecture and Planning Programs

JMI's Faculty of Architecture & Ekistics is particularly notable, being one of the few dedicated faculties of its kind. It provides specialized education in architectural and urban planning disciplines. The faculty boasts experienced educators, including those with PhDs and expertise as urban and rural planners and architects. They also host guest lectures by leading experts, offering students valuable insights and networking opportunities.

### M.Arch Specializations and Seats

The M.Arch program at JMI's Faculty of Architecture & Ekistics offers several specializations, with a total of 144 seats available. Each of the six specializations has 24 seats:

*   M.Arch. in Ekistics
*   M.Arch. in Architecture Pedagogy
*   M.Arch. in Building Services
*   M.Arch. in Health Architecture
*   M.Arch. in Recreation Architecture
*   M.Arch. in Urban Regeneration

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to JMI programs primarily relies on entrance examinations conducted by the university or national-level tests. For example, B.Arch admissions consider scores from NATA and JEE Main, while most M.Arch and M.Plan programs require candidates to clear the JMI Entrance Exam.

For engineering aspirants, after the JEE Main session 2 results are out, those targeting IITs and scoring above the 98th percentile should immediately begin preparing for JEE Advanced. Students in the 95th-98th percentile range are advised to focus on mock tests and previous year's papers to enhance their chances for JEE Advanced.

## Placements at Jamia Millia Islamia

JMI has a commendable placement record, particularly for its B.Tech and MBA programs. The university's strong alumni network plays a significant role in facilitating placement opportunities for its students. This network helps connect current students with various industries and organizations, contributing to strong placement outcomes year after year.

According to media reports, the highest packages offered during JMI placements in 2023 were:

*   **B.Tech:** INR 20 LPA – INR 25 LPA
*   **MBA:** INR 25 LPA

Students from Jamia Millia Islamia's Mass Communication Research Centre (MCRC) often secure better placements compared to some other Delhi institutions, largely due to the robust alumni connections that provide excellent industry exposure and job prospects.

## Facilities and Campus Life

Jamia Millia Islamia provides a range of facilities to its students, including separate hostel accommodations for boys and girls. The university campus fosters an environment conducive to learning and personal growth, offering resources that support academic and co-curricular activities. While the overall atmosphere encourages focus and dedication, students who maintain a strong mindset and engagement tend to have a rewarding college experience.
