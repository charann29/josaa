Lal Bahadur Shastri Institute of Management (LBSIM), located in Delhi near the metro station, is a well-regarded institution for management education. It offers a range of postgraduate and doctoral programs designed to prepare students for leadership roles in various industries.

## Programs Offered at LBSIM Delhi

LBSIM provides a diverse portfolio of management programs. These include several Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) specializations, an Executive PGDM, and a Fellowship Programme in Management (FPM).

### Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)

LBSIM offers multiple PGDM specializations, each tailored to specific industry needs. These full-time programs typically involve a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, preparing students for dynamic business environments. Some of the key PGDM specializations include:

*   **PGDM General:** A broad-based management program covering core business functions.
*   **PGDM Financial Management (FM):** Focuses on finance, investment, and financial markets.
*   **PGDM Research & Business Analytics (RBA):** Emphasizes data analysis, market research, and business intelligence.
*   **PGDM E-Business:** Concentrates on digital business strategies, e-commerce, and online operations.
*   **PGDM Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS):** Aims to equip students with skills in AI, machine learning, and data science for business applications.

### Executive PGDM

This program is designed for working professionals seeking to enhance their management skills without interrupting their careers. It typically accommodates a significant number of students, around 80-100, and requires a minimum of two years of executive-level work experience.

### Fellowship Programme in Management (FPM)

The FPM is a doctoral-level program for those interested in academic research and teaching in management. It involves rigorous study and research in a chosen area of specialization.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to LBSIM's programs is primarily entrance-based, ensuring a competitive selection process. The institute accepts scores from major national-level management entrance examinations.

### Entrance Exams Accepted

For PGDM and Executive PGDM programs, LBSIM considers scores from:

*   Common Admission Test (CAT)
*   Xavier Aptitude Test (XAT)
*   Management Aptitude Test (MAT)
*   Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT) (for Executive PGDM)

### Selection Stages

After meeting the initial entrance exam cutoff criteria, shortlisted candidates proceed through several stages:

1.  **Written Ability Test (WAT):** Assesses candidates' writing and analytical skills.
2.  **Group Discussion (GD):** Evaluates teamwork, leadership, and communication abilities.
3.  **Personal Interview (PI):** A one-on-one interaction to gauge personality, motivation, and suitability for the program.

Final admission decisions are based on a comprehensive evaluation of entrance exam scores, performance in WAT, GD, and PI, and academic record.

### FPM Application Process

Candidates for the FPM program need to apply online through the LBSIM website, submitting the required application fee and all relevant academic documents. Shortlisted candidates are then invited for a Personal Interview at the institute.

## Fees Structure

The application fee for the FPM program is typically around INR 1500, though this amount can vary slightly each year. Specific program fees for PGDM and Executive PGDM courses are detailed on the official LBSIM website and are subject to change annually.
