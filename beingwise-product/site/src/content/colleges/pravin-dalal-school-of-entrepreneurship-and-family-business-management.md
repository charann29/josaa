The Pravin Dalal School of Entrepreneurship and Family Business Management, part of NMIMS in Mumbai, offers unique programs designed for students aspiring to build their own ventures or take charge of family businesses. Located in Vile Parle, West, this institution focuses on nurturing future leaders with a blend of academic rigor and practical exposure.

## Pravin Dalal School of Entrepreneurship and Family Business Management: An Overview

This specialized school within NMIMS is dedicated to fostering an entrepreneurial mindset and equipping students with the skills needed for the dynamic world of business. It provides a supportive environment for learning about innovation, strategic management, and the nuances of family-run enterprises.

## Courses Offered

The Pravin Dalal School offers a range of programs tailored to different career stages and aspirations. These include undergraduate, postgraduate, and integrated courses.

*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)**: A three-year undergraduate program focusing on foundational business principles, leadership, and entrepreneurship.
*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA)**: A postgraduate program designed for those looking to deepen their management expertise, with a strong emphasis on entrepreneurship and family business strategies.
*   **Integrated BBA + MBA**: A five-year integrated program that combines undergraduate and postgraduate studies, offering a seamless progression for students committed to a career in business management.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the Pravin Dalal School is competitive and involves specific eligibility criteria and entrance examinations.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **For BBA and Integrated BBA+MBA**: Candidates must have secured at least 50% marks in their Class 12 examinations from a recognized board.
*   **For MBA**: Applicants generally need a bachelor's degree from a recognized university.

### Application Steps

1.  **Online Registration**: Prospective students need to register on the official NMIMS Pravin Dalal School website.
2.  **Application Submission**: Complete the application form with all necessary details and input the required information.
3.  **Entrance Exam**: Appear for the NMIMS NMAT (for BBA and Integrated BBA+MBA) or the in-house entrance test (for MBA).
4.  **Personal Interview (PI)**: For integrated courses and MBA programs, a Personal Interview is a mandatory part of the selection process.

### Application Fees

*   **For Undergraduate (UG) and Integrated BBA+MBA programs**: INR 2000
*   **For Postgraduate (PG) programs**: INR 2500

### Required Documents for Admission

Applicants should be prepared to submit the following documents:

*   Class 10 Marksheet and Certificate (for all programs)
*   Class 12 Marksheet and Certificate (for UG and Integrated PG programs)
*   Graduation Marksheet and Certificate (for PG programs)
*   Valid Identity Proof (e.g., Aadhaar Card, Passport, PAN Card)
