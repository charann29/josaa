Symbiosis Institute of Computer Studies and Research (SICSR), a part of Symbiosis International (Deemed University) in Pune, is a well-regarded institution for those looking to build a career in computer science and IT. This page offers a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered at SICSR Pune

SICSR Pune focuses on postgraduate programs, particularly in the realm of business administration with an IT focus and computer applications. The flagship programs include:

*   **MBA (Information Technology)**: This program is designed for individuals aspiring to leadership roles in the IT sector, blending management principles with technological expertise.
*   **MBA (Digital Transformation)**: Catering to the evolving digital landscape, this course prepares students to spearhead digital initiatives and manage technological change within organizations.
*   **M.Sc. (Computer Applications)**: A more technical and specialized program, it delves deep into various aspects of computer science and its applications.

## Admission Process for MBA Programs

Securing a spot in SICSR's MBA programs involves a structured selection procedure. Prospective students must meet specific eligibility criteria and successfully navigate through several stages.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the MBA programs, candidates typically need a bachelor's degree from a recognized university with at least 50% aggregate marks. A relaxation of 5% is usually provided for SC/ST candidates.

### Application Steps

1.  **Online Application**: Candidates must first visit the official Symbiosis website to fill out the application form. This involves providing personal, academic, and professional details, along with uploading necessary documents.
2.  **Entrance Exam**: Performance in a national-level management entrance examination is a crucial component of the admission process. SICSR primarily accepts scores from the SNAP (Symbiosis National Aptitude Test).
3.  **Shortlisting**: Based on academic records and entrance exam scores, a list of eligible candidates is prepared for the subsequent selection rounds.
4.  **Group Exercise (GE) & Personal Interview (PI)**: Shortlisted candidates participate in a group exercise, followed by a personal interview. These rounds assess communication skills, leadership potential, and overall suitability for the program.
5.  **Final Selection & Fee Payment**: Successful candidates receive an admission offer. To confirm their seat, they must pay the first installment of the course fees within the stipulated timeframe.

## Seat Intake

SICSR offers a limited number of seats for its MBA programs. For the MBA (Information Technology) program, there are approximately 90 seats available. The final seat intake can be influenced by various factors, including the total number of applications received, the performance of candidates in the selection process, and the institute's capacity.

## Fee Structure

The fees for programs at SICSR Pune are subject to change, so it's always best to check the latest details on the official website. However, generally, the fees for MBA programs are structured annually and can be paid in installments. Specific amounts for each program will be detailed in the admission brochure.

## Placements at SICSR Pune

SICSR has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with leading companies across various industries. The institute has a consistent record of placing its graduates in reputable organizations. Here's a glance at recent placement trends for different programs:

| Program                      | Highest Package (2024) | Average Package (2024) | Eligible Students (2024) | Students Placed (2024) |
| :--------------------------- | :--------------------- | :--------------------- | :----------------------- | :--------------------- |
| MBA (Information Technology) | INR 11.59 LPA          | INR 7.12 LPA           | 28                       | 26                     |
| MBA (Digital Transformation) | INR 7 LPA              | INR 5.34 LPA           | 11                       | 10                     |
| M.Sc. (Computer Applications) | INR 11.46 LPA          | INR 6.97 LPA           | 19                       | 17                     |

Comparing these with previous years, the placement statistics show a dynamic trend:

*   **2023**: MBA (IT) saw a highest package of INR 12.96 LPA and an average of INR 6.54 LPA. MBA (DT) had a highest of INR 12 LPA and an average of INR 6.84 LPA. M.Sc. (CA) recorded a highest of INR 90 LPA and an average of INR 10.52 LPA.
*   **2022**: MBA (IT) had a highest package of INR 25.91 LPA and an average of INR 7.42 LPA. M.Sc. (CA) recorded a highest of INR 14 LPA and an average of INR 7.37 LPA.

These figures demonstrate the varied opportunities available to SICSR graduates, with roles in IT consulting, software development, data analytics, and digital strategy being common.

## Required Documents for Application

When applying online for the MBA programs, candidates need to upload scanned copies of several documents:

*   Class 10th Marksheet and Certificate
*   Class 12th Marksheet and Certificate
*   Graduation Marksheet and Degree Certificate
*   SNAP Exam Scorecard
*   Migration Certificate
*   Transfer Certificate
*   Character Certificate
*   Caste Certificate (if applicable)

It is essential to have all these documents ready before starting the application process to ensure a smooth submission.
