IIM Kashipur, situated in the scenic foothills of Uttarakhand, is a prominent institution among India's elite management schools. It offers a dynamic learning environment focused on developing future business leaders. This guide provides a clear overview of its flagship programs, admissions process, fees, and career opportunities.

## IIM Kashipur - Indian Institute of Management: Courses, Fees, Admission, Cutoff & Placements

### Flagship Programs and Fees

IIM Kashipur is best known for its two-year full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) program, which is its most sought-after offering. The total program fee for the MBA ranges between INR 12 Lakh and INR 17.5 Lakh. This fee covers tuition and other academic expenses, though it's always wise to check the latest fee structure directly with the institute for exact figures.

### Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the MBA program at IIM Kashipur is highly competitive and does not offer any direct entry. Prospective students must meet specific eligibility criteria and achieve a qualifying percentile in the Common Admission Test (CAT).

**Eligibility Criteria:**

*   A Bachelor's degree from a university recognized by UGC/AIU with a minimum aggregate of 50% marks for general category candidates.
*   A minimum of three years of relevant post-graduation work experience (managerial, entrepreneurial, or professional) is typically required for certain programs.

**Application Process:**

Candidates need to submit several documents when applying for the MBA program. These typically include:

*   Class 10 and 12 mark sheets
*   Undergraduate degree certificate
*   Work experience certificates (if applicable)
*   Aadhaar Card and proof of date of birth
*   Address proof
*   Caste category certificate (if applicable)
*   Scanned images of photograph and signature
*   Details for online fee payment (credit/debit card or internet banking)

### CAT Cutoff for IIM Kashipur

IIM Kashipur uses CAT scores for its MBA admissions. While the exact cutoff for the upcoming academic year is released closer to the admission cycle, historical data provides a good indication. For general category candidates, the overall CAT percentile has consistently been around 93.

To give you an idea, here's a look at the sectional and overall CAT cutoffs from previous years for general category candidates:

| Section | 2020 | 2021 | 2022 |
| :------ | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Overall | 94   | 93   | 93   |
| Quants  | 80   | 93   | 93   |
| VA-RC   | 80   | 93   | 93   |
| DI-LR   | 80   | 93   | 93   |

Candidates aiming for a call from IIM Kashipur should target a CAT percentile of 93 or higher based on past trends.

### Placements and Career Opportunities

IIM Kashipur has a strong track record of placements, attracting leading companies from various sectors. For the batch of 2023, the average package stood at INR 18.11 LPA, with the highest domestic package reaching INR 37 LPA.

**Sector-wise Placement Distribution (MBA, 2025 data):**

*   IT: 26%
*   BFSI (Banking, Financial Services & Insurance): 20%
*   Manufacturing: 17%
*   Consulting: 16%
*   FinTech: 8%
*   Others: 13%

This distribution highlights a diverse range of opportunities available to graduates, with strong representation in technology, finance, and manufacturing sectors.

### Campus Life and Environment

Life at IIM Kashipur is vibrant and student-centric. The campus operates on a student-driven model, with various student bodies managing everything from placements and academics to extracurricular activities. Students actively organize major events like the annual festival 'Agnitraya' and management conferences, providing valuable hands-on experience in leadership, teamwork, and event management.

The institute is located in a peaceful setting in Uttarakhand, offering a pleasant environment for study and personal growth. Students often enjoy views of the Himalayas from their rooms during good weather, adding to the unique campus experience. Facilities include student-run canteens that support late-night study sessions, alongside the main mess and several restaurants.

### Faculty and Learning Experience

IIM Kashipur boasts an experienced faculty, many of whom hold PhDs and have extensive industry or academic backgrounds. The institution also invites visiting faculty members to conduct lectures and workshops, enriching the learning experience with diverse perspectives and real-world insights. The faculty's expertise ensures a rigorous and relevant curriculum, preparing students for the challenges of the business world.
