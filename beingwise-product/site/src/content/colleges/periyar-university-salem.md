Periyar University, located in Salem, Tamil Nadu, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Established to provide quality education and research opportunities, the university focuses on holistic student development.

## Periyar University: An Overview

Periyar University has consistently been recognized for its academic excellence. In the NIRF Ranking 2023, the university achieved the 59th position in the 'University' category, highlighting its standing among India's higher education institutions.

## Courses and Fees

The university offers diverse programs across various disciplines. While specific fee details can vary by course, the general range for MBA programs, for instance, is between INR 16,000 and INR 5 lakh. It's always advisable to check the official university prospectus for the most current fee structure for your chosen program.

### Popular Programs Offered

Periyar University provides courses in fields such as Arts, Science, Commerce, Management, and Technology. Some of the well-known programs include:

*   Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
*   Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
*   Bachelor of Science (BSc)
*   Master of Business Administration (MBA)
*   Master of Science (MSc)
*   Master of Technology (MTech)

## Admission Process

Admissions at Periyar University are based on a combination of academic merit, performance in entrance exams, and fulfillment of specific eligibility criteria for each course. The process typically involves several steps:

1.  **Application Submission:** Candidates must complete an online application form with all required details and submit it, along with the application fee, to the university's Head Office.
2.  **Entrance Examination:** Most undergraduate and postgraduate courses require applicants to appear for an entrance test conducted by the respective department or school. A good score in this test is crucial for eligibility.
3.  **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates are then considered for admission based on their performance in the qualifying examination or the entrance test. For programs like MBA, this stage may also include group discussions and personal interviews.
4.  **Fee Payment and Confirmation:** Successful candidates receive an offer of admission and must pay the prescribed admission fees within the given timeframe to confirm their seat.

## Cutoff Information

The cutoff for admission to various courses at Periyar University depends on factors such as the number of applicants, the difficulty of the entrance exam, and the availability of seats. Specific cutoff marks are determined annually by each department and are usually announced after the entrance examinations.

## Placements at Periyar University

Periyar University has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with potential employers. According to media reports from 2023, 20 students from the Energy Science and Technology Department received an annual package of INR 3.6 LPA.
