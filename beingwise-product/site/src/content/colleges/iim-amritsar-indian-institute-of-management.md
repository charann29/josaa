The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Amritsar is a prominent institution offering quality management education. Located in the historic city of Amritsar, it provides a range of programs designed to equip students with essential business skills and leadership qualities.

## Programs Offered at IIM Amritsar

IIM Amritsar provides a diverse portfolio of programs, catering to different academic backgrounds and career aspirations. These include full-time management degrees, an integrated program, and an executive option.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

This is a flagship two-year, full-time residential program. It's designed for graduates looking to build a strong foundation in business management. The curriculum covers various aspects of business administration, preparing students for leadership roles across industries.

### Executive MBA

Tailored for working professionals, this two-year program operates in a hybrid mode, blending online and in-person learning. It allows experienced individuals to enhance their management skills without interrupting their careers. Applicants need a bachelor's degree and a minimum of three years of work experience.

### Integrated Program in Management (IPM)

This is a unique five-year program that leads to an MBA degree. Students can choose to exit after three years with a Bachelor of Science in Quantitative Finance and Economics. It's suitable for students right after their 12th standard, offering a comprehensive path to management education.

### Master of Science (MSc) in Data Science and Management (MSDSM)

This specialized program focuses on equipping students with expertise in data science and its application in management. It's designed for those interested in leveraging data analytics for strategic decision-making.

### Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)

The PhD program at IIM Amritsar is for individuals aspiring to contribute to management research and academia. It involves rigorous coursework and dissertation work under expert faculty guidance.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to IIM Amritsar programs is competitive and primarily entrance-exam based. Each program has specific eligibility criteria and selection processes.

### MBA Admission

*   **Eligibility:** A bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved categories).
*   **Selection:** Candidates must have a valid CAT score. Shortlisted candidates proceed to a Personal Interview (PI) round. Final admission is based on a composite score considering CAT performance, PI, academic record, and work experience.

### Executive MBA Admission

*   **Eligibility:** A bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate (45% for reserved categories) and at least three years of work experience.
*   **Selection:** Valid scores in CAT, GRE, GMAT, or GATE are accepted. Alternatively, candidates can take the IIM Amritsar EMBA Aptitude Test (IAAT). This is followed by a Personal Interview.

### IPM Admission

*   **Eligibility:** Typically, candidates need to have completed their 12th standard with a minimum percentage (details provided in the admission brochure).
*   **Selection:** Shortlisting is based on IPMAT scores, followed by a Personal Interview round.

### PhD Admission

*   **Eligibility:** Master's degree or equivalent in any discipline with a minimum of 55% aggregate marks, or a professional qualification (CA, CS, ICWA) with a bachelor's degree, or a four-year bachelor's degree with a minimum of 6.5 CGPA.
*   **Selection:** Valid scores in CAT, GMAT, GRE, GATE, JRF (UGC/CSIR), or IIMB Test. This is followed by a research proposal presentation and interview.

## Important Dates (CAT 2023 for 2024 MBA Admissions)

For admission to the 2024 MBA batch, the Common Admission Test (CAT) 2023 was held on November 26, 2023. The exam was conducted in a Computer Based Testing (CBT) mode across various test centers in three slots:

*   **Morning Slot:** 8:30 AM to 10:30 AM
*   **Afternoon Slot:** 12:30 PM to 2:30 PM
*   **Evening Slot:** 4:30 PM to 6:30 PM

Since 2022, the CAT exam pattern includes a total of 66 questions, with a duration of 120 minutes. Prospective students should always refer to the official CAT website for the latest updates on exam dates and patterns for future admissions.
