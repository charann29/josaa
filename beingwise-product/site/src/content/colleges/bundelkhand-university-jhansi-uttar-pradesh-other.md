Bundelkhand University, located in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, is a well-established public institution offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, rankings, and placement opportunities to assist you in making an informed decision.

## About Bundelkhand University, Jhansi

Established in 1975, Bundelkhand University is a public university recognized with an 'A+' Grade accreditation by NAAC. It is known for its diverse academic offerings across various fields such as Pharmacy, Design, Commerce, and Computer Applications. Popular programs include B.Tech, B.Sc, and MBA.

### Rankings and Reputation

Bundelkhand University holds notable rankings in specific categories. For instance, it was ranked 68th in the Pharmacy category by NIRF 2025. Additionally, QS University Rankings 2026 placed it at 297th in the Asian University Rankings – Southern Asia category. The university prides itself on its experienced and dedicated faculty members.

## Admission Process

Admissions at Bundelkhand University are currently open and conducted entirely online. Prospective students can apply directly through the university's official portal. The application process typically involves submitting an online form, uploading necessary documents, and paying the application fee. Specific deadlines and entrance exam requirements, if any, will be detailed on the university website.

## Campus Facilities and Student Life

Bundelkhand University boasts a modern campus with state-of-the-art infrastructure designed to support student learning and development. Key facilities include:

*   **Innovation Center:** Encouraging research and new ideas.
*   **Digital Infrastructure:** Computer labs, Wi-Fi enabled campus, and virtual learning resources.
*   **Central Library:** A comprehensive resource for academic pursuits.
*   **Residential Campus:** Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls.
*   **Sports Facilities:** Both indoor and outdoor options, including a football ground, running track, badminton courts, table tennis, volleyball, and basketball. Professional training is also provided.
*   **Utility Centers:** Various services for daily needs.
*   **Eco-Friendly and Anti-Ragging Campus:** The university maintains a safe and secure environment with strict anti-ragging policies.

### Hostel Accommodation

The university provides well-furnished hostel accommodation separately for boys and girls. Currently, there are six girls' hostels, five for students and one for working women. For boys, the Lord Buddha PG hostel accommodates over 150 students, with a new hostel for reserved category students also planned. All hostels offer internet connectivity, 24/7 electricity backup, basic furniture, recreational areas, and mess facilities to ensure a comfortable stay.
