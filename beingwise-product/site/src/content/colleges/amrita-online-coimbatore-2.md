Amrita Online, based in Coimbatore, offers a flexible pathway to higher education through its diverse range of online programs. Whether you're looking to start your undergraduate journey or advance your career with a postgraduate degree, understanding the courses, fees, admission process, and potential outcomes is key. This guide from BeingWise aims to provide a clear overview to help you make an informed decision.

## Amrita Online: Programs on Offer

Amrita Online provides a variety of undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG), and certificate courses, all delivered in an online format. This flexibility allows students to pursue quality education from anywhere.

**Key Undergraduate Programs:**

*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** A popular choice, this three-year program is divided into six semesters. Students have the flexibility to complete the course over a maximum of six years. Specializations include:
    *   FinTech
    *   Digital Marketing & Sales
    *   Data Analytics
*   **Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA):** Focuses on foundational computer science and application development.
*   **Bachelor of Commerce (BCom):** Covers essential commerce and business principles.

**Key Postgraduate Programs:**

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** A flagship program designed for career advancement.
*   **Master of Computer Applications (MCA):** For those seeking advanced knowledge in computer applications.
*   **Master of Commerce (MCom):** Deepens understanding in commerce-related fields.

**Certificate Programs:**

Amrita Online also offers unique certificate courses that delve into cultural and philosophical topics, such as:

*   Mahabharata Upanayanam Certificate
*   Essence of Hinduism
*   Introduction to Hinduism

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admissions for all online UG and PG programs at Amrita Online are currently open. The selection process is primarily merit-based, evaluating a candidate's performance in their most recent qualifying examination.

### General Application Steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the Amrita Online admissions portal.
2.  **Initiate Enquiry:** Click on 'Apply Now' and fill out the initial enquiry form to create your login credentials.
3.  **Complete Application:** Log in using your new credentials, fill out the detailed application form, and submit the required application fee.

### Eligibility Criteria by Program Level:

*   **Undergraduate Programs (BBA, BCA, BCom):** Admission primarily considers Class 12 scores. A minimum aggregate of 45% in Class 12 is generally required for most programs. For BBA specifically, candidates should have passed Class 12 with at least 55% aggregate. Applicants with slightly lower percentages (below 55%) may still be considered after a short interview.
*   **Postgraduate Programs (MBA, MCA, MCom):** For PG courses, a bachelor's degree is mandatory. Class 12 scores alone are not sufficient. For MCA, applicants need an undergraduate degree and must have studied Mathematics either in Class 12 or at the graduation level.

Given the online, merit-based admission process, Amrita Online does not typically publish traditional 'cut-off' scores in the same way as campus-based programs. Admission is based on the merit of your last qualifying examination and meeting the specified eligibility criteria.
