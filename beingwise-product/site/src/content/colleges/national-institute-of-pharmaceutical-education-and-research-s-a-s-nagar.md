The National Institute of Pharmaceutical Education and Research (NIPER) in S.A.S. Nagar, Punjab, is a premier institution dedicated to advanced studies and research in pharmaceutical sciences. Established under the Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, Government of India, NIPER Mohali aims to be a center of excellence, fostering innovation and high-quality education.

## Programs Offered

NIPER S.A.S. Nagar provides a diverse range of postgraduate and doctoral programs in various pharmaceutical disciplines. These include:

*   **M.S. (Pharm)**: A master's program focused on research and specialized knowledge in pharmaceutical sciences. This two-year program is offered across nine specializations.
*   **M.Pharm**: A master's degree in Pharmacy, offering in-depth study in specific areas.
*   **M.Tech (Pharm)**: A technology-focused master's program within the pharmaceutical domain.
*   **MBA (Pharm)**: A Master of Business Administration program specifically tailored for the pharmaceutical industry, combining management principles with pharmaceutical knowledge.
*   **PhD**: Doctoral programs for advanced research and significant contributions to pharmaceutical science.

## Admission Process

Admission to NIPER S.A.S. Nagar's various programs is highly competitive and primarily conducted through the NIPER Joint Entrance Examination (NIPER JEE). This national-level examination is organized by the NIPERs and widely publicized.

### Key Points for Admission:

*   **NIPER JEE**: This is the main entrance exam for master's and PhD programs across all NIPER institutes, including S.A.S. Nagar.
*   **GPAT**: For M.Pharm courses, a strong score in the Graduate Pharmacy Aptitude Test (GPAT) is generally preferred.
*   **State-Level Entrance Exams**: Candidates who may not have excelled in GPAT can sometimes explore admission through state-level pharmacy entrance examinations, depending on specific program requirements and available seats.
*   **Counselling**: Following the NIPER JEE, a centralized counseling process determines seat allocation across the seven NIPER institutes located in Ahmedabad, Guwahati, Hajipur, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Rae Bareli, and S.A.S. Nagar.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for various programs at NIPER S.A.S. Nagar is determined by the institute. Specific details for each course are provided during the admission process and can be found on the official NIPER website. Generally, the fees cover tuition, laboratory charges, and other academic expenses.

## Cutoff Information

The cutoff for admission to NIPER S.A.S. Nagar depends on the performance in the NIPER JEE. For 2024, a total of 1050 Master of Pharmacy (MPharm) and 180 PhD seats are available across all NIPER institutes combined. The cutoffs vary each year based on the number of applicants, their performance, and the availability of seats in specific specializations.

## Placements

NIPER S.A.S. Nagar has a strong record of placements, with many graduates securing positions in leading pharmaceutical companies. The institute consistently attracts top recruiters from the industry.

### Placement Highlights (2022):

*   **M.Pharm Placements**: Out of 249 M.Pharm students, 200 were successfully placed.
*   **Median Salary**: The median package for M.Pharm graduates in 2022 was INR 6.35 LPA.
