IndSearch Institute of Management Studies and Research, located at the Bavdhan Campus in Pune, is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

IndSearch primarily focuses on postgraduate management education. The institute offers the following flagship programs:

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA)**
*   **Master of Management Studies (MMS)**

These programs are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), ensuring their quality and recognition. The total intake capacity across all programs at the Bavdhan campus is 180 students.

## Fee Structure

Specific fee details for each course are typically updated annually. Prospective students are encouraged to check the official IndSearch website or contact the admissions office directly for the most current fee information for the MBA and MMS programs.

## Admission Process

Admission to IndSearch's management programs generally involves a multi-stage process:

1.  **Application:** Candidates must submit a completed application form, either online or offline.
2.  **Entrance Exam:** Applicants are usually required to appear for a national-level management entrance examination (e.g., CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, ATMA, MH-CET) or the institute's own entrance test, if applicable.
3.  **Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates from the entrance exam stage are invited for a Group Discussion and Personal Interview round.
4.  **Final Selection:** Selection is based on a composite score, considering academic record, entrance exam performance, and GD/PI results.

## Cutoff Information

Cutoffs for admission to IndSearch's MBA/MMS programs vary each year depending on the number of applicants, their performance in entrance exams, and the overall difficulty of the tests. Candidates are advised to consult the institute's admission brochure or website for specific cutoff percentiles for the relevant admission cycle.
