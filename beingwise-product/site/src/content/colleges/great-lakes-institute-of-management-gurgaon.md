Great Lakes Institute of Management (GLIM), Gurgaon, is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. This guide from BeingWise provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, selection criteria, and placement outcomes to assist you in making an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

GLIM Gurgaon focuses on two primary full-time management programs:

*   **Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM):** This is a one-year program designed for candidates with prior work experience, aiming to fast-track their careers or transition into leadership roles.
*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** This is a two-year program suitable for fresh graduates or those with limited work experience, providing a comprehensive foundation in management principles.

These programs are considered the flagship offerings at Great Lakes, Gurgaon.

## Fees Structure

The specific fee details for the PGPM and PGDM programs are typically updated annually. Prospective students should check the official GLIM Gurgaon website for the most current information regarding tuition and other associated costs.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to GLIM Gurgaon's PGPM and PGDM programs is competitive and involves several stages.

### Application Process

The application window for the PGPM and PGDM courses usually opens around August 1st each year. Interested candidates can apply online through the institute's official website by filling out the application form and paying a fee of INR 2,000. It's also necessary to provide the official email address of a recommender.

### Eligibility Criteria

GLIM Gurgaon considers a holistic profile for admissions. Key factors include:

*   **Academic Background:** Strong past academic records are important.
*   **Work Experience:** Particularly crucial for the PGPM program.
*   **Entrance Exam Scores:** Applicants must submit scores from GMAT, CAT, or XAT. For the PGDM program, a minimum CAT percentile of 70 is generally required.
*   **Essays and Recommendations:** These provide insight into a candidate's personality and goals.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** Performance in the personal interview is a critical component of the selection process.

### Evaluation Criteria

The institute evaluates candidates based on their overall performance, looking beyond just academic excellence. The assessment considers academic achievements, work experience, recommendations, essays (often collected during the interview stage), and performance in the entrance exams and personal interview.

## Cutoff Scores

For admission to the PGDM program, candidates are typically expected to achieve a minimum of **70 percentile** in the CAT examination. While this is a baseline, higher scores are always advantageous due to the competitive nature of admissions.

## Campus Life and Facilities

GLIM Gurgaon provides a supportive environment for its students, including:

*   **Dining Facilities:** The mess offers a variety of healthy Indian meals from different regions, with a student-led Food Committee ensuring diverse options throughout the week for breakfast, lunch, snacks, and dinner.
*   **Medical Services:** A health center with qualified medical personnel is available on campus to provide first aid and address minor health concerns or injuries. Emergency medical services are also accessible to both students and faculty.
