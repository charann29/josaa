Guru Jambheshwar University of Science and Technology (GJUST), located in Hisar, Haryana, is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of programs in science, technology, and humanities. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fees, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities to assist prospective students and their families.

## GJUST: An Overview

GJUST is dedicated to fostering academic excellence and practical skills across various disciplines. The university aims to prepare students for successful careers in both the public and private sectors through its comprehensive curriculum and focus on research.

### Academic Faculties and Departments

The university is structured into several faculties, each housing multiple departments that offer specialized programs:

*   **Faculty of Engineering & Technology:**
    *   Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
    *   Department of Civil Engineering
    *   Department of Computer Science & Engineering
    *   Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering
    *   Department of Mechanical Engineering
    *   Department of Printing Technology

*   **Faculty of Environmental And Bio Sciences & Technology:**
    *   Department of Biotechnology
    *   Department of Botany
    *   Department of Environmental Science & Engineering
    *   Department of Food Technology
    *   Department of Zoology

*   **Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences:**
    *   Department of Applied Psychology
    *   Department of Economics
    *   Department of English and Foreign Languages
    *   Department of Geography
    *   Department of Hindi
    *   Department of Library and Information Science
    *   Department of Mass Communication

*   **Faculty of Education:**
    *   Department of Education
    *   Board of Studies & Research in Education (primarily for affiliated colleges)

### Popular Courses and Fees

GJUST offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. Here's a look at some popular courses and their approximate fees:

*   **B.Tech:** This is one of the most sought-after programs at GJUST, providing a strong foundation in various engineering disciplines.
*   **B.Pharm:** The Bachelor of Pharmacy program has a sanctioned intake of 60 students. Admission is based on performance in qualifying examinations, followed by a counseling round.
*   **M.A.:** The Master of Arts program is offered with various specializations. The total fees vary by specialization:

| MA Specialization   | Approximate Total Fee (INR) |
| :------------------ | :-------------------------- |
| English             | 25,100                      |
| Hindi               | 25,100                      |
| Mass Communication  | 28,200                      |
| Education           | 35,500                      |

*   **M.Sc. Programs:** The Department of Biotechnology, for instance, offers integrated B.Sc-M.Sc programs, M.Sc. Biotechnology, and M.Sc. Microbiology, along with Ph.D. options in Biotechnology and Microbiology. Other departments also offer various M.Sc. specializations like Botany, Zoology, and Chemistry.

### Admission Process

Admission to GJUST programs typically involves evaluating academic merit from qualifying examinations. For some courses, admission may also involve entrance tests, followed by counselling rounds for final seat allocation. Specific details for each program are usually available on the university's official admissions portal.

### Placement Opportunities

GJUST has a dedicated placement cell that facilitates career opportunities for its students. The university sees a good number of companies participating in its annual recruitment drives.

**Key Placement Highlights (2023):**

*   **Highest Package:** INR 14.75 LPA
*   **Total Students Placed:** 330
*   **Participating Companies:** 77
