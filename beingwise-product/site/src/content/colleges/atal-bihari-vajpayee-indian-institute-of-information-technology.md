The Atal Bihari Vajpayee Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management (ABV-IIITM) in Gwalior is a prominent institution for higher education, particularly in the fields of information technology and management. This guide offers a clear overview of its academic programs, fee structure, admission criteria, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities.

## Programs Offered

ABV-IIITM Gwalior provides a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs designed to equip students with strong theoretical knowledge and practical skills in IT and management. A key offering is the unique Integrated B.Tech (Information Technology) + MBA program, which combines technical engineering education with business management principles over five years.

### Integrated B.Tech (Information Technology) + MBA

This five-year integrated program is tailored for students aspiring to blend engineering expertise with management acumen. It offers a comprehensive curriculum covering core IT subjects alongside essential business administration modules. The program aims to produce graduates who are not only technically proficient but also possess strong leadership and managerial capabilities.

### Other Programs

Beyond the integrated B.Tech + MBA, the institute also offers:

*   **M.Tech Programs:** Specializations are available in areas like Computer Science and Engineering, and Information Technology.
*   **MBA Programs:** A Master of Business Administration focusing on various management domains.
*   **Ph.D. Programs:** Research opportunities across diverse fields within information technology and management.

## Fee Structure

Fees at ABV-IIITM Gwalior vary by program. For the integrated B.Tech + MBA program, students typically pay tuition fees per semester, in addition to hostel, mess, and other miscellaneous charges. The institute aims to keep its fee structure competitive while providing high-quality education and facilities. Prospective students should check the official institute website for the most current and detailed fee breakdown for their specific program of interest.

## Admission Process

Admission to the integrated B.Tech + MBA program at ABV-IIITM Gwalior is primarily based on performance in the JEE Main examination. Candidates must register for the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main and achieve a competitive score to be eligible for counseling.

### JEE Main for B.Tech + MBA

Following the JEE Main results, eligible candidates participate in the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) counseling process or the Central Seat Allocation Board (CSAB) special rounds. Seats are allocated based on JEE Main All India Ranks, choices filled by candidates, and seat availability under various categories.

### Other Programs

*   **M.Tech Admission:** Typically requires a valid GATE score.
*   **MBA Admission:** Often based on scores from national-level management entrance exams like CAT, MAT, or CMAT, followed by group discussions and personal interviews.
*   **Ph.D. Admission:** Involves an entrance test conducted by the institute, followed by an interview, often requiring a strong academic record and a research proposal.

## Cutoff Trends

The cutoff ranks for admission to the integrated B.Tech + MBA program at ABV-IIITM Gwalior are determined by JEE Main scores and vary each year based on factors such as the number of applicants, seat availability, and the overall performance of candidates. Generally, a higher JEE Main rank is required for admission.

Recent trends have shown some fluctuations in closing ranks. For instance, in 2021, the closing rank for the General All India Quota for B.Tech was around 18109, which then became more competitive, decreasing to 14912 in 2022. Similar shifts were observed across other categories:

| Category | 2021 Closing Rank | 2022 Closing Rank |

| OBC      | 29609             | 22029             |
| SC       | 68071             | 76891             |
| ST       | 178342            | 124467            |
| EWS      | 23911             | 18355             |
| PWD      | 1240              | 708               |

These cutoffs can also differ slightly for female candidates within each category. Prospective students should review the official JoSAA/CSAB archives for the most precise cutoff data for their specific category and year.

## Placements

ABV-IIITM Gwalior has a strong track record of placements, with students securing positions in leading IT companies, consulting firms, and management roles. The institute's dedicated placement cell works diligently to connect students with industry opportunities, facilitating internships and final placements.

The integrated B.Tech + MBA program, in particular, prepares graduates for diverse roles that require both technical and managerial acumen, making them highly sought after by recruiters. Companies from various sectors, including software development, data analytics, product management, and business consulting, regularly recruit from the campus.

## Campus Life and Facilities

Spread across approximately 160 acres, the ABV-IIITM Gwalior campus offers a comprehensive and vibrant environment for learning and personal growth. The infrastructure includes well-equipped academic blocks, advanced computer laboratories, and data processing centers.

For student recreation and well-being, the campus boasts extensive sports facilities. These include a swimming pool, gymnasium, lawn tennis courts, basketball courts, squash courts, billiards rooms, table tennis boards, and badminton courts. Additionally, separate sports amenities like a cricket ground and football field are available within the hostel premises. The institute also organizes various seminars and events to foster intellectual engagement and extracurricular development.

## Faculty
