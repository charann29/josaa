KCC Institute of Technology and Management, located in Greater Noida, is a well-regarded institution offering a range of technical and management programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff details, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## KCC Institute of Technology and Management: An Overview

KCC Institute of Technology and Management is recognized as a Tier 2 institution. It holds an 'A' grade accreditation from NAAC, is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), and is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU).

## Courses and Eligibility

KCC Institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and management. Admission to these courses is primarily based on entrance examination scores. Here’s a look at the eligibility and selection criteria for some key programs:

| Course | Eligibility | Selection Criteria |
| :----- | :---------- | :----------------- |
| B.Tech | Class 12 with at least 45% marks (40% for reserved categories) in Physics and Mathematics. | JEE Main |
| M.Tech | B.Tech degree from a recognized university with a minimum CGPA/CPI of 6.74. | GATE / UPCET (for non-GATE candidates) |
| MBA | Graduation with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. | CAT / C1 level English proficiency (CEFR) + Personal Interview (PI) |

## Application Process

Applying to KCC Institute of Technology and Management is straightforward and conducted online. Prospective students can follow these steps:

*   **Visit the Official Website:** Go to the institute's admissions portal.
*   **Register:** Click on 'Apply Now' and complete the basic registration with your details.
*   **Log In:** Use your newly created credentials to log in to the application portal.
*   **Fill the Form:** Complete the detailed application form with all necessary information.
*   **Pay Fee & Submit:** Pay the application fee online and submit your form.

Applications for various courses are currently open, and candidates should apply directly through the official admissions website.

## M.Tech Admissions

Admission to the M.Tech program at KCC Institute is entrance-based. Candidates are required to have a valid GATE score. Those without a GATE score can seek admission based on their performance in UPCET (formerly known as UPSEE). An M.Tech degree from KCC can open doors to diverse career paths in research and development, entrepreneurship, corporate roles, and teaching.

## Placement Opportunities

KCC Institute of Technology and Management has a dedicated placement cell that facilitates excellent career opportunities for its students. The placement season for 2024-25 showed promising results:

### Placement Highlights (2024-25)

*   **Highest Package (B.Tech):** INR 32.17 LPA
*   **Highest Package (MBA):** INR 27.47 LPA
*   **Average Package (B.Tech):** INR 12.18 LPA
*   **Average Package (MBA):** INR 12.63 LPA
*   **Total Recruiters:** 157 (including Fortune 500 companies, Silicon Valley startups, and Series A & B funded ventures)
*   **Total Offers (B.Tech & MBA):** 852

**Some of the popular recruiting companies include:** Cognizant, HCL Tech, and Infosys.

### Industry-wise MBA Placements (2024-25)

During the MBA placements, the IT & Services sector was the dominant recruiter. Here's a breakdown by industry:

*   **IT & Services:** 47%
*   **Banking & Finance:** 25%
*   **Digital Media & Marketing:** 13%
*   **FMCG:** 7%
*   **Product:** 6%
*   **Consulting:** 2%
