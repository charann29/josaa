Planning your higher education journey in India can feel overwhelming, but understanding key entrance exams like IPU CET is a great first step. The Indraprastha University Common Entrance Test (IPU CET) is your gateway to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs offered by Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) and its affiliated colleges.

## IPU CET 2026: Key Dates at a Glance

Staying informed about important dates is crucial for a smooth application process. Here's a summary of the expected timeline for IPU CET 2026:

| Event             | Tentative Date (2026) |
|-------------------|-----------------------|
| Application Form Release | Expected February     |
| Application Deadline | April 11              |
| Admit Card Release | A few days before exam |
| Exam Dates        | April 25 - May 17     |
| B.Tech (Biotechnology) Exam | April                 |
| Result Declaration | A few days after exam (course-wise) |
| Counselling Begins| Post result declaration |

## Understanding the IPU CET Application Process

The application process for IPU CET 2026 involves several steps, all of which are conducted online. It's important to complete each stage carefully to ensure your application is valid.

### Step-by-Step Application Guide

1.  **Registration:** Create your account on the official GGSIPU admission portal.
2.  **Form Filling:** Accurately enter all personal, academic, and contact details.
3.  **Document Upload:** Upload scanned copies of required documents, including your photograph, signature, and thumb impression.
4.  **Fee Payment:** Pay the application fee online. Please note, this fee is non-refundable.
5.  **Submission:** Review your application thoroughly before final submission.

### Documents to Keep Handy for Application

Before you start filling out the form, gather these essential documents:

*   Class 10th Mark Sheet
*   Class 12th Mark Sheet
*   Diploma Certificate (if applicable)
*   Date of Birth Certificate
*   Caste/Category Certificate (if applicable)
*   Passport-sized photograph
*   Scanned signature
*   Scanned left-hand thumb impression

## IPU CET Exam Details

IPU CET is a national-level entrance examination conducted by GGSIPU. It assesses candidates' aptitude for admission to a wide range of programs.

### Exam Dates and Specific Courses

The IPU CET 2026 exams for various undergraduate and postgraduate programs are scheduled between April 25 and May 17, 2026. Specifically, the B.Tech (Biotechnology) exam is expected to be held in April 2026.

### Result Declaration

Results for different courses will be announced separately, a few days after the respective exams conclude. For courses like Lateral Entry B.Tech for Diploma Holders (CET Code - 128), Lateral Entry B.Tech for B.Sc. Holders (CET Code - 129), and Biotechnology (CET Code - 130), results are tentatively expected in June 2026. Candidates should regularly check the official website for updates.

## Counselling and Admission Process

Once the results are declared, the counselling process begins. This is a critical stage where your IPU CET score and preferences determine your seat allocation.

### Choice Filling

During counselling, you'll have the opportunity to fill in your preferred courses and colleges. There's no limit to the number of choices you can make, so it's advisable to list as many as possible to maximize your chances of securing a seat based on your score and availability.

### Document Verification

After provisional seat allotment, candidates must undergo a document verification process. Ensure you have the following original documents ready:

*   JEE Main Admit Card & Result (for B.Tech courses)
*   IPU CET 2026 Score Card (for B.Tech Biotechnology, B.Tech Lateral Entry for B.Sc. Graduates, B.Tech Lateral Entry for Diploma Holders)
*   Caste Category Certificate (if applicable)
*   Admission Receipt
*   Provisional Allotment Letter
*   Character Certificate

Providing all necessary documents is essential for confirming your admission.

## Courses, Fees, Cutoff & Placements

GGSIPU and its affiliated colleges offer a diverse range of programs. While specific fees vary by course and college, generally, admission is highly competitive, with cutoffs depending on the number of applicants and their performance in the IPU CET. The university has a strong track record of placements across various industries, providing good career opportunities for its graduates.
