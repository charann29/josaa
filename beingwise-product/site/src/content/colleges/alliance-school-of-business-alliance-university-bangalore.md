The Alliance School of Business, part of Alliance University in Bangalore, is a well-regarded institution for management and commerce education. It offers a range of programs designed to equip students with the skills needed for the modern business world.

## Courses Offered and Their Fees

Alliance School of Business provides various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Here's a look at some of their key offerings and associated fees:

*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** This is a three-year undergraduate program. The total fee for the BBA course is generally around INR 9 lakh. For the four-year BBA (Honours) program, the total fee will be higher. A typical fee breakdown for BBA might include an initial registration fee, followed by tuition fees paid in installments.
*   **Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com):** Details about the B.Com fee structure are available upon application.
*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** The MBA is a flagship program at the Alliance School of Business. Specific fee details are provided during the admission process.
*   **Executive Post Graduate Diploma in Management (EPGDM):** This program is designed for working professionals. The total fee for the EPGDM course is approximately INR 2.50 lakh.
*   **PhD Programs:** The university also offers doctoral programs in various specializations.

## Admission Process

Applying to the Alliance School of Business involves several steps, from application to final selection. The entire process is conducted online.

### Application Procedure

All candidates must apply online. An application fee is required, which varies by program:

*   **BBA, B.Com, MBA:** INR 1,000
*   **PhD:** INR 3,000

### Selection Rounds

The selection criteria differ based on whether you're applying for an undergraduate or postgraduate course.

*   **For MBA:** Candidates need to have a valid score from national-level management tests such as MAT, CAT, XAT, NMAT, CMAT, or KMAT. The university also accepts scores from its own ALLIANCE-MAT. Following this, applicants participate in an Oral Extempore Speech and a Personal Interview (PI).
*   **For BBA and B.Com:** Applicants must qualify through the Alliance University Scholastic Aptitude Test (AUSAT), which is then followed by a Personal Interview round.
*   **For EPGDM:** Admission is based on academic merit from the last qualifying exam, combined with a Personal Interview. Candidates need a graduation degree with at least 50% aggregate marks and two years of full-time work experience. Those with a Diploma and a minimum of five years of work experience are also eligible.

### Merit List and Counselling

After the selection rounds, a merit list is published based on candidate performance. Those whose names appear on the merit list are invited for a counselling session. Admission is confirmed upon payment of the course fees.

## Placements

The Alliance School of Business has a strong focus on career development and placements. The MBA program, being a flagship offering, prepares graduates for significant roles.

*   **MBA Roles:** Graduates are typically placed in managerial and leadership positions, often involving team leadership, strategic planning, project execution, client liaison, and inter-departmental coordination.
*   **Industry Focus:** The curriculum and placement cell aim to connect students with opportunities across various sectors, ensuring they are well-prepared for the demands of the corporate world.

Alliance School of Business strives to provide a comprehensive education that leads to promising career paths for its graduates.
