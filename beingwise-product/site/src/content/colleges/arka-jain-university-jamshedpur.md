Arka Jain University, located in Jamshedpur, offers a diverse range of academic programs. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, eligibility criteria, and placement opportunities to help prospective students and their families make informed decisions.

## Courses and Eligibility

Arka Jain University provides undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) programs across various disciplines. Here's a look at some popular courses and their general eligibility requirements:

| Course Name       | Eligibility                                                               | Selection Criteria                                  |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :-------------------------------------------------- |
| B.Tech            | Class 12 with at least 45% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, and Math (40% for reserved categories) | JEE Mains, AJU-Combined Entrance Test, or CUET      |
| MBA               | Graduation with a minimum of 50% aggregate (45% for reserved categories) | CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, or AJU-Combined Entrance Test (AJUCET) |
| BBA LLB (Hons)    | Class 12 with at least 45% aggregate (42% for OBC, 40% for SC/ST)      | CUET, CLAT, or AJU-Combined Entrance Test (AJUCET) |
| M.Com             | Any graduate                                                              | AJU-Combined Entrance Test (AJUCET)                 |
| B.Sc (Hons)       | Class 10 and Class 12 from Science Stream with at least 50% aggregate   | Merit-based                                         |

Generally, a minimum of 40% in Class 12 can make you eligible for several courses, including B.Tech and M.Tech, depending on the specific program.

## Fee Structure

The total tuition fees at Arka Jain University vary significantly by course. Below are approximate tuition fees for some of the prominent programs:

*   **B.Tech:** INR 4.8 lakh to INR 8 lakh
*   **MBA:** INR 5 lakh
*   **BBA LLB (Hons):** INR 5 lakh
*   **B.Pharma:** INR 6 lakh

*Please note that these figures are indicative and based on information from official sources; actual fees may change.*

## Admission Process

Applying to Arka Jain University can be done both online and offline. For online applications, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official university website.
2.  Navigate to the "Admissions" section and click "Apply Online."
3.  Register by providing your name, mobile number, and email address.
4.  Complete the application form with all necessary details.
5.  Submit the form.
6.  Pay the application fee online using debit/credit cards or net banking.

### Required Documents for Admission

When applying, ensure you have the following documents ready:

*   Class 10 mark sheet and certificate
*   Class 12 mark sheet and certificate
*   Graduation degree and mark sheets (for PG courses)
*   Post-graduation degree and mark sheets (for PhD courses)
*   No Objection Certificate (NOC) from your current employer (if applying for PhD while employed)
*   Character certificate
*   Migration certificate
*   Entrance test scorecard (if applicable)
*   Recent passport-size photographs
