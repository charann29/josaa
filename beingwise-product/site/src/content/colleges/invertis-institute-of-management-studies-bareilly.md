## Invertis Institute of Management Studies, Bareilly: An Overview

Located in Bareilly, IIMS offers focused management education. The institute aims to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed for a successful career in business and management. Students have consistently rated the institute well for its infrastructure and faculty.

## Programs Offered

IIMS offers both undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs in Business and Management Studies. These full-time courses are designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the management field.

*   **Undergraduate Program:** Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
*   **Postgraduate Program:** Master of Business Administration (MBA) / Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)

The total student intake across all programs at Invertis Institute of Management Studies is approximately 450.
