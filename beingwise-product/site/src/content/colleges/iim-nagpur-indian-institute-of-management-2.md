The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Nagpur is a prominent institution offering world-class management education. Located in the vibrant city of Nagpur, it provides a nurturing environment for aspiring business leaders.

## Courses Offered at IIM Nagpur

IIM Nagpur provides a range of programs designed to cater to different career stages and aspirations. These include postgraduate, doctoral, and executive-level courses. The flagship offering is the two-year Master of Business Administration (MBA) program, available for both domestic and international students. Additionally, the institute offers a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD), Executive PhD, and Executive MBA, alongside various other specialized courses.

## IIM Nagpur MBA Program Details

The MBA program at IIM Nagpur has a total intake capacity of 280 students. To be eligible, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university with at least 50% aggregate marks. A 5% relaxation in the minimum marks is provided for candidates belonging to reserved categories.

### Admission Process for MBA

Admission to the MBA program is highly competitive and entrance-based. The primary entrance examination accepted is the Common Admission Test (CAT). The selection process involves several stages:

1.  **CAT Score Shortlisting:** Candidates are initially shortlisted based on their CAT scores.
2.  **Written Ability Test (WAT) / Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates must participate in either a Written Ability Test and Personal Interview or a Group Discussion and Personal Interview round, depending on the year's selection criteria.
3.  **Final Merit List:** A comprehensive final merit list is prepared, considering multiple factors such as academic performance, CAT score, interview performance, gender diversity, and relevant work experience.

### Important Application Guidelines

When filling out the IIM Nagpur MBA application form, it is crucial to ensure all details are accurate. This includes personal information like date of birth, academic qualifications (degree titles, percentages), and work experience. Any discrepancies between the information provided in the online application and the supporting documents submitted later will lead to the cancellation of the admission offer. Therefore, double-checking all entries before submission is vital.

If you have received interview calls from other IIMs but did not attend them, you will still be considered for IIM Nagpur's MBA program. The institute conducts its personal interview process independently. As long as you have appeared for CAT, cleared the first stage cut-offs, and submitted your application by the deadline, you should await an email regarding your shortlist for the Personal Interview round, which is based on clearing the comprehensive score (CS) cut-off.

## Placement Highlights at IIM Nagpur

IIM Nagpur is dedicated to providing excellent placement assistance to its students, helping them secure desirable career opportunities. The institute consistently attracts a diverse range of recruiters from various sectors.

In the 2024 placement session, 255 students participated, and the institute welcomed 62 new recruiters. Key sectors that actively recruited included Consulting, BFSI (Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance), ITES (IT Enabled Services), Manufacturing, FMCG/FMCD (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods/Durables), and Conglomerates.
