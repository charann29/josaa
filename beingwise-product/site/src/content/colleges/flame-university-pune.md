FLAME University, located in Pune, stands out as a distinctive institution focusing on liberal education. It aims to provide students with a holistic learning experience that goes beyond traditional academics.

## Courses Offered at FLAME University

FLAME University provides a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, primarily in the fields of Business Administration, Science, and Management. Popular choices among students include:

*   **Undergraduate Programs (3 years):**
    *   Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
    *   Bachelor of Science (BSc)
    *   Bachelor of Arts (BA)

*   **Postgraduate Programs (2 years):**
    *   Master of Business Administration (MBA)
    *   MBA (Communications Management)

These programs are designed to equip students with critical thinking skills and a broad understanding of their chosen disciplines.

## Fees Structure

Understanding the financial commitment is a key part of choosing a university. For the undergraduate BSc program at FLAME University, the total tuition fee is approximately INR 22.2 lakhs. Specific fee details for other courses can be found on the university's official admissions portal.

## Admission Process and Deadlines

Admissions to FLAME University are conducted in multiple cycles throughout the year, primarily through the FLAME Entrance Aptitude Test (FEAT) and also by considering national-level entrance exam scores. The application process is entirely online and involves several steps:

1.  **Visit the official website:** Navigate to the FLAME University admissions section.
2.  **Start your application:** Click on 'Apply' and then 'go to application portal' to begin registration.
3.  **Register and Login:** Provide basic details to register and then log in using your new credentials.
4.  **Complete the application form:** Fill in all required personal, educational, and other relevant information.
5.  **Upload documents & pay fee:** Submit necessary documents and complete the application fee payment.
6.  **Submit and print:** Finalize your application and keep a printout for your records.

### Important Dates for Admissions (Expected for 2026 Admissions)

FLAME University releases its FEAT dates for undergraduate courses around October. The admission process generally runs from December to the end of May. Here are the anticipated deadlines and interview dates for various cycles:

| Cycle             | Application Deadline | Personal Interview Dates      |
| :---------------- | :------------------- | :---------------------------- |
| Online Cycle I    | January 4, 2026      | January 9 - 11, 2026          |
| Online Cycle II   | February 1, 2026     | February 9 - 13, 2026         |
| Online Cycle III  | March 29, 2026       | April 6 - 10, 2026            |
| Online Cycle IV   | May 3, 2026          | May 8 - 14, 2026              |

After taking the FEAT, candidates will also participate in an Essay Writing segment followed by a Personal Interview on the same day. FEAT results are typically announced 10-15 days after the exam, accessible via your login on the university's website.

## Cutoff Information

FLAME University primarily uses its own entrance test, FEAT, along with other national-level exam scores and a comprehensive evaluation of candidates' profiles, including essay writing and personal interviews. There isn't a fixed 'cutoff score' in the traditional sense, as admission decisions are based on a holistic assessment of each applicant's academic record, test performance, and personal attributes.

## Campus Life and Facilities

FLAME University's campus in Pune is known for its modern facilities and a safe environment, with CCTV surveillance ensuring student security. The university offers a range of amenities to support academic and extracurricular pursuits, including:

*   Amphitheaters for events and presentations.
*   Specialized laboratories like the J.C. Bose - Biology Lab and Bhaskarachar labs.
*   A Kabir Recording Studio.
*   Well-connected location for easy accessibility.

## Faculty at FLAME University

The university prides itself on a team of experienced faculty members who hold advanced degrees, including PhDs. The academic staff comprises specialists and full-time educators, complemented by guest lecturers and opportunities for student and faculty exchange programs, enriching the learning experience.
