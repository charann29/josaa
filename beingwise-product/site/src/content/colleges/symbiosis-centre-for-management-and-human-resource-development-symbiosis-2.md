The Symbiosis Centre for Management & Human Resource Development (SCMHRD), a constituent of Symbiosis International (Deemed University) in Pune, is a well-regarded institution for management education. It offers a range of postgraduate programs designed to prepare students for leadership roles in various industries. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Courses Offered at SCMHRD

SCMHRD primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs. The flagship offering is the Master of Business Administration (MBA) with various specializations, designed to cater to different career aspirations and industry needs. These programs are structured to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical exposure.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

SCMHRD offers a full-time MBA program with distinct specializations:

*   **MBA (Human Resources):** This specialization focuses on developing professionals for HR leadership roles, covering areas like talent management, organizational development, and industrial relations.
*   **MBA (Business Analytics):** Tailored for the data-driven world, this program equips students with skills in data analysis, business intelligence, and decision-making using analytical tools.
*   **MBA (Infrastructure Management):** This unique program addresses the growing demand for professionals in the infrastructure sector, covering project management, policy, and finance in infrastructure development.
*   **MBA (Marketing):** A traditional yet dynamic specialization focusing on consumer behavior, market research, branding, and sales strategies.

## Fee Structure

The fee for the MBA programs at SCMHRD is structured to cover tuition and other academic expenses. Specific details are usually updated annually on the official website. Students should refer to the latest prospectus for the most accurate fee information.

## Admission Process

Admission to the MBA programs at SCMHRD is a multi-stage process, ensuring a comprehensive evaluation of candidates. The primary entrance examination accepted is the Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP).

### Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must hold a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university with a minimum aggregate percentage (typically 50% for general category and 45% for SC/ST candidates).

### Application Steps

1.  **SNAP Registration:** Applicants must first register for the SNAP examination through the official SNAP website.
2.  **SCMHRD Application:** Alongside SNAP registration, candidates must also apply specifically to SCMHRD and select their preferred MBA program through the institute's official admissions portal.
3.  **SNAP Exam:** Appear for the SNAP entrance examination.
4.  **Shortlisting:** Based on SNAP scores, candidates are shortlisted for the next stages of the admission process.
5.  **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates participate in the following rounds:
    *   **Written Ability Test (WAT):** Evaluates written communication skills and analytical thinking.
    *   **Group Exercise (GE):** Assesses teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving abilities.
    *   **Personal Interaction (PI):** A one-on-one interview to gauge personality, motivation, and suitability for the program.
6.  **Final Selection:** Candidates are selected based on a composite score derived from their SNAP score, WAT, GE, and PI performance.
7.  **Document Verification & Fee Payment:** Selected candidates must complete document verification and pay the first installment of fees to confirm their admission.

## Cutoff

The cutoff for admission to SCMHRD's MBA programs is determined by the SNAP score. These cutoffs can vary each year based on factors such as the number of applicants, the difficulty of the exam, and the overall performance of candidates. While specific cutoff scores are not publicly disclosed, a competitive SNAP score is essential for shortlisting.
