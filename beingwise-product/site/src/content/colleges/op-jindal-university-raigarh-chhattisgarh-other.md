OP Jindal University (OPJU) in Raigarh, Chhattisgarh, offers a diverse range of programs for students aspiring to build strong careers. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, cutoff trends, and placement opportunities.

## Courses Offered and Fees

OPJU provides various undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma programs across engineering, management, and science disciplines. The fee structure varies significantly by program and is typically updated each academic year. It's always best to check the official university website for the most current and detailed fee information.

While specific course fees are subject to change, here's a general overview of the types of programs available:

*   **Undergraduate Programs:** B.Tech, B.Sc (Hons), B.Com (Hons), BBA
*   **Postgraduate Programs:** M.Tech, MBA, M.Sc
*   **Diploma Programs:** Various specializations

## Admission Process

Admission to OPJU programs generally involves a merit-based selection process, often considering entrance exam scores and academic performance. Prospective students should:

1.  **Check Eligibility:** Ensure they meet the academic requirements for their chosen course.
2.  **Application:** Complete the online application form on the OPJU website.
3.  **Entrance Exams:** Depending on the course, candidates may need to appear for specific entrance exams (e.g., JEE Main for B.Tech, CAT/MAT/XAT for MBA).
4.  **Selection Rounds:** This might include group discussions or personal interviews for certain programs.
5.  **Merit List & Counseling:** Selected candidates are invited for counseling and document verification.

Detailed admission guidelines and important dates are published on the university's official admissions portal.

## Cutoff Information

OPJU considers scores from various national-level entrance exams for admission to its engineering and management programs. The cutoff marks can vary each year based on factors like the number of applicants, the difficulty of the exam, and the availability of seats. For B.Tech programs, JEE Main scores are commonly considered. Applicants are advised to review previous year's cutoff trends on the university website or through official admission brochures to gauge their chances.

## Hostel Facilities

OPJU provides on-campus hostel accommodation for its students, featuring various room options to suit different preferences and budgets. The hostel charges are typically per semester and depend on the type of occupancy and whether the room is air-conditioned.

Here are the approximate per-semester hostel charges:

| Type of Bed Occupancy | AC (per semester) | Non-AC (per semester) |
| :-------------------- | :---------------- | :-------------------- |
| Single Occupancy      | INR 1.2 Lakh      | INR 85,000            |
| Double Occupancy      | INR 1 Lakh        | INR 60,000            |
| Triple Occupancy      | INR 80,000        | INR 48,000            |
| Four Seater           | Not Applicable    | INR 45,000            |

**Important Notes on Accommodation:**

*   Single occupancy rooms are generally reserved for third and fourth-year students.
*   Four-seater accommodations are typically offered to students pursuing Diploma, B.Sc (Hons), and M.Sc courses.
*   Hostel fees are subject to change, and students should confirm the latest rates with the university's administration.
