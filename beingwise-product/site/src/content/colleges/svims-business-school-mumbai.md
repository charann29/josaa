## Programs Offered at SVIMS Business School

SVIMS Business School focuses on postgraduate management education. Their flagship offering is the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM).

### PGDM Specializations

The PGDM program at SVIMS allows students to specialize in key areas, with a notable focus on Sales & Marketing, preparing them for dynamic roles in the business world.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

To be considered for admission to the PGDM program at SVIMS Business School, prospective students need to meet specific academic and entrance examination requirements.

### Academic Requirements

Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. A minimum of 50% marks in their graduation is generally required, with a slightly relaxed criterion of 45% for candidates belonging to reserved categories.

### Entrance Exams

Applicants must also have appeared for a valid management entrance examination. The specific exams accepted are typically outlined by the institution during the admission cycle.
