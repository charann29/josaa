IBS Bangalore, part of the ICFAI Business School network, is a well-regarded institution for management education in Karnataka. It's known for its focused approach to business studies and aims to prepare students for the corporate world.

## Courses Offered at IBS Bangalore

IBS Bangalore primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs, alongside undergraduate options. The institution is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** This is the flagship program, designed to provide comprehensive business knowledge and practical skills.
*   **Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):** An undergraduate program for students aspiring to build a foundation in business management.

It's important to note that while IBS Bangalore is a prominent business school, it does not offer an MBA in Agri-Business Management.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to IBS Bangalore programs can be either entrance exam-based or merit-based, depending on the specific course. Here's a general overview:

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **For PGDM:** Typically requires a bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum percentage (often 50%), and valid scores from management entrance exams like IBSAT, CAT, NMAT, or GMAT.
*   **For BBA:** Requires successful completion of 10+2 with a certain minimum percentage.

### Required Documents

When applying for admission, students generally need to submit the following:

*   Class 10 mark sheet
*   Class 12 mark sheet
*   Graduation mark sheet (if applying for PGDM)
*   Scorecard of relevant entrance exams (if applicable)
*   Transfer Certificate

## Fees Structure

The fee structure for courses at IBS Bangalore is competitive and reflects the quality of education and facilities provided. For the most precise and current fee details, it's always best to check the official IBS Bangalore website.

## Campus Facilities and Faculty

IBS Bangalore offers a conducive learning environment with essential facilities and experienced faculty members.

### Infrastructure

The campus provides modern amenities to support student learning and comfort:

*   **Wi-Fi Connectivity:** Available across the campus.
*   **Library:** A well-stocked library with over 27,000 books and access to a Bloomberg Terminal Hub.
*   **Classrooms:** Equipped with audio-visual learning aids.
*   **Hostel Accommodation:** Separate hostels for boys and girls are available within a short walk (5-10 minutes) from the campus, ensuring safety and security.

### Faculty

IBS Bangalore prides itself on its highly qualified faculty. Many faculty members hold PhDs and Master's degrees, bringing a wealth of academic and industry experience to the classroom. Key academic leadership includes:

*   Prapti Paul, Dean (Academics) and ACO
*   Girish GP, Dean (Academics)
*   Sharon K Jose, Associate Dean (SDCC)
*   Radhika Ramesh, Associate Dean (Academics) & IBSAF Coordinator

IBS Bangalore aims to provide a comprehensive educational experience, combining rigorous academics with practical exposure and strong placement support.
