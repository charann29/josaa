## Courses Offered at IIET

IIET offers a variety of full-time degree programs across undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) levels, primarily in Engineering and Business & Management Studies. The college has 7 UG and 5 PG courses designed to equip students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge.

### Undergraduate Programs (B.E. / B.Tech)

Students can pursue a Bachelor of Engineering or Bachelor of Technology in several popular disciplines:

*   **Civil Engineering**
*   **Computer Science Engineering**
*   **Electrical Engineering**
*   **Electronics & Communication Engineering**
*   **Information Technology**
*   **Mechanical Engineering**
*   **Petroleum Engineering**

### Postgraduate Programs

For those looking to specialize further, IIET offers master's degrees:

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA)**
*   **Master of Engineering (M.E.) / Master of Technology (M.Tech)** with specializations in:
    *   Construction Engineering
    *   Computer Science Engineering
    *   Power Engineering

## Admission Process and Eligibility Criteria

Admission to IIET's programs is based on specific eligibility requirements. Here’s a breakdown for different course levels:

### B.E. / B.Tech Admissions

Candidates must have completed their 8th, 9th, 10th, 11th, and 12th standards from schools situated in Tamil Nadu. Special provisions exist for:

*   **Tamil Nadu Natives:** If any of the qualifying standards (8th-12th) were completed outside Tamil Nadu, an Original Nativity Certificate is required.
*   **Children of Central Government Employees:** Eligibility applies if parents or guardians (in the absence of both parents) have been continuously serving in Tamil Nadu for the past five years.
*   **Children of All India Service Tamil Nadu Cadre Officers:** A self-declaration from the parent is needed.
*   **Children of Public Sector/Government Recognized Institution Employees:** Eligibility applies if parents have worked in their organizations for the past five years, regardless of domicile.
*   **Other State Candidates:** If all qualifying standards (8th-12th) were studied in Tamil Nadu.

### MBA/PGDM Admissions

*   **Educational Qualification:** A Bachelor’s degree of at least three years duration with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for reserved categories).
*   **Degree Patterns Accepted:** 10+2+3/4/5 years pattern; 10+3 years Diploma +3 years pattern; B.E./B.Tech./B.Arch./B.Pharm.; 10+2+AMIE; or 10+3 years Diploma + AMIE.
*   **AMIE Candidates:** Must have two years of regular full-time experience in the relevant field.
*   **Ineligibility:** Candidates admitted through Lateral Entry in degree courses, or those with degrees obtained without studying 10th/12th standard or through a three-year degree program, are not eligible.
*   **Final Year Students:** Candidates appearing for their final semester/year examinations are also eligible to apply.

### M.E./M.Tech Admissions

*   **Educational Qualification:** A B.E./B.Tech. degree in Civil Engineering or Civil and Structural Engineering is required for M.E./M.Tech. in Construction Engineering. For other M.E./M.Tech programs, specific B.E./B.Tech degrees are needed as per the specialization.
*   **Minimum Marks:** Reserved category candidates require 45% in their qualifying examination.
*   **Degree Patterns Accepted:** 10+2+AMIE or 10+3 years Diploma + AMIE.
*   **AMIE Candidates:** Must have two years of regular full-time experience in the relevant field.
*   **Ineligibility:** Candidates with B.E./B.Tech. degrees obtained through Distance/Weekend mode, or those with degrees obtained without studying 10th/12th standard or through a three-year degree program, are not eligible.
*   **Final Year Students:** Candidates appearing for their final semester/year examinations are also eligible to apply.
