RIIM - Arihant Group of Institutes, located in Pune, is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. This page provides a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, entrance exam cutoffs, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered

RIIM Pune primarily focuses on postgraduate management education. The institute offers a full-time Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) and an MBA program affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU).

### PGDM Program

The PGDM is a comprehensive program designed to equip students with essential management skills. It is approved by AICTE.

### MBA Program

The MBA program at RIIM Pune is offered in affiliation with SPPU. This program also includes an Employability Program and a Management Development Programme, enhancing career readiness.

## Fees Structure

The fees for the management programs at RIIM Pune vary depending on the specific course and its format. It's important to note these are for two years.

*   **MBA (University Program) + Employability Program + Management Development Programme:** INR 6,48,000
*   **PGDM (Physical Course):** INR 4,50,000
*   **PGDM (Digital Course):** INR 6,50,000

## Admission Process

Admission to both the PGDM and MBA programs at RIIM Pune is entrance-based, followed by a selection process. Here’s a breakdown:

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission to the PGDM and MBA courses, candidates must:

*   Hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
*   Have a minimum aggregate score of 50% in their bachelor's degree.

### Entrance Exams Accepted

RIIM Pune accepts scores from various national and state-level management entrance examinations. A valid score in any of these exams is mandatory:

*   CAT
*   XAT
*   MAT
*   CMAT
*   ATMA
*   GMAT
*   NMAT
*   Any other competitive exam approved by AICTE

### Selection Rounds

After submitting a valid entrance exam score, eligible candidates must participate in the following selection rounds:

*   Written Ability Test (WAT)
*   Group Discussion (GD)
*   Personal Interview (PI)

Interested candidates can apply online through the institute’s official website.

## Seat Intake

The total combined seat intake for the MBA and PGDM programs at RIIM Pune is 600. This information is indicative and subject to change by the official sanctioning bodies.
