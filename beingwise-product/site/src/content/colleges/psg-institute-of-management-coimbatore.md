PSG Institute of Management (PSGIM) in Coimbatore is a well-regarded institution for higher education in business. It offers a range of postgraduate programs designed to equip students with essential management skills and knowledge. This guide provides a clear overview of its courses, fees, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Programs and Specializations

PSGIM primarily focuses on postgraduate management education. The flagship program is the Master of Business Administration (MBA), a full-time, two-year course. Students can choose to specialize in various areas, allowing them to tailor their education to their career aspirations.

### MBA Specializations Offered:

*   Finance
*   Human Resources
*   Marketing
*   Decision Science and Analytics
*   Waste Management & Social Entrepreneurship

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to PSGIM's programs is competitive and based on a combination of academic performance, entrance exam scores, and personal assessment. The institute considers a holistic profile of each applicant.

### Eligibility Criteria for MBA:

*   Candidates must hold a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 60% in their undergraduate studies is required.

### Selection Process:

1.  **Entrance Examination:** Applicants must appear for and achieve a valid score in one of the accepted national or state-level management entrance exams. These include CAT, MAT, ATMA, or TANCET.
2.  **Application Submission:** Candidates need to complete and submit the application form.
3.  **Shortlisting:** Based on entrance exam scores and academic records, candidates are shortlisted for the subsequent rounds.
4.  **Group Discussion (GD):** Shortlisted candidates participate in a group discussion to assess their communication and teamwork skills.
5.  **Personal Interview (PI):** A personal interview is conducted to evaluate the candidate's personality, motivation, and suitability for the program. The institute also considers factors like work experience and diversity during this stage.

## TANCET Counselling Process

For students applying through TANCET, the Directorate of Technical Education, Chennai, manages the online counselling process. This involves several stages, including a physical document verification round.

### Steps in TANCET Counselling:

1.  **Counselling Registration:** Online registration for counselling.
2.  **Publication of TANCET Rank List:** Announcement of candidate ranks.
3.  **Choice Filling:** Candidates select their preferred colleges and courses.
4.  **Publication of Tentative Allotment:** Provisional seat allocation based on choices and ranks.
5.  **Confirming Tentative Allotment:** Candidates confirm their acceptance of the allotted seat.
6.  **Publication of Provisional Allotment:** Final seat allocation.
7.  **Supplementary Counselling Round:** Additional rounds for remaining seats.
8.  **SCA to SC Counselling:** Specific counselling for Scheduled Caste (Arunthathiyar) to Scheduled Caste candidates.
