ITM Business School in Navi Mumbai, located in Kharghar, is a well-known institution for management education. It is part of the extensive ITM Group of Institutions, established in 1991, and offers a range of career-focused programs designed to prepare students for the professional world.

## Programs Offered

ITM Business School provides a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Their flagship offerings include the MBA iConnect and BBA iConnect, both of which are designed with a strong emphasis on industry relevance and practical skills.

*   **MBA iConnect:** This two-year postgraduate program is approved by AICTE & NBA and affiliated with AIU. It features a unique curriculum that includes a five-month national or international internship, industry workshops, and global immersion programs. Students can choose from multiple specializations to align with their career aspirations.
*   **BBA iConnect:** This undergraduate program also incorporates an industry-focused curriculum, aiming to equip students with essential business acumen and practical experience.

## Fees Structure

The fee structure at ITM Business School covers various components, including admission, examination, and security deposits. It's always advisable to confirm the latest figures directly with the institution, as fees can be subject to change.

*   **BBA iConnect:** The total tuition fee for the BBA program is approximately INR 11.9 Lakh.

## Admission Process

ITM Business School has a structured admission process for its programs. Prospective students are typically evaluated based on their academic record, entrance exam scores, and performance in group discussions and personal interviews. Detailed information regarding application dates, eligibility criteria, and specific entrance exams accepted can be found on the school's official website.

## Cutoff Information

Information regarding specific cutoff scores for entrance examinations is usually released during the admission cycle. Candidates aspiring for admission to ITM Business School's MBA iConnect program should aim for competitive scores in national-level management entrance exams.

## Faculty and Infrastructure

ITM Business School prides itself on offering a supportive learning environment with experienced faculty and modern facilities.

*   **Faculty:** The teaching staff comprises PhD and diploma holders, many of whom bring significant industry experience. The institution also arranges guest lectures and invites accomplished professionals, such as Mr. Amitabh Dalal (VP, Institutional Sales with UTI Mutual Fund), to share their insights and motivate students.
