The International School of Business and Media (ISB&M) in Kolkata is a well-regarded institution for management education. If you're considering a postgraduate program in business, understanding its offerings, admission process, and career prospects is key.

## Programs Offered at ISB&M Kolkata

ISB&M Kolkata primarily focuses on its Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This full-time program is designed to equip students with comprehensive business knowledge and practical skills for the corporate world.

## Admission Process for PGDM

Admission to the PGDM program at ISB&M Kolkata is a structured, multi-stage process that evaluates candidates on various parameters, including academic performance, entrance exam scores, and personal attributes.

### Application Procedure

Applications for ISB&M Kolkata typically open in September and close by January each year. Prospective students can apply either online through the institute's official website or by collecting an application form directly from the campus. The application involves filling out the form, uploading necessary documents, and paying the application fee.

### Selection Criteria and Weightage

ISB&M Kolkata assesses applicants based on a combination of factors. Here's a breakdown of the selection criteria and their respective weightage:

| Selection Parameter        | Weightage |
| :------------------------- | :-------- |
| Entrance Test Score        | 25%       |
| Academic Performance       | 25%       |
| Group Discussion & Personal Interview | 40%       |
| Work Experience            | 10%       |

### Entrance Exam Requirements

Candidates must have a valid score from one of the following management entrance examinations:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test): A score of 70 percentile or above.
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test): A score of 65 percentile or above.
*   CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)
*   GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)

Alternatively, candidates can opt for the ISB&M written competency test if they do not have the specified percentile in other exams, provided they meet certain academic prerequisites.

### Academic Prerequisites

Beyond entrance exam scores, a strong academic background is important. Applicants are generally expected to have:

*   Class 10: 75% aggregate
*   Class 12: 75% aggregate
*   Graduation: 60% aggregate or an equivalent CGPA from a recognized university.

### Group Discussion and Personal Interview (GD & PI)

Following the application and initial screening, shortlisted candidates participate in a Group Discussion and Personal Interview round, usually conducted in February. This stage is crucial, carrying a significant weightage in the overall selection process.

### Admission Confirmation

Once selected, candidates can confirm their admission by paying the required fees within the stipulated timeframe. The admission cycle typically concludes by July.

## Fees Structure

The specific fee details for the PGDM program are best confirmed directly from the official ISB&M Kolkata website or admissions office, as they can vary slightly each academic year. However, it's important to budget for tuition, living expenses, and other associated costs.

## Placements and Career Prospects

ISB&M Kolkata has a commendable track record in terms of student placements, offering significant opportunities for career growth. The institute focuses on preparing students for roles across various industries.

### Placement Highlights

*   **Highest Package:** The highest salary package offered to PGDM students has shown consistent growth, reaching INR 20.56 LPA for the 2024 batch.
*   **Average Package:** The average salary package for the 2022-24 PGDM batch stood at INR 11.4 LPA.
*   **Median Package:** The median salary for PGDM graduates, according to available data, is INR 7 LPA.

### Top Recruiting Sectors

The Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI) sector has historically been a major recruiter, accounting for a significant portion of placements (around 24%). Students find opportunities in diverse sectors, including IT, consulting, manufacturing, and retail.

## Campus Life and Infrastructure

ISB&M Kolkata provides a supportive learning environment with modern facilities. The campus infrastructure is designed to enhance the academic and residential experience for students.

Key facilities include:

*   **Auditorium:** For events, seminars, and presentations.
*   **Hostels:** Separate accommodation options for both boys and girls.
*   **Cafeteria:** Providing meals and refreshments.
*   **Labs:** Equipped for practical learning and research.
*   **Library:** A resource hub with extensive collections of books, journals, and digital materials.

Students often rate the campus life and infrastructure highly, indicating a vibrant and well-maintained environment conducive to learning and personal development.
