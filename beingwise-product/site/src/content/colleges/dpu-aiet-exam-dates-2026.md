Considering higher education in management? Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth (DPU), Pune, offers various programs through its All India Entrance Test (AIET). This page outlines the key details for the DPU AIET 2026, including important dates, available courses, fee structures, the admission process, expected cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## DPU AIET 2026: An Overview

DPU AIET is the gateway to management programs at Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth. It's an online, remotely proctored examination designed to assess candidates' aptitude for business studies. Understanding the exam structure and syllabus is crucial for aspiring students.

### Exam Pattern at a Glance

The DPU AIET is a multiple-choice question (MCQ) based examination conducted in English. Here’s a quick look at its format:

| Feature            | Details                                     |
|--------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Exam Medium        | English                                     |
| Mode of Exam       | Online Proctor Based Test                   |
| Duration           | 150 minutes                                 |
| Question Type      | Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ)             |
| Marking Scheme     | 1 mark for each correct answer              |
| Negative Marking   | No                                          |
| Sections (MBA)     | 5 (Numerical Ability, GK, English, Reasoning, Business Judgement) |
| Sections (BBA)     | 4 (Numerical Ability, GK, English, Reasoning) |
| Total Marks (MBA)  | 150                                         |
| Total Marks (BBA)  | 100                                         |

### Syllabus for MBA and BBA

The AIET syllabus covers core aptitude areas essential for management studies. While the BBA syllabus generally mirrors the MBA topics, its difficulty aligns with Class 12 standards. Key areas include:

*   **Numerical Ability:** Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Mensuration, Data Interpretation, Number System, Sets.
*   **General Knowledge:** Static GK and Current Affairs.
*   **English Language:** English Comprehension, Verbal Ability, Grammar, Vocabulary.
*   **Reasoning Ability:** Syllogism, Statement and Conclusion, Critical Reasoning, Puzzles, Visual Reasoning, Arrangements.
*   **Business Judgement (for MBA only):** This section assesses decision-making skills relevant to business scenarios.

Students who have prepared for other national-level entrance exams will find significant overlap in the DPU AIET syllabus, making preparation potentially easier.

## Admission Process for DPU AIET 2026

The application process for DPU AIET is straightforward and conducted online.

### How to Apply

1.  Visit the official DPU AIET website.
2.  Locate the “Apply Now” option and create a unique login ID.
3.  Use your new login credentials to access and complete the application form.
4.  Pay the required application fee online to finalize your submission.

### The Online Proctored Exam

The DPU AIET is an online proctored test, meaning you can take it from any suitable location. You will need a reliable desktop or laptop computer with a functioning camera and a stable, high-speed internet connection. On exam day, candidates must log in approximately one hour before the test begins for identification procedures. The exam typically runs from 11:00 AM to 12:30 PM.

**Important Tips for the Online Proctored Test:**

*   Ensure your computer and internet connection are fully functional.
*   Sit in a well-lit room with a plain background, ensuring your face is clearly visible to the camera.
*   Upload your DPU AIET admit card and a valid photo ID as instructed by the exam software.
*   Do not leave your screen or allow anyone else into the room during the exam.
*   If technical issues arise (e.g., camera or computer shutdown), reconnect immediately and contact the exam agency for support.
