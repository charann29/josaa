IIM Calcutta, located in the vibrant city of Kolkata, stands as a premier institution for management education in India. With a legacy spanning over 60 years, it's renowned for its flagship MBA program and commitment to academic excellence. This guide offers a comprehensive overview of its courses, fees, admission process, cutoffs, and placement opportunities.

## Why Choose IIM Calcutta?

IIM Calcutta is a government-established institute and holds the distinction of being the first national institution for postgraduate and research studies in management in India. It boasts international accreditation from AACSB and AMBA, affirming its global standards in management education. The institute fosters a dynamic learning environment through collaborations with national and international organizations and universities, including various seminars and workshops. It also operates an international center of excellence for management education, facilitating student exchange programs worldwide.

## Programs Offered and Fees

IIM Calcutta offers a range of postgraduate and doctoral programs. While the MBA is its most sought-after course, other specialized programs cater to diverse career aspirations.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA)

This is the flagship program, attracting top talent from across the country. Known for its rigorous curriculum and industry relevance, the MBA program at IIM Calcutta prepares students for leadership roles globally.

### PhD (Doctoral Program in Management - DPM)

For those aspiring to careers in academia or research, the PhD program offers an immersive research experience. Students enrolled in the PhD program receive a monthly stipend starting at INR 30,000, which can increase to INR 35,000. Additionally, student development grants of up to INR 65,000 are provided for the first year, and up to INR 25,000 for subsequent years. These stipends and grants are typically available for four years, with potential extensions based on research progress. Field research, if required, can receive additional financial support of up to INR 50,000.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to IIM Calcutta's programs, especially the MBA, is highly competitive. Candidates are typically evaluated based on their scores in national-level entrance exams, academic performance, work experience, and performance in subsequent interview rounds.

Specific cutoff scores for entrance exams like CAT are crucial for shortlisting candidates for the interview stage. These cutoffs vary each year based on the applicant pool and program demand.

## Campus Facilities and Infrastructure

Spread across 135 acres, the IIM Calcutta campus provides a conducive environment for learning and personal growth. The infrastructure includes:

*   **Academic Buildings:** Featuring classrooms of various sizes, conference rooms, and dedicated group-study areas.
*   **Hostel Facilities:** Separate, secure hostels are available for both male and female students, ensuring a safe living environment.
*   **Other Amenities:** The campus is designed with modern teaching infrastructure and academic buildings that are regularly updated to facilitate an optimal learning experience.

IIM Calcutta maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy against ragging, with an internal committee and a dedicated cell to address and prevent any such incidents, ensuring a secure and respectful environment for all students.

IIM Calcutta boasts an impressive track record of 100% placements for its MBA batches. The institute consistently attracts top recruiters from various sectors, offering lucrative opportunities to its graduates.
