St. Joseph's Institute of Management (SJIM) in Bangalore is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. If you're considering a career in business, understanding SJIM's offerings, admission process, and career prospects is essential.

## Courses Offered at SJIM

SJIM primarily focuses on its flagship Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This two-year, full-time course is designed to equip students with comprehensive management knowledge and practical skills.

### PGDM Program Details

The PGDM at SJIM is approved by the AICTE, ensuring its curriculum meets national standards. The total intake capacity for all programs combined at SJIM is 180 seats.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the PGDM program at SJIM is competitive and based on a combination of academic performance and entrance exam scores. Here's a breakdown of the requirements:

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the PGDM program, applicants must meet the following academic qualifications:

*   A minimum of 60% aggregate marks in Class 10, Class 12, and their undergraduate degree.
*   Undergraduate degrees can be in various disciplines, including B.E./B.Tech, B.Com, BBA, B.Sc., or B.A., from a recognized university or board.
*   Candidates who are in their final year of graduation are also welcome to apply.

### Entrance Exams and Selection

Admission is strictly entrance-based. Candidates must appear for and achieve a qualifying score in one of the following national-level management entrance exams:

*   CAT
*   MAT
*   XAT
*   GMAT
*   ATMA
*   CMAT

Following the entrance exam, shortlisted candidates will participate in a Personal Interview (PI) round and an English Language Test conducted by SJIM.

## Fees Structure

The total tuition fee for the PGDM program at St. Joseph's Institute of Management is approximately INR 930,000. It's important to note that this figure typically covers tuition and may not include other potential costs like examination fees, application fees, or other charges that can arise during the course of study.

SJIM has a strong track record when it comes to student placements, with a high percentage of students securing job offers. The institute aims to provide valuable career opportunities to its graduates.

SJIM offers a vibrant campus environment with a range of facilities designed to support student learning and overall well-being. The institute has received positive feedback from students regarding its infrastructure and campus life.

The quality of education at SJIM is supported by its experienced and qualified faculty members.
