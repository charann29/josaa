For students aiming for a top-tier management education in India, NMIMS School of Business Management (SBM) in Mumbai stands out as a highly respected institution. This guide provides a clear overview of its offerings, from academic programs and fees to the admissions process, cutoff scores, and career opportunities.

## Programs Offered at NMIMS SBM Mumbai

NMIMS SBM offers a range of postgraduate and doctoral programs designed to equip students with strong business acumen. The flagship program is the Master of Business Administration (MBA), available in various specializations.

### MBA Programs

The MBA programs at NMIMS SBM are highly sought after. Key specializations include:

*   **MBA Core:** A comprehensive program covering fundamental business disciplines.
*   **MBA Human Resources (HR):** Focuses on strategic HR management and talent development.
*   **MBA Tech:** Integrates technology and management principles, ideal for those interested in tech-driven businesses.

### Doctoral Programs (PhD)

For those interested in advanced research, NMIMS SBM offers a PhD program. This doctoral degree typically spans three to four years and is available across several research domains. The total intake for the PhD program is 170 students, distributed as follows:

| PhD Specialization             | Seat Intake |
| :----------------------------- | :---------- |
| Management                     | 20          |
| Engineering & Technology Management | 86          |
| Pharmaceutical Sciences        | 24          |
| Sciences                       | 10          |
| Law                            | 10          |
| Statistics                     | 20          |

## Fees and Admission Process

Specific fee details for each program are updated annually and are best confirmed on the official NMIMS SBM website. The admission process for MBA programs typically involves appearing for the NMAT by GMAC exam, followed by a Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) round. For PhD programs, the selection process usually includes an entrance test and an interview.

## Cutoff Scores

Admission to NMIMS SBM's MBA programs is highly competitive, with cutoff scores for the NMAT by GMAC exam varying each year based on the applicant pool and program demand. Candidates need to achieve a strong score to be considered for the subsequent selection rounds.

## Placements at NMIMS SBM Mumbai

NMIMS SBM has a strong track record of placements, attracting numerous prominent recruiters across various sectors. The institute consistently reports impressive placement statistics for its MBA graduates.

## Faculty and Infrastructure

NMIMS SBM prides itself on its experienced and qualified faculty, many of whom hold advanced degrees and have significant industry or academic backgrounds. The campus infrastructure is modern and well-equipped, featuring air-conditioned classrooms with advanced display systems, a large cafeteria, and student lounges on every floor for group activities. The Mumbai campus spans over 8 acres and includes a ten-story building with over 60 classrooms, a comprehensive library, learning resource centers, reading rooms, conference facilities, and administrative offices.
