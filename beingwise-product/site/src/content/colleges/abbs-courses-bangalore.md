Planning your higher education journey in Bangalore? Acharya Bangalore Business School (ABBS) is a well-known institution, and understanding its offerings is key. This guide breaks down everything you need to know about ABBS courses, fees, how to get in, and what to expect after graduation.

## ABBS Courses and Programs

ABBS provides a diverse range of undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) programs across various disciplines, catering to different academic interests and career aspirations. These programs are designed to be comprehensive and industry-relevant.

### Undergraduate Programs

ABBS offers several Bachelor's degrees, with a strong focus on enhancing student skills and preparing them for the professional world. All UG programs require completion of Class 12 from a recognized board.

*   **Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com):** This three-year program, affiliated with Bangalore University, comes in two specializations:
    *   **B.Com (General):** A broad-based commerce degree covering essential business principles.
    *   **B.Com (Supply Chain & Logistics):** This specialized program includes value-added certificates in Supply Chain Management and Logistics & Shipping Management, providing practical skills for a thriving industry.
    The B.Com curriculum is structured to be diversified, emphasizing skill development through components like Ability Enhancement Compulsory Courses (AECC), Discipline Specific Core (DSC) courses, Skill Enhancement Courses (SEC), Open Elective Courses (OEC), and Discipline Specific Elective (DSE) courses. Learning includes lectures, tutorials, and practical sessions (L+T+P), with assessment through Continuous Internal Evaluation (CIE) and Semester End Examinations (SEE).

*   **Bachelor of Science (B.Sc):** ABBS offers B.Sc programs, details of which are available on their official website. Eligibility typically requires completing Class 12 from a recognized board.

### Postgraduate Programs

For those looking to advance their studies, ABBS offers postgraduate options, including a Master of Business Administration (MBA).

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** This program is designed for graduates seeking leadership roles. To be eligible, candidates must hold a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university with an aggregate of 50% or above. Final-year degree students are also welcome to apply.

## Admission Process and Key Dates

Admission to ABBS is a structured process involving applications, aptitude tests, and interviews. There is no provision for direct admission.

### General Eligibility Criteria

*   **For Undergraduate Courses:** You must have successfully completed Class 12 (or PUC equivalent) from a recognized board. A minimum aggregate of 50% in Class 10 and 12 is generally required. Students with 40-50% in Class 12 may still qualify by completing an online application and paying the fee.
*   **For Postgraduate Courses:** You need a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university with an aggregate of 50% or more. Final-year students can also apply.

### Application Steps

1.  **Online Registration:** Visit the official ABBS website and register your details.
2.  **Application Submission:** Complete the application form, upload all necessary documents, and pay the application fee.
3.  **Aptitude Test:** Register for and appear in the Acharya Bangalore Business School Scholastic Aptitude Test (ABBSSAT).
4.  **Personal Interview:** Candidates who perform well in the ABBSSAT will be invited for a personal interview.
5.  **Merit List and Admission Confirmation:** ABBS will publish a merit list based on ABBSSAT performance, interview results, and academic records. Selected students must confirm their admission by making an initial deposit towards the first semester's tuition fee.

### Important Dates

ABBS admissions typically follow a consistent schedule each year:

*   **UG Applications:** Expected deadline is July 1st.
*   **PG Applications:** Expected deadline is August 30th.

## Cutoff Information

Admission to ABBS programs, particularly the MBA, involves meeting specific cutoff criteria.

*   **MBA Cutoff:** For MBA admissions in 2023, candidates needed a minimum of 50 percentile in relevant entrance exams.

## Placement Opportunities

ABBS has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with leading companies. The institution has released placement statistics for the 2024 drive, showcasing promising career opportunities for its graduates.

Here's a snapshot of the 2024 placements:

| Placement Metric | Salary (INR) |
| :--------------- | :----------- |
| Highest Package  | 16.43 LPA    |
| Average Package  | 7.56 LPA     |
| Median Package   | 6.12 LPA     |

It's worth noting that the highest, average, and median packages have shown varying trends from 2022 to 2024, indicating dynamic opportunities in the job market.

## Fees Structure

Specific fee details for each course are typically provided during the admission process or can be found on the official ABBS website. Prospective students are encouraged to check the latest fee schedule for their chosen program.
