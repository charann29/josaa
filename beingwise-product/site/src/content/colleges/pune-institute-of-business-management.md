The Pune Institute of Business Management (PIBM) is a well-known institution in Pune, offering a range of management programs. If you're considering a career in business, understanding what PIBM offers in terms of courses, fees, admissions, and career prospects is key.

## About PIBM Pune

Established in 2007, PIBM is a private management college approved by the AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education). It is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and holds accreditations from both NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) and NBA (National Board of Accreditation). PIBM is also a member of the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB).

## Programs Offered and Fees

PIBM Pune primarily focuses on postgraduate management education. The institute offers two main programs:

*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** This program has an intake capacity of 120 students.
*   **Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM):** This program has a higher intake of 360 students.

While specific fee details for each program are not provided in the source, it's important for prospective students to check the latest fee structure directly on the PIBM website.

## Admission Process

Admission to PIBM's management programs is a multi-stage process, ensuring that candidates meet both academic and aptitude requirements. Here’s a breakdown:

### Eligibility Criteria

For the MBA program, candidates must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. Additionally, a valid score in one of the accepted national-level entrance exams is mandatory.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

PIBM accepts scores from various management aptitude tests, including:

*   CAT
*   XAT
*   CMAT
*   MAT
*   MAH-CET
*   PMAT (PIBM Management Aptitude Test)

### Application Procedure

1.  **Online Application:** Visit the official PIBM Pune website and click on the 'Apply Now' section.
2.  **Registration and Form Filling:** Register yourself and accurately complete the online application form with all required details.
3.  **Application Fee:** A non-refundable application fee of INR 1,000 must be paid to finalize your application.
4.  **Shortlisting:** Based on your application and entrance exam scores, eligible candidates will be shortlisted.
5.  **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates will participate in an aptitude test, a group discussion (GD), and a personal interview (PI).

### Important Dates

For the upcoming MBA batch, the last date to submit applications is January 20th.

## Cutoff Information

While specific cutoff scores for entrance exams are not detailed, admission is competitive and depends on your performance in the accepted national-level tests and the subsequent selection rounds (aptitude test, GD, and PI). Candidates are advised to prepare thoroughly for these exams to enhance their chances of admission.

## Placements

PIBM Pune has a track record of offering decent placement opportunities to its PGDM graduates. The institute aims to provide valuable career starts for its students.

### Placement Statistics

For the 2025 batch, the average package offered during PIBM Pune placements was INR 10.3 LPA.
