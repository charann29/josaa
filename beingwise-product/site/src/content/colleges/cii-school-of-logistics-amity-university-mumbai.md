The CII School of Logistics, located within Amity University Mumbai, offers specialized education in the crucial field of logistics and supply chain management. This institution aims to equip students with the skills needed for a dynamic career in this sector. Let's explore its programs, costs, admission process, and career prospects.

## About CII School of Logistics, Amity University Mumbai

Established through a collaboration between the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) and Amity University, the CII School of Logistics focuses specifically on logistics and supply chain management education. It operates under the umbrella of Amity University, Mumbai, and holds approval from the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

## Courses Offered

The primary program at the CII School of Logistics is a Master of Business Administration (MBA) with a specialization in Logistics and Supply Chain Management. This postgraduate degree is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical skills for managing complex supply chains.

## Fees Structure

Detailed fee information for the MBA program is typically available on the official Amity University Mumbai website. It's advisable for prospective students to check the latest fee schedule directly from the university's admissions portal for the most accurate figures.

## Admission Process

Admission to the MBA program at CII School of Logistics is a multi-stage process designed to select well-rounded candidates. Here's a breakdown:

### Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must first meet the basic academic requirements for an MBA program, which typically include holding a bachelor's degree from a recognized university.

### Selection Steps

The selection process involves several components:

*   **Entrance Exam Score:** Applicants need to have a valid score from one of the recognized national-level management entrance examinations. These include CAT, MAT, GMAT, XAT, CMAT, NMAT. Alternatively, candidates can appear for the Amity Noida entrance exam.
*   **Graduation Percentage:** Academic performance at the undergraduate level is also considered.
*   **Extempore Test:** Candidates will participate in an extempore (on-the-spot speech) test to assess their communication and critical thinking skills.
*   **English Language Test:** An evaluation of English language proficiency is part of the process.
*   **Personal Interview:** A personal interview helps the admissions committee understand the candidate's motivation, aptitude, and suitability for the program.

### Application Fee

A non-refundable application fee of INR 1,500 is required when applying for admission to the MBA program. This fee can be paid online during the application submission process.
