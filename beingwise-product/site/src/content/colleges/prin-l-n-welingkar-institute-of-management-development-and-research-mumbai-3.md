Prin. L. N. Welingkar Institute of Management Development and Research, often simply called WeSchool, is a well-regarded institution for management education located near Matunga in Mumbai. This page offers a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admissions process, cutoff scores, and placement opportunities to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at WeSchool Mumbai

WeSchool Mumbai provides a diverse range of Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) programs, along with a Master of Management Studies (MMS) course. These programs are designed to cater to various career aspirations in the business world.

### Popular PGDM Specializations

The institute offers several specialized PGDM programs, including:

*   PGDM Core
*   PGDM E-Business
*   PGDM Business Design
*   PGDM Human Capital Management (HCM)
*   PGDM Retail Management
*   PGDM Rural Management
*   PGDM Media & Entertainment
*   PGDM Research and Business Analytics
*   Post Graduate Program in Foreign Trade Management (FTM)

### Master of Management Studies (MMS)

WeSchool Mumbai also offers an MMS program, which is another popular choice for postgraduate management studies.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to WeSchool's management programs is competitive and based on a combination of entrance exam scores, academic performance, and other selection criteria.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **For PGDM & MMS:** Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university with a minimum aggregate score of 50%.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

WeSchool considers scores from various national-level management entrance examinations for admission to its PGDM and MMS programs. These include:

*   CAT
*   CMAT
*   MH-CET
*   MAT
*   ATMA
*   XAT
*   GMAT

It's important to note that a CAT score is not mandatory; applicants with valid scores from any of the other accepted tests are eligible to apply.

## Cutoff Scores (Illustrative)

While cutoff scores can vary each year, understanding past trends can provide an indication. For the 2023 admissions cycle, some illustrative cutoffs for PGDM and Foreign Trade Management (FTM) programs were:

*   **MAH CET:** Round 1 cutoff for General All India category was 99.97 percentile.
*   **CAT:** An overall percentile of 85 for General All India category students.
*   **CMAT:** An overall percentile of 85.
*   **ATMA:** A percentile of 90.

## Fees Structure

Specific fee details for each program are best confirmed directly with the institute, as they can be subject to change. However, WeSchool aims to provide quality education at a competitive fee structure for its postgraduate management programs.
