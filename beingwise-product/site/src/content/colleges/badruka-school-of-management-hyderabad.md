The Badruka School of Management (BSM) in Hyderabad is a new institution built on a rich educational legacy, offering a modern approach to management education. Established in 2024, BSM focuses on preparing students for the dynamic business world through its AICTE-approved PGDM program.

## Overview of Badruka School of Management

BSM extends the 70-year heritage of the Badruka Educational Society, founded by Sri Raja Bankatlal Badruka. Located in Medchal, Telangana, this private institution aims to provide high-quality management education with a strong emphasis on industry relevance and student support. The school offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) with various specialisations, designed to equip graduates with essential business skills and knowledge.

## PGDM Program and Specialisations

BSM offers a full-time, two-year Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program. This comprehensive course is structured to cover core business principles while allowing students to specialise in areas that align with their career aspirations. The available specialisations include:

*   Finance
*   Marketing
*   Strategy
*   Innovation and Entrepreneurship
*   Data Analytics
*   Digital Technologies

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to the PGDM program at Badruka School of Management is based on a combination of academic merit and entrance exam scores. The application process is conducted online.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission, candidates must meet the following academic requirements:

*   A minimum aggregate of 60% in Class 12.
*   A minimum aggregate of 55% in their undergraduate degree or postgraduate degree.

### Entrance Exams Accepted

Applicants must also have valid scores from any of the following management entrance examinations:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   MAT (Management Aptitude Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)

### Application Steps

1.  **Online Application:** Register and complete the application form on the official BSM website.
2.  **Document Submission:** Upload all required academic and other relevant documents.
3.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are shortlisted based on their academic record and entrance exam scores.
4.  **Personal Interview:** Shortlisted candidates will participate in a personal interview.
5.  **Final Offer:** Admission offers are extended based on the overall performance in the selection process, followed by fee payment.

## Fees Structure

The total expenses for the two-year PGDM program at Badruka School of Management include tuition fees and hostel charges. Prospective students should budget for these costs, along with other recurring personal expenses.

*Note: These figures represent the total fees for the entire two-year duration of the program. Additional charges may apply, which will be communicated by the institution.*
