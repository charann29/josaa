The SCMS Cochin School of Business, located in Kochi, is a well-regarded institution for management education. It provides a stimulating environment for students aiming for careers in business, with a strong focus on practical learning and industry connections.

## Courses Offered at SCMS Cochin School of Business

SCMS Cochin offers a variety of postgraduate programs designed to equip students with essential management skills. The flagship program is the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM), which is approved by AICTE. This program leads to a dual degree and dual specialization, culminating in both a PGDM and an MBA.

For those interested in advanced research, the school also offers a Fellow Programme in Management (FPM).

## Fees Structure

Specific fee details for each program are best found on the official SCMS Cochin School of Business website, as these can be updated periodically. Generally, the fee structure for the PGDM program covers tuition, study materials, and access to campus facilities. The FPM program also has its own fee structure, which includes a fee for research abstract submission.

## Admission Process

Admission to SCMS Cochin School of Business is a structured process, ensuring that deserving candidates are selected for its competitive programs.

### PGDM Admission

For the PGDM program, admission is primarily entrance-based. Candidates must have a graduate degree with at least 50% aggregate marks. The school accepts scores from various national and state-level management entrance exams, including:

*   CAT
*   XAT
*   CMAT
*   MAT
*   ATMA
*   KMAT

Following the entrance exam, shortlisted candidates participate in a Group Discussion (GD) and a Personal Interview (PI) round. The final selection considers academic records, work experience (if any), extracurricular activities, and performance in the GD and PI.

### FPM Admission

Admission to the FPM program is more research-oriented. Candidates are evaluated based on their academic qualifications and any relevant work experience. A crucial part of the application is submitting a research abstract of approximately 5,000 words on their chosen area of interest. Shortlisted candidates then present their research proposal to a selection committee and undergo a personal interview.

## Cutoff Information

The cutoff percentiles for PGDM admission at SCMS Cochin School of Business can vary each year. This fluctuation depends on factors such as the total number of applications received, the overall performance of applicants in the entrance exams, and the academic profiles of the candidate pool. While a minimum of 50% in graduation is required, achieving a strong percentile in one of the accepted entrance exams significantly improves admission chances. For the most current cutoff figures, it is advisable to check the institution's official admissions portal.

The school boasts a team of 100 dedicated and experienced full-time faculty members who are well-qualified professionals in their respective fields. Additionally, SCMS Cochin invites guest faculty from the industry to share their practical insights and expertise, enriching the learning experience. These guest lecturers often include senior leaders from multinational companies and CFOs of prominent organizations.
