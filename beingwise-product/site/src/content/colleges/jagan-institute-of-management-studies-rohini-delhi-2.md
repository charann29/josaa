Jagan Institute of Management Studies (JIMS), located in Rohini, Delhi, is a well-regarded institution offering postgraduate management programs. Established in 1993, JIMS aims to provide quality education to aspiring business professionals.

## Programs Offered

JIMS Rohini primarily focuses on its Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) programs. These two-year, full-time courses are designed to be equivalent to an MBA, providing students with comprehensive management knowledge and skills.

### PGDM Specializations

Students can choose from various specializations within the PGDM program to align with their career aspirations. While the source does not explicitly list all specializations, common areas in management education often include Finance, Marketing, Human Resources, and Operations.

## Admission Process

Admission to the PGDM programs at JIMS Rohini is a multi-stage process, focusing on both academic scores and personal aptitude.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission, candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university. There are no specific restrictions on the stream of study for the undergraduate degree.

### Entrance Exams

JIMS Rohini accepts scores from several national-level management entrance examinations. Applicants must appear for one of the following:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)
*   CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)
*   ATMA (AIMS Test for Management Admissions)

It is important to note that a strong score in any of these exams is crucial for progressing in the admission process.

### Selection Rounds

Following the entrance exam, shortlisted candidates will participate in further selection rounds conducted by the institute. These typically include:

*   **Written Test:** An assessment to evaluate analytical and communication skills.
*   **Group Discussion (GD):** A forum to assess teamwork, communication, and leadership qualities.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** An interaction to understand the candidate's motivation, career goals, and suitability for the program.

### Application Procedure

Interested candidates should visit the official JIMS Rohini website to access the online application form and detailed instructions. It is advisable to apply well before the deadline.

### Required Documents for Application

When filling out the admission form, applicants should be prepared to submit digital copies of the following documents:

*   Class 10th Marksheet
*   Class 12th Marksheet
*   Graduation Marksheets (all years/semesters)
*   Scorecard of the chosen entrance exam (CAT/XAT/GMAT/CMAT/ATMA)
*   Supporting documents for any work experience (if applicable)
*   Certificates or documents related to extracurricular activities
