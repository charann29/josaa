The All India Management Association (AIMA) in Delhi offers a range of management programs designed to boost your career. This guide provides a clear overview of their courses, fees, admission process, and career prospects to help you make an informed decision.

## Programs Offered at AIMA

AIMA provides various postgraduate and certificate programs in management. These include full-time and part-time options, catering to different student needs and career stages. Their offerings span several key areas within business and management.

### Popular Specializations

Students at AIMA can choose from a diverse set of specializations, allowing them to focus on specific industry demands. Some of the prominent areas include:

*   **MBA/PGDM:** Information Technology & Systems
*   **Certificate Programs:** Finance, International Business, Digital Marketing, Business Analytics, Human Resources, Sales & Marketing, Retail, Operations, Financial Modelling, Entrepreneurship, Public Policy, Cyber Security
*   **PG Diploma Programs:** Business Analytics, Digital Marketing, Retail

AIMA also offers the flexibility of dual specialization within its PGDM program. Students can opt for an additional specialization and complete it within five years, though an extra fee applies for the additional module.

## Admission Process

Admission to AIMA's postgraduate and certificate programs is primarily entrance-based. For MBA programs, candidates typically need to clear the CAT exam. After meeting the eligibility criteria, applicants can apply through the official AIMA website or visit the campus for direct admission under the management quota. It's always advisable to check the specific eligibility requirements for your chosen program on their website before applying.
