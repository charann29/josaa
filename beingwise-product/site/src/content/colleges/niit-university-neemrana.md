NIIT University, located in Neemrana, Rajasthan, is known for its technology-focused programs and industry-aligned curriculum. This guide offers a clear overview of its courses, fee structure, admission process, selection criteria, and placement opportunities.

## Courses Offered

NIIT University provides a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. These include popular choices like B.Tech, BBA, and MBA, alongside specialized offerings.

## Admission Process

Admissions at NIIT University are typically based on entrance examination scores, either from national-level tests or the university's own assessment. The university considers scores from exams such as CUET, NUAT (NIIT University Admission Test), SAT, and IBSAT, among others. The application portal is generally open online, and prospective students can apply for their preferred courses through the official university website.

### MBA Admission

For MBA programs, NIIT University offers two distinct options:

*   **iMBA:** This integrated MBA program requires candidates to have completed Class 12 with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks, with English as a compulsory subject. Selection is based on the NUAT.
*   **MBA (Information Systems and Data Engineering) with Coforge Ltd.:** This specialized MBA requires a B.Tech degree with at least 55% aggregate marks, coupled with a minimum of 18 months of professional work experience post-graduation. Admission for this program is merit-based.

### BBA Program Qualities

For students aspiring to join the BBA program, NIIT University looks for individuals who possess:

*   A strong aptitude and desire to undertake an undergraduate program that will equip them with essential skills for roles in the evolving knowledge economy.
*   Critical thinking abilities and a solution-oriented mindset.
*   Effective communication skills.

## Fees Structure

Specific fee details vary by program and can be found on the official NIIT University website. It is advisable to check the latest fee schedule directly from the university's admissions section.

## Cutoff Information

While specific cut-off percentiles can fluctuate each year based on applicant pool and program demand, admission to NIIT University relies on competitive scores from accepted entrance exams. Students are encouraged to perform well in these examinations to enhance their chances of selection.

## Placements

NIIT University has a commendable placement record, with students securing positions in various reputable companies. The university focuses on preparing students for industry roles, leading to strong outcomes.

Leading companies such as Airtel, Amazon, Cisco, HCL, IBM, TCS, and Tech Mahindra are among the top recruiters at NIIT University.
