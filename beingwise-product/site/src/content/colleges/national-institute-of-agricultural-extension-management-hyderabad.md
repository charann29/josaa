The National Institute of Agricultural Extension Management (MANAGE), located in Hyderabad, is a prominent institution dedicated to agricultural business and management. It offers specialized programs designed to equip students with the skills needed for careers in the agricultural sector.

## Programs Offered at MANAGE

MANAGE focuses on specific areas within business and management, particularly those related to agriculture. The institute offers postgraduate and diploma courses that are well-regarded in the industry.

### Postgraduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) - Agri-Business Management

This is the flagship program, approved by AICTE, designed for graduates aspiring to leadership roles in agri-business. It covers various aspects of agricultural management, food processing, rural marketing, and related fields.

### Other Diploma Programs

MANAGE also provides undergraduate diploma courses. These programs are tailored for students interested in specific technical or operational roles within agriculture and related engineering fields.

**Specializations:**

*   Agriculture & Food Business
*   Agriculture & Farm Engineering

**Total Intake Capacity:** The institute has an approved intake capacity of up to 100 students across its various programs.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to MANAGE's programs, especially the PGDM, is competitive. Candidates typically need a bachelor's degree in agriculture or allied subjects, or a relevant discipline, with a minimum percentage as specified by the institute. Selection often involves national-level entrance exam scores, followed by group discussions and personal interviews.

### Application Dates

Prospective students should regularly check the official MANAGE website for the latest updates on application deadlines, entrance exam requirements, and other critical dates.

## Course Fees

While specific fee structures can change, the total fees for the PGDM program at MANAGE are generally around INR 9,00,000. This amount typically covers tuition and other academic charges for the entire duration of the course. For detailed and up-to-date fee information for all programs, it is best to consult the institute's official admissions brochure or website.
