XIM University, located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, is a well-regarded institution known for its diverse academic programs and focus on holistic development. It offers a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral courses across various disciplines, attracting students from all over India.

## Courses and Fees at XIM University

XIM University provides a comprehensive selection of programs, with varying fee structures. It's always advisable to check the official university website for the most current fee details, as these can change.

### Undergraduate Programs

*   **B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering (CSE):** This program admits students based on entrance exam scores. The curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in computer science, preparing students for the evolving tech industry.
*   **B.A. LL.B (Hons):** A five-year integrated course that offers a robust legal education. Admission requires an entrance test, and the program aims to equip students with essential legal knowledge and skills.
*   **B.Sc. Programs:** XIM University offers various Bachelor of Science courses. These are three-year programs structured into six semesters, providing a systematic and in-depth exposure to scientific curricula.
*   **B.A. Programs:** Similar to the B.Sc. offerings, the Bachelor of Arts programs are three-year courses divided into six semesters, designed to immerse students in a comprehensive humanities curriculum.

### Postgraduate Programs

*   **MBA (Master of Business Administration):** The university offers specialized MBA programs, including MBA in Business Management (BM), MBA in Human Resource Management (HRM), and MBA in Rural Management (RM). These are semester-based programs.

## Admission Process and Cutoffs

Admission to XIM University programs typically involves entrance examinations followed by other selection rounds. Specific cutoff scores for entrance exams like XAT, CAT, GMAT, or the university's own tests (like X-GMAT for MBA and specific tests for UG programs) play a crucial role.

For undergraduate courses like B.A. LL.B (Hons), admission is through a dedicated entrance test. For B.Tech (CSE), entrance exam scores are the primary criteria. It's important for prospective students to apply through the university's official admission portal within the specified deadlines. Admission is confirmed only after fulfilling eligibility criteria and submitting the required fees.

## Placements at XIM University

XIM University has a strong track record when it comes to student placements, particularly for its management programs. The university actively facilitates connections between students and potential employers.

For the MBA Business Management batch of 2022-24, 101 companies participated in the placement drive. The MBA Human Resource Management placements for 2025 saw participation from 69 companies. The university also attracts new recruiters each year, indicating growing industry recognition.

## Faculty and Campus Facilities

XIM University prides itself on its experienced and knowledgeable faculty members. They are known for their commitment to providing in-depth assistance and instilling core values in students, reflected in positive student feedback regarding their expertise.

The university offers excellent residential facilities with separate hostels for men and women, catering to doctoral, postgraduate, executive, and bachelor degree programs. These residences are well-equipped with modern amenities, including 24-hour Wi-Fi, printing and copying services, water coolers, geysers, washing machines, televisions, telephones, and indoor games like carrom and table tennis. Additionally, the residences house a multi-purpose hall, a cooperative shop, and book clubs, enhancing the student living experience.
