# CUET UG 2026: City Intimation Slip, Exam Dates, Admit Card, Syllabus, Expected Cutoff

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET UG) is a crucial exam for students aspiring to join undergraduate programs in central and participating state universities across India. Administered by the National Testing Agency (NTA), CUET UG simplifies the admission process by providing a single window for various courses.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Exam Dates:** CUET UG 2026 is scheduled to be held between May 11 and May 31, 2026, in a Computer Based Test (CBT) format.
*   **City Intimation Slip:** This preliminary document, expected around April 30, 2026, informs candidates about their allotted exam city, date, and slot timings, typically released about 10 days before the exam.
*   **Admit Card:** The official admit card will be available for download 2-3 days before your specific exam date, accessible via the NTA CUET portal using your application number and password.
*   **Correction Window:** The NTA recently opened a photo correction window until April 10, 2026, for candidates with mismatches between their photo and identity proof.

## CUET UG 2026 City Intimation Slip

The CUET UG 2026 advance city intimation slip is an important pre-exam document that helps candidates prepare their travel plans. This slip, generally released in phases approximately ten days before the examination dates, will tentatively be available by April 30, 2026. It specifies your allotted examination city, the date of your selected subjects, and their corresponding slot timings. It's important to remember that this is *not* your admit card and is not required at the exam centre itself.

### How to Download Your Advance Intimation Slip

To access your CUET UG 2026 advance city intimation slip, follow these simple steps:

1.  Visit the official NTA CUET website.
2.  Look for the link titled 'CUET UG Advance City Slip 2026 Download'.
3.  Enter your CUET UG 2026 application number and password.
4.  Your intimation slip will appear on the screen in PDF format.
5.  Carefully check all the details mentioned.

## CUET UG 2026 Exam Dates and Schedule

The NTA will conduct the CUET UG 2026 examination from May 11 to May 31, 2026. This period covers all subjects and the General Test. The specific datesheet PDF, detailing the schedule for individual subjects and slots, is expected to be released soon on the official NTA CUET website. Candidates should also look out for their personalized schedules once the city intimation slips are out.

## CUET UG 2026 Admit Card

The official CUET UG 2026 admit card is a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall. It will be issued by the NTA approximately 2-3 days before your scheduled exam date. To download it, you will need your application number and password. The admit card will contain crucial details such as your exam centre address, reporting time, and specific instructions for the exam day. Ensure you download it, check all details, and carry a printout to the centre.

## CUET UG 2026 Syllabus and Exam Pattern

The CUET UG 2026 exam is conducted in 13 languages, offering flexibility for students. It includes a General Test and 23 domain-specific subjects. The syllabus for domain subjects is generally based on the Class 12 NCERT curriculum. The General Test assesses general knowledge, current affairs, numerical ability, logical reasoning, and quantitative reasoning. The exam is entirely in CBT mode.

### Subject Categories

*   **Languages:** 13 options available.
*   **Domain Subjects:** 23 subjects covering various disciplines like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, History, Political Science, Economics, etc.
*   **General Test:** Compulsory for many courses and universities, evaluating general aptitude.

## Expected Cutoff and Participating Universities

CUET UG scores are utilized by numerous prominent universities, including Delhi University, Banaras Hindu University, Allahabad University, Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University, and many others, to generate their admission lists, merit lists, or cutoff scores. The cutoff for admission to various courses will vary significantly based on the university, the program chosen, the number of applicants, and the overall performance of candidates in the exam. Candidates should refer to the official university websites post-results for specific cutoff details.

Stay updated with official announcements from NTA and the respective universities for the latest information.
