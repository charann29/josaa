# Best Law Colleges in Bhubaneswar: Fees, Admission 2026, Courses, Placements

Bhubaneswar, the capital city of Odisha, offers a variety of reputable law colleges providing diverse programs such as integrated LLB, 3-year LLB, LLM, and various certificate courses. These institutions include both government-funded and private universities, catering to a wide range of academic aspirations.

## Key takeaways

*   Bhubaneswar boasts over 10 law colleges, including prominent names like KIIT School of Law, Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA) University, and Utkal University.
*   Admission to these colleges is primarily based on Class 12 academic merit or scores from national-level entrance examinations like CLAT.
*   Annual tuition fees for law programs in Bhubaneswar generally range from INR 1 lakh to over INR 15 lakh, depending on the institution and course.
*   Many colleges offer excellent infrastructure, including modern moot courts, and focus on practical legal training.

## Eligibility Criteria for Law Programs

To secure admission to a law college in Bhubaneswar, candidates must meet specific academic requirements. While these can vary slightly by institution, the general criteria are as follows:

*   **For 3-year LLB programs:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university with an aggregate score of typically 45% or higher.
*   **For integrated Law courses (e.g., BA LLB, BBA LLB):** Candidates must have successfully completed their Class 12 (or equivalent) examination from a recognized board, usually with a minimum aggregate of 45% marks.

Admission can be granted based on academic merit, performance in national-level entrance exams like CLAT, or specific state-level tests such as CPET for certain institutions.

## Accepted Entrance Examinations

For many top law colleges in Bhubaneswar, admission to integrated and postgraduate law programs relies on standardized entrance examinations. The most widely accepted exam is:

*   **CLAT (Common Law Admission Test):** This national-level entrance examination is crucial for admission to many undergraduate and postgraduate law programs across India, including those in Bhubaneswar. Information regarding the CLAT 2026 schedule and cutoff trends can be found on the official CLAT consortium website.

Some institutions might also consider scores from other university-specific entrance tests or state-level examinations.

## Leading Private Law Colleges in Bhubaneswar

Bhubaneswar is home to several highly-regarded private law colleges known for their modern facilities, strong faculty, and placement opportunities. Institutions like KIIT School of Law and SOA University's National Institute of Law are often ranked among the top law colleges nationally. These private colleges typically offer excellent infrastructure, including advanced moot courtrooms, extensive libraries, and dedicated career services to prepare students for the legal profession.

## Renowned Government Law Colleges in Bhubaneswar

Government-funded institutions also play a significant role in legal education in Bhubaneswar. Utkal University's University Law College, for instance, is a well-established institution offering quality legal education at a generally more affordable fee structure. These colleges often have a long-standing reputation and contribute significantly to legal research and public service.
