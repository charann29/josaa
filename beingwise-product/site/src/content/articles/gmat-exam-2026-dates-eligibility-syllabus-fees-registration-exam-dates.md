# GMAT Exam 2026: Dates, Eligibility, Syllabus, Fees, Registration, Exam Dates, Scores

The Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT) is a globally recognized standardized exam for admission to graduate-level business programs. Conducted by GMAC, it assesses essential skills like quantitative reasoning, verbal comprehension, and data analysis, providing business schools with a comprehensive view of an applicant's readiness for advanced study.

## Key Takeaways

*   The GMAT is a 2-hour and 15-minute computer-adaptive test with multiple-choice questions across three sections: Quantitative Reasoning, Verbal Reasoning, and Data Insights.
*   Scores are accepted by over 2,400 business schools worldwide for more than 7,700 programs, including MBA, MIM, and other postgraduate business courses.
*   The exam fee is USD 275 for test center examinations and USD 300 for online home-based tests.
*   Registration slots are available year-round, typically opening between six months and 24 hours before the desired test date.

## Understanding the GMAT

The GMAT is designed to evaluate a candidate's abilities in logical reasoning, problem-solving, critical thinking, and data interpretation – skills crucial for success in graduate business education. While it doesn't require prior advanced business knowledge, a strong performance indicates a solid foundation for the rigors of a postgraduate business curriculum. Many business schools also consider GMAT scores for scholarship opportunities.

The current version of the GMAT is its 11th edition, developed collaboratively by GMAC and leading business schools to ensure its relevance and effectiveness in identifying top talent.

## GMAT 2026 Eligibility Criteria

There are no specific age limits or educational prerequisites set by GMAC for taking the GMAT. However, candidates must meet the individual academic requirements of the business schools they are applying to. Generally, applicants are expected to hold a bachelor's degree or its equivalent from a recognized institution. It is always advisable to check the specific admission criteria of your target programs.

## GMAT 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The GMAT exam features three distinct sections, each testing a different set of skills. The test is computer-adaptive, meaning the difficulty of subsequent questions adjusts based on your performance on previous ones.

| Section              | Number of Questions | Duration    | Skills Assessed                               |
| :------------------- | :------------------ | :---------- | :-------------------------------------------- |
| Quantitative Reasoning | 21                  | 45 minutes  | Problem-solving, Data Sufficiency             |
| Verbal Reasoning     | 23                  | 45 minutes  | Reading Comprehension, Critical Reasoning, Sentence Correction |
| Data Insights        | 20                  | 45 minutes  | Data Interpretation, Multi-source Reasoning, Graphics Interpretation, Two-part Analysis, Table Analysis |

Candidates are allowed one optional 10-minute break during the exam. The total score ranges from 205 to 805, reflecting performance across all sections.

## GMAT 2026 Fees and Registration

The fee for the GMAT exam varies slightly based on the mode of examination:

*   **Test Centre Exam:** USD 275 (approximately INR 25,022, plus taxes)
*   **Online Home Exam:** USD 300 (approximately INR 27,300, plus taxes)

Registration for the GMAT is an ongoing process, with slots available throughout the year. Candidates can book their exam slot on the official GMAT website, typically between six months and 24 hours prior to their preferred date. It is recommended to register well in advance to secure your desired date and location.

## GMAT Scores and Validity

After completing the GMAT, official scores are usually released within 3-5 business days. These scores are valid for five years from the date of the exam. Candidates can retake the GMAT up to five times within a rolling 12-month period and a maximum of eight times in their lifetime. This flexibility allows test-takers to improve their scores if needed.

For Indian students, GMAC offers dedicated support, and contact details are available on the official GMAT portal for any queries related to registration or the exam.

Success in the GMAT can be a significant step towards achieving your postgraduate business education goals, both in India and internationally.
