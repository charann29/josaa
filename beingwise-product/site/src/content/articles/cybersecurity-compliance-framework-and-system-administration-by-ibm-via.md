# Cybersecurity Compliance Framework & System Administration by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In our increasingly connected world, where digital threats are constant, understanding cybersecurity is crucial for individuals and organisations alike. This program, developed by IBM and offered through Coursera, provides foundational knowledge in cybersecurity compliance frameworks and system administration.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn about industry standards and key cybersecurity compliance principles.
*   Understand essential commands for user and server security administration.
*   Gain insights into endpoint security, patch management, encryption, and cryptography.
*   No prior experience is necessary, making it accessible for beginners.

## Program Overview

Cybercrimes are a significant concern in today's data-driven landscape, with offenders operating across borders and online interfaces. For companies handling sensitive information, robust cybersecurity measures are not just beneficial, but essential. This certification aims to equip learners with the necessary background to navigate industry standards and critical cybersecurity compliance requirements.

The knowledge gained from this program is valuable for various roles within an organisation. Participants will develop a strong understanding of basic commands for user and server administration related to security. The course also thoroughly covers endpoint security and patch management, alongside in-depth skills in encryption and cryptography to understand their impact on software development.

This training is ideal for anyone looking to acquire a fundamental understanding of compliance, security frameworks, endpoint management, cryptography, or encryption.

## What You Will Learn

This comprehensive program delivers authentic and extensive education on cybersecurity compliances directly to your computer, complete with practical exercises. Upon completion, you will be well-versed in several key fundamentals:

*   Distinguish between endpoint protection and detection strategies.
*   Learn how user management is implemented for both Linux and Windows operating systems.
*   Identify common security vulnerabilities in Windows and Linux environments.
*   Understand typical pitfalls organisations face with cryptography during product development.
*   Gain an overview of the roles, functions, and designs involved in user and server administration.
*   Grasp the concepts of endpoint protection and patching as they relate to client system administration.
*   Acquire a complete understanding of encryption principles.

## Course Structure and Duration

This self-paced program is delivered through a combination of video lectures and text-based materials. It is designed to be completed in approximately 11 hours, offering flexible deadlines to suit your schedule. The learning experience includes course videos, readings, assignments, and graded quizzes to reinforce your understanding.

## Fees and Certification

While the course content is offered by IBM, the certification is provided by Coursera. The program includes graded assignments that contribute to your final assessment. To earn the shareable certificate, you must meet specific criteria:

*   **Identity Verification:** Your name and identity must be verified through your registered account.
*   **Passing Grade:** Achieve a passing grade in all graded assignments. If the course is cumulatively graded, you must cross the overall passing threshold.
*   **Certification Fee:** Pay the certification fee after a seven-day period, unless you have been granted financial aid.

The certification fee structure for this program is as follows:

| :-------------------------------- | :----------------- |
| Introduction to Git and GitHub (audit only) | Free               |
| Introduction to Git and GitHub - 1 month | Rs. 1,683/-        |

This program offers a valuable opportunity to build essential cybersecurity skills, enhancing your professional profile in an increasingly digital world.
