# Technical Analysis Series by PW Skills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to understand the dynamics of financial markets, the Technical Analysis Series by PW Skills offers a valuable learning opportunity. This program is designed to equip aspiring traders and analysts with the essential skills to interpret market movements and make strategic trading choices.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a self-paced, online certification course delivered through video and text, available in both English and Hindi.
*   The program spans three months and is completely free of charge, making it accessible to a wide audience.
*   It caters to beginners and those with no prior experience in trading or technical analysis.
*   Upon completion, learners can explore roles like Trader Analysts, potentially seeing significant salary improvements.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The Technical Analysis Series is a comprehensive program focused on empowering individuals with the knowledge to make informed trading decisions. It delves into the world of technical analysis, teaching participants how to identify patterns and trends within market behavior. The course content is delivered through recorded videos and text, allowing learners the flexibility to study at their own pace.

The curriculum covers fundamental concepts such as an introduction to technical analysis, understanding market trends, identifying support and resistance levels, charting basics, candlestick patterns, and key technical indicators like Moving Averages. The program is specifically designed to be beginner-friendly, requiring no previous experience in the field.

## Who Can Benefit?

This course is particularly well-suited for individuals currently working in or aspiring to roles such as:

*   Business Analysts
*   Financial Analysts
*   Market Research Analysts
*   Marketing Analysts

The skills gained from this series are highly relevant for anyone looking to enhance their understanding of market dynamics and apply analytical techniques to financial data.

## Program Offerings and Structure

The Technical Analysis Series is delivered entirely online, providing a flexible learning environment. Key aspects of the program include:

*   **Practical Projects:** Opportunities to apply learned concepts in real-world scenarios.
*   **Mentorship:** Guidance from experienced professionals.
*   **Networking:** Chances to connect with peers and industry experts.
*   **Feedback:** Constructive input on your progress.
*   **Notes:** Supplementary study materials.
*   **Doubt Solving:** Support for clarifying complex topics.

### Syllabus Overview

The course is structured into several modules, guiding learners from foundational concepts to more advanced analytical tools:

*   **Module 1: Introduction to Technical Analysis**
    *   Defining technical analysis and its distinction from fundamental analysis.
    *   Understanding its importance, disadvantages, and different types of traders.
    *   Setting up a trading platform (e.g., Zerodha Kite).
*   **Module 2: Understanding Trends, Support and Resistance**
    *   Exploring uptrends, downtrends, and sideways trends.
    *   Learning about trend lines, support and resistance levels, and the importance of trend confirmation and timeframes.
    *   Practical explanations using a trading console.
*   **Module 3: Charting Basics and Patterns**
    *   Understanding various types of stock charts and how to read them.
    *   Introduction to chart patterns, including their importance and different types (bearish and bullish).
    *   Basic single candlestick patterns.
*   **Module 4: Candlesticks – Single Candlestick Patterns**
    *   Detailed look into what candlesticks are, their importance, and history.
    *   Anatomy of a basic candlestick.
    *   Specific single candlestick patterns like Marubuzo and Spinning Tops.
