# Eruditus Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Eruditus Courses

Eruditus collaborates with top Indian and international institutions to offer a range of online programs designed for working professionals and those looking to upskill. These courses cover various in-demand fields, providing practical knowledge and certifications.

## Key takeaways

*   Eruditus partners with premier institutions like IIM Kozhikode and TAPMI Manipal to deliver specialized online programs.
*   Courses often blend live online sessions, projects, and sometimes on-campus components for a comprehensive learning experience.
*   Programs focus on highly relevant skills such as business analytics, digital marketing, and data science.
*   Certificates are awarded upon successful completion, enhancing professional credentials.

## Advanced Management Programme in Business Analytics (IIM Kozhikode)

This advanced-level program, offered in collaboration with the Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode, is tailored for individuals seeking to understand and leverage business analytics for strategic growth and improved decision-making. The curriculum is designed to provide deep insights into how analytics can drive business value.

*   **Duration:** 12 Months
*   **Format:** Online live sessions (typically 3 hours on Sundays), projects, and an on-campus component.
*   **Faculty:** Led by distinguished academicians from IIM Kozhikode, including Professor Soumya Roy (Quantitative Methods & Operations Management) and Professor Anirban Ghata (Economics).
*   **Key Skills:** Decision-making, business analytics knowledge, business management.
*   **Fee:** ₹2,94,000

Participants gain practical experience through projects and benefit from direct engagement with faculty. A program completion certificate is issued by IIM Kozhikode upon successful fulfillment of the course requirements.

## Post Graduate Certificate in Digital Marketing (TAPMI Manipal)

Recognizing the critical shift of consumer presence online, this program from TAPMI Manipal focuses on equipping professionals and entrepreneurs with cutting-edge digital marketing skills. It emphasizes the integration of digital marketing with analytics to uncover consumer trends and maximize brand impact.

*   **Duration:** 12 Months
*   **Format:** Live online classes.
*   **Target Audience:** Professionals in sales and marketing, and entrepreneurs.
*   **Key Skills:** Social media proficiency, advanced marketing strategies.
*   **Fee:** ₹1,35,000

The course explores how digital spends often yield superior returns compared to traditional marketing efforts, making this certificate highly valuable for those looking to expand brand awareness and drive revenue growth.

## Post Graduate Certificate in Data Science (TAPMI Manipal)

This program, also from TAPMI Manipal, addresses the global demand for skilled data science professionals capable of making informed business decisions. It aims to bridge the gap created by a shortage of qualified data analytics experts by providing comprehensive training.

*   **Duration:** Not specified in the provided information, but typically similar to other PG Certificate programs.
*   **Format:** Emphasizes practical learning through real-world case studies and capstone projects.
*   **Core Concepts:** Programming languages like Python and R, RDBMS for data warehousing, statistical techniques.
*   **Key Skills:** Data interpretation, predictive modeling, business decision support.

The curriculum is structured to provide candidates with a thorough understanding of statistical techniques and practical business applications, ensuring they are well-prepared for challenges in the data science field.

These programs offer a robust pathway for professional development, combining academic rigor with practical industry relevance.
