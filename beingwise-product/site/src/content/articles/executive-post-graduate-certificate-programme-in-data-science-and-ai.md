# Executive Post Graduate Certificate Programme in Data Science & AI by upGrad IIIT Bangalore: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For professionals aspiring to excel in the dynamic fields of data science and artificial intelligence, the Executive Post Graduate Certificate Programme in Data Science & AI, offered in collaboration with IIIT Bangalore, provides a robust pathway to career advancement.

## Key takeaways

*   This six-month online programme is designed for working professionals seeking to enhance their expertise in data science and AI.
*   The curriculum covers essential topics like Python, SQL, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, and Big Data.
*   Participants will gain practical skills through case studies and capstone projects, guided by industry experts.
*   A Bachelor's or Master's degree with at least 50% marks is required for eligibility.

## Programme Overview and Structure

This certificate programme aims to equip participants with a comprehensive understanding of core concepts and practical skills in data science and artificial intelligence. The curriculum focuses on preparing learners to tackle complex industry challenges by providing a deep dive into various essential tools and methodologies.

The programme is delivered through a flexible online learning format, combining self-study modules with virtual classroom sessions. The content is presented via video and text-based resources, allowing working professionals to manage their learning alongside their careers effectively.

## What You Will Learn

The programme's syllabus is meticulously designed to build a strong foundation and advanced skills across several critical areas:

*   **Advanced Math & Programming:** This module covers conditional probability, probability distributions, advanced linear algebra, multivariate calculus, GenAI for coding, object-oriented programming, Python data science libraries, database design, SQL querying, introduction to NoSQL databases, and version control.
*   **Data Analysis & Exploration:** Learn data analysis with Python, exploratory data analysis, inferential statistics, and hypothesis testing.
*   **Cloud Computing & Big Data:** Gain expertise in cloud computing using platforms like AWS, GCP, or Microsoft Azure, and perform big data analysis with PySpark.
*   **Foundations of ML:** Explore machine learning paradigms, linear regression, and logistic regression.
*   **Deep Learning & NLP:** Understand deep learning fundamentals, convolutional neural networks, and recurrent neural networks.

Through hands-on projects and case studies, participants will develop the ability to analyze complex datasets, build predictive models, and derive actionable insights relevant to real-world scenarios.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Executive Post Graduate Certificate Programme in Data Science & AI, candidates must hold a Bachelor's or Master's degree from a recognized institution with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks or an equivalent grade.

## Fees and Certification

The total course fee for the Executive Post Graduate Certificate Programme in Data Science & AI is ₹1,80,000. For those preferring installment payments, an EMI option of approximately ₹5,977 per month is available.

Upon successful completion of the programme, participants will receive a certificate directly from IIIT Bangalore, signifying their achievement and enhanced expertise in data science and artificial intelligence.

## Application Process

Prospective candidates can apply for the programme by following a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official programme webpage.
2.  Select the 'Apply Now' option next to your chosen pricing plan.
3.  Complete and submit the application form with all required details.
4.  Attempt the scholarship test, if applicable.
5.  Make the necessary fee payment to secure your enrollment.

This programme is ideal for professionals currently working as Data Analysts, Data Scientists, Data Administrators, or Artificial Intelligence Engineers who are looking to upskill and advance their careers in these rapidly evolving domains.
