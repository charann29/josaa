# Secondary: Data Entry Operations (229) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program offers a fundamental introduction to computer systems and essential data entry skills, delivered through the Swayam platform. It's an excellent opportunity for anyone looking to build a strong foundation in digital operations at the secondary school level.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is entirely self-paced and delivered through a blend of video lectures and reading materials.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certification from the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS).
*   It carries 10 academic credit points, making it valuable for secondary-level learning.
*   The program duration is 24 weeks, covering core aspects of computer basics and office applications.

## Course Overview

The "Secondary: Data Entry Operations (229)" training is a comprehensive online course designed to introduce students to the foundational elements of computing and practical data entry skills. Coordinated by Professor Radhika B. from the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS), this program is specifically tailored for learners at the secondary education level.

Throughout the course, participants will gain proficiency in crucial areas such as creating and formatting documents using word processing software, working with spreadsheets, and developing presentations. The learning experience is supported by engaging video lectures, detailed PDF notes, regular quizzes to test understanding, and a final examination.

## What You Will Learn

This program is structured to provide a thorough understanding of various computer applications and concepts. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Computer Fundamentals:** Grasping the basics of computer systems, including input/output devices and different types of software.
*   **Windows File Management:** Efficiently organizing and managing files and folders within the Windows operating system.
*   **MS Word 2007 Proficiency:** Mastering document creation, formatting, and advanced features like mail merge.
*   **MS Excel 2007 Skills:** Learning the essentials of spreadsheets, including data entry, formatting worksheets, and using basic formulas.
*   **MS PowerPoint Presentation:** Developing the ability to create and edit effective presentations.

## Program Structure and Duration

This self-study course spans 24 weeks, offering a flexible learning environment. It is delivered through a combination of video and text-based materials, ensuring comprehensive coverage of the syllabus. The program is recognized for 10 credit points, which can be a valuable addition to a student's academic profile at the secondary level.

Learning is reinforced through assignments, peer discussion forums, and both theory and practical examinations. The medium of instruction is English.

## Certification and Eligibility

Upon successful completion of the course, students will be awarded a "Secondary: Data Entry Operations (229)" certification by the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) via the Swayam platform. To earn this certificate, candidates must achieve a minimum aggregate score of 33% across both the theory and practical examinations.

This course is open to anyone interested in acquiring fundamental computer and data entry skills, making it accessible to a wide audience of secondary-level students and beyond.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the "Secondary: Data Entry Operations (229)" certification course is a straightforward process through the official Swayam portal. Here are the steps to follow:

1.  Visit the official Swayam online courses portal.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the new page that opens, you will have the option to either sign in with existing credentials or create a new account to proceed with your enrollment.

This program is an excellent stepping stone for anyone looking to enhance their digital literacy and prepare for various roles requiring data entry and basic computer operations.
