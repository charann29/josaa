# MBA in HR: Course Fees, Admission 2026, Subjects, Syllabus, Salary in India

An MBA in Human Resources (HR) is a postgraduate program designed to equip students with the essential skills for managing an organization's most valuable asset: its people. It focuses on preparing individuals for strategic management roles within the field of Human Resource Management.

## Key Takeaways

*   An MBA in HR is a two-year postgraduate degree focusing on workforce management and development.
*   Admission typically requires a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks and a valid score in common entrance exams like CAT, MAT, XAT, or CMAT.
*   Course fees can vary widely, from approximately INR 1 lakh to INR 20 lakh, depending on the institution.
*   Graduates can pursue roles such as HR Manager, HR Business Partner, or Talent Acquisition Specialist, with initial salaries typically ranging from INR 4 LPA to INR 8 LPA.

## MBA in HR: Course Overview and Eligibility

The Master of Business Administration in Human Resources is a two-year, full-time postgraduate degree. It delves into various aspects of human capital, including recruitment, training, performance management, compensation, employee relations, and organizational development. The curriculum aims to develop leaders who can effectively manage human resources to achieve organizational goals.

To be eligible for an MBA in HR, candidates generally need to hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline (Science, Arts, or Commerce) from a recognized university, with a minimum aggregate score of 50%. Specific colleges might have slightly different percentage requirements. Beyond academic qualifications, most reputable institutions require candidates to clear national or state-level entrance examinations.

## Admission Process and Key Entrance Exams for 2026

Admission to MBA in HR programs is typically based on a combination of entrance exam scores, academic performance, work experience (if any), and performance in group discussions and personal interviews. Prospective students for the 2026 academic year should be aware of the following important dates for various entrance exams:

*   **MAT Feb 2026 PBT:** Registration closes on February 23, 2026, with the exam on March 1, 2026.
*   **MAT Feb 2026 CBT:** Registration closes on March 2, 2026, with the exam on March 8, 2026.
*   **ATMA 2026 (Feb):** Registration is open until February 10, 2026, and the exam will be held on February 22, 2026.
*   **MAH MBA CET 2026:** Registration is open until February 12, 2026, with exams scheduled from April 7 to April 8, 2026.
*   **APICET 2026:** The exam is slated for April 28, 2026.
*   **OJEE 2026:** Registrations have commenced, and the exam will be conducted from May 4 to May 9, 2026.
*   **CUET 2026:** The application process is open until January 30, 2026, with exams from May 11 to May 31, 2026.

Candidates are advised to regularly check the official websites of NTA and other exam conducting bodies for the most up-to-date information on application deadlines and exam schedules.

## Course Fees and Top Recruiters

The financial investment for an MBA in HR can vary significantly across institutions. Generally, course fees range from approximately INR 1 lakh to INR 20 lakh for the entire two-year program. Premier institutions and private universities tend to have higher fee structures compared to state universities or government-aided colleges.

Upon graduation, MBA HR professionals are highly sought after by a wide array of companies across various sectors. Some of the top recruiters include major IT companies, consulting firms, manufacturing giants, and service industries. Notable recruiters often include TCS, Wipro, Deloitte, Samsung, Nokia, Reliance, and KPMG.

## Syllabus and Potential Career Paths

The MBA in HR syllabus covers a broad spectrum of subjects designed to provide a holistic understanding of human resource management. While specific subjects may vary by college, common areas of study include:

*   Organizational Behavior
*   Human Resource Planning
*   Talent Acquisition and Management
*   Compensation and Benefits Management
*   Training and Development
*   Industrial Relations and Labor Laws
*   Performance Management Systems
*   HR Analytics
*   Strategic Human Resource Management

Graduates with an MBA in HR have diverse career opportunities. Common job roles include:

*   **HR Manager:** Oversees all aspects of human resources practices and processes.
*   **Senior HR Manager:** Leads HR departments, developing and implementing HR strategies.
*   **HR Business Partner:** Works closely with business units to align HR strategies with business goals.
*   **Talent Acquisition Specialist:** Focuses on recruiting and onboarding new employees.
*   **Compensation and Benefits Analyst:** Designs and manages employee compensation and benefits programs.
*   **Training and Development Manager:** Identifies training needs and develops programs to enhance employee skills.

Starting salaries for MBA HR graduates in India typically range from INR 4 LPA to INR 8 LPA, depending on the college, individual skills, and the recruiting company. With experience, these figures can increase substantially.

An MBA in HR can open doors to a fulfilling career, empowering you to shape organizational culture and drive employee success.
