# XAT Answer Key 2027 (After Exam); Download Previous Year Answer Key PDF

The XAT answer key is an essential tool for candidates to estimate their performance in the Xavier Aptitude Test before the official results are out. Released by XLRI Jamshedpur, the conducting body for XAT, this key helps you cross-reference your responses with the correct answers.

## Key Takeaways

*   The XAT answer key typically becomes available on the official XAT website within four to five days of the exam date.
*   It is released in two stages: a provisional key, followed by a final one after considering candidate objections.
*   You'll need your XAT ID and password to access and download your response sheet and the answer key.
*   Candidates have the opportunity to challenge the provisional answer key if they find any discrepancies.

## Accessing Your XAT Answer Key 2027

To view and download your XAT answer key along with your individual response sheet, you will need to visit the official XAT portal. The response sheet usually highlights the correct answers in green, making it easy for you to compare. Here’s a simple guide:

1.  Navigate to the official XAT examination website.
2.  Look for the direct link titled 'XAT key and objection' or similar.
3.  Log in using your unique XAT ID and password.
4.  Once logged in, click on the option to view your candidate response, which will display both your submitted answers and the official answer key.

## How to Challenge the Provisional XAT Answer Key 2027

XLRI provides a window for candidates to raise objections against the provisional answer key. This process allows you to submit claims if you believe an answer provided in the provisional key is incorrect. There is no fee for challenging the provisional answer key. Follow these steps:

*   Go to the official XAT website.
*   Click on the 'XAT key and objection' tab.
*   Enter your XAT 2027 login credentials (ID and password).
*   Locate and access the 'raise objection' form.
*   Select the specific question IDs you wish to challenge.
*   Upload any supporting documents or evidence for your claim.
*   Submit your objection.

## Understanding XAT Score Calculation and Percentile

Your XAT raw score is calculated based on the official marking scheme, and this raw score is then used to determine your XAT percentile. The General Knowledge (GK) section score is not included in the percentile calculation for admission to XLRI and other XAT-accepting institutes.

### XAT Marking Scheme 2027

To calculate your raw score, keep the following in mind:

*   Each correct answer fetches you 1 mark.
*   There is a deduction of 0.25 marks for every incorrect answer.
*   For every question left unattempted after the first eight consecutive unattempted questions, 0.10 marks are deducted.
