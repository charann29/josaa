# Certified HR Staffing Manager by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This Vskills certification program is designed to equip aspiring and current HR professionals with essential skills in talent acquisition and management. It covers the full spectrum of staffing, from strategic planning to employee evaluation and retention.

## Key takeaways

*   Gain comprehensive knowledge in staffing strategies, recruitment, and employee management.
*   Receive a government-certified credential upon successful completion of the online exam.
*   Access study materials, video tutorials, and practice tests for a lifetime.
*   Benefit from enhanced visibility on major Indian job portals with the 'Vskills certified' tag.

## Course Overview

The Certified HR Staffing Manager course by Vskills offers a deep dive into the critical aspects of human resource staffing. It's structured to provide a thorough understanding of how to meet an organization's recruitment and staffing needs effectively. The curriculum covers various concepts, including the analysis of job roles, effective recruiting techniques, sourcing strategies, employee evaluation methods, and overall organizational management. Participants will learn the theoretical foundations and practical applications needed to build and manage a skilled workforce, from forming recruitment teams to organizing training sessions.

## Who is this course for?

This certification is particularly beneficial for individuals already working in the HR domain who wish to enhance their skills and career prospects. It's also ideal for those aspiring to enter the HR field, specifically in staffing or recruitment roles. HR staffing managers looking to formalize their expertise or secure promotions will find this course valuable. Essentially, anyone aiming to strengthen their capabilities in talent acquisition and management within an organization can benefit significantly from this program.

## Learning Experience and Materials

The Vskills Certified HR Staffing Manager course is delivered through a self-study model, primarily using video and text-based materials. The instruction medium is English. Upon enrollment, candidates receive both soft and hard copies of the study materials, along with access to online video tutorials. A significant advantage is the lifetime access to the learning platform, ensuring you can revisit the content whenever needed. To aid preparation for the certification exam, free practice tests and mock tests are also provided.

## Certification and Examination Details

To earn the Vskills Certified HR Staffing Manager credential, candidates must successfully pass an online examination. The test is 60 minutes long and consists of 50 multiple-choice questions. Each correct answer contributes one mark, and there is no negative marking for incorrect responses. To qualify for the certificate, a candidate must achieve a minimum score of 25 marks, which translates to 50% of the total available marks. The certificate issued by Vskills holds lifetime validity.

## Course Fee and Application Process

The fee for the Certified HR Staffing Manager course is ₹3,499 for all aspiring candidates. The application process is straightforward and conducted entirely online:

1.  Visit the official Vskills program page for the Certified HR Staffing Manager course.
2.  Locate and click the 'BUY NOW' button.
3.  You will be prompted to enter your email ID and password. If you are a new user, you will need to register.
4.  Complete the registration process by providing the necessary details.
5.  Follow the on-screen instructions to finalize your enrollment and make the payment.

This certification offers a robust pathway to enhancing your HR career, providing valuable skills and a recognized credential in the competitive field of human resources.
