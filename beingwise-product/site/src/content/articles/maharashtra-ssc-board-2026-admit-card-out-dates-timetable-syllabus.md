# Maharashtra SSC Board 2026: Admit Card (Out), Dates, Timetable, Syllabus, Question Papers, Pattern, Result

The Maharashtra Secondary School Certificate (SSC) examination is a crucial milestone for students in Maharashtra. Conducted annually by the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE), these exams mark the completion of secondary education.

## Key takeaways

*   The Maharashtra SSC exams for 2026 concluded on March 18, 2026.
*   The official timetable for the 2026 exams was released on October 31, 2025.
*   Approximately 1.7 million students participate in the SSC examinations each year.
*   Results for the Maharashtra SSC Board 2026 are expected to be announced in May 2026.

## Understanding the Maharashtra SSC Exam

The Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE), based in Pune, is the authoritative body responsible for conducting the Maharashtra SSC Board exams. This board oversees the entire examination process, including setting the curriculum, designing the exam pattern, creating the timetable, and publishing textbooks.

Key subjects covered in the Maharashtra SSC exams typically include English, Social Science, Hindi, Marathi, Science, and Mathematics. The examination is held once a year and has a duration of three hours for most papers.

## Important Dates for Maharashtra SSC Exams 2026

Staying informed about key dates is vital for students. Here's a quick overview of the Maharashtra SSC 2026 schedule:

| Dates               | Event                               |
| :------------------ | :---------------------------------- |
| Oct 31, 2025        | Release of Official Timetable       |
| Feb 20 - Mar 18, 2026 | Conduct of Maharashtra SSC Exams    |
| May 2026            | Release of Maharashtra SSC Result (Tentative) |

The daily exam timings for 2026 were generally from 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM and 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM.

## Maharashtra SSC Result 2026

The Maharashtra Board will soon declare the SSC results for 2026. Once announced, the direct link to check results will be available on the official MSBSHSE website. Students will need to log in with their required credentials to access their scorecards. In previous years, the result date and time have been communicated through a press release.

In 2025, the overall pass percentage for the Maharashtra SSC exams was 94.10%. Girls outperformed boys, with a pass percentage of 96.14% compared to 92.31% for boys.

### How to Check Maharashtra SSC Result 2026?

To view your Maharashtra SSC result, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official MSBSHSE website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Maharashtra SSC Result 2026' link.
3.  Enter your required credentials (e.g., roll number, mother's first name).
4.  Your Maharashtra Board SSC result 2026 will be displayed on the screen.
5.  Download and save a copy for your records.

### Maharashtra SSC Passing Marks

To successfully pass the Maharashtra Board SSC exam, students must achieve a minimum of 35% marks in each subject. For theory papers typically graded out of 80 marks, this translates to a requirement of 28 marks. In practical examinations, usually scored out of 20, students need to secure at least 7 marks.

## Syllabus and Exam Pattern

The MSBSHSE defines a comprehensive syllabus for the SSC exams, covering all the core subjects. Students should refer to the official syllabus available on the Maharashtra Board's website to understand the topics they need to study. The exam pattern outlines the structure of the question papers, including the types of questions, marking scheme, and duration for each section. Familiarity with both the syllabus and exam pattern is crucial for effective preparation.

## Preparation Tips and Question Papers

Effective preparation for the Maharashtra SSC exams involves a combination of thorough study and strategic practice. Students are strongly advised to consult the official timetable and exam pattern. Solving previous years' question papers is an excellent way to understand the exam format, identify important topics, and improve time management skills. Consistent revision and clarity on concepts across all subjects will contribute significantly to success.
