# Web Analytics Course Certification Online by Digital Vidya: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding how people interact with websites is crucial in today's digital world. A Web Analytics certification can equip you with the skills to interpret user behavior and make informed decisions, whether for business growth or personal projects.

## Key takeaways

*   This program offers a blend of self-paced video lessons and live, instructor-led online sessions.
*   It focuses on practical application through real-time projects and case studies.
*   Successful completion leads to industry certifications, including one from Vskills.
*   The course includes a research-based internship and provides lifetime access to updated content.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Web Analytics Course Certification Online by Digital Vidya is designed to teach you the fundamental concepts of web analytics. This includes understanding traffic sources, visitor behavior, e-commerce analytics, and leveraging various web analytics tools. The program also delves into social media analytics and how to derive actionable insights from data.

The curriculum helps you use statistical algorithms to explore, analyze, and understand data, ultimately generating valuable insights. The course content is delivered through pre-recorded video lessons and presentations developed by experienced professionals. Additionally, it features live, instructor-led online sessions for interactive learning.

### Learning Modes and Resources

The program offers flexibility with a self-paced module, allowing you to review lessons at your convenience. It also incorporates real-time projects, hands-on assignments, and opportunities for interactive feedback. Students gain access to a substantial collection of over 15,000 free SEO tools for practical implementation. A unique aspect is the research-based internship, where participants contribute blog posts to the official Digital Vidya website, gaining valuable experience and visibility.

### Program Highlights

*   **Flexible Learning:** Online, pre-recorded classes combined with instructor-led live sessions.
*   **Practical Focus:** Project-based learning, real-time case studies, and interactive assignments.
*   **Extensive Tools:** Access to over 15,000 SEO tools for hands-on experience.
*   **Career Support:** Includes a research-based internship and a 100% interview guarantee.
*   **Certifications:** Earn multiple industry certifications, including a completion certificate from Vskills.
*   **Lifetime Access:** Continuous access to all course content and future updates.
*   **Orientation:** Complimentary access to an orientation session to introduce the course.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Web Analytics Course Certification Online is ₹15,000, which includes GST. Upon successful completion of the course, you will receive a Web Analytics completion certificate. The certificate providing authority for this program is Vskills.

| Fee Components | Amount |
| :------------- | :----- |
| Programme fees | ₹15,000 + 18% GST |

## Eligibility and Certification Criteria

To be eligible for certification, participants must first enroll in the course, which grants them access to one month of live, instructor-led web analytics sessions conducted online. After completing these sessions, you become eligible to apply for the final certification exam. A mandatory 60% attendance in the live sessions is required to appear for the exam. To successfully clear the exam and receive the certificate, participants need to achieve a minimum score of 70%.

## Skills You Will Acquire

Upon completing this program, you will develop a comprehensive understanding of web analytics and acquire proficiency in several key areas:

*   **Web Analytics Expertise:** In-depth knowledge of what web analytics entails, its operational mechanisms, and how its application varies between businesses of different sizes.
*   **Google Analytics Mastery:** Familiarity with Google Analytics, including its functionality, navigation, and essential metrics.
*   **Campaign Management:** Ability to execute Google campaigns, understand AdSense and AdWords, and identify various traffic sources.
*   **Advanced Tracking:** Knowledge of event tracking, site search analysis, and optimizing landing pages.
*   **Reporting and Benchmarking:** Skills in performing analytics on unique visitors, generating technical reports, and conducting benchmarking.
*   **Goal Setting and E-commerce:** Confidence in setting up goals, analyzing goal reports, and implementing e-commerce tracking.
*   **Data-Driven Culture:** Ability to find actionable insights and foster a data-driven decision-making environment.
*   **Tool Implementation:** Acquaintance with the framework and implementation strategies for various web analytics tools.

This course aims to empower you with the decision-making skills and technical knowledge necessary to excel in the field of web analytics.
