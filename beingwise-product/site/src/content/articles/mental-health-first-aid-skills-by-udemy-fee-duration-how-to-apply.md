# Mental Health First Aid Skills by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding mental health and knowing how to offer initial support can be incredibly valuable in today's world. This Udemy course aims to equip individuals with essential mental health first aid skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is delivered online, allowing for flexible, self-paced learning through video and text content.
*   It covers a broad spectrum of mental health topics, from awareness of specific conditions to identifying red flags and understanding triggers.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Udemy, validating their acquired skills.
*   The full course fee is ₹2,499, which provides access to all learning materials and the certificate.

## Course Overview

This Mental Health First Aid Skills course is designed to provide a foundational understanding of various mental health conditions and practical strategies for offering immediate support. It's structured for self-study, meaning you can learn at your own pace, fitting it around your existing commitments. The content is presented in English, using a mix of video lectures and written materials to explain complex topics clearly.

## What You Will Learn: Course Syllabus

The curriculum is divided into several units, progressively building your knowledge from basic awareness to practical response techniques. Here's a glimpse of the topics covered:

### Unit 1: Mental Health Awareness

This section introduces you to various mental health conditions, helping you understand what they are and their key characteristics. Topics include:

*   What is Mental Health?
*   Depression
*   Anxiety
*   Bipolar Disorder
*   Chronic Stress
*   Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
*   Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
*   Post Natal Depression (in women and men)
*   Psychosis & Schizophrenia

### Unit 2: Red Flags of Mental Health Issues

Learning to recognise the signs of distress is crucial for early intervention. This unit focuses on identifying the indicators of different conditions, such as:

*   Signs of Depression
*   Anxiety & Panic Attacks
*   Red Flags of PTSD
*   Alcohol & Substance Abuse
*   Social Anxiety
*   Eating Disorders
*   Self Harm
*   Manic Behaviour
*   Red Flags of OCD
*   Suicidal Tendencies

### Unit 3: Triggers & Causes

Understanding what can contribute to or worsen mental health issues is vital for providing empathetic support. This unit explores various factors, including:

*   Alcohol & Substance Misuse
*   Biological Factors
*   Grief and Bereavement
*   Trauma
*   Daily stressors and chronic stress
*   How Social Media & Cyber Bullying affect Mental Health
*   Cognitive Distortions

### Unit 4: Initial Actions & Responses

This practical unit teaches you how to respond effectively when someone is struggling. It covers:

*   Immediate support and signposting to professional help
*   Developing Resilience
*   Suicide Prevention strategies
*   Basic principles of Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)
*   Mindfulness Techniques
