# What is Data Science by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Embarking on a journey into data science can seem daunting, but foundational courses like the "What is Data Science" program, offered by IBM through Coursera, provide an excellent starting point. This course is designed to introduce you to the exciting world of data science, outlining its core concepts and career possibilities.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a beginner-friendly online course, ideal for those new to data science.
*   It offers a flexible, self-paced learning structure with an estimated commitment of 10 hours per week.
*   You can choose between a free audit option or paid versions that grant a shareable certificate and full access to graded assessments.
*   The course is delivered in English but also supports 22 other languages, making it accessible to a wide audience.

## Course Overview

This introductory program is structured to give learners a fundamental understanding of what data science entails and its growing importance in our data-rich world. While the core content can be covered in about a week, the flexible format allows you to progress at your own speed. The curriculum touches upon essential areas like Big Data, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Data Mining, equipping you with a foundational skillset.

Learners will benefit from insights shared by data science professionals and experts, gaining a realistic perspective on the field. The course also includes hands-on projects to solidify understanding and practical application of new concepts.

## Who Is This Course For?

This certification program is particularly well-suited for aspiring students and working professionals looking to break into the field of data science or enhance their existing skills. It's beneficial for individuals considering roles such as a Data Scientist or Data Analyst, providing them with the necessary introductory knowledge and a clear understanding of career pathways in data science. Whether you're a complete beginner or someone looking to formalize their understanding, this course offers valuable insights into why data science is considered a top career choice in the 21st century.

## Learning Outcomes and Skills Acquired

Upon successful completion of the Data Science training, participants will gain a clear understanding of what data science is, the role of data scientists, and the significance of data literacy. You will be introduced to key concepts and skills, including:

*   **Data Science Fundamentals:** Understanding its importance and applications.
*   **Big Data Concepts:** An introduction to handling large datasets.
*   **Machine Learning Basics:** Core principles of machine learning.
*   **Deep Learning Introduction:** An overview of deep learning techniques.
*   **Data Mining Essentials:** Techniques for extracting valuable insights from data.

Furthermore, the course explores various career paths within data science, offering advice and perspectives from industry professionals, helping you envision your potential journey in this dynamic field.

## Fee Structure and Certification

The "What is Data Science" course offers a flexible fee structure, allowing learners to choose an option that best suits their needs. Here’s a breakdown of the costs:

| Certification Option           | Fee Amount (INR) |
| :----------------------------- | :--------------- |
| What is Data Science (Audit)   | Free             |
| What is Data Science - 1 month | ₹1,699           |
| What is Data Science - 3 months | ₹3,499           |
| What is Data Science - 6 months | ₹5,199           |

The audit option provides free access to most course materials, allowing you to explore the content without financial commitment. However, to receive a shareable certificate and access all graded assessments, you will need to enroll in one of the paid subscription plans. The certificate is officially provided by Coursera, acknowledging your completion of the program.

## Course Syllabus Highlights

The program is structured into modules, with Module 1 focusing on foundational definitions and roles within data science. It includes a mix of video lectures, readings, and assignments designed to build a strong understanding.

### Module 1: Defining Data Science and What Data Scientists Do

*   **Videos:** Cover topics like "What is Data Science?", "Fundamentals of Data Science," "The Many Paths to Data Science," and "A Day in the Life of a Data Scientist." These provide an overview of the field and insights into the daily work of professionals.
*   **Readings:** Include the course syllabus, tips for course completion, and detailed overviews of the lessons, offering supplementary information.
*   **Assignments:** Feature graded quizzes on defining data science and understanding what data scientists do, along with practice quizzes to reinforce learning.
*   **Discussion Prompt:** An introductory prompt to encourage learner engagement.

This comprehensive module lays the groundwork for further exploration into data science. The course provides a robust introduction, preparing you for more advanced studies or career opportunities in this in-demand field.
