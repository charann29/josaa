# Psychology XI Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course offers an introduction to Psychology for Class XI students, exploring the intricacies of the human mind and behavior. Delivered through Swayam, it provides a flexible learning environment with video lectures and digital texts.

## Key takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Introduction:** Covers fundamental psychological concepts relevant to Class XI. 
*   **Flexible Learning:** Self-paced study with video and text-based materials, accessible from anywhere.
*   **NCERT Certification:** Earn a certificate from the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) upon successful completion.
*   **Expert Instruction:** Taught by faculty from NCERT's Department of Educational Psychology and Foundations of Education.

## Course Overview

The Psychology XI Part-I course is a massive open online course (MOOC) designed to introduce students to the core principles of psychology at the Class XI level. It delves into various aspects of human thought and behavior, connecting complex psychological ideas to everyday experiences. The curriculum includes four chapters, delivered through engaging video lectures and supplementary web resources, making the learning process interactive and comprehensive.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this certification course, participants will gain foundational knowledge in several key areas of psychology, including:

*   An introduction to the field of psychology.
*   Understanding different methods of inquiry used in psychological research.
*   Insights into human behavior and its underlying mechanisms.
*   Exploration of human development across various stages.

## Course Structure and Content

The course spans 24 weeks and is delivered entirely in English. It uses a self-study model, combining video tutorials with e-text materials. Students can engage with the content through interactive discussions, self-assessment quizzes, and multiple-choice questions. The syllabus is structured across four main chapters, each followed by an assignment to reinforce learning:

*   **Chapter 1: What is Psychology?** - A multi-part exploration of the definition and scope of psychology.
*   **Chapter 2: Methods of Enquiry in Psychology** - Detailed examination of research methodologies.
*   **Chapter 3: Human Behavior** - Covers evolutionary, biological, and cultural roots, including the nervous system and socialization.
*   **Chapter 4: Human Development** - Traces development from prenatal stages through infancy, childhood, adolescence, adulthood, and old age.

There is also a final assessment at the end of the course.

## Eligibility and Certification

This course is primarily designed for Class XI students who wish to build a strong foundation in psychology. To earn the official certificate from NCERT, students must achieve a score of at least 70% or higher in the final assessment. The certificate attests to the successful completion of the program, verifying your understanding of the course material.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Psychology XI Part-I Certification Course is straightforward. You can register through the Swayam online platform. Simply visit the official Swayam portal, locate the course, and sign in or register for a new account using your preferred credentials. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your enrollment.

This course offers a valuable opportunity to explore the fascinating world of psychology under the guidance of NCERT experts.
