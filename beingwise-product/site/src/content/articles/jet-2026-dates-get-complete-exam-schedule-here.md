# JET 2026 Dates: Get Complete Exam Schedule Here

For students aiming to join Jain University, the Jain Entrance Test (JET) is a crucial step. This page provides a comprehensive overview of the JET 2026 examination schedule for various undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

## Key takeaways

*   The application window for JET 2026 is currently open for all courses.
*   JET 2026 is conducted in multiple phases, allowing candidates flexibility in choosing their exam dates.
*   Specific dates for the entrance exam, essay writing, presentations, and personal interviews vary significantly by course.
*   Final results for the MBA program are expected by mid-April 2026.

## Understanding the JET 2026 Schedule

The JET exam is designed for admission to a wide array of programs at Jain University, including B.Tech, MBA, B.Pharma, and other undergraduate and postgraduate courses. The university structures the admission process with distinct dates for different course categories, often including an entrance test, followed by an essay, presentation, and personal interview (PI) round.

### Important Dates for Postgraduate Programs

For postgraduate programs, the JET 2026 entrance exam is scheduled for **April 30, 2026**. Following the written examination, the online personal interview round will take place on **May 2, 2026**.

### Important Dates for MBA Program

The MBA entrance test for JET 2026 will be held on **April 1 and 2, 2026**. Candidates can expect their results for this phase to be released on **April 3, 2026**. The subsequent rounds, including essay writing, presentations, and personal interviews, are scheduled from **April 9 to 11, 2026**. The final results for the MBA program will be declared on **April 15, 2026**.

### Important Dates for Undergraduate Programs

The schedule for undergraduate courses varies by program:

*   **B.Tech:** The JET B.Tech entrance exam will be conducted on **April 9 and 10, 2026**.
*   **B.Sc, BCA, BMS, BA, BFA, B.Des, BPES & B.Sc Sports and Law:** The entrance exam for these courses is on **April 9, 2026**, with the personal interview round following on **April 10, 2026**.
*   **BBA, B.Com:** The JET for these programs will be held on **April 9 and 10, 2026**. Personal interviews for BBA and B.Com applicants are scheduled for **April 13 and 14, 2026**.

## Applying for JET 2026

Candidates interested in any of the programs offered by Jain University can apply online. The application forms are exclusively available on the official Jain University portal. Ensure you complete and submit your application well before the deadlines to avoid any last-minute issues.
