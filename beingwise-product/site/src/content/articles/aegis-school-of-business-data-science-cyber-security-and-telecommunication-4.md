# Aegis School of Business Data Science Cyber Security and Telecommunication Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Aegis School of Business Data Science Cyber Security and Telecommunication Courses

Aegis School of Business, Data Science, Cyber Security, and Telecommunication offers specialized programs designed to equip professionals with cutting-edge skills in rapidly evolving fields. These courses, often developed in collaboration with industry leaders like IBM, cater to both aspiring and experienced individuals seeking to advance their careers.

## Key takeaways

*   Aegis offers specialized postgraduate and executive programs in data science, business analytics, and telecommunication management.
*   Many programs are developed in association with IBM, providing industry-relevant curriculum and expertise.
*   Courses are often delivered in a hybrid format, combining in-person sessions with online learning, or entirely online.
*   The curriculum is designed to develop practical, leadership-oriented skills for various roles in technology and management.

## Hybrid EPGP in Data Science, Business Analytics, and Big Data (in association with IBM)

This 12-month Hybrid Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP) is a collaborative offering between Aegis School of Business & Telecommunication and IBM. Recognized as one of India's pioneering high-end data science programs, it provides a comprehensive curriculum in data science, business analytics, and big data.

The program's hybrid structure involves weekday classes conducted at the Centre for Excellence in Telecom Technology and Management (CETTM) in Mumbai, complemented by online sessions on Sundays. This blend ensures both immersive learning and flexibility for participants. The curriculum uniquely combines core subjects with a range of elective courses, aiming to develop essential skills for a successful career in business analytics and big data. Upon completion, students are expected to possess both the technical and quantitative abilities needed to create business and social value across various industries, gaining exposure to a wide array of tools and software.

## Post Graduate Program in Telecommunication Management (in association with IBM)

An 11-month certificate course, the Post Graduate Program in Telecommunication Management is jointly offered by Aegis and IBM. This program is highly regarded within the industry, having benefited executives from over 35 countries, including those from vendors and telecom service providers.

The curriculum focuses on providing aspiring telecom leaders with a deep understanding of information technology and telecommunication operations from crucial leadership perspectives, encompassing finance, management, and legal aspects. Participants are prepared to become confident technology managers capable of making informed decisions for the betterment of the telecom management business. The program also allows for the development of specific functional skills relevant to individual domains.

## Executive Masters Program in Telecom Management (in association with IBM Online Live)

This 11-month Executive Masters Program in Telecom Management is delivered entirely online in a live format, jointly by experts from Aegis and IBM. It is considered a premier Telecom Management course, often equated to an MBA in Telecommunication in India. Similar to other programs, it has a global reach, benefiting executives from over 35 countries, including those associated with telecom service providers and vendors.

The program is specifically designed to cultivate future leaders in the information technology sector. Its syllabus is meticulously developed with inputs and suggestions from major telecom industry players such as Qualcomm, Bharti Airtel, Amdocs, Ericsson, ZTE, Reliance Communication, and BSNL. This industry-driven approach ensures that the curriculum addresses both the current and future needs of telecom managers and leaders, helping participants develop critical knowledge and skills.
