# Data Analytics Programme by AccioJob: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in the rapidly expanding field of data, the Data Analytics Programme offered by AccioJob provides a structured pathway. This programme is designed to equip participants with essential analytical and technical skills, catering to both freshers and experienced professionals.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a six-month, nationally recognised online programme with live classes.
*   It focuses on practical skills like SQL, Python, Excel, and PowerBI, alongside strategic thinking.
*   No prior experience in data analytics is required to enrol.
*   The programme includes extensive career support, such as mock interviews, portfolio building, and job placement assistance.

## Programme Overview and Certification

This Data Analytics Course is certified by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), making it a nationally recognised qualification. The curriculum is designed to broaden participants' thinking capabilities, covering not just technical expertise but also crucial soft skills like problem-solving, strategic thinking, and effective communication. Students will learn directly from experienced data leaders who are adept at tackling real-world business challenges.

Upon successful completion, participants receive an NSDC-accredited certificate, which will be issued via the Skill India portal. The programme also grants access to AccioJob's learning portal, where students can connect with industry professionals, including IIT alumni, for guidance on career paths and job opportunities.

## What You Will Learn

The programme covers a comprehensive range of tools and concepts vital for a data analytics career. Key areas of learning include:

*   **Technical Skills:** Proficiency in industry-standard tools such as SQL, Python, Microsoft Excel (from beginner to advanced, including pivot tables and custom charts), and data visualisation with Power BI. Basic concepts of Machine Learning are also introduced.
*   **Analytical Mindset:** Developing strong analytical abilities and problem-solving skills.
*   **Business Acumen:** Gaining diverse business knowledge to apply data insights effectively.
*   **Communication Skills:** Enhancing verbal communication, an essential skill for presenting data findings.

Through practical projects and weekly contests, participants will build a robust portfolio demonstrating their capabilities.

## Fees and Duration

The Data Analytics Programme is a six-month course delivered through live online classes, typically held on weekends. The learning format combines self-study with virtual classroom sessions, utilising video and text-based materials.

The total fee for the programme is ₹85,000. Upon successful completion, an NSDC-accredited certificate is provided, validating the skills acquired.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

There are no specific course prerequisites, making it accessible to individuals from various backgrounds interested in data analytics. The admission process is straightforward:

1.  **Visit the Official Page:** Candidates should visit the official AccioJob Data Analytics programme page.
2.  **Registration:** Register by providing basic personal details.
3.  **Online Test:** Complete a short online assessment.
4.  **Counsellor Interaction:** Successful candidates will then have a one-on-one call with a counsellor.
5.  **Fee Payment:** Upon qualifying and discussion, the final step is to pay the programme fee to begin the training.

## Career Prospects

This programme is ideal for individuals aspiring to roles such as Data Analyst, Data Scientist, Business Analyst, or Financial Analyst. The curriculum is specifically designed to equip participants with the skills demanded by these professions, supported by features like multiple mock interviews, one-on-one practice interviews, and exclusive job opportunities to aid in placement.

Embarking on this Data Analytics Programme can be a strategic move for anyone looking to enter or advance within the data industry, providing both foundational knowledge and practical application skills.
