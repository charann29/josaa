# Advanced Artificial Intelligence on Microsoft Azure Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning and Applied AI by CloudSwyft Global Systems, Inc via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This micro-credential program offers an in-depth exploration of advanced Artificial Intelligence concepts, focusing on practical application using Microsoft Azure. It's designed for individuals looking to build and implement cutting-edge AI solutions.

## Key takeaways

*   This professional certification course is offered in collaboration with Microsoft.
*   It includes hands-on lab sessions powered by Azure and CloudSwyft.
*   Participants receive an exam voucher for the Microsoft Azure AI Engineer Associate (AI-100) certification.
*   The program emphasizes project-based learning and practical skills for career advancement.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Advanced Artificial Intelligence on Microsoft Azure program is an intermediate-level course suitable for undergraduate students and professionals. Delivered through a blend of video tutorials and text-based materials, the learning experience is primarily self-paced with virtual classroom elements. The expected study commitment is about 5-6 hours per week over a period of 21 weeks.

Throughout the course, students engage in rigorous projects using the cloud computing platform provided by Microsoft Azure and CloudSwyft. This practical approach ensures that learners gain direct experience in building and implementing advanced AI technologies, including deep learning and reinforcement learning.

## Program Offerings and Highlights

This micro-credential focuses on career-oriented learning, equipping participants with professional skills in a rapidly evolving field. Key aspects of the program include:

*   **Collaboration with Microsoft:** The course content and approach are aligned with industry standards, developed in partnership with Microsoft.
*   **Industry-Expert Instructors:** Learning is facilitated by experts in the field, providing valuable insights and guidance.
*   **Hands-on Experience:** Extensive use of simulation lab classes and project-based assignments ensures practical skill development.
*   **Professional Certification:** Upon successful completion, candidates receive a certificate from CloudSwyft Global Systems, Inc.
*   **Exam Voucher:** A significant benefit is the provision of an exam voucher to sit for the Microsoft Azure AI Engineer Associate (AI-100) certification, a valuable industry credential.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in this program, applicants should possess foundational skills in several key areas. These include basic mathematics, computer literacy, and programming knowledge, specifically with Python. A fundamental understanding of statistics and probability, as it applies to AI, is also required. Successful completion of the curriculum is necessary to earn the CloudSwyft certificate.

## Course Fee and Payment Information

The program fee for the Advanced Artificial Intelligence on Microsoft Azure Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning and Applied AI course is ₹53,749. This fee covers access to all course materials, lab environments, and the completion certificate. Payments can typically be made using major credit cards such as Visa, MasterCard, and American Express, or through PayPal.

This course is designed to empower learners with comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in advanced AI. You will gain expertise in:

*   **Core AI and Data Science Concepts:** Deepen your understanding of artificial intelligence and data science principles.
*   **Python for AI:** Enhance your Python programming skills specifically for AI applications.
*   **Deep Learning and Machine Learning:** Explore advanced topics in deep learning, including neural networks, and various machine learning algorithms.
*   **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** Learn to analyze, extract, and process meaning from natural language, including building smart bots with LUIS integration.
