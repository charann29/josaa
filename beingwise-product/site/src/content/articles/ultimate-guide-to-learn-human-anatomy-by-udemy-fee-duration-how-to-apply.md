# A Ultimate guide to learn Human Anatomy by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding human anatomy is fundamental for anyone pursuing a career in medicine or other health sciences. This online certification course, delivered through Udemy, offers a comprehensive platform for medical students to delve deep into the intricate structures of the human body.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a self-paced online course with over 46 hours of video content, accessible on various devices.
*   Learners receive a certificate of completion from Udemy, validating their understanding of human anatomy.
*   The curriculum covers major body regions, including the head, neck, thorax, abdomen, and limbs, along with detailed bone and neural system studies.
*   The course includes a 30-day money-back guarantee and offers unlimited access to its learning resources.

## Course Overview

This certification, titled "A Complete Anatomy learning platform for Medical students," is specifically designed for MBBS students and those pursuing bachelor's degrees in surgery or medicine. It aims to equip them with a thorough understanding of the principles and methodologies used in studying human anatomy. The course content, developed by Anatomy Medical, an educational platform specializing in anatomy for medical and dental students, is delivered through the Udemy platform.

Over 46 hours of detailed lectures explain the anatomy of various parts of the human body. This includes in-depth explorations of regions like the head, neck, thorax, and abdomen, as well as detailed discussions on bones, limbs, and the neural system. The learning format combines video lectures with text-based resources, allowing for flexible, self-study at the learner's own pace.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of this certification course, participants will gain a strong grasp of both foundational and advanced concepts in human anatomy. The curriculum focuses on helping learners understand how organs and other body parts function, providing essential knowledge for medical practice. Key learning outcomes include:

*   A comprehensive understanding of fundamental and advanced principles related to human anatomy research.
*   Familiarity with various tools and techniques used in examining the human body's anatomy.
*   Detailed knowledge of the anatomy of specific human regions such as the head, neck, abdomen, thorax, limbs, bones, and the neural system.

## Program Offerings and Fees

This online course provides a rich set of offerings designed to support effective learning. These include extensive learning resources, unlimited access to course materials, and the flexibility to access content on mobile devices and smart TVs. For added assurance, a 30-day money-back guarantee is provided. Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Udemy.

### Fees Information

The course fee is ₹449, which is a discounted price from the original ₹1,999. This fee grants learners full access to all course content and resources.

## Course Syllabus Highlights

The syllabus is structured to provide a detailed, region-by-region understanding of human anatomy. Here are some of the key areas covered:

### Upper Limb

*   Introduction to the upper limb, including the shoulder region, mammary gland, and axilla.
*   Detailed study of the brachial plexus, scapular muscles, arm region, and cubital fossa.
*   Anatomy of the forearm, palm, and nerves of the palm region.

### Bones Of Upper Limb

*   In-depth examination of the clavicle, scapula, humerus, radius, and ulna.

### Lower Limb

*   Focus on the genicular anastomosis and femoral triangle.

### Abdomen Anatomy

*   Introduction to the abdomen, inguinal canal, and abdominal aorta.
*   Detailed study of organs like the stomach, duodenum, caecum, liver, portal vein, pancreas, kidney, urinary bladder, and uterus.
*   Exploration of the anal canal, perineum, pelvic diaphragm, and urogenital triangle.

### Neuro Anatomy

*   Study of dura folds, cavernous sinus, third and fourth ventricles, and the hypothalamus.
*   Understanding the internal capsule, arterial supply of the brain, and the facial nerve.

### Thorax

*   Detailed analysis of the arterial and nerve supply of the heart.

### Head and Neck Anatomy

*   Examination of the anterior, carotid, posterior, and thyroid triangles.
*   Study of the mandibular and maxillary nerves, nasal cavity, parotid gland, middle ear, orbit, ophthalmic artery, ciliary ganglion, palate, paranasal air sinuses, tongue, pharynx, temporomandibular joint (TMJ), and larynx.

This comprehensive curriculum ensures that students gain a holistic and practical understanding of human anatomy.

This course offers a valuable opportunity for aspiring medical professionals to enhance their knowledge and skills in human anatomy, all from the convenience of their home.
