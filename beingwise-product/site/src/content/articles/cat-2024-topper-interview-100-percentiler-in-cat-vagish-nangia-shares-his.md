# CAT 2024 Topper Interview: 100 Percentiler in CAT, Vagish Nangia, Shares his Preparation Journey

BeingWise brings you an exclusive interview with Vagish Nangia, one of the exceptional candidates who achieved a perfect 100 percentile in the CAT 2024 examination. A final-year Chemical Engineering student from IIT Delhi, Vagish shares his journey, strategies, and insights that led to this remarkable accomplishment.

## Key Takeaways

*   Vagish Nangia was among the 14 candidates nationwide to secure a 100 percentile in CAT 2024.
*   He is currently pursuing Chemical Engineering at IIT Delhi, demonstrating a strong academic background.
*   His preparation strategy emphasized a supportive home environment and regular breaks for hobbies like sports and quizzes to maintain mental freshness.
*   Vagish aims to gain admission to top-tier management institutes, including the premier IIMs and other renowned colleges like FMS.

## Meet Vagish Nangia: The 100 Percentiler

When the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) announced the CAT 2024 results on their official website, Vagish Nangia's name stood out among the elite group of 14 individuals who achieved the coveted 100 percentile. His impressive performance across all sections highlights a well-rounded and dedicated approach to the challenging management entrance exam. Here's a quick look at his scores:

| Section           | Percentile Score |

| Overall           | 100              |
| VARC (Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension) | 98.64            |
| DILR (Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning) | 99.99            |
| QA (Quantitative Ability) | 100              |

## The Role of Environment and Hobbies in Preparation

Vagish emphasized the significant role his family played in creating a conducive study environment, which he credits as crucial to his success. Beyond academics, he believes in maintaining a balanced lifestyle. To keep his mind sharp and prevent burnout during intense preparation, Vagish engaged in various hobbies. He enjoys watching sports, delving into articles and books, and actively participating in inter-college quiz competitions. These activities served as effective stressbusters and helped him return to his studies with renewed focus.

The CAT 2024 examination saw a total of 2.93 lakh candidates appear. Out of this vast pool, only 14 candidates managed to achieve the perfect 100 percentile.
