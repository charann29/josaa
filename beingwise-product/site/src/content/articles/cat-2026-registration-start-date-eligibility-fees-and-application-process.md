# CAT 2026 Registration: Start Date, Eligibility, Fees & Application Process

Preparing for the Common Admission Test (CAT) is a significant step towards pursuing an MBA at India's top management institutes. The registration process is the first crucial stage, requiring careful attention to detail and adherence to deadlines.

## Key Takeaways

*   CAT 2026 registration is expected to begin in the first week of August 2026, following an official notification in late July.
*   The application process involves online registration, filling out a detailed form, uploading necessary documents, and paying the application fee.
*   Candidates will need a valid email ID and mobile number, and it's recommended to use a desktop or laptop for filling the form.
*   The CAT 2026 exam is scheduled for November 29, 2026.

## Understanding the CAT 2026 Registration Process

The entire CAT registration and application process is conducted online through the official CAT website. It's essential to have a stable internet connection and use a desktop or laptop, as filling the form on a smartphone is not recommended. For the best experience, consider using web browsers like Google Chrome (version 59 and above), Mozilla Firefox (version 56 and above), or Edge Chromium.

### Step-by-Step Application Guide

1.  **New Candidate Registration:**
    *   Visit the official CAT website.
    *   Locate and click on the 'Register' tab under 'New Candidate Registration'.
    *   Provide your full name, date of birth, active email ID, mobile phone number, and nationality.
    *   Click 'Generate OTP' to receive a One-Time Password on your registered mobile number and email.
    *   Verify your registration using this OTP.

2.  **Filling the CAT Application Form:**
    Once registered, you'll proceed to fill out a comprehensive application form, which typically has six sections:

    *   **Personal Details:** This section requires information such as your name, date of birth, father's name, gender, category (if applicable), nationality, communication address, email, and mobile number. You will also need to upload a recent passport-sized photograph and a scanned image of your signature.
    *   **Academic Details:** Here, you'll enter your scores for Class 10 and 12 examinations, details of your bachelor's degree, including marks, and information about any other professional qualifications you may hold.
    *   **Work Experience:** This is an optional section where you can provide details of your professional experience, including company name, duration of employment, and your designation.
    *   **Programmes:** In this crucial section, you will select your preferred IIMs and the specific MBA programmes you wish to apply for. This choice is vital for subsequent shortlisting for the Personal Interview (PI) rounds.
    *   **Test City Preference:** You can choose up to five preferred test cities from a provided list, ranking them in order of your preference.
    *   **Payment:** The final step involves paying the application fee using a Credit/Debit Card, Internet Banking, or mobile wallets like Paytm/PayU.

## Important Dates for CAT 2026

While the exact dates will be confirmed by an official notification in late July 2026, here's what to expect:

*   **Official Notification Release:** Last week of July 2026
*   **Registration Start Date:** First week of August 2026
*   **CAT 2026 Exam Date:** November 29, 2026

## Documents Required for Application

To ensure a smooth application process, keep the following documents and information ready:

*   Valid Email ID and Mobile Phone Number
*   Scanned Passport-sized Photograph (as per specifications)
*   Scanned Signature (as per specifications)
*   Class 10 Mark Sheet
*   Class 12 Mark Sheet
*   Bachelor's Degree Mark Sheets and Certificate
*   Details of work experience (if any), including company name, duration, and designation.
*   Category certificate (if applying under a reserved category).

## CAT 2026 Application Fee

The application fee for CAT 2026 will be announced with the official notification. Historically, there are different fees for General/NC-OBC candidates and SC/ST/PwD candidates. The fee is non-refundable once paid.

## Eligibility Criteria for CAT 2026

The fundamental eligibility for CAT 2026 requires candidates to hold a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks or an equivalent CGPA from a recognized university. For candidates belonging to SC, ST, and PwD categories, the minimum requirement is 45% marks or an equivalent CGPA. Final-year bachelor's degree students are also eligible to apply, provided they complete their degree requirements within the stipulated timeframe set by the IIMs.

Successfully completing the CAT 2026 registration is your first step towards a rewarding management career. Ensure all details are accurate and submitted on time.
