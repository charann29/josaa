# UCEED 2026: Result (OUT), Scorecard (OUT), Merit List, Counselling Dates, Admission Process

The Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design (UCEED) is a crucial national-level exam for students aspiring to pursue a Bachelor of Design (B.Des) degree at premier institutions like the IITs and IIITDM Jabalpur. Administered by IIT Bombay, UCEED scores are essential for admission to these highly sought-after design programs.

## Key takeaways

*   UCEED 2026 results were officially announced on March 6, 2026, by IIT Bombay.
*   Scorecards are available for download from the official UCEED website from March 10, 2026, until July 31, 2026.
*   The B.Des application window for participating IITs and IIITDM Jabalpur closed on April 10, 2026.
*   IIT Bombay is responsible for conducting the joint seat allocation process for UCEED 2026 admissions.

## UCEED 2026 Results and Scorecard

The UCEED 2026 results for the B.Des programs were released by IIT Bombay on March 6, 2026. Following the result announcement, candidates can now access and download their individual scorecards from the official UCEED 2026 website. These scorecards became available on March 10, 2026, and will remain accessible until July 31, 2026. To download your scorecard, you will need your login ID and password. It's important to note that the UCEED scorecard is valid for one year from its date of release.

## UCEED 2026 B.Des Application

The application process for B.Des programs at the participating institutions for the 2026 academic year has concluded. The common application form, which was released by IIT Bombay, opened on March 14, 2026, and remained active until April 10, 2026. Only candidates who successfully cleared the UCEED 2026 examination were eligible to apply for these programs.

## UCEED 2026 Participating Institutes and Admission Process

UCEED scores are utilized for admissions to B.Des programs at seven prestigious institutions: IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Guwahati, IIT Hyderabad, IIT Indore, IIT Roorkee, and IIITDM Jabalpur. IIT Bombay plays a central role in the admission process, not only conducting the UCEED exam but also processing the common application form and managing the joint seat allocation for all these participating institutes.

Admission is primarily merit-based, determined by a candidate's performance in the UCEED 2026 entrance exam. Merit lists are prepared based on these scores, which are then used to shortlist candidates for the counselling and seat allocation process. Additionally, several result-sharing institutes also use UCEED 2026 scores to fill seats in their undergraduate design courses.

## UCEED 2026 Counselling and Merit List

While specific dates for counselling and the release of merit lists are usually announced by IIT Bombay, the joint seat allocation process will be managed centrally by IIT Bombay. Candidates who have applied for B.Des programs at the participating institutes based on their UCEED 2026 scores will be considered for admission. The merit list will reflect the candidates' UCEED scores and their respective ranks, guiding the seat allocation process.

Stay tuned to the official UCEED portal for further updates regarding the counselling schedule and detailed admission procedures.
