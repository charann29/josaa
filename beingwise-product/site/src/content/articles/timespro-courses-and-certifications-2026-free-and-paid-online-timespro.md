# TimesPro Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online TimesPro Courses

TimesPro offers a range of online and blended learning programs designed to help students and working professionals enhance their skills and advance their careers. These programs often collaborate with top Indian and international educational institutions.

## Key takeaways

*   TimesPro facilitates access to various degree and certification programs from reputed institutions.
*   Programs cover diverse fields like Business Analytics, AI, and General Management.
*   Options include blended learning, purely online, and executive formats to suit different needs.
*   Course durations and fees vary significantly depending on the program and the collaborating institution.

## Online Degree Programs through TimesPro

TimesPro partners with several esteemed institutions to offer online and blended degree programs. These programs are structured to provide flexibility while maintaining academic rigour.

### Blended MBA in Business Analytics and AI (IIM Ahmedabad)

This two-year blended MBA program, offered in collaboration with IIM Ahmedabad, focuses on the rapidly evolving fields of Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence. It is designed for individuals looking to gain advanced expertise in leveraging data and AI for strategic business decisions. The program fee is ₹20,00,000.

### Executive MBA (IIM Sirmaur)

IIM Sirmaur offers a two-year Executive MBA program through TimesPro, tailored for experienced professionals. This non-residential, weekend-based program uses a hybrid delivery model, including optional on-campus modules. The curriculum is industry-focused, integrating lectures, case studies, group activities, simulations, and ICT-based learning. It aims to equip learners with strategic insights and practical expertise for today's complex business environment. Elective courses are available across various management disciplines, including Decision Sciences, Finance & Accounting, Marketing Management, and Strategic Management. The program fee is ₹8,00,000.

### Online MBA (BS Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology)

This two-year, four-semester online MBA program from BS Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology is designed for both working professionals and freshers. It offers flexibility and convenience with online classes, interactive learning modules, and virtual lectures. Admission is typically based on previous academic performance. The program fee is ₹1,89,400.

### Master of Business Administration (MBA) (Lincoln University, Oakland)

Lincoln University, Oakland, offers a 22-month online MBA program through TimesPro. This program aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of management and entrepreneurship. It covers essential modules in leadership, organizational behavior, marketing management, business research, and accounting skills, delivered by experienced professionals. The fee for this program is not specified in the provided information.

## Understanding Program Formats

TimesPro facilitates various learning formats. Blended programs combine online learning with occasional in-person sessions, offering a mix of flexibility and direct interaction. Fully online programs provide maximum flexibility, allowing students to learn at their own pace from anywhere. Executive programs are specifically designed for working professionals, often with weekend schedules or modular structures to accommodate their busy lives.

Whether you're looking to specialize in emerging technologies or gain a broad understanding of business management, TimesPro's collaborations offer diverse avenues for professional growth and skill enhancement.
