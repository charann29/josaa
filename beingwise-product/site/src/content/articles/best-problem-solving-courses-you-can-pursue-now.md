# 17 Best Problem Solving Courses You Can Pursue Now

Life, whether personal or professional, constantly presents us with challenges. The ability to effectively address these obstacles is a highly valued skill, especially in today's dynamic work environments. Mastering problem-solving techniques can significantly enhance your effectiveness and career prospects.

## Key takeaways

*   Problem-solving is a crucial skill sought after by employers across all industries.
*   Numerous online and in-person courses are available to help develop and refine your problem-solving abilities.
*   Courses range from short introductions to comprehensive programs covering various methodologies like lean problem-solving, critical thinking, and computational approaches.
*   Investing in problem-solving education can lead to improved decision-making and better career opportunities.

## Why Problem-Solving Matters

In any role, from entry-level to leadership, the capacity to identify, analyze, and resolve issues is paramount. Employers consistently look for individuals who can not only recognize problems but also devise and implement effective solutions. This skill goes beyond just finding quick fixes; it involves critical thinking, creativity, and the ability to make informed decisions under pressure. Developing strong problem-solving skills can transform how you approach challenges, making you a more valuable asset in any team or organization.

## Popular Problem-Solving Course Formats

Problem-solving courses come in various durations and depths, catering to different learning needs and schedules. Some are concise, focusing on specific techniques, while others offer a broader exploration of methodologies. Many are available through well-known online learning platforms, providing flexibility for working professionals and students alike. These platforms often allow you to learn at your own pace, making it easier to integrate skill development into your busy life.

### 1. Effective Problem-Solving and Decision-making

This course focuses on teaching you to apply suitable problem-solving and decision-making processes. It helps you recognize common hurdles in practical problem-solving. By the end, you'll be equipped to use analytical and synthetic approaches, along with positive inquiry, for organizational issues and to evaluate potential solutions effectively.

*   **Duration:** 5 hours
*   **Platform:** Coursera
*   **Key Topics:** Introduction to Problem Solving, Charts and Diagrams, Decision-Making Methods, Implementing Decisions.

### 2. Lean Problem-Solving for Team Members Leaders

Designed for those involved in lean management or continuous improvement, this program helps cultivate the mindset and habits of team-based problem-solving to boost performance. You'll practice brainstorming and consensus-building skills, which are vital for collaborative environments.

*   **Duration:** 2.5 hours
*   **Platform:** Udemy
*   **Key Topics:** Principles and Attitudes of Problem Solving, Learning to Think Together - Brainstorming skills, Root Cause Analysis, Problem-solving models, Analyzing and Improving Human Performance.

### 3. Free Problem Solving Techniques

This introductory course teaches various methods to pinpoint the root cause of problems, moving beyond superficial symptoms. It introduces tools like mind maps and decision trees to help generate and explore potential solutions.

*   **Duration:** 1h 32m
*   **Platform:** LinkedIn Learning
*   **Key Topics:** Introduction, Identify the real problem.
