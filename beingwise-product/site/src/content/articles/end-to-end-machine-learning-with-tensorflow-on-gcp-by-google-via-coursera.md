# End-to-End Machine Learning with TensorFlow on GCP by Google via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This Coursera program, developed by Google Cloud, offers an in-depth exploration of machine learning principles and their practical application using TensorFlow on the Google Cloud Platform (GCP). It's designed for individuals looking to build and deploy robust, production-ready machine learning models.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Learning:** The program covers building, improving, and measuring production-grade ML systems across various data types like structured, time-series, natural language, and images.
*   **Flexible Schedule:** It's a self-paced, online course with pre-recorded modules, allowing you to learn at your convenience without strict deadlines.
*   **Practical Focus:** Beyond theoretical concepts, the curriculum emphasizes hands-on experience with advanced machine learning techniques and Google Cloud Platform products.
*   **Recognized Certification:** Upon successful completion, you receive a shareable certificate from Coursera, endorsed by Google Cloud.

## Course Overview and Structure

This course is a key part of a larger 5-course specialization focusing on advanced machine learning with Google Cloud Platform. It builds upon foundational knowledge and dives into real-world scenarios, concluding with an exploration of recommendation systems. The learning experience combines video lectures, readings, practice quizzes, and assignments to ensure a thorough understanding.

## What You Will Learn

Through this program, you will gain specialized knowledge in advanced machine learning using TensorFlow on GCP. You will work on projects that leverage various Google Cloud Platform products, developing an understanding of the components and practical approaches needed for high-performing ML systems in production environments. This includes mastering the creation of innovative and accurate models for diverse data types.

## Eligibility Criteria

To get the most out of this advanced-level course, it is recommended that applicants have prior knowledge of Python programming, TensorFlow, and basic SQL. Successful completion of all required assignments and viewing the pre-recorded modules are necessary to earn the certificate.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for the End-to-End Machine Learning with TensorFlow on GCP course is ₹2,934, payable after a 7-day free trial period. Coursera is the authority that provides the certificate of completion. This certificate can be shared electronically with potential employers and within your professional network to highlight your expertise.

## How to Apply

Applying for the End-to-End Machine Learning with TensorFlow on GCP program is straightforward:

1.  **Visit the Course Page:** Navigate to the official course page on Coursera.
2.  **Enroll for Free:** Select the "Enroll for free" option to begin your 7-day free trial. You will need to sign up or log in to your Coursera account first.
3.  **Continue Learning:** After the trial period, if you wish to continue, you will be prompted to pay the course fee.
4.  **Complete Payment:** Provide your billing details and choose your preferred payment method.
5.  **Confirmation:** Once the transaction is successful, you will receive a confirmation of your enrollment, granting you full access to the course content.

This program is an excellent opportunity to enhance your skills in a high-demand field, directly from Google Cloud experts, and prepare for real-world machine learning challenges.
