# Ayurvedic Nutrition Masterclass: Ayurveda Beginner to Pro!

This course offers a foundational and advanced understanding of Ayurvedic nutrition, emphasizing how this ancient science views food not just as sustenance but as medicine. Developed by Ayurvedic specialist Monika Chakrika, it guides learners through creating balanced meals for optimal digestion and nutrient absorption.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn the fundamental principles of Ayurveda, including the three doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.
*   Understand how to tailor diet based on individual body types and specific health conditions.
*   Explore concepts of digestive fire (Agni), self-healing, energy enhancement, and weight management.
*   Gain practical strategies for addressing various health concerns through Ayurvedic dietary approaches.

## Course Structure and Content

The "Ayurvedic Nutrition Masterclass: Ayurveda Beginner to Pro" is a concise, self-paced online program delivered through video lessons and supplementary downloadable resources. It's designed for anyone keen to delve into Ayurvedic principles for better health and nutrition.

The curriculum is structured to take you from the basics to more advanced applications:

### Introduction

*   An overview of the course and what to expect.

### Basics of Ayurveda

*   Detailed exploration of the three fundamental elements (doshas): Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.
*   Understanding their qualities, seasonal influences, and impact on daily life.
*   The crucial role of 'Digestive Fire' (Agni).
*   Ayurvedic perspective on daily routines and food categories and combinations.

### 6 Tastes in Ayurveda

*   A deep dive into the six tastes (sweet, sour, salty, pungent, bitter, astringent) and their effects on the body and doshas.

### Emotions in Ayurveda

*   How Vata, Pitta, and Kapha doshas manifest in everyday emotions and mental states.

### Ayurvedic Diary - Real-life Showcases

*   Practical examples and case studies demonstrating the application of Ayurvedic principles in real individuals' lives.

## Duration and Learning Mode

This masterclass is a short-term program, comprising approximately two hours of pre-recorded video content. It also includes seven downloadable resources to support your learning journey. As a self-study course, you have the flexibility to learn at your own pace, fitting it into your schedule whenever convenient. The instruction medium is English, and the content is delivered through a blend of video and text formats.

## Course Fee and Certification

The fee for the "Ayurvedic Nutrition Masterclass: Ayurveda Beginner to Pro" is ₹449. Upon successful completion of the course, learners will receive a certificate of completion from the platform. This certification validates your understanding of fundamental and advanced Ayurvedic nutrition principles.

## Who Is This Course For?

This course is ideal for individuals who are new to Ayurveda and wish to build a strong foundational understanding, as well as those looking to deepen their existing knowledge. It is particularly beneficial for:

*   Anyone interested in holistic health and natural healing.
*   Individuals wanting to understand their body type (dosha) and tailor their diet accordingly.
*   People seeking natural strategies for weight management, energy boosts, and improved digestion.
*   Those looking for complementary approaches to manage health conditions such as obesity, Crohn's, epilepsy, hypothyroidism, lactose intolerance, and more, through diet.

## Meet Your Instructor

The course is created and taught by Ms. Monika Chakrika, an Ayurvedic specialist. Her expertise guides learners through the intricacies of Ayurvedic nutrition, offering practical insights and a clear understanding of the subject matter.

Embark on this journey to transform your understanding of food and well-being through the wisdom of Ayurveda.
