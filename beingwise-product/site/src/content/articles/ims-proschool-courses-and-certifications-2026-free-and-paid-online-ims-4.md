IMS Proschool offers a variety of programs designed to equip students and professionals with essential skills in finance and accounting. These courses are structured to provide practical knowledge and industry-relevant certifications, catering to diverse learning preferences.

## Financial Planning Certifications

For those aspiring to a career in financial advisory, IMS Proschool offers several programs. The **CFPCM Certification Training Program** is a prominent offering, focusing on comprehensive financial planning principles. This certification is highly regarded in the financial sector, opening doors to excellent employment opportunities.

Students can enroll in this program through various flexible study modes: a traditional classroom setup, a live virtual classroom, or a self-paced learning format. The classroom option includes weekend batches, making it convenient for individuals balancing other commitments. The program concludes with a computer-based examination featuring multiple-choice questions and case studies. Successful completion allows candidates to apply for the official CFPCM certification.

Another valuable offering is the **Certificate in Financial Planning**, which is based on the CFP-FBSP curriculum. This course serves as an excellent starting point for anyone interested in finance, from undergraduates to working professionals. It covers fundamental financial planning concepts through high-quality video sessions and can prepare students for other industry benchmark certificates. Graduates of this program can explore roles in investment advisory and management, with placement support from IMS Proschool also available.

| Program Name                      | Duration   | Fees (Approx.) |
| :-------------------------------- | :--------- | :------------- |
| CFPCM Certification Training      | 15 Months  | ₹ 60,000       |
| Certificate in Financial Planning | 15 Months  | ₹ 50,000       |

## ACCA Course: Skills Level

The **ACCA Course: Skills Level** is an advanced program from IMS Proschool, designed to impart crucial business and accounting skills. This certification extensively covers various concepts vital for a career in accounting and finance.

Students have two main study options: an offline classroom program or live online training. The online mode provides interactive sessions delivered via the CISCO platform, led by educators qualified by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA). The learning experience includes recorded video lectures, assignments, mock tests, and live classes, with faculty feedback provided to aid performance improvement.

The curriculum for the ACCA Skills Level certification is structured into six distinct modules. These modules cover a wide array of topics, including Financial Management, Audit and Assurance, Taxation, and Financial Reporting. Furthermore, meeting specific criteria within this program can also lead to an advanced diploma in accounting and business.

*   **Duration:** 15 Months
*   **Fees (Approx.):** ₹ 1,25,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Decision-making, Financial Management, Taxation Knowledge

## Investment Banking Course with PG Certificate

IMS Proschool also offers a **PGP in Investment Banking & Capital Markets**, a program tailored to develop the expertise required for professionals in investment finance. This program emphasizes project-based learning, providing practical knowledge and exposure to real-world scenarios in investment banking.

This course is ideal for individuals looking to gain a comprehensive understanding of capital markets and the intricate operations within investment banking. It equips participants with the necessary skills to navigate this dynamic sector effectively.

Whether you're looking to start a new career or enhance existing skills, IMS Proschool's diverse offerings provide pathways to recognized qualifications and professional growth in the finance and accounting industries.
