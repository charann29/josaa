# Highest Salary Package in CSE at SRKR College Bhimavaram

SRKR Engineering College in Bhimavaram is a well-regarded institution, particularly for its Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) program. Many students are keen to understand the career prospects and salary potential after graduating from this program.

## Key Takeaways

*   The top salary package recently secured by a CSE graduate at SRKR College was 11.2 LPA.
*   Amazon was noted as a leading recruiter, offering this highest package.
*   The average salary package for CSE students at SRKR College stands at 5.6 LPA.
*   A significant number of students, approximately 75% of the batch, have already secured placements.

## Understanding Placement Opportunities

SRKR College has a strong track record of placing its engineering graduates. For the most recent placement cycle, over 1,200 students across various disciplines have secured job offers. Specifically for CSE, the placement rate is commendable, with about three-quarters of the students finding employment even before the cycle fully concludes.

## Top Salary Achievements

The most impressive salary package achieved by a CSE student at SRKR College was 11.2 LPA. This offer was extended by Amazon, a globally recognized technology giant, highlighting the caliber of opportunities available to top-performing students from the institution.

## Average Salary Insights

While the highest package grabs attention, the average salary provides a more holistic view of typical earnings. For CSE graduates at SRKR College, the average salary package is 5.6 LPA. This figure indicates a healthy earning potential for the majority of students completing the program.

## Leading Recruiters and Roles

SRKR College attracts a wide array of companies for campus recruitment. Prominent names include IT service leaders like TCS, Infosys, Cognizant, and Accenture. Beyond these, several product-based companies also participate, offering diverse roles. The most common positions offered to CSE graduates are typically in analyst roles, which involve data interpretation, system analysis, and problem-solving within technology domains.

## Internship Opportunities

Internships play a crucial role in preparing students for the professional world and often lead to pre-placement offers. Around 55% of students at SRKR College secure internships with various companies, including many of the top recruiters mentioned previously. These experiences provide valuable industry exposure and practical skills.

SRKR College continues to be a strong choice for students aspiring to build a career in Computer Science and Engineering, offering solid placement support and attractive salary prospects.
