# How Many Attempts Can One Make for IIM Indore Admission?

Aspiring to join IIM Indore, whether for its flagship MBA (PGP) or its unique Integrated Program in Management (IPM), is a goal for many. A common question that comes up is about the number of attempts allowed for admission.

## Key Takeaways

*   There is no official limit on the number of attempts for the CAT exam, which is essential for MBA (PGP) admission.
*   Similarly, there's no fixed attempt limit for the IPMAT exam, required for IPM admission.
*   The primary constraints for both programs are age and academic eligibility criteria, not the number of times you've appeared for the entrance tests.

## Understanding Attempts for the MBA (PGP) Program

For those aiming for IIM Indore's postgraduate MBA program, the Common Admission Test (CAT) is the gateway. The good news here is that there isn't any specific restriction on how many times you can take the CAT exam. You can appear for the CAT multiple times, year after year, as long as you meet the fundamental eligibility criteria set by IIMs, which typically include a bachelor's degree with a minimum percentage of marks.

This means your focus should be on preparing thoroughly and achieving a strong score rather than worrying about a cap on attempts. Each year, you have a fresh opportunity to demonstrate your aptitude through the CAT.

## Understanding Attempts for the Integrated Program in Management (IPM)

The Integrated Program in Management (IPM) at IIM Indore is a highly sought-after five-year course for students right after Class 12. Admission to this program is through the IPM Aptitude Test (IPMAT). Similar to the CAT, there's no official limit on how many times you can sit for the IPMAT.

However, there is a crucial age criterion that indirectly limits your attempts. To be eligible for the IPM program:

*   General, OBC, and EWS category candidates must be 20 years of age or less as of July 31st of the admission year.
*   SC, ST, and PwD category candidates receive a five-year age relaxation.

Therefore, while you can attempt the IPMAT every year, you must ensure you fall within these specified age brackets. Once you cross the upper age limit for your category, you will no longer be eligible to apply for the IPM program, irrespective of how many attempts you've made.

## Focus on Eligibility, Not Just Attempts

It's important to shift your focus from the number of attempts to consistently meeting and exceeding the eligibility requirements. For both the MBA and IPM programs, IIM Indore, like other premier institutions, looks for a strong academic record and performance in the respective entrance exams.

Ensure you carefully review the official IIM Indore admission notifications for the specific year you plan to apply. These documents will provide the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding academic prerequisites, age limits, and other essential criteria.

In summary, IIM Indore does not impose a fixed limit on the number of times you can attempt its entrance examinations. Your eligibility is primarily governed by age and academic qualifications, providing ample opportunities for dedicated aspirants.
