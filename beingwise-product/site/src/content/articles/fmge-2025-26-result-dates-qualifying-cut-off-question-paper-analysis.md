# FMGE 2025-26: Result, Dates, Qualifying Cut off, Question Paper Analysis, Pattern

The Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE) is a crucial licensing exam for Indian citizens and Overseas Citizens of India (OCIs) who have earned their primary medical qualification abroad and wish to practice medicine in India. Conducted by the National Board of Examinations in Medical Sciences (NBEMS), this exam is a mandatory step to register with the National Medical Commission (NMC) or a State Medical Council (SMC).

## Key Takeaways

*   **Mandatory for Foreign Graduates:** FMGE is a licensing exam, not an entrance test, essential for foreign medical graduates to practice in India.
*   **Qualifying Score:** Candidates must achieve a minimum of 150 out of 300 marks to pass the examination.
*   **Results and Certificates:** Results for the December 2025 session were released on January 29, 2026, with pass certificates distributed in person between February 23 and April 14, 2026.
*   **Exam Difficulty:** The December 2025 FMGE session was generally considered to be of moderate difficulty by students.

## FMGE 2025 December Session Overview

The FMGE December 2025 examination was successfully conducted on January 17, 2026. Following the exam, NBEMS released the results on January 29, 2026. These results included essential details such as the candidate's serial number, roll number, application ID, total score, and their overall result status. Only candidates who secured the minimum qualifying marks were declared successful.

## FMGE 2025 Certificate Distribution

For those who qualified in the FMGE December 2025 exam, the official schedule for collecting pass certificates was announced by NBEMS. Eligible candidates were required to collect their certificates in person from the NBEMS office located in Sector-09, Dwarka, New Delhi. The collection period spanned from February 23 to April 14, 2026. Candidates were advised to adhere strictly to the date and time specified on their individual Entry Slips for certificate collection.

## FMGE Qualifying Cut-off and Pass Rate

To be declared qualified in the FMGE, candidates must achieve a minimum score of 150 marks out of a total of 300. This threshold ensures that successful candidates possess the fundamental knowledge required to practice medicine in India. Historically, the pass rate for FMGE has been challenging. For instance, in 2023, out of 38,355 candidates, only 8,309 managed to pass, indicating a pass percentage of approximately 23%.

The FMGE is a comprehensive examination designed to assess a foreign medical graduate's understanding of various medical subjects.
