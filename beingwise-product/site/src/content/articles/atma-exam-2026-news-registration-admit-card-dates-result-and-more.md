# ATMA Exam 2026 News - Registration, Admit Card, Dates, Result & more

The AIMS Test for Management Admissions (ATMA) is a crucial entrance examination for students aspiring to pursue MBA or MCA programs at various institutions across India. Conducted multiple times throughout the year, ATMA offers flexibility for candidates to choose their preferred session.

## Key takeaways

*   ATMA is a national-level entrance exam for MBA and MCA admissions, administered by the Association of Indian Management Schools (AIMS).
*   The exam is held in a computer-based format at designated test centers.
*   ATMA is conducted multiple times annually, allowing candidates several opportunities to appear.
*   Over 750 management institutes across India accept ATMA scores for their postgraduate programs.

## ATMA Application Process

Applying for ATMA involves a few straightforward steps. Candidates typically need to register online, pay the application fee, and then fill out the detailed application form. It's essential to complete all sections accurately and upload any required documents as per the instructions on the official ATMA portal. Keep an eye on the official schedule for specific dates related to form release and submission deadlines for the 2026 sessions.

## ATMA Admit Card

The ATMA admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the examination. It contains vital information such as your name, roll number, photograph, exam date, time, and test center details. Candidates must download their admit card from the official ATMA website within the specified window. Remember to print it out and carry a valid photo ID to the examination center.

## ATMA Results

Once the ATMA examination is conducted, the results are typically released a few days later on the official portal. Candidates can check their scores by logging in with their credentials. The ATMA scorecard will display your overall score and sectional scores, which are crucial for applying to participating management institutes.

## Institutes Accepting ATMA Scores

ATMA scores are widely accepted by a large number of reputable management colleges and universities across India. These institutions offer various MBA and MCA specializations. Some of the well-known universities and colleges that consider ATMA scores include:

*   Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), Pune
*   University of Mumbai (MU), Mumbai
*   College of Engineering, Pune (COEP)
*   Christ University, Bangalore

It is always advisable to check the specific admission criteria and accepted entrance exams for the particular program and institute you are interested in, as these can vary.
