# MBA in Fashion Management: Course Fees, Admission 2026, Subjects, Syllabus, Salary in India

An MBA in Fashion Management is a postgraduate program designed to equip individuals with the business acumen needed to excel in the dynamic fashion industry. It blends core management principles with specialized knowledge of fashion, preparing graduates for leadership roles.

## Key takeaways

*   This two-year postgraduate degree focuses on the business side of fashion, covering areas like brand management, merchandising, and global marketing.
*   Admission typically requires a bachelor's degree and a valid score in common management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, CMAT, or MAT.
*   Graduates can pursue diverse roles like Brand Manager, Retail Manager, or Fashion Consultant, with average starting salaries ranging from INR 4 LPA to INR 10 LPA.
*   Top recruiters in this field include major fashion retailers, luxury brands, and e-commerce giants.

## Course Overview and Details

An MBA in Fashion Management is a specialized two-year program at the postgraduate level. It aims to develop a strong understanding of fashion business operations, from design conceptualization to market delivery. The curriculum is structured to provide expertise in managing fashion brands, retail operations, and supply chains in a global context.

## Admission 2026 and Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for an MBA in Fashion Management program in 2026, applicants generally need to meet specific eligibility requirements and successfully navigate the admission process. The primary criterion is holding a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. While some institutions may prefer candidates from fashion-related backgrounds, many accept graduates from any discipline.

The admission process typically involves:

1.  **Entrance Examination:** Candidates must appear for national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, CMAT, or MAT. Some colleges might conduct their own entrance tests.
2.  **Application Submission:** Filling out the application form for the desired colleges, usually available on their official websites.
3.  **Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates often undergo GD and PI rounds to assess their communication skills, industry awareness, and suitability for the program.

It is advisable to check the specific requirements of each college, as they may have additional criteria or minimum percentage requirements for undergraduate studies.

## Syllabus and Key Subjects

The MBA in Fashion Management syllabus is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of both business management and the unique aspects of the fashion industry. While the exact curriculum may vary by institution, common subjects include:

### Core Subjects

*   **Fashion Marketing and Branding:** Strategies for positioning fashion products and building strong brand identities.
*   **Retail Management:** Principles of managing fashion retail operations, including store management and customer experience.
*   **Merchandising and Buying:** Understanding product development, sourcing, and inventory management in fashion.
*   **Supply Chain Management in Fashion:** Logistics and operations specific to the fashion industry, from manufacturing to distribution.
*   **Fashion Forecasting and Trends:** Analyzing future fashion trends and their impact on design and business strategies.
*   **Global Fashion Business:** Exploring international markets, trade, and cross-cultural aspects of the fashion industry.
*   **Financial Management for Fashion:** Financial planning and analysis relevant to fashion businesses.
*   **Human Resource Management in Fashion:** Managing talent and teams within fashion organizations.

Elective subjects might cover areas like luxury brand management, e-commerce in fashion, sustainable fashion, or visual merchandising.

## Career Scope and Salary in India

An MBA in Fashion Management opens doors to a wide array of career opportunities within the vibrant and ever-evolving fashion industry. Graduates are well-prepared for management and leadership roles across various segments, including apparel, accessories, luxury goods, and retail.

### Popular Job Profiles

*   **Fashion Brand Manager:** Responsible for developing and executing brand strategies, ensuring brand consistency, and increasing market share.
*   **Retail Manager:** Oversees daily operations of fashion retail stores, manages sales teams, and focuses on achieving sales targets.
*   **Merchandiser:** Involved in planning product ranges, sourcing materials, negotiating with suppliers, and managing inventory.
*   **Fashion Consultant/Stylist:** Provides expert advice on fashion trends, personal styling, and image management to individuals or brands.
*   **Product Manager:** Manages the lifecycle of a fashion product, from conception and design to launch and market performance.
*   **Luxury Brand Manager:** Specializes in managing high-end fashion and luxury brands, focusing on exclusivity and premium positioning.

### Salary Expectations

The average salary for MBA in Fashion Management graduates in India typically ranges from INR 4 Lakh to INR 10 Lakh per annum. However, this can vary significantly based on factors such as the college, the specific role, the company, location, and individual experience. With experience, professionals in this field can command much higher salaries, especially in leadership roles or with established luxury brands.
