# IIM Ahmedabad Shortlist & Admission Criteria 2026-2028: PI Calls Out; Check CAT Cut Offs 2025

IIM Ahmedabad has announced the shortlist for its Post Graduate Programme (PGP/MBA) batch of 2026-2028, with interview calls now being released. Aspiring candidates should understand the revised admission criteria, including the updated CAT cut-offs and weightage given to various academic and interview components.

## Key Takeaways

*   The minimum qualifying CAT cut-off for the General category has significantly increased to 95 percentile for the 2026-2028 batch.
*   The admission process includes a two-stage selection: an initial CAT score-based shortlisting followed by an Analytical Writing Test (AWT) and Personal Interview (PI).
*   Academic performance in Class 10, Class 12, and Graduation holds substantial weight in the overall selection process.
*   Candidates who meet the CAT cut-offs must complete an online form on the institute's official website to participate in the PI round.

## IIM Ahmedabad Admission Criteria 2026-2028

IIM Ahmedabad's admission policy for the 2026-2028 MBA batch reflects a notable change in its minimum CAT cut-off. For the General category, the qualifying CAT percentile has been raised to 95, a significant increase from previous years. For candidates in the Persons with Disabilities (PwD) category, the qualifying cut-off is 85 percentile.

Meeting this initial CAT cut-off is the first step towards being considered for the next stages of the selection process. Once shortlisted, candidates will proceed to the Analytical Writing Test (AWT) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds, which are crucial for final selection.

## Rating of Academic Scores

IIM Ahmedabad places significant importance on a candidate's academic background. Your performance in Class 10, Class 12, and your undergraduate degree is evaluated and assigned a score. This academic rating contributes to your overall composite score, which determines your chances of receiving an interview call.

### Academic Rating Components:

*   **Class 10 Marks:** A weighted score is assigned based on your percentage in the secondary school examination.
*   **Class 12 Marks:** Your higher secondary examination percentage also carries a specific weight.
*   **Graduation Marks:** The percentage obtained in your bachelor's degree is a critical factor, with different weights for various academic disciplines (e.g., Engineering, Arts, Commerce, Medicine).

## Final Merit List for Admission

The final selection for admission to IIM Ahmedabad's PGP program is based on a comprehensive evaluation. This involves combining scores from your CAT performance, academic record, and the AWT-PI rounds. The weightage for each component is carefully calculated to create a final merit list.

### Components of Final Merit List:

*   **CAT Score:** Your performance in the CAT exam remains a significant factor.
*   **Academic Rating:** The scores derived from your Class 10, Class 12, and Graduation marks.
*   **Analytical Writing Test (AWT):** Your ability to articulate thoughts clearly and concisely in a written format.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** This assesses your communication skills, leadership potential, general awareness, and suitability for a management career.

Candidates are encouraged to check their PI status and complete the necessary online application on the official IIM Ahmedabad website to proceed with the admission process.
