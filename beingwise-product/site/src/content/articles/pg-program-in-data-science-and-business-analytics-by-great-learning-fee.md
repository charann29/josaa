# PG Program in Data Science and Business Analytics by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aiming to build a strong career in data science and business analytics, Great Learning offers a comprehensive Post Graduate Program. This program is designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in this rapidly growing field, combining theoretical understanding with practical application.

## Key takeaways

*   **Blended Learning Format:** The program offers a flexible learning approach, combining self-study with structured weekend classroom sessions.
*   **Industry-Relevant Curriculum:** The syllabus covers foundational concepts, advanced analytics techniques, and practical domain applications.
*   **Prestigious Certification:** Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Great Lakes Institute of Management (GLIM), Chennai.
*   **Financial Flexibility:** The program fee can be managed through convenient EMI options.

## Program Structure and Learning Mode

The PG Program in Data Science and Business Analytics is delivered in English, utilizing a blended learning model. This includes self-paced study materials, video lectures, and text-based resources. A key feature is the inclusion of campus-based or physical classroom sessions, held specifically on weekends. This structure caters to working professionals or those who prefer a mix of independent learning and direct interaction.

## Detailed Course Fee

Investing in a quality education is a significant decision. The total program fee for the PG Program in Data Science and Business Analytics is ₹2,95,000, plus applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST). To make this accessible, Great Learning also provides an Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) option, allowing participants to pay approximately ₹7,319 per month. This financial flexibility can help students manage the cost effectively.

## Comprehensive Syllabus Overview

The program's curriculum is thoughtfully designed, progressing from foundational concepts to advanced analytical techniques and real-world applications. It is divided into several modules, ensuring a holistic learning experience.

### Foundation Courses

*   **Python for Data Science:** This module covers Python programming fundamentals, data manipulation with NumPy and Pandas, data visualization using Seaborn and Matplotlib, exploratory data analysis, and data preprocessing techniques.
*   **Inferential Statistics:** Students will learn about probability, various probability distributions (Binomial, Normal, Uniform), sampling, the Central Limit Theorem, point estimation, confidence intervals, and hypothesis testing, including common statistical tests like z-test, t-test, Chi-square, and ANOVA.

### Analytics Techniques

This section delves into advanced methods crucial for data analysis:

*   **Predictive Modeling:** Focuses on learning from data, simple and multiple linear regression, regression metrics, assumptions, and statistical inferences.
*   **Machine Learning (Parts 1 & 2):** Covers logistic regression, classification metrics, Bayes Rule, Naive Bayes, KNN, Decision Trees (including impurity measures, pruning, and regression trees), ensemble methods like Random Forests, Bagging, Boosting (AdaBoost, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost), cross-validation, sampling techniques, regularization, data leakage, and hyperparameter tuning.
*   **Unsupervised Learning:** Introduces K-means clustering, hierarchical clustering, dimensionality reduction, and Principal Component Analysis (PCA).
*   **Introduction to SQL:** This module teaches database fundamentals, fetching, filtering, aggregating data, using in-built functions, joins, window functions, subqueries, and normalization.
*   **Time Series Forecasting:** Explores components of time series, naive forecasting, exponential smoothing, stationarity, ARIMA, and SARIMA models.
*   **Data Visualization using Tableau:** Covers dimensions, measures, data types, calculations, filtering, various visualizations, parameters, sets, blends, interactive dashboards, and storyboarding.

### Domain Applications of Analytics

This practical section applies analytical skills to specific business areas:

*   **Marketing and Retail Analytics:** Includes marketing terminology, RFM analysis, cluster analysis, churn rate prediction, market basket analysis, and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) modeling.
*   **Financial and Risk Analytics:** Focuses on credit risk modeling (PD Model, Altzman's, Discriminant Function), commercial and corporate credit risk, market risk, returns, and market risk optimization.

### Capstone Project

The program culminates in a Capstone project, allowing participants to apply their acquired knowledge to a real-world problem, consolidating their learning.

### Additional Modules (Self-Paced)

For those interested in further exploration, self-paced modules are available, including an introduction to data science (its history, industry transformation, underlying math and stats, and lifecycle) and web and social media analytics (digital data, consumer behavior, Google Trends).

## Certification Authority

Upon successful completion of all program requirements, participants will be awarded a certificate by the Great Lakes Institute of Management (GLIM), Chennai. This certification adds significant value and recognition to your professional profile in the data science and business analytics domain.

This program offers a robust pathway for individuals looking to establish or advance their careers in data science and business analytics, backed by a strong curriculum and a reputable certifying body.
