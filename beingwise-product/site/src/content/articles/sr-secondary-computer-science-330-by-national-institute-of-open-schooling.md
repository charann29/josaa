# Sr.Secondary: Computer Science (330) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students in their senior secondary years looking to build foundational computer science skills, the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) offers a comprehensive online course. Delivered through the Swayam platform, this program, titled "Sr.Secondary: Computer Science (330)," is designed to introduce learners to the core concepts of computing.

## Key takeaways

*   This 24-week program provides 10 credit points, focusing on practical computer skills and C++ programming.
*   It's a self-paced, online course delivered through video lectures and reading materials, suitable for senior secondary students.
*   Learners benefit from interactive elements like quizzes after each lesson and peer discussion forums.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from NIOS and Swayam.

## Course Overview

This specialized course aims to equip students with a solid understanding of computers and their various components. Coordinated by Radhika B. from NIOS, the curriculum covers a range of topics from basic computing principles to object-oriented programming concepts using C++, the fundamentals of the internet, and database management.

The self-study format allows students to learn at their own pace, supported by both video and text-based content. The medium of instruction is English, making it accessible to a wide audience.

## What You Will Learn

Through this program, you will develop essential computer skills vital for anyone considering a career in technology. The curriculum is structured to cover a broad spectrum of topics, including:

*   Basic Computing and Operating Systems
*   Office Productivity Tools
*   Programming in C++
*   Database Concepts and Management Systems
*   Web Designing using HTML
*   Fundamentals of Data Structure
*   Project Management and Entrepreneurship
*   Professional Communication

Each module includes multiple-choice questions, which students can attempt unlimited times to reinforce their understanding of the concepts.

## Duration and Credit Points

The Sr.Secondary: Computer Science (330) course spans 24 weeks, providing a structured learning experience over approximately six months. Upon successful completion, students will earn 10 credit points, which can be a valuable addition to their academic profile.

## Certification and Fees

Yes, a certificate of completion is available for this course. This certificate is jointly provided by Swayam and the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS), confirming your successful engagement with the course material. While the source does not specify any direct course or certificate fees, Swayam courses are generally offered free of charge for learning, with an optional fee usually applicable for taking the final proctored exam if a certificate is desired. Students should check the official Swayam platform for the most current information regarding any examination fees for certification.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Sr.Secondary: Computer Science (330) course on Swayam is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam platform.
2.  Look for the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically located in the top right corner of the page.
3.  Follow the prompts to either create a new account or log in with your existing credentials. You can usually register using your email ID or through various social media or government ID options.
4.  Once logged in, search for the "Sr.Secondary: Computer Science (330)" course and click to enroll.
