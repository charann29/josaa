# WBJEE Exam 2026: Form Correction (Started), Date, Syllabus, Eligibility, Pattern, Cutoff

For students aspiring to pursue engineering or pharmacy in West Bengal, the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination (WBJEE) is a crucial gateway. This state-level entrance exam is conducted by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) for admission into various undergraduate programs offered by government and self-financing institutions across the state.

## Key takeaways

*   The application correction window for WBJEE 2026 is currently open from April 10 to April 12, 2026.
*   Most details in the application form can be edited, except for the candidate's name, father's name, mother's name, and date of birth.
*   Successful applicants should download and keep a printout of their confirmation page, as it is essential for future processes like e-counselling.
*   Candidates with photograph or signature discrepancies must re-upload corrected images promptly to avoid application rejection.

## WBJEE 2026 Application Process Overview

The application process for WBJEE 2026 concluded on April 8, 2026, and involved several key steps:

1.  **Registration:** Candidates first registered by providing fundamental details such as their name, father's name, date of birth, and mobile number. It's vital to note that information entered at this stage (applicant's name, father's name, date of birth) cannot be changed later during the correction window.
2.  **Filling Application Form:** Applicants then proceeded to fill in specific details related to their eligibility, desired courses, preferred examination cities, and communication information.
3.  **Document Upload:** Essential documents, including a photograph and signature, needed to be uploaded in the specified format and size. Here are the guidelines:

| Particular  | Size         | Dimension & Format       |
| :---------- | :----------- | :----------------------- |
| Photograph  | 3kb - 100kb  | 4cm x 3cm & JPEG/JPG     |
| Signature   | 3kb - 30kb   | 4cm x 1.5cm & JPEG/JPG   |

4.  **Application Fee Payment:** To finalize the application, candidates paid the fee online using credit/debit cards or net banking. The application fees were ₹500 for General category candidates and ₹400 for SC/ST/OBC-A/OBC-B candidates.
5.  **Confirmation Page Printout:** After successful payment and submission, candidates were advised to download and print the confirmation page. This page is crucial for all future correspondence and especially for the e-counselling process.

### Fee Concession for PwD Candidates

Candidates with disabilities are eligible for a 40% reduction in the WBJEE 2026 application fees. To avail of this concession, PwD candidates must formally apply in writing to the Chairman, WBJEEB. This application, along with a copy of their WBJEE confirmation page and PwD certificate, needs to be submitted to the Board office. Refunds are processed after the Board receives the application.

## WBJEE Application Form 2026 Correction Window

The WBJEEB has opened a dedicated window for correcting errors in the WBJEE 2026 application form, running from April 10 to April 12, 2026. This is a critical opportunity for candidates to rectify any inaccuracies.

*   **Editable Fields:** Most details within the application form can be edited or corrected. However, certain core details such as the candidate's name, father's name, mother's name, and date of birth cannot be modified.
*   **Final Opportunity:** Candidates must ensure the accuracy of all corrections during this period, as no further chances will be provided to amend these details.

### How to Correct Your WBJEE 2026 Application Form

Follow these steps to make corrections:

1.  Log in to the official WBJEE portal using your WBJEE application number and password.
2.  Navigate to the correction section and edit all necessary details.
3.  Carefully verify all changes before submitting the corrected form.
4.  Download and save the updated confirmation page for your records.

### Addressing Photo/Signature Discrepancies

If the WBJEEB identifies any issues with your uploaded photograph or signature, you may receive an email or SMS notification. In such cases:

*   You must immediately upload the corrected image within 24 hours of receiving the notification.
*   Failure to do so could lead to the rejection of your WBJEE 2026 application.

### Retrieving Lost Application Details

If you have misplaced your application number or password:

*   **Forgot Application Number:** On the login window, click the 'Forgot Application No' option. You will then need to enter your Candidate Name, Father's Name, Mother's Name, Date of Birth, and a Security PIN to retrieve it.
*   **Forgot Password:** On the official website's login window, select the 'Forgot Password' link. You will be guided through steps to reset your password, typically involving your registered mobile number or email ID.

Stay vigilant with your application details and make sure all information is accurate for a smooth WBJEE experience.
