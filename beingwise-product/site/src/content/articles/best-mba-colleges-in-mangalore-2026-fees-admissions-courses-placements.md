# Best MBA Colleges in Mangalore 2026: Fees, Admissions, Courses, Placements

Mangalore offers a compelling choice for pursuing an MBA, especially for students seeking quality management education without the intense competition or higher costs often found in larger cities like Bangalore. With over 30 MBA colleges, including more than 20 private institutions and 4 government-owned ones, it provides diverse options for aspiring leaders.

## Key Takeaways

*   Mangalore has over 30 MBA colleges, offering a range of choices for students.
*   Fees are generally more budget-friendly compared to larger metropolitan areas.
*   Karnataka PGCET is a widely accepted entrance exam for MBA admissions in Mangalore.
*   Top specialisations include Human Resources, Sales & Marketing, Operations, and IT & Systems.

## Overview of MBA Colleges in Mangalore

For those considering an MBA in Mangalore, the city's institutions present a strong case. These colleges aim to provide robust management and leadership education, preparing students for the corporate world. The fee structures are often more accessible, making quality education attainable for a broader group of students. Many colleges in Mangalore accept scores from various entrance exams, with the Karnataka PGCET being particularly prominent.

### Top MBA Colleges in Mangalore

Some of the well-regarded institutions for an MBA in Mangalore include:

*   National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK) Surathkal
*   Mangalore Institute of Technology & Engineering (MITE)
*   NMAM Institute of Technology
*   NITTE University
*   A.J. Institute of Management
*   St. Aloysius Institute of Management
*   St. Joseph Engineering College

## Eligibility and Admissions for MBA in Mangalore

To be eligible for an MBA program in Mangalore, candidates typically need a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university. A minimum aggregate score, usually around 50% (45% for reserved categories), is often required. Final-year students are generally welcome to apply, provided they meet the degree requirements by the time of admission.

Admission to most MBA colleges in Mangalore relies on scores from various management entrance exams. While national exams like CAT, XAT, and MAT are accepted, the Karnataka PGCET and KMAT are particularly important for admissions within the state.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

*   Karnataka PGCET
*   KMAT
*   MAT
*   CAT
*   XAT
*   KCET

## Fee Structure and Specialisations

The fee ranges for MBA programs in Mangalore are quite varied, catering to different budgets. Many colleges offer programs with fees under INR 3 Lakh for the entire course, while some may go above INR 5 Lakh. This flexibility allows students and parents to choose an institution that aligns with their financial planning.

Common specialisations offered by MBA colleges in Mangalore include:

*   Human Resources
*   Sales and Marketing
*   Operations
*   IT & Systems
*   Finance

## Placements and Career Prospects

MBA graduates from Mangalore's colleges often find opportunities across various sectors. The institutions strive to provide strong placement support, connecting students with potential employers. While specific placement statistics vary by college, top recruiters often come from industries such as IT, banking, finance, retail, and manufacturing.

Many colleges also offer internship opportunities, which are crucial for gaining practical experience and making industry connections before graduation.
