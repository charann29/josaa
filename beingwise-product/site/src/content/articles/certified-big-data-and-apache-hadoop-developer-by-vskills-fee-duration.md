# Certified Big Data and Apache Hadoop Developer by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's data-driven world, the ability to manage and analyze vast amounts of information is a crucial skill. The Vskills Certified Big Data and Apache Hadoop Developer program offers a pathway for individuals to gain expertise in this rapidly growing field, equipping them to tackle complex data challenges.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a government-certified program, enhancing its credibility in the job market.
*   The certification holds lifelong validity, meaning no need for periodic renewals.
*   The course is delivered through self-paced online learning, offering flexibility.
*   It covers foundational to advanced concepts in Big Data and Apache Hadoop, preparing you for real-world applications.

## Course Overview

The sheer volume of data being generated globally is staggering, projected to double every two years. This explosion of information underscores the critical need for professionals skilled in Business Intelligence and Big Data Analysis. The Vskills Certified Big Data and Apache Hadoop Developer course is designed to meet this demand, starting with fundamental concepts and progressing to advanced Big Data and Hadoop techniques. You'll learn how to develop, manage, and process data on Hadoop, and build sophisticated Big Data applications, thereby future-proofing your career in a competitive landscape.

## Program Highlights and Offerings

This certification program comes with several valuable features aimed at supporting your learning journey and career growth. Key highlights include:

*   **Government Certification:** The program is certified by a government body, adding significant weight to your qualifications.
*   **Lifetime Validity:** Once certified, your credential remains valid indefinitely, eliminating concerns about expiration.
*   **Flexible Learning:** The course content is accessible online, allowing for self-paced study through video and text-based materials.
*   **Study Resources:** You'll receive lifelong access to e-learning content and a hard copy of the study material.
*   **Assessment Support:** Free mock tests and practice tests are provided to help you prepare for the certification exam.
*   **Support System:** A call-back facility is available for any queries or assistance you might need.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Certified Big Data and Apache Hadoop Developer program, candidates should have a foundational understanding of the Java programming language and object-oriented programming principles. This prerequisite ensures that participants can effectively grasp the technical concepts taught throughout the course.

To earn the certification, candidates must successfully pass an online examination. This exam consists of 50 questions, to be completed within 60 minutes. A minimum score of 50% (25 correct answers) is required to pass, with no negative marking for incorrect responses.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of the Certified Big Data and Apache Hadoop Developer course, you will gain an in-depth understanding of essential Big Data platforms and tools. The curriculum is designed to equip you with practical skills in:

*   Apache Hadoop
*   MapReduce
*   Hive
*   HDFS (Hadoop Distributed File System)
*   HBase
*   Yarn & Pig
*   Sqoop & Flume

This comprehensive knowledge will enable you to apply Big Data Hadoop techniques to solve real-world data challenges.

The fee for the Certified Big Data and Apache Hadoop Developer program is ₹3,499 (plus GST). This amount covers the training, e-learning access, study materials, and the certification exam.

Payment can be conveniently made using various options:
