# Chaturanga Anatomy Clinic - Yoga 101 by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to deepen their understanding of a fundamental yoga pose, the Chaturanga Anatomy Clinic offers a focused exploration. This course is designed to help practitioners refine their technique and prevent injuries by understanding the underlying anatomy.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn about the specific anatomy and biomechanics involved in Chaturanga Dandasana.
*   Understand common misalignments and how to correct them for safer practice.
*   Gain insights into shoulder mobility and stability, crucial for this pose.
*   Receive a certificate upon successful completion, issued by Udemy.

## Course Overview

This clinic is delivered through a self-study format, primarily utilizing video and text-based materials. The language of instruction is English, making it accessible to a wide audience. It's structured to provide a thorough understanding of Chaturanga, moving beyond just the physical movement to its anatomical foundations.

## Understanding the Curriculum

The course syllabus is thoughtfully designed to guide students through various aspects of Chaturanga. It begins with an introduction to the pose and general anatomical principles, followed by a visual recap to reinforce learning. A significant portion is dedicated to shoulder mobility and stability, vital for proper execution. Students will delve into the biomechanics and alignment specific to Chaturanga, learn about proprioception (the body's awareness of its position), and identify common misalignments and potential injuries. The clinic concludes with a slow-flow Chaturanga class to integrate the knowledge and a quiz to test understanding.

## Fee Structure and Certification

The fee for enrolling in the Chaturanga Anatomy Clinic is ₹449, which is a discounted rate from its original price of ₹999. Upon successful completion of the course, participants are eligible to receive a certificate. This certificate is provided by Udemy, confirming your engagement with and understanding of the course material.

## How to Apply

Since this is an online, self-paced course, the application process is straightforward. Prospective students can typically enroll directly through the Udemy platform. There are no specific application deadlines or entrance exams. Simply navigate to the course page on Udemy and follow the instructions to purchase and begin the course.

This clinic provides a valuable opportunity to enhance your yoga practice with a strong foundation in anatomical knowledge.
