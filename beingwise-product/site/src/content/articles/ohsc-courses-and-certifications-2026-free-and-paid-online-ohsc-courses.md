# OHSC Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online OHSC Courses

For students and professionals looking to enhance their skills or explore new career paths, Oxford Home Study Centre (OHSC) offers a diverse range of online certificate courses. These programs cater to various interests, from business management to creative fields, providing flexible learning opportunities.

## Key takeaways

*   OHSC provides both free and paid online certificate courses across multiple disciplines.
*   Many paid courses offer QLS Level 3 certification, indicating a recognised standard of learning.
*   Course durations are typically around 200 hours or 200 weeks, offering flexibility for learners.
*   Popular fields include business, beauty, digital marketing, and administrative skills.

## Exploring Business and Management Programs

OHSC features several valuable courses in the business and management domain, designed to equip learners with essential professional skills. The **Certificate in Business Management**, for instance, is a free program that delves into crucial aspects like strategic planning, team leadership, and human resource management. It covers topics such as creating effective organisational structures, understanding the key roles of an HR manager, and guiding teams through various development stages. Successful completion leads to a Certificate in Business Management from OHSC, which is also recognised as a Continuing Professional Development (CPD) activity.

Another option is the **Certificate in HR Management**, which aims to strengthen your abilities in this vital area.

## Administrative and Secretarial Skills

For those interested in administrative roles, OHSC offers specialised certificates. The **Advanced Certificate in Admin, Secretarial & PA at QLS Level 3** provides an insightful introduction to the dynamic world of administrative support. This program helps students understand responsibilities like reporting to management, drafting documents, data entry, and scheduling. Learners will also develop proficiency in crafting effective emails and professional letters. The syllabus covers document preparation, proofreading techniques, and the importance of document presentation. Upon completion, students receive both an Advanced Certificate from OHSC and a Certificate of Achievement from the Quality Licence Scheme. This course typically takes 200 hours and costs £99.

Similarly, the **Advanced Certificate in Office Management at QLS Level 3** offers comprehensive training for managing office environments, also priced at £99 for 200 hours of study.

## Beauty, Fashion, and Customer Service

OHSC also caters to creative and service-oriented fields. The **Certificate in Beauty Therapy & Salon Management QLS Level 3** is designed for individuals passionate about beauty but lacking formal training. It focuses on the fundamentals of modern beauty therapy, including health and safety regulations, professional appearance standards, and essential tools. The curriculum guides students through skin type identification, massage therapy, mask applications, and various cosmetic products. Graduates earn a Certificate in Beauty Therapy & Salon Management from OHSC and a Certificate of Achievement from the Quality Licence Scheme. This program spans 200 weeks and is priced at £385.

For aspiring designers, a free **Certificate in Fashion Design** is available, while those looking to excel in client interactions can consider the free **Certificate in Customer Services** and the **Certificate in Call Centre Training QLS Level 3**.

## Digital Marketing and Advanced Writing

In the digital age, specialised skills are highly sought after. OHSC offers the **Certificate in Creating Google Ads Campaign QLS Level 3**, a 200-hour course priced at £99, to help learners master online advertising strategies. For those aiming to refine their communication, the **Certificate in Advanced Writing Skills QLS Level 3** provides comprehensive training, also costing £385 for 200 hours.

OHSC's diverse portfolio of online certificates provides accessible pathways for skill development and career enhancement across a wide array of industries.
