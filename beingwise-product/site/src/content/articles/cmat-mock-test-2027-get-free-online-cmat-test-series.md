## Accessing the NTA-CMAT Official Mock Test

It is highly recommended that all candidates attempt the official NTA-CMAT mock test at least once. This provides invaluable experience with the online test environment and process, which can save precious time during the actual examination. To access the official NTA mock test, follow these steps:

*   Visit the official NTA website.
*   Navigate to the 'Student' section.
*   Click on the 'Mock Test' option.
*   Enter your designated username and password.
*   Click 'Login' to begin your mock test.

Experiencing the test interface beforehand can help you feel more comfortable and focused on exam day.
