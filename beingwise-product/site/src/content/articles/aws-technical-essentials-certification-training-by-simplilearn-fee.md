# AWS Technical Essentials Certification Training by Simplilearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This training program introduces you to the core concepts of Amazon Web Services (AWS), a leading cloud platform. It's designed to equip you with the fundamental skills needed to understand and work with AWS, preparing you for various roles in cloud computing.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn the basics of AWS cloud, including its vocabulary, concepts, and benefits.
*   Gain practical experience with the AWS Management Console and key services like RDS and EBS.
*   Benefit from a self-paced learning format with lifetime access to course materials.
*   Complete two industry-based projects and two simulation exams to solidify your understanding.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The AWS Technical Essentials Certification Training aims to provide a comprehensive introduction to AWS. The curriculum is structured into 14 modules, each covering different aspects of the platform. It's designed for flexibility, allowing participants to learn at their own pace through video and text-based content. The course emphasizes practical application, including hands-on projects using the AWS Management Console and two industry-based projects to simulate real-world scenarios.

Upon completion, you should be proficient in identifying various AWS services, understanding their deployment options, and leveraging them to meet business needs. The program also includes two simulation exams to help assess your readiness and reinforce learning.

## What You Will Learn

This program focuses on building a strong foundational understanding of AWS. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Familiarity with fundamental cloud computing processes.
*   Understanding core AWS concepts and terminology.
*   Proficiency in using the AWS Management Console and recognizing AWS compute services.
*   Ability to utilize Amazon RDS for client requirements.
*   Knowledge of AWS storage options, including Amazon S3 Bucket, and how to manage them.
*   Skill in using EC2 and EBS to fulfill client needs and execute projects on the AWS Console.

## Who Is This Program For?

The AWS Technical Essentials Certification Training is suitable for a wide range of individuals looking to enter or advance in the cloud computing field. While a basic understanding of any programming language can be helpful, it's not strictly required. The course is particularly beneficial for:

*   IT Developers working with AWS services.
*   SysOps Administrators.
*   IT Analysts and Engineers.
*   Anyone interested in gaining foundational knowledge about AWS and its technological advantages.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for the self-paced learning option of the AWS Technical Essentials Certification Training is ₹ 5,670. Payment must be completed to begin the program. Upon successful completion, a certificate is provided by the certifying authority, Simplilearn.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the AWS Technical Essentials Certification Training, you typically need to visit the official Simplilearn website. From there, you will find the specific course page where you can initiate the application process and complete the fee payment. The platform usually guides you through the necessary steps to register and access the course materials.

This program offers a valuable entry point into the world of cloud computing with AWS, providing practical skills and a recognized certification.
