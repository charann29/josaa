# UPMSP Board Exams 2026: Result (Soon), Dates & Time Table, Admit Card, Syllabus, Question Paper

The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Parishad (UPMSP) oversees the Class 10 and 12 board examinations for students across Uttar Pradesh. This guide provides essential information regarding the 2026 exams, from key dates to result announcements and preparation resources.

## Key takeaways

*   **Results Expected Soon:** The UPMSP Class 10 and 12 results for 2026 are anticipated in the third or fourth week of April 2026.
*   **Official Websites for Results:** Students can access their results on upresults.nic.in, results.upmsp.edu.in, and upmsp.edu.in.
*   **Exam Schedule:** The UP Board exams for both classes were conducted from February 18 to March 12, 2026.
*   **Step-Based Evaluation:** UPMSP has adopted a step-based evaluation system, meaning students may receive partial marks for correct steps in their answers.

## UPMSP Board Exam Results 2026

The evaluation of answer sheets for the 2026 UPMSP Class 10 and 12 board exams is currently underway and nearing completion. Following this, the board will finalize the result drafting process. An exact date and time for the announcement will be shared by UPMSP shortly before the results are released. Students are advised to regularly check the official UPMSP portals for the latest updates.

To check your results once they are declared, you will need your examination roll number and roll code. The results will be available on the following official websites:

*   upresults.nic.in
*   results.upmsp.edu.in
*   upmsp.edu.in

In addition to the main results, UPMSP will also release information regarding compartment and scrutiny results.

## UPMSP Board Exam Dates 2026

The UPMSP conducted the Class 10 and 12 examinations for the academic year 2026 between February 18 and March 12, 2026. The exams were held in a traditional pen-and-paper format, spread across two daily shifts:

*   **Morning Shift:** 8:30 AM to 11:45 AM
*   **Afternoon Shift:** 2:00 PM to 5:15 PM

## Admit Card, Syllabus, and Question Papers

While the main examinations for 2026 have concluded, information regarding admit cards for any supplementary or compartment exams will be released by UPMSP closer to those dates. For future reference, admit cards are typically made available on the official UPMSP website for students to download.

Students preparing for upcoming UPMSP exams (e.g., 2027 batch) should refer to the official syllabus released by UPMSP for Class 10 and 12. This syllabus outlines the topics and units that will be covered in the examinations. Accessing previous years' question papers and sample papers from the official UPMSP portal is also highly recommended for effective preparation, as they provide insight into the exam pattern and marking scheme.

## Important Announcements and Warnings

UPMSP has issued warnings to students and parents regarding fraudulent calls or messages offering to increase marks in exchange for money. Students and parents should be vigilant and rely only on official announcements from UPMSP. The board handles all evaluation and result processes transparently, and any such offers are illegitimate.

Stay informed by regularly visiting the official UPMSP website for all authentic news and updates regarding your board examinations.
