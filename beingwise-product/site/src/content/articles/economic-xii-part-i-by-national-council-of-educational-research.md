# Economic XII Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course offers a deep dive into the foundational concepts of economics typically covered in Class XII, designed to help students understand societal aspects and global affairs. Coordinated by Dr. Jaya Singh from the Department of Education in Social Sciences (DESS), National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT), New Delhi, it's accessible through the official Swayam learning platform.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is a self-paced, online program delivered through video lectures and text materials.
*   It spans 24 weeks and covers core microeconomics principles.
*   Successful completion, including assessments, leads to a certificate from NCERT and Swayam.
*   The entire learning experience and certification are provided free of charge.

## Course Content and Structure

This program focuses on microeconomics, introducing students to its fundamental principles. You'll explore consumer and producer behavior, delve into various market structures like perfect and imperfect competition, and understand concepts such as demand, supply, costs, and market equilibrium. The curriculum is structured into several chapters, each followed by an assignment, culminating in a final assessment.

The course is delivered entirely in English, using a blend of video tutorials, e-texts, and web resources to facilitate learning. It's designed for self-study, allowing students to progress at their own pace.

## Certification and Eligibility

Upon successfully completing all course lessons and achieving a score of at least 70% in the final assessment, students will be awarded a certificate. This certificate is jointly provided by NCERT and Swayam, validating the knowledge gained in the subject. There are no specific enrollment eligibility criteria beyond a willingness to learn Class XII level economics.

## Learning Experience and Support

The online platform offers an interactive learning environment. Beyond video lectures and e-texts, students can engage with self-assessment quizzes to gauge their understanding and participate in discussion forums to interact with peers and clarify doubts. These features are designed to enhance the learning experience and provide comprehensive support throughout the 24-week duration.

## How to Enroll

Enrolling in the Economic XII Part-I Certification Course is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official Swayam website.
2.  Look for the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, usually located in the top right corner of the page.
3.  If you're a new user, register for an account using your preferred credentials. If you already have an account, simply sign in.
4.  Once logged in, search for the "Economic XII Part-I" course and follow the prompts to enroll.

This course is an excellent opportunity for Class XII students to strengthen their understanding of economics principles, guided by expert faculty from NCERT, all from the convenience of their homes.
