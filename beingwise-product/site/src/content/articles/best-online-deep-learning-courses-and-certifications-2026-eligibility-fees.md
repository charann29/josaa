# Best Online Deep Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Scope

Deep learning, a powerful subset of artificial intelligence, is transforming industries worldwide. This guide explores some of the top online certification programs designed to equip you with the skills needed to thrive in this rapidly evolving field.

## Key Takeaways

*   Leading institutions like IISc Bangalore and IIIT Hyderabad offer comprehensive online deep learning programs.
*   Courses typically blend theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on project work.
*   Many programs include live online sessions, expert mentorship, and opportunities for campus visits.
*   Successful completion can open doors to roles at major global technology firms.

## PG Level Advanced Certification Programme in Deep Learning by IISc Bangalore

Offered by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore, this advanced certification is designed for professionals seeking to master deep learning concepts and their real-world applications. The program focuses on developing machine learning algorithms and understanding their interaction with hardware.

### Course Structure and Delivery

This 10-month online program is conducted entirely in English, with classes held exclusively on weekends to accommodate working professionals. Instruction is provided by IISc faculty, complemented by insights from industry experts who share their experiences in applying deep learning in various sectors. Participants also have the unique opportunity for a campus visit to IISc Bangalore, typically scheduled at the beginning or end of the course.

### Fees and Skills Covered

The fee for this program is ₹3,72,000, payable online. Key skills developed include a deep understanding of deep learning principles, big data analytics, data visualization, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

## PG Level Certification Course in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning by IIIT Hyderabad

IIIT Hyderabad offers a robust online PG-level certification in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This 9-month program is specifically tailored for tech professionals aiming to enhance their expertise in programming languages and essential tools within AI and ML.

### Program Highlights

The course dedicates three months to intensive hands-on projects, allowing participants to apply their learning in practical scenarios. The curriculum covers a wide array of crucial topics, delivered through live interactive lectures, mentored lab sessions, projects, and hackathons. Participants also benefit from opportunities for campus visits and networking with a strong peer group.

### Career Prospects and Fees

Upon successful completion, graduates are well-prepared for roles at prominent global organizations such as Accenture, Capgemini, Microsoft, and Deloitte. The program fee is ₹2,00,000. Skills covered include strong programming abilities, and in-depth knowledge of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.

## Post Graduate Certificate Program in Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning by MAHE

In collaboration with Jigsaw Academy, the Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) presents a Post Graduate Certificate Program in Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning. This blended/online program is ideal for individuals looking to gain mastery in Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Deep Learning in today's technologically advanced landscape.

### Curriculum and Practical Exposure

The curriculum emphasizes practical application, featuring extensive hands-on experience with GPU labs on real-world case studies and over 100 hours of cloud access. The program aims to develop participants' ability to plan and implement AI and Deep Learning solutions for diverse business challenges.

These programs offer excellent pathways for professionals to upskill and advance their careers in the exciting domain of deep learning and artificial intelligence.
