# Data Analytics from Scratch by Coding Invaders: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This guide explores the "Data Analytics from Scratch" certification course offered by Coding Invaders, designed for individuals aspiring to build a career in data analytics from the ground up. It covers essential details like the curriculum, learning format, fees, and the application process.

## Key Takeaways

*   An online, career-focused certification spanning 6.5 to 8 months.
*   Features a unique simulator-based learning approach with live sessions and personal mentorship.
*   No prior background in data analytics is required, making it suitable for beginners.
*   Offers comprehensive career support and a course completion certificate.

## Course Overview

The "Data Analytics from Scratch" program by Coding Invaders is an online certification designed to equip students with the foundational knowledge and practical skills necessary for a career as a data analyst. Delivered over approximately 6.5 to 8 months, the course emphasizes a hands-on, career-oriented approach, making it ideal for those looking to transition into the data analytics field without prior experience.

The learning experience is primarily simulator-based, complemented by live online sessions and the guidance of professional mentors. This blend ensures students not only grasp theoretical concepts but also gain practical experience in data processing, optimization, and visualization. Upon successful completion, participants receive a course certificate, signifying their acquired expertise.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a holistic understanding of data analytics, covering key tools and concepts essential for entry-level data analyst roles. Students will develop proficiency in:

*   **Big Data Concepts:** Understanding the fundamentals of large datasets.
*   **Data Visualization:** Techniques for presenting data clearly and effectively.
*   **Statistical Skills:** Core statistical methods relevant to data analysis.
*   **SQL Knowledge:** Mastering Structured Query Language for database management.
*   **Data Science Fundamentals:** An introduction to the broader field of data science.

The program specifically trains students in using industry-standard tools such as Python, Power BI, SQL, and Google Sheets, ensuring they are well-prepared for real-world analytical tasks.

## Program Structure and Learning Experience

Coding Invaders employs an innovative simulator format for this program, allowing students to learn by doing. The course structure includes:

*   **Simulator-Based Learning:** Practical application through simulated industrial projects.
*   **Live Sessions:** Interactive online classes for deeper understanding and Q&A.
*   **Personal Mentorship:** One-on-one guidance from experienced professionals.
*   **Assignments and Exercises:** Regular tasks to reinforce learning.
*   **Mid-Term Exams:** Assessments to track progress.
*   **Recorded Theory:** Access to theoretical content for self-paced study.
*   **Business Case Solving:** Applying analytical skills to solve real-world business problems.
*   **Career Development Track:** Dedicated support for job searching and career growth.

The medium of instruction is English, and the mode of delivery is a combination of video and text-based content, supporting both self-study and virtual classroom interactions.

### Detailed Syllabus Overview

The course begins with foundational tools and progresses to more advanced analytical techniques. Here's a glimpse into the initial modules:

**Google Sheets**

*   **Module 1: Basics**
    *   Preparation, Formatting, and Calculation
    *   Automation techniques
*   **Module 2: Functions**
    *   Key functions like IF, VLOOKUP, COUNTIF, SUMIF
*   **Module 3: Pivot Tables and Dashboards**
    *   Introduction to pivot tables and data analysis using them
    *   Creating comprehensive dashboards
*   **Projects:** Real-world projects such as creating reports for a retail store and a group project for a video content platform.

The curriculum then moves on to SQL, providing a strong base in database management and querying.

## Fee Structure and Certification

The program fee for the "Data Analytics from Scratch" certification is structured flexibly. While the standard program fee is Rs 8,143 per month, Coding Invaders often provides a sale offer, reducing the monthly cost to Rs 6,500. There is also a certification fee of Rs 11,490 to join the course.

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive a course completion certificate directly from Coding Invaders, validating their achievement and skills in data analytics.

## How to Apply

Prospective students interested in joining the "Data Analytics from Scratch" program can apply directly through the official Coding Invaders course website. The application process typically involves a few straightforward steps:

1.  Locate the specific course page for "Data Analytics from Scratch" on the Coding Invaders website.
2.  Click on the designated "Get Started" or application link.
3.  Fill in the required personal and contact details in the online form.
4.  Engage with a course counsellor who will guide you through the enrollment process and answer any further questions you may have.

This structured approach ensures that individuals from various backgrounds, including finance, sales, and marketing, can easily enroll and begin their journey toward becoming proficient data analysts.
