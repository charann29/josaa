# Data Science and AI with PwC Academy by upGrad: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in the rapidly expanding fields of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, the program offered by PwC Academy in collaboration with upGrad provides a robust learning pathway. This course is designed to equip both newcomers and experienced professionals with essential skills for the AI-driven world.

## Key takeaways

*   The program includes over 110 hours of live, interactive sessions, complemented by video and text-based self-study materials.
*   Participants will gain hands-on experience through 11 real-world projects and learn to use 17 different AI tools.
*   The curriculum covers fundamental to advanced topics, including Python, SQL, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Generative AI applications.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from upGrad and access to career support services.

## Who is this program for?

This Data Science and AI program is suitable for anyone aiming to enter or advance in data-centric roles. While no prior work experience is necessary, candidates should be pursuing or have completed a graduation degree in any discipline. The course is particularly beneficial for:

*   Aspiring Data Analysts
*   Future Data Scientists
*   Data Administrators
*   Artificial Intelligence Engineers

## What you will learn

The comprehensive curriculum is structured to provide a strong foundation and practical skills in various data science and AI domains. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Python Proficiency**: Mastering Python for data analysis, manipulation, and model development, including essential libraries like NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.
*   **Database Management**: Acquiring skills in SQL to effectively extract and manage data from relational databases.
*   **Machine Learning**: Understanding and implementing supervised and unsupervised machine learning algorithms, including linear and logistic regression, clustering, and basic text analytics.
*   **Deep Learning Fundamentals**: Gaining insights into neural networks and their applications.
*   **Generative AI**: Exploring the landscape of Generative AI and its practical applications in problem-solving.
*   **Data Analysis Tools**: Proficiency in tools like Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Power BI, and Tableau.
*   **Real-world Application**: Engaging in practical projects that simulate industry scenarios, allowing for direct application of learned concepts.

## Program Structure and Duration

The program is delivered through a blended learning approach, combining self-study modules with over 110 hours of live virtual classroom sessions. The content is presented via video and text-based materials, ensuring flexibility and depth of learning. The curriculum is divided into several key courses:

*   **Prep Content**: Covers essential mathematics for data science, business problem-solving, and an introduction to the Generative AI landscape.
*   **Python**: Focuses on coding prerequisites, data structures, control structures, and problem-solving, including using Generative AI.
*   **Python for DS**: Delves into fundamental Python libraries for data science, descriptive statistics, data preprocessing, and exploratory data analysis.
*   **DA Toolkit**: Teaches practical skills in Microsoft Excel, SQL/MySQL, and Microsoft Power BI.
*   **Statistics and Machine Learning**: Explores inferential statistics, hypothesis testing, various supervised and unsupervised machine learning algorithms, and applying Generative AI.
*   **Additional Content**: Includes advanced topics like Tableau, advanced machine learning, fundamentals of deep learning, and advanced natural language processing.
*   **Placement Training**: Offers soft skills and aptitude training to prepare participants for career opportunities.

## Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Data Science and AI with PwC Academy program is ₹70,000, which includes GST. To make the program accessible, EMI payment plans are available:

| Tenure (Monthly) | EMI (Approx.) |
| :--------------- | :------------ |
| 3                | ₹21,667       |
| 6                | ₹10,833       |

Upon successful completion of the course, participants will receive a certificate provided by upGrad.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Data Science and AI with PwC Academy program involves a straightforward application process:

1.  Visit the official program webpage.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Apply Now' button next to your preferred pricing plan.
3.  Complete and submit the application form with your details.
4.  Take the scholarship test, if applicable.
5.  Make the required fee payment to secure your enrollment in the program.

This program offers a structured path to gain in-demand skills and advance your career in the dynamic fields of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence.
