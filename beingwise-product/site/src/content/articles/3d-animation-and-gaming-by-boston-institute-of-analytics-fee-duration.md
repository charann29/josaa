# 3D Animation and Gaming by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Are you fascinated by the world of animated movies, video games, and visual effects? The 3D Animation & Gaming certification offered by the Boston Institute of Analytics (BIA) can equip you with the skills needed to turn your passion into a profession. This comprehensive program focuses on visual storytelling and practical application in the dynamic fields of graphics, animation, and gaming.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 4-month, 100-hour program leading to a certification from the Boston Institute of Analytics.
*   It offers a blended learning experience, combining virtual and physical classroom sessions, primarily on weekends.
*   The curriculum covers essential software like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Maya, Unity, and Unreal Engine.
*   The program is open to candidates from all academic backgrounds, including arts, science, commerce, and engineering.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The Graphics, Animation, and Gaming course is designed to provide a well-rounded education, preparing students for various roles in the industry. Through expert instruction from professionals and hands-on training, you will gain proficiency in a wide array of tools and techniques.

### What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to build skills progressively, covering:

*   Foundational tools and techniques in graphics and animation.
*   Image editing and retouching using Adobe Photoshop.
*   Typography and designing effective layouts.
*   Motion graphics and animation principles in Adobe After Effects.
*   3D modeling, texturing, shading, lighting, and rendering in Maya and 3D Max.
*   Game design fundamentals, environment creation, character animation, and interactivity using Unreal Engine.

This practical approach ensures that by the end of the program, participants will have a strong portfolio showcasing their creativity and technical abilities.

## Program Structure and Duration

This certification program spans 4 months and includes 100 hours of instruction. Classes are primarily held on weekends, making it accessible for both students and working professionals. The learning mode is flexible, offering both virtual classroom sessions and campus-based physical classes.

### Medium of Instruction and Delivery

All instructions are delivered in English. The course content is provided through a mix of video and text-based materials, complemented by live lectures.

## Eligibility Criteria

One of the notable features of this program is its inclusive eligibility criteria. Candidates from any academic stream—be it arts, science, commerce, or engineering—are welcome to apply. The certification is awarded upon successful completion of the course requirements.

## How to Apply

The application process for the 3D Animation & Gaming certification is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official Boston Institute of Analytics website.
2.  Locate the "Enquire Now" option and submit the form with your contact details.
3.  A representative from the institute will then reach out to you to guide you through the next steps.
4.  Upon payment of the course fees, your enrollment will be confirmed.

This program aims to equip individuals with the necessary skills to embark on a successful career in animation studios, advertising agencies, gaming companies, and other related creative industries.
