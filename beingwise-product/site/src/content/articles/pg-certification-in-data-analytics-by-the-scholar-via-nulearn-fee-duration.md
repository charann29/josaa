# PG Certification in Data Analytics by The Scholar via Nulearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Data analytics is a rapidly expanding field, and this Post Graduate Certification program, offered by The Scholar in collaboration with Nulearn, aims to equip individuals with the essential skills for a successful career as a data analyst. It's designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in the industry.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 6-month certification program focused on practical data analytics skills.
*   It includes comprehensive learning with 90+ hours of content and 25+ case studies.
*   The program offers 100% job assistance and dedicated support for career development.
*   Eligibility is open to any graduate with at least 50% marks; no prior programming experience is required.

## Course Overview and Highlights

This certification program is specifically designed to develop skilled data analytics professionals. It combines theoretical foundations with extensive practical experience, using real-world data problems. The curriculum covers essential tools such as Python, PowerBI, Tableau, and SQL, which are crucial for today's data analysis roles.

The program stands out with several key features:

*   **Duration:** A concise 6-month program.
*   **Learning Format:** Live interactive sessions, one-on-one mentorship with industry experts, and 24/7 access to the Learning Management System (LMS).
*   **Practical Experience:** Focus on building a strong project portfolio, including 25+ case studies and capstone projects, with real-time industry implementation.
*   **Career Support:** Offers 100% job assistance, AI-based resume building, and portfolio development using platforms like GitHub.
*   **Mentorship:** Sessions are led by highly experienced industry mentors.

## Who Can Benefit from This Program?

This program is suitable for both fresh graduates and working professionals who are keen to enter or advance in the data analytics field. It does not require any prior experience in programming or data analytics, making it accessible to a wide range of candidates. Individuals from various backgrounds, including IT engineers, software developers, lecturers, and existing data analysts, can find this course beneficial for upskilling or career transition. It also caters to middle and senior-level executives across different sectors looking to understand and apply data analytics in their roles.

## Eligibility Criteria and Certification

To be eligible for this PG Certification in Data Analytics, applicants must hold a graduate degree with a minimum of 50% marks. There is no requirement for prior programming knowledge or work experience, making it an excellent opportunity for newcomers to the field.

Upon successful completion of the program, including passing an entrance exam and fulfilling all course requirements, participants will receive a Post Graduate Certificate in Data Analytics. This certificate is awarded by The Scholar, in collaboration with Nulearn.

## Fees and Admission Process

The total fee for the Post Graduate Certification in Data Analytics program is ₹95,000. This amount covers the entire course and examination fees.

### How to Apply

Prospective students can apply for admission to the PG Certification in Data Analytics by visiting the official Nulearn website. The application process typically involves registering online and following the instructions provided there to complete the necessary steps for enrollment.

This program offers a robust foundation and practical skills for anyone aspiring to a career in the dynamic world of data analytics.
