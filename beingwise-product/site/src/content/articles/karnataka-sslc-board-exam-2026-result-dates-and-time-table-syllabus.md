# Karnataka SSLC Board Exam 2026: Result, Dates & Time Table, Syllabus, Question Papers

The Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (KSEAB) conducts the SSLC (Class 10) examinations, a crucial milestone for students in Karnataka. This page provides essential information for students and parents regarding the 2026 SSLC exams, including results, important dates, syllabus details, and how to access past question papers.

## Key takeaways

*   The Karnataka SSLC Exam 1 for 2026 was conducted from March 18 to April 2, 2026.
*   Results for the SSLC Exam 1 are expected to be announced in May 2026 on the official KSEAB websites.
*   SSLC Exam 2 for 2026 is scheduled to take place from May 18 to May 25, 2026.
*   Students can download sample question papers and the official timetable from the KSEAB website to aid their preparation.

## Karnataka SSLC Exam Dates and Time Table 2026

The KSEAB officially releases the examination schedule well in advance to give students ample time to prepare. The final timetable for the 2026 SSLC exams was published in November 2025 on the official KSEAB portal.

Here are the key dates for the 2026 examinations:

*   **SSLC Exam 1:** March 18 to April 2, 2026
*   **SSLC Exam 2:** May 18 to May 25, 2026

Students are advised to regularly check the official KSEAB website for any updates or changes to the examination schedule.

## Karnataka SSLC Result 2026

The KSEAB is anticipated to declare the results for the SSLC Exam 1 in May 2026. Students will be able to check their results online by visiting the official websites, such as `kseab.karnataka.gov.in` and `karresults.nic.in`.

To access your result, you will typically need to enter your registration number. It is advisable to download and print a provisional marksheet for immediate reference, though the official mark sheet will be issued by your school later.

For SSLC Exam 2, results are usually announced a few weeks after the exams conclude. Historically, results for Exam 2 and Exam 3 (if applicable) are declared in June and July, respectively, for subsequent attempts.

## Karnataka SSLC Syllabus and Exam Pattern 2026

The KSEAB outlines the detailed syllabus and examination pattern for all subjects on its official website. The syllabus covers various subjects including languages, Mathematics, Science, and Social Science. It is crucial for students to thoroughly understand the prescribed syllabus to ensure comprehensive preparation.

The exam pattern generally includes a mix of objective and subjective questions. Students should refer to the official KSEAB resources for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding question paper structure, marking scheme, and internal assessment weightage.

## Karnataka SSLC Question Papers and Preparation Tips

Practicing with previous years' question papers and official sample papers is an excellent way to prepare for the SSLC exams. The KSEAB makes sample question papers available on its official website, which can help students understand the format, types of questions asked, and time management required during the actual examination.

**Preparation Tips:**

*   **Understand the Syllabus:** Go through the entire syllabus for each subject to know what topics need to be covered.
*   **Create a Study Schedule:** Plan your study time effectively, allocating sufficient time to each subject and topic.
*   **Practice Regularly:** Solve sample papers and previous year question papers to get familiar with the exam pattern and improve speed and accuracy.
*   **Revision is Key:** Regularly revise all subjects to retain information better.
*   **Stay Healthy:** Ensure you get adequate sleep, eat nutritious food, and take short breaks to avoid burnout.

## Karnataka SSLC Admit Card 2026

The admit card for the SSLC exams is a mandatory document that students must carry to the examination hall. The KSEAB releases the admit cards, which schools then download through their login portals and distribute to students. The admit card contains vital information such as the student's name, roll number, examination center, and schedule. Students should collect their admit cards from their respective schools well before the examination dates and carefully check all the details mentioned on it.

This comprehensive guide should help students and parents navigate the Karnataka SSLC Board Exam 2026 process with greater clarity and confidence.
