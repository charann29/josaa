Your privacy is important to BeingWise. This policy explains how we handle the personal information you share with us when you use our website or mobile applications.

## Key takeaways

*   You can browse most of our content without sharing any personal details.
*   We collect information primarily when you register or use specific services like counselling or application forms.
*   We use your data to personalize your experience, offer relevant guidance, and process your requests.
*   We are committed to protecting your information and using it responsibly.

## What Information We Collect

When you visit BeingWise, you can explore a wealth of information about exams, colleges, and courses without needing to provide any personal data. However, to access certain features and services, registration is required. During registration, or when you use specific tools, we may ask for the following:

*   **Basic Contact Details:** Your name, email address, and phone number.
*   **Demographic Information:** Your current location or residential address.
*   **Educational Background:** Details about your current education level, year of passing, and academic interests (e.g., preferred study location, courses, or exams).
*   **Career Interests:** Information about your work experience (if any), occupation, and areas you wish to upskill in.

### For Online Coaching and Counselling

If you opt for our online coaching or counselling services, we might collect additional information to provide tailored recommendations, especially where admissions cut-offs are influenced by these factors:

*   Your state and specific location.
*   Socio-economic profile, gender, and religion.
*   Your scores and ranks in various entrance exams (e.g., results from NTA exams).
*   Date of birth.
*   Enrolment numbers for specific exams or institutions.

### For Application Forms

When you use our platforms to apply to colleges or universities, through a common application form or other means, we may gather further details to assist in processing your application:

*   The specific course, category, or stream you are applying for, along with your preferred study location.
*   Gender, nationality, and date of birth.
*   Parent/guardian names and occupations.
*   Academic records from Class 10 and 12 (or undergraduate level, if applicable), including marks, school name, and board.
*   Work experience details, if relevant, such as previous companies, years of experience, and job roles.

## How We Collect Information

We gather personal information through fair and lawful methods. This can be directly from you, or from an authorized representative, or sometimes from a third party we've partnered with to offer services. We also collect data related to your usage patterns on our platforms, such as your IP address, device type, operating system, and how you interact with different pages and products. This includes test results and transactional information.

## Financial Information

If you purchase any products or services from BeingWise, we will require financial information, such as bank account details, credit card, debit card, or other payment information, solely for the purpose of processing your payment securely.

## How We Use Your Information

The information we collect helps us provide you with a more personalized and effective experience. We use your data to:

*   Create and manage your BeingWise account.
*   Offer customized recommendations for courses, colleges (referencing NIRF rankings where relevant), and career paths.
*   Process your applications for colleges or universities.
*   Provide online coaching and counselling services.
*   Improve our platforms and develop new features based on user engagement and preferences.
*   Communicate with you about updates, new services, and relevant opportunities.

We are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information and using it only in ways that support your educational and career journey.
