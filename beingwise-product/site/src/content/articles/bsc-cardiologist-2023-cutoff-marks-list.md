# BSc Cardiac Technology 2023 Cutoff Marks

For students aiming to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Cardiac Technology, understanding the cutoff scores is a crucial step in their admissions journey. These scores, primarily based on the NEET entrance exam, determine eligibility for various programs across India.

## Key Takeaways

*   Admission to BSc Cardiac Technology programs largely depends on NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) scores.
*   The minimum qualifying score for general category candidates was 147.
*   Candidates under the General-PH (Physically Handicapped) category needed a minimum score of 129.
*   These cutoffs are essential for gauging your eligibility and planning your applications.

## Understanding NEET Scores for Cardiac Technology

NEET is the gateway for many medical and allied health science courses in India, including BSc Cardiac Technology. Universities and colleges offering this specialized program typically use NEET scores as a primary criterion for shortlisting candidates. While some institutions may conduct their own entrance exams or interviews, a strong NEET performance significantly boosts your chances.

## Category-Wise Cutoff Details

The cutoff marks are not uniform for all candidates; they vary based on the reservation categories. For the 2023 admissions cycle, the National Testing Agency (NTA) specified the following minimum qualifying scores:

*   **General Category:** Candidates belonging to the general category needed to secure at least 147 marks in the NEET exam to be considered eligible for BSc Cardiac Technology programs.
*   **General-PH (Physically Handicapped) Category:** For candidates under the General-PH category, the minimum qualifying score was set at 129.

These cutoffs are the baseline; admission to top colleges will often require significantly higher scores due to intense competition.

## Factors Influencing Cutoff Marks

Several factors contribute to the fluctuation of cutoff marks each year:

*   **Number of Applicants:** A higher number of students appearing for NEET can drive up the cutoff scores.
*   **Difficulty Level of NEET:** If the NEET exam is perceived as more challenging in a particular year, the cutoffs might slightly decrease, and vice-versa.
*   **Total Available Seats:** The number of seats offered by colleges for BSc Cardiac Technology programs directly impacts how many students can be admitted, thereby affecting the cutoffs.
*   **College Popularity:** More sought-after institutions generally have higher cutoff scores due to increased demand.

## Preparing for Future Admissions

For prospective students looking towards future admissions cycles, it's advisable to aim for scores well above the minimum qualifying cutoffs. Consistent preparation for the NEET exam, focusing on Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, is key. Regularly checking official updates from the NTA and individual college admission portals will provide the most accurate and current information regarding application processes and specific requirements.

Understanding these cutoff marks is a vital first step in your journey towards a rewarding career in cardiac technology.
