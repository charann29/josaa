# Graphic Design Course by Upskillist: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to dive into the world of visual communication or enhance their existing design skills, Upskillist offers an online Graphic Design course. This program aims to equip learners with essential design principles and practical software knowledge.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a fully online, self-paced course offered by Upskillist, in collaboration with Austin Peay State University.
*   The program spans 16 weeks and includes 32 lessons and assignments across four modules.
*   Participants will gain proficiency in industry-standard Adobe software like Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.
*   Upon successful completion, a globally recognized certification is awarded.

## Course Overview and What You Will Learn

This 16-week online graphic design program focuses on both foundational and advanced aspects of visual design. You will explore core design elements and principles, learning how they contribute to effective communication.

The curriculum covers practical applications, including the creation of corporate identity elements such as logos, business cards, and email signatures. A significant part of the course is dedicated to mastering Adobe software. You'll learn to use Photoshop for advanced picture manipulation and advertisement design, Illustrator for creating custom lines and shapes, and InDesign for magazine layouts and organizing visual content with grids.

Beyond software, the course delves into the art of typography as a powerful communication tool and design element. It also covers advanced color theory and print terminology, preparing you for professional design projects.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The course is delivered through a blend of video and text-based content, allowing for flexible, self-study learning. It is structured into four main modules, each building upon the previous one. The program includes:

*   Online learning materials
*   Examinations
*   Online assessments
*   A globally recognized certificate upon completion

### Modules and Key Topics

#### Module 1: Diploma in Graphic Design

*   Introduction to Graphic Design
*   Elements and Principles of Design
*   Adobe Illustrator basics and Logo Design
*   Flyer Design using Illustrator
*   Photo Manipulation in Adobe Photoshop
*   Movie Poster Design in Photoshop
*   Introduction to Adobe InDesign
*   Brochure Design in InDesign

*   Mood Board Creation

## Eligibility and Certification

There are no specific prior eligibility criteria mentioned for enrolling in this course, making it accessible to a wide range of individuals, from beginners to those looking to upskill. 

To earn the certification from Upskillist, certified by Austin Peay State University, candidates must successfully complete all 32 lessons and assessments. This includes passing the exams integrated into each of the four modules.

## Fees and How to Apply

While the exact fee structure is not detailed, Upskillist typically operates on a monthly subscription model, allowing learners to pay as they progress. The course also offers a free trial, enabling you to experience some content before committing financially.

To enroll in the Graphic Design Course:

1.  Visit the official Upskillist website.
2.  Navigate to the 'Design' category within their course catalog.
3.  Select the 'Graphic Design' course to open its dedicated page.
4.  Click on the 'Start your free trial' button.
5.  Create an account by providing your name, email, and phone number.
6.  Sign in to begin your free trial and then proceed with monthly payments to continue with the full course.

No separate application form is required for admission; the process is straightforward through the Upskillist platform.

This program is an excellent opportunity for aspiring graphic designers or professionals seeking to enhance their digital design capabilities with industry-relevant tools and techniques.
