# JEST 2026 Exam: Exam Date (Apr 12), Admit Card (OUT), Result & Counselling

The Joint Entrance Screening Test (JEST) is a crucial national-level examination for students aspiring to pursue PhD or Integrated PhD programs in specific science disciplines. This page provides essential information about JEST 2026, including key dates and the admission process.

## Key takeaways

*   The JEST 2026 admit card has been released by IISER Tirupati and is available for download.
*   The JEST 2026 examination is scheduled for April 12, 2026.
*   JEST is a gateway for PhD and Integrated PhD admissions in Physics and Theoretical Computer Science at various participating institutions.
*   Candidates must carry their admit card to the exam hall; failure to do so will prevent them from taking the test.

## JEST 2026 Admit Card

The admit card for JEST 2026 was released on April 7, 2026, and will be available for download until April 12, 2026. Only registered candidates can access their admit cards online. It is mandatory to present your admit card at the examination centre to be allowed to sit for the test.

### How to Download Your JEST 2026 Admit Card

To download your admit card, follow these steps:

*   Visit the official JEST website.
*   Look for the admit card download link.
*   Enter your login credentials, which typically include your registration number and password.
*   Once logged in, download your admit card.
*   Carefully check all details on the admit card, such as your name, roll number, and exam centre information.

## What is JEST?

The Joint Entrance Screening Test (JEST) is an annual screening examination conducted by the Science & Engineering Research Board (SERB). It serves as a qualifying test for admission to PhD and Integrated PhD programs across various participating institutes. JEST specifically covers two subjects: Physics and Theoretical Computer Science. The eligibility criteria for admission to these programs may vary among the different participating institutions.

## JEST 2026 Result and Counselling

Following the examination, the JEST 2026 results are tentatively expected in April 2026. Candidates who qualify the JEST exam will then be eligible to apply for counselling and admission to the participating institutes. Each institute will have its own admission process, which may include interviews or further screening rounds. It is advisable to refer to the official JEST website and the websites of the individual participating institutes for detailed information on post-result procedures and counselling schedules.
