# CAT 2025 Result (OUT): Your Guide to Scores, Cutoffs & Next Steps

The Common Admission Test (CAT) 2025 results were officially announced on December 24, 2025. This crucial scorecard, which includes both overall and sectional percentiles, is your gateway to various MBA programs across India.

## Key Takeaways

*   The CAT 2025 scorecard was released on December 24, 2025, and remains accessible for download from the official IIMCAT website until December 31, 2026.
*   Following the results, IIMs initiated their shortlisting process for Personal Interview (PI) rounds, which are currently underway.
*   A remarkable 12 candidates achieved a perfect 100 percentile in CAT 2025, with an additional 26 candidates scoring 99.99 percentile.
*   Many MBA colleges are still accepting applications for their full-time programs, making this a critical period for prospective students.

## How to Download Your CAT 2025 Scorecard

Accessing your CAT 2025 result is straightforward. You will need your login credentials to download the scorecard from the official IIMCAT portal. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1.  Visit the official IIMCAT website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'CAT 2025 scorecard download' link.
3.  Enter your registered User ID and Password.
4.  Navigate to the 'scorecard' tab to view and download your result.

This process is similar for mobile users; simply open your web browser, go to the IIMCAT website, and follow the same steps.

## Understanding Your CAT Scorecard Details

The CAT scorecard is more than just a number; it provides a comprehensive overview of your performance. It typically includes:

*   Your personal details (name, CAT registration number).
*   Your sectional scores and percentiles for Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC), Data Interpretation and Logical Reasoning (DILR), and Quantitative Ability (QA).
*   Your overall raw score and overall percentile.
*   Important instructions regarding the validity of the scorecard.

It's crucial to review all details carefully to ensure accuracy.

## What Happens After Your CAT Result?

Once your CAT results are out, the focus shifts to the admission process for various MBA programs. IIMs and other top B-schools use your CAT percentile, along with other factors like academic record, work experience, and diversity, to shortlist candidates for further rounds. These typically include:

*   **Written Ability Test (WAT):** An essay-writing task to assess your communication and analytical skills.
*   **Group Discussion (GD):** A discussion among a group of candidates on a given topic to evaluate teamwork, leadership, and communication.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** A one-on-one interaction to gauge your personality, motivation, and suitability for the program.

Keep an eye on the official websites of your target institutions for specific deadlines and instructions regarding their admission processes.

Your CAT 2025 result is a significant milestone; now it's time to strategically plan your next steps towards securing admission to your dream MBA program.
