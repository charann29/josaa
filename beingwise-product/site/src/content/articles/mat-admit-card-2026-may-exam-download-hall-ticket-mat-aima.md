# MAT Admit Card 2026 (May Exam): Download Hall Ticket @mat.aima.in

The MAT admit card is a crucial document for all candidates appearing for the Management Aptitude Test. This page provides essential information about downloading your hall ticket for the May 2026 examination, along with important dates and instructions to ensure a smooth exam day experience.

## Key takeaways

*   The MAT May 2026 admit card for the Paper-Based Test (PBT) will be released on May 28, 2026.
*   The admit card for the Computer-Based Test (CBT) will be available on June 11, 2026.
*   Candidates must carry a physical printout of their session-specific admit card to the test center.
*   Entry to the examination hall will not be permitted without a valid admit card.

## How to Download Your MAT 2026 Admit Card

Downloading your MAT admit card is a straightforward process through the official AIMA-MAT website. Follow these steps carefully:

1.  Visit the official AIMA-MAT website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Login' tab.
3.  Enter your registered email ID and password in the respective fields.
4.  Click 'Login' to access your personal dashboard.
5.  Find and click on the tab specifically for the admit card.
6.  Your MAT 2026 admit card will appear on the screen.
7.  Download and save the admit card, then take a clear printout.

It's important to remember that only a hard copy of the admit card will be accepted at the test center. Digital copies or photocopies are not considered valid for identification.

## Details on Your MAT Admit Card

Your MAT 2026 admit card contains vital information about you and your examination. Please ensure all details are correct. The admit card will typically include:

*   Candidate's Name
*   Date of Birth
*   Registration Number
*   Candidate's Photograph
*   Exam Date and Time
*   Reporting Time at the Center
*   Allotted Test Center Address
*   Test Center Barcode
*   Important Exam Day Instructions

### Correcting Errors on Your Admit Card

If you find any discrepancies or errors on your MAT admit card, such as incorrect personal details or exam information, it is crucial to contact the AIMA helpdesk immediately. Do not wait until the last minute to report such issues.

## Documents to Carry on Exam Day

On the day of the MAT examination, along with your admit card, you must carry specific identification documents. These are essential for verification purposes:

*   **MAT 2026 Admit Card:** A clear printout.
*   **Valid Photo ID Proof:** This can be your Aadhaar Card, Passport, Driving License, PAN Card, or Voter ID Card. Ensure the ID is original and not a photocopy.

## Exam Day Instructions

To ensure a smooth and fair examination process, candidates must adhere to the following instructions:

*   Arrive at the test center well before the reporting time mentioned on your admit card.
*   Do not carry any electronic gadgets such as mobile phones, calculators, smartwatches, or any other unauthorized items into the examination hall.
*   Follow all instructions given by the invigilators strictly.
*   Maintain decorum and silence in the examination hall.
*   Do not engage in any unfair means or malpractice; this will lead to immediate disqualification.

By following these guidelines, you can ensure a hassle-free experience on your MAT exam day. Wishing you all the best for your examination!
