# National Stock Exchange of India Ltd Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online National Stock Exchange of India Ltd Courses

The National Stock Exchange of India (NSE) offers a range of courses and certifications designed to equip students and professionals with essential financial market knowledge and skills. These programs, developed in collaboration with leading institutions, cover various aspects of finance, from market fundamentals to advanced technologies like Artificial Intelligence.

## Key Takeaways

*   NSE partners with renowned institutions like IMI Kolkata and TCS iON to deliver specialized financial market programs.
*   Courses are available for both freshers and experienced professionals, covering areas from derivatives to AI in finance.
*   Many programs offer joint certificates and additional regulatory certifications upon successful completion.
*   The curriculum often includes practical, application-based learning, preparing participants for diverse roles in the financial sector.

## Post Graduate Certificate in Financial Market (PGCFM)

This comprehensive 11-month program is a collaborative effort between the International Management Institute (IMI) Kolkata and NSE Academy Ltd. Structured across three trimesters, the PGCFM aims to provide participants with a deep understanding of financial markets. The training sessions are conveniently held on weekends, making it accessible for working professionals.

Upon successful completion, including a capstone project and examinations, participants receive a joint Post Graduate certificate along with eight additional regulatory certificates. Graduates of this program are well-prepared for roles in banking institutions, consulting firms, and credit rating agencies, with potential positions such as relationship manager, risk manager, content developer, and operations manager.

*   **Duration:** 11 Months
*   **Fee:** ₹1,50,000
*   **Key Skills:** Financial Management

## Advanced Program in AI for Financial Markets

Designed for professionals looking to leverage cutting-edge technology in finance, this 6-month live online program focuses on Artificial Intelligence for financial markets. It is delivered through TalentSprint, an NSE group company, and benefits from the extensive expertise of NSE, recognized as India’s largest stock exchange and the world's largest derivatives exchange in 2020.

This program is particularly suitable for individuals with experience in investment banking, commodity markets, hedge funds, and stock markets. It aims to strengthen foundational knowledge in mathematics and programming, enabling participants to unlock the transformative power of AI technologies within the financial domain.

*   **Duration:** 6 Months
*   **Fee:** ₹2,00,000
*   **Key Skills:** Artificial Intelligence, DeepTech Learning

## Financial Derivatives

Offered in collaboration with TCS iON, the Financial Derivatives online course provides an in-depth understanding of derivative instruments, their characteristics, and the strategies involved in trading. This career-focused certification program is designed for individuals seeking to enhance their expertise in commercial and financial aspects, with a typical completion time of five to six months.

The course employs experimental learning methodologies and benefits from the guidance of academic and industry experts, including Dr. Priti S. Aggarwal, Joint Director of the Indian Institute of Banking and Finance, and Ramesh Lakshman, proprietor of Ramesh Lakshman and Company. Successful participants earn six credits and a digital certificate, gaining valuable insights into the banking and trading industry with a strong emphasis on derivatives trading.

*   **Duration:** 5-6 Months
*   **Fee:** ₹11,800
*   **Key Skills:** Financial Knowledge, Economics, Risk Management, Financial Management, Accounting Proficiency, Knowledge of Stocks

## Financial Markets and Services

Another valuable offering through the TCS iON digital learning hub, the Financial Markets and Services online course is tailored to develop a strong foundational knowledge of financial markets and their various functions. This program helps students understand how financial markets operate and their crucial role in the economy.

Participants will gain insights into the structure, instruments, and participants within the financial ecosystem, preparing them for entry-level roles or to further their studies in finance. The course content is designed to be accessible and comprehensive, providing a solid grounding for anyone interested in a career in finance.

These programs offer excellent pathways for both aspiring and seasoned professionals to enhance their financial market acumen and career prospects.
