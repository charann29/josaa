The Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is a crucial step for many aspiring MBA students. As CMAT 2026 approaches on January 25, 2026, understanding the exam day guidelines is vital for a smooth experience and to avoid any last-minute stress.

## Key Takeaways

*   Always carry two printed copies of your CMAT 2026 admit card, with a recent passport-sized photograph affixed to each.
*   Bring an original, valid photo identification proof to the test center.
*   Arrive at the examination venue well before the designated reporting time.
*   Familiarize yourself with the list of prohibited items to ensure you don't accidentally carry anything that could lead to disqualification.

## Essential Documents to Carry

On the day of the CMAT 2026 exam, ensure you have the following critical items:

*   **CMAT 2026 Admit Card:** You must bring two printed copies of your admit card, printed on A4-sized paper. Remember to affix a recent passport-sized photograph on each copy. This photograph should ideally be the same one you used in your CMAT application form.
*   **Valid Photo ID:** An original, government-issued photo identification proof is mandatory. Acceptable IDs typically include an Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Driving License, Passport, or Voter ID.
*   **Passport-sized Photograph:** Carry an extra passport-sized photograph, preferably the one uploaded during your application, to paste on the attendance sheet at the exam center.
*   **PwD Certificate (if applicable):** Candidates under the Persons with Disability (PwD) category must carry their original PwD certificate and, if applicable, a scribe certificate issued by a competent authority.

## Reaching the Exam Center and Verification

Timeliness is paramount on exam day. Plan your travel to ensure you reach the examination center according to the reporting time mentioned on your admit card. This allows ample time for the necessary security checks and verification procedures.

Upon arrival, cooperate fully with the test center staff during the identification and verification process. This ensures a smooth entry for everyone and helps maintain the integrity of the examination.

## Prohibited Items and Activities

To ensure a fair and secure testing environment, the National Testing Agency (NTA) strictly prohibits certain items and activities inside the examination hall. Candidates found with any of these items may face disqualification.

**Do NOT carry:**

*   Any electronic devices, including mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, iPads, or any other gadget.
*   Watches of any kind.
*   Study material, notes, or any paper other than the admit card.
*   Bags, wallets, or purses.
*   Eatables or beverages.
*   Any kind of jewelry or metallic items that could trigger security alarms.

Remember that an on-screen timer will be available on your computer system to help you manage your time during the exam, so there's no need for a personal watch.

## During the Exam

The CMAT is a computer-based test, conducted under CCTV surveillance. While attempting the paper, focus on your screen and manage your time effectively. It's often a good strategy to aim to complete the test about 10 minutes before the official end time, allowing you a crucial window to review your answers.

Rough work will be done on sheets provided at the center. Once the exam concludes, ensure you drop all rough sheets into the designated drop box before exiting the test center. This is a mandatory step.

Following these guidelines meticulously will help you focus on your performance and ensure a hassle-free CMAT experience.

## CMAT City Intimation Slip

The NTA issues a City Intimation Slip a week or so before the admit card. This slip informs candidates of the city where their test center will be located, though it does not provide the exact address. This is particularly helpful for candidates from rural or distant areas, allowing them to make necessary travel and accommodation arrangements in advance. The city intimation slip is typically available for download for a few days only; once the admit card is released, it is no longer required as the admit card contains the full center details.
