# Post Graduate Program in Electric Vehicle Design by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those keen to enter the rapidly expanding electric vehicle (EV) industry, Great Learning offers a Post Graduate Program in Electric Vehicle Design. This program is structured to equip participants with essential skills and knowledge in this cutting-edge field.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is delivered entirely online, featuring a blend of self-study materials and virtual classroom sessions.
*   It covers a comprehensive curriculum, from foundational EV concepts to advanced topics like battery technology and embedded systems.
*   Successful completion leads to a certificate from Great Learning, validating your expertise.
*   A significant capstone project allows participants to apply all learned concepts to design a complete EV system.

## Program Overview and Structure

This Post Graduate Program focuses specifically on the design aspects of electric vehicles. It's delivered through an online format, which includes both video and text-based learning materials, complemented by virtual classroom interactions. The primary language of instruction is English, making it accessible to a wide audience.

The curriculum is thoughtfully organized into several modules, progressing from preparatory knowledge to advanced design principles. This structured approach ensures a thorough understanding of all critical components involved in EV development.

## Detailed Syllabus

The program's syllabus is designed to provide a deep dive into various facets of electric vehicle design:

### Preparatory Module

This initial module sets the stage by introducing the fundamental differences between electric vehicles and traditional automobiles. It also familiarizes learners with essential simulation tools and the basics of Embedded C programming, which are crucial for later modules.

### Module 1: Electric Vehicle Foundations

Here, participants explore the core principles of electric vehicles and their supporting infrastructure. Topics include vehicle dynamics, methods for estimating battery and motor capacity, understanding EV architecture, and overall system design and performance considerations.

### Module 2: Electric Motors and Power Converters

This module delves into the specifics of electric motor design for propulsion, including traction motors. It also covers the design of motor controllers, power converters, and important aspects like braking mechanisms for electric motors, motor drives, and thermal management.

### Module 3: Battery Technology and Control Systems

Focusing on energy storage, this section covers battery technology and the design of battery packs. Learners will also study Battery Management Systems (BMS) and explore the design of fuel cells and capacitors relevant to EVs.

### Module 4: Designing Embedded Systems for Electric Vehicles

This advanced module introduces automotive modeling and embedded systems, including the AUTOSAR standard. It covers Printed Circuit Board (PCB) design, C programming concepts, embedded C programming, and model-based development and testing specific to automotive embedded systems.

### Module 5: Quality Assurance and Troubleshooting

The final taught module addresses critical aspects of quality assurance and safety procedures in EV design. It also covers troubleshooting techniques and provides insights into after-sales support for electric vehicles.

### Capstone Project

A cornerstone of the program, the capstone project requires participants to integrate all concepts learned across Modules 1 to 5 to design a complete electric vehicle system. This practical application solidifies understanding and provides valuable hands-on experience.

## Fees and Certification

While specific fee details are not provided in the source, it is important for prospective students to check the official Great Learning website for the most current information regarding program costs. Upon successful completion of all modules and the capstone project, participants will receive a certificate from Great Learning, acknowledging their achievement in the Post Graduate Program in Electric Vehicle Design.

This program offers a robust pathway for individuals looking to gain specialized expertise in the rapidly evolving electric vehicle sector.
