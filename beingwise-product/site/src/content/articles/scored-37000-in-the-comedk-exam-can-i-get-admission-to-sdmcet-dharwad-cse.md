# I scored 37000 in the COMEDK exam. Can I get admission to SDMCET, Dharwad, CSE course in the 2nd and 3rd rounds?

Understanding your chances for admission to a competitive course like Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at a reputable institution like SDM College of Engineering & Technology (SDMCET), Dharwad, with a COMEDK rank of 37,000 is crucial. Let's break down the possibilities for the upcoming counseling rounds.

## Key Takeaways

*   A COMEDK rank of 37,000 makes direct admission to CSE at SDMCET, Dharwad, very challenging, even in later rounds.
*   Historically, CSE closing ranks at SDMCET are significantly lower, typically falling within the 15,000 to 18,000 range.
*   While later counseling rounds (2nd and 3rd) sometimes see slight rank adjustments, they are unlikely to bridge such a large gap for a high-demand branch.
*   Exploring other engineering branches at SDMCET or CSE at other COMEDK-affiliated colleges with higher cutoff ranks might be more realistic.

## Understanding SDMCET CSE Cutoffs

SDMCET, Dharwad, is a well-regarded institution, and its Computer Science and Engineering program is consistently in high demand among applicants. This popularity translates into very competitive cutoff ranks in the COMEDK entrance exam. Based on past trends and official COMEDK counseling data, the closing ranks for CSE at SDMCET generally fall within the range of 15,000 to 18,000.

Your rank of 37,000 is considerably higher than these historical closing ranks. This indicates a significant difference from what has typically been required for admission to this specific course at SDMCET.

## The Reality of Later Counseling Rounds

It's a common misconception that later counseling rounds (like the 2nd and 3rd) offer a dramatically easier path to admission, especially for highly sought-after branches. While it's true that some seats might become vacant due to candidates withdrawing or opting for other colleges, the rank relaxation in these subsequent rounds is usually marginal for programs as competitive as CSE.

For a rank difference as substantial as between 15,000-18,000 and 37,000, it is highly improbable that the cutoffs would shift enough to accommodate your rank, even if a few seats open up. These later rounds primarily benefit candidates who are just outside the initial cutoff range, not those with a significant rank disparity.

## Exploring Alternative Options

Given the competitive nature of CSE at SDMCET with your current rank, it's wise to consider alternative strategies during COMEDK counseling:

### Other Branches at SDMCET

While CSE might be out of reach, SDMCET offers other excellent engineering disciplines. Branches like Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) or Information Science and Engineering (ISE) often have slightly higher closing ranks compared to CSE. These programs also provide strong career prospects and quality education. It's worth checking their historical cutoff ranks to see if they align more closely with your COMEDK score.

### CSE at Other COMEDK Colleges

Many other colleges participate in COMEDK counseling. Some of these institutions might have higher closing ranks for their CSE programs, making them a more viable option with a rank of 37,000. Thoroughly research the previous year's closing ranks for CSE in various COMEDK-affiliated colleges. You can find this data on the official COMEDK counseling portal, which publishes detailed rank lists for different rounds.

### Participate in All Rounds

Regardless of the initial assessment, it is always advisable to participate actively in all COMEDK counseling rounds. Fill your choices strategically, including aspirational colleges and courses, but also realistic options. Sometimes, unexpected movements can occur, though it is important to manage expectations for top-tier branches.

While admission to CSE at SDMCET, Dharwad, with a COMEDK rank of 37,000 appears very difficult, carefully exploring all your options during the counseling process is the best approach to secure a good engineering seat.
