# Generative AI: Elevate Your Data Science Career by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's rapidly evolving technological landscape, understanding generative AI is becoming crucial for data science professionals. This program, offered through Coursera and developed by IBM, provides a comprehensive introduction to this cutting-edge field, designed to enhance your existing data science skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   An intermediate-level program focusing on generative AI for data science.
*   Approximately 12 hours of content, with a flexible schedule for self-paced learning.
*   Covers practical applications of generative AI tools like GPT 3.5 and ChatCSV.
*   Offers a shareable certificate upon successful completion of the paid track.

## What This Program Covers

This certification course delves into the core concepts of generative AI and its direct applications within data science. You'll explore how these powerful AI models can be used to query and create data, providing a new dimension to data analysis and manipulation. The curriculum is designed to help you understand real-world scenarios where generative AI is effectively deployed.

The program offers a blend of learning materials, including video lectures, readings, assignments, and practical labs. These hands-on components are crucial for mastering the application of generative AI techniques in developing and refining machine learning models. The content is delivered in English, with additional language options available to cater to a wider audience.

## Who Is This Program For?

This program is ideal for both aspiring professionals and those already working in data-centric roles who wish to expand their expertise into generative AI. It's particularly beneficial for:

*   Data Analysts
*   Data Scientists
*   Data Engineers
*   Big Data Engineers

If you're looking to update your skillset and stay competitive in the fast-paced world of data and AI, this course offers valuable insights and practical knowledge.

## Program Structure and Learning Experience

The course is structured for self-study, allowing you to progress at your own pace. While the total learning effort is estimated at around 12 hours, you can spread this out according to your availability, with a suggestion of approximately 10 hours per week. The learning is primarily delivered through video and text-based modules.

Highlights of the learning experience include:

*   Guidance from industry experts who have contributed to the course content.
*   Seven assessments to test your understanding and application of concepts.
*   Access to videos, readings, and practical plugins to enhance learning.

## Fees and Certification

The Generative AI: Elevate Your Data Science Career program offers flexible payment options depending on your desired access level and duration. You can choose to audit the course content for free, which allows you to access most of the learning materials without a certificate.

For those seeking a shareable certificate and full access to graded assignments, subscription plans are available:

| Subscription Duration | Monthly Fee (INR) |
| :-------------------- | :---------------- |
| 1 month               | 1,699             |
| 3 months              | 1,166             |
| 6 months              | 867               |

The certificate is provided by Coursera upon successful completion of the paid experience, either during or after the audit period.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Generative AI: Elevate Your Data Science Career program is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official course page on Coursera.
2.  Click on the "Enroll" button.
3.  You can sign up for a new account using your basic information, or log in directly with your Google, Facebook, or Apple account if you prefer.
4.  If you already have a Coursera account, simply log in with your existing credentials.

This will grant you access to the course content, and you can then choose your preferred enrollment option for certification.

By completing this program, you'll be well-equipped to leverage generative AI tools and techniques, making a significant impact in your data science career.
