*   UGAT 2026 application forms are now available online.
*   The deadline to submit your application and fee is June 6, 2026.
*   The exam is a gateway to various undergraduate management courses like BBA, BCA, BHM, and integrated MBA programs.
*   AIMA conducts UGAT to help students secure admissions into participating institutions across India.

## Understanding UGAT 2026

UGAT is a national-level aptitude test conducted by the All India Management Association (AIMA). It serves as a common admission test for students aspiring to join a range of undergraduate programs, including Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM), and integrated MBA courses offered by numerous colleges and universities.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for UGAT 2026, candidates generally need to have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination from a recognized board. Specific percentage requirements may vary depending on the participating institution and the program you are applying for. It's always advisable to check the eligibility criteria of your target colleges in addition to the general UGAT requirements.

## UGAT 2026 Application Process

The application process for UGAT 2026 is entirely online. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you complete your registration:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Navigate to the official AIMA website.
2.  **Register/Sign Up:** Create a new account by providing basic details such as your name, email ID, and phone number.
3.  **Log In:** Use your newly created credentials to log in to the applicant portal.
4.  **Fill the Application Form:** Carefully fill in all the required personal, academic, and contact details in the online form.
5.  **Upload Documents:** You will need to upload scanned images of your recent photograph and signature. Ensure these images are clear and within the specified size limits (up to 50 KB for each).
6.  **Pay the Application Fee:** The application fee for UGAT 2026 is INR 750. You can pay this through:
    *   **Online Mode:** Using a Credit Card, Debit Card (ATM Card), or Net Banking.
    *   **Demand Draft:** Obtain a Demand Draft of INR 750 in favor of 'All India Management Association,' payable at Delhi, and then proceed with online registration by entering the DD details.
7.  **Submit and Print:** After completing all sections and paying the fee, submit your application. It is highly recommended to take a printout of the final submitted form for your records.

### Documents Required for Registration

Before you begin the online application, ensure you have the following ready:

*   A valid and active email ID.
*   Scanned image of your photograph (up to 50 KB).
*   Scanned image of your signature (up to 50 KB).
*   Credit Card/Debit Card (ATM Card) or Net Banking details for online payment, or Demand Draft details if opting for that payment method.

## UGAT 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

While specific details for 2026 will be confirmed by AIMA, UGAT generally tests candidates on several key areas. The exam typically consists of multiple-choice questions covering:

*   **General English:** Reading comprehension, vocabulary, grammar, and sentence correction.
*   **Numerical and Data Analysis:** Basic mathematics, data interpretation, and quantitative aptitude.
*   **Reasoning and General Intelligence:** Logical reasoning, analytical reasoning, and critical thinking.
*   **General Knowledge:** Current affairs, business awareness, and static general knowledge.

Candidates are advised to refer to the official AIMA UGAT bulletin for the most accurate and detailed syllabus and exam pattern closer to the exam date.

Preparing well for UGAT 2026 can open doors to numerous undergraduate management programs. Good luck with your application!
