# ATMA Books 2026: Check out best books for exam preparation

Preparing for the ATMA 2026 exam effectively requires the right study materials. Choosing the best books is a crucial first step to understanding the ATMA syllabus and exam pattern thoroughly.

## Key takeaways

*   The ATMA exam is a popular entrance test for MBA and PGDM programs in over 700 B-schools across India.
*   While generally considered less challenging than exams like CAT or XAT, a strategic approach with suitable books is essential for a good score.
*   The ATMA question paper is divided into six sections covering Analytical Reasoning, Verbal Skills, and Quantitative Skills.
*   Utilizing NCERT Mathematics books for Class 9 and 10 can be a strong foundation for the Quantitative Skills section.

## Understanding the ATMA Exam Structure

The AIMS Test for Management Admissions (ATMA) is a widely recognized MBA entrance examination. It serves as a gateway to numerous management programs, including MBA and PGDM, offered by hundreds of institutions nationwide. The exam assesses candidates across three main areas: Analytical Reasoning, Verbal Skills, and Quantitative Skills. Each of these areas is further divided into two parts within the question paper.

## Best Books for Analytical Reasoning (Part 1 and 2)

This section tests your ability to think critically and solve problems using logic. Key topics often include Critical Reasoning, Syllogisms, Statement and Inference, Arguments, Data Sufficiency, and Arrangements. To excel here, consider these highly recommended books:

| Name of the book                  | Author/ Publisher          |
| :-------------------------------- | :------------------------- |
| A Modern Approach to Verbal Reasoning | R.S Aggarwal               |
| Analytical Reasoning              | M.K. Pandey                |
| Verbal Reasoning                  | Bhupendra Kumar Singh      |
| A New Approach to Reasoning       | B.S. Sijwali and S. Sijwali |
| Multi-Dimensional Reasoning       | Mishra Kumar and Dr. Lal   |
| Logical and Analytical Reasoning  | K. Gupta                   |

## Best Books for Verbal Skills (Part 1 and 2)

This segment focuses on your command of the English language. It typically covers Reading Comprehension, Grammar, Vocabulary, Sentence Completion, Phrases and Idioms, and Para Jumbles. Here are some excellent resources to sharpen your verbal abilities:

| Name of the book                                                   | Author/ Publisher |
| :----------------------------------------------------------------- | :---------------- |
| Word Power Made Easy                                               | Norman Lewis      |
| High School English Grammar and Composition                        | Wren and Martin   |
| Objective General English                                          | R.S. Aggarwal     |
| How to Prepare for Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension for the CAT | Arun Sharma       |
| The Pearson Guide to Verbal Ability and Logical Reasoning for the CAT | Nishit K. Sinha   |

## Best Books for Quantitative Skills (Part 1 and 2)

This section primarily evaluates your mathematical aptitude. It includes topics such as Arithmetic, Geometry, Graphs, Number Systems, Averages, Ratio and Proportion, and Profit and Loss. Building a strong foundation in this area is crucial. Consider these books for effective preparation:

| Name of the book                               | Author/ Publisher |

| Quantitative Aptitude                          | R.S. Aggarwal     |
| Quantum CAT                                    | Sarvesh K. Verma  |
| How to Prepare for Quantitative Aptitude for CAT | Arun Sharma       |

| NCERT Mathematics Books for Class 9 and 10     | NCERT             |

Thorough preparation with the right materials can significantly boost your performance in the ATMA 2026 examination.
