# RISE Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online RISE Courses

RISE offers a range of online postgraduate programs and certifications designed to help students and working professionals enhance their skills in high-demand fields. These programs often feature collaborations with industry leaders, providing practical, career-focused learning experiences.

## Key Takeaways

*   RISE provides online postgraduate programs in areas like Cybersecurity, Data Science, AI & Machine Learning, and Finance & Accounting.
*   Many courses are developed in partnership with prominent industry firms such as ThriveDx SaaS, KPMG, and Grant Thornton.
*   Programs typically include live sessions, hands-on projects, case studies, and mentorship, aiming for practical skill development.
*   While most programs are paid, they lead to certifications that can significantly boost career prospects in their respective fields.

## Postgraduate Program in Cybersecurity

The Post Graduate Program in Cybersecurity from RISE is an online course focused on various aspects of cyber defense. It's designed for graduates and professionals, even those without a prior IT background, who are looking to enter the cybersecurity domain. This program, created in collaboration with ThriveDx SaaS, an Israeli ed-tech company, covers essential topics such as network forensics, anti-forensics, and ethical hacking, alongside practical training.

The curriculum emphasizes hands-on projects to build real-world cybersecurity capabilities. Upon successful completion, participants receive a course certification accredited by CertNexus for CyberSec First Responder®. The program spans 12 months.

## PG Program in Data Science

RISE WPU, in partnership with KPMG, offers an 11-month online PG Program in Data Science. This certification is tailored for both students and working professionals aiming for advanced learning in data science. It includes a 2-month capstone project, providing in-depth exposure to machine learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Python, R programming, and data visualization.

Learners will gain proficiency in developing and visualizing data using tools like MySQL and Tableau. The program's syllabus consists of 11 modules, with dedicated industry and capstone projects. It features 144 hours of live sessions, 32 case studies, and over 10 sessions with industry experts, fostering peer learning through group projects. Graduates are prepared for roles such as Data Scientists, Data Analysts, and Data Engineers.

## PG Program in Finance and Accounting

Developed jointly by RISE and Grant Thornton, the PG Program in Finance and Accounting is an 11-month online course, including a one-month project. This program is suitable for both recent graduates and working professionals interested in the financial services industry. Taught by professionals from Grant Thornton, it offers a blend of live lectures, video content, assessments, mentorship, and internship opportunities.

Participants gain experiential learning and practical knowledge in finance and accounting, covering contemporary topics like cryptocurrency, fintech, and blockchain. The course aims to provide a comprehensive understanding and relevant skills for the industry.

## PG Program in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

RISE, in collaboration with KPMG in India, presents a 10-month online PG Program in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This course is structured to equip learners with the necessary skills and knowledge in these rapidly evolving technological fields. It focuses on preparing individuals for careers that leverage AI and Machine Learning applications, covering core concepts and practical implementations.

RISE's online programs offer structured pathways to acquire specialized skills, often backed by industry collaborations, to help individuals advance their careers in competitive sectors.
