# CBR Network Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online CBR Network Courses

CBR Network, through the Swayam platform, offers valuable online courses focused on the application of technology for individuals with disabilities. These programs aim to build understanding and skills in assistive technologies and digital tools for rehabilitation and inclusion.

## Key Takeaways

*   CBR Network provides specialized online courses via Swayam, focusing on technology for people with disabilities.
*   The flagship course, "Information & Communication Technology (ICT) in Rehabilitation," is a free, six-month program.
*   These courses emphasize how digital tools and assistive technologies can enhance independence and inclusion.
*   Learning materials include e-texts, presentations, podcasts, and activities, with support available through discussion forums and email.

## Understanding ICT in Rehabilitation

The Certificate Course in Information & Communication Technology (ICT) in Rehabilitation is designed to educate participants on the specific technological needs of people with special needs and disabilities. The program thoroughly explains how ICT can be strategically used to promote and accelerate their educational and socio-economic integration into society.

This course highlights how various assistive technologies empower individuals with disabilities to lead more independent lives. It covers applications that facilitate access to essential services like transportation, employment, and education. Furthermore, it addresses how these technologies can assist with daily activities such as shopping, using ATMs, or enjoying entertainment like movies. A significant focus is also placed on the needs of underserved communities in rural and tribal areas, as well as in developing nations.

## Course Structure and Learning Experience

The Certificate Course in Information & Communication Technology (ICT) in Rehabilitation spans six months. Learners receive comprehensive digital content designed for self-study. This includes a variety of resources such as e-texts, detailed presentations, informative podcasts, and interactive activities. To support the learning process, discussion forums are available for posting questions and engaging with peers. Additionally, learners can seek clarifications directly from instructors via email.

## Skills Developed

Participants in the ICT in Rehabilitation course will gain crucial skills, particularly a strong understanding and practical knowledge of various digital tools. This equips them to identify, adapt, and apply technological solutions to address the challenges faced by individuals with disabilities, fostering greater independence and inclusion.

## Course Fees and Platform

Currently, the Certificate Course in Information & Communication Technology (ICT) in Rehabilitation is offered free of charge. These programs are delivered through Swayam, an official initiative of the Government of India designed to provide accessible and affordable education to all. This makes specialized learning in this vital field accessible to a broad audience without financial barriers.

These online offerings from CBR Network provide an excellent opportunity to gain expertise in a field dedicated to leveraging technology for social good and inclusion.
