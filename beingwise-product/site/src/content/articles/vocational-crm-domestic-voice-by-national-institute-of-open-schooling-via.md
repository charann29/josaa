# Vocational: CRM Domestic Voice by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students aspiring to build a career in customer service, the CRM Domestic Voice course, offered through Swayam and coordinated by the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS), provides a valuable foundation. This program focuses on developing essential skills for managing customer relationships and effective communication in a professional setting.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 24-week, self-paced vocational course designed for school-level learners.
*   It carries 10 academic credits and is delivered entirely online through video lectures and PDF study materials.
*   The curriculum covers crucial aspects of Business Process Outsourcing (BPO), communication, personality development, and fundamental computer skills.
*   While the course provides comprehensive knowledge, it does not offer a formal certificate upon completion.

## Course Overview

This online program introduces learners to the core concepts of Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) and emphasizes the critical role of strong communication and personal growth in the workplace. The course aims to enhance basic language proficiency by exploring components of speech, alongside practical skills for handling customer interactions.

Coordinated by Radhika B. from the National Institute of Open Schooling, this skill-focused course is designed to cultivate robust customer relationship management abilities. It helps students understand the importance of quality customer service, identify different customer types, and efficiently resolve customer queries.

Beyond communication and customer service, the curriculum also provides foundational knowledge of computer systems, including internet domain structures, webpages, and common internet services like social networking platforms. This practical orientation helps students improve their overall technical and presentation skills.

## What You Will Learn

The CRM Domestic Voice Training course is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of various topics crucial for a career in customer support. Upon completing the program, you will gain insights into:

*   Key aspects of Business Process Outsourcing (BPO).
*   Effective communication strategies in a professional environment.
*   Steps involved in personal development and growth.
*   The significance of team building and collaboration.
*   Methods for delivering excellent customer service.
*   Strategies for achieving a healthy work-life balance.
*   An overview of computer applications and operating systems.
*   The fundamental workings of internet systems.

## Course Structure and Delivery

This course spans 24 weeks and is equivalent to 10 academic credits. The learning experience is entirely self-directed, utilizing a blend of video lectures from experts and downloadable PDF content. The medium of instruction is English, ensuring accessibility for a wide range of learners.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the CRM Domestic Voice course is a straightforward process. You can apply by following these steps:

1.  Navigate to the official Swayam platform for the course.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in/Register" option, typically found in the top-right corner of the page.
3.  A new window will appear, prompting you to create a new account. Provide your desired username, password, and a valid email address.
4.  After submission, you will receive a verification code in your registered email inbox.
5.  Enter the verification code to confirm your credentials and finalize the application process.

This course is an excellent starting point for individuals looking to develop practical skills for the customer service industry.
