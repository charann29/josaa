# Chemistry XI Part-II by National Council of Educational Education and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online certification course, offered by the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) through the Swayam platform, covers the second part of the Class 11 Chemistry syllabus. It's designed to help students build a strong foundation in key chemistry concepts.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Coverage:** The course delves into essential Class 11 Chemistry topics, including Redox Reactions, Hydrocarbons, and Environmental Chemistry.
*   **Flexible Learning:** As a self-paced, online program, it allows students to learn at their convenience through video lessons and e-text materials.
*   **Certification Opportunity:** Successful completion, including self-assessments and a final exam, leads to a certificate from NCERT and Swayam.
*   **Expert Instruction:** The content is coordinated by Professor R.K. Parashar from DESM, NCERT, ensuring high-quality academic guidance.

## Course Overview and Duration

The Chemistry XI Part-II course is a massive open online course (MOOC) spanning 24 weeks, or approximately six months. It is structured around the Class 11 NCERT Chemistry textbook, providing a structured approach to learning. The primary mode of instruction is English, delivered through a combination of video lectures and digital text resources. Students engage in self-study, supported by web resources and interactive elements like discussion forums.

## Learning Experience and Assessment

The learning experience is designed to be comprehensive and engaging. Students will access specialized video lessons, e-texts, and supplementary web resources to deepen their understanding. To track progress and reinforce learning, the course includes self-assessments in the form of online quizzes. The final certification is contingent upon successfully completing these self-assessments and a final comprehensive assessment. Achieving a score of 70% or higher in the final assessment is required to earn the certificate.

## Who Is This Course For?

This course is particularly well-suited for Class 11 students who wish to strengthen their grasp of chemistry concepts, or for Class 12 students looking to revise foundational topics. It's an excellent resource for anyone aiming to build a solid understanding of the subject as outlined in the NCERT curriculum.

## Syllabus Highlights

The course covers a range of critical topics from the Class 11 Chemistry syllabus, divided into chapters with corresponding assignments:

*   **Chapter 8: Redox Reactions** (Parts 1 & 2)
*   **Chapter 9: Hydrogen** (Parts 1 & 2)
*   **Chapter 10: The s-Block Elements** (Alkali and Alkaline earth metals) (Parts 1 & 2)
*   **Chapter 11: The p-Block Elements** (Parts 1 & 2)
*   **Chapter 12: Organic Chemistry – Some Basic Principles and Techniques** (Parts 1, 2, 3 & 4)
*   **Chapter 13: Hydrocarbons** (Parts 1, 2, 3 & 4)
*   **Chapter 14: Environmental Chemistry** (Parts 1 & 2)

Each chapter concludes with an assignment, and the entire course culminates in a final assessment.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Chemistry XI Part-II online course is a straightforward process through the Swayam portal. Prospective students need to:

1.  Visit the official Swayam online learning platform.
2.  Locate the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  Either sign in with existing credentials or create a new account to proceed with enrollment.

Upon successful completion of the course requirements, including the final assessment, students will receive a certificate jointly issued by NCERT and Swayam. This certificate attests to their understanding of the core Class 11 Chemistry topics covered.
