# Growing Microgreens for Business and Pleasure by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Discover the exciting world of microgreens, tiny, nutrient-packed plants that are gaining popularity in the culinary and health spheres. This course offers a comprehensive guide to cultivating these greens, whether for personal enjoyment or to launch a thriving business.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn how to grow various microgreens and superfoods efficiently and affordably.
*   Understand the essentials of setting up a microgreens business, including marketing and sales strategies.
*   Gain insights into different growing mediums, such as soil and hydroponics, and best agricultural practices.
*   Receive a certificate of completion from Udemy after finishing the self-paced curriculum.

## Course Overview

Microgreens are essentially young vegetable greens, typically harvested when they are just a few inches tall. They offer a concentrated source of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, often surpassing the nutritional value of their mature counterparts. This online certification, developed by urban farming expert Alex Field, is offered through Udemy and caters to a wide audience.

The program is ideal for individuals interested in budget-friendly microgreen cultivation, healthcare product manufacturers, and aspiring entrepreneurs looking to enter a growing market. It features 1.5 hours of detailed video lectures complemented by three downloadable resources.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this certification, you will have a solid understanding of fundamental gardening and farming principles. The course delves into the specifics of planting and growing various microgreens and superfoods. You'll explore effective strategies for establishing, marketing, and selling microgreens, covering crucial aspects like business planning and customer outreach.

Furthermore, the curriculum covers different growing techniques, including traditional soil-based methods and hydroponic systems. You will also learn about essential practices for plant safety, health, and cleanliness, ensuring successful and sustainable cultivation.

## Course Structure and Content

The course is divided into several modules, guiding you from the very basics of microgreens to advanced business strategies:

### Microgreens - The Basics

*   Defining microgreens and their health benefits.
*   Assessing if microgreen cultivation is suitable for you.

### Equipment

*   Understanding necessary tools: lights, shelves, trays, and watering equipment.
*   Guidance on selecting and sanitizing seeds, and the use of fertilizers.

### Growing Mediums

*   An overview of different growing options.
*   Detailed instruction on soil-based growing and hydroponic matting.

### What Do Microgreens Need?

*   Essential environmental factors: heat, humidity, ventilation, and water.

### Finding a Location

*   Tips for choosing an optimal growing venue.

### Growing And Harvesting Microgreens

*   Techniques for soaking and chitting seeds.
*   Step-by-step planting instructions for both soil and hydroponic matting.
*   Methods for stacking trays, watering, and harvesting.
*   Proper drying and packaging of cut greens.

### Logistics

*   Importance of record-keeping.
*   Guidance on selecting which microgreens to grow.

### Troubleshooting

*   Addressing common issues like poor germination, mold, and clinging shells.

### Cleanliness/Health and Safety

*   Basic cleanliness practices and tray bleaching.
*   General health and safety guidelines.

### Marketing and Selling

*   Identifying target customers.
*   Creating promotional materials and connecting with clients.
*   Closing sales and pricing strategies.
