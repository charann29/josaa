# Biology XI Part-II by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course offers a comprehensive study of Biology for Class XI students, focusing on essential physiological processes in the human body. Delivered through the Swayam platform, it provides a flexible learning experience with valuable certification upon completion.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is entirely self-paced, delivered through video lectures and e-text materials.
*   It spans 24 weeks, covering core Class XI Biology topics in depth.
*   Certification is provided by NIOS and Swayam upon successful completion and a minimum score in the final assessment.
*   The curriculum is designed and taught by faculty from NCERT, ensuring high-quality academic content.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Biology XI Part-II training is specifically designed for senior secondary students aiming to deepen their understanding of biological concepts. The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT), through its Department of Education in Science and Mathematics (DESM), New Delhi, has developed this program. Dr. Puneet Sharma serves as the course coordinator, guiding learners through interactive online modules.

The learning experience is rich, combining video lectures, detailed e-texts, and additional web resources. Students will engage with online assessments, including multiple-choice questions, to regularly check their progress. A unique feature is the availability of discussion forums, fostering interaction among students and with instructors.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this certification course, students will gain profound insights into various critical biological topics. The curriculum is structured to cover both plant physiology and detailed human physiological processes. Key areas of study include:

*   **Plant Physiology:** Transport in plants, mineral nutrition, photosynthesis (including light reactions and early experiments), and respiration in plants.
*   **Human Physiology:** Digestion and absorption, breathing and gas exchange, body fluids and circulation, excretory products and their elimination, locomotion and movement, neural control and coordination (nervous system organization, brain, spinal cord, neurons, sensory organs), and chemical coordination and integration.

## Eligibility and Certification

This course is primarily intended for Class XI students who wish to strengthen their foundational knowledge in Biology. There are no specific prerequisites for enrollment beyond being a student at this academic level. To earn the official certification from NIOS and Swayam, participants must achieve a score of 70% or higher in the final online assessment.

## Duration and Learning Mode

The Biology XI Part-II course has a structured duration of 24 weeks. It is delivered in a self-study mode, allowing students to learn at their own pace and convenience. The learning materials are accessible through the Swayam online portal and include a combination of video and text-based content, making it flexible for various learning styles.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Biology XI Part-II Certification Course is a straightforward online process. Follow these steps to begin your learning journey:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal for the course.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the subsequent page, you can either sign in using your existing Swayam credentials or create a new account if you are a first-time user.

Once registered and signed in, you can access all the course materials and begin your study of Class XI Biology.

This course offers an excellent opportunity for Class XI students to build a strong foundation in Biology, guided by expert faculty from NCERT, all from the comfort of their homes.
