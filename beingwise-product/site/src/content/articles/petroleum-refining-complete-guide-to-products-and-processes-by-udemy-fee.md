This online certification course, "Petroleum Refining - Complete Guide to Products & Processes," offers a deep dive into the intricacies of this field, designed by an expert in chemical engineering.

*   Learn at your own pace through over 14.5 hours of video lessons, articles, and downloadable resources.
*   Gain a solid understanding of fundamental petroleum refining processes and products.
*   Receive a certificate of completion upon successfully finishing the course.
*   Enjoy a 30-day money-back guarantee and unlimited access to course materials.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This comprehensive online program is structured to provide a robust understanding of petroleum refining operations. It's particularly beneficial for students in chemical engineering, as well as professionals working or aspiring to work in the petroleum or petrochemical industries. The course combines video lectures with practical articles and downloadable materials, making the learning experience engaging and thorough.

The curriculum covers essential topics such as distillation, various cracking processes (like steam cracking and fluid cracking), blending, and hydrogenation. You'll also explore a wide range of petroleum products, including Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG), kerosene, diesel, gasoline, and propane, understanding how each is derived and utilized.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this certification, you will have a strong grasp of the fundamentals of the oil, gas, and petroleum sectors. The course introduces you to the methodologies involved in refining petroleum and the principles behind various petroleum products and processes. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Understanding the basics of oil, gas, and petroleum.
*   Exploring different petroleum products and their applications.
*   Delving into advanced refining processes such as fractionation, vacuum distillation, atmospheric distillation, hydrotreating, hydrogenation, reforming, blending, isomerization, and alkylation.
*   Learning about specialized treatments like coking and gas sweetening for sour gases.

The course is meticulously structured, starting with an introduction to set the context and progressing through detailed modules on the industry and specific refining processes. Here's a glimpse into the syllabus:
