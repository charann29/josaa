# ICAR AIEEA PG 2025: Counselling, Result, Answer Key & More

For students aspiring to pursue master's degrees in agriculture and allied sciences, the ICAR All India Entrance Examination for Admission (AIEEA) PG is a crucial national-level test. This examination opens doors to various postgraduate programs at India's leading agricultural universities, including the Indian Agricultural Research Institute (IARI), Indian Veterinary Research Institute (IVRI), National Dairy Research Institute (NDRI), and the Central Institute of Fisheries Education (CIFE).

## Key takeaways

*   The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducts the ICAR AIEEA PG exam annually for admission to postgraduate courses.
*   Admission is offered to approximately 30% of seats in various agricultural universities across the country.
*   The counselling process involves multiple rounds, including seat vacancy checks, choice filling, document verification, and fee payment.
*   Key dates for counselling rounds, seat allotment, and document submission are released officially by NTA.

## What is the ICAR AIEEA PG Exam?

The ICAR AIEEA PG is a national-level entrance examination administered by the National Testing Agency (NTA). It is designed for candidates seeking admission to master's degree programs in a wide range of agricultural science disciplines offered by various agricultural universities throughout India. Successful candidates can secure a place in these prestigious institutions, gaining access to advanced studies and research opportunities in fields such as agronomy, horticulture, animal husbandry, and fisheries science.

## ICAR AIEEA PG 2025 Counselling Process

The counselling for ICAR AIEEA PG 2025 involves several stages, typically spanning multiple rounds. Candidates who qualify in the entrance exam are eligible to participate. The process generally includes:

1.  **Registration and Choice Filling:** Eligible candidates register on the official counselling portal and select their preferred courses and institutions based on the available seat vacancy list.
2.  **Seat Allotment:** Based on merit, choices filled, and seat availability, seats are allotted to candidates. The seat allotment results are published on the official portal.
3.  **Document Verification:** Candidates must upload necessary documents for online verification. This step is critical for confirming eligibility and the authenticity of submitted information.
4.  **Seat Acceptance and Fee Payment:** Upon successful document verification, candidates need to pay a seat acceptance fee and download a provisional admission letter to confirm their acceptance of the allotted seat.
5.  **Mop-Up/Final Rounds:** After initial rounds, if seats remain vacant, a mop-up or final round of counselling is conducted, allowing more opportunities for candidates to secure admission.

For instance, the Mop-Up/Final Round for ICAR AIEEA PG 2025 began on January 31, 2026, with choice filling ending on February 3, 2026. Seat allotment for this round commenced on February 5, 2026. Similarly, Round 1 seat allotment results for the Veterinary Science stream were declared on January 15, 2026, with document verification on January 16, 2026, and the fee payment deadline on January 17, 2026.

## ICAR AIEEA PG 2025 Important Dates (Tentative)

While specific dates are subject to official announcements, candidates should keep track of the general timeline for the examination and counselling process. The ICAR AIEEA PG 2025 exam was held on July 3, 2025. Counselling rounds typically follow the exam, with Round 1 starting around October 23, 2025, and subsequent rounds continuing through November and into the new year. For the most accurate and up-to-date schedule, always refer to the official NTA or ICAR counselling portal.

## ICAR AIEEA PG 2025 Result and Answer Key

The ICAR AIEEA PG result is declared by NTA on its official website. Candidates can access their scores and ranks using their login credentials. Alongside the results, NTA also releases the official answer key. This allows candidates to review their responses and calculate their probable scores before the final results are announced. Any discrepancies in the answer key can usually be challenged within a specified window as per NTA guidelines.

Stay updated with the official ICAR AIEEA PG portal for all announcements regarding results, answer keys, and counselling schedules.
