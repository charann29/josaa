# Relationship Management in Corporate Banking by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aspiring to excel in the dynamic world of corporate banking, understanding how to build and maintain strong client relationships is crucial. This certification course, offered through Udemy, provides a practical guide to the daily life and essential skills of a corporate banking relationship manager.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn the core responsibilities and strategies for client acquisition and maintenance in corporate banking.
*   Gain insights into critical legal, compliance, and risk assessment aspects relevant to relationship managers.
*   Develop practical skills in negotiation, conflict resolution, and credit application preparation.
*   Benefit from self-paced learning with over 5.5 hours of video content and downloadable resources.

## Course Overview

Designed by Ana Lucas, an experienced corporate banker and advisor, this program dives deep into the multifaceted role of a relationship manager. It's structured to give participants a clear picture of what a professional in this field does day-to-day. The curriculum covers everything from the initial client acquisition process to managing ongoing relationships, ensuring profitability, and navigating the complexities of credit structures and risk.

## What You Will Learn

The course is meticulously crafted to equip learners with a comprehensive skill set. You'll explore effective strategies for establishing and nurturing client relationships within corporate banking, understanding the nuances of customer relationship management. Key areas of study include the fundamentals of credit transactions, various credit structures, and the process of preparing for client meetings. Furthermore, the program delves into crucial aspects like negotiation tactics, loss prevention, thorough risk assessment, conflict resolution, and strategic pricing.

## Course Structure and Content

The learning experience is primarily delivered through video and text-based materials, allowing for a flexible, self-study approach. The course includes more than 5.5 hours of pre-recorded video lectures, supplemented by 6 articles and 26 downloadable resources. This rich content ensures a thorough understanding of topics such as:

*   **Understanding the Relationship Manager's Role:** Defining the job description, client acquisition strategies, and effective follow-up.
*   **Client Meetings:** Preparation, execution of acquisition meetings, and negotiation techniques.
*   **Know Your Client (KYC):** The importance of understanding a client's business through financial analysis, compliance, and other sources.
*   **Profitable Relationships:** Calculating client profitability, correct product pricing, and building sustainable connections.
*   **The Credit Application:** The relationship manager's responsibility in preparing and seeing through credit requests.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for this certification course is ₹4,099. Upon successful completion, participants will receive a certificate from Udemy, signifying their grasp of relationship management principles in corporate banking. The course also comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee and offers unlimited access to the materials, which can be viewed on mobile devices and TVs, providing maximum convenience for learners.

This course is ideal for public relations executives, public relations specialists, and aspiring relationship managers looking to build a strong foundation in corporate banking client engagement.
