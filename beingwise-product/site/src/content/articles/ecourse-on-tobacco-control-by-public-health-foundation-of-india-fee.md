# ECourse on Tobacco Control by Public Health Foundation of India: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

The Public Health Foundation of India (PHFI) offers an eCourse on Tobacco Control, designed to equip professionals with essential knowledge and skills in this critical public health area. Launched in 2011, this program focuses on enhancing understanding of tobacco control strategies and practical application in program design and implementation.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a three-month, entirely online program with a flexible learning format.
*   The curriculum includes eight core modules and a choice of three electives from eight options.
*   Participants will learn through video lectures, text materials, quizzes, and a final assessment.
*   Successful completion earns a certificate from the Public Health Foundation of India.

## Course Overview and Structure

The eCourse on Tobacco Control is a comprehensive online learning experience spanning three months. It is offered twice a year, providing flexibility for participants. The curriculum is delivered through a blend of video and text-based content, accessible via Moodle, an open-source Learning Management System (LMS). This platform creates a virtual classroom environment, allowing students to engage with study materials and live or recorded lectures.

The program is structured around eight core modules, which cover foundational aspects of tobacco control. In addition, students can select three elective modules from a choice of eight, allowing them to tailor their learning to their specific professional or educational background.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the eCourse on Tobacco Control, candidates must hold a bachelor’s degree in one of the following fields:

*   Dentistry
*   Medicine
*   Public Health
*   Pharmacy
*   Psychology
*   Nursing
*   Social Sciences
*   Biological Sciences
*   Allied Health Sciences
*   Or an equivalent qualification.

Beyond academic qualifications, participants must successfully pass the course-end assessment, which includes both multiple-choice questions (MCQs) and short answer questions, to earn their certification.

## Course Fee Structure

The fee for the eCourse on Tobacco Control varies based on the candidate's nationality. Here's a breakdown:

| Candidate Category        | Fee Amount |
| :------------------------ | :--------- |
| Indian Candidates         | ₹ 10,000   |
| South Asian Candidates    | USD 250    |
| Other International Candidates | USD 300    |

This fee covers access to all course materials, lectures, quizzes, and the final assessment. Upon successful completion, a certificate is awarded.

## How to Apply

The application process for the eCourse on Tobacco Control involves a few straightforward steps:

1.  **Visit the PHFI Portal:** Navigate to the official Public Health Foundation of India portal, which hosts information on their eCourse programs.
2.  **Locate the Course:** Under the 'eCourse programs' section, find and click on the 'eCourse on Tobacco Control' to access its dedicated page.
3.  **Register and Apply:** Register on the PHFI website by providing personal details such as your name, contact information, email ID, and nationality. Then, complete and submit the online application form.
4.  **Await Approval & Pay:** After submitting your application, you will need to wait for approval. Once approved, you can proceed to pay the course fees to begin your learning journey.

This eCourse offers a valuable opportunity for those looking to make a significant impact in the field of tobacco control, enhancing both knowledge and practical program implementation skills.
