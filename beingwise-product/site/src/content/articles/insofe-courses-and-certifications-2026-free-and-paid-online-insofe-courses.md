# Insofe Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Insofe Courses

INSOFE (International School of Engineering) offers a range of specialized online degree programs in collaboration with esteemed international universities. These programs are designed to equip professionals and aspiring techno-managers with advanced skills in data science, business analytics, and related engineering fields.

## Key Takeaways

*   INSOFE collaborates with global institutions like Rennes School of Business and Case Western Reserve University to offer advanced online degrees.
*   Programs focus on cutting-edge areas such as Data Science, Business Analytics, and Biomedical Engineering with Digital Health Analytics.
*   Many courses integrate practical research, industry mentorship, and opportunities for international exposure.
*   These degrees aim to enhance career prospects in data-driven roles and leadership positions.

## Doctor of Business Administration in Data Science

This doctoral research program, offered in partnership with Rennes School of Business, France, is a three-year online course tailored for professionals. It aims to deepen business knowledge, enhance data science capabilities, and foster an AI-driven perspective on business challenges. Students engage in research, with opportunities to publish their work and develop patentable resources.

The program is part-time and online, requiring candidates to complete research work and defend their thesis at the Rennes School of Business campus. The curriculum focuses on developing business goals, applying research practices, and formulating business strategies. Students have the flexibility to choose mentors based on their specific research interests, guided by experts from both Rennes School of Business and INSOFE.

## MS in Biomedical Engineering with Digital Health Analytics

INSOFE, in collaboration with Case Western Reserve University (CWRU), USA, offers a Master of Science in Biomedical Engineering with a dual specialization in Digital Health Analytics. This 24-month program bridges the fields of engineering, data science, and medicine, leveraging CWRU's highly regarded Biomedical Engineering Department.

The 30-credit master's degree is jointly managed, with INSOFE in India covering topics such as machine learning algorithms, data science fundamentals, data visualization techniques, and data mining. Students then proceed to the Case School of Engineering in the United States to fulfill the remaining degree requirements. The curriculum covers a broad range of subjects including algorithms, data mining, big data, and core biomedical engineering concepts.

## M.Sc in Data & Business Analytics

This 24-month online Master of Science program is a collaboration between INSOFE and Rennes School of Business, France. It is specifically designed for engineers aspiring to become modern techno-managers, capable of leading businesses with data-driven insights. The program awards a full-time international Master's degree upon completion.

The M.Sc in Data & Business Analytics training is an advanced engineering program that integrates seamlessly with various enterprise applications. It aims to transform candidates into leaders who can drive breakthroughs using data. A mandatory four-month internship, which can be completed anywhere globally, including India and France, is part of the curriculum. The syllabus includes diverse topics such as social media analytics, retail analytics, corporate social responsibility, marketing research, and customer data analysis.
