# Talend Certification Training For Big Data Integration by Edureka: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program is designed to help you become proficient in using Talend for integrating large datasets, equipping you with the skills to manage and process big data effectively. It's an instructor-led course that combines theory with practical application to ensure a comprehensive learning experience.

## Key takeaways

*   The course offers live, instructor-led online sessions, providing an interactive learning environment.
*   You will earn a 'Talend for DI and Big Data' certificate upon successful completion of an industry-based project.
*   The curriculum focuses on hands-on experience, including real-world case studies and a dedicated project.
*   Lifetime access to the Learning Management System (LMS) and community forums ensures ongoing support and resources.

## Course Overview

The Talend Certification Training For Big Data Integration program is specifically created to help learners master the various tools and components of Talend. The goal is to prepare you for real-time challenges in big data. Through an interactive, instructor-led model, you'll gain expertise in using Talend for data integration, including its integration with frameworks like Apache Kafka, Hadoop, Hive, and Pig.

BeingWise understands the importance of practical skills, and this course aligns with that by offering online sessions, interactive presentations, assignments, and real-life case studies. You'll work on an industry-based project to solidify your learning and gain valuable hands-on experience.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, you will acquire a range of critical skills and knowledge in big data and Talend. These include:

*   A thorough understanding of ETL concepts, Talend's architecture, its various components, and its scope in data integration.
*   The ability to solve real-world business problems using Talend.
*   Proficiency in automating data integration and data warehousing tasks with Talend.
*   A clear grasp of Hadoop and Big Data concepts, and how Talend integrates effectively with Hadoop (HDFS).
*   Skills to integrate Talend with the broader Hadoop ecosystem, Apache Kafka, Pig, and Hive.
*   Competence in working with various target files, databases, and source files.
*   The capability to remotely parameterize and access a Job using Talend.
*   Knowledge of using Pig for scripting and Kafka for streaming Talend jobs.

## Program Structure and Features

The training is delivered through a blend of virtual classroom and self-study modules, primarily in English. Classes are typically held on weekends to accommodate working professionals. The program highlights include:

*   **Live Instructor-Led Sessions:** Direct interaction with instructors for enhanced learning.
*   **Comprehensive Certification:** Receive an Edureka certificate upon fulfilling the project requirements.
*   **24/7 Technical Support:** Access assistance whenever you need it.
*   **Real-Life Case Studies:** Apply theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios.
*   **Industry-Based Project:** Work on a significant project to demonstrate your skills.
*   **Flexible Batches:** Weekend classes for convenience.
*   **Lifetime LMS Access:** Continuous access to course materials and updates.
*   **Community Forum:** Engage with fellow learners and instructors.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for the instructor-led online training is ₹17,795, inclusive of GST. For payment flexibility, a no-cost EMI option is also available. Upon satisfactory completion of the live project, which is reviewed by industry specialists, you will receive an Edureka certificate with a grade.

## Eligibility Criteria and Application

There are no mandatory prerequisites to enroll in the Talend Certification Training. However, a basic understanding of data warehousing can be beneficial for a smoother learning experience. To support this, Edureka offers a complimentary “Data Warehousing Certification Training” course alongside the main program.

To earn your Edureka certification, you must successfully complete a live project that allows you to apply your newly acquired skills. This project is then assessed by a panel of industry experts. Detailed application steps are typically available on the official Edureka platform.

This program offers a robust pathway to developing essential skills in big data integration using Talend, preparing you for relevant roles in the industry.
