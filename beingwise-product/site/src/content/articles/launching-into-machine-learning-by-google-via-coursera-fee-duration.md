# Launching into Machine Learning by Google via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Embarking on a journey into machine learning can open doors to exciting career opportunities. This program, part of the broader Machine Learning with TensorFlow specialization from Google Cloud, offers an accessible entry point for beginners looking to understand the fundamentals of this rapidly evolving field.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn directly from Google Cloud professionals in a 100% online, self-paced format.
*   Gain foundational knowledge in machine learning, deep learning, and model optimization.
*   The course is beginner-friendly and requires approximately 14 hours to complete.
*   Receive a shareable certificate upon successful completion, recognized by Coursera.

## What the Program Offers

This online professional course is specifically designed for individuals new to machine learning. It's delivered entirely online, allowing you to learn at your own pace and convenience. The instruction is in English, and subtitles are also available to aid understanding. The curriculum is internationally recognized, ensuring you gain relevant and up-to-date knowledge.

## What You Will Learn

The Launching into Machine Learning program focuses on practical skills and conceptual clarity. Upon completion, you will be better equipped to:

*   Understand the core principles and importance of deep learning.
*   Identify and address common challenges encountered in machine learning projects.
*   Develop foundational knowledge for bootstrapping machine learning code.
*   Learn how to create scalable and repeatable test datasets for training and evaluation.
*   Evaluate machine learning models effectively using loss functions and various performance metrics.
*   Gain insights into different types of machine learning and strategies for model optimization.

## Course Structure and Duration

This beginner-level course is structured for self-study and includes video and text-based content. It is estimated to take around 14 hours to complete, offering flexibility to fit into your schedule. The program includes internal exercises to reinforce learning, and all study materials are downloadable for offline access.

### Syllabus Overview

The course begins with an introductory module before diving into data understanding:

*   **Module 1: Introduction**
    *   Course introduction video
*   **Module 2: Get to know your data: Improve data quality**
    *   This module focuses on understanding data and techniques to enhance its quality.

## Fees and Certification

The Launching into Machine Learning course is offered with a subscription model, providing flexibility in how you pay. The fees vary based on the duration of access you choose:

| Access Duration | Approximate Fee (INR) |
| :-------------- | :-------------------- |
| 1 Month         | ₹4,117                |
| 3 Months        | ₹8,234                |
| 6 Months        | ₹12,352               |

Upon successfully completing all course materials and participating in the in-course exercises, you will receive a professional certificate from Coursera. This certificate is shareable on professional networking platforms, allowing you to showcase your new skills.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Launching into Machine Learning course is straightforward and does not involve a traditional application form. Follow these steps to enroll:

1.  **Visit the Course Page:** Navigate to the official course webpage on Coursera.
2.  **Enroll:** Click on the 'Enroll' button. You'll typically find an option to 'Select Free Trial' if it's your first time using the platform.
3.  **Account Creation (if new):** If you're a new user, you'll need to create an account by providing your name, email address, and setting a password. You can often sign up quickly using your Google, Apple ID, or Facebook accounts.
4.  **Payment Details:** To access the course beyond the free trial (if applicable) or to subscribe, you will need to enter your card details and complete the payment. This gives you access to the course and potentially other courses if you opt for a Coursera Plus subscription.
5.  **Access Course:** Once registered and payment is confirmed, you can use your login credentials to sign in and begin your learning journey.

This program provides a solid foundation for anyone aspiring to build a career in machine learning, guided by the expertise of Google Cloud professionals.
