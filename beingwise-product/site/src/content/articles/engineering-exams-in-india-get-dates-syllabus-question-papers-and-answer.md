# Engineering Exams in India - Get Dates, Syllabus, Question Papers and Answer keys

Navigating the world of engineering entrance exams in India can feel overwhelming. This guide provides essential information about key exams, helping you stay informed about dates, syllabi, and other crucial details for your admission journey.

## Key takeaways

*   **JEE Main** is the gateway to NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs, and also serves as a prerequisite for JEE Advanced to enter IITs.
*   **State-level exams** like MHT CET and KCET are vital for admissions to engineering colleges within their respective states.
*   **BITSAT** offers a pathway to the prestigious Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS) campuses.
*   Always refer to the official exam portals for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding dates, application procedures, and results.

## Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main

JEE Main is one of the most significant engineering entrance examinations in India, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). It is a crucial step for admission to undergraduate engineering programs at National Institutes of Technology (NITs), Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs), and Government Funded Technical Institutes (GFTIs). Furthermore, a strong performance in JEE Main is necessary to qualify for JEE Advanced, which is the entrance exam for the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs).

Key dates for JEE Main 2026 Session 2:

*   **Answer Key & Response Sheet Release:** April 11, 2026
*   **Result Announcement:** On or before April 20, 2026

Students should regularly check the official NTA JEE Main portal for updates on application forms, admit cards, exam patterns, and counselling processes, which are typically managed through the official JoSAA portal.

## Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (MHT CET)

MHT CET is a state-level entrance examination conducted for admission to various undergraduate courses, including engineering and pharmacy, in colleges across Maharashtra. The State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, oversees this examination.

Key dates for MHT CET 2026 Session 1:

*   **PCM Group Exam Dates:** April 11 - April 20, 2026
*   **PCB Group Exam Dates:** April 21 - April 26, 2026

Candidates aspiring for engineering admissions in Maharashtra should refer to the official MHT CET website for details on the syllabus, exam pattern, admit card release, and result declarations.

## Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET)

KCET is another prominent state-level entrance examination, managed by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA). It is mandatory for students seeking admission to engineering, pharmacy, agriculture, and other professional courses in government and private institutions across Karnataka.

Key dates for KCET 2026:

*   **Exam Dates:** April 23 - April 24, 2026
*   **Tentative Result Date:** May 2026

Information regarding the application form, admit card, detailed syllabus, and previous year's question papers can be found on the official KEA website.

## Birla Institute of Technology & Science Admission Test (BITSAT)

BITSAT is an entrance examination conducted by the Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS) for admissions to its integrated first-degree programs across its campuses in Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad. BITS is highly regarded, consistently ranking well in national frameworks like the NIRF rankings.

Key dates for BITSAT 2026 Session 1:

*   **Exam Dates:** April 15 - April 16, 2026
*   **Application with 12th Marks & Preference Submission:** April 17 - June 1, 2026

Prospective students should visit the official BITSAT portal for comprehensive information on application procedures, syllabus, exam pattern, and counselling details.

Staying organised and informed about these crucial exams will significantly aid your journey towards securing an engineering seat in your desired institution.
