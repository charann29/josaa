# PG programme in Technology Management by KPMG International via Edology: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Technology management is a rapidly growing field, crucial for organisations looking to innovate and adapt in a fast-paced digital world. This Postgraduate (PG) programme, offered by KPMG International through its educational partner Edology, is designed to equip professionals with the skills needed to excel in this dynamic domain.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 10-month hybrid learning programme, blending online and in-person sessions.
*   It requires a commitment of approximately 4-5 hours per week, making it suitable for working professionals.
*   The curriculum includes real-life case studies, capstone projects, and offers specialisation options in Data Science or Big Data and Cloud.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a PG Programme in Technology Management certificate from KPMG and Edology.

## Programme Overview and Structure

This unique programme aims to develop skilled technology managers who can bridge the gap between technological innovation and business strategy. It addresses the increasing demand for professionals who can manage technology implementation, redesign existing systems, and guide organisations through digital transformation.

Learning occurs through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content. The programme features two in-person mentoring sessions, providing valuable direct interaction with industry experts. Each batch is limited to 25 students, ensuring personalised attention.

## Curriculum and Specialisations

The programme's curriculum is carefully crafted to provide both foundational knowledge and advanced skills. Participants will learn to tackle management challenges using technological solutions and foster business growth as agile techno-managers. A key feature is the opportunity to choose a specialisation, allowing students to deepen their expertise in either:

*   **Data Science:** Focusing on extracting insights and knowledge from data.
*   **Big Data and Cloud:** Concentrating on managing and leveraging large datasets within cloud environments.

The learning journey includes simulated client scenarios, group assignments, and real-life capstone projects culminating in a final project presentation. Recorded lectures are also available for flexible learning.

## Fees and Payment Information

The total fee for the PG Programme in Technology Management is ₹339,000, plus Goods and Services Tax (GST). This amount includes a non-refundable registration fee of ₹25,000. 

### Fee Structure

| Description         | Amount              |
| :------------------ | :------------------ |
| Registration Fee    | ₹25,000             |
| Total Course Fee    | ₹339,000 + GST      |

Payments can be made using debit cards, credit cards, or net banking. For those seeking financial assistance, zero-interest education loans are available through FinancePeer, the corporate financial partner. After paying the registration fee, the remaining balance is typically due in a single instalment ten days before the programme begins.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for this programme, applicants must meet the following requirements:

*   **Educational Qualification:** A bachelor's degree from any recognised national university, with any specialisation.
*   **Work Experience:** While not mandatory, candidates with approximately two years of work experience are preferred as it can enhance their learning experience and application of concepts.

## Career Prospects and Who Should Apply

This programme is ideal for individuals aspiring to build a career in technology management or those already in technical roles looking to transition into management. It is particularly beneficial for:

*   Human Resource Managers
*   Data Analysts
*   Data Scientists
*   Business Analysts
*   Data Engineers
*   Data Architects
*   Technical Managers
*   Cloud Data Engineers

Graduates will be equipped to manage diverse and global teams, drive innovation, and strategically implement technological solutions within organisations.
