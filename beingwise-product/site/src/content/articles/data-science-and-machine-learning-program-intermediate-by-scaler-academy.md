# Data Science and Machine Learning Program - Intermediate by Scaler Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program offers a comprehensive learning experience in data science and machine learning, designed to equip participants with the skills to tackle real-world business challenges. It aims to build strong foundational knowledge and practical problem-solving abilities in these rapidly evolving fields.

## Key Takeaways

*   The curriculum is shaped by insights from over 50 tech companies and 100 data science professionals, ensuring industry relevance.
*   Participants benefit from live online sessions led by experienced industry mentors and experts.
*   The program targets significant career growth, with a reported potential for a salary increase of up to two times for experienced professionals.
*   A completion certificate is awarded by Scaler Academy upon successfully finishing the program.

## Program Overview and Approach

Scaler Academy's Data Science and Machine Learning Program focuses on providing participants with the confidence and knowledge needed to address typical challenges faced by data scientists and machine learning engineers. The training encourages students to apply data science skills to solve complex business problems effectively. The program's diverse curriculum is delivered through live sessions, drawing on insights from a wide range of tech companies and experienced professionals to create an industry-tested learning path.

## What You Will Learn

This program is structured to provide in-depth knowledge across various advanced concepts within data science and machine learning. Participants will delve into areas such as deep learning, structural analysis, big data technologies, and advanced data analytics. The curriculum emphasizes practical application, preparing students to overcome common obstacles encountered by data scientists and to work on real-world problem statements.

### Key Learning Modules

#### Module 1: Data Analysis and Visualization

This module introduces essential tools and techniques for data manipulation, analysis, and visualization. It covers:

*   **Python Libraries:** In-depth use of libraries like NumPy, Pandas for data handling, and Matplotlib, Seaborn for visualization.
*   **Data Acquisition:** Techniques for gathering data, including Web API integration, web scraping with tools like BeautifulSoup, and social media data collection using Tweepy.
*   **Probability and Applied Statistics:** Fundamental concepts including probability theory, Bayes Theorem, various distributions, descriptive statistics, outlier treatment, confidence intervals, the Central Limit Theorem, hypothesis testing (including A/B testing), ANOVA, correlation, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), feature engineering, and methods for handling missing values.

## Who Should Apply

This program is ideal for recent graduates (from 2020 or earlier) and working professionals who are keen to develop strong coding skills in data science and machine learning. It is particularly suited for individuals aspiring to become machine learning engineers or data scientists, seeking to enhance their career prospects and technical capabilities.

## Eligibility and Application Process

To be eligible for this program, interested candidates must have completed their graduation in or before the year 2020. The application process is straightforward and conducted online:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official Scaler Academy website.
2.  **Locate 'Apply Now':** On the homepage, find and click the 'Apply Now' button.
3.  **Complete Registration Form:** Fill out the registration form with required details such as your full name, email address, a verifiable phone number, and information about your academic or professional status. Direct admission options may also be available through social media accounts like Facebook, LinkedIn, GitHub, or Google.
4.  **Entrance Test:** After registration, candidates will undertake a 30-minute online entrance test. This test typically includes coding questions and Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).
5.  **Admission Confirmation:** Upon the announcement of test results, successful candidates will be informed of their admission and the subsequent steps to proceed.

The program offers a structured curriculum, live classes, projects based on top company challenges, access to critical tech tools, one-on-one mentorship, and mock interviews to prepare participants for the industry.
