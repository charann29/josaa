# Ayurveda Online Course - The Science of Self Healing: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Discover the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda and its principles for well-being through this online course, designed to help you understand your body's natural rhythms and foster self-healing. This program offers a practical approach to integrating Ayurvedic practices into your daily life for improved health and harmony.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn the core concepts of Ayurveda, including the three Doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha).
*   Understand how to apply Ayurvedic principles to daily and seasonal routines for better health.
*   Gain insights into various self-care techniques like Abhyanga and Shirodhara.
*   The course is self-paced, allowing you to learn at your convenience with lifetime access to materials.

## Course Overview

This certification program, titled "Ayurveda Online Course - The Science of Self Healing," is designed for individuals eager to explore the foundational concepts of Ayurvedic healing. Developed by Pablo Mariana, an expert in Ayurveda, Reiki, and Ho'oponopono, the course aims to guide students in recognizing their inner strength and making positive changes to their daily habits.

The curriculum blends video-based lessons with downloadable resources and quizzes, making the learning experience engaging and comprehensive. It covers essential topics such as wellness, health, the concept of Doshas, Dinacharya (daily routines), Ritucharya (seasonal routines), and specific Ayurvedic therapies like Shirodhara. The goal is to empower learners to understand the elemental forces within themselves and cultivate healthier, more balanced lifestyles.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this course, you will acquire a detailed understanding of Ayurvedic principles and their application in self-healing. You will delve into the fundamentals of the three Doshas – Vata, Pitta, and Kapha – and learn how these elemental forces influence the health of your mind, body, and soul. The program also introduces you to practices like Abhyanga (oil massage) and Shirodhara (forehead oil pouring), alongside the importance of daily and seasonal routines (Dinacharya and Ritucharya) in maintaining overall well-being. This knowledge will enable you to discover and harness your inherent power for self-healing.

## Program Structure and Content

The course is delivered in English, primarily through video and text, and is designed for self-study. It includes approximately 9 hours of pre-recorded video content, complemented by 6 downloadable resources and interactive quizzes to reinforce learning. You'll have unlimited access to all materials, which are accessible on various devices, including mobile phones and TVs. A certificate of completion is provided upon finishing the course.

### Detailed Syllabus

*   **Introduction:** Understanding Ayurveda as the Art of Life.
*   **The Birth Of Ayurveda:** Exploring the origins of this ancient science.
*   **The 3 Doshas:** In-depth study of Vata, Pitta, and Kapha, including self-assessment quizzes.
*   **How To Live A Healthy Balanced Life:** Focusing on the five pillars of wellness.
*   **Ayurveda's Teachings:** Concepts of similarities, opposites, and the importance of real food.
*   **The Elements That Compose The Universe:** Understanding the five elements and their connection to the five senses.
*   **The Attributes Of The Universe:** Learning about the three Gunas and their quiz.
*   **Ayurveda Therapies:** Introduction to Life Mandala, Abhyanga, Shirodhara, and natural remedies.
*   **Ayurveda Healthy Routines & Habits:** Detailed exploration of Dinacharya and Ritucharya, including practical techniques like tongue cleaning.
*   **Relax & Meditation:** Guided meditations and practices such as Chi Kung, Jin Ji Du Li, Surya Namaskar, and Nadi Sodhana.

## Course Fee and Certification

While the original price of this course is ₹2,299, it is often available at a discounted fee of ₹499. This fee grants you unlimited access to all course materials and resources. Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate of completion from the course provider, confirming your understanding of the principles of Ayurvedic self-healing. The course also comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee.
