# CAT 2025 Topper Interview: How Harsh Khudania Scored 99.93 Percentile with Consistent Practice

Harsh Khudania achieved an outstanding 99.93 percentile in the CAT 2025 examination. His success story highlights how dedicated, consistent practice, especially with mock tests, played a crucial role in his journey to becoming a CAT topper, even while managing a full-time job.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Consistency is Key:** Harsh emphasizes that regular, sustained effort over time is more impactful than sporadic bursts of intense study.
*   **Mock Tests are Non-Negotiable:** Frequent mock tests are essential for understanding exam patterns, identifying weak areas, and improving time management.
*   **Strategic Improvement:** His journey from a 98.83 percentile to 99.93 percentile showcases the power of targeted preparation and learning from previous attempts.
*   **Balancing Work and Study:** It's possible to excel in CAT even with a demanding job by effectively structuring your study time.

## The Power of Consistent Practice

Harsh attributes his remarkable CAT 2025 score primarily to his consistent study routine and diligent practice. He firmly believes that it's not about the number of hours put in, but the quality and regularity of the effort. Even with a full-time job, he made sure to allocate dedicated time daily for CAT preparation. This disciplined approach allowed him to steadily build his concepts, improve his problem-solving speed, and refine his test-taking strategies.

## Mastering Mock Tests

One of the cornerstones of Harsh's preparation was his extensive use of CAT mock tests. He treated each mock test as a real exam, meticulously analyzing his performance afterward. This involved not just checking correct and incorrect answers but understanding *why* certain mistakes were made and identifying patterns in his errors. This analytical approach helped him pinpoint his weaker sections and specific question types that needed more attention, enabling him to tailor his practice effectively.

## Advice for Future CAT Aspirants

For those aspiring to crack the CAT, Harsh offers valuable guidance:

*   **Start Early:** Give yourself ample time to cover the syllabus and practice thoroughly.
*   **Understand the Basics:** Ensure a strong foundation in all three sections (VARC, DILR, QA).
*   **Regular Revision:** Consistently revisit concepts to ensure long-term retention.
*   **Stay Positive:** Maintain a positive mindset and learn from every challenge.

Harsh Khudania's journey is an inspiring example that with dedication, strategic practice, and a resilient attitude, achieving a top CAT percentile is well within reach for any aspirant.
