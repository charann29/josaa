# CAT 2026 Exam Dates & Schedule (Tentative): Application, Admit Card, Exam & Result

For aspiring MBA students, the Common Admission Test (CAT) is a crucial gateway to India's top management programs. Understanding the tentative schedule for CAT 2026 is essential for effective planning and preparation.

## Key Takeaways

*   The CAT 2026 examination is tentatively scheduled for November 29, 2026, across multiple cities.
*   Registration is expected to commence in the first week of August 2026.
*   The application window typically remains open for about 45 days.
*   Official results are generally declared in the third week of December.

## CAT 2026 Tentative Schedule

While the full, official schedule will be announced in late July 2026, candidates can anticipate the following timeline for CAT 2026, based on previous years' patterns:

| Event                  | Tentative Date (2026)      |
| :--------------------- | :------------------------- |
| Official Notification  | Last week of July          |
| Application Starts     | First week of August       |
| Application Deadline   | Third week of September    |
| Admit Card Release     | Second week of November    |
| CAT 2026 Exam          | November 29                |
| Answer Key Release     | First week of December     |
| Answer Key Challenge   | Early December             |
| Result Announcement    | Third week of December     |

## Application Process for CAT 2026

The CAT 2026 registration process typically spans around 45 days. To apply, candidates must first create a unique registration ID on the official IIMCAT website. The application form will require personal details, academic qualifications, and preferences for examination cities. The application fee must be paid during submission. After the initial submission, a short correction window, usually lasting 2-3 days, allows candidates to edit their photograph, signature, and selected test cities. No other details can be changed during this period.

## CAT 2026 Admit Card Details

The admit card for CAT 2026 is expected to be released in the second week of November. Candidates will be able to download it from the official exam website using their login credentials. This crucial document will contain important information such as the candidate's name, photograph, signature, category, gender, address, date of birth, the specific exam date, allocated slot, examination center details, timings, and reporting time. It is vital to download and print the admit card, as it is required not only for entry into the examination hall but also for the subsequent admission process.

## CAT 2026 Examination Structure

The CAT 2026 exam will be a computer-based test conducted across numerous centers nationwide. It is typically held in three distinct slots:

*   **Morning Slot:** 8:30 AM to 10:30 AM
*   **Afternoon Slot:** 12:30 PM to 2:30 PM
*   **Evening Slot:** 4:30 PM to 6:30 PM

The examination has a sectional time limit of 40 minutes for each section. Candidates with disabilities (PwD) are granted an additional 13 minutes and 20 seconds per section.

## Answer Key and Results

Following the exam, the official CAT answer key and individual candidate response sheets will be released through the login portal. A window, usually 2-3 days long, will be provided for candidates to challenge any discrepancies in the provisional answer key, with a fee of INR 1500 per question. The examination authority will review all objections and, if valid, will revise the answer key. Subsequently, IIMCAT will announce the CAT 2026 results in the third week of December. The scorecard will be available for download from the official IIMCAT website.

Staying informed about these dates and preparing diligently are key steps toward success in CAT 2026.
