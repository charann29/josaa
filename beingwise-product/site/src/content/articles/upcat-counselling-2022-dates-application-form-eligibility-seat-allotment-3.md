# UPCAT Counselling 2022: Dates, Application Form, Eligibility, Seat Allotment

For students aspiring to join various professional courses in Uttar Pradesh, the counselling process is a crucial step. This guide explains the key aspects of UPCAT Counselling for 2022 admissions, providing clarity on the current admission landscape.

## Key Takeaways

*   The UPCET exam, previously known as UPSEE, has been discontinued for 2022 admissions.
*   Admissions to undergraduate courses in Uttar Pradesh state universities are now primarily based on national-level exams like JEE Main, CUET (UG), and NATA.
*   For postgraduate programs, CUET (PG) is the main entrance exam, while integrated courses use CUET (UG).
*   Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), and other state universities in Uttar Pradesh now consider these national exam scores for admissions.

## What Happened to UPCET 2022?

The Uttar Pradesh Combined Entrance Test (UPCET), which was formerly known as UPSEE, served as a common entrance exam for various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in state-affiliated institutions. However, for the 2022 academic year, the National Testing Agency (NTA) announced that UPCET would no longer be conducted. Instead, admissions are now facilitated through other well-established national entrance exams.

## Eligibility for Admissions in 2022

While the UPCET exam itself is no longer held, the general eligibility principles for admission to institutions like AKTU and MMMUT largely remain. Prospective students should meet the criteria set by the respective national entrance exams (JEE Main, CUET, NATA) and also satisfy the domicile requirements of Uttar Pradesh. 

Typically, candidates are eligible if they have passed their qualifying examination from an institution in Uttar Pradesh, or if their parents hold a domicile certificate for Uttar Pradesh. There is generally no age limit for these admissions.

## Admission Process and Seat Allotment

With the shift to national exams, the admission process for 2022 involves applying to the relevant national test (CUET, JEE Main, NATA) and achieving a valid score. Following this, individual universities and the state counselling body conduct their respective counselling rounds based on these scores. The seat allotment process considers the candidate's rank, preferences, and the availability of seats, adhering to state reservation policies. Detailed schedules for counselling and seat allotment are typically released on the official portals of the participating universities or the state's technical education department.

### Courses Offered Through National Exams (2022)

Many programs previously accessible via UPCET are now covered by these national entrance tests:

*   **Undergraduate Courses:** B.Tech (Lateral Entry), B.Des, B.FAD/B.F.A, B.Pharm/B.Pharm (Lateral), BHMCT are now generally covered by CUET (UG), JEE Main, or NATA scores.
*   **Postgraduate Courses:** M.Tech, M.Pharm, M.Des, M.Arch, MURP, MBA/MBA (Integrated), MCA/MCA (Integrated)/MCA (Lateral) are primarily offered through CUET (PG) scores, with integrated MBA and MCA courses using CUET (UG).
