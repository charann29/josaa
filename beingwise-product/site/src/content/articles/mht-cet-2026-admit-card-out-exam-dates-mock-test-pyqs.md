# MHT CET 2026: Admit Card (OUT), Exam Dates, Mock Test, PYQs

The Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test (MHT CET) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to pursue professional courses like Engineering (BE/BTech) and Pharmacy (BPharm/PharmD) in various government and private institutions across Maharashtra. This page provides all the essential information you need for MHT CET 2026, including admit card details, exam dates, and preparation resources.

## Key Takeaways

*   The MHT CET 2026 exam is conducted by the Maharashtra State CET Cell for admissions to Engineering and Pharmacy courses.
*   For the first time, the MHT CET exam will be held in two separate sessions for both PCM and PCB groups.
*   The MHT CET 2026 admit cards for PCM candidates have already been released, with PCB admit cards expected soon.
*   The exam is a computer-based test conducted exclusively at designated centres within Maharashtra.

## What is MHT CET?

MHT CET is a state-level entrance examination that serves as a gateway for admission to undergraduate courses in Engineering, Technology, and Pharmacy across Maharashtra. It is a mandatory test for students seeking admission to colleges affiliated with the state's technical education board. The exam assesses candidates' knowledge in subjects relevant to their chosen stream (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics for Engineering; Physics, Chemistry, Biology for Pharmacy).

## MHT CET 2026 Admit Card

The Maharashtra State CET Cell has officially released the MHT CET 2026 admit cards for candidates appearing in the PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) group. Students can download their hall tickets from the official MHT CET website by logging in with their registered email ID and password. The admit card is a vital document containing details such as the candidate's name, photograph, signature, exam date, time, and the address of the test centre. It is crucial to download and print a copy of the admit card, as it must be presented at the examination centre.

### How to Download Your Admit Card:

1.  Visit the official MHT CET 2026 website.
2.  Click on the link for MHT CET 2026 admit card download.
3.  Enter your registered email ID and password.
4.  Click 'Submit'.
5.  Your MHT CET 2026 hall ticket will appear on the screen.
6.  Download and take a printout for future reference and to carry to the exam centre.

Should you find any discrepancies on your admit card, it is important to contact the MHT CET Cell immediately for corrections.

### Exam Day Guidelines

On the day of the examination, candidates must adhere to specific guidelines:

*   Carry a printed copy of your MHT CET 2026 admit card.
*   Bring a valid photo identification proof (e.g., Aadhar card, Passport, Driving License).
*   Report to the exam centre at the time specified on your admit card.
*   Do not bring any prohibited items, such as electronic gadgets, study materials, or food items, into the examination hall.
*   It is advisable to carry a small bottle of hand sanitiser.

## MHT CET 2026 Exam Dates and Schedule

For the first time, the MHT CET exam will be conducted in two distinct sessions. This change aims to provide more flexibility and potentially reduce logistical challenges. Candidates should carefully note the dates relevant to their subject group (PCM or PCB).

| Group | Session 1 Dates | Session 2 Dates |
| :---- | :-------------- | :-------------- |
| PCM   | April 11 - April 20 (excluding April 12) | May 12 - May 16 |
| PCB   | April 21 - April 26 | May 10 - May 11 |

It is important for candidates to re-download their city slip if there have been any revisions due to exam clashes, as announced by the Maharashtra State CET Cell.

## Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs) and Mock Tests

To effectively prepare for MHT CET 2026, engaging with previous year's question papers (PYQs) and taking mock tests is highly recommended. PYQs offer insights into the exam pattern, types of questions asked, and important topics. Mock tests, on the other hand, simulate the actual exam environment, helping you manage time, identify weak areas, and build confidence. Many online platforms and coaching centres offer free MHT CET mock tests, which can be a valuable resource for your preparation strategy.

For comprehensive preparation, candidates should also refer to the official MHT CET syllabus and exam pattern available on the Maharashtra State CET Cell website.

Stay informed by regularly checking the official MHT CET portal for the latest updates and announcements regarding the examination and admission process.
