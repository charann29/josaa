# Maths Subject Knowledge: Understanding Numbers by STEM Learning Limited via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This course, developed by the National STEM Learning Centre, is designed to enhance the mathematical understanding of numbers, particularly for secondary school teachers. It offers a structured approach to appreciating the everyday use of numbers and exploring their underlying patterns.

## Key Takeaways

*   A one-month, self-paced online course focusing on number theory and its applications.
*   Requires approximately three hours of study per week, making it manageable alongside other commitments.
*   A certificate of achievement is awarded by FutureLearn upon successful completion.
*   Primarily aimed at secondary teachers, but also beneficial for primary teachers transitioning to secondary levels or anyone seeking to deepen their understanding of numbers.

## Course Overview

The "Maths Subject Knowledge: Understanding Numbers" certification is a concise, one-month program. Its content, crafted by the experts at the National STEM Learning Centre, is specifically tailored to help secondary educators strengthen their grasp of mathematical concepts related to numbers. The curriculum is designed to make participants appreciate the ubiquitous presence of numbers in daily life and to uncover the fascinating patterns and connections that emerge when delving into the structure of mathematics.

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this course, participants will be equipped with a deeper understanding of various numerical concepts. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Understanding Number Systems:** Gaining insight into the fundamental workings of our daily number system.
*   **Pattern Recognition:** Identifying patterns when exploring concepts such as multiples, factors, and prime factorization.
*   **Explaining Operations:** Being able to clearly explain why, for instance, two negative numbers multiply to a positive number.
*   **Describing Operations:** Clearly articulating how various mathematical operations are performed.
*   **Exploring Sequences:** Navigating and understanding the wide variety of number sequences.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideally suited for both new and experienced secondary school teachers who may not have a specialization in mathematics but are eager to improve their understanding of mathematical methods. While primarily designed for secondary educators, primary school teachers can also benefit from this course, especially if they are preparing to teach at the secondary level and wish to familiarize themselves with the curriculum. Essentially, anyone keen to explore the intricacies of numbers will find value in this course.

## Fees and Certification

The program fee for the "Maths Subject Knowledge: Understanding Numbers" course is ₹9,149. Upon successfully completing at least 90% of the course steps and accompanying assessments, participants will receive an official certificate from FutureLearn, acknowledging their achievement. This certificate confirms the completion of an accredited course designed to enhance mathematical subject knowledge.

## How to Apply

Admission to this course is straightforward. Candidates simply need to follow the application process outlined on the official FutureLearn platform. There are no strict prerequisites regarding specific bachelor's or master's degrees in mathematics; individuals holding degrees in any subject are eligible. However, being currently involved in teaching or studying to become a teacher is considered an advantage for prospective applicants.

This course provides a valuable opportunity to solidify and expand your understanding of numbers, equipping you with enhanced subject knowledge for effective teaching.
