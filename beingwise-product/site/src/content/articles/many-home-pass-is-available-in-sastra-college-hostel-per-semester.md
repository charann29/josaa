When considering hostel life at SASTRA University, understanding the rules around going home is often a key concern for prospective students and their parents. This guide aims to clarify the general policies for girls' hostels regarding home visits each semester.

## Key Takeaways

*   Students are typically permitted to go home twice per semester for general leave.
*   An additional leave may be granted for genuine health reasons.
*   Specific extended holidays like Diwali and Pongal also allow for home visits.
*   Sunday outings are possible if parents manage pick-up and drop-off by 6 PM.

## Understanding Semester Home Passes

SASTRA University generally allows female hostel residents to take leave to go home a set number of times each semester. Based on past student experiences, the standard provision is for two home passes per semester. These are intended for general visits and personal time away from campus.

## Provisions for Health-Related Leave

Recognizing that unforeseen circumstances can arise, SASTRA University does make provisions for health-related absences. If a student experiences a genuine health issue that necessitates a trip home, an additional leave pass may be granted. This flexibility ensures student well-being is prioritized.

## Special Holiday Leaves

Beyond the standard home passes, the university calendar includes several significant festivals that are observed with extended breaks. During these periods, such as Diwali and Pongal, students are typically allowed to go home. These structured holidays provide dedicated opportunities for students to spend time with their families without utilizing their regular semester passes.

## Sunday Outings and Parental Involvement

For students who wish to leave campus on weekends, SASTRA University permits Sunday outings. However, there's a specific requirement for girls' hostels: parents must personally pick up and drop off their daughters. The student must return to the hostel by 6 PM on Sunday. This policy aims to ensure student safety and accountability during weekend excursions.

## Requesting Additional Leave for Specific Reasons

Should a student need to take leave for a compelling reason outside of the standard provisions or designated holidays, there is a formal process. Students are generally advised to meet with the Dean of Student Affairs in person to present their request and explain the circumstances. Such requests are typically evaluated on a case-by-case basis, considering the urgency and validity of the reason.

Navigating hostel life at SASTRA University involves understanding these clear guidelines for home visits and outings, ensuring a balance between academic pursuits and personal well-being.
