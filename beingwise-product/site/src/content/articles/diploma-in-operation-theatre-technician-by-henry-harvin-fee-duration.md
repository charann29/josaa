# Diploma in Operation Theatre Technician by Henry Harvin: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students aspiring to a vital role in the healthcare sector, a Diploma in Operation Theatre Technician (OTT) can be a significant step. This program equips individuals with essential skills and knowledge to assist surgical teams effectively. This article explores the details of the OTT Diploma offered by Henry Harvin, covering its structure, learning outcomes, and application process.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is delivered through a blend of self-study and virtual classroom sessions, primarily using video and text-based materials.
*   Classes are scheduled on weekdays, providing a structured learning environment.
*   The curriculum covers a broad spectrum of topics, from medical fundamentals like anatomy and anesthesia to practical skills such as infection control and patient communication.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Henry Harvin, affirming their qualification as an Operation Theatre Technician.

## Understanding the Program Structure

The Diploma in Operation Theatre Technician is designed to be comprehensive, ensuring students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical understanding. The instruction medium is English, making it accessible to a wide range of learners. The course delivery combines self-paced learning with interactive virtual classroom sessions, allowing flexibility while maintaining engagement. Learning materials are provided in both video and text formats.

## Detailed Syllabus Overview

The curriculum is thoughtfully organized into several modules, each focusing on a critical aspect of operation theatre management and patient care. Here's a breakdown of the key areas covered:

*   **COVID-19: General Awareness:** This module provides crucial information on the pandemic, including epidemiology, prevention strategies, mask usage, home quarantine guidelines, and mental health awareness during the crisis.
*   **Anesthesia and Operative Drugs:** Students learn about different types of anesthesia (general and regional), pre-anesthetic evaluation, premedication, and the various drugs used in anesthetic procedures.
*   **Nursing Care and Management:** This section highlights the importance of nursing care within the operative environment.
*   **Revised Anatomy and Physiology:** A foundational module covering the structural organization of the human body, cells, tissues, and detailed studies of all major organ systems, including digestive, respiratory, skeletal, nervous, endocrine, reproductive, integumentary, urinary, circulatory, and muscular systems.
*   **Medical Terminology:** Essential for healthcare professionals, this module teaches the language of medicine, including anatomical positions, planes, directional terms, and common prefixes, suffixes, abbreviations, and symbols.
*   **Infection Control and Biomedical Waste Management:** A critical module focusing on preventing infections, understanding the chain of infection, hand hygiene, sterilization techniques, vaccination for healthcare workers, NABH norms for infection control, and proper management and disposal of biomedical waste, along with the use of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).
*   **Basic Level Computer Skills:** This module introduces fundamental computer knowledge, including operating systems (Windows) and essential applications like MS Word and MS Excel, which are increasingly relevant in modern healthcare settings.
*   **Patient Communication:** Emphasizing soft skills, this module covers effective communication techniques, listening skills, information sharing, and overcoming communication barriers, specifically in healthcare worker-patient interactions.
*   **Medical Emergencies:** This practical module teaches basic medical emergency procedures, life support skills, monitoring vital signs (temperature, pulse, respiratory rate, blood pressure, pulse oximetry), managing emergencies, adult and child CPR, and managing conditions like stroke and wounds.

## Certification and Learning Experience

Upon successfully completing all modules and meeting the program's requirements, participants are awarded a certificate by Henry Harvin. The learning experience is designed to be interactive and engaging, with weekday classes ensuring a consistent learning pace. The combination of self-study and virtual classrooms caters to different learning styles, providing a flexible yet structured educational journey.

For specific details on fees and the exact duration of the program, prospective students should consult the official Henry Harvin program page. The application process typically involves registering through their portal and fulfilling any admission criteria they may have.
