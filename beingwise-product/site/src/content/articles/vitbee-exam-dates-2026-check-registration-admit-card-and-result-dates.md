# VITBEE Exam Dates 2026: Check Registration, Admit Card & Result Dates

The VIT Business School Entrance Exam (VITBEE) is your gateway to the esteemed MBA programs offered by VIT Business School campuses. Understanding the key dates for registration, the exam, and results is crucial for all aspiring candidates.

## Key takeaways

*   The application period for VITBEE 2026 has already begun, exclusively online.
*   The VITBEE 2026 examination is scheduled for May 3, 2026, conducted in a computer-based format.
*   Results will be announced following the exam and personal interview rounds, identifying candidates shortlisted for admission.
*   VIT Business School holds AACSB accreditation, a globally recognized standard for business education quality.

## VITBEE 2026 Application Process

Registration for VITBEE 2026 has commenced, and interested applicants can complete their application online through the official admissions portal at admissions.vit.ac.in. It is essential to submit your application form well before the deadline of April 12, 2026. The entire application process, including form submission, is conducted solely online.

## VITBEE 2026 Examination Details

The VITBEE 2026 exam will take place on May 3, 2026. This entrance test is administered in a computer-based format, requiring candidates to be familiar with digital test environments. Further details regarding the exam structure and guidelines will be available on the official VIT website closer to the exam date.

## Important Dates for VITBEE 2026

Staying informed about the complete schedule for VITBEE 2026 is vital for all candidates. Below is a detailed timeline of significant events:

| Event                      | Dates                   |
| :------------------------- | :---------------------- |
| VITBEE Application Period  | October 31, 2025 – April 12, 2026 |
| VITBEE 2026 Exam Date      | May 3, 2026             |
| VITBEE Result Announcement | To be announced after exam and interviews |

## VITBEE 2026 Result and Admission

Candidates who appear for the VITBEE 2026 exam and successfully complete the personal interview round will have their results announced shortly thereafter. The result declaration will list the names of candidates who have been shortlisted for admission to the MBA programs at VIT Business School. Further instructions for the admission process will be communicated to the selected candidates.

## Institutes Accepting VITBEE Scores

VITBEE scores are primarily accepted for admission to the Master of Business Administration (MBA) programs across the various campuses of VIT Business School. These include:

*   VIT Business School, Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore
*   VIT Business School, Chennai
*   VIT Business School, Bhopal
*   VIT-AP School of Business, VIT-AP University, Amaravati

Each of these campuses offers a comprehensive 2-year full-time MBA program. The total fees for the MBA program at these institutions are approximately ₹6.97 Lakh. Besides VITBEE, these institutions also accept scores from other national-level MBA entrance exams like CAT, XAT, and MAT.

Prepare thoroughly and mark these dates in your calendar to ensure a smooth application and examination experience for VITBEE 2026.
