# Cardiac Care Technician Certification by VIVO Healthcare: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students aspiring to a vital role in healthcare, the Cardiac Care Technician certification offered by VIVO Healthcare provides a structured path to a specialized career. This program is designed to equip individuals with the essential skills and knowledge needed to support cardiologists and care for patients with heart conditions.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 12-month certification program focusing on practical and theoretical aspects of cardiac care.
*   It includes a significant internship component, offering hands-on experience in real healthcare settings.
*   The curriculum covers crucial areas like ECG interpretation, cardiac ultrasound procedures, and assisting with various cardiac interventions.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a VIVO Healthcare certification, accredited by NSDC/VIVO.

## Program Overview and Structure

The Cardiac Care Technician program spans 12 months, totaling 1500 hours of learning. This comprehensive training is delivered through a blended approach, combining virtual classroom sessions, video-based content, and extensive practical experience. The curriculum is meticulously designed to build a strong foundation in healthcare communication, human anatomy, physiology, and crucial soft skills.

The learning journey is divided into two main components: 840 hours dedicated to theory and simulation exercises, and a substantial 660-hour internship. This practical immersion is a cornerstone of the program, ensuring students gain real-world experience in monitoring cardiac patients across diverse healthcare environments. Key topics covered include ECG, clinical pathology, biomedical waste management, and basic life support, all aimed at developing proficiency in cardiac care.

## What You Will Learn

Through this certification, participants will develop expertise in a range of critical cardiac care functions. The syllabus is designed to make students proficient in:

*   Performing and interpreting Electrocardiograms (ECGs).
*   Conducting cardiac ultrasound procedures, including 2D Echo, Colour flow, Trans Oesophageal, and Doppler studies.
*   Assisting cardiologists with Treadmill Stress Tests (TMT).
*   Preparing detailed reports and images following patient scans.
*   Collaborating with doctors to understand underlying cardiac conditions.
*   Supporting cardiologists during invasive and non-invasive procedures such as Holter monitoring, angiograms, angioplasties, stress echoes, pacemaker implants, and tilt table tests.
*   Identifying critical pathologies and ensuring timely communication with medical authorities.
*   Differentiating between normal and abnormal cardiac anatomy.
*   Maintaining strict patient confidentiality and data security.

## Eligibility and Certification

To be eligible for the Cardiac Care Technician Certification course by VIVO Healthcare, applicants must have completed 10+2 education. A background in Science or prior experience as an ECG Technician is required. 

Receiving the VIVO-NSDC accredited certificate is contingent upon fulfilling several requirements:

*   Full payment of the course fee.
*   Successful completion of all course sessions and the internship.
*   Maintaining a minimum of 70% attendance in course sessions.
*   Achieving at least 85% attendance in the pre-internship exam.
*   Scoring a minimum of 70% marks in the pre-internship examination.

## Fees and How to Apply

Specific details regarding the total course fee are provided during the application process. However, a registration fee of Rs. 2000 is typically required at the time of admission.

To apply for the program, prospective students should follow these steps:

1.  Locate the 'Apply Now' option on the official VIVO Healthcare platform.
2.  Follow the on-screen admission instructions, selecting the desired course and skill centre.
3.  Utilize the 'Find a centre' feature to identify the nearest VIVO skill centre.
4.  Visit the chosen skill centre to obtain and submit the admission form along with all necessary documents.
5.  Proceed with the payment of the course fee and the Rs. 2000 registration fee.

Upon successful completion of these steps, applicants will be officially enrolled in the program.

This certification opens doors to rewarding career opportunities in the dynamic field of cardiac care.
