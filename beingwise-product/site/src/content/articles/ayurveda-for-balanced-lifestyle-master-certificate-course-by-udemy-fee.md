# Ayurveda for Balanced Lifestyle - Master Certificate Course

Embracing a balanced lifestyle is increasingly vital in our fast-paced world, touching upon all aspects of well-being, from relationships and work to health and emotional stability. This Master Certificate Course, designed by Ayurveda Doctor Dr. Pragya Gupta, offers a comprehensive online training program to integrate Ayurvedic principles into modern living.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn the foundational principles of Ayurveda to foster holistic well-being.
*   Understand your unique body constitution (Prakriti) and learn how to balance your doshas.
*   Gain practical knowledge on Ayurvedic diet, daily routines (Dincharya), and herbal remedies.
*   Benefit from a self-paced learning experience with engaging video lectures and supplementary materials.

## Course Overview

This online program is structured for individuals seeking to understand and apply Ayurvedic wisdom to their daily lives. The curriculum spans approximately 11 hours of video content, complemented by 11 articles and interactive quizzes. It delves into core Ayurvedic concepts such as Prakriti (individual constitution), Gunas (qualities), Agni (digestive fire), Dhatus (tissues), and the significance of different tastes in diet. Beyond theory, the course provides practical guidance on meditation, mindful eating, detoxification methods like fasting, and daily and seasonal regimens (Dincharya).

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this course, you will be equipped with a deep understanding of:

*   **Ayurvedic Fundamentals:** Explore the basics of Doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha), their subtypes, and how imbalances manifest. You'll also learn about the importance of Prakriti analysis and how to determine your own body constitution.
*   **Lifestyle and Diet:** Discover the Ayurvedic approach to diet, including mindful eating practices, food combinations, and the role of fasting for detoxification. The course provides specific dietary tips and sample menus tailored to different Prakriti types.
*   **Daily and Seasonal Routines (Dincharya & Ritucharya):** Understand the significance of daily and seasonal regimens for maintaining health and balance. This includes concepts like non-sleep deep rest (Yoga Nidra), meditation techniques, and building immunity.
*   **Herbal Remedies and Digestion:** Learn about various Ayurvedic herbs, the preparation of Ayurvedic teas, and the principles of Agni (digestive fire) and its role in overall health. You'll also gain insights into understanding digestion from an Ayurvedic perspective.

## Course Structure and Content

The course is divided into several modules, each building upon the previous one:

### Introduction

*   Overview of the course and introduction to Ayurveda.
*   Understanding the Five Basic Elements (Panchamahabhutas).
*   Introduction to the Three Doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.

### Three Doshas & Imbalance

*   Detailed exploration of Vata, Pitta, and Kapha Doshas and their subtypes.
*   Understanding the three states of Doshas and identifying signs of imbalance.
*   Practice quizzes to test your knowledge.

### Prakriti (Body Constitution)

*   The importance of Prakriti analysis and how to calculate your own.
*   Personalized tips and dietary recommendations for Vata, Pitta, and Kapha personalities.
*   Strategies for balancing dual-dosha constitutions (e.g., Vata-Pitta, Pitta-Kapha).

### Agni (Digestive Fire) & Mindful Eating

*   The critical role of Agni in health and the 13 types of Agni.
*   Strategies for balancing Agni and understanding Ayurvedic digestion.
*   Principles and practical tips for mindful eating.

### Additional Topics

*   Dhatu (body tissues).
*   The concept of Malas (waste products).
*   Gunas (qualities of nature).
*   Tastes (Rasa) and their impact.
*   Yoga Nidra, herbal remedies, and detoxification.
