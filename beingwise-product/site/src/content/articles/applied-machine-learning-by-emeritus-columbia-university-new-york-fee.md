# Applied Machine Learning by Emeritus Columbia University, New York: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This comprehensive online program in Applied Machine Learning, developed in collaboration with Columbia University, New York, offers a deep dive into machine learning principles and practical applications. It's designed to equip learners with essential skills to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of data science and AI.

## Key Takeaways

*   A 5-month online certificate program in Applied Machine Learning.
*   Features over 240 video lectures, live online sessions, and 12 practical application projects.
*   Certification is awarded by Columbia University, New York, upon successful completion.
*   The program fee is USD 2,203, with flexible payment options available.

## Program Overview

This Applied Machine Learning certificate course is structured to provide both theoretical understanding and hands-on experience. It covers key aspects of Python programming, data analytics, and machine learning. The curriculum is delivered through a blend of self-study materials, virtual classrooms, and video-based content, requiring an estimated 8-10 hours of study per week.

The program is divided into two parts, each containing 12 modules, ensuring a thorough exploration of the subject matter. To reinforce learning, participants will engage with 45 quizzes and tests throughout the course.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to impart crucial skills in machine learning, data science, and algorithms. Upon completion, participants will be able to:

*   Design and train models for various data types.
*   Construct regression models for predicting unknown outputs.
*   Develop classification models to categorize datasets (e.g., identifying spam emails).
*   Create unsupervised models, such as recommender systems, to uncover hidden patterns in large datasets.
*   Identify latent factors within data to enhance model prediction accuracy.
*   Build probabilistic data models to forecast a range of possible outcomes, considering real-world uncertainties.

## Program Offerings and Highlights

This program is rich in resources and interactive elements to support effective learning:

*   **Duration:** 5 months.
*   **Content:** Over 240 video lectures, 12 application projects, 45 quizzes and assignments.
*   **Interaction:** Live online sessions, 18 moderated discussion boards, and more than 20 Q&A sessions.
*   **Certification:** A certificate from Columbia University, New York.
*   **Faculty:** Learning under the guidance of subject matter experts and practitioners in the field.

## Eligibility Criteria

Prospective participants should possess an undergraduate-level understanding of fundamental mathematical and statistical concepts. Specifically, a background in Statistics, Calculus, Linear Algebra, and Probability is recommended to fully benefit from the course content.

## Course Fee and Payment

The course fee for the Applied Machine Learning program is USD 2,203. The original fee is USD 2,475, but a discounted rate is currently offered. Flexible payment options are available to make the program accessible, and special group enrollment pricing may be an option for those joining with colleagues.

## How to Apply

Detailed application procedures and enrollment forms can typically be found on the official program portal associated with Emeritus Columbia University. Interested candidates should review the eligibility requirements and prepare any necessary documentation to complete their application.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to advance your skills in a high-demand field, backed by the academic rigor of Columbia University.
