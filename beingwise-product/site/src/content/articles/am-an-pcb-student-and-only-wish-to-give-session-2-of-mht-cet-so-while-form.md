# MHT CET Session 2 for PCB Students: Choosing Your Attempt

If you're a PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) student planning to take the MHT CET exam and wish to appear *only* for Session 2 (typically held in May), understanding the correct option during the application process is crucial. This guide will clarify which selection to make to ensure you're registered for the right exam session.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Select "First Attempt"**: When filling out the MHT CET application, if you intend to appear only for Session 2 (May), choose the "First Attempt" option. This might seem counter-intuitive, but it's how the system often registers your *first chosen* session.
*   **Confirm PCB Group**: Always ensure you explicitly select the PCB group during registration to appear for the correct subjects.
*   **Single Payment**: You only need to pay the application fee once for your chosen group (PCB), even if there are multiple sessions.
*   **No April Session Registration**: Selecting "First Attempt" for your intended May exam means you will *not* be registered for the earlier April session.

## Understanding "First Attempt" in MHT CET Registration

The MHT CET application portal can sometimes label exam sessions in a way that might be confusing. When you see options like "First Attempt" and "Both Attempts," it's essential to interpret them correctly in the context of your desired exam schedule. For students specifically targeting Session 2 (May) for their PCB group, selecting "First Attempt" typically registers you for the *first session you wish to take*. In this scenario, if you're skipping the April session, your May session becomes your "first attempt" within the system's logic.

## Focusing on the PCB Group

As a PCB student, your goal is to secure admission to courses like Pharmacy or Agriculture. Therefore, it is paramount that you correctly select the "PCB" group during the application process. This ensures you are enrolled to take the Physics, Chemistry, and Biology papers relevant to your desired programs. Double-check this selection before final submission.

## Application Fees for PCB Students

The application fee for the MHT CET PCB group is a one-time payment. For the general category, this fee is typically around INR 1300. This fee covers your registration for the selected session(s) within the PCB group. If you choose "First Attempt" (for Session 2), you will pay this amount once, and it will cover your entry for the May examination.

## What Happens if You Select "First Attempt" for Session 2?

By choosing "First Attempt" with the intention of appearing in Session 2 (May), the MHT CET system will register you exclusively for that later session. You will not be automatically enrolled or expected to appear for the earlier April session. This is the correct procedure if your strategy is to prepare thoroughly and take the exam only once in May.

Always refer to the official MHT CET information brochure and portal for the most accurate and up-to-date instructions during the application period to ensure a smooth registration process.
