# MAH CET Counselling 2026: MBA CAP Round Wise Result, Merit List, Cutoff & Top Colleges

For aspiring MBA/MMS students in Maharashtra, the MAH CET Centralised Admission Process (CAP) is the gateway to top management institutes. This counselling process, managed by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, determines admissions based on MAH CET scores and other national-level exam results.

## Key Takeaways

*   MAH CET 2026 counselling is expected to take place in July-August, following the result declaration in June.
*   Both MAH CET qualifiers and candidates with valid CAT, MAT, XAT, ATMA, GMAT, or CMAT scores can participate.
*   The CAP involves online registration, choice filling, and seat allotment across multiple rounds.
*   Seat allocation is strictly based on cut-offs released by the State CET Cell for each round and category.

## Understanding the MAH CET Counselling Process

The MAH CET CAP is a multi-stage process for admission to around 300 management colleges across Maharashtra, including prestigious institutions like JBIMS, SIMSREE, K J Somaiya Institute of Management, and PUMBA. It's a blend of online and offline activities where candidates submit their preferences, and seats are allotted by the authorities. The core of this process is the merit list and category-wise cut-offs that determine who gets into which program.

Candidates who achieve a score greater than zero in the MAH CET 2026 exam are eligible to participate. Those who appeared for the exam twice can use their better score for the CAP rounds. Additionally, candidates from outside Maharashtra with scores from other national exams (CAT, MAT, XAT, ATMA, GMAT, CMAT) can also apply under the All India category by paying the CAP registration fee.

## Eligibility for MAH MBA CET CAP 2026

To be eligible for the MAH CET CAP, candidates must meet specific criteria. Primarily, this includes having a valid score in MAH CET 2026, or for All India category applicants, a qualifying score in CAT, MAT, XAT, ATMA, GMAT, or CMAT. Beyond exam scores, candidates must fulfill academic requirements, typically a bachelor's degree with a minimum percentage of marks, as specified in the official information brochure released by the State CET Cell.

## How to Register for MAH MBA CET CAP 2026

Registration for the MAH CET CAP is a crucial first step. Eligible candidates must visit the official website of the Maharashtra State CET and complete the CAP registration form before the deadline. This involves providing personal, academic, and contact details, along with uploading necessary documents. A registration fee is also required, which applies to both Maharashtra State candidates and those applying under the All India category.

## MAH MBA CET CAP Round Process

The CAP process is conducted in multiple rounds, each involving:

1.  **Online Registration:** Candidates register and submit their details.
2.  **Document Verification:** Documents are verified at designated facilitation centers.
3.  **Choice Filling:** Candidates submit their preferred colleges and courses in order of priority.
4.  **Seat Allotment:** Based on merit, cut-offs, and preferences, seats are allotted.
5.  **Acceptance of Seat:** Allotted candidates must accept the seat and freeze their admission or opt for betterment in subsequent rounds.
6.  **Reporting to Institute:** Finally, candidates report to the allotted institute with original documents and pay the admission fees to confirm their admission.

The State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, will release round-wise and category-wise cut-offs and seat allotment lists on its official website throughout the counselling period.

## Top Colleges Accepting MAH MBA CET Scores

Many reputable institutions in Maharashtra accept MAH CET scores for their MBA/MMS programs. While specific cut-offs vary each year and by program, some of the consistently top-ranked colleges include:

*   Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management Studies (JBIMS)
*   Sydenham Institute of Management Studies, Research and Entrepreneurship Education (SIMSREE)
*   K J Somaiya Institute of Management
*   Department of Management Sciences (PUMBA), Savitribai Phule Pune University
*   Prin. L. N. Welingkar Institute of Management Development & Research (WeSchool)

These colleges are often highly sought after, and achieving a competitive MAH CET percentile is key to securing admission.

Stay updated with official announcements from the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, for the most accurate and timely information regarding the MAH CET 2026 counselling process.
