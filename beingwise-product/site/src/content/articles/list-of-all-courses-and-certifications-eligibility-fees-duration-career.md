# List of all Courses & Certifications - Eligibility, Fees, Duration, Career Options

Exploring higher education and professional development can feel overwhelming with the sheer number of options available. This guide aims to simplify your search by outlining various courses and certifications, detailing their structure, costs, and the career paths they can open up.

## Key Takeaways

*   Online degrees from top institutions like IIT Madras offer flexibility and recognized qualifications in fields like Data Science and Electronic Systems.
*   Professional certifications, such as those in Cloud-Native Architecture or Data Analysis with Power BI, are designed for rapid skill acquisition and career advancement.
*   Course durations vary significantly, from a few months for certifications to four years for full bachelor's degrees.
*   Eligibility often includes basic computer literacy or foundational knowledge in related subjects, making many programs accessible to a wide audience.

## Online Bachelor's Degrees from IIT Madras

IIT Madras provides innovative online Bachelor of Science (BS) degrees, offering a flexible pathway to a prestigious qualification. These programs are structured with multiple exit points, allowing students to earn certificates or diplomas even if they don't complete the full degree.

### BS in Electronic Systems

This is a four-year online undergraduate program from IIT Madras, designed to impart a comprehensive understanding of electronic systems. The curriculum is divided into three levels: Foundation, Diploma, and Degree. Students can choose to exit after completing any level and receive a corresponding certificate. To be eligible for the full BS degree, students must successfully complete the first four semesters.

### BS in Data Science and Applications

Another four-year online BS program from IIT Madras, this degree focuses on building a strong foundation in mathematics, statistics, programming, and their applications to real-world problems. Like the Electronic Systems degree, it features multiple exit points (Foundation, Diploma, Degree). The program emphasizes practical experience through hands-on projects and case studies, covering essential areas such as machine learning and data visualization.

### BS in Management and Data Science

IIT Madras also offers a BS degree combining Management and Data Science. This program aims to equip learners with skills at the intersection of business strategy and data-driven decision-making.

## Professional Certifications for Skill Enhancement

For those looking to rapidly acquire specific skills or advance in their current roles, professional certifications provide focused training over shorter durations. These programs are often designed by industry experts to meet current market demands.

### Cloud-Native Application Architecture

This certification program helps students understand and work with cloud applications. Over approximately four months, the course includes multiple projects to provide practical, real-world experience. Topics covered range from Cloud-Native Fundamentals and Message Passing to Microservices Security and Docker Attack Surface Analysis. Successful completion helps participants master skills for expertise in cloud-native applications.

*   **Duration:** Approximately 4 months
*   **Fees:** Around ₹ 84,545
*   **Skills Covered:** Cloud computing knowledge, cloud-native ecosystem evaluation, microservices security.
*   **Experience Level:** Intermediate

### Data Analysis and Visualization with Microsoft Power BI

This program focuses on in-demand data pre-processing, analysis, and visualization skills using Microsoft Power BI. The curriculum teaches how to connect various data sources, transform data, and apply best design practices for creating insightful visualizations and dashboards. Basic familiarity with MS Excel is helpful for this course.

*   **Duration:** Approximately 3 months
*   **Fees:** Around ₹ 84,545
*   **Skills Covered:** Data visualization, data transformation, business intelligence with Power BI.
*   **Experience Level:** Beginner (with basic MS Excel knowledge)
*   **Additional Support:** Includes placement assistance, resume support, LinkedIn profile optimization, and technical mentor support.

Choosing the right course or certification depends on your career goals, current experience, and the time you can commit. Whether it's a comprehensive online degree or a focused skill-building certification, these programs offer valuable pathways to enhance your profile and career prospects.
