# Can We Register Now Onwards for BITSAT Registration? When Will Slot Booking Start for BITSAT?

Many students aspiring for admission to the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) Pilani often wonder about the BITSAT registration and slot booking process. Understanding the timelines is crucial for a smooth application.

## Key Takeaways

*   BITSAT application forms are typically released well in advance of the examination.
*   There's a specific window for both initial registration and a separate one for application corrections.
*   Slot booking is a mandatory step after registration to select your preferred exam date and time.
*   Missing the slot booking window usually means the exam authority will assign a slot for you.

## BITSAT Application Status

For the current academic year, the BITSAT application form was made available on January 11, accompanied by the official prospectus. This initial release allows prospective students ample time to review the details and prepare their applications.

## Registration Deadlines

The deadline for completing the online registration process for BITSAT is March 31. It's highly recommended to complete your application well before this date to avoid any last-minute technical issues or stress. Timely submission ensures your application is considered for the examination.

## Application Correction Window

After the initial registration closes, there's a dedicated period for applicants to make any necessary corrections to their submitted forms. This correction window is scheduled from April 1 to April 6. This is a vital opportunity to rectify any errors in your application details, ensuring accuracy before the examination process moves forward.

## BITSAT Slot Booking

Slot booking is a critical step that follows successful registration. This process allows you to choose your preferred date and time for appearing in the BITSAT examination. The slot booking facility will be accessible online through the official BITS Pilani Online Test Booking System portal. Candidates will be able to reserve their date and book their slot between April 10 and April 18.

It's important to actively participate in the slot booking process to secure a time that best suits your schedule. Once you have successfully booked your slot, an admit card will be generated, which is essential for appearing in the examination. Should you miss the official slot booking window, the examination conductors will typically assign a slot for you, though it might not be your preferred choice.

Stay updated with the official BITS admissions portal for the most precise and current information regarding all BITSAT processes.
