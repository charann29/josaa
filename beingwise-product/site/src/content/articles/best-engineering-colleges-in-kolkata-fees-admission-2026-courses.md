Kolkata is home to over 60 engineering institutions, offering a wide array of B.Tech programs. These colleges, both public and private, are recognized by the AICTE and NBA, and provide a strong foundation for aspiring engineers.

## Key takeaways

*   Kolkata boasts more than 60 engineering colleges, including prestigious government and private options.
*   Admission to most B.Tech programs primarily relies on scores from the WBJEE and JEE Main entrance examinations.
*   Leading institutions like Jadavpur University and IIEST Shibpur are celebrated for their strong academic offerings and impressive placement records.
*   Popular specializations such as Computer Science Engineering (CSE) and Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) are widely available across many colleges.
