# Post Graduate Program in CAE by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For engineers looking to deepen their expertise in Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE), this program offers a comprehensive pathway. It's designed to equip you with advanced theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing you for high-demand roles in the industry.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 12-month intensive program focused on advanced CAE concepts and tools.
*   It offers flexible learning through self-study, virtual classrooms, and pre-recorded video content.
*   Upon successful completion, you receive a certification from Skill Lync and build a project portfolio.
*   Payment options include a one-time fee or a 0% EMI plan.

## Program Overview

The Post Graduate Program in CAE is specifically tailored for engineers who already have a foundational understanding of computer-aided engineering and its applications in analysis. This year-long program comprises five distinct courses, meticulously designed to cover both the underlying engineering theories and the practical application of various CAE tools. It also emphasizes the mathematical principles that drive machine operations.

The curriculum is delivered through a blend of pre-recorded videos, allowing students the flexibility to learn at their own pace and convenience, alongside virtual classroom sessions. By the end of the program, participants will possess in-depth knowledge of advanced technologies and tools crucial for CAE engineers aiming to join leading Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs).

## What You Will Learn

The program is structured to impart comprehensive engineering knowledge, focusing on the latest advancements in applications and technologies. Key areas of study include:

*   **FEA Concepts:** A deep dive into the fundamentals and advanced applications of Finite Element Analysis.
*   **Batch Meshing:** Techniques for efficient and automated mesh generation.
*   **Morphing Techniques:** Understanding and applying methods for geometric modification.
*   **Macro Generation:** Learning to create macros for automating tasks within CAE software.

Beyond these specific skills, you will gain a holistic understanding of the CAE domain, including different solvers and analysis types used in the industry. The program begins with an introduction to the CAE domain and FEA basics, familiarizing you with the Graphical User Interface (GUI) of essential tools like ANSA. You'll also learn about geometric cleanup and deck setup processes.

## Program Offerings and Highlights

This program is designed with several features to enhance the learning experience and career prospects:

*   **Flexible Learning:** Combines online learning with live webinars and workshops.
*   **Practical Experience:** Includes extensive project work, add-on industry projects, and opportunities for paid internships.
*   **Certification:** Successful completion earns you a 'Certificate of Completion' from Skill Lync. Top performers also receive a Merit Certificate.
*   **Career Support:** Helps in building a unique project portfolio page linked to resumes, enhancing visibility to top manufacturing units.
*   **Financial Flexibility:** A free demo is available, and a 0% EMI option makes the program more accessible.

## Eligibility and Fees

### Eligibility Criteria

This program is ideal for engineers from the following disciplines:

*   Aerospace Engineering
*   Automotive Engineering
*   Mechanical Engineering

### Course and Certificate Fees

The total course fee for the Post Graduate Program in CAE is Rs. 2,75,000. For those preferring installment payments, an EMI option is available at Rs. 14,375 per month. The certification is provided by Skill Lync upon successful completion of the course.

## How to Apply

Admitting to the CAE Master's Certification Program involves a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Official Course Page:** Navigate to the program's official page.
2.  **Enroll Now:** Click on the 'Enroll Now' button.
3.  **Choose Your Plan:** Select your preferred master plan, whether it's the full payment or the EMI option.
4.  **Payment:** Proceed to pay the required fee. Review the transaction details displayed on the screen.
5.  **Billing and Payment Method:** Enter your billing information and choose a payment method from the available options.
6.  **Confirmation:** Once the transaction is successfully completed, you will receive an enrollment confirmation.

This program is a significant step for engineers aiming to specialize in advanced CAE and secure positions in leading global manufacturing sectors, including roles like Technicians, Aircraft Machinists, Digital Accelerators, C++ developers, Analysts, and Maintenance Engineers.
