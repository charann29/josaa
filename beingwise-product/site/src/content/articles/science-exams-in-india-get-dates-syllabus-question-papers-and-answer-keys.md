# Science Exams in India - Get Dates, Syllabus, Question Papers and Answer keys

For students aspiring to pursue higher education in science, numerous entrance exams across India open doors to prestigious universities and specialized institutions. Understanding these exams, including their schedules, content, and application processes, is crucial for effective preparation.

## Key takeaways

*   Many science entrance exams are conducted annually for undergraduate and postgraduate admissions.
*   Key information like application deadlines, exam dates, syllabus details, and result announcements are typically released by the respective organizing bodies.
*   Familiarizing yourself with the exam pattern and practicing with previous year's question papers can significantly boost your preparation.
*   Always refer to the official websites of the conducting authorities for the most accurate and up-to-date information.

## Common University Entrance Test (CUET) UG & PG

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) is a national-level examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in central and participating universities across India. It streamlines the admission process, replacing multiple individual university entrance exams.

### CUET UG 2026 Tentative Dates

*   **Intimation Slip:** To be notified
*   **Admit Card:** To be notified

### CUET PG 2026 Tentative Dates

*   **Student Response Sheet:** April 2026
*   **Result:** April 2026

For detailed information on the syllabus, exam pattern, application process, and more, students should regularly check the official NTA CUET portal.

## IISER Entrance Exam (IAT)

The Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research (IISERs) are premier institutions offering integrated undergraduate and postgraduate science programs. Admission to these highly sought-after programs is primarily through the IISER Aptitude Test (IAT).

### IISER Aptitude Test 2026 Tentative Dates

*   **Registration Window:** March 5, 2026 – April 13, 2026
*   **Correction Window:** April 16, 2026 – April 18, 2026

Prospective candidates should visit the official IISER admissions portal for comprehensive details regarding the application form, syllabus, admit card, and preparation guidelines.

## Scholastic Assessment Test (SAT) India

The SAT is a globally recognized standardized test often used for admissions to undergraduate programs, including some institutions in India. While primarily for international admissions, some Indian universities and colleges also accept SAT scores for their science courses.

### SAT India (May Attempt) 2026 Tentative Dates

*   **Last date to apply:** April 17, 2026
*   **Exam Date:** May 2, 2026

Information on registration, test centers, and syllabus can be found on the College Board's official website.

## Uttar Pradesh Combined Agriculture and Technology Entrance Test (UPCATET)

UPCATET is a state-level entrance examination conducted for admissions to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in agriculture and technology offered by state agricultural universities in Uttar Pradesh.

### UPCATET 2026 Tentative Dates

*   **Application Fee Payment Last Date:** May 1, 2026
*   **Correction Window Date:** May 2, 2026

For details on the application form, admit card, syllabus, and important dates, refer to the official UPCATET website.

## Indian Council of Agricultural Research All India Entrance Examination for Admission (ICAR AIEEA)

ICAR AIEEA is conducted for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate programs in agriculture and allied sciences across various agricultural universities and colleges that fall under the purview of the Indian Council of Agricultural Research.
