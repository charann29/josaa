# INI CET 2026 (May Exam): Application Form (OUT), Eligibility, Dates, Syllabus, Pattern, Cutoff

The Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test (INI CET) is a crucial gateway for aspiring medical and dental postgraduates in India. Conducted by AIIMS Delhi, this exam opens doors to prestigious MD, MS, MDS, DM, and M.Ch courses across several top institutions.

*   **Application Window:** The application period for INI CET May 2026 runs from March 28 to April 25, 2026.
*   **Exam Date:** The entrance examination is scheduled for May 16, 2026.
*   **Eligibility:** Candidates must complete their internship by July 31, 2026, to be eligible.
*   **Institutions:** INI CET scores are accepted by 19 AIIMS, JIPMER Puducherry, PGI Chandigarh, NIMHANS Bengaluru, and SCTIMST Trivandrum.

## Understanding INI CET

INI CET is a national-level postgraduate entrance examination administered by the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) in Delhi. It serves as the sole admission test for various postgraduate medical and dental programs, including Master of Surgery (MS), Doctor of Medicine (MD), Master of Dental Surgery (MDS), Doctorate of Medicine (DM), and Master of Chirurgiae (MCh).

Before INI CET, these institutions conducted individual entrance exams. Now, a single INI CET score is used for admissions to 19 AIIMS campuses, JIPMER Puducherry, Postgraduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGI) Chandigarh, National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS) Bengaluru, and Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology (SCTIMST) Trivandrum. The exam is held twice annually, for the January and July academic sessions.

## Application Process and Eligibility

The application form for INI CET May 2026 was released along with the official prospectus on the AIIMS website on March 28, 2026. Prospective candidates must apply online, as no offline application method is available. The deadline for submitting applications is April 25, 2026. Following this, a correction window will be provided between April 30 and May 2, 2026, for applicants to rectify any errors in their submissions.

A critical eligibility criterion is the completion of the mandatory internship. Candidates must complete their internship on or before July 31, 2026, to be considered eligible for registration and subsequent admission processes. Those who complete their internship after this date will not be eligible to apply.

## Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The INI CET is conducted as a computer-based test (CBT). While the official prospectus provides comprehensive details, generally, the exam covers subjects from the MBBS and BDS curricula, aligning with the postgraduate medical and dental studies. The question paper typically includes multiple-choice questions designed to assess a candidate's knowledge across various disciplines.

## Admit Card and Results

The admit cards for INI CET May 2026 will be made available for download on May 9, 2026. Candidates must download and print their admit cards from the official AIIMS portal. It is essential to carry the admit card to the examination center. The results for the INI CET May 2026 exam are expected to be announced by May 23, 2026. Qualified candidates will then be informed about the subsequent counselling process.

Stay updated with official announcements on the AIIMS website for precise information regarding the syllabus, pattern, and counselling procedures.
