# The Scholar Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online The Scholar Courses

BeingWise offers an overview of various online learning opportunities, including free and paid courses and certifications, to help you navigate your academic and career journey. This page specifically highlights programs offered by The Scholar, designed to equip you with in-demand skills for the future.

## Key Takeaways

*   The Scholar offers specialized Post Graduate (PG) Certification programs in Data Science and Data Analytics.
*   These programs are designed with industry relevance, taught by experienced professionals, and aim to prepare students for specific career roles.
*   Course content includes popular programming languages like Python, along with essential tools and concepts in statistics, machine learning, and data visualization.
*   Both certifications are paid programs, varying in duration and fees, and are delivered through an online platform.

## PG Certification in Data Science

This Post Graduate Certification in Data Science is a comprehensive program designed to introduce individuals to the world of data science, regardless of their prior experience. It aims to prepare students for a variety of exciting and high-demand careers in this rapidly evolving field.

The curriculum delves deep into essential coding languages and critical skills. You will gain proficiency in Python-based data science, traditional programming principles, core mathematics, statistical analysis, and machine learning techniques. The program is structured to provide a thorough understanding of real-world applications, ensuring that the knowledge you acquire is directly applicable in professional settings.

Industry experts, each with over eight years of experience, lead the instruction for this online certification. Notable instructors include Mr. Rahul Tiwary, Mr. Rakesh Sharma, Mr. Rajesh Kumar, Mr. Deepak Arora, Ms. Ritu Shukla, and Mr. Muzzammil. The program has a duration of 10 months and is priced at ₹1,40,000.

### Skills Covered:

*   Machine Learning
*   Python Programming

## PG Certification in Data Analytics

The Scholar's PG Certification in Data Analytics is a unique program that blends theoretical knowledge with practical application in data analytics and its industrial uses. The primary goal is to cultivate skilled data analytics professionals, addressing the significant demand for such expertise in the job market.

This program focuses on training individuals to become proficient Data Analysts. Recognizing the existing gap in skilled resources, the course is tailored to develop a talent pool capable of meeting current industry requirements. It familiarizes students with computational statistics applications and provides hands-on experience in solving real-world data analytics challenges.

The online curriculum for this PG Certification in Data Analytics covers essential tools and languages such as Python, PowerBI, Tableau, and SQL. The course is taught by highly experienced industry mentors, including Mr. Rahul Tiwary, Mr. Rakesh Sharma, and Ms. Ritu Shukla. The program spans 6 months and costs ₹95,000.

### Skills Covered:

*   Python Programming
*   SQL Knowledge
*   Tableau Knowledge

## Who Are These Certifications For?

These PG certifications are ideal for fresh graduates, early-career professionals, or anyone looking to reskill or upskill in the fields of data science and data analytics. The programs are structured to provide a strong foundation and practical skills necessary for immediate application in industry roles. Whether you are aiming for a career as a Data Scientist or a Data Analyst, these structured online learning paths offer a credible way to achieve your goals.

We encourage you to explore these programs to enhance your skill set and open doors to new career opportunities in the dynamic world of data.
