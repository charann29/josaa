# Discontinued Mar(2026) MBA in International Business by UPES Online: Fee, Review, Duration

For those considering an MBA in International Business from UPES Online, it's important to note that this specific program is scheduled to be discontinued in March 2026. This article provides a comprehensive overview of the program's structure, fee, and what it aimed to offer, to help prospective students understand its historical context.

## Key takeaways

*   The MBA in International Business from UPES Online is slated for discontinuation in March 2026.
*   It was an online, two-year postgraduate degree designed for working professionals.
*   The total program fee was ₹1.75 Lakh, with an option for installment payments.
*   The curriculum focused on developing techno-commercial, legal, and leadership skills essential for global business operations.

## Program Overview

The MBA in International Business offered by UPES Online was a postgraduate degree program spanning 24 months. Delivered entirely online, it was specifically tailored to meet the needs of working professionals seeking to advance their careers in the global business landscape. The program aimed to cultivate professionals with a strong grasp of international business management, encompassing technical, commercial, legal, and leadership competencies.

## Course Details and Curriculum Focus

This MBA program was crafted by industry experts with a clear vision: to equip students with the skills needed to navigate the complexities of international trade and business. The curriculum was designed to cover a broad spectrum of topics, including:

*   Human Resource Management in a global context
*   Foreign Trade principles and practices
*   International Logistics and supply chain management
*   Project Management for international ventures
*   International Marketing strategies
*   Understanding international agreements and investment frameworks
*   Integrating economics, geopolitics, legal, and cultural dimensions in multinational operations

The program emphasized explaining business expansion abroad, identifying key operational issues in diverse countries, and developing effective market entry strategies. Students were expected to learn how to compare and contrast global cultures and societies using relevant frameworks, and gain expertise in the legal and policy frameworks governing international trade.

## Who Was This Program For?

The MBA in International Business was particularly recommended for professionals across various sectors who aspired to build a career in international trade or global business. It was highly suitable for individuals working in:

*   Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG)
*   Textile industry
*   Export houses
*   Logistics companies
*   Export-oriented manufacturing firms

The course aimed to provide these professionals with an effective understanding of overseas markets, international agreements, and the dynamics of global trade and investment. It also sought to foster an understanding of both local and global perspectives on international business operations.

## Fee Structure and Payment Options

The total fee for the two-year online MBA in International Business program at UPES Online was ₹1.75 Lakh. A significant highlight for many students was the flexibility to pay this fee in installments, making the program more accessible to working professionals managing their finances.

## Important Note on Discontinuation

Prospective students should be aware that while this program offered a comprehensive curriculum, it is scheduled to be discontinued in March 2026. Those interested in similar specializations should explore other available programs or contact UPES Online directly for information on alternative offerings.
