# UPCAT Counselling 2022: Dates, Application Form, Eligibility, Seat Allotment

For students aspiring to join various professional courses in Uttar Pradesh, the counselling process is a crucial step. This guide outlines the key details for UPCAT Counselling in 2022, helping you navigate admissions to institutions affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and other state universities.

## Key takeaways

*   **Exam Transition**: The UPCET exam (formerly UPSEE) has been discontinued. Admissions for 2022 are now based on national-level exams like CUET, JEE Main, and NATA.
*   **Undergraduate Admissions**: For B.Tech, B.Des, B.Pharm, BHMCT, and BFA/BFAD, admissions rely on JEE Main, CUET (UG), and NATA scores.
*   **Postgraduate Admissions**: MBA, MCA, M.Tech, M.Pharm, M.Arch, M.Des, and MURP admissions are conducted through CUET (PG).
*   **Integrated Courses**: Integrated MBA and Integrated MCA courses use CUET (UG) scores for admission.

## Understanding the New Admission Landscape

Previously known as UPSEE and later UPCET, the Uttar Pradesh Combined Entrance Test served as the primary gateway for admissions to numerous undergraduate and postgraduate programs across state universities in Uttar Pradesh. However, for the 2022 academic year, the National Testing Agency (NTA) has transitioned admissions to recognized national entrance examinations.

This means that aspiring students will apply for colleges affiliated with AKTU, Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT) Gorakhpur, and Harcourt Butler Technical University (HBTU) Kanpur, among others, using their scores from:

*   **JEE Main**: For B.Tech and B.Arch programs.
*   **CUET (UG)**: For various undergraduate courses including B.Des, B.Pharm, BHMCT, BFAD/BFA, Integrated MBA, and Integrated MCA.
*   **NATA**: Specifically for B.Arch programs.
*   **CUET (PG)**: For postgraduate courses such as MBA, MCA, M.Tech, M.Pharm, M.Arch, M.Des, and MURP.

## Eligibility Criteria for Counselling

To be eligible for counselling, candidates generally need to meet specific academic and domicile requirements. While there is no age limit for most courses, key criteria include:

*   **Academic Qualification**: Candidates must have passed the relevant qualifying examination (e.g., 10+2 for UG courses, bachelor's degree for PG courses) from an institution located in Uttar Pradesh.
*   **Domicile Rule**: If a candidate has not completed their qualifying examination from an institution in Uttar Pradesh, they must provide a domicile certificate for their parents from Uttar Pradesh. However, candidates who meet all other academic criteria but not the domicile requirements may still be considered under specific conditions.
*   **National Exam Scores**: A valid score in the respective national-level entrance exam (JEE Main, CUET UG, NATA, or CUET PG) is mandatory.

## Application and Counselling Process

The counselling process for admissions through these national exams typically involves several stages, managed by the respective state counselling authorities or AKTU. While specific dates for 2022 counselling rounds would have been announced by the official counselling portals, the general steps include:

1.  **Online Registration**: Candidates register on the official counselling portal, providing personal and academic details.
2.  **Fee Payment**: A counselling registration fee is paid online.
3.  **Choice Filling and Locking**: Aspirants select their preferred courses and institutions from the available options. It's crucial to list choices in order of preference.
4.  **Seat Allotment**: Based on merit, preferences, and seat availability, candidates are allotted seats.
5.  **Document Verification**: Allotted candidates must report to designated centres for verification of their original documents.
6.  **Fee Submission**: Upon successful verification, candidates pay the admission fee to confirm their seat.

## Seat Allotment and Reservation

Seat allotment is a merit-based process, taking into account the candidate's rank in the respective entrance exam, their chosen preferences, and the availability of seats. Uttar Pradesh also follows specific reservation policies as per government norms, ensuring opportunities for various categories. These typically include reservations for SC, ST, OBC (non-creamy layer), EWS, and other special categories like physically handicapped, freedom fighter dependents, and armed forces dependents.

For precise details on reservation percentages and category definitions, candidates should refer to the official information brochure released by the counselling authority for the 2022 admissions.

Navigating the counselling process effectively requires careful attention to detail and adherence to deadlines. Stay informed by regularly checking official announcements from AKTU and the NTA.
