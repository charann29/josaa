# Kerala Plus two Migration Certificate 2026: Your Guide to Applying

If you've successfully completed your Kerala Plus Two examination and plan to pursue higher education outside Kerala, you'll need a migration certificate. This document is issued by the Directorate of Higher Secondary Education (DHSE) and is essential for admission to colleges and universities in other states.

## Key Takeaways

*   The Kerala Plus Two migration certificate is mandatory for students moving out of Kerala for higher studies.
*   The Directorate of Higher Secondary Education (DHSE) is the issuing authority.
*   The application process is primarily offline, requiring submission of specific documents and a fee.
*   Students should apply soon after the Kerala 12th results are announced.

## Understanding the Kerala Higher Secondary Migration Certificate

The migration certificate serves as official proof that you have completed your higher secondary education from the Kerala state board and are now eligible to transfer to another educational board or institution. It's a standard requirement for inter-state academic transitions. The DHSE typically makes these certificates available after the Kerala 12th results are declared, which is usually around May each year. For 2026, the Kerala Plus Two exams are scheduled between March 6 and March 28.

Here's a quick overview of the application essentials:

| Particulars | Details |
| :-------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- |
| Issuing Authority | Directorate of Higher Secondary Education (DHSE) |
| Document Type | Kerala 12th Migration Certificate |
| Application Method | Offline |
| Application Fee | INR 50 |

## Documents Required for Application

When you submit your application for the Kerala 12th Migration Certificate, ensure you include the following necessary documents:

*   An attested copy of your Plus Two certificate.
*   The original treasury challan for INR 50, clearly indicating the title '0202-01-102-97(03)' for 'Other Receipts'. This is proof of your fee payment.
*   A self-addressed long post cover (dimensions 9.35 x 4.25 inches) with appropriate postage, so the certificate can be mailed to you.

## Step-by-Step Application Process

Applying for your migration certificate involves a few straightforward steps:

1.  **Obtain the Application Form:** Download the official application form for the Kerala 12th Migration certificate.
2.  **Complete the Form:** Fill in all required personal details accurately. This includes your name, parent's name, the year you passed your examination, your Plus Two registration number (as found on your admit card), and your complete postal address.
3.  **Principal's Recommendation:** Ensure the recommendation letter section on the form is duly filled and signed by your school principal. This step is crucial for validation.
4.  **Submission:** Compile the completed application form along with all the required documents mentioned above. Send this package to the Joint Director (Exam), Directorate of Higher Secondary Education, Housing Board Buildings, Santhinagar, Thiruvananthapuram-1.

## Important Instructions for Submission

There are specific guidelines depending on when you apply:

*   **Within One Year of Examination:** If you are applying for the migration certificate within one year of clearing your Plus Two examination, you should submit your application directly to your school Principal.
*   **After One Year or for Old Scheme:** For all other cases, including applications submitted more than one year after the examination or for students under the Old Scheme, the application, with a recommendation from your Principal, must be sent directly to the Joint Director (Exam) at the DHSE office in Thiruvananthapuram. Remember to attach a self-addressed stamped envelope and the original challan receipt for the fee.

Obtaining your migration certificate is a simple but vital step for your academic journey outside Kerala. Make sure to follow these instructions carefully to ensure a smooth process.
