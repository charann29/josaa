# Full-Stack Android Development by Masai School: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Are you aspiring to become an Android Developer? Masai School offers a comprehensive Full-Stack Android Development program designed to equip you with the skills needed for this in-demand career path.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is a full-time, intensive 30-week course focused on Android development.
*   It operates on an Income Share Agreement (ISA) model, meaning you pay fees only after securing a job with a salary of ₹5 LPA or more.
*   Training covers modern tools like Java, Kotlin, Spring Boot, and essential concepts such as System Design and Android architectures.
*   Eligibility requires a Class 12 pass, successful completion of the MSAT entrance test, and being between 18 and 28 years old.

## Program Overview

This 30-week Full-Stack Android Development course is crafted to align with current industry demands, guiding students toward becoming proficient Android developers. The curriculum is delivered through a blend of virtual and campus-based learning, with classes available on both weekdays and weekends. The instruction medium is English, utilizing video and text-based materials.

Students engage in daily stand-up sessions with peers to review study materials and receive coding assistance from mentors (Sherpas) and Teaching Assistants (TAs) via dedicated communication channels. The program also incorporates meditation classes to support student well-being.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to provide a deep understanding of Android software development and full-stack principles. Key learning areas include:

*   **Android Development:** Mastering Android architectures, designing user interfaces, and understanding application lifecycle management, such as pausing media when an app is not visible.
*   **Backend Development:** Gaining practical insights into creating REST APIs and connecting them to databases, along with visualizing how design principles apply to backend systems.
*   **Core Technologies:** Working with essential tools and languages like Java, Kotlin, Android, Gradle, Spring Boot, as well as Algorithms (ALGO) and Data Structures (DS).
*   **Project-Based Learning:** Applying concepts through various projects, discussing learnings in group settings, and exploring multiple approaches to problem-solving.

## Fees and Payment Structure

Masai School's Full-Stack Android Development program operates under an Income Share Agreement (ISA), making it free to pursue upfront. The fee structure is as follows:

*   **Upfront Cost:** The course has no initial fee.
*   **Post-Placement Payment:** Once you secure a job with an annual income of ₹5 Lakh or more, you will pay 15% of your monthly income for three years, or until a total of ₹3,00,000 (including GST) has been paid, whichever comes first.

Upon successful completion of the program, Masai School awards a Certificate of Completion.

## Eligibility and Application Process

To be eligible for the Full-Stack Android Development program, candidates must meet the following criteria:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants must have successfully passed Class 12.
*   **Age Limit:** Candidates should be between 18 and 28 years of age.
*   **Admission Test:** Prospective students need to qualify for the Masai School Admissions Test (MSAT). This aptitude test assesses skills such as general aptitude, typing speed, computer literacy, and attention to detail.

The application process typically involves submitting an online application, followed by taking the MSAT. Successful candidates are then offered admission to the program.

This program offers a structured and industry-focused pathway for individuals keen on building a career in Android development.
