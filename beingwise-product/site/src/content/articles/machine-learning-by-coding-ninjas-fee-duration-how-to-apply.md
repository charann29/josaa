# Machine Learning by Coding Ninjas: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students passionate about coding and keen to explore the world of artificial intelligence, the online Machine Learning certification program from Coding Ninjas offers a comprehensive learning pathway. This program is designed to equip learners with essential skills in machine learning, preparing them for modern industry demands.

## Key takeaways

*   The program offers flexible learning, combining self-study with virtual classroom sessions and live mentor support.
*   It emphasizes practical application through six projects and provides placement assistance upon completion.
*   A strong foundation in programming fundamentals and data structures is a prerequisite for enrollment.
*   Successful certification requires achieving at least 60% in program assessments.

## Program Structure and Learning Experience

The Machine Learning course by Coding Ninjas is delivered through a blend of video tutorials and text-based content, allowing students to learn at their own pace. The estimated learning commitment is between 12 to 18 hours per week, making it suitable for those balancing other commitments. The program includes over 35 hours of video content and features an interactive classroom experience with support from well-trained faculty and live mentors.

Students also benefit from a 15-day course extension option, providing flexibility if they need more time to complete the modules. The curriculum is meticulously crafted to cover contemporary requirements in the field of machine learning.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this certification, students will gain a solid understanding of key machine learning concepts and practical skills. The syllabus is divided into two main sections:

### Data Structures and Algorithms

This foundational module ensures participants have a strong grasp of programming essentials before diving into machine learning. It covers:

*   **Introduction to Programming:** Basics, loops, functions, arrays/lists, strings, and 2D lists.
*   **Data Structures & Algorithms:** Problem-solving techniques, Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), linear data structures, trees, advanced data structures, and dynamic programming.

### Machine Learning

This core module delves into advanced topics, providing a comprehensive overview of machine learning principles and applications:

*   **Introduction to Machine Learning**
*   **Linear and Logistic Regression**
*   **Decision Trees and Random Forests**
*   **Naive Bayes, KNN, and SVM**
*   **Principal Component Analysis (PCA)**
*   **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**
*   **Neural Networks, TensorFlow, and Keras**
*   **Deep Learning Algorithms**

Throughout the course, students will also be introduced to the fundamentals of Python, a crucial language in machine learning.

## Fees and Certification

The program offers various fee plans, reflecting different levels of access or features. While a basic plan may be advertised at around ₹14,000, other plans are available at higher price points, such as ₹22,999, ₹30,999, and ₹36,999. It's important to note that these fees do not include Goods and Services Tax (GST), and pricing can vary based on monthly admissions batches. For precise and up-to-date fee details, students should consult the official course webpage. A 7-day refund policy is available for those who decide to withdraw after enrollment.

Upon successful completion, Coding Ninjas issues a certificate to students who achieve a minimum of 60% in their course assessments.

## Eligibility and Application Process

To be eligible for the Machine Learning certification program, applicants must possess a good understanding of data structures and be well-acquainted with programming fundamentals. There are no specific educational qualifications beyond this programming prerequisite.

Applying for the course involves a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official course page on the Coding Ninjas website.
2.  Select your preferred language for the course content.
3.  Choose your desired enrollment month and click the "Enrol now" option.
4.  Proceed to deposit the applicable course fee.

This structured approach ensures that dedicated students can easily access and benefit from the program.
