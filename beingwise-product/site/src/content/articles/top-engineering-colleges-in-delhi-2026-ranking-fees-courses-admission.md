## Admission Process and Eligibility Criteria 2026

Admission to top engineering colleges in Delhi typically involves a multi-stage process, beginning with academic performance in Class 12 and culminating in competitive entrance examination scores. For B.Tech programs in government colleges, candidates must meet specific eligibility criteria, including a minimum percentage in their Class 12 examinations (often 50% or higher, depending on the institution and category).

The primary entrance examination for most top engineering colleges in Delhi is JEE Main. Candidates must achieve a good score in this national-level test conducted by NTA. Additionally, for admission to state-funded institutions like DTU, NSUT, and IGDTUW, candidates participate in the Joint Admission Counselling (JAC Delhi) process, which considers JEE Main scores. The JEE Main 2026 session 2 exams are scheduled from April 2 to April 9, 2026.
