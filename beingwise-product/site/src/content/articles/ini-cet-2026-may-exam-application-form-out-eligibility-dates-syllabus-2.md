# INI CET 2026 (May Exam): Application Form (OUT), Eligibility, Dates, Syllabus, Pattern, Cutoff

The Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test (INI CET) is a crucial gateway for aspiring medical and dental postgraduates in India. Conducted by AIIMS Delhi, this exam opens doors to prestigious MD, MS, MDS, DM, and M.Ch courses across several top institutions.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Application Window:** The application period for the INI CET May 2026 exam is from March 28 to April 25, 2026. 
*   **Exam Date:** The computer-based test is scheduled for May 16, 2026.
*   **Eligibility:** Candidates must complete their internship by July 31, 2026, to be eligible.
*   **Institutions:** Scores are accepted by 19 AIIMS, JIPMER Puducherry, PGI Chandigarh, NIMHANS Bengaluru, and SCTIMST Trivandrum.

## Understanding INI CET

INI CET is a national-level postgraduate entrance examination administered by the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) Delhi. It serves as the sole admission test for various postgraduate medical and dental programs, including Master of Surgery (MS), Doctor of Medicine (MD), Master of Dental Surgery (MDS), Doctorate of Medicine (DM), and Master of Chirurgiae (MCh). This examination streamlines the admission process for institutions that previously held separate entrance tests.

## Application Process and Eligibility

The application form for INI CET 2026 (May exam) was released online on March 28, 2026, along with the official prospectus. Aspiring candidates must complete their applications by April 25, 2026. It is crucial to note that the application process is entirely online. AIIMS will also provide a window for correcting application details from April 30 to May 2, 2026.

### Eligibility Criteria

One of the most critical eligibility requirements is the completion of the mandatory internship. Candidates must complete their internship on or before July 31, 2026. Those whose internships conclude after this date will not be eligible to register for the examination.

## Exam Pattern and Syllabus

INI CET is conducted as a computer-based test (CBT). While the official prospectus provides comprehensive details, generally, the exam covers subjects from the MBBS and BDS curriculum. The questions are designed to assess a candidate's knowledge across preclinical, paraclinical, and clinical subjects. A thorough understanding of the core concepts taught during undergraduate medical and dental studies is essential for success.

## Admit Card and Results

The admit cards for INI CET May 2026 will be made available for download on May 9, 2026. Candidates are advised to download and print their admit cards well in advance of the examination date. Following the exam, the results will be declared by May 23, 2026. Qualified candidates will then be notified regarding the subsequent counselling process for seat allocation.
