# MBA in Financial Services and Financial Planning by Chitkara University Online: Fee, Review, Duration

Chitkara University Online offers a specialized Master of Business Administration (MBA) program focusing on Financial Services and Financial Planning. This online degree is designed for individuals looking to deepen their understanding of the financial sector and advance their careers in this dynamic field.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a two-year, online postgraduate degree program from Chitkara University.
*   The total program fee is ₹2.25 Lakh, with options for installment payments.
*   It targets working professionals and others aiming for career growth in financial services or personal wealth management.
*   The curriculum covers a broad spectrum of financial topics, from investment strategies to estate planning and business finance.

## Program Overview

The online MBA in Financial Services and Financial Planning is structured to equip students with the essential knowledge and skills needed to excel in the finance industry. The program emphasizes both business advisory and financial advisory, making it suitable for those aspiring to roles where they can guide financial decisions for individuals or organizations. Being an online course, it offers flexibility for learners to study from anywhere.

### Program Details

| Feature           | Detail                                        |
| :---------------- | :-------------------------------------------- |
| **Duration**      | 24 months (2 years)                           |
| **Total Fee**     | ₹2.25 Lakh                                    |
| **Learning Mode** | Online                                        |
| **Course Level**  | Postgraduate Degree                           |

## Who Is This Program For?

This MBA is particularly well-suited for several groups:

*   **Working Professionals:** Individuals currently employed in the financial services sector who wish to upskill, gain a formal qualification, and move into more senior or specialized roles.
*   **Aspiring Entrepreneurs:** Those looking to start their own financial advisory firms or manage investments for clients.
*   **Homemakers:** Individuals interested in gaining comprehensive knowledge to manage family wealth effectively and avoid common financial pitfalls.
*   **Career Changers:** Professionals from other fields seeking a transition into the lucrative financial services industry.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to provide a 360-degree view of financial services and planning. Key areas of learning include:

*   **Investment Strategies:** Understanding various investment avenues like equity, bonds, mutual funds, real estate, and even emerging assets like cryptocurrencies.
*   **Financial Planning Tools:** Mastering techniques for personal and corporate financial planning, including estate planning and wealth transfer strategies.
*   **Risk Management:** Analyzing financial statements, evaluating investment opportunities, and managing financial risks effectively.
*   **Business Banking & Finance:** Gaining a technical understanding of economic policies and financial markets, and their impact on businesses, including MSMEs.
*   **Digital Financial Skills:** While the source highlights skills like digital marketing, email marketing, and SEO, the core focus remains on applying innovative financial models and managing various financial products to optimize return-risk parameters for clients.

Upon completion, students are expected to be capable of designing client portfolios, incorporating derivatives for hedging, and strategically utilizing financial products. The program also aims to foster an understanding of managing wealth for both domestic and NRI clients, whether individuals or organizations.

## Placement Support and Networking

Chitkara University Online provides comprehensive 360-degree placement support for its MBA graduates. This includes assistance with resume building and interview preparation, helping students to present themselves effectively to potential employers. The online format also encourages networking possibilities through discussions, allowing students to exchange ideas and perspectives, which enhances their learning experience and professional connections.

This MBA offers a robust pathway for individuals seeking to make a significant impact in the world of finance.
