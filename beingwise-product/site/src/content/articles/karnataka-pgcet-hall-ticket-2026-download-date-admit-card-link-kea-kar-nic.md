# Karnataka PGCET Hall Ticket 2026: Download Date, Admit Card Link @kea.kar.nic.in

The Karnataka PGCET Hall Ticket is an essential document for all candidates appearing in the Karnataka Post Graduate Common Entrance Test. Issued by the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA), this admit card is your gateway to the examination hall for various postgraduate courses.

## Key Takeaways

*   The Karnataka PGCET hall ticket is released online by the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) on its official website.
*   Candidates need their application number and password to download the admit card.
*   A physical printout of the hall ticket is mandatory for entry into the examination centre.
*   The hall ticket is issued only to those who successfully complete their application form before the deadline.

## Understanding the Karnataka PGCET Hall Ticket

The Karnataka PGCET is a state-level entrance examination for admission to postgraduate courses like MBA/PGDM, MCA, ME, M.Tech, M.Tech Lateral Entry, and M.Arch. The hall ticket, also known as the admit card, is a crucial document that verifies your eligibility to appear for the exam. It contains vital information such as your name, roll number, exam date, time, and the assigned test centre.

## How to Download Your Karnataka PGCET Admit Card 2026

The process for downloading your hall ticket is straightforward and can be completed online. Follow these steps:

1.  **Visit the Official KEA Website:** Navigate to the official website of KEA.
2.  **Locate the Admissions Section:** On the homepage, find and click on the 'Admissions' tab.
3.  **Select PGCET-2026:** From the options, choose 'PGCET-2026'.
4.  **Find the Hall Ticket Link:** Look for the specific link to download the Karnataka PGCET 2026 Hall Ticket. This link will be updated on the official KEA portal.
5.  **Enter Login Credentials:** You will be prompted to enter your Karnataka PGCET application number and password.
6.  **Download and Print:** After successful login, your hall ticket will appear on the screen. Download it and take a clear printout. It is advisable to print multiple copies for safety.

## Essential Details on Your Hall Ticket

Upon downloading, carefully review all the information printed on your Karnataka PGCET hall ticket. It typically includes:

*   Candidate's Name
*   Roll Number
*   Application Number
*   Date of Birth
*   Category
*   Photograph and Signature
*   Course Applied For
*   Examination Date and Time
*   Reporting Time
*   Examination Centre Name and Address
*   Important Exam Day Instructions

If you find any discrepancies in the details, it is crucial to contact the KEA authorities immediately for correction.

## Documents to Carry on Exam Day

On the day of the examination, ensure you carry the following:

*   **Printed Karnataka PGCET 2026 Hall Ticket:** A mandatory document without which entry will be denied.
*   **Valid Photo ID Proof:** This could be your Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Driving License, Passport, or Voter ID.
*   **Passport-sized Photographs:** It is advisable to carry a couple of recent passport-sized photographs, similar to the one uploaded in your application form.

Remember to reach the examination centre well before the reporting time to avoid any last-minute rush.
