# Data Analysis with Excel for Complete Beginners by CloudSwyft Global Systems, Inc via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to step into the world of data analysis, this beginner-friendly online course offers a solid foundation using Microsoft Excel. It's designed to introduce core concepts and practical skills for managing, analysing, and visualising data.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn at your own pace with a self-study format, combining video and text-based materials.
*   The course is delivered entirely in English, making it accessible to a wide audience.
*   Expect to dedicate approximately 5 hours per week to complete the course material.
*   Upon successful completion, a certificate is available directly from Futurelearn.

## Course Structure and Learning Approach

This program is structured for complete beginners, breaking down complex topics into digestible modules. The learning is self-paced, allowing students to fit their studies around existing commitments. Content is delivered through a mix of video lessons and text-based explanations, ensuring different learning styles are accommodated. Hands-on labs are integrated to provide practical experience with Excel.

## Fees and Certification

Enrolling in the Data Analysis with Excel for Complete Beginners course incurs a monthly fee of Rs. 900. A certificate of completion is available for students who successfully finish the program. This certificate is issued by Futurelearn, the platform hosting the course.

## Comprehensive Syllabus Breakdown

The course is divided into several key sections, each focusing on a different aspect of data analysis using Excel:

### Introduction to Data Analysis using Excel for Absolute Beginners

This introductory module sets the stage by exploring the significance of data in today's world and the concept of data fluency. It covers an initial data walkthrough, helping learners understand the basic flow of data analysis.

*   **Course Introduction:** Overview of the program and its objectives.
*   **Our Data-Driven World:** Understanding the prevalence and importance of data.
*   **Data Fluency:** Developing a fundamental understanding of data concepts.
*   **Your First Data Walkthrough:** A guided initial experience with data.
*   **Unpacking the Walkthrough:** Deconstructing the steps and insights from the walkthrough.

### Data Structure, Analysis and Visualisation

This section delves into the foundational elements of data, including how data is organised, different methods for analysis, and the basics of presenting data visually.

*   **Data Structures:** Understanding various ways data can be organised.
*   **Data Anatomy:** Dissecting the components of a dataset.
*   **Data Joins:** Combining data from multiple sources.
*   **Data Analysis Methods:** Introduction to common analytical techniques.
*   **Data Visualisation:** Creating basic visual representations of data.

### Data Analysis in Context

Here, the course broadens its scope to discuss the broader implications of data analysis, including ethical considerations and potential career paths.

*   **Data Analysis in Context:** Applying analysis in real-world scenarios.
*   **Data Ethics:** Understanding responsible data handling.
*   **Career Paths:** Exploring job opportunities in data analysis.

### How to Use Microsoft Excel for Data Analysis

This practical module focuses specifically on Excel's capabilities for data handling, reporting, and analysis, featuring multiple hands-on lab exercises.

*   **Introduction to Excel Table and Pivot Table Fundamentals:** Basics of organising and summarising data in Excel.
*   **Reporting, Analysing and Comparing Data in Excel:** Creating dashboards, performing profitability analysis, and comparing datasets using advanced Excel features.

### Data Visualisation Fundamentals for Absolute Beginners

Building on earlier concepts, this module dedicates itself to the art and science of effective data visualisation, from basic charts to advanced business visualisations.

*   **Welcome to the Course:** An overview of visualisation principles.
*   **Visualisations are Everywhere:** Recognising the role of visuals in daily life.
*   **Visualisation Fundamentals:** Core principles of creating effective visuals.
*   **Visuals for Comparing and Relating:** Choosing appropriate charts for comparison.
*   **Visuals for Interacting and Analysing Data:** Designing interactive visual tools.
*   **Business Visualisations and Visualisations of Tomorrow:** Applying visuals in a business context and exploring future trends.

### Essential Mathematics for Data Analysis in Microsoft Excel

This final section covers the fundamental mathematical and statistical concepts crucial for robust data analysis, all within the Excel environment.

*   **Introduction to Working with Data:** Understanding data types, variables, and basic notation.
*   **Summarising and Graphically Representing Data:** Using statistical measures and graphs to summarise datasets.
*   **Business Statistics and KPIs:** Applying statistical concepts to business metrics and an introduction to forecasting.

This comprehensive program equips beginners with the necessary skills to confidently approach data analysis using one of the most widely used tools in the business world, Microsoft Excel.
