# TANCET 2026 Registration (Extended): Last Date (Apr 15), Direct Link, Fees & How to fill Form

For those aiming to pursue MBA or MCA programs in Tamil Nadu, the TANCET 2026 registration process is currently underway. Anna University, the conducting body, has extended the application deadline, offering candidates more time to complete their submissions.

## Key Takeaways

*   The application window for TANCET 2026 has been extended, with the final submission date now set for April 15, 2026.
*   Registration is exclusively online through the official TANCET website, tancet.annauniv.edu.
*   Prospective students need to provide personal, academic, and contact details, and upload specific documents like a photograph and signature.
*   A community certificate (for SC/ST/SCA candidates) is required to avail fee concessions.

## Documents and Information Needed for Application

Before you begin filling out the TANCET 2026 application form, it's wise to gather all necessary details and documents. This preparation will help ensure a smooth and error-free submission. Anna University may place applications on hold or even reject them if there are inaccuracies or issues with uploaded documents.

### Details Required:

*   A working email address and a valid mobile phone number.
*   Your Class 10 registration number/roll number and year of passing.
*   Your Class 12 registration number and year of passing.
*   Bank account details for making the online application fee payment.

### Documents to Upload:

Ensure these documents meet the specified format and size requirements to avoid any problems with your application.

*   **Colour Passport-Size Photograph:** Your recent photograph.
*   **Scanned Signature:** A clear image of your signature.
*   **Community Certificate (PDF):** For candidates belonging to SC/ST/SCA categories, this is essential for fee concessions.

## Image and Document Upload Specifications

Adhering to the specific upload requirements for your photograph, signature, and community certificate is critical. Any deviation might lead to your application being temporarily withheld until corrections are made.

## How to Complete Your TANCET 2026 Application

Filling the TANCET 2026 application form is a multi-step online process. Follow these instructions carefully to ensure your submission is successful:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Go to the official TANCET portal at tancet.annauniv.edu.
2.  **New User Registration:** Click on the 'New User Registration' link. You will need to provide your name, date of birth, email ID, and mobile number to create your login credentials.
3.  **Login:** After successful registration, log in using the credentials you just created.
4.  **Fill Application Details:** Carefully enter all required personal information, academic qualifications, and communication details in the respective sections of the form.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Upload your scanned photograph, signature, and community certificate (if applicable), ensuring they meet the specified format, dimensions, and size requirements.
6.  **Review and Verify:** Before proceeding, thoroughly review all the information you've entered and the documents you've uploaded. Make sure everything is accurate and correctly positioned.
7.  **Pay Application Fee:** Proceed to the payment gateway to pay the TANCET application fee online using net banking, credit card, or debit card. The fee structure typically varies by category.
8.  **Final Submission:** Once the payment is successful, submit your application form. It is highly recommended to download and print a copy of your submitted application and the payment receipt for your records.

Completing these steps accurately will ensure your TANCET 2026 application is successfully submitted and considered for admission.
