# IBSAT Exam Dates 2026: Check Registration, Admit Card, & Result Dates Here

For students aiming to pursue an MBA or PGPM program at one of the ICFAI Business School (IBS) campuses, understanding the IBSAT 2026 examination schedule is crucial. This page provides a clear overview of the key dates for registration, admit card availability, the exam itself, and result announcements.

## Key takeaways

*   IBSAT 2026 registration is expected to begin in July 2026 and conclude by late December 2026.
*   The examination will likely take place in the last week of December 2026, conducted in an online proctored format.
*   Admit cards will be available for download shortly after candidates successfully book their exam slots.
*   Results are typically announced in late December 2026, followed by selection rounds in February 2027.

## IBSAT 2026 Registration Dates

Prospective candidates can anticipate the IBSAT 2026 application forms to become available online, likely from July 1, 2026. It's essential for applicants to meet the specified eligibility criteria before completing the form, as failure to do so could lead to cancellation of their candidature. Keeping necessary documents ready beforehand will streamline the application process.

The application window is expected to remain open until the last week of December 2026. Candidates should regularly check the official IBS website for the precise dates and to access the application portal.

## IBSAT 2026 Admit Card and Exam Dates

Once candidates successfully register and pay the application fee (expected to be INR 1800), they will be able to download their admit cards. The admit card becomes available soon after an exam slot is booked. It is vital for candidates to use their login details to download this document, as it contains important information such as candidate details and exam center specifics.

The IBSAT 2026 examination is typically conducted over a two-day window, with the test anticipated in the last week of December 2026. This computer-based test will be delivered in an online proctored mode, allowing candidates to take it from a suitable location. Booking an exam slot is mandatory to appear for the test, and slots are usually assigned on a first-come, first-served basis.

## IBSAT 2026 Result and Selection Process Dates

The results for IBSAT 2026 are expected to be declared online shortly after the examination, likely in the last week of December 2026. Candidates will need their login credentials to access and download their scorecards, which will display both section-wise and overall scores.

Following the declaration of results, ICFAI Business School will commence its selection rounds. These are generally held at the IBS Hyderabad campus, with the process for IBSAT 2026 expected to begin in February 2027. Shortlisted candidates must report to the designated center on the specified date and time. The selection process typically includes a micro-presentation and a personal interview. Final admission results for the 2027 intake are usually announced in the last week of February 2027.
