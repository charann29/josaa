# Professional Certificate Programme in Data Science with Generative AI by IIIT Bangalore: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This Professional Certificate Programme, offered in collaboration with IIIT Bangalore, is designed for individuals keen to build expertise in the rapidly expanding fields of data science and generative artificial intelligence. It aims to equip learners with essential skills through a practical, hands-on approach.

## Key takeaways

*   A comprehensive 6-month programme focusing on data science and generative AI.
*   No prior coding experience is needed, making it accessible to a wider audience.
*   Features practical elements like capstone projects, case studies, and workshops.
*   Successful completion earns a Professional Certificate from IIIT Bangalore.

## Programme Overview

The curriculum of this certificate programme is carefully structured to provide a strong foundation across core areas. It covers fundamental concepts in programming, data analysis, machine learning, and advanced AI technologies, including generative AI. The programme emphasizes practical application, with participants engaging in various hands-on projects and case studies designed to simulate real-world challenges. By the end of the course, participants are expected to be well-prepared for roles in data analysis, AI development, and related domains.

## What You Will Learn

The programme is meticulously designed to impart a range of critical skills and knowledge:

*   **Python Programming & SQL:** Develop a solid understanding of Python for data science and SQL for efficient data handling and manipulation.
*   **Machine Learning & Deep Learning:** Become proficient in implementing various machine learning algorithms and deep learning techniques to solve complex problems.
*   **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** Gain skills to work effectively with text data and build AI-driven applications that understand and process human language.

## Who Is This Programme For?

This Professional Certificate Programme is ideal for a diverse group of professionals looking to enhance their skill sets or transition into data-centric roles. The target audience includes, but is not limited to:

*   **Data Analysts:** Those looking to deepen their analytical capabilities and move into more advanced AI applications.
*   **Business Analysts:** Professionals aiming to leverage data science and AI for better business insights and decision-making.
*   **Software Engineers:** Engineers interested in specializing in AI development, machine learning, and data-driven software solutions.

## Programme Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Professional Certificate Programme in Data Science with Generative AI is ₹99,000, which includes GST. For those who prefer flexible payment options, an EMI plan is available at approximately ₹4,847 per month.

Upon successful completion of the programme, participants will receive a Professional Certificate directly from IIIT Bangalore, signifying their achievement and acquired expertise.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Professional Certificate Programme involves a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Official Programme Page:** Navigate to the dedicated webpage for the Professional Certificate Programme in Data Science with Generative AI.
2.  **Initiate Application:** Locate and click the 'Apply Now' button, typically found alongside the pricing plans.
3.  **Complete Application Form:** Fill out the required application form with your details.
4.  **Scholarship Test (Optional):** If applicable, attempt the scholarship test to explore potential financial aid.
5.  **Secure Your Seat:** Make the necessary fee payment to confirm your enrollment in the programme.

This programme offers a robust pathway to acquiring in-demand skills in data science and generative AI, backed by the academic rigor of IIIT Bangalore.
