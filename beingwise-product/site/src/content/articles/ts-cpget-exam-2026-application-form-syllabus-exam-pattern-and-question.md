# TS CPGET Exam 2026: Application Form, Syllabus, Exam Pattern & Question Paper

The Telangana State Common Post Graduate Entrance Tests (TS CPGET) is a crucial state-level examination for students aspiring to pursue postgraduate studies in Telangana. Conducted by Osmania University, this test opens doors to a wide array of Master's degrees and other PG diploma programs across several universities in the region.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Application Period:** The application window for TS CPGET 2026 is expected to open in June, with forms available on the official Osmania University website.
*   **Exam Mode:** The entrance examination will be conducted online, typically in August.
*   **Courses Offered:** TS CPGET provides admission to various postgraduate programs including M.Sc, M.A, M.Com, M.Ed, MSW, and PG Diploma courses.
*   **Conducting Body:** Osmania University, Hyderabad, is responsible for organizing and overseeing the TS CPGET.

## Understanding the TS CPGET Entrance Exam

TS CPGET, or Telangana State Common Post Graduate Entrance Test, is an annual examination designed for admission into numerous postgraduate courses across Telangana's state universities. This includes programs like Master of Science (M.Sc), Master of Arts (M.A), Master of Commerce (M.Com), Master of Education (M.Ed), and Master of Social Work (MSW), among others.

The exam itself is an online test, featuring multiple-choice questions, and candidates are given two hours to complete it. Before applying, prospective students must carefully review the eligibility criteria to ensure they meet all requirements.

## Application Process and Important Dates

Candidates interested in TS CPGET 2026 should prepare to apply online through the official Osmania University portal. The application process is anticipated to commence in June. It is essential to submit the application form before the specified deadline.

Upon successful application, candidates will be able to download their admit cards (hall tickets) using their login credentials. These admit cards are mandatory for appearing in the examination, which is generally scheduled for August.

## TS CPGET Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The TS CPGET is an online examination comprising multiple-choice questions. While the exact syllabus varies by subject, candidates should expect questions covering the core concepts of their chosen postgraduate discipline. It is advisable to refer to the official TS CPGET information brochure, once released, for detailed syllabus information for each specific course.

## Post-Exam Procedures

After the TS CPGET results are announced, candidates who qualify based on their scores will be invited to participate in the counselling process. This stage involves document verification and seat allocation based on merit and preferences. Detailed information regarding counselling will be provided by Osmania University after the examination.
