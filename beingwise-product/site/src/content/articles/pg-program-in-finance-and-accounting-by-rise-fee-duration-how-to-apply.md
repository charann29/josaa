# PG Program in Finance and Accounting by RISE: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aiming to build a strong career in the financial services sector, the Post Graduate Program in Finance and Accounting, offered by RISE in collaboration with Grant Thornton, presents a robust online learning opportunity. This program is designed to equip both recent graduates and working professionals with essential skills and knowledge in the dynamic fields of finance and accounting.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an 11-month online program, including a one-month industry project, delivered through a mix of self-study and virtual classrooms.
*   The curriculum covers vital areas like FinTech, blockchain, and cryptocurrency, alongside traditional finance and accounting principles.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹95,000 plus applicable GST, with flexible payment options available.
*   Participants will benefit from live weekend sessions, industry leader interactions, and career assistance.

## Program Overview and Structure

The PG Program in Finance and Accounting is an 11-month journey into the world of financial services, culminating in a practical one-month industry project. Developed jointly by RISE and Grant Thornton, a leading professional services firm, the course content is curated by experienced educators from Grant Thornton.

The program adopts a blended learning approach, combining video and text-based materials with live virtual classroom sessions. Students are expected to dedicate approximately three hours per week to their studies. The comprehensive structure includes live lectures, ongoing assessments, mentorship, and an internship component, ensuring a well-rounded learning experience.

## What You Will Learn

This program is meticulously designed to provide participants with deep insights into managing an organization's financial health. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Financial Management:** Understanding the intricacies of financial structures and how to maintain an organization's financial well-being.
*   **Accounting Proficiency:** Gaining expertise in applying tax accounting principles and other fundamental accounting practices.
*   **FinTech and Emerging Technologies:** Exploring modern financial technologies, including blockchain, cryptocurrency, and the broader FinTech landscape.
*   **Practical Application:** Learning through over 20 case studies and a dedicated one-month industry project to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios.

## Who Is This Program For?

The PG Program in Finance and Accounting is suitable for a diverse group of individuals looking to advance or transition into the finance and accounting sectors:

*   **Working Professionals:** Individuals currently in roles such as finance executives, financial planners, financial advisors, or those in business development and strategy, who wish to enhance their financial acumen.
*   **Recent Graduates:** Those aspiring to careers as auditors, financial analysts, or accountants in leading finance and accounting firms.
*   **Career Changers:** Professionals from other fields seeking a competitive edge by transitioning into the finance industry.

Ideal candidates are typically between 22 and 35 years old, reside in Tier 1 or Tier 2 cities, and have a foundational background in areas like investment, banking, accounts, or payroll.

## Fees and Certification

### Program Fees

The total fee for the PG Program in Finance and Accounting certification is ₹95,000, in addition to applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST). RISE offers flexible payment options to help manage this investment.

| Description   | Amount        |
| :------------ | :------------ |
| Course Fee    | ₹95,000 + GST |

### Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a certification jointly issued by RISE, Grant Thornton, and MIT World Peace University. This certification acknowledges the comprehensive learning and skill development achieved through the program.

## How to Apply

Prospective candidates interested in the PG Program in Finance and Accounting can begin their application process by visiting the official RISE website. The application typically involves completing an online form with personal and educational details.

This program offers a valuable pathway for individuals to gain industry-ready skills and knowledge, backed by the expertise of RISE and Grant Thornton.
