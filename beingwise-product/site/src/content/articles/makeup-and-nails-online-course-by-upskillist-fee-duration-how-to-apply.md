# Makeup & Nails Online Course by Upskillist: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those passionate about beauty and looking to build a career in professional makeup and nail care, the Upskillist Makeup & Nails Online Course offers comprehensive training. This program is designed by experts to equip students with essential skills and knowledge in the fashion and skincare industry.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is a 16-week online program, covering 32 lessons across four modules.
*   It provides a strong foundation in professional makeup, skincare (including men's and hormonal skincare), and various nail application techniques.
*   Students receive a completion certificate from Upskillist, which holds global recognition.
*   A free trial period is available, allowing prospective students to experience the course content before committing.

## Course Structure and Content

This online certification spans 16 weeks, delivering 32 lessons organised into four distinct modules. The curriculum dives deep into various aspects of beauty and skincare, aiming to provide a holistic understanding. You'll explore topics like:

*   **Nail Techniques:** This includes applying nail tips and wraps, acrylic nails, and gel nails.
*   **Skincare Essentials:** Learn about body wraps, addressing different body conditions, SPF and sun care, hormonal skincare, and even specific men's skincare routines.
*   **Hair Removal:** Fundamental methods and techniques for hair removal.
*   **Lifestyle and Acne:** Understanding the impact of lifestyle choices on skin health and strategies for managing acne.
*   **Professional Makeup Skills:** Comprehensive training in applying makeup and foundations professionally.
*   **Pedicures and Eyebrow Enhancements:** Practical skills for these popular beauty services.

Upon successful completion, students will have a solid grasp of beauty basics and be well-prepared to embark on their professional journey as beauty specialists.

## Learning Experience and Support

The Upskillist Makeup & Nails Online Course is delivered through a self-study model, primarily utilising video and text-based lessons in English. The program offers a flexible e-learning environment with several supportive features:

*   **Offline Module Access:** Allowing you to study even without an internet connection.
*   **Assignments and Assessments:** To reinforce learning and track progress.
*   **Continuous Assessment:** Ensuring ongoing evaluation of your understanding.
*   **Chat Support:** Access to assistance for any queries or challenges.
*   **Live Educator Chat Support:** Direct interaction with instructors for personalised guidance.
*   **Lifetime Access:** Once enrolled, you get lifetime access to the course lessons, allowing for review and refreshers whenever needed.

## Certification and Fees

Upon successfully completing the program within the stipulated timeframe, students will receive a certificate from Upskillist. This certification is globally recognised, adding significant value to your professional profile in the beauty industry.

While the course offers a free trial, details regarding the full course fee are typically provided after the trial period or during the application process. The certificate itself is included as part of the program completion.

## Who Should Consider This Course?

This online course is ideally suited for individuals who are passionate about beauty and aspire to build a professional career in the domains of makeup, skincare, and nail care. Whether you're a beginner looking to enter the industry or someone wanting to formalise and enhance existing skills, this program offers a structured pathway to achieve your goals.

## How to Apply

The application process for the Makeup & Nails Online Course is straightforward. Here's a general guide:

1.  **Visit the Official Program Page:** Navigate to the official Upskillist website for the Makeup & Nails Online Course.
2.  **Start Your Free Trial:** Look for and click on the 'Start your free trial' option.
3.  **Answer Initial Questions:** You will be directed to a page with a few questions to answer.
4.  **Provide Personal Details:** After the initial questions, you'll be prompted to enter some personal information, such as your name.
5.  **Create Your Student ID:** Follow the steps to set up your unique student ID.
6.  **Access the Trial:** Once your ID is created, you can access the program for the free trial period. To continue beyond the trial and complete the full course, you will need to make the necessary payment.

This online course offers a fantastic opportunity to gain professional expertise and a recognised qualification in the dynamic beauty sector.
