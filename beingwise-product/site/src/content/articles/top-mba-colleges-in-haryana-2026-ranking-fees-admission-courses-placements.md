# Top MBA Colleges in Haryana 2026: Ranking, Fees, Admission, Courses, Placements

Choosing the right MBA program is a crucial step for your career. Haryana offers several esteemed institutions that provide quality management education, attracting students from across the country. This guide will help you navigate the top MBA colleges in the state, covering their rankings, fee structures, admission processes, and placement opportunities for the 2026 academic year.

## Key takeaways

*   **Leading Institutions:** MDI Gurgaon and IIM Rohtak are consistently ranked among the top management institutes in India.
*   **Diverse Programs:** Colleges in Haryana offer a range of MBA specializations, catering to various career interests.
*   **Competitive Admissions:** Admission to top MBA colleges typically requires strong scores in national-level entrance exams like CAT, XAT, or GMAT, followed by group discussions and personal interviews.
*   **Strong Placements:** Many premier institutes in Haryana boast impressive placement records, with high average salaries reflecting their industry connections and quality education.

## Understanding Rankings: NIRF and Other Indicators

When evaluating MBA colleges, rankings provide a valuable reference point. The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), published by the Ministry of Education (MHRD), is a widely respected and official source. Other publications, such as those by Times, also offer insights. It's important to consider these rankings alongside other factors like faculty expertise, curriculum, and campus culture.

For the 2025 NIRF rankings, several Haryana-based institutions have achieved notable positions nationally:

*   **Management Development Institute (MDI), Gurgaon:** Ranked 9th nationally by NIRF 2025.
*   **Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Rohtak:** Secured the 19th position in the NIRF 2025 rankings.
*   **Great Lakes Institute of Management, Gurgaon:** Ranked 50th nationally by NIRF 2025.
*   **School of Management, BML Munjal University, Gurgaon:** Ranked 77th nationally by NIRF 2025.

### Management Development Institute (MDI), Gurgaon

Known for its rigorous academic programs and strong industry connections, MDI Gurgaon offers a Post Graduate Programme in Management (PGPM) and various other specialized management courses. Its consistent high ranking and excellent placements make it a top choice for aspiring managers.

### IIM Rohtak - Indian Institute of Management

As one of the newer IIMs, IIM Rohtak has quickly established itself as a premier institution. It offers a Post Graduate Programme (PGP) and focuses on developing socially conscious and globally aware business leaders. The institute has a growing reputation for strong placements across diverse sectors.

### Great Lakes Institute of Management, Gurgaon

Great Lakes Gurgaon is recognized for its industry-relevant curriculum and focus on experiential learning. It offers a Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM) designed to prepare students for leadership roles in a dynamic business environment.

### School of Management, BML Munjal University

BML Munjal University's School of Management provides an MBA program with a strong emphasis on practical application and entrepreneurship. The curriculum is designed in collaboration with industry experts, aiming to equip students with contemporary business skills.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to these top MBA colleges is highly competitive and generally follows a multi-stage process:

1.  **Entrance Exams:** Candidates must appear for national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, GMAT, or MAT. Each institution specifies which exams it accepts and the minimum percentile required.
2.  **Application:** Submit an online application to the desired colleges, usually along with an application fee.
3.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are shortlisted based on their entrance exam scores, academic record, work experience (if any), and other criteria.
4.  **Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates participate in a Group Discussion and Personal Interview round, which assesses their communication skills, leadership potential, and overall suitability for the program.
5.  **Final Selection:** A final merit list is prepared based on a composite score that considers entrance exam performance, GD/PI performance, academic profile, and work experience.

**Eligibility Criteria:** While specific criteria vary, common requirements include a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks (or equivalent CGPA) from a recognized university. Final year students are also often eligible to apply.
