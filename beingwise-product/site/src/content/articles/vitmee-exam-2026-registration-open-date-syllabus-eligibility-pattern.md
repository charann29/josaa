# VITMEE Exam 2026 - Registration (Open), Date, Syllabus, Eligibility, Pattern, Admission

The Vellore Institute of Technology Master's Entrance Examination (VITMEE) is your gateway to postgraduate studies in M.Tech and MCA programs at VIT's various campuses. This guide covers all essential details for the 2026 examination, from registration to admission.

## Key takeaways

*   **Application Window:** Registration for VITMEE 2026 began on November 21, 2025, with the deadline for applications set for April 12, 2026.
*   **Exam Dates:** The entrance examination will be conducted on two separate days: April 28 and May 3, 2026.
*   **Eligibility:** Candidates need a full-time undergraduate degree from a recognized university with at least 60% aggregate marks.
*   **Courses Offered:** VITMEE facilitates admissions into various M.Tech and MCA specializations across VIT's Vellore, Chennai, Bhopal, and Andhra Pradesh campuses.

## About VITMEE

VITMEE is a crucial entrance exam conducted by the Vellore Institute of Technology for admission to its Master of Technology (M.Tech) and Master of Computer Applications (MCA) courses. Your performance in this examination directly influences your eligibility for the subsequent counseling process and ultimately, admission to your desired program at one of VIT's campuses. All specific details regarding the exam, including the syllabus, pattern, and eligibility criteria, are released by VIT through its official information brochure.

## Important Dates for VITMEE 2026

Staying informed about key dates is vital for a smooth application and examination process. Here are the important timelines for VITMEE 2026:

*   **Application Form Release:** November 21, 2025
*   **Application Deadline:** April 12, 2026
*   **Examination Dates:** April 28, 2026, and May 3, 2026
*   **Counselling Dates:** To be announced on the official website after the exam results.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for VITMEE 2026, candidates must meet specific academic requirements. It is essential to confirm your eligibility before applying:

*   Applicants must hold a full-time bachelor's degree from a university recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC).
*   A minimum aggregate score of 60% in their undergraduate degree is mandatory.
*   Specific program-wise eligibility, especially for different M.Tech specializations, might require a relevant background in engineering or technology. Candidates should consult the official VITMEE information brochure for these detailed requirements.

## Application Process

The application for VITMEE 2026 is conducted online through the official VIT website. Here's a general overview of the steps involved:

1.  **Registration:** Visit vit.ac.in and register by providing basic details to create a login ID and password.
2.  **Form Filling:** Log in and accurately fill out the application form with personal, academic, and contact information.
3.  **Document Upload:** Upload scanned copies of required documents, such as photographs and signatures, as per the specified format and size.
4.  **Fee Payment:** Pay the application fee online through the available payment gateways.
5.  **Submission:** Review all entered details carefully before final submission. It's advisable to take a printout of the submitted application for your records.
