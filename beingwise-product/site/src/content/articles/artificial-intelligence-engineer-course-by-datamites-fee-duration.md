# Artificial Intelligence Engineer Course by DataMites: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to build a career in the exciting field of Artificial Intelligence, the AI Engineer Course offered by DataMites provides comprehensive training. This program is designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in the AI industry.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course offers over 120 hours of live instruction and includes an internship with real-world AI projects.
*   Learning is flexible, with options for virtual live sessions, blended learning, or in-person classroom training.
*   Successful completion leads to a certification accredited by IABAC, a globally recognized credential.
*   Participants gain access to course materials and resources for a full year after finishing the training.

## Course Overview and Structure

This advanced-level program focuses on practical, industry-relevant AI skills. The curriculum is meticulously crafted with input from industry experts to ensure it meets current demands. Beyond theoretical knowledge, the course emphasizes hands-on experience through live projects and a capstone project.

Students will delve into core AI concepts, machine learning algorithms, natural language processing, deep learning, and essential statistical methods for data science. The program starts with foundational AI principles and progresses to advanced topics, ensuring a thorough understanding.

## Learning Modes and Program Offerings

DataMites understands that different learners have different needs, which is why the AI Engineer Course is available in three flexible formats:

*   **Virtual Live Sessions:** Participate in live online classes from anywhere, interacting directly with instructors.
*   **Blended Learning:** A mix of self-paced study with video and text-based materials, combined with live interactive sessions.
*   **Classroom Training:** Experience in-person instruction for a traditional learning environment.

Regardless of the chosen mode, all participants receive extensive support, including reading materials, live lectures, mentorship, access to cloud labs, datasets, cheat sheets, and regular newsletters. A key component is the included internship, providing practical application of learned skills on live AI projects.

The investment for the Artificial Intelligence Engineer Course varies depending on the chosen learning mode. Here's a breakdown of the current fees:

Upon successful completion of the course, students are awarded a certification. This certification is provided by IABAC (International Association of Business Analytics Certifications), adding significant value and recognition to your profile.

## Eligibility and Who Should Apply

To enroll in the Artificial Intelligence Engineer Course, candidates should possess a basic understanding of statistical tools and techniques, along with foundational knowledge of a programming language like Python. No prior work experience is required, making it accessible to a wide range of individuals.

This program is ideal for:

*   Recent graduates or freshers aspiring to build a career in Artificial Intelligence.
*   Analytics professionals looking to transition into AI roles.
*   Team leaders and senior analysts who want to enhance their skills in AI.

## How to Apply

Prospective students can find detailed admission information and the application process on the official DataMites website. The enrollment process is straightforward, guiding you through the necessary steps to begin your AI learning journey.

Embark on your journey to become a skilled AI Engineer and unlock numerous career opportunities in this rapidly evolving field.
