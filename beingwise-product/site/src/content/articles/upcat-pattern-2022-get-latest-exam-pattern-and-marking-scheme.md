# UPCAT Pattern 2022: Get Latest Exam Pattern & Marking Scheme

Understanding the exam pattern for the Uttar Pradesh Common Entrance Test (UPCET) is a crucial first step for any aspiring student. This guide will walk you through the structure, marking scheme, and course-specific details for the 2022 examination.

## Key Takeaways

*   UPCET 2022 is a computer-based test (CBT) for admission to various undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG), and lateral entry courses.
*   The National Testing Agency (NTA) is responsible for conducting the exam and releasing official pattern details.
*   Admissions for B.Tech programs are based on JEE Main scores, so there is no UPCET exam pattern for B.Tech.
*   Each correct answer in UPCET 2022 will be awarded 4 marks.

## UPCET 2022 Examination Overview

UPCET 2022 serves as an entrance examination for a wide range of courses offered by institutions like AKTU, MMMUT, and HBTU. Knowing the exam pattern helps candidates prepare effectively by understanding what to expect on test day. The examination covers various disciplines, from management and design to pharmacy and computer applications.

Here's a general look at the exam's key features:

| Particulars        | Details                                                                                                                                                                          |
| :----------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Mode of Exam       | Computer-Based Test (Online)                                                                                                                                                     |
| Duration of Exam   | B.Pharm: 3 hours<br>Other courses: 2 hours                                                                                                                                       |
| Type of Questions  | Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)                                                                                                                                                 |
| Medium of Exam     | UG (MBA Integrated, MCA Integrated): English and Hindi<br>PG courses: English                                                                                                   |
| Marking Scheme     | 4 marks awarded for every correct answer; there is no mention of negative marking in the official pattern details.                                                               |

## Course-wise Examination Structure

The structure of the UPCET 2022 exam varies depending on the specific course you are applying for. It's essential to identify your chosen program to understand its unique section distribution and the number of questions you'll face in each.

### Undergraduate and Integrated Programs

*   **BHMCT, BFA, BFAD, BVoc., BBA, and MBA (Integrated):**
    *   Numerical Ability and Analytical Aptitude: 25 Questions
    *   Reasoning and Logical Deduction: 25 Questions
    *   General Knowledge and Current Affairs: 25 Questions
    *   English Language: 25 Questions

*   **B.Des:**
    *   Numerical Ability and Analytical Aptitude: 20 Questions
    *   Reasoning and Logical Deduction: 20 Questions
    *   General Knowledge and Current Affairs: 20 Questions
    *   English Language: 20 Questions
    *   Design: 20 Questions

*   **B.Pharm:**
    *   Physics: 50 Questions
    *   Chemistry: 50 Questions
    *   Biology/Mathematics: 50 Questions (Candidates can choose either Biology or Mathematics)

### Postgraduate and Lateral Entry Programs

*   **MCA:**
    *   Numerical Ability and Analytical Aptitude: 25 Questions
    *   Reasoning and Logical Deduction: 25 Questions
    *   Mathematics: 25 Questions
    *   Computer Awareness: 25 Questions

*   **MCA (Integrated):**
    *   Numerical Ability and Analytical Aptitude: 25 Questions
    *   Reasoning and Logical Deduction: 25 Questions
    *   Mathematics: 25 Questions
    *   Computer Awareness: 25 Questions

*   **Lateral Entry to B.Tech (for Diploma holders):**
    *   Engineering Mechanics: 25 Questions
    *   Engineering Graphics: 25 Questions
    *   Basic Electrical Engineering: 25 Questions
    *   Basic Electronics Engineering: 25 Questions

*   **Lateral Entry to B.Tech (for B.Sc graduates):**
    *   Mathematics: 50 Questions
    *   Computer Awareness: 50 Questions
    *   Engineering Aptitude: 50 Questions

*   **M.Tech:**
    *   General Aptitude: 50 Questions
    *   Specific Engineering Discipline: 50 Questions (e.g., Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Computer Science)

*   **MBA/MBA (Integrated):**
    *   Numerical Ability and Analytical Aptitude: 25 Questions
    *   Reasoning and Logical Deduction: 25 Questions
    *   General Knowledge and Current Affairs: 25 Questions
    *   English Language: 25 Questions

## Important Notes for Preparation

Candidates are strongly advised to refer to the official NTA website for the most up-to-date and comprehensive details regarding the UPCET 2022 exam pattern and syllabus. Thoroughly understanding the pattern is the first step towards a well-structured preparation strategy.
