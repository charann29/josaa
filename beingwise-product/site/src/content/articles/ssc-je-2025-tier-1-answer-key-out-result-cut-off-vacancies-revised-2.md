# SSC JE 2025: Tier 1 Answer Key (OUT), Result, Cut Off, Vacancies (Revised) & Syllabus

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) conducts the Junior Engineer (JE) examination for various technical roles in government departments. This page provides a comprehensive overview of the SSC JE 2025 process, including details on the recently released Tier 1 answer key, important dates, result expectations, cut-off trends, revised vacancies, and the examination syllabus.

## Key takeaways

*   The provisional answer key for SSC JE Tier 1 2025 has been released on the official SSC portal.
*   Candidates had a window from December 19 to December 22, 2025, to challenge the provisional answer key online.
*   The total number of vacancies for SSC JE 2025 has been significantly increased from 1,340 to 1,731.
*   The Tier 1 examination was held across multiple dates in December 2025.

## SSC JE Tier 1 Provisional Answer Key 2025

The Staff Selection Commission has made the tentative answer key for the SSC JE 2025 Paper 1 available on its official website. Along with the answer key, candidates can also access their individual question papers and response sheets. This allows applicants to review their performance and estimate their scores.

To download your answer key and response sheet:

*   Visit the official SSC portal.
*   Log in using your registration number and password.
*   Locate and click on the link for the SSC JE Tier 1 answer key.
*   The answer key, along with your response sheet, will be displayed. You can download and save it for your records.

Candidates were given a specific period, from 6:00 PM on December 19 to 6:00 PM on December 22, 2025, to raise objections against any question or answer in the provisional key. A fee of INR 50 per challenge was applicable.

## Important Dates for SSC JE 2025

Staying informed about the examination schedule is crucial for all aspirants. Here are some key dates related to the SSC JE 2025 examination process:

| Event                                 | Date                 |

| Application Registration Period       | June 30 - July 21, 2025 |

## SSC JE 2025 Result and Cut-Off

The Staff Selection Commission will announce the SSC JE 2025 Paper 1 results on its official website. The results are typically published in a PDF format, which lists the roll numbers of candidates who have qualified for the next stage of the selection process. Candidates can access this PDF by visiting the official SSC website and clicking on the relevant result link.

Following the result declaration, the Commission will also release the cut-off marks for SSC JE 2025. These cut-off scores will be category-wise and specific to each engineering discipline (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Quantity Surveying & Contracts). The cut-off marks indicate the minimum scores required to qualify for the next phase of the examination and are usually available within the result PDF itself.

## Revised Vacancies for SSC JE 2025

In a significant update, the Staff Selection Commission revised the total number of vacancies for the SSC JE 2025 examination. On August 5, 2025, the vacancies were increased from an initial 1,340 to a total of 1,731. These positions are open for various engineering disciplines, including Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Quantity Surveying & Contracts, across different government departments.

## SSC JE Syllabus and Exam Pattern

Before appearing for the examination, candidates are strongly advised to thoroughly review the official SSC JE syllabus and exam pattern. Understanding the topics covered and the structure of the examination is vital for effective preparation. The syllabus typically covers General Intelligence and Reasoning, General Awareness, and a specific Engineering discipline paper relevant to the candidate's chosen field. Detailed information on the syllabus and exam pattern is available on the official SSC website.
