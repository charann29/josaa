# I am an PCB student and only wish to give session 2 that is may atttempt of mht cet then which option to choose during form filling first attempt or both attempt?

For PCB students planning to take only the May session of MHT CET, understanding the form-filling options is crucial. This guide will help you navigate the choices to ensure you register correctly for your preferred attempt.

## Key takeaways

*   **Select "First Attempt" carefully:** If the form presents options like "First Attempt" or "Both Attempts," and you intend to appear only in the May session, choose "First Attempt." The MHT CET form typically considers the first *available* attempt you register for, even if it's the second chronological session.
*   **Look for Session-Specific Registration:** Some forms might directly ask you to select the specific session(s) you wish to appear for (e.g., "March Session," "May Session," or "Both"). In this case, simply select the "May Session."
*   **Review the Information Bulletin:** Always refer to the official MHT CET Information Bulletin for the most accurate and up-to-date instructions on form filling and session selection. This document is your primary source of truth.
*   **Payment Implication:** Your fee payment will generally correspond to the number of sessions you select. Choosing only one session will mean paying the fee for a single attempt.

## Understanding the "First Attempt" Option

When the MHT CET application form presents options such as "First Attempt" or "Both Attempts," it can sometimes be a bit confusing for students who only want to appear in the later session. For PCB students aiming exclusively for the May session, selecting "First Attempt" is generally the correct choice if the form is structured to mean the *first attempt you are registering for* rather than the *first chronological session available*.

This means that if you only wish to appear in the May session and the form only gives you these two choices, picking "First Attempt" indicates you are registering for one specific attempt, which in your case will be the May one. The system usually then prompts you to select the actual session dates or slots you prefer.

## Session-Specific Selection

Some application portals are more direct and allow you to explicitly choose which session you want to appear for. You might see options like:

*   March Session (Session 1)
*   May Session (Session 2)
*   Both Sessions (March and May)

If your form presents these clear options, simply select "May Session." This is the most straightforward scenario and leaves no room for ambiguity regarding your intention to appear only in Session 2.

## Why Not Choose "Both Attempts"?

Choosing "Both Attempts" would mean you are registering and paying for both the March and May sessions. Since your goal is to appear only in the May session, selecting "Both Attempts" would lead to unnecessary registration for the March session and an increased fee payment for an attempt you do not intend to take.

## Always Consult the Official Information Bulletin

Before finalizing your application, it is highly recommended to carefully read the official MHT CET Information Bulletin. This document, released by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, contains precise instructions for the application process, including how to select your preferred sessions. Any specific nuances or changes in the application interface will be detailed there. Referring to this official source will ensure you complete your form accurately and avoid any potential issues.

Ensure you review all your selections carefully before final submission to confirm you are registered for the correct session.
