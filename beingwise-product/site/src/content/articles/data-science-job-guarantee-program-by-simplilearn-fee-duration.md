# Data Science Job Guarantee Program by Simplilearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen on building a career in data science, Simplilearn offers a specialized online program with a job guarantee. This intensive 6-month course is designed to equip participants with essential skills and practical experience for the data science industry.

## Key Takeaways

*   A focused 6-month online bootcamp structure, requiring 10-15 hours of study each week.
*   Covers a wide array of data science concepts, tools, and real-world projects.
*   Includes a job guarantee, subject to specific terms and conditions, aiming to support career placement.
*   Features live online classes led by industry experts and offers lifetime access to course content.

## Program Structure and Learning Approach

The Data Science Job Guarantee program is delivered entirely online, combining self-study modules with virtual classroom sessions. The learning materials are presented through a mix of video and text formats. Classes are scheduled on both weekdays and weekends, offering flexibility for learners. Participants are expected to dedicate approximately 10-15 hours per week to their studies to keep pace with the curriculum.

## What You Will Learn

This program provides a deep dive into core data science principles and their practical applications. Key learning areas include:

*   **Fundamental Concepts:** Gain a strong understanding of data science, data structures, and data manipulation techniques.
*   **Mathematical and Statistical Skills:** Develop proficiency in probability and statistics, multivariate calculus, and linear and logistic regression.
*   **Database Management:** Learn essential skills for managing and querying databases.
*   **Data Visualization and Storytelling:** Master techniques for presenting data insights effectively.
*   **Machine Learning Models:** Explore supervised and unsupervised learning models, including linear regression, logistic regression, clustering, k-NN, and dimensionality reduction.
*   **Key Tools and Technologies:** Get hands-on experience with industry-standard tools such as Python, R, NumPy, SciPy, Pandas, MySQL, Tableau, TensorFlow, and Keras.

The curriculum also features electives in Data Science with R and Deep Learning with Keras and TensorFlow, allowing for specialized learning paths.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Data Science Job Guarantee program, candidates must meet specific academic and programming prerequisites:

*   **Academic Background:** Applicants should hold an undergraduate degree such as B.Tech, BCA, B.Sc (IT), or a postgraduate degree like M.Tech, MCA, M.Sc, M.A (Economics), or MBA from an accredited institution. Final-year students are also welcome to apply.
*   **Academic Performance:** A consistent academic record of at least 60% throughout 10th, 12th, undergraduate, and postgraduate studies (if applicable) is required.
*   **Programming Knowledge:** Prior experience or understanding of programming concepts is beneficial.

## Program Fees and Payment Options

The total program fee for the Data Science Job Guarantee certification is ₹198,004, inclusive of GST. To make the program more accessible, Simplilearn offers installment payment options. Students can choose to pay an EMI of ₹6,573 per month.

| Payment Type        | Amount (INR) |
| :------------------ | :----------- |
| Total Program Fee   | ₹198,004     |
| Monthly EMI         | ₹6,573       |

Upon successful completion of the course, participants will receive a certificate of achievement from Simplilearn, which can be shared on professional networking platforms to enhance their resumes.

This program aims to provide a robust foundation and practical skills, preparing individuals for successful careers in the dynamic field of data science.
