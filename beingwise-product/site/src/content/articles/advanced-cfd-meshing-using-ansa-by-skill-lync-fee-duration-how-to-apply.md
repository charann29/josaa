## Detailed Syllabus

The course is structured into five weekly modules, each building upon the previous one to provide a comprehensive understanding of ANSA and CFD meshing.

### Week 1: Introduction to ANSA GUI and Tools

This introductory module familiarizes learners with the ANSA software environment. It covers the Graphical User Interface (GUI), introduces various solvers and analysis types, and teaches fundamental tools for geometric cleanup and deck setups. Key topics include an overview of ANSA, its GUI, and essential geometric and topology cleanup tools.

### Week 2: 2D (Surface) Meshing to Pressure Valve

In this module, participants will work with a pressure valve model to learn surface meshing. The curriculum covers PID (Part ID) creation and assignment, different selection techniques, visibility tools, basic topology cleanup, and the core tools used in surface meshing.

### Week 3: 3D (Volume) Meshing to Turbocharger

This module delves into volumetric meshing using a turbocharger model. Students will learn how to prepare geometry for volume definition, perform various geometry checks, conduct surface meshing according to quality criteria, and execute volumetric meshing based on specific requirements.

### Week 4: CFD Meshing to BMW M6 Model inside Wind Tunnel

This advanced module focuses on performing CFD meshing on a complex BMW M6 model situated within a wind tunnel. Topics include advanced topology cleanup for volume definition, variable surface meshing on different parts, resolving quality-failed elements, applying symmetry operations for surface and mesh elements, and creating and meshing a wind tunnel.

### Week 5: Surface Wrap to an Automotive Assembly

The final module introduces participants to an automotive assembly comprising engine, transmission, and gearbox models. The focus is on performing a surface wrap for CFD analysis of outer flow. This involves geometry cleanups specific to surface wrapping, merging multiple models, and executing surface wrapping for the entire assembly.

This program offers a robust foundation and advanced techniques for anyone aspiring to excel in the field of Computational Fluid Dynamics through expert meshing skills.
