# Closing Ranks for MD Physiology at Bangalore Medical College

Understanding previous year's closing ranks is a crucial step for any aspiring medical student. This information helps you gauge your chances of securing a seat in your desired program and institution.

## Key Takeaways

*   For the 2024 admissions, the final closing rank for MD Physiology at Bangalore Medical College and Research Institute (BMCRI) under the All India Quota (General Category) was 110,373.
*   The previous year's closing ranks for the same program and category were significantly lower, typically ranging between 49,600 and 50,600.
*   A rank below 50,000 in the General Category (All India Quota) generally offered a strong chance of admission to MD Physiology at BMCRI in prior years.
*   Admission trends can vary year to year, influenced by factors like the number of applicants, seat availability, and candidate preferences.

## Understanding Closing Ranks

Closing ranks represent the rank of the last candidate admitted to a specific course and college in a particular admission round. These ranks are dynamic and can change with each counseling round. For programs like MD Physiology, especially at a prestigious institution like BMCRI, the competition is usually quite high.

## MD Physiology at Bangalore Medical College and Research Institute (BMCRI)

Bangalore Medical College and Research Institute (BMCRI) is a highly respected institution in Karnataka, known for its strong medical programs and clinical exposure. The MD Physiology program here offers comprehensive training in the fundamental principles of human body function, preparing students for careers in academia, research, or clinical applications.

## All India Quota (General Category) Ranks for 2024

For the 2024 admission cycle, under the All India Quota for the General Category, the closing ranks for MD Physiology at BMCRI progressed as follows through the counseling rounds:

*   **Round 1:** 54,493
*   **Round 2:** 57,292
*   **Round 3:** 84,538
*   **Round 4 (Final Round):** 110,373

These figures indicate that seats remained available and were filled in subsequent rounds, with the closing rank increasing significantly by the final round.

## Previous Year's Rank Trends

In the preceding admission cycle, the closing ranks for MD Physiology at BMCRI for the General Category (All India Quota) were considerably tighter, generally falling within the range of 49,600 to 50,600. This suggests that candidates with ranks under approximately 50,000 had a very good prospect of securing a seat in that period. It's important for applicants to note these year-on-year variations when planning their applications.

## Factors Influencing Rank Fluctuations

Several elements can cause closing ranks to shift from one year to the next. These include the total number of candidates appearing for the NEET PG examination, the overall performance level of those candidates, the number of seats available in the program, and the preferences exercised by candidates during the counseling process. Official data, often released by bodies like the Medical Counseling Committee (MCC), provides the most accurate reflection of these trends.

Keeping an eye on official announcements and previous year's data, such as those found on the official MCC portal, can provide valuable insights for your admission strategy.
