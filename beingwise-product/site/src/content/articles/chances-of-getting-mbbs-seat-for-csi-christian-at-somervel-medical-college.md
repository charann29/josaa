# What are the Chances of Getting an MBBS Seat for a CSI Christian at Somervell Medical College with Kerala Rank 9000?

*   While a rank of 9000 is not exceptionally high, there might be a possibility, particularly if you belong to a specific reserved category.

## Understanding Seat Distribution at Somervell Medical College

Dr. Somervell Memorial CSI Medical College, a well-regarded institution, allocates its MBBS seats under the Kerala state quota across various categories. For the state quota, there are 35 seats distributed as follows:

*   **SM (State Merit):** 20 seats
*   **EZ (Ezhava):** 3 seats
*   **MU (Muslim):** 3 seats
*   **BH (Other Backward Hindu):** 1 seat
*   **DV (Dheevara):** 1 seat
*   **KN (Kusavan):** 1 seat
*   **ZX (CSI):** 7 seats
*   **KU (Other Christian):** 1 seat
*   **SC (Scheduled Caste):** 3 seats
*   **ST (Scheduled Tribe):** 1 seat
*   **NX (Viswakarma):** 1 seat
*   **NB (Latin Catholic/Anglo-Indian):** 2 seats

It's important to note the specific allocation for CSI Christians (ZX category), which has 7 seats. This is a significant factor when assessing your chances.
