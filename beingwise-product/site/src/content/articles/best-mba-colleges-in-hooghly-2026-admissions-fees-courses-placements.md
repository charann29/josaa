## Accepted Entrance Exams for MBA in Hooghly

Admission to the MBA/PGDM program in Hooghly primarily relies on performance in specific entrance examinations. The most widely accepted test for securing a seat in the region's management program is the Joint Entrance Management Aptitude Test (JEMAT).

Students planning to apply for 2026 admissions should prioritize preparing for JEMAT. It is crucial to check the official JEMAT website for application deadlines, exam dates, syllabus, and participating institutions. While other national-level exams like CAT, XAT, or CMAT are popular across India, JEMAT holds particular relevance for institutions within West Bengal, including the college in Hooghly.
