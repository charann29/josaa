## Admission Process and Eligibility

To secure a seat in an MBA program in Nadia for 2026, candidates typically need to meet specific eligibility criteria and follow a defined admission process. Generally, this includes:

*   **Educational Qualification:** A bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university, usually with a minimum aggregate percentage (often 50% or 45% for reserved categories).
*   **Entrance Exams:** Most MBA programs require a valid score from national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, or state-level exams like WBJEE MBA. Some institutions might also conduct their own entrance tests.
*   **Application:** Submitting a completed application form, either online or offline, by the specified deadline.
*   **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates may need to participate in group discussions (GD) and personal interviews (PI) as part of the final selection process.

It is crucial to refer to the official admission brochures of each college for precise eligibility requirements and application procedures for the 2026 intake.
