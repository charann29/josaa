# NMAT Exam Dates 2025 (OUT): Check Additional Phase Schedule for Registration, Exam, Results

The NMAT (NMAT by GMAC) is a crucial entrance examination for MBA aspirants seeking admission to over 50 participating business schools across India. Conducted by the Graduate Management Admission Council (GMAC), the exam offers a unique opportunity for candidates to attempt the test multiple times within a defined window.

## Key takeaways

*   NMAT 2025 registration is expected to commence in the first week of August 2024.
*   Candidates can take the NMAT exam up to three times within the provided testing window.
*   Slot booking is a mandatory step after registration to select your preferred exam date and venue.
*   NMAT results are typically available very quickly, often within 48 hours of taking the exam.

## NMAT 2025 Registration and Exam Schedule

The NMAT exam window typically spans 40-50 days, allowing flexibility for candidates. Here’s an overview of the anticipated schedule for NMAT 2025:

| Dates                     | Upcoming Exam Dates (Tentative) |
| :------------------------ | :------------------------------ |
| August 2024 - October 2024 | NMAT 2025 Registration Window   |
| August 2024 - October 2024 | NMAT 2025 Scheduling Window     |
| October 2024 - December 2024 | NMAT 2025 Admit Card Issuance   |
| November 2024 - December 2024 | NMAT 2025 Exam Window           |
| November 2024 - December 2024 | NMAT 2025 Result Declaration    |

## NMAT 2025 Registration Process

The registration for NMAT 2025 will be conducted online through the official NMAT website, mba.com/nmat. The registration window is anticipated to open in the first week of August 2024 and close by the second week of October 2024. It’s important to remember that if you plan to take the exam more than once, you will need to register for each attempt separately. The retake registration window will only become accessible after you have completed your initial attempt. During the application process, you will also be asked to select up to five preferred business schools to receive your scores.

## NMAT 2025 Exam Scheduling and Slot Booking

Once you have successfully registered for NMAT, the next crucial step is to complete the exam scheduling, also known as slot booking. This process allows you to choose your desired exam date and venue. The scheduling window is expected to run concurrently with the registration period, from August to October 2024. Separate slot booking is required for each NMAT attempt.

Should you need to alter your chosen exam date or venue after booking, GMAC provides a rescheduling option. This usually involves paying a processing fee for the change.

## NMAT 2025 Exam Dates

The NMAT 2025 examination window is generally scheduled from November to December 2024. While GMAC releases the full, confirmed schedule in early August, the table below outlines the expected key dates related to the exam itself:

| Events                      | NMAT Exam Dates (Expected)   |
| :-------------------------- | :--------------------------- |
| NMAT exam scheduling begins | First week of August 2024    |
| NMAT scheduling last date   | Second week of October 2024  |
| NMAT exam dates             | November to December 2024    |

## NMAT 2025 Result Declaration

One of the convenient features of NMAT is the immediate availability of scores. After completing your exam and submitting your responses, you will typically receive your score notification. The official NMAT scorecard, which can be downloaded from the official website, is usually made available within 48 hours of your exam delivery. The overall result declaration window for NMAT 2025 is expected to be from November to December 2024, aligning with the exam period.

Staying informed about these dates is essential for a smooth NMAT application journey.
