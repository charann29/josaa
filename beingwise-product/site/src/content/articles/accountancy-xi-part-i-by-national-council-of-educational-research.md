# Accountancy XI Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course offers a foundational understanding of accounting principles specifically designed for Class XI students. It's an excellent opportunity to build a strong base in financial accounting concepts from the convenience of your home.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Learning:** The course covers essential Class XI accountancy topics, from basic introductions to rectifying errors and understanding special purpose books.
*   **Flexible Study:** Delivered through a self-paced, video, and text-based format, it allows students to learn at their own speed and convenience.
*   **NCERT Certification:** Upon successful completion and assessment, participants receive a certificate from NCERT and Swayam.
*   **Rich Resources:** Beyond video lectures, learners get access to e-resources, external web links, and supplementary materials to enhance their understanding.

## Course Overview

Coordinated by Professor Shipra Vaidya from the Department of Education in Social Sciences (DESS), NCERT, New Delhi, this massive open online course (MOOC) is structured to provide a deep dive into financial accounting. It focuses on helping students grasp core ideas like monetary inflows, outflows, and various accounting transactions, which are crucial for a solid academic foundation.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to equip students with both financial knowledge and accounting proficiency. By the end of this program, you will have a clear understanding of:

*   Introduction to Accountancy
*   The Accounting Equation
*   Rules of Debit and Credit
*   Special Purpose Books (like Cash Book, Purchases Book, Sales Book)
*   Ledger
*   Bank Reconciliation Statement
*   Trial Balance
*   Rectification of Errors
*   Depreciation, Provisions & Reserves
*   Bills of Exchange

## Course Structure and Materials

The learning experience is multifaceted, incorporating various resources to support your study journey. You will engage with:

*   **Video Lectures:** Detailed explanations of each topic.
*   **E-resources:** Digital study materials for in-depth reading.
*   **External Weblinks:** Curated links to additional relevant information.
*   **Enrichment Materials:** Supplementary content to broaden your understanding beyond textbooks.
*   **Self-Assessment Inventory and Checklist:** Tools to evaluate your progress and identify areas for improvement.
*   **Assignments:** Regular tasks to test your comprehension of each chapter.

## Certification and Eligibility

Upon successfully completing the course and achieving a score of 70% or higher in the final assessment, you will be awarded a certificate. This certificate is provided jointly by NCERT and Swayam, validating your understanding of the Class XI accountancy curriculum. There are no specific prerequisites to enroll in the course; it is open to all students keen on mastering accounting concepts at the school level.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Accountancy XI Part-I Course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam platform for the course.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the new page, you can either sign in with an existing account or create a new one using your username and password to proceed with enrollment.

This course is ideal for any student aiming to build a strong foundation in accountancy and potentially pursue a career in finance or accounting in the future.
