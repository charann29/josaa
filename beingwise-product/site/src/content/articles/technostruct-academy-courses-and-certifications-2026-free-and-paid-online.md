# TechnoStruct Academy Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online TechnoStruct Academy Courses

TechnoStruct Academy offers a range of online courses and certifications designed to equip students and professionals with essential skills in Building Information Modeling (BIM) and related fields. These programs cater to various specializations within the Architecture, Engineering, and Construction (AEC) industry, aiming to provide practical, industry-relevant knowledge.

## Key Takeaways

*   TechnoStruct Academy provides specialized online BIM courses for different AEC disciplines.
*   Programs range from 3.5 to 8 months, with fees varying based on course depth and duration.
*   Many courses offer hands-on experience, live project interactions (often with USA-based projects), and potential internship opportunities.
*   Certifications are awarded upon successful completion, validating specialized BIM skills for career advancement.

## Understanding Building Information Modeling (BIM) with TechnoStruct Academy

Building Information Modeling (BIM) is a crucial process in the AEC industry, involving the creation and management of digital representations of physical and functional characteristics of places. TechnoStruct Academy's courses delve into various aspects of BIM, preparing individuals for roles such as BIM engineers, coordinators, and managers. The academy focuses on practical application, often incorporating live project work and interactions to give students real-world exposure.

## Featured Certification Programs

TechnoStruct Academy offers several distinct certification programs, each tailored to specific career paths within the AEC sector. Here's a closer look at some of their key offerings:

### BIM Ready+

This comprehensive 8-month live online course focuses on advanced BIM concepts. It's ideal for seasoned AEC professionals aiming for an International Post-Graduate Certification in BIM Management. The program includes extensive e-live classes, practical sessions, and live projects, often involving interactions with USA-based projects. Successful participants receive a certificate of completion and may also get a chance for a free one-month internship under professional guidance. The course fee is ₹1,70,000.

### BIM Complete

An advanced-level 6-month online course, BIM Complete is designed for engineering and architecture students and professionals aspiring to become BIM engineers. It covers industry-specific BIM methods and fundamentals. The curriculum includes e-live and online classes, along with live project work and international project interactions. Upon completion, learners receive an International Certification in BIM Modeling & Coordination. The course fee is ₹59,000.

### BIM Ready (MEP)

This 4-month online certification focuses on mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) aspects of BIM. It's suitable for mechanical and electrical engineering students, aiming to make them BIM MEP engineering experts. The program includes 30 hours of live sessions and 30 hours of practical sessions, offering hands-on experience with software like AutoCAD 2D & 3D, AutoCAD MEP, Revit MEP, Dynamo, Navisworks, Autodesk BIM 360, and Scan to BIM. Participants can also get free internships and work with BIM professionals. The course fee is ₹50,000.

### BIM Ready (Architecture)

Geared towards architects and architecture students, this 3.5-month live online course explores BIM and Computer-Aided Design (CAD) in the context of contemporary architecture. It equips learners with advanced software skills relevant to architectural, technical, and structural design. The course features e-live classes, live projects, and a hands-on learning approach led by BIM experts. Upon successful completion, candidates receive a course certificate and may find placement opportunities in multinational corporations. The course fee is ₹29,500.
