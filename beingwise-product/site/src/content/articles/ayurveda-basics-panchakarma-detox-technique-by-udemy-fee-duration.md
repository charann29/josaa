## What is Panchakarma?

Panchakarma, a Sanskrit term meaning "five actions" or "five procedures," is a cornerstone of Ayurvedic therapy. It's a comprehensive detoxification and rejuvenation program designed to cleanse the body of accumulated toxins (ama) and restore balance to its fundamental energies (doshas). The goal is to bring the body back to its natural, healthy constitution, promoting overall well-being and preventing disease.
