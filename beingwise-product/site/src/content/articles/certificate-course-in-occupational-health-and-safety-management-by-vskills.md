# Certificate Course in Occupational Health and Safety Management by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For professionals keen on ensuring a safe and healthy work environment, the Certificate Course in Occupational Health and Safety Management, offered by Vskills, provides a valuable opportunity. This online program is designed to equip individuals with essential knowledge and skills in managing workplace safety and health hazards.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a government-certified online program focused on occupational health and safety.
*   The course is approximately 12 hours long and can be completed through self-study.
*   It offers a certificate with lifetime validity, enhancing long-term career prospects.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹3,499.

## About the Program

The Certificate Course in Occupational Health and Safety Management is a fully online, self-paced program delivered through video and text-based modules. It emphasizes a systematic approach to identifying, assessing, and controlling health and safety risks in the workplace. The program is particularly beneficial for those in management roles who are responsible for preventing work-related injuries and illnesses.

Upon successful completion, participants receive a government-recognized certificate that holds lifetime validity. This certification is a significant advantage for career progression in the field of occupational safety.

## Who Should Consider This Course?

This program is ideally suited for a range of professionals dedicated to maintaining high safety standards in their organizations. Suitable candidates include:

*   Safety Managers
*   HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment) Engineers
*   Occupational Safety and Health (OSH) professionals and managers
*   Individuals working in ISO 45001 or ISO 18001 certified companies
*   Management Systems Consultants
*   Safety Auditors
*   Quality Managers

The course content is structured to help these professionals review existing workplace hazards and implement effective prevention and resolution strategies.

## Course Structure and Learning Outcomes

The 12-hour online program covers fundamental aspects of occupational health and safety. Key learning areas include:

*   **OHS Concepts and Principles:** Understanding the basics of occupational health, safety, hygiene, and ergonomics.
*   **Accident, Hazard, and Prevention:** Learning about accident basics, hazard identification, analysis, control measures, investigation, and reporting.
*   **OHS Legislations:** Gaining knowledge of relevant Indian laws such as The Factories Act, The Mines Act, The Workmen’s Compensation Act, The Employee’s State Insurance Act, and The Occupational Safety, Health And Working Conditions Code, 2020.
*   **OHS Planning and Implementation:** Strategies for effective OHS planning and the role of ISO 45001 standards.

Participants will develop a comprehensive understanding of risk management and the systematic management of safety and health hazards.

## Fee and Certification

The total fee for the Certificate Course in Occupational Health and Safety Management is ₹3,499. This fee covers access to the online learning management system, study materials, and the final online test. Upon successfully passing the final examination with a minimum score of 25 out of 50 marks, candidates will receive a government-aided certificate with lifetime validity from Vskills.

## How to Apply

The admission process for this certificate course is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official Vskills website.
2.  Locate the 'Certificate Course in Occupational Health and Safety Management' and click on the 'Buy Now' button.
3.  Complete the registration form with the required minimal details.
4.  Proceed to pay the program fee.
5.  Upon successful payment and registration, you will receive admission confirmation and access to the course materials.

This program offers a robust foundation for anyone looking to advance their expertise in occupational health and safety, directly contributing to safer and healthier workplaces across industries.
