# EC-Council Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online EC-Council Courses

EC-Council is a globally recognized organization that provides certifications and training in cybersecurity. Their programs are designed to equip individuals with the skills needed to defend against cyber threats and ensure digital security.

## Key Takeaways

*   EC-Council offers a range of cybersecurity courses, from foundational to advanced levels.
*   Programs often combine theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on exercises.
*   Certifications from EC-Council are widely respected in the cybersecurity industry.
*   Courses are available through various partners, including universities and online learning platforms.

## Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security

This comprehensive six-month program, offered in collaboration with MIT Schwarzman College of Computing, focuses on critical areas of cybersecurity. It covers data protection, risk assessment, compliance, mitigation strategies, and cloud security. The curriculum is structured with modules developed by both MIT and EC-Council.

The program includes six practical assessments and real-world industry case studies, providing a strong foundation in applied cybersecurity. A notable instructor for this program is Nickolai Zeldovich, the Edwin Sibley Webster Professor at MIT CSAIL, whose expertise lies in computer security, applied cryptography, and computer architecture.

Upon successful completion, participants receive multiple credentials:

*   An Ethical Hacker Certificate from EC-Council.
*   A Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security Certification from the offering institution, along with industry-recognized certificates for individual courses within the learning path.
*   An Executive Programme certification from MIT Schwarzman College of Computing.

The total fee for this program is ₹1,22,499.

## Cyber Security Foundations: Start Building Your Career in Cyber Defense

This introductory online course, available through EC-Council via FutureLearn, provides a practical pathway into the field of cybersecurity. It's designed to give learners essential skills for protecting organizations from various cyber threats, emphasizing cyber resilience – the ability to anticipate, respond to, and recover from attacks.

Throughout this 15-week intermediate-level course, participants will explore fundamental cybersecurity concepts and issues. They will examine real-life examples of malicious attacks like phishing, ransomware, and social engineering. The course also teaches vital defense strategies against malware, how to create effective malware protection plans, and the importance of robust data security policies.

A significant portion of the course is dedicated to identity and access management, a crucial aspect of safeguarding sensitive information. Learners will gain skills in implementing access controls, understanding single sign-on (SSO), and various authentication techniques to strengthen overall data security practices.

The fee for this certification course is ₹900.

## Advanced Cyber Security Training: Network Security

Also offered by EC-Council through FutureLearn, this advanced program focuses specifically on network security. It equips individuals with the knowledge and skills to identify and prevent cyberattacks that can impact both business and home networks. The course aims to teach participants how to enhance their network security posture.

Learners in this program will gain insights into the mindset of malicious hackers, understanding their methods for breaching network defenses. The course includes hands-on exercises utilizing various network security platforms such as OSINT, Spiderfoot, OpenVas, Nmap, Nessus, and ExploitDB. This practical approach is designed to develop expert-level cybersecurity skills.

Upon completion, participants receive an industry-specific e-certificate, which can be used to highlight newly acquired, job-relevant skills on resumes and professional networking platforms like LinkedIn.

EC-Council's diverse offerings provide valuable pathways for individuals at different stages of their cybersecurity careers, from beginners to those seeking advanced specialization.
