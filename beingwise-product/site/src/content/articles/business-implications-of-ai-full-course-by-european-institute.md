# Business Implications of AI: Full course by European Institute of Innovation and Technology via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming the business world. This online course, offered by the European Institute of Innovation and Technology (EIT), provides a practical understanding of AI's strategic importance and applications for business leaders and professionals.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Beginner-Friendly:** Designed for those new to AI in a business context, requiring no prior technical expertise.
*   **Flexible Learning:** A completely self-paced, 100% online program allowing you to learn on your own schedule.
*   **Certification Option:** A professional certificate is available upon successful completion and payment of the course fee.
*   **Comprehensive Overview:** Covers AI basics, its current business applications, and how to develop effective AI strategies.

## Course Overview

This certification course focuses on artificial intelligence from a business perspective. You'll explore how AI can be integrated into various business functions, understand its significance, and learn to make informed strategic decisions related to AI implementation. The program is structured into four modules, combining video lectures, readings, and practice exercises to facilitate learning.

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this program, participants will gain a solid understanding of:

*   The fundamental principles and practical applications of AI in business.
*   Neural networks from a strategic, non-technical business viewpoint.
*   The typical workflows and processes involved in deploying AI solutions.
*   Developing adaptive strategies for AI implementation in dynamic business environments.
*   Identifying potential consequences and challenges of AI in business and strategies to overcome them.
*   The concept of Moore's Law as it applies to the advancement of artificial intelligence.

## Course Structure and Effort

The course is entirely self-study, delivered through video and text-based materials. With an estimated learning effort of just one hour per week, the total duration of the program is approximately five hours. This flexible structure makes it easy to fit into a busy schedule. The course is offered in English and is available in an audit (free) mode, allowing you to explore the content without charge.

## Fees and Certification

While the course content can be accessed in audit mode for free, obtaining a professional certificate requires purchasing the course. The fee for the course and the certificate is **₹5,068**. Financial aid may be available for eligible candidates, which can be explored through the official platform. The certificate is awarded by Coursera upon meeting the completion criteria and payment.

### Eligibility for Certification

To earn a certificate, aspirants must purchase the course and successfully complete all required modules and assessments. This ensures that certified individuals have engaged with the full curriculum.

## How to Apply

Applying for the course is a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official course page on the platform offering the program.
2.  Create an account if you don't already have one.
3.  Locate and click the 'Enroll' tab for the course.
4.  Provide any necessary information to complete your enrollment.
5.  Once enrolled, you can access the course materials by clicking 'Go to course'.

This course offers a valuable opportunity to understand the critical role AI plays in modern business, equipping you with essential knowledge for navigating the future of technology.
