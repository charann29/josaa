# Game Development- Mobile, AR & VR by Arena Animation: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those passionate about crafting digital worlds and interactive experiences, the Game Development- Mobile, AR & VR (Unity) course by Arena Animation offers a comprehensive pathway to becoming a game developer. This program is designed to equip you with the skills needed to design and build games from the ground up, even without prior coding knowledge.

## Key takeaways

*   This 10-month, job-oriented program focuses on practical game development using the Unity engine.
*   It covers creating 2D/3D games for mobile and desktop, along with Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) experiences.
*   The curriculum is industry-centric, offering placement assistance and access to exclusive e-learning resources.
*   Financial aid options, including loan facilities, are available to help students fund their studies.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This specialized course is tailored for individuals eager to dive into the world of game creation, whether they are newcomers or looking to expand their skill set. The program emphasizes hands-on training, guiding students through the process of developing games using the Unity 2D/3D engine. You'll learn to translate your game design ideas into functional prototypes and finished products.

The learning environment is supported by certified faculty and industry mentors, fostering a discussion-based approach. Regular workshops and industry interactions are integrated into the curriculum to provide practical exposure and insights into current trends in game development. Upon successful completion, participants receive a certification from Arena Animation, acknowledging their newly acquired expertise.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a deep understanding of game development principles and technical skills. Key areas of learning include:

*   **Unity Engine Mastery:** Gaining proficiency in using Unity 2D/3D Game Kits for various projects.
*   **C# Programming Fundamentals:** Learning the basics of C# from scratch, essential for game logic.
*   **2D and 3D Game Creation:** Developing complete games for both mobile and desktop platforms, including asset creation.
*   **AR and VR Development:** Crafting immersive Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality experiences within Unity.
*   **Artwork and User Interface Design:** Creating visual assets and intuitive user interfaces for games.
*   **Storytelling and Animation:** Integrating narrative elements and particle effects using Unity's features.

## Duration and Commitment

The Game Development- Mobile, AR & VR (Unity) program spans 10 months. Students are expected to dedicate approximately 2 hours daily, six days a week, to their studies and practical assignments. This consistent commitment ensures a thorough understanding of the course material and ample time for hands-on project work.

## Fees and Financial Assistance

The total fees for the Game Development- Mobile, AR & VR (Unity) course range from ₹2,25,000 to ₹2,50,000. Arena Animation understands the financial considerations of pursuing higher education and offers loan facilities to eligible students. This support aims to make the program accessible to a wider range of aspiring game developers.

## Eligibility and Application Process

To be eligible for this program, applicants must have successfully completed their 10+2 education in any stream. Undergraduates and graduates are also welcome to apply. No prior coding experience is required, making it suitable for beginners.

The application process is straightforward:

1.  **Visit the official Arena Multimedia website** and navigate to the course page for Game Development- Mobile, AR & VR (Unity).
2.  **Locate the "Get in touch" section** on the page and fill in the required details.
3.  **Arena Animation will then contact you** based on the information provided to guide you through the next steps of enrollment.

This program offers a robust foundation for anyone looking to enter the exciting field of game development, providing both the technical skills and industry insights necessary for a successful career.
