# Diploma in Cloud Computing and Devops by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Cloud computing and DevOps are rapidly expanding fields, offering numerous career opportunities in the technology sector. This diploma program by the Boston Institute of Analytics (BIA) is designed to equip students with essential skills for these in-demand areas.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 6-month program combining training with an internship opportunity.
*   It offers dual certification from the Boston Institute of Analytics.
*   The course covers fundamental concepts and practical applications of AWS and Azure cloud platforms.
*   It is open to students from all academic backgrounds, including Arts, Science, Commerce, and Engineering.

## Program Overview

The Diploma in Cloud Computing & DevOps is a comprehensive training initiative spanning six months. The curriculum is structured to provide a solid foundation in both cloud computing principles and DevOps practices. Students will engage in hands-on learning, creating and managing accounts on leading cloud platforms such as Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Microsoft Azure. The program explores various cloud deployment and service models, including Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS).

Key topics include understanding and utilizing core AWS and Azure services like EC2, S3, RDS, and Lambda. Practical exercises focus on managing EC2 instances, configuring networking within AWS (VPCs, subnets, security groups), and working with storage services (S3, EBS, EFS). The course also introduces managed database services like Amazon RDS and DynamoDB.

## Course Structure and Duration

The standard Diploma in Cloud Computing & DevOps program runs for a total of six months. This includes a four-month intensive training period followed by a two-month internship. The total course content involves approximately 100 hours of instruction. BIA also offers variations of this program, including a shorter 4-month certification and a longer 10-month master diploma or postgraduate master diploma, catering to different learning needs and career aspirations.

The instruction is delivered in English, utilizing a blended learning approach that combines virtual classrooms, physical campus-based sessions, and video/text-based materials. The program is designed to be flexible, with live lectures often scheduled on weekends to accommodate various student schedules.

## Eligibility Criteria

This diploma program welcomes a wide range of candidates. There are no specific prerequisites regarding academic stream; students from Arts, Science, Commerce, and Engineering backgrounds are all eligible to apply. The primary requirement is a desire to gain skills in cloud computing and DevOps. Upon successful completion of the course, participants will receive a certificate from the Boston Institute of Analytics.

## Fees and Certification

Specific details regarding the course fee are typically provided directly by the Boston Institute of Analytics upon inquiry. However, it is confirmed that upon successful completion of the program, students will receive a certificate from the Boston Institute of Analytics. The program offers a dual certification benefit, acknowledging proficiency in both cloud computing and DevOps.

## How to Apply

To begin the application process for the Diploma in Cloud Computing & DevOps, interested candidates should visit the official website of the Boston Institute of Analytics. On the website, locate the "Enquire Now" option and complete the contact form with your details. A representative from the institute will then reach out to guide you through the next steps, including information on fee payment and enrollment.

This program offers a practical pathway for individuals looking to build a career in the dynamic world of cloud technology and software development operations.
