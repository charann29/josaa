# Data Science Methodology by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This guide explores the Data Science Methodology course offered by IBM on Coursera, a program designed to introduce learners to the fundamental steps involved in data science projects. It's an excellent starting point for anyone looking to understand how data insights are derived from raw information.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is beginner-friendly, requiring no prior experience in data science.
*   It focuses on the industry-standard CRISP-DM methodology, covering its six stages in detail.
*   Learners can progress at their own pace, making it suitable for students and working professionals.
*   A shareable certificate is available upon completion, enhancing your professional profile.

## Course Overview and Structure

This certification program provides a thorough understanding of the data science methodology, outlining all its key aspects. It's structured for beginners, allowing participants to learn comfortably at their own speed. The course duration is approximately 6 hours, delivered through a mix of video lessons and text-based materials.

Participants will gain practical knowledge in applying the six stages of the Cross-Industry Standard Process for Data Mining (CRISP-DM) to analyze real-world case studies. The skills acquired include Data Science, Data Analysis, CRISP-DM, Methodology, and Data Mining, all crucial for real-world application.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this training, you will be equipped to:

*   Determine the right analytical model and identify appropriate data sources for a data science analysis.
*   Understand the business context and define an analytical approach.
*   Grasp the processes of data understanding, preparation, and cleaning.
*   Navigate through the entire data science lifecycle, from understanding to preparation, modeling, evaluation, deployment, and feedback.

These learnings will provide a solid foundation in data science, enabling you to tackle various data-related challenges effectively.

## Who Is This Course For?

This certification course is ideal for aspiring students and current professionals who wish to develop essential data science knowledge and skills. It is particularly beneficial for roles such as:

*   Data Analysts
*   Data Scientists
*   Data Engineers
*   Big Data Engineers

Whether you're looking to start a career in data science or enhance your existing professional capabilities, this course offers valuable insights.

## Fees and Certification

The Data Science Methodology course offers flexible enrollment options, including a free audit track. For those seeking a verifiable certificate, different fee structures apply based on the duration of access:

| Certification Option | Fee Amount (INR) |
| :------------------- | :--------------- |
| Data Science Methodology (Audit) | Free |
| Data Science Methodology - 1 month | Rs. 1,699 |
| Data Science Methodology - 3 months | Rs. 3,499 |
| Data Science Methodology - 6 months | Rs. 5,199 |

Upon successful completion of the graded assessments, a shareable certificate is provided by Coursera, validating your newly acquired skills.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Data Science Methodology course, simply visit the official Coursera platform. You can search for the course by name and choose your preferred enrollment option – whether it's auditing for free or opting for a paid plan to receive the certificate. The self-paced nature means you can start at any time that suits your schedule.

This program is an excellent opportunity to build a foundational understanding of data science principles and practices, guided by industry experts.
