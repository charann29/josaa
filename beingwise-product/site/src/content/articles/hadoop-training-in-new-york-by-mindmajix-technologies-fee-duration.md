# Hadoop Training in New York by Mindmajix Technologies: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build expertise in handling vast datasets, Mindmajix Technologies offers an online Hadoop training program. This course aims to equip learners with the skills needed to manage and process large-scale information, an essential skill in today's data-driven world.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program focuses on Apache Hadoop, an open-source framework for storing and processing big data.
*   It covers core Hadoop components like HDFS, YARN, MapReduce, Pig, Hive, Flume, Sqoop, and Oozie.
*   Training includes practical, hands-on projects guided by experienced industry professionals.
*   Upon completion, students will be prepared for the Hadoop CAA175 certification.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This online certification from Mindmajix Technologies is designed to help participants develop solutions for big data challenges using programming and troubleshooting models. The curriculum is structured to provide a deep understanding of Hadoop's architecture and its various tools. Students will engage in real-world scenarios and projects, such as Solution Advisor Analysis, to apply their knowledge practically.

The course offers flexible learning options, including online live sessions and self-paced video access. Support is available around the clock, and one-on-one doubt-clearing sessions ensure personalized attention. The entire program is delivered in English, with materials accessible via video and text.

## Who Is This Program For?

This training is particularly beneficial for professionals in various tech roles who deal with large datasets or aspire to work in big data. Ideal candidates include:

*   Software Developers
*   Project Managers
*   Data Engineers
*   Software Architects

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is comprehensive, covering fundamental to advanced concepts in Hadoop and big data. Key learning areas include:

*   **Understanding Big Data and Hadoop:** Introduction to big data, limitations of existing analytics, Hadoop features, ecosystem, and core components like HDFS and MapReduce.
*   **YARN (Yet Another Resource Negotiator):** Delving into YARN's architecture, its role as the next-generation MapReduce, and its components: Resource Manager, Application Master, and Node Manager.
*   **Hadoop Architecture and HDFS:** Detailed study of Hadoop 2.x cluster architecture, including federation and high availability, typical production cluster setups, cluster modes, and common shell commands.
*   **Hadoop MapReduce Frameworks:** Use cases for MapReduce, its architecture in Hadoop 2.x, components, YARN MR application execution flow, and practical demonstrations covering input splits, combiners, and partitioners.
*   **Pig:** Introduction to Apache Pig, its use cases, programming structure, running modes, components, and Pig Latin for data models, types, and relational operators.

Upon completing the course, learners will gain knowledge in database management, big data concepts, MongoDB, Hadoop storage, the Hadoop ecosystem, and NoSQL databases.

## Program Offerings and Certification

Mindmajix Technologies provides a robust set of features to support learners throughout their journey:

*   Flexible scheduling for both live online and self-paced training.

The program includes approximately 25 hours of live sessions. Upon successful completion, participants will receive a certificate from Mindmajix Technologies, validating their expertise in Hadoop.
