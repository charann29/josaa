# Certificate Program in Big Data on Cloud (Developer) by 361Degree Minds Consulting Private Limited via TCS iON: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For IT professionals looking to advance their expertise in the rapidly evolving field of big data and cloud development, this certificate program offers a structured learning path. It's designed to equip developers with the crucial skills needed for modern data challenges.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a fully online, self-paced program delivered over 12 weeks.
*   It requires a commitment of 10-11 hours per week and is taught entirely in English.
*   The curriculum is mapped to the TCS iON Procert Big Data Development standard.
*   Successful completion leads to a joint certification from TCS iON and 361Degree Minds Consulting Private Limited.

## Program Overview

The Certificate Program in Big Data on Cloud (Developer) is tailored for individuals with a foundational understanding of programming languages like Java, SQL, and scripting. The course aims to deepen your knowledge and enhance your practical skills as a developer in the big data and cloud domain. It's particularly beneficial for working IT professionals or those already involved in big data development who wish to upgrade their capabilities.

Throughout the 12-week program, you'll gain proficiency in various essential technologies and languages, including Kafka, Spark, Python, and Java. The learning format combines video lectures and text-based materials, allowing for flexible study. The content is carefully aligned with the TCS iON Procert for Big Data Development, ensuring industry relevance and preparing you for advanced roles within major IT companies, including TCS itself.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in this certificate program, candidates must be IT professionals with a minimum of two years of relevant work experience. This requirement ensures that participants have a foundational understanding of the IT landscape, which will help them grasp the advanced concepts taught in the course more effectively.

## What You Will Learn

The program's syllabus is structured into distinct modules, each focusing on specific aspects of big data and cloud development. This modular approach ensures comprehensive coverage of topics, with quizzes and assessments integrated into each module to track your progress and reinforce learning.

Key learning outcomes include:

*   A deep understanding of big data concepts and their application in cloud environments.
*   Proficiency in various tools and technologies used for cloud application development.
*   Enhanced skills in programming languages vital for big data processing, such as Java, Python, and Spark.
*   Knowledge of distributed streaming platforms like Kafka.

Upon successful completion of all modules and associated assessments, you will be well-equipped to tackle complex big data development challenges and contribute to cutting-edge projects.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Certificate Program in Big Data on Cloud (Developer) is ₹18,000, which includes GST. This is a one-time payment that needs to be made online via the TCS iON portal at the time of registration. Once the payment is processed, your access to the course materials and classes will be activated.

### Certification

Upon successfully completing all course modules and passing the required assessments and quizzes, you will be awarded a Certificate Program in Big Data on Cloud (Developer) certification. This certification is jointly provided by TCS iON and 361Degree Minds Consulting Private Limited and can be downloaded directly from the portal after you fulfill all program requirements.

## How to Apply

Interested candidates can register and pay the course fee directly through the official TCS iON portal. The online payment will grant you access to the course content and learning platform.

This program presents a valuable opportunity for IT professionals to upskill and advance their careers in the high-demand field of Big Data on Cloud development.
