# Coursera Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Coursera Courses

Coursera offers a wide range of online courses and degree programs from leading universities and institutions worldwide, providing flexible learning opportunities for students and professionals alike. These programs cover various in-demand fields, helping learners acquire new skills or advance their careers.

## Key Takeaways

*   Coursera hosts online degree programs from prestigious Indian institutions like IIT Guwahati and BITS Pilani.
*   These online degrees are designed to be flexible, catering to both recent school leavers and working professionals.
*   Programs focus on highly sought-after skills in areas such as Data Science, AI, and Computer Science.
*   Learning involves practical projects, case studies, and industry-relevant curriculum to ensure job readiness.

## Online Bachelor's Degrees from Top Indian Institutions

Coursera has partnered with premier Indian educational bodies to offer fully online undergraduate degrees. These programs provide the academic rigor of traditional degrees with the flexibility of online learning, making higher education accessible to a broader audience.

### IIT Guwahati: Online B.Sc (Hons) in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

IIT Guwahati, through its Mehta Family School of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, offers a four-year online Bachelor of Science (Honours) degree. This program is specifically designed to meet the evolving demands of the technology industry, preparing students for roles such as Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, AI Engineer, and Data Consultant. The curriculum is structured to accommodate diverse learners, from Class 12th students to experienced professionals, with a total program fee of around ₹3,49,000 over 48 months.

### BITS Pilani: Online B.Sc Computer Science

BITS Pilani provides a three-year online Bachelor of Science in Computer Science through Coursera. This degree focuses on equipping students with critical skills in Machine Learning, Human-Computer Interaction, Database Programming, and Mobile Application Development. Graduates are prepared for lucrative careers in fields like Data Science, Application Development, Software Engineering, and Product Management. The program emphasizes hands-on projects and real-world problem-solving, with an approximate fee of ₹3,13,000 over 36 months.

## Global Online Bachelor's Degrees

Beyond Indian institutions, Coursera also features online degrees from renowned international universities, offering a global perspective and diverse specializations.

### University of London: B.Sc Computer Science

Goldsmiths, University of London, offers a three-to-six-year online Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. This comprehensive program delves into essential computer science topics, including web development, data science, virtual reality, and artificial intelligence. The curriculum is structured in three parts, progressing from foundational computer programming and mathematical basics to advanced software project development, graphical programming, and data science. The final stage allows students to specialize and undertake an individual software development project, enhancing their problem-solving abilities through real-world case studies.

## Master's Level Programs

For those looking to specialize further, Coursera also hosts online master's degrees in high-demand areas.

### HSE University & MISiS: Master of Business Analytics

This online Master of Business Analytics program explores the critical role of data in business growth. It covers various practices for investigating business metrics and data to develop successful strategies using advanced skills and techniques. The curriculum includes financial theories, fundamentals of asset pricing and valuation (derivatives, bonds, stocks), financial management, corporate finance, market infrastructure, regulation, and financial intermediation. Learners also gain expertise in valuation management, strategic management, and accounting for business development and scaling.

Coursera's platform continues to expand, offering a pathway to quality education from leading institutions, making advanced learning accessible and flexible for a global audience.
