# Big Data Essentials: HDFS, MapReduce and Spark RDD by Yandex via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to build a career in the rapidly expanding big data industry, this certificate course offers a strong foundation. It introduces essential tools and concepts to help you navigate this dynamic field effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a self-paced, online certificate program offered by Yandex through Coursera.
*   It covers fundamental big data technologies like HDFS, MapReduce, and Apache Spark RDDs.
*   The course includes approximately 43 hours of learning content and can be completed in about six weeks.
*   Upon successful completion, including graded assignments, you earn a shareable certificate.

## Course Overview

This program is designed to equip you with the foundational knowledge of big data technologies. You'll delve into the basics of distributed file systems (HDFS), understanding why they exist and how they function. The curriculum also explores the MapReduce framework, a core component in many big data applications, and introduces Apache Spark RDDs, a powerful computational framework.

The course goes beyond theoretical concepts, guiding you through the internal workings of these systems and their practical applications in real-world scenarios. It aims to develop your skills in using these tools to solve problems across various sectors, including social networks, finance, and telecommunications.

## Learning Experience and Content

The learning experience is flexible and self-directed, allowing you to study at your own pace. The course provides a rich set of resources, including online video lectures, comprehensive reading materials, and various practice exercises. Crucially, it includes graded assignments that offer hands-on experience and the opportunity to apply your knowledge to real-world big data challenges. Some assignments may even be evaluated on a real cluster, providing valuable practical exposure.

### What You Will Learn

Throughout the program, you will gain an understanding of:

*   The core technologies of the modern big data industry: HDFS, MapReduce, and Spark.
*   Distributed file systems, their purpose, functionality, and internal mechanisms.
*   The framework and operations of MapReduce, a cornerstone of big data processing.
*   The fundamentals and applications of Spark technology in computational frameworks.
*   Practical skills in selecting and applying various industry tools to address real-world big data problems.

## Duration and Flexibility

This online program is designed to be completed in approximately six weeks, assuming a consistent study schedule. However, its self-paced nature means you have the flexibility to adjust deadlines and progress through the material at a pace that suits your individual schedule. The total learning content is estimated to be around 43 hours.

## Fees and Certification

The Big Data Essentials course is available for a fee of approximately INR 2,159. This fee includes access to all course materials, graded assignments, and the official certificate upon successful completion. The certificate is awarded by Coursera to those who complete all course requirements and graded assignments satisfactorily. It is a shareable credential that can be added to your LinkedIn profile, CV, and professional portfolio.

### Audit Option

For those interested in exploring the content without committing to the full certificate program, an 'audit for free' option is available. This allows you to access all video content. However, please note that the audit option does not include access to graded assignments and does not provide a certificate of completion.

## How to Apply

Enrollment for this course is typically straightforward through the Coursera platform. You can simply visit the course page on Coursera, choose your preferred enrollment option (paid certificate track or free audit), and follow the on-screen instructions to register.

This program offers a valuable entry point into the exciting world of big data, equipping you with practical skills from a reputable provider.
