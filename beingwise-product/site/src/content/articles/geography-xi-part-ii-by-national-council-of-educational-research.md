# Geography XI Part-II by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course offers an in-depth exploration of Class 11 Geography, specifically focusing on India's physical environment and geographical makeup. It's an excellent opportunity for students to deepen their understanding of key geographical concepts from the comfort of their homes.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is a 24-week program delivered through video lectures and e-texts.
*   It is coordinated by Dr. Tannu Malik from the Department of Education in Social Sciences (DESS), NCERT.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from NCERT and Swayam.
*   The learning format is self-paced, allowing flexibility for students.

## Course Overview

Designed for Class 11 students, this Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) covers essential aspects of Indian geography. It delves into the diverse physical features of India, providing a comprehensive understanding of its environment. The program is hosted on the Swayam platform, a government initiative for online education, ensuring accessibility and quality content developed by NCERT.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a thorough grasp of India's physical geography. Key topics include:

*   India's Physical Environment: Its location and spatial relationships globally.
*   Geological Structure and Physiography: Understanding the formation and physical divisions of the country, including the Himalayas, Indo-Ganga-Brahmaputra Plain, Peninsular India, deserts, coastal plains, and islands.
*   Drainage System: A detailed study of India's rivers and water bodies.
*   Climate: Exploration of the monsoon climate, its mechanisms, seasons, climatic regions, and associated vulnerabilities.
*   Natural Vegetation: Types of forests, their ecological significance, and environmental well-being.
*   Soils: Soil as an organism, different soil types, degradation, erosion, and conservation methods.
*   Natural Hazards and Disasters: An overview of earthquakes, tsunamis, tropical cyclones, landslides, floods, and droughts.

Each chapter is complemented by assignments to reinforce learning, culminating in a final assessment.

## Duration and Learning Mode

This self-study course spans 24 weeks, offering ample time for students to engage with the material at their own pace. The instruction medium is English, and content is delivered through a blend of video lectures, supporting e-texts, and additional web resources. The platform encourages an interactive learning experience with discussion forums and self-assessment tools, including multiple-choice questions.

## Certification and Eligibility

Upon successfully completing the course and achieving a minimum score of 70% in the final assessment, students will be awarded a certificate. This certificate is jointly provided by NCERT and Swayam, validating the knowledge gained. There are no specific pre-qualification criteria for enrollment beyond being a student interested in Class 11 Geography.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Geography XI Part-II course on Swayam is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal for the course.
2.  Look for the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically located in the top right corner of the page.
3.  If you are a new user, you will need to sign up for an account. Existing users can sign in with their credentials.
4.  Once signed in, you can enroll in the course to begin your learning journey.

This course is an invaluable resource for Class 11 students aiming to build a strong foundation in Indian geography, offering a structured, flexible, and certified learning experience.
