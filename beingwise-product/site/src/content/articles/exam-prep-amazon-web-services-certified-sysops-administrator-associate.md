# Exam Prep Amazon Web Services Certified SysOps Administrator Associate by Amazon Web Services via Edx: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to advance their career in cloud computing, the AWS Certified SysOps Administrator – Associate exam preparation course offers a structured path to gain essential skills. This program, delivered by Amazon Web Services, is designed to equip you with the knowledge needed to manage and operate systems on the AWS platform.

## Key Takeaways

*   This course is a self-paced, video and text-based program, delivered entirely in English.
*   It focuses on preparing individuals for the AWS Certified SysOps Administrator – Associate certification exam.
*   The learning effort required is approximately 2-4 hours per week.
*   Upon successful completion and passing the official exam, participants receive a certificate directly from Amazon Web Services.

## Course Overview and Learning Approach

This preparation course is structured for self-study, allowing you to learn at your own pace. The content is delivered through a blend of video lectures and textual materials, all in English. This flexible format is ideal for working professionals or students who need to integrate learning into their existing schedules, with an estimated commitment of 2 to 4 hours per week.

## Syllabus Highlights

The curriculum is thoughtfully organized into weekly modules, covering key domains essential for an AWS SysOps Administrator. Here's a breakdown of what you'll learn:

### Week 1: Foundations of AWS Certifications

*   An introduction to the various AWS certifications available.
*   Guidance on how to prepare for, register for, and successfully take an AWS certification exam.

### Week 2: Core Operational Domains

*   **Monitoring, Logging, and Remediation (20% weightage):** Understanding how to observe system performance, gather logs, and respond to issues.
*   **Deployment, Provisioning, and Automation (18% weightage):** Learning about deploying applications, setting up resources, and automating operational tasks.

### Week 3: Reliability and Networking

*   **Reliability and Business Continuity (16% weightage):** Focusing on designing resilient systems and ensuring continuous operations.
*   **Networking and Content Delivery (18% weightage):** Covering AWS networking services and efficient content distribution.

### Week 4: Security and Optimization

*   **Security and Compliance (16% weightage):** Implementing security best practices and adhering to compliance standards on AWS.
*   **Cost and Performance Optimization (12% weightage):** Strategies for managing AWS costs effectively and enhancing system performance.

## Expert Instructors

The course content is developed and delivered by experienced cloud technologists from Amazon Web Services. You will learn from:

*   **Mr. Seph Robinson, Cloud Technologist, Amazon Web Services**
*   **Mr. Blaine Sundrud, Senior Cloud Technologist, Amazon Web Services**

Their expertise provides valuable insights and practical knowledge for aspiring SysOps administrators.

## Certification and Fees

Upon successfully completing the course and passing the official AWS Certified SysOps Administrator – Associate exam, you will be awarded a certification directly by Amazon Web Services. Specific details regarding the course fee and any associated exam fees should be checked on the official Edx platform where the course is hosted, as these can vary.

Embarking on this preparation course can be a significant step towards a rewarding career in cloud operations.
