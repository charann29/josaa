# KCET Exam Date 2026 (OUT): KCET Admit Card (April 10), Exam on April 23 & 24, Result Dates

The Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to pursue various undergraduate courses in Karnataka. Understanding the key dates for application, admit card release, examination, and results is essential for a smooth admission process.

## Key Takeaways

*   The KCET 2026 examination is scheduled for April 23 and 24, 2026.
*   Admit cards for KCET 2026 were released on April 10, 2026, for all eligible candidates.
*   The exam will be conducted in an offline, pen-and-paper format across designated centres.
*   The KCET result for 2026 is tentatively expected in May 2026.

## KCET 2026 Application and Correction Window

The application process for KCET 2026 began with the official notification released by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) on January 18, 2026. Candidates had ample time to submit their applications, with the final date for fee payment extended until March 7, 2026. Following the application submission, a correction window was provided from February 24 to March 30, 2026, allowing applicants to rectify any errors in their submitted forms. This facility ensures that all candidate details are accurate before the admit card generation.

## KCET 2026 Admit Card Details

The KCET 2026 admit card was made available for download on April 10, 2026. Only candidates who successfully completed their application and fee payment were issued this essential document. It is imperative for all candidates to download their admit card from the official KEA website. Upon downloading, applicants must carefully review all details printed on it, such as their name, photograph, exam centre, and examination dates. In case of any discrepancies, candidates should immediately contact the examination authority for rectification. Carrying the admit card, along with a valid government-issued photo identification, is mandatory for entry into the examination hall.

## KCET 2026 Examination Schedule

The KEA has officially announced the dates for the KCET 2026 entrance examination. The main examination will take place on April 23 and 24, 2026. Additionally, a Kannada language test is scheduled for April 22, 2026. The examination format will be offline, requiring candidates to mark their answers on OMR sheets using a pen. Candidates must arrive at their designated exam centres well in advance, carrying their admit card and a valid photo ID.

## KCET 2026 Result and Counselling

Following the examination, candidates eagerly await their results. The KCET 2026 results are tentatively scheduled to be released in May 2026. Once the results are declared, qualified candidates will be eligible to participate in the counselling process. The KEA will release detailed information regarding the answer key, result declaration, and the subsequent counselling schedule on its official portal. This counselling process will determine admissions to various undergraduate courses, including B.E./B.Tech, B.Sc, B.Pharm, D.Pharm, and others, across colleges in Karnataka.

Stay updated with the official KEA website for any further announcements regarding the KCET 2026 process.
