# Agile Project Management by Google via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to step into the dynamic world of project management, Google, in collaboration with Coursera, offers an online certificate course in Agile Project Management. This program is designed to equip learners with the essential skills and knowledge needed to manage projects effectively using Agile and Scrum methodologies.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a beginner-level, fully online course with a flexible schedule, making it accessible for a wide range of learners.
*   No prior educational qualifications or experience are needed to enroll, opening doors for anyone interested in project management.
*   The course is part of the broader Google Project Management Certificate program and offers a verified certificate upon completion.
*   It covers foundational concepts of Agile and Scrum, preparing students for real-world project management roles.

## Course Overview and Structure

This Agile Project Management course is delivered entirely online, featuring video lectures, readings, quizzes, activities, and case studies. The content is presented in English, with subtitles available to assist learners. While the course is designed to be completed in approximately 19 hours, it is structured over four weeks, allowing for a flexible self-study pace.

The curriculum is thoughtfully developed by Google Career Certificates, ensuring that the content is relevant and practical for today's job market. It delves into the core principles of Agile and Scrum, their applications, and how to effectively implement them in project scenarios.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, you will gain a comprehensive understanding of Agile and Scrum. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Grasping the fundamental theories, methodologies, standards, and concepts of Agile project management.
*   Understanding the foundations of Scrum and its supporting principles.
*   Identifying and comparing crucial roles within a Scrum team and factors influencing their effectiveness.
*   Learning to create and maintain a Product Backlog and refine it.
*   Organizing the five main Scrum events and preparing a Scrum team for each.
*   Identifying value plans and implementing value-driven methods of Agile.
*   Acquiring basic Agile training for project handling.
*   Preparing for Agile roles, including tips for successful interviews.

## Course Fees and Certificate

The course offers flexible payment options, typically based on the duration you need to complete the program. The fees are structured as follows:

Upon successful completion of all required activities, preparation sessions, and reading materials, participants will receive a verified certificate from Coursera. This certificate confirms your proficiency in Agile Project Management and can be a valuable addition to your professional profile.

## Eligibility and How to Apply

One of the significant advantages of this program is that it has no specific educational prerequisites. It is designed for beginners and anyone keen to learn about Scrum and Agile methodologies and earn a Google-certified credential in project management.

To enroll in the Agile Project Management online program and work towards your certificate, you can follow these simple steps:

1.  Visit the official course page on the Coursera platform.
2.  Locate and click the "Enroll" option.
3.  Proceed by selecting "Next" on the screen.
4.  Complete the application or enrollment form with all the necessary details.
5.  Make the required course fee payment to gain access to the lectures and coursework for the verified certificate.

This course offers a fantastic opportunity to develop essential project management skills and gain a recognized certification, setting you on a path towards a successful career in project management.
