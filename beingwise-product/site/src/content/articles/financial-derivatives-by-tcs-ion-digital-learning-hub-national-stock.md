# Financial Derivatives by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub National Stock Exchange of India Ltd: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen to deepen their understanding of financial markets, the Financial Derivatives course, a collaboration between TCS iON Digital Learning Hub and the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd (NSE), offers a structured learning path. This program focuses on derivative instruments, their characteristics, and trading strategies, delivered through a blend of self-study and virtual classroom sessions.

## Key takeaways

*   This online certification provides six academic credits and is designed to be completed within five to six months.
*   The curriculum covers essential financial concepts, risk management, and practical trading applications using tools like NSE SMART.
*   Learners benefit from guidance by industry experts and academic professionals, ensuring a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical insights.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a digital certificate from TCS iON, signifying their enhanced expertise in derivatives trading.

## Course Overview

This career-focused program is tailored for individuals looking to build expertise in commercial and financial sectors, particularly in derivatives trading. The course content is delivered through video and text-based modules, supported by online lectures, tutorials, and hands-on sessions. It emphasizes experiential learning, guided by experts such as Dr. Priti S. Aggarwal from the Indian Institute of Banking and Finance, and Ramesh Lakshman, proprietor of Ramesh Lakshman and Company.

## Program Offerings and Learning Experience

The course provides a comprehensive learning environment with a variety of resources and opportunities:

*   **Online Resources:** Access to extensive online materials, lectures, and tutorials.
*   **Interactive Learning:** Hands-on sessions, assignments, and practice tests to reinforce understanding.
*   **Community Engagement:** Connect with academic and industry peers through dedicated communities.
*   **Evaluations:** The program includes summative tests to assess learning outcomes.
*   **Career Support:** The course aims to prepare students for roles such as derivatives analysts, banking executives, and derivatives advisors.

## Eligibility and Certification Criteria

While a basic understanding of commerce concepts is recommended for applicants, the program is designed to enhance the expertise of individuals aiming for careers in trading and stock exchanges. To earn the digital certificate of achievement from TCS iON, participants must meet specific criteria:

*   Achieve a minimum of 40% in Part A and 50% in Part B of the assessments.
*   Maintain an overall average score of 50% across all evaluations.
*   Completing the course, industrial assignments, projects, and practice assessments also qualifies participants for a certificate of participation.

## Course Duration and Fees

The Financial Derivatives program is structured to be completed over five to six months. The entire course, including access to all online classes and resources, has a fee of ₹11,800, which includes GST. This fee is payable after registration to unlock full access to the learning content.

| Particular   | Details    |
| :----------- | :--------- |
| **Course Fee** | ₹11,800    |

## What You Will Learn

The syllabus is meticulously designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical application in derivatives trading. Key learning outcomes include:

*   In-depth knowledge of principal derivative instruments and their attributes.
*   Understanding various trading strategies and their practical applications.
*   Hands-on experience with market trading applications like NSE SMART.
*   Enhanced skills in financial knowledge, economics, risk management, and accounting proficiency.

This course is an excellent opportunity for commerce students and professionals to gain a competitive edge in the dynamic world of financial derivatives.
