# Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI) by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a rapidly expanding field, transforming various sectors from science and technology to healthcare and education. For students eager to explore the possibilities within AI, this introductory certification by IBM, offered through Coursera, provides a solid foundation.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a beginner-friendly course requiring no prior experience in computer science or programming.
*   The program is self-paced, delivered online through videos and text, and designed for completion in approximately 12 hours.
*   It covers fundamental AI concepts, machine learning, deep learning, neural networks, and ethical considerations.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a shareable certificate from Coursera.

## Course Overview and Content

Developed by IBM, the "Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI)" certification is designed for anyone interested in understanding this dynamic field, regardless of their academic background. The course moves beyond theoretical concepts to provide practical exposure and insights into real-world AI applications.

Participants will gain an understanding of key terminology and concepts such as machine learning, deep learning, and neural networks. It also delves into crucial, often overlooked aspects like AI ethics and bias, preparing learners for a comprehensive view of the technology's impact.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a holistic understanding of AI. By the end of this course, you will be able to:

*   Define core AI concepts, including machine learning, deep learning, and neural networks.
*   Identify various applications and uses of AI across different industries.
*   Understand the ethical considerations, potential biases, and job opportunities within the AI landscape.
*   Gain practical insights into AI in action, including using computer vision for image classification.
*   Formulate an informed perspective on the future of AI and its societal implications.
*   Discuss the benefits and costs of implementing AI solutions in diverse fields.

## Course Format and Duration

This certification is delivered entirely online and is self-paced, offering flexible deadlines to accommodate various schedules. The learning materials include course videos, readings, graded quizzes, and assignments. While the course provides a 7-day full access free trial, the total estimated time for completion is around 12 hours.

## Fees and Certification

To earn the certification, candidates must verify their identity, successfully complete all assignments, and meet the course's passing threshold. The certification is provided by Coursera.

The fees for the course are structured as follows, offering different durations for access:

| Access Duration | Amount (INR) |
| :-------------- | :----------- |
| 1 month         | Rs. 1,683    |
| 3 months        | Rs. 3,384    |
| 6 months        | Rs. 5,051    |

Financial aid options may also be available for eligible candidates, as detailed on the official Coursera platform.

## Eligibility and How to Apply

One of the significant advantages of this course is its accessibility. There are no specific prerequisites regarding work experience in computer science or programming. Anyone with an interest in AI, whether from a technical or non-technical background, can enroll.

To apply, individuals can visit the official Coursera platform and search for the "Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI)" course by IBM. You can begin with the free trial to explore the content before committing to the full certification.

This certification offers a valuable starting point for anyone looking to enter or understand the exciting world of Artificial Intelligence.
