# IIT JAM 2026: Scorecard (OUT), Counselling, Choice filling, Seat Allotment

The Joint Admission Test for Masters (JAM) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to pursue postgraduate science programs at India's premier institutions. This page provides essential information regarding the IIT JAM 2026 scorecard, the subsequent counselling process, choice filling, and seat allotment.

## Key takeaways

*   The IIT JAM 2026 scorecard was officially released on March 26, 2026, ahead of its initially scheduled date.
*   Candidates whose scores meet or exceed the qualifying cutoff are eligible to participate in the IIT JAM 2026 admission process.
*   The counselling registration for IIT JAM 2026 commenced on March 27, 2026, marking the beginning of the seat allocation procedure.
*   The official JAM 2026 results, including individual marks and ranks, were declared by IIT Bombay on May 19, 2026.

## Understanding Your IIT JAM 2026 Scorecard

The IIT JAM 2026 scorecard is a vital document that contains your marks, All India Rank (AIR), and the qualifying cutoff for your respective test paper. It was made available on March 26, 2026, through the official JAM Online Application Processing System (JOAPS) portal. Candidates can access their scorecards by logging in with their credentials.

Before the scorecard release, the conducting authority, IIT Bombay, had also published the master question paper and the official answer key, allowing candidates to estimate their scores. The qualifying marks for JAM 2026 were released on March 21, 2026.

### How to Check Your IIT JAM 2026 Result and Scorecard

To view your result and download your scorecard, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official IIT JAM 2026 website.
2.  Locate and click on the link for the JOAPS portal.
3.  Enter your enrollment ID/email address and password.
4.  Your result and scorecard will be displayed for you to review and download.

## IIT JAM 2026 Counselling and Admission Process

The admission process for IIT JAM 2026 officially began with counselling registration starting on March 27, 2026. Only candidates who have qualified by scoring above the minimum cutoff marks are eligible to participate in this process. The counselling involves several stages, including registration, choice filling, and seat allotment.

### Choice Filling

During the choice filling stage, qualified candidates will log into the JOAPS portal and select their preferred postgraduate programs and institutions from the list of participating IITs and IISc. It is crucial to fill choices carefully, considering your rank, program interests, and institutional preferences. Candidates should also be aware of the approximately 2000 seats available for postgraduate programs like M.Sc. and M.A. through JAM scores.

### Seat Allotment

Seat allotment is conducted in multiple rounds. Seats are allocated based on the candidate's All India Rank, the choices filled, and the availability of seats in various categories. Once a seat is allotted, candidates will need to accept the offer and complete the admission formalities within the stipulated timeframe, which usually involves paying an admission fee.
