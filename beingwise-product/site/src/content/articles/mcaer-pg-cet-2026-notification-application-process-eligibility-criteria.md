# MCAER PG CET 2026: Notification, Application Process, Eligibility Criteria and Other Details

The Maharashtra Council of Agricultural Education and Research (MCAER) conducts a state-level entrance examination for admission to various postgraduate programs in agricultural and allied sciences across State Agricultural Universities (SAUs) in Maharashtra. This guide provides essential information for aspiring students planning to appear for the MCAER PG CET 2026.

## Key Takeaways

*   The official notification for MCAER PG CET 2026 is expected in February 2026.
*   Application forms will likely be available online from mid-February 2026.
*   The examination for postgraduate courses such as M.Sc., M.Tech., and M.F.Sc. is tentatively scheduled for June 2026.
*   Results are anticipated in July 2026, followed by the counselling process in August 2026.

## Understanding MCAER PG CET

The MCAER PG CET is an annual entrance examination administered by the Maharashtra Agricultural Universities Examination Board. Its primary purpose is to shortlist candidates for admission to postgraduate courses like Master of Science (M.Sc.), Master of Engineering and Technology (M.Tech.), and Master of Fisheries Science (M.F.Sc.) offered at State Agricultural Universities (SAUs) throughout Maharashtra. This state-level test is typically conducted in a pen-and-paper format and sees participation from approximately 10,000 students each year.

## Courses Offered Through MCAER PG CET

The MCAER PG CET serves as the gateway to a range of postgraduate programs in agricultural and allied fields. The primary courses for which this exam is conducted include:

*   Master of Science (M.Sc.)
*   Master of Technology (M.Tech.)
*   Master of Fisheries Science (M.F.Sc.)

These programs are available at various State Agricultural Universities (SAUs) affiliated with MCAER.

## MCAER PG CET 2026 Application Process

Aspiring candidates will need to complete an online application process. While specific details for 2026 will be released with the official notification, generally, the steps involve:

1.  **Registration:** Visiting the official MCAER website and completing the initial registration.
2.  **Form Filling:** Providing personal, academic, and contact details accurately in the application form.
3.  **Document Upload:** Uploading scanned copies of required documents, such as photographs and signatures, as per specified guidelines.
4.  **Fee Payment:** Paying the application fee through the available online payment modes.
5.  **Form Submission:** Reviewing all entered information and submitting the application form.

It is highly recommended to keep a printout of the submitted application form and the fee receipt for future reference. Candidates must adhere to the deadlines for application submission and fee payment, which are typically in February.

## Eligibility Criteria for MCAER PG CET 2026

Before applying, candidates must ensure they meet the eligibility criteria set by MCAER. While the detailed criteria will be in the official brochure, typically, candidates must possess a relevant bachelor's degree from a recognized university in the field they wish to pursue their postgraduate studies. Specific percentage requirements and domicile rules for Maharashtra residents may also apply. Always refer to the official notification for the precise and updated eligibility conditions for 2026.

Preparing well and staying updated with official announcements on the MCAER portal will be key to a successful application and examination experience for MCAER PG CET 2026.
