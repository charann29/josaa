# Edureka Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Edureka Courses

Edureka offers a variety of online courses and certification programs designed to help students and professionals enhance their skills in rapidly evolving fields. These programs often combine expert-led instruction with practical, industry-relevant projects.

## Key Takeaways

*   Edureka collaborates with top Indian institutions like NIT Warangal, NIT Rourkela, and IMT Ghaziabad for specialized postgraduate programs.
*   Programs are primarily online, featuring live, instructor-led sessions, often scheduled on weekends to accommodate working professionals.
*   Curricula are developed by industry experts and academic faculty, focusing on hands-on experience through projects and case studies.
*   Courses cover in-demand areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Big Data Engineering, and Digital Marketing.

## Postgraduate Diploma in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

This comprehensive program is a joint initiative by Edureka and the E&ICT Academy at NIT Warangal. It's designed for both programmers and non-programmers who wish to delve into the fields of Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), aiming to either upskill or embark on a new career path.

### Program Structure and Content

The diploma spans 11 months and involves over 450 hours of intensive learning. It's delivered through live, instructor-led online classes, conveniently scheduled on weekends. Participants are expected to dedicate 8-10 hours per week. The curriculum, crafted by NIT Warangal professors and industry specialists, covers a wide range of topics from foundational concepts to advanced industry standards.

### Practical Learning and Skills

A strong emphasis is placed on practical application, with more than 20 industry projects and over 100 case studies to provide real-world exposure. Upon successful completion, participants receive an industry-recognized AI certification from Edureka. Key skills covered include:

*   Deep Learning
*   Machine Learning
*   Natural Language Processing (NLP)
*   Python programming

### Fees and Duration

*   **Duration:** 11 Months
*   **Program Fee:** ₹1,89,999

## Advanced Executive Program in Big Data Engineering

In today's data-driven world, companies across various sectors are heavily investing in Big Data capabilities to extract valuable insights and enhance their operations. This surge in demand has created a significant need for skilled Big Data Engineers, a gap this program from Edureka, in collaboration with NIT Rourkela, aims to fill.

### Program Highlights

This 9-month postgraduate certification introduces participants to essential tools and concepts like Linux, SQL, and Java within the Big Data ecosystem. The program delves into the building blocks of big data, followed by distributed and parallel computing. It's structured to provide a realistic learning experience through two mid-program projects and a final industry-grade exposure project.

### Skills You Will Learn

Participants will gain proficiency in critical areas vital for Big Data engineering roles:

*   Machine Learning concepts relevant to data processing
*   SQL for database management and querying
*   Knowledge of Apache Spark for large-scale data processing

### Fees and Duration

*   **Duration:** 9 Months
*   **Program Fee:** ₹1,97,500

## Post Graduate Program in Marketing with Specialization in Digital Marketing

IMT Ghaziabad, recognized among the top business schools in India, partners with Edureka to offer this specialized postgraduate program. It is specifically designed for individuals aspiring to build a career in the dynamic field of Digital Marketing.

### Curriculum and Faculty

The program builds upon fundamental traditional marketing concepts, including STP (Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning), consumer behavior, and product mix decisions. The instruction is delivered by the full-time faculty of IMT Ghaziabad, ensuring high academic standards and industry relevance. The curriculum guides students through the transition from conventional marketing strategies to modern digital approaches.

Edureka's collaborations with leading institutions ensure that these programs are current, comprehensive, and career-focused, preparing learners for success in their chosen domains.
