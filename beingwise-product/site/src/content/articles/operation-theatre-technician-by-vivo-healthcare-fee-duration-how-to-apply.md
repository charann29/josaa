# Operation Theatre Technician by VIVO Healthcare: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students aspiring to a vital role in healthcare, the Operation Theatre Technician program offered by VIVO Healthcare provides comprehensive training to become a skilled assistant in surgical settings. This one-year course equips you with the knowledge and practical abilities needed to support surgeons, doctors, and anaesthetists during operations.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 12-month program focusing on practical skills and theoretical knowledge for operation theatre roles.
*   The curriculum covers essential aspects like anatomy, physiology, infection control, and equipment management.
*   Upon successful completion, including an internship and passing a pre-internship exam, you receive a VIVO Healthcare certification.
*   The program includes placement assistance and an internship opportunity to gain real-world experience.

## Program Overview

The Operation Theatre Technician certification is designed to make you proficient in the critical tasks required within an operating theatre. You will learn to prepare the OT, assist medical professionals during procedures, and ensure all protocols are strictly followed before, during, and after surgeries. The training combines classroom sessions with hands-on laboratory experience, providing a well-rounded education.

Key topics covered include human anatomy and physiology, methods of disinfection and infection prevention, understanding surgical positions, maintaining and operating emergency equipment, sterilisation techniques, and patient preparation. You will also learn about biomedical waste management, radiation safety, and essential documentation procedures, preparing you to monitor patients and independently assist in the OT.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Operation Theatre Technician program, candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 education, specifically from the science stream. This foundational academic background is essential for grasping the medical and technical concepts taught in the course.

To earn the final Operation Theatre Technician certificate, students must meet several requirements: full payment of the course fee, achieving at least 70% in the pre-internship examination, and maintaining a minimum of 85% attendance during the mandatory clinical internship. These criteria ensure that certified individuals possess both the theoretical understanding and practical commitment required for the role.

## Course Duration and Certification

The VIVO Healthcare Operation Theatre Technician program spans a duration of 12 months. Upon successful completion of all academic and practical requirements, including passing the pre-internship exam and fulfilling internship attendance, you will be awarded a certificate by VIVO Healthcare. This certification is a testament to your professional qualification in the field and can significantly enhance your resume when applying for roles as a Junior OT Technician, paving the way for career progression to a Senior OT Technician.

## Admission Process

The admission process for the Operation Theatre Technician course involves a few straightforward steps:

1.  **Access the Application Form:** You can download the admission form directly from the official VIVO Healthcare course portal or obtain a physical copy from your nearest VIVO Skill Centre.
2.  **Complete and Submit:** Fill out the form accurately with your personal details, contact information, residential address, email ID, and educational qualifications. Along with the completed form, you will need to submit a photo ID proof, copies of your previous educational certificates/mark sheets (such as Degree, Intermediate, or SSC), and three passport-sized photographs. These documents should be submitted at a VIVO Skill Centre.
3.  **Fee Payment:** After your application is verified, you can proceed to pay the registration fee, which is approximately Rs. 2000, and the course fee. Payments can be made at the Skill Centre. Alternatively, for online payment, visit the course portal, click on “Apply Now,” and register or log in to complete the transaction.

This program offers a structured pathway to a rewarding career in the healthcare sector, providing you with the necessary skills and official certification to begin your professional journey as an Operation Theatre Technician.
