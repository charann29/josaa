# Diploma in Graphic Design, Video Editing and 2D Animation by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This diploma program offers a comprehensive pathway for students interested in visual storytelling through graphic design, video editing, and 2D animation, equipping them with practical skills for a dynamic industry.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is a 6-month, 100-hour course offered by the Boston Institute of Analytics.
*   It provides hands-on training in industry-standard software like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects.
*   Students will gain skills in image editing, typography, motion graphics, and 2D animation fundamentals.
*   The course is designed for a wide range of academic backgrounds and culminates in a certification from the institute.

## Course Overview

The Diploma in Graphic Design, Video Editing and 2D Animation is structured to provide students with a strong foundation and practical expertise in these creative fields. The curriculum focuses on essential tools and techniques, preparing learners for various roles in the animation and video industry. Through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video-based content, students learn to create compelling visual narratives.

## What You Will Learn

Throughout the program, participants will delve into various aspects of graphic design, video editing, and 2D animation. Key learning areas include:

*   **Graphic Design Fundamentals:** Mastering essential tools and techniques, image editing, and retouching using Adobe Photoshop, and understanding typography and layout design.
*   **Video Editing and Motion Graphics:** Learning animation principles and keyframes in Adobe After Effects to create engaging motion graphics.
*   **2D Animation:** Introduction to 2D animation concepts and workflows.
*   **Project-Based Learning:** Developing a strong portfolio by working on practical assignments that showcase creativity and technical skills.

## Eligibility Criteria and Admissions

Students from any academic background—be it arts, science, commerce, or engineering—are eligible to apply for this diploma. The primary requirement is an interest in the field and a willingness to learn. Upon successful completion of the course, participants will receive a certification from the Boston Institute of Analytics.

To apply for the program, interested candidates should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official Boston Institute of Analytics website for the Graphic Design, Video Editing and 2D Animation diploma.
2.  Locate and click on the "Enquire Now" option.
3.  Fill out the inquiry form with your contact details; a representative from the institute will then get in touch with you.
4.  Once the necessary fees are paid, your enrollment in the course will be confirmed.

## Course Fee and Duration

This diploma program spans a duration of **six months** and involves approximately **100 hours** of learning content. For specific details regarding the course fees, it is recommended to directly contact the Boston Institute of Analytics through their official inquiry process, as fees can vary and are best confirmed with the institution.

This program is ideal for individuals aspiring to become animators, video game designers, multimedia animators, or film and video editors, offering a solid starting point for a career in these creative industries.
