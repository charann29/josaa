# Graphic Design, Video Editing and 2D Animation by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen on visual storytelling and digital creativity, the Boston Institute of Analytics (BIA) offers a certification program in Graphic Design, Video Editing, and 2D Animation. This comprehensive course aims to equip learners with practical skills for the dynamic animation and multimedia industries.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is a 4-month, 100-hour certification course.
*   It offers a dual certification from the Boston Institute of Analytics.
*   Learning modes include both virtual and physical classroom sessions, primarily on weekends.
*   It covers essential software like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, and Maya.

## Course Overview

This certification program is meticulously designed to provide a well-rounded education in graphics, animation, and visual media. It focuses on developing skills vital for visual storytelling, covering various facets of digital design and animation. Students will receive instruction from industry professionals and engage in hands-on training using popular software tools.

The curriculum includes key areas such as image editing and retouching, typography, motion graphics, 3D modeling fundamentals, lighting, rendering, and core animation principles. By the end of the course, participants are expected to build a strong portfolio showcasing their acquired skills and creative work.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The BIA program emphasizes practical, hands-on learning through live lectures and project-based assignments. It's structured for accessibility, offering classes primarily on weekends and allowing for both online and in-person participation. Key features include:

*   **Dual Certification:** Participants earn two certifications upon successful completion.
*   **Foundation Course:** Provides a strong base for beginners in the field.
*   **Hands-on Learning:** Focus on practical application of tools and techniques.
*   **Live Lectures:** Interactive sessions with instructors.
*   **Weekend Classes:** Designed for flexibility, catering to working professionals and students.

## Eligibility and Application Process

### Eligibility Criteria

Candidates from any academic background—be it arts, science, commerce, or engineering—are welcome to apply for this course. The primary requirement for certification is the successful completion of the training program.

### How to Apply

To enroll in the program, interested individuals should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official Boston Institute of Analytics website.
2.  Locate the "Enquire Now" option and submit the inquiry form with your contact details.
3.  A representative from the institute will then contact you to guide you through the next steps.
4.  Upon payment of the course fees, your enrollment will be confirmed.

## Course Content and Learning Outcomes

Throughout the program, participants will delve into various topics, including:

*   Fundamental tools and techniques in graphic design and animation.
*   Proficiency in image editing and retouching using Adobe Photoshop.
*   Mastering typography and creating engaging text effects.
*   Designing effective graphics and layouts.
*   Learning animation principles and keyframes in Adobe After Effects.
*   Introduction to 3D modeling, texturing, and shading using tools like Maya.
*   Understanding lighting and rendering techniques for visual projects.

This course is ideal for individuals aspiring to careers as animators, video game designers, multimedia animators, or film and video editors. It provides the necessary skills to bring creative ideas to life and produce compelling visuals using industry-standard software.

## Duration and Fees

The Graphic Design, Video Editing, and 2D Animation certification program spans 4 months, totaling 100 hours of instruction. For specific details regarding the course fee, it is recommended to contact the Boston Institute of Analytics directly through their official inquiry process.

This program prepares students for successful careers in animation studios, advertising agencies, game development companies, and other related industries.
