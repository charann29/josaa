# Full Stack Web Development (Part-Time) by Masai School: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Masai School offers a part-time Full Stack Web Development certification course designed to transform aspiring learners into job-ready web developers. This program is structured to fit around your existing commitments, providing comprehensive training in essential web technologies.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 52-week, part-time online certification course from Masai School.
*   Classes are held on weekdays (Monday-Thursday) and Saturdays, delivered through virtual classrooms.
*   The unique fee structure means you pay nothing upfront; fees are only due after securing a job with a package of ₹5 lakhs per annum or more.
*   The curriculum covers core web development skills, preparing students for various roles like Full Stack Developer and Software Engineer.

## Course Overview and Structure

This Full Stack Web Development program spans 52 weeks and is delivered in a part-time, online format. The course curriculum is divided into six modules, updated regularly to align with the latest industry technologies and demands. The primary goal is to equip students with practical skills and a solid understanding of web development principles.

Throughout the course, you will delve into foundational concepts such as JavaScript, Loops, Arrays, the Document Object Model (DOM), and CSS. The training also emphasizes critical problem-solving techniques like Bruteforce and Backtracking, which are integral to efficient web application development. The learning experience includes regular classes, comprehensive reading materials, assessments, and continuous mentor support, culminating in various projects to apply your knowledge.

Upon successful completion, students receive a Full Stack Web Development certification from Masai School, opening doors to diverse career opportunities in the tech industry.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for this part-time Full Stack Web Development certification course, candidates must meet the following requirements:

*   **Age:** Applicants must be between 18 and 28 years old.
*   **Education:** Candidates should have completed Class 12 and hold a graduate or postgraduate degree from the years 2021 or 2022.
*   **Work Experience:** While prior work experience is not mandatory, individuals with professional experience are welcome to apply.

To earn the certification, students must complete the entire course curriculum and maintain at least 90% attendance until their graduation date.

## Fee Structure and Payment Model

One of the distinctive features of this program is its Income Share Agreement (ISA) model. The total course fee is ₹3,00,000, but students are not required to pay anything during their learning period. The fee payment begins only after a candidate successfully completes the course and secures a job with an annual salary package of ₹5 lakhs or more. Once employed, the fee of ₹3,00,000 can be paid over a period of three years.

This model is designed to make quality education accessible by removing the upfront financial burden, allowing students to focus on their learning and career development.

## How to Apply

Details on the application process, including specific deadlines and required documents, would typically be available on the official Masai School admissions portal. Interested candidates should visit the official source to understand the step-by-step application procedure and any entrance evaluations.

This part-time program offers a flexible and career-focused pathway for individuals looking to build a strong foundation in full-stack web development without immediate financial commitment.
