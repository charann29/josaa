# Introduction to Structural Analysis using ANSYS Workbench by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For engineering students and professionals looking to master advanced structural analysis, this course provides a comprehensive introduction to Finite Element Analysis (FEA) using the powerful ANSYS Workbench software. It's designed to equip learners with practical skills for performing simulations.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is delivered online over 8 weeks, featuring video lectures and text-based materials.
*   Learners will gain proficiency in ANSYS Workbench, including its Mechanical APDL solver.
*   Upon completion, all participants receive a certificate, with a merit certificate awarded to the top 5%.
*   Flexible fee options are available, including EMI plans, and extensive support is provided.

## Course Overview

This introductory program focuses on the fundamentals of advanced structural analysis and Finite Element Analysis (FEA). Through its curriculum, participants will learn to effectively use ANSYS Workbench, a leading software tool in the field. The training specifically covers how to conduct simulations using ANSYS's Mechanical APDL solver.

The course content includes an overview of FEA basics, an introduction to ANSYS Workbench, and detailed modules on engineering data, ANSYS SpaceClaim, and the ANSYS mechanical modeller. Practical application is emphasized through topics like static structural analysis, transient structural analysis, and explicit dynamics.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for a range of individuals in the engineering domain:

*   **Students:** Those currently pursuing a B.E. or B.Tech degree in Mechanical Engineering, Automotive Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, or Metallurgical and Materials Engineering.
*   **Working Professionals:** Engineers in mechanical, materials, automotive, or aerospace fields, particularly those working in quality, production, or manufacturing, who wish to enhance their skills and career prospects.
*   **Freshers:** Recent graduates looking to build a strong foundation in structural analysis and FEA.

To be eligible, candidates should either be enrolled in or hold a B.E. or B.Tech degree in one of the specified engineering disciplines.

## Learning Experience and Support

The course offers a rich learning environment with a blend of self-study materials, including video lectures and text. To ensure a thorough understanding and practical application, the program incorporates weekly assignments and industry-oriented technical projects. Participants will also benefit from a personalized project portfolio page to showcase their work.

Comprehensive support mechanisms are in place, including:

*   One-on-one and group Zoom support sessions.
*   Email and telephone support.
*   A dedicated course-specific forum group for discussions.
*   Personalized hands-on support from expert engineers.
*   A one-on-one demo session and course counseling are also available.

## Fees and Certification

The program offers three distinct fee structures, each leading to a valid certificate of completion. All payment options are available for a duration of three months. Skill Lync is the authority providing the certificates.

| Description                                                    | Amount in INR (per month for 3 months) |

| Introduction to Structural Analysis using ANSYS Workbench - Basic  | Rs. 7,000                              |
| Introduction to Structural Analysis using ANSYS Workbench - Pro    | Rs. 10,000                             |
| Introduction to Structural Analysis using ANSYS Workbench - Premium | Rs. 15,000                             |

For added convenience, 0% EMI options are also available for fee payments. While all participants receive a certificate of completion, the top 5% of learners will be awarded a special merit certificate, recognizing their exceptional performance.

This course offers a valuable opportunity to develop critical skills in structural analysis, making you more competitive in the engineering job market.
