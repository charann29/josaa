# HPCET Exam 2026: Date (Out), Registration, Syllabus, Eligibility, Pattern, Admission Process

The Himachal Pradesh Common Entrance Test (HPCET) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to pursue various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in technical and professional institutions across Himachal Pradesh. Conducted by the Himachal Pradesh Technical University (HPTU), Hamirpur, this exam opens doors to a wide range of courses.

## Key takeaways

*   The application form for HPCET 2026 is currently available on the official HPTU website, himtu.ac.in.
*   The application process involves online registration, filling in personal and academic details, uploading necessary documents, and paying the application fee.
*   Eligibility criteria must be met before applying, including academic qualifications and age limits.
*   After fee payment, a confirmation page is generated, which needs to be submitted as per the instructions provided by the university.

## HPCET 2026 Application Process

Applying for HPCET 2026 is a straightforward online process designed to be user-friendly. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you through:

### Step 1: Online Registration

Begin by visiting the official HPTU website. You'll need to provide basic information such as your name, date of birth, a valid email address, and a mobile number. After entering these details, you will create a unique password. Upon successful registration, a distinct form number will be sent to your registered email ID. This number, along with your created password and username, will be essential for all future logins.

### Step 2: Filling in Application Details

Once registered, log in to the portal using your username, password, and the application number received. Here, you will meticulously fill in your personal information, academic history, and contact details. Ensure all information is accurate to avoid any discrepancies later.

### Step 3: Document Upload

After completing the details, the next step involves uploading scanned images of your photograph and signature. It is vital to adhere to the specific file size and format guidelines provided by the authorities on their portal. Incorrect uploads can lead to application rejection.

### Step 4: Review and Edit

The system allows you to review all the information you have entered. This is your opportunity to make any necessary corrections or edits before proceeding to the final steps. Double-check everything, especially academic percentages and personal details.

### Step 5: Application Fee Payment

Payment of the application fee is the next crucial step. The fee structure for HPCET 2026 is as follows:

| Category                 | Application Fee |
| :----------------------- | :-------------- |
| SC/ST/BPL Candidates     | INR 800         |
| Other Candidates         | INR 1600        |

You can pay the fee through online modes using a credit card or debit card. An offline payment option is also available via a PNB Challan.

### Step 6: Confirmation and Submission

Upon successful payment, a confirmation page in PDF format will be generated. This PDF serves as proof of your application. You must either email this PDF to himtuadmission@gmail.com or submit a physical copy personally at the office of the "Controller of Examinations, Himachal Pradesh Technical University, Hamirpur (H.P.)-177001" within one week of its generation. Ensure you keep a copy for your records.

## HPCET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for HPCET 2026, candidates must meet specific eligibility requirements. These typically include academic qualifications such as passing 10+2 with a minimum percentage in relevant subjects for undergraduate courses or a bachelor's degree for postgraduate programs. Detailed eligibility criteria for each course are available on the official HPTU website.

## HPCET 2026 Syllabus and Exam Pattern

The HPCET syllabus generally covers subjects relevant to the chosen course, often including Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics for engineering programs. The exam pattern typically involves multiple-choice questions. It is highly recommended to refer to the official HPTU website for the precise syllabus and exam pattern for 2026, as these can be updated periodically.

## HPCET 2026 Important Dates

While the application form is out, other key dates such as the last date for application, admit card release, exam date, and result declaration will be announced by HPTU. Keep an eye on the official HPTU portal for these important updates to ensure you don't miss any deadlines.

Completing your HPCET application accurately and on time is the first step towards securing your desired admission.
