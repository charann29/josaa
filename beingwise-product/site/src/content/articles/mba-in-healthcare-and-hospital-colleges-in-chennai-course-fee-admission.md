Chennai offers excellent opportunities for those looking to pursue an MBA in Healthcare and Hospital Management. This specialized postgraduate program prepares individuals for leadership roles within the dynamic healthcare sector.

## Key takeaways

*   There are 13 colleges in Chennai offering MBA programs in Healthcare and Hospital Management.
*   A significant majority, 11 of these institutions, are privately owned.
*   Common entrance exams include MAT, XAT, CAT, and CMAT.
*   Tuition fees for these programs generally range from INR 1 lakh to over INR 5 lakh, depending on the institution.

## Eligibility Criteria for MBA in Healthcare & Hospital Management

To be considered for admission to an MBA in Healthcare and Hospital Management program in Chennai, candidates typically need to meet specific academic requirements. The primary criterion is holding a bachelor's degree from a recognized university in any discipline. Most institutions require a minimum aggregate score, often around 50%, in the undergraduate degree.

Beyond academic qualifications, applicants must also clear one of the accepted management entrance examinations. Performance in these exams plays a crucial role in the selection process.

## Popular Entrance Exams Accepted

Admission to MBA programs in Healthcare and Hospital Management in Chennai is primarily based on scores from national-level management entrance exams. Some of the widely accepted examinations include:

*   **CAT (Common Admission Test):** Conducted by the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs).
*   **MAT (Management Aptitude Test):** Administered by the All India Management Association (AIMA).
*   **XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test):** Conducted by XLRI Jamshedpur.
*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test):** Conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

Some colleges might also consider scores from other state-level or university-specific entrance tests. It's always advisable to check the specific requirements of each institution you are interested in.
