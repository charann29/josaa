# Rajasthan JET 2025: Final Counselling for UG, Result Link for JET and Pre PG & More

The Rajasthan Joint Entrance Test (JET) is a key examination for students aspiring to pursue undergraduate courses in agriculture and allied sciences within Rajasthan. This page outlines important updates regarding the JET 2025 counselling process, result announcements, and other essential details.

## Key takeaways

*   The final round of online option form filling for UG counselling concluded as per official notifications.
*   The deadline for paying the seat acceptance fee and confirming admission for the final counselling round was December 20, 2025.
*   Campus reporting for the final round was scheduled for December 30, 2025.
*   The provisional allotment list for the final counselling round was released on December 24, 2025.

## Understanding the Rajasthan JET Exam

The Rajasthan JET is an entrance examination designed for students seeking admission to various undergraduate programs offered by agricultural universities across Rajasthan. These programs include Agriculture, Horticulture, Home Science, and Fisheries Science. Qualifying this exam opens doors to esteemed institutions like Maharana Pratap University of Agriculture and Technology (MPUAT) in Udaipur, Swami Keshwanand Rajasthan Agricultural University (SKRAU) in Bikaner, and Rajasthan University of Veterinary and Animal Sciences (RAJUVAS), among others. The examination is conducted in an offline mode.

## Important Dates and Counselling Schedule

Staying updated with the official schedule is crucial for all candidates. Here are some key dates related to the Rajasthan JET 2025 counselling and results:

*   **Rajasthan JET 2025 Result:** Announced on July 30, 2025, on the official portal for JET and Pre PG.
*   **First Provisional Allotment List (UG Counselling):** Released on December 1, 2025.
*   **Last Date for First Provisional List Seat Acceptance Fee:** December 7, 2025 (until midnight).
*   **Final Round Provisional Allotment List:** Declared on December 24, 2025.
*   **Final Round Seat Acceptance Fee & Admission Confirmation Deadline:** December 20, 2025.
*   **Final Round Campus Reporting:** Scheduled for December 30, 2025.

Candidates are strongly advised to regularly check the official portal for any further updates or changes to the schedule.
