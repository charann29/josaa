# PIM MAT 2026: Registration, Exam Dates, Eligibility, Syllabus, Pattern, Results

The R.A. Podar Institute of Management Aptitude Test (PIM MAT) is an annual entrance examination conducted by the R.A. Podar Institute of Management in Rajasthan. This test is essential for students aspiring to join the institute's various Master of Business Administration (MBA) programs.

## Key takeaways

*   PIM MAT 2026 registration is expected to begin in the last week of April 2026.
*   The exam will be conducted offline and is tentatively scheduled for the second week of June 2026.
*   Candidates need a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks (45% for reserved categories) to be eligible.
*   The application fee for PIM MAT 2026 is INR 1,500.

## PIM MAT 2026 Overview

PIM MAT is a university-level aptitude test designed to assess candidates for admission to the R.A. Podar Institute of Management. The institute offers several MBA specializations, including a general MBA, MBA in Services Management, and an MBA-Executive Course. The test is held once a year and is a crucial step for aspiring management students.

## Eligibility Criteria and Application Process

To be considered for PIM MAT 2026, candidates must meet specific eligibility requirements laid out by the R.A. Podar Institute of Management. The application process is entirely online.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants must hold a graduate degree in any discipline from a recognized university or an equivalent qualification. A minimum aggregate score of 50% is required for general category candidates. For SC/ST/OBC/SBC categories, the minimum aggregate is 45%.
*   **Final Year Students:** Students in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply. However, they must provide proof of their graduation at the time of document verification.
*   **Reservation Policy:** Seat reservations will be applied as per the prevalent statutory provisions of the Government of Rajasthan. This includes reservations for SC, ST, OBC (Non-Creamy Layer), and SBC categories, among others, as per government norms.

### How to Apply for PIM MAT 2026

The application window for PIM MAT 2026 is expected to open in the last week of April 2026. Interested candidates can register online by following these steps:

1.  Visit the official University of Rajasthan's admissions portal (admissions.univraj.org).
2.  Look for and click on the 'Application form for PIM MAT 2026' link.
3.  You will be directed to the registration page. Create your login credentials.
4.  Complete the application form with all required personal and academic details.
5.  Pay the application fee of INR 1,500 online through the available payment gateways.

## PIM MAT Exam Pattern and Syllabus

Understanding the exam pattern and syllabus is crucial for effective preparation. The PIM MAT is a two-hour offline examination conducted in English.

### Exam Pattern

The exam comprises multiple-choice questions (MCQs) covering various aptitude areas. While the exact number of questions per section may vary, the overall structure remains consistent.

### Syllabus

The PIM MAT syllabus is designed to assess a candidate's aptitude across key areas relevant to management studies. The main sections include:

*   **Business Mathematics:** This section tests quantitative abilities, including topics like arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and data sufficiency.
*   **Verbal Ability:** This assesses English language proficiency, covering areas such as reading comprehension, grammar, vocabulary, and sentence correction.
*   **Current Affairs:** Questions in this section will gauge your awareness of recent national and international events, business news, and general knowledge.
*   **Logical Reasoning:** This part evaluates problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and the ability to deduce conclusions from given information.
*   **Data Interpretation:** Candidates will be tested on their ability to analyze and interpret data presented in various formats like tables, charts, and graphs.

Thorough preparation across these sections is recommended to perform well in the PIM MAT.

For more detailed information and updates, always refer to the official University of Rajasthan admissions portal.
