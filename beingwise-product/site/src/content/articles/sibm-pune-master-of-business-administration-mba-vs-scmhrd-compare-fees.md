# SIBM Pune Master of Business Administration (MBA) Vs SCMHRD: Compare Fees, Placement, Reviews, Cutoffs

Choosing the right MBA program is a pivotal decision for your career. This guide offers a comprehensive comparison between two prominent Symbiosis institutes in Pune: Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM Pune) and Symbiosis Centre for Management & Human Resource Development (SCMHRD), focusing on their flagship MBA programs.

## Key takeaways

*   SIBM Pune, established earlier, generally holds a higher overall reputation and typically secures better rankings.
*   Both institutes offer full-time, two-year MBA degrees, but their specific course offerings and specializations might vary.
*   SIBM Pune consistently reports higher median and highest placement salaries compared to SCMHRD.
*   Student reviews indicate SIBM Pune often scores higher in placements, infrastructure, and campus life, while SCMHRD is competitive in faculty and value for money.

## Institute Overview

SIBM Pune, founded in 1978, is a well-established institution known for its strong general management and specialization-focused MBA programs. SCMHRD, established in 1993, has carved a niche, particularly in Human Resources, though it also offers other management specializations.

Both are private institutions under the Symbiosis International (Deemed University) umbrella, offering a two-year, full-time, classroom-based Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree.

## Rankings and Reputation

When it comes to national recognition, SIBM Pune generally holds a more prominent position. According to recent NIRF rankings, SIBM Pune has consistently been placed higher than SCMHRD. For instance, SIBM Pune secured the 11th spot in 2025 and 12th in 2024, while SCMHRD was ranked 13th in 2024. These rankings reflect aspects like teaching, learning resources, research, and professional practice.

## Student Reviews and Ratings

Student feedback offers valuable insights into the on-campus experience. Based on verified student reviews:

*   **SIBM Pune:** Achieved an overall rating of 4.4 out of 5 from 71 reviews. It scored particularly high in Placements (4.5), Infrastructure (4.6), and Crowd & Campus (4.7). Faculty received a 4.0, and Value for Money was rated 4.2.
*   **SCMHRD:** Received an overall rating of 4.1 out of 5 from 40 reviews. Its strengths were in Placements (4.1), Faculty (4.0), and Crowd & Campus (4.4). Infrastructure was rated 3.7, and Value for Money 4.3.

These ratings suggest SIBM Pune tends to offer a slightly more robust experience across multiple parameters, though SCMHRD's faculty and value for money are well-regarded.

## Admission Cutoffs

Both SIBM Pune and SCMHRD admit students primarily through the Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP). While specific cutoff percentages can vary year to year based on the number of applicants and exam difficulty, SIBM Pune generally has a higher SNAP cutoff due to its higher ranking and demand. Candidates aspiring for SIBM Pune typically need to aim for a higher percentile in the SNAP exam compared to those targeting SCMHRD.

Ultimately, both SIBM Pune and SCMHRD offer quality MBA programs within the Symbiosis framework. Your choice should align with your career aspirations, preferred specializations, and the kind of campus environment you seek.
