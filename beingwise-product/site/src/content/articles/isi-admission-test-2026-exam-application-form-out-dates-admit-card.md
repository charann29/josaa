# ISI Admission Test 2026 Exam - Application Form (Out), Dates, Admit Card, Syllabus, Pattern, Question Papers, Cutoff

The Indian Statistical Institute (ISI) conducts its annual admission test for various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. This comprehensive guide will help you understand the key aspects of the ISI Admission Test 2026, from application to results.

## Key takeaways

*   The ISI Admission Test is your gateway to diverse programs in Statistics, Mathematics, and Computer Science.
*   Application forms for most programs closed on March 28, 2026, but BSDS applications begin on April 28, 2026.
*   The main ISI 2026 examination is scheduled for May 10, 2026.
*   M.Tech interviews for eligible candidates are set to commence from April 17, 2026.

## Application Process and Fees

Prospective students need to submit their applications online through the official ISI admissions portal. It's important to note the application fees, which vary based on your category:

*   **General Category:** ₹1500
*   **Female Candidates (Unreserved Category):** ₹1000
*   **SC/ST/OBC (NCL)/PwD/EWS Candidates:** ₹750

Ensure you complete your application and pay the fee before the respective deadlines. For the BSDS program, applications will open later, on April 28, 2026.

## Understanding the ISI Admission Test and M.Tech Admissions

The ISI Admission Test is a competitive examination for entry into various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs offered by the Indian Statistical Institute. These programs span fields like Statistics, Mathematics, and Computer Science.

For M.Tech courses, ISI also considers GATE scores for admission to specific programs. The M.Tech offerings include:

*   M.Tech Computer Science
*   M.Tech Cryptology and Security
*   M.Tech Quality, Reliability & Operations Research

If you have applied for M.Tech programs, the interview schedule has been released on the official admissions website. Interviews are scheduled to begin from April 17, 2026, and you can find your specific date, time, and centre address there.

## Preparing for the ISI Admission Test

To perform well in the ISI Admission Test, it's essential to thoroughly understand the exam pattern and syllabus for your chosen program. This will help you create an effective study plan and focus your preparation. While specific details for 2026 will be available on the official ISI admissions portal, reviewing previous years' question papers can also provide valuable insights into the exam's structure and question types.

Stay updated with all official announcements and prepare diligently for your ISI Admission Test 2026.
