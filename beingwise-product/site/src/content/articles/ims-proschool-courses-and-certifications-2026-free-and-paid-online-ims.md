IMS Proschool offers a variety of programs designed to equip students and professionals with essential skills for the finance and accounting sectors. These courses range from foundational certifications to advanced professional qualifications, catering to different career aspirations within the industry.

## Key Takeaways

*   IMS Proschool provides a range of certifications in financial planning, investment banking, and accounting.
*   Programs offer flexible learning options, including classroom, live online, and self-paced formats.
*   Many courses are designed to prepare students for globally recognized professional exams.
*   Some programs include placement assistance to help graduates enter the workforce.

## CFPCM Certification Training Program

The CFPCM Certification Training Program is a leading qualification in financial planning, a field known for its excellent career opportunities. This program is structured to prepare participants for the CFPCM examination, along with other related financial planning certifications.

IMS Proschool, as an approved education provider, offers this program through several convenient study modes. Students can choose from a traditional classroom setting, which includes weekend batches for flexibility, a live virtual classroom experience, or a self-paced learning format.

The curriculum culminates in a computer-based examination featuring multiple-choice questions and case studies. Successful completion of this exam allows candidates to apply for the official CFPCM certification.

*   **Duration:** 15 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹60,000
*   **Key Skills:** Financial Management

## ACCA Course: Skill Level

The ACCA Course: Skill Level offered by IMS Proschool is an advanced program that delves into a wide array of business and accounting principles. It aims to develop crucial skills required in the modern financial landscape.

This course is taught by educators qualified by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) and provides two primary study options: an in-person classroom program or live online training conducted on the CISCO platform. The online mode ensures an interactive learning experience.

Throughout the training, students engage with recorded video lectures, assignments, mock tests, and live classes. Regular faculty feedback is also provided to help students improve their performance. The syllabus is structured into six distinct modules, covering topics such as Financial Management, Audit and Assurance, Taxation, and Financial Reporting. Completing specific criteria within this program also awards an advanced diploma in accounting and business.

*   **Duration:** 15 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹1,25,000
*   **Key Skills:** Decision-making, Financial Management, Taxation Knowledge

## Certificate in Financial Planning

For individuals looking to start a career in finance, particularly in investment advisory and management, the Certificate in Financial Planning is an ideal starting point. This program is based on the CFP-FBSP curriculum and covers the fundamentals of financial planning, from basic concepts to advanced strategies for wealth generation and risk management.

This course is suitable for undergraduates, graduates, and working professionals. It prepares participants for other industry benchmark certificates in financial planning. High-quality video sessions deliver the core content, and IMS Proschool also offers placement support to help graduates secure employment opportunities.

*   **Duration:** 15 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹50,000
*   **Key Skills:** Knowledge of Law, Financial Management

## Investment Banking Course with PG Certificate

The Post Graduate Program (PGP) in Investment Banking & Capital Markets is a project-based learning experience designed to impart the practical skills and knowledge essential for a professional in investment finance. This program offers a real-world perspective on investment banking operations.

IMS Proschool's offerings provide clear pathways to professional growth in the financial sector, equipping learners with relevant skills for today's competitive job market.
