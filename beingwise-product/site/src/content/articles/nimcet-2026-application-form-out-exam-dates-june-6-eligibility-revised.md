# NIMCET 2026: Application Form (OUT), Exam Dates (June 6), Eligibility, Revised Syllabus, Exam Pattern

For students aspiring to pursue a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) degree from some of India's top technical institutions, the NIMCET 2026 exam is your gateway. This national-level entrance test is crucial for securing a seat in participating NITs and IIITs.

## Key takeaways

*   **Applications are open:** The NIMCET 2026 application window is currently active, with the deadline for submission set for May 1, 2026.
*   **Exam Date:** The computer-based test (CBT) for NIMCET 2026 will be held on June 6, 2026, from 2 PM to 4 PM.
*   **Participating Institutes:** Admission is offered to 13 prestigious institutions, including 11 National Institutes of Technology (NITs) and 2 Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs).
*   **Admission Process:** Selection is based solely on your NIMCET rank, followed by a centralized online counselling procedure.

## What is NIMCET?

The National Institute of Technology Master of Computer Applications Common Entrance Test (NIMCET) is an annual examination designed to assess candidates for admission to MCA programs. It's a highly competitive exam, with approximately 25,000 students appearing each year for around 1,285 available seats across the participating institutes.

**Participating Institutions:**

The 11 NITs include Agartala, Allahabad, Bhopal, Delhi, Jamshedpur, Kurukshetra, Meghalaya, Patna, Raipur, and Tiruchirappalli (Trichy). Additionally, two IIITs, Bhopal and Vadodara, also accept NIMCET scores.

For the 2026 session, the National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli will be the conducting body for the NIMCET exam.

## NIMCET 2026 Application Form

The application process for NIMCET 2026 is entirely online. Interested candidates must register and log in on the official NIMCET admissions portal to fill out the application form. It's essential to complete all sections accurately and submit the required fees before the deadline.

**Application Fees:**

*   **General/OBC candidates:** INR 2,500
*   **SC/ST/PwD candidates:** INR 1,250

The application window closes on May 1, 2026, so ensure you complete your registration well in advance.

## NIMCET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for NIMCET 2026, candidates generally need to have a Bachelor's degree of at least three years duration in any discipline with Mathematics or Statistics as one of the subjects at the undergraduate level. A minimum aggregate percentage is also usually required, as specified in the official NIMCET information brochure. Please refer to the official NIMCET website for the precise and most current eligibility requirements.

## NIMCET 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The NIMCET 2026 examination is a computer-based test (CBT) comprising 120 multiple-choice questions. The total marks for the exam are 1000. It's important to note that there is negative marking for incorrect answers and sectional timings are enforced.

**The syllabus covers four key areas:**

*   **Mathematics:** This section typically carries the highest weightage and includes topics like Set Theory, Probability and Statistics, Algebra, Coordinate Geometry, Calculus, and Vector Algebra.
*   **Logical Reasoning:** This section assesses your analytical and problem-solving abilities.
*   **Computer Awareness:** Questions here cover basic computer fundamentals, organization, data representation, and computer architecture.
*   **General English:** This section evaluates your comprehension, vocabulary, and grammar skills.

The detailed and updated syllabus for the 2026 session has been released alongside the official NIMCET notification. Candidates are strongly advised to consult the official syllabus to prepare effectively.

Preparing thoroughly for NIMCET 2026, understanding the exam pattern, and adhering to the application deadlines will significantly enhance your chances of securing admission to your desired MCA program.
