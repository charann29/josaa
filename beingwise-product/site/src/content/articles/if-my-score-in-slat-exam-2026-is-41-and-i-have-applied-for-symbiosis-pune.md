# If my score in SLAT Exam 2026 is 41 and I have applied for Symbiosis Pune and Noida, do I stand a chance for a Personal Interview?

Understanding your chances for a Personal Interview (PI) at Symbiosis Law Schools (SLS) in Pune and Noida with a SLAT score of 41 requires looking at past trends and the overall admission process.

## Key Takeaways

*   A score of 45-50 is generally considered strong for SLS Pune. Your score of 41 is close but might be on the lower side for a guaranteed call.
*   SLS Noida's cut-offs are often slightly lower than SLS Pune, potentially increasing your chances there.
*   Shortlisting for the PI round is not solely based on the SLAT score; it's a competitive process that varies each year.
*   Even if shortlisted, a strong performance in the PI and Written Ability Test (WAT) is crucial for final admission.

## Understanding SLAT Scores and Shortlisting

The Symbiosis Law Admission Test (SLAT) is the initial hurdle for aspiring law students seeking admission to Symbiosis International (Deemed University)'s law programs. While a SLAT score is a significant factor, it's important to remember that it's just one part of the multi-stage selection process. The university aims to identify well-rounded candidates, not just those with high test scores.

Historically, for SLS Pune, a score in the range of 45 to 50 marks has been considered quite competitive, often leading to a Personal Interview (PI) shortlisting. With a score of 41, you are not far from this range, which means there's a possibility, though not a certainty, of receiving a call. Admissions are highly competitive, and the exact cut-off can fluctuate based on the number of applicants, their overall performance, and the availability of seats.

## Comparing Chances for SLS Pune vs. SLS Noida

When you've applied to both SLS Pune and SLS Noida, it's helpful to consider their individual selection dynamics. SLS Pune is often the most sought-after campus, and consequently, its cut-offs tend to be higher. This means that a score of 41 might place you in a more challenging position for SLS Pune compared to SLS Noida.

SLS Noida, while also very reputable, sometimes has slightly more accommodating cut-offs due to varying applicant preferences and seat matrix. Therefore, your chances of receiving a PI call from SLS Noida with a score of 41 could be higher than for SLS Pune. It's not uncommon for candidates to get shortlisted for one campus but not the other with similar scores.

## The Role of PI and WAT in Admissions

Even if you are shortlisted for the Personal Interview (PI) and Written Ability Test (WAT) rounds, your SLAT score alone won't secure your admission. These subsequent stages are critical. The WAT assesses your writing skills, logical reasoning, and ability to articulate thoughts concisely. The PI evaluates your communication skills, general awareness, personality, and suitability for a legal career.

Performing exceptionally well in both the WAT and PI can significantly boost your overall prospects, even if your SLAT score is on the borderline. Candidates who demonstrate strong interpersonal skills, clarity of thought, and genuine interest in law during these rounds often gain an advantage.

## What to Do Next

If you're awaiting SLAT 2026 results or have received your score, it's wise to prepare thoroughly for the PI and WAT. Focus on current affairs, legal aptitude, and practice expressing your thoughts clearly and confidently. Keep an eye on the official Symbiosis International (Deemed University) admissions portal for specific shortlisting announcements and further instructions.

While a score of 41 for SLS Pune might be borderline, your application to SLS Noida could offer a stronger opportunity for a PI call. Remain optimistic and prepare diligently for the next stages.
