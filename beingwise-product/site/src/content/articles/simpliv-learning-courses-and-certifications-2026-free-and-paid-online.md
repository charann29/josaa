# Simpliv Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Simpliv Learning Courses

Simpliv Learning offers a diverse range of online courses and certifications designed to help individuals acquire new skills or enhance existing ones. These programs cover various in-demand fields, catering to both beginners and experienced professionals.

## Key takeaways

*   Simpliv Learning provides both live virtual training and self-paced online courses.
*   Programs are available across popular domains like Data Science, Machine Learning, AI, and Software Development.
*   Many courses offer lifetime access to learning materials, allowing for flexible study.
*   Certificates are awarded upon successful completion, which can boost career prospects.

## Exploring Simpliv Learning's Course Offerings

Simpliv Learning's portfolio includes a mix of short, intensive live training sessions and more flexible, self-paced online courses. The content is generally geared towards practical application, aiming to equip learners with job-ready skills. Course durations vary significantly, from a few days for live sessions to longer periods for comprehensive self-paced programs.

## Popular Course Categories and Examples

Simpliv Learning focuses on several key areas that are currently experiencing high demand in the job market. Here's a look at some prominent categories and specific course examples:

### Data Science and Machine Learning

This category is particularly strong, with courses like "Data Science With Machine Learning And Data Analytics." This program explores data science using Python, R, SQL, and the WEKA toolkit, covering topics from data collection to predictive modeling. Another option is "Machine Learning Using R And Python," which delves into machine learning techniques using these popular programming languages.

### Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning

For those interested in cutting-edge technologies, Simpliv offers programs such as "3 Days Live Virtual Training on AI and Deep Learning with Python." These courses aim to provide a foundational understanding and practical skills in artificial intelligence and deep learning concepts.

### Software Development and Engineering Tools

Simpliv Learning also provides training in specific software and development environments. Examples include "MATLab For Engineering And Computational Sciences" and "5 Day Live Training on AutoCAD 2D Beginner to Advanced." For mobile development enthusiasts, "IOS 10 & Swift 3: From Beginner To Paid Professional™" offers an intensive bootcamp on iOS development, covering iOS 10 and Swift 3.

### Project Management and Quality Assurance

Courses like "4 Days Live Training on Six Sigma Black Belt" cater to professionals looking to enhance their project management and quality improvement skills, which are valuable across various industries.

## Course Structure and Learning Experience

Most Simpliv Learning courses are delivered online. Live virtual training sessions are interactive and typically run for a set number of days, often with a fixed schedule. Self-paced courses, on the other hand, provide recorded video lectures and materials that learners can access anytime after a one-time payment, often granting lifetime access. This flexibility allows individuals to learn at their own pace and revisit content as needed.

## Who Can Benefit?

Simpliv Learning courses generally target graduates, working professionals, and anyone looking to upskill or reskill in a particular domain. Some courses may have prerequisites, such as basic programming knowledge in R or Python for data science programs, or a computer with OSX/macOS for iOS development courses. It's always advisable to check the specific requirements for each course before enrolling.

## Fees and Certification

Course fees vary depending on the program's duration, format (live vs. self-paced), and content complexity. Prices are typically listed in US dollars. Upon successful completion of a course, participants receive a certificate from Simpliv Learning, which can be a valuable addition to a professional resume.

Simpliv Learning provides a practical pathway for individuals to gain specialized knowledge and certifications in rapidly evolving technological fields.
