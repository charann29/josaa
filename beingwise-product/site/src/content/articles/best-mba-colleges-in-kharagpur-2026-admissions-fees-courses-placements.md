## Admission Process and Entrance Exams

Admission to MBA/PGDM programs in Kharagpur's top colleges is highly competitive and primarily based on national-level entrance examination scores. Aspiring candidates must prepare thoroughly for these exams to meet the eligibility criteria.

Commonly accepted entrance exams include:

*   **CAT (Common Admission Test)**: Conducted by the IIMs, CAT is the most widely accepted entrance exam for MBA programs across India.
*   **XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)**: Accepted by XLRI and many other top B-schools.
*   **GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)**: An internationally recognized exam, also accepted by some Indian institutions.
*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)**: Conducted by NTA, CMAT scores are accepted by numerous AICTE-approved institutions.

After clearing the entrance exam, candidates typically participate in subsequent admission rounds, which may include Group Discussions (GD), Written Ability Tests (WAT), and Personal Interviews (PI).
