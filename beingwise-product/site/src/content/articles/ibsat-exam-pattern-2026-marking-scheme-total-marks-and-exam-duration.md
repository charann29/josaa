# IBSAT Exam Pattern 2026: Marking Scheme, Total Marks & Exam Duration

Understanding the IBSAT exam structure is crucial for anyone planning to pursue an MBA at ICFAI Business School. This guide will walk you through the key aspects of the IBSAT 2026 test, including its format, duration, and scoring system.

## Key Takeaways

*   IBSAT 2026 will be conducted as a home-proctored online exam, allowing you to take it from a convenient location.
*   The test has a total duration of 120 minutes and features 140 multiple-choice questions (MCQs).
*   The exam is divided into four main sections, covering Verbal Ability, Reading Comprehension, Quantitative Aptitude, and Data Adequacy & Data Interpretation.
*   Each correct answer earns one mark, and importantly, there is no negative marking for incorrect responses.

## IBSAT 2026 Exam Structure at a Glance

The IBSAT is designed to assess your aptitude across various skills essential for management studies. It is similar in structure to many other MBA entrance exams, focusing on analytical and verbal abilities. The exam is conducted entirely in English.

Here's a quick overview of the IBSAT 2026 exam pattern:

| Exam Aspect          | Details                                    |
| :------------------- | :----------------------------------------- |
| Exam Mode            | Home-Proctored Test (Online)               |
| Duration             | 120 minutes                                |
| Number of Questions  | 140 MCQs                                   |
| Total Marks          | 140 marks                                  |
| Language             | English                                    |
| Sections             | 4 (Verbal Ability, Reading Comprehension, Quantitative Aptitude, Data Adequacy & Data Interpretation) |
| Marking Scheme       | +1 for each correct answer, No negative marking |

## Section-wise Breakdown of IBSAT 2026

The 140 questions in IBSAT 2026 are distributed across four distinct sections. Each section targets specific skills, and understanding their weightage can help you plan your preparation effectively.

| Sections                                    | Number of Questions |
| :------------------------------------------ | :------------------ |
| Verbal Ability (VA)                         | 50                  |
| Reading Comprehension (RC)                  | 30                  |
| Quantitative Aptitude (QA)                  | 30                  |
| Data Adequacy/Sufficiency & Data Interpretation (DA/DI) | 30                  |
| **Total**                                   | **140**             |

## Marking Scheme and Total Marks

The IBSAT 2026 exam carries a total of 140 marks. For every question answered correctly, you will be awarded one mark. A significant advantage of the IBSAT is the absence of negative marking. This means there is no penalty for incorrect answers, encouraging candidates to attempt all questions without fear of losing marks.

Preparing for IBSAT requires a clear understanding of these details to strategize your study plan effectively and maximize your score.
