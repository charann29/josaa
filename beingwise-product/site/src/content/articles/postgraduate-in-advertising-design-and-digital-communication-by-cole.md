# Postgraduate in Advertising, Design & Digital Communication by École Intuit Lab New Delhi: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

École Intuit Lab in New Delhi offers a comprehensive postgraduate program in Advertising, Design & Digital Communication, crafted for individuals aiming to excel in the dynamic creative industry. This two-year, full-time course provides a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing students for impactful roles in advertising, branding, and digital communication.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a two-year, full-time postgraduate program, taught entirely in English.
*   The curriculum focuses on developing skills in creating brand narratives, visual engagement, and innovative digital experiences.
*   Students will undertake two mandatory internships, participate in global workshops, and gain international exposure.
*   The program emphasizes a multidisciplinary approach, integrating creative strategy, design thinking, and digital communication.

## About the Program

The Postgraduate in Advertising, Design & Digital Communication course is designed to equip participants with the expertise needed to craft compelling brand stories, engage audiences visually, and design innovative experiences across both digital and physical platforms. The curriculum is continuously updated to reflect current industry trends, combining classroom learning with hands-on studio work and real-time client projects. A significant component of the program includes international exposure and a mandatory internship to ensure students gain professional insights and are well-prepared for global creative careers.

## What You Will Learn

Throughout the program, students will gain comprehensive expertise in designing effective advertising campaigns, digital communication strategies, and brand experiences. The practical training involves studio-based exercises, workshops, and client projects, directly addressing real-world challenges in the creative sector. The curriculum integrates aesthetics, technology, and storytelling to help students refine their ability to create visually compelling and strategically effective designs.

Key skills developed include:

*   Animation and storytelling abilities
*   Artistic and visual expression skills
*   Knowledge of UI/UX design principles
*   Creative writing and copywriting
*   Branding and marketing strategies
*   Computer graphics and web design

Beyond technical proficiencies, the program also cultivates essential professional skills such as teamwork, problem-solving, client engagement, and portfolio management. Exposure to international workshops and study trips enhances cross-cultural understanding, enabling students to adapt their creative approaches to diverse markets.

## Program Highlights and Offerings

The program stands out with several key features designed to provide a rich learning experience:

*   **Duration:** A two-year full-time commitment.
*   **Internships:** Participants complete two internships, gaining valuable professional experience.
*   **Global Exposure:** Opportunities for worldwide cultural immersion and participation in global workshops.
*   **Mentorship:** Learning from expert mentors and a faculty of practicing designers and industry specialists.
*   **Practical Learning:** Focus on live projects, studio practice, industry workshops, and technical labs.
*   **Portfolio Development:** Dedicated portfolio reviews to help students build a strong professional showcase.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

To be eligible for the Postgraduate in Advertising, Design & Digital Communication program, applicants must have successfully completed their graduation in any discipline. The admission process is straightforward:

1.  **Application:** Visit the official course page, click on "Apply Now," and complete the application form.
2.  **Document Submission:** Submit all necessary documents and pay the application fees.
3.  **Assessment:** Candidates will then undertake a creative aptitude test followed by an interview.

Upon successful completion of the program, a certificate is awarded by École Intuit Lab New Delhi.

## Who is This Program For?

This postgraduate course is ideal for individuals aspiring to high-impact roles in the creative industry. It is particularly well-suited for:

*   Aspiring Art Directors
*   Creative Strategists
*   Brand and Packaging Designers
*   Digital Communication Specialists
*   Animators
*   Graphic Designers

Graduates will be prepared to pursue careers across global advertising and design ecosystems, equipped with a curated portfolio, professional experience, and the strategic mindset essential for success in competitive creative fields.
