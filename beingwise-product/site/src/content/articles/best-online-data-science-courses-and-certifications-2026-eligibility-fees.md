# Best Online Data Science Courses & Certifications 2026 - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Scope

Data science is a rapidly expanding field, offering exciting career paths for those who understand how to extract insights from vast amounts of information. Online courses and certifications provide a flexible way to gain these essential skills, making higher education accessible to a wider range of students.

## Key Takeaways

*   Several Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) offer comprehensive online Bachelor of Science (B.S.) degrees in Data Science and related fields.
*   These programs often feature multiple exit points, allowing students to earn certificates or diplomas even if they don't complete the full degree.
*   Eligibility typically requires a Class 12 pass with English, and Class 10 with English and Mathematics.
*   Online data science degrees from IITs are generally 3-4 years long, with total fees ranging from approximately ₹2.21 lakhs to ₹4.78 lakhs.

## Online B.S. Programs from IIT Madras

IIT Madras is a prominent institution offering several online Bachelor of Science degrees focused on data science and its applications. These programs are designed to provide a strong foundation in the core principles of mathematics, statistics, and programming, along with their practical application to real-world challenges.

### Online B.S. Data Science and Applications

This four-year program (48 months) from IIT Madras aims to equip learners with robust skills in data science. It covers essential areas like machine learning, data visualization, and computational thinking. Students gain practical experience through hands-on projects and case studies. A key feature is its flexible structure, allowing students to exit at various stages—Foundation, Diploma, or the full B.S. degree. The total fee for this program is approximately ₹3,51,000.

### Online B.S. Management and Data Science

Another four-year offering (48 months) from IIT Madras, this degree combines management functional areas with deep expertise in analytics. It's ideal for students looking to understand how data science drives business decisions. Like its counterpart, it offers flexible exit options, including a Foundation Level Certificate, a Diploma in Data Analytics for Business, and the complete B.S. Degree. The curriculum is taught by experts from IIT Madras and other premier business schools. Applicants typically need to have passed Class 12 with English, and Class 10 with English and Mathematics, and must apply for a qualifier exam. The total fee for this program is around ₹3,52,000.

### Online B.Sc. Programming and Data Science

This three-year online undergraduate degree (36 months) from IIT Madras focuses on programming alongside data science. It is structured into three levels: Foundational, Diploma, and Degree. Students must complete all levels to earn the B.Sc. degree but have the flexibility to exit at any level, receiving a Foundational Certificate from the Centre for Continuing Education, IIT Madras, or a Diploma degree from IIT Madras. The approximate total fee for this program is ₹2,21,000.

## B.Sc. Applied AI and Data Science from IIT Jodhpur

IIT Jodhpur also offers a B.Sc. in Applied AI and Data Science. This four-year program (48 months) is designed to immerse students in the practical aspects of artificial intelligence and data science. The total fee for this course is approximately ₹4,78,000.

## Doctor of Business Administration in Data Science

For professionals seeking advanced research opportunities, Rennes School of Business, in partnership with INSOFE, offers a Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) in Data Science. This three-year, part-time online doctoral research program helps professionals enhance their business knowledge and data science skills, viewing business challenges through an AI lens. Students work with mentors, publish research, and develop patentable resources, with the research work and thesis defense typically completed on the Rennes School of Business campus.

Online data science programs offer a valuable pathway to a future-proof career, providing rigorous academic training with the convenience of remote learning.
