# Indian Ethnic Couture by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone passionate about India's rich textile heritage and intricate fashion, The Designer's Class offers an online course dedicated to Indian Ethnic Couture. This program is designed to guide aspiring designers and enthusiasts through the historical and contemporary aspects of traditional Indian attire, from its origins to modern-day creations.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn about the evolution of Indian ethnic styles and the creative processes of renowned couturiers.
*   Understand traditional weaving, textile techniques, and various Indian embroideries.
*   Gain insights into design processes, couture construction, and finishing techniques.
*   Benefit from a self-paced learning format with approximately 1 hour and 46 minutes of video lectures, assignments, and downloadable resources.

## Course Overview

This online certification course, developed by celebrated designers Abu Jani and Sandeep Khosla, explores the diverse styles, historical context, and manufacturing processes behind Indian ethnic couture. It aims to help students grasp the aesthetic inspirations of designers and translate these concepts into unique and beautiful creations. The program is ideal for individuals looking to deepen their knowledge of Indian ethnic culture, whether to start their own design line or simply to appreciate the craft.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum covers fundamental fashion design principles specific to Indian ethnic wear. You'll delve into the evolution of ancient Indian styles, get acquainted with prominent Indian ethnic couturiers, and explore traditional weaving techniques and textiles. The course also teaches various traditional embroideries, the complete design process, and essential couture finishes and construction methods. Upon completion, you will have a solid understanding of Indian ethnic culture and developed skills in traditional Indian crafts, including weaving, printing, and surface embroideries.

## Course Content and Structure

The program is delivered through video and text-based modules, allowing for self-paced learning. It includes approximately 1 hour and 46 minutes of video lectures, complemented by 14 assignments and 15 downloadable resources. Students will have access to the course material for one year.

### Syllabus Highlights:

*   Introduction to Indian ethnic couture
*   Modern evolution of old Indian styles
*   Modern Indian ethnic couturiers
*   Indian traditional weaving techniques and textiles
*   Indian traditional embroideries
*   Couture finishes – borders & trims
*   Design process (I, II, III)
*   Drafting patterns for Indian ethnic clothing
*   Couture construction – construction & finishes
*   Couture presentation

## Fees and Application

The fee for the Indian Ethnic Couture online course is ₹2,499. As an online, self-study program, there isn't a formal application process in the traditional sense; interested individuals can typically enroll directly through The Designer's Class platform. While the course provides valuable learning, it's important to note that a certificate of completion is not issued for this specific program.

## Instructors

The course is led by two titans of Indian fashion, Mr. Abu Jani and Mr. Sandeep Khosla, co-founders of the iconic brand Gulabo By Abu Sandeep. Their expertise and insights form the core of the learning experience.

This course offers a fantastic opportunity to explore the intricate world of Indian ethnic couture from the comfort of your home, guided by industry legends.
