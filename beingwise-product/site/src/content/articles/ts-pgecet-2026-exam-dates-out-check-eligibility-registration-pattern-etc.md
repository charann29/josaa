# TS PGECET 2026: Exam Dates OUT, Check Eligibility, Registration, Pattern, etc.

The Telangana State Post Graduate Engineering Common Entrance Test (TS PGECET) is a crucial state-level examination for students aspiring to pursue postgraduate courses in Engineering, Technology, Architecture, and Pharmacy within Telangana. Conducted by Jawaharlal Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH) on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TGCHE), this exam opens doors to various M.E., M.Tech., M.Pharm., and M.Arch programs.

## Key Takeaways

*   The TS PGECET 2026 application forms have been released, and registration is currently open on the official TS PGECET portal.
*   The last date to apply without a late fee is May 6, 2026; however, submissions with a late fee will be accepted until May 9, 2026.
*   The computer-based entrance exam is scheduled to take place from May 28 to May 31, 2026, across two daily shifts.
*   Candidates who have qualified in GATE or GPAT are exempt from appearing for the TS PGECET exam but must still apply through the designated process.

## TS PGECET 2026 Important Dates

Staying informed about key dates is essential for a smooth application process. Here's a quick overview of the TS PGECET 2026 schedule:

| Event                                      | Date                 |
| :----------------------------------------- | :------------------- |
| Application Form Submission (without late fee) | Feb 27, 2026 - May 6, 2026 |
| Application Form Submission (with INR 250 late fee) | Up to May 9, 2026    |
| TS PGECET 2026 Examination Dates           | May 28 - May 31, 2026|

## Eligibility Criteria for TS PGECET

To be eligible for TS PGECET 2026, candidates must meet specific academic qualifications. Generally, applicants should hold a bachelor's degree in Engineering, Technology, Pharmacy, or Architecture from a recognized university, with a minimum aggregate percentage as specified by the TGCHE. It is always advisable to refer to the detailed eligibility criteria on the official TS PGECET website before applying.

## Application Process for TS PGECET 2026

The application process for TS PGECET 2026 is entirely online. Interested candidates should visit the official TS PGECET portal to fill out the application form. The steps typically involve:

1.  **Registration:** Creating a new account with basic details.
2.  **Fee Payment:** Paying the application fee online. The fee is INR 1100 for General category candidates and INR 600 for SC/ST/PWD candidates.
3.  **Form Filling:** Completing the application form with personal, academic, and contact details.
4.  **Document Upload:** Uploading scanned copies of required documents, such as photographs and signatures.
5.  **Submission:** Reviewing and submitting the application form.

Candidates who have qualified GATE or GPAT also need to complete this application process, although they are generally exempted from the entrance exam itself. For precise instructions and required documents, always check the official TS PGECET website.

## TS PGECET 2026 Exam Pattern

The TS PGECET is a computer-based test (CBT) designed to assess a candidate's aptitude for postgraduate studies. Here’s a breakdown of the exam pattern:

*   **Mode of Exam:** Computer-Based Test (CBT)
*   **Duration:** 2 hours (120 minutes)
*   **Total Questions:** 120 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Marking Scheme:** One mark awarded for each correct answer. There is no negative marking for incorrect answers.
*   **Subjects:** The syllabus varies depending on the chosen postgraduate program (e.g., Civil Engineering, Computer Science, Pharmacy, etc.). The detailed syllabus for each discipline is available on the official TS PGECET portal.

## Counselling Process

Following the announcement of results, qualified candidates will be invited for the TS PGECET counselling process. This typically involves online registration, document verification, choice filling for colleges and courses, and seat allotment based on rank and availability. Candidates should keep all necessary academic and personal documents ready for verification during this stage.
