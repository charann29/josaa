# ICAS Certification Exam Dates 2025 for June & December Cycle

For students pursuing courses with the Indian Council for Astrological Sciences (ICAS), understanding the examination schedule is crucial. The ICAS certification exams are conducted twice annually, offering opportunities in both June and December.

## Key Takeaways

*   ICAS certification exams are held online, typically in June and December each year.
*   Results for the June 2025 session are expected by September 15, 2025.
*   Results for the December 2025 session are expected by March 15, 2026.
*   Students must register for each specific course's certification exam via the official ICAS Portal using their unique login details.

## Application Process for ICAS Exams 2025

ICAS typically opens registrations for its courses in January and June, with classes commencing in the first week of these respective months. When applying for a course, students are generally required to pay both the registration fee and the examination fee concurrently. For the actual exam registration, after completing their coursework, students should log into their dashboard on the official ICAS portal and complete the necessary steps there.

## Examination Mode and Admit Cards

Students have the flexibility to appear for the ICAS certification exams in either online or offline mode. For those opting for the offline mode, examinations will be conducted at designated centers corresponding to their chosen course on the specified exam date. Admit letters, which are essential for appearing in the exam, will be made available on the student portal for downloading.

## ICAS Exam Result Release 2025

The results for the ICAS certification exams are published online on the official ICAS portal. Students can access their results using their individual login credentials. For the June 2025 examination session, results are anticipated to be released by September 15, 2025. Similarly, for the December 2025 examination session, candidates can expect their results to be available by March 15, 2026.

Staying informed about these dates and procedures will help you navigate your ICAS certification journey smoothly.
