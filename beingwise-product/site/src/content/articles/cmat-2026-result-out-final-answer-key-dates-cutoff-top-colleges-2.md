# CMAT 2026: Result (OUT), Final Answer Key, Dates, Cutoff, Top Colleges

The Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is a crucial national-level entrance examination for aspiring MBA students across India. Conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), CMAT scores are accepted by numerous AICTE-approved institutions for admission to their management programs.

## Key takeaways

*   The CMAT 2026 exam took place on January 25, 2026, with results and the final answer key released on February 17, 2026.
*   Approximately 41,872 candidates appeared for the exam out of 53,453 applicants, seeking admission to over 1,300 MBA colleges.
*   The final answer key saw one revision to question number 97, confirming the accuracy of the remaining questions.
*   Top management institutes have now opened their admission windows, inviting applications based on CMAT 2026 scores.

## CMAT Exam Structure and Pattern

CMAT is a three-hour, computer-based test designed to assess a candidate's aptitude for management studies. The exam comprises 100 multiple-choice questions (MCQs), equally divided into five sections, with each section carrying 20 questions. The test is administered entirely in English.

The five sections are:

*   Quantitative Techniques & Data Interpretation
*   Logical Reasoning
*   Language Comprehension
*   General Awareness
*   Innovation and Entrepreneurship

For general category male candidates, the examination fee was INR 2,500, while general category female candidates and those from reserved categories paid INR 1,250.

## CMAT 2026 Result and Final Answer Key

The NTA officially declared the CMAT 2026 results on February 17, 2026. Along with the results, the final answer key was also published on the official CMAT portal. Candidates who appeared for the exam can access their scorecards by following these steps:

1.  Visit the official CMAT 2026 website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'CMAT-2026 scorecard' link.
3.  Enter your application number and either your password or date of birth.
4.  Click 'Submit' to view and download your CMAT 2026 result and scorecard.

It's worth noting that after reviewing objections submitted during the challenge window, the NTA made one revision to question number 97 in the final answer key, ensuring fairness and accuracy in scoring.

## CMAT Cutoffs and Top Colleges

CMAT scores are widely accepted by approximately 1,300 AICTE-approved management institutes across India, including those participating in the Maharashtra State institutes' admission process through MAH CET CAP. The cut-off percentile for admission varies significantly from one institution to another, depending on factors such as the college's ranking, intake capacity, and the overall performance of candidates in a given year.

To secure admission into top-tier MBA colleges, candidates generally need a high percentile score. Many reputable institutions release their specific cutoffs post-result declaration. Aspiring students should research individual college websites or refer to official admission brochures for detailed cutoff information and application procedures.
