# MHT CET 2026 Exam Date (Revised): Admit Card, Answer Key, Result Date

MHT CET 2026 is a crucial entrance examination for students aspiring to pursue professional courses like Engineering (BE/BTech), Pharmacy (BPharm/PharmD), and Architecture (BArch) in Maharashtra. The Maharashtra CET Cell has recently updated the exam schedule, and it's important for all candidates to be aware of these changes.

## Key Takeaways

*   The MHT CET 2026 examination for both PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) and PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) groups will be conducted in two separate sessions this year.
*   Revised dates for the PCM group have been announced, with exams specifically not taking place on April 12 to prevent conflicts with other national entrance tests.
*   The MHT CET 2026 admit card for the PCM group has already been released, and candidates should download it promptly.
*   Key dates for the answer key and results are expected around June 2026, with exact timelines to be confirmed by the Maharashtra CET Cell.

## MHT CET 2026 Dates and Schedule

The MHT CET 2026 examination will be held across two sessions, a format similar to national-level exams like JEE Main. This approach aims to provide more flexibility and opportunities for candidates. Below is the detailed schedule for the upcoming MHT CET 2026 events:

| :------------------------ | :--------------------------------------------------- |
| April 11 - April 20, 2026 | MHT CET 2026 Date for PCM Group (Session 1)          |
| April 21 - April 26, 2026 | MHT CET 2026 Exam for PCB Group (Session 1)          |
| May 10 - May 11, 2026     | MHT CET 2026 Exam for PCB Group (Session 2)          |
| May 12 - May 16, 2026     | MHT CET 2026 Date for PCM Group (Session 2)          |
| June 2026                 | MHT CET 2026 Answer Key (Tentative)                  |

*   **April 4, 2026:** Provisional MHT CET 2026 Hall Ticket issued.
*   **March 19 - March 20, 2026:** MHT CET 2026 Registration with Late Fee window.

## MHT CET 2026 Admit Card Date

The final admit cards for the MHT CET 2026 PCM group have been made available as of April 7, 2026. Students can download their hall tickets from the official portal, cetcell.mahacet.org, by logging in with their registered email ID and password. It is mandatory to print this admit card and carry it to the examination center.

Your admit card will contain vital information such as your specific exam date and time, the address of your assigned test center, the paper you are appearing for, and your personal details including name, photograph, and signature. Candidates for the PCB group can expect their admit cards to be released around April 14-15, 2026. Keep an eye on the official MHT CET website for the latest updates.

## MHT CET 2026 Answer Key Date

The Maharashtra CET Cell is expected to release the provisional MHT CET 2026 answer key online, likely within a week after the completion of the exams. This key will be accessible through the candidate login on cetcell.mahacet.org. Alongside the provisional answer key, an objection window will be activated.

If candidates find any discrepancies, they will have a specific period to challenge the answer key online. This involves submitting supporting documents and paying a processing fee. After reviewing all valid objections, the authorities will then publish the final MHT CET 2026 answer key, which will form the basis for result calculations.

## MHT CET 2026 Result Date

The MHT CET 2026 results are tentatively scheduled to be announced in June 2026. The results will be declared online on the official website. Candidates will be able to check their scores by logging into their accounts. The result will include the marks obtained and the percentile score, which is crucial for the subsequent counseling process.

Staying informed about these dates and preparing thoroughly will help you navigate the MHT CET 2026 process smoothly. We wish you the best for your examinations!
