# SNAP Syllabus 2026 with Weightage PDF: Download Section & Topics wise Syllabus

Preparing for the Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP) requires a clear understanding of its syllabus. This guide outlines the key sections and topics you need to master for the 2026 examination, as prescribed by the Symbiosis International (Deemed University) (SIU) exam conducting panel.

## Key takeaways

*   The SNAP 2026 syllabus covers General English, Quantitative Ability, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency, and Analytical & Logical Reasoning.
*   Quantitative Ability, Data Interpretation, and Data Sufficiency are combined into one section, as are Analytical and Logical Reasoning.
*   The exam consists of 60 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) to be completed within 60 minutes.
*   A dedicated preparation period of approximately three months is generally recommended to thoroughly cover the syllabus.

## SNAP Exam Syllabus: Section-Wise Highlights 2026

The SNAP exam syllabus shares common ground with other major MBA entrance examinations. When you begin your preparation for SNAP 2026, it's wise to identify the most frequently tested topics to prioritize your study plan effectively.

| SNAP Exam Sections                                 | Important SNAP Topics                                                                                                      |
| :------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| General English (Verbal Reasoning, Verbal Ability) | Syllogisms, Analogies, Grammar Rules, Antonyms-Synonyms, One-Word Substitutions, Similar Sounding Words, Para Jumbles, Fill in the Blanks |

## SNAP Syllabus for Quantitative Ability, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency

This section typically features 20 questions. To excel, candidates must grasp fundamental concepts and commit essential theorems and formulas to memory. Below are the key topics for the QA, DI-DS section of the SNAP Test syllabus:

*   **Algebra:** Linear Equations, Quadratic Equations, Set Theory, Progressions, Binomial Theorem, Surds and Indices, Inequalities
*   **Arithmetic:** Ratio and Proportion, Percentages, Partnership, Time-Speed-Distance, Mixtures & Alligations
*   **Geometry & Mensuration:** Geometry, Trigonometry, Mensuration, Co-ordinate Geometry
*   **Number System:** Number System, Logarithm
*   **Data Interpretation:** Graphs (Pie Charts, Bar Graphs), Tables
*   **Other Topics:** Permutation & Combination, Probability Functions, Series

## SNAP Syllabus for Analytical and Logical Reasoning

The Analytical and Logical Reasoning section of the SNAP 2026 exam often contains a significant number of questions. While the difficulty level is usually moderate, time management presents a considerable challenge. Consistent practice is therefore crucial. Focus on the following topics from the SNAP Test syllabus to master this section:

*   **Logical Deductions:** Syllogisms, Analogies & Series
*   **Arrangements:** Seating Arrangements
*   **Relationship-based Problems:** Family Tree/Blood Relations
*   **Coding & Decoding:** Coding/Decoding
*   **Spatial Reasoning:** Direction Sense
*   **Data-based Reasoning:** Mathematical Reasoning, Puzzles, Column Graphs, Pie Charts

## SNAP Syllabus for General English

The General English section assesses your command over the language, including vocabulary, grammar, and comprehension skills. Regular reading and practice with different question types will be highly beneficial.

*   **Vocabulary:** Antonyms, Synonyms, One-Word Substitution, Similar Sounding Words
*   **Grammar:** Parts of Speech, Tenses, Articles, Prepositions, Subject-Verb Agreement, Error Detection, Fill in the Blanks
*   **Verbal Ability:** Para Jumbles, Analogies, Sentence Completion
*   **Reasoning-based Verbal:** Syllogisms

By systematically covering these topics and regularly practicing with mock tests, you can build confidence and improve your performance in the SNAP 2026 examination.
