# UPCAT Counselling Dates 2022: Get Complete Schedule Here

For students aspiring to join various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Uttar Pradesh, the UPCET (Uttar Pradesh Common Entrance Test) is a crucial examination. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the UPCET 2022 counselling schedule, offering clarity on important dates and processes.

## Key Takeaways

*   The UPCET 2022 application window was expected to open in the first week of April 2022.
*   The examination itself was tentatively scheduled for the fourth week of June 2022.
*   Results were anticipated by the third week of July 2022.
*   The counselling process for seat allocation was likely to commence in August 2022.

## Understanding UPCET 2022

The UPCET examination, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), serves as a gateway to numerous courses offered by participating institutions across Uttar Pradesh. These programs span a wide range of disciplines, including:

*   BHMCT (Bachelor of Hotel Management and Catering Technology)
*   MURP (Master of Urban and Regional Planning)
*   BTech Lateral Entry
*   BDes (Bachelor of Design)
*   BFAD/BFA (Bachelor of Fine Arts and Design / Bachelor of Fine Arts)
*   MCA/MCA (Integrated)/MCA (Lateral Entry)
*   MTech (Master of Technology)
*   MDes (Master of Design)
*   BPharm/BPharm (Lateral) (Bachelor of Pharmacy)
*   MArch (Master of Architecture)
*   MPharma (Master of Pharmacy)
*   MBA/MBA (Integrated)

Candidates who successfully qualify in the UPCET exam become eligible to participate in the counselling rounds for admission to these programs.

## Important Dates for UPCET 2022

Staying informed about the official schedule is vital for all applicants. Below is a breakdown of the key dates for UPCET 2022, as announced by the examination authorities.

## The Counselling Process

Following the announcement of results, qualified candidates will be invited to participate in the UPCET 2022 counselling process. This multi-stage procedure involves registration, choice filling, seat allotment, and document verification. The specific dates and detailed instructions for each counselling round are typically released on the official UPCET portal. It is crucial for candidates to regularly check the official portal for the most up-to-date information regarding counselling schedules and procedures.

## Previous Year's Counselling Rounds

While the 2022 counselling dates were yet to be fully detailed, looking at the previous year's schedule can offer some insight into the typical timeline. For instance, in 2021, seat allotment rounds extended through November:

*   UPCET 2021 Seat Allotment - Round 7: November 29, 2021
*   UPCET 2021 Seat Allotment - Round 6: November 24, 2021
*   UPCET 2021 Seat Allotment - Round 5: November 20, 2021

This historical data suggests that the counselling process can involve multiple rounds and extend over several weeks.

We encourage all prospective students to keep a close watch on the official UPCET website for the latest updates and detailed counselling schedules to ensure a smooth admission journey.
