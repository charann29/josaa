# Post Graduate Program in Data Analytics and Data Science by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a strong foundation and advanced skills in the rapidly growing fields of data analytics and data science, this postgraduate program offers a comprehensive learning experience. It combines theoretical knowledge with practical, project-based learning to prepare individuals for careers in the digital world.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 24-week program focusing on advanced data analytics and data science concepts.
*   The curriculum includes nine distinct courses, with some available at physical centers in major Indian cities and others fully online.
*   Participants will gain hands-on experience through technical projects and receive a merit certificate upon successful completion.
*   The program offers extensive support, including expert instructors, technical assistance, career guidance, and placement opportunities.

## Program Overview and Structure

This Post Graduate Program in Data Analytics and Data Science is designed as a 24-week intensive course. It delves into both fundamental and advanced aspects of data analytics and data science, emphasizing real-world applications. The program is structured into nine courses. The initial five courses offer flexibility, allowing students to attend them at designated Skill-Lync centers located in Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Pune. The remaining four courses are delivered entirely online through the Skill-Lync learning platform, providing accessibility for students across various locations.

Upon successfully completing all components of the program, participants will be awarded a merit certificate, acknowledging their achievement and newly acquired expertise. Throughout the entire duration, students benefit from the guidance of expert instructors and dedicated technical support engineers. Additionally, the program includes career support services and placement assistance, aiming to help graduates transition into relevant roles.

## Eligibility Criteria and What You Will Learn

To enroll in this program, candidates should possess foundational knowledge of Python programming. This prerequisite ensures that participants can effectively engage with the course material, which builds upon these basic concepts.

The curriculum is comprehensive, covering a wide array of topics essential for a career in data science and analytics. Key areas of study include:

*   **Mathematical Foundations:** Concepts like set theory, trigonometric functions, permutations, combinations, and the principles of statistics and probability.
*   **Core Programming & Tools:** In-depth Python programming, data analysis with Pandas, basics of MS Excel, and SQL for data analysis and visualization.
*   **Data Handling:** Techniques for data cleaning and computation.
*   **Business Intelligence & Visualization:** Understanding Power BI, data modeling, and Tableau functions.
*   **Machine Learning & AI:** Fundamentals and advanced concepts of machine learning, artificial intelligence, and deep learning, including backpropagation.
*   **Application Development:** Introduction to scalable web/desktop applications.

This program is ideal for anyone with a keen interest in the fields of data science and data analytics, seeking to develop practical skills and a strong theoretical understanding.

## Program Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Post Graduate Program in Data Analytics and Data Science is ₹2,75,000. For those who prefer a more flexible payment approach, EMI options are available, with installments starting from ₹14,375 per month. The program fee covers lifetime access to the course content and all associated learning resources.

## How to Apply

Prospective students can easily enroll in the Post Graduate Program in Data Analytics and Data Science by following a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official Skill-Lync website for the Post Graduate Program in Data Analytics and Data Science.
2.  Locate and click on the "Enroll Now" option, typically found on the course page.
3.  Proceed to select a suitable payment package. You will then be prompted to provide your name, email address, and phone number to unlock full access to the program.

This simple admission process allows candidates to quickly begin their journey into data analytics and data science.

## Program Highlights

This program stands out due to several key features designed to enhance the learning experience and career prospects of its participants:

*   **Merit Certificate:** A formal recognition of successful completion.
*   **Expert Instructors:** Learning from experienced professionals in the field.
*   **Live Training Sessions:** Interactive sessions for real-time learning and doubt resolution.
*   **Technical Support Engineers:** Dedicated assistance for any technical queries.
*   **Career Support & Placement Opportunities:** Guidance and assistance in securing relevant job roles.
*   **Technical Projects:** Hands-on experience through practical projects to build a portfolio.
*   **1-on-1 Demo Session & Course Counseling:** Personalized guidance to understand the program better.

These elements collectively aim to provide a supportive and effective learning environment, preparing students for the demands of the data industry.
