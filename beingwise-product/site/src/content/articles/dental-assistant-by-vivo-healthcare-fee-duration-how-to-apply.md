# Dental Assistant by VIVO Healthcare: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those interested in a healthcare career that involves direct patient interaction and supports dental professionals, VIVO Healthcare offers a dedicated Dental Assistant certification. This programme is designed to equip individuals with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in a dental office environment.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 6-month, full-time training programme, totalling 700 hours.
*   It combines 510 hours of theoretical learning and simulations with a 190-hour practical internship.
*   Successful completion leads to an all-India recognised certificate from VIVO Healthcare, enhancing career prospects.
*   The course is accessible to individuals who have completed their 10th standard education.

## Course Overview

The Dental Assistant certification from VIVO Healthcare is crafted for individuals who possess strong communication skills and a desire to contribute to oral healthcare. The programme focuses on developing efficient dental assistants who can competently support dentists, thereby enhancing the quality of patient care. The curriculum covers a wide array of duties typically performed in a dental office, including supportive tasks, assisting with intra-oral restorative and endodontic procedures, and understanding dental radiography. It also covers professional conduct, ensuring graduates are well-rounded.

The course structure emphasises both technical proficiency and interpersonal skills, crucial for securing roles in various dental setups. The blend of simulated learning and practical internship ensures that students gain hands-on experience and a solid understanding of a dental assistant's daily responsibilities.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successfully completing the Dental Assistant programme, you will be proficient in a range of critical tasks and responsibilities, including:

*   Assisting dentists during various treatment procedures.
*   Taking and developing dental radiographs (X-rays).
*   Sterilising and preparing dental equipment and instruments.
*   Providing post-treatment oral care instructions to patients.
*   Educating patients on effective oral hygiene practices.
*   Taking impressions of patients' teeth for models or study casts.
*   Managing administrative duties such as scheduling appointments, ordering supplies, and handling billing.
*   Providing direct patient care support in specialised dental fields like pediatric dentistry, orthodontics, oral surgery, and periodontics.

## Eligibility Criteria and Certification

To be eligible for the Dental Assistant training, candidates must have successfully completed their 10th standard education. The certification is awarded by VIVO Healthcare and is recognised across India. To earn this certificate, students must fulfil specific requirements:

*   Maintain a minimum of 70% attendance in lectures.
*   Achieve at least 85% attendance during the clinical internship.
*   Score a minimum of 70% in the pre-internship examination.

This comprehensive evaluation ensures that certified individuals are well-prepared and competent in their roles.

## Programme Fees and Application Process

Details regarding the exact course fees can be obtained directly from VIVO Healthcare during the application process. The application process is straightforward and typically involves the following steps:

1.  **Visit a VIVO Skill Centre:** Candidates are usually required to visit their nearest VIVO skill centre to collect the admission form for the Dental Assistant course.
2.  **Form Submission:** Complete the application form with all necessary details and submit it along with any required paperwork.
3.  **Fee Payment:** The course fee can be paid either at the skill centre or online. For online payment, you will need to register by providing your full name, phone number, email ID, city of residence, and a password.
4.  **Enrolment:** Once the admission process is complete and payment is processed, you will be officially enrolled in the programme.

This programme offers a clear pathway to a rewarding career in dental assistance, providing both theoretical knowledge and practical experience.
