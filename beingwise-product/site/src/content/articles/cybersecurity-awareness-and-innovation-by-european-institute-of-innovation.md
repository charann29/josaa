# Cybersecurity Awareness and Innovation by European Institute of Innovation and Technology via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In our increasingly digital world, understanding cybersecurity is crucial for everyone, from students to professionals. The European Institute of Innovation and Technology (EIT) Digital, in collaboration with industry and academia, offers a valuable online certification program designed to equip individuals with essential knowledge to combat cyber threats effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   This beginner-friendly course provides fundamental cybersecurity awareness, focusing on identity management and common threats.
*   It's a self-paced, online program with approximately six hours of video and text-based learning content.
*   While course materials are free, a shareable certificate of completion from Coursera requires a fee or financial aid.
*   The program blends practical exercises, case studies, and expert insights to offer a comprehensive learning experience.

## Course Overview

The Cybersecurity Awareness and Innovation certification program is meticulously crafted to help individuals understand and mitigate the risks associated with cyberattacks. It delves into critical areas such as securing identity credentials, managing email threats, and understanding web hacking techniques. A unique aspect of this course is its focus on innovation in cybersecurity, featuring an insightful interview with a renowned expert in cybercrime and fraud.

The teaching faculty includes Román Ramírez, a recognized hacker and cybersecurity expert, and Iván Pau, a UPM researcher specializing in usable security. The curriculum integrates real-world cybersecurity incident case studies, engaging video lectures, and interactive exercises to ensure a deep and practical understanding for participants.

This initiative is a collaborative effort involving Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM), a globally recognized technical university known for its quality education and research, EIT Digital, and Ferrovial, a leading private investor in infrastructure and cities with a significant global presence.

## What You Will Learn

This program is designed to provide an in-depth understanding of cybersecurity mechanisms and their practical implementation, regardless of your academic background. Key learning outcomes include:

*   A thorough analysis of the concept of digital identity.
*   An introduction to the importance of credentials and their management.
*   Insights into new trends and innovations in the field of web hacking.
*   An examination of Enigmedia startup's approach to cybersecurity methods.
*   Understanding the modus operandi of web hackers and cybercriminals.
*   Best practices for creating and managing strong passwords.
*   Identification of key threats related to password security.

## Course Duration and Learning Format

The program is structured for self-paced learning, allowing you to progress at your convenience. It comprises approximately six hours of online content, delivered through a mix of video lectures, text-based materials, and interactive elements. The entirely online format offers flexibility, making it accessible for busy individuals looking to enhance their cybersecurity knowledge.

## Fees and Certification

While the core course materials are accessible for free, obtaining a shareable certificate of completion from Coursera requires a fee. The certificate fee for the Cybersecurity Awareness and Innovation program is ₹2,491. Financial aid options are also available for eligible learners. To receive the certification, verified learners who have either paid the fee or qualified for financial aid must achieve a passing grade in all course assignments.

## How to Apply

Admissions for the Cybersecurity Awareness and Innovation certification training are ongoing. To enroll:

1.  Locate the official course page on Coursera.
2.  Select the "Enroll for Free" option.
3.  You will then have a choice: either audit the course for free to access learning materials, or opt to purchase the course along with the certification and graded assignments. If choosing the latter, proceed to make the payment.

Upon successful enrollment, you can begin your learning journey immediately.

This program offers a fantastic opportunity to build foundational cybersecurity knowledge and awareness, essential skills in today's digital landscape.
