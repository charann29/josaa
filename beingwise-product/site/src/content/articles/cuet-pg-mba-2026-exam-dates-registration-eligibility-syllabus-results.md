# CUET-PG MBA 2026 Exam: Dates, Registration, Eligibility, Syllabus, Results

For students aspiring to pursue an MBA in India, the Common University Entrance Test (CUET-PG) offers a unified platform to gain admission into numerous postgraduate programs across central and participating universities. This guide provides essential information for the 2026 examination, covering key aspects from registration to results.

## Key Takeaways

*   The CUET-PG MBA exam is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) as a Computer-Based Test (CBT).
*   It serves as an admission gateway for various postgraduate courses, including MBA, MSc, MA, M.Tech, MCA, and M.Com.
*   Candidates can challenge the provisional answer key for a non-refundable fee of INR 200 per objection.
*   Results are determined based on the final answer key, which is prepared after reviewing all received objections.

## Understanding CUET-PG for MBA Admissions

The CUET-PG is a nationwide entrance examination designed to streamline admissions to postgraduate courses in participating universities. For MBA aspirants, this means a single exam can open doors to various institutions, simplifying the application process. The examination is entirely computer-based, ensuring a standardized and efficient testing environment across the country.

## CUET-PG Answer Key and Objection Process

The NTA releases a provisional answer key for the CUET-PG exam on its official website, accessible through a candidate's login. This allows students to review their responses against the provisional correct answers. If a candidate believes an answer provided in the key is incorrect, they have the opportunity to raise an objection.

To challenge an answer key, candidates must log in to the CUET-PG portal, select the specific question and the challenged option, and upload supporting documents in PDF format. A non-refundable fee of INR 200 is required for each objection submitted, payable online. After reviewing all objections, the NTA will release a final answer key, which will be used for calculating candidate scores and preparing the results.

## CUET-PG Results and Counselling

Once the final answer key is published, the NTA proceeds to calculate the scores and declare the CUET-PG results. Candidates can download their scorecards from the official NTA website using their application number and password. The result forms the basis for admission to various MBA programs. Following the result declaration, participating universities will conduct their individual counselling processes, details of which will be made available on their respective portals or through the official NTA website.

Preparing well for the CUET-PG MBA 2026 exam is your first step towards securing a coveted spot in a top B-school. Stay informed with official updates from the NTA and plan your preparation strategically.
