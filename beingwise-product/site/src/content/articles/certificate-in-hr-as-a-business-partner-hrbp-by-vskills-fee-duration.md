# Certificate in HR as a Business Partner (HRBP) by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aiming to excel in human resources, the Vskills Certificate in HR as a Business Partner (HRBP) online course offers a structured path. This program is designed to equip you with the skills to align HR functions strategically with business objectives, making you a more valuable asset to any organization.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is delivered online through self-study, utilizing video and text-based materials.
*   It covers modern HR strategies, including business analytics and navigating the 'VUCA World'.
*   Upon successful completion of an online exam, you receive a permanent specialization certificate from Vskills.
*   The program fee is ₹3,499, with various convenient payment options available.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This certification focuses on transforming traditional HR roles into strategic business partnerships. You'll learn how HR can actively contribute to an organization's overall success, moving beyond administrative tasks to strategic decision-making and implementation.

The curriculum is comprehensive, covering the distinctions between a traditional HR role and an HR Business Partner. It introduces tools for strategic decision-making, change management, and enhancing employee engagement. The course also emphasizes the use of HR analytics to improve employee retention, boost return on investment (ROI), and resolve workplace challenges effectively.

Learning is self-paced, allowing you to study at your own convenience through Vskills' e-learning platform, which provides unlimited access to course materials.

## What You Will Learn

The Vskills Certificate in HR as a Business Partner (HRBP) training offers extensive coursework for aspiring HRBPs. As a certified HR Business Partner, you will be prepared to facilitate employee growth and align with organizational agendas. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Understanding business partnerships through GAP analysis.
*   Applying strategic management tools like SWOT and PESTLE analysis.
*   Utilizing strategic decision-making frameworks.
*   Aligning HR strategies with overall business goals.
*   Developing interpersonal skills such as empathy and trust.
*   Employing consulting skills to manage resistance.
*   Applying effective counselling techniques.
*   Implementing successful change management steps.
*   Facilitating employee development.
*   Measuring ROI and applying HR analytics metrics.
*   Ensuring legal compliance with labor and taxation laws.
*   Solving problems and protecting employees in the workplace.

## Program Highlights and Offerings

The Vskills HRBP program comes with several benefits designed to support your learning and career progression:

*   Free exercise materials for practical application.
*   Simulated preparation tests to help you get ready for the certification exam.
*   Full e-learning access through a limitless Learning Management System (LMS).
*   Assistance with resume building to highlight your new skills.
*   Placement support to help you find suitable opportunities.
*   A permanent specialization certificate upon successful completion.
*   Lifetime validity for the certificate, verified by Vskills.
