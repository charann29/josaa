# Applied Data Science Capstone by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This capstone course, offered by IBM through Coursera, provides a practical, hands-on experience designed to simulate the real-world tasks of a data scientist. It's an excellent opportunity to apply your data science skills to a comprehensive project.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is delivered entirely online and is self-paced, allowing for flexible learning.
*   It focuses on practical application, including working with location data, web scraping, and data analysis using Python.
*   You can audit the course content for free, with an option to pay for a verified certificate and an IBM digital badge.
*   Financial aid is available for those who qualify, making the certification more accessible.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This program is structured to give you a deep dive into the practical aspects of data science. You'll engage with various processes and applications that are central to a data scientist's daily work. A key component involves understanding and utilizing location data, including interacting with providers like Foursquare and making RESTful API calls to retrieve information.

Should readily available datasets not suffice, the course also equips you with skills in web scraping and parsing HTML code to gather your own data. You'll refine your data exploration and analysis abilities using Python, specifically leveraging its powerful Pandas library. To effectively communicate your findings, you'll learn to use the Folium library for visualizing geospatial data on maps. The course is taught by Alex Aklson from IBM and is delivered in English through video and text-based modules.

## Certification and Fees

Upon successful completion of the course curriculum and all graded assignments, you can earn a certification provided by Coursera. An IBM digital badge is also awarded, which can be a valuable addition to your professional profile. While you can audit the course content for free, obtaining the official certification and badge requires a subscription.

The fee structure for certification is subscription-based, with costs varying depending on the duration of your access. Here's a breakdown:

| Certification Period | Monthly Fee (approx.) |
| :------------------- | :-------------------- |
| 1 month              | Rs. 1,683             |
| 3 months             | Rs. 1,128             |
| 6 months             | Rs. 842               |

These fees are approximate and subject to change. A free seven-day trial is also available, allowing you to explore the course content before committing to a paid plan. For those who face financial constraints, financial aid options are provided by Coursera, which can help cover the cost of the certificate.

## Eligibility and Course Content

There are no strict eligibility criteria to enroll in this course; it's designed for anyone interested in pursuing or advancing a career as a data scientist. The primary requirement for earning the certification is the successful completion of all course modules and graded assignments.

The course syllabus is designed to offer practical insights and hands-on learning. Key topics you will cover include:

*   Utilizing the Folium library for effective communication of data findings.
*   Techniques for parsing HTML code and scraping data from the web.
*   Proficient use of Python and its Pandas library for data manipulation and analysis.
*   Understanding location data and engaging with location data providers such as Foursquare.
*   Making RESTful API calls to the Foursquare API to retrieve relevant data.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Applied Data Science Capstone course is straightforward. You can begin by visiting the course page on the official Coursera platform. From there, you have a few options:

1.  **Start a 7-day trial:** This allows you to access the full course content for a week to decide if it's right for you.
2.  **Enroll for free:** This option typically allows you to audit the course, meaning you can view most course materials but may not have access to graded assignments or a certificate.
3.  **Subscribe:** If you wish to earn the certificate and digital badge, you can subscribe to a paid plan directly.

To proceed, you will either sign in with an existing Coursera account or create a new one. You can also use your Google or Facebook account for quick registration. Once signed in, you'll confirm your choice regarding auditing, the free trial, or a paid subscription.

This course offers a valuable pathway for aspiring and current data scientists to enhance their practical skills and earn a recognized credential from IBM.
