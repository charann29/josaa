# Data Analytics Program by CareerFoundry: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Are you looking to kickstart a career in the rapidly growing field of data analytics or enhance your existing skills? The Data Analytics Program by CareerFoundry is an online course designed to equip you with the necessary expertise to become a data analyst.

## Key takeaways

*   This 10-month, fully online program is structured for flexibility, allowing you to learn at your own pace alongside other commitments.
*   It offers comprehensive support, including an industry expert mentor, daily tutor, student advisor, and a dedicated career specialist.
*   The curriculum is project-based, helping you build a professional portfolio with real-world projects.
*   CareerFoundry provides a job guarantee: if you don't secure employment within six months of graduation, your tuition fee is refunded.

## Program Overview

The CareerFoundry Data Analytics Program is an entirely online, self-paced course spanning ten months. It's built to be flexible, meaning you can fit your studies around your personal and professional life. The learning content is delivered through a mix of video and text formats, requiring an estimated 15-20 hours of study per week.

A core aspect of this program is the hands-on learning experience. You'll receive regular feedback from an industry expert mentor and a daily tutor. The curriculum covers essential data analytics topics, including data preparation, data analysis, data visualization, storytelling with data, SQL, Python fundamentals, and creating interactive dashboards. Project milestones are flexible, allowing you to complete assignments at a speed that suits you, with a progress tracker to help you monitor your performance.

## Building Your Portfolio and Career Support

An employment-focused approach is central to the program. You'll work on real-world projects that contribute to a strong professional portfolio. Upon completion, a career specialist will assist you in refining your resume to highlight these projects effectively. This dedicated career support aims to prepare you for the job market. CareerFoundry stands by its program with a job guarantee, promising a full refund of your tuition if you don't find a job within six months of graduating.

### What You Will Learn

The program is suitable for beginners, intermediate learners, and professionals looking to upskill. It covers a broad range of data analytics concepts, ensuring you gain proficiency in:

*   Data analytics fundamentals, including preparing and analyzing datasets.
*   Effective data storytelling and visualization techniques.
*   Working with SQL and database management.
*   Understanding Python fundamentals for data analysis.
*   Creating and utilizing dashboards.
*   Exploratory data analysis.
*   Data ethics and responsible data practices.
*   Data mining techniques.

## Fees and Duration

The total fee for the CareerFoundry Data Analytics Program is $7,900. This amount covers all program materials and support services. Payment plans, including EMI options, may be available to help manage the cost. Upon successful completion of the course, you will receive a certificate from CareerFoundry.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

There are no specific academic prerequisites for enrolling in the Data Analytics Program. However, prospective students must have a personal computer (PC or Mac) with a stable internet connection and demonstrate B2-level proficiency in both spoken and written English.

To apply for the program, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official CareerFoundry portal for the Data Analytics Program.
2.  Select your preferred payment track and click on the 'Enrol now' option.
3.  Create a new student account by providing the required details, or log in if you already have an account.
4.  Choose your desired payment plan and select your preferred start date for the program.
5.  Complete the necessary payment to finalize your enrollment.

This program is an excellent option for individuals aiming to transition into a data analytics career, those with analytical experience seeking job readiness, or professionals looking to integrate data analytics skills into their current roles.
