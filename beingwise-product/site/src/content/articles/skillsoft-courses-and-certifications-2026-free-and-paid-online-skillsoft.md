# Skillsoft Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Skillsoft Courses

Skillsoft offers a range of online courses and certifications designed to enhance your professional skills in various in-demand fields. Collaborating with platforms like TCS iON, these programs provide practical knowledge and industry-relevant expertise to help you advance your career.

## Key Takeaways

*   Skillsoft, often in partnership with TCS iON, provides industry-focused online certification courses.
*   These programs typically last between five to six months and include a blend of theoretical and practical learning.
*   Many courses feature mentorship from academic experts and industry professionals.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive digital, shareable certificates.

## Understanding Skillsoft's Offerings

Skillsoft courses are structured to give students a deep dive into specific technological domains. They emphasize practical application, ensuring that learners not only grasp theoretical concepts but also understand how to implement them in real-world scenarios. The curriculum often incorporates project-based learning, assessments, and sometimes even internship and career support, as seen in programs offered through TCS iON.

## Featured Certification Courses

Let's explore some of the key certification programs available, often through partnerships with platforms like TCS iON:

### Applied Cloud Computing

This course focuses on the essential principles of cloud computing, covering data storage, processing, and management using remote servers and networking. It's designed for individuals looking to build practical skills in cloud strategies and system resources. The program incorporates lectures, mentorship, and practical exercises, culminating in a digital certificate upon successful completion. Key skills you'll develop include a strong understanding of cloud computing, AWS technology, database management, and networking.

*   **Duration:** 6 months
*   **Fee:** ₹ 20,000

### Practical Approach to Data Mining and Analytics

This certification delves into the fundamentals and advanced concepts of data mining and analytics. It teaches you how to effectively process and transfer data to support informed decision-making. The curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, guided by mentors who are experts in computer science and industry consultants. Graduates receive a digital certificate recognizing their enhanced skills in data analysis.

*   **Duration:** 6 months
*   **Fee:** ₹ 20,000

### Information Security - Practitioner's Perspective

This program is tailored for those interested in information risk management, focusing on preventing, protecting, and securing organizational data. Over five to six months, students engage in online lectures, tutorials, and practical sessions. The course aims to provide insights into information security strategies, helping learners identify and mitigate potential threats to information and network systems. Mentorship from chief security officers and directors further enriches the learning experience, and successful participants earn five academic credits along with a digital certificate.

*   **Duration:** 5 months
*   **Fee:** ₹ 20,000

## Learning Methodology and Support

The online courses typically blend theoretical instruction with hands-on practical sessions. Many programs, especially those offered via TCS iON, benefit from guidance by both academic professionals and industry veterans. This dual mentorship approach ensures that students gain both foundational knowledge and real-world insights, preparing them for industry challenges. The virtual learning environment is often supported by resources designed to enhance competence in specific domains.

These Skillsoft certifications provide a structured path to acquire valuable skills, making you more competitive in today's job market.
