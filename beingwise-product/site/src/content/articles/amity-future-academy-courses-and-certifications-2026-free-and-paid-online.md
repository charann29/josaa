# Amity Future Academy Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Amity Future Academy Courses

Amity Future Academy offers a range of online certification programs designed to equip professionals and aspiring students with in-demand skills for the modern job market. These programs are structured to provide practical, industry-relevant knowledge through flexible online learning.

## Key takeaways

*   Amity Future Academy provides specialized Post Graduate Programs in high-demand fields like Product Management, UX Design, and Data Science.
*   Programs typically span 12 months and require a commitment of 7-10 hours per week, blending recorded lectures with interactive elements.
*   Courses often feature collaborations with international universities and industry leaders, enhancing the curriculum and global perspective.
*   Dedicated career support, including counseling and job fair access, is a significant component of many programs.

## Post Graduate Program in Product Management (PGP-PM)

The Post Graduate Program in Product Management (PGP-PM) is a comprehensive online certification course aimed at developing skilled product managers. This program is designed to be highly interactive, requiring participants to dedicate approximately 7-10 hours per week over 12 months.

The curriculum is crafted by seasoned industry professionals, ensuring that the learning extends beyond theoretical concepts to real-world application. Students gain access to pre-recorded video lectures that can be reviewed at their convenience, alongside extensive study materials.

Key tools and platforms covered include Mixpanel, Google Analytics, Jira, Balsamiq, and InVision. The course emphasizes practical learning through case studies and real-world problems, preparing students to tackle industry scenarios effectively. Towards the program's conclusion, participants can benefit from career counseling to help them prepare for interviews and align with current industry standards.

*   **Duration:** 12 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹2,15,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Data Science knowledge, Product Management

## Post Graduate Program in UX Design Strategies (PGP - UXD)

For those interested in the rapidly growing field of User Experience (UX) design, the Post Graduate Program in UX Design Strategies (PGP - UXD) certification course focuses on cultivating top-tier talent in UX architecture and consumer testing. This program delves into the principles of user analysis, design meaning, design policy, thesis, and architecture, guided by leading UX design academics and industry experts.

This certification aims to bolster careers in UX by enhancing both efficiency and intellectual skills. It offers opportunities to work on real-life ventures and case studies, providing practical experience vital for excelling in the design world. The online course is developed in collaboration with Berkeley Global and IBM, adding a global and industry-specific dimension to the learning.

The Post Graduate Program in UX Design Strategies (PGP - UXD) syllabus incorporates a balanced assessment approach, including assignments, online quizzes, and learning through case studies. The program's vision is to make students highly proficient in UX architecture and client study from a career perspective.

*   **Duration:** 12 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹1,85,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Designing skills

## Post-Graduate Program in Data Science (PGP-DS)

The Post-Graduate Program in Data Science (PGP-DS) is a robust 12-month online program providing a comprehensive understanding of data science concepts, encompassing both theoretical foundations and practical applications. Participants will master essential areas such as data analytics, machine learning, data visualization, and deep learning.

The program covers popular technologies including R, Tableau, Hadoop, Python, Spark, Hive, and SQL. Amity Future Academy offers this course in partnership with eCornell, the online education department of Cornell University, based in New York.

This certification course emphasizes career advancement, featuring dedicated career assistance, access to a virtual job fair, and recruitment platforms like Hiration and CutShort.io. Students also benefit from four case studies by Harvard Business Publishing, various projects, and high-quality video lessons delivered by industry experts.

Towards the program's end, a capstone project allows students to consolidate all the knowledge gained throughout the online Post-Graduate Program in Data Science (PGP-DS). Upon successful completion of all course modules and assignments, candidates receive a certificate.

This range of programs from Amity Future Academy is tailored to provide crucial skills and career pathways in today's dynamic professional landscape.
