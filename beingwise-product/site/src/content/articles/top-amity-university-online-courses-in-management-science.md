# Top Amity University Online Courses in Management Science

Amity University began offering a variety of online programs, including those in Management Science, in 2020. These digital learning options ensure that students can access quality education remotely.

## Key Takeaways

*   Amity University's online management courses are approved by the UGC and hold WASC accreditation from the US.
*   Programs like the BBA and MBA are highly sought after and offer flexibility for students.
*   The online MBA provides ten specializations, allowing for focused career development.
*   Fees for these online diplomas and degrees are structured per semester or as a total program cost.

## Why Choose Amity Online for Management Science?

Amity University is recognized for its commitment to quality education, holding approval from the University Grants Commission (UGC) in India. Furthermore, it is the only university in India to have accreditation from the WASC Senior College and University Commission (WSCUC) in the United States, signifying a global standard of academic excellence. For students keen on pursuing management studies through a flexible online format, Amity's offerings present a valuable opportunity.

## Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) Online

The online BBA is one of Amity University's most popular undergraduate programs. It is designed for students from any academic background who have completed their 10th and 12th years of formal education. This three-year program is divided into six semesters, offering a comprehensive study of business administration principles, covering both theoretical concepts and practical applications. The total fee for this online BBA program is Rs 1,65,000, which includes Rs 30,000 for each odd semester and Rs 25,000 for each even semester.

## Master of Business Administration (MBA) Online

For graduates looking to advance their business knowledge without pausing their careers, the online MBA is an excellent choice. This two-year, four-semester program allows professionals to learn and work simultaneously. As one of Amity's top online management programs, it offers ten diverse specializations, enabling students to gain in-depth expertise in a specific industry and prepare for specialized roles. A bachelor's degree and proficiency in English are the primary requirements for this UGC-approved course.

### MBA Specializations Offered:

*   Accounting
*   Marketing & Sales Management
*   Entrepreneurship & Leadership Management
*   Finance & Accounting Management
*   Human Resource Management
*   Management in Information Technology
*   Management of International Business
*   Management of Production and Operations
*   Retail Management
*   Rural Management
*   Family Business Management

## Post Graduate Diploma in Advertising and Brand Management

This one-year Post Graduate Diploma provides a detailed exploration of both advertising and brand management, highlighting how these two critical areas of business strategy are interconnected. To be eligible, applicants typically need to have completed their 10th and 12th grades, along with a bachelor's degree. While any bachelor's degree is accepted, a background in business management or related fields can be particularly advantageous. The program aims to transform learners into skilled professionals in both advertising and brand management. The total fee for this program is Rs 1,20,000.

Amity University's online management science courses provide accessible and high-quality pathways for students and working professionals to enhance their careers in the dynamic business world.
