# ICAR AIEEA Exam 2026: Application Form, Exam Date, Syllabus, Pattern & Question Paper

For students aspiring to build a career in agriculture and related sciences, the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) All India Entrance Examination for Admission (AIEEA) is a crucial pathway. This national-level exam facilitates entry into postgraduate and research programs across a wide network of agricultural universities in India.

## Key Takeaways

*   The ICAR AIEEA is a national entrance exam primarily for postgraduate (PG) and research programs in agricultural sciences.
*   Admission to undergraduate (UG) agricultural courses within ICAR-affiliated universities is now based on CUET scores, not the AIEEA.
*   Approximately 11,000 postgraduate seats are available through the AIEEA across 75 participating universities.
*   The National Testing Agency (NTA) is responsible for conducting the ICAR AIEEA exam.

## Understanding ICAR AIEEA

The Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) oversees the AIEEA, an annual examination designed to admit students into various PG and Ph.D. level programs. These programs are offered at 75 agricultural universities throughout the country. This extensive network includes 64 State Agricultural, Veterinary, Horticultural, and Fisheries Universities (SAUs), four ICAR Deemed Universities (like IARI and NDRI), three Central Agricultural Universities, and four Central Universities with agricultural faculties.

While the AIEEA is vital for postgraduate admissions, it's important to note that undergraduate admissions into these institutions are now conducted through scores obtained in the Common University Entrance Test (CUET).

## Important Dates for ICAR AIEEA 2026

The National Testing Agency (NTA) is expected to commence the application process for ICAR AIEEA 2026 around April. The examination itself is tentatively scheduled to take place in June. Prospective candidates should regularly check the official NTA website for precise dates and notifications.

## Application Process

Candidates interested in applying for ICAR AIEEA 2026 will need to visit the official NTA website. The application form will be made available online, typically in April. It's crucial for applicants to carefully fill out all required details, upload necessary documents, and pay the application fee as specified by the NTA to successfully complete their submission.

## Syllabus and Exam Pattern

The ICAR AIEEA syllabus and exam pattern are designed to assess candidates' knowledge in agricultural and allied sciences relevant to their chosen postgraduate discipline. While specific details for 2026 will be released by the NTA, the exam generally features multiple-choice questions. Candidates are advised to refer to the previous year's information bulletins and question papers available on the official NTA portal to understand the scope and format of the examination. Preparation should focus on core subjects pertinent to the desired field of study.

## Counselling and Admission

Following the examination, a counselling process will be conducted for final admissions. This counselling is typically held separately for both undergraduate (based on CUET scores) and postgraduate (based on AIEEA scores) admissions. Candidates who qualify will be invited to participate in the counselling rounds, where seats will be allocated based on merit and preferences, in accordance with ICAR guidelines.

We wish you the very best in your preparation for ICAR AIEEA 2026 and your journey into agricultural education.
