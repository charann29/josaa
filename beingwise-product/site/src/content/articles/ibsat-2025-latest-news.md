# IBSAT 2025 Latest News

For students aspiring to pursue an MBA, the IBSAT exam is a significant gateway to various ICFAI Business School campuses. Staying updated on the latest news and announcements is crucial for a smooth application process.

## Key Takeaways

*   Applications for IBS Business School programs are currently open, with deadlines approaching.
*   IBSAT scores are accepted by nine different ICFAI Business School campuses across India.
*   Beyond IBSAT, several other prominent MBA entrance exams like CAT, XAT, NMAT, and GMAT are also accepted by these institutions.
*   Prospective students should monitor the official IBSAT portal for updates on application forms, exam dates, and admit card releases.

## About IBSAT

The IBSAT (ICFAI Business School Aptitude Test) is a computer-based entrance examination conducted by the ICFAI Foundation for Higher Education. It serves as a qualifying test for admission to various postgraduate management programs, primarily the MBA/PGPM programs offered by the nine campuses of ICFAI Business School. The exam assesses a candidate's analytical skills, quantitative ability, data interpretation, and verbal ability, which are essential for success in a demanding management curriculum.

## IBSAT 2025 Application Process

The application window for IBSAT 2025 will be announced on the official IBSAT portal. Typically, the process involves online registration, filling out the application form with personal and academic details, uploading necessary documents, and paying the application fee. It is highly recommended that candidates complete their applications well before the closing dates to avoid any last-minute technical issues. Keep an eye on the official website for specific dates regarding the application opening and closing.

## Participating ICFAI Business Schools

IBSAT scores open doors to nine ICFAI Business School campuses, each offering a two-year, full-time MBA program. Here's a brief overview of some key institutions, including their locations and approximate total fees for the MBA program:

| Campus Name | Location | Total MBA Fees (Approx.) | Other Accepted Exams |
| :-------------------------------- | :----------- | :----------------------- | :---------------------------------- |
| ICFAI Business School, Hyderabad | Hyderabad    | ₹ 17.01 Lakh             | XAT, NMAT, CAT, GMAT                |
| ICFAI Business School, Bangalore | Bangalore    | ₹ 13.05 Lakh             | GMAT, CAT, NMAT                     |
| ICFAI Business School, Mumbai    | Mumbai       | ₹ 8.29 Lakh              | NMAT, CAT, GMAT, XAT                |
| ICFAI Business School, Dehradun  | Dehradun     | ₹ 7.20 Lakh              | NMAT, CAT, GMAT, XAT                |

These campuses provide a diverse learning environment and accept scores from multiple national-level management entrance exams, offering students flexibility in their application strategy. While IBSAT is a primary route, candidates can also apply with scores from CAT, XAT, NMAT, or GMAT.
