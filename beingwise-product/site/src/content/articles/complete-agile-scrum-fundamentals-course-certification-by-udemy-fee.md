## Course Overview

Created by Mauricio Rubio, this online program aims to provide a thorough understanding of Agile Scrum. You'll explore everything from basic Scrum concepts and values to the tools and rituals involved in successful Scrum project execution. The goal is to make you proficient in using Scrum to enhance business value and manage projects efficiently.

The curriculum covers a wide array of topics, including Scrum Fundamentals, Scrum Values, Scrum Rituals, and the various Scrum Roles. Crucially, no prior knowledge or experience with Scrum is needed.

Upon completing this online training, you will master various Scrum tools and concepts, including Scrum Values, Principles, and Pillars. You'll also gain an understanding of Scrum's history and develop the fluency needed to create and manage projects using the Scrum framework effectively.
