Welcome to BeingWise's guide for the Andhra Pradesh Secondary School Certificate (SSC) Board Exams in 2026. This page offers essential information for students and parents about upcoming dates, results, and preparation resources for the BSEAP 10th class examinations.

## Key takeaways

*   The Andhra Pradesh SSC Board Exams for 2026 are scheduled from March 16 to April 1, 2026.
*   Results for the 2026 exams are anticipated in April 2026, accessible on the official BSEAP results portal.
*   Supplementary exams will take place in May 2026 for students who need to re-appear.
*   A minimum of 35% marks in each subject (Paper 1 and Paper 2 combined), and an overall aggregate, is required to pass the exams.

## AP SSC Exam Dates 2026

The Board of Secondary Education, Andhra Pradesh (BSEAP) has announced the schedule for the SSC exams. The main examinations will commence on March 16, 2026, and conclude on April 1, 2026. The official time table was released on November 21, 2025, on the BSEAP official website. Students should refer to this official schedule for precise dates for each subject.

## Accessing Your AP SSC Result 2026

The Andhra Pradesh SSC results for 2026 are expected to be declared in April 2026. Students can check their results online by following these steps:

1.  Visit the official BSEAP results website: results.bse.ap.gov.in.
2.  Look for the link related to 'Class 10 (SSC) Results 2026'.
3.  Enter your unique roll number in the designated field.
4.  Your detailed mark sheet will appear on the screen.
5.  It's advisable to download and print a copy for your records, as this serves as a provisional mark sheet.

To successfully pass the AP SSC exams, students must secure a minimum of 35% marks in each subject, considering both Paper 1 and Paper 2 combined. For the second language subject, the minimum passing mark is 20. Failing to meet this criterion will require students to appear for the supplementary examinations.

## Hall Tickets and Examination Day Guidelines

BSEAP released the hall tickets for the 2026 exams on March 5, 2026. Students are required to collect their official hall tickets directly from their respective schools. The hall ticket is a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall and contains crucial details such as the student's roll number, examination center, and specific exam dates. Always check all details on your hall ticket carefully.

## Syllabus and Preparation Resources

To prepare effectively for the AP SSC exams, students should thoroughly review the official syllabus provided by BSEAP. Understanding the syllabus is the first step towards focused study. Additionally, practicing with previous year's question papers and model papers is highly recommended. These resources help students understand the exam pattern, the types of questions asked, and time management strategies. Official textbooks prescribed by BSEAP are the primary study material.
