# Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp by HyperionDev: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Considering a career in web development? The Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp by HyperionDev is an online program designed to equip you with the skills to build dynamic web applications from start to finish. This comprehensive bootcamp offers a flexible learning path, allowing you to tailor the experience to your schedule and career goals.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Flexible Learning:** The program can be completed in 3 to 6 months, with options for both part-time and full-time study.
*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** Learn essential technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, MongoDB, Express, and React to become a proficient full-stack developer.
*   **Personalised Support:** Benefit from one-on-one mentorship, detailed professional feedback, and an interactive online community.
*   **Career-Focused Services:** Receive assistance with resume building, interview preparation, and access to a dedicated hiring portal.

## What You Will Learn

This bootcamp is structured to provide a deep understanding of both front-end and back-end web development, enabling you to build complete web applications. The curriculum covers a range of critical topics and technologies, including:

*   **Website Foundation:** Understanding the principles of building robust websites.
*   **HTML:** Mastering the standard markup language for creating web pages.
*   **CSS:** Learning to style web pages and create visually appealing layouts.
*   **JavaScript:** Gaining proficiency in the programming language that brings interactivity to websites.
*   **Node.js:** Exploring server-side JavaScript for building scalable network applications.
*   **MongoDB:** Working with a popular NoSQL database for managing application data.
*   **Express and React:** Utilising these frameworks to build powerful and responsive user interfaces and robust server-side applications.

Through practical experience and expert guidance, you will develop the skills needed to design, develop, and deploy modern web applications.

## Program Structure and Duration

The Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp is entirely online, delivered through video and text-based modules. This format allows for significant flexibility, catering to various student schedules. You have the option to choose between:

*   **Part-time Study:** Ideal for those balancing other commitments, extending the completion time.
*   **Full-time Study:** Suitable for individuals who wish to accelerate their learning and complete the program sooner.

Regardless of your chosen pace, the program is designed to be completed within **three to six months**. This personalised scheduling ensures you can progress at a comfortable yet challenging rate.

## Fees and Certification

The total program fee for the Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp is **$7,995**. This amount covers the comprehensive curriculum, personalised mentorship, access to the learning platform, and career services. Upon successful completion of the bootcamp, you will receive a Full Stack Web Development Certification issued by HyperionDev, validating your newly acquired skills.

### Scholarship Opportunities

HyperionDev offers a **Needs-Based Scholarship** specifically for full-time students or unemployed individuals residing in South Africa. This scholarship provides a significant 37% discount on the bootcamp tuition. Each month, five eligible applicants are awarded this scholarship, making the program more accessible to those who qualify.

## How to Apply

The application process for the Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp is straightforward and can be completed online:

1.  **Visit the Course Page:** Navigate to the official Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp course webpage.
2.  **Enrol Now:** Click on the 'Enrol Now' button and create your profile on the HyperionDev platform.
3.  **Choose Your Bootcamp:** Select the Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp from the available options.
4.  **Start Your Free Trial:** Before committing, you can begin a free trial to experience the course structure and content firsthand.
5.  **Select a Payment Plan:** Once you've completed the trial and decided to proceed, choose a suitable payment plan.
6.  **Begin Learning:** After your admission is confirmed and payment is processed, you can commence your learning journey on the designated start date.

The initial sign-up form requires basic details such as your name, email address, and a chosen password, along with a simple question to get started.

This bootcamp provides a robust pathway to a career in web development, combining expert instruction with practical, hands-on learning experiences and dedicated career support.
