# Data Science Course by OdinSchool: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in the rapidly expanding field of data science, OdinSchool offers a comprehensive certification program. This bootcamp is designed to equip individuals with the necessary skills for various data-related roles.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is a live, instructor-led bootcamp delivered online, primarily on weekends.
*   It focuses on practical, industry-aligned skills, including Python, SQL, Machine Learning, and Power BI.
*   Participants receive extensive career support, including mock interviews, resume building, and placement assistance with a network of over 500 hiring partners.
*   Scholarships are available, potentially reducing the course fee significantly.

## Course Overview

OdinSchool's Data Science certification course is a live online bootcamp structured to provide an immersive learning experience. The curriculum is broken down into assignments, tasks, projects, and quizzes, requiring approximately 15 hours of study per weekend. This structure allows participants to continuously apply what they learn and build a strong portfolio.

The course aims to prepare individuals for a range of data science careers, whether they are seeking a career change, an upgrade, or a launch into the field. A dedicated placement team works with graduates to connect them with relevant job opportunities upon completion.

## Curriculum and Learning Outcomes

The program's curriculum is designed to be industry-aligned, ensuring that participants gain job-ready skills. Key areas covered include:

### Core Modules

*   Introduction to Data Science
*   Statistics for Data Science
*   SQL Fundamentals
*   Python Programming for Data Science
*   Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
*   Machine Learning Concepts
*   Power BI for Data Visualization
*   Business Intelligence Principles
*   Data Storytelling

### Elective Modules

Participants can also explore advanced topics such as Git and GitHub, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) processes, Apache Spark, Apache Airflow, Azure cloud services, Azure Data Factory, and advanced Power BI techniques.

Upon successful completion, candidates will possess a strong understanding of these tools and methodologies, making them suitable for roles like Data Analyst, Data Scientist, Data Engineer, Power BI Developer, and Machine Learning Engineer.

## Fees and Scholarship Opportunities

The standard fee for the Data Science certification course is ₹90,000 plus GST. However, OdinSchool offers a discounted fee of ₹75,000 plus GST. Additionally, eligible students may qualify for scholarships worth up to ₹30,000, which can further reduce the financial commitment.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

There are no specific academic prerequisites to enroll in this Data Science course, making it accessible to a wide range of individuals interested in the field. To qualify for the certification, participants must complete all live classes, quizzes, assessments, assignments, team tasks, and project work.

To apply for the course, prospective students should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official OdinSchool website for the Data Science course.
2.  Locate and fill out the inquiry form typically found on the right-hand side of the page to request a callback.
3.  After receiving a callback, an admissions representative will guide you through the subsequent steps of the enrollment process.

This structured approach ensures that individuals are well-informed and supported throughout their journey into the data science profession.
