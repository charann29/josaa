# Certification Course in CATIA- Computer Aided Design and Drafting by Diyguru Education and Research Private Limited: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This BeingWise guide explores the Certification Course in CATIA- Computer Aided Design and Drafting offered by Diyguru Education and Research Private Limited. This program aims to equip students with essential skills in CATIA software for various design and drafting applications.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn 2D engineering, 3D modeling, animation, and assembly using CATIA software.
*   The course offers a flexible, self-paced learning environment with 24/7 access to materials.
*   It emphasizes project-based training, focusing on real-world design tasks.
*   Upon successful completion, candidates receive an e-certificate from Diyguru.

## Course Overview

The Diyguru CATIA certification course is designed to provide comprehensive guidance in using CATIA software. It adopts a task-based approach, ensuring learners grasp the practical application of the tools. Starting from fundamental concepts, the curriculum covers the entire workflow within CATIA, including creating and manipulating 2D engineering drawings, developing 3D models, understanding animation principles, and assembling components.

The program focuses on project-based learning, which means students will tackle real-world design problems. This hands-on method helps them understand various commands, menus, and options as they complete specific design tasks. The goal is to build confidence and proficiency in using CATIA software, enhancing participants' skill sets and improving their career prospects in design and engineering fields.

## Program Structure and Learning Experience

The course is delivered through a blend of virtual classroom and self-study modules, primarily utilizing video and text-based content. It offers a highly flexible learning environment, allowing students 24/7 access to all course materials. The estimated learning effort is approximately 3-5 hours per week, making it manageable for those with other commitments.

Key features of the program include:

*   **Online Access:** Learn anytime, anywhere, with 24/7 access to video lectures and study materials.
*   **Experiential Learning:** The curriculum integrates real-world problem statements to facilitate practical skill development.
*   **Progress Evaluation:** Regular evaluations are conducted to monitor and assess the candidate's learning progress.
*   **E-learning:** The entire course is available digitally, providing a complete e-learning experience.

## Eligibility Criteria

Diyguru's CATIA certification course welcomes a diverse range of applicants:

*   **Academic Background:** Students holding a B.E. or B.Tech degree, or an equivalent diploma in fields like Mechanical, Mechatronics, Automotive, EEE, ECE, or Instrumentation, are eligible.
*   **Work Experience:** Professionals working in industries such as automotive, auto components, R&D, design, analysis, project management, manufacturing, or maintenance can enroll to enhance their skills.
*   **Enthusiasts:** Even individuals without mandatory academic qualifications but with a keen interest in design are encouraged to apply.

To earn the certificate, candidates must complete 100% of their online modules, demonstrating competency in CATIA Computer-Aided Design and Drafting. Upon successful completion, an e-certificate will be issued by Diyguru.

## Course Fees

The fee structure for the Certification Course in CATIA- Computer-Aided Design and Drafting is as follows:

| Fee Category    | Actual Price (INR) | Discounted Price (INR) |
| :-------------- | :----------------- | :--------------------- |
| Programme Fee   | ₹7,500 + GST       | ₹2,999 + GST           |

*Please note that the discounted price may be available for a limited period.* The fee includes access to all course materials and the final e-certificate upon successful completion.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, participants will gain a strong understanding of engineering design principles and practical expertise in CATIA software. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Sketching:** Proficiency in creating and editing 2D sketch curves.
*   **Drafting:** Developed skills in technical drafting.
*   **3D Modeling:** Ability to create and manipulate 3D models.
*   **Assembly Design:** Knowledge of assembling multiple components.
*   **Animation:** Basic understanding of animation within CATIA.

This course is designed to empower you with the skills needed to confidently navigate CATIA software and apply it to various design challenges.
