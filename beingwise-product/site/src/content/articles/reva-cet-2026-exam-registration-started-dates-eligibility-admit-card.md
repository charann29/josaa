# REVA CET 2026 Exam - Registration (Started), Dates, Eligibility, Admit Card, Syllabus, Pattern, Result

REVA CET is an entrance examination conducted by REVA University, Bengaluru, for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide covers essential details for prospective students aiming for the 2026 academic year.

## Key takeaways

*   The registration process for REVA CET 2026 has officially begun, offering both online and offline application options.
*   Candidates must complete a basic registration, fill in personal and academic details, and pay the application fee to finalize their submission.
*   The application fee varies by program level: INR 500 for undergraduate courses and INR 1000 for postgraduate courses.
*   It's crucial to review all entered details before final submission, as corrections are only possible before the form is locked.

## REVA CET 2026 Application Process

The application window for REVA CET 2026 is now open. Aspiring students can choose to apply either through the university's online portal or by obtaining a physical application form. The process involves several steps to ensure all necessary information is captured.

### Online Application Steps

1.  **Visit the Admissions Portal:** Navigate to the official REVA University admissions website.
2.  **Initial Registration:** Provide fundamental details such as your name (as it appears on your Class 10 certificate), date of birth, desired faculty and program, email address, and mobile number.
3.  **Password Receipt:** Following successful basic registration, a unique password will be sent to your registered email address.
4.  **Login and Detail Entry:** Use your registered email ID and the received password to log in. Proceed to fill in comprehensive personal and academic information.
5.  **Fee Payment:** Submit the application fee. This can be done through various digital payment methods, including IMPS, NEFT, UPI, or Net Banking.
6.  **Application Number:** After successful payment, an application number will be generated. This crucial number will also be sent to you via SMS and email. Keep it safe for future reference.

### Offline Application Steps

Candidates preferring a physical form can purchase it directly from the Office of Admissions at REVA University. The cost for the offline application form is INR 1000, which includes the application fee. Once filled out manually, the form must be submitted back to the University office.

### Application Fee

*   **Undergraduate Programs:** INR 500
*   **Postgraduate Programs:** INR 1000

## Correcting Application Details

If you need to make any changes to your application form, this is only possible before you submit it definitively. To amend details, log in using your registered mobile number, email ID, and the application number. All corrections must be finalized before the official submission deadline.

## REVA CET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility requirements for REVA CET vary depending on the specific program (UG or PG) you are applying for. Generally, candidates need to have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) for undergraduate admissions and a relevant bachelor's degree for postgraduate programs. Specific percentage requirements and subject combinations will be detailed on the official REVA University admissions portal.

## REVA CET 2026 Admit Card, Syllabus, Pattern & Result

### Admit Card

The REVA CET 2026 admit card will be released on the official website closer to the examination date. It is a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall. Candidates will typically need to log in using their application credentials to download and print it.

### Syllabus and Pattern

The syllabus for REVA CET 2026 will generally align with the core subjects relevant to the chosen program. For engineering courses, this usually includes Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. The exam pattern will consist of multiple-choice questions. Detailed information regarding the syllabus and exam structure will be published on the official REVA University website.

### Result

The results for REVA CET 2026 will be announced on the official REVA University portal. Candidates can check their scores by logging in with their application details. Post-result, successful candidates will be invited for counselling and admission procedures.

Prepare thoroughly and ensure all application steps are completed accurately for a smooth admission journey.
