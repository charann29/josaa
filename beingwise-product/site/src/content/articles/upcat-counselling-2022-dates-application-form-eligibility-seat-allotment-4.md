# UPCAT Counselling 2022: Dates, Application Form, Eligibility, Seat Allotment

For students aspiring to join various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Uttar Pradesh, the admission process previously handled by UPCET has undergone significant changes. This guide will clarify the new pathways for admissions in 2022 and beyond for state universities like Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), and Harcourt Butler Technical University (HBTU).

## Key takeaways

*   The UPCET exam has been discontinued for admissions from 2022 onwards.
*   Admissions to UG and PG courses in UP state universities are now primarily through national-level exams like CUET, JEE Main, and NATA.
*   Specific courses like B.Tech, B.Arch, B.Des, B.Pharm, and their lateral entry equivalents will use JEE Main, NATA, or CUET scores.
*   Postgraduate courses such as MBA, MCA, M.Tech, M.Pharm, M.Arch, and M.Des, along with integrated MBA/MCA, will primarily use CUET PG or CUET scores.

## Evolution of UPCET Admissions

Previously known as UPSEE and later UPCET, this state-level entrance examination served as the gateway for admissions to numerous professional courses across various universities in Uttar Pradesh. The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducted this computer-based test annually. However, from 2022, the state has transitioned to accepting scores from broader national entrance examinations, streamlining the application process for many students.

## New Admission Pathways for 2022

With the discontinuation of UPCET, candidates seeking admission to affiliated institutions in Uttar Pradesh must now appear for relevant national entrance exams. Here’s a breakdown of which exams are applicable for different course types:

*   **Undergraduate Courses (B.Tech, B.Arch, B.Des, B.Pharm, BHMCT, BFAD/BFA, B.Tech Lateral Entry, B.Pharm Lateral Entry):** Admissions for these programs will primarily be based on scores from exams like JEE Main (for B.Tech), NATA (for B.Arch), and CUET (for other UG courses like B.Des, B.Pharm, BHMCT, BFAD/BFA, and lateral entry options).
*   **Postgraduate Courses (MBA, MCA, M.Tech, MURP, M.Pharm, M.Des, M.Arch, Integrated MBA, Integrated MCA):** For postgraduate and integrated master's programs, the primary entrance exam will be CUET PG or CUET, depending on the specific course.

Students are advised to refer to the official websites of AKTU, MMMUT, and HBTU for the most current and detailed admission notifications, including specific exam requirements and counselling schedules for 2022 and beyond.

## Eligibility Criteria Overview

While the entrance exam has changed, the fundamental eligibility criteria for admission to Uttar Pradesh universities generally remain consistent. These typically include:

*   **No Age Limit:** There is generally no upper age restriction for applicants.
*   **Domicile Requirements:** Candidates must either have passed their qualifying examination from an institution located in Uttar Pradesh, or their parents must hold a domicile certificate of Uttar Pradesh. Even if the domicile requirement is not met, candidates can still be eligible if they fulfill other specified criteria.
*   **Academic Qualification:** Applicants must have passed the relevant qualifying examination (e.g., 10+2 for UG courses, a bachelor's degree for PG courses) or be in their final year awaiting results. Specific minimum marks in the qualifying examination may apply depending on the course and university.

It is crucial to consult the admission brochures released by the respective universities for precise eligibility details for each program.

## Counselling and Seat Allotment

Following the national entrance examinations, the counselling and seat allotment process will be managed by the state's counselling authority, likely AKTU, for admissions to various colleges. This process typically involves online registration, choice filling, document verification, and multiple rounds of seat allotment based on merit and reservation policies. Candidates will need to stay updated with the official counselling portal for dates and procedures, including specific rounds for different courses and categories.

Navigating university admissions can seem complex, but by understanding the new pathways through national exams, you can prepare effectively for your higher education journey in Uttar Pradesh.
