ExcelR Solutions offers a range of programs designed to equip students and professionals with in-demand skills, particularly in the rapidly evolving fields of data science and artificial intelligence. These courses aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements, preparing learners for successful careers.

## Key Takeaways

*   ExcelR Solutions collaborates with Steinbeis University, Berlin, and Steinbeis Global Institute, Tubingen, for some of its advanced programs.
*   Programs cover essential skills in data science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and various programming languages.
*   Learning formats include virtual classroom sessions, self-paced training, and access to e-learning resources.
*   Courses are taught by experienced industry professionals, often with backgrounds from top-tier institutions.

## Postgraduate Program in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

This specialized postgraduate program, offered in collaboration with Steinbeis University, Berlin, and Steinbeis Global Institute, Tubingen, is an 11-month intensive course. It focuses on integrating data science with artificial intelligence, recognizing the growing demand for professionals skilled in both areas. The curriculum is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical insights, preparing students to become successful practitioners in this dynamic field. The program aims to address the current shortage of adequately skilled professionals by providing a robust learning experience.

**Skills covered in this program include:**

*   Python Programming
*   Data Science Fundamentals
*   Artificial Intelligence Concepts
*   SQL
*   Data Mining Techniques
*   Microsoft Excel
*   Amazon Web Services (AWS)
*   Apache Spark
*   R Programming

## Data Science Course Training in Delhi

Recognizing the surge in data generation and the subsequent demand for data professionals due to digitalization, ExcelR Solutions offers a dedicated Data Science Course Training in Delhi. This program is designed to enhance the capabilities of individuals aspiring to excel in data science roles. The course aims to bring students' expertise up to the level of an experienced data science professional.

**The syllabus comprehensively covers concepts such as:**

*   Data Cleansing
*   Data Transformation
*   Data Collection
*   Data Mining
*   Data Extraction
*   Data Visualization

**It also teaches various skills and techniques, including:**

*   Python Programming
*   Machine Learning
*   Hypothesis Testing
*   Neural Networks
*   Regression Modeling
*   Microsoft Azure
*   R Programming
