# MAH MBA CET Cutoff for SIMSREE Mumbai (Expected): Check Round-wise Previous Year Cutoffs

For aspiring MBA students in Maharashtra, the Sydenham Institute of Management Studies, Research, and Entrepreneurship Education (SIMSREE) in Mumbai is a highly sought-after institution. Gaining admission often hinges on a strong performance in the MAH MBA CET, with cutoff scores consistently remaining very high. This page will help you understand the expected cutoff for SIMSREE and review previous years' trends.

## Key Takeaways

*   SIMSREE Mumbai's MAH MBA CET cutoff is anticipated to be around the 99.97 percentile for the upcoming academic year.
*   Admission to SIMSREE's Master of Management Studies (MMS) program is primarily through the Centralized Admission Process (CAP) conducted by the Maharashtra State CET Cell.
*   The institute also considers scores from other national-level MBA entrance exams like CMAT, CAT, MAT, and ATMA, with similarly high cutoffs.
*   Competition for the roughly 180 available seats in the MMS program is intense, with thousands of applicants each year.

## Understanding SIMSREE and its MMS Program

SIMSREE is a prestigious management institute affiliated with the University of Mumbai, renowned for its academic excellence. The flagship offering is the two-year, full-time Master of Management Studies (MMS) program. This program allows students to specialize in various key areas, including Marketing, Finance, Operations, Human Resources, and Systems, preparing them for diverse roles in the corporate world.

## SIMSREE Eligibility and Admission Process

To be considered for the MMS program at SIMSREE, candidates must meet specific eligibility criteria, typically including a bachelor's degree with a minimum aggregate score (usually 50% for general category and 45% for reserved categories). The primary route for admission is through the Centralized Admission Process (CAP) managed by the Maharashtra State CET Cell, which allocates seats based on MAH MBA CET scores. While MAH MBA CET is the main gateway, SIMSREE also accepts scores from other prominent entrance exams such as CAT, CMAT, MAT, and ATMA, though the cutoffs for these exams are also quite competitive.
