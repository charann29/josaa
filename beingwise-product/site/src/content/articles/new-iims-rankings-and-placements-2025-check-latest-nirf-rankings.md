# New IIMs Rankings & Placements 2025 - Check Latest NIRF Rankings & Salary Offers of 14 New IIMs

India's Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) are highly sought-after institutions for management education. While the older, established IIMs are well-known, the newer IIMs are rapidly gaining recognition for their quality education and promising career opportunities.

## Key Takeaways

*   The fourteen newer IIMs, established from 2008 onwards, are increasingly competitive in terms of academic rigor and placement outcomes.
*   Many of these newer IIMs consistently feature in the top 50 B-schools in India according to the NIRF rankings.
*   They attract significant interest from leading companies, offering strong placement records with competitive salary packages.
*   These institutions provide a valuable pathway to a high-quality MBA for students aiming for top management careers.

## Understanding the IIM Landscape

The IIM journey began in 1961 with the establishment of IIM Ahmedabad and IIM Calcutta. Over the years, the network expanded to six prominent campuses. A significant expansion phase occurred between 2008 and 2015, introducing fourteen new IIMs across the country.

The oldest among these newer institutions is IIM Shillong, founded in 2008. This was followed by IIM Ranchi and IIM Rohtak in 2009, and IIM Raipur in 2010. In 2011, IIM Kashipur, IIM Trichy, and IIM Udaipur were established. The year 2015 saw the launch of six more IIMs: Sambalpur, Sirmaur, Bodh Gaya, Nagpur, Amritsar, and Visakhapatnam. IIM Jammu began its operations in 2016, mentored by IIM Lucknow. More recently, NITIE was renamed IIM Mumbai in 2023, and IIM Guwahati is set to welcome its first full-time MBA batch in 2026.

## Why Consider a New IIM?

Choosing a newer IIM offers several advantages. These institutions benefit from modern infrastructure, evolving curricula that are responsive to industry needs, and often smaller batch sizes, which can lead to more personalized attention. They are actively building their alumni networks and establishing strong industry connections, providing excellent opportunities for career growth.

The newer IIMs present compelling options for students aiming for quality management education and promising career trajectories, continually striving to match the standards set by their older counterparts.
