# UNext Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online UNext Learning Courses

UNext Learning, an initiative from Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), provides a platform for various online courses and certifications designed to help students and professionals enhance their skills. These programs often combine academic rigor with practical, industry-relevant training.

## Key Takeaways

*   UNext Learning offers a range of online certification programs, often in collaboration with institutions like MAHE and Jigsaw Academy.
*   Programs cover high-demand fields such as Data Science, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity.
*   Many courses are designed for individuals with varying levels of prior experience, including those new to programming.
*   Curricula emphasize hands-on learning, practical tools, and industry expert interaction.

## Understanding UNext Learning

UNext Learning serves as the online education arm of the esteemed Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE). Its primary goal is to make quality education accessible to a wider audience through digital platforms. By partnering with various academic and industry experts, UNext Learning curates programs that are not only academically sound but also aligned with current industry needs and future trends.

## Featured Postgraduate Certification Programs

UNext Learning currently highlights several postgraduate certification programs that cater to the growing demand for specialized skills in technology and data. These programs are structured to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical application, ensuring participants are well-equipped for their chosen careers.

### Postgraduate Certification Program in Data Science and Machine Learning

This 9-month virtual program is a key offering, often delivered in collaboration with institutions like Jigsaw Academy and MAHE. It's specifically designed to transform individuals with or without prior programming experience into skilled data science and machine learning professionals. Admission typically involves an online assessment and video interview process to gauge suitability.

The curriculum dives deep into essential topics such as neural networks, artificial intelligence, machine learning algorithms, data visualization, and statistical data analysis. Participants gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools like SQL and Python. A significant feature of this program is its focus on practical application, allowing students to work with the latest software and applications in data analytics. The program aims to prepare participants for relevant job opportunities across various sectors, benefiting from insights shared by leading industry minds.

**Key Details:**

*   **Duration:** 9 months
*   **Fee:** ₹1,50,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Machine Learning, Data Science, Neural Networks, AI, Data Visualization, Statistical Analysis, SQL, Python.

### Post Graduate Certificate Program in Cybersecurity

Another significant offering through UNext Learning, often in collaboration with Jigsaw Academy, is the Post Graduate Certificate Program in Cybersecurity. This program addresses the critical need for skilled professionals in protecting digital assets and information.

**Key Details:**

*   **Duration:** 8 months
*   **Fee:** ₹1,75,000
*   **Focus:** Core cybersecurity principles, threat detection, and prevention strategies.
