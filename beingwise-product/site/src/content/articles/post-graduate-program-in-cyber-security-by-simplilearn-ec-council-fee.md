# Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security by Simplilearn EC-Council: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a strong foundation in cybersecurity, the Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security, offered by Simplilearn in collaboration with EC-Council and with modules from MIT Schwarzman College of Computing, provides a comprehensive learning path. This program is designed to equip individuals with essential skills to protect digital assets and understand the complexities of cyber threats.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program spans six months, with classes conveniently scheduled on weekends.
*   It incorporates learning modules developed by MIT Schwarzman College of Computing and EC-Council.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive multiple certifications, including an Executive Programme certificate from MIT Schwarzman College of Computing and a Certified Ethical Hacker certificate from EC-Council.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹122,499, inclusive of GST, with EMI options available.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

This online Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security is structured over six months, primarily through virtual classroom sessions held on weekends. The curriculum covers critical areas such as data protection, risk analysis, compliance, mitigation strategies, and cloud-based security. The learning methodology combines video and text-based content, ensuring a flexible yet thorough educational experience.

The program includes over 110 hours of instructor-led training and an additional 40 hours of e-learning content, culminating in more than 150 hours of blended learning. Participants will engage with six assessments and various industry-based case studies to apply their knowledge practically. A notable instructor for the course is Nickolai Zeldovich, the Edwin Sibley Webster Professor at MIT CSAIL, whose expertise lies in computer security, applied cryptography, and computer architecture.

## Certifications and Career Advancement

Successfully completing the program leads to a trifecta of valuable certifications. Participants will earn a Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security Certification from Simplilearn, alongside an Executive Programme certificate from MIT Schwarzman College of Computing. Additionally, they will receive an industry-recognized Certified Ethical Hacker Certificate from EC-Council, a significant credential in the cybersecurity field. The program also offers a job assistance program and includes capstone projects to enhance practical industry knowledge.

## Eligibility and Application Details

To be eligible for the Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security, candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks. Prior experience in programming or professional work experience is not a mandatory requirement, making it accessible to a broader range of aspirants. Interested individuals can apply directly through the official Simplilearn portal.

## Program Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security is ₹122,499, which includes all applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST). Simplilearn offers convenient payment solutions, including no-cost EMI options, making the program more accessible financially. For instance, EMI payments can be as low as ₹5,485 per month.
