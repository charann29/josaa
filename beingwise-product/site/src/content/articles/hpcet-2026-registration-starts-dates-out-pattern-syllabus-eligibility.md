# HPCET 2026: Registration (STARTS), Dates (OUT), Pattern, Syllabus, Eligibility, Admission Process

The Himachal Pradesh Common Entrance Test (HPCET) 2026 is an annual examination conducted by Himachal Pradesh Technical University (HPTU) for admissions to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide covers all essential details for prospective students, from registration to the final admission process.

## Key takeaways

*   HPCET 2026 application forms are now available online on the official HPTU website.
*   The last date to register for HPCET 2026 is April 19, 2026.
*   The exam will be held offline on May 10, 2026, as a pen-and-paper test.
*   Beyond HPCET, B.Tech admissions also consider JEE Main scores and Class 12 marks.

HPTU has released the complete schedule for HPCET 2026. Candidates should mark these dates carefully to ensure they do not miss any deadlines.

## Eligibility Criteria for HPCET 2026

To be eligible for HPCET 2026, candidates must meet specific academic and domicile requirements. For B.Tech programs, typically, candidates must have passed their 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects from a recognized board. Specific percentage requirements and age limits, if any, will be detailed in the official information brochure on the HPTU website.

## HPCET 2026 Application Process

Registration for HPCET 2026 has begun, and the application form is accessible exclusively online through the official HPTU website, himtu.ac.in. No other method of application will be accepted. Candidates need to fill out the form carefully, upload necessary documents, and pay the application fee. The fee structure is INR 1,600 for the General category and INR 800 for SC/ST/BPL categories.

### Steps to Apply:

1.  Visit the official HPTU portal.
2.  Locate the HPCET 2026 application link.
3.  Register by providing basic details to create login credentials.
4.  Fill in the application form with personal and academic information.
5.  Upload scanned copies of required documents (photograph, signature, etc.).
6.  Pay the application fee online.
7.  Submit the form and take a printout for future reference.

## HPCET 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The HPCET 2026 examination for B.Tech aspirants will be a pen-and-paper-based test. It will consist of 150 multiple-choice questions (MCQs) to be completed within 3 hours and 15 minutes. The paper will be divided into three sections: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

*   **Total Questions:** 150
*   **Sections:** Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
*   **Duration:** 3 hours 15 minutes
*   **Marking Scheme:** +4 marks for each correct answer, -1 mark for each incorrect answer.
*   **Medium:** English

The syllabus for HPCET typically aligns with the Class 11 and 12 curriculum of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Candidates are advised to refer to the official HPCET syllabus document released by HPTU for detailed topic lists.

## Admission Process

Following the HPCET 2026 examination, results will be declared tentatively by May 21, 2026. Qualified candidates will then need to register for the counselling process. Admissions to various programs in participating institutions across Himachal Pradesh will be based on HPCET scores. For B.Tech admissions specifically, HPTU also considers JEE Main scores and marks obtained in the Class 12 qualifying examination, providing multiple pathways for students.

For the most precise and up-to-date information, always refer to the official HPTU website.
