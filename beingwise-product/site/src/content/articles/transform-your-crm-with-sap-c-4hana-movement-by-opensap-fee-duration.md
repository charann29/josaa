# Transform Your CRM With Sap C/4hana Movement by openSAP: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For professionals looking to enhance their customer relationship management (CRM) skills, the "Transform Your CRM with SAP C/4HANA Movement" course by openSAP offers a valuable opportunity. This program is designed to help you understand how to transition traditional, on-premise SAP CRM systems to modern, cloud-based solutions like SAP Commerce and SAP Marketing.

## Key takeaways

*   **Free and Flexible Learning:** The entire course is available at no cost and can be completed at your own pace, fitting into your schedule.
*   **Expert-Led Content:** Learn directly from experienced faculty at openSAP through video lectures and text materials.
*   **Dual Certification Options:** Depending on your performance, you can earn either a 'Confirmation of Participation' or a 'Record of Achievement'.
*   **Practical Cloud Migration Skills:** Gain insights into the business benefits and technical steps involved in moving CRM solutions to the cloud.

## Course Structure and Delivery

This self-paced program spans four weeks, delivered through a combination of video units and text-based content. It is entirely in English, with subtitles provided to assist learners. You can expect to dedicate approximately 2-3 hours per week to the course material. The flexible format means you can access the learning materials from anywhere in the world, at any time that suits you.

## What You Will Learn

The course focuses on equipping you with the knowledge needed to effectively manage and transform CRM systems using SAP C/4HANA. By the end of the program, you will understand:

*   The core capabilities and processes within SAP C/4HANA.
*   The strategic business case for migrating CRM to the cloud.
*   Practical methods for moving on-premise SAP CRM solutions to a cloud environment.
*   Strategies for delivering an exceptional customer experience.
*   How to stay current with the latest customer experience trends using SAP C/4HANA.
*   Available tools, services, and assets that facilitate a smooth cloud migration.

## Who Is This Course For?

This certification training is ideal for various professionals involved in CRM, particularly those with a foundational understanding of the SAP CRM application. It is especially beneficial for:

*   CRM application consultants.
*   CRM technology consultants.
*   Professionals working in all areas of CRM.
*   Business decision-makers involved in CRM strategy.

## Certification and Fees

There is no fee to enroll in and complete the "Transform Your CRM with SAP C/4HANA Movement" online course. Certification is available from openSAP, with two tiers:

| Certificate Type             | Requirements                                                                                |
| :--------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Confirmation of Participation | Completion of at least 50% of the course content.                                           |
| Record of Achievement        | Scoring 50% or higher on all peer-reviewed assignments, graded assignments, and the final exam. |

Should you wish to re-access graded assignments or take the final assignments after the initial period, a reactivation option is available.

## How to Apply

To register for the "Transform Your CRM with SAP C/4HANA Movement" program, you will need to visit the official course page on openSAP. Simply navigate to the specific course URL and follow the registration steps provided there.

This program offers a fantastic opportunity to stay ahead in the evolving world of customer relationship management and cloud technology.
