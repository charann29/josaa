## CFP® Certification Training Program

The CFP® Certification Training Program is a globally recognized qualification in financial planning. This certification is highly valued in the financial services sector, opening doors to excellent employment opportunities. The program at IMS Proschool is structured to thoroughly prepare you for the CFP® exam and other related financial planning certifications.

Students can choose from several study formats to suit their learning preferences and availability:

*   **Classroom Program:** Features weekend batches, making it convenient for those balancing studies with other commitments.
*   **Live Virtual Classroom:** Offers interactive online sessions.
*   **Self-Paced Learning:** Provides flexibility to study at your own speed.

The program culminates in a computer-based examination comprising multiple-choice questions and case studies. Successful completion of this exam allows you to apply for the official CFP® certification, maximizing the benefits of your training.

*   **Duration:** 15 Months
*   **Approximate Fee:** ₹ 60,000
*   **Key Skills:** Financial Management

## ACCA Course: Skill Level

The ACCA Course: Skill Level certification, provided by IMS Proschool, is an advanced program focused on a broad spectrum of business and accounting principles. It is designed to equip students with essential skills required in the global accounting and finance industry.

This course offers two primary study methods:

*   **Offline Classroom Program:** Traditional in-person instruction.
*   **Live Online Training:** Delivered interactively on the CISCO platform, featuring expert educators qualified by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA).

Learning in the ACCA Skills Level training involves a mix of recorded video lectures, examinations, assignments, mock tests, and live classes. Students also receive faculty feedback to help improve their performance. The curriculum is divided into six distinct modules, covering areas such as Financial Management, Audit and Assurance, Taxation, and Financial Reporting. Upon meeting specific criteria, students can also earn an advanced diploma in accounting and business.

*   **Duration:** 15 Months
*   **Approximate Fee:** ₹ 1,25,000
*   **Key Skills:** Decision-making, Financial Management, Taxation Knowledge

This program is ideal for individuals looking to enter investment advisory and management roles. It covers financial planning from basic concepts through high-quality video sessions. The course also prepares participants for other benchmark industry certifications. It is suitable for undergraduates, graduates, and working professionals aspiring to a career in financial planning. IMS Proschool also offers placement support to help graduates secure employment opportunities.
