# Informatica Big Data Edition Training by Mindmajix Technologies: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen on mastering big data technologies, Mindmajix Technologies offers a specialized online training program in Informatica Big Data Edition. This course aims to equip learners with essential skills for handling large datasets using Informatica tools within a Hadoop environment.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program provides comprehensive training in Informatica Big Data Edition, covering topics from Hadoop ingestion to data warehouse optimization.
*   It offers flexible learning options: live online sessions or self-paced video access, with 24/7 lifetime support.
*   Practical skills are developed through 20 hours of lab sessions and real-time project use cases.
*   Upon completion, participants receive a certificate and placement assistance, including interview preparation.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This training delves deep into the capabilities of Informatica Big Data, focusing on how to integrate and process massive amounts of data within a Hadoop framework. Participants will learn to utilize ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) tools in a Hadoop environment, implement data mappings on Hadoop clusters, and optimize data warehouse processing. The curriculum also covers the use of Informatica PowerCenter and Developer tools for building Hadoop information stores.

The course emphasizes practical application, with dedicated lab sessions totaling 20 hours. This hands-on approach ensures that learners gain tangible experience in applying theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios.

## Flexible Learning and Support

Mindmajix Technologies provides multiple enrollment options to suit different learning preferences and schedules. Students can choose between interactive online live training sessions or a self-paced learning model, which offers lifetime access to video content. The program is designed to be highly flexible, with classes available on both weekdays and weekends.

To ensure a smooth learning journey, participants benefit from 24/7 lifetime support. This includes one-on-one doubt clearing sessions, which are invaluable for addressing specific questions and challenges. A free demo is also available upon request, allowing prospective students to experience the course content and teaching style before committing.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to provide a thorough understanding of big data concepts and their application through Informatica. Key topics include:

*   **Introduction to Informatica Big Data Edition:** Fundamental concepts and architecture.
*   **Parameters:** Understanding different parameter types, binding, and parameterizable properties.
*   **Mapping and Developer Enhancements:** Exploring intended uses, dynamic schemas, dynamic ports, input rules, and dynamic expressions.
*   **Dynamic Mappings:** Practical application of dynamic schemas, ports, and expressions.
*   **Entity Extraction and Data Classification on Hadoop:** Techniques for processing unstructured data.
*   **Mixed Workflows:** Designing and implementing complex data flows.
*   **Partitioned Mappings:** Optimizing performance with MxN partitioning and configurable parallelism.
*   **Data Processor Transformation:** Installing libraries, using library objects, and running mapplets.

Additionally, the program includes practice tests and interview questions to prepare learners for career opportunities in big data development.

## Certification and Career Assistance

Upon successful completion of the training, participants receive a certificate directly from Mindmajix Technologies. Beyond certification, the program extends its support to career advancement. This includes assistance with job placement and comprehensive preparation for interviews, helping graduates confidently step into roles as big data developers.

This training serves as a robust foundation for anyone looking to build or advance their career in the dynamic field of big data.
