# Marketing Launchpad Part Time by Kraftshala School of Business: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students, recent graduates, and working professionals looking to dive into the world of digital marketing without hitting pause on their current commitments, Kraftshala School of Business offers a part-time program designed to build practical, industry-ready skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   An 11-month online program with live weekend sessions and weekday project work.
*   Focuses on hands-on learning with real budgets on platforms like Google Ads and Meta Ads.
*   Includes a unique job placement accountability, offering a 60% fee refund if placement is below ₹4.5 LPA.
*   The curriculum integrates AI tools and is regularly updated to meet current industry demands.

## Program Overview and Structure

This part-time digital marketing course is crafted for individuals eager to gain robust marketing skills. The program spans 11 months, structured with live virtual sessions primarily on weekends, complemented by structured project work during the weekdays. This format allows participants to balance their learning with existing studies or jobs.

The core of the curriculum revolves around practical marketing execution, data-driven decision-making, creative strategy, and performance marketing. Participants engage with live brand projects, applying concepts directly on major platforms such as Google Ads, Meta Ads, various e-commerce tools, and programmatic advertising. This hands-on approach ensures that learners develop analytical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills vital for modern marketing roles.

## What You Will Learn

The Marketing Launchpad Part Time program covers a comprehensive range of digital marketing competencies, blending technical expertise with essential workplace skills. Key areas of learning include:

*   **Digital Advertising:** Mastering platforms like Meta Ads, Google Ads (Search, Display, YouTube, Shopping).
*   **E-commerce Marketing:** Including Amazon Ads and Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO).
*   **Programmatic Advertising:** Utilizing tools such as DV360.
*   **Data Analysis:** Developing proficiency in advanced Excel for marketing data interpretation.
*   **Content Management & SEO:** Understanding strategies for organic reach.
*   **Problem-Solving & Communication:** Enhancing critical thinking and presentation skills crucial for professional roles.

The program emphasizes a practical learning approach, with approximately 70% of the curriculum dedicated to hands-on projects where students manage real budgets for brands. This ensures that graduates are well-prepared for roles as digital marketing analysts or executives.

## Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Marketing Launchpad Part Time course is ₹1,75,820 plus 18% GST. Kraftshala School of Business offers flexible EMI options through its partners to make the program more accessible. Here's a breakdown of potential monthly payments based on different durations:

## Job Placement and Accountability

This program is designed with a strong focus on career outcomes, acting as a job-linked initiative with placement accountability. A distinctive feature is the 60% fee refund policy: if a participant is not placed in a role with an annual package of ₹4.5 Lakhs or above, 60% of their course fee is refunded. This commitment underscores the program's dedication to preparing learners for successful entry into digital marketing careers.

Preparation for job placement includes industry-reviewed projects, personalized feedback, and structured career readiness support, moving beyond theoretical knowledge to practical, job-ready skills.

To apply for the program, interested individuals, whether freshers or working professionals from any field or educational background, should demonstrate a strong interest and clear intent to build a career in marketing.
