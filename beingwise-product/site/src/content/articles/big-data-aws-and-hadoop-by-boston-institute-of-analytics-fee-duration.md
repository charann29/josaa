# Big Data, AWS and Hadoop by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to master the complexities of large datasets and make data-driven decisions, the Boston Institute of Analytics (BIA) offers a specialized certification in Big Data, AWS, and Hadoop. This program aims to equip participants with practical skills that are directly relevant to current industry demands.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is designed for a duration of four months.
*   It offers a dual certification from the Boston Institute of Analytics.
*   Learning modes include a blend of virtual classroom sessions, self-study, and video/text-based content.
*   The curriculum covers essential aspects of big data analytics, including AWS and Hadoop.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The BIA's Big Data, AWS, and Hadoop certification is structured to provide a comprehensive and practical learning journey. It focuses on giving students the tools to analyze vast amounts of data, identify patterns, and ultimately make informed business decisions. The program emphasizes hands-on learning, live lectures, and practical exercises, ensuring that participants gain real-world experience.

Upon completing the course, you will have a deep understanding of handling large datasets, recognizing data patterns and trends, and applying analytical techniques. The training is designed to help you navigate real-world challenges and develop expertise aligned with the latest industry practices in big data.

## Who Is This Course For?

This certification is particularly beneficial for professionals and individuals who wish to enhance their skills in big data analytics or transition into this dynamic field. It is well-suited for:

*   Data Analysts
*   Business Intelligence Analysts
*   Data Scientists
*   Analytics Managers
*   Business Analysts

## Course Syllabus Highlights

The syllabus is divided into several key modules, starting with foundational concepts and progressing to advanced techniques. Here's a glimpse into what you will learn:

### Big Data Analytics: Orientation

This introductory section covers the evolution of big data, its characteristics and challenges, and how cloud platforms are used for big data. It also includes practical sessions on setting up a cloud-based data environment.

### Data Processing and Analysis

Here, you will delve into data ingestion and processing, including an introduction to Apache Kafka for real-time data streaming and Apache Flink for stream processing. The module also covers advanced analysis techniques like deep learning, neural networks, TensorFlow, and Keras, with practical exercises on building real-time data pipelines and implementing deep learning models.

### Data Governance and Business Decision Making

This part of the course focuses on the critical aspects of data governance, including its importance, compliance regulations like GDPR and data privacy, and auditing and monitoring in big data projects.

## Fees and Certification

The Boston Institute of Analytics provides a certificate upon successful completion of the Big Data, AWS, and Hadoop course. Specific details regarding the course fee can be obtained during the admission process.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Big Data Analytics program at BIA, follow these straightforward steps:

1.  Visit the official Boston Institute of Analytics platform.
2.  Locate and select the "Enquire Now" option. Fill out the form with your contact details, and a representative will get in touch with you.
3.  After completing the necessary steps, pay the course fee to gain access to the learning modules.

This program is an excellent opportunity for anyone looking to gain a competitive edge in the rapidly expanding field of big data analytics.
