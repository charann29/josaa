# Intro to Interior Design Course: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone aspiring to shape spaces and create beautiful environments, a foundational course in interior design can be an excellent starting point. This particular program offers a comprehensive introduction to the field, covering everything from core concepts to practical project execution.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is delivered in English, making it accessible to a wide audience.
*   It's structured for self-paced learning, combining video and text-based materials.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate.
*   The curriculum spans fundamental design principles, technical drawing, and basic CAD software use.

## Course Structure and Learning Mode

This interior design course is designed for flexible, self-guided study. All instructional content is presented in English, featuring a mix of video lectures and written materials. This blended approach allows learners to engage with the material at their own pace and revisit complex topics as needed, fitting easily into various schedules.

## Fee and Certification

The fee for enrolling in this introductory interior design course is ₹2,799. Upon successfully completing all course requirements, participants are eligible to receive a certificate. This certificate is provided by the course platform, acknowledging your successful completion of the program's curriculum.

## Detailed Course Syllabus

The course is divided into several modules, guiding learners from basic definitions to practical project work. Here’s a breakdown of what you'll learn:

### Introduction and Core Concepts

*   Understanding the differences between interior design and interior decoration.
*   Exploring the various stages of the interior design process.
*   Identifying different interior design styles and their characteristics.
*   Familiarizing yourself with basic and optional home interior spaces, including extensions.
*   Learning fundamental and extended home designing and decorating principles.

### Foundational Interior Design Knowledge

*   Discovering essential tools used by designers.
*   Grasping key design elements and principles.
*   Developing inspiration and conceptualizing design ideas.
*   Understanding color themes and material selection.

### Advanced Design Understanding

*   Principles of plan, elevation, and section drawings.
*   Techniques for accurate measurement.
*   Understanding scale in design.
*   How to interpret floorplans effectively.

### Technical Drawing Skills

*   Definition and principles of technical drawing.
*   Hands-on practice with oblique, isometric, and perspective drawing techniques (1 and 2 vanishing points).
*   Creating floorplan and elevation drawings.
*   Introduction to CAD (Computer-Aided Design) concepts and sketching principles.

### Basic CAD Software Use

*   Introduction to fundamental operations in Photoshop.
*   Basic functionalities of AutoCAD.
*   Understanding more about CAD applications.
*   An overview of 3Ds Max basics.

### Sample Interior Design Project (Step-by-Step)

The course culminates in a practical, guided project, broken down into ten steps:

1.  **Space Surveying and Client Brief:** Initial client meeting and understanding project requirements.
2.  **Working with Floorplans:** Utilizing floorplans as a design foundation.
3.  **Furnishing Floorplan:** Guiding through furnishing assignments and reviewing solutions.
4.  **Researching Tips:** Techniques for effective design research.
5.  **Sketching:** Developing initial design sketches, including Photoshop enhancements.
6.  **Model Making Principles:** Understanding how to create design models.
7.  **Presentation Boards:** Preparing a first proposal presentation and organizing project files.
8.  **Technical Drawings with Furniture:** Drawing detailed sections with furniture layouts.
9.  **3D Modeling and Material Application:** Creating 3D floorplan models, editing materials, and placing furniture.
10. **Finalization:** Preparing the second and final project presentation board.

This course offers a well-rounded introduction for anyone eager to step into the world of interior design, equipping you with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills to begin your creative journey.
