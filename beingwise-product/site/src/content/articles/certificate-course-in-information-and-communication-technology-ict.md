# Certificate Course in Information & Communication Technology (ICT) in Rehabilitation by CBR Network via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This certificate program delves into how Information and Communication Technology (ICT) can transform the lives of individuals with special needs and disabilities. It explores the vital role ICT plays in fostering their educational and socio-economic inclusion, particularly in underserved communities.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn how ICT can empower people with disabilities for greater independence.
*   Understand the application of assistive technologies in daily life, education, and employment.
*   Explore universal accessibility standards and accessible design principles.
*   Gain insights into indigenous and innovative technologies for inclusive solutions.

## Course Overview

The Certificate Course in Information & Communication Technology (ICT) in Rehabilitation is designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of the ICT requirements of people with disabilities. The curriculum highlights how technology can facilitate access to essential services like transportation, employment, and education, and assist with routine tasks such as shopping, banking, and entertainment.

A significant focus is placed on addressing the needs of communities in rural and tribal areas, as well as developing countries, where access to such technologies can be particularly impactful. The course is delivered in English through a self-study model, primarily utilizing video and text-based content.

## Program Structure and Learning Experience

This six-month certificate program is credit-based, offering 28 course credits. Participants will engage with a rich array of digital learning materials, including e-texts, presentations, podcasts, and interactive activities. The program incorporates 224 hours of notional learning and 336 hours of practical curriculum, ensuring a thorough and hands-on learning experience.

To support learners, discussion forums are available for posting queries and doubts, and clarifications can also be sought via email. Upon successful completion, participants will receive a certificate from CBR Network.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in this certificate course, candidates must be currently in their fifth semester of an engineering or other technical degree program. The program is specifically tailored for students with a technical background interested in applying their skills to the field of rehabilitation.

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this program, participants will develop a strong understanding of various aspects of ICT in rehabilitation, including:

*   **Digital Tools and Assistive Technologies:** Proficiency in using and understanding digital tools and devices, particularly those based on electronics and mechanical engineering, that assist people with disabilities and senior citizens.
*   **Inclusive Technologies:** Knowledge of indigenous and innovative technological solutions that enhance the quality of life and facilitate inclusion.
*   **Accessibility Standards:** Familiarity with universal accessibility standards and principles of accessible design in construction.
*   **Software Applications:** Understanding of specialized software applications designed for individuals with special needs.

## Fees and Certification

This certificate course is offered free of charge. A completion certificate will be provided by CBR Network to all participants who successfully finish the program requirements.

## How to Apply

Interested candidates can join the online Certificate Course in Information & Communication Technology (ICT) in Rehabilitation by following these steps on the official Swayam portal:

1.  Navigate to the specific course page on Swayam.
2.  Click the 'Join' button.
3.  Log in to your existing Swayam account. If you don't have one, you can easily create an account or use your Google, Microsoft, or Facebook credentials to log in.
4.  Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your enrollment by filling in the necessary details.

This program is an excellent opportunity for technical students to contribute to a meaningful cause and develop skills in a growing, impactful field.
