# UPCAT Centres 2022: Check State-wise Exam Centres List

When preparing for the Uttar Pradesh Common Admission Test (UPCAT), knowing the available exam centres is crucial for smooth planning. This guide provides a comprehensive list of cities where the examination is conducted, both within Uttar Pradesh and in other states.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Wide Network:** UPCAT is conducted in numerous cities across Uttar Pradesh, alongside several major cities in other Indian states.
*   **Preference-Based Allotment:** Candidates are advised to select four preferred exam cities during the application process.
*   **Admit Card Confirmation:** The final allotted exam centre details will be clearly mentioned on the official admit card, typically released about 15 days before the exam.
*   **No Change Requests:** Once an exam centre is allotted, requests for changes are generally not entertained.

## Understanding UPCAT Exam Centres

The National Testing Agency (NTA) is responsible for releasing the list of UPCAT exam centres. This list helps candidates choose locations that are most convenient for them. During the application process, you will be prompted to indicate your preferred cities. The NTA then allocates centres based on these preferences and the availability of slots.

It's important to remember that UPCET (Uttar Pradesh Common Entrance Test) was the official name for this exam, which has since been referred to as UPCAT for admissions into various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. For B.Tech admissions specifically, scores from JEE Main are now primarily considered, rather than UPCAT.

## Programs Covered by UPCAT

UPCAT facilitates admissions to a diverse range of courses. These include, but are not limited to:

*   Bachelor of Hotel Management and Catering Technology (BHMCT)
*   Master of Urban and Regional Planning (MURP)
*   B.Tech Lateral Entry
*   Bachelor of Design (B.Des)
*   Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFAD/BFA)
*   Master of Computer Applications (MCA/MCA Integrated/MCA Lateral Entry)
*   Master of Technology (M.Tech)
*   Master of Design (M.Des)
*   Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm/B.Pharm Lateral)
*   Master of Architecture (M.Arch)
*   Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharma)
*   Master of Business Administration (MBA/MBA Integrated)

## UPCAT Admit Card and Centre Details

The official admit card, released by NTA on its website approximately two weeks before the examination, is your essential document for the UPCAT. It will contain crucial information, including your roll number, exam date and time, and the complete address of your allotted exam centre. Candidates must download and print their admit card, as entry to the examination hall will not be permitted without it.

Careful consideration of your preferred exam cities during the application process can significantly ease your test-day logistics. Always refer to the official NTA website for the most current and accurate information regarding UPCAT.
