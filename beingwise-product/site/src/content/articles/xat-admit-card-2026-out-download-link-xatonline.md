## Checking for Errors in Your XAT Admit Card

It is crucial to meticulously check all the details on your downloaded admit card. Any discrepancy could lead to issues at the test center. Pay close attention to the following:

*   **Name:** Ensure your name is spelled correctly and completely.
*   **Registration Number:** Verify that your XAT ID matches the one you received during registration.
*   **Date of Birth:** Confirm that your date of birth is accurate.
*   **PwD Status:** If applicable, ensure your Persons with Disability (PwD) status is correctly reflected.
*   **Exam Details:** Double-check the exam date, timing, and reporting time.
*   **Test City and Centre:** Confirm that the test city and the specific exam center address are as per your preference and correctly mentioned.

### How to Correct Errors in Your XAT Admit Card

Should you find any errors on your XAT 2026 admit card, it is imperative to contact the XAT exam helpdesk immediately. Do not delay, as corrections may take some time. The contact details for the helpdesk are usually available on the official XAT website.

## XAT Exam Day Instructions

The XAT examination is conducted across various centers in numerous cities. To ensure a smooth experience, candidates must adhere to specific guidelines:

*   **Arrival Time:** Arrive at the XAT examination center well before the reporting time mentioned on your admit card to complete the identification and verification process.
*   **Documents:** Present your printed admit card and a valid photo identification proof (such as Aadhar card, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving License) for verification.

*   **Technical Issues:** If you encounter any technical problems with your computer system, promptly inform the invigilator. Do not waste precious exam time trying to fix it yourself.
*   **Instructions:** Carefully read all the instructions provided before starting the test.
