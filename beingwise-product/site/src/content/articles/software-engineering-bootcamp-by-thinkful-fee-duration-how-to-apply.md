# Software Engineering Bootcamp by Thinkful: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Considering a career in software engineering? Thinkful offers a comprehensive bootcamp designed to equip you with the essential skills and career support needed to enter the tech industry. This program focuses on practical learning and personalized guidance.

## Key takeaways

*   Choose between full-time (5 months) and part-time (6 months) study options, both leading to a certificate.
*   The program emphasizes career readiness, offering extensive job search support including mock interviews, LinkedIn reviews, and resume help.
*   Flexible payment options are available, including upfront payment, monthly installments, and loan facilities.
*   No upfront fee is charged if you don't secure a job after completing the program.

## Program Overview

Thinkful's Software Engineering Bootcamp is crafted by industry experts to provide a solid foundation in programming. The curriculum covers fundamental concepts and advanced techniques, preparing students for real-world software development roles. Students benefit from an online learning environment that includes live video mentor sessions, workshops, and assignments. The program is delivered through a virtual classroom, combining video and text-based content.

## Course Structure and Duration

Students have the flexibility to choose between two distinct learning paths:

*   **Full-time program:** This intensive option spans 5 months. It includes a mandatory 4-week self-paced preparatory course before the main curriculum begins.
*   **Part-time program:** Designed for those balancing other commitments, this option extends over 6 months. Students can start immediately without a pre-course.

Both programs require an estimated 20-30 hours of learning effort per week and culminate in a certificate upon successful completion.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Software Engineering Bootcamp, applicants generally need to meet the following requirements:

*   Possess a high school diploma or an equivalent degree from an accredited institution recognized by the US Department of Education.
*   Demonstrate proficiency in English language skills.
*   Have basic computer operation skills.
*   For the full-time program, completion of a 4-week self-paced preparatory course is a prerequisite.

Successful completion of the course within the stipulated timeframe is necessary to qualify for the program certificate.

## Fees and Payment Options

The standard tuition fee for the Software Engineering Bootcamp is $9,975. Thinkful provides various payment structures to accommodate different financial situations:

| Payment Method | Cost |
| :--------------- | :----------- |
| Pay Upfront | $9,975 |
| Month-to-Month | $2,100/month |

Additionally, students can explore loan options to finance their education. A unique aspect of this program is its outcome-based payment model: if a student does not secure a job after completing the bootcamp, they are not required to pay the tuition fee. For precise details on payment plans and financing, it is advisable to consult the official Thinkful website.

## Career Support and Outcomes

A significant component of the Software Engineering Bootcamp is its robust career support system. Thinkful is committed to helping graduates launch their careers in software engineering. This support includes:

*   Individual and group coaching sessions.
*   Mock behavioral and technical interviews to hone interviewing skills.
*   Assistance with crafting compelling cover letters and optimizing LinkedIn profiles.
*   Workshops on various career-related topics and job search navigation strategies.
*   Curated technological content and personalized career guidance.

The curriculum is designed to teach students key industry practices, including REST software architecture, Agile methodologies, and techniques for impressing potential employers during the hiring process.

Embark on your journey to becoming a software engineer with a program that prioritizes both skill development and career placement.
