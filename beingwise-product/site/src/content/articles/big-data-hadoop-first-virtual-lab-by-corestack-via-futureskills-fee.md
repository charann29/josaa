# Big Data Hadoop - First Virtual Lab by CoreStack via FutureSkills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those keen to dive into the world of big data analytics, the Big Data Hadoop - First Virtual Lab certification course offers a practical pathway. Designed by CoreStack and available through FutureSkills Prime, this program focuses on equipping learners with essential skills in handling and analyzing large datasets using Hadoop technologies.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Hands-on Experience:** The course emphasizes practical application through an enterprise-grade virtual lab environment.
*   **Self-Paced Learning:** You can progress through the material at your own convenience, supported by video and text-based content.
*   **Comprehensive Coverage:** It delves into core big data components like Cloudera Manager, Hive, Spark, and Impala.
*   **Certification:** Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from CoreStack.

## Course Overview and Structure

This certification program is meticulously designed for individuals looking to gain a solid understanding of big data fundamentals and advanced Hadoop concepts. The curriculum is structured to provide extensive hands-on experience, making use of a dedicated virtual lab architecture.

The learning format is entirely self-paced, allowing students to access content whenever it suits them. Instructions are delivered in English through a combination of video lectures and supporting text materials. The course material is accessible on various devices, including mobiles, ensuring flexibility in learning.

## What You Will Learn

The program aims to impart both foundational and advanced knowledge of big data and Hadoop. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Understanding the core principles of big data analytics.
*   Gaining proficiency in Hadoop functionalities.
*   Practical experience with essential big data components such as:
    *   Cloudera Manager
    *   Hive
    *   Spark
    *   Oozie
    *   Impala

Through 20 hours of dedicated video-based lectures and practical exercises, learners will develop a strong grasp of how these components work together in a real-world big data environment. The course also offers 24/7 support to assist students throughout their learning journey.

## Who Is This Course For?

This virtual lab program is particularly beneficial for professionals and aspiring individuals in roles such as:

*   Data Analysts
*   Data Scientists
*   Big Data Analytics Engineers
*   Data Engineers

It caters to anyone seeking to enhance their skill set in big data technologies and gain practical experience with Hadoop.

## Fees and Certification

### Course Fee

The total fee for the Big Data Hadoop - First Virtual Lab certification course is ₹750, which includes GST. This one-time payment grants you lifetime access to the course materials.

### Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a certificate directly from CoreStack, acknowledging their mastery of the course content.

## How to Apply

To enroll in this program, interested candidates can visit the FutureSkills Prime platform. The application process typically involves registering on their portal and then selecting the Big Data Hadoop - First Virtual Lab course for enrollment. As it is a self-paced, virtual course, there are no specific application deadlines or entrance exams; enrollment is generally open.

Embark on your journey into big data with this comprehensive and practical virtual lab experience.
