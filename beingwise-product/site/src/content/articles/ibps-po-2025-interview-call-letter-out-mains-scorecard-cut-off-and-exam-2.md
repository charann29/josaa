## IBPS PO 2025: Interview Call Letter (OUT), Mains Scorecard, Cut Off & Exam Date 2026 Released
The Institute of Banking and Personnel Selection (IBPS) has recently announced the final results for the IBPS PO 2025 examination, alongside crucial dates for the upcoming 2026 cycle. This page provides a comprehensive overview of these important updates, helping aspiring banking professionals stay informed.

## Key takeaways
*   The IBPS PO 2025 final results were released on January 15, 2026, with the window to access them closing on February 14, 2026.
*   A total of 6189 Probationary Officer vacancies are being filled through the 2025 examination.
*   The official IBPS Calendar 2026 is out, detailing tentative dates for the Prelims (August 22 & 23, 2026) and Mains (October 4, 2026) exams.
*   The Mains scorecard for 2025 was made available on January 9, and the Mains result on December 1, 2025.

## IBPS PO 2025 Result and Cut-Off Information
The final results for the IBPS PO 2025 recruitment drive became available on January 15, 2026, on the official IBPS portal. Candidates can access their results until February 14, 2026. This announcement also included the cut-off marks for various categories. Previously, the Mains scorecard was released on January 9, 2025, following the Mains exam conducted on October 12, 2025. To view their results, candidates need to log in using their registration number and password or date of birth.

### How to Access Your IBPS PO Result 2025
Follow these steps to download your result:
1.  Visit the official IBPS website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'IBPS PO result' link on the homepage.
3.  On the login screen, enter your registration number or roll number, along with your date of birth or password.
4.  Your IBPS PO result will be displayed on the screen.
5.  Download and save a copy for your records.

### Details on the IBPS PO Scorecard
The scorecard provides essential information about your performance, including:
*   Candidate's Name
*   Roll Number
*   Registration Number
*   Exam Date
*   Category
*   Maximum scores (overall and section-wise)
*   IBPS PO cut-off marks
*   Scores obtained in individual sections and overall

## IBPS PO 2026 Exam Dates Announced
Good news for future aspirants! The official IBPS Calendar for 2026 was released on January 16, providing key dates for the next examination cycle. The preliminary examination for IBPS PO 2026 is tentatively scheduled for August 22 and 23, 2026. The main examination is planned for October 4, 2026. The official notification and application form link are expected to be released in June 2026.

## Understanding the IBPS PO Marking Scheme
For every correct answer in the IBPS PO exam, candidates are awarded one mark. It's crucial to note that there is a penalty for incorrect answers; 0.25 marks are deducted for each wrong response. The final total marks are calculated based on the number of correct and incorrect answers.

## Normalisation of IBPS PO Scores
Given that the IBPS PO examination is conducted over multiple shifts and days, the difficulty level of question papers can vary. To ensure fairness in evaluation, IBPS employs a normalisation method to calculate candidate scores. This process helps to balance out any discrepancies in difficulty across different exam sessions, leading to a standardised and equitable assessment for all candidates.

For more detailed information, always refer to the official IBPS portal.
