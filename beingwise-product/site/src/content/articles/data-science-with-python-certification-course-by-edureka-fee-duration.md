# Data Science with Python Certification Course by Edureka: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This certification course is designed to equip aspiring data scientists with essential skills in Python, machine learning, and statistical analysis. It offers a comprehensive curriculum, hands-on projects, and expert-led instruction to prepare you for a career in data science.

## Key takeaways

*   The course focuses on practical application, including real-time case studies across various industries like aviation and healthcare.
*   Participants gain lifetime access to the learning management system (LMS) and 24/7 technical support.
*   The program includes resume-building assistance to help graduates secure positions at leading companies.
*   Successful completion awards a certificate from Edureka, validating your data science expertise.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The Data Science with Python Certification program aims to transform you into a skilled data scientist. The curriculum covers fundamental concepts such as time series analysis, statistics, and a wide array of machine learning algorithms. You'll delve into supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning, mastering techniques like clustering, regression, Random Forest, Decision Trees, Q-Learning, and Naïve Bayes. A significant part of the learning involves solving industry-relevant case studies, providing practical experience in areas like HR, social media, and healthcare.

The learning experience is rich and interactive, featuring instructor-led live classes, regular assignments, and a community forum for peer interaction. You also benefit from lifetime access to the LMS and continuous 24/7 technical support to address any queries.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

While direct experience in Python development is highly beneficial for enrolling in this course, candidates with fundamental data analysis skills using tools like SAS or R are also encouraged to apply. For those who enroll, Edureka provides a complimentary self-paced program titled "Python Statistics for Data Science" to ensure all participants have a strong foundational understanding.

## Fees and Certification

The Data Science with Python Certification Course is priced at a discounted fee of ₹19,795, down from its original price of ₹21,995. For convenience, a No Cost EMI option is available, starting at ₹6,599 per month. Upon successful completion of the course, you will receive an official certificate from Edureka, acknowledging your newly acquired data science skills. This certificate, along with a 'resume-building tool' provided as part of the program, can significantly enhance your professional profile for roles at top-tier companies.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Data Science with Python Certification Course, interested candidates should visit the official program web page. From there, you can locate and click on the "Enroll Now" option to begin the admission process. The platform will guide you through the necessary steps to secure your spot in the program.

This program offers a robust pathway for individuals looking to build a strong career in the rapidly expanding field of data science, leveraging the power of Python.
