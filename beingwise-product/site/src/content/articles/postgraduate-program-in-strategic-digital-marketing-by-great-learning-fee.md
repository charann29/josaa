# Postgraduate Program in Strategic Digital Marketing by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Digital marketing has become an essential part of modern business strategy, driven by the increasing presence of the internet in daily life. This program offers a comprehensive pathway to master strategic digital marketing, equipping participants with the skills needed to thrive in this rapidly evolving field.

## Key takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** The program covers various facets of digital marketing, including self-paced learning, personalized mentorship, and practical capstone projects.
*   **Certification:** Upon successful completion, participants receive an Advanced Certificate in Strategic Digital Marketing from Great Lakes Executive Learning.
*   **Flexible Learning:** Designed for working professionals, the program features online learning, weekend classes, and self-study options.
*   **Career Support:** Participants benefit from dedicated career assistance to help them transition into or advance within the digital marketing industry.

## Program Overview and Structure

The Postgraduate Program in Strategic Digital Marketing is developed in collaboration with Great Lakes Executive Learning. It's tailored for individuals aspiring to build a strong career in digital marketing, leveraging the power of online platforms for brand promotion and customer engagement.

The program spans six months and combines various learning methodologies to ensure a thorough understanding of the subject. These include self-paced modules, interactive virtual classroom sessions, and practical assignments. The instruction medium is English, and content is delivered through a mix of video and text-based materials. Classes are typically held on weekends, making it convenient for those balancing studies with other commitments.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Postgraduate Program in Strategic Digital Marketing, candidates must meet specific academic qualifications:

*   Applicants should hold a graduate degree or a diploma from a recognized university. This includes degrees in various disciplines such as arts, humanities, science, or engineering.

Once screened by the authorities, eligible candidates can proceed with the admission process.

## Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Advanced Certificate in Strategic Digital Marketing is ₹1,25,000 plus GST. An admission fee of ₹15,000 plus GST is payable upon enrollment to secure a spot and begin the course immediately.

Participants have several flexible payment options:

*   **Full Payment:** The entire course fee can be paid upfront.
*   **EMI Plans:** For greater flexibility, various EMI options are available. These plans can extend up to 60 months, with some tenures offering 0% interest rates and 0% processing fees. This makes the program accessible to a wider range of aspiring professionals.

Payment can be made through debit cards, credit cards, cheques, or demand drafts via the official portal.

## Program Offerings and Learning Experience

The program is designed to provide a rich and practical learning experience:

*   **Online Learning:** All course content and interactions are conducted online, offering flexibility.
*   **Mentorship:** Participants receive personalized guidance from industry experts.
*   **Group Projects and Assignments:** Hands-on projects and regular assignments ensure practical skill development.
*   **Industry Tools:** Learners gain familiarity with essential digital marketing tools used by professionals.
*   **Capstone Projects:** These real-world projects allow participants to apply their knowledge and build a portfolio.
*   **Career Support:** Assistance with resume building, interview preparation, and job placement is provided to help participants achieve their career goals.

Upon successful completion of the program, including all projects, self-learning modules, and assessments, participants will be awarded an Advanced Certificate in Strategic Digital Marketing by Great Learning.

This program offers a robust foundation and advanced skills for anyone looking to make a significant impact in the dynamic field of digital marketing.
