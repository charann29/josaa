# TG ICET Application Form 2026 (with Late Fee): Last Date, Fees, How to Apply for TG ICET
The TG ICET 2026 application window is currently open for candidates who wish to apply with a late fee. This important state-level examination facilitates admissions into MBA and MCA programs across various institutions in Telangana.

## Key Takeaways

*   The TG ICET 2026 application is available online, with various late fee options extending until early May.
*   Candidates must complete fee payment first, followed by the online application submission.
*   Mahatma Gandhi University (MGU), Nalgonda, is the conducting body for the TG ICET examination.
*   Ensure all necessary documents and image uploads meet the specified criteria before applying.

## TG ICET 2026 Application Process and Important Dates

The application process for TG ICET 2026 involves online registration and fee payment, followed by filling out and submitting the detailed application form. It's crucial to be aware of the deadlines, especially if applying with a late fee.

### Application Schedule and Late Fees

| Dates                     | Application Phase                                   |
| :------------------------ | :-------------------------------------------------- |
| February 12 – March 31, 2026 | Registration without Late Fee                     |
| April 1 – April 15, 2026  | Registration with a late fee of INR 250             |
| April 16 – April 25, 2026 | Registration with a late fee of INR 500             |
| April 18 – April 26, 2026 | TS ICET Application Form Correction Window          |
| April 26 – May 1, 2026    | Registration with a late fee of INR 5000            |
| May 2 – May 3, 2026       | Registration with a late fee of INR 10000           |
| May 4 – May 14, 2026      | TG ICET Hall Ticket Download Window                 |
| May 13 – May 14, 2026     | TG ICET 2026 Exam Dates                             |

Candidates are strongly advised to complete their applications well before the final deadlines to avoid the higher late fees.

## Documents Required for TG ICET Registration 2026

Before you begin your application, gather the following documents. Having them ready will streamline the process:

*   Receipt from AP/TS Online, e-Seva, or Citizen Services Center (if applicable for fee payment).
*   Hall Ticket Number of your qualifying examination (either appeared or passed).
*   SSC, Birth Certificate, or an equivalent document for date of birth verification.
*   Income Certificate (if you are applying for any income-based considerations).
*   Caste Certificate (if applicable).
*   Certificate from the competent authority for special categories such as NCC, PH (Physically Handicapped), Sports, or CAP (Children of Armed Personnel).

### Image Upload Specifications

You will need to upload scanned images of your passport-sized photograph and signature. Ensure they meet these specifications:

| Document    | Format   | File Size   |
| :---------- | :------- | :---------- |
| Photograph  | jpg/jpeg | Up to 50 KB |
| Signature   | jpg/jpeg | Up to 30 KB |

## Important Instructions for Filling the TG ICET Application Form

Keep these guidelines in mind for a smooth application experience:

*   The application form must be filled out online through the official TG ICET portal.
*   All application fees paid are non-refundable.
*   If you pay your application fee at an AP/TS online center, retain the transaction receipt for your records.
*   Ensure your scanned photograph and signature are prepared according to the size and format specifications before you start filling the form.
*   Familiarize yourself with the various test zones and provide three preferences for your exam center.
*   To avail of any fee concessions, you must upload the relevant category certificate.
*   To avoid additional charges, apply as early as possible and do not wait until the final moments before a late fee deadline.

## How to Apply for TG ICET 2026 Exam

The application process for TG ICET 2026 involves a few straightforward steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official TG ICET 2026 website, as specified by the conducting body.
2.  **Fee Payment:** Complete the registration fee payment. This is usually the first step, and you will receive a transaction ID upon successful payment.
3.  **Fill Application Form:** Proceed to fill out the detailed online application form. This will require entering personal details, academic information, and contact information.
4.  **Upload Documents:** Upload your scanned photograph and signature as per the specified guidelines.
5.  **Review and Submit:** Carefully review all the information entered to ensure accuracy before final submission. Once submitted, it's advisable to take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.

Mahatma Gandhi University, Nalgonda, will subsequently release the TG ICET hall ticket for all eligible applicants on the official portal.
