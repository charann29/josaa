# Talentedge Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Talentedge Courses

These programs are designed to equip working professionals with in-demand skills and knowledge.

## Key Takeaways

*   Talentedge partners with top institutions like IIIT Bangalore, IIM Raipur, and IIM Kozhikode to deliver specialized online courses.
*   Programs are tailored for working professionals, offering practical skills in areas like full-stack development and general management.
*   Courses typically range from 12 to 13 months, providing in-depth learning experiences.
*   Fees vary by program and institution, with options available for different career stages.

## Executive Post Graduate Program in Full Stack Software Development

This 13-month online program, offered in collaboration with IIIT Bangalore, is designed for individuals aspiring to become proficient full-stack software developers. The curriculum focuses on practical application and industry-standard practices, enabling participants to build a strong portfolio through various projects.

The program covers fundamental concepts such as Java programming, object-oriented design, software engineering principles, and data structures and algorithms. It aims to fast-track participants into the software development industry by enhancing their technical skills and preparing them for real-world challenges.

**Key Details:**

*   **Duration:** 13 Months
*   **Approximate Fees:** ₹ 2,11,017
*   **Skills Covered:** Java, Algorithms, Object-Oriented Programming, Software Engineering, Data Structures

## Executive Certificate Program in General Management

Developed in partnership with IIM Raipur, this 12-month interactive course is crafted to refine the management capabilities of professionals. It delves into various business management tactics, including those utilized by seasoned managers across the country. The program also touches upon entrepreneurship, digital economics, and the importance of analytical skills for effective decision-making.

Participants will gain a deeper understanding of general management principles and business management strategies. The curriculum is structured to progressively enhance knowledge week by week, offering insights into the critical role of sound management within business environments.

**Key Details:**

*   **Duration:** 12 Months
*   **Approximate Fees:** ₹ 1,60,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Marketing, Digital Marketing, Human Resource Management, Analytical Skills, Business Strategy
