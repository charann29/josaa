# AP ICET Hall Ticket 2026: Release Date (OUT), Download APICET Admit Card @cets.apsche.ap.gov.in

The AP ICET 2026 hall ticket is a crucial document for all candidates aspiring to pursue MBA or MCA programs in Andhra Pradesh. It contains essential details about your examination and serves as your entry pass to the test centre.

## Key Takeaways

*   The AP ICET 2026 hall ticket will be available for download starting April 15, 2026.
*   The examination is scheduled for May 02, 2026, and will be conducted online in Computer-Based Test (CBT) mode.
*   Candidates must carry their hall ticket and a valid photo ID to the exam centre; entry will be denied without these documents.
*   Always verify all details on your downloaded admit card and contact the help desk immediately if any discrepancies are found.

## How to Download Your AP ICET 2026 Hall Ticket

To access your AP ICET 2026 hall ticket, follow these straightforward steps on the official APSCHE AP ICET website:

1.  Visit the official APSCHE AP ICET 2026 portal.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Hall Ticket' link.
3.  Enter your AP ICET registration number, your qualifying examination hall ticket number, and your date of birth.
4.  Click the 'Download Hall Ticket' tab.
5.  Save the admit card as a PDF file and take a clear printout. It is advisable to print multiple copies for safety.

## Details to Verify on Your AP ICET Hall Ticket

Upon downloading your hall ticket, carefully review all the information printed on it. Ensure the following details are accurate:

*   Your full name and roll number
*   Your photograph
*   Your signature
*   The exact exam date and venue
*   Reporting time for the examination
*   All APICET exam day instructions

### Correcting Errors on Your Hall Ticket

If you find any inaccuracies on your AP ICET 2026 hall ticket, it is crucial to contact the AP ICET 2026 help desk immediately. You can reach them between 10 AM and 5 PM at:

*   **Andhra University, Visakhapatnam**
*   **Phone:** 0891-284 4992 or 0891-2711744
*   **Email:** helpdeskapicet@apsche.org

## Essential Documents to Carry to the Exam Centre

On the day of the AP ICET examination, candidates must bring the following items to the test centre:

*   Your printed AP ICET 2026 hall ticket.
*   A valid government-issued photo identification proof (e.g., Aadhaar card, PAN card, Voter ID, Driving License, Passport).
*   A recent passport-sized photograph (preferably the same one uploaded during application).

Without these mandatory documents, you will not be permitted to enter the examination hall. Please ensure you prepare these items well in advance to avoid any last-minute stress.
