# XAT Syllabus 2027: Check Section Wise Topics and Weightage

Preparing for the XAT 2027 exam requires a clear understanding of its syllabus. XLRI Jamshedpur, the conducting body, releases the official XAT syllabus and exam pattern each year on its website, xatonline.in. This detailed guide will help you navigate the topics and weightage for each section.

## Key takeaways

*   The XAT 2027 exam is divided into two main parts: Part 1 (percentile-calculating sections) and Part 2 (General Knowledge).
*   Part 1 includes Verbal Ability & Logical Reasoning, Decision Making, and Quantitative Aptitude & Data Interpretation.
*   Part 2 consists of General Knowledge, with questions on both static and current affairs.
*   Familiarizing yourself with the official XAT syllabus is crucial for effective preparation and exam day strategy.

## XAT 2027 Syllabus Overview

The XAT syllabus is structured into two distinct parts. Part 1, which primarily determines your percentile score, comprises three sections: Verbal Ability and Logical Reasoning (VALR), Decision Making (DM), and Quantitative Aptitude and Data Interpretation (QA & DI). Part 2, the General Knowledge (GK) section, is also an important component. The total exam duration is 180 minutes, with 170 minutes allocated to Part 1 and 10 minutes specifically for the General Knowledge section.

## XAT 2027 Syllabus for Verbal Ability and Logical Reasoning (VALR)

This section typically features around 26 questions designed to assess your command of the English language and your ability to reason logically. Key topics often include:

*   Reading Comprehension (various passage types)
*   Vocabulary (Synonyms, Antonyms, Analogies)
*   Grammar and Sentence Correction
*   Para Jumbles and Para Completion
*   Critical Reasoning
*   Series Completion
*   Fill in the Blanks

## XAT 2027 Syllabus for Decision Making (DM)

The Decision Making section is unique to XAT and evaluates your ability to make sound judgments in complex scenarios. It usually presents around 21 questions based on caselets and situations. Important areas covered are:

*   Data Arrangement and Conditioning
*   Grouping and Complex Arrangements
*   Caselet-based scenarios
*   Identifying Assumptions, Premises, and Conclusions
*   Ethical dilemmas and business situations

## XAT 2027 Syllabus for Quantitative Aptitude and Data Interpretation (QA & DI)

This section assesses your numerical ability and skill in interpreting data, with approximately 28 questions. The syllabus covers a broad range of mathematical concepts and data analysis tools:

### Quantitative Aptitude

*   Arithmetic (Percentages, Profit & Loss, Ratio & Proportion, Time & Work, Speed, Time & Distance)
*   Algebra (Equations, Inequalities, Functions)
*   Geometry and Mensuration (Triangles, Circles, Quadrilaterals, Volume, Surface Area)
*   Number Systems
*   Modern Math (Permutations & Combinations, Probability)
*   Surds and Indices

### Data Interpretation

*   Bar Diagrams
*   Pie Charts
*   Tables
*   Line Graphs
*   Data Sufficiency

## XAT 2027 Syllabus for General Knowledge (GK)

The General Knowledge section typically contains about 25 questions, covering both static GK and current affairs. While this section does not contribute to your percentile score, it is considered by some participating institutes for final selection. Topics frequently encountered include:

*   **Static GK:** Indian History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science, Arts & Culture, Sports, Awards, International Organizations.
*   **Current Affairs:** Recent events in Business, Economy, Politics, Science & Technology, National and International news of significance.

## XAT 2027 Marking Scheme

Understanding the marking scheme is vital for strategic test-taking. For Part 1 sections (VALR, DM, QA & DI), generally, each correct answer fetches +1 mark, and there is a penalty of -0.25 marks for incorrect answers. Unattempted questions beyond 8 may also incur a penalty of -0.10 marks each. For the General Knowledge section, there is typically no negative marking. It's always best to refer to the official XAT notification for the most precise and updated marking scheme for 2027.

Familiarizing yourself with these sections and their respective topics as outlined in the official XAT syllabus from XLRI Jamshedpur will provide a robust foundation for your preparation.
