*   The course is fully online and self-study, delivered through video and text-based materials.
*   It offers a government certification with lifetime validity.

## Understanding NLP and the Certification

Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) is an approach focused on personal development, helping individuals understand and modify their thought and behavior patterns. This Vskills Certified NLP Practitioner course provides a structured way to learn these techniques. The curriculum covers foundational NLP concepts, including communication models, sensory acuity, goal setting, and strategies for building rapport and managing emotional states.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The Certified NLP Practitioner course is an e-learning program with approximately 12 hours of content. It's designed for online self-study, allowing participants to learn at their own pace. The program includes access to a learning management system, study materials, and a final online test. Upon successful completion of the test, participants receive a government-recognized certification that is valid for life.

## Course Fee and Certification

The total fee for the Certified NLP Practitioner certification by Vskills is ₹3,499, plus 18% GST. This fee covers the entire program, including access to study materials, the online test, and the final certificate. The certificate is issued by Vskills and holds government recognition.

## Eligibility and Certification Process

There are no specific prerequisites to enroll in this course. To earn the Certified NLP Practitioner certificate, candidates must pass the final online exam. The passing score requires achieving 25 marks out of a total of 50.

## What You Will Learn

Through the Certified NLP Practitioner syllabus, you will gain knowledge and skills in several key areas:

*   **Cognitive Functions and Self-Awareness:** Understanding how your mind works and developing greater self-awareness.
*   **NLP Strategies:** Learning about NLP modeling, building rapport, and applying techniques like the Meta Model and Milton Model.
*   **Goal Setting and Behavioral Change:** Enhancing skills in effective goal setting and developing strategies to remove unwanted habits and feelings.
*   **Sensory Acuity:** Improving your ability to perceive and interpret sensory information.
