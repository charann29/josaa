# IBSAT Application Form 2026: Registration Date, Fees & How to Apply @admissions.ibsindia.org

For those aspiring to join the MBA or PGPM programs at ICFAI Business School (IBS) campuses, the IBSAT application process for 2026 is a crucial first step. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about registering, including important dates, fees, and the step-by-step application procedure.

## Key Takeaways

*   IBSAT 2026 registrations are expected to open on July 1, 2026, and will continue until December 2026.
*   The application fee for IBSAT 2026 is INR 1800, payable online or offline.
*   The IBSAT 2026 examination is tentatively scheduled for the last week of December 2026.
*   Admission to IBS programs is also possible through valid scores from other management entrance exams like CAT, NMAT, XAT, or GMAT.

## IBSAT 2026 Application Process: Online Steps

The most common way to apply for IBSAT 2026 is through the online portal. Follow these steps carefully to ensure your application is complete:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Go to the official admissions website, admissions.ibsindia.org.
2.  **Create Login Details:** Look for the 'Apply Now' tab and click on it to create your unique login ID and password. This will be essential for accessing your application.
3.  **Log In and Fill the Form:** Use your newly created login credentials to access the application form.
4.  **Enter Personal Information:** Provide all required personal details accurately.
5.  **Pay Application Fee:** The IBSAT registration fee of INR 1800 must be paid online. You can use credit cards, debit cards, net banking, or UPI for this transaction.

## IBSAT Eligibility Criteria 2026

Before you proceed with filling out the application form, it's vital to confirm you meet the eligibility requirements. Failure to do so will lead to the cancellation of your candidature.

*   Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university or institution, with a minimum aggregate score as specified by IBS.
*   Candidates possessing valid scores from other national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, CMAT, XAT, NMAT, or GMAT are often exempted from needing to take the IBSAT 2026.

## How to Fill IBSAT Application Form 2026 (Offline)

For those who prefer an offline application method, IBS also provides this option:

1.  **Purchase Application Form:** You can buy the IBSAT 2026 application form directly from any IBS campus. This involves a cash payment of INR 1800.
2.  **Receive Login Details:** Upon purchase, you will receive a packet containing a unique login ID and a voucher number.
3.  **Online Registration:** Use these details to log in to the official portal and complete your registration.
4.  **Set New Password:** You will be prompted to set a new password for your IBSAT 2026 account.
5.  **Complete Application:** Use your login ID and new password to access and fill out the IBSAT 2026 application form online.

## Documents Required for IBSAT Application 2026

To ensure a smooth application process, have the following documents and information ready:

*   Academic certificates and mark sheets for Class 10, Class 12, and Graduation.
*   A valid email ID and mobile number.
*   A recent photograph with a clear, full face. This should be in .jpeg or .gif format, not exceeding 25kb in size, and preferably between 140 to 160 pixels.
*   A scanned copy of your signature.
*   If you are applying with a GMAT, NMAT by GMAC, or CAT score, you will need to email a scanned copy of your score report or certificate to ibsat@ibsindia.org.

Make sure to review all information before submitting your application to avoid any discrepancies. Good luck with your IBSAT 2026 application!
