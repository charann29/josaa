# Ultimate SOLIDWORKS Course: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring engineers and design professionals looking to master industry-standard CAD software, the Ultimate SOLIDWORKS Course offers a comprehensive learning experience. This program aims to equip participants with the practical skills needed to excel in design and engineering roles.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course provides a deep dive into SOLIDWORKS, covering everything from basic sketching to advanced surface modeling and rendering.
*   It emphasizes hands-on, project-based learning, allowing students to build a professional portfolio.
*   The program is self-paced and delivered online, offering flexibility for students and working professionals.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a course certificate, with a merit certificate for top performers.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Ultimate SOLIDWORKS Course is designed to make you proficient in SOLIDWORKS, a crucial tool in mechanical and automotive design. Developed with input from industry experts, the curriculum covers essential topics such as concept generation, creating detailed sketches, understanding the SOLIDWORKS user interface, solid modeling, advanced sketching techniques, assembly creation, and surface modeling. The program also delves into rendering to create photo-realistic visualizations.

Instruction is delivered through pre-recorded video lectures by experienced faculty members. Learning is highly practical, featuring weekly assignments and industry-relevant projects. These projects are reviewed by technical support engineers and are crucial for building a strong professional portfolio, which can significantly enhance job prospects after completing the course. The entire program is structured for self-paced learning over three months, with all content accessible online.

## Eligibility and Certification

While there are no strict prerequisites for enrolling in the Ultimate SOLIDWORKS Course, it is primarily intended for individuals who are currently pursuing or have completed a degree in Mechanical or Automotive Engineering. This background ensures participants have the foundational knowledge necessary to benefit fully from the advanced CAD training.

To earn the course completion certificate, students must successfully complete all training modules, weekly assignments, and industry-grade projects. An additional merit certificate is awarded to the top 5% of performers in the program, recognizing exceptional achievement and skill mastery. All certificates are provided by the course authority.

## Fee Structure and Payment Options

The fees for the Ultimate SOLIDWORKS Course are structured to offer flexibility, with monthly payment plans available throughout the course duration. There are three distinct payment options to suit different needs regarding content access:

| Plan Type | Monthly Fee (INR) |
| :-------------------------------- | :---------------- |
| Basic (Two-month content access)  | ₹7,000            |
| Pro (Four-month content access)   | ₹10,000           |
| Premium (Lifetime content access) | ₹15,000           |

These options allow students to choose the level of access that best fits their learning pace and budget.

## Who Should Enroll?

This course is ideal for a range of individuals looking to enhance their design capabilities. It is particularly beneficial for:

*   **SAE team students:** Those involved in student automotive engineering teams can learn to translate their design concepts into precise 3D models and sketches.
*   **Mechanical and Automotive Engineers:** Professionals seeking to upgrade their existing SOLIDWORKS skills to take on more complex projects or advance their careers.
*   **Students aspiring to CAD engineering roles:** Individuals looking to build a strong foundation in CAD and prepare for careers in design and engineering.

## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of the Ultimate SOLIDWORKS Course, participants will:

*   Possess a comprehensive understanding of SOLIDWORKS basics, including part modeling, assembly modeling, and photo-realistic rendering.
*   Be fluent in the core concepts of SOLIDWORKS and their practical application in design.
*   Understand the fundamentals of generating design concepts.
*   Be proficient in using both surface and solid modeling tools within SOLIDWORKS.
*   Be well-prepared to pursue a career in CAD engineering.

This course provides a robust foundation for anyone serious about a career in design and engineering, offering practical skills and a portfolio to help you stand out.
