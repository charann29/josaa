# PG Program in Product Management and Analytics by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career at the intersection of technology, design, and business, Great Learning offers a comprehensive Postgraduate Program in Product Management and Analytics. This program aims to equip you with the skills needed to conceptualize, develop, and launch innovative products in today's dynamic market.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program culminates in a PG certificate from the Great Lakes Institute of Management.
*   It focuses on practical, case study-based learning with over 10 hands-on projects and a significant product innovation project.
*   The curriculum covers essential skills from design thinking and market analysis to data analytics using tools like Google Analytics and Excel.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹1,35,000, plus applicable GST.

## Program Overview

This postgraduate program is designed to transform participants into well-rounded product managers. It provides a strong foundation in both product management principles and data analytics. You will learn to navigate the complete product lifecycle, from identifying market opportunities and designing user experiences to launching and iterating products based on data-driven insights. The pedagogical approach emphasizes rigorous analytical methods and practical application, ensuring you gain the ability to solve complex problems and create compelling digital products.

Upon successful completion, you will not only receive a PG certificate but also build a diverse portfolio of projects. This e-portfolio can be a powerful tool for showcasing your capabilities to potential employers and enhancing your professional visibility.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to impart a wide range of critical skills and knowledge essential for a product management role. Key areas of learning include:

*   **Analytical Mindset:** Developing decision-making skills using tools like Google Analytics and MS Excel to analyze user interaction and product performance.
*   **Design Thinking:** Employing wireframing tools to create mock-ups and test user interfaces and experiences, focusing on creating delightful digital products.
*   **Market and Product Analysis:** Understanding how to identify market gaps, recognize unmet consumer needs, and analyze product opportunities.
*   **Product Strategy:** Cultivating a product thinking mindset for both tactical and strategic decision-making, balancing internal stakeholder needs with market demands.
*   **Marketing Skills:** Gaining an understanding of marketing principles relevant to product launch and growth.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The program blends self-paced learning with interactive sessions to provide a rich educational experience. Here's a breakdown of what's included:

*   Over 65 hours of self-paced video lectures
*   More than 15 weekly online mentorship sessions
*   4 Innovation Sprint Masterclasses
*   1 major product innovation project
*   Over 10 hands-on project assignments
*   24 quizzes to reinforce learning
*   Case study-based pedagogy for practical application
*   A shareable e-portfolio to showcase your work
*   A completion certificate from Great Learning

## Fees and Eligibility

### Program Fees

The total fee for the PG Program in Product Management and Analytics is **₹1,35,000**. Additionally, Goods and Services Tax (GST) will be applicable at the prevailing rate of 18%.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for this program, candidates must hold a Bachelor's Degree with a minimum of 50% marks or an equivalent Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA). For candidates belonging to the SC/ST categories, the minimum requirement is 45% marks.

## Who Is This Program For?

This program is ideally suited for a diverse group of professionals looking to enter or advance in the field of product management:

*   **Software Developers:** Those aspiring to lead product development initiatives and contribute strategically to their organizations.
*   **Project Managers and Project Leads:** Professionals seeking to transition into Product Manager or Product Owner roles.
*   **Early-Career Professionals:** Individuals looking to build a strong foundation in product management from the outset of their careers.
*   **UX/UI Designers:** Designers aiming to expand their skill set by acquiring proficiency in product management principles.
*   **Sales, Marketing, and Engineering Professionals:** Early to mid-career individuals from these backgrounds who wish to pivot into product management roles.

## How to Apply

The application process for the PG Program in Product Management and Analytics is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official Great Learning website for the Product Management and Analytics program.
2.  Locate and select the "Apply Now" option.
3.  Begin your application by completing the online application form with your details.
4.  Your application will then be reviewed by the faculty panel.

This program offers a structured pathway for individuals to gain expertise and a recognized credential in the high-demand field of product management and analytics.
