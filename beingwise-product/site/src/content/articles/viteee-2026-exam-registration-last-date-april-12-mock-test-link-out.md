# VITEEE 2026 Exam: Registration Last Date (April 12), Mock Test Link OUT, Eligibility, PYQs here

For aspiring engineers eyeing admission to the Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) campuses, the VITEEE 2026 application deadline has been extended. This is a crucial update for all candidates planning to pursue a B.Tech degree at one of VIT's esteemed institutions.

## Key takeaways

*   The final date to submit your VITEEE 2026 application form online is April 12.
*   The VITEEE 2026 entrance examination will take place from April 28 to May 3, 2026.
*   A mock test link has been released by VIT to help students understand the exam format.
*   Slot booking for the exam, where you choose your preferred date and time, is expected to begin in the second week of April 2026.

## Important Dates for VITEEE 2026

Staying informed about key dates is essential for a smooth application process. Here's a quick overview of the critical deadlines:

*   **Application Form Last Date (Extended):** April 12, 2026
*   **Exam Dates:** April 28 to May 3, 2026
*   **Slot Booking Start (Expected):** Second week of April 2026

Candidates must complete their application before the extended deadline. The examination will be conducted across multiple slots within the specified dates.

## VITEEE 2026 Application Process

The application process for VITEEE 2026 is entirely online. Candidates need to visit the official VITEEE portal to register and submit their forms. This multi-stage process requires careful attention to detail. Once the application is complete, the next step involves slot booking.

## Slot Booking and Admit Card

VIT will open its Online Test Booking System (OTBS) portal in the second week of April 2026 for slot booking. This system allows you to select your preferred exam date, time, and centre from the available options. After successfully booking your slot, your admit card (hall ticket) will become available for download approximately 48 hours before your chosen exam slot. The admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the examination.

## VITEEE 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Before applying, it's vital to ensure you meet the eligibility requirements set by VIT. While specific details should always be checked on the official VIT website, general criteria typically include:

*   **Nationality:** Indian Nationals, NRIs, PIOs, and OCIs are generally eligible.
*   **Age Limit:** Candidates must have been born on or after July 1, 2004.
*   **Educational Qualification:** A pass in the 10+2 (or equivalent) examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology as compulsory subjects. A minimum aggregate percentage in these subjects is usually required, which may vary by category (e.g., General, SC/ST, North Eastern states). For example, a common requirement is an aggregate of 55% for General category students and 45% for candidates from SC/ST communities or specific North Eastern states.

## Mock Test and Previous Year's Question Papers

To help students prepare effectively, VIT has activated the VITEEE 2026 mock test link. Engaging with this mock test is highly recommended as it provides a realistic simulation of the actual exam environment, question types, and time constraints. Additionally, practicing with previous year's question papers (PYQs) can offer valuable insights into the exam pattern, important topics, and help in time management. These resources are crucial tools for effective preparation.

Make sure to utilize all available resources and adhere to the deadlines to maximize your chances of admission to VIT.
