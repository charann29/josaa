# Post Graduate Diploma in Clinical Research by Clini Launch Research Institute: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

The Post Graduate Diploma in Clinical Research (PGDCR) from Clini Launch Research Institute (CLRI) is a certification program designed to equip students with essential knowledge and skills for a career in clinical research. This program aims to provide a thorough understanding of the processes involved in clinical trials, from design to management.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 12-month certification course offered by Clini Launch Research Institute.
*   The program focuses on practical learning, daily assessments, and interactive virtual classes.
*   It covers key aspects like protocol development, data analysis, pharmacovigilance, and regulatory compliance.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from CLRI.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This Post Graduate Diploma is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of the clinical research field. It explores the detailed steps in designing, conducting, monitoring, and managing clinical trials. A core focus is placed on the ethical, regulatory, and scientific dimensions that govern clinical research.

The curriculum integrates theoretical concepts with practical applications, encouraging critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Students will delve into topics such as developing study protocols, methods for data collection and analysis, pharmacovigilance (drug safety monitoring), and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. The learning experience includes course readings, practical exercises, and daily assessments, delivered through a blend of self-study and virtual classroom sessions, primarily using video and text-based materials.

## Program Duration and Fees

The Post Graduate Diploma in Clinical Research is a 12-month certification program. The total fee for the course is ₹1,20,000. This amount covers all course components and the certification upon successful completion.

| Fees Component | Amount |
| :------------- | :----- |
| Course Fees    | ₹1,20,000 |

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Post Graduate Diploma in Clinical Research, candidates must hold a graduate or postgraduate degree from a recognized university. To qualify for the certification, students are required to complete the entire course program.

## Who Is This Program For?

This program is particularly beneficial for clinical practitioners, such as clinicians, and individuals working as Clinical Research Coordinators. It is designed to update their skills with the latest technological advancements and business trends in the healthcare and pharmaceutical industries. The course aims to provide participants with a competitive edge, enabling them to contribute significantly to healthcare advancements and open doors to diverse career opportunities within the sector.

## How to Apply

Prospective candidates interested in joining the Post Graduate Diploma in Clinical Research classes need to follow a simple application process. This typically involves visiting the official CLRI website dedicated to this program and completing an online application form. Candidates will need to provide necessary personal and academic information, including their primary email address, name, and phone number.

This program offers a valuable pathway for those looking to advance or begin their careers in the vital field of clinical research.
