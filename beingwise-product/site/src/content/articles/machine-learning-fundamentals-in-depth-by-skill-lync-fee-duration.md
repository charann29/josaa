# Machine Learning Fundamentals in Depth by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a strong foundation in the exciting field of Machine Learning (ML), a comprehensive program covering core concepts and practical applications is essential. This course offers a detailed exploration of ML fundamentals, designed to equip learners with the necessary skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is delivered entirely in English, using a self-study format with video and text-based materials.
*   The total fee for the Machine Learning Fundamentals in Depth course is ₹ 40,000.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Skill Lync.
*   The curriculum spans 12 weeks, covering everything from probability basics to neural networks.

## Course Structure and Learning Experience

This self-paced program is structured for flexibility, allowing learners to progress through the material at their own speed. The instruction is entirely in English, making it accessible to a wide audience. Learning resources include both video lectures and supporting text, ensuring a multi-modal approach to understanding complex topics. This format is ideal for individuals who prefer to manage their study schedule independently while engaging with in-depth content.

## Program Fees and Certification

The total cost for enrolling in the Machine Learning Fundamentals in Depth program is ₹ 40,000. This fee covers all course materials and access to the learning platform. Upon successful completion of the program, participants will be awarded a certificate directly by Skill Lync, acknowledging their understanding and proficiency in machine learning fundamentals. This certification can be a valuable addition to a professional portfolio, demonstrating specialized knowledge in the field.

## Detailed Syllabus

The 12-week curriculum is thoughtfully designed to progressively build knowledge, starting with foundational concepts and moving towards more advanced ML techniques. Here’s a week-by-week breakdown:

### Week 01: Basics of Probability and Statistics

*   Introduction to fundamental probability concepts.
*   Understanding core statistical principles.
*   An overview of Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI).

### Week 02: Basics of Machine Learning (ML) & Artificial Intelligence (AI)

*   Exploring Normal Distribution and Standard Normal Distribution.
*   Introduction to business moments.
*   Deeper dive into Artificial Intelligence concepts.

### Week 03: Supervised Learning - Prediction

*   Introduction to supervised learning paradigms.
*   Understanding Linear Regression.
*   Concepts of One-Hot Encoding.
*   Exploring Cost Function and Gradient Descent.

### Week 04: Supervised Learning - Classification

*   Introduction to classification problems.
*   Understanding Logistic Regression.
*   Further application of Cost Function and Gradient Descent in classification.

### Week 05: Supervised Learning - Classification

*   Detailed study of Decision Trees.
*   Understanding Entropy.
*   Exploring Information Gain.

### Week 06: Random Forest & Model Evaluation

*   Introduction to Random Forest algorithms.
*   Concepts of Bootstrapping and Majority Rule.
*   Methods for evaluating classifier performance.

### Week 07: Supervised Learning - Classification

*   In-depth look at Support Vector Machines (SVM).
*   Mathematical intuition behind SVM.
*   Comparing SVM with other classification techniques.

### Week 08: Supervised Learning - Classification

*   Understanding K-Nearest Neighbour (KNN).
*   Exploring KNN as a Lazy Algorithm.
*   Introduction to Single-Layer Neural Networks.

### Week 09: Unsupervised Learning - K-means

*   What is clustering and its importance.
*   Detailed study of K-means clustering.
*   Understanding the Elbow Curve method.

### Week 10: Unsupervised Learning - Hierarchical

*   Exploring Hierarchical Clustering.
*   Understanding Dendrograms.
*   Methods for evaluating clustering algorithms.

### Week 11: Unsupervised Learning - PCA

*   Introduction to Feature Selection.
*   Understanding Principal Component Analysis (PCA).
*   Mathematical intuition behind PCA.

### Week 12: Supervised Learning - Classification

*   Advanced concepts of Artificial Neural Networks (ANN).
*   Introduction to Deep Learning.
*   Understanding different activation functions.
*   Exploring the process of backpropagation.

This comprehensive curriculum ensures that learners gain both theoretical knowledge and practical understanding across various machine learning techniques.
