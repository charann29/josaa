# Executive Post Graduate Program in Medical Technology by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to advance their expertise in the rapidly evolving field of medical technology, an Executive Post Graduate Program can be a significant step. This program, offered by Skill Lync, is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge in various aspects of medical technology.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is delivered in English, with both self-study and physical classroom options available.
*   Learning content is provided through video and text formats.
*   Participants receive a certificate upon successful completion, issued by Skill Lync.
*   The curriculum covers a wide range of topics from human anatomy and physiology to specific physiological systems and their related medical technologies.

## Program Overview

This Executive Post Graduate Program in Medical Technology aims to equip professionals with a strong foundation in the core principles of medical science and technology. It combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications relevant to the industry. The flexible learning modes cater to working professionals, allowing them to upskill without disrupting their careers.

## Course Content and Syllabus

The program's syllabus is meticulously structured into various courses and modules, ensuring a thorough understanding of the subject matter. Here's a detailed look at what you will learn:

### Course 1: Anatomy and Physiology

This foundational course delves into the intricate structures and functions of the human body. It's divided into several modules:

*   **Module 1 - Introduction to Cells:** Covers the basic units of the human body, including cells, tissues, and blood components, along with anatomical terms.
*   **Module 2 - Anatomy of the Musculoskeletal & Connective Tissue Systems:** Explores bones, cartilage, joints, and muscle structures.
*   **Module 3 - Anatomy of the Cardiovascular & Respiratory Systems – Nose to Alveoli:** Focuses on the heart, blood vessels, blood components, and the entire respiratory tract.
*   **Module 4 - Anatomy of the Digestive System - Mouth to Large Intestine & Urogenital System:** Details the structures and functions of the digestive organs and the male and female urogenital systems.
*   **Module 5 - Anatomy of the Nervous System & Special Senses:** Examines the central and peripheral nervous systems, brain structures, spinal cord, and sensory organs like the ear and eye.
*   **Module 6 - Anatomy of Endocrine System:** Provides an overview of major endocrine glands such as the pituitary, thyroid, adrenal, pancreas, and pineal glands.
*   **Module 7 - Introduction to Physiological Systems:** Introduces fundamental physiological concepts, cell functions, and the principle of homeostasis.
*   **Module 8 - Gastrointestinal System:** Covers the GI tract, accessory organs, digestion, absorption, common problems, surgeries, and related market products.
*   **Module 9 - Urinary System (Urology and Nephrology):** Discusses kidney anatomy, urine formation, kidney stones, common surgeries, and differences between urology and nephrology.
*   **Module 10 - Respiratory System:** Explores the respiratory tract, breathing mechanisms, respiratory volumes, and artificial respiration.
*   **Module 11 - Cardiovascular System:** Delves into blood vessels, cardiac muscle, heart anatomy, conduction system, blood pressure regulation, and associated medical procedures.
*   **Module 12 - Nervous and Endocrine Systems:** Expands on the organization of the nervous system, neurons, the brain's lobes and cortical areas, and an introduction to the spinal cord.
