# JEE Main Exam 2026: Official Papers OUT, Answer Key (April 11), Analysis, Expected Cutoff

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main is a crucial stepping stone for aspiring engineers and architects in India, opening doors to various undergraduate programs like B.E./B.Tech, B.Arch, and B.Plan. The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducts this examination, which draws its syllabus from Class 11 and 12 topics.

## Key Takeaways

*   The NTA has officially released the JEE Main 2026 Session 2 question papers for the April attempt.
*   The official answer key and individual response sheets for Session 2 are expected to be available on April 11.
*   Results for JEE Main 2026 Session 2 are anticipated on or before April 20.
*   The top 2.5 lakh candidates from JEE Main will be eligible to appear for JEE Advanced, the gateway to the prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs).

While the official cutoff scores for JEE Main 2026 will be announced by the NTA along with the results, an expected cutoff can be estimated based on previous years' trends and the overall difficulty of the current year's examination.
