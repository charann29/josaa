# Aptech Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Aptech Learning Courses

Aptech Learning offers a range of career-focused courses designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills across various domains like IT, finance, and e-commerce. These programs aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and professional requirements, preparing individuals for the workforce.

## Key takeaways

*   Aptech Learning provides specialized certification courses in high-demand fields such as E-commerce, Banking, and Accounting.
*   Programs are structured to deliver practical, job-oriented skills, often including hands-on training and industry expert interaction.
*   Course durations vary significantly, from a few months for specialized certifications to nearly two years for comprehensive professional programs.
*   Fees for these certification courses range from approximately INR 54,000 to INR 77,400, depending on the program's length and depth.

## ACCP Pro – E-commerce Course

The ACCP Pro – E-commerce course from Aptech Learning is a comprehensive program designed to give students a deep understanding of the e-commerce landscape. This certification spans 23 months and covers essential concepts from web development to e-commerce implementation.

Students will gain proficiency in areas such as:

*   Web productivity tools
*   Logic building and programming fundamentals (including Java and CSS)
*   Building modern websites
*   E-commerce strategy and execution
*   Database management (SQL knowledge)

Upon completion, participants are expected to be well-prepared for various career opportunities within the dynamic e-commerce industry, equipped with both technical and professional skills to thrive.

**Duration:** 23 Months

**Fees:** ₹ 77,400

## Banking Specialist Certification

Recognizing India's rapidly expanding banking sector, Aptech Learning offers the Banking Specialist certification to train individuals in core banking and finance principles. This program is particularly beneficial for commerce graduates seeking to enter one of the world's largest and fastest-growing banking industries.

The course is structured into three parts, delivered over 16 months, with in-class lectures scheduled three days a week for two hours each. The curriculum is designed to impart specialized knowledge, enabling learners to handle diverse aspects of banking operations.

**Duration:** 16 Months

**Fees:** ₹ 54,000

## Aptech Certified Accounts Professional

The Aptech Certified Accounts Professional program is an intensive 15-month training course aimed at making candidates job-ready in the accounting and finance domain. The curriculum is divided into two terms, each covering a broad range of modules essential for modern business accounting.

Key areas of learning include:

*   Fundamental computer applications for business accounting
*   Proficiency in Tally software
*   Advanced topics like business communication, statutory compliance, TDS, GST, and direct taxation
*   Latest accounting techniques and financial management

This program emphasizes practical, employment-driven education, ensuring that graduates possess both basic and advanced accounting skills, including the ability to perform complex financial transactions for organizations.

**Duration:** 15 Months

**Fees:** ₹ 70,000

Aptech Learning's diverse offerings provide structured pathways for students to acquire specialized skills and certifications, enhancing their employability in competitive sectors.
