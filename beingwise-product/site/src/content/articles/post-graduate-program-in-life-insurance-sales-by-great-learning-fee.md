# Post Graduate Program in Life Insurance Sales by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aspiring to build a career in life insurance sales, Great Learning offers a dedicated Post Graduate Program designed to equip individuals with essential industry knowledge and practical sales skills. This program aims to prepare participants for roles in a growing sector.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program focuses on life insurance sales, covering product knowledge, sales techniques, and modern marketing approaches.
*   It is delivered through a blend of self-study and virtual classroom sessions, with content available in video and text formats.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹99,000, which includes Goods and Services Tax (GST).
*   Participants receive a certificate upon successful completion from Great Learning.

## Program Overview and Collaboration

This Post Graduate Program is a collaborative effort, with Bharti AXA Life Insurance contributing industry insights and expertise. The entire curriculum is delivered in English, making it accessible to a wide audience. The learning experience is flexible, combining self-paced study with interactive virtual classroom sessions. Content is presented through both video and text-based modules, catering to different learning preferences.

## Curriculum Highlights

The program's syllabus is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of the life insurance sales domain, from foundational knowledge to advanced selling strategies. Key modules include:

*   **Industry and Role Introduction:** This section provides an overview of the life insurance industry, introduces Bharti AXA Life Insurance, and clarifies the responsibilities of a sales professional in this sector.
*   **Sales Fundamentals:** Participants will learn crucial sales skills, including effective communication, strategies for handling objections and rejections, and developing key behavioral traits necessary for success in sales.
*   **Product and Distribution Training:** This module delves into specific life insurance products offered by the collaborating company, details company policies, and explores various distribution channels such as agency, bancassurance, and direct sales.
*   **Modern Sales Techniques:** The program covers contemporary approaches to life insurance marketing, including an introduction to digital marketing principles and the use of digital tools relevant to sales.
*   **Personality Development:** A dedicated segment focuses on enhancing personal attributes and professional demeanor, which are vital for building client relationships and achieving sales targets.

## Program Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Post Graduate Program in Life Insurance Sales is ₹99,000. This amount is inclusive of GST. Great Learning is the authority responsible for issuing the program certificate upon successful completion. It is worth noting that while the primary program fee is ₹99,000, any additional or optional resume preparation services, if separately offered, would have a distinct cost, such as an illustrative example of ₹600. Potential participants should confirm all fee components with the official program details.

## How to Apply

Details regarding the application process, including specific deadlines and required documentation, are typically available on Great Learning's official program page. Prospective students should visit this official source to understand the step-by-step application procedure, eligibility criteria, and any early bird discounts that might be offered.

This program offers a structured pathway for individuals looking to embark on a career in life insurance sales, providing both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
