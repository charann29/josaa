# Mastering Cardiac Anatomy, Physiology & Medical Terminology by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding the complexities of the human heart is crucial for anyone in the medical field or those looking to expand their knowledge of cardiology. This course offers a detailed exploration of cardiac anatomy, physiology, and essential medical terminology.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is delivered in English through a self-study format, combining video and text-based lessons.
*   It covers foundational to advanced topics, including detailed cardiac anatomy, impulse propagation, and ECG basics.
*   Participants receive a certificate upon successful completion, issued by Udemy.
*   The program is designed for flexible learning, allowing students to progress at their own pace.

## Course Overview and Learning Format

This program is structured for independent learning, providing comprehensive content in both video and text formats. The entire course is taught in English, making it accessible to a wide audience. Its self-study nature means you can fit the learning into your personal schedule, revisiting complex topics as needed until you feel confident.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for this valuable course is ₹1,499. Upon successfully completing all modules and requirements, participants will be awarded a certificate. This certificate is provided by Udemy, signifying your achievement and enhanced understanding of cardiac subjects.

## What You Will Learn: The Syllabus

The curriculum is thoughtfully designed to build knowledge progressively, starting with core concepts and moving into more specialized areas. Here's a glimpse of the key topics covered:

*   **Introduction:** Setting the stage for understanding cardiac functions.
*   **Channelopathy:** Delving into conditions like short and long QT syndromes and Brugada syndrome.
*   **Detailed Cardiac Anatomy:** An in-depth look at the structure of the heart.
*   **Impulse Propagation:** Understanding how electrical signals travel through the conduction system and arrhythmia cells.
*   **Cellular Mechanisms of Heart Conduction:** Exploring the fundamental processes at a cellular level.
*   **Anatomy of Left Atria and Left Ventricle:** A focused study on these critical chambers.
*   **Basics of ECG:** Learning the fundamentals of electrocardiography.
*   **Cardiac Examination:** Practical aspects of assessing heart health.

## Meet Your Instructor

The course is led by Dr. N. Kumar, a seasoned Professor and Freelancer with extensive academic credentials including a B.E./B.Tech, other Bachelor's and Master's degrees, and a Ph.D. Dr. Kumar brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the program, ensuring a high-quality learning experience.
