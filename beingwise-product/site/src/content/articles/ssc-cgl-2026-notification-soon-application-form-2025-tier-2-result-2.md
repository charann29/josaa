# SSC CGL 2026: Notification (Soon), Application Form, 2025 Tier 2 Result (Released), Cutoff, Syllabus & News

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) conducts the Combined Graduate Level (CGL) examination each year to recruit eligible candidates for various Group B and C positions across numerous government ministries, departments, and organizations. The official notification for SSC CGL 2026 is expected soon.

## Key Takeaways

*   The SSC CGL 2026 notification, including details on vacancies, application process, and important dates, is anticipated to be released shortly on the official SSC website.
*   The application window will open concurrently with the notification, allowing candidates a specific timeframe to submit their forms online.
*   The SSC CGL Tier 1 examination for 2026 is tentatively scheduled for May or June 2026.
*   The SSC CGL 2025 Tier 2 results, along with post-group-wise cutoffs, have been officially declared.

## SSC CGL 2026 Notification and Application Form

The Staff Selection Commission is expected to release the SSC CGL 2026 notification on its official website, ssc.nic.in, in the near future. This comprehensive PDF document will contain all essential information regarding the examination, such as the total number of vacancies (categorized by post), the detailed application procedure, the selection process, the syllabus, exam pattern, and crucial dates. The online application process will commence alongside the notification release, and candidates will have a designated period to complete and submit their forms.

Last year, a total of 14,582 vacancies were announced. The commission recruits for Group B and C roles in various government bodies through this examination.

## SSC CGL 2025 Tier 2 Result and Cutoff

The Staff Selection Commission has officially released the results for the SSC Combined Graduate Level Exam 2025 Tier 2 on ssc.nic.in. The result PDF includes candidates' roll numbers, names, parents' names, categories, and marks obtained in the Tier 2 examination.

Alongside the results, the commission also published the post-group-wise SSC CGL cutoff scores. The Tier 2 examination for 2025 was conducted on January 18 and 19. Previously, the Tier 1 results for 2025 were declared on December 18, 2025, following the examination held from September 12 to 26 and on October 14 for 14,582 vacancies.

### How to Check SSC CGL 2025 Tier 2 Result

Candidates can check their results by following these steps:

*   Visit the official website: ssc.nic.in
*   Navigate to the 'Results' section and click on the SSC CGL Result PDF link.
*   Open the relevant post-wise result PDF that corresponds to the posts applied for.
*   Search for your roll number within the document.

It is advisable to save a copy of the result for future reference.

## SSC CGL Syllabus and Pattern 2026

The official notification for SSC CGL 2026 will detail the updated syllabus and examination pattern. Generally, the CGL exam comprises multiple tiers, typically including a computer-based Tier 1, a computer-based Tier 2, and in some cases, a Tier 3 (descriptive paper) and a Tier 4 (Data Entry Skill Test/Computer Proficiency Test). Candidates should refer to the upcoming official notification for precise details on the subjects, marking scheme, and duration for each tier.

Stay tuned to the official SSC website for the latest updates on the SSC CGL 2026 examination, including the notification, application form, and exam schedule.
