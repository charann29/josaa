# BITS BAAT 2026: Results, Dates, Shortlist, Syllabus, BITS MBA Admission Process

The BITS Business Analytics Aptitude Test (BITS BAAT) is the entrance examination conducted by the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani, for admission to its MBA in Business Analytics program. This page provides a comprehensive overview of the BITS BAAT 2026, including key dates, the application process, and selection criteria.

## Key Takeaways

*   BITS BAAT is a computer-based test for admission to the MBA in Business Analytics program at BITS Pilani.
*   The exam evaluates candidates on Management Aptitude and Analytics Aptitude over a three-hour duration.
*   Eligibility requires a bachelor's degree with at least 60% aggregate marks.
*   Selection involves a combination of BAAT scores, academic performance, work experience, and a personal interview, among other factors.

## Understanding BITS BAAT

BITS BAAT, or the BITS Business Analytics Aptitude Test, is specifically designed for candidates seeking admission to the Master of Business Administration (MBA) program with a specialization in Business Analytics at BITS Pilani. The examination is structured to assess a candidate's readiness for a demanding program that combines business acumen with analytical skills. It comprises two main sections: Management Aptitude and Analytics Aptitude, and candidates are given three hours to complete the entire test.

## BITS BAAT 2026 Results and Selection Process

BITS Pilani releases the BITS BAAT 2026 scorecards for all candidates who appeared for the computer-based test. Based on these scores, a shortlist of candidates is prepared for the subsequent selection rounds. These rounds typically include group discussions (GD), personal interviews (PI), written ability tests (WAT), or extempore speaking sessions. BITS Pilani is responsible for conducting these interview rounds online for the shortlisted candidates.

### BITS MBA Selection Criteria

Admission to the MBA in Business Analytics program at BITS Pilani is based on a comprehensive evaluation of various components, each assigned a specific weightage. This holistic approach ensures that candidates are assessed on multiple dimensions beyond just their entrance exam performance.

| BITS Selection Components   | Weightage |
| :-------------------------- | :-------- |
| Class 12 Performance        | 5         |
| Qualifying Degree Performance | 5         |
| CAT/XAT/GMAT Performance    | 10        |
| Work Experience             | 5         |
| Personal Interview          | 20        |
| Extempore                   | 5         |
| BAAT Performance            | 50        |

## BITS BAAT 2026 Application Process

The application for BITS BAAT 2026 is conducted online through the official BITS admission portal, bitsadmission.com. Prospective candidates must ensure they complete their registration and submit the application form before the specified deadline. Eligibility for application requires a bachelor's degree with an aggregate score of 60% or above.

For the most current information and to begin your application, always refer to the official BITS admission website.
