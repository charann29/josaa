# Digital Marketing Certification Training by Edureka: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to dive into the dynamic world of digital marketing, Edureka offers a certification program designed to equip you with essential skills. This training aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of various digital marketing facets, making you proficient in strategies that drive business growth.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program offers 30 hours of live, instructor-led online classes, ensuring interactive learning.
*   Participants gain practical experience through assignments and real-life case studies, culminating in a certification project.
*   Upon successful completion, Edureka awards a 'Digital Marketing Professional' certificate.
*   The course is suitable for both new learners and experienced professionals, with flexible payment options available.

## Course Overview

Edureka's Digital Marketing Certification Training focuses on building expertise across key areas of digital media. You will learn critical skills such as Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Keyword Planning, Google Analytics, Email Marketing, Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Affiliate Marketing, and Social Media Marketing (SMM). The curriculum is structured to provide a strategic understanding of how digital marketing impacts brand presence and sales.

The training covers best practices, tools, and techniques vital for business expansion. It also delves into budgeting, planning, reporting, and tracking mechanisms specific to digital marketing, highlighting its advantages over traditional methods. The program features continuous communication with instructors through live online sessions and offers lifetime access to the learning management system (LMS).

## Learning Experience and Content

The course is delivered in English, primarily through a mix of virtual classroom sessions and self-study materials, including video and text. Classes are typically held on weekends, making it accessible for working professionals. The program emphasizes hands-on learning with practical assignments and real-life case studies, allowing you to develop digital marketing strategies for both B2B and B2C products.

Key learning outcomes include:

*   Mastering various features of digital marketing and creating effective marketing plans.
*   Understanding SEO logic and driving website traffic through keyword planning.
*   Utilizing webmaster tools for On-Page SEO and increasing organic traffic.
*   Becoming proficient in social media marketing strategies.
*   Gaining knowledge of Google Analytics for data-driven decisions.

## Eligibility and Certification

While a basic understanding of marketing and internet usage is beneficial, it is not strictly mandatory for enrolling in the Digital Marketing Certification Training. The program is designed to accommodate individuals from diverse backgrounds, including freshers and seasoned professionals.

To earn the 'Digital Marketing Professional' certificate from Edureka, participants must attend all live online sessions. The certification process culminates in a project, where your performance will be evaluated by a panel of industry experts. Based on this evaluation, you will be awarded the certificate along with a grade.

## Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Digital Marketing Certification Training program is ₹19,995, inclusive of GST. Edureka offers flexible payment solutions, including the option to pay in a lump sum or through no-cost EMIs, which can start from approximately ₹6,665 per month. Potential discounts on the course price may also be available.
