# NID Entrance Exam 2026: B.Des Admit Card (OUT), B.Des Result (OUT), City-wise M.Des Interview Date (OUT), Expected Cutoff

The National Institute of Design (NID) Entrance Exam is a highly competitive national-level test for aspiring designers. This page offers crucial updates and details for the 2026 admissions cycle for both Bachelor of Design (B.Des) and Master of Design (M.Des) programs.

## Key takeaways

*   The NID DAT Studio Sensitivity Test admit cards for B.Des Mains were released on April 9, 2026, for shortlisted candidates.
*   NID DAT B.Des Prelims results have been declared, and the rechecking window recently closed on April 4, 2026.
*   M.Des interviews for 2026 were conducted between March 21 and April 1, 2026, across Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, and Gandhinagar.
*   The NID DAT Prelims exam for both B.Des and M.Des programs took place on December 21, 2025.

## What is the NID DAT Entrance Exam?

The NID Design Aptitude Test (DAT) is a comprehensive examination conducted by the National Institute of Design to assess candidates' creative aptitude, problem-solving abilities, innovative thinking, and visual perception. It serves as the gateway for admission to the prestigious B.Des and M.Des programs offered at NID campuses across India.

## NID Admit Card and Results 2026

### B.Des Admit Card (Studio Sensitivity Test)

The admit cards for the NID DAT B.Des Studio Sensitivity Test (Mains Exam) were released on April 9, 2026, at 4 PM. Only candidates who successfully cleared the NID DAT Prelims were issued these admit cards. NID has yet to announce the specific dates for the B.Des Mains exams.

### B.Des Prelims Result

The NID DAT Prelims results for the B.Des program have been declared. A rechecking window was available for candidates until April 4, 2026, at 11:59 PM.

### M.Des Interview Schedule

NID conducted interviews for the M.Des program between March 21, 2026, and April 1, 2026. These interviews were held at designated centers in Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, and Gandhinagar. The precise address for each center was provided on the candidate's NID Admit Card. It was mandatory for candidates to carry the same admit card used for their Studio Test, bearing the official seal of the NID Studio Test Centre.

## Expected Cutoff and Further Steps

While official cutoff scores are released by NID post-results, candidates can anticipate the cutoffs to be competitive, reflecting the high demand for NID programs. Factors such as the number of applicants, the difficulty of the exam, and the available seats influence these scores. Keep an eye on the official NID website for the release of final cutoff lists and further instructions regarding counseling and admissions.
