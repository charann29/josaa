# Creative Courses After 12th Commerce - Check Eligibility, Duration, Top Colleges

While many commerce students traditionally aim for careers in finance or accounting, a vibrant world of creative professions also awaits. Your business acumen from commerce studies can be a significant advantage, helping you turn creative passions into successful ventures.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your commerce background provides a solid foundation in business, valuable for entrepreneurial creative pursuits.
*   Creative fields offer diverse career paths beyond traditional commerce roles, including design, media, and culinary arts.
*   Admission to top creative programs often involves entrance exams, portfolio submissions, and interviews, not just academic scores.
*   Many creative courses do not require specific subjects like mathematics in 12th grade, focusing more on aptitude and passion.

## Creative Courses: Event Management

Event Management is a dynamic field that allows you to orchestrate various gatherings, from corporate conferences to grand weddings. This course develops skills in planning, logistics, marketing, and client relations, making it a great fit for commerce students who enjoy organization and communication.

**Eligibility and Admission:** Typically, you need a minimum of 45-50% aggregate marks in your 10+2 examinations. Admissions are often based on either your academic merit or success in an entrance exam conducted by the respective institution.

**Top Colleges:** Some well-regarded institutions include NIEM Mumbai, AAFT Noida, and NDIEM New Delhi.

## Creative Courses: Design

Design is a vast and exciting domain with numerous specializations like fashion, textile, footwear, jewellery, and product design. A Bachelor of Design (B.Des) program, usually lasting three to four years, hones your creative vision, production skills, and understanding of manufacturing processes relevant to your chosen industry.

**Eligibility and Admission:** You'll generally need 50-60% aggregate marks in 10+2. Admission to premier design institutes often requires clearing competitive entrance exams such as NID DAT, NIFT Entrance Exam, or UCEED. This is usually followed by a personal interview and the submission of a creative portfolio demonstrating your aptitude.

**Top Colleges:** Leading institutions include the National Institute of Design (NID) Ahmedabad, various National Institutes of Fashion Technology (NIFTs), IIT Bombay, NMIMS, Srishti Manipal Institute of Art, Design & Technology Bangalore, and CEPT University Ahmedabad. You can find more details on top colleges through resources like the NIRF rankings.

## Creative Courses: Liberal Arts

Liberal Arts is a multidisciplinary undergraduate program designed to broaden your intellectual horizons and help you discover your passions across various fields. It allows you to combine majors and minors from diverse subjects like mathematics, English, philosophy, psychology, performing arts, visual arts, and media studies, fostering critical thinking and a well-rounded perspective.

**Eligibility and Admission:** Most programs require 50-60% aggregate marks in 10+2. The admission process typically involves an entrance examination followed by an interview.

These creative paths offer exciting alternatives for commerce students to build unique and fulfilling careers.
