## Fees and Admission Process

While specific fee structures vary by institution and program, aspiring students should budget for tuition, examination fees, and other associated costs. The admission process generally involves:

1.  **Application:** Submitting an online or offline application to the desired college.
2.  **Entrance Exam:** Appearing for and qualifying in one of the accepted entrance exams (MAT, XAT, CAT, JEMAT).
3.  **Shortlisting:** Colleges shortlisting candidates based on exam scores, academic record, and work experience (if any).
4.  **GD/PI Round:** Participating in Group Discussions (GD) and Personal Interviews (PI).
5.  **Final Offer:** Receiving an admission offer based on overall performance.

It's always advisable to refer to the official college websites for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding application deadlines, fee structures, and the detailed admission process for the 2026 academic year.
