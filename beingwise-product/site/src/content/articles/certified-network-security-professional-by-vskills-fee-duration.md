# Certified Network Security Professional by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to build a career in the crucial field of cybersecurity, the Certified Network Security Professional program by Vskills offers a valuable credential. This self-paced, online course is designed to equip you with essential knowledge and skills to protect digital networks from various threats.

## Key takeaways

*   The certification is recognized by the government and provides a 'Vskills certified' tag for your professional profile.
*   Learning is entirely online and self-directed, allowing you to study at your own convenience.
*   The program covers a broad spectrum of network security topics, from foundational concepts to advanced defense mechanisms.
*   A lifelong valid certificate is awarded upon successful completion of the online evaluation.

## Course Overview

This program delves into the core aspects of network security, preparing participants to understand and defend against cyber intrusions. You will gain insights into various types of network vulnerabilities and the strategies to mitigate them, crucial for safeguarding an organization's digital assets. The curriculum is structured to enhance your ability to implement and manage robust security measures.

Topics covered include fundamental networking concepts, principles of firewalls, hardware security, and comprehensive security management. The course also explores web and wireless security, operating system security, and cryptographic techniques, providing a holistic view of the cybersecurity landscape.

## Who Is This Certification For?

This certification is particularly beneficial for individuals aspiring to, or already working in, roles such as network technicians, network architects, or IT engineers. It is an excellent choice for graduates and professionals who wish to specialize in network security or enhance their current skill set to pursue better job opportunities within IT departments or security management roles across various industries. If you are aiming for employment in the network security domain, this program can significantly boost your profile.

## Program Offerings and Learning Experience

Vskills provides a comprehensive online learning environment for this certification. The program includes:

*   **Online Test:** An evaluation to assess your understanding and knowledge.
*   **E-learning:** Access to all course materials in digital format.
*   **Online Expert Seminars:** Opportunities to learn from industry professionals.
*   **Online Practice Examination & Mock Exams:** Tools to help you prepare thoroughly for the final assessment.

The instruction medium is English, and the learning mode is entirely self-study, delivered through a blend of video and text-based content. This flexibility allows you to progress through the material at your own pace, fitting study into your personal schedule.

## Fees and Certification Details

The fee for the Certified Network Security Professional course is **₹ 3,499**. This amount covers access to all learning materials and the online examination. Upon successfully passing the evaluation, you will receive a certificate from Vskills, which is recognized by the government.

To earn the certification, candidates must achieve a minimum score of 50% on an online multiple-choice examination. This exam consists of 50 questions designed to test your grasp of the course content. Successful candidates will not only receive the government-witnessed certificate but also a 'Vskills certified' tag, which can be prominently displayed on professional networking and job search platforms.

This certification offers a practical pathway to acquiring critical skills in network security, making you a more competitive candidate in the evolving job market.
