# EDGE Metaversity Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online EDGE Metaversity Courses

EDGE Metaversity offers a range of specialized certificate programs designed for individuals passionate about the evolving fields of animation, virtual production, visual effects (VFX), and game development. These courses aim to equip students with industry-relevant skills through practical, in-person training.

## Key Takeaways

*   EDGE Metaversity provides professional certificate courses in cutting-edge creative technologies.
*   Programs cover areas like Unreal Engine Virtual Production, Animation with Autodesk Maya, and VFX Compositing with NukeX and Houdini.
*   Most master certificate courses are 9-11 months long and require in-person attendance at EDGE campuses across India.
*   No prior experience in game design, CG, or VFX is needed for many courses, making them accessible to enthusiastic beginners.

## Master Certificate in Unreal Engine Virtual Production

This 9-month (300-hour) Master Certificate program focuses on the intricacies of Unreal Engine for virtual production. Delivered in person, it's designed for aspiring storytellers eager to blend visual effects with narrative. The curriculum progresses from foundational concepts like computer graphics, storytelling principles, and camera techniques to advanced topics in computer graphics and compositing.

A key advantage of this course is its accessibility; you don't need any previous background in game design, computer graphics, or VFX to enroll. Upon completion, students will have developed a portfolio film, showcasing their expertise in virtual production.

*   **Duration:** 9 months (300 hours)
*   **Mode:** In-person
*   **Fee:** ₹ 2,97,050

## Master Certificate in Animation with Autodesk Maya

For those dreaming of a career in animation, the Master Certificate in Animation with Autodesk Maya offers a comprehensive 9-month offline training. This program is exclusively offered by EDGE Metaversity and is affiliated with Trazos, a highly regarded animation school based in Spain. The curriculum emphasizes mastering Autodesk Maya, a standard software in the animation industry.

The demand for computer-generated images and animations is rapidly expanding across television, films, games, and virtual reality platforms. This course aims to enhance students' productivity and proficiency in animation, preparing them for a dynamic industry. Skills covered include a broad range of animation techniques.

*   **Duration:** 9 months
*   **Mode:** In-person
*   **Fee:** ₹ 2,97,050

## Master Certificates in VFX Compositing and Effects

EDGE Metaversity offers two distinct master certificate programs for visual effects, both 9 months long and taught offline at Pearl Campuses:

### Master Certificate in VFX Compositing with NukeX

This professional course, also affiliated with Trazos, focuses on NukeX technology, which is instrumental in creating the stunning environments and backgrounds seen in major action films. The curriculum is meticulously crafted to equip candidates with in-depth knowledge of VFX animation technologies, enabling them to build a successful career in compositing. Skills covered include advanced animation and compositing techniques.

### Master Certificate in VFX with Houdini

SideFX Houdini is a highly sought-after software for generating visual effects in both domestic and international films and television series. This 9-month professional certificate program from EDGE Metaversity guides participants through creating breathtaking effects using Houdini, preparing them for roles in the fast-paced world of visual effects. This course is ideal for those looking to specialize in procedural generation and dynamic simulations.
