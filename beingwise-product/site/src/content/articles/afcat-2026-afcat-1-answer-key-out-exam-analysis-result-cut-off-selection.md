# AFCAT 2026: Answer Key, Exam Analysis, Result, Cut off, and Selection Process

*   Successful candidates from the online test will proceed to the Air Force Selection Board (AFSB) interview stage.

## AFCAT Selection Process

The AFCAT selection process is a multi-stage procedure designed to identify suitable candidates for the Indian Air Force. It primarily consists of two main phases:

1.  **Online Written Examination (AFCAT):** This is the initial screening test, assessing candidates on General Awareness, Verbal Ability in English, Numerical Ability, and Reasoning and Military Aptitude.
2.  **Air Force Selection Board (AFSB) Interview:** Candidates who clear the written exam are called for the AFSB interview. This comprehensive evaluation includes psychological tests, group tasks, and personal interviews to assess leadership qualities, personality, and aptitude for a career in the Air Force.

Successful candidates from the AFSB interview then undergo a medical examination. A final merit list is prepared based on the combined performance in the written test and the AFSB interview, subject to medical fitness and availability of vacancies.

## AFCAT 2026 Result and Cut-Off

The results for AFCAT 1 2026 are anticipated to be announced in February. Candidates can check their results on the official Indian Air Force recruitment portal. Along with the results, the cut-off marks for qualifying for the AFSB interview will also be released. The cut-off varies each year based on factors such as the number of vacancies, the difficulty level of the exam, and the total number of candidates who appeared.

Keep an eye on the official Indian Air Force recruitment website for the latest updates on results and cut-off scores.
