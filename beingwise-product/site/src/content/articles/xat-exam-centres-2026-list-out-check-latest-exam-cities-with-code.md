# XAT Exam Centres 2026 List (OUT): Check Latest Exam Cities with Code

The XAT exam is a crucial step for many aspiring management students. Understanding the available test centres and how to select them is vital for a smooth examination experience. This guide provides a comprehensive list of XAT 2026 exam cities and important information regarding the centre allocation process.

## Key takeaways

*   The XAT 2026 exam will be conducted in 109 cities across India, with at least one centre in each city.
*   Applicants can select up to six preferred cities when filling out the XAT application form.
*   Centre allocation is based on a first-come, first-served basis, making early application beneficial.
*   The specific address of your allotted exam centre will be printed on your XAT 2026 admit card.

## How to Select XAT Test Cities

The process for choosing your preferred XAT test cities is integrated into the online application form. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1.  Begin by registering for XAT 2026 on the official XAT website.
2.  Log in using your credentials to access the application form.
3.  Navigate through the form until you reach the 'select test city' section.
4.  From the provided list, choose up to six cities in order of your preference.
5.  Complete the remaining sections of the application form.
6.  Proceed to make the required fee payment and submit your application.

### Important Considerations for Selecting Exam Cities

When making your selections, keep the following points in mind:

*   You have the option to pick up to six cities from the official list.
*   Always list your cities in genuine order of preference, with your most convenient location as the first choice.
*   Consider selecting your current hometown or residence as your top preference to minimize travel stress on exam day.
*   If all seats in your first-choice city are filled, you will be allocated a centre in your next preferred city.
*   The examination authority retains the right to modify or cancel any test centre.
*   The final decision regarding centre allotment rests solely with the exam conducting body.
*   No requests for changes to your allotted centre will be entertained once the XAT 2026 admit card has been released. Therefore, choose your cities carefully.
