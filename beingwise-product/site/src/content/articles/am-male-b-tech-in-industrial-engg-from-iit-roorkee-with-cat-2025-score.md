# Your Chances in Top B-Schools with an IIT Roorkee Background and 98.89 CAT Percentile

With a B.Tech in Industrial Engineering from IIT Roorkee and a strong CAT 2025 score of 98.89 percentile, your profile is indeed very competitive for India's leading business schools.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your IIT background significantly strengthens your application, especially for schools that consider overall profile.
*   While the very top IIMs (Ahmedabad, Bangalore) are highly competitive for male engineers, your strong sectional scores and academic pedigree give you a fighting chance.
*   You have excellent prospects for interview calls from many other highly-ranked IIMs and top private B-schools.
*   Thorough preparation for the Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) will be crucial for converting these calls into admissions.

## Understanding the Top IIMs: A, B, and C

For the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) in Ahmedabad and Bangalore, the admission process is intensely competitive, particularly for male engineers. While your 98.89 percentile is exceptional, these institutes often see cutoffs pushing into the 99+ range for general male candidates. Your IIT Roorkee degree is a significant advantage, potentially helping you secure a waitlist or borderline interview call. However, the final decision will heavily depend on your overall academic record, including your 10th and 12th-grade marks, and your interview performance.

IIM Calcutta tends to be slightly more flexible. While a high CAT percentile is essential, your comprehensive profile, including your academic achievements and any extracurriculars, will play a more defining role. If your past academic scores (10th and 12th grade) are strong, your chances here improve considerably.

## FMS Delhi, SPJIMR Mumbai, and Other Top Institutes

Faculty of Management Studies (FMS), University of Delhi, is renowned for being highly CAT-score focused. While your 98.89 percentile is robust, FMS often sees even higher percentiles for admission, with a preference for strong performance in the Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC) section. Despite this, your score places you in a very competitive position.

SPJIMR Mumbai takes a holistic approach, evaluating not just CAT scores but also academic history, work experience (if any), and extracurricular involvement. Your IIT background is a major asset here, potentially offsetting a percentile that's just shy of the 99+ mark.

For other highly-regarded institutions such as IIM Lucknow, IIM Kozhikode, and IIM Indore, your profile is very well-suited. These IIMs consider a blend of CAT scores and academic background, where your IIT degree will be a strong positive. Similarly, for MDI Gurgaon and DMS IIT Delhi, your chances are excellent, given your strong academic foundation and impressive CAT score.

## Preparing for the Next Steps: WAT and PI

Receiving interview calls is the first major hurdle; converting them is the next. Given your strong profile, you are very likely to receive calls from a wide range of top B-schools. Your preparation for the Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) will be paramount. Focus on articulating your experiences from IIT Roorkee, your career aspirations, and how a management degree aligns with your goals. Be ready to discuss current affairs, ethical dilemmas, and demonstrate strong communication skills.

Your profile is highly competitive, and with diligent preparation for the interview process, you stand an excellent chance of securing admission to a top B-school.
