# Online Executive Part Time PGP in Cloud Computing in association with IBM by Aegis School of Business, Data Science, Cyber Security and Telecommunication: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This executive Post Graduate Program (PGP) in Cloud Computing, developed in collaboration with IBM, offers a comprehensive online learning experience designed for professionals. It aims to bridge the current industry gap in skilled cloud computing experts by providing in-depth knowledge and practical application.

## Key Takeaways

*   An 11-month part-time online program focusing on cloud computing.
*   Live interactive sessions are held on weekends, complemented by self-study and video/text-based content.
*   Includes a PGP certificate upon completion from Aegis and IBM.
*   Offers valuable practical experience through projects and assignments.

## Program Overview

This specialized PGP is an 11-month online program jointly offered by Aegis School of Business, Data Science, Cyber Security and Telecommunication and IBM. It is specifically designed to equip participants with the skills needed to develop and utilize various cloud applications, covering both their business aspects and crucial security considerations. The curriculum also explores the convergence of cloud and mobile computing.

Classes are delivered through a blend of online live sessions conducted on weekends and self-paced learning resources available throughout the week. The program is taught entirely in English and features a world-class curriculum with 36 course credits, recognized globally for further master's degree pursuits.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a deep understanding of cloud computing concepts and their real-world applications. Participants will gain expertise in:

*   Developing and deploying applications in cloud environments.
*   Understanding the business implications of cloud technologies.
*   Implementing robust security measures for cloud-based applications.
*   The interplay between cloud computing and mobile technology.

The program offers a diverse range of core and elective subjects, ensuring a well-rounded and cutting-edge learning experience.

## Eligibility and Certification

To successfully complete the program and receive the certification, participants must meet all course requirements. Upon successful completion, candidates will be awarded a PGP Certification in Cloud Computing program jointly from IBM and Aegis School of Business, Data Science, Cyber Security and Telecommunication. This certification is a testament to industry-relevant skills acquired during the program.

## Admission Process

The admission process for the Online Executive Part-Time PGP in Cloud Computing is straightforward:

1.  **Online Registration:** Candidates begin by visiting the official program portal and initiating the registration process.
2.  **Application Form:** After registration, applicants must complete a detailed application form, providing information such as their name, educational qualifications, employment details, CV, and referee information.
3.  **Personal Interview:** Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a personal interview.
4.  **Offer Letter:** Successful candidates who clear the interview will receive an offer letter for admission to the program.

## Program Benefits

This program offers several advantages for professionals looking to advance their careers in cloud computing:

*   **Industry Collaboration:** The association with IBM ensures a curriculum that is current and aligned with industry demands.
*   **Global Recognition:** The 36 credit units earned are accepted internationally, potentially aiding in future academic pursuits like master's degrees.
*   **Practical Learning:** The program emphasizes practical application through live projects, assignments, and access to a robust Learning Management System (LMS).
*   **Career Advancement:** The PGP certification is designed to enhance employability and open doors to high-paying roles in the cloud computing sector.

This program is an excellent opportunity for professionals to gain specialized knowledge and a recognized qualification in the rapidly expanding field of cloud computing.
