# CUET Exam Date 2026 (Out): Check Complete Schedule for CUET UG Subjects Here

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) for undergraduate programs in 2026 is a crucial examination for students aiming for admission to various central, state, deemed, and private universities across India. The National Testing Agency (NTA) is responsible for conducting this exam and releasing all official dates and schedules.

## Key Takeaways

*   The CUET UG 2026 examination is tentatively scheduled to take place between May 11 and May 31, 2026.
*   The NTA will release the detailed subject-wise schedule and shift timings in a PDF format on the official CUET website.
*   Advance intimation slips, detailing exam city and date, are expected in the last week of April 2026.
*   Admit cards will likely be available for download approximately 2-3 days before the examination commences.

## Understanding the CUET UG 2026 Schedule

The NTA is expected to announce the complete CUET UG 2026 datesheet shortly on the official CUET portal. This schedule will outline the specific dates and shifts for each subject, allowing candidates to prepare effectively. The examination will be conducted in Computer Based Test (CBT) mode.

## Application Process and Correction Window

The application process for CUET UG 2026 saw a re-opening window from February 23 to February 26, 2026, providing an additional opportunity for interested candidates to register. Prior to this, a correction window was available from February 09 to February 11, 2026, allowing registered applicants to make necessary edits to their application forms. It's crucial for candidates to stay updated with official announcements from NTA regarding any further application or correction windows.

## Intimation Slip and Admit Card

### Advance Intimation Slip

Around the last week of April 2026, the NTA is expected to release the advance intimation slips for CUET UG 2026. These slips will inform registered candidates about their examination city and the tentative date of their exam. This allows students to make necessary travel arrangements in advance. Candidates can download these slips from the official CUET website using their login credentials.

### CUET UG Admit Card

The official admit card for CUET UG 2026 will be made available for download approximately 2-3 days before the commencement of the examination. It is anticipated that the admit cards will be downloadable from May 08, 2026, onwards. The admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the exam and will contain vital information such as the candidate's name, roll number, photograph, signature, exam date and time, specific subject details, the allotted CUET exam centre, and important test day guidelines. Candidates must carefully check all details on their admit card and contact the NTA immediately if they find any discrepancies.

## Wide Range of Courses Through CUET

CUET UG facilitates admissions to a diverse array of undergraduate programs across participating universities. Some of the popular courses accessible through CUET include:

*   Bachelor of Science (BSc), Integrated Master of Science (MSc)
*   Bachelor of Technology (BTech), Integrated Master of Technology (MTech)
*   Bachelor of Vocational Studies (BVoc), Integrated BSc BEd
*   Bachelor of Arts (BA), Integrated Master of Arts (MA), Integrated BA BEd
*   Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
*   Integrated BA LLB (Hons), Bachelor of Laws (LLB)
*   Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
*   Bachelor of Commerce (BCom), Integrated Master of Commerce (MCom)
*   Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA)
*   Bachelor of Performing Arts (BPA) in Music, and various Diploma programs.

This comprehensive list highlights the extensive opportunities CUET offers to students from various academic backgrounds.
