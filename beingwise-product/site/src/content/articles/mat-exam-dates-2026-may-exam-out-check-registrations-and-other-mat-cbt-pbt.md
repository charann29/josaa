# MAT Exam Dates 2026 (May Exam OUT): Check Registrations & Other MAT CBT/PBT Dates

The Management Aptitude Test (MAT) is a widely recognized national-level entrance examination for MBA and allied programs. The All India Management Association (AIMA) has officially announced the MAT May 2026 exam dates, and registration is currently open.

## Key takeaways

*   The MAT May 2026 Paper-Based Test (PBT) is scheduled for May 31, 2026.
*   The MAT May 2026 Computer-Based Test (CBT) will take place on June 14, 2026.
*   Registration for the MAT May 2026 session is ongoing, with separate deadlines for PBT and CBT modes.
*   The application fee for MAT has seen a slight increase of INR 100 starting from the May 2026 session.

## MAT 2026 Application Process and Fees

To apply for MAT 2026, candidates must complete and submit the application form online via the official AIMA MAT website. The application process is consistent for both PBT and CBT modes; applicants simply select their preferred exam format within the form.

The deadline for PBT registration is May 25, 2026, while for CBT, it is June 8, 2026. The application fee is INR 2300 for a single MAT session. If a candidate wishes to appear for two sessions, the fee is INR 4000.

## MAT 2026 Admit Card Details

AIMA issues separate admit cards for each MAT session. For the May 2026 session, admit cards will be released approximately 2-3 days before the respective exam dates. Candidates can download their admit cards from the AIMA website using their login credentials. It is mandatory to carry a printed copy of the admit card to the examination center on the day of the test.

## Understanding the MAT Exam Format

The MAT examination is conducted in a single morning shift, typically from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM. The test has a duration of two hours, during which candidates are required to attempt 150 questions. There are no scheduled breaks during the exam. While it's not compulsory to answer every question, candidates should manage their time effectively to maximize their score.

## MAT 2026 Result and Scorecard

Once the MAT exams are conducted, AIMA will upload the scorecards to the candidate dashboards on the official exam website. Test-takers will also receive email notifications regarding the result release. Candidates can download their MAT results using their login details. The scorecards for both CBT and PBT modes are usually released on the same day, and the download procedure is identical.

## MAT Admission Process Based on Scores

Upon result declaration, AIMA forwards the MAT scores directly to the five B-schools that candidates select during their application process. If a candidate's score meets the cut-off criteria set by these institutions, they will be invited to participate in the subsequent MBA selection rounds. Additionally, candidates can independently apply to other colleges that accept MAT scores, leveraging their performance in the exam.

Stay informed with these key dates to plan your MAT 2026 preparation and application strategy effectively.
