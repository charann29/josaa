# AWS: Essential Optimisation for Cloud Cost Management by Amazon Web Services via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding and managing expenses in the cloud is crucial for any organisation leveraging Amazon Web Services (AWS). This course, offered through Futurelearn in collaboration with AWS, provides foundational knowledge to effectively control and optimise your cloud spending.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn essential strategies for managing and reducing your AWS cloud costs.
*   Understand various AWS tools designed for cost visibility and reporting.
*   The course is self-paced and delivered entirely online through video and text-based modules.
*   While the course content is free, a certificate of completion is available from Futurelearn.

## Course Overview and Structure

This program is designed for self-study, allowing you to learn at your own pace. It is estimated to require approximately four hours of effort per week. The instruction is in English, and the content is delivered through a blend of video and text formats.

The curriculum is thoughtfully divided into three weekly modules:

*   **Week 1: Welcome to Cloud Cost Management**
    *   This initial module sets the stage, preparing you for the core concepts and principles of cloud cost management.
*   **Week 2: AWS Services for Cost Visibility**
    *   You will delve into specific AWS services and features that provide insight into your spending patterns and resource utilisation.
*   **Week 3: Cost Optimisation Strategies**
    *   The final week focuses on practical strategies to optimise your cloud expenditure, culminating in a final assessment to test your understanding.

## Certification Details

While the learning content for this course is accessible at no charge, a certificate of completion is available for those who wish to formally recognise their achievement. This certificate is provided by Futurelearn, affirming your successful completion of the course material and assessments.

## Meet Your Instructors

The course content is developed and delivered by experienced professionals, ensuring you receive insights from industry experts:

*   **Ms. Morgan Willis**
    *   A Senior Cloud Technologist at Amazon Web Services, Ms. Willis brings valuable real-world expertise to the curriculum.
*   **Mr. Russ Sayers**
    *   An independent instructor, Mr. Sayers contributes his teaching experience to guide you through the course material.

## Who Should Enroll?

This course is ideal for anyone involved in managing AWS environments, including cloud architects, developers, IT managers, finance professionals, and business owners looking to gain better control over their cloud expenditure. A basic understanding of AWS services would be beneficial but is not strictly required.

Taking this course can significantly enhance your ability to make informed decisions about your cloud infrastructure and budget.
