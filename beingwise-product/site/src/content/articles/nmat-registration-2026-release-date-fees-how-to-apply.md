# NMAT Registration 2026: Release Date, Fees, How to Apply

For those aiming for MBA admissions, the NMAT by GMAC exam is a crucial step. This guide outlines the registration process for NMAT 2026, including key dates, fees, and how to successfully complete your application.

## Key Takeaways

*   NMAT 2026 registration is expected to open in the first week of August 2026.
*   You can attempt the NMAT exam up to three times within the testing window.
*   A 15-day gap is required between two consecutive NMAT attempts.
*   The entire application and fee payment process is conducted online via the official NMAT portal.

## NMAT 2026 Registration and Exam Dates

The registration and examination period for NMAT 2026 typically spans several months. It's important to keep these tentative dates in mind when planning your application and study schedule.

| Event                            | Tentative Dates       |

| NMAT 2026 Registration Window    | August - October 2026 |
| NMAT 2026 Scheduling Window      | August - October 2026 |
| NMAT 2026 Admit Card Issuance    | October - December 2026 |

## Understanding the NMAT Registration Process

Registering for the NMAT exam involves a few straightforward steps, all completed online. Here’s a breakdown of how to fill out the application form:

1.  **Visit the Official NMAT Website:** Navigate to the official NMAT by GMAC website.
2.  **Create an Account:** You'll need to create an account on the mba.com portal. This involves providing basic details and verifying a captcha code. This same account can be used for other GMAC exams like the GMAT.
3.  **Log In:** Use your newly created credentials to log in to your dashboard.
4.  **Access NMAT Dashboard:** From your login area, select the option to go to the NMAT by GMAC Dashboard.
5.  **Fill Personal Details:** Complete the personal information section and upload a recent passport-sized photograph. Ensure the photo meets the specified guidelines.
6.  **Enter Contact Information:** Provide your contact details, including an alternative number, communication address, and permanent address.
7.  **Academic and Work Experience:** Detail your educational background and any relevant work experience.
8.  **Select School Preferences:** Choose the management schools you wish to send your NMAT scores to. You may also be asked about other MBA exams you plan to take.
9.  **Review and Pay:** Carefully review all the information you've entered in the application form. Once confirmed, proceed to pay the NMAT registration fee.

## NMAT Registration Fees 2026

The NMAT exam allows candidates to attempt it multiple times. Each attempt, including the initial registration and any subsequent retakes, requires a separate fee payment. The Graduate Management Admission Council (GMAC) sets these fees, and they must be paid online during the registration process. The specific fee structure for 2026 will be released by GMAC closer to the registration opening. There may also be a separate fee for rescheduling an exam.

## Image Upload Guidelines for NMAT Application

To avoid rejection of your NMAT application, it's vital to adhere to the specified guidelines for photograph submission. Here are the key requirements:

*   **Format:** The photograph must be in JPG, PNG, or JPEG format.
*   **Orientation:** Upload a portrait-oriented photograph.
*   **Visibility:** Your eyes must be open and clearly visible.
*   **Background:** The background should be white or a light, plain colour.
*   **Recency:** The photograph should not be more than six months old.
*   **Positioning:** You should be facing forward, with your face distinctly visible and centered in the image.
*   **Quality:** Photos that do not meet these specifications or size requirements may lead to application rejection.

Embarking on your MBA journey begins with a smooth application process. By following these instructions carefully, you can successfully register for the NMAT 2026 exam and take the first step towards your management education goals.
