# CAT 2025 Answer Key (Official Available): Download PDF

The Common Admission Test (CAT) answer key is a crucial document for aspirants to review their performance and estimate their scores. This page provides essential details about the official CAT 2025 answer key, including how to access it and important dates.

## Key Takeaways

*   The final CAT 2025 answer key was released on December 17, 2025, after considering candidate objections.
*   The provisional answer key and individual response sheets became available on December 4, 2025.
*   Candidates had a specific window from December 8 to December 10, 2025, to challenge the provisional answer key.
*   Out of 187 objections raised by test-takers, only one, related to the Quantitative Aptitude section, was officially accepted.

## Final CAT 2025 Answer Key

The final CAT 2025 answer key, like the provisional one, was accessible through the official IIMCAT login portal. This final release incorporated any valid changes identified after the objection-raising period.

Here's how candidates could access the final answer key:

*   Visit the official IIMCAT website.
*   Locate and click on the 'login' option.
*   Enter your unique user ID and password.
*   Navigate to the option for the final answer key.
*   Review the PDF to see the status of objections that were raised.

## Information Mentioned on the Official CAT Answer Key

The official CAT answer key typically includes several important pieces of information that help candidates verify their answers and calculate their probable scores. ## How to Calculate CAT Score with Answer Key?

Once you have your response sheet and the official answer key, you can calculate your raw score. For every correct answer, typically 3 marks are awarded. For every incorrect answer, 1 mark is usually deducted. Unattempted questions carry no penalty. By applying this marking scheme, you can arrive at your estimated raw score before normalization.
