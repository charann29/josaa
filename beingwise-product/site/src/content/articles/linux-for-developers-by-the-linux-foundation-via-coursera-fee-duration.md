# Linux for Developers by The Linux Foundation via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring and current developers looking to deepen their understanding of the Linux environment, this online course offers a comprehensive introduction. Delivered by The Linux Foundation through Coursera, it's designed to equip you with the essential skills for navigating and utilizing Linux effectively in software development.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a beginner-friendly, self-paced online course, ideal for those with some development background.
*   It focuses on foundational Linux concepts, command-line proficiency, and open-source software development.
*   The course offers flexible deadlines, approximately 18 hours of content, and a shareable certificate upon completion.
*   Available in English with subtitles in multiple languages, making it accessible to a wider audience.

## Course Overview and What You'll Learn

Part of the broader Open Course Software Development, Linux, and Git Specialization, the "Linux for Developers" course is structured to transform beginners into skilled professionals capable of working efficiently within a Linux system. You will gain a solid grasp of how the Linux environment operates and learn crucial methods and tools essential for meeting modern developer demands.

The curriculum covers:

*   **Fundamentals of Linux:** Understand the core concepts and historical context of the operating system.
*   **Open-Source Development:** Explore the world of open-source software development and Linux's role within it.
*   **Kernel Understanding:** Learn about the separation of the kernel from the operating system and contributions to its development.
*   **Command-Line Mastery:** Become proficient in using the command line for daily tasks and monitoring utilities.
*   **File System Management:** Gain skills in understanding the layout and partitioning of the Linux file system.
*   **Graphical System:** Comprehend the workings of the graphical system within the Linux environment.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

This course is primarily aimed at individuals with a background in development. While it's a beginner-level course for Linux itself, having some prior experience working with the command line will be beneficial. There are no strict academic prerequisites beyond an interest in computer science and software development.

## Course Duration and Structure

The "Linux for Developers" course is designed for self-paced learning, allowing you to progress at your own speed. It's estimated to take approximately 18 hours to complete. The learning format includes:

*   Online videos and reading materials.
*   Programming assignments.
*   Graded quizzes and assignments to test your understanding.
*   Opportunities for peer feedback.

Flexible deadlines mean you can fit the learning around your existing commitments.

## Fees and Certification

The course offers various subscription plans through Coursera. The fees are structured based on the duration you wish to access the content and complete the course:

Upon successfully completing all course requirements, including readings and practice exercises, you will receive a shareable course completion certificate from Coursera. This certificate validates your new skills and understanding of Linux for developers.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the "Linux for Developers" course is a straightforward online process:

1.  **Visit the Course Page:** Navigate to the official course page on Coursera.
2.  **Browse Computer Science:** Look for the "Explore" option, typically found on the platform, and select the "Computer Science" category, then specifically "Linux."
3.  **Find the Course:** From the list of available Linux programs, select "Linux for Developers." This will take you to the course-specific page.
4.  **Enroll:** On the course page, locate and click the "Enroll" button.
5.  **Follow Instructions:** Proceed with the on-screen instructions to finalize your enrollment. You may also have the option to start with a 7-day free trial.

There is no separate application form; enrollment is directly through the Coursera platform.

This course offers a valuable opportunity to enhance your development toolkit with essential Linux expertise, setting you on a path to greater proficiency in open-source environments.
