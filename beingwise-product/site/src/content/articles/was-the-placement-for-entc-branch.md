# What was the placement for ENTC Branch at K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology?

Understanding placement statistics is a crucial step for any student considering an engineering degree, especially for a specialized field like Electronics and Telecommunication (ENTC). Let's explore the general placement scenario at K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology, as specific branch-wise data can often be part of broader institutional reports.

## Key Takeaways

*   K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology has a robust placement record, attracting a significant number of companies annually.
*   The institute has seen its highest package reach ₹13 Lakhs per annum.
*   Graduates can generally anticipate an average annual salary package ranging between ₹6 to ₹7 Lakhs.
*   A diverse set of companies, including well-known names, regularly recruit from the campus.

## Overview of Placement Activities

K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology actively facilitates placements for its students across various engineering disciplines, including Electronics and Telecommunication. The institution focuses on preparing students for the industry, which reflects in the number of companies that visit the campus for recruitment drives.

In a recent academic year, the institute welcomed over 18 new companies, contributing to a substantial total of 207 companies participating in the placement process. This broad participation indicates strong industry interest in the talent pool developed at K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology.

## Salary Packages Offered

When evaluating placement opportunities, salary packages are a key consideration. The institute has reported impressive figures, with the highest annual salary package offered to a student reaching ₹13 Lakhs. This figure highlights the potential for students to secure high-value roles after completing their engineering studies.

For most graduates, the average annual salary package falls within the range of ₹6 to ₹7 Lakhs. This average provides a realistic expectation for students pursuing engineering, including those from the ENTC branch, upon graduation. It's important to remember that individual packages can vary based on skills, academic performance, and the specific company and role.

## Major Recruiting Companies

The quality and reputation of recruiting companies are strong indicators of an institute's industry connections. K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology attracts a mix of established corporations and growing firms. Some of the prominent companies that have recruited from the institute include Jaro Education, Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Accenture, Pinclick, and Amazon.

These companies represent various sectors, from IT services and education technology to e-commerce, offering a wide array of career paths for engineering graduates. For ENTC students, this diversity means opportunities in areas like software development, telecommunications, electronics design, and IT consulting, among others.

## Preparing for Placements

While the institute provides excellent placement opportunities, student preparation is equally vital. Developing strong technical skills, participating in internships, and enhancing soft skills like communication and problem-solving can significantly boost a student's chances of securing a desirable placement. The institute's career services typically offer guidance and training to help students excel in interviews and recruitment processes.

Understanding the general placement trends at K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology can help prospective ENTC students make an informed decision about their higher education journey.
