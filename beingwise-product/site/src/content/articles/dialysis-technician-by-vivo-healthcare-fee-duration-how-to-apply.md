# Dialysis Technician by VIVO Healthcare: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students interested in a vital role within the healthcare sector, VIVO Healthcare offers a comprehensive training program to become a Dialysis Technician. This course equips individuals with the essential skills to operate and maintain dialysis equipment, providing crucial support for patients with kidney conditions.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 12-month undergraduate program combining extensive theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on simulation and clinical experience.
*   Successful completion leads to a certification from VIVO Healthcare and the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC).
*   The curriculum focuses on the practical aspects of hemodialysis, patient monitoring, and equipment management.
*   Students receive placement assistance upon earning their certification, helping them transition into professional roles.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Dialysis Technician program is designed to transform aspiring healthcare professionals into skilled experts in dialysis care. The training spans 12 months, incorporating a total of 1600 hours of learning. This includes 800 hours dedicated to classroom theory and simulation exercises, followed by an equally important 800-hour clinical internship.

The curriculum is taught entirely in English and covers critical areas such as renal anatomy and physiology, the scientific principles of hemodialysis, and managing a dialysis unit. Students will learn how to operate dialysis machines, monitor patients during procedures, and manage vascular access for hemodialysis sessions.

## Eligibility Criteria and Certification

To be eligible for the Dialysis Technician course, applicants must have successfully completed their 10+2 education. There are no specific subject requirements beyond this general educational qualification.

Certification is awarded upon successful completion of the entire program. This includes passing various assessments throughout the course, such as weekly and module-based tests, a pre-internship examination, and a final foundation exam. Additionally, candidates must complete the clinical internship and ensure all course fees are paid. The certification is jointly provided by VIVO Healthcare and the NSDC, enhancing its recognition and value in the healthcare industry.

## What You Will Learn

The program provides a deep dive into the practical and scientific aspects of dialysis technology. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Operating Dialysis Machines:** Students will gain proficiency in setting up and operating hemodialysis machines according to a nephrologist's instructions.
*   **Patient and Machine Monitoring:** Develop skills to continuously monitor both the patient's condition and the dialysis machine during a procedure, and respond appropriately to alarms or changes.
*   **Vascular Access Management:** Learn how to assess and cannulate vascular access points for effective hemodialysis.
*   **Procedure Completion:** Understand the proper protocols for disconnecting a patient from the dialysis machine after a session.
*   **Unit Management:** Acquire knowledge in managing a dialysis unit efficiently and safely.

## Admission Process

Applying for the Dialysis Technician course with VIVO Healthcare involves a straightforward process:

1.  **Online Application:** Begin by visiting the official VIVO Healthcare website to access the application portal for the Dialysis Technician course.
2.  **Download Form:** Download the official admission form available on the course page.
3.  **Complete and Submit:** Fill out the application form with all required personal, educational, and guardian details. This includes information such as your name, contact details, Aadhar number, educational qualifications, and bank details. After completion, submit the form along with attested copies of necessary documents at a VIVO skill centre near you.
4.  **Fee Payment:** Secure your admission by depositing the full course fee after submitting your application and documents.

This structured approach ensures that students are well-prepared for a challenging yet rewarding career as a Dialysis Technician, making a significant difference in patients' lives.
