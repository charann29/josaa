# Introduction to Aero-Thermal Simulation using ANSYS Fluent by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For engineering students and professionals keen on mastering computational fluid dynamics (CFD) and thermal analysis, a specialized program focusing on ANSYS Fluent can be a significant career boost. This course offers an in-depth exploration of aero-thermal simulation techniques, equipping participants with practical skills in a crucial industry tool.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to use ANSYS Fluent for complex aero-thermal simulations, a vital skill in aerospace, automotive, and other engineering fields.
*   Understand fundamental concepts of fluid dynamics, aerodynamics, and turbulence modeling.
*   Gain hands-on experience with mesh generation using ICEM CFD and setting up various simulation scenarios.
*   The program culminates in a certificate upon successful completion, validating your expertise in this advanced simulation software.

## Course Overview

This comprehensive program is designed to provide a robust understanding of aero-thermal simulation using ANSYS Fluent. It combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, enabling participants to analyze and solve real-world engineering problems. The learning format is flexible, incorporating both self-study and virtual classroom sessions, delivered through a mix of video and text-based content. The instruction medium for the entire course is English.

## Program Fees and Certification

The total fee for the "Introduction to Aero-Thermal simulation using ANSYS Fluent" program is ₹40,000. This amount covers all aspects of the course. Upon successful completion, participants will receive a certificate from Skill Lync, acknowledging their proficiency in the subject matter and their ability to apply ANSYS Fluent for aero-thermal simulations.

## Detailed Syllabus

The 12-week curriculum is structured to progressively build expertise, starting from fundamental concepts and moving towards advanced simulation techniques:

*   **Week 1: Introduction to Fluid Flows** - Covers types of fluid properties, Newtonian and Non-Newtonian fluids, descriptions of fluid flows, and an overview of CFD methods.
*   **Week 2: Introduction to Aerodynamics** - Explores the role of aerodynamics in design, key parameters, instruments in aircraft, similarity parameters, and aerodynamic forces and moments.
*   **Week 3: International Standard Atmosphere** - Focuses on understanding ISA and calculating properties based on height.
*   **Week 4: Tetra/Prism Generation in ICEM CFD** - Introduces ICEM CFD for domain setup, mesh generation, and quality assessment.
*   **Week 5: Introduction to Flow Over Airfoil** - Discusses airfoils, forces acting on them, using ICEM CFD for domain and mesh generation for incompressible and compressible flow over airfoils, and mesh quality assessment.
*   **Week 6: Introduction to Turbulence Modelling** - Explains turbulence, its importance, how to model turbulent flows, and available turbulence models in commercial CFD packages.
*   **Week 7: Simulate the Flow Over a NACA0012 Airfoil** - Teaches how to set up simulations for aerodynamic forces on an airfoil and analyze results for various angles of attack.
*   **Week 8: Introduction to Moving Zones** - Covers moving zones and approaches to model them, including Moving Reference Frame and Moving Mesh.
*   **Week 9: Comparison of Moving Reference and Moving Mesh Approach** - Uses a turbomachinery component to compare MRF and MM approaches, setting up simulations, and analyzing results.
*   **Week 10: Transient Flow Over Air Compressor** - Involves working on a sector of a large air compressor, setting up periodic zones in FLUENT console, configuring simulations, and analyzing results for turbomachinery components.
*   **Week 11: Introduction to Computational Aeroacoustics** - Introduces acoustics, acoustic analogy methods, and CAA methodologies.
*   **Week 12: Broadband Noise Modelling** - Focuses on using ANSYS Mesher for domain mesh generation, setting up symmetric models in FLUENT, and running acoustic solvers to analyze noise sources.
