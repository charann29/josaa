# Fundamentals of Artisan Business Development by Smithsonian Institution via Edx: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring artisans looking to transform their craft into a thriving business, the Smithsonian Institution, in collaboration with Edx, offers a valuable online program: "Fundamentals of Artisan Business Development." This course provides essential knowledge and skills to help you navigate the commercial aspects of the art world.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn crucial business skills like costing, marketing, and export strategies tailored for artisans.
*   The program is self-paced, allowing you to study at your convenience over 16 weeks.
*   It is delivered primarily in Armenian, with some legal content specific to Armenia.
*   While the course content is accessible for free, a verified certificate requires a fee.

## Understanding the Program

This introductory online course falls under the Art and Culture category and has been developed by the Smithsonian Institution as part of its 'My Armenia Program.' It's designed to equip budding artisans with the foundational business acumen needed to elevate their craft from a hobby to a professional enterprise. Key topics covered include effective marketing techniques, accurate pricing strategies, understanding export procedures, and the process of business registration.

## Course Structure and Language

Led by Ashkhen Khudaverdyan, an enterprise specialist at the Smithsonian Institution, the program is primarily delivered through videos and text in the Armenian language. While the core business principles are universal, certain sections, particularly those related to local laws and procedures, are specifically tailored for an Armenian audience. The course is self-paced, meaning you can set your own study schedule, typically requiring just one to two hours of effort per week.

## Duration and Effort

This comprehensive program spans 16 weeks, offering ample time to absorb the material without feeling rushed. The recommended weekly commitment of 1-2 hours makes it highly manageable for individuals balancing other responsibilities. Its self-study format ensures maximum flexibility, allowing you to progress through the curriculum at your own speed.

## Eligibility and Benefits

While there are no strict prerequisites for enrolling, having your own artisan workshop is recommended to gain the most practical benefit from the course content. By the end of the program, you will have a solid understanding of:

*   **Costing and Pricing:** How to accurately determine the cost of your products and set competitive prices.
*   **Marketing:** Strategies to effectively promote your artisan products.
*   **Export:** Basics of taking your products to international markets.
*   **Business Registration:** The steps involved in formally registering your artisan business.
*   **Law and Procedure:** Relevant legal aspects, with some parts specific to Armenia.

This program is ideal for anyone passionate about their craft who wishes to develop the business skills necessary to build a sustainable and profitable career.

## Fees and Certification

The core learning materials for the Fundamentals of Artisan Business Development course are available for free through the audit track on Edx. This allows you to access most of the course content without any financial commitment. However, if you wish to receive a verified certificate upon completion, a fee will be required. This certificate can be a valuable credential to showcase your acquired business skills.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the "Fundamentals of Artisan Business Development" program is a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Official Course Page:** Navigate to the program's official page on Edx.
2.  **Review Course Details:** Read through all the information provided about the course content, structure, and offerings.
3.  **Enroll Now:** If you decide to proceed, click on the 'Enroll now' option.
4.  **Log In or Sign Up:**
    *   If you already have an Edx account, simply log in to begin the course on the audit track (free access to materials).
    *   To pursue the verified track and obtain a certificate, you will need to pay the associated fee.
    *   If you are new to Edx, you can easily sign up by providing your credentials or by using your existing Google, Microsoft, Apple, or Facebook account for quick registration.

There is no complex application form to fill out; an active Edx account is all you need to start your learning journey.

This program offers a fantastic opportunity for artisans to gain crucial business knowledge, setting them on a path to greater success and recognition for their craft.
