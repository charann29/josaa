# Data Science Fundamentals on Microsoft Azure by CloudSwyft Global Systems, Inc via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online program offers a foundational understanding of data science principles and their practical application using Microsoft Azure, designed for individuals keen on entering the world of AI and data analysis.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Introductory Level:** Perfect for beginners with a basic grasp of mathematics, no prior data science or Azure experience is necessary.
*   **Flexible Learning:** The course is 100% online, self-paced, and delivered through video and text, requiring about 5-6 hours of study per week.
*   **Microsoft Accreditation:** Upon successful completion, participants receive a certification accredited by Microsoft.
*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** Spanning approximately 18 weeks, the program covers data analysis, research design, experimental modeling, and Azure tools for AI.

## Course Overview

This certification program, offered through Futurelearn, provides a solid introduction to data science and how to build artificial intelligence solutions using Microsoft Azure. It's structured into four distinct courses, guiding learners through various aspects of data science, from fundamental concepts to hands-on application with Azure's powerful services.

Participants will delve into topics such as data analysis techniques, the methodology behind data science research design, experimental data analysis, and modeling. A significant portion of the course focuses on leveraging Microsoft Azure for AI, teaching the use of tools like Azure Machine Learning, Azure SQL Database, Azure Synapse Analytics, and Azure Cognitive Services. While no prior expertise in data science or Azure is required, a basic understanding of mathematics and a willingness for self-paced learning are beneficial. This program is also suitable for professionals looking to upskill or transition into data science and AI roles.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to equip learners with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills essential for a career in data science and cloud computing. Throughout the course, participants will gain:

*   An understanding of core data science concepts and their application to real-world challenges.
*   Proficiency in Microsoft Azure fundamentals and its suite of tools and services for data analysis and AI development.
*   A grasp of the mathematical principles and techniques crucial for programming and data science.
*   Skills in Transact-SQL, a robust query language for managing relational databases.
*   Knowledge of data science research design, including hypothesis testing, experimental design, and data visualization methods.

## Program Structure and Duration

This introductory-level course is entirely online and self-directed, allowing learners to progress at their own pace. The total estimated time to complete the program is approximately 18 weeks, with a recommended weekly effort of 5-6 hours. The learning materials are delivered through a combination of videos and text, ensuring a comprehensive and engaging experience.

## Fees and Certification

The course fee for the Data Science Fundamentals on Microsoft Azure program is structured as a monthly subscription. The cost is Rs. 900 per month. Upon successful completion of all course requirements, participants will be awarded a certification accredited by Microsoft, issued by Futurelearn. This certification validates the acquired skills in data science and Microsoft Azure.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Data Science Fundamentals on Microsoft Azure online course is a straightforward process. Interested individuals should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official Futurelearn platform.
2.  Register for an account on the Futurelearn website.
3.  Subscribe to the FutureLearn service to gain access to the course content and begin your learning journey.

This program is ideal for aspiring data analysts, data scientists, data engineers, and artificial intelligence engineers looking to build a strong foundation in these rapidly growing fields.
