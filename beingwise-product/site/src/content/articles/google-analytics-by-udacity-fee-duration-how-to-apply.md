# Google Analytics by Udacity: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to master the intricacies of web analytics, the Google Analytics program offered by Udacity, in collaboration with Google and e-nor, provides a comprehensive learning path. This course is designed to equip professionals with practical skills and in-depth knowledge of popular web analytics tools.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a self-paced, online program developed with Google and e-nor, focusing on practical Google Analytics skills.
*   The course is delivered through videos and text, offering flexibility for learners to study at their own convenience.
*   Participants will gain hands-on experience in building dashboards, evaluating campaigns, and understanding user behavior.
*   A certificate of completion from Udacity, powered by Google and e-nor, is awarded upon successfully finishing the program.

## Course Overview and Highlights

This program is tailored for individuals aiming to excel in Google Analytics. It provides practical guidance and mentor support to help candidates build a strong foundation in web analytics. Throughout the four-month, self-paced learning journey, students will learn to refine Google Analytics data, create dashboards in Data Studio, understand channel customization, and utilize tools like Google Tag Manager and UTM tags for campaigns. The online format allows for significant flexibility, enabling participants to complete the course at their own pace.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide end-to-end knowledge in various areas of Google Analytics:

*   **Foundational Knowledge:** Develop a strong understanding of Google Analytics principles.
*   **Data Refinement and Dashboards:** Learn to refine data and build effective dashboards using Data Studio.
*   **Customization and Campaign Tracking:** Master functionalities such as channel customization, Google Tag Manager, and UTM tags for campaign analysis.
*   **Reporting and Segmentation:** Create custom reports, advanced graphical displays, and segment audiences effectively within Data Studio and Google Analytics.
*   **Advanced Topics:** Explore advanced displays, segmentation, measurement strategy, implementation, and delve into acquisition, conversion, e-commerce, and attribution models.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in this program, candidates should possess a basic understanding of computers and feel comfortable working in an online environment. A working knowledge of data is also beneficial for prospective students. To receive the certificate of completion, candidates must successfully finish all required components of the Google Analytics certification training.

## Course Fee

The fee for the Google Analytics program is USD 999. This amount covers the entire course and is payable to gain admission.

| Particulars | Amount |
| :---------- | :----- |
| Course fee  | USD 999 |

## How to Apply

Admission to the Google Analytics program requires candidates to visit the official Udacity program page. It's important to note that the program is currently on hold. Prospective applicants are advised to monitor the Udacity website for any updates or notifications regarding its reopening.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to gain industry-recognized expertise in Google Analytics, enhancing your professional profile and analytical skills.
