# Certified Organisational Leadership Professional by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aiming to excel in leadership roles, the Certified Organisational Leadership Professional program by Vskills offers a pathway to enhance your skills and gain a recognised certification. This program focuses on equipping individuals with the competencies needed to drive change and create value within organisations.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a government-approved certification, enhancing its credibility in the job market.
*   The certification, once earned, remains valid for a lifetime, eliminating the need for renewal.
*   Study materials and practice tests are provided, with lifetime access to the learning resources.
*   The entire learning and examination process is conducted online, offering flexibility.

## Understanding the Program

The Certified Organisational Leadership Professional course is designed to help aspiring and current leaders develop a comprehensive understanding of effective leadership within businesses. It delves into how successful leaders evaluate their own performance, build collaborative teams, and adeptly manage conflicts and negotiations.

The curriculum is structured with an exam-oriented approach, providing in-depth course material in both soft and hard copy formats. Alongside the study material, Vskills offers practice tests and sample questions to help participants prepare thoroughly for the certification exam.

Upon successfully completing the course material and passing the final certification exam, participants receive a government-approved certificate from Vskills. This accreditation is intended to boost employability and support career progression, particularly for team leaders, and middle to senior-level managers.

## Who Is This Program For?

This online program is tailored for individuals looking to cultivate and refine their management abilities to become more impactful leaders. It's particularly well-suited for:

*   Team Leaders
*   Middle-level Managers
*   Senior Managers
*   Project Managers and Supervisors

## What You Will Learn

The program focuses on developing crucial managerial skills. Upon successful completion, participants will gain familiarity with key concepts such as:

*   Core components of organisational leadership
*   Developing and communicating an organisational vision
*   Achieving marketing and operational excellence
*   Effective cost and finance management
*   Navigating organisational politics
*   Managing projects and ensuring project quality
*   Crisis management and business continuity strategies

## Fees and Certification Details

The fee for the Certified Organisational Leadership Professional program is ₹3,499. This covers access to all course materials, practice resources, and the final certification exam. Payment can be conveniently made using net banking, credit cards, or debit cards.

**Fees Overview**

| Course                                     | Fees       |
| :----------------------------------------- | :--------- |
| Certified Organisational Leadership Professional | ₹ 3,499 |

The certification is provided by Vskills, a government-approved body. To receive the certificate and the 'Vskills Certified' tag, candidates must pass the certification test. The minimum passing score for the exam is 2.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Certified Organisational Leadership Professional program is a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official Vskills website.
2.  Use the search bar on the homepage to find the course by typing "Certified Organisational Leadership Professional."
3.  Once on the course page, locate and click the "Buy Now" button, which will direct you to the payment gateway.
4.  Complete the payment process using your preferred method (net banking, credit card, or debit card).

Upon successful payment, you will gain access to the program's learning resources and can begin your journey toward certification.

This certification can be a valuable asset for professionals aiming to solidify their leadership capabilities and advance their careers in today's competitive business landscape.
