# Mathematics XI Part-II by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online certification course, developed by the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) and offered through the Swayam platform, is designed to support Class XI students in mastering key mathematical concepts. It serves as a valuable resource for reinforcing school-level understanding and developing problem-solving skills.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is entirely online, self-paced, and delivered through a combination of video lectures and e-texts.
*   It spans 24 weeks and covers essential Class XI mathematics topics, including Binomial Theorem, Sequences and Series, and Calculus fundamentals.
*   Successful completion, including a final assessment, leads to a certificate from NCERT and Swayam.
*   The program is free to enroll in and access, with no course or certificate fees mentioned.

## Course Overview

The Mathematics XI Part-II course is a comprehensive online program structured into 25 modules, all accessible via the Swayam learning platform. The instruction is provided by subject matter experts, with coordination from Professor T. P. Sarma of the Department of Education in Science and Mathematics (DESM), NCERT, New Delhi. The learning experience is enriched with video lectures, digital textual materials, problem-solving demonstrations, and additional web resources. Students can gauge their understanding through online quizzes and a final assessment.

## What You Will Learn

This program is meticulously designed to enhance mathematical proficiency, covering a range of topics crucial for Class XI students. Upon completion, learners will have a solid grasp of concepts such as:

*   Binomial Theorem
*   Sequences and Series (including Arithmetic and Geometric Progressions)
*   Straight Lines
*   Conic Sections
*   Introduction to Three-Dimensional Geometry
*   Limits and Derivatives
*   Mathematical Reasoning
*   Statistics (Mean Deviation, Variance, Standard Deviation)
*   Probability

Each major topic includes dedicated assignments to reinforce learning.

## Course Structure and Delivery

The course is delivered in English, primarily through self-study, utilizing video and text-based content. It features an interactive platform that includes discussion forums for peer interaction and doubt clarification. Throughout the 24-week duration, students will engage with e-texts, video tutorials, and self-assessment tools like online quizzes. These components are designed to facilitate a thorough understanding of the curriculum and prepare students for the final assessment.

## Certification and Fees

Upon successfully completing all course modules and achieving a score of 70% or higher in the final assessment, participants will be awarded a certificate. This certificate is jointly provided by NCERT and Swayam, validating the learner's understanding of the Class XI Mathematics Part-II syllabus. There are no course fees or certificate fees associated with this program, making it an accessible option for all eligible students.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Mathematics XI Part-II Online Course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal for the course.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the new page that appears, you can either sign in with an existing Swayam account or create a new one using your username and password.

Once registered and signed in, you will gain full access to the course content and learning materials.

This course offers a fantastic opportunity for Class XI students to deepen their understanding of mathematics with expert guidance from NCERT.
