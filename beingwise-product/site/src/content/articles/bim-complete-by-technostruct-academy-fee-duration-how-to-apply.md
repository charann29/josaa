# BIM Complete by TechnoStruct Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Building Information Modeling (BIM) is transforming the architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) industry. The BIM Complete online course from TechnoStruct Academy offers a comprehensive program designed to equip students and professionals with essential BIM skills.

## Key takeaways

*   This advanced course focuses on industry-specific BIM methods and concepts.
*   It offers an international certificate in BIM Modeling & Coordination upon completion.
*   The program includes 90 days of learning, with 60 hours of e-live classes and 60 hours of practical sessions.
*   Participants benefit from a one-month free internship and placement assistance with multinational corporations.

## Course Overview

The BIM Complete Online Course is an advanced program tailored for individuals aspiring to become BIM engineers. It provides a deep dive into the fundamentals and practical applications of BIM, suitable for those in engineering and architecture fields. The curriculum is delivered through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content, ensuring a flexible learning experience.

Throughout the course, learners engage with live projects, including interactions with USA-based projects, offering exposure to global industry practices. The training is led by experienced BIM professionals, ensuring high-quality instruction and practical insights.

## Program Offerings and Duration

This comprehensive program spans 90 days, designed to provide intensive learning and skill development. It includes:

*   **60 hours of e-live classes:** Interactive sessions with instructors.
*   **60 hours of practical learning:** Hands-on experience with BIM tools and concepts.
*   **Live projects:** Real-world application of learned skills.
*   **Interactions with USA-based projects:** Gaining exposure to international project workflows.
*   **One-month free internship:** Practical experience in a professional setting.
*   **Placement assistance:** Support for securing employment in multinational companies.

Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from TechnoStruct Academy, which also serves as an International Certification in BIM Modeling & Coordination.

## Eligibility Criteria

The BIM Complete course is designed for a broad range of professionals and students in the AEC sector. Eligible candidates include:

*   Architecture Engineering students and professionals
*   Civil Engineering students and professionals
*   Mechanical Engineering students and professionals
*   Electrical Engineering students and professionals

Essentially, anyone looking to gain proficiency in BIM and advance their career in this specialized domain can apply.

## Course Content and Skills Acquired

The curriculum is structured to provide a holistic understanding of BIM, covering various aspects crucial for a BIM engineer. Key topics include:

*   Basics of BIM Architecture
*   BIM Structure
*   BIM MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Fire Fighting, and all Building Services)
*   Parametric modeling
*   Shop drawings
*   4D, 5D, 6D modeling and applications
*   BIM and project collaboration
*   Virtual Reality (VR) enabled models

Participants will also develop software skills in various industry-standard tools, such as AUTO-CAD 2D & 3D, AUTO-CAD MEP, REVIT (Arch/Stru/MEP), DYNAMO, NAVISWORKS, Autodesk BIM 360, Scan to BIM, ENSCAPE, REVIT Live, and 3DS Max. The practical sessions and live projects are designed to help learners create digital information, including drawings, rendered images, videos, and animations.

## Fees and Application Process

The fee for the BIM Complete certification course is ₹59,000 plus GST. This fee covers all aspects of the program, including certification.

To apply for the BIM Complete Online Course, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official TechnoStruct Academy website.
2.  Locate and click on the "Enrollment Form" option.
3.  Fill in the required details accurately and submit the form to complete your application.

This course is a valuable investment for those looking to build a career in the rapidly evolving field of Building Information Modeling, offering both theoretical knowledge and practical, industry-relevant skills.
