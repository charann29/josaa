# INI CET 2026 (May Exam): Application Form (OUT), Eligibility, Dates, Syllabus, Pattern, Cutoff

The Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test (INI CET) is a crucial exam for medical graduates aspiring to pursue postgraduate courses in top institutions across India. Conducted by AIIMS Delhi, this test opens doors to MD, MS, MCh, DM, and MDS programs.

## Key Takeaways

*   The application window for INI CET 2026 (May exam) is currently open, with the prospectus released on March 28, 2026.
*   The deadline to submit your online application is April 25, 2026.
*   A correction window for submitted applications will be available from April 30 to May 2, 2026.
*   The INI CET 2026 examination is scheduled for May 16, 2026, with results expected by May 23, 2026.

## Understanding INI CET

INI CET is a national-level entrance examination administered by the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi. It serves as the gateway for admissions to various postgraduate medical and dental courses, including Master of Surgery (MS), Doctor of Medicine (MD), Master of Dental Surgery (MDS), Doctorate of Medicine (DM), and Master of Chirurgiae (MCh). This single examination replaces the individual entrance tests previously conducted by participating institutions. Successful candidates can gain admission to 19 AIIMS branches, JIPMER Puducherry, PGI Chandigarh, NIMHANS Bengaluru, and SCTIMST Trivandrum.

## Application Process and Eligibility

The INI CET 2026 application form is available exclusively online on the official AIIMS website. Candidates must complete their applications before the April 25, 2026, deadline. It's crucial to note the eligibility criteria regarding internships: only candidates who will complete their mandatory internship on or before July 31, 2026, are eligible to register for this examination. Those completing their internship after this date will not be considered eligible.

## Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The INI CET is a computer-based test (CBT) conducted online. While the official prospectus provides comprehensive details, generally, the exam assesses knowledge across various subjects taught during the MBBS/BDS curriculum. The syllabus typically covers subjects from Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pathology, Pharmacology, Microbiology, Forensic Medicine, Community Medicine, Ophthalmology, ENT, General Medicine, General Surgery, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Paediatrics, and other allied specialities. The question paper consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs) designed to evaluate a candidate's clinical reasoning and foundational medical knowledge.

## Admit Card and Results

Once the application process concludes, candidates who have successfully registered will be able to download their admit cards from the official AIIMS website starting May 9, 2026. The admit card is a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall. Following the exam, the results for INI CET 2026 (May session) are scheduled to be released by May 23, 2026. Qualified candidates will then be notified about the subsequent counselling process for seat allocation.

Prepare thoroughly and stay updated with the official AIIMS announcements for a smooth application and examination experience.
