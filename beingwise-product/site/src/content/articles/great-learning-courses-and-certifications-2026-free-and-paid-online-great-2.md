# Great Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Great Learning Courses

Great Learning offers a diverse range of online courses and certifications designed to help students and professionals enhance their skills and advance their careers. These programs cover various in-demand fields, often in collaboration with leading global universities and institutions.

## Key Takeaways

*   Great Learning provides a mix of free and paid online programs across popular domains like Data Science, AI, Machine Learning, and Management.
*   Many paid programs are offered in partnership with prestigious international universities, offering globally recognized certifications.
*   Courses are structured to include both theoretical foundations and practical, hands-on experience, often culminating in capstone projects.
*   Dedicated career support, including placement assistance and job board access, is a common feature for many paid programs.

## Master of Science in Data Science Programme

This online Master's degree is a collaboration between Northwestern University's School of Professional Studies and Great Learning. Northwestern University is consistently ranked among the top US universities, offering students a chance to earn a degree from a highly regarded institution.

The curriculum for the MS in Data Science Programme is carefully designed to balance foundational data science theory with practical exposure to popular programming frameworks, languages, and libraries in a real-world environment. Students also complete a Capstone project to demonstrate their acquired skills. The program benefits from faculty with significant industry experience.

Enrolled students receive dedicated career support, including access to job boards and one-on-one guidance, to help them secure positions with leading companies. This degree can provide a significant competitive advantage in the job market.

*   **Duration:** 18 Months
*   **Approximate Fee:** $13,000

## Executive Post Graduate Program in Management (ePGPM)

The ePGPM is a one-year executive post-graduate program offered by Great Learning in association with Great Lakes Institute of Management (GLIM), Chennai. It's an excellent option for both fresh graduates and working professionals looking to accelerate their careers and develop strong managerial and leadership skills.

This program follows a similar rigorous structure to a full-time Post-Graduate Program in Management but is condensed into a 12-month format. A key advantage is that participants can continue their full-time jobs while pursuing this course, applying their newly gained skills directly in their professional roles.

*   **Duration:** 12 Months
*   **Approximate Fee:** ₹2,35,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Marketing, Data Science, Machine Learning, Database Management, Artificial Intelligence, Python, Social Media Marketing, Digital Marketing.

## Post Graduate Program in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

This Post Graduate Program (PGP) in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning is offered in collaboration with IIIT Delhi and the University of Texas, Austin. IIIT Delhi is recognized for its strong research and quality education in IT and interdisciplinary areas, ensuring a robust learning experience.

The program's syllabus provides a comprehensive understanding of algorithms while building a solid foundation in Computer Science. It is suitable for both students and working professionals aiming to upskill in the rapidly evolving fields of AI and Machine Learning. Participants receive guidance throughout their learning journey and benefit from dedicated career support.

*   **Duration:** 12 Months
*   **Approximate Fee:** ₹2,75,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Programming skills, Python programming.

## Post Graduate Program in Data Science

Offered in collaboration with The McCombs School of Business at The University of Texas at Austin and Great Lakes in India, this Post Graduate Program in Data Science is a professional management-oriented course. It's designed to equip individuals with the skills needed for a successful career in Data Science and Business Analytics.

The program comprehensively covers the latest analytics tools and techniques, emphasizing their business applications. It blends theoretical knowledge with practical application, providing a well-rounded education in the field.

Great Learning's collaborations with esteemed institutions ensure that these programs offer high-quality content and valuable certifications, enhancing career prospects for participants.
