# Cryptography by International Information System Security Certification Consortium via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to understand the fundamentals of securing digital information, the Cryptography certification course, offered by the International Information System Security Certification Consortium (ISC)² in collaboration with Coursera, provides an excellent foundation. This program helps you grasp essential cryptographic concepts and learn how to apply them to protect vital data.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a beginner-friendly, 100% online course designed for self-study.
*   It forms the fourth part of the six-course (ISC)² Systems Security Certified Practitioner (SSCP) certification pathway.
*   The course requires approximately 14 hours to complete and offers a shareable certificate from Coursera upon successful completion.
*   It covers key management, public key infrastructure, and the implementation of secure protocols.

## What You Will Learn

This certification program is designed to equip you with practical knowledge and skills in cryptography. Upon finishing the course, you will be able to:

*   Clearly describe the basic requirements and fundamental concepts of cryptography.
*   Understand and implement cryptographic systems, including the differences between symmetric and asymmetric encryption.
*   Identify methods for supporting secure protocols and applying them effectively.
*   Explain key management concepts, including validation and administration, and grasp the principles of Public Key Infrastructure (PKI).

## Course Structure and Duration

The Cryptography course is delivered entirely online through video lectures and text-based materials, allowing for a flexible, self-paced learning experience. While it's estimated to take about 14 hours, you can complete the curriculum at your own convenience. The course includes graded assignments and hands-on projects, ensuring a practical understanding of the material. English subtitles are available to assist learners.

### Syllabus Overview

The course is structured to build your knowledge systematically:

*   **Week 1: Cryptography Fundamentals**
    *   **Readings:** Module 1 Overview, Introduction to Cryptography.
    *   **Practice Exercises:** Applied Scenario Review focusing on Confidentiality and Integrity, Knowledge Checks on these topics, and Fundamental Concepts of Cryptography.
*   **Week 1: Module 2: Supporting Symmetric Encryption**
    *   **Readings:** Deep dive into algorithms like Rijndael.
    *   **Practice Exercises:** Knowledge Checks on Symmetric Key Concepts.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for individuals aspiring to roles such as ethical hackers, computer programmers, and system security analysts. It's particularly beneficial for anyone looking to build a career in cybersecurity or enhance their current skills in protecting digital assets.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Cryptography certification course is a straightforward process through Coursera:

1.  **Visit the Course Page:** Navigate to the official Cryptography course page on the Coursera platform.
2.  **Find Enrollment Option:** Look for the "Enroll for Free" button. Check if the indicated start date aligns with your schedule.
3.  **Sign Up:** You will be prompted to sign up. You can create an account using your email address or Google account.
4.  **Review Fees and Confirm:** Examine the course fee details (if applicable, beyond the free trial) and click on the "Continue to Enroll" button.
5.  **Payment:** Complete the payment process to finalize your enrollment and gain instant access to the course materials.

This course offers a valuable opportunity to gain critical cybersecurity skills from a reputable organization in a flexible online format.
