# ATMA Exam Centres 2026: Check State-wise Test Centres List

The ATMA examination is a significant entrance test for admission to MBA and PGDM programs across India. Knowing the available test centres is crucial for candidates planning to appear for the exam.

## Key Takeaways

*   Candidates can select up to three preferred ATMA exam centres during the application process.
*   The ATMA exam is conducted multiple times a year, offering flexibility to aspirants.
*   Over 100 test centres are available nationwide, including 28 recently added locations.
*   The specific centre details will be clearly indicated on your ATMA admit card.

## How to Choose Your ATMA 2026 Exam Centre

When filling out the ATMA application form, you will have the opportunity to select your preferred test cities. It's important to choose these carefully and in order of your preference.

Follow these steps to select your centres:

1.  **Register:** Begin by registering on the official ATMA AIMS website.
2.  **Log In:** Use your newly created login credentials to access the ATMA application form.
3.  **Select Centres:** From the provided drop-down menu, choose up to three test centres. Make sure to list them in your desired order, from first to third preference.
4.  **Complete & Submit:** Finish filling out the rest of the application form and then submit it.

## Important Points to Remember for ATMA Exam Centres

Once you have submitted your application, changes to your chosen test centre are generally not permitted. The exam conducting body reserves the right to make changes or cancel any centre if necessary. Always refer to your admit card for the definitive details of your assigned test centre.

Candidates should aim to arrive at the ATMA test centre at least one hour before the scheduled start time of the exam for necessary identification and verification procedures. The exam typically begins at 2:00 PM, so arriving by 1:00 PM is advisable.

## ATMA Exam Centre: Dos and Don'ts

To ensure a smooth experience on exam day, adhere to the following guidelines:

### Dos

*   Carry a printed copy of your ATMA admit card. Digital copies are not accepted.
*   Bring an original photo identification proof along with a photocopy.
*   Have two recent passport-size colour photographs ready.
*   Carry a copy of your ATMA application form for verification.

### Don'ts

*   Do not bring any electronic devices such as mobile phones, laptops, or calculators into the exam hall.

Make sure to review all instructions provided on your admit card and the official ATMA AIMS website before your exam day.
