# Applied Cloud Computing by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub Skillsoft: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Cloud computing has become a cornerstone of modern technology, offering flexible and scalable solutions for data storage, processing, and management. The "Applied Cloud Computing" program, offered through the TCS iON Digital Learning Hub in collaboration with Skillsoft, is designed to equip students with practical skills in this rapidly evolving field.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a self-paced, online program delivered in English, integrating video and text-based learning with virtual classroom sessions.
*   The course awards 5 credit points upon successful completion and includes valuable career support and internship opportunities.
*   Participants gain hands-on experience through project-based learning and industry assignments.
*   A digital, shareable certificate is issued by TCS iON to those who meet the certification criteria.

## Program Overview

This industry-focused course delves into the practical applications of cloud computing, covering essential strategies and principles. It aims to enhance your understanding of how computer system resources are utilized and managed within a cloud environment. The curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with practical methodologies, including mentorship programs, lectures, and assessments, alongside beneficial opportunities like internships and career guidance.

## What You Will Learn

The program is structured to develop a strong understanding of cloud computing concepts and relevant technologies. You will explore tools such as AWS (Amazon Web Services) and Azure technologies, learning how to accurately and efficiently store, process, manage, and collaborate on data and information in the cloud. Key areas of learning include:

*   Core principles of cloud computing
*   Fundamentals of AWS and Azure technologies
*   Database management within cloud environments
*   Networking concepts relevant to cloud infrastructure

## Who Is This Program For?

This program is particularly beneficial for students pursuing bachelor's degrees in fields such as Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. It is also suitable for individuals with a foundational understanding of database management systems. The skills acquired are highly relevant for aspiring professionals in roles such as:

*   Cloud Junior Developer
*   Cloud Software Engineer
*   Cloud Architect
*   Network Engineer

## Fees and Certification

The total program fee for the "Applied Cloud Computing" certification training is ₹20,000, inclusive of GST. This fee grants you access to the complete course curriculum and all program offerings.

### Certification Details

TCS iON provides different types of recognition based on your engagement:

*   **Digital Certificate of Achievement:** Awarded to students who successfully complete the summative assessments. To qualify, you must score at least 40% in Part A, 50% or higher in Part B, and achieve an overall score of 50% or more.
*   **Participation Certificate:** Issued to students who complete the course content but choose not to take the summative assessment.
*   **Scorecard:** Provided to students who attempt the summative assessment but do not meet the minimum passing criteria.

## How to Apply

Admission to the "Applied Cloud Computing" program is straightforward. You can register and apply online through the official course page on the TCS iON Digital Learning Hub. Simply locate the specific course page and follow the online application instructions.

This program offers a valuable pathway for students and professionals to enhance their cloud computing expertise, opening doors to diverse career opportunities in the tech industry.
