# ATMA Exam Admit Card 2026 (Feb Session): Download ATMA Hall Ticket @atmaaims.com

For those aspiring to pursue MBA/PGDM or MCA programs, the ATMA examination is a crucial step. Successfully registered candidates for the ATMA February 2026 session were able to download their admit cards from the official ATMA AIMS website.

## Key Takeaways

*   The ATMA admit card is exclusively available for download from the official ATMA AIMS portal; no physical copies are sent.
*   Only candidates who have completed their ATMA registration successfully can access their admit card.
*   You will need your PID number and password to log in and download the hall ticket.
*   It's mandatory to carry a printed copy of your ATMA admit card to the examination center on the day of the exam.

## ATMA 2026 Exam Dates and Schedule (February Session)

The ATMA 2026 February session admit cards were released on February 18, 2026, with the examination conducted on February 22, 2026. Typically, admit cards become available a few days before the exam date. Candidates appearing for multiple ATMA sessions must download a separate admit card for each session.

## How to Download Your ATMA Admit Card 2026

Follow these steps to download your ATMA hall ticket from the official ATMA AIMS website:

1.  Visit the official ATMA AIMS website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Student Login' section on the homepage.
3.  First, select the specific exam date (e.g., February session).
4.  Enter your unique PID number and your password.
5.  Click on 'Print E-Admit Card'. Carefully read the instructions displayed on the screen.
6.  Confirm by typing 'Yes' in the designated box at the bottom of the page and then click 'Print Admit Card'.
7.  Your ATMA admit card will appear on your screen. Download it and take at least two printouts. Remember to affix two colored passport-sized photographs in the space provided on each printout.

## Important Details on Your ATMA Admit Card

Your ATMA admit card is a vital document and will contain several key pieces of information. Please check all details carefully once you download it. The admit card typically includes:

*   Your PID number, roll number, and password.
*   Your full name and residential address.
*   Your photograph and a space for an additional photograph.
*   The complete address of your designated examination center.
*   Crucial exam day instructions.
*   Space for your signature.

### What to Check for on Your Admit Card

Before taking printouts, it's essential to verify all the information on your ATMA admit card for accuracy. Look out for any discrepancies in:

*   **Your Name:** Ensure your name is spelled correctly and matches your official identification.
*   **Registration/Roll Number:** Confirm that your registration/roll number and PID are accurate and clearly visible.

If you find any errors, contact the ATMA authorities immediately for rectification.

## Documents to Carry on Exam Day

On the day of the ATMA examination, you must bring the following items to the test center:

*   Your printed ATMA admit card with a passport-sized photograph affixed.
*   A valid photo identification proof (e.g., Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving License, Passport) in original.

Ensure you have these documents ready to avoid any issues at the examination center.
