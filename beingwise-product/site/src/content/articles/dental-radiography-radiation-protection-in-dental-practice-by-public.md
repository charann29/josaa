# Dental Radiography: Radiation Protection in Dental Practice by Public Health England via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For dental professionals looking to enhance their skills in safe X-ray practices, this online course offers a comprehensive understanding of radiation protection. Developed by Public Health England, it focuses on ensuring patient and staff safety during dental radiography procedures.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn essential principles of dental radiography and radiation protection from Public Health England experts.
*   Understand UK legislation and guidelines, including IRMER17 and IRR17, crucial for safe practice.
*   Gain the knowledge to fulfill the role of a Radiation Protection Supervisor in a dental setting.
*   Earn a certificate upon completion, which counts towards Continuing Professional Development (CPD).

## Course Overview

This five-week online program, designed by the Dental X-ray Protection Service (DXPS) of Public Health England, delves into the fundamentals of safe dental X-ray procedures. It emphasizes adhering to UK safety standards and legal requirements. Participants will explore the potential hazards associated with dental X-radiation and learn strategies to minimize exposure for both patients and dental staff.

The curriculum is structured to equip you with practical skills and knowledge in radiation protection, enabling you to implement these principles effectively in your dental practice. A significant outcome of this course is the preparation it offers for taking on the responsibilities of a Radiation Protection Supervisor.

## Who Should Attend?

This course is specifically tailored for dental professionals who routinely work with dental X-ray equipment. It is highly beneficial for:

*   Dentists
*   Dental Nurses
*   Practice Managers
*   Dental Therapists
*   Any general healthcare or dental professional involved with X-ray procedures.

## Course Structure and Learning Experience

The program is delivered entirely online and is self-paced, requiring approximately one hour of study per week over five weeks. The learning materials include video lectures, articles, and practical activities, complemented by an online discussion forum for interaction with peers and instructors. This flexible format allows participants to integrate learning into their existing professional schedules.

## Fees and Certification

Enrolling in the premium version of this course, which includes a certificate, costs ₹9,479. Upon successfully completing at least 90% of the course steps and assignments, you will be eligible to receive a certificate of achievement. This certificate is issued by Public Health England and is recognized as valuable for Continuing Professional Development (CPD) for dentists.

## How to Apply

Applying for this program is straightforward. There is no formal application form to submit. Simply follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official course page.
2.  Locate and click the 'Join' button, which will take you to the registration page.
3.  Create a new account by providing basic information, or log in using your Google or Facebook ID.
4.  Proceed to the checkout page, enter your payment details, and complete the enrollment by paying the course fee.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to enhance your expertise in a critical aspect of modern dental practice, ensuring the highest standards of safety and care.
