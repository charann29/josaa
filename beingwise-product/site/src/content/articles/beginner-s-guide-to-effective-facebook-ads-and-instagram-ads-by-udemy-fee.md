# Beginner's Guide to Effective Facebook Ads & Instagram Ads by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's digital world, mastering online advertising is crucial for any business or personal brand. This course from Udemy offers a comprehensive introduction to creating powerful ad campaigns on Facebook and Instagram, designed to help you reach more customers and grow your online presence.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to craft effective Facebook and Instagram ads for various business goals.
*   Understand essential tools like Facebook Business Manager and Creator Studio.
*   Gain practical skills through 29.5 hours of video content, assignments, and downloadable resources.
*   Receive a certificate of completion upon finishing the self-paced program.

## Course Overview

This certification program, developed by the SEO, Facebook Ads & Digital Marketing Academy in collaboration with Beck Robertson, is presented by Udemy. It's specifically designed for individuals looking to leverage Facebook marketing strategies, including entrepreneurs, e-commerce professionals, and bloggers. The course aims to equip learners with the knowledge to become skilled Facebook ad consultants, develop new career paths in digital marketing, or expand their local or e-commerce businesses.

Through its curriculum, you'll learn how to use Facebook Reach ads effectively to expand your customer base and target them more precisely. The program also delves into attracting more consumers and optimizing your advertising efforts.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this online certification, you will be introduced to both fundamental and advanced concepts of Facebook marketing and broader digital marketing. The course explores the features of key platforms like Facebook Business Manager and Facebook Creator Studio. You will study various aspects of Facebook ads, including:

*   Facebook Ads Library
*   Facebook Pixel
*   Facebook Traffic Ads
*   Facebook Engagement Ads
*   Facebook Reach Ads
*   Facebook Audience Insights
*   Facebook Local Ads
*   Ad Reporting
*   Lead Generation
*   Instagram Ads

Additionally, you'll cover the fundamentals of e-commerce, A/B testing, and website development, acquiring the expertise to create robust online marketing strategies specifically for Facebook and Instagram.

## Course Structure and Resources

The program is delivered through a self-paced, online format, primarily using video lectures and text-based materials. The medium of instruction is English. Here's what the course includes:

*   **Duration:** 29.5 hours of pre-recorded video content.
*   **Resources:** 43 downloadable materials to support your learning.
*   **Engagement:** Assignments and quizzes to test your understanding.
*   **Access:** Unlimited access to course materials, viewable on mobile devices and TVs.
*   **Guarantee:** A 30-day money-back guarantee is also provided.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for this comprehensive online course is ₹3,499. Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate from Udemy, acknowledging your new skills and knowledge in Facebook and Instagram advertising. This certificate can be a valuable addition to your professional portfolio.

This program is ideal for a range of professionals and aspiring marketers, including:

*   Entrepreneurs

*   Digital Marketing Executives

Anyone looking to enhance their digital advertising capabilities and drive better results from their online campaigns will find this course beneficial.
