# Fusion 360 Beginners Course by Simpliv Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen on diving into the world of 3D design and manufacturing, the Fusion 360 Beginners Course offered by Simpliv Learning provides a structured entry point. This program aims to equip learners with fundamental skills in computer-aided design (CAD) and computer-aided manufacturing (CAM).

## Key takeaways

*   The course is delivered entirely in English, making it accessible to a wide audience.
*   Learning modes include self-study and virtual classroom sessions, with content provided through videos and text.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Simpliv Learning.
*   The program covers essential topics from basic sketching to advanced 3D modeling tools.

## Understanding the Course Structure

This beginner-friendly course is designed to be flexible, catering to different learning preferences. It combines self-paced study with interactive virtual classroom experiences, ensuring a comprehensive learning environment. The content is delivered through a mix of video tutorials and written materials, allowing learners to revisit concepts as needed.

## Course Fees and Certification

The Fusion 360 Beginners Course is currently available at a discounted fee. The original cost for the program is $583, but it is presently offered at $349. This makes it a more accessible option for those looking to acquire new design skills without a significant financial outlay.

Upon successful completion of all course modules, participants will be awarded a certificate. This certificate is issued by Simpliv Learning, validating the skills and knowledge gained during the program. It can be a valuable addition to a professional portfolio, demonstrating proficiency in Fusion 360.

## What You Will Learn: The Syllabus Overview

The curriculum is thoughtfully structured to guide beginners through the core functionalities of Fusion 360. Here’s a breakdown of the key areas covered:

*   **Introduction, Units, and Basic Settings:** This section sets the foundation, introducing learners to the software interface and essential configuration.
*   **Course Introduction:** An overview of what to expect from the program and its learning objectives.
*   **Working With Sketches:** Mastering 2D sketching, which is crucial for creating robust 3D models.
*   **Basic 3D Modeling Tools:** Hands-on experience with fundamental tools to transform 2D sketches into 3D objects.
*   **Advanced 3D Modeling Tools:** Exploring more complex modeling techniques for intricate designs.
*   **Text, Patterns, and Measurement:** Learning to incorporate text, create patterns, and accurately measure components within designs.

## How to Apply

Specific application details for this course can typically be found directly on the Simpliv Learning platform. Prospective students would generally need to register on their website, select the Fusion 360 Beginners Course, and proceed with the enrollment process, which includes fee payment. It is always advisable to check the official Simpliv Learning course page for the most up-to-date application instructions and enrollment windows.

This course offers a practical pathway for individuals to gain proficiency in Fusion 360, a widely used tool in various design and engineering fields.
