# BITS BAAT Result 2026 (OUT): Download Scorecard, Rank List Here

The Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani, has officially released the BITS BAAT 2026 results for all candidates who participated in the online examination. Your performance in the BITS BAAT exam is crucial, as it determines your eligibility for the subsequent selection rounds, which include Group Discussions (GD), Personal Interviews (PI), Written Ability Tests (WAT), and Extempore speaking.

## Key takeaways

*   BITS BAAT 2026 results are now available on the official BITS admissions portal.
*   Candidates can download their individual scorecards by logging into the admissions website.
*   Shortlisted candidates will proceed to the online Personal Interview (PI) round, along with other selection stages.
*   The final selection for the MBA program considers multiple factors beyond just the BAAT score, including academic history and performance in other management entrance exams.

## How to Check BITS BAAT 2026 Results

Checking your BITS BAAT 2026 result and downloading your scorecard is a straightforward process. Follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official BITS admissions website.
2.  Locate the login section for BITS BAAT results.
3.  Enter your registered credentials (e.g., application number and password).
4.  Complete any required captcha verification.
5.  Click on the 'submit' button to view your result and access your scorecard.

Your scorecard will display your BITS BAAT score, which is essential for the next stages of the admission process.

## What Happens After BITS BAAT Results 2026?

Once the BITS BAAT results are out, BITS Pilani will shortlist candidates based on their scores for the second phase of the admission process. This subsequent phase is designed to evaluate various aspects of a candidate's profile and typically includes:

*   **Group Discussion (GD):** To assess teamwork, leadership, and communication skills.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** A one-on-one interaction to evaluate personality, motivation, and suitability for the MBA program.
*   **Written Ability Test (WAT):** To test written communication and analytical skills.
*   **Extempore:** An impromptu speaking session to gauge quick thinking and articulation.

Detailed guidelines for these second-round admission processes will be communicated to all shortlisted candidates along with the result declaration.
