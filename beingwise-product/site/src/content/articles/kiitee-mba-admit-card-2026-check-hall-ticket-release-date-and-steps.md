# KIITEE MBA Admit Card 2026: Check Hall Ticket Release Date & Steps to Download

The KIITEE MBA admit card is a crucial document for all candidates appearing for the KIIT School of Management (KSOM) MBA entrance exam. This page guides you through understanding its release, download process, and essential instructions for the examination.

## Key Takeaways

*   The KIITEE MBA 2026 admit card was released on January 13, 2026, for registered candidates.
*   Candidates must download their admit card online using their login credentials; no physical copies are mailed.
*   The admit card includes vital information such as exam dates, timings, and venue details.
*   It is mandatory to carry the admit card along with a valid photo ID to the examination center.

## How to Download Your KIITEE MBA Admit Card 2026

Downloading your admit card is a straightforward process, but it must be done from the official KSOM website. Follow these steps carefully:

1.  Visit the official website for KIIT-SOM.
2.  Look for the link or tab labeled 'KIITEE Management Admit Card'.
3.  Enter your login details, which typically include your index number and date of birth.
4.  Once logged in, you will see your admit card. Click to download it.
5.  After downloading, make sure to print a clear copy. Digital versions are not accepted at the exam center.

If you face any difficulties downloading your admit card, it is important to contact the Admission Coordinator immediately. Provide them with your application number, registration number, name, date of birth, and proof of fee payment to resolve the issue.

## What Information is on Your Admit Card?

The KIITEE MBA admit card serves as your official pass to the examination hall and contains several critical pieces of information. It is crucial to verify all details immediately after downloading. The admit card will typically display:

*   Your full name
*   Your unique index number
*   The specific date of your examination
*   The scheduled time for your exam slot
*   The complete address of your examination venue
*   Your contact information
*   Any additional important instructions or guidelines for the exam day

Should you find any discrepancies or errors in the information printed on your admit card, contact the admission coordinator without delay to get it corrected and obtain a revised admit card.

## Essential Guidelines for Exam Day

Adhering to the instructions provided with your admit card is vital for a smooth examination experience. Keep the following points in mind:

*   **Mandatory Document:** The KIITEE MBA admit card is a compulsory document. You will not be permitted to take the exam without it.
*   **Valid Photo ID:** Always carry a valid government-issued photo identification proof (e.g., Aadhar Card, Passport, Driving License) along with your admit card.
*   **No Digital Copies:** Only a printed hard copy of the admit card will be accepted at the examination center.
*   **Arrival Time:** Plan to reach your exam center at least one hour before the scheduled start time. This allows for necessary checks and to settle down.
*   **Preserve Your Admit Card:** Keep your admit card safe until the entire admission process is concluded, as it may be required at various stages.

KSOM also provides official mock tests for registered candidates to familiarize themselves with the exam pattern and experience. These can be accessed through your login on the official portal.

Ensure you prepare well and follow all guidelines for a successful KIITEE MBA examination.
