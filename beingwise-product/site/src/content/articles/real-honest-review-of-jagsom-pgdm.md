# Real honest review of JAGSom PGDM?

JAGSoM, or Jagdish Sheth School of Management, offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) that can be a good choice for students seeking quality management education. Let's take an honest look at what the program offers and if it might be the right fit for your career aspirations.

## Key Takeaways

*   JAGSoM provides a solid PGDM program, particularly if you're exploring options beyond the top-tier institutions.
*   The program fee is INR 17.50 Lakhs, which is an important consideration for prospective students and their families.
*   Recent placement data indicates an average CTC of INR 10.32 LPA, with the highest domestic package reaching INR 20.68 LPA.
*   The curriculum often emphasizes practical, industry-relevant learning, preparing students for real-world challenges.

## Understanding the PGDM Program at JAGSoM

The PGDM at JAGSoM is designed to equip students with comprehensive management skills and a strategic outlook. The curriculum typically covers core business functions like finance, marketing, operations, and human resources, alongside electives that allow for specialization. Many students appreciate the faculty's industry experience and the interactive learning environment.

## Fees and Financial Planning

One of the primary considerations for any higher education program is the cost. The Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) at JAGSoM has a program fee of INR 17.50 Lakhs. It's advisable to factor in additional expenses such as living costs, study materials, and other personal expenditures when planning your finances. Exploring education loan options from various banks can help manage this investment.

## Placements and Career Prospects

For many students, placements are a crucial factor in choosing a B-school. JAGSoM reports an average CTC of INR 10.32 LPA for its recent graduating batch. The highest domestic package achieved by a student from the 2025 batch was INR 20.68 LPA. These figures suggest that graduates find opportunities across various sectors, including consulting, finance, marketing, and technology. It's always beneficial to review the detailed placement reports available on the institution's official website to understand the sector-wise and role-wise distribution of placements.

## Campus Life and Learning Environment

JAGSoM aims to foster a vibrant campus environment that supports both academic and personal growth. The institution often promotes various student clubs and activities, ranging from academic interest groups to cultural and sports committees. This holistic approach helps students develop leadership skills, network with peers, and engage in extracurricular pursuits that complement their management education.

Ultimately, JAGSoM can be a valuable stepping stone for a management career, especially for those who are looking for a strong program with good placement outcomes outside of the very top-ranked institutions.
