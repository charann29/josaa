# Best MBA Colleges in Dharwad 2023: Fees, Admissions, Courses, Placements

Dharwad, a city known for its educational institutions, offers several good options for students aspiring to pursue a Master of Business Administration (MBA). An MBA is a two-year postgraduate program designed to equip individuals with essential management skills and knowledge, opening doors to diverse career paths in the business world.

## Key takeaways

*   Dharwad has a total of 5 MBA colleges, providing various choices for management aspirants.
*   Among these, 2 institutions are privately managed, and 1 is a government-owned college.
*   The primary entrance exams considered for MBA admissions in Dharwad are Karnataka PGCET and KMAT.
*   Annual fees for most MBA programs in Dharwad range between INR 1-2 lakh, making quality management education accessible.

## Eligibility Criteria for MBA in Dharwad

To be considered for MBA admission in Dharwad colleges, candidates generally need to meet the following requirements:

*   A bachelor's degree or an equivalent qualification from a recognized university or institution is mandatory. Applicants should typically have secured a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in their undergraduate studies.
*   Many colleges in Dharwad require applicants to submit scores from specific entrance exams. Meeting the minimum cutoff scores set by individual institutions is crucial for admission.

## Popular Entrance Exams for MBA in Dharwad

Two major entrance examinations are widely accepted by MBA colleges in Dharwad:

*   **Karnataka PGCET:** The Karnataka Post Graduate Common Entrance Test is conducted annually by the Karnataka Examination Authority. This offline exam is a gateway for admission to various postgraduate courses, including MBA, MCA, M.E., M.Tech, and M.Arch programs across Karnataka. It consists of 100 multiple-choice questions to be completed within 150 minutes.
*   **KMAT:** The Karnataka Management Aptitude Test is administered by the Karnataka Post Graduate Private Colleges' Association (KPPGCA). KMAT scores are used for admissions to MBA, PGDM, and MCA courses in participating institutions.

## Top MBA Colleges in Dharwad

Dharwad is home to reputable institutions offering quality MBA programs.
