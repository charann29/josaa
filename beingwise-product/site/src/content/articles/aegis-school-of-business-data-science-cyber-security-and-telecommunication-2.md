# Aegis School of Business Data Science Cyber Security and Telecommunication Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Aegis School of Business Data Science Cyber Security and Telecommunication Courses

Aegis School of Business, Data Science, Cyber Security, and Telecommunication offers a range of specialized programs designed to equip professionals with cutting-edge skills in rapidly evolving industries. These courses are often developed in collaboration with industry leaders like IBM, providing a unique blend of academic rigor and practical application.

## Key takeaways

*   Aegis offers specialized postgraduate and executive programs in data science, business analytics, big data, and telecommunication management.
*   Many programs are delivered in a hybrid format, combining online learning with in-person sessions at their Mumbai campus.
*   Collaborations with industry giants like IBM ensure the curriculum is relevant and aligned with current industry demands.
*   These certifications aim to develop both technical and leadership skills for career advancement in high-demand fields.

## Hybrid EPGP in Data Science, Business Analytics, and Big Data in association with IBM

This 12-month Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP) is a pioneering course in India, specifically designed for individuals aspiring to excel in data science, business analytics, and big data. Developed jointly by Aegis School and IBM, it offers a comprehensive curriculum that blends core subjects with relevant electives, a feature not commonly found in similar programs.

The program's hybrid delivery model includes weekday classes held at the Centre for Excellence in Telecom Technology and Management (CETTM) in Mumbai, complemented by online sessions on Sundays. This structure allows for an immersive learning experience while accommodating working professionals. Graduates of this program gain robust technical and quantitative skills, enabling them to create significant business and social value across various industries. It's an ideal choice for those seeking an analytical course with extensive exposure to real-world tools and software.

**Skills Covered:** Data science principles, business analytics methodologies, big data technologies.

## Post Graduate Program in Telecommunication Management in association with IBM

This 11-month certificate course, a joint offering from Aegis and IBM, is recognized as a premier program in Telecommunication Management. It has benefited numerous executives from over 35 countries, including those working for telecom service providers and vendors. The curriculum is crafted to provide participants, especially aspiring telecom leaders, with a deep understanding of information technology and telecommunication operations from strategic leadership perspectives, encompassing finance, management, and legal aspects.

Upon completion, candidates are prepared to take on technology management roles, confidently making decisions that drive the telecommunication business forward. The program also focuses on enhancing individual functional skills relevant to specific domain areas within telecom.

**Skills Covered:** IT industry knowledge, telecommunication operations, strategic management.

## Executive Masters Program in Telecom Management in association with IBM Online Live

Another 11-month program jointly delivered by Aegis and IBM, this Executive Masters Program is offered in a live online format. It is widely considered equivalent to an MBA in Telecommunication in India, providing a high-level educational experience. Experts from both IBM and Aegis lead the sessions, sharing their extensive industry knowledge.

This program has consistently benefited executives from approximately 35 countries, working for various telecom service providers and vendors. The curriculum is meticulously developed with valuable inputs and suggestions from leading telecom organizations such as Qualcomm, Bharti Airtel, Amdocs, Ericsson, ZTE, Reliance Communication, and BSNL. This ensures that the program addresses both the current and future needs of telecom managers and leaders, equipping participants with the necessary skills to become future leaders in the dynamic world of information technology and telecommunications.

**Skills Covered:** Advanced IT and telecom management, leadership in telecommunications, strategic decision-making.
