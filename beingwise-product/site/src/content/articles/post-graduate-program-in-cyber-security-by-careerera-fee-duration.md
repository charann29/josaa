# Post Graduate Program In Cyber Security by Careerera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's digital world, protecting information is more crucial than ever. The Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security, offered by Careerera, aims to equip students with the essential knowledge and skills to safeguard digital assets and infrastructure from evolving cyber threats.

## Key Takeaways

*   This online program focuses on developing practical skills for the cybersecurity industry.
*   It covers topics from foundational concepts to advanced techniques in cyber defense.
*   The course is taught by experienced professionals and includes live projects.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from Careerera.

## Program Overview

The Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of how to protect sensitive data and IT systems. The curriculum is structured to guide learners from basic principles to advanced problem-solving in the cybersecurity domain. Mentors with significant industry experience lead the instruction, ensuring that participants gain insights into real-world challenges and solutions.

The program's syllabus is crafted to meet the demands of the modern cyberworld, helping students develop expertise in identifying vulnerabilities, understanding cyber threats, and implementing robust security policies. It emphasizes practical application through multiple live projects and capstone projects.

## Program Highlights and Offerings

This one-year online program features a student-faculty ratio of 15:1, ensuring personalized attention. Key highlights include:

*   **Experienced Faculty:** Learning from professionals with extensive industry backgrounds.
*   **Solid Foundation:** Building core cybersecurity knowledge from the ground up.
*   **Practical Experience:** Engaging in multiple live projects and a capstone project.
*   **Learning Resources:** Access to mock papers, videos, notes, assignments, and student handouts.
*   **Interactive Learning:** Live online lectures and surprise tests to reinforce understanding.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security, candidates generally need to meet specific academic requirements:

*   A Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or Technology (B.E., B.Tech, or B.S.) with a minimum of 50% marks.
*   Alternatively, candidates holding a degree in MCA, BCA, or B.Sc in Information Technology (IT) are also eligible.

### Certification Details

Successful completion of the program, including passing an examination conducted by Careerera at the end of the course, will lead to a program certificate issued by Careerera.

## Fee and Duration

The Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security has a duration of 12 months. The program fee is \$1,499. This fee includes access to all course materials, live lectures, and project work. It's important to note that fees are subject to change, so prospective students should always confirm the latest figures on the official Careerera portal.

## How to Apply

The application process for the Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security is straightforward and can be completed online:

1.  Visit the official program page on the Careerera website.
2.  Look for the 'Upcoming Batches' section and select your preferred batch.
3.  Click on the 'Enroll Now' button to initiate the application.
4.  Fill in the required personal and academic details, and upload any necessary documents.
5.  Complete the payment of the program fee.
6.  Once enrolled, you can begin your training on the scheduled start date.

This program offers a valuable pathway for those looking to build or advance their careers in the critical field of cybersecurity, providing both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
