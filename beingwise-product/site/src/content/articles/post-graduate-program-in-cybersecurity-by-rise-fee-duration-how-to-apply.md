# Post Graduate Program in Cybersecurity by RISE: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to enter the dynamic field of cybersecurity, the Post Graduate Program in Cybersecurity offered by RISE provides a comprehensive online learning experience. This program is designed to equip individuals with essential skills and knowledge, even if they have no prior IT background.

## Key takeaways

*   This online postgraduate program spans 12 months and is delivered through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content.
*   It covers a wide range of cybersecurity topics, including ethical hacking, network forensics, and anti-forensics, with a strong emphasis on hands-on practical training.
*   The program is a collaboration between RISE and ThriveDx SaaS, an Israeli ed-tech company, and offers an accredited certification upon completion.
*   It's suitable for graduates and working professionals aged 21-35 from Tier 1 or Tier 2 cities, with at least one year of work experience.

## Program Overview and Structure

The PG Program in Cybersecurity is an online, year-long course developed by RISE in partnership with ThriveDx SaaS. It's structured to provide a deep dive into various aspects of cybersecurity, preparing students for real-world challenges. The curriculum includes over 320 hours of learning, with more than 90% of sessions being lab-oriented, covering over 50 industry tools. Students benefit from weekly live sessions, interactions with ThriveDx SaaS facilitators, and dedicated doubt-clearing via an LMS message center. A one-month capstone project allows participants to apply their accumulated knowledge.

## Eligibility and Ideal Candidates

This program is open to graduates and working professionals who are eager to build a career in cybersecurity. While a background in technology can be helpful, it's not a strict requirement. Applicants should be between 21 and 35 years old, possess strong English reading proficiency, and have fundamental math and logic skills. A minimum of one year of work experience is also necessary. The program is specifically targeted at individuals residing in metropolitan Tier 1 or Tier 2 cities, aiming to serve those looking to transition into roles like ethical hacker or system security analyst.

## Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the PG Program in Cybersecurity is INR 1,98,000, plus applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST). Additionally, there is a registration fee of INR 5,000. RISE offers the convenience of installment payments, making the program more accessible. Early applicants may also be eligible for special discounts. Upon successful completion, participants receive a certification accredited by CertNexus for CyberSec First Responder®.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to provide a thorough understanding of key cybersecurity concepts and practices. Through experiential learning, including over 100 hands-on lab activities and case studies, students will gain proficiency in:

*   The NIST framework and cybersecurity workforce concepts.
*   Computer memory forensics and memory dump analysis.
*   Processes and methodologies of ethical hacking.
*   Network forensics and anti-forensics techniques.
*   Steganography and dark web investigation methods.

Students also receive up to one year of lab access to practice and refine their skills. The program further supports career aspirations with placement and internship assistance, alongside opportunities for peer learning.

This program offers a robust pathway for individuals to gain specialized knowledge and practical skills, opening doors to successful careers in the ever-evolving field of cybersecurity.
