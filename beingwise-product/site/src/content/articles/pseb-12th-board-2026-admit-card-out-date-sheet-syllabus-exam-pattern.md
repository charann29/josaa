# PSEB 12th Board 2026: Admit Card (OUT), Date Sheet, Syllabus, Exam Pattern (Revised) & Question papers

For students appearing in the Punjab School Education Board (PSEB) Class 12 examinations in 2026, it's crucial to stay informed about all the key details. This guide covers important updates regarding your admit card, date sheet, syllabus, exam pattern, and how to utilize past question papers for effective preparation.

## Key takeaways

*   The PSEB Class 12 exams for 2026 commenced on February 17 and are scheduled to conclude on April 4, 2026.
*   The official date sheet for the 2026 examinations was released by the Punjab Board on December 30, 2025, with a notable revision for the Chemistry exam.
*   Admit cards for regular students have been issued to schools, and students must collect them from their respective institutions.
*   The board has already conducted practical exams from February 2 to February 12, 2026.

## PSEB 12th Board 2026 Important Dates

Staying updated with important dates is essential for smooth preparation and examination. The main theory exams are conducted across various test centers between 11:00 AM and 2:15 PM daily. The evaluation of answer sheets is being carried out digitally by the Punjab Board.

## PSEB 12th Date Sheet 2026

The Punjab Board officially released the Class 12 date sheet for 2026 on December 30, 2025. This comprehensive document includes exam dates, timings, and general instructions for all subjects across Science, Commerce, and Arts streams. It's important to note a revision in the schedule: the Chemistry exam, initially set for April 4, 2026, was preponed to March 20, 2026.

Students can download the official date sheet by following these steps:

1.  Visit the official PSEB website.
2.  Navigate to the 'Important Links' section and click on the 'Date Sheet' tab.
3.  Look for and select the link titled 'Senior Secondary PSEB Date Sheet 2026 Class 12 Arts, Science & Commerce'.
4.  The date sheet will appear as a PDF file, which you can then download and save for your reference.

## PSEB Class 12 Admit Card 2026

The Punjab School Education Board has already made the Class 12 admit cards for regular students available. These admit cards are provided online to schools, which can download them using their unique login credentials. It is mandatory for all students to collect their admit card from their respective schools well in advance of the exams.

Remember to carry your admit card to the examination center on all exam days. Failure to present a valid admit card will result in you being denied permission to sit for the examination.

## PSEB 12th Syllabus, Exam Pattern (Revised) & Question Papers

To excel in the PSEB Class 12 exams, a thorough understanding of the syllabus and exam pattern, combined with strategic practice, is vital.

### Syllabus

The official PSEB website provides the complete Class 12 syllabus for 2026. Students should carefully review this to ensure they cover all topics and chapters prescribed for their respective subjects. This will help in focused preparation and avoid missing any crucial content.

### Exam Pattern

The PSEB Class 12 exam pattern for 2026 indicates that the examinations are conducted in a pen-and-paper format, with each paper generally lasting three hours. A minimum of 33% marks is required to pass each subject. Familiarizing yourself with the exam pattern provides insights into the marking scheme, types of questions, and overall structure of the papers.

### Sample Papers and Previous Years' Question Papers

Practicing with PSEB Class 12 sample papers and previous years' question papers is an invaluable preparation strategy. These resources help students:

*   Become familiar with the examination structure and question formats.
*   Understand the typical difficulty level and weightage of different topics.
*   Improve time management skills during the actual exam.
*   Identify strong and weak areas for targeted revision.

By diligently working through these papers, students can boost their confidence and readiness for the board examinations.
