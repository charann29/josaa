# ATMA May Exam Registration 2026 (Started): Direct Link, Application Fees & How to Apply

Registration for the ATMA May 2026 examination has officially begun, offering prospective students a pathway to MBA and PGDM programs. This guide provides all the essential information for applying, including key dates, fees, and a step-by-step application process.

## Key takeaways

*   The application window for ATMA May 2026 opened on February 24, 2026.
*   The final date to pay application fees is April 19, 2026.
*   The last day to submit the completed application form is April 21, 2026.
*   The examination will be conducted as a center-based online test.

## ATMA 2026 Application Process Overview

The application process for ATMA 2026 is entirely online. It involves two main stages: first, paying the application fee, and then, completing and submitting the detailed application form. There are no age restrictions for candidates applying for the ATMA exam.

## Important Dates for ATMA May 2026

Staying informed about the schedule is crucial for a smooth application. Here's a breakdown of the important dates for the ATMA May 2026 session:

| Event                                 | Date           |
| :------------------------------------ | :------------- |
| ATMA May 2026 Registration Begins     | Feb 24, 2026   |
| Last Date for Fee Payment (May 2026)  | Apr 19, 2026   |
| Last Date for Application (May 2026)  | Apr 21, 2026   |
| Admit Card Release (May 2026)         | Apr 29, 2026   |
| ATMA Exam Date (May 2026)             | May 03, 2026   |

## ATMA Application Fees 2026

The standard application fee for ATMA is INR 2000. However, AIMS (Association of Indian Management Schools), the conducting body for ATMA, offers fee concessions for certain applicant categories. Women candidates and those from North-Eastern states of India are eligible for a reduced fee. Payments can only be made online using debit cards, credit cards, net banking, or UPI.

## Documents Required for ATMA Registration 2026

Before you begin the online application, ensure you have the following documents ready in digital format:

*   **Photograph and Signature:** A scanned copy, with a maximum file size of 512 KB.
*   **Category Certificate:** If applicable (for OBC, SC, ST, DAP), a scanned copy of the latest certificate, up to 1 MB in size.
*   **Academic Mark Sheets:** Scanned copies of mark sheets for Class 10, Class 12, and Graduation, each up to 1 MB in size.
*   **Sponsoring Certificate:** For sponsored students, a certificate in the format specified on the official ATMA website.
*   **Payment Details:** Your debit card, credit card, net banking, or UPI details for online fee payment.

## How to Fill the ATMA Application Form 2026

The ATMA application process involves two primary steps: fee payment and form submission. Follow these instructions carefully:

### Step 1: Pay the Application Fee

1.  Visit the official ATMA website for registration.
2.  You will be directed to the fee payment page.
3.  Enter your personal details, including your email ID, mobile number, and Aadhaar number.
4.  Click on 'Proceed to Fees Payment'.
5.  Select your preferred online payment method (debit/credit card, net banking, or UPI) and complete the transaction.

### Step 2: Fill and Submit the Application Form

1.  After successful fee payment, you will receive a PID (Personal Identification Number) and password via email or SMS. Keep these safe.
2.  Log in to the applicant portal using your PID and password.
3.  Carefully fill in all required personal, academic, and contact details in the application form.
4.  Upload the scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and other necessary documents as per the specified size and format guidelines.
5.  Review all the entered information thoroughly to ensure accuracy and correctness.
6.  Submit the application form.
7.  It is highly recommended to download and print a copy of your submitted application form for future reference.

Remember to double-check all details before final submission to avoid any issues. Good luck with your ATMA May 2026 application!
