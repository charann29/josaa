# Pearl Academy Entrance Exam 2026: Application (OPEN), Exam Date (OUT), Syllabus, Results

For students aspiring to build a career in design, fashion, business, or media, Pearl Academy offers a well-regarded pathway. Admission to its diverse programs hinges on clearing the Pearl Academy Entrance Exam, followed by a personal interview.

## Key takeaways

*   **Applications are currently open** for the Pearl Academy Entrance Exam 2026 for Bachelor's and Master's programs.
*   The application fee for undergraduate and postgraduate courses is INR 2,000, while for certificate courses, it is INR 500.
*   The final date to submit your application is April 22, 2026.
*   The entrance examination is scheduled for April 25 and April 26, 2026.

## Understanding the Pearl Academy Entrance Exam

Established in 1993, Pearl Academy has grown to be a prominent institution offering a range of programs in design, fashion, business, and media. To gain entry into these sought-after courses, candidates must successfully navigate the Pearl Academy Entrance Exam, often referred to as the PAF Entrance Exam. This written test is a crucial first step, leading to a mandatory personal interview round.

Pearl Academy conducts distinct entrance exams and interview rounds for each admission cycle, ensuring a thorough evaluation of all applicants. Successful candidates can secure admission to various Bachelor's, Master's, and professional courses across its campuses.

## Pearl Academy Application Process 2026

The application window for the Pearl Academy Entrance Exam 2026 is currently open for both Bachelor of Design (B.Des) and Master of Design (M.Des) programs. The application fee is INR 2,000 for these longer-duration courses. For those interested in shorter certificate courses, the application fee is INR 500.

Applicants have until April 22, 2026, to submit their application forms. The academy accepts applications through both online and offline modes, providing flexibility for candidates. It is always recommended to refer to the official Pearl Academy portal for detailed instructions on how to apply.

## Exam Pattern and Selection Criteria

The Pearl Academy Entrance Exam typically consists of two main components: the General Proficiency Test (GPT) and a specialized aptitude test, depending on the chosen field of study. For design courses, candidates take the Design Aptitude Test (DAT), while for media courses, they take the Media Aptitude Test (MAT). It's also worth noting that Pearl Academy may accept CUET scores for B.Des admissions.

After the written examination, shortlisted candidates proceed to a Personal Interview. The final result for Pearl Academy 2026 admissions will be determined by assigning appropriate weightage to all components of the selection process, including the written exam and the interview round.

Aspiring students should visit the official Pearl Academy website for the most up-to-date information regarding the syllabus, detailed exam pattern, and eligibility criteria for their chosen program.
