# Smart Pro Digital Marketing by Aptech Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in the dynamic field of digital marketing, the Smart Pro Digital Marketing program from Aptech Learning offers a comprehensive training pathway. This program is designed to equip students with essential skills for the modern digital landscape.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program focuses on critical areas like social media optimization, analytics, and web development.
*   It's a nine-month course, with classes held three times a week for two hours each.
*   Learning can be done online through video lectures and projects, or by attending a physical center.
*   The fee ranges from approximately ₹41,100 to ₹56,200, exclusive of taxes, and student loan facilities are available.

## Course Overview

The Smart Pro Digital Marketing program dives deep into key aspects of digital marketing, emphasizing skills that are highly sought after by employers today. The curriculum is structured into two terms to provide a thorough understanding of the subject.

The first term covers foundational web development using the Laravel Framework with PHP, along with HTML5, JavaScript, and CSS. This ensures students have a strong technical base. The second term then shifts focus to core digital marketing strategies, including Search Engine Optimization (SEO), display advertising, mobile advertising, and search advertising. You'll also learn about email marketing and Google Ads (AdWords).

A significant part of the training involves understanding how social media optimization can boost an organization's visibility in organic search results and expand its reach. Furthermore, the course teaches how social media analytics can be used to improve customer satisfaction, identify market trends, enhance customer relationship management (CRM), and support faster business decision-making.

## Duration and Learning Mode

This certification program spans nine months. Classes are scheduled three times per week, with each session lasting two hours. Students have the flexibility to choose their learning mode: either entirely online through video lectures, projects, and practical applications, or by attending classes at an Aptech Learning center. The online format includes access to an online community, 24/7 interaction with industry experts, and placement assistance.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Smart Pro Digital Marketing program, candidates must hold a graduate or postgraduate degree. Working professionals and MBA students are also eligible to apply, making it suitable for those looking to upskill or transition their careers.

## Program Fees and Financial Aid

The fee for the Smart Pro Digital Marketing program varies depending on the specific Aptech Learning center location. The general fee range is between ₹41,100 and ₹56,200. It's important to note that these figures are exclusive of applicable taxes. For precise fee details, prospective students should contact their nearest Aptech Learning center. Additionally, Aptech Learning offers instant student loan facilities to help manage the course expenses.

## How to Apply

Admission to the Smart Pro Digital Marketing course involves a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Aptech Learning website.
2.  Navigate to the 'Courses' section and locate the 'Smart Pro Digital Marketing Course'.
3.  Click on the course link to access the detailed information page.
4.  Complete the career guidance form with the required personal details and submit it.
5.  Aptech Learning will then contact you via phone, SMS, or email to schedule career counseling.

Upon completing these steps and the counseling session, you can proceed with your enrollment.

This program aims to provide you with the practical skills and industry insights necessary to thrive in the competitive digital marketing sector.
