Welcome to BeingWise! Getting into an IIM is a dream for many, and understanding the admission process is crucial. Today, we're looking at the experience of Adarsh Navlakha, a high-achiever who successfully converted his admission call to IIM Bangalore.

## Key Takeaways

*   Adarsh, a 99.2 percentile scorer in CAT 2022, successfully secured admission to IIM Bangalore.
*   His preparation included not just CAT, but also IIFT and SNAP, where he achieved 99.9 percentile in both.
*   The IIM Bangalore selection process involves a Written Ability Test (WAT) followed by a Personal Interview (PI).
*   Adarsh's profile highlights a diverse background, including engineering, business analysis, and leading a non-profit.

## Adarsh's Journey to IIM Bangalore

Adarsh Navlakha, originally from Bhopal, demonstrated exceptional performance in various management entrance exams. Beyond his impressive CAT score, he also excelled in IIFT and SNAP, showcasing his strong aptitude across different test formats. His academic background includes a Bachelor of Engineering from Shri G S Institute of Technology & Science. Professionally, he worked as a Business Analyst at Trident Group and currently runs 'The Happy Club,' a non-profit focused on personality and public speaking development. Adarsh also has an interest in content creation, having published a book through Kindle.

## The IIM Bangalore Written Ability Test (WAT)

The Written Ability Test (WAT) is a key component of the IIM Bangalore selection process, designed to assess a candidate's ability to articulate thoughts clearly and concisely under time pressure. Adarsh shared his experience with the WAT, noting the importance of structuring arguments logically and presenting a well-reasoned perspective. While the specific topic can vary, the core expectation is to demonstrate analytical skills and effective written communication.

## The IIM Bangalore Personal Interview (PI)

The Personal Interview (PI) at IIM Bangalore is a comprehensive interaction where the panel evaluates a candidate's personality, communication skills, leadership potential, and overall fit for the program. Adarsh's interview covered various aspects of his profile. The panel typically delves into academic background, work experience, extracurricular activities, and future aspirations. Candidates should be prepared to discuss their motivations for pursuing an MBA, their understanding of current affairs, and how their unique experiences align with the IIM Bangalore ethos.

## Adarsh's Insights and Tips for IIM Bangalore PI-WAT

Based on his successful conversion, Adarsh emphasizes the importance of authenticity and preparation. For the WAT, practicing essay writing on diverse topics, especially those related to current events and business, can be beneficial. For the PI, understanding your own resume thoroughly, being ready to discuss your strengths and weaknesses, and having clear reasons for wanting to join IIM Bangalore are vital. He also highlighted that engaging in activities like his non-profit work and content creation can provide valuable talking points and demonstrate initiative and leadership qualities to the interview panel.

Adarsh's journey offers valuable insights for aspiring IIM candidates, reinforcing that a strong academic record combined with a well-rounded profile and thoughtful preparation can lead to success in the highly competitive IIM admission process.
