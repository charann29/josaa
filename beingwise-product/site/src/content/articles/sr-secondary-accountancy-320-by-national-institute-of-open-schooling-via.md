# Sr.Secondary: Accountancy (320) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students aspiring to build a strong foundation in financial accounting, the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) offers a comprehensive online course in Accountancy (320) for the Senior Secondary level, delivered through the Swayam platform. This program is designed to provide a deep understanding of accounting principles and practices.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is a 24-week, self-paced online program focused on Senior Secondary Accountancy.
*   It awards 10 credit points upon successful completion, along with a certificate from NIOS and Swayam.
*   Learning happens through engaging video lectures, reading materials, quizzes, and interactive discussion forums.
*   The curriculum covers fundamental accounting concepts, partnership accounts, company accounts, and an introduction to computerized accounting.

## Course Overview and Structure

This online Accountancy course (code 320) is a Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) developed by Professor Ms. Anshul Kharbanda from the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS). It is structured into seven modules, progressing logically through key financial accounting concepts and principles. The course is entirely in English and uses a self-study approach, with content delivered through both video and text.

The program aims to equip learners with the ability to understand and analyze various aspects of accounting, laying a crucial groundwork for future careers in finance and accounting roles.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this certification program, you will gain proficiency in a wide range of accounting topics. The curriculum covers essential areas from foundational concepts to more advanced applications, including:

*   An introduction to finance and accounting principles.
*   Understanding basic accounting terms, concepts, conventions, and standards.
*   Preparing ledgers, cash books, and special purpose books.
*   The role of trial balance and an introduction to computerized accounting systems.
*   Identifying and rectifying accounting errors.
*   Preparing and analyzing financial statements for both profit and non-profit organizations.
*   Accounting for partnerships, including admission, retirement, death, and dissolution.
*   Company accounts, focusing on the issue, forfeiture, and reissue of shares, as well as debentures.
*   Analysis of financial statements and the application of computers in financial accounting, including spreadsheets.

## Learning Experience and Resources

The Sr. Secondary: Accountancy (320) course on Swayam offers a rich and interactive learning environment. Participants will benefit from:

*   **Video Lectures:** Expert-led video content to explain complex topics clearly.
*   **Reading Materials:** Comprehensive textual resources to supplement the video lessons.
*   **Quizzes:** Regular assessments to help you test your understanding and track progress.
*   **Discussion Forums:** An interactive platform to clarify doubts, ask questions, and engage with peers and instructors.

This blend of resources ensures a well-rounded and engaging educational experience, allowing you to learn at your own pace while staying connected with the course community.

## Duration and Certification

The course spans 24 weeks, providing ample time to delve into each topic thoroughly. Successful completion of the program, including assignments and quizzes, leads to a certificate. This certificate is jointly awarded by NIOS and Swayam, validating your achievement and understanding of Senior Secondary Accountancy. The course also carries 10 credit points.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Sr. Secondary: Accountancy (320) course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal for the course.
2.  Locate the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  If you are a new user, select "Register" to create an account. Existing users can simply "Sign-in" with their credentials.
4.  Once logged in, you can enroll in the course to begin your learning journey.

This course is an excellent opportunity for anyone looking to build a strong foundation in accountancy, whether for academic progression or career development.
