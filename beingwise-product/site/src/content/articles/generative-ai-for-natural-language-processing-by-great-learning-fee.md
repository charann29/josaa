# Generative AI for Natural Language Processing by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This certification program aims to equip participants with the skills to leverage generative Artificial Intelligence (AI) for various Natural Language Processing (NLP) tasks. It's designed to help learners use advanced AI models to create human-like text and innovative solutions for NLP challenges.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 17-week online program, offering a certificate upon completion.
*   The curriculum includes over 10 real-world case studies and 2 hands-on projects for practical learning.
*   The program fee is ₹1,20,000, inclusive of GST.
*   It is particularly beneficial for professionals like Data Scientists, Software Engineers, and Machine Learning Engineers.

## Program Overview

The Generative AI for Natural Language Processing certification course by Great Learning provides a deep dive into the principles and applications of generative AI in NLP. Students will gain a solid understanding of how NLP systems work and learn to implement these advanced AI techniques effectively. The program emphasizes practical application, ensuring participants can develop and deploy solutions for complex language-related problems.

## What You Will Learn

The syllabus covers core NLP concepts, moving into advanced topics like text summarization, generation, and memory reasoning. Participants will enhance their querying abilities and develop a strong foundation in natural language processing. The course is structured to guide students through interactive lectures, practical coding exercises, and real-world projects, all under the guidance of industry experts. Completing this program can open doors to careers in prompt engineering and related AI fields.

### Tools and Technologies Covered

During the program, learners will work with several key tools and libraries essential for modern NLP and generative AI. These include:

*   SpaCy
*   Beautiful Soup
*   Scikit-learn
*   TensorFlow/Keras
*   PyTorch
*   Hugging Face
*   OpenAI
*   LangChain
*   NLTK

## Program Structure and Content

The 17-week online program is divided into modules, starting with foundational knowledge and progressing to advanced generative AI concepts. The learning experience combines self-study with virtual classroom sessions, delivered through video and text-based content. The initial module focuses on preparing students with necessary prerequisites:

### Module 0: Pre-work

*   **Week 0: Precursors to Generative NLP**
    *   Machine Learning Refresher
    *   Text Preprocessing for NLP
    *   Deep Learning for NLP

This foundational module ensures all participants have a common understanding before diving into the core Generative AI and Large Language Model (LLM) topics.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Generative AI for Natural Language Processing certification program is ₹1,20,000, which includes GST. Upon successful completion of all course modules, participants will receive a Certificate of Completion directly from Great Learning, validating their acquired skills in generative AI for NLP.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Generative AI for Natural Language Processing certification course involves a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official Great Learning program page for this course.
2.  Complete and submit the online application form, providing all necessary personal and contact details, such as your name, primary email address, and phone number.
3.  After submitting your application, you will be contacted with further information regarding the course.
4.  Proceed to pay the course fee through the provided payment gateway.
5.  Once payment is confirmed, you will gain access to the course materials and can begin your learning journey.

This program is an excellent opportunity for anyone looking to advance their career in the rapidly evolving field of generative AI and natural language processing.
