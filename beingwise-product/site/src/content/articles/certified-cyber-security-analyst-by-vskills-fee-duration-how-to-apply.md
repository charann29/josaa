# Certified Cyber Security Analyst by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's digital world, understanding cybersecurity is more crucial than ever. The Certified Cyber Security Analyst program by Vskills offers a comprehensive pathway for individuals to master essential cybersecurity concepts, equipping them with the knowledge to combat online threats and protect digital assets.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a government-certified, self-paced online program focused on core cybersecurity principles.
*   No prior educational qualifications are required, making it accessible to a wide range of aspirants.
*   The course covers a broad spectrum of topics, from cybercrime and forensics to incident handling and the IT Act 2000.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a Vskills certification, enhancing their career prospects in the IT and cybersecurity sectors.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The Certified Cyber Security Analyst program is designed to provide participants with a strong foundation in cybersecurity. Delivered through video and text-based modules, the course allows for flexible, self-study learning. The curriculum delves into critical areas such as keyloggers, SQL injections, cyberterrorism, and DDoS attacks. It also covers important aspects like computer and wireless forensics, identity theft, phishing, and the legal framework provided by the IT Act 2000.

Participants will gain practical knowledge to help employers and individuals protect against online crimes. The program aims to prepare candidates for various roles in cybersecurity by providing insights into prevention, protection, and resolution techniques for cyber incidents.

## Who is This Course For?

This certification is suitable for a diverse audience, including:

*   **Aspiring IT and Cybersecurity Professionals:** Individuals looking to kickstart their careers in the rapidly growing fields of IT and cybersecurity.
*   **Students:** Those who wish to enhance their skill set and gain a competitive edge in the job market.
*   **Existing Professionals:** Employees seeking career advancement or a more specialized role within their organizations.

Whether you are new to the field or already have some exposure, this program offers valuable insights and a recognized certification to boost your professional journey.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Certified Cyber Security Analyst program is ₹3,499, excluding taxes. Goods and Services Tax (GST) will be applied at the time of checkout. This fee covers access to all course materials, including online classrooms, study materials, sample questions, and demo classes.

Upon successful completion of the certification exam, Vskills, the certifying authority, will issue a government-certified document. This certification is a valuable asset for anyone looking to validate their cybersecurity expertise.

## Eligibility and Examination Process

One of the key advantages of this program is its open eligibility criteria – **no specific educational background is required** to enroll. This makes it accessible to anyone with an interest in cybersecurity.

To earn the certification, candidates must pass an online examination conducted by Vskills. The exam consists of 50 multiple-choice questions, and a minimum score of 25 marks is required to pass. Participants have the flexibility to choose their preferred date, time, and venue for taking the online exam.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Certified Cyber Security Analyst program is a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official Vskills website for the certification program.
2.  Locate and click on the 'BUY NOW' option, typically found on the right side of the page.
3.  You will be directed to a login page. Either log in with your existing credentials or register as a new user.
4.  Proceed to the billing section, enter your details, and select your preferred payment method.
5.  Once the payment is complete, you will gain access to the course materials and can begin preparing for your exam.

## Syllabus Highlights

The comprehensive syllabus ensures that participants gain a holistic understanding of cybersecurity. Key areas covered include:

*   **Cyber Technology Fundamentals:** Networking, Wireless LAN Basics, Web Basics, and Website security.
*   **Cyber Ethics and Crimes:** Understanding ethical hacking, various types of cybercrimes, and their impact.
*   **Prevention and Protection:** Learning different methods and techniques to prevent and protect against cyber threats.
*   **Incident Handling:** Strategies for managing and responding to security incidents.
*   **Forensics:** Introduction to computer and wireless forensics.

This structured curriculum ensures that certified analysts are well-prepared to tackle real-world cybersecurity challenges.

With its flexible learning format, comprehensive curriculum, and government-recognized certification, the Vskills Certified Cyber Security Analyst program offers an excellent opportunity to build a rewarding career in cybersecurity.
