# Psychology XII Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For Class 12 students interested in understanding the basics of psychology, the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) offers a comprehensive online course. Delivered via the Swayam platform, this program provides a solid foundation in key psychological concepts.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a self-paced, online certificate course designed for Class 12 students.
*   The program spans 24 weeks and is delivered through video lectures and reading materials.
*   Participants can earn a certificate from NCERT upon successfully completing the course and final assessment.
*   The curriculum covers fundamental psychological attributes, self and personality, psychological disorders, and therapeutic approaches.

## Course Overview and Structure

This online course is specifically designed to introduce Class 12 students to the core principles of psychology. It aims to help learners grasp disciplinary knowledge and apply psychological concepts to understand their own behavior and that of others. The learning mode is entirely self-study, allowing flexibility for students to progress at their own pace.

The course content is delivered through a blend of video lectures, detailed e-text materials, and supplementary web resources. To reinforce learning, self-assessment quizzes and discussion forums are integrated into the program. Dr. Prabhat K. Mishra from the Department of Education in Psychology and Foundations of Education (DEPFE), NCERT, New Delhi, leads the instruction.

## Duration and Certification

The Psychology XII Part-I course has a duration of 24 weeks. Upon successful completion of all course lessons and achieving a minimum score of 70% on the final assessment, participants will be awarded a certificate. This certificate is officially provided by NCERT, affirming the student's understanding of the course material.

## What You Will Learn

This program is structured to provide a broad understanding of various aspects of psychology. Key topics covered include:

*   **Fundamentals of Psychology:** An introduction to the basic concepts and principles that underpin the field.
*   **Psychological Attributes:** Exploring different human attributes and how they are measured and understood.
*   **Self and Personality:** Delving into theories of self, personality types, traits, and assessment methods.
*   **Meeting Life Challenges:** Understanding stress, its effects, coping mechanisms, and life skills for stress management.
*   **Psychological Disorders:** An overview of various psychological disorders and their characteristics.
*   **Therapeutic Approaches:** Introduction to different types of psychotherapy and their processes.

## How to Enroll

Enrolling in the Psychology XII Part-I Certification Course is a straightforward process through the official Swayam portal. Here are the steps:

1.  Visit the specific course page on the Swayam platform.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the new page, you will have the option to sign in with an existing account or create a new one using your username and password to proceed with enrollment.

This course offers a valuable opportunity for Class 12 students to explore the fascinating world of psychology, gain foundational knowledge, and potentially consider it as a future academic or career path.
