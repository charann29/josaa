# Digital Vidya Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Digital Vidya Courses

Digital Vidya offers a range of online courses designed to help students and professionals gain expertise in various digital domains. From digital marketing to data analytics and machine learning, these programs are structured to provide practical, industry-relevant skills.

## Key takeaways

*   Digital Vidya offers both short-term and longer executive programs in in-demand digital fields.
*   Courses often combine theoretical knowledge with practical projects and case studies.
*   Some programs provide dual certification, enhancing their value in the job market.
*   Learning formats vary, including self-paced modules and instructor-led sessions.

## Executive Program in Digital Marketing

This comprehensive program is offered in collaboration with Deakin University, Melbourne, and is tailored for individuals looking to master digital marketing strategies for business growth and online presence optimization. The curriculum emphasizes the crucial role of digital marketing in today's landscape, particularly its expanded importance post-pandemic.

Participants will delve into core internet marketing concepts, including effective brand presence, navigating the social media sphere, and understanding Search Engine Optimization (SEO). The course also covers Search Engine Marketing (SEM) and the metrics associated with enhancing visibility on search engines. Upon successful completion, students receive a certification from Deakin University.

*   **Duration:** 10 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹ 1,50,000
*   **Key Skills:** SEO, Google Ads knowledge

## Certified Digital Marketing Course

Digital Vidya's Certified Digital Marketing course provides a focused overview of essential digital marketing components and the tools used to create significant online impact. Designed by industry experts, this program ensures that the curriculum aligns with the crucial skills and techniques required for a digital marketer role.

The course covers fundamental areas such as Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Social Media Marketing (SMM), and Email Marketing. It is delivered as an online, self-paced program, allowing learners to progress through video lessons, PDFs, and PPTs at their convenience. Completing this program awards participants a Digital Marketing Expert Certificate.

*   **Duration:** 4 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹ 55,000
*   **Key Skills:** SEO

## Data Analytics Course

This program from Digital Vidya offers an in-depth exploration of data science and data visualization, blending theoretical concepts with practical application. The training modules are designed to build a strong understanding of essential tools like QlikView, PowerBI, SQL, Excel, and Tableau.

Alongside advanced Excel training, the course covers critical data management skills such as data cleaning, manipulation, analysis, comparison, visualization, sorting, and filtering. Learners benefit from pre-recorded sessions by industry leaders, hands-on projects, and assignments in a self-paced format. The program also includes capstone projects for real-world exposure, such as risk assessment in banking, IPL data analysis, and data science in computer rental businesses. Participants gain lifetime access to learning modules and are eligible for dual certification from Digital Vidya and Vskills.

*   **Duration:** 16 Weeks
*   **Fees:** ₹ 12,500
*   **Key Skills:** Excel, SQL, Data Visualization, Tableau
