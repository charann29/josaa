# Advanced Program in AI for Financial Markets by National Stock Exchange of India Ltd via TalentSprint: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For finance professionals looking to integrate cutting-edge artificial intelligence into their work, the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd (NSE), in collaboration with TalentSprint, offers a specialized program. This program is designed to equip participants with the AI skills necessary to navigate and innovate within the dynamic financial markets.

## Key Takeaways

*   A six-month, live online program focusing on AI applications in finance.
*   Developed with the expertise of NSE, India's largest stock exchange.
*   Features hands-on labs, case studies, and a capstone project for practical learning.
*   Offers a completion certificate from the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd.

## Program Overview and Structure

This Advanced Program in AI for Financial Markets is a comprehensive six-month certification course delivered through live online sessions. It's tailored for professionals aiming to strengthen their foundational knowledge in mathematics and programming, essential for deep-tech learning. The curriculum incorporates video and text-based materials, alongside virtual classroom interactions and self-study components.

The program leverages the extensive knowledge of the NSE, recognized globally as a leading derivatives exchange. It aims to empower financial market professionals to harness the transformative power of AI technologies, enhancing their analytical and decision-making capabilities.

## Who Is This Program For?

This program is ideal for professionals with 1 to 10 years of experience across various financial domains, including investment banking, commodity markets, hedge funds, and stock markets. It is also suitable for individuals in roles such as managers, co-founders, product management analysts, and software engineers who wish to apply AI in a financial context.

To be eligible, applicants must hold a graduation degree from any recognized university.

## What You Will Learn

The Advanced Program in AI for Financial Markets syllabus is designed to provide participants with practical AI knowledge and its applications in finance. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Utilizing AI to provide informed advice to investors and drive impactful results.
*   Developing advanced analytical and decision-making skills.
*   Building and expanding a consulting portfolio focused on AI in finance.
*   Gaining deep insights into applying AI for stock trading strategies.

The program includes masterclass lectures, faculty-led sessions, and hands-on labs, culminating in case studies and capstone projects to ensure practical application of learned concepts.

## Fees and Payment Options

The program fee for domestic participants is ₹2,00,000. For international participants, the fee is $2,800. There may be early bird scholarship opportunities that reduce the fee to ₹1,70,000 for domestic participants and $2,300 for international participants. The fee is non-refundable. Additionally, a 0% EMI option is available to make the program more accessible.

## How to Apply

The application process for the Advanced Program in AI for Financial Markets is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official program application portal.
2.  Complete the online program application form.
3.  Await confirmation of your application and selection status.
4.  Upon selection, proceed with the necessary steps to secure your spot in the program.

This program presents a unique opportunity for finance professionals to upskill with AI, guided by the expertise of the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd and delivered through TalentSprint's robust learning platform.
