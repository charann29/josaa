# WBJEE JECA 2026: Application Form (April 9), Exam Dates (June 14), Eligibility, Revised Syllabus, Exam Pattern

For those aspiring to pursue a Master of Computer Application (MCA) degree in West Bengal, the WBJEE JECA is the gateway. This exam, conducted by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB), opens doors to various institutions across the state.

## Key Takeaways

*   The WBJEE JECA 2026 examination is scheduled for **June 14, 2026**.
*   Online applications will commence on **April 9, 2026**.
*   The exam is a crucial step for admission to MCA programs in West Bengal.
*   Eligibility includes Indian citizenship and a completed bachelor's degree or appearing in the final year of graduation in 2026.

## Understanding WBJEE JECA

WBJEE JECA stands for West Bengal Joint Entrance Exam for Computer Application. It is an annual postgraduate entrance examination specifically designed for candidates seeking admission to MCA courses within West Bengal's colleges and universities. The WBJEEB is the official body responsible for organizing this examination.

Staying informed about key dates is essential for a smooth application and examination process. Here's a look at the confirmed and tentative schedule for WBJEE JECA 2026:

## Application Process and Eligibility Criteria

The application form for WBJEE JECA 2026 will be available online on the official WBJEEB website from April 9, 2026. Candidates must ensure they meet the eligibility requirements before applying.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **Citizenship:** Applicants must be Indian citizens.
*   **Educational Qualification:** Candidates should have completed their graduation or be appearing for their final year of graduation in 2026. Specific details regarding minimum marks or subjects might be outlined in the official information bulletin.
*   **Age Limit:** There is no upper or lower age limit to apply for WBJEE JECA.

### Application Fee

The application fee varies by category:

*   **General Category:** INR 500
*   **SC/ST/OBC-A/OBC-B/EWS/Third Gender:** INR 400

## Exam Pattern and Syllabus Overview

The WBJEE JECA is conducted offline and consists of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) in English. A thorough understanding of the exam pattern and syllabus is vital for effective preparation.

### Exam Pattern Highlights

| Feature             | Detail                                                                  |
| :------------------ | :---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Conducting Body     | West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB)                  |
| Exam Level          | Postgraduate                                                            |
| Course Offered      | Master of Computer Application (MCA)                                    |
| Mode of Exam        | Offline (Pen and Paper based)                                           |
| Medium of Exam      | English                                                                 |
| Type of Questions   | Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)                                        |

### Revised Syllabus

The WBJEEB typically releases a detailed syllabus for JECA on its official portal. Candidates are strongly advised to refer to this official syllabus to understand the topics and weightage for subjects like Mathematics, Computer Awareness, Logical Reasoning, and English. Preparing according to the revised syllabus ensures that your efforts are aligned with the examination's requirements.

## After the Exam: Admit Cards, Results, and Counselling

Successful submission of the application form leads to the issuance of the WBJEE JECA admit card, which is mandatory for appearing in the exam. Following the examination, the WBJEEB will release model answer keys, allowing candidates to estimate their scores, and an official result. Qualified candidates will then be invited for WBJEE JECA Counselling 2026. Seat allocation during counselling depends on your rank, preferred colleges, category, and seat availability.

We encourage all aspiring MCA students to regularly check the official WBJEEB website for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding WBJEE JECA 2026.
