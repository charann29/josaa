# Artificial Intelligence for Real-World Application by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub Skillsoft: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program offers a deep dive into Artificial Intelligence (AI) and its practical uses, designed for individuals looking to apply AI concepts in real-world scenarios. It's a comprehensive course developed to equip learners with essential AI skills through a blend of digital and hands-on learning.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Industry-Relevant Certification:** Earn a verifiable digital certificate from TCS iON upon successful completion, signifying your proficiency in AI for real-world applications.
*   **Blended Learning Approach:** The program combines self-study with virtual classroom sessions, digital lectures, and practical assignments to ensure a well-rounded learning experience.
*   **Career Advancement Opportunities:** Benefit from connections to industry and academic communities, potentially leading to internships and job placements with leading companies.
*   **Comprehensive AI Curriculum:** The syllabus covers core AI topics from foundational concepts to advanced areas like Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and TensorFlow.

## Course Overview

The Artificial Intelligence for Real-World Application course is structured to explore the intricacies of AI and its advanced uses. Delivered by experienced professionals, the curriculum blends digital lectures, e-learning materials, and practical, hands-on exercises. The main goal is to provide participants with a clear understanding of how AI is applied in industrial and practical settings.

Upon successfully completing all course modules and passing the final assessment, participants will receive an official Artificial Intelligence for Real-World Application Certification from TCS iON. The program also fosters a supportive environment through academic and industry communities, offering learners opportunities to connect and explore career paths.

## Program Structure and Highlights

This program is delivered in English, making it accessible to a wide audience. The learning format includes both self-study and virtual classroom sessions, with content provided through videos and text. The course is designed to be completed over approximately 5 to 6 months and awards 5 course credits.

Key features of the program include:

*   A certificate of achievement and a verifiable digital certificate.
*   Access to extensive digital learning resources and expert-led digital lectures.
*   Opportunities for practice assessments and industry-focused assignments.
*   A summative assessment to evaluate learning outcomes.
*   Guidance from renowned industry experts.
*   Access to industry and academic connect communities.
*   Potential for internship and job opportunities.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Artificial Intelligence for Real-World Application course and certification is ₹20,000, which includes GST. This fee covers all aspects of the program, from learning materials to the final assessment and certification.

To be eligible for the certificate of achievement, candidates must meet specific criteria in the summative assessment:

*   Score 40% or more in Part A.
*   Score 50% or more in Part B.
*   Achieve an overall score of 50 marks or more in the summative assessment.

Those who do not meet these criteria or miss the summative assessment will receive a certificate of participation. Candidates who fail the summative assessment will receive a performance scorecard.

## Eligibility and Who Should Apply

This program is particularly suited for engineering students in their 5th, 6th, 7th, or 8th semesters across any discipline (B.E. or B.Tech). 

**Prerequisites:**

*   **Mandatory:** Prior knowledge of Object-Oriented Programming Systems (OOPs) is essential.
*   **Recommended (Optional):** Basic understanding of sensors is beneficial but not strictly required.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Artificial Intelligence for Real-World Application course is a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Navigate to the official TCS iON website dedicated to this course.
2.  **Initiate Enrollment:** Look for and click on the "Activate Now" option.
3.  **Account Access:** If you are a new user, you will need to sign up. Existing users can simply sign in with their credentials.
4.  **Complete Registration:** Fill in all the necessary details as prompted to finalize your enrollment.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to build a strong foundation in Artificial Intelligence and enhance your career prospects in this rapidly evolving field.
