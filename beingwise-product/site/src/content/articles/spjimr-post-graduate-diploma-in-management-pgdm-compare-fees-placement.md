# SPJIMR Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM): Compare Fees, Placement, Reviews, Cutoffs

For students aspiring to a top-tier management career, the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) at S.P. Jain Institute of Management and Research (SPJIMR) in Mumbai is a highly regarded option. This program offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to prepare future business leaders.

## Key takeaways

*   SPJIMR's PGDM is a two-year, full-time program based in Mumbai, established in 1981.
*   The program has a strong placement record, achieving 100% placements with a median salary of INR 32.85 Lakh.
*   Admission requires a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks and valid scores in CAT or GMAT.
*   Student reviews consistently rate the program highly, particularly for placements and faculty.

## Program Overview and Structure

The SPJIMR PGDM is a two-year postgraduate diploma course. It is delivered in a full-time, classroom-based format, offering an intensive learning experience. The institute, a private establishment founded in 1981, is approved by AICTE, ensuring its academic standards meet regulatory requirements.

## Fees and Funding

The total fees for the two-year PGDM program at SPJIMR amount to INR 22.50 Lakh. Prospective students should factor this into their financial planning and explore available scholarship or loan options.

## Admissions: Eligibility and Cutoffs

To be eligible for the SPJIMR PGDM program, applicants must hold a graduation degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. Admission to the program is highly competitive and requires strong scores in management entrance exams. The accepted examinations are:

*   **CAT:** A cutoff of 85 percentile was observed in Round 1 for the General category, All India.
*   **GMAT:** Applicants must also submit valid GMAT scores.

It's important to note that these cutoffs are indicative and can vary each year based on the applicant pool and program requirements.

## Placements: A Strong Career Launchpad

SPJIMR's PGDM program boasts an impressive placement record, reflecting the quality of its graduates and industry connections. According to official reports, the program achieved 100% placements for its batch. Key placement statistics include:

*   **Highest Salary:** INR 75.00 Lakh
*   **Median Salary:** INR 32.85 Lakh

These figures highlight the strong career opportunities available to SPJIMR PGDM alumni across various sectors.

## Student Reviews and Rankings

Based on feedback from 26 verified students, the SPJIMR PGDM program has an overall rating of 4.2 out of 5. Students particularly commend the program in several areas:

*   **Placements:** 4.3/5
*   **Faculty:** 4.3/5
*   **Crowd & Campus Life:** 4.4/5
*   **Value for Money:** 4.3/5

In terms of external recognition, SPJIMR has consistently performed well in national rankings. In the NIRF rankings, the institute was placed 3rd in 2024 and 20th in 2025 (as per available data).

## Campus Facilities

SPJIMR offers a well-equipped campus to support student learning and well-being. Facilities include:

*   Library
*   Cafeteria
*   Hostel accommodation
*   Sports complex and gym
*   Medical facilities
*   Wi-Fi enabled campus
*   Auditorium and A/C classrooms
*   Convenience store and labs

These amenities contribute to a conducive environment for academic and personal growth.

Considering all these aspects, the SPJIMR PGDM remains a highly sought-after program for management aspirants in India.
