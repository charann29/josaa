# Angular Certification Training Course: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to master the Angular framework, a comprehensive training program is available to help you develop dynamic web applications from the ground up. This course is designed to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge needed to become a proficient Angular developer.

## Key takeaways

*   The program offers instructor-led online sessions combined with practical assignments and projects.
*   Participants gain lifetime access to course materials, including videos, PDFs, and presentations.
*   Upon successful completion of a certification project, an Angular Developer Certificate is awarded.
*   Technical support is available 24/7 for any queries that arise during your learning journey.

## Course Overview

This training course delves into the complete Angular 8 framework, starting with fundamental concepts and progressing to advanced topics. You will explore key elements such as components, modules, and data binding, along with Angular animations, forms, and the use of Pipes and Directives for manipulating the Document Object Model (DOM).

Beyond the basics, the curriculum covers more intricate subjects like Dependency Injection (DI) and Services for effective component interaction. You'll also learn how to communicate with backend services using the HTTP protocol. The course structure emphasizes hands-on learning through practical assignments, projects, and industry-level case studies, guided by experienced IT professionals.

## Learning Format and Resources

The program offers a flexible learning experience, primarily through virtual classrooms and self-study modules. Instruction is delivered in English, with classes typically held on weekends. Learning materials include video sessions, PDFs, and presentations, ensuring a variety of resources to support your understanding. Participants also benefit from a learner's community forum, fostering interaction and collaborative learning.

## Eligibility and Certification

To enroll in this Angular certification program, candidates should have a foundational understanding of object-oriented concepts, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Prior experience in JavaScript web development is beneficial.

To earn the 'Angular Developer' Certificate, enrolled candidates must successfully complete a course-end certification project. This project allows you to apply the concepts learned throughout the training. The completed project needs to be submitted via email within two weeks of course completion for evaluation by a panel of experts. Your performance on this project will determine your grade and the awarding of the certificate.

## Fees and Payment Options

The standard fee for the online training program is ₹19,995. However, a discounted price of ₹13,996 is often available. For those seeking flexible payment solutions, an EMI option may be offered, with installments starting at approximately ₹4,666 per month. It's advisable to check the official course page for the most current pricing and payment details.
