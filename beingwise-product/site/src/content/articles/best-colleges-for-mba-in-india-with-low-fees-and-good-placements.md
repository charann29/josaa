# Best Colleges for MBA in India with Low Fees and Good Placements

Pursuing an MBA can be a significant step for your career, and it doesn't always have to come with a hefty price tag. Many top institutions in India offer excellent management education with strong placement records at a budget-friendly cost.

## Key takeaways

*   Several highly-ranked management institutes offer MBA programs with total fees under INR 7 Lakhs.
*   Admission to these colleges typically requires strong scores in national-level entrance exams like CAT, MAH CET, MAT, ATMA, or CUET PG.
*   Despite lower fees, these institutions boast impressive average placement packages, often exceeding INR 25 LPA.
*   These colleges provide quality infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections.

## Top MBA Colleges with Affordable Fees and Strong Placements

When considering an MBA college with a lower fee structure, it's crucial to also look at the quality of education and placement opportunities. Here are some of India's leading institutions that strike this balance:

### Faculty of Management Studies (FMS), Delhi University

FMS, affiliated with Delhi University, consistently ranks among the top management institutes in India. It is renowned for offering an MBA program at one of the most affordable fees, starting around INR 2.32 Lakhs for the entire course. Admission is highly competitive, based on CAT scores followed by personal interviews. Graduates from FMS secure excellent placements, with an average salary package reported at INR 34.1 LPA. Leading companies such as Deloitte, OLA, PwC, L'Oreal, Info Edge, Adobe, Google, and Amazon recruit from FMS.

### Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management Studies (JBIMS), Mumbai

Established in 1965 and affiliated with the University of Mumbai, JBIMS is a public institute offering full-time and part-time MMS programs, with its MBA equivalent being particularly popular. The two-year program fee is approximately INR 6.10 Lakhs. JBIMS accepts scores from various entrance exams including CAT, MAH CET, MAT, and ATMA. It consistently ranks among the top 10 B-schools in India and has a vast network of successful alumni. The average salary package for its graduates is around INR 26.12 LPA, with recruiters like McKinsey & Company, JP Morgan Chase, Accenture, Axis Bank, and Amazon.

### Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS), Mumbai

TISS Mumbai is another excellent institution providing quality management education at a very reasonable cost. The fee for its MBA equivalent program is approximately INR 1.4 Lakhs. Historically, admissions were based on the TISSNET score, but currently, admission to TISS is conducted through the Central University Entrance Test-PG (CUET PG). TISS graduates also achieve strong placement outcomes, with an average salary from placements reported at INR 28.2 LPA.

## Other Popular MBA Colleges with Low Fees

Beyond these top institutions, several other colleges across India offer valuable MBA programs at an affordable cost, maintaining good academic standards and placement support. These often include various university departments and government-aided institutions that prioritize accessibility without compromising on quality.

Choosing the right MBA program means balancing your budget with your career aspirations, and these institutions prove that high-quality education doesn't always require a high investment.
