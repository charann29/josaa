# List of 11 ICFAI University Campuses: Branches, Courses, Fees and Admission

The ICFAI Group is a well-known educational institution in India, offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. Established in 1984, the group operates through its ICFAI Universities and ICFAI Business Schools (IBS), providing quality education in fields like Management, Engineering, Science, Arts, and Commerce.

## Key takeaways

*   The ICFAI Group comprises 20 campuses in major Indian cities, including 11 ICFAI Universities and 9 ICFAI Business Schools.
*   ICFAI Foundation for Higher Education (IFHE) in Hyderabad holds 'Deemed-to-be University' status, while other ICFAI Universities are state-recognized private universities.
*   Admission to management programs often considers scores from IBSAT or other national MBA entrance exams.
*   Courses span Management, Engineering, Law, Computer Applications, Science, and Humanities.

## Understanding the ICFAI University Network

The ICFAI Group has expanded significantly since its inception, creating a network of educational institutions designed to cater to diverse academic interests. Among its various entities, the 11 ICFAI Universities play a crucial role in delivering a broad spectrum of programs. While IFHE Hyderabad stands out as a Deemed-to-be University, the remaining ten are recognized as state private universities, each contributing to the group's educational mission across different regions of India.

## ICFAI Foundation for Higher Education (IFHE), Hyderabad

IFHE Hyderabad is a flagship institution within the ICFAI Group, known for its extensive campus and academic excellence. Spread over 91 acres, it is a fully residential campus accommodating a large student body with modern amenities. These include separate residential facilities for male and female students, dining halls, and various sports and recreational facilities like swimming pools and gymnasiums.

Established in 1956, IFHE is a Deemed-to-be University offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs through its three main faculties:

*   **IBS Hyderabad:** Offers MBA, Executive MBA, PGP, BBA, PhD, and various certificate courses in management.
*   **Faculty of Science and Technology:** Provides B.Tech, B.Sc, M.Tech, and PhD programs.
*   **Faculty of Law:** Includes BBA-LLB, BA-LLB, LLM, PhD, and certificate courses in legal studies.

### Accreditations and Rankings for IFHE Hyderabad

IFHE has received significant recognition for its academic standards. It holds an 'A' Grade accreditation from NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) with an institutional CGPA score of 3.43 out of 4. Furthermore, IBS Hyderabad was among the initial B-Schools in South Asia to receive SAQS accreditation from the Association of Management Development Institutions in South Asia (AMDISA) in 2006.

## Other ICFAI University Campuses

Beyond IFHE Hyderabad, the ICFAI Group operates ten other state-recognized private universities across India. These campuses collectively offer a diverse portfolio of programs, including those in management, engineering, science, arts, and commerce. Each university is designed to serve the educational needs of its respective region, maintaining the ICFAI Group's commitment to quality education.

## Admission Process and Fees

Admission to ICFAI Universities generally involves meeting specific eligibility criteria for each program. For management courses, scores from exams like IBSAT or other national-level MBA entrance tests are often considered. For other programs, the process may include an application review, written tests, and personal interviews, followed by selection based on academic records and performance in these rounds.

Fee structures vary significantly across campuses and programs. It is always advisable to check the official prospectus or website of the specific ICFAI University campus for the most accurate and up-to-date fee information.

Making an informed decision about higher education requires careful consideration of various factors, and the ICFAI Universities offer a range of options for students across India. Always refer to the official university portals for the most current details on admissions, courses, and fees.
