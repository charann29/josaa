# VIVO Healthcare Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online VIVO Healthcare Courses

VIVO Healthcare offers a range of professional certificate programs designed to equip individuals with essential skills for the rapidly growing healthcare sector in India. These programs focus on practical training and industry-relevant knowledge, helping students build successful careers.

## Key takeaways

*   VIVO Healthcare provides specialized certificate courses in high-demand allied healthcare fields.
*   Programs combine theoretical learning with hands-on simulations and internships.
*   Courses typically range from 12 to 14 months in duration.
*   Certification is often provided by VIVO Healthcare and/or NSDC, enhancing employability.
*   Placement assistance and education loan support are available for many programs.

## Emergency Medical Technician-Paramedic

This 14-month (1800 hours) program is designed to transform aspirants into skilled Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) professionals capable of delivering top-tier paramedic care. Beyond core medical treatment, the curriculum also integrates managerial skills, preparing graduates to lead rescue teams effectively.

The comprehensive syllabus is regularly updated to meet current industry standards, covering vital topics such as patient assessment, managing trauma emergencies, safe ambulance operations, pediatric care, and out-of-hospital medications. The learning methodology includes theoretical classes, practical simulations, and valuable internships. Upon successful completion, students receive a certification and benefit from complete placement support, along with assistance in securing education loans.

## Radiology Technician

With a high demand for skilled professionals across the country, the Radiology Technician certification course is a 14-month program focused on operating advanced radiographic equipment. Students learn to proficiently use CT scanners, MRI machines, X-Ray machines, and other diagnostic tools to produce high-quality images of the human body.

The training blends practical and theoretical lessons, emphasizing the critical safety measures required when working with radiation in a healthcare setting. Graduates receive certification from VIVO Healthcare and/or NSDC. This credential helps secure entry-level positions, such as a Junior X-Ray Technician, with clear pathways for career progression to roles like Radiology Technician in Charge.

## Dialysis Technician

This 12-month program, comprising 800 hours of theory and simulation, trains allied healthcare professionals in the precise use and maintenance of dialysis machines and related equipment. The curriculum specifically prepares students to conduct hemodialysis (HD) sessions, meticulously follow nephrologists' instructions, and diligently monitor both the patient and the dialysis machine throughout the procedure.

The course incorporates various assessments, including weekly tests, module-based exams, a pre-internship examination, and a final foundation exam, to continuously evaluate learning progress. Successful completion leads to a certification from VIVO Healthcare and/or NSDC, significantly enhancing employment prospects in this specialized field.

## Medical Laboratory Technician

Medical Laboratory Technicians play a vital role in hospitals, blood banks, and diagnostic laboratories. This course teaches students how to perform diagnoses by analyzing fluid and tissue samples. The training covers techniques for testing results for accuracy and reliability, which is crucial for patient care and treatment decisions.

By providing hands-on experience and theoretical knowledge in laboratory procedures, the program prepares individuals to contribute effectively to diagnostic processes. Graduates are equipped with the skills necessary to work in various laboratory settings, supporting medical professionals in identifying and monitoring health conditions.

VIVO Healthcare's certificate programs offer focused, practical pathways for individuals looking to enter or advance their careers in essential allied healthcare professions.
