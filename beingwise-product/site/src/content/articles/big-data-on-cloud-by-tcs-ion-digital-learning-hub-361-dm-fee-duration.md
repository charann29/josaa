# Big Data on Cloud by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub 361 DM: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This certification program offers an in-depth exploration of big data concepts within a cloud environment, equipping students with essential skills for data management and application development. Designed for a duration of five to six months, the course is delivered through the TCS iON digital learning platform.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn about data storage, processing, and application creation on the cloud.
*   Gain five academic credit points upon successful completion.
*   Benefit from a hybrid learning approach, including virtual classes and practical sessions.
*   Receive guidance from both academic experts and an industry professional from TCS.

## Course Overview

The Big Data on Cloud certification is structured to provide a thorough understanding of managing large datasets and leveraging cloud infrastructure for data-driven applications. The curriculum covers fundamental aspects like data analysis, long-term data retention strategies, and the design of data models and pipelines.

The learning experience combines self-study with interactive virtual classrooms. You'll engage with online lectures, tutorials, hands-on practical sessions, and assignments that reflect real-world industry challenges. The program also offers access to an academic community and an industry connect community, fostering networking and collaborative learning.

## Expert Mentorship

Students in this program will learn from a distinguished panel of educators and industry leaders. Dr. Rajiv Misra and Dr. Sriparna Sahi, both Assistant Professors in the Computer Science and Engineering department at IIT Patna, bring academic rigor to the curriculum. Complementing their expertise is Dr. Arabinda Mandal, an Enterprise Architect at TCS, who provides valuable industry insights and practical perspectives.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to impart a comprehensive skill set in data engineering and cloud technologies. Key areas of study include:

*   **Big Data Concepts:** Understanding the principles and technologies behind managing and processing vast amounts of data.
*   **Cloud Computing:** Gaining proficiency in cloud platforms, with practical sessions utilizing Amazon Web Services (AWS).
*   **Data Visualization:** Learning techniques to represent complex data in an understandable and insightful manner.
*   **Data Engineering:** Developing skills in extracting, processing, and automating data pipelines, and designing data lakes.

This program aims to develop expertise in in-memory computation, data model design, and the overall derivation of information from large datasets.

## Eligibility and Certification

To enroll in the Big Data on Cloud program, applicants should have a foundational understanding of object-oriented programming. The course is particularly beneficial for students pursuing Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, and Electronics and Communication Engineering. Professionals such as big data engineers, big data developers, and database architects can also enhance their skills through this certification.

Upon completion, two types of certificates are awarded by TCS iON:

*   **Certificate of Achievement:** Requires a minimum score of 40% in Part A, 50% in Part B, and an overall average of 50% across all assessments.
*   **Certificate of Participation:** Awarded to students who complete the entire course, including all projects, industry-based assignments, and practice assessments.

## Fees and Application

The total fee for the Big Data on Cloud certification course is ₹20,000, which includes GST. This payment provides full access to all course materials and online classes. The fee is paid online during the registration process.

| Course Fee   | ₹20,000 |

Registration for the program is conducted online through the official TCS iON digital learning hub.
