# RPF Constable 2025: PET Dates OUT, Pattern, Vacancy, Salary & News

For those aspiring to join the Railway Protection Force (RPF) and Railway Protection Special Force (RPSF) as Constables, the 2025 recruitment drive brings some significant updates. This year, the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) will be overseeing the examination, marking a change from previous years when the Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) managed the process.

## Key takeaways

*   The RPF Constable examination for 2025 will now be conducted by the Staff Selection Commission (SSC).
*   Eligibility criteria have been updated, with the age limit set between 18 and 28 years, and a minimum educational qualification of Class 10 pass or equivalent.
*   The selection process involves multiple stages: a Computer-Based Test (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Measurement Test (PMT), and Document Verification.
*   Important PET dates for 2025 have been announced, ranging from November 13 to December 6.

## RPF Constable Recruitment Exam Overview

The RPF Constable exam is a national-level competitive examination designed to recruit both male and female candidates for Constable roles within the RPF and RPSF. With the SSC now at the helm, the recruitment process aligns with their established standards for similar examinations. This ensures a streamlined and standardized selection procedure for all applicants.

## Eligibility Criteria for RPF Constable 2025

To be considered for the RPF Constable role, candidates must meet specific eligibility requirements. The age limit for applicants is between 18 and 28 years. In terms of education, candidates must have successfully passed Class 10 or an equivalent examination from a recognized board.

## RPF Constable Selection Process

The selection process for RPF Constable is comprehensive and designed to assess candidates on various parameters. It typically involves three main stages:

### Stage 1: Computer-Based Test (CBT)

This is the initial screening stage where candidates take an online examination. The CBT evaluates general awareness, arithmetic, general intelligence, and reasoning abilities.

### Stage 2: Physical Efficiency Test (PET) & Physical Measurement Test (PMT)

Candidates who clear the CBT proceed to the PET and PMT. The PET assesses physical capabilities through various tasks like running, long jump, and high jump. The PMT measures physical standards such as height and chest (for male candidates).

### Stage 3: Document Verification (DV) and Medical Examination

Successful candidates from the PET/PMT stage undergo document verification to confirm the authenticity of their submitted credentials. This is followed by a medical examination to ensure candidates meet the prescribed health standards for the role.

## RPF Constable Salary

RPF Constable positions are central government jobs, offering competitive salaries and benefits. The salary for an RPF Constable generally ranges between INR 37,420 and INR 44,460 per month, along with other allowances as per government norms.
