# Board Infinity Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Board Infinity Courses

Board Infinity offers a variety of online courses and certifications designed to help students and working professionals enhance their skills and advance their careers. These programs cover in-demand fields, providing practical knowledge and industry-relevant training.

## Key takeaways

*   Board Infinity provides both short-term bootcamps and more extensive certification programs.
*   Courses often include live online sessions, case studies, and projects for hands-on learning.
*   Many programs focus on career readiness, with some offering placement support.
*   Popular domains include Full Stack Development, Digital Marketing, and Content Marketing.

## Full Stack Development with Certification

This comprehensive online program is tailored for individuals aiming to master both front-end and back-end web development. It focuses on the MERN Stack, which includes MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js, essential technologies for modern web applications.

The curriculum is structured over 20 weeks and involves more than 120 hours of live online instruction. Students engage in practical case studies and projects, gaining experience with technologies like JavaScript, JQuery, SQL, and REST APIs. The goal is to equip learners with the ability to design and build responsive web applications, transforming them into competent full-stack developers.

**Key Details:**

*   **Duration:** 20 Weeks
*   **Approximate Fee:** ₹ 1,00,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Web application development, CSS, SQL, Database Management.

## Learning Path in Digital Marketing Course with Placement

Designed for beginners and intermediate learners, this live online certificate program helps participants build expertise in the dynamic field of digital marketing. A significant feature of this course is the placement assistance provided until students secure a suitable role in digital marketing.

Over 17 weeks, the curriculum delves into various aspects of digital marketing, including Search Engine Optimization (SEO), web analytics, mobile marketing, and email marketing. Practical projects, such as consumer research, social media strategy development, and SEO audit analysis, ensure that students acquire industry-relevant skills and knowledge.

**Key Details:**

*   **Duration:** 17 Weeks
*   **Approximate Fee:** ₹ 70,000
*   **Skills Covered:** SEO, Digital marketing strategies.

## Express Bootcamp for Content Marketing

In today's digital age, compelling content is crucial for organizational visibility and marketability. This bootcamp is designed for those eager to develop effective content strategies for businesses. It guides participants in creating content that resonates with audiences and enhances an organization's market presence.

The three-month program incorporates both theoretical knowledge and practical exercises. Industry experts from leading companies contribute to the training, helping participants refine their content marketing skills. Successful completion of projects and assessments leads to a Board Infinity certification.

**Key Details:**

*   **Duration:** 3 Months
*   **Approximate Fee:** ₹ 50,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Digital marketing, Content writing, Content strategy.
