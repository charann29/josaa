# Aptech Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Aptech Learning Courses

Aptech Learning offers a variety of career-focused courses designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills across multiple domains like IT, banking, and finance. These programs aim to prepare learners for specific job roles through structured curricula and expert guidance.

## Key Takeaways

*   Aptech Learning provides specialized certificate courses in high-demand sectors such as E-commerce, Banking, and Accounting.
*   Programs are structured to deliver practical, job-oriented skills, often including hands-on training and exposure to industry tools.
*   Course durations vary, typically ranging from 3 months to 23 months, with corresponding fee structures.
*   The curriculum emphasizes both foundational knowledge and advanced techniques to ensure graduates are workplace-ready.

## ACCP Pro – E-commerce Course

The ACCP Pro – E-commerce course from Aptech Learning is designed for individuals looking to enter the rapidly expanding E-commerce sector. This comprehensive 23-month program dives deep into the essentials of E-commerce, providing a detailed understanding of the digital marketplace.

Students will gain expertise in several critical areas, including:

*   Web productivity tools
*   Logic building for applications
*   Developing next-generation websites
*   Fundamental programming concepts
*   Practical E-commerce implementation strategies

The course aims to develop professionals capable of navigating and thriving in the E-commerce industry, equipping them with both technical and professional skills. The fee for this extensive program is ₹77,400.

**Skills Covered:** SQL, Automation, Taxation, Financial Knowledge, CSS, Java.

## Banking Specialist Certification

Recognizing the robust growth of the Indian banking sector, Aptech Learning offers the Banking Specialist certification to prepare individuals for careers in this dynamic field. This program focuses on providing foundational and specialized knowledge in banking and finance.

The Banking Specialist course is structured into three parts, delivered over 16 months, with in-class lectures scheduled three days a week for two hours each. The curriculum is crafted to impart skills necessary for various banking operations and roles. The program fee is ₹54,000.

**Skills Covered:** Comprehensive banking knowledge.

## Aptech Certified Accounts Professional

The Aptech Certified Accounts Professional program is a 15-month intensive training course designed to create job-ready accounting professionals. This curriculum is divided into two terms, each covering a broad range of modules essential for modern accounting practices.

Participants will develop expertise in:

*   Core computer applications vital for business accounting
*   Proficiency in Tally software
*   Advanced topics like business communication, statutory compliance, TDS, GST, and direct taxation

This course focuses on providing the latest accounting techniques, financial management skills, and taxation knowledge, ensuring graduates are equipped to handle complex transactions and contribute effectively to an organization's financial operations. The fee for this program is ₹70,000.

**Skills Covered:** Accounting proficiency, Business Management.

Aptech Learning's commitment to employment-driven education ensures that its courses are constantly updated to meet industry demands, providing students with a strong foundation for their chosen careers.
