# Pulmonology: Chest Medicine and Respiratory Medicine

For anyone looking to understand the core concepts of respiratory health, this online certification offers a concise yet comprehensive introduction. It's designed to equip you with fundamental knowledge in managing various chest and lung conditions.

## Course Overview

Pulmonology is a vital medical specialisation focused on the respiratory system, which includes the lungs, airways, and related structures. Pulmonologists are experts in diagnosing and treating conditions that affect breathing, often managing patients who require mechanical ventilation and life support.

This certification program, developed by Dr. S.S. B., a Gynecologist and Radiologist, aims to provide foundational knowledge in this field. It's particularly useful for individuals aspiring to understand the principles and methods involved in pulmonology, or for healthcare professionals looking to refresh their knowledge.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this online certification, you will be introduced to essential concepts and techniques in pulmonology for addressing a range of respiratory and pulmonary diseases. The course explores the roles of different medicines in treating various ailments. You will gain insight into treatment strategies for conditions such as:

*   Lung cancer
*   Bronchiectasis
*   Kartagener syndrome
*   Tuberculosis
*   Pneumonia
*   Aspergillosis
*   COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease)
*   DLCO (Diffusing capacity of the lung for carbon monoxide) issues
*   Cyanosis

Additionally, the course covers disorders like clubbing and lung cavities, and the procedures involved in a tuberculin test.
