# Top 21 MBA Colleges in Maharashtra Through MAH CET 2026

For students aspiring to pursue an MBA or MMS in Maharashtra, the MAH CET is a crucial entrance examination. This state-level test opens doors to numerous management institutes across the state, including some highly regarded institutions in Mumbai and Pune. Understanding which colleges accept MAH CET scores and their admission criteria is vital as you prepare for the exam.

## Key takeaways

* The MAH CET exam is a gateway to over 350 management institutes in Maharashtra, including top B-schools.
* Admission to these colleges is based on your MAH CET percentile score, followed by a Centralised Admission Process (CAP).
* For premier institutions, a percentile score of 97 to 99+ is generally required.
* The MAH CET 2026 exam will be conducted in two phases, giving candidates two attempts, with the best score considered for admission.

## Understanding the MAH CET Exam and Admission Process

The State Entrance Test Cell conducts the MAH CET, which is a significant examination for management aspirants in Maharashtra. Each year, over one lakh candidates apply for this exam, aiming to secure a seat in one of the state's affiliated colleges. For 2026, the MAH CET is scheduled across two phases: the first phase in early April and the second phase in early May. This allows candidates to take the exam twice, and their best score will be used for admission purposes.

Following the examination, results for all sessions are typically released together, usually in June. After the results are out, the CET Cell organises a Centralised Admission Process (CAP), which is expected to take place in July-August 2026. This common counselling round allocates seats based on merit, determined by your MAH CET percentile and the minimum cut-off scores set by individual colleges.

## MAH CET Cut-offs: What to Expect

Securing admission to a top management institute through MAH CET requires meeting specific percentile cut-offs. These cut-offs vary significantly from college to college, generally ranging from 50 to over 99 percentile. For the most sought-after colleges, a high score is essential. For instance, the top 10 MBA colleges in Maharashtra typically require a MAH CET percentile of 97 to 99 or even higher.

As you move down the list to colleges ranked from 11 to 30, the cut-offs generally fall within the 90 to 95 percentile range. It's important to research the specific percentile requirements for your target colleges as you plan your preparation strategy.
