## Understanding Surface Geology for Exploration

Surface geology refers to the study of loose materials like sand, gravel, and clay that cover the solid bedrock. These materials are crucial in mineral exploration, especially for surface mining methods such as strip mining, open-pit mining, and quarrying. Unlike underground mining, surface mining involves removing the overlying soil and rock to access mineral deposits. This course delves into how to effectively plan for such exploration by examining the geology of the near-surface environment.
