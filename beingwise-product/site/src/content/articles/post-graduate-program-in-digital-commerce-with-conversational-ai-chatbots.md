# Post Graduate Program in Digital Commerce with Conversational AI Chatbots by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program is designed for individuals eager to build a career in digital commerce, with a specific focus on developing and implementing conversational AI chatbots. It's a comprehensive course ideal for aspiring business analysts and those looking to enhance their technical skills in this evolving field.

## Key takeaways

*   A focused 6-month program delivered through video and text-based self-study.
*   Covers essential topics like cloud computing, project management, and data analytics.
*   Includes extensive support options, such as one-on-one and group Zoom sessions.
*   Offers a completion certificate, with a merit certificate for the top 5% of learners.

## Program Overview

The Post Graduate Program in Digital Commerce with Conversational AI Chatbots is a specialized, six-month course focusing on the creation of applications within the digital commerce landscape. It's particularly well-suited for individuals aiming for a career as a business analyst, providing them with a strong foundation in relevant technologies.

The curriculum is delivered through expert video lectures, practical technical projects, and supplementary course materials. Key areas of study include fundamental concepts of cloud computing, project management principles, Natural Language Processing (NLP), data analytics, software testing methodologies, and database management. The program is structured into eight distinct courses, each delving into specialized topics crucial for this field.

Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate. Additionally, the top 5% of learners will be awarded a merit certificate, recognizing their exceptional performance.

## Who Is This Program For?

This program is an excellent fit for students in their final year of undergraduate studies or working professionals who are interested in pursuing or advancing a career as a business analyst. The content is tailored to equip learners with the necessary skills to navigate the complexities of digital commerce and AI integration.

## What You Will Learn

The program provides insights into various critical areas, ensuring a well-rounded understanding of digital commerce with AI. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Front-End Development:** Mastering the complete front-end development process.
*   **Java Programming:** Gaining an introduction to Java programming, including data structures and algorithms.
*   **Databases:** Understanding various aspects of database management.
*   **Business Analysis:** Building fundamental knowledge for aspiring business analysts.
*   **Node.js API Development:** Learning to develop APIs using Node.js.
*   **Mongo Database Administration:** Skills in administering Mongo databases.
*   **Conversational AI:** An introduction to platforms like Yellow Messenger for chatbot development.

## Program Support and Features

The program offers a robust support system designed to assist learners throughout their journey. These include:

*   **Flexible Course Fees:** Options to suit different financial needs.
*   **Expert Instructors:** Learning from experienced professionals in the field.
*   **Dedicated Support:** Access to one-on-one and group Zoom support sessions, as well as email and telephone assistance.
*   **Community Engagement:** Participation in course-specific forum groups for peer interaction.
*   **Practical Experience:** Engagement in industry-oriented technical projects to build a professional portfolio page.
*   **Personalized Guidance:** One-on-one demo sessions and course counseling for tailored advice.
*   **Hands-On Assistance:** Direct support from expert engineers.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Post Graduate Program in Digital Commerce with Conversational AI Chatbots, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official program page.
2.  Locate and select the "Enroll Now" option on the course page.
3.  Choose your preferred payment package and complete the registration by providing your name, email ID, and phone number to gain access.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to develop specialized skills in a rapidly growing sector, preparing you for a successful career in digital commerce and AI.
