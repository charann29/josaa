# NIELIT CCC 2025 November Cycle: Exam Dates (Out), Admit Card, Result

For students looking to build foundational computer skills, the National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT) offers the popular Course on Computer Concepts (CCC). The exam dates for the November 2025 cycle have now been announced, providing a clear timeline for aspiring candidates.

## Key takeaways

*   The NIELIT CCC November 2025 examinations are scheduled from November 18 to November 21, 2025.
*   Admit cards for this cycle will be available for download online starting November 10, 2025.
*   The CCC course aims to equip individuals with essential IT literacy for everyday personal and professional tasks.
*   Successful completion of the exam, which requires a minimum of 50%, leads to a NIELIT CCC certificate.

## Understanding the NIELIT CCC Course

NIELIT provides a range of digital literacy courses, from basic to advanced levels, to enhance computer knowledge across various age groups. Among these, the Course on Computer Concepts (CCC) is particularly well-known, designed to impart fundamental IT literacy. This course helps learners become proficient in using computers for common tasks such as drafting documents, navigating the internet, managing emails, creating presentations, and handling small databases.

Here’s a quick overview of the CCC course:

| Parameter | Detail |
| :-------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Course Name | NIELIT Course on Computer Concepts |
| Abbreviation | CCC |
| Course Duration | 90 Hours (30 hours theory, 60 hours practical) |
| Application Portal | student.nielit.gov.in |
| Exam Fee | Rs. 500 |
| Number of Papers | 1 |
| Exam Mode | Online |
| Minimum Pass Marks | 50% |
| Outcome | Award of NIELIT CCC certificate upon successful completion |

## NIELIT CCC 2025 Examination Details

The NIELIT CCC exam is conducted online at designated NIELIT centres. To be eligible to appear for the exam, candidates must properly complete and submit the online examination application form to NIELIT. The exam consists of 100 multiple-choice questions (MCQs), with each question carrying one mark. There is no negative marking, and candidates are given 90 minutes to complete the paper.

## Downloading Your Admit Card

The admit card for the NIELIT CCC November 2025 cycle will be released online on November 10, 2025. Registered candidates can download their admit cards from the official student.nielit.gov.in portal. You will typically need your application number and date of birth to access it. The admit card is crucial as it contains important details such as your examination date and the assigned exam centre. It's important to ensure all information on your admit card is accurate; any discrepancies could lead to issues.

## Checking Your NIELIT CCC Result

Results for the NIELIT CCC examination are usually declared online approximately 15 days after the completion of the exams. Candidates can check their results by logging into the student.nielit.gov.in portal using their NIELIT login credentials. The results are presented in grades, and a minimum score of 50% is required to successfully qualify for the course and receive the NIELIT CCC certificate.

Mastering basic computer skills is an invaluable asset in today's digital world, and the NIELIT CCC course provides an excellent foundation for this journey.
