## Admission Process and Eligibility for 2026

For admission to top science colleges in Mumbai for the academic year 2026, the primary criterion is performance in Class 12 examinations. Most colleges emphasize academic merit, and there are generally no specific entrance exams for B.Sc. programs in these institutions.

### Eligibility Criteria:

* Candidates must have successfully passed their Class 12 (or equivalent) examination from a recognized board.
* A minimum aggregate score, typically around 50% in the Science stream, is often required.
* Some programs or colleges might prefer or require specific subject combinations, such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology (PCMB), depending on the specialization chosen.

Students should regularly check the official websites of their preferred colleges for the most up-to-date admission notifications and detailed eligibility requirements.

### Placements and Salary

Graduates from top science colleges in Mumbai find opportunities across various sectors, including research, education, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, IT, and data science. While specific salary figures can vary widely based on the specialization, individual skills, and the company, a B.Sc. degree from a reputable institution provides a strong foundation for career growth.
