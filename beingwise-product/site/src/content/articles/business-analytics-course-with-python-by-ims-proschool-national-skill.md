# Business Analytics Course with Python by IMS Proschool National Skill Development Corporation: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's data-driven world, understanding business analytics is crucial for many careers. This program, offered jointly by IMS Proschool and the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), aims to equip learners with essential skills in business analytics using Python, preparing them for the demands of the industry.

## Key takeaways

*   This program is a 15-week course focusing on business analytics with Python, offered in both online and offline formats.
*   Participants can earn a completion certificate from IMS Proschool and NSDC, with an optional advanced certification from NSE Academy.
*   The curriculum covers vital topics like statistical techniques, machine learning, and predictive analytics, along with hands-on tool experience.
*   There are no specific prerequisites for enrollment, making it accessible to a wide range of aspiring professionals.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Business Analytics with Python program delves into key concepts such as Big Data, Business Intelligence, Data Science, and Data Mining. It's designed to provide comprehensive learning through a mix of in-depth content, practical industry projects, and expert instruction. The program spans 15 weeks and aims to make participants proficient in various aspects of business analytics.

The curriculum is structured to cover: 
*   Fundamental business analytics concepts and essentials.
*   Statistical techniques, including data types, P-value, error types, and hypothesis testing.
*   Regression techniques, such as logistical and multiple linear regression.
*   Applied Machine Learning.
*   Predictive analytics.
*   Essential tools like Python, R, Excel, Tableau, and SQL.

Training is delivered by current industry experts, ensuring that the content is relevant and up-to-date with industry standards.

## Learning Modes and Flexibility

This program offers significant flexibility in how you can learn. It is available in multiple modes to suit different schedules and preferences:

*   **Classroom Programme:** An offline option conducted during weekends, ideal for those who prefer face-to-face interaction.
*   **Live Virtual Classroom:** An online, interactive learning experience that allows participation from anywhere.
*   **Self-Study with Video and Text:** A flexible online format where you can learn at your own pace.

Classes are scheduled on both weekdays and weekends, accommodating various learner availability. Additionally, free webinars are offered, providing a glimpse into the program's content and approach.

## Certifications Available

Upon successful completion of the Business Analytics with Python program, participants can receive two types of certifications:

1.  **Completion Certificate:** Awarded jointly by IMS Proschool and NSDC after candidates successfully complete all internal quizzes, assessments, and end-examinations.
2.  **NSE Academy Certification:** An optional, advanced certification provided by the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd (NSE Academy). To obtain this, candidates must pass a separate examination conducted by NSE Academy.

These certifications add significant value to a professional's profile, validating their skills and knowledge in business analytics.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

One of the notable features of this program is its open enrollment policy. There are **no specific prerequisites** for joining the Business Analytics Course with Python. This makes it accessible to anyone interested in building a career in business analytics, regardless of their prior academic background or professional experience.

### How to Apply

The application process is straightforward:

1.  **Registration:** You will need to register or log in on the official portal for IMS Proschool and NSDC programs.
2.  **Program Selection:** For the 'Live Virtual Classroom' or 'Classroom Programme' options, select the 'Learn More' button and follow the login/registration prompts to proceed.
3.  **Free Webinar:** If you are interested in a free webinar, select the 'Register Here' option and follow the instructions.
4.  **Payment:** To successfully enroll in either the online or offline program track, you must complete the payment for the course fee.

Detailed guidance for filling out the enrollment form for the Classroom Programme and Live Virtual Classroom options is available on the portal during the application process.

## Fees and Duration

The program has a duration of **15 weeks**. Specific details regarding the course fee are typically provided on the official program portal during the application process. It is advisable to check the portal for the most current fee structure.

This program offers a robust pathway for individuals to gain crucial skills in business analytics, enhancing their career prospects in a rapidly evolving job market.
