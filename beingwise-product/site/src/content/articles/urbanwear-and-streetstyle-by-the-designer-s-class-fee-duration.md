# Urbanwear & Streetstyle by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Explore the dynamic world of urbanwear and street style with this concise online course from The Designer's Class, crafted by celebrity stylist Rhea Kapoor. This program offers a unique insight into the cultural movements and design principles that define this influential fashion segment.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn about the evolution and global impact of urbanwear and street style from an industry expert.
*   The course is beginner-friendly and can be completed in approximately 1 hour and 41 minutes.
*   Access downloadable resources and assignments to enhance your learning experience.
*   Enjoy one year of access to all course materials, available in both English and Hindi.

## Course Overview

This online certification in Urbanwear & Streetstyle provides a foundational understanding of this distinct fashion genre. Taught by Rhea Kapoor, a renowned stylist, the course delves into the history of street style movements, the significance of brands, and current trends in urban fashion worldwide. Students will gain insights into contemporary concepts shaping the industry, such as body positivity, activism, inclusivity, and the evolving definitions of luxury and lifestyle.

The curriculum is designed to explore Ms. Kapoor's creative journey and her extensive experience with urbanwear, offering students a personal and professional perspective on this vibrant segment of fashion design.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing the Urbanwear & Streetstyle course, you will develop a deeper appreciation for the cultural shifts that have shaped urbanwear and streetwear. The program covers essential topics including:

*   Subculture and Counter-Culture influences.
*   The specific landscape of streetwear in India.
*   Understanding customer profiles and the design process.
*   How to effectively style streetwear.
*   The role of branding and logos in urban fashion.
*   Connections between sportswear and streetwear.
*   The impact of prints and accessories.
*   Projections for the future of streetwear.

## Program Structure and Features

The course is delivered online through video and text-based modules, allowing for self-paced learning. It is structured for beginners and includes:

*   Approximately 1 hour and 41 minutes of core learning content.
*   16 downloadable resources to aid your study.
*   18 assignments to reinforce your understanding.
*   One year of access to all course materials.
*   The option to learn in either English or Hindi.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for the Urbanwear & Streetstyle online course is ₹2,499. Please note that while the program offers valuable learning and assignments, a formal certificate of completion is not provided.

## How to Apply

Interested candidates can enroll directly in the online program by paying the specified fee through The Designer's Class platform. There are no specific prerequisites mentioned, making it accessible to anyone keen on exploring urbanwear and street style.

This program offers a practical and engaging way to understand the evolving landscape of urban fashion and its cultural significance.
