# TS EAPCET (EAMCET) Exam 2026: Registration (Started), Date, Syllabus, Pattern, Cutoff, Admission Process

For students aspiring to pursue engineering, agriculture, or medical courses in Telangana, the TS EAPCET (formerly EAMCET) is a crucial entrance examination. This guide provides essential information about the 2026 exam, helping you navigate the application process and prepare effectively.

## Key takeaways

*   The application period for TS EAPCET 2026 concluded on April 4, 2026.
*   JNTU, Hyderabad, on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE), conducts this state-level entrance test.
*   Successful application requires careful adherence to eligibility criteria, payment of fees, and submission of all necessary documents.
*   A correction window is provided for applicants to modify certain details in their submitted forms.

## Understanding the TS EAPCET 2026 Application Process

The application for TS EAPCET 2026 involves several steps, starting with registration and fee payment, followed by filling out the detailed form and uploading required documents. It's vital to complete each step accurately to ensure your candidature is accepted.

### Application Fee Payment

The first step is to pay the registration fee. This can be done online using a Credit Card, Debit Card, or Net Banking, or offline through TS (AP) Online Centres. For general category candidates, the fee is INR 900, while SC/ST candidates pay INR 500. Upon successful payment, a unique transaction ID will be generated, which you should keep safe for future reference.

### Filling Out the Application Form

Once the fee is paid, you can proceed to fill the application form on the official website, eamcet.tgche.ac.in. This section requires you to input personal details, contact information, and academic qualifications. Accuracy is paramount here, as these details form the basis of your application.

### Uploading Required Documents

Candidates must upload scanned images of their photograph and signature according to the specified format and size guidelines. Ensure these are clear and meet the requirements to avoid any issues.

| Particulars | Details |
| :---------- | :------------------------------------ |
| Photograph  | File Size – Less than 50 KB, Format - JPG |
| Signature   | File Size – Less than 30 KB, Format – JPG |

### Final Submission and Printout

After submitting the online application, it's essential to take a printout of the confirmation page. You will need to affix a recent photograph to this printout and get it attested by the Principal of your last attended college or a Gazetted Officer. This physical copy serves as proof of your application.

## Application Form Correction Window

Recognizing that errors can occur, the authorities will open a correction window for TS EAPCET 2026. This period, from April 6 to April 8, 2026, allows registered candidates to modify certain details in their submitted application forms. It's crucial to utilize this opportunity to rectify any mistakes, as incorrect information can lead to issues with your candidature.

Always refer to the official TS EAPCET website for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding the exam schedule, syllabus, pattern, and admission process.
