# IBM Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online IBM Courses

IBM, a global technology leader, collaborates with various educational institutions to offer a range of programs designed to equip professionals and aspiring students with cutting-edge skills in high-demand fields like data science, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence. These programs blend theoretical knowledge with practical application, often including hands-on experience with industry-standard tools.

## Key takeaways

*   IBM offers both hybrid and executive weekend programs in partnership with institutions like Aegis School of Business.
*   Courses cover critical areas such as Data Science, Business Analytics, Big Data, Cloud Computing, and Applied AI.
*   Programs are designed for both freshers and working professionals looking to upskill or transition careers.
*   Many courses provide globally recognized certifications and career support.

## Hybrid Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP) in Data Science, Business Analytics, and Big Data

This 12-month hybrid program, a collaboration between Aegis School of Business and IBM, is recognized as one of India's pioneering high-end data science courses. It is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of data science, business analytics, and big data concepts.

Classes are typically conducted in a hybrid format, with weekday sessions held at the Centre for Excellence in Telecom Technology and Management (CETTM) in Mumbai and online classes on Sundays. This blend offers flexibility while ensuring in-depth learning. The curriculum is uniquely designed with a mix of core and elective subjects, aiming to develop both technical and quantitative skills essential for creating business and social value across diverse industries.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Data Science fundamentals
*   Business Analytics techniques
*   Big Data technologies and applications

## Weekend Executive Part-Time Post Graduate Program in Cloud Computing

Addressing the growing demand for skilled cloud professionals, this 11-month part-time program is specifically tailored for working executives. Offered by Aegis and IBM, it aims to bridge the industry skill gap in cloud computing, a rapidly evolving field in information technology.

The program is conducted on weekends, making it accessible for professionals who wish to enhance their skills without disrupting their current careers. Sessions are generally held at the CETTM, Powai campus. Participants gain expertise in deploying enterprise applications in the cloud and understanding their integration with mobile computing. Upon completion, students receive a valid certification.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Comprehensive Cloud Computing knowledge
*   Business Analytics in a cloud context
*   Big Data and Mobility aspects related to cloud environments

## Post Graduate Program (PGP/MS) in Applied AI, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning

This 11-month program, offered in partnership with Aegis and IBM, addresses the increasing demand for professionals skilled in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and deep learning. The curriculum is thoughtfully designed to provide a strong foundation and practical expertise in these advanced technological domains.

Students benefit from a globally acclaimed credit facility and receive career placement assistance. The course materials, including PDFs, PPTs, and video lectures, are accessible through a world-class mobile application or cloud platform. Additionally, eligible meritorious students may have opportunities to apply for scholarships to assist with program fees.

IBM's collaborative programs offer a robust pathway for individuals to gain specialized knowledge and certifications, preparing them for successful careers in the dynamic tech industry.
