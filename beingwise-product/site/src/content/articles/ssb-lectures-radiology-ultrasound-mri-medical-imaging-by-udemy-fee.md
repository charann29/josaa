*   Learn core and advanced concepts in medical imaging, including CT scans, MRI, and ultrasound.
*   The course is self-paced, delivered through video and text, making it flexible for busy schedules.
*   It provides a certificate of completion upon successful course engagement.

## Course Overview

This certification program, created by Dr. Ss B, a certified physician, focuses on fundamental and advanced principles of medical imaging. It covers a range of topics crucial for anyone looking to excel in medical careers as physicians or medical technicians. The curriculum delves into areas like radiology, ultrasound, and Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), offering a comprehensive look at how these technologies are used in diagnosis and treatment.

The course content is structured into concise, manageable sections, making it easier to absorb complex information despite a total video duration of approximately 1.5 hours. This format is designed to maintain focus and engagement, even with a heavy technical workload.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this online certification, students will gain in-depth knowledge across several key areas of medical imaging. The curriculum covers core concepts of MRI, CT scans, radiology, and ultrasound. Participants will explore the underlying physics of ultrasound, including transducers and various Doppler types, and understand principles like tissue harmonic imaging and spatial compound imaging.

Furthermore, the course addresses advanced topics such as elastography, fusion imaging, cone beam CT, and dual-energy CT. It also covers common artifacts encountered in both ultrasound and MRI, providing a practical understanding of image interpretation challenges.

### Key Syllabus Topics

*   **Physics of Ultrasound:** Transducers, Doppler physics, tissue harmonic imaging, spatial compound imaging.
*   **Advanced Imaging Techniques:** Cone Beam CT, Dual Energy CT, Elastography, Fusion Imaging, Real-Time Virtual Sonography.
*   **Artifacts:** Understanding and identifying ultrasound and MRI artifacts.
*   **Diagnostic Methods:** Principles of radiodiagnosis and sonography.
