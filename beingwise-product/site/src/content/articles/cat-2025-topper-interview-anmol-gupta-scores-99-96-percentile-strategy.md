    a: >-
      The qualifying CAT cut-off percentiles, released publicly by IIMs,
      represent the minimum scores (sectional and overall) required for
      candidates to be considered eligible for the Written Ability Test (WAT)
      and Personal Interview (PI) rounds. These are initial eligibility scores,
      not guarantees of selection. The final CAT cut-off percentiles, which are
      not publicly disclosed, are the actual scores based on which candidates
      are selected for the WAT-PI round. Qualifying cut-offs are category-wise
      (General, NC-OBC, General-EWS, SC, ST, PwD), with higher cut-offs for the
      General category. The final cut-offs are typically higher than the
      qualifying ones.

# CAT 2025 Topper Interview: Anmol Gupta Scores 99.96 Percentile; Strategy, Mocks & Preparation Tips

Following the announcement of CAT 2025 results, we had the opportunity to speak with Anmol Gupta, a high-achiever who secured an impressive 99.96 percentile. Anmol shared valuable insights into his preparation journey and exam-day tactics.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Consistency is Crucial:** Anmol emphasized the importance of a steady, sustained effort over time, rather than sporadic intense study sessions.
*   **Master Mock Tests:** Regular mock tests and their in-depth analysis were central to his strategy, helping him identify weaknesses and refine his approach.
*   **Perfecting DILR:** He achieved a rare 100 percentile in the Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning section, demonstrating the power of focused practice.
*   **Past Papers are Gold:** Solving previous years' CAT questions was a key component of his preparation, familiarizing him with exam patterns and question types.

## Anmol Gupta: At a Glance

Anmol Gupta, an engineering graduate from ABES Engineering College, is among the top scorers in CAT 2025. His remarkable performance, including a perfect score in DILR and strong showings in Quantitative Aptitude and Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension, offers a blueprint for aspiring candidates.

## The Journey: Persistence Pays Off

This was Anmol's third attempt at the CAT exam, and his second serious one. He attributes his success to unwavering consistency and patience. Instead of strictly following a rigid study timetable, his preparation revolved around frequent mock tests, thorough analysis of his performance, and diligently working through past CAT question papers. This adaptive and analytical approach allowed him to understand the nuances of the exam and build confidence steadily.

## Section-Wise Strategy

### Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (DILR): The Perfect Score

Achieving a 100 percentile in DILR is a testament to Anmol's dedicated strategy for this section. He focused on understanding various types of sets and practicing them extensively. His approach involved not just solving problems but also analyzing different ways to tackle them, improving both speed and accuracy. He recommends aspirants dedicate significant time to DILR, as it often proves to be a differentiator.

### Quantitative Aptitude (QA): Building a Strong Foundation

For Quantitative Aptitude, Anmol's strategy involved revisiting fundamental concepts and practicing a wide range of problems. He emphasized identifying strong and weak areas and then dedicating extra effort to improve in the latter. Regular practice with varying difficulty levels helped him build both speed and precision in this section.

### Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension (VARC): Enhancing Understanding

While his VARC percentile was slightly lower than his other sections, it was still a strong performance. Anmol focused on improving his reading speed and comprehension, along with practicing different question types in verbal ability. Regularly reading diverse articles and practicing RC passages were key to his strategy.

## Exam Day Approach and Advice

Anmol stressed the importance of a calm and composed mindset on exam day. He advised aspirants to stick to their practiced strategies, manage time effectively, and not get bogged down by any single difficult question. His experience highlights that a well-prepared mind, combined with a confident approach, can significantly impact performance. He encourages future aspirants to embrace consistency, learn from every mock test, and trust their preparation.
