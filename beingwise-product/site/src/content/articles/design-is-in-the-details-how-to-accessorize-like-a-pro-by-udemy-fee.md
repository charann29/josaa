# Design is in the Details: How to Accessorize Like a Pro by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Transforming a living space often comes down to the smaller elements. This course from Udemy, "Design is in the Details: How to Accessorize Like a Pro," focuses on mastering these nuances to elevate your home's aesthetic.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to strategically use accessories to enhance any room.
*   Understand the art of mixing different metal and wood finishes seamlessly.
*   Discover how architectural molding can dramatically alter a space.
*   Gain practical skills through hands-on exercises for various furniture pieces.

## Course Overview

This self-paced, video and text-based course is delivered entirely in English. It's designed for anyone looking to refine their interior design skills, whether for personal home improvement or professional development. The focus is on practical application, guiding learners through various techniques to make a significant impact with subtle details.

## Fee and Certification

The course fee is ₹3,499. Upon successful completion, participants will receive a certificate directly from Udemy, acknowledging their newfound expertise in accessorizing and detailing interior spaces.

## What You Will Learn: The Syllabus

The curriculum is structured to build your understanding from foundational concepts to advanced techniques. It begins with an introduction to the importance of details in interior design and an overview of the course content, including frequently asked questions and five essential accessories every room should feature.

### Accessorizing for Impact

This section delves into the definition and significance of accessories. You'll learn seven golden rules for accessorizing and practice decorating various furniture pieces like console tables, coffee tables, fireplace mantels, bookcases, and dining tables. The course also covers effective pillow arrangement, proper artwork hanging, and tips on where to source quality home accessories.

### Mastering Finishes

A crucial aspect of interior design is harmonizing different finishes. You'll learn clear guidelines for mixing metal finishes in kitchens and bathrooms, as well as techniques for blending various wood finishes throughout your home. This includes identifying wood undertones and matching appropriate paint colors.

### The Power of Molding

Discover how architectural molding can transform a room. This module covers selecting the right crown molding and baseboards, using door and window casing to add character, and incorporating chair rail and panel molding to elevate design. You'll also explore picture rail molding for a touch of charm, with practical exercises to apply these concepts.

### Conclusion and Assessment

The course concludes with a final quiz to test your retention of key concepts and a summary reinforcing how attention to detail creates exceptional interior design. A bonus lecture is included for those passionate about interior design.

This comprehensive course offers a practical pathway to mastering the art of accessorizing and detailing, enabling you to create truly polished and inviting spaces.
