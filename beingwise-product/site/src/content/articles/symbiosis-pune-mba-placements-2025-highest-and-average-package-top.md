# Symbiosis Pune MBA Placements 2025: Highest & Average Package; Top Recruiters

Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM) Pune is a highly regarded institution for management education in India. Understanding the placement statistics is crucial for prospective students, as it reflects the career opportunities and return on investment an MBA from SIBM Pune can offer.

## Key Takeaways

*   The highest package recorded during the 2025 MBA placements at SIBM Pune was INR 53.58 LPA.
*   The average package for the 2025 batch stood at INR 28.83 LPA, while the median package was INR 26.04 LPA.
*   A total of 109 recruiters participated in the placement drive, offering diverse roles across various sectors.
*   The BFSI sector emerged as the top recruiting industry, followed by Consulting.

## SIBM Pune MBA Placements 2025: Overview

The 2025 placement cycle at SIBM Pune demonstrated strong industry demand for its MBA graduates. The institute successfully placed its batch with attractive compensation packages, indicating a robust placement record. The detailed report highlights the institute's strong connections with the corporate world, leading to a wide array of opportunities for its students.

### Salary Highlights

The final placement process for the 2025 MBA batch at SIBM Pune saw impressive salary figures:

*   **Highest Package:** INR 53.58 Lakh Per Annum (LPA)
*   **Average Package:** INR 28.83 LPA
*   **Median Package:** INR 26.04 LPA

These figures, as per the official placement report, underscore the value employers place on an SIBM Pune MBA degree.

## Sector-Wise Placement Trends

Recruitment spanned across a wide range of industries, showcasing the versatility of SIBM Pune graduates. The most active sectors included:

*   Automotive
*   BFSI (Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance)
*   Consulting
*   FMCG/FMCD (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods/Durables)
*   Healthcare & Pharmaceuticals
*   IT/ITES (Information Technology/IT-Enabled Services)
*   Manufacturing

The BFSI sector led in terms of the number of students hired, while Marketing roles, on average, commanded the highest salary packages.

## Roles and Domains Offered

Graduates from SIBM Pune were offered diverse roles, reflecting the comprehensive nature of their MBA curriculum. Key domains and roles included:

*   Consulting & Strategy
*   Finance
*   General Management
*   Human Resources
*   Operations & Supply Chain
*   Sales & Marketing

These roles provide ample opportunities for students to apply their management knowledge and skills in various functional areas.

## Top Recruiters at SIBM Pune

The participation of 109 recruiters highlights the institute's strong industry ties. While specific company names are often dynamic, the presence of such a large number of organizations from diverse sectors ensures a broad spectrum of career paths for students.

### Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs)

A significant number of students, specifically 98, secured Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs). These offers were a result of successful summer internships, live projects, and corporate competitions, indicating that students often convert their internship experiences into full-time employment opportunities.

## Gender Diversity in Placements

SIBM Pune also reported statistics regarding gender diversity in placements. The average salary offer for female students stood at INR 29.22 LPA, while for male candidates, it was INR 28.60 LPA, demonstrating equitable opportunities and compensation across genders.

An MBA from SIBM Pune continues to be a strong launchpad for a successful career, backed by impressive placement statistics and a wide network of recruiters.
