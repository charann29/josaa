# Intro To Computer Science With Python & Jupyter (UNIT01) by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students eager to dive into the fundamentals of computer science, this introductory course offers a practical pathway using Python and Jupyter. It's designed to build a strong foundational understanding through a self-paced, flexible learning experience.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is delivered entirely in English, making it accessible to a wide audience.
*   Learners can progress at their own speed, as it's a self-study program with video and text-based materials.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Udemy.
*   The curriculum covers core concepts from arithmetic and algebra to an introduction to integers and solving equations.

## Course Overview and Structure

This program, titled "Intro To Computer Science With Python & Jupyter (UNIT01)," is structured to introduce beginners to essential computer science principles. The learning is facilitated through a blend of video lectures and text-based explanations, allowing students to engage with the material in a format that suits them best. The self-study mode means there are no fixed schedules, offering significant flexibility.

## Fees and Certification

Enrolling in this course comes with a fee of ₹799. Upon successfully completing all course requirements, participants are eligible to receive a certificate. This certificate is provided by Udemy, signifying the completion of the foundational computer science unit.

## Detailed Syllabus

The UNIT01 syllabus is carefully crafted to introduce fundamental concepts progressively. It begins with an general introduction to the course and then moves into specific modules:

*   **Introduction:** Setting the stage for what the course will cover.
*   **LAB101 Arithmetic & Numerics:** Practical exercises in basic arithmetic and number systems.
*   **LECTURE102 Converting To Decimal:** Understanding how to convert various number systems to decimal.
*   **LAB103 Algebra & Symbolics:** Hands-on experience with algebraic concepts and symbolic representations.
*   **LECTURE104 Converting From Decimal:** Learning the process of converting numbers from decimal to other bases.
*   **LAB105 Binomials & Loops:** Exploring binomial expressions and the concept of loops in programming.
*   **LECTURE106 Adding & Subtracting:** Further lessons on arithmetic operations within a computational context.
*   **LAB107 Solving Equations:** Practical application of techniques to solve different types of equations.
*   **LECTURE108 Integers:** A dedicated module on understanding integers and their properties.
*   **LECTURE109 preTEST1A:** An assessment designed to test understanding of the concepts covered.
