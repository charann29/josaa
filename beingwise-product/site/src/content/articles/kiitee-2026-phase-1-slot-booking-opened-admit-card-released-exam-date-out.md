# KIITEE 2026: Phase 1 Slot Booking (Opened), Admit Card (Released), Exam Date (Out)

For students aspiring to join Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) in Bhubaneswar for their undergraduate or postgraduate studies, the application process for KIITEE 2026 Phase 1 is now underway. This entrance examination is conducted in multiple phases, offering various opportunities for admission.

## Key Takeaways

*   KIITEE 2026 Phase 1 registration began on November 11th.
*   The application form is available on the official KIITEE portal.
*   It's crucial to have all necessary documents ready before starting the online application.
*   The entrance exam is a pathway to numerous UG and PG programs at KIIT.

## Preparing Your Documents for Application

Before you begin filling out the KIITEE 2026 application form, ensure you have the following essential documents and information readily accessible. This will help make the process smooth and error-free:

*   A recent color passport-sized photograph (scanned, maximum 50 KB, JPG format).
*   Your signature (scanned, maximum 50 KB, JPG format).
*   An active email address and mobile phone number for communication.
*   Your Class 10th mark sheet and certificate (scanned, maximum 1 MB, JPG format).

Having these items prepared in advance will save you time and prevent any last-minute rush.

## Step-by-Step Guide to Filling the KIITEE 2026 Application Form

The application process for KIITEE 2026 is entirely online. Follow these steps carefully to ensure a successful submission:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal**: Navigate to the official KIITEE website.
2.  **Start Application**: Look for and click on the "Apply Now" or similar option.
3.  **Initial Registration**: Provide your basic details such as your name and contact information. You will also need to set a password and complete a captcha verification.
4.  **Submit Initial Details**: After entering the required information, click the "Submit" button.
5.  **Select Programs**: Choose all the academic programs you are interested in applying for at KIIT.
6.  **Confirm Selection**: Once you have made your choices, click "Submit" again, followed by "Confirm" to finalize your program selections.
7.  **Complete Application**: Proceed to fill in the remaining details. This includes personal information (like date of birth, gender, nationality, category, parents' names), academic history, and your preferred exam center location.
8.  **Review and Print**: Before final submission, carefully review all the entered information. It's highly recommended to take several printouts of your application confirmation window for your records.

### Key Personal Details Required:

*   Candidate's Full Name
*   Date of Birth
*   Gender
*   Nationality
*   Reservation Category (if applicable)
*   Father's Name
*   Mother's Name
*   Active Mobile Number
*   Valid Email ID

## Retrieving Your KIITEE Application Number

Should you forget your KIITEE application number, don't worry. The official website provides a straightforward method to retrieve it:

1.  Go to the official KIITEE website.
2.  Locate the application login section.
3.  Click on the option typically labeled 'Find Application Number of KIITEE 2026'.
4.  Enter your name, date of birth, and gender as provided during your initial registration.
5.  Click the 'Proceed' tab to retrieve your application number.

Ensure you keep your application number safe once retrieved, as it is essential for all future correspondence and accessing your admit card and results.
