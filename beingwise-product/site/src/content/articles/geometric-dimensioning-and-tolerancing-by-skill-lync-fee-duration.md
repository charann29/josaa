# Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) is a critical quality control method in manufacturing, ensuring precision in component design and assembly. This certification program from Skill Lync provides a deep dive into GD&T, equipping participants with industry-relevant skills and practical experience.

## Key Takeaways

*   This 12-week online program focuses on practical application of GD&T principles using industry-standard software.
*   It's designed for mechanical, aerospace, or automotive engineering students and graduates, requiring existing CAD knowledge.
*   The curriculum includes hands-on projects and training with Siemens NX CAD for design, modeling, and assembly.
*   Successful participants receive a completion certificate, with merit certificates for top performers.

## Course Overview

This certification program, titled "Certificate Programme On Geometric Dimensioning And Tolerancing," is meticulously crafted by industry experts, many of whom have experience with Fortune 500 companies. The course runs for 12 weeks and emphasizes a hands-on approach through real-world industrial projects. This structure ensures that participants not only acquire theoretical knowledge but also learn to apply it effectively to complex manufacturing challenges. The program aims to develop a strong understanding of best practices in drafting and design, crucial for achieving precise manufacturing of various components.

GD&T itself is a standardized system for defining the permissible variations in the size, orientation, location, and form of parts using specific symbols. Its primary purpose is to accurately define the geometry of individual parts and their assembly. Through this course, participants will receive expert-led instruction on Siemens NX CAD software, a widely used tool for assembly, designing, and drafting.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The learning experience is flexible, combining self-study with virtual classroom sessions. The content is delivered through a mix of video and text-based materials, all in English. Key offerings include:

*   Online classes with expert instructors.
*   Comprehensive video support.
*   Practical assignments to reinforce learning.
*   An industrial project to apply skills in a realistic scenario.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the "Certificate Programme On Geometric Dimensioning And Tolerancing," candidates should meet the following educational requirements:

*   Be currently pursuing or have already completed a B.E. or B.Tech degree in Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, or Automotive Engineering.
*   Possess a foundational working knowledge of CAD software.

## Fees and Certification

The total course fee for the Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing program is ₹40,000. Upon successful completion of the program, all participants will receive a completion certificate from Skill Lync. Additionally, candidates who achieve grades within the top 5% of their class will be awarded a special merit certificate, acknowledging their exceptional performance. This certification helps in creating an e-verified profile, validating the acquired skills.

## What You Will Learn

This program provides valuable insights for both design and dimensional engineers, covering essential aspects of quality control and engineering knowledge. Participants will learn to:

*   Understand manufacturing constraints from a dimensional engineering perspective, crucial for design engineers.
*   Upskill existing design professionals by mastering NX CAD for GD&T design applications.
*   Gain practical insight into when and how to apply various standard GD&T symbols for manufacturing and component reproducibility.
*   Develop hands-on expertise in designing, modeling, and assembling components using Siemens NX CAD.
*   Update their professional skill set with practical knowledge that can lead to career advancement.

This course is particularly suitable for mechanical and automotive engineering graduates aiming for a successful career in dimensional engineering, as well as aerospace engineers looking to enhance their design and manufacturing understanding.
