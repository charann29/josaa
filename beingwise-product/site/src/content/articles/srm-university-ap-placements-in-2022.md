SRM University AP, also known as SRM Andhra Pradesh, has a strong track record when it comes to student placements. The institution consistently focuses on preparing its graduates for successful careers across various industries.

## Key Takeaways

*   SRM AP maintains an impressive placement rate, typically ranging from 70% to 80% annually.
*   Even after the pandemic, placement figures have shown significant improvement across B.Tech and M.Tech programs.
*   Graduates receive job offers from leading multinational corporations.
*   Average salary packages for core engineering branches typically fall between INR 4 LPA and INR 7 LPA, with top performers securing offers above INR 12 LPA.

## Consistent Placement Success

SRM University AP has consistently demonstrated its commitment to student career development through its placement statistics. The university prides itself on achieving a high placement percentage year after year, with 70-80% of its students typically securing job offers. This trend indicates a robust system in place to connect students with employment opportunities.

## Post-Pandemic Placement Growth

Following the global health challenges, many institutions faced uncertainties in placements. However, SRM University AP managed to not only maintain but also improve its placement figures. This resilience highlights the strong industry connections and the adaptability of the university's career services department, ensuring that students continue to find valuable employment opportunities even in challenging economic climates.

## Top Recruiters and Diverse Opportunities

Graduates from SRM AP's B.Tech and M.Tech programs are sought after by a wide array of companies. Leading multinational corporations frequently recruit from the campus, offering diverse roles across various sectors. Some prominent names that have hired SRM AP students include Flipkart, Microsoft, Amazon, and Wipro. This shows the quality of education and the industry relevance of the programs offered at the university.

## Competitive Salary Packages

For students in core engineering disciplines, the average salary packages typically range from INR 4 Lakhs per annum (LPA) to INR 7 LPA. These figures are competitive and reflect the value employers place on SRM AP graduates. Furthermore, high-achieving and exceptionally skilled students have the opportunity to secure even more lucrative offers, with some packages exceeding INR 12 LPA. This demonstrates the potential for significant career growth and financial reward for top talent coming out of SRM AP.

SRM University AP remains dedicated to fostering a career-ready student body, ensuring a smooth transition from academia to the professional world.
