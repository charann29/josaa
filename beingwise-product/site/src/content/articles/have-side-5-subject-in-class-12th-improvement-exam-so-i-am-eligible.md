# BITSAT 2020 Eligibility: Understanding Improvement Exams and Past Class 12th Scores

If you're wondering whether your Class 12th improvement exam in a fifth subject, combined with having passed Class 12th in 2019, makes you eligible for BITSAT 2020, you've come to the right place. Let's break down the official requirements.

## Key Takeaways

*   Candidates who passed Class 12th in 2019 or were appearing in 2020 were generally eligible for BITSAT 2020.
*   A minimum aggregate of 75% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) or Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) was required.
*   You also needed at least 60% marks in each individual subject (Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology).
*   Proficiency in English was also a stated requirement.

## Official BITSAT Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for BITSAT, candidates must meet specific academic qualifications. These are set by the institution to ensure that students have a strong foundation in core subjects before entering their engineering or science programs.

### Academic Qualifications

Firstly, you must have successfully passed your Class 12th examination from a recognized central or state board. This ensures that your qualifying examination is from an accredited educational body.

Secondly, the core subjects are crucial. You need to have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects in your Class 12th. For students aspiring to certain programs, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology might be the relevant combination. Additionally, a good grasp of the English language is also a stated requirement, indicating its importance for the curriculum.

### Minimum Marks Required

One of the most important criteria revolves around your scores. Candidates were required to achieve an aggregate score of at least 75% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (or Physics, Chemistry, and Biology for relevant streams). Beyond the overall aggregate, there was also a minimum individual subject score: you needed to secure at least 60% marks in each of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (or Biology).

### Year of Passing Class 12th

For BITSAT 2020, the eligibility criteria regarding the year of passing Class 12th were clear. Students who had passed their Class 12th examination in the year 2019 were eligible. Those who were appearing for their Class 12th examination in 2020 were also eligible to apply.

## Your Situation: Improvement Exam in a Fifth Subject

Given that you passed your Class 12th in 2019 and took an improvement exam in a fifth subject, your eligibility primarily hinges on whether your scores in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (or Biology) meet the 75% aggregate and 60% individual subject minimums. The improvement exam in a *fifth* subject typically wouldn't affect your core PCM/PCB scores unless that fifth subject was used to calculate the aggregate or individual subject percentages for PCM/PCB, which is generally not the case for BITSAT's specific subject requirements. As long as your core PCM/PCB marks from your 2019 passing year meet the criteria, you would have been eligible for BITSAT 2020.

Always refer to the official BITSAT information brochure for the most precise and up-to-date eligibility details for any given year.
