# NIFT Entrance Exam 2026: Stage 2 PG Interview (April 06 to April 11), Situation Test Date, Stage 1 Result (OUT), & Expected Cutoff

The National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) entrance examination is a crucial step for aspiring designers and fashion professionals. The results for Stage 1 are out, and the Stage 2 interviews for postgraduate programs are currently underway.

## Key Takeaways

*   The NIFT Stage 1 results for all programs were announced on March 20, 2026.
*   Interview call letters for postgraduate (PG) candidates have been released, and interviews are scheduled from April 06 to April 11, 2026.
*   Shortlisted candidates for Bachelor of Design (B.Des) programs will proceed to the Situation Test, while PG candidates will attend personal interviews.
*   The NIFT entrance exam was held on February 08, 2026, across 102 cities in both computer-based (GAT) and paper-based (CAT) formats.

## NIFT Stage 1 Results Announced

The National Testing Agency (NTA), responsible for conducting the NIFT entrance exam, declared the Stage 1 results on March 20, 2026. Candidates can access their scores and check their qualification status by logging in to the NTA-NIFT website using their application ID and password. This result is a vital step, as it shortlists candidates who are eligible to appear for the second stage of the admission process.

## Postgraduate Interviews Underway

For those aspiring to postgraduate programs like M.Des, MFM, and MFTech, the Stage 2 interviews are a critical component. NIFT has released the interview call letters, which can be downloaded using your application ID and password. These interviews are scheduled to take place from April 06 to April 11, 2026. It is mandatory for candidates to bring their call letter and a valid photo ID proof. While not strictly required, carrying a design portfolio can be beneficial for M.Des candidates to showcase their creative abilities.

## The NIFT Selection Process: Stage 2 Overview

The NIFT entrance exam is a multi-stage process designed to assess various abilities crucial for a career in fashion and design. After the initial written examinations (GAT and CAT), shortlisted candidates proceed to Stage 2, which varies by program:

*   **For B.Des candidates:** The second stage involves a **Situation Test**. This practical test evaluates a candidate's material handling skills and innovative approach to a given scenario.
*   **For M.Des, MFM, and MFTech candidates:** The second stage is a **Personal Interview**. This round assesses communication skills, subject knowledge, career aspirations, and overall personality.

Admission to the B.F.Tech program is solely based on the GAT scores, without a Situation Test or Personal Interview component.

## Understanding the NIFT Exam Structure

The NIFT entrance exam, held on February 08, 2026, comprises two main written tests:

*   **General Ability Test (GAT):** This computer-based test is mandatory for all programs and assesses quantitative ability, communication ability, English comprehension, analytical ability, and general knowledge and current affairs.
*   **Creative Ability Test (CAT):** This paper-based test is specifically for B.Des and M.Des candidates. It evaluates intuitive ability, power of observation, concept development, and design ability.

The final merit list for most programs is prepared by assigning specific weightage to the written exams and the Stage 2 components (Situation Test or Personal Interview).
