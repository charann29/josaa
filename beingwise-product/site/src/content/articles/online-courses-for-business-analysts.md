# 19 Online Courses for Business Analysts

Embarking on a career in business analytics requires a strong grasp of how to interpret and utilise business data. Online courses offer a flexible and effective way to develop these crucial skills, whether you're just starting out or looking to enhance your existing expertise.

## Key Takeaways

*   Online courses provide a convenient path to gain essential business analysis skills.
*   Many programs offer certifications that can boost your professional profile.
*   Courses range from short introductions to comprehensive, multi-month programs.
*   Both foundational and advanced topics are covered, including data modelling, strategic analysis, and specific software tools.

## Why Pursue Online Business Analytics Courses?

Online courses in business analytics are designed to give you an in-depth understanding of how data analysis drives business decisions. Upon successful completion, many of these programs issue a certification. This credential can be a valuable addition to your resume and can be shared on professional networking platforms, showcasing your commitment and capability in the field. These courses cater to various learning paces and schedules, making them accessible to a wide audience.

## Spotlight on Select Courses

### Plan the Project as a Business Analyst

This Udemy course focuses on equipping business analysts with project management skills through a practical five-step process. It's designed to help students master the essential knowledge needed to develop a comprehensive project plan.

*   **Platform**: Udemy
*   **Duration**: 5.5 hours

### The Business Analysis Certification Program (IIBA - ECBA)

Also offered on Udemy, this program is structured into 15 distinct sections, each functioning as a separate module. This modular approach allows for flexible learning, covering various aspects of business analysis in depth to build strong skills and competencies.

*   **Platform**: Udemy
*   **Duration**: 31 hours

### Business Analytics Foundations

This foundational course from PW Skills introduces core business analytics tools and concepts. Key topics include data analysis, Excel proficiency, data extraction, cleaning, transformation, filtering, aggregation, efficient handling of large datasets, and data modelling.

*   **Platform**: PW Skills
*   **Duration**: 3 months

### Strategy for Management Consultants & Business Analysts

Available on Udemy, this course is tailored to teach strategic concepts relevant to both management consultants and business analysts. It focuses on developing a strategic mindset and applying analytical skills to solve complex business challenges.

*   **Platform**: Udemy
*   **Duration**: 6 hours

These online courses provide excellent avenues to cultivate the analytical and strategic thinking skills vital for success in the dynamic field of business analytics.
