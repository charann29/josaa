This page explores a Big Data Hadoop certification program designed to equip individuals with essential skills for the rapidly growing field of big data. It's tailored for those looking to master the big data ecosystem and become proficient in industry-standard tools.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program offers flexible learning options, including self-paced and instructor-led formats.
*   It covers key big data technologies like Hadoop and Spark, preparing students for real-world challenges.
*   Participants will gain hands-on experience through industry-based projects across various sectors.
*   The curriculum is designed to help prepare individuals for the Cloudera CCA 175 Spark and Hadoop Developer exam.

## Course Overview

This certification training is meticulously structured to introduce students to the vast world of the Big Data ecosystem. It aims to develop participants into experts in the methodologies and tools crucial for tackling everyday big data problems in the industry. The program provides a fundamental understanding of the Hadoop framework, Spark, and other big data platforms, setting the stage for a successful career in the big data sector.

Beyond theoretical knowledge, the course emphasizes practical application through industry-relevant projects. These projects draw from diverse fields such as stock markets, sentiment analysis, insurance, and e-commerce, offering valuable hands-on experience. A significant benefit of completing this program is the preparation it offers for the Cloudera CCA 175 Spark and Hadoop Developer certification exam.

## Learning Experience and Content

The program offers a comprehensive learning experience, blending various instructional methods. Key components include 52 hours of live, instructor-led training sessions and an additional 22 hours of self-paced video content, allowing for flexible study. Students benefit from 24/7 learner assistance and access to live classes taught by experienced instructors. The learning is delivered through both video and text-based materials, with classes typically scheduled on weekends.

Upon completion, participants are expected to be proficient in:

*   Performing operations on real-time streaming data efficiently.
*   Achieving near-instantaneous output from data processing.
*   Understanding functional programming, Scala fundamentals, Scala operators, REPL, Collections, and various Scala functions.
*   Developing applications using Spark.
*   Creating, pairing, processing, and performing other operations on Spark RDDs.
*   Working effectively with Spark SQL to process data and understanding its architecture.
*   Implementing DataFrame operations and processing DataFrames.

## Program Fees and Certification

The fee for the self-paced learning option is ₹21,420. Corporate training options are also available, with details provided upon inquiry. A certificate is awarded upon successful completion of the program, issued by the course provider.

## Eligibility and Certification Requirements

To be eligible for certification, students must meet specific criteria based on their chosen learning mode:

*   **Online Classroom:** Participants must attend all scheduled Big Data Hadoop Certification Training Course batches, complete one project, and pass a simulation test with a minimum score of 80%.
*   **Online Self-Paced Learning:** Students need to complete 85% of the course material, finish one project, and achieve at least 80% on a simulation test.

This course is suitable for a wide range of professionals and students, including:

*   Software Developers
*   Architects
*   Data Scientists
*   Project Managers
*   Analytics professionals seeking to enhance their skills.
*   IT professionals interested in transitioning to analytics roles.
*   Business Intelligence professionals looking to upgrade their analytical capabilities.
*   Students pursuing graduation or post-graduation in IT or Computer Science disciplines.

To enroll in the Big Data Hadoop Certification Training Course, interested candidates typically need to fill out an application form available on the official course portal.
