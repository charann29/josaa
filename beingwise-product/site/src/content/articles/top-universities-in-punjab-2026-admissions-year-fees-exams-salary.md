# Top Universities in Punjab 2026: Admissions [year], Fees, Exams, Salary

Punjab offers a vibrant educational landscape with several reputable universities, attracting students from across India. Whether you're considering a government institution or a private one, this guide will help you understand the key aspects of admissions, fees, and career prospects for the 2026 academic year.

## Key takeaways

*   Punjab is home to approximately eight universities, evenly split between government and private institutions.
*   Major entrance exams for admission include CUET, CUET-PG, PUCET, and CUCET.
*   Leading universities in the state often feature in national rankings, such as those published by NIRF.
*   Annual fees can vary significantly, ranging from less than INR 1 Lakh to over INR 5 Lakh, depending on the institution and program.
