# Developmental Psychology: A Journey of Growth within Relationships by IsraelX via Edx: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This IsraelX course, offered through the Edx platform, explores the fascinating field of developmental psychology, focusing on how relationships shape our growth from infancy into adulthood. It delves into the social, emotional, and cognitive experiences that define human development.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Coverage:** The course examines developmental processes and the profound impact of relationships throughout early childhood and how these patterns extend into adult life, influencing intimate connections, work styles, and parenting approaches.
*   **Core Academic Subject:** Developmental psychology is a fundamental subject often found in the first and second-year curricula of various academic disciplines, making this course valuable for foundational learning.
*   **Flexible Learning:** While instructor-led and scheduled over 12 weeks, the course requires a manageable commitment of 3-4 hours per week, with all content delivered in English.
*   **Certification Option:** Students can audit the course for free for a limited period or opt for a verified certificate upon completion by paying the specified fee.

## Course Overview

This program is designed to take you on an in-depth exploration of how children develop their social, emotional, and cognitive abilities. You'll gain insights into the critical role relationships play in early childhood and how these foundational interactions continue to influence our adult personalities, our most significant relationships, and even our professional and parenting styles. It's a journey from the earliest stages of life to understanding the complexities of adult behavior.

Recognized as a professional development course by the Ministry of Education, this training is highly recommended for anyone interested in child development, parenting challenges, or the broader processes of human growth. It's an introductory-level program in social sciences.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing all the modules, you will have a solid grasp of key psychological concepts, including:

*   The interplay between environmental and genetic factors in human development.
*   Understanding emotional regulation and containment during early childhood.
*   The distinct stages of cognitive, social, physical, and emotional development in young children.
*   Insights into both classic and contemporary theories of development.
*   How various developmental processes characterize children at different ages.
*   The fundamental meaning of a developmental approach to psychology.
*   The evolution and impact of gender and individual roles.
*   The influence of prenatal development and associated risk factors during pregnancy.
*   How early childhood attachment patterns can shape adult relationships.

## Who Is This Course For?

This certification program is ideal for individuals keen on understanding child development and parenting processes. It’s particularly beneficial for those in fields like clinical psychology or anyone with a personal interest in the intricate journey of human growth and the relationships that foster it.

## Course Format and Duration

This IsraelX offering is an instructor-led, scheduled program in social sciences, running for a duration of 12 weeks. Participants should dedicate approximately 3-4 hours per week to the material. The entire course is delivered in English, with English transcripts available for all video lectures, ensuring accessibility for learners.
