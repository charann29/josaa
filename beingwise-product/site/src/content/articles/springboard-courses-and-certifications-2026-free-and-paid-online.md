# Springboard Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Springboard Courses

Springboard offers a variety of online bootcamps and certification programs designed to help individuals build in-demand skills and launch new careers in technology. These programs are structured to be flexible, comprehensive, and career-focused.

## Key Takeaways

*   Springboard provides immersive, online bootcamps in fields like UI/UX Design, Software Engineering, and Data Science.
*   Many programs include a job guarantee, offering a full refund if you don't secure a relevant position within six months of graduation.
*   Students benefit from personalized mentorship, career coaching, and portfolio development support.
*   The courses are typically self-paced and can be completed online, making them accessible to a wide range of learners.

## UI/UX Design Bootcamp

This 9-month, fully online certification course focuses on equipping students with the skills needed to become a UI/UX designer. The curriculum emphasizes practical projects and assignments, helping participants build a strong portfolio that showcases their abilities. There's no specific academic background required, though a bachelor's degree in any field or strong visual and communication skills are common among successful students.

Upon completion, graduates have secured roles at leading companies such as Capital One, Ford, Boeing, Wayfair, Kaiser Permanente, and HP. The program also includes extensive career support, offering unlimited coaching for up to six months post-completion, assistance with job search strategies, interview preparation, and guidance in identifying suitable job titles and companies. For those unsure about their career path, admission counselors are available to provide guidance.

*   **Duration:** 9 months
*   **Format:** 100% online, self-paced
*   **Key Skills:** Design thinking, user research, wireframing, prototyping, usability testing.
*   **Cost:** Approximately $8,690

## Software Engineering Bootcamp: Become a Software Engineer. Guaranteed

Designed to transform individuals into proficient software engineers, this 9-month online bootcamp covers both front-end and back-end web development in depth. A standout feature of this program is its six-month job guarantee: if a graduate does not secure a job in the field within six months of completing the course, the entire tuition fee is refunded. This commitment underscores Springboard's confidence in its curriculum and career services.

Students engage with numerous learning resources, including practice exercises and career-focused coursework. A unique aspect is the one-on-one mentorship, where students work directly with a personal mentor on two full-stack capstone projects to build a professional portfolio. Additionally, dedicated career coaches assist with resume building, offer negotiation strategies, and conduct mock interviews to prepare students for the job market.

*   **Duration:** 9 months
*   **Format:** 100% online, self-paced
*   **Key Skills:** Web application development, front-end development, back-end development, data structures, algorithms.
*   **Cost:** Approximately $13,860

## Data Science Foundations to Core Bootcamp

This 7-month online bootcamp is tailored for both beginners and experienced professionals looking to establish or advance their careers in data science. The comprehensive curriculum covers essential data science skills, starting with foundational concepts and progressing to more advanced topics. Students will learn about probability, Python fundamentals, and statistics, which form the bedrock of data science.

As the course progresses, learners delve into technical units such as machine learning, data wrangling, and SQL databases. By the end of the program, participants will not only grasp basic probability concepts but also gain the ability to tackle complex business problems using various data science tools for data understanding and visualization. This program aims to provide the core competencies needed for a successful career as a data scientist.

*   **Duration:** 7 months
*   **Format:** 100% online
*   **Key Skills:** Python programming, statistics, probability, machine learning, data wrangling, SQL.
*   **Cost:** Approximately $13,900

Springboard's programs offer a structured and supported pathway for individuals aiming to enter or transition within the tech industry, backed by robust career services and, in many cases, a job guarantee.
