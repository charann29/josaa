# NMAT Syllabus 2026 (Section-wise): Download NMAT by GMAC Syllabus PDF, Important Topics

The NMAT by GMAC exam is a crucial step for many students aiming for admission to top management programs. Understanding the syllabus thoroughly is the first and most important step in your preparation journey.

## Key takeaways

*   The NMAT 2026 exam features three main sections: Language Skills, Quantitative Skills, and Logical Reasoning.
*   Each section contains 36 questions, making a total of 108 questions for the entire exam.
*   Candidates will have a total of 2 hours to complete the test.
*   Familiarity with the official NMAT syllabus and exam pattern is essential for effective preparation.

## Understanding the NMAT 2026 Syllabus

The NMAT exam structure requires candidates to answer 108 questions within a 2-hour timeframe. The Graduate Management Admission Council (GMAC) designs the question paper based on the official NMAT syllabus. Each of the three sections — Quantitative Skills, Logical Reasoning, and Language Skills — carries 36 questions. Let's delve into the specific topics covered in each section.

### Language Skills Syllabus

This section evaluates your proficiency in the English language, covering various aspects from comprehension to grammar. Key topics include:

*   Reading Comprehension passages
*   English Grammar and Vocabulary
*   English Usage
*   Fill in the Blanks
*   Synonyms and Antonyms
*   Idioms, Phrases, and Clauses
*   Sentence Error/Correction
*   Word Meaning
*   Para Jumbles
*   Statement Correction

### Quantitative Skills Syllabus

The Quantitative Skills section tests your mathematical aptitude across a range of topics. Be prepared to tackle questions from:

*   Algebra (Linear and Quadratic Equations)
*   Number Systems
*   Percentages
*   Trigonometry
*   Ratio and Proportion
*   Data Interpretation (Line graphs, Bar graphs, Pie Charts, Tables)
*   Profit and Loss
*   Arithmetic
*   Averages
*   2D and 3D Geometry and Statistics
*   Time, Speed, and Distance
*   Modern Math
*   Work and Time
*   Mixtures and Solutions
*   Simple and Compound Interest
*   Mensuration
*   Permutations and Combinations
*   Probability
*   Coordinate Geometry

### Logical Reasoning Syllabus

This section aims to assess your critical thinking, analytical, and decision-making abilities. Important topics you should focus on include:

*   Coding and Decoding
*   Numeric Grids
*   Series
*   Critical Reasoning
*   Strong and Weak Arguments
*   Calendars
*   Multi-dimensional Arrangements
*   Syllogisms
*   Puzzles
*   Statement Conclusions
*   Arrangements
*   Course of Action
*   Family Trees
*   Blood Relations

## NMAT 2026 Exam Pattern

Understanding the exam pattern is as vital as knowing the syllabus. It helps you strategize your time and approach to the test. Here's a quick overview of the NMAT 2026 exam pattern:

| Feature           | Details                                   |
| :---------------- | :---------------------------------------- |
| Exam Mode         | Online                                    |
| Test Duration     | 2 hours (120 minutes)                     |
| Total Questions   | 108 questions                             |
| Total Marks       | 360 marks                                 |
| Question Type     | Objective-type with four choices per question |
| Marking Scheme    | Three marks awarded for each correct answer |

## Preparing for NMAT 2026

With a clear understanding of the syllabus and exam pattern, you can now structure your preparation effectively. Utilize official resources, practice regularly, and focus on improving your speed and accuracy in each section. Remember, consistent effort and a strategic approach are key to performing well in the NMAT by GMAC exam.
