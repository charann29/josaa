# SU JEE 2026: Registration (Ongoing), Exam Dates, Eligibility & Syllabus

Sandip University conducts its own Joint Entrance Examination (SU JEE) for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This exam also serves as a gateway to merit-based scholarships, helping deserving students pursue their academic goals across fields like engineering, pharmacy, design, law, and sciences.

## Key takeaways

*   **Application Window:** The application process for SU JEE 2026 Phase 2 concluded on April 1, 2026.
*   **Exam Format:** The SU JEE is an online, remotely proctored examination, featuring multiple-choice questions.
*   **Flexibility:** If an emergency prevents a candidate from appearing for the scheduled exam, they have the option to reappear on a subsequent date.
*   **Scholarship Opportunity:** SU JEE is also a scholarship test, with merit scholarships offered to approximately 15% of students.

## SU JEE 2026 Exam Dates and Schedule

Understanding the key dates is crucial for candidates planning to appear for SU JEE 2026. The examination is structured into different phases, with specific timelines for application and testing.

| Event                               | Date          |
| :---------------------------------- | :------------ |
| SU JEE 2026 Phase 2 Application Last Date | April 1, 2026 |
| SU JEE 2026 Phase 2 Exam Dates      | April 4-5, 2026 |
| SU JEE 2026 Result Announcement     | April 2026    |

Candidates who successfully registered received their examination link, user ID, and password to access the online portal before their scheduled test. The exam duration is 2 hours.

## Programs Offered Through SU JEE

SU JEE serves as the entrance examination for a diverse range of programs at Sandip University. While primarily known for engineering, it also covers other significant academic streams.

### Undergraduate Programs

*   B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology)
*   Diploma Engineering
*   B.Sc. Forensic Science

### Postgraduate Programs

*   M.Tech (Master of Technology)
*   M.Sc. (e.g., Microbiology)
*   MBA (Master of Business Administration)
*   MCA (Master of Computer Applications)
*   MA (Master of Arts)

Additionally, the examination facilitates admissions for Design, Law, and other Science courses.

## Eligibility Criteria for SU JEE 2026

Before applying for SU JEE 2026, it is essential for all prospective candidates to carefully review the eligibility requirements specified by Sandip University. Meeting these conditions is mandatory for admission.

### General Eligibility Highlights

While specific criteria vary by program, typically, candidates for undergraduate programs like B.Tech and Diploma Engineering are expected to have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination from a recognized board. For postgraduate programs, a relevant bachelor's degree is generally required.

Detailed eligibility information for each specific course is available on the official Sandip University website.

## SU JEE Application Process

The application for SU JEE 2026 is an entirely online process. Interested candidates can fill out the form through the official Sandip University portal.

### Steps to Apply:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the Sandip University website.
2.  **Locate Application Link:** Find the SU JEE 2026 application link.
3.  **Fill the Form:** Provide all necessary personal, academic, and contact details accurately.
4.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of required documents as per the specifications.
5.  **Pay Application Fee:** Complete the application by paying the prescribed fee online.
6.  **Submit:** Review all entered information and submit the application form.

It is crucial to complete the application within the stipulated deadlines. Post-submission, candidates should keep a copy of their submitted form for future reference.

## Examination Pattern and Syllabus

### Exam Pattern

The SU JEE 2026 is an online, remotely proctored examination. Key aspects of the exam pattern include:

*   **Mode:** Online (Remotely Proctored Test)
*   **Type of Questions:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Duration:** 2 hours

### Syllabus

The syllabus for SU JEE largely aligns with the curriculum of qualifying examinations relevant to the chosen program. For engineering courses, this typically covers subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics from the 10+2 level. Candidates are advised to refer to the official Sandip University website for the detailed syllabus for their specific program of interest to prepare effectively.
