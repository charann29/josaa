# NIFT 2026: NIFT PG Interview (April 6 to 11), Stage 1 Result (Declared), Score Card Link and Cutoff

For design aspirants across India, the NIFT Entrance Exam is a crucial step towards a career in fashion and design. The 2026 admission process is well underway, with significant updates regarding results and upcoming interviews for postgraduate programs.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Stage 1 Results Declared:** The results for the initial written examination (GAT and CAT) for NIFT 2026 have been officially released by the National Testing Agency (NTA).
*   **PG Interviews Scheduled:** Candidates who have qualified for postgraduate programs (M.Des, M.FTech, M.F.M) will participate in personal interviews from April 6 to April 11, 2026.
*   **Scorecard Access:** Aspirants can now download their individual scorecards from the official NIFT-NTA portal using their application number and date of birth.
*   **Final Selection Weightage:** The ultimate selection for NIFT admissions will consider scores from the written exam, the Situation Test (for UG B.Des), and the Personal Interview (for PG programs).

## NIFT 2026 Stage 1 Results and Scorecard

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has announced the results for Stage 1 of the NIFT 2026 Entrance Examination. This stage included the General Ability Test (GAT) and the Creative Ability Test (CAT), conducted on February 8, 2026. Candidates can access their individual scorecards exclusively online through the official NIFT-NTA portal.

To view and download your NIFT 2026 Stage 1 scorecard, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the NIFT-NTA official portal and locate the result link.
2.  A login window will appear, prompting for your credentials.
3.  Enter your NIFT 2026 application number and your date of birth.
4.  Input the security code displayed on the screen.
5.  Upon successful login, your scorecard will be available for download.

It's important to note that the NTA does not send physical scorecards; all results must be checked and downloaded online.

## NIFT PG Interview Schedule (April 6-11, 2026)

For candidates vying for postgraduate design and fashion technology programs (M.Des, M.FTech, M.F.M), the next critical phase is the Personal Interview. These interviews are scheduled to take place from April 6 to April 11, 2026. The interview round is a significant component of the selection process, allowing the admissions committee to assess a candidate's communication skills, aptitude, and suitability for the chosen program. Qualified candidates should ensure they have all necessary documents and are prepared for their scheduled interview dates.

## Understanding the NIFT 2026 Cutoff

The NIFT cutoff represents the minimum score required for candidates to qualify for the next stage of the admission process. While specific official cutoff marks for NIFT 2026 are typically released post-results, they are influenced by several factors, including the difficulty of the examination, the total number of applicants, and the availability of seats across various NIFT campuses and programs. Aspirants should monitor the official NIFT admissions website for the release of detailed cutoff information for different categories and courses.
