# What's the College Code for G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur?

Understanding the college code is a crucial step for prospective students applying to engineering programs in Maharashtra. This code helps identify G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering (GHRCE), Nagpur, during the admissions process.

*   GHRCE, Nagpur, is an autonomous institution offering technical education.
*   The official institution code for GHRCE, Nagpur, is **4116**.
*   This code is primarily used for state-level admissions processes like MHT CET counselling.
*   GHRCE, Nagpur, is known for its strong technical programs.

## About G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur

Located in Nagpur, Maharashtra, G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering (GHRCE) is an autonomous institution dedicated to providing quality technical education. It welcomes both Indian and international students seeking to pursue various engineering disciplines. Over the years, GHRCE has established a reputation for academic excellence in the technical field.

## The Importance of the College Code

The college code, **4116**, is a unique identifier assigned to GHRCE, Nagpur. This code is essential during the centralized admission process, particularly for state-level entrance examinations and counselling procedures such as those conducted for MHT CET. When students fill out their application forms or participate in choice filling for counselling rounds, they will need to accurately enter this code to select GHRCE as one of their preferred institutions. Incorrect codes can lead to errors in the application or counselling process, so double-checking is always recommended.

## Admissions and Programs at GHRCE, Nagpur

GHRCE, Nagpur, offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs. Admission to these programs is primarily based on performance in relevant entrance examinations. For undergraduate Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) courses, scores from examinations like MHT CET or JEE Main are typically considered. The institution aims to provide a comprehensive learning environment, preparing students for successful careers in their chosen engineering fields.

## Recognitions and Accreditations

GHRCE, Nagpur, has received various accolades and recognitions for its contributions to technical education. These acknowledgements reflect the institution's commitment to maintaining high academic standards and providing a conducive environment for learning and research.
