# Emergency Medical Technician – Advanced by VIVO Healthcare: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aspiring to elevate their skills in emergency patient care, the Emergency Medical Technician – Advanced program by VIVO Healthcare offers a robust pathway. This comprehensive course is designed to equip individuals with the advanced knowledge and practical expertise required to handle a wide range of medical emergencies.

## Key takeaways

*   This is an intensive, 9-month (1200-hour) offline program focusing on advanced emergency medical care.
*   The curriculum includes extensive theoretical learning, simulations, and significant hands-on experience through hospital and ambulance internships.
*   Eligibility requires a Class 12 Science background or completion of an Emergency Medical Technician-Basic course.
*   VIVO Healthcare provides complete placement support and offers financing options to help with course fees.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Emergency Medical Technician – Advanced course is carefully structured by VIVO Healthcare to provide an in-depth understanding and practical application of advanced patient care. The program spans 9 months, totaling 1200 hours of instruction and practical training.

Out of these 1200 hours, a substantial 750 hours are dedicated to theoretical classes and simulation exercises. The remaining 450 hours are crucial for hands-on experience, involving mandatory internships within ambulances and hospitals. This balanced approach ensures students gain both foundational knowledge and real-world exposure.

The medium of instruction for the entire program is English, and it is delivered as a 100% offline learning experience. Upon successful completion, participants receive a certification approved by VIVO/NSDC.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing the Emergency Medical Technician – Advanced program, graduates will possess a comprehensive skill set in healthcare sciences. The curriculum is designed to impart knowledge and abilities in several key areas:

*   Understanding the diverse roles of Emergency Medical Services (EMS) within the broader healthcare system.
*   Demonstrating professional conduct expected of an Advanced Emergency Medical Technician (EMT-A).
*   Executing duties with a strong focus on personal safety, the safety of others, and adherence to legal, medical, and ethical standards, including working within their scope of practice under medical direction.
*   Applying principles of physiology, pathophysiology, anatomy, therapeutic communication, and lifespan development to patient assessment and management.
*   Implementing emergency medical services principles, including managing multiple casualty incidents, air medical transportation, and ambulance operations.
*   Proficiency in advanced airway management, advanced patient assessment, advanced trauma and medical interventions, advanced pharmacology, and basic cardiology.

## Eligibility and Certification

To enroll in the Emergency Medical Technician – Advanced course, candidates must meet specific criteria. Applicants should have completed their Class 12 education in the Science stream, or alternatively, have successfully finished the Emergency Medical Technician-Basic course.

Certification from VIVO Healthcare is awarded upon meeting several requirements. These include paying the full course fee, successfully passing a pre-internship examination with a minimum of 70% marks, and completing the clinical internship. Students are also required to participate in weekly and module assessments, a final foundation exam, and a pre-internship exam. A minimum attendance of 70% is necessary to be eligible for these exams, with an 85% attendance requirement for the clinical internship.

## Admission Process and Support

The admission process for the Emergency Medical Technician – Advanced course by VIVO Healthcare is straightforward. Prospective students typically begin by visiting the official course website. From there, they can select their preferred skill centre to proceed with the application.

VIVO Healthcare is committed to supporting its students throughout their journey. This includes offering comprehensive placement assistance to help graduates secure employment in the emergency medical services field. Furthermore, financial aid options are available to assist students with the course fees, making this valuable training more accessible.

This program is an excellent opportunity for individuals looking to either begin a career in emergency medical services or advance their existing profession in this critical field.
