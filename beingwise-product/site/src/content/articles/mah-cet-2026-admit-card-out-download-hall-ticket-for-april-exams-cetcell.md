The MAH CET 2026 admit card for the April examination session is now available for download. Candidates who have successfully registered for the April exams can access their hall tickets by logging into the official portal.

*   The admit card for MAH MBA CET 2026 April session has been released.
*   Candidates must download their admit card from the official MAH CET website using their registration ID and password.
*   Two printed copies of the admit card, along with a valid photo ID, are mandatory for entry to the examination center.
*   The April session exams are scheduled for April 6, 7, and 8, 2026.

## How to Download Your MAH MBA CET 2026 Admit Card

Accessing your MAH MBA CET 2026 admit card is a straightforward process through the official website. It's crucial to download and print two copies of your admit card well in advance of your exam date.

Follow these steps to download your hall ticket:

1.  Visit the official MAH CET examination portal for the AY 2026-2027.
2.  Locate and click on the specific link for the MBA/MMS exam section.
3.  Enter your unique MAH MBA CET 2026 registration ID and password in the designated fields.
4.  Navigate to the 'Admit Card' option.
5.  Your MAH MBA CET hall ticket will be displayed on the screen.
6.  Click the download button to save it to your device and then print two clear copies.

## Essential Details on Your Admit Card

Your MAH MBA CET 2026 admit card is more than just an entry pass; it contains vital information you must verify carefully. Check for the following details:

*   Your Name and Photograph
*   Your Roll Number and Application Number
*   Date of Birth
*   Category (if applicable)
*   Exam Date and Time
*   Examination Center Name and Address
*   Reporting Time
*   Important Exam Day Instructions

If you find any discrepancy in the details printed on your admit card, contact the MAH CET Cell immediately for rectification before your exam date.

## Documents Required on Exam Day

To ensure a smooth entry into the examination center, candidates must carry specific documents. Failing to present these will result in denial of entry.

*   **MAH MBA CET 2026 Admit Card:** Two printed copies of your hall ticket, preferably with a recent passport-sized photograph affixed (the same one used in your application form).
*   **Valid Photo Identity Proof:** Original (not photocopied or scanned) government-issued ID such as an Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Driving License, or Voter ID.

Digital copies of the admit card or ID proof are not accepted. The admit card will be manually checked, signed, and stamped by the center authorities, with one copy submitted at the center and one retained by the candidate.

## Important Exam Day Instructions

Adhering to the exam day guidelines is critical for a hassle-free experience. Familiarize yourself with these instructions provided on your admit card:

*   Arrive at the examination center well before the reporting time mentioned on your admit card.
*   Do not carry any electronic devices, study material, or unauthorized items into the exam hall. This includes mobile phones, calculators, smartwatches, and notes.
*   Follow all instructions given by the invigilators strictly.
*   Maintain decorum and silence throughout the examination.

Remember to review the city intimation slip that was released earlier, which provided details about your exam city to help with travel planning. The admit card specifies the exact center.

Prepare thoroughly for your MAH MBA CET 2026 exam and good luck!
