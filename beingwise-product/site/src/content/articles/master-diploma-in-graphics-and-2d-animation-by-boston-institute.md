# Master Diploma in Graphics and 2D Animation by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students eager to dive into the vibrant world of visual storytelling, the Master Diploma in Graphics and 2D Animation from the Boston Institute of Analytics (BIA) offers a comprehensive pathway. This program aims to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in the dynamic fields of graphics and animation.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 10-month master diploma program with 100 hours of instruction, culminating in a BIA certification.
*   The course is delivered through a blended learning approach, combining virtual and physical classroom sessions.
*   It includes six months of valuable on-job training, providing practical industry exposure.
*   Students from all academic backgrounds – Arts, Science, Commerce, and Engineering – are welcome to apply.

## Course Overview

The Master Diploma in Graphics and 2D Animation is a detailed program designed to provide a well-rounded education in visual design and animation. It focuses on practical skills and industry-standard software, preparing students for professional roles. The curriculum covers a broad spectrum of topics, from fundamental design principles to advanced animation techniques.

Expert instructors from the industry guide students through hands-on training using popular software like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects. The program also introduces 3D aspects with Maya and game development tools like Unity and Unreal Engine, ensuring a comprehensive skill set.

## What You Will Learn

Throughout the program, participants will gain proficiency in various areas crucial for a career in graphics and animation. The syllabus is structured to build skills progressively, starting with foundational concepts and moving to more complex applications. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Mastering essential tools and techniques in graphics and animation software.
*   Image editing and retouching using Adobe Photoshop.
*   Developing strong typography and text effects skills.
*   Designing compelling graphics and layouts.
*   Understanding animation and keyframing in Adobe After Effects.
*   Introduction to 3D modeling, texturing, and shading using Maya.
*   Learning lighting and rendering techniques for visual projects.

## Program Structure and Duration

This Master Diploma is an intensive 10-month program, comprising 100 hours of course content. Classes are primarily held on weekends, making it accessible for individuals balancing other commitments. The learning mode is flexible, offering both virtual classroom sessions and campus-based physical classroom options, supported by video and text-based delivery.

A significant feature of this program is the inclusion of six months of on-job training. This practical experience is invaluable for applying learned skills in a real-world setting and building a strong professional portfolio.

## Eligibility and How to Apply

### Eligibility Criteria

The program is open to candidates from all academic streams, including Arts, Science, Commerce, and Engineering. There are no specific prerequisites regarding prior design or animation experience, making it suitable for both beginners and professionals looking to upskill.

### Application Process

To apply for the Master Diploma in Graphics and 2D Animation, interested candidates should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official Boston Institute of Analytics website.
2.  Locate and click on the "Enquire Now" button.
3.  Complete the inquiry form with your contact details. A representative from BIA will then get in touch with you.
4.  Upon payment of the course fees, your enrollment in the program will be confirmed.

## Career Prospects

This diploma is ideal for individuals passionate about pursuing a career in the video and animation industry. Graduates will be well-prepared for various roles, including:

*   **Animator:** Creating motion for characters, objects, and visual effects.
*   **Video Game Designer:** Contributing to the visual and interactive elements of games.
*   **Multimedia Animator:** Developing animated content for various platforms.
*   **Film and Video Editor:** Assembling raw footage into a finished product.

This program provides a solid foundation to launch or advance your career in these exciting fields.
