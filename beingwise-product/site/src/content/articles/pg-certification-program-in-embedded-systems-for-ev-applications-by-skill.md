# PG Certification Program in Embedded Systems for EV Applications by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those keen on a career in the rapidly evolving electric vehicle (EV) sector, understanding embedded systems is crucial. This postgraduate certification program focuses on equipping students with the essential knowledge and practical skills needed for embedded systems applications specifically within the EV domain.

## Key Takeaways

*   A six-month online certification program designed for electrical engineering aspirants.
*   Covers 13 detailed courses on embedded systems relevant to EV applications.
*   Offers comprehensive support, including technical expert counseling, job assistance, and lifetime access to course materials.
*   Awards a PG Certification from Skill Lync, with merit certificates for top performers.

## Program Overview and Structure

This six-month online postgraduate certification program is structured to provide a deep dive into embedded systems, with a particular emphasis on their use in electric vehicle technology. The curriculum is delivered through a blend of self-study materials, virtual classroom sessions, and video/text-based content, all in English.

The program encompasses 13 distinct courses, meticulously designed to build a strong foundation and advanced understanding in this specialized field. It's ideal for individuals looking to enhance their electrical engineering skills and apply them directly to the innovative world of EVs.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is crafted to impart both theoretical knowledge and practical application skills in embedded systems. Key areas of learning include:

*   **Embedded Programming Essentials:** This foundational module covers C language programming tailored for embedded systems, including data types, operators, control statements, and function prototypes. Students will learn about the traits and architecture of embedded systems, the preference for C in embedded software development, and the use of tools like STM32CubeIDE and GCC Compiler.
*   **Core Embedded Concepts:** Expect to delve into crucial topics like understanding embedded hardware and software, input/output functions, and data representation (fixed-point vs. floating-point).
*   **Real-world Application:** The program aims to bridge theoretical knowledge with practical scenarios, ensuring participants can apply their learning to actual EV-related embedded system challenges.

## Program Offerings and Support

Beyond the core curriculum, the program provides a range of features designed to support student success:

*   **Online Learning:** The entire program is delivered online, offering flexibility for learners.
*   **Projects:** Practical projects are integrated into the curriculum to provide hands-on experience.
*   **Demo Sessions:** Prospective students can access demo sessions to understand the course content and delivery style.
*   **Job Assistance:** The program includes support for career placement, helping graduates transition into relevant roles.
*   **Lifetime Course Access:** Students benefit from indefinite access to all course materials, allowing for continuous learning and revision.
*   **Completion Certificate:** Upon successful completion, participants receive a PG Certification from Skill Lync.
*   **Dedicated Support:** Assistance is available via video, email, and telephone for any course-related queries.
*   **Paid Internship Opportunities:** The program also facilitates opportunities for paid internships, offering valuable industry exposure.

## Fees and Certification

The program fee for the PG Certification Program in Embedded Systems for EV Applications is **Rs. 2,75,000**. For those preferring installment options, an EMI plan is available at **Rs. 14,375 per month**.

Upon successful completion, students will be awarded a PG Certification Program in Embedded Systems for EV Applications by Skill Lync. Additionally, the top 5% of participants will receive an extra merit certificate, acknowledging their exceptional performance.

## Eligibility and Application Process

This program is tailored for aspiring candidates looking to build a career in electrical engineering, particularly those interested in embedded systems for EV applications. It serves as an excellent opportunity to refresh fundamental concepts and apply them to modern industrial needs.

To apply for the program, candidates should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official Skill Lync website dedicated to electrical engineering courses.
2.  Locate the PG Certification Program in Embedded Systems for EV Applications.
3.  Complete the account creation form on the portal and submit it.
4.  Proceed with logging in and depositing the program fees to finalize the admission process.

This program presents a valuable pathway for electrical engineering enthusiasts to specialize in a high-demand field, contributing to the future of electric mobility.
