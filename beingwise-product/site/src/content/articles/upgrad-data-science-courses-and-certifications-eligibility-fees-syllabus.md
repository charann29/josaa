# UpGrad Data Science Courses & Certifications - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Career Options

Data science is a rapidly expanding field, offering exciting career prospects for those with the right skills. UpGrad collaborates with various reputable universities to offer a range of online programs designed to help working professionals and aspiring data scientists build expertise.

## Key Takeaways

*   UpGrad provides diverse data science programs, from postgraduate diplomas to master's degrees, in partnership with leading institutions like the University of Arizona, IIIT Bangalore, and IU International University of Applied Sciences.
*   Programs cater to different career stages, including those seeking to upskill, transition into data science, or pursue advanced academic qualifications.
*   Learning is primarily online, featuring live sessions, hands-on projects, and industry-relevant case studies, often with opportunities for mentorship.
*   Curricula cover essential data science domains such as machine learning, deep learning, business intelligence, and data engineering.

## Master of Science in Data Science (University of Arizona)

This 24-month online Master of Science program, offered in collaboration with the University of Arizona, Tucson, is specifically designed for working professionals aiming to establish a strong career in data science. The curriculum emphasizes practical, industry-specific skills through rigorous hands-on learning, including numerous projects and case studies.

### Program Structure and Benefits

Students engage in live online sessions led by experienced data science faculty and industry leaders. This format allows for direct interaction with expert mentors, fostering a deeper understanding and skill development. Upon successful completion, graduates receive a Master of Science degree from the University of Arizona and gain alumni status with the university.

## Master Degree in Data Science (IU International University of Applied Sciences & IIIT Bangalore)

This two-year master's degree program is a unique blend of online and on-campus learning, curated in partnership with IU International University of Applied Sciences, Erfurt (Germany), and IIIT Bangalore. The first year is completed online through IIIT Bangalore, followed by the second year on campus in Germany.

### Curriculum and Specializations

With over 500 hours of learning content and 60+ projects, the syllabus covers a broad range of topics, including data toolkits, machine learning, deep learning, business intelligence, and data engineering. The program offers a customizable curriculum, allowing students to choose a specialization based on their career interests. Mentorship and personalized feedback are integral components, alongside additional benefits such as a NASSCOM certificate, doubt resolution, and expert coaching sessions.

## Executive PG Programme in Data Science (IIIT Bangalore)

For individuals with an undergraduate degree or diploma looking to enhance their data science knowledge, the Executive PG Programme in Data Science from IIIT Bangalore is a suitable option. This 12-month online postgraduate program focuses on key concepts in big data analytics, machine learning, and data science, aiming to boost career prospects.

### Key Features and Specializations

Classes are conducted through live online sessions, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience. This program, developed with NASSCOM approval, allows students to specialize in areas like Business Intelligence, Business Analytics, Deep Learning, Data Engineering, or Natural Language Processing. The curriculum includes learning 14 programming tools, taught by industry experts, with mentorship support and an optional complimentary Python Programming Bootcamp.

## Fees and Eligibility

While specific fee details for all programs can vary, the Executive PG Programme in Data Science from IIIT Bangalore is listed at ₹3,25,000. Eligibility typically requires a bachelor's degree or a diploma, often with some prior work experience, particularly for the executive and master's programs designed for working professionals. Prospective applicants should consult the official program pages for the most current and detailed information on fees, eligibility criteria, and application deadlines.

Embarking on a data science journey through these programs can open doors to numerous career opportunities in a data-driven world.
