# NID Entrance Exam 2026: B.Des Admit Card (OUT), B.Des Result (OUT), City-wise M.Des Interview Date (OUT), Expected Cutoff

The National Institute of Design (NID) Entrance Exam is a crucial step for aspiring designers in India, offering admissions to its highly sought-after Bachelor of Design (B.Des) and Master of Design (M.Des) programs. This page provides the latest updates and essential information regarding the NID Design Aptitude Test (DAT) for the 2026 admission cycle.

## Key takeaways

*   The NID DAT Prelims results for the B.Des program have been announced, and the rechecking window has now closed.
*   Admit cards for the NID DAT Mains Studio Aptitude Test for shortlisted B.Des candidates were released on April 9, 2026.
*   M.Des interviews were conducted in Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, and Gandhinagar from March 21 to April 1, 2026.
*   The NID DAT Prelims exam for both B.Des and M.Des programs took place on December 21, 2025.

## Understanding the NID DAT Exam

The NID Design Aptitude Test (DAT) is a national-level entrance examination conducted by the National Institute of Design. It serves as the gateway for admission to NID's undergraduate (B.Des) and postgraduate (M.Des) programs. The exam is designed to assess a candidate's creative abilities, problem-solving skills, visual perception, and innovative thinking.

## NID DAT 2026 Admit Card and Results

### B.Des Admit Card for Studio Aptitude Test (OUT)

For candidates who successfully cleared the NID DAT Prelims, the admit cards for the Studio Aptitude Test (Mains Exam) were released on April 9, 2026, at 4 PM. Only shortlisted candidates can access these admit cards. It's essential to carry this admit card, bearing the seal of the NID Studio Test Centre, to the Mains exam and any subsequent interview rounds.

### B.Des Prelims Result Declared (OUT)

NID has announced the results for the B.Des Prelims examination. A rechecking window was provided for candidates to review their scores, which remained open until April 4, 2026, 11:59 PM. Candidates are advised to check the official NID portal for their individual results.

## M.Des Interview Schedule and Locations

The interview phase for the M.Des program was conducted between March 21, 2026, and April 1, 2026. These interviews took place at NID campuses in Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, and Gandhinagar. The precise address for each candidate's interview centre was specified on their NID Admit Card. Candidates were required to bring the same admit card used for their Studio Test, ensuring it bore the official seal of the NID Studio Test Centre.

## NID DAT 2026 Expected Cutoff

The cutoff scores for NID DAT vary each year based on factors like the number of applicants, the difficulty level of the exam, and the available seats. While specific cutoffs are not yet released for 2026, candidates should aim for strong performances in both the Prelims and Mains stages. Historically, higher scores in the Prelims are crucial for being shortlisted for the Mains, and a good performance in the Studio Test and Interview significantly impacts final selection.
