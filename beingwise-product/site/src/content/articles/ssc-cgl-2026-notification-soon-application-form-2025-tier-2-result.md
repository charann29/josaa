# SSC CGL 2026: Notification (Soon), Application Form, 2025 Tier 2 Result (Released), Cutoff, Syllabus & News

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) is preparing to release the notification for the Combined Graduate Level (CGL) examination 2026, a crucial step for graduates aspiring to join various government ministries and departments. This page provides the latest updates, including the recently declared 2025 Tier 2 results and what to expect for the upcoming 2026 cycle.

## Key takeaways

*   The official notification for SSC CGL 2026 is expected soon on the SSC's website, ssc.gov.in, detailing vacancies, application procedures, and exam specifics.
*   The SSC CGL 2025 Tier 2 results were released on April 8, 2026, on the official portal, along with post-group-wise cutoff marks.
*   The application process for SSC CGL 2026 will commence shortly after the notification, offering candidates a specific window to apply online.
*   The Tier 1 examination for SSC CGL 2026 is tentatively scheduled for May or June 2026.

## SSC CGL 2025 Tier 2 Result and Cutoff

The Staff Selection Commission announced the results for the SSC CGL 2025 Tier 2 examination on April 8, 2026. This significant announcement included a PDF document listing roll numbers, names, parent's names, categories, and marks obtained by candidates. The Tier 2 exam took place on January 18 and 19, following the Tier 1 results released on December 18, 2025. The Tier 1 exam itself was conducted from September 12 to 26 and on October 14, 2025, for a total of 14,582 vacancies.

Alongside the results, the Commission also published the cutoff marks for various categories and post groups. This information is vital for candidates to understand their standing and the selection criteria.

### How to Check SSC CGL Result 2026?

To view your SSC CGL results, follow these steps:

*   Visit the official SSC website, ssc.gov.in.
*   Navigate to the results section and click on the SSC CGL Result PDF link.
*   Open the relevant post-wise result file that corresponds to your application.
*   Search for your roll number within the document.

It is advisable to save a copy of your result for future reference.

### Tie Resolution Policy

The Staff Selection Commission outlines its tie-resolution principles in the official notification and often within the result PDF. Candidates should review these details carefully as the Commission may update the formula for resolving ties among candidates with a simple notice.

## SSC CGL 2026 Notification and Application Form

The Staff Selection Commission is expected to release the SSC CGL 2026 notification shortly on its official website. This comprehensive PDF document will contain all critical information, including the number of vacancies across different posts and categories, the detailed application procedure, selection process, syllabus, exam pattern, and important dates.

Simultaneously with the notification, the online application process for SSC CGL 2026 will begin. Candidates will be provided with a specific timeframe to complete and submit their application forms through the official portal. In the previous cycle, 14,582 vacancies were announced, giving an indication of the scale of recruitment.

## SSC CGL 2026 Exam Dates

While the precise dates will be confirmed in the upcoming official notification, the SSC CGL 2026 Tier 1 examination is provisionally scheduled to be conducted in May or June 2026. Aspirants should regularly check the official SSC website for the confirmed schedule.

Keep an eye on the official SSC website for the latest updates and prepare diligently for the upcoming examination cycle.
