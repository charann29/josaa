# IUET 2026 Exam: Registration, Exam Dates, Eligibility, Syllabus, Pattern, Admit Card, Results

The Integral University Entrance Test (IUET) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to join various undergraduate and postgraduate programs at Integral University. This guide covers essential details for the IUET 2026, helping you prepare thoroughly for your application.

## Key takeaways

*   IUET is the primary entrance exam for admissions to many courses at Integral University.
*   Eligibility criteria vary by program, focusing on 10+2 academic background and minimum marks.
*   Certain national or state-level entrance exam qualifiers may be exempt from the IUET.
*   The application process, including registration and admit card download, is conducted online.

## Eligibility Criteria for B.Sc Programs

Meeting the specific eligibility requirements is essential for all candidates. Failure to do so will lead to disqualification from the admission process. The general criteria often include academic qualifications and minimum aggregate marks in your qualifying examination.

### B.Sc Agriculture, Horticulture, and Forestry

Candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 (or an equivalent examination) with a combination of subjects from Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM); Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB); Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology (PCMB); or Agriculture. A minimum aggregate score of 50% in the qualifying examination is required. Students who have cleared national or state-level engineering entrance exams, or entrance exams from other prominent universities, might be exempt from taking the IUET.

### B.Sc Home Science

Applicants should have passed their 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and one additional subject from Mathematics, Biology, Agriculture, or Home Science. A minimum of 50% marks in the qualifying examination is necessary. Similar to other programs, candidates with strong scores in national or state-level engineering entrance exams or prominent university entrance exams may be considered for exemption from IUET.

### B.Sc Biotechnology, Life Sciences, and Biochemistry

For these programs, candidates need to have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and either Biology or Biotechnology. A minimum aggregate of 50% marks in the qualifying examination is mandatory. Exemption from IUET may be granted to those who have qualified in recognized national or state-level engineering entrance exams or university-specific entrance exams.

### B.Sc Industrial Chemistry and Environmental Science

Prospective students must have passed their 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and either Mathematics or Biology. A minimum of 50% marks in the qualifying examination is required. Exemption criteria for national/state-level entrance exam qualifiers apply here as well.

### General B.Sc Programs

For a general B.Sc degree, candidates should have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry. A minimum aggregate of 50% marks in the qualifying examination is essential. Candidates who have excelled in national or state-level engineering entrance exams or those conducted by other prominent universities may be exempt from the IUET.

## Application Process and Important Dates

The application process for IUET 2026 will typically commence online. Prospective students are advised to regularly check the official Integral University website for the latest updates on registration dates, application deadlines, and other important announcements. Key dates for admit card release, examination, and result declaration will be published on the official portal.

## Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The IUET exam pattern generally includes multiple-choice questions (MCQs) covering subjects relevant to the chosen program. The syllabus for each B.Sc program will align with the 10+2 curriculum, focusing on Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology as applicable. It is recommended to refer to the official Integral University syllabus for detailed topics to be covered.

## Admit Card and Results

The admit card, a mandatory document for appearing in the exam, will be available for download from the official Integral University website a few weeks before the examination date. Candidates must carry a printout of their admit card along with a valid photo ID to the examination center. Results for IUET 2026 will also be published on the official university portal, along with details on counseling and admission procedures.

Preparing well for the IUET 2026 can pave the way for a bright academic future at Integral University.
