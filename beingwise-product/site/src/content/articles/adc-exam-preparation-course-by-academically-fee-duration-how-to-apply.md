# ADC Exam Preparation Course by Academically: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For Indian dentists aspiring to practice in Australia, navigating the Australian Dental Council (ADC) examination is a critical step. This page details a comprehensive preparation course designed to help internationally qualified dentists successfully clear these exams and meet Australian dental practice standards.

## Key takeaways

*   The course offers extensive training over four months, with 100 hours of live online sessions.
*   It includes AI-based mock tests, comprehensive assessments, and one-on-one feedback.
*   Participants will gain skills in clinical decision-making, patient communication, and ethical practice.
*   Scholarships offering up to 50% fee exemption are available to eligible candidates.

## Course Overview and Structure

This preparation program is specifically tailored to equip dentists with the knowledge and skills needed for the ADC examination process. The entire course runs for four months, featuring a blend of live online training and self-study resources. The instruction medium is English, and content is delivered through videos and text, alongside virtual classroom sessions.

Key features of the program include:

*   100 hours of live online training with experienced ADC trainers.
*   AI-based mock tests and comprehensive final assessments.
*   Strategies for effectively approaching the ADC exam.
*   Personalized one-on-one feedback sessions.
*   Access to recorded sessions for flexible learning.
*   Extensive study materials and a final grand test mimicking the actual ADC exam.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to enhance both clinical knowledge and practical skills essential for dentistry in Australia. Participants will develop:

*   The ability to make informed clinical decisions, crucial for both the ADC exam and daily practice.
*   Improved communication skills for explaining treatment plans and procedures to patients clearly.
*   A strong understanding and application of ethical principles in dentistry, in line with Australian standards.
*   Proficiency in dental radiography and effective management of dental emergencies.

### Comprehensive Syllabus Modules

The course covers a wide range of dental disciplines, ensuring thorough preparation across all areas assessed by the ADC. The syllabus includes:

*   Dental Emergencies
*   Endodontics
*   General Medicine (including medical emergencies and special needs dentistry)
*   Infection Prevention and Control
*   Oral Medicine and Pathology
*   Oral Surgery
*   Paediatric Dentistry and Orthodontics
*   Pain and Behaviour Management
*   Periodontics
*   Preventive Dentistry
*   Subdisciplines (assessed across multiple disciplines)
*   Radiography
*   Implants
*   Removable Prosthodontics
*   Pharmacology
*   Restorative Dentistry (including fixed prosthodontics)

## Fees and Scholarship Opportunities

It's important to distinguish between the preparation course fees and the official ADC examination fees. While specific course fees are not detailed in the source, the official ADC examination fees are structured as follows:

| Fee Type             | Amount (AUD) |
| :------------------- | :----------- |
| Initial Assessment   | 647          |
| Written Exam         | 2,122        |
| Practical Exam       | 4,775        |

**Scholarship Details:** The program offers scholarships to help alleviate the financial burden on students. These scholarships can provide financial assistance, with potential fee exemptions of up to 50% for eligible participants. More details on eligibility and application for scholarships can be found during the admission process.

## How to Apply

Admission to the ADC Exam Preparation Course is straightforward. Interested candidates need to:

1.  Visit the official course page on the Academically website.
2.  Locate and click the "Apply Now" button.
3.  Complete the required information for enrollment.
4.  Once all details are submitted, access to the course content will be provided.

This course is primarily designed for qualified dentists seeking to register and practice in Australia.
