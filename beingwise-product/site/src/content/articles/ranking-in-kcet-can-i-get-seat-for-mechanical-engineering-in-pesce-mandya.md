## Understanding KCET Ranks and Admissions

The Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) is a crucial examination for aspiring engineering students in Karnataka. Your rank in KCET determines your eligibility and position in the counselling process, which is managed by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA). Different colleges and specific engineering branches have varying cutoff ranks each year, influenced by factors like the number of applicants, seat availability, and the overall performance of students in the exam.
