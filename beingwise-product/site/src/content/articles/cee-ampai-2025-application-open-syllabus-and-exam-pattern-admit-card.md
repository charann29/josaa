# CEE AMPAI 2025: Application (Open), Syllabus & Exam Pattern, Admit Card

The Common Entrance Examination (CEE AMPAI) 2025 is now open for applications. This examination is conducted by the Association of Minority Professional Academic Institutes (AMPAI) for admissions into B.Tech and B.Pharm programs.

## Key takeaways

*   The application window for CEE AMPAI 2025 is currently open, with a deadline of July 22, 2025.
*   The exam is scheduled for July 27, 2025, offering admissions to 4-year B.Tech and B.Pharm courses.
*   Preference for admission is given to Sikh minority students, with other seats open to all eligible Indian citizens.
*   Admit cards will be available for download from July 24, 2025, on the official AMPAI website.

## Understanding CEE AMPAI

CEE AMPAI is an entrance examination organized by AMPAI, an association dedicated to professional academic institutes. The primary goal is to facilitate admissions into the first year of B.Tech and B.Pharm degree courses offered by its member institutions. While the exam is open to all eligible candidates across India, a special preference is given to Sikh minority students for admissions. Any remaining seats are then made available to students from other religious or caste backgrounds, provided they meet the set eligibility requirements.

## Important Dates for CEE AMPAI 2025

Staying informed about key dates is crucial for a smooth application process. Here's a quick overview of the CEE AMPAI 2025 schedule:

*   **Online Application Opens:** July 3, 2025
*   **Last Date for Application:** July 22, 2025
*   **Admit Card Download:** July 24, 2025
*   **CEE AMPAI 2025 Exam Date:** July 27, 2025

All applications and admit card downloads must be completed through the official AMPAI portal.

## Eligibility Criteria

Before applying for CEE AMPAI 2025, candidates must ensure they meet the specific eligibility criteria. Failing to meet these requirements will result in disqualification, even if a candidate performs well in the exam. The essential criteria include:

*   **Citizenship:** Applicants must be citizens of India.
*   **Age Limit:** Candidates must be at least 17 years old as of December 31, 2025. There is no specified upper age limit for appearing in the examination.

Further detailed eligibility guidelines are available on the official AMPAI website.

## Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The CEE AMPAI 2025 examination is designed to assess candidates' foundational knowledge relevant to engineering and pharmaceutical studies. The exam includes three main sections:

*   **Mathematics**
*   **Physics**
*   **Chemistry**
*   **Biology**

Candidates applying for B.Tech programs typically focus on Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. Those aiming for B.Pharm might also need to cover Biology, depending on the specific program requirements of the participating institutes. It's advisable to check the detailed syllabus on the official AMPAI portal to prepare effectively.

## Participating Institutions

The CEE AMPAI 2025 examination grants admission to several reputable institutions for their 4-year B.Tech and B.Pharm degree programs. These colleges are affiliated with the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), formerly known as West Bengal University of Technology (WBUT). Some of the participating institutes include:

*   JIS College of Engineering (JISCE), Kalyani, West Bengal (UGC Autonomous)
*   Narula Institute of Technology (NIT), Agarpara, Kolkata (UGC Autonomous)
*   Guru Nanak Institute of Technology (GNIT), Sodepur, Kolkata (UGC Autonomous)
*   Guru Nanak Institute of Pharmaceutical Science & Technology (GNIPST), Sodepur, Kolkata
*   Dr. Sudhir Chandra Sur Institute of Technology & Sports Complex (DSCSITSC), Dumdum, Kolkata

For a complete and updated list of participating institutions and their specific program offerings, candidates should refer to the official AMPAI information brochure.

Ensure you complete your application on time and prepare thoroughly for the examination to secure your spot in these professional programs.
