# Hi, while registering for session 1 I mistakenly chose pcm as my subject combination, however i'm aiming for pharma and only want to give pcb in bitsat. Can i change my details while registering for the 2nd attempt or during the correction window of the 2nd attempt if i do not give the first one?

It's a common concern to realize you've made an error during an important application process. If you've incorrectly selected PCM for BITSAT Session 1 but intend to pursue Pharmacy with PCB, there are indeed ways to address this.

## Key Takeaways

*   You can usually correct your subject combination during a designated correction window for the session you applied for.
*   Registering separately for BITSAT Session 2 allows you to enter the correct PCB details from scratch.
*   Always verify the specific correction rules on the official BITS Pilani admissions website.
*   Not appearing for Session 1 does not prevent you from applying correctly for Session 2.

## Understanding Correction Windows

BITS Pilani typically provides a specific window for applicants to make corrections to their submitted application forms. This is your primary opportunity to rectify mistakes like an incorrect subject combination. During this period, you might be able to change PCM to PCB, provided the exam authority's rules for that particular session permit such a significant alteration. It's crucial to regularly check the official BITSAT website for announcements regarding these correction windows and the types of fields that can be edited.

## Registering for BITSAT Session 2

If you decide not to appear for BITSAT Session 1, or even if you do, you have the option to register afresh for BITSAT Session 2. When you fill out the application for Session 2, it's a new submission, giving you the chance to accurately select PCB as your desired subject combination for your Pharmacy aspirations. This is often the most straightforward way to ensure your details are correct if you missed or were unable to use the Session 1 correction window effectively.

## Official Guidelines are Key

While these general practices are usually in place, the ability to change from PCM to PCB, especially after the initial application, is always subject to the official guidelines published by BITS Pilani. Before taking any action, make it a priority to read the detailed information on their admissions portal. Look for sections on application corrections, eligibility criteria, and instructions for subject combination selection. This will confirm exactly what changes are permissible and when.

## What if I Don't Give Session 1?

Not appearing for BITSAT Session 1 has no bearing on your ability to apply and appear for Session 2. The two sessions are often treated as independent opportunities to take the exam. If you choose not to sit for Session 1, you can still register for Session 2, pay the applicable fees, and fill in all your details, including the correct PCB subject combination, without any penalty or restriction stemming from your Session 1 application.

Ensuring your application details are accurate is vital for your BITSAT journey. By understanding the correction windows and the option to apply for Session 2, you can confidently move forward with your Pharmacy goals.
