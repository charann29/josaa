# Post Graduate Diploma in Design and Development of a Passenger Car by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For engineering students and professionals passionate about automotive design, the Post Graduate Diploma in Design and Development of a Passenger Car offers a deep dive into the intricate world of vehicle engineering. This online program, provided by Skill Lync, focuses on the core concepts and modeling techniques essential for creating modern passenger cars.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn essential engineering concepts and 3D modeling for car design.
*   Gain practical skills in industry-standard software like NX CAD and CATIA V5.
*   Access flexible fee options with varying course durations and support levels.
*   Receive a certificate of completion, with top performers earning a merit certificate.

## Course Overview

This specialized online diploma explores the comprehensive engineering principles and modeling components involved in designing a passenger car. The curriculum introduces participants to crucial industry techniques such as Benchmarking, Localization, and Cost Reduction. You'll gain valuable insights into the entire vehicle development process, including advanced 3D modeling, detailed vehicle design, and thorough analysis.

The course is delivered in English, primarily through self-study, utilizing a mix of video and text-based materials. Upon successful completion, all learners receive a certificate, and the top 5% of participants are awarded a special merit certificate.

## What You Will Learn

The program is structured to provide a robust understanding of automotive design and engineering. Key topics covered include:

*   Fundamentals of research in automotive design.
*   Advanced sketching techniques and visualization prospects.
*   Assembly modeling using NX CAD software.
*   Understanding and navigating the CATIA interface.
*   Modeling automotive plastic components and assemblies with CATIA V5.
*   Insights into advanced automotive seating systems.
*   The application and usage of automobile lighting.
*   The design process for wiring harnesses.
*   Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerancing (GD&T) principles.

## Who Is This Course For?

This diploma is ideal for a diverse group of individuals interested in the automotive sector:

*   **Engineers and Engineering Students:** Particularly those in their third or final year of an engineering degree, looking to specialize in automotive design.
*   **Aspiring Automotive Professionals:** Anyone with a keen interest in the car development process, seeking to build a strong foundation for higher studies or a career in the industry.
*   **Job Seekers in Automotive Engineering:** Individuals aiming for roles within Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs), Tier I, or Tier II suppliers in the automotive sector.
