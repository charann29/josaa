# Cloud Computing for Business Leaders by Udacity: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding cloud computing is no longer just for IT professionals; it's a vital skill for modern business leaders. This program from Udacity is designed to equip you with the strategic knowledge needed to leverage cloud technology effectively within your organization.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn to build compelling business cases for cloud adoption and manage the cultural shifts involved.
*   Gain insights into advanced cloud governance models, including FinOps, DevOps, and DevSecOps, to ensure security, cost-efficiency, and compliance.
*   Understand how to analyze cloud spending and implement effective governance strategies to optimize financial outcomes.
*   Develop the ability to design innovative business solutions using cloud capabilities and drive organizational agility.

## What the Course Covers

This certification focuses on empowering future business leaders to strategically understand and implement cloud computing. You'll explore essential topics such as creating a robust business case for moving to the cloud, managing the processes and cultural changes involved in cloud adoption, and developing strategies to overcome common challenges related to people and organizational structure. The program also delves into establishing a strong governance model for cloud environments, which is crucial for evaluating organizational policies and enhancing business agility.

The curriculum covers transitioning existing workloads and creating new ones while minimizing risks. It introduces key concepts like DevOps, FinOps, and DevSecOps, and the establishment of a Cloud Center of Excellence (CCOE). A significant portion of the course is dedicated to the governance aspects of cloud technology, emphasizing the components of a robust governance model and examining cutting-edge services offered by various cloud providers. Practical case studies are integrated to deepen your understanding.

## Program Highlights and Offerings

This program provides immediate access to its learning materials and is led by the CEO of Troposcale LLC, ensuring expert-led training. Upon successful completion, you will receive a certification from Udacity, a recognized provider of online learning.

The program offers a comprehensive learning experience, including personalized feedback on your work, real-world projects designed to apply your knowledge, and thorough project reviews. You'll also benefit from technical mentor support, access to a vibrant student community, and a flexible online learning environment. The curriculum is supplemented with quizzes, a dedicated workspace, and structured study plans to guide your progress.

## Eligibility and Certification

This program is intended for learners who possess a foundational understanding of business environments and have experience with technical or IT projects, as well as business decision-making processes. Ideal candidates should be able to identify various workloads (such as applications and business processes) within their organizations and recognize existing roles, organizational silos, skill gaps, and procedural limitations.

Participants who successfully complete the entire program will be awarded the Cloud Computing for Business Leaders Certification.
