# Best MBA Colleges in Aurangabad: Fees, Admissions 2026, Courses, Salary

Aurangabad offers a growing landscape for management education, with over 20 institutions providing MBA programs. These colleges, including both private and government-supported options, are mostly affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University (BAMU).

## Key takeaways

*   Aurangabad has more than 20 MBA colleges, offering diverse learning environments.
*   Many institutions are affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University (BAMU) and hold AICTE and NAAC accreditations.
*   Popular specialisations include Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, and Operations.
*   Admission typically requires a bachelor's degree and scores from exams like MAH CET, CAT, or XAT.

## Top MBA Colleges in Aurangabad

Several institutions stand out for their MBA offerings in Aurangabad. These colleges provide quality management education and are often recognised for their infrastructure and faculty.

Some of the prominent colleges include:

*   Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University
*   Deogiri Institute of Engineering and Management Studies
*   MGM Institute of Management and Research
*   Shreeyash College of Engineering and Technology
*   Maharashtra Institute of Technology

Most of these colleges are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and many also have accreditation from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), ensuring a certain standard of education.

## MBA Specialisations Offered

Students pursuing an MBA in Aurangabad can choose from a range of specialisations, allowing them to focus on specific career paths. The most commonly offered specialisations include:

*   **Finance:** For those interested in corporate finance, investment banking, and financial analysis.
*   **Human Resources (HR):** Focuses on talent management, organisational development, and employee relations.
*   **Marketing:** Covers areas like market research, brand management, sales, and digital marketing.
*   **Operations:** Deals with supply chain management, logistics, and optimising business processes.

## Admission Process and Eligibility for 2026

To be considered for MBA programs in Aurangabad for the 2026 academic year, candidates generally need to meet specific eligibility criteria and clear entrance examinations. The process typically involves:

### Eligibility Criteria

*   A bachelor's degree from a recognised university with a minimum aggregate score, usually around 50% (45% for reserved categories).
*   Final-year students awaiting results may also apply, provided they submit their passing certificates within the stipulated time.

### Entrance Exams Accepted

Admission to most MBA colleges in Aurangabad is based on scores from national or state-level management entrance exams. Common exams include:

*   **MAH CET:** The state-level common entrance test for Maharashtra.
*   **CAT (Common Admission Test):** A national-level entrance exam for IIMs and other top B-schools.
*   **XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test):** Accepted by XLRI and various other institutions.

Following the exam, candidates usually participate in group discussions and personal interviews conducted by the respective colleges.
