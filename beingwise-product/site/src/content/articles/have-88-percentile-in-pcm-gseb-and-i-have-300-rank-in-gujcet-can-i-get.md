*   The Home State (HS) Female quota could further enhance your chances.

## Understanding Your GUJCET Rank and Percentile

Your GUJCET rank of 300 is a significant advantage. This rank places you well within the competitive range for many engineering programs in Gujarat. While your 88th percentile in GSEB PCM is also a good score, for Nirma University admissions, the GUJCET rank often plays a more direct and crucial role, especially through the ACPC counselling process.

## Nirma University AI-ML: Historical Admission Trends

Your current GUJCET rank of 300 is better than even the first-round closing rank from the previous year, which is a very positive indicator.

## The Home State (HS) Female Quota Advantage

Being a female candidate from the home state (Gujarat) often provides an additional benefit in the admission process. Many institutions, including Nirma University, reserve a certain percentage of seats for home state candidates, and within that, there can be further reservations for female applicants. This quota typically means that the closing ranks for candidates under this category can be slightly more relaxed compared to the general open category. While specific quota cut-offs vary each year, your strong GUJCET rank combined with this quota makes your application even more robust.

## Admission Process and Next Steps

Admission to Nirma University's B.Tech programs, including AI-ML, is primarily conducted through the Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC) counselling based on GUJCET scores. You will need to register for ACPC counselling, fill in your choices, and participate in the various rounds. It's crucial to correctly list Nirma University and the AI-ML program as high preferences. Keep an eye on the official ACPC portal for counselling schedules, seat matrix details, and round-wise allotment results.

Considering your strong GUJCET rank and the benefit of the HS Female quota, you appear to have a very good chance of securing admission to Nirma University's AI-ML program. We wish you all the best for your admissions journey!
