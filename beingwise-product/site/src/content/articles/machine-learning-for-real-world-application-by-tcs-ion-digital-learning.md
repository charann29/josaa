# Machine Learning for Real-World Application by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub Packt: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program offers a deep dive into the practical side of machine learning, a crucial component of artificial intelligence. It's designed to equip learners with the skills to apply advanced machine learning techniques in real-world scenarios.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn from industry experts through a blend of digital lectures, resources, and practical assignments.
*   Gain a verifiable digital certificate from TCS iON upon successful completion.
*   The curriculum is highly relevant to current industry needs, including business case studies.
*   Potential for internship and job opportunities after finishing the course.

## Course Overview

The Machine Learning for Real-World Application certification is delivered through a self-study and virtual classroom model, utilizing video and text-based materials. The medium of instruction is English. The course focuses on understanding and applying machine learning approaches, which are fundamental to artificial intelligence technology. Learners will engage with digital learning resources, attend digital lectures, and complete industry-specific assignments. The program culminates in a summative assessment to evaluate the acquired knowledge and skills.

## Program Structure and Duration

This comprehensive program typically spans five to six months. It's structured to provide a thorough learning experience, covering essential concepts from statistics and core machine learning principles to advanced algorithms and real-life use cases. The learning journey is enriched with practice assessments and business case studies, ensuring a well-rounded understanding of the subject matter. The course offers 5 credits.

### What You Will Learn

Upon completing this certification, participants will gain expertise in:

*   **Statistics:** Including linear algebra and mathematical statistics.
*   **Machine Learning Basics:** Introduction to supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning.
*   **Machine Learning Methodology (CRISP DM):** Covering data understanding, preparation, and exploratory data analysis.
*   **Key Concepts:** Such as data sampling, model selection, regression algorithms, and model validation.
*   **Algorithms:** Practical applications of various machine learning algorithms with real-life examples.

## Eligibility and Certification

This program is particularly suitable for engineering students in their 5th, 6th, 7th, or 8th semesters, across all B.E. / B.Tech. streams. To earn the full Certificate of Achievement from TCS iON, candidates must pass a two-part summative assessment. A minimum score of 40% is required for Part A, 50% for Part B, and an overall score of at least 50 marks. Those who do not meet these criteria or miss the assessment will receive a Certificate of Participation. If a candidate fails the assessment, they will only receive a performance scorecard.

## Fees and How to Apply

The total fee for the Machine Learning for Real-World Application certification is ₹20,000, inclusive of GST. To enroll in this live course, interested candidates should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official TCS iON website.
2.  Locate and click on the "Activate Now" option for the Machine Learning course.
3.  New users will need to sign up, while existing users can sign in using their credentials.
4.  Complete the required details to finalize the application process.

This program offers a robust foundation in machine learning, preparing students for practical applications in the rapidly evolving field of artificial intelligence.
