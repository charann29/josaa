# Top Universities in Karnataka: Ranking 2026, Fees, Cutoff, Placements, Admission

Karnataka is home to a vibrant higher education landscape, offering numerous excellent universities for students pursuing various fields. This guide provides an overview of some of the top institutions in the state, including details on admissions, fees, and placements.

## Key takeaways

*   Karnataka features approximately 17 leading universities, with a balanced mix of 9 government and 8 private institutions.
*   Key entrance exams for admission include ICAR AIEEA, KCET, CUET, and IIT JAM, depending on the program and university.
*   The Indian Institute of Science (IISc) consistently holds the top position in national rankings.
*   Tuition fees vary significantly, with options available from under INR 1 lakh to over INR 5 lakh, catering to diverse budgets.

## Understanding Admissions in Karnataka Universities

Admission to top universities in Karnataka generally requires candidates to have successfully completed their Class 12 education from a recognized board. Prospective students must also appear for specific entrance examinations, which are crucial for securing a seat in their desired programs. Common entrance exams include:

*   **ICAR AIEEA:** For agricultural and allied sciences.
*   **KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test):** For engineering, pharmacy, agriculture, and other professional courses within Karnataka.
*   **CUET (Common University Entrance Test):** Increasingly adopted by central and state universities for various undergraduate programs.
*   **IIT JAM (Joint Admission Test for M.Sc.):** For postgraduate science programs, particularly for institutions like IISc.

Students should regularly check the official portals for each exam and university for the most up-to-date eligibility criteria and application deadlines.
