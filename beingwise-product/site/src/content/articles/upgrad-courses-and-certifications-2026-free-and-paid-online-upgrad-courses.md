# UpGrad Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online upGrad Courses

UpGrad is a prominent online education platform that collaborates with various universities and institutions worldwide to offer a diverse range of programs. These programs are designed for working professionals and students looking to enhance their skills or pursue higher education in a flexible, online format.

## Key takeaways

*   UpGrad offers a blend of online degrees, executive programs, and certification courses across various fields.
*   Programs often involve collaborations with international universities, providing global exposure and qualifications.
*   Many courses are structured with a mix of online learning and, in some cases, offline components for a blended experience.
*   The platform emphasizes industry-relevant curricula, hands-on projects, and mentorship from experienced professionals.

## Executive Doctor of Business Administration (EDBA) from SSBM

For professionals aiming for advanced leadership roles, the Executive Doctor of Business Administration (EDBA) program, offered in collaboration with the Swiss School of Business and Management (SSBM) Geneva, is a comprehensive option. This 36-month online course delves into multiple specializations crucial for today's global business environment.

### Program Highlights:

*   **Specializations:** Covers areas such as Machine Learning, Data Science, Human Resource Management, International Business, Leadership, Finance, Logistics and Supply Chain Management, Entrepreneurship, Energy Management, and Financial Accounting.
*   **Mentorship:** Students receive one-to-one interaction with supervisors and are mentored by international industry professionals who have advised numerous multinational firms.
*   **Curriculum:** The syllabus is developed with Swiss-quality education standards, in partnership with over 30 business entities, ensuring practical relevance.
*   **Flexibility:** Candidates have significant flexibility in choosing their dissertation topic, allowing for deep specialization in a business sector of their interest.
*   **Alumni Status:** Upon completion, participants gain alumni status with SSBM Geneva.
*   **Fees:** The program fee is ₹7,50,000.

## Masters in International Management (MIM) with IU International University of Applied Sciences & IMT Ghaziabad

This unique 24-month Master's in International Management (MIM) program is designed for individuals aspiring to build a career in global management. It adopts a blended learning approach, combining online and offline study.

### Program Structure:

*   **Part 1 (Online):** The initial phase of the course is conducted online by the Institute of Management Technology (IMT), Ghaziabad.
*   **Part 2 (Offline):** The second part requires offline study at the IU International University of Applied Sciences (IU) in Germany.

### Key Benefits:

*   **Specializations:** Students can choose between Finance and Accounting or International Marketing.
*   **Skill Development:** The curriculum focuses on developing a strong understanding of management and leadership skills relevant to the international business landscape.
*   **Career Prospects:** The program aims to open up career opportunities in Marketing, Business Development, and Management Counselling, with a high likelihood of job placement within six months of graduating from IU, Germany.

## Master of Science in Data Science from The University of Arizona

The Master of Science in Data Science is a 24-month, fully online specialized course tailored for working professionals aiming to establish a successful career in the rapidly growing field of data science. This program is offered in collaboration with The University of Arizona, Tucson.

### Learning Experience:

*   **Curriculum:** Features an industry-specific curriculum taught by experienced leaders through a rigorous, hands-on learning approach.
*   **Practical Application:** Includes extensive industry-relevant projects and case studies to ensure practical skill development.
*   **Mentorship:** Conducted through live sessions by leading data science faculty and industry experts, offering opportunities for direct interaction with mentors.
*   **Outcome:** Successful completion leads to a degree from The University of Arizona, along with alumni status.

## Master Degree in Data Science with IU International University of Applied Sciences & IIIT Bangalore

Another option for aspiring data scientists is the Master Degree in Data Science, a two-year program curated by a reputable German university. This program, offered in collaboration with IU International University of Applied Sciences and IIIT Bangalore, provides a robust foundation in data science principles and applications.
