# The Complete Financial Analyst Course by UDEMY: Fee, Review, Duration

For anyone aspiring to build a strong foundation in finance, this course aims to equip you with the practical skills needed to excel as a financial analyst. It covers essential areas from accounting fundamentals to advanced financial modeling.

## Key takeaways

*   This online program offers a comprehensive curriculum, spanning 21 hours of video content.
*   It focuses on practical application, including extensive use of Microsoft Excel for financial tasks.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate, validating their newly acquired skills.
*   The course provides lifetime access to materials and a 30-day money-back guarantee, ensuring flexibility and confidence for learners.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Complete Financial Analyst Course is designed for individuals seeking to master the core competencies of financial analysis. It's an online, self-paced program with an estimated duration of 21 hours. The difficulty level is categorized as intermediate, making it suitable for those with some basic understanding or a strong desire to learn finance principles.

The curriculum is structured to progressively build skills, starting with a robust introduction to Microsoft Excel, then moving into accounting principles, financial statement analysis, and ultimately, financial modeling and valuation techniques. The emphasis is on hands-on learning, ensuring that students can apply what they learn in real-world scenarios.

## Learning Outcomes and Skills Acquired

This course is crafted to empower learners with a wide array of practical skills crucial for a successful career in finance. You will learn to work proficiently with Microsoft Excel, mastering professional spreadsheet formatting, data manipulation, and chart creation. Key accounting and bookkeeping principles are covered, enabling you to construct profit & loss statements and balance sheets from the ground up.

Furthermore, the course delves into financial statement analysis, teaching you to calculate and interpret liquidity, solvency, profitability, and growth ratios. You'll gain an understanding of 10-K reports, capital budgeting techniques like Net Present Value (NPV), loan schedule calculations, and industry analysis. A significant portion is dedicated to understanding company valuation drivers and creating professional presentations using Microsoft PowerPoint, essential for communicating financial insights effectively.

## Who Should Consider This Course?

This program is ideal for individuals who are ambitious and eager to accelerate their learning in financial analysis. It's particularly well-suited for:

*   Anyone aiming for a career in finance and looking to acquire practical, job-ready skills.
*   Professionals who want to enhance their existing financial knowledge and become more proficient in financial modeling and analysis.
*   Students or recent graduates who wish to supplement their academic learning with industry-relevant application.

## Fee and Features

The total fee for The Complete Financial Analyst Course is ₹599, making it an accessible option for many. Beyond the core content, the course offers several valuable features:

*   **Certificate of Completion:** A credential to showcase your new skills.
*   **Lifetime Access:** Once purchased, you have unlimited access to all course materials.
*   **Downloadable Resources:** Includes 541 downloadable resources to aid your learning.
*   **Device Flexibility:** Access the course content on mobile devices and TV.
*   **Money-Back Guarantee:** A 30-day money-back policy provides assurance for new learners.

This course offers a practical pathway to understanding the intricacies of financial analysis, designed to help you become a confident and capable professional in the finance sector.
