# Imarticus Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Imarticus Courses

Imarticus Learning offers a variety of online courses and certifications designed to help students and professionals enhance their skills in high-demand fields like data science, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. These programs often feature collaborations with leading academic institutions and industry experts.

## Key Takeaways

*   Imarticus provides specialized online courses, many developed in partnership with renowned institutions like IIM Lucknow, IIT Guwahati, KPMG, and UCLA.
*   Programs cover cutting-edge areas such as Data Science, AI for Business, and Software Engineering for Cloud, Blockchain, and IoT.
*   Course durations typically range from 8 to 9 months, offering both part-time and full-time study options.
*   Many certifications include practical elements like capstone projects, real-world case studies, and industry mentorship.

## Specialized Programs with Academic and Industry Leaders

Imarticus Learning distinguishes itself by partnering with top-tier educational institutions and industry giants to deliver its certification programs. These collaborations ensure that the curriculum is current, relevant, and recognized by employers. For instance, the **Data Science Prodegree** is developed with KPMG, incorporating their industry expertise into capstone projects and practical case studies. Similarly, programs in software engineering benefit from the academic rigor of institutions like IIT Guwahati.

## Exploring Key Certification Offerings

Let's look at some prominent certification courses available through Imarticus:

### Data Science Prodegree

This 9-month program, offered in collaboration with KPMG, delves deep into data science techniques and tools. It introduces learners to essential analytical tools such as R, Python, SQL, and Tableau, alongside foundational data science concepts. Students receive project mentorship from KPMG industry experts and can choose between part-time or full-time study. Upon successful completion, participants earn a certificate.

**Key Skills Covered:** Programming skills, Machine Learning, Data Science knowledge, Python, SQL, Data Visualization.

**Program Duration:** 9 Months

**Program Fee:** ₹58,000

### Certification in Software Engineering for Cloud, Blockchain, and IoT

Designed by experts from E&ICT Academy, IIT Guwahati, and Imarticus Learning, this 9-month course focuses on the tools and techniques vital for modern software engineers. It includes a 3-day campus immersion experience and covers the fundamentals of cloud computing, IoT, and blockchain through real-world business scenarios. Successful completion leads to a certificate.

**Key Skills Covered:** Engineering knowledge, IT industry knowledge, Blockchain, Big Data.

**Program Duration:** 9 Months

**Program Fee:** ₹1,99,999

### Executive Programme in AI for Business

This 9-month executive program, offered in partnership with IIM Lucknow, is tailored for individuals aspiring to leadership roles in AI within the business sector. It explores the application of AI in business through six industry projects and covers machine learning and deep learning algorithms. The curriculum also details Google Cloud, Azure, and AWS. Participants receive an industry-recognized certificate from IIM Lucknow upon completion.

**Program Duration:** 9 Months

**Program Fee:** ₹3,18,300

### Post Graduate Program in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

An 8-month program administered by experts from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), this course provides a comprehensive understanding of data science and artificial intelligence tools and techniques. It begins with foundational concepts in statistics and Python programming, delivered through live online sessions and exercises. Upon completion, learners receive a Post Graduate certificate.

**Program Duration:** 8 Months
