# Executive Programme in Generative AI for Leaders by upGrad IIIT Bangalore: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For business leaders aiming to integrate cutting-edge AI into their organisations, the Executive Programme in Generative AI for Leaders, offered through a collaboration between upGrad, IIIT Bangalore, and Microsoft, provides a comprehensive learning experience. This programme is designed to equip senior professionals with the strategic frameworks and practical knowledge needed to spearhead AI transformation.

## Key Takeaways

*   A five-month programme focused on applying Generative AI strategically in business contexts.
*   Participants earn dual certification from IIIT Bangalore and Microsoft, alongside an NSDC certification.
*   Includes hands-on capstone projects, expert mentorship, and immersive visits to Microsoft facilities.
*   Designed for experienced professionals, typically with over four years of work experience and a bachelor's degree.

## Programme Overview

This executive programme is tailored for business leaders, functional heads, and mid-to-senior professionals who want to lead AI initiatives within their organisations. It goes beyond theoretical concepts, focusing on practical application of Generative AI, including understanding domain knowledge, strategic frameworks, and direct exposure to these advanced technologies. The curriculum is built around the A.D.A.P.T. framework, which helps participants operationalise Generative AI effectively in real-world business environments.

## Duration and Learning Experience

The programme spans approximately five months, offering a blend of self-study and virtual classroom sessions. The learning content is delivered through video and text-based modules, complemented by over 25 live sessions. A significant part of the experience includes domain workshops and immersive on-site exposure, providing a robust and interactive learning journey. Participants will also engage in real-world capstone projects, receive personalised mentorship, and have opportunities to network with industry leaders.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for this Executive Programme, candidates generally need to hold at least a Bachelor's or Master's degree, or an equivalent qualification. The programme is primarily designed for experienced professionals, with a preference for applicants who have typically accumulated four or more years of professional work experience. This ensures that participants can bring their existing business acumen to the advanced concepts taught in the programme.

## Programme Fees

The total fee for the Executive Programme in Generative AI for Leaders is ₹2,25,000, which includes GST. For those who prefer a staggered payment approach, EMI options are available, with monthly payments starting from ₹7,583. This fee covers all programme components, including access to learning materials, live sessions, workshops, and certification upon successful completion.

| Fee Component | Amount |
| :------------------ | :------------- |
| Programme Fees | ₹2,25,000 |
| EMI (per month) | ₹7,583 |

## How to Apply

Prospective participants interested in enrolling in the Executive Programme in Generative AI for Leaders can follow a straightforward application process. The first step involves visiting the official programme webpage. Once on the page, applicants should locate and click the 'Apply Now' button, usually positioned next to the preferred pricing plan. This will lead to an application form that needs to be completed with the required personal and professional details.

This programme offers a unique opportunity for leaders to master Generative AI and drive significant innovation within their enterprises.
