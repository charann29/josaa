# Certificate in Journalism, PR, Image Strategisation and Brand Custodianship Program by Indian Institute of Creative Skills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This professional certification program from the Indian Institute of Creative Skills (IICS) is designed for individuals eager to forge a career in the dynamic fields of journalism, public relations, and digital content. It aims to equip students with the skills needed for modern media roles.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a two-year certification program offered by the Indian Institute of Creative Skills.
*   The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, from traditional journalism and PR to digital marketing, content creation, and podcasting.
*   The program is certified by both the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and the Media & Entertainment Skills Council (MESC).
*   Participants will build a professional digital portfolio, including a functioning podcast and social media profiles.

## Program Overview and Structure

The Certificate in Journalism, PR, Image Strategisation and Brand Custodianship is a 24-month program delivered through a blend of video and text-based modules. It is taught entirely in English. The program is specifically tailored for those aspiring to work as digital marketers, content creators, social media influencers, or podcasters. It provides in-depth training across essential areas such as Search Engine Optimization (SEO), public relations, content marketing, paid advertising strategies, social media growth tactics, and podcast production.

A key outcome of the program is the development of a strong professional digital portfolio, which is intended to help graduates launch and advance their careers in the digital media landscape.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of various facets of modern media and communication. You will gain expertise in:

*   **Digital Marketing Skills:** This includes strategies for SEO, content marketing, and managing paid advertising campaigns.
*   **Content Management Skills:** Learn to create engaging and effective content for diverse digital platforms.
*   **Social Media Skills:** Develop proficiency in managing and growing social media accounts.
*   **Public Relations Skills:** Understand techniques for effective public relations in both traditional and digital contexts.
*   **Brand Management:** Acquire the knowledge to strategize, build, and maintain a strong brand presence.
*   **Podcast Production:** Learn the process of creating compelling podcasts from conceptualization to distribution.

### Semester-wise Syllabus Highlights:

*   **Semester 1: Foundations of Media & Communication**
    *   Fundamentals of Communication
    *   Journalism Fundamentals
    *   Introduction to Public Relations
    *   Introduction to Social Media

*   **Semester 2: Digital Media & Social Media Marketing**
    *   Digital Media Marketing
    *   Digital Storytelling
    *   Public Relations in the Digital Age
    *   Introduction to Artificial Intelligence in Media
    *   Elective Courses: Social Media Analytics & Measurement, Social Media for Business

*   **Semester 3: Specialization** (Further details typically provided by the institute)

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for this certificate program, applicants must meet the following requirements:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Candidates must have successfully completed their 12th grade from a recognized school board.
*   **Age Limit:** Applicants should be at least 18 years old and not older than 40 years at the time of application.
*   **Creative Portfolio:** A collection of previous creative work must be submitted to demonstrate the applicant's creative abilities and potential.

## Fees and Application Process

### Course Fees

The fees for the Certificate in Journalism, PR, Image Strategisation and Brand Custodianship Program are structured as follows:

| Fee Component   | Amount     |
| :-------------- | :--------- |

### How to Apply

The admission process for the program is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official website of the Indian Institute of Creative Skills.
2.  Locate and click on the "Admission Now" tab.
3.  Fill out the application form with all required details.
4.  Submit the completed form along with the necessary fees.
5.  Upon successful submission of the form and fees, you will be able to join the course.
