# Best Law Colleges in Hyderabad 2026: Ranking, Fees, Courses, Admission, Placements

Planning to study law in Hyderabad? The city is home to a highly-regarded institution for legal education. This guide provides essential details on its rankings, courses, admission process, fees, and placement opportunities.

## Popular Law Courses and Specializations

Law programs are available at various levels, including undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG), and doctoral (PhD). While traditional specializations like Criminal and Civil Law remain popular, newer fields such as Cyber Law, Patent Law, and Corporate Law are also gaining significant traction. Students can pursue integrated five-year programs after their 12th standard or a three-year LLB after graduation, followed by an LLM or PhD if desired.

## Admission Process and Eligibility for 2025

Admission to law programs in Hyderabad primarily depends on entrance examination scores and fulfilling specific eligibility criteria. The main entrance exams accepted for leading law colleges include the Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) and the Telangana State Law Common Entrance Test (TS LAWCET).

### Eligibility Criteria:

*   **For UG Law Courses (e.g., BA LLB, BBA LLB):** Candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 (Higher Secondary School) or an equivalent examination from a recognized board, generally with a minimum aggregate score of 45%.
*   **For PG Law Courses (e.g., LLM):** Applicants typically need to hold an LLB degree or an equivalent qualification from a recognized university.

*This information is based on official admission guidelines and may vary slightly.*
