# TSBIE Board Exams 2026: Result, Dates, Time Table, Hall Ticket, Syllabus

For students in Telangana, the TSBIE intermediate examinations are a significant milestone. This guide provides essential information for the 2026 board exams, covering key dates, results, and preparation resources.

## Key takeaways

*   The Telangana State Board of Intermediate Education (TSBIE) conducted the 2026 intermediate exams from February 25 to March 18, 2026.
*   Results for the TS Intermediate exams are anticipated in the second week of April 2026.
*   Over 9.97 lakh students participated in the TS Intermediate 2026 exams.
*   The official syllabus and exam pattern are released by TSBIE to aid student preparation.

## TSBIE Result 2026

The TSBIE is expected to announce the TS Intermediate results for 2026 in the second week of April. Students will be able to access their results on the official TSBIE website using their hall ticket number. The online result will be provisional, detailing the student's roll number, name, marks obtained, and grades. Original mark sheets will be distributed by the schools at a later date.

### Steps to check TS Intermediate 2026 result:

1.  Visit the official results portal: results.cgg.gov.in.
2.  Select the link for either 1st-year or 2nd-year results.
3.  Enter your hall ticket number accurately.
4.  Click on the "Get Result" button to view your scores.

### TS Intermediate 2026 Grading System

| Marks Obtained | Grade |
| :------------- | :---- |
| 100% to 75%    | A     |
| 75% to 60%     | B     |
| 60% to 50%     | C     |
| 50% to 35%     | D     |

## About the Telangana Intermediate Board

The Telangana State Board of Intermediate Education, now officially known as TGBIE, is the primary body responsible for regulating and conducting intermediate-level education and examinations across Telangana. The board conducts these pen-and-paper examinations annually, typically in March.

Beyond conducting exams, TSBIE also plays a crucial role in providing educational resources. It publishes the official syllabus, exam patterns, question banks, model test papers, and previous years' question papers. These materials are invaluable for students to understand the marking scheme, question types, and difficulty levels they can expect in the actual examinations.

## TSBIE Time Table 2026

The official timetable for the TS Intermediate 2026 examinations was released by TSBIE on December 22, 2025. The examinations for both 1st and 2nd-year students were held in offline mode between February 25 and March 18, 2026. The timetable specifies the exact dates and days for each subject, along with important instructions for candidates.

## TSBIE Syllabus 2026

TSBIE has published the comprehensive syllabus for the 2026 intermediate examinations on its official website, tsbie.cgg.gov.in. The syllabus covers all subjects for Arts, Commerce, and Science streams for both 1st and 2nd years. It outlines the important topics, exam pattern, and distribution of marks for each subject. All questions in the board exams are derived directly from this prescribed syllabus, making it an essential resource for effective study planning.
