# Advanced Aerodynamic Simulations using CONVERGE by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program offers a comprehensive pathway for students and professionals to master advanced aerodynamic simulations using CONVERGE CFD software. It's designed to equip you with practical skills, from fundamental concepts to complex real-world applications.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn entirely online through a blend of self-study, virtual classes, and video/text-based content.
*   The course spans 12 weeks, providing in-depth training and practical experience.
*   Gain expertise in setting up and analyzing complex simulations for various aerodynamic scenarios.
*   Receive dedicated technical support and a certificate upon successful completion.

## Course Overview and What You Will Learn

This 12-week online course is ideal for anyone looking to build strong skills in setting up and executing advanced aerodynamic simulations using CONVERGE CFD. You'll progress from understanding basic applications, like flow through a simple pipe, to visualizing intricate flow patterns around objects such as an Ahmed body or an FSAE car.

The curriculum is structured to provide hands-on experience, challenging you with projects and exercises. You will learn to leverage CONVERGE CFD to achieve desired simulation results and use Paraview for post-processing and visualizing these outcomes. The program also touches upon the working principles of engines, allowing you to apply Computational Fluid Dynamics to diverse, real-life engineering challenges.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is particularly beneficial for:

*   Students currently pursuing degrees in Mechanical, Aerospace, or Automotive Engineering.
*   Undergraduate students keen to develop expertise in aerodynamics and simulation from the ground up.
*   Aspiring Aerospace Engineers, Mechanical Engineers, and Automation Test Engineers looking to enhance their skill set.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in this advanced simulation course, candidates must hold a B.E/B.Tech degree in Mechanical, Aerospace, or Automotive Engineering. A foundational understanding of CONVERGE CFD is also required to make the most of the program content.

Upon completion, all participants will receive a course completion certificate. Furthermore, candidates who perform exceptionally well and rank in the top 5% of their class will be awarded a certificate of merit.

## Program Offerings and Fees

The program is delivered entirely online and includes a range of features designed to support your learning journey:

*   **Industry-oriented projects:** Apply your knowledge to practical scenarios.
*   **Course videos:** Access comprehensive video lessons.
*   **Technical support:** Get assistance with your doubts and technical queries.
*   **Weekly assignments:** Reinforce your learning with regular practice.
