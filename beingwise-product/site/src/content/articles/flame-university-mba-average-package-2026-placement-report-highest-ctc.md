# FLAME University MBA Average Package 2026: Placement Report, Highest CTC & Top Companies

FLAME University has released its MBA Placement Report for the 2025-26 academic year, providing a clear picture of career opportunities and compensation for its graduates. This report highlights strong hiring across various sectors for both the MBA and MBA (Communication Management) programs.

## Key Takeaways

*   The average package for the MBA program reached INR 10.3 LPA, while the MBA (Communication Management) program saw an average of INR 9.8 LPA.
*   The highest salary offered across both programs was INR 19 LPA.
*   A significant 85% of MBA students and 79% of MBA (Communication Management) students secured placements.
*   Top industries for recruitment included BFSI, advertising, IT/ITES, retail, and consulting.

## FLAME University MBA Placement Statistics 2026

The placement report for the 2025-26 batch showcases impressive outcomes for FLAME University's MBA programs. Approximately 46% of MBA students and 56% of MBA (Communication Management) students received job offers from a diverse range of companies. The average package for the MBA program was INR 10.3 LPA, which is higher than the previous year, and for MBA (Communication Management), it was INR 9.8 LPA.
