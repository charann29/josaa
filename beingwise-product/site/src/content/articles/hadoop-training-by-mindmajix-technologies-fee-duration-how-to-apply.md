# Hadoop Training by Mindmajix Technologies: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Mindmajix Technologies offers an online certification program in Hadoop Training, designed to equip individuals with a thorough understanding of Big Data and Hadoop tools, from foundational concepts to advanced methodologies. This program aims to provide practical skills for professionals looking to excel in the field of big data analytics.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course covers a comprehensive range of Big Data and Hadoop topics, including architecture, MapReduce, and various analytical tools like Hive, Pig, and Apache Spark.
*   Participants can choose between self-paced video learning or live online classes, offering flexibility to suit different learning preferences.
*   The program includes hands-on experience through two real-world, industry-based projects, ensuring practical application of learned concepts.
*   Upon completion, trainees receive a certificate from Mindmajix Technologies, along with lifetime access to self-paced videos and 24/7 support.

## Course Structure and Learning Options

The Hadoop Training program is structured to provide a deep dive into Big Data and Hadoop. It offers two primary learning modes:

1.  **Self-Paced Videos:** This option allows learners to progress at their own speed using pre-recorded HD video lectures and e-learning materials.
2.  **Live Online Classes:** For those who prefer interactive learning, live sessions are conducted by an instructor via platforms like Zoom or Google Meet, fostering real-time engagement and doubt resolution.

The curriculum encompasses approximately 40 hours of detailed video instruction, covering essential topics such as Hadoop architecture, the MapReduce framework, and a suite of analytical tools including Hive, Pig, Sqoop, Flume, MongoDB, and Apache Spark.

## Hands-on Experience and Project Work

A crucial aspect of this training is the emphasis on practical application. Participants will engage in two real-time, industry-based projects. These projects are designed to provide hands-on experience, allowing learners to apply their knowledge of Hadoop and Big Data tools in scenarios that mirror actual industry challenges. This practical component is vital for building confidence and demonstrating proficiency to potential employers.

## Program Highlights and Support

Mindmajix Technologies includes several beneficial features to enhance the learning experience:

*   **Certificate of Completion:** Awarded by Mindmajix Technologies upon successful course completion.
*   **Certification-Oriented Curriculum:** The course content is specifically designed to align with industry certification requirements.
*   **Lifetime Self-Paced Video Access:** Learners retain access to course videos indefinitely, allowing for review and refreshers.
*   **Flexible Schedule:** Both learning modes offer flexibility to accommodate busy schedules.
*   **Quizzes & Mock Tests:** Regular assessments help gauge understanding and prepare for certification.
*   **Free Demo:** Prospective students can request a free demo to experience the course content and teaching style.
*   **One-on-One Doubt Resolution:** Personalized support is available to address individual queries.
*   **24/7 Lifetime Assistance:** Continuous support is provided to all learners.

## Who Can Benefit?

This Hadoop Training program is particularly well-suited for professionals in various data-related roles, including:

*   Data Analysts
*   Data Scientists
*   Big Data Analytics Engineers

Individuals aspiring to enter or advance in the field of big data and analytics will find this course highly beneficial for developing a strong foundation and practical skills.

## Course Syllabus Overview

The comprehensive syllabus covers fundamental to advanced concepts:

### Understanding Big Data and Hadoop

*   Introduction to Big Data and its limitations
*   Overview of Hadoop features and ecosystem
*   Hadoop 2.x core components: HDFS (storage) and MapReduce (processing)
*   Different Hadoop distributions and cluster modes

### YARN (Yet Another Resource Negotiator)

*   Understanding YARN as the next-generation MapReduce
*   YARN architecture: Resource Manager, Application Master, Node Manager

### Hadoop Architecture and HDFS

*   Hadoop 2.x cluster architecture, including federation and high availability
*   Setting up single-node and multi-node Hadoop clusters
*   Common Hadoop shell commands and administration

### Hadoop MapReduce Frameworks

*   MapReduce use cases and its architecture in Hadoop 2.x
*   Components and execution flow of YARN MapReduce applications
*   Concepts like input splits, combiners, and partitioners

### Pig

*   Introduction to Pig, its use cases, and comparison with MapReduce
*   Programming structure, running modes, and components of Pig
*   Pig Latin programs, data models, and data types

This training provides a robust pathway for individuals to gain expertise in the rapidly evolving domain of Big Data with Hadoop.
