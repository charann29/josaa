# MAH MCA CET 2026: Provisional Answer Key (Soon), Result, Cut off and Counselling here

## What is the MAH MCA CET Exam?

The Maharashtra MCA Common Entrance Test, or MAH MCA CET, is a state-level entrance examination designed for admissions into Master of Computer Applications (MCA) programs offered by various institutions across Maharashtra. Conducted annually by the State Common Entrance Test (CET) Cell, Maharashtra, this exam is a prerequisite for candidates seeking to enrol in MCA courses within the state. It is administered as a computer-based test (CBT) at designated centres.

### Participating Colleges in MAH MCA CET

Numerous colleges and institutes in Maharashtra accept MAH MCA CET scores for their MCA programs. Some of the well-regarded institutions include:

*   Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI), Mumbai
*   Sardar Patel Institute of Technology (SPIT), Mumbai
*   College of Engineering Pune (COEP)
*   Colleges affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU)
