# CMAT Exam Date 2026: Registration, Admit Card, & Result Dates

The Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is a national-level entrance examination for admission to various management programs across India. Understanding the key dates for CMAT 2026 is crucial for all aspiring candidates to plan their application process effectively.

## Key takeaways

*   The CMAT 2026 examination was conducted on January 25, 2026, across numerous cities in India.
*   Application forms for CMAT 2026 were submitted online via the official NTA CMAT website.
*   The CMAT 2026 admit card became available for download on January 21, 2026.
*   Candidates could access their CMAT 2026 results and scorecards starting February 17, 2026.

## CMAT 2026 Registration Details

For CMAT 2026, candidates needed to complete their application process on the official NTA CMAT website. The application fee was INR 2,500, with a reduced fee of INR 1,250 for reserved categories, women, and transgender candidates. After the registration window closed, a correction facility was provided, allowing candidates to make necessary edits, including changing their preferred test city.

## CMAT 2026 Exam Schedule

The CMAT 2026 exam took place on January 25, 2026. The test was administered in two separate sessions. Each session comprised 100 multiple-choice questions, divided equally across five sections: Quantitative Techniques & Data Interpretation, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, General Awareness, and Innovation and Entrepreneurship. Each question carried 4 marks, and there was a total duration of 180 minutes for the exam.

Here's a quick overview of the key dates for CMAT 2026:

| Event                          | Date              |
| :----------------------------- | :---------------- |
| CMAT 2026 Exam                 | January 25, 2026  |
| Answer Key Objection Window    | Jan 31 - Feb 2, 2026 |
| CMAT 2026 Result Declaration   | February 17, 2026 |

## CMAT 2026 Admit Card Release

The CMAT 2026 admit cards were released online on January 21, 2026. Candidates were required to download their admit cards from the official NTA CMAT website using their login credentials. It's important for candidates to retain their admit card, as it may be needed during the admission process to management institutes.

## CMAT 2026 Answer Key and Objections

The National Testing Agency (NTA) released the preliminary answer key for CMAT 2026 on January 31, 2026. Along with the answer key, candidates could also download their individual response sheets. A window for raising objections against the official answer key was open from January 31 to February 2, 2026. If any objections were found to be valid, NTA would then release a revised final answer key.

## CMAT 2026 Result Declaration

The results for CMAT 2026 were announced on February 17, 2026. Candidates could check their scores and download their official scorecards from the cmat.nta.nic.in website by logging in with their credentials. The scorecard provided both sectional and overall percentiles, which are crucial for applying to colleges that accept CMAT scores for admission.

Staying informed about these critical dates ensures a smooth application journey for your MBA aspirations.
