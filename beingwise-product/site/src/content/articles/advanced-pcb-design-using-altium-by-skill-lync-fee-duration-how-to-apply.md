# Advanced PCB Design Using Altium by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are fundamental to nearly every electronic device we use daily, providing both electrical connections and mechanical support for components. This Skill Lync program offers a comprehensive dive into designing these essential components using Altium, a powerful ECAD software.

## Key Takeaways

*   This online program focuses on practical PCB design using the industry-standard Altium software.
*   The course spans three months, making it a focused skill development opportunity for electrical and electronics engineering students and graduates.
*   Participants benefit from a flexible fee structure, dedicated technical support, and career guidance, including job assistance.
*   Successful completion leads to a course certificate, with a merit certificate available for top performers.

## Course Overview

Skill Lync's "Advanced PCB Design Using Altium ECAD Software" is tailored for individuals looking to master the intricacies of PCB creation. Delivered through a blend of video lectures and text-based materials, the program emphasizes hands-on learning with Altium, a leading tool in electronic design. Expert instructors guide students through theoretical concepts and practical applications, ensuring a deep understanding of PCB design principles.

## What You Will Learn

The syllabus is structured to build expertise in key areas of PCB design. You will gain proficiency in:

*   **Component Selection:** Understanding how to choose appropriate electronic components for various applications.
*   **Schematic Design:** Creating detailed circuit diagrams that form the blueprint for the PCB.
*   **Layout Design:** Arranging components and routing traces on the board to ensure optimal performance and manufacturability.
*   **Fabrication Procedures:** Gaining insight into the steps involved in producing a physical PCB.

Through practical projects, the course helps solidify these concepts, allowing you to apply your knowledge in real-world scenarios.

## Who Is This Program For?

This program is ideally suited for:

*   **Electrical and Electronics Engineering Students:** Those currently pursuing degrees in these fields can gain valuable project experience and enhance their design skills.
*   **Aspiring PCB Design Engineers:** Individuals at the beginner level looking to deepen their understanding and build a strong foundation in the domain.
*   **Working Professionals:** Electronics and electrical engineers aiming to specialize in PCB design or advance their careers in the electronics industry.

## Program Offerings and Support

The Skill Lync program is designed to provide comprehensive support throughout your learning journey:

*   **Learning Materials:** Access to course videos and detailed lectures.
*   **Interactive Support:** Forum and email support, along with a dedicated support engineer for technical assistance.
*   **Practical Experience:** Multiple projects to build a strong portfolio and an e-verified profile.
*   **Career Development:** Job assistance, career guidance, and a demo session to explore the program's content.
*   **Certification:** A course completion certificate for all participants, and a merit certificate for students ranking in the top 5%.

## Fees and Duration

This three-month online program has a fee of ₹40,000. Skill Lync offers flexible payment structures and different access versions to accommodate various needs. All enrolled students will receive a course completion certificate from Skill Lync. Those who demonstrate exceptional performance by scoring in the top 5% of their class will also be awarded a merit certificate.

## How to Apply

Admission to the "Advanced PCB Design Using Altium ECAD Software" online course is processed through the official Skill Lync website. Prospective students should visit the portal to find detailed application instructions and complete the enrollment process.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to develop specialized skills in a critical area of electronics engineering, equipping you with the knowledge and tools needed for a successful career in PCB design.
