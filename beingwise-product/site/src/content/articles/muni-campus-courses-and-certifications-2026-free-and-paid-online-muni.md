Welcome to BeingWise's guide on mUni Campus, a platform offering a range of courses and certifications, including specialized programs developed in collaboration with leading industry partners like IBM. These programs are designed to equip students and professionals with cutting-edge skills for the evolving job market.

## Key takeaways

*   mUni Campus offers both free and paid online courses, alongside hybrid programs.
*   Many certificate programs are developed in partnership with industry leaders such as IBM.
*   Courses cover in-demand fields like Data Science, Business Analytics, Big Data, and Telecommunication Management.
*   Programs are designed for a diverse audience, from fresh graduates to experienced professionals seeking career advancement.

## Understanding mUni Campus Offerings

While mUni Campus provides a variety of educational content, some of its most notable programs are delivered in association with Aegis School of Business, Data Science, Cyber Security and Telecommunication, and IBM. These collaborations bring together academic rigor and industry expertise to create highly relevant and impactful learning experiences.

## Featured Programs for 2026

Here's a closer look at some of the prominent certificate programs available through mUni Campus:

### Hybrid EPGP in Data Science, Business Analytics and Big Data in association with IBM

This 12-month Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP) is a pioneering offering in India, designed for individuals aspiring to excel in data science. It's a hybrid program, meaning classes are conducted both on weekdays at the Centre for Excellence in Telecom Technology and Management (CETTM) in Mumbai and online on Sundays. The curriculum is comprehensive, blending core subjects with elective options, and aims to develop both technical and quantitative skills essential for creating business value in various sectors. Graduates will be well-prepared for analytical roles, leveraging exposure to industry-standard tools and software.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Data Science fundamentals and advanced concepts
*   Business Analytics strategies
*   Big Data technologies and applications

### Post Graduate Program in Telecommunication Management in association with IBM

An 11-month certificate course, this program is recognized as a leading offering in Telecom Management. It has benefited executives from over 35 countries, including those from telecom service providers and vendors. The curriculum focuses on providing crucial leadership perspectives on information technology and telecommunication operations, covering financial, management, and legal aspects. Participants will gain the confidence and skills to become effective technology managers and make informed decisions in the telecom industry.

**Skills Covered:**

*   In-depth knowledge of the IT and Telecommunication industry
*   Leadership and management skills in telecom
*   Strategic decision-making for telecom businesses

### Executive Masters Program in Telecom Management in association with IBM Online Live

This 11-month live online program is jointly delivered by experts from Aegis and IBM. It is often considered equivalent to an MBA in Telecommunication in India, offering a robust educational experience for aspiring leaders in information technology. The syllabus is meticulously crafted with input from major telecom companies like Qualcomm, Bharti Airtel, Amdocs, Ericsson, ZTE, Reliance Communication, and BSNL, ensuring it addresses current and future needs of telecom managers and leaders. The program helps participants develop specific functional skills relevant to their domains.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Advanced telecom management strategies
*   Leadership in the IT and telecom sector
*   Understanding of industry trends and future needs
