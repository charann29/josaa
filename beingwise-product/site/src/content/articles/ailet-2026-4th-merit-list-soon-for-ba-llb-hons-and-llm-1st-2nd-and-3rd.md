# AILET 2026: 4th Merit List (Soon for BA LLB Hons & LLM), 1st, 2nd & 3rd Merit List PDF, Expected Cutoff

The All India Law Entrance Test (AILET) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to join National Law University Delhi (NLU Delhi) for its prestigious law programs. This page provides a comprehensive overview of the AILET 2026 merit lists, including release dates, counselling details, and what to expect regarding cutoffs.

## Key Takeaways

*   The fourth provisional merit list for AILET 2026 BA LLB (Hons) and LLM programs is expected on May 5, 2026.
*   NLU Delhi releases merit lists for successful candidates based on their AILET scores and counselling participation.
*   To confirm admission, candidates must pay a non-refundable fee of INR 50,000 by the specified deadline after each merit list.
*   Official cutoffs for AILET 2026 will be published by NLU Delhi after the third round of seat allocation concludes.

## AILET 2026 Merit List Schedule

National Law University Delhi (NLU Delhi) is responsible for conducting AILET and publishing all official merit lists. These lists are vital for candidates seeking admission to the BA LLB (Hons) and LLM courses offered by the university. It's essential for selected candidates to adhere to the payment deadlines to secure their provisional admission, as failure to do so will result in the offer being withdrawn.

## AILET 2026 Counselling and Admission Process

AILET counselling is a multi-stage process that begins with the release of the answer key and results. The AILET 2026 examination was conducted on December 14, 2025. Subsequently, the AILET 2026 Answer Key and Results were released on December 18, 2025. Following the result declaration, NLU Delhi proceeds with the provisional merit list releases and subsequent counselling rounds.

Each merit list includes candidates provisionally selected for admission. These candidates must complete the necessary steps, including fee payment, by the specified deadlines. Missing these deadlines can lead to the forfeiture of the allotted seat. The counselling schedule provides a detailed timeline for these important steps.

## AILET 2026 Expected Cutoff

The official cutoff scores for AILET 2026 will be released by NLU Delhi only after the completion of the third round of seat allotment. The cutoff marks are determined by various factors, including the number of applicants, the difficulty level of the exam, and the availability of seats in the BA LLB (Hons) and LLM programs. Candidates are advised to monitor the official NLU Delhi website for the release of these crucial cutoff details.

Stay updated with the latest announcements from NLU Delhi to ensure you don't miss any critical dates or requirements for your AILET 2026 admission journey.
