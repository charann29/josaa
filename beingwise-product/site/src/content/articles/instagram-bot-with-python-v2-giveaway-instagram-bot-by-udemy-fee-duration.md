# Instagram Bot with Python V2 Giveaway Instagram Bot by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those interested in automating Instagram interactions, particularly for participating in giveaways, this course offers a practical guide to building an Instagram bot using Python. It's designed to teach you how to set up and operate a bot for automated likes, comments, and follows.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is delivered entirely in English, making it accessible to a wide audience.
*   It operates on a self-study model, allowing learners to progress at their own pace.
*   Content is provided through a mix of video and text-based materials for comprehensive learning.
*   A certificate of completion is awarded by the platform upon successful course completion.

## Course Overview and Fees

This program is structured as a self-paced learning experience, offering flexibility to fit into your schedule. The entire course content is delivered digitally, combining video lectures with supporting text materials to explain concepts and code. The fee for enrollment in this course is ₹799.

Upon successfully finishing all modules and requirements, participants will receive a certificate. This certificate is issued directly by Udemy, recognizing your effort and achievement in completing the course.

## What You Will Learn: The Syllabus

The curriculum is thoughtfully structured to guide you from foundational setup to advanced bot operations. It aims to equip you with the skills to create a functional Instagram bot for giveaway participation.

### Introduction

*   **Setting Up Your Environment:** This section covers the essential first steps, including how to install Python, the programming language central to the bot, and PyCharm, a popular integrated development environment (IDE) for Python.
*   **WebDriver Installation:** You'll learn about WebDriver, a tool that allows your Python scripts to interact with web browsers, which is crucial for automating web actions on Instagram.

### InstaBot Operation

*   **Instagram Login Automation:** Discover how to program your bot to automatically log into an Instagram account, bypassing manual entry.
*   **Navigating Giveaway Pages:** Learn the techniques to direct your bot to specific Instagram pages where giveaways are hosted.
*   **Interacting with Posts:** This module teaches the bot to identify and click on Instagram pictures, a common requirement for giveaway entries.
*   **Advanced Picture Interaction:** Further enhance your bot's ability to interact with multiple pictures or specific types of posts within a giveaway context.
*   **Automated Likes and Comments:** Master the art of programming your bot to automatically like posts and leave predefined comments, streamlining your participation.
*   **Automated Following and Posting Comments:** This section delves into automating the process of following accounts and posting specific comments, often key criteria for giveaway entries.

## Meet the Instructor

The course is led by Mr. Paschal Kallimanis, an experienced freelancer with a background in various fields, holding both bachelor's and master's degrees. His expertise guides learners through the practical aspects of building and operating the Instagram bot.
