# MAT May 2026 Registration (Started) @mat.aima.in: Check Last Date, Fees & How to fill MAT Exam Form

For those aspiring to pursue an MBA or PGDM, the MAT (Management Aptitude Test) is a crucial step. The registration process for the May 2026 MAT examination has officially begun, inviting candidates to apply through the official website. This page provides all the essential details you need to know about the application process, key dates, and fees.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Registration Underway:** The application window for MAT May 2026 is currently open.
*   **Dual Mode Options:** Candidates can choose to appear for the exam in either Paper-Based Test (PBT) or Computer-Based Test (CBT) format, or both.
*   **Increased Fees:** The application fee for a single MAT session has been revised to INR 2300. Opting for both PBT and CBT in the same phase will cost INR 4000.
*   **No Correction Window:** It is critical to fill the application form accurately as there is no provision for corrections once submitted.

## Important Dates for MAT May 2026

Understanding the schedule is vital to ensure you don't miss any deadlines. Here's a breakdown of the key dates for the upcoming MAT May 2026 examination:

| Event                               | Dates                       |
| :---------------------------------- | :-------------------------- |
| MAT May 2026 Registration (PBT)     | April 1, 2026 - May 25, 2026 |
| MAT May 2026 Registration (CBT)     | April 1, 2026 - June 8, 2026 |
| MAT May 2026 PBT Admit Card Release | May 28, 2026                |
| MAT May 2026 PBT Exam Date          | May 31, 2026                |
| MAT May 2026 CBT Admit Card Release | June 11, 2026               |
| MAT May 2026 CBT Exam Date          | June 14, 2026               |

## Application Fees for MAT 2026

The All India Management Association (AIMA) has updated the application fees for the MAT exam. Candidates should be aware of the following fee structure:

*   **For a single MAT session (either PBT or CBT):** INR 2300
*   **For both PBT and CBT in the same examination phase:** INR 4000

It's important to note that the application fee is non-refundable under any circumstances.

## Documents Required for Registration

Before you begin filling out the MAT application form, ensure you have the necessary documents ready in the specified format. Incorrect document uploads can lead to issues with your application.

| Document    | File Size           | Specifications                                                                                             |

| Photograph  | 10 KB to 50 KB      | Scanned color photograph (not older than six months), in JPEG/JPG format only.                         |

## How to Fill the MAT Application Form 2026

The application process for MAT is straightforward, but careful attention to detail is crucial. Follow these general steps to complete your registration:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Go to the official MAT website as designated by AIMA.
2.  **New Registration:** Click on the 'New Candidate Registration' link and provide basic details like your name, date of birth, email ID, and mobile number to create your login credentials.
3.  **Login:** Use the generated login details to access the application form.
4.  **Fill Details:** Accurately fill in all personal, academic, and contact information. Ensure that the details match your government-issued identification documents.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Upload your recent passport-sized photograph and signature according to the specified size and format guidelines.
6.  **Select Exam Mode & City:** Choose your preferred examination mode (PBT, CBT, or both) and select your desired test city from the available options.
7.  **Pay Application Fee:** Proceed to pay the application fee online using the available payment methods.
8.  **Review and Submit:** Thoroughly review all the information entered before final submission. Remember, no corrections can be made after submission.

## Important Instructions for Applicants

To avoid common pitfalls and ensure a smooth application process, keep the following points in mind:

*   **Accuracy is Key:** Double-check all information before submission. There is no provision for editing the form once it's submitted.
*   **Government ID Match:** Ensure that your personal details, especially your name, match those on your official government identification documents or Class 10 certificate.
*   **Eligibility Check:** Verify that you meet the MAT eligibility criteria before applying. Applications from ineligible candidates will be rejected.
*   **Non-Refundable Fee:** The application fee, once paid, will not be refunded.
*   **Multiple Sessions:** If you plan to appear for MAT in different examination phases, you will need to fill separate application forms and pay the fee for each session.

Preparing for MAT requires not just academic readiness but also careful attention to the application process. By following these guidelines, you can ensure a successful registration for MAT May 2026.
