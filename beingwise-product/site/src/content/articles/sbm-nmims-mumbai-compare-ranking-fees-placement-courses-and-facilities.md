# SBM NMIMS Mumbai: Compare Ranking, Fees, Placement, Courses & Facilities @

Choosing the right business school is a significant decision for any aspiring student. This guide offers a comprehensive look into SBM NMIMS Mumbai, providing key details on its academic offerings, campus life, and career prospects to help you make an informed choice.

## Key takeaways

*   SBM NMIMS Mumbai is a well-regarded institution, consistently rated highly by its students.
*   The school offers a diverse range of postgraduate programs, including popular MBA/PGDM, PG Diploma, and Ph.D. options.
*   Placement records indicate strong career outcomes for its graduates, with competitive median salaries.
*   The institute boasts solid infrastructure and faculty, contributing to a positive learning environment.

## Student Perspectives and Ratings

Based on feedback from over 150 verified students, SBM NMIMS Mumbai holds an impressive overall rating of 4.2 out of 5. Students particularly appreciate the infrastructure and faculty, which received ratings of 4.5 and 4.4 respectively. Placements were rated 4.2, and campus life 4.1, reflecting a vibrant student experience. The 'Value for Money' aspect was rated 3.9, suggesting a good return on investment for the education provided.

## Academic Programs and Fees

SBM NMIMS Mumbai offers a variety of courses tailored for different career aspirations. Here's a look at some of its popular programs:

### MBA/PGDM

This flagship two-year program is a top choice for many. The total tuition fees for the MBA/PGDM range from approximately ₹16 Lakhs to ₹27 Lakhs. Admissions typically require a valid score in the NMIMS Written Test, among other criteria. Graduates from this program have seen median salaries ranging from ₹10.4 Lakhs to ₹25.13 Lakhs, indicating strong placement potential.

### PG Diploma

For those seeking a shorter, focused program, the one-year PG Diploma is an excellent option. The total tuition fee for this course is around ₹50,000. Admission is primarily based on the NMIMS Written Test.

### Ph.D.

The four-year Ph.D. program is designed for students interested in advanced research. The total tuition fee for the Ph.D. is approximately ₹2.5 Lakhs. Candidates are typically admitted through the NMIMS Entrance Admission Test.

## Placements and Career Prospects

SBM NMIMS Mumbai has a commendable track record when it comes to student placements. The median salary figures, especially for the MBA/PGDM program, highlight the strong industry connections and career support the institution provides. While specific highest salary figures are not always disclosed publicly, the median figures offer a reliable indicator of the earning potential for graduates across various sectors.

## Campus and Facilities

Established in 1981, SBM NMIMS Mumbai is a private institution known for its robust campus environment. While specific campus size details are not widely published, student reviews consistently praise the infrastructure, including modern classrooms, libraries, and other essential learning resources. The positive rating for campus life further suggests a supportive and engaging atmosphere for students.
