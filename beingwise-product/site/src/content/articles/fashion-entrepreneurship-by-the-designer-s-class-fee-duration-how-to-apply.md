# Fashion Entrepreneurship by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone dreaming of launching their own venture in the dynamic world of fashion, this online program offers a practical pathway. Developed by a renowned industry figure, it's designed to equip aspiring fashion entrepreneurs with essential business acumen and creative insights.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Expert-Led Content:** The course is crafted by Masaba Gupta, offering unique perspectives from a leading fashion designer.
*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** It covers everything from business model development and product creation to marketing strategies and intellectual property rights.
*   **Flexible Learning:** As a self-paced, video-based course, it fits around your schedule, accessible for one year from enrollment.
*   **Practical Skills Focus:** Emphasizes developing real-world skills in merchandising, brand management, and business operations.

## Course Overview

The Fashion Entrepreneurship online course delves into the intricacies of establishing and growing a fashion business. It's an ideal program for those who want to understand the industry's fast pace and learn how to navigate its challenges. The curriculum introduces core concepts like creating effective business models, developing fashion products, understanding intellectual property, and building a strong brand identity.

Participants will also learn about managing entrepreneurial ventures, crafting modern marketing strategies, and utilizing contemporary promotion tools. The program aims to provide a holistic understanding of turning creative vision into a successful commercial enterprise.

## Who is This Course For?

This certification is specifically designed for aspiring fashion designers, current fashion professionals, or anyone with a keen interest in launching a business within the fashion sector. It's particularly beneficial for individuals looking to gain entrepreneurial skills and a deeper understanding of the business side of fashion, moving beyond just design to include brand management, finance, and marketing.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, participants will gain a strong grasp of various career opportunities within the fashion industry. The course outlines strategies for business model development and provides insights into creating and managing a fashion brand from conception to market.

Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Business Management:** Understanding the fundamentals of running a fashion enterprise.
*   **Brand Building:** Strategies for developing a strong brand identity and market presence.
*   **Marketing & Promotion:** Modern techniques for advertising, visual merchandising, and public relations.
*   **Financial Literacy:** Basic principles of finance relevant to a fashion business.
*   **Product Development:** Insights into the creation and packaging of fashion products.
*   **Intellectual Property:** Awareness of rights and protections for creative works.

## Course Structure and Duration

The Fashion Entrepreneurship program is delivered through high-definition video lectures, totaling approximately 1 hour and 54 minutes of core content. It’s a self-paced course, allowing learners to progress at their own speed. The program includes 15 assignments and 15 downloadable resources to reinforce learning. Enrolled students will have access to all study materials for one year from their enrollment date.

Each video lecture is followed by an evaluation, enabling learners to test their comprehension of the topics covered. The course concludes with a final assessment to consolidate learning.

### Syllabus at a Glance

*   Introduction to Fashion Entrepreneurship
*   Creating Value and Problem Solving
*   Business Model Development
*   Brand Building and Identity
*   Understanding and Packaging the Product
*   Marketing Strategies and New Media Marketing
*   Understanding Finance
*   Intellectual Property
*   Fashion Advertising, Visual Merchandising & Public Relations
*   Fashion Business Management
*   Strategies for Going Global

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Fashion Entrepreneurship online course is ₹2,499. While the program offers comprehensive learning, it's important to note that a formal certificate of completion is not provided upon finishing the course.

Embark on your journey to becoming a successful fashion entrepreneur by leveraging the insights and expertise offered through this specialized online program.
