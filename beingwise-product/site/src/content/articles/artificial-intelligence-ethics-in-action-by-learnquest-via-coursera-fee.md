# Artificial Intelligence Ethics in Action by LearnQuest via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course offers an opportunity to delve into the practical application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) ethics. It's designed for individuals eager to apply ethical frameworks to real-world AI scenarios, building upon foundational knowledge in AI ethics.

## Key Takeaways

*   An intermediate-level program, it focuses on applying AI ethics through three distinct projects.
*   The course is self-paced and can be completed in approximately 5 hours, making it flexible for busy schedules.
*   It is offered by Coursera in collaboration with LearnQuest, a well-known IT and business training provider.
*   Successful completion and passing all assignments lead to a certificate from both Coursera and LearnQuest.

## Course Overview and Structure

The "Artificial Intelligence Ethics in Action" program is structured around three hands-on projects, allowing learners to actively engage with ethical dilemmas in AI. These projects are designed to bridge the gap between theoretical understanding and practical implementation of AI ethics. Alongside the project work, the course incorporates discussion forums and graded assignments to solidify learning and encourage deeper critical thinking.

This program is a continuation for those who have already completed the preceding courses in the "Ethics in the Age of AI Specialization," serving as a practical capstone to that series. The learning materials include a mix of video lectures and readings, all delivered in English, with subtitles available in multiple languages to support a global learner base.

## Who Is This Course For?

This certification is particularly well-suited for professionals and researchers who are directly involved with AI development and analysis. This includes:

*   AI Developers seeking to integrate ethical considerations into their work.
*   Researchers exploring the societal impacts and ethical dimensions of AI.
*   Doctoral Researchers focusing on advanced topics in AI ethics.

It caters to individuals who wish to move beyond theoretical concepts and gain hands-on experience in analyzing and addressing ethical challenges presented by artificial intelligence in various contexts.

## Fee Structure and Enrollment Options

Enrollment in the "Artificial Intelligence Ethics in Action" course comes with flexible payment options, allowing learners to choose a plan that best fits their learning pace and budget. The fees are structured based on the duration for which you wish to access the course content.

| Enrollment Option | Fees (Approximate) |

These fees provide access to all course materials, including readings, videos, practical exercises, and participation in discussion forums. Upon successful completion of all graded assignments, learners will receive a certificate.

## How to Apply and Financial Assistance

Applying for the "Artificial Intelligence Ethics in Action" course is a straightforward process. You can begin by visiting the official course page on the Coursera platform. Once there, you will have the option to "Enrol for Free," which typically allows you to audit parts of the course content. To gain full access to all materials, graded assignments, and to be eligible for the certificate, you will need to choose one of the paid enrollment options.

For students who face financial constraints, Coursera offers a financial aid program. Interested learners can apply for this assistance directly through the platform, which can help make the course more accessible. This support ensures that financial difficulties do not become a barrier to ethical AI education.

## What You Will Learn

The syllabus is designed to provide a thorough understanding of artificial intelligence ethics through practical application. The modules guide learners through real-world case studies and project-based learning.

*   **Module 1: Introduction**
    *   Video: Ethics in Action: Introduction
*   **Module 2: Project 1 - OpenAI: GPT-3 Analysis**
*   **Module 2: Project 2 - Alleghany Screening Analysis**
*   **Module 3: Project 3 - Game Theory Algorithm Design**

Through these modules, you will gain the ability to critically analyze AI systems from an ethical standpoint and develop practical solutions for ethical challenges in different areas and contexts.

This program equips you with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, making you better prepared to navigate the ethical landscape of AI development and research.
