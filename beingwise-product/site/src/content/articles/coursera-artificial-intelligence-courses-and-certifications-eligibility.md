# Coursera Artificial Intelligence Courses & Certifications - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Career Options

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming industries worldwide, making it a crucial skill for future careers. Coursera offers a range of AI courses and certifications, developed by leading universities and tech companies, to help you build expertise in this exciting field.

## Key takeaways

*   Coursera provides AI courses for all levels, from beginners to experts.
*   Programs are developed by industry leaders like IBM and universities such as the University of Pennsylvania.
*   Many courses offer hands-on projects and practical application of AI concepts.
*   Certification upon completion can enhance your professional profile and career prospects.

## Developing AI Applications on Azure

This expert-level certification, offered by LearnQuest through Coursera, focuses on building and deploying AI solutions using Microsoft Azure. It's designed for individuals who want to create, test, and manage machine learning models in a cloud environment.

### What you will learn:

*   **Azure Machine Learning Workspace:** How to set up and operate within Azure's environment for machine learning.
*   **Machine Learning Fundamentals:** Exploration of various algorithms and types of machine learning.
*   **Python for ML:** Practical application of Python, including scientific ecosystem packages, for machine learning solutions.
*   **Data Analytics:** Standardized approaches to data analysis, including Microsoft's Team Data Science Approach.
*   **Cognitive Services:** Familiarization with pre-trained AI models available as REST APIs, covering concepts like facial recognition, sentiment analysis, and computer vision.

### Course Details:

*   **Duration:** Approximately 5 weeks.
*   **Skills Covered:** Python, data science, cloud computing.
*   **Fees:** This course is available for free.

## Building AI Applications with Watson APIs

Developed by IBM and available on Coursera, this intermediate-level program teaches you how to create applications that integrate various IBM Watson AI services. You'll gain proficiency in leveraging Watson's capabilities for intelligent solutions.

### What you will learn:

*   **Watson AI Services:** Incorporating services like Discovery, Assistant, Speech to Text, and Text to Speech into applications.
*   **Interactive Systems:** Building data retrieval systems that combine Watson Discovery and Assistant.
*   **Industry Best Practices:** Learning optimal methods for integrating Watson services.

### Course Details:

*   **Duration:** Approximately 5 weeks.
*   **Skills Covered:** Chatbot development, Watson AI, machine learning, Python programming, artificial intelligence, computer skills.
*   **Fees:** The course fee is ₹3,202.

## AI Fundamentals for Non-Data Scientists

Offered by the University of Pennsylvania via Coursera, this introductory course is perfect for anyone interested in understanding the core concepts of AI without needing a background in data science. It provides a foundational glimpse into machine learning and artificial intelligence.

### What you will learn:

*   **AI Basics:** Fundamental concepts of artificial intelligence and machine learning.
*   **Industry Insights:** Exclusive interviews with leaders from reputable companies, offering real-world perspectives.

### Course Details:

*   **Duration:** Can be completed in about 9 hours.
*   **Skills Covered:** Artificial Intelligence, financial management.
*   **Fees:** The course fee is ₹1,699.
*   **Note:** This is the first course in the four-program AI For Business Specialization.

These Coursera certifications offer a flexible and accessible way to gain valuable AI skills, whether you're starting fresh or looking to specialize further in the field.
