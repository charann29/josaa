This page will guide you through the fee structure and application process for the MBA in International Business (IB) distance learning program at the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT). Understanding these details is crucial for prospective students planning their higher education.

## Key Takeaways

*   IIFT's MBA (IB) distance learning program has a total fee of ₹10,50,000.
*   This fee includes a mandatory alumni fee of ₹10,000.
*   Students from SC, ST, and PWD categories are eligible for a 50% concession on the tuition fee component.
*   The total fee can be conveniently paid in six installments.

## Understanding the Fee Structure for IIFT's MBA (IB) Distance Learning

The MBA (IB) distance program at IIFT comes with a specific fee structure. The total cost for the program is ₹10,50,000. This comprehensive fee covers various aspects of your learning journey, including an alumni fee of ₹10,000, which is a standard inclusion for all students.

One of the helpful features of IIFT's payment policy is the flexibility it offers. Students can choose to pay the total program fee in six convenient installments, making it easier to manage the financial commitment over time.

## Fee Concessions for Specific Categories

IIFT is committed to promoting inclusive education and offers significant fee concessions for eligible candidates. Students belonging to the Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), and Persons with Disabilities (PWD) categories receive a 50% concession. It's important to note that this concession applies specifically to the tuition fee component of the total program cost.

## Application Process for IIFT's MBA (IB) Distance Learning

The application process for IIFT's MBA (IB) distance learning program follows a structured timeline. For the 2025-27 batch, the application window has already closed, typically in January of the admission year. If you are interested in applying, you would need to wait for the next application cycle to open. IIFT officially announces the application dates on its official portal, and prospective students should regularly check there for the most current information regarding future admissions.

## General Information on MBA (IB) Distance Learning Fees in India

While IIFT has a specific fee, it's worth noting that MBA (IB) distance learning programs across India generally have a wide fee range. Depending on the institution, program duration, and facilities, these fees can vary significantly. IIFT's program is considered a premium offering within this landscape, reflecting its reputation and the quality of its curriculum.

Staying informed about application deadlines and fee details directly from official sources is always the best approach for planning your academic future.
