# I belong to OBC category and wish to apply for the same in Neet mds 2026. Will I be asked to upload Non Creamy Layer Certificate during registration process? Or is it only required during Councelling?

If you're an OBC candidate planning for NEET MDS 2026, understanding when and how your Non-Creamy Layer (NCL) certificate comes into play is crucial. This guide explains the process, from application to counselling, ensuring you're well-prepared.

## Key takeaways

*   You will indicate your OBC-NCL status during the NEET MDS application form submission.
*   While an upload of the certificate might be requested during registration, the primary verification occurs during counselling.
*   The Medical Counselling Committee (MCC) is responsible for verifying documents for All India Quota seats.
*   Ensure your OBC-NCL certificate is valid and issued by the competent authority.

## Indicating your category during NEET MDS registration

When you fill out your NEET MDS 2026 application form, you will select your category. For those belonging to the Other Backward Classes (OBC) who fall under the Non-Creamy Layer, it's important to specifically choose 'OBC-NCL'. This initial selection informs the National Board of Examinations in Medical Sciences (NBEMS), the organizing body for NEET MDS, of your reservation claim. In certain instances, as part of the online application process, you might be prompted to upload a scanned copy of your OBC-NCL certificate or a self-declaration form. Always follow the instructions provided on the official NEET MDS portal during registration.

## The critical role of counselling for document verification

While you declare your category and potentially upload a document during registration, the definitive verification of your OBC-NCL status takes place during the counselling rounds. For All India Quota seats, this crucial step is managed by the Medical Counselling Committee (MCC). During the document verification phase of counselling, you will be required to present your original OBC-NCL certificate. This is when the authorities meticulously check its authenticity, validity, and whether it meets all specified criteria.

## Why the OBC-NCL certificate is vital

Your OBC-NCL certificate serves as official proof that you qualify for the reservation benefits extended to the Non-Creamy Layer within the OBC category. Without a valid and correctly issued certificate, your claim for reservation might not be upheld, potentially affecting your eligibility for seats reserved for OBC-NCL candidates. It's essential to have this document ready well in advance of the counselling process, as obtaining it can sometimes take time.

## What to remember about your certificate

*   **Validity:** Check the validity period of your certificate. It must be current and valid for the academic year 2026-27. The specific format and validity period are usually outlined in the official NEET MDS information bulletin.
*   **Issuing Authority:** Ensure your certificate is issued by a competent authority as prescribed by the government. Certificates from unauthorized sources will not be accepted.
*   **Details:** Verify that all personal details on the certificate (name, father's name, address, etc.) match those on your other identification documents and your NEET MDS application form.

Being proactive about your documentation will ensure a smoother application and counselling experience for NEET MDS 2026.
