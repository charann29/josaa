Welcome to BeingWise! This page explores various online courses and certifications offered through Jaro Education, a platform that collaborates with leading Indian institutions to provide upskilling opportunities. These programs are designed for working professionals looking to enhance their careers in diverse fields.

## Key takeaways

*   Jaro Education partners with top institutions like IIT Delhi, IIM Trichy, IIM Ahmedabad, and IIM Nagpur.
*   Programs typically last 12 months, offering a focused learning experience.
*   Courses cover in-demand areas such as Electric Vehicle Technology, Business Analytics, and General Management.
*   Many programs offer practical, hands-on experience and lead to valuable alumni status with the partnering institution.

## Online PG Diploma in Electric Vehicle Technology (IIT Delhi)

This specialized 12-month online PG Diploma is offered by IIT Delhi and focuses on the rapidly expanding electric vehicle (EV) sector. It's tailored for professionals aiming to gain expertise in core electrical components crucial for EVs, including motors, charging infrastructure, powertrains, and energy storage systems.

A significant advantage of this program is the opportunity for hands-on experience with EV systems in IIT Delhi's labs. Participants will master EV electrical systems and develop expertise in designing electric drivetrains and charging infrastructure. The program fee is ₹4,50,000.

## Post Graduate Certificate Programme in Business Analytics and Applications (IIM Trichy)

IIM Trichy offers this 12-month Post Graduate Certificate Programme, which equips students with essential programming tools and languages for business analytics. The curriculum covers Tableau, Microsoft Excel, SPSS, PowerBI, R, and Python. The training includes live interactive sessions, weekend classes, and a campus immersion experience.

The program's goal is to teach participants how to extract valuable insights from data to foster business growth. The syllabus delves into business statistics, data collection, predictive modeling with R, data visualization, AI and ML programming, and data mining. A dedicated section focuses on the practical applications of analytics. Upon completion, participants undertake an application project and gain IIM Trichy alumni status. The program fee is ₹2,67,000.

## Accelerated General Management Programme (IIM Ahmedabad)

Designed for mid-level working executives, IIM Ahmedabad's 12-month Accelerated General Management Programme aims to bridge skill gaps in contemporary global business management. With classes held twice a week, the program introduces modern concepts and technologies essential for competing effectively on an international scale.

Participants will gain a deep understanding of theoretical aspects and practical applications in management. The course prepares them for effective decision-making, strategic planning, and leadership, covering areas like change management and communication. The program fee is ₹8,00,000.

## PG Certificate Programme in Business Management for IT Professionals (IIM Nagpur)

This 12-month blended learning program from IIM Nagpur is a skill-based training designed specifically for IT professionals. It focuses on enhancing business management acumen relevant to the technology sector.

These programs represent excellent opportunities for professionals to upskill and advance their careers through structured learning from India's premier educational institutions.
