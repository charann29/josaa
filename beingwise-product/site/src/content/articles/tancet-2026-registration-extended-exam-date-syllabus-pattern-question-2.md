# TANCET 2026: Registration (Extended), Exam Date, Syllabus, Pattern, Question Papers

The Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test (TANCET) is a crucial state-level examination for aspiring students seeking admission to MBA and MCA programs across various colleges in Tamil Nadu. Conducted by Anna University, this test opens doors to postgraduate studies in over 200 institutions.

## Key takeaways

*   The application deadline for TANCET 2026 has been extended to April 15, 2026.
*   The TANCET 2026 examination for both MBA and MCA courses is scheduled for May 9, 2026.
*   Anna University conducts TANCET annually in an offline mode, featuring 100 multiple-choice questions to be completed in two hours.
*   Successful candidates will participate in counselling administered by DTE, Chennai, for final admissions.

## TANCET Exam Overview

TANCET is a significant entrance examination for postgraduate admissions in Tamil Nadu. Here are the key highlights of the TANCET 2026 examination:

*   **Full Form:** Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test
*   **Conducting Body:** Anna University
*   **Exam Level:** State-level
*   **Frequency:** Once a year
*   **Exam Mode:** Offline (Pen and Paper-based)
*   **Duration:** 2 hours
*   **Question Type:** 100 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Medium:** English
*   **Application Fee:** INR 1,000 for unreserved categories; INR 500 for reserved categories (residents of Tamil Nadu)
*   **Courses Offered:** MBA and MCA programs in over 200 affiliated colleges.

## TANCET 2026 Application Process

The application window for TANCET 2026 has been extended, offering candidates more time to complete their forms. Prospective students for MBA or MCA programs in Tamil Nadu must ensure their applications are submitted by April 15, 2026. After applying, it is advisable to log in to the official Anna University portal to verify successful submission and address any potential errors, such as incorrect document uploads.

## TANCET Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The TANCET exam is designed to assess a candidate's aptitude across various domains. For MBA aspirants, the paper typically covers sections like Business Analysis, Quantitative Aptitude, Reading Comprehension, Verbal Ability, Data Sufficiency, and General English. The MCA exam pattern also focuses on relevant analytical and mathematical skills. Detailed syllabi for both MBA and MCA can be found on the official TANCET website, which is essential for focused preparation.

## Preparing with TANCET Question Papers

Practicing with previous years' TANCET question papers is an invaluable part of preparation. These papers help candidates understand the exam structure, question types, and time management strategies. Analyzing past questions can also highlight frequently tested topics, allowing for more targeted study. Candidates should download official sample papers and previous year's questions from the Anna University portal to enhance their readiness.
