# Government Jobs in India 2025 - After 10th, 12th, Graduation

Many individuals in India aspire to government jobs, drawn by the promise of stability, security, and a range of benefits like pensions, housing allowances, medical facilities, and paid leave. These positions attract a diverse pool of talented students and professionals seeking a fulfilling career path.

## Key takeaways

*   Government job opportunities are available for candidates with qualifications ranging from Class 10 to graduation and beyond.
*   Major recruitment drives are expected in sectors like railways, banking, insurance, and education.
*   Application dates for several significant roles are concentrated around August and September 2025.
*   Proficiency in regional languages and specific certifications (like ITI or special education diplomas) can be crucial for certain roles.

## Oriental Insurance Company Limited (OICL) Assistants

OICL has announced 500 vacancies for Assistant (Class) positions. This is an excellent opportunity for graduates, particularly those proficient in the regional language of the state they wish to apply from. The application deadline is August 17th, 2025. Selection involves a three-stage process: preliminary exams, main exams, and a regional language test, all managed by OICL.

## Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC) Eastern Railway Apprentices

The RRC, Eastern Railway, is recruiting for 3,115 Act Apprentice positions across its various units. This significant opportunity is open to candidates who have completed Class 10 with at least 50% marks and hold an ITI certificate from an NCVT/SCVT-recognised institute. The online application window is from August 14th, 2025, to September 13th, 2025, via the official RRC Eastern Railway Portal.

## Punjab Education Recruitment Board Primary and Trained Graduate Teachers

The Punjab Education Recruitment Board has released 725 vacancies for Primary Teacher (PRT) and Trained Graduate Teacher (TGT) roles, specifically within the special education category. Candidates with a Diploma in Special Education (D.Ed. Spl.Ed.) and valid PSTET scores are eligible. The final date for online applications is August 27th, 2025, and candidates will be shortlisted based on their academic qualifications.

## Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) Customer Service Associates

IBPS has declared a substantial recruitment drive for 10,277 Customer Service Associates (Clerks) across 11 participating public sector banks under the CRP CSA-XV. Graduates with basic computer skills, aged between 20 and 28 years, can apply online from August 1st, 2025, to August 21st, 2025, through the official IBPS portal.

## Jammu and Kashmir Services Selection Board (JKSSB) Healthcare Roles

For those interested in healthcare sector government jobs, the JKSSB has announced 621 vacancies for positions like Staff Nurse, Junior Pharmacist, and more. Interested candidates can apply online until September 3rd, 2025. The selection process typically involves a written examination and document verification, with detailed information available in the official JKSSB notification.

Exploring these upcoming opportunities can be a strategic step towards a secure and rewarding career in public service.
