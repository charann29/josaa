# MAH CET Exam Dates 2026 (OUT): Registration, Admit Card, & Result Dates for MBA/MMS

For students aspiring to pursue MBA or MMS programs in Maharashtra, the MAH CET is a crucial entrance examination. Understanding the key dates for registration, the exam itself, admit card release, and results is essential for a smooth application journey.

## Key Takeaways

*   The MAH MBA CET 2026 exam was held in April, with a second opportunity scheduled for May 9, 2026.
*   Candidates who registered for both attempts will have their best score considered for admissions.
*   Registration for the exam took place in January 2026, followed by a form correction window in February-March 2026.
*   Results for both exam sessions are expected to be announced together, approximately one month after the final exam date.

Here’s a comprehensive look at the MAH CET 2026 schedule, including past and upcoming events:

| Dates              | Event                          |

## MAH MBA CET 2026 Registration and Admit Card

The registration process for MAH MBA CET 2026 was conducted in January 2026. Following registration, candidates had an opportunity to correct their application forms in late February and early March 2026. The admit card, which is mandatory for appearing in the exam, is typically released a few days before each examination session. Candidates need to download their admit cards from the official Maharashtra State CET Cell portal using their login credentials.

## MAH MBA CET 2026 Exam Details

The Maharashtra State CET Cell organized the MAH MBA CET 2026 in two distinct attempts. The first attempt was held on April 6, 7, and 8, while the second attempt is scheduled for May 9, 2026. This dual-attempt provision allows candidates to take the exam twice, with their better score being considered for admission purposes. The exam pattern for both attempts remains consistent: 200 objective-type questions across Logical Reasoning, Abstract Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, and Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension, to be completed in two and a half hours without sectional time limits.

## MAH MBA CET 2026 Answer Key and Results

Following the examination, the provisional answer key for MAH MBA CET 2026 is expected to be released on the official website. Candidates will have a short window, usually 2-3 days, to raise objections against any answers they believe are incorrect. These objections, if found valid by the examination authority, may lead to a revised final answer key. The MAH CET 2026 results for both the April and May sessions will be declared together, approximately one month after the final exam. Candidates can download their scorecards from the official portal using their login ID and password.

## MAH MBA CET 2026 Counselling Process

The Centralised Admission Process (CAP) for MAH MBA CET 2026 involves several stages. This includes online registration, verification of documents, and confirmation of the application form at designated Setu Suvidha Kendras (SETU). Candidates must provide a printout of their filled application form along with copies of uploaded documents for verification. Seat allocation in MBA/MMS programs occurs in rounds, based on the candidate's chosen colleges and the year's MAH CET cut-offs.

Staying informed about these dates and processes from the official Maharashtra State CET Cell portal will help you navigate your admission journey effectively.
