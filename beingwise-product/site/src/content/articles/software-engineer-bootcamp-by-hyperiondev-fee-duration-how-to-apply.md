# Software Engineer Bootcamp by HyperionDev: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aspiring to enter the dynamic world of software development, the HyperionDev Software Engineer Bootcamp offers a structured pathway to acquire essential coding skills. This program is designed to equip learners with the practical knowledge needed to thrive in the digital economy.

## Key takeaways

*   The bootcamp covers core software engineering principles, including Python, Java, machine learning, and data science.
*   It offers flexible learning options, allowing completion in 3 to 6 months through part-time or full-time study.
*   Participants receive personalized mentorship, career coaching, and a certificate upon successful completion.
*   A free trial is available to experience the bootcamp content before committing to the full program.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

The Become a Software Engineer Bootcamp by HyperionDev is an intensive online program focused on practical application. It teaches fundamental concepts across computer science, data science, and software engineering. You'll gain proficiency in programming languages like Python and Java, explore machine learning, and understand object-oriented programming.

The curriculum is structured to guide beginners through their coding journey, starting with an introduction to programming and software engineering, and progressing to data science and algorithms. A key feature of the program is its emphasis on writing industry-quality code, with assignments receiving feedback from experienced mentors.

## Duration and Learning Mode

This bootcamp is designed for flexibility, allowing students to complete the program in a timeframe of 3 to 6 months. It operates on a self-study model, utilizing video and text-based materials for instruction. While the learning is self-paced, it's supported by one-on-one assistance, ensuring that learners receive guidance when needed. You can choose between full-time or part-time schedules to fit your personal commitments.

## Fees and Scholarship Opportunities

The program fee for the Become a Software Engineer Bootcamp is \$7,995. This covers all aspects of the curriculum, mentorship, and career support services. HyperionDev also offers a needs-based scholarship for eligible full-time students or unemployed individuals in South Africa. This scholarship provides a 37% discount on tuition fees. To be considered, applicants must apply online and attend an interview with a course advisor.

## How to Apply

Applying for the HyperionDev Software Engineer Bootcamp is a straightforward process, beginning with a free trial:

1.  **Visit the official course page:** Navigate to the Become a Software Engineer program section on the HyperionDev website.
2.  **Create an account:** Provide your name, email ID, and password, then answer a simple mathematical question. Alternatively, you can use your Google or Facebook login credentials.
3.  **Start the free trial:** This allows you to explore a portion of the bootcamp content and understand the learning style.
4.  **Upgrade to the full bootcamp:** After completing the free trial, you can choose your preferred payment method to enroll in the complete program.

Upon successful completion, you will receive a Bootcamp certificate from HyperionDev, recognizing your new software engineering skills and readiness for the job market.
