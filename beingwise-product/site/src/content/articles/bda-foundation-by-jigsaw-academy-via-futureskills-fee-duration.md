# BDA Foundation by Jigsaw Academy via FutureSkills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals looking to build a strong foundation in big data analytics, the BDA Foundation program, offered through Jigsaw Academy and available on the FutureSkills platform, provides a comprehensive learning experience. This program is designed to equip learners with essential skills in key big data technologies.

## Key Takeaways

*   The BDA Foundation program is delivered entirely in English through a self-study, video, and text-based format.
*   The total fee for the course, including GST, is ₹4,999.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Jigsaw Academy.
*   The curriculum covers fundamental concepts in HDFS, MapReduce, Spark, and an introduction to Machine Learning with Spark.

## Understanding the Program

This foundation course is structured for individuals who prefer a flexible learning approach. The instruction medium is English, and the entire program is designed for self-paced study. Learning materials are provided through a combination of videos and text, allowing students to engage with the content at their convenience and revisit complex topics as needed.

## Course Fees and Certification

The investment for the BDA Foundation program is ₹4,999. This amount includes Goods and Services Tax (GST). A significant benefit of completing this program is the official certification provided by Jigsaw Academy, a recognized name in data science education. This certificate can be a valuable addition to a professional portfolio, demonstrating proficiency in foundational big data analytics concepts.

## What You Will Learn: The Syllabus Overview

The program's syllabus is carefully crafted to cover core components of big data analytics, starting from the basics and progressing to more advanced topics. Here's a breakdown of the key areas:

### HDFS and MapReduce Fundamentals

*   **HDFS Architecture and Operations:** Understanding how the Hadoop Distributed File System works, including its structure and common operations.
*   **MapReduce Concepts and Architecture:** Delving into the core principles of MapReduce, its architecture, and how it processes large datasets.
*   **YARN and MapReduce Internals:** Exploring Yet Another Resource Negotiator (YARN) and the inner workings of MapReduce for efficient resource management.
*   **Hands-on Exercises:** Practical application of learned concepts to solidify understanding.

### Introduction to Spark and Scala

*   **Spark Ecosystem and Abstractions:** An overview of Apache Spark, its components, and key abstractions like Resilient Distributed Datasets (RDDs).
*   **Programming in Scala:** Learning the basics of Scala, the primary language for Spark development.
*   **Spark Properties and Use Cases:** Understanding various Spark configurations and real-world applications.

### RDD Operations and Advanced Spark

*   **Basics and Advanced RDD Operations:** Comprehensive coverage of transformations and actions performed on RDDs.
*   **RDD Persistence:** Techniques for optimizing performance by persisting RDDs in memory or on disk.
*   **Shared Variables:** Exploring accumulators and broadcast variables for efficient data sharing across Spark tasks.
*   **Job Submission, Execution, and Performance Tuning:** Practical aspects of running Spark jobs and optimizing their performance.

### Spark Streaming, SQL, and Machine Learning

*   **Spark Streaming Architecture and Structured Streaming:** Real-time data processing with Spark Streaming.
*   **Spark SQL and DataFrames:** Working with structured data using Spark SQL and the DataFrame API.
*   **Machine Learning with Spark:** An introduction to building machine learning models using Spark's MLlib library.
*   **Machine Learning Case Studies:** Practical examples of applying machine learning techniques with Spark.

This structured curriculum ensures a progressive learning path, allowing students to build competence in each domain.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the BDA Foundation program, interested individuals can typically register directly through the FutureSkills platform. The self-study format means there are generally no specific application deadlines or entrance exams. Learners can usually sign up and begin the course at their convenience.

This program offers a valuable entry point for anyone aspiring to a career in big data analytics, providing fundamental knowledge and practical skills from a reputable institution.
