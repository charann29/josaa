# Rajasthan JET 2025 Dates: Counselling Schedule for UG, Result, Cutoff and Other details

For students aspiring to pursue agricultural and allied science courses in Rajasthan, the Joint Entrance Test (JET) is a crucial pathway. Understanding the key dates for the application, examination, and particularly the counselling process, is essential for a smooth admission journey.

## Key Takeaways

*   The Rajasthan JET counselling process typically involves Main, Final, and Spot rounds for various undergraduate, postgraduate, and Pre-PG/PhD programs.
*   Admission to participating agricultural colleges is based on a candidate's JET scores and their eligibility against the official cutoff.
*   Key counselling dates, including fee submission and provisional allotment lists, are released on the official JET portal.
*   Always refer to the official Rajasthan JET website for the most accurate and updated schedule.

## Steps to Check Rajasthan JET 2025 Dates

Keeping track of important dates is vital. Here’s how you can find all the official announcements related to the Rajasthan JET exam and admission process:

1.  **Visit the Official Rajasthan JET Website:** Navigate to the designated official portal for Rajasthan JET.
2.  **Locate the 'Important Instruction' Tab:** On the homepage, look for a section or tab specifically labeled for important instructions or notifications.
3.  **Access the Dates PDF:** Clicking this tab will usually redirect you to a new page or open a PDF document containing all critical dates, deadlines, and guidelines for upcoming events.
4.  **Plan Ahead:** Reviewing these dates in advance allows you to prepare necessary documents and meet deadlines for the application, examination, and subsequent admission stages.

## Rajasthan JET 2025 Important Dates & Schedule

The counselling process for Rajasthan JET typically unfolds in multiple stages. While specific dates for 2025 will be officially announced, previous years' schedules offer an insight into the timeline. For instance, in a past cycle, the deadline for submitting option fees was December 20, with the provisional allotment list released on December 24. The final date for online option fee payment was December 28, and campus reporting for admitted students was set for December 30. The second provisional list for counselling saw fee deposits by December 13 and campus reporting by December 15.

Candidates who meet the Rajasthan JET cutoff criteria become eligible to participate in the counselling process to secure admission to their preferred institutions and courses.

## Rajasthan JET 2025 Admit Card Date

Following successful registration, candidates must download their Rajasthan JET 2025 Admit Card from the official portal. This admit card is a mandatory document for entry into the examination center. Along with the admit card, candidates are typically required to carry a valid photo identification proof. It is advisable to arrive at the examination center at least 30 minutes before the scheduled exam time. Key details to verify on your admit card include your registration or roll number and the examination center details.

## Rajasthan JET 2025 Counselling

Rajasthan JET counselling is conducted in multiple rounds – generally Main, Final, and Spot rounds – to facilitate admissions into various undergraduate, postgraduate, and Pre-PG/PhD courses in agriculture and allied fields. Eligibility for counselling is determined by a candidate's performance in the JET examination and their adherence to the officially declared cutoff scores. The counselling process involves online registration, choice filling, seat allotment based on merit and preferences, fee payment, and document verification at the allotted college.

Staying informed about the official announcements on the Rajasthan JET portal will ensure you don't miss any crucial steps in your admission journey.
