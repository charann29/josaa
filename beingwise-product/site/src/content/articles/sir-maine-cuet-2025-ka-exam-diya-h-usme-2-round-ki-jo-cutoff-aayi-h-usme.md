# CUET 2025 Counselling: Fee Payment Issues and What to Do

It can be incredibly stressful when you've cleared a CUET cut-off, but the fee payment option isn't showing up, especially with a deadline looming. Don't panic; there are clear steps you can take to resolve this.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Act Immediately:** With today being the last date, every minute counts. Start troubleshooting right away.
*   **Document Everything:** Take screenshots of the issue for proof.
*   **Contact Support Directly:** Reach out to both the university and CUET/NTA helplines.
*   **Try Alternatives:** Sometimes, a simple change of device or browser can fix technical glitches.

## Troubleshooting Technical Glitches

Before assuming a major problem, try these common solutions for online portal issues:

*   **Different Browser:** If you're using Chrome, try Firefox, Edge, or Safari. Sometimes, browser-specific settings or cached data can interfere with website functionality.
*   **Another Device:** If you're on a laptop, try a desktop computer or even a different mobile device. Ensure your internet connection is stable.
*   **Clear Cache and Cookies:** In your browser settings, clear your browsing data, especially cached images and files, and cookies. This can often resolve display issues on websites.
*   **Check for Maintenance:** While less common during active counselling rounds, it's worth checking if the official CUET or university portal has announced any scheduled maintenance that might temporarily affect payment gateways.

## Urgent Communication with Authorities

This is perhaps the most crucial step. You need to inform the relevant authorities about your situation immediately.

*   **University Helpline/Admissions Office:** Your primary contact should be the university you've been allotted to. Look for their dedicated admissions helpline numbers or email IDs on their official website. Explain your situation clearly, mentioning your CUET application number, roll number, and the specific round of counselling.
*   **CUET Support (NTA):** While the university manages admissions after CUET results, NTA is the conducting body for CUET. If the issue seems to be with the common counselling portal itself, contacting NTA's official support channels might also be helpful. Their contact details are usually available on the official NTA CUET website.

## Documenting Your Efforts

In situations like this, having proof of your attempts to pay and contact support is vital. This can protect you if there's a dispute later.

*   **Screenshots:** Take clear screenshots of the page where the fee payment option is missing. Include the date and time in the screenshot if possible. Capture any error messages that appear.
*   **Email Correspondence:** When you email the university or CUET support, keep a copy of the sent email. Note down the date and time of any phone calls you make, and if possible, the name of the person you spoke with.
*   **Proof of Selection:** Keep handy the official communication or screenshot showing your selection in the second round of counselling.

## Understanding the Deadline

Today being the last date adds urgency. Do not wait for a response after your initial contact. Keep trying the payment portal periodically while also maintaining communication with support teams. Your documented efforts to resolve the issue before the deadline can be very important if an extension or alternative payment method is offered later.

It's important to remain calm and systematic in your approach. By following these steps, you significantly increase your chances of resolving the issue and securing your admission.
