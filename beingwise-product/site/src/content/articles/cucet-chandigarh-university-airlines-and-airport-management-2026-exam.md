# CUCET Chandigarh University Airlines and Airport Management 2026 Exam: Dates, Registration, Eligibility, Syllabus, Results

For students aspiring to a career in the dynamic aviation sector, the Chandigarh University Common Entrance Test (CUCET) is a crucial step towards enrolling in the Airlines and Airport Management program. This page provides a comprehensive guide to the CUCET 2026 for this specific course, covering key details from application to results.

## Key takeaways

*   The CUCET 2026 Phase 1 application period closes on April 15, 2026.
*   The exam is conducted online as a remotely proctored test, requiring a laptop, webcam, and stable internet.
*   Successful candidates may be eligible for scholarships based on their CUCET performance and other criteria.
*   Results are typically communicated via email or through the candidate's login portal after the exam.

## CUCET 2026 Exam Dates and Schedule

The CUCET 2026 is conducted in multiple phases. For Phase 1, the registration window for the exam is open until April 15, 2026. Candidates can take the online, remotely proctored exam up to April 16, 2026. Applicants have the flexibility to select their preferred exam slot within this period. Information regarding Phase 2 dates will be announced by the university later.

## CUCET 2026 Application Process and Eligibility

To apply for the Airlines and Airport Management program through CUCET 2026, candidates must submit their application form either online through the official CUCET portal or offline by purchasing forms from designated admission offices or authorized centers. The last date for Phase 1 online registration is April 15, 2026.

While specific eligibility criteria for the Airlines and Airport Management program are detailed on the university's official admissions page, generally, candidates must have completed their 10+2 education from a recognized board. It is advisable to review the program-specific requirements on the Chandigarh University website before applying.

## CUCET 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The CUCET 2026 is a two-hour, remotely proctored online examination. Candidates will need a laptop, a functional webcam, and a reliable internet connection to take the test from their chosen location. During the exam, a live chat invigilator will be available to assist with any technical queries or issues.

The syllabus for the Airlines and Airport Management program entrance typically assesses a candidate's aptitude in areas relevant to the field, which may include general awareness, English language proficiency, quantitative aptitude, and logical reasoning. For precise details on the CUCET 2026 syllabus for this specific program, candidates should refer to the official CUCET information available on the Chandigarh University website.

## CUCET 2026 Results and Scholarships

After completing the CUCET 2026 exam, candidates can check their results by logging into their applicant portal on the official CUCET website or by checking their registered email address. The university will notify candidates about the result declaration. Based on their performance in CUCET and other academic qualifications, successful applicants may become eligible for various scholarships offered by Chandigarh University. These scholarships aim to support meritorious students and are awarded based on specific criteria outlined by the university.

Embarking on a journey in Airlines and Airport Management can lead to a rewarding career, and CUCET provides a structured pathway to achieve this goal at Chandigarh University.
