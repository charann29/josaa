# Senior Secondary: Business Studies (319) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students at the senior secondary level interested in understanding the world of business, the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS), in collaboration with Swayam, offers an online course in Business Studies (Code 319). This program is designed to introduce fundamental business concepts and management principles, preparing learners for future studies or careers in this dynamic field.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 24-week, self-paced online course, offering 10 credit points upon successful completion.
*   The curriculum covers core business concepts, from organizational forms to marketing and finance.
*   Learners receive a certificate from NIOS and Swayam after meeting the course requirements.
*   The program is delivered through a blend of video lectures, reading materials, and interactive discussion forums.

## Course Overview

This comprehensive online course, coordinated by Prof. Ms. Anshul Kharbanda of NIOS, delves into the essential aspects of business studies and financial management relevant to the senior secondary curriculum. It is structured into eight modules, each exploring different principles and approaches within business management. The entire course is taught in English, using a self-study approach with both video and text-based content to facilitate learning.

Upon successful completion, students are eligible to receive a certificate from NIOS and Swayam, which can be a valuable addition to their academic profile.

## What You Will Learn

The program is meticulously designed to build a strong foundation in business management. Key topics covered include:

*   The evolution and scope of business activities.
*   Different structures and forms of business organizations.
*   The core functions of business management, such as planning, organizing, staffing, directing, coordination, and controlling.
*   Understanding various sources of business funding, including long-term finance.
*   Principles of financial management and the Indian financial market.
*   Introduction to marketing, including the marketing mix, advertising, and salesmanship.
*   Concepts of internal and external trade, and crucial aspects of consumer protection.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The Senior Secondary: Business Studies (319) course is delivered through an interactive online platform, providing a rich learning experience. Students can expect:

*   **Duration:** The course spans 24 weeks, allowing for a structured yet flexible learning pace.
*   **Credit Points:** Successful completion awards 10 credit points.
*   **Learning Resources:** Access to comprehensive video lectures and detailed reading materials.
*   **Engagement:** Opportunities for peer discussion through online forums and self-assessment quizzes to test understanding.
*   **Certification:** A certificate of completion jointly issued by NIOS and Swayam, validating your learning.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Senior Secondary: Business Studies (319) course is a straightforward process. Interested candidates should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal where the course is hosted.
2.  Look for the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically located in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the new page that appears, you can either sign in with an existing account or create a new one using your preferred username and password to proceed with the enrollment.

There are no specific course or certificate fees mentioned for this program, making it an accessible option for many students.

## Detailed Syllabus

The course is structured week-by-week to ensure a logical progression through the subject matter:

*   **Weeks 1-7:** Nature, Scope, and Forms of Business Organization, including Public Sector Enterprises.
*   **Weeks 8-9:** Self-Employment and Readiness for Wage Employment.
*   **Weeks 10-14:** Fundamentals of Management, encompassing Planning, Organizing, Staffing, Directing, Co-ordination, and Controlling.
*   **Weeks 15-20:** Financing of Business, Sources of Long-Term Finance, Financial Management, and the Indian Financial Market.
*   **Weeks 21-26:** Introduction to Marketing, Marketing Mix, Advertisement, Salesmanship, Internal and External Trade, and Consumer Protection.

This course is ideal for anyone looking to build a foundational understanding of business and management, whether for academic advancement or future career aspirations.
