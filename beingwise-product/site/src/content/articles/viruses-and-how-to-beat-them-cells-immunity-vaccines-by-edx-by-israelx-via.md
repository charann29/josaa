# Viruses and How to Beat them: Cells, Immunity, Vaccines

Understanding viruses, how they affect our bodies, and how we fight them is more crucial than ever. This course offers an accessible dive into the fascinating world of microbiology, immunity, and vaccine development.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an introductory, self-paced online course, requiring about 2-3 hours of study per week over seven weeks.
*   It covers fundamental concepts in virology, immunology, and cell biology, ideal for anyone curious about how our bodies battle infections.
*   While the course content is free to access, an official, instructor-signed certificate from Tel Aviv University (via IsraelX) can be obtained for a fee.
*   No prior scientific background is needed, making it suitable for a wide range of learners.

## Course Overview

This certification program is designed to educate students on viral diseases and the ingenious ways our immune systems respond to them. You'll explore the core concepts of viruses, cells, and immunity, and gain insight into the development of vaccines. The seven-week curriculum focuses on medical science without requiring any prerequisites, making it open to anyone interested in understanding how viruses attack and how our bodies defend themselves.

The course delves into basic cell formation, cell duplication, and DNA replication, providing a solid foundation in fundamental biology. It also examines significant epidemics like Ebola and AIDS in detail, offering historical and scientific context. A key aspect is learning to identify viral threats and understanding the intricacies of our own immune system. The curriculum integrates basic biology, immunology, and virology to ensure a comprehensive learning experience.

## What You Will Learn

Through this program, you will develop a deeper understanding of microbiology and enhance your skills in several key areas:

*   **Cell Biology Fundamentals:** Learn about the basic processes of cell formation, structure, and duplication, crucial for understanding how viruses spread.
*   **Genetic Concepts:** Explore DNA replication and the genetic code to grasp the origins and mechanisms of viral infections.
*   **Vaccination Science:** Master the domain of vaccination, understanding different types of vaccines and the role of toxins in their development.
*   **Immune System Response:** Gain detailed knowledge about how the immune system identifies and fights off pathogens.
*   **Pandemics and Public Health:** Study major pandemics of recent decades, understanding their impact and the scientific efforts to combat them.

## Course Structure and Commitment

The course is delivered entirely online and is self-paced, allowing you to fit learning into your schedule. It involves video lectures and text-based materials, all in English. You should plan to dedicate approximately 2-3 hours per week to the course content, including assignments. This introductory level course is partnered by Tel Aviv University, Israel, ensuring a high standard of academic content.

## Fees and Certification

Access to the learning materials for this course is free. However, if you wish to receive an official, instructor-signed certificate upon completion, there is a fee. As of the latest information, the fee for the certificate is ₹12,064. This certificate is provided by IsraelX, an initiative of Tel Aviv University.
