# VITBEE Application Form 2026: Check Registration Date, Fees & How to Apply

Planning your MBA journey? The VIT Business Entrance Exam (VITBEE) is an important step for admission to MBA programs at VIT. Here's what you need to know about applying for VITBEE 2026.

## Key Takeaways

*   The application window for VITBEE 2026 runs from October 31, 2025, to April 12, 2026.
*   The VITBEE 2026 examination is scheduled for May 3, 2026.
*   Applications must be submitted online through the official VIT admissions portal.
*   The application fee for VITBEE 2026 is INR 1200.

## Important Dates for VITBEE 2026

It's crucial to keep track of key dates to ensure you don't miss any deadlines for your VITBEE application. Here’s a summary of the schedule:

| Event                      | Date                 |
| :------------------------- | :------------------- |
| VITBEE Application Opens   | October 31, 2025     |
| VITBEE Application Deadline| April 12, 2026       |
| VITBEE Exam Date           | May 03, 2026         |

## Essential Documents and Information for Application

Before you begin filling out the online application form for VITBEE 2026, gather all necessary documents and information. This preparation will help ensure a smooth application process. You will need:

*   **Academic Records:** Your Class 10 and 12 board certificates (for date of birth verification), and semester-wise/year-wise/consolidated mark sheets for your undergraduate or integrated degree. If you are awaiting final year results, upload mark sheets up to your pre-final year.
*   **Certificates:** Provisional, degree, transfer, and migration certificates (if already issued).
*   **Category-Specific Certificates:** Community certificate (for SC/ST candidates) and nativity certificate (for candidates from North Eastern states).
*   **Entrance Exam Scores (Optional but Recommended):** Valid scorecards for CAT, XAT, or MAT. If your scores are awaited, you may submit them via email once available.
*   **Identification:** Your Aadhaar Card.

## Step-by-Step Guide to Filling the VITBEE 2026 Application Form

The VITBEE application process is entirely online. Follow these steps carefully to complete your application:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Go to the official VIT admissions website.
2.  **Access the Application:** Look for the VITBEE online application 2026 link on the screen.
3.  **New User Registration:** Click on the 'new user' tab, usually located on the right side of the page.
4.  **Create Login Credentials:** Provide the required details to set up your account. This includes your name, date of birth, nationality, gender, mobile number, email ID (and confirmation), the program you're applying for, and a verification code. After entering these, click 'check' and then 'register'.
5.  **Log In:** Use the credentials you just created to log back into the portal.
6.  **Complete the Application Form:** Fill in all sections of the application form with accurate information.
7.  **Pay Application Fee:** Submit the application fee of INR 1200. This fee is non-refundable.
8.  **Save and Download:** After successful submission and payment, save and download a copy of your completed application form for your records.

## Institutions Accepting VITBEE Scores

VITBEE scores are primarily used for admission to MBA programs within the Vellore Institute of Technology system. The main institutions include:

*   **VIT Business School, Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore:** A private institution offering a 2-year full-time MBA program. The total fees are approximately INR 6.97 Lakhs. Besides VITBEE, it also accepts CAT, XAT, and MAT scores.
*   **VIT Business School, Chennai:** Located on Vandalur Kelambakkam Road, Chennai, this is another private institution under VIT. It also offers an MBA program and accepts scores from CAT, XAT, MAT, VITBEE, and TANCET.

Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for your chosen campus and program before applying.

Completing your application accurately and on time is vital for your MBA aspirations at VIT. Good luck!
