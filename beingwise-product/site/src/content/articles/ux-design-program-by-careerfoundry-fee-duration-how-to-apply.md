# UX Design Program by CareerFoundry: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

User Experience (UX) design is a rapidly growing field, and this comprehensive online program by CareerFoundry aims to equip aspiring designers with the skills needed to thrive. This article provides a detailed overview of the program, covering its structure, costs, and application process.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is a 10-month, fully online course designed to make you job-ready in UX design.
*   It offers a project-based curriculum, allowing you to build a professional portfolio.
*   Students receive dedicated support from a personal tutor, mentor, and career specialist.
*   The program requires approximately 15-20 hours of study per week and B2 level English proficiency.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

The CareerFoundry UX Design Program is structured as a 10-month online course, focusing on practical, job-ready skills. It emphasizes a project-based learning approach, ensuring that by the end of the program, you will have a strong portfolio showcasing your abilities.

Throughout the course, you'll delve into various aspects of UX design, starting with foundational concepts and progressing to understanding user needs, and mastering the end-to-end design process. A unique feature is the ability to choose a specialization track after completing the initial two modules, allowing you to tailor your learning to specific areas of interest within UX.

While the program offers flexibility for self-paced learning, it incorporates regular deadlines to help keep you on track. Upon completion, you'll receive a certificate from CareerFoundry and benefit from personalized coaching from their career services team, aimed at preparing you for job interviews and helping you secure a role as a UX designer.

## Curriculum Highlights

The program's syllabus is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of UX design, from theory to practical application. Key modules include:

*   **Intro to UX Design**: This module covers fundamental concepts such as design thinking, user research, creating user personas, information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.
*   **UX Immersion: Thinking like a UXer**: Here, you'll explore the role of a UX designer, the history of UX, human needs and motivations in design, the user-centered design process, and competitive analysis.

By completing these modules and the subsequent specialization track, you will learn to apply various processes, skills, and tools essential for a UX designer. The program aims to empower you to build an impressive portfolio and successfully land a job in the field.

## Fees and Eligibility

The total fee for the UX Design Program is $7,900. This amount covers all course materials, mentor support, and career services.

To be eligible for the program and to gain the most benefit, it is recommended that applicants possess a B2 level proficiency in both written and spoken English. Additionally, access to a stable internet connection and a personal computer (either PC or Mac) is required for participation in the online modules and activities.

## How to Apply

The application process for the CareerFoundry UX Design Program is straightforward and can be completed online:

1.  **Visit the Program Page**: Navigate to the official UX Design program page on the CareerFoundry website.
2.  **Initiate Enrollment**: Look for the option to "Get in touch with us" or "Enroll Now" on the webpage.
3.  **Create an Account**: You will be directed to an account creation page where you need to provide essential details such as your full name, email ID, and a password. You will also need to accept the terms and conditions.
4.  **Select Payment Plan**: After creating your account, you can choose your preferred payment plan for the program.
5.  **Complete Payment**: Enter your payment details to finalize the transaction. Once the payment is processed, your enrollment will be successful.

This structured approach ensures a smooth transition into the program, allowing you to quickly begin your journey into the world of UX design.

Embarking on a UX design career through this program offers a robust foundation and dedicated support to help you achieve your professional goals.
