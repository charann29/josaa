# Can I Use My 12th Board Merit to Get Admission into UPES Dehradun for BBA-MBA Integrated Course?

Yes, you can absolutely use your Class 12th board examination results to seek admission into the integrated BBA-MBA program at UPES Dehradun, provided you meet their specific academic criteria.

## Key Takeaways

*   A minimum of 50% marks in both Class 10 and Class 12 is essential for eligibility.
*   Direct admission based purely on board merit is possible if you achieve 75% or higher in both Class 10 and Class 12.
*   Alternatively, you can gain admission through scores from entrance exams like UPESMET-UG, UGAT, or CUET.
*   The application process is conducted online via the official UPES admissions portal.

## Understanding the BBA-MBA Integrated Program

The integrated BBA-MBA course at UPES Dehradun is a comprehensive five-year program designed for students aspiring to build a strong foundation in business administration followed by advanced management studies. This program typically offers specializations in key areas such as Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, and Operations, allowing students to delve deeper into their chosen field of interest.

## Eligibility and Admission Routes

To be considered for the BBA-MBA integrated program, all applicants must satisfy the fundamental eligibility criteria: a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in both their Class 10 and Class 12 board examinations from a recognized educational board. Beyond this basic requirement, UPES offers multiple pathways for admission:

### Board Merit-Based Admission

For students who have excelled academically in their secondary and higher secondary education, UPES provides an opportunity for direct admission based on board merit. If you have secured 75% or more marks in both your Class 10 and Class 12 board exams, you may be eligible for this route. This option recognizes and rewards consistent high academic performance.

### Entrance Exam-Based Admission

For those who do not meet the higher board merit percentage or prefer to demonstrate their aptitude through competitive examinations, UPES accepts scores from several recognized entrance tests. These include:

*   **UPESMET-UG:** This is the university's own entrance examination for undergraduate programs.
*   **UGAT:** The Undergraduate Aptitude Test.
*   **CUET:** The Common University Entrance Test, conducted by NTA.

Performance in these entrance exams plays a significant role in the selection process, particularly if you are not opting for the direct board merit route.

## The Application and Selection Process

The application for the BBA-MBA integrated program is an online process, accessible through the official UPES admissions portal. After submitting your application, candidates who meet the initial criteria, whether through board merit or entrance exam scores, may be shortlisted. Shortlisted candidates are typically invited to participate in a Group Discussion (GD) followed by a Personal Interview (PI). These stages are crucial for assessing a candidate's communication skills, critical thinking, and overall suitability for the program.

Your 12th board marks are certainly a valuable asset for gaining admission to the BBA-MBA integrated course at UPES Dehradun, especially if you have achieved a high percentage, alongside other potential admission routes.
