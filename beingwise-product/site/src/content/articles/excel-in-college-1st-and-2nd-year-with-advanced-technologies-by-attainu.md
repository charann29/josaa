# Excel in College 1st & 2nd Year with Advanced Technologies by AttainU: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For college students in their initial years, gaining practical skills in advanced technologies can provide a significant edge. This program is designed to equip first and second-year college students with in-demand technical and soft skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   A six-month, live online program focusing on advanced technologies.
*   Includes training in web development, programming, and emerging fields like Web 3.0.
*   Offers career-oriented workshops, placement preparation, and post-training internship support.
*   Flexible payment options, including EMI, make the program accessible.

## Program Overview and Structure

This intensive six-month certification is delivered through live, interactive online sessions, primarily held on weekends. The curriculum combines self-study modules with virtual classroom experiences, using video and text-based materials. Daily assessments and workshops are integrated to ensure continuous learning and preparation for future career opportunities.

Beyond technical proficiencies, the program also emphasizes crucial soft skills and an introduction to entrepreneurship. Students receive six months of internship support after completing the training. Mentors are available during live web development sessions to address any questions or difficulties, ensuring a supportive learning environment.

## What You Will Learn

The program's syllabus is comprehensive, covering fundamental programming skills and delving into advanced technological concepts. Key areas of study include:

*   **Programming and Problem Solving using C:** This foundational module covers topics from an introduction to programming, arithmetic expressions, conditional branching, loops, arrays, basic algorithms, functions, recursion, structures, and pointers.
*   **Web Development:** Essential skills for building modern websites and applications.
*   **Game Development:** An introduction to the principles and practices of game creation.
*   **Web 3.0 Technologies:** Exploring cutting-edge concepts like Blockchain, Cryptocurrency, and the Metaverse.
*   **Soft Skills:** Training to enhance communication, teamwork, and professional conduct.
*   **Entrepreneurship Program:** Insights into starting and managing ventures.
*   **Career Building Skills:** Practical advice and strategies for career advancement.

This curriculum is particularly suitable for students aspiring to careers as web developers or game designers.

## Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Excel in College 1st & 2nd Year with Advanced Technologies certification course is ₹44,000, inclusive of GST. Additionally, there is a one-time registration fee of ₹3,700.

Students have flexible payment options:

*   The full fee can be paid in three installments.
*   An EMI facility is available, allowing payments over 12 months at approximately ₹3,355 per month.

### Fee Breakdown

| Description             | Amount (INR) |
| :---------------------- | :----------- |
| Total Course Fee        | ₹44,000      |
| Registration Fee        | ₹3,700       |
| Remaining Fee (12-month EMI) | ₹3,355/month |

## Eligibility and Admission Process

The program is open to college students currently in their first, second, third, or fourth year of study. There are no specific academic prerequisites beyond current college enrollment.

To apply for admission, follow these steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Access the dedicated admission link provided by the program. (Note: Always use official links for application.)
2.  **Pay Registration Fee:** Secure your seat by paying the non-refundable registration amount.
3.  **EMI Facility (if opting):** If you choose the EMI option, submit the necessary documents to the associated bank for loan approval.
4.  **Confirm Admission:** Once the loan is approved (if applicable) and fees are settled, your admission will be confirmed, and you can begin the program.

This program offers a valuable opportunity for college students to proactively build a strong foundation in advanced technologies, enhancing their future career prospects.
