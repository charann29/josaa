# Best Free Human Resource Management Courses & Certifications - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Career Options

Human Resources (HR) is a vital function in any organisation, focusing on managing its most valuable asset: its people. If you're looking to start a career in HR or enhance your existing skills without a financial burden, several excellent free online courses are available.

## Key takeaways

*   **Accessible Learning:** The Indian government's Swayam platform offers multiple free HR courses from reputable institutions like IGNOU, Gujarat University, and IIT Roorkee.
*   **Diverse Focus Areas:** Courses cover core HR management, HR for non-HR professionals, HR development systems, and talent acquisition.
*   **Skill Enhancement:** Participants can gain practical insights into employee relations, compensation, staffing, training, and legal compliance.
*   **Career Advancement:** These certifications can significantly boost your resume, helping you start or advance your career in the dynamic field of Human Resource Management.

## Free Core Human Resource Management Course

**MS 2: Management of Human Resources**

This comprehensive program is offered through Swayam by IGNOU, making it an excellent choice for anyone interested in a career in Human Resource Management without worrying about course fees. It's designed to help you develop a critical understanding of the various responsibilities an HR Manager handles to ensure an organisation runs smoothly.

Upon completing this 16-week course, you will have a deep understanding of key HR Management concepts, common challenges, and effective techniques. The curriculum focuses on four main areas of HR:

*   Employee and Employment Relations
*   Compensation Management
*   Staffing Management
*   Human Resource Development

This course is entirely free and covers essential HR skills.

## HR for Non-HR Managers

**HRM for Non-HR Managers Programme**

Also available on Swayam, this program is offered by the B.K. School of Business Management, Gujarat University, Ahmedabad. It aligns with the government's objective of providing quality education through online platforms.

This 12-week course is ideal for anyone, regardless of their prior HR experience, who wants to understand the fundamentals of Human Resource Management. Often called 'People Management,' this field demands strong communication and interpersonal skills. You will gain insights into core HR processes and functions, including:

*   Conducting employee orientations
*   Ensuring legal compliance for discipline and grievance handling
*   Performing employee surveys to build trust
*   Organising training for new employees

This free course helps bridge the knowledge gap for professionals in other fields who interact with HR functions.

## Human Resource Development: Systems & Strategies

**HRD: Systems & Strategies**

This self-paced, online elective course, also from the B.K. School of Business Management, Gujarat University, Ahmedabad, via Swayam, is offered at no cost. It's designed to be accessible to all students, including those from economically disadvantaged backgrounds, and offers transferable credits.

To earn the certification for this 12-week program, participants need to achieve the required passing marks by submitting assignments on time and clearing a proctored examination at designated centres. The course aims to enhance participants' skills and knowledge, particularly in improving workforce and organisational effectiveness. It also significantly boosts leadership and management qualities.

This free course is beneficial for those looking to start or restart their careers, providing them with methods and techniques to improve organisational performance.

## Talent Acquisition and Management

**Talent Acquisition and Management**

Offered by IIT Roorkee through Swayam, this certification course falls under management studies and is suitable for both graduates and postgraduates. It focuses on the critical processes involved in attracting, selecting, and retaining skilled employees.

This program delves into modern strategies for identifying talent, conducting effective interviews, and managing the onboarding process. It's an excellent resource for anyone aiming to specialise in the recruitment and talent development aspects of HR.

These free courses offer a fantastic opportunity to gain valuable HR knowledge and certifications from reputable Indian institutions, setting you on a path to a successful career in Human Resources.
