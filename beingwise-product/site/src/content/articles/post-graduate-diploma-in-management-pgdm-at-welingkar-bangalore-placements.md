# Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) at Welingkar Bangalore: Placements, Fees, Admission & Eligibility

Welingkar Institute of Management Development and Research, commonly known as Welingkar Bangalore, offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) program designed to equip students with essential management skills. This two-year, full-time postgraduate diploma is a popular choice for those aiming for a career in business and management.

## Key Takeaways

*   The PGDM program at Welingkar Bangalore is a two-year, full-time diploma. 
*   The total tuition fee for the program is ₹14.00 Lakh.
*   The average placement package for PGDM graduates stands at ₹11.00 Lakh per annum.
*   Admission requires a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks and a valid score in competitive exams like CAT, XAT, ATMA, CMAT, or GMAT.

## Program Overview and Fees

The Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) at Welingkar Bangalore is a comprehensive two-year program. It is structured to provide a strong foundation in various aspects of management, preparing students for diverse roles in the corporate world. The institute admits a limited number of students, with a total seat intake of 60 for this program.

The total tuition fee for the entire two-year PGDM course is ₹14.00 Lakh. It's important for prospective students to consider this fee when planning their postgraduate education.

## Admission and Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the PGDM program at Welingkar Bangalore, candidates must meet specific academic requirements and clear a competitive entrance examination. The general eligibility criteria are:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in their graduation is typically required.
*   **Entrance Exams:** Candidates need to appear for and secure a valid score in one of the following management entrance tests: CAT, XAT, ATMA, CMAT, or GMAT. The scores from these exams are a crucial part of the admission process.

Beyond these initial criteria, the admission process usually involves subsequent stages such as group discussions and personal interviews to assess a candidate's overall profile, communication skills, and suitability for the program.

## Placements: Career Opportunities and Salary Trends

Welingkar Bangalore has a dedicated placement cell that works to connect students with leading companies across various sectors. The institute's location in Bangalore, a major economic hub, often provides an advantage in terms of placement opportunities.

Based on official reports, the average package offered to PGDM graduates from Welingkar Bangalore is ₹11.00 Lakh per annum. This figure indicates the general earning potential for students completing the program.

## Rankings and Reputation

Welingkar Bangalore, established in 1977, is a private institute that has built a reputation in management education. The institute's consistent efforts in academic delivery and industry engagement contribute to its standing among management schools. While specific rankings can vary by publication, Welingkar Bangalore is often recognized for its management programs. Aspiring students can refer to official NIRF rankings or other credible bodies for detailed ranking information.

Considering a PGDM at Welingkar Bangalore means looking at a program that blends academic rigor with practical industry exposure, aiming to prepare you for a successful management career.
