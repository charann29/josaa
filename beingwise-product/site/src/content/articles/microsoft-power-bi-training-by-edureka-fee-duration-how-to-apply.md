# Microsoft Power BI Training by Edureka: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This training program is designed to introduce you to Microsoft Power BI, a powerful business intelligence tool. It covers essential aspects of data analysis and visualization, helping you develop skills crucial for today's data-driven roles.

## Key takeaways

*   The program offers flexible learning options: a 21-day weekday schedule or a 7-week weekend format, both featuring live online instructor-led sessions.
*   Participants gain lifetime access to online learning materials and round-the-clock technical support.
*   The curriculum includes real-life case studies and practical assignments to ensure hands-on experience.
*   Upon successful completion of a project, you will receive a "Business Analyst using Power BI" certification from Edureka.

## Course Overview

The Microsoft Power BI Training course provides a comprehensive introduction to Power BI Desktop and its integration with Power Pivot for data modeling. You'll learn about data visualization, Data Analysis Expressions (DAX), and how to explore and visualize enterprise data using natural language queries with Power BI Q&A.

Key topics covered include core Business Intelligence concepts, the Power BI workflow, creating matrixes and tables, and implementing row-level security. By the end of the program, you will be proficient in data integration and visualization, utilizing content packs, data gateways, and the Power BI Report Server.

## Learning Experience and Support

The program includes 27 hours of live online instructor-led classes. For weekend batches, these are structured as nine 3-hour sessions. A significant benefit is lifetime access to all online resources and continuous 24x7 expert technical support, ensuring you always have assistance when needed. The learning approach emphasizes practical application through regular assignments and case studies based on real-world scenarios.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Microsoft Power BI Training program is ₹17,999. EMI options are also available, starting at ₹6,000.

Upon successful completion of the course and a required project, Edureka will award a "Business Analyst using Power BI" certification. The project is designed to assess your ability to apply the concepts learned during the program. You must submit your project within 15 days of course completion for evaluation by Edureka's experts.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

There are no strict prerequisites for enrolling in this Microsoft Power BI Training course. However, having some prior experience with Microsoft Excel and Python can be beneficial and may contribute to a smoother learning experience. The course is particularly well-suited for professionals looking to enhance their skills in data analytics, data visualization, and business intelligence.

### Who Should Enroll?

This program is ideal for a range of professionals, including:

*   Project Managers
*   Statisticians and Analysts
*   Business Analysts
*   Business Intelligence Managers
*   Data Scientists

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Microsoft Power BI Training program, interested candidates should visit the official Edureka website and follow the registration instructions provided there.
