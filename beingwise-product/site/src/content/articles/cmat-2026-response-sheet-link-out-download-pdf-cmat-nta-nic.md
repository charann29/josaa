# CMAT 2026 Response Sheet Link (OUT): Download PDF @cmat.nta.nic.in

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has officially released the Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) 2026 response sheet, which includes the provisional answer key. This crucial document allows candidates to review their attempted answers against the official correct solutions and calculate their probable scores.

## Key Takeaways

*   The CMAT 2026 response sheet and provisional answer key were made available on January 31, 2026.
*   Candidates can download this document as a PDF from the official NTA CMAT website by logging in with their credentials.
*   The response sheet enables self-evaluation of performance by comparing marked answers with the official key.
*   A window for raising objections against the provisional answer key is open from January 31 to February 2, 2026, with a fee of INR 200 per challenged question.

## Understanding the CMAT 2026 Response Sheet and Answer Key

The CMAT 2026 response sheet is a comprehensive document that presents both the questions asked in the examination and the answers you selected. Alongside this, the provisional answer key provides the officially recognized correct answers for each question. This combination is invaluable for candidates to get a clear picture of their performance immediately after the exam.

The CMAT 2026 examination was conducted on January 25 in a single shift. Experts generally considered the paper to be of moderate difficulty. Having access to your responses and the official answers helps in understanding where you might have scored well and areas that might need further review.

## How to Download Your CMAT 2026 Response Sheet

Accessing your response sheet and provisional answer key is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to download your PDF:

1.  Visit the official CMAT 2026 website.
2.  Look for and click on the 'CMAT answer key 2026 objection' tab.
3.  You will be prompted to enter your CMAT application ID and password.
4.  Once logged in, your CMAT answer key and response sheet will be displayed on the screen.
5.  Locate the download icon and save the document as a PDF for future reference.

It's highly recommended to download and save your response sheet as soon as it's available, as these links may not remain active indefinitely, especially after the final results are declared.

## Calculating Your CMAT Score

With your response sheet and the provisional answer key in hand, you can accurately estimate your CMAT 2026 score. The scoring pattern for CMAT is typically as follows:

*   **+4 marks** for every correct answer.
*   **-1 mark** for every incorrect answer.
*   No marks deducted for unattempted questions.

To calculate your score, count the total number of correct answers and multiply by four. Then, count the total number of incorrect answers and multiply by one. Subtract the negative total from the positive total to arrive at your estimated score. This self-assessment provides a good indication of your performance before the official results are published.

## Challenging the Provisional Answer Key

NTA provides a specific window for candidates to challenge any question in the provisional answer key if they believe an official answer is incorrect. This year, the objection window is from January 31 to February 2, 2026. A fee of INR 200 is applicable for each question challenged. It is crucial to provide strong justification and supporting evidence for your challenge.

To raise an objection:

1.  Log in to the official NTA CMAT website using your application number and password.
2.  Navigate to the answer key objection section.
3.  Select the question(s) you wish to challenge.
4.  Provide your justification and upload any necessary supporting documents.
5.  Pay the required fee per question online.

NTA will review all submitted objections. If an objection is found to be valid, the answer key will be revised, and the final answer key will be used for result compilation.

Accessing and reviewing your CMAT 2026 response sheet is a vital step in your post-exam process, offering clarity on your performance and the opportunity to engage with the evaluation process if needed.
