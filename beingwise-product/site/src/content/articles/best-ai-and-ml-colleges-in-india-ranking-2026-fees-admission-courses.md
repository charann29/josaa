# Best AI & ML Colleges in India: Ranking 2026, Fees, Admission, Courses, Placements

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) are rapidly evolving fields at the forefront of technological innovation. Pursuing a degree in these disciplines can open doors to exciting career opportunities. India offers a wide array of institutions providing quality education in AI and ML, from undergraduate to postgraduate levels.

## Key takeaways

*   Over 1,700 institutions across India offer AI and ML programs, with the majority being privately owned.
*   Top entrance exams for B.Tech programs include JEE Main and TNEA, while GATE is crucial for M.Tech admissions.
*   Leading institutes like IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, and VIT Vellore are prominent choices for AI and ML studies.
*   Annual fees for these programs vary significantly, with options available across all budget ranges.

## Understanding AI & ML Programs in India

AI and ML are interconnected branches of computer science focused on creating intelligent systems that can learn and adapt. Many colleges nationwide now offer specialized B.Tech and M.Tech programs in these areas. These courses delve into simulating human intelligence through machines, making advancements like robotic systems and sophisticated data analysis possible.

Approximately 1,700 colleges in India provide AI and ML education. Of these, over 1,400 are private institutions, while more than 160 are government-owned, and a smaller number (around 15) are semi-government bodies.

## Admission Process and Key Entrance Exams

Admission to AI and ML programs typically involves competitive entrance examinations. For undergraduate (B.Tech) courses, major exams include JEE Main, conducted by NTA, and various state-level tests like TNEA (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions). For postgraduate (M.Tech) programs, the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is a primary requirement.

Prospective students should regularly check the official JoSAA portal for joint seat allocation information and the specific college websites for detailed admission criteria, application deadlines, and exam schedules for the upcoming academic year.

## Top AI & ML Institutions in India

Several institutions consistently rank among the best for AI and ML education. These colleges are known for their strong academic programs, research opportunities, and placement records. Some of the highly regarded institutions include:

*   IIT Bombay
*   IIT Delhi
*   IIT Madras
*   IIT Kharagpur
*   IIT Hyderabad
*   IIT Roorkee
*   Delhi Technological University (DTU)
*   Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT Vellore)
*   National Institute of Technology (NIT) Surathkal
*   National Institute of Technology (NIT) Warangal
*   National Institute of Technology (NIT) Rourkela

These institutions are often featured in prestigious rankings, such as the NIRF rankings, for their engineering and technology programs.

## Fee Structure and Placement Prospects

The annual fees for AI and ML programs in India vary widely based on the institution type (government, private) and its ranking. Students can find options ranging from less than INR 1 lakh per year to over INR 5 lakh per year. Approximately 90+ colleges have fees under INR 1 lakh, while over 400 colleges charge more than INR 5 lakh annually. A significant number of colleges fall within the INR 2-3 lakh and INR 3-5 lakh brackets.

Graduates from top AI and ML programs often secure excellent placements with competitive salaries. The demand for skilled professionals in these fields is consistently high across various industries, including IT, finance, healthcare, and research and development.

Choosing the right AI and ML program requires careful consideration of academic rigor, faculty expertise, infrastructure, and career support services offered by each institution.
