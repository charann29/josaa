# SNAP 2025 Exam Day Guideline: Know Important Dos and Don’ts

The SNAP exam is a crucial step for aspiring MBA students hoping to join Symbiosis International (Deemed) University. To ensure a smooth experience on test day, it's essential to be fully aware of all guidelines and instructions. This guide will walk you through the key dos and don'ts for SNAP 2025, helping you avoid common pitfalls and focus solely on your performance.

## Key Takeaways

*   Arrive at your test center well in advance of the official reporting time to complete all pre-exam formalities.
*   Always carry a color printout of your SNAP 2025 admit card along with a valid, original photo ID.
*   Strictly adhere to all identification and security procedures, including biometric data capture.
*   Be aware of prohibited items and ensure you do not bring any unauthorized materials into the examination hall.

## Essential Items to Carry

When heading to your SNAP 2025 examination center, ensure you have the following vital documents and items:

*   **SNAP 2025 Admit Card:** A clear, color printout of your admit card on A4 size paper is mandatory. This document is your pass to enter the examination hall.
*   **Valid Photo Identification:** You must present one original, government-issued photo ID for verification. Acceptable IDs include:
    *   Driving License
    *   Passport
    *   PAN Card
    *   Voter ID
    *   Aadhaar Card
    *   *Note: The photo ID will be returned to you after verification.*
*   **Ballpoint Pen:** A simple black or blue ballpoint pen is needed for signing the attendance sheet and any rough work, if required.
*   **Name Change Documents (If applicable):** If your name has changed, you must bring original supporting documents such as a Marriage Certificate, Divorce Decree, or Legal Name Change document.

## Reporting Time and Entry Procedures

Timeliness is critical on exam day. The SNAP 2025 exam is scheduled from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM. Candidates are advised to reach their test centers by 12:00 PM. No candidate will be permitted to enter the examination center after 2:00 PM under any circumstances. Upon arrival, cooperate fully with the test center staff during the frisking and identification process. This includes biometric data capture (image and fingerprint) before you proceed to your designated seat.

## Prohibited Items and Conduct

To maintain the integrity of the examination, certain items are strictly forbidden inside the test center. Bringing these items can lead to disqualification. Avoid carrying:

*   Mobile phones, smartwatches, or any electronic gadgets.
*   Calculators, log tables, or any other stationery apart from the specified pen.
*   Notebooks, loose papers, or study materials.
*   Any metallic accessories, caps, headgears, or outfits with multiple pockets and hoodies.
*   Closed shoes (it's often recommended to wear open footwear like sandals or slippers).
*   Bags, valuables, or any food items.

Once seated, carefully read all instructions displayed on the screen before beginning the test. If you encounter any technical issues with your workstation, immediately report it to the invigilator.

## During the Exam

Maintain silence and focus solely on your test. Do not engage in any form of communication with other candidates or attempt to use unfair means. Your SNAP admit card will be collected for verification during the test, along with your photo ID. After verification, your ID will be returned to you. Ensure you follow all instructions given by the invigilators.

By following these guidelines, you can ensure a stress-free and fair examination experience for SNAP 2025.
