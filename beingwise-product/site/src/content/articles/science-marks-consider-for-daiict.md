# 12th Science Marks for DAIICT Admissions: What You Need to Know

When aspiring for a B.Tech degree at DAIICT (Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology), your JEE Main performance is undoubtedly crucial. However, your academic record from Class 12, especially in science subjects, also plays a significant role in determining your eligibility.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your Class 12 board marks are essential for fulfilling the basic eligibility criteria for DAIICT's B.Tech programs.
*   A specific combination of science subjects, including Physics and Mathematics, is mandatory.
*   For the All India category, DAIICT does not impose a minimum percentage requirement in Class 12, but you must have successfully passed.
*   Eligibility criteria can differ slightly for Gujarat state domicile candidates, so it's wise to check the official DAIICT admissions brochure.

## Understanding the Subject Requirements

To be considered for admission to DAIICT's B.Tech programs, candidates must have successfully completed their Class 12 (or equivalent) examination with a specific set of subjects. The core subjects are Physics and Mathematics. In addition to these, you must have studied one of the following subjects:

*   Chemistry
*   Computer Science
*   Biology
*   Biotechnology

This ensures that admitted students have a foundational understanding across key scientific disciplines relevant to engineering and technology studies.

## The Role of Your Class 12 Percentage

It's a common misconception that a very high Class 12 percentage is always required for top engineering institutes. For DAIICT, specifically for the All India category, the official eligibility criteria state that there is no minimum percentage cut-off for your Class 12 examination. The primary requirement is that you must have passed your Class 12 board exams with the mandatory subject combination.

While a higher percentage might reflect strong academic performance, it's your JEE Main rank that primarily drives the merit-based selection process. Your Class 12 marks serve as a gateway, confirming you meet the foundational academic prerequisites.

## Special Considerations for Gujarat State Candidates

DAIICT, being located in Gujarat, also reserves a certain percentage of seats for candidates with Gujarat state domicile. The eligibility criteria for these seats, particularly regarding Class 12 marks and subject combinations, might have slight variations compared to the All India category. It is always recommended to refer to the latest official DAIICT admissions brochure or the official Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC) guidelines for Gujarat state candidates to get the most accurate and up-to-date information.

## The Overall Admission Process

Admission to DAIICT's B.Tech programs primarily relies on your performance in the JEE Main examination. Once you meet the Class 12 subject and passing criteria, your All India Rank (AIR) from JEE Main is used for merit listing. The counselling process, often managed through platforms like the official JoSAA portal for All India seats, then allocates seats based on ranks and candidate preferences.

In essence, while your Class 12 marks open the door by confirming your eligibility, your JEE Main score is what truly drives your chances of securing a seat at DAIICT.
