# VIVO Healthcare Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online VIVO Healthcare Courses

VIVO Healthcare offers a range of professional certificate programs designed to equip individuals with essential skills for various roles in the healthcare sector. These courses combine theoretical knowledge with practical training, preparing students for immediate employment.

## Key takeaways

*   VIVO Healthcare provides job-focused certificate courses in high-demand allied healthcare fields.
*   Programs blend classroom learning, simulations, and hands-on internships for comprehensive skill development.
*   All courses include certification upon successful completion, often in collaboration with NSDC.
*   Placement assistance and education loan support are typically available for enrolled students.

## Emergency Medical Technician-Paramedic

This 14-month program, spanning 1800 hours of intensive learning, is designed to transform aspirants into skilled Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) professionals. The curriculum focuses on advanced paramedic treatment and also incorporates managerial skills to lead rescue operations effectively. The course structure includes theoretical classes, simulated scenarios, and practical internships.

### What you will learn:

*   Patient assessment techniques
*   Handling trauma emergencies
*   Ambulance operations and safety protocols
*   Specialized care for infants and children
*   Administering out-of-hospital medications
*   Leadership skills for rescue teams

Upon successful completion, students receive a certification and benefit from placement support, helping them launch or advance their careers in emergency medical services. VIVO Healthcare also assists with financing options through tie-ups with education loan providers.

## Radiology Technician

The Radiology Technician certification program addresses the significant demand for skilled professionals capable of operating advanced medical imaging equipment. This 14-month course integrates practical and theoretical lessons, focusing on the safe and effective use of various diagnostic tools.

### Key areas of training:

*   Operating Computed Tomography (CT) scanners
*   Using Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) scanners
*   Proficiency with X-Ray machines and other radiographic equipment
*   Producing high-quality diagnostic images
*   Adhering to radiation safety measures in healthcare settings

Graduates receive a certification from VIVO/NSDC, enabling them to secure positions as Junior X-Ray Technicians. This role serves as a stepping stone towards becoming a Radiology Technician in Charge, offering a stable career path with consistent salary prospects in various healthcare facilities.

## Dialysis Technician

This 12-month training course, comprising 800 hours of theory and simulation, is tailored to train allied healthcare professionals in the precise operation and maintenance of dialysis machines and related equipment. The program focuses on practical skills required for performing hemodialysis (HD) sessions under the guidance of a Nephrologist.

### Course structure and evaluation:

*   **Theory and Simulation:** In-depth learning about dialysis procedures and equipment.
*   **Patient Monitoring:** Skills to observe both the patient and the machine during dialysis.
*   **Assessments:** Regular evaluations including weekly tests, module-based exams, a pre-internship exam, and a final foundation exam to track progress.

Successful completion leads to a certification from VIVO/NSDC, validating the newly acquired skills and opening doors to employment opportunities in dialysis centers and hospitals.

## Medical Laboratory Technician

The Medical Laboratory Technician course is designed for individuals aspiring to play a critical role in diagnostics. Medical laboratory technicians are indispensable in hospitals, blood banks, and diagnostic laboratories. This program trains students to perform various diagnostic tests using fluid and tissue samples.

### Core competencies developed:

*   Collecting and handling biological samples (fluid and tissue)
*   Performing a wide array of diagnostic tests
*   Analyzing and interpreting test results accurately
*   Ensuring quality control in laboratory procedures

This certification equips graduates with the essential skills to contribute significantly to patient diagnosis and treatment planning.

These VIVO Healthcare programs provide structured pathways for individuals to enter the growing healthcare industry with specialized skills and recognized certifications.
