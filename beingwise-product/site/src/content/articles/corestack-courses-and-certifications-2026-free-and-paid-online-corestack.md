# CoreStack Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online CoreStack Courses

CoreStack offers a selection of online courses and certifications designed to help individuals build crucial skills in rapidly evolving technology domains like data science and cloud computing. These programs are structured to provide practical knowledge and hands-on experience.

## Key Takeaways

*   CoreStack courses focus on practical skills in data science and AWS cloud platforms.
*   Programs are generally short, around 20 hours, making them suitable for quick upskskilling.
*   Learners benefit from access to specialized tools like Jupyter, RStudio, and limited AWS environments.
*   All courses include 24-hour support to assist learners with any challenges they might face.

## Data Science with Python

Python has become a cornerstone in the field of data science due to its clear design and readability, allowing complex ideas to be expressed with less code compared to other programming languages. The CoreStack course, delivered via FutureSkills Prime, is specifically designed to introduce students to the methods and techniques used in data science operations, all through the lens of Python.

This 20-hour program covers both foundational and advanced principles. A key feature of the course is the use of Jupyter notebooks, which enables participants to create and launch Python and PySpark notebooks for practical application. The curriculum also delves into Power BI for data visualization and analysis. Additionally, the course provides 24-hour support to ensure learners receive prompt assistance with any queries or difficulties.

## Data Science with R

For those interested in another powerful language for data analysis, CoreStack also offers a Data Science with R course. R is widely recognized and utilized in data science and analytics for its accessibility and robust capabilities, making it a preferred choice for many data scientists globally. This program, also available through FutureSkills Prime, targets learners aiming to master data science concepts using the R programming language.

Similar to the Python course, this 20-hour training emphasizes hands-on experience. Participants gain access to RStudio, a popular integrated development environment that facilitates writing and executing R code. The course is structured to provide practical skills in data science using R, complemented by 24-hour support to aid the learning journey.

## AWS Cloud Certifications

CoreStack provides training for essential Amazon Web Services (AWS) certifications, catering to the growing demand for cloud computing professionals. These courses are developed by CoreStack and offered through FutureSkills Prime, giving individuals a pathway to understand and work with one of the leading cloud service providers.

### AWS Certification SysOps Administrator Associate

This certification course is tailored for individuals who want to understand AWS from a systems operations perspective. The program includes over 20 hours of video lectures covering a wide range of AWS services, starting with monitoring tools like CloudWatch and content delivery with CloudFront, then moving to more complex topics such as EC2 (virtual servers), S3 (storage), VPC (networking), RDS (databases), SQS (messaging), and DynamoDB (NoSQL database). A significant benefit is the provision of limited access to AWS services without needing a personal subscription, allowing for hands-on practice. Like other CoreStack offerings, 24-hour support is available.

### AWS Certification Solutions Architect Associate

This course focuses on designing scalable, highly available, and fault-tolerant systems on AWS. It enables learners to immediately begin working with AWS services. This program is ideal for those looking to develop expertise in architecting solutions on the AWS cloud platform.
