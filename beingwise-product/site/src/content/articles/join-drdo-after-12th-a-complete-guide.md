# How to join DRDO after 12th - A Complete Guide

The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) is a cornerstone of India's defence capabilities, renowned for its advanced research and technological innovations. While direct entry as a scientist immediately after Class 12 is not an option, many pathways exist for students to build a fulfilling career within DRDO. This guide will help you understand how to navigate these opportunities after completing your 12th grade.

## Key takeaways

*   Direct entry into DRDO after Class 12 is possible for certain administrative and technical support roles.
*   For scientific and higher technical positions, a bachelor's degree in engineering or science is generally required.
*   A strong foundation in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) in Class 11 and 12 is essential for future scientific careers.
*   Qualifying the GATE examination is a critical step for aspiring scientists in DRDO.

## Can You Join DRDO After 12th?

Yes, joining DRDO after Class 12 is a realistic goal for dedicated students. While scientific roles typically demand higher education, DRDO offers various entry-level positions suitable for candidates with Class 10 or 12 qualifications. These roles provide an excellent opportunity to enter the government sector and contribute to national defence in a supporting capacity.

## Entry-Level Positions in DRDO After 12th

For those looking for early career opportunities, DRDO frequently recruits for administrative and technical support roles. These positions are vital for the smooth functioning of its labs and facilities across the country.

### Admin and Allied Posts (Direct Entry After 12th)

Students who have completed Class 12 can directly apply for various administrative and allied roles. These positions are crucial for providing clerical and logistical support within DRDO. Eligibility criteria, including any specific skills like typing or a valid driving license, are detailed in the official recruitment notifications.

Typical roles include:

*   Administrative Assistant ‘A’
*   Store Assistant ‘A’
*   Security Assistant ‘A’
*   Vehicle Operator ‘A’
*   Fire Engine Driver, Fireman

### Technical Positions (12th with ITI/Diploma/B.Sc)

For those interested in technical fields, DRDO offers roles that combine Class 10 or 12 qualifications with vocational training or a science degree.

#### 1. Technician ‘A’

Candidates with a Class 10 certificate and an Industrial Training Institute (ITI) certificate in a relevant trade can apply for Technician ‘A’ roles. These positions involve the maintenance and operation of equipment in DRDO laboratories.

**Eligibility:**

*   Class 10 or equivalent from a recognised board.
*   ITI certificate in a relevant trade (e.g., Fitter, Electrician, Welder) from a recognised institute.

#### 2. Senior Technical Assistant ‘B’

More advanced technical roles, such as Senior Technical Assistant ‘B’, require higher educational qualifications. These positions come with increased responsibilities and better remuneration.

**Eligibility:**

*   A three-year Engineering Diploma, or a Bachelor's degree in Science (B.Sc) in a relevant discipline.
*   Final year students may also apply, provided they meet all eligibility criteria by the time of final selection.

## Dreaming Bigger? How to Become a Scientist in DRDO After 12th?

If your ambition is to become a scientist at DRDO, a strategic academic plan starting right after Class 12 is essential. This path requires dedication to higher education and specific qualification exams.

### Step-by-Step Roadmap

**Step 1: Choose PCM in Class 11–12**

Your foundation begins with a strong focus on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) in Class 11 and 12. These subjects are fundamental for any engineering or science degree that leads to a scientific career in DRDO.

**Step 2: Earn a B.E./B.Tech or B.Sc Degree**

After Class 12, pursue a bachelor's degree in engineering (B.E./B.Tech) or a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in a discipline relevant to DRDO's work. Popular choices include Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, and various core science streams.

**Step 3: Qualify GATE**

After completing your bachelor's degree, a crucial step is to qualify the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE). GATE scores are often a primary criterion for selection into DRDO's Scientist 'B' positions through the Scientists Entry Test (SET) or direct recruitment.

| Stage                     | Requirement                                            |
| :------------------------ | :----------------------------------------------------- |
| **After Class 10**        | ITI Certificate (for Technician 'A' roles)             |
| **After Class 12**        | Direct entry for Admin/Allied posts                    |
| **After Graduation**      | B.E./B.Tech or B.Sc in a relevant discipline           |
| **Post-Graduation Entry** | Valid GATE score for Scientist 'B' roles               |

Joining DRDO after Class 12, whether in a support role or as a stepping stone to a scientific career, offers a chance to contribute to India's technological advancement and national security.
