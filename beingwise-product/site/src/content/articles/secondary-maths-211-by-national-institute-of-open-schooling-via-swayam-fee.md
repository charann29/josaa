# Secondary: Maths (211) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students looking to strengthen their foundational mathematical skills at the secondary level, the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) offers an online course through the Swayam platform. This program, titled "Secondary: Maths (211)", is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of core mathematical concepts.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 24-week, self-paced online course, offering 10 credit points upon successful completion.
*   The curriculum covers fundamental areas of mathematics, including Algebra, Geometry, and Statistics, through video lectures and text materials.
*   Coordinated by NIOS, the course aims to enhance students' quantitative reasoning and problem-solving abilities.
*   A certificate of completion is awarded by NIOS via Swayam to learners who fulfill all course requirements.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Secondary: Maths (211) online course is a comprehensive program focused on building a strong base in mathematics. It is coordinated by Professor Dr. Rajendra Kumar Nayak from the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS). The learning experience is designed to be highly effective, improving participants' knowledge in both basic quantification and more advanced mathematical ideas.

Spanning 26 lessons, the course is structured into six main modules: Algebra, Commercial Mathematics, Geometry, Mensuration, Trigonometry, and Statistics. Beyond theoretical lessons, it integrates 30 practical mathematical activities covering algebra, geometry, and mensuration. Students will also engage with multiple-choice questions (MCQs) and self-assessment tools to monitor their progress.

## Learning Experience and Materials

This program employs a self-study model, delivered through a blend of video lectures and textual content. Students will have access to a variety of resources to support their learning journey. These include detailed reading materials, engaging video lectures, and opportunities to participate in peer discussion forums. The interactive platform encourages active learning and provides a space for students to clarify doubts and engage with their peers.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing the Secondary: Maths (211) certification classes, participants will gain significant insights and skills across several key mathematical domains. The curriculum is carefully designed to cover all core concepts relevant at the secondary school level, including symbols and various problem-solving approaches in mathematics. Specific topics include:

*   Algebra
*   Commercial Mathematics
*   Geometry
*   Mensuration
*   Trigonometry
*   Statistics

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Secondary: Maths (211) online course is a straightforward process. Interested individuals can access the course through the official Swayam portal. To begin, navigate to the specific course page. On the top right corner of the page, you will find options to "Sign-in" or "Register." If you are a new user, you will need to register by creating a username and password. Existing users can simply sign in to proceed with enrollment.
