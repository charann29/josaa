# List of University Entrance Exam: Dates, Results, Cut-Offs, Merit List, Colleges and Universities

Navigating higher education in India involves understanding various entrance exams that open doors to undergraduate and postgraduate programs. These examinations are crucial for gaining admission to a wide array of central, state, and private universities across the country.

## Key takeaways

*   **Eligibility is foundational:** Always ensure you meet the minimum academic criteria, such as completing Class 12 for undergraduate programs or a bachelor's degree for postgraduate studies.
*   **Diverse opportunities:** Entrance exams cater to various fields, from general arts and sciences to specialized areas like agriculture and integrated science programs.
*   **Merit-based admissions:** Your performance in these entrance exams, combined with qualifying exam merit and reservation policies, largely determines your admission chances.
*   **Top universities await:** Strong scores can lead to admission to prestigious institutions, enhancing future employment and learning prospects.

## Understanding the Common University Entrance Test (CUET)

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) has become a pivotal examination for aspiring undergraduate and postgraduate students. Conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), CUET streamlines admissions to numerous central, state, and even some private universities throughout India.

### CUET UG

For undergraduate programs like BA, B.Sc, B.Com, and BCA, the CUET UG is essential. It serves as the gateway to prominent central universities such as Delhi University, Jawaharlal Nehru University, Banaras Hindu University, Jamia Millia Islamia, and the University of Hyderabad. After results are declared by NTA, participating universities conduct their own counselling processes based on CUET UG scores.

### CUET PG

Similarly, the CUET PG facilitates admissions to various postgraduate programs across approximately 203 universities nationwide. Candidates must meet the specified eligibility criteria for their chosen PG courses to appear for this exam.

## Specialized Entrance Exams for Science and Research

Beyond the general university admissions, several specialized exams cater to specific fields, particularly in science and research.

### IIT JAM (Joint Admission Test for M.Sc.)

The Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) conduct IIT JAM annually. This national-level examination is for admission to various postgraduate science programs, including M.Sc. (Two Years), Joint M.Sc.-Ph.D., M.Sc.-Ph.D. Dual Degree, and M.Sc.-M.Tech., at the IITs. It also covers Integrated PhD programs offered at IISc Bangalore. IIT JAM provides access to over 3000 seats across approximately 90 postgraduate programs at 22 IITs, as well as opportunities at NITs and IISc Bangalore.

### ICAR AIEEA PG

For those interested in agricultural sciences, the NTA conducts the ICAR AIEEA exam. This test is for admission to postgraduate and research courses across 75 agricultural universities. Successful candidates are eligible for the admission process in these institutions, with selection based on their scores.

### NEST (National Entrance Screening Test)

NEST is designed for admission to integrated M.Sc. programs at the National Institute of Science Education and Research (NISER Bhubaneswar) and UM DAE Centre for Excellence in Basic Sciences (CEBS, Mumbai). To be eligible, candidates must have passed Class XII from a recognized Indian board with at least 60% aggregate marks (55% for SC, ST, and Divyangjan candidates) and have appeared for NEST.

## Other Notable University Entrance Exams

Many universities also conduct their own entrance tests for specific programs or general admissions.

### IPU CET

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) conducts the IPU CET exam for admission to a range of its undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Aspiring students for GGSIPU should monitor the official university website for specific dates and details.

Securing admission to your desired program and university involves careful preparation and awareness of these critical entrance examinations. Always refer to the official NTA website or the specific university portals for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding exam dates, eligibility, results, and counselling procedures.
