# Is BK School of Management accept MAT score?

Many students aspiring to pursue an MBA wonder about the various entrance exams accepted by different institutions. Let's clarify the specific requirements for admission to the prestigious BK School of Business Management.

## Key Takeaways

*   BK School of Business Management *does not* accept MAT scores for its MBA program.
*   Admission to their two-year, full-time MBA program is based on the CMAT exam.
*   Candidates need a Bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST categories).
*   CMAT is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

## Understanding the Admission Process

The BK School of Business Management, known for its rigorous MBA program, has a clear and defined admission process. Unlike some other institutions that might accept a wider range of management aptitude tests, BK School specifically relies on one national-level examination.

## The Crucial Role of CMAT

For anyone aiming for an MBA at BK School of Business Management, the Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is the mandatory entrance exam. This test is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) on behalf of AICTE New Delhi. Your performance in the CMAT will be a primary factor in your eligibility for the program.

## Eligibility Criteria for MBA

Beyond the CMAT score, there are academic prerequisites that all applicants must meet. To be considered for the two-year, full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) programme at BK School of Business Management, you must:

*   Hold a Bachelor's degree from a university recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC).
*   Have secured a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in your qualifying Bachelor's degree examination.
*   For candidates belonging to the Scheduled Caste (SC) or Scheduled Tribe (ST) categories, a slightly relaxed criterion of 45% aggregate marks in the Bachelor's degree is applicable.

It's important to ensure you meet both the entrance exam requirement and these academic qualifications before applying.

## Why CMAT?

The CMAT is a national-level entrance examination designed to evaluate candidates' aptitude across various sections, including Quantitative Techniques & Data Interpretation, Logical Reasoning, Language Comprehension, General Awareness, and Innovation & Entrepreneurship. By focusing on CMAT, BK School of Business Management ensures a standardized and comprehensive assessment of potential MBA students from across the country.

Prospective students should focus their preparation on the CMAT exam to gain admission to the BK School of Business Management's esteemed MBA program.
