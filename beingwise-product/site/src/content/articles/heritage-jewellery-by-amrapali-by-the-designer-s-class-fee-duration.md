Embark on a fascinating journey into the world of traditional Indian adornments with the "Heritage Jewellery by Amrapali" certification course, presented by The Designer's Class. This online program offers a unique opportunity to explore the intricate craft and business of heritage jewellery, drawing insights from the renowned Amrapali brand.

## Key takeaways

*   **Expert-Led Learning:** Gain knowledge directly from Tarang Arora, Amrapali's Creative Director.
*   **Flexible and Self-Paced:** Study at your own convenience with 48 hours of video and text-based content.
*   **Beginner-Friendly:** Designed for those new to the jewellery business or looking to specialize in heritage pieces.
*   **Certification & Potential Internships:** Earn a certificate of completion and potentially pursue internship opportunities based on your portfolio.

## Course Overview

This certification program provides an in-depth look at the heritage jewellery sector, using Amrapali's celebrated journey as a case study. Over nine comprehensive lessons, you'll delve into how a brand is built and sustained in this specialized domain. The curriculum not only covers the business aspects but also offers an exclusive exploration of Amrapali's diverse collections and design philosophies. The entire course is delivered in English, through a self-study model featuring pre-recorded videos and supplementary text materials, allowing you to learn at your own pace.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for aspiring or current professionals in various creative and business roles who wish to understand the heritage jewellery industry. It is particularly beneficial for:

*   **Jewellery Designers:** To specialize in traditional Indian designs.
*   **Accessory Designers:** To broaden their scope into precious adornments.
*   **Entrepreneurs:** Looking to start or grow a business in the jewellery sector.
*   **Brand Managers & Executives:** Seeking to understand branding and marketing strategies specific to heritage luxury goods.

## What You Will Learn

The "Heritage Jewellery by Amrapali" syllabus is meticulously crafted to equip learners with practical knowledge and strategic insights. Upon completion, you will be able to:

*   Grasp the fundamental principles of the heritage jewellery business.
*   Develop strategies for brand creation and effective marketing within this niche.
*   Understand various operational aspects crucial to a successful jewellery venture.
*   Familiarize yourself with distinctive heritage jewellery pieces and their significance.
*   Learn about leveraging celebrity collaborations for brand enhancement.

## Course Structure and Fees

The program is structured into nine distinct lessons, totaling 48 hours of learning content. Learners gain one year of access to all course materials, including pre-recorded videos and assignments. A certificate of completion is awarded by The Designer's Class to those who achieve at least 70% in the final assessment. The fee for this comprehensive certification is **₹ 2,999**.
