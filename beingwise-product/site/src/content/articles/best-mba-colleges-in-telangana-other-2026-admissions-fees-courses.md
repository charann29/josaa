*   Telangana has 46 colleges offering MBA/PGDM programs in regions other than Hyderabad.

*   While many general management programs are available, specialized MBAs in areas like International Business, Retail, and Sales & Marketing are also offered by some institutions.

Telangana's educational landscape is vibrant, and its regions outside of Hyderabad also contribute significantly to management education. ## Admission Process and Entrance Exams

Admission to MBA/PGDM programs in Telangana - Other colleges primarily relies on scores from various entrance examinations. The Telangana State Integrated Common Entrance Test (TSICET) is the most widely accepted exam, with 45 colleges considering its results for admission. This state-level entrance exam is crucial for those planning to study in Telangana. Additionally, some institutions may also accept national-level test scores like MAT, providing more avenues for aspiring students.

Candidates should regularly check the official websites of their chosen colleges and the respective examination bodies for specific application deadlines, eligibility criteria, and counseling schedules for 2026 admissions.

## Fees and Placements

While specific fee structures vary widely across institutions, government colleges generally have lower fees compared to private ones. Prospective students should research the fee details for each college directly from their official brochures or websites. Regarding placements, many colleges in Telangana - Other strive to provide good placement opportunities. They often have dedicated placement cells that assist students with internships and final job placements, connecting them with various companies across sectors. Average placement packages are influenced by factors such as the college's reputation, the specialization chosen, and the student's individual performance.

Choosing the right MBA college involves careful consideration of programs, fees, and career prospects, ensuring a strong foundation for your future.
