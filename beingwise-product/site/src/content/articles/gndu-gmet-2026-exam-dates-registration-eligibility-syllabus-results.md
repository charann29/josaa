# GNDU GMET 2026 Exam: Dates, Registration, Eligibility, Syllabus, Results

The Guru Nanak Dev University Management Entrance Test (GNDU GMET) is a key examination for students aspiring to join the Master of Business Administration (MBA) programs at Guru Nanak Dev University (GNDU) and its affiliated regional campuses. This guide provides essential information for the 2026 examination cycle.

## Key Takeaways

*   The GNDU GMET 2026 is an online entrance exam conducted by Guru Nanak Dev University for MBA admissions.
*   It features 200 multiple-choice questions (MCQs) across four sections, to be completed in 150 minutes.
*   Application forms for GNDU GMET 2026 are expected to be released online in May 2026.
*   Eligibility requires a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST candidates), or being in the final year of such a degree.

## GNDU GMET 2026 Exam Dates and Application Process

The official notification detailing the GNDU GMET 2026 schedule is anticipated in the first week of May 2026. Following this, the application window is expected to open in the second week of May 2026.

To apply, candidates must visit the official GNDU website. The process involves registering to create a unique login ID and password, then completing the online application form, and finally submitting the application fee. Payment can typically be made via debit/credit card or net banking.

| Event                          | Tentative Dates       |
| :----------------------------- | :-------------------- |
| Official Notification Release  | First week of May 2026|
| Application Form Availability  | May - July 2026       |
| Exam Date                      | To be announced       |

### Application Fees

*   **General Category:** INR 1,200
*   **SC/ST Category:** INR 600

## Eligibility Criteria for GNDU GMET 2026

Prospective candidates must meet specific academic requirements to be eligible for the GNDU GMET 2026:

*   Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree or an equivalent qualification from a recognized university.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 50% in the bachelor's degree is required for general category candidates.
*   For candidates belonging to the SC/ST categories, the minimum aggregate score required is 45%.
*   Students who are currently in their final year of a bachelor's degree program are also eligible to apply.

## GNDU GMET 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The GNDU GMET is an online examination with a duration of 150 minutes. The question paper consists of 200 multiple-choice questions (MCQs).

### Exam Pattern Highlights

| Feature           | Details                                                  |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------------------------------- |
| Exam Mode         | Online (Computer-Based Test)                             |
| Duration          | 150 Minutes                                              |
| Total Questions   | 200 MCQs                                                 |
| Sections          | Business Aptitude, English, Mental Ability & Numerical Ability, General Knowledge |
| Language of Paper | English                                                  |

### Syllabus Overview

The GNDU GMET syllabus is designed by Guru Nanak Dev University to assess a candidate's aptitude for management studies. It covers the following broad areas:

*   **General Knowledge:** Current affairs, history, geography, science, and other general awareness topics.
*   **English:** Reading comprehension, vocabulary, grammar, and verbal ability.
*   **Mental & Numeric Ability:** Logical reasoning, analytical skills, data interpretation, and basic numerical aptitude.
*   **Business Aptitude:** Understanding of business concepts, economics, and management principles.

## GNDU GMET 2026 Results

After the examination, candidates can expect their results to be announced on the official GNDU portal. The results will typically include individual scores and may be followed by further admission rounds such as counseling or group discussions, as determined by the university.

For any specific queries or assistance, candidates can refer to the contact information provided on the official Guru Nanak Dev University website.
