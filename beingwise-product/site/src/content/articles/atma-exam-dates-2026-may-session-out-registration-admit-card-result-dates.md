## ATMA Admit Card Dates 2026

The admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the ATMA examination. For the May 2026 session, the admit cards will be released on April 29, 2026. Only candidates who have successfully registered and paid the application fee will be able to download their admit cards from the official ATMA portal using their login credentials. The admit card will contain essential details such as your name, PID number, exam center address, and the precise date and time of the examination. Ensure you carry a printed copy of this document to the test center.

## ATMA Result Dates 2026

Post-examination, the results for the ATMA exam are typically declared within a week. For the upcoming May 2026 session, candidates can expect their results to be announced on May 9, 2026.
