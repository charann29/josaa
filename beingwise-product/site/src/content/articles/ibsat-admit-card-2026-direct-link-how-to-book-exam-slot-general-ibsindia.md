# IBSAT Admit Card 2026: Direct Link, How to Book Exam Slot @general.ibsindia.org

The IBSAT 2026 admit card is a crucial document for all candidates appearing for the exam. This guide will walk you through the process of booking your exam slot and downloading your admit card, ensuring you're fully prepared for test day.

## Key Takeaways

*   The IBSAT 2026 admit card will be available only after you successfully book your exam slot.
*   Slot booking and admit card downloads are expected to open around October 2026, with the exam likely in late December 2026.
*   The exam will be conducted in a home-proctored format, requiring you to display your admit card to the invigilator via webcam.
*   It's essential to download and print your admit card, as no physical copies will be mailed to you.

## Understanding IBSAT Slot Booking 2026

Booking your exam slot is the first step towards getting your admit card. This process allows you to select your preferred date and time for the IBSAT 2026 examination. Once a slot is booked, it cannot be changed, so choose carefully. The official IBSAT website will host the slot booking portal.

## How to Download Your IBSAT Admit Card 2026

Once you've booked your slot, downloading your admit card is a straightforward process. Follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official IBSAT website, ibsindia.org.
2.  Locate and click on the login section for IBSAT 2026.
3.  Enter your login credentials, which typically include your application number and date of birth in DD/MM/YY format.
4.  After logging in, you will be prompted to book your exam slot if you haven't already.
5.  Once your slot is confirmed, your admit card will be generated.
6.  Download the admit card and take a clear printout.

Remember, the conducting body, ICFAI Business School (IBS), will not send a hard copy of the admit card. You must print it yourself.

### Troubleshooting Admit Card Download Issues

If you encounter difficulties downloading your admit card, consider these common solutions:

*   **Internet Connection:** Ensure you have a stable and active internet connection.
*   **Website Traffic:** High traffic on the website can cause slowdowns. Try again after some time or on the next day.
*   **Forgotten Password:** If you've forgotten your password, use the 'forgot password' option to reset it.
*   **Device Compatibility:** For the best experience, try downloading the admit card on a desktop or laptop rather than a mobile phone.

## Important Details on Your IBSAT Admit Card

Your IBSAT 2026 admit card will contain several important pieces of information. It's crucial to review all details carefully for any errors or discrepancies. The admit card will typically display:

*   Your Name
*   Your IBSAT Application Number
*   Your Photograph
*   Name of the Examination (IBSAT)
*   Course Name
*   IBSAT Exam Date
*   Your Allotted Slot Timing
*   Essential Exam Day Instructions

### What to Do if There's an Error on Your Admit Card?

Should you find any incorrect information on your IBSAT 2026 admit card, it is imperative to contact the admissions officer immediately. You can reach out to the IBS Admissions Office using the contact details provided on the official website. Do not delay in reporting any errors.

## Documents and Instructions for Exam Day

On the day of your IBSAT 2026 exam, besides your printed admit card, you must also have a valid original photo identification proof. Acceptable ID proofs include:

*   Driving License
*   PAN Card
*   Passport
*   College ID
*   Election/Voter Card

Before logging in for your home-proctored exam, ensure you have both your IBSAT 2026 admit card and an original photo ID readily available. Always follow all exam day instructions provided to ensure a smooth testing experience.
