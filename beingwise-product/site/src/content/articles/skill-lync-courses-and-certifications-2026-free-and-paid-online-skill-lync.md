# Skill Lync Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Skill Lync Courses

Skill Lync offers a variety of online programs designed to help engineers and technical professionals enhance their skills and advance their careers. These courses combine theoretical knowledge with practical, project-based learning to prepare students for real-world industry challenges.

## Key takeaways

*   Skill Lync provides specialized online programs in various engineering disciplines, including power electronics, computational fluid dynamics, and computer-aided engineering.
*   Most advanced programs are typically 12 months long, offering in-depth learning with a strong emphasis on practical application.
*   Courses often include multiple projects and cover essential industry software and tools.
*   Successful completion can lead to a project portfolio, which is valuable for showcasing skills to potential employers.

## Post Graduate Program in Power Converter Design and Analysis

This comprehensive 12-month postgraduate program is tailored for individuals looking to specialize in the design and analysis of power converters. Power conversion, which involves transforming electrical energy from one form to another, is a critical aspect of modern electrical engineering. The course delves deeply into the principles and applications of these converters.

The program structure includes six distinct courses and four hands-on projects focused on design and analysis. Through this curriculum, participants gain both theoretical understanding and practical experience, enabling them to design and construct advanced converters with sophisticated control systems. Key skills developed include:

*   Design methodologies
*   Mathematical modeling
*   Proficiency in MATLAB
*   Understanding of electronics and physics principles
*   General engineering knowledge

Upon completion, engineers are better equipped to develop efficient power converters and apply their design and analysis expertise in the field. The fee for this program is ₹2,75,000.

## CFD Engineer Master's Certification Program

The Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) Master's Certification Program is an intensive 12-month course designed for those fascinated by concepts like drag, aerodynamics, fluid dynamics, and internal combustion engine simulations. CFD technology is crucial in industries demanding consistent quality, automation, and safety in their processes.

This master's program comprises six courses that impart essential engineering concepts and introduce various tools vital for a CFD Engineer. Participants gain in-depth knowledge of industry-standard software such as MATLAB, ANSYS Fluent, OpenFoam, Cantera, Python, ANSYS ICEPAK, and GT-POWER. The program is ideal for beginners looking to enter core mechanical engineering fields.

Skills acquired during this program include:

*   Advanced knowledge of Python programming
*   Expertise in various CFD software tools
*   Practical experience through hands-on projects

After completing the program, individuals will possess the necessary skills to work as CFD Engineers or Managers, particularly in top equipment manufacturing organizations. The fee for this program is ₹2,75,000.

## Post Graduate Program in CAE

The Post Graduate Program in Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) is specifically developed for engineers who already have a foundational understanding of CAE tools used for task analysis. This rigorous 12-month program includes five courses that cover engineering theories, tools, and the mathematical principles applied by machines. The program utilizes pre-recorded video content, offering flexibility for learners to access materials at their convenience.

By the end of this course, participants will have an in-depth understanding of advanced technologies and tools essential for CAE engineers. A unique benefit of this program is the creation of a personalized project portfolio page, which showcases certifications and completed work, directly linkable to resumes and job profiles. This specialized program aims to enhance candidates' chances of employment in leading manufacturing units globally. The fee for this program is ₹2,75,000.
