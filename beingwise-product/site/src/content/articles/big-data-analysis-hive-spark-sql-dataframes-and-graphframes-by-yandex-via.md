# Big Data Analysis: Hive, Spark SQL, DataFrames and GraphFrames by Yandex via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's digital world, the sheer volume of data being generated is immense, and the ability to analyze this "big data" is a crucial skill for organizations and professionals alike. This online program, offered through Coursera by Yandex, equips you with the essential tools and techniques to effectively process and interpret large datasets.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to leverage powerful tools like Hive, Spark SQL, DataFrames, and GraphFrames for big data analysis.
*   Gain practical skills in optimizing and debugging Spark applications for peak performance.
*   The course is delivered entirely online, providing a flexible learning experience.
*   A shareable electronic certificate is available upon successful completion of all graded assignments.

## Understanding Big Data Analysis

Big data refers to extremely large datasets that may be structured or unstructured, which can be analyzed computationally to reveal patterns, trends, and associations, especially relating to human behavior and interactions. For businesses and professionals, understanding and utilizing big data is vital for making informed decisions, streamlining operations, and achieving cost efficiencies. This program focuses on the practical application of key technologies that enable such analysis.

## Course Structure and Content

The "Big Data Analysis: Hive, Spark SQL, DataFrames and GraphFrames" course is designed for self-paced online learning, primarily through video and text-based materials. The program covers approximately 39 hours of content, allowing participants to manage their study schedule and assignment deadlines flexibly. You will engage with various learning elements including instructional videos, readings, practice exercises, and quizzes to reinforce your understanding.

Upon completing this program, you will be able to:

*   Construct and effectively use Spark DataFrames.
*   Write specialized analytical tasks using Spark DataFrames.
*   Process graph data efficiently with Spark GraphFrames.
*   Optimize and troubleshoot Spark application performance to its fullest potential.
*   Utilize Hive, Spark DataFrames, and Spark SQL for diverse analytical needs.

## Fees and Certification Details

Coursera offers this program with two main options: a purchase course option and an audit-only option. The cost for purchasing the full course, which includes complete access to all materials and graded assignments, is ₹2,159. For those interested in accessing course materials without earning a certificate, the audit-only mode is available at no charge, though it does not include access to graded assessments necessary for certification.

To earn the electronic certificate of completion, participants must successfully complete and pass all graded components of the course. These typically include quizzes and other assignments as applicable. Once these requirements are met, Coursera will issue the certificate, which can be shared online or printed.
