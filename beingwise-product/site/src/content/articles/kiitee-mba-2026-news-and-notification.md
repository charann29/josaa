# KIITEE MBA 2026 News & Notification

For students aspiring to pursue an MBA or Super MBA from the Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT), staying informed about the KIITEE Management entrance exam and its admission process is essential. This page brings you the latest updates and key dates for the 2026 academic year.

## Key Takeaways

*   Applications for KIITEE Management 2026 Phase 1 will close on April 8, 2026.
*   Phase 1 examinations for KIITEE Management 2026 are scheduled from April 16 to April 20, 2026.
*   KIITEE Management is accepted by three distinct schools within KIIT for their MBA programs.
*   Beyond KIITEE Management, other national-level exams like CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, and GMAT are also considered for admission.

## Important Dates for KIITEE Management 2026 (Phase 1)

Prospective candidates should mark these crucial dates for the first phase of KIITEE Management 2026:

*   **Last Date to Apply (Phase 1):** April 8, 2026
*   **Phase 1 Exam Dates:** April 16 to April 20, 2026

Candidates are advised to regularly check the official KIIT admissions portal for any further announcements or changes to the schedule.

KIITEE Management scores open doors to MBA programs at multiple prestigious schools under the KIIT umbrella. Here’s a look at the institutes and their offerings:

| Institute Name              | Location    | Type    | Total Fees (Approx.) | Duration | Other Accepted Exams          |
| :-------------------------- | :---------- | :------ | :------------------- | :------- | :---------------------------- |
| KIIT School of Management (KSOM) | Bhubaneswar | Private | ₹ 9.70 Lakh          | 2 years  | CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, GMAT     |
| KIIT School of Rural Management (KSRM) | Bhubaneswar | Private | ₹ 4.24 Lakh          | 2 years  | CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, GMAT     |
| KIIT School of Leadership   | Bhubaneswar | Private | ₹ 10.40 Lakh         | 2 years  | CAT, XAT, MAT, GMAT, CMAT |

These institutes offer full-time, two-year MBA programs with varying fee structures and specializations, catering to diverse career aspirations.

## Application Process and Eligibility

Details regarding the application process, including eligibility criteria, required documents, and step-by-step instructions, will be available on the official KIIT admissions website. It is crucial for all applicants to carefully review these guidelines before submitting their applications to ensure a smooth process.

To succeed in the KIITEE Management exam, candidates should focus on a structured preparation strategy. This typically involves understanding the exam pattern, reviewing the syllabus, practicing with past papers, and taking mock tests. Resources for preparation are often provided or recommended on the official KIIT portal.
