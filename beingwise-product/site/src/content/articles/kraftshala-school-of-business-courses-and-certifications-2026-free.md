Kraftshala School of Business offers a range of online programs designed to equip students and professionals with practical, industry-ready skills in marketing and business leadership. These courses emphasize hands-on learning and aim to bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world application, making participants job-ready.

## Key takeaways

*   Kraftshala focuses on practical, skill-based learning through live projects and industry mentorship.
*   Several programs include placement support and a unique fee refund policy if specific salary benchmarks aren't met.
*   Courses cover in-demand areas like digital marketing, AI-led marketing, and sales leadership.
*   Programs are generally short-term (9-11 months) and designed for students, graduates, and working professionals.

## Marketing Launchpad Part-Time

This 11-month part-time program is tailored for individuals who want to build strong digital marketing skills without interrupting their current studies or jobs. It features live Sunday sessions combined with structured weekday project work, ensuring a balanced and practical learning experience.

The curriculum dives into real-world marketing execution, data-driven decision-making, creative strategy, and performance marketing. Participants engage with live brand projects using tools like Google Ads, Meta Ads, e-commerce platforms, and programmatic advertising. This hands-on approach helps develop analytical thinking, communication, and practical application skills essential for modern marketing roles.

It's a job-linked program that includes a unique placement accountability clause: 60% of the course fee is refunded if a student is not placed at a package of ₹4.5 LPA or above. The total fee for this program is ₹1,75,820.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Digital Marketing
*   Advertising
*   SEO & Content Management
*   E-commerce Knowledge
*   Data Analysis & Excel
*   Problem Solving

## PGP in AI-led Marketing

The Post Graduate Program (PGP) in AI-led Marketing is a comprehensive, 9-month industry-focused training designed to equip participants with advanced marketing skills relevant in today's AI-driven business environment. This program is suitable for students, recent graduates, and working professionals looking to enhance their expertise.

The course combines live sessions, real-world projects, and mentorship from industry experts. It focuses on performance marketing, brand management, analytics, and digital growth strategies. Unlike traditional theoretical courses, this PGP emphasizes hands-on learning through case studies and live projects, exposing learners to tools and platforms used by leading companies. The fee for this program is ₹3,70,000.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Marketing & Brand Management
*   Strategic Mindset
*   Digital Marketing
*   Social Media Skills

## PGP in Sales, Marketing and Business Leadership

This 9-month online PGP focuses on sales excellence, category management, and digital growth, blending Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) tactics with Business-to-Business (B2B) Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) sales and marketing strategies. The program includes over 300 hours of live projects and leads to an INDUSTRYCreds® certification.

Participants develop practical expertise in persuasion, storytelling, and data-driven business intelligence. The program boasts an average CTC of ₹10.3 LPA. Kraftshala's accountability clause offers a 60% tuition refund if graduates are placed below the ₹7.5 LPA benchmark. The fee for this program is ₹2,50,000.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Problem Solving
*   Marketing & Sales Skills

Kraftshala's programs are designed to provide practical, job-oriented skills and career support for aspiring marketing and business professionals.
