# Cloud Migration Specialist Certification by Vepsun Technologies: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to advance their career in the rapidly expanding field of cloud technology, the Cloud Migration Specialist Certification by Vepsun Technologies offers a comprehensive training program. This certification is designed to equip individuals with the essential skills and knowledge needed to manage and execute cloud migration projects.

## Key Takeaways

*   This certification program focuses on the end-to-end process of cloud migration, covering key platforms like Azure and AWS.
*   The course is delivered through live online sessions, with options for both weekday and weekend batches, making it flexible for working professionals and students.
*   Participants receive lifetime access to course materials, career assistance, and personalized mentorship, along with a certification upon completion.
*   The program is structured to be completed within a duration of three months, requiring an estimated commitment of 5-6 hours per week.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

Vepsun Technologies, a training company specializing in IT and business, offers this certification to delve into various aspects of cloud migration. The curriculum covers a wide range of concepts, including Azure migration, data migration strategies, and the utilization of various AWS services. The program is specifically designed for professionals such as Cloud Engineers and Cloud Solution Developers, as well as students keen on specializing in cloud migration.

The learning experience is enhanced through instructor-led sessions, real-life case studies, and practical assignments. Students also benefit from a dedicated Student Success Manager and personalized resume feedback, ensuring a well-rounded and supportive learning environment.

### What You Will Learn

Upon completing the training, candidates will gain proficiency in database management and AWS technology. The syllabus covers critical platforms such as:

*   Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (Amazon EC2)
*   Amazon Virtual Private Cloud (AWS-VPC)
*   Amazon DynamoDB
*   Amazon Relational Database Service (AWS-RDS)

The program also includes a detailed learning path for Azure Cloud Migration, focusing on planning, assessment, data migration, and implementing migration using Azure Migrate Services.

## Course Duration and Effort

The Cloud Migration Specialist Certification program is designed to be completed within a period of three months. To successfully keep up with the course material and assignments, participants are recommended to dedicate approximately 5-6 hours per week to their studies. The flexible schedule, with both weekday and weekend online batches, allows individuals to integrate the learning into their existing commitments.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Cloud Migration Specialist Certification is ₹3,80,000, plus Goods and Services Tax (GST). This fee covers the entire course and all associated benefits. Upon successful completion of the course, participants will receive a certification from Vepsun Technologies. Additionally, there is an opportunity to receive a certificate of appreciation from industry leaders like Microsoft, AWS, or GCP, recognizing their achievement and expertise.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Cloud Migration Specialist Certification program, interested candidates should follow a straightforward application process:

1.  Visit the official Vepsun Technologies website for the Cloud Migration Specialist program.
2.  Locate the course page and select the 'Apply Now' option.
3.  Fill in the required details in the application form.

Once the details are submitted, the Vepsun Technologies team will connect with the applicant to guide them through the subsequent steps of the admission process.

This certification provides a valuable pathway for individuals aiming to build a strong foundation and successful career in cloud migration.
