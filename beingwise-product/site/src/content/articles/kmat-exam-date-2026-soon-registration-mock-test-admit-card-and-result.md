# KMAT Exam Date 2026 (Soon): Registration, Mock Test, Admit Card & Result Dates

For students aspiring to pursue MBA/PGDM or MCA programs in Karnataka, the KMAT exam is a crucial step. This page provides a comprehensive overview of the expected schedule for KMAT 2026, helping you plan your application journey effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   KMAT 2026 is anticipated to be held in two sessions, with the first session likely in early September 2026.
*   The examination is conducted online, allowing candidates to take it from home under remote proctoring.
*   Registration for KMAT 2026 is expected to commence in May 2026.
*   KMAT serves as a gateway to approximately 169 colleges across Karnataka for management and computer applications courses.

## KMAT 2026 Registration Dates

The registration window for KMAT 2026 Session 1 is expected to open around the second week of May 2026. Aspiring candidates will need to visit the official KMAT India website to complete their application. The registration process typically involves several steps:

*   Initial registration to create a candidate profile.
*   Filling out the detailed application form.
*   Uploading necessary documents, such as photographs and signatures.
*   Paying the application fee online.

It's important to complete all steps accurately to ensure a successful application.

## KMAT Admit Card 2026 Dates

Candidates who successfully register for KMAT 2026 can expect their admit cards to be released, likely in the first week of September 2026 for Session 1. The admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the exam and contains vital information such as exam day instructions, reporting time, and candidate details. It will be available for download on the official KMAT India website until the day of the examination.

### Steps to Download KMAT Admit Card

1.  Navigate to the official KMAT India website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'KMAT 2026 Admit Card' link, which will direct you to the candidate login portal.
3.  Enter your application number and date of birth to log in.
4.  Your admit card will be displayed on the screen. Carefully verify all the details printed on it.
5.  Download the admit card and take a printout for your records and for the exam day.

## KMAT Exam Date 2026

KMAT 2026 is anticipated to be conducted in two distinct sessions, each with its own registration period. The first session is tentatively scheduled for the first week of September 2026. This examination is designed as a home-based, online proctored test, allowing candidates to appear for it from the convenience of their homes while maintaining examination integrity through remote supervision. KMAT is often considered one of the final management entrance examinations of the academic year.

## KMAT Result Dates 2026

The results for KMAT 2026 will be announced on the official KMAT India website. Candidates will be able to access their scores through their individual candidate login. Following the release of results, colleges accepting KMAT scores will declare their cut-offs. Candidates who meet these cut-off criteria will typically proceed to the next stage of the admission process, which often includes a Personal Interview.

Stay tuned to the official KMAT India website for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding KMAT 2026 dates and announcements.
