## What is Design Thinking?

Design thinking is a human-centered methodology for innovation. It involves understanding users' needs, challenging assumptions, redefining problems, and creating innovative solutions. This iterative process typically includes phases like empathizing, defining, ideating, prototyping, and testing. It's not just for designers; professionals in business, engineering, and many other fields use design thinking to develop user-friendly products, services, and strategies.

## Top Online Design Thinking Courses and Certifications

Several prestigious institutions offer online programs in design thinking, each with unique features and target audiences. Here's a look at some prominent options:

### Postgraduate Diploma in Innovation and Design Thinking

This comprehensive 9-month online diploma program is designed to provide a deep understanding of innovation and design thinking techniques. It emphasizes systematic problem-solving while keeping the user experience at the core. The curriculum explores how to identify customer needs and translate them into products and services that drive organizational growth. Participants learn about creativity and 'closed-world innovation' using specific templates to turn patterns into practical solutions.

### Advance Strategy Management & Design Thinking

Offered by the faculty of the Indian Institute of Management Rohtak, this 6-month certification is tailored for senior managers and leaders. It focuses on contemporary strategic management alongside design thinking techniques. The program utilizes an intensive online learning system, complemented by two valuable in-campus learning sessions at IIM Rohtak. Upon successful completion, learners receive professional certification from this premier business school. The fee for this program is approximately ₹1,55,000.

### Professional Certificate Programme in Design Thinking and Innovation Management

This 5-month professional certification from the Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode (IIM Kozhikode) aims to equip individuals with the ability to transform products and services into economical, accessible alternatives. The program adopts a human-centric approach, applying theoretical skills to practical, innovation-driven projects. It focuses on enhancing design thinking, problem-solving, leadership, and collaboration skills, preparing candidates to manage transformative undertakings. This course is offered with support from the Laboratory for Innovation, Venturing and Entrepreneurship (IIMK LIVE) and the Department of Science and Technology. The fee for this program is approximately ₹1,02,500.

### Certificate Program in Design Thinking

Offered by IIT Bombay, this program focuses on how successful organizations leverage design thinking to simultaneously address consumer needs and achieve business outcomes. It highlights design thinking as a crucial tool for driving higher business value. While specific duration and fee details were not provided, such programs typically cover core design thinking principles and their application in various industry contexts.
