# APICET 2026 Exam Date (OUT): Form Correction, Admit Card, Exam Date & Result Dates

The Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test (APICET) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to pursue MBA and MCA programs in colleges across Andhra Pradesh. Administered by the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE), the exam plays a key role in admissions to various postgraduate courses.

## Key takeaways

*   The APICET 2026 application correction window is currently open for category 2 details, closing on April 6, 2026.
*   The official APICET 2026 exam is scheduled for May 2, 2026, conducted in Computer-Based Test (CBT) mode.
*   Admit cards for registered candidates will be released on April 15, 2026, and are mandatory for appearing in the exam.
*   Important dates for application, admit card, exam, and results are officially announced and should be carefully noted by all aspirants.

## Application Form and Correction Window

The application process for APICET 2026 began on February 5, 2026, without a late fee. Candidates who missed this window had another opportunity to register with a late fee between March 24 and April 5, 2026. The APSCHE has also opened a critical application correction window. As of now, candidates can edit specific details in their application forms. This correction window is active until April 6, 2026. It is crucial for applicants to review their submissions and make any necessary corrections before this deadline to avoid issues with their candidature.

## APICET 2026 Admit Card Release

The admit card, often referred to as the hall ticket, is a mandatory document for all registered candidates appearing for the APICET 2026 exam. The APSCHE will release the APICET 2026 admit cards on April 15, 2026. Candidates will be able to download their admit cards from the official APSCHE portal by entering their qualifying examination hall ticket number and date of birth. It is anticipated that the download link will be active by 5 PM on the release day. The admit card will contain vital information such as the allotted exam slot, exam center address, and candidate details. Aspirants must download and print their admit card and carry it, along with a valid photo ID, to the examination hall. Entry will be denied without these documents.

## APICET 2026 Examination Details

The APICET 2026 exam will be conducted on May 2, 2026, through Computer-Based Test (CBT) mode. The examination will be held in two shifts to accommodate all candidates. Understanding the shift timings is important for planning your exam day.

| Event            | Exam Date   | Shift Timings        |
| :--------------- | :---------- | :------------------- |
| APICET 2026 Exam | May 2, 2026 | 09:00 AM to 11:30 AM |
|                  |             | 02:00 PM to 04:30 PM |

## APICET 2026 Answer Key and Results

Following the examination, the APSCHE will release the preliminary answer key for APICET 2026 on May 5, 2026. This allows candidates to download the answer key along with their response sheet to estimate their probable scores. A window will also be provided for candidates to challenge the preliminary answer key if they find any discrepancies. The final answer key and the APICET 2026 results will be announced subsequently, followed by the counseling schedule for admissions.

Stay updated with all official announcements on the APSCHE portal for the latest information regarding APICET 2026.
