# Best Colleges accepting NMAT Score in India

For students aspiring to pursue an MBA in India, the NMAT by GMAC is a widely recognized entrance examination. A strong NMAT score can open doors to a diverse range of reputable business schools across the country, offering various specializations.

## Key takeaways

*   Over 160 management institutions in India accept NMAT scores for their MBA and equivalent programs.
*   The vast majority of these colleges, around 133, are privately managed, with a few government and public-private institutions also on the list.
*   Prominent institutions like NMIMS School of Business Management, K J Somaiya Institute of Management, and T A Pai Management Institute are among the popular choices.
*   Eligibility generally requires a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks, along with a valid NMAT score.

## Eligibility and Admission Process for NMAT Colleges

To secure admission to MBA programs accepting NMAT scores, candidates generally need to meet specific eligibility criteria:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree or an equivalent qualification from a recognized university or institution. A minimum aggregate score of 50% in their undergraduate studies is typically required.
*   **Entrance Exam:** A valid NMAT score is mandatory. Colleges set their individual cutoff scores, which candidates must meet to be considered for further rounds of admission.

Beyond the NMAT score, most institutions conduct further selection processes, which may include Group Discussions (GD) and Personal Interviews (PI), to assess a candidate's overall profile, communication skills, and leadership potential.
