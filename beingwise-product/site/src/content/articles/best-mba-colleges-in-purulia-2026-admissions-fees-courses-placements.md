## Admission Process and Eligibility for 2026

Admissions to MBA/PGDM programs typically involve a multi-stage process. For 2026 admissions, candidates will generally need to meet specific eligibility criteria and often appear for national-level entrance examinations.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   **Educational Qualification:** A bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university, usually with a minimum aggregate percentage (often 50% for general category, slightly relaxed for reserved categories).
*   **Entrance Exams:** Most MBA programs require valid scores from common management entrance tests such as CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, or ATMA. The specific exam accepted will vary by institution.

### Application Process

*   **Application Forms:** Candidates must fill out application forms, usually available online on the respective college's official website.
*   **Entrance Exam:** Appear for the required management entrance examination.
*   **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates are invited for subsequent selection rounds, which may include Group Discussions (GD) and Personal Interviews (PI).
*   **Final Selection:** Based on entrance exam scores, academic record, work experience (if any), and performance in GD/PI, final admission offers are made.

Students should regularly check the official websites of their target colleges for precise admission timelines and requirements for the 2026 intake.
