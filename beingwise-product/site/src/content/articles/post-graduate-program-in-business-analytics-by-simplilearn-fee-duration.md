# Post Graduate Program In Business Analytics by Simplilearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program is designed for individuals eager to master the art of business analytics, offering a blend of self-paced learning and practical application. It aims to equip participants with essential skills for data-driven decision-making in various industries.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program offers flexible self-paced video learning, complemented by virtual classroom sessions.
*   Participants will engage with two industry-focused projects and eight practical exercises.
*   It provides training in Business Intelligence (BI) using Microsoft Power BI.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from Simplilearn.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

The Post Graduate Program in Business Analytics is a comprehensive course that combines self-paced video lessons with hands-on practice. It delves into the core concepts of data analysis and statistical analysis, crucial for making informed business decisions. A significant component of the training involves using Microsoft Power BI for business intelligence, alongside exploring quantitative analysis and predictive modeling techniques to extract valuable insights from large datasets.

Throughout the program, you will learn to utilize data mining, predictive analytics, and statistical methods with Excel to transform raw data into actionable intelligence. The curriculum is structured to enhance your problem-solving abilities and decision-making skills, leading to improved business outcomes.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, you will gain proficiency in several key areas, including:

*   **Data Manipulation in Excel:** Learn to combine various data types from multiple cells for faster analysis.
*   **Advanced Excel Functions:** Master data analytics tools and functions available in Excel.
*   **Conditional Formatting:** Utilize Excel's extensive library of predetermined conditions or create custom rules for efficient data sorting.
*   **Pivot Tables and Slicers:** Create insightful reports using pivot tables and filter them effectively with slicers.
*   **Scenario Analysis:** Handle multiple cells to view different values, results, and data under varying scenarios.
*   **Decision-Making Reports:** Develop business decision-making reports using the Solver tool to find solutions in different situations.
*   **Goal Seek Tool:** Employ Excel's Goal Seek feature to determine input values for desired results.
*   **Statistical Analysis:** Apply ANOVA to compare means of different datasets and use Hypothesis testing with Excel based on your data, sample size, and required outcomes.

## Eligibility and Certification

This program is ideal for graduates and professionals, especially those with a background in computer science, who are keen to develop expertise in business analytics, data visualization, business intelligence (BI), data analysis, and predictive analytics. BI reporting professionals will also find this course highly beneficial.

To earn the Post Graduate Program in Business Analytics certification from Simplilearn, students must complete at least 85% of the course content and successfully finish one project.

## Fees and Financing Options

The fee for the Self-Paced Learning option is ₹54,000. Simplilearn also offers financing options, allowing participants to pay in installments of ₹4,500 per month. A certificate is provided by Simplilearn upon successful completion of the program requirements.

## Who Is This Program For?

The program is highly recommended for professionals interested in Business Intelligence (BI) across various industries, including IT and finance. Common professional profiles that would benefit from this program include:

*   Data Analysts
*   Data Scientists
*   Project Managers
*   BI Reporting Professionals

This program promises to be a valuable step for anyone looking to advance their career in the dynamic field of business analytics.
