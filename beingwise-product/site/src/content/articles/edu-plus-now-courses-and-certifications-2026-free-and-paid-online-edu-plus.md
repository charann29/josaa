# Edu plus now Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online edu plus now Courses

Edu plus now offers a range of online courses and certifications, often in collaboration with prestigious institutions like the Indian Statistical Institute, Pune. These programs are designed to equip students and professionals with in-demand skills for various industries, particularly in data science and analytics.

## Key takeaways

*   Edu plus now collaborates with the Indian Statistical Institute, Pune, for specialized post-graduate programs.
*   Programs focus on practical, industry-relevant skills in data science, data analytics, and data engineering.
*   Courses often include real-world projects and offer placement assistance to help graduates start their careers.
*   Duration and fees vary significantly between programs, catering to different career goals and budgets.

## Post Graduate Program in Data Science

This comprehensive online program, co-created by Edu plus now and the Indian Statistical Institute, Pune, is tailored for individuals aiming for a career as a skilled data scientist. Lasting nine months, it follows a weekend batch schedule with classes from 8 AM to 1 PM.

The curriculum is extensive, covering a wide array of tools and concepts crucial for data science professionals. Key topics include:

*   SQL and Functional Analytics
*   Data Analysis and Regression Modeling
*   Text Mining and Hypothesis Testing
*   Machine Learning using R and Python
*   Predictive Analytics and Neural Networks
*   Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing
*   Tableau, Predictive Modeling, Big Data Hadoop, and Apache Spark

Participants engage in various industry projects spanning sectors like Market & Customer Analytics, IT, HR, Retail, Supply Chain, Finance, Healthcare, Insurance, Sales, and R&D. The program fee is ₹2,00,000.

## Post Graduate Program in Data Analytics

Designed for aspiring data analytics professionals, this four-month certification course is also a collaboration between Edu plus now and the Indian Statistical Institute, Pune. The program features faculty from well-known institutions, ensuring high-quality instruction.

The 240 hours of training cover essential data analytics tools and statistical concepts, such as:

*   R Studio, Tableau, and Power BI
*   Core Statistics Concepts
*   Deep Learning and Advanced Excel
*   Machine Learning using Python

Beyond the certificate, candidates receive placement assistance to help them connect with reputable companies. The program fee for this course is ₹1,20,000.

## Post Graduate Program in Data Engineering

The 'Post Graduate Program in Data Engineering' is an intensive four-month online course offered by Edu plus now, in partnership with the Indian Statistical Institute, Pune. It's specifically designed for individuals looking to establish themselves in the field of data engineering.

Students will develop expertise in critical software tools and frameworks, including:

*   SQL and MongoDB
*   Big Data (Hadoop) and Cloud computing (AWS Technology)
*   Python and Apache Spark

The program emphasizes experiential learning, allowing candidates to work on industrially relevant projects to gain hands-on experience with the concepts taught. The fee for this program is ₹80,000.
