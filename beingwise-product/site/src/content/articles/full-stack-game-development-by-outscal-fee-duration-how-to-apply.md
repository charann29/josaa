# Full Stack Game Development by Outscal: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Are you passionate about creating games and ready to turn that passion into a career? Outscal offers a comprehensive Full Stack Game Development program designed to equip you with the skills to build your own games from the ground up and accelerate your professional journey in the gaming industry.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 12-month certification course delivered through live online sessions, with lifetime access to study materials.
*   The curriculum focuses on building a strong foundation in C++, Unity, and data structures, culminating in over 10 game projects for your portfolio.
*   Students receive extensive support, including weekly mentor and doubt-clearing sessions, code reviews, and mock interviews.
*   Outscal provides lifetime placement support, connecting students with over 100 hiring partners and boasting a 35% alumni career transition rate.

## Course Overview and Highlights

Outscal's Full Stack Game Development certification is an intensive 12-month program aimed at aspiring game developers and those looking to upskill. The course is conducted through live online sessions, making it accessible from anywhere. A significant advantage is the lifetime access to all study materials, ensuring you can revisit concepts whenever needed.

Throughout the program, you'll learn directly from experienced industry professionals, gaining practical insights and getting your questions answered in real-time. The curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, guiding you to develop more than 10 unique games suitable for showcasing in a professional portfolio.

## Program Offerings and Support

Outscal is committed to student success, offering a robust support system alongside the core curriculum:

*   **Mentorship:** One dedicated mentor session per week to guide your progress.
*   **Doubt Clearing:** Two doubt-clearing sessions per week to address any challenges.
*   **Code Reviews:** Two code review sessions per week to refine your programming skills.
*   **Interview Preparation:** Two mock interviews per month to prepare you for job opportunities.
*   **Placement Support:** Lifetime placement assistance and access to a network of over 100 hiring partners. Official figures indicate a 35% career transition rate among alumni.

## Eligibility and Certification

This program is ideal for individuals who are full-stack developers or game designers looking to specialize further in game development. While specific academic prerequisites aren't detailed, a foundational understanding of programming concepts would be beneficial.

Upon successful completion of the course, you will receive a Full Stack Game Development certification directly from Outscal, validating your newly acquired skills and knowledge.

## What You Will Learn

The comprehensive syllabus is structured to provide a solid understanding of game programming and design, enabling you to build games effectively. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Mastering game development principles and practices.
*   Enhancing your skills in game development using data structures and design patterns.
*   Developing a strong foundation in game programming and design concepts.
*   Creating a portfolio of over 10 game projects to demonstrate your abilities.

## Admission Process

To join Outscal's Full Stack Game Development program, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official Outscal website for the Full Stack Game Development course.
2.  Sign up or register on the website.
3.  Join the candidate waitlist.
4.  Pay the required registration fee.
5.  Participate in an orientation call.
6.  Complete the payment for your first EMI.
7.  After these steps, you will receive a final onboarding call, completing your admission.

## Course Syllabus Highlights

The curriculum is divided into several modules, each focusing on critical aspects of game development:

### C++ (4 Weeks - 4 Projects)

*   Fundamentals of C++ Programming
*   Object-Oriented Programming (OOPS) concepts: inheritance, polymorphism, abstraction, encapsulation
*   Memory management, pointers, and smart pointers
*   Standard Template Library (STL)
*   Virtual functions and abstract classes

### Unity Fundamentals (5 Weeks - 3 Projects)

*   Basics of Unity layout and C# programming
*   Unity's method execution order
*   Fundamentals of Unity Physics (forces, velocity, rigid body)
*   Level management and game save functionality
*   Creating enemies, enemy AI, player movement, and combat systems
*   Unity UI elements and event systems
*   Completing a game loop

### Data Structures Fundamentals (6 Weeks - 2 Projects)

*   Arrays, Stacks, Queues, Linked Lists, Strings
*   Project 1: Connect4 Game
*   Project 2: Minesweeper Game
*   Interview preparation

### Unity Advanced (6 Weeks - 3 Projects)

*   Generic Singleton pattern
*   Model-View-Controller (MVC) architecture

This program offers a comprehensive pathway for anyone serious about a career in game development, providing both the technical skills and the career support needed to succeed.
