# Kerala Government Jobs 2025: Latest Vacancies and Opportunities

For many aspiring individuals, securing a government job in Kerala offers stability, a respectable career path, and the chance to contribute to public service. The year 2025 brings a range of new opportunities across various state departments and public sector undertakings.

## Key Takeaways

*   Over 1200 vacancies are open across diverse sectors like administration, healthcare, education, and public services.
*   Opportunities are available for candidates with educational qualifications ranging from 10th pass to professional degrees.
*   Key organizations like IOCL, Indian Navy, Kerala PSC, and Cochin Port Authority are actively recruiting.
*   Application windows typically open in August and September 2025, with specific deadlines varying by position.

## Diverse Opportunities with Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL)

Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) has announced a significant recruitment drive for 475 apprentice positions in its southern region for 2025, operating under the Apprentice Act of 1961. These roles span various categories including Trade, Technician, and Graduate Apprentices.

To be eligible, applicants should be between 18 and 24 years of age as of August 2025, with age relaxations for reserved categories as per government norms. Educational requirements vary by role: an ITI qualification is needed for Trade Apprentices, a three-year engineering Diploma for Technician Apprentices, and a full-time graduate degree for Graduate Apprentices, as detailed in the official notification. The online application portal will be active from August 8, 2025, closing on September 5, 2025.

## Join the Indian Navy as a Short Service Commission (SSC) Officer

The Indian Navy is inviting applications for 260 Short Service Commission (SSC) Officer vacancies for the June 2026 batch. This recruitment drive aims to strengthen various branches, including Executive, Education, and Technical.

Candidates with relevant qualifications such as BE/B.Tech, M.Sc, ME/M.Tech, or MCA are encouraged to apply. The online application process begins on August 9, 2025, and concludes on September 1, 2025.

## Kerala Public Service Commission (PSC) Recruitment

The Kerala Public Service Commission (PSC) has released an Extra Ordinary Gazette on July 31, 2025, detailing recruitment for 284 positions. These roles cover a wide spectrum, including Medical Officer, Professor, Assistant Engineer, and many others.

This presents an excellent chance for individuals with diverse academic backgrounds, from those who have completed 10th and 12th standard to those holding professional degrees such as LLB, MBBS, Graduate, Postgraduate, and BEd. Interested candidates must submit their applications online via the official Kerala PSC website before September 3, 2025.

## Managerial Roles at Cochin Port Authority

The Cochin Port Authority has issued a notification for six contract-based managerial positions. These include Senior Manager (Business Development), Manager (IT), Manager (Legal), Manager (Estate), Deputy Manager (HR), and Senior Project Manager.

Candidates with qualifications such as a Bachelor’s degree, BE/B.Tech, LLB, Diploma, or a Postgraduate degree (including MBA/PGDM) are targeted for these roles. The online application window opens on August 1, 2025, and closes on August 22, 2025. The monthly remuneration for these positions is structured as follows:

| Position                      | Monthly Remuneration (INR) |
| :---------------------------- | :------------------------- |
| Senior Manager (BD)           | 1,60,000                   |
| Manager (IT)                  | 1,20,000                   |
| Manager (Estate)              | 1,20,000                   |
| Deputy Manager (HR)           | 80,000                     |

## Project Technical Support at SCTIMST

Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology (SCTIMST) has announced a walk-in interview for one vacancy in the Project Technical Support III position. This role is ideal for candidates with an engineering degree in Mechanical, Biomedical, Automotive, Production, or Aerospace Engineering. A master’s degree in a relevant field would be an added advantage.

Selected candidates will receive a remuneration of Rs. 28,000 per month, along with an 18% House Rent Allowance (HRA).

Keeping an eye on official notifications from the Kerala government and relevant departments is crucial for anyone looking to secure a government job in the state for 2025.
