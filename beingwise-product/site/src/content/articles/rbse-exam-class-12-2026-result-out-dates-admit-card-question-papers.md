# RBSE Exam Class 12 2026: Result (OUT), Dates, Admit Card, Question Papers, Pattern

The Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (RBSE), Ajmer, conducts the Class 12 board examinations for students across the state. These examinations are a crucial step for students as they transition from school to higher education.

## Key Takeaways

*   The RBSE Class 12 results for 2026 were declared on March 31, 2026, at 10:00 AM.
*   Students can access their mark sheets online using their roll numbers on the official RBSE website.
*   The main theory examinations for Class 12 in 2026 took place from February 12 to March 11, 2026.
*   Practical examinations were held earlier, between January 1 and January 20, 2026.

## RBSE Class 12 Result 2026

The Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education announced the Class 12 results for all streams – Science, Arts, and Commerce – simultaneously on March 31, 2026. The results were released through a press conference and subsequently made available online. Students can check their individual results by visiting the official RBSE website, rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in, and entering their roll number.

To pass the RBSE Class 12 examination, students must secure a minimum of 33% marks in each subject. This passing criterion applies to both theoretical and practical components of a subject, where applicable, with marks counted separately for each. The board typically releases a list of toppers along with the results, though in some recent years (2021, 2022, 2023), specific merit lists were not published. In 2020, Sameer Kumar Chorediya topped the Science stream with 99% marks.

## RBSE 12th Exam Dates and Schedule 2026

The RBSE Class 12 examinations for 2026 followed a structured schedule, with practical exams preceding the main theory papers. The theory exams were generally conducted in a single shift, from 8:30 AM to 11:45 AM, with some exceptions.

## Admit Card for RBSE Class 12 Exams 2026

The admit card for the RBSE Class 12 examinations is a mandatory document that students must carry to the examination hall. It contains crucial details such as the student's name, roll number, examination center, and subject schedule. For the 2026 exams, the admit cards were made available in February 2026. Students typically collect their admit cards from their respective schools, which receive them from the RBSE.

## Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The RBSE Class 12 examination pattern is designed to assess students' comprehensive understanding of the subjects. It includes a mix of objective and subjective questions. Students are advised to thoroughly review the official syllabus for each subject to prepare effectively. The board also releases sample papers, which are invaluable resources for understanding the question format, marking scheme, and types of questions that may appear in the actual examination. These resources can be found on the official RBSE website.

## Preparation Tips for RBSE Class 12 Exams

Effective preparation is key to success in the RBSE Class 12 exams. Students should focus on understanding concepts rather than rote learning. Creating a study timetable, practicing with previous years' question papers, and clarifying doubts with teachers regularly are highly recommended strategies. Adequate revision and maintaining a healthy lifestyle are also crucial during the examination period.

For more details and updates, students and parents should regularly check the official RBSE portal.
