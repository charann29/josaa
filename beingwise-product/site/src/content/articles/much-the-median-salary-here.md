# How much the median salary here

Understanding the potential earnings after graduation is a key factor for many students and parents considering higher education. This page offers insights into the median salaries at JNTUH College of Engineering Hyderabad (JNTUHCEH), helping you make an informed decision.

## Key takeaways

*   The median salary for undergraduate B.Tech programs stands at ₹9.42 Lakhs per annum.
*   For postgraduate M.Tech programs, the median salary is slightly higher at ₹9.71 Lakhs per annum.
*   JNTUHCEH boasts an overall placement rate of 73% for its graduates.
*   A variety of reputable companies recruit from the campus, indicating strong industry connections.

## What is a median salary?

The median salary represents the middle point of all salaries received by placed students. If you arrange all the salaries in ascending order, the median is the value exactly in the middle. This means half of the placed students earn this amount or more, and the other half earn this amount or less. It's often considered a more reliable indicator of typical earnings than an average, which can be skewed by a few very high or very low figures.

## Placement performance at JNTUHCEH

JNTUHCEH has demonstrated consistent placement support for its students. With an overall placement rate of 73%, a significant majority of graduates secure employment opportunities. This figure reflects the college's efforts in career guidance, skill development, and connecting students with potential employers.

## Top recruiters and salary ranges

The campus attracts a diverse range of companies from various sectors. Prominent recruiters that have visited JNTUHCEH include major IT service providers like Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Wipro, and Cognizant, as well as technology giants such as Amazon and CommVault. Financial sector companies like Goldman Sachs also participate in recruitment drives, offering varied career paths to graduates.

While the median salaries provide a realistic expectation, it's also worth noting the broader salary spectrum. In recent years, the highest salary packages secured by JNTUHCEH graduates have ranged between ₹40 Lakhs and ₹46 Lakhs per annum. These top-tier offers highlight the potential for exceptional earning opportunities for high-performing students.

## Why placement data matters

Placement statistics, especially median salaries, offer valuable insights into the quality of education and industry relevance of a program. A strong median salary indicates that graduates are well-prepared for the job market and are securing roles that offer competitive compensation. This information, alongside academic rigor and campus life, helps prospective students and their families assess the potential return on investment for their education at JNTUHCEH.

Choosing a college involves looking at many factors, and understanding the career prospects, as indicated by placement data, is undoubtedly one of the most important considerations.
