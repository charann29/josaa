# Post Graduate Certificate Program In Design Innovation & Strategy (PGCP) by Jigsaw Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to merge creativity with strategic thinking, the Post Graduate Certificate Program in Design Innovation & Strategy (PGCP) from Jigsaw Academy offers a focused online learning experience. This program aims to equip participants with the skills and mindset needed to become innovative leaders in various fields.

## Key takeaways

*   This is an online, instructor-led program delivered over 5.5 months.
*   It covers 8 distinct courses, culminating in a real-time Capstone Project.
*   The curriculum emphasizes entrepreneurial skills and design for customer experience.
*   Both fresh graduates and experienced professionals are eligible to apply.

## Program Overview

The PGCP is designed to provide participants with sought-after skills, strategic frameworks, and toolkits essential for innovation and entrepreneurship. Developed with insights from industry experts, the program focuses on fostering creative leadership and preparing individuals to navigate the digital economy. It enables students to apply their creativity to real-world industrial projects, understanding how design and strategic thinking can drive innovation and solve complex challenges.

## Course Structure and Offerings

This program features a comprehensive curriculum delivered through various engaging methods. The 5.5-month duration includes instructor-led sessions and covers 8 unique courses. A significant component is the Capstone Project, where students work on a real-time industrial challenge, applying their learned skills. Participants also benefit from one-on-one sessions with international industry experts, gaining valuable insights and mentorship.

Beyond the core courses and projects, the program offers a rich learning environment through:

*   White papers and project case studies
*   Medium articles for broader industry perspectives
*   Mind Lab sessions and workshops
*   Seminars and interactive classes
*   Opportunities for direct interaction with student mentors

Upon successful completion, participants receive certification from MIND (Manipal Innovation and Design).

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the Post Graduate Certificate Program in Design Innovation & Strategy, applicants need to meet specific educational and professional requirements:

*   **Education:** Candidates must hold a diploma or degree equivalent to 12+3 years of education. A minimum of 50% marks in the qualifying degree is required.
*   **Work Experience:** The program welcomes a wide range of applicants, including both fresh graduates and experienced professionals who are looking to enhance their skills in design and strategy.

## Fees and Certification

The program fee for the Post Graduate Certificate Program in Design Innovation & Strategy is structured as follows:

| Head                          | Amount                      |
| :---------------------------- | :-------------------------- |
| Programme Fees (Indian Students) | ₹ 1,20,000 plus applicable taxes |
| International Students Fees   | US Dollar 2,350             |

The certification for this program is provided by Jigsaw Academy, in collaboration with MIND (Manipal Innovation and Design), affirming the quality and recognition of the completed coursework.

## How to Apply

Prospective participants interested in the PGCP need to complete an online application to gain admission. The process typically involves filling out the application form available on the official Jigsaw Academy portal. Further steps and specific instructions will be provided during the application process to guide candidates through their submission.

This program is an excellent opportunity for students, fresh graduates, and working professionals who aspire to become creative leaders and drive innovation in their respective fields.
