# Hack Reactor Software Engineering Online Immersive by Galvanize: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring software engineers looking to kickstart their careers, the Hack Reactor Software Engineering Online Immersive program, offered by Galvanize, provides a comprehensive and intensive learning experience. This bootcamp is designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in the fast-paced tech industry.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a fully online, immersive software engineering bootcamp with a curriculum identical to its in-person counterpart.
*   The program emphasizes practical skills, including Full Stack JavaScript and core computer science concepts, through projects and pair programming.
*   Students receive dedicated career support, including resume building, interview practice, and salary negotiation strategies.
*   Various payment options are available, including an Income Share Agreement, making the program accessible to a wider range of students.

## Program Overview

The Hack Reactor Software Engineering Online Immersive is structured to deliver a high-quality education remotely. It mirrors the robust curriculum and daily schedule of the on-site bootcamp, ensuring a consistent and rigorous learning journey. The program focuses on developing proficiency in Full Stack JavaScript, enabling participants to design and build sophisticated applications. Beyond coding, it delves into fundamental computer science principles and industry best practices crucial for a successful software engineering career.

To prepare students for the demanding curriculum, a preparatory program is offered, focusing on JavaScript fundamentals. This ensures that all participants have a solid foundation before diving into the core immersive content.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of the Hack Reactor Software Engineering course, students will have developed a strong skill set in several key areas:

*   **Web Application Development:** Gain expertise in building and deploying modern web applications.
*   **Computer Science Essentials:** Master core strategies and concepts that underpin effective software engineering.
*   **Full Stack JavaScript:** Become proficient in JavaScript across both front-end and back-end development, enabling work on complex codebases.
*   **Application Design Techniques:** Learn methodologies for designing robust, scalable, and user-friendly applications.

## Fees and Payment Options

The tuition fee for the Hack Reactor Software Engineering Online Immersive program is **$17,980**. Galvanize offers several flexible payment arrangements to accommodate different financial situations, though a $2,000 deposit is typically required for all options except scholarships.

| Payment Option | Details |
| :------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Upfront Payment** | Full tuition of $17,980 paid on the first day of the program. |
| **Split Payment** | A portion of the $17,980 tuition is paid on day one, with the remaining balance due on day one of week seven. |
| **Income Share Agreement (ISA)** | No upfront payment required. Tuition is paid once you secure employment after graduation, based on a percentage of your income. |

Scholarships are also available to eligible candidates, further enhancing accessibility to the program.

## How to Apply

The application process for the Hack Reactor Software Engineering program by Galvanize is straightforward:

1.  **Visit the Official Program Page:** Navigate to the specific webpage for the Hack Reactor Software Engineering Online Immersive course.
2.  **Complete the Online Application:** Fill in the required details, which typically include your full name, active email address, and contact number. You may also be asked to select a preferred campus (even for online programs) and indicate how you learned about the program.
3.  **Submit Your Application:** Once all fields are completed, submit the form.
4.  **Await Contact:** An Enrollment Officer from Galvanize will then reach out to you to guide you through the subsequent admission steps, which may include assessments or interviews to ensure your readiness for the intensive bootcamp environment.

This immersive online experience is designed not just to teach you to code, but to transform you into a professional software engineer, ready to tackle real-world challenges.
