# Best Design Colleges in Gujarat - Courses, Fees, Admissions 2026, Cutoff, Placements

Gujarat has emerged as a significant hub for design education, offering a wide array of programs for aspiring designers. With nearly 86 design colleges, including a mix of private, government, and public-private institutions, students have numerous options to pursue their creative passions and build a strong foundation for a career in design.

## Key takeaways

*   Gujarat hosts approximately 86 design colleges, with the majority being private institutions.
*   Popular specialisations include Fashion Design, Graphic Design, Game Design, and Textile Design.
*   Admission to top design programs often requires scores from national-level entrance exams like UCEED, NID Entrance Exam, and NIFT Entrance Exam.
*   Several colleges offer programs with annual fees under Rs 1 lakh, making design education accessible.

## Eligibility Criteria for Design Courses

To apply for design programs in Gujarat, candidates must meet specific academic requirements:

*   **Undergraduate (UG) Design Courses:** Applicants should have successfully completed their 10+2 examination from a recognised board, regardless of their stream (Science, Commerce, or Arts).
*   **Postgraduate (PG) Design Courses:** Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognised university or college.

Gujarat is home to several highly-rated design institutions. Based on student feedback and various parameters such as faculty expertise, campus facilities, curriculum, and placement support, some of the top colleges include:

*   Arena Animation, Surat
*   Department of Design, Nirma University
*   CEPT University

*   Footwear Design and Development Institute (FDDI)
*   National Institute of Design (NID), Ahmedabad
*   Unitedworld Institute of Design (UID)

## Admission Process and Key Entrance Exams

Admission to design colleges in Gujarat is primarily based on performance in design aptitude tests. Key entrance exams accepted by various institutions include:

*   **UCEED (Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design):** Conducted by IIT Bombay for B.Des admissions.
*   **NID Entrance Exam:** For admission to NID's highly sought-after design programs.
*   **UID Design Aptitude Test (DAT):** For admission to Unitedworld Institute of Design.
*   **NIFT Entrance Exam:** For fashion and textile design programs at NIFT.
*   **FDDI AIST (All India Selection Test):** For programs at the Footwear Design and Development Institute.
*   **CEED (Common Entrance Examination for Design):** For M.Des admissions, also conducted by IIT Bombay.
*   **GLS Institute of Design DAT:** For admissions to GLS Institute of Design.
*   **PID DAT:** For specific design programs.

Students are advised to check the official websites of their desired colleges for specific admission deadlines and application procedures for the 2026 academic year.

## Fees and Placements

Regarding affordability, approximately 20% of design colleges in Gujarat have an annual fee of less than Rs 1 lakh, while about 7% have fees between Rs 2-3 lakh. This indicates a range of options for different budget considerations.

Graduates from design programs in Gujarat are recruited by various companies across industries. While specific placement statistics vary by institution, design professionals find opportunities in fashion houses, advertising agencies, IT firms, manufacturing units, and e-commerce companies. Top recruiters often include leading brands in apparel, lifestyle, digital media, and product development sectors.

Choosing a design college in Gujarat offers a vibrant environment to cultivate creativity and prepare for a rewarding career in the design industry.
