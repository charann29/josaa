# Data Analytics Certification Course by Upskillist: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen to enter the dynamic field of data analytics, Upskillist offers a certification course designed to build fundamental skills. This program provides a flexible online learning experience, combining theoretical knowledge with practical applications.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is delivered entirely online, featuring video and text-based content for self-paced study.
*   It offers significant flexibility, allowing learners to set their own schedules and even pause their studies when needed.
*   Participants benefit from live educator support and continuous assessment through assignments and exams.
*   The certification is globally recognized and certified by Austin Peay State University.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Data Analytics Certification Course by Upskillist is structured to progressively build your expertise in data analytics. Each week introduces modules of increasing difficulty, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the subject matter. The curriculum is designed with an international focus, covering essential topics from basic statistics to practical programming and data visualization tools.

One of the standout features is the global accreditation of the certification, coupled with direct support from educators. Regular evaluations, including weekly assignments and module-end exams, help reinforce learning and track progress. The course also offers a free trial, allowing prospective students to experience the platform before committing.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is carefully crafted to equip learners with a solid foundation in key data analytics concepts and tools. Upon completion, participants will have a strong grasp of:

*   **Statistical Fundamentals:** Understanding core statistical terminologies, theories, and their application.
*   **R Programming:** An introduction to the R programming language, a powerful tool for statistical computing and graphics.
*   **Excel Proficiency:** Using Microsoft Excel for descriptive statistics and data manipulation.
*   **Tableau Basics:** Gaining foundational knowledge of Tableau for data interpretation and visualization.
*   **Data Interpretation:** Skills to interpret data analysis results and present them effectively.

### Detailed Syllabus

The course is divided into modules, each focusing on specific aspects of data analytics:

*   **Module 1: Diploma in Data Analytics**
    *   Starting Your Data Analyst Journey
    *   Exploring Data
    *   Probability
    *   Distributing Data
    *   How Confident Are You in the Sample?
    *   Hypothesising About the Outcome
    *   Testing for Differences: Categorical Variables
    *   Testing for Differences: Numerical Variables
*   **Module 2: Intermediate Data Analytics**
    *   *Further topics in data analysis and advanced techniques.*

## Eligibility and Certification

To earn the Data Analytics Certification, participants must successfully complete weekly assignments and a final exam at the end of each module. Assessments typically consist of 20 questions, while module exams have 50 questions. A minimum score of 50% in both assessments and exams is required to qualify. Grades are provided instantly upon completion.

The certification is globally recognized and issued by Upskillist, with academic certification provided by Austin Peay State University.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Data Analytics Certification Course is a straightforward online process:

1.  Begin by visiting the official Upskillist course page for Data Analytics.
2.  Click on the 'Start your free trial' option to initiate the application.
3.  You will be prompted to answer a few survey questions about your background and goals.
4.  Provide your personal details, including your name, age group, email address, gender, and contact information.
5.  Create a password, specify your educational objective, and select your preferred class schedule.
6.  After a brief tour of the platform, you can begin your learning journey.

This course is an excellent opportunity for anyone looking to build a career in data analytics, offering a blend of flexibility, expert support, and a comprehensive curriculum.
