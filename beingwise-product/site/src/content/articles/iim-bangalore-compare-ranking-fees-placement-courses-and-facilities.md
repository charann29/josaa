# IIM Bangalore: Compare Ranking, Fees, Placement, Courses & Facilities @

IIM Bangalore stands as a premier institution for management education in India, attracting aspiring leaders from across the globe. For students and parents considering this esteemed institute, understanding its offerings, standing, and outcomes is crucial.

## Key takeaways

*   IIM Bangalore consistently ranks among the top management institutes in India, as per official sources like the NIRF rankings.
*   The institute offers a diverse range of programs, including full-time MBA/PGDM, Executive MBA, and various Certificate courses.
*   Placements at IIM Bangalore are highly competitive, with impressive median salary figures reported for its flagship programs.
*   Student feedback consistently highlights strong ratings for placements, infrastructure, and faculty.

## Official Rankings and Reputation

IIM Bangalore consistently holds a top position in national rankings. For instance, in the 2023 NIRF rankings for management, IIM Bangalore secured the second position nationally. This consistent high ranking reflects its strong academic programs, research output, and overall institutional excellence.

## Diverse Course Offerings

IIM Bangalore provides a wide array of management programs catering to different career stages and aspirations. Here's a look at some of its popular offerings:

*   **MBA/PGDM (Post Graduate Programme in Management):** This flagship two-year program is designed for fresh graduates and young professionals. It typically has tuition fees ranging from ₹20 L to ₹26 L and requires strong performance in entrance exams like the CAT, along with other criteria such as GMAT for specific cohorts.
*   **Executive MBA/PGDM (Post Graduate Programme in Enterprise Management):** A one-year program tailored for experienced professionals, with tuition fees around ₹26.5 L. Admission often requires a valid GMAT score and significant work experience.
*   **Certificate Programs:** These short-duration courses, ranging from 5 days to 11 months, cover specialized areas of management. Tuition fees vary considerably, from ₹3.5 L to ₹12.87 L, depending on the program's length and focus.
*   **B.Sc.:** A four-year undergraduate program with tuition fees around ₹8.5 L. Admissions typically consider scores from exams like the National Aptitude Test.

## Placements and Career Outcomes

IIM Bangalore boasts an excellent track record in placements, attracting top recruiters from various sectors. The median salaries reported are highly competitive, reflecting the quality of education and the strong industry connections of the institute.

| Program              | Median Salary (Approx.) |
| :------------------- | :---------------------- |
| MBA/PGDM             | ₹32.61 L                |
| Executive MBA/PGDM   | ₹33.1 L                 |
| Certificate Programs | Data Not Specified      |
| B.Sc.                | Data Not Specified      |

These figures demonstrate the significant return on investment for students pursuing management education at IIM Bangalore, especially for its flagship MBA and Executive MBA programs.

## Campus Life and Facilities

Student feedback consistently praises the vibrant campus life, modern infrastructure, and dedicated faculty at IIM Bangalore. The institute is rated highly across various parameters, including:

*   **Placements:** 4.8/5
*   **Infrastructure:** 4.6/5
*   **Faculty & Course:** 4.6/5
*   **Campus Life:** 4.7/5
*   **Value for Money:** 4.7/5

These ratings, based on verified student reviews, underscore the positive overall experience and high satisfaction levels among the student community regarding the academic environment, facilities, and support systems available.

Choosing IIM Bangalore means investing in a world-class management education that can significantly shape your career trajectory.
