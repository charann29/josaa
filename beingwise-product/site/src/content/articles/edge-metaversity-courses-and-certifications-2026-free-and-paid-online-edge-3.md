# EDGE Metaversity Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online EDGE Metaversity Courses

EDGE Metaversity offers a range of specialized certificate programs designed to equip students with in-demand skills in creative digital fields like animation, virtual production, visual effects, and game development. These programs are structured to provide practical, hands-on training for aspiring professionals.

## Key Takeaways

*   EDGE Metaversity provides specialized master certificate courses in areas like Unreal Engine Virtual Production, Animation with Autodesk Maya, VFX Compositing with NukeX, and VFX with Houdini.
*   These are primarily offline, in-person programs conducted at well-equipped campuses across India.
*   Most master certificate courses are 9 months long, with one program extending to 11 months.
*   The fee for these master certificate programs is approximately ₹2,97,050.

## Master Certificate in Unreal Engine Virtual Production

This 9-month (300-hour) master certificate program focuses on the principles and practices of virtual production using Unreal Engine. It's an in-person course suitable for individuals passionate about visual storytelling and integrating special effects, even without prior experience in game design, computer graphics (CG), or visual effects (VFX).

The curriculum covers everything from foundational concepts like computer graphics, narrative techniques, and camera operation to advanced topics such as sophisticated computer graphics and advanced compositing. A key outcome of this program is the creation of a portfolio film, allowing students to showcase their abilities in virtual production.

## Master Certificate in Animation with Autodesk Maya

This 9-month offline course is designed for those aspiring to a career in animation, leveraging Autodesk Maya software. The program is offered in affiliation with Trazos, a highly regarded animation school based in Spain.

Given the rapidly expanding use of computer-generated imagery and animation in television, films, games, and virtual reality—a sector experiencing significant growth—mastery of industry-standard software like Maya is crucial. This course aims to make students proficient and productive in the animation industry.

## Master Certificate in VFX Compositing with NukeX

Another 9-month in-person master certificate program, this course focuses on VFX compositing using NukeX. This technology is instrumental in creating the immersive environments and fantastical backgrounds seen in many major motion pictures. Like the animation course, this program is offered in collaboration with Trazos.

The curriculum is structured to provide candidates with comprehensive knowledge of VFX animation technologies, enabling them to contribute to the visually stunning effects prevalent in modern cinema and television.

## Master Certificate in VFX with Houdini

This 9-month offline professional certificate program teaches students to create visual effects using SideFX Houdini, a highly sought-after software in the film and television industry. The course is designed to empower trainees to produce striking effects for various media productions, similar to those seen in popular TV series and movies.
