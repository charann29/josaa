# Chemistry XI Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course from the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT), delivered through the Swayam platform, offers a comprehensive study of Class 11 Chemistry Part-I. It's designed to help students grasp fundamental chemical principles, covering topics from atomic structure to states of matter, all at their own pace.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is entirely free to enroll in and access, with an optional certificate available from NCERT.
*   It has a duration of 24 weeks, allowing ample time for self-paced learning.
*   Learning materials include video lectures, e-text, web links, and self-assessment questions.
*   Successful completion of a final assessment with a score of 70% or more is required to earn the certificate.

## Course Overview

The Chemistry XI Part-I course is a massive open online course (MOOC) developed by NCERT, specifically for students in Class 11. It delves into the core aspects of chemistry, focusing on the composition, structure, and properties of matter, along with essential foundational concepts. The course content has been meticulously designed and is delivered by Professor R.K. Parashar of the Department of Education in Science and Mathematics (DESM), NCERT, New Delhi.

## Learning Experience and Materials

This self-study program utilizes a blend of modern educational tools to facilitate learning. Participants will engage with video tutorials that explain complex topics clearly, alongside comprehensive e-resources. The platform also includes self-assessment questions to help students check their understanding and web links for further exploration of concepts. A discussion forum is also available, fostering an interactive environment where students can clarify doubts and engage with peers and instructors.

## Syllabus Highlights

The course covers crucial chapters from the Class 11 Chemistry curriculum. Each chapter is broken down into multiple parts, followed by an assignment to reinforce learning. The main topics include:

*   **Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry:** Introducing fundamental ideas and calculations.
*   **Structure of Atom:** Exploring the atomic model, subatomic particles, and quantum numbers.
*   **Classification of Elements and Periodicity in Properties:** Understanding the periodic table and elemental trends.
*   **Chemical Bonding and Molecular Structure:** Delving into how atoms bond and form molecules.
*   **States of Matter:** Examining the properties of solids, liquids, and gases.
*   **Thermodynamics:** Studying energy transformations and chemical reactions.
*   **Equilibrium:** Understanding chemical and ionic equilibrium.

## Certification and Eligibility

While the course content is free for everyone, participants have the option to earn a certificate upon successful completion. To qualify for the certificate, students must achieve a score of 70% or higher in the final assessment. This certificate is issued by NCERT, adding value to a student's academic profile. There are no specific enrollment eligibility criteria; the course is open to anyone interested in learning Class 11 Chemistry, particularly those currently studying in Class 11.

## How to Enroll

Enrolling in the Chemistry XI Part-I Online Course is a straightforward process. Interested individuals need to visit the official Swayam platform. Once there, locate the course and either sign in if you already have an account or register for a new one. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your enrollment and begin your learning journey.

This course offers a valuable opportunity for students to strengthen their foundational knowledge in Chemistry, directly from NCERT's expertly curated content.
