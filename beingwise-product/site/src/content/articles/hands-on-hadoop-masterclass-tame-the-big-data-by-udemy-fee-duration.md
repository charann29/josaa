## Understanding Big Data and Hadoop

Big data refers to datasets so vast and complex that traditional data processing applications are inadequate to deal with them. It demands specialized tools and techniques for collection, storage, processing, and analysis. Hadoop, an open-source software framework, is a cornerstone of big data technology, designed to store and process enormous amounts of data across clusters of computers. Its strength lies in handling diverse data types and executing applications on a large scale.

This masterclass delves into how Hadoop functions as a robust solution for managing and analyzing such extensive information, making it invaluable for anyone aspiring to work with large datasets.
