# Web Connectivity and Security in Embedded Systems by European Institute of Innovation and Technology via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course delves into the critical areas of web connectivity and security within embedded systems, equipping learners with the knowledge to design and protect internet-enabled devices. Offered through Coursera by the European Institute of Innovation and Technology (EIT Digital), it's a valuable program for anyone looking to understand the intricate world of connected devices and their security challenges.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is an intermediate-level course focusing on internet-enabled devices and their security.
*   It is an eight-week self-paced online course with flexible deadlines, requiring about 4 hours of effort per week.
*   Learners can audit the course content for free or pursue a shareable certificate by completing graded assignments and a peer-reviewed exam.
*   Financial aid options are available for those seeking the certificate.

## Course Overview and Structure

This certification program is designed to explore the technologies that enable devices to connect to the internet, as well as the essential security measures needed to protect them. The curriculum is broadly divided into two main sections over its eight-week duration. The first part addresses web connectivity challenges specific to cyber-physical systems, while the second focuses on various security measures to defend against cyberattacks.

The course uses video lectures and text-based materials, all delivered in English, with multi-language subtitles available. The learning effort is estimated to be approximately four hours per week, making it manageable alongside other commitments. The total estimated completion time for all course materials and assessments is around 12 hours.

## What You Will Learn

Through this program, you will gain a comprehensive understanding of designing network architectures for cyber-physical systems. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Communication Protocols:** How to select and assemble appropriate communication protocols for your system's specific needs.
*   **Security Requirements:** Defining the essential security requirements for embedded systems.
*   **Attack Models:** Understanding potential attack models and strategies to mitigate them.
*   **System Components:** Assembling various components to build robust cyber-physical systems.
*   **Privacy and Security Techniques:** Selecting and applying effective privacy and security techniques for protection.

## Instructors

The course is taught by experienced instructors Juha Plosia and Farhoud Hosseinpour. Their expertise guides learners through the complexities of web connectivity and security in embedded systems.

## Certificate and Fees

While the course content, including video lectures, can be audited for free, obtaining an official certificate requires payment and successful completion of the graded assignments and a final peer-reviewed examination. The certificate is provided by Coursera upon meeting these requirements.

The fee structure for accessing the graded content and earning the certificate is as follows:

| Access Duration | Amount (INR) |
| :-------------- | :----------- |
| 1 month         | Rs. 1,683    |
| 3 months        | Rs. 3,384    |
| 6 months        | Rs. 5,051    |

Financial aid is also available for eligible candidates who wish to pursue the certificate but face financial constraints.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Web Connectivity and Security in Embedded Systems course is straightforward. You can begin by visiting the official course page on Coursera. If you already have a Coursera account, simply log in. Otherwise, you can sign up using your email address, a Google account, or a Facebook account. Once logged in or signed up, look for the 'Enroll for free' option to start learning immediately. There is no complex application form to fill out.

This course offers an excellent opportunity to enhance your skills in a rapidly evolving field, providing both foundational knowledge and practical insights into securing connected devices.
