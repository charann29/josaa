# I Forgot My IIMB UG Result 2025 User ID and Lost My Admit Card – What Now?

It can be quite stressful to misplace your admit card and forget your user ID, especially when an important result like the IIMB UG 2025 is due. But don't worry, there are clear steps you can take to retrieve your details.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your registered email and phone are the first places to check for your User ID.
*   The official IIM Bangalore UG admissions portal has a recovery option for forgotten login details.
*   If online recovery fails, directly contacting the IIM Bangalore admissions office is your next step.
*   Always download and save your admit card once you regain access.

## Step 1: Check Your Registration Records

Before heading to the official portal, take a moment to look through your personal records. During the application process for IIM Bangalore UG admissions, you would have received confirmation messages. These often contain crucial information like your User ID or application number.

*   **Email Inbox:** Search your email (including spam/junk folders) for any messages from IIM Bangalore or related to your UG application. Keywords like "IIMB UG application," "registration confirmation," or "User ID" might help you find these emails quickly.
*   **SMS Messages:** Similarly, check your phone's SMS messages. Confirmation texts frequently include your application number or User ID.

This simple check can often resolve the issue quickly without needing further steps.

## Step 2: Utilize the Official Portal's Recovery Option

If checking your personal messages doesn't yield your User ID, the next step is to visit the official IIM Bangalore UG admissions website. Most reputable institutions provide an online mechanism for recovering forgotten login credentials.

*   **Locate "Forgot User ID/Password":** On the login page, look for a link or button typically labelled "Forgot User ID" or "Forgot Password." This option is designed precisely for situations like yours.
*   **Provide Registered Details:** You will likely be prompted to enter information you used during registration, such as your registered email ID, mobile number, or date of birth. Ensure you enter these details accurately.
*   **Follow Instructions:** After successful verification, the system will usually send your User ID or instructions to reset your password to your registered email address or mobile number. Follow these instructions carefully.

## Step 3: Contact the IIM Bangalore Admissions Office Directly

Should the online recovery method not work for any reason – perhaps your registered email or phone is no longer accessible – your best course of action is to directly contact the IIM Bangalore admissions office. They are equipped to handle such unique situations.

*   **Gather Your Information:** Before contacting them, collect as much detail about your application as possible. This includes your full name, date of birth, the program you applied for, and any other registration details you can recall. The more information you can provide, the easier it will be for them to locate your records.
*   **Contact Methods:** Look for the official contact details (email ID and phone numbers) for UG admissions on the IIM Bangalore website. Avoid using unofficial channels. Clearly explain your situation – that you've forgotten your User ID and lost your admit card/hall ticket.
*   **Be Patient and Clear:** The admissions team will guide you through the process to verify your identity and help retrieve your User ID. Be patient and articulate your issue clearly.

## Step 4: Re-download Your Admit Card

Once you have successfully recovered your User ID and can access the portal, the very first thing you should do is re-download your admit card or hall ticket. This document is essential for checking your results and for any future admission processes.

*   **Save Multiple Copies:** Save a digital copy of your admit card on your computer, cloud storage, and even print a physical copy. This ensures you have backups and won't face the same issue again.

By following these steps, you should be able to recover your User ID and access your IIMB UG Result 2025 without further hassle.
