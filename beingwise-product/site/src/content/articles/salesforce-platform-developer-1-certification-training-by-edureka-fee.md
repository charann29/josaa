# Salesforce Platform Developer 1 Certification Training

This training program is designed to equip you with the essential skills to develop and deploy business logic and user interfaces using the powerful programmatic features of the Force.com platform. It's a comprehensive course for anyone looking to build a career in Salesforce development.

## Key takeaways

*   The program spans 6.5 weeks and includes instructor-led sessions.
*   Participants will gain hands-on experience through real-life case studies and end-of-chapter quizzes.
*   Successful completion leads to a course certificate from the provider.
*   The fee for the entire program is ₹19,995, with EMI options and a limited-time discount available.

## Course Structure and Learning Experience

The training is delivered through a blend of self-study and virtual classroom sessions, with content available in video and text formats. Classes are typically held on weekends, making it convenient for working professionals. You'll also receive lifetime access to the Learning Management System (LMS) and 24/7 expert support through a community forum.

### What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, you will develop a strong understanding of:

*   Cloud computing concepts and various deployment models.
*   How to write business logic customizations using Apex triggers and classes, leveraging SOQL and DML.
*   Designing programmatic solutions that complement declarative customizations.
*   Developing programs for a multi-tenant platform.
*   Crafting Visualforce markup and code to personalize user interfaces.
*   Utilizing the built-in testing framework for Apex and Visualforce code.
*   Data querying, importing, and exporting on the Salesforce platform.
*   Exception handling and the application development lifecycle within Salesforce.

## Program Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Salesforce Platform Developer 1 Certification Training program is ₹19,995. You have the flexibility to pay this amount as a lump sum or through convenient EMI options, which start at ₹6,665. Additionally, for a limited period, a 10% discount is available for new enrollments.

## Eligibility and Certification

While there are no strict prerequisites, a foundational understanding of SQL, HTML, and JavaScript is highly recommended to get the most out of the course. The program is ideal for individuals with a passion for CRM and cloud computing.

### Who Should Enroll?

This certification course is particularly beneficial for:

*   Professionals working with Siebel or other CRM products.
*   Experienced individuals seeking a career transition into the Cloud Computing platform.
*   Developers with experience in languages like Java, C#, or mainframe technologies looking to upgrade their skills.
*   Functional consultants aiming to specialize in Salesforce.
*   Freshers eager to kickstart a career in Salesforce development.

Upon successful completion of a project assigned at the end of the course, you will receive a course completion certificate from the training provider, validating your newly acquired skills.

This program offers a robust pathway to becoming proficient in Salesforce platform development, opening up numerous career opportunities in the rapidly expanding cloud computing sector.
