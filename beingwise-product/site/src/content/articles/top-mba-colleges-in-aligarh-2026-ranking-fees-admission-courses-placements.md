# Top MBA Colleges in Aligarh 2026: Ranking, Fees, Admission, Courses, Placements

Aligarh, a prominent city in Uttar Pradesh, offers several institutions for students aspiring to pursue a Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree. This guide provides essential information for the 2026 academic year, covering top colleges, their rankings, fee structures, admission processes, available courses, and placement opportunities.

## Key takeaways

*   Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) is a leading institution for MBA in Aligarh, recognized nationally.
*   Admission to MBA programs in Aligarh often requires scores from national-level entrance exams like CUET-PG or institute-specific tests.
*   Tuition fees for MBA programs in Aligarh vary significantly, ranging from approximately ₹1.33 Lakh to ₹5.87 Lakh.
*   Graduates from Aligarh's MBA colleges have secured median salaries ranging from ₹2 LPA to over ₹5 LPA.

## Top MBA Colleges in Aligarh

When considering an MBA in Aligarh, several institutions stand out for their academic offerings and student support. While many colleges offer quality education, Aligarh Muslim University often features prominently in national rankings.

### Aligarh Muslim University (AMU)

Aligarh Muslim University is a central university with a long-standing reputation for academic excellence. Its MBA program attracts students from across the country.

*   **NIRF Ranking 2025 (All India):** 69
*   **Total Tuition Fees:** Approximately ₹5.87 Lakh
*   **Median Salary:** Approximately ₹5.26 Lakh

### Other Notable Institutions

Apart from AMU, other colleges in Aligarh offer diverse MBA and PGDM programs. These include:

*   **Mangalayatan University:** Offers multiple MBA/PGDM courses with tuition fees ranging from ₹2.4 Lakh to ₹2.9 Lakh. It accepts scores from MUJEE and CUET-PG, with a reported median salary of ₹4.8 LPA.
*   **Al-Barkaat Institute of Management Studies:** Provides an MBA/PGDM program with approximately 120 seats. The total tuition fee is around ₹1.33 Lakh, and it considers CUET-PG scores. The median salary reported is ₹2 LPA.
*   **Aligarh College of Engineering and Technology:** Offers an MBA/PGDM course with around 60 seats, with a total tuition fee of about ₹1.33 Lakh. Admission typically involves CUET-PG scores.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to MBA programs in Aligarh typically involves a multi-stage process:

1.  **Eligibility Criteria:** Candidates usually need a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university, often with a minimum aggregate percentage (e.g., 50%).
2.  **Entrance Exams:** Most colleges require scores from national-level entrance exams. For example, AMU often conducts its own admission test, while others like Mangalayatan University, Al-Barkaat Institute, and Aligarh College of Engineering and Technology accept CUET-PG scores. Mangalayatan University also considers MUJEE.
3.  **Application:** Prospective students must apply directly to the chosen institutions, usually online, by the specified deadlines.
4.  **Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates may need to participate in group discussions (GD) and personal interviews (PI) as part of the final selection process.

Students are advised to check the official admission portals of each university for precise dates and requirements for the 2026 intake.

## MBA Courses Offered

The MBA programs in Aligarh cover a broad spectrum of management specializations, preparing students for various roles in the corporate world. Common specializations include:

*   Finance
*   Marketing
*   Human Resources
*   Operations Management
*   International Business
*   Information Technology

Colleges like AMU and Mangalayatan University offer a richer choice of specializations within their MBA/PGDM programs.
