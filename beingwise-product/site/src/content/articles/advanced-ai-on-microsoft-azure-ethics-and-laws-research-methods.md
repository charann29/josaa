# Advanced AI on Microsoft Azure: Ethics and Laws, Research Methods and Machine Learning by CloudSwyft Global Systems, Inc via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This professional certificate program offers a deep dive into advanced Artificial Intelligence concepts, focusing on ethical considerations, legal frameworks, and practical machine learning implementation on the Microsoft Azure platform. It's designed to elevate your AI career by equipping you with crucial skills for developing and deploying AI solutions responsibly.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 12-week online program delivered through self-study and virtual classrooms.
*   It covers advanced data research methods using Python, alongside the ethics and legal aspects of AI and machine learning.
*   Participants will learn to design, implement, and deploy AI solutions, including predictive models, on the Microsoft Azure cloud platform.
*   Upon successful completion, you will receive a digital certificate from CloudSwyft Global Systems, Inc.

## Program Structure and Learning Approach

This comprehensive program is delivered entirely online, combining video and text-based modules with virtual classroom sessions. Designed for self-study, it recommends dedicating approximately 5-6 hours per week to the course material. The instruction is in English, making it accessible to a wide audience.

The curriculum is structured around project-based assessments, ensuring hands-on training and practical application of the concepts learned. You will benefit from live sessions and expert instructors, all contributing to a world-class learning experience.

## What You Will Learn

The Advanced AI on Microsoft Azure program is designed to provide you with a robust understanding of various AI and machine learning topics. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **AI Ethics and Law:** Understanding the legal and ethical principles crucial for responsible AI development and deployment.
*   **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** Learning how software is used to analyze, process, and derive insights from natural language data.
*   **Data Science Research:** Applying advanced data science research techniques, particularly using the Python programming language.
*   **Azure Machine Learning:** Becoming proficient in utilizing advanced Azure machine learning capabilities to develop predictive AI models.
*   **AI Solution Design:** Gaining expertise in designing and implementing AI solutions on the Microsoft Azure cloud platform.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

To successfully complete this microcredential, candidates should possess foundational knowledge in several areas. This includes a basic understanding of a programming language, preferably Python, along with statistics and mathematics. Some familiarity with Visual Studio or C# is also beneficial.

Furthermore, prior experience working with data from various sources such as text files, databases, or Excel spreadsheets is recommended. Meeting these prerequisites will ensure you can fully engage with the advanced concepts presented in the program.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for the Advanced AI on Microsoft Azure program is ₹53,749. Payments can be conveniently made via PayPal, MasterCard, American Express, or Visa cards.

Upon successfully completing all the material and assessments within the program, you will be eligible to receive a digital certificate of completion. This certificate is provided by CloudSwyft Global Systems, Inc, acknowledging your achievement and newly acquired skills. Additionally, participants may also earn digital badges as they progress through the course.

This program is an excellent opportunity to gain cutting-edge skills in a rapidly evolving field, preparing you for advanced roles in AI and data science.
