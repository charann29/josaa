## MBA Admission Process and Eligibility 2026

To be considered for MBA admission in Coimbatore for the 2026 intake, candidates generally need to meet specific eligibility criteria and successfully clear entrance examinations. The common requirements are:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants must possess an undergraduate degree from a recognized university with at least a 50% aggregate score.
*   **Entrance Exams:** Successful completion of accepted entrance exams like MAT or TANCET is mandatory.
*   **Final Year Students:** Candidates in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply, provided they meet all other criteria upon completion of their degree.
