# Post Graduate Certificate Program in Cybersecurity by Jigsaw Academy via UNext Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's digital world, cybersecurity skills are highly sought after. This Post Graduate Certificate Program, offered by Jigsaw Academy and delivered through UNext Learning, aims to equip professionals with the knowledge and practical abilities to protect digital assets and respond to cyber threats.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is delivered in English, primarily through self-study and virtual classroom sessions.
*   Classes are scheduled on weekends, making it suitable for working professionals.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Jigsaw Academy.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹175,000.

## Program Structure and Learning Mode

This certificate program is designed for flexibility, catering to individuals who may be balancing their studies with professional commitments. The instruction medium is English, and the learning experience combines self-paced study with interactive virtual classroom sessions. The content is delivered through a mix of video and text-based materials, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of complex cybersecurity concepts. Weekend classes are a key feature, allowing participants to dedicate focused time to their studies without disrupting their weekday schedules.

## Detailed Curriculum

The program covers a wide array of topics crucial for a robust understanding of cybersecurity, progressing from foundational concepts to advanced threat management and incident response. Here's an overview of the modules:

*   **CYS 501 - Cyber Security Fundamentals:** Introduces core cybersecurity concepts, workplace security, and asset protection.
*   **CYS 502 - Network Security & Cryptography:** Explores network principles, security measures, cryptographic techniques, and web application basics.
*   **CYS 503 - Cyber Threats & Attacks:** Delves into various attack types, stages of an attack, exploitation methods, security intelligence, vulnerabilities, and the OWASP Top 10.
*   **CYS 504 - Threats & Threat Vector Classification:** Focuses on threat hunting, analysis, and modeling methodologies like STRIDE, Octave, DREAD, and MITRE ATT&CK.
*   **CYS 505 - Preventing Cyber Security Breaches:** Covers operating system hardening, database security, identity and access management, application security controls, and security testing.
*   **CYS 506 - Respond & Recover From Cyber Attacks:** Addresses incident preparedness, response, aftermath, various incident handling models, and an overview of SIEM deployments, including Splunk.
*   **CYS 507 - Capture The Flag Exercises:** Provides hands-on experience in solving real-world cybersecurity challenges.
*   **CYS 508 - Capstone Project:** A final project based on real-time datasets and simulated scenarios, allowing participants to apply their learned skills comprehensively.

## Program Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Post Graduate Certificate Program in Cybersecurity is ₹175,000. Upon successful completion of all program requirements, participants will be awarded a certificate directly from Jigsaw Academy, acknowledging their newly acquired expertise in the field of cybersecurity.
