# Coding Dojo Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Coding Dojo Courses

Coding Dojo offers a range of online bootcamps designed to help individuals, from beginners to working professionals, gain in-demand tech skills. These programs provide flexible learning paths to accommodate various schedules and career aspirations.

## Key takeaways

*   Coding Dojo provides both accelerated and flexible part-time online bootcamps in software development and cybersecurity.
*   Programs include live instructor support, hands-on assignments, and dedicated career services.
*   Options are available for those seeking to learn one, two, or three programming stacks.
*   Some courses focus on specific technologies like Python, while others cover broader full-stack development.

## Software Development Online Part-Time Accelerated

This certification program is tailored for individuals aiming to become full-stack developers without pausing their current jobs. Delivered through Coding Dojo's online platform, it allows students to progress from a beginner level to a proficient developer in a matter of weeks.

Being a part-time option, it offers greater flexibility compared to intensive full-time curricula. Students benefit from real-time support from instructors, engaging in practical assignments and exercises. This course is ideal for those unable to commit to a full-time schedule, offering the choice to learn one, two, or three programming stacks over 18 to 34 weeks. The weekly commitment involves 2-4 hours of live sessions and approximately 30 hours of self-study, opening doors to long-term career opportunities in software development.

## Software Development Online Part-Time Flex

The Part-Time Flex Bootcamp is a 28 to 30-week program designed for both students and working professionals. It provides comprehensive career services and lifetime access to a supportive community. Participants also receive one-on-one mentor support, ensuring personalized guidance throughout their learning journey.

This program delivers the same quality and benefits as a full-time course but with a more manageable time commitment. It's specifically created for individuals who cannot pursue a full-time option. Lectures are conducted live, fostering an interactive environment where students can ask questions and clarify doubts. For those who miss a live session, recordings are available on the learning platform. Additional benefits include ongoing community support, interview preparation, mock interviews, and assistance with contract negotiation. Key skills covered include SQL knowledge and CSS.

## Cybersecurity Bootcamp Online Part-Time

Recognizing the growing demand for cybersecurity professionals, Coding Dojo offers a 24-week Online Part-Time Cybersecurity Bootcamp certification. As technology reliance increases, so does the need to safeguard networks from cyber threats. This course equips professionals with essential protection skills.

This professional certificate program trains students to identify, assess, report on, and mitigate security risks using various resources and technologies. Towards the end of the course, participants spend roughly 75% of their time on practical exercises and labs, supported by experienced mentors. No prior experience or specific cybersecurity background is required for admission; interested candidates only need to attend and pass an entrance exam. The program focuses on developing a strong understanding of cybersecurity principles.

## Admissions and Course Structure

Entry requirements for these bootcamps typically involve an entrance exam, as prior experience in the field is often not a prerequisite. The flexible nature of these programs allows students to balance their studies with existing commitments. Courses like the Part-Time Accelerated Bootcamp provide full access to the curriculum, live support, and small group coding review sessions. While some courses, like the Flex option, might focus on specific technologies such as Python, others offer a broader full-stack experience.

Embarking on a Coding Dojo bootcamp can be a significant step towards a rewarding career in technology, offering practical skills and ongoing career support.
