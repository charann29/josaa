# UG program in Multimedia Game Art and Design by École Intuit Lab New Delhi: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students passionate about the dynamic world of gaming and interactive entertainment, École Intuit Lab in New Delhi offers a specialized undergraduate program. This comprehensive course is designed to equip aspiring professionals with the artistic and technical skills needed to excel in the global game industry.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a four-year Bachelor's degree program focused on game art and design.
*   The curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, live projects, and portfolio development.
*   It covers the entire game production pipeline, from conceptual art to real-time rendering.
*   Graduates are prepared for various roles in game studios, animation houses, and digital production companies.

## Program Overview and Duration

École Intuit Lab's UG program in Multimedia Game Art and Design is a full-time, four-year undergraduate course. It aims to provide a robust foundation and advanced training in creating visual assets and experiences for video games. The instruction medium for the program is English, ensuring accessibility for a wide range of students.

## What You Will Learn

The program offers an in-depth curriculum that balances creative expression with technical proficiency. Students begin with fundamental art principles like drawing, design theory, color science, and storytelling. As they progress, they delve into specialized areas such as:

*   Character design and digital sculpting
*   Environment art and 3D modeling using industry-standard software
*   Lighting, texturing, and rendering techniques
*   User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design for interactive media
*   Integration of visual elements within real-time game engines

The training also includes practical aspects like understanding game mechanics, storyboarding, and project-based learning to simulate real-world studio environments. Skills developed include strong visualization abilities, computer proficiency, and an understanding of digital marketing principles relevant to the industry.

## Eligibility and Application Process

To be eligible for this undergraduate program, applicants must have successfully completed Grade 12. The application process involves several steps:

1.  **Online Application:** Visit the official École Intuit Lab website and complete the application form for the Multimedia Game Art and Design program.
2.  **Document Submission & Fees:** Submit all required documents and pay the application fees.
3.  **Creative Aptitude Test:** Candidates will need to take a creative aptitude test to assess their potential.
4.  **Interview:** A personal interview is conducted as part of the selection process.

## Career Opportunities

Graduates of this program are well-prepared for diverse roles within the interactive entertainment and digital media sectors. Potential career paths include:

*   Game Designer
*   Artist (Concept Artist, 3D Modeler, Animator, Texture Artist)
*   UI Designer
*   UX Architect
*   Art Director

The program focuses on developing a professional portfolio and offers opportunities for internships and studio exposure, which are crucial for securing positions in leading game studios, animation houses, and digital production companies worldwide.

This program provides a solid pathway for students looking to turn their passion for games into a rewarding professional career.
