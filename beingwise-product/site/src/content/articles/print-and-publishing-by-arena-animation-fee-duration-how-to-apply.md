# Print and Publishing by Arena Animation: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to step into the vibrant world of visual communication, Arena Animation offers a certification course in Print and Publishing. This program is designed to give you a strong foundation in the essential skills needed for graphic design and advertising, preparing you for a creative career.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an introductory certification course focusing on fundamental skills in print and publishing.
*   The program emphasizes practical learning and industry exposure, with placement assistance available.
*   Eligibility requires completing 10+2 in any stream, or being enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate program.
*   Course fees range from ₹30,000 to ₹60,000, with student loan options for affordability.

## Course Overview

The Print and Publishing certification from Arena Animation is crafted to introduce beginners to the core concepts of design for print media. It covers a range of topics from basic graphic design principles to advanced techniques used in advertising. Taught by experienced professionals, the course aims to equip students with up-to-date skills in typography, illustration, artwork creation, and designing for various print and advertising platforms. The curriculum is structured to not only impart knowledge but also to foster creative problem-solving, allowing students to apply their learning through practical exercises and projects.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, you will gain proficiency in several key areas crucial for a career in graphic design and publishing. The course content includes:

*   **Design and Visualisation:** Understanding how to conceptualize and create effective visual solutions.
*   **Typography:** Mastering the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing.
*   **Graphics and Illustration:** Fundamentals of creating compelling graphics and illustrations for print.
*   **Artwork Creation:** Hands-on experience in developing professional artworks for various media.
*   **Designing for Print and Advertising:** Applying current trends and techniques to design materials for print publications and advertising campaigns.
*   **Media Publishing and Print Portfolio:** Gaining insights into media publishing processes and building a strong professional portfolio.
*   **Software Skills:** Practical application of industry-standard software like Photoshop CC for image manipulation and design.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Print and Publishing certification course, candidates must meet a straightforward academic requirement. Applicants need to have successfully completed their 10+2 (intermediate) education from any recognized board, regardless of their stream. The program is also open to students currently pursuing any undergraduate or postgraduate degree, making it accessible to a wide range of aspiring designers.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for the Print and Publishing certification course by Arena Animation typically ranges from **₹30,000 to ₹60,000**. Students have the flexibility to pay the course fee online. For those who might find affordability a concern, student loan facilities are available to help manage the costs. Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive a certificate directly from Arena Animation, acknowledging their newly acquired skills and knowledge.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Print and Publishing certification course is a simple process. Interested candidates should visit the official Arena Animation website. On the website, use the search bar to locate the 'Print and Publishing' course page. Follow the instructions provided on that page to complete your application. Arena Animation will then communicate further details and admission notifications via email.

This certification can be a valuable first step towards a fulfilling career as a graphic designer, illustrator, visualiser, or layout designer in the dynamic print and advertising industry.
