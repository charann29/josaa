# XLRI Selection/ Admission Process Through XAT 2026: Check XLRI Interview Dates, Cutoff

Securing a spot at XLRI Xavier School of Management, Jamshedpur, is a dream for many aspiring management professionals. The admission process for its highly sought-after MBA-HRM and MBA-BM programs involves several crucial steps, primarily relying on your XAT score and performance in subsequent selection rounds.

## Key takeaways

*   The XLRI selection process for 2026 includes an Analytical Essay Writing (AEW) component and a Personal Interview (PI).
*   Candidates are shortlisted for these rounds based on their XAT 2026 scores and academic records.
*   XAT 2026 was conducted on January 4, 2026, with results declared on January 16, 2026.
*   PI call letters were issued via email on January 28, and interviews will take place in major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Jamshedpur, and Bangalore.

## XLRI Selection Process 2026

The admission journey to XLRI involves a multi-stage evaluation designed to assess candidates comprehensively. After the XAT scores are released and cutoffs are determined, shortlisted candidates proceed to the next critical phases:

### Analytical Essay Writing (AEW)

This component evaluates your ability to articulate thoughts clearly and concisely on current affairs. Candidates are typically given a topic related to economics, social issues, business, or an abstract concept. You will need to present your viewpoint in a short essay, usually around 250 words, demonstrating coherence, conciseness, and persuasive writing skills. Previously part of the XAT exam, AEW is now conducted separately for shortlisted candidates during the interview phase.

### Personal Interview (PI)

The Personal Interview is a crucial part of the selection process, allowing the admissions committee to understand your personality, motivations, and suitability for the programs. XLRI conducts these interviews across various cities to accommodate candidates. The interview panel assesses your communication skills, leadership potential, general awareness, and how well you align with XLRI's values.

## XLRI XAT Cut Offs

The XAT cutoff scores are a primary factor in shortlisting candidates for the AEW and PI rounds. These cutoffs vary each year based on factors such as the difficulty of the exam, the number of applicants, and the overall performance of test-takers. XLRI releases these cutoffs after the XAT results are announced. Candidates who meet or exceed these percentile cutoffs for their respective programs (MBA-HRM or MBA-BM) are then invited for the subsequent selection stages.

## PI Call Letters for XLRI Admission 2026

Following the declaration of XAT results and the establishment of cutoffs, XLRI sends out interview call letters to shortlisted candidates. For XAT 2026, these call letters were sent via email on January 28. Invited candidates were required to complete an online form by January 30 to confirm their participation in the Analytical Essay Writing and Personal Interview rounds. It's vital for applicants to regularly check their registered email accounts for these important communications and adhere to all deadlines.

The XLRI admission process is rigorous, aiming to identify future leaders who can contribute significantly to the management world. Success in these stages requires not only academic prowess but also strong communication and analytical abilities.
