# Certified Professional Coder (CPC)® Online Training Course by AAPC: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in medical coding, the Certified Professional Coder (CPC)® Online Training Course from AAPC offers a comprehensive pathway. This program is designed to equip students with the essential knowledge and skills needed to excel in the field and successfully pass the CPC certification examination.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a self-paced online course, allowing you to learn at your convenience over 16 weeks.
*   The curriculum covers medical coding principles across the three main code books: CPT®, ICD-10-CM, and HCPCS Level II.
*   Successful completion leads to a certificate from AAPC, provided you meet the required academic and performance criteria.
*   The course includes valuable resources like AAPC student membership, online practice tests, and access to coding tools.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Certified Professional Coder (CPC)® Online Training Course is delivered entirely online, using a mix of video and text-based materials. While it is primarily a self-study program, some versions may include live instructor-led sessions, offering flexibility for different learning styles. The entire program is structured to be completed within 16 weeks.

The core focus of the course is to provide a deep understanding of medical coding, preparing students not just for the exam but for practical application in the healthcare industry. You will delve into the official ICD-10-CM coding guidelines and learn how to apply coding conventions accurately.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

To enroll in this program, participants should have a foundational understanding of medical terminology and anatomy. This can be gained either through prior academic study in medicine or equivalent real-world experience. If you lack these fundamentals, AAPC also offers introductory courses that can serve as prerequisites.

For those aiming to receive the completion certificate from AAPC, there are specific performance requirements. You must complete the entire course within the four-month timeframe. Additionally, you need to achieve a score of 70% or higher on all chapter exams, the final exam, and maintain an overall final course score of 70% or higher.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing the CPC Online Training Course, you will gain proficiency in the principles of medical coding related to the three primary code books:

*   **CPT® (Current Procedural Terminology):** Learn about coding for medical procedures and services.
*   **ICD-10-CM Code Set:** Understand how to apply diagnostic codes according to official guidelines.
*   **HCPCS Level II:** Explore coding for products, supplies, and services not covered by CPT®.

The curriculum also covers the business aspects of medicine, providing an overview of ICD-10-CM, and detailed sections on applying its guidelines for accurate coding. You will also learn about modifiers and specific body systems such as the integumentary, musculoskeletal, respiratory, cardiovascular, hemic & lymphatic, digestive, urinary, and male/female genital systems.

## Who Is This Course For?

This course is ideal for both aspiring professionals looking to enter the medical coding field and current professionals seeking to enhance their skills and knowledge. It is particularly beneficial for individuals in roles such as:

*   Pharmacists
*   Researchers
*   Forensic Scientists
*   Data Analysts
*   Computer Programmers
*   Clinical Research Coordinators

## Admission Details and Fees

To enroll in the Certified Professional Coder (CPC)® Online Training Course, you can visit the official AAPC website. The process typically involves selecting the desired course, adding it to your cart, and proceeding through the sign-in and checkout steps. Information regarding course fees and any associated costs will be clearly outlined on the official portal during the enrollment process.

Upon successful completion of the course and meeting all certification criteria, AAPC will issue your certificate, recognizing your achievement and readiness for the CPC examination.

To embark on your journey in medical coding, explore the official AAPC website for the most current enrollment details and program offerings.
