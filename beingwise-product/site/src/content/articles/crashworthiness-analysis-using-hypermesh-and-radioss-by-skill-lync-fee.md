# Crashworthiness Analysis Using HyperMesh and Radioss by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding how vehicles and aircraft perform in a crash is vital for safety, and this is where crashworthiness analysis comes in. This Skill Lync course offers a deep dive into using industry-standard tools like HyperMesh and Radioss for simulating and evaluating crash scenarios.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn essential concepts of Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) for crash simulations.
*   Gain hands-on experience with HyperMesh for creating complex models and Radioss for multidisciplinary crash analysis.
*   Develop job-ready skills in automotive and aerospace safety, including debugging and post-processing crash test results.
*   Receive a course completion certificate, with a merit certificate awarded to the top 5% of performers.

## Course Overview

This training program focuses on the critical area of crash testing and simulation, which is indispensable in the automotive and aerospace industries before any product reaches the market. It ensures both passenger and pedestrian safety by determining how well a vehicle can withstand impact. The course provides a foundational understanding of crashworthiness, along with practical skills in using HyperMesh for model generation and Radioss, a powerful solver for crash analysis. You will learn to perform various types of meshing, conduct quality checks, and resolve errors, culminating in the ability to post-process final results.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideally suited for a range of individuals looking to build or advance their careers in the crash and safety domain. It's particularly beneficial for:

*   Students currently pursuing or holding a B.E./B.Tech degree in Automotive Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or Aerospace Engineering.
*   Fresh graduates aiming to gain practical project experience with HyperMesh, FEA, and Radioss.
*   Professionals seeking to transition into roles focused on crash and safety analysis.
*   Individuals interested in jobs related to automobile safety systems, quality assurance, and result validation.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this training, participants will acquire comprehensive knowledge and practical skills, including:

*   Fundamental concepts of Finite Element Analysis (FEA).
*   Techniques for generating intricate models using HyperMesh.
*   How to perform FEA by effectively meshing and editing geometries.
*   Understanding the application of various elements and material cards within Radioss.
*   An introduction to the pre-processing and post-processing tools in Radioss, such as HyperCrash and HyperView.
*   Knowledge of dummy standards utilized in crash testing.
