# Karnataka 2nd PUC Board Exams 2026: Result (Declared), Dates & Timetable, Hall Ticket, Question Paper

The Karnataka 2nd PUC examinations are a crucial milestone for students in the state, opening doors to higher education. This page provides essential information for the 2026 exams, covering results, key dates, and preparation resources.

## Key takeaways

*   The Karnataka School Examination and Assessment Board (KSEAB) declared the 2nd PUC Exam 1 results on April 9, 2026, at 3 PM.
*   The 2nd PUC Exam 2 for 2026 is scheduled to take place between April 30 and May 13, 2026.
*   Official timetables, hall tickets, and model question papers were released by KSEAB on their official website.
*   Students can access their results and other important updates on karresults.nic.in and kseab.karnataka.gov.in/.

## Karnataka 2nd PUC Result 2026

The results for the Karnataka 2nd PUC Exam 1, 2026, were officially released on April 9, 2026, at 3 PM. Students can access their scorecards through the official state board website, karresults.nic.in. 

### How to Check Your 2nd PUC Result 2026:

1.  Visit the official results portal: karresults.nic.in.
2.  Look for the notification link related to the Karnataka PUC Result 2026.
3.  Enter your unique registration number in the provided field.
4.  Submit the details to view, download, or print your Karnataka 2nd PUC Result 2026.

## Karnataka 2nd PUC Hall Ticket 2026

The hall tickets for the Karnataka 2nd PUC theory examinations were released in February 2026. For practical examinations, admit cards were distributed to students through their respective schools. It is mandatory for students to carry their hall ticket to the examination center, as entry will not be permitted without it.

## Karnataka 2nd PUC Question Papers and Answer Key 2026

To aid students in their preparation, KSEAB released model question papers for the 2nd PUC exams in November 2025. These papers are invaluable resources for understanding the exam pattern and types of questions. After Exam 1, the board also published provisional answer keys for some subjects, allowing students to review their responses. Students had a window until March 16, 2026, to submit any objections to these answer keys online.

## About the Karnataka 2nd PUC Board

The Department of Pre-University Education, Karnataka, established in 1966, is responsible for overseeing pre-university education in the state. This board manages over 2,770 examination centers across Karnataka and is tasked with conducting the final second-year Pre-University Certificate (PUC) examinations, which are crucial for students' academic progression.

Stay updated with all official announcements by regularly checking the KSEAB official website for the latest information regarding the 2nd PUC examinations.
