# Can a Delhi Resident and Student Apply for AP EAPCET 2021?

Many students from outside Andhra Pradesh wonder if they can apply for the Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (AP EAPCET). While applications are open to a broad range of candidates, specific eligibility criteria must be met.

## Key Takeaways

*   Students from Delhi can apply for AP EAPCET 2021 if they meet specific academic residency requirements.
*   The primary condition involves having completed intermediate education (10+2) from either Andhra Pradesh or Telangana.
*   Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) and Person of Indian Origin (PIO) cardholders are also eligible under certain conditions.
*   Meeting these criteria is essential, regardless of your current state of residence.

## Understanding the Core Eligibility for AP EAPCET

The fundamental requirement for appearing in AP EAPCET is linked to your academic background rather than your current address. To be eligible, a candidate must have successfully completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination.

### The Andhra Pradesh/Telangana Academic Requirement

The crucial point for students from Delhi and other states is that this 10+2 examination needs to have been passed from a board officially recognized in either Andhra Pradesh or Telangana. This means that even if you live in Delhi, if your intermediate education was completed in one of these two states, you would generally be considered eligible to apply for AP EAPCET 2021.

## Eligibility for OCI/PIO Card Holders

For students holding an Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) card or a Person of Indian Origin (PIO) card, the eligibility rules offer an alternative path. These candidates are also considered eligible to apply for AP EAPCET. This provision aims to facilitate access for individuals with strong ties to India, even if their primary education might have occurred abroad or under different circumstances.

## Important Considerations for Delhi Students

If you are a student residing in Delhi and are contemplating applying for AP EAPCET 2021, it is vital to carefully review these conditions. Your residential status in Delhi does not automatically disqualify you, but your academic history is the deciding factor. Ensure that your 10+2 qualification meets the specified regional board criteria or that you fall under the OCI/PIO category. Always refer to the official AP EAPCET information bulletin for the most precise and up-to-date eligibility details.

By ensuring you meet these specific conditions, Delhi residents can certainly pursue their aspirations through AP EAPCET 2021.
