# Fundamentals of Scalable Data Science by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This introductory online program, developed in collaboration with IBM and offered through Coursera, provides a solid foundation in essential data science concepts. It's designed for anyone looking to understand the core principles of handling and analyzing data, especially big data, using popular tools like Python and Apache Spark.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a beginner-friendly program, requiring no prior expertise in data science.
*   The course is self-paced, allowing you to complete the material at your convenience.
*   You can explore the content for free through an audit mode or a trial period.
*   A verifiable certificate is available upon completing the course with a paid subscription.

## Program Overview and Structure

Designed for self-study, this program delivers content through a mix of videos and text. The entire curriculum is structured to be completed in approximately 21 hours of learning, typically spread over four weeks, though your pace can be faster or slower. It covers fundamental aspects of data science, including data preprocessing, statistical analysis, data visualization, and the application of Apache Spark with PySpark and Python for handling large datasets.

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this program, you will gain practical understanding and skills in several key areas:

*   **Statistical Measures:** Learn how to identify patterns and trends within datasets using various statistical techniques.
*   **Data Visualization:** Understand how to represent data effectively in both 2D and 3D formats to uncover insights.
*   **Data Analysis:** Explore data features, trends, patterns, outliers, and deviations to make informed observations.
*   **Big Data Processing:** Get introduced to analyzing large datasets using parallel and partitioning analysis methods, particularly with Apache Spark.
*   **Programming Skills:** Develop foundational skills in Python, especially in the context of data manipulation and analysis.

## Course Fee and Certification

While you can access the course material for free for a seven-day trial period or through an audit mode, obtaining a certificate requires a paid subscription. The fee structure for a subscription, which includes access to graded assignments and a certificate upon completion, is as follows:

| Subscription Duration | Fee Amount (INR) |
| :-------------------- | :--------------- |
| 1 month               | Rs. 4,018        |
| 3 months              | Rs. 8,037        |
| 6 months              | Rs. 12,056       |

These fees are approximate and subject to change by Coursera. The certificate is issued directly by Coursera upon successful completion of all course requirements under a paid subscription.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

This program is explicitly designed for beginners and does not have stringent prerequisites related to prior data science experience. However, a basic familiarity with mathematics, fundamental programming concepts in Python, and SQL is beneficial to get the most out of the course content. To earn the certificate, participants must complete all lessons and graded assignments within the online program.

This program offers an accessible entry point into the exciting field of scalable data science, equipping learners with foundational knowledge and skills for further exploration.
