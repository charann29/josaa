# Master of Business Administration by CityUHK: Fee, Review, Duration

For professionals aiming to advance their careers and acquire a robust understanding of modern business, the Master of Business Administration (MBA) program from City University of Hong Kong (CityUHK) offers a comprehensive online learning experience. This program is designed to equip individuals with the skills needed to excel in leadership and strategic roles across various sectors.

## Key takeaways

*   The CityUHK MBA is a one-year, online postgraduate degree program.
*   It focuses on developing skills in strategic decision-making, financial management, and operational leadership.
*   The curriculum is structured in two parts, covering a wide array of business disciplines from international marketing to corporate finance.
*   It is particularly suited for experienced professionals looking to transition roles or industries, and those from non-business backgrounds seeking a strong foundation in management.

## Program Overview and Structure

The CityUHK MBA is a postgraduate degree tailored to prepare students for significant managerial responsibilities within business, professional, and public sectors. The program provides a broad education, exposing participants to the intricate relationships between government, business, and consumers. Its core objective is to impart essential managerial knowledge, research skills, and a professional mindset crucial for effectiveness in today's workplace.

The program is delivered entirely online, offering flexibility for working professionals. It spans a duration of one year, making it an intensive yet efficient pathway to an advanced business qualification.

## Curriculum Highlights

The MBA curriculum is thoughtfully divided into two main parts, ensuring a holistic understanding of business principles and practices.

### Program Outline - Part A: Extended Diploma in Leadership & Management (SCQF Level 11)

This segment focuses on foundational leadership and management skills, including:

*   Strategic Leadership and HR Practices in Organisations
*   Sustainability and Business
*   International Marketing Management
*   Strategic Change Management
*   Operations and Project Management
*   Accounting for Next Generation Leaders

### Program Outline - Part B

Building on the initial modules, Part B delves into more specialized and advanced business areas:

*   Corporate Finance
*   Management Information System
*   Managerial Economics
*   Organisations: Behaviour, Structure, Processes
*   International Business
*   Research Methodology
*   Project Paper

Throughout the program, students engage with assignments for module assessments, benefit from regular webinars led by industry experts, and have access to recorded sessions for flexible offline viewing. An online library is also available to enhance learning and research.

## Who Should Consider This MBA?

This program is an excellent fit for several types of professionals:

*   **Experienced individuals** who aspire to transition into senior management roles, shift industries, or develop specialized expertise in areas like finance, marketing, or operations.
*   **Professionals from non-business backgrounds** (such as engineering, healthcare, or education) who wish to move into business-related positions or industries and require a solid grounding in business fundamentals.

The MBA aims to equip students with critical skills in financial management, project management, operations management, marketing, accounting, and strategic leadership.
