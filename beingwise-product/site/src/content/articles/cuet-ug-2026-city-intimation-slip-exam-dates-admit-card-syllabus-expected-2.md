# CUET UG 2026: City Intimation Slip, Exam Dates, Admit Card, Syllabus, Expected Cutoff

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET UG) is a nationwide examination for admission to various undergraduate programs offered by participating central, state, deemed, and private universities across India. This guide covers essential information for the CUET UG 2026 examination, including important dates, procedures, and what to expect.

## Key takeaways

*   The CUET UG 2026 examination is expected to take place between May 11 and May 31, 2026, in a Computer-Based Test (CBT) format.
*   The City Intimation Slip, indicating your allocated exam city, will likely be released by April 30, 2026, approximately 10 days before your specific exam schedule.
*   Your Admit Card, crucial for entry to the exam centre, will be available for download 2-3 days before your examination date.
*   The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducts CUET UG, and scores are used by numerous universities, including Delhi University and Banaras Hindu University, for admissions.

## CUET UG 2026 Important Dates and Schedule

The NTA will soon release the detailed CUET UG 2026 datesheet PDF on their official website. However, based on previous trends, the examination window is typically from the second week of May to the end of May. Candidates should regularly check the official NTA CUET website for the most accurate and updated schedule for their chosen subjects and slots.

**Expected Timeline:**

*   **Photo Correction Window:** Closes April 10, 2026 (for candidates with photo/ID mismatches).
*   **City Intimation Slip Release:** Tentatively by April 30, 2026.
*   **Examination Dates:** May 11 to May 31, 2026.
*   **Admit Card Release:** 2-3 days before individual exam dates.

## City Intimation Slip 2026

The CUET UG advance city intimation slip is a preliminary document released by the NTA to inform candidates about their allotted examination city, date, and slot timings. This helps students plan their travel arrangements well in advance. It is important to note that this is **not** your Admit Card and is not required for entry into the exam centre.

### How to Download the Advance Intimation Slip:

1.  Visit the official CUET NTA website.
2.  Look for the link titled 'CUET UG Advance City Slip 2026 Download'.
3.  Enter your CUET UG 2026 application number and password.
4.  Your intimation slip will appear on the screen in PDF format.
5.  Verify the details mentioned, such as your name, applied subjects, exam city, and slot timings.

## CUET UG 2026 Admit Card

The Admit Card is a mandatory document for appearing in the CUET UG 2026 examination. It contains vital information like your roll number, exam centre address, exam date, and reporting time. Without a valid Admit Card, you will not be permitted to enter the examination hall.

### Downloading Your Admit Card:

Candidates will be able to download their Admit Cards from the official NTA CUET website approximately 2-3 days before their respective exam dates. You will need your CUET application number and password to access and download it. It is advisable to download and print multiple copies of your Admit Card and keep them safe.

## CUET UG Syllabus and Exam Pattern

The CUET UG exam is conducted in 13 languages and covers 23 domain-specific subjects, along with a General Test. The syllabus for domain subjects is generally based on the NCERT Class 12 curriculum. The exam pattern includes multiple-choice questions (MCQs) in a CBT format.

### Key aspects of the exam:

*   **Medium:** 13 languages available for the exam.
*   **Sections:** Includes Language, Domain-Specific Subjects, and a General Test.
*   **Question Type:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).
*   **Mode:** Computer-Based Test (CBT).

For a detailed syllabus for each subject, candidates should refer to the official CUET UG information bulletin released by the NTA.

## Expected Cutoff and Participating Universities

After the CUET UG results are declared, participating universities will release their individual cutoff lists, merit lists, or admission lists based on CUET scores. Universities like Delhi University, Banaras Hindu University, Allahabad University, and BBAU are among the many institutions that accept CUET UG scores for various undergraduate programs.

The cutoff scores can vary significantly each year depending on factors such as the number of applicants, the difficulty level of the exam, and the number of available seats in specific courses and universities. Candidates should aim for a strong performance to maximize their chances of admission to their preferred institutions.
