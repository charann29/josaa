# Economic XI Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course, developed by the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) and delivered through the Swayam platform, offers a comprehensive introduction to Indian economics for Class 11 students. It's designed to provide foundational knowledge about the structure and functioning of the Indian economy.

## Key takeaways

*   The course spans 24 weeks and is delivered entirely online, allowing for self-paced study.
*   It covers key topics from the Class 11 Economics NCERT textbook, focusing on the Indian economy.
*   Successful completion leads to a certificate from NCERT and Swayam.
*   The learning experience is enriched with video lectures, e-texts, and self-assessment tools.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Economic XI Part-I course is a six-month program mirroring the Class 11 economics curriculum as outlined by NCERT. It aims to equip learners with essential knowledge about the Indian economy's framework and operations. The content is expertly coordinated by Professor Dr. Neeraja Rashmi from the Department of Education in Social Sciences (DESS), NCERT, New Delhi.

Delivered via the interactive Swayam platform, the course utilizes a blend of video lectures and digital text materials. It is systematically organized into four main sections, ensuring a structured learning path. Upon completing all course modules, self-assessments, and a final evaluation, students will receive a certificate jointly issued by NIOS and Swayam.

## What You Will Learn

Through this course, participants will gain a solid understanding of fundamental economic concepts related to India. Key learning areas include:

*   The historical context of the Indian Economy leading up to independence.
*   An in-depth look at various sectors within the Indian Economy.
*   Major economic reforms implemented over time.
*   The overall impact of economic policies and changes.
*   Significant challenges currently facing the Indian economy.

## Eligibility and Certification

This course is primarily designed for students in Class 11 who wish to grasp the core concepts of Economics. To earn the certificate, students must achieve a minimum score of 70% or higher in the final assessment. The certificate is issued by NCERT, affirming the completion of the program.

## Course Content Highlights

The syllabus is thoughtfully structured, covering a range of critical topics in Indian economics. Each chapter is accompanied by an assignment to reinforce learning.

*   **Chapter 1:** Indian Economy on the Eve of Independence
*   **Chapter 2:** Indian Economy (1950-1990)
*   **Chapter 3:** Economic Reform Since 1991 – Part 1 & Part 2
*   **Chapter 4:** Poverty
*   **Chapter 5:** Human Capital Formation – Part 1 & Part 2
*   **Chapter 6:** Rural Development – Part 1 & Part 2
*   **Chapter 7:** Growth of Employment and Workforce in India, Informalization and Unemployment Problem
*   **Chapter 8:** Infrastructure – Part 1 & Part 2
*   **Chapter 9:** Environment and Sustainable Development
*   **Chapter 10:** Comparative Development Experiences of India and its Neighbors
*   **Final Assessment:** A comprehensive evaluation of all learned material.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Economic XI Part-I Online Course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal for this specific course.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the subsequent page, you can either sign in with your existing username and password or create a new account to proceed with your enrollment.

This course offers an excellent opportunity for Class 11 students to build a strong foundation in Indian economics through a flexible and engaging online format.
