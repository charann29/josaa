# BET Exam Dates 2026: Check Registration, Admit Card & Result Dates

For students aspiring to pursue management programs, the BET exam is an important step. Understanding the key dates for registration, admit card release, and results is crucial for effective planning. This guide provides a clear overview of the BET 2026 schedule.

## Key takeaways

*   BET 2026 applications span multiple rounds, generally from mid-August 2025 to mid-April 2026.
*   The final submission deadline for BET 2026 applications is April 15, 2026.
*   Admit cards are issued online after candidates successfully book their exam slot.
*   Results are typically announced approximately two weeks after a candidate takes the exam.

## BET 2026 Application and Exam Schedule

The BET exam application window for 2026 is structured in several rounds, offering flexibility to applicants. The entire application period extends from mid-August 2025 through early April 2026. It's important to note the specific deadlines for each round to avoid missing out.

Here’s a detailed look at the BET 2026 schedule:

| Event                               | Date           |
| :---------------------------------- | :------------- |
| **Upcoming Submission Deadline**    |                |
| Round 4 Application Submission      | April 15, 2026 |
| **Past Submission Deadlines**       |                |
| Round 3 Application Submission      | March 3, 2026  |
| Round 2 Application Submission      | January 13, 2026 |
| Round 1 Application Submission      | November 4, 2025 |

## BET 2026 Registration Process

Filling out the BET application form is an entirely online process. Candidates need to follow a structured approach to ensure their application is complete and correctly submitted. The registration typically involves three main steps:

1.  **Initial Registration:** Creating an account on the official BET portal.
2.  **Form Completion:** Filling in personal, contact, and academic details accurately.
3.  **Fee Payment:** Submitting the required application fee online.

It is advisable to keep all necessary documents and information ready before starting the application to ensure a smooth process.

## BET Admit Card 2026

The admit card is an essential document for appearing in the BET exam. It is made available to candidates only after they have successfully blocked their preferred exam slot. To download your admit card, you will need your login credentials (Registration Number and Password).

Here’s how to access your BET admit card:

*   Visit the official BET website.
*   Enter your unique login details.
*   Locate and click on the 'BET admit card download' option.
*   Download and print a copy of your admit card. Remember to carry this printout to the examination center.

## BET Result Dates 2026

BET results are generally declared approximately two weeks after a candidate completes their examination. The scorecard, which is a crucial document, will also be dispatched to candidates by post from the SDA Bocconi Asia Center. Upon receiving the scorecard, candidates will need to confirm their admission as per the instructions provided.

To check your BET results online:

*   Navigate to the official SDA Bocconi Asia Center website.
*   Look for the BET 2026 results link.
*   Enter your login credentials, including your User ID and Password, to download your BET scorecard.

## BET Exam Pattern 2026

Understanding the exam pattern is key to preparing effectively for BET 2026. The test is conducted online and is designed to assess candidates across several critical reasoning areas. Here’s a breakdown of the exam structure:

| Exam Structure             | Details                                   |
| :------------------------- | :---------------------------------------- |
| **Number of Sections**     | 3 (Numerical Reasoning, Verbal Reasoning, Inductive Reasoning) |
| **Exam Mode**              | Online                                    |
| **Medium of Exam**         | English                                   |
| **Duration**               | 70 Minutes                                |
| **Total Questions**        | 54 Multiple Choice Questions              |
| **Questions per Section**  | 18 questions each for Numerical, Verbal, and Inductive Reasoning |
| **Marking Scheme**         | +1 for each correct answer; No negative marking |

Stay updated with the official announcements from the SDA Bocconi Asia Center for any changes or further details regarding the BET 2026 examination.
