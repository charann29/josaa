# Managing Big Data With R and Hadoop by Partnership For Advanced Computing in Europe via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This five-week online program, offered through the FutureLearn platform in collaboration with the Partnership for Advanced Computing in Europe (PRACE), provides essential skills for handling large datasets. It's designed to equip learners with practical expertise in statistical learning and big data management using powerful tools like R and Hadoop.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to effectively manage and analyze big data for organizations.
*   Gain proficiency in using R, R Studio, and Hadoop within a Linux environment.
*   Understand statistical learning concepts crucial for data science.
*   The course is entirely online, self-paced, and leads to a certificate of completion.

## Course Overview and What You Will Learn

This program delves into the core aspects of big data, teaching you how to leverage R and Hadoop for complex data challenges. You'll explore statistical learning techniques and become adept at using Linux commands, especially with Hadoop. Specifically, the curriculum covers:

*   **Linux and Data Management:** Grasping the fundamentals of Linux and its application in handling data.
*   **AWK with Hadoop:** Learning to integrate AWK for data processing alongside Hadoop software.
*   **Hadoop and Statistical Learning:** Understanding how to combine Hadoop with statistical learning principles for robust analysis.

Instructors Janez Povh, Biljana Mileva Boshkoska, and Leon Kos deliver the content in English, ensuring a clear and accessible learning experience.

## Who Is This Program For?

This course is ideal for a diverse group of individuals keen on mastering big data. Professionals working as data scientists or data analysts will find it invaluable for enhancing their skills in collecting and analyzing vast amounts of data. Undergraduate and PhD students in fields like data analysis, statistics, or bioinformatics can also significantly benefit by learning to manage big data with applications such as Hadoop and R. Essentially, anyone interested in data science, computational statistics, or machine learning will find this program highly relevant.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in this program, candidates should possess a foundational understanding of Linux and Bash, or have practical experience with R and matrix operations. Meeting these prerequisites ensures you can keep pace with the course material and maximize your learning.

## Course Duration and Learning Effort

This program is structured as a five-week course, delivered entirely online. It is designed for self-study, allowing you to learn at your own pace. The estimated learning effort is approximately six hours per week, making it manageable alongside other commitments.

## Certificate and Fees

Upon successful completion of all course modules and passing the online assessments, participants will qualify for a certificate of completion. This certificate is provided by FutureLearn, acknowledging your acquired skills in managing big data with R and Hadoop. While the general course structure is free, obtaining the certificate typically involves a fee, details of which are available on the official FutureLearn platform.

## How to Apply

To apply for the "Managing Big Data With R and Hadoop" program, interested candidates need to visit the official FutureLearn portal. There, you will register an account, log in, and then search for the specific course to enroll. The process is straightforward, requiring you to provide necessary registration details and then select the course for admission.

Embrace the opportunity to enhance your data management skills and open doors to advanced roles in the rapidly expanding field of big data.
