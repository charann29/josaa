# UG program In Multimedia & Digital Product Design by École Intuit Lab New Delhi: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

École Intuit Lab in New Delhi offers a comprehensive four-year undergraduate program in Multimedia & Digital Product Design, crafted to equip students with the skills needed to excel in the rapidly evolving digital landscape. This program blends design thinking, user experience (UX) research, user interface (UI) development, and digital strategy to prepare students for impactful careers.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Four-Year Immersive Program:** A full-time, English-medium bachelor's degree focusing on digital product design.
*   **Global Exposure:** Benefit from international workshops, student exchange programs, and a diverse learning environment across campuses in India and Europe.
*   **Practical Learning:** The curriculum emphasizes hands-on studio training, live projects, and mandatory internships (both short-term and semester-long).
*   **Industry-Ready Skills:** Graduates are prepared for roles such as UI/UX designers, web designers, and product designers, combining creative expression with technical expertise.

## Course Overview and Learning Outcomes

This program is meticulously designed to transform creative concepts into functional digital solutions. Students will learn to develop intuitive designs for websites, mobile applications, and complex digital platforms. The curriculum delves into understanding user needs, conducting thorough research, and applying design thinking principles to solve real-world problems.

Key learning areas include UI development, motion design, typography, and advanced prototyping. Beyond technical proficiency, students will develop strategic thinking for product innovation, integrating business objectives and market insights into their design solutions. Through workshops, internships, and global exchange initiatives, the program fosters creativity, cross-cultural understanding, and professional confidence.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

To be eligible for the UG program in Multimedia & Digital Product Design, applicants must have successfully completed Grade 12. The admission process is straightforward and designed to identify creative and analytical talent.

Here's how to apply:

1.  **Online Application:** Visit the official École Intuit Lab website and locate the application form for the Bachelor's Degree in Digital Product Design.
2.  **Form Submission:** Fill out the application form thoroughly, attach all required documents, and pay the application fees.
3.  **Aptitude Test & Interview:** Candidates will then be invited to take a creative aptitude test, followed by a personal interview.

## Program Structure and Features

The four-year program is structured to provide a strong foundation and progressive specialization. It includes two mandatory internships – a short-term placement and a semester-long internship – which can be undertaken either in India or internationally, offering invaluable practical exposure.

Highlights of the program include:

*   **Live Projects:** Work on real-world design challenges.
*   **Studio Practice:** Extensive hands-on training in dedicated design studios.
*   **Portfolio Reviews:** Regular feedback and guidance to build a strong professional portfolio.
*   **Industry Workshops:** Engage with leading design professionals and gain insights into current industry trends.
*   **Technical Labs:** Access to state-of-the-art facilities for digital design and development.
*   **Mentorship:** Receive guidance from global design experts.

## Detailed Syllabus

The curriculum is divided into foundational and advanced modules, ensuring a holistic understanding of digital product design:

### Lab 1: Foundation Program

This initial year focuses on fundamental design principles and creative exploration:

*   Analytical Drawing, Live Model Sketching, Colour, Creativity, Illustration
*   Volume, Perspective, Human Sciences, History of Art
*   Project Week, Environmental Design, Sketching, Exterior Drawing
*   Marker Renderings, Props Design, Anatomy
*   French, Story Boarding, Story Telling
*   Introduction to AI, Introduction to Interaction, Mechanics of Games
*   Visual Expressions, Workshop week, Personal Project

### Lab 2: Designing with Users: Foundations of UX & Interaction

The second year delves into user-centric design methodologies:

*   UCD & UX (User-Centered Design & User Experience) 1, 2 & 3
*   UI Design 1 & 2
*   Management 1, Copywriting (AI)
*   Motion Design, Typography, Computer Graphics, Graphic Design
*   Human Sciences, Page Layout, French
*   Project Week, International Workshop

### Short Internship (1–2 months)

### Lab 3: Strategic Digital Products: Interfaces in Practice

The third year focuses on advanced strategies and practical application in digital interfaces:

*   Management 2, 4 & 5
*   Tech 1 & 2
*   Management 3

The program continues to build on these foundations, integrating advanced technical skills and strategic management principles in the final year to prepare students for leadership roles in design.

This program is ideal for aspiring UI designers, UX architects, web designers, and art directors looking to make a mark in the digital product space.
