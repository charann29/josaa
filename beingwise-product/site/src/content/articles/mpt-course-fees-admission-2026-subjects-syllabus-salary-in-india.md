The Master of Physiotherapy (MPT) is a two-year postgraduate program for those who have completed their Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT). It allows graduates to gain specialized knowledge and skills in advanced physical rehabilitation across various areas like sports, orthopaedics, and neurology.

## Key Takeaways

*   MPT is a two-year postgraduate degree, building on a BPT qualification.
*   Admission typically requires a BPT degree and performance in an entrance exam like CUET PG.
*   Course fees can vary significantly, ranging from approximately INR 1 Lakh to INR 15 Lakh.
*   Graduates can find roles as Physiotherapists, Lead Physiotherapists, and Physical Education Teachers, with opportunities in hospitals and government sectors.

## MPT Course Overview

The MPT program deepens understanding of human anatomy, physiology, and therapeutic techniques. It prepares students for advanced clinical practice, research, and teaching roles in physiotherapy. Specializations within MPT allow students to focus on specific areas, enhancing their expertise.

## Eligibility and Admission Process for MPT 2026

To be eligible for an MPT program, candidates generally need to have completed a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) degree. Specific percentage requirements in the BPT program may vary by institution, often around 50%.

For admission in 2026, the process typically involves:

*   **BPT Degree:** Possessing a BPT degree or an equivalent undergraduate healthcare degree.
*   **Entrance Exam:** Appearing for a national-level entrance exam such as CUET PG. Performance in this exam is crucial for securing admission.
*   **Application:** Submitting applications to desired universities or institutes, often using scores from the relevant entrance exam.
*   **Counselling and Document Verification:** Participating in counselling sessions conducted by the universities based on entrance exam ranks and completing verification of academic and other required documents.
*   **Fee Payment:** Finalizing admission by paying the course fees.

## MPT Course Fees in India

The cost of an MPT program in India can vary widely depending on the type of institution (government or private), its reputation, and the facilities offered. Generally, MPT course fees can range from approximately INR 1 Lakh to INR 15 Lakh for the entire two-year program. It is always advisable to check the official prospectus of individual colleges for precise fee structures.

## MPT Syllabus and Specializations

The MPT syllabus is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge in advanced physiotherapy. While the core curriculum covers subjects like advanced biomechanics, research methodology, and rehabilitation sciences, students also choose specializations. Common specializations include:

*   **Orthopaedic Physiotherapy:** Focusing on musculoskeletal conditions.
*   **Neurological Physiotherapy:** Dealing with neurological disorders.
*   **Sports Physiotherapy:** Specializing in sports injuries and performance enhancement.
*   **Cardio-Pulmonary Physiotherapy:** Addressing heart and lung conditions.
*   **Community Based Rehabilitation:** Focusing on public health and community-level interventions.
*   **Geriatric Physiotherapy:** Concentrating on the unique needs of older adults.
*   **Paediatric Physiotherapy:** Working with children and developmental conditions.

The specific subjects and specializations offered can vary between institutions. Candidates should review the detailed syllabus on the official college websites.

## Career Scope and Salary after MPT

An MPT degree opens up diverse career opportunities in India and abroad. Graduates can work in hospitals, rehabilitation centers, sports clinics, private practices, and academic institutions. Some common job profiles include:

*   **Physiotherapist:** Providing direct patient care and developing treatment plans.
*   **Lead Physiotherapist/Senior Physiotherapist:** Overseeing teams and managing complex cases.
*   **Physical Education Teacher:** In educational settings, particularly those with a focus on health and sports.
*   **Clinical Instructor:** Guiding BPT students in practical settings.
*   **Researcher:** Contributing to advancements in physiotherapy through research.

Top recruiters for MPT graduates include major hospital chains like Apollo Hospitals and Fortis Hospital, as well as specialized clinics and even the Defence Ministry of India. The salary for MPT graduates can vary based on experience, specialization, and the employing organization. Entry-level salaries might start from around INR 3.3 LPA, with experienced professionals potentially earning upwards of INR 8.1 LPA or more.

An MPT degree equips you with advanced skills and knowledge, positioning you for a rewarding career in the evolving field of physical rehabilitation.
