# AI ML Bootcamp by TalentSprint: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen on diving into the world of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, the TalentSprint AI ML Bootcamp offers a structured pathway. This program aims to equip participants with essential skills in Python programming, data handling, and core AI/ML algorithms.

## Key takeaways

*   The bootcamp is a 26-week online program focused on practical AI and Machine Learning skills.
*   It offers a curriculum developed with industry relevance in mind, including capstone projects.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹2,00,000, with scholarship options reducing it to ₹1,50,000, and various EMI plans available.
*   Eligibility requires a strong academic record (60% in 10th, 12th, and engineering) and a recent engineering degree (2023-2025 graduates) in specific branches.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

This AI ML Bootcamp is designed to provide comprehensive training in critical areas such as Python programming, exploring data, framing and solving problems, understanding AI/ML algorithms, and applying deep learning methods. The program emphasizes hands-on learning, allowing participants to apply their newly acquired skills to real-world challenges through capstone projects.

The entire course is delivered online over 26 weeks, featuring live virtual classes led by expert instructors. It includes flexible learning options, weekly assessments to track progress, mock interviews, and dedicated placement support to help graduates transition into relevant roles.

## Who Should Consider This Bootcamp?

This program is particularly well-suited for recent engineering graduates looking to specialize in high-demand technology fields. Specifically, it targets graduates from Computer Science Engineering (CSE), Information Technology (IT), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), and Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) branches. If you graduated in 2023, 2024, or are set to graduate in 2025, and consistently maintained at least 60% marks in your 10th, 12th, and engineering degrees, you meet the academic eligibility criteria.

The skills gained are highly relevant for aspiring software developers, software engineers, and software quality assurance engineers who wish to integrate AI and ML expertise into their careers.

## Fees and Payment Options

The standard fee for the AI ML Bootcamp is ₹2,00,000, which includes GST. TalentSprint also offers scholarship opportunities that can reduce the program cost to ₹1,50,000. 

Here’s a breakdown of the fee structure:

### Without Scholarship

| Fee Type        | Amount      |
| :-------------- | :---------- |
| Total Fee       | ₹2,00,000   |
| Registration Fee| ₹20,000     |
| Balance Fee     | ₹1,80,000   |

### With Scholarship

| Fee Type        | Amount      |
| :-------------- | :---------- |
| Total Fee       | ₹1,50,000   |
| Registration Fee| ₹20,000     |
| Balance Fee     | ₹1,30,000   |

To make the program accessible, various EMI options are available for those who prefer to pay in installments. The monthly payment ranges depend on the chosen tenure:

| :-------- | :---------------------- |
| 6 months  | ₹41,251 - ₹41,532       |

| 18 months | ₹15,029 - ₹15,155       |
| 24 months | ₹11,751 - ₹11,858       |
| 30 months | ₹9,784 - ₹9,880         |
| 36 months | ₹8,473 - ₹8,561         |

Upon successful completion of the bootcamp, participants receive a certificate directly from TalentSprint.

## How to Apply

Applying for the AI ML Bootcamp is a straightforward online process. Interested candidates should visit the official TalentSprint website dedicated to this bootcamp. On the program page, look for an "Apply Now" button. You will then need to fill out the required application form with your details. Once all information is submitted and the initial fees are processed, you will gain access to the course materials and live sessions.

This bootcamp offers a robust opportunity to gain expertise in AI and Machine Learning, paving the way for promising career advancements in the tech industry.
