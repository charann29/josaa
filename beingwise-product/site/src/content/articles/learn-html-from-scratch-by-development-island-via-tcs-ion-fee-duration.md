# Learn HTML from Scratch by Development Island via TCS iON: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Starting your journey in web development often begins with HTML. This course, offered by Development Island through TCS iON, is designed to introduce you to the fundamentals of HTML, enabling you to build and manage your own websites from the ground up.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to structure and design websites using core HTML concepts, including HTML5 features.
*   Gain practical experience through a hands-on approach with video lectures, a guidebook, and source code.
*   Receive a certificate of completion from Development Island after successfully finishing the course.
*   Enjoy the flexibility of a self-paced, week-long program requiring approximately six hours of effort.

## Course Overview and What You Will Learn

This online certification program is ideal for complete beginners with no prior coding experience, as well as those who have some familiarity with web development and wish to strengthen their HTML skills. The curriculum focuses on teaching you how to create, structure, and manage websites by writing HTML code.

Upon completing the course, you will have a solid understanding of HTML programming, including how to write effective HTML code for web development projects. You'll learn the standard practices for website structure and setup, and be introduced to HTML5 programming, which is crucial for modern web design due to its enhanced features.

## Course Structure and Content

The "Learn HTML from Scratch" course is structured to provide a comprehensive yet concise learning experience. It includes approximately two hours of video content spread across 21 lectures. Alongside the videos, you'll receive a 21-page course guidebook that contains all the necessary source codes, allowing for practical, hands-on learning.

The course follows a results-oriented methodology, emphasising practical application. To test your understanding and reinforce learning, a quiz is included at the end of the program.

### Syllabus at a Glance

*   Introduction and Source Code
*   Creating Your First Website
*   Setting Up Free Hosting with FireFTP
*   Headings, Paragraphs, Links, and Images
*   Inline vs. Block Elements
*   Iframes (Activity: Embedding a YouTube Video)
*   Lists
*   Tables, Entities, and Forms
*   Text Decoration and Comments
*   Test Your Knowledge

## Program Highlights and Flexibility

The course offers several benefits designed for effective and flexible learning:

*   **Certificate of Completion:** Awarded by Development Island upon successful completion.
*   **Duration:** A focused one-week course.
*   **Effort:** Requires an estimated six hours of study time.
*   **Pacing:** Self-paced, allowing you to learn at your convenience.
*   **Medium:** All video content is in English.
*   **Resources:** Includes downloadable materials and source codes.
*   **Assessments:** Features quizzes to evaluate your learning.
*   **Access:** Provides unlimited access to course materials.
*   **Accessibility:** Content is available on mobile devices.

## Course Fee and Certification

There is a course fee for this program, which grants you access to all learning materials and the final assessment. Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate from Development Island, confirming your understanding of HTML fundamentals.

## Who is This Course For?

This course is particularly beneficial for aspiring web developers and software engineers who need to build a strong foundation in HTML. It's also suitable for anyone interested in understanding how websites are built and gaining the skills to create their own web pages.

Embark on your web development journey today and master the language of the web with this foundational HTML course.
