# Secondary: Social Science (213) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course offers an in-depth exploration of Social Science topics at the secondary school level, making it ideal for students and anyone keen to understand India and the world better. Delivered through the Swayam platform, it provides a flexible learning experience from the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS).

## Key takeaways

*   Gain a comprehensive understanding of social science concepts relevant to secondary education.
*   Earn a valuable certificate jointly issued by NIOS and Swayam upon successful completion.
*   Benefit from a structured learning path with video lectures, reading materials, and interactive forums.
*   The course awards 10 credit points, acknowledging your academic effort and achievement.

## Course Overview

The Secondary: Social Science (213) program is designed as a Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) to provide accessible education in social sciences. It covers essential subjects that trace the development of India and the global landscape through various historical periods. The curriculum is structured into 4 modules, comprising a total of 27 lessons, and is taught by Dr. Azmat Noori from the National Institute of Open Schooling.

## Learning Experience and Content

This self-paced course primarily uses English as the medium of instruction. It combines video lectures with comprehensive text-based reading materials to facilitate learning. Participants can engage with their peers through discussion forums, fostering a collaborative learning environment. The program aims to impart knowledge across several key areas:

*   **History:** Explore India and the world through ancient, medieval, and modern periods, including the impact of British rule and the Indian National Movement.
*   **Geography:** Understand India's physiography, climate, biodiversity, agriculture, transport, communication, and population dynamics.
*   **Political Science:** Delve into India's constitutional values, political system, fundamental rights and duties, local and national governance, and the democratic process.
*   **Sociology/Economics:** Learn about socio-economic development, challenges to Indian democracy, national integration, secularism, and disaster management.

## Duration and Certification

Designed to be completed over 24 weeks, this program offers a flexible schedule for learners. Upon successfully finishing the course content and passing the required evaluations, participants will receive a Secondary: Social Science (213) Certification. This certificate is jointly provided by the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) and the Swayam platform, adding significant value to your academic profile.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Secondary: Social Science (213) course on Swayam is a straightforward process. Interested candidates should:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal for the course.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the subsequent page, you can either sign in with existing credentials or create a new account to proceed with your enrollment.

| Week(s) | Topics Covered |
| :------ | :------------- |
| 1-4     | Introduction to Social Science, Ancient, Medieval, and Modern World History |
| 5-8     | Impact of British Rule, Religious & Social Awakening, Popular Resistance, Indian National Movement |
| 9-14    | Physiography, Climate, Bio-Diversity, Agriculture, Transport & Communication, Population Consumption |
| 15-22   | Constitutional Values, Political System, Fundamental Rights & Duties, Governance (Local, State, Union), Political Parties, People's Participation |
| 23-26   | Challenges to Indian Democracy, National Integration & Secularism, Socio-Economic Development, Economic Degradation & Disaster Management, Peace & Security |

This course is an excellent opportunity for anyone looking to strengthen their foundational knowledge in social sciences at the secondary level, offering a recognized certification and a rich learning experience.
