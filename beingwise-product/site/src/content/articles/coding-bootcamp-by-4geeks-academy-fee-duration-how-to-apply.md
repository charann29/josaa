# Coding Bootcamp by 4Geeks Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Embarking on a career in technology often requires practical, up-to-date skills. The Coding Bootcamp by 4Geeks Academy offers an intensive program designed to equip students with the knowledge needed to become proficient full-stack developers.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 16-week certificate program focusing on full-stack development.
*   The course blends virtual classroom sessions with self-study, requiring a significant time commitment.
*   Participants gain expertise in both front-end and back-end technologies, along with career support.
*   Scholarships and flexible payment plans are available to help manage the course fees.

## Course Overview and Structure

The 4Geeks Academy Coding Bootcamp is a 16-week certificate course crafted to build a strong understanding of computer logic and programming principles. It delves into how different components of an application interact, helping students grasp the complete picture of software development.

Classes are held three times a week, but students are expected to dedicate at least nine additional hours weekly to self-study and assignments. This blend of structured learning and independent practice ensures a comprehensive learning experience. The medium of instruction is English, and the course content is delivered through a mix of video and text-based materials.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of the bootcamp, participants will have mastered essential programming and coding skills necessary for developing real-world applications. The curriculum is designed to foster a problem-solving mindset and creative thinking in programming.

The core areas of learning include:

*   **Front-end development:** Building user interfaces with technologies like HTML, CSS, and React.js.
*   **Back-end development:** Server-side programming using Python and Flask.
*   **Database management:** Working with SQL to manage and retrieve data.
*   **Other key concepts:** Understanding the Document Object Model (DOM) and distributed process management.

Graduates will be proficient in creating responsive web designs and implementing various web platforms, opening doors to diverse job opportunities in the tech sector.

## Who Is This Bootcamp For?

This certification program is ideally suited for individuals aspiring to launch or advance their careers in software development. It provides a robust foundation for roles such as:

*   Full Stack Developer
*   Programmer
*   Junior Developer

The program offers lifetime access to technical and career support, connecting graduates with one of the largest coding communities globally.

## Fees and Financial Aid

The fees for the Coding Bootcamp can be managed through various options. Students may be eligible for scholarships, with potential awards of up to €2300. Additionally, 4Geeks Academy provides tailored financing plans, allowing payments to be spread across 4 to 30 installments, with options for interest-free plans.

## How to Apply

The application process for the Coding Bootcamp is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official 4Geeks Academy website for the Coding Bootcamp.
2.  Locate and click on the 'APPLY NOW' option, which will redirect you to the application page.
3.  Fill out the required information accurately on the application form.
4.  Submit your application. The course administrators will then contact you to guide you through the subsequent steps of the admission process.

This bootcamp aims to provide a fast-paced yet thorough pathway for a career transition into the dynamic world of coding.
