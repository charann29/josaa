# Top Medical Colleges in Maharashtra 2026: Ranking, Fees, Admission, NEET UG Cutoff

For students aspiring to pursue a medical career, Maharashtra offers several reputable institutions. This guide provides an overview of top medical colleges in the state, covering key aspects like admission criteria, fees, and rankings.

## Key takeaways

*   Maharashtra is home to a select number of highly-regarded medical colleges, including both government and private institutions.
*   The NEET UG examination is the primary entrance test for securing admission to MBBS programs in these colleges.
*   Eligibility typically requires a strong academic record in science subjects during Class 12, along with meeting specific age and domicile criteria.
*   Notable institutions include Dr. D.Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Datta Meghe Institute of Higher Education and Research, and Armed Forces Medical College.

## Overview of Medical Colleges in Maharashtra

As of the latest data, there are three prominent medical colleges recognized in Maharashtra. Of these, two are government-affiliated, while one operates as a private institution. Admission to these colleges primarily relies on performance in the NEET UG exam.

### Fees at a Glance

When considering the financial aspect, one government college has an annual fee of less than INR 1 lakh. The remaining two colleges, including the private institution, have fees exceeding INR 5 lakh per year. Specific course fees will be detailed in the respective college sections.

## Admission Process and Eligibility for 2026

While specific requirements can vary slightly between institutions, the fundamental eligibility criteria for medical and health science courses in Maharashtra are generally consistent:

*   **Academic Qualification:** Candidates must have successfully completed their Class 12 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Zoology/Botany as core science subjects. English must also have been a main subject in the qualifying exam.
*   **Age Limit:** Applicants must be 17 years of age or older at the time of admission.
*   **Entrance Exam:** A valid score in the NEET UG exam is mandatory.
*   **Domicile Requirements:** For state quota seats, students must fulfill the Maharashtra NEET Domicile requirements.
*   **MCI Guidelines:** All applicants must meet the eligibility standards set by the Medical Council of India (MCI).
