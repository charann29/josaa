# Machine Learning with PySpark for Big Data by Jigsaw Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's data-driven world, the ability to process and analyze vast amounts of information is crucial for those aspiring to careers in Artificial Intelligence and Big Data. This certification course from Jigsaw Academy offers a focused program designed to equip learners with essential skills in Machine Learning using PySpark for big data applications.

## Key Takeaways

*   A three-month, self-paced online certification course.
*   Focuses on Spark MLlib, using Python or Scala for large-scale data processing.
*   Includes case studies, quizzes, assignments, and faculty support.
*   Provides a certificate from Jigsaw Academy upon successful completion.

## Course Overview and Structure

This program is specifically crafted for individuals interested in the intersection of machine learning and big data, particularly those who want to leverage a unified analytics engine for large-scale processing. The curriculum places a strong emphasis on Spark MLlib, allowing participants to work with either Python or Scala, two prominent languages in the data science landscape.

Being a self-paced online course, it offers significant flexibility, allowing learners to delve into each module thoroughly without feeling rushed. Beyond the core content, the program also provides additional reading materials to encourage continuous learning and deeper exploration of concepts.

## Program Offerings and Learning Experience

The course is delivered entirely online, providing learners with convenient access to all materials from their preferred location. The learning experience is enhanced through various interactive components:

*   **Self-Paced Learning:** Study at your own rhythm, fitting the course around your existing commitments.
*   **Online Access:** All course content, including video and text-based modules, is available digitally.
*   **Case Studies:** Apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios through practical examples.
*   **Quizzes and Assignments:** Test your understanding and reinforce learning with regular assessments.
*   **Faculty & Technical Support:** Access assistance from instructors and technical teams when needed.
*   **Mobile Access:** Learn on the go with content accessible from mobile devices.

Learners will gain proficiency in key tools and technologies like Python, Apache Spark, Spark Streaming, and Spark MLlib, all of which are highly sought after in the industry.

## Eligibility and Certification Process

### Who is this course for?

This program is ideal for aspiring data scientists or anyone with a foundational understanding of Python who wishes to specialize in machine learning for big data environments.

### Prerequisites

Candidates should possess primary knowledge of Python to make the most of this program. While the course covers various tools, a basic grasp of Python is considered beneficial for entry.

### Certification

Upon completing all course modules, candidates become eligible to take a certification test. Successful completion of this test, administered by Jigsaw Academy, will lead to the issuance of a certificate within approximately one month of passing.

## Course Fees

The total fees for the Machine Learning with PySpark for Big Data program are structured as follows:

| Type of Fee       | Amount     |
| :---------------- | :--------- |
| Programme Fees    | ₹ 24,000   |
| Taxes (18%)       | ₹ 4,320    |
| **Total Fees**    | **₹ 28,320** |

These fees cover all aspects of the program, including access to course materials, support, and the certification process.

## How to Apply

The application process for this program is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official Jigsaw Academy website to locate the specific program page for "Machine Learning with PySpark for Big Data."
2.  Register or sign up on the Jigsaw Academy platform. You can typically use existing accounts like Gmail, Facebook, or LinkedIn for convenience.
3.  Once registered and logged in, find and click the 'Enroll Now' button associated with the course.
4.  Follow the subsequent instructions to complete your enrollment and payment.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to enhance your skills in a rapidly evolving field, preparing you for advanced roles in data science and artificial intelligence.
