# Advanced Training by Google: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's rapidly evolving educational landscape, integrating technology into the classroom is no longer an option but a necessity. Google offers an Advanced Training program designed to equip educators with the skills to effectively leverage its suite of tools for enhanced teaching and learning.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a free, self-paced online program offered directly by Google.
*   Upon completion, participants receive the Google Certified Educator Level 2 certification.
*   The course focuses on mastering various Google products for classroom application, covering approximately 15.1 hours of content.
*   It is ideal for teachers at all levels, from kindergarten to high school, who wish to enhance their digital teaching skills.

## What is Google's Advanced Training Program?

This intermediate-level online course is specifically crafted for educators looking to deepen their understanding of how Google tools can transform their classrooms. It's an excellent opportunity for teachers to become proficient in using technology to create engaging and dynamic learning environments.

The program is entirely self-paced, allowing participants to learn at their own convenience through a mix of video and text-based materials. The instruction medium is English, making it accessible to a wide audience.

## What You Will Learn

The Advanced Training Certification syllabus guides learners through a comprehensive exploration of various Google tools and products, demonstrating their practical application in diverse teaching and learning scenarios. Beyond just using the tools, the course also covers strategies for connecting with parents and guardians and effectively utilising online educational resources. Key Google tools and products covered include:

*   Google Sites
*   Blogger
*   Google Docs
*   Google Drive
*   Google Forms
*   Google Drawings
*   Google Sheets
*   Gmail
*   Google Slides
*   Google Chrome
*   Google Calendar
*   Google Learning Centre
*   Google Classroom
*   YouTube (creating playlists)

### Program Structure

The training is divided into several units, each focusing on different aspects of digital education:

*   **Unit 1: Promote and Model the Effective Use of Digital Tools** - Covers setting up mentorship, curating online resources, and general digital tool integration.
*   **Unit 2: Leverage Learning Models to Personalize Learning** - Explores alternate learning models and using Google tools for personalised education and visual showcases of student knowledge.
*   **Unit 3: Use Advanced Features to Optimize Workflow** - Focuses on creating shared calendars, optimising Gmail features, sharing Chrome apps/extensions, and automating tasks with add-ons.
*   **Unit 4: Connect with Guardians** - Guides educators on effective communication and organisation when interacting with parents and guardians.

## Who Should Take This Course?

While anyone interested in applying technology, especially Google tools, in an educational setting can benefit, this program is highly recommended for teachers. Specifically, high school teachers, kindergarten teachers, and other educators will find the content directly applicable to their professional roles. It's for those who want to upgrade their skills and become more adept at integrating digital solutions into their daily teaching practices.

## Course Fee and Certification

One of the most appealing aspects of this Advanced Training is that it is completely free of charge. There are no fees associated with accessing the training materials or completing the course. Upon successful completion, Google itself provides the certification, specifically the Google Certified Educator Level 2. This certification is a valuable credential for any educator looking to demonstrate their proficiency in using Google tools for teaching.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Advanced Training program is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official Google training portal for this program. (Look for it on the official Google for Education website).
2.  Log in using your existing Google account or create a new one if you don't have one.
3.  Once logged in, you can begin the free training modules at your own pace.

This program offers a fantastic opportunity for educators to enhance their digital literacy and bring innovative teaching methods into their classrooms, all while earning a recognised certification from Google.
