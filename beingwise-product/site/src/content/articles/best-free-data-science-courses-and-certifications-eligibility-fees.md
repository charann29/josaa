# Best Free Data Science Courses & Certifications - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Career Options

Data science is a rapidly growing field that combines statistics, computer science, and domain expertise to extract insights from data. For aspiring professionals, numerous free online courses offer an excellent starting point to build foundational knowledge and essential skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   Free data science courses provide a cost-effective way to explore the field and gain valuable skills.
*   Many programs are offered by top global institutions like MIT and IITs, ensuring high-quality content.
*   Certification often requires a paid upgrade or passing an exam, even for otherwise free courses.
*   These courses can open doors to career opportunities in various industries, from healthcare to engineering.

## Collaborative Data Science for Healthcare (MIT via edX)

This unique program from MIT focuses on the critical application of data science within healthcare, especially highlighting the need for localized research. While traditional research often generalizes findings from developed countries, this course emphasizes integrating data and research at the core of all healthcare operations.

The syllabus covers various data science tools used in healthcare, featuring numerous exercises and hands-on workshops. It promotes a multidisciplinary approach, ideally encouraging clinicians and computer scientists to learn together. Taught by members of the MIT Critical Data team, this is a 12-week, self-paced online course. You can choose a free audit mode for limited access or a paid mode for unlimited access and a completion certificate. Key skills covered include machine learning, data science knowledge, and artificial intelligence.

## Learning Analytics Tools (IIT Bombay via Swayam + NPTEL)

Offered by IIT Bombay through the Swayam platform and NPTEL, this 12-week course is designed to introduce learners to various tools used in learning analytics. While specific syllabus details are not provided here, courses from IIT Bombay are generally rigorous and provide a strong academic foundation. This program is available free of charge.

## Data Science for Engineers (IIT Madras via Swayam + NPTEL)

This eight-week online program from IIT Madras, in collaboration with NPTEL, is specifically tailored for engineers looking to delve into data science. It explores two core concepts: Big Data and Data Science, which involves using machine learning and statistical techniques to analyze raw data and uncover patterns.

Professors Raghunathan Rangasamy and Shankar Narasimhan teach the course, aiming to introduce participants to the R programming language, mathematical foundations for data science, and essential data science algorithms. It also provides practical knowledge through case studies. To qualify for certification, candidates must submit periodic assignments and pass an offline end-of-term exam. The program is recognized by organizations such as Honeywell, Gyan Data Pvt. Ltd., Ford, and ABB, making it valuable for career progression in similar industries. The primary skill covered is data science knowledge.

## Spatial Data Science and Applications (Yonsei University via Coursera)

This specialized course from Yonsei University in Seoul focuses on understanding data science and its applications within a spatial context. With the increasing need for programming and data analysis across industries, companies require employees skilled in analyzing data structures and formats.

The curriculum thoroughly explains topics like Database Management Systems (DBMS), Spatial Database Management Systems (SDBMS), and Hadoop Distributed File System (HDFS). This online course offers flexible deadlines, making it a convenient option for learners seeking in-depth knowledge in spatial data science.
