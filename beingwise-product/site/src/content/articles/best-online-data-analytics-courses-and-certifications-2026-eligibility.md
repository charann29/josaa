# Best Online Data Analytics Courses & Certifications 2026 - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Scope

Data analytics has become a crucial skill across many industries, making online courses a popular way to gain expertise without interrupting your current commitments. This guide explores some of the top online degree programs in data analytics, helping you understand what they offer.

## Key takeaways

*   Leading institutions like IIT Patna and IIT Kanpur offer specialized online degrees in data analytics and related fields.
*   Programs range from undergraduate (BCA, BS) to postgraduate (M.Sc), catering to different academic backgrounds.
*   Many courses feature industry-relevant curricula, practical projects, and flexible study options.
*   Graduates are well-prepared for roles such as Data Analyst, Business Analyst, Software Developer, and can pursue further higher education.

## Online BS Computer Science and Data Analytics at IIT Patna

IIT Patna offers a comprehensive four-year Bachelor of Science (BS) in Computer Science and Data Analytics. This program is delivered in a hybrid format, blending online learning with opportunities for campus immersion. The curriculum is designed to prepare students for data-centric careers by focusing on core areas like data analytics, algorithms, database systems, and computational theory.

A unique feature of this BS degree is its flexible exit options. Students can receive a Foundation Certificate after one year, a Diploma Certificate after two years, and a BSc (Hons) Degree after three years. Completing the full four years leads to the prestigious BS Degree from IIT Patna. The total fee for this program is approximately ₹4,57,200.

## Online BCA Data Analytics Programs

Several institutions provide online Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) degrees with a specialization in Data Analytics, typically lasting three years.

*   **Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science (HITS), Chennai:** Their online BCA in Data Analytics focuses on equipping students with robust data science and analytics skills. Graduates are prepared for roles like Software Developer, System Engineer, Software Consultant, and Web Developer, or can pursue higher studies. The program fee is around ₹1,05,000.

*   **Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU Online), Belagavi:** VTU Online offers a three-year BCA in Data Analytics, with an option to extend completion by up to two additional years for flexibility. This fully online program covers programming, databases, and data handling, emphasizing practical learning through assignments and projects. It prepares students for roles such as Data Analyst and Business Analyst, and provides a foundation for further education like MCA or MBA.

## Online M.Sc Economics and Data Analytics at IIT Kanpur

For those seeking advanced knowledge, IIT Kanpur offers a two-year online Master of Science (M.Sc) in Economics and Data Analytics. This postgraduate program combines in-depth economic theory with advanced data analytics tools. It trains learners to interpret complex datasets and solve real-world economic problems in finance, policy, and business. The curriculum emphasizes hands-on training, blending core economic principles with practical data application.
