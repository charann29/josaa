# Big Data and Hadoop & Spark by Board Infinity: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This article outlines the Big Data and Hadoop & Spark certification course offered by Board Infinity, designed to equip individuals with essential skills in managing and analyzing complex datasets using leading industry tools.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn to handle and analyze vast amounts of data using key technologies like Hadoop and Apache Spark.
*   The course includes live online sessions, practical case studies, and assignments to reinforce learning.
*   Successful completion earns you a certificate from Board Infinity, validating your expertise in big data analytics.
*   The program is delivered through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content.

## Course Overview

Board Infinity's Big Data and Hadoop & Spark certification course is specifically crafted for job seekers and students aiming to enhance their skills in the rapidly growing field of big data. The program focuses on teaching you how to systematically gather and analyze datasets that are too large and complex for traditional processing methods. You'll gain proficiency in critical technologies such as Hadoop and Apache Spark, which are fundamental to modern data analysis.

The curriculum covers a wide array of topics, including HDFS, YARN, Hive, PySpark, NoSQL, and various big data analytics techniques. Beyond theoretical knowledge, the course emphasizes practical application through live video classes, real-world case studies, and hands-on assignments. Upon finishing the course, you will receive a certificate from Board Infinity, confirming your understanding of these crucial big data analytics concepts.

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this certification, you will have a solid grasp of big data fundamentals and an introduction to Hadoop and Spark for data analytics. The program will teach you about their core concepts and basic functionalities. Key learning areas include:

*   **Big Data with Hadoop:** Introduction to big data, Hadoop architecture, distributed storage (HDFS), data ingestion, ETL processes, and the Map-Reduce framework.
*   **HDFS & YARN:** Understanding the uses of big data analytics, challenges with traditional systems, the motivation behind Hadoop, and its ecosystem.
*   **Big Data with Spark:** Spark architecture, Resilient Distributed Datasets (RDDs), JOINS, Spark SQL, and performance optimization.
*   **Big Data with PySpark:** Introduction to PySpark, RDDs, DataFrames, transformations, and data processing techniques.
*   **Big Data with Hive:** Hive as a data warehouse, partitioning, bucketing, and Hive QL.

## Program Offerings and Mentorship

This online course provides more than just learning modules. Participants benefit from expert mentorship, gaining insights and guidance from professionals in the field. You'll have unlimited access to learning resources, ensuring you can revisit content as needed. The course content is also accessible on mobile devices, offering flexibility for learning on the go.

Experienced data scientists and analysts, including Mr. Gowtham Jayachandiran, Mr. Sumit Shukla, Mr. Anurag Mukherjee, Mr. Rajanikant Ghate, Mr. Kadhiravan Jayachandiran, Mr. Akhil Thimmaiah, Mr. Saandeep Sreerambatla, and Mr. Vinal Reddy, serve as instructors. Their diverse backgrounds from various companies bring valuable real-world perspectives to the classroom.

## Course Fee and Certification

The fee for the Big Data and Hadoop & Spark certification course is ₹15,000, reduced from an original price of ₹20,000. Upon successfully meeting the course requirements, Board Infinity will issue a certificate of completion. This certificate serves as official recognition of your acquired skills in big data analytics.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for individuals aspiring to or currently working in roles such as:

*   Data Scientist
*   IT Engineer
*   Big Data Analytics Engineer
*   Big Data Engineer

It provides a valuable skill upgrade for professionals looking to specialize in handling and interpreting large datasets.

Embark on your journey to master big data analytics and enhance your career prospects with this comprehensive program.
