# SNAP Registration 2026: Dates, Fees, Document, How to Apply @snaptest.org

For aspiring MBA students aiming for Symbiosis International University (SIU) institutes, the Symbiosis National Aptitude (SNAP) Test is a crucial step. This page provides a comprehensive guide to the SNAP 2026 registration process, covering key dates, fees, required documents, and a step-by-step application guide.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Application Window:** SNAP 2026 registration is expected to commence in the first week of August 2026.
*   **Online Process:** The entire application, including fee payment, is conducted online via the official SNAPTEST portal.
*   **Multiple Attempts:** Candidates can choose to appear for up to three SNAP exam sessions, typically held in December.
*   **Institute Selection:** Applicants must select their preferred SIU institutes and MBA/PGDM programs during the application process.

## SNAP 2026 Important Dates and Schedule

SIU typically announces the detailed schedule for SNAP in August. While specific dates for 2026 will be confirmed then, here’s an anticipated timeline based on previous years:

## SNAP Registration Process: A Step-by-Step Guide

To ensure a smooth application, it is recommended to use a desktop or laptop with a stable internet connection and a compatible browser (such as Internet Explorer 6.0+, Mozilla Firefox, or Google Chrome). Avoid using smartphones for registration. Here’s how to apply for SNAP 2026:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Go to the official SNAPTEST website.
2.  **Start New Registration:** Click on 'Apply Now' and then select the 'Register' option.
3.  **Read Instructions:** Carefully read all the instructions provided, then check the declaration box to proceed.
4.  **Fill Personal Details:** Enter all the required personal information accurately.
5.  **Select Exam Details:** Choose your preferred test dates and examination cities.
6.  **Upload Photograph:** Upload a recent passport-sized photograph. Ensure it meets the specified size and format requirements.
7.  **Review and Proceed:** Click 'Save and Next' to move to the next section.
8.  **Pay SNAP Test Fee:** Make the payment for the SNAP entrance exam fee, which is INR 2,250.
9.  **Enter Academic and Program Details:** Provide your academic qualifications, select the MBA/PGDM courses you are interested in, and fill in your address details.
10. **Select Institutes and Pay Fees:** The system will then guide you to a payment window for selecting and paying the application fees for the individual SIU institutes you wish to apply to. Complete this payment to finalize your application.

## SNAP Registration Fees 2026

The application process involves two distinct fee payments:

*   **SNAP Test Fee:** INR 2,250 (Non-refundable) for appearing in the SNAP exam.
*   **Institute Application Fee:** An additional fee of INR 1,000 per SIU institute and per program you apply to. This fee is also non-refundable.

Candidates must pay both fees to complete their application to SIU programs.

## Documents to Upload During SNAP 2026 Application

Applicants will need to upload specific documents during the online registration. The most critical document is a recent passport-sized photograph. Ensure the photograph is clear, taken against a light background, and meets the size and resolution specifications mentioned on the official portal. It's advisable to keep scanned copies of your academic mark sheets and certificates ready, though they might not be uploaded at the initial registration stage, they will be required for subsequent admission processes.

## SNAP Eligibility Criteria

Before applying, candidates must ensure they meet the SNAP eligibility requirements:

*   **Educational Qualification:** A Bachelor's degree from any recognized university with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe/Persons with Disability categories).
*   **Final Year Students:** Candidates appearing for their final year Bachelor's degree examination are also eligible to apply, provided they complete their degree with the required marks by the time of admission.
*   **Specific Subject Requirements:** For certain specialized MBA programs, candidates might need to have studied particular subjects at the undergraduate level.

For detailed eligibility criteria, refer to the official SNAPTEST portal.
