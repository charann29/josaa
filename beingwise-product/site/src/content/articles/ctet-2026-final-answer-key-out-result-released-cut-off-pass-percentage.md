# CTET 2026: Final Answer Key (OUT), Result (Released), Cut Off, Pass Percentage, Question Papers & More

For aspiring teachers in India, the Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET) is a crucial national-level examination. Administered by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), it serves as an essential qualification for teaching positions in central government schools like Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVS) and Navodaya Vidyalayas (NVS) for Classes 1 to 8.

## Key takeaways

*   The Final Answer Key for CTET February 2026 has been released by CBSE on the official website.
*   Results for the February session of CTET 2026 were declared on March 30, with 597,061 candidates qualifying.
*   The overall pass percentage for CTET 2026 (February session) was 25.68% across both papers.
*   A CTET qualification certificate is now valid for a lifetime, offering long-term eligibility for teaching roles.

## CTET 2026 Schedule at a glance

The CTET 2026 examination cycle for the February session commenced with the official notification on November 27. The application window was open from November 27 to December 18. The main examination was conducted on February 7 and 8, with a re-exam held on March 1. The window for submitting objections to the provisional answer key was available from March 12 to March 15.

## CTET Result 2026: Details and Statistics

The CTET February 2026 results were announced on March 30. Candidates can access their scorecards, which display the marks obtained, on the official CTET website or through the CBSE results portal. Those who successfully qualify receive an eligibility certificate.

### How to Check Your CTET Result 2026

To view your CTET result and download your scorecard, follow these steps:

*   Visit the official CTET website.
*   Locate and click on the 'CTET 2026 Result' link.
*   Enter your roll number as prompted.
*   Download your CTET scorecard.
*   It is advisable to print a copy for your records.

## CTET Certificate and Validity

Upon qualifying the CTET examination, candidates are awarded an eligibility certificate and mark sheet. These documents are made available on the DigiLocker platform shortly after the results are declared. A significant change is that the CTET certificate now holds **lifetime validity**. This means that once qualified, candidates can apply for teacher recruitment in central schools or other institutions that accept CTET scores at any point, provided they meet other eligibility criteria set by the recruiting agency or school.

## CTET Answer Key 2026

Following the examination, a provisional answer key is released, allowing candidates to review their responses and raise objections if they find discrepancies. After considering all valid objections, CBSE releases the final answer key. The Final Answer Key for CTET February 2026 is now available on the official CTET website.

Qualified candidates can now confidently pursue their career aspirations in teaching, leveraging their CTET certification to apply for various government and private school positions across the country.
