# WBJEE Exam Centres in Maharashtra: What You Need to Know

Many students from Maharashtra aspiring to study engineering or pharmacy in West Bengal often wonder if they can take the WBJEE exam closer to home. It's important to clarify the official policy regarding examination centres outside West Bengal.

## Key Takeaways

*   The WBJEE (West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations) exam is generally not conducted in Maharashtra.
*   Examination centres for WBJEE are primarily located within West Bengal.
*   Limited centres are also available in a few neighbouring states, specifically Assam and Tripura.
*   Candidates from any other state, including Maharashtra, must select an exam centre from the options provided within West Bengal, Assam, or Tripura.

## Understanding WBJEE Exam Centre Policy

The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) is responsible for conducting the WBJEE. According to the official information released by the board in their examination brochure, the geographical scope for exam centres is quite specific. The primary objective is to facilitate candidates within West Bengal, with a few strategic locations in nearby regions.

## Available Exam Centre Locations

When you register for the WBJEE, you will be presented with a list of available examination cities. For students residing in Maharashtra, it's crucial to understand that cities like Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, or Nashik will not appear on this list. The centres are typically distributed across various districts within West Bengal. Additionally, to accommodate students from the Northeast, centres are usually established in select cities in Assam and Tripura.

### List of States with WBJEE Exam Centres

*   West Bengal
*   Assam
*   Tripura

## Implications for Maharashtra Candidates

If you are a student from Maharashtra planning to appear for the WBJEE, you will need to make arrangements to travel to one of the designated examination cities. This means factoring in travel time, accommodation, and other logistics for the exam day. It's advisable to choose a centre that is convenient for you to reach, considering flight or train connectivity from Maharashtra.

## Planning Your WBJEE Application from Maharashtra

When filling out your WBJEE application form, pay close attention to the section where you select your preferred exam centre. Since Maharashtra is not an option, you will need to choose from the available cities in West Bengal, Assam, or Tripura. It's recommended to select your centre early, as choices can sometimes be limited or fill up. Always refer to the official WBJEE information brochure for the most accurate and up-to-date list of examination zones and cities for the specific year you are applying.

By understanding these guidelines, students from Maharashtra can effectively plan their WBJEE application and examination journey.
