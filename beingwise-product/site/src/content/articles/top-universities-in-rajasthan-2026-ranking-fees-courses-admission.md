# Top Universities in Rajasthan 2026: Ranking, Fees, Courses, Admission

Rajasthan, India's largest state, is a growing hub for higher education, offering a diverse range of academic programs across various disciplines. Students can find opportunities in arts, science, commerce, management, and more, delivered through traditional, distance, and residential learning formats.

## Key Takeaways

*   Rajasthan boasts several highly-ranked institutions, including private universities known for their infrastructure and industry connections.
*   The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is a key resource for evaluating universities based on various parameters like teaching, learning, resources, and placements.
*   Leading institutions like BITS Pilani and Banasthali Vidyapith consistently feature among the top universities nationally.
*   Placement opportunities and median salary packages are important considerations when choosing a university in Rajasthan.

## Understanding University Rankings

University rankings provide valuable insights into an institution's quality and performance. In India, the NIRF rankings, released by the Ministry of Education, are a widely respected benchmark. These rankings consider factors such as teaching, learning, resources, research and professional practice, graduation outcomes, outreach and inclusivity, and perception.

For 2025, BITS Pilani achieved an impressive 23rd position in the overall NIRF rankings, while Banasthali Vidyapith secured the 61st position. These rankings reflect their commitment to academic excellence and student success.

## Leading Universities in Rajasthan

Let's explore some of the top universities in Rajasthan, highlighting their key features:

### Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani

BITS Pilani is consistently recognized as one of India's premier engineering and technology institutes. It holds a strong reputation for its rigorous academic programs and excellent career prospects. According to official data, BITS Pilani maintains a high placement rate, often exceeding 90% annually. The highest salary package offered has been reported at INR 35 LPA, with a median package around INR 10.95 LPA. Top recruiting companies often include major IT and consulting firms.

### Banasthali Vidyapith

Banasthali Vidyapith is a unique residential university specifically for women, offering a wide array of courses. While its B.Tech programs generally see good placements, other disciplines also provide decent career opportunities. The median salary package for graduates from Banasthali Vidyapith has been reported around INR 3 LPA.

### Manipal University, Jaipur

Manipal University, Jaipur, is another prominent institution in the state, offering diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs. It has also been recognized in the NIRF rankings, securing the 58th position in the 2025 overall university category. The university focuses on providing a holistic educational experience with modern infrastructure and industry-relevant curricula.

## Admission Process and Fees

Admission criteria and fees vary significantly across universities and programs. For instance, BITS Pilani conducts its own entrance examination, BITSAT, for undergraduate admissions. Other universities might accept scores from national-level tests like JEE Main (for engineering) or NEET (for medical courses), or conduct their own entrance exams. Postgraduate admissions often depend on graduate-level test scores or university-specific entrance tests.

Prospective students should always refer to the official university websites for the most accurate and up-to-date information on application deadlines, eligibility criteria, and fee structures. For engineering admissions, the official JoSAA portal is a crucial resource for counselling and seat allocation based on JEE Main ranks.
