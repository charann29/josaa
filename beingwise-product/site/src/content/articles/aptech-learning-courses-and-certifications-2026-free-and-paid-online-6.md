# Aptech Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Aptech Learning Courses

Aptech Learning offers a range of career-focused courses designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills across various domains, including information technology, banking, and finance. These programs aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and professional requirements, preparing individuals for the modern job market.

## Key takeaways

*   Aptech Learning provides career-oriented certification courses in high-demand sectors like IT, banking, and finance.
*   Programs are structured to deliver practical skills and knowledge, often incorporating industry expert insights.
*   Course durations and fees vary significantly, from short-term specializations to multi-year professional diplomas.
*   The curriculum emphasizes job readiness, focusing on skills that are immediately applicable in professional roles.

## ACCP Pro – E-commerce Course

The ACCP Pro – E-commerce course by Aptech Learning is a comprehensive program designed to introduce learners to the core concepts and practical implementation of e-commerce. Spanning 23 months, this course is structured to provide a detailed understanding of the digital marketplace.

Participants will gain proficiency in several key areas:

*   **Web Productivity Tools**: Essential applications for effective online operations.
*   **Logic Building**: Foundational skills for problem-solving and programming.
*   **Next-Generation Website Development**: Techniques for creating modern, functional websites.
*   **Elementary Programming**: Basic coding skills relevant to e-commerce platforms.
*   **E-commerce Implementation**: Practical steps for setting up and managing online businesses.

Upon completion, students are expected to possess the professional skills and expertise needed to establish and sustain a career in the e-commerce industry. The total fee for this extensive program is ₹77,400.

## Banking Specialist Certification

Recognizing the robust growth of the Indian banking sector, Aptech Learning offers the Banking Specialist certification to prepare individuals for careers in this dynamic field. India's banking industry is among the largest and fastest-expanding globally, creating a continuous demand for skilled professionals.

This 16-month course, priced at ₹54,000, is divided into three parts, each focusing on different facets of banking. Students attend 2-hour in-class lectures three days a week, receiving specialized knowledge in banking and finance. The curriculum is tailored to equip learners with the fundamental understanding and practical skills necessary to navigate various banking operations and roles.

## Aptech Certified Accounts Professional

For those aspiring to a career in accounting and finance, the Aptech Certified Accounts Professional program offers an intensive 15-month training course. Priced at ₹70,000, this program is meticulously designed to provide employment-driven education, ensuring candidates gain industry-relevant knowledge.

The curriculum is structured into two comprehensive terms, covering both basic and advanced accounting principles. Key areas of study include:

*   **Basic Computer Applications**: Essential software for business accounting, including Tally.
*   **Business Communication**: Skills vital for professional interaction.
*   **Statutory Compliance**: Understanding regulatory requirements.
*   **Taxation**: Detailed knowledge of TDS, GST, and direct taxes.
*   **Advanced Accounting Techniques**: Proficiency in complex financial transactions.

Graduates of this program are expected to be job-ready, equipped with expertise in the latest accounting techniques, finance, banking, and taxation, making them eligible for immediate placement in various accounting roles.

Aptech Learning's diverse range of courses provides structured pathways for students seeking to enhance their professional skills and secure employment in competitive industries.
