# BBA in Event Management: Course Fees, Admission 2026, Subjects, Syllabus, Salary in India

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Event Management is a three-year undergraduate program designed to equip students with both core business administration skills and specialized knowledge in planning, organizing, and executing various types of events.

## Key takeaways

*   This three-year undergraduate degree combines general business principles with specific event management techniques.
*   Admission typically requires a Class 12 pass in any stream, with some institutions also considering entrance exam scores like CUET or UGAT.
*   Course fees can vary significantly, generally ranging from around INR 1 Lakh to INR 9 Lakh across different colleges.
*   Graduates can pursue diverse roles such as Event Manager, Wedding Planner, or Sponsorship Manager in a dynamic industry.

## Understanding the BBA in Event Management Program

This BBA specialization focuses on the practical aspects of event planning, from concept development and budgeting to marketing, logistics, and post-event analysis. Students learn how to manage resources, teams, and stakeholders effectively to deliver successful events, whether they are corporate conferences, music festivals, or private celebrations.

## Admission Process and Eligibility for 2026

For admission to the BBA in Event Management program in 2026, aspiring students generally need to have completed their Class 12 (or equivalent) from a recognized board, typically with a minimum aggregate score, often around 50%. Some institutions offer a relaxation in this percentage for candidates from reserved categories.

While many colleges base admissions primarily on Class 12 academic performance (merit-based), a growing number of universities also require candidates to appear for entrance examinations. Popular entrance exams for BBA programs, which may include event management specializations, are:

*   **CUET (Common University Entrance Test):** Administered by NTA, this exam is crucial for admission to many central and participating state universities.
*   **UGAT (Undergraduate Aptitude Test):** Conducted by AIMA, this test is accepted by numerous private and public universities for various undergraduate programs.
*   **University-specific tests:** Some institutions, like Symbiosis International University (SET) or Bharati Vidyapeeth (BUMAT), conduct their own entrance exams.

It is always advisable to check the specific eligibility and admission criteria on the official website of your chosen college or university for the most accurate and up-to-date information.

## Course Fees and Top Institutions

The fee structure for a BBA in Event Management can vary widely depending on the type of institution (government, private), its location, facilities, and reputation. Generally, you can expect the total course fee for the three years to range from approximately INR 1 Lakh to upwards of INR 9 Lakh.

Several reputable institutions across India offer this specialized BBA program. Some colleges known for their BBA programs that might include or offer an event management specialization are:

*   Amity University (multiple campuses)
*   NIMS Institute of Business Studies
*   MIT-WPU (MIT World Peace University)
*   Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand University (DBUU)
*   SAGE University

When evaluating colleges, consider factors like faculty expertise, industry connections, internship opportunities, and alumni success stories, in addition to NIRF rankings where available.

## BBA in Event Management Syllabus and Key Subjects

The curriculum for a BBA in Event Management is designed to provide a strong foundation in business principles alongside specialized event-specific skills. While the exact syllabus may vary by institution, common subjects typically include:

### Core Business Management Subjects

*   Principles of Management
*   Business Communication
*   Financial Accounting
*   Marketing Management
*   Human Resource Management
*   Business Law and Ethics

### Specialized Event Management Subjects

*   Introduction to Event Management
*   Event Planning and Design
*   Event Marketing and Sponsorship
*   Logistics and Operations Management
*   Risk Management and Legal Aspects of Events
*   Public Relations and Media Management
*   Venue Management
*   Catering and Hospitality Management
*   Project Management for Events
*   Event Budgeting and Financial Control

Students also often undertake practical projects, internships, and case studies to gain hands-on experience in the field.

## Career Scope and Salary Expectations

A BBA in Event Management opens doors to a vibrant and expanding industry. Graduates are well-prepared for roles in event management companies, hospitality chains, media houses, public relations firms, and even starting their own ventures. The demand for skilled event professionals continues to grow with the increasing number of corporate events, weddings, festivals, and cultural gatherings.

### Common Job Profiles:

*   **Event Manager:** Oversees all aspects of an event from conception to completion.
*   **Wedding Planner:** Specializes in organizing and executing wedding ceremonies and related events.
*   **Sponsorship Manager:** Focuses on securing funding and partnerships for events.
*   **Logistics Manager:** Handles the operational aspects, including venue setup, transportation, and equipment.
*   **Production Manager:** Manages the technical and creative elements of an event.
*   **Client Servicing Executive:** Acts as a liaison between the event company and its clients.

### Salary in India:

Starting salaries for BBA in Event Management graduates in India can range from approximately INR 3 LPA to INR 6 LPA. With experience, expertise, and a strong portfolio of successful events, professionals can see their salaries grow significantly, potentially reaching INR 10 LPA or more for senior roles.

Top recruiters often include large event management agencies, hotel chains, tourism boards (like the Indian Tourism Development Corporation - ITDC), and entertainment companies.

This program offers a dynamic career path for those with creativity, organizational skills, and a passion for bringing ideas to life.
