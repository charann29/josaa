# CUET-PG MBA Result 2026: Download Scorecard, Rank List Here

The Common University Entrance Test (PG) 2026 results for MBA programs are a crucial step for aspiring management students. The National Testing Agency (NTA) is responsible for releasing these results, which will determine eligibility for counselling and admissions into various participating universities.

## Key takeaways

*   The NTA will announce CUET-PG 2026 results, including for MBA, tentatively in April/May 2026.
*   Scorecards will be available for download from the official NTA CUET-PG portal using your application details.
*   Participating universities will conduct individual counselling processes based on these scores, as NTA does not manage counselling.
*   The result is compiled using the official answer key, with a clear marking scheme of +4 for correct and -1 for incorrect answers.

## CUET-PG 2026 Result Announcement

The National Testing Agency (NTA) is expected to release the CUET-PG 2026 results for all postgraduate programs, including MBA, around April or May 2026. This announcement will be made exclusively on the official NTA CUET-PG website. Candidates who appeared for the examination, which concluded by March 27, 2026, will be able to access their individual scorecards online.

It's important to remember that the NTA's role is to conduct the examination and declare the results. The subsequent admission processes, including counselling and seat allocation, are managed independently by the universities participating in CUET-PG.

## How to Check Your CUET-PG 2026 Result and Scorecard

To download your CUET-PG 2026 scorecard, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official CUET-PG website. The NTA will provide the direct link for results.
2.  Locate the login section for CUET-PG 2026 results.
3.  Enter your CUET-PG 2026 Application Number and your Password or Date of Birth.
4.  Input the security pin displayed on the screen and click 'Submit'.
5.  Your CUET-PG 2026 scorecard will be displayed. Download it and take several printouts for future reference. This scorecard is a mandatory document for the counselling and admission procedures.

## Understanding Your CUET-PG 2026 Score Calculation

The CUET-PG score is calculated based on a defined marking scheme. It's vital for candidates to understand this to interpret their results accurately.

*   **Correct Answers:** Each correct response is awarded four marks.
*   **Incorrect Answers:** For every incorrect answer, one mark is deducted from the total score.
*   **Unattempted Questions:** Questions that are not attempted or left blank receive no marks.

### Calculating Your Raw Score

Before the official result, candidates can estimate their raw score using the provisional answer key and their response sheet. The process involves:

1.  Downloading your response sheet and the provisional answer key from the official CUET-PG portal.
2.  Counting the total number of questions you attempted.
3.  Comparing your answers with the provisional answer key to identify correct and incorrect responses.
4.  Applying the marking scheme to calculate your raw score using the formula:
    **(Number of correct answers × 4) - (Number of incorrect answers × 1)**

## What Happens After the CUET-PG Result?

Once the CUET-PG 2026 results are declared, qualified candidates will need to prepare for the counselling process. As mentioned, the NTA does not conduct counselling. Instead, each participating university or institution will release its own merit lists and counselling schedules based on the CUET-PG scores. Candidates should regularly check the official websites of their desired universities for specific admission notices, application deadlines, and counselling details.

Ensure you keep your scorecard safe, as it is a critical document required during the entire admission journey.
