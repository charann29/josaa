# Aligarh Muslim University (AMU): Courses, Fees, Admission 2026, Placements, Ranking, Eligibility

Aligarh Muslim University (AMU), established in 1920, is a renowned public university in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh. It holds an A+ accreditation from NAAC and is recognized by the UGC, offering a vast array of programs across diverse academic fields.

## Key takeaways

*   AMU offers around 300 courses at undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral levels, spanning various disciplines.
*   Admission to many courses at AMU is based on entrance examinations, including AMU's own tests, CUET UG/PG, NEET UG/PG, and NEET MDS.
*   The university has consistently performed well in national rankings, securing the 10th position in the 'University' category by NIRF in 2025.
*   AMU provides a robust academic environment with options for both regular and distance education.

## AMU Rankings and Recognition

Aligarh Muslim University maintains a strong presence in various national and international ranking frameworks. In the QS World University Rankings 2026, AMU was placed in the 1001-1200 band. Domestically, the institution achieved the 10th rank among universities in the NIRF 2025 rankings, highlighting its academic excellence and research contributions.

## AMU Courses and Fees 2026

AMU provides a comprehensive range of approximately 300 courses across disciplines like Science, Humanities, Medicine, Engineering, and Management. These programs are available at undergraduate, postgraduate, PhD, and diploma levels. The university also facilitates distance education through its Centre for Distance and Online Education (CDOE). While specific fees vary by program, they are generally competitive for a public university.

## AMU Admission Process 2026

Admission to Aligarh Muslim University for 2026 is ongoing for various courses. Prospective students should visit the official university website to apply. The admission process combines both entrance examination scores and merit for different programs.

Key entrance examinations and dates for 2026 admissions include:

*   **CUET UG 2026**: Registration concluded; exam scheduled from May 11 to May 31, 2026, for several undergraduate courses.
*   **CUET PG 2025**: Results have been announced for postgraduate admissions.
*   **NEET PG 2026**: Tentatively scheduled for August 30, 2026, for MD, MS, and PG Diploma courses.
*   **NEET MDS 2026**: Application portal is closed; exam will be held on May 2, 2026.
*   **AMU Entrance Exam**: Results for B.Tech, B.Arch, B.A. L.L.B., B.Com (Hons), B.Sc. (Hons), and B.A. (Hons) from the 2025 cycle are expected by June 2025.

Candidates are advised to regularly check the official AMU admissions portal and relevant national testing agency websites (like NTA for CUET) for the latest updates on application deadlines, exam dates, and result declarations.
