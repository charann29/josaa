# MBA in International Finance and Accounting (with ACCA) by Chitkara University Online: Fee, Review, Duration

For professionals aiming to excel in the dynamic world of global finance and accounting, Chitkara University Online offers a specialized MBA program designed to enhance your expertise and career prospects.

## Key Takeaways

*   This online MBA is a two-year postgraduate degree combining international finance and accounting with an integrated path towards the globally recognized ACCA qualification.
*   The total program fee is ₹3.00 Lakh, with the flexibility of installment payments.
*   It is specifically tailored for working professionals looking to advance their careers in a global financial context.
*   Students benefit from comprehensive career support, including resume building, interview preparation, and placement assistance.

## Program Overview

Chitkara University Online's MBA in International Finance and Accounting is a comprehensive two-year postgraduate program delivered entirely online. This specialization aims to provide a deep understanding of global financial systems and equip students with the necessary skills to manage financial operations across international borders. A key feature is its integration with the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) qualification, preparing students for its globally recognized examinations.

## Who Should Consider This MBA?

This program is ideally suited for working professionals who aspire to elevate their careers in finance or accounting, particularly those with an interest in a global context. If your goal is to gain advanced knowledge in international financial markets, develop expertise in financial reporting and analysis, and acquire leadership skills, this MBA can be a significant step forward. It's also an excellent choice for individuals seeking to achieve the prestigious ACCA qualification alongside their MBA.

## Skills and Deliverables

Upon successful completion of this MBA, students are expected to gain a robust set of skills and knowledge:

*   **Advanced International Finance Knowledge:** A thorough understanding of global financial markets, including foreign exchange, international trade, and investment strategies.
*   **Accounting and Financial Analysis Expertise:** Proficiency in financial reporting, auditing, and analysis, enabling informed decision-making based on financial data.
*   **Leadership and Management Skills:** Development of abilities to lead teams and organizations effectively through coursework in organizational behavior, strategy, and operations management.
*   **Ethical Decision-Making:** An understanding of ethical considerations in finance, such as corruption, fraud, and conflicts of interest, fostering sound judgment in complex scenarios.
*   **ACCA Qualification Readiness:** The program provides the foundational knowledge and skills required to pursue and complete the ACCA exams, a globally recognized standard for finance professionals.

## Fee Structure and Duration

This online MBA program spans two years. The total program fee is ₹3.00 Lakh. Chitkara University Online offers the convenience of paying this fee in installments, making it more accessible for working professionals.

## Career Opportunities

Graduates of this program are well-prepared for a variety of roles within the global finance and accounting sectors. Potential career paths include:

*   Investment Banker
*   Accountant
*   Financial Analyst
*   Auditor

The university also provides 360-degree placement support, which includes assistance with resume building, interview preparation, and connecting students with potential employers. Additionally, students can benefit from over 20 masterclass sessions led by corporate leaders from the banking, financial, and accounting sectors.

This MBA offers a strategic pathway for professionals to enhance their qualifications and thrive in the competitive international finance and accounting landscape.
