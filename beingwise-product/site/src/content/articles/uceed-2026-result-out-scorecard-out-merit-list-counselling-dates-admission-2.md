# UCEED 2026: Result (OUT), Scorecard (OUT), Merit List, Counselling Dates, Admission Process

The Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design (UCEED) is a key national-level exam for students aspiring to pursue a Bachelor of Design (B.Des) degree at several prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and IIITDM Jabalpur. Administered by IIT Bombay, UCEED scores are essential for admission to these top design programs.

## Key takeaways

*   UCEED 2026 results were announced on March 6, 2026, and scorecards became available on March 10, 2026.
*   The B.Des application window for IITs and IIITDM Jabalpur for UCEED 2026 qualified candidates was from March 14 to April 10, 2026.
*   IIT Bombay manages the common application process and subsequent joint seat allocation for participating institutes.
*   UCEED scores are valid for one year from their release date and are crucial for merit-based admissions.

## UCEED 2026 Results and Scorecard

The UCEED 2026 results were officially declared by IIT Bombay on March 6, 2026. Following this, individual scorecards were made available for download from the official UCEED website starting March 10, 2026. Candidates could access their scorecards using their login credentials until July 31, 2026. It's important to note that the UCEED scorecard remains valid for a period of one year from its release date.

## B.Des Application and Joint Seat Allocation

For candidates who successfully cleared UCEED 2026, the application window for the B.Des program at participating institutes was open from March 14, 2026, to April 10, 2026. This year, the participating institutes included IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Guwahati, IIT Hyderabad, IIT Indore, IIT Roorkee, and IIITDM Jabalpur. A common application form was used for admissions to these institutions, with IIT Bombay overseeing this process and conducting the joint seat allocation. This centralized approach streamlines the admission journey for design aspirants.

## Participating Institutes and Admission Process

UCEED scores are fundamental for admission to the B.Des programs offered by the participating institutes. These scores are used to prepare merit lists, which then determine eligibility for counseling and seat allocation. While IIT Bombay conducts the UCEED exam and manages the joint seat allocation, other result-sharing institutes also utilize UCEED 2026 scores to fill their undergraduate design seats.

Here are the key institutes that accept UCEED scores for their B.Des programs:

*   IIT Bombay (IITB)
*   IIT Delhi (IITD)
*   IIT Guwahati (IITG)
*   IIT Hyderabad (IITH)
*   IIT Indore (IITI)
*   IIT Roorkee (IITR)
*   IIITDM Jabalpur (IIITDMJ)

The admission process is entirely merit-based, relying on the candidate's performance in the UCEED entrance examination. After the results are out and applications are submitted, the joint seat allocation process, managed by IIT Bombay, finalizes admissions based on merit and candidate preferences.
