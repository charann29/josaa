# Great Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Great Learning Courses

Great Learning offers a diverse range of online courses and certifications designed to help students and working professionals enhance their skills and advance their careers. These programs cover various in-demand fields, often in collaboration with leading global universities.

## Key Takeaways

*   Great Learning provides a mix of free and paid online programs across various domains.
*   Many paid programs are offered in partnership with prestigious international and Indian universities.
*   Courses often include practical projects, career support, and industry-relevant curricula.
*   Options range from short-term certifications to more extensive post-graduate and master's degree programs.

## Master's in Data Science Programme

This online Master of Science degree in Data Science is a collaboration between Northwestern University's School of Professional Studies and Great Learning. Northwestern University is consistently ranked among the top U.S. universities by publications like U.S. News & World Report.

The 18-month curriculum is crafted to provide a strong foundation in data science theory, alongside practical experience with popular programming frameworks, languages, and libraries. Students also complete a Capstone project to apply their learning. The program is developed by experienced faculty with significant industry backgrounds.

Enrolled students receive dedicated career support, including access to job boards and one-on-one guidance, to help them secure positions in leading companies. This degree can provide a significant competitive advantage in the job market.

**Duration:** 18 Months

**Fees:** Approximately $13,000

## Executive Post Graduate Program in Management (ePGPM)

The ePGPM is a 12-month executive post-graduate program in management, offered in association with Great Lakes Institute of Management (GLIM) in Chennai. This program is an excellent opportunity for both fresh graduates and working professionals looking to accelerate their careers and develop managerial and leadership skills.

The ePGPM covers a similar course structure to a full-time Post-Graduate Programme in Management but is condensed into one year. A key benefit is the ability to pursue this program while continuing with a full-time job, allowing participants to immediately apply new skills in their current roles.

**Duration:** 12 Months

**Fees:** Approximately ₹2,35,000

**Skills Covered:** Marketing, Data Science, Machine Learning, Database Management, Artificial Intelligence, Python, Social Media Marketing, Digital Marketing.

## Post Graduate Program in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

This Post Graduate Program (PGP) in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning is offered in collaboration with IIIT Delhi and the University of Texas at Austin. IIIT Delhi is recognized for its research and quality education in IT and interdisciplinary areas.

The program provides a comprehensive learning experience, building a solid foundation in Computer Science while covering essential AI and Machine Learning algorithms. It is suitable for both students and working professionals aiming to upskill in these rapidly evolving domains. Participants receive guidance throughout their learning journey and dedicated career support.

**Duration:** 12 Months

**Fees:** Approximately ₹2,75,000

**Skills Covered:** Programming, Python.

## Post Graduate Program in Data Science

This professional managerial course in Data Science is offered by Great Learning in collaboration with The McCombs School of Business at The University of Texas at Austin and Great Lakes in India. It is designed to build a successful career in Data Science and Business Analytics.

The curriculum covers the latest analytics tools and techniques, exploring their business applications through a blend of practical and theoretical knowledge. This comprehensive program is aimed at individuals seeking to master data science and business analytics for career growth.

Great Learning's collaborations with esteemed institutions ensure that its programs are current, industry-relevant, and provide valuable credentials for career advancement.
