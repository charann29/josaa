# Secondary: Economics (214) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online course offers an introduction to economics at the secondary school level, covering fundamental concepts and a detailed look into the Indian economy. Designed for self-paced learning, it's an excellent opportunity for students to build a strong foundation in the subject.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course provides 10 credit points, delivered over 24 weeks through video lectures and reading materials.
*   It is taught in English and is entirely self-study, making it flexible for learners.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS).
*   The curriculum covers core economic principles, market dynamics, and specific aspects of the Indian economic landscape.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Secondary: Economics (214) course is a comprehensive online program designed to introduce learners to the core ideas of economics. It delves into various economic branches and provides a detailed exploration of the Indian economy. The course is led by Prof. Dr. Manish Chugh from the National Institute of Open Schooling.

Participants will learn about the different types of economies, economic activities, and essential concepts such as demand, supply, and market functions. The learning experience is enriched through expert video lectures, curated reading materials, and interactive discussion forums on the Swayam platform. This structure supports a thorough understanding of the subject matter, culminating in a certification upon completion.

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this program, you will have a solid grasp of fundamental economic principles and their application, particularly within the Indian context. Key topics include:

*   Introduction to economics and its various branches.
*   Understanding goods, services, and different economic systems.
*   Basic economic activities, production, cost, and revenue.
*   Concepts of demand, supply, and market mechanisms.
*   Insights into savings, insurance, and the role of money and banking.
*   A comprehensive overview of the Indian economy, including its sectors and challenges.
*   Topics like sustainable development and consumer awareness.

## Who Is This Course For?

This course is ideal for anyone with an interest in economics, especially school students looking to strengthen their understanding of the subject. It also benefits individuals considering a future in economics or those who simply wish to gain a foundational knowledge of how economies function.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Secondary: Economics (214) course is a straightforward process through the Swayam platform:

1.  Visit the official course page on Swayam.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner.
3.  Follow the prompts to either log in with an existing account or create a new one using your username and password.

Once registered, you will gain access to all course materials and features.
