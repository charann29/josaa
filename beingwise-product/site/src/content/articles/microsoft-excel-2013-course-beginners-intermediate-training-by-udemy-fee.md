# Microsoft Excel 2013 Course Beginners/Intermediate Training by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Mastering Microsoft Excel is a highly sought-after skill in today's professional landscape, essential for data management, analysis, and presentation. This course provides comprehensive training for beginners and intermediate users looking to build a strong foundation in Excel 2013.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn at your own pace through self-study, with all content delivered via video and text.
*   Gain practical skills from basic data entry and formatting to advanced formulas and chart creation.
*   The course covers essential Excel features, including workbook management, mathematical functions, and data security.
*   Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate from Udemy, validating your new skills.

## Course Structure and Delivery

This Microsoft Excel 2013 course is designed for flexible learning. It is delivered entirely in English, making it accessible to a wide audience. The learning mode is self-study, allowing you to progress through the material at your convenience. All instructional content is provided through a combination of videos and text, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.

## Detailed Syllabus Overview

The course is structured into several modules, guiding learners from the very basics to more advanced functionalities of Excel 2013. Here's a glimpse into what you will learn:

### Getting Started

This introductory section covers essential setup, including downloading working files, understanding the Excel workbook interface, and customizing the Quick Access Toolbar.

### Managing Workbooks and Worksheets

You'll learn how to create, save, open, and navigate Excel workbooks. This module also covers adding, removing, renaming, copying, and moving worksheets, along with saving and accessing files on SkyDrive.

### Data Entry and Editing

This crucial section focuses on entering various data types (text, numbers, dates), editing cell contents, and utilizing fundamental actions like undo, redo, cut, copy, and paste. It also delves into using the Fill Handle, inserting/deleting cells, columns, and rows, and adjusting their sizes.

### Basic Mathematics and Formulas

Understand the fundamentals of mathematical formulas in Excel, including the order of operations (BODMAS). You'll be introduced to basic formulas, learn how to copy them effectively, and display formulas on your worksheet.

### Formatting Worksheets

This module covers extensive formatting options, such as modifying fonts, sizes, and colours, merging and wrapping cell contents, applying number formats, and aligning text. You'll also learn about borders, date formats, the Format Painter, and clearing cell contents versus formatting.

### Advanced Workbook Management

Beyond the basics, this section teaches you about worksheet navigation, coloured tabs, moving/copying worksheets between workbooks, viewing multiple workbooks simultaneously, and hiding/unhiding sheets.

### Page Setup and Printing

Learn to prepare your spreadsheets for printing with options like print titles, print areas, paper size, orientation, margins, and scaling. Creating headers and footers, using page break views, and controlling print settings are also covered.

### Mathematical Formulas (Advanced)

Dive deeper into Excel's mathematical capabilities with functions like SUM, AVERAGE, MIN, MAX, and various COUNT functions. Concepts like absolute referencing, formulas across worksheets, 3D referencing, and named cells/ranges are explained.

### Advanced Formatting and Security

Enhance your worksheets with themes, add/edit comments, create custom autofill lists, and convert data into tables. For security, learn to activate/deactivate worksheet protection, protect specific parts of a worksheet, and password-protect entire workbooks.

### Templates and Graphics

Explore using Excel's built-in templates and create your own. This module also covers inserting and formatting online images, SmartArt graphics, shapes, and screenshots, along with managing object layering and grouping.

### Using Charts in Excel

Discover how to effectively visualize your data using charts. Learn about the 'Recommended Charts' option, creating charts using different methods, and formatting them using Excel 2013's shortcut options.

This course offers a practical pathway to becoming proficient in Microsoft Excel 2013, equipping you with skills valuable across numerous industries.
