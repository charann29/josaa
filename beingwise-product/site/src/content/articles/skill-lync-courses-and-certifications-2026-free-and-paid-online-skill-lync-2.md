## Specialized Postgraduate Programs

Skill Lync's postgraduate programs are tailored for engineers looking to deepen their expertise in specific technical domains. These intensive, year-long programs focus on practical skills and tools essential for industry roles.

### Post Graduate Program in Power Converter Design and Analysis

This 12-month program is designed for individuals interested in the intricacies of electrical power conversion. Power converters are crucial for transforming electrical energy from one form to another, a fundamental process in modern electrical systems. The curriculum encompasses six courses and four design and analysis projects.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Design principles
*   Mathematical modeling
*   MATLAB proficiency
*   Electronics knowledge
*   Physics applications
*   Engineering fundamentals

Through hands-on experience, participants learn to design and construct advanced converters with contemporary control systems, enhancing their electrical engineering capabilities for career growth.

### CFD Engineer Master's Certification Program

For those fascinated by concepts like drag, aerodynamics, fluid dynamics, and internal combustion (IC) engine simulations, this 12-month master's certification is an excellent choice. Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) technology is vital for ensuring quality, automation, and safety in industrial processes.

This program features six courses that cover essential engineering concepts and tools for a CFD Engineer. It provides in-depth knowledge of software such as MATLAB, ANSYS Fluent, OpenFoam, Cantera, Python, ANSYS Icepak, and GT-POWER. Upon completion, participants will be well-prepared for roles like CFD Engineer or CFD Manager, with practical experience relevant to top equipment manufacturing industries.

### Post Graduate Program in CAE

This 12-month Post Graduate Program in Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) is ideal for engineers familiar with basic CAE applications for task analysis. The program includes five courses, instructing candidates in engineering theories, tools, and the mathematical principles applied in machine analysis. The flexible, pre-recorded video format allows learners to access content at their convenience.

Upon completion, participants will possess advanced knowledge of technologies and tools crucial for CAE engineers in leading Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs). The program also helps participants build a unique project portfolio, which can be linked to their resumes to showcase their certifications and work, potentially increasing their hiring prospects in top manufacturing units.
