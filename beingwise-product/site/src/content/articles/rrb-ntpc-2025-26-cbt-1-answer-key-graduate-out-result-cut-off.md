# RRB NTPC 2025-26: CBT 1 Answer Key Graduate (OUT), Result, Cut Off & Notification

The Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) conduct the Non-Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) examination, a major recruitment drive for various positions across Indian Railways. This page provides essential updates on the RRB NTPC 2025-26 exam cycle, including answer keys, results, and upcoming notifications.

## Key takeaways

*   The RRB NTPC 2026 notification is anticipated in August, along with the application forms.
*   The provisional answer key for the RRB NTPC 2025 CBT 1 Graduate posts (CEN 06/2025) was released on April 6, 2026, with an objection window open until April 12, 2026.
*   Candidates could make corrections to their RRB NTPC Graduate level application forms until December 9, 2025, with the application window closing on December 4, 2025.
*   The RRB NTPC 2025 cycle announced 3058 vacancies for undergraduate posts and 8875 for graduate posts.

## RRB NTPC 2025 Answer Key for Graduate Posts

The Railway Recruitment Boards released the provisional answer key for the RRB NTPC 2025 CBT 1 exam (CEN 06/2025) on April 6, 2026. This key is specifically for graduate-level posts, for which the exam was conducted from March 16 to March 27, 2026. Candidates can view their question papers, responses, and the official answer key on the official and regional RRB websites.

If candidates identify any discrepancies, they have the option to raise objections against the provisional answer key until April 12, 2026. A fee of INR 50 plus applicable bank service charges is required for each question an objection is raised against.

To download the answer key, candidates typically need to log in with their user ID and password. This key allows candidates to estimate their scores based on the official marking scheme.

## How to Download the RRB NTPC Answer Key

Follow these steps to access your RRB NTPC answer key:

*   Visit any of the 21 official RRB websites.
*   Look for the link related to the RRB NTPC answer key (CEN 06/2025).
*   Enter your required login credentials, such as user ID and password.
*   The answer key, along with your responses and question paper, will appear on the screen.
*   Download and save it for future reference and to calculate your probable score.

## RRB NTPC 2024 Exam Updates

For the previous cycle, the RRB NTPC Undergraduate (UG) CBT 2 result was declared on January 23, 2024. This release included the cut-off scores and individual scorecards. The CBT 2 UG exam itself took place on December 20, 2024. Furthermore, the RRB NTPC Typing Skill Test (TST) for UG posts was held on February 13, 2026, and the Computer-Based Aptitude Test (CBAT) for Graduate Level posts was conducted on December 28, 2024. However, a specific Typing Skill Test exam was cancelled, and new dates are yet to be announced. The total vacancies for the RRB NTPC 2024 recruitment stood at 11,558.

## Upcoming RRB NTPC 2026 Notification

The Railway Recruitment Boards are expected to release the official notification for RRB NTPC 2026 in August. This notification will contain comprehensive details regarding the examination, including eligibility criteria, application process, important dates, syllabus, and exam pattern. Candidates who meet the specified eligibility requirements will be able to apply once the application window opens alongside the notification.

Stay tuned to the official RRB portals for all the latest announcements and updates regarding the RRB NTPC examination cycle.
