# Is the IIM Kozhikode BMS Admit Card Out Yet?

For students eagerly awaiting news about the IIM Kozhikode Bachelor of Business Management (BMS) Aptitude Test, we have an important update. The admit cards are indeed available for download.

## Key Takeaways

*   The IIM Kozhikode BMS Aptitude Test admit cards have been released.
*   They became available on the official IIMK BMS webpage starting June 13, 2025.
*   Candidates can download their admit card by logging in with their registration credentials.
*   You will need your user ID and password from your 2025 application form submission.

## Accessing Your Admit Card

The process to download your admit card is straightforward. You need to visit the designated section on the official IIM Kozhikode website for the BMS program. Look for a link related to admit card downloads or applicant login. Once there, you will be prompted to enter your unique user ID and the password you created during your initial application for the 2025 intake.

### Why the Admit Card is Crucial

Your admit card is more than just a piece of paper; it's your entry ticket to the examination hall. It contains vital information such as your roll number, the test venue, date, and time. It also usually includes important instructions you must follow on the day of the test. Without a valid admit card, you will not be permitted to sit for the IIM Kozhikode BMS Aptitude Test, so downloading and printing it well in advance is essential.

## What to Do If You Face Issues

While the download process is generally smooth, technical glitches can sometimes occur. If you encounter any problems downloading your admit card, such as login issues or an inability to find the download link, it's best to contact the IIM Kozhikode admissions office directly. They can provide specific guidance and resolve any technical difficulties you might be experiencing. Make sure to have your application details handy when you reach out.

Ensure you secure your admit card promptly to avoid any last-minute stress before the examination.
