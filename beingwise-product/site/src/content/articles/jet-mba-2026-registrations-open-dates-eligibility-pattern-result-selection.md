# JET MBA 2026: Registrations OPEN, Dates, Eligibility, Pattern, Result, Selection Process

The JAIN Entrance Test (JET) is a crucial examination for aspiring MBA students seeking admission to JAIN (Deemed-to-be University). This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the JET MBA 2026, covering key dates, eligibility criteria, exam structure, and the selection process.

## Key takeaways

*   JET MBA 2026 written test results were released on April 3, with further selection rounds scheduled for mid-April.
*   The entire JET examination for all programs, including MBA, is conducted online in a remote proctored mode, allowing candidates to take the test from home.
*   Eligibility for MBA requires a bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks.
*   The selection process for MBA includes a written test, essay writing, presentation, and a personal interview.

## Understanding the JAIN Entrance Test (JET)

JET is a unified entrance examination facilitating admissions to a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including MBA, offered by JAIN (Deemed-to-be University). The university also considers scores from other national-level exams like JEE Main and CUET for specific programs, such as BTech. The remote proctored format of JET aims to provide convenience and flexibility for test-takers, enabling them to appear for the exam from their personal computers after registering and selecting a preferred session on the official university website.

## Eligibility Criteria for JET MBA 2026

To be considered for the MBA program through JET, candidates must meet specific academic requirements. Applicants should have successfully completed an undergraduate degree from a recognized university or institution, securing a minimum aggregate of 50% marks. It is always advisable to check the official JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) admissions portal for the most current and detailed eligibility guidelines.

## JET MBA 2026 Exam Pattern and Selection Process

The JET MBA selection process is designed to comprehensively evaluate a candidate's aptitude and suitability for the program. It comprises multiple stages:

1.  **JET Entrance Test:** This is the initial online remote proctored examination. It assesses general aptitude and is a qualifying stage for subsequent rounds.
2.  **Essay Writing:** Shortlisted candidates proceed to an essay writing component, evaluating their written communication skills and ability to articulate ideas.
3.  **Presentation:** Following the essay, candidates are required to give a presentation, which assesses their public speaking and analytical skills.
4.  **Personal Interview (PI):** The final stage involves a personal interview, where candidates interact with a panel to discuss their academic background, career aspirations, and suitability for the MBA program.

Candidates who successfully navigate all these stages will be considered for final admission to the JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) MBA program.
