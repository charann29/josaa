# MHT CET 2021 Syllabus: Was it Reduced?

Many students preparing for the MHT CET 2021 examination had questions about potential syllabus reductions, especially given the changes in the Maharashtra state board curriculum. Let's clarify the situation regarding the MHT CET 2021 syllabus.

## Key Takeaways

*   The MHT CET 2021 syllabus remained unchanged from its standard structure, despite reductions in the state board curriculum.
*   It covered topics from both Class 11 and Class 12 of the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education.
*   The examination typically allocates 20% of its questions to Class 11 topics and 80% to Class 12 topics.
*   The official syllabus details were released by the State Common Entrance Test Cell on their official website.

## Maharashtra State Board Curriculum Changes

In July 2020, the Maharashtra government announced a 25% reduction in the curriculum for classes 1 through 12 for the academic year. This decision was made to ease the academic burden during an unusual period. Naturally, this led to speculation among students and parents about whether this reduction would also apply to competitive entrance exams like the MHT CET.

## MHT CET 2021 Syllabus Structure

The MHT CET is designed to assess candidates on their understanding of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (for PCM group) or Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (for PCB group) topics. These topics are drawn from the Maharashtra State Board's Class 11 and Class 12 syllabi. Historically, the distribution of questions has been consistent: approximately 20% from the Class 11 curriculum and 80% from the Class 12 curriculum.

## Official Stance on Syllabus Reduction for MHT CET 2021

Despite the changes implemented for the regular state board school curriculum, the State Common Entrance Test Cell did not announce any corresponding reduction in the MHT CET 2021 syllabus. This meant that candidates were expected to prepare all topics typically included in the MHT CET from both Class 11 and Class 12, as per the established pattern.

## Where to Find Official Syllabus Information

For the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding the MHT CET syllabus, students should always refer to the official website of the State Common Entrance Test Cell. This portal is the authoritative source for all examination-related notifications, including detailed syllabus breakdowns, exam patterns, and important dates.

In summary, while the state board curriculum saw reductions, the MHT CET 2021 syllabus maintained its standard comprehensive coverage from both Class 11 and Class 12 topics.
