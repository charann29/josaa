# Best MBA Colleges in Nagpur: Fees, Admission 2026, Courses, Salary, Ranking

Nagpur, a central hub in Maharashtra, offers a robust landscape for management education with over 70 institutions providing MBA programs. These colleges, including both government and private entities, are typically approved by AICTE and affiliated with Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RTMNU).

## Key takeaways

*   Nagpur is home to more than 70 MBA colleges, offering diverse specialisations like Finance, Marketing, and Human Resources.
*   Admission to top MBA programs generally requires scores from national or state-level entrance exams such as CAT, MAH CET, or MAT.
*   Leading institutions like IIM Nagpur and IMT Nagpur are highly regarded, with IIM Nagpur consistently ranking among the top management institutes nationally.
*   Annual fees for MBA programs in Nagpur vary widely, from under INR 1 lakh to over INR 15 lakh, catering to different budget requirements.

## MBA Admission Eligibility Criteria 2026

To be eligible for MBA programs in Nagpur, candidates typically need to meet the following criteria:

*   **Educational Qualification:** A bachelor's degree from a recognised university in any discipline.
*   **Minimum Marks:** Generally, candidates must have secured at least 50% marks in their graduation (45% for reserved categories).
*   **Entrance Exams:** Valid scores in management entrance examinations are usually mandatory. These include national tests like CAT, MAT, or CMAT, and state-level exams such as MAH CET.

## Accepted Entrance Exams for MBA in Nagpur

Most MBA colleges in Nagpur consider scores from various entrance examinations for admission. These exams assess a candidate's aptitude in areas such as quantitative ability, verbal ability, logical reasoning, and data interpretation.

Commonly accepted entrance exams include:

*   **CAT (Common Admission Test):** A national-level exam accepted by IIMs and many other top B-schools.
*   **MAH CET (Maharashtra Common Entrance Test):** The state-level entrance exam for admission to MBA/MMS courses in Maharashtra.
*   **MAT (Management Aptitude Test):** A national-level test conducted multiple times a year.
*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test):** Another national-level exam facilitating admissions to AICTE-approved institutions.
