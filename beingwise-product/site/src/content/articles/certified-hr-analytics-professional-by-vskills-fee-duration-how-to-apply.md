# Certified HR Analytics Professional by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's data-driven world, understanding and leveraging human resources data is crucial for organizational success. The Certified HR Analytics Professional program by Vskills offers a comprehensive pathway for individuals to master the skills needed to analyze HR data effectively and drive strategic decisions.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a government-approved certification, adding significant value to your professional profile.
*   The program offers flexible, self-paced learning through video and text-based materials, accessible for life.
*   The certification includes a 'Vskills certified' tag for your CV and professional platforms, enhancing job prospects.
*   A minimum of a graduate degree is required to enroll, making it suitable for both fresh graduates and working HR professionals.

## Understanding HR Analytics

Analytics involves gathering and interpreting data to gain deeper insights and make informed decisions. In the context of Human Resources, HR Analytics focuses on various HR processes to understand and influence aspects like employee productivity, motivation, and performance. This Vskills certification delves into the fundamentals of analytics and statistics, specifically tailored for the HR domain, making it an excellent opportunity for HR professionals to advance their careers.

## Course Structure and Offerings

The Certified HR Analytics Professional course is delivered through a self-study model, primarily using video and text-based materials in English. This allows learners to proceed at their own pace, fitting the study around their existing commitments. The program highlights include:

*   **Government-Approved Certification:** The certification carries government approval, providing added credibility.
*   **Lifetime Access:** You'll receive lifelong access to the study materials and the validity of your certificate never expires.
*   **Practice and Mock Tests:** Free access to practice tests, sample questions, and tutorials helps prepare you for the final exam.
*   **Professional Recognition:** Successful completion earns you a government certificate and a 'Vskills certified' tag to include on your CV and professional networking sites.

## Eligibility and Examination Details

To enroll in the Certified HR Analytics Professional course, candidates must hold at least a graduate degree. This makes the program accessible to recent graduates looking to enter the analytics field, as well as current HR professionals, consultants, and managers seeking to enhance their analytical capabilities.

To earn the certificate, you must pass an online examination. These exams are conducted monthly, and you can schedule your test date at your convenience using the exam calendar. To pass, candidates need to secure a minimum of 25 out of 50 marks.

## What You Will Learn

The Vskills Certified HR Analytics Professional program is designed to equip you with a strong foundation in HR management and analytical skills. The comprehensive syllabus covers essential concepts, including:

*   Core principles of analytics and statistics.
*   Practical application of tools like MS Excel for data analysis.
*   Fundamentals of human resource management in a workplace setting.
*   Strategies for improving employee motivation and productivity.
*   Techniques for employee training and retention.
*   Various features of analytics specifically used within Human Resource Management.

The total fee for the Certified HR Analytics Professional training course is ₹3,499, plus Goods and Services Tax (GST). This single fee covers both the tuition and the certification process.
