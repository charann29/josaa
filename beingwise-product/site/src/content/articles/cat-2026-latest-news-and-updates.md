# CAT 2026 Latest News and Updates

Welcome to your essential guide for the Common Admission Test (CAT) 2026! This page brings you the most recent and important updates regarding India's premier management aptitude examination, helping you stay informed for your MBA journey.

## Key takeaways

*   The CAT exam is typically held in late November each year, across three shifts.
*   Registration for CAT usually begins in early August and concludes by mid-September.
*   Following the exam, candidates can expect answer key releases, an objection window, results, and finally, interview shortlists from various IIMs and other top B-schools.
*   Key dates for CAT 2026, including notification release, registration, admit card, and exam day, will be announced by the conducting IIM.

## Understanding the CAT Exam

The Common Admission Test (CAT) is a crucial entrance examination for aspiring management professionals in India. It serves as the gateway for admissions to the prestigious Post Graduate Programmes (PGP) in Management offered by the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) and numerous other highly-ranked business schools across the country. The exam assesses a candidate's aptitude in areas such as Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC), Data Interpretation and Logical Reasoning (DILR), and Quantitative Ability (QA).
