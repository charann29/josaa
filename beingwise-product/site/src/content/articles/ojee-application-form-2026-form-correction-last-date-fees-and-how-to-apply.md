# OJEE Application Form 2026: Form Correction, Last Date, Fees, & How to Apply

The Odisha Joint Entrance Examination (OJEE) is a crucial state-level examination for students aspiring to pursue various professional courses in Odisha. This guide covers the essential details for the OJEE 2026 application process, including how to apply, important dates, fees, and the form correction window.

## Key Takeaways

*   The OJEE 2026 application window typically opens in late January and closes in late March.
*   A dedicated form correction window is provided after the initial application deadline.
*   Application involves online registration, form filling, document uploads, and fee payment.
*   Always refer to the official OJEE website for the most accurate and up-to-date information.

## OJEE 2026 Application Overview and Key Dates

For the OJEE 2026 examination, the application forms were made available online on the official OJEE website starting January 28, 2026. Prospective candidates had until March 29, 2026, to complete their registrations. Following the main application period, a form correction window was opened from March 30, 2026, to April 1, 2026, allowing applicants to rectify any errors in their submitted forms. It is vital for all candidates to meet the specific eligibility criteria for their chosen course to ensure their application is considered valid.

The OJEE exam facilitates admissions into a diverse range of programs, including:

*   MBA/PGDM
*   BAMS, BHMS
*   MCA, MCA (Lateral Entry)
*   B.Pharma, B.Pharma (Lateral Entry)
*   B.Tech (Lateral Entry)
*   M.Tech
*   M.Pharma
*   M.Arch
*   M.Plan
*   BCAT
*   M.Sc

## How to Fill the OJEE 2026 Application Form

The OJEE 2026 application process is entirely online and consists of four main steps: online registration, filling out the detailed application form, uploading necessary documents, and finally, paying the application fee and printing the confirmation page. Incomplete forms or those submitted after the deadline will be rejected.

### Step 1: Online OJEE Registration

1.  Visit the official OJEE website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Registration for OJEE 2026' link.
3.  On the new registration page, select 'Fresh Candidate Registration'.
4.  Provide personal details such as your name, date of birth, gender, father's name, and contact information. Create a password.
5.  Enter the security code displayed and click 'Submit'.
6.  Review all entered details carefully before clicking 'Final Submit'.

### Step 2: OJEE 2026 Application Form Filling

1.  Upon successful registration, you will receive a unique Application Number.
2.  Click on the 'Complete Application Form' button.
3.  Fill in all additional required details accurately.
4.  Review all information before 'Final Submit'.

### Step 3: Uploading Documents

Candidates must upload scanned images of their recent colored photograph and full signature (in black or blue ink). These images should be in JPEG/JPG format and adhere to the specified dimensions and file sizes.

| Document                | File Size       | Dimensions    |
| :---------------------- | :-------------- | :------------ |
| Candidate's Photograph  | 10KB to 200KB   | 3.5cm X 4.5cm |
| Candidate's Signature   | 4KB to 30KB     | 3.5cm X 1.5cm |

### Step 4: Fee Payment and Printing Confirmation Page

1.  Use your Application Number to proceed to the fee payment section.
2.  Pay the application fee exclusively through online modes, using a credit card, debit card (VISA/MASTER/Maestro), or internet banking.
3.  After successful payment, it is crucial to print out the confirmation page for your records.

## OJEE 2026 Application Fee

The application fee for OJEE 2026 must be paid online. Specific fee amounts may vary based on the course applied for or category. Always check the official OJEE information bulletin for the exact fee structure applicable to your chosen program.

## OJEE Form Correction 2026

After the initial application submission period, the OJEE Cell provides a dedicated window for form correction. For OJEE 2026, this window was active from March 30, 2026, to April 1, 2026. During this period, applicants could log in to their application portal using their credentials and make necessary edits to their submitted information. It is highly recommended to utilize this period to correct any discrepancies to avoid issues later in the admission process.

Ensure you regularly check the official OJEE portal for any announcements or updates regarding the application process.
