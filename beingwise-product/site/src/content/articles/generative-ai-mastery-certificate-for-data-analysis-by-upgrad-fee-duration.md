# Generative AI Mastery Certificate for Data Analysis by upGrad: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's fast-evolving digital world, combining traditional data analysis with the power of generative artificial intelligence (AI) is becoming crucial. This certificate program aims to equip professionals with the skills to leverage AI for more efficient and insightful data analysis.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 2-month, entirely online program designed for working professionals.
*   The curriculum focuses on practical applications of AI tools, including Microsoft Copilot and Azure OpenAI.
*   Participants will work on capstone projects and receive a joint certificate upon completion.
*   The program is currently offered at a discounted fee, making it more accessible.

## Program Overview

This certificate course is meticulously designed to bridge the gap between established data analysis methods and the transformative capabilities of generative AI. It focuses on practical applications, teaching participants how to use AI for tasks like data cleaning, discovering hidden patterns, and automating workflows. The program integrates both theoretical knowledge and hands-on skills, preparing students to tackle real-world data challenges using advanced AI techniques.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured into four key modules, each building on the previous one to provide a comprehensive understanding of generative AI in data analysis:

*   **Modern Analytics Principles with AI:** This module introduces industry-standard analytics principles and explores how AI can enhance data processing, workflow automation, and overall productivity. It also covers guidelines for integrating AI effectively into the data analysis and reporting pipeline.
*   **AI-Powered Analytics with Microsoft Power BI:** Participants will delve into using Power BI for analytics, focusing on data visualization, reporting, and dashboard creation. The module also covers DAX querying and optimization, with a special emphasis on automating reports and dashboards using Microsoft Copilot.
*   **Data Governance with AI:** This section addresses crucial aspects of data management, including data lineage, compliance tracking, data cleaning, and metadata management. It teaches how to build AI-powered workflows and scalable pipelines for robust data governance.
*   **Text Analytics with GenAI:** The final module focuses on text processing techniques, Python programming for Natural Language Processing (NLP), and leveraging Azure OpenAI GPT for advanced text analytics.

Throughout these modules, participants will gain the expertise to build and deploy AI agents that can automate complex data analysis tasks, streamlining workflows and improving accuracy.

## Who Is This Program For?

This certificate is ideal for professionals looking to upgrade their skills and integrate generative AI into their data analysis practices. The target audience includes:

*   Data Analysts
*   Software Engineers
*   Product Managers
*   AI Engineers

If you are involved in data-driven decision-making or software development and wish to harness the power of AI, this program can significantly enhance your career prospects.

## Fees and Duration

The Generative AI Mastery Certificate for Data Analysis is a 2-month online program. The standard fee for the course is ₹49,999. However, it is currently available at a discounted fee of ₹24,999. This fee covers all course materials, access to virtual classrooms, and the joint certificate upon successful completion.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Generative AI Mastery Certificate for Data Analysis is a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Official Program Page:** Locate the dedicated webpage for this certificate program.
2.  **Select Your Plan:** Click on the 'Apply Now' button next to your preferred pricing option.
3.  **Complete the Application Form:** Fill in the required personal and professional details.
4.  **Attempt Scholarship Test (If Applicable):** If a scholarship opportunity is available, you may be asked to take a test.
5.  **Make Payment:** Complete the fee payment to secure your enrollment in the program.

This certificate offers a valuable opportunity to gain cutting-edge skills in generative AI for data analysis, directly applicable to today's demanding job market.
