# AP ICET 2026: Mock Test (OUT), Hall Ticket, Exam Date, Pattern, Syllabus

The Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test (AP ICET) is a crucial state-level examination for students aspiring to pursue MBA or MCA programs in colleges across Andhra Pradesh. Conducted by Andhra University on behalf of the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE), this computer-based test is your gateway to postgraduate management and computer application courses.

## Key Takeaways

*   The official AP ICET 2026 mock test is now available, offering a vital opportunity to familiarize yourself with the exam format.
*   Hall tickets for AP ICET 2026 are scheduled for release on April 15, 2026, and will be downloadable from the official portal.
*   The AP ICET 2026 examination will take place on May 02, 2026, conducted in two shifts across various districts in Andhra Pradesh.
*   The exam evaluates candidates on Analytical Ability, Mathematical Ability, and Communication Ability through 200 multiple-choice questions.

## Understanding AP ICET

AP ICET is a highly competitive entrance exam, typically seeing around 35,000 candidates appear each year, with a significant number qualifying. It is a computer-based test (CBT) administered in 25 districts across Andhra Pradesh. The scores from AP ICET are accepted by over 290 colleges throughout the state for admissions into MBA and MCA programs.

## AP ICET 2026 Mock Test

The official AP ICET 2026 mock test has been released online. It is highly recommended that all registered candidates attempt this mock test. Engaging with the mock test provides invaluable experience of the actual examination environment, helping you understand the question format, time management, and overall exam pattern. This is a crucial step in assessing your preparation level and identifying areas for improvement before the main exam.

## AP ICET 2026 Hall Ticket

The AP ICET 2026 hall tickets are scheduled to be issued on April 15, 2026. Only candidates who have successfully registered for the examination will be able to download their admit cards. You will need your login credentials to access and download the hall ticket from the official APSCHE portal. Remember to print multiple copies and keep them safe, as the hall ticket is mandatory for entry into the examination hall.

## AP ICET 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The AP ICET examination is structured to evaluate a candidate's aptitude across three main sections: Analytical Ability, Mathematical Ability, and Communication Ability. The test consists of 200 multiple-choice questions, to be completed within 150 minutes. All questions will be in English. A thorough understanding of the prescribed syllabus for each section is vital for effective preparation.

For detailed information on the syllabus and exam pattern, candidates should refer to the official AP ICET information brochure available on the APSCHE website.

Prepare diligently and good luck with your AP ICET 2026 examination!
