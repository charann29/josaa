# Wileynxt Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Wileynxt Courses

BeingWise explores a range of online programs offered in collaboration with leading Indian institutions like the IITs and IIMs. These programs aim to equip professionals and aspiring leaders with cutting-edge skills for the evolving job market.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Premium Collaborations:** Wileynxt partners with top-tier institutions such as IIT Jodhpur, IIM Kozhikode, and IIM Lucknow to deliver specialized online certifications.
*   **Diverse Specializations:** Courses span high-demand fields like Data Engineering, Cloud Computing, Customer Experience Management, and Strategic Finance.
*   **Career Advancement Focus:** Programs are designed to enhance specific professional skills, preparing participants for leadership roles and digital transformation challenges.
*   **Structured Learning:** Curricula are often developed by faculty and industry experts, incorporating video lectures, case studies, and practical projects.

## Post Graduate Diploma in Data Engineering & Cloud Computing (IIT Jodhpur)

This 12-month online Post Graduate Diploma, offered in collaboration with IIT Jodhpur, focuses on the crucial intersection of data engineering and cloud computing. It's tailored for individuals aspiring to leverage data for solving complex business and social problems.

The program's curriculum is structured into three modules, spread across three trimesters. It emphasizes practical skills in a Python environment, teaching participants how to interpret data, manage data structures, and extract valuable insights through cleaning and examination. Key skills covered include database management, big data concepts, and cloud computing knowledge.

*   **Duration:** 12 Months
*   **Fee:** ₹2,99,000

## Advanced Management Certification in Transformational Customer Experience (IIM Kozhikode)

In partnership with IIM Kozhikode, this 9-month executive education program delves into advanced customer experience (CX) management. It's designed for professionals looking to master futuristic approaches to customer engagement.

The certification explores various aspects of CXM, including its business return on investment (ROI), transformational strategies, and methods for building strong customer relationships to ensure retention. The core skill developed is advanced customer service.

*   **Duration:** 9 Months
*   **Fee:** ₹1,99,000

## Executive Program in Strategic Finance (IIM Lucknow)

This 9-month executive program, offered with IIM Lucknow, is tailored for emerging CFOs and FP&A leaders. It goes beyond traditional accounting, focusing on developing the qualities and responsibilities needed to effectively partner with a Chief Financial Officer.

The program encourages participants to navigate digital transformation with a broader understanding of financial strategy. It equips aspiring financial leaders with the skills and mindset necessary to become key business stakeholders, driving growth in dynamic business environments and digitally disruptive economies. Key skills include leadership and knowledge of FinTech.

*   **Duration:** 9 Months
*   **Fee:** ₹2,49,000

## Post Graduate Certification in Cloud Computing & DevOps (IIT Roorkee)

IIT Roorkee, in collaboration with Wileynxt, offers a Post Graduate Certification in Cloud Computing & DevOps. This program is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in these critical areas, essential for modern IT infrastructure and software development.

Further details regarding the syllabus, duration, and fees for this specific program can typically be found on the official Wileynxt portal or IIT Roorkee's executive education pages.

These collaborations provide excellent opportunities for professionals to upskill and remain competitive in rapidly evolving industries.
