# List of ICFAI Business Schools (IBS): Courses, fees & ranking

ICFAI Business School (IBS) is a well-known name in management education across India, offering a variety of programs designed to equip students for the corporate world. With a network of nine campuses, IBS provides numerous opportunities for aspiring business leaders.

## Key takeaways

*   IBS offers full-time and part-time management programs, including MBA, BBA, PGPM, and PhD.
*   Admission to IBS MBA/PGPM programs can be secured through IBSAT scores or other national-level exams like CAT, GMAT, NMAT, and MAT.
*   Course fees for MBA and PGPM programs vary significantly by campus, ranging from approximately INR 3.51 lakh to INR 8.505 lakh per annum.
*   IBS Hyderabad is typically the most sought-after campus, with its two-year MBA program costing around INR 16 lakh.

## Programs Offered by ICFAI Business Schools

ICFAI University, under which IBS operates, has several departments beyond just management, covering areas like law, education, applied sciences, and social sciences. This broad academic foundation supports a diverse range of programs. For management aspirants, IBS specifically offers:

*   Master of Business Administration (MBA)
*   Postgraduate Program in Management (PGPM)
*   Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
*   Executive MBA
*   PhD programs
*   Various certificate courses

These programs are available in both full-time and part-time formats, catering to different student needs and career stages. IBS also engages in collaborations with international organizations, enhancing its academic offerings and global perspective.

## ICFAI Business School Campuses: An Overview

IBS has established a presence in key cities across India, providing regional access to its management education. The nine campuses that accept IBSAT scores for admission to their MBA/PGPM programs are:

*   **IBS Hyderabad:** Often considered the flagship campus, known for its extensive program and higher fee structure.
*   **IBS Ahmedabad:** A prominent campus in Gujarat.
*   **IBS Bangalore:** Located in a major IT and business hub.
*   **IBS Dehradun:** Situated in a scenic and academic environment.
*   **IBS Gurgaon:** Positioned in the bustling corporate corridor of the NCR.
*   **IBS Jaipur:** Offering management education in Rajasthan.
*   **IBS Kolkata:** Serving students in Eastern India.
*   **IBS Mumbai:** Located in India's financial capital.
*   **IBS Pune:** A well-regarded campus in Maharashtra.

Each campus has its own fee structure and specific program offerings, so prospective students should check the official IBS website for the most current details relevant to their chosen location.

## Admission Process for IBS Management Programs

Admission to the MBA and PGPM programs at ICFAI Business Schools follows a structured process:

1.  **Eligibility Check:** Candidates must first meet the minimum eligibility criteria set by IBS for their desired program.
2.  **Application:** Complete the online application form. This step typically involves providing academic details and personal information.
3.  **Entrance Examination:** Applicants can gain admission through their scores in national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, GMAT, NMAT by GMAC, or MAT. Alternatively, IBS conducts its own entrance exam, IBSAT, which is specifically designed for admission to its nine campuses.
4.  **Selection Process:** Shortlisted candidates are invited for a selection round, which usually includes group discussions and personal interviews.
5.  **Final Result:** Based on the overall performance in the entrance exam and selection process, final admission offers are extended.

Candidates with valid scores from other recognized entrance exams are generally exempt from taking the IBSAT exam, streamlining their application process.

## Placements and Recruiters at IBS

IBS campuses consistently attract a range of companies for placements, offering students opportunities across various sectors. While specific placement statistics can vary by campus and year, IBS aims to provide its graduates with strong career starts. Top recruiters often include companies from sectors such as banking and financial services, consulting, IT, manufacturing, and retail.
