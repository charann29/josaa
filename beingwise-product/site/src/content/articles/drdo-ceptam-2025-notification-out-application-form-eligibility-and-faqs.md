# DRDO CEPTAM 2025: Notification OUT, Application Form, Eligibility & FAQs

The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), through its Centre for Personnel Talent Management (CEPTAM), has released the recruitment notification for CEPTAM-11. This is an excellent opportunity for individuals aspiring to join India's premier defence research agency in various technical and administrative roles.

## Key takeaways

*   The DRDO CEPTAM-11 notification has been released, announcing a total of 764 vacancies.
*   Positions are available for Senior Technical Assistant-B (STA-B) and Technician-A (TECH-A).
*   The application form for DRDO CEPTAM 2025 is currently accessible.
*   Eligibility criteria, including educational qualifications, vary depending on the specific post.

## What is DRDO CEPTAM?

CEPTAM stands for Centre for Personnel Talent Management, a dedicated body within the Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO). Its primary role is to oversee the recruitment of non-gazetted technical, administrative, and allied staff for DRDO. Originally established as the Personnel Assessment Centre (PEACE), CEPTAM is crucial for sourcing talent for the DRTC (DRDO Technical Cadre) and also facilitates training and assessment for these roles.

## Vacancies Announced

The DRDO CEPTAM-11 recruitment drive aims to fill 764 positions across two main categories:

*   **Senior Technical Assistant-B (STA-B):** These roles typically require a degree or diploma in a relevant engineering or science discipline.
*   **Technician-A (TECH-A):** These positions often require a Class 10 qualification along with an ITI certificate in a specific trade.

It's important for candidates to check the official notification for the detailed breakdown of vacancies per discipline and category.

## Application Process for DRDO CEPTAM 2025

The application form for DRDO CEPTAM 2025 is now available. Prospective candidates should visit the official CEPTAM portal to access the form and detailed instructions. Ensure all required documents and information are ready before starting the application process to avoid any last-minute issues. It is crucial to submit the application before the specified deadline.

## Eligibility Criteria: Educational Qualifications

The educational requirements for DRDO CEPTAM posts differ significantly based on the specific role. Candidates must carefully review the official notification for the exact qualifications needed for their desired position. Here’s a general overview:

*   **For Technician-A (TECH-A) posts:** Candidates generally need to have passed Class 10 along with a certificate from an Industrial Training Institute (ITI) in the relevant trade.
*   **For Senior Technical Assistant-B (STA-B) posts:** A Bachelor's degree in Science or a three-year Diploma in Engineering or Technology from a recognized institution in the specified discipline is typically required.
*   **For Administrative & Allied (A&A) posts (from previous recruitments):** Qualifications could range from Class 10 pass for roles like Clerk, Assistant Halwai-cum-Cook, Vehicle Operator, Fire Engine Driver, and Fireman, to Class 12 pass for positions such as Stenographer, Administrative Assistant (English/Hindi Typing), and Store Assistant (English/Hindi Typing), and Security Assistant. For Multi Tasking Staff (MTS) roles, a Class 10 pass or equivalent is generally required.

Candidates are strongly advised to refer to the official CEPTAM notification for the precise and up-to-date eligibility requirements for each post advertised in CEPTAM-11.

Candidates interested in a career in defence research and development should thoroughly read the official CEPTAM-11 notification and apply well within the stipulated timeframe.
