# SNAP 2026 Exam: Dates, Registration, Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Pattern, Preparation

The Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP) is a vital entrance examination for students aspiring to join MBA programs at various institutes under Symbiosis International (Deemed) University (SIU). This national-level test assesses candidates' aptitude for business management and is a crucial step in the admission process.

## Key Takeaways

*   SNAP 2026 is conducted by SIU for admission to its MBA/PGDM programs across all constituent institutes.
*   Candidates can attempt the exam up to three times in a single academic year, with the best score being considered for admissions.
*   Registration typically begins in August, and the exams are held in December.
*   The selection process includes the SNAP exam, followed by Group Exercise (GE), Personal Interview (PI), and Written Ability Test (WAT).

## SNAP 2026 Exam Highlights

The SNAP exam is designed to be a speed-based test that evaluates a candidate's readiness for business studies. Here's an overview of the key details for SNAP 2026:

| Feature              | Details                                                                                                 |
| :------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Full Form            | Symbiosis National Aptitude Test                                                                        |
| Conducting Body      | Symbiosis International (Deemed) University (SIU)                                                       |
| Exam Level           | National                                                                                                |
| Exam Frequency       | Once a year (with three attempts allowed)                                                               |
| Exam Mode            | Online                                                                                                  |
| Exam Duration        | 60 minutes                                                                                              |
| Questions            | 60 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)                                                                     |
| Courses Offered      | MBA/PGDM                                                                                                |
| Test Cities          | Approximately 80 cities across India                                                                    |

## SNAP Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for SNAP 2026, candidates must meet the following requirements, as stipulated by SIU:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
*   **Minimum Marks:** A minimum of 50% aggregate marks is required for general category candidates. For candidates belonging to the Reserved category, the minimum requirement is 45%.
*   **Final Year Students:** Those in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply, provided they complete their degree within the specified timeframe.
*   **Professional Degrees:** Candidates with professional qualifications like CA, CS, or ICWA are also eligible to appear for the SNAP exam.

## SNAP Registration Process and Fees

The registration process for SNAP 2026 is conducted entirely online. Candidates will need to visit the official SNAP TEST portal to fill out the application form. The fees associated with the SNAP exam are as follows:

*   **Exam Fee:** INR 2,250 per attempt.
*   **Institute Selection Fee:** An additional INR 1,000 for each SIU constituent institute selected by the candidate in their application form.

## SNAP Syllabus and Exam Pattern

Understanding the syllabus and exam pattern is crucial for effective preparation. The SNAP 2026 exam is a 60-minute test covering three main sections:

### Exam Pattern

| Section                                     | Number of Questions | Marks per Question | Total Marks |
| :------------------------------------------ | :------------------ | :----------------- | :---------- |
| General English                             | 15                  | 1                  | 15          |
| Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency | 20                  | 1                  | 20          |
| Analytical & Logical Reasoning              | 25                  | 1                  | 25          |
| **Total**                                   | **60**              |                    | **60**      |

*   **Marking Scheme:** Each correct answer carries 1 mark. There is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for every incorrect answer.

### Syllabus

*   **General English:** This section typically includes topics like Reading Comprehension, Verbal Reasoning, Synonyms, Antonyms, Fill in the Blanks, Sentence Correction, and Grammar.
*   **Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency:** Expect questions on Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Mensuration, Modern Math, Number System, Percentages, Ratio & Proportion, Data Interpretation (tables, graphs, charts), and Data Sufficiency.
*   **Analytical & Logical Reasoning:** This section covers areas such as Blood Relations, Coding-Decoding, Seating Arrangements, Puzzles, Syllogisms, Critical Reasoning, Clocks & Calendars, and Series Completion.

## SNAP 2026 Preparation Tips

Effective preparation for SNAP 2026 involves a strategic approach. Here are some tips to help you succeed:

*   **Understand the Basics:** Ensure you have a strong foundation in all subjects covered in the syllabus. Regular revision of fundamental concepts is key.
*   **Practice Regularly:** Solve a variety of practice questions from each section. Focus on improving your speed and accuracy, as SNAP is a time-bound exam.
*   **Take Mock Tests:** Regularly appearing for full-length mock tests is essential. This helps you get accustomed to the exam environment, manage time effectively, and identify your strengths and weaknesses.
*   **Analyze Performance:** After each mock test, thoroughly analyze your performance. Understand where you are losing marks and devise strategies to improve in those areas.
*   **Time Management:** Since the exam duration is only 60 minutes for 60 questions, efficient time management during the test is critical. Practice allocating time to each section based on your proficiency.
*   **Revision:** Dedicate time for regular revision of formulas, concepts, and common question types. Create short notes for quick review.

With thorough preparation and a clear strategy, you can confidently approach the SNAP 2026 exam and move closer to your dream of pursuing an MBA at a Symbiosis institute.
