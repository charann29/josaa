# SNAP 2026: Exam Dates, Application, Eligibility, Pattern, Syllabus, Preparation, Cut Offs

The Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP) is a vital entrance examination for aspiring MBA students looking to join Symbiosis International (Deemed) University (SIU) and its affiliated institutions. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of everything you need to know about SNAP 2026.

## Key takeaways

*   SNAP 2026 will be held in December across three separate sessions, allowing candidates multiple attempts.
*   The application process is expected to begin in August 2026, with forms available on the official SNAPTEST portal.
*   The exam evaluates candidates on General English, Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency, and Analytical & Logical Reasoning.
*   Successful SNAP qualifiers proceed to a Group Exercise (GE) and Personal Interview (PI) round for final selection.

## What is the SNAP Exam?

The SNAP Test is an annual computer-based examination conducted by Symbiosis International (Deemed) University for admission to its various MBA and PGDM programs. While primarily for SIU, some other institutions, like JK Business School-Gurgaon, also accept SNAP scores. Candidates can attempt the exam up to three times within the academic session, with the best score considered for admissions.

## SNAP 2026 Important Dates and Schedule

The registration window for SNAP 2026 is anticipated to open in the first week of August 2026 and close by November 2026. The examination itself is typically held in December, spread across three sessions. Candidates can choose to appear for one, two, or all three sessions. Below is an estimated schedule based on previous years:

| Event                           | Tentative Dates        |
| :------------------------------ | :--------------------- |
| SNAP 2026 Registration Process  | August - November 2026 |
| Admit Card for Session 1        | Late November 2026     |
| SNAP 2026 Session 1             | Early December 2026    |
| Admit Card for Session 2        | Early December 2026    |
| SNAP 2026 Session 2             | Mid-December 2026      |
| Admit Card for Session 3        | Mid-December 2026      |
| SNAP 2026 Session 3             | Late December 2026     |

## SNAP 2026 Exam Highlights

SNAP offers pathways to over 30 MBA/PGDM courses across 16 SIU constituent institutes. When applying, candidates must select their preferred colleges to be considered for the subsequent PI shortlist. Here are the key details for SNAP 2026:

*   **Exam Name:** Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP)
*   **Conducting Body:** Symbiosis International (Deemed) University (SIU)
*   **Exam Frequency:** Once a year (with three attempts available)
*   **Exam Mode:** Computer Based Test (CBT)
*   **Exam Duration:** 60 minutes
*   **Total Questions:** 60 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Sections:** General English; Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency; Analytical & Logical Reasoning
*   **Language:** English
*   **Application Fee:** INR 2,250 (subject to change)
*   **Purpose:** Admission to MBA/PGDM programs at SIU affiliated colleges
*   **Number of Test Cities:** Approximately 80 across India

## SNAP 2026 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for SNAP 2026, candidates must meet specific academic requirements. Generally, applicants need to hold a bachelor's degree from any recognized university with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST candidates). Those in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply, provided they can submit their passing certificate and mark sheets by the stipulated date.

## SNAP 2026 Application Process

Applying for SNAP 2026 involves a few straightforward steps:

1.  **Registration:** Visit the official SNAPTEST website and register by creating a user ID and password.
2.  **Fill Application Form:** Complete the online application with personal, academic, and contact details.
3.  **Select Programs/Institutes:** Indicate your preferred MBA/PGDM programs and SIU institutes directly within the SNAP application form. This is crucial for being considered for their respective GE-PI rounds.
4.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature as per the specifications.
5.  **Pay Application Fee:** Submit the non-refundable application fee online through debit/credit card or net banking. Remember, a separate institute-specific registration fee might also be required later for individual SIU colleges.

## SNAP 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The SNAP exam is designed to assess a candidate's aptitude for management studies. It is a 60-minute test comprising 60 questions.

### Exam Pattern

| Section                                     | Number of Questions | Total Marks |
| :------------------------------------------ | :------------------ | :---------- |
| General English                             | 15                  | 15          |
| Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency | 20                  | 20          |
| Analytical & Logical Reasoning              | 25                  | 25          |
| **Total**                                   | **60**              | **60**      |

Each correct answer typically fetches 1 mark, and there is usually a negative marking for incorrect answers (e.g., 0.25 marks deducted).

### Syllabus

*   **General English:** Reading Comprehension, Verbal Reasoning, Jumbled Paragraphs, Sentence Correction, Fill in the Blanks, Vocabulary.
*   **Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency:** Arithmetic (Time & Work, Profit & Loss, Percentages), Algebra, Geometry, Modern Math, Number System, Data Interpretation (Tables, Graphs, Charts), Data Sufficiency.
*   **Analytical & Logical Reasoning:** Blood Relations, Seating Arrangements, Syllogisms, Coding-Decoding, Puzzles, Critical Reasoning, Visual Reasoning.

## SNAP 2026 Preparation Tips

Effective preparation is key to a good SNAP score. Here are some tips:

*   **Understand the Pattern:** Familiarize yourself thoroughly with the exam structure, marking scheme, and types of questions asked.
*   **Master Fundamentals:** Strengthen your basics in English grammar, mathematical concepts, and logical reasoning techniques.
*   **Practice Regularly:** Solve a wide variety of practice questions and previous year's papers to improve speed and accuracy.
*   **Time Management:** Since the exam is only 60 minutes, practice managing your time effectively across sections.
*   **Mock Tests:** Take several full-length mock tests to simulate exam conditions and identify your strengths and weaknesses.
*   **Focus on Accuracy:** With negative marking, it's crucial to prioritize accuracy over attempting every question.

## SNAP 2026 Cut Offs and Selection Process

After the SNAP results are declared, each SIU institute will release its own cut-off scores for shortlisting candidates for the subsequent selection rounds. These cut-offs vary annually based on factors like the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. Generally, top SIU institutes might have cut-offs ranging from 90+ percentile, while others could be lower.

### Selection Process

1.  **SNAP Test:** Candidates appear for the computer-based test.
2.  **Shortlisting:** Based on SNAP scores and institute-specific cut-offs, candidates are shortlisted for the GE-PI rounds.
3.  **Group Exercise (GE):** Shortlisted candidates participate in a group exercise where their teamwork, communication, and leadership skills are assessed.
4.  **Personal Interview (PI):** This round evaluates a candidate's personality, communication skills, general awareness, and suitability for an MBA program.
5.  **Final Merit List:** A composite score, typically combining SNAP score, GE, PI, and academic profile, is used to generate the final merit list for admissions.

For the most precise and up-to-date information, always refer to the official SNAPTEST website.
