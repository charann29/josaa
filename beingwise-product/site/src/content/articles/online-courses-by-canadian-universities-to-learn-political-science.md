# 20+ Online Courses by Canadian Universities to Learn Political Science

Political Science is a dynamic field that explores the intricacies of governance, international relations, and public policy. For those aspiring to careers in politics, diplomacy, or policy analysis, building a strong foundation in this subject is essential. Fortunately, a wide array of online courses from Canadian universities offers flexible pathways to achieve this.

## Key Takeaways

*   Canadian universities offer a diverse range of online Political Science programs, from undergraduate to doctoral levels.
*   These programs provide a strong foundation for careers in government, international organizations, and policy-making.
*   Online learning from Canadian institutions offers global recognition and flexible study options.
*   Courses cover various aspects, including Canadian politics, international trade, human security, and Indigenous governance.

## Why Consider Canadian Universities for Political Science?

Canadian universities are renowned globally for their high academic standards and innovative research. Opting for an online Political Science course from a Canadian institution means you'll receive an education that is respected worldwide. These programs often integrate both Canadian and global perspectives, providing a comprehensive understanding of political systems and challenges. The flexibility of online learning also allows students to pursue their education without geographical constraints, making it an excellent investment for a future-proof career.

## Building Your Foundation: Undergraduate and Diploma Options

For those just starting their journey in Political Science, several Canadian universities offer foundational online programs. A Bachelor of Arts in Political Science or Political Economy can provide a broad understanding of political theories, institutions, and processes. These degrees typically take around four years to complete. Additionally, shorter diploma programs, such as a Diploma in Political Science, can offer a focused introduction to the field, often completed within a few months, preparing students for entry-level roles or further study.

## Advancing Your Expertise: Master's and Graduate Programs

If you're looking to deepen your knowledge or specialize in a particular area, Canadian universities offer numerous online master's and graduate diploma programs. These can range from a Master of Arts in Political Science, focusing on advanced theories and research, to specialized degrees like a Master of International Trade, MA in Human Security and Peacebuilding, or Master of Arts in Policy Studies. The duration for master's programs typically varies from 12 months for course-based options to up to two years for thesis-based curricula, while graduate diplomas can be completed in about a year.

## Specialized Certificates and Doctoral Studies

For those interested in niche areas or aiming for academic careers, Canadian universities also provide online certificate programs and doctoral degrees. Certificates in areas like Indigenous Governance and Politics or Politics and Law offer focused learning on specific topics, enhancing professional skills. For the highest level of academic pursuit, a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Political Science is available, typically taking four to six years, preparing scholars for research and teaching roles in academia.
