# Interior Design Certificate Course Online by Upskillist: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone passionate about aesthetics and functional spaces, an online certificate in interior design can be a fantastic way to gain professional skills. This program, offered by Upskillist, provides a structured path to learn the fundamentals and practical applications of interior design, making it accessible to a wide audience.

## Key takeaways

*   This is an online, self-paced professional design course certified by AP State University.
*   It offers globally recognized certification upon successful completion.
*   The program spans 16 weeks and includes approximately 32 lessons across four modules.
*   Learners benefit from live chat support, downloadable materials, and regular assessments.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Upskillist Interior Design Course Online falls under their 'Continuing Education' category. It's designed for flexible learning, allowing students to progress at their own convenience through video and text-based lessons. The curriculum is divided into four main modules, each further broken down into smaller, manageable sections. To ensure comprehensive understanding, the course incorporates multiple exams, assessments, and weekly assignments, alongside an assignment after each module.

Upon completing all four modules and submitting the required assignments, students become eligible for an e-diploma.

## What You Will Learn

This program aims to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills essential for an interior designer. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Distinguishing between the seven core principles of design.
*   Developing practical skills for executing diverse interior design projects.
*   Mastering communication through sketching techniques.
*   Analyzing current design trends and applying them effectively.
*   Understanding and utilizing various tools and tricks employed by professional designers.
*   Learning critical elements of design sketching.
*   Reading and creating floor plans with precision.
*   Gaining proficiency in computer-aided interior design programs.
*   Building a professional work portfolio to showcase design capabilities.

## Eligibility and Certification

One of the significant advantages of this course is its open eligibility. Anyone with an interest in acquiring professional accreditation in interior design can enroll, regardless of their prior educational background. The primary requirement for certification is the successful completion of the online course content and submission of all module-end assignments. The e-diploma is provided by Upskillist itself, with the course being certified by AP State University and recognized globally.

## Course Fees and Duration

The program is structured for 16 weeks of online learning. While the source does not detail specific course fees, it does confirm the availability of a certificate provided by Upskillist upon meeting the qualification criteria. The course includes approximately 32 lessons, delivered through an international-level curriculum curated by in-house experts.

## How to Apply

The admission process for the Upskillist Interior Design Course Online is straightforward:

1.  **Visit the Course Webpage:** Begin by navigating to the specific course page on the Upskillist platform.
2.  **Locate the Course:** Click on the 'Course Catalogue' tab, select 'Design,' and then choose 'Learn Interior Design' from the available options. This will take you directly to the course's dedicated webpage.
3.  **Register Your Account:** Look for a tab, possibly labeled 'Get 4 Weeks Free' or similar, to start creating your student ID. You will need to provide your email address and set a password to complete the registration.
4.  **Begin Learning:** Once registered, you can log in using your new student ID and commence your professional interior design studies.

This course offers a flexible and comprehensive pathway for aspiring interior designers to gain a globally recognized certification and develop essential skills from the comfort of their home.
