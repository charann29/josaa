# Edology Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Edology Courses

Edology provides a platform for a range of online and hybrid postgraduate programs, collaborating with industry leaders and academic institutions to deliver specialized education. These courses are designed to equip students with practical skills and industry-recognized certifications in high-demand fields.

## Key Takeaways

*   Edology offers various postgraduate programs in collaboration with top industry partners like KPMG and IBM, as well as academic institutions like UPES Online.
*   Programs typically last 10 months and combine online learning with practical, project-based experiences.
*   Course fees vary significantly depending on the program and its industry partner, ranging from approximately ₹53,000 to ₹3,39,000.
*   Graduates receive certificates from the collaborating institutions or industry giants, enhancing their professional credentials.

## Postgraduate Program in Technology Management

This 10-month hybrid learning program is offered through Edology in collaboration with KPMG. It's tailored for individuals aspiring to lead technological initiatives within organizations. Technology managers play a crucial role in implementing new software and optimizing digital infrastructure, making them highly sought after in today's evolving business landscape.

The program requires a commitment of 4-5 hours per week, blending online modules with in-person sessions led by KPMG experts. Students engage in simulated client scenarios, working in groups to solve real-world data challenges. A key feature of this course is the opportunity to specialize in either Data Science or Big Data and Cloud, allowing students to deepen their expertise in a chosen area. The fee for this program is approximately ₹3,39,000.

## Post Graduate Program in Data Science

Edology partners with IBM, Innovation Centre for Education, and University to offer this 10-month postgraduate program focused on the fundamentals of data science. This course is designed to impart a comprehensive understanding of how data science underpins modern technology and business strategies.

Students engage in various projects and case studies, culminating in a capstone project that allows them to apply their learnings in a practical context. The curriculum emphasizes hands-on experience, enabling participants to develop and showcase analytical and technical skills relevant to diverse business dimensions. Upon completion, students receive a certificate from IBM, a globally recognized name in the technology sector. The approximate fee for this program is ₹1,20,000.

## Programs in Industrial Safety and Air Business Management

Edology also facilitates postgraduate programs in specialized areas such as Industrial Safety and Air Business Management, offered in collaboration with UPES Online. These 10-month programs cater to specific industry needs:

*   **Post Graduate Program in Industrial Safety:** This program focuses on critical aspects of safety management in industrial environments. The fee is approximately ₹53,000.
*   **Post Graduate Program in Air Business Management:** Designed for those interested in the aviation sector, this course covers various facets of air business operations and management. The fee is approximately ₹75,000.

These offerings demonstrate Edology's commitment to providing diverse educational pathways in collaboration with reputable academic partners.

For the most current information on eligibility, specific curriculum details, and application procedures for any of these programs, students are advised to refer to the official Edology platform or the respective partner institution's official websites.
