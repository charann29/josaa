# For Management Quota in CBIT 2026 BTech CSE: Full Fee with Hostel

Considering a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) Hyderabad through the management quota for the 2026 intake? This guide breaks down the approximate full costs, including hostel expenses, to help you plan effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   The management quota, also known as Category B, offers an alternative admission pathway.
*   Expect the total tuition fee for the four-year B.Tech CSE program to be in the range of ₹9 lakh to ₹11.5 lakh.
*   Hostel accommodation for four years typically adds around ₹2.2 lakh to the overall cost.
*   The combined approximate total for tuition and hostel fees will range from ₹11.2 lakh to ₹13.7 lakh, not including other expenses.

## Understanding Management Quota (Category B) Admissions

CBIT, like many private engineering colleges, reserves a certain percentage of its seats under a 'management quota' or 'Category B' for direct admissions. These seats are typically filled based on criteria set by the college, often involving applications directly to the institution. It's distinct from seats filled through state-level counselling like TS EAMCET, which primarily fall under Category A.

## B.Tech CSE Tuition Fees for Management Quota

For students aspiring to pursue a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at CBIT through the management quota, the tuition fees cover the academic aspects of the four-year program. Based on current estimates, the full tuition fee for the entire duration of the course can range approximately from ₹9 lakh to ₹11.5 lakh. This figure is solely for academic instruction and does not include any living expenses or additional college charges.

## Hostel Accommodation Costs at CBIT

For students requiring on-campus accommodation, CBIT offers hostel facilities. The estimated cost for hostel accommodation over the full four years of the B.Tech program is approximately ₹2.2 lakh. This fee generally covers the room and basic amenities provided within the hostel premises. It's important to note that this amount typically does not include food or mess charges, which are usually billed separately.

## Estimating the Total Four-Year Expense

To get a clearer picture of the primary financial commitment, we can combine the estimated tuition fees and hostel charges. For a B.Tech CSE degree via management quota at CBIT, the approximate total cost, encompassing both tuition and hostel fees for four years, would fall within the range of ₹11.2 lakh to ₹13.7 lakh.

### Other Potential Expenses to Consider

It's crucial to budget for additional expenses beyond tuition and basic hostel fees. These often include:

*   **Mess/Food Charges:** These are generally separate from hostel fees and can vary based on your dietary choices and the college's mess plan.
*   **Examination Fees:** Charged per semester or year for appearing in exams.
*   **Library Fees:** For accessing library resources, though sometimes included in tuition.
*   **Personal Expenses:** Daily commuting, study materials, project costs, and other miscellaneous personal needs.

These additional costs can significantly impact your overall budget, so it's wise to factor them in when planning your finances for the full four years at CBIT.

This breakdown should provide a solid starting point for understanding the financial commitment for a B.Tech CSE management quota seat with hostel accommodation at CBIT for the 2026 academic year.
