# Secondary: Business Studies (215) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students keen to explore the world of commerce at the secondary level, the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) offers a comprehensive online course in Business Studies (215) through the Swayam platform. This program provides a foundational understanding of business concepts, delivered in an accessible format for self-paced learning.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 24-week, self-study online course delivered through video lectures and reading materials.
*   It awards 10 credit points upon successful completion, recognized by NIOS.
*   The curriculum covers fundamental business concepts, from organizational structures to consumer awareness and career paths.
*   The course is offered on the interactive Swayam platform, making it accessible to a wide audience.

## Course Overview and Structure

This certification course, identified as Secondary: Business Studies (215), is an extensive online program designed to introduce students to the core principles of business. Administered by Ms. Anshul Kharbanda from NIOS, the course is structured into seven modules and delivered entirely online via the interactive Swayam platform. The learning journey is supported by both video lectures and comprehensive reading materials, often supplemented with project work and case studies to enhance practical understanding.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to provide a broad understanding of business management and its various facets. Upon completing the course, participants will have gained insights into:

*   The fundamental nature and scope of business.
*   Different forms of business organizations.
*   Understanding various service sectors.
*   Principles of buying and selling.
*   Techniques and channels for distribution.
*   Aspects of consumer awareness and protection.
*   Exploring diverse career opportunities within business and management.

### Detailed Syllabus

The 24-week program is thoughtfully structured to cover a wide array of topics:

*   **Weeks 1-3:** Nature and Scope of Business, Industry and Commerce.
*   **Weeks 4-6:** Forms of Business Organization, Cooperative Societies, and Joint Stock Companies.
*   **Weeks 7-14:** Various Services including Transport, Warehousing, Communication, Postal, Courier, Banking, Insurance, and Outsourcing.
*   **Weeks 15-19:** Purchase and Sale, Channels of Distribution, Retail Trade, Advertising, Sales Promotion, and Personal Selling.
*   **Weeks 20-23:** Rights and Responsibilities of Consumers, Consumer Protection.
*   **Weeks 24-26:** Choosing a Career, Entrepreneurship, and a dedicated Project Work segment.

## Duration and Certification

The Secondary: Business Studies (215) course spans 24 weeks, or approximately six months, of dedicated study. Upon successful completion, students are eligible to receive a certificate from NIOS. This certificate attests to the acquisition of 10 credit points, which can be valuable for further academic pursuits or career development.

## How to Enroll

Enrolling in the Secondary: Business Studies (215) online course is a straightforward process through the official Swayam portal. Interested candidates need to:

1.  Navigate to the specific course page on the Swayam platform.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the subsequent page, either sign in with an existing account or register for a new one using a username and password to proceed with the enrollment.

This course is an excellent resource for anyone at the secondary level looking to build a strong foundation in business studies and develop practical skills for future endeavors.
