# AIAPGET 2026: Exam Date (Soon), Registration, Eligibility, Syllabus, Pattern

The All India AYUSH Postgraduate Entrance Test (AIAPGET) is your pathway to postgraduate studies in Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy. Conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) on behalf of the Ministry of AYUSH, this annual computer-based exam opens doors to MD, MS, and PG Diploma courses across India.

## Key Takeaways

*   AIAPGET 2026 is expected to be held in July 2026.
*   Registration for AIAPGET 2026 is likely to commence in May 2026.
*   The NTA conducts AIAPGET as a computer-based test.
*   Successful candidates qualify for counselling conducted by the AYUSH Admissions Central Counseling Committee (AACCC).

## AIAPGET 2026 Dates (Expected)

While the official dates for AIAPGET 2026 are yet to be announced by the Ministry of AYUSH, candidates should anticipate the examination in July 2026. The NTA will release a detailed information brochure containing all crucial dates, including registration deadlines, admit card availability, and result declaration. Keep an eye on the official AIAPGET portal for the latest updates.

## AIAPGET 2026 Application Process

The application process for AIAPGET 2026 is entirely online. Aspiring candidates will need to register on the official NTA website. Here’s a general overview of the steps involved:

1.  **Online Registration:** Create an account on the official AIAPGET website using your email ID and mobile number.
2.  **Fill Application Form:** Log in and complete the application form with personal details, academic qualifications, and other required information.
3.  **Choose Exam Centres:** Select your preferred examination cities from the available options.
4.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of necessary documents, such as photographs and signatures, as per the specifications.
5.  **Pay Application Fee:** Submit the application fee online through various payment modes.
6.  **Final Submission:** Review all details carefully before final submission and take a printout of the confirmation page for your records.

NTA is expected to open the registration window for AIAPGET 2026 in May 2026.

## AIAPGET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for AIAPGET 2026, candidates must meet specific academic qualifications. While the detailed criteria will be provided in the official information brochure, typically, candidates must possess a Bachelor's degree in an AYUSH discipline (BAMS, BHMS, BSMS, or BUMS) from a recognised institution. It is also mandatory to have completed a rotatory internship of a specified duration. Always refer to the official NTA notification for the precise and updated eligibility requirements.

## AIAPGET 2026 Syllabus and Exam Pattern

The AIAPGET syllabus is comprehensive, covering the entire undergraduate curriculum of the respective AYUSH discipline. For instance, the syllabus for Ayurveda postgraduate courses will be based on the BAMS curriculum. The NTA releases the detailed syllabus on the official AIAPGET website. It is extensive and requires thorough preparation of all subjects studied during the undergraduate degree.

### Exam Pattern Highlights:

*   **Mode of Exam:** Computer-Based Test (CBT)
*   **Type of Questions:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Language:** English (for Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy papers); English and Hindi (for Ayurveda paper).
*   **Duration:** Typically 2 hours.
*   **Marking Scheme:** Details on positive and negative marking will be provided in the official information brochure.

Candidates are advised to download the official information brochure from the NTA website once it is released to understand the exact syllabus and exam pattern for their chosen discipline.

## AIAPGET 2026 Admit Card and Result

Once the application process concludes, candidates who successfully register will be able to download their AIAPGET 2026 admit cards from the official NTA portal. The admit card is a mandatory document to be carried to the examination centre. It will contain important details such as the candidate's name, roll number, exam centre address, and exam timings.

Following the examination, the NTA will release the AIAPGET 2026 results online. Qualified candidates will be able to download their scorecards. These scorecards are essential for the subsequent counselling process conducted by the AYUSH Admissions Central Counseling Committee (AACCC), where admissions to MD, MS, and PG Diploma courses are allocated based on merit.

Preparing for AIAPGET 2026 requires dedication and a strategic approach. Stay updated with official announcements and prepare diligently to secure your spot in a postgraduate AYUSH program.
