# Self-Driving Car Engineer by Udacity: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Are you fascinated by the future of transportation and keen to build cutting-edge autonomous systems? Udacity's Self-Driving Car Engineer Nanodegree program offers an immersive learning experience designed to equip you with the essential skills for this rapidly evolving field.

## Key takeaways

*   This Nanodegree program focuses on practical, industry-relevant skills for self-driving car engineering, covering areas like computer vision, deep learning, and sensor fusion.
*   The curriculum is developed in collaboration with leading automotive and tech companies such as Mercedes-Benz, BMW, and NVIDIA, ensuring up-to-date and valuable content.
*   Students get hands-on experience, including the unique opportunity to test their code on Carla, Udacity's own autonomous vehicle.
*   The program offers flexible, self-paced learning, supported by technical mentors, career coaches, and expert project reviewers.

## Course Overview

This exclusive Nanodegree program is meticulously crafted to cover all the core concepts and abilities required to become a proficient self-driving car engineer. You'll delve into critical subjects like computer vision, deep learning, localization, controllers, sensor fusion, automotive hardware, and vehicle kinematics.

A significant advantage of this course is the chance to apply your learning in real-world scenarios. You'll work on numerous industry-relevant projects, guided by renowned experts. A standout feature is the opportunity to run your developed code on Carla, Udacity’s dedicated autonomous vehicle, providing a tangible way to see your skills implemented.

## Program Structure and Support

The Self-Driving Car Engineer Nanodegree is designed for flexibility, allowing you to learn at your own pace, which is ideal for busy schedules. The program includes comprehensive support features to enhance your learning journey:

*   **Technical Mentor Support:** Get help clearing doubts and understanding complex topics.
*   **Personal Career Coaches:** Receive guidance on your career path in the autonomous vehicle industry.
*   **Experienced Project Reviewers:** Benefit from detailed feedback on your project submissions.
*   **Student Community:** Connect with peers and collaborate on learning.
*   **Career Services:** Access resume services, LinkedIn profile reviews, GitHub reviews, and interview preparation to boost your job readiness.

## Collaborative Design and Learning Outcomes

This program's curriculum is a result of collaboration with major players in the technology and automotive sectors, including BMW, DiDi, Uber ATG, NVIDIA, Mercedes, and McLaren. This partnership ensures that you are learning the most relevant and in-demand skills directly from industry leaders.

Upon successful completion of the Nanodegree, you will be able to:

*   Understand the intricate operations of self-driving cars.
*   Apply deep learning and computer vision to solve automotive challenges, such as predicting steering angles, detecting lane lines, and classifying traffic signs.
*   Implement sensor fusion to integrate data from various sensors for accurate environmental perception.
*   Utilize localization principles to determine a vehicle's precise position.
*   Apply the three stages of planning for vehicle maneuvering.
*   Develop proportional-integral-derivative (PID) controllers for vehicle actuation.
*   Program self-driving cars for autonomous operation.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Self-Driving Car Engineer program, candidates should possess foundational knowledge in several key areas:

*   **Programming:** Intermediate C++ (including linking, memory management, and classes) and intermediate Python (covering data structures and classes).
*   **Mathematics:** Basic Calculus (integrals, derivatives) and introductory Linear Algebra (vectors, matrices, matrix multiplication).
*   **Science:** Basic Physics concepts and fundamentals of Statistics (standard deviation, mean, Gaussian distribution).

## Fees and Duration

The program is typically completed in approximately five months, assuming a commitment of around 10 hours per week. The fee structure for the Nanodegree is as follows:

| Fee Component      | Amount (INR)     |
| :----------------- | :--------------- |
| All Access monthly | ₹ 20,500 /month  |
| All Access bundle  | ₹ 17,425 /month  |
| One-time payment   | ₹ 78,131         |

Upon successful completion, Udacity provides a certificate of completion.
