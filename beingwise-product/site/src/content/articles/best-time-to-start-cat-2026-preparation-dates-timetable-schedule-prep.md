# Best Time to Start CAT 2026 Preparation: Dates, Timetable, Schedule, Prep Videos

Deciding when to begin your preparation for the Common Admission Test (CAT) can feel overwhelming, but a well-timed start is crucial for success. This guide will help you understand the ideal preparation timeline for CAT 2026, which is scheduled for November 29, 2026.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Early Start is Best:** Aim to begin your CAT 2026 preparation by March or April 2026 to allow ample time for comprehensive coverage and revision.
*   **Syllabus Completion by August:** Experts recommend completing the entire CAT syllabus for Quantitative Aptitude, Verbal Ability, Data Interpretation, and Logical Reasoning by August 2026.
*   **Focus on Mocks Later:** The months leading up to the exam, from September onwards, should be dedicated to taking full-length mock tests and analyzing your performance.
*   **Flexibility is Key:** While an early start is ideal, a focused 6-month plan can still be effective if you begin in May or June, and even a 4-month intensive plan is possible for July starters.

## Understanding the CAT Exam Timeline

The CAT is a highly competitive examination, with a significant number of aspirants vying for a limited number of seats in top management programs, including the prestigious Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs). With CAT 2026 scheduled for November 29, 2026, candidates have a clear target date to work towards. The preparation journey is extensive, requiring not just knowledge but also speed and accuracy under pressure. Historically, only a small percentage of test-takers achieve the coveted 100 percentile, and most top B-schools look for scores above the 94 percentile.

## Expert Advice on When to Start

Many candidates wonder about the optimal starting point for CAT preparation. While individual abilities and daily study time play a role, there's a general consensus among experts. According to Parasharan Chari, an MBA expert and COO at Endeavour Careers, candidates should aim to finish the entire CAT syllabus by August. This suggests that starting your preparation by May at the very latest is advisable. He emphasizes covering the core sections – Data Interpretation, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, and Quantitative Aptitude – during June, July, and August. Following this, the focus should shift towards building speed, improving accuracy, and finally, attempting full-length mock tests.

## A Month-Wise Preparation Timetable for CAT 2026

Here’s a general timetable that can guide your CAT 2026 preparation, designed to help you cover all aspects of the exam systematically:

| Month        | Focus Area                                                              | Key Activities                                                                                                                              |
| :----------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **March-April**| **Foundation Building**                                                 | Understand the syllabus, identify strengths/weaknesses, begin basic concepts for Quant, Verbal, DI, LR.                                    |
| **May-June**   | **Core Syllabus Coverage (Phase 1)**                                    | Deep dive into Quant topics (Arithmetic, Algebra), Verbal (Grammar, Reading Comprehension), DI (tables, graphs), LR (arrangements).           |
| **July-August**| **Core Syllabus Coverage (Phase 2) & Revision**                         | Complete remaining Quant (Geometry, Modern Math), Verbal (vocabulary, critical reasoning), advanced DI/LR sets. Start regular short quizzes. |
| **September**  | **Speed & Accuracy Building + Mock Tests**                              | Begin taking sectional tests and 1-2 full-length mock tests per week. Analyze performance, identify weak areas.                               |
| **October**    | **Intensive Mock Tests & Strategy Refinement**                          | Increase mock test frequency (2-3 per week). Focus on time management, question selection, and test-taking strategies.                       |
| **November**   | **Final Revision & Stress Management**                                  | Review notes, attempt a few final mocks, focus on maintaining a positive mindset and good health.                                           |


Even if you start your preparation in May or June 2026, a disciplined 6-month plan can still lead to success. For those beginning in July, a focused 4-month strategy from August to November will require intense dedication.

## CAT Exam Structure and Slots

For the past few years, the CAT exam has been conducted in three distinct slots on the test day. These slots typically run from 8:30 AM to 10:30 AM, 12:30 PM to 2:30 PM, and 4:30 PM to 6:30 PM. Each slot is two hours long, a change from the earlier three-hour format. The examination authorities allocate these slots to candidates, accommodating a large number of aspirants across numerous test centers in India.

Choosing the right time to start your CAT 2026 preparation is a personal decision, but aiming for an early start will undoubtedly provide a significant advantage, allowing you to build a strong foundation and refine your skills effectively.
