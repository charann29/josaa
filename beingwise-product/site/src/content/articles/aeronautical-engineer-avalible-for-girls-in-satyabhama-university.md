# Is Aeronautical Engineering Available for Girls at Sathyabama University, and What are the Fees and Placements Like?

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology, a well-regarded institution, offers various engineering programs. This page clarifies the availability of Aeronautical Engineering for female students, its fee structure, and placement opportunities.

## Key Takeaways

*   Yes, Aeronautical Engineering is available for female students at Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology.
*   The total fee for the entire B.E./B.Tech Aeronautical Engineering course is approximately ₹70,000.
*   The institution has a consistent placement record, with an average salary package for Aeronautical Engineering graduates ranging from ₹4 to ₹6 lakhs per annum.
*   In a recent year, 268 companies recruited for Aeronautical Engineering, leading to 1827 student placements across all disciplines.

## Aeronautical Engineering for Female Students

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology is inclusive in its educational offerings, and the B.E./B.Tech Aeronautical Engineering program is indeed open to female applicants. The university prides itself on providing a diverse and welcoming environment for all students pursuing various fields of study, including specialized engineering branches like aeronautical engineering.

## Understanding the Fee Structure

The total fee for the entire B.E./B.Tech Aeronautical Engineering course at Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology is approximately ₹70,000. It's always advisable to refer to the official university prospectus or website for the most current and detailed breakdown of fees, including any additional charges like examination fees, hostel fees, or miscellaneous expenses.

## Placement Opportunities in Aeronautical Engineering

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology has a dedicated placement cell that consistently works to connect students with industry opportunities. For Aeronautical Engineering graduates, the placement record has been encouraging. In a recent academic year, 268 companies visited the campus specifically for recruitment in this field. Across all programs, a total of 1827 students secured placements.

The average salary package offered to Aeronautical Engineering graduates typically falls between ₹4 to ₹6 lakhs per annum. This reflects the demand for skilled professionals in the aerospace sector and the university's efforts in preparing students for industry roles.

## University Rankings and Recognition

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology has received notable recognition in various national rankings. According to the NIRF 2021 rankings, the institute was positioned 61st in the overall category and 55th in the engineering category. In the university category, it held the 40th rank in 2021, a slight adjustment from its 39th rank in 2020. These rankings indicate the institution's standing and commitment to academic excellence.

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology continues to be a viable option for students, including girls, aspiring to pursue a career in Aeronautical Engineering, offering a structured curriculum, reasonable fees, and promising placement support.
