# Advanced Sheet Metal Design using NXCAD by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Sheet metal design is a fundamental skill in many engineering fields, crucial for creating components used in everything from machinery to vehicle bodies. This program offers a deep dive into using Siemens NX CAD software, a leading tool in the industry, to master the intricacies of sheet metal design and fabrication.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to expertly use the sheet metal module within NX CAD (also known as UG NX).
*   Develop skills to apply real-world sheet metal design constraints and create industry-standard models.
*   The course is delivered through a flexible blend of self-study and virtual classroom sessions.
*   Successful completion earns you a certificate from Skill Lync, with merit certificates for top performers.

## Understanding Sheet Metal Design with NX CAD

Sheet metal parts form the backbone of numerous industrial applications, including brackets, enclosures, and various machine components, often manufactured using brake presses. Siemens NX CAD sheet metal design software is specifically engineered to handle the unique aspects of this process. It incorporates material and process information directly into its modeling features, allowing for the creation of flanges, bends, cut-outs, tabs, beads, louvers, dimples, and various edge and corner treatments.

This powerful software also enables quick conversion of existing solid models into sheet metal parts and the design of sheet metal enclosures for other components. With its widespread adoption across the sheet metal industry, proficiency in NX CAD is a highly valued skill.

## Course Structure and Learning Experience

The program is designed to provide a comprehensive learning experience through various modes. You will engage with video lessons, participate in webinars, attend workshops, and complete assignments and quizzes to solidify your understanding. The instruction medium is English, and the learning mode combines self-study with virtual classroom interaction, ensuring a flexible yet thorough educational journey. The course duration is flexible, allowing participants to learn at their own pace.

## Who Should Enroll?

The eligibility criteria require participants to hold or be currently pursuing a degree in Aerospace, Automotive, or Mechanical Engineering.
