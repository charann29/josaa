# TS TET 2026: Result & Final Answer Key (OUT), Score Card, Cutoff

The Department of School Education, Telangana, has officially announced the results for the Telangana State Teacher Eligibility Test (TS TET) 2026. Along with the results, the final answer key has also been made public, allowing candidates to verify their performance.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Results and Final Answer Key Released:** The TS TET 2026 results and the final answer key are now available on the official TS TET portal.
*   **Exam Dates:** The TS TET 2026 examination was conducted across various centers from January 3 to January 20, 2026.
*   **Scorecard Details:** Candidates can download their scorecard, which includes personal details, subject-wise marks, and overall scores.
*   **Lifetime Validity:** The TS TET eligibility certificate is now valid for a lifetime, offering long-term career prospects for qualified candidates.

## Checking Your TS TET 2026 Result

Candidates can access their TS TET 2026 results directly from the official TS TET website. Follow these steps to view and download your scorecard:

1.  Visit the official TS TET portal.
2.  Locate and click on the link for 'TS TET Result 2026'.
3.  Enter your required login credentials, typically your hall ticket number and date of birth.
4.  Your TS TET scorecard will be displayed on the screen.
5.  Download and print the scorecard for future reference and records.

### Details on Your Scorecard

The TS TET scorecard is a crucial document that provides a comprehensive overview of your performance. It typically includes the following information:

*   Candidate's Name
*   Roll Number
*   Father's Name
*   Mother's Name
*   Category (e.g., General, BC, SC, ST, PwD)
*   Subject(s) appeared for
*   Qualifying Status (Pass/Fail)
*   Marks obtained in each subject
*   Total TS TET score

## Understanding the TS TET 2026 Final Answer Key

The final answer key for TS TET 2026 has been released alongside the results. This key is instrumental in calculating the final scores and provides the correct answers to all questions asked in the examination. The tentative answer key was initially released on January 30, 2026, and candidates had until February 1, 2026, to submit any objections. The final answer key reflects any changes made after reviewing these objections.

## TS TET Certificate and Its Significance

Candidates who successfully qualify the TS TET examination are awarded an eligibility certificate. This certificate is a testament to their qualification for teaching positions in Telangana schools. A significant change is that the TS TET certificate now holds lifetime validity, removing the need for re-qualification. Furthermore, the TS TET score carries a 20 percent weightage in the Teacher Recruitment Tests (TRT) conducted in Telangana, making it a vital component for aspiring teachers.

## TS TET 2026 Qualifying Marks and Cutoff

To be considered qualified in the TS TET examination, candidates must achieve a minimum percentage of marks based on their category. These qualifying marks, often referred to as cutoff marks, are set by the Government of Telangana. The breakdown is as follows:

| Category    | Passing Marks       |
| :---------- | :------------------ |
| General     | 60% and above       |
| BC          | 50% and above       |
| SC/ ST/ PwD | 40% and above       |

Meeting these criteria is essential for candidates to receive their eligibility certificate and become eligible for teacher recruitment processes in the state. The TS TET application window for 2026 was from November 15 to November 29, 2025.
