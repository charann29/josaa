# Advanced Machine Learning and Signal Processing by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen on deepening their understanding of data science, IBM, in collaboration with Coursera, offers an advanced online program focused on Machine Learning and Signal Processing. This course is designed to equip learners with practical skills in cutting-edge data analysis techniques.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an advanced-level, 100% online program offered by IBM through the Coursera platform.
*   The total duration for completing the course content is approximately 28 hours, with flexible deadlines to suit your schedule.
*   Upon successful completion, you will receive a shareable certificate from Coursera and an IBM digital badge.
*   The course introduces key Python frameworks like SparkML and Scikit-Learn, essential for advanced data science applications.

## Course Overview

This online program is part of IBM's specialized offerings in advanced data science. It delves into the intricacies of machine learning and signal processing, providing a robust foundation for those looking to excel in these fields. The curriculum is structured to be self-paced, allowing learners to engage with video lectures, assignments, and practice sets at their convenience. The primary language of instruction is English, with subtitles available in several other languages including Russian, Arabic, French, and Spanish.

## What You Will Learn

The Advanced Machine Learning and Signal Processing course covers a range of critical topics designed to enhance your analytical and problem-solving skills. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Understanding and applying signal decomposition techniques.
*   Detailed exploration of wavelet transforms.
*   Developing practical skills in Support Vector Machine (SVM) demonstrations.
*   Familiarity with SystemML and its applications.
*   In-depth study of logistic regression principles and mechanisms.
*   Introduction to machine learning pipelines and the use of Apache SystemML in IBM Watson Studio.

## Program Duration and Structure

The estimated time to complete all course materials is around 28 hours. The program is delivered entirely online, featuring a blend of video lessons, textual resources, and interactive elements. Learners will engage with various components such as self-assessment quizzes and programming assignments to reinforce their understanding. The flexible deadlines mean you can progress through the course at a pace that fits your personal and professional commitments.

## Fees and Certification

While an audit option is available for free, providing access to course materials without graded assignments or a certificate, full enrollment requires a subscription. The fee structure for accessing the complete program, including graded assignments and a certificate, is as follows:

| Particulars                          | Fee Amount (INR) |
| :----------------------------------- | :--------------- |
| Audit course                         | Free             |
| 1-month full access                  | ₹4,018           |
| 3-month full access                  | ₹8,037           |
| 6-month full access                  | ₹12,056          |

Upon successfully completing the course within the stipulated time, candidates will be awarded a certificate by Coursera and an IBM digital badge, signifying their achievement and proficiency in advanced machine learning and signal processing.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Advanced Machine Learning and Signal Processing program is a straightforward online process:

1.  **Visit the Official Course Page:** Navigate to the official course page for the Advanced Machine Learning and Signal Processing program on Coursera.
2.  **Create an Account:** If you don't already have one, you will need to create a registered account on the Coursera platform.
3.  **Enroll:** Click the "Enroll" button to begin the registration process.
4.  **Payment:** Proceed with the required fee payment based on your chosen subscription duration (1, 3, or 6 months) to gain full access to the course content and assignments.
5.  **Start Learning:** Once the payment is successfully processed, you can immediately begin your learning journey.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to enhance your skills in a rapidly evolving field, making you more competitive in the data science landscape.
