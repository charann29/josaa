# Chandigarh Government Jobs 2025: Latest Government Job Vacancies

Chandigarh is set to offer a significant number of government job opportunities in 2025, with approximately 774 vacancies across various departments. This presents an excellent chance for individuals seeking a stable and secure career within the public sector.

## Key Takeaways

*   Over 770 government job vacancies are expected in Chandigarh for 2025.
*   Opportunities cater to a wide range of educational backgrounds, from diplomas to postgraduate degrees.
*   Both fresh graduates and experienced professionals can find suitable roles.
*   Application methods vary, with options for both online and offline submissions.

## Current and Upcoming Government Job Openings in Chandigarh

Several key departments and organizations in Chandigarh are announcing recruitment drives for 2025. Here's a look at some of the prominent opportunities:

### 1. Punjab & Sind Bank Recruitment

The Punjab & Sind Bank is currently seeking a Physiotherapist for its Zonal Office in Chandigarh. This is a contract-based position. Candidates should hold a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) or a Master of Physiotherapy (MPT) from a recognized university, with MPT holders receiving preference. A minimum of two years of experience as a physiotherapist in a hospital or clinic is essential. The deadline for submitting applications offline with self-attested copies is August 20, 2025.

### 2. Samagra Chandigarh Junior Basic Teacher (JBT) Recruitment

Samagra Chandigarh has issued a notification for 218 vacancies for Junior Basic Teachers (Primary Teachers). Eligible candidates must be graduates with a two-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed). The official notification was released on July 28, 2025, and the application window is open until August 28, 2025. The final date for fee payment is August 30, 2025. Selected candidates can expect a fixed monthly remuneration of Rs. 45,260. Applicants must be between 21 and 37 years of age.

### 3. Directorate of Social Security and Women and Child Development Department, Punjab (SSWCD Punjab)

This department has announced openings for Translators, Interpret/Sign Language Interpreters, and Special Educators. These roles are ideal for individuals passionate about child welfare. Interested candidates should submit their applications offline using the format provided on the official website. The last date for submission is August 28, 2025.

### 4. Reserve Bank of India (RBI) Bank Medical Consultant Recruitment

The RBI is recruiting for one Bank Medical Consultant (BMC) position on a contractual basis for three years. Applicants must possess an MBBS degree from a recognized university, and a postgraduate degree in General Medicine is also a valid qualification. Successful candidates will receive an hourly compensation of Rs 1,000, along with an additional Rs 1,000 for conveyance and Rs 1,000 monthly for mobile expenses.

### 5. CSIR-Central Scientific Instruments Organisation (CSIR CSIO) Technical Assistant Recruitment

CSIR CSIO is accepting applications for 25 Technical Assistant Grade III positions. These opportunities span various disciplines, including Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science, Electronics, Bioengineering, Chemistry, and Civil Engineering. Candidates must hold a Diploma or a B.Sc. degree in their respective fields. The age limit for applicants is 28 years, with relaxations for reserved categories. Selected candidates will receive a monthly salary under Level 6 of the pay matrix. The online application window closes on August 15, 2025.

### 6. Chandigarh Judicial Academy Recruitment

The Chandigarh Judicial Academy is inviting applications for five regular Multi Utility Staff roles, which include Peon, Chowkidar, and Hostel Attendant. Candidates for these positions must have passed Class 12th from a recognized board and meet the specified age criteria.

## How to Apply

Application procedures vary for each recruitment drive. Candidates should carefully review the official notifications released by the respective departments or organizations. Some positions require online applications, while others mandate offline submissions via post or in-person. Always refer to the official portals for precise instructions, required documents, and application deadlines.

Staying informed about these diverse opportunities can pave the way for a rewarding career in the Chandigarh government sector.
