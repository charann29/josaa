## NPAT Exam 2026: Registration Open, Exam Dates Out, Pattern, Eligibility, Syllabus

For students aiming for undergraduate programs at NMIMS, the NPAT 2026 application window is now open. This exam is your gateway to various sought-after courses across multiple campuses.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Applications are live:** Registration for NPAT 2026 commenced on January 28, 2026, via the official NMIMS portal.
*   **Multi-phase deadlines:** The first phase of registration closes on March 22, 2026, with a second phase extending until May 26, 2026.
*   **Flexible exam schedule:** The NPAT 2026 examination will be conducted over an extended period, from February 10 to June 1, 2026, in multiple sessions.
*   **Multiple attempts allowed:** Candidates have the opportunity for one main attempt and two retakes, offering flexibility to improve their scores.

## What is NPAT?

NPAT, which stands for NMIMS Programs After Twelfth, is a national-level entrance examination conducted by SVKM's Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies. It's designed for students aspiring to join a variety of undergraduate and integrated degree programs offered across NMIMS campuses. These include locations like Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Navi Mumbai, Indore, Ahmedabad, and Chandigarh.

## Programs Offered Through NPAT

NPAT opens doors to a diverse range of undergraduate programs, catering to various academic interests. Some of the popular programs include:

*   Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
*   Integrated BBA MBA
*   B.Sc. in Finance
*   B.Sc. in Economics
*   B.Com (including a minor in Business Analytics)
*   BBA in Fintech
*   BBA in Branding & Advertising
*   BBA in International Business
*   BBA in Marketing Management
*   B.A. in Liberal Arts

## Eligibility Criteria for NPAT 2026

To be eligible for NPAT 2026, candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 or an equivalent examination from a recognized board. It's crucial to meet the minimum prescribed marks specified by NMIMS. Before proceeding with registration, applicants should carefully review the detailed eligibility requirements available on the official NMIMS admissions portal.

## Registration Process and Important Dates

The application form for NPAT 2026 is available on the official NMIMS website. Candidates must ensure all details are accurately filled and submitted within the specified deadlines. Remember, completing the registration successfully is essential for downloading your admit card, which is a mandatory document for appearing in the exam.

| Event                  | Phase 1 Deadline | Phase 2 Deadline |
| :--------------------- | :--------------- | :--------------- |
| Application Deadline   | March 22, 2026   | May 26, 2026     |
| Exam Period            | Feb 10 - June 1, 2026 (multiple sessions) |



Stay updated with the official NMIMS admissions portal for any further announcements regarding admit card release and result dates.

Your journey towards a promising undergraduate degree at NMIMS begins with NPAT 2026. Prepare diligently and ensure all application steps are completed on time.
