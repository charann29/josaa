# Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security by Aegis School of Business, Data Science, Cyber Security and Telecommunication: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

The Post Graduate Program (PGP) in Cyber Security, offered by Aegis School in collaboration with IBM, is an advanced course designed to equip professionals with essential skills to combat growing cyber threats. This program aims to bridge the significant skill gap in the cybersecurity industry.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an 11-month, globally recognized program with a strong focus on practical and academic training.
*   Classes are held on weekends, making it suitable for working executives, and are available in both online and offline formats.
*   The curriculum is developed with IBM experts and industry professionals, leading to an IBM-associated PGP certificate.
*   The program includes a 2-3 month paid internship, offering valuable real-world experience.

## Program Overview and Structure

The Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security is an intensive 11-month course. It's specifically structured to provide a blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience, preparing participants for the dynamic world of cyber defense. The program's design, with weekend classes, caters to the schedules of working professionals seeking to upskill or transition into the cybersecurity domain.

Instruction is delivered in English, utilizing a flexible mix of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content. The program benefits from the expertise of IBM professionals and other leading faculty members, ensuring a world-class learning experience.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is comprehensive, covering a wide array of critical cybersecurity concepts and technologies. Participants will gain in-depth knowledge in areas such as:

*   Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing
*   Cloud Security (including AWS Cloud Security)
*   Cryptography and Digital Forensics
*   Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence in Security
*   Database Security and Security Access Management
*   Vulnerability Analysis and Privacy

This holistic approach ensures that graduates are well-versed in both offensive and defensive cybersecurity strategies, preparing them for roles like vulnerability analyst, security researcher, or security architect.

## Eligibility and Certification

### Educational Background

Candidates must hold a bachelor's, master's, or Ph.D. degree in fields such as Computer Science, Software Development, Engineering, or Information Technology.

### Work Experience

Preference is given to applicants with 2 to 25 years of relevant work experience, though the program is open to all eligible candidates.

### Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, participants are awarded a Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security certificate by Aegis School of Business, Data Science, Cyber Security and Telecommunication, associated with IBM.

## Admission Process

Applying to the Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security involves a straightforward, multi-step process:

1.  **Online Registration:** Begin by visiting the official program page and clicking the 'Apply' button. You will need to register with your phone number, name, email address, and a password.
2.  **Application Fee:** A non-refundable application fee of Rs 500 is required during the application process.
3.  **Personal Interview:** Shortlisted candidates will undergo a personal interview to assess their suitability for the program.
4.  **Document Submission:** Following the interview, candidates will be asked to submit necessary documents and potentially a demand draft for verification.
5.  **Offer Letter:** Successful applicants will receive an offer letter from Aegis School.
6.  **Fee Payment:** To secure admission, candidates must pay the program fees.

This program is an excellent opportunity for individuals looking to make a significant impact in the critical field of cybersecurity.
