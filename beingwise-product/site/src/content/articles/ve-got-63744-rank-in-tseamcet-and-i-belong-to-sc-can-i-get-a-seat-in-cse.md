# I've got 63744 rank in TSEAMCET and I belong to SC. Can I get a seat in CSE?

## Key Takeaways

*   Your SC category rank of 63744 suggests opportunities in specific colleges for CSE through TSEAMCET.
*   Top-tier colleges generally have much lower closing ranks for CSE, even for reserved categories.
*   Several reputable institutions in Telangana offer CSE seats at your rank range, with varying fee structures.
*   Thorough research into individual college cut-offs and fee details is essential for making an informed decision.

## Understanding TSEAMCET Ranks and SC Category Admissions

The Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture & Medical Common Entrance Test (TSEAMCET) is a crucial gateway for professional courses in Telangana. Admissions are highly competitive, especially for popular branches like CSE. For candidates belonging to the Scheduled Caste (SC) category, reservations play a significant role, often leading to different closing ranks compared to the general category. This means your SC rank of 63744 needs to be evaluated against the SC category cut-offs from previous years.

## Navigating CSE Demand and College Choices

CSE remains one of the most popular engineering disciplines due to its strong career prospects. This high demand often pushes closing ranks lower for premier institutions. For example, colleges like MVSR typically see SC closing ranks for CSE well below 20,000. However, many other quality institutions offer CSE programs, and these are where your rank becomes more viable.
