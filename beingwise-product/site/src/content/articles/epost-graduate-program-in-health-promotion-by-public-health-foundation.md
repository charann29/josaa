# EPost Graduate Program in Health Promotion by Public Health Foundation of India: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those passionate about improving community well-being, the ePost Graduate Program in Health Promotion, offered by the Public Health Foundation of India (PHFI), provides a comprehensive online learning experience. This program aims to equip participants with essential skills and knowledge in health promotion strategies.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a one-year, online post-graduate level program in health promotion.
*   It is taught entirely in English, using a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content.
*   The course covers fundamental public health skills, health promotion theories, and practical implementation and evaluation.
*   Successful completion leads to a certificate, based on continuous evaluation through quizzes, assignments, and forum participation.

## Program Overview

Launched in 2011, the ePost Graduate Program in Health Promotion was developed with significant input from leading institutions like the Keck School of Medicine at the University of Southern California and the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health. The curriculum is designed to help learners grasp core concepts and applications within the field of health promotion.

The program spans one year, with the initial eight months dedicated to foundational health promotion concepts. For the final three months, participants have the flexibility to choose from various elective subjects, allowing for specialization or deeper exploration of specific areas of interest.

## Learning Experience

This program is delivered entirely online through an e-learning platform. It uses Moodle as its Learning Management System (LMS), which allows participants to access all course materials, video lectures, and discussion forums from any internet-connected browser. This setup facilitates a virtual classroom environment, making learning convenient and accessible.

Throughout the course, participants engage with video lectures, text-based content, quizzes, and assignments. An active discussion forum also encourages interaction among learners and faculty, fostering a collaborative learning atmosphere.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the ePost Graduate Program in Health Promotion, candidates must hold a Bachelor's degree in one of the following fields:

*   Medicine
*   Public Health
*   Nursing
*   Dentistry
*   Biological Sciences
*   Psychology
*   Allied Health Sciences
*   Social Sciences
*   Or an equivalent qualification.

For certification, participants must diligently complete all course quizzes, assignments, and actively participate in discussion forums. Performance and responses in these areas contribute to continuous evaluation, leading to the eventual awarding of the program certificate.

## Course Fees and Duration

This is a one-year post-graduate level program. The course fees vary based on the candidate's region:

| Candidate Category      | Amount     |
| :---------------------- | :--------- |
| Indian candidates       | ₹ 36,000   |
| South Asian candidates  | 600 USD    |
| Other international candidates | 1200 USD   |

## How to Apply

Applying for the ePost Graduate Program in Health Promotion involves a straightforward online process:

1.  **Visit the PHFI Portal:** Navigate to the official Public Health Foundation of India portal for online programs.
2.  **Locate the Program:** On the homepage, find the 'ePost Graduate Program in Health Promotion' listed under ePost Graduate programs.
3.  **Initiate Application:** Click on the 'Apply' button. If you are a new applicant, you will need to register by providing your personal and educational details to submit the online application form.
4.  **Fee Payment:** Once your online application is approved, you can proceed to deposit the course fee. Upon successful payment, you will gain access to the program and can begin your studies.

The application form requires standard personal information such as your name, gender, date of birth, nationality, contact details (phone number, correspondence, and current address), and educational qualifications. Ensure all details are accurate before submission.

This program offers a valuable pathway for those looking to make a difference in public health through effective health promotion initiatives.
