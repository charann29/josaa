# DSSSB 2025: TGT Application Form, Exam Dates, Admit Card, Result, Syllabus & Vacancy

The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) conducts recruitment examinations to select eligible candidates for various teaching and non-teaching positions within government departments in Delhi. This page focuses on the TGT (Trained Graduate Teacher) recruitment for 2025.

## Key takeaways

*   The official notification for TGT posts was released on October 3, 2025.
*   A total of 5346 vacancies are available for TGT roles under Advertisement No. 06/2025.
*   The application window for TGT positions was from October 9 to November 7, 2025.
*   Exam dates for TGT posts will be announced by DSSSB soon.

## Understanding DSSSB Recruitment 2025

The DSSSB is responsible for filling various positions across Delhi government departments, including PGT (Post Graduate Teacher), TGT, Assistant Teacher, Counsellor, LDC Clerk, and Patwari. The recruitment drive for 2025 includes a significant number of openings, particularly for TGT roles.

Candidates interested in these government job opportunities should regularly check the official DSSSB portal for the latest announcements regarding application forms, exam schedules, and other crucial updates.

## Application Process for DSSSB TGT 2025

To apply for the DSSSB TGT and other advertised posts under Advertisement No. 06/2025, candidates had to follow a specific online procedure. The steps generally involved:

*   Visiting the official DSSSB online application portal.
*   Registering as a new user or signing in if already registered.
*   Carefully filling out the online application form with all required details.
*   Uploading scanned copies of photographs and signatures as per specifications.
*   Paying the application fee. For General and OBC categories, the fee was INR 100, while SC/ST/PwD/Ex-S/Female candidates were exempted from paying the fee.
*   Submitting the completed form and saving a copy for future reference.

### Important Application Dates

The application window for TGT and other related posts under Advertisement No. 06/2025 was clearly specified in the official notification:

| Post                | Advertisement No.         | Opening Date    | Closing Date    |
| :------------------ | :------------------------ | :-------------- | :-------------- |
| TGT and other Posts | ADVERTISEMENT NO. 06/2025 | October 9, 2025 | November 7, 2025 |

## DSSSB TGT 2025 Vacancy Details

The official notification from DSSSB outlined the number of vacancies for various posts. For the TGT positions, a substantial number of openings were announced, providing a significant opportunity for aspiring teachers.

| Advertisement Number | Vacancies Announced |
| :------------------- | :------------------ |
| 06/2025              | 5346                |

Candidates are advised to refer to the official DSSSB notification for a detailed breakdown of vacancies across different subjects and categories.

## Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility requirements for DSSSB positions vary depending on the specific post. While general parameters apply, candidates must meet the detailed criteria outlined in the official notification for their chosen role. For TGT roles, this typically includes educational qualifications such as a bachelor's degree in the relevant subject and a B.Ed. degree, along with age limits and other conditions.

Always consult the official DSSSB notification for the precise eligibility criteria pertaining to the post you are applying for.

Stay updated by regularly checking the official DSSSB website for exam dates, admit card releases, syllabus details, and result announcements for the TGT 2025 recruitment drive.
