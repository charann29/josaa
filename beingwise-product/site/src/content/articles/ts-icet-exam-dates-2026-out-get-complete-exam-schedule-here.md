# TS ICET Exam Dates 2026 (OUT): Get Complete Exam Schedule Here

For students aiming for MBA or MCA programs in Telangana, the TS ICET exam is a crucial step. This page provides all the essential dates and details for the TS ICET 2026 examination, helping you plan your application and preparation effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   The TS ICET 2026 exam will be held on May 13 and 14, conducted by Mahatma Gandhi University (MGU), Nalgonda.
*   The examination is spread across two days with a total of three shifts: two on the first day and one on the second.
*   Late fee registration is currently open, with varying amounts depending on the application date, culminating on May 3.
*   Hall tickets will be available for download from May 4 to May 14.

## TS ICET 2026 Registration Details

The online registration process for TS ICET 2026 is managed by the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE). Candidates have the option to register without a late fee during the initial window, or with increasing late fees as the deadline approaches. It's highly recommended to complete your application well in advance to avoid additional charges and last-minute rushes.

### Application Form Correction Window

TSCHE provides a specific period for candidates to make corrections to their submitted application forms. This window typically lasts for a few days. While many details can be edited, it's important to remember that not all information is modifiable. Therefore, always fill out your application carefully to minimize errors. The exact dates for the correction window are released along with the full TS ICET event schedule.

## Downloading Your TS ICET 2026 Hall Ticket

The hall ticket, or admit card, is a mandatory document for appearing in the TS ICET 2026 exam. It will be released for all candidates who successfully complete their registration by the final deadline. You will need to use your login credentials on the official TS ICET website to download it. The hall ticket contains crucial information such as your exam date, time, venue, and important exam day instructions. Remember to carry a printed copy of your hall ticket, along with a valid photo identification proof, to the examination center. Entry to the exam hall will be denied without these documents.

## TS ICET 2026 Examination Structure

The TS ICET 2026 exam, conducted by TSCHE, is a computer-based test (CBT) lasting two and a half hours. It covers three main sections: Analytical Ability, Mathematical Ability, and Communication Ability. The exam is designed to assess the aptitude of candidates for admission into MBA and MCA courses. As mentioned, the exam will be held across three shifts over two days in May.

Staying informed about these dates and preparing thoroughly will significantly enhance your chances of success in TS ICET 2026. Keep an eye on the official TS ICET portal for any further updates or announcements.
