Planning to pursue undergraduate studies at Symbiosis International (Deemed University)? The Symbiosis Entrance Test (SET) is your gateway. Understanding the key dates and schedule for SET 2026 is crucial for a smooth application process.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Registration Begins Early:** The application window for SET 2026 opens in December 2025.
*   **Two Exam Phases:** The SET exam will be conducted in two distinct phases in May 2026.
*   **Admit Card Release:** Admit cards will be available online a few days before each respective exam phase.
*   **Results in May:** Expect the SET 2026 results to be announced towards the end of May 2026.

## SET 2026 Important Dates at a Glance

Symbiosis International (Deemed University) has officially released the schedule for SET 2026. This includes dates for application submission, admit card availability, exam days, and result declaration. Keeping track of these dates is essential for all aspiring candidates.

Here’s a detailed breakdown of the SET 2026 schedule:

| Event | Tentative Dates |
| :------------------------------ | :------------------------------------- |
| SET Application Form Release | December 12, 2025 |
| SET Mock Test Date | April 6, 2026 |
| Last Date to Apply for SET | April 15, 2026 |
| Last Date for Application Fee Payment | April 15, 2026 |
| SET Admit Card Live (Phase 1) | April 24, 2026 |
| SET Admit Card Live (Phase 2) | April 30, 2026 |
| SET Exam Date (Phase 1) | May 2, 2026 |
| SET Exam Date (Phase 2) | May 10, 2026 |
| SET Result Announcement | May 20, 2026 |

## Application Process and Deadlines

The application process for SET 2026 commences on December 12, 2025. Candidates are required to register on the official Symbiosis International University website. This involves creating an account with a valid email ID and password before proceeding to fill out the detailed application form. The final date for submitting your application and paying the associated fee is April 15, 2026. It is highly recommended to complete these steps well before the deadline to avoid any last-minute technical issues.

When filling out the application form, ensure all personal and academic details are accurate and complete. Any discrepancies could lead to complications later in the admission process.

## Admit Card Details

The SET admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the examination. For candidates taking Phase 1 of the exam, the admit card will be accessible for download from April 24, 2026. Those appearing for Phase 2 will be able to download their admit cards from April 30, 2026. You will need your login credentials to access and download your admit card from the official portal. Remember to carry a printed copy of your admit card along with a valid photo ID to the examination centre.

## Exam Structure, Duration, and Timings

The SET 2026 examination will be conducted in two phases, giving candidates flexibility. Phase 1 is scheduled for May 2, 2026, and Phase 2 will take place on May 10, 2026. Each phase of the exam will be held on a single day. The exam duration is one hour.

Here are the specific timings for both phases:

| Particulars | Date | Timings |
| :---------- | :---------- | :-------------- |
| Phase-1     | May 2, 2026 | 09:00 am to 10:00 am |
| Phase-2     | May 10, 2026 | 09:00 am to 10:00 am |

It is crucial to arrive at the examination centre well in advance of the scheduled start time to complete all necessary pre-exam formalities.

Stay updated by regularly visiting the official Symbiosis International University website for any announcements or changes to the schedule.
