# Machine Learning with R Course by DataMites: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This article explores a certification program focused on Machine Learning using the R programming language, designed to equip participants with essential skills for data science roles. It covers core machine learning concepts, R programming, and model building through a structured curriculum.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is offered in multiple formats: live virtual, blended learning, and in-person classroom, allowing flexibility for students and working professionals.
*   Course durations vary by learning mode, ranging from a 5-month intensive period with significant learning hours to a year of self-paced study.
*   Participants gain practical experience through case studies and projects, culminating in an industry-recognized certification from IABAC.
*   The program includes support for career advancement, offering internship and job assistance to help participants transition into relevant roles.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The Machine Learning with R training is a comprehensive program available online and, for some options, offline. It's designed to teach participants about applying machine learning concepts using the R programming language. Key topics covered include various machine learning algorithms, the R language itself, and methods for building effective machine learning models.

The curriculum extends over approximately five months for intensive modes, delivering insights through video lectures, practical projects, and real-world case studies. The program is structured with different learning plans to suit diverse needs, and beyond the core study material, it provides valuable internship and job placement assistance. Upon successful completion, participants receive a recognized certificate.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The program is a foundational-level certification that can be pursued through several modes: live virtual classes, blended learning (combining self-study with instructor interaction), or traditional classroom training. The duration and learning hours differ based on the chosen mode:

*   **Live Virtual Classes and Classroom Sessions:** Typically involve 350 hours of learning over a 5-month period.
*   **Blended Learning:** Offers a more flexible, self-paced approach with up to one year of access to materials.

All modes emphasize practical application, featuring extensive case studies and projects. Participants in classroom training often receive cloud lab access. A significant benefit is the provision of job assistance, helping graduates secure positions in the data science field. The certification is accredited by IABAC, adding industry recognition to the qualification.

Key learning resources include:

*   Detailed case studies
*   Video lectures
*   Quick reference guides (cheatsheets)
*   Hands-on projects
*   Practice datasets
*   Comprehensive study materials
*   Regular newsletters

## Eligibility and What You Will Learn

This program is suitable for individuals looking to enter data science roles, as well as working professionals aiming to upskill. While there are no strict prerequisites for prior certification, candidates are expected to have some foundational understanding of machine learning algorithms and coding concepts.

Upon completing the course and passing a qualifying exam, participants will achieve certification. The curriculum is designed to impart knowledge in several critical areas:

*   Proficiency in the R programming language.
*   In-depth understanding of various machine learning algorithms.
*   Skills in statistical analysis relevant to machine learning.
*   Ability to construct diverse machine learning models.
*   Techniques for advanced data manipulation and preprocessing.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Machine Learning with R program, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official course homepage to access detailed program information.
2.  Click on the 'Enquire Now' option to connect with a Corporate Relationship Manager who can provide further guidance.
3.  Select your preferred course plan and complete the payment process.
4.  Once enrolled, you can begin your learning journey.
