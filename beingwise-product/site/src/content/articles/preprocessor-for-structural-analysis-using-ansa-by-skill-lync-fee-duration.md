# Preprocessor for Structural Analysis using ANSA by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program offers a comprehensive pathway to mastering ANSA, a crucial pre-processing tool in Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) and Finite Element Analysis (FEA). It's designed to equip aspiring and current engineers with practical skills for structural analysis.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn to use ANSA for pre-processing tasks like geometry clean-up, meshing (1D, 2D, 3D), and morphing.
*   Gain industry-relevant skills through project-based learning and expert mentorship.
*   The course is delivered online, offering flexibility with self-paced learning and virtual classroom sessions.
*   Successful completion leads to a course certificate, with a merit certificate for top performers.

## Course Overview

Developed in collaboration with industry experts, the Preprocessor for Structural Analysis using ANSA program focuses on applying ANSA for effective FEA analysis. The curriculum covers essential CAE engineering concepts, including the ANSA Graphical User Interface (GUI), manual mid-surfacing, 2D meshing, handling cast parts, solid meshing, batch meshing, and the fundamentals of morphing.

Beyond basic concepts, the program also delves into professional techniques such as advanced morphing, geometry clean-up procedures, 3D meshing, and shell meshing. Learning is facilitated through pre-recorded video lectures and weekly assignments, guided by experienced mentors who share industry best practices.

The core of the program is the ANSA software itself, a vital computer-aided tool used for both CDB analysis and Finite Element Analysis. Through practical, project-based assignments, participants will gain proficiency in the software and understand its real-world implementation for modeling and pre-processing tasks. Upon satisfactory completion, participants receive a certificate, enabling them to pursue CAE roles, particularly in the automotive sector and other related industries.

## Who is this program for?

This program is an excellent fit for several groups of individuals:

*   **Students:** Those currently pursuing or recently graduated with a B.E./B.Tech in Automotive or Mechanical Engineering.
*   **Professionals:** Engineers working in production, manufacturing, or related domains who aspire to transition into roles with greater responsibility in CAE.
*   **Aspiring Engineers:** Graduates interested in mastering FEA meshing techniques and geometry clean-up procedures before analysis.
*   **Skill Enhancers:** Individuals who wish to confidently mesh and clean any engineering model.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Preprocessor for Structural Analysis using ANSA training program, candidates must meet the following educational requirements:

*   They should either be graduates or currently pursuing a B.E./B.Tech degree in Automotive Engineering or Mechanical Engineering.

## Program Fees and Certification

The program fee for the Preprocessor for Structural Analysis using ANSA is **Rs. 40,000**. This fee covers the entire curriculum and access to learning resources.

Upon successful completion of the course, participants will receive a **Course Completion Certificate**. Additionally, outstanding candidates who rank among the top 5 percent of participants will be awarded a **Merit Certificate**, acknowledging their exceptional performance.

To register for the Preprocessor for Structural Analysis using ANSA training program, follow these straightforward steps:

1.  Visit the official Skill Lync website.
2.  Navigate through the website to locate the "Preprocessor for Structural Analysis using ANSA" training program.
3.  Click on the program title to access its dedicated webpage.
4.  On the course page, select your preferred payment option and then click on the "Enroll Now" button.
5.  Complete the enrollment form by providing your name, phone number, and email address, and then proceed to finalize your registration.
