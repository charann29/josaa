# HITS: Courses, Fees, Admission 2026, Placements, Ranking

Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science (HITS), established in 1985 by K.C.G. Verghese, is a well-known private deemed-to-be university located in Kelambakkam, Chennai. As a part of the esteemed Hindustan Group of Institutions, HITS offers a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines.

## Key Takeaways

*   HITS is a private deemed-to-be university in Chennai, founded in 1985.
*   Admissions for UG and PG courses for 2026 are currently open.
*   The institute accepts scores from national-level entrance exams like JEE Main, CUET, CLAT, CAT, and TANCET, along with its own HITSEEE.
*   HITS offers diverse programs in Engineering, Technology, Management, Law, and more.

## Admission Process and Important Dates 2026

For the academic year 2026, HITS has opened its application portals for various UG and PG courses. Prospective students should keep track of the following key dates and entrance examinations:

*   **CLAT PG 2026:** The third merit list has been released for LLM. The fourth list is expected on May 2, 2026 (10 PM).
*   **TNEA 2026:** Applications for BE/BTech counselling are anticipated to open in the first week of May 2026.
*   **CUET UG 2026:** Registration for various UG courses has closed. The CUET exam is scheduled from May 11 to May 31, 2026.
*   **CAT 2026:** Registration for MBA admissions is likely to commence in August 2026.
*   **CUET PG 2025:** Results for PG course admissions have been released. Candidates can download scorecards from the official website.
*   **HITSEEE 2026:** Applications for BTech, BArch, and BDes are open until April 22, 2026. The exam will be held from April 27 to May 2, 2026.
*   **TANCET 2026:** Application forms for MBA are live until April 10, 2026, with the exam slated for May 9, 2026.
*   **CLAT UG 2026:** The fourth merit list for integrated LLB courses will be published on May 2, 2026.

Students are advised to regularly check the official HITS admissions portal and the respective entrance exam websites (e.g., NTA for CUET, Consortium of NLUs for CLAT) for the most up-to-date information and application procedures.
