# Data Analytics by DevMountain: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's digital world, data is everywhere, and understanding it is key to making smart decisions. DevMountain's Data Analytics program helps you gain the skills needed to turn raw data into valuable insights, opening doors to exciting career opportunities.

## Key takeaways

*   This comprehensive program equips you with essential data analysis tools and techniques, even if you're new to coding.
*   You can choose between full-time (16 weeks) or part-time (24-36 weeks) study, with both in-person and remote learning options.
*   The course includes practical, industry-relevant projects to build a strong portfolio and offers extensive career support.
*   Upon successful completion, you'll receive a Data Analytics certificate from DevMountain.

## Program Overview and Curriculum

The DevMountain Data Analytics course is designed to teach you how to collect, analyze, and extract meaningful information from various datasets. Over 16 weeks for full-time students or 24-36 weeks for part-time students, you will explore the use and application of modern analytical tools and languages.

The curriculum focuses on core areas such as data cleansing, data modeling, data interpretation, and data visualization. You'll gain hands-on experience with important data analysis tools and programming languages, including Python, SQL, Git, GitHub, Linux, pandas, Splunk, and Jupyter Notebooks. The program is structured to provide a robust understanding of how to transform complex data into clear, actionable insights.

## Learning Experience and Support

DevMountain offers a dynamic learning environment, whether you choose to study remotely or attend in-person classes. The program is flexible, with classes available on both weekdays and weekends, delivered through a mix of video and text-based materials. The instruction medium is English.

A significant advantage of this program is its emphasis on practical application. You'll work on industry-relevant projects that will form a strong portfolio, demonstrating your abilities to potential employers. Beyond technical skills, DevMountain provides comprehensive career support, including help with resume creation, interview preparation assistance, and valuable employer networking opportunities. Scholarships are also available to eligible students.

## Fees and Duration

The full-time Data Analytics program runs for 16 weeks, while the part-time option extends from 24 to 36 weeks. The total fee for the Data Analytics program, specifically for the full-time remote option, is **$9,900 USD**. It's important to note that the exact fee can vary based on the campus location, specific course type, and enrolment dates.

Upon successful completion of the training, you will be awarded a Data Analytics certificate by DevMountain, acknowledging your newly acquired skills and expertise in the field.

## Eligibility and Application Process

One of the welcoming aspects of the DevMountain Data Analytics course is that it's open to students even without prior coding experience. The program is designed to build foundational knowledge from the ground up, making it accessible to a wide range of aspiring data analysts.

To apply, you would typically follow these steps:

1.  **Visit the Official DevMountain Website:** Navigate to the dedicated Data Analytics program page.
2.  **Review Program Details:** Carefully examine the course outline, fee structure, schedule, application procedure, and career support services.
3.  **Initiate Application:** Click on the 'Apply' button, which will redirect you to the application portal.
4.  **Select Program Options:** Choose your desired certification course, specify your preferred campus location (remote or in-person), and select between full-time or part-time training. After making these selections, you will see a list of available cohorts; pick a suitable start date.
5.  **Create an Account:** If you are a new applicant, create an account using your full name and email ID, and set a strong password.
6.  **Complete the Application Form:** Fill in your personal information, including your contact number, mailing address, city, state, zip code, birthday, and gender.

Further steps in the application process would typically involve providing additional details to complete your submission.

This program offers a clear pathway into the in-demand field of data analytics, equipping you with both the technical skills and career support needed to succeed.
