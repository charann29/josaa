# Salesforce Training Course: Admin & App Builder by BeingWise: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This comprehensive program is designed to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge to become proficient in Salesforce administration and application development, preparing you for key industry certifications.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course covers core Salesforce building blocks, data models, Sales and Service Cloud functionalities, and integration options.
*   It aims to prepare participants for the Salesforce ADM 201 and DEV 401 certifications.
*   Learning modes include self-study and virtual classroom sessions, available on both weekdays and weekends.
*   The program offers lifetime access to the Learning Management System (LMS) and 24/7 professional support.

## Course Overview

The Salesforce Training Course: Admin & App Builder delves into the fundamental principles of Salesforce, including its architecture, various licenses, and the intricacies of cloud computing. You will gain a deep understanding of SFDC data models and security models, enabling you to effectively work with both custom and standard objects. The curriculum also emphasizes mastering different models and tools for automating business processes.

Beyond technical skills, the program focuses on data management and validation. By the end of the course, you will be adept at creating and customizing reports, modifying dashboards, understanding deployment processes, and managing the application lifecycle of a Salesforce project.

## Learning Experience and Support

The learning experience is designed to be interactive and supportive. It includes instructor-led live online sessions complemented by real-life illustrations and case studies to enhance understanding. Participants receive lifetime access to an enterprise-grade LMS, which houses presentations and recordings of all classes. Additionally, 24/7 professional support is available to assist with any queries. Assignments are provided after each class to reinforce learning, and a community forum allows for peer interaction and knowledge sharing.

## Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Salesforce Administrator and Programmer using App Builder program is ₹19,995, inclusive of GST. From time to time, a 15% discount may be offered for a limited period. For added flexibility, No Cost EMI options are available, starting from approximately ₹5,999 per month for three or six months. For specific details on availing these payment plans, it is advisable to contact the program provider directly.

## Eligibility and Certification

There are no strict prerequisites for enrolling in the Salesforce Training Course: Admin & App Builder. However, individuals with existing working knowledge as an administrator, developer, or technical consultant may find it beneficial. Upon successful completion of a project, participants will be awarded a course completion certificate, which is a paid certificate provided by the program authority.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this certification course, you will acquire a robust set of skills, including:

*   A strong grasp of cloud computing fundamentals and Salesforce.com.
*   The ability to differentiate and utilize the building blocks of Force.com platforms.
*   In-depth knowledge of enforcing data validation, quality, and integrity.
*   Proficiency in using business process automation and Lightning process builders for standard and custom objects.
*   Mastery of various data models and security models, including their features, objects, relationships, and troubleshooting techniques.

This program provides a solid foundation for anyone looking to build a career in the Salesforce ecosystem.
