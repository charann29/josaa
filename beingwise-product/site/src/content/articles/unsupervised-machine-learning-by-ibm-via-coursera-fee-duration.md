# Unsupervised Machine Learning by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those keen to explore the intricacies of machine learning without the need for pre-labelled data, the "Unsupervised Machine Learning" course offered by IBM through Coursera provides a structured pathway. This program helps learners grasp how machines can discover patterns and structures in data on their own.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is delivered in English, entirely online, and designed for self-paced learning through videos and text.
*   It covers essential concepts like K-Means clustering, various distance metrics, and dimensionality reduction techniques.
*   While auditing the course content is free, obtaining a verified certificate involves a monthly subscription fee.
*   The program is flexible, allowing you to choose a subscription duration that suits your learning pace and budget.

## Course Overview and Learning Mode

This "Unsupervised Machine Learning" course is a comprehensive program designed to introduce you to a crucial branch of machine learning. The entire course content is provided in English, making it accessible to a wide audience. It is structured for self-study, meaning you can progress through the material at your own convenience and pace. The learning experience is a blend of video lectures and textual explanations, ensuring a multi-modal approach to understanding complex topics.

## Fee Structure and Certification

One of the flexible aspects of this course is its pricing model. You have the option to audit the course content completely free of charge, which allows you to access most of the learning materials without any financial commitment. However, if you wish to receive an official certificate upon completion, you will need to subscribe to the course. The subscription fees are structured monthly:

| Certification Option                | Fee Amount (INR) |
| :---------------------------------- | :--------------- |
| Unsupervised Machine Learning (Audit) | Free             |
| Unsupervised Machine Learning - 1 month | Rs. 3,277 /- per month |
| Unsupervised Machine Learning - 3 months | Rs. 2,185 /- per month |
| Unsupervised Machine Learning - 6 months | Rs. 1,639 /- per month |

The certificate is issued by Coursera, confirming your successful completion of the program. Choosing a longer subscription plan offers a more cost-effective per-month rate, which can be beneficial if you anticipate needing more time to complete the course thoroughly.

## Detailed Syllabus

The course is divided into several modules, each focusing on distinct aspects of unsupervised learning:

### Module 1: Introduction to Unsupervised Learning and K-Means

This module sets the foundation, introducing what unsupervised learning is and diving into K-Means clustering. You'll watch videos explaining the concepts, read supporting materials, and complete assignments. Practical labs are included to give you hands-on experience with K-Means clustering.

### Module 2: Selecting a Clustering Algorithm

Building on the basics, this module explores various distance metrics crucial for clustering. It covers the 'curse of dimensionality' and introduces advanced clustering algorithms like Hierarchical Agglomerative Clustering, DBSCAN, and Mean Shift. You'll compare these algorithms and work through practical labs.

### Module 3: Dimensionality Reduction

This module introduces techniques to reduce the number of variables in your dataset while preserving important information. It focuses on Principal Component Analysis (PCA) and Non-Negative Matrix Factorization, including practical examples and assignments.

### Module 4: Dimensionality Reduction (Continued)

This module provides a broader overview of dimensionality reduction, with a deeper dive into Principal Component Analysis. It includes optional notebook sessions and an example of dimensionality reduction applied to imaging. There are both graded and ungraded assignments to test your understanding.

### Module 5: Nonlinear and Distance-Based Dimensionality Reduction

The final module explores more advanced dimensionality reduction techniques, such as Kernel Principal Component Analysis and Multidimensional Scaling. It includes video lectures, readings, and assignments, along with practical labs to apply these concepts.

## How to Apply

To enroll in this course, you would typically visit the official Coursera platform. You can search for the "Unsupervised Machine Learning" course offered by IBM. Once on the course page, you will find options to either audit the course for free or subscribe to a paid plan to earn a certificate. Follow the on-screen instructions to register and begin your learning journey.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to develop practical skills in unsupervised machine learning, a key area in data science and artificial intelligence.
