# Snap Percentile of 92.12: SIBM Pune and SCMHRD Calls Possible for Kashmiri Migrants?

If you're a Kashmiri Migrant candidate with a SNAP percentile of 92.12, you're likely wondering about your prospects for top Symbiosis institutes like SIBM Pune and SCMHRD. This article explores your chances and what to expect next.

## Key Takeaways

*   A SNAP percentile of 92.12 places you in a very strong position for SIBM Pune and SCMHRD as a Kashmiri Migrant candidate.
*   Both institutes offer diverse MBA programs designed for comprehensive student development.
*   Admission to the flagship MBA programs at SIBM Pune and SCMHRD primarily relies on SNAP scores, followed by subsequent selection rounds.
*   Always refer to the official Symbiosis admissions portal for the most accurate and up-to-date information on cut-offs and processes.

## Understanding Your Strong Position

With a SNAP percentile of 92.12 and falling under the Kashmiri Migrant (KM) category, your chances of receiving interview calls from both SIBM Pune and SCMHRD are exceptionally high. The KM category often has specific reservation policies, which can significantly improve an applicant's prospects compared to the general category for the same percentile.

## SIBM Pune: A Premier Management Destination

Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM) Pune is widely recognized for its commitment to holistic student development. It offers a range of postgraduate management programs, including its flagship Master of Business Administration (MBA), an MBA Executive program, and an MBA in Innovation and Entrepreneurship. These programs are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed for leadership roles across various specializations. Admission to the full-time MBA program at SIBM Pune is primarily based on a valid SNAP score, followed by Group Exercise, Personal Interview, and Written Ability Test rounds.

## SCMHRD Pune: Cultivating HR and Business Leaders

Established in 1993, Symbiosis Centre for Management and Human Resource Development (SCMHRD) Pune has built a strong reputation for nurturing talent in human resource management and other business domains. SCMHRD's MBA program is highly sought after, and like SIBM Pune, admission to its MBA program is determined by SNAP entrance exam scores, followed by subsequent selection processes. For its Executive MBA program, valid work experience is also a key criterion.

## What to Do Next

Given your excellent percentile, your focus should now shift to preparing for the next stages of the admission process. This typically includes Group Exercises, Written Ability Tests (WAT), and Personal Interviews (PI). These rounds are crucial for demonstrating your communication skills, analytical thinking, and overall suitability for the programs. Keep a close watch on the official Symbiosis admissions portal for specific dates and instructions regarding these selection processes.

Your strong SNAP score and Kashmiri Migrant status put you in an enviable position for these prestigious institutions, so prepare well for the upcoming selection rounds to maximize your chances.
