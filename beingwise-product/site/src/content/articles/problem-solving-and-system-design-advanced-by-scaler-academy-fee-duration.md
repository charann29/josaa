# Problem Solving & System Design - Advanced by Scaler Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For tech professionals looking to sharpen their skills and advance their careers, the Problem Solving & System Design - Advanced certification from Scaler Academy offers a structured pathway. This program is designed to transform software engineers into top-tier professionals, making them both interview and job-ready.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 9.5-month advanced program delivered through live virtual classes and self-study materials.
*   It focuses on core Computer Science fundamentals, advanced programming, and system design using real-world projects.
*   Participants benefit from 1:1 mentorship, an industry-tested curriculum, and dedicated career support.
*   A prerequisite entrance test ensures the program is a good fit for aspiring candidates.

## Program Overview and Structure

This 9.5-month advanced certification is specifically crafted for experienced software engineers. It aims to fill the gap left by unstructured online learning, providing a comprehensive curriculum that covers essential Computer Science fundamentals, robust programming constructs, and in-depth system design principles.

The program features live classes conducted by expert faculty twice a week, complemented by video and text-based self-study materials. The immersive structure, combined with weekly assignments and contests, fosters a competitive yet supportive learning environment. Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Scaler Academy.

## What You Will Learn

Through its carefully designed syllabus, the Problem Solving & System Design - Advanced course helps participants develop crucial problem-solving abilities and advanced programming skills. Key learning outcomes include:

*   A deep understanding of Computer Science fundamentals.
*   Enhanced problem-solving capabilities through regular assignments and coding contests.
*   Opportunities for one-on-one system design discussions with industry experts.
*   Specialization options in full-stack or backend development, with a strong emphasis on designing complex systems through various development projects. This prepares learners to excel in challenging technical interviews.

## Who Is This Program For?

This certification is ideal for individuals who meet specific criteria, ensuring a cohesive and aspirational peer group. Suitable candidates include:

*   Professionals with any amount of work experience in engineering or development roles within software product or service companies.
*   Individuals who possess foundational knowledge in programming languages like C, Java, or Python and are comfortable solving basic coding problems.
*   Learners who may struggle with basic Data Structures and Algorithms questions and wish to strengthen these areas.
*   Those who are comfortable writing code for implementation and manipulating basic strings.

## Admission Process

Admission to the Problem Solving & System Design - Advanced program involves a clear, step-by-step process:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official Scaler Academy website.
2.  **Initiate Application:** Click on the 'Apply Now' button.
3.  **Registration:** Complete the registration form with your details to set up your account.
4.  **Entrance Test:** Take a 40-minute multiple-choice question (MCQ) entrance test.
5.  **Fee Deposit:** If you qualify, proceed to make the necessary fee deposit to secure your enrollment.

## Program Fees and Certification

While specific fee amounts are not detailed, the program does involve a fee deposit upon qualification. A certificate of completion, acknowledging your advanced skills in Problem Solving and System Design, is awarded by Scaler Academy at the culmination of the program.

This program is a significant investment in your professional growth, designed to equip you with the skills demanded by leading tech companies.
