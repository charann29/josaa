# Amrita CAT 2026: Application Form (OUT), Exam Date, Eligibility, Pattern, Syllabus, Results

Amrita Common Aptitude Test (Amrita CAT) is a national-level entrance examination conducted by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham for admission to its Master of Business Administration (MBA) programs across its various campuses. This guide provides essential information for prospective students considering the Amrita CAT 2026.

## Key takeaways

*   The Amrita CAT 2026 application period began on October 6, 2025, with the final date for submitting forms being January 26, 2026.
*   The examination for Amrita CAT 2026 Phase 1 was conducted on February 7 and February 8, 2026.
*   Beyond Amrita CAT, the university also accepts scores from other prominent management entrance exams like CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, KMAT Kerala, GMAT, and GRE.
*   Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham offers MBA programs with various specializations, including Marketing, AI and Business Analytics, Operations and Supply Chain Management, and HR Management.

## Amrita CAT 2026 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Amrita CAT 2026, candidates must meet specific academic requirements. Generally, applicants need a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university. It is advisable to refer to the official Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham website for the most precise and updated eligibility details, including minimum percentage requirements.

## Amrita CAT 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The Amrita CAT is a computer-based test (CBT) designed to assess a candidate's aptitude for management studies. The exam consists of 100 questions covering four main sections:

*   **Quantitative Aptitude:** This section tests numerical ability, problem-solving skills, and mathematical concepts.
*   **Data Interpretation and Analysis:** Candidates will encounter questions based on charts, graphs, tables, and other forms of data, requiring analytical skills.
*   **Verbal Reasoning and Language Comprehension:** This part evaluates English language proficiency, including reading comprehension, grammar, and vocabulary.
*   **General Knowledge on Indian and Global Scenarios:** Questions in this section assess awareness of current affairs, business, economy, and general knowledge relevant to India and the world.

Detailed information regarding the marking scheme and specific topics within each section can be found on the official Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham portal.

## Amrita CAT 2026 Application Process

The application form for Amrita CAT 2026 was available online on the official website of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. Candidates were required to register, fill in their personal and academic details, upload necessary documents, and pay the application fee. It is important to complete all steps accurately to ensure a successful application. For those applying to the MBA program, remember that the university also considers scores from other national-level management exams, offering multiple pathways to admission.

For further details and to stay informed about any updates, always consult the official Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham website.
