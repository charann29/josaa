# IIM Ahmedabad Master of Business Administration (MBA) Vs IIM Bangalore: Compare Fees, Placement, Reviews, Cutoffs

Choosing between IIM Ahmedabad (IIMA) and IIM Bangalore (IIMB) for your MBA is a significant decision, as both are premier institutions in India. This comparison aims to help you understand their key differences in terms of fees, placements, student feedback, and other important aspects.

## Key takeaways

*   IIMA was established earlier in 1961, while IIMB followed in 1973, making both well-established, autonomous government institutions.
*   Both institutes offer a two-year, full-time MBA degree, but their fee structures and placement statistics show some variations.
*   Student reviews generally rate IIM Bangalore slightly higher than IIM Ahmedabad, particularly in areas like placements and overall campus experience.
*   NIRF rankings consistently place both IIMs among the top management institutes in the country, often alternating between the top two spots.

## Institute Overview

IIM Ahmedabad, founded in 1961, holds a rich legacy and is often considered a pioneer in management education in India. IIM Bangalore, established in 1973, has also carved out a strong reputation, particularly known for its focus on technology and entrepreneurship. Both are public, autonomous institutions that have consistently produced top leaders and innovators.

## MBA Program Details

Both IIMA and IIMB offer a flagship two-year, full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree. These programs are designed to provide comprehensive management education, covering various aspects of business, leadership, and strategy. While the core curriculum might share similarities, each institute has its unique pedagogical approach and specializations.

## Fees and Financial Commitment

Understanding the financial investment is crucial when considering an MBA. Here's a look at the approximate total fees for the two-year MBA program at both institutes:

*   **IIM Ahmedabad:** The total fees for the two-year MBA program are approximately INR 27.50 Lakh.
*   **IIM Bangalore:** The total fees for the two-year MBA program are approximately INR 26.00 Lakh.

These figures represent the program fees and may not include other expenses like accommodation, personal expenses, or study materials, which can vary. It's always advisable to check the official institute websites for the most current and detailed fee breakdowns.

## Placement Opportunities

Placements are a major draw for MBA aspirants, and both IIMA and IIMB boast excellent career prospects. Here's a comparison based on recent placement data:

| Institute     | Median Salary       | Highest Salary      |
| :------------ | :------------------ | :------------------ |
| IIM Ahmedabad | INR 34.59 Lakh per annum | INR 1.10 Crores per annum |
| IIM Bangalore | INR 32.61 Lakh per annum | (Data not provided in source) |

IIM Ahmedabad reports a slightly higher median and highest salary compared to IIM Bangalore, based on the provided data. However, both institutes attract top recruiters from various sectors, offering diverse roles in consulting, finance, marketing, operations, and general management. For comprehensive details, students should refer to the official placement reports published by each institute.

## Student Feedback and Rankings

Student feedback offers valuable insights into the campus experience. Based on verified student reviews:

*   **IIM Ahmedabad:** Has an overall rating of 4.6 out of 5 from 35 student reviews. Students rated placements at 4.6, infrastructure at 4.5, faculty at 4.7, crowd & campus at 4.5, and value for money at 4.7.
*   **IIM Bangalore:** Has an overall rating of 4.8 out of 5 from 25 student reviews. Students rated placements at 4.9, infrastructure at 4.7, faculty at 4.7, crowd & campus at 4.8, and value for money at 4.8.

In terms of rankings, both institutes consistently feature at the top nationally. According to the NIRF rankings, both IIM Ahmedabad and IIM Bangalore are typically ranked among the top two management institutions in India, often exchanging the #1 and #2 positions year after year. Their global standing is also recognized by various international ranking bodies.

Ultimately, the choice between IIM Ahmedabad and IIM Bangalore depends on your personal preferences, career goals, and the specific learning environment you seek. Both offer world-class education and unparalleled opportunities.
