# Certified Accessibility Testing Professional by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's digital world, ensuring that websites and applications are usable by everyone, including people with disabilities, is crucial. The Vskills Certified Accessibility Testing Professional course teaches you how to test software and web services to make them inclusive and accessible for all users.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn essential skills for testing software and web products to meet accessibility standards.
*   Gain a government-certified credential that can enhance your professional profile.
*   Enjoy the flexibility of self-paced online learning with lifelong access to study materials.
*   Benefit from placement support and access to the Vskills job centre.

## Course Overview

As digital services and brands increasingly prioritize a wider user base, the field of software testing has evolved to address the needs of individuals with disabilities. This Vskills certification focuses on accessibility testing, a specialized area within usability testing. The program is designed to equip you with the knowledge and practical methods required to identify and resolve accessibility barriers in web products and applications. By mastering these techniques, you'll contribute to creating a more inclusive digital environment for everyone.

## What You Will Learn

The Certified Accessibility Testing Professional syllabus covers critical aspects of software development and testing, specifically tailored for accessibility. You will gain skills and knowledge in:

*   Understanding the fundamentals of accessibility testing and its importance.
*   Learning how to evaluate software functionality from the perspective of a person with special needs.
*   Identifying faults and defects in software through comprehensive usability and accessibility tests.
*   Developing innovative solutions to enhance the inclusivity of applications and web services.

## Eligibility and Examination

To earn the Certificate for Accessibility Testing Professional Course by Vskills, candidates must successfully pass an online examination. The eligibility for certification is based on achieving a minimum score of 50% on this online exam. Importantly, there is no negative marking in the examination, allowing candidates to attempt questions without penalty.

## Course Fees and Duration

The fee for the Certified Accessibility Testing Professional Course by Vskills is ₹3,499. This fee can be conveniently paid online using net banking, credit cards, or debit cards. The course is structured for self-study and includes approximately 12 hours of learning material, delivered through video and text-based modules. You will have lifelong access to the study materials, even after completing the course.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Vskills Certified Accessibility Testing Professional course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Vskills website.
2.  Navigate to the 'Testing' section and locate the 'Accessibility Testing Certification'.
3.  Review the course details thoroughly.
4.  Proceed to enroll for the course by registering on the Vskills platform.
5.  Complete the payment process online to finalize your admission.

This certification can open doors to various roles, including Computer Programmer, IT Engineer, Software Quality Assurance Engineer, Software Engineer, Software Developer, Computer System Analyst, Computer Systems Administrator, and Computer Systems Engineer, by adding a valuable, in-demand skill to your profile.
