# Geography XII Part-II by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online certificate course, offered through the Swayam platform, provides a comprehensive study of Class 12 Geography Part-II, covering essential topics from the NCERT curriculum. It's designed for self-paced learning, making it accessible to students across India.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is entirely free to enroll in and complete, with an optional certificate available.
*   It spans 24 weeks and is delivered through a blend of video lectures and reading materials.
*   Successful completion leads to a certificate jointly issued by NCERT and Swayam.
*   The curriculum is structured around the official Class 12 Geography textbook, ensuring academic relevance.

## Course Overview and Content

This program delves into crucial aspects of human geography and India's resources, as outlined in the Class 12 Geography Part-II syllabus. Key areas of study include human resources, various settlement patterns, the management of land, water, and mineral resources, India's agricultural systems, and the intricacies of transport, communication, and international trade. The course also explores a geographical perspective on contemporary issues.

The content is delivered through a combination of video lectures, detailed e-texts, and supplementary web resources, all designed for self-study. Dr. Tannu Malik from the Department of Education in Social Sciences (DESS), NCERT, New Delhi, leads the instruction.

## Course Duration and Structure

The Geography XII Part-II course is structured to be completed over 24 weeks. It is divided into several modules, each focusing on specific chapters and topics from the Class 12 syllabus. Regular assignments are integrated throughout the course to help students assess their understanding, culminating in a final assessment.

### Detailed Syllabus Breakdown

The course covers the following chapters and modules:

*   **Chapter 1: Population: Distribution, Density, Growth & Composition**
    *   Modules on population distribution, growth variations, consequences, adolescents, and various compositions (rural-urban, linguistic, religious, working population).
*   **Chapter 2: Migration**
    *   Modules on different types of migration, their causes, and consequences.
*   **Chapter 3: Human Development in India**
    *   Two modules dedicated to understanding human development concepts within the Indian context.
*   **Chapter 4: Human Settlements**
    *   Two modules introducing human settlements, with a focus on rural settlements.
*   **Chapter 5: Land Resources and Agriculture**
    *   Three modules covering various aspects of land resources and the Indian agricultural system.
*   **Chapter 6: Water Resources**
    *   Two modules exploring water resources in India.

Each chapter includes assignments to reinforce learning, leading up to a comprehensive final assessment.

## Certification and Eligibility

Upon successful completion of the course, students are eligible to receive a certificate. To earn this certificate, participants must achieve a score of at least 70% in the final assessment. The certificate is jointly provided by NCERT and Swayam.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Geography XII Part-II Certification Course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam platform.
2.  Locate the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  Proceed to sign in with an existing account or register for a new one using your preferred credentials.
4.  Once signed in, search for the Geography XII Part-II course and enroll.

This course is ideal for Class 12 students who wish to deepen their understanding of Geography, providing a flexible and comprehensive learning experience from a trusted educational body.
