# Continuous Quality Improvement: Tools And Techniques by National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This article explores a valuable course offered through Swayam, focusing on Continuous Quality Improvement (CQI) in healthcare. Developed by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (NABH), this program is designed to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills to drive quality enhancements within their organizations.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is offered online through Swayam, making it accessible for self-paced learning.
*   While enrollment and learning are free, a certificate requires passing a proctored examination.
*   It's a 12-week program, ideal for postgraduate students or professionals interested in quality management.
*   Successful completion can open doors to better job prospects in leading healthcare organizations.

## Course Overview

The "Continuous Quality Improvement: Tools and Techniques" course aims to empower participants with a deep understanding of quality improvement principles. The healthcare sector, in particular, demands the highest standards, and this program helps individuals grasp why quality is paramount and how to implement effective strategies. It encourages innovative thinking and practical application of CQI methods.

This course is well-suited for aspiring business owners, individuals keen on management studies, or professionals already involved in developing and improving business processes within an organization. It's an ongoing course, delivered through a combination of video and text-based materials, and carries 4 course credits.

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this certification program, participants will gain a comprehensive understanding of how continuous quality improvement integrates into a hospital's culture and its significant benefits for the healthcare sector. You will become proficient in implementing CQI methods within your own organization and develop the ability to actively participate in or even lead CQI initiatives.

Key learning outcomes include:

*   Understanding the role of continuous quality improvement in healthcare.
*   Gaining skills to implement various CQI tools and techniques.
*   Developing the capacity to lead or contribute effectively to quality improvement projects.
*   Enhancing problem-solving abilities related to quality challenges.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

To get the most out of this course, it is recommended that applicants hold a postgraduate degree, particularly in business management studies, and possess a foundational understanding of management principles. Meeting these prerequisites will not only aid in course comprehension but can also significantly enhance your opportunities for high-paying positions in prominent healthcare organizations such as Apollo Hospitals, Narayana Health, and Fortis Healthcare.

## Certification Details

While enrollment and access to course materials are free, obtaining a certificate from the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (NABH) requires successfully passing an examination. The certification is a testament to a student's dedication and hard work throughout the program.

To qualify for an e-certificate, candidates must achieve:

*   An average score of 25% on weekly assignments.
*   An average score of 75% on the final proctored examination.

The overall passing percentage for the certificate is a minimum of 40% in weekly assessments and a minimum of 40% in the final examination. Failure to meet these scores will result in ineligibility for the certificate.

Here’s how final scores translate into grades:

| Final Score Range     | Grade |
| :-------------------- | :---- |
| Less than 40%         | No Certificate |
| 40% - 49.99%          | Grade D |
| 50% - 59.99%          | Grade C |
| 60% - 84.99%          | Grade B |
| 85% and above         | Grade A |

## How to Apply

To enroll in the "Continuous Quality Improvement: Tools and Techniques" course, you can register through the official Swayam portal. The registration process is web-based and straightforward. There are no fees to enroll in the course or to access the learning materials. Technical support is available 24/7, and a grievance resolution system is in place to assist students.

This program presents an excellent opportunity to enhance your professional skills and contribute meaningfully to the quality of care in the healthcare industry.
