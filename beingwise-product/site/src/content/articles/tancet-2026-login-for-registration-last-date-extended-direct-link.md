# TANCET 2026 Login for Registration (Last Date Extended): Direct Link at tancet.annauniv.edu

Anna University provides a dedicated online login portal for candidates taking the TANCET exam. This portal is essential for various stages of the examination process, from initial registration to checking results and downloading scorecards. 

## Key Takeaways

*   The TANCET 2026 registration login window is currently active.
*   Anna University has extended the final date for TANCET registration to April 15th.
*   Candidates must use their registered email ID and password to access the login portal for all online TANCET activities.
*   The TANCET login dashboard is crucial for filling out the application, downloading the admit card, viewing answer keys, and checking results.

## TANCET 2026 Registration: Accessing the Login Portal

To begin your TANCET 2026 application, you'll need to create and access your candidate login. The direct link to the official registration portal is available on tancet.annauniv.edu. When you first register, you'll provide fundamental personal details such as your name, gender, category, and the program you wish to apply for, along with your email ID. These details help create your unique login credentials, which are vital for all subsequent steps.

## Important Dates for TANCET 2026 Login

It's crucial to keep track of key dates related to the TANCET login portal. The registration window for TANCET 2026 has been extended, and candidates now have until April 15th to complete their application forms. Always refer to the official Anna University website for the most accurate and updated schedule for all TANCET events.

## Functions of the TANCET Candidate Login Dashboard

The TANCET login dashboard serves as your central hub for managing your examination journey. Here's what you can do through this portal:

*   **Application Form Submission:** This is where you will fill out and submit your TANCET application form after creating your initial login.
*   **Admit Card Download:** Before the examination, your admit card (hall ticket) will be available for download through your login. This document is mandatory for entry into the exam hall.
*   **Answer Key Access:** Once released, the official TANCET answer key can be viewed and downloaded via your login, allowing you to cross-check your responses.
*   **Result and Scorecard:** After the exam, you will use your login credentials to check your TANCET results and download your official scorecard. This scorecard is a critical document for the subsequent admission counselling process.

## Retrieving Forgotten Login Credentials

Should you forget your password, the TANCET login portal typically offers a 'Forgot Password' or similar option. You will usually need to provide your registered email ID or other identifying information to reset your password and regain access to your account. It's always a good practice to keep your login details secure but accessible.

Ensure you complete all necessary steps on the official TANCET portal before the deadlines to avoid any last-minute issues.
