# MSBI Certification Training Course: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in Business Intelligence using Microsoft tools, a structured training program can provide a strong foundation. This article explores a popular online certification course designed to equip learners with essential Microsoft Business Intelligence (MSBI) skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 7-week, instructor-led online course focused on Microsoft Business Intelligence and data warehousing.
*   The program aims to provide a practical understanding of MSBI tools and their application in real-world scenarios.
*   Upon successful completion of a project, participants earn a "Microsoft Business Intelligence Analyst" certification.
*   The course fee is ₹17,999, with EMI options available.

## Course Overview and Structure

The MSBI Certification Training is a seven-week program introducing participants to core concepts of Business Intelligence and Data Warehousing. The curriculum provides an overview of various Microsoft BI components and the broader field of business intelligence.

The course emphasizes the MSBI architecture and the critical role of SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS) for data integration. It covers data flow components, differentiating between transformations like Data Conversion, Multicast, and Union, and includes hands-on demonstrations of features such as the Foreach loop.

The modules are specifically designed to offer insights into different tools within the Microsoft BI Suite. By the end of the course, participants are expected to have a practical grasp of data warehousing principles and efficient use of MSBI tools.

## Learning Experience and Support

The training is delivered through online, instructor-led sessions, typically held on weekends. The learning experience is enhanced by assignments, real-life case studies, and a dedicated community forum for learner discussions.

Participants receive lifetime access to the Learning Management System (LMS) and benefit from 24x7 expert support. The mode of learning combines self-study with virtual classroom sessions, utilizing video and text-based content.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the MSBI Certification Training course is ₹17,999. Payment options include a lump sum or Equated Monthly Installments (EMI), with EMI plans starting from ₹6,000. Variable discounts may be offered periodically.

Upon successful completion of the program, participants receive a "Microsoft Business Intelligence Analyst" certification. To qualify for this certificate, candidates must complete a certification project implementing the course learnings. This project needs to be submitted to the support team within two weeks of course completion for evaluation by industry experts.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

There are no strict prerequisites for enrolling in the MSBI Certification Training course. However, a basic understanding of Relational Database Management Systems (RDBMS) can be beneficial for a smoother learning experience. The course is suitable for a wide range of professionals, including:

*   IT Professionals
*   Analytics or Software Professionals
*   Individuals with a background in RDBMS, ETL, OLAP, or other reporting tools
*   Anyone aspiring to a career in Business Intelligence using Microsoft's suite of tools.

## What You Will Learn

Completing this program will equip you with several key skills, including:

*   Implementing an end-to-end ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) system using SQL Integration Services.
*   Developing comprehensive business intelligence solutions by integrating various Microsoft BI tools.
*   Utilizing SQL Server Reporting Services (SSRS) to present data through diverse report types and visualizations.
*   Understanding fundamental Data Warehousing concepts such as ETL processes and multi-dimensional modeling.

This training offers a structured path for individuals to enhance their skills in a high-demand area of data and business analytics.
