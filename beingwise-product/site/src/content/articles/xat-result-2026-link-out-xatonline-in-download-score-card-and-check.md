## How to Download Your XAT 2026 Scorecard

Accessing your XAT 2026 result is a straightforward process, exclusively available through the official XAT portal. Follow these steps to download your scorecard:

1.  Visit the official XAT website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Login' option.
3.  Navigate to the 'Scorecard' tab.
4.  Enter your unique XAT ID and password.
5.  Input the security text displayed on the screen.
6.  Click on 'Candidate Scorecard' to view and download your result in PDF format.

It's important to note that the scorecard will be available for download only until July 31, 2026. Therefore, candidates are strongly advised to download and secure a copy for all future reference and admission processes.

## How Your XAT Score is Calculated

The XAT examination is conducted in a single session, which means there is no need for score normalization. Your XAT percentile is directly derived from your raw XAT score. To understand how your raw score is determined, let's look at the XAT marking scheme:

| Marking Criteria        | Marks Awarded |
| :---------------------- | :------------ |
| Correct Answer          | +1 Mark       |
| Incorrect Answer        | -0.25 Marks   |
| Unattempted Questions   | 0 Marks       |

For general knowledge questions, there is no negative marking. However, for other sections, a penalty is applied for incorrect responses. This raw score is then used to calculate your sectional and overall percentiles, indicating your performance relative to other test-takers.
