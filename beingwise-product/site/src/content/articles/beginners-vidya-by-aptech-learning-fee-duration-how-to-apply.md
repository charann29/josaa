# IT for beginners- Vidya by Aptech Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to build a strong foundation in computer basics and internet essentials, the IT for beginners- Vidya program from Aptech Learning offers a structured pathway. This short-term course is designed to equip students with fundamental computer literacy skills.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a beginner-level course focused on essential computer skills and internet basics.
*   The program specifically covers Microsoft Office 2016 applications like Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and MS.
*   It has a duration of two months, with classes typically held for two hours, three times a week.
*   The course is delivered through a virtual classroom model, using video-based instruction in English.

## Course Overview and What You Will Learn

This training program is tailored for individuals taking their first steps into the world of computing. The curriculum is comprehensive, focusing on practical skills that are highly relevant in today's job market across various industries. Participants will gain proficiency in computer fundamentals, internet essentials, and the core applications within MS Office 2016. This includes mastering MS Word for document creation, MS Excel for data management and analysis, and MS PowerPoint for presentations, along with other MS applications.

Upon completion, learners will be well-prepared for roles requiring strong foundational computer skills, such as MS Office Specialist or MS Excel Specialist.

## Eligibility and Who It Is For

The IT for beginners- Vidya program is open to a wide range of individuals, including graduates, postgraduates, engineers, and working professionals from any academic background. Since it's an introductory course, no prior computer knowledge is required. It's ideal for anyone who wants to enhance their digital literacy for personal development or career advancement.

## Course Duration and Fees

The program spans two months, with a flexible schedule of two-hour classes held three times a week. This allows students to balance their learning with other commitments.

Regarding fees, the approximate cost for the IT for beginners- Vidya program ranges from ₹5,000 to ₹6,700. It's important to note that the exact fee can vary based on the specific learning center location, and applicable taxes will be added to this amount. It is advisable to confirm the precise fee structure with the chosen Aptech Learning center.

## How to Apply

The application process for the IT for beginners- Vidya training is straightforward:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the Aptech Learning official website.
2.  **Locate Short-Term Courses:** Look for the 'short-term course' section on the website and click on it.
3.  **Find the Program:** On the subsequent page, scroll down to locate and select "IT for beginners- Vidya."
4.  **Fill the Enquiry Form:** On the course page, you will find an option to "Fill the enquiry form." Proceed with this.
5.  **Submit Details:** Provide the required personal information, including your name, phone number, email address, state, the course name, and your preferred learning center location in the career guidance form.
6.  **Counselling Session:** An Aptech Learning representative will then contact you to arrange a career counseling session, which is also offered free of charge.

This program offers a valuable opportunity for individuals to gain essential computer skills, opening doors to various career possibilities and enhancing overall digital competence.
