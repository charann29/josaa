# International Institute of Digital Technologies Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online International Institute of Digital Technologies Courses

For students and professionals keen on advancing their careers in cutting-edge digital fields, the International Institute of Digital Technologies (IIDT) offers a range of specialized postgraduate programs. These residential courses are designed to equip learners with industry-relevant skills through expert-led instruction and modern facilities.

## Key Takeaways

*   IIDT, established under the Andhra Pradesh Electronics & IT Agency (APEITA), focuses on enhancing the IT and Electronics sector.
*   Programs are intensive, 11-month residential courses culminating in a postgraduate certification.
*   Courses cover high-demand areas like Data Science, Cyber Security, and AI & Machine Learning.
*   Learning involves global experts, modern digital tools, and term-end examinations.

## About the International Institute of Digital Technologies (IIDT)

IIDT was founded as an initiative under the Andhra Pradesh Electronics & IT Agency (APEITA). Its primary mission is to foster growth and development within the Information Technology and Electronics industries. By providing advanced training and state-of-the-art learning environments, IIDT aims to prepare a skilled workforce capable of contributing significantly to these rapidly evolving sectors.

## Post Graduate Programme in Data Science

This residential program delves into the essential components of data analytics, highlighting its critical role in business growth, informed decision-making, and strategic competitive advantage within the IT industry. Over 11 months, participants gain a deep understanding of how data science powers modern enterprises.

### Skills Covered:

*   Data Science fundamentals
*   Python programming
*   Deep Learning and Machine Learning concepts
*   Data Visualization techniques
*   Statistical analysis
*   Excel proficiency
*   Big Data principles
*   Financial Management insights

Instruction is provided by global experts who utilize advanced digital tools and techniques. The program includes two end-term examinations, and successful completion leads to a postgraduate certification.

## Post Graduate Programme in Cyber Security

Focused on safeguarding digital assets, this residential course addresses the core aspects of IT security. It covers methods to protect digital systems, applications, networks, and data from unauthorized access and cyber threats. The curriculum explores current challenges and emerging opportunities in securing both government and private sector digital infrastructure.

### Skills Covered:

*   Comprehensive Cyber Security knowledge

This 11-month program, offered by IIDT, provides specialized training to equip learners for investigating cyber security threats. The faculty from Gujarat Forensic Science University delivers the course content. Admission requires an entrance test, and assessments are based on term-end examinations. The program fee is ₹3,00,000.

## Post Graduate Programme in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

This advanced residential program, also 11 months in duration, helps learners grasp the fundamental concepts of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). It emphasizes their crucial roles in automation and their significant impact on the modern Information and Technology industry.

### Skills Covered:

*   Artificial Intelligence principles
*   Machine Learning methodologies
*   Data Science concepts
*   Python programming
*   Deep Learning techniques

Industry experts lead interactive online sessions, utilizing advanced tools and techniques. Student performance is evaluated through term-end examinations. The fee for this program is ₹3,00,000.

These programs represent a significant opportunity for individuals looking to build or enhance their careers in the dynamic fields of digital technology, guided by expert instruction and a robust curriculum.
