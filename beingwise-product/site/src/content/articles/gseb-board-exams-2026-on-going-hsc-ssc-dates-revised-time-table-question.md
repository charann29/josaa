# GSEB Board Exams 2026 (On-going): HSC, SSC Dates (Revised), Time Table, Question Papers, Syllabus, Pattern

The Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSEB) is currently conducting its Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC) board examinations for the 2026 academic year. Students and parents should stay informed about crucial dates, revised schedules, and result announcements.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Exam Period:** The GSEB SSC and HSC examinations for 2026 are being held from February 26, 2026, to March 18, 2026.
*   **Revised Schedule:** A specific exam originally slated for March 4, 2026, was rescheduled and conducted on March 18, 2026, reflecting a revision in the initial timetable.
*   **Admit Cards:** Hall tickets for both SSC and HSC exams were distributed to students through their respective schools in February 2026, following school login access to the official portal.
*   **Result Announcement:** The GSEB is expected to declare the results for both SSC and HSC examinations in May 2026 on its official website.

## About the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSEB)

The GSEB is the primary authority responsible for conducting Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC) board examinations annually in Gujarat. These examinations typically take place in March. The HSC examinations are bifurcated into a General Stream, which encompasses Arts and Commerce subjects, and a Science Stream. Exams for both streams are administered concurrently. For all official updates, students and parents should refer to the official GSEB website.

## Accessing Your Admit Card

The GSEB issued the admit cards for both SSC and HSC board exams in February 2026. These hall tickets were made available to schools through their official login on the GSEB portal. Schools were then responsible for distributing the physical admit cards to their students. This process also included the release of admit cards specifically for the HSC Science practical examinations.

## Understanding the GSEB Syllabus and Exam Pattern

To prepare effectively, students must be familiar with the official GSEB syllabus and examination pattern for 2026. The syllabus outlines the specific topics and units that will be covered in each subject, while the exam pattern details the structure of the question papers, including types of questions, marking schemes, and duration of the exams. Students are advised to refer to the official GSEB resources for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding the syllabus and pattern.

## Previous Year Question Papers and Preparation Tips

Practicing with previous year's question papers is an excellent way to prepare for the GSEB board exams. These papers help students understand the exam format, identify important topics, and improve time management. Additionally, consistent study, revision of key concepts, and maintaining a healthy routine are crucial for success. Students should focus on understanding concepts rather than rote learning and seek clarification from teachers for any doubts.
