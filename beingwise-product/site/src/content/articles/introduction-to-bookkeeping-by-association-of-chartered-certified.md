# Introduction to Bookkeeping by Association of Chartered Certified Accountants via Edx: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to begin a career in finance or simply understand the fundamentals of business transactions, the "Introduction to Bookkeeping" course offered by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) via Edx provides an excellent starting point. This program is designed to equip you with essential accounting knowledge, setting a strong foundation for future professional growth.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn the core principles of double-entry bookkeeping and common business transactions.
*   Understand the documentation and procedures involved in recording financial activities.
*   The course is self-paced, requiring 5-8 hours of study per week over six weeks.
*   No prior educational qualifications are needed, making it accessible for beginners.

## Course Overview

This introductory course delves into the fundamental concepts of bookkeeping, which is a crucial skill in the business world. You will gain a solid understanding of how various business transactions are processed and recorded, along with the relevant documentation. The curriculum is structured to provide practical insights into accounting proficiency, preparing you for more advanced studies or entry-level roles in the finance sector.

## What You Will Learn

The program focuses on developing your accounting skills and exposing you to key areas such as:

*   The principles of double-entry bookkeeping.
*   How to handle common business transactions.
*   Familiarity with different types of business transactions and their associated documentation.
*   Understanding banking processes and financial transactions.

## Eligibility and Certification

One of the significant advantages of this course is that it has no prior educational prerequisites, making it ideal for individuals from all backgrounds looking to step into the world of finance. While the course itself does not issue a direct certificate from Edx, participants have the option to pursue an official ACCA examination. Achieving a score of 60% or higher in this ACCA exam can lead to obtaining a professional certificate, validating your bookkeeping skills.

## Course Format and Duration

This is a six-week, self-paced online course. It's designed to be flexible, requiring approximately 5 to 8 hours of study per week. The learning materials are delivered through a combination of video lectures and text, supported by helpful handouts and discussion forums. The course content is primarily in English, with video transcripts also available in Spanish, French, and Chinese, enhancing accessibility for a diverse group of learners.

## How to Apply

Applying for the "Introduction to Bookkeeping" course is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official program page on the Edx platform.
2.  Click on the "Enroll Now" button.
3.  Log in to your existing Edx account or create a new one.
4.  You will then be given the option to either audit the course for a limited period (free access) or enroll in the verified track for unlimited access, which may involve a fee if you wish to pursue the ACCA examination for certification.

This course offers a fantastic opportunity to build a foundational understanding of bookkeeping, whether you're starting a new career or simply aiming to refresh your knowledge in the field of business and finance.
