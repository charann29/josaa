# Certified Professional Biller (CPB)® Medical Billing Training Course by AAPC: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those interested in a career in medical billing, the Certified Professional Biller (CPB)® training course from AAPC offers comprehensive preparation. This program equips individuals with the necessary knowledge and skills to manage the complex world of healthcare claims and patient billing.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course provides training for the Certified Professional Biller (CPB)® credential, a valuable certification in medical billing.
*   It is offered in a flexible, self-study or virtual classroom format, primarily delivered through video and text-based materials.
*   The curriculum covers essential topics from healthcare basics to advanced billing concepts, including different insurance types and claim processing.
*   Successful completion leads to a certification from AAPC, a well-recognized authority in medical coding and billing.

## Course Structure and Learning Modes

The CPB® training is designed for flexible learning, catering to different schedules and preferences. The instruction is entirely in English, making it accessible to a wide audience. You can choose between a self-study approach, where you learn at your own pace using provided materials, or a virtual classroom setting, which might offer more structured interaction. The course content is delivered through a combination of video lectures and text-based modules, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.

## What You Will Learn: The Syllabus

The curriculum for the Certified Professional Biller training is thorough, covering all critical aspects of medical billing. It starts with foundational knowledge and progresses to more specialized topics. Key areas of study include:

*   **Introduction to Healthcare:** Understanding the basic framework of the healthcare system.
*   **Health Insurance Models:** Exploring different types of health insurance plans.
*   **Patient Registration Process/Data Capture:** Learning how patient information is collected and managed.
*   **Introduction to Coding:** Gaining an understanding of ICD-10-CM, CPT®, and HCPCS Level II coding systems, which are vital for claims.
*   **Medical Necessity:** Determining when services are considered medically necessary for billing purposes.
*   **Claim Forms (CMS-1500 and UB-04):** Detailed instruction on completing standard billing forms.
*   **Billing and Collections:** Core concepts of medical billing, accounts receivable (A/R) management, and collection strategies.
*   **Government Carriers:** Specific billing rules for Medicare, Medicaid, and TRICARE.
*   **Private and Commercial Insurance:** Handling claims for Blue Cross/Blue Shield and other commercial insurance providers.
*   **Workers' Compensation:** Understanding the unique aspects of billing for work-related injuries.
*   **Final Exam Preparation:** The course culminates in preparation for the certification exam.

## Fees and Certification Details

The fee for the Certified Professional Biller Medical Billing Training course is structured to include both the training and the certification. While the original fee might be higher, there is often a discounted rate available. For specific pricing, it's always best to check the official AAPC portal, but generally, the course is offered at a reduced fee of approximately $1,495 (or Rs. 1495.00 if converted, though pricing is typically in USD), down from an original price of around $2,990 (or Rs. 2990.00). Upon successful completion of the course and passing the final exam, participants receive a certificate from AAPC, signifying their qualification as a Certified Professional Biller.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Certified Professional Biller (CPB)® Medical Billing Training Course, prospective students should visit the official AAPC website. The application process typically involves creating an account, selecting the desired course package, and completing the registration and payment steps. Details regarding course start dates, access to materials, and virtual classroom schedules will be provided during the enrollment process on the official portal.

Embarking on this training can open doors to a rewarding career in the essential field of medical billing, contributing significantly to the healthcare industry.
