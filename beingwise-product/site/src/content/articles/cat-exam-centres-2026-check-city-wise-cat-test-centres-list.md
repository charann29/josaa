# CAT Exam Centres 2026: Check City Wise CAT Test Centres List

When preparing for the CAT exam, knowing where you'll take the test is an important part of your planning. The Common Admission Test (CAT) 2026 will be held in various cities across India at specially designated examination centres. These centres are assigned based on your preferences indicated during the application process.

## Key takeaways

*   You can select up to five preferred cities for your CAT 2026 test centre during the application process.
*   The CAT exam is typically conducted in around 170 cities nationwide, with the 2026 exam expected to follow a similar pattern.
*   Your specific test centre and slot details will be revealed only after your admit card is released.
*   The exam is held in three distinct shifts throughout the day to accommodate all candidates.

## Understanding CAT Exam Centres

A CAT exam centre is essentially a computer laboratory equipped to host the online examination. These centres are prepared to ensure a smooth and fair testing environment for all candidates. To manage the large number of aspirants, typically over 2.5 lakh, the exam is conducted in three different shifts at each centre. This also allows for necessary checks and sanitisation between sessions.

## How to Select Your Preferred CAT Test City

When you fill out your CAT 2026 application form, you'll have the opportunity to indicate your preferred cities for the test centre. It's common practice for candidates to be allotted a centre in their first-choice city, although this is not always guaranteed. Here’s a simple guide to selecting your cities:

1.  **Register Online:** Begin by registering on the official IIMCAT website, iimcat.ac.in.
2.  **Access Application Form:** Once your login credentials are created, use them to access and fill out the application form.
3.  **Navigate to Test City Section:** Find the dedicated section for 'select test city' within the form.
4.  **Choose Your Cities:** From the provided dropdown menu, you can select up to five cities in order of your preference.
5.  **Complete and Submit:** After selecting your cities and filling in all other required details, make the payment for the exam fee and submit your application.
