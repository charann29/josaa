# NTA Answer Key 2026 - JEE Main, NEET, CMAT, UGC NET, NCHM, DUET

The National Testing Agency (NTA) plays a crucial role in India's higher education landscape, conducting numerous entrance examinations. For candidates aspiring to join various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, the NTA releases official answer keys for these exams, providing a transparent way to assess performance.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Provisional and Final Keys:** NTA typically releases a provisional answer key first, allowing candidates to review and challenge specific answers if they believe there's an error.
*   **Challenge Mechanism:** Students can raise objections to the provisional answer key, often with a nominal fee per question, which may be refunded if the challenge is found to be valid.
*   **Score Prediction:** The answer key enables candidates to estimate their probable scores even before the official results are declared.
*   **Accessibility:** All answer keys are published on the respective official NTA examination portals, accessible using candidate login credentials.

## Understanding the NTA Answer Key Process

After an entrance examination concludes, NTA makes available a provisional answer key. This key contains the correct responses to all questions asked in the exam. Candidates can use this to compare with their own marked answers. If a candidate identifies a discrepancy or believes an answer provided by NTA is incorrect, they are given a specific window to challenge it. These challenges usually require an online submission and, for some exams, a processing fee per question. NTA then reviews all submitted challenges. Based on these reviews, a final answer key is prepared and released, often alongside the exam results. This final key is considered definitive.

## JEE Main Answer Key

For engineering aspirants, NTA publishes the official JEE Main answer key on the dedicated JEE Main portal. This key is vital for students to cross-check their responses and get an early indication of their performance. Similar to other NTA exams, a provisional key is released first, followed by a period for challenges. Any valid objections are considered before the final answer key is made public.

## NEET Answer Key

Medical and dental college hopefuls rely on the NEET answer key, released by NTA following the conclusion of the examination. The provisional key allows candidates to scrutinize answers. If a candidate wishes to challenge a question, they typically need to pay a non-refundable fee per question online and provide supporting evidence. If a challenge is upheld, the fee for that specific question is usually refunded, and appropriate marks are awarded to all affected candidates. The final NEET answer key is then released, often concurrently with the results.

## CMAT, UGC NET, NCHM, and DUET Answer Keys

NTA also releases answer keys for other significant examinations it conducts:

*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test):** The provisional CMAT answer key is made available online, offering candidates a chance to challenge any perceived errors before the final key is published.
*   **UGC NET (University Grants Commission National Eligibility Test):** Candidates for UGC NET can access their provisional answer key on the official website. NTA also displays the recorded responses of candidates alongside the provisional key, facilitating a thorough review before results are declared.
*   **NCHM (National Council for Hotel Management Joint Entrance Examination):** For those seeking admission to hospitality programs, NTA releases the NCHM JEE answer key, following the standard provisional and final release pattern.
*   **DUET (Delhi University Entrance Test):** The provisional answer key for DUET (which covers various DU postgraduate admissions) is released by NTA after the exam. Students can use this to calculate their potential scores and raise objections to specific questions if necessary.

## How to Download the NTA Answer Key

Accessing the NTA answer key is a straightforward process. Candidates need to visit the specific official NTA portal for the examination they appeared for (e.g., jeemain.nta.nic.in for JEE Main). They will typically be required to log in using their application number and date of birth or password. Once logged in, the option to view or download the provisional answer key, along with their recorded responses, will be available. The answer keys are usually provided in a PDF format.

Staying informed about the NTA answer key releases is an important step in your post-exam journey, helping you to understand your performance and prepare for subsequent admission processes.
