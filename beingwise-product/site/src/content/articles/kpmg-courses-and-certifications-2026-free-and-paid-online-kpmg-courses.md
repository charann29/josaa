# KPMG Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online KPMG Courses

KPMG, a globally recognized professional services firm, offers a variety of online courses and certifications designed to help students and working professionals enhance their skills in high-demand areas like technology, data science, and finance. These programs often combine expert-led instruction with practical, real-world case studies.

## Key Takeaways

*   KPMG collaborates with various educational platforms to deliver specialized online programs.
*   Courses cover in-demand fields such as Technology Management, Data Science, and Corporate Finance.
*   Many programs incorporate hands-on projects and industry mentorship for practical learning.
*   Certification from KPMG in India is a significant credential for career advancement.

## Postgraduate Programme in Technology Management

This comprehensive 10-month hybrid learning program is designed for individuals looking to excel in technology management roles. It addresses the growing need for professionals who can bridge the gap between business strategy and technological implementation, whether it's deploying new software or redesigning digital platforms.

Students dedicate approximately 4-5 hours per week to both online and in-person sessions led by KPMG experts. The curriculum features simulated client scenarios and group projects, encouraging collaborative problem-solving. A key advantage of this program is the ability to specialize in either Data Science or Big Data and Cloud, allowing learners to tailor their expertise. The fee for this program is ₹3,39,000.

## Data Science Prodegree

For those interested in the intricate world of data, the Data Science Prodegree offers a deep dive into essential techniques and tools. This specialized 9-month course, developed in collaboration with KPMG, focuses on practical application through capstone projects and real-world case studies.

Participants are introduced to analytical tools such as R, Python, SQL, and Tableau, alongside foundational data science concepts. A significant benefit is the project mentorship provided by KPMG industry experts. Upon successful completion, learners receive a certificate. This program can be pursued on a part-time or full-time basis, with a fee of ₹58,000.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Programming skills
*   Machine learning fundamentals
*   Data science knowledge
*   Python and SQL proficiency
*   Data visualization techniques

## Certification Program in Corporate Finance & Analytics

This 6-month online program is specifically crafted for working professionals aiming to advance their careers in finance. It focuses on developing strategic finance and analytical competencies crucial for senior roles.

The curriculum, spread across seven modules, emphasizes using analytics for long-term strategic planning and understanding the connection between strategy and financial outcomes. Participants gain critical behavioral skills and hands-on training with modern technologies and tools through over 100 hours of interactive learning. Completing this program earns participants a certification from KPMG in India, a respected leader in the finance sector. The program fee is ₹96,000.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Financial Management
*   Strategic Analytics
*   Decision-making
*   Corporate Finance principles

## Future Leader Programme

Designed to equip professionals for leadership in today's dynamic global environment, the Future Leader Programme combines academic rigor with practical experience. This program helps participants cultivate essential leadership qualities.

Key areas of development include executive presence, effective people management, strategic thinking, financial and business acumen, and the art of storytelling through data. This certification is offered in association with Deakin University and KPMG in India, providing a robust foundation for aspiring leaders.

These programs offer valuable opportunities to gain specialized knowledge and industry-recognized certifications, helping individuals build a strong foundation for their professional journeys.
