# KIITEE MBA Syllabus 2026: Check Section-wise Syllabus Here

Preparing for the KIITEE MBA entrance exam requires a clear understanding of the syllabus. This guide outlines the key topics you need to cover for the 2026 examination, as prescribed by KSOM.

## Key takeaways

*   The KIITEE MBA exam evaluates candidates across five distinct sections.
*   Beyond traditional subjects, there's a unique section on 'Issues of Social Concern'.
*   Quantitative Aptitude and Analytical Reasoning carry the highest weightage in terms of questions.
*   Thorough preparation across all listed topics is essential for success.

## KIITEE MBA Syllabus 2026: Section-Wise Weightage

The KIITEE MBA question paper for 2026 is structured into five core areas. Understanding the distribution of questions across these sections can help you prioritize your study plan. The sections are Quantitative Aptitude, Analytical Reasoning, English, General Knowledge, and Issues of Social Concern. Here’s a breakdown of the question count for each:

| KIITEE Management Sections | Number of Questions |
| :------------------------- | :------------------ |
| Quantitative Aptitude      | 40                  |
| Analytical Reasoning       | 40                  |
| English                    | 30                  |
| General Knowledge          | 20                  |
| Issue of Social Concern    | 20                  |

## KIITEE MBA Syllabus: Quantitative Aptitude

This section assesses your numerical ability and problem-solving skills. It covers a broad range of mathematical concepts typically encountered in management entrance exams. Important topics include:

*   Mathematics Fundamentals (Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry)
*   Arithmetic (Percentages, Profit and Loss, Simple & Compound Interest)
*   Time, Speed, and Distance
*   Permutation & Combination
*   Probability
*   Logarithm
*   Mensuration

## KIITEE MBA Syllabus: Analytical Reasoning

The Analytical Reasoning section tests your logical thinking and ability to interpret data and patterns. Key areas to focus on include:

*   Arrangements (Linear, Circular, etc.)
*   Blood Relations
*   Coding-Decoding
*   Clocks and Calendars
*   Data Structures (basic understanding)
*   Direction Sense
*   Puzzles
*   Series (Number, Alphabet, Mixed)

## KIITEE MBA Syllabus: English

Proficiency in English is crucial for MBA programs. This section evaluates your verbal ability, comprehension, and grammar. The topics typically covered are:

*   Reading Comprehension
*   Vocabulary (Antonyms/Synonyms, Foreign words used in English, One Word Substitution)
*   Grammar (Sentence Correction, Fill in the Blanks, Contextual Usage)
*   Verbal Reasoning (Syllogisms, Analogies)
*   Paragraph completion and inference (Jumbled Paragraphs, Para Completion)
*   Idioms/Phrases and Different Usage of the Same Word

## KIITEE MBA Syllabus: General Knowledge and Issues of Social Concern

These two sections gauge your awareness of current affairs, static general knowledge, and your perspective on societal issues. While General Knowledge covers a wide array of topics, 'Issues of Social Concern' requires specific preparation to understand contemporary social challenges and their implications. Candidates should stay updated with national and international news, economic trends, and significant social developments.

By thoroughly preparing for each of these sections, you can significantly enhance your chances of performing well in the KIITEE MBA 2026 examination.
