# Certified Internet of Things Security Professional by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to understand and secure the rapidly expanding world of connected devices, the Certified Internet of Things (IoT) Security Professional course from Vskills offers a valuable pathway. This program helps individuals build expertise in safeguarding IoT systems against various threats.

## Key takeaways

*   The course focuses on practical IoT security knowledge, supported by case studies.
*   It is a self-paced online program, accessible through video and text-based modules.
*   Successful completion leads to a certification from Vskills, enhancing career prospects.
*   The total fee, including GST, is around ₹3,499.

## Course Overview

This certification program is designed to equip participants with a thorough understanding of the Internet of Things, particularly its security aspects. It delves into the core concepts of IoT systems, exploring the hardware and software components involved, and how to apply security principles effectively. The curriculum uses real-world case studies to illustrate technologies and their security architectures, helping learners grasp complex ideas through practical examples.

Whether you are just starting your career in technology or are an experienced professional seeking to specialize, this course aims to provide a solid foundation. For beginners, it can serve as an excellent entry point into the IoT security domain or as a prerequisite for more advanced studies. Experienced individuals can deepen their insights and gain confidence in tackling real-world security challenges.

## Program Structure and Learning Experience

The Certified Internet of Things Security Professional course is delivered entirely online, allowing for flexible self-study. Participants gain lifetime access to e-learning modules, which include both video and text-based content. To aid in preparation, Vskills provides sample questions and study materials. While there are no live online classes, the comprehensive resources are designed to support independent learning.

### What You Will Learn

Through this program, you will gain:

*   In-depth knowledge of IoT systems and their operational principles.
*   Understanding of various technologies and security architectures through practical case studies.
*   Skills to apply learned concepts to real-world IoT security problems.
*   Familiarity with the hardware and software components critical to IoT security.

## Eligibility and Certification

There are no specific academic prerequisites for enrolling in this course. To earn the certification, candidates must successfully pass an online examination conducted by Vskills. This exam consists of 50 marks, and a minimum score of 25 marks is required to obtain the certification. The flexibility of the online exam allows participants to choose their preferred date, time, and location for taking it.

## Fees and Application Process

The base fee for the Certified Internet of Things Security Professional course is ₹3,499. Please note that Goods and Services Tax (GST) will be applied at the time of checkout, bringing the total payable amount to slightly higher than the base fee.

### How to Apply

Applying for the course is a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official Vskills course page for the Certified Internet of Things Security Professional program.
2.  Look for and click on the 'BUY NOW' option, which will direct you to the login page.
3.  If you are an existing Vskills user, log in with your credentials. New users will need to register for an account.
4.  After logging in or registering, you will be prompted to select your preferred payment method and complete the transaction.

## Career Advancement

Upon successful completion and certification, individuals can leverage this credential to stand out in the competitive job market. Vskills provides access to job portals like Shine and Monster, enhancing profile visibility for certified professionals. This course is beneficial for students seeking foundational knowledge, existing employees aiming to enhance their skill sets, and anyone looking for career advancement in the rapidly growing field of IoT security.
