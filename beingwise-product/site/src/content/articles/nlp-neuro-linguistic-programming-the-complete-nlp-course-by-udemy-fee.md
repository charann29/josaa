## What is Neuro-Linguistic Programming?

NLP is essentially a practical approach to understanding how people organize their thinking, feeling, language, and behavior to produce the results they do. It's about breaking down the structure of human experience and then learning how to replicate excellence. The course delves into core NLP concepts, providing a foundation for its practical application in various aspects of life.
