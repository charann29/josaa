# Top B Sc in Computer Science colleges in India 2026: Admissions 2026, Fees, Courses, Placements, Cut Off

A Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Computer Science is a popular three-year undergraduate program in India, designed to give students a strong foundation in computer systems, software development, algorithms, and emerging technologies. This page provides essential information for prospective students eyeing admissions in 2026.

## Key takeaways

*   India offers over 2,400 colleges for BSc Computer Science, with a significant number being private institutions.
*   The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) is a primary entrance examination for admission to many top BSc Computer Science programs.
*   Eligibility typically requires a 10+2 qualification with Science subjects, often including Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, or Computer Science.
*   Prominent institutions offering BSc Computer Science include Jamia Millia Islamia, Delhi University, and VIT Vellore.

## Admissions 2026: Eligibility and Entrance Exams

To be considered for a BSc in Computer Science program in 2026, candidates generally need to meet specific academic criteria. Most institutions require applicants to have successfully completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.

Crucially, students must have studied Science subjects at the 10+2 level, with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics often being mandatory. Some colleges also accept candidates who have studied Computer Science at the intermediate level.

Admission to many leading universities is based on performance in entrance examinations. The CUET, administered by the National Testing Agency (NTA), has become a crucial gateway for undergraduate admissions across numerous central and participating universities. Candidates should regularly check the official NTA website for CUET 2026 application and examination schedules.

## Top Institutions for BSc Computer Science

India boasts a wide array of colleges offering BSc Computer Science programs. These institutions vary in their ownership, with over 1,200 private colleges, more than 400 government-owned institutions, and over 300 semi-government colleges. Some of the highly regarded institutions include:

*   Jamia Millia Islamia
*   Delhi University
*   VIT Vellore
*   UNIPUNE (University of Pune)
*   Aligarh Muslim University
*   Chandigarh University
*   Woxsen University
*   Anna University
*   Lovely Professional University
*   SAGE University

These universities are known for their academic rigor, infrastructure, and placement opportunities. Prospective students should research individual college websites for specific course offerings and admission criteria.

## Placements and Career Prospects

A BSc in Computer Science opens doors to various career paths in the rapidly evolving technology sector. Graduates are well-prepared for roles in software development, web development, database administration, system analysis, and IT support, among others. Many colleges have dedicated placement cells that facilitate internships and job opportunities for their students. While specific placement statistics vary by institution, a strong foundation in computer science principles is highly valued by employers across industries. Top recruiters often include IT services companies, tech startups, and organizations requiring in-house software expertise.

Choosing the right BSc Computer Science college is a significant decision that can shape your academic and professional future. We encourage you to explore your options thoroughly and prepare diligently for the admission process.
