# Post Graduate Programme in Data Science by International Institute of Digital Technologies: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

The International Institute of Digital Technologies (IIDT), established under the Andhra Pradesh Electronics & IT Agency (APEITA), offers a Post Graduate Programme in Data Science. This residential program aims to equip students with essential data analytics skills, preparing them for the rapidly evolving IT industry.

## Key takeaways

*   This is an 11-month, residential Post Graduate Programme in Data Science.
*   The program focuses on practical learning with real-life projects and mentorship from global experts.
*   Eligibility includes specific undergraduate and postgraduate degrees with minimum marks, and an age limit of 20-30 years.
*   The total course fee is Rs. 3,00,000, with additional charges for optional hostel facilities.

## Programme Overview

The Post Graduate Programme in Data Science at IIDT is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge in data analytics. The curriculum emphasizes the strategic importance of data science in business growth, informed decision-making, and developing competitive strategies within the IT sector. The institute prides itself on offering state-of-the-art facilities and leveraging modern digital tools for instruction.

Classes are delivered by industry experts, utilizing advanced digital techniques to ensure a hands-on learning experience. The program culminates in two end-term examinations, and successful candidates receive a post-graduation certification upon completion.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the Post Graduate Programme in Data Science, applicants must meet specific academic and age requirements. The age limit for applicants is between 20 and 30 years.

Academic qualifications include:

*   An M.E. / M.Tech. degree in any discipline, or a B.E. / B.Tech. degree in any discipline with a minimum of 60% aggregate marks from a recognized university.
*   An MBA / MCA / M.Com. / M.Sc. degree with a minimum of 65% aggregate marks from a recognized university. The undergraduate degree must have included Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, Accountancy, Mathematics, Statistics, Biotechnology, or allied science and technology disciplines as one of the subjects.
*   A BBA / BCA / B.Sc. / B.Com. / BAF degree or an equivalent degree from a recognized university with a minimum of 70% aggregate marks. Similar to the postgraduate requirement, the undergraduate degree must have included Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, Accountancy, Mathematics, Statistics, Biotechnology, or allied science and technology disciplines as one of the subjects.

Additionally, there should not be a gap of more than one year after completing a bachelor's or master's degree. Working professionals with any of the degrees mentioned above and a minimum of 60% aggregate marks are also eligible to apply. Candidates who have qualified in GATE, GMAT, CAT, or GRE with a valid score may receive a relaxation from the written examination component of the application process. A strong foundation in programming and coding skills is also a mandatory prerequisite.

## Fees and Payment Information

The tuition fee for the Post Graduate Programme in Data Science is structured across two terms. The application fee is Rs. 1,000, which is non-refundable unless there is a specific case of repeated credit. The commitment fee is also non-refundable.

### Programme Fee Structure

| Particular            | Amount       |
| :-------------------- | :----------- |
| First-term course fee | Rs. 1,50,000 |
| Second-term course fee| Rs. 1,50,000 |

For students opting for hostel accommodation, an additional charge of Rs. 87,500 applies for each term. Payment can be made through both online and offline channels. Offline payments can be processed at any branch of Andhra Bank. Educational loan facilities may also be available to assist students with fee payments.

## How to Apply

Prospective students can initiate their application by submitting the required application fee. Further details regarding the application process, including submission of necessary documents and the selection procedure, can be found on the official IIDT portal. Candidates should ensure they meet all eligibility criteria before applying.

This program offers a robust foundation for a career in data science, combining theoretical knowledge with practical application under expert guidance.
