## Certified Financial Planner (CFP) Training Program

The CFPCM Certification Training Program from IMS Proschool is a leading qualification in the financial planning sector, a field known for its excellent career prospects. This program is structured to prepare candidates for the CFPCM exam and other related financial planning certifications.

Students can choose from several learning modes to suit their schedules and preferences. These include traditional classroom programs, live virtual classrooms, and self-paced study options. Weekend batches are available for classroom programs, making it convenient for those balancing studies with other commitments. The program typically lasts 15 months and has a fee of ₹60,000.

### Examination and Certification

The training includes a computer-based examination featuring multiple-choice questions and case studies. Successful completion of this exam allows candidates to apply for the official CFPCM certification, maximizing the benefits of the program. Key skills covered include financial management.

## ACCA Course: Skills Level

IMS Proschool offers the ACCA Course: Skills Level certification, an advanced program focusing on a broad spectrum of business and accounting principles. This course is designed to equip participants with essential skills vital for a career in accounting and finance.

Training is delivered by educators qualified by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA). Students can opt for an offline classroom program or live online training, which is conducted on a professional virtual platform. The learning experience includes recorded video lectures, assignments, mock tests, and interactive live classes, with faculty feedback provided to aid performance improvement. This program spans 15 months and costs ₹1,25,000.

### Syllabus and Diploma

The ACCA Skills Level syllabus is divided into six distinct modules, covering topics such as Financial Management, Audit and Assurance, Taxation, and Financial Reporting. Upon meeting specific criteria, participants can also earn an advanced diploma in accounting and business. Skills emphasized include decision-making, financial management, and taxation knowledge.

## Certificate in Financial Planning

For those looking to begin a career in finance, especially in investment advisory and management, the Certificate in Financial Planning by IMS Proschool is an excellent starting point. This curriculum is based on the CFP-FBSP framework, providing a solid foundation in financial planning from basic concepts through high-quality video sessions. This course is 15 months long with a fee of ₹50,000.

This program is suitable for undergraduates, graduates, and working professionals alike. It also prepares students for other industry benchmark certificates and includes placement support to help graduates secure employment opportunities. Key skills gained include knowledge of financial law and financial management.

## Investment Banking Course with PG Certificate

The PGP in Investment Banking & Capital Markets is a program designed to impart the necessary skills and knowledge for a professional role in investment finance. It emphasizes project-based learning, offering practical experience that mirrors real-world scenarios.
