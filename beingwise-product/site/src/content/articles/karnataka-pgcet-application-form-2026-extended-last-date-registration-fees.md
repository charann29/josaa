## How to Apply for Karnataka PGCET 2026
Filling out the Karnataka PGCET application form is a multi-step online process. Candidates need to visit the official Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) portal, `cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea/pgcet2026`, to initiate their application. The process typically involves registration, filling in personal and academic details, uploading required documents, and finally, paying the application fee.

### Step-by-Step Application Guide:
1.  **Online Registration:** Visit the official KEA PGCET 2026 website and complete the initial registration to generate login credentials.
2.  **Fill Application Form:** Log in using your credentials and accurately fill in all personal, academic, and communication details as requested.
3.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and left thumb impression in the specified format and size.
4.  **Pay Application Fee:** Proceed to pay the application fee online through the available payment gateways.
5.  **Print Confirmation Page:** After successful submission and payment, download and print the confirmation page for your records.

## Documents and Information Required
Before starting the application process, it is highly recommended to gather all necessary documents and information. Having these ready will make the online form filling much quicker and error-free.

*   Class 10th and 12th mark sheets
*   Graduation degree details and mark sheets
*   Scanned passport-sized photograph
*   Scanned signature
*   Scanned left thumb impression
*   Valid government-approved ID proof
*   Active email ID and mobile number
*   Category certificates (if applicable)
*   Bank details for fee payment
*   Preferred examination city choices

Ensure you have a stable internet connection and access to a printer for the confirmation page. The KEA recommends using Internet Explorer 9 or above, or the latest versions of Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox for optimal viewing of the application portal.

## Eligibility Criteria Overview
To be eligible for Karnataka PGCET 2026, candidates must meet specific criteria set by KEA. Generally, a candidate must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. Specific eligibility conditions, such as minimum aggregate marks, may vary depending on the chosen postgraduate course (e.g., MBA, MCA, M.E., M.Tech, M.Arch). It is crucial to review the detailed eligibility criteria available on the official KEA portal before applying.
