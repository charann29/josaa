# KIITEE MBA Result 2026: Release Date, Link & Scorecard Download Process

The KIITEE MBA 2026 results have been officially announced, marking a crucial step for aspiring management students. This page will guide you through accessing your scorecard and understanding the subsequent admission process for the MBA program at KIIT School of Management (KSOM).

## Key takeaways

*   The KIITEE MBA 2026 results were released on January 28, 2026, following exams held on January 16, 17, and 18, 2026.
*   Candidates can download their scorecards directly from the official KSOM website using their login credentials.
*   The next stage involves shortlisting candidates for the Writing Ability Test (WAT), Group Discussion (GD), and Personal Interview (PI) rounds.
*   Final admission to the KSOM MBA program will be based on a comprehensive evaluation of academic record, entrance test performance, and the WAT/GD/PI rounds.

## How to Download Your KIITEE MBA 2026 Result

Accessing your KIITEE Management result is a straightforward online process. No physical scorecards will be dispatched. Follow these steps to download your result:

1.  Visit the official website of KIIT School of Management (KSOM).
2.  Locate and click on the 'Download KIITEE MBA 2026 Result' tab.
3.  You will be prompted to enter your unique index number.
4.  Next, input your date of birth.
5.  Click on the 'Login' button to view and download your scorecard.

It is essential to download and print a copy of your scorecard for future reference and the admission process.

## Details on Your KIITEE MBA Scorecard

Your KIITEE MBA 2026 scorecard is a critical document containing important personal and performance details. It will typically include:

*   Your full name
*   Your category
*   Your gender
*   Your date of birth
*   The date and time you appeared for the exam
*   Your overall score and section-wise scores

Candidates should carefully review all details on the scorecard. If you identify any factual errors in your personal information, it's advisable to contact KSOM for rectification. Please note that changes to exam scores are generally not permitted.

## What Happens After the KIITEE MBA Results?

Upon the release of KIITEE MBA results, KSOM will publish a merit list of candidates who are shortlisted for the next stages of the admission process: the Group Discussion (GD), Writing Ability Test (WAT), and Personal Interview (PI) rounds. Only those candidates who make it to this merit list will receive an invitation to participate.

The GD, WAT, and PI rounds are typically conducted at the KIIT-SOM campus, and candidates are required to attend in person. The specific schedule and venue for these rounds will be communicated to selected candidates via email, likely around April 2026, after the application process for non-KIITEE routes is completed.

Final admission to the two-year MBA program at KSOM is granted based on a holistic assessment. This includes your academic performance, your scores in the entrance examination, and your performance in the GD, WAT, and PI rounds. Successful candidates will receive an official admission offer letter. To secure their seat, selected candidates must formally accept the offer within the specified deadline. Failure to do so may result in the cancellation of the admission offer.

The total course fee for the MBA program is approximately INR 17.90 lakh, payable in five installments.

## Documents Required for KSOM MBA Admission

After receiving a final admission offer, candidates will be required to submit a set of essential documents to complete their enrollment. While the exact list will be provided by KSOM, typically these include academic transcripts, entrance exam scorecards, identity proof, and other relevant certificates.
