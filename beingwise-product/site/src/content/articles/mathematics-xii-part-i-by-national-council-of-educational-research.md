# Mathematics XII Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For senior secondary students looking to strengthen their understanding of Class 12 Mathematics, the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) offers a comprehensive online course. Delivered through the Swayam platform, this program covers essential mathematical concepts in an accessible, self-paced format.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 24-week, self-study online course designed for Class 12 students.
*   Learning is facilitated through video tutorials, e-text, and web resources, all in English.
*   Successful completion of assignments and a final assessment leads to a certificate from NCERT and Swayam.
*   The course is structured week-wise, including self-assessments and discussion forums for interactive learning.

## Course Overview and Structure

This online course focuses on the first part of the Class 12 Mathematics syllabus. It's coordinated by Professor T. P. Sarma from DESM, NCERT, New Delhi, ensuring expert-led content. The program aims to provide an interactive learning experience, helping students build both knowledge and practical skills in mathematics.

The course content is delivered primarily through video tutorials and accompanying web resources. It follows a week-by-week schedule, integrating self-assessments to help students gauge their progress and reinforce their understanding of key topics. There are also discussion forums available, allowing students to interact with peers and clarify doubts.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to cover fundamental mathematical concepts crucial for Class 12. Upon completing the course, participants will have a solid grasp of the following areas:

*   Relations and Functions
*   Inverse Trigonometric Functions
*   Matrices
*   Determinants
*   Continuity and Differentiability
*   Applications of Derivatives

Each of these broad topics is broken down into specific sub-topics and includes dedicated assignments to ensure thorough learning and practice.

## Eligibility and Certification

This course is specifically tailored for senior secondary students who wish to learn or revise their Class 12 Mathematics. There are no specific academic prerequisites to enroll, making it accessible to a wide audience.

To earn the course certificate, students must successfully complete all self-assessments and achieve a minimum score of 70% in the final assessment. The certificate is jointly issued by NCERT and Swayam, validating the successful completion of the program.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Mathematics XII Part-I Certification Course on Swayam is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam platform for the course.
2.  Look for the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, usually located in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the new page that appears, you can either sign in with an existing account or create a new one to proceed with your enrollment.

This course offers a valuable opportunity for Class 12 students to enhance their mathematical skills and gain a recognized certificate from NCERT and Swayam, all from the comfort of their home.
