# MPBSE Exam Class 12 2026: Result (Soon), 2nd Main Time Table & Dates (Out), Admit Card, Question Papers, Syllabus, Pattern

The Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (MPBSE) is a key body overseeing secondary education in the state. For students aiming for higher education, understanding the Class 12 exams is crucial. This page provides essential information for the 2026 MPBSE Class 12 examinations, covering results, exam dates, and preparation resources.

## Key takeaways

*   The MPBSE Class 12 results for 2026 are expected by April 15, 2026, with an official announcement of the exact date coming soon.
*   The second main examination phase for Class 12 will take place from May 7 to May 25, 2026.
*   Practical exams for the second main session are scheduled from May 5 to May 25, 2026.
*   Students require a minimum of 30% marks to pass the MPBSE Class 12 examinations.

## MPBSE Class 12 Result 2026

Students eagerly awaiting their Class 12 results can expect them around April 15, 2026. Board officials have indicated that the result processing is nearing completion. The precise date and time for the result declaration will be announced through a press conference. Following this, the results will be accessible online on the official MPBSE portals: mpresults.nic.in, mpbse.mponline.gov.in, and mponline.gov.in. Students will need their roll numbers to check their scores.

### Pass Percentage

To successfully clear the MPBSE Class 12 exams, students must secure at least 30% marks in each subject. In the previous year, the overall pass percentage for Class 12 stood at 72.72%.

## MPBSE Class 12 Exam Dates 2026

The schedule for the MPBSE Class 12 exams is vital for student planning. While the main examinations have concluded, the dates for the second main examination phase have been released. Additionally, information regarding compartment exams and supplementary results is provided below.

### Second Main Exam Schedule

The second main examinations for MPBSE Class 12 in 2026 will be held from May 7 to May 25. Practical examinations for this phase are scheduled between May 5 and May 25.

## MPBSE Class 12 Admit Card 2026

The admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the MPBSE Class 12 examinations. It contains crucial details such as the student's name, roll number, examination center, and subject schedule. Typically, the admit cards are released approximately a month before the exams. Students can download their admit cards from the official MPBSE website using their application number and other required credentials once released.

## MPBSE Class 12 Syllabus and Exam Pattern 2026

Understanding the syllabus and exam pattern is fundamental for effective preparation. The MPBSE Class 12 syllabus outlines the topics and units to be covered for each subject, ensuring students focus on relevant content. The exam pattern details the structure of the question paper, including types of questions, marking scheme, and duration.

Students are advised to refer to the official MPBSE syllabus for the 2025-26 academic year. The examination is conducted offline, with a duration of three hours for most papers. Familiarity with the pattern helps students manage their time efficiently during the exam.

## MPBSE Class 12 Question Papers 2026

Practicing with previous years' question papers is an excellent way to prepare for the MPBSE Class 12 exams. These papers offer insights into the types of questions asked, the difficulty level, and the distribution of marks across different topics. Solving them under timed conditions can also improve speed and accuracy, helping students simulate the actual exam environment. Official question papers are often available on the MPBSE website.

Preparing thoroughly for the MPBSE Class 12 exams is a significant step towards achieving your academic goals and pursuing higher education. Stay updated with official announcements from the Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education for the latest information.
