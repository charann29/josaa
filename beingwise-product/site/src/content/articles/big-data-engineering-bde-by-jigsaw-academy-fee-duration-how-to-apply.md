# Big Data Engineering (BDE) by Jigsaw Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Big Data Engineering (BDE) is a field experiencing rapid growth, not just in technology but also in its crucial role within business strategy. As data technologies continue to advance, professionals need specialized knowledge to manage and leverage vast amounts of information effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   This online, self-paced program from Jigsaw Academy focuses on building expertise in handling large datasets and automating data pipelines.
*   The course runs for three months, with participants getting six months of access to the learning materials.
*   A basic understanding of programming is a prerequisite for enrollment.
*   Upon successful completion and passing a certification test, participants receive a certificate from Jigsaw Academy.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

The Big Data Engineering program by Jigsaw Academy is designed to enhance data skills, focusing on practical applications like automating data pipelines, building robust databases, and developing data models. Through a blend of video and text-based modules, alongside instructor-led webinars and case studies, learners gain hands-on experience. The program is delivered entirely online, allowing for a flexible, self-study approach, and even offers mobile app access for convenience.

## What You Will Learn

This certification course aims to equip participants with a deep understanding of big data concepts and practical skills. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Mastering big data engineering principles for analytics.
*   Developing the ability to construct data warehouses and models.
*   Skills in automating complex data pipelines.
*   Expertise in managing and processing massive datasets effectively.
*   Transforming existing technical skills into productive big data capabilities.

## Eligibility and Certification

To enroll in the Big Data Engineering program, candidates should possess a foundational knowledge of programming. There are no specific educational degree requirements mentioned beyond this programming background.

Upon completing the course content, participants must take and successfully pass a certification test. The official certificate from Jigsaw Academy is typically awarded within one month of clearing this assessment.

## Program Fees

The total fee for the Big Data Engineering online course is ₹28,320. This amount includes both the program fee and applicable taxes. Here’s a breakdown:

| Particulars       | Amount (INR) |
| :---------------- | :----------- |
| Program Fees      | ₹24,000      |
| Taxes             | ₹4,320       |
| **Total Fees**    | **₹28,320**  |

## How to Apply

Applying for the Big Data Engineering program involves a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official Jigsaw Academy website for the Big Data Engineering course.
2.  Locate and click the “Enroll Now” button, which will direct you to the checkout page.
3.  If you have a coupon code, enter it in the designated box and click “APPLY” before proceeding to “CHECKOUT.”
4.  You will then be prompted to log in using your Facebook, Google, or LinkedIn credentials for verification.
5.  Proceed to the payment page to complete your transaction using your preferred payment method.
6.  After successful payment, it is recommended to email your transaction slip to the concerned department for confirmation of your enrollment.

This program offers a valuable opportunity for professionals looking to specialize in the rapidly evolving field of big data engineering.
