# Data Analytics Program by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and recent graduates looking to enter the dynamic field of data analytics, the Data Analytics Bootcamp program offered by Great Learning Career Academy provides a structured pathway. This program aims to equip participants with essential industry-relevant skills, making them job-ready for data-driven roles.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a four-month intensive program focusing on core data analytics skills.
*   It offers flexibility with both full-time classroom options in major cities and live online classes.
*   The program emphasizes career readiness, including guaranteed placement opportunities.
*   The total fee for the certification is ₹97,000 plus GST, with EMI options available.

## Program Overview and Structure

The Data Analytics Bootcamp is a comprehensive four-month program designed to build foundational and advanced skills necessary for a career in data analytics. It covers a range of topics from data wrangling to machine learning basics, ensuring participants gain practical expertise. The learning is delivered through a blend of virtual classrooms and self-study materials, including video and text-based content.

For those who prefer in-person learning, classroom facilities are available in several cities, including Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Gurugram, and Pune. This hybrid approach allows students to choose the learning mode that best suits their needs and location.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is meticulously designed to cover key areas crucial for a data analytics professional. Here’s a breakdown of the core modules:

### Basics of Excel

*   **Data Wrangling:** Techniques for cleaning and transforming raw data.
*   **Basic Visualizations:** Creating meaningful charts and graphs.
*   **Data Problem Solving:** Applying Excel functions to solve business problems.

### SQL Programming

*   **Relational Databases:** Introduction to database concepts.
*   **SQL Commands:** Mastering statements, clauses, operators, and functions.
*   **Advanced SQL:** Joins, subqueries, temporary tables, views, and normalization.

### Python Programming

*   **Foundations:** Basic Python syntax and data structures.
*   **Pandas:** Data manipulation and analysis library.
*   **Visualization:** Creating insightful plots and graphs.
*   **Exploratory Data Analysis:** Techniques to understand data patterns.
*   **Introduction to Machine Learning:** Basic concepts and algorithms.

### Tableau

*   **Data Connection:** Importing datasets and connecting to data sources.
*   **Measures and Dimensions:** Understanding key Tableau concepts.
*   **Chart Types:** Creating various visualizations like bar, line, and map charts.
*   **Dashboards and Storytelling:** Building interactive dashboards and presenting insights.
*   **Blends and Actions:** Advanced Tableau functionalities.

## Eligibility Criteria and Application Process

This program is specifically tailored for recent graduates and students in their final semester who are eager to secure employment in reputable companies or enhance their existing data analytics skill sets. The course is ideal for aspiring data analysts and data scientists.

To enroll in the Data Analytics Program, interested candidates should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official program page.
2.  Register on the Great Learning Career Academy website.
3.  Consult with the admissions team and complete either a center-based aptitude test or a video interview with a faculty panel.
4.  Upon selection, candidates will receive an offer letter and can secure their seat by paying the admission fees.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Data Analytics Program certification is ₹97,000, in addition to Goods and Services Tax (GST). For convenience, monthly EMI options are available, starting at approximately ₹4,918 per month.

Upon successful completion of the program, participants receive a certificate of accomplishment directly from Great Learning, validating their acquired skills and knowledge in data analytics. The program also highlights significant career support, including connections to over 2,500 hiring companies and a guarantee of seven placement opportunities with leading organizations.

This program offers a robust foundation and career launchpad for individuals aiming to thrive in the data analytics industry.
