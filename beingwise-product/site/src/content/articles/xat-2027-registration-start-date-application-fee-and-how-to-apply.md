# XAT 2027 Registration: Start Date, Application Fee & How to Apply @xatonline.in

For those aiming for MBA/PGDM programs at XLRI Jamshedpur, other XAMI member institutions, and over 250 other business schools across India, the XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test) is a crucial step. The registration window for XAT 2027 is expected to open in July, allowing eligible candidates to apply online via the official XAT portal, xatonline.in.

## Key Takeaways

*   XAT 2027 registration is anticipated to begin in July, with the main exam scheduled for January 3, 2027.
*   A bachelor's degree from a recognized university is the fundamental eligibility criterion for all applicants.
*   The application fee for XAT 2027 is INR 2,400. An additional INR 200 is required if you wish to apply specifically for XLRI programs.
*   The application process involves a one-time registration, filling out personal, academic, and work experience details, uploading necessary documents, and paying the fee.

## Understanding the XAT 2027 Application Process

The XAT application is an entirely online process, designed to be straightforward. Candidates must ensure they complete all steps accurately before the final submission deadline. The process begins with initial registration and culminates in fee payment.

### Step-by-Step Registration Guide

To successfully register for XAT 2027, follow these steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Go to xatonline.in.
2.  **Initiate Registration:** Click on the 'Registration' option.
3.  **Provide Basic Information:** Enter your full name, active phone number, email ID, create a password, and input the captcha code as displayed.
4.  **Receive Credentials:** A unique XAT ID and a verification link will be sent to your registered email address. Use these to proceed.
5.  **Access Application Form:** Once verified, you will be able to access the XAT 2027 application form.
6.  **Fill Details:** Accurately enter your academic qualifications, personal information, and any relevant work experience.
7.  **Upload Documents:** Upload a recent passport-sized photograph and your scanned signature. Ensure these meet the specified size requirements (usually under 1 MB).
8.  **Select Test Cities:** Choose your preferred cities for the examination center.
9.  **Review and Declare:** Carefully review all entered details for accuracy and check the declaration box.
10. **Pay Application Fee:** Proceed to pay the XAT examination fee online.

## XAT 2027 Application Fee Structure

The application fee for XAT 2027 must be paid online. It's important to note the additional fee for those specifically interested in XLRI programs.

| Category                                  | XAT Exam Fees                                                 |
| :---------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------ |
| All Candidates (Unreserved & Reserved)    | INR 2,400                                                     |
| Additional Fee for XLRI Programs Interest | INR 200 (Applicable if you select XLRI programs in your application)|

## Essential Documents and Information for Registration

While you won't need to upload all these documents, having them readily available ensures you can accurately fill out the online form. Only your photograph and signature need to be uploaded.

*   Class 10th Marksheet (for academic details)
*   Class 12th Marksheet (for academic details)
*   Graduation Marksheets (for academic details)
*   A valid Email ID and Mobile Phone Number
*   Proof of Date of Birth
*   Address Proof
*   Aadhaar Card or other valid Photo ID
*   Category Certificate (if applicable, for reserved category candidates)
*   Scanned digital photograph (under 1 MB)
*   Scanned signature (under 1 MB)
*   Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking details for online fee payment

Ensure all details are entered correctly and documents are prepared according to the specifications to ensure a smooth application process. Starting early allows ample time to gather information and address any potential issues.
