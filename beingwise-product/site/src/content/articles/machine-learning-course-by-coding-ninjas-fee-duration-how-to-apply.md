# Machine Learning Course by Coding Ninjas: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This article explores a popular online certification program designed to introduce students to the exciting field of Machine Learning. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to formalize your knowledge, this program aims to equip you with essential skills for a career in this rapidly growing domain.

## Key takeaways

*   The course covers foundational programming concepts like Python, data structures, and algorithms, alongside core Machine Learning topics.
*   It's a self-paced online program, offering over 90 hours of video content, projects, and assignments, all supervised by industry experts.
*   No prior programming experience is necessary, making it accessible for newcomers to the tech world.
*   Participants can earn a completion certificate or even a certificate of excellence based on their performance.

## Course Overview

The Machine Learning certification program is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of Machine Learning principles, particularly for those aspiring to engineering careers. It progresses from fundamental programming skills to advanced Machine Learning concepts. The curriculum includes essential subjects such as data structures, algorithms, and Python programming, ensuring a solid base for learners. The program offers more than 90 hours of video lectures, numerous assignments, and practical projects, all supported by a dedicated chatroom for peer and instructor interaction. Industry professionals guide students throughout their learning journey.

## What You Will Learn

This program is designed to impart a wide array of skills and knowledge crucial for a Machine Learning role. You will delve into:

*   **Programming Fundamentals:** Grasping the basics of Python, including operators, patterns, and two-dimensional lists, along with foundational data structures and algorithms.
*   **Core Computer Science Concepts:** Understanding recursion, linked lists, and dynamic programming techniques.
*   **Machine Learning Essentials:** Learning to set up a Machine Learning environment and use relevant tools and libraries.
*   **Key Algorithms:** Exploring multivariable regression, gradient descent, decision trees, random forests, Naive Bayes, KNN, and SVM.
*   **Advanced Topics:** Gaining insights into Principal Component Analysis, Natural Language Processing, Neural Networks, TensorFlow, Keras, and various deep learning algorithms.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The course utilizes a flexible, self-paced online format, allowing students to learn at their convenience. Key aspects of the program include:

*   **Instructional Medium:** All content is delivered in English, through a combination of video lectures and text-based materials.
*   **Learning Effort:** Students are expected to dedicate 12-18 hours per week to the course material.
*   **Support:** Access to study materials, projects, assignments, and a dedicated Slack chatroom for support and discussions.
*   **Flexibility:** A 15-day free extension is available for revision purposes.

## Fees and Certification

The program offers several enrollment plans, with varying features and costs. The fees include applicable GST. A 7-day refund policy is available for students who decide to withdraw after enrollment. For precise and up-to-date fee details, always refer to the official course webpage.

| Plan      | Amount in INR |
| :-------- | :------------ |
| Basic     | 21,239        |
| Standard  | 30,089        |
| Pro       | 41,239        |
| Premium   | 50,089        |

Upon successful completion, students can receive a certificate. To earn a completion certificate, a minimum score of 60% is required. For those who excel, a minimum score of 90% qualifies them for a certificate of excellence. These certificates are provided by Coding Ninjas.

## How to Apply

The admission process for this Machine Learning program is straightforward and entirely online. Follow these steps to enroll:

1.  Visit the official course page for the Machine Learning program.
2.  Create an account on the platform.
3.  Locate and click the 'Enrol now' option on the course page.
4.  Proceed to pay the required course fee to finalize your enrollment and begin your learning journey.

This program offers a robust pathway into the world of Machine Learning, providing foundational knowledge and practical skills for aspiring professionals.
