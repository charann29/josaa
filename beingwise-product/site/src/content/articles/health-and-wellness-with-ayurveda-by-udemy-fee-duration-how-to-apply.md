# Health & Wellness with Ayurveda by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online certification explores the principles of Ayurveda, an ancient Indian system of medicine, to guide individuals towards a balanced and healthy life. It offers practical insights into diet, lifestyle, and understanding your unique mind-body constitution.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn foundational Ayurvedic concepts like doshas, prakruti, and vikruti.
*   Understand how to apply Ayurvedic principles to your diet and daily habits.
*   The course is self-paced, includes video lectures, downloadable resources, and quizzes.
*   A certificate of completion is provided upon finishing the program.

## Course Overview

Ayurveda views health as a holistic outcome of an individual's physical and mental activities, emphasizing the deep connection between the body and mind. This online certification, titled "Health & Wellness with Ayurveda," is designed for anyone interested in adopting a balanced lifestyle through Ayurvedic wisdom. It was developed by Preeti Syal, an M.Sc. in Foods & Nutrition, Registered Holistic Nutritionist (R.H.N.), and Ayurvedic consultant.

The program is structured to be self-paced, allowing learners to progress at their own convenience. It includes approximately four hours of video content, supplemented by four downloadable resources and interactive quizzes. The curriculum focuses on empowering students to develop a preventive approach to health and provides valuable knowledge on modifying dietary and lifestyle choices for improved well-being. Participants will learn to integrate Ayurvedic fundamentals into their daily lives, recognizing food as medicine, and exploring concepts such as doshas, prakruti, vikruti, Ayurvedic lifestyle, Ayurvedic diet, mind-body constitution, and Ayurvedic dietetics.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing the Health & Wellness with Ayurveda certification, students will gain a comprehensive understanding of core Ayurvedic principles for health and wellness. The course delves into essential concepts like doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha), prakruti (individual constitution), and vikruti (imbalances).

You will learn to identify the signs and symptoms of imbalanced doshas and discover methodologies to restore balance in both mind and body. Furthermore, the program covers the fundamentals of the Ayurvedic diet, lifestyle practices, and Ayurvedic dietetics, providing practical tools to integrate these ancient teachings into modern living.

## Program Structure and Content

The course is delivered through an online format, featuring pre-recorded video lectures and accompanying learning resources. It is accessible across various devices, including mobile phones and televisions, offering flexibility for learners.

### Syllabus highlights:

*   **Introduction:** A brief introduction to the course instructor.
*   **Basics of Ayurveda:** Covering an introduction to Ayurveda, its foundational laws and rules, detailed explanations of Vata, Pitta, and Kapha Doshas, and methods for Prakruti analysis.
*   **Factors Affecting Dosha:** Exploring how doshas are influenced by stages of life, time of day, seasons, and geographical location.
*   **The Disease Process:** Understanding Vikruti (the nature of imbalance), the six stages of disease, accumulation and aggravation of doshas, and diagnostic techniques like Ayurvedic tongue and stool analysis.
*   **Ayurvedic Dietetics:** Delving into the science of the six tastes, principles of Ayurvedic dietetics, and specific food recommendations for each dosha.

## Fees and Certification

The course fee is ₹499, a significant reduction from its original price of ₹1,999. Upon successful completion of all course modules and quizzes, participants will receive a certificate of completion from Udemy, the platform offering the course. The program also includes a 30-day money-back guarantee, ensuring a risk-free learning experience, and provides unlimited access to the course content once purchased.

This course offers a valuable opportunity to explore ancient wisdom for modern well-being.
