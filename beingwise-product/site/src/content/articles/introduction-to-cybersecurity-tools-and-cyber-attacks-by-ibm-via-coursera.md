# Introduction to Cybersecurity Tools & Cyber Attacks by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Embark on a foundational journey into the world of cybersecurity with this introductory online course, designed for anyone keen to understand digital threats and protective measures. Offered in collaboration with IBM and available through Coursera, this programme demystifies essential cybersecurity concepts and tools.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a beginner-friendly course requiring no prior academic background in the field.
*   The entire programme is self-paced and delivered online, allowing you to learn at your convenience.
*   Developed in partnership with IBM, it offers insights into real-world cybersecurity practices.
*   Upon successful completion, you can earn a shareable certificate to showcase your new skills.

## Course Overview

The "Introduction to Cybersecurity Tools & Cyber Attacks" certification course delves into the core aspects of cybersecurity. It covers fundamental terminology, various types of cyberattacks, and the tools and techniques used to defend against them. Key topics include understanding malicious software, the evolution of cybersecurity, and concepts like access management and incident response.

This programme is entirely online and self-paced, meaning you can set your own study schedule without strict deadlines. The total estimated time to complete the course content is approximately 11 hours. While the course materials and lessons can be accessed for free in audit mode, obtaining a certificate requires a paid subscription.

## What You Will Learn

Upon finishing this course, you will have a solid grasp of crucial cybersecurity principles. The curriculum is structured to provide a comprehensive introduction, covering:

*   The historical development and evolution of cybersecurity.
*   Different categories of malicious software, including malware and ransomware.
*   Common cyberattack methods and the organizations behind them.
*   Essential cybersecurity tools and techniques such as antivirus software, firewalls, cryptography, penetration testing, and digital forensics.
*   Key concepts like the CIA triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability), access management, and incident response.
*   An overview of IT governance processes related to cybersecurity.

## Who Is This Course For?

This introductory course is perfectly suited for individuals who are:

*   Aspiring to begin a career as a cybersecurity analyst.
*   Looking to build a foundational understanding before pursuing more advanced cybersecurity programmes.
*   Simply curious about how to protect themselves and their data in the digital world.

## Course Structure and Offerings

The learning experience is designed to be engaging and comprehensive, incorporating various elements to facilitate understanding. These include:

*   Video lectures and readings to convey core concepts.
*   Practice exercises, quizzes, and tests to reinforce learning.
*   Assignments to apply your knowledge.
*   A discussion platform to interact with fellow learners and instructors.

## Fee and Certification

While the course content can be explored without charge in audit mode, a fee is associated with earning the official certificate. The certificate is provided by Coursera upon successful completion of all course requirements. The subscription costs, which grant access to graded assignments and the certificate, are as follows:

| Subscription Duration | Amount (approx.) |
| :-------------------- | :--------------- |
| 1 month               | Rs. 1,683        |
| 3 months              | Rs. 3,384        |
| 6 months              | Rs. 5,051        |

To qualify for the certificate, you must complete all modules and assignments within the course. The certificate is shareable, allowing you to easily add it to your professional profiles.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the "Introduction to Cybersecurity Tools & Cyber Attacks" course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official course page on the Coursera platform.
2.  If you don't already have one, create an account on Coursera.
3.  Locate and click the "Enroll" option on the course page to begin your learning journey.

This programme offers a fantastic starting point for anyone interested in the critical field of cybersecurity, equipping you with essential knowledge and skills to navigate the digital landscape safely and effectively.
