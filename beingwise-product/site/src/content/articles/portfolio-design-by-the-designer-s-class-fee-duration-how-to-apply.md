# Portfolio Design by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Crafting a compelling portfolio is essential for anyone aspiring to enter advanced design programs or the professional design world. The Portfolio Design course from The Designer's Class aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge to create a portfolio that truly stands out.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a self-paced online course delivered through video sessions.
*   The total video content is just under two hours, complemented by numerous assignments and downloadable resources.
*   You get one year of access to the course materials.
*   The course is taught by renowned designer Masaba Gupta.

## Course Overview

This online program focuses on understanding what leading design schools and the industry look for in a prospective student's portfolio. It guides learners through a structured approach to developing and organizing their work, helping them identify and showcase their unique talents. The curriculum emphasizes conveying design concepts effectively through observational drawings, process drawings, and innovative designs.

The course, instructed by Masaba Gupta, provides a comprehensive explanation of how to build a professional and impactful portfolio. It covers the entire journey, from an introduction to designers and portfolio design principles to advanced topics like portfolio storytelling, specific requirements for different portfolios, planning, and organization. A crucial segment also teaches how to establish a strong online presence for your design work.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this certification, you will gain a detailed understanding of the skills necessary to become a successful professional designer. The course delves into various elements involved in creating a high-quality portfolio. You will explore different design processes, including:

*   **Observational Drawing:** Enhancing your ability to capture the world around you.
*   **Process Drawing:** Documenting your design journey and thought process.
*   **Material Studies:** Understanding and representing different textures and substances.

Beyond these foundational skills, the program positions portfolio design as a critical starting point for a career in fashion and design. It will equip you with diverse strategies and methods for portfolio creation, including:

*   **Portfolio Storytelling:** Weaving a narrative through your work.
*   **Portfolio Planning & Organization:** Structuring your portfolio for maximum impact.

## Course Structure and Content

The course is delivered entirely online via video, making it flexible for learners. It consists of approximately 1 hour and 54 minutes of video lectures. Alongside the video content, students will engage with 14 assignments and have access to 15 downloadable resources to support their learning.

### Syllabus Highlights

The curriculum is thoughtfully designed to cover all aspects of portfolio creation:

*   Introduction to the Designer and Portfolio Design
*   Observational Drawing and Material Studies
*   Process Drawing
*   Elements of Portfolio: Typography and other key components
*   Portfolio Storytelling and Specificities
*   Portfolio Planning & Organization
*   Establishing a Digital Presence
*   Ethical Portfolio Practices
*   Final Assessment

## Fees and Access

The fee for the Portfolio Design online course is ₹2,499. Once enrolled, students receive unlimited access to the study material for one year, allowing them to learn at their own pace and revisit content as needed. Please note that while it is a certification course, an official certificate of completion is not provided.

This course is ideal for aspiring fashion designers and anyone looking to enhance their design portfolio for academic or professional pursuits.
