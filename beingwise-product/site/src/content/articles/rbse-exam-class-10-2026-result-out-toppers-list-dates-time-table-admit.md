# RBSE Exam Class 10 2026: Result (OUT), Toppers List, Dates, Time Table, Admit Card

The Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE) Ajmer recently announced the results for the Class 10 examinations. This guide provides all essential details for students and parents regarding the 2026 exams, including results, upcoming changes, and key dates.

## Key takeaways

*   The RBSE Class 10 results for 2026 were declared on March 24, 2026, at 1 PM.
*   The overall pass percentage for 2026 stood at 94.22%, with girls outperforming boys.
*   Starting from the 2026-27 academic session, the RBSE Class 10 exam will be held twice a year.
*   The primary exams for 2026 took place between February 12 and February 28, 2026.

## RBSE Class 10 Result 2026

The Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education officially released the Class 10 results on March 24, 2026, at 1 PM. Students can access their results on the official RBSE website by entering their roll number. The overall pass rate was 94.22%, with girls achieving a pass percentage of 94.20% compared to boys at 93.63%. For those facing issues with the official website due to high traffic, alternative methods such as SMS, DigiLocker, and the Umang App are available to check scores.

## Upcoming Changes to RBSE Class 10 Exams

An important update for students and parents is the announcement by the Education Minister that the RBSE Class 10 examination will be conducted twice a year, starting from the 2026-27 academic session. The first exam will be mandatory and held in March 2027. Students who have passed the first exam will have the option to reappear in up to three subjects in a second exam, scheduled for May-June 2027, to improve their scores. There will be no supplementary exams under this new system. Both examination attempts will cover the complete syllabus, and the exam pattern will remain consistent, offering students an additional opportunity to enhance their performance.

## RBSE Class 10 Exam Dates and Schedule 2026

The primary RBSE Class 10 examinations for the 2026 academic year were conducted in an offline, pen-and-paper format from February 12 to February 28, 2026. The exams were held in a single shift each day, from 8:30 AM to 11:45 AM. More than 10 lakh students participated in these examinations across the state.

## Checking Your RBSE Class 10 Admit Card 2026

The admit card is a crucial document for appearing in the RBSE Class 10 exams. It contains essential details such as the student's name, roll number, examination center, and schedule. Students typically receive their admit cards through their respective schools a few weeks before the examination dates. It is vital to keep the admit card safe as the roll number is required to check the results online.

## RBSE Class 10 Toppers List 2026

While the Rajasthan Board generally announces the names of Class 10 toppers along with the results, it's worth noting that in some previous years, the board did not release a specific toppers list. Should the RBSE decide to publish the 2026 toppers list, details will be made available through official channels. Students are encouraged to check official RBSE announcements for any updates regarding the toppers list.

We wish all students the very best for their academic journey and future endeavors.
