# CMAT Exam Centres 2026 (OUT): Check State wise Test Centres List

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has officially announced the examination centres for CMAT 2026. Candidates have been assigned their test locations based on their preferences submitted during the application process, with details available on their admit cards.

## Key takeaways

*   The NTA allocates CMAT exam centres, usually aligning with candidates' first-choice city from their application.
*   Detailed centre information, including address and reporting time, is provided on the CMAT admit card.
*   Candidates must bring two copies of their admit card, a valid photo ID, and a passport-sized photograph to the centre.
*   Changes to test city preferences are generally not permitted after the admit card is released.

## How to Select Your CMAT Test City

When filling out the CMAT application form, applicants had the opportunity to choose up to four preferred cities for their examination. The process typically involved these steps:

1.  **Registration:** Register on the official CMAT website.
2.  **Login and Form Filling:** Log in using your registration ID and password, then proceed to fill in your personal and academic details.
3.  **City Selection:** Navigate to the test city section and select four cities from the provided list in order of your preference.
4.  **Fee Payment and Submission:** Complete the application fee payment and submit your CMAT 2026 form.

It's important to remember that while preferences are considered, the final allocation depends on the availability of seats at each centre. The NTA reserves the right to change or cancel a centre under unforeseen circumstances. Any modifications to test city choices were typically only possible during the application correction window.

## Documents to Carry to the Exam Centre

On the day of the CMAT exam, candidates must ensure they have all necessary documents for verification. Failing to produce these could lead to denial of entry. Here's a checklist:

*   **CMAT 2026 Admit Card:** Two printed copies of your admit card, which includes your photograph and signature.
*   **Valid Photo Identification:** An original, government-issued photo ID such as an Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Driving License, Passport, or Voter ID. The name on the ID must match the name on your admit card.
*   **Passport-sized Photograph:** One recent passport-sized photograph, preferably the same one uploaded during the application process, to be affixed on the attendance sheet at the centre.

Remember to check your admit card for specific reporting times and any additional instructions from the NTA.

## CMAT Exam Day: Dos and Don'ts

To ensure a smooth experience on exam day, adhere to these guidelines:

### Dos:

*   Arrive at the exam centre well before the reporting time mentioned on your admit card.
*   Carry only the essential documents: admit card (two copies), valid photo ID, and a passport-sized photograph.
*   Follow all instructions given by the invigilators and centre staff.
*   Maintain decorum and silence inside the examination hall.
*   Read all instructions on the computer screen carefully before starting the test.

### Don'ts:

*   Do not carry any electronic devices such as mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, or any other prohibited items into the examination hall.
*   Do not bring any study material, books, or notes.
*   Do not engage in any form of unfair means or malpractice.
*   Do not leave your seat without permission from the invigilator.
*   Do not try to tamper with the computer system or examination interface.

By following these guidelines, you can ensure a focused and fair examination experience. We wish you the best for your CMAT 2026 exam!
