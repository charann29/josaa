# Diploma in Diagnosis, Treatment and Management of ADHD by Alison: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding and managing Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is crucial for improving the quality of life for individuals and their families. This diploma course offers comprehensive insights into ADHD, from its clinical definition to advanced treatment strategies.

## Key Takeaways

*   This diploma provides a thorough understanding of ADHD across different life stages, from childhood to adulthood.
*   The course covers various approaches to diagnosis, treatment, and management, including medication, behavioral therapy, and holistic methods.
*   It equips parents and caregivers with essential coping strategies and awareness of behavioral patterns.
*   The program is delivered entirely online, offering flexibility through video and text-based learning materials.

## Course Overview

This diploma program is designed for anyone interested in gaining a deeper understanding of ADHD. It delves into the history, clinical definitions, and the profound impact of ADHD on individuals and their families. The curriculum is structured to provide practical knowledge on identifying symptoms, obtaining diagnoses, and exploring various treatment pathways.

## Course Content and Syllabus

The program is divided into several modules, each focusing on a specific aspect of ADHD. Here's a glimpse into the topics covered:

### Module 1: Clinical Definition of ADHD and its History

This module introduces the fundamental clinical definition of ADHD, tracing its historical understanding and evolution. It helps learners grasp how the perception and management of ADHD have changed over time.

### Module 2: The Family Picture of ADHD

Focused on the family unit, this section explores how ADHD manifests in children and the dynamics within the family. It provides guidance for parents and siblings on coping and supporting a child with ADHD.

### Module 3: Diagnosis of ADHD and its First Treatments

Learners will discover the initial symptoms to watch for, the process of obtaining a diagnosis, and the common first-line treatment options available. It also covers how to assess the effectiveness of these early interventions.

### Module 4: The Picture of ADHD and how it Affects the Child

This module examines the internal experience of a child with ADHD, how their condition affects their behavior, and strategies for managing initial outbursts and preventing escalation.

### Module 5: Moving into Adolescence with ADHD

Addressing the challenges of ADHD in teenagers, this module discusses the implications of a later diagnosis, signs of ADHD in adolescence, and how the condition impacts their development into adulthood. It also offers advice on navigating this crucial period.

### Module 6: Moving into Adulthood with ADHD

This section explores the progression of ADHD into adulthood, presenting the latest research findings. It covers how adults can still seek and receive treatment, strategies for individual support, and the potential links between ADHD and other personality disorders.

### Module 7: Treatment with Medications and Behavioral Therapy

An in-depth look at key medications used for ADHD, including their potential side effects. It also covers various behavioral therapies and what steps to take if initial treatments are not yielding desired results.

### Module 8: Diploma in Diagnosis, Treatment and Management of ADHD - First Assessment

This module likely contains an initial assessment to gauge understanding of the material covered so far.

### Module 9: Treatment with the Holistic Approach

This module introduces holistic approaches to ADHD management, detailing key treatments associated with this method and their potential effectiveness. It also explores how to combine holistic and conventional options for an improved quality of life.

### Module 10: Patterns of Behavior for Parents to be Aware of

This section helps parents identify specific behavioral patterns related to ADHD, offering guidance on how to react effectively and when to be concerned.

### Module 11: How Parents Can Cope with a Child That Has ADHD

Provides practical steps for parents in handling a child with ADHD, highlighting common mistakes to avoid and emphasizing the importance of seeking support rather than struggling alone.

### Module 12: Everyday Issues to Be Overcome

This module addresses common daily challenges faced by individuals with ADHD.

## Fees and Certification

The course itself is free to access. However, for those who wish to receive official certification, there is a fee. The certificate is issued directly by Alison, the course provider.

## How to Apply and Get Started

To enroll in this diploma, you simply need to visit the official Alison platform. The registration process is straightforward, allowing you to begin learning at your own pace immediately after signing up. As an online course, there are no specific application deadlines; you can start whenever you are ready.

This diploma offers a valuable opportunity to deepen your understanding of ADHD and contribute positively to the lives of those affected by it.
