# Best Colleges accepting CAT Score in Hyderabad

Hyderabad offers a vibrant educational landscape for aspiring MBA students, with over 40 institutions accepting CAT scores for their full-time programs. These colleges provide a strong foundation in various business specialisations, preparing graduates for leadership roles.

## Key takeaways

*   Over 40 MBA colleges in Hyderabad accept CAT scores for admission to their full-time programs.
*   The majority of these institutions are privately owned, with over 20 private colleges, 7 government colleges, and one semi-government institution.
*   Popular specialisations include Finance, Sales & Marketing, Human Resources, Business Analytics, Operations, IT & Systems, and Supply Chain.
*   Prominent colleges in the region include IMT Hyderabad, NIAEM, IPE Hyderabad, IBS Hyderabad, IIRM Hyderabad, and Woxsen University.

## MBA Admission Process in Hyderabad for CAT Scorers

While specific admission criteria can vary between colleges, the fundamental eligibility requirements for MBA programs accepting CAT scores in Hyderabad are generally consistent:

*   **Graduation:** Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline or an equivalent qualification from a recognised university.
*   **Minimum Marks:** Most institutions require a minimum aggregate score of 50% in graduation. For candidates belonging to reserved categories, this minimum aggregate is often relaxed to 45%.

Beyond these basic criteria, colleges typically conduct their own selection rounds, which may include Group Discussions (GD) and Personal Interviews (PI), to assess a candidate's overall suitability for the program.

## Fee Structure at Hyderabad MBA Colleges

The tuition fees for MBA programs in Hyderabad vary considerably across institutions, ranging from less than INR 1 lakh to over INR 5 lakh. This diversity in fee structures allows students to find programs that align with their financial planning. It's important for prospective students to research the specific fee details for their preferred colleges directly from their official websites.
