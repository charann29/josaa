# Bocconi Entrance Test (BET) 2026: Registration (Started), Exam Dates, Eligibility, Syllabus, Results

The Bocconi Entrance Test (BET) is a crucial step for aspiring students aiming for the International Master in Business program at SDA Bocconi Asia Center. This page provides a comprehensive guide to the BET 2026, covering everything from registration to results.

## Key Takeaways

*   The BET 2026 application process is currently open, with multiple submission rounds.
*   The exam is conducted online, featuring 54 multiple-choice questions across three sections.
*   Eligibility generally requires a bachelor's degree, and the application fee is INR 2500.
*   The test assesses Verbal, Numerical, and Inductive Reasoning skills, with no negative marking.

## What is the Bocconi Entrance Test (BET)?

The BET serves as the primary entrance examination for admission to the flagship International Master in Business (IMB) program offered by SDA Bocconi Asia Center. It is administered in several phases throughout the year, allowing candidates flexibility in their application timeline. After completing the online application for a chosen phase, applicants are assigned a slot to take the BET exam.

## BET 2026 Exam Dates & Schedule

The BET 2026 examination window spans from mid-August 2025 to early April 2026. The application process is structured into multiple rounds, each with its own submission deadline. Candidates should monitor the official SDA Bocconi Asia Center website for the most up-to-date schedule.

Here’s a look at the key application deadlines:

| Round | Submission Deadline |
| :---- | :------------------ |
| Round 1 | November 21, 2025   |
| Round 2 | January 22, 2026    |
| Round 3 | March 23, 2026      |
| Round 4 | April 15, 2026      |

## BET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the BET and the International Master in Business program, candidates typically need to hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized institution. Specific academic requirements and any work experience criteria will be detailed on the official SDA Bocconi Asia Center admissions portal. It is always advisable to check the official guidelines thoroughly before applying.

## BET 2026 Syllabus and Exam Pattern

The Bocconi Entrance Test is an online, computer-based examination lasting 70 minutes. It consists of 54 multiple-choice questions (MCQs), with no negative marking for incorrect answers.

### Exam Pattern Details

| Feature           | Details                                    |
| :---------------- | :----------------------------------------- |
| Exam Mode         | Online (Computer-Based Test)               |
| Duration          | 70 minutes                                 |
| Total Questions   | 54 MCQs                                    |
| Sections          | 3                                          |
| Marks per Question| 1                                          |
| Negative Marking  | No                                         |
| Language          | English                                    |

### Syllabus Breakdown

The BET syllabus is divided into three core sections, each containing 18 questions:

*   **Verbal Reasoning:** This section assesses your comprehension, grammar, and vocabulary skills. Topics often include Reading Comprehension, Tenses, Parts of Speech, and error identification.
*   **Numerical Reasoning:** This part tests your ability to interpret data, solve quantitative problems, and apply mathematical concepts.
*   **Inductive Reasoning:** This section evaluates your logical thinking and pattern recognition abilities.

## BET 2026 Results and Selection Process

After taking the BET, candidates will receive their results as part of the overall admission process. Admission to the International Master in Business program is not solely based on the BET score. The selection process at SDA Bocconi Asia Center is comprehensive and typically includes an evaluation of academic records, professional experience (if any), letters of recommendation, and a personal interview, in addition to the BET score or other accepted standardized test scores (like CAT, GMAT, GRE, NMAT by GMAC). Successful candidates will be notified by the institution regarding their admission status.

For any queries, you can contact the BET helpdesk at 022-4086 7000.
