# Certificate in Hair, Makeup and Prosthetics by Indian Institute of Creative Skills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those passionate about transforming appearances for the stage or screen, the Indian Institute of Creative Skills (IICS) offers a specialized Certificate in Hair, Makeup, and Prosthetics. This program aims to equip aspiring artists with the practical skills needed to thrive in the dynamic world of theatre, film, television, and digital media.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 12-month certificate program delivered through video and text-based modules in English.
*   It is officially recognized by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) Academy and operated by the Media and Entertainment Skills Council (MESC).
*   The course focuses on practical skills, portfolio development, and professional growth in the performing arts industry.
*   Eligibility requires completing 12th grade, being between 18 and 40 years old, and submitting a portfolio of creative work.

## Program Overview and Affiliation

The Certificate in Hair, Makeup, and Prosthetics is a comprehensive offering from IICS, meticulously designed to provide participants with essential skills and hands-on experience. The curriculum covers a wide range of techniques, from fundamental makeup applications to intricate prosthetic designs, all tailored for the entertainment industry.

A significant advantage of this program is its affiliation. It is recognized by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) Academy and managed under the Media and Entertainment Skills Council (MESC). This affiliation ensures that the curriculum meets industry standards and that the certification holds value in the professional landscape.

## What You Will Learn

This program delves deep into the art of visual transformation. You will explore various aspects of hair styling, makeup application, and prosthetic design, learning to craft diverse looks from natural appearances to elaborate special effects. The emphasis is on understanding character development through visual cues and gaining proficiency with industry-standard tools and products.

The syllabus is structured across four semesters, covering distinct yet interconnected areas:

### Semester 1: Makeup Artistry Course

*   Fundamentals of Makeup
*   Essential Techniques
*   Advanced Techniques
*   Special Effects Makeup
*   Business and Portfolio Development

### Semester 2: Hair Styling Course

*   Basics of Hair Styling
*   Cutting and Shaping Techniques
*   Styling Techniques
*   Hair Coloring
*   Professional Development

### Semester 3: Prosthetics and Special Effects

*   Introduction to Prosthetics
*   Sculpting and Molding
*   Application Techniques
*   Advanced Special Effects
*   Industry Insights and Portfolio Building

### Semester 4: Entrepreneurship and Industry Exposure

*   Industry Insights and Portfolio Building

## Eligibility and Application Process

To be considered for this certificate program, applicants must meet specific criteria:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Completion of 12th grade from a recognized school board.
*   **Age Limit:** Candidates must be at least 18 years old and not older than 40 years.
*   **Creative Portfolio:** Submission of a collection of previous work demonstrating creative abilities is required.

### How to Apply

The admission process for the Certificate in Hair, Makeup & Prosthetics course is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official IICS portal.
2.  Locate and click on the "Admission now" tab.
3.  Complete the application form with all necessary details.
4.  Submit the required prospectus fee.
5.  Once the form and fees are submitted, you can join the course.

## Course Fees and Certification

The fee structure for the Certificate in Hair, Makeup and Prosthetics course includes a prospectus fee. As per the official information, the prospectus fee is Rs. 2,000.

| Fee Component   | Amount     |
| :-------------- | :--------- |
| Prospectus Fees | Rs. 2,000 |

Upon successful completion of the 12-month program, participants will receive a certificate directly from the Indian Institute of Creative Skills. This certification validates expertise in hair, makeup, and prosthetics for the entertainment sector, enhancing employability and opening doors to various roles in film, television, and other media.

This certification is designed to provide a competitive edge in the job market, preparing graduates for successful careers in the performing arts industry.
