# Complete Beginner's Guide to Anatomy and Figure Drawing pt1: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring artists and designers, mastering human anatomy is crucial for creating believable and dynamic figures. This introductory online course offers a solid foundation in both anatomy and figure drawing techniques.

## Key takeaways

*   The course provides a comprehensive introduction to human anatomy for artists.
*   It includes over 35 hours of video content, articles, and downloadable resources.
*   Students gain lifetime access and a certificate of completion upon finishing.
*   The learning is self-paced, allowing flexibility for busy schedules.

## Course Overview

This program is designed to equip learners with fundamental skills in drawing human figures from various angles and positions. It focuses on techniques that not only enhance current work but also build a strong base for future advanced studies in art. The course content is developed by Rich Graysonn, an experienced illustrator, designer, and instructor.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this certification, you will develop a deeper understanding of human anatomy principles. You'll learn to sketch human muscles, draw both male and female figures accurately, and create dynamic poses without relying on external references or copying other artists' work. The curriculum is structured to improve your overall drawing skills and anatomical knowledge.

## Course Content and Format

The course is delivered in English, with multi-language subtitles available for broader accessibility. It features over 35 hours of pre-recorded video lectures, supplemented by articles and 16 downloadable resources. The learning mode is entirely self-paced, allowing you to progress at your own speed through video and text-based materials. You can access the course on various devices, including mobile phones and TVs.

### Syllabus Highlights

The curriculum covers essential topics, starting with foundational drawing principles and moving into detailed anatomical studies:

*   **Welcome To Learning Anatomy:** An introduction to the course and essential tools.
*   **New Basic Training:** Covers perspective for figure drawing, creating simple planes, and placeholder figures.
*   **New Learning Basic Proportion Pt 1: The Torso:** Focuses on understanding and drawing the torso from front, side, back, and three-quarter views, including male vs. female proportions.
*   **New Learning Proportion Pt 2: Arms And Legs:** Delves into drawing hands, simple stick arms, and arms in various bending positions.

## Who Is This Course For?

This course is particularly beneficial for aspiring game designers, graphic designers, illustrators, digital painters, and anyone looking to enhance their character design skills or general artistic abilities related to the human form.

This comprehensive guide aims to provide you with the foundational knowledge and practical skills needed to confidently draw the human figure.
