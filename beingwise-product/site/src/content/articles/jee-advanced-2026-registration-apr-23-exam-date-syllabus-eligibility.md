# JEE Advanced 2026: Registration (Apr 23), Exam Date, Syllabus, Eligibility & Previous Year Papers

JEE Advanced is the gateway to undergraduate engineering and science programs at the prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs). This highly competitive examination is a crucial step for aspiring engineers and scientists across India.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Registration for Indian nationals** for JEE Advanced 2026 begins on April 23, 2026.
*   The **exam date** for JEE Advanced 2026 is May 17, 2026, consisting of two papers on the same day.
*   Only the **top 2.5 lakh candidates** from JEE Main are eligible to apply for JEE Advanced.
*   The syllabus for JEE Advanced 2026 remains **unchanged** from the previous year, covering Class 11 and 12 Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

## What is JEE Advanced?

JEE Advanced is the qualifying examination for admissions to the Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) and other science programs offered by the 23 Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs). These institutions collectively offer around 18,160 undergraduate seats. Beyond India, certain international universities, such as the University of Cambridge and the National University of Singapore, also consider JEE Advanced scores for their admissions processes. The exam is administered annually by one of the seven zonal IITs under the guidance of the IIT Joint Admission Board (JAB).

The structure of JEE Advanced typically involves two papers, both focusing on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. It's important to note that the total marks, the number of questions, and the specific marking scheme can vary each year, as decided by the organizing IIT.

## JEE Advanced 2026 Important Dates

For Indian candidates, the registration window for JEE Advanced 2026 will open on April 23, 2026. However, foreign candidates, including Overseas Citizens of India (OCI) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIO), have already commenced their registration process as of April 6, 2026, handled by IIT Roorkee, the organizing institute for this year. The examination itself is scheduled for May 17, 2026. Both Paper 1 and Paper 2 will be conducted on this single day, in two distinct shifts.

## Eligibility Criteria for JEE Advanced 2026

To be eligible for JEE Advanced 2026, candidates must fulfill several criteria:

*   **JEE Main Qualification:** Aspirants must appear for JEE Main and secure a rank within the top 2.5 lakh candidates (across all categories) in the B.E./B.Tech paper.
*   **Age Limit:** Candidates must meet the specified age criteria.
*   **Number of Attempts:** There's a limit on the number of times a candidate can attempt JEE Advanced.
*   **Class 12 Passing Year:** Specific requirements regarding the year of passing the Class 12 board examinations must be met.

Foreign nationals are exempt from appearing in JEE Main and can apply directly for JEE Advanced, provided they meet the other eligibility conditions.

## Syllabus and Exam Pattern

The JEE Advanced 2026 syllabus remains consistent with the previous year, covering core concepts from Class 11 and Class 12 Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Candidates can typically download the official syllabus PDF from the JEE Advanced website. While the subjects are fixed, the specific distribution of questions, the overall marking scheme, and the total marks are not standardized and may vary from year to year. Reviewing previous year's question papers is highly recommended to understand the typical exam pattern and question styles.

## Previous Year Papers

Engaging with previous year's JEE Advanced question papers is an invaluable part of preparation. These papers offer insights into the types of questions asked, the difficulty level, and how the marking scheme might be structured. Although the exact exam pattern isn't fixed, studying past papers helps candidates familiarize themselves with the examination environment and improve their time management skills. Official question papers from previous years are usually made available on the JEE Advanced website.

Preparing for JEE Advanced requires dedication, a clear understanding of concepts, and strategic practice. Stay updated with official announcements from the organizing IIT for any changes or additional details.
