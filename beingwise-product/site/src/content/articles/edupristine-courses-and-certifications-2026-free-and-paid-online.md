# Edupristine Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Edupristine Courses

These programs cater to both those new to the industry and experienced professionals seeking to upgrade their expertise.

## Financial Risk Manager (FRM) Certification Training

Edupristine provides comprehensive training for the Financial Risk Manager (FRM) certification, which is a globally recognized professional qualification awarded by the Global Association of Risk Professionals (GARP, USA). This certification is highly valued by banking and finance professionals aiming to specialize in risk management.

The FRM program covers a rigorous curriculum, including essential concepts of risk management, financial markets, financial products, credit risk management, investment management, integrated risk management, valuation, risk models, and quantitative analysis strategies. Edupristine is an authorized training provider for the FRM examination in India, offering a structured path to help candidates prepare.

The curriculum is divided into two levels, designed to help participants clear the FRM examination effectively. The training incorporates practical learning through case studies, mock exams, advanced question banks, sample problems, revision sessions, and task-solving exercises. The typical duration for this program is around 12 months.

## US Certified Management Accountant (CMA) Program

The US Certified Management Accountant (CMA) designation represents a top-tier certification in management accounting, significantly enhancing an accountant's professional credibility. Edupristine's US CMA program focuses on developing accountants' ability to understand the 'why' behind financial figures, not just the 'how,' giving them a competitive edge for career growth in management roles.

Edupristine is recognized as an IMA GOLD Approved Course Provider by the Institute of Management Accountants (IMA), USA, underscoring the quality of its program. The course content is delivered through case studies, hands-on experience, and real-life business scenarios. The US CMA certification holds global recognition and can accelerate career progression into leadership positions.

The duration of the Edupristine US CMA Course can range from 6 months to 3 years, depending on the individual's learning pace. It includes intensive classroom training, soft skill development, and career assistance. Additionally, a self-paced module developed by Wiley allows candidates to progress at their own convenience.

## Financial Modeling Course

Edupristine's Financial Modeling Course is designed to enhance participants' skills in creating financial model templates using Excel. This program covers the fundamentals of financial modeling, preparing individuals for roles in project management, investment banking, business planning, equity research, fund management, private equity funds, and commercial banking.

Financial modeling is considered a highly sought-after skill for finance professionals in India. This module, offered in collaboration with BSE Institute Ltd., enables candidates to independently develop financial models. The 70-hour training course equips participants with the ability to use financial models for analyzing an organization's investments, operations, valuation, and financing. The program also includes two detailed case studies from the FMCG sector and Accenture, ensuring industry-relevant insights and grooming by industry leaders.
