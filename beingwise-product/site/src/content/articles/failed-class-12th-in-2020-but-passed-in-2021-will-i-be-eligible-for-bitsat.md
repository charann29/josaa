# I failed class 12th in 2020 but passed in 2021, will I be eligible for BITSAT 2022?

Navigating university entrance exams can be tricky, especially when your academic journey has had a few detours. If you passed your Class 12 exams in 2021 after an initial attempt in 2020, you might be wondering about your eligibility for BITSAT 2022.

## Key Takeaways

*   Students who passed their Class 12 or equivalent examination in **2021 or 2022** are eligible for BITSAT 2022.
*   If you took multiple attempts to clear Class 12, only your **latest performance** is considered, provided it covers all prescribed subjects.
*   You need a minimum of **75% aggregate** in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (or Biology, if applicable) in Class 12.
*   Additionally, you must secure **at least 60% marks in each** of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology individually.

## BITSAT 2022 Eligibility: The Passing Year

The most crucial point for your situation is the year of passing Class 12. According to the official BITSAT eligibility criteria, only candidates who passed their Class 12 or equivalent examination in **2021 or 2022** are considered eligible for BITSAT 2022. This means that if you cleared your Class 12 exams in 2021, even after an earlier attempt in 2020, you meet this specific criterion.

Students who passed their Class 12 examination in 2020 or earlier are **not** eligible to appear for BITSAT 2022.

## Understanding Multiple Attempts

The BITSAT eligibility guidelines are clear on multiple attempts: if a candidate has taken more than one attempt to pass Class 12 or its equivalent, only their **latest performance** will be considered. This consideration is valid as long as the latest attempt covered the full set of subjects/courses required for Class 12.

So, even though your first attempt was in 2020, your successful completion in 2021 is what matters for BITSAT 2022 eligibility, provided all other conditions are met.

## Academic Performance Requirements

Beyond the passing year, your academic scores in Class 12 are critically important. To be eligible for BITSAT 2022, you must have achieved:

*   A minimum aggregate of **75% marks** in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics subjects (if you are opting for Mathematics-based programs).
*   Alternatively, if you are pursuing Biology-based programs, a minimum aggregate of **75% marks** in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology subjects.
*   Furthermore, you must have secured **at least 60% marks** individually in each of the Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology subjects.

These criteria are applied to your latest Class 12 performance (i.e., your 2021 results).

## Document Verification

During the admissions process, universities like BITS Pilani will verify your academic documents. It's essential to ensure that your Class 12 mark sheet clearly reflects your passing year and the marks obtained in the relevant subjects. Always refer to the official BITSAT admission website for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding eligibility and required documentation.

If you meet all these conditions, your successful Class 12 completion in 2021 makes you eligible for BITSAT 2022.
