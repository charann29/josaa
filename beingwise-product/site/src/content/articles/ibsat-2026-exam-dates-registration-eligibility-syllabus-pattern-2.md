# IBSAT 2026: Exam Dates, Registration, Eligibility, Syllabus, Pattern & Results

The IBS Aptitude Test (IBSAT) is a crucial national-level entrance examination for students aspiring to pursue MBA or PGPM programs at the nine ICFAI Business School (IBS) campuses across India. Administered by IBS Hyderabad, this test provides a pathway to postgraduate management education at reputable institutions.

## Key takeaways

*   **Registration Begins Early:** The application window for IBSAT 2026 is expected to open in July 2026, offering ample time to apply.
*   **Remote Proctored Exam:** The IBSAT 2026 examination will be conducted online, with candidates taking the test from their homes under remote proctoring.
*   **Multiple Admission Routes:** While IBSAT is a primary route, IBS campuses also accept scores from other national-level exams such as CAT, XAT, NMAT by GMAC, and GMAT.
*   **Nine Campuses:** Successful candidates can gain admission to IBS Hyderabad, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Pune, Gurgaon, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, and Dehradun.

## What is IBSAT?

The IBSAT is a computer-based aptitude test specifically designed for admission to the Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM) courses offered by the ICFAI Business Schools. It's a national-level assessment, making it a competitive gateway for management aspirants. The exam is conducted online, typically over a few days in the final week of December, and consists entirely of multiple-choice questions.

## IBSAT 2026 Important Dates

Staying informed about key dates is essential for a smooth application process. Here's an overview of the anticipated schedule for IBSAT 2026:

| Event                      | Tentative Dates       |
| :------------------------- | :-------------------- |
| Registration Period        | July 2026 - December 2026 |

Candidates are advised to regularly check the official IBS website for any updates or changes to these tentative dates.

## IBSAT Exam Pattern 2026

Understanding the structure of the IBSAT exam is vital for effective preparation. Here are the key highlights:

*   **Exam Name:** ICFAI Business School Aptitude Test (IBSAT)
*   **Conducting Body:** IBS Hyderabad
*   **Exam Level:** National
*   **Frequency:** Once a year
*   **Mode of Exam:** Home proctored mode (online)
*   **Duration:** 120 minutes (2 hours)
*   **Question Type:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Total Questions:** 140
*   **Sections:** Verbal Ability, Reading Comprehension, Quantitative Ability, Data Adequacy, and Data Interpretation
*   **Language:** English
*   **Application Fee:** INR 1,800
*   **Purpose:** Admission to MBA/PGPM programs at nine IBS campuses

## IBSAT 2026 Eligibility and Application Process

To be eligible for IBSAT 2026, candidates must meet specific academic requirements. Generally, applicants need to possess a bachelor's degree or an equivalent qualification in any discipline, with a minimum aggregate score of 50 percent. It's crucial for all aspiring candidates to verify the detailed eligibility criteria on the official IBS website before applying.

The application process for IBSAT 2026 is conducted entirely online. Candidates will need to visit the official IBS website, typically starting in July 2026, to fill out the application form. This involves providing personal and academic details, uploading necessary documents, and paying the application fee. Only registered candidates will be able to book their exam slots and download their admit cards when these facilities become available.

Prepare diligently and keep an eye on the official IBS website for the most accurate and timely information regarding IBSAT 2026.
