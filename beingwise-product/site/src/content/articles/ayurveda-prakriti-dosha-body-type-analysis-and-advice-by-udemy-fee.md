# Ayurveda - Prakriti - Dosha Body Type Analysis And Advice

This course offers an engaging introduction to the fundamental principles of Ayurveda, focusing on the concept of 'Prakriti' (body constitution) and 'Doshas' (biological energies). It's designed for anyone curious about understanding their unique body type and how it influences their health and lifestyle.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn about the three primary Ayurvedic Doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha, and their impact on mind and body.
*   Understand how to analyze your own 'Prakriti' or body type through various features.
*   Explore Ayurvedic principles for diet and lifestyle tailored to different Dosha constitutions.
*   Gain insights into ancient Indian healthcare wisdom from revered Ayurvedic masters.

## Course Overview

In the traditional Indian healing system of Ayurveda, every individual possesses a unique body constitution, known as 'Prakriti', which is determined by the balance of three biological energies called 'Doshas' – Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. This certification course, created by Ayurveda practitioner Janardhana V Hebbar, aims to demystify these concepts for a general audience.

The program offers over 3.5 hours of pre-recorded video lessons, complemented by three downloadable resources. It's structured to provide a solid grasp of Ayurvedic fundamentals, including body constitution analysis, lifestyle recommendations, and dietary guidelines based on your Dosha type.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this course, you will have a clear understanding of core Ayurvedic principles. The curriculum delves into:

*   **Dosha and Prakriti:** A detailed exploration of these foundational concepts.
*   **Body Type Analysis:** Methodologies for identifying your unique body constitution.
*   **Ancient Indian Healthcare:** Insights into the historical context and wisdom of Ayurveda.
*   **Ayurvedic Diet and Lifestyle:** Practical guidance on adapting your diet and daily routines according to your Dosha.
*   **Classical Perspectives:** Examination of Dosha and Prakriti as described by ancient masters like Sushruta, Charaka, and Vagbhata.
*   **Dosha Calculation:** Techniques to determine the percentage of Dosha features in an individual.
