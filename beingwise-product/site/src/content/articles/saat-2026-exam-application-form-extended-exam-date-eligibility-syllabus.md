# SAAT 2026 Exam: Application Form (Extended), Exam Date, Eligibility, Syllabus & Pattern

For students aiming to join Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA) University, the SAAT (SOA Admission Test) is a crucial entrance examination. This page provides a clear guide to the application process, key dates, and other important details for SAAT 2026.

## Key Takeaways

*   The application window for SAAT 2026 opens on December 7, 2025, and extends until April 30, 2026.
*   The entire application process, including registration and form submission, is conducted online.
*   A significant benefit for applicants is that there are no application fees for SAAT 2026.
*   Successful submission of your application will be confirmed via email.

## SAAT 2026 Application Process: A Step-by-Step Guide

Applying for SAAT 2026 involves a straightforward online procedure. Here's how you can complete your application:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Start by navigating to the designated registration section on the official SOA University website.
2.  **Initial Registration:** Provide your full name, a valid email address, and an active mobile number to register yourself.
3.  **OTP Verification:** You will receive a One-Time Password (OTP) on the mobile number you registered. Enter this OTP on the portal for verification, which will then generate your unique login ID.
4.  **Log In to the Application:** Use your newly created login ID (typically your registered email) to access the main application form.
5.  **Fill the Application Form:** Carefully enter all required personal details, academic qualifications, and any other pertinent information.
6.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature as per the specified guidelines.
7.  **Review and Submit:** Before final submission, thoroughly review all the information you have provided to ensure accuracy.
8.  **Confirmation:** After successful submission, you will receive an email confirming that your SAAT 2026 application has been received.
9.  **Print for Records:** It's advisable to download and print a copy of your submitted application form for your own records.

## Important Documents for Application

Before you begin filling out the SAAT 2026 application form, ensure you have the following documents ready in the specified format and size:

| S. No. | Document          | Maximum File Size | Format          |
| :----- | :---------------- | :---------------- | :-------------- |
| 1.     | Scanned Photograph | Less than 1 MB    | PNG, JPG, JPEG  |
| 2.     | Scanned Signature | Less than 1 MB    | PNG, JPG, JPEG  |

Ensure these digital files are clear and meet the size and format requirements to avoid any issues during the upload process.

## SAAT 2026 Application Fees

One of the notable aspects of SAAT 2026 is that the application process is entirely free of charge. Candidates are not required to pay any registration or application fees to apply for the SAAT entrance examination.

## Key Dates for SAAT 2026

Mark these important dates on your calendar to ensure you don't miss any deadlines:

*   **Application Form Activation:** December 7, 2025
*   **Application Submission Deadline:** April 30, 2026

Stay updated by regularly checking the official SOA University website for any announcements regarding exam dates, admit card releases, and other crucial information.

Navigating the application process for SAAT 2026 is straightforward if you follow these guidelines carefully. We wish you the best in your application!
