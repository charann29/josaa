# Post Graduate Program in Solidworks Design and Analysis by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring and current engineers looking to master a crucial industry tool, the Post Graduate Program in Solidworks Design and Analysis offers a deep dive into this essential software. This program is designed to equip you with practical skills for product design and optimization.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Online Learning:** This six-month program is delivered entirely online through video and text-based modules.
*   **Industry-Focused Curriculum:** Learn solid modeling, surface modeling, drafting, rendering, assembly modules, and analysis using FEA and CFD.
*   **Project-Based Skill Development:** Gain hands-on experience through assignments, industry-grade projects, and exercises.
*   **Career Support:** The program offers job assistance and helps students build a strong project portfolio.

## Program Overview

This Post Graduate Program in Solidworks Design and Analysis focuses on the core engineering concepts and practical application of SOLIDWORKS software. The curriculum, crafted by industry experts, aims to bring participants up to industry standards in using the SOLIDWORKS interface for design and analysis tasks.

The program covers a wide array of topics, including solid modeling, technical drafting, advanced surface modeling, realistic rendering, assembly creation, and various analysis tools like Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD). It also emphasizes multi-body dynamics, ensuring you develop a comprehensive skill set. The intensive online format spans six months and includes seven distinct courses, all designed to enhance your product design and optimization capabilities.

## Who Is This Program For?

This specialized program is particularly well-suited for individuals in specific engineering fields. It is ideal for:

*   **Aerospace Engineers:** Enhance your design and analysis skills for aircraft components.
*   **Mechanical Engineers:** Develop expertise in product design, simulation, and optimization.
*   **Automotive Engineers:** Gain proficiency in designing and analyzing vehicle parts and systems.

Additionally, students currently pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering (BE) or Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree in mechanical, aerospace, or automotive engineering are also eligible to enroll, providing them with an early advantage in their careers.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of the program, you will gain practical expertise in various aspects of SOLIDWORKS, including:

*   In-depth understanding and hands-on proficiency in SOLIDWORKS functionalities, operations, commands, configurations, and installation.
*   Techniques for effectively designing and optimizing diverse products.
*   Skills to create realistic simulations of designed products using both static and dynamic tools, enabling real-time result visualization.
*   Methods to apply aesthetic principles for improved visualization of design features.
*   Practical experience in modeling complex designs, such as a Boeing 747-8, within the SOLIDWORKS environment.
*   Ability to perform Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and flow simulations using SOLIDWORKS tools.

## Fees and Certificates

The total program fee for the Post Graduate Program in Solidworks Design and Analysis is Rs. 2,75,000. For those preferring installment options, an EMI plan is available at Rs. 14,375 per month.

Upon successful completion of the program, including all weekly assignments, projects, and e-learning videos, participants will receive a course completion certificate from Skill Lync. High-achieving students, specifically those in the top 5% of the class, may also be awarded a merit certificate.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the program, candidates must meet the eligibility criteria: either be a practicing aerospace, mechanical, or automotive engineer, or be currently pursuing a BE/B.Tech degree in one of these disciplines. Further details on the application process and any required documentation would typically be available on the official program portal.

This program offers a robust pathway for engineers to enhance their skills in a critical industry software, opening doors to advanced career opportunities.
