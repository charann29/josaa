# TechXcelerator Boot Camp by IITM Pravartak via BodhBridge Educational Services Private Limited (fundaspring): Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring tech professionals looking to gain an edge in the competitive job market, the TechXcelerator Boot Camp offers a comprehensive finishing school program. This initiative, a collaboration with IITM Pravartak, is designed to equip participants with essential skills and practical experience for a thriving career in technology.

## Key Takeaways

*   A collaborative program with certification recognized by IIT Madras Pravartak.
*   Focuses on hands-on learning, real-world projects, and mentorship.
*   Requires a relevant engineering or science degree and at least six months of work experience.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹30,000.

## Program Overview

This boot camp is structured as a joint finishing school program, bringing together the academic excellence of institutions like IIT Madras Pravartak with industry insights. The curriculum is crafted by both seasoned educators and industry experts, ensuring a learning experience that is both academically rigorous and professionally relevant. The goal is to not only impart technical proficiency but also cultivate the crucial problem-solving mindset needed to succeed in the fast-paced tech world.

## What You Will Learn

The TechXcelerator Boot Camp covers a range of vital topics, preparing students to become well-rounded and highly skilled tech professionals. Key areas of study include:

*   **Programming Languages:** Fundamental and advanced concepts to build robust applications.
*   **Software Development Methodologies:** Understanding and applying industry-standard practices for efficient software creation.
*   **Project Management:** Skills to plan, execute, and oversee technology projects successfully.
*   **Collaborative Teamwork:** Learning to work effectively in a team environment, a critical skill in modern tech workplaces.

Through real-world projects, participants gain practical experience, sharpen their analytical abilities, and apply theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios. This hands-on approach helps bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the TechXcelerator Boot Camp, candidates must meet specific academic and professional requirements:

*   **Academic Qualification:** Applicants should hold a degree in relevant fields such as BE, B. Tech, ME, M. Tech (in IT, CSE, EEE, ECE), MCA, or MSc (in CSE, Data Science) from a recognized university.
*   **Work Experience:** A minimum of six months of professional work experience is required.

To earn the certification, participants must successfully complete the entire course and all assigned projects.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the TechXcelerator Boot Camp is ₹30,000. This fee covers all course materials, virtual classroom sessions, and access to video and text-based learning content. Upon successful completion of the program, including all coursework and projects, participants will receive a completion certificate recognized by IIT Madras Pravartak.

## How to Apply

Prospective candidates interested in joining the TechXcelerator Boot Camp should follow these steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Navigate to the official application portal for the program.
2.  **Complete the Online Application:** Fill out the online application form with all necessary and accurate personal and academic information.

Further details regarding the application process, including specific deadlines, will be available on the official portal.

This boot camp offers a valuable opportunity to enhance your technical skills and advance your career in the dynamic technology sector.
