# Aptech Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Aptech Learning Courses

Aptech Learning offers a range of career-focused courses designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills across various domains, including IT, banking, finance, and hardware networking. These programs aim to prepare individuals for specific job roles and opportunities in a dynamic job market.

## Key takeaways

*   Aptech Learning provides specialized certification programs tailored for career readiness.
*   Course durations vary from a few months to nearly two years, depending on the program's depth.
*   Fees are structured per course, offering different investment levels for various specializations.
*   Programs often combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills, preparing students for immediate employment.

## ACCP Pro- E-commerce Course

The ACCP Pro – E-commerce course from Aptech Learning is a comprehensive program spanning 23 months, designed to give students a deep understanding of the e-commerce landscape. This certification aims to introduce learners to the fundamental principles and advanced applications within the e-commerce sector.

The curriculum covers essential areas such as web productivity tools, logic building, developing modern websites, foundational programming concepts, and practical e-commerce implementation. Graduates are expected to develop the professional skills and expertise necessary to launch and sustain a career in the e-commerce industry. The total fee for this extensive program is ₹77,400.

**Skills Covered:**

*   SQL proficiency
*   Automation techniques
*   Taxation knowledge
*   Financial understanding
*   CSS
*   Java

## Banking Specialist Certification

Recognizing India's rapidly expanding banking and finance industry, the Banking Specialist certification by Aptech Learning is crafted for individuals aspiring to careers in this sector. This 16-month program focuses on imparting core knowledge in banking and finance, preparing students for various roles within the industry.

The course is structured into three parts, delivered through two-hour in-class lectures three days a week. It aims to provide specialized knowledge and skills across different banking functions. The fee for the Banking Specialist program is ₹54,000.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Comprehensive banking knowledge

## Aptech Certified Accounts Professional

The Aptech Certified Accounts Professional program is an intensive 15-month training course, specifically designed to make candidates job-ready in the accounting field. The curriculum is divided into two terms, each with a detailed set of modules focusing on employment-driven education.

This program equips students with expertise in basic computer applications essential for business accounting, including Tally. It also delves into advanced topics such as business communication, statutory compliance, TDS, GST, and direct taxation. The goal is to provide candidates with the latest accounting techniques, finance, banking, and taxation skills, enabling them to perform complex transactions and secure roles immediately after completion. The total fee for this certification is ₹70,000.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Accounting proficiency
*   Business management principles

Aptech Learning's commitment to career-oriented education ensures that students gain practical, relevant skills for today's job market, making them valuable assets to various industries upon graduation.
