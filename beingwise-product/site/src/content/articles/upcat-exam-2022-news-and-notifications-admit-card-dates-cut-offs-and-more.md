# UPCAT Exam 2022 News & Notifications: Admit Card, Dates, Cut Offs & more

For students aspiring to pursue various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Uttar Pradesh, the UPCAT exam is a crucial gateway. This page provides the latest information regarding the UPCAT 2022 examination, including important dates, application details, and more.

## Key Takeaways

*   The UPCAT exam is conducted for a wide range of courses, from B.Tech Lateral Entry to MBA and M.Pharma programs.
*   Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) is the conducting body for the UPCAT examination.
*   Application dates and exam schedules are subject to change; candidates should regularly check official AKTU announcements.
*   The application process includes form submission, correction windows, and timely access to admit cards.

## UPCAT 2022 Application Form News

Staying updated on application timelines is vital for all prospective candidates. Here's a look at key announcements related to the UPCAT application process:

*   **May 17, 2020:** The deadline for UPCAT 2020 applications was extended until May 31, 2020. The correction window for submitted forms closed on June 3, 2020.
*   **May 06, 2020:** AKTU officially announced new examination dates for UPCAT 2020.
*   **May 03, 2020:** The application portal for UPCAT 2020 was reopened by AKTU, and the facility for corrections was also extended for candidates.
*   **April 08, 2020:** Due to the prevailing COVID-19 pandemic, AKTU postponed the UPCAT 2020 examination.

Candidates are advised to refer to the official AKTU website for the most current information regarding application dates and any changes to the schedule.

## Programs Offered Through UPCAT

UPCAT serves as an entrance exam for admission to a diverse array of courses across various disciplines. These include, but are not limited to:

*   Bachelor of Hotel Management and Catering Technology (BHMCT)
*   Master of Urban and Regional Planning (MURP)
*   B.Tech Lateral Entry
*   Bachelor of Design (B.Des)
*   Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFAD/BFA)
*   Master of Computer Applications (MCA/MCA Integrated/MCA Lateral Entry)
*   Master of Technology (M.Tech)
*   Master of Design (M.Des)
*   Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm/B.Pharm Lateral Entry)
*   Master of Architecture (M.Arch)
*   Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharma)
*   Master of Business Administration (MBA/MBA Integrated)

## Participating Institutions

Many colleges and universities in Uttar Pradesh accept UPCAT scores for admissions to their programs. One notable example is the Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology (NIET) in Greater Noida, a private institution that offers various courses, including those for which UPCAT scores are considered. For instance, NIET offers a 5-year full-time program with a total fee of approximately ₹2.10 Lakh, accepting UPCAT scores for admission. Students can explore a comprehensive list of institutes accepting UPCAT scores on the official counselling portal.

## Important Dates and Notifications

While the specific dates for UPCAT 2022 will be announced by AKTU, candidates should be prepared for a typical examination cycle. Key events include application form release, last date for submission, admit card availability, exam date, answer key release, result declaration, and counselling registration. Always check the official AKTU notifications for precise timelines.

Keep an eye on official announcements from Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University for all the latest updates on UPCAT 2022.
