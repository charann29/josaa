## SRCC GBO: An Overview

The SRCC Global Business Operations (GBO) exam is a national-level management entrance test administered by Sri Ram College of Commerce, University of Delhi. It serves as the gateway for admission to their highly regarded PGDM Global Business Operations program, designed to equip students with comprehensive business management skills. The exam is conducted online at various centers across India.

## SRCC GBO 2026 Result and Admission Process

The SRCC GBO 2026 results are expected to be announced in the second week of April 2026. Candidates who appeared for the exam will be able to download their scorecard online using their login credentials. The official SRCC GBO portal will host these results.

To download your scorecard:

1.  Visit the official SRCC GBO website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Admission 2026-28' link.
3.  Log in to the candidate portal using your User ID and password.
4.  Click on 'SRCC GBO result' to view and download your scorecard.

Following the result declaration, the admission process includes several stages:

*   Submission of the SRCC GBO application form.
*   Issuance of the SRCC GBO admit card.
*   Appearing for the SRCC GBO examination.
*   Declaration of SRCC GBO results.
*   Participation in Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds.
