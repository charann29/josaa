# Tackling Antibiotic Resistance: What Should Dental Teams Do?

This online course offers dental professionals a vital opportunity to understand and combat the growing global threat of antibiotic resistance. Designed for the entire dental team, it highlights how their daily practices can make a significant difference in preserving the effectiveness of these crucial medications.

## Key takeaways

*   A comprehensive three-week online program focused on antimicrobial resistance in dentistry.
*   Developed by the British Society for Antimicrobial Chemotherapy and the FDI World Dental Federation.
*   Requires approximately one hour of study per week, allowing for flexible learning.
*   Participants can earn a digital certificate upon successful completion of the course requirements.

## Course Overview

Antibiotics are essential for treating bacterial infections, but their overuse and misuse have led to a critical global health challenge: antimicrobial resistance (AMR). This three-week online course, expertly crafted by the British Society for Antimicrobial Chemotherapy and the FDI World Dental Federation, specifically addresses the role of dental teams in tackling this issue. Led by Professor Wendy Thompson, the program emphasizes that effective antibiotics must remain available when truly needed.

Throughout the course, you will explore the worldwide impact of antibiotic resistance, understand how resistant infections affect dental practice, and learn why collaborative efforts within a dental team are crucial for effective AMR combat.

## Learning Structure and Format

The course is delivered entirely online through a mix of video and text-based materials, designed for self-paced learning. Each week requires about one hour of effort, making it manageable for busy professionals. You'll have access to all course materials and can test your understanding with included assessments. The platform also encourages discussion, allowing you to engage with other learners and share insights.

This program is structured into three main weeks:

### Week 1: Antibiotic Resistance: How is dentistry a part of the global problem?

*   Welcome and introduction to the course.
*   Understanding the fundamental problem of Antibiotic Resistance (ABR).
*   Summary of Week 1 topics.

Subsequent weeks delve deeper into the specific impacts on dentistry and practical strategies for the dental team.

## Who Is This Course For?

This intermediate-level course is specifically designed for all members of the dental team, both clinical and non-clinical. This includes:

*   Dentists
*   Dental Assistants
*   Dental Surgeons

It is also beneficial for other healthcare professionals who encounter patients presenting with dental pain or abscesses, providing them with a deeper understanding of responsible antibiotic stewardship in a dental context.

Upon completion, you will be able to explain the global issue of ABR, summarize its impact on dentistry, reflect on why it affects everyone, investigate risks to patients, and determine your personal contribution to fighting ABR. You will also understand the importance of local solutions to global problems and how they involve the entire dental team and beyond.
