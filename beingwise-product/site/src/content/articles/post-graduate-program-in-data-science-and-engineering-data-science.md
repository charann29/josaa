# Post Graduate Program in Data Science and Engineering (Data Science Specialization) by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring data scientists, the Post Graduate Program in Data Science and Engineering (Data Science Specialization) offered by Great Learning provides a comprehensive pathway into this in-demand field. This program is designed to equip learners with essential skills and practical experience, making it suitable for both fresh graduates and those with limited work experience.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 9-month online postgraduate program, including a one-month preparatory module.
*   The curriculum covers key areas like Python, Tableau, SQL, data analysis, and machine learning.
*   Participants engage in capstone projects across various industries and receive dedicated career support for three months post-program.
*   The total program fee is ₹2,75,000 plus applicable GST, with EMI options available.

## Program Overview and Structure

The Post Graduate Program in Data Science and Engineering is a 9-month certification course meticulously developed by Great Lakes Executive Learning. It aims to provide participants with a strong foundation and advanced knowledge in data science. The program begins with a crucial one-month preparatory module, ensuring all learners are up to speed with fundamental concepts before diving into more complex topics.

Throughout the program, students will benefit from a blend of self-study, virtual classroom sessions, and video-based learning, with classes typically held on weekends. This flexible structure allows individuals to balance their studies with other commitments. A significant component of the program involves working on capstone projects across more than 15 domains, guided by industry experts. These projects offer invaluable hands-on experience, allowing participants to solve real-world business problems using data science techniques.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to build skills progressively, starting from foundational tools and moving to advanced analytical techniques. Key learning areas include:

*   **Preparatory Module:** Introduction to programming tools such as Python and data visualization with Tableau.
*   **Foundational Module:** Deep dive into data analysis, statistical decision-making methodologies, and SQL programming for database management.
*   **Advanced Module:** Comprehensive coverage of machine learning techniques and their practical application in various projects.

Beyond theoretical knowledge, the program emphasizes practical application through quizzes, assignments, case studies, and lab sessions. This ensures participants not only understand concepts but can also implement them effectively.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission to this program, candidates must meet specific academic and professional requirements:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants should either be in their final semester of graduation or have recently completed their degree. A mandatory requirement is a minimum of 60% marks in their 10th, 12th, and undergraduate examinations.
*   **Work Experience:** The program is ideal for individuals with zero to a maximum of three years of work experience, making it suitable for both freshers and early-career professionals eager to transition into data science.

Upon successful completion of all training programs, quizzes, case studies, project reports, and assignments, participants will receive a Post Graduate Program in Data Science and Engineering certification from Great Lakes Executive Learning.

## Program Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Post Graduate Program in Data Science and Engineering is ₹2,75,000, in addition to applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST). To make the program accessible, Great Lakes Executive Learning has collaborated with financial partners like Propelld and Liquiloans to offer convenient payment solutions, including EMI options starting from approximately ₹11,029 per month. For precise details on the fee structure and payment plans, it is advisable to consult the official program brochure or admissions team.

## Career Support and Outcomes

One of the standout features of this program is its dedicated career support, which extends for three months after the formal program concludes. This support is designed to help participants secure desirable jobs in the market. It includes:

*   Mentorship for interviews.
*   Assistance with personalized resume building.
*   Access to a network of over 500 hiring partners.

This comprehensive support aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry placement, providing participants with the necessary guidance to launch their careers successfully in data science.

This program offers a robust foundation and practical skills for a rewarding career in data science, backed by strong industry connections and dedicated career assistance.
