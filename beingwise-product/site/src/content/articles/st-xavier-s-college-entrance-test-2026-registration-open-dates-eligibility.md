# St. Xavier's College Entrance Test 2026: Registration Open, Dates, Eligibility, Pattern, Syllabus

St. Xavier's College, Mumbai, is a highly respected institution, and gaining admission to its Bachelor of Commerce (Management Studies) program is a significant achievement. The college conducts its own entrance examination, the XET, to assess candidates' readiness for this rigorous undergraduate course.

## Key Takeaways

*   The XET 2026 is an online, objective-type examination for the B.Com (Management Studies) program.
*   It features multiple-choice questions across subjects like General Knowledge, Mathematics, English, and Reasoning.
*   There is no negative marking, encouraging candidates to attempt all questions.
*   The exam is designed to evaluate both general aptitude and specific subject understanding.

## Understanding the XET 2026 Examination Pattern

The XET 2026 for the B.Com in Management Studies (formerly known as BMS) program is structured to evaluate a candidate's comprehensive abilities. It's a computer-based test conducted entirely in English.

Here's a breakdown of the exam's key features:

| Aspect | Details |
| :-------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Total Marks | 100 |
| Language Medium | English only |
| Marking Scheme | +1 mark for each correct answer; no negative marking |
| Duration | 2 hours |
| Course | B.Com in Management Studies |
| Question Type | Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) |
| Mode of Examination | Online (Computer-Based Test) |

The examination is divided into several sections, designed to test a broad range of skills essential for management studies.

## XET 2026 Subject Areas and Weightage

The entrance test covers a diverse range of subjects, each with a specific weightage, indicating its importance in the overall score calculation. Candidates should prepare accordingly to maximize their performance across all sections.

### Subject Breakdown:

*   **General Knowledge/General Awareness & Current Affairs:** This section assesses your awareness of national and international events, as well as static general knowledge. It accounts for 30% of the total marks.
*   **Mathematics & Data Interpretation:** This part tests your numerical ability, problem-solving skills, and your capacity to interpret data presented in various formats. This section also holds a significant weight of 30%.
*   **Logical/Visual Reasoning and Critical Analysis:** Here, your ability to think logically, identify patterns, solve puzzles, and analyze situations critically is evaluated. This section contributes 25% to your total score.
*   **English Language & Comprehension Skills:** This section measures your proficiency in English, including grammar, vocabulary, and reading comprehension. It carries a weightage of 15%.

### Sectional Weightage at a Glance:

| Subject Area | Weightage |
| :---------------------------------------------------- | :--------- |
| General Knowledge/General Awareness & Current Affairs | 30% |
| Mathematics and Data Interpretation | 30% |
| Logical/Visual Reasoning and Critical Analysis | 25% |
| English Language & Comprehension Skills | 15% |

## Eligibility and Application Process for XET 2026

While specific dates and detailed eligibility criteria for 2026 will be officially announced closer to the application period, prospective students should generally meet the academic requirements set by St. Xavier's College. Typically, this involves having completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination with a certain minimum percentage from a recognized board. It is crucial to refer to the official St. Xavier's College admissions portal for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding eligibility, application deadlines, and the registration process once it opens.

## Preparing for the XET 2026

Effective preparation for the XET involves a strategic approach. Given the diverse subject areas, it's advisable to dedicate time to each section based on its weightage. Regular practice with sample questions, staying updated on current affairs, and refining your logical and analytical thinking skills will be beneficial. Focusing on strengthening your fundamentals in mathematics and enhancing your English language proficiency will also be key to performing well in this competitive examination.

Staying informed through the official St. Xavier's College website will be essential for all aspiring candidates.
