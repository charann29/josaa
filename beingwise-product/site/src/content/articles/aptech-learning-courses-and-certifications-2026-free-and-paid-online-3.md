# Aptech Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Aptech Learning Courses

Aptech Learning provides a range of career-focused courses designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills across various domains like banking, finance, hardware, networking, and information technology. These programs aim to prepare individuals for employment by offering access to course materials and opportunities to engage with industry experts.

## Key takeaways

*   Aptech Learning offers specialized certificate programs in high-demand sectors such as E-commerce, Banking, and Accounting.
*   Course durations vary, from 15 months for accounting to 23 months for e-commerce, with corresponding fee structures.
*   The curriculum emphasizes practical, job-oriented skills, preparing students for immediate entry into their chosen fields.
*   Programs are structured to provide both foundational knowledge and advanced techniques, often including modules on communication and compliance.

## ACCP Pro – E-commerce Course

The ACCP Pro – E-commerce course from Aptech Learning is a comprehensive program designed to introduce learners to the core concepts and practical implementation of E-commerce. This 23-month certificate course aims to provide a detailed understanding of the E-commerce landscape.

Students enrolled in this program will receive training in several key areas:

*   Web productivity tools
*   Logic building and programming fundamentals
*   Development of modern websites
*   Elementary programming skills
*   E-commerce implementation strategies

The curriculum is structured to ensure that participants gain the necessary professional skills and expertise to thrive in the E-commerce industry, enabling them to pursue various career opportunities effectively. The fee for this extensive program is ₹77,400.

**Skills Covered:** SQL knowledge, automation skills, taxation knowledge, financial knowledge, CSS, Java.

## Banking Specialist Certification

Recognizing the rapid growth of the Indian banking sector, Aptech Learning offers the Banking Specialist certification to prepare individuals for a career in this dynamic industry. This program focuses on providing fundamental knowledge of banking and finance.

This 16-month certificate course is divided into three parts, with classes held three days a week for two hours each. The structure ensures that learners acquire specialized knowledge across different areas of banking. The fee for the Banking Specialist program is ₹54,000.

**Skills Covered:** Comprehensive knowledge of banking operations and principles.

## Aptech Certified Accounts Professional

The Aptech Certified Accounts Professional program is an intensive 15-month training course designed to make candidates job-ready in the field of accounting. The curriculum is divided into two terms, each covering a broad set of modules essential for modern accounting practices.

Upon completion, participants will possess expertise in:

*   Basic computer applications crucial for business accounting
*   Proficiency in Tally software
*   Advanced topics such as business communication, statutory compliance, TDS, GST, and direct taxation

This course aims to equip students with the latest accounting techniques, financial principles, banking knowledge, and taxation skills, ensuring they are well-prepared for immediate placement. The fee for the Aptech Certified Accounts Professional program is ₹70,000.

**Skills Covered:** Accounting proficiency, business management, complex transaction handling.

Aptech Learning's commitment to employment-driven education ensures that all its programs are tailored to meet industry demands, providing students with practical skills and knowledge for a successful career start.
