# CGCUET 2026 Exam: Registrations (Ongoing), Eligibility, Date, Syllabus, Results

The CGCUET is a significant scholarship examination for students aspiring to pursue undergraduate degrees across various disciplines at Chandigarh Group of Colleges (CGC) Mohali. It offers financial assistance based on your performance, making higher education more accessible.

## Key Takeaways

*   CGCUET 2026 registrations are currently open for students seeking financial aid for UG programs.
*   The scholarship exam must be scheduled within 15 days of your online registration.
*   It is conducted twice a year, offering multiple opportunities for aspiring students.
*   The scholarship is available for a wide range of undergraduate courses, including Engineering, Management, Commerce, Law, Computer Applications, Journalism, and Science.

## What is CGCUET?

The CGCUET, or Chandigarh Group of Colleges Undergraduate Entrance Test, is primarily a scholarship assessment designed to support students financially in their higher education journey at CGC Mohali. It is exclusively for undergraduate courses, and the amount of scholarship awarded depends on the marks you achieve in the exam. This initiative aims to make quality education in fields like Engineering, Management, Commerce, Law, Computer Applications, Journalism, and Science more affordable for deserving candidates.

## Important Dates for CGCUET 2026

Staying informed about the key dates is crucial for a smooth application process. Registrations for CGCUET 2026 began on November 1, 2025, and are expected to continue until March 31, 2026. Once registered, candidates must schedule their exam within 15 days. The specific last date for upcoming exam phases will be announced by the Chandigarh Group of Colleges.

## CGCUET 2026 Application Process

To apply for the CGCUET 2026 scholarship exam, candidates must complete the application form online. It's essential to register before the stated deadline to be eligible to appear for the exam and be considered for financial assistance. Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria before proceeding with your application.

## Eligibility Criteria and Exam Pattern

Candidates must have successfully completed their Class 12th examination from a recognized board to be eligible for the CGCUET scholarship exam. The exam is conducted by the Chandigarh Group of Colleges and is held twice a year. The registration fee for the CGCUET exam is INR 1000. Further details on the syllabus and specific exam pattern will be made available by the Chandigarh Group of Colleges on their official portal.

## CGCUET 2026 Results and Scholarship

The CGCUET is specifically designed to provide financial assistance for admission to various undergraduate programs. The scholarship amount is determined by the candidate's performance in the examination. Detailed information regarding the result declaration and scholarship distribution process will be communicated by the Chandigarh Group of Colleges following the examination.

Prepare well and seize this opportunity to secure financial assistance for your undergraduate studies at CGC Mohali.
