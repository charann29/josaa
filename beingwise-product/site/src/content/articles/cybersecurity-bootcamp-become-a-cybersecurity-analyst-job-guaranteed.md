# Cybersecurity Bootcamp: Become a Cybersecurity Analyst. Job guaranteed.

Embark on a journey to become a certified Cybersecurity Analyst through a comprehensive bootcamp designed to equip you with essential skills and a job guarantee. This program offers intensive training and mentorship, preparing you for a thriving career in cybersecurity.

## Key takeaways

*   **Industry-Recognized Certification:** Earn a Springboard certification alongside the globally respected CompTIA Security+ credential.
*   **Job Guarantee:** The program offers a unique six-month job guarantee, providing a full refund if you don't secure a relevant position within that timeframe.
*   **Expert-Led Learning:** Benefit from a robust curriculum curated by industry experts, featuring weekly one-on-one mentor calls and community support.
*   **Flexible Learning:** Engage in approximately 10-20 hours of self-paced study per week, complemented by virtual classroom sessions and live office hours.

## Program Structure and Learning Experience

This bootcamp is delivered through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content. The curriculum, totaling 360 hours, is enriched with high-quality cybersecurity resources from global industry experts. You'll engage in interactive learning, receive regular feedback, and participate in weekly live office hours. A dedicated student advisor will pair you with a mentor who will guide you through the course and address general queries.

Weekly one-on-one calls with your mentor are a cornerstone of the program, offering personalized project feedback and valuable industry insights. A community manager is also available to answer questions about the curriculum and the broader cybersecurity landscape.

## What You Will Learn

The program is meticulously designed to transform aspiring individuals into skilled cybersecurity professionals. Key learning objectives include:

*   **Secure Software Development:** Understand the principles of secure software development and the importance of application security.
*   **IT Project Planning:** Gain the ability to create simple and complex IT project plans.
*   **Blue Team Security Operations:** Delve into blue-team operations, covering encryption, incident response workflows, security toolsets, and procedures.
*   **Foundational Scripting:** Develop essential scripting skills using Python, crucial for cybersecurity analysis.
*   **Network Security:** Learn about network architecture, security scanning, and network hardening techniques.
*   **Assessments and Remediation:** Engage in discussions on security assessments and recommended remediation actions.

## Fees and Certification

The course fee for the Cybersecurity Bootcamp is \$11,900. However, an upfront discount brings the cost down to \$10,900. Several payment options are available to suit different financial situations:

| Payment Option      | Amount          |
| :------------------ | :-------------- |
| Upfront Discount    | \$10,900        |
| Month-to-Month      | \$1,983/month   |
| Deferred Tuition    | \$395-\$\$519/month |
| Financed Tuition Loan | \$59-\$\$183/month   |

Upon successful completion of the course, submission of your capstone project, and passing both the program's certification exam and the CompTIA Security+ exam, you will receive a globally recognized Cyber Security Bootcamp Certification with CompTIA Security+ from Springboard.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Cyber Security Bootcamp, candidates must meet specific eligibility requirements:

*   **Age:** Applicants must be 18 years or older.
*   **Education:** A bachelor's degree in any discipline is required.
*   **Soft Skills:** Candidates must demonstrate strong communication skills, professionalism, motivation, commitment to learning, and an analytical mindset. An excellent command of both written and spoken English is also essential.

This bootcamp provides a robust pathway to a rewarding career in cybersecurity, backed by expert guidance and a commitment to your success.
