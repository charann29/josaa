# Symbiosis MBA Fees: Check Campus Wise MBA Fees and Scholarships

For many aspiring management professionals in India, an MBA from Symbiosis International (Deemed University) (SIU) is a highly sought-after qualification. This page offers a clear breakdown of the MBA fees across various Symbiosis institutes and details the available scholarship opportunities.

## Key takeaways

*   The average fee for an MBA program at Symbiosis institutes is approximately INR 15 lakh, payable in installments.
*   Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM) Pune offers specific MBA programs with varying fee structures.
*   Several scholarships and financial aid options are available for eligible students across different Symbiosis campuses.
*   Fees can differ by program and campus, so it's essential to check the specific institute's details.

## Symbiosis Pune MBA Fees

SIBM Pune, consistently ranked among the top MBA institutions in India according to the NIRF rankings, offers a range of MBA programs. The fees for these programs generally reflect the comprehensive full-time nature of the courses. Below are the first-year fees for the Master of Business Administration (MBA) and MBA (Innovation and Entrepreneurship) programs for the 2025-26 academic year.

| Programme Fees | 1st Year Academic Year 2025-26 (Amount in INR) |
| :----------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------- |
| **Master of Business Administration (MBA)**      |                                              |
| Academic Fees (Per Annum)                        | INR 12,45,000                                |
| Institute Deposit (Refundable)                   | INR 20,000                                   |
| **Master of Business Administration (Innovation and Entrepreneurship)** |                                              |
| Academic Fees (Per Annum)                        | INR 8,86,000                                 |
| Institute Deposit (Refundable)                   | INR 20,000                                   |

*Please note that these figures are for the 2025-26 academic year and may be subject to change in subsequent years.* Hostels and other living expenses are typically separate from these academic fees.

## Campus-Wise Symbiosis MBA Fees

While SIBM Pune often garners significant attention, SIU comprises several other constituent institutes offering diverse and specialized MBA programs. Each institute has its own fee structure, which can vary based on the specialization and campus location. Aspirants are advised to consult the official websites of the specific Symbiosis institutes they are interested in for the most current and detailed fee information.

## Symbiosis MBA Fee Refund Policy

Symbiosis International (Deemed University) has a clear fee refund policy. Students considering withdrawal from a program should familiarize themselves with these guidelines. The specifics of the refund policy, including deadlines and the percentage of fees refundable, are typically outlined in detail on the official SIU admissions portal.

## Symbiosis MBA Scholarships and Financial Assistance

SIU is committed to supporting deserving students through various scholarships and financial aid schemes. These scholarships aim to make quality management education accessible to a wider range of students. Some of the notable scholarships offered under Symbiosis International (Deemed University) for eligible students include:

*   **Late Jayatee Deshmukh Scholarship:** Awarded to female toppers in the SNAP/SET examinations.
*   **Merit Scholarship Scheme for Students from North-East Region (NER):** Designed to support students from the North-Eastern states of India.
*   **International Scholarships:** Available for both postgraduate and undergraduate international students.
*   **Dr. Anandi Gopal Scholarship Scheme:** Specific to certain constituent colleges, such as Symbiosis Medical College for Women (SMCW).
*   **Merit Scholarship for Semester Topper:** Recognizes academic excellence among postgraduate and undergraduate students.
*   **Merit Scholarship for IIT Graduates:** Offered to IIT graduates enrolling in SIU's MBA programs.
*   **SIBM Merit Scholarship:** An institute-specific scholarship at SIBM Pune.

For a complete list of scholarships, eligibility criteria, and application procedures, students should refer to the official SIU scholarship portal or the specific institute's website.

Planning your MBA journey involves understanding both the academic requirements and the financial commitment. By researching the fees and exploring the available scholarships, you can make an informed decision about pursuing your management education at Symbiosis.
