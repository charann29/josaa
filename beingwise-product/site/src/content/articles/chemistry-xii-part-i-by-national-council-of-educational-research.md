# Chemistry XII Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This course offers Class 12 students a comprehensive online learning experience in Chemistry Part-I, developed by the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) and delivered through the Swayam platform. It's an excellent opportunity to strengthen foundational chemistry concepts crucial for academic success.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is a 24-week, self-paced online program focusing on Class 12 Chemistry Part-I topics.
*   It offers a certificate from NCERT and Swayam upon successful completion of assessments.
*   Learning materials include video lectures, e-texts, web links, and interactive discussion forums.
*   The course covers nine essential chapters, from Solid State to Coordination Compounds.

## Course Overview

The Chemistry XII Part-I online course is designed specifically for students in Class 12. Spanning 24 weeks, this program delves into key chemistry principles, guided by subject matter experts. Dr. Alka Mehrotra from DESM, NCERT, New Delhi, coordinates the course, ensuring high-quality academic content.

The learning methodology combines diverse resources such as informative video lectures, supplementary e-texts, and relevant web links to elaborate on complex concepts. Students can also engage in online sessions and discussion forums for a more interactive learning experience. Successful completion of the course, including all assessments, leads to a certificate jointly issued by NCERT and Swayam.

## What You Will Learn

This course is structured to provide a deep understanding of core Class 12 Chemistry topics. Upon finishing the program, participants will have enhanced their knowledge across several critical areas, including:

*   The Solid State
*   Solutions
*   Electrochemistry
*   Chemical Kinetics
*   Surface Chemistry
*   General Principles and Processes of Isolation of Elements
*   The p-Block Elements
*   The d- and f-Block Elements
*   Coordination Compounds

## Eligibility and Certification

This course is primarily for Class 12 students who wish to deepen their understanding of Chemistry. To earn the course certificate, students must achieve a minimum score of 70% or higher in the final assessment. The certificate is a testament to their successful completion and understanding of the course material, provided by NCERT through the Swayam platform.

## How to Enroll

To enroll in the Chemistry XII Part-I Certification Course, prospective students should visit the official Swayam portal for this specific program. Look for the “Sign-in” or “Register” option, typically located in the top right corner of the page. You will then be prompted to sign in with an existing account or create a new one to proceed with enrollment.
