# IPU CET Exam 2026: Application Form (Extended), Exam Date (Out), Pattern & Syllabus

For students aspiring to join Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) for various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, the Indraprastha University Common Entrance Test (IPU CET) is a key step. This university-level entrance examination is your gateway to a wide array of courses offered by GGSIPU and its affiliated institutions.

## Key Takeaways

*   The application deadline for IPU CET 2026 has been extended until April 11.
*   A single, non-refundable fee of INR 2,500 covers both registration and counselling for all candidates.
*   IPU CET 2026 examinations are scheduled to take place between April 25 and May 17.
*   The exam will be conducted in a hybrid mode, offering flexibility.

## Understanding IPU CET

IPU CET, or Indraprastha University Common Entrance Test, is administered by Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University to facilitate admissions into a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. These include courses like BBA, BCA, BJMC, B.A., M.A., B.Sc., M.Sc., BHMCT, and specific B.Tech (Biotech) programs. It's important to note that for some programs, admissions are based on scores from national-level tests conducted by other agencies, rather than IPU CET.

## Application Process and Important Dates

The application window for IPU CET 2026 has been extended, giving prospective students more time to complete their registration. The final date to submit your application is April 11. To apply, candidates must visit the official GGSIPU website. It's crucial to ensure you meet the specific eligibility criteria for your chosen course before applying.

All applicants are required to pay a consolidated fee of INR 2,500. This fee covers both the initial registration for the entrance test and participation in the subsequent counselling process, simplifying the application journey.

### Examination Schedule

The IPU CET 2026 examinations are slated to be conducted from April 25 to May 17. The university will hold these tests in a hybrid format, combining online and offline elements. Successful completion of the entrance test is mandatory for candidates seeking admission through IPU CET.

## Programs Offered Through IPU CET

GGSIPU offers a vast selection of courses through the IPU CET. This entrance exam is primarily for admissions to various undergraduate and postgraduate degrees. Some popular programs include:

*   Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
*   Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
*   Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)
*   Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
*   Bachelor of Journalism and Mass Communication (BJMC)
*   Bachelor of Hotel Management and Catering Technology (BHMCT)
*   Master of Arts (M.A.)
*   Master of Science (M.Sc.)
*   M.Tech
*   B.Tech (Biotechnology)

For detailed information on specific program offerings and their respective eligibility, candidates should refer to the official GGSIPU admission brochure.

## Post-Exam Procedures: Counselling

Following the entrance examination, Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University will conduct a comprehensive counselling process for all candidates who successfully qualify for the IPU CET. This counselling stage is where qualified students will be allocated seats in their chosen programs based on their performance and preferences.
