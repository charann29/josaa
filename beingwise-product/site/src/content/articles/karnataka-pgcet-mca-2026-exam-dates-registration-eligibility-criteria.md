# Karnataka PGCET MCA 2026 Exam: Dates, Registration, Eligibility Criteria, Syllabus, Results

The Karnataka Post Graduate Common Entrance Test (PGCET) is a state-level examination conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) for admission to various postgraduate programs, including the Master of Computer Applications (MCA). This page focuses specifically on the MCA program, providing essential details for prospective students.

## Key takeaways

*   The Karnataka PGCET is conducted annually by the KEA for MCA and other postgraduate courses.
*   Registration for the 2026 exam has been extended, with the final application deadline set for April 20, 2026.
*   The MCA entrance exam is scheduled for May 24, 2026.
*   Eligibility generally requires a relevant bachelor's degree.

## Important Dates for Karnataka PGCET MCA 2026

Staying informed about key dates is crucial for a smooth application process. The KEA has announced the following schedule for the Karnataka PGCET 2026, including important dates for MCA aspirants:

*   **Online Registration Deadline:** April 20, 2026
*   **Last Date for Fee Payment:** April 22, 2026
*   **Exam Date (MBA & MCA):** May 24, 2026
*   **Exam Date (M.Tech Courses):** May 23, 2026

Candidates should regularly check the official KEA portal for any updates or changes to this schedule.

## Registration Process and Fees

To apply for the Karnataka PGCET MCA 2026, candidates must complete the online registration process through the official KEA website, cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea/pgcet2026. The application window has been extended, giving applicants more time to submit their forms. 

### Steps to Apply:

1.  Visit the official KEA website.
2.  Locate the PGCET 2026 registration link.
3.  Fill out the application form with accurate personal and academic details.
4.  Upload any required documents, such as photographs and signatures.
5.  Pay the application fee online before the deadline. The fee payment deadline is April 22, 2026.

It is vital to ensure all information is correct before final submission, as corrections might be limited or not possible later.

## Eligibility Criteria for MCA

Candidates aspiring for MCA admission through Karnataka PGCET 2026 must meet specific eligibility requirements. While detailed criteria are available on the official KEA website, the general requirement is holding a relevant bachelor's degree. This typically means a degree with mathematics at the 10+2 level or at the graduation level. Prospective applicants should carefully review the official information to confirm their eligibility before applying.

## Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The Karnataka PGCET MCA exam is designed to assess a candidate's aptitude for the program. The examination is conducted offline and consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs).

### Exam Pattern Highlights:

*   **Total Questions:** 100 MCQs
*   **Total Time:** 150 minutes (2 hours 30 minutes)
*   **Marking Scheme:** One mark is awarded for each correct answer.
*   **Medium:** English

The syllabus for the MCA exam typically covers areas such as Mathematics, Computer Awareness, General Intelligence, and General Knowledge. The KEA releases the detailed syllabus on its official website, which is an indispensable resource for preparation. Candidates are strongly advised to refer to the official syllabus to understand the topics and sub-topics that will be tested.

## Results and Counselling

After the examination, the KEA will announce the Karnataka PGCET results. The rank list is prepared based on the marks secured by candidates in the entrance test. In cases where two or more candidates have the same score, tie-breaking rules are applied. These usually involve considering the aggregate marks in the qualifying examination, followed by the age of the candidates, with older candidates given preference.

Successful candidates will then participate in the centralized counselling process conducted by the KEA. This process determines seat allocation in various colleges across Karnataka offering MCA programs based on ranks, preferences, and availability. Detailed information regarding results and counselling will be published on the official KEA portal post-examination.

Preparing thoroughly and staying updated with official announcements will significantly aid your journey towards securing an MCA seat through Karnataka PGCET 2026.
