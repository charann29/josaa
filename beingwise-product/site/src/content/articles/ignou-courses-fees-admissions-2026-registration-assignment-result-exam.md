# IGNOU: Courses, Fees, Admissions 2026, Registration, Assignment, Result, Exam Form

Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) stands as a beacon of accessible higher education in India, offering a vast array of programs through distance learning. Established in 1985, this public university has grown to serve millions of students both within India and internationally, providing flexible learning opportunities for diverse educational needs.

## Key takeaways

*   IGNOU offers over 200 undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, certificate, and doctoral programs.
*   Admissions are conducted twice a year, in January and July cycles, primarily based on merit.
*   The university is accredited with an A++ grade by NAAC, reflecting its commitment to quality education.
*   IGNOU has been recognized as the top 'Open University' in India in the NIRF 2025 rankings.

## Admissions 2026: Process and Deadlines

For the academic year 2026, IGNOU admissions are currently open for various courses. Prospective students can submit their applications online through the official IGNOU website. The university follows a dual admission cycle, allowing candidates to enroll either in the January or July session, offering flexibility for aspiring learners. Most admissions are merit-based, considering academic performance in previous qualifications.

### Popular Programs and Eligibility

IGNOU provides a wide range of programs, with popular choices including Bachelor of Arts (BA), Bachelor of Science (BSc), Master of Arts (MA), and Master of Science (MSc). Additionally, specialized programs like B.Ed and M.Ed are also offered, with specific entrance exam requirements and eligibility criteria detailed on the official IGNOU portal. For example, the B.Ed program typically requires a bachelor's degree with a certain percentage and may involve an entrance test.

## Course Fees and Structure

The fee structure at IGNOU is designed to be affordable, making higher education accessible to a broader population. Fees vary significantly depending on the program chosen. For instance, an MBA from IGNOU has its own fee structure, eligibility, and specialization options. Detailed fee information for each course is available on the official IGNOU website. Students should check the specific program prospectus for exact figures.

## Registration, Assignments, and Examinations

Upon successful admission, students complete their course registration. A crucial part of the learning process at IGNOU involves submitting assignments, which contribute to the final evaluation. Students are required to submit these assignments by specified deadlines for each academic session. 

### Exam Forms and Results

Before term-end examinations, students must fill out and submit the official exam form. Information regarding exam dates, hall tickets, and results is regularly updated on the official IGNOU website. Students can check their admission status, assignment status, and examination results through the dedicated portals provided by the university.

## Rankings and Accreditation

IGNOU is a well-regarded institution in the field of distance education. It holds an A++ accreditation from NAAC, signifying high standards in its educational offerings. In a notable achievement, the official NIRF 2025 rankings recognized IGNOU as the No. 1 institution under the newly introduced 'Open Universities' category, further cementing its position as a leader in flexible learning. 

IGNOU continues to empower individuals across the nation and beyond with quality education, fostering skill development and academic growth through its accessible and well-structured programs.
