# Certified Brand Manager by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals aiming to build a strong foundation in brand management, the Certified Brand Manager course by Vskills offers a comprehensive learning experience. This program is designed to enhance your understanding of brand strategy, development, and protection in today's competitive market.

## Key takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Online Learning:** The course is delivered entirely online, providing flexibility for self-paced study through video and text-based materials.
*   **Government-Recognised Certification:** Vskills is a certification body that aims to boost employability, and this certification is valued across the industry.
*   **Affordable Fees:** The program is priced at an accessible ₹3,499, inclusive of all taxes.
*   **Lifetime Access:** Once enrolled, you gain lifetime access to the learning materials, allowing you to revisit concepts whenever needed.

## Course Overview and Objectives

This Vskills certification is tailored to equip learners with essential skills in brand management, digital marketing, and developing consumer-insight driven strategies. It's particularly suitable for graduates and individuals aspiring to careers in management, offering a deep dive into the fundamentals of brand building and the entire brand management process.

Participants will learn about various critical aspects, including brand planning, enhancing brand potential, leveraging social media for branding, brand positioning, evaluation techniques, and navigating the legal landscape of brand protection. The ultimate goal is to enable you to craft effective brand strategies that attract new opportunities and solidify brand success.

## Eligibility and Certification Process

To earn the Certified Brand Manager certificate, candidates must successfully complete an online test conducted by Vskills. This assessment consists of 50 multiple-choice questions, with each correct answer earning one mark. There is no negative marking for incorrect answers. To qualify for the certification, candidates need to achieve a minimum score of 25 marks.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a thorough understanding of various brand management concepts. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Defining brands and understanding their core characteristics.
*   Strategies for nurturing strong brand-customer relationships.
*   Developing effective brand symbols and characters for robust brand building.
*   Techniques for creating compelling brand names and managing brand extensions.
*   Mastering perceptual mapping for strategic brand positioning.

## Program Offerings and Benefits

The Vskills Certified Brand Manager course provides several valuable resources to support your learning journey:

*   **Online Learning Platform:** Access to an intuitive online portal for all course content.
*   **E-learning Modules:** Engaging video and text-based lessons.
*   **Practice Tests:** Tools to help you prepare for the certification exam.
*   **Hard Copy Study Material:** Physical study materials delivered to you.
*   **Industry-Valued Certification:** A certificate that is recognised by employers.
*   **Profile Tagging:** Your profile will be tagged on job portals like Monster and Shine, enhancing your visibility to potential employers.

## How to Apply

To register for the Certified Brand Manager course by Vskills, follow these simple steps:

1.  Visit the official Vskills website for the Brand Manager certification.
2.  Locate and click the "Buy Now" button for the course.
3.  You will be directed to the checkout page. If you don't already have one, you will need to create an account with Vskills.
4.  After successfully creating your account, proceed to select your preferred payment method.
5.  Review your order summary to ensure all details are correct.
6.  Confirm your order and complete the payment using your chosen method.

Upon successful payment, you will gain access to the course materials and can begin your learning journey towards becoming a certified brand manager.

This certification can be a valuable step for social media managers, brand managers, advertising managers, marketing managers, and social media specialists looking to advance their careers.
