# Karnataka PGCET Exam 2026 - Registration (Started), Date, Syllabus, Pattern, Question Paper, Admission Process

The Karnataka Post Graduate Common Entrance Test (PGCET) is your gateway to postgraduate studies in various disciplines across Karnataka. Conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA), this exam helps students secure admissions into MBA, MCA, ME, M.Tech, and M.Arch programs, including lateral entry options for MCA.

## Key Takeaways

*   The registration window for Karnataka PGCET 2026 has been extended, with the new deadline for applications set for April 20, 2026.
*   Application fee payments must be completed by April 22, 2026.
*   The PGCET 2026 examination is scheduled to take place on May 23, 2026.
*   GATE-qualified candidates are exempt from the PGCET for M.Tech admissions and can apply directly based on their GATE scores.

## What is the Karnataka PGCET Exam?

The Karnataka PGCET is a crucial entrance examination for students aspiring to pursue postgraduate degrees in Karnataka. It facilitates admissions into a wide array of programs, including Master of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Computer Applications (MCA), Master of Engineering (ME), Master of Technology (M.Tech), and Master of Architecture (M.Arch). These programs are offered by various government, university, private aided, and private non-aided institutions throughout the state.

### Eligibility and Application

Before applying, candidates must carefully review the Karnataka PGCET 2026 eligibility criteria, which are detailed on the official KEA portal. The application process involves filling out the online form and submitting the required fee. The registration link is active on the official KEA website, cetonline.karnataka.gov.in.

## Exam Pattern and Syllabus

Understanding the exam structure and content is vital for effective preparation. The Karnataka PGCET is a two-hour examination consisting of 100 multiple-choice questions (MCQs). Each correct answer fetches one mark, and importantly, there is no negative marking for incorrect responses. Candidates are strongly encouraged to familiarize themselves with the official syllabus and practice with previous years' question papers to enhance their preparation.

## Important Dates for Karnataka PGCET 2026

Here’s a quick overview of key dates for the Karnataka PGCET 2026:

| Event                          | Date                |
| :----------------------------- | :------------------ |
| Registration Deadline Extended | April 20, 2026      |
| Application Fee Payment Due    | April 22, 2026      |
| Examination Date               | May 23, 2026        |

*Please note that these dates are subject to change by the Karnataka Examinations Authority. Always refer to the official KEA announcements for the most current information.*

## Admission Process

Candidates who qualify in the Karnataka PGCET will be eligible for the subsequent counseling rounds. The admission process typically involves online registration for counseling, document verification, choice filling for preferred colleges and courses, seat allotment based on merit and preferences, and finally, reporting to the allotted college to complete admission formalities. For M.Tech programs, GATE-qualified candidates have a direct application route based on their GATE scores and are not required to appear for the PGCET.

Prepare thoroughly and stay updated with official announcements from the Karnataka Examinations Authority to navigate your postgraduate admission journey successfully.
