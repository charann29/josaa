# Australasian Veterinary Examination (AVE) Preparation Course by Academically: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For veterinarians qualified outside Australia and New Zealand, passing the Australasian Veterinary Examination (AVE) is a crucial step to practicing in these countries. Academically offers a comprehensive preparation course designed to help you confidently clear this exam and transition into the Australian veterinary sector.

## Key takeaways

*   This course runs for four months and is offered by Academically, a respected name in healthcare education.
*   It combines live virtual classes with recorded sessions, providing flexibility for busy professionals.
*   The curriculum is extensive, covering all essential veterinary topics and clinical skills required for the AVE.
*   Participants benefit from expert instruction, mock tests, and personalised feedback to enhance their preparation.

## What the Course Offers

Academically's AVE preparation course is structured to provide a thorough understanding of veterinary science as it applies to the Australasian context. The learning format is a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based materials, all delivered in English.

The program's highlights include:

*   **Four-month duration:** A focused timeframe for intensive preparation.
*   **Expert Instructors:** Learn from experienced professionals in the field.
*   **120 hours of recorded live sessions:** Revisit complex topics at your convenience.
*   **Over 1000 recall questions:** Practice extensively with questions mirroring the exam format.
*   **One-on-one feedback:** Receive personalised guidance on your progress.
*   **Comprehensive syllabus coverage:** Ensure no topic is left untouched.
*   **Mock tests:** Simulate the exam environment to build confidence.
*   **CV writing and job-securing techniques:** Practical support for career transition.

## Who Should Consider This Course?

This preparation course is ideal for overseas-qualified veterinary doctors, technologists, and assistants who aspire to practice in Australia. It specifically targets professionals seeking to gain the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the AVE and smoothly integrate into the Australian veterinary landscape.

## Course Curriculum Overview

The course delves into critical areas, focusing on developing clinical competencies, enhancing veterinary knowledge, and improving communication skills essential for Australian practice. Participants will gain proficiency in diagnostic and surgical skills through interactive sessions and practical assessments.

The syllabus is divided into three main modules:

### Module 1: General Knowledge and Veterinary Science

*   Surgical, Anaesthesia, and Imaging Principles
*   Professional Practice and Public Health
*   Pharmacology, Therapeutics, and Toxicology
*   Infectious Diseases, Husbandry, and Welfare
*   Physiology

### Module 2: Application of Knowledge, Critical Thinking, and Species Covered

This module covers the application of veterinary knowledge across various animal species, including:

*   Dogs and Cats
*   Equine (horses)
*   Cattle and Other Ruminants (sheep, goats, alpacas)
*   Pigs and Poultry
*   Pocket Pets & Birds

### Module 3: Clinical Presentations Covered

Focuses on common clinical presentations and their management:

*   Jaundice, Pallor, and Bleeding
*   Dyskinesia, Constipation, and Straining (Tenesmus)
*   Vomiting and Regurgitation
*   Abdominal Pain and Distension
*   Diarrhoea
*   Fistula, Fistulae, and Abscesses
*   Skin conditions: Lumpy, Hairy, Bald, and Itchy
*   Eye conditions: Red Eye, Blindness, Sore Eye
*   Lameness

## Fees and Application Process

Academically's AVE preparation course does not offer a separate certificate upon completion; its primary goal is to prepare you for the official AVE. For detailed course fees, you will need to visit Academically's official website. The application process is straightforward:

1.  Visit the dedicated AVE course page on the Academically website.
2.  Locate and click the "Apply Now" button.
3.  Submit all the required information as prompted.
4.  Once your details are successfully submitted, you will gain access to the course materials.

This comprehensive program is your pathway to a successful veterinary career in Australia.
