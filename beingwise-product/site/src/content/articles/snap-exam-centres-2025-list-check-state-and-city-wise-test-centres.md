# SNAP Exam Centres 2025 List: Check State and City wise Test Centres

When you're preparing for the SNAP exam, knowing where you'll take the test is a crucial detail. The Symbiosis International (Deemed University) (SIU) designates specific venues across various cities for candidates to appear for this important MBA entrance examination. These centres are fully equipped with the necessary computer systems and facilities to ensure a smooth testing experience for everyone.

## Key Takeaways

*   Candidates can choose up to three preferred test cities when filling out the SNAP application form.
*   The examination authority aims to allocate a test centre in the candidate's first-choice city.
*   Once the application is submitted, there is no option to change the selected test cities or the allotted centre.
*   Reviewing your allocated centre's location on the admit card a day before the exam is highly recommended for proper travel planning.

## How to Select Your SNAP Test City

Choosing your preferred test cities is an integral part of the SNAP application process. It's a one-time decision, as changes are not permitted after submission. Follow these steps to make your selection:

1.  **Register:** Begin by registering for SNAP 2025 on the official SNAP website.
2.  **Log In:** Once your login credentials are created, access the SNAP application form.
3.  **Navigate to Test City Section:** Locate the specific section for test city preferences.
4.  **Choose Dates and Cities:** Select your desired SNAP test dates and then choose one city for each session you plan to attempt.
5.  **Complete Application:** Fill in the remaining details of the form, make the required fee payment, and then submit your application.

It is advisable to select your home city or current residence as your first preference, as the examination authorities generally try to accommodate candidates in their top-choice location.

## Important Points About SNAP Exam Centres

Understanding the guidelines regarding test centres can help you avoid any last-minute stress. The examination authority strives to ensure a fair and organized testing environment.

*   **Admit Card is Key:** Your SNAP admit card will clearly state the precise address of your allotted test centre. It's essential to download and print this document.
*   **No Changes Post-Submission:** Be aware that once your application form is submitted, requests to change your test city or centre will not be entertained.
*   **Check Location in Advance:** To prevent any inconvenience on exam day, it's wise to visit or research the exact location of your assigned centre at least a day before the examination. This allows you to plan your travel route and estimated time of arrival effectively.

## Dos and Don'ts at the SNAP Exam Centre

Adhering to the rules and regulations at the test centre is crucial for a smooth examination experience.

### Do's:

*   Carry your SNAP admit card and a valid photo ID proof (such as Aadhar card, Passport, Driving License, or PAN card).
*   Reach the test centre well in advance of the reporting time to complete verification procedures without haste.
*   Follow all instructions provided by the invigilators and centre staff.
*   Maintain decorum and a quiet environment inside the examination hall.

### Don'ts:

*   Do not carry any electronic gadgets, including mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, or any other unauthorized items into the examination hall.
*   Do not engage in any form of unfair means or malpractice; this will lead to immediate disqualification.
*   Do not try to change your seat or disturb other candidates during the exam.
*   Do not leave the examination hall without the explicit permission of the invigilator.

Preparing well for the SNAP exam also means being fully prepared for the logistics of exam day. By carefully selecting your preferred cities and understanding the centre-related guidelines, you can ensure a focused and stress-free testing experience.
