# What is Film & Video Design?: Subjects, Course Fees, Admission 2025, Career Options

Film and Video Design is a dynamic field focused on creating and integrating moving images and sound to tell stories, convey messages, and entertain. It covers a broad spectrum, from traditional filmmaking to modern digital video production.

## Key takeaways

*   Film and Video Design courses are available at undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and certificate levels.
*   Eligibility for undergraduate programs typically requires completing Class 12 from any academic stream.
*   Admission often involves both entrance exams and academic merit, with key exams including NID Entrance, UCEED, and CUET.
*   Career opportunities span various roles in film, television, animation, and digital media, with top recruiters like UTV Motion Pictures and Balaji Telefilms.

## Understanding Film and Video Design

This field delves into the art and technical aspects of visual storytelling through film and video. Students explore subjects like photography, videography, filmmaking techniques, film language, cinematography, production design, and visual effects. The curriculum is designed to equip students with both creative vision and practical skills needed in the fast-paced media industry.

### Course Levels and Duration

Film and Video Design programs are offered at various academic levels to suit different aspirations and backgrounds:

*   **Undergraduate (UG) Courses**: These typically last 3 to 4 years, leading to a bachelor's degree.
*   **Postgraduate (PG) Courses**: These are generally 2-year programs for those seeking advanced specialization after a bachelor's degree.
*   **Diploma and Certificate Programs**: Ranging from a few weeks to one year, these courses offer focused training in specific areas of film and video production.

## Eligibility Criteria for Admission 2025

To pursue a course in Film and Video Design, candidates need to meet specific educational qualifications:

*   **For Undergraduate Programs**: Applicants must have successfully completed Class 12 from any recognized board, regardless of their academic stream (Arts, Science, or Commerce).
*   **For Postgraduate Programs**: A bachelor's degree in a relevant discipline is usually required.
*   **For Diploma/Certificate Programs**: Eligibility often requires completing Class 10 with fundamental subjects.

## Admission Process and Key Entrance Exams 2025

Admission to Film and Video Design courses is typically based on a combination of entrance exam performance and academic merit. Prospective students should prepare for design-focused entrance tests.

Some of the prominent entrance exams for Film and Video Design programs include:

*   **NID Entrance Exam**: Conducted by the National Institute of Design, this is a highly respected test for design courses.
*   **NIFT Entrance Exam**: Administered by the National Institute of Fashion Technology, it also covers design disciplines.
*   **UCEED**: The Undergraduate Common Entrance Exam for Design, useful for admission to IITs and other institutes.
*   **CUET**: The Common University Entrance Test, which some universities use for design program admissions.

## Course Fees and Top Colleges

The financial commitment for Film and Video Design courses can vary significantly based on the institution, course level, and facilities offered. Fees can range from approximately INR 3,600 to INR 21.92 Lakh.

Some of the well-regarded institutions offering Film and Video Design programs include:

*   NID Ahmedabad
*   Symbiosis Institute of Design
*   World University of Design
*   MIT Institute of Design
*   UPES
*   Lovely Professional University
