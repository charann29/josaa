# Data Analytics Course by Digital Vidya: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen on mastering data, Digital Vidya offers a comprehensive Data Analytics program. This course is designed to equip learners with essential skills in data handling, analysis, and visualization, crucial for today's data-driven world.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program offers a self-paced learning model, allowing flexibility for students and working professionals.
*   It spans 16 weeks and includes practical assignments and three capstone projects for real-world experience.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive dual certification from Digital Vidya and Vskills.
*   The course fee is ₹12,500 plus GST, with potential discounts available.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This Data Analytics course blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on practice, focusing on key tools like QlikView, PowerBI, SQL, Excel, and Tableau. The curriculum covers various aspects of data management, including cleaning, manipulation, analysis, comparison, sorting, and filtering, with a strong emphasis on advanced Excel techniques.

The learning journey includes pre-recorded sessions delivered by industry experts, ensuring high-quality instruction. Students engage in practical assignments and gain vital real-time exposure through capstone projects. These projects simulate industry scenarios, such as risk assessment in banking, data analysis for the Indian Premier League (IPL), and data science applications in computer rental businesses. The course also features interactive training sessions, bi-monthly live Q&A panels, and access to case studies.

## Program Structure and Duration

The Data Analytics program is structured as a self-paced module, allowing learners to progress at their own convenience. The total duration of the course is 16 weeks. Students benefit from lifetime access to the learning management system (LMS) and course materials, ensuring they can revisit content whenever needed. Before committing, interested individuals have the option to audit the course through a demo session.

## Eligibility and Certification

Digital Vidya has kept the eligibility criteria for this Data Analytics training quite open. While there are no formal prerequisites, a fundamental understanding of mathematics, including statistics, algebra, calculations, and probability, is highly recommended due to the course's quantitative nature.

To earn the certification, participants must successfully complete the entire course, including all practical assignments and case studies. Upon fulfilling these requirements, learners will be awarded a dual certification from Digital Vidya and Vskills, adding significant value to their professional profiles.

## Fees and How to Enroll

The program fee for the Data Analytics course is ₹12,500, exclusive of GST. Digital Vidya occasionally offers discounts, which prospective students can inquire about. Enrollment is straightforward, typically involving an online application and payment process. For the most current details on fees and application steps, it's advisable to refer to the official Digital Vidya portal.

## Skills You Will Acquire

By the end of this certification, participants are expected to become proficient data analysts, equipped with a robust set of skills. These include expertise in using Excel and SQL for data analysis, a strong foundation in statistics for deriving insights from large datasets, and mastery of data visualization techniques using tools like Power BI and Tableau. Learners will also understand inferential and descriptive statistics and be able to build interactive dashboards.

This program is ideal for anyone looking to build a career in data analytics, from account and sales managers to supply chain professionals, seeking to leverage data for better decision-making.
