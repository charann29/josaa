# LPUNEST 2026: Slot Booking, Registration, Exam, Syllabus, Pattern, Result

The LPUNEST (Lovely Professional University National Entrance and Scholarship Test) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to join a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs at Lovely Professional University. This test serves a dual purpose: it acts as an entrance examination and also determines scholarship eligibility based on your performance.

## Key takeaways

*   LPUNEST 2026 Phase 2 applications are currently open, with April 15 as the last date to apply.
*   Candidates can book their preferred exam slots while filling out the application form.
*   The exam is offered in two modes: remote proctored (April 4-20) and test centre based (April 4-30).
*   Phase 1 results have been announced, and Phase 2 results and counselling begin on May 2, 2026.

## Understanding LPUNEST 2026

Lovely Professional University conducts the LPUNEST annually for admissions to over 150 diverse programs. Beyond being an entry ticket, your scores in LPUNEST can significantly impact the scholarship amount you receive. LPU holds a strong position in the NIRF Rankings, 2025, appearing in the Top 100 overall category, which includes prestigious institutions like IITs and Central Universities. It's also ranked 50th among government and private universities in India and holds a NAAC A++ Grade.

## Important Dates for LPUNEST 2026

Staying informed about key dates is vital for a smooth application and examination process. Here’s a summary of the ongoing and upcoming schedule for LPUNEST 2026:

## Application and Slot Booking Process

The application form for LPUNEST 2026 Phase 2 is currently available. Prospective students can complete their registration and simultaneously book their preferred examination slot. It's advisable to complete this process well before the April 15 deadline to secure a convenient slot, especially if you have specific date or time preferences. The official LPU portal provides detailed instructions for filling out the application and booking your slot.

## LPUNEST Exam Pattern and Syllabus

To prepare effectively for LPUNEST, it’s crucial to understand the examination structure and content. The test is conducted online. While specific details for each program vary, the general pattern typically includes sections on Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (or Biology for relevant programs), and English. The syllabus usually aligns with the Class 11 and 12 curriculum of major Indian boards. Candidates should refer to the official LPUNEST information booklet for the precise syllabus and exam pattern relevant to their chosen program of study.

## Results and Counselling

Phase 1 LPUNEST results have already been declared. For Phase 2, results are scheduled to be announced on May 2, 2026. Following the result declaration, the counselling process for admissions will commence on the same day. Candidates who qualify will be invited for counselling, where they can discuss program options and finalize their admission based on their rank and eligibility.

We encourage all applicants to regularly check the official LPU admissions portal for the latest updates and detailed guidelines regarding the exam, results, and counselling procedures.
