# TS ICET Hall Ticket 2026: Download TG ICET Admit Card @icet.tgche.ac.in

The Telangana State Integrated Common Entrance Test (TS ICET) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to pursue MBA or MCA programs in Telangana. Administered by the Telangana Council of Higher Education (TGCHE), the TS ICET hall ticket is an essential document for all candidates appearing for the test.

## Key takeaways

*   The TS ICET 2026 hall ticket will be available for download online on the official TGCHE website.
*   Candidates will need their registration number, qualifying exam hall ticket number, and date of birth to access their admit card.
*   The hall ticket contains vital information such as the exam date, time, test centre address, and important guidelines.
*   It is mandatory to carry a printed copy of the admit card along with a valid photo identification to the examination centre.

## How to Download Your TS ICET Hall Ticket 2026

The process for downloading the TS ICET 2026 hall ticket is straightforward and can be completed online. Follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official TS ICET website, managed by TGCHE.
2.  Look for the link specifically designated for 'TS ICET 2026 Hall Ticket Download' or 'Admit Card'.
3.  You will be prompted to enter your login credentials: your TS ICET registration number, your qualifying exam hall ticket number, and your date of birth.
4.  After entering the required details, click on the 'Submit' or 'Get Hall Ticket' button.
5.  Your TS ICET 2026 admit card will be displayed on the screen.
6.  Carefully review all the information on the hall ticket. If everything is correct, download and print multiple copies for your records and exam day.

It's recommended to use web browsers like Firefox or Chrome for a smooth download experience.

## Details on Your TS ICET Admit Card

Once you download your TS ICET 2026 hall ticket, it's crucial to verify all the information printed on it. The admit card will typically include the following details:

*   Your full name
*   Your father's name
*   Your date of birth
*   Your category (e.g., General, SC, ST, OBC)
*   The name of the examination (TS ICET 2026)
*   The date(s) and shift time(s) of your exam
*   The duration of the examination
*   The complete address of your allotted TS ICET exam centre
*   Your photograph and signature
*   Important instructions and guidelines for the exam day

Carefully check for any discrepancies. If you find any errors, contact the TS ICET helpdesk immediately for corrections.

## What to Carry to the Exam Centre

On the day of the TS ICET 2026 examination, candidates must bring specific documents to the test centre. Failing to do so may result in being denied entry. Ensure you have:

*   A hard copy (printout) of your TS ICET 2026 hall ticket.
*   A valid original photo identification proof. Acceptable IDs typically include an Aadhaar card, Passport, Driving License, PAN card, or Voter ID card.

It is advisable not to carry any electronic gadgets, study materials, or unauthorized items into the examination hall, as per the guidelines mentioned on the admit card.

The TS ICET hall ticket is your gateway to the examination, so handle it with care and ensure all details are accurate well in advance of the test date.
