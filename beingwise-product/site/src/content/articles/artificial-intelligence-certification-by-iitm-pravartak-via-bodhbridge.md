# Artificial Intelligence Certification by IITM Pravartak via BodhBridge Educational Services Private Limited (fundaspring): Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This certification program, offered in collaboration with IIT Madras Pravartak, provides an in-depth exploration of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, equipping participants with the skills to tackle real-world challenges. It's designed for both newcomers to the field and experienced professionals looking to enhance their expertise.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an online, video-based certification program with virtual classroom instruction, delivered in English.
*   The course spans six months and offers a certificate of completion recognized by IIT Madras Pravartak.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹59,000.
*   The curriculum emphasizes practical, hands-on learning through projects and lab modules.

## Course Overview and Highlights

The Artificial Intelligence Certification aims to empower individuals with essential knowledge and practical skills in AI. The program is developed in collaboration with IIT Madras Pravartak, an initiative that focuses on technology innovation and skill development.

Participants will engage with course readings and practical learning exercises throughout the program. A key benefit is the emphasis on hands-on experience, ensuring that learners can apply theoretical concepts to solve complex problems.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of the certification, participants will have a comprehensive understanding of AI fundamentals, statistical concepts, and various machine learning models. The curriculum covers both supervised and unsupervised learning techniques, including specific models like Random Forests and Logistic Regression.

The strong focus on practical application through lab modules and projects makes this course particularly suitable for:

*   Professionals looking to transition into AI careers.
*   Entrepreneurs aiming to launch AI-focused startups.
*   Individuals currently working in technology or IT roles who wish to upskill.

## Who Is This Certification For?

This program is ideal for a diverse group of professionals and aspiring AI enthusiasts. It is particularly beneficial for those in roles such as:

*   Data Scientists
*   Machine Learning Engineers
*   Artificial Intelligence Engineers
*   Business Intelligence Analysts
*   Data Analysts

The course structure is designed to cater to individuals seeking to either enter the field of AI or deepen their existing expertise.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Artificial Intelligence Certification program is ₹59,000. Candidates are required to complete the payment via the provided payment gateway during the admission process.

### Fee Structure

| Course Details                        | Total Fees |
| :------------------------------------ | :--------- |
| Artificial Intelligence Certification | ₹59,000    |

A certificate of completion is awarded by IITM Pravartak upon successfully finishing all course modules and projects.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Artificial Intelligence Certification program, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official application portal (fundaspring).
2.  Complete the online application form, providing all necessary details such as your primary email address, name, phone number, and a motivation letter.
3.  You will then be contacted with further information regarding the course.
4.  Proceed to pay the course fee through the designated payment gateway.
5.  Once payment is confirmed, you will gain access to the course materials and can begin your learning journey.

## Program Syllabus

The certification program is structured into several modules designed to build a strong foundation in AI and Machine Learning:

*   **Module 1:** Orientation
*   **Module 2:** Introduction to Python Programming
*   **Module 3:** Statistics
*   **Module 4:** Linear Algebra
*   **Module 5:** (Further modules will cover advanced AI/ML topics)

This comprehensive syllabus ensures a thorough understanding of both foundational concepts and practical applications in artificial intelligence.
