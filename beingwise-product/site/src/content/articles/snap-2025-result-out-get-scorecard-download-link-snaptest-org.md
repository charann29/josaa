# SNAP 2025 Result (OUT): Get Scorecard Download Link @snaptest.org

The results for SNAP 2025 were officially announced on January 9, 2026. Candidates who appeared for the Symbiosis National Aptitude Test can now access and download their scorecards from the official SNAPTEST website. This scorecard is essential for those aspiring to join the MBA/PGDM programs offered by Symbiosis International (Deemed University) institutes.

## Key Takeaways

*   SNAP 2025 results were declared on January 9, 2026, on the official SNAPTEST portal.
*   Candidates must log in using their credentials to download their individual scorecards.
*   A valid SNAP scorecard is mandatory for participating in the subsequent Group Exercise (GE) and Personal Interaction (PI) rounds conducted by SIU institutes.
*   The final admission process, including GE-PI rounds, typically occurs in February and March, with final selections announced by May.

## How to Download Your SNAP 2025 Scorecard

Accessing your SNAP 2025 result is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to download your scorecard from the official exam website:

1.  Visit the official SNAPTEST website.
2.  Look for the 'Download Scorecard for SNAP 2025' link on the homepage and click on it.
3.  You will be prompted to enter your User ID and Password.
4.  After entering your details, click on the 'Login' button.
5.  Your SNAP 2025 scorecard will be displayed on the screen.
6.  Download the scorecard and save it as a PDF for future reference.

## Understanding SNAP Scoring and Percentile Calculation

The SNAP exam structure allows candidates multiple attempts, and the scoring system accounts for this without normalization across attempts. Instead, a scaling-down process is used to arrive at the final score. Here’s a breakdown of the marking scheme:

*   Each correct answer fetches 1 mark.
*   For every incorrect answer, 0.25 marks are deducted.
*   The total raw score, calculated based on correct and incorrect attempts, is then scaled down to a total of 50 marks.
*   Your final percentile is determined based on this scaled-down score relative to the total number of qualifying candidates.

### SNAP Score vs. Percentile

The SNAP result is presented in the form of a percentile, which indicates how well you performed compared to other test-takers. The raw scores are converted into percentiles to provide a clear ranking. Below is an approximate conversion of SNAP scores to percentiles:

## What Happens After SNAP 2025 Results?

Once the SNAP 2025 results are out, candidates who meet the cut-off criteria set by individual SIU institutes are shortlisted for the subsequent admission rounds. These rounds typically include a Group Exercise (GE) and a Personal Interaction (PI). The selection for these stages is primarily based on your SNAP score and academic record.

### SNAP Selection Criteria and Weightage

To secure final admission into the MBA/PGDM programs at SIU institutes, shortlisted candidates must perform well in the GE-PI rounds. The final selection is based on a comprehensive evaluation, with specific weightage given to different components:

| SNAP Selection Criteria | Points |
| :---------------------- | :----- |
| SNAP Exam Score         | 50     |

| **Total**               | **100**  |

## SNAP 2025 Cut-Offs

SNAP cut-offs are the minimum percentile scores required by candidates to be eligible for the subsequent admission processes, including the personal interview and group exercise rounds.
