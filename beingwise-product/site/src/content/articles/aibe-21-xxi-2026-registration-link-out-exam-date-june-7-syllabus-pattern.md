# AIBE 21 (XXI) 2026: Registration Link (OUT), Exam Date (June 7), Syllabus, Pattern, Previous Year Question Papers

The All India Bar Examination (AIBE) is a crucial national-level certification for law graduates aspiring to practice law in India. Clearing this exam is essential to obtain your Certificate of Practice (CoP), enabling you to begin your legal career.

## Key takeaways

*   The AIBE 21 examination for 2026 is scheduled for June 7, 2026.
*   Registration for AIBE 21 is currently open and can be completed through the official AIBE portal, allindiabarexamination.com.
*   The deadline for submitting your AIBE 21 application form is April 30, 2026.
*   This exam is conducted by the Bar Council of India (BCI) once a year.

## AIBE 2026 Registration Process

The registration for AIBE 21 is entirely online. Candidates need to provide specific details on the official AIBE website. Ensure you have all necessary information ready before starting the application.

### Steps for Registration:

1.  Visit the official AIBE website.
2.  Fill in your personal details accurately.
3.  Enter your academic qualifications.
4.  Provide your State Bar Council Enrollment Number, if available.
5.  Select your preferred test cities.
6.  Choose your desired test language.

After successfully filling out the form, you will proceed to pay the application fee. It's crucial to complete this step to finalize your registration.

## AIBE 2026 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for AIBE 21, candidates must possess either a 3-year or a 5-year LLB degree from a university or institution recognized by the Bar Council of India. The AIBE is open to all law graduates who wish to practice law within the country.

## AIBE 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The AIBE is a pen-and-paper examination designed to assess a candidate's fundamental understanding of various legal subjects. It consists of 100 objective-type questions.

*   **Exam Mode:** Offline (pen-and-paper)
*   **Duration:** 3 hours (1 PM to 4 PM)
*   **Question Type:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Total Questions:** 100
*   **Subjects:** The syllabus covers a wide range of law subjects, including Constitutional Law, IPC, CrPC, CPC, Evidence Act, Family Law, Law of Contract, Specific Relief, Property Law, Negotiable Instruments Act, Environmental Law, Cyber Law, and more. Candidates are strongly advised to refer to the official AIBE syllabus PDF available on the Bar Council of India's portal for a detailed breakdown of topics.
