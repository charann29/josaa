# CAT 2025 Payment Showing Failed Despite Debit? Here's What To Do

It can be incredibly stressful when you've successfully paid your CAT 2025 application fee, only to see a 'failed' status on the official portal. This situation, while frustrating, is not uncommon and usually points to a technical delay. Here’s a guide on how to navigate this issue.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Stay Calm and Patient:** Technical glitches can cause delays in system updates, often resolving on their own.
*   **Keep All Proof:** Securely save your bank statements, transaction IDs, and any payment receipts.
*   **Re-engage Helpdesk:** Follow up with the CAT helpdesk, providing comprehensive details.
*   **Consider Escalation:** If the issue persists, direct contact with IIM Kozhikode's admissions office might be necessary.

## Understanding the Discrepancy

When your bank statement shows a successful debit to the IIM Kozhikode ICICI bank account, but the CAT portal indicates a failed transaction, it typically signifies a delay in communication between the payment gateway and the application system. Even after 48 hours, these updates can sometimes take longer to reflect accurately. The money is usually held by the payment gateway or bank and will either reconcile with the CAT system or be automatically refunded if the transaction truly failed on their end.

## Essential Steps to Take

### 1. Document Everything

Before taking any further action, ensure you have all relevant documentation readily accessible. This includes:

*   **Bank Statement:** A clear screenshot or PDF showing the debit, date, time, and recipient (IIMK ICICI bank account).
*   **Transaction ID:** The unique reference number provided by your bank or the payment gateway for the successful transaction.
*   **Receipts:** Any email confirmations or on-screen receipts you might have received immediately after payment.

Having these details at hand will make your communication with the helpdesk much more effective.

### 2. Follow Up with the CAT Helpdesk

Since you've already contacted them, it's time for a follow-up. When you reach out again, be sure to provide:

*   Your CAT 2025 Application Number.
*   The exact date and time of the payment.
*   The exact amount debited.
*   The transaction ID from your bank.
*   A clear description of the issue (payment debited, portal showing failed).
*   Attach screenshots of your bank statement showing the debit.

Also, check your email inbox (and spam/junk folders) regularly. Sometimes, the helpdesk might send an automated response with a ticket number, even if you haven't received a direct reply yet.

### 3. Consider Escalating the Issue

If, after a few more days, you still haven't received a satisfactory response from the CAT helpdesk and the status on the portal remains unchanged, it might be appropriate to escalate the matter. You can do this by directly contacting the admissions office of IIM Kozhikode, which is the conducting IIM for CAT 2025. Look for official contact details on the CAT 2025 website or the IIM Kozhikode official portal. When you contact them, provide all the same details you shared with the helpdesk, emphasizing the lack of resolution.

Remember, while frustrating, such technical hiccups are usually resolved. Your persistence and clear documentation are your best tools in getting this sorted out.
