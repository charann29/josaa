# BIEAP (Intermediate Board Exam 2026 AP): Result (April 12), Dates, Admit Card, Syllabus, Pattern

The Board of Intermediate Education, Andhra Pradesh (BIEAP) conducts the Intermediate (Class 11 and 12) examinations for students across Andhra Pradesh. These crucial exams are a gateway to higher education and play a significant role in a student's academic journey.

## Key takeaways

*   The BIEAP Intermediate exam results for 2026 are expected to be announced on April 12, 2026.
*   The theory examinations for 2026 took place between February 24 and March 23, 2026.
*   Hall tickets for the theory exams were released on February 13, 2026, while practical exam hall tickets were available from January 24, 2026.
*   Students can access their provisional results online using their hall ticket number and date of birth.

## BIEAP Intermediate Results 2026

The Andhra Pradesh Intermediate results for 2026 are highly anticipated, with an expected release date of April 12, 2026. Students can check their provisional mark sheets on the official BIEAP websites, bieap.apcfss.in and resultsbie.ap.gov.in. It is important to remember that the online result is provisional, and students must collect their official mark sheets from their respective schools.

Should a student be dissatisfied with their scores, they will have the option to apply for supplementary examinations. These supplementary exams are tentatively scheduled for May 2026, with their results expected in June 2026.

To check your results online, follow these steps:

*   Visit the official BIEAP website.
*   Look for the link related to "AP Inter Results 2026."
*   Enter your roll number and date of birth as indicated on your admit card.
*   Click "Submit" to view your results.
*   Download and save a copy of your provisional results for future reference.

## Admit Card (Hall Ticket) Information

The admit card, often referred to as the hall ticket, is a mandatory document for appearing in the BIEAP Intermediate examinations. For the 2026 exams, hall tickets for practical examinations were made available on January 24, 2026. Subsequently, the hall tickets for the theory examinations were released on February 13, 2026. Students must download and print their admit cards from the official BIEAP portal and carry them to the examination hall. Without a valid admit card, entry to the exam will not be permitted.

BIEAP periodically reviews and updates its curriculum to align with educational standards. The board had released a revised syllabus for the AP Intermediate 2027 academic year. Students preparing for the 2026 examinations should ensure they are studying according to the officially prescribed syllabus for their respective subjects.
