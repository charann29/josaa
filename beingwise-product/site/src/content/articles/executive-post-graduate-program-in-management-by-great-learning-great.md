# Executive Post Graduate Program in Management by Great Learning Great Lakes Chennai (GLIM): Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For professionals aiming to elevate their careers without pausing their current roles, the Executive Post Graduate Program in Management (ePGPM) offered by Great Learning in collaboration with Great Lakes Chennai (GLIM) presents a valuable opportunity. This one-year program is designed to equip participants with essential managerial and leadership capabilities.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 12-month, online executive program designed for both recent graduates and working professionals.
*   The curriculum aims to impart leadership and management skills, mirroring the content of a full-time PGPM.
*   The program fee is ₹2,35,000, which includes GST, with various EMI options available.
*   Participants gain practical exposure through real-world projects, expert mentorship, and career support services.

## Program Overview and Structure

The ePGPM is an intensive, year-long program delivered through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content. It allows individuals to enhance their skills and knowledge while continuing their full-time employment. The course structure is comprehensive, covering core management principles and offering specializations like Marketing Management or Data Science and Analytics.

Participants benefit from live lectures, industry exposure sessions, and real-world projects, all guided by world-class faculty and industry experts. The program emphasizes practical application, ensuring that students can immediately use their newly acquired skills in their professional lives.

## Who Should Consider This Program?

This executive program is specifically crafted for two main groups: recent graduates looking to gain a strong foundation in management, and working professionals seeking career advancement or a transition into new, high-growth fields. Whether you aspire to become a human resource manager, a product development manager, or simply wish to enhance your leadership acumen, the ePGPM provides a structured pathway. It's ideal for those who want to acquire advanced skills in areas such as marketing, data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, without taking a break from their careers.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the Executive Post Graduate Program in Management, candidates must hold a Bachelor's Degree in any discipline. A crucial requirement is that applicants must have secured at least 50 percent marks in their undergraduate studies. There are no additional criteria regarding work experience, making it accessible to both freshers and seasoned professionals.

## Application Process

The admission process for the ePGPM is straightforward. Interested candidates need to complete an online application form. This form typically requires basic personal information, educational background details, and possibly a statement of purpose outlining career aspirations. Detailed instructions for filling out the application will be available on the official Great Learning portal.

Upon successful completion of the program, participants receive a certificate from Great Learning, signifying their achievement and enhanced managerial capabilities.
