# Artificial intelligence Design and Engineering with Microsoft Azure by CloudSwyft Global Systems, Inc via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program offers a comprehensive introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI) and its practical application using Microsoft Azure, a leading cloud platform. It's designed for individuals keen to develop AI solutions, from understanding core concepts to building functional systems.

## Key takeaways

*   This online program is self-paced, requiring approximately 5-6 hours of study per week over 17 weeks.
*   It is an introductory-level course, making it accessible even without prior AI or cloud computing experience.
*   The curriculum is structured to prepare learners for two significant Microsoft Azure certification exams: AI-900 (AI Fundamentals) and AI-100 (AI Engineer Associate).
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certification, enhancing their career prospects in AI and related fields.

## Course Overview

The AI Design and Engineering program focuses on equipping learners with the skills to design and implement AI solutions using Microsoft Azure. The learning approach combines video lectures with text-based materials, quizzes, practical exercises, and projects to ensure a thorough understanding.

The course is divided into five core modules:

*   **Cloud Computing Fundamentals:** Understanding the basic principles of cloud services.
*   **Python Programming:** Developing essential coding skills for AI applications.
*   **Conversational AI with Azure Bot Service:** Learning to create AI-powered chatbots.
*   **Machine Learning with Azure Machine Learning:** Exploring the concepts and applications of machine learning.
*   **Computer Vision Solutions:** Gaining knowledge in AI applications related to image and video analysis.

## Who is this program for?

This program is suitable for a wide range of individuals, from complete beginners to professionals looking to advance their expertise in AI and cloud solutions. While it's an introductory course, a basic understanding of Python programming and fundamental data science concepts, along with some exposure to cloud computing, would be beneficial. It is particularly relevant for:

*   Students pursuing data science.
*   Individuals aiming for entry-level positions in the AI and Machine Learning industries.
*   Software Developers.
*   DevOps Engineers.
*   Cloud Solution Architects.
*   Database Architects.

## What you will learn

Through this program, participants will cultivate a robust skill set in AI and Microsoft Azure. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Applying mathematical concepts within Python programming for AI tasks.
*   Mastering the creation process of AI-driven chatbots.
*   Developing proficiency in navigating and utilizing the Microsoft Azure platform.
*   Gaining a comprehensive understanding of Machine Learning principles and their practical applications.
*   Acquiring foundational knowledge of the Python programming language, crucial for AI development.

## Fees and Certification

The AI Design and Engineering with Microsoft Azure program is offered on a monthly subscription basis. The fee is Rs. 900 per month.

Upon successful completion of the course modules and projects, participants will receive a certificate from Futurelearn. This certification is designed to validate the skills and knowledge acquired throughout the program and is accredited by Microsoft.

## How to Apply

To enroll in this online course, prospective learners should register on the Futurelearn platform and follow the application steps provided there.

This program offers a valuable pathway for anyone looking to enter or advance within the exciting field of Artificial Intelligence, leveraging the power of Microsoft Azure.
