# CAT 2024 Topper Interview: Rahul Sinha, 99.49 Percentiler Shares His Success Story

Rahul Sinha, a mechanical engineering graduate from Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, achieved an impressive 99.49 percentile in CAT 2024. He shares his preparation journey, insights, and the strategies that helped him succeed while balancing a full-time job.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Early Start is Key:** Rahul began his CAT preparation in February, understanding the need for extra time to balance studies with a full-time job.
*   **Focus on Fundamentals:** He emphasized building a strong foundation in all subjects before moving on to advanced practice.
*   **Strategic Mock Test Approach:** Starting mock tests early and using them to refine section-specific strategies was crucial for his success.
*   **Persistence Pays Off:** Despite fluctuating mock scores and confidence dips, consistent effort and a belief in long-term results kept him motivated.

## Aiming High: Setting Ambitious Goals

For Rahul, aiming for a 100 percentile was not just a pipe dream but a guiding principle. Inspired by Norman Vincent Peale's quote, "Shoot for the moon. Even if you miss, you’ll land among the stars," and his teacher Jeet Singh's encouragement, he set his sights high from the beginning. While there were moments of self-doubt, especially when mock test scores didn't align with his efforts, he chose to maintain focus and believe that consistent hard work would eventually yield results.

## The Advantage of an Early Start and Prior Experience

Rahul's journey to a 99.49 percentile wasn't his first interaction with CAT. He had taken the exam the previous year without significant preparation, scoring 90.7 percentile. This initial attempt provided him with valuable insight into the exam pattern. Armed with this understanding, he decided to approach CAT 2024 with a structured preparation plan, commencing as early as February. He recognized that managing studies alongside a demanding full-time role at PwC India's D&A department would require ample time and consistent effort.

## A Straightforward Yet Effective Preparation Strategy

Rahul's preparation strategy was built on clear steps: first, mastering the basics to build a solid foundation. Once the fundamentals were strong, he dedicated time to solving a wide variety of questions, ensuring he covered diverse question types. However, the most critical element of his strategy was the early integration of mock tests. He believes that simply completing the syllabus and practicing questions isn't enough; mock tests are essential for fine-tuning one's approach for each section and identifying effective strategies for exam day.

## Overcoming Challenges: Time Management and Consistency

Balancing a full-time job with rigorous CAT preparation presented significant challenges, particularly in time management and maintaining consistency. Initially, integrating studies into his daily routine proved difficult. However, over time, Rahul discovered a rhythm that allowed him to study consistently without experiencing burnout. He also strategically took short breaks during his preparation period to recharge, ensuring he remained fresh and focused for the final push after August, when he committed to an unbroken stretch of study.

Rahul's success story is a testament to the power of clear goals, strategic planning, and unwavering dedication, offering valuable lessons for aspiring CAT candidates.
