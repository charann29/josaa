# Getting Started with AI using IBM Watson by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This introductory program offers a fantastic opportunity to explore the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence, specifically through the lens of IBM Watson. It's designed to give you a solid understanding of how AI works and its practical applications.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a beginner-friendly course, making it accessible even if you have no prior AI experience.
*   The entire program is online and self-paced, allowing you to learn at your convenience.
*   You can complete the core learning content in approximately 9 hours.
*   While the course content is free to audit, a paid subscription is required to earn an official certificate.

## Understanding the Course

The "Getting Started with AI using IBM Watson" certification course is offered by IBM and delivered through the Coursera platform. It's structured to introduce you to the core concepts of Artificial Intelligence, with a particular focus on IBM Watson's capabilities.

The curriculum covers essential topics such as how Watson operates, its various AI services, and practical methods for building applications using these services. Beyond theoretical knowledge, the course also delves into real-world market scenarios and current industry trends, providing a holistic view of AI's impact.

This program is divided into four modules, featuring a blend of video lectures, readings, quizzes, assignments, tests, and practice exercises. It's a 100% online experience, ensuring flexibility for learners.

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this program, you will gain valuable insights into several key areas of AI and IBM Watson:

*   A deep understanding of Watson's Artificial Intelligence features and how they function.
*   Knowledge about intelligent chatbots, virtual assistants, and their underlying technology.
*   An introduction to computer vision and its diverse applications.
*   An awareness of specialized Watson applications such as Watson for Oncology, Genomics, and clinical trial matching.
*   The ability to distinguish between consumer-focused and business-oriented AI systems.
*   Familiarity with specific Watson tools like Visual Recognition, Personality Insights, and Tone Analyzer.

## Course Duration and Learning Mode

This is a highly flexible, self-study program. The estimated time commitment to go through all the learning material is approximately 9 hours. As it's entirely online, you can progress at your own pace, fitting the learning around your existing schedule. The instruction medium is English, and subtitles are available in ten different languages, enhancing accessibility for a global audience.

## Fees and Certification

One of the significant advantages of this course is the option to audit the content completely free of charge. This means you can access all the learning materials, videos, and exercises without any payment.

However, if you wish to receive an official certificate upon successful completion, a subscription to the course is required. The fee structure for certification is as follows:

| Particulars                          | Fee Amount (INR) |
| :----------------------------------- | :--------------- |
| Getting Started with AI using IBM Watson (audit only) | Free             |
| Getting Started with AI using IBM Watson - 1 month | ₹3,198           |
| Getting Started with AI using IBM Watson - 3 months | ₹6,397           |
| Getting Started with AI using IBM Watson - 6 months | ₹9,596           |

The certificate is provided by Coursera upon successful completion of all course requirements and payment of the subscription fee.

## How to Apply

The admission process for this program is straightforward. Interested candidates can register directly through the Coursera platform. Simply visit the official course page on Coursera to begin your enrollment and start your AI learning journey.
