# Microsoft Business Applications: Dynamics 365 and Power Platform for Sales Professionals

For sales professionals looking to boost their career, understanding modern business applications like Microsoft Dynamics 365 and the Power Platform is crucial. This program, offered through Futurelearn, provides a comprehensive, self-paced learning experience to equip you with these essential skills.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn how to leverage Microsoft Dynamics 365 for sales, marketing, and customer service.
*   Master the Power Platform applications for creating custom business solutions.
*   Understand cloud services, security, and deployment within the Microsoft ecosystem.
*   Develop skills in data modeling, automation, and integration for enhanced business processes.

## Course Structure and Learning Mode

This program is delivered entirely in English, designed for self-study at your own pace. The learning materials are a blend of video and text-based content, making it accessible and flexible. It's estimated that you'll need to dedicate approximately 5 hours per week to effectively complete the modules and grasp the concepts.

## Fees and Certification

Investing in your professional development is straightforward with this program. The course fees are structured at an affordable rate of Rs. 900 per month. Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate directly from Futurelearn, validating your newly acquired expertise in Microsoft Business Applications.

## Detailed Syllabus

The program is broken down into several key courses, each focusing on different aspects of Dynamics 365 and the Power Platform:

### Microsoft Dynamics 365 Fundamentals

This introductory section covers the basics, including an overview of Dynamics 365 business applications like Sales, Marketing, Customer Service, and Field Service. It also introduces Unified Operations Applications, Business Central, and the Power Platform. You'll explore cloud services, deployment strategies, security, and how to get started with Dynamics 365.

### Dynamics 365: Using Power Platform Applications

Here, you'll dive deeper into configuring Power Platform environments and creating applications. The curriculum includes discovery and planning, data modeling, understanding relationships, and configuring model-driven apps. You'll also learn to build Canvas applications and manage security roles and business units.

### Dynamics 365: Working with Power Platform Automation

This course focuses on automating business processes. Topics include platform automation, business rules, and advanced workflows. You'll also explore Microsoft Flow, working with Common Data Service (CDS), approval flows, and configuring business process flows.

### Dynamics 365: Implementing Power Platform Integrations

Integration is key in modern business, and this section covers how to integrate Dynamics 365 with other systems. You'll learn about email integration, working with Office applications like SharePoint and Microsoft Teams, and managing data through duplicate detection, importing, exporting, and bulk deletion.

### Dynamics 365: Testing and Deploying Power Platform Applications

The final course prepares you for the practical aspects of managing and deploying applications. It covers managing instances and applications, understanding solution concepts, planning and creating solutions, and working with solution components. You'll also learn about distributing solutions, managed solutions, and essential considerations for documentation and testing.

This comprehensive program is designed to provide sales professionals with a robust understanding and practical skills in Microsoft's powerful business application ecosystem.
