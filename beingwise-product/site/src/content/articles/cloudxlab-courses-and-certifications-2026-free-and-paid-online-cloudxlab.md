# CloudxLab Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online CloudxLab Courses

CloudxLab, in collaboration with prestigious institutions like IIT Roorkee and Intel, offers a range of online courses and certifications designed to equip students and professionals with in-demand skills in data science, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. These programs are structured to provide practical, hands-on experience through cloud lab access and numerous projects.

## Key Takeaways

*   CloudxLab partners with IIT Roorkee and Intel to deliver specialized online certification programs.
*   Courses often include extensive cloud lab access and practical projects to ensure hands-on skill development.
*   Programs cover cutting-edge topics in Data Science, AI, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.
*   Some certifications are sponsored by government bodies, like the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India, indicating their relevance and quality.

## Executive PG Certificate Program in Data Science by IIT Roorkee

This 12-month online program, developed by IIT Roorkee and delivered via CloudxLab, is designed for individuals looking to build a strong career in data science. Before the course officially begins, participants attend an immersion program, providing an initial understanding of the curriculum and expectations.

The program's curriculum focuses on current and emerging technological concepts within the data science domain. Students gain unlimited access to the cloud lab, allowing them to solve practical problems and gain valuable experience. Financial assistance is also available through scholarships and no-cost EMI options, making the program more accessible. Upon completion, participants may also attend proctored eligibility tests to aid in job placement.

**Key Skills Covered:** Data science principles, programming, machine learning, business analytics, and Big Data concepts.

## Advanced Certification in Data Science & AI by E&ICT Academy, IIT Roorkee

Offered through the E&ICT Academy, IIT Roorkee, and CloudxLab, this 11-month online certification delves into the latest advancements in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. The curriculum incorporates cutting-edge technologies such as TensorFlow 2.0 and Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs).

Participants benefit from cloud lab access, which facilitates practical application of learned concepts and hands-on experience. The course covers a wide array of essential skills, including Python, Pandas, NumPy, Spark, Hadoop, TensorFlow 2.0, Random Forests, RNNs, CNNs, and Reinforcement Learning. With over 29 projects, students have ample opportunities to showcase their newly acquired expertise. This certification course is sponsored by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India.

**Key Skills Covered:** Machine learning, deep learning, Big Data, and data science methodologies.

## Advanced Certification in Machine Learning & AI by E&ICT Academy, IIT Roorkee

This 8-month online program, also from E&ICT Academy, IIT Roorkee, in partnership with CloudxLab, offers a comprehensive curriculum in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. The course blends live instructor-led sessions with interactive self-paced lectures.

Students will explore various programming languages and tools, including NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, RNNs, TensorFlow 2.0, CNNs, SVM, and GANs. Practical experience is a core component, with more than 25 projects integrated into the course. Features like blended learning, timely doubt resolution, and 270 days of cloud lab access enhance the learning experience. A discussion forum is available for peer interaction, complemented by 24/7 academic support. Graduates will be prepared to tackle real-world industry challenges in fields like VLSI and semiconductors.

**Key Skills Covered:** Machine learning and deep learning techniques.

## Certificate Course on Developing Deep Learning Applications by Intel

In collaboration with Intel, CloudxLab presents this specialized certificate course focused on developing deep learning applications. This program is tailored to provide participants with the skills necessary to work with advanced deep learning frameworks and tools.

This course is ideal for those looking to specialize in building and deploying deep learning solutions. The curriculum emphasizes practical application, ensuring students gain competence in developing real-world deep learning projects. Details regarding duration and fees would be available on the official CloudxLab platform.

These programs represent valuable opportunities for individuals to enhance their skills and career prospects in the rapidly evolving fields of data science and artificial intelligence.
