# CMAT 2026 Latest News & Notification

The Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is a crucial national-level entrance examination for admission to various management programs across India. Conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), CMAT scores are accepted by over 1,300 business schools.

*   The CMAT 2026 examination is scheduled for January 25, 2026.
*   Admit cards and city intimation slips for CMAT 2026 have been released by the NTA.
*   The registration window for CMAT 2026 was extended, with the process now complete.
*   Candidates should regularly check the official NTA website for the most current updates regarding the exam.
