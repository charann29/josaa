*   The Amrita CAT 2026 exam was conducted on February 7 and February 8, 2026.

## Amrita CAT Registration Dates 2026

Prospective candidates for the Amrita CAT 2026 had a specific window to complete their registration. The registration process commenced on **October 6, 2025**, and concluded on **January 26, 2026**. Additionally, the final date to register for the MBA Phase 1 exam was **February 15, 2026**.

### Amrita CAT 2026 Application Fee

The application fee for Amrita CAT 2026 was INR 1880, which includes GST. This fee could be paid conveniently through various online transaction methods, including credit card, debit card, internet banking, or UPI.

## Amrita CAT Exam Dates 2026

The Amrita CAT 2026 examination was successfully conducted on **February 7, 2026, and February 8, 2026**. This computer-based test did not have a sectional time limit, allowing candidates to manage their time across different sections as they deemed fit. The exam scores are accepted for MBA admissions across all campuses of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.

## Amrita CAT Result Dates 2026

Following the examination, the Amrita CAT 2026 results are expected to be declared online. Candidates who appeared for the exam will be able to check their results on the official website of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham using their unique login credentials. The university has tentatively scheduled the release of these results for the **last week of April 2026**.

Staying informed about these dates is essential for a smooth application and admission process to Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham's MBA programs.
