Welcome to BeingWise! This page explores the Problem Solving & System Design - Intermediate program offered by Scaler Academy, an online course designed to enhance your programming and system design capabilities. It's ideal for those looking to deepen their understanding of computer science fundamentals and practical application.

## Key takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** The program covers a wide array of topics from basic programming constructs to advanced data structures and system design principles.
*   **Flexible Learning:** Enjoy self-paced study with recorded sessions and the option to pause and resume your learning journey with a future batch.
*   **Specialised Tracks:** Choose between Backend and Full-stack specialisations, with optional advanced electives in areas like Advanced DSA, Concurrent Programming, or Product Management.
*   **Dedicated Support:** Benefit from live classes, one-on-one mentorship, and teaching assistants available to answer your questions.

## Course Overview

The Problem Solving & System Design - Intermediate program is an approximately 11.5-month online course from Scaler Academy. It's structured for students and professionals seeking to build strong problem-solving skills and understand system design principles. The instruction is in English, delivered through a blend of self-study materials, video content, and virtual classroom interactions. A key feature is the flexibility to learn at your own pace; you can watch recorded sessions if you miss live classes and even pause your course for a few months before rejoining with a subsequent batch. Learners also have the opportunity to collaborate with classmates to decide on convenient class timings.

## Who Can Apply?

This program is open to both students and working professionals who completed their graduation in or before the year 2020. It's designed for individuals with some foundational knowledge who are ready to dive deeper into complex computer science concepts and their practical applications.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing the Problem Solving & System Design - Intermediate course, participants will develop a robust set of skills, including enhanced problem-solving abilities, strong programming proficiency, and a solid grasp of computer networking. The curriculum delves into a wide range of topics, such as:

*   Programming constructs and computer science fundamentals
*   Time complexity analysis
*   Strings, arrays, binary search, and recursion
*   Two pointers, hashing, and bit manipulation
*   Sorting algorithms, greedy approaches, queues, and tries
*   Heap, linked lists, stacks, trees, and dynamic programming (DP)
*   Graphs, operating systems (OS), databases (DB), and computer networking

## Admission Process

To enroll in the Problem Solving & System Design - Intermediate online certification course from Scaler Academy, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official Scaler Academy website.
2.  Create a new account or sign in using your existing social network credentials.
3.  Take a 30-minute multiple-choice question (MCQ) exam. This assessment focuses on aptitude and fundamental coding to help ensure you choose the most suitable course.
4.  Once assessed, you can sign up for one of the available EMI programs to begin your learning journey.

## Course Structure and Fees

The program offers a structured curriculum with live lectures, online classes, assignments, hands-on projects, and notes. Participants also have access to various electives. While specific fee details are best confirmed on the official Scaler Academy portal, the program does lead to a certificate upon successful completion. To earn the Problem Solving & System Design - Intermediate certification, candidates must complete the core curriculum, all projects, assignments, and chosen specialisations and electives. The certificate is awarded by Scaler Academy itself.

This program is a solid choice for those looking to significantly upgrade their technical skills and career prospects in the competitive world of software development and system design.
