# PGDM in HR: Course Fees, Admission 2026, Subjects, Syllabus, Salary in India

A Post Graduate Diploma in Human Resources (PGDM in HR) is a specialized two-year postgraduate program designed to equip students with the essential administrative and leadership principles needed to effectively manage an organization's human capital.

## Key Takeaways

*   The PGDM in HR is a two-year postgraduate diploma focusing on modern HR practices.
*   Admission typically requires a bachelor's degree and strong scores in national entrance exams like CAT, XAT, MAT, or GMAT.
*   The curriculum covers vital areas such as talent acquisition, employee engagement, labor laws, and training and development.
*   Graduates can pursue roles like HR Manager, Talent Acquisition Specialist, or Industrial Relations Manager.

## Understanding the PGDM in HR Program

This industry-focused course is approved by AICTE and aims to develop strategic HR professionals. It delves into contemporary concepts relevant to human resource management, preparing candidates with job-ready skills for the dynamic corporate world. The program emphasizes practical application and current industry trends.

## Eligibility Criteria and Admission Process 2026

To be considered for a PGDM in HR program, candidates generally need to meet specific requirements:

*   **Educational Qualification:** A bachelor's degree from a recognized university is mandatory. There is usually a minimum percentage requirement, often around 50% for general category candidates and a slightly relaxed percentage for reserved categories.
*   **Entrance Exams:** Applicants must appear for and achieve competitive scores in national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, MAT, SNAP, or GMAT. Each institution may have its preferred exams.
*   **Selection Rounds:** Beyond exam scores, the admission process typically includes a Group Discussion (GD) and a Personal Interview (PI) round. These stages assess communication skills, leadership potential, and overall suitability for the program.

It's advisable to check the specific eligibility criteria and application deadlines for each college you are interested in, as they can vary.

## PGDM in HR Course Fees

The tuition fees for a PGDM in HR program in India can vary significantly based on the institution's reputation, facilities, and location. Generally, the course fees can range from approximately INR 2 Lakh to upwards of INR 27 Lakh for the entire two-year duration. Prestigious institutions and private colleges often have higher fee structures compared to others.

## PGDM in HR Syllabus and Subjects

The PGDM in HR curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of human resource functions. While specific subjects may vary by institution, common areas of study include:

*   **Human Resource Management Fundamentals:** Introduction to HR, organizational behavior, and management principles.
*   **Talent Acquisition and Management:** Recruitment, selection, onboarding, performance management, and succession planning.
*   **Employee Relations and Labor Laws:** Industrial relations, legal framework for HR, trade unions, and dispute resolution.
*   **Training and Development:** Needs assessment, program design, delivery, and evaluation of employee training.
*   **Compensation and Benefits:** Salary structures, incentive plans, benefits administration, and payroll management.
*   **HR Analytics:** Using data to make informed HR decisions.
*   **Strategic HR:** Aligning HR strategies with overall business objectives.

## Career Scope and Salary in India

Graduates with a PGDM in HR have a wide array of career opportunities across various industries. The demand for skilled HR professionals is consistent as organizations recognize the strategic importance of human capital.

### Common Job Profiles

*   **HR Manager:** Oversees all aspects of HR functions within an organization.
*   **Talent Acquisition Specialist:** Focuses on recruiting and hiring new employees.
*   **Industrial Relations Manager:** Manages relationships between management and employees, often dealing with unions.
*   **HR Consultant:** Provides expert advice to organizations on HR strategies and challenges.
*   **Training and Development Manager:** Designs and implements employee training programs.
*   **Compensation and Benefits Analyst:** Manages salary structures, benefits, and payroll.

### Salary Expectations

The starting salary for a PGDM in HR graduate in India can vary based on the college, individual skills, and the hiring company. Entry-level salaries might range from INR 4 LPA to INR 8 LPA. With experience and a proven track record, HR professionals can command significantly higher packages, with experienced managers earning INR 10 LPA to INR 20 LPA or more. Top recruiters typically include IT companies, manufacturing firms, consulting agencies, and financial services.

## Top Institutions for PGDM in HR

Several esteemed institutions across India offer excellent PGDM in HR programs. Some of the well-known names include XLRI Xavier School of Management, IIM Indore, IIM Raipur, and Management Development Institute (MDI). These institutions are often recognized for their rigorous curriculum, faculty expertise, and strong placement records, as reflected in various rankings like the NIRF rankings.

Embarking on a PGDM in HR can open doors to a fulfilling career in human resource management, allowing you to play a pivotal role in organizational success.
