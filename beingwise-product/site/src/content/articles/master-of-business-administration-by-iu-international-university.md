# Master of Business Administration by IU International University of Applied Sciences: Fee, Review, Duration

Considering an MBA can be a big step towards advancing your career. The IU International University of Applied Sciences offers an online Master of Business Administration program designed to equip you with essential business knowledge and leadership skills.

## Key takeaways

*   This online MBA program can be completed in 18 months.
*   The total program fee is approximately ₹4.89 Lakh.
*   It focuses on preparing students for leadership roles and entrepreneurship.
*   The university offers flexible monthly payment options and a money-back guarantee.

## Program Overview

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) at IU International University of Applied Sciences is a postgraduate degree program delivered entirely online. It aims to provide students with a deep understanding of core business principles and practices, helping them to develop the competencies needed for management and leadership positions in various industries. The curriculum is structured to cover a broad range of business functions, making it suitable for individuals aspiring to lead teams, manage projects, or even launch their own ventures.

## Course Structure and Duration

This MBA program is designed to be completed over 18 months, offering a focused and relatively quick pathway to a master's degree. As an online program, it provides flexibility for students to manage their studies alongside other commitments. The learning experience includes both live and recorded tutorials each month, allowing students to engage with course material and instructors at their convenience. This blended approach ensures access to learning resources and opportunities for interaction.

## Fees and Financial Flexibility

For prospective students in India, the total fee for the Master of Business Administration program is approximately ₹4.89 Lakh. A notable feature of this program is the financial flexibility it offers; students can opt to pay their fees through monthly installments. Additionally, the university provides a 4-week money-back guarantee, allowing students to experience the program before fully committing.

## Skills and Career Preparation

The MBA curriculum is designed to impart a range of critical business skills. Key areas of learning include human resource management, performance management, operations management, sales management, marketing, and strategic management. The program is particularly suited for individuals looking to step into leadership roles or for entrepreneurs who wish to establish and grow their own businesses. Graduates are prepared to tackle complex business challenges and contribute effectively to a dynamic global marketplace.

### Skills You Will Develop:

*   Human Resource Management
*   Performance Management
*   Operations Management
*   Sales Management
*   Marketing
*   Strategic Management

### Key Course Deliverables:

*   Entrepreneurship
*   Financial Management
*   Operations Management
*   Supply Chain Management

## Additional Benefits

Beyond the core MBA curriculum, students of this program also have the opportunity to learn German and English for free. This can be a significant advantage for those looking to enhance their language skills, which can be beneficial for career prospects in an international context.

This online MBA program from IU International University of Applied Sciences offers a structured yet flexible path to advanced business education, aiming to prepare you for future leadership opportunities.
