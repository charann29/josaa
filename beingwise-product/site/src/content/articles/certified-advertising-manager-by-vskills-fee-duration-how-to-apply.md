# Certified Advertising Manager by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in the dynamic world of advertising, the Certified Advertising Manager program by Vskills offers a comprehensive pathway. This online course is designed to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in advertising strategy and execution.

## Key takeaways

*   This is an entirely online, self-paced program with video and text-based learning materials.
*   Upon successful completion and passing the exam, you receive a government-recognized certificate with lifetime validity.
*   The total fee for the program and certification is a one-time payment of ₹3,499.
*   Vskills certification can enhance your visibility on major job platforms, potentially improving career prospects.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The Certified Advertising Manager program focuses on developing your ability to plan, evaluate, and implement effective advertising strategies. You'll gain a strong understanding of how to combine strategic decision-making with creative approaches to make impactful advertising choices. The curriculum is structured to provide both foundational and advanced industry-oriented skills.

The learning experience is flexible, offered through an e-learning platform with access to study materials and practice tests. This self-study model allows you to progress at your own pace, making it suitable for working professionals or students with other commitments. A sample chapter is also available for reference, giving you a glimpse into the course content before committing.

## What You Will Learn

The program covers a wide range of topics crucial for an advertising professional. Key areas include:

*   **Advertising Management:** Understanding the core principles and functions.
*   **Brand Building:** Strategies for developing and strengthening brands through advertising.
*   **Decision-Making & Planning:** Crafting effective advertising plans and making informed choices.
*   **Advertising Tactics:** Practical approaches to campaign execution.
*   **Marketing Communication:** Integrating advertising within broader marketing efforts.
*   **Global Marketing & Advertising:** Navigating international advertising landscapes.
*   **Laws and Ethics:** Adhering to legal and ethical standards in advertising.
*   **Media Strategy:** Developing effective media plans and tactics.

This comprehensive syllabus ensures you develop a holistic understanding of the advertising domain.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Certified Advertising Manager program is **₹3,499**. This is a one-time payment that covers access to the learning materials, practice tests, and the certification exam. Payment can be conveniently made through online methods like net banking.

Upon successfully passing the online certification exam, you will receive a certificate from Vskills. A notable advantage of this certification is its **lifetime validity**, meaning you won't need to worry about renewals. Vskills also highlights certified individuals on major job portals, which can be a significant boost for your job search in roles such as Advertising Manager, Marketing Manager, Digital Marketing Executive, or Affiliate Marketer.

## Eligibility and Application Process

There are no specific prerequisites for enrolling in the Vskills Certified Advertising Manager program. The main eligibility criterion for receiving the certificate is successfully passing the online certification exam. You need to score at least 25 out of 50 questions to qualify. The exam can be scheduled at your convenience.

To apply for the course, follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official Vskills website.
2.  Search for the "Certified Advertising Manager" course.
3.  Select the appropriate certification from the search results.
4.  Proceed to checkout by clicking "Buy Now."
5.  Enter your details and log in to your Vskills account.
6.  Complete the registration by making the fee payment through your preferred online method.

This program offers a practical and accessible way to gain a recognized certification in advertising management, enhancing your professional profile and career opportunities.
