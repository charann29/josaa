# Psychology XI Part-II by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For school students keen to explore the fascinating world of the human mind and behavior, the 'Psychology XI Part-II' online course offers an excellent foundational introduction. Delivered by the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) through the Swayam platform, this program is designed to build essential knowledge in psychology.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a free online course, making foundational psychology accessible to all students.
*   The program spans 24 weeks and is taught entirely in English, featuring video and text-based learning.
*   Successful completion awards a course certificate from NCERT and two academic credits.
*   The curriculum covers core psychological concepts like memory, learning, thinking, motivation, and emotions.

## Course Overview

This introductory-level program is specifically crafted for school students to give them a solid understanding of psychology and its potential career paths. The course material delves into various psychological concepts, including how we remember things, the processes involved in learning, different types of thinking, and the complexities of human emotions and motivation.

The course is coordinated by Dr. Prabhat K. Mishra from the Department of Educational Psychology and Foundations of Education (DEPFE), NCERT, New Delhi. The content is structured into nine chapters, grouped under four main topics, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.

## What You Will Learn

The 'Psychology XI Part-II' syllabus is meticulously designed to provide students with a deep understanding of psychological principles related to the human mind and behavior. Participants will develop expertise in theories of memory, perception, and thinking, along with insights into the processes of learning, sensation, and attention. The course also explores the practical applications of psychological principles in everyday life.

## Eligibility and Certification

This course is primarily intended for school students who wish to gain foundational knowledge in psychology. While there are no strict eligibility criteria to enroll and participate in the course content, to receive the official course completion certificate from NCERT, students must successfully complete the program and achieve a score of 70% in the final assessment. Upon meeting these requirements, students will be awarded a certificate and two academic credits, which can be valuable additions to their academic records.

## How to Apply

Joining the 'Psychology XI Part-II' course on Swayam is a straightforward process. Interested candidates need to register through the official Swayam course website. Here are the steps:

1.  Navigate to the specific course page on the Swayam platform.
2.  Look for the 'Sign-In/Register' option and click on it.
3.  You will then be prompted to create a new account using an email address and password, or you can sign in quickly using an existing Microsoft, Google, or Facebook ID.
4.  Once registered, you can join the course and begin your learning journey.

## Course Structure and Offerings

The 24-week program is delivered through a combination of video lectures and text-based materials. Students will engage with a variety of learning resources, including:

*   Detailed course modules
*   Weekly assignments to reinforce learning
*   Recommended books and reference materials
*   A comprehensive final assessment
*   A course completion certificate upon successful fulfillment of requirements
