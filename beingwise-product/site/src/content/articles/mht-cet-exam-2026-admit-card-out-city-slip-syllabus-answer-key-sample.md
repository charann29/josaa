# MHT CET Exam 2026 - Admit Card (Out), City Slip, Syllabus, Answer Key, Sample Papers, Cutoff

The MHT CET is a crucial state-level entrance examination for admission to undergraduate engineering, pharmacy, and agriculture courses in various colleges across Maharashtra. This guide provides essential information for aspiring students aiming for the 2026 examination.

## Key takeaways

*   The MHT CET 2026 results are anticipated to be released in June 2026 on the official cetcell.mahacet.org website.
*   Candidates will need their registration number, admit card number, and date of birth to access their results.
*   The examination offers two main groups: Physics, Chemistry, Biology (PCB) and Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (PCM).
*   Understanding previous years' statistics and toppers can offer valuable insights into the competitive landscape.

## Checking Your MHT CET 2026 Result

Once declared, your MHT CET 2026 result will be available on the official website. This result will display your total marks and the rank you've secured in the entrance test. It's important to download and print a copy of your scorecard for future reference during the admission process.

### Steps to check your MHT CET result 2026

1.  Visit the official MHT CET 2026 website.
2.  Locate and click on the result or scorecard link.
3.  Enter your unique application number and your date of birth.
4.  Click on the 'Login' button.
5.  Your MHT CET 2026 result and scorecard will then be displayed.
6.  Review your result, download it, and print a copy for your records.
