# Top MBA Colleges Accepting CMAT Scores 80-90 Percentile

For many aspiring MBA students in India, the Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is a crucial gateway. Achieving a CMAT score in the 80-90 percentile range opens doors to numerous reputable management institutions across the country.

## Key Takeaways

*   A CMAT score between 80-90 percentile is considered competitive for admission to many well-regarded MBA/PGDM programs.
*   Colleges in this percentile bracket offer diverse specializations and varying fee structures, providing good options for different student preferences.
*   Beyond the CMAT score, colleges will also consider academic performance, work experience, and performance in group discussions and personal interviews.
*   Always verify the latest cut-offs and application deadlines directly on the respective college websites, as these can change annually.

## Understanding CMAT Percentiles and Admissions

The CMAT exam, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), is widely accepted by over a thousand management colleges. While the most elite institutions typically look for percentiles above 95, a strong performance in the 80-90 percentile still places you in a favorable position for admission to many excellent Tier 2 colleges. This range is often considered a safe bet, as it corresponds to a significant number of popular MBA programs.

Admission to these programs is not solely based on your CMAT percentile. Institutions typically have a multi-stage selection process that includes evaluating your academic background, any relevant work experience, and your performance in subsequent rounds like Group Discussions (GD) and Personal Interviews (PI). Therefore, while your CMAT score is vital, a holistic profile strengthens your application.

## Colleges to Consider with an 80-90 Percentile CMAT Score

If you have secured a CMAT percentile between 80 and 90, several prominent institutions should be on your radar. These colleges are known for their strong faculty, good placements, and robust curriculum. It is advisable to research each college thoroughly, including their specific program offerings, faculty profiles, and placement reports, to find the best fit for your career aspirations.

Some of the popular MBA colleges that have historically accepted CMAT scores in this range include Prin L N Welingkar Institute of Management Development & Research Mumbai, Great Lakes Institute of Management Gurgaon, Jaipuria Institute of Management Noida, and IMT Nagpur, among others. Each of these institutions offers distinct advantages, from location-specific industry exposure to specialized program tracks.

## Course Fees for MBA/PGDM Programs

Understanding the financial commitment is a crucial part of your decision-making process. The course fees for MBA/PGDM programs at colleges accepting CMAT scores in the 80-90 percentile range can vary significantly. This variation depends on several factors, including the institution's reputation, infrastructure, faculty, and the specific program chosen. It's important to note that these fees generally cover tuition and may not include living expenses, books, or other miscellaneous charges. Always refer to the official college prospectus for the most accurate and up-to-date fee information.
