# BSEH 2026 Exams - Date Sheet (OUT), Syllabus, Results & Sample Papers

The Board of School Education Haryana (BSEH) conducts annual examinations for students in Classes 10 and 12. This page provides essential information for students preparing for the BSEH 2026 board exams, covering key dates, preparation resources, and results.

## Key takeaways

*   The BSEH released the Class 10 and 12 date sheets for the 2026 exams on January 27, 2026.
*   Class 10 exams are scheduled from February 26 to March 20, 2026, while Class 12 exams will run from February 25 to April 1, 2026.
*   These examinations will be conducted offline, using a pen-and-paper format.
*   Students should refer to the official BSEH website for detailed date sheets, admit cards, syllabus, and sample papers.

## About the Board of School Education Haryana (BSEH)

Established in 1969 under the Haryana Act No. 11, the Board of School Education Haryana (BSEH) is responsible for regulating and conducting public examinations at middle, matriculation, and senior secondary levels. Initially headquartered in Chandigarh, the board's main office moved to Bhiwani in 1981. BSEH also manages entrance examinations such as the Haryana NTSE, Haryana DEd, and the Haryana Teachers Eligibility Test (HTET). The board oversees approximately 3,672 affiliated schools and examines around six lakh students annually.

## BSEH 2026 Exam Schedule

The official date sheets for the BSEH Class 10 and 12 exams for 2026 were released on January 27, 2026. Students can find the comprehensive schedule on the official BSEH portal. The examinations are set to take place in February, March, and April 2026.

| Exam | Start Date | End Date |
| :------------------- | :--------- | :--------- |
| HBSE Class 10 Exams | Feb 26, 2026 | Mar 20, 2026 |
| HBSE Class 12 Exams | Feb 25, 2026 | Apr 1, 2026 |

## Syllabus, Sample Papers, and Exam Pattern

To aid students in their preparation, BSEH releases the official syllabus, sample papers, and details of the exam pattern on its website. Reviewing these resources is crucial for understanding the structure of the question papers, the types of questions asked, and the marking scheme. Practicing with sample papers helps students develop effective time management skills and familiarise themselves with the difficulty level of the exams. Students are advised to download these materials from the official BSEH portal well in advance.

## Admit Cards and Results

Admit cards for the BSEH 2026 exams will be issued closer to the examination dates. It is mandatory for all students to carry their admit card to the examination hall. Information regarding the release of admit cards will be updated on the official BSEH website. Similarly, the results for the BSEH Class 10 and 12 exams will be announced a few weeks after the examinations conclude, typically in May. Students can check their results by visiting the official BSEH portal and entering their roll number.

Stay updated with all official announcements by regularly checking the Board of School Education Haryana's website.
