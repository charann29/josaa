# MBA in Finance by Chitkara University Online: Fee, Review, Duration

Chitkara University Online offers a Master of Business Administration (MBA) with a specialization in Finance, delivered entirely through an online learning format. This program is designed for individuals aiming to build expertise in the dynamic financial sector and advance their professional careers.

## Key takeaways

*   This online MBA in Finance is a two-year postgraduate degree program.
*   The total fee for the course is ₹2.00 Lakh, with an option for installment payments.
*   It offers comprehensive placement support, including resume building and interview preparation.
*   The curriculum is designed to equip students for roles such as Investment Banker, Financial Analyst, and Wealth Manager.

## Program Overview and Structure

The online MBA in Finance from Chitkara University is structured to provide a deep understanding of financial markets, investment strategies, and corporate finance. The program is specifically tailored for working professionals or those seeking to transition into leadership positions within the finance industry.

Students will delve into various core financial topics, including financial analysis, investment management, risk management, and financial modeling. The learning experience combines online coursework with practical, experiential elements like case studies, business simulations, and project-based assignments to ensure a well-rounded education.

### Skills You Will Develop

Upon completing this program, students are expected to develop several key competencies, such as:

*   A strong grasp of financial market operations, key participants, and economic influences.
*   The ability to assess financial risks and returns for various investment opportunities.
*   Proficiency in financial analysis techniques for evaluating organizational health and performance.
*   Expertise in utilizing FinTech tools and technologies to enhance financial services.
*   Mastery of investment banking processes, including valuation and mergers and acquisitions.

## Fees and Duration

This online MBA program has a duration of two years. The total program fee is ₹2.00 Lakh. Chitkara University Online provides the flexibility to pay these fees in installments, making the program more accessible to a wider range of students.

## Faculty and Learning Experience

The program boasts faculty members with significant experience in the financial domain. Notable faculty include GP Sharma and Rajeev Sood, who bring their expertise to the online classroom. Beyond the core faculty, students benefit from over 20 masterclass sessions led by corporate leaders from the banking, financial services, and insurance (BFSI) sectors. This exposure to industry veterans provides valuable real-world insights and networking opportunities.

## Admissions and Eligibility

To be eligible for the MBA in Finance program, applicants must hold a graduation degree or an equivalent qualification. A minimum aggregate of 50% marks in their undergraduate studies is required for admission. Candidates are advised to check the official Chitkara University Online portal for the most current admission guidelines and application deadlines.

## Career Prospects and Placement Support

The online MBA in Finance prepares graduates for a variety of in-demand roles within the financial sector. Potential career paths include:

*   Investment Banker
*   Financial Analyst
*   Wealth Manager
*   Portfolio Manager
*   Risk Manager

Chitkara University Online emphasizes comprehensive placement support for its students. This 360-degree assistance covers critical aspects such as resume building, interview preparation, and connecting students with potential employers.
