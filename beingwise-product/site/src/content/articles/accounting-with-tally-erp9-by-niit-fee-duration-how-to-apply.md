# Accounting with Tally.ERP9 by NIIT: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to build a career in accounting, understanding Tally.ERP9 is almost essential. This program by NIIT is designed to equip you with practical skills in this widely used accounting software, along with foundational accounting principles and essential Microsoft Office tools.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a classroom-based certification program offered by NIIT, focusing on Tally.ERP9.
*   The course involves 36 hours of instructor-led learning, complemented by hands-on practice.
*   You'll learn core accounting concepts, Tally functionalities, and how to use Microsoft Office for financial tasks.
*   Eligibility generally requires basic computer knowledge and English communication skills, with a preference for commerce students.

## Program Overview

This certification aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of Tally, India's most popular business accounting software. Beyond just Tally, the curriculum also covers fundamental accounting principles, including the double-entry system, and how to leverage Microsoft Office tools like Excel and Word for reporting and analysis. The goal is to make you proficient in managing business transactions and maintaining company accounts efficiently.

The program combines 36 hours of classroom instruction with practical exercises using real-time datasets. This blend ensures that participants not only grasp the theoretical aspects but also gain hands-on experience in applying their knowledge to solve everyday accounting challenges. You'll also explore the role of information systems in modern accounting practices.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing the program, you will develop a range of valuable skills, including:

*   **Accounting Proficiency:** A solid understanding of basic accounting principles and the double-entry system.
*   **Tally.ERP9 Expertise:** The ability to understand and utilise Tally's core components and various functionalities to record and process business transactions, and manage an organisation's account information.
*   **Microsoft Office Skills:** Competency in using MS Excel for data entry, computations, and modifying worksheets, as well as creating professional documents with Microsoft Word.
*   **Efficient Task Management:** The capability to perform daily accounting tasks more effectively and efficiently.

## Eligibility and Certification

To enroll in the Accounting with Tally.ERP9 program, you should have a basic understanding of computers, including PC essentials and familiarity with the Windows Operating System. Good conversational English skills are also necessary. While not strictly mandatory, students who have studied Commerce in Class XI and XII may be given preference, as they often have a foundational understanding of business concepts.

Regarding certification, completing the entire course is essential. NIIT awards a graded certificate to participants who meet the minimum performance criteria throughout the program. If these credentials are not met, a certificate of participation will still be provided, acknowledging your involvement in the training.

## How to Apply

The admission process for the Accounting with Tally.ERP9 program is straightforward:

1.  **Enquiry Form:** Visit the official NIIT course page for this program. You will find a short online form to fill out.
2.  **Provide Details:** In this form, you will need to enter your name, email address, mobile number, and the state you reside in.
3.  **Expert Call:** After submitting your details, an expert from NIIT will contact you shortly to provide further information and guide you through the admission process.

This program is ideal for undergraduate and graduate students, or anyone looking to establish a strong foundation in Tally and enter the accounting domain.
