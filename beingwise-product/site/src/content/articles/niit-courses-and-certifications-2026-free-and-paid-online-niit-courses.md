# NIIT Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online NIIT Courses

NIIT offers a diverse range of programs designed to equip students and professionals with cutting-edge skills for various industries. These include specialized certifications, postgraduate diplomas, and advanced programs in partnership with leading organizations.

## Key takeaways

*   NIIT provides both short-term certifications and longer postgraduate diploma programs.
*   Courses span high-demand fields like banking, data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.
*   Many programs incorporate practical, hands-on training, including internships and on-the-job experience.
*   Some advanced programs are designed for individuals with no prior background in programming or data analytics.

## Specialised Banking Programs

NIIT, in collaboration with the NIIT Institute of Finance, Banking & Insurance (IFBI) and partners like Axis Bank, offers focused programs for aspiring banking professionals. A prominent example is the Priority Banking Programme.

### Priority Banking Programme

This comprehensive, year-long program is a joint initiative with Axis Bank, designed to immerse participants in the practical aspects of priority banking. It aims to develop skills mirroring those of an Axis Bank Priority Banking Relationship Manager (PBRM).

The curriculum covers the business environment, including an understanding of financial instruments like bonds, stocks, and mutual funds, with participants preparing reports based on their observations. The program structure includes:

*   Six months of residential training.
*   Four and a half months of on-the-job training.
*   One and a half months of a guided, paid internship.

Throughout the program, students engage in agile projects. The academic content is divided into four terms, each lasting 12 weeks. Upon successful completion, participants receive a Post Graduate Diploma in Banking and Relationship Management from NIIT University (NU).

**Duration:** 12 Months

**Fees:** ₹2,25,000

**Skills Covered:** Communication skills, banking product knowledge, relationship management.

## Advanced Programs in Data Science and AI

Through StackRoute, NIIT delivers advanced professional courses in data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. These programs cater to individuals looking to enter or advance within these rapidly evolving technology fields.

### Advanced PGP in Data Science and Machine Learning (Part-Time)

This StackRoute-certified program from NIIT is tailored for individuals who may not have previous programming or data analytics experience. It's a part-time offering, providing advanced training for those aiming for Level 2 and Level 3 data science roles.

The course emphasizes experiential learning, covering core concepts such as:

*   Data preparation, analysis, and visualization.
*   Statistical analysis and data modeling.
*   Applied machine learning models.
*   Natural Language Processing (NLP) for text analysis.
*   Deep learning techniques.
*   Data-driven storytelling using various software tools and frameworks.

This outcome-based program includes projects designed to equip students with the necessary skills to tackle real-world data science challenges.

**Duration:** 50 Weeks

**Fees:** ₹2,25,000

**Skills Covered:** Data science fundamentals, machine learning, data visualization, Python programming, deep learning, statistical analysis, Excel, NLP, data modeling, data mining, decision-making, analytical skills.

### PGP in Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence

This program is designed to transform individuals without a background in data analytics or programming into confident data science professionals. The curriculum focuses on developing skills essential for contributing effectively to data collection and analysis activities.

Key areas of study include:

*   Applying machine learning models.
*   Utilizing Natural Language Processing (NLP) for text analysis.
*   Deep learning concepts.
*   Data-based storytelling.

Over 41 weeks, the program's syllabus aims to build the engineering, technical, and professional skills required to confidently join Data Science Field Teams. Upon successful completion of all challenges, exams, and projects, trainees will possess the knowledge and skills to begin a career as a Junior Data Scientist or Data Analyst.

**Duration:** 41 Weeks

**Fees:** ₹2,25,000

**Skills Covered:** Machine learning, data science knowledge, data analysis, data preparation, data visualization, statistical analysis, data modeling, deep learning, NLP.

NIIT continues to be a significant provider of industry-relevant education, preparing learners for successful careers in dynamic sectors.
