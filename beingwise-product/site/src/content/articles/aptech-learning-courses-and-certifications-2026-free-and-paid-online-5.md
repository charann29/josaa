# Aptech Learning Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Aptech Learning Courses

Aptech Learning offers a variety of career-focused courses designed to help students gain industry-relevant skills and prepare for employment. These programs cover diverse fields such as information technology, banking, finance, and hardware networking.

## Key takeaways

*   Aptech Learning specializes in career-oriented training across various sectors.
*   Courses often include practical skills and are structured to align with industry demands.
*   Programs like ACCP Pro– E-commerce, Banking Specialist, and Aptech Certified Accounts Professional are available.
*   Course durations and fees vary significantly based on the program chosen.

## ACCP Pro– E-commerce Course

The ACCP Pro– E-commerce course from Aptech Learning is designed to provide a deep understanding of the e-commerce landscape. This program introduces learners to essential e-commerce concepts, preparing them for roles in this rapidly expanding industry.

Over 23 months, participants will develop skills in web productivity tools, logic building, advanced website development, fundamental programming, and the practical implementation of e-commerce strategies. The curriculum aims to equip students with the professional expertise needed to launch and sustain a career in e-commerce. The fee for this comprehensive course is ₹77,400.

**Skills Covered:**

*   SQL knowledge
*   Automation skills
*   Taxation knowledge
*   Financial understanding
*   CSS
*   Java

## Banking Specialist Certification

Recognizing the growth of India's banking sector, the Banking Specialist certification by Aptech Learning aims to provide individuals with foundational knowledge in banking and finance. India's banking industry is one of the largest and fastest-growing globally, creating a high demand for skilled professionals.

This three-month program is divided into three parts, delivered through two-hour in-class lectures three days a week. It is designed to impart specialized knowledge, preparing fresh commerce graduates and other aspirants for various career opportunities within the banking domain. The total fee for this certification is ₹54,000.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Comprehensive banking knowledge

## Aptech Certified Accounts Professional

The Aptech Certified Accounts Professional program is an intensive 15-month training course structured to provide employment-driven education in accounting. The curriculum is divided into two terms, each with a detailed set of modules focusing on industry-relevant knowledge.

Upon completion, candidates will be proficient in essential business accounting software like Tally and basic computer applications. The course also covers advanced topics such as business communication, statutory compliance, TDS, GST, and direct taxation. The program aims to make candidates job-ready by equipping them with the latest accounting techniques, finance, banking, and taxation skills. The fee for this course is ₹70,000.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Accounting proficiency
*   Business Management

Aptech Learning continues to offer various programs tailored to meet the evolving demands of different industries, helping students build successful careers.
