# Action Research in Educational Technology by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This page explores a valuable online course offered by the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) through the SWAYAM platform, focusing on Action Research in Educational Technology. It's designed to equip educators and learners with practical skills to integrate research into the teaching and learning process.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is delivered online, offering self-paced learning through video lectures and text materials.
*   It's an undergraduate-level program spanning 24 weeks, completely free to access.
*   A certificate is available from NCERT upon successful completion of assignments and a paid final exam.
*   The curriculum covers the entire action research process, from problem identification to report writing.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Action Research in Educational Technology course is meticulously crafted by the Central Institute of Educational Technology (CIET), NCERT, New Delhi. Its primary goal is to enhance the educational landscape by promoting collaborative and cooperative problem-solving through action research. Participants will gain the competency to apply action research methodologies within school curricula, using them as a tool for both problem-solving and refining teaching practices.

This self-study program is delivered through a combination of video and text-based modules. While the course content is accessible at no cost, those aiming for official certification must register for and clear a final examination, which incurs a nominal fee. The program provides a comprehensive understanding of action research, detailing its procedures, how to formulate research questions, methods for data recording and interpretation, and effective report writing.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing all modules of this course, participants will develop strong research skills relevant to educational settings. The key learning outcomes include:

*   A thorough understanding of the action research process.
*   The ability to integrate action research effectively into educational practices.
*   Skills in defining and identifying educational problems.
*   Proficiency in interpreting and recording data.
*   Competence in conducting action research and writing comprehensive reports.

## Eligibility and Certification

While the course content is open to everyone, securing the Action Research in Educational Technology Course certificate from NCERT requires dedicated engagement. Candidates must diligently complete all course materials, submit assignments, pass objective tests, prepare a written proposal, and produce a comprehensive final report. Only after fulfilling these requirements and passing the proctored final exam will the certificate be awarded.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Action Research in Educational Technology course, prospective learners should follow these steps:

1.  Navigate to the official SWAYAM portal.
2.  On the portal, look for the course, typically listed under 'Upcoming Courses' or 'Ongoing Courses'. Select 'Action Research in Educational Technology'.
3.  Sign in to the SWAYAM platform using an existing account or through linked accounts like Google, Facebook, or Microsoft. Then, click the 'JOIN' option on the course page.
4.  Provide all necessary personal and educational details to finalize the enrollment and begin the program.

During registration, you will need to create an account if you don't have one, and then log in to fill in your personal information, contact details, and educational background. The SWAYAM portal will also provide information on important dates, lecture schedules, and options for selecting your preferred exam date for certification.

This course offers an excellent opportunity to enhance your professional portfolio and integrate effective research methods into your educational endeavors.
