# Streetwear Design by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Streetwear design is a dynamic and evolving field within fashion, focusing on contemporary trends, cultural influences, and innovative marketing. The Designer's Class offers an online certification course crafted by renowned designer Kunal Rawal, aimed at helping aspiring designers develop a modern and multi-dimensional approach to this exciting area.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn about the history, evolution, and core concepts of streetwear design.
*   Understand how socio-cultural identities, celebrity culture, and influencers shape fashion trends.
*   Develop skills in creative concept development, product creation, and portfolio building.
*   The course is self-paced, delivered through video and text, and provides one year of access to materials.

## Course Overview

This certification program moves beyond traditional fashion design by incorporating elements like creative marketing, addressing global issues, and reflecting popular culture. It's designed for individuals who want to express bold ideas and capture the attention of today's generation. The curriculum covers a comprehensive approach to creative concept development, from understanding the roots of streetwear to exploring modern promotional strategies.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this online certification, you will gain a deep understanding of the factors that drive streetwear fashion trends. The course covers essential design elements, the historical growth of streetwear, and the concepts behind current trends. You will also explore the impact of celebrity culture and influencers on fashion choices. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Design Ideation:** Generating creative concepts for streetwear.
*   **Design Parameters:** Understanding the criteria for effective streetwear design.
*   **Product Development:** Bringing design ideas to life.
*   **Portfolio Design:** Creating a strong portfolio to showcase your work.
*   **Industry Insights:** Identifying significant challenges and opportunities within the fashion industry, such as the threat of fast fashion.

## Program Structure and Duration

This self-paced course offers flexibility for learners. It includes approximately 1 hour and 31 minutes of video lectures, supported by 12 assignments and 13 downloadable resources. Participants receive one year of access to all study materials, allowing ample time to complete the program at their convenience. The medium of instruction is English, and learning is facilitated through video and text-based content.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for the Streetwear Design certification course is ₹2,499. While the program provides valuable learning and skills, it's important to note that a formal certificate is not issued upon completion.

## Who is This Course For?

This course is ideal for aspiring fashion designers, current designers looking to specialize in streetwear, or anyone with a keen interest in understanding the nuances of contemporary fashion, cultural influences, and creative marketing within the streetwear industry.

## How to Apply

Details regarding the application process are typically available on The Designer's Class official platform. Interested individuals should visit their website to enroll and gain access to the course materials.

This program offers a unique opportunity to learn from an industry expert and delve into the vibrant world of streetwear design, equipping you with relevant skills for a dynamic career path.
