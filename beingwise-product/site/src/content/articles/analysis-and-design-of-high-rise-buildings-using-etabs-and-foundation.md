# Analysis and Design of High Rise Buildings using ETABS and Foundation Design using SAFE for Seismic Loads by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In our rapidly urbanizing world, the demand for high-rise structures is ever-increasing, making it crucial for civil engineers to master the design principles that ensure both safety and economic viability. This online course focuses on equipping civil engineering students and professionals with the essential skills for analyzing and designing tall buildings, specifically addressing seismic loads using industry-standard software.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 3-month online course offered by Skill Lync, delivered through video and text-based modules.
*   The curriculum is designed by industry experts, covering structural analysis and reinforced concrete design with a focus on ETABS and SAFE software.
*   Participants will gain practical skills in designing earthquake-resistant multi-story buildings and receive a completion certificate.
*   It is suitable for civil engineering students (3rd or final year) and postgraduate structural engineering students, with no prior experience required.

## Course Overview

This certification program, titled "Analysis and Design of High Rise Buildings using ETABS and Foundation Design using SAFE for Seismic Loads," is tailored for civil engineering students and professionals. As urban areas face shrinking land availability, the emphasis on constructing high-rise buildings that are both economical and structurally sound becomes paramount. The course delves into structural analysis and reinforced concrete design, with a particular focus on using ETABS and SAFE software to create code-compliant reinforced concrete and steel structures.

Successful completion of this program will help candidates enhance their professional portfolio and improve their career prospects by showcasing their specialized skills in high-rise building design.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this course, participants will develop a strong understanding of critical civil engineering skills, enabling them to:

*   Grasp the fundamentals of seismic design, including how it differs from wind design and the crucial role of ductility in earthquake resistance.
*   Understand and apply the principles of Structural Dynamics to Earthquake Engineering.
*   Gain in-depth knowledge of designing multi-story buildings to withstand seismic forces.
*   Learn to perform ductile reinforcement detailing according to Indian Standards, preparing detailed designs for various structural elements like beams, columns, beam-column joints, and shear walls.
