# Top MBA Colleges in Amritsar 2026: Ranking, Fees, Admission, Courses, Placements

Amritsar, a city renowned for its rich cultural heritage, is also emerging as a notable hub for quality management education. Pursuing an MBA here offers students the advantage of a serene yet dynamic environment for their studies. This guide provides essential information for prospective students considering MBA programs in Amritsar for the 2026 academic year.

## Key takeaways

*   **IIM Amritsar** stands out as the premier institution for MBA studies in the city, consistently featured in national rankings.
*   Admission to top MBA programs typically requires strong scores in national-level entrance exams like CAT, CMAT, and CUET-PG.
*   Fees for MBA programs in Amritsar can vary significantly, ranging from approximately ₹1.2 Lakh to over ₹14 Lakh, depending on the institution.
*   Placement opportunities are robust, particularly at IIM Amritsar, with competitive average salaries.

## Top MBA Colleges and Their Rankings

When evaluating MBA colleges, national rankings provide valuable insights into an institution's academic quality, faculty, infrastructure, and placements. For 2025-2026, the primary focus for rankings comes from sources like the NIRF (National Institutional Ranking Framework) by the Ministry of Education.

### Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Amritsar

IIM Amritsar is a leading management institute in India, consistently recognized in national rankings. For example, it secured an All India Rank of 64 in the NIRF 2025 rankings. It is highly regarded for its rigorous curriculum and strong industry connections.

### Other Notable Institutions

While IIM Amritsar leads, other institutions in the city also offer respected MBA/PGDM programs. These include:

*   **Guru Nanak Dev University (GNDU), Amritsar:** A well-established university offering a range of MBA/PGDM courses with varied specializations.
*   **Khalsa College Amritsar:** Known for its academic legacy, it also provides management education.
*   **Amritsar Group of Colleges:** Offers MBA programs with an emphasis on practical skills.
*   **Global Institutes, Amritsar:** Provides management courses with a focus on holistic development.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

The admission process for MBA programs in Amritsar generally involves several stages, starting with entrance exams.

### Entrance Exams

*   **IIM Amritsar:** Primarily accepts scores from the Common Admission Test (CAT). Candidates must meet the sectional and overall CAT cut-offs specified by the institute.
*   **Other Colleges:** Many other colleges accept scores from national exams like CMAT, CUET-PG, and sometimes their own institutional entrance tests.

### Eligibility Criteria

Typically, candidates need to hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university with a minimum aggregate score (often 50% for general category, 45% for reserved categories). Final year students are usually eligible to apply, provided they complete their degree within the stipulated time.

## Course Offerings and Fees

MBA programs in Amritsar offer various specializations to cater to diverse career aspirations.

### IIM Amritsar

IIM Amritsar's flagship program is the Post Graduate Programme (PGP) in Management, equivalent to an MBA. The total tuition fees for the program are approximately ₹14.20 Lakh.

## Placements and Career Prospects

Placements are a crucial factor for MBA aspirants, reflecting the return on investment for their education. Institutions in Amritsar, particularly IIM Amritsar, have a strong track record.

### IIM Amritsar Placements

IIM Amritsar consistently achieves excellent placements. The average salary package for its graduates is around ₹16.00 Lakh per annum. Top companies from various sectors, including consulting, finance, marketing, and IT, recruit from the campus.

### Other Colleges' Placements

*   **Guru Nanak Dev University:** Reports a median salary ranging from ₹0.64 LPA to ₹7.7 LPA for its management graduates.
*   **Khalsa College Amritsar:** Has a median salary of approximately ₹2.5 LPA.
*   **Amritsar Group of Colleges** and **Global Institutes** also facilitate placements, though specific median salary data may vary and should be confirmed with the respective institutions.

Choosing the right MBA college involves careful consideration of rankings, fees, admission criteria, and placement records. Amritsar offers a range of options, with IIM Amritsar leading the way for those seeking a top-tier management education.
