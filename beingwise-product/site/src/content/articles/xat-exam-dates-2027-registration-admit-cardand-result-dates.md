The Xavier Aptitude Test (XAT) is a crucial national-level entrance examination for admission to MBA and PGDM programs at XLRI Jamshedpur and numerous other esteemed management institutes across India. Understanding the key dates for XAT 2027 – from registration to results – is essential for every aspiring candidate.

## Key Takeaways

*   XAT 2027 is scheduled for January 3, 2027.
*   Registration is expected to open in the second week of July 2026 and close in the first week of December 2026.
*   The official XAT notification, containing all confirmed dates and details, will be released by XLRI Jamshedpur on its official website.
*   Admit cards will likely be available for download in December 2026.

## XAT 2027 Exam Schedule Overview

Staying informed about the XAT schedule is vital for effective preparation and application. Here’s a tentative overview of the important dates for XAT 2027:

| Event                          | Tentative Dates       |
| :----------------------------- | :-------------------- |
| Registration Start Date        | July 2026 (2nd week)  |
| Registration End Date          | December 2026 (1st week)|
| Mock Test Availability         | September - November 2026 |
| Application Form Correction    | October 2026          |
| Admit Card Release             | December 2026         |
| XAT 2027 Exam Date             | January 3, 2027       |
| Result Announcement            | January 2027 (Last week)|

*Please note that these dates are projections based on previous years' patterns. Candidates should always refer to the official XAT portal for the most accurate and up-to-date information.*

## XAT Notification Release

XLRI Jamshedpur, the conducting body for XAT, will issue the official XAT 2027 notification on its designated website, xatonline.in. This comprehensive notification is the primary source for all confirmed dates, including the registration window, admit card release, and the final exam date. It will also detail any structural changes to the exam, eligibility criteria, application fees, and the step-by-step application process. Aspiring candidates should regularly check the official XAT portal for this crucial announcement.

## XAT 2027 Registration Process

The XAT 2027 registration window is anticipated to open in the second week of July 2026 and close in the first week of December 2026. The entire application process is conducted online. Candidates will need to visit the official XAT website, create a unique login ID, and then proceed to fill out the detailed application form. It's important to accurately provide all personal, academic, and contact details. Along with the XAT application, candidates often need to indicate their preferred participating institutes. XLRI usually provides a brief window for form correction, allowing applicants to rectify any minor errors made during the initial submission.

## XAT Admit Card and Exam Day

Following successful registration, candidates will be able to download their XAT 2027 admit cards, likely in December 2026. The admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the examination. It contains vital information such as the candidate's name, roll number, photograph, the allocated test center address, exam date, and reporting time, along with important instructions for exam day. Candidates must carry a printed copy of their admit card to the test center. The XAT exam is typically held in a single shift across more than 100 test centers nationwide, usually on the first Sunday of January. For XAT 2027, this falls on January 3.

## XAT 2027 Result Announcement

The XAT 2027 results are generally declared by the last week of January 2027. Candidates can access their scorecards by logging into the official XAT website using their credentials. The scorecard will display sectional and overall scores, along with percentile ranks. These scores are then used by XLRI and other XAT-accepting institutes for shortlisting candidates for subsequent selection rounds, which typically include Group Discussions and Personal Interviews.

Planning your XAT journey around these critical dates will help ensure a smooth application process and focused preparation.
