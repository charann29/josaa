Masai School offers a range of online courses designed to equip students with in-demand tech skills. These programs aim to make individuals job-ready in various fields like web development, Android development, and UI/UX design, often with innovative payment models.

## Key Takeaways

*   Masai School provides full-time and part-time online certification courses in high-demand tech skills.
*   Programs cover areas like Full-Stack Web Development, Full-Stack Android Development, and UI/UX Design.
*   Many courses feature an Income Share Agreement (ISA) where fees are paid only after securing a job with a minimum salary.
*   The curriculum is industry-focused, developed by experienced professionals, and includes practical projects and career support.

## Full-Stack Web Development Programs

Masai School offers two distinct pathways for aspiring web developers: a full-time and a part-time option. Both are designed to provide comprehensive training in modern web technologies.

### Full-Stack Web Development (Full-Time)

This intensive 30-week program is ideal for those who can dedicate themselves fully to learning. Students receive expert guidance from technical instructors and a dedicated career coach. The curriculum, crafted by accomplished developers, covers essential programming languages and frameworks like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, preparing students to become proficient software developers. A significant benefit of this course is its Income Share Agreement (ISA) model, allowing students to pay tuition only after securing a job with an annual salary of ₹5 lakhs or more.

### Full-Stack Web Development (Part-Time)

For individuals balancing other commitments, the 52-week part-time online course offers flexibility. This program is structured into six weekly modules, focusing on emerging technologies to ensure graduates are industry-ready. It reinforces fundamental concepts such as loops, arrays, JavaScript, DOM, and CSS. Upon completion, students are well-prepared for diverse roles including web developer, software engineer, full-stack developer, and backend engineer. Experienced engineers and faculty deliver the training.

## Full-Stack Android Development

This 30-week full-time program is tailored for those aspiring to become Android developers. The curriculum is developed to meet current industry demands, focusing on popular technologies. Students engage in daily stand-up sessions, meditation breaks, and receive coding assistance from peers, Sherpas, and Teaching Assistants (TAs) via platforms like Slack. The course emphasizes practical application through projects, fostering a deeper understanding of concepts like System Design, Android architectures, and user interface design. Key tools and languages covered include Java, Kotlin, Android, Gradle, Spring Boot, algorithms, and data structures.

## UI/UX Design

Masai School's UI/UX Design course, spanning 30 weeks, is a full-time program aimed at developing skilled user interface and user experience designers. The curriculum covers essential design principles, tools, and methodologies. Students learn to create intuitive and engaging digital experiences, focusing on user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. This program also includes the Income Share Agreement (ISA) model, where students pay the course fee only after securing a job with a minimum annual salary of ₹5 lakhs.

Masai School's commitment to practical, industry-relevant education, combined with flexible payment options, makes its programs an attractive choice for many aspiring tech professionals.
