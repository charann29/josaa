# LPUNEST Exam 2026: Registration (Started), Eligibility, Slot Booking, Question Paper, Result, Counselling, Cutoff

Lovely Professional University National Entrance and Scholarship Test (LPUNEST) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to join various undergraduate and postgraduate programs at LPU. This guide covers all essential details for the 2026 examination cycle.

## Key takeaways

*   LPUNEST 2026 Phase 3 registration and slot booking have commenced.
*   The exam is offered in both remote-proctored and test-centre based formats.
*   Eligibility generally requires a 10+2 qualification for undergraduate programs.
*   Scholarships are available based on both qualifying exam marks and LPUNEST performance.

## Understanding LPUNEST 2026

LPUNEST is an important entrance and scholarship test conducted by Lovely Professional University for admission to over 150 diverse programs, including B.Tech, Law, Agriculture, Design, Hotel Management, and MBA. Each year, approximately 75,000 candidates participate in this examination.

## Important Dates for LPUNEST 2026 (Phase 3)

Keeping track of key dates is vital for a smooth application process. Here are the important dates for LPUNEST 2026 Phase 3:

*   **Registration Start Date:** March 26, 2026
*   **Slot Booking Start Date:** March 26, 2026
*   **Remote Proctored Exam Dates:** April 4 to April 20, 2026
*   **Test Centre-Based Exam Dates:** April 4 to April 30, 2026

### Previous Phases at a Glance

While Phase 3 is currently active, it's helpful to note the timeline of earlier phases:

*   **Phase 1 Results:** Announced on February 8, 2026
*   **Phase 2 Test Centre Exams:** Began on March 15, 2026
*   **Phase 2 Counselling Registration:** Commenced on April 3, 2026

## Eligibility and Application Process

To be eligible for LPUNEST 2026, candidates generally need to have qualified their 10+2 examination. The application form for Phase 3 is available on the official LPU website. Once registered, candidates can proceed with slot booking to select their preferred exam date and time. After successful slot booking, the admit card for LPUNEST 2026 can be downloaded online.

### Preparing for the Exam

Candidates are strongly advised to familiarize themselves with the official LPUNEST 2026 syllabus and exam pattern. Understanding these aspects is crucial for effective preparation and performing well in the examination.

## Results, Counselling, and Scholarships

### Results and Cutoff

The LPUNEST 2026 results are announced in phases. For instance, Phase 1 results were declared on February 8, 2026. Candidates who meet the LPUNEST cutoff 2026 are eligible to participate in the counselling process.

### Counselling Process

The counselling for LPUNEST 2026 is conducted online. To check their seat allotment, candidates will need to log in using their application number and password. It's important to monitor the official LPU portal for specific counselling registration and allotment dates, such as the Phase 2 counselling registration which began on April 3, 2026.

### Scholarship Opportunities

LPU offers significant scholarship opportunities to deserving candidates. These scholarships are generally merit-based and can be availed through various channels, including performance in qualifying examinations and the LPUNEST itself.

Here's a general overview of scholarship criteria based on academic performance:

| Scholarship Bracket | Criteria (after 10+2/Diploma/Lateral Entry) | Criteria (after Graduation Programmes) |
| :------------------ | :------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------- |
| Bracket - 1         | 98% or above in qualifying exam              | 95% or above in qualifying exam        |
| Bracket - 2         | 95% to 97.99% in qualifying exam             | 90% to 94.99% in qualifying exam       |
| Bracket - 3         | 90% to 94.99% in qualifying exam             | 80% to 89.99% in qualifying exam       |
| Bracket - 4         | 80% to 89.99% in qualifying exam             | 70% to 79.99% in qualifying exam       |

Scholarships are also awarded based on LPUNEST performance, categorized into different slabs based on scores equal to or higher than Category A or Category B cut-offs. For example, depending on the program group, candidates scoring above the Category A cut-off might receive 35% to 60% of the program fee as a scholarship, while those above Category B cut-off might receive 30% to 45%.
