# Aegis School of Business Data Science Cyber Security and Telecommunication Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Aegis School of Business Data Science Cyber Security and Telecommunication Courses

Aegis School of Business, Data Science, Cyber Security, and Telecommunication offers specialized programs designed to equip professionals and aspiring leaders with cutting-edge skills in high-demand fields. These programs are often developed in collaboration with industry leaders like IBM, providing a blend of academic rigor and practical industry insights.

## Key takeaways

*   Aegis School offers specialized postgraduate and executive programs in data science, business analytics, and telecommunication management.
*   Many programs are developed in association with IBM, integrating industry expertise into the curriculum.
*   Courses are delivered in various formats, including hybrid (part-time online, part-time in-person) and fully online live classes.
*   The curriculum is designed to foster both technical and leadership skills relevant to modern business and technology sectors.

## Hybrid EPGP in Data Science, Business Analytics, and Big Data (in association with IBM)

This 12-month Hybrid Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP) is a flagship offering from Aegis School, developed in collaboration with IBM. It holds the distinction of being recognized as one of India's pioneering high-end data science programs. The hybrid format involves weekday classes conducted at the Centre for Excellence in Telecom Technology and Management (CETTM) in Mumbai, complemented by online sessions on Sundays.

### Program Focus and Skills

The curriculum is meticulously structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of data science, business analytics, and big data. It features a unique combination of core and elective courses, designed to develop essential skills for a successful career in these rapidly evolving domains. Graduates are expected to gain strong technical and quantitative abilities, enabling them to create significant business and social value across various industries. The program offers hands-on exposure to a range of industry-standard tools and software.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Data science methodologies
*   Business analytics techniques
*   Big Data concepts and applications

## Post Graduate Program in Telecommunication Management (in association with IBM)

This 11-month certificate program, a joint offering by Aegis and IBM, is recognized as a leading program in Telecommunication Management. It has benefited executives from over 35 countries, including professionals from telecom service providers and vendors. The program aims to provide participants with a deep understanding of information technology and telecommunication operations from critical leadership perspectives, encompassing finance, management, and legal aspects.

### Career Impact

Candidates completing this program are prepared for roles as technology managers, equipped with the confidence to make informed decisions that contribute to the growth and improvement of the telecom management business. The course also focuses on enhancing specific functional skills relevant to individual participants' domains.

**Skills Covered:**

*   In-depth knowledge of the IT and telecommunication industry
*   Strategic management in telecom

## Executive Masters Program in Telecom Management (Online Live, in association with IBM)

Another distinguished 11-month program from Aegis and IBM, this Executive Masters Program is delivered entirely online in a live format. It is widely regarded as one of India's premier telecom management courses, often considered equivalent to an MBA in Telecommunication. The program benefits from the combined expertise of IBM and Aegis professionals.

### Industry-Driven Curriculum

The curriculum for this program is continuously updated with valuable input and suggestions from major telecommunication companies such as Qualcomm, Bharti Airtel, Amdocs, Ericsson, ZTE, Reliance Communication, and BSNL. This ensures that the program addresses both the current and future needs of telecom managers and leaders, preparing participants to become future leaders in the dynamic world of information technology and telecommunication.

Aegis School's commitment to industry collaboration ensures that its programs remain relevant and impactful, preparing students for leadership roles in the fast-paced technology and business landscape.
