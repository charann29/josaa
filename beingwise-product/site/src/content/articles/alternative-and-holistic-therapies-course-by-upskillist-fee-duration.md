# Alternative and Holistic Therapies Course by Upskillist: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Are you curious about the world of natural healing and well-being? The Alternative and Holistic Therapies course offered by Upskillist provides a comprehensive online introduction to various holistic practices, suitable for anyone looking to understand these fields better.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 16-week online course, delivered through self-paced video and text lessons in English.
*   It covers a wide range of topics from aromatherapy and reflexology to yoga and stress management.
*   Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate, with an additional certificate issued by Austin Peay State University.
*   The program includes educator support and offers a free trial period to explore the content.

## Course Overview

This online program is designed to introduce learners to the core principles and practices of alternative and holistic therapies. Spread over 16 weeks, the curriculum is structured into four modules with 32 distinct lessons. The flexible self-study format allows students to access course materials – a mix of video and text – at their convenience, making it accessible for individuals from diverse academic backgrounds and schedules.

Throughout the course, you'll delve into various aspects of holistic health. Topics include understanding a wellness lifestyle, the basics of massage, an introduction to meditation, reflexology mapping, fundamental fitness concepts, Ayurvedic healing, Pilates for beginners, natural skincare, stress management techniques, mindful eating, and general self-care practices. A significant focus is placed on meditation, exploring its components and effective methods for developing moment-to-moment awareness.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to provide a broad understanding of alternative therapies. You will gain knowledge in several key areas, including:

*   Detailed techniques and procedures involved in aromatherapy.
*   Various methods and approaches to massage.
*   The significance of energy channels within holistic healing systems.
*   The importance of fitness and its role in overall well-being.
*   Fundamental principles of nutrition.
*   Understanding pressure point therapy systems.
*   Processes related to crystal healing and body chakras.
*   The basics of mindfulness.
*   An introduction to acupuncture.
*   The principles and practices of yoga.

## Program Highlights and Offerings

The Upskillist Alternative and Holistic Therapies course comes with several valuable features:

*   **Free Trial:** You can start with a trial period to experience the course content before committing.
*   **Globally Recognised:** The program aims to provide internationally applicable knowledge.
*   **Dual Certification:** A certificate is issued by Upskillist, with an additional certificate provided by Austin Peay State University.
*   **Flexible Learning:** The 16-week duration is self-paced, allowing you to pause the course if needed.
*   **Educator Support:** Access to live chat support from educators is available.
*   **Lifetime Access:** Once enrolled, you get lifetime access to the lesson materials.
*   **Offline Learning:** Materials can be accessed offline, enhancing flexibility.
*   **Assessments and Exams:** The course includes assessments and exams to gauge your understanding.

## Eligibility and Certification

There are no specific prior academic eligibility criteria mentioned for enrolling in this course, making it open to a wide audience. To receive the Alternative and Holistic Therapies Course Certification, students must successfully complete the entire 16-week program, including any associated assessments.

## Fees and How to Apply

While the source mentions a free trial, specific fee details for the full course are not provided upfront. After completing the free trial, payment will be required to continue accessing the full course content. The application process is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official Upskillist website for the Alternative and Holistic Therapies course.
2.  Look for and click on the 'Start your free trial' option.
3.  You will then be guided through a series of multiple-choice questions.
4.  Enter your personal details to create a login ID and gain access.
5.  You can then begin the course with the free trial. To continue beyond the trial, you will need to make the required payment.

This course offers a fantastic opportunity to explore the diverse and enriching world of alternative and holistic therapies from the comfort of your home.
