# OJEE 2026: Exam Date (OUT), Form Correction, Admit Card, Syllabus, Pattern

The Odisha Joint Entrance Examination (OJEE) is a key state-level entrance exam for students seeking admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Odisha. It's conducted by the OJEE Cell annually to facilitate entry into a wide range of professional courses.

## Key takeaways

*   **Exam Dates:** OJEE 2026 is scheduled to take place on May 4, 5, 7, 8, and 9.
*   **Application Window:** The registration period for OJEE 2026 ran from January 28 to March 29, 2026.
*   **Form Correction:** A dedicated window for application corrections was available from March 30 to April 1, 2026.
*   **Mode of Exam:** OJEE is conducted as a Computer-Based Test (CBT).

## Understanding OJEE: What it is and courses offered

OJEE, or the Odisha Joint Entrance Examination, is a crucial state-level examination administered by the OJEE Cell. Its primary purpose is to streamline admissions into numerous B.Pharm, MBA/PGDM, MCA, and M.Tech programs across participating institutions in Odisha. Beyond these, OJEE also serves as an entry point for lateral entry B.Tech and MCA courses. Additionally, specific programs like those in Cinematic Art and Technology at Biju Pattanaik Film and Television Institute of Odisha (BPFTIO), and M.Sc Computer Science, utilize OJEE scores for admissions.

Here are some of the key courses accessible through OJEE:

*   MBA/PGDM
*   BAMS, BHMS
*   MCA, MCA (Lateral Entry)
*   B.Pharma, B.Pharma (Lateral Entry)
*   B.Tech (Lateral Entry)
*   M.Tech
*   M.Pharma
*   M.Arch
*   M.Plan
*   BCAT
*   M.Sc

## OJEE 2026 Admit Card

The OJEE 2026 Admit Card is a mandatory document for appearing in the examination. It will be released on April 25, 2026, on the official OJEE portal. Candidates must download and print their admit card, as it contains vital information like their roll number, exam center details, and exam timings. Entry to the examination hall will not be permitted without a valid admit card and a government-issued photo ID.

## OJEE 2026 Syllabus and Exam Pattern

The OJEE syllabus varies significantly depending on the course a candidate applies for. For instance, the OJEE MBA syllabus typically covers sections such as Quantitative Techniques, Verbal Reasoning and Comprehension, Analytical and Logical Reasoning, and General Awareness and Business Fundamentals. It's essential for candidates to refer to the official OJEE information bulletin for the detailed syllabus specific to their chosen program.

Regarding the exam pattern, OJEE is conducted in a Computer-Based Test (CBT) format. The duration and number of questions also differ per course. Each program has a distinct question paper structure designed to assess relevant aptitudes and knowledge. Detailed information about the marking scheme and paper structure for each course is available on the official OJEE website.

Preparation for OJEE requires a focused approach, understanding the specific syllabus and exam pattern for your chosen course, and regular practice.
