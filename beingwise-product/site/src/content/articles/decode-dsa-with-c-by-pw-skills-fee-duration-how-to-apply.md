# Decode DSA with C++ by PW Skills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students aspiring to build strong foundational programming skills and launch a career in software development, the "Decode DSA with C++" certification from PW Skills offers a structured online learning path. This program focuses on Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) using the C++ language, a critical skill set for many tech roles.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 6-month online certification course delivered in English, primarily through self-study with video and text-based materials.
*   The program includes over 150 hours of instruction, quizzes in every module, and a certificate upon successful completion.
*   Learners gain access to live and recorded lectures, practice exercises, doubt-clearing sessions, and resume-building support.
*   The total course fee is ₹5,500, with an EMI payment option available for flexibility.

## Course Overview

The "Decode DSA with C++" course is designed to equip students with comprehensive knowledge of Data Structures and Algorithms using C++. Over six months, participants will delve into programming fundamentals, understand time and space complexity, explore the importance of Object-Oriented Programming (OOPs), and master concepts like binary trees and graphs. The ultimate goal is to prepare individuals for roles as software developers by making them proficient in these core areas.

Learning is facilitated through a blend of live sessions, recorded lectures, and interactive components like doubt-clearing sessions and practice exercises within the PWLab environment. To earn the official certificate, students must complete all assignments and quizzes, demonstrating their grasp of the course material.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a deep dive into C++ programming and essential data structures. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **C++ Fundamentals:** Mastering the basics of C++ programming, including output, variables, input, and operators. Familiarity with development tools like GCC, IDEs, and VS Code.
*   **Data Structures:** Understanding both linear data structures (like arrays and strings) and non-linear data structures (such as binary trees and graphs).
*   **Advanced Concepts:** Exploring Object-Oriented Programming (OOPs) paradigms, backtracking techniques, and graph terminology.

This comprehensive syllabus ensures that students develop a robust understanding of how to design efficient and scalable software solutions.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for anyone looking to build or enhance their expertise in Data Structures and Algorithms with C++. While particularly beneficial for aspiring software developers, it also caters to professionals already in the field who wish to upgrade their skills. Specifically, it can be valuable for:

*   Aspiring Software Developers
*   Current Software Engineers looking to strengthen their DSA knowledge
*   Software Architects seeking to reinforce fundamental concepts

## Course Fees and Certification

The total fee for the "Decode DSA with C++" certification is ₹5,500. For convenience, an EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) option is available, allowing students to pay the course fee in manageable installments. Upon successfully completing all video assignments and quizzes, students will receive a certificate from PW Skills, validating their proficiency in the course content.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the "Decode DSA with C++" course is a straightforward process. Interested candidates can follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official course page on the PW Skills platform.
2.  Locate and click on the "Enrol Now" button.
3.  Provide the necessary registration details as prompted.
4.  Proceed with the payment of the course fee (or select the EMI option).
5.  Once the payment is confirmed, you can begin your learning journey.

By following these steps, aspirants can quickly gain access to the course materials and start their path to mastering DSA with C++.
