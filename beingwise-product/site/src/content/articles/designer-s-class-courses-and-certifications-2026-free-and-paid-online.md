# The Designer's Class Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online The Designer's Class Courses

BeingWise presents an overview of popular design-focused courses and certifications offered by The Designer's Class, an online platform dedicated to creative education. These programs are crafted to help aspiring designers and enthusiasts develop practical skills across various creative fields.

## Key Takeaways

*   The Designer's Class offers a range of online courses in fields like photography and jewellery design.
*   Programs are taught by industry experts, including renowned photographers and business leaders.
*   Courses cater to different skill levels, from beginners to intermediate learners.
*   Many certifications include practical demonstrations, study materials, and access to course content for a specified period.

## Introduction to Photography

This certification course, designed and led by well-known Bollywood photographer Rohan Shrestha, introduces participants to the fundamentals of photography. Spanning 48 hours, the program is ideal for anyone interested in understanding how cameras work and the core skills needed in professional photography.

The curriculum is highly practical, featuring demonstration-based lessons and comprehensive study materials. Students will learn essential camera principles and gain insights into various lighting setups, including indoor, beauty, outdoor, and advertisement lighting techniques. This course aims to equip even complete beginners with a solid foundation in photographic knowledge.

## Wedding Photography

For those looking to specialize, The Designer's Class offers a Wedding Photography course led by IIM graduate and acclaimed wedding photographer Joseph Radhik. This intermediate-level program, with a duration of 3 hours and 31 minutes, delves into the intricacies of capturing special moments.

The course focuses on understanding client expectations to consistently deliver high-quality services. Participants will learn how to plan and manage wedding photoshoots effectively and gain strategies for career growth within the wedding photography industry.

## The Business of Jewellery by De Beers Forevermark

This beginner-friendly online certification, instructed by Sachin Jain, explores the commercial aspects of the jewellery industry. Offered through The Designer's Class, the program consists of 16 lessons and a total learning duration of 48 hours.

Students will gain insights into various jewellery business models and the backend operations of the industry. Key topics include developing a business plan and understanding the critical role of inventory and merchandise planning. Upon purchase, participants receive one year of access to the complete course. Successful completion of a final assessment is required to earn a certificate from The Designer's Class, and high-achieving students may also have internship opportunities.

## Lab-Grown Diamonds by Limelight

Another specialized offering from The Designer's Class is the Lab-Grown Diamonds by Limelight certification. Taught by Pooja Seth, this short online course is designed for beginners and focuses on the creation process of diamonds in laboratory settings.

The 48-hour program, delivered in English, covers the advantages of using lab-grown diamonds and their application in designing various jewellery pieces. It provides a comprehensive introduction to this evolving segment of the diamond industry.

These programs from The Designer's Class provide accessible pathways for individuals to learn from industry experts and enhance their creative and business skills in design-related fields.
