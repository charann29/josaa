# Diploma in the Principles of Drug Delivery by Alison via NPTEL: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This diploma program delves into the fundamental principles behind how therapeutic drugs are designed, formulated, and delivered within the body. It's an excellent opportunity for anyone interested in the science of medicine and pharmaceutical development.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is offered completely free of charge, with an optional certificate available from Alison.
*   It covers essential topics like pharmacokinetics, biomedical polymers, and advanced delivery systems, including nano- and micro-particles.
*   The learning is delivered through video-based modules, making it flexible for students.
*   Two assessments are included midway through the course, followed by a final course assessment.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Diploma in the Principles of Drug Delivery is structured into several modules, guiding learners through various aspects of drug formulation and action. The curriculum progresses from foundational concepts to more complex topics, ensuring a comprehensive understanding.

The course content is delivered in English and is entirely video-based, providing an accessible learning experience. There are also two assessment modules embedded within the course, designed to test understanding of the material covered up to that point, before a final comprehensive assessment at the end.

## Detailed Syllabus

The program is divided into distinct modules, each focusing on a critical area of drug delivery science. Here’s a breakdown of what you will learn:

### Module 1: Introduction to Drug Delivery and Pharmacokinetics

This introductory module sets the stage by explaining the basics of drug delivery and how drugs move through and are processed by the body (pharmacokinetics). It also introduces pro-drugs and various types of polymers, discussing their synthesis and properties relevant to drug formulations.

### Module 2: Biomedical Polymers and Controlled Systems

Building on the first module, this section explores specific biomedical polymers, including biodegradable ones. You'll learn about drug conjugates, diffusion-controlled systems, and the principles behind controlled drug release mechanisms.

### Module 3: First Assessment

This module is dedicated to the first assessment, evaluating your grasp of the concepts introduced in Modules 1 and 2.

### Module 4: Release Systems and Hydrogels

Here, the course delves deeper into various controlled release systems, distinguishing between erodible and non-erodible systems. It also covers the fundamentals of hydrogels – their definition, synthesis, cross-linking, and mathematical treatments related to their drug release properties.

### Module 5: Nano- and Micro-Particles

This advanced module focuses on the exciting field of nano- and micro-particles in drug delivery. It covers different types of particle synthesis, including solvent evaporation, and explores how these tiny particles function in drug delivery, including liposomes, micelles, and the impact of shape, charge, and elasticity.

### Module 6: Protein Adsorption

This module examines the interaction of proteins with biomaterials. You will learn about adsorption to biomaterial surfaces, the role of pre-absorbed proteins, and methods for quantifying protein adsorption.

### Module 7: Second Assessment

This module is for the second assessment, covering the material from Modules 4, 5, and 6.

### Module 8: Course Assessment

This final module contains the comprehensive assessment for the entire diploma program.

## Fees and Certification

One of the most appealing aspects of this program is that the course content itself is completely free to access. While the learning material is available without any cost, students have the option to purchase a certificate upon successful completion of the course from Alison, the certificate-providing authority.

## How to Apply

To enroll in this Diploma, you typically need to visit the official Alison platform. Search for the "Diploma in the Principles of Drug Delivery" to find the course page. You will likely need to create an account on their platform to begin your learning journey.

This diploma offers a valuable foundation in a crucial area of pharmaceutical science, accessible to all interested learners.
