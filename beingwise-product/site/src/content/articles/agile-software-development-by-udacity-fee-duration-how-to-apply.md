# Agile Software Development by Udacity: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to enhance their understanding of modern software project management, the Agile Software Development Nanodegree program from Udacity offers a flexible, online learning path. This program is designed for professionals and students keen on mastering agile methodologies in the tech world.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 100% online Nanodegree program with flexible learning, typically completed in about three months of dedicated study.
*   No specific prior educational background is required, though basic computer and internet skills are recommended.
*   Students receive personalized feedback, project reviews from experienced mentors, and career support services like resume and LinkedIn profile reviews.
*   The program focuses on practical application, including real-world projects and interview preparation.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

The Agile Software Development Nanodegree is structured for self-paced learning, delivered through video lectures and text-based materials. While the duration is flexible, successful students often complete the curriculum within approximately three months, committing about 10 hours per week. The program emphasizes hands-on experience, providing opportunities to work on real-world projects, which are then reviewed by experienced professionals who offer personalized feedback.

Beyond technical learning, the program also includes robust career support. This encompasses assistance with crafting an effective resume, optimizing your LinkedIn profile, and preparing for job interviews. Students also benefit from technical mentor support and a vibrant mentor-led student community and forums for collaborative learning and problem-solving.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of this Nanodegree, participants will gain practical skills in various aspects of agile software development. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Developing a release plan for a Minimum Viable Product (MVP).
*   Creating metrics to accurately track project status and progress.
*   Effectively communicating project updates both within and outside the development team.
*   Differentiating between popular agile frameworks such such as Scrum, Kanban, and Extreme Programming (XP).
*   Fostering an environment conducive to high-performing teams.
*   Managing iteration planning using established agile techniques.
*   Understanding the foundational principles of agile and various agile frameworks.
*   Delivering value through agile planning and prioritization strategies.
*   Enhancing progress tracking, communication, and overall organizational agility.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

One of the strengths of this program is its accessibility. There are no strict educational prerequisites to enroll. While a background in computer science is beneficial, it is not mandatory. The main requirement is a basic familiarity with computers and fundamental internet navigation skills. The program is ideal for a diverse range of individuals, including software developers, project managers, product managers, product development managers, and Scrum Masters, or anyone aspiring to these roles.

## Fees and Certification

Udacity offers flexible payment options for the Agile Software Development Nanodegree. You can choose to pay monthly while you are enrolled, or opt for a bundled payment plan which typically offers a discount. Financial support may also be available for eligible students who demonstrate need.

| Fee Component        | Amount (Approximate) |
| :------------------- | :------------------- |
| Monthly Access       | ₹11,590 / month      |
| Bundled Access       | ₹9,852 / month       |
| Individual Course    | ₹46,499.75 (one-time) |

*Please note that these figures are approximate and subject to change by Udacity.*

Upon successful completion of all course modules and projects, Udacity awards a certification for the Agile Software Development Nanodegree. This certification validates your mastery of the program's content and your ability to apply agile principles in real-world scenarios.

## How to Apply

The application process for the Udacity Agile Software Development Nanodegree is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official Udacity platform.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Sign-in' option.
3.  Create a new account if you don't already have one.
4.  Navigate to the Agile Software Development Nanodegree program page.
5.  Click the 'Enroll Now' button to begin your registration.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to gain essential skills for a thriving career in software development and project management.
