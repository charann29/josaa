# Dental Assistant by MedCerts: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aspiring to enter the dental field, the MedCerts Dental Assistant program offers a pathway to a fulfilling career. This program is designed to equip students with the essential knowledge and skills needed to support dentists and provide excellent patient care.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is delivered entirely online, offering flexibility for learners.
*   It focuses on crucial areas like dental anatomy, infection control, and radiation safety.
*   The total fee for the course is $4,000, with monthly payment plans available.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from MedCerts.

## Course Structure and Learning Experience

The MedCerts Dental Assistant program is structured for self-paced learning, delivered through a combination of video and text-based materials. The medium of instruction is English. Students are encouraged to dedicate approximately 15-20 hours per week to their studies to keep up with the curriculum and complete the program effectively.

## Program Fees

The total cost for the Dental Assistant course is $4,000. To make this accessible, MedCerts also offers monthly payment options, which can be as low as $367 per month. This allows students to manage their finances while investing in their professional development.

## What You Will Learn: The Syllabus

The curriculum covers a range of vital topics essential for a competent dental assistant. These include:

*   **Professionalism in Allied Health:** Understanding ethical conduct, communication skills, and the importance of a professional demeanor in a healthcare setting.
*   **Radiation Health and Safety in Dentistry:** Learning about safe practices for dental X-rays, including proper handling of equipment and patient protection.
*   **Dental Assisting Essentials:** Core duties and responsibilities of a dental assistant, from chairside assistance to administrative tasks.
*   **Dental Anatomy, Morphology, and Physiology:** A deep dive into the structure, form, and function of teeth and surrounding oral tissues.
*   **Infection Control in Dental Healthcare Settings:** Mastering protocols and procedures to prevent the spread of infections, crucial for patient and staff safety.

## Certification

Upon successful completion of all course requirements, students will be awarded a certificate by MedCerts. This certification validates the skills and knowledge acquired through the program, enhancing career prospects in the dental field.

Embarking on this program can be a significant step towards a rewarding career as a dental assistant, providing you with the foundational expertise to thrive in a dental practice.
