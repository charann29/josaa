# Cybersecurity Law & Policy by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding the legal landscape of the digital world is becoming increasingly vital. This online certification course offers a foundational insight into the rules and regulations governing cybersecurity and internet governance.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn about the rapidly evolving field of cybersecurity law, including privacy, cyberattacks, and digital currencies.
*   Gain practical knowledge on recognising key legal concerns in cybersecurity and understanding when professional legal advice is needed.
*   Benefit from a flexible, self-paced learning structure with pre-recorded video content and downloadable resources.
*   Receive a certificate of completion upon successfully finishing the course.

## Course Overview

The online certification in Cybersecurity Law & Policy is designed to introduce learners to the critical legal aspects of the digital age. Developed by Professor Victoria Sutton, a law expert, the program covers essential topics such as internet governance, data privacy, and the legal implications of cyberattacks and digital currencies like Bitcoin.

The course structure includes approximately two hours of video lectures, complemented by eight downloadable resources. These materials explore the current state of cybersecurity laws and delve into future trends, addressing subjects like net neutrality, cybercrime, the Internet of Things (IoT), and social media's role in litigation. By the end of this program, participants will be equipped to identify significant cybersecurity legal issues and understand when to seek an attorney's counsel.

## What You Will Learn

This course provides a comprehensive understanding of cybersecurity principles and the legal frameworks that govern the internet. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Internet Governance and Cybersecurity Laws:** Develop an in-depth understanding of the foundational principles.
*   **Cybercrime and Cyberattacks:** Explore strategies for dealing with and mitigating the impact of digital crimes.
*   **Social Media Implications:** Learn about the legal ramifications of social media in litigation scenarios.
*   **Digital Technologies:** Examine the advantages and disadvantages associated with the Internet of Things (IoT) and digital currencies such as Bitcoin.

## Who Is This Course For?

This certification is particularly beneficial for professionals and aspiring individuals in various fields who need to navigate the complexities of cybersecurity law. Ideal candidates include:

*   Lawyers (especially corporate and cyber lawyers)
*   Entrepreneurs
*   Scientists
*   Management consultants

The program is structured into two main sections, covering fundamental concepts and specific areas within cybersecurity law:

### Foundations in Cybersecurity Law

*   Introduction to Cybersecurity Law

*   Cybersecurity Law and Net Neutrality
