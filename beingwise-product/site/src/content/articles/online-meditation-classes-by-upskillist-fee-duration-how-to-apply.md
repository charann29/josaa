# Online Meditation Classes by Upskillist: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Discover how a professional online meditation course can help you cultivate inner peace and enhance your overall well-being. This program offers practical techniques to manage your mind and emotions effectively.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 16-week, self-paced online course certified by Austin Peay State University.
*   The curriculum features 32 lessons, weekly assignments, and expert-curated content.
*   No specific academic qualifications are needed; anyone interested in meditation can enroll.
*   The course includes downloadable lectures and live chat support from educators.

## Course Overview

This online meditation certification is designed to help individuals unlock their potential and achieve greater personal growth. Categorized under 'Continuing Education' by Upskillist, the program is entirely delivered online through a combination of video and text-based lessons.

The curriculum is structured into four modules, containing a total of 32 lessons and accompanying in-course assignments. These high-quality materials are developed by subject matter experts, ensuring you receive training that is both relevant and effective. The course aims to equip you with smart strategies to navigate life's challenges and release unhelpful thoughts and emotions.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of all modules, you will gain practical knowledge and skills in various aspects of mindfulness and self-awareness. The program covers essential topics such as:

*   Understanding the benefits of mindfulness practices.
*   Techniques for cultivating and practicing gratitude.
*   Methods for letting go of unwanted thoughts and emotions.
*   The importance and practice of self-reflection.
*   Introduction to Yoga Nidra.
*   Strategies for integrating meditation into your daily life.

## Course Structure and Content

The 16-week program provides a comprehensive learning experience with a flexible online format. The course content is globally recognized and includes:

*   **Module 1: Diploma in Mindful Practice** - Focuses on foundational concepts like bringing your whole self, cultivating gratitude, understanding mind-body connections, addressing stress, and practicing everyday mindfulness.
*   **Module 2: Intermediate in Mindful Practice** - Explores topics such as the art of just being, waking up from autopilot, meditation in motion, emotional intelligence, and building support systems.
*   **Module 3: Advanced Mindful Practice** - (Details from source suggest this module continues the progression of topics, building on the previous two.)

Each module comprises several lessons designed for a smooth and consistent learning journey. You'll also benefit from weekly assignments and the ability to download lectures for offline access.

## Fees and Certification

While specific fee details are not provided in the source, the course does offer a free trial, allowing you to experience the content before committing. Upon successful completion, a professional certification will be issued by Upskillist, confirming your proficiency in the learned meditation practices.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Online Meditation Classes is a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official Upskillist website.
2.  **Find the Course:** Look for the 'Course Catalogue' tab, then select 'Health Wellness', and finally choose 'Online Meditation Classes' from the available options. This will direct you to the dedicated course page.
3.  **Start Your Free Trial:** On the course webpage, click the 'Start your free trial' button. You will be prompted to sign up and create a student ID using your email address.
4.  **Access the Course:** After successful registration, you will gain unlimited access to the professional course content with your new student ID.

This course offers a valuable opportunity to develop mental clarity and emotional resilience through structured meditation practices.
