# DataMites Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online DataMites Courses

DataMites offers a range of online and offline programs designed to equip students and professionals with essential skills in the rapidly growing fields of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. These courses aim to provide practical knowledge and industry-relevant certifications.

## Key Takeaways

*   DataMites provides comprehensive programs in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, catering to various skill levels.
*   Courses often include live sessions, practical projects, and dedicated placement support to enhance career prospects.
*   Many certifications are globally recognized through accreditation bodies like IABAC.
*   Learning options typically include blended learning, classroom training, and live virtual sessions for flexibility.

## Artificial Intelligence Engineer Course

This advanced program from DataMites focuses on developing expertise in Artificial Intelligence. It features over 120 hours of live instruction and lectures, complemented by an internship opportunity involving real-world AI projects. Students gain access to all course materials for one year after completing the training.

The curriculum is developed with industry requirements in mind, ensuring that the skills taught are relevant and in demand. To accommodate different learning preferences, the course is offered in three formats: blended learning, traditional classroom training, and live virtual sessions.

### Key Details:

*   **Duration:** 9 Months
*   **Approximate Fee:** ₹1,54,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence concepts.

## Certified Data Scientist Course

DataMites' Certified Data Scientist program is a highly specialized offering that delves into the core principles of data science. It provides a holistic learning experience by integrating statistics, machine learning, and essential business domain knowledge.

While the course primarily emphasizes Python, it also includes content on R for professionals who work with that language. Throughout the program, participants benefit from expert guidance through theoretical lessons and hands-on experience with over 10 industry-specific projects. Upon successful completion, DataMites' dedicated Placement Assistance Team offers end-to-end job support, including career enhancement counseling. Students also receive unlimited access to a Data Science Cloud Lab for ongoing practice. This certification holds global recognition as it is accredited by the International Association of Business Analytics Certification (IABAC).

### Key Details:

*   **Duration:** 8 Months
*   **Approximate Fee:** ₹1,10,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Data Science knowledge, Statistical skills, Data Mining, Data Visualization, Machine Learning.

## Statistics for Data Science

Given that data science largely revolves around extracting meaningful insights from data, a strong foundation in statistics is crucial. This course is specifically designed to impart essential statistical knowledge to both aspiring and practicing data scientists in an efficient manner.

The program adopts a practical approach, applying statistical concepts through various real-life case studies using the Python platform. The syllabus covers key areas such as inferential statistics, descriptive statistics, exploratory data analysis (EDA), data harnessing, correlation, and regression. A significant advantage of this program is the IABAC board certificate awarded upon completion, which can significantly enhance a professional's resume or portfolio.

### Key Details:

*   **Duration:** 6 Months
*   **Approximate Fee:** ₹26,000
*   **Skills Covered:** Statistical skills, Python proficiency.

## Python for Data Science

Python stands out as the most widely used programming language in the fields of Deep Learning and Data Science. This course is tailored to equip learners with the necessary Python skills for these domains. It typically covers fundamental Python programming, data manipulation with libraries like Pandas, numerical computing with NumPy, and data visualization tools such as Matplotlib and Seaborn. The emphasis is on practical application, allowing students to effectively use Python for various data-related tasks.

These programs offer valuable pathways for individuals looking to build or advance their careers in the dynamic fields of data science and artificial intelligence.
