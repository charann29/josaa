# Certified Solar Energy Professional by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to step into the dynamic field of renewable energy, the Vskills Certified Solar Energy Professional program offers a valuable opportunity. This online certification is designed to equip individuals with essential knowledge and skills in solar technology, making it suitable for both fresh graduates and experienced professionals.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is entirely online, offering flexibility with self-study materials and video lessons.
*   Upon successful completion, you receive a government-approved certification valid for life.
*   The course fee is INR 3,499, which includes study materials and access to practice tests.
*   No specific prior academic qualifications are strictly required, making it accessible to a wide audience.

## Understanding Solar Energy

Solar energy harnesses the sun's radiant heat and light using various technologies like solar thermal energy, photovoltaics, and solar architecture. This clean energy source is gaining immense importance globally. The Vskills Certified Solar Energy Professional course delves into these fundamental concepts, providing a comprehensive understanding of how solar systems work, from basic radiation principles to advanced grid integration and PV system design.

## What the Program Offers

This certification program is structured for a convenient learning experience. You'll gain lifetime access to online e-learning resources (LMS), which include both video and text-based content. A hard copy of the study material is also provided. The program emphasizes practical learning with free practice tests to help you prepare for the certification exam. Vskills also offers 24/7 technical assistance and recruitment support to help participants leverage their new skills in the job market.

## Course Fee and Certification Details

The Certified Solar Energy Professional course is priced at **INR 3,499**. This is a one-time purchase fee. Payment can be made through various online options, including Net Banking, Debit Cards from all major Indian banks, MasterCard, and American Express. If you pay online, there's no need to fill out a separate application form.

To earn the certificate, candidates must pass an online examination conducted by Vskills. A score of at least 50% (25 out of 50 marks) is required to qualify. The certificate, once earned, is government-approved and valid for a lifetime.

## Eligibility and Who Should Apply

While there are no strict eligibility criteria mentioned for this course, having a basic understanding of solar cells and solar energy concepts can be beneficial. Graduates, particularly those with a B.Sc degree, may find this course particularly complementary to their existing knowledge. However, the program is broadly suitable for:

*   Science enthusiasts interested in renewable energy.
*   Recent graduates looking to enter the green energy sector.
*   Professionals already working in related fields who wish to upgrade their skills and enhance their CVs.
*   Anyone seeking to understand clean energy technologies and their applications.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing the Certified Solar Energy Professional course, you will develop a strong foundation in various aspects of solar energy. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Grasping the fundamental principles of solar energy.
*   Understanding solar radiation and its applications.
*   In-depth knowledge of solar thermal energy collectors and systems.
*   Concepts related to grid integration and solar optimization.
*   Details on PV system design, certification, installation, maintenance, safety, and power quality considerations.

This comprehensive curriculum ensures you gain a holistic understanding of solar energy technologies and their practical implementation.

This Vskills certification can be a significant step towards a rewarding career in the rapidly growing solar energy industry, helping you stand out in the competitive job market.
