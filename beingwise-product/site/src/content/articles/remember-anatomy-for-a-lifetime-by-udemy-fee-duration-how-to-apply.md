# How to remember anatomy for a lifetime!

Understanding human anatomy is fundamental for anyone pursuing a career in medicine or related health sciences. This particular online course aims to simplify the complex world of anatomy, making it easier to grasp and retain for the long term.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is delivered entirely online, allowing you to learn at your own pace through video and text.
*   It covers all major anatomical regions of the human body, from head to toe.
*   The course is taught by an experienced medical resident.
*   Upon successful completion, you receive a certificate.

## Course overview and accessibility

This self-paced online course is taught in English, utilising a blend of video lectures and written materials. This flexible format means you can access the content whenever and wherever suits you best, fitting your studies around other commitments.

## Course fee and certification

The fee for enrolling in this anatomy course is ₹1,499. After successfully completing all modules, participants are awarded a certificate, confirming their engagement with the course content.

## What you will learn: A detailed syllabus

The curriculum is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of human anatomy, broken down into distinct regions and systems. Here's a look at the key areas covered:

### Head and Neck Anatomy

This section begins with an introduction and then delves into the intricate structures of the head and neck, including:

*   The skull
*   Scalp and meninges
*   Muscles of the head
*   Arteries and veins of the head and neck
*   Nerves of the head and neck
*   Helpful mnemonics specific to this region.

### Neuroanatomy

Focusing on the nervous system, this module explores:

*   The brain
*   The spinal cord
*   Sensory systems and ascending tracts
*   Cranial nerves (in two parts)
*   Mnemonics for neuroanatomy.

### Thorax

This part covers the chest area, including:

*   The chest wall
*   The respiratory system
*   The heart and mediastinum.

### Abdomen and Pelvis

An in-depth look at the abdominal and pelvic regions, covering:

*   The abdominal wall
*   Major blood vessels and nerves of the abdomen and pelvis
*   The peritoneum and peritoneal cavity
*   The gastrointestinal tract
*   The liver, gallbladder, and spleen
*   The urinary system.

### Back

This dedicated section focuses specifically on the anatomy of the back.

### Pectoral Girdle & Upper Limb

Exploring the upper body, this module includes:

*   Muscles, arteries, and nerves of the shoulder girdle and arm
*   The elbow and forearm
*   Bones, joints, muscles, and nerves of the wrist and hand.

### Pelvic Girdle & Lower Limb

Finally, the course moves to the lower body, covering:

*   The pelvic girdle, gluteal region, and thigh
*   The hip and knee
*   The leg
*   The ankle and foot.
