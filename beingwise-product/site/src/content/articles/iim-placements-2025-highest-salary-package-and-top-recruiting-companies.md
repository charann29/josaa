# IIM Placements 2025: Highest Salary Package & Top Recruiting Companies

For aspiring management professionals in India, the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) represent a pinnacle of education and career opportunity. A key indicator of an IIM's value and industry standing is its annual placement report, which details the salary packages and recruiting companies that engage with its graduates.

## Key Takeaways

*   IIM placement processes for MBA/PGP programs typically occur between December and February each year.
*   While average salary packages generally range from INR 20-25 lakh per annum across IIMs, the top institutes often see averages exceeding INR 30 lakh.
*   Highest domestic salary offers at leading IIMs have historically reached between INR 70 lakh and INR 1 crore CTC, with international offers often exceeding INR 70 lakh.
*   A wide array of prominent companies from various sectors consistently recruit from IIM campuses, offering diverse managerial roles.

## Understanding IIM Placement Reports

IIMs release their final placement reports for the graduating batch between January and July. These reports provide crucial insights into the career prospects of their MBA/PGP graduates, including detailed statistics on salaries and the types of roles offered. While some IIMs like Ahmedabad, Lucknow, Indore, and Calcutta have already published data for 2025, reports from other institutes are expected in the coming months.

## Salary Trends at IIMs

The salary packages offered during IIM placements are a significant highlight. For the more established IIMs, average domestic packages often fall in the range of INR 20-25 lakh per annum. Newer IIMs typically see average packages between INR 10-20 lakh per annum. Historically, the highest domestic salary offers at top-tier IIMs such as IIM Ahmedabad, IIM Bangalore, and IIM Calcutta have been substantial, sometimes reaching up to INR 1.10 crore per annum. International offers have also been impressive, with packages often starting from INR 70 lakh per annum and above. For example, IIM Ahmedabad's latest data indicates a highest domestic package of INR 1.10 crore per annum and a highest international package of INR 91.88 lakh per annum.

## Top Recruiting Companies and Roles

The IIM placement process attracts a diverse group of leading companies seeking to hire management graduates. Regular recruiters include major players from consulting, finance, e-commerce, technology, and manufacturing sectors. Some of the well-known companies that frequently participate in IIM placements include E&Y, Deloitte, Walmart, Accenture, JP Morgan Chase, Barclays, Morgan Stanley, Goldman Sachs, Amazon, Zomato, ICICI Bank, Bajaj, Axis, Tata, and Wipro.

Graduates from IIM MBA/PGP programs are offered a variety of managerial roles across different sectors and domains. Function-wise, a significant number of offers are typically for positions in consulting and management, reflecting the comprehensive business education provided by these institutes.
