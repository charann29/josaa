# B.Arch Admissions in Maharashtra: Your Guide After NATA & JEE Main

Navigating B.Arch admissions in Maharashtra after qualifying both NATA and JEE Main can seem complex, but understanding the registration process is key. This guide will walk you through the essential steps for securing your spot.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your NATA and JEE Main Paper 2 scores are both crucial for B.Arch admissions in Maharashtra.
*   The MHT CET Cell manages the centralized admission process (CAP) for B.Arch programs.
*   Online registration on the official MHT CET Cell portal is the mandatory first step for all qualified candidates.
*   Carefully review all entered information before final submission to avoid errors.

## Understanding the Admission Process

Maharashtra's B.Arch admissions are conducted through a Centralized Admission Process (CAP) managed by the MHT CET Cell. This process is designed to streamline applications for various architecture colleges across the state. Even if you haven't appeared for MHT CET, your NATA and JEE Main Paper 2 scores are considered for B.Arch admissions.

## Initial Registration on the MHT CET Portal

To begin your admission journey, you must register on the official MHT CET Cell website. This is a crucial step for all eligible candidates, regardless of whether you've taken the MHT CET exam or only NATA/JEE Main Paper 2. Here’s a general outline of how to proceed:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Go to the designated MHT CET Cell portal for admissions.
2.  **Locate the Registration Link:** Look for an option clearly marked 'Registration' or 'New Candidate Registration'.
3.  **Provide Basic Details:** You will typically be asked to enter a valid email address and a mobile number. An OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to verify these details.
4.  **Fill Personal Information:** Proceed to fill in your personal details such as your full name, date of birth, and other required demographic information.
5.  **Review and Submit:** Before finalizing, carefully double-check all the information you have entered to ensure accuracy. Once confirmed, submit your registration.

This initial registration creates your profile within the CAP system, allowing you to participate in further steps like filling out the application form and choice filling.

## Document Verification and Application Submission

After initial registration, the next phase involves submitting your detailed application and undergoing document verification. This typically includes uploading scanned copies of your academic certificates, NATA scorecard, JEE Main Paper 2 scorecard, domicile certificate (if applicable), and other relevant documents. The MHT CET Cell provides specific guidelines for document sizes and formats. Post-submission, candidates usually need to verify their documents, either online or at designated physical Facilitation Centers (FCs), as per the official schedule.

## Merit List and Counseling Rounds

Based on your NATA score or JEE Main Paper 2 score (whichever is higher, as per the rules for B.Arch admissions in Maharashtra), a state-level merit list will be published. This merit list determines your eligibility and ranking for college admissions. Following the merit list, the MHT CET Cell conducts multiple counseling rounds, where you can fill in your preferred colleges and courses. Allotments are made based on your merit rank, preferences, and seat availability.

Successfully navigating these steps will bring you closer to securing your B.Arch admission in Maharashtra.
