# Secondary: Psychology (222) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Explore the fundamentals of human psychology and its real-world applications through this online course from the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS), delivered via the Swayam platform. This program is designed to provide learners with insights into human behavior and mental well-being.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a self-study, school-level course focused on foundational psychology.
*   The program spans 24 weeks and awards 10 credit points upon successful completion.
*   Learning is facilitated through video lectures, reading materials, and discussion forums.
*   Participants receive a certificate from NIOS after meeting all course requirements.

## Course Overview

The Secondary: Psychology (222) online course delves into the core principles of human psychology, emphasizing how these concepts apply to daily life. It encourages learners to develop a deeper understanding of their surroundings and promotes positive conduct. The curriculum is structured into seven modules, aiming to foster positive mental health practices and a healthy lifestyle.

Upon successfully finishing all course steps, learners will earn 10 credit points and receive a Secondary: Psychology (222) Certification issued by NIOS.

## What You Will Learn

This course offers a comprehensive introduction to various aspects of psychology, covering both theoretical foundations and practical applications. Key areas of study include:

*   Foundations of psychology
*   Basic psychological processes, including memory and problem-solving
*   Human development across different life stages
*   Social psychological processes and their impact
*   Understanding health, happiness, and mental well-being
*   Exploring work-life balance and environmental concerns
*   The role of Yoga as a holistic approach to life

The detailed syllabus unfolds over 24 weeks, covering topics such as individual differences, learning, motivation, emotion, attitudes, and various social and educational challenges. It also touches upon mental disorders, hygiene, and the importance of self-development and Yoga for overall well-being.

## Course Structure and Delivery

This program is delivered entirely online through the Swayam platform, utilizing a self-study model. The medium of instruction is English. Learners will engage with the content through a combination of video lectures and provided reading materials. An interactive platform supports peer discussion forums, allowing students to connect and share insights.


## Fees and Certification

While specific fee details for the course are not provided in the source material, it is confirmed that a certificate of completion is available. This certificate is issued by the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) to learners who successfully complete all aspects of the Secondary: Psychology (222) program.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Secondary: Psychology (222) Certification Course is a straightforward process. Interested candidates should visit the official Swayam platform. Look for the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically located in the top right corner of the page. From there, you can either sign in with an existing account or create a new one to proceed with your enrollment.

This course provides a valuable foundation for anyone interested in understanding the complexities of the human mind and behavior, offering insights that can contribute to personal growth and a healthier lifestyle.
