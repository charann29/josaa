# GLAET 2026: Registration (Open), Exam Dates, Eligibility, Syllabus, Pattern, Admission Process

The GLA University Entrance Test (GLAET) is a crucial online examination for students aspiring to join GLA University, Mathura, for various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the GLAET 2026, covering everything from registration to the admission process.

## Key takeaways

*   GLAET 2026 application forms are currently available for all UG and PG programs offered by GLA University.
*   Candidates can choose their preferred exam date through an online slot booking system or appear directly at a university test centre.
*   The exam is computer-based, featuring objective-type questions with a specific marking scheme.
*   Successful GLAET candidates for certain programs (BPharm, MPharm, MBA-Business Analytics & MBA Hons) will also need to complete a personal interview.

## What is GLAET?

GLAET, or the Ganeshi Lal Agarwal University Entrance Test, is the official entrance examination for admission to almost all undergraduate, postgraduate, and certificate courses at GLA University, Mathura. The only exception is PhD programs, which have a separate admission process. The exam is conducted online and is available at various centres across India, as well as an option to take it from home or a computer centre.

## GLAET 2026 Exam Dates and Schedule

GLA University has opened the application window for GLAET 2026. A unique feature of the GLAET is its flexible scheduling: candidates can book an exam slot online according to their convenience. Alternatively, applicants can visit any GLA University test centre on any day without prior slot booking and take the exam directly. This flexibility allows students to plan their test date around other commitments.

## GLAET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for GLAET 2026, candidates must meet the specific academic requirements for their desired program. These criteria typically include minimum marks in qualifying examinations (like 10+2 for undergraduate courses or a bachelor's degree for postgraduate courses). Applicants are strongly advised to check the detailed eligibility conditions on the official GLA University website before filling out the application form to ensure they qualify.

## GLAET 2026 Application Process

Applying for GLAET 2026 involves an online registration process. Interested candidates can find the application form on the official GLA University website. The steps generally include:

1.  **Online Registration:** Create an account on the university's admission portal.
2.  **Fill Application Form:** Provide personal, academic, and contact details accurately.
3.  **Upload Documents:** Submit required documents such as photographs and signatures as specified.
4.  **Pay Application Fee:** Complete the payment for the application fee through the available online methods.
5.  **Slot Booking:** For those taking the exam online, select a preferred date and time for the GLAET through the slot booking system. This step is essential to receive your admit card details.

## GLAET 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The GLAET 2026 is conducted online and consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs). The exam pattern includes:

*   **Question Type:** Objective Type Questions (MCQs).
*   **Marking Scheme:** Each correct answer fetches 4 marks. For every incorrect answer, 1 mark will be deducted.
*   **Syllabus:** The syllabus varies depending on the course applied for. Generally, for engineering and technology programs, it covers subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. For management and other programs, it might include sections on General English, Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning, and General Knowledge. Candidates should refer to the official GLA University website for the detailed syllabus relevant to their chosen program.

### Courses Offered Through GLAET 2026

GLAET is the gateway to a wide array of programs at GLA University, including but not limited to:

*   B.Tech, B.Tech (Lateral Entry), Diploma in Engineering
*   B.Pharma, B.Pharma (Lateral Entry), D.Pharma
*   BBA, BBA (Family Business), BBA (Hons)
*   B.Com
*   BCA
*   MCA, MCA (Lateral Entry)
*   MBA, MBA (Logistics and Supply Chain Management), MBA (Business Analytics), MBA (Hons)
*   M.Tech
*   M.Pharma
*   B.Sc, M.Sc
*   B.Ed

## GLAET 2026 Admission Process

Following the GLAET, candidates will receive their test scores, typically within 24 hours of completing the exam. The admission process generally involves:

1.  **Result Declaration:** GLAET scores are released quickly.
2.  **Personal Interview (PI):** For specific programs like B.Pharm, M.Pharm, MBA-Business Analytics, and MBA (Hons), candidates who qualify GLAET must also appear for a Personal Interview.
3.  **Counselling:** Based on GLAET scores and PI performance (where applicable), eligible candidates will be called for counselling. During counselling, students can choose their desired program and complete the admission formalities, including fee payment.
