# UG program in Multimedia & Visual Communication by École Intuit Lab New Delhi: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

École Intuit Lab in New Delhi offers a comprehensive undergraduate program in Multimedia & Visual Communication, blending French design principles with practical, industry-focused learning. This four-year course is designed to equip students with the skills needed for dynamic careers in the creative industries.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a four-year, full-time undergraduate program taught entirely in English.
*   The curriculum emphasizes hands-on studio practice, live projects, and two mandatory internships.
*   Students gain expertise in areas like visual storytelling, graphic design, UI/UX, and motion graphics.
*   The program aims to prepare graduates for roles such as Graphic Designer, UI Designer, UX Architect, and Art Director.

## Course Overview and Structure

The UG program in Multimedia & Visual Communication at École Intuit Lab India provides a transformative educational experience. It focuses on building a strong foundation in visual storytelling, branding, digital media, graphic design, UI/UX, and motion graphics. The institution prides itself on its global perspective, integrating French design pedagogy with an industry-aligned curriculum.

Throughout the program, participants are exposed to real-world industry practices through live projects, workshops, and mentorship from experienced design professionals. The course structure includes:

*   **Lab 1: Foundation Studies**
*   **Lab 2: Design Principles and Techniques**
*   **Short Internship**
*   **Lab 3: Concepts and Communication with Design**
*   **Optional - Lab 4 (India): Professional Design and Brands in the Market**
*   **Long Internship**
*   **Option 2: International Master’s Diploma (France)**

This structured approach ensures that students not only grasp theoretical concepts but also develop practical expertise and a professional portfolio.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to help students master both the creative and technical dimensions of design. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Visualisation Skills:** Developing a strong understanding of visual language, color theory, layout composition, and typography.
*   **Computer Skills:** Gaining proficiency in industry-standard tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, prototyping platforms, and digital publishing software.
*   **Digital Marketing Skills:** Exploring branding systems, identity creation, advertising concepts, and digital campaign design.
*   **UI/UX Knowledge:** Mastering UI/UX design principles, interaction design, and multimedia storytelling.
*   **Soft Skills:** Enhancing creative problem-solving, presentation abilities, teamwork, and client communication, which are crucial for success in professional design environments.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

To be eligible for the UG program in Multimedia & Visual Communication, applicants must have successfully completed Grade 12. The admission process involves several steps:

1.  **Online Application:** Visit the official course page and complete the application form.
2.  **Document Submission & Fees:** Submit all necessary documents and pay the application fees.
3.  **Creative Aptitude Test:** Candidates are required to take a creative aptitude test.
4.  **Interview:** A personal interview is conducted as part of the selection process.

## Program Highlights and Career Prospects

The program stands out due to several key offerings that enhance the student experience and career readiness:

*   **Two Internships:** Providing invaluable real-world experience.
*   **Global Workshops & Student Exchange Initiatives:** Offering international exposure and diverse learning perspectives.
*   **Expert Mentors:** Guidance from seasoned design professionals.
*   **Live Projects & Portfolio Reviews:** Practical application of skills and development of a strong professional portfolio.
*   **Studio Practice & Technical Labs:** Hands-on learning in a professional environment.

Graduates of this program are well-prepared for dynamic careers in various fields, including advertising, branding, digital marketing, user experience design, and content creation. The certification is provided by École Intuit Lab New Delhi, ensuring that graduates are industry-ready upon completion.

This program is an excellent choice for students aspiring to become creative professionals in the fast-evolving digital landscape.
