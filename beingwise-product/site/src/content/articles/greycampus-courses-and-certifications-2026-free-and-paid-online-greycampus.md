# GreyCampus Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online GreyCampus Courses

GreyCampus offers a wide array of online courses and certifications designed to help individuals enhance their professional skills across various in-demand fields. These programs cater to different learning needs, from short introductory modules to comprehensive bootcamps.

## Key takeaways

*   GreyCampus provides both free and paid online courses, making professional development accessible to a broad audience.
*   Programs span critical areas like Project Management, Data Science, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, and Software Development.
*   Many courses lead to industry-recognized certifications, which can significantly boost career prospects.
*   The duration of courses varies greatly, from a few hours for foundational topics to several months for intensive bootcamps.

## Exploring Project Management Certifications

Project management is a crucial skill in almost every industry, and GreyCampus offers several pathways to formalize expertise in this area. These certifications are often highly valued by employers looking for structured project leadership.

### Popular Project Management Courses:

*   **PRINCE2® Foundation and Practitioner Combo Training & Certification:** This comprehensive program typically runs for 3 days and is designed to equip learners with both foundational knowledge and practical application skills in the PRINCE2 methodology, with a fee around ₹81,400.
*   **PMP® Certification Training:** A globally recognized certification for project managers, this training often costs approximately ₹14,440.
*   **CAPM® Certification Training:** Ideal for those new to project management, this course is typically priced around ₹14,800.
*   **PRINCE2® Foundation Certification Training Course:** A beginner-friendly option, usually available for about ₹40,700.
*   **Certified Scrum Master® (CSM®) Training and Certification Course:** Focuses on Agile methodologies.
*   **Agile Scrum Master Certification Training:** Another option for those interested in Agile practices.
*   **Project Management Fundamentals:** A shorter, foundational course, typically completed in about 6 hours.
*   **Construction Project Management:** A specialized course, generally taking 2 days.
*   **Microsoft Project Training:** Focuses on using Microsoft Project software, often priced around ₹10,360.
