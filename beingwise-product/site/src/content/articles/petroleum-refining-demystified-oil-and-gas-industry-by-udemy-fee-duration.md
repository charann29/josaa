## What the Course Covers

This program offers a deep dive into the petroleum refining sector. You'll gain a comprehensive understanding of different types of crude oil, their chemical makeup, and how gas companies separate these components through distillation. The curriculum also explains how to refine petroleum cuts to create higher-quality blending ingredients for gasoline, diesel, and other fuels.

## Course Syllabus at a Glance

The curriculum is structured to guide you through the refining process systematically:

*   **Course Introduction:** Outlining learning objectives.
*   **Crude Oil Refining Overview:** A general introduction to refining activities.
*   **Petroleum Properties:** Understanding crude oil's composition and characteristics.
*   **Distillation and Fractionation:** Covering atmospheric and vacuum distillation, along with light cut fractionation.
*   **Converting Petroleum Cuts:** Exploring isomerization, alkylation, and catalytic reforming.
*   **Hydrofining Petroleum Cuts:** Details on hydrotreating and sulfur plant operations.
*   **Cracking Heavy Petroleum Cuts:** Examining fluid catalytic cracking, hydrocracking, and delayed coking.
*   **Blending Operation:** The process of blending finished petroleum products.
*   **Making Asphalts:** An insight into asphalt production.
*   **A Typical Refinery Operation:** A look at a standard refinery scheme, including a quiz to test your knowledge.
*   **Downloadable Resources:** Supplementary materials and bonus content.
