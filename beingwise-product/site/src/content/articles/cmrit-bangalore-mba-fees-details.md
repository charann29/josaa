# Cmrit Bangalore MBA Fees Details

Considering an MBA at CMR Institute of Technology (CMRIT) in Bangalore? Understanding the fee structure is a crucial step in your application journey. This page breaks down the typical costs and important factors to keep in mind for their two-year Master of Business Administration program.

## Key Takeaways

*   **VTU Quota Fees:** For students admitted through the Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) quota, the total MBA tuition fee for the entire two-year program generally ranges from approximately ₹1.17 lakh to ₹1.28 lakh.
*   **Management Quota Fees:** If you are seeking admission under the management quota, the total cost for the two-year MBA program is significantly higher, typically around ₹9 lakh.
*   **Annual Breakdown:** The overall two-year program fee for regular admissions is often cited around ₹3.5 lakh, which translates to about ₹1.75 lakh per year.
*   **Category Uniformity:** For the VTU quota, the tuition fees are generally consistent across all regular categories, including General Merit (GM), Other Backward Classes (OBC), Scheduled Castes (SC), and Scheduled Tribes (ST).

## Understanding the Fee Structure

CMRIT offers its MBA program with different fee structures depending on the admission pathway. It's important to distinguish between seats allocated through the VTU quota and those available under the management quota. The VTU quota, often associated with state-level entrance exams and counselling, typically has a much lower fee.

For students entering through the VTU quota, the total tuition fee for the full two-year MBA course is usually in the range of ₹1.17 lakh to ₹1.28 lakh. This fee structure is generally applied uniformly across various student categories such as GM, OBC, SC, and ST.

In contrast, the management quota seats come with a substantially higher fee. For these seats, the total cost for the two-year MBA program is approximately ₹9 lakh, which can be broken down to about ₹4.5 lakh per year.

It's also worth noting that the overall program fee for the two-year MBA, encompassing various components, is frequently stated to be around ₹3.5 lakh. This averages out to roughly ₹1.75 lakh per year.

## MBA Specializations Offered

The MBA program at CMRIT is designed to provide students with specialized knowledge in various business domains. The institute offers a range of specializations to cater to diverse career interests. Some of the popular specializations include:

*   Marketing & Human Resource Management (HRM)
*   Marketing & Finance
*   Business Analytics & Human Resources (HR)

These specializations allow students to tailor their learning experience and develop expertise in specific functional areas of business, enhancing their career prospects upon graduation.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

To be considered for the MBA program at CMRIT, prospective students must meet certain eligibility criteria, including possessing a valid score in one of several recognized entrance examinations. The institute accepts scores from:

*   Common Admission Test (CAT)
*   Management Aptitude Test (MAT)
*   Common Management Admission Test (CMAT)
*   Karnataka Management Aptitude Test (KMAT)
*   Karnataka Post Graduate Common Entrance Test (PGCET)

Admission to the program is typically facilitated through a counselling process, where candidates are selected based on their entrance exam scores and other academic qualifications.

## Important Considerations

While the figures provided offer a good estimate, it is always advisable to confirm the precise and most current fee details directly with the CMRIT admissions office before finalizing your application. Fee structures can sometimes be subject to minor adjustments, and the official source will provide the most accurate information. Additionally, eligible candidates may have access to various scholarships or fee waiver programs, which could help reduce the overall financial burden.

We hope this information helps you in planning your MBA journey at CMRIT Bangalore.
