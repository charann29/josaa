# Full Stack Web Developer – MEAN Stack by Simplilearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Becoming a Full Stack Web Developer is a sought-after career path in today's tech-driven world. This program focuses on the MEAN stack, a popular collection of technologies that empowers you to build robust web applications from front to back. Whether you're just starting out or looking to enhance your existing development skills, this course aims to equip you with the expertise needed to excel.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to develop, test, and deploy full-stack applications using the MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, Node.js).
*   The program combines self-paced video lessons with live online classes, offering a flexible learning experience.
*   It includes numerous projects, including capstone projects, to provide practical, real-world development experience.
*   Both freshers and experienced professionals with a foundational understanding of programming can enroll.

## Program Overview and Learning Approach

This certification in Full Stack Web Development, specifically focusing on the MEAN stack, is designed for individuals aspiring to build a career in web development. The curriculum is structured to provide comprehensive knowledge of essential testing technologies and software development tools. It covers core components like Node.js, JavaScript, Docker, Angular, and Protractor, alongside the fundamental MEAN technologies (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, Node.js).

The learning experience adopts a unique blended approach, combining self-study and virtual classroom sessions. The course content is delivered through video and text, with live classes typically held on weekends. Participants are encouraged to dedicate approximately 10 hours per week to their studies to get the most out of the program.

## What You Will Learn

The syllabus is crafted to build a strong foundation and then advance to practical application development. Key areas of learning include:

*   **Computer Science Fundamentals:** A refresh of basic computer science concepts to ensure a solid understanding for development work.
*   **Core Programming Skills:** In-depth learning of software programming principles essential for any developer.
*   **MEAN Stack Technologies:** Hands-on training with MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, and Node.js to build interactive services and applications.
*   **Development Tools:** Proficiency in tools like GIT, CSS, HTML, and JavaScript, crucial for modern web development.
*   **Application Development Lifecycle:** Practical experience in developing, testing, and deploying applications efficiently.

Throughout the program, you will engage with over 20 in-demand tools and complete numerous projects, including 10 lesson-end projects, 4 phase-end projects, and comprehensive capstone projects that simulate real-life development scenarios.

## Eligibility and Who Should Enroll

The program is open to a wide range of candidates, from those just starting their careers to experienced professionals looking to upskill. The primary eligibility criteria are:

*   **Educational Background:** A technical degree such as a B.Tech, M.Tech, or an engineering degree, or a Bachelor's or Master's in Computer Science. Equivalent qualifications are also considered.
*   **Programming Knowledge:** A basic understanding of programming concepts is beneficial, though the course is designed to build foundational skills.
*   **Work Experience:** Freshers with no prior work experience are welcome to apply. The course is also suitable for existing software developers and IT analysts aiming to specialize in full-stack development.

## Fees and Certification

The program fee for the Full Stack Web Developer – MEAN Stack certification is ₹53,999. Flexible payment options, including EMI starting at ₹2,418 per month, are available to make the program more accessible. Upon successful completion of the course and its projects, participants will receive an industry-recognized completion certificate from Simplilearn.
