# Best MBA Colleges in Nashik 2026: Admissions, Fees, Courses, Placements

Nashik offers a compelling option for management education, often proving more budget-friendly than larger cities like Mumbai and Pune. With over 40 MBA colleges, it provides a range of choices for aspiring students.

## Key takeaways

*   Nashik boasts over 40 MBA colleges, including more than 20 private institutions and one government college.
*   Many MBA programs in Nashik are quite affordable, with approximately 25 institutes offering courses within the INR 1-3 Lakh fee range.
*   Symbiosis Institute of Operations Management (SIOM) and Sandip University are among the highly-regarded private institutions, recognized in the 2025 Times B-School rankings.
*   Beyond the state-specific MAH CET, colleges in Nashik also accept national entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, MAT, and CMAT, often with more accessible cutoff scores compared to premier national institutes.

## Leading MBA Colleges in Nashik

Among the many institutions, Symbiosis Institute of Operations Management (SIOM) and Sandip University are particularly noteworthy. Both have received recognition in the 2025 Times B-School rankings, placing them among the top 100 MBA colleges across India. The Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (MUHS) stands as the sole government MBA college in the city.

## Admission Criteria and Entrance Exams

To secure admission into an MBA program in Nashik, candidates typically need a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. Most colleges require applicants to appear for a management entrance examination.

The primary entrance exam for admission to MBA programs in Maharashtra, including Nashik, is the MAH CET. However, many colleges also accept scores from national-level tests such as:

*   CAT (Common Admission Test)
*   XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test)
*   MAT (Management Aptitude Test)
*   CMAT (Common Management Admission Test)

Symbiosis Institute of Operations Management (SIOM) specifically requires candidates to take the SNAP (Symbiosis National Aptitude Test).

## Fees and Specialisations

One of Nashik's strengths as an MBA destination is its affordability. A significant number of colleges, around 25, offer MBA programs with fees ranging from INR 1-3 Lakh. This makes it an attractive choice for students seeking quality management education without a hefty price tag.

Colleges in Nashik offer a diverse range of specialisations to cater to various career interests. Some of the popular areas of study include:

*   Human Resources
*   Finance
*   International Business
*   IT & Systems
*   Agriculture & Food Business
*   Operations

## Placement Opportunities

While specific placement figures vary by institution, MBA colleges in Nashik strive to provide strong career opportunities for their graduates. Placement cells actively engage with industries to facilitate internships and final placements. Companies from various sectors, including manufacturing, IT, finance, and consumer goods, recruit from these campuses.
