# UPCAT Counselling 2022: Dates, Application Form, Eligibility, Seat Allotment

For students aspiring to join various professional undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Uttar Pradesh, the Uttar Pradesh Combined Entrance Test (UPCET) was previously a key pathway. However, for 2022 admissions, the UPCET examination was discontinued and replaced by national-level entrance exams.

## Key takeaways

*   **UPCET Replaced:** From 2022 onwards, UPCET (formerly UPSEE) has been replaced by national entrance exams for admissions to colleges affiliated with AKTU, MMMUT, and other state universities in Uttar Pradesh.
*   **Undergraduate Admissions:** Admissions to UG courses like BHMCT, BTech Lateral Entry, BDes, BFAD/BFA, BPharm/BPharm (Lateral), Integrated MBA, and Integrated MCA are now primarily through CUET, JEE Main, or NATA.
*   **Postgraduate Admissions:** PG courses such as MBA, MCA, MTech, MURP, MPharm, MDes, and MArch are offered through the CUET PG exam.
*   **No Age Limit:** Generally, there is no upper age limit for applying to these courses.

## Understanding the Change: From UPCET to National Exams

Previously, UPCET served as the common entrance test for admission to numerous professional programs across institutions like Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) in Lucknow, Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT) in Gorakhpur, and Harcourt Butler Technical University (HBTU) in Kanpur. The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducted this state-level, computer-based examination.

However, for the 2022 academic year, the structure changed significantly. Instead of a dedicated UPCET, candidates now need to appear for various national examinations depending on their chosen course and level of study. This change aims to streamline admissions and align with broader national education policies.

## Eligibility Criteria for Uttar Pradesh Admissions

While the entrance exam has changed, the general eligibility principles for admission to Uttar Pradesh institutions remain largely consistent.

### Domicile Requirements

*   Candidates who have completed their qualifying examination from an institution located in Uttar Pradesh are eligible.
*   Alternatively, candidates whose parents hold a domicile certificate of Uttar Pradesh are also eligible.
*   If neither of the above conditions is met, candidates may still be eligible if they fulfill specific criteria related to their parents' Uttar Pradesh domicile.

### Academic Qualifications

Specific academic requirements vary by course. Generally, candidates must have passed the relevant qualifying examination (e.g., 10+2 for undergraduate courses, a bachelor's degree for postgraduate courses) from a recognized board or university, often with a minimum percentage of marks as stipulated by the respective university or course.

## Application Process and Counselling

With the shift to national entrance exams, the application process for these courses now follows the procedures of CUET, JEE Main, NATA, or CUET PG. After appearing for these exams, the counselling and seat allotment process is typically managed by the respective state authorities or universities, often through a centralized online portal.

### Key Steps in the Admission Process:

1.  **Appear for the relevant national entrance exam:** This could be JEE Main for B.Tech, NATA for B.Arch, CUET for various UG programs, or CUET PG for postgraduate courses.
2.  **Check results:** Candidates must check their scores on the official NTA or respective examination portal.
3.  **Register for counselling:** Eligible candidates will need to register on the state's counselling portal (e.g., for AKTU affiliated colleges) by paying a fee and providing necessary details.
4.  **Choice filling and locking:** Candidates select their preferred courses and institutions based on their eligibility and ranking.
5.  **Seat Allotment:** Based on merit, preferences, and availability, seats are allotted in various rounds.
6.  **Document Verification and Admission:** Allotted candidates must report to the designated centers or institutions for document verification and to complete admission formalities.

## Important Dates for 2022 Admissions

While UPCET-specific dates are no longer relevant, it's crucial for students to monitor the official schedules for CUET, JEE Main, NATA, and CUET PG, along with the subsequent counselling schedules released by the Uttar Pradesh state admission authorities or specific universities.
