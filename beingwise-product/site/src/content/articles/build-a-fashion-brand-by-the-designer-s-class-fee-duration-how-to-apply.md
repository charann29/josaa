# How to Build a Fashion Brand by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone passionate about fashion, creating your own brand and label often stands as a significant aspiration. The "How to Build a Fashion Brand" online course offers a structured approach to turn this dream into a reality.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a beginner-level online course designed for self-paced learning through video and text.
*   The entire program can be completed in approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes, with one year of access to all materials.
*   It is taught by celebrity stylist Rhea Kapoor and covers market understanding, product development, and marketing strategies.
*   The course fee is ₹2,499 and includes downloadable resources and assignments.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This online program, developed and taught by celebrity stylist Rhea Kapoor, guides students through the complete process of establishing a fashion brand. It aims to equip learners with the knowledge to infuse their unique style and fashion ideas into a viable brand.

The curriculum covers essential aspects from initial concept to market launch, including understanding market dynamics, developing products, and strategizing marketing and sales. As a beginner-level course from The Designer's Class, it provides insights into branding principles, business techniques, retail fundamentals, and basic marketing concepts relevant to the fashion industry.

The learning format is entirely online, delivered through a combination of video and text-based content. It supports self-study, allowing you to progress at your own pace. The course is available in both English and Hindi, catering to a wider audience.

## What You Will Learn

Through this certification, participants will gain a detailed understanding of fashion and luxury branding, along with practical tools and techniques for launching a fashion brand. The syllabus is comprehensive, covering both historical context and future trends.

Key learning areas include:

*   Introduction to Fashion Branding and its history
*   The process of creating a luxury brand
*   Understanding production and distribution channels
*   Effective brand management strategies
*   Principles of fashion communication
*   Insights into fashion retail design and business strategy
*   Methods for choosing the right market segment
*   An overview of fashion management

## Program Structure and Resources

The course is structured into various modules, designed to be concise and impactful. While the total completion time is approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes, students benefit from one year of access to all course materials.

Key features include:

*   **Online Course:** Accessible from anywhere with an internet connection.
*   **Duration:** Approximately 1 hour 30 minutes for completion.
*   **Level:** Beginner-friendly.
*   **Resources:** 13 downloadable resources to aid learning.
*   **Assignments:** 12 assignments to reinforce understanding.
*   **Availability:** Offered in both English and Hindi.

## Fee and Certification

The fee for the "How to Build a Fashion Brand" online course is ₹2,499. This fee grants access to all course content, downloadable resources, and assignments. Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a certificate of completion.

## How to Apply

Interested candidates can enroll directly through The Designer's Class official portal. The process typically involves registering on their platform and completing the payment for the course fee.

This program offers a valuable foundation for aspiring fashion entrepreneurs looking to establish their presence in the industry.
