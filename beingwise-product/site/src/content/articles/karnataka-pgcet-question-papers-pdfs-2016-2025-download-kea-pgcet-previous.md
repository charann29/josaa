## Why Practice with Previous Year Papers?

Engaging with past Karnataka PGCET question papers is a highly effective study strategy. The Karnataka PGCET is designed to evaluate candidates for admission to various postgraduate courses. While the specific syllabus varies slightly by program (MBA, MCA, M.Tech, etc.), the general format often includes sections testing your quantitative ability, logical reasoning, general knowledge, and English language skills. Reviewing past papers for your specific course will give you a clear picture of what to expect.
