# Automation in Structural Analysis and Design using MATLAB & MASTAN by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This article explores a specialized program designed to equip students and professionals with essential skills in automating structural analysis and design using industry-standard software like MATLAB and MASTAN. It's a valuable opportunity for those looking to enhance their technical expertise in civil and architectural engineering.

## Key takeaways

*   The program focuses on practical application of MATLAB and MASTAN for structural analysis.
*   It offers a self-paced, online learning format with video lessons and project-based assignments.
*   Industry experts guide participants through key concepts, ensuring relevant skill development.
*   Upon completion, participants receive a course certificate, validating their acquired expertise.

## Course Overview

This comprehensive program is crafted to build proficiency in using powerful software tools such as MASTAN and MATLAB for structural analysis and design. The curriculum delves into fundamental concepts, including structural analytics basics, understanding structural systems, global analysis equations, principles of virtual work, stiffness analysis, frame analysis, and even nonlinear analysis. Special analytical procedures are also covered, providing a holistic understanding of the field.

The course content is delivered through pre-recorded video lessons, complemented by weekly assignments. A strong emphasis is placed on hands-on learning, with participants required to complete practical projects. This project-based approach helps cement learned skills and introduces industry best practices, preparing students for real-world challenges.

## Learning with MATLAB and MASTAN

The program specifically incorporates MATLAB and MASTAN, two high-performance software widely utilized in structural analysis operations. Gaining fluency in these tools is a major objective, as it directly translates into a stronger resume, potentially opening doors to scholarships, grants, and various job opportunities in the engineering sector. By the end of the course, and after successfully completing the required projects, participants will be awarded a course completion certificate.

## Eligibility and Target Audience

To enroll in this program, individuals should either be pursuing or have completed a B.Tech/B.E. degree in Civil, Architectural, or Mechanical Engineering. The course is particularly well-suited for:

*   Architectural Engineering students
*   Civil Engineering students
*   University freshers seeking practical experience in structural analysis projects
*   Practicing Civil Engineers looking to upgrade their automation skills

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this training, you can expect to:

*   Achieve fluency in using software like MASTAN and MATLAB for structural applications.
*   Develop the ability to apply virtual displacement procedures for creating analytical solutions.
*   Master various concepts for automating structural analysis, including frame analysis, global analysis equations, virtual displacement principles, and nonlinear analysis.
*   Learn to implement automated scripts to solve practical, real-world engineering problems.
*   Gain in-depth knowledge of specialized analysis procedures such as sub-structuring, condensation, joint coordinates, and constraints.

This program offers a robust foundation for anyone aiming to excel in the field of structural analysis and design through automation.
