# Item Writing for GMA (Visual Spatial) by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online certification course from TCS iON Digital Learning Hub is designed to teach you how to create effective assessment questions for General Mental Ability (GMA) tests, specifically focusing on the non-verbal, visual-spatial reasoning components. It's an excellent opportunity to develop specialized skills in educational assessment.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn to design assessment items that evaluate visual-spatial reasoning.
*   The program is entirely online, self-paced, and includes virtual classroom sessions over three months.
*   Gain five academic credit points upon successful completion.
*   Receive mentorship from industry experts and get access to digital certificates.

## Course Overview

The 'Item Writing for GMA (Visual-Spatial)' course is a three-month online certification program offered by the TCS iON Digital Learning Hub. Its primary goal is to equip participants with the skills to develop high-quality, non-verbal assessment items that measure an individual's visual-spatial reasoning abilities. This is a crucial skill for creating comprehensive mental ability evaluations.

Throughout the program, you'll engage in a virtual learning environment that combines digital lectures, tutorials, and practical exercises. The course is structured to provide both academic and industry insights, guided by experienced mentors like Sarika Tulsiani (a trainer) and Dr. U.N. Dash (a psychology expert). Successful completion awards five credit points and a digital certificate, based on specific performance criteria.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is crafted to enhance your writing and language skills specifically for assessment development, alongside deepening your understanding of psychology as it relates to mental ability. You will gain expertise in framing assessment questions that effectively measure visual-spatial reasoning, offering a better perspective on how to evaluate individual performance in this critical area of general mental ability.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for students currently pursuing bachelor's degrees in Education, Psychology, or Mathematics. It's also highly beneficial for teachers looking to enhance their professional expertise in assessment design. Completing this course can open doors to careers such as teaching non-verbal reasoning or working as an item writer specializing in visual-spatial reasoning assessments.

## Program Offerings and Learning Experience

The 'Item Writing for GMA (Visual-Spatial)' course provides a rich learning experience through various components:

*   **Digital Lectures and Online Resources:** Access to comprehensive learning materials.
*   **Online Tutorials and Practicals:** Hands-on sessions to apply your knowledge.
*   **Quizzes and Assessments:** Regular checks on your understanding, including practice and summative assessments.
*   **Community Engagement:** Connect with peers and industry professionals through dedicated academic and industry communities.
*   **Assignments and Internships:** Industry-oriented assignments and opportunities for internships to gain practical experience.
*   **Mentorship:** Guidance from experienced professionals in the field.
*   **Digital Certificates:** Recognition for your achievement upon meeting the specified criteria.

## Fees and Certification

### Course Fees

The total fee for the 'Item Writing for GMA (Visual-Spatial)' certification training is ₹11,800, which includes GST. This payment must be made online to gain access to the course materials and learning platform.

| Particular   | Details    |
| :----------- | :--------- |
| **Course Fee** | ₹11,800    |

### Certificate Availability

Digital certificates are provided by TCS iON upon meeting specific criteria. There are two types of certificates:

*   **Certificate of Achievement:** Awarded to students who achieve a minimum of 40% in Part A of the summative assessment, 50% in Part B, and an overall average of at least 50%.
*   **Certificate of Participation:** Awarded to students who complete all industrial projects, practice assignments, and the full course study, even if they do not attempt or pass the summative assessment.

Students who do not pass the summative assessment will receive a scorecard detailing their performance.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the 'Item Writing for GMA (Visual-Spatial)' online course, applicants should have a foundational understanding in one of the following areas: Education, Psychology, or Mathematics.

This course is an excellent way to specialize your skills in educational assessment and open up new career pathways in a growing field.
