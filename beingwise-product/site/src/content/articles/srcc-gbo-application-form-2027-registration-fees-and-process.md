# SRCC GBO Application Form 2027: Registration, Fees & Process

Applying for the SRCC GBO program is an important step towards pursuing your postgraduate management studies. This guide will walk you through the online application process for the 2027 intake, covering registration, required documents, and fee payment.

## Key Takeaways

*   The SRCC GBO 2027 application window is expected to open in the second week of December 2026.
*   The entire registration and application process is conducted online through the official SRCC GBO admissions portal.
*   Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria of at least 50% in your undergraduate degree, with relaxations for reserved categories.
*   Keep all necessary documents, including academic mark sheets and a recent photograph, ready in digital format before you begin.

## How to Apply for SRCC GBO 2027

The application process for the SRCC GBO program is straightforward and takes place entirely online. Here's a step-by-step breakdown:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the Shriram College of Commerce website and locate the 'GBO Admissions 2027' section.
2.  **Initial Registration:** Provide your basic details such as your name, mobile number, date of birth, email address, and gender. This will create your preliminary registration.
3.  **Receive Login Credentials:** After successful initial registration, you will receive a unique username and password via your registered mobile number and email. It's crucial to save these details for all future logins.
4.  **Complete the Application Form:** Log in using your new credentials and proceed to fill out the comprehensive application form. This will require detailed personal, contact, and academic information.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and all relevant academic mark sheets. Ensure these documents meet the specified size and format requirements.
6.  **Pay Application Fee:** Conclude your application by paying the required fee online. This can typically be done using a credit card, debit card, or net banking.

## Eligibility Criteria for SRCC GBO 2027

Before you begin your application, it's essential to confirm you meet the program's eligibility requirements:

*   Candidates must hold a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university with a minimum aggregate score of 50% or an equivalent grade.
*   Students who are currently in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply, provided they meet the minimum percentage requirement upon completion.
*   A 5% relaxation in the minimum aggregate score (i.e., 45%) is provided for candidates belonging to the OBC, SC, ST, and PwD categories.

## Important Dates for SRCC GBO 2027

While the official dates for SRCC GBO 2027 will be announced with the detailed notification, here's a look at the typical schedule based on previous years to help you plan:

| SRCC GBO Registration 2027  | Mid-December 2026 - Late January 2027     |
| SRCC GBO Admit Card Release | Mid-February 2027                         |

## Documents Required for Application

To ensure a smooth application process, have the following documents ready in digital format:

*   A recent scanned passport-sized photograph.
*   A scanned copy of your signature.
*   Scanned copies of your Class 10th and Class 12th mark sheets.
*   Scanned copies of all mark sheets from your undergraduate degree (or a consolidated mark sheet).
*   If applicable, a scanned copy of your latest OBC (Non-creamy layer) / SC / ST / PwD category certificate.
*   Details of your credit card, debit card, or internet banking for online fee payment.

## Key Considerations While Filling the Form

*   **Completeness:** Ensure all mandatory fields in the application form are filled. Incomplete applications may be rejected.
*   **Accuracy:** Double-check all information provided, especially personal details and academic scores, to avoid discrepancies.
*   **Document Specifications:** Adhere strictly to the prescribed specifications for uploading your photograph and signature (e.g., file size, dimensions).
*   **Timely Application:** It is advisable to apply well before the closing date to avoid last-minute technical issues or system overload.
*   **Confirmation:** After successful fee payment, ensure you receive a confirmation of your application. If not, contact the SRCC admissions office.

By following these guidelines, you can successfully navigate the SRCC GBO 2027 application process and take a significant step towards your academic goals.
