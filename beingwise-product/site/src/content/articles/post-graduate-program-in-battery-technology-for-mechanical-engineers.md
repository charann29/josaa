# Post Graduate Program in Battery Technology for Mechanical Engineers by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

The electric vehicle (EV) industry is rapidly expanding, creating a strong demand for engineers skilled in battery technology. This program is designed to equip mechanical engineers with the specialized knowledge needed to thrive in this exciting field.

## Key takeaways

*   This is an 8-month online postgraduate program focused on battery technology for electric vehicles.
*   It covers essential topics from electrochemistry to thermal management and battery pack design.
*   The program is delivered through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and hands-on projects.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a merit certificate.

## About the Program

The Post Graduate Program in Battery Technology for Mechanical Engineers is a specialized course aimed at those looking to build a career in battery systems or the broader EV industry. It delves into the core principles of battery technology, ensuring participants become proficient in the design, development, and management of battery systems.

The curriculum is delivered through online video lectures, practical technical projects, and hands-on exercises, all spread over an 8-month period. The instruction is entirely in English, utilizing a mix of video and text-based materials for comprehensive learning.

## Who is this Program For?

This program is ideal for a specific group of individuals:

*   **Engineering Graduates:** Those with degrees in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or Mechatronics who aspire to work in the research and development sectors of the automation and energy storage industries.
*   **Working Professionals and Scholars:** Individuals currently employed or engaged in academic pursuits who wish to transition into or advance within the EV industry, its supplier networks, or applied research institutions.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, you will gain a deep understanding of several critical areas, enabling you to contribute effectively to the battery technology sector. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Foundational Skills:** Basics of MATLAB, mathematical modeling, and simulation techniques.
*   **Electrochemistry:** Fundamentals and terminology of electrochemistry, crucial for understanding battery operation.
*   **Battery Systems:** Insights into battery pack construction, various energy storage technologies, Battery Management Systems (BMS), and charging mechanisms.
*   **Thermal Management:** Principles of thermal management, Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), and solving standard CFD problems using tools like OpenFOAM.
*   **Cooling System Design:** Engineering principles, working mechanisms, sizing, and component selection for cooling systems.
*   **Practical Applications:** Cell characterization and battery configuration using MATLAB, along with battery pack range estimation for vehicles using Microsoft Excel.

## Program Fees and Duration

The Post Graduate Program in Battery Technology for Mechanical Engineers has a total program fee of **Rs. 2,75,000**. For those preferring monthly payments, an EMI option is available at **Rs. 14,375 per month**.

The program duration is **8 months**.

Upon successful completion, participants are awarded a merit certificate directly from Skill Lync.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Post Graduate Program in Battery Technology for Mechanical Engineers is a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official Skill Lync website.
2.  **Locate the Course:** Find the specific course page for the Post Graduate Program in Battery Technology for Mechanical Engineers.
3.  **Initiate Enrollment:** Click on the "Enroll Now" option presented on the course page.
4.  **Complete Registration:** Select your preferred payment package and provide your name, email ID, and phone number to gain access to the program.

This program offers a robust pathway for mechanical engineers to specialize in the high-demand field of battery technology for electric vehicles.
