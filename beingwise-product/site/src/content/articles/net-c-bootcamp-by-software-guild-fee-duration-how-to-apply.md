# .NET/C# Bootcamp by Software Guild: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to start a career in software development, the .NET/C# Bootcamp by Software Guild offers a comprehensive online program. This 12-week course is designed to equip beginners with the essential skills needed to become a junior developer, all from the convenience of your home.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Beginner-Friendly:** The bootcamp is structured for individuals with no prior coding experience.
*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** It covers core programming concepts, tools like Visual Studio IDE, and practical application development.
*   **Extensive Career Support:** Participants receive assistance with resume building, interview preparation, and access to a network of over 450 employers.
*   **Flexible Learning:** The program is delivered entirely online through video and text-based modules, allowing for self-study and virtual classroom interaction.

## Program Overview

Developed by industry experts, this online bootcamp focuses on practical skills relevant to server-side programming. Over 12 weeks, you'll delve into object-oriented programming, learn to work with classes and objects, and gain proficiency in creating REST Web Services. The curriculum also includes hands-on experience with database interaction and various development tools such as .NET/C#, Visual Studio IDE, arrays, LINQ, and HTML. The course aims to transform participants into job-ready junior developers.

## Course Structure and Learning Experience

This rigorous program is delivered through a blend of self-study and virtual classroom sessions, utilizing video and text-based content. It’s designed to be accessible as a part-time course, making it suitable for a wide range of learners. Throughout the bootcamp, you will engage with assignments, aptitude tests, and projects that reinforce your learning. A strong emphasis is placed on practical application, ensuring you build a solid portfolio. Beyond technical skills, the program also includes training in soft skills, which are crucial for professional success.

## Fees and Financial Aid

The total fee for the .NET/C# Bootcamp is USD 10,125. This amount includes a registration fee of USD 125 and a tuition fee of USD 10,000. To make the program more accessible, Software Guild offers financial aid options through two plans: the Skills Fund Plan and the Climb Plan. Scholarships may also be available to eligible candidates.

## How to Apply

Applying for the .NET/C# Bootcamp involves a straightforward online process. Interested candidates should visit the official Software Guild website. On the program's dedicated page, you will find an "Apply Now" icon. Clicking this will lead you to an application form where you need to provide personal details such as your name, address, country, date of birth, highest education qualification, email, phone number, and any prior coding experience. The final step in the application process is to submit the registration fee.

## Certification and Career Support

Upon successful completion of the bootcamp, participants receive a certificate from Software Guild and digital badges. These badges are designed to help with career advancement and job placement. The program offers extensive career support, including resume building assistance, interview training, and guidance on leveraging social media for professional networking. Graduates also gain access to Software Guild's network of over 450 employers, significantly enhancing their job search prospects. The program has been recognized for its quality, receiving accolades such as "Best Bootcamp" from Switchup and Course Report.

This bootcamp provides a structured path for individuals aiming to enter the software development industry, equipping them with both technical expertise and essential career support.
