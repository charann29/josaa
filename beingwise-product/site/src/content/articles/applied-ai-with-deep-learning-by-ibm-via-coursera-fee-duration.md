# Applied AI with Deep Learning by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen to explore the cutting edge of Artificial Intelligence, the "Applied AI with Deep Learning" course, offered by IBM through Coursera, provides a robust foundation. This program is designed to equip learners with essential skills and insights into deep learning models.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 100% online, self-paced program, making it accessible from anywhere.
*   It is an advanced-level course, part of the broader IBM Advanced Data Science Specialization.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a shareable and verifiable certificate.
*   The course is delivered in English, with subtitles available in multiple languages, and offers flexible deadlines for assignments.

## Course Overview

This certification program delves into the core principles of deep learning, covering topics from linear algebra and neural networks to advanced concepts like time series forecasting, natural language processing (NLP), and image recognition. It aims to provide participants with a deep understanding of cloud architecture in AI and practical experience with various deep learning frameworks.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical skills. You will gain detailed insights into:

*   **Cloud Architecture for AI & Deep Learning:** Understanding how cloud environments support AI applications.
*   **Neural Networks:** Exploring both convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and recurrent neural networks (RNNs).
*   **Deep Learning Frameworks:** Introduction to TensorFlow with sequential models in Keras, as well as PyTorch installation and package usage.
*   **Advanced Concepts:** Working with SystemML, tensor creation, computational graphs, and linear models.
*   **Practical Applications:** Applying deep learning to real-world problems such as anomaly detection, time series forecasting, image classification, and text classification.
*   **Scaling and Deployment:** Learning to scale and deploy deep learning models using Apache Spark through Apache SystemML.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in this advanced program, candidates should possess:

*   **Coding Proficiency:** Demonstrated skills in Python programming language.
*   **Mathematical Foundation:** A basic understanding of linear algebra is essential.

To earn the verified certificate, participants must successfully complete all practice quizzes and the final examination within each module, adhering to the specified timelines.

The "Applied AI with Deep Learning" course is estimated to take approximately 25 hours to complete. ## How to Apply

Applying for the "Applied AI with Deep Learning" online program is a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Official Platform:** Navigate to the Coursera website.
2.  **Locate the Course:** Search for "Applied AI with Deep Learning by IBM."
3.  **Enroll:** Select the "Enroll" option, which might appear as a button or within a drop-down menu.
4.  **Complete Registration:** Follow the on-screen prompts to fill out any required qualification details.
5.  **Payment:** Proceed with the payment of the course fee to gain full access to all learning materials, video lectures, quizzes, and practice exercises.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to enhance your skills in a rapidly evolving field, preparing you for advanced roles in AI and data science.
