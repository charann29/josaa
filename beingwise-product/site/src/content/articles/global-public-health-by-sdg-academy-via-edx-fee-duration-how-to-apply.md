# Global Public Health by SDG Academy via Edx: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program offers a deep dive into global public health, exploring its critical role in achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). It's designed to help individuals understand the intricate connections between health, societal well-being, and sustainable development worldwide.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn from experts affiliated with institutions like Harvard and NITI Aayog.
*   Gain a verified certificate upon completion, suitable for professional profiles.
*   The course is fully online, self-paced, and takes approximately 10 weeks.
*   No specific academic or professional background is required to enroll.

## Course Overview

Offered by the SDG Academy, an initiative of the UN Sustainable Development Solutions Network, this program is designed for intermediate learners. It delves into the fundamental importance of individual and collective health, aligning its curriculum with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals. The course content covers how a nation's strength is built upon four crucial pillars: economic stability, individual health, social equity, and environmental sustainability.

The curriculum is structured with one module per week, allowing participants to progress at their own convenience. While the course material is available for a limited free audit, a purchasable certificate is offered for those who wish to formally complete the program and receive recognition for their learning.

## What You Will Learn

The Global Public Health program provides a comprehensive understanding of epidemiology and its global implications. Participants will explore:

*   The planet's epidemiological and demographic shifts over time.
*   Current and future trends shaping public health initiatives worldwide.
*   Historical and ongoing global impacts on collective well-being.
*   How infrastructure and institutions influence the healthcare industry.

### Syllabus Breakdown

The course is structured into weekly modules, starting with foundational concepts:

*   **Introducing Public Health**
    *   Introduction & History of Public Health
    *   Multiple Determinants of Health
    *   Evolution of Global Public Health
    *   Measuring Health Status

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for a broad audience, including students, practitioners, and professionals in fields such as healthcare, international development, and public policy. It also welcomes anyone with a keen interest in understanding the core concepts and practices aimed at eradicating poverty and promoting universal education. The course is taught by a panel of distinguished experts from institutions like Harvard, the Public Health Foundation of India, NITI Aayog, and the Royal Institute of International Affairs.

## Fees and Duration

The Global Public Health program is a 10-week, self-paced online course. While specific certificate fees are not detailed here, participants can opt to purchase a verified certificate upon completion. This certificate can be shared on professional networking platforms like LinkedIn or included in resumes and portfolios. The course is delivered through video lectures and text-based materials, requiring approximately 4-6 hours of study per week.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Global Public Health program is a straightforward online process. There are no specific eligibility criteria regarding educational background or professional experience, making it accessible to a wide range of learners.

1.  **Visit the Platform:** Access the course through the official platform where it is hosted.
2.  **Enroll:** Locate and click the 'Enrol' option, typically found at the top of the course page.
3.  **Create an Account:** You will be prompted to either log in or create a new account. New users can sign up using existing accounts from Microsoft, Google, Apple, or Facebook, or by manually entering details such as name, email address, country, and creating a unique username and password.
4.  **Access Learning Materials:** Once registered, you can access all course content and materials through your personalized student dashboard.

This program provides a valuable opportunity to contribute to global health discussions and initiatives, fostering a deeper understanding of sustainable development goals.
