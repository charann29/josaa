# What is the Cutoff for SC Category for Oral Pathology through All India Quota?

Understanding the cutoff ranks for admission to MDS Oral Pathology programs through the All India Quota (AIQ) is crucial for SC category candidates. These cutoffs fluctuate annually based on various factors like the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats.

## Key Takeaways

*   MDS Oral Pathology cutoffs for the SC category under AIQ are subject to change each year.
*   Specific college preferences significantly impact the rank required for admission.
*   Broadly, a higher rank (lower numerical value) increases your chances of securing a seat.
*   Always refer to official counseling authorities for the most precise and up-to-date information.

## Understanding All India Quota (AIQ) Admissions

The All India Quota comprises 50% of the total seats in government dental colleges across India for MDS courses. These seats are open to candidates from all states, and admission is based on merit determined by the NEET MDS examination. The Medical Counseling Committee (MCC) manages the counseling process for AIQ seats, including those reserved for the Scheduled Caste (SC) category.

## How Cutoffs Are Determined

Cutoffs are essentially the last rank at which admission was granted to a particular course and college in a specific category during the counseling rounds. For the SC category in MDS Oral Pathology, this means the rank of the last SC candidate who successfully secured a seat through the AIQ. Several elements influence these cutoffs:

*   **Number of Applicants:** A higher number of candidates applying for Oral Pathology can drive ranks higher.
*   **Availability of Seats:** The total number of MDS Oral Pathology seats in a given year, especially those reserved for SC, plays a direct role.
*   **NEET MDS Performance:** The overall performance of candidates in the NEET MDS exam affects the competitive landscape.
*   **Choice Filling:** The preferences filled by candidates during counseling significantly influence where the cutoffs land for various institutions.

## Finding Current Cutoff Information

For the most accurate and up-to-date cutoff information, candidates should always refer to the official Medical Counseling Committee (MCC) website during the counseling period. The MCC publishes detailed cutoff ranks after each round of AIQ counseling, broken down by college, course, and category. This is the definitive source for understanding the exact ranks required for admission in the current year.

Careful research and strategic choice filling based on these trends and your NEET MDS rank will be key to your admission journey.
