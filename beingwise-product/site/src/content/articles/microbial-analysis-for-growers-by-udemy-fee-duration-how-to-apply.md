# Microbial Analysis for Growers by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone involved in agriculture, understanding the microscopic world beneath our feet is crucial for healthy crops and sustainable farming. The "Microbial Analysis for Growers" online course, offered through Udemy, is designed to enhance your knowledge and practical skills in assessing soil microbiology.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to identify microbial indicators that contribute to soil health.
*   Understand how to use various equipment for soil biology assessments.
*   Improve plant quality, reduce input needs, and potentially increase yields.
*   Receive a certificate of completion after successfully finishing the self-paced program.

## Course Overview

This certification program focuses on applying microbiological, biochemical, molecular, or chemical methods to identify, characterize, or quantify microorganisms in substances, particularly soil. It's a valuable skill for eradicating disease-causing germs and preventing spoilage, ultimately leading to more robust plant growth. Created by systems biologist Mary Lucero, the course aims to deepen participants' understanding of microbial physiology relevant to agriculture.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this course, you will develop a foundational understanding of farming principles and specific strategies for microbial analysis of soil. The curriculum covers fundamental concepts related to different types of soil, plants, and microbes, including eukaryotes and prokaryotes. You'll gain insights into examining soil quality, microbial density, and diversity, along with practical techniques for using microscopes and managing agricultural guides.

## Course Structure and Content

The "Microbial Analysis for Growers" program is delivered through a self-study format, primarily using video and text-based materials. It includes approximately three hours of video lessons, supplemented by two articles and nine downloadable resources. The course also incorporates assignments and quizzes to reinforce learning. You'll have unlimited access to the materials, and the content is accessible on various devices, including mobile phones and televisions.

### Syllabus Highlights

The course begins with an introduction explaining its purpose. It then moves into practical aspects of Microbial Density and Diversity (MDD) analysis, covering:

*   **Why Microbes Matter:** Understanding the roles microbes play.
*   **Performing MDD Analysis:** The importance and benefits of this analysis.
*   **Equipment Selection:** Choosing the right microscopes and other tools.
*   **Sampling Techniques:** How to take informative soil samples.
*   **Sample Preparation:** Protocols for preparing samples for analysis.
*   **Microbial Classification:** Understanding different microbial communities.
*   **Beneficial Microbes:** Identifying desirable microbial kingdoms in your soil.
*   **Measuring Microbes:** Techniques for quantifying microorganisms.
*   **Cell Recognition:** Identifying living cells and prokaryotes.
