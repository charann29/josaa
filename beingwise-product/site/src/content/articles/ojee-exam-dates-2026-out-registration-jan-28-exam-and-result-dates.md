# OJEE Exam Dates 2026 (OUT): Registration (Jan 28), Exam, & Result Dates

The Odisha Joint Entrance Examination (OJEE) is a crucial state-level entrance test for admissions into various undergraduate and postgraduate programs across Odisha. This page provides a comprehensive overview of the OJEE 2026 schedule, including registration, admit card release, exam dates, and result announcements.

## Key takeaways

*   OJEE 2026 registration opened on January 28, 2026.
*   The application correction window was open from March 30 to April 1, 2026.
*   The OJEE 2026 main examination will be conducted from May 4 to May 9, 2026.
*   Admit cards will be available for download from April 25, 2026, on the official OJEE portal.

## OJEE Registration and Application Process 2026

The registration window for OJEE 2026 commenced on January 28, 2026, and concluded on March 29, 2026. Candidates were advised to complete their applications and fee payments within this period. Following the initial registration, a short window for application form corrections was provided between March 30 and April 1, 2026. This allowed applicants to rectify any errors in their submitted forms, ensuring accuracy before the admit card generation.

## OJEE Admit Card 2026

The OJEE 2026 admit cards are scheduled for release on April 25, 2026. Candidates who successfully submitted their applications will be able to download their admit cards from the official OJEE website, ojee.nic.in. It is mandatory for all candidates to carry a printed copy of their admit card to the examination center, along with a valid photo ID. Entry to the examination hall will not be permitted without these essential documents.

## OJEE 2026 Examination Dates

The OJEE 2026 examination will be conducted in a computer-based test (CBT) format across multiple days: May 4, May 5, May 7, May 8, and May 9, 2026. This state-level entrance exam facilitates admission into various programs including MBA, MCA, M.Tech, M.Pharm, B.Pharm, BAMS, BHMS, and lateral entry options for B.Tech and MCA. The specific exam dates for the Special OJEE, which addresses vacant seats in certain programs, are typically announced later, usually in May.

## OJEE Answer Key and Results 2026

Following the examination, the OJEE Cell will release the official answer key on ojee.nic.in. This typically happens a few days after the final exam date. A window will be provided for candidates to raise objections against any discrepancies they find in the answer key, requiring supporting documentation. The OJEE 2026 results are tentatively expected in the second week of June 2026. Candidates will be able to access their scorecards online through their candidate portals on the official website. Results for both the main OJEE and the Special OJEE will be published online.
