## SRCC GBO Application and Exam Details

The application window for the SRCC GBO 2026 program was open from December 15, 2025, until January 23, 2026. Prospective candidates were required to complete their registration during this period. The entrance examination itself was conducted on February 28, 2026. It was a computer-based test, held in a single shift from 3 PM to 5 PM, with a sectional time limit of 30 minutes for each section.

## Answer Key and Objection Process

Following the examination, the official SRCC GBO answer key for 2026 was made available for download on the official GBO website. Candidates were provided an opportunity to challenge the answer key by filing objections from March 5, 2026, with the deadline for submissions being March 16, 2026. The examination authority thoroughly reviews all submitted objections. If any are deemed valid, the official answer key is revised, and the final answer key is then released alongside the results.

## Result and Selection Process

The SRCC GBO 2026 written exam results are anticipated to be released in the second week of April 2026. Candidates can access their scorecards by logging into the official SRCC GBO portal. Those who qualify based on their written exam scores will then proceed to the subsequent selection rounds. The counselling process, which includes Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds, is expected to commence in May 2026. These rounds are typically conducted on campus, and the results for the PI round are also published on the official exam website.
