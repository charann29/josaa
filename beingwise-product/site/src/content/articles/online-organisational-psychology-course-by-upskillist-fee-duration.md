# Online Organisational Psychology Course by Upskillist: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This BeingWise guide provides a detailed look into the online Organisational Psychology course offered by Upskillist, designed to enhance your understanding of human behaviour in the workplace. It's a great opportunity for anyone looking to build skills in managing teams and improving organisational dynamics.

## Key takeaways

*   **University Certification:** The course is certified by Austin Peay State University (APSU), USA, adding a recognised academic credential.
*   **Flexible Learning:** A 16-week, self-paced program with lifelong access to course materials, allowing you to learn at your convenience.
*   **Comprehensive Content:** Features 32 video lessons, quizzes, and assessments across four modules, covering core concepts of organisational psychology.
*   **Global Recognition:** The certification is recognised by the International Accreditation Organisation (IAO) and the International Council of Specialised Online Certifications (ICSOC).

## Course Overview

The Online Organisational Psychology Course is a 16-week training program developed by Upskillist and certified by Austin Peay State University (APSU) in the United States. Its primary goal is to provide participants with deep insights into the field of organisational psychology, exploring its fundamental concepts and practical applications within an organisational context. The curriculum aims to help you understand systemic issues that can impact an organisation's effectiveness and how to address them.

This program is designed to equip you with the knowledge to effectively interact with individuals and teams at work, by understanding the underlying psychological processes. It features an up-to-date curriculum delivered through 32 video lessons, accompanied by quizzes and assessments, structured into four distinct modules.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of this online curriculum, you will gain a thorough understanding of organisational psychology and be able to:

*   Analyse how individual psychology influences behaviour and thought processes within a professional setting.
*   Identify and address systemic problems to improve an organisation's overall functioning.
*   Define and explain the core concepts and practical scope of organisational psychology.
*   Develop effective strategies for managing teams in the workplace.
*   Anticipate emerging trends that could impact organisations globally.

## Who Is This Course For?

This course is particularly well-suited for individuals who hold managerial responsibilities or lead teams and wish to deepen their understanding of employee psychology within an organisation. If you are looking to enhance your ability to manage people effectively and foster a more productive work environment, this program can provide valuable insights and tools.

## Course Structure and Offerings

The program is delivered through a self-directed learning model, allowing participants to progress at their own pace. Key features include:

*   **Duration:** 16 weeks of training.
*   **Content:** 32 video lessons organised into 4 comprehensive modules.
*   **Access:** Unlimited, lifelong access to course materials, including downloadable lectures.
*   **Support:** Live educator support is available.
*   **Flexibility:** The option to pause your training if needed.
*   **Assessment:** Weekly assessments and a final exam at the end of each module, with instant online grading.

## Certification and Eligibility

Upon meeting the certification criteria, you will receive a globally accredited certificate from Upskillist. To qualify for the certificate, you must achieve a minimum score of 50% on all assessments. The weekly assessments consist of 20 questions, while the final module exams have 50 questions each.

## Fee and Application Process

Details regarding the course fee are typically available on the official Upskillist course page. To enrol, you generally follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official Upskillist website and navigate to the Organisational Psychology course page.
2.  Look for a button or link that allows you to start a free trial or begin the application process.
3.  Complete the application form by selecting your preferences regarding learning goals and time commitment.
4.  Proceed with the enrolment steps as guided on the platform.

This course offers a valuable opportunity to develop critical skills in organisational psychology, enhancing your professional capabilities and understanding of workplace dynamics.
