# Certified HR Compliance Professional by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to excel in Human Resources, understanding compliance is crucial. The Certified HR Compliance Professional course from Vskills offers a comprehensive online program designed to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the complex world of HR regulations.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a self-paced, online certification program, allowing you to learn at your own convenience.
*   The course provides a government-recognised certificate from Vskills upon successful completion.
*   It covers vital areas like labor law compliance, international HR compliance, and managing workplace diversity.
*   The program includes practice tests and study materials to prepare you for the final online assessment.

## Understanding the Course

This certification is tailored for HR executives, graduates from any background, and professionals aiming to deepen their expertise in human resources. The curriculum is designed to enhance your ability to manage a company's human capital effectively, ensuring compliance with current legal frameworks and employee rights.

The course delves into various aspects of human resource management, including:

*   Fundamentals of HR compliance
*   International HR compliance standards
*   Submissions related to HR compliance
*   Labor law compliance
*   Implementing HR compliance strategies
*   Organizational HR compliance
*   Labor welfare principles

By completing this program, you can gain a significant advantage in the competitive HR industry, understanding the challenges and developing new perspectives to address them.

## Course Structure and Offerings

The Certified HR Compliance Professional program is delivered through a self-study model, utilizing both video and text-based materials. As a participant, you'll gain access to Vskills' Learning Management System (LMS), which hosts all the necessary study materials and tutorials.

Key features and offerings of the program include:

*   **Government-recognised certificate:** Awarded by Vskills upon successful completion.
*   **Self-paced learning:** Study at your own schedule and pace.
*   **Free practice tests:** Essential for preparing for the final certification exam.
*   **Comprehensive study material:** All resources provided through the e-learning portal.
*   **Interview preparation:** Support to help you prepare for career opportunities.
*   **Internship facility:** An added benefit for practical exposure.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Certified HR Compliance Professional course is ₹3,499. This covers access to all learning materials, practice tests, and the final online assessment. The fee structure is straightforward, with a single payment required for the self-paced learning option.

Upon successfully passing the online certification exam with a minimum score of 50%, you will receive your certificate from Vskills. This certificate signifies your expertise in HR compliance and is a valuable addition to your professional profile.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Certified HR Compliance Professional certification is a simple online process:

1.  **Visit the official Vskills webpage** for the Certified HR Compliance Professional course.
2.  **Locate the 'Buy Now' button** on the course page and click it to proceed to the 'Checkout' page.
3.  **Register with Vskills** by providing your contact and other necessary details. If you already have an account, you can log in.
4.  **Choose your preferred online payment mode** and complete the required payment to finalize your enrollment.
5.  **Receive login credentials:** Vskills will send you an email with your login details, granting you immediate access to the LMS to begin your studies.

This straightforward application process ensures you can quickly start your journey towards becoming a certified HR compliance professional.

## Who is this course for?

This program is ideal for a diverse group of individuals, including:

*   **HR Professionals:** Those already working in HR who wish to formalize their knowledge in compliance or advance their career.
*   **Graduates:** Individuals from any academic background looking to enter the HR field with specialized knowledge.
*   **Business Owners/Managers:** Anyone responsible for managing employees who needs to ensure their operations are legally compliant.

By enhancing your understanding of HR compliance, you can improve employee productivity, mitigate legal risks, and contribute significantly to your organization's success.
