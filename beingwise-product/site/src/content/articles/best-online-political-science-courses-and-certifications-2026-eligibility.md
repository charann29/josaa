# Best Online Political Science Courses & Certifications 2026 - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Scope

Online Political Science courses and certifications offer a flexible way to delve into the complexities of government, policy, and international relations. These programs are ideal for students and working professionals looking to enhance their understanding of political systems without the constraints of traditional on-campus study.

## Key takeaways

*   **Flexibility:** Online degrees in Political Science, from Bachelor's to Master's levels, provide the convenience of remote learning.
*   **Diverse Curriculum:** Courses cover foundational political theories, modern governance, international relations, and public administration.
*   **Career Advancement:** Graduates can pursue roles in public policy, research, academia, journalism, and advocacy.
*   **Accessible Eligibility:** Most undergraduate programs require a 10+2 qualification, while postgraduate degrees are open to graduates from any stream.

## Undergraduate Online Political Science Programs

Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) offers a prominent online Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Political Science. This three-year program is structured over six semesters and is designed to introduce students to the core principles of Political Science. The curriculum includes topics such as modern government structures, contemporary models of governance, key issues in Indian politics, and international relations.

### Eligibility and Fees for Online BA Political Science

To be eligible for the online BA Political Science at AMU, candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 education from any academic stream. The annual fee for Indian students is ₹8,500, while international students pay US$400 per year. Further details regarding admissions and eligibility can be found on the official AMU portal.

## Postgraduate Online Political Science Programs

Several esteemed institutions offer online Master of Arts (MA) in Political Science programs, catering to graduates from diverse backgrounds who wish to deepen their understanding of political dynamics.

### Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) Online MA Political Science

AMU also provides an online Master of Arts in Political Science. This program is crafted to give students an in-depth understanding of political thought, international relations, and global politics. Key subjects include Comparative Politics, Political Sociology, Principles of Public Administration, and International Law. Graduates from any discipline are welcome to apply.

### University of Lucknow Online MA Political Science

The University of Lucknow's online MA in Political Science aims to provide a comprehensive grasp of political theories, constitutional frameworks, and global political dynamics. This program emphasizes both Indian and Western political thought and prepares students for managerial and administrative roles in public administration, governance, research, and policy-making.

### Integral University Online MA Political Science

Integral University, Lucknow, offers a two-year online MA in Political Science. The curriculum focuses on advanced knowledge and skills in politics, covering areas like political theory, international politics, and peace and conflict studies. This program prepares students for careers in academia, public policy, advocacy, government, and media.

### Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya (GGU) Bilaspur Online MA Political Science

GGU Bilaspur, through its Centre for Distance and Online Education (CDOE), offers an online MA in Political Science. This postgraduate degree is designed for those interested in political systems, governance, and public policy, providing a strong academic foundation for understanding both Indian and global political structures. It helps students develop analytical and research skills for contemporary political issues.

## Career Scope After Online Political Science Degrees

An online degree in Political Science opens doors to a variety of career paths. Graduates can pursue roles such as:

*   **Policy Advisor:** Working with government bodies or NGOs to shape public policy.
*   **Public Relations Officer:** Managing communication and public perception for political entities or organizations.
*   **Teacher/Professor:** Educating future generations in academic institutions.
*   **Researcher/Analyst:** Conducting political research for think tanks, universities, or media houses.
*   **Journalist/Writer:** Reporting on political events and analyzing current affairs.
*   **Election Campaign Manager:** Organizing and executing political campaigns.
*   **International Relations Specialist:** Working with international organizations or diplomatic services.

These online programs provide a robust academic foundation and practical skills, preparing individuals for impactful contributions in various sectors.
