# AccioJob Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online AccioJob Courses

AccioJob offers a range of online certification programs designed to equip students and professionals with in-demand skills for the technology industry. These courses cover various aspects of full-stack development and data analytics, aiming to enhance career prospects through practical training and industry-aligned content.

## Key takeaways

*   AccioJob provides online certification courses in MERN Full Stack, JAVA Full Stack, and Data Analytics.
*   Many programs are accredited by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), adding significant value to the certificates.
*   Courses typically include live classes, practical assignments, projects, and often offer job assistance upon successful completion.
*   The curriculum is often developed and delivered by experienced industry professionals and IIT alumni.

## MERN Full Stack Web Development Program

The MERN Full Stack Web Development course is a comprehensive certificate program offered by AccioJob, in collaboration with the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). This program dives deep into all areas of MERN stack development, covering essential programming concepts, fundamental and advanced JavaScript, and backend development techniques.

This 8-month program provides participants with access to live learning sessions for a full 12 months. Successful candidates receive an NSDC-accredited certificate. The curriculum emphasizes practical application through assignments, internships, and real-world projects, giving students valuable industry insights. Additional features include tests, one-on-one doubt-clearing sessions, and mock interviews. AccioJob also extends job placement support to those who meet specific performance criteria.

## JAVA Full Stack Development Program

AccioJob's JAVA Full Stack Development course is an online program designed to provide in-depth knowledge of Java full-stack development, preparing students for relevant job roles. The course covers a broad spectrum of topics, including algorithms, data structures, and programming principles. It also incorporates practical experience through projects and internships.

Under the guidance of IIT alumni and seasoned industry experts, students gain a detailed understanding of JavaScript, CSS, HTML, and Spring Boot. The live online classes are held daily and include features like doubt-clearing sessions, weekly contests, and practice interviews. This 8-month program offers 12 months of live learning access. Upon completion, participants receive an NSDC-accredited certificate. Job assistance is provided to candidates who achieve a minimum score of 70% in placement mock interviews and 60% in mid-course and full-course mock interviews.

## Data Analytics Program

The Data Analytics Program from AccioJob is another NSDC-certified offering. This course is structured to develop not only technical skills but also critical thinking abilities such as strategic thinking, problem-solving, and effective communication. It is suitable for both freshers and experienced individuals.

Students in this program learn directly from top data leaders who specialize in solving guesstimates and case studies. The training covers a variety of industry-standard tools, including SQL, PowerBI, Excel, Python, and introduces basic Machine Learning concepts. Completing the course earns students an NSDC-accredited certificate. Furthermore, participants gain access to AccioJob's learning portal, where they can connect with IITians working in the data industry for career guidance and job search support.

These programs aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry demands, offering a structured path to career advancement in the tech sector.
