The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT), in collaboration with Swayam, offers an online certification course for Class 12 Geography Part-I. This program is designed to deepen students' understanding of key geographical concepts, including human geography, population dynamics, and economic activities.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a free, self-paced online course focusing on Class 12 Geography Part-I.
*   It spans 24 weeks and is delivered through video lectures, e-text, and self-assessment modules.
*   Participants can earn a certificate upon successful completion, awarded by NCERT and Swayam.
*   The course covers essential topics like human development, international trade, and various human activities.

## Course Overview

This comprehensive online program delves into the environmental and social aspects of geography, exploring fundamental principles of human geography and the intricate relationship between people and their surroundings. Coordinated by Dr. Tannu Malik from NCERT's Department of Education in Social Sciences (DESS), the course is hosted on the Swayam platform, providing accessible learning for students across India.

The curriculum is structured into various modules, each featuring engaging video lectures, supplementary audio materials, e-tutorials, and a wealth of web resources. To reinforce learning, students are required to participate in self-assessments, which include multiple-choice questions and other evaluative tests.

## Learning Experience and Resources

The Geography XII Part-I course emphasizes an interactive learning approach. Beyond video and text-based content, it incorporates discussion forums where students can engage with peers and instructors, fostering a collaborative learning environment. The self-assessment tools are crucial for tracking progress and ensuring a solid grasp of the material. All course materials are provided in English, making it accessible to a wide audience.

## Certificate and Eligibility

Upon successfully completing all course evaluations, students will be eligible to receive a certificate. To earn this certification, participants must achieve a score of at least 70% in the final assessment. The certificate is jointly issued by NCERT and Swayam, validating the student's acquired knowledge in the subject. There are no fees associated with enrolling in the course or receiving the certificate.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Geography XII Part-I Certification Course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam platform.
2.  Locate the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the new page that appears, you can either sign in with an existing account or create a new one using your username and password.

## Course Syllabus Highlights

The course is structured into ten chapters, each with multiple modules and corresponding assignments. Key topics include:

*   **Chapter 1:** Nature and scope of human geography.
*   **Chapter 2:** Global population distribution, density, and growth.
*   **Chapter 3:** Population composition.
*   **Chapter 4:** Human development.
*   **Chapter 5:** Primary human activities, including agriculture.
*   **Chapter 6:** Secondary activities, focusing on manufacturing and industries.
*   **Chapter 7:** Tertiary, quaternary, and quinary activities.
*   **Chapter 8:** Various modes of transport (road, rail, water, air, pipelines) and communication.
*   **Chapter 9:** International trade.
*   **Chapter 10:** Rural settlements.

Each chapter concludes with an assignment to test understanding, culminating in a final assessment at the end of the course.

This course is an excellent opportunity for Class 12 students to strengthen their geographical knowledge and earn a valuable certification from a reputable national body.
