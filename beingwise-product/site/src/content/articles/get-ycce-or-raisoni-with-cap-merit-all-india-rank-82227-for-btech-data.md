# Can I Get YCCE or Raisoni with CAP Merit All India Rank 82227 for B.Tech Data Science?

Navigating B.Tech admissions in Maharashtra can be complex, especially when aiming for specific branches like Data Science at popular colleges. Let's explore your prospects for YCCE and GH Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur, with an All India Rank (AIR) of 82,227.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your AIR of 82,227 presents a good opportunity for B.Tech Data Science at YCCE, Nagpur, through CAP counseling.
*   GH Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur, for Data Science may be challenging at this rank, as their cutoffs are typically higher.
*   Strategic option filling in CAP rounds is crucial to maximize your chances, particularly for YCCE.
*   Consider related branches like Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS) to broaden your options.

## Understanding CAP Counselling and Cutoffs

The Centralized Admission Process (CAP) is the primary method for B.Tech admissions in Maharashtra's engineering colleges. Colleges declare cutoff ranks for various branches, which represent the last rank admitted in a particular category during a specific round. These cutoffs can fluctuate each year based on factors like the number of applicants, seat availability, and the preferences of students.

For Data Science, a rapidly growing field, competition can be significant. However, colleges often have different cutoff trends. It's important to differentiate between colleges when assessing your chances.

## Your Chances at YCCE, Nagpur for Data Science

Yashwantrao Chavan College of Engineering (YCCE), Nagpur, has historically shown a broader range in its B.Tech Data Science cutoffs. For candidates in the open category, it's common to see cutoffs extend to a much higher AIR, sometimes even up to 2 to 2.5 lakh in later CAP rounds. Given your AIR of 82,227, you are well within this historical range. This suggests a strong likelihood of securing a seat in B.Tech Data Science at YCCE, especially if you participate actively in the various CAP rounds and fill your preferences wisely.

## Prospects at GH Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur

GH Raisoni College of Engineering (GHRCE), Nagpur, generally experiences higher demand and, consequently, stricter cutoffs for sought-after branches like Data Science. Based on past trends, an AIR of 82,227 might be on the borderline or potentially above the closing rank for Data Science at GHRCE, particularly in the earlier CAP rounds. While it's not impossible, your chances here are comparatively lower than at YCCE, unless there are unusual circumstances such as vacant seats in the very late stages of counseling.

## Strategic Option Filling in CAP Rounds

To optimize your admission prospects, a well-thought-out strategy for filling your options during the CAP rounds is essential. Prioritize YCCE's B.Tech Data Science program high on your list. If you are also open to related fields, consider including B.Tech Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS) at both colleges, as this can offer more flexibility. Always refer to the official CAP information brochure and cutoff lists released by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, for the most accurate and up-to-date data during the admission cycle.

With careful planning and strategic choices during CAP counselling, your goal of pursuing B.Tech Data Science is well within reach.
