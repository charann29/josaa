# XLRI Jamshedpur Shortlist Criteria 2026: Check XAT Cut Offs, Admission and PI Process

Securing a spot at XLRI Jamshedpur, a premier institution for management education, involves a rigorous selection process primarily based on XAT scores. This guide outlines the key steps, XAT cut-offs, and the subsequent interview rounds for the 2026 admissions.

## Key takeaways

*   XLRI shortlists candidates for its PGDM programs (BM, HRM, GMP) based on XAT scores and academic performance.
*   The XAT cut-offs are crucial for initial shortlisting, with separate percentiles for male and female candidates.
*   Shortlisted candidates must complete an online interview slot booking form within a specified timeframe.
*   The selection process includes an Essay Writing component and a Personal Interview (PI) round.

## Understanding the XLRI Shortlisting Process

XLRI Jamshedpur initiates its admission process by evaluating candidates' performance in the XAT examination. For the PGDM Business Management (BM) and PGDM Human Resource Management (HRM) programs, the institute considers both XAT scores and the applicant's academic history to create the shortlist for the subsequent rounds.

Following the XAT result declaration, typically in mid-January, XLRI sends out interview letters to shortlisted candidates. These candidates are then required to fill out an online interview shortlist form by a specific deadline to confirm their eligibility for the Essay Writing and Personal Interview (PI) rounds.

## XAT Cut-Offs for XLRI Jamshedpur

The XAT cut-offs are a critical component of the initial shortlisting phase. These cut-offs can vary slightly each year based on the overall performance of test-takers and the number of applicants. For the 2026 admissions cycle, the cut-offs are anticipated to remain in a similar range to the previous year.

Here's an overview of the expected XAT 2026 cut-offs for the PGDM BM and HRM programs:

| Program | Category | Expected XAT 2026 Cut-off Percentile |
| :------ | :------- | :----------------------------------- |
| PGDM BM | Male     | 96                                   |
| PGDM BM | Female   | 91                                   |
| PGDM HRM| Male     | 95                                   |
| PGDM HRM| Female   | 90                                   |

*Note: These are indicative cut-offs. The official cut-offs will be released by XLRI Jamshedpur.* 

## The XLRI Selection Procedure 2026

Once shortlisted, candidates proceed to the final stages of the admission process, which include Essay Writing and Personal Interview (PI). These rounds are generally conducted from mid-February to March, with candidates selecting their preferred slot and city. Detailed information regarding the PI rounds for 2026 admission will be communicated directly to shortlisted candidates via email.

### Essay Writing

In the Essay Writing round, candidates are given a topic and asked to compose a short essay. This segment assesses a candidate's ability to articulate thoughts clearly and effectively, along with their overall communication skills. It's an opportunity to demonstrate structured thinking and written expression.

### Personal Interview (PI)

The Personal Interview is a direct interaction between the candidate and the selection panel. Common questions often revolve around personal background, career aspirations, motivations for pursuing an MBA, and specific interest in XLRI. This round aims to gauge a candidate's personality, leadership potential, problem-solving skills, and suitability for the demanding management programs at XLRI.

Candidates must ensure they select their interview slot within the stipulated time by filling out the online application form available on the official XLRI or XAT website.
