## CFPCM Certification Training Program

The CFPCM Certification Training Program is a leading qualification in financial planning, a field with significant career prospects. IMS Proschool, as an approved educational provider, delivers comprehensive training to prepare individuals for the CFPCM exam and other related financial planning certifications.

This program offers flexible learning avenues, including traditional classroom sessions (often with weekend batches for convenience), live virtual classrooms, and a self-paced study format. The curriculum culminates in a computer-based examination featuring multiple-choice questions and case studies. Successful completion of this exam allows participants to apply for the CFPCM certification.

*   **Duration:** 15 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹ 60,000
*   **Key Skills:** Financial Management

## ACCA Course: Skill Level

The ACCA Course: Skill Level certification, offered by IMS Proschool, is an advanced program focusing on core business and accounting principles. It aims to develop crucial skills for a career in finance and accounting.

Students can choose between an offline classroom program and live online training. The online sessions are interactive, conducted on the CISCO platform, and led by educators qualified by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA). The learning experience includes recorded video lectures, assignments, mock tests, and regular feedback from faculty. The syllabus is structured into six modules, covering areas like Financial Management, Audit and Assurance, Taxation, and Financial Reporting. Completing specific criteria within this program can also lead to an advanced diploma in accounting and business.

*   **Duration:** 15 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹ 1,25,000
*   **Key Skills:** Decision-making, Financial Management, Taxation Knowledge

## Certificate in Financial Planning

The Certificate in Financial Planning is a program built on the CFP-FBSP curriculum, designed to introduce individuals to the fundamentals of financial planning. This course is ideal for those looking to begin a career in investment advisory and management roles.

Through high-quality video sessions, students learn the basics of financial planning, asset allocation, and risk management strategies. This program is suitable for undergraduates, graduates, and working professionals aspiring to enter the financial planning sector. IMS Proschool also offers placement support to help graduates secure employment opportunities.

*   **Duration:** 15 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹ 50,000
*   **Key Skills:** Legal Knowledge, Financial Management

## Investment Banking Course With PG Certificate

The Post Graduate Program (PGP) in Investment Banking & Capital Markets focuses on providing the practical skills and knowledge necessary for a professional career in investment finance. This program emphasizes project-based learning, offering a real-world perspective on investment banking operations.
