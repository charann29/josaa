To make the most of these free e-books and sample papers, consider these strategies:

1.  **Simulate Exam Conditions:** When attempting a sample paper, try to replicate the actual exam environment. Set a timer and avoid distractions to get a realistic sense of the time pressure.
2.  **Review Solutions Thoroughly:** Don't just check if your answer is right or wrong. Go through the detailed solutions to understand the concepts and problem-solving approaches. This is where significant learning happens.
