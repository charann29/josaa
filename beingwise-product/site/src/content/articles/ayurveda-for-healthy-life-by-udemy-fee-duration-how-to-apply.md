# Ayurveda for Healthy Life by BeingWise: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Living a healthy life allows us to truly enjoy more aspects of our existence. It's a way of being that significantly lowers the chances of developing serious illnesses or facing premature health issues, encompassing not just physical well-being but also our social and mental health.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn the fundamental principles of Ayurveda and how to apply them for a healthier lifestyle.
*   Gain insights into concepts like Tridosha, Dhatus, and Prakriti, understanding your unique body constitution.
*   Explore mindful eating, traditional Ayurvedic practices, and the role of herbs and spices.
*   Benefit from a self-paced, online learning experience with comprehensive resources and a certificate of completion.

## Course Overview

This online certificate program, "Ayurveda for Healthy Life," is designed to introduce you to the core wisdom of Ayurveda and guide you in integrating its principles into your daily routine. Developed by Dr. Pragya Gupta, an experienced Ayurveda Doctor, the course aims to demystify ancient Ayurvedic concepts and make them actionable for modern living.

The program includes approximately 5 hours of video lessons, supplemented by 4 articles, 3 downloadable resources, and quizzes to reinforce your learning. It covers a wide range of topics, from understanding your body's constitution (Prakriti) to the importance of mindful eating, meditation, and specific Ayurvedic practices.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing the "Ayurveda for Healthy Life" online classes, you will achieve a thorough understanding of both basic and advanced Ayurvedic principles for maintaining a healthy life. The curriculum delves into foundational concepts such as Tridosha (Vata, Pitta, Kapha), Dhatus (body tissues), Agni (digestive fire), and Malas (waste products), alongside the crucial concept of Prakriti, or individual body constitution.

You will learn about the significance of mindful eating for a robust immune system and optimal digestion, and explore various Ayurvedic herbs, spices, and tastes. The course also introduces practical techniques like oil pulling and tongue scraping, and highlights the importance of Pranayama (breathing exercises) and meditation for overall well-being.

## Program Structure and Content

The course is structured into several modules, guiding you from an introduction to Ayurveda through detailed explorations of its core concepts:

### Introduction

*   An overview of what you will gain from the course.
*   Introduction to your instructor.
*   The foundational five basic elements (Panchamahabhutas).
*   An introduction to the three Doshas.

### Understanding the Three Doshas (Biological Energies)

*   Detailed study of Vata, Pitta, and Kapha Doshas.
*   Understanding the three states of Doshas.
*   Quizzes and practical life hacks related to Doshas.

### Dosha Imbalance & Prakriti

*   Identifying imbalances in Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.
*   The importance of Prakriti analysis and how to calculate your own body constitution.
*   Tailored tips for Vata, Pitta, and Kapha personalities.

### Agni (The Digestive Fire)

*   Understanding the critical role of Agni in digestion.
*   Exploring the 13 types and 4 states of Agni.
*   Strategies to balance Agni, including allergy precautions and Ayurvedic recipes for digestion.

### Mindful Eating, Dhatus, Malas, and Immunity

*   The significance of mindful eating with practical tips.
*   An overview of the 7 types of Dhatus (tissues) and 3 types of Malas (toxins).
*   Ayurvedic approaches to boosting immunity, including food, hygiene tips, and yoga practices.
*   Introduction to Pranayama techniques.

### Dinacharya (Daily Regimen) & Ritucharya (Seasonal Regimen)

*   Developing a daily routine based on Ayurvedic principles.
*   Practical daily regimen checklists, including oil pulling and tongue scraping.
*   Understanding the six seasons in Ayurveda and how they relate to Doshas.
*   Implementing seasonal regimens (Ritucharya).

The "Ayurveda for Healthy Life" certificate course is offered at a current fee of ₹449, a significant reduction from its original price. Upon successful completion of the course, you will receive a certificate of completion from the platform, confirming your understanding of Ayurvedic principles. This self-paced online course, delivered in English through video and text, provides a flexible and comprehensive way to embark on your journey toward a healthier, more balanced life with Ayurveda.
