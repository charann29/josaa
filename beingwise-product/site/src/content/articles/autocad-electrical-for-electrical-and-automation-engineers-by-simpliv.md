# AutoCAD Electrical for Electrical and Automation Engineers by Simpliv Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring and current electrical and automation engineers, mastering industry-standard software is crucial. This program, offered by Simpliv Learning, focuses on AutoCAD Electrical, a specialized tool for designing electrical control systems.

## Key takeaways

*   The course is delivered in English and offers both self-study and virtual classroom options.
*   It covers essential skills for creating and managing electrical drawings and projects.
*   Participants receive a certificate upon successful completion from Simpliv Learning.
*   The program is currently available at a discounted fee of $349.

## What You Will Learn: Course Syllabus Highlights

The curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of AutoCAD Electrical, equipping students with practical skills for real-world applications. Here's a glimpse into the key modules:

*   **Project File Management:** Learn to understand and effectively manage project files within AutoCAD Electrical.
*   **Schematic and Panel Drawing Creation:** Gain expertise in creating, modifying, and refining both schematic and panel drawings.
*   **Working with PLC Symbols:** Understand how to integrate and utilize Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) symbols in your designs.
*   **Custom Symbol Development:** Discover how to create and implement your own custom symbols to streamline design processes.
*   **Report Generation:** Master the skill of generating various reports from your AutoCAD Electrical projects.

## Learning Format and Medium

This program emphasizes flexibility and accessibility for learners. It is conducted entirely in English, ensuring a clear understanding of technical concepts. Students have the choice between a self-study approach, allowing them to learn at their own pace, or a virtual classroom environment for more interactive engagement. The content itself is delivered through a combination of video and text-based materials.

## Course Fees and Certification

The investment for this specialized training is currently set at a discounted rate. While the original fee for the AutoCAD Electrical course is $583, it is presently offered for $349. Upon successful completion of the program, participants will be awarded a certificate by Simpliv Learning, validating their acquired skills in AutoCAD Electrical.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the AutoCAD Electrical for Electrical and Automation Engineers program, prospective students should visit the official Simpliv Learning platform. The application process typically involves selecting the course, registering an account if you don't already have one, and completing the payment for the discounted fee. Specific instructions and any prerequisites will be detailed on their official course page.

This course offers a valuable opportunity for engineers to enhance their skillset with an essential industry tool.
