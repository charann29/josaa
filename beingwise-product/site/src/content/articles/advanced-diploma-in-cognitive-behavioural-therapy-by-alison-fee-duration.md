# Advanced Diploma in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy by Alison: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) is a widely recognised and effective approach in mental health, helping individuals understand and manage their thoughts and behaviours. This advanced diploma from Alison offers an accessible way to learn about CBT principles and their practical applications.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is delivered online, primarily through video lectures, allowing for flexible learning.
*   While the course content is free, a certificate of completion is available from Alison for a fee.
*   The curriculum covers both the foundational theories of CBT and its application in treating various conditions like depression, anxiety, and addiction.
*   It's a valuable stepping stone for those interested in mental health support roles or enhancing their understanding of therapeutic techniques.

## Understanding the Course: What is CBT?

This advanced diploma is designed to introduce learners to the core concepts of Cognitive Behavioural Therapy. CBT is a goal-oriented psychotherapy treatment that takes a hands-on, practical approach to problem-solving. It aims to change patterns of thinking or behaviour that are behind people's difficulties, and so change the way they feel. The course helps you grasp how CBT works and its significance in modern psychological practice.

## Course Structure and Content

The diploma is divided into distinct modules, guiding learners through a comprehensive understanding of CBT. The delivery method is entirely video-based, making it convenient for those who prefer visual learning.

### Module 1: Fundamentals of Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

This introductory module lays the groundwork, covering essential topics such as:

*   Defining CBT and its core principles.
*   Exploring behavioural neuroscience relevant to CBT.
*   An introduction to Acceptance Commitment Therapy (ACT).
*   Understanding the structured approach of CBT therapy sessions.

### Module 2: Applications of Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Building on the fundamentals, this module delves into how CBT is practically applied in various real-world scenarios. Topics include:

*   Using CBT in the treatment of eating disorders.
*   Applying CBT techniques for depression, stress, and anxiety.
*   How CBT can improve interpersonal relationships.
*   The role of CBT in treating addiction.
*   Insights into working as a CBT therapist.

### Module 3: Course Assessment

The final module involves a course assessment designed to evaluate your understanding and retention of the material covered throughout the diploma. Successful completion of this assessment is necessary to finish the program.

## Fees and Certification

One of the notable aspects of this program is that the learning content itself is free of charge. This means you can access all the video lectures and study materials without any upfront cost. However, if you wish to receive an official certificate upon completion, there will be a fee payable directly to Alison, the certificate-providing authority. This allows learners to choose whether they need formal recognition for their studies.

## How to Apply

As an online, self-paced course, there isn't a traditional application process with deadlines or entrance exams. To begin, you typically need to visit the official Alison platform, register for an account, and then enroll in the "Advanced Diploma in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy" course. You can start learning immediately after enrollment.

This diploma offers a solid introduction to the principles and applications of Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, making it an excellent choice for anyone looking to expand their knowledge in this vital field.
