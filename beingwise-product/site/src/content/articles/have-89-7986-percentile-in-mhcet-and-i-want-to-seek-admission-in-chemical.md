## Understanding Your MHT CET Rank

Your MHT CET rank is a crucial piece of information for the admission process. While you have your percentile, the rank provides your specific position among all test-takers. To find your rank, you need to access your official MHT CET scorecard.

Historically, MHT CET results, including scorecards, are made available on the official MHT CET portal. You would typically navigate to the 'Important Links' section and look for an option to check your 'PCM Score Card' or similar. You will then need to enter your application number and date of birth, complete any security verification (like ticking a checkbox), and log in to view and download your detailed scorecard, which will include your rank.

## The Centralized Admission Process (CAP)

Admission to most undergraduate engineering courses in Maharashtra, including Chemical Engineering, is conducted through the Centralized Admission Process (CAP) rounds, managed by the State Common Entrance Test Cell (CET Cell), Maharashtra. This process involves online registration, document verification, choice filling (where you list your preferred colleges and courses), and seat allotment rounds.

During the CAP rounds, seats are allotted based on your MHT CET rank, the preferences you've submitted, and the availability of seats in various categories. It's vital to stay updated with the official CAP schedule and instructions released by the CET Cell to ensure you don't miss any deadlines.

Keep a close eye on the official MHT CET and CET Cell Maharashtra websites for all announcements regarding results, scorecards, ranks, and the CAP rounds.
