## How to Download Your SRCC GBO 2026 Admit Card

Accessing your admit card is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to download your hall ticket from the official SRCC portal:

1.  Visit the official website for SRCC.
2.  Look for the 'PGDBO Admit Card 2026' link on the homepage and click on it.
3.  You will be redirected to a login page.
4.  Enter your application number and password in the designated fields.
5.  Click on the 'Login' button.
6.  Your SRCC GBO 2026 admit card will be displayed on the screen.
7.  It is strongly advised to download and print a physical copy of your admit card, as no hard copies will be dispatched by post.

## Details on Your SRCC GBO Admit Card

Your admit card is more than just an entry pass; it's a personalized document containing vital information about you and your examination. Please verify all details carefully. The admit card will typically include:

*   Your name and roll number
*   Your date of birth
*   Your father's name
*   Complete details of your assigned test center
*   The specific date and time of your examination
*   Your photograph
*   Your signature

It is crucial to ensure all information is accurate. In case of any discrepancies, contact the examination authorities immediately.

## Documents to Carry to the Exam Center

On the day of the SRCC GBO exam, beyond your admit card, you must also bring a valid photo identification. Acceptable forms of ID include:

*   Aadhaar Card
*   Passport
*   Driving License
*   PAN Card
*   Ration Card

## SRCC GBO Exam Day Guidelines

To ensure a smooth examination experience, candidates must adhere to certain guidelines:

*   Arrive at the examination center at the reporting time specified on your admit card.
*   Remember to carry a printed copy of your SRCC GBO admit card, a valid photo ID, and a few passport-sized photographs.
*   Candidates with disabilities (PwD) should bring all necessary certificates and documents as required by the examination authorities.
*   Bring a blue or black ballpoint pen for any required signing, such as on the attendance sheet.
*   It is advisable to carry personal drinking water, hand sanitizer, a face mask, and gloves for health and safety.
*   Cooperate fully with the staff at the test center and follow all instructions provided during entry and throughout the exam.
*   Ensure you write your name and roll number on any rough sheets provided by the invigilator and sign them.

Following these instructions will help you have a hassle-free exam day. Good luck with your SRCC GBO 2026 examination!
