# Business of Jewellery by De Beers Forevermark by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online certification course, offered by The Designer's Class in collaboration with De Beers Forevermark, provides a foundational understanding of the jewellery business. It's designed for anyone keen to explore the intricacies of this fascinating industry, from design to retail.

## Key takeaways

*   A beginner-friendly online course covering the business side of jewellery.
*   Features 48 hours of learning content across 16 distinct lessons.
*   Successful completion leads to a certificate from The Designer's Class.
*   Offers opportunities for internships for qualifying candidates after the course.

## Course Overview and Structure

The "Business of Jewellery" course is an online, self-paced program delivered through video and text-based modules, all in English. Taught by Sachin Jain, the curriculum delves into the operational aspects of a jewellery business, exploring various business models and the critical elements of developing a strong brand. It spans 16 lessons, amounting to a comprehensive 48 hours of study material.

Upon purchasing the course, students gain one-year access to all content, including reading materials and assignments. The program culminates in a final assessment, which must be passed to earn the official certificate.

## What You Will Learn

This course is structured to provide a holistic view of the jewellery business. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Omnichannel Retail:** Understanding how to build a retail brand that seamlessly integrates online and offline experiences.
*   **Market Trends:** Gaining insights into current trends in diamond purchasing and consumer behaviour.
*   **Branding and Visual Resonance:** Learning the importance of brand identity and visual appeal in the jewellery sector.
*   **Sales and Marketing:** Developing effective strategies for promoting and selling jewellery products.
*   **Inventory and Merchandise Planning:** Mastering the crucial role of inventory management and merchandise planning for business efficiency.

## Who Is This Course For?

This certification is ideal for a broad range of individuals interested in the jewellery industry. It's perfect for aspiring entrepreneurs looking to start a jewellery business, as well as professionals already working in the field who wish to enhance their business acumen. Specific roles that would benefit include:

*   Jewellery Designers
*   Accessory Designers
*   Brand Managers
*   Marketing Managers
*   Customer Service Managers
*   Consultants in the jewellery sector

Essentially, anyone with an interest in the business development and operational aspects of the jewellery industry will find this course valuable.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the "Business of Jewellery" certification course is ₹2,999. This single payment grants students full access to all course materials for one year. Upon successfully completing the course and passing the final assessment, students will receive a certificate issued by The Designer's Class.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the "Business of Jewellery" course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official course page on The Designer's Class website.
2.  Locate the "Buy now" option and click on it to add the course to your cart.
3.  Proceed with the payment process to complete your purchase.

Once purchased, you will receive immediate access to the course content and can begin your learning journey.

This course offers a fantastic opportunity to dive into the commercial side of the glamorous jewellery world, equipping you with practical knowledge and skills.
