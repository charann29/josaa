# Drafting & Construction- Womenswear by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program offers an accessible introduction to the fundamentals of designing and creating women's apparel, guided by an established figure in the fashion industry. It's designed for aspiring designers and enthusiasts eager to learn the practical skills behind garment construction.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn essential pattern drafting and garment construction techniques for womenswear.
*   The course is self-paced, delivered through video and text, and available in both English and Hindi.
*   Developed and taught by renowned fashion designer Payal Singhal.
*   Includes downloadable resources and assignments to enhance practical learning.

## Course Overview and What You Will Learn

This online certification in Drafting & Construction- Womenswear is tailored for beginners, providing a thorough understanding of design, fit, and style in creating women's garments. Over approximately 15 hours of instructional content, you'll delve into various aspects, from foundational pattern making to advanced construction techniques.

By the end of the program, participants are expected to possess a strong grasp of creating a garment from its initial concept. Key learning areas include:

*   Understanding garments and human anatomy in relation to design.
*   Mastering drafting and pattern-making principles.
*   Developing hand construction techniques.
*   Drafting and constructing both unstructured and structured garments.

## Program Structure and Content

The course is structured to provide a comprehensive learning experience, blending theoretical knowledge with practical application. It covers several modules, starting with an introduction to the designer and fundamental concepts, progressing to more advanced construction methods.

Key topics in the syllabus include:

*   Introduction to Garments and Human Anatomy
*   Introduction to Drafting & Patternmaking
*   Introduction to Garment Construction
*   Hand Construction Techniques
*   Drafting & Construction of Un-Structured Garments
*   Drafting & Construction of Structured Garments
*   A final assessment to evaluate understanding.

The program offers approximately 5 hours and 16 minutes of core learning content, supplemented by 13 downloadable resources and 7 assignments. Participants will have access to these resources for one year.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for individuals aspiring to become fashion designers, students keen on understanding garment creation, or anyone looking to enhance their practical knowledge in womenswear design. It's a beginner-level course, making it suitable even for those with limited prior experience in fashion design.

## Fees and How to Apply

The fee for the Drafting & Construction- Womenswear online course is ₹2,499. Enrollment typically involves making the payment directly through The Designer's Class platform. Further details on the application process would be available on their official portal.

## Instructor: Payal Singhal

The course is developed and tutored by Ms. Payal Singhal, the founder of the Payal Singhal fashion design house, established in 1999. Her expertise and industry insights provide learners with valuable tips and tricks for creating outstanding womenswear garments and mastering fundamental pattern drafting concepts.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to gain practical skills and insights into womenswear design from an industry expert, setting a strong foundation for a career in fashion.
