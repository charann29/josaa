# Diploma in Graphics and 2D Animation by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Are you keen to explore the dynamic world of graphics and 2D animation? The Boston Institute of Analytics (BIA) offers a comprehensive diploma program designed to equip you with the essential skills for this exciting field.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 6-month diploma program focusing on graphics, animation, and gaming.
*   It includes 100 hours of coursework plus a 2-month internship opportunity.
*   Learning modes include both virtual and physical classroom options, with video and text-based delivery.
*   The program leads to dual certification from the Boston Institute of Analytics.

## Course Overview

The Diploma in Graphics & 2D Animation is a six-month intensive program created to give students the necessary expertise to thrive in the animation industry. It emphasizes visual storytelling and provides a well-rounded education by covering various aspects of graphics, animation, and gaming. The curriculum includes hands-on training with industry-standard software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Maya, Unity, and Unreal Engine.

Students will delve into fundamental tools and techniques, including image editing, retouching, typography, motion graphics, 3D modeling, lighting, rendering, and game design basics. By the end of the course, participants will have developed a robust portfolio showcasing their creative and technical abilities. This program is suitable for both beginners entering the field and professionals looking to enhance their existing skill set.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this diploma, you will gain proficiency in a range of essential graphics and animation tools and techniques. Key learning areas include:

*   **Adobe Photoshop:** Mastering image editing, retouching, photo manipulation, and designing graphics and layouts.
*   **Adobe Illustrator:** Understanding vector graphics, working with colors and gradients, typography, text effects, layers, and masks.
*   **Adobe After Effects:** Learning animation principles, working with keyframes, and motion graphics.
*   **Maya & 3D Max:** Exploring 3D modeling, texturing, shading, lighting, and rendering techniques.

## Eligibility and Admissions Process

### Eligibility Criteria

Candidates from any academic background – Arts, Science, Commerce, or Engineering – are welcome to apply for this diploma program. There are no specific prior qualifications required beyond a general academic stream.

### How to Apply

To enroll in the Diploma in Graphics & 2D Animation program, follow these straightforward steps:

1.  Visit the official Boston Institute of Analytics website.
2.  Locate and select the "Enquire Now" option. Fill out the inquiry form with your contact details. A representative from BIA will then get in touch with you.
3.  Complete the course fee payment to gain access to the program modules and begin your learning journey.

## Program Fees and Certification

The Boston Institute of Analytics issues dual certifications upon successful completion of this program. While specific fee details are best confirmed directly with BIA during the inquiry process, the program is structured to provide significant value over its six-month duration, including a two-month internship component.

This diploma offers a practical, hands-on learning experience through live lectures, often conducted on weekends, making it accessible for a wider range of students.

## Who Is This Program For?

This program is ideal for anyone interested in a career in the video and animation industry. Specifically, it is highly beneficial for aspiring:

*   Animators
*   Multimedia Animators
*   Video Game Designers
*   Film and Video Editors

Embark on your creative journey with the Diploma in Graphics & 2D Animation and unlock new career possibilities in a rapidly growing industry.
