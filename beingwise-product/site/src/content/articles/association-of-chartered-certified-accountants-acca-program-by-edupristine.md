# Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) Program by Edupristine: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aspiring to build a strong career in accounting and finance, the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) program offers a globally recognised qualification. This program, offered through Edupristine, aims to equip students with practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge.

## Key takeaways

*   The program offers flexible learning options, including self-study and virtual classrooms, with classes typically held on weekends.
*   It focuses on practical, experience-based training guided by seasoned industry professionals.
*   Successful candidates can gain an edge with top employers in India, such as KPMG and Accenture.
*   Eligibility generally requires a 10+2 qualification with specific marks in key subjects, alongside potential work experience requirements.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

The ACCA program by Edupristine is designed to enhance your accounting career prospects and make you stand out to major employers in India. It adopts a practice-oriented approach, providing experiential training under the mentorship of industry experts. The curriculum includes extensive hands-on experience and real-life scenarios, helping candidates grasp and apply the practical aspects of daily accounting tasks.

Beyond technical skills, the program also incorporates soft skills training, offering a clearer understanding of the intersection between business and finance. You'll hone essential professional abilities like negotiation and presentation, alongside developing critical decision-making and problem-solving skills through real-world challenges.

## What the Program Offers

This ACCA preparation program comes with comprehensive resources and support to help you succeed. Key features include:

*   **Exam-focused Content:** Learning materials are specifically designed to prepare you for the ACCA examinations.
*   **Expert Instruction:** Training is delivered by professionals with significant industry experience.
*   **Interactive Learning:** Benefit from dedicated discussion forums and task-based simulations.
*   **Exam Preparation:** Access a mock exam environment, Kaplan exam kits, and extensive learning content by Kaplan Inc.
*   **Career Support:** Receive customised career guidance, assistance with resume preparation, and workshops on interview skills.
*   **Flexible Learning:** Choose from live virtual training, self-study packages, or classroom training options.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants Program by Edupristine, candidates must meet specific academic requirements:

*   **Educational Qualification:** You should have successfully completed your 10+2 examinations.
*   **Academic Performance:** A minimum aggregate of 65% in Mathematics/Accounts and English is required, along with at least 50% in other subjects.
*   **Work Experience:** Additionally, candidates need to have a minimum of three years of relevant work experience to join the program. Please note that this is a common requirement for professional certifications.

To become a certified Accountant, candidates must pass the official ACCA course examinations, which are typically held annually in June and December.

## Who Should Consider This Program?

The Association of Chartered Certified Accountants Program by Edupristine is ideal for a diverse group of individuals looking to advance their careers in accounting and finance:

*   **Commerce Graduates:** Students currently pursuing degrees such as B.Com, BMS, or BBA.
*   **Arts or Science Graduates:** Those with a foundational understanding of accountancy.
*   **Aspiring Professionals:** Students preparing for competitive accounting examinations like CPT and IPCC.
*   **Existing Chartered Accountants:** Professionals seeking to enhance their credentials with a globally recognised qualification.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Edupristine Association of Chartered Certified Accountants Course involves a straightforward process:

1.  Begin by visiting the official Edupristine website.
2.  Navigate to the 'Accounts' section under 'Programs' and select the 'ACCA' option.
3.  On the ACCA program webpage, locate and click the "Apply Now" button.
4.  You will then be directed to a page displaying detailed information about the course, where you can complete your application.

## Program Syllabus Highlights

The ACCA syllabus is structured into different levels, covering a broad spectrum of accounting and business topics. Some key areas include:

### Knowledge Level

*   Business and Technology (BT)
*   Management Accounting (MA)
*   Financial Accounting (FA)

### Skills Level

*   Corporate and Business Law (LW)
*   Performance Management (PM)

The program's comprehensive syllabus ensures a thorough understanding of both foundational and advanced accounting principles, preparing candidates for the complexities of the global financial landscape.

This program offers a robust pathway for individuals aiming to achieve a distinguished qualification in the accounting field, supported by practical training and expert guidance.
