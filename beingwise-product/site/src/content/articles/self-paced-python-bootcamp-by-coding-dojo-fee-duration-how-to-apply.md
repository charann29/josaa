# Self-Paced Python Bootcamp by Coding Dojo: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to dive into the world of full-stack Python development at their own pace, Coding Dojo offers a comprehensive Self-Paced Python Bootcamp. This program is designed to equip learners with essential skills through a flexible online format, making it suitable for individuals balancing other commitments.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Flexible Learning:** The bootcamp is entirely self-paced, allowing students to learn on their own schedule without strict deadlines for video lectures.
*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** It covers full-stack Python development, including front-end and back-end concepts, database management, and web security.
*   **Expert Support:** Participants benefit from one-on-one instructor support and guidance throughout their learning journey.
*   **Certification:** Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate, validating their newly acquired skills.

## Course Overview

This virtual certification course focuses on full-stack Python development. The curriculum spans approximately 10 weeks of content, delivered through a blend of video lectures, interactive quizzes, and practical projects. While the core content is 10 weeks, students can choose between two payment plans that allow access for either two or four months, accommodating different learning speeds. The self-paced nature means there are no fixed timings for classes, offering complete flexibility.

## Program Offerings and What You Will Learn

The bootcamp provides a rich learning experience through its various components. Students will engage with video sessions, structured modules, practical projects, and quizzes designed to reinforce understanding. The content is delivered in English, utilizing a mix of video and text-based materials.

Key areas of learning include:

*   **Python Fundamentals:** Grasping the basics of Python programming.
*   **Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) in Python:** Understanding core OOP principles.
*   **Web Development Concepts:** Learning about MVC frameworks, TDD (Test-Driven Development), authentication, and CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations.
*   **Database Management:** Insights into SQL and Entity-Relationship Diagrams (ERD).
*   **Front-end Technologies:** How to build custom JavaScript libraries and handle AJAX requests.
*   **Web Security:** Basics of securing web applications.
*   **C# Concepts:** An introduction to OOP in C#.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Self-Paced Python Bootcamp, candidates are expected to have prior work experience as a software developer. This prerequisite ensures that participants have a foundational understanding of software development principles, allowing them to benefit most from the advanced topics covered in the bootcamp.

## Course Fees and Duration

Coding Dojo offers two distinct plans for this bootcamp, each with a different fee structure and access duration:

| Plan Duration | Course Fee (USD) |
| :------------ | :--------------- |
| 2 Months      | $1,950           |
| 4 Months      | $3,850           |

Both plans provide access to the same 10 weeks of core curriculum content. The choice between the 2-month and 4-month plan depends on how much time a student anticipates needing to complete the material at their own pace. A certificate of completion is awarded to students who successfully finish the course requirements.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Self-Paced Python Bootcamp is a straightforward online process:

1.  **Visit the Official Course Webpage:** Navigate to the dedicated page for this bootcamp.
2.  **Click 'Enroll Now':** Locate and select the enrollment option.
3.  **Complete the Application Form:** Fill in all required personal and professional details accurately.
4.  **Make Payment:** Submit the applicable course fee based on your chosen plan (2-month or 4-month).
5.  **Join the Course:** Once payment is processed, you will gain access to the course materials and can begin your learning journey.

This bootcamp is an excellent opportunity for developers to enhance their skills and gain a strong footing in full-stack Python, all while managing their personal and professional commitments effectively.
