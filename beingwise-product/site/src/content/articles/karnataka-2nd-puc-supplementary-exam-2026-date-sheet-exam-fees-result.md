The Karnataka School Examination and Assessment Board (KSEAB) conducts supplementary examinations for students who wish to improve their scores or clear subjects in the 2nd PUC exams. This second examination, often referred to as Exam 2, provides a crucial opportunity for students to achieve their academic goals. This page outlines the key details for the 2026 supplementary exams, including important dates, fees, and result checking procedures.

## Key takeaways

*   The Karnataka 2nd PUC Supplementary Exam 2026 will be held from April 30 to May 13, 2026.
*   The official date sheet has been released by KSEAB on their official website.
*   Students can apply for re-evaluation of their answer sheets even if they scored low marks, by paying the specified fee.
*   Results for the main 2nd PUC exam were declared on April 9, 2026.

## How to Download the Karnataka 2nd PUC Supplementary Time Table 2026

Accessing the official timetable is straightforward. Students can follow these general steps to download the schedule from the official KSEAB portal:

1.  Visit the official website of the Karnataka School Examination and Assessment Board (KSEAB).
2.  Look for the 'Latest News' or 'Notifications' section on the homepage.
3.  Find the link specifically for the '2nd PUC Supplementary Exam 2026 Time Table' or 'Exam 2 Time Table'.
4.  Click on the link to view or download the PDF document.
5.  It is recommended to save a copy of the timetable for future reference.

Information regarding the fee payment dates for the 2nd PUC Supplementary Exam 2026 will be announced by KSEAB. ## Karnataka 2nd PUC Exam 2 Result 2026: How to Check

After the supplementary exams conclude, KSEAB will process and release the results. The results for the main 2nd PUC examination 2026 were declared on April 9, 2026, at 3 PM, on the official KSEAB results website, karresults.nic.in. Students can expect the supplementary results to be released similarly. To check your results, you will typically need to enter your registration number on the official results portal.
