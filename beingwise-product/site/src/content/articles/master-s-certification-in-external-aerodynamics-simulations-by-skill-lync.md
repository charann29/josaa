# Master's Certification in External Aerodynamics Simulations by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring and current engineers in the fields of aerospace, mechanical, and automotive engineering, the Master's Certification in External Aerodynamics Simulations by Skill Lync offers an intensive program to master crucial simulation techniques. This certification aims to equip participants with the practical skills needed for advanced aerodynamics and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) applications in the industry.

## Key takeaways

*   **Industry-Focused Curriculum:** The program is meticulously designed by industry experts, emphasizing tools and techniques vital for an aerodynamics engineer.
*   **Hands-on Project Experience:** Learning is heavily project-based, including industry-grade projects and practical exercises to build real-world simulation expertise.
*   **Comprehensive Skill Development:** Participants will gain proficiency in leading CFD platforms like ANSYS Fluent, StarCCM+, OpenFOAM, and Converge CFD.
*   **Dedicated Support and Community:** Enrolled students benefit from dedicated support and a community forum for collaborative learning and problem-solving.

## Course Overview

This six-month Master's Certification focuses on the core principles and platforms used in aerodynamics and CFD. It delves into complex CFD features such as free surface modeling and flexible solving, ensuring participants can implement external aerodynamics simulations at an industry-standard level. The curriculum combines video lectures with numerous in-course assignments and exercises to reinforce learning.

Upon successful completion, Skill Lync awards a course completion certificate. Additionally, high-achieving students, specifically those ranking in the top 5% of their batch, will also receive a merit certificate.

### Learning Format

The program is delivered in English, primarily through video and text-based materials, supporting a self-study learning mode. This flexible approach allows participants to learn at their own pace while still engaging with a structured curriculum.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in this Master's Certification, candidates should either be practicing mechanical, aerospace, or automotive engineers, or currently pursuing a degree in one of these disciplines. The program is specifically tailored for:

*   Aerospace Engineers
*   CFD Professionals
*   Mechanical Engineers
*   Automotive Engineers

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing all modules of the training program, participants will develop an extensive skill set, including:

*   The ability to perform realistic aerodynamics simulations applicable to real-life industry problems.
*   In-depth knowledge of how leading aerodynamic simulation platforms like ANSYS Fluent, StarCCM+, OpenFOAM, and Converge CFD operate.
*   A foundational understanding of the prerequisites for conducting aerodynamics simulations in CFD.
*   A strong mathematical grasp of CFD principles, including proficiency in equations like the Navier-Stokes equation, the Energy equation, and the Axis-symmetric Formulation of the Navier-Stokes equation.

## Program Fees and Duration

The Master's Certification in External Aerodynamics Simulations is a six-month program. The fees are structured as follows:

| Head          | Amount (INR) |
| :------------ | :----------- |
| Programme fees | ₹2,75,000    |
| EMI (per month) | ₹14,375      |

Skill Lync is the certifying authority for this program, providing certificates upon successful completion of the training curriculum, assignments, and projects. As mentioned, a merit certificate is also available for top performers.

## How to Apply

To apply for the Master’s Certification in External Aerodynamics Simulations, follow these simple steps:

1.  Visit the official course webpage for the Master’s Certification in External Aerodynamics Simulations.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Enroll Now' option.
3.  Choose the payment plan that best suits your preferences and proceed with the registration.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to enhance your expertise in a critical engineering domain, preparing you for advanced roles in the industry.
