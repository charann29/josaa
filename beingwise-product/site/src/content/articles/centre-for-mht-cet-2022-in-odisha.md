# Is there any centre for MHT CET 2022 in Odisha?

Many students from outside Maharashtra consider taking the MHT CET for admission to engineering, pharmacy, and agriculture courses within the state. A common question is whether exam centers are available in their home state. Let's clarify the situation for Odisha.

## Key takeaways

*   No MHT CET 2022 exam centers were located in Odisha.
*   The MHT CET is a state-level entrance exam primarily conducted within Maharashtra.
*   However, exam centers were available in several other states across India.
*   Students from Odisha wishing to take the MHT CET had to travel to a designated center in one of these listed states.

## MHT CET: A Maharashtra State Exam

The MHT CET (Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test) is specifically designed for admissions to various undergraduate professional courses in Maharashtra. It is administered by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra. While its primary focus is on students from Maharashtra, it also attracts applicants from other parts of India who are interested in pursuing higher education in the state.

## Exam Center Availability Outside Maharashtra

Although the MHT CET is a Maharashtra-centric exam, the authorities understand that many prospective students reside outside the state. To accommodate these candidates, exam centers were indeed established in a select few states beyond Maharashtra. This arrangement aimed to make the examination more accessible for a wider pool of applicants.

## States with MHT CET Exam Centers (2022)

For the MHT CET 2022, in addition to the numerous centers spread across all 36 districts of Maharashtra, test centers were also available in the following states:

*   Bihar
*   Chhattisgarh
*   Delhi-NCR
*   Gujarat
*   Jharkhand
*   Karnataka
*   Madhya Pradesh
*   Rajasthan
*   Telangana
*   Uttar Pradesh

## No MHT CET Centers in Odisha

Unfortunately, for students residing in Odisha, the state was not included in the list of locations where MHT CET 2022 exam centers were established. This means that any candidate from Odisha who wished to appear for the MHT CET had to make arrangements to travel to one of the aforementioned states to take the examination. It's always advisable to check the official MHT CET information bulletin for the most current and precise details regarding exam center locations for any given year.

We hope this clarifies your query regarding MHT CET 2022 centers in Odisha.
