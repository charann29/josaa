# Yandex Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Yandex Courses

Yandex, a leading technology company, offers a range of online courses and certifications designed to equip learners with essential skills in high-demand fields like Big Data and Machine Learning. These programs are often delivered through major online learning platforms, making advanced tech education accessible to a global audience.

## Key Takeaways

*   Yandex provides specialized online courses, primarily focused on Big Data and Machine Learning.
*   Many of these programs are available for free, offering a valuable opportunity for skill development.
*   Courses cover practical applications of technologies like HDFS, MapReduce, Spark, Hive, and advanced ML concepts.
*   They are structured with practical assignments and real-world problem-solving scenarios.

## Dive into Big Data Essentials

For those aiming to build a career in the rapidly expanding Big Data industry, the "Big Data Essentials: HDFS, MapReduce and Spark RDD" certificate course is an excellent starting point. This program introduces fundamental Big Data technologies such as HDFS (Hadoop Distributed File System), MapReduce, and Spark RDD (Resilient Distributed Datasets).

Over six weeks, learners delve into the core concepts, understanding the purpose and functionality of distributed file systems. The curriculum emphasizes building a strong foundational understanding of Big Data technologies and their practical applications in various sectors like social networks, finance, and telecommunications. A key feature of this course is the opportunity to work on relevant assignments and evaluate solutions on a real cluster, providing hands-on experience. Basic knowledge of Python and Apache Spark is beneficial for this intermediate-level course.

## Mastering Big Data Analysis Tools

In today's data-driven world, the ability to analyze vast amounts of structured and unstructured data is crucial for informed decision-making across organizations. The "Big Data Analysis: Hive, Spark SQL, DataFrames and GraphFrames" online program focuses on equipping professionals with the tools needed for effective Big Data analysis.

This six-week course, designed for expert-level learners, covers essential analytical tools such as Hive, Spark SQL, DataFrames, and GraphFrames. Participants will gain practical skills in using these technologies to efficiently process and interpret large datasets, enabling them to contribute to optimizing business processes and making strategic decisions. Prior familiarity with Apache Spark is helpful for those undertaking this program.

## Applying Machine Learning at Scale

Machine Learning (ML) is at the forefront of technological innovation, transforming how problems are solved across industries. The "Big Data Applications: Machine Learning at Scale" certification program offers an advanced exploration of ML concepts and their large-scale implementation.

This five-week course by Yandex focuses on understanding how real-life challenges can be addressed using ML. Learners will develop skills in building ML models through practical assignments and even learn to create their own recommender systems. The program incorporates extensive reading materials, practice exercises, quizzes, and video lessons from experienced instructors to provide a comprehensive learning experience.
