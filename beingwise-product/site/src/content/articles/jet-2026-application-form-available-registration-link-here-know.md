# JET 2026 Application Form (Available): Registration Link Here, Know How to Apply, Fees

The application process for the Jain Entrance Test (JET) 2026 is currently open, offering a pathway to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Prospective students can register for the exam by completing the university's online application form.

## Key takeaways

*   The JET 2026 application window is active for the April session across multiple courses.
*   Applicants must create a unique login ID to access and fill out the online application form.
*   The registration deadline for MBA programs is March 31, 2026.
*   Ensure all details are accurate before final submission, as correction options are not available.

## How to Fill JET Application Form 2026

To successfully apply for the JET 2026 exam, follow these steps:

1.  **Register:** Begin by registering on the official portal using a valid email address and mobile number. This will be your primary contact for all communications.
2.  **Initial Details:** Provide essential personal information such as your full name, date of birth, gender, and state of residence.
3.  **Email Verification:** A verification email will be dispatched to your registered email ID. Click the link within to confirm your account.
4.  **Application Access:** After successful verification, you will be redirected to the main application form.
5.  **Personal Information:** Fill in all required personal details accurately.
6.  **Test Centre Selection:** Choose your preferred test centre for the JET exam from the available options.
7.  **Document Upload:** Upload a recent passport-sized photograph and your 10th-class mark sheet as specified. Ensure they meet the required format and dimensions.
8.  **Submit:** Review all entered information carefully before clicking the submit button.

## JET 2026 Application Fee Payment

The application fee for JET 2026 is INR 600. Please note the following points regarding fee payment:

*   The fee can be paid conveniently using a debit card, credit card, or net banking.
*   It is crucial to ensure the payment is completed successfully before closing the webpage. If the fee is not processed, your application will not be considered complete.
*   The application fee is non-refundable.
*   If you encounter any issues, such as payment being deducted but no acknowledgment generated, contact the application help desk immediately.

## Important Considerations for JET 2026 Application

When filling out your JET 2026 application form, keep these critical points in mind:

*   **Accuracy:** Provide valid and correct details, as all information will be verified during the admission process.
*   **No Corrections:** There is no provision for correcting the application form once submitted. Double-check everything before final submission.
*   **Completeness:** Incomplete forms or those with errors are subject to rejection.
*   **Review Before Payment:** Once the application fee is paid, you cannot edit the form. A thorough review before payment is essential.
*   **Errors After Submission:** If an error is discovered after submission and payment, you will need to re-register and pay the fee again to submit a new, corrected application.
*   **Document Specifications:** Ensure all uploaded documents, such as photographs and mark sheets, adhere to the specified format and dimension guidelines.

Completing the JET 2026 application accurately and on time is a crucial step towards your academic goals.
