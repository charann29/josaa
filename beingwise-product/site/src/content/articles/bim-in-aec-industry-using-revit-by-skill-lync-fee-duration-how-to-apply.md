# BIM in AEC Industry using Revit by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aspiring to excel in civil engineering and architecture, understanding Building Information Modeling (BIM) is crucial in today's construction landscape. This program focuses on equipping you with essential Revit skills for the Architecture, Engineering, and Construction (AEC) sector.

## Key takeaways

*   A 3-month online certification designed to provide a strong foundation in BIM processes and Revit tools.
*   The curriculum covers architectural, structural, and MEP modeling, along with documentation and collaboration aspects.
*   Offers comprehensive support including expert-led sessions, project-based learning, and a professional portfolio.
*   Suitable for civil engineering and architecture students, diploma holders, and working professionals looking to upskill.

## Understanding the Program

This certification course, offered by Skill Lync, is structured to impart fundamental knowledge and practical application of BIM within the AEC industry, specifically using Revit software. It's an online, self-paced learning experience delivered through video and text-based modules, all in English. The program aims to bridge the gap between theoretical understanding and industry-specific expertise in handling digital models.

Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate. Additionally, a select 5% of learners demonstrating exceptional performance may be awarded a merit certificate.

## Who is this Program For?

This course is ideal for individuals with an interest in civil engineering and architecture, regardless of their current experience level with BIM. It caters to:

*   Students pursuing a Diploma in Civil Engineering or Architecture.
*   Undergraduates (B.Tech in Civil, B.Arch) and postgraduates (M.Tech).
*   Working professionals in civil engineering or architecture who wish to enhance their skills in BIM for the AEC industry.

The program is designed to accommodate both those with no prior knowledge and those with some existing familiarity with BIM concepts.

## Course Structure and Learning Outcomes

Over the three months, the curriculum guides learners through various aspects of BIM and Revit. Key areas of study include:

*   **BIM Fundamentals**: Understanding core terminology, uses, and implementation strategies within the AEC sector.
*   **Revit Interface and Architectural Modeling**: Getting familiar with the software interface, basic architectural tools, managing materials, families, and generating 3D views.
*   **Organizing and Documenting Models**: Learning about project browser organization, adding tags, annotations, dimensions, and creating draft and legend views.
*   **Advanced Modeling**: Exploring 3D BIM modeling, Revit structural tools, MEP components, and creating basic families.
*   **Collaboration and Automation**: Insights into Common Data Environments (CDEs) and modeling simple elements using Dynamo.

By the end of the program, participants are expected to have a solid grasp of BIM processes and practical proficiency in using Revit for architectural, structural, and MEP modeling, along with documentation.

## Fees and Support

The program fee for the BIM in AEC Industry using Revit course is Rs. 40,000. This fee provides access to all course materials and support services.

Skill Lync offers a robust support system for learners, which includes:

*   **Instructional Support**: Course videos, expert instructors, and personalized hands-on assistance from engineers.
*   **Interactive Sessions**: One-on-one and group Zoom support sessions.
*   **Communication Channels**: Email and telephone support, as well as a dedicated course-specific forum group.
*   **Practical Experience**: Industry-oriented projects, case studies, and the creation of a professional portfolio page.

These resources are designed to ensure learners receive comprehensive assistance throughout their journey.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the BIM in AEC Industry using Revit program is a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Official Page**: Navigate to the official Skill Lync website for the BIM in AEC Industry course.
2.  **Enroll Now**: Locate and click the "Enroll Now" option on the course page.
3.  **Complete Registration**: Choose a suitable payment package and provide your name, email ID, and phone number to gain access to the program.

This program presents a valuable opportunity to develop critical skills for a successful career in the evolving AEC industry.
