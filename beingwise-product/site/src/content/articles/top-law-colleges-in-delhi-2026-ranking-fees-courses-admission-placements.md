# Top Law Colleges in Delhi 2026: Ranking, Fees, Courses, Admission, Placements

Delhi is a prime destination for aspiring legal professionals, offering access to some of the nation's most prestigious law institutions. These colleges provide a robust academic environment, competitive admissions, and promising career opportunities.

## Key takeaways

*   Delhi is home to four prominent government law colleges, including NLU Delhi and Faculty of Law, Jamia Millia Islamia.
*   Admission to these institutions primarily relies on entrance exams like AILET, CLAT, IPU CET, and the JMI Entrance Exam.
*   Tuition fees vary significantly, with options ranging from under INR 1 lakh to over INR 5 lakh per year.
*   Graduates from these top colleges often secure attractive placements with leading law firms and corporate houses.

## Admissions and Eligibility for Law Programs in Delhi

To pursue a law degree in Delhi, candidates must meet specific academic criteria for different program levels:

*   **Undergraduate (UG) Programs:** Applicants generally need to have passed Class 10 and Class 12 from a recognized board, with a minimum aggregate score of 45%.
*   **Postgraduate (PG) Programs (LLB):** A bachelor's degree from a recognized university is typically required.
*   **LLM Programs:** Candidates must hold an LLB or an equivalent law degree from a recognized university.
*   **PhD Programs:** For doctoral studies (LLD), a valid postgraduate degree from a recognized university with at least 55% marks is usually mandatory. Admission also involves a Research Entrance Test conducted by the university, followed by an interview round.

## Entrance Exams for Top Law Colleges in Delhi

Admission to the best law colleges in Delhi is highly competitive and primarily through various national and university-specific entrance examinations. Key exams include:

*   **AILET (All India Law Entrance Test):** Conducted for admission to NLU Delhi.
*   **CLAT (Common Law Admission Test):** Used by many national law universities, though NLU Delhi conducts its own exam.
*   **IPU CET (Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University Common Entrance Test):** For admission to law programs offered by GGSIPU and its affiliated colleges.
*   **JMI Entrance Exam:** Specific to Jamia Millia Islamia for its law courses.

Candidates should consult the official websites of the respective examination bodies for precise schedules and application details.
