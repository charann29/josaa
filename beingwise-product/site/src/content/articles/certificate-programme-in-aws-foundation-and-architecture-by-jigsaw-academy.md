# Certificate Programme in AWS Foundation and Architecture by Jigsaw Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Cloud computing skills are in high demand, and this certificate program offers a focused pathway to understanding Amazon Web Services (AWS), a leading cloud platform. Designed for IT professionals, it combines theoretical knowledge with extensive practical experience.

## Key takeaways

*   The program spans two months and provides six months of access to learning materials.
*   It includes over 12 hours of live webinars and two mini-projects on the AWS platform.
*   Participants will complete more than 30 hands-on exercises and can earn an official AWS badge.
*   The course is delivered in English through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

This certificate program is specifically designed to train and certify individuals on Amazon Web Services (AWS), one of the most widely used cloud platforms globally. It stands out by offering hands-on lab experience for every task, ensuring a practical understanding of AWS operations.

Throughout the online course, students will engage in over 12 hours of live webinars that explain concepts and guide them through implementation using lab practice and assignments. The curriculum also features two mini-projects on the AWS platform, providing opportunities to apply learned skills. With more than 30 hands-on exercises, participants will gain real-time experience working in a cloud environment.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of the program, participants will develop a strong command of AWS technology. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Mastering the skills required to perform various tasks on the AWS Platform.
*   Gaining in-depth operational efficiency across different AWS services such as Redshift, Kinesis, Simple Storage Services (AWS S3), Simple Queue Service, and Dynamo DB.
*   Acquiring comprehensive knowledge that can aid in preparing for further certifications like AWS Certified Sysops Administrator and AWS Solution Architect – Associate.
*   Developing a fundamental understanding of both private and public cloud platforms.

## Eligibility Criteria

This program is tailored for IT professionals with a specific background and experience level. Applicants should have an IT background and possess between two to eight years of work experience, preferably in Infrastructure or Cloud Technology.

Professionals in roles such as IT support engineers, Linux or Windows administrators, developers, engineers, and those already working in cloud environments are particularly encouraged to apply.

## Program Offerings and Resources

The program provides a rich learning environment with various resources to support student success:

*   **Interactive Learning:** The curriculum emphasizes active participation and practical application.
*   **Industry Interaction:** Opportunities for engagement with industry experts through meetups and webinars.
*   **Live Online Classes:** Scheduled live sessions for direct instruction and Q&A.
*   **Access to AWS Platform:** Participants receive access to the AWS Educate job portal and the 'Classroom' platform feature from AWS Educate for projects and assignments.
*   **AWS Credits:** Students are granted USD 75 in credits on AWS Educate to perform hands-on tasks in public cloud environments.
*   **Support:** Continuous faculty and technical support are available throughout the program duration.

## Course Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Certificate Programme in AWS Foundation and Architecture is ₹40,000, plus applicable taxes. The detailed fee structure is as follows:

| Head                | Amount in INR |
| :------------------ | :------------ |
| Programme Fees      | 40,000        |
| GST (18%)           | 7,200         |
| **Amount Payable**  | **47,200**    |

Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a Certificate of Participation from Jigsaw Academy.

This program is an excellent opportunity for IT professionals to upskill and gain valuable expertise in the rapidly expanding field of cloud computing.
