### How to Access Your RRB Group D Result

Once declared, results for CEN No. 08/2024 will be available on the regional RRB portals. Here's a general guide to checking your result:

1.  Visit the official website of your respective regional RRB.
2.  Look for the link related to the RRB Group D result for CEN No. 08/2024.
3.  Clicking the link will typically open a PDF document containing the roll numbers of shortlisted candidates.
4.  You can use `Ctrl+F` (or your device's search function) to find your roll number within the PDF.
5.  Download and save the result PDF for future reference.

Along with the PDF list of qualified candidates, the RRB will also release zone-wise cutoff marks and individual scorecards. Candidates can download their scorecards by using their login credentials, which include their registration number and date of birth.

## Eligibility for RRB Group D

To be eligible for the RRB Group D examination, candidates must meet specific criteria regarding age, educational qualifications, and physical standards. Generally, candidates need to have completed 10th standard or possess an ITI certificate from recognized institutions. Specific age limits apply, with relaxations for reserved categories as per government norms. Detailed eligibility criteria are outlined in the official notification for each CEN (Centralized Employment Notification).

## Selection Process for RRB Group D

The selection process for RRB Group D typically involves several stages:

1.  **Computer-Based Test (CBT):** This is the initial screening stage, assessing candidates' knowledge in subjects like General Science, Mathematics, General Intelligence and Reasoning, and General Awareness and Current Affairs.
2.  **Physical Efficiency Test (PET):** Candidates who qualify the CBT are called for PET. This stage evaluates physical fitness and is qualifying in nature, meaning candidates must pass it to proceed.
3.  **Document Verification (DV):** Shortlisted candidates from PET undergo verification of their educational, age, and other relevant documents.
4.  **Medical Examination:** A medical fitness test is conducted to ensure candidates meet the required health standards for the posts.

The final merit list is prepared based on the candidate's performance in the CBT, provided they successfully clear all subsequent stages of the selection process.
