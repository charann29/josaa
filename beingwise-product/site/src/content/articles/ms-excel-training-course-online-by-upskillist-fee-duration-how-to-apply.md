*   The course spans 19 weeks and is divided into four modules, comprising 37 lessons and assessments.
*   It offers a flexible, self-paced learning experience with lifetime access to materials and educator support.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a globally recognized certificate, certified by Austin Peay State University.
*   A free trial is available, allowing prospective students to experience the course content and support before committing.

## Course Overview and Structure

This comprehensive program is designed to provide a deep understanding of MS Excel. It covers various aspects, including essential functions, formulas, managing datasets, and advanced data visualization. The curriculum is structured into four modules, starting with a diploma-level introduction and progressing to more complex topics.

Each module builds on the previous one, ensuring a logical learning path. The 37 lessons are delivered through a combination of video and text-based content, supporting both self-study and virtual classroom environments. Regular assessments are integrated throughout the course to help reinforce learning and track progress.

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this program, participants are expected to gain a strong command over various Excel functionalities, enhancing their ability to manage and analyze data effectively. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Mastering diverse data analysis techniques.
*   Proficiency in error checking and creating efficient input sheets.
*   Expertise in formatting data using spreadsheets for clarity and impact.
*   Understanding basic VBA (Visual Basic for Applications) techniques for automation.
*   Strategies for ensuring data integrity and accuracy.

## Flexibility and Support

Recognizing the diverse needs of online learners, this course offers significant flexibility. Students benefit from:

*   **Flexible Online Classes:** Learn at your own pace and schedule.
*   **Lifetime Access:** All lesson materials are accessible indefinitely, allowing for review and continuous learning.
*   **Live Educator Support:** Assistance is available from educators to help with queries and complex topics.
*   **Pause Feature:** The ability to pause your learning journey and resume when convenient.
*   **Offline Mode:** Lectures can be downloaded, enabling study without an internet connection.

These features are designed to create a supportive and adaptable learning environment, making it easier for students to balance their studies with other commitments.
