# Big Data Hadoop Administration Certification Training Course by BeingWise: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to step into the world of managing large datasets, a certification in Hadoop Administration can open many doors. This program is designed to equip you with the essential skills to efficiently handle and operate complex Hadoop clusters.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course focuses on practical skills for managing, monitoring, and configuring Hadoop clusters.
*   It covers open-source components and deployments of Cloudera Hadoop 2.0, including security implementations like Kerberos.
*   Learning is facilitated through interactive live sessions led by industry experts, with lifetime access to learning materials.
*   No strict prerequisites exist, though a basic understanding of Linux command-line interfaces is beneficial.

## Course Overview and Learning Objectives

This training aims to transform you into a certified Hadoop Administrator, capable of overseeing the entire lifecycle of a Hadoop cluster. You will gain proficiency in crucial aspects such as cluster installation, strategic planning, continuous monitoring, and fine-tuning configurations for optimal performance.

The curriculum delves into the core responsibilities of a Hadoop Administrator, offering practical preparation for real-world industry challenges. It emphasizes understanding Big Data concepts and the limitations of traditional solutions. Furthermore, the program ensures you are well-versed in open-source components and deployments, specifically focusing on Cloudera Hadoop 2.0. You'll also learn about implementing robust security measures using Kerberos and Hadoop v2 through practical, industry-relevant case studies.

Upon completion, you will be able to:

*   Plan, execute, and deploy a Hadoop Cluster effectively.
*   Load data, deploy, and run applications across various Hadoop platforms.
*   Create, manage, supervise, and troubleshoot Hadoop Clusters.
*   Configure and tune cluster performance, understanding the administrator's role.
*   Master cluster security, management, backup, and recovery through hands-on assignments.
*   Gain insights into Hadoop 2.x, including NameNode high availability, HDFS Federation, YARN, and MapReduce v2.
*   Become familiar with Apache HBase administration in Java and high-level Hadoop platforms like Pig and Apache's Oozie.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The training is delivered through live, instructor-led interactive sessions, ensuring that learners with varying capacities can benefit. The program includes:

*   Live classroom sessions with industry expert practitioners.
*   Access to industry-level case studies for practical application.
*   Focus on Apache Hadoop distribution.
*   Assignments after each class to reinforce learning.
*   24/7 expert support to address queries.
*   Lifetime access to the Learning Management System (LMS).
*   A course completion certificate upon successfully meeting the program requirements.

## Eligibility Criteria

There are no formal prerequisites to enroll in the Hadoop Administration Certification Training Course. However, having a foundational understanding of the Linux command-line interface can significantly aid in successfully completing the course and grasping its concepts more easily.

## Fees and Certification

The course fee is ₹7,199, which is a discounted rate from the original price of ₹8,999. EMI options are also available, starting at ₹2,400. Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a course completion certificate from the certifying authority.

## Who Should Enroll?

This certification is ideal for a wide range of IT professionals looking to specialize in Big Data administration. It is particularly suitable for:

*   Unix, Linux, Windows, and Database Administrators
*   IT Professionals and System Administrators
*   Infrastructure Administrators
*   Big Data Analysts
*   Computer Systems Administrators
*   Data Administrators

This program is a valuable credential for anyone aiming to advance their career in managing large-scale data environments.
