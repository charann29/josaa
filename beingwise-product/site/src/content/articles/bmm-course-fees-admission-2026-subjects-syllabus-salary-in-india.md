## BMM: Course Fees, Admission 2026, Subjects, Syllabus, Salary in India

The Bachelor of Mass Media (BMM) is an undergraduate degree designed to give students a strong foundation in various aspects of media, communication, and journalism. It's a three-year program that equips you with essential skills for the dynamic media industry.

## Key Takeaways

*   BMM is a three-year undergraduate program focusing on media, communication, and journalism.
*   Eligibility typically requires completing Class 12 from a recognized board.
*   Course fees can range from approximately INR 30,000 to INR 2.58 Lakh per year, varying by institution.
*   Graduates can pursue diverse roles in advertising, PR, journalism, film, and digital media, with average salaries around INR 3.8 LPA.

## BMM Eligibility Criteria & Admission 2026

To be eligible for the BMM program, candidates generally need to have successfully completed their Class 12 examinations from a recognized educational board. While some colleges admit students based on their Class 12 scores, others may require candidates to appear for an entrance examination.

For admission in 2026, prospective students should regularly check the official websites of their preferred colleges for specific application deadlines, entrance exam dates, and detailed eligibility criteria. Some universities might conduct their own entrance tests, while others may consider scores from common mass communication entrance exams.

## BMM Subjects and Syllabus

The BMM syllabus is comprehensive, covering a wide array of topics that provide a holistic understanding of the media landscape. Students develop crucial skills in communication, critical thinking, creativity, and digital media tools. The curriculum is designed to foster storytelling abilities, audience engagement, and media production expertise.

Some of the core subjects you might encounter include:

*   Modern World History
*   Introduction to Public Relations
*   Introduction to Creative Writing
*   Press Law and Ethics
*   Communication Theories
*   Journalism Fundamentals
*   Advertising Principles
*   Film Studies
*   Digital Marketing and Social Media

Through these subjects, students gain practical experience and theoretical knowledge essential for a successful career in media.

## BMM Course Fees

The cost of pursuing a BMM degree can vary significantly depending on the type of institution (government or private) and its location. Generally, the annual course fees for a BMM program can range from approximately INR 30,000 to INR 2.58 Lakh. It is always advisable to check the official fee structure on the website of the specific college you are interested in, as these figures can change.

## Career Scope: Jobs, Salary, and Top Recruiters

A BMM degree opens doors to a wide range of career opportunities across various sectors of the media and communication industry. Graduates are well-prepared for roles in journalism, advertising, public relations, film, television, digital media, and content creation.

### Potential Job Roles:

*   Journalist
*   Content Writer
*   News Anchor
*   Copywriter
*   Public Relations Executive
*   Event Manager
*   Social Media Manager
*   Film Director
*   Sound Engineer
*   Columnist

### Salary Expectations:

The starting salary for BMM graduates in India can range from INR 2 LPA to INR 10 LPA, depending on the role, company, location, and individual skill set. The average annual salary for BMM graduates is often around INR 3.8 LPA. With experience and specialization, earning potential significantly increases.

### Top Recruiters:

Graduates often find employment with leading media houses, advertising agencies, and corporate communication departments. Some prominent recruiters include:

*   The Times of India
*   The Indian Express
*   NDTV
*   News 18
*   Radio Mirchi
*   Red FM

Many graduates also choose to become independent content creators or freelancers, leveraging their diverse skills in the evolving digital landscape.

This dynamic field offers continuous learning and growth, making a BMM degree a valuable asset for those passionate about communication and media.
