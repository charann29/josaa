# TANCET Exam Dates 2026 (OUT): Check Registration, Admit Card & Results Schedule

For students aspiring to pursue MBA and MCA programs in Tamil Nadu, the TANCET examination is a crucial step. Anna University conducts this statewide entrance test, and staying updated on its schedule is essential for a smooth application process.

## Key Takeaways

*   TANCET 2026 registration has been extended, with the final deadline for applications set for April 15, 2026.
*   The examination for both MBA and MCA courses is scheduled for May 9, 2026.
*   Admit cards are expected to be available for download approximately 10-15 days before the exam date.
*   All official updates, including eligibility criteria and the information brochure, are published on the official TANCET portal by Anna University.

## TANCET 2026 Registration Details

The application window for TANCET 2026 is from March 16 to April 15, 2026. The registration process is managed by Anna University on its official TANCET portal. This initial step for TN MBA/MCA admissions requires candidates to accurately fill in their personal and academic details, followed by the payment of the examination fee. It's critical to complete this process before the deadline, as late submissions will not be entertained, leading to disqualification from the admission cycle.

Candidates who encounter issues while uploading documents like photographs or signatures are often provided a chance to rectify these errors. Such corrections can typically be made by logging back into the application portal before a specified cut-off date to re-upload images in the correct format.

## TANCET 2026 Admit Card Information

All applicants who successfully register for TANCET 2026 will receive their admit cards online. The download link for the hall ticket will be made available on the official TANCET portal approximately 10 to 15 days prior to the examination date. It is mandatory for candidates to download and print a hard copy of their TANCET admit card. Entry to the examination hall will be strictly denied to anyone who fails to produce a physical copy of their admit card on the day of the exam. The admit card will remain available for download until the TANCET 2026 exam date.

## TANCET 2026 Examination Details

Anna University conducts the TANCET examination in an offline format. The official exam date for TANCET 2026 for MBA and MCA courses has been announced as May 9, 2026. The test comprises 100 multiple-choice questions, which candidates must complete within a two-hour timeframe. The questions are designed to assess various aptitudes relevant for postgraduate management and computer application programs in colleges across Tamil Nadu.

Ensure you mark these dates in your calendar and prepare diligently for TANCET 2026 to secure your admission into your desired MBA or MCA program.
