# AIIMS Paramedical 2026: Registration (Started), Exam Date (July 4), Pattern & Syllabus

For students aspiring to build a career in allied health sciences, the AIIMS Paramedical entrance examination offers a pathway to esteemed Bachelor of Science (BSc) Paramedical courses. This guide provides essential information for the 2026 examination, helping you prepare effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   The application window for AIIMS Paramedical 2026 opened on March 29, 2026, and closes on April 24, 2026.
*   The examination is scheduled for July 4, 2026, and will be conducted online.
*   Successful applicants need to have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
*   The exam features 90 multiple-choice questions, to be completed in 90 minutes.

## Important Dates for AIIMS Paramedical 2026

Staying informed about key dates is crucial for a smooth application process. Here's a quick overview of the AIIMS Paramedical 2026 schedule:

| Event                          | Date              |
| :----------------------------- | :---------------- |
| Application Form Release       | March 29, 2026    |
| Application Deadline           | April 24, 2026    |
| Examination Date               | July 4, 2026      |

Candidates should regularly check the official AIIMS website for any updates or changes to this schedule.

## Eligibility Criteria

Before applying, it's important to confirm you meet the eligibility requirements set by AIIMS Delhi for the Paramedical courses. The primary criterion is academic qualification:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination from a recognized board.
*   **Subject Combination:** You must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as core subjects in your 10+2.

Meeting these criteria is essential for your application to be considered valid.

## How to Apply for AIIMS Paramedical 2026

The application process for AIIMS Paramedical 2026 is entirely online. Here are the general steps to follow:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official AIIMS Delhi examination portal.
2.  **Registration:** Complete the initial registration process to generate your login credentials.
3.  **Fill the Application Form:** Log in and accurately fill out all required details in the application form, including personal, academic, and contact information.
4.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of necessary documents, such as photographs and signatures, in the specified format and size.
5.  **Pay Application Fee:** Remit the application fee through the available online payment modes.
6.  **Submit and Print:** Review all entered information, submit the form, and take a printout of the confirmation page for your records.

Ensure all details are correct before final submission, as corrections might be limited or not permitted after the deadline.

## Examination Pattern and Syllabus

Understanding the structure and content of the exam is vital for effective preparation. The AIIMS Paramedical 2026 exam will follow this pattern:

*   **Mode:** Online (Computer-Based Test)
*   **Medium:** English and Hindi
*   **Duration:** 90 minutes
*   **Total Questions:** 90
*   **Question Type:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Subjects:** Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (or Mathematics, depending on the course applied for)

Regarding the syllabus, candidates are advised to refer to the official AIIMS information. Generally, the syllabus aligns with the 10+2 curriculum for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Focusing on these core subjects and practicing previous year's papers will help identify high-weightage topics and improve time management.

## Preparation Strategy

Cracking the AIIMS Paramedical exam requires dedicated and smart preparation. Here are some tips:

*   **Thorough Understanding:** Build a strong foundation in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology concepts from your 10+2 syllabus.
*   **Practice Regularly:** Solve a variety of multiple-choice questions and practice previous years' question papers to get familiar with the exam format and question types.
*   **Time Management:** Since the exam is 90 minutes for 90 questions, practice solving questions quickly and accurately.
*   **Revision:** Regularly revise all subjects to retain information and strengthen your weak areas.

With consistent effort and a strategic approach, you can significantly enhance your chances of securing a seat in the AIIMS Paramedical courses.

The AIIMS Paramedical examination is a significant opportunity for students aiming for a career in the healthcare sector. By staying informed and preparing diligently, you can take a confident step towards achieving your academic and professional goals.
