# Datascience and Machine Learning Engineer by 4Geeks Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aspiring to a high-paying career in the rapidly expanding fields of data science and machine learning, this online bootcamp offers a focused pathway. It's designed to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel as a machine learning or data science engineer.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 16-week, part-time online program offered by 4Geeks Academy.
*   Classes are held three days a week, providing practical skills through real-life projects.
*   No prior experience or knowledge in data science or programming is required.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certification recognized by various organizations.

## Program Overview

This certification program, titled "Datascience and Machine Learning Engineer," is an intensive online bootcamp. It focuses on practical applications, ensuring participants gain a thorough understanding of key concepts. The curriculum covers essential tools and methodologies, including Python, deep learning, machine learning, and core data science principles.

The program is delivered entirely online, using a blend of video and text-based materials, alongside virtual classroom sessions. Instruction is available in both English and Spanish, catering to a diverse student body.

## What You Will Learn

Through this program, you will develop a strong foundation in Python programming, a crucial skill for data professionals. The syllabus is structured to build your expertise progressively, covering:

*   **Python Fundamentals:** Introduction to Python and practical projects.
*   **Statistical Foundations:** Understanding statistics and linear algebra with Python.
*   **Data Collection & Storage:** Techniques for gathering and storing data.
*   **Data Cleaning & Manipulation:** Mastering data manipulation using Python.
*   **Machine Learning Models:** Practical application of machine learning, including supervised and unsupervised learning, decision trees, and k-nearest neighbors algorithms.
*   **AI Deployment:** Concepts of deploying AI models in production environments.
*   **Final Project:** An opportunity to apply all learned skills to solve a real-world problem using machine learning.

The program also incorporates real-life projects throughout, ensuring a hands-on learning experience.

## Who Is This Program For?

This bootcamp is ideal for anyone interested in building a career in data science and machine learning, regardless of their current background. It's particularly beneficial for aspiring:

*   Data Analysts
*   Data Scientists
*   Data Engineers
*   Machine Learning Engineers

The program is structured to accommodate individuals with no prior experience in these fields, providing a comprehensive introduction and skill development.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for the Datascience and Machine Learning Engineer certification course is **$10,999**. This amount covers the entire 16-week program and all learning materials.

Upon successful completion of the course requirements, including all projects, participants will be awarded a Certificate of Completion by 4Geeks Academy. This certificate is recognized by multiple organizations, validating your newly acquired skills in data science and machine learning.

## How to Apply

The application process for the Datascience and Machine Learning Engineer program is straightforward:

1.  **Visit the Official Page:** Navigate to the official 4Geeks Academy website for this specific program.
2.  **Initiate Application:** Look for and select the 'APPLY NOW' option, which will direct you to a new application page.
3.  **Provide Details:** Fill in your personal information, including your name, mobile number, email address, and region.
4.  **Submit Application:** Click 'Apply' to submit your initial details.
5.  **Follow-Up:** A representative from 4Geeks Academy will then contact you via email to guide you through the subsequent steps of the admission process.

This program offers a structured and practical approach to entering the dynamic world of data science and machine learning, preparing you for significant career opportunities.
