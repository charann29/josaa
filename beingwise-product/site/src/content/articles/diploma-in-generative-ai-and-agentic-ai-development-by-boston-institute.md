# Diploma in Generative AI & Agentic AI Development by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to enter the rapidly evolving field of Artificial Intelligence, the Boston Institute of Analytics (BIA) offers a Diploma in Generative AI & Agentic AI Development. This program is designed to equip students with the latest skills in AI, focusing specifically on generative models and autonomous AI systems.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 6-month, online diploma program offered by the Boston Institute of Analytics.
*   The curriculum covers both generative AI models and agentic AI development, blending theory with practical application.
*   No prior work experience is needed, but a bachelor's degree in a relevant field and basic Python knowledge are prerequisites.
*   The program includes placement support and opportunities to interact with industry experts.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Diploma in Generative AI & Agentic AI Development aims to provide participants with deep expertise in advanced AI. It combines in-depth technical knowledge with practical applications, showing how these cutting-edge AI technologies can be used across various industries.

The course runs for **6 months** and is delivered online through a mix of video lessons, text-based materials, and virtual classroom sessions. The instruction is in English. Throughout the program, you'll benefit from hands-on labs, interactive sessions, and guidance from mentors.

## What You Will Learn

This diploma will give you a comprehensive understanding of both the theoretical foundations and practical implementation of AI. You'll start with core concepts in machine learning and deep learning, then progress to advanced generative AI models such as GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks), VAEs (Variational Autoencoders), and Transformer architectures.

A significant part of the curriculum focuses on agentic AI development, teaching you how autonomous AI agents function, make decisions, and interact within dynamic environments. The program also covers various AI frameworks and tools essential for real-world deployment.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in this diploma program, you need to meet the following requirements:

*   **Educational Qualification:** A bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics, or a related discipline.
*   **Technical Skills:** Basic proficiency in Python programming.
*   **Work Experience:** No prior professional work experience is required, making it suitable for recent graduates and early-career professionals.

## Who Is This Program For?

This diploma is ideal for a range of professionals and students looking to specialize in AI. It's particularly beneficial for:

*   Data Scientists
*   Machine Learning Engineers
*   Software Engineers
*   Artificial Intelligence Engineers
*   Product Managers
*   Undergraduate students looking to specialize
*   Experienced technologists seeking to upskill

## How to Apply

The admission process for the Diploma in Generative AI & Agentic AI Development is straightforward:

1.  Locate the official application portal for the Boston Institute of Analytics.
2.  Click on the "Enquire Now" option.
3.  Submit the inquiry form with your contact details.
4.  A representative from BIA will then contact you to guide you through the next steps.
5.  Once the course fees are paid, your enrollment in the program will be confirmed.

## Program Highlights

This program offers several key features designed to enhance your learning experience and career prospects:

*   Industry-aligned curriculum focused on Generative AI & Agentic AI.
*   Hands-on projects with practical, real-world applications.
*   Live sessions and mentorship from AI industry experts.
*   Access to a dedicated AI learning community for peer interaction.
*   Comprehensive coverage of essential AI frameworks and tools.
*   Placement assistance to help connect you with top AI employers.
*   Flexible online learning mode to accommodate various schedules.
*   Option for No-Cost EMI for fee payment.

Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate from the Boston Institute of Analytics.

This diploma aims to open doors to emerging job roles such as AI Developer, Machine Learning Engineer, Data Scientist, and Generative AI Specialist, providing a strong foundation for a rewarding career in Artificial Intelligence.
