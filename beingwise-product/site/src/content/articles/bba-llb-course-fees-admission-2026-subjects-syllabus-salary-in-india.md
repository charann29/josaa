# BBA LLB: Course Fees, Admission 2026, Subjects, Syllabus, Salary in India

The BBA LLB is a unique five-year integrated undergraduate program that combines a Bachelor of Business Administration with a Bachelor of Legislative Law. This dual-degree course is designed for students who have completed Class 12 and are keen to build a career at the intersection of business and law.

## Key Takeaways

*   The BBA LLB is a five-year integrated program, offering a dual degree in business administration and law.
*   Admission typically involves competitive entrance exams like CLAT, CUET, and SLAT, alongside academic merit.
*   The curriculum covers a wide range of subjects from corporate law and financial management to human resource management.
*   Graduates are well-prepared for diverse roles in corporate legal departments, law firms, and business consulting, with competitive salary prospects.

## Understanding the BBA LLB Course

This integrated program equips students with a strong foundation in both business principles and legal frameworks. It's particularly beneficial for those aspiring to careers in corporate law, legal consulting, or management roles that require a deep understanding of legal compliance and business strategy. The course helps students develop critical thinking, analytical reasoning, and persuasive communication skills essential for both legal and business professions.

### Eligibility and Admission for 2026

To be eligible for the BBA LLB program, candidates must have successfully completed their Class 12 examinations from a recognized board. Admissions are primarily based on performance in national and state-level entrance examinations, though some institutions may also consider academic merit.

Key entrance exams for BBA LLB in 2026 include:

*   **CLAT (Common Law Admission Test):** A national-level exam for admission to various National Law Universities (NLUs) and other affiliated colleges.
*   **CUET (Common University Entrance Test):** Used by several central and state universities for admission to their integrated law programs.
*   **MH CET Law:** The application window for the five-year LLB course has been extended until March 15, 2026, with the exam tentatively scheduled for May 8, 2026.
*   **AP LAWCET:** The entrance exam is scheduled for May 4, 2026.
*   **TS LAWCET:** Registration without a late fee is open until April 1, 2026, and the exam will be held on May 18, 2026.
*   **IPU CET, SLAT:** Other prominent university-specific or consortium-based exams.

Students are advised to regularly check the official websites of NTA (for CUET) and respective state examination bodies for the most accurate and updated information regarding application deadlines and exam dates.

## BBA LLB Subjects and Syllabus

The BBA LLB syllabus is comprehensive, integrating core subjects from business administration with fundamental and advanced legal studies. The curriculum is designed to provide a holistic understanding of how legal principles apply within a business context. 

Typical subjects covered throughout the five years include:

*   **Business Administration Modules:** Principles of Management, Financial Management, Human Resource Management, Marketing Management, Business Ethics, Corporate Strategy.
*   **Legal Modules:** Constitutional Law, Contract Law, Criminal Law, Family Law, Property Law, Corporate Law, Intellectual Property Rights, Environmental Law, Administrative Law.
*   **Integrated Modules:** Corporate Governance, Mergers and Acquisitions, International Business Law, Taxation Law, Legal Aspects of Banking and Insurance, Competition Law.

Practical training often includes moot court competitions, internships with law firms or corporate legal departments, legal aid clinics, and research projects, providing students with real-world exposure.

## Course Fees and Top Institutions

The fees for a BBA LLB program can vary significantly depending on the type of institution (government, private, or NLU) and its location. Generally, the total course fees can range from approximately INR 65,000 to INR 33.75 Lakh for the entire five-year duration.

Some of the well-regarded institutions offering BBA LLB programs include:

*   Symbiosis Law School, Pune
*   National Law University (NLU), Ranchi
*   National Law University (NLU), Odisha
*   Chanakya National Law University (CNLU), Patna
*   Lovely Professional University (LPU)
*   Alliance School of Law, Bengaluru

Prospective students should refer to the official NIRF rankings and individual university websites for detailed fee structures and admission criteria.

## Career Scope and Salary

Graduates of the BBA LLB program possess a unique skill set that makes them highly sought after in various sectors. The combination of business acumen and legal knowledge opens doors to a wide array of career opportunities.

### Potential Job Roles:

*   **Corporate Lawyer:** Advising businesses on legal matters, contracts, mergers, and compliance.
*   **Legal Advisor/Consultant:** Providing expert legal guidance to individuals or organizations.
*   **Compliance Manager:** Ensuring that an organization adheres to external laws and internal policies.
*   **Human Resources Manager:** Handling legal aspects of employment, industrial relations, and policy formulation.
*   **Business Consultant:** Advising businesses on strategy, operations, and legal risks.
*   **Company Secretary:** Managing corporate governance, regulatory compliance, and board affairs.
*   **Legal Operations Analyst:** Streamlining legal processes and leveraging technology in legal departments.

### Salary Expectations:

The average starting salary for BBA LLB graduates in India can vary based on the employer, location, and individual skills. While a general average might be around INR 9.5 LPA, it's important to note that salaries can range significantly. Top-tier law firms and multinational corporations often offer higher compensation packages, especially to graduates from prestigious institutions. With experience and specialization, earning potential tends to increase substantially.

The BBA LLB program offers a robust foundation for a dynamic career, blending the strategic world of business with the critical discipline of law. It's an excellent choice for those looking to make a significant impact in the corporate and legal landscapes.
