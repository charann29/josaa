# SBI Clerk 2025: Mains Scorecard (OUT), Result (Released), Cut Off, Exam Date, Pattern, Syllabus & FAQs

The State Bank of India (SBI) recently announced the results for its Junior Associate (Customer Support and Sales) Mains examination. Aspiring candidates can now view their scorecards, answer keys, and response sheets on the official SBI website, marking a significant step in the recruitment process.

## Key takeaways

*   The SBI Clerk Mains result for the 2025 examination (Advertisement No: CRPD/CR/2025-26/06) was officially released on February 27.
*   Candidates who appeared for the Mains exam, held on November 21, can access their individual scorecards, along with response sheets and the official answer key, through the SBI portal.
*   Successful candidates from the Mains exam will now proceed to the Language Proficiency Test (LPT), which is a qualifying stage.
*   The Prelims exam results were announced on November 4, following examinations conducted on September 20, 21, and 27.

## SBI Clerk Mains Result and Scorecard

The SBI Clerk Mains scorecard, response sheet, and answer key have been made available for candidates. The Mains result, which is the final stage before the Language Proficiency Test, was declared on February 27. Candidates can download their results and scorecards by logging into the official SBI website using their registration or roll number.

The result is typically released in two formats: a PDF document listing the roll numbers of all qualified candidates and an individual scorecard detailing section-wise and overall marks obtained by each candidate.

To download your result:

*   Visit the direct link provided on the official SBI portal for result checking.
*   Enter your registration number or roll number as requested.
*   Your result will be displayed on the screen.
*   Download and save the result for future reference.

## SBI Clerk Cut Off 2025

The cut-off marks for the SBI Clerk Mains examination are expected to be released soon. These cut-offs are declared state-wise and category-wise along with the results. It's important for candidates to note that there is no sectional cut-off in the SBI Clerk exam; selection is based solely on the overall Mains cut-off. Separate cut-offs are released for both the preliminary and main examinations, tailored to different categories and states.

## Recruitment Process Overview

The SBI Clerk recruitment typically involves several stages:

1.  **Preliminary Examination:** This is the first screening stage. Candidates must clear this to be eligible for the Mains.
2.  **Main Examination:** This is the primary selection stage. Marks obtained in the Mains exam are considered for final merit.
3.  **Language Proficiency Test (LPT):** This is a qualifying test. Candidates must demonstrate proficiency in the local language of the state they applied for.
4.  **Document Verification:** Selected candidates undergo verification of their educational and other relevant documents.

Successful candidates who clear all these stages will receive an appointment letter for the Junior Associate position.
