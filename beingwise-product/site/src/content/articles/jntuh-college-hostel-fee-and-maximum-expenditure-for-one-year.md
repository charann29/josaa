# JNTUH College Hostel Fee and Maximum Expenditure for One Year?

Understanding the financial commitment for higher education is crucial for both students and parents. This guide breaks down the potential costs associated with studying and living at Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH) for one year.

## Key Takeaways

*   Annual tuition for standard four-year undergraduate programs is ₹10,000.
*   Five-year integrated courses (like MBA/M.Tech) have an annual fee of ₹35,000.
*   Integrated Double Degree Master's Programs (IDDMP) are significantly higher at ₹70,000 per semester.
*   On-campus hostel accommodation, including mess charges, totals approximately ₹60,000 per year.

## Understanding JNTUH Course Fees

JNTUH offers a variety of programs, and the tuition fees vary based on the course structure and duration. It's important to differentiate between these to accurately estimate your annual expenses.

For a standard four-year undergraduate degree, such as a B.Tech, the annual tuition fee is quite reasonable, set at ₹10,000. This makes quality technical education accessible to many students.

Integrated five-year programs, which combine undergraduate and postgraduate studies (for example, B.Tech + M.Tech or B.Tech + MBA), have a higher annual fee of ₹35,000. These programs offer a streamlined path to a master's degree.

Specialized Integrated Double Degree Master's Programs (IDDMP) carry a substantially different fee structure. These programs are priced per semester, with a fee of ₹70,000. This means for a full academic year (two semesters), the tuition alone would be ₹140,000.

## JNTUH Hostel and Mess Charges

For students opting for on-campus living, JNTUH provides hostel facilities. The costs associated with accommodation and food are calculated separately from the tuition fees.

The monthly hostel fee is ₹2,000. This covers the accommodation aspect. Annually, this amounts to ₹24,000.

In addition to the hostel fee, there are mess charges for food. These are ₹3,000 per month. Over a year, the mess charges sum up to ₹36,000.

Combining both the hostel and mess charges, the total annual expenditure for on-campus living at JNTUH is approximately ₹60,000 (₹24,000 for accommodation + ₹36,000 for food).

## Estimating Total Annual Expenditure

To get a clearer picture of the maximum expenditure for one year, we need to combine the relevant tuition fees with the hostel and mess charges. Here’s a breakdown:

### For a 4-Year Undergraduate Course:

*   **Tuition Fee:** ₹10,000
*   **Hostel & Mess Charges:** ₹60,000
*   **Total Annual Expenditure:** ₹70,000

### For a 5-Year Integrated Course (e.g., B.Tech + M.Tech/MBA):

*   **Tuition Fee:** ₹35,000
*   **Hostel & Mess Charges:** ₹60,000
*   **Total Annual Expenditure:** ₹95,000

### For an Integrated Double Degree Master's Program (IDDMP):

*   **Tuition Fee (per year, two semesters):** ₹140,000 (₹70,000 x 2)
*   **Hostel & Mess Charges:** ₹60,000
*   **Total Annual Expenditure:** ₹200,000

These figures represent the primary costs. Students should also factor in additional expenses such as books, personal spending, travel, and any specific course materials, which can vary greatly depending on individual habits and needs. Always refer to the official JNTUH admissions portal for the most current and detailed fee structures.
