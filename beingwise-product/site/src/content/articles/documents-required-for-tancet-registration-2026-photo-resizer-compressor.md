# Documents Required for TANCET Registration 2026; Photo Resizer, Compressor

Registering for TANCET is a crucial step towards your postgraduate studies in Tamil Nadu. To ensure a smooth application process, it's vital to have all your documents ready and correctly formatted.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Online Process Only:** TANCET registration is exclusively online; no offline applications are accepted.
*   **Accuracy is Key:** All details entered in the application form must precisely match your official certificates.
*   **Image Specifications:** Scanned copies of your photograph and signature must adhere to specific size and dimension guidelines.
*   **Rectification Opportunity:** If there are errors in uploaded documents, Anna University may put your application on hold, allowing you a chance to correct them.

## Essential Documents for Reference During TANCET Registration

While you won't upload all these, you'll need them handy to accurately fill out your TANCET application form. Any discrepancies between the information you provide and your official documents can lead to issues during the verification process.

*   **Personal Identification:** Your full name, date of birth, and parent's names should match your 10th-grade mark sheet or Aadhar card.
*   **Academic Records:** Details of your undergraduate degree (course, university, marks/CGPA, year of passing) and any previous qualifying examinations.
*   **Category Certificate:** If you belong to a reserved category, you'll need your community certificate to accurately select your category.
*   **Contact Information:** A valid email address and mobile number for all communication from Anna University.

## Documents to Upload During TANCET 2026 Application Process

During the online registration, you are specifically required to upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature. For candidates applying under a reserved category, a community certificate must also be uploaded. It's important to meet the specified guidelines for these uploads; otherwise, your application might be put on hold for corrections.

## Photo Resizer/Compressor for TANCET Registration

It's common for images taken with smartphones or digital cameras to be larger than the specified file size and dimensions required for online applications. If your scanned photograph or signature doesn't meet the TANCET upload guidelines, you'll need to adjust them.

You can use various online tools or mobile applications for this purpose. These tools typically allow you to:

*   **Resize:** Change the pixel dimensions (width and height) of an image. You can often input new dimensions or a percentage to reduce the size.
*   **Compress:** Reduce the file size (in KB or MB) without significantly altering the dimensions, by optimizing image data. 

Simply upload your image to one of these tools, adjust the settings according to the official TANCET specifications, and then download the optimized file for upload. Always double-check the final file size and dimensions before uploading.

## Important Instructions While Filling the TANCET Application Form

Filling out the TANCET application form requires careful attention to detail. Here are some key points to remember:

*   **Review Before Submission:** Before final submission, thoroughly review all the information you've entered. Once submitted, making changes can be difficult or impossible.
*   **Keep Proof:** Print a copy of your completed application form and the payment receipt for your records.
*   **Check Application Status:** After registration, regularly check the official TANCET portal for updates on your application status.

Ensuring you have all necessary documents and adhere to the guidelines will make your TANCET registration process smooth and stress-free.
