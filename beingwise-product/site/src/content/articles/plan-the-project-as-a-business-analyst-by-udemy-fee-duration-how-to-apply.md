# Plan the Project as a Business Analyst by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For business analysts looking to strengthen their project planning and management capabilities, the "Plan the Project as a Business Analyst" course offered on Udemy provides a structured approach. This program is designed to equip you with the skills to independently develop comprehensive project plans.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn a 5-step process for effective project planning, from initiation to performance measurement.
*   Gain practical insights through real-life examples and case studies.
*   Access course materials for a lifetime, including videos, articles, and downloadable resources.
*   Receive a certificate of completion from Udemy, enhancing your professional profile.

## Course Overview

This online course is specifically tailored for business analysts who need to master the art of project planning or those keen on developing strong project management skills. Created by Jeremy Aschenbrenner, an experienced Business Analyst Trainer and Coach, the curriculum covers all essential concepts required to craft an effective project plan.

The program guides learners through a five-step process for project management, ensuring a holistic understanding:

1.  **Project Plan Initiation:** This phase focuses on engaging stakeholders and creating a Project Charter, laying the groundwork for your project.
2.  **Requirement Management Plan:** Here, you'll learn how to detail, trace, reuse, store, and access project requirements effectively.
3.  **Project Execution:** This step covers strategies to execute projects efficiently, ensuring they stay on schedule and within estimated effort.
4.  **Stakeholder Analysis and Engagement:** Discover techniques for analyzing stakeholders, developing communication plans, and clearly defining roles and responsibilities.
5.  **Performance Measurement:** The final step involves identifying key performance indicators (KPIs) to track and measure project success.

Throughout the course, practical knowledge is reinforced with real-life examples, preparing you to plan a complete project with confidence.

## Program Highlights and Offerings

This self-paced, online course offers significant flexibility and resources. Key features include:

*   **Mode of Instruction:** English videos with multi-language subtitles.
