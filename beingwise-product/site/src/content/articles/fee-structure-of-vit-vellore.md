# What is the fee structure of VIT Vellore?

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a well-regarded institution offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Understanding the fee structure is a crucial step for prospective students and their families.

## Key takeaways

*   Fees at VIT Vellore vary significantly based on the specific program and, for B.Tech, on the category assigned through VITEEE ranks.
*   B.Tech programs have a tiered fee structure, with Category 1 being the most economical and Category 5 the highest, determined by your VITEEE performance.
*   A refundable caution deposit is typically required in the first year for most programs, in addition to the annual tuition fees.
*   Beyond tuition, students should also budget for hostel accommodation, mess charges, and other personal expenses.

## Understanding B.Tech Fee Categories

Admission to VIT Vellore's B.Tech programs involves a unique fee categorisation system. Your performance in the VIT Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE) directly influences which fee category you are placed into. Generally, a higher rank in VITEEE leads to a lower fee category, making the program more affordable. There are typically five categories, with Category 1 having the lowest fees and Category 5 having the highest.

For the academic year 2024, the annual tuition fees for B.Tech programs based on these categories are:

*   **Category 1:** Rs. 1,73,000 per year
*   **Category 2:** Rs. 2,32,000 per year
*   **Category 3:** Rs. 3,40,000 per year
*   **Category 4:** Rs. 3,85,000 per year
*   **Category 5:** Rs. 4,48,000 per year

## Undergraduate Program Fees (Other than B.Tech)

VIT Vellore offers a diverse range of undergraduate courses beyond B.Tech, each with its own fee structure. It's important to note that a refundable caution deposit is usually part of the first-year payment.

Here's an overview of some key undergraduate programs and their approximate annual tuition fees for 2024-2025:

| Program                        | Annual Tuition Fee (Approx.) | Caution Deposit (One-time, Refundable) |
| :----------------------------- | :--------------------------- | :------------------------------------- |
| B.Arch                         | Rs. 3,10,000                 | Rs. 3,000                              |
| B.Des. (Industrial Design)     | Rs. 1,73,000                 | Rs. 3,000                              |
| B.Sc. (Computer Science), BCA  | Rs. 55,000 - Rs. 90,000      | Rs. 2,000                              |
| B.Com., BBA                    | Rs. 55,000 - Rs. 90,000      | Rs. 2,000                              |

*Please note that the exact fees for B.Sc., BCA, B.Com., and BBA programs depend on the specific specialization chosen. The caution deposit is typically added to the first year's total payment.*

## Postgraduate Program Fees

For students aspiring to pursue higher education, VIT Vellore provides various postgraduate options. The fees for these programs also vary by specialization.

Here's a look at the annual tuition fees for some popular postgraduate courses for 2024-2025:

*   **MBA Program:** The annual tuition fee is Rs. 3,48,500. A caution deposit of Rs. 3,000 is also required.
*   **MCA Program:** The annual tuition fee is Rs. 1,37,000. A caution deposit of Rs. 3,000 is also required.
*   **M.Tech Programs:** Tuition fees range from Rs. 1,50,000 to Rs. 1,90,000 per annum, depending on the specialization (e.g., Biomedical Engineering, CAD/CAM, Construction Technology and Management). A caution deposit of Rs. 3,000 is also applicable.

## Additional Expenses to Consider

While tuition fees form the core of your educational expenses, it's essential to factor in other costs when planning your budget for VIT Vellore. These typically include:

*   **Hostel Fees:** VIT offers accommodation on campus, and charges vary based on the type of room (e.g., single, shared, AC/non-AC) and facilities.
*   **Mess Charges:** Food expenses are usually separate from hostel fees and depend on the meal plan chosen.
*   **Examination Fees:** These are typically collected separately during each academic session.
*   **Personal Expenses:** Budget for books, study materials, travel, and other daily necessities.

For the most precise and up-to-date fee information, always refer to the official VIT Vellore admissions portal.
