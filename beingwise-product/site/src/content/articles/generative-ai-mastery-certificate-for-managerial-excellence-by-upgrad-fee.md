# Generative AI Mastery Certificate for Managerial Excellence by upGrad: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For managers and business leaders aiming to leverage the power of Artificial Intelligence, this specialized certificate program offers practical expertise in generative AI. Developed in collaboration with Microsoft and the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), it focuses on applying AI tools to solve real-world business challenges, enhance decision-making, and drive innovation within organizations.

## Key Takeaways

*   A concise, 2-month program designed for busy professionals.
*   Earn a joint certificate from upGrad and Microsoft, boosting your professional credentials.
*   Learn to develop no-code AI solutions, build AI agents, and automate workflows without extensive programming.
*   Gain essential AI business knowledge and strategies for career growth in an AI-driven landscape.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

This program delves into the core principles of generative AI and its transformative impact on business operations. Participants will learn to identify opportunities where AI can significantly enhance efficiency, foster innovation, and improve decision-making processes. A key emphasis is placed on developing no-code AI solutions, empowering participants to create prototypes and applications even without extensive programming knowledge.

The learning experience combines self-study with virtual classroom sessions, delivered through video and text-based content. You'll engage with real-world projects, gain hands-on experience with leading AI platforms, and learn directly from industry experts.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of generative AI's application in a managerial context:

### Module 1: Understanding Generative AI and its Business Potential

*   Enhancing personal productivity for managers using AI.
*   The underlying technology behind Generative AI.
*   Exploring diverse business use-cases for AI.
*   Leveraging Microsoft 365 Copilot for workforce empowerment (as per Microsoft Learn).

### Module 2: Identifying and Evaluating AI Opportunities

*   Techniques for effective problem identification within an organization.
*   Strategies for building effective AI-driven solutions.

### Module 3: Managing Gen AI Solutions

*   Developing a robust AI strategy for your business.
*   Building and leading effective AI teams.
*   Planning and implementing AI projects successfully.
*   Transforming your business with Microsoft AI (as per Microsoft Learn).

Upon completion, you will possess knowledge in artificial intelligence, business analytics, project management, and product management, all tailored for an AI-centric environment.

## Who Is This Program For?

This certificate is ideal for a range of professionals looking to integrate AI into their work and leadership roles. Target audiences include:

*   Chief Technology Officers (CTOs)
*   Product Managers
*   Project Managers
*   Business Managers
*   Entrepreneurs
*   Artificial Intelligence Engineers seeking managerial insights

## Fees and Duration

This intensive program spans 2 months. The fee structure for the Generative AI Mastery Certificate for Managerial Excellence is:

Enrolling in the Generative AI Mastery Certificate for Managerial Excellence program is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official program webpage.
2.  Click on the 'Apply Now' button, usually located next to your preferred pricing plan.
3.  Complete the application form with your details.
4.  You may be required to attempt a scholarship test, if applicable.
5.  Make the necessary fee payment to secure your enrollment.
