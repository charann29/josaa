# Exam Prep Amazon Web Services Certified Solutions Architect Associate by Amazon Web Services via Edx: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals aiming to validate their expertise in cloud architecture, the Amazon Web Services (AWS) Certified Solutions Architect – Associate exam is a crucial step. This preparation course, offered by AWS through Edx, is designed to equip you with the knowledge needed to pass this challenging certification.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course is delivered entirely in English through a self-paced, video and text-based format.
*   It requires an estimated commitment of 2-3 hours per week.
*   Upon successful completion, an official certificate from Amazon Web Services is available.
*   The total fee for the course and certificate is ₹4,114.

## Course Structure and Learning Approach

This preparation program is structured to provide a comprehensive review of the domains covered in the AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate exam. The learning approach is flexible, allowing you to study at your own pace through a combination of video lessons and textual content. This self-study model is ideal for individuals balancing their studies with other commitments.

## Syllabus Overview

The course is divided into four weekly modules, each focusing on a key domain of the AWS Solutions Architect Associate exam. This structured approach ensures that all critical areas are covered systematically:

*   **Week 1: Designing Resilient Architectures (Domain 1)** – Learn how to design highly available and fault-tolerant systems on AWS.
*   **Week 2: Designing High-performing Architectures (Domain 2)** – Focus on creating performant and scalable solutions using AWS services.
*   **Week 3: Designing Secure Applications and Architectures (Domain 3)** – Understand best practices for securing your applications and data on the AWS platform.
*   **Week 4: Designing Cost-optimized Architectures (Domain 4)** – Explore strategies for designing cost-effective solutions while meeting performance and security requirements.

## Instructor

The course content is developed and delivered by Ms. Julie Elkins, who serves as an Exam Prep Curriculum Developer at Amazon Web Services. Her expertise ensures that the material is directly relevant to the certification exam and reflects the latest AWS best practices.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for enrolling in this exam preparation course and obtaining the official certificate is ₹4,114. The certificate, which confirms your completion of the program, is provided directly by Amazon Web Services. This certification can significantly enhance your professional profile in the cloud computing industry.

This preparatory course offers a valuable pathway for aspiring cloud architects to gain a recognized certification in a rapidly growing field.
