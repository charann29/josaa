# BBA in HR: Course Fees, Admission 2026, Subjects, Syllabus, Salary in India

A Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Human Resources (HR) is a three-year undergraduate program designed to equip students with the essential knowledge and skills needed to manage an organization's most valuable asset: its people. This specialisation combines core business management principles with a deep dive into human resource functions like recruitment, training, and employee relations.

## Key Takeaways

*   The BBA in HR is a three-year undergraduate degree focusing on personnel management and organizational development.
*   Eligibility generally requires completing Class 12 from any stream, with admissions often based on Class 12 marks or entrance exams like CUET and UGAT.
*   Course fees can vary widely, from approximately INR 30,000 to over INR 20 lakhs, depending on the institution.
*   Graduates can pursue roles such as HR Manager in Talent Acquisition or Compliance, with potential recruiters including major consulting firms and IT companies.

## Eligibility and Admission for BBA in HR 2026

To be considered for a BBA in HR program, aspiring students typically need to have completed their Class 12 examinations from a recognized board. While specific requirements can vary by college, a common criterion is an aggregate score of 50% or more in Class 12. Some institutions may also have a minimum age limit, often around 17 years.

### Admission Process

The admission process for BBA in HR programs can be either merit-based, relying on your Class 12 scores, or entrance-based. Many prominent colleges accept scores from national-level entrance examinations. It is crucial for applicants to check the specific eligibility criteria and admission process for each college they are interested in, as these details can differ significantly.

### Entrance Exams

While some colleges admit students solely based on their Class 12 performance, many others require applicants to appear for entrance tests. Two of the commonly accepted entrance exams for BBA programs, including the HR specialization, are:

*   **Common University Entrance Test (CUET):** This exam is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to various undergraduate programs in central and participating universities.
*   **Undergraduate Aptitude Test (UGAT):** Administered by AIMA (All India Management Association), UGAT is another popular test for undergraduate programs in management.

## BBA in HR Course Syllabus and Subjects

The BBA in HR curriculum is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of both general management principles and specialized human resource functions. Over the three years, students will typically cover a range of subjects, blending theoretical knowledge with practical applications.

### Core Subjects

Students can expect to study foundational business subjects alongside their HR specialization. Some common subjects include:

*   Principles of Management
*   Business Economics
*   Financial Accounting
*   Marketing Management
*   Organizational Behavior
*   Business Law
*   Human Resource Management Fundamentals
*   Recruitment and Selection
*   Training and Development
*   Compensation and Benefits Management
*   Industrial Relations
*   Performance Management
*   HR Analytics

## Course Fees for BBA in HR

The financial investment for a BBA in HR program shows a broad range across India. Depending on the type of institution (government-funded, private, or deemed university), the facilities offered, and the reputation of the college, the total course fees can range from approximately INR 30,000 per year to upwards of INR 20 lakhs for the entire program. Prospective students should thoroughly research the fee structure of their preferred colleges.

## Career Scope and Salary After BBA in HR

A BBA in HR opens doors to various roles within the human resources domain across different industries. Graduates are equipped to handle critical functions that support an organization's workforce.

### Job Profiles

Some popular job profiles for BBA in HR graduates include:

*   **HR Manager - Talent Acquisition:** Responsible for finding, attracting, and hiring suitable candidates for job vacancies.
*   **HR Manager - Compliance:** Ensures that the organization adheres to all labor laws and regulations.
*   **HR Generalist:** Handles a wide range of HR duties, from recruitment to employee relations.
*   **Training and Development Executive:** Designs and implements training programs to enhance employee skills.
*   **HR Consultant:** Provides expert advice to organizations on HR strategies and challenges.
*   **HR Professor:** For those interested in academia, teaching at management institutes is an option.

### Average Salary

The starting salary for BBA in HR graduates can vary based on factors like the hiring company, location, and the graduate's skills and experience. Typically, average salaries can range from INR 4 to 10 LPA and can increase significantly with experience and advanced qualifications.

### Top Recruiters

Many leading companies actively recruit BBA in HR graduates. These can include consulting firms, IT companies, manufacturing units, and service industries. Some prominent recruiters in the past have included:

*   Boston Consulting Group (BCG)
*   KPMG
*   Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

Choosing a BBA in HR is a step towards a dynamic career in managing human capital, a critical function in every successful enterprise.
