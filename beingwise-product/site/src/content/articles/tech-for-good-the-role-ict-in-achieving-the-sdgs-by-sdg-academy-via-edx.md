# Tech for good: The role ICT in achieving the SDGs by SDG Academy via Edx: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program explores how Information and Communication Technologies (ICTs) are crucial tools for advancing the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). It showcases how digital innovations are transforming societies and empowering individuals worldwide.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn how ICTs drive progress across various SDG areas, from healthcare to education.
*   Understand the importance of digital inclusion and robust ICT infrastructure.
*   Explore key concepts like Artificial Intelligence (AI), cybersecurity, and data privacy in a development context.
*   Gain insights into the challenges and opportunities of digital transformation in government services.

## Course Overview

The "Tech for Good" program, originally developed by UNESCO and Centic.br, focuses on building a global information society. This online course brings together leaders and innovators in ICT and sustainable development to illustrate the profound impact of digital technology on daily life. It delves into how technology is being leveraged in government services and for citizens, offering a comprehensive look at ICT infrastructure, promoting digital equity, and expanding knowledge access. The curriculum also covers emerging topics such as artificial intelligence, data analysis metrics, real-world case studies, and the challenges associated with technological advancement.

## Learning Experience and Duration

This is a self-paced, online program designed for flexibility. It is estimated to require 3 to 5 hours of study per week and spans approximately 10 weeks to complete. The learning materials are delivered through a combination of videos and text, ensuring an engaging and accessible experience. Discussion activities, graded assignments, and discussion forums are integrated to foster interactive learning. Transcripts are also available, potentially in multiple languages, to aid understanding.

## Who Should Enroll?

This course is ideal for undergraduate and graduate students interested in the intersection of ICT, digitalization, and sustainable development. It's also highly relevant for sustainable development practitioners who wish to understand and leverage technology for positive change. Anyone keen to learn about the importance of digital tools in addressing global challenges will find this program beneficial.

## Fees and Certification

The "Tech for Good: The Role of ICT in achieving the SDGs" program is available for free. However, participants have two enrollment options: the 'free audit track' and the 'verified track'. The free audit track provides limited-duration access to the course content. For those who opt for the verified track, unlimited access to course materials and a certificate of completion are provided. Financial assistance, covering up to 90% of the verified track fee, may also be available for eligible candidates.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the program, candidates simply need to register online. Once registered, they can follow the provided link to begin the course. The process is straightforward, allowing immediate access to the learning materials.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to understand how technology can be a powerful force for global good and sustainable progress.
