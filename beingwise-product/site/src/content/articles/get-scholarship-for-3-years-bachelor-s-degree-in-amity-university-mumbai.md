# Can I get a Scholarship for a 3-Year Bachelor's Degree at Amity University, Mumbai?

Amity University, Mumbai offers various opportunities for students seeking financial assistance for their undergraduate studies. Understanding the eligibility criteria is key to securing one of these valuable scholarships for your three-year bachelor's degree.

## Key Takeaways

*   Amity University, Mumbai primarily offers scholarships for the first year of bachelor's degrees based on Class 12 academic performance.
*   These scholarships are merit-based, with higher percentages in Class 12 increasing your eligibility.
*   Continuation of the scholarship in subsequent years depends on maintaining a strong academic record at the university.
*   Scholarships are generally not available for postgraduate courses at Amity University, Mumbai.

## Understanding Amity University, Mumbai Scholarships

Amity University, Mumbai provides a limited number of scholarships to deserving students entering their bachelor's degree programs. These are typically awarded on a first-come, first-served basis, so it's advisable to apply early if you meet the criteria. The primary focus for these scholarships is academic excellence demonstrated in your Class 12 board examinations.

## Eligibility Criteria for Undergraduate Scholarships

The scholarships are mainly geared towards students who have performed exceptionally well in their Class 12 examinations. Here's a breakdown of the general eligibility:

*   **For CBSE/ICSE Board Students:**
    *   **100% Scholarship:** Awarded to students with 95% or above in Class 12. To continue this scholarship, students typically need to maintain 80% marks in their graduation studies at Amity. This scholarship covers the full tuition fee for the first year.
    *   **50% Scholarship:** Awarded to students with 90% to 94.9% in Class 12. For continuation, students usually need to maintain 75% marks in their graduation studies at Amity. This covers half of the tuition fee for the first year.

*   **For State Board Students:**
    *   **25% Scholarship:** Awarded to students with 90% or above in Class 12 from a recognized state board. Continuation often requires maintaining 75% marks in their graduation studies at Amity. This covers a quarter of the tuition fee for the first year.

It's important to note that these percentages are general guidelines. For the most precise and up-to-date information, students should always refer to the official Amity University, Mumbai admissions brochure or contact their admissions office directly.

## Scholarship Continuation for Subsequent Years

While the initial scholarship is granted based on your Class 12 performance, its continuation for the second and third years of your bachelor's degree is contingent upon your academic performance *at* Amity University, Mumbai. Students are generally required to maintain a certain minimum percentage or GPA in their university examinations to retain the scholarship for subsequent semesters and years. This ensures that the scholarships continue to support consistently high-achieving students throughout their degree program.

## Scholarships for Postgraduate Courses

It's important for prospective students to understand that Amity University, Mumbai, typically does not offer scholarships for postgraduate (PG) courses. The scholarship programs are primarily designed to support students entering their undergraduate bachelor's degree programs.

Securing a scholarship can significantly ease the financial burden of higher education, making your dream of studying at Amity University, Mumbai more accessible.
