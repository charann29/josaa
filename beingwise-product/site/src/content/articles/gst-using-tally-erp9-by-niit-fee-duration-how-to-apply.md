# GST Using Tally.ERP9 by NIIT: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to master the intricacies of Goods and Services Tax (GST) within the practical framework of Tally.ERP9 software, the NIIT training program offers a comprehensive pathway. This course is designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge of GST and the hands-on skills to apply it in real-world accounting scenarios.

## Key takeaways

*   The program focuses on both GST concepts and their practical application using Tally.ERP9.
*   It is a 44-hour, instructor-led course delivered through classroom and hands-on sessions.
*   Participants will receive a certificate of participation from NIIT, with an additional Tally e-certificate available upon passing an online exam.
*   The curriculum covers GST registration, transaction recording, return filing, and business readiness for GST.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This NIIT certification program is meticulously crafted to ensure proficiency in various GST principles and their statutory obligations. Students will delve into recording and processing GST transactions directly within the Tally.ERP9 software. The program's structure includes extensive study materials, dedicated classroom training, and practical, hands-on sessions that allow students to apply theoretical knowledge on the Tally ERP9 platform.

The 44-hour duration is packed with learning experiences facilitated by industry expert faculty. The delivery mode combines video and text-based content with physical classroom instruction, ensuring a well-rounded educational journey.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is comprehensive, covering a wide array of topics essential for GST implementation and Tally.ERP9 mastery. By the end of this program, participants will understand:

*   The new indirect taxation structure in India.
*   The process of maintaining accounting books under GST.
*   Different scenarios for GST implementation.
*   How to record advanced entries for both goods and services.
*   Migration procedures for the latest Tally.ERP9 release.
*   The fundamental concepts of Goods and Services Tax (GST).
*   The official registration procedure under GST.
*   Strategies to prepare a business for GST compliance.
*   Methods for return filing and GST adjustments.

## Eligibility and Certification

To enroll in the GST Using Tally.ERP9 training, prospective students should be from a commerce background and possess a foundational working knowledge of Tally.ERP9 software. Both undergraduate and graduate students aspiring to understand GST concepts and their practical implementation are encouraged to apply.

Upon successful completion of the entire course, participants will be awarded a certificate of participation by NIIT. Furthermore, those who pass an online examination conducted by Tally will be eligible for an official Tally e-certificate, adding significant value to their professional profile.

## How to Apply

Prospective students interested in the GST Using Tally.ERP9 program can initiate their application process by visiting the official NIIT course page. On this page, an inquiry form is available for completion. Applicants will need to provide basic details such as their name, email address, and state of residence. After submitting this information and agreeing to the privacy policy, NIIT experts will get in touch using the registered contact details to guide them through the next steps of admission.

This program is an excellent opportunity to gain crucial skills in a vital area of modern Indian business accounting.
