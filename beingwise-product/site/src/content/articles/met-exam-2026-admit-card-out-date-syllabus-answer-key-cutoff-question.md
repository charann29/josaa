# MET Exam 2026 - Admit Card (Out), Date, Syllabus, Answer Key, Cutoff, Question Papers

The Manipal Entrance Test (MET) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to join various undergraduate and postgraduate programs at Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) and its associated institutions. This guide provides essential information about the MET 2026, including key dates, application details, and what to expect.

## Key Takeaways

*   The MET 2026 admit cards for Phase 1 are now available for download on the official Manipal website.
*   The examination will be conducted online across two phases: Phase 1 in April and Phase 2 in May 2026.
*   The application fee for MET 2026 is ₹2000, covering both the application and entrance test charges.
*   MET is the gateway to diverse B.Tech, B.Pharm, BBA, and other programs at MAHE campuses.

## Understanding the MET Exam

The Manipal Entrance Test (MET), formerly known as the Manipal University Online Entrance Test (MU-OET), is conducted by Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE). This university-level entrance examination facilitates admissions into a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate courses offered by MAHE and its constituent colleges. These programs include, but are not limited to, Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech), Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm), Master of Technology (M.Tech), and Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA).

## Important Dates and Application Process

The application window for MET 2026 commenced on September 30 and concluded on March 15, 2026. However, candidates still have an opportunity to register for Phase 2 until April 26, 2026. The total application fee for MET 2026 is ₹2000, which includes a ₹800 application fee and a ₹1200 entrance test fee. The admit card for Phase 1 was released on April 10, 2026, and can be downloaded from the official Manipal website using your application number and OTBS password.

## Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The MET 2026 will be an online examination. Students are advised to thoroughly review the official MET exam pattern and syllabus, which are available on the Manipal Academy of Higher Education's website. The syllabus generally covers core subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English, tailored to the requirements of the chosen program.
