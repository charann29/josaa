# GATE 2026 Branch-Wise Cutoff OUT: Check Qualifying and Admission Marks

The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati has announced the results for GATE 2026, alongside the crucial qualifying cutoffs for various engineering disciplines. These scores are vital for students aspiring to pursue M.Tech programs or secure positions in Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs).

## Key Takeaways

*   **Results and Cutoffs Released:** IIT Guwahati has published the GATE 2026 results and the official qualifying marks across all branches.
*   **Access Your Score:** Candidates can view their individual GATE scores and results on the official GATE Online Application Processing System (GOAPS) portal using their enrollment ID and password.
*   **Eligibility for Counselling:** Achieving a score equal to or above the declared qualifying cutoff makes candidates eligible to participate in the M.Tech counselling processes conducted via COAP and CCMT.
*   **Understanding Cutoffs:** The cutoff represents the minimum marks required to be considered a 'qualified' candidate, making you eligible for further admission processes.

## Understanding GATE Cutoff Marks

The GATE cutoff is essentially the minimum score a candidate must achieve to be declared qualified in the examination. This qualifying mark varies by branch and category (General, OBC-NCL/EWS, SC/ST/PwD). It's important to differentiate between the 'qualifying cutoff' and the 'admission cutoff'. While the qualifying cutoff makes you eligible, the admission cutoff for specific IITs, NITs, or PSUs will often be significantly higher, depending on competition and seat availability.

## Counselling Process for Qualified Candidates

Candidates who have successfully met the GATE 2026 qualifying cutoff marks are eligible to participate in the centralized counselling processes. For M.Tech admissions to IITs, the Common Offer Acceptance Portal (COAP) will manage the application and offer acceptance. For NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs, the Centralized Counselling for M.Tech/M.Arch/M.Plan (CCMT) will be the platform. The official COAP and CCMT portals will provide detailed schedules and guidelines for registration and participation. It is crucial to monitor these platforms for updates on counselling dates, which are expected to begin in May.

## How to Check Your GATE 2026 Result

To access your GATE 2026 score, visit the official GOAPS portal. You will need your enrollment ID and password to log in and view your detailed scorecard, which includes your raw marks, GATE score, and qualifying status. Along with the individual results, IIT Guwahati has also released a list of toppers for GATE 2026, showcasing outstanding performances across various disciplines.

Keep an eye on the official JoSAA portal for updates regarding IIT admissions and the CCMT portal for NIT, IIIT, and GFTI admissions once counselling begins.
