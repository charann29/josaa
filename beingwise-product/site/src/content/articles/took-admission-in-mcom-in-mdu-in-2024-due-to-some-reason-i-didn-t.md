# Your M.Com. Admission at MDU: What Happens Next for Your B.Com. Migration

It sounds like you had a challenging experience with your M.Com. admission at Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU) in 2024. When you don't complete your studies by missing exams and not paying subsequent fees, your enrollment status at the university changes significantly.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your M.Com. admission likely stands cancelled due to non-payment of fees and non-appearance in exams.
*   This cancellation of your M.Com. program does not affect your previously earned B.Com. degree from MDU.
*   You are eligible to obtain a Migration Certificate based on your MDU B.Com. degree.
*   Always confirm your official status by checking the MDU student portal or contacting the university directly.

## Understanding Your M.Com. Status

When a student takes admission to a program like M.Com. at MDU but then doesn't appear for exams for all semesters and fails to pay the fees for the subsequent year, the university generally considers this a withdrawal from the program. According to MDU's regulations, such non-continuation typically leads to the cancellation of your admission. Your name would likely be removed from the university's active student rolls for that specific M.Com. program.

### Re-admission Possibilities

MDU does have provisions for re-admission in certain circumstances. Often, a student might be allowed to re-join a program if they apply within a specific timeframe, pay a re-admission fee, and meet any other conditions like attendance requirements. However, if the stipulated re-admission window has passed, or if the conditions were not met, then re-admission to the same M.Com. session might no longer be an option. In such cases, you would generally need to apply for fresh admission to the M.Com. program in a future academic session if you wish to pursue it again.

## Your B.Com. Degree and Migration Certificate Eligibility

Crucially, the cancellation of your M.Com. admission does not impact your previously awarded B.Com. degree from MDU. Your B.Com. qualification is a separate, completed academic achievement. Therefore, you are absolutely eligible to obtain a Migration Certificate from MDU based on your B.Com. degree. A Migration Certificate is issued by a university to students who have completed a program and wish to pursue further studies at a different university. It confirms that you have completed your degree and are free to transfer to another institution.

## Next Steps: Confirming Your Status and Applying for Migration

To get a definitive understanding of your M.Com. status and to initiate the process for your B.Com. Migration Certificate, here's what you should do:

1.  **Check Your MDU Student Portal:** Log in to your MDU student portal. This is often the quickest way to see your official enrollment status for the M.Com. program and any outstanding fees or remarks.
2.  **Contact the University:** Reach out to the MDU Registrar's Office or the college office where you took admission for M.Com. They can provide precise information on your M.Com. admission cancellation and guide you on the process for obtaining your B.Com. Migration Certificate. Be prepared to provide your B.Com. enrollment details and any M.Com. admission reference numbers you might have.
3.  **Apply for Migration Certificate:** Once you confirm your B.Com. details, you can apply for the Migration Certificate. This process usually involves filling out a form, submitting relevant documents (like your B.Com. degree and mark sheets), and paying a nominal fee. The MDU official website or the Registrar's office will provide the exact procedure and required documents.

It's always best to clarify directly with the university to ensure you have the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding your academic records.
