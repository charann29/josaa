# Working with Tally.ERP9 - Level 1 by NIIT: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals looking to build essential skills in financial accounting software, the 'Working with Tally.ERP9 – Level 1' course by NIIT offers a focused learning path. This program is designed to equip participants with practical knowledge of Tally.ERP9, a widely used tool in businesses for managing accounts and inventory.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a classroom-based program focusing on practical application of Tally.ERP9.
*   The total duration of the course is 108 hours, delivered through instructor-led sessions.
*   Participants will gain skills in basic and advanced accounting, inventory management, and understanding business processes.
*   Successful completion leads to a graded certificate from NIIT, with a participation certificate available for others.

## Course Overview

This short-term course falls under the banking and finance category, providing comprehensive training in computerised accounting using Tally.ERP9. The curriculum is delivered through a blend of expert-led instruction and hands-on practice with real-time data sets. Beyond just software proficiency, the program also introduces participants to various business processes and the importance of information systems in an organisational context.

### What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, you will develop proficiency in several key areas, including:

*   Recording financial transactions accurately.
*   Utilising information systems effectively.
*   Fundamentals of inventory and accounting.
*   Understanding diverse business processes.
*   Advanced inventory and accounting concepts, including VAT.
*   Practical application skills for Tally.ERP9.
*   Core principles of business processes.

## Duration and Learning Mode

The 'Working with Tally.ERP9 – Level 1' course is an intensive program spanning 108 hours. It follows a classroom-based learning model, meaning students attend physical classes. The instruction medium is English, and the delivery combines video and text-based materials, complemented by direct interaction with faculty and practical exercises.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for this NIIT program, applicants should be commerce graduates. A strong command of English is necessary, as classroom interactions and materials are in English. Additionally, candidates should have a basic working knowledge of the Windows operating environment.

### Certification Details

NIIT awards a graded certificate to participants who achieve a score of 50% or more (CWAP - Continuous Work and Appraisal Performance) in the program. Those who do not appear for the appraisal or score less than 50% will receive a participation certificate.

## Course Structure

The program is divided into three main modules designed to build skills progressively:

*   **Business Process Fundamentals:** This module lays the groundwork by explaining essential business operations.
*   **Accounting on Computers with Tally.ERP 9 - Volume 1: Basic Accounting & Inventory:** Focuses on the foundational aspects of Tally.ERP9 for managing basic accounts and inventory.
*   **Accounting on Computers with Tally.ERP 9 - Volume 2: Advanced Accounting, Inventory, & VAT:** Delves into more complex accounting scenarios, advanced inventory management, and Value Added Tax (VAT) within Tally.ERP9.

## How to Apply

To register for the 'Working with Tally.ERP9 – Level 1' course, you will need to complete a simple online registration process. This typically involves visiting the official NIIT website, locating the specific course page, and clicking on the 'Register' button. You will then be prompted to fill in your personal details, such as your first name, last name, an active email ID, and a mobile number. An OTP (One-Time Password) verification might be required to confirm your details before submitting the registration form.

This course is an excellent opportunity for commerce graduates to gain industry-relevant skills in Tally.ERP9, enhancing their career prospects in accounting and finance roles.
