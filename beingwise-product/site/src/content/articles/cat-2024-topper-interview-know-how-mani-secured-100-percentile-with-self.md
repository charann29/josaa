# CAT 2024 Topper Interview: Know How Mani Secured 100 Percentile with Self Study

IIM Calcutta announced the CAT 2024 results on December 19, revealing a select group of high achievers. Among them was Mani Praneeth Chilukuri, an IIT Bombay alumnus, who distinguished himself by scoring a perfect 100 percentile. Mani, who is targeting top-tier institutions like the ABC IIMs or the Young Leaders Programme at ISB Hyderabad, shared insights into his entirely self-driven preparation strategy.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Self-Study Success:** Mani's journey proves that dedicated self-study can lead to a 100 percentile score, eliminating the need for external coaching.
*   **Strategic Sectional Focus:** He emphasized a balanced approach to all sections, with particular attention to areas needing improvement.
*   **Mock Test Mastery:** Regular and analytical engagement with mock tests was crucial for understanding exam patterns and refining his strategy.
*   **Targeted Practice:** Consistent practice, especially for challenging sections like Data Interpretation and Logical Reasoning (DILR), was a cornerstone of his plan.

## Mani Praneeth Chilukuri: At a Glance

Mani Praneeth Chilukuri, a BTech graduate in Computer Science Engineering from IIT Bombay, achieved a remarkable 100 percentile in CAT 2024. He was one of 14 candidates nationwide to reach this pinnacle. His sectional scores were impressive across the board:

| Section | Percentile Score |
| :------ | :--------------- |
| Overall | 100              |
| VARC    | 98.72            |
| DILR    | 99.99            |
| QA      | 99.98            |

## The Self-Study Advantage

Mani's success story is particularly inspiring for those considering self-preparation. He attributes his achievement to a disciplined approach that involved understanding the CAT syllabus thoroughly, practicing consistently, and critically evaluating his performance in mock tests. He did not rely on any coaching institute, instead crafting a study plan tailored to his strengths and weaknesses.

## Mastering Mock Tests

Central to Mani's strategy was his rigorous engagement with mock tests. He didn't just take mocks; he meticulously analyzed each one. This involved reviewing every question, understanding why an answer was correct or incorrect, and identifying recurring patterns in his mistakes. This analytical approach helped him refine his time management skills and develop a robust exam-day strategy.

## Section-Wise Preparation Strategy

*   **Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC):** Mani focused on improving his reading speed and comprehension through extensive reading of diverse articles and editorials. He also practiced different types of VARC questions to familiarize himself with the nuances of the section.
*   **Data Interpretation and Logical Reasoning (DILR):** Acknowledging DILR as often the most challenging section, Mani dedicated significant time to practicing a wide variety of sets. He emphasized understanding the underlying logic rather than just memorizing solution methods.
*   **Quantitative Ability (QA):** For QA, his approach involved solidifying fundamental concepts and then practicing advanced problems. He focused on accuracy and speed, ensuring he could solve problems efficiently under timed conditions.

## CAT 2024 Topper Statistics

The CAT 2024 results, as released by IIM Calcutta, highlighted some interesting trends. Out of approximately 2.93 lakh candidates who appeared for the exam, 14 achieved a perfect 100 percentile. Among these top scorers, 13 were male and 1 was female. The majority of 100 percentile achievers (13 out of 14) came from an engineering background. These toppers hailed from various states, with Maharashtra and Telangana having multiple candidates in this elite group.

Mani Praneeth's journey underscores that with a clear strategy, consistent effort, and smart self-study, a perfect CAT score is an achievable goal.
