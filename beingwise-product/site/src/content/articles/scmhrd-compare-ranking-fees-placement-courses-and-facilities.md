# SCMHRD: Compare Ranking, Fees, Placement, Courses & Facilities @

Symbiosis Centre for Management & Human Resource Development (SCMHRD), located in Pune and part of Symbiosis International, is a well-regarded institution for management education. This page provides a comprehensive overview of SCMHRD, covering its programs, costs, career opportunities, and campus environment to help you make an informed decision.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Established Legacy:** SCMHRD was founded in 1993, offering decades of experience in management education.
*   **Diverse MBA Programs:** The institute provides a range of MBA specializations, including HR, Marketing, Finance, and Operations, alongside an MBA in Business Analytics.
*   **Strong Placement Record:** SCMHRD consistently achieves impressive placement outcomes, with a notable median salary reported for its MBA graduates.
*   **Accredited Institution:** The college holds an A++ accreditation, reflecting its commitment to quality education.

## Understanding SCMHRD's Programs and Fees

SCMHRD primarily focuses on postgraduate management programs. The flagship offering is the Master of Business Administration (MBA). As per official information, the total tuition fees for the MBA/PGDM programs generally range from approximately ₹6.22 Lakhs to ₹24.6 Lakhs, depending on the specific specialization and year of admission. These programs typically span two years.

Admission to SCMHRD's MBA programs requires candidates to take the Symbiosis National Aptitude (SNAP) Test.

## Placement Success and Return on Investment (ROI)

SCMHRD has a strong track record when it comes to student placements. For its MBA/PGDM programs, the median salary for graduating students has been reported to be between ₹13.6 Lakhs and ₹20 Lakhs. This robust placement performance highlights the potential return on investment for students pursuing their management education at SCMHRD.

## Campus Life and Facilities

The campus environment at SCMHRD is designed to support a holistic learning experience. Students have access to a variety of facilities that contribute to both academic and personal growth. These include multiple computer labs, with specialized facilities for software like SAP and Oracle, indicating a strong emphasis on practical, industry-relevant skills.

Beyond academics, the campus offers essential amenities such as Wi-Fi connectivity, a well-stocked library, a health centre, and areas dedicated to fitness, including a gym, yoga space, and Zumba classes. There are also open sports areas, promoting physical well-being and recreation. These facilities are generally well-maintained, contributing to a positive campus experience.

## Student Perspectives and Overall Rating

Based on feedback from verified students, SCMHRD has received an average rating of 4.0 out of 5 stars. This rating reflects various aspects of the institution, including:

*   **Placements:** Rated at 4.0
*   **Infrastructure:** Rated at 3.7
*   **Faculty & Course:** Rated at 3.9
*   **Campus Life:** Rated at 4.3
*   **Value for Money:** Rated at 4.1

These ratings suggest that students generally find the placements and campus life to be strong points, while infrastructure and faculty also receive positive, though slightly lower, scores.

SCMHRD offers a compelling option for those aspiring to pursue management education, backed by its established reputation, diverse programs, and promising career opportunities.
