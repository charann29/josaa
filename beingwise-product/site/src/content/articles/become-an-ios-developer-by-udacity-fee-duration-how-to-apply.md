# Become an iOS Developer by Udacity: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Are you looking to dive into the world of Apple app development? Udacity's iOS Developer Nanodegree program offers a comprehensive pathway to mastering Swift programming and creating applications for Apple's ecosystem.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a five-month online program designed for self-paced learning, requiring about 10 hours of study per week.
*   You'll gain practical experience by completing six real-world projects, with personalized feedback from industry experts.
*   The program covers everything from Swift fundamentals to advanced app features like networking and data persistence.
*   Upon successful completion of all projects, you will receive a certification from Udacity.

## Program Overview

This certification course guides you through the essential skills needed to become an iOS developer. You'll begin with the Swift programming language and Apple's Xcode development environment. The curriculum is structured to help you build your first iOS applications using fundamental tools like UIKit, layouts, and views. As you progress, you'll tackle more complex applications, incorporating networking features, Apple's Grand Central Dispatch, and Core Data for robust data management. The journey culminates in a capstone project, preparing you to potentially publish your own app to the App Store.

The program emphasizes developing secure and efficient applications, focusing on the unique strengths of the iOS platform. It's designed to enhance your programming abilities and provide you with the expertise to create sophisticated mobile software.

## What You Will Learn

The iOS Developer program is structured to upgrade your skills in several key areas:

*   **Swift Programming Fundamentals:** Master the core concepts of Swift, the modern programming language for Apple platforms, essential for building robust applications.
*   **iOS Application Development:** Learn to develop applications specifically for Apple's diverse environment, understanding its unique architecture and guidelines.
*   **UIKit Essentials:** Gain proficiency in UIKit, the framework for constructing fast and intuitive user interfaces for iOS apps.
*   **Data Persistence:** Understand how to manage and navigate data within your applications, ensuring information remains consistent and accessible even when your app's data model changes.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

One of the program's significant advantages is its inclusive eligibility criteria. There are no specific educational background requirements, nor is prior work experience necessary. This makes the program accessible to a wide audience:

*   **Experience Level:** Both beginners with no prior programming knowledge and individuals with existing programming skills are welcome to apply.
*   **Educational Background:** Candidates from any academic discipline can enroll. The primary requirement is an interest in mobile application development, particularly for the iOS platform.

To earn the Udacity certification, participants must successfully complete and submit six assigned projects within the program's duration, typically six months. Failure to submit or meet the project requirements may necessitate redoing the work, as certification is contingent upon successful project completion.

## Course Fee and Certification

The total fee for the iOS Developer Nanodegree program is ₹69,700. This fee grants you access to all course materials, program offerings, and support services. Upon successfully completing all six required projects, you will receive an official certification directly from Udacity, validating your newly acquired skills as an iOS developer.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Udacity iOS Developer Nanodegree is a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official Udacity website for the iOS Developer Nanodegree program.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Enroll Now' option on the program webpage.
3.  Complete the application form with your details.

This program is an excellent opportunity for anyone aspiring to build a career in iOS development, from those just starting out to junior-level developers looking to enhance their expertise.
