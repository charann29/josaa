# IMU CET Exam 2026 - Date (Out), Registration (Open), Syllabus, Sample Papers, Result, Cutoff

The Indian Maritime University Common Entrance Test (IMU CET) is a national-level examination for admission to a variety of maritime undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This guide offers essential information for aspiring students, covering key dates, eligibility, and the application process for the 2026 academic session.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Registration is open:** The application window for IMU CET 2026 is currently open until April 24, 2026.
*   **Exam Date:** The IMU CET 2026 entrance examination is scheduled for May 24, 2026.
*   **Eligibility:** Candidates need at least 60% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) and 50% in English in their 10+2 examinations, along with meeting the minimum age requirement of 17 years.
*   **Exam Format:** The test is a computer-based exam consisting of 200 multiple-choice questions (MCQs) to be completed in three hours.

## What is IMU CET?

IMU CET is an annual computer-based test conducted by the Indian Maritime University (IMU) to select candidates for admission to its six campuses and affiliated institutes. This entrance exam is crucial for those seeking to pursue careers in marine engineering, nautical science, naval architecture, shipbuilding, and ocean engineering at diploma, undergraduate, and postgraduate levels. IMU also offers management programs in maritime logistics.

While some courses may consider scores from other national exams like GATE or CUET, preference is generally given to candidates who appear for and perform well in IMU CET. All admissions are merit-based and processed through IMU's online counselling system.

## Eligibility Criteria for IMU CET 2026

To be eligible for IMU CET 2026, candidates must fulfill specific academic and age requirements:

*   **Academic Qualification:** Applicants must have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination.
*   **Minimum Marks:** A minimum of 60% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) is required. Additionally, candidates must have scored at least 50% in English in their 10+2 or 10th standard examinations.
*   **Age Limit:** The minimum age to apply is 17 years.

## IMU CET 2026 Application Process

The application process for IMU CET 2026 is entirely online. Interested candidates can apply by visiting the official IMU website. It is important to complete the application form accurately and submit it before the deadline of April 24, 2026.

## IMU CET 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

Understanding the exam structure and content is vital for effective preparation. The IMU CET 2026 will follow these guidelines:

### Exam Pattern

*   **Mode:** Computer-Based Test (CBT)
*   **Duration:** 3 hours
*   **Total Questions:** 200 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Sections:** The paper will comprise questions from Physics (50), Mathematics (50), Chemistry (25), English (25), General Knowledge (25), and General Aptitude (25).

### Syllabus

The syllabus for IMU CET primarily aligns with the Class 11 and 12 CBSE/State Board curriculum for Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Candidates should also prepare for sections on English, General Knowledge, and General Aptitude.

## IMU CET 2026 Sample Papers and Preparation

Practicing with sample papers and mock tests is highly recommended for IMU CET preparation. While official sample papers might be available on the IMU portal, candidates can also refer to previous year's question papers to understand the question types and difficulty level. Focusing on conceptual clarity for PCM subjects and regularly updating general knowledge will be beneficial.

## IMU CET 2026 Result and Counselling

Following the examination, IMU will announce the results. Successful candidates will then be invited for the online counselling process, where they can choose their preferred courses and campuses based on their IMU CET rank and seat availability.

Prepare thoroughly and stay informed through official IMU announcements to navigate your path to a career in the maritime sector.
