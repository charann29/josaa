# Big Data Hadoop and Spark with Scala by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This certification course offers a deep dive into the world of Big Data, covering essential tools and frameworks like Hadoop and Spark, with a focus on Scala programming. It's designed for individuals looking to build or enhance their skills in big data development and analytics.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** The course covers both foundational and advanced aspects of the Hadoop ecosystem, NoSQL databases, and Spark with Scala.
*   **Flexible Learning:** As a self-paced online program, it offers full lifetime access to course materials, allowing students to learn at their own convenience.
*   **Practical Focus:** The syllabus includes numerous practical sessions to ensure hands-on experience with the tools and concepts taught.
*   **Certification:** Upon successful completion, learners receive a certificate, validating their acquired skills in Big Data technologies.

## Course Overview

Developed by Harish Masand, a Technical Lead, this online program is structured to provide a thorough understanding of Big Data Hadoop, Spark, and Scala. It explores the intricate ecosystem surrounding these technologies, including Hadoop Distributed File System (HDFS), YARN, Map-Reduce, Pig, Hive, and Oozie. The course also delves into modern aspects like NoSQL databases, HBase, Sqoop, Flume, and Spark SQL, preparing students for real-world big data challenges.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for aspiring and current professionals in various data-centric roles. It particularly benefits:

*   Big Data Developers
*   Big Data Analytics Engineers
*   Data Engineers
*   Big Data Engineers

The content is tailored to equip these individuals with the necessary skills to design, implement, and manage big data solutions effectively.

## Course Content and Structure

The learning experience is delivered through 44.5 hours of on-demand video content, supplemented by one article and downloadable resources. The medium of instruction is English, and the learning mode is self-study, making it accessible on both mobile devices and televisions. A key feature is the full lifetime access to the course content.
