# Top IIMs in India 2026: Rankings, Fees, Admission, Courses, Seat Intake, Cutoff, Placements

The Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) are premier government-owned business schools offering top-tier management education. Aspiring students often target these institutions for their rigorous academic programs and excellent career prospects. This guide provides an overview of the leading IIMs for the 2026 admission cycle.

## Key takeaways

*   There are 21 IIMs across India, all publicly funded.
*   The Common Admission Test (CAT) is the primary entrance exam for most IIM programs.
*   Leading IIMs consistently feature high in national rankings, known for their strong placements.
*   Annual tuition fees for most IIMs exceed INR 5 Lakh, with several charging upwards of INR 20-25 Lakh for their flagship MBA programs.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to IIMs is highly competitive. The primary route is through the CAT exam, conducted by one of the IIMs on a rotational basis. Beyond the CAT score, institutes also consider academic performance, work experience, and performance in subsequent rounds like Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI).

### Eligibility Criteria

Generally, candidates must meet the following basic requirements:

*   Hold a Bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks (or an equivalent CGPA). A relaxation may apply for reserved categories.
*   Final year degree students are also eligible to apply, provided they meet the minimum aggregate marks by a specified date.
*   A valid score in the CAT exam is mandatory.

## Fee Structure and Popular Courses

The tuition fees for flagship MBA/PGP programs at IIMs can vary significantly. While some IIMs offer programs for around INR 14 Lakh, many of the top-tier institutes have fees in the range of INR 24-25 Lakh for their two-year courses. These fees typically cover tuition, but living expenses are usually extra.

IIMs offer a diverse range of specializations within their management programs, including Finance, Human Resources, Operations, Sales & Marketing, and IT & Systems, among others.

## Placements and Career Prospects

IIMs are renowned for their strong placement records, attracting top companies from various sectors. Students often secure roles in consulting, finance, marketing, operations, and general management with attractive salary packages. While specific figures vary by institute and year, the return on investment for an IIM degree is generally considered high due to excellent career opportunities.

Choosing the right IIM involves careful consideration of rankings, fees, course offerings, and personal career aspirations. We encourage you to visit the official websites of individual IIMs for the most current and detailed information.
