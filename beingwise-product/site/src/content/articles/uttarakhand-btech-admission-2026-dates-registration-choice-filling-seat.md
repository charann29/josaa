# Uttarakhand BTech Admission 2026: Dates, Registration, Choice Filling, Seat Allotment, Selection Criteria

Uttarakhand offers a streamlined process for B.Tech admissions, managed by Veer Madho Singh Bhandari Uttarakhand Technical University, Dehradun. This guide provides a clear overview of the key steps for aspiring engineers.

## Key takeaways

*   **Centralised Online Process:** All admissions for B.Tech in Uttarakhand are handled through a single online counselling system.
*   **No State Entrance Exam:** Admissions rely on JEE Main scores, with Class 12 marks considered next.
*   **Priority for JEE Main:** Candidates with JEE Main ranks are given preference during seat allotment.
*   **Key Dates in June & July:** Registration, choice filling, and seat allotment typically occur during these months.

## Understanding Uttarakhand B.Tech Admission Counselling

The admission process for Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) programs in Uttarakhand is a centralised online counselling system. This process is organised by Veer Madho Singh Bhandari Uttarakhand Technical University, which was formerly known as Uttarakhand Technical University (UTU). It facilitates admissions to the first year of B.Tech courses in the university's own departments, campus institutions, affiliated government and private institutes across Uttarakhand, and also includes the College of Technology, G.B. Pant University of Agriculture and Technology, Pantnagar.

Seat allotment is primarily based on a candidate's JEE Main rank. If seats remain, Class 12 marks are then taken into consideration.

## Eligibility and Selection Criteria for B.Tech Admission

To be eligible for B.Tech admission in Uttarakhand, candidates must meet specific academic requirements. The primary criteria for selection are:

*   **JEE Main Score:** Performance in the JEE Main examination is the foremost consideration for seat allotment. This national-level engineering entrance exam is conducted by NTA.
*   **Class 12 Marks:** For candidates who do not have a JEE Main score, or after all JEE Main-qualified candidates have been considered, Class 12 marks are used for admission.

It is important to note that the university does not conduct its own entrance examination for B.Tech admissions. The detailed eligibility criteria, including minimum marks in qualifying examinations, will be specified in the official information brochure released by the university.

## The Online Counselling Process

The Uttarakhand B.Tech counselling process is entirely online and involves several key stages:

### Registration

Interested candidates must register on the official website, uktech.ac.in, to participate in the counselling. This registration typically begins in the last week of June. During registration, applicants will need to provide their personal and academic details.

### Choice Filling

After successful registration, candidates will proceed to fill their choices of colleges and B.Tech courses. It is advisable to research participating institutes and their programs thoroughly beforehand. The order of preferences is critical, as seat allotment will be done based on these choices, combined with eligibility and merit.

### Seat Allotment

Seats are allotted based on the candidate's JEE Main rank or Class 12 marks, along with the choices they have filled. Candidates with JEE Main scores are given priority. Once a seat is allotted, candidates will have options like 'Freeze,' 'Float,' or 'Withdraw,' similar to the JoSAA counselling process. 'Freeze' means accepting the allotted seat, 'Float' means accepting the seat but hoping for an upgrade in subsequent rounds, and 'Withdraw' means declining the seat and exiting the counselling process.

### Reporting to Allotted Institute

Candidates who accept an allotted seat must complete the admission formalities by reporting to their respective institutes within the stipulated timeframe. This involves document verification and fee payment.

This structured online process aims to ensure fair and transparent admissions for all eligible candidates.
