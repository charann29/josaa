# Fashion Fundamentals by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to step into the vibrant world of fashion, The Designer's Class offers an online program called "Fashion Fundamentals." This course is designed to build a strong foundational understanding of fashion, from its creative aspects to the business side.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Beginner-Friendly:** This program is specifically created for individuals new to the fashion industry or fashion business.
*   **Comprehensive Overview:** It covers fashion as an art, a cultural phenomenon, a historical journey, and a business venture.
*   **Expert-Led:** The course is developed and taught by acclaimed designer Payal Singhal.
*   **Flexible Learning:** As an online, self-paced course, it allows learners to study at their own convenience.

## Who is This Course For?

The "Fashion Fundamentals" course is ideal for aspiring designers, fashion enthusiasts, and anyone considering a career in the fashion industry. It caters specifically to beginners, providing a structured introduction to both the creative and commercial elements of fashion. The program aims to equip students with a solid understanding of how designers operate within the broader fashion landscape.

## Course Content and Structure

This online certification program delves into various crucial aspects of fashion. Students will gain insights into the core theories of fashion, exploring its deep connections with art and culture. The curriculum also covers the contemporary fashion industry, the design process, different fashion materials, and the essential business side of fashion.

Here’s a glimpse of the topics covered:

*   Introduction to Designer Payal Singhal
*   Introduction to Fashion Fundamentals
*   History of Art and Fashion
*   Fashion and Society
*   Film, Popular Media, and Fashion
*   Design Process
*   Fashion Material
*   Business of Fashion
*   Final Assessment

The course includes downloadable resources and assignments to reinforce learning. A unique feature is a workbook provided to participants, which helps deepen understanding through external internet resources, various activities, and multiple-choice questions at the end of each section.

## Learning Experience and Duration

The "Fashion Fundamentals" course is delivered entirely online through video and text-based modules, allowing for self-paced study. The content is available in both English and Hindi. While the core learning material can be completed in approximately 1 hour and 9 minutes, participants receive one year of access to all course resources, enabling them to revisit topics and complete assignments at their leisure. There are 6 downloadable resources and 8 assignments included to enhance the learning experience.

## Fees and Application Process

The fee for the "Fashion Fundamentals" online course is ₹2,999. Interested individuals can enroll in the program by paying this fee. Since it's an online course offered by The Designer's Class, the application process is straightforward, typically involving direct enrollment through their platform after payment. It's important to note that while the course provides valuable learning and certification of completion, it does not issue an academic degree.

This program offers a fantastic opportunity to gain foundational knowledge and practical insights into the dynamic world of fashion from an industry expert.
