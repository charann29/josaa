# Building AI Applications with Watson APIs by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program, developed by IBM and offered through Coursera, provides a practical pathway for individuals to gain expertise in building artificial intelligence applications. It focuses specifically on leveraging IBM's Watson AI services to create intelligent and interactive systems.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn to integrate multiple Watson AI services like Discovery, Assistant, Speech to Text, and Text to Speech into your applications.
*   Develop skills in creating interactive data retrieval systems by combining Watson Discovery and Watson Assistant.
*   The course is self-paced, entirely online, and typically requires about 18 hours to complete.
*   Upon successful completion and payment, you will receive a shareable certificate from Coursera.

## What You Will Learn

This certification course is designed to equip you with practical skills in various AI and programming domains. You will delve into the nuances of chatbot development, understanding different options for user interaction. A significant portion of the program focuses on deploying these chatbots to popular platforms such as Slack and Facebook Messenger. Furthermore, you will gain hands-on experience with key Watson services, specifically Watson Discovery and Watson Assistant, learning how to effectively utilize them in your AI applications.

The curriculum covers essential concepts in:

*   Chatbot design and implementation
*   Watson AI services
*   Machine learning fundamentals
*   Python programming for AI
*   General artificial intelligence principles

## Course Structure and Content

The program is delivered through a combination of engaging videos, informative readings, practical exercises, and quizzes. This blended approach ensures a comprehensive learning experience, allowing you to grasp theoretical concepts and apply them practically.

Here’s a glimpse into the course syllabus:

### Week 1: Introduction and Creating a Chatbot

*   **Videos:** Introduction to building AI applications, course overview, and creating a chatbot using Watson Assistant.
*   **Readings:** General information, syllabus details, and an overview of technologies used.
*   **Practice:** A quiz to test your understanding of the introduction.

### Week 2: Giving it a Voice

*   **Videos:** Exploring voice options for chatbots and integrating Watson Speech Services.
*   **Practice:** A quiz focusing on Speech APIs.

### Week 5: (Optional) Enhancing your Chatbot with Discovery

*   **Videos:** Understanding Watson Discovery, creating a chatbot with Watson Assistant, and integrating Watson Assistant with Discovery.

## Fees and Certification

The fees for the Building AI Applications with Watson APIs certification are structured based on the duration of access you choose:

| Description     | Amount (INR) |
| :-------------- | :----------- |
| Fee for 1 month | Rs. 3,202/-  |
| Fee for 3 months | Rs. 6,404/-  |
| Fee for 6 months | Rs. 9,606/-  |

Upon successful completion of all assignments and payment of the applicable fees, you will be awarded a shareable certificate by Coursera. This certificate can be a valuable addition to your professional profile.

## How to Enroll

Enrolling in this program is a straightforward process:

1.  Begin by visiting the official program page on Coursera.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Enroll for Free' button, which will guide you through the sign-up process. You can create a login ID using your Google or Facebook account for convenience.
3.  Once logged in, a personalized dashboard will be created for you, granting immediate access to a 7-day free trial of the program content.
4.  After the free trial period concludes, you will need to make a monthly payment to continue accessing the course material.
5.  Select your preferred payment method to complete the transaction and gain full, uninterrupted access to the program.

This program is ideal for aspiring AI developers and IT engineers looking to enhance their skills in building intelligent applications using industry-leading IBM Watson APIs.
