# Best Free Data Analysis Courses & Certifications - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Career Options

Data analysis skills are becoming essential in almost every industry today. Fortunately, numerous high-quality, free online courses are available to help you build a strong foundation in this field, allowing you to explore career opportunities without an initial financial commitment.

## Key takeaways

*   Top institutions like Georgia Tech and IITs offer free online courses in data analysis.
*   These programs cover crucial skills such as programming, machine learning, operations research, and statistical analysis.
*   Many courses provide a free audit mode for learning, with optional paid certificates for verified achievement and graded assignments.
*   Certification often requires passing a proctored exam, enhancing the value of your credential.

## Computing for Data Analysis by Georgia Tech

This certification course, offered by the Georgia Institute of Technology through an online learning platform, provides an advanced introduction to data analytics. It uniquely blends computer science and mathematics, making it ideal for those who want to strengthen their understanding of data-intensive computing and numerical methods.

The program aims to develop a new way of thinking about core programming principles, which is directly applicable to modern data analysis and data mining practices. You'll gain hands-on experience with the entire data analysis pipeline, including data pre-processing, storage, analysis, and fundamental machine learning concepts. Basic mathematical tools like numerical linear algebra and algorithms are also covered to help you manage large datasets effectively.

Students can enroll in either a free audit mode, which offers limited-time access to course materials, or a paid mode that provides unlimited access and includes graded assignments for a verified certificate. The course duration is approximately 15 weeks.

**Skills Covered:** Data mining, machine learning, programming skills, data science knowledge.

## Data Analysis and Decision Making – I by IIT Kanpur

Offered by IIT Kanpur via Swayam and NPTEL, this certification course is the first in a three-part series focusing on Data Analysis and Decision Making (DADM-I, DADM-II, DADM-III). It primarily covers Operations Research and its practical applications. The DADM series emphasizes real-world applications over theoretical concepts.

The broader DADM curriculum addresses three main areas: operations research tools, multivariate statistical analysis, and various decision-making models like DEA, AHP, ANP, and TOPSIS. This particular course is application-oriented and aims to provide industry-relevant skills, with potential support from sectors such as chemical, cement, steel, and manufacturing industries.

This 12-week elective course is taught by Prof. Raghu Nandan Sengupta from IIT Kanpur. An e-certificate is provided upon successful completion, which typically involves registering for and passing a proctored exam conducted by Swayam at designated centers. This program is particularly suitable for learners with backgrounds in:

*   Masters in Statistics/Mathematics
*   Masters in Operations Research/Operations Management
*   Masters in Business Administration
*   Masters in Economics
*   Ph.D. in related fields

**Skills Covered:** Operations analysis.

## Data Analytics with Python by IIT Roorkee

This certification course from IIT Roorkee, available through Swayam and NPTEL, teaches you how to analyze data effectively using the Python programming language. It includes many practical examples of data analytics applications across various industrial sectors and even demonstrates how these skills can be applied in everyday life to boost career opportunities.

A significant feature of this program is the emphasis on hands-on experience, where you will build your own analytical models. A discussion forum is also available for students to clarify doubts and engage in academic conversations related to data analytics and Python. The course covers essential topics such as regression, probability, distributions, and hypothesis testing, providing a comprehensive understanding of data analysis techniques.
