# Latest Education News: Admissions, Exam Results, Board Exams, Admit Cards and Educational Events

Staying informed about the latest developments in Indian education is crucial for students and parents alike. This page provides a consolidated overview of key announcements, exam updates, and admission timelines to help you navigate your academic journey.

## Key takeaways

*   **Board Exam Results:** Major state board results, including MP Board and TS Inter, are being declared or are imminent for Class 10 and 12.
*   **Entrance Exam Updates:** Important engineering entrance exams like JEE Main, KCET, MHT CET, and BITSAT have recent updates regarding answer keys, admit cards, or ongoing exams.
*   **Admit Card Releases:** Several entrance exams, such as KCET, MET, and BITSAT, have recently released their admit cards.
*   **Application Deadlines:** Keep an eye on extended registration dates for various entrance tests, like TANCET, to ensure you don't miss out.

## Board Exam Results and Updates

Students awaiting their Class 10 and 12 results from various state boards have seen significant activity. The MP Board Class 10 and 12 results for 2026 are currently live, with students able to check their performance online. Similarly, the Andhra Pradesh Inter Results 2026 are also live, and the Telangana State Board of Intermediate Education (TGBIE) has confirmed the date and time for the TS Inter Results 2026 for both 1st and 2nd year, which are expected soon on their official portal, tgbie.cgg.gov.in.

For Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) students, the CBSE 10th Result 2026 live updates are available, guiding students on how to check their Class 10 results online via cbse.gov.in once released.

## Engineering Entrance Exam News

The engineering admissions landscape is dynamic, with several major exams seeing recent updates:

*   **JEE Main 2026:** The National Testing Agency (NTA) has released the answer key for JEE Main 2026 Session 2. Candidates can download their response sheets and challenge the answer key through the official jeemain.nta.nic.in website.
*   **KCET 2026:** Admit cards for the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) 2026 are now available for download. Students can obtain their hall tickets from the official cetonline.karnataka.gov.in portal.
*   **MHT CET 2026:** The Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test (MHT CET) for PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) group saw its April 11 exam conclude. Analysis of the question paper for both shifts is now available, helping students understand the exam's difficulty and expected cut-offs.
*   **BITSAT 2026:** Admit cards for the Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test (BITSAT) 2026 have been released. Candidates should download these from the official BITSAT portal.
*   **MET 2026:** The Manipal Entrance Test (MET) 2026 admit cards are also live, and students can download them to appear for the upcoming examination.

## Other Important Admissions and Exam Updates

Beyond the major engineering and board exams, there are other crucial updates:

*   **TANCET 2026 Registration:** The registration deadline for the Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test (TANCET) 2026, which is important for MBA and MCA admissions in Tamil Nadu, has been extended. Aspiring candidates can still apply online through the official tancet.annauniv.edu website.

Staying up-to-date with these announcements can significantly impact your academic planning and success. Always refer to the official websites and notifications for the most accurate and timely information.
