# Professional Certificate Course in Data Science by Newton School: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Data science is a rapidly expanding field, crucial in our data-rich world for extracting meaningful insights and driving informed decisions. The Professional Certificate Course in Data Science, offered by Newton School, provides a comprehensive online pathway for individuals looking to enter this dynamic domain.

## Key takeaways

*   This is an online, beginner-friendly program lasting 8-12 months.
*   No prior experience in data science or related fields is necessary for enrollment.
*   The course covers foundational mathematics, practical tools like Python, SQL, Excel, and advanced topics such as machine learning and deep learning.
*   Newton School offers placement assistance, including resume building and mock interviews, to help participants secure professional roles.

## Course Overview and Structure

This Professional Certificate Course is designed to give students a strong foundation in data interpretation, analysis, and manipulation. Delivered through live online classes, the program spans 8 to 12 months and is structured into eight modules. It starts with the very basics of data science, making it accessible even for those with no prior background in the field.

The curriculum is comprehensive, beginning with foundational mathematics, which is highly valued in the industry. As the course progresses, participants learn practical tools and languages essential for data analysis and visualization. This includes gaining proficiency in Excel, a widely used tool, and hands-on experience with key Python libraries such such as NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. SQL, a critical skill for business analysts, is also a core component. Furthermore, students are trained in popular data visualization tools like Power BI and Tableau, which many companies rely on.

Towards the end of the program, the focus shifts to more advanced topics, including machine learning and deep learning algorithms. Participants will learn to use tools like Scikit-learn for machine learning and TensorFlow and Keras for solving complex problems, such as weather prediction. The final module is dedicated to preparing students for placements.

## Program Offerings and Benefits

Newton School emphasizes practical career readiness through several key offerings:

*   **Placement Assistance:** Comprehensive support is provided, including help with resume building and mock interviews.
*   **Individual Sessions:** Opportunities for personalized guidance.
*   **Expert Faculty:** Learning from experienced professionals in the field.
*   **Live Online Sessions:** Interactive learning experience through real-time classes.

These elements are designed to not only equip participants with technical skills but also to enhance their employability and potential for salary growth in data science roles.

## Fees and Certification

The fees for the Professional Certificate Course in Data Science start with an EMI option of ₹6,378. Newton School also offers an early applicant concession of ₹10,000, making it more accessible for those who apply promptly.

Upon successful completion of the program, participants receive a certificate from Newton School, validating their new skills and knowledge in data science.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for individuals aspiring to enter the data science domain, regardless of their current professional background. It caters to those looking to transition into the field or enhance their existing skill set. After completing the course, participants will be prepared for various professional roles, including:

*   Data Scientist
*   Business Analyst
*   Data Analyst
*   Machine Learning Engineer

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Professional Certificate Course in Data Science, interested candidates should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official website of Newton School.
2.  Locate the section for the Professional Certificate Course in Data Science.
3.  Follow the instructions to complete the application process, which typically involves filling out an application form and potentially an initial assessment.

This structured approach ensures a smooth application process for all prospective students.

Embark on your data science journey with this certificate course and unlock new career opportunities in a booming industry.
