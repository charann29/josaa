# Association of Chartered Certified Accountants by Zell Education: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

The Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) qualification is a globally recognized credential for aspiring finance professionals. This program, offered in collaboration with Zell Education, prepares students for a career in accountancy, valid in numerous countries worldwide.

## Key Takeaways

*   The ACCA qualification is a global certification, often referred to as "Global CA," recognized in over 180 countries.
*   The program is delivered online, offering flexibility with self-study and virtual classroom options.
*   It involves completing 13 papers across three distinct levels, with an average duration of 6 to 24 months.
*   Eligibility generally requires strong academic performance in 10+2, with an alternative entry route available for other candidates.

## Understanding the ACCA Qualification

ACCA is a prestigious professional accounting qualification granted by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants, a UK-based global body. It is designed to provide students with comprehensive knowledge in accounting, finance, and business management, enabling them to work as chartered certified accountants internationally. The curriculum covers essential areas such as taxation, auditing, financial reporting, financial management, and professional ethics.

## Program Structure and Duration

The ACCA program is structured into three levels, requiring candidates to pass a total of 13 papers. The levels are:

1.  **Knowledge Level:** Focuses on foundational accounting principles.
2.  **Skills Level:** Builds upon the knowledge level with more advanced technical skills.
3.  **Professional Level:** Addresses strategic and leadership aspects of finance.

The typical duration for completing the entire qualification ranges from 6 to 24 months, depending on the student's pace and chosen study mode. Exams are conducted quarterly, allowing for regular progress.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for direct registration with ACCA, students typically need to have passed their 10+2 examinations with a minimum of 65% in Mathematics/Accounts and English, and at least 50% in other subjects.

For students who have recently completed Class 10 or do not meet the direct entry criteria, there is an alternative pathway. They can enroll through the Foundation in Accountancy (FIA) route, which provides a stepping stone to the main ACCA qualification.

## Course Delivery and Learning Experience

This ACCA program is delivered entirely online, offering a blend of self-study and virtual classroom learning. The instruction medium is English, utilizing video and text-based materials. Students benefit from pre-recorded lectures, extensive live question-solving sessions, and live lectures. The program also includes unit and mock tests for practice, dedicated student mentorship, and doubt-solving support. Zell Education, in collaboration with F2F Mumbai, a Platinum Approved Learning Partner, ensures a high-quality learning experience.

## Fees Structure

The fees for the ACCA program vary based on the chosen study level (Knowledge, Skills, or Professional) and the learning methodology (Pro, Premium, or Lite). Here is an overview of the fee structure:

| Offerings           | Knowledge Level | Skill Level | Professional Level |
| :------------------ | :-------------- | :---------- | :----------------- |
| Pro (Weekday)       | Rs. 1,26,000    | Rs. 1,57,500 | -                 |
| Premium (Weekend)   | Rs. 86,100      | Rs. 1,26,000 | Rs. 1,22,850       |
| Lite (Self-Paced)   | Rs. 52,000      | Rs. 67,500  | Rs. 56,000         |

*Note: These fees are subject to change. Candidates should confirm the latest fee details with Zell Education.* 

Upon successful completion of all 13 examinations across the three levels with a minimum passing score, eligible candidates will receive a course completion certificate from Zell Education, acknowledging their achievement in the ACCA program.
