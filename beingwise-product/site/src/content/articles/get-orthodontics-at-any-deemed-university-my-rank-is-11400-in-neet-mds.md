# Can I get orthodontics at any Deemed University? My rank is 11400 in NEET MDS 2021.

Securing a Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) seat, especially in a sought-after specialty like Orthodontics, requires careful planning and understanding of the admission process. Let's explore your options for Deemed Universities with a NEET MDS 2021 rank of 11400.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your rank of 11400 in NEET MDS 2021 presents a moderate chance for securing an MDS Orthodontics seat in Deemed Universities.
*   Admission to Deemed Universities typically involves counseling rounds managed by the Medical Counselling Committee (MCC).
*   Cut-offs can fluctuate year to year, but historical data suggests possibilities in later counseling rounds.
*   Look into colleges like Sri Siddhartha Dental College, DY Patil University School of Dentistry, and SRM Dental College, which have previously offered seats in this range.

## Understanding NEET MDS Ranks and Deemed University Admissions

The NEET MDS examination is the gateway for admissions to MDS courses across India. Your rank of 11400 indicates your position among all candidates nationwide. For Deemed Universities, the admission process is handled centrally by the Medical Counselling Committee (MCC), which conducts multiple rounds of counseling. Unlike government colleges where cut-offs are generally much higher, Deemed Universities often have a wider range of ranks that can secure admission, especially in later rounds.

## Exploring Cut-offs and Historical Trends

While specific cut-offs vary each year based on factors like the number of applicants, seat availability, and candidate preferences, past trends offer valuable insights. Historically, some Deemed Universities have seen MDS Orthodontics cut-offs extend to ranks in the 7000-8000 range, and sometimes even higher, particularly in the second or third rounds of counseling. Your rank of 11400 means you might be on the borderline or just outside this range for the initial rounds, but definitely within contention for subsequent rounds or mop-up rounds if seats remain vacant.

## Potential Deemed Universities for Orthodontics

Several Deemed Universities are well-regarded for their MDS programs, including Orthodontics. Based on previous admission cycles, some institutions that you might consider and monitor during counseling include:

*   **Sri Siddhartha Dental College, Tumkur:** Known for its dental programs, it's often a college where seats might be available in later rounds for various specialties.
*   **DY Patil University School of Dentistry, Navi Mumbai:** This institution is a popular choice and has a comprehensive dental faculty. Keep an eye on its seat matrix during counseling.
*   **SRM Dental College, Kattankulathur:** Part of a larger university, SRM Dental College also offers MDS programs and could be a potential option.

It's crucial to refer to the official MCC website for the most up-to-date information on seat availability, college lists, and cut-offs during the counseling process. Do not rely solely on historical data; always check the current year's announcements.

## Navigating the Counseling Process

The MCC counseling process involves registration, choice filling, seat allotment, and reporting to the allotted college. For Deemed Universities, the fees are generally higher than government colleges, which can sometimes lead to more seat availability in later rounds as some candidates may opt out due to financial considerations or other preferences. Be prepared to participate actively in all rounds, including the mop-up round, as this is where candidates with ranks like yours often find opportunities.

With careful planning and active participation in the MCC counseling for Deemed Universities, there's a reasonable chance to secure an MDS Orthodontics seat with your NEET MDS 2021 rank of 11400.
