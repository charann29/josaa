# Best Online Automation Courses & Certifications 2026 - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Scope

Automation is rapidly transforming industries worldwide, making skills in this field highly sought after. Pursuing an online certification can be an excellent way to gain expertise and advance your career in this dynamic domain.

## Key takeaways

*   Automation courses cover a wide range of topics, from Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to specific tools like UiPath, Ansible, and CATIA V5.
*   Programs vary significantly in duration, from a few days to several months, with fees ranging from approximately ₹1,000 to over ₹70,000.
*   Many courses require a basic understanding of computer systems or programming, while others are suitable for beginners.
*   Certifications from reputable institutions can significantly enhance career prospects in various sectors looking to streamline operations and improve efficiency.

## Understanding Automation and Its Impact

Automation involves using technology to perform tasks with minimal human intervention. This field encompasses several key areas, including Robotic Process Automation (RPA), which uses software robots to automate repetitive tasks; Artificial Intelligence (AI), which enables machines to learn and solve problems; and Cognitive Automation (CA), which blends AI with RPA to handle more complex, cognitive tasks. Industry reports consistently highlight the significant time and cost savings, as well as reduced error rates, that automation brings to businesses.

For instance, a report by PwC indicates that robotic process automation can save up to 90% of process time and substantially lower error rates. Furthermore, Gartner forecasts the global market for automation and robotics systems to reach approximately $1.89 billion in 2021, underscoring the growing demand for professionals skilled in these areas.

## Popular Online Automation Certifications

Several institutions offer comprehensive online courses tailored to different aspects of automation. Here's a look at some notable options:

### RPA using UiPath (ImaginXP)

This certificate program focuses on Robotic Process Automation using the UiPath software framework. It's designed to equip learners with professional-level skills in creating and deploying software robots to simplify business processes. The course helps participants understand how data flows between applications and actions, and how to use selectors for user interface automation.

*   **Duration:** 3 Months
*   **Fees:** Approximately ₹70,000
*   **Key Learning:** UiPath Studio, building automation solutions, understanding logical execution flow.

### Automation: Implementation in Business (LSE via GetSmarter)

This program explores the strategic implementation of new service technologies within organizations. It delves into AI, CA, and RPA, examining how these innovations can enhance efficiency, customer satisfaction, and profitability. The course also addresses ethical considerations related to AI and the future of employment.

*   **Duration:** 6 Weeks
*   **Fees:** Approximately ₹62,817
*   **Key Learning:** Integrating new technologies ethically, AI and societal benefit, automation and robotic skills.
*   **Certification:** UK CPD certified.

### Automation with Ansible, Puppet, and Salt (Packt via TCS iON)

This certification course is ideal for IT professionals looking to automate system administration tasks. It covers three powerful automation tools: Ansible, Puppet, and Salt. Participants will gain hands-on experience with managing large infrastructure, automating IT configurations, and deploying web applications.

*   **Duration:** 2 Weeks
*   **Fees:** Approximately ₹1,115
*   **Prerequisites:** Basic understanding of system administration and Linux systems; system with Vagrant and VirtualBox installed.
*   **Key Learning:** Ansible Galaxy and Tower, Puppet for IT configurations, Salt for deployment and advanced concepts like Docker containers.

### RPA Developer (Udacity)

This Nanodegree program focuses on developing professional skills for an RPA Developer role. It guides candidates through the fundamentals of RPA, teaching them to build automation solutions using UiPath Studio. The curriculum emphasizes understanding data processing and logical execution flow, along with mastering selectors for UI automation.

*   **Key Learning:** Building RPA solutions with UiPath Studio, data processing, selectors for UI automation, basic programming skills refresh.

## Specialised and Shorter Duration Courses

Several institutions also offer shorter, more focused programs:

*   **Basics of CATIA V5 (Skill Lync):** A 4-week beginner-level course on CATIA V5, costing approximately ₹40,000.
*   **Emerging Trends and Technical Developments in Automated Machines (NIT Warangal):** A 5-day certificate program.
*   **Lecture Series on Logic, Automata & Computation (NIT Trichy):** A 3-day certificate program.

## Career Scope in Automation

The demand for automation professionals is growing across various industries, including IT, finance, manufacturing, and healthcare. Graduates with these certifications can pursue roles such as RPA Developer, Automation Engineer, Business Process Analyst (with an automation focus), or IT Automation Specialist. The skills gained are highly transferable and valuable for organizations aiming to boost efficiency and innovate.

Investing in an automation certification is a strategic move for anyone looking to future-proof their career in a technology-driven world.
