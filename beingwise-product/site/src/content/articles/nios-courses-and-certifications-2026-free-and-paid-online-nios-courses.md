# NIOS Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online NIOS Courses

For students seeking flexible learning opportunities at secondary and senior secondary levels, the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) offers a variety of courses, many of which are accessible through the Swayam online platform. These programs provide a structured pathway to gain qualifications and enhance skills.

## Key takeaways

*   NIOS offers both secondary and senior secondary level courses, including core subjects and vocational training.
*   Many NIOS courses are available for free on the Swayam platform, coordinated by NIOS faculty.
*   Courses typically last 24 weeks and include video lectures, reading materials, assignments, and self-assessments.
*   Successful completion leads to certifications from NIOS and Swayam, valuable for academic and career progression.

## Understanding NIOS and Swayam Offerings

NIOS is an autonomous institution under the Ministry of Education, Government of India, dedicated to providing education through open and distance learning. Swayam, an initiative by the Ministry of Education, hosts free online courses from various Indian educational institutions, including NIOS. This collaboration makes quality education accessible to a wider audience.

## Featured Academic Courses

Many core academic subjects are available for study through NIOS on Swayam, catering to different educational stages. These courses are designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and prepare students for further studies or entry-level careers.

### Secondary Level

*   **Accountancy (Course Code 224):** This 24-week program introduces fundamental accounting concepts at the secondary level. Coordinated by Ms. Anshul Kharbanda from NIOS, it covers recording monetary transactions, debit/credit rules, and preparing financial statements. The course uses interactive sessions, video lectures, and specialized reading materials.

### Senior Secondary Level

*   **Accountancy (Course Code 320):** Also a 24-week course, this program delves deeper into financial accounting principles and concepts across seven modules. Designed by Prof. Ms. Anshul Kharbanda, it utilizes video lectures, assignments, quizzes, and discussion forums to facilitate learning. Completion can be a first step towards an accounting career.
*   **Biology (Course Code 314):** Coordinated by Prof. Dr. Sanghmitra Suryapani, this senior secondary biology course covers both theoretical and practical aspects over five modules and 31 lessons. It is delivered through interactive online sessions.
*   **Chemistry (Course Code 313):** Prof. Dr. Rajeev Prasad coordinates this program, which explores fundamental chemistry areas at the senior secondary level. It includes eight modules with theoretical and practical components, supported by text, audio/video tutorials, self-assessments, and discussion forums.

## Vocational Courses

Beyond academic subjects, NIOS also offers vocational training to equip students with practical skills relevant to various industries. One such example is:

*   **CRM Domestic Voice:** This course introduces students to the core principles of Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) and Customer Relationship Management (CRM) for domestic voice processes. It focuses on developing communication skills, customer service etiquette, and understanding typical BPO operations.

## How to Enroll and Certify

Enrolling in these courses typically involves registering on the Swayam platform. While the courses themselves are generally free to access, there might be an option to pay a fee for an examination and receive a verified certificate upon successful completion. The evaluation process usually includes assignments, quizzes, and a final examination. Upon passing, learners receive a certification jointly from NIOS and Swayam.

These flexible and accessible programs from NIOS through Swayam offer excellent opportunities for educational advancement and skill development.
