It's a bridge between biology and chemistry, helping us understand life at a molecular level. This introductory course from Udemy offers a foundational understanding of key biochemical concepts.

*   This self-paced online course provides an essential introduction to biochemistry.
*   It covers fundamental topics like carbohydrates, proteins, lipids, and nucleic acids.

*   Upon completion, students receive a digital certificate from Udemy.

## Understanding Biochemistry

Biochemistry delves into the chemical reactions that drive life, from the smallest cells to complex organisms. It examines how biological substances interact to enable functions within and between cells, which is crucial for comprehending tissues, organs, and entire biological systems. The field is broadly divided into structural biology, enzymology, and metabolism, all of which contribute to our understanding of life's intricate activities. Biochemical approaches are continually advancing nearly all biological sciences.

## Course Content and Structure

The "Introduction to Biochemistry" course is designed to introduce learners to the core building blocks of life. It covers the following main areas:

*   **Introduction to Biochemistry:** What biochemistry is, its historical development, and its importance.
*   **Carbohydrates:** Definition, properties, types (monosaccharides, disaccharides, polysaccharides), and functions.
*   **Proteins:** Definition, properties, types of structures, and their vital roles in living systems.
*   **Lipids:** What they are, their structures, functions, and various types.
*   **Nucleic Acids:** Composition, types (DNA, RNA), structure of nucleotides, and how DNA and RNA are assembled.

An assignment is included to help learners recap and reinforce their understanding of these fundamental concepts.
