# BIM Ready (Structural) by TechnoStruct Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Building Information Modeling (BIM) is transforming the construction industry, and for those interested in structural engineering, specialized skills are becoming essential. The "BIM Ready (Structural)" online certification from TechnoStruct Academy offers a focused program to equip students and professionals with these crucial capabilities.

## Key takeaways

*   This online certification course is designed to enhance skills in structural engineering within the BIM framework.
*   It combines structured virtual classroom sessions with hands-on practical experience using industry-standard software.
*   The program includes a one-month free internship opportunity, offering valuable real-world exposure.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a course completion certificate from TechnoStruct Academy.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The "BIM Ready (Structural)" course provides an in-depth exploration of building information modeling concepts as they apply to structural engineering. The curriculum delves into the technical aspects of design and structure, aiming to build industrial competence among participants. Over 45 days, the program dedicates 30 hours to online classes and another 30 hours to practical, hands-on sessions.

Participants will gain practical experience with a range of software tools vital for structural engineering and BIM, including:

*   AutoCAD 2D & 3D
*   STAAD Pro
*   Revit Structure
*   Dynamo
*   Navisworks
*   Autodesk BIM 360
*   Scan to BIM

Beyond software proficiency, the course also emphasizes professional skill development and offers internship privileges.

## Who is this Course For?

This program is particularly beneficial for:

*   **Civil Engineering Students:** Those looking to gain a competitive edge in the modern construction sector.
*   **Civil Engineers:** Professionals aiming to update their skills with BIM methodologies.
*   **Architects:** Individuals who wish to understand the structural aspects within a BIM environment.
*   **Structural Engineers:** Professionals seeking to deepen their expertise in BIM applications for structural analysis and design.

The course is delivered entirely online, with instruction in English, and utilizes a blend of self-study, virtual classroom interactions, and video/text-based content.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of technical and design features in building construction. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Project Management Skills:** Understanding the flow and execution of construction projects.
*   **Civil Engineering Expertise:** Enhancing fundamental knowledge in civil engineering principles.
*   **Architectural and Construction Insights:** Gaining knowledge about various drawing types like shop drawings, spool drawings, and construction drawings with detailed specifications.
*   **Structural Analysis:** Learning how to analyze building components structurally within a BIM context.

## Program Fees and Certification

The fee for the "BIM Ready (Structural)" certification program is ₹29,500 plus Goods and Services Tax (GST). This fee is to be paid after course registration.

### Fee Structure

| Particular    | Details         |
| :------------ | :-------------- |
| Program Fee   | ₹29,500 + GST |

Upon successful completion of the course, participants will receive a course completion certificate, issued by TechnoStruct Academy. This certification attests to their expertise in BIM structural engineering.

## How to Apply

Interested candidates can apply for the "BIM Ready (Structural)" program online. The general steps involve visiting the official TechnoStruct Academy website, locating the enrollment form for this specific course, filling in the required personal and educational details, and then following the instructions to complete the registration process.

This certification can significantly benefit individuals by providing a detailed understanding of structural engineering within the BIM framework, thereby enhancing their career prospects in civil engineering and architectural design.
