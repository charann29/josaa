# RBSE Board Exam 2026: Class 12 Result (OUT), Dates, Question Papers, Syllabus

The Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE) oversees the Class 10 and 12 examinations for students across Rajasthan. These exams are crucial milestones, and understanding the key dates, results, and preparation resources is essential for all students and parents.

## Key Takeaways

*   The RBSE Class 12 results for 2026 were announced on March 31, 2026, with an impressive overall pass percentage of 96.23%.
*   Class 10 results for 2026 were declared on March 24, with a 94.22% overall pass rate.
*   From the academic year 2026-2027, Class 10 board exams will be held twice a year, eliminating supplementary exams.
*   All board exams are conducted in a single morning shift, from 8:30 am to 11:45 am.

## RBSE Class 12 Results 2026

The RBSE Class 12 results were officially released on March 31, 2026, at 10:00 am. The announcement was made by the Education Minister, Madan Dilawar, during a press conference. Shortly after, the direct link to check results was activated on the official RBSE website, rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in. Students can access their results by entering their roll number.

For the 2026 examinations, the Class 12 students achieved an outstanding overall pass percentage of 96.23%.

## RBSE Class 10 Results 2026 and New Exam Structure

The RBSE Class 10 results for 2026 were declared on March 24 at 1:00 pm. Over 10 lakh students appeared for these exams, with an overall pass percentage of 94.22%. Girls slightly outperformed boys, with a pass percentage of 94.20% compared to 93.63% for boys.

During the Class 10 result announcement, a significant change was revealed for the upcoming academic session. Starting from 2026-2027, the RBSE Class 10 board examinations will be held twice a year. This new system means that there will no longer be supplementary exams for Class 10 students.

## Examination Dates and Schedule 2026

The RBSE conducted the Class 12 board examinations for 2026 between February 12 and March 11, 2026. The practical examinations for Class 12 were held earlier, from January 1 to January 20, 2026.

Class 10 board examinations for 2026 took place from February 12 to February 28, 2026.

All theoretical board exams for both classes were conducted in a single shift, from 8:30 am to 11:45 am, using a pen-and-paper format.

## Accessing Question Papers and Syllabus

To aid students in their preparation, the RBSE typically provides access to previous years' question papers and the current academic session's syllabus on its official website. Reviewing these resources is an excellent way to understand the exam pattern, marking scheme, and important topics. Students should regularly check the official RBSE portal for the most up-to-date syllabus and question paper resources for the 2026-27 academic year.
