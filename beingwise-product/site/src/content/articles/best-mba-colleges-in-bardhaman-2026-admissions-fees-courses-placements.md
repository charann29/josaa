## Admission Process and Entrance Exams

To secure a seat in an MBA or PGDM program in Bardhaman for the 2026 academic year, candidates typically need to clear one of the prominent national-level management entrance examinations. These exams assess a candidate's aptitude in areas like quantitative ability, verbal ability, data interpretation, and logical reasoning.

Commonly accepted entrance exams include:

*   **CAT (Common Admission Test):** Conducted by the IIMs, it's one of the most widely accepted exams for MBA admissions across India.
*   **XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test):** Administered by XLRI Jamshedpur, it's another highly respected exam.
*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test):** Conducted by NTA, CMAT scores are accepted by a large number of institutions.
*   **MAT (Management Aptitude Test):** Organised by AIMA, MAT is held multiple times a year in both paper-based and computer-based formats.
*   **ATMA (AIMS Test for Management Admissions):** Conducted by the Association of Indian Management Schools (AIMS).

After achieving a valid score in one of these exams, candidates usually go through subsequent stages, which may include Group Discussions (GD) and Personal Interviews (PI). The final selection is based on a composite score considering exam results, academic performance, work experience (if any), and GD/PI performance.
