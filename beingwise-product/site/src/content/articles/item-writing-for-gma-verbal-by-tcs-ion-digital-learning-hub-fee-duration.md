# Item Writing for GMA (Verbal) by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

The 'Item Writing for GMA (Verbal)' course from TCS iON Digital Learning Hub is an online program designed to equip individuals with the skills needed to create effective verbal reasoning tests. This certification helps participants understand how to design assessments that accurately measure an individual's general mental ability.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a three-month, self-paced online course delivered in English, offering five academic credits upon completion.
*   Participants benefit from mentorship by academic and industry experts, gaining practical skills in verbal assessment design.
*   The program aims to enhance career prospects through skill development, industry connections, and potential internship opportunities.
*   Successful candidates receive a digital certificate, with options for an 'Achievement' or 'Participation' certificate based on performance.

## Course Overview and Structure

This online certification program spans three months and is delivered through a virtual learning environment. It combines self-study with virtual classroom sessions, utilizing video and text-based materials. The curriculum is structured to provide a thorough understanding of verbal reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making, specifically focusing on crafting questions for General Mental Ability (GMA) tests.

The course offers a blend of online materials, lectures, tutorials, and practical sessions. It also facilitates engagement through academic and industry connect communities, fostering a holistic learning experience. Mentorship is a key component, with guidance provided by experienced professionals like Dr. Gargi Jain (academic expert) and Dr. U. N. Dash (industry expert).

## Program Offerings and Learning Outcomes

Participants in this program can expect to develop strong skills in writing and framing assessments for verbal ability. The syllabus is designed to impart professional knowledge in creating questions that test an individual's verbal reasoning capabilities. Key learning outcomes include a deep understanding of problem-solving strategies, decision-making processes, and effective question formation for verbal GMA tests.

Beyond theoretical knowledge, the program emphasizes practical application through practice assessments, industry assignments, and a summative test. It also offers career support, including potential internships and job assistance, making it a career-oriented course aimed at improving professional potential.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the 'Item Writing for GMA (Verbal)' online program, applicants should possess foundational knowledge in one or more of the following domains: Education, English, Psychology, or Mathematics. While teachers are a primary target audience, students from these backgrounds who wish to explore careers in teaching verbal reasoning or as item writers are also encouraged to apply.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the 'Item Writing for GMA (Verbal)' course is ₹11,800, inclusive of GST. This fee can be paid online through the official website during the registration process.

Upon successful completion of the program, participants are eligible for a digital certificate from TCS iON. There are two types of certificates:

*   **Certificate of Achievement:** Awarded to candidates who achieve a minimum of 40% in Part A, 50% in Part B, and an overall average of at least 50% in the summative tests. Those who do not meet these criteria will receive a scorecard.
*   **Certificate of Participation:** Granted to students who complete the course study, all industry projects and assignments, and the practice assessments.

## How to Apply

Detailed admission information and the registration process for the 'Item Writing for GMA (Verbal)' online course are available on the official TCS iON Digital Learning Hub platform. Prospective students should visit the platform to complete their application and fee payment.

This course provides a valuable opportunity for individuals to enhance their skills in a niche and important area of educational and professional assessment.
