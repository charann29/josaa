# Smart Pro User Interface & Experience Design by Aptech Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Aptech Learning offers the Smart Pro User Interface & Experience Design program, an 11-month course designed to equip students with the essential skills for creating intuitive and effective digital experiences. This program focuses on both the visual aspects (User Interface) and the overall user satisfaction (User Experience) of digital products.

## Key Takeaways

*   This 11-month program is delivered online, primarily through video and text-based materials, in English.
*   It is structured into two terms, covering foundational UX principles and advanced design elements.
*   The course is ideal for IT graduates and working professionals looking to enhance their design capabilities.
*   Financial assistance, including student loan facilities, is available, and career counselling is provided.

## Course Overview

The Smart Pro User Interface & Experience Design course aims to provide a thorough understanding of how to create user-centric digital products. The curriculum is designed to teach participants the tools and methodologies needed to optimize both the user interface (UI) and user experience (UX), ensuring that users can achieve their goals efficiently and effectively. The program offers a fresh perspective on interface and design principles, moving beyond just aesthetics to focus on functionality and user satisfaction.

## Program Structure and Duration

This comprehensive program spans approximately 11 months and is divided into two distinct terms. The first term introduces students to the core concepts of User Experience (UX) design, laying a strong foundation. The second term then delves into more advanced topics, covering fundamental design elements and the practical application of design principles. The course is delivered entirely online, providing a flexible learning environment through video lectures, readings, practical applications, and industry articles.

## Who Is This Course For?

The Smart Pro User Interface & Experience Design program is specifically tailored for individuals looking to build or advance their careers in the design field. It is particularly well-suited for:

*   IT graduates seeking to specialize in UI/UX design.
*   Engineering professionals and degree holders interested in design roles.
*   Working professionals who wish to upskill or transition into user interface and experience design.
*   Aspiring UI Designers and UX Architects.

## Fees and Financial Assistance

The fee for the Smart Pro User Interface & Experience Design course typically ranges from ₹63,800 to ₹91,800. It's important to note that this amount is exclusive of taxes and can vary based on the specific Aptech Learning center location. For precise fee details, prospective students are encouraged to attend an online counselling session. Aptech Learning also provides student loan facilities to help candidates manage their course expenses, making the program more accessible.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Smart Pro User Interface & Experience Design program is a straightforward process. Interested candidates can begin by visiting the official Aptech Education website. Once on the site, use the search bar to find “Smart Pro User Interface and Experience Design.” After selecting the course from the search results, you will be directed to its dedicated webpage. From there, locate and fill out the enquiry form. Completing this form is the first step towards joining the online classes and embarking on your UI/UX design journey. Free career counselling is also available to help guide your decision.

This program provides a robust foundation for anyone looking to make an impact in the world of digital design, equipping you with industry-ready skills and career support.
