# Ethics, Laws and Implementing an AI Solution on Microsoft Azure by CloudSwyft Global Systems, Inc via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program delves into the crucial intersection of artificial intelligence, ethical considerations, and legal frameworks, particularly within the context of Microsoft Azure. It's designed for anyone looking to understand how to responsibly develop and deploy AI solutions.

## Key takeaways

*   The program covers essential ethical and legal principles for AI development and data handling.
*   It offers practical, hands-on experience through CloudSwyft labs, focusing on Azure AI solutions.
*   Learning is self-paced and delivered through a blend of video and text, requiring approximately 5-6 hours per week.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate from Futurelearn.

## Program Structure and Learning Experience

This comprehensive program is structured as a series of four distinct courses, each building on the previous one to provide a holistic understanding of AI implementation on Azure. The instruction medium is English, and the learning mode is entirely self-study, allowing flexibility for participants. Content is delivered through video lectures and supplementary text materials, complemented by practical lab exercises to reinforce theoretical concepts.

## Course Modules

The program is divided into four main courses, each addressing a specific aspect of AI development and responsible implementation:

### 1. Microsoft Future Ready: Ethics and Law in Data and Analytics

This foundational module introduces the ethical and legal underpinnings of data and AI. Topics include data's ethical and legal foundations, ethical data practices, and the impact of data on individuals, society, and business. It also covers data bias, privacy, and the responsible use of data in a business context, alongside an introduction to AI and design principles.

### 2. Microsoft Future Ready: Data Science Research Methods Using Python Programming

This course focuses on the methodologies behind data science research, with a strong emphasis on Python programming. Participants will learn about the research process, the psychology of data provision, planning for analysis, and various types of research claims (frequency, association, and causal). It also covers power and sample size planning, survey design, reliability, validity, and different experimental designs.

### 3. Microsoft Future Ready: Principles of Machine Learning with Python Programming

Delving into the core of machine learning, this module introduces fundamental concepts, exploratory data analysis for both regression and classification, and essential data cleaning and preparation techniques using Python. Key topics include regression and classification models, ROC curves, principles and techniques for model improvement (such as cross-validation and dimensionality reduction), and an introduction to decision trees and ensemble methods like boosting.

### 4. Microsoft Future Ready: Designing and Implementing an Azure AI Solution

The final module brings together all learned concepts into the practical application of designing and implementing AI solutions on Microsoft Azure. It covers an overview of Azure Cognitive Services, setting up accounts, and an introduction to bots. Participants will learn about the Bot Framework, Bot Emulator, Language Understanding (LUIS), building intents, utterances, entities, and utilizing QnA Maker and knowledge bases for bot interactions.

## Fees and Certification

The program is offered on a subscription basis, with a fee of Rs. 900 per month. Upon successful completion of all course requirements, participants will receive a certificate. This certificate is provided by Futurelearn, acknowledging the successful acquisition of skills and knowledge in ethics, laws, and implementing AI solutions on Microsoft Azure.

## How to Apply

For specific application procedures and enrollment details, interested candidates should refer to the official Futurelearn platform. The self-study format allows for immediate enrollment and commencement of the program, subject to registration on their portal.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to gain expertise in a rapidly evolving field, ensuring you're well-equipped to build ethical and legally compliant AI solutions.
