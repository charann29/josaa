# Top Medical Colleges in Karnataka 2026: Ranking, Fees, Courses, Admission, Cutoff

For aspiring medical professionals, Karnataka offers a robust selection of institutions recognized by the Medical Council of India and affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences. Admission to these esteemed colleges is primarily merit-based, often determined by performance in the NEET entrance examination.

## Key takeaways

*   Karnataka is home to several top-tier medical colleges, including NIMHANS, KMC Manipal, and St. John's Medical College.
*   The NEET UG examination is the primary entrance test for undergraduate medical admissions, with the 2026 exam scheduled for May 3.
*   Medical programs are available at undergraduate (MBBS, BAMS, BHMS, BPT), postgraduate, and diploma levels.
*   Eligibility typically requires a minimum of 50% in Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, alongside a qualifying NEET score.

## Admission Eligibility Criteria

To secure admission to medical colleges in Karnataka, candidates must meet specific academic and entrance examination requirements. While exact criteria can vary by institution and program, general guidelines are consistent across the state.

*   **Academic Qualification:** Applicants must have successfully completed their Class 12 (or equivalent) examination with a minimum aggregate score of 50%. The core science subjects required are Physics, Chemistry, and either Zoology or Botany.
*   **Entrance Examination:** A mandatory requirement is a qualifying score in an approved medical entrance examination, primarily the NEET exam, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).
*   **Age Limit:** Candidates must also satisfy the prescribed age criteria as set by the regulatory bodies.

Karnataka's medical colleges offer excellent opportunities for a rewarding career in healthcare, attracting students from across India with their quality education and strong clinical exposure.
