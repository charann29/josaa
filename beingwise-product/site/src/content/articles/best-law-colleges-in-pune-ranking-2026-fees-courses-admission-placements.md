# Best Law Colleges in Pune: Ranking 2026, Fees, Courses, Admission, Placements
Pune, a vibrant educational hub, offers promising opportunities for students aspiring to pursue a career in law. This guide provides essential information about top law colleges in the city, covering admissions, programs, and career prospects.

## Key takeaways

*   Pune features one highly-ranked law institution, which operates as a private entity.
*   Students can choose between five-year integrated Bachelor of Law programs after Class 12 or a three-year LLB after graduation.
*   Symbiosis Law School (SLS) Pune consistently ranks among the top law colleges nationally, as per the NIRF rankings.
*   Admission to these programs typically involves national-level entrance exams and specific eligibility criteria.

## Understanding Law Programs in Pune

Law education in Pune caters to diverse academic backgrounds. After completing Class 12, students can enroll in integrated five-year programs such as:

*   BA LLB
*   BBA LLB
*   BSc LLB
*   BTech LLB

For graduates holding a bachelor's degree in any discipline, a three-year Bachelor of Legislative Law (LLB) program is available. For advanced studies, a Master of Laws (LLM) degree can be pursued after an LLB or an equivalent law degree.

### Eligibility for Law Admissions

General eligibility criteria for law programs in Pune are:

*   **Undergraduate (UG) Law Courses (e.g., BA LLB, BBA LLB):** Candidates must have passed the Higher Secondary School/Intermediate (10+2) examination or its equivalent from a recognized board, typically with a minimum aggregate score of 45%.
*   **Postgraduate (PG) Law Courses (e.g., 3-year LLB, LLM):** For the three-year LLB, applicants need a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. For the LLM program, an LLB or an equivalent law degree from a recognized university is required.

## Top Law College in Pune: NIRF Ranking 2024

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) evaluates and ranks higher education institutions across India annually. These rankings are based on various factors, including teaching, learning, resources, research, professional practice, graduation outcomes, outreach, and inclusivity. Symbiosis Law School, Pune, has consistently performed well in these national assessments.
