# Packt Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Packt Courses

BeingWise explores various online learning opportunities to help students and professionals enhance their skills. Among these, the collaborative programs offered by TCS iON and Packt provide specialized training in high-demand technology fields, focusing on practical application and industry relevance.

## Key takeaways

*   TCS iON and Packt offer a range of certification courses in cutting-edge technologies like Machine Learning, Data Modeling, IoT, and Deep Learning.
*   These programs are designed for practical, real-world application, combining digital lectures, learning resources, and industry assignments.
*   Each course typically runs for about six months and costs around ₹20,000.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a verifiable digital certificate and, in some cases, earn academic credit points.

## Machine Learning for Real-World Application

This certification program delves into the practical aspects and advanced techniques of machine learning, a core component of artificial intelligence. It's structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of how machine learning is applied in various scenarios. The curriculum is delivered through digital lectures, extensive digital learning materials, and practical industry assignments. The program culminates in a summative assessment to evaluate a learner's grasp of the concepts.

Industry experts guide participants, sharing insights into the field. A significant benefit of this course is the potential for both internship and job opportunities that it can open up for successful learners. Those who complete the program successfully are awarded a verifiable digital certificate, validating their newly acquired skills. Key skills covered include a deep understanding of algorithms and machine learning principles.

## Data Modeling and Visualization

The Data Modeling and Visualization certification focuses on the crucial area of data analysis. It teaches participants how to effectively use data visualization and modeling techniques while also addressing the common challenges and risks associated with data analytics. This online course is conducted within a virtual learning environment provided by the TCS iON digital platform. The program spans approximately five to six months and incorporates online materials, lectures, digital tutorials, and various hands-on activities and assignments.

Mentors with strong academic and industry backgrounds, including professors and consultants, offer expert assistance throughout the course. The learning methods are extensive, and evaluation techniques are designed to ensure both theoretical and practical knowledge acquisition. Successful participants earn five credit points and a verifiable digital certificate, subject to meeting eligibility criteria. Essential skills developed include data visualization, decision-making, analytical abilities, R Programming, statistical knowledge, problem-solving, and database management.

## IoT and its Applications using Raspberry Pi

This certification program is tailored for individuals interested in understanding and developing Internet of Things (IoT) solutions using Raspberry Pi microcontroller tools and various sensors. It's a career-oriented online course delivered via the TCS iON digital learning platform. Ideal candidates typically have a background in programming languages and Node.js.

The program emphasizes practical, online learning methodologies, guiding participants through writing code to classify sensors and utilize web-based dashboard APIs efficiently. The course benefits from the guidance of academically qualified faculty from institutions like IIT Jodhpur and industry experts, such as IoT edge solution architects. Upon meeting eligibility conditions, students can earn five course credits and a valuable course certificate. Core skills include Python programming, cloud computing, engineering knowledge, software development, and data science fundamentals.

## Application of Deep Learning and Neural Networks

This online course covers concepts related to neural networks and deep learning, ranging from foundational principles to advanced applications. Led by domain experts, the program helps learners grasp the practical utility of deep learning and artificial neural networks in various real-world scenarios. The course content is delivered through comprehensive digital learning resources and engaging digital lectures.

Upon successful completion of the Application of Deep Learning and Neural Networks Certification, learners are awarded a certificate of achievement, recognizing their mastery of these advanced topics. This certification is crucial for those looking to specialize in advanced AI fields.

These programs represent a significant opportunity for individuals to gain specialized knowledge and verifiable credentials in rapidly evolving technological domains, supported by the collaborative expertise of TCS iON and Packt.
