# PERA CET 2026: Registration, Dates, Eligibility, Pattern and more

PERA CET 2026 is an important common entrance test for students aspiring to join various private universities in Maharashtra. Conducted by PERA India, this exam offers a pathway to a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

## Key takeaways

*   PERA CET 2026 facilitates admissions to diverse programs like B.Tech, BBA, MBA, and Pharmacy in private universities across Maharashtra.
*   The exam is typically held across multiple sessions in April and May, with registration details released on the official PERA India portal.
*   Alongside PERA CET, scores from national-level exams like JEE Main or state-level exams like MHT CET are also accepted by participating institutions.
*   The test is administered online, either as a remote-proctored exam or at designated centers, followed by individual university counseling.

## What is PERA CET?

The Preeminent Education and Research Association (PERA) is a collective of private universities in Maharashtra dedicated to fostering quality education in fields such as Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, and Fine Arts. PERA CET serves as a multi-stream entrance examination for students seeking admission to both undergraduate and postgraduate courses within these associated institutes. It acts as an additional admission route alongside other recognized entrance exams like JEE, MHT CET, ATMA, CAT, and MAT.

## PERA CET Eligibility Criteria 2026

Meeting the eligibility requirements is a mandatory step for all candidates wishing to appear for PERA CET 2026. The examination is specifically designed for professional programs. Here are the general criteria:

### Eligibility Criteria for Preeminent Education and Research Association - Common Entrance Test

*   **For Undergraduate (UG) programs:** Candidates must have successfully completed their 12th standard or an equivalent examination from a recognized board. Essential subjects typically include Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, with Biology or Biotechnology also being relevant for certain streams.
*   **For Postgraduate (PG) programs:** Applicants need to possess an undergraduate degree from a university or institution recognized by the appropriate authorities.

## PERA CET Registration

The application process for PERA CET 2026 is conducted online. Aspiring candidates must visit the official PERA India portal to access the application form. It is essential to accurately fill in all required details and submit the form along with the prescribed application fee before the stated deadline. Keeping track of the submission window is vital to ensure your application is considered.

## PERA CET 2026 Exam Pattern

The PERA CET 2026 examination is an online test, offered through either a remote-proctored setup or at designated test centers. The exam duration is typically two hours. While specific details on the number of questions and marking scheme are usually released with the official notification, candidates should prepare for a multiple-choice question format covering subjects relevant to their chosen program.

After successfully qualifying the PERA CET, candidates will need to participate in the centralized counseling process, which is conducted individually by each university accepting PERA CET scores. It is advisable to regularly check the official PERA India portal for the most current information regarding the examination and admission procedures.
