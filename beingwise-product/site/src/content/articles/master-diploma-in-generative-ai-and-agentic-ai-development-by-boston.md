# Master Diploma in Generative AI & Agentic AI Development by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Artificial intelligence is rapidly transforming industries worldwide, and generative AI along with agentic AI systems are at the forefront of this revolution. The Boston Institute of Analytics (BIA) offers a Master Diploma designed to equip individuals with cutting-edge expertise in these advanced AI domains.

## Key Takeaways

*   This 10-month Master Diploma focuses on both generative AI models and agentic AI systems.
*   The program combines in-depth technical knowledge with practical, industry-relevant applications.
*   It's delivered by experienced professionals, featuring hands-on projects and live expert sessions.
*   No prior work experience is required, but a bachelor's degree in a relevant field and basic Python skills are essential.

## Course Overview

The Master Diploma in Generative AI & Agentic AI Development from the Boston Institute of Analytics is crafted to empower participants with specialized knowledge in advanced artificial intelligence. The curriculum seamlessly blends theoretical understanding with practical implementation strategies, demonstrating how these sophisticated AI technologies can be applied across various sectors.

The program runs for 10 months and is taught by industry practitioners and academic experts in AI innovation. It covers fundamental AI concepts, practical programming, various AI frameworks, and real-world deployment strategies. Students will benefit from hands-on laboratory sessions, interactive learning environments, and mentorship throughout the course.

This diploma is ideal for undergraduate students, early-career professionals, or experienced technologists looking to enhance their skills. It opens doors to emerging career paths such as AI Developer, Machine Learning Engineer, Data Scientist, and Generative AI Specialist. BIA also provides placement assistance, scholarship opportunities, and flexible learning options.

## What You Will Learn

Participants will gain a comprehensive understanding of both the theoretical principles and practical applications of AI. The journey begins with foundational concepts in machine learning and deep learning. Subsequently, the program delves into advanced techniques in generative AI models, including Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), Variational Autoencoders (VAEs), and Transformer architectures.

A significant portion of the course is dedicated to agentic AI development, teaching students how to design autonomous AI agents that can operate independently, make informed decisions, and interact effectively within dynamic environments.

## Program Highlights and Offerings

This diploma emphasizes practical learning and career readiness. Key features include:

*   **Duration:** A focused 10-month program.
*   **Curriculum:** Industry-aligned content focusing on Generative AI and Agentic AI.
*   **Projects:** Hands-on projects based on real-world scenarios.
*   **Expert Interaction:** Live sessions with leading AI industry experts.
*   **Tools & Frameworks:** Comprehensive coverage of essential AI frameworks and tools.
*   **Community:** Access to a dedicated AI learning community for peer interaction and support.
*   **Support:** Placement assistance to help graduates secure roles.
*   **Additional Benefits:** Access to a peer and alumni community, doubt-clearing sessions, case studies, and no-cost EMI options for fees.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in this Master Diploma, prospective participants should meet the following requirements:

*   **Education:** A bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics, or a closely related discipline.
*   **Skills:** Basic proficiency in Python programming.
*   **Experience:** No prior work experience is mandatory.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is designed for a diverse group of professionals and aspiring individuals looking to specialize in advanced AI. The target audience includes:

*   Data Scientists
*   Software Engineers
*   Machine Learning Engineers
*   Artificial Intelligence Engineers
*   Product Managers

## How to Apply

Applying for the Master Diploma in Generative AI & Agentic AI Development is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Boston Institute of Analytics website.
2.  Locate the "Enquire Now" option for the Generative AI & Agentic AI Development program.
3.  Submit the inquiry form with your contact details; a representative will then get in touch with you.
4.  Once the fee payment is processed, your enrollment in the course will be confirmed.

This Master Diploma offers a robust pathway for individuals eager to build a rewarding career in the rapidly expanding field of artificial intelligence.
