# Biology XII Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For Class 12 students keen to deepen their understanding of biology, the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) offers an online course, "Biology XII Part-I," delivered through the Swayam platform. This program provides a structured approach to key biological concepts, aligning with the Class 12 syllabus.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 24-week, self-paced online course with 2 credit points.
*   It covers essential Class 12 Biology topics through video tutorials, e-text, and interactive elements.
*   Learners receive a certificate from NCERT and Swayam upon successful completion of assessments.
*   The course is coordinated by Dr. C.V. Shimray from DESM, NCERT, New Delhi.

## Course Structure and Content

The Biology XII Part-I course is designed to be comprehensive, utilizing a blend of video lectures, animations, and detailed images to clarify complex biological ideas. Students will find discussion forums to pose questions, which are then addressed by subject matter experts. The course materials, including e-text and web resources, are downloadable for convenient access. Regular self-assessment quizzes are integrated to help students monitor their progress and reinforce learning.

### What You Will Learn

The curriculum is divided into modules covering fundamental areas of biology:

*   Reproduction in Organisms
*   Sexual Reproduction in Flowering Plants
*   Human Reproduction and Reproductive Health
*   Principles of Inheritance and Variation
*   Molecular Inheritance and Evolution

## Eligibility and Certification

This course is primarily beneficial for Class 12 students aiming to strengthen their biology knowledge. To earn the official certificate, participants must successfully complete weekly self-assessment tests and achieve a minimum score of 70% in the final assessment. The certificate is jointly provided by NCERT and Swayam, validating the completion of the program.

## Application Process

Enrolling in the Biology XII Part-I Certification Course on Swayam is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal for this course.
2.  Look for the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, usually located in the top right corner of the page.
3.  You will be directed to a new page where you can either sign in with an existing account or create a new one using your username and password.
4.  Once signed in, you can proceed to enroll in the course.
