# IBSAT Selection Process 2025: Results (OUT), Dates, Check Detailed Counselling Process

The selection process for IBSAT 2025 MBA and PGPM programs has concluded, and the results have been officially announced. These crucial selection rounds were conducted offline at the IBS Hyderabad Campus for all shortlisted candidates.

## Key Takeaways

*   The final results for the IBSAT 2025 selection process are now available on the official IBS website.
*   Eligible candidates included those with valid scores from IBSAT 2025 or other major management entrance exams (CAT, MAT, XAT, SNAP, NMAT, GMAT, MAH CET, or state-level exams from 2023 onwards).
*   The selection rounds primarily involved a Micro Presentation and a Personal Interview.
*   Admissions are offered to all nine IBS campuses – Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Dehradun, Jaipur, Ahmedabad, Gurgaon, Kolkata, Mumbai, and Pune – based on overall performance in these rounds.

## Checking Your IBSAT Selection Process Result 2025

Candidates can easily access their final admission results by following these steps on the official IBS website:

1.  Visit the official IBS India website.
2.  Locate and click on the link for 'IBSAT Selection Process Result 2025'.
3.  Enter your IBSAT login credentials.
4.  Your final admission result will then be displayed on the screen.

## Understanding the IBSAT Selection Briefing

Before the formal selection rounds begin, IBS conducts a 'Selection Briefing' session. This is an informative event designed to familiarise candidates with the various management programs offered by IBS and provide a clear overview of the subsequent admission process. After this briefing, candidates proceed to the Micro Presentation and Personal Interview rounds according to a predefined schedule.

## The IBSAT 2025 Selection Process Explained

The IBSAT selection process is conducted in an offline format, primarily at the IBS Hyderabad head office and other IBS campuses. Candidates aspiring for admission to the various MBA and PGPM programs must successfully navigate these stages:

*   **Micro Presentation:** In this round, candidates are given three topics and must choose one to deliver a presentation on for three minutes. This exercise assesses a candidate's communication skills, analytical thinking, and ability to articulate ideas clearly under time constraints.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** Following the micro presentation, candidates attend a personal interview. This round is designed to evaluate a candidate's overall personality, confidence, communication fluency, business acumen, and general attitude. Maintaining composure and a positive demeanor is key during this final assessment.

Upon successful completion of these rounds and based on the final selection outcome, candidates can secure admission to one of the nine IBS Business Schools, subject to meeting the specific cutoff and selection criteria.

## IBSAT Final Merit List 2025

The final merit list for IBS admissions is prepared by assigning specific weightage to different components of the selection process. This comprehensive evaluation ensures that a candidate's overall profile is considered.

| Components                | Weightage |
| :------------------------ | :-------- |
| Micro Presentation        | 25%       |
| Personal Interview (PI)   | 25%       |
| Previous Academic Records | 50%       |

This structured approach helps IBS identify candidates who possess the right blend of academic strength, communication abilities, and personal attributes suitable for their management programs.
