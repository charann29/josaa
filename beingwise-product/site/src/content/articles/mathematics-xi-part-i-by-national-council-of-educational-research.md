# Mathematics XI Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For Class 11 students looking to strengthen their foundational understanding of mathematics, the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) offers a comprehensive online certification course. Delivered through the Swayam platform, this program delves into core mathematical concepts crucial for senior secondary education.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 24-week self-paced online course, ideal for Class 11 students.
*   It covers essential topics like sets, functions, trigonometry, and quadratic equations.
*   Successful completion leads to a certificate from NCERT and Swayam.
*   Learning is facilitated through video lectures, e-text, and online assessments.

## Course Overview

This certification course is specifically designed for students in Class 11 who wish to gain a deep understanding of key mathematical principles. The curriculum is based on the standard Class 11 mathematics syllabus and is coordinated by Professor T. P. Sarma from the Department of Education in Science and Mathematics (DESM) at NCERT, New Delhi.

The learning experience is entirely self-study, utilizing a blend of video lectures and textual materials. Participants will engage with expert-led content that aims to clarify complex mathematical ideas and approaches, ensuring a solid grasp of the subject matter.

## What You Will Learn

The program is structured to enhance mathematical skills and provide detailed insights into several fundamental areas. Upon completing the course, learners will have a strong understanding of:

*   Sets and Functions
*   Trigonometric Functions
*   Quadratic Equations
*   Principle of Mathematical Induction
*   Complex Numbers
*   Linear Inequalities
*   Permutation and Combination

These topics are presented through a combination of expert video lessons, comprehensive reading materials, and other digital resources to facilitate effective learning.

## Course Structure and Offerings

The Mathematics XI Part-I course spans 24 weeks and is delivered in English. It is structured to support flexible learning through a variety of resources:

*   **Video Lectures:** Expert-led sessions explaining concepts.
*   **E-text and Reading Materials:** Digital textbooks and supplementary readings.
*   **Weblinks:** Curated external links for additional exploration.
*   **Discussion Forums:** Interactive platforms for peer and instructor interaction.
*   **Online Assessments:** Regular evaluations to test understanding.
*   **Certificate of Completion:** Awarded upon successful fulfillment of course requirements.

## Eligibility and Certification

This course is primarily aimed at students currently in Class 11 who are eager to deepen their understanding of mathematics. While there are no specific academic prerequisites to enroll, obtaining the course certificate requires a strong performance in the final assessment.

To be eligible for the official certificate, participants must achieve a score of 70% or higher in the final evaluation. The certificate is jointly provided by NCERT and Swayam, validating the successful completion of the program.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Mathematics XI Part-I course on Swayam is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to begin your learning journey:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal for the course.
2.  Locate the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  If you are a new user, create an account by signing up. Existing users can simply sign in with their credentials.
4.  Once logged in, you can enroll in the Mathematics XI Part-I course.

This course is an excellent opportunity for Class 11 students to build a robust mathematical foundation with the guidance of NCERT experts, all from the convenience of their homes.
