# Patient Care Technician by MedCerts: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Considering a career in healthcare support? The Patient Care Technician program offered by MedCerts provides comprehensive training to equip you with the essential skills needed to assist patients in various medical settings. This program is designed for individuals seeking to enter the healthcare field with practical, in-demand skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is delivered entirely online, allowing for flexible self-paced study.
*   It covers vital areas like patient care fundamentals, electrocardiography, and phlebotomy.
*   The total fee for the course is $4,000, with monthly payment plans available.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from MedCerts.

## Program Overview and Learning Format

This Patient Care Technician program is structured for online learning, making it accessible to a wide range of students. The instruction is provided in English, utilizing a blend of video and text-based materials to facilitate self-study. Students are expected to dedicate approximately 15-20 hours per week to their studies to keep pace with the curriculum.

The self-paced nature of the program means you can tailor your learning schedule to fit your personal commitments, making it an ideal option for those balancing work or other responsibilities.

## Course Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Patient Care Technician course is $4,000. MedCerts understands that upfront payment can be a challenge for some students, which is why they offer flexible monthly payment options. These plans can be as low as $367 per month, making the program more financially manageable.

Here’s a breakdown of the fee components:

| Fee Component | Amount |
| :---------------- | :----- |
| Course Fees       | $4,000 |
| Monthly Payment Options (as low as) | $367 |

## What You Will Study: The Syllabus

The curriculum for the Patient Care Technician program is designed to provide a well-rounded education in patient support. It covers several critical areas, preparing you for real-world scenarios in healthcare. The syllabus includes:

*   **Professionalism in Allied Health:** This module focuses on the ethical considerations, communication skills, and professional conduct expected in a healthcare environment.
*   **Fundamentals of Patient Care:** You will learn the basic principles of patient assistance, including hygiene, mobility support, and vital sign monitoring.
*   **Electrocardiography (ECG):** This section teaches you how to perform ECGs, a crucial diagnostic procedure for heart conditions.

*   **Phlebotomy:** You will gain skills in drawing blood, a common procedure in hospitals and clinics.
*   **Introduction to Human Anatomy and Medical Terminology:** This foundational module provides an understanding of the human body's structure and the specialized language used in medicine.

## Certification

Upon successful completion of all course requirements, students will be awarded a certificate. This certificate is provided by MedCerts, recognizing your achievement and readiness to work as a Patient Care Technician.

## How to Apply

To apply for the Patient Care Technician program, you would typically visit the official MedCerts website. There, you can find detailed information on admission requirements, the application process, and upcoming start dates. It's always recommended to review all official documentation to ensure you meet the necessary criteria.
