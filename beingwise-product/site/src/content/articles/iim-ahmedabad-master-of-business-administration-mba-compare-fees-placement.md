# IIM Ahmedabad Master of Business Administration (MBA): Compare Fees, Placement, Reviews, Cutoffs

IIM Ahmedabad's Master of Business Administration (MBA) program is a highly sought-after postgraduate degree, known for its rigorous curriculum and exceptional career opportunities. This page offers a detailed look into what makes this program a top choice for aspiring business leaders.

## Key Takeaways

*   IIM Ahmedabad's MBA program is a two-year, full-time, classroom-based course, established in 1961.
*   The total program fees are approximately INR 27.50 Lakh for two years.
*   The institute boasts impressive placements, with a median salary of INR 34.59 Lakh and a highest reported salary of INR 1.10 Crores.
*   Admission requires a strong CAT or GMAT score, with CAT cutoffs typically around the 99th percentile for the general category.

## Program Overview and Structure

The MBA at IIM Ahmedabad is a comprehensive two-year postgraduate degree designed to equip students with advanced management skills and knowledge. The program is delivered in a full-time, on-campus format, fostering an immersive learning environment. IIM Ahmedabad, a public and autonomous institution, has a long-standing reputation for academic excellence since its establishment in 1961.

## Fees and Funding

Investing in an MBA from IIM Ahmedabad is a significant decision, and understanding the financial commitment is crucial. The total program fee for the two-year Master of Business Administration is around INR 27.50 Lakh. This fee typically covers tuition, course materials, and other academic charges. Prospective students are encouraged to check the official IIM Ahmedabad website for the most current and detailed fee structure, including information on payment schedules and potential scholarships or financial aid options.

## Placements and Career Prospects

IIM Ahmedabad consistently demonstrates outstanding placement records, reflecting the high regard employers have for its graduates. According to official reports, the median salary offered to MBA graduates is INR 34.59 Lakh per annum. The highest salary reported reached an impressive INR 1.10 Crores. These figures highlight the strong career trajectories and lucrative opportunities available to students upon completing the program, spanning various sectors and roles both in India and internationally.

## Admission Criteria and Cutoffs

Admission to the IIM Ahmedabad MBA program is highly competitive. Candidates are primarily evaluated based on their scores in recognized management entrance exams. The accepted exams are the Common Admission Test (CAT) for Indian applicants and the Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT) for international applicants or those applying through specific channels. For the general category, the CAT cutoff in the final round typically stands at the 99th percentile. Additionally, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks to be eligible. The selection process also includes subsequent stages like Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI).

## Student Experience and Facilities

The campus life at IIM Ahmedabad is enriched by a wide array of facilities designed to support both academic and personal growth. Students have access to a well-stocked library, modern A/C classrooms, and advanced labs. For well-being, the campus features a sports complex, gym, and hospital/medical facilities. Other amenities include a cafeteria, hostel accommodation, Wi-Fi campus, auditorium, and even a music room, ensuring a holistic and comfortable living and learning environment.

IIM Ahmedabad's MBA program stands as a beacon of management education, promising a transformative experience and a launchpad for successful careers.
