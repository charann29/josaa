# Certificate in Performing Arts and Media Management by Indian Institute of Creative Skills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those passionate about a career in the dynamic world of performing arts and media, the Indian Institute of Creative Skills (IICS) offers a comprehensive Certificate in Performing Arts and Media Management. This program is designed to equip aspiring artists and media professionals with the essential skills and practical experience needed to thrive.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a two-year certificate program, including intensive training, projects, and internships.
*   It's officially certified by the NSDC Academy and managed by the Media and Entertainment Skills Council (MESC).
*   The curriculum focuses on practical skills in acting, media production, content creation, and communication.
*   Graduates gain real-world exposure through a capstone project and a paid internship.

## Program Overview and Structure

The Certificate in Performing Arts and Media Management is a detailed, two-year course from IICS. It's carefully structured to provide students with the specialized knowledge and hands-on experience crucial for success in theatre, film, television, and digital media.

The program spans 24 months, broken down into several phases:

*   **21 Months of Intensive Training:** This core period focuses on expert-led learning across various modules.
*   **3 Months of Project Work:** Students dedicate this time to creating video and theatrical performances, applying what they've learned.
*   **6 Months of Industry Projects:** Practical application of skills in real-world scenarios.
*   **6 Months of Paid Internships:** Valuable professional experience and industry exposure.

Throughout the course, students benefit from expert mentors and hands-on training in areas like acting, media production, and content creation. There are also workshops covering crucial skills such as anchoring, public speaking, and media relations.

## What You Will Learn

This certificate program delves deep into various facets of performing arts and media. Participants will:

*   **Master Advanced Acting Techniques:** Explore methods for both stage and camera performances, enhancing versatility.
*   **Understand Theatre Arts:** Gain insights into theatre production and direction.
*   **Develop Content Creation Skills:** Learn to craft engaging narratives suitable for different media platforms.
*   **Refine Communication Abilities:** Workshops on anchoring, public speaking, and media relations are integral to engaging diverse audiences.
*   **Acquire Social Media Proficiency:** Develop skills essential for modern media engagement.

Key learning areas include speaking skills, social media expertise, and acting proficiency, all vital for a successful career in the entertainment industry.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the Certificate in Performing Arts and Media Management, applicants must meet the following requirements:

*   **Education:** Completion of 12th grade from a recognized school board.
*   **Age:** Must be at least 18 years old and not older than 40 years.
*   **Portfolio:** Submission of a collection of previous creative work showcasing their abilities.
*   **Interest:** A genuine interest and enthusiasm for a career in the media and entertainment industry.

## Course Fees and Certification

The fees for the Certificate in Performing Arts and Media Management program are structured as follows:

| Fee Component   | Amount      |
| :-------------- | :---------- |
| Prospectus Fees | Rs. 2,000   |

This initial fee covers the prospectus. Further details on the full course fee structure would typically be provided upon inquiry or during the admission process. Upon successful completion of the program, a certificate is provided by the Indian Institute of Creative Skills, with official certification from NSDC and MESC.

## How to Apply

The admission process for the Certificate in Performing Arts and Media Management is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official Indian Institute of Creative Skills website.
2.  Locate and click on the "Admission Now" tab.
3.  Fill out the admission form with the required details and submit the prospectus fees.
4.  Once the form and fees are submitted, you will be able to join the course.

This program offers a robust pathway for individuals aspiring to make their mark in the vibrant performing arts and media sectors, providing a strong foundation of skills and real-world experience.
