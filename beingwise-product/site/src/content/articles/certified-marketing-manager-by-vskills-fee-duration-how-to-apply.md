# Certified Marketing Manager by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to build a strong foundation in marketing management, the Vskills Certified Marketing Manager program offers a comprehensive pathway. This certification is designed to equip individuals with the skills needed to develop effective marketing plans, understand market dynamics, and drive brand success.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a self-paced, online course delivered through video and text, accessible 24/7.
*   The program culminates in an industry-recognized certificate from Vskills, a government-approved certification body.
*   There are no specific eligibility requirements, making it accessible to a wide range of learners.
*   The total fee for the course and certification is ₹3,499, payable online.

## Course Overview

The Vskills Certified Marketing Manager course provides a deep dive into the core concepts and strategies of modern marketing. It covers 13 detailed modules aimed at helping candidates understand how to develop strategic marketing plans, gather crucial market insights, and identify new opportunities. The curriculum is regularly updated to reflect current industry trends, including extensive coverage of e-commerce marketing and digital customer targeting.

This program is ideal for aspiring entrepreneurs, current business owners, and B2B or B2C marketing professionals who wish to enhance their expertise. Marketing managers are in high demand across various sectors, including marketing agencies, banks, multinational corporations, and both private and public organizations. Companies like Titan, Cipla Ltd, and Kirloskar are known to hire professionals with such marketing management skills.

To support learners, Vskills provides practice tests, free sample papers, and supplementary learning content. A physical copy of the study material is also included.

## Program Features and Offerings

The Vskills Certified Marketing Manager program comes with several valuable features designed to enhance the learning experience and support candidates:

*   **Lifetime LMS Access:** Continuous access to the learning management system (LMS).
*   **Government-Approved Certification:** An industry-recognized certificate from Vskills.
*   **Study Materials:** Both video and text-based tutorials, plus a hard copy of the learning material.
*   **Practice and Preparation:** Free practice tests and sample questions to help prepare for the exam.
*   **Flexible Exam Schedule:** Candidates can choose a convenient time for their certification exam.
*   **Reappearance Provision:** The option to retake the exam if needed.
*   **Career Support:** Vskills-certified accounts on job portals like Shine and Monsterindia to assist with job searches.

## Fee and Payment Details

The total fee for the Certified Marketing Manager course and certification is **₹3,499**. This amount covers all course materials, access to the LMS, and the certification exam.

Payment can be made quickly and securely through various online methods, including:

*   Credit/Debit Card
*   UPI ID
*   Internet Banking

## Eligibility and Certification

One of the key advantages of the Vskills Certified Marketing Manager online course is that **there are no specific eligibility criteria** for registration. This means anyone interested in learning marketing management, regardless of their academic background or professional experience, can enroll.

To earn the Vskills certificate, learners must achieve a minimum score of 25 marks in the final certification exam. Failing to meet this minimum score will mean the certificate cannot be awarded.

## What You Will Learn

The program is structured to impart essential marketing skills, enabling participants to formulate strategies and boost brand visibility. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Understanding the fundamentals of marketing and the distinctions between marketing and sales.
*   Developing effective marketing strategies and outlining robust marketing plans.
*   Capturing valuable marketing insights from target audiences.
*   Cultivating strong customer relationships to build a loyal customer base.
*   Analyzing consumer and business markets to identify opportunities.
*   Approaches for segmenting diverse customer groups.
*   Developing comprehensive product strategies and building strong brand presence.
*   Understanding e-commerce marketing strategies and best practices.
*   Delivering value through integrated marketing channels.
*   Creating effective pricing strategies and managing services to shape market offerings.

## Who Is This Course For?

This certification is designed for a broad audience seeking to excel in the marketing field. It is particularly beneficial for:

*   **Entrepreneurs:** To effectively market their businesses.
*   **Marketing Managers:** To update and refine their skills.
*   **Marketing Executives:** To advance their careers.
*   **Marketing Analysts:** To deepen their strategic understanding.
*   **Marketing Directors:** To stay current with industry trends.

Businesses globally are constantly seeking professionals adept at managing marketing functions, making this program a valuable asset for career advancement.

With its flexible online format and comprehensive curriculum, the Vskills Certified Marketing Manager program offers a practical path to gain expertise and a recognized certification in the dynamic field of marketing.
