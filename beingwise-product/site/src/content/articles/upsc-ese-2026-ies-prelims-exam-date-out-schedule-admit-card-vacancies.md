# UPSC ESE 2026: IES Prelims Exam Date (OUT), Schedule, Admit Card, Vacancies, Final result 2025 OUT

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has officially announced key dates for the Engineering Services Examination (ESE) 2026, also known as IES. This page provides essential information for aspiring engineers, including exam schedules, admit card details, and vacancy numbers.

## Key Takeaways

*   The UPSC IES 2026 Prelims exam is scheduled for **February 8, 2026 (Sunday)**, conducted in two shifts.
*   Approximately **474 vacancies** have been announced for ESE 2026, with potential for an increase.
*   The application window for ESE 2026 closed on **October 16, 2025**, following the notification release on **September 26, 2025**.
*   The final results for UPSC IES 2025 have been declared, with Mains results on September 4, 2025, and Prelims results on June 20, 2025.

## IES 2026 Prelims Exam Schedule

The UPSC ESE 2026 Prelims examination will take place on **February 8, 2026**. The exam will be held in two distinct shifts:

*   **Shift 1:** 9:30 AM to 11:30 AM
*   **Shift 2:** 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM

Candidates are advised to use this time effectively for thorough revision, focusing on the exam pattern and syllabus as they prepare for this crucial stage.

## UPSC IES 2026 Admit Card and Exam Centres

The admit card for the UPSC IES 2026 Prelims exam will be released on the official UPSC website a few days before the examination date. It is mandatory for all candidates to download and print their admit card, as it contains vital information such as the candidate's roll number, exam centre, and reporting times.

### How to Download Your IES 2026 Admit Card

Follow these steps to access your admit card once it is released:

1.  Visit the official UPSC website.
2.  Navigate to the 'E-Admit Cards' section for various UPSC examinations.
3.  Select the link specifically for the IES 2026 exam admit card.
4.  Carefully read all instructions provided and proceed to download.
5.  Enter the required details (such as registration ID and date of birth) and submit.
6.  Your IES 2026 admit card will appear on the screen.
7.  Download and take at least two printouts for your records and for exam day.
8.  Verify all details on the admit card. In case of any discrepancies, contact UPSC authorities immediately.

### IES 2026 Exam Centres

The list of designated exam centres for both the Prelims and Main examinations will be detailed in the official ESE 2026 notification. UPSC allocates exam centres on a first-come, first-served basis. While Delhi, Dispur, Kolkata, and Nagpur are generally available, other centres may have limited seating. Candidates should note that requests for changes to the allotted examination centre will not be accommodated under any circumstances.

## UPSC IES 2026 Vacancies

For the Engineering Services Examination 2026, the Union Public Service Commission has provisionally announced approximately **474 vacancies**. It is important for candidates to know that this number is subject to change and may increase, particularly with the potential inclusion of vacancies from the Indian Railway Management Service.

## UPSC IES 2025 Final Result Declared

For candidates who appeared for the UPSC IES 2025 examination, the final results have been announced. The Mains 2025 result was released on September 4, 2025, for the exam conducted on August 10, 2025. Prior to this, the IES 2025 Prelims result was declared on June 20, 2025, following the exam held on June 8, 2025.

Stay updated with official UPSC announcements for any further details regarding the ESE 2026 examination cycle.
