# Financial Markets and Services by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub National Stock Exchange of India Ltd: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program, a collaboration between TCS iON Digital Learning Hub and the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd (NSE), offers an in-depth exploration of financial markets. It's designed to equip learners with practical knowledge and skills essential for navigating the complex world of finance.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn about portfolio management, share trading, and broking directly from industry experts.
*   Gain practical experience using the NSE SMART trading application.
*   Earn six course credits and a digital certificate upon successful completion.
*   Benefit from career support and potential internship opportunities.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Financial Markets and Services online course focuses on developing a strong understanding of how financial markets operate. It delves into various aspects of the finance industry, including portfolio management, broking, share transfers, and the dissolving of shares. The program is self-paced, delivered through video and text-based modules, and includes virtual classroom sessions.

### Learning Experience

Students will engage with online resources, lectures, and tutorials. The program emphasizes hands-on learning through projects, assignments, and practice tests. A key feature is the opportunity to work with the NSE SMART market trading application, allowing learners to simulate securities trading without financial risk. The course also fosters an academic and industry-based community for collaborative learning.

## Expert Mentorship

This certification program is guided by experienced academic and industry professionals. Mentors like Sundar Sankaran (Founder-Director of Advantage-India Consulting Pvt. Ltd and Mavuca Capital Advisors Pvt. Ltd) and Anita Toshniwal (a corporate trainer in the Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance segment) provide valuable insights and guidance throughout the course.

## Program Fees and Certification

The fee for the Financial Markets and Services certification is ₹11,800. This amount is paid online through the course website to access all program materials and classes.

### Certificate Details

Upon meeting the qualifying criteria, students can earn a digital certificate from TCS. There are two types of certificates:

*   **Certificate of Achievement:** Awarded to students who score a minimum of 40% in Part A, 50% in Part B, and achieve an overall average of 50% in the summative assessment.
*   **Certificate of Participation:** Awarded upon completion of the course, including projects, industry-based assignments, and practice assessments.

## Eligibility and Target Audience

This online course is suitable for individuals with prior knowledge of Commerce concepts. It is particularly beneficial for undergraduate commerce students and professionals already working in the banking and financial sectors. This includes roles such as bank branch managers, investment bankers, brokers, depository participants, financial services analysts, financial services advisors, and mutual fund agents.

## How to Apply

Admission to the Financial Markets and Services online course is straightforward and conducted entirely online. Interested candidates should visit the official TCS iON course website. From there, locate the 'Activate Now' option, click on it, and proceed to fill in the required registration details to join the course.

Embark on this learning journey to enhance your understanding and career prospects in the dynamic financial markets.
