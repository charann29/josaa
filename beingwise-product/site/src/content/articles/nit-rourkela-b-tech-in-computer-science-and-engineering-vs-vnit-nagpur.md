# NIT Rourkela B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering Vs VNIT Nagpur: Compare Fees, Placement, Reviews, Cutoffs

Choosing the right institute for your B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering is a significant decision. This comparison helps you understand the key differences and similarities between NIT Rourkela and VNIT Nagpur for this popular program, assisting you in making an informed choice.

## Key takeaways

*   Both NIT Rourkela and VNIT Nagpur offer highly-rated B.Tech CSE programs, with student feedback indicating strong satisfaction with placements and campus life.
*   The total fees for the four-year B.Tech CSE program are similar at both institutions, making cost less of a differentiating factor.
*   Placement statistics, including median and highest salaries, are comparable, suggesting excellent career opportunities from both NITs.
*   NIRF rankings place NIT Rourkela consistently higher than VNIT Nagpur in the engineering category, which might be a factor for some prospective students.

## Institute Overview

Both National Institute of Technology (NIT) Rourkela and Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT) Nagpur are well-established, publicly-funded institutions with a strong legacy in engineering education. NIT Rourkela was founded in 1961, while VNIT Nagpur was established a year earlier in 1960. Both institutes offer a full-time, four-year Bachelor of Technology degree in Computer Science and Engineering.

## Student Ratings and Reviews

Student feedback offers valuable insights into the on-campus experience. For the B.Tech CSE program, NIT Rourkela has received an overall rating of 4.4 out of 5, based on reviews from 83 verified students. VNIT Nagpur's B.Tech CSE program holds an overall rating of 4.3 out of 5, based on feedback from 47 students. Both institutes score highly across various parameters, including placements, infrastructure, and campus environment.

Here's a detailed look at how students rated specific aspects:

| :---------------- | :---------------------- | :--------------------- |
| Placements        | 4.4                     | 4.3                    |
| Infrastructure    | 4.4                     | 4.3                    |
| Faculty           | 3.9                     | 4.3                    |
| Crowd & Campus    | 4.6                     | 4.4                    |
| Value for Money   | 4.4                     | 4.3                    |

## Fees Structure

The total fees for the four-year B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering program are quite similar at both institutes. For the complete duration of the course, the approximate total fee at NIT Rourkela is INR 5.00 Lakhs, which is also the approximate total fee for VNIT Nagpur. It's always advisable to check the official institute websites for the most current and detailed fee breakdowns, including hostel and mess charges, which are not included in this total.

## Placement Opportunities

Both NIT Rourkela and VNIT Nagpur boast strong placement records for their B.Tech CSE graduates. The statistics highlight excellent career prospects from both institutions.

*   **NIT Rourkela (B.Tech CSE):** The median salary for graduates was INR 11.50 Lakhs per annum, with the highest reported salary reaching INR 62.44 Lakhs per annum.
*   **VNIT Nagpur (B.Tech CSE):** Graduates secured a median salary of INR 11.52 Lakhs per annum, and the highest reported salary was INR 64.00 Lakhs per annum. VNIT Nagpur also reported a high placement rate of 93.4% for the batch.

These figures demonstrate that both institutes are highly regarded by recruiters, offering competitive salary packages and good job placement rates.

## Rankings

Rankings can provide an external perspective on an institute's overall standing. According to the NIRF (National Institutional Ranking Framework) rankings, NIT Rourkela has generally been positioned higher than VNIT Nagpur in the engineering category. For example, NIT Rourkela was ranked 13th in the NIRF '23 engineering rankings, while VNIT Nagpur was ranked 44th in the same category. Prospective students often consider these rankings as one of several factors in their decision-making process.

Ultimately, the choice between NIT Rourkela and VNIT Nagpur for B.Tech CSE may come down to personal preferences regarding location, specific faculty research interests, and campus culture, given their comparable academic quality and career outcomes.
