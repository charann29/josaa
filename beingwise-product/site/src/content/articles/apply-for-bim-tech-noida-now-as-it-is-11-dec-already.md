## Can I apply for BIM Tech Noida now as it is 11 Dec. already?

It's important to keep track of application deadlines when planning your higher education. Let's clarify the current application status for BIMTECH Greater Noida's programs.

## Key takeaways

*   The deadline for profile-based selection at BIMTECH Greater Noida has already passed.
*   As of December 11th, you cannot apply for this particular selection round.
*   BIMTECH offers various postgraduate management programs.
*   The institution has a strong record of placements, with competitive salary packages.

## Understanding BIMTECH's Application Process

BIMTECH, located in Greater Noida, offers a range of postgraduate management programs. They utilize a common application form for all their programs to streamline the process for prospective students. This approach simplifies applying to multiple specializations within the institute.

## Important Deadlines: Profile-Based Selection

For those interested in profile-based selection at BIMTECH, the application window has closed. The official deadline for this specific application route was December 2nd. This means that as of December 11th, you are past the submission date for this particular selection round.

It's crucial for aspiring students to note these dates carefully to avoid missing opportunities. Always refer to the official BIMTECH admissions portal for the most accurate and up-to-date information on application timelines and specific program requirements.

## What is Profile-Based Selection?

Profile-based selection often considers a holistic view of an applicant's background. This can include academic performance, work experience, extracurricular activities, and other achievements, rather than relying solely on entrance exam scores. It's designed to identify well-rounded candidates who can contribute significantly to the program and the institution.

## Placement Overview at BIMTECH

BIMTECH has a commendable track record when it comes to student placements. In their 2021 placement drive, the institution reported an average salary of ₹8.07 Lakhs per annum (LPA). The highest salary package offered during the same period reached ₹16 LPA. These figures highlight the career opportunities and potential returns on investment for students graduating from BIMTECH programs.

Always ensure you check the official BIMTECH admissions page for precise and current application details for future admission cycles.
