# EDGE Metaversity Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online EDGE Metaversity Courses

EDGE Metaversity offers a range of professional certificate programs designed to equip students with in-demand skills in animation, virtual production, visual effects (VFX), and game development. These programs are delivered through an immersive, hands-on approach.

## Key Takeaways

*   EDGE Metaversity provides specialized, professional certificate courses in cutting-edge digital creation fields.
*   Programs are primarily offered offline, ensuring practical, in-person training at well-equipped campuses across India.
*   No prior experience in game design, computer graphics (CG), or VFX is required for many of these courses, making them accessible to passionate learners.
*   Graduates often develop a strong portfolio, which is crucial for career entry in these creative industries.

## Master Certificate in Unreal Engine Virtual Production

This 9-month (300-hour) master certificate program focuses on the intricacies of virtual production using Unreal Engine. It's an offline course conducted at EDGE campuses, providing comprehensive training from foundational concepts to advanced techniques. Students will explore core elements like computer graphics, effective storytelling, and camera techniques, progressing to sophisticated computer graphics and advanced compositing. This course is ideal for individuals with a passion for visual storytelling and integrating visual effects, regardless of their previous background in game design, CG, or VFX. A key outcome of this program is the creation of a portfolio film, showcasing the student's proficiency in virtual production.

## Master Certificate in Animation with Autodesk Maya

EDGE Metaversity offers this 9-month offline master certificate course in Animation, utilizing Autodesk Maya – a leading software in the animation industry. This program is designed to help aspiring animators build a successful career in a rapidly growing field. The demand for computer-generated images and animations in television, movies, games, and virtual reality is expanding significantly. The curriculum focuses on mastering standard Maya software, enhancing students' productivity and expertise in animation. The program is affiliated with Trazos, a highly regarded animation school in Spain, recognized among the top institutions in Europe and globally.

## Master Certificate in VFX Compositing with NukeX

Another 9-month offline master certificate course, this program specializes in VFX Compositing using NukeX. Taught at EDGE campuses, it aims to prepare students for careers in visual effects. NukeX is the technology behind many of the spectacular environments and backgrounds seen in major action films. The curriculum is crafted to provide candidates with a deep understanding of VFX animation technologies, enabling them to create compelling visual effects. Like the Maya animation course, this program also benefits from an affiliation with Trazos, a prominent animation school.

## Master Certificate in VFX with Houdini

This 9-month professional certificate course focuses on visual effects creation using SideFX Houdini, a highly sought-after software in the film and television industry. Offered offline through EDGE Metaversity, this program trains participants to produce stunning effects similar to those seen in popular movies and TV series. It's an excellent opportunity for students interested in specializing in advanced visual effects development.
