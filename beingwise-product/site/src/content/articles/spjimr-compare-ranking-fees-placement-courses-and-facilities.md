# SPJIMR: Compare Ranking, Fees, Placement, Courses & Facilities @

S.P. Jain Institute of Management and Research (SPJIMR) in Mumbai is a highly regarded institution for management education in India. This page offers a comprehensive overview of SPJIMR, covering its academic offerings, student experience, and career prospects, to help prospective students and their families make informed decisions.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Strong Student Satisfaction:** SPJIMR generally receives positive feedback from students, with an overall rating of 4.2 out of 5 based on verified reviews, highlighting satisfaction with placements, faculty, and campus life.
*   **Competitive Placements:** The institute consistently demonstrates robust placement records, with a median salary for its flagship MBA/PGDM programs ranging between ₹22.8 L and ₹31.5 L.
*   **Diverse Program Portfolio:** SPJIMR offers a variety of management programs, including full-time MBA/PGDM, specialized PG Diplomas, Certificate courses, and a Fellow Programme in Management (FPM).
*   **Holistic Development Focus:** Beyond academics, SPJIMR emphasizes a well-rounded student experience, reflected in positive ratings for campus life and value for money.

## Rankings and Reputation

SPJIMR is recognized among the top management institutes in India. While specific NIRF rankings for the current year should be checked on the official NIRF portal, the institute consistently features prominently in national management school assessments. Its strong faculty, innovative curriculum, and industry connections contribute significantly to its reputable standing.

## Courses and Fees

SPJIMR offers a range of postgraduate and doctoral programs designed to cater to different career aspirations. Here's a look at some of its popular offerings:

### MBA/PGDM

*   **Total Tuition Fees:** This program's fees typically range from ₹1.5 L to ₹26.1 L, depending on the specific variant and duration (12 months to 2 years).
*   **Accepted Exams:** Admission usually requires scores from exams like GMAT, along with other specified tests.

### Certificate Programs

*   **Total Tuition Fees:** Fees for certificate courses vary widely, from ₹25 K to ₹18.98 L, based on the program's specialization and length (12 weeks to 25 months).
*   **Accepted Exams:** These programs generally consider GMAT scores and other relevant entrance examinations.

### PG Diploma

*   **Total Tuition Fees:** A typical PG Diploma program might cost around ₹10.95 L.
*   **Median Salary:** Graduates from these programs have reported a median salary of approximately ₹22.8 L.
*   **Course Duration:** These are often intensive 11-month programs.
*   **Accepted Exams:** GMAT and other relevant entrance tests are usually considered for admission.

### Fellow Programme in Management (FPM)

*   **Course Duration:** This doctoral-level program typically spans 4 years.
*   **Accepted Exams:** Admission to the FPM often requires scores from exams like UGC JRF, among others.
