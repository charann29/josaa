## Understanding IIT Madras MBA Admissions

Admissions to the MBA program at IIT Madras DOMS are highly competitive. The process typically involves a multi-stage evaluation, with the CAT (Common Admission Test) score being a critical initial filter. Candidates who clear the CAT sectional and overall cut-offs are then considered for further rounds, which may include a Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI).

## The Role of CAT Percentile

For the open category, the CAT overall percentile cut-off for IIT Madras DOMS has historically been very high. Data from previous years, such as 2018 and 2019, indicates that this cut-off often hovers in the range of 96 percentile and above. For example, in those years, it was reported to be around 98.2 percentile for the open category. Your CAT 2019 score of 71.31 percentile, while a good first attempt, unfortunately falls significantly below this typical range. This makes it challenging to receive a call for the subsequent admission rounds.
