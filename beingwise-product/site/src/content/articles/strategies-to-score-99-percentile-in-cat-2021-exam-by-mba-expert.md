# Strategies to Score 99+ Percentile in CAT 2021 Exam by MBA Expert

The Common Admission Test (CAT) is a highly competitive national-level examination administered by the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs). Achieving a 99+ percentile in CAT can open doors to top-tier business schools across India, including the IIMs themselves, Faculty of Management Studies (Delhi), Shailesh J. Mehta School of Management (Mumbai), and SP Jain Institute of Management and Research (Mumbai).

## Key Takeaways

*   **Unwavering Commitment:** From day one, set your sights firmly on a 99+ percentile, refusing to settle for anything less.
*   **Intense Practice:** Consistently push your boundaries by solving numerous mock tests daily, treating each one like the actual exam.
*   **Strategic Approach:** Develop and refine specific test-taking strategies based on the exam pattern and your performance in mocks.
*   **Calm Under Pressure:** Maintain composure during the exam, especially when encountering challenging questions, to avoid time loss.

## Be Firm About Your Aim

To join the elite group of candidates who score 99+ percentile, your mindset is crucial. Right from the start, cultivate a strong belief that you will achieve this target. Visualize yourself in your desired IIM and align your efforts towards the specific percentile score required for admission there. This determined approach will fuel your preparation journey.

## Push Your Limits with Consistent Practice

Success in CAT demands relentless effort. Dedicate yourself wholeheartedly to your studies. A key strategy involves solving a minimum of 3-4 mock tests every day. Each mock should be approached with the seriousness of the actual CAT exam. After completing a mock, thoroughly review your performance, identify incorrect answers, and practice those specific areas to prevent repeating mistakes. Continuously work on topics you find challenging, and don't neglect subjects you're already proficient in. Every question deserves your complete focus.

## Develop and Refine Your Strategies

Before diving deep into practice, familiarize yourself with the CAT exam pattern. Understand how many questions you need to answer correctly in each section to hit your 99+ percentile goal. Experiment with different strategies during your mock tests. This trial-and-error process will help you discover which approach yields the best results for you. Consider factors like time allocation per section and the maximum time you'll spend on a single question. The most effective strategy you develop through practice should be the one you implement on exam day.

## Evaluate Your Performance Systematically

Regular evaluation is critical for improvement. After every practice session or mock test, meticulously review your performance. Document your mistakes to identify your strong and weak areas. This insight will guide your preparation, helping you decide which types of questions to prioritize. Avoid overconfidence in your strengths and never ignore your weaknesses. Strive to transform your weaker areas into strengths through dedicated practice.

## Do Not Succumb to Pressure

One distinguishing factor among high scorers is their ability to manage exam pressure. Regardless of any unexpected challenges during the test, it's vital to remain calm and composed. If you encounter a question that you cannot solve immediately, it's wise to move on and return to it later if time permits. Panicking and dwelling on a single difficult question can consume valuable time, potentially preventing you from completing other solvable parts of the paper.

By adopting these focused strategies and maintaining a resilient attitude, you can significantly enhance your chances of achieving a 99+ percentile in CAT 2021, paving your way to a top MBA program.
