# CAT 2024 Topper Interview: How Dhruv Mittal Transitioned from CA Topper to CAT Success of 99.69

Dhruv Mittal, a standout achiever from Gwalior, secured an impressive 99.67 percentile in the CAT 2024 exam. Having already excelled in the intermediate level of the Chartered Accountancy (CA) exam, his journey offers valuable insights into achieving top scores in competitive exams.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Early Start for Comprehensive Preparation:** Dhruv began his CAT preparation eight months before the exam, allowing ample time to build a strong foundation across all sections, especially in areas he found challenging.
*   **Strategic Section Prioritization:** He initially focused on Quantitative Aptitude (QA) and Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (DILR), sections he felt more comfortable with, before tackling Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC).
*   **Importance of Mock Tests:** In the final month, Dhruv dedicated himself to daily mock tests, which were crucial for refining his strategy and improving his performance under exam conditions.
*   **Holistic Development:** Despite his rigorous academic schedule, Dhruv maintained a balanced lifestyle, pursuing extracurricular activities like volleyball, emphasizing the importance of a well-rounded personality.

## Dhruv's CAT Score Breakdown

Dhruv's performance across the different sections of CAT 2024 demonstrates a strong command of all areas:

*   **Overall CAT Percentile:** 99.67
*   **VARC Percentile:** 95.23
*   **DILR Percentile:** 99.51
*   **QA Percentile:** 99.67

## Starting the CAT Journey

Dhruv commenced his CAT preparation in March 2024, dedicating eight months to the rigorous process. This was during his final year of graduation at Maharani Laxmibai College of Excellence, Gwalior, which he completed in August 2024. This early start allowed him to manage his academic commitments alongside his CAT aspirations effectively.

## Preparation Strategy: A Phased Approach

Dhruv's preparation strategy was methodical. He started by focusing on the Quantitative Aptitude (QA) section, finding it more accessible. For the first one to two months, he concentrated on building a strong base in QA and Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (DILR). He then spent another two to three months practicing extensively and revisiting foundational concepts through preparation videos. Initially, he postponed VARC preparation due to apprehension, but eventually integrated it into his study plan, solving questions across all three sections. The final month was entirely dedicated to taking CAT mock tests daily, which he found instrumental in his success.

## Overcoming Challenges: The VARC Section

One of Dhruv's biggest challenges was the Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC) section. He admitted to postponing its preparation due to initial apprehension. However, his eventual focus on this section, combined with consistent practice, enabled him to achieve a respectable 95.23 percentile, contributing significantly to his overall high score. His experience highlights that addressing weaker sections, even if daunting, is essential for comprehensive success.

Dhruv Mittal's journey from a CA topper to a 99.67 percentile in CAT 2024 is a testament to strategic planning, consistent effort, and a balanced approach to preparation.
