# Big Data, Hadoop and Spark Basics by IBM via Edx: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's data-driven world, understanding and managing vast amounts of information is crucial for businesses. This foundational course, offered by IBM through edX, provides an excellent starting point for anyone looking to enter the exciting field of big data.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a beginner-friendly online course, delivered entirely in English, focusing on the fundamentals of big data and its processing tools.
*   The program is self-paced and designed to be completed in approximately 6 weeks.
*   You can audit the course content for free or opt for a verified certificate from IBM by paying a fee.
*   It covers essential big data concepts and introduces key technologies like Apache Hadoop and Apache Spark.

## Course Overview and Structure

This introductory computer science course is specifically designed to help learners grasp the core concepts of big data, its significance in modern business, and the primary tools used to manage it. It's ideal for individuals interested in data management, particularly those with basic computer and IT literacy. The course is structured into four modules:

*   **Module 1: What is Big Data?** This section defines big data, discusses its impact, explores parallel processing concepts, and reviews various big data use cases and tools.
*   **Module 2: Introduction to the Hadoop Ecosystem** Learners will dive into Hadoop, understand MapReduce, and explore components like HDFS, Hive, and HBase, including practical lab exercises.
*   **Module 3: Introduction to Apache Spark** This module explains why Apache Spark is essential, covers functional and parallel programming basics using Resilient Distributed Datasets (RDDs), and introduces DataFrames and SparkSQL.
*   **Module 4: DataFrames and SparkSQL** The final module focuses on DataFrames and SparkSQL in more detail, including their role in parallel programming, Catalyst and Tungsten optimizers, and Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) processes with DataFrames.

## Learning Experience and Medium

The course is delivered online through a self-study model, primarily using video and text-based materials. All instructions and content are in English, with video transcripts also available in English. Learners will engage with graded assignments and exams to test their understanding throughout the program. This self-paced format allows students to learn at their own convenience and schedule.

## Eligibility and Target Audience

While there are no strict academic prerequisites, edX recommends that participants have a good understanding of basic computer and IT concepts and a genuine interest in data management processes. This program is particularly beneficial for aspiring professionals in roles such as:

*   Big Data Analytics Engineer
*   Big Data Developer
*   Big Data Engineer

It serves as an excellent foundation for anyone looking to build a career in these specialized fields.

## Fees and Certification

The Big Data, Hadoop and Spark Basics course offers two tracks. You can audit the entire course content for free, which provides access to all learning materials. However, to receive a shareable certificate of completion from IBM, you will need to upgrade to the verified track by paying a fee. The exact fee for the verified certificate track can be found on the official edX course page when you enroll.

## How to Apply

To enroll in this program, simply visit the official edX website and locate the "Big Data, Hadoop and Spark Basics" course offered by IBM. From there, you can choose the 'Enroll' option to get started immediately. You will be guided through the registration process on the edX platform.

This course is a fantastic opportunity to gain fundamental knowledge in big data, equipping you with skills highly sought after in today's technology landscape.
