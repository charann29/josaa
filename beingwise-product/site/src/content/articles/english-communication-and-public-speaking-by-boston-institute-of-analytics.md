# English Communication and Public Speaking by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to significantly improve their language proficiency and presentation skills, the English Communication and Public Speaking certification from the Boston Institute of Analytics (BIA) offers a comprehensive program. This course aims to equip learners with essential tools for impactful communication in various professional environments.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program leads to a dual certification in Public Speaking and Leadership from the Boston Institute of Analytics.
*   It combines self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content, offering over 200 hours of learning.
*   The course emphasizes practical, hands-on learning with real-world assignments and live lectures.
*   It covers modern communication tools and technologies, including presentation software and public speaking analysis tools.

## Course Overview

This certification is designed to be a transformative journey, focusing on both language mastery and the art of public speaking. Participants will delve into strategies for effective communication, learn techniques for engaging diverse audiences, and explore how to integrate innovative technologies into their presentations. The curriculum includes practical assignments that build expertise in areas such as public relations, media, and content development, opening doors to various career opportunities from marketing agencies to non-profit organizations.

## Program Structure and Duration

The English Communication and Public Speaking course spans four months, providing over 200 hours of learning content. It adopts a flexible learning model that includes self-study, virtual classroom sessions, and content delivered through video and text. This blended approach ensures a rich and engaging learning experience, combining the convenience of online access with the benefits of interactive instruction.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the English Communication and Public Speaking certification course is ₹85,000. Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a dual certification directly from the Boston Institute of Analytics. This certification acknowledges proficiency in both public speaking and leadership, adding significant value to a professional's profile.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for individuals who wish to elevate their communication and public speaking abilities. It is particularly beneficial for professionals in roles such as:

*   Public Relations Specialists
*   Communication Managers
*   Journalists
*   Teachers

The course provides the skills and confidence needed to excel in these and many other communication-intensive professions.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the English Communication and Public Speaking program, follow these straightforward steps:

1.  Begin by visiting the official Boston Institute of Analytics website.
2.  Locate and select the "Enquire Now" option. Fill out the inquiry form with your contact details; a representative from BIA will then reach out to you.
3.  After discussions, proceed to pay the course fee to gain access to all the learning modules and resources.

This structured approach ensures a smooth admission process for all prospective students.

By enhancing your communication and public speaking skills, this program can significantly boost your professional growth and effectiveness.
