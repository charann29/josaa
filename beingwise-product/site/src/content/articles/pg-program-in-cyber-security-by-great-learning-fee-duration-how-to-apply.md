# PG Program in Cyber Security by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in the critical field of cybersecurity, Great Learning offers a dedicated Post Graduate Program. This online certification, developed in collaboration with Great Lakes Executive Learning, aims to equip students with essential skills and knowledge.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a six-month, postgraduate-level online program focused on various aspects of cybersecurity.
*   The course is designed for self-paced learning, providing flexibility for working professionals or students.
*   It covers topics from network scanning and cryptography to ethical hacking and cloud security.
*   The total program fee is ₹1,25,000 plus GST, with EMI options available.

## Program Overview and Structure

The PG Program in Cyber Security is an online certification course that runs for six months. It is delivered through a blend of video and text-based modules, allowing participants to learn at their own pace. While the core content is self-study, the program also includes virtual classroom sessions, typically held on weekends, for interactive learning and doubt clarification. The entire instruction is in English.

Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from Great Learning, recognizing their achievement in this postgraduate-level program. The curriculum is crafted by industry experts to ensure relevance and practical application in today's cybersecurity landscape.

## What You Will Learn

This program is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge across various cybersecurity domains. Participants will gain insights into:

*   Fundamentals of networking and scripting relevant to security.
*   Principles of security design and architecture.
*   Concepts of ethical hacking and penetration testing.
*   Understanding different types of threats, malware, and system vulnerabilities.
*   Specific areas like cloud security, mobile security, browser, and session security.
*   Techniques for defending against and analyzing application security attacks.
*   The core concepts of cryptography and its applications.

## Who Should Consider This Program?

This program is particularly beneficial for professionals and graduates aiming to either enter or advance within the cybersecurity field. It is well-suited for:

*   Information Technology professionals.
*   Software Developers and Software Engineers.
*   Network Security Engineers.
*   System Architects and System Analysts.
*   Individuals interested in VAPT (Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing) or becoming penetration testers.

Basic familiarity with programming and computer systems is recommended, and a foundational understanding of IT and technology can be an advantage.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the PG Program in Cyber Security is ₹1,25,000, in addition to applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST). Great Learning also offers convenient EMI options, with installments starting from approximately ₹2,876 per month, as per information available on their official program page.

To earn the program certificate, participants must successfully complete all modules and requirements of the course. The certificate is issued by Great Learning, confirming the participant's successful completion of the postgraduate program.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for this program, applicants generally need to hold a Bachelor's degree from a recognized institution. A basic understanding of computer systems and programming is advisable, and any prior exposure to IT or technology concepts would be beneficial for a smoother learning experience.

## How to Apply

The application process for the PG Program in Cyber Security is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official program webpage for Great Learning's PG Program in Cyber Security.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Apply Now' button, which will typically lead to an application form.
3.  Fill in the required basic personal information and submit this initial form.
4.  Proceed to provide professional details as prompted on the subsequent page.
5.  Finally, complete the admission process by submitting the program fees. Once the fees are paid, you can join the course and begin your learning journey.

This program offers a structured pathway for individuals to gain specialized knowledge and skills in the in-demand field of cyber security.
