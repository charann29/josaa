# IBPS RRB 2025: Clerk Prelims Cut off (OUT), Scorecard, Admit Card, PO Interview Call Letter OUT & Exam Date

For those aiming for a career in India's Regional Rural Banks (RRBs), the Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) conducts the IBPS RRB exams annually. This national-level recruitment drive opens doors to various officer and assistant roles across the country.

## Key Takeaways

*   The IBPS RRB Clerk 2025 preliminary exam results were announced on January 23. The exam was held on December 6, 7, 13, and 14, 2025.
*   The preliminary cut-off and scorecards for IBPS RRB Clerk 2025 were released on January 29.
*   Interview call letters for qualified candidates for IBPS RRB PO (Officer Scale I) and SO (Officer Scale II & III) posts have been issued.
*   The IBPS RRB PO 2025 mains exam cut-off and scorecards were released on January 30.

## Recent IBPS RRB 2025 Updates

Several important announcements have recently been made regarding the IBPS RRB 2025 recruitment cycle. The preliminary examination results for Office Assistant (Clerk) positions were declared on January 23, following exams held throughout December 2025. Subsequently, the official preliminary cut-off marks and individual scorecards for the Clerk posts became available on January 29. 

For Officer Scale I (PO) positions, the mains exam results, including cut-offs and scorecards, were published on January 30. Furthermore, candidates who cleared the mains and single online exams for Officer Scale I, II, and III have received their interview call letters, released on January 21. The mains exam for these officer roles took place on December 28. The mains exam for Office Assistants (Clerk) was conducted on February 1, 2026, and its results are expected in the third week of February 2026.

## Understanding the IBPS RRB Exam

The IBPS RRB exam is a highly anticipated national recruitment test for individuals aspiring to join Regional Rural Banks. These banks play a crucial role in rural development, and successful candidates are placed in various roles, from Office Assistants (Clerks) to Officers across different scales. The selection process typically involves preliminary and main examinations, followed by an interview for officer-level positions. The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) is the official body responsible for conducting this examination.

## Upcoming IBPS RRB 2027 Exam Dates

For those planning ahead, the IBPS has already announced key dates for the IBPS RRB 2027 examinations. The preliminary exams for IBPS RRB PO are scheduled for November 21 and 22, 2026. For IBPS RRB Clerk, the preliminary exams will be conducted on December 6, 12, and 13, 2026. The mains exam for Officer Scale I is slated for December 20, 2026, while the mains exam for Office Assistants will take place on January 30, 2027.

Stay tuned to the official IBPS website for all the latest updates and detailed notifications regarding the IBPS RRB examinations.
