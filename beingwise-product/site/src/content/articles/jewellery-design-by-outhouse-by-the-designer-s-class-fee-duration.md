# Jewellery Design by Outhouse by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those passionate about the intricate world of jewellery and keen to learn its creation process, The Designer's Class offers an online certification program in Jewellery Design, mentored by the renowned duo Kaabia and Sasha Grewal of Outhouse. This course provides a foundational understanding of designing and crafting jewellery, ideal for beginners.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a self-paced, online certification course offered by The Designer's Class.
*   The program is taught by Kaabia and Sasha Grewal, founders of Outhouse.
*   It spans 48 hours of learning content, delivered through 14 lessons, and is accessible for one year.
*   Upon successful completion with a 70% score on the final assessment, participants receive a certificate and may be considered for internship opportunities.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Jewellery Design by Outhouse program is structured into 14 comprehensive lessons, designed to guide learners through the entire journey of jewellery creation. The content is delivered through a blend of video and text-based materials, all in English. This format allows students to progress at their own speed, making it suitable for those balancing other commitments. Total learning time is approximately 48 hours, and access to the course content is provided for one year from the date of purchase.

### What You Will Learn

The curriculum is thoughtfully designed to cover essential aspects of jewellery design, including:

*   A complete walkthrough of the jewellery creation process, from concept to final product.
*   Insights into various materials and precious stones commonly used in jewellery making.
*   Exclusive design techniques and tips employed by Outhouse.
*   Strategies for understanding customer preferences and identifying current market trends in jewellery.

## Eligibility and Certification

This course is open to anyone with a passion for jewellery design, particularly those aspiring to become accessory or jewellery designers. There are no specific prerequisites to enrol. To earn the official certificate of completion from The Designer's Class, participants must achieve a minimum score of 70% on the final assessment.

## Fee and Program Offerings

The fee for the Jewellery Design by Outhouse certification course is ₹2,999. This one-time payment grants full access to all course materials for a duration of one year. Beyond the comprehensive lessons and pre-recorded videos, the program includes assignments to reinforce learning, a certificate of completion upon meeting the criteria, and potential consideration for an internship based on portfolio screening.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Jewellery Design by Outhouse certification course is a straightforward process. Interested individuals can visit the official The Designer's Class website. Once on the course page, simply select the "Buy now" option and complete the payment to gain immediate access to the learning materials.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to delve into the art and business of jewellery design, guided by industry experts, and to gain a recognized certification that can enhance your career prospects in this creative field.
