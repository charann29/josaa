# KIITEE MBA 2026 Registration: Check Application Form Link, Fees & Process

For aspiring MBA students, the KIIT School of Management (KSOM) offers an excellent opportunity through its KIITEE Management entrance examination. The application window for KIITEE MBA 2026 is currently open, allowing candidates to apply using their scores from national-level tests like CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, or GMAT.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Application Window:** Registration for KIITEE MBA 2026 began on October 9, 2025, and concludes on April 15, 2026.
*   **Application Fee:** A non-refundable fee of INR 1,500 is required, payable online.
*   **Eligibility:** Applicants need a bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks, or must be in the final year of their undergraduate program.
*   **Application Mode:** The entire application process is conducted online via the official KSOM website.

## KIITEE Management 2026 Application Process

The application process for KIITEE Management 2026 is entirely online and straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official KSOM website.
2.  **Initiate Application:** Look for and click on the 'Apply Online' link.
3.  **Basic Registration:** Provide your name, email address, mobile number, city, and state. After reviewing, agree to the terms and click 'Register'.
4.  **Complete the Form:** Fill in all required details in the KIITEE Management 2026 application form. Ensure all personal, academic, and contact information is accurate.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of necessary documents, including your passport-sized photograph and signature. If applicable, also upload your Gen-EWS/PwD/OBC-NCL/SC/ST certificate.
6.  **Fee Payment:** Pay the application fee of INR 1,500 using a debit card, credit card, or net banking.

After successful submission, it's advisable to keep a copy of the filled application form for your records.

## Key Points for Filling the KIITEE Application Form 2026

When completing your KIITEE MBA application, keep the following essential points in mind:

*   **Accuracy is Key:** Ensure all fields are filled completely and accurately. Incomplete forms may lead to rejection.
*   **Name and DOB Matching:** Your name, parent's name, and date of birth should precisely match what is stated on your Class 10 pass certificate.
*   **No Correction Window:** There is no provision to correct the application form after it has been submitted. Double-check all details before final submission.
*   **Single Attempt:** Candidates are typically allowed one attempt to apply for KIITEE Management per admission cycle.
*   **Online Mode Only:** The entire application and fee payment process is strictly online.

By carefully following these guidelines, you can ensure a successful application for KIITEE MBA 2026.
