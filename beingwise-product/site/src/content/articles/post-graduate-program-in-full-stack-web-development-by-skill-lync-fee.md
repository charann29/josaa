# Post Graduate Program in Full Stack Web Development by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in web development, the Post Graduate Program in Full Stack Web Development offered by Skill Lync provides a comprehensive online learning experience covering essential skills from front-end to back-end development.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an online, master's level program focusing on full-stack web development.
*   It offers flexible learning plans, including options for 9 months, 18 months, or lifetime access.
*   The program includes career support services to assist with job placement after completion.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a program certificate, with a merit certificate awarded to the top 5% of performers.

## Program Overview

The Post Graduate Program in Full Stack Web Development is an online certification designed to equip students with the skills needed to develop and manage complete web applications. The curriculum covers a wide array of topics, including web application architecture, database management, algorithms, and popular programming languages such as Java, JavaScript, and SQL.

Delivered entirely online through video and text-based modules, the program uses English as its medium of instruction. It's structured to provide a deep understanding of how websites function, from their initial design to their deployment and maintenance, preparing participants for professional roles in the tech industry.

## Learning Plans and Duration

Skill Lync offers three distinct learning plans for this program, each catering to different preferences for access duration and support:

*   **Basic Plan:** Provides 9 months of access to the course content.
*   **Pro Plan:** Offers extended access for 18 months.
*   **Premium Plan:** Grants lifetime access to the course materials, allowing students to revisit content as needed.

All plans are self-paced, allowing learners to fit their studies around existing commitments.

## Curriculum Highlights

The program's syllabus is divided into six main parts, which are further broken down into numerous subtopics to ensure a thorough understanding of full-stack development. Key areas of study include:

*   **Web Fundamentals:** Understanding how the internet works and the evolution of web technologies.
*   **Front-End Development:** Mastering HTML5 for structuring web pages, CSS for styling, and JavaScript, AJAX, and JSON for interactive elements.
*   **Back-End Development:** Gaining proficiency in Java for data structures and algorithms, and learning about various database technologies, including SQL and MongoDB, for creating and managing robust databases.
*   **Advanced Concepts:** Exploring web security, web services, optimizing storage and data flow, and principles of user interface (UI) design.

Learning is reinforced through online sessions, projects, workshops, case studies, and video content.

## Eligibility and Certification

**Eligibility Criteria:**

Candidates are eligible to apply if they hold a B.E., M.E., B.Tech, or M.Tech degree in fields such as ECE, IT, EEE, CSE, Mechanical, or Civil engineering.

**Certification:**

Upon successful completion of the program, all participants will receive a program completion certificate from Skill Lync. Additionally, the top 5% of students based on their performance throughout the course will be awarded a special merit certificate, recognizing their exceptional achievement.

## Program Fees

The total fee for the Post Graduate Program in Full Stack Web Development is ₹2,75,000. For flexibility, EMI options are available, with installments starting from ₹14,375 per month.

Skill Lync also provides career support services to assist students with job placement after they complete the program, helping them transition into professional roles in web development.

For more detailed information on application procedures and upcoming batch dates, interested candidates should refer to the official Skill Lync program page.
