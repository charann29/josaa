# Health Informatics for Better and Safer Healthcare by IsraelX via Edx: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

As healthcare worldwide becomes more complex, technology is playing an increasingly vital role in understanding and managing it. This program is designed for professionals and graduates keen to explore how and why healthcare providers are increasingly relying on digital tools like electronic health records.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn how health information systems contribute to safer and more reliable patient care.
*   Understand the core principles and applications of health information technology, including electronic health records and clinical decision support.
*   Discover career paths within the rapidly growing field of health information technology.
*   Engage with self-paced, video-based learning content over approximately four weeks.

## Course Overview

Developed in collaboration with IsraelX, this online course delves into the promise of technical advancements for a more secure and dependable healthcare system. You will explore the mechanisms behind these technologies and how they are tailored to meet the specific demands of the healthcare sector. The curriculum spans health and safety, computer science, and medicine, aiming to bridge existing gaps in health informatics education.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this course, you will have a solid grasp of various critical concepts in health informatics. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Careers in Health Information Technology:** Explore diverse professional opportunities in this field.
*   **Core Disciplines:** Understand the fundamental areas that make up health information technology.
*   **Health Terminologies:** Learn about the importance and structure of medical terminology.
*   **Clinical Decision Support:** Discover how these systems aid healthcare professionals.
*   **Text and Data Mining:** Understand their application in healthcare for insights and analysis.
*   **Patient Safety:** Learn how health information systems enhance patient safety.
*   **Electronic Health Records (EHRs):** Gain knowledge about their function and usage.
*   **Usability Challenges:** Examine the impact of low usability in health information systems.
*   **Image Processing:** Understand its benefits and applications in health imagery.

## Course Structure and Duration

This is a self-paced learning program delivered through video and text-based content. It is designed to be completed over approximately four weeks, requiring an estimated 3-4 hours of effort per week. The entire course content is delivered in English.

## Eligibility and Certification

There are no specific prerequisites in terms of educational background or work experience to enroll in this course. Anyone interested in the subject can register and begin learning.

### Certificate Details

While the course content can be audited for free, obtaining a verified certificate requires payment of the applicable fee. To be eligible for the institutionally-verified certificate, learners must also complete all 11 modules and submit the final project.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the course, follow these simple steps:

1.  Visit the official edX website.
2.  Locate the search bar on the website.
3.  Type "Health Informatics for Better and Safer Healthcare" into the search bar.
4.  Select the course when it appears in the search results to proceed with registration.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to delve into the evolving world of health informatics, equipping you with knowledge crucial for modern healthcare challenges.
