# CMAT 2026 Registration: Last Date, Fees, How to Apply @cmat.nta.nic.in

For aspiring MBA students, the Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is a significant gateway to numerous management programs across India. Understanding the registration process, key dates, and requirements for CMAT 2026 is crucial for a smooth application.

## Key Takeaways

*   The CMAT 2026 registration window was open from October 17 to November 24, 2025.
*   The application fee was INR 1200 for General male candidates and INR 1000 for Reserved category male, female, and transgender candidates.
*   Candidates needed to complete the application online through the official CMAT-NTA website.
*   The CMAT 2026 exam took place on January 25, 2026, with results announced on February 17, 2026.

## CMAT 2026 Application Process

The application for CMAT 2026 was an entirely online process, managed by the National Testing Agency (NTA). Here’s a breakdown of the steps candidates followed:

### Step 1: Online Registration

Candidates began by visiting the official CMAT-NTA website. On the registration page, they had to provide essential personal details such as their name, date of birth, city, email ID, mobile number, and create a password. After entering an OTP received on their mobile, they would submit these details, and a unique 12-digit application number would be generated.

### Step 2: Filling the Application Form

Using the newly generated application number and their chosen password, candidates logged back into the portal. This step involved filling out the detailed application form, which included:

*   **Academic Information:** Details about their educational background.
*   **Personal Information:** Further personal details.
*   **Test City Selection:** Candidates could choose up to four preferred test cities from a list of 124 options. It's important to note that once submitted, these choices could not be altered.

Candidates were advised to carefully review all entered information before final submission, as changes might not be possible later.

### Step 3: Fee Payment and Confirmation

The final step involved paying the application fee online. After successful payment, candidates were required to download and save the fee acknowledgment page as proof of their application submission.

## CMAT 2026 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for CMAT 2026, candidates needed to meet the following conditions:

*   Hold a bachelor's degree or an equivalent qualification from a recognized university.
*   Students in their final year of a bachelor's degree program were also eligible to apply.
*   While CMAT itself does not have a minimum score requirement for graduation, many colleges accepting CMAT scores typically require a minimum of 50% in graduation (45% for reserved category candidates).

## Important Dates for CMAT 2026

Here’s a look at the key dates for the CMAT 2026 examination cycle:

| Event                          | Date              |
| :----------------------------- | :---------------- |
| CMAT 2026 Registration Window  | Oct 17 - Nov 24, 2025 |
| CMAT 2026 Form Correction      | Nov 26 - Nov 28, 2025 |
| CMAT 2026 Admit Card Release   | Jan 21, 2026      |
| CMAT 2026 Exam Date            | Jan 25, 2026      |
| CMAT 2026 Result Announcement  | Feb 17, 2026      |

## Documents Required for CMAT 2026 Registration

To ensure a smooth application process, candidates were advised to have the following documents ready before starting their CMAT 2026 registration:

*   Class 10 Marksheet
*   Class 12 Marksheet
*   Graduation Marksheet (for those who have completed their degree; final year students could use average scores of previous years)
*   Caste Certificate (if applicable)
*   PWD/DA Certificate (if applicable)
*   Scanned Signature on a white paper
*   Recent Passport-sized Photograph (preferably against a white background)
*   Details for online payment (Debit/Credit Card or Net Banking)

For the upcoming CMAT 2027 exam, NTA is expected to commence the registration process around October 2026. Keep an eye on the official CMAT-NTA website for the latest updates.
