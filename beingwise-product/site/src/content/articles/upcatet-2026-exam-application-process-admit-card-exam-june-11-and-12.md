# UPCATET 2026 Exam: Application Process, Admit Card, Exam (June 11&12), Result & More

The Uttar Pradesh Combined Agricultural and Technology Entrance Test (UPCATET) is a key state-level examination for students aspiring to pursue agricultural and related courses in various universities across Uttar Pradesh. This annual test opens doors to a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

## Key takeaways

*   The application window for UPCATET 2026 opened on March 31, 2026, with the final date to apply being May 1, 2026.
*   The UPCATET 2026 examination for undergraduate courses is scheduled for June 10, 2026, while postgraduate and PhD exams will be held on June 11, 2026.
*   Admit cards will be available for download from the official UPCATET portal between June 1 and June 9, 2026.
*   Results for the UPCATET 2026 exam are expected to be released on June 24, 2026.

## Understanding UPCATET

UPCATET is a crucial entrance examination conducted once a year to assess candidates seeking admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in agricultural fields. It is specifically designed for students who hold a domicile in Uttar Pradesh. The exam facilitates entry into prestigious institutions like Chandra Shekhar Azad University of Agriculture & Technology, Kanpur; Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel University of Agriculture and Technology, Meerut; Narendra Deva University of Agriculture and Technology, Kumarganj; and Banda University of Agriculture and Technology, Banda, among others.

The examination is conducted offline, in a pen-and-paper format, and has a duration of three hours.

### Courses Offered Through UPCATET

UPCATET provides admission to a diverse array of programs, including:

*   B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science)
*   B.F.Sc. (Bachelor of Fisheries Science)
*   B.Tech. (Bachelor of Technology)
*   B.V.Sc. & A.H. (Bachelor of Veterinary Science and Animal Husbandry)
*   M.Sc. (Master of Science)
*   M.Tech. (Master of Technology)
*   M.V.Sc. (Master of Veterinary Science)
*   MBA (Master of Business Administration)

## UPCATET 2026 Application Process

Candidates interested in applying for UPCATET 2026 must submit their applications online through the official UPCATET website. The application process began on March 31, 2026, and the last date to complete the submission is May 1, 2026. Along with the online application form, candidates are required to pay the application fee using an online payment method.

## UPCATET 2026 Admit Card

The admit card for UPCATET 2026 is a mandatory document for appearing in the examination. It will be released online on the official portal between June 1 and June 9, 2026. Candidates must download their hall ticket from the official website by providing their registration number, preferred course, and date of birth. It is advisable to take a printout of the admit card for examination day and future reference.

## UPCATET 2026 Examination Details

The UPCATET 2026 exam dates are set for June 10 and June 11, 2026. Undergraduate course examinations will take place on June 10, 2026, while postgraduate and PhD course examinations will be held on June 11, 2026. The exam consists solely of multiple-choice questions (MCQs), each with four options. This format helps evaluate both the speed and fundamental knowledge of the candidates across various topics.

## UPCATET 2026 Result

The results for the UPCATET 2026 examination are scheduled to be announced on June 24, 2026. Candidates will be able to download their results directly from the official UPCATET website once they are published.

Staying informed about these dates and processes is crucial for all aspiring candidates. We wish you the best in your preparation for UPCATET 2026!
