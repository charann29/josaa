# App Academy Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online App Academy Courses

App Academy offers intensive coding bootcamps designed to transform individuals into skilled software engineers. These programs focus on practical, in-demand skills and provide comprehensive support to help students launch their careers in technology.

## Key takeaways

*   App Academy provides immersive coding bootcamps for aspiring software engineers.
*   Both 16-week and 24-week programs are available, covering essential programming languages and web development skills.
*   A key feature is the deferred tuition option, allowing students to pay only after securing a high-paying job.
*   Programs include extensive project work, career guidance, and strong peer and instructor support.

## The 24-Week Software Engineering Immersive

This comprehensive online bootcamp is tailored for individuals eager to dive deep into software engineering. It's an excellent choice for learning from home while mastering crucial programming abilities.

The curriculum is thoughtfully structured, featuring hands-on lessons in core languages like Python and JavaScript. Throughout the program, students complete four individual projects and various group assignments, which are vital for building a strong professional portfolio. The course begins with preparatory work to ensure all participants have the foundational skills needed for success.

Students in the 24-week immersive program benefit from a collaborative environment, working alongside motivated classmates on group projects. They also receive dedicated guidance from a career advisor, a cohort mentor, module instructors, and teaching assistants. A significant advantage of this program is its deferred tuition model, allowing students to enroll without upfront costs and pay only after securing a well-compensated position in the tech industry.

### Skills Covered

*   Python proficiency
*   SQL database knowledge
*   CSS for styling
*   Web application development

## The 16-Week "Become a Software Engineer" Program

This accelerated training aims to equip students with the necessary skills to become successful software developers in just 16 weeks. It's designed to turn individuals into proficient programmers, even those without prior coding experience.

The program covers high-demand skills such as JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, HTML, SQL, and CSS, enabling students to design a compelling professional portfolio. Similar to its longer counterpart, this bootcamp offers built-in support mechanisms and a deferred payment plan, meaning tuition is only due once you are employed.

Participants learn from a dedicated team of technical instructors, career coaches, and fellow students, ensuring support at every stage. App Academy is recognized for its commitment to preparing students for thriving careers. This intensive training transforms individuals into full-stack web developers. While the first three weeks are conducted remotely, the remainder of the program is completed on campus.

### Skills Covered

*   SQL database knowledge
*   CSS for styling
*   Web application development
