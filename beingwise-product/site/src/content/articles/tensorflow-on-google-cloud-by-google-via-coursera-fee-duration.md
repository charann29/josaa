# TensorFlow on Google Cloud by Google via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen on diving into machine learning, the "TensorFlow on Google Cloud" program, offered through Coursera, provides a practical introduction to one of the most widely used open-source machine learning frameworks.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an intermediate-level online course focused on TensorFlow, designed for self-paced learning.
*   It requires approximately 19-20 hours of study and can be completed flexibly over about four weeks.
*   While auditing the course content is free, verified certificates and full access to graded assignments come with a fee.
*   No specific academic qualifications are needed to enroll, making it accessible to a broad audience.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This online certification course is structured to introduce learners to TensorFlow 2.x, focusing on its application within the Google Cloud environment. The program covers essential aspects like designing and building TensorFlow models, working with datasets, and understanding the TensorFlow API hierarchy. It's delivered through a mix of video lectures, readings, quizzes, assignments, and practice exercises, fostering an interactive learning experience.

The course emphasizes self-study, allowing participants to progress at their own speed. There are no strict deadlines for completing modules or limitations on attendance, providing maximum flexibility for those balancing studies with other commitments. Subtitles are available in multiple languages, including Russian, Spanish, Portuguese, English, and French, to assist learners with language preferences.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of the program, participants will gain valuable skills and knowledge in several key areas:

*   **Keras API:** Understand and apply both the functional and sequential APIs within Keras for model building.
*   **Data Pipelines:** Learn to design and construct input data pipelines using TensorFlow 2.x.
*   **Data Manipulation:** Utilize the `tf.data` library effectively for managing and manipulating large datasets.
*   **Cloud AI Platforms:** Gain insights into deploying machine learning models at scale using Google Cloud AI platforms.
*   **TensorFlow Architecture:** Familiarize yourself with the TensorFlow API hierarchy.
*   **Core Concepts:** Explore activation functions and various regularization techniques crucial for model optimization.

## Fees and Certification

While the course offers a free audit option, providing access to much of the learning material, obtaining a verified certificate and full participation in graded assignments requires a fee. A 7-day free trial is also available for those wishing to preview the paid content.

Here’s a breakdown of the typical fee structure:

| Particulars                          | Fee Amount (INR) |
| :----------------------------------- | :--------------- |
| TensorFlow on Google Cloud - Audit   | Free             |
| TensorFlow on Google Cloud - 1 month | ₹4,115           |
| TensorFlow on Google Cloud - 3 months | ₹8,230           |
| TensorFlow on Google Cloud - 6 months | ₹12,345          |

The certificate is issued by Coursera upon successful completion of all course lessons and requirements.

## How to Apply

Applying for the "TensorFlow on Google Cloud" course is a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official course page on the Coursera platform.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Enroll' button, which will guide you through the registration.
3.  Follow the on-screen instructions to either enroll for free (audit track) or choose a paid subscription plan for full access and certification.

This program offers an excellent pathway for anyone looking to build a foundational understanding of TensorFlow and its practical applications in cloud environments.
