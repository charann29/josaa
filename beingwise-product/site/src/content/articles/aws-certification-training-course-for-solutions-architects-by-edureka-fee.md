## AWS Certification Training Course for Solutions Architects: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Cloud computing has become a cornerstone for many global businesses, with Amazon Web Services (AWS) leading the charge. This training program aims to equip aspiring and current IT professionals with the essential skills to design and implement robust AWS solutions.

## Key takeaways

*   Gain comprehensive knowledge of AWS architectural principles and services.
*   Prepare for the AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate (SAA-C02) exam.
*   Benefit from a flexible learning format including live and self-paced options.
*   Receive an industry-recognized certificate upon successful completion of the course.

## Course Overview

This program is designed to provide an in-depth understanding of AWS architectural principles and the diverse services offered. The curriculum ensures participants are well-prepared to build reliable and highly available AWS infrastructures, meeting the demands of modern digital enterprises. Upon completion, participants will receive a certification, enhancing their professional profile in the cloud computing domain.

## Learning Experience and Support

The course offers a dynamic learning environment that includes live online classes, self-paced study options, and access to online classrooms. Participants benefit from lifetime access to course materials and dedicated 24/7 expert support to address any queries. The instruction is delivered by skilled professionals, ensuring a high-quality learning experience.

### Program Offerings

*   Online learning modules
*   Practical assignments
*   Comprehensive study materials
*   Access to online forums for peer interaction and discussion
*   Introductory demo sessions

## Course Fee and Certification

The total course fee for this program is ₹16,195. For those who prefer a staggered payment approach, an EMI option is available at ₹5,399 per month. Upon successful completion of the training, participants will receive an AWS Solutions Architect Certificate from the course provider.

### Fee Details

| Fee Component | Amount |
| :------------ | :----- |
| Course Fee    | ₹16,195 |
| EMI (no cost) | ₹5,399 / month |

## Who Should Enroll

This certification training is suitable for a wide range of professionals looking to enhance their cloud computing skills and career prospects. It is particularly recommended for:

*   Freshers aiming for careers in cloud-based technology fields.
*   Developers and architects seeking to specialize in AWS.
*   Professionals interested in migrating to and utilizing AWS solutions.
*   Individuals with a background in IT infrastructure.
*   Professionals with experience in virtualization.
*   Those with knowledge of server management, network security, compliance, and application development.

## Admission Process

To enroll in the AWS Certification Training Course for Solutions Architects, follow these simple steps:

1.  Locate the 'Enroll Now' button on the program page.
2.  Click 'Enroll Now' and provide your email address and phone number in the pop-up window.
3.  You will be redirected to the payments page. Here, you can select your preferred batch or choose to decide later.
4.  If you have a coupon code, apply it before proceeding to finalize the payment.

Once the payment is completed, you gain access to the course and can begin your journey toward AWS certification.

## What You Will Learn

Throughout this course, you will gain extensive knowledge of Amazon Web Services, covering critical areas such as:

*   Understanding AWS Architecture and various cloud computing models.
*   Utilizing compute services like AWS Lambda and Elastic Beanstalk.
*   Working with management tools including AWS CloudTrail and AWS CloudWatch.
*   Exploring different migration services, such as DataSync.
*   Leveraging various storage services like AWS EBS, AWS Global Accelerator, and Storage Gateway.

This program is your gateway to mastering AWS and advancing your career in cloud architecture.
