# RPF SI 2025: Preference Form Out, Final Result, Merit List, Cut Off Marks & Vacancies

The Railway Protection Force (RPF) Sub-Inspector (SI) recruitment is a significant opportunity for individuals aspiring to join the Indian Railways. This page provides essential updates on the 2025 recruitment cycle, including recent announcements regarding results, cut-offs, and vacancy details.

## Key Takeaways

*   The final RPF SI 2025 results, along with the merit list and cut-off marks, were officially released on August 26, 2025.
*   Candidates were required to submit their zone-wise preference forms by September 3, 2025, following the release of vacancy details on August 28, 2025.
*   A total of 452 vacancies for the Sub-Inspector post within the RPF and Railway Protection Special Force (RPSF) are being filled through this recruitment.
*   The Physical Test (PET/PMT) for shortlisted candidates was conducted from June 22 to July 2, 2025.

## RPF SI Final Result and Merit List 2025

The Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) announced the final results for the RPF SI 2025 examination on August 26, 2025. These results were published as a comprehensive final merit list in PDF format, available on the official regional websites of the RRBs. The merit list contains the roll numbers of all candidates who have successfully qualified for the Sub-Inspector positions. To be considered for selection, candidates needed to achieve the category-specific qualifying marks set by the examination authority. Selection is strictly based on merit.

## RPF SI Cut-Off Marks 2025

Alongside the final results, the official cut-off marks for the RPF SI 2025 examination were also released on August 26, 2025. These cut-off scores are crucial for candidates to understand their standing and were published separately for male and female applicants. The qualifying marks vary by category, ensuring a fair selection process based on the performance of candidates in each group.

| Category    | Qualifying Marks (in Percent) |
| :---------- | :---------------------------- |
| General/OBC | 35                            |
| SC and ST   | 30                            |

## RPF SI Vacancies and Zonal Preference 2025

On August 28, 2025, the RRBs released the detailed zone-wise vacancies for the RPF SI 2025 recruitment. Simultaneously, the zonal preference form was made available for candidates. This form allowed qualified candidates to indicate their preferred railway zones for posting. The deadline for submitting these preference forms was September 3, 2025. The recruitment drive aims to fill a total of 452 Sub-Inspector positions across the Railway Protection Force and the Railway Protection Special Force.

## Key Dates for RPF SI 2025 Recruitment

Understanding the timeline of the RPF SI recruitment process is essential for all applicants. Here are some of the critical dates:

*   **Computer-Based Test (CBT) Dates:** December 2, 3, 9, 12, and 13, 2024
*   **Answer Key Release:** December 17, 2024 (along with question papers and response sheets)
*   **Objection Window for Answer Key:** Until December 22, 2024
*   **Initial Exam Results Release:** March 3, 2025
*   **Physical Test (PET/PMT) Dates:** June 22 to July 2, 2025
*   **Final Result, Merit List & Cut-off Release:** August 26, 2025
*   **Zone-Wise Vacancies & Preference Form Release:** August 28, 2025
*   **Last Date for Preference Form Submission:** September 3, 2025

This comprehensive overview should help candidates stay informed about the RPF SI 2025 recruitment process as they embark on their journey with the Indian Railways.
