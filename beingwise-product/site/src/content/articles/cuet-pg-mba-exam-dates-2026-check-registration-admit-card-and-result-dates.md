# CUET-PG MBA Exam Dates 2026: Check Registration, Admit Card & Result Dates

The Common University Entrance Test for Post Graduate (CUET PG) admissions is a crucial examination for students aspiring to pursue various master's programs, including MBA, across numerous Indian universities. Understanding the key dates for registration, admit cards, exams, and results is essential for a smooth application process.

## Key takeaways

*   The CUET PG 2026 application window was open from December 14, 2025, to January 23, 2026.
*   The actual examinations for CUET PG 2026 took place between March 6 and March 27, 2026.
*   Provisional answer keys and candidate response sheets are expected in April 2026, with an objection window to follow.
*   The final CUET PG 2026 results are tentatively scheduled for release in April 2026.

## CUET PG 2026 Application and Exam Schedule

For those planning to take the CUET PG for MBA admissions, the application process for 2026 has already concluded. The National Testing Agency (NTA), which conducts the exam, made the application forms available from December 14, 2025, until January 23, 2026. Following the application phase, the examinations were held over several days in March.

## Understanding the CUET PG Answer Key and Objection Process

Following the completion of the exams, the NTA will release a provisional answer key. This key, along with individual candidate response sheets, will be accessible through the official NTA website using your application ID and password. The response sheet will display the questions, the NTA's tentative answer, and your selected answer. This allows candidates to estimate their scores.

Crucially, there will be a specific window during which candidates can raise objections against any answers they believe are incorrect. The NTA will announce the last date for submitting these objections through its official communication. After reviewing all submitted objections, the NTA will then prepare and release a final answer key, which will form the basis for calculating the final scores.

## CUET PG 2026 Result and Scorecard

The CUET PG 2026 results are tentatively expected in April 2026, shortly after the final answer key is published. The NTA will calculate candidate scores based on this final answer key. Along with the results, candidates will receive a CUET PG scorecard. This scorecard is a vital document, detailing your performance and scores, which you will need for the subsequent counselling and admission processes.

The NTA will also compile a merit list, potentially including category-wise lists, which will be instrumental in determining eligibility for counselling rounds at various participating institutions.

## CUET PG 2026 Counselling and Admissions

Once the results are declared, the participating universities and institutions will commence their individual counselling processes. This typically begins within approximately one month of the result announcement. Admission to specific MBA programs will depend on several factors, including your CUET PG score, your preferred choices of institutions and courses, your category, and the specific cut-off scores set by each institution for their programs.

Candidates are advised to regularly check the official websites of their chosen universities for detailed counselling schedules and requirements, as these processes are managed by the individual institutions rather than the NTA.

Staying informed about these dates and processes is key to successfully navigating your path to postgraduate MBA studies through CUET PG 2026.
