# Data Analyst with Business Analysts by CodeAsylums: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a strong foundation in the rapidly growing fields of data and business analysis, the Data Analyst with Business Analysts program by CodeAsylums offers a comprehensive online learning experience. This three-month course is designed to equip aspiring professionals with essential skills for various roles in data science, machine learning, and business intelligence.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a three-month, fully online program delivered through live virtual sessions and self-study materials.
*   It offers extensive live mentorship, hands-on coding, case studies, and interview preparation.
*   The course fee is ₹35,000, inclusive of GST.
*   Upon successful completion of all program requirements, participants receive a verified certificate.

## Program Overview and Structure

The Data Analyst with Business Analysts program is structured over three months, featuring more than 45 live sessions conducted every other day. These sessions are complemented by discussion forums and case studies, providing a practical and interactive learning environment. The curriculum is delivered in English, using a blend of video and text-based content for flexibility.

The program focuses on developing core competencies crucial for today's data-driven roles. It aims to introduce participants to key tools and concepts, preparing them for future positions such as Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, Business Analyst, or Database Administrator.

## What You Will Learn

The syllabus is carefully crafted to provide a robust understanding of fundamental data analysis tools and techniques. Key areas covered include:

*   **Fundamentals:** A solid introduction to Microsoft Excel, quantitative aptitude (guesstimates), and case study analysis.
*   **Programming Languages:** In-depth knowledge of Python and R, essential for data manipulation and statistical analysis.
*   **Database Management:** Proficiency in Sequential Query Language (SQL), covering operators, functions, queries, and module packages.
*   **Data Visualization:** Practical skills in Tableau, a leading tool for creating interactive data dashboards.

Participants will engage in hands-on live coding sessions and work through at least one comprehensive case study to apply their learning in real-world scenarios.

## Eligibility and Certification

There are no specific educational background requirements to enroll in the Data Analyst with Business Analysts program. It is open to anyone with an interest in starting a career in data and business analysis.

To earn the verified certificate from CodeAsylums, participants must successfully complete all program requirements. These include various assessments, tests, case studies, and active participation in discussions, ensuring a thorough grasp of the material.

## Fee Structure and Admission Process

The total fee for the Data Analyst with Business Analysts program is ₹35,000, which is inclusive of GST. This fee covers all aspects of the three-month curriculum, including live sessions, mentorship, and access to learning materials.

### How to Apply

Enrolling in the Data Analyst with Business Analysts online course involves a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Official Program Page:** Begin by navigating to the official webpage for the Data Analyst with Business Analysts program.
2.  **Request a Callback:** Fill in the required details on the form to request a callback from the program advisors.
3.  **Receive Enrollment Details:** A representative will contact you to provide all necessary information and clarify any queries regarding enrollment.
4.  **Complete Fee Payment:** The final step involves paying the course fee to confirm your enrollment and begin your learning journey.

This program is an excellent opportunity for aspiring data enthusiasts to gain practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge to kickstart their careers.
