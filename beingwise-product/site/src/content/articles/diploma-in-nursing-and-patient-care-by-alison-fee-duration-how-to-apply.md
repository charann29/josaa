# Diploma in Nursing and Patient Care by Alison: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For individuals in India aspiring to enter the healthcare field or enhance their existing patient care skills, Alison offers a Diploma in Nursing and Patient Care. This online program is designed to provide foundational knowledge and practical insights into various aspects of patient support and nursing assistance.

## Key Takeaways

*   This diploma is delivered entirely online, making it accessible from anywhere with an internet connection.
*   The course content focuses on essential patient care techniques, hygiene, safety, and basic medical procedures.
*   Upon successful completion, students can receive a certificate from Alison, validating their acquired skills.
*   The program is structured with regular assessments to ensure understanding of the material.

## Course Structure and Learning Approach

The Diploma in Nursing and Patient Care is offered through a virtual classroom environment, utilizing video-based learning modules. The medium of instruction is English, ensuring broad accessibility for Indian students. This self-paced format allows learners to progress through the material at their own convenience, fitting studies around other commitments.

## Detailed Syllabus

The curriculum covers a comprehensive range of topics crucial for patient care. It is divided into modules, each with specific learning outcomes. Regular assessments are integrated throughout the course to test comprehension.

### Core Modules:

*   **Adult Patient Care:** Understanding basic human needs, patient care equipment, and unit cleaning.
*   **Advanced Principles of Patient Hygiene:** Techniques for patient bathing, oral hygiene, back massages, and personal care.
*   **Environmental Health and Practical Nursing:** Impact of environmental factors on nursing practice and safety measures in patient settings.
*   **Health and Safety for Healthcare Professionals:** Proper body mechanics and preventing pathogen transmission.
*   **Positioning and Ambulating the Adult Patient:** Safe techniques for patient positioning and assisting with movement.
*   **Active and Passive Ranges of Motion:** Exercises for immobile patients and understanding the effects of immobility.
*   **Diet Therapy:** Factors influencing diet, therapeutic diets, and the nurse's role in dietary management.
*   **The Physical Examination:** Function of examinations, the nurse's role, patient positioning, and examining body systems.
*   **Specimen Collection:** Procedures for throat cultures and various types of specimen collection.
*   **The Physical Assessment:** Detailed examination of patient appearance, behavior, and assessment of cardiovascular, respiratory, gastrointestinal, genitourinary, musculoskeletal, and integumentary systems.
*   **Measuring Vital Signs:** Accurate measurement of temperature, pulse, respiration rate, and blood pressure.

### Assessments:

The program includes multiple assessments throughout its duration:

*   Diploma in Nursing and Patient Care - First Assessment
*   Diploma in Nursing and Patient Care - Second Assessment
*   Diploma in Nursing and Patient Care - Third Assessment
*   Diploma in Nursing and Patient Care - Fourth Assessment
*   Diploma in Nursing and Patient Care - Final Assessment (Course Assessment)

## Certification and Fees

Upon successful completion of all modules and assessments, students are eligible to receive a certificate directly from Alison. While the course itself is often available for free, there may be charges associated with obtaining the official certificate. Details regarding certificate fees and different certificate types (e.g., digital, printed) are typically provided on the official Alison platform.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Diploma in Nursing and Patient Care is a straightforward online process. Prospective students need to visit the official Alison website, locate the course, and register for an account. Once registered, they can enroll in the diploma program and begin their learning journey immediately. There are no specific entrance exams or complex application forms; the process is designed for ease of access.

This diploma can be a valuable step for those looking to start or advance their career in patient care, offering essential skills and knowledge through a flexible online format.
