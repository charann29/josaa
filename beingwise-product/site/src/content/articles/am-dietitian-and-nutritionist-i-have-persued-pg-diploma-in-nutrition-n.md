# I am dietitian & Nutritionist. I have persued Pg diploma in Nutrition n Dietetics. I am having an hearing impairment. what qualification is needed to get in to Air force as a dietitian and also what's the qualification needed to be an space nutritionist in NASA? my age is 27.

It's wonderful that you're aiming for such ambitious and specialized roles in dietetics and nutrition. Your existing postgraduate diploma is a great foundation, and with some strategic planning, you can work towards these exciting career paths. Let's explore the requirements for both the Indian Air Force and NASA.

## Key takeaways

*   For the Indian Air Force, a bachelor's degree in a relevant field followed by a postgraduate diploma in dietetics/nutrition is typically required, along with Indian Dietetic Association registration.
*   Your hearing impairment for the Air Force will need direct clarification from their Medical Branch.
*   To become a NASA Space Nutritionist, a Ph.D. in nutrition or a related scientific field, coupled with significant research experience, is essential.
*   NASA has an Equal Opportunity Office that can provide details on accommodations for hearing impairments.

## Becoming a Dietitian in the Indian Air Force

To serve as a dietitian in the Indian Air Force, you'll generally need a strong academic background in nutrition and dietetics. Given your current qualifications, you're already on a good track, but there are specific requirements to fulfill.

### Educational and Professional Qualifications

Typically, candidates are expected to have completed a bachelor's degree in fields like Home Science with a specialization in Food and Nutrition, or even an MBBS, followed by a recognized postgraduate diploma in Dietetics or Nutrition. Your PG diploma fits this requirement well. Additionally, professional registration with the Indian Dietetic Association is usually a mandatory step. This ensures that you meet the professional standards for dietetic practice in India.

### Age Limit

At 27, you are within a suitable age range for many entry-level positions. For Short Service Commission roles, the age limit can sometimes extend up to 40 years, but it's crucial to refer to the latest official recruitment notifications released by the Indian Air Force for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding age criteria for dietitians.

### Hearing Impairment Considerations

Regarding your hearing impairment, this is a critical aspect that requires direct communication with the authorities. The Indian Air Force has specific medical standards for all personnel. We strongly advise you to contact the Air Force Medical Branch directly. They will be able to provide precise information on their policy concerning your specific hearing impairment and whether any accommodations or waivers are possible for a dietitian's role.

## Pursuing a Space Nutritionist Role at NASA

Becoming a Space Nutritionist at NASA is an incredibly specialized and competitive field, demanding a high level of academic achievement and research expertise. It involves contributing to the health and performance of astronauts in extreme environments.

### Advanced Education and Research

For a role like a Space Nutritionist, a Ph.D. is almost always a prerequisite. This would typically be in Nutrition or a closely related scientific discipline such as human physiology, biochemistry, or food science. Extensive research experience is not just beneficial but crucial. Your Ph.D. research should preferably be in areas relevant to human physiology, metabolic responses, or nutrition in challenging environments.

### Relevant Experience and Skills

Prior research specifically in space nutrition, human physiology, or similar fields that involve understanding the body's response to extreme conditions (like microgravity or isolation) is highly desirable. Beyond academic qualifications, strong analytical skills, a proven research track record, excellent communication abilities, and the capacity to work both independently and as part of a multidisciplinary team are essential for success in such a demanding scientific environment.

### Hearing Impairment and Accommodations at NASA

NASA, as a federal agency, adheres to equal opportunity employment principles. For details regarding accommodations and specific requirements for roles, especially concerning medical conditions like hearing impairment, you should reach out to NASA's Equal Opportunity Office. They can provide comprehensive information on their policies and how they handle such situations for potential employees.

Always ensure you refer to the official Indian Air Force recruitment notifications and NASA's official career portals for the most current and detailed requirements. Your determination and existing qualifications are excellent starting points for these extraordinary career paths.
