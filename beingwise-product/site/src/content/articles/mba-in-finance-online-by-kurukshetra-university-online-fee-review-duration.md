# MBA in Finance Online by Kurukshetra University Online: Fee, Review, Duration

For those aiming to advance their careers in the financial sector, an online MBA in Finance offers a flexible pathway to gain specialized knowledge and skills. Kurukshetra University Online provides such a program, designed to equip students for leadership roles in commerce and industry.

## Key Takeaways

*   This online MBA program is a two-year postgraduate degree focused on finance and management.
*   The total program fee is ₹90,000, with an option for installment payments.
*   It emphasizes practical skills through projects, preparing students for real-world financial challenges.
*   The university holds an A++ NAAC accreditation and was established in 1956, indicating a strong academic foundation.

## Program Overview and Structure

Kurukshetra University Online's MBA in Finance is a comprehensive postgraduate degree spanning two years. It's delivered entirely online, providing flexibility for working professionals or those who cannot attend a traditional campus. The curriculum is structured to offer an in-depth understanding of both general management principles and specialized financial concepts.

Students will delve into various aspects of financial management, market analysis, and strategic decision-making. The program aims to foster entrepreneurial skills and research capabilities, crucial for navigating the complexities of modern business and finance.

## Who Should Consider This Course?

This online MBA is particularly well-suited for individuals who already have a background in business or finance and aspire to take on senior management positions in the financial sector. It's designed for those who wish to enhance their expertise in areas like financial statement analysis, investment evaluation, and risk management.

Graduates are expected to develop proficiency in:

*   **Financial Analysis:** Interpreting financial statements and assessing investment opportunities to boost shareholder value.
*   **Risk Management:** Identifying, evaluating, and managing financial risks across various business operations.
*   **Corporate Finance:** Understanding capital budgeting, cost of capital, and corporate restructuring to optimize financial resources.
*   **Financial Markets:** Gaining insights into equity, bond, derivatives, and alternative investment markets, along with the role of financial institutions globally.

## Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the online MBA in Finance program at Kurukshetra University Online is ₹90,000. A significant advantage for many students is the availability of installment payment options, making the program more accessible financially. For international students, the fee structure is different, set at ₹2,00,000.

## Curriculum Highlights

The program's curriculum is thoughtfully designed to cover essential management and finance topics across its four semesters. Here's a glimpse into the subjects covered:

| Semester 1                               | Semester 2                            |
| :--------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------ |
| Management Process and Org. Behaviour    | Human Resource Management             |
| Fundamentals of Statistics for Business  | Computer Applications in Management   |
| Managerial Economics                     | Marketing Management                  |
| Financial Accounting                     | Financial Management                  |
| Business Communication                   | Business Research Methods             |
| Indian Economy and Business Environment  | Production and Operations Management  |

This structure ensures a strong foundation in core business principles before diving deeper into specialized financial topics.

## University Recognition

Kurukshetra University, established in 1956, is a well-regarded public/government institution. It holds an A++ accreditation from NAAC, which signifies a high standard of educational quality and institutional excellence. This accreditation is an important indicator for prospective students considering the credibility and value of their degree.

An online MBA in Finance from Kurukshetra University Online can be a valuable step for professionals looking to enhance their financial acumen and career prospects, offering a blend of academic rigor and practical relevance in a flexible format.
