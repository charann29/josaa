# Shall my son get call from IIM A B C at 99.27 CAT 2025 percentile with all sectional marks above 93 percentile and 10th 89%,12th 80%,B tec engi 8.5pointer (85%)and work experience of 2years..pl let me know

It's natural to wonder about the chances of securing interview calls from the top IIMs. Your son's profile is strong, and while a high CAT percentile is crucial, other factors play a significant role in the selection process for IIM Ahmedabad, Bangalore, and Calcutta.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your son has a competitive profile for IIM A, B, and C, with a strong CAT score and valuable work experience.
*   Chances for IIM Bangalore and IIM Calcutta appear quite good, given their specific selection criteria.
*   IIM Ahmedabad might be a bit more challenging due to its emphasis on exceptionally high and consistent academic scores from Class 10 and 12.
*   Interview performance and well-articulated goals will be critical for final selection across all institutes.

## Understanding IIM Ahmedabad's Selection Process

IIM Ahmedabad is known for its rigorous selection process, which heavily emphasizes academic consistency and excellence. While a CAT percentile of 99.27 with strong sectionals is excellent and typically meets the high cut-offs (often 99+ overall), IIM A also looks closely at past academic records. Many candidates shortlisted by IIM Ahmedabad have Class 10 and Class 12 scores exceeding 90%. Your son's 89% in Class 10 and 80% in Class 12 are good, but might be considered slightly below the very top tier for IIM A, where academic scores are often a distinguishing factor. A call is possible, but it would depend on how his overall profile, including his engineering degree and work experience, compares within his specific academic category.

## IIM Bangalore: A Strong Contender

For IIM Bangalore, your son's profile aligns very well with their selection criteria. IIM B places significant importance on work experience, and two years of experience is often considered ideal. Combined with his strong CAT percentile, solid sectional scores, and an engineering background, he presents a very strong case for an interview call. Provided he meets all the academic eligibility requirements and any normalization criteria set by the institute, his chances here are notably better.

## IIM Calcutta: CAT Score and Engineering Background

IIM Calcutta's selection process, particularly for candidates from an engineering background, tends to be more heavily weighted towards the CAT score. With a 99.27 percentile and strong sectional scores, your son stands a very good chance of receiving an interview call. IIM Calcutta traditionally gives relatively less weight to Class 12 marks compared to IIM Ahmedabad, making his profile particularly competitive for this institution.

## Beyond the Top Three

While the focus is often on IIM A, B, and C, it's important to remember that many other top-tier business schools offer excellent programs. Your son's profile makes him a very strong candidate for interview calls from other highly-ranked IIMs such as IIM Lucknow, IIM Kozhikode, IIM Indore, and IIM Shillong. Additionally, he is likely to be considered by leading non-IIM institutions like FMS Delhi, SPJIMR, and the management programs at IIT Bombay and IIT Delhi.

## The Crucial Role of the Personal Interview

Receiving an interview call is a significant step, but the final conversion to an admission offer depends heavily on performance in the Personal Interview (PI) round. This stage assesses clarity of career goals, the quality and relevance of work experience, communication skills, and overall personality. Focused preparation for the PI, including mock interviews and refining his responses, will be absolutely vital for securing a final admission offer.

Your son has built an impressive profile. With strategic preparation for the interview stage, he can maximize his chances of admission to a top-tier management program.
