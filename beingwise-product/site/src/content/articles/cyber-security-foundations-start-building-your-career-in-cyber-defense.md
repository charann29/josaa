# Cyber Security Foundations: Start Building Your Career in Cyber Defense by EC-Council via Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's digital world, protecting information from online threats is incredibly important. The "Cyber Security Foundations: Start Building Your Career in Cyber Defense" online course, offered through Futurelearn and designed by the EC-Council, provides a practical introduction to safeguarding organizations against cyberattacks.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn essential cybersecurity concepts and how to defend against common attacks.
*   The course is entirely online, self-paced, and takes approximately 15 weeks to complete, requiring about two hours of study per week.
*   Gain a certification accredited by the International Council of E-Commerce Consultants (EC-Council).
*   It's an intermediate-level program suitable for those looking to start or advance a career in cybersecurity or related fields.

## Course Overview and Highlights

This comprehensive program focuses on building cyber resilience – the ability for organizations to anticipate, respond to, and recover from cyber incidents. You'll explore foundational cybersecurity concepts, looking at real-world examples of threats like phishing, ransomware, and social engineering attacks. The curriculum also covers strategies for malware protection and the importance of robust data security policies.

A significant portion of the course is dedicated to identity and access management, a critical area for protecting sensitive information. You'll learn about implementing access controls, understanding single sign-on (SSO) technologies, and various authentication techniques to strengthen data security practices.

### Key Features:

*   **Duration:** Approximately 15 weeks, with an estimated effort of 2 hours per week.
*   **Format:** 100% online, self-study, delivered through video and text-based content.
*   **Certification:** Accredited by the International Council of E-Commerce Consultants.
*   **Level:** Intermediate.

## What You Will Learn

Through this course, participants will develop a strong understanding of cybersecurity principles and practices. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Delving into the core principles and prevalent challenges within cybersecurity.
*   Analyzing the current landscape of cybersecurity threats.
*   Assessing various methods to reduce vulnerabilities and minimize risks.
*   Examining different types of cybersecurity attacks and their potential consequences.

## Who Is This Course For?

This certification program is ideal for individuals keen on pursuing a career in cybersecurity or those in related fields who wish to enhance their defense skills. It caters to a diverse range of professions, including:

*   Cybersecurity Forensic Experts
*   Data Scientists
*   Data Analysts
*   Software Architects

Essentially, anyone interested in understanding and mitigating cyber threats will find value in this program.

## Fees and Certification

The course is structured with a monthly subscription fee. For the "Cyber Security Foundations: Start Building Your Career in Cyber Defense" program, the fee is Rs. 900 per month. Upon successful completion of the course content, a certificate will be provided by Futurelearn, acknowledging your achievement and new skills.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in this online course is a straightforward process. You will need to:

1.  Visit the Futurelearn platform.
2.  Register for an account on the Futurelearn website.
3.  Subscribe to the Futurelearn service to begin your learning journey.

This course offers a valuable opportunity to build a solid foundation in cyber defense, preparing you for a career in an increasingly vital field.
