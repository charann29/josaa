# TANCET 2026: Registration (Extended), Exam Date, Syllabus, Pattern, Question Papers

The Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test (TANCET) is a crucial state-level examination for students seeking admission to postgraduate programs like MBA and MCA in colleges across Tamil Nadu. Conducted by Anna University, TANCET is the gateway to over 200 institutions in the state.

## Key Takeaways

*   Anna University has extended the TANCET 2026 application deadline to April 15, 2026.
*   The TANCET 2026 examination for both MBA and MCA programs is scheduled for May 9, 2026.
*   Approximately 40,000 candidates appear for TANCET MBA and MCA exams annually.
*   The Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Chennai, manages the counseling process for qualified candidates.

## TANCET 2026 Application Process

Anna University has announced an extension for the TANCET 2026 application period. Prospective MBA and MCA students in Tamil Nadu can now complete their application forms until April 15, 2026. It is vital for all applicants to log in to the official TANCET portal to confirm their submission status. Some candidates may need to correct errors or re-upload documents according to the specified guidelines.

For detailed instructions on how to fill out the application form, candidates should refer to the official TANCET application guidelines provided by Anna University.

## TANCET Exam Pattern and Syllabus

TANCET is a pen-and-paper based examination consisting of 100 multiple-choice questions (MCQs) to be completed within a two-hour duration. The exam is conducted in English. Here’s an overview of the TANCET MBA exam pattern and syllabus:

### Exam Pattern Highlights

*   **Exam Mode:** Offline (Pen & Paper Based)
*   **Duration:** 2 Hours
*   **Total Questions:** 100 (MCQ format)
*   **Medium:** English
*   **Conducting Body:** Anna University
*   **Exam Level:** State-level

### MBA Syllabus Sections

The MBA TANCET exam typically covers five main sections designed to assess a candidate's aptitude for management studies:

*   Business Analysis
*   Quantitative Aptitude
*   Reading Comprehension and Verbal Ability
*   Data Sufficiency
*   General English

## TANCET Question Papers

Practicing with previous years' question papers is an excellent way to prepare for TANCET. Familiarizing yourself with the types of questions asked, the difficulty level, and the time constraints can significantly boost your confidence and performance on exam day. Candidates can usually find official past papers or sample questions on the Anna University TANCET website.

## TANCET Exam Fees

The application fees for TANCET 2026 are as follows:

*   **INR 1,000** for unreserved categories.
*   **INR 500** for reserved categories (specifically for candidates belonging to Tamil Nadu).

Ensure you complete your application and fee payment before the extended deadline to avoid any last-minute issues.
