# Post Graduate Program in Business Analytics and Visualization (PGP-BA&V) by Amity Future Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a strong career in the rapidly expanding field of data, Amity Future Academy offers a Post Graduate Program in Business Analytics and Visualization (PGP-BA&V). This program is designed to equip students with essential skills for data-driven decision-making in various business contexts.

## Key takeaways

*   The PGP-BA&V is a self-paced, virtual classroom program delivered through video and text-based modules.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹1,00,000, inclusive of Goods and Services Tax (GST).
*   Participants should plan to dedicate approximately 8-10 hours per week to their studies.
*   Upon successful completion, a certificate is awarded by Amity Future Academy.

## Program Overview and Learning Format

The Post Graduate Program in Business Analytics and Visualization is structured to provide a flexible learning experience. The medium of instruction is English, making it accessible to a wide audience. The program follows a self-study model, complemented by virtual classroom sessions, allowing students to learn at their own pace while still benefiting from interactive elements. The content is delivered through a combination of videos and text, catering to different learning preferences. To succeed in the program, students are advised to set aside about 8-10 hours each week for their studies.

## Program Fees and Certification

The total cost for the Post Graduate Program in Business Analytics and Visualization is ₹1,00,000. This amount covers all program expenses, including GST. There are no additional hidden charges mentioned. Upon successful completion of all program requirements, participants will receive a certificate directly from Amity Future Academy, validating their acquired skills and knowledge in business analytics and visualization.

## Comprehensive Syllabus Structure

The PGP-BA&V curriculum is thoughtfully designed to cover a broad spectrum of topics, moving from fundamental concepts to advanced analytical techniques. The program is divided into nine modules, each focusing on a specific area of business analytics:

*   **Module 1: The Science of Data Driven Decision Making**
    *   Understanding data classifications (continuous & discrete, structured vs unstructured).
    *   Fundamentals of R programming.
    *   Data manipulation and visualization techniques.
*   **Module 2: Fundamental Data Analysis to Real Business Problems**
    *   Describing and summarizing data.
    *   Concepts of sampling and estimation.
    *   Hypothesis testing and linear regression (single and multiple variables).
*   **Module 3: Inferential Statistics**
    *   Measuring and modeling uncertainty.
    *   Bayes Theorem and probability distributions.
    *   Advanced inferential statistics, including ANOVA.
*   **Module 4: Supervised Learning Algorithms**
    *   In-depth study of algorithms like Linear Regression, Logistic Regression, K-nearest Neighbor, Decision Trees, and Random Forests.
*   **Module 5: Forecasting Techniques**
    *   Linear optimization and using Excel Solver for optimal solutions.
    *   Sensitivity analysis, time series analysis, exponential smoothing, and ARIMA modeling.
*   **Module 6: Optimization Analytics Techniques**
    *   Clustering, prescriptive analytics, and association rules.
    *   Understanding variables, constraints, and objective functions in optimization.
*   **Module 7: Dimension Reduction Techniques**
    *   Model diagnosis and outlier analysis.
    *   Principal Component Analysis (PCA) and Factor Analysis.
*   **Module 8: Ensemble Learning Techniques**
    *   Resampling methods, K-fold cross-validation.
    *   Bagging, boosting, gradient boosting, and bootstrapping.
*   **Module 9: Primer on Big Data and Artificial Intelligence**
    *   Introduction to data mining with traditional and advanced tools, including Big Data technologies.

## Program Instructors

The program benefits from the expertise of a team of instructors and a program director. The faculty brings a blend of academic knowledge and practical experience to the virtual classroom. Key individuals involved in delivering the program include Mr. Tushar Kakkaiya, Dr. Suresh Varadarajan (holding a Ph.D.), Dr. Karthic Narayanan (holding a Ph.D.), and Mr. Pranav Shastri (holding a Ph.D.), who serves as the Program Director.

This Post Graduate Program in Business Analytics and Visualization by Amity Future Academy offers a robust curriculum for individuals aspiring to excel in the data analytics domain.
