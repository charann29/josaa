# Post Graduate Program in Software Development and Engineering by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For working professionals aiming to advance their careers in the dynamic field of software engineering, the Post Graduate Program in Software Development and Engineering by Great Learning offers a comprehensive online learning experience. This program is designed to equip individuals with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in modern tech roles.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 9-month online program specifically crafted for working professionals.
*   It offers extensive hands-on coding, teaching sessions, and dedicated career support, including guaranteed interview opportunities.
*   The curriculum covers foundational programming, data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented design, culminating in real-world system design projects.
*   Flexible fee payment options, including interest-free EMIs, make the program accessible.

## Program Overview and Structure

The Post Graduate Program in Software Development and Engineering is an intensive 9-month online bootcamp. It focuses on delivering in-demand software development skills through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video-based content. The program aims to transform participants into skilled software engineers ready for rewarding tech careers.

The curriculum is meticulously designed to cover core areas such as foundational programming, essential data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented design principles. Learners benefit from industry-relevant lectures, substantial hands-on practice, and mentorship from experienced professionals. The program concludes with practical system design projects, allowing participants to apply their newly acquired knowledge to real-world scenarios.

## Learning Experience and Career Support

Participants can expect a rich learning environment that includes over 300 hours of coding practice and more than 240 hours of dedicated teaching sessions. The emphasis is on experiential learning and case studies, ensuring a deep understanding of concepts.

Crucially, the program provides robust career support. This includes personalized career mentoring, assistance with resume building, and thorough interview preparation. A significant benefit is the provision of 10 guaranteed job interviews, designed to help learners secure suitable employment opportunities in the competitive software industry.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for admission to this program, applicants must meet specific academic and professional requirements:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Candidates need a minimum score of 60% in their 10th, 12th, and graduation examinations. Proficiency in at least one programming language is also mandatory.
*   **Work Experience:** Applicants should possess 1 to 4 years of professional experience, ideally within service-based companies.

This program is particularly suited for individuals currently working as IT Engineers, Software Developers, Software Engineers, or Computer Engineers who are looking to upskill and advance.

## Fee Structure and Payment Options

The total fee for the Post Graduate Program in Software Development and Engineering is ₹1,90,000 plus GST. Great Learning offers flexible payment solutions to ease the financial burden on students.

Applicants are required to pay an application fee during registration, with the remaining program fee payable in installments. Notably, there are 0% interest EMI options available for up to 18 months, with monthly installments starting from approximately ₹4,616. Payments can be made conveniently via net banking, credit card, or debit card.

Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a certificate directly from Great Learning, acknowledging their achievement and newly acquired expertise.

## How to Apply

The application process for the Post Graduate Program in Software Development and Engineering is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official course page for the program.
2.  Locate and click on the "Apply Now" button.
3.  Complete the online application form with all required details.
4.  Submit your application and subsequently take an entrance test.

Successful candidates will then be shortlisted for further admission procedures.

This program provides a structured pathway for professionals to enhance their software development skills and make a significant impact in the tech world.
