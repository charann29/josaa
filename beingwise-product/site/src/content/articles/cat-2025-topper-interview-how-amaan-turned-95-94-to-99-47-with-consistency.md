# CAT 2025 Topper Interview: How Amaan Turned 95.94% to 99.47% with Consistency

Meet Amaan Hussain, a CAT 2025 high-achiever who significantly boosted his percentile from 95.94% to an impressive 99.47%. His journey highlights the power of consistent effort and strategic preparation.

## Key takeaways

*   **Consistency is paramount:** Amaan emphasized that regular, focused effort trumps sporadic bursts of intense study.
*   **Mock analysis is crucial:** Simply taking mocks isn't enough; deep analysis of performance is key to identifying and improving weak areas.
*   **Quant focus pays off:** Dedicated practice in Quantitative Aptitude (QA) helped solidify his overall score.
*   **Targeted practice:** Understanding the specific demands of each section and tailoring practice accordingly is vital.

## Amaan's CAT 2025 Scorecard

Amaan Hussain, an alumnus of Maharaja Surajmal Institute, Delhi, achieved a remarkable 99.47 percentile in CAT 2025. His sectional scores demonstrate a balanced yet strong performance across all areas:

| Section | Percentile Score |
| :------ | :--------------- |
| VARC    | 99.38            |
| DILR    | 98.04            |
| QA      | 96.71            |
| Overall | 99.47            |

With these scores, Amaan is now aiming for top-tier institutions, including the IIMs (Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Calcutta) and FMS Delhi.

## The Power of Consistency Over Hard Work

When asked about his preparation strategy, Amaan strongly advocated for consistency. He explained that while hard work and smart work are important, they are less effective without a consistent study routine. Regularly dedicating time to preparation, even if for shorter durations, helps build momentum and ensures continuous improvement. This approach prevents burnout and allows for better retention of concepts.

## The Role of Mock Tests and Analysis

Amaan highlighted that taking mock tests was only half the battle. The real learning, he stressed, came from thoroughly analyzing each mock. This involved reviewing not just incorrect answers but also correct ones, understanding the reasoning, and identifying patterns in mistakes. Analyzing mocks helped him pinpoint his strengths and weaknesses, allowing him to refine his strategy for each section and manage time more effectively during the actual exam.

## A Focused Approach to Quantitative Aptitude

Quantitative Aptitude (QA) was a key area of focus for Amaan. He dedicated significant time to practicing various types of questions and understanding underlying concepts. This dedicated practice helped him build speed and accuracy, which are critical for excelling in the QA section. While he performed well across all sections, his emphasis on QA ensured a strong foundation and contributed significantly to his overall percentile.

## Advice for Future Aspirants

Amaan's advice for those preparing for CAT 2026 is straightforward: start early, stay consistent, and don't underestimate the power of mock test analysis. He encourages aspirants to identify their target institutions (like those featured in the NIRF rankings for management programs) and tailor their preparation to meet the expected cut-offs. Regular revision and staying calm under pressure are also vital components of a successful CAT journey.

Amaan's journey from a strong percentile to an exceptional one is a testament to focused effort and smart strategizing, offering valuable lessons for all aspiring MBA candidates.
