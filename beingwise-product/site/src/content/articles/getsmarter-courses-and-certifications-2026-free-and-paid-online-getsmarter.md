# GetSmarter Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online GetSmarter Courses

BeingWise explores a selection of online courses and certifications available through GetSmarter, a platform known for offering programs from various universities. These courses are designed to help professionals and students enhance their skills across diverse fields.

## Key takeaways

*   GetSmarter provides online certifications developed in partnership with leading global universities.
*   Programs cover areas like legal practice, business management, and project management.
*   Courses vary in duration and cost, offering different levels of specialization.
*   These certifications can help individuals advance their careers or gain new expertise.

## Paralegal Practitioner Certification

This 16-week online course, offered in collaboration with the University of Cape Town (UCT), is designed for individuals interested in a career as a paralegal. It aims to equip participants with both the practical skills and the foundational legal knowledge necessary for this role. Reports from the US Bureau of Labor Statistics indicate a projected 12% growth in employment for legal and paralegal assistants by 2028, highlighting the increasing demand for these professionals.

The curriculum provides a comprehensive understanding of the legal system, covering key areas such as the South African Constitution, the Bill of Rights, criminal law, private law, and commercial law. Participants will also develop essential administrative and technical skills through units focused on legal writing, research, reading, and interview techniques. The course fee is ₹88,417.

## MBA Essentials Certification

Developed in partnership with the University of Stellenbosch Business School, this 15-week certification focuses on enhancing the business and technical skills critical for effective managerial decision-making. It covers the three core pillars of business: quantitative methods, economics, and managerial accounting, alongside insights into profitability theory and cost behaviors.

The program delves into key economic principles, including factors of production, economic dilemmas, and opportunity costs. Microeconomic concepts like supply and demand are also explored. Important topics such as the time value of money, profit distribution, accounting practices, and cash management are included, preparing candidates for advanced business studies like a full Master of Business Administration degree. The cost for this program is ₹1,26,143.

## Executive and Management Coaching

Also offered through UCT, this 12-week online course addresses the growing need for coaching in professional environments. It introduces participants to fundamental coaching concepts and practices, preparing them to manage formal coaching relationships effectively. Executive coaches play a vital role in helping managers improve performance, which can positively impact overall employee productivity.

The curriculum covers how to leverage technology for coaching in the digital age and guides participants through the various stages of the coaching process. It provides essential tools and ideas for establishing a coaching practice, enhancing one's managerial style, building a personal coaching brand, and promoting oneself as either an independent consultant or an internal corporate leader. The course fee is ₹80,204.

## Advanced Project Management

As organizations increasingly adopt dynamic work methodologies, the demand for skilled project management professionals continues to rise. This course, also from UCT, is designed to meet this need by developing advanced project management capabilities.

This selection of GetSmarter courses offers valuable opportunities for skill development and career enhancement across various professional domains.
