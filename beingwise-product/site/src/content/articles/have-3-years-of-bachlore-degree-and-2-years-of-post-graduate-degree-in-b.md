# I have 3 years of bachelor's degree and 2 years of postgraduate degree in B.Com. Can I apply for SJMSOM IIT Mumbai for MBA?

It's a common question for many aspiring MBA candidates whether their specific academic background meets the eligibility criteria for top institutions like SJMSOM IIT Bombay. Let's break down the requirements to see if your qualifications fit.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your academic background, including a 3-year bachelor's and 2-year postgraduate degree, generally meets the duration requirement.
*   The primary criteria are your marks/CPI and having completed or being in the final year of your degree.
*   SJMSOM IIT Bombay's MBA program is highly competitive, and meeting eligibility is just the first step.
*   Always refer to the official SJMSOM IIT Bombay admissions page for the most current and detailed information.

## Understanding SJMSOM MBA Eligibility

SJMSOM, the Shailesh J. Mehta School of Management at IIT Bombay, is one of India's premier management schools. Their MBA program is highly sought after, and they have clear guidelines for applicants. The core requirement revolves around your undergraduate or postgraduate degree.

According to the official SJMSOM IIT Bombay admissions information, candidates need to possess either a bachelor's degree or a master's degree.

## Academic Performance Requirements

Beyond just having a degree, your academic performance is crucial. You must have achieved a minimum of 60% marks in your qualifying degree. For candidates belonging to the SC/ST/PWD categories, this requirement is slightly relaxed to 55% marks.

Alternatively, if your institution uses a Cumulative Performance Index (CPI) or Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) system, you would need a minimum CPI of 6.5 out of 10. For SC/ST/PWD candidates, this alternative requirement is a minimum CPI of 6 out of 10.

## Your Degree Structure and Eligibility

Given your academic profile — a 3-year bachelor's degree followed by a 2-year postgraduate degree in B.Com — you would typically satisfy the degree duration requirement. The key here is that you hold a valid degree, which in your case includes a postgraduate qualification. As long as your B.Com (postgraduate) degree meets the minimum percentage or CPI criteria mentioned above, you should be eligible to apply.

It's also important to note that even if you are in the final year of your degree and awaiting results, or if you have completed all degree requirements but are waiting for the final certificate, you are generally permitted to apply. However, any offer of admission would typically be provisional and contingent upon successfully meeting all final degree requirements by a specified deadline.

## Next Steps for Aspiring Candidates

Once you've confirmed your eligibility, the next steps involve preparing for and taking the Common Admission Test (CAT). SJMSOM IIT Bombay uses CAT scores as a primary shortlisting criterion. Following the CAT, shortlisted candidates typically undergo a Written Ability Test (WAT) and a Personal Interview (PI) round.

It's highly recommended to thoroughly review the official SJMSOM IIT Bombay website for the specific year you plan to apply, as admission policies and dates can be updated annually.

With your academic background, provided you meet the minimum percentage/CPI, you are indeed eligible to apply for the MBA program at SJMSOM IIT Bombay. Good luck with your application!
