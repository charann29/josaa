# GUJCET 2026 Dates: Answer Key, Result & Complete Schedule

For students aiming for engineering and pharmacy programs in Gujarat, the Gujarat Common Entrance Test (GUJCET) is a crucial step. Administered by the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSEB), this exam's schedule, from application to counselling, is vital for all aspirants.

## Key takeaways

*   The GSEB manages the entire GUJCET examination and admission process.
*   The official answer key for GUJCET 2026 was released in early April, followed by the final answer key.
*   Candidates can expect the GUJCET 2026 results to be announced soon, accessible via the official GSEB portal.
*   Counselling for GUJCET qualified candidates will commence after the results are declared, likely in the summer months.

## GUJCET 2026 Exam Dates & Schedule

The complete schedule for GUJCET 2026, including application deadlines, admit card release, exam date, answer key publication, results, and counselling, is progressively announced by the GSEB. Students should regularly check the official GSEB portal for the most up-to-date information.

Here’s a look at the key dates:

| Event                      | Date           |
| :------------------------- | :------------- |
| GUJCET Answer Key 2026     | April 4, 2026  |
| Answer Key Challenge Window| April 4 - 8, 2026 |
| GUJCET Final Answer Key 2026 | April 10, 2026 |
| GUJCET Result 2026         | To Be Notified |
| GUJCET Counselling 2026    | To Be Notified |

## GUJCET 2026 Answer Key Date

The official GUJCET 2026 answer key was published by the GSEB on April 4, 2026. This initial release allowed candidates to review their responses and raise any objections within a specified challenge window, which closed on April 8, 2026. Following this, the final answer key, incorporating valid challenges, was released on April 10, 2026. Students can use this final key to estimate their scores before the official results are out.

## GUJCET 2026 Result Date

While the exact date for the GUJCET 2026 results is yet to be announced, it is expected soon after the final answer key release. The GSEB will declare the results and make scorecards available on their official website. To access their results, candidates will need to log in using their six-digit seat number, which is printed on their GUJCET admit card.

### How to check GUJCET Result 2026?

Follow these steps to check your GUJCET 2026 result:

1.  Visit the official GSEB portal.
2.  Locate the result login section on the homepage.
3.  Enter your six-digit seat number and submit.
4.  Your GUJCET scorecard will appear on the screen in PDF format.
5.  Review your scores, download the scorecard, and print it for future reference.

## GUJCET 2026 Counselling Date

After the GUJCET 2026 results are declared, the Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC) will conduct the counselling process. This multi-stage process will involve online registration, choice filling, mock allotment, and final seat allocation. The counselling schedule, including registration dates and round-wise allotments, will be released by the ACPC on their official portal. It is generally anticipated that counselling will commence in June or July.

Staying informed about these dates and announcements from the official GSEB and ACPC portals is crucial for a smooth application and admission journey.
