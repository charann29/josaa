# UG program in Multimedia & Animation And Motion Design by École Intuit Lab New Delhi: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students passionate about bringing stories to life through visuals, École Intuit Lab in New Delhi offers a comprehensive undergraduate program in Multimedia & Animation and Motion Design. This four-year course is designed to transform creative individuals into skilled professionals ready for the dynamic animation and motion design industry.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a four-year, full-time undergraduate program taught entirely in English.
*   The curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with extensive practical experience, including two internships.
*   Students gain proficiency in 2D and 3D animation, VFX, cinematic techniques, and interactive storytelling.
*   The program aims to prepare graduates for roles such as motion designers, VFX artists, and multimedia creators.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The program at École Intuit Lab focuses on developing both technical animation skills and strong creative storytelling abilities. Students will engage in hands-on learning through practical studio classes, participate in global workshops, and work on real-world projects. This approach ensures they are well-prepared for industry demands upon graduation.

The curriculum is structured to provide a deep understanding of animation and motion design principles. Students will learn about the mechanics of motion, color theory, composition, and sound design, applying these to create compelling visual narratives. The program also emphasizes developing proficiency in industry-standard software and tools.

## What You Will Learn

Throughout the four years, students will develop a wide range of skills essential for success in the multimedia and animation fields. Key learning areas include:

*   **Artistic Ability:** Developing drawing, illustration, and visual expression skills.
*   **Storytelling:** Crafting narratives, scriptwriting, and storyboarding for animation.
*   **Animation Skills:** Mastering 2D and 3D animation, character animation, and cinematic techniques.
*   **Motion Design:** Creating advanced motion graphics, typography for motion, and interactive animation.
*   **Technical Proficiency:** Gaining expertise in software used for VFX, web animation, and digital content creation.
*   **Professional Development:** Learning project management, collaboration, and client-focused design through live projects and internships.

## Program Structure and Internships

The four-year program includes two internships, providing invaluable real-world experience both within India and internationally. These opportunities allow students to apply their classroom learning in professional settings and build a strong portfolio. The program also features:

*   Live industry projects
*   Regular portfolio reviews
*   Extensive studio practice
*   Industry workshops led by experts
*   Access to technical labs

Partnerships with industry leaders, such as Jio Creative Labs, offer students practical exposure and applied learning in diverse scenarios.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

To be eligible for the UG program in Multimedia & Animation and Motion Design, applicants must have successfully completed Grade 12. The admission process typically involves several steps:

1.  **Application:** Visit the official École Intuit Lab website, fill out the application form, and submit necessary documents along with the application fee.
2.  **Creative Aptitude Test:** Candidates are required to take a creative aptitude test to assess their potential.
3.  **Interview:** A personal interview is conducted to understand the applicant's aspirations and suitability for the program.

## Course Fees and Duration

This is a four-year undergraduate program. For detailed information regarding the course fees, it is recommended to refer to the official École Intuit Lab New Delhi website, as specific figures can vary. Upon successful completion of the program, a certificate is awarded by École Intuit Lab New Delhi.

This program is an excellent pathway for aspiring animators, graphic designers, and multimedia creators looking to build a robust career in the visual media industry.
