# TalentSprint Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online TalentSprint Courses

TalentSprint offers a range of online professional development programs, often in collaboration with leading Indian academic institutions. These certifications are designed to equip professionals with advanced skills and knowledge to excel in various demanding fields.

## Key Takeaways

*   TalentSprint partners with premier institutions like IIM Calcutta and IISc Bangalore for specialized executive and advanced programs.
*   Programs typically span 12 months and cover critical areas such as general management, global business, and computational data science.
*   Courses aim to enhance leadership, strategic thinking, and technical skills for career advancement in both corporate and non-corporate sectors.
*   Fees for these advanced programs range from approximately ₹4,50,000 to ₹4,80,000, reflecting the premium academic partnerships.

## Executive Programme in General Management (EPGM)

Offered in collaboration with IIM Calcutta, the Executive Programme in General Management (EPGM) is a 12-month certification designed for aspiring and current managers. This highly interactive program focuses on developing the efficiency and leadership capabilities required in modern organizations. Participants gain valuable managerial skills to navigate diverse business scenarios effectively.

The curriculum integrates lectures, assignments, and case studies, preparing individuals for key roles in finance, operations, general management, and accounting. It also benefits executives in public sector and non-profit organizations, as well as professionals from fields like medicine, engineering, and chartered accountancy transitioning into corporate or non-corporate roles. The program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical application, equipping participants with strategic managerial skills. The fee for this program is approximately ₹4,50,000.

**Skills Covered:** Leadership, Decision-making skills, Critical thinking, Business Management.

## Executive Programme in Global Business Management (EPGBM)

Another 12-month offering from IIM Calcutta via TalentSprint, the Executive Programme in Global Business Management (EPGBM) is tailored for professionals seeking to thrive in the competitive global business environment. This redesigned program addresses the ever-evolving landscape of international commerce.

It is particularly suitable for managers in multinational companies operating in India, or Indian companies looking to expand globally, including those in export departments. The program distinguishes itself by first strengthening foundational general management areas through introductory lectures, followed by three modules focused on global management concepts, business strategies, and skill-building to cultivate a global mindset. The cost for this program is approximately ₹4,80,000.

**Skills Covered:** Business Management.

## Advanced Programme in Computational Data Science

In partnership with IISc Bangalore, ranked India's number one institute by NIRF, TalentSprint offers the 12-month Advanced Programme in Computational Data Science. This program addresses the significant global demand for data analysts and data scientists, with the Bureau of Labor Statistics predicting substantial job growth in data science by 2026.

This certification provides professionals with practical, hands-on experience crucial for making an impact in this rapidly expanding industry. The curriculum, delivered by IISc faculty, caters to various target groups including research scientists, R&D professionals, and engineers. It aims to develop competent managers capable of navigating hyper data-driven and technologically advanced societal landscapes. The online course includes an immersive three-day campus visit to IISc Bengaluru.

These programs provide a structured pathway for professionals to upskill and advance their careers through recognized certifications from top-tier Indian academic institutions.
