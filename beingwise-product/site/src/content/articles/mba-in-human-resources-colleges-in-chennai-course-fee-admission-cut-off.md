# MBA in Human Resources Colleges in Chennai - Course Fee, Admission, Cut Off, Placements

For those aspiring to build a career in managing an organization's most valuable asset – its people – an MBA in Human Resources (HR) offers a strategic pathway. Chennai, a prominent educational hub, hosts numerous institutions offering this specialized postgraduate degree, equipping students with essential skills for workforce management and development.

## Key takeaways

*   Chennai is home to 52 colleges offering MBA programs with a Human Resources specialization.
*   The majority of these institutions, 47 to be precise, are privately managed, while 3 are government-owned.
*   Admission typically requires a bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks.
*   Common entrance exams for these programs include TANCET MBA, CAT, MAT, XAT, and CMAT.

## Understanding an MBA in Human Resources

An MBA in HR is a specialized program designed to cultivate professionals adept at navigating the complexities of human capital within an organization. The curriculum typically covers a wide array of topics, including talent acquisition, employee relations, compensation and benefits, organizational development, and HR analytics. This degree is highly sought after as every organization, regardless of its size or industry, relies on skilled HR professionals to foster a productive and engaged workforce.

## Admission Process and Entrance Exams

Securing a spot in a reputable MBA HR program in Chennai generally involves a multi-stage admission process. The primary step is meeting the academic eligibility criteria, which usually mandates a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university, with a minimum aggregate score of 50%. Some institutions might have slightly higher percentage requirements.

Following this, candidates must appear for and achieve a competitive score in one of the accepted management entrance examinations. The most commonly accepted exams by MBA HR colleges in Chennai include:

*   **TANCET MBA:** A state-level examination for admission to MBA programs in Tamil Nadu.
*   **CAT (Common Admission Test):** A national-level exam for IIMs and other top business schools.
*   **MAT (Management Aptitude Test):** A national-level exam conducted multiple times a year.
*   **XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test):** Primarily for XLRI and other associated institutions.
*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test):** A national-level exam facilitated by NTA.
*   **ATMA (AIMS Test for Management Admissions):** Another national-level entrance test.

After the entrance exam, shortlisted candidates typically undergo further assessment rounds such as Group Discussions (GD) and Personal Interviews (PI) before final selection.

## Top MBA HR Colleges in Chennai

Chennai boasts several well-regarded institutions for an MBA in Human Resources. These colleges are known for their academic rigor, faculty expertise, and placement support. Some of the prominent names include:

*   Great Lakes Institute of Management, Chennai
*   Loyola Institute of Business Administration (LIBA)
*   DoMS IIT Madras
*   Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences
*   Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science
*   B.S. Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology

These institutions are recognized for their comprehensive curriculum and industry exposure, preparing students for diverse roles in human resource management.

## Fee Structure for MBA HR Programs

The cost of an MBA in Human Resources in Chennai can vary significantly based on the institution, its affiliation, and whether it is a private or government college. Generally, fees can range from less than INR 1 lakh to over INR 5 lakh per annum. It's advisable to check the official websites of individual colleges for the most current fee details.
