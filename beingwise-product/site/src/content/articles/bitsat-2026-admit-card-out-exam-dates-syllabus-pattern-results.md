# BITSAT 2026: Admit Card (OUT), Exam Dates, Syllabus, Pattern, Results

The Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test (BITSAT) is a crucial entrance examination for students aspiring to join undergraduate programs at BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses. This test assesses a candidate's aptitude across various subjects to determine their readiness for B.E., B.Pharm., and M.Sc. degrees.

## Key Takeaways

*   BITSAT 2026 is conducted in two sessions, with revised dates for Session 1 on April 15 & 16, and Session 2 from May 24 to 26, 2026.
*   The admit card for BITSAT 2026 Session 1 has been released and is essential for gaining entry to the examination center.
*   New this year, BITS Pilani has introduced tuition-blind admissions and four-year scholarships for the top 500 BITSAT rank holders.
*   Changes have been made to the eligibility criteria, specifically regarding reappearing for Class 12 subjects.

## BITSAT 2026 Admit Card

The admit card for BITSAT 2026 Session 1 is now available for all registered candidates. This document is vital as it contains your final exam date, time, and center details. Even if you missed the slot booking, your exam schedule is confirmed on the admit card.

To download your hall ticket, visit the official BITS Pilani admissions website, admissions.bits-pilani.com. You will need your application form number and password to log in. It is mandatory to download and print a copy of your admit card.

### How to Download the BITSAT Hall Ticket 2026?

Follow these steps to access your BITSAT hall ticket:

1.  Navigate to admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in.
2.  Click on the link for the BITSAT hall ticket.
3.  Enter your application number and password to log in.
4.  Download and print your hall ticket.

Remember to carry your BITSAT admit card along with one original photo ID proof (such as an Aadhaar card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driver's License, or School ID) to the exam center. Without these documents, you will not be permitted to take the examination. Separate admit cards will be issued for each examination session.

## BITSAT Exam Pattern 2026

The BITSAT exam is designed to test a candidate's knowledge and analytical skills. It covers subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (or Biology for B.Pharm. aspirants), English Proficiency, and Logical Reasoning. The exam is conducted online, and candidates must manage their time effectively across these sections.

## BITSAT 2026 Syllabus

The syllabus for BITSAT 2026 is comprehensive, drawing heavily from the Class 11 and 12 curricula of various central and state boards. It is advisable to refer to the official BITS Pilani website for the detailed syllabus breakdown for each subject: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, English Proficiency, and Logical Reasoning. Understanding the complete syllabus is key to structured preparation.

## What's New in BITSAT 2026?

This year brings a few significant updates for BITSAT aspirants:

*   **Revised Eligibility Criteria:** BITS Pilani has adjusted its eligibility norms, particularly concerning candidates reappearing for all subjects in their Class 12 examinations. Students should carefully review the official criteria.
*   **Tuition-Blind Admissions:** A major new initiative, the top 500 BITSAT rank holders will now be offered tuition-blind admissions, accompanied by a four-year scholarship. This aims to make quality education more accessible to high-achieving students.

Staying informed about these changes is crucial for all applicants.
