The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) conducts the Combined Higher Secondary (10+2) Level (CHSL) Examination to recruit candidates for various Group C posts in different government departments and ministries. This page provides a comprehensive overview of the SSC CHSL 2025 exam, including recent updates, results, and preparation details.

## Key takeaways

*   The SSC CHSL 2025 Tier 1 results have been officially released on ssc.gov.in.
*   The Tier 1 examination was held from November 12 to November 30, 2025.
*   The provisional answer key for Tier 1 was released on December 8, with objections accepted until December 11.
*   A total of 3,131 vacancies for Group C posts are to be filled through this examination cycle.

## SSC CHSL 2025 Tier 1 Result

The Staff Selection Commission has declared the Tier 1 result for the SSC CHSL 2025 examination. The results are available on the official SSC portal, ssc.gov.in, in a PDF format. This PDF contains the roll numbers, names, and parents' names of candidates who have qualified for the next stage. Those who have successfully cleared Tier 1 are eligible to appear for the Tier 2 examination, which is tentatively scheduled for March or April 2026.

To check your Tier 1 result:

1.  Visit the official website, ssc.gov.in.
2.  Click on the 'Result' tab located at the top of the homepage.
3.  Select the 'CHSL' exam tab.
4.  Locate and click on the result link for the SSC CHSL 2025 Tier 1.
5.  Download the PDF and search for your roll number.

### SSC CHSL Scorecard

The individual scorecards for the SSC CHSL Tier 1 exam, which will include raw scores, normalized marks, category details, and cut-off scores, are expected to be released soon after the final result and cut-off marks are published. Candidates can download their scorecards by logging into their accounts on the official SSC website using their registration number and password.

## SSC CHSL 2025 Vacancies

For the 2025 examination cycle, the Staff Selection Commission aims to fill a total of 3,131 vacancies for various Group C positions. These roles are spread across different government departments and organizations.

## SSC CHSL 2025 Answer Key

The provisional answer key for the SSC CHSL Tier 1 examination was made available on December 8, 2025. Candidates were given a window until December 11, 2025, to review the key and submit any objections against the provisional answers. After considering valid objections, the final answer key is usually released alongside or before the final results.

## SSC CHSL Minimum Qualifying Marks

To qualify for the SSC CHSL examination, candidates must achieve a minimum aggregate percentage. The qualifying marks vary by category, as detailed below:

| Category            | Qualifying Percentage | Minimum Marks |
| :------------------ | :-------------------- | :------------ |
| Unreserved (UR)     | 30%                   | 60            |
| OBC/EWS             | 25%                   | 50            |
| All Other Categories| 20%                   | 40            |

This information helps candidates understand the basic threshold they need to cross to be considered for further stages of the selection process.

Stay updated with official announcements on the Staff Selection Commission website for all developments regarding the SSC CHSL 2025 examination.
