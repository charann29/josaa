# Financial Analysis and Decision Making with Xero and Tableau by Futurelearn: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This microcredential course offers a deep dive into using powerful tools like Xero and Tableau for financial analysis and strategic business decisions. It's designed to equip you with the skills to interpret financial data and drive organisational growth.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a postgraduate-level microcredential validated by Coventry University, London.
*   The program focuses on practical application of Xero and Tableau for financial insights.
*   It's a 12-week, self-paced online course, offering 15 UK academic credits.
*   Assessments are project-based and marked by tutors, leading to a professional certificate.

## Course Overview

Developed in collaboration with business intelligence experts and leading software providers Xero and Tableau, this program aims to transform your ability to make data-driven decisions. You'll gain hands-on experience with Xero, a globally recognised accounting platform, and learn to visualise complex financial data using Tableau, a top business intelligence tool. The goal is to enhance organisational performance through robust financial analysis.

## What You Will Learn

Throughout the 12 weeks, you will develop a comprehensive understanding of financial analysis and data visualisation. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Recognising and addressing ethical challenges in financial contexts.
*   Applying organisational values to business choices.
*   Demonstrating strong analytical skills by synthesising financial reports and data.
*   Utilising Xero software to apply fundamental managerial and financial accounting techniques.
*   Effectively communicating critical business insights to inform strategic decision-making.

## Program Structure and Delivery

This microcredential is delivered entirely online, allowing for a flexible, self-study approach. The learning materials include video lectures and text-based content. You'll also benefit from live broadcasts and recorded sessions, along with tutor-marked exercises to guide your progress. The program culminates in project-based assessments designed to test your practical application of the learned skills.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Financial Analysis and Decision Making with Xero and Tableau microcredential is ₹52,979. Payments can be made via PayPal or major credit cards like Visa, American Express, or MasterCard. Upon successfully completing the project-based, tutor-marked assessments and achieving the minimum qualifying marks, you will be awarded an accredited professional certificate by Futurelearn.

## Eligibility Criteria

While there are no strict prerequisites, it is highly recommended that applicants hold a graduate degree or possess equivalent work experience in finance or accounting. This background will help you make the most of the course content and practical exercises.

This program provides a valuable opportunity to refine your financial analysis skills, making you a more effective and data-savvy decision-maker in today's business landscape.
