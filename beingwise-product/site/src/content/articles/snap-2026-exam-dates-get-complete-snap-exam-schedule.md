# SNAP 2026 Exam Dates: Get Complete SNAP Exam Schedule

For students aspiring to pursue an MBA at Symbiosis International University (SIU) institutes, the Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP) is a crucial step. This article outlines the complete schedule for SNAP 2026, including registration, admit card availability, exam days, and result announcements, to help you plan your application journey effectively.

## Key takeaways

*   SNAP 2026 will be conducted on three separate days in December, allowing candidates multiple attempts.
*   The application process is expected to commence in the first week of August 2026.
*   Candidates can appear for up to three sessions, with the highest score considered for admission.
*   A separate application fee is required for each attempt.

## SNAP 2026 Application Dates

The registration for SNAP 2026 will be conducted online. Aspiring candidates will need to visit the official SNAP Test website to register and complete the application form. This form requires details such as your name, category, date of birth, contact information, academic background, preferred SNAP test cities, the number of exam sessions you plan to attempt, and your chosen SIU courses. Completing the registration fee payment is a mandatory step to finalize your application.

## SNAP Admit Card 2026 Dates

The SNAP Admit Card for 2026 will be issued separately for each examination session. Candidates will find different download dates for the admit card corresponding to each session they have applied for. It is essential to carry the session-specific SNAP admit card to the test center. The admit card will contain vital information, including your personal details, the test center address, exam timings, reporting instructions, a list of documents to bring, and rules regarding entry, exit, and prohibited items. It is advisable to keep your admit card safe until the entire admission process is complete.

## SNAP 2026 Exam Dates

SNAP 2026 will be conducted in three distinct sessions, typically spaced about a week apart. When filling out the application form, candidates can choose their preferred exam dates. You have the flexibility to appear for one, two, or all three sessions. For admission purposes, SIU will consider your best score out of all the attempts you make. The SNAP exam is a 60-minute test without any breaks, featuring multiple-choice questions (MCQs). The marking scheme is straightforward: one mark for every correct answer and a deduction of 0.25 marks for each incorrect answer.

## SNAP 2026 Result Date

The results for SNAP 2026 are anticipated to be announced in the first week of January 2027. Candidates will be able to download their scorecards by logging into the official website. For those who appeared in multiple sessions, the highest score achieved across all attempts will be used for consideration in the SIU admission process.

Preparing for SNAP 2026 requires careful planning and timely action. Stay updated with official announcements on the SNAP Test website for any changes or additional information.
