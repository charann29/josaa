# What is the Annual Fee for MDS in Orthodontics at KLE Belgaum?

If you're considering a Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) in Orthodontics at KLE Belgaum, understanding the fee structure is a crucial part of your planning. This guide breaks down the costs involved for this specialized postgraduate program.

## Key Takeaways

*   The primary annual tuition for MDS Orthodontics at KLE Belgaum is ₹9,00,000.
*   An additional annual development fee of ₹1,00,000 applies.
*   The total initial payment for the first year, including one-time charges, comes to ₹10,04,000.
*   There are only 4 available seats for this highly sought-after program.

## Understanding the Annual Tuition and Development Fees

The core cost for pursuing your MDS in Orthodontics at KLE Belgaum is divided into two main components. The annual tuition fee stands at ₹9,00,000. On top of this, there is an annual development fee of ₹1,00,000. These two figures represent the recurring costs you can expect each year of your program.

## One-Time Charges to Consider

Beyond the annual fees, there are a couple of one-time payments that are part of the initial admission process. An eligibility fee of ₹2,000 is required, which is non-refundable. Additionally, a caution money deposit of ₹2,000 is collected. This caution money is refundable once you successfully complete your course, provided there are no outstanding dues or damages.

## Total First-Year Outlay

Combining all these elements, the total financial commitment for your first year in the MDS Orthodontics program at KLE Belgaum amounts to ₹10,04,000. This figure includes the annual tuition, the development fee, and both the non-refundable eligibility fee and the refundable caution money.

## Limited Intake for a Specialized Program

It's important for aspiring students to note the highly competitive nature of this program. KLE Belgaum offers a very limited intake for the MDS in Orthodontics, with only 4 seats available. This small number underscores the demand and exclusivity of the specialization, making thorough preparation for admissions essential.

To summarize the fee structure for the first year, here's a quick breakdown:

| Annual Tuition Fee  | 9,00,000     | Per Annum    | Main course fee                      |
| Development Fee     | 1,00,000     | Per Annum    | Additional annual charge             |

Understanding these figures can help you plan your finances effectively for pursuing your MDS in Orthodontics at KLE Belgaum.
