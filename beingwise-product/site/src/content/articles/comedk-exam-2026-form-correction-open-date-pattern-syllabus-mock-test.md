# COMEDK Exam 2026: Form Correction (Open), Date, Pattern, Syllabus, Mock Test

The COMEDK Undergraduate Entrance Test (UGET) is a crucial national-level examination for students aspiring to secure admission into Bachelor of Engineering (BE) programs across numerous private, unaided engineering colleges in Karnataka. This page offers a comprehensive guide to COMEDK UGET 2026, covering important dates, the application correction window, exam structure, and preparation resources.

## Key Takeaways

*   The application form correction window for COMEDK UGET 2026 is currently open from April 10 to April 13, 2026.
*   The COMEDK UGET 2026 examination is scheduled for May 9, 2026 (Saturday).
*   The exam is a computer-based test (CBT) with 180 multiple-choice questions (MCQs) in total.
*   Eligibility requires passing or appearing in Class 12 with at least 45% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM).

Staying informed about key dates is vital for a smooth application and examination process. Here's a quick overview of the COMEDK UGET 2026 schedule:

## COMEDK UGET 2026 Application Form Correction

The Consortium of Medical, Engineering & Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) has opened a window for candidates to rectify any errors in their submitted application forms. This correction facility is active from April 10, 2026, and will close on April 13, 2026. It is crucial for all registered applicants to review their details carefully and make any necessary changes within this period to ensure their application is accurate and complete.

### Eligibility Criteria for COMEDK UGET 2026

To be eligible for COMEDK UGET 2026, candidates must meet the following conditions:

*   Have passed or be appearing for their Class 12 (or equivalent) examination.
*   Achieved a minimum of 45% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics combined in their qualifying examination. (For reserved categories, this percentage might be slightly lower as per official norms).
*   Must have passed all subjects from a single recognized board.
*   There is no upper age limit for appearing in the COMEDK UGET.

## COMEDK UGET 2026 Exam Pattern

The COMEDK UGET is a computer-based test designed to assess a candidate's aptitude in core science subjects. Understanding the exam pattern is essential for effective preparation:

*   **Mode of Exam**: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
*   **Total Questions**: 180 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Sections**: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
*   **Questions per Section**: 60 questions from each subject
*   **Duration**: 3 hours
*   **Marking Scheme**: 1 mark for each correct answer. There is no negative marking for incorrect answers.
*   **Medium**: English only

## COMEDK UGET 2026 Syllabus

The syllabus for COMEDK UGET 2026 primarily covers topics from the Class 11 and Class 12 PCM curriculum. Candidates are advised to refer to the official COMEDK website for the detailed and most up-to-date syllabus. Generally, the syllabus includes:

*   **Physics**: Mechanics, Heat & Thermodynamics, Optics, Electricity & Magnetism, Modern Physics, etc.
*   **Chemistry**: Physical Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, Inorganic Chemistry, etc.
*   **Mathematics**: Algebra, Trigonometry, Coordinate Geometry, Calculus, Vectors, Probability, etc.

Thorough preparation across these areas is crucial for a good score.

## COMEDK UGET 2026 Mock Test

To familiarize themselves with the computer-based test environment and the question pattern, candidates should regularly attempt mock tests. The official COMEDK portal usually provides access to mock tests, which are invaluable for practicing time management, understanding the interface, and identifying areas for improvement. Regularly taking mock tests can significantly boost confidence and performance on the actual exam day.
