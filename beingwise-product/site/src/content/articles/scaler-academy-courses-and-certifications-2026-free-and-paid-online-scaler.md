# Scaler Academy Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Scaler Academy Courses

Scaler Academy offers a range of online programs designed to help individuals enhance their skills in various tech domains, from data science to system design. These courses cater to different experience levels, aiming to equip participants with industry-relevant knowledge and practical abilities.

## Key Takeaways

*   Scaler Academy provides specialized online programs in areas like Data Science, Machine Learning, Problem Solving, and System Design.
*   Courses are typically structured with live expert-led sessions, hands-on projects, and personalized mentoring.
*   Programs vary in duration, generally ranging from 11.5 to 15 months, and are designed to be flexible for working professionals.
*   Successful completion can lead to significant career advancement, with a focus on job-readiness and potential salary growth.

## Data Science and Machine Learning Program - Beginner

This 15-month program is tailored for individuals new to data science and machine learning. It aims to build a strong foundation, enabling participants to confidently tackle real-world challenges faced by data scientists and machine learning engineers from the outset.

The curriculum is delivered through live sessions by industry experts, focusing on practical application. Students will engage in projects developed with input from leading tech companies, allowing them to apply data science and machine learning concepts to solve and improve business problems. The program emphasizes preparing candidates for job placement, often highlighting potential for substantial salary growth in prominent organizations.

**Key Skills Covered:**

*   Machine Learning concepts
*   Data Science principles

**Program Fee:** Approximately ₹3,69,000

## Problem Solving & System Design - Beginner

Designed for engineers, this 11.5-month beginner-level program offers a structured yet adaptable learning path to master problem-solving and system design. It aims to instill confidence and proficiency in these critical areas.

The course incorporates hands-on projects and exercises, allowing students to put theoretical principles into practice. It offers personalized learning experiences, including tailored subjects, peer interaction, and a flexible pace to meet individual needs. The syllabus is industry-relevant and rigorously tested to ensure graduates are job-ready. Optional electives such as product management, advanced data structures and algorithms (DSA), and concurrent programming are also available. The program includes live lectures, practical experience with real-world projects, and regular one-on-one mentoring from industry professionals, along with career assistance and access to an alumni network.

**Key Skills Covered:**

*   Problem Solving Ability
*   Programming skills
*   Knowledge of Computer Networking

## Problem Solving & System Design - Intermediate

This 11.5-month intermediate-level program from Scaler Academy caters to those with some foundational knowledge. It offers significant flexibility, allowing students to watch recorded sessions, learn at their own pace, and even pause and resume their course with a later batch. Students can also collaborate with classmates to determine optimal class timings.

The comprehensive syllabus covers a wide array of topics, including:

*   Programming constructs (recursion, functions, pointers, structures)
*   Data structures (unions & dynamic arrays, strings, arrays, hashing, graphs)
*   Algorithms (sorting, asymptotic notations)
*   Computer Science fundamentals
*   Problem-solving techniques

Upon completing the core curriculum, learners can choose from two specializations: backend or full-stack development. Additionally, optional electives like advanced DSA, concurrent programming, and product management are available to further support engineers in their career progression.

Scaler Academy's programs provide a robust framework for professional development in the tech industry, combining expert instruction with practical experience and career support to help individuals achieve their goals.
