# Artificial Intelligence Certification Course by Edureka: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This page offers a detailed look at the Artificial Intelligence Certification Course, designed for individuals keen on mastering Natural Language Processing (NLP) and text analytics using Python. It's a comprehensive program that blends theoretical knowledge with practical application.

## Key Takeaways

*   The course focuses on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and text analytics, crucial areas within Artificial Intelligence.
*   It's delivered through a mix of self-study and virtual classroom sessions, with classes typically held on weekends.
*   Participants will gain hands-on experience, learning to build text classifiers and work with real-time data.
*   A certificate of completion is awarded upon successfully clearing all projects and tests.

## Course Overview

Edureka's Artificial Intelligence Certification Course provides a structured pathway to understanding and applying NLP and text analytics, primarily using the Python programming language. NLP is a significant branch of AI that explores how computers and humans interact through language. The curriculum is crafted to cover essential concepts, from basic text processing to advanced semantic analysis and machine learning techniques.

Students will delve into practical scenarios, allowing them to apply their learning immediately. Key topics include text processing, semantic analysis, and various machine learning algorithms relevant to text data. By the end of the program, participants should be equipped to develop their own machine learning models for text classification, addressing real-world problems.

## Learning Experience and Highlights

The program incorporates several features to enhance the learning journey:

*   **Instructor-Led Sessions:** Guided learning provides direct interaction and expert insights.
*   **Assignments and Case Studies:** Practical assignments and real-life case studies ensure hands-on application of concepts.
*   **Lifetime Access:** Participants benefit from continuous access to course materials.
*   **24/7 Expert Support:** Assistance is available around the clock for any queries or challenges.
*   **Learning Forum:** A dedicated platform for collaborative learning and discussion among peers and experts.
*   **Certification:** A formal certificate is issued by Edureka upon successful completion of the course requirements.

## Fees and Certification Details

The course fee is structured as follows:

| Head            | Amount in INR |

No-cost EMI options are also available, starting from ₹5,999 per month. A certificate of completion is provided by Edureka to candidates who successfully clear all projects and submit the required tests.

## Eligibility Criteria

Prospective students should have a foundational understanding of machine learning concepts and some familiarity with Python programming. For those who need to brush up on their Python skills, a complimentary introductory Python course is offered. The program is suitable for a wide range of individuals, including:

*   Developers aiming for a career as a 'data scientist'.
*   College students with programming exposure.
*   Technical architects and leads in organizations.
*   Analytics managers leading teams of analysts.
*   Python professionals interested in designing automated predictive models for text data.
*   Business analysts seeking to understand text mining techniques.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of this course, you will be able to:

*   Build your own text classifiers.
*   Understand and implement processes such as text processing, semantic analysis, and machine learning.
*   Work effectively with NLTK corpora, a popular Python library for NLP.
*   Analyze sentence structures and extract, clean, and preprocess text data.
*   Efficiently handle real-time data.
*   Gain a deep understanding of the role of an NLP engineer.
*   Master step-by-step text processing using IPython notebooks.
*   Utilize n-gram models to analyze bags of words from a corpus.
*   Learn about latent semantic analysis and its application in processing context-aware semantic content.
*   Understand bag-of-words modeling and text tokenization.
*   Access and modify common file types relevant to text data.
*   Convert text into vectors using techniques like word frequency count and TF-IDF.

This course is an excellent opportunity for anyone looking to advance their skills in Artificial Intelligence, particularly in the growing field of Natural Language Processing.
