# Medical Assistant by MedCerts: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Considering a career in healthcare as a Medical Assistant? MedCerts offers a comprehensive online program designed to equip you with the essential skills for this vital role. This page provides all the key details about their Medical Assistant course.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is delivered entirely online, allowing for self-paced learning through video and text materials.
*   It requires an estimated commitment of 15-20 hours per week.
*   The total course fee is $4,000, with monthly payment plans available from $367.
*   Upon successful completion, you receive a certificate from MedCerts.

## Course Overview and Structure

The MedCerts Medical Assistant program is structured for online learning, offering flexibility for students. The primary language of instruction is English, and the content is delivered through a combination of video lectures and text-based materials. This self-study format means you can progress through the curriculum at your own pace, fitting it around your existing commitments, provided you dedicate approximately 15-20 hours per week to your studies.

## Understanding the Fees

The total cost for the MedCerts Medical Assistant certification program is $4,000. For those who prefer to pay in installments, monthly payment options are available, starting as low as $367. This structure aims to make the program accessible to a wider range of students.

## What You Will Learn: The Syllabus

The curriculum is designed to provide a well-rounded education in medical assisting, covering both administrative and clinical aspects. Key modules you will study include:

*   **Professionalism in Allied Health:** This section focuses on the ethical conduct, communication skills, and professional responsibilities expected in a healthcare setting.
*   **Clinical Medical Assisting:** Here, you will learn practical clinical skills essential for assisting doctors and nurses.
*   **Introduction to Human Anatomy and Medical Terminology:** This foundational module covers the basics of human body structure and the specialized language used in medicine.
*   **Medical Office Procedures and Administration:** This part of the course deals with the administrative tasks involved in running a medical office, such as scheduling, record-keeping, and billing.

## Certification

Upon successful completion of all course requirements, MedCerts awards a certificate for the Medical Assistant program. This certification signifies that you have acquired the necessary knowledge and skills to perform the duties of a Medical Assistant.

This program offers a structured pathway for individuals looking to enter the healthcare field with a valuable and in-demand skill set.
