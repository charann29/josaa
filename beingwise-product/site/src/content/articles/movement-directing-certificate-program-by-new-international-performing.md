# Movement Directing Certificate Program by New International Performing Arts Institute: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring choreographers, theatre artists, and movement directors, the New International Performing Arts Institute (NIPAI) offers a specialized one-year Movement Directing Certificate Program. This course is designed to equip students with the skills needed to create, perform, and direct movement within the creative arts industry.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a one-year certificate program focused on movement directing for theatre and film.
*   The curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on experience, including 500 hours of practical work.
*   The program is delivered through a mix of self-study, virtual classrooms, and four weeks of in-person sessions.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from NIPAI, enhancing their portfolio and career prospects.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

This certificate program aims to develop advanced interpersonal and coaching skills, essential for working in the creative industry. It provides both theoretical and practical learning opportunities, familiarizing students with the intricacies of acting, performing, and directing in diverse, multicultural environments. The course structure encourages engagement with real-life scenarios, helping students strengthen their creative abilities and work effectively with artistic teams.

### What You Will Learn

The syllabus covers fundamental elements of performing arts, including choreography and acting. Students will gain a deep understanding of the core principles within creative arts, enabling them to collaborate and network with other creative professionals. The program explores what goes into the performing arts, from conceptualization to execution.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the Movement Directing Certificate Program, candidates must meet specific requirements:

*   **Academic Qualification:** A high school diploma is mandatory.
*   **Language Proficiency:** Demonstrated proficiency in English is required.
*   **Motivation:** Applicants should possess a strong desire to pursue a career in movement directing or related fields.

### Application Requirements

Along with academic qualifications, applicants must submit several documents:

*   A scanned copy of a passport or valid ID card.
*   A creative portfolio showcasing previous work.
*   A comprehensive CV (Curriculum Vitae).
*   Proof of high school graduation.
*   A motivation letter explaining their reasons for applying to the program.

## Course Duration and Structure

The Movement Directing Certificate Program is a one-year certification course. It incorporates a flexible learning model, combining self-study with virtual classroom sessions. A significant component includes four weeks of intensive in-class sessions, complemented by 500 hours of practical, hands-on experience. The program provides course readings and emphasizes practical learning with flexible deadlines, allowing students to manage their studies effectively.

## Program Fees

The total fee for the Movement Directing Certificate Program is 9500 EUR. This amount covers all course details and certification. Payment is processed through an online gateway after the application submission.

## Who is This Program For?

This certification course is particularly beneficial for actors and film directors looking to deepen their understanding of movement's role in various performing arts scenarios. It helps students learn how to communicate and delve into the concepts of acting, choreography, and direction to bring stories and performances to life.

Upon successful completion of all course tasks and requirements, students will receive a completion certificate from NIPAI, validating their expertise in movement directing.
