# Full Stack Software Development Bootcamp Program by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Starting a career in full-stack software development can be an exciting journey. This bootcamp program aims to equip recent graduates and freshers with the essential skills and practical experience needed to thrive in this dynamic field.

## Key Takeaways

*   A comprehensive 6-month full-time bootcamp designed for freshers and recent graduates.
*   Guaranteed placement opportunities with an expected annual salary range of Rs. 3-6 lakhs.
*   Offers both in-person classroom learning in select cities and live online virtual classes.
*   Focuses on industry-relevant skills, including Java, data structures, algorithms, and database management.

## Program Overview and Structure

This Full-Stack Software Development Bootcamp is a full-time, intensive 6-month program. It's specifically tailored for individuals looking to kickstart their career in software development, providing them with a strong foundation and practical expertise. The program culminates in a certificate of completion from the issuing authority.

Participants have the flexibility to choose their learning environment. They can opt for traditional classroom learning at campuses located in Chennai, Bangalore, or Hyderabad, or they can join live online classes from anywhere. The learning methodology combines self-study, virtual classroom sessions, and video and text-based content delivery, ensuring a well-rounded educational experience.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for this program, candidates must meet specific academic requirements:

*   Applicants should be B.Tech./B.E./BCA/BSc (IT) graduates from 2019 onwards.
*   A minimum passing score of 60% in their qualifying examination is required.
*   Students in their final year of graduation are also welcome to apply.

This program is ideally suited for individuals aspiring to become Full Stack Developers or Software Developers.

## What You Will Learn: Syllabus Highlights

The curriculum is designed to provide a robust understanding of full-stack development, addressing challenges professionals face in the industry. Key areas covered include:

*   **Core Java:** Fundamental programming constructs, Object-Oriented Programming (OOPs) concepts, loops, conditionals, and flow statements.
*   **Introduction to Data Structures & Algorithms:** Recursion, analysis of algorithms (time and space complexity), arrays, linked lists, strings, hash maps, sorting, and searching algorithms.
*   **Advanced Java:** Collections Frameworks, file handling, and Java 8 features like Lambda Expressions and Streams.
*   **Database Management System:** Database principles, data handling, introduction to MySQL, and SQL (DDL and DML statements).

Additionally, participants have the option to explore self-paced modules in React to further enhance their skillset.

## Career Support and Placement Opportunities

A significant advantage of this bootcamp is the robust career support and placement assistance provided. The program is designed to offer participants seven guaranteed placement opportunities upon successful completion. This support includes:

*   One-on-one career mentoring sessions.
*   Personalized resume building and LinkedIn profile reviews.
*   One-on-one mock interviews to prepare for real-world scenarios.
*   Dedicated placement drives with access to a network of over 2800 hiring companies.

The program has a track record of successfully placing over 6000 freshers, indicating its effectiveness in connecting graduates with employment opportunities.

## Application Process

The application process is straightforward and involves three main steps:

1.  **Basic Information:** Candidates begin by providing essential personal details such as their name, phone number, year of graduation, and email address.
2.  **Professional Details:** The second part requires applicants to share their professional background, which helps the institute evaluate their profile comprehensively.
3.  **Submission and Tracking:** Finally, candidates submit their application form and can then track its status for confirmation.

This program offers a structured path for aspiring full-stack developers to gain essential skills and secure employment in the IT industry.
