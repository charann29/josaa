# Is Full Fees Reimbursement Available for BC-D Students at RVR & JC College?

Understanding fee reimbursement policies is crucial for many students aspiring to pursue higher education. This page clarifies the government's fee reimbursement scheme, particularly for students from the BC-D category at RVR & JC College of Engineering, Guntur.

## Key Takeaways

*   Full fee reimbursement is possible for eligible BC-D category students at RVR & JC College through the Andhra Pradesh government scheme.
*   A key eligibility criterion for full reimbursement is an AP EAMCET rank below 10,000 for BC-D candidates.
*   Partial reimbursement may be available if your rank is above 10,000, provided other conditions are met.
*   Parental income and employment status are significant factors in determining eligibility for both full and partial schemes.

## Government Fee Reimbursement Scheme in Andhra Pradesh

The Government of Andhra Pradesh offers a comprehensive fee reimbursement program to support students from various reserved categories, including BC-D, in pursuing professional courses. This scheme aims to make quality education accessible by reducing the financial burden on eligible families. RVR & JC College of Engineering, being a government-recognized institution participating in AP EAMCET counseling, is included under this scheme.

### Eligibility Criteria for BC-D Category

For students belonging to the BC-D category, specific criteria must be met to qualify for fee reimbursement. These generally include:

*   **AP EAMCET Rank:** To be eligible for *full* fee reimbursement, BC-D candidates typically need to secure an AP EAMCET rank below 10,000. This is a primary condition set by the government for full financial support.
*   **Parental Income:** The annual income of your parents (or guardians) must be below a specified threshold. Currently, this limit is often Rs. 2,00,000 per annum. It's important to verify the exact figure from official government notifications as these can be updated.
*   **Parental Employment Status:** Generally, students whose parents are government employees are not eligible for full fee reimbursement. This criterion aims to support families with limited financial means.
*   **College Recognition:** The college must be recognized by the Government of Andhra Pradesh and participate in the AP EAMCET counseling process, which RVR & JC College of Engineering does.

## Full vs. Partial Fee Reimbursement

Based on your eligibility, you might qualify for either full or partial fee reimbursement:

*   **Full Fee Reimbursement:** If you meet all the stringent eligibility criteria, including the AP EAMCET rank requirement (below 10,000 for BC-D) and the income/employment conditions, the government may cover the entire tuition fee.
*   **Partial Fee Reimbursement:** If your AP EAMCET rank is above 10,000 but you still satisfy other key criteria (like parental income and employment status), you might be eligible for partial fee reimbursement. This means a portion of your tuition fees would be covered by the government, reducing your overall financial outlay.

### How to Apply for Reimbursement

Students who believe they are eligible for fee reimbursement must apply for the scholarship scheme through the official Andhra Pradesh government scholarship portal. This typically involves submitting various documents, such as caste certificates, income certificates, AP EAMCET rank cards, and study certificates, within specified application windows. It is crucial to monitor official announcements regarding application dates and required documentation.

## RVR & JC College of Engineering and Fee Reimbursement

RVR & JC College of Engineering, Guntur, is a well-regarded institution that participates in the AP EAMCET counseling process. As such, it facilitates the government's fee reimbursement scheme for eligible students. If you secure admission to RVR & JC College through AP EAMCET counseling and meet the government's criteria, you can avail of the fee reimbursement benefits directly through the college administration in coordination with the state's welfare department.

Always refer to the latest official notifications from the Government of Andhra Pradesh's Department of Technical Education or Social Welfare for the most accurate and up-to-date information on eligibility and application procedures.
