Understanding how to connect with your customers is fundamental for anyone looking to excel in sales or promote their own ventures. This course, offered through Udemy, focuses on building lasting relationships and effective sales strategies.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to build strong, long-term customer relationships for repeat business.
*   Master communication, networking, and advertising for effective self-promotion.
*   Gain insights into creating a positive customer experience that fosters loyalty.
*   Access 31 hours of video content at your own pace, with a certificate upon completion.

## Course Overview

This certification program, titled "How To Talk To Your Customers For Sales And Self Promotion," is designed by business and sales expert Mike Korytny. It aims to equip individuals with the skills needed to become highly effective in sales, focusing on strategies that lead to repeat customers and enduring professional connections.

The curriculum emphasizes a customer-centric approach, teaching you how to deliver such a positive experience that clients will consistently choose your services. It also covers techniques for promoting your business effectively within the market and among your professional network.

## What You Will Learn

Throughout the 31 hours of video-based instruction, you will delve into several crucial areas:

*   **Communication Skills:** Develop a strong understanding of business communication and persuasive techniques essential for sales.
*   **Sales Fundamentals:** Grasp core sales principles and cultivate the skills necessary for success in various sales environments.
*   **Networking and Promotion:** Learn how to organically expand your business through effective networking, strategic promotions, and advertising.
*   **Customer Relationship Management:** Discover methods for building and maintaining long-term professional relationships, ensuring customer satisfaction and loyalty.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is particularly beneficial for professionals in roles such as:

*   Sales Manager
*   Marketing Manager
*   Customer Service Manager
*   Marketing Executive
*   Anyone aspiring to build a career in sales or seeking to enhance their self-promotion capabilities.

## Program Details and Fees

The course is delivered in English, primarily through self-paced video and text content. Key features include:

*   **Self-Paced Learning:** Study at your convenience.
*   **Extensive Content:** 31 hours of pre-recorded video lessons.
*   **Certificate of Completion:** Awarded by Udemy upon successful course completion.
*   **Unlimited Access:** Enjoy perpetual access to the course materials.
*   **Money-Back Guarantee:** A 30-day money-back guarantee is available.
*   **Accessibility:** Content is accessible on mobile devices and televisions.

The course fee is ₹799. This fee includes access to all course materials and the certificate of completion.

## Course Syllabus Highlights

The comprehensive syllabus covers various modules, including:

*   **Introduction and Overview:** Setting the stage for effective selling and networking.
*   **The Deal:** Strategies for presenting compelling offers and understanding customer perception.
*   **The Customer Experience:** Emphasizing the importance of excellent customer service and building strong relationships.
*   **Networking:** Techniques for connecting with others to generate more business.
*   **Sales:** In-depth exploration of sales structures, relationship building, and diverse sales approaches.
*   **Connecting with Social Media:** Leveraging social platforms for advertising, self-promotion, and long-term engagement.

This course offers a practical pathway to mastering the art of customer interaction, leading to enhanced sales performance and effective self-promotion.
