# Test-Driven Development Overview by LearnQuest via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online certification course, offered through Coursera in collaboration with LearnQuest, provides a comprehensive introduction to Test-Driven Development (TDD). It's designed to help participants understand what TDD is, its applications, and how to implement it effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an intermediate-level, self-paced online program delivered in English.
*   The course is approximately 14 hours long, requiring about 4 hours of study per week.
*   A basic understanding of Python programming is recommended as a prerequisite.
*   Upon successful completion and subscription, participants receive a professional certificate.

## Course Overview

The Test-Driven Development Overview program is structured to guide learners through the core concepts of TDD. It delves into the benefits of TDD for both individuals and organizations, exploring various types of testing and how TDD contributes to creating clean, efficient code. The course is entirely self-paced, allowing students the flexibility to learn at their convenience.

Study materials include video lectures, reading assignments, practice exercises, quizzes, and assessments. The entire program consists of only four modules, making it a focused learning experience.

## What You Will Learn

Through this course, participants will gain practical insights into the world of TDD. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Understanding the definition and practical application of TDD.
*   Recognizing the advantages of TDD for various stakeholders.
*   Exploring solutions for those adopting TDD methodologies.
*   Learning how TDD helps in developing high-quality, clean code.
*   Familiarizing oneself with different testing types.
*   Appreciating the significance and necessity of adopting TDD.
*   Tracing the origins and understanding the fundamental terminology of TDD.

## Eligibility and Prerequisites

While there are no strict educational prerequisites, a foundational understanding of Python programming is essential to grasp the concepts taught in the course. This background will enable participants to fully engage with the material and understand the practical examples presented.

To earn the professional certificate, students must enroll in the subscribed mode of the course by paying the applicable fees and successfully complete all course requirements.

## Course Fees and Certification

The Test-Driven Development Overview course offers flexible subscription options, with fees varying based on the duration of access:

| Head      | Amount (INR) |
| :-------- | :----------- |
| 1 Month   | 1,683        |
| 3 Months  | 3,384        |
| 6 Months  | 5,051        |

Certification is provided by Coursera upon successful completion of the course in the subscribed mode. Scholarships may also be available for eligible candidates.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Test-Driven Development Overview course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official course page on the Coursera platform.
2.  Create a Coursera account if you do not already have one.
3.  Click on the 'Enroll' option for the course.
4.  Proceed to the course content by selecting 'Go to course'.

This structured approach ensures a smooth entry into your learning journey.
