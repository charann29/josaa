# Google Cloud Certification Training: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to advance their career in cloud computing, understanding the Google Cloud Platform (GCP) is a valuable step. This training aims to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge required for the official Google Cloud certification.

## Key takeaways

*   The program offers flexible learning options, including self-study and virtual classrooms, with weekend classes.
*   It provides practical experience through real-life case studies, assignments, and a final project.
*   Participants gain lifetime access to the learning management system (LMS) and 24/7 technical support.
*   A completion certificate is awarded based on project performance.

## Course Overview

This certification training is designed to prepare you thoroughly for the Google Cloud Platform certification exam. You will delve into the core principles and foundational concepts of GCP, building a strong understanding of how cloud infrastructure operates.

The curriculum covers the management of various GCP services, including databases, networking, and security. It starts with the basics of cloud computing and progresses to the core infrastructure components of Google Cloud. The course content strictly follows the official exam guide provided by Google, ensuring you learn exactly what's needed for certification.

Furthermore, the training emphasizes practical application. You will learn to use key command-line tools for importing data into Google Cloud Storage and running queries for data analysis. Developing skills in creating migration plans, designing strategies, and collecting and analyzing performance metrics are also integral parts of the program.

## Learning Experience and Resources

The training incorporates a variety of features to enhance your learning journey:

*   **Online Instructor-Led Live Sessions:** Engage with experienced instructors in real-time.
*   **Class Recordings:** Access recordings of all sessions for review and self-paced learning.
*   **Real-Life Industry Case Studies:** Apply theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios.
*   **Assignments and Projects:** Evaluate your understanding and build a portfolio of work.
*   **Lifetime LMS Access:** Continuously refer to course materials and updates.
*   **24x7 Technical Support:** Get assistance whenever you need it.
*   **Industry Application Assignments:** Work on tasks relevant to real-world cloud environments.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Google Cloud Certification Training program is ₹19,995, which includes the course fee and applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST). There might also be limited-period discounts available, such as a 15% reduction on the course fee.

This certificate is provided by the training provider, acknowledging your expertise in Google Cloud. To qualify for the certificate, students must successfully complete a final project, which will be assessed.

## Eligibility and What You Will Learn

There are no specific prerequisites for enrolling in this GCP Certification Training. Individuals with a basic understanding of IT Service Management will find the course beneficial. It is ideal for IT professionals looking to explore career opportunities in cloud computing.

Upon completing the training, you will be able to:

*   Grasp the fundamentals of cloud computing and Google Cloud Platform (GCP).
*   Develop in-depth knowledge of various GCP products and services.
*   Effectively use GCP services like Cloud Storage, Compute Engine, BigQuery, and Cloud SQL.
*   Manage diverse GCP environments using the Console, Command-line interface, and App methods.
*   Implement and configure Deployment Manager, Cloud Launcher, and deploy applications using App Engine.
*   Acquire deep knowledge of GCP's Machine Learning (ML) and Big Data services.
*   Build proficiency in various technologies, designs, and methods for implementing security, high availability, and scalability within GCP.

This comprehensive training prepares you to confidently navigate and manage Google Cloud environments, opening doors to various career roles in the cloud computing domain.
