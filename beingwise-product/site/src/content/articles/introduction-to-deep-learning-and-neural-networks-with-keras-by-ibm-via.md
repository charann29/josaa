# Introduction to Deep Learning & Neural Networks with Keras by IBM via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Deep Learning is a rapidly evolving field, crucial for understanding today's technological advancements, especially with the rise of the Internet of Things (IoT). This Coursera certification, developed by IBM, offers a comprehensive introduction to deep learning models and neural networks, equipping participants with practical skills using the Keras library.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to build and deploy deep learning models using the Keras library.
*   Understand the fundamental concepts of neural networks and their applications.
*   Explore various deep learning algorithms, including supervised and unsupervised models.
*   Receive a certificate of completion from Coursera, backed by IBM's expertise.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This program is designed for self-paced learning, delivered through a mix of video lectures and text-based materials, all in English. It's structured to provide a deep dive into deep learning models, starting with an introduction to their exciting real-world applications. You will learn about algorithms and neural networks, drawing inspiration from how the human brain processes information, and understand how data flows through these networks.

Key topics covered include optimizing variables, the gradient descent algorithm, backpropagation, and adjusting weights and biases in neural networks. The course also addresses activation functions and common challenges like the vanishing gradient problem. Learning materials include pre-recorded videos, reading assignments, practice exercises, and quizzes to reinforce understanding.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, you will gain proficiency in several key areas, essential for roles such as an AI developer, data scientist, or machine learning engineer. Specifically, you will be able to:

*   Distinguish between deep learning models and neural networks.
*   Understand supervised deep learning models like convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and recurrent neural networks (RNNs).
*   Grasp unsupervised deep learning models such as autoencoders and restricted Boltzmann machines.
*   Construct deep learning and artificial intelligence models using the Keras library.
*   Become familiar with various deep learning libraries, including Keras, TensorFlow, and PyTorch.
*   Develop regression and classification models using Keras.

## Program Duration and Fees

The estimated time required to complete the entire program is approximately 9 hours. Coursera offers flexible subscription options, allowing you to complete the course at your own pace. The fees are structured based on the duration of your subscription:

| Subscription Duration | Amount (approx.) |
| :-------------------- | :--------------- |
| 1 month               | Rs. 1,676        |
| 3 months              | Rs. 3,369        |
| 6 months              | Rs. 5,029        |

Financial aid assistance may also be available for eligible candidates, as stated by Coursera.

## Eligibility and Certification

There are no specific prior eligibility criteria mentioned for enrolling in this course. To earn the official certificate of completion, you must successfully finish all program modules and assignments. The certificate is awarded by Coursera, acknowledging your achievement and the skills you've gained through this IBM-developed curriculum.

## How to Apply

To begin your learning journey, you can enroll directly through the Coursera platform. Simply visit the official Coursera website, search for the "Introduction to Deep Learning & Neural Networks with Keras" course, and follow the on-screen instructions to enroll. A 7-day free trial is often available, allowing you to explore the course content before committing to a subscription.
