## MBA Admissions 2026: Eligibility and Entrance Exams

To secure admission into a top MBA program in Chennai for the 2026 academic year, candidates generally need to meet specific eligibility criteria and perform well in entrance examinations. The general requirements are:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognised university with a minimum aggregate score, typically 50%.
*   **Entrance Exams:** Successful completion and a valid score in accepted management entrance exams are mandatory. These commonly include CAT, XAT, NMAT, and TANCET.
*   **Final Year Students:** Candidates who are currently in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply, provided they can submit their final degree results by the institute's stipulated deadline.
