# Digital Academy 360 Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Digital Academy 360 Courses

Digital Academy 360 offers various online programs designed to equip students with essential skills in the rapidly evolving digital landscape. These courses cater to different experience levels, from foundational knowledge to advanced leadership in digital marketing.

## Key takeaways

*   Digital Academy 360 provides a range of certification programs in digital marketing, AI, and e-commerce.
*   Programs emphasize practical, hands-on learning through live projects, agency campaigns, and case studies.
*   Courses often include industry expert guidance, internship opportunities, and globally recognized certifications.
*   Scholarship assistance may be available for some programs, making them more accessible.

## Leadership in Digital Marketing, AI & Entrepreneurship Course

This comprehensive 9-month program is tailored for individuals aspiring to leadership roles in the digital sphere. It delves deep into advanced digital marketing strategies, artificial intelligence applications, and entrepreneurial principles.

The curriculum is extensive, featuring 30 in-depth courses and 10 specialized modules. Students gain significant practical exposure by working on over 15 real-time projects and executing more than 20 live agency campaigns across diverse sectors like education, real estate, banking, and tourism. A core focus is on performance marketing, with hands-on experience in managing paid campaigns across major platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google Ads, and Quora.

The program benefits from the guidance of over 12 industry experts and includes a mandatory 3-month internship at prominent digital marketing agencies. Successful completion can lead to more than 22 globally recognized certifications, preparing participants for a dynamic career.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Artificial Intelligence concepts
*   Advanced Digital Marketing techniques
*   Entrepreneurial strategies

## PGCP In Digital Marketing and E-Commerce

The Post Graduate Certification Program (PGCP) in Digital Marketing and E-Commerce is a 6-month interactive program delivered through live, one-on-one sessions. It focuses on building robust digital marketing skills essential for the e-commerce sector.

This program features a curriculum with 20 courses, enabling students to develop expertise in four advanced specializations. A significant emphasis is placed on applied learning, with participants engaging directly in live projects and case studies to simulate real-world digital marketing scenarios. Financial assistance, such as scholarships of up to ₹20,000, may be available to make the program more accessible to a wider audience.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Social Media Marketing
*   Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
*   Content Management

## Skill Diploma in Digital Marketing and Analytics

Designed to equip learners with fundamental digital marketing skills, the Skill Diploma in Digital Marketing and Analytics is a 6-month live, one-on-one interaction program. It provides a solid foundation for those new to the field or looking to solidify their basic understanding.

The curriculum comprises 8 courses, complemented by 3 live projects and 20 case studies. This program also strongly emphasizes experiential learning, ensuring students are constantly involved in practical applications. This approach prepares them effectively for the common challenges encountered in the digital marketing industry.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Social Media basics
*   Social Media Marketing strategies
*   Search Engine Optimization (SEO) fundamentals
*   General Digital Marketing skills

These programs from Digital Academy 360 offer structured pathways for individuals to enhance their digital capabilities and advance their careers in a competitive industry.
