# Full Stack Web Development by Geekster: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Geekster offers a comprehensive part-time certification program in Full Stack Web Development, designed for individuals looking to build a career in software development. This program focuses on practical skills and career readiness, making it suitable for students, recent graduates, and working professionals.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Duration:** The program spans 8 months, with classes held online from Monday to Saturday, typically after office hours.
*   **Curriculum:** It covers programming fundamentals, data structures, algorithms, and full-stack development, alongside essential soft skills.
*   **Fee Structure:** The program fee is ₹124,999 + GST, with an Income Share Agreement (ISA) option allowing students to pay after securing a job.
*   **Eligibility:** Open to final-year students, recent graduates, and working professionals, with a preference for those with basic programming knowledge.

## Course Overview

This 8-month certification in Full Stack Web Development is delivered through live online classes on Geekster's platform. It's structured as a part-time program, making it manageable for individuals balancing other commitments. Sessions are typically held from Monday to Saturday, between 8:00 PM and 10:30 PM, to accommodate working professionals. The program aims to provide a strong foundation in core computer science concepts and advanced web development techniques.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to equip participants with a wide range of skills essential for web application development. Key areas of study include:

*   **Programming Fundamentals:** Introduction to various programming languages, variables, data types, and operators.
*   **Computer Science Core Concepts:** Algorithms and data structures.
*   **Full Stack Development:** Comprehensive training in both front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML and CSS.
*   **Soft Skills and Interview Preparation:** Training to enhance communication, problem-solving, and other essential professional skills, along with mock interviews and quantitative aptitude sessions.
*   **Capstone Project:** Practical application of learned skills through a significant project.

The learning approach is blended, combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on practice to ensure a thorough understanding of web development.

## Program Fees and Certification

The total program fee for the Full Stack Web Development course is ₹124,999, in addition to Goods and Services Tax (GST). Geekster provides an Income Share Agreement (ISA) option, which allows students to defer fee payment until they are successfully placed in a job. Under this model, students contribute a percentage of their salary towards the fee each month after employment.

Upon successful completion of the course, participants receive a Full Stack Web Development certification issued by Geekster.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for this program, candidates should be:

*   Final-year students.
*   Recently graduated individuals.
*   Working professionals looking to upskill or transition their careers.

While not strictly mandatory, some prior knowledge of basic programming is preferred to ensure a smoother learning experience.

### How to Apply

The admission process for the Full Stack Web Development program involves several steps:

1.  **Online Application:** Visit the official Geekster website and complete the application form. This form will require details such as your full name, email address, degree and year of completion, and information about your programming knowledge.
2.  **Admission Test:** After submitting the application, candidates must take an admission test.
3.  **Pre-Course Session:** Successful candidates will then attend a mandatory 7-day pre-course session.
4.  **ISA Documentation:** Following the pre-course session, applicants will need to sign the Income Share Agreement (ISA) documentation, which outlines the 'pay after placement' plan.
5.  **Secure Admission:** Upon signing the ISA, admission to the program is confirmed.

This structured process ensures that all participants are well-prepared and committed to the program's objectives.

Geekster's Full Stack Web Development program is an excellent opportunity for aspiring software developers to gain in-demand skills and launch their careers with robust support.
