# Best Free Simplilearn Courses & Certifications - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Career Options

Simplilearn, through its SkillUp Online initiative, offers a selection of valuable free courses designed to introduce learners to in-demand skills and emerging technologies. These programs provide a solid foundation for those looking to explore new career paths or enhance their existing expertise without any financial commitment.

## Key takeaways

*   Simplilearn provides several free online courses through its SkillUp Online platform.
*   These courses cover popular fields like Digital Marketing, Salesforce, IoT, and Enterprise Architecture.
*   All featured courses are completely free of charge and include a completion certificate.
*   They are self-paced, allowing learners to study at their convenience and gain foundational knowledge.

## Introduction to Digital Marketing Fundamentals Course

This introductory course is perfect for anyone keen to understand the basics of digital marketing. It aims to equip learners with the skills to make a measurable impact on a company's performance using various digital channels. The program, developed by industry experts, offers over five hours of high-quality online content, complemented by 13 quizzes to test understanding after each chapter.

Upon finishing, participants receive a Digital Marketing training certificate. The course is self-paced, granting individual learners lifetime access to the content, allowing them to study at their preferred speed. Businesses can also opt for corporate training to upskill their employees.

*   **Duration:** 5 hours
*   **Cost:** Free
*   **Skills Covered:** Foundational digital marketing skills
*   **Career Options:** Entry-level roles in digital marketing, social media marketing, content marketing, or as a stepping stone to more advanced digital marketing specializations.

## Salesforce Administrator & App Builder

This course introduces the essential concepts of Salesforce administration and application development. It covers key topics such as Salesforce objects, fields, validation rules, Lightning components, and security controls. The program is specifically designed to help learners prepare for the official Salesforce certification exam, enabling them to become certified Salesforce professionals.

By the end of this course, participants will be skilled in developing and managing applications for Salesforce using the Salesforce Lightning framework. They will also learn how to configure Salesforce to efficiently collect, analyze, and retrieve crucial customer data, ultimately improving business performance. A completion certificate is provided upon finishing the course.

*   **Duration:** 16 hours
*   **Cost:** Free
*   **Skills Covered:** Salesforce administration, app building, Lightning framework, security controls, data management.
*   **Career Options:** Salesforce Administrator, Salesforce App Builder, CRM Analyst, or roles requiring Salesforce platform management.

## TOGAF® 9 Combined Level 1 and Level 2 Training Course

This training provides an introduction to TOGAF (The Open Group Architecture Framework), a widely recognized framework for enterprise architecture. TOGAF is crucial for designing, planning, implementing, and governing an organization's information architecture. The course covers the core concepts of TOGAF 9th Edition, including the Architecture Development Method (ADM), the Enterprise Continuum, the Architecture Repository, and Architecture Governance.

The program also explains how to adapt the ADM to different contexts, apply various guidelines and techniques, and produce architecture deliverables and views. It prepares learners for the TOGAF Foundation certification exam, a respected credential for aspiring enterprise architects.

*   **Duration:** 8 hours
*   **Cost:** Free
*   **Skills Covered:** TOGAF 9th Edition principles, enterprise architecture design, ADM application, architecture governance.
*   **Career Options:** Junior Enterprise Architect, Business Architect, IT Consultant, or roles involved in strategic IT planning and governance.

## Introduction to IoT Training Course

Simplilearn's Introduction to IoT Training program is a self-paced course designed to provide comprehensive knowledge about the origins, applications, and impacts of the Internet of Things (IoT) industry. The curriculum also delves into the integration of business applications within IoT to achieve better results.

Candidates will receive two hours of in-depth e-learning material, offering a foundational understanding of this rapidly evolving technology.

*   **Duration:** 2 hours
*   **Cost:** Free
*   **Skills Covered:** IoT fundamentals, applications of IoT, business integration with IoT.
*   **Career Options:** Foundation for roles in IoT development, IoT solutions architect, smart device management, or any field leveraging connected technologies.

These free offerings from Simplilearn's SkillUp Online are an excellent way to gain foundational knowledge and explore new opportunities in high-demand fields, all while earning a valuable certificate of completion.
