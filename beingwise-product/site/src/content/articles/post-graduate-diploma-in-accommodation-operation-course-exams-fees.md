# Post Graduate Diploma in Accommodation Operation: Course, Exams, Fees, Colleges, Career

The Post Graduate Diploma (PGD) in Accommodation Operation is a specialised master's level programme designed for individuals aspiring to build a career in the dynamic hospitality sector. This course equips students with essential skills and knowledge to effectively manage hotel operations, from staff supervision to financial oversight.

## Key Takeaways

*   This 1.5-year postgraduate diploma focuses specifically on the operational aspects of hotel accommodation.
*   Graduates learn about staff management, accounting, and overall hotel functioning, combining classroom theory with practical industrial training.
*   Eligibility generally requires a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognised Indian institution, with admissions based on merit or entrance exams.
*   Career paths include roles such as Front Office Executive, Housekeeping Executive, and Revenue Manager within leading hotel chains.

## Understanding the PGD in Accommodation Operation

This postgraduate diploma spans one and a half years, providing a comprehensive education in hospitality management, with a strong emphasis on accommodation services. The curriculum is crafted to give students a deep understanding of how hotels operate on a day-to-day basis. It covers crucial areas like managing diverse teams, ensuring guest satisfaction, and maintaining accurate financial records related to hotel operations. A significant component of the programme is mandatory industrial training, which offers invaluable real-world experience in a professional hotel environment.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

To be considered for the Post Graduate Diploma in Accommodation Operation, candidates must meet specific criteria set by the admitting institutions. While these can vary slightly from one college to another, the fundamental requirement is a bachelor's degree from a university or institution recognised in India. Students from any academic background are generally welcome to apply.

Admission into the programme typically follows one of two paths: either through a competitive entrance examination conducted by the institution or based on the candidate's academic merit from their undergraduate studies. Some institutions may also have an age limit, often around 25 years (with relaxations for SC/ST categories), and might require a physical fitness certificate from a registered medical practitioner.

## Career Opportunities and Salary Expectations

Upon successful completion of the PGD in Accommodation Operation, graduates unlock a variety of exciting career prospects within the hospitality industry. The skills acquired during the programme are highly valued by leading hotel chains and resorts, both in India and internationally.

Common job roles include:

*   **Front Office Executive:** Managing guest arrivals, departures, and inquiries.
*   **Housekeeping Executive:** Overseeing cleanliness, maintenance, and guest room standards.
*   **Revenue Manager:** Optimising room rates and occupancy to maximise hotel income.
*   **Hotel Manager Trainee:** Learning comprehensive hotel operations with a view to future leadership roles.

Top recruiters frequently include prestigious names like Taj Hotels, Oberoi Hotels & Resorts, ITC Hotels, Marriott International, and Hyatt Hotels Corporation. The average starting salary for graduates in roles like Front Office Executive can be around Rs. 2.7 Lakhs per annum, though this figure can vary significantly based on the specific role, the employing hotel, and the individual's skills and experience.

This diploma provides a robust foundation for a rewarding career in the flourishing hospitality sector, preparing you for immediate industry roles and future leadership opportunities.
