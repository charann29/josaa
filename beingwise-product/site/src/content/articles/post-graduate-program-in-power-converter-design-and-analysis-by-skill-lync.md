# Post Graduate Program in Power Converter Design and Analysis by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This comprehensive online program is designed for electrical engineers and research associates looking to deepen their expertise in the crucial field of power converter design and analysis. Delivered through video and text-based modules, it offers a flexible, self-paced learning experience to enhance your professional skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Online and Self-Paced:** The entire program is delivered online, allowing you to learn at your own convenience over a 12-month period.
*   **Practical Focus:** It includes six courses and four dedicated design and analysis projects to build hands-on skills.
*   **Career Support:** Skill Lync provides technical and career guidance, including job assistance and a project portfolio to showcase your work.
*   **Flexible Payment Options:** Various payment plans, including EMI options, are available to suit different financial needs.

## Program Overview

The Master's Program in Power Converter Design and Analysis aims to equip participants with advanced theoretical and practical knowledge in electrical power conversion. Power converters are essential devices that transform electrical energy from one form to another, and this program delves into their intricate design and analytical principles. The curriculum is structured to provide a holistic learning experience, enabling engineers to design and construct modern converters with sophisticated controls.

## What You Will Learn

Throughout this 12-month program, you will develop a strong foundation in several key areas:

*   **Design and Engineering Skills:** Gain proficiency in the methodologies required for developing efficient power converters.
*   **Software Proficiency:** Learn to use industry-standard tools like MATLAB and Simulink for simulating and designing power converters, and Altium for advanced Printed Circuit Board (PCB) design.
*   **Core Power Electronics Concepts:** Understand the principles of AC-DC rectifiers, DC-DC converters, harmonics, and relevant industry standards.
*   **Physics and Electronics:** Reinforce your understanding of the underlying physics and electronic concepts critical to electrical engineering.

By the end of the program, you will be capable of applying these concepts to real-world industrial power electronic converter designs.

## Program Offerings and Support

Skill Lync is committed to supporting its learners through various channels:

*   **Technical Support:** Access to support engineers via individual video, group video, email, and forum for any technical queries.
*   **Career Guidance:** Receive career assistance and guidance to help you advance professionally.
*   **Project Portfolio:** Build a robust portfolio of projects, an E-verified profile, and a demo video to showcase your capabilities to potential employers.
*   **Certificates:** Upon successful completion, you will receive a course completion certificate. Top-performing students (within the top 5%) will also be awarded a merit certificate.

To enroll in the program, interested candidates can visit the official Skill Lync website. The application process typically involves registering online and selecting your preferred payment plan.
