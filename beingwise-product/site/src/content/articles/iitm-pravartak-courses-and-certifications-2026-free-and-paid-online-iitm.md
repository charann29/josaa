# IITM Pravartak Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online IITM Pravartak Courses

IITM Pravartak, an initiative hosted by IIT Madras, offers a range of online certification programs designed to enhance skills in cutting-edge technological and financial domains. These courses aim to provide practical knowledge and industry-relevant expertise for students and professionals alike.

## Key takeaways

*   IITM Pravartak provides certification courses in collaboration with industry partners, focusing on practical, applied learning.
*   Programs cover high-demand areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, and specialized tech skills.
*   Course durations vary from 14 weeks to 6 months, with fees ranging from approximately ₹8,850 to ₹1,77,000.
*   These certifications are suitable for both beginners looking to enter a new field and experienced professionals aiming to upgrade their skills.

## Artificial Intelligence Certification

This six-month program, offered in collaboration with IITM Pravartak, focuses on equipping participants with essential Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence capabilities. The curriculum is structured to help learners tackle real-world problems and develop effective solutions using AI principles.

It is designed for a broad audience, including those new to the field as well as seasoned practitioners who wish to deepen their AI knowledge. The course emphasizes practical insights and hands-on experience, preparing individuals for the evolving demands of the AI landscape. The fee for this certification is approximately ₹59,000.

## TechXcelerator Boot Camp

The TechXcelerator Boot Camp is a 14-week joint finishing school program aimed at aspiring technology professionals. This collaborative effort brings together industry experts and educators to deliver a transformative learning experience. The primary goal is to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the dynamic technology sector.

This program integrates hands-on training, real-world project work, and mentorship. It focuses not only on technical proficiency but also on developing a crucial problem-solving mindset, essential for success in a rapidly changing tech environment. The skills covered include project management and software development. The approximate fee for this boot camp is ₹30,000.

## Large Scale Data Analytics Driven Systems Design

This three-month certification course delves into the complexities of designing and implementing systems that leverage large-scale data analytics. Participants will gain a deep understanding of the core principles and methodologies required for building robust systems capable of processing massive datasets.

The program explores advanced techniques in data analytics, machine learning, and distributed computing, providing hands-on experience with industry-standard technologies. This course empowers individuals to contribute to innovation in the field of large-scale data analytics-driven systems. The fee for this specialized training is approximately ₹1,77,000.

## Certification Program in Mutual Funds

Designed to equip individuals with the necessary knowledge and skills for mutual fund management, this three-month program provides a comprehensive understanding of the principles, strategies, and regulations governing mutual funds. It enables participants to navigate the complexities of the financial market with confidence.

Topics covered include portfolio management, risk assessment, regulatory compliance, and investment analysis. The course incorporates practical case studies, interactive discussions, and real-world simulations to develop practical insights and strategic decision-making abilities. The approximate fee for this program is ₹8,850.

These diverse certification programs from IITM Pravartak offer valuable opportunities for skill enhancement and career advancement in various high-growth sectors.
