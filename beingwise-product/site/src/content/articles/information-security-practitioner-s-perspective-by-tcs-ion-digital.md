# Information Security - Practitioner's Perspective by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub Skillsoft: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in safeguarding digital assets, the 'Information Security - Practitioner's Perspective' online certification course from TCS iON Digital Learning Hub offers a practical pathway. This program focuses on equipping learners with the skills to manage information risks, protect organizational data, and secure network systems effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Comprehensive Learning:** The course covers information risk management, threat identification, and security strategies.
*   **Expert Mentorship:** Learn from industry leaders like Ajit Menon (Chief Security Officer of TCS) and Alok Tripathi (Director of NIELIT).
*   **Industry Recognition:** Gain five course credits and a digital certificate from TCS iON upon successful completion.
*   **Blended Learning:** The program combines self-study with virtual classroom sessions, video, and text-based materials.

## Course Overview and Structure

This online certification is designed to provide deep insights into information security strategies and help participants identify potential threats to information and network systems. Delivered over approximately five to six months, the course utilizes online lectures, tutorials, and practical sessions to create an engaging virtual learning environment.

The curriculum is structured to foster industrial competence, supported by both academic and industry professionals. It aims to develop a strong understanding of preventing risks and implementing robust data protection measures within organizations.

## Program Offerings and Learning Experience

The 'Information Security - Practitioner's Perspective' course offers a rich learning experience through various components:

*   **Instructional Materials:** Access to online lectures, tutorials, practical sessions, and comprehensive digital resources.
*   **Community Engagement:** Opportunities to connect with both academic and industry communities.
*   **Assignments:** A blend of industry-specific, practice, and summative assignments to reinforce learning.
*   **Career Development:** Includes mentorship, career guidance, and internship opportunities to provide real-world exposure.
*   **Certification:** Successful candidates receive a digital certificate from TCS iON.

The medium of instruction for this self-study and virtual classroom program is English, making it accessible to a wide audience.

## Eligibility Criteria and Certification Details

To enroll in this program, prospective students should have foundational knowledge in information systems, computer networks, and software development. The course is particularly well-suited for engineering students from computer science, information technology, and electronics and communication backgrounds, aspiring to roles such as security administrators or network engineers.

Certification is awarded based on specific performance criteria:

*   **Certificate of Achievement:** Requires a minimum of 40% in Part A, 50% in Part B, and an overall average of at least 50% in the summative assessment. Those who do not meet these criteria will receive a scorecard.
*   **Certificate of Participation:** Awarded upon completion of the course, including all projects and practice assignments.

## Course Fees and Payment

The total fee for the 'Information Security - Practitioner's Perspective' certification course is ₹20,000, inclusive of GST. This fee can be conveniently paid online through the course website.

| Particular | Details |
| :----------------- | :---------- |
| **Course Fee** | ₹20,000 |

Upon successful completion and meeting the certification criteria, a digital certificate will be provided by TCS iON.

## What You Will Learn

The syllabus is meticulously designed to equip students with professional skills and knowledge in security management for information and network systems. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Cybersecurity Fundamentals:** A strong grasp of cybersecurity principles.
*   **ITIL Concepts:** Understanding of IT Infrastructure Library (ITIL).
*   **Computer Networking:** In-depth knowledge of network systems.
*   **Risk Management:** Ability to identify, assess, and mitigate information security risks.

Participants will learn to pinpoint potential risks and threats, developing effective solutions and security models to prevent and overcome these challenges.

This program offers a robust foundation for a successful career in the critical field of information security.
