# Sr.Secondary: Economics (318) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students aspiring to build a strong foundation in economics at the senior secondary level, the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) offers a comprehensive online course through the Swayam platform. This program, titled "Sr.Secondary: Economics (318)," is designed to introduce learners to core economic principles and the Indian economy.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a self-paced, online course delivered over 24 weeks.
*   The program offers 10 academic credit points upon successful completion.
*   Learners will receive a certificate from NIOS and Swayam after meeting all requirements.
*   The course covers both microeconomics and macroeconomics, with a focus on the Indian context.

## Course Overview and Structure

This economics course is an introductory program suitable for students at the senior secondary level. It's offered as a Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) on the Swayam platform, ensuring accessibility for a wide audience. The curriculum is delivered through a blend of video lectures and reading materials, allowing for flexible learning.

The course spans 24 weeks and is structured into 29 distinct lessons. It is coordinated by Professor Dr. Manish Chugh from the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS), ensuring expert guidance throughout the program. The learning experience is enriched by interactive sessions and opportunities for peer discussion.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is thoughtfully designed to provide a holistic understanding of economics. Key topics covered include:

*   **Introduction to Economics:** Understanding its scope and fundamental problems.
*   **Microeconomics:** Concepts like consumer equilibrium, demand and supply, production functions, cost of production, market forms, and price determination.
*   **Indian Economy:** An overview, economic planning, growth and development, and challenges such as unemployment, poverty, and inequality.
*   **Macroeconomics:** National income and its measurement, consumption, saving, investment, and the theory of income determination.
*   **Statistics for Economics:** Collection, classification, presentation of data, measures of central tendency, dispersion, correlation analysis, and index numbers.

## Duration and Credit Points

This Sr.Secondary: Economics (318) course is designed to be completed over a period of 24 weeks. Upon successful completion of all course modules and evaluations, students will earn 10 credit points. These credits signify the academic weight and learning hours associated with the program, which can be valuable for academic progression.

## Certification and Fees

Upon successfully fulfilling all course requirements, including assessments, learners will be awarded a Sr.Secondary: Economics (318) Certification. This certificate is jointly issued by the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) and Swayam, validating your achievement. While the course itself is generally free to access and learn, there might be a nominal fee associated with taking the final proctored examination if you wish to obtain the official certificate. Specific details regarding examination fees are typically available on the official Swayam platform.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Sr.Secondary: Economics (318) Certification Course on Swayam is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal for the course.
2.  Look for the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, usually located in the top right corner of the page.
3.  If you are a new user, you will need to register for an account using your email ID or existing social media profiles. If you already have an account, simply sign in.
4.  Once logged in, you can enroll in the Sr.Secondary: Economics (318) course.

This course offers an excellent opportunity to gain foundational knowledge in economics, preparing students for further studies or simply enhancing their understanding of how economies function.
