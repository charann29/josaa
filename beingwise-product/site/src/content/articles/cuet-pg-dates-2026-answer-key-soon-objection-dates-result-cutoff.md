# CUET PG Dates 2026: Answer Key Soon, Objection Dates, Result, Cutoff, Counselling Dates

The Common University Entrance Test for Post Graduate Admissions (CUET PG) is a crucial examination for students aspiring to pursue various master's degrees across central universities in India. With the 2026 examination period now concluded, attention turns to the upcoming stages of the admission process.

## Key Takeaways

*   The National Testing Agency (NTA) is expected to release the provisional CUET PG 2026 answer key and candidate response sheets shortly on its official website.
*   A window will be provided for candidates to raise objections against the provisional answer key, after which a final answer key will be published.
*   The CUET PG 2026 results will be declared based on the final answer key, followed by the release of individual scorecards.
*   Counselling processes for various postgraduate programs, including MSc, MBA, MA, M.Tech, MCA, and M.Com, will commence after the results are announced.

## CUET PG 2026 Answer Key and Objections

The NTA will first release a provisional answer key along with individual candidate response sheets. This allows students to review their marked answers against the officially provided solutions. Candidates can access these documents by logging into the official NTA website using their application ID and password. This provisional key is crucial for estimating scores before the official results are declared.

Should a candidate find any discrepancy or believe an answer in the provisional key is incorrect, they will be given a specific window to challenge it. The NTA will announce the last date for submitting these objections through an official notification. After reviewing all valid objections, the NTA will then prepare and release the final answer key, which will be used for calculating the official results.

### Calculating Your Estimated Score

To estimate your score using the provisional answer key, follow this marking scheme:

*   **Total Questions:** 75
*   **Maximum Marks:** 300
*   **Correct Answer:** +4 marks
*   **Incorrect Answer:** -1 mark
*   **Unattempted Answer:** 0 marks

## CUET PG 2026 Result and Scorecard

Once the final answer key is published, the NTA will proceed to declare the CUET PG 2026 results. These results will be based solely on the final answer key. Following the result declaration, individual scorecards will be made available for all candidates who appeared for the examination. These scorecards will contain important details such as the candidate's name, roll number, marks obtained, and qualifying status, essential for the subsequent counselling and admission processes.

## CUET PG 2026 Counselling and Cutoff

After the CUET PG 2026 results are out, participating universities will begin their respective counselling processes. Each university will release its own cutoff scores for various postgraduate programs. Candidates who meet these cutoff criteria will be eligible to participate in the counselling rounds. It's important for students to regularly check the official websites of their desired universities for specific counselling schedules, registration procedures, and required documents. The counselling typically involves online registration, choice filling, seat allocation, and document verification.

Keep an eye on the official NTA portal for all the latest updates regarding CUET PG 2026.
