Welcome to BeingWise's hub for the latest updates in Indian higher education. We bring you timely and accurate information on entrance exams, board results, and admissions processes to help you make informed decisions about your academic future.

## Key takeaways

*   **Board Results:** Stay informed about recent and upcoming results for CBSE 10th, TS Inter, and AP Inter examinations.
*   **Engineering Entrance Exams:** Get the latest on JEE Main answer keys, KCET admit cards, and MHT CET exam analysis.
*   **MBA Admissions:** Be aware of extended registration deadlines for exams like TANCET.
*   **Official Sources:** Always refer to official portals for the most reliable information on results, admit cards, and application processes.

## CBSE 10th Result 2026

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) is expected to announce the Class 10th results for 2026 soon. Students can check their results directly on the official CBSE website once they are released. Keep an eye on the official portal for the exact date and time of the announcement.

## Engineering Entrance Exam Updates

Several key engineering entrance exams have seen recent developments:

*   **JEE Main 2026:** The National Testing Agency (NTA) is anticipated to release the answer key and response sheet for JEE Main Session 2 shortly. Candidates will also have an opportunity to challenge the provisional answer key through the official JEE Main portal.
*   **KCET 2026:** Admit cards for the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) 2026 are now available for download. Students appearing for the exam should visit the official Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) website to access their hall tickets.
*   **MHT CET 2026:** The Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test (MHT CET) for PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) group recently concluded. Detailed analysis of the question paper for both shifts of the April 11th exam is now available, offering insights into difficulty levels and expected cut-offs.

## State Board Intermediate Results

Students awaiting their intermediate (1st and 2nd year) results from various state boards should note the following:

*   **TS Inter Results 2026:** The Telangana State Board of Intermediate Education (TGBIE) has confirmed the date and time for the release of 1st and 2nd year intermediate results. Students can check their scores on the official TGBIE website.
*   **AP Inter Results 2026:** The Board of Intermediate Education, Andhra Pradesh (BIEAP), will also be announcing its 1st and 2nd year intermediate results soon. Keep an eye on the official BIEAP portal for timely updates.

## TANCET 2026 Registration Extended

For those aspiring to pursue MBA through the Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test (TANCET) 2026, it's important to note that the registration deadline has been extended. Candidates who have not yet applied should visit the official TANCET website, managed by Anna University, to complete their application process before the new deadline.
