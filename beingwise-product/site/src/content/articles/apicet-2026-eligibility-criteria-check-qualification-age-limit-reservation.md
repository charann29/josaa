# APICET 2026 Eligibility Criteria: Check Qualification, Age Limit, Reservation

To pursue an MBA or MCA program at universities across Andhra Pradesh, aspiring students must clear the APICET exam. Fulfilling the APICET 2026 eligibility criteria is a mandatory first step before you can even apply for the test. This page will guide you through all the essential requirements.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Mandatory for Application:** Meeting the APICET 2026 eligibility criteria is crucial for submitting your application form; failure to do so will lead to disqualification.
*   **Academic Qualification:** A bachelor's degree of three or four years, or appearing for the final year of such a degree, is generally required.
*   **Minimum Marks:** General category candidates need at least 50% aggregate marks in their qualifying degree, while reserved categories require 45%.
*   **No Age Restriction:** There is no specified minimum or maximum age limit for candidates appearing for APICET.

## Understanding APICET Eligibility

APICET, or Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test, sets specific guidelines for who can appear for the exam and subsequently gain admission to MBA and MCA courses in participating institutions. These criteria cover aspects like nationality, educational background, and academic performance. The AP State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) oversees these requirements, and you can find the detailed information on their official portal, cets.apsche.ap.gov.in.

## Nationality and Age Requirements

To be eligible for APICET 2026, candidates must be Indian nationals. Beyond this, one of the key aspects of the APICET eligibility is its flexibility regarding age. Unlike many other entrance exams, there is **no minimum or maximum age limit** specified for candidates wishing to appear for the APICET exam. This means students of any age who meet the academic qualifications can apply.

## Educational Qualification and Marks

This is a critical component of the APICET eligibility. Candidates must possess a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. The degree should be either a three-year or four-year program. Crucially, even if you are in the final year of your degree program, you are eligible to apply, provided you can furnish proof of passing the qualifying examination at the time of admission.

Regarding academic performance, the minimum aggregate marks required in the qualifying degree vary by category:

*   **General Category:** Candidates must have secured at least 50% aggregate marks in their bachelor's degree.
*   **Reserved Categories:** Candidates belonging to reserved categories (as per government norms) need a minimum of 45% aggregate marks in their bachelor's degree.

It is important to note that specific university or college admission processes might have additional criteria, so always check the individual institution's requirements alongside the general APICET guidelines.

## Important Guidelines for Applicants

Before filling out the APICET 2026 application form, ensure you have thoroughly reviewed all the eligibility conditions. Any discrepancy or failure to meet the stated criteria, even after applying, can lead to your candidature being rejected at a later stage. Keep all your academic documents ready, as they will be required for verification during the counseling and admission process. The APSCHE portal is the definitive source for any updates or clarifications regarding eligibility.

## APICET 2026 Eligibility Overview

Here’s a quick summary of the key eligibility points:

| Feature             | Details                                                                  |
| :------------------ | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Name of Examination | Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test (APICET)                  |
| Nationality         | Indian                                                                   |
| Age Limit           | No minimum or maximum age limit                                          |
| Qualifying Degree   | Graduation (3 or 4 years) or appearing for final year examinations       |
| Minimum Marks (General) | 50% aggregate in qualifying degree                                       |
| Minimum Marks (Reserved) | 45% aggregate in qualifying degree                                       |

Meeting these eligibility criteria is your first step towards a successful application for APICET 2026 and opening doors to MBA and MCA programs in Andhra Pradesh.
