# Certificate Program in Big Data Technologies by 361Degree Minds Consulting Private Limited via TCS iON: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For IT professionals looking to enhance their skills in handling vast and complex datasets, the Certificate Program in Big Data Technologies offers a comprehensive online learning experience. This program, a joint effort by TCS iON and 361Degree Minds, is designed to equip learners with the foundational and advanced knowledge needed to excel in the field of big data.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a fully online, self-paced certificate program delivered through video and text-based content.
*   The course runs for 8 weeks and requires a commitment of 8-9 hours per week.
*   It's specifically designed for IT professionals with at least two years of work experience.
*   Successful completion leads to a certification from TCS iON and 361Degree Minds, and prepares you for the TCS iON Procert assessment.

## Understanding the Program

In today's data-driven world, the ability to extract, analyze, and process information from large, intricate datasets is crucial for organizational growth and informed decision-making. Big data technologies provide the tools to manage and make sense of structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data effectively. This certificate program guides learners through the core concepts and practical applications of these technologies.

The curriculum is structured to take participants from basic principles to advanced techniques, ensuring a thorough understanding of the software and methodologies used in big data analysis. The course content is mapped to the TCS iON Procert assessment, which can open doors to various opportunities in data analysis roles.

## Eligibility and Commitment

To enroll in this program, candidates must be IT professionals with a minimum of two years of relevant work experience. The course is tailored for individuals who already have a professional background in information technology and wish to specialize in big data. While the learning mode is self-study, consistent engagement is key.

The program spans 8 weeks, and learners are expected to dedicate approximately 8-9 hours per week to the course material, quizzes, and assessments. This commitment ensures that participants can absorb the concepts effectively and complete the program within the stipulated timeframe.

## Course Structure and Learning Experience

The Certificate Program in Big Data Technologies is delivered entirely online, making it flexible and convenient for working professionals. The learning materials include video demonstrations and text-based content, all provided in English. Throughout the 8-week duration, participants will engage with various modules, each followed by assessments and quizzes to reinforce learning.

A significant aspect of the program is its preparation for the TCS iON Procert assessment. The course content is specifically designed to align with the requirements of this professional assessment, aiming to enhance career prospects for certified data analysts.

## Program Fees and Certification

The fee for the Certificate Program in Big Data Technologies is ₹18,000, which includes GST. This amount is payable online during the registration process. Upon successful registration and payment, candidates will receive an activation code to access the course content.

Upon successful completion of all course requirements, including assessments, participants will receive a Certificate Program in Big Data Technologies certification. This certificate is jointly issued by TCS iON and 361Degree Minds Consulting Private Limited, acknowledging the learner's proficiency in big data technologies.
