# Data Engineering with Hadoop by Edupristine: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's data-driven world, the demand for skilled data engineers is soaring, and understanding powerful tools like Hadoop is essential. This program, offered by Edupristine, aims to equip IT and analytics professionals with the expertise needed to excel in this rapidly expanding field.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program offers 60 hours of instructor-led training, focusing on practical skills in the Hadoop ecosystem.
*   It covers a wide array of Big Data tools, including HDFS, MapReduce, Spark, Hive, and HBase, among others.
*   Participants will gain hands-on experience through real-time projects and case studies, culminating in a data engineering certification.
*   Career support, including job updates and interview preparation, is provided upon course completion.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This comprehensive training is designed to provide an in-depth understanding of data engineering, with a strong emphasis on Hadoop and its associated technologies. The curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with practical application, ensuring participants not only grasp concepts but also learn to implement them effectively.

The learning format includes virtual classroom sessions, video content, and text-based materials, supporting both self-study and instructor-led engagement. Throughout the program, expert instructors guide students from software installation to mastering complex functions, providing support every step of the way.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successfully completing this program, you will be proficient in several critical areas of data engineering. Key learning outcomes include:

*   A thorough understanding of Hadoop and its core components: Hadoop Distributed File System (HDFS), Hadoop MapReduce, and Hadoop YARN.
*   Proficiency in installing and configuring various Spark components like MySQL, Hive, Pig, and SparkR.
*   The ability to use over ten different Big Data tools for analyzing large datasets effectively.
*   Familiarity with essential concepts such as data setup, data flow, and ensuring data integrity within software systems.

## Program Highlights and Offerings

The Data Engineering with Hadoop program comes with several features designed to enhance the learning experience and career prospects of its participants. These include:

*   Sixty hours of intensive, instructor-led training.
*   Access to a dedicated Learning Management System (LMS) with comprehensive study notes.
*   Real-life case studies and simulations to apply learned concepts.
*   Hands-on experience with real-time cluster configuration and live projects.
*   Post-course engagement opportunities and dedicated discussion forums.
*   End-to-end career services, including job updates and interview preparation assistance.

## Eligibility and Target Audience

While there are no strict minimum eligibility criteria for enrolling in this program, it is primarily aimed at IT and analytics professionals. Given the specialized nature of data engineering, a foundational understanding of programming languages and their functionalities is expected to help participants get the most out of the course.

This program is particularly beneficial for individuals looking to advance their careers in roles such as:

*   Data Analyst
*   Big Data Developer
*   Big Data Analytics Engineer
*   Data Engineer
*   Data Architect

## Course Fee and Certification

Details regarding the specific course fee are typically available directly from Edupristine. Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a Data Engineering Certification, issued by Edupristine, acknowledging their acquired skills and knowledge in the field.

This program offers a robust pathway for professionals to gain vital skills in data engineering, positioning them for success in a competitive job market.
