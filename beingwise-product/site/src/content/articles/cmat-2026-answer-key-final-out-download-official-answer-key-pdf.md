# CMAT 2026 Answer Key (Final OUT): Download Official Answer Key PDF

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has officially released the final answer key for CMAT 2026. This crucial document, available in PDF format, helps candidates verify their responses and estimate their scores.

## Key Takeaways

*   The CMAT 2026 final answer key was published on January 31, 2026.
*   It includes revised answers based on expert review of challenges submitted by candidates.
*   Candidates can download their answer key and response sheet from the official NTA-CMAT website using their login credentials.
*   The objection window for the provisional answer key was open from January 31 to February 2, 2026, with a fee of INR 200 per challenged question.

## How to Download the CMAT 2026 Answer Key

Accessing your CMAT 2026 answer key and individual response sheet is a straightforward process. Follow these steps on the official NTA-CMAT portal:

1.  Visit the official NTA-CMAT website.
2.  Look for the link titled 'CMAT 2026 Answer Key' and click on it.
3.  You will be prompted to log in using your CMAT Application Number and Password.
4.  Once logged in, your CMAT 2026 answer key and response sheet will be displayed.
5.  Download the PDF files to your device for future reference.

## Understanding the Answer Key Challenge Process

Before the final answer key was released, NTA provided an opportunity for candidates to challenge the provisional answer key. Here’s how that process worked:

1.  Candidates logged into the official NTA-CMAT 2026 website.
2.  They selected the 'Answer Key Challenge for CMAT 2026' option.
3.  After logging in with their application number, password, and security pin, they could view the questions and NTA's provisional correct answers.
4.  To challenge a question, candidates selected the relevant option ID(s).
5.  Supporting documents for challenges could be uploaded as a single PDF file.
6.  A fee of INR 200 per challenged question was payable online via Debit/Credit Card or Net Banking.

An expert panel reviewed all submitted objections. If any objections were found to be valid, the provisional answer key was revised, leading to the final answer key now available.

## Calculating Your CMAT Score and Percentile

With the final answer key in hand, you can now accurately calculate your raw score. Each correct answer typically fetches 4 marks, while incorrect answers result in a deduction of 1 mark. Unattempted questions carry no penalty.

Your total raw score is then used to determine your percentile. The percentile indicates the percentage of candidates who scored less than or equal to you. This percentile is crucial for shortlisting by various B-schools for their admission processes.

Reviewing the final answer key is a vital step in your CMAT journey, providing clarity on your performance.
