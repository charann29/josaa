# Post Graduate Program in Full-stack Development by Careerera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aiming to become skilled full-stack developers, the Post Graduate Program in Full-stack Development by Careerera offers comprehensive training designed to equip you with essential industry knowledge and practical experience.

## Key Takeaways

*   This 12-month program focuses on both front-end and back-end development, covering technologies like Java, JavaScript, and SQL.
*   Learning is delivered through a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content, all in English.
*   Successful completion leads to a Careerera certification, and job assistance is provided.
*   The program emphasizes hands-on learning through projects, assignments, and lab sessions.

## Program Overview

The Post Graduate Program in Full-stack Development is crafted to build well-rounded full-stack competencies. It delves into various technologies crucial for modern application development, preparing students to handle both the user-facing side (front-end) and the server-side operations (back-end). The curriculum goes beyond just coding, highlighting the broader skills a full-stack developer needs, including project management, cloud development, database management, and quality assurance.

The program's intensive approach ensures that participants not only learn theoretical concepts but also gain practical experience by working on real-world industrial projects and lab sessions. This hands-on methodology aims to solidify understanding and develop practical application skills.

## Who Is This Program For?

This Post Graduate Program is particularly well-suited for a range of professionals and aspiring developers looking to enhance their skills or transition into full-stack roles. It's ideal for:

*   Existing Information Technology Professionals
*   Software Developers
*   Data Analysts
*   Engineers aspiring to become Full-Stack Developers, Frontend Developers, Backend Developers, or UI Designers.

Upon completion, participants will have a strong grasp of UI development, front-end and back-end development, and the entire spectrum of technologies involved in software creation.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for this program, applicants must meet the following requirements:

*   Possess a Bachelor’s degree in engineering with a minimum of 50% marks.
*   Have a basic understanding of programming concepts.

To earn the official Careerera certification, candidates must successfully complete a dedicated Careerera Certification Exam after finishing the course modules.

## Course Fees and Duration

The Post Graduate Program in Full-stack Development is a 12-month program. The fee for the e-learning format is $799. This fee covers all course materials, access to virtual classrooms, and other program offerings.

## How to Apply

Admission to the Post Graduate Program in Full-stack Development follows a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official Careerera website for the Post Graduate Program in Full-stack Development.
2.  Browse the 'Upcoming Batches' section and select your preferred batch.
3.  Initiate the registration by clicking the 'Enroll Now' button.
4.  Complete the application form with your details and submit any required documentation.
5.  Candidates will undergo an interview, and selections will be based on interview performance.
6.  Upon selection, pay the course fee to finalize your enrollment and begin your learning journey.

This program is designed to provide you with the skills and certification needed to thrive in the dynamic field of full-stack development.
