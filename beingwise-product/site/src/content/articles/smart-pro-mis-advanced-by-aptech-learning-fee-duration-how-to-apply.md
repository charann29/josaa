# Smart Pro MIS Advanced by Aptech Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

The Smart Pro MIS Advanced program from Aptech Learning offers a focused pathway for individuals looking to build strong skills in Management Information Systems. This career-oriented course combines essential accounting knowledge with practical programming and office automation expertise.

## Key Takeaways

*   This 10-month program is designed for IT graduates, undergraduates, and working professionals.
*   It covers vital skills like advanced Excel, MS Access, programming principles, and computerised financial accounting.
*   Learning is flexible, combining classroom and online resources, including the Onlinevarsity app.
*   The course aims to prepare students for roles such as MIS professional, VBA programmer, and Tally specialist.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Smart Pro MIS Advanced program is structured to provide comprehensive training in areas critical for an MIS professional. It introduces students to both fundamental and advanced concepts in accounting and programming. Key areas of study include office automation tools, in-depth Excel for financial analysis, MS Access for database management, and core programming principles.

The program spans 10 months, typically requiring students to attend classes for two hours a day, three days a week. This schedule allows for consistent learning without overwhelming commitments. Students also benefit from Onlinevarsity, Aptech's learning application, which offers free access to study materials. Additionally, an online learning forum connects students with industry experts, providing a valuable resource for networking and continuous learning.

## Eligibility Criteria

Aptech Learning's Smart Pro MIS Advanced program is primarily designed for individuals with an Information Technology (IT) background. This includes:

*   IT graduates
*   IT undergraduates
*   Working professionals in the IT field

These criteria ensure that participants have a foundational understanding that allows them to fully benefit from the program's advanced topics.

## What You Will Learn

Upon successful completion of the Smart Pro MIS Advanced course, participants will gain a robust set of skills highly valued in the industry. These include:

*   **Data Analysis and Presentation:** Proficiency in creating spreadsheets, documents, and presentations to effectively analyse and present large datasets.
*   **Advanced Excel:** Expertise in designing sophisticated spreadsheets and performing financial analysis using Microsoft Excel.
*   **Database Management:** Ability to normalise raw data and create well-structured, organised database tables using MS Access.
*   **Web Productivity:** Familiarity with various web productivity tools to enhance task management and communication efficiency.
*   **Core MIS Concepts:** A solid understanding of advanced presentation techniques and essential MS Access functionalities.

These skills are geared towards developing a well-rounded MIS professional capable of handling diverse data and system management tasks.

## Fees and Financial Assistance

The fee for the Smart Pro MIS Advanced program varies by centre location. The general fee range is approximately ₹17,300 to ₹23,600, plus applicable taxes. It's important to note that these figures are indicative and candidates should confirm the exact amount with their chosen Aptech Learning centre.

For students who may require financial support, student loan facilities are available. This provision helps ensure that financial constraints do not become a barrier to accessing this valuable education.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Smart Pro MIS Advanced program involves a straightforward process:

1.  **Visit the Course Page:** Navigate to the official Aptech Learning website and locate the Smart Pro MIS Advanced course page.
2.  **Explore Finance & Accounting:** Within the courses section, find and select the 'finance and accounting' category.
3.  **Fill the Enquiry Form:** On the course page, you will find a link or option to 'Fill the enquiry form'. Complete this form with your details.

Following these steps will initiate your application process, and Aptech Learning will typically get in touch to guide you through the next stages, which may include a free online counselling session.

This program is an excellent opportunity for IT professionals to enhance their skill set and advance their careers in the dynamic field of Management Information Systems.
