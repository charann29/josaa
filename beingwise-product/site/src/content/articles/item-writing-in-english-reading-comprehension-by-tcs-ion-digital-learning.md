# Item Writing in English Reading Comprehension by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those passionate about English language education and assessment, the 'Item Writing in English Reading Comprehension' certification program by TCS iON Digital Learning Hub offers a unique opportunity. This online course is designed to equip individuals with the skills needed to create effective English language assessments, particularly focusing on reading comprehension.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a fully online, self-paced program with virtual classroom components, delivered in English.
*   It awards 4 academic credits and includes mentorship, industry exposure, and access to an academic community.
*   Upon successful completion and passing the summative assessments, participants receive a digital certificate from TCS iON.
*   The total fee for the program, including GST, is ₹11,800.

## Course Overview and Objectives

This certification program aims to enhance participants' English language skills and develop their ability to design robust assessments for various levels of English language testing. It focuses specifically on strategies for evaluating an individual's reading competency. Through a blend of lectures, tutorials, and experimental learning, the course helps learners understand the nuances of creating effective reading comprehension tests.

By the end of the program, participants should be proficient in drafting tests that accurately analyze language skills in English. They will also gain the ability to structure different levels of tests and identify characteristics of strong readers, ultimately preparing them to create comprehensive and fair assessments.

## Who Is This Program For?

This course is particularly well-suited for graduates in arts and education, as well as anyone aspiring to build a career as an English language teacher or an item writer specializing in reading comprehension. If you have a strong command of the English language and are interested in the methodology of language assessment, this program could be a valuable step for your professional development.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The 'Item Writing in English Reading Comprehension' program is delivered entirely online, allowing for flexible learning. It incorporates a variety of resources and learning methodologies:

*   **Learning Mode:** Self-study combined with virtual classroom sessions.
*   **Delivery:** Content is provided through video and text-based formats.
*   **Resources:** Participants get access to digital learning materials, online lectures, and practice assessments.
*   **Support:** The program includes mentorship, opportunities for academic networking, and industry-related assignments.
*   **Assessment:** Both practice and summative assessments are part of the curriculum.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the 'Item Writing in English Reading Comprehension' certification program is **₹11,800**, which includes GST. This payment provides full access to the course curriculum and all its offerings. The fee is typically paid online during the application process.

Upon successfully completing the course study and clearing the summative assessments, participants will receive an official E-Certificate of Achievement from TCS iON. Those who complete the course but do not take the summative assessment will receive a Certificate of Participation.

## How to Apply

The application process for the 'Item Writing in English Reading Comprehension' certification is straightforward and entirely online. You will need to visit the official TCS iON website to register. Look for the specific course page and follow the instructions to activate your enrollment and create a course account.

This program offers a valuable credential for those looking to enhance their expertise in English language assessment and assessment design, provided by a recognized name in digital learning.
