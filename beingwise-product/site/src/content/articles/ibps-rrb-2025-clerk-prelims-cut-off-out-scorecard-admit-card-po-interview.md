# IBPS RRB 2025: Clerk Prelims Cut off (OUT), Scorecard, Admit Card, PO Interview Call Letter OUT & Exam Date

For those aiming for a career in India's Regional Rural Banks, the Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) conducts the IBPS RRB exam annually. This page provides the latest updates regarding the 2025 examinations, including result announcements, cut-offs, and upcoming schedules.

## Key takeaways

*   The IBPS RRB Clerk 2025 Prelims results were announced on January 23, with the exam held across various dates in December 2025.
*   Both the Clerk Prelims and PO Mains scorecards and cut-offs for 2025 were released in late January.
*   Interview call letters for qualified candidates for IBPS RRB PO and SO positions have been issued.
*   The IBPS RRB Clerk Mains exam for 2025 took place on February 1, 2026, and its results are expected in the third week of February 2026.

## Understanding the IBPS RRB Exam

The IBPS RRB exam is a national-level recruitment process designed to select candidates for various positions within Regional Rural Banks across India. These roles include Office Assistants (Clerk), Officer Scale I (PO), Officer Scale II, and Officer Scale III. The selection process typically involves preliminary and main examinations, followed by an interview for Officer roles.

## Recent Updates: Results and Cut-offs

The IBPS has been actively releasing results and associated details for the 2025 cycle. The preliminary exam results for IBPS RRB Clerk 2025 were declared on January 23. Following this, the official cut-off scores and individual scorecards for the Clerk Prelims were made available on January 29. Similarly, the cut-offs and scorecards for the IBPS RRB PO 2025 Mains exam were released on January 30.

For Officers Scale I, II, and III, the results for the mains and single online exams were announced on January 21. Candidates who have qualified for the Officer Scale I, II, and III positions have also received their interview call letters, marking the next crucial step in their selection journey.
