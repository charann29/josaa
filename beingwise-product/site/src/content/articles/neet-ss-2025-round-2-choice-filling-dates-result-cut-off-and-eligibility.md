# NEET SS 2025: Round 2 Choice Filling, Dates, Result, Cut Off & Eligibility

For medical graduates aspiring to pursue super speciality courses in India, the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test Super Speciality (NEET SS) is a crucial examination. This page provides essential information regarding the NEET SS 2025 counselling process, particularly focusing on Round 2 choice filling, important dates, results, and eligibility criteria.

## Key takeaways

*   NEET SS 2025 Round 2 choice filling dates have been extended; candidates should regularly check the official MCC portal for updates.
*   The Round 2 seat allotment result is expected to be announced soon by the Medical Counselling Committee (MCC).
*   NEET SS 2025 counselling involves online registration, choice filling, and seat allotment across multiple rounds.
*   The NEET SS examination was conducted on December 26 and 27, 2025, with results declared on January 23, 2026.

## NEET SS 2025 Counselling Process

The NEET SS 2025 counselling is being conducted entirely online by the Medical Counselling Committee (MCC). The process includes several stages: registration, choice filling and locking, seat allotment, and reporting to the allotted institutions. While Round 2 registrations have concluded, the choice filling window has been extended, giving candidates more time to finalize their preferences. The MCC has also released the seat matrix for Round 2, detailing both virtual and clear vacancies, along with any newly added seats.

Candidates must meticulously fill and lock their choices of super speciality courses and institutions. Based on these choices, their NEET SS rank, and seat availability, the MCC allocates seats. After seat allotment, candidates need to report to their assigned college, complete the admission formalities, and pay the requisite fees. There are typically three rounds of counselling, but additional rounds may be conducted if seats remain vacant.

## Checking NEET SS Seat Matrix and Allotment Results

The NEET SS seat matrix for each round is released on the official MCC website. This document is crucial for candidates to understand the available seats across various institutes and specialities. To check the Round 2 seat matrix, candidates should visit mcc.nic.in, navigate to the Super Speciality section, and look for the relevant seat matrix links for MD, MCh, or DrNB courses.

Once the seat allotment results are declared, candidates can check their status by logging into the official MCC portal. The result typically includes details of the allotted institute and course. If a candidate is satisfied with their allotted seat, they must download the allotment letter and proceed with reporting to the institute within the stipulated timeframe. Those wishing for an upgrade can opt for it in subsequent rounds.

## NEET SS 2025 Eligibility Criteria and Cut Off

To be eligible for NEET SS, candidates must possess a recognized postgraduate medical degree (MD/MS/DNB) or an equivalent qualification. They must also have completed their compulsory rotating internship. The National Board of Examinations (NBE) releases the NEET SS cut-off marks along with the results. This cut-off represents the minimum qualifying score required for candidates to be eligible for counselling. The cut-off varies based on the speciality group and is a critical factor in determining a candidate's chances of securing a seat.

The NEET SS 2025 results were announced on January 23, 2026, following the examination held on December 26 and 27, 2025. Along with the results, the NBE also published the group-wise cut-off marks and merit list.

For the latest updates and detailed information, always refer to the official MCC and NBE websites.
