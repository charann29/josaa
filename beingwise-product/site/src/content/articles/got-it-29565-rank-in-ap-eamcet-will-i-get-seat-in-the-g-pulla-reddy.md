This means that the cut-off rank for a BC-B girl could be different, and often more favorable, than the general merit cut-off for the same branch.

## The Role of Counselling Rounds

AP EAMCET counselling typically involves multiple rounds. The first round often sees candidates with very high ranks securing their preferred seats. As these seats are filled, or if some candidates choose not to accept their allotted seats, vacancies arise for subsequent rounds. This creates opportunities for candidates with slightly lower ranks.

*   **Second and Third Rounds:** These rounds are particularly important for you. Seats that remain vacant after the first round, or those released by candidates who got better options elsewhere, become available. This can lead to a slight drop in cut-off ranks, making admission possible for candidates like yourself who might be on the borderline in earlier rounds. It's crucial to participate actively and be prepared to make informed choices in these later rounds.
