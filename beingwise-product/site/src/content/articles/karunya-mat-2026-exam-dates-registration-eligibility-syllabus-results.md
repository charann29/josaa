# Karunya MAT 2026 Exam: Dates, Registration, Eligibility, Syllabus, Results

Karunya Management Aptitude Test (KMAT) is a university-level entrance examination conducted by Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences for admission to its MBA program. This exam is offered in multiple phases throughout the year, providing flexibility for aspiring students.

## Key takeaways

*   Karunya MAT 2026 is an online, remote-proctored exam for MBA admissions at Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences.
*   The exam is structured with four sections, totaling 100 marks, and assesses skills similar to other national-level MBA entrance tests.
*   Candidates can choose their preferred exam slot and date when filling out the application form.
*   Apart from KMAT, the institute also accepts scores from other national-level MBA entrance examinations for admission.

## Karunya MAT 2026 Dates and Schedule

The Karunya MAT is conducted in several phases, allowing candidates to pick a convenient time. The application process requires candidates to select their desired exam slot and date during registration.

Here's a look at the schedule for Karunya MAT 2026:

| Event                          | Dates                      |
| :----------------------------- | :------------------------- |
| Phase 1 Exam Date              | January 10 - 11, 2026      |
| Phase 2 Exam Date              | March 10 - 12, 2026        |
| Phase 3 Exam Date              | May 9 - 10, 2026           |
| Phase 4 Exam Date              | June 13, 2026              |
| Phase 5 Exam Date              | July 25 - 26, 2026         |

## Karunya MAT 2026 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for Karunya MAT 2026, candidates must meet specific academic requirements. Both Indian nationals and NRI/foreign candidates are welcome to apply.

*   Applicants must hold an undergraduate degree from a recognized institution.
*   Students awaiting their final year results are also eligible to apply. However, any admission offered will be provisional, pending the submission of their passing degree certificates.

## Karunya MAT 2026 Application Process

Interested candidates must complete the Karunya MAT application form to register for the online examination. During this process, applicants will need to select their preferred exam slot and date from the available options. Detailed instructions for the application can be found on the official Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences admissions portal.

## Karunya MAT 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The Karunya MAT is a 2-hour, objective-type examination conducted in English. It is an online, remote-proctored test designed to assess various aptitude areas essential for management studies.

The exam is divided into four sections, with a total of 100 marks:

*   **Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension:** 30 questions
*   **Data Interpretation and Logical Reasoning:** 20 questions
*   **Quantitative Aptitude:** 20 questions
*   **Indian and Global Business Environment:** 30 questions

The syllabus covers topics typically found in MBA entrance exams, with an additional focus on general business awareness, reflecting the institute's comprehensive approach to management education.

## Karunya MAT 2026 Results

Following the examination, Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences will announce the results for Karunya MAT 2026. Candidates can typically check their results on the official admissions portal by logging in with their credentials. Further details regarding result declaration dates and the subsequent admission process will be communicated by the institute.

For the most current information and official announcements, always refer to the official Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences admissions portal.
