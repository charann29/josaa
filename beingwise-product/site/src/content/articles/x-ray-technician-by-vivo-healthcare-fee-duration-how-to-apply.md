# X-Ray Technician by VIVO Healthcare: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Becoming an X-Ray technician is a vital role in modern healthcare, helping doctors diagnose conditions through imaging. VIVO Healthcare offers a comprehensive program designed to equip students with the necessary skills to excel in this field.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is a 12-month, full-time course combining theoretical knowledge and extensive practical training.
*   It includes 600 hours of classroom and hands-on learning, followed by a mandatory 1000-hour hospital internship.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from VIVO Healthcare, with the potential for National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) certification.
*   The course is delivered offline with instructor-led training and includes placement assistance and education loan options.

## Understanding the X-Ray Technician Program

The X-Ray Technician program by VIVO Healthcare is crafted to develop proficient X-Ray professionals. The curriculum focuses on teaching you how to operate X-Ray equipment safely and effectively, process exposed radiographs, maintain patient records, and ensure equipment functionality. Throughout the program, you'll gain expertise in patient care during the imaging process, aiming for clear diagnostic results.

## Course Structure and Duration

This is a 12-month program with a significant emphasis on practical experience. The training is structured as follows:

*   **Theory and Practical Training:** 600 hours of combined classroom instruction and hands-on practice.
*   **Mandatory Hospital Internship:** 1000 hours of supervised clinical experience in a hospital setting.

In total, the program encompasses 1600 hours of dedicated learning and practical application. The medium of instruction for the entire course is English.

## Eligibility and Assessment

To be eligible for the X-Ray Technician program, candidates must have completed their 10+2 education in any stream. Admission to the clinical internship requires a minimum of 70% marks in the pre-internship examination.

Throughout the program, students will undergo various assessments to monitor their progress:

*   Weekly assessments
*   Module-based assessments
*   Pre-internship examination
*   Final foundation examination

To qualify for certification, students must maintain at least 70% attendance in classes and 85% attendance during the clinical internship. Successful completion of all examinations and full payment of course fees are also prerequisites for receiving the certificate.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to impart a broad range of skills and knowledge essential for an X-Ray technician. Upon completing the course, you will be able to:

*   Operate X-Ray machinery and prepare patients for procedures.
*   Provide high-quality patient care throughout the X-Ray process.
*   Assess X-Ray films and collaborate with physicians for diagnosis.
*   Maintain accurate records of X-Ray films and associated cassettes.
*   Evaluate equipment settings, such as range and angle, to ensure optimal imaging results.
*   Oversee equipment cleanliness and functionality for accurate diagnoses.

## How to Apply

Applying for the X-Ray Technician program through VIVO Healthcare involves a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official VIVO Healthcare website and navigate to the X-Ray Technician course page.
2.  Locate and click the 'Apply Now' button, which will take you to a registration or sign-in page.
3.  If you have an existing account, log in. Otherwise, create a new account by providing basic details and registering.
4.  An application form will appear after successful registration. Fill in all required information, including course details, personal information, and preferred location, then submit the form.
5.  Complete the enrollment by paying the specified course fee.

Upon successful completion of the application and fee payment, you will be enrolled in the program. The application form will require details such as your full name, contact information, residential address, and educational background.
