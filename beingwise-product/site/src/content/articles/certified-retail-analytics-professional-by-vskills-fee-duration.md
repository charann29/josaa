# Certified Retail Analytics Professional by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

In today's competitive market, understanding retail analytics is crucial for business growth and efficient inventory management. The Vskills Certified Retail Analytics Professional program is designed to equip you with the essential skills to excel in this dynamic field.

## Key takeaways

*   Gain a government-recognised certification, enhancing your professional profile.
*   Enjoy lifelong access to study materials and practice tests for continuous learning.
*   Benefit from resume assistance and potential internship opportunities to kickstart your career.
*   The certification is valid for life, offering enduring professional recognition.

## Course Overview

The Certified Retail Analytics Professional course from Vskills is a self-paced, online program delivered through video and text-based modules in English. It provides a comprehensive curriculum covering vital topics such as advanced Excel formulas, spreadsheet modelling, problem-solving, decision-making, distribution fitting, and data preparation. By completing this program, you will develop a strong foundation in the core concepts and practical skills needed for successful retail operations and analytics.

## Who Can Benefit?

This certification is ideal for a wide range of individuals looking to enhance their expertise in retail analytics. Retail professionals, consultants, and managers can leverage this course to refine their advanced analytical skills. Additionally, recent graduates or anyone with an interest in retail management and operations will find this program highly beneficial for improving their career prospects and understanding the intricacies of modern retail.

## What You Will Learn

The program focuses on developing both statistical prowess and practical application skills, particularly with MS-Excel. Upon successful completion, you will be proficient in:

*   **Retail Management:** Understanding core principles and strategies.
*   **SKU Optimization:** Techniques for efficient stock-keeping unit management.
*   **Market Basket Analysis:** Identifying purchasing patterns and relationships.
*   **MS-Excel for Analytics:** Applying Excel tools for data analysis.
*   **Category Management:** Strategies for managing product categories effectively.
*   **Basics of Analytics and Statistics:** Foundational knowledge for data-driven decisions.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Certified Retail Analytics Professional program is ₹3,499. This amount includes both the course fee and applicable GST. Vskills accepts payments through various convenient methods, including net banking, credit cards, and debit cards.

Upon passing the certification exam, you will receive a government-recognised certificate from Vskills. To qualify for the certification, you must achieve a minimum score of 50% on the exam. Vskills provides extensive preparatory materials, including tutorials and practice tests, accessible through your Learning Management System (LMS) to help you prepare effectively.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Certified Retail Analytics Professional course is a straightforward online process:

1.  Visit the official Vskills website for certifications.
2.  Navigate to the course page for "Certified Retail Analytics Professional."
3.  Click on the 'Buy Now' button.
4.  You will be prompted to register with Vskills by providing your contact information.
5.  Proceed to pay the course fee using your preferred payment method.
6.  Once the payment is confirmed, Vskills will send your LMS login credentials via email, allowing you to begin your learning journey immediately.

This certification offers a valuable opportunity to gain expertise in a rapidly evolving sector, providing you with a strong foundation for a successful career in retail analytics.
