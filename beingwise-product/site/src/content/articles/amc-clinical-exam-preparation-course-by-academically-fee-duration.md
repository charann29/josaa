# AMC Clinical Exam Preparation Course by Academically: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For international medical graduates aiming to practice in Australia, the AMC Clinical Exam is a crucial step. This preparation course from Academically is designed to equip you with the essential clinical and communication skills needed to succeed.

## Key takeaways

*   A comprehensive four-month program covering critical medical knowledge and practical skills.
*   Features over 100 hours of live Zoom sessions, recorded lectures, and AI-driven mock tests.
*   Offers one-on-one feedback from expert faculty to refine your approach.
*   Specifically tailored for International Medical Graduates (IMGs) aspiring to settle and practice in Australia.

## Course Overview and Structure

This preparation course is a focused, four-month program delivered through a blend of self-study and virtual classroom learning. It utilizes video and text-based content, all taught in English. The curriculum systematically covers clinical skills, medical knowledge, and effective communication, which are vital for the AMC Clinical Exam.

It is specifically designed for international medical graduates who aim for postgraduate opportunities and eventual settlement in Australia.

## What You Will Learn

The program focuses on developing a strong foundation in clinical practice and communication. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Patient History and Examination:** Mastering techniques for efficient and empathetic patient interviews and refining physical examination skills.
*   **Clinical Reasoning and Management:** Learning to develop and implement comprehensive treatment plans that align with current best practices and AMC standards.
*   **Effective Communication:** Enhancing your ability to convey information clearly and compassionately to patients and healthcare teams.
*   **Medical Knowledge:** Strengthening your understanding of medical sciences, pathobiology, pathophysiology, and current management strategies across various specialities.

### Course Modules

The curriculum is structured into several modules to ensure a thorough preparation:

*   **Module 1: Adult Health** (Medicine and Surgery)
    *   Medical sciences
    *   Pathobiology and pathophysiology
    *   Practised clinical skills
    *   Principles of treatment and current management strategies
*   **Module 2: Women’s Health**
*   **Module 3: Child Health**
*   **Module 4: Mental Health**
*   **Module 5: Population Health and Ethics**
*   **Module 6: Clinical Scenarios**

## Course Highlights and Features

The course offers a robust learning experience through various features:

*   **Duration:** 4 months of intensive preparation.
*   **Live and Recorded Sessions:** Over 100 hours of live Zoom sessions complemented by recorded lectures for flexible learning.
*   **Expert Faculty:** Guidance from experienced educators.
*   **Personalized Feedback:** One-on-one feedback sessions to address individual strengths and areas for improvement.
*   **Assessment:** AI-driven mock tests and a final MCQ-based exam to gauge readiness.
*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** Covering all essential topics for the AMC Clinical Exam.

## Fees and How to Apply

The fees for the AMC Clinical Examination itself are AUD 4,391. This preparation course is designed to help you prepare for this significant examination.

To apply for the Academically AMC Clinical Exam Preparation Course:

1.  Visit the official Academically portal for the AMC Clinical Exam Preparation Course.
2.  Click on the 'Apply Now' option.
3.  Submit all the required personal and academic information.
4.  Once your enrollment is complete, you will gain access to the course materials and sessions.

## Who Is This Course For?

This course is primarily for international medical graduates (IMGs) who aspire to pursue postgraduate medical careers and settle in Australia. While broad in its appeal, it is particularly beneficial for clinicians, clinical research coordinators, nurses, and health inspectors looking to meet Australian medical registration standards.

This program offers a structured pathway to help you confidently approach the AMC Clinical Exam and achieve your professional goals in Australia.
