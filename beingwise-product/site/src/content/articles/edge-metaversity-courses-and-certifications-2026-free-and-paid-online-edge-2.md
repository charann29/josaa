# EDGE Metaversity Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online EDGE Metaversity Courses

EDGE Metaversity offers a range of specialized certificate programs designed to equip students with in-demand skills in creative technology fields like animation, virtual production, visual effects, and game development. These programs are structured to provide practical, industry-relevant training for aspiring professionals.

## Key takeaways

*   EDGE Metaversity focuses on immersive, professional certificate courses in cutting-edge creative technologies.
*   Programs are generally 9-11 months long and delivered in an offline, in-person format at well-equipped campuses.
*   No prior experience in design, CG, or VFX is typically required, making these courses accessible to passionate beginners.
*   Courses cover industry-standard software like Unreal Engine, Autodesk Maya, NukeX, and Houdini.

## Master Certificate in Unreal Engine Virtual Production

This 9-month (300-hour) master certificate program is conducted offline, offering comprehensive training in virtual production using Unreal Engine. It's ideal for individuals passionate about visual storytelling and integrating special effects, even without a background in game design, computer graphics, or visual effects.

The curriculum progresses from foundational concepts such as computer graphics, narrative techniques, and camera operation to advanced topics like sophisticated computer graphics and advanced compositing. A key outcome of this course is the creation of a portfolio film, allowing students to showcase their proficiency in virtual production to potential employers.

## Master Certificate in Animation with Autodesk Maya

This 9-month offline course, affiliated with Trazos (a highly-ranked animation school in Spain), focuses on mastering animation using Autodesk Maya. It's designed for those aiming for a successful career in the rapidly expanding animation industry.

With the increasing demand for computer-generated imagery and animation across television, films, games, and virtual reality, this program helps students develop high productivity and expertise in Maya software. The course aims to equip learners with essential animation skills, preparing them for the demands of the modern animation landscape. The fee for this course is ₹2,97,050.

## Master Certificate in VFX Compositing with NukeX

Another 9-month offline master certificate program, this course is also affiliated with Trazos and is delivered at Pearl campuses. It's specifically tailored for aspiring professionals in visual effects, focusing on compositing using NukeX technology.

NukeX is crucial for creating the stunning environments and backgrounds seen in major action films. This program ensures candidates gain a deep understanding of VFX animation technologies, enabling them to build a strong career in this specialized field. The fee for this course is ₹2,97,050.

## Master Certificate in VFX with Houdini

This 9-month professional certificate course, offered offline by EDGE Metaversity, focuses on visual effects creation using SideFX Houdini. Houdini is widely recognized and sought after for producing compelling visual effects in both domestic and international films and television series.

The training in this program empowers participants to create breathtaking effects, similar to those seen in popular TV series and movies, directly addressing the industry's need for skilled Houdini artists. The fee for this course is ₹2,97,050.

EDGE Metaversity's offerings provide a structured pathway for creative individuals to gain specialized skills and build professional portfolios in dynamic digital fields.
