# CUET-PG MBA Answer Key 2026, OMR Sheet: Download Here

For students aspiring to pursue an MBA through the CUET-PG exam, understanding how to access and utilize the answer key and response sheet is crucial. This guide provides essential information on downloading these documents and the process for challenging provisional answers.

## Key Takeaways

*   The National Testing Agency (NTA) is expected to release the provisional CUET-PG answer key around April 10, 2026, for all 157 subjects, including MBA.
*   Candidates can challenge incorrect answers in the provisional key by paying a fee of INR 200 per objection within a specified window.
*   The NTA will release a final answer key after reviewing objections, which will be the basis for calculating final scores and results.
*   Candidates can also view their individual response sheets and the question paper they attempted on the NTA website.

## Understanding the CUET-PG Answer Key Release

The National Testing Agency (NTA) manages the release of the CUET-PG answer keys. Initially, a provisional answer key is published online for all candidates who appeared for the Computer-Based Test (CBT). This allows students to cross-reference their attempted answers with the officially released key and estimate their scores. Following a period for objections, the NTA will then issue a final answer key, which is definitive and used for result compilation.

## How to Download Your CUET-PG 2026 Answer Key

Accessing your answer key is a straightforward process. Both the provisional and final answer keys will be available exclusively on the official NTA CUET-PG website. Here’s how you can download it:

1.  Visit the official NTA CUET-PG website (exams.nta.nic.in/CUET-PG/).
2.  Look for the 'Answer Key' link on the homepage and click it.
3.  You will be prompted to log in using your application number and date of birth.
4.  Enter the security pin displayed on the screen and click 'Submit'.
5.  Your CUET-PG 2026 answer key will be displayed. You can then check and download it for your records.

While the provisional key is available only to those who took the exam, the final answer key will generally be accessible to everyone.

## Understanding Your CUET-PG 2026 Response Sheet

Before the official results are declared, the NTA provides candidates with an opportunity to view their individual response sheets. This sheet displays the answers you marked during the examination, alongside the question paper you attempted. This display typically remains active on the NTA website for a short period, usually two to three days. It's an invaluable tool for candidates to verify their responses against the provisional answer key and accurately calculate their probable scores.

## Challenging the CUET-PG 2026 Provisional Answer Key

If you believe there is an error in the provisional answer key, the NTA provides a window to raise objections. This process is critical for ensuring fairness and accuracy in the final results. Here’s what you need to know:

*   **Online Challenge:** Challenges must be submitted online through the designated link on the official NTA website.
*   **Objection Fee:** A fee of INR 200 per question challenged is required. This fee is typically non-refundable unless the challenge is found to be valid.
*   **Justification:** Challenges must be accompanied by appropriate justification or evidence to support your claim.
*   **Deadline:** Only challenges submitted within the stipulated timeframe and with the required fee will be considered by the NTA.

After the objection window closes, the NTA's subject matter experts will review all submitted challenges. Based on their assessment, they will then prepare and release the final answer key.
