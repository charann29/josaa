# TANCET 2026 Last Date to Apply (April 15), Exam Date (May 9), Syllabus PDF, Pattern & Eligibility

TANCET (Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test) is a state-level examination conducted by Anna University for admission to MBA and MCA programs across various colleges in Tamil Nadu. Over 250 institutions accept TANCET scores for their postgraduate management and computer application courses.

## Key Takeaways

*   The application deadline for TANCET 2026 has been extended to April 15. Make sure to complete your registration by this date.
*   The TANCET 2026 examination for both MBA and MCA courses is scheduled for May 9.
*   Anna University conducts the online registration process on its official website, tancet.annauniv.edu.
*   Candidates with a bachelor's degree meeting specific percentage criteria are eligible to apply.

## TANCET 2026 Important Dates

Staying informed about key dates is crucial for a smooth application process. Here are the essential dates for TANCET 2026:

| Event                          | Date         |
| :----------------------------- | :----------- |
| Application Last Date (Extended) | April 15, 2026 |
| Exam Date                      | May 9, 2026  |

Candidates are advised to regularly check the official Anna University website for any further updates or announcements regarding the TANCET 2026 schedule.

## TANCET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Before applying, it's important to ensure you meet the eligibility requirements set by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Tamil Nadu. Here are the main criteria:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree or an equivalent qualification from a recognized university.
*   **Minimum Marks:** General category candidates need a minimum aggregate of 50% in their bachelor's degree. For candidates belonging to reserved categories, the minimum aggregate required is 45%.
*   **Final Year Students:** Students currently in their final year of a bachelor's degree program are also eligible to apply. However, their admission will be provisional and subject to them fulfilling the academic eligibility requirements at the time of TN MBA/MCA admissions.

## TANCET 2026 Exam Pattern

The TANCET examination is a computer-based test consisting of multiple-choice questions. Understanding the structure can help you prepare effectively:

*   **Total Questions:** 100 MCQs.
*   **Duration:** 120 minutes (2 hours).
*   **Sections for MBA:** The MBA paper is divided into five sections:
    *   Analysis of Business Situations
    *   Reading Comprehension
    *   Quantitative Aptitude
    *   Data Sufficiency
    *   General English
*   **Sections for MCA:** The MCA paper comprises four sections:
    *   Quantitative Aptitude
    *   Analytical Reasoning
    *   Logical Reasoning
    *   Computer Awareness

## TANCET 2026 Syllabus

To prepare thoroughly, candidates should familiarize themselves with the detailed syllabus for their chosen course. While the exact syllabus can be found on the official Anna University portal, here’s a general overview:

### MBA Syllabus

The MBA syllabus focuses on evaluating a candidate's analytical, comprehension, and numerical abilities relevant to business management. Key areas include interpreting business scenarios, understanding complex texts, solving mathematical problems, assessing data, and demonstrating proficiency in English language skills.

### MCA Syllabus

The MCA syllabus tests a candidate's aptitude for computer applications. It covers numerical ability, logical thinking, problem-solving skills, and fundamental computer knowledge.

## TANCET 2026 Registration Process

The registration for TANCET 2026 is conducted online through the official Anna University examination website, tancet.annauniv.edu. The process typically involves:

1.  **Visiting the Official Website:** Navigate to tancet.annauniv.edu.
2.  **One-Time Registration:** Create an account by providing necessary details.
3.  **Filling the Application Form:** Accurately enter your personal, academic, and contact information.
4.  **Uploading Documents:** Upload scanned copies of required documents, such as photographs and signatures, as per the specified format and size.
5.  **Paying the Application Fee:** Complete the fee payment online using available methods.
6.  **Submitting the Form:** Review all details and submit your application.

Always ensure you keep a copy of your submitted application form and payment receipt for future reference.
