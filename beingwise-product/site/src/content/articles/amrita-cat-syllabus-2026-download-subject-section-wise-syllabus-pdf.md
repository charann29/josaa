# Amrita CAT Syllabus 2026: Download Subject/Section wise Syllabus PDF

Understanding the Amrita CAT syllabus is your first step towards preparing effectively for this important MBA entrance exam. This guide breaks down the essential topics you need to master across all sections to perform well.

## Key takeaways

*   The Amrita CAT 2026 exam is structured into four main sections.
*   It covers Verbal Ability, Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning, General Awareness, and Quantitative Techniques.
*   Familiarising yourself with the specific topics under each section is crucial for targeted study.
*   The exam is conducted online, lasts 150 minutes, and contains 100 questions.

## Amrita CAT Syllabus 2026 Overview

The Amrita Common Admission Test (CAT) for MBA admissions assesses candidates across four key areas. These sections are designed to evaluate various skills necessary for a successful management career. The four sections you'll encounter in the exam are:

*   Verbal Reasoning and Language Comprehension
*   Data Interpretation and Analysis
*   General Knowledge on Indian and Global Scenarios
*   Quantitative Aptitude
