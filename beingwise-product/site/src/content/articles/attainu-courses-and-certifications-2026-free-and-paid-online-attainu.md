# AttainU Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online AttainU Courses

AttainU offers a range of online certification programs designed to equip students and professionals with in-demand skills for the modern job market. These courses focus on practical application and career readiness, catering to various experience levels.

## Key takeaways

*   AttainU provides industry-focused online courses in areas like full-stack development, advanced technologies for college students, and sales & business development.
*   Many programs include dedicated placement support, with assistance often beginning during the course itself.
*   Courses feature live interactive sessions, regular assessments, and one-on-one doubt-clearing opportunities.
*   Completion certificates are provided, which are shareable on professional networking platforms.

## Full Stack Developer Course with Placement

This 7-month program is tailored for beginners aspiring to become full-stack developers. It involves intensive live online training sessions with faculty, focusing on a comprehensive understanding of development principles. The curriculum incorporates periodic tests, daily assignments, and regular individual doubt-clearing sessions to ensure thorough learning.

A significant feature of this course is the integrated placement procedure, which commences after the fifth month. Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate that can be shared on platforms like WhatsApp and LinkedIn, validating their new skills.

*   **Duration:** 7 Months
*   **Target Audience:** Beginners
*   **Fee:** ₹ 68,000

## Excel in College 1st & 2nd Year with Advanced Technologies

Designed as a 6-month intensive live training, this course aims to enhance the skills of first and second-year college students using advanced technologies. It features live interactive lectures, daily assessments, and weekend workshops focused on placement preparation. Beyond technical skills, the program also includes training in soft skills and entrepreneurship.

Students benefit from a 6-month internship support period post-training. Mentors are available during live web development sessions to resolve any doubts or issues students may encounter, ensuring a smooth learning experience and career readiness.

*   **Duration:** 6 Months
*   **Skills Covered:** Programming skills
*   **Fee:** ₹ 44,000

## Sales and Business Development Course

This 4-month live training program is structured to prepare participants with industry- and research-backed sales and business development skills. Ideal for beginners, the course conducts evening classes, with live sessions also accessible later as recorded content on the learning portal.

Placement support begins right after the third month, helping students prepare for roles in human resources, sales, or business development. The curriculum includes daily or monthly tests, assignments, and case studies. Successful completion can lead to job opportunities with potential annual salaries ranging from ₹ 4 lakhs to ₹ 7 lakhs.

*   **Duration:** 4 Months
*   **Target Audience:** Beginners
*   **Skills Covered:** Sales skills
*   **Fee:** ₹ 37,000
