# ISC Board Exam 2026: Admit Card (OUT), Time Table (Released), Syllabus, Pattern, Specimen Papers

The Indian School Certificate (ISC) Class 12 examinations are a crucial milestone for students pursuing higher education. Conducted by the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE), these exams cover a wide range of subjects across Science, Commerce, and Arts streams.

## Key Takeaways

*   The ISC Class 12 exams for 2026 commenced on February 12 and are scheduled to conclude on April 6.
*   CISCE released the admit cards for the ISC 2026 examinations on January 27, 2026.
*   The official time table for the ISC 2026 exams was published by CISCE in November 2025.
*   Approximately 1 lakh students participate in the ISC examinations each year.

## ISC Admit Card 2026

The admit card for the ISC 2026 examinations was released by CISCE on January 27, 2026. It is mandatory for all students to collect their admit card from their respective schools. This document is crucial and must be carried to the examination hall on every exam day, as no student will be permitted to appear for the exam without it. Students should ensure the admit card is kept safe until the final results are announced.

## ISC Time Table 2026

CISCE officially released the ISC time table for 2026 in November 2025 on its official website, cisce.org. The date sheet provides comprehensive details regarding the examination dates and timings for all subjects. Students should refer to the detailed schedule to plan their studies effectively.

## ISC 12th Specimen Sample Papers 2026 and Exam Pattern

To excel in the ISC exams, students are strongly advised to practice with specimen sample papers for 2026 and previous years' question papers. These resources are invaluable as they help students become familiar with the exam pattern, including the marking scheme, types of questions asked, and the overall difficulty level. Regularly solving these papers can significantly boost confidence and improve time management during the actual examination.

## ISC Syllabus 2026 and Preparation Tips

The ISC syllabus outlines all the topics and chapters that students need to cover for their examinations. CISCE has released the detailed syllabus for all subjects for the 2026 exams. It is recommended that students aim to complete the entire syllabus at least two months before the exams begin to allow ample time for revision.

Here are some key preparation tips:

*   **Create a Study Schedule:** Develop a comprehensive study plan that allocates sufficient time to all subjects.
*   **Make Concise Notes:** Jot down difficult concepts, important formulas, and key points. These notes will be extremely helpful for quick revision, especially on the day before an exam.
*   **Practice Previous Papers:** Solve as many previous years' question papers as possible to understand recurring question patterns and improve problem-solving speed.

We wish all students the very best for their ISC 2026 examinations.
