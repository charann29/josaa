# BodhBridge Educational Services Private Limited (Fundaspring) Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Courses

BodhBridge Educational Services Private Limited, operating under the brand Fundaspring, offers a range of online certification courses designed to equip students and professionals with in-demand skills. These programs, often in collaboration with IITM Pravartak, cover various fields from cutting-edge technology to financial services.

## Key takeaways

*   Fundaspring, in partnership with IITM Pravartak, provides several certification courses.
*   Programs focus on practical skills in areas like AI, data analytics, software development, and mutual funds.
*   Courses vary in duration from 3 months to 6 months, with different fee structures.
*   They aim to provide hands-on experience and industry-relevant knowledge.

## Artificial Intelligence Certification

This certification program, offered in collaboration with IIT Madras Pravartak, focuses on essential Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence skills. It's designed for both newcomers to the field and experienced professionals looking to upgrade their expertise. The goal is to empower learners to tackle real-world problems and develop effective solutions using AI principles. The course has a duration of 6 months and a fee of ₹59,000.

## TechXcelerator Boot Camp

The TechXcelerator Boot Camp is a joint 'Finishing School Program' certification aimed at aspiring technology professionals. This intensive 14-week program, priced at ₹30,000, combines insights from industry experts and educators to offer a transformative learning experience. Key skills covered include project management and software development. Through hands-on training, real-world projects, and mentorship, participants develop both technical proficiency and a crucial problem-solving mindset for the fast-evolving tech industry.

## Large Scale Data Analytics Driven Systems Design

This certification course, spanning 3 months and costing ₹1,77,000, dives deep into the complexities of designing and implementing systems that leverage large-scale data analytics. It provides a thorough understanding of the principles and methodologies needed to build robust systems capable of handling massive datasets. The curriculum explores advanced techniques in data analytics, machine learning, and distributed computing, offering practical experience with standard industry technologies. This course is ideal for those looking to contribute to innovation in data analytics-driven systems.

## Certification Program in Mutual Funds

Designed for individuals keen on the financial sector, this 3-month certification program, with a fee of ₹8,850, equips students with the necessary knowledge and skills for mutual fund management. It covers the core principles, strategies, and regulatory frameworks governing mutual funds. Topics include portfolio management, risk assessment, regulatory compliance, and investment analysis. The program incorporates practical case studies, interactive discussions, and real-world simulations to foster practical insights and strategic decision-making abilities.

Fundaspring's certification courses provide valuable opportunities for skill enhancement and career advancement across various high-demand sectors.
