## Key takeaways

*   Learn to grow your own organic food through sustainable bio-organic practices.
*   Understand soil health, pest control, and optimal harvesting conditions.
*   Enjoy a self-paced learning experience with video lectures, articles, and downloadable resources.
*   Receive a certificate of completion from Udemy upon finishing the course.

## Course Overview

The "Bio-Organic Farming & Gardening: GROW Your OWN FOOD" certification is designed by im Simpl, an environmental engineer and organic farmer. Offered through Udemy, this online program teaches you the methods and concepts behind bio-organic gardening and farming to produce nutritious, chemical-free food.

The course includes over 6.5 hours of pre-recorded video content, complemented by two articles and ten downloadable resources. Quizzes are also integrated to help reinforce your understanding of topics such as effective pest control, soil analysis techniques, ideal harvest conditions, and efficient watering methods.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this online certification, you will gain a solid foundation in bio-organic gardening principles for cultivating organic food. The curriculum covers various methodologies, including establishing and managing a farm, understanding row farming techniques, and implementing strategies for plant watering and pest control. You will also learn practical approaches for analyzing soil health and determining the best harvest times.

### Core Learning Areas:

*   **Introduction to Bio-Organic Principles:** Understanding the fundamentals of organic farming and its benefits.
*   **Soil Health and Composting:** Techniques for observational soil analysis, creating aerobic compost, and enriching soil.
*   **Farm Planning and Preparation:** Steps for planning your farm layout, resource assessment, and companion planting.
*   **Cultivation Techniques:** Methods for row farming, plant care, and sustainable practices.
*   **Pest and Disease Management:** Organic approaches to controlling pests and maintaining plant health.
*   **Harvesting and Post-Harvest:** Identifying optimal harvest conditions and techniques.
