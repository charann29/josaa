# What's your highest package in 2020-21?

Understanding the placement landscape is a crucial part of choosing the right engineering college. For the 2020-21 academic year, CVR College of Engineering in Hyderabad showed some impressive figures that prospective students and their parents will find very encouraging.

## Key Takeaways

*   CVR College of Engineering achieved over 1300 placements in the 2020-21 academic year, a new record for the institution.
*   The highest salary package offered reached a significant ₹30.5 Lakhs per annum.
*   A substantial number of students, around 60, secured offers above ₹8 Lakhs.
*   More than 300 students received job offers with salaries exceeding ₹5 Lakhs.

## A Record-Breaking Year for Placements

The 2020-21 academic year marked a significant milestone for CVR College of Engineering. For the first time, the college surpassed 1300 placements, demonstrating a strong performance in connecting its graduates with employment opportunities. This figure is particularly noteworthy when compared to other institutions in its peer group.

## Top Salary Offers

One of the most exciting aspects of the 2020-21 placement season was the peak salary package. The highest offer extended to multiple students was an impressive ₹30.5 Lakhs per annum. This indicates the high demand for CVR graduates in specialized roles and the quality of education provided by the institution.

## A Broad Spectrum of High-Value Offers

Beyond the top package, the college also saw a healthy distribution of high-value offers across its student body. Approximately 60 students secured job offers with annual salaries greater than ₹8 Lakhs. Furthermore, over 300 students received packages exceeding ₹5 Lakhs. These figures highlight a strong overall placement performance, where a significant portion of the graduating class secured well-paying positions.

## Leading the State in Placement Outcomes

Based on these results, CVR College of Engineering has positioned itself as a leader in the state concerning job offers, especially those with higher salary packages. The institution's commitment to facilitating strong career starts for its students is reflected in its high placement percentage, which is among the best in the entire state.

These placement statistics provide valuable insight into the career prospects for students at CVR College of Engineering, Hyderabad, showcasing a robust and successful placement drive for the 2020-21 academic year.
