# Sr.Secondary: Chemistry (313) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This course offers a comprehensive study of Chemistry at the senior secondary level, designed for students keen to build a strong foundation in the subject. Delivered by the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) through the Swayam platform, it combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Flexible Learning:** The program is self-paced, allowing you to study through video lectures, text materials, and self-assessment tools at your convenience.
*   **Comprehensive Curriculum:** It covers fundamental chemistry topics, from atomic structure to organic chemistry and everyday applications, across 8 distinct modules.
*   **Official Certification:** Upon successful completion of examinations and practicals, you will receive a Sr.Secondary: Chemistry (313) Certificate issued by NIOS and Swayam.
*   **Interactive Support:** The course includes discussion forums for peer interaction and expert guidance, enhancing the learning experience.

## Course Overview

The Sr.Secondary: Chemistry (313) program is structured to provide a deep understanding of core chemical concepts relevant to the senior secondary curriculum. Coordinated by Professor Dr. Rajeev Prasad from NIOS, this course is delivered in English, primarily through self-study, utilizing both video and text-based content. It offers 10 credit points upon completion.

The curriculum is spread over 26 weeks, integrating both theoretical lessons and practical components. Students will engage with lesson-wise video tutorials and multiple-choice questions for continuous self-assessment. The course uses numerous examples and videos to clarify complex chemical ideas, making learning accessible and engaging.

## What You Will Learn

This course is meticulously designed to equip learners with a solid grasp of various chemistry domains. Upon completing the program, you will have enhanced knowledge in areas such as:

*   Basic Concepts of Chemistry
*   Atomic Structure and Chemical Bonding
*   States of Matter
*   Chemical Energetics and Dynamics
*   Chemistry of Elements (s-block, p-block, d-block, f-block)
*   Chemistry of Organic Compounds (Hydrocarbons, Haloalkanes, Alcohols, Aldehydes, Carboxylic Acids, Nitrogen-containing compounds)
*   Coordination Compounds
*   Chemistry in Everyday Life (Biomolecules, Drugs, Soaps, Detergents, Polymers)

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for anyone interested in developing a strong foundation in chemistry at the school level. It particularly benefits students preparing for senior secondary examinations or those aspiring to pursue careers in chemistry-related fields. Individuals keen on refreshing their chemistry knowledge will also find this course valuable.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Sr.Secondary: Chemistry (313) course through Swayam is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to begin your learning journey:

1.  Visit the official Swayam portal for this specific course.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  On the subsequent page, you can either sign in with your existing Swayam account credentials or create a new account if you are a first-time user.
4.  Once signed in, you can proceed to enroll in the course.

There are no specific course or certificate fees mentioned for this program, making it an accessible option for many students.
