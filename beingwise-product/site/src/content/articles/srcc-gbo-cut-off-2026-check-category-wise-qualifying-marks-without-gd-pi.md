# SRCC GBO Cut off 2026: Check Category-wise Qualifying Marks Without GD/PI

Understanding the SRCC GBO cut-off scores is crucial for aspiring students aiming for the Post Graduate Diploma in Global Business Operations (PGDGBO) at Shri Ram College of Commerce. These cut-offs determine eligibility for the next stages of the admission process.

## Key takeaways

*   The SRCC GBO 2026 cut-off scores will be released following the exam results, expected in early April 2026.
*   Different cut-offs apply to various categories, reflecting the college's reservation policies.
*   Approximately 94 seats are available for the PGDGBO program, influencing the competitiveness of the cut-offs.
*   The initial cut-off determines who is invited for the Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds.

## Understanding SRCC GBO Cut-Off Scores

The SRCC GBO examination for 2026 took place on February 28, 2026. Shortly after the results are announced, the official cut-off scores will be published. Candidates whose scores meet or exceed these cut-offs will qualify to proceed to the subsequent selection stages for the PGDGBO course. A provisional answer key for the SRCC GBO 2026 exam was released on March 5, providing an early indication for candidates.

## Factors Influencing the SRCC GBO Cut-Off

Several elements contribute to setting the SRCC GBO cut-off scores each year. These factors collectively determine the minimum score required for admission consideration:

*   **Exam Difficulty:** The overall difficulty level of the SRCC GBO question paper.
*   **Applicant Volume:** The total number of candidates who appeared for the examination.
*   **Available Seats:** The fixed number of seats offered for the PGDGBO program at SRCC.
*   **Marking Scheme:** The specific scoring methodology used for the exam.
*   **Reservation Policies:** Government-mandated reservation criteria for different categories.
*   **Previous Year Trends:** Historical cut-off data from past SRCC GBO exams.
