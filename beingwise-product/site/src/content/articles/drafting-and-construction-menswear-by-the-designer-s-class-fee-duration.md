# Drafting & Construction Menswear by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For aspiring fashion designers and enthusiasts keen on the intricacies of menswear, The Designer's Class offers an online course focused on drafting and constructing men's garments. This program aims to demystify the process of creating well-fitted and stylish clothing for men.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn the fundamentals of pattern drafting and garment construction specifically for menswear.
*   Gain practical skills through 9 assignments and access to 16 downloadable resources.
*   Benefit from an 8-hour and 16-minute video lecture series led by designer Kunal Rawal.
*   Enjoy the flexibility of a self-paced learning model with one year of access to course materials.

## Course Overview

This online certification delves into the specialized skills required to create patterns and construct various types of men's clothing. It covers the complexities of achieving the right fit, style, and overall design for structured garments. The curriculum focuses on developing patterns and building shirts, trousers, and other structured menswear items.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this program, you will develop a solid understanding of the drafting and construction processes unique to menswear. The course will enhance your fashion designing concepts for men's apparel, enabling you to analyze different methods and strategies for tailoring. You'll also explore current trends influencing men's fashion choices and gain hands-on knowledge in drafting and constructing shirts, trousers, and various structured garments.

## Program Structure and Duration

The course is delivered through video and text-based materials, designed for self-study. It includes an extensive 8-hour and 16-minute video lecture series that guides you through the drafting and fabrication of key menswear pieces. To reinforce learning, there are 9 assignments and 16 downloadable resources provided. You will have one year of access to all study materials, allowing you to learn at your own pace.

## Fee and Certification

The fee for the Drafting & Construction Menswear online course is ₹2,999. It's important to note that while the program offers comprehensive learning, it does not include a formal certificate upon completion.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for fashion designers, students, and anyone with an interest in understanding the technical aspects of creating menswear. If you aspire to specialize in men's fashion or wish to refine your pattern-making and construction skills in this area, this course is designed for you.

This program offers a focused opportunity to build essential skills in menswear design and construction, guided by an industry expert.
