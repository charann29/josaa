# Data Science Course by Digital Vidya: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Embarking on a career in data science can open up many opportunities in today's data-driven world. Digital Vidya offers a comprehensive Data Science program designed to equip aspiring professionals with essential skills and industry-relevant knowledge.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program offers flexible learning options, including self-study and virtual classrooms, with weekend classes.
*   It provides two certifications: one from Digital Vidya and another from NASSCOM FutureSkills.
*   The curriculum covers core data science concepts, including Python, machine learning, and data manipulation, with practical capstone projects.
*   Participants receive career support, including resume building and interview assistance.

## Program Overview and Curriculum

Digital Vidya's Data Science program is meticulously crafted by experienced industry professionals. The curriculum has been reviewed and validated by over 2,800 enterprises to ensure it meets current industry standards and demands. This course is designed to be a crucial step for individuals aiming to become proficient Data Scientists.

The training covers fundamental programming concepts in Python, including variables, data types, and various operators (logical, arithmetic, and comparison). You will also delve into data manipulation techniques using the Pandas library, alongside essential skills for Regression Analysis, Decision Trees and Forests, and key statistical concepts.

A significant component of the program involves solving Capstone Projects. These practical assignments allow you to apply your learning in real-world scenarios across niches such as Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, Bank Management, and Healthcare Analysis, thereby enhancing your industry knowledge.

## What You Will Learn

The Data Science program offers a comprehensive syllabus covering end-to-end data science. You will strengthen your foundational knowledge in areas like Machine Learning, Exploratory Data Science, SQL, and statistics. Upon successful completion, you will be able to:

*   Utilize Python and Tableau for data visualization.
*   Access and use libraries such as Matplotlib, Pandas, NumPy, and scikit-learn.
*   Effectively use Jupyter Notebook for coding, visualizations, and creating narrative texts.
*   Gain proficiency in the R programming language.

The course is suitable for both programmers looking to upskill and non-programmers with strong analytical abilities and a passion for data science. It is particularly beneficial for B.Tech, BE, MCA, or MCS students aspiring to enter fields like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, or Data Analytics.

## Certifications and Eligibility

Upon successful completion of the program, participants are eligible to receive two distinct certifications. The first is awarded by Digital Vidya after you complete all assignments and at least one Capstone Project. The second is the NASSCOM FutureSkills Certification, awarded upon the successful completion of the entire Data Science Programme.

There are no strict programming prerequisites for this course. Individuals with good analytical skills and a strong desire to learn about data science will find this program rewarding. Your Digital Vidya certification as a "Data Scientist" will be awarded by industry experts after they assess your projects and assignments, specifically after you complete a Capstone Project.

## Program Highlights and Support

The program offers several features designed to enhance the learning experience and support career progression:

*   **Expert Training:** Instruction is provided by seasoned industry experts.
*   **Flexible Learning:** Enjoy lifetime access to study materials and the convenience of studying at your own pace, with live question and answer sessions available.
*   **Practical Focus:** Emphasis on practical projects and solving Capstone Projects.
*   **Career Assistance:** Dedicated placement cell, resume building support, and guaranteed interviews after course completion.
*   **NASSCOM Alignment:** The curriculum is aligned with NASSCOM standards, leading to a NASSCOM certificate of completion.
*   **Student Benefits:** A 15% student discount is available.
