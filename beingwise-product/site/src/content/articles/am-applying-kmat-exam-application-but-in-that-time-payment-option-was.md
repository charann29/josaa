# I am applying KMAT exam application but in that time payment option was not shown and payment was not done...so how can I confirm my application was done or not

It can be quite concerning when you've spent time filling out an important application like KMAT but aren't sure if it's complete due to a payment issue. Don't worry, there are clear steps you can take to confirm your application status.

## Key Takeaways

*   Always log back into the official KMAT application portal to check your submission status.
*   Look for specific indicators like "Application Complete" or "Payment Pending."
*   Keep an eye on your registered email for any official communications from KMAT.
*   If in doubt, directly contact the KMAT helpline for personalized assistance.

## Checking Your Application Status Online

The very first step is to revisit the official KMAT application portal. Use the login credentials you created during the application process. Once you're logged in, navigate through the dashboard. There should be a dedicated section that displays the current status of your application. This section is crucial as it will typically show whether your application is fully submitted, awaiting payment, or if there's another issue.

## Reviewing Your Email for Confirmations

After submitting any online application, it's standard practice for the organizing body to send an automatic email confirmation. Check the inbox of the email address you registered with KMAT. Look for emails from official KMAT addresses. Sometimes these emails might land in your spam or junk folder, so be sure to check those as well. A successful application confirmation, even if payment was a separate step, might provide some clarity.

## Reattempting the Payment Process

If your application status on the portal clearly states "Payment Pending" or a similar message, it indicates that the payment step was indeed not completed. In this scenario, the portal should offer an option to reattempt payment. Before proceeding, ensure that you have sufficient funds in your bank account or that your chosen payment method is active. Follow the on-screen instructions carefully, as payment gateways can sometimes be sensitive to network issues or browser settings.

## Contacting KMAT Support Directly

If, after checking the portal and your email, you are still unsure about your application status, or if the payment reattempt isn't working, the most reliable course of action is to contact the KMAT authorities. They have a dedicated helpline or customer support system designed to assist applicants with such queries. Be ready to provide your application number, registered email ID, and any other relevant details so they can quickly locate your record and provide accurate information.

Taking these steps will help you confirm whether your KMAT application was successfully submitted or if further action is needed to complete the process.
