# Introduction and Applications of Hydrologic Engineering Center’s- River Analysis System (HEC-RAS)

This program explores the diverse applications of the Hydrologic Engineering Center's River Analysis System (HEC-RAS), a crucial software in civil engineering for understanding open channel hydraulics and river behavior.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn to model and analyze river systems using HEC-RAS software over a three-month duration.
*   Gain practical experience through hands-on projects involving software like AutoCAD, QGIS, HEC-HMS, and MS-Excel.
*   Understand the theoretical foundations, capabilities, and limitations of HEC-RAS for hydraulic assessments.
*   Receive a course completion certificate, with a merit certificate for top performers.

## Course Overview

This specialized online course focuses on the practical and theoretical aspects of HEC-RAS, a powerful tool for civil engineers. It delves into various uses of open channel hydraulics, including creating flood hazard maps, analyzing roadway crossings, and designing bridges. The program is structured to help students master the analysis of river models and their behavior using HEC-RAS.

Over the three-month duration, the curriculum integrates theoretical knowledge with practical application. Students will engage with the HEC-RAS software on two experimental projects and utilize other essential software such as AutoCAD, QGIS, HEC-HMS, and MS-Excel. The course provides a deep understanding of HEC-RAS's modeling potential, its drawbacks, and its various tools. It also offers practical insights into modeling river bridges and culverts, analyzing road crossings, and solving problems related to computing floodways and flood fringe volumes.

## Who is This Course For?

This online certification program is ideally suited for engineering students, research associates, and hydraulic modelers who are keen to deepen their expertise in HEC-RAS. It provides a comprehensive learning experience for those looking to enhance their skills in hydraulic assessment and river system analysis.

## Fees and Certification

The program fee for the Introduction and Applications of Hydrologic Engineering Center’s- River Analysis System (HEC-RAS) is ₹40,000. Upon successful completion, students will receive a course completion certificate. Additionally, those who rank among the top 5% of the class will be awarded a merit certificate.
