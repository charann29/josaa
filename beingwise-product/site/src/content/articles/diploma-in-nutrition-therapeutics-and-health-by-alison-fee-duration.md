# Diploma in Nutrition Therapeutics and Health by Alison: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding the vital connection between nutrition and overall health is more important than ever. The Diploma in Nutrition Therapeutics and Health, offered by Alison, provides a comprehensive introduction to this field, covering essential concepts from basic digestion to therapeutic diets for various health conditions.

## Key takeaways

*   This diploma program is offered completely free of charge.
*   The course content is delivered through a blend of video lectures and text-based materials.
*   Upon successful completion, a certificate is available directly from Alison.
*   The entire curriculum is taught in English.

## Understanding the Course Structure

This diploma is designed to equip learners with fundamental knowledge in nutrition and its therapeutic applications. It's structured into several modules, guiding students through various aspects of diet and health management.

### Medium and Mode of Instruction

The program's instructional language is English, making it accessible to a wide audience. The learning materials are presented in a dual format, combining video content with written text, which allows for different learning preferences and reinforces key concepts.

## Course Fees and Certification

One of the most appealing aspects of this program is that the course itself is entirely free. While the learning content is free, Alison provides an official certificate upon successful completion. Details regarding any associated fees for obtaining this certificate would be available through Alison's official platform.

## Detailed Syllabus

The curriculum is thoughtfully divided into eleven modules, including two assessments and a final course assessment. Here's a look at what you'll learn:

*   **Module 1: Introduction to Nutrition and Health**
    *   Relationship between food, nutrition, and health.
    *   Digestion, absorption, and nutrient utilization.
    *   Recommended Dietary Allowances.

*   **Module 2: Nutrients and Their Role I**
    *   In-depth study of carbohydrates, fiber, protein, and fat.

*   **Module 3: Nutrients and Their Role II**
    *   Understanding energy, fat-soluble vitamins, and water-soluble vitamins.

*   **Module 4: Nutrients and Their Role III**
    *   Focus on major and trace minerals essential for health.

*   **Module 5: Diploma in Nutrition, Therapeutics and Health - First Assessment**

*   **Module 6: Meal Planning**
    *   Importance of water, nutritional disorders, balanced diets, and food groups.
    *   Practical aspects of food selection and other factors influencing diet.

*   **Module 7: Sanitation and Therapeutic Nutrition**
    *   Food sanitation and hygiene, water purification.
    *   Principles and therapeutic adaptation of normal diets.
    *   Diet during fevers and lung diseases.

*   **Module 8: Therapeutic Nutrition I**
    *   Dietary management for gastrointestinal disorders (constipation, diarrhoea).
    *   Diets for liver disorders, gallbladder diseases, diabetes, and heart/blood vessel conditions.

*   **Module 9: Therapeutic Nutrition II**
    *   Dietary approaches for myocardial infarction, kidney disorders (including renal failure).
    *   Nutrition in cancer, metabolic disorders, stress, burns, and surgery.

*   **Module 10: Diploma in Nutrition, Therapeutics and Health - Second Assessment**

*   **Module 11: Course Assessment**

## How to Apply

Applying for this diploma program is straightforward. Prospective students can typically register directly on Alison's official website. The process usually involves creating an account and enrolling in the desired course. Since the course is free, there are no complex admission criteria or application fees beyond the potential charge for the official certificate.

This diploma offers a valuable foundation for anyone interested in nutrition, health, or considering further studies in dietetics and related fields.
