# Post Graduation Programme in Digital Marketing by Indian Institute of Digital Education: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

The Indian Institute of Digital Education (IIDE) offers an 11-month Post Graduation Programme in Digital Marketing, designed for individuals aiming to build a career in the dynamic digital industry. This program focuses on developing both core digital marketing and essential managerial skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   An 11-month postgraduate program with certification aligned with industry leaders like Facebook and Google.
*   Includes over 750 hours of learning, 13+ live projects, and one-on-one student mentorship.
*   Features two distinct specializations and assures placement opportunities, including a two-month internship.
*   Offers flexible learning modes through virtual classrooms, video, and text-based content, with classes available on weekdays and weekends.

## Program Overview and Structure

This comprehensive program is structured to provide deep knowledge and practical skills in digital marketing. It covers a wide range of topics, ensuring students are well-prepared for various roles in the digital landscape. The learning experience combines self-study with virtual classroom sessions, utilizing video and text-based materials.

Throughout the course, students engage in over 13 live projects, gaining hands-on experience that complements the theoretical learning. The curriculum also emphasizes crucial managerial skills, preparing graduates not just for executive roles but also for leadership positions in digital marketing.

## Specializations and Career Support

The program offers two distinct specializations, allowing students to tailor their learning path to specific career interests within digital marketing management. A significant benefit of this program is the assured placement assistance, which includes a mandatory two-month internship period. This internship provides invaluable real-world experience and networking opportunities. Students also receive direct mentorship from industry experts working at leading agencies and brands, offering insights and guidance.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Post Graduation Programme in Digital Marketing, candidates generally need to meet specific academic qualifications:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree or an equivalent qualification from any recognized university or discipline, with a minimum score of 55%. Students in their final year of a degree examination may apply for provisional admission, which will be confirmed upon submission of their final results.
*   **Alternative Eligibility:** Candidates who do not meet the above academic criteria but have completed 10 or 12 years of education, combined with relevant work experience of 5 or 3 years respectively, may also apply. Diploma holders with at least 3 years of work experience are also eligible.

## Fees and Application Process

The course fee for the Post Graduation Programme in Digital Marketing is Rs. 30,678 per month. IIDE also offers an option for 0% EMI to make the program more accessible. Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from IIDE, aligned with Google and Facebook, signifying their expertise.

To apply for admission, prospective students should follow these steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Access the official IIDE website for the Post Graduation Programme in Digital Marketing.
2.  **Check Eligibility and Apply:** Verify your eligibility and proceed to submit any required fees for the IIDE Admissions Test. You will then need to sit for this test.
3.  **Application Review and Interview:** The admissions committee will review your application. Shortlisted candidates will then be invited for a personal interview to assess their suitability for the program.
4.  **Admission Confirmation:** Successful candidates will be informed and sent an official admission confirmation letter.

This program is ideal for individuals aspiring to become Digital Marketing Executives or Digital Marketing Managers, providing them with the necessary skills and certifications to excel in these roles.
