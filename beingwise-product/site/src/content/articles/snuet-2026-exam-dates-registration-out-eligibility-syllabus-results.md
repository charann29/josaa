# SNUET 2026 Exam: Dates, Registration (OUT), Eligibility, Syllabus, Results

The Sister Nivedita University Entrance Test (SNUET) is your gateway to various undergraduate programs at Sister Nivedita University, Kolkata. This exam is conducted online, allowing you to take it conveniently from home.

## Key takeaways

*   **Registration is open:** Applications for SNUET 2026 are currently being accepted on the official Sister Nivedita University website.
*   **Online and Proctored:** The exam uses a Remote Proctored Online Mode (RPOM), meaning you can appear from home using a computer, but will be monitored.
*   **Multiple Phases:** The SNUET 2026 exam is expected to be held in several phases between February and June 2026.
*   **Alternative Admissions:** For B.Tech courses, Sister Nivedita University also considers JEE Main, WBJEE, and CUET UG scores, and you can participate in WBJEE counselling without needing to take SNUET.

## Understanding SNUET 2026

SNUET is a national-level entrance examination specifically designed for admission to undergraduate courses at Sister Nivedita University. The university itself conducts this exam once a year to shortlist eligible candidates.

The exam is a Computer-Based Test (CBT) and is conducted in English. For B.Tech, B.Arch, and B.Plan courses, the exam duration is 1.5 hours, consisting of one paper with five sections and a total of 100 marks. Each correct answer fetches 2 marks, and there is no negative marking for incorrect responses.

### Courses Offered Through SNUET

SNUET scores are considered for a wide range of undergraduate programs, including:

*   BBA
*   BCom
*   BCA
*   BSc (Hons) and BSc - Hospitality & Tourism Administration
*   BA
*   BStat (Hons in Statistics)
*   B.E. / B.Tech

## SNUET 2026 Registration Process

The registration for SNUET 2026 is currently open. To apply, you must visit the official Sister Nivedita University website. During the application process, candidates are also required to complete their slot booking for the Remote Proctored Online Mode (RPOM) exam. This allows you to choose a convenient time slot to appear for the test from your home.

## SNUET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

While specific eligibility details for each course will be available on the official university website, generally, candidates must have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognized board. For B.Tech programs, having studied Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in 10+2 is usually a prerequisite.

## SNUET 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The SNUET 2026 question paper is structured with seven different subject modules. Candidates are required to attempt any five modules relevant to the specific course they are applying for. For B.Tech admissions, candidates must compulsorily answer questions from Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, in addition to English and Logical Reasoning, which serve as elective modules.

More detailed information about the paper pattern and the complete syllabus for each course will be available on the official Sister Nivedita University website.

## SNUET 2026 Results and Counselling

Once the exams are conducted across various phases, the SNUET 2026 results are expected to be declared between March and June. Following the result announcement, successful candidates will be invited for counselling, which is tentatively scheduled for June to July 2026. This counselling process will determine final admissions based on merit and available seats.
