# Certified Software Quality Assurance Professional by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to build a career in ensuring software quality, the Certified Software Quality Assurance Professional program by Vskills offers a structured path. This self-study course helps participants develop essential skills to meet client needs and maintain high standards in software development.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is designed for self-paced learning, delivered through video and text-based materials in English.
*   It covers vital areas like risk management, SQA metrics, audit and control, and fundamental quality models.
*   Certification requires passing an online exam with a minimum score of 50%, with no negative marking.
*   The course is suitable for current professionals, students, and those aspiring to roles in IT or software testing.

## Course Overview

This certification aims to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary to effectively oversee software quality throughout its lifecycle. It delves into various aspects of Software Quality Assurance (SQA), including advanced concepts and practical techniques. Participants will gain insights into managing software configuration, understanding quality models, and implementing analytical methods to ensure product excellence. The program provides relevant study materials to help candidates tackle real-world challenges confidently, guided by expert insights.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of software quality assurance. Key learning areas include:

*   **Quality Concepts:** Fundamental principles of Software Quality Assurance.
*   **Management Tools and Standards:** Understanding various tools, standards, and models used in SQA.
*   **Product Quality:** Techniques for ensuring the overall quality of software products.
*   **Risk Management:** Learning different strategies and techniques for identifying and mitigating risks.
*   **Advanced SQA Concepts:** Deeper dives into more complex aspects of software quality assurance.

This knowledge prepares individuals to contribute significantly to software development teams, ensuring that products are reliable and meet user expectations.

## Who Is This Program For?

This certification is highly recommended for a diverse group of individuals who wish to enhance their expertise in software quality. It is particularly beneficial for:

*   **Existing Professionals:** Those seeking a career transition or aiming to specialize further within the IT industry.
*   **Students:** Individuals looking to strengthen their resumes and gain a competitive edge in the job market.
*   **Aspiring IT Professionals:** Candidates who wish to enter the IT sector, especially in software testing or quality assurance departments.
*   **Knowledge Seekers:** Anyone desiring in-depth understanding of Software Quality Assurance concepts and practices.
*   **Career Advancers:** Individuals aiming to validate their skills and secure higher certifications to prove their value.

## Program Offerings and Highlights

The program provides a rich learning experience through several key offerings:

*   **Study Materials:** Comprehensive materials designed to facilitate effective self-study.
*   **Online Classes and Demo Chapters:** Access to online learning resources, including introductory demo chapters.
*   **Practice Sets and Sample Question Papers:** Tools to help candidates prepare thoroughly for the certification exam.
*   **Mock Tests:** Simulated exam environments to build confidence and assess readiness.

Additionally, the program offers support features like online classroom access and course monitoring. Upon certification, candidate profiles are tagged on prominent job portals, which can assist with career advancement.

## Fee and Certification Details

The enrollment fee for the Certified Software Quality Assurance Professional program is **₹ 3,499**. This amount covers the base fee for the program and its associated resources. Upon successful completion of the online examination, candidates will receive a certificate from Vskills, the certifying authority.

### Certification Examination

To achieve certification, candidates must pass an online exam. This exam can be taken at a date, venue, and time chosen by the candidate. A minimum score of 50% of the total marks is required to pass, and there is no negative marking for incorrect answers. Study materials are provided to support candidates in their preparation for this assessment.

To enroll in the Certified Software Quality Assurance Professional program, candidates should follow a straightforward application process:

1.  **Visit the Official Vskills Portal:** Navigate to the specific program page on the official Vskills website.
2.  **Initiate Purchase:** Locate and click on the 'BUY NOW' option, typically found on the program's page.
