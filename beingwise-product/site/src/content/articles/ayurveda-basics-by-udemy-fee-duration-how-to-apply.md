# Ayurveda Basics by BeingWise: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Ayurveda, an ancient Indian medical system, offers a holistic approach to well-being, emphasizing natural methods for physical and mental health. This online certification provides a foundational understanding of Ayurveda's principles, designed for individuals interested in preventative healthcare and natural living.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn the fundamental concepts of Ayurveda, including elements, doshas, and their constitutions.
*   Understand how to apply Ayurvedic principles to daily routines for improved balance and health.
*   The course offers self-paced learning through pre-recorded videos and downloadable resources.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive an official certificate.

## Course Overview and Highlights

This online certification, developed by Vedic Sciences Instructor Janet M, delves into the core tenets of Ayurveda. It covers topics such as the five elements, the three doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha), doshic constitutions, and their impact on health. The curriculum also explores related practices like yoga, pranayama, and abhyanga (self-massage), alongside concepts of karma and physiology.

The program is structured to help participants identify imbalances and make informed decisions to achieve an optimal state of health. It includes a comprehensive assessment to reinforce learning.

Key features of this course include:

*   **Certificate of Completion:** Awarded upon finishing the course.
*   **Self-Paced Learning:** Study at your own convenience.
*   **Comprehensive Content:** 8 hours of pre-recorded video lectures.
*   **Supplementary Materials:** 18 downloadable resources to enhance learning.
*   **Accessibility:** Content can be accessed on mobile devices and TVs.
*   **Money-Back Guarantee:** A 30-day money-back guarantee is available, as per the course provider.
*   **Unlimited Access:** Once purchased, the course content is available indefinitely.

## Duration and Learning Mode

This self-study course is delivered through a combination of video and text-based content. While the total video content is approximately 8 hours, the self-paced nature means participants can complete it according to their individual schedules. The course is entirely in English, making it accessible to a wide audience.

## What You Will Learn

By the end of this certification, participants will have a deeper understanding of:

*   **Core Ayurvedic Principles:** Grasp the fundamentals of elements, gunas, doshas, and their characteristics.
*   **Doshic Constitutions:** Learn to identify your doshic type and make lifestyle choices accordingly.
*   **Holistic Practices:** Explore concepts related to yoga, mind, and karma, and how they integrate with Ayurveda.
*   **Daily Routines:** Understand traditional Ayurvedic practices for tongue cleaning, bowel movement, skin brushing, neti pot, pranayama, and abhyanga.
*   **Physiology and Digestion:** Gain knowledge of basic physiology, the role of Agni (digestive fire), and the six tastes of food.
*   **Understanding Disease:** Learn about the three causes and six stages of disease from an Ayurvedic perspective.

## Course Fees and Application

Details regarding the course fee are typically provided on the official course page. At the time of writing, the course is listed with a discounted fee of ₹499, reduced from an original price of ₹2,699. This fee grants access to all course materials and the certificate upon completion.

To apply, interested individuals would typically visit the official course platform and complete the registration and payment process directly. There are no complex application forms or entrance exams for this particular certification.

## Course Syllabus Highlights

The comprehensive syllabus covers various aspects of Ayurveda, structured to provide a logical learning path:

*   **Introduction:** Welcome to Ayurveda, instructor introduction, and learning objectives.
*   **What is Ayurveda?:** Distinguishing Ayurveda from other medical systems, the four goals in life, and the three pillars of health.
*   **Your Mind:** Exploring the mind, body, and soul connection, the three Gunas, five Koshas, and mind functions.
*   **Yoga and Karma:** Understanding yoga types for different Ayurvedic constitutions, the wheel of karma, and the four paths and eight limbs of yoga.
*   **Doshas:** In-depth study of the five-element theory, Vata, Pitta, and Kapha doshas, their roles, and how to determine your dosha.
*   **Digestion and Food:** The importance of Agni, the six tastes of food, digestion processes, and classifications of diet (Sattvic, Rajasic, Tamasic).
*   **"Dis-Ease":** Ayurvedic perspectives on the causes and stages of disease, including specific examples like Type 2 Diabetes and high blood pressure.
*   **Daily Routines:** Practical guidance on Ayurvedic daily rituals such as tongue cleaning, dry skin brushing, Abhyanga (self-massage), Pranayama (breathing exercises), and Neti Pot usage.
*   **Basic Western Physiology:** A brief overview to complement the Ayurvedic understanding.

This course offers a practical and accessible way to begin your journey into the world of Ayurveda, providing foundational knowledge to enhance your personal health and well-being.
