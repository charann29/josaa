LawSikho offers a range of online legal courses and certifications designed to provide practical skills and in-depth knowledge for aspiring and practicing legal professionals. These programs cover various specialisations, from foundational paralegal skills to advanced technology and data protection laws.

## Key takeaways

*   LawSikho programs focus on practical, real-world application of legal concepts.
*   Courses are typically 12 months long, offering comprehensive study and expert guidance.
*   They cater to a diverse audience, including law students, young lawyers, and other professionals.
*   Curricula often include hands-on experience, such as drafting documents and managing legal processes.

## Paralegal Associate Diploma

This 12-month diploma is structured to equip individuals with essential skills for supporting legal professionals. The curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with practical modules, focusing on tasks like assisting lawyers, managing documents, and understanding the US legal framework. Classes are scheduled for convenience, either on Sundays or weekday evenings after 8 PM.

Participants learn to draft effective emails, review contracts, identify specific clauses, and navigate the organisational structure of a law firm. The program is designed to provide comprehensive support through industry experts, helping students develop crucial paralegal competencies.

## Diploma in International Data Protection and Privacy Laws

This one-year diploma delves into the critical area of data privacy, covering topics from data collection and processing to protection, transfer, and dispute resolution. It's particularly relevant for law students, young lawyers, in-house counsel, chartered accountants, and company secretaries.

The course provides deep insights into data privacy impact assessments and audits. Students benefit from extensive support from leading industry experts, gaining hands-on experience that can be applied in real-world data protection scenarios.

## Diploma in Technology Law, Fintech Regulations and Technology Contracts

This 12-month program is tailored for legal professionals and students interested in the dynamic fields of cyberlaw, technology law, banking, and investment law. It's suitable for law students, graduates, young lawyers, litigators, company secretaries, and accountants.

The curriculum focuses on developing expertise in drafting various contracts related to cyber and technology laws, fintech, and e-commerce. Practical skills covered include software-related due diligence, intellectual property audits, setting up online businesses, addressing disparaging advertisements, and drafting relevant legal agreements.

## Diploma in Advanced Contract Drafting, Negotiation and Dispute Resolution

This 12-month diploma offers an extensive understanding of drafting, negotiating, and reviewing high-value commercial contracts. It is ideal for law students, lawyers, dispute resolution specialists, decision-makers, entrepreneurs, executive directors, and officers seeking to enhance their skills in this domain.

The course curriculum includes an orientation to effective contract drafting tools, an introduction to contract components and initial clauses, and comprehensive contract negotiation strategies. A key feature is the hands-on experience in drafting over 100 types of commercial contracts, guided by industry experts.

These programs aim to provide practical, career-focused legal education, preparing students for the demands of the modern legal landscape.
