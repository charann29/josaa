# Jainx Academy Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Jainx Academy Courses

Jainx Academy, an initiative of Jain (Deemed-to-be) University, Bengaluru, offers a range of online postgraduate certificate programs designed to equip professionals with industry-relevant skills. These programs blend academic rigor with practical application, preparing learners for evolving career opportunities.

## Key takeaways

*   Jainx Academy provides specialized online postgraduate certificate courses in high-demand fields.
*   Programs typically run for 12 months and include practical components like live projects or industrial training.
*   Each course is priced at ₹1,20,000 and focuses on developing specific, career-oriented skills.
*   Instruction is often delivered by industry experts, ensuring real-world relevance.

## Specialized Postgraduate Certificate Programs

Jainx Academy currently offers several distinct postgraduate certificate programs, each tailored to a specific industry need. These courses are structured to provide comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience in their respective domains.

### Predictive Business Analytics

This 12-month postgraduate program focuses on teaching individuals how to manage, process, and streamline large datasets to enhance organizational decision-making. The curriculum, designed by Jainx Academy, provides exposure to various statistical software crucial for predictive analytics. Students will learn to utilize advanced models like R and Python for data analysis, applicable across sectors such as healthcare, finance, retail, hospitality, manufacturing, and aerospace.

**Skills Covered:** Business analytics, Google Analytics knowledge.

### Design for Business

This certification, also a 12-month online course, emphasizes design techniques and methods for creating rapid prototypes from initial concepts to drive business initiatives. With 750 hours of interactive online sessions, the program offers experiential learning. Taught by industry professionals like Amit Mirchandani (Design Director, Lucid) and Saravana Mani (Global Head - Innovation/Accelerator, Kmart Group Australia), it includes a three-month live project for industrial exposure and technical expertise with software tools.

**Skills Covered:** Designing skills, rapid prototyping, UI knowledge, econometrics, design thinking.

### Health Tech

Addressing the convergence of healthcare and technology, this 12-month online postgraduate certificate explores the application of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and robotics within the healthcare sector. The 750-hour program includes a three-month industrial training component through live projects. Instructors, including Divya Hegde (Partner) and Manoj S (Co-Founder, Marble AI), bring their industry insights to guide students. Upon completion, participants receive a course certificate and career support.

**Skills Covered:** Healthcare sciences knowledge, technical skills, AI knowledge, cybersecurity knowledge, robotic skills.

## Program Structure and Fees

All postgraduate certificate programs offered by Jainx Academy share a similar structure and fee model:

*   **Duration:** 12 Months
*   **Total Learning Hours:** Approximately 750 hours (for some programs)
*   **Practical Experience:** Includes 3 months of live projects or industrial training.
*   **Fees:** ₹1,20,000 per program

These programs are delivered entirely online, providing flexibility for working professionals and students alike. The curriculum is consistently updated to reflect current industry demands, ensuring that the skills acquired are highly relevant.

For specific admission timelines and application procedures, candidates are encouraged to refer to the official Jainx Academy portal.
