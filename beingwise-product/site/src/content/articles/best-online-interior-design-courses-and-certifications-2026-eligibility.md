# Best Online Interior Design Courses & Certifications 2026 - Eligibility, Fees, Syllabus, Scope

Embarking on a career in interior design offers a dynamic path for creative individuals. This guide explores some excellent online and certificate programs that can help you build the skills needed to succeed in this exciting field.

## Key takeaways

*   Several reputable institutions offer certificate courses in interior design, often with practical components.
*   Programs vary in duration, from a few months to nearly a year, catering to different learning paces.
*   Eligibility typically requires a Class XII qualification, making these courses accessible to a broad audience.
*   Curricula often blend theoretical knowledge with hands-on skills, including CAD software and architectural planning.

## Certificate in Interior Design & Architecture Planning (SOL DU)

The Campus of Open Learning (COL), Delhi University, in collaboration with RK Academy of Arts and Design (RKAAD), offers a comprehensive Certificate in Interior Design & Architecture Planning. This program is designed to equip learners with the expertise required in the rapidly growing interior design and architectural planning sector. It focuses on developing both the creative and technical abilities essential for professional success.

**Course Details:**

*   **Duration:** 10 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹45,000
*   **Eligibility & Admission:** Interested candidates need to complete an application form, attach necessary documents, and submit them to COL-DU. Admissions are based on a screening process.
*   **Syllabus Highlights:** The course covers both theoretical principles and practical applications of interior design and architectural planning, delivered by RKAAD experts.
*   **Certification:** Upon successful completion, a certificate is jointly awarded by RKKAD and COL-DU.

## Certificate in Interior Design & CAD (SOL DU)

Another valuable offering from the University of Delhi (DU), in partnership with R.K. Films & Media Academy (RKFMA), is the Certificate in Interior Design & CAD. This program emphasizes the scientific understanding of human behavior in relation to space, helping students create functional and aesthetically pleasing interiors. It delves into interior architecture, working closely with architectural concepts, and gaining experience relevant to the construction field.

**Course Details:**

*   **Duration:** 6 Months
*   **Fees:** ₹20,000
*   **Eligibility & Admission:** Details are similar to the Interior Design & Architecture Planning course, involving application submission and a screening process.
*   **Syllabus Highlights:** Key areas include understanding interior architecture, style inspiration, color schemes, texture, and functionality. A significant part of the course introduces freehand sketching and Computer-Aided Design (CAD), covering basic CAD commands, tools, multi-view drawing, and dimensioning techniques. The program also focuses on developing detailed execution drawings and basic knowledge of furnishing residential and other spaces.
*   **Certification:** A joint certification is provided by the University of Delhi and RKFMA upon successful completion, which includes a comprehensive examination.

## Certificate Course In Expert Professional Interior Design (George Institute of Interior Design, Pune)

The George Institute of Interior Design in Pune offers a focused Certificate Course In Expert Professional Interior Design. This program is structured to provide students with a strong foundation in the core principles of interior design.

**Course Details:**

*   **Duration:** 4 Months
*   **Eligibility:** Applicants must have qualified Class XII.
*   **Syllabus Highlights:** The curriculum includes essential elements of design such as colors, design principles, and geometric projections (e.g., cube, cuboid, table, chair). The institute aims to equip students with practical skills and knowledge, guided by industry experts.

## Interior Design Certificate Course Online (Upskillist)

Upskillist provides an online professional design course under its 'Continuing Education' category, titled Interior Design Certificate Course Online. This program is certified by the AP State University and holds global recognition, offering flexibility for learners worldwide.

**Course Details:**

*   **Format:** Online
*   **Certification:** Certified by AP State University and recognized internationally.
*   **Syllabus Highlights:** While specific details vary, online courses typically cover design fundamentals, color theory, space planning, materials, and digital design tools, allowing students to learn at their own pace.

Choosing the right interior design program depends on your career aspirations, time commitment, and budget. These certificate courses offer excellent pathways to acquire specialized skills and enter the dynamic world of interior design.
