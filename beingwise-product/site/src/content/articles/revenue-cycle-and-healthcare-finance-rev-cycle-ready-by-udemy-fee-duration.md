# Revenue Cycle and Healthcare Finance - Rev Cycle Ready by Udemy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding the financial aspects of healthcare is crucial for anyone looking to build a career in this dynamic sector. The "Rev Cycle Ready" course offers a comprehensive look into healthcare revenue cycle management, designed to equip you with essential skills.

## Key Takeaways

*   This course focuses on the intricacies of healthcare revenue cycle management.
*   It covers vital areas like Relative Value Units (RVUs), Accounts Receivable (AR), and managing write-offs and denials.
*   The learning format is self-paced, utilizing video and text-based materials.
*   Upon successful completion, a certificate is provided by the course platform.

## Course Overview

This program is structured to provide a deep dive into the revenue cycle within healthcare finance. It breaks down complex topics into digestible modules, allowing learners to grasp the financial flow from patient registration to payment collection. The course is delivered entirely in English, making it accessible to a wide audience.

## Learning Format and Duration

The "Rev Cycle Ready" course is designed for self-study, offering flexibility to learners. You can progress at your own pace through a combination of video lectures and supporting text materials. Since it's a self-paced online course, there isn't a fixed duration; learners can complete it according to their individual schedules and commitment.

## Course Fees and Certification

The fee for enrolling in this comprehensive program is ₹3,099. Upon successfully completing all modules and requirements, participants will receive a certificate. This certification is issued by the platform hosting the course, acknowledging your proficiency in healthcare revenue cycle management.

## Detailed Syllabus

The course is divided into several key modules, each addressing a specific component of the revenue cycle:

### Rev Cycle 101

*   An introductory module setting the foundation for understanding the revenue cycle.

### RVU Ready

This section delves into Relative Value Units, a critical component in healthcare reimbursement.

*   Introduction to RVUs, including an accompanying Excel file for practical application.
*   Detailed RVU Calculation methods.
*   Understanding Locality Modifiers and their impact.
*   Application of CPT Modifiers.
*   Connecting RVU to actual Payment processes.
*   Insights into Billing Transactions.
*   Performing RVU Analysis to optimize financial outcomes.

### AR Ready

This module focuses on Accounts Receivable within healthcare.

*   Introduction and Background to AR, supported by an Excel file.
*   A thorough explanation of AR concepts.
*   Understanding Data Explanations relevant to AR.
*   Guidance on how to Build Pivot Tables for data analysis.
*   Identifying and utilizing AR Key Metrics.
*   Performing an AR Self-Assessment to gauge understanding.

### Write-Off Ready

This section addresses the management of write-offs and adjustments.

*   Introduction to Write-Offs and understanding Unavoidable Adjustments, with an Excel file.
*   Identifying and managing Avoidable Adjustments.
*   Self-Assessment #1 to test knowledge.
*   Conducting Root Cause Analysis - By Specialty.
*   Self-Assessment #2.
*   Performing Root Cause Analysis - By Department.
*   Self-Assessment #3.

### Denials Ready

The final module focuses on understanding and managing claim denials.

*   Introduction to Denials, including an Excel file for practical exercises.
*   Clear Denial Definitions.
*   Analyzing Denial Data.
*   Reviewing a Denial Example.
*   Understanding Denials GCR (Gross Collection Ratio).
*   Calculating and interpreting the Denials Rate.

This course is an excellent resource for anyone aiming to enhance their skills in healthcare finance and revenue cycle management, offering practical knowledge and a clear path to certification.
