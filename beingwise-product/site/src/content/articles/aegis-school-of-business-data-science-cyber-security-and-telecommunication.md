# Aegis School of Business Data Science Cyber Security and Telecommunication Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Aegis School of Business Data Science Cyber Security and Telecommunication Courses

Aegis School of Business, Data Science, Cyber Security, and Telecommunication offers a range of specialized programs designed to equip professionals and aspiring leaders with cutting-edge skills in high-demand fields. These programs often feature collaborations with industry giants like IBM, providing a blend of academic rigor and practical industry insights.

## Key takeaways

*   Aegis offers specialized postgraduate and executive programs in data science, business analytics, and telecommunication management.
*   Many programs are developed in association with IBM, integrating industry expertise into the curriculum.
*   Courses are often delivered in a hybrid format, combining online learning with in-person sessions at their Mumbai center.
*   The curriculum is designed to foster both technical and leadership skills, preparing students for strategic roles.

## Hybrid EPGP in Data Science, Business Analytics, and Big Data (in association with IBM)

This 12-month Hybrid Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP) is a pioneering offering from Aegis School and IBM. Recognized as one of India's first high-end data science programs, it provides a comprehensive curriculum covering data science, business analytics, and big data.

The program's hybrid structure involves weekday classes conducted at the Centre for Excellence in Telecom Technology and Management (CETTM) in Mumbai, complemented by online sessions on Sundays. This format allows for both immersive classroom learning and the flexibility of remote access.

The curriculum is uniquely structured with a mix of core and elective courses, designed to develop crucial skills for a successful career in business analytics and big data. Graduates gain strong technical and quantitative abilities, empowering them to create business and social value across various industries. Participants also gain exposure to a range of industry-standard tools and software.

**Skills Covered:** Data Science, Business Analytics, Big Data.

## Post Graduate Program in Telecommunication Management (in association with IBM)

This 11-month certificate course is a collaboration between Aegis and IBM, distinguished as a leading program in Telecommunication Management. It has benefited executives from over 35 countries, including those from telecom service providers and vendors.

The program is tailored to help current and future telecom leaders understand the operational aspects of information technology and telecommunication from critical leadership perspectives, including finance, management, and legal viewpoints. Candidates who complete this program are well-prepared to become technology managers, confident in making strategic decisions that benefit the telecom management sector. The course also allows for the development of specific functional skills relevant to individual domains.

**Skills Covered:** IT Industry Knowledge, Telecommunication Management.

## Executive Masters Program in Telecom Management (in association with IBM Online Live)

Jointly delivered by Aegis and IBM, this 11-month Executive Masters Program is an online live offering considered equivalent to an MBA in Telecommunication in India. It is specifically designed for executives and professionals in the telecom sector.

The program has a strong industry foundation, with its syllabus developed using inputs and suggestions from major telecom companies such as Qualcomm, Bharti Airtel, Amdocs, Ericsson, ZTE, Reliance Communication, and BSNL. This ensures the curriculum addresses the current and future needs of telecom managers and leaders.

This program aims to cultivate future leaders in the information technology and telecommunication world. It continuously benefits executives from around 35 countries, including those working with telecom service providers and vendors, by enhancing their strategic and operational capabilities.

## Conclusion

Aegis School, through its partnerships with industry leaders like IBM, offers robust and relevant programs that cater to the evolving demands of the data science, business analytics, and telecommunication sectors. These courses are ideal for professionals seeking to advance their careers with specialized knowledge and leadership skills.
