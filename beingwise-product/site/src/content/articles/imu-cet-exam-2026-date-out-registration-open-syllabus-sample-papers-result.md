# IMU CET Exam 2026 - Date (Out), Registration (Open), Syllabus, Sample Papers, Result, Cutoff

The Indian Maritime University Common Entrance Test (IMU CET) is a crucial national-level examination for students aspiring to pursue a career in the maritime industry. This exam facilitates admission to a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs offered by IMU and its affiliated institutions.

## Key Takeaways

*   IMU CET 2026 registration commenced on February 6, 2026, on the official IMU website.
*   The application deadline for IMU CET 2026 is April 24, 2026.
*   The examination for IMU CET 2026 is scheduled for May 24, 2026.
*   Eligibility requires a minimum of 60% in PCM in 10+2, plus 50% in English in 10th and 12th grades.

## What is the IMU CET Exam?

The IMU CET is a computer-based test (CBT) conducted by the Indian Maritime University. It serves as the gateway for admissions to numerous maritime courses, including B.Tech in Marine Engineering, B.Sc in Nautical Science, MBA in Port and Shipping Management, and more. Success in this exam is essential for securing a spot in DGS-approved Marine Training Institutes (MTIs) as well as IMU's own campuses.

## Important Dates for IMU CET 2026

Keeping track of key dates is vital for a smooth application process. Here are the confirmed dates for IMU CET 2026:

*   **Registration Start Date:** February 6, 2026
*   **Registration End Date:** April 24, 2026
*   **Examination Date:** May 24, 2026

Candidates should regularly check the official IMU portal for any announcements or updates regarding these dates.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the IMU CET 2026, candidates must meet specific academic requirements:

*   **10+2 Marks:** A minimum aggregate of 60% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) in their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination.
*   **English Language:** A minimum of 50% marks in English in both Class 10 and Class 12 examinations.

Meeting these criteria is a prerequisite for applying to the exam.

## IMU CET Syllabus and Exam Pattern

For B.Tech programs, the IMU CET comprises 150 multiple-choice questions covering five core subjects:

*   Physics
*   Chemistry
*   Mathematics
*   English
*   General Aptitude

It is highly recommended that candidates refer to the official IMU CET syllabus for detailed topic breakdowns to guide their preparation. Understanding the exam pattern, including question types and marking scheme, is also crucial for effective study.

## Preparing with Sample Papers

Attempting IMU CET sample papers is an excellent way to familiarize yourself with the examination structure and identify important topics and chapters that have been frequently tested in previous years. This practice can significantly enhance your preparation strategy and time management skills during the actual exam.

## Admission Process and Types of CETs

The admission process to various maritime courses through IMU involves different types of Common Entrance Tests (CETs) or other national-level exams, depending on the program. Here's a breakdown:

| For Admissions to Courses                         | Process of Admission   | CET Name / Basis                                   | Basis for Admission         |
| :------------------------------------------------ | :--------------------- | :------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------- |
| B.Sc(NS), B.Tech(ME), B.Tech(NAOE), DNS (Aug/Feb) | By CET                 | IMU-CET for UG – Technical Courses & CUET (UG)     | Merit in CET                |
| MBA-PSM & MBA-ITLM                                | By CET                 | IMU-CET for MBA Programmes / CAT / MAT / CMAT / UG | Merit in CET                |
