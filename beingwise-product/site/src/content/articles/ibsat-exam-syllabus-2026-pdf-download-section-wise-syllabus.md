# IBSAT Exam Syllabus 2026 PDF: Download Section-wise Syllabus

Preparing for the IBSAT 2026 examination requires a clear understanding of the syllabus. This page will guide you through the key topics you need to cover for each section to help you prepare effectively.

## Key takeaways

*   The IBSAT 2026 syllabus covers Verbal Ability, Reading Comprehension, Quantitative Aptitude, and Data Adequacy/Sufficiency & Data Interpretation.
*   Unlike some other MBA entrance exams, IBSAT does not include a General Awareness section.
*   The exam will be conducted in the last week of December 2026, using a remote proctored format.
*   There is no negative marking for incorrect answers in the IBSAT exam.

## Understanding the IBSAT 2026 Syllabus

The IBSAT syllabus is structured into four main sections, designed to assess different aptitudes crucial for management studies. While similar in many aspects to other national-level MBA entrance exams, it has its unique focus areas. Notably, the Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension components are treated as distinct sections within the exam structure.

## IBSAT 2026 Exam Pattern

Familiarity with the IBSAT exam pattern is just as important as knowing the syllabus. This will help you understand the structure of the question paper and manage your time effectively during the examination.

*   The exam consists of 140 objective-type questions.
*   Each question carries one mark.
*   The total duration for the examination is 120 minutes.
*   There are no specific time limits for individual sections.
*   Crucially, there is no negative marking for incorrect answers, encouraging candidates to attempt all questions.
