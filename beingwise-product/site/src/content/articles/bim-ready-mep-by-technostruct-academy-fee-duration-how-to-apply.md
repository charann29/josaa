# BIM Ready (MEP) by TechnoStruct Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For engineering students and professionals looking to specialize in the intersection of Building Information Modeling (BIM) and Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) systems, the BIM Ready (MEP) certification from TechnoStruct Academy offers a focused online program. This course is designed to equip participants with both theoretical understanding and practical skills in this vital area of modern construction and design.

## Key Takeaways

*   This online certification course is specifically tailored for mechanical and electrical engineering students and professionals.
*   It offers a blend of live online sessions and practical exercises over 45 days.
*   Participants gain hands-on experience with industry-standard BIM software tools.
*   The program includes opportunities for free internships and direct interaction with BIM professionals.

## Course Overview

The BIM Ready (MEP) program aims to transform participants into skilled BIM MEP engineering experts. Delivered entirely online, the curriculum spans 45 days, featuring 30 hours of live instruction and an additional 30 hours dedicated to practical, hands-on sessions. The medium of instruction is English, and the learning format combines self-study with virtual classroom interaction, utilizing video and text-based resources.

## What You Will Learn

The syllabus is structured to enhance fundamental knowledge of building information modeling within the MEP context. Key learning areas include developing competence in creating various engineering drawings such as shop drawings, spool drawings, and construction drawings with precise specifications. The course also covers core concepts in the design of Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC), drainage and water supply systems, electrical services, and fire fighting services. Beyond traditional 3D modeling, students will delve into advanced topics like visual programming, 4D, 5D, and 6D modeling, alongside their practical applications.

## Software and Practical Experience

A significant component of the program is hands-on training with a range of essential BIM software tools. Participants will gain proficiency in AUTO CAD 2D & 3D, AUTO-CAD MEP, REVIT MEP, DYNAMO, NAVISWORKS, Autodesk BIM 360, and Scan to BIM. This practical exposure is further bolstered by opportunities for free internships, allowing participants to work alongside experienced BIM professionals and gain invaluable real-world experience.

## Fees and Certification

The fee for the BIM Ready (MEP) certification program is ₹50,000 plus Goods and Services Tax (GST). Payment can be made through the official TechnoStruct website after registration. Upon successful completion of the course study, participants will receive a certificate from TechnoStruct Academy, validating their newly acquired professional skillsets in BIM MEP engineering.

## Eligibility and Application Process

This program is ideal for individuals with a background in mechanical or electrical engineering, including current students and working professionals looking to upskill. To apply, interested candidates should visit the TechnoStruct Academy website. The admission process involves registering online, where applicants will need to provide personal details such as their name, phone number, email address, educational background, and country. They will also select the course name and relevant subject during the enrollment process.

This certification can significantly benefit those aiming to build a career or advance their existing roles in the dynamic field of BIM for mechanical and electrical engineering.
