# IIM Kozhikode Selection Criteria 2026-2028: PI Shortlist Calls Out, Check Status and CAT Cut Offs

IIM Kozhikode has officially released the shortlist for Personal Interview (PI) rounds for its Post Graduate Program (PGP) for the academic years 2026-2028. Candidates who applied to IIM Kozhikode through their CAT 2025 application can now check their PI shortlist status.

## Key Takeaways

*   The PI shortlist for IIM Kozhikode's PGP 2026-2028 admissions was released on January 1, 2026.
*   A minimum CAT 2025 percentile of 85 is generally required for General category candidates to qualify for the PI round.
*   The final selection process includes significant weightage for the Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds, along with academic records and a resume score.
*   Applicants must have a minimum of 60% marks in their graduation to be eligible for the PGP program.

## IIM Kozhikode Admission Process: Step-by-Step

Admissions to IIM Kozhikode's flagship PGP program follow a structured three-stage process:

1.  **Stage 1: Initial Registration based on CAT Score:** Candidates who meet the preliminary CAT cut-offs and other eligibility criteria register their interest in IIM Kozhikode.
2.  **Stage 2: Shortlisting for Personal Interview (PI):** Based on CAT scores, academic performance, and other factors, a list of candidates is shortlisted for the subsequent Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds.
3.  **Stage 3: Final Selection for Admission:** After the WAT and PI, a comprehensive evaluation leads to the final selection of candidates for admission to the PGP.

## IIM Kozhikode CAT Cut Offs 2026

The Common Admission Test (CAT) score is a crucial initial filter for IIM Kozhikode admissions. For the General category, the qualifying CAT percentile for shortlisting for the PI round is 85. It's important to note that these are minimum qualifying cut-offs, and actual scores for final admissions can be higher depending on the applicant pool and other selection criteria.

## Shortlisting for PI: What's Evaluated?

To be shortlisted for the Personal Interview (PI) round, IIM Kozhikode considers several factors beyond just the CAT score. These typically include:

*   **CAT 2025 Percentile:** Meeting or exceeding the category-wise cut-offs is essential.
*   **Academic Record:** Performance in 10th standard, 12th standard, and undergraduate degree is assessed. A minimum of 60% in graduation is a general eligibility requirement.
*   **Work Experience:** Relevant work experience, if any, is also considered.
*   **Diversity Factors:** IIMs often consider academic and gender diversity to ensure a well-rounded batch.

## Final Selection Criteria for PGP 2026-2028

The final admission to IIM Kozhikode's PGP is determined by a composite score that gives weightage to various components. For the 2026-2028 batch, the weightage distribution is as follows:

| Component          | Weightage (Points) |
| :----------------- | :----------------- |
| CAT Score          | 35                 |
| WAT & PI           | 55                 |
| Resume/Profile Score | 10                 |
| **Total**          | **100**            |

This distribution highlights the significant importance placed on the Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) performance in the final selection process.

For more detailed information, candidates are advised to refer to the official IIM Kozhikode admissions portal.
