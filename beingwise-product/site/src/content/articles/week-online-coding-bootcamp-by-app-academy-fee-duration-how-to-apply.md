# 24 Week Online Coding Bootcamp by App Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone passionate about a career in software engineering, App Academy offers a comprehensive 24-week online bootcamp designed to equip you with essential programming skills. This immersive program allows you to learn from the comfort of your home, focusing on practical knowledge crucial for a successful software developer.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a full-time, online software engineering program spanning 24 weeks.
*   No prior coding experience is required, as the course includes preparatory work.
*   The program emphasizes practical skills, portfolio building through projects, and career support.
*   App Academy offers an Income Share Agreement (ISA), allowing students to defer tuition payments until they secure a high-paying job.

## Program Overview

The 24 Week Software Engineering Immersive program is meticulously designed to transform aspiring individuals into proficient software engineers. The curriculum dives deep into practical lessons covering popular languages like Python and JavaScript, alongside fundamental web development concepts. Throughout the course, you'll engage in four individual projects and numerous group assignments, which are vital for building a robust professional portfolio.

The program begins with essential preparatory work, ensuring all participants have a solid foundation before tackling more advanced topics. You'll be part of a supportive learning community, collaborating with fellow students on group projects. Additionally, you'll receive dedicated guidance from various support staff, including a career advisor, a cohort mentor, module instructors, and teaching assistants, all committed to your success.

## What You Will Learn

This intensive program covers a broad spectrum of technical skills, ensuring you are well-rounded and job-ready. The curriculum is structured to build knowledge progressively, starting from core programming principles and moving towards advanced web development techniques. Key areas of learning include:

*   **Programming Fundamentals:** JavaScript, Node, Git and GitHub, Command Line
*   **Computer Science Concepts:** Sorting algorithms, data structures, Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), testing methodologies
*   **Web Development Basics:** HTTP protocols, server interactions, HTML, CSS, responsive design, Flexbox, deployment tools like Netlify and Heroku
*   **Back-End Engineering:** Express, Python, SQL, Flask, SQL Alchemy
*   **Front-End Engineering:** ReactJS
*   **Web Application Development:** Skills to build and accelerate projects using modern frameworks.

## Course Structure and Support

The 24-week program is delivered through a combination of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content, with classes typically held on weekdays. The full-time nature of the course ensures an immersive learning experience. App Academy provides a comprehensive support system, including:

*   **Prep Work:** Initial modules to get you up to speed with necessary programming skills.
*   **Built-in Support:** Access to technical instructors, teaching assistants, and cohort mentors.
*   **Collaborative Learning:** Opportunities for pair programming and group reviews with classmates.
*   **Career Services:** Guidance from a dedicated career advisor to help with job placement and interview preparation.
*   **Project-Based Learning:** Focus on building a strong portfolio through practical, real-world projects.

## Fees and Payment Options

App Academy offers flexible payment options for its 24 Week Software Engineering Immersive program. The standard upfront tuition fee is \$15,900. However, a distinctive feature of this program is its Income Share Agreement (ISA) model.

With the ISA, you have the option to pay no tuition upfront. Instead, you agree to pay a percentage of your income once you secure a high-paying job after completing the program. This model aligns App Academy's success with yours, making quality coding education accessible without immediate financial burden. There is also a month-to-month tuition option at \$3,000 per month.

| Head                | Amount in USD     |
| :------------------ | :---------------- |
| Upfront Tuition     | \$15,900          |
| Month-to-Month Tuition | \$3,000/month     |

It's important to note that while the program provides comprehensive training, it does not issue a formal certificate upon completion.

## Eligibility and Application Process

The App Academy 24 Week Software Engineering Immersive program is open to individuals without any prior coding experience or specific prerequisites. The initial prep work is designed to teach all necessary programming fundamentals. The application process involves several steps:

1.  **Online Application Form:** Begin by accessing and completing the application form on the official App Academy website. You'll need to provide personal details such as your full name, email ID, date of birth, address, ZIP code, phone number, and LinkedIn URL.
2.  **Non-Technical Assessments:** Complete a series of non-technical evaluations, which may include tests for logical reasoning, personality traits, and typing speed.
3.  **Technical Video Interview:** Participate in a 25-minute video interview focusing on technical aspects.
4.  **Technical Challenges:** Upon approval, you will gain access to technical challenges covering areas like HTML/CSS, Git, and Boolean logic. Successful completion of these challenges is required for enrollment.

By following these steps, you can embark on your journey to becoming a skilled software engineer with App Academy.
