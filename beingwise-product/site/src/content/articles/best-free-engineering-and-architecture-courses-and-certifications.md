## Advances in Agricultural Engineering (IIT Kharagpur)

Agriculture is a cornerstone of the Indian economy, and staying current with advancements in this sector is vital for sustainable national development. This certification program from IIT Kharagpur acts as a refresher, providing insights into the latest developments in agricultural engineering.

Developed by experienced faculty, including teachers, engineers, and agricultural scientists from IIT Kharagpur, this course is also an AICTE-approved Faculty Development Programme (FDP). The curriculum is structured into five sections, each involving approximately four hours of video lectures and an additional four hours for self-study. Participants will gain practical understanding through various case studies. This is a core component of the Annual Refresher Programme in Teaching (ARPIT) and concludes with an examination.

*   **Duration:** 16 Weeks
*   **Fees:** Free
*   **Skills Covered:** Modern agricultural practices and engineering principles.

## Numerical Methods in Civil Engineering (IIT Bombay)

This online course, offered by IIT Bombay via Swayam, introduces students to essential numerical methods used in civil engineering. The syllabus covers a range of topics crucial for analyzing and solving complex engineering problems.

Key areas include collocation methods, the method of Galerkin, various finite difference schemes, the method of weighted residuals, implicit and explicit methods, interpolation and extrapolation, solutions for linear and nonlinear equations, numerical approximation, curve fitting, error analysis, and numerical differentiation and integration. This 16-week program focuses on helping students grasp fundamental principles. Upon completion, participants will be better equipped to analyze and evaluate the accuracy and applicability of various numerical methods in real-world civil engineering scenarios.

*   **Duration:** 16 Weeks
*   **Fees:** Free
*   **Skills Covered:** Core civil engineering numerical analysis techniques.

## Fundamental Concepts of Electricity (IIT Bombay)

Also offered by IIT Bombay through Swayam, this 16-week online course delves into the fundamental concepts of electricity. It's designed to clarify essential topics for students and professionals alike.

The curriculum covers basic physical laws, mutually coupled circuits and transformers, single-phase and three-phase transformers, circuit elements, sinusoidal steady-state response for both single-phase and three-phase systems, natural transients, steady-state response, frequency response of circuits, and power supply systems. The program employs experimental demonstrations, real-life examples, analysis, and illustrations to enhance understanding. It also provides practice sets and a live discussion series. This is an AICTE-approved FDP course.

*   **Duration:** 16 Weeks
*   **Fees:** Free
*   **Skills Covered:** Foundational knowledge of electrical circuits and power systems.

## Material Science (IGNOU)

This 16-week online course from IGNOU explores the fascinating field of material science. It helps students understand different types of materials, their properties, and the crucial process of selecting the right material for specific applications.

The course emphasizes the multidisciplinary nature of material science, highlighting its significant impact across various branches of science and engineering. Participants will learn how material properties influence design and functionality in diverse contexts.

*   **Duration:** 16 Weeks
*   **Fees:** Free
*   **Skills Covered:** Material properties, selection criteria, and their engineering applications.

These free online courses provide an excellent opportunity to expand your knowledge and skills in key engineering disciplines, directly from some of India's most respected educational institutions.
