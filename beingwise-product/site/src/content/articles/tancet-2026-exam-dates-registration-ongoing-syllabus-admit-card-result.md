# TANCET 2026: Exam Dates, Registration Ongoing, Syllabus, Admit Card, Result, Cutoff & Counselling

The Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test (TANCET) is a vital examination for students aspiring to pursue postgraduate degrees like MBA, MCA, and M.E./M.Tech/M.Arch/M.Plan in various colleges across Tamil Nadu. Conducted by Anna University, Chennai, this exam is the gateway to numerous reputable institutions.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Registration Started**: The application window for TANCET 2026 opened on March 16, 2026, following the official notification released on March 10, 2026.
*   **Application Deadline**: Prospective candidates must complete their registration and application submission by April 15, 2026.
*   **Online Process**: The entire application process, including registration, form filling, document upload, and fee payment, is conducted online.
*   **Eligibility Check**: Always review the eligibility criteria carefully before applying to ensure your application is not rejected later.

## TANCET 2026 Registration Process

Anna University has initiated the registration phase for TANCET 2026. Eligible students can access the application portal through the official TANCET website. It's crucial to complete all steps accurately before the deadline.

### Documents Required for Application

Before you begin filling out the form, ensure you have the following documents and details readily available:

*   A valid personal email address and mobile phone number.
*   Your Class 12 (or equivalent) registration/roll number and year of passing.
*   Your Diploma registration/roll number and year of passing (if applicable).
*   A recent passport-sized photograph in JPG format.
*   A scanned image of your signature in JPG format.
*   A copy of your community certificate in PDF format (mandatory for SC, ST, and SCA categories in Tamil Nadu to avail fee concessions).
*   Details for online payment: Credit card, debit card, or net banking information.

### Step-by-Step Application Guide

1.  **Online Registration**: Start by providing basic information such as your name, gender, date of birth, email ID, and a chosen password to create your account.
2.  **Fill Application Form**: Log in using your registered email ID and password. Proceed to fill in all required details, including your chosen TANCET entrance test, personal information, communication address, and academic qualifications.
3.  **Upload Photograph**: Upload your scanned passport-sized photograph. Ensure it is of good quality and within the specified file size (typically 20 KB to 30 KB).
4.  **Fee Payment**: Pay the application fee online using net banking, a debit card, or a credit card.
    *   For General/OBC categories: ₹1000
    *   For SC/ST/SCA categories (Tamil Nadu residents): ₹500
    *   *Note*: To qualify for the reduced fee, SC, ST, and SCA candidates from Tamil Nadu must upload their community certificate.
5.  **Submit Application**: Carefully review all the information entered in your application form. Once satisfied, submit the form and make sure to take at least two printouts for your records.

## TANCET 2026 Syllabus and Exam Pattern

While the official details for TANCET 2026 will be released, the syllabus generally covers core areas relevant to the chosen postgraduate program. For MBA and MCA, sections often include Quantitative Ability, Analytical Reasoning, Logical Reasoning, Computer Awareness, and Reading Comprehension. For M.E./M.Tech/M.Arch/M.Plan, the syllabus is typically subject-specific, testing knowledge in the relevant engineering or architecture discipline. Candidates should refer to the official TANCET website for the precise syllabus and exam pattern once it's published.

## TANCET 2026 Admit Card and Results

The TANCET admit card is an essential document for appearing in the examination. It will be made available for download on the official TANCET website a few weeks before the exam date. Students must download and print their admit card, as entry to the examination hall will not be permitted without it. Following the examination, results will also be declared online, and candidates can check their scores using their login credentials. Keep an eye on the official portal for specific dates regarding admit card release and result declaration.

## TANCET 2026 Cutoff and Counselling

After the TANCET results are announced, participating institutions will release their respective cutoffs for admission. These cutoffs depend on factors like the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and available seats. Qualified candidates will then be invited for the counselling process, which is typically conducted by Anna University. Counselling involves document verification, choice filling for colleges and courses, and seat allotment based on merit and preferences. Staying updated with announcements on the official TANCET portal is crucial for navigating the cutoff and counselling stages.

We wish you the best in your TANCET 2026 preparations and application process. Keep a close watch on the official Anna University TANCET website for all the latest updates and announcements.
