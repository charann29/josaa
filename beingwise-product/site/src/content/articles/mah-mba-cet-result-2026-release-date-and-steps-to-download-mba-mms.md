# MAH MBA CET Result 2026: Release Date & Steps to Download MBA/MMS Scorecard

The MAH MBA CET is a crucial entrance examination for aspiring MBA/MMS students in Maharashtra. Understanding when and how to access your results is the first step towards securing your desired management seat.

## Key Takeaways

*   The MAH MBA CET 2026 results are anticipated in June 2026.
*   Candidates had two attempts for the exam: April and May, with results expected to be released together.
*   The scorecard will be available exclusively online on the official CET website.
*   Post-result, the MBA CAP 2026 counselling process will commence, likely in July-August 2026, for seat allocation.

## MAH MBA CET 2026 Important Dates

The MAH MBA CET 2026 examination was conducted across two attempts. Here’s a quick overview of the key dates:

| Dates               | Event                     |
| :------------------ | :------------------------ |
| April 6, 7, 8, 2026 | MAH MBA CET 2026 Exam 1   |
| May 9, 2026         | MAH MBA CET 2026 Exam 2   |
| June 2026           | MAH MBA CET 2026 Result   |
| July-August 2026    | MBA CAP 2026 Counselling |

## How to Download Your MAH MBA CET Result 2026

The MAH MBA CET MBA/MMS result and scorecard will be accessible online through the official portal. Follow these steps to download your scorecard:

1.  Visit the official CET website (cetcell.mahacet.org).
2.  Locate and click on the link for 'MAH MBA CET 2026 Result' or 'Scorecard'.
3.  Enter your registered email ID and password to log in to your candidate portal.
4.  Once logged in, click on the 'Scorecard' tab.
5.  Your MAH MBA CET 2026 result will be displayed on the screen.
6.  Download and save your scorecard for future reference and the counselling process.

## Details on Your MAH MBA CET 2026 Scorecard

The MAH MBA CET scorecard is an essential document for the admission process. It contains vital information that validates your performance in the exam. Please ensure all details are accurate. The scorecard typically includes:

*   Candidate’s Name
*   Candidate’s Date of Birth
*   Candidate’s Category
*   Exam Date and Year
*   Candidate’s MAH MBA CET 2026 Registration Number
*   Candidate’s Roll Number
*   Overall MBA CET Score
*   Overall MBA CET Percentile

**Note:** If you find any discrepancy in your personal details or information other than the scores, it is crucial to contact the examination authority immediately for rectification. The scores and percentiles mentioned on the scorecard are considered final, and no requests for changes to these will be entertained.

## Understanding MAH MBA CET Score Calculation

The MAH MBA CET score calculation involves a multi-step process to ensure fairness across all exam sessions:

*   **Raw Score Calculation:** For every correct answer, 1 mark is awarded. There is no negative marking for incorrect or unanswered questions. The total number of correct answers constitutes your 'Corrected Score' or raw score.
*   **Scaled Score Calculation:** Since the exam is conducted in multiple sessions, the difficulty level can vary. To maintain fairness, the raw scores of all candidates across different sessions are normalized. This process converts raw scores into 'Scaled Scores', ensuring that candidates are evaluated equitably regardless of their exam slot.
*   **Percentile Calculation:** Finally, the scaled scores are used to determine the percentile. Your percentile indicates the percentage of candidates who scored less than or equal to you in the exam. This is the primary metric used for admissions.

## What Happens After the MAH MBA CET Result?

Once the MAH MBA CET 2026 results are declared, the next critical phase is the Centralized Admission Process (CAP) for MBA/MMS programs. This counselling process is expected to begin in July-August 2026. During CAP, candidates will register, declare their preferences for colleges and courses, and seats will be allotted based on their MAH MBA CET scores and the cut-offs set by participating institutes. It's important to stay updated with official announcements regarding the CAP schedule and procedures.

Securing a good percentile in the MAH MBA CET is your gateway to a range of reputable management institutes across Maharashtra. Prepare well for the CAP process by researching colleges and understanding their cut-offs.
