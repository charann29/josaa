# Things to Carry / Not Carry on CMAT 2026 Exam Day: Get Complete Checklist

Preparing for the CMAT 2026 exam isn't just about studying; it also involves knowing exactly what you need to bring and what to leave at home on test day. This guide will help you ensure a smooth entry and a stress-free examination experience.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your CMAT 2026 admit card and an original photo ID are absolutely essential for entry to the exam center.
*   Only specific types of photo identification are accepted, so double-check yours in advance.
*   Electronic devices, metallic items, and most personal belongings are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall.
*   Diabetic candidates have specific allowances for carrying certain food items.

## Essential Documents for CMAT 2026

To gain entry to the CMAT 2026 examination hall, candidates must present specific documents for verification. It's crucial to carry the original copies of these items, as photocopies or digital versions will not be accepted.

### Mandatory Documents:

*   **CMAT 2026 Admit Card:** You need two printouts of your admit card, each on an A4 size sheet, and ensure the self-declaration (undertaking) section is properly filled out.
*   **Passport-sized Photograph:** Bring one recent passport-sized photograph. Ideally, this should be the same photo you uploaded during your CMAT application process. This photo will be affixed to the attendance sheet at the center.
*   **Original Photo Identification:** You must present one valid, non-expired, original photo ID from the list below:
    *   PAN Card
    *   Passport
    *   Aadhaar Card (with photograph)
    *   E-Aadhaar (with photograph)
    *   Class 12 Board Admit Card (with photograph)
    *   Bank Passbook (with photograph)
    *   School Identity Card
*   **PwD/PwBD Certificate (if applicable):** Candidates applying under the Persons with Disability category must carry their original certificate issued by a recognized medical authority.

### Unacceptable ID Proofs:

Some forms of identification are explicitly not accepted at the CMAT center. Please do not bring:

*   ID cards issued by schools, colleges, universities, or coaching centers.
*   Aadhaar enrollment receipts without a proper Aadhaar number.
*   Digital copies or photocopies of any ID proofs.

## Other Permitted Items

Beyond your essential documents, there are very few other items allowed inside the examination hall.

*   **Blue/Black Ballpoint Pen:** You will need this for any rough work or signing purposes. No other stationery is permitted.
*   **Sugar Tablets/Fruits:** Only candidates with diabetes are permitted to carry sugar tablets or whole fruits like a banana, apple, or orange. This is a specific allowance for medical reasons.

## Prohibited Items at the CMAT Center

To maintain the integrity of the examination, a strict list of items is prohibited. Carrying any of these can lead to disqualification.

*   **Electronic Devices:** This includes mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, iPads, pagers, headphones, or any other electronic gadgets.
*   **Jewellery and Metallic Items:** Avoid wearing any metallic accessories or jewellery.
*   **Bags and Valuables:** Exam centers typically do not have provisions for storing personal belongings. It's best to avoid bringing bags, wallets, or other valuables.
*   **Food and Beverages:** Apart from the specific allowance for diabetic candidates, no other eatables or drinks are allowed.
*   **Stationery (other than pen):** Do not bring pencils, scales, geometry boxes, or any other stationery items.
*   **Rough Paper:** All necessary rough sheets will be provided at the center.
*   **Specific Clothing Items:** Caps, headgear, goggles, hoodies, and closed shoes are generally not permitted. Avoid shirts or pants with multiple pockets.

Remember, the examination authorities, NTA, are very strict about these guidelines. Adhering to them will help ensure a smooth and fair testing environment for everyone.
