# BITS BAAT Exam Dates 2026 (OUT): Check Registration, Admit Card & Result Dates

For students aspiring to pursue an MBA, the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani, conducts the BITS Admission Test (BAAT). Understanding the key dates for registration, admit card release, examination, and results is crucial for a smooth application process.

## Key Takeaways

*   The BITS BAAT 2026 examination is scheduled for March 22.
*   Admit cards were issued between March 16 and March 22, 2026, for registered candidates.
*   The second round of the admission process, including Group Discussion (GD), Personal Interview (PI), Written Ability Test (WAT), and Extempore, will take place online in April.
*   BITS BAAT scores are tentatively expected to be released in April 2026.

## BITS BAAT Registration and Form Correction Dates 2026

The BITS MBA application process involves both initial registration and a subsequent opportunity for form correction. The official schedule for these activities is published alongside the exam notification on the official BITS admissions portal.

1.  **BITS BAAT Registration:** Eligible candidates must complete their application form before the specified deadline. This involves providing accurate personal, academic, and contact information.
2.  **BITS BAAT Application Form Correction:** After submitting the application, BITS typically provides a window for candidates to review and correct any errors. This facility is available until a certain date, ensuring all submitted details are accurate.

## BITS BAAT Admit Card Dates 2026

Candidates who successfully complete their application before the deadline will receive their BITS BAAT admit card as per the announced schedule. The admit card is released online on the official website. Typically, the download link becomes active a few days before the examination date. Your BITS BAAT hall ticket will contain essential information such as the exam date, test centre address, specific exam day instructions, and your personal details provided during registration. It is mandatory to carry a printed copy of your BITS BAAT 2026 admit card to the examination centre, along with a valid photo identification proof. Test centre officials will verify these documents before allowing entry.

## BITS BAAT Exam Dates 2026

The BITS BAAT examination dates are officially released on the BITS Pilani website. All candidates who have successfully submitted their application and received an admit card are eligible to appear for the BAAT exam. The exam date is also clearly mentioned on the issued admit card. As confirmed, the BITS BAAT 2026 examination will be held on March 22.

## BITS BAAT Result Dates 2026

Following the examination, the Birla Institute of Technology and Science will release the BAAT scores. Candidates can access their BITS BAAT results directly from the official website. While the exact date is tentative, results are expected to be available in April 2026. BITS does not typically send individual notifications regarding the release of exam scores.

We encourage all aspiring candidates to regularly check the official BITS admissions portal for any updates or announcements regarding the BITS BAAT 2026 schedule.
