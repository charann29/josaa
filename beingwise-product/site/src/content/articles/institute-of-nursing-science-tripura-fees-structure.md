# Institute of Nursing Science Tripura Fees Structure

Understanding the financial commitment for higher education is a crucial step for aspiring students and their families. This guide breaks down the fee structure for the B.Sc. Nursing program at the Institute of Nursing Science, Tripura, helping you plan effectively for your academic journey.

## Key Takeaways

*   The total first-year cost, including one-time and annual fees, amounts to ₹1,31,910.
*   A significant portion of the initial payment includes refundable security deposits for laboratory and library access.
*   Annual recurring fees, primarily tuition, total ₹1,04,400.
*   Hostel and mess charges are separate, each costing ₹24,000 annually.

## One-Time Admission Charges

When you first enroll in the B.Sc. Nursing program, there are several non-refundable charges that contribute to your overall admission cost. These are paid only once at the time of joining the institute.

*   Admission Fee: ₹2,000
*   Students Welfare Fund: ₹200
*   Registration Fee: ₹310

The total for these non-refundable, one-time charges comes to ₹2,510.

## Refundable Security Deposits

In addition to the admission charges, students are required to pay certain refundable security deposits. These amounts are held by the institute and are typically returned upon successful completion of the course, provided there are no outstanding dues or damages.

*   Laboratory Security Fee: ₹15,000
*   Library Security Fee: ₹10,000

These refundable deposits total ₹25,000.

## Annual Recurring Fees

Every academic year, students will incur recurring fees that cover various aspects of their education and campus life. The largest component of this is the tuition fee.

| Fee Type            | Amount (₹) |
| :------------------ | :--------- |
| Tuition Fee         | 99,000     |
| College Magazine    | 300        |
| Examination Fee     | 2,500      |
| Games               | 300        |
| Cultural Activities | 600        |
| Library             | 500        |
| Annual Charge       | 1,200      |
| **Total Annual Fee**| **1,04,400**|

## Hostel and Mess Expenses

For students opting for on-campus accommodation, separate charges apply for hostel facilities and mess services. These are annual costs and are not included in the academic fee structure.

*   Hostel Charge: ₹24,000 per year
*   Mess Charge: ₹24,000 per year

## Total First-Year Outlay

Combining all the one-time, refundable, and annual recurring fees, the grand total for the first year of the B.Sc. Nursing program, excluding hostel and mess, is ₹1,31,910. It's worth noting that while the source mentions the possibility of paying tuition in installments, with each installment being ₹60,000, students should confirm the exact installment plan with the institute's admissions office.

This detailed breakdown should assist you in preparing financially for your B.Sc. Nursing education at the Institute of Nursing Science, Tripura.
