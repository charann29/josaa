# Microsoft Corporation Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Microsoft Corporation Courses

Microsoft Corporation offers a wide array of online courses and certifications designed to help individuals, from educators to IT professionals, enhance their skills and career prospects. These programs often combine Microsoft's industry expertise with academic rigor from leading universities and online learning platforms.

## Key takeaways

*   Microsoft collaborates with top institutions like MIT and the University of Michigan, as well as platforms like edX and Great Learning, to deliver its courses.
*   Programs cover diverse fields such as educational leadership, generative AI, and data analytics with Power BI.
*   Many courses offer official certifications from Microsoft or its partners upon successful completion.
*   Both free and paid options are available, catering to different learning needs and budgets.

## Elevating Education with Innovation

Microsoft plays a significant role in empowering educators and school leaders through specialized programs. The "Launching Innovation in Schools" course, developed in collaboration with MITx and edX, is a prime example. This intermediate-level program spans 16 weeks and is tailored for school leaders who are keen on initiating improvements in learning and teaching environments.

Participants engage in collaborative assignments and experimental activities, focusing on developing their vision, building capacity, and fostering partnerships with various stakeholders. The course aims to equip them to lead instructional improvement initiatives and see themselves as agents of change within their institutions. It's part of the broader Microsoft K-12 Education Leadership initiative, providing essential resources for schools adapting to evolving educational demands.

Another program, "Leading Change: Go Beyond Gamification with Gameful Learning," offered through edX in partnership with the University of Michigan, also targets teachers and school leaders. This course focuses on strategies and tools to integrate gameful learning techniques into educational settings, moving beyond simple gamification to create more engaging and effective learning experiences.

## Mastering Generative AI with Microsoft Azure OpenAI

For those interested in the cutting edge of artificial intelligence, Microsoft offers the "Generative AI for Business with Microsoft Azure OpenAI" program, available through Great Learning. This 16-week online course is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of generative AI principles and hands-on experience in leveraging Microsoft Azure for AI applications.

The curriculum is robust, incorporating both coding and no-code approaches on the Azure Cloud Platform. Students gain practical skills using tools such as Azure OpenAI Service, Azure OpenAI Playground, Azure OpenAI Chat API, and GPT-3.5-Turbo. Upon completion, participants receive a certificate, validating their expertise in this rapidly growing field. The fee for this program is ₹1,20,000.

## Becoming a Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst

Data analysis is a critical skill in today's job market, and Microsoft's "PL-300 - Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst Certification Training" is designed to meet this demand. This 10-week online program, also offered through Great Learning, focuses on developing expertise in data modeling, data transformations, query optimization, and the creation of interactive dashboards.

Delivered through live virtual classes by Microsoft Certified Trainers, the course includes an exam preparation guide and practical projects. It equips students with advanced skills in using Power BI for data analysis and visual representation. Successful completion leads to a joint certificate from Microsoft and Great Learning. The fee for this certification training is ₹91,600.

These diverse offerings from Microsoft Corporation provide valuable pathways for professionals across various sectors to upskill and achieve their career goals.
