# Accredian Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Accredian Courses

Accredian offers a range of online programs designed for professionals looking to advance their careers and acquire new skills. These courses, often developed in collaboration with leading institutions like IIM Visakhapatnam, provide in-depth knowledge and practical expertise across various domains.

## Key takeaways

*   Accredian provides professional certificate and diploma programs in partnership with reputable Indian institutions.
*   Courses are tailored for working professionals, featuring flexible online schedules and practical, industry-relevant content.
*   Programs cover areas like finance, human resources, and general management, aiming to develop strategic leadership and specialized skills.
*   Fees and durations vary by program, with typical durations around 12 months.

## PG Certificate Program in Investment Banking and Capital Markets

This postgraduate certificate program is crafted for individuals aspiring to build a career in the dynamic world of investment banking and capital markets. It aims to equip participants with a solid understanding of financial modeling, equity research, mergers and acquisitions, and financial advisory services.

### Program Details:

*   **Duration:** 12 months
*   **Fees:** ₹ 2,70,000
*   **Key Skills Covered:** Financial knowledge, financial management
*   **Curriculum Highlights:** The program integrates live interactive sessions, real-world case studies, and practical projects to ensure both theoretical grounding and hands-on experience. Participants will explore the intricacies of the trading lifecycle and receive guidance from experienced industry mentors.
*   **Ideal For:** Graduates, finance professionals, and those looking to transition into investment banking roles.

## Chief Human Resource Officer Programme

Designed for seasoned HR professionals, the Chief Human Resource Officer (CHRO) Programme prepares individuals for strategic leadership positions such as HR Director or Head of People Strategy. The program focuses on advanced HR management concepts and leadership frameworks, emphasizing how HR can drive business transformation.

### Program Details:

*   **Duration:** Approximately 12 months
*   **Fees:** ₹ 2,50,000
*   **Key Skills Covered:** Human resource management, strategic mindset, leadership, decision-making skills
*   **Curriculum Highlights:** This executive program features weekly live online classes, making it convenient for working professionals. It covers strategic HR leadership, digital transformation in HR, workforce analytics, organizational design, and change management through interactive sessions, case studies, and real-world HR projects.
*   **Learning Experience:** Delivered through live online platforms, the program encourages peer discussions, mentorship, and networking opportunities.

## Post Graduate Diploma in Management for Working Executives (PGDMEx)

The PGDMEx is a comprehensive program tailored for working professionals seeking to enhance their managerial knowledge and leadership capabilities without interrupting their careers. It blends theoretical frameworks with practical insights, focusing on leadership, decision-making, and strategic management.

### Program Details:

*   **Duration:** Structured to accommodate working executives.
*   **Curriculum Highlights:** The diploma features flexible learning schedules, industry-relevant case studies, and interactive sessions with experienced faculty. It aims to provide participants with actionable skills for immediate application in their professional roles.
*   **Ideal For:** Professionals aiming to advance into higher managerial or leadership positions.

Accredian's offerings are designed to empower professionals with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in today's competitive landscape, fostering career growth and leadership development.
