# TSICET 2026 Exam: Dates (OUT), Registration (with Late Fee), Syllabus PDF, Pattern, Cutoff

For students aspiring to pursue MBA or MCA programs in Telangana, the Telangana State Integrated Common Entrance Test (TSICET) is a crucial state-level examination. This computer-based test opens doors to numerous universities and affiliated colleges across the state.

## Key takeaways

*   The TSICET 2026 examination is scheduled for May 13 and 14, 2026.
*   Registration with a late fee is available until May 3, 2026, on the official TSCHE website.
*   Mahatma Gandhi University, Nalgonda, conducts TSICET on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education.
*   The exam is a 2.5-hour computer-based test with 200 objective-type questions.

## Understanding TSICET

TSICET is an annual entrance examination administered by Mahatma Gandhi University, Nalgonda, on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE). It serves as the gateway for admissions into Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Master of Computer Applications (MCA) programs offered by various universities and their affiliated colleges in Telangana. Each year, approximately 80,000 candidates appear for this examination, vying for seats in over 200 institutions that accept TSICET scores.

## TSICET 2026 Important Dates and Schedule

The TSICET 2026 examination will be held over two days. On May 13, 2026, the exam will be conducted in two shifts. The second day, May 14, 2026, will feature a single examination shift. Candidates should regularly check the official TSCHE portal for any updates to the schedule.

### Application Process and Late Fee

The registration window for TSICET 2026 is currently open. Candidates who missed the initial deadline can still apply with a late fee of INR 250 until April 15, 2026. The final deadline for registration with a late fee is May 3, 2026. All applications must be submitted through the official website, icet.tgche.ac.in.

## TSICET 2026 Exam Pattern

The TSICET is a computer-based test (CBT) with a total duration of 150 minutes (2.5 hours). The paper consists of 200 multiple-choice questions (MCQs), with each question carrying one mark. There is no negative marking for incorrect answers, encouraging candidates to attempt all questions. The exam is divided into three main sections:

*   **Analytical Ability:** This section assesses a candidate's problem-solving and reasoning skills.
*   **Mathematical Ability:** This part evaluates mathematical aptitude, covering topics from arithmetic, algebra, and geometry.
*   **Communication Ability:** This section tests English language proficiency, including vocabulary, grammar, and reading comprehension.

## TSICET 2026 Syllabus

The syllabus for TSICET is designed to evaluate a candidate's aptitude for management and computer applications. While the official syllabus PDF can be downloaded from the TSCHE website, generally, the three sections cover the following:

*   **Analytical Ability:** Data Sufficiency, Problem Solving (Sequences and Series, Data Analysis, Coding and Decoding, Date, Time & Arrangement Problems).
*   **Mathematical Ability:** Arithmetical Ability (Ratio & Proportion, Time & Work, Averages, Percentage, Profit & Loss), Arithmetical Ability, Geometrical Ability (Lines, Triangles, Quadrilaterals, Circles), Statistical Ability (Frequency Distribution, Mean, Median, Mode, Standard Deviation).
*   **Communication Ability:** Vocabulary, Business and Computer Terminology, Functional Grammar (Parts of Speech, Tenses, Articles), Reading Comprehension.

## TSICET Cutoff Trends

The cutoff scores for TSICET vary each year based on factors such as the difficulty level of the exam, the number of applicants, and the availability of seats. Different colleges and universities set their own minimum qualifying scores. Candidates aspiring for admission to top institutions should aim for a higher score. Previous years' cutoff trends are often published by colleges and can provide an indication of the expected scores for various rank ranges. It is advisable to review these trends on the respective college websites or official counselling portals once counselling begins.

Preparing thoroughly for each section and practicing with past papers can significantly enhance your chances of securing a good rank in TSICET 2026.
