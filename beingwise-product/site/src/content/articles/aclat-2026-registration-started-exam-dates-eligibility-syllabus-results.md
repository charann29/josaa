The Alliance University Common Law Admission Test (ACLAT) is your gateway to the Master of Laws (LLM) program at Alliance School of Law. This university-level entrance examination is conducted annually by Alliance University to assess candidates for their postgraduate law studies.

## Key Takeaways

*   ACLAT 2026 registration has commenced, and the application link is live on the official university website.
*   The exam is specifically for admission to the LLM program; undergraduate law courses like BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons) have a merit-based admission process.
*   Candidates who successfully register will receive an admit card, which is essential for appearing in the exam.
*   Key dates for the ACLAT 2026 exam will be announced shortly by Alliance University.

## ACLAT 2026: Important Dates and Schedule

While the exact date for ACLAT 2026 is yet to be announced by Alliance University, the application process has already begun. Prospective candidates should keep a close watch on the official university website for updates on the examination schedule.

## Eligibility Criteria for ACLAT 2026

To be eligible for the LLM program through ACLAT 2026, candidates must meet specific academic requirements set by Alliance University:

*   Applicants must hold an LLB/BL degree or any other graduate degree in Law that is recognized by the Bar Council of India.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 45% in their qualifying law degree is required. For candidates belonging to reserved categories, the minimum aggregate score is 40%.
*   Candidates who are in their final year of their LLB/BL degree and are awaiting results are also eligible to apply.

It is important to note that eligibility for BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons) courses differs, as admission to these programs is based on merit from 10+2 examinations, not ACLAT.

## ACLAT 2026 Application Process

The application for ACLAT 2026 is an online process. Follow these steps to complete your application:

1.  **Registration:** Visit the official Alliance University website and register using a valid mobile number and email ID.
2.  **Login:** After successful registration, you will receive login credentials via your registered email. Use these to log in to the application portal.
3.  **Form Filling:** Complete the online application form with all the required personal and academic details.
4.  **Fee Payment:** Pay the application fee. The fee is INR 1000 for Indian applicants and US$50 for international applicants.

After submitting the form and fee, ensure you keep a copy of the application for your records. Alliance University will then issue the ACLAT 2026 admit card to eligible, registered candidates.
