# Industrial IoT Fundamentals on AWS by Amazon Web Services: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students and professionals keen on the cutting edge of technology, Amazon Web Services (AWS) offers a valuable course on Industrial IoT (IIoT) Fundamentals. This program, delivered through an online learning platform, provides a solid introduction to connecting industrial operations with cloud computing.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn the core concepts of Industrial IoT and its distinction from general IoT.
*   Understand how AWS services, specifically AWS IoT Greengrass, are used in IIoT solutions.
*   Explore the role of machine learning and security considerations within industrial settings.
*   Gain foundational knowledge from AWS experts, with the option to earn an official certificate.

## Course Overview and Learning Format

This program is designed for flexible learning, allowing participants to study at their own pace. The instruction is entirely in English, utilizing a mix of video lessons and text-based materials. The estimated weekly commitment is between three to six hours, making it manageable alongside other commitments. As a self-study course, you have the freedom to decide your learning schedule.

## Syllabus Highlights

The curriculum covers essential topics for understanding IIoT. You'll begin with an introduction to what Industrial IoT entails and how it differs from the broader concept of the Internet of Things (IoT). The course then delves into the fundamental components, such as sensors, gateways, and cloud infrastructure. A significant portion is dedicated to AWS IoT Greengrass, a service crucial for extending AWS capabilities to edge devices. Furthermore, the program explores the application of Machine Learning in IIoT and addresses critical security considerations in industrial environments.

### Detailed Modules:

*   Introduction to Industrial IoT
*   Distinguishing IoT from IIoT
*   Understanding Sensors, Gateways, and Cloud Interactions
*   Introduction to AWS IoT Greengrass
*   Leveraging Machine Learning in IIoT
*   Security Best Practices in Industrial IoT

## Instructors: Experts from Amazon Web Services

The course content is delivered by a team of experienced professionals from Amazon Web Services, ensuring that you receive insights directly from industry leaders. The instructors include:

*   **Mr. Neel Mitra**, Solutions Architect, Amazon Web Services
*   **Mr. Rafael Lopes**, Senior Cloud Technologist, Amazon Web Services
*   **Ms. Alana Layton**, Technical Curriculum Developer, Amazon Web Services
*   **Mr. Russell Sayers**, Senior Cloud Technologist, Amazon Web Services

## Course Fees and Certification

The core course content is available for free, allowing anyone to access the valuable learning materials. For those who wish to receive formal recognition of their completion, an official certificate is available. This certificate is provided by Amazon Web Services itself and carries a fee of ₹4,114. Obtaining the certificate can be a valuable addition to your professional profile, demonstrating your foundational understanding of Industrial IoT on AWS.

This program offers a fantastic opportunity to build foundational knowledge in a rapidly growing technological field, guided by experts from one of the world's leading cloud providers.
