# I got 40436 rank in EAMCET, can I get Neil Gogte College with BCD category?

Navigating college admissions can be a complex process, especially when considering specific ranks and categories. Let's break down your chances of securing a seat at Neil Gogte Institute of Technology with a TS EAMCET rank of 40436 under the BC-D category.

## Key takeaways

*   Your TS EAMCET rank of 40436 makes admission to popular branches at Neil Gogte Institute of Technology challenging.
*   Previous year cutoff trends for Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and CSE (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning) were significantly lower.
*   The BC-D category provides reservation, but the overall rank still plays a crucial role.
*   It's important to consider all available options during the EAMCET counselling process.

## Understanding TS EAMCET Ranks and Cutoffs

TS EAMCET ranks are a primary factor for admission into engineering colleges in Telangana. Each college and specific engineering branch will have a 'cutoff rank,' which is the lowest rank at which a student was admitted in previous years. These cutoffs vary annually based on factors like the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and the availability of seats.

## Neil Gogte Institute of Technology: Popular Branches and Past Trends

Neil Gogte Institute of Technology is known for its engineering programs. Two highly sought-after branches at the institute are Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning).

Looking at the admission trends from 2020, the cutoff ranks for these branches were considerably lower than your current rank of 40436:

*   **Computer Science and Engineering (CSE):**
    *   Male candidates: Approximately 13436
    *   Female candidates: Approximately 13617
*   **Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning):**
    *   Male candidates: Approximately 16793
    *   Female candidates: Approximately 17013

These figures represent the general category cutoffs, and while category reservations do help, the difference between your rank and these past cutoffs is quite significant.

## The Role of BC-D Category Reservation

As a candidate belonging to the BC-D category, you are eligible for reservation benefits as per the Telangana state government's policies. This means a certain percentage of seats are reserved for candidates from your category, potentially allowing admission at a slightly higher rank compared to the general category.

However, even with category reservations, the gap between your rank (40436) and the typical admission ranks for popular branches like CSE and CSE (AI/ML) at Neil Gogte Institute of Technology, based on previous years' data, suggests that securing a seat in these specific programs might be challenging.

## Counselling Process and Alternative Options

During the TS EAMCET counselling process, you will have the opportunity to select your preferred colleges and courses. It's crucial to participate actively and make informed choices. While Neil Gogte Institute of Technology might be your primary choice, it's wise to also consider:

*   **Mock Counselling:** Utilize any available mock counselling tools or previous year seat matrix data to understand realistic possibilities.

Always refer to the official TS EAMCET website and counselling notifications for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding cutoffs, seat availability, and the counselling schedule. Final answer keys and results for TS EAMCET are typically released by the NTA, followed by counselling details.

While admission with a 40436 rank in competitive branches at Neil Gogte Institute of Technology might be difficult based on past trends, thoroughly exploring all options during counselling is your best approach.
