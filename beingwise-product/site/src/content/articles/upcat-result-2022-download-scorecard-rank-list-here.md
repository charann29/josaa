# UPCAT Result 2022: Download Scorecard, Rank List Here

For students aspiring to pursue higher education in Uttar Pradesh, the UPCAT (Uttar Pradesh Combined Admission Test) is a crucial entrance examination. This page provides essential information regarding the UPCAT 2022 results, including how to access your scorecard and understanding the rank list.

## Key Takeaways

*   The National Testing Agency (NTA) is responsible for conducting UPCAT and releasing its results.
*   Results are typically accessed via the official NTA UPCET portal using your application number and date of birth.
*   A new merit list was released in 2021 due to initial discrepancies, highlighting the importance of checking official updates.
*   The UPCAT scorecard is vital for the subsequent counselling process and final admissions.

## Understanding Your UPCAT Result

The UPCAT result, also known as the scorecard, is a comprehensive document that details your performance in the entrance examination. It includes your personal information, the marks you've scored, and most importantly, your merit position or rank. This rank is what determines your eligibility and preference during the counselling and seat allotment phases for various courses.

## Courses Covered by UPCAT

UPCAT serves as the gateway for admissions into a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across Uttar Pradesh. These include:

*   Bachelor of Hotel Management and Catering Technology (BHMCT)
*   Master of Urban and Regional Planning (MURP)
*   B.Tech Lateral Entry
*   Bachelor of Design (BDes)
*   Bachelor of Fine Arts and Applied Arts (BFAD/BFA)
*   Master of Computer Applications (MCA), including Integrated and Lateral entry options
*   Master of Technology (MTech)
*   Master of Design (MDes)
*   Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharm) and BPharm (Lateral)
*   Master of Architecture (MArch)
*   Master of Pharmacy (MPharma)
*   Master of Business Administration (MBA), including Integrated options

It's important to note that for B.Tech admissions, candidates are typically considered based on their JEE Main scores, not UPCAT.

## How to Check Your UPCAT Result and Download Scorecard

Accessing your UPCAT result is a straightforward process. Follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official NTA UPCET website.
2.  Look for the link specifically designated for UPCET 2022 results or scorecards.
3.  You will be prompted to enter your UPCET 2022 application number and your date of birth.
4.  After entering the required details, click on the 'Submit' button.
5.  Your UPCAT scorecard will then be displayed on your screen.
6.  It is strongly advised to download and print a copy of your result for future reference, especially for the counselling and admission procedures.

## What Your Scorecard Contains

Your UPCAT scorecard is a detailed document that provides the following key information:

*   Your Name
*   Your Roll Number
*   Date of Birth
*   The specific course you applied for
*   The paper(s) for which you appeared
*   Your category and sub-category (e.g., General, OBC, SC, ST, PwD)
*   Your overall merit position or rank
*   The total marks you scored in the examination
*   Marks obtained in each individual subject or section
