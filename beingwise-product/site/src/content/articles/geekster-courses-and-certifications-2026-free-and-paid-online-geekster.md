## Full Stack Web Development Certification

Geekster's Full Stack Web Development certification is an 8-month, part-time online program tailored for individuals seeking to enter or advance in the web development field. It's designed to fit around a professional's existing work schedule, with live online sessions typically held Monday through Saturday, from 8:00 PM to 10:30 PM.

The course covers a comprehensive range of topics, including programming fundamentals, core computer science principles, algorithms, data structures, and various aspects of full-stack development. Beyond technical skills, the program also incorporates training in essential soft skills, preparing participants for the demands of the modern job market. A notable feature of this program is its income-sharing agreement, where students pay a portion of their salary towards the course fee only after securing employment.

## Mentorship Program

For those looking for a more focused and shorter-term skill enhancement, Geekster offers a 3-month Mentorship Program. This blended learning certificate program is conducted entirely online and aims to provide a stepping stone for individuals aspiring to become software developers.

The program includes 12 dedicated mentoring sessions and 4 mock interview sessions. Mentors from Geekster conduct these sessions exclusively on weekends for one hour, making it convenient for professionals to balance their studies with their careers. The goal of this program is to boost participants' confidence in coding and software development, helping them prepare for and secure their desired jobs. The teaching methodologies employed are designed for easy understanding, simplifying the path to acquiring positions in successful companies.
