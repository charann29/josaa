# CMAT Result 2026 (OUT) @cmat.nta.nic.in; How to Download NTA CMAT Scorecard

The CMAT 2026 results have been officially released by NTA. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now download their scorecards from the official CMAT-NTA website using their login credentials. This scorecard is a crucial document for MBA admissions across India.

## Key Takeaways

*   The CMAT 2026 results were announced on February 17, 2026.
*   Candidates must download their scorecard from the official NTA CMAT portal.
*   The scorecard includes your personal details, sectional and overall scores, percentiles, and All India Rank.
*   Over 1300 MBA institutions nationwide consider CMAT scores for their admission processes.

## How to Check CMAT Result 2026

To access your CMAT 2026 scorecard, follow these straightforward steps on the official NTA CMAT website:

1.  Navigate to the official CMAT portal.
2.  Look for and click on the 'CMAT-2026 Scorecard' link.
3.  Enter your CMAT 2026 application number, your password or date of birth, and the security pin displayed.
4.  Click the 'Submit' button to open your dashboard.
5.  Download your CMAT 2026 result and print a copy for your records.

## What Your CMAT Scorecard Contains

The CMAT 2026 result cum scorecard is an important document for your MBA applications. It's essential to check all the details carefully. If you find any discrepancies (other than score revisions), contact the exam authorities immediately for rectification. Your scorecard will display the following information:

*   Candidate's Name and Roll Number
*   Date of Birth
*   Category and Gender
*   Candidate's Photograph
*   Guardian's Name
*   CMAT 2026 Registration Number
*   CMAT Exam Date and Year
*   CMAT Exam Slot
*   Sectional Percentile Scores
*   Overall Percentile Score
*   All India Rank (CMAT 2026)
*   Candidate's Contact Information

## What Happens After CMAT Results?

Once you have your CMAT scorecard, the next step is to apply to MBA colleges that accept CMAT scores. Many institutions, including those participating in the MAH MBA CET CAP in Maharashtra, use these scores for their admissions. Each college will have its own specific admission criteria, which often include a minimum CMAT percentile, followed by group discussions (GD) and personal interviews (PI). It's advisable to research the cut-offs for your preferred B-schools and prepare for the subsequent selection rounds.
