# Agile Methodology by Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited via Edx: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program offers an accessible introduction to Agile Methodology, a flexible approach to project management that is highly valued in today's professional landscape. Developed in collaboration with Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited, this course aims to equip learners with essential skills and a strategic mindset for modern project environments.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn core Agile principles, practices, and frameworks like Scrum and Scrumban.
*   Gain insights from industry experts through an instructor-led, self-paced online format.
*   The course is offered at an introductory level, making it suitable for beginners.
*   While registration is free, a fee is required to obtain the official certificate from Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited.

## Course Overview

The Agile Methodology program is designed to introduce you to the fundamental concepts of agile development. It delves into the practices, principles, and the mindset required to effectively implement agile techniques in various projects. The curriculum is crafted by seasoned industry professionals, ensuring practical relevance and up-to-date content. Through video lectures and textual materials, you will explore real-world scenarios, guided by narrative examples featuring characters like Maria and Roman, illustrating successful application of agile methods.

## What You Will Learn

This course covers a comprehensive range of topics within Agile Methodology, focusing on both theoretical understanding and practical application. You will learn to:

*   Understand the foundational concepts and historical context of the agile domain.
*   Develop an agile mindset crucial for navigating complex projects.
*   Master the twelve core principles of agile and how to integrate them into your work.
*   Strategize for managing project roadblocks effectively using agile approaches.
*   Explore the Scrum framework for planning and guiding projects.
*   Get acquainted with Scrumban, a hybrid approach combining Scrum and Kanban.
*   Discover efficient practices for applying agile methodology in real-world settings.
*   Cultivate agile leadership qualities and foster an agile culture within teams.

## Program Structure and Effort

The program is structured as a 6-week course, requiring an estimated commitment of 2-6 hours per week. It is delivered through a self-study model, allowing you to learn at your own pace. The instruction is entirely in English and utilizes a mix of video and text-based content. The course includes various learning components such as lectures, assignments, projects, and tests to reinforce your understanding. Transcribed videos are also available to aid learning.

## Fees and Certification

While the initial registration for the Agile Methodology course is free, there is a fee if you wish to receive an official certificate upon completion. The fee to obtain the certificate from Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited is ₹14,903. This certificate signifies your successful completion and understanding of the course material. Occasionally, discounts may be available; for instance, early applicants might find a coupon code like R2DLAGILE20 for a 20% discount on the certificate fee. Upgrading to the certificate also provides access to additional resources, such as the Rolls-Royce R2 data tables.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Agile Methodology course, you typically register through the platform offering the program. Since the registration is free, you can begin the course without any upfront payment. If you decide to pursue the official certificate, you would then proceed with the payment process through the platform to receive your certification from Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited.
