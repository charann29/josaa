# CAT 2024 Topper Interview: How Sarthak Jaiswal Achieved 99.97 Percentile in 2.5 Months

Sarthak Jaiswal, a remarkable CAT 2024 topper, secured an outstanding 99.97 percentile in the highly competitive exam. Beyond his academic prowess, Sarthak is a fitness enthusiast, a passionate mentor for competitive exams like JEE Main, and a Formula 1 fan. In this exclusive interview, he shares his accelerated preparation strategy and valuable insights for future CAT aspirants.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Quick Start:** Sarthak began his CAT preparation just 2.5 months before the exam, demonstrating that focused effort can yield exceptional results in a short timeframe.
*   **Strategic Self-Study:** He primarily relied on self-study, using specific books and online resources, rather than extensive coaching class materials.
*   **Balanced Approach:** Despite a short preparation window, he managed to cover all sections effectively, focusing on foundational concepts and consistent practice.
*   **Mock Test Importance:** Taking numerous mock tests was central to his strategy for understanding exam patterns and improving time management.

## Setting Realistic Expectations and Exceeding Them

When asked about his score, Sarthak admitted to initially having modest expectations, aiming for a scaled score of 115-120. However, the exam went better than he anticipated, and he was thrilled with his final 99.97 percentile. His primary goal was to secure calls from top Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs), and this score has certainly put him in a strong position to achieve that.

This was Sarthak's first attempt at the CAT exam, making his achievement even more impressive, especially given his short preparation period.

## The Compressed Preparation Timeline

Sarthak decided to prepare for CAT 2024 in September, just two and a half months before the exam. This late start meant he had to adopt a highly efficient and targeted preparation strategy, foregoing the luxury of going through extensive coaching materials. He instead focused on quick, effective learning methods and consistent practice.

## Section-Wise Preparation Strategy

### Quantitative Aptitude (QA)

For QA, Sarthak started by acquiring books, specifically mentioning 'Quantitative Aptitude for CAT by Arun Sharma'. His strategy wasn't to delve deep into one chapter at a time. Instead, he focused on completing Level 1 questions from all chapters first, then moving on to Level 2. This approach ensured he stayed in touch with a broad range of mathematical concepts, completing all chapters twice within the first month.

### Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (DILR)

Sarthak did not follow a rigid strategy for DILR. He leveraged YouTube videos to understand different question types and their solving techniques. Following this, he practiced extensively by solving questions from various CAT preparation books, honing his problem-solving skills through consistent application.

### Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension (VARC)

To excel in VARC, Sarthak dedicated a couple of hours daily to uninterrupted reading. He would then summarise what he had read and understood. This practice helped him develop strong reading comprehension and inference skills, which are crucial for tackling VARC questions effectively.

Sarthak's journey is a testament to the fact that with dedication, a smart strategy, and consistent effort, a high CAT percentile is achievable even within a limited timeframe. His tips offer valuable guidance for anyone aspiring to crack the CAT exam.
