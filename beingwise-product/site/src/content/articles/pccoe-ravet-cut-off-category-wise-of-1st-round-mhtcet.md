# Pccoe Ravet Cut Off Category Wise of 1st Round MHT CET

Understanding the MHT CET cutoffs is crucial for students aspiring to join Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE), Ravet, Pune. These figures indicate the minimum scores or ranks required for admission to various engineering programs, based on the first round of counselling.

## Key Takeaways

*   **High Demand for Core Branches:** Computer Engineering and Information Technology consistently showed the highest cutoffs, reflecting their popularity.
*   **General vs. OBC Home State:** While General category cutoffs were competitive, the OBC Home State category also required strong percentiles and ranks.
*   **Varying Percentiles:** The MHT CET cutoff percentiles for the General category ranged significantly across different engineering disciplines.
*   **Rank is Key:** For specific branches, All India Ranks provide a more precise indicator of admission chances than just percentiles.

## Understanding MHT CET Cutoffs for PCCOE Ravet

The MHT CET cutoffs represent the percentile scores or ranks of the last admitted candidate in a particular category and branch. These numbers fluctuate each year based on factors like the number of applicants, exam difficulty, and seat availability. The data we're discussing here pertains to the first round of MHT CET counselling for the 2025 academic year, as officially released.

## First Round Percentile Overview

For the General category, the MHT CET cutoff percentiles for various engineering branches at PCCOE Ravet spanned a range from approximately 90.59% to 96.99%. This wide range highlights the differing levels of competition for various programs. Students aiming for the most sought-after branches needed to achieve percentiles at the higher end of this spectrum. For candidates under the OBC Home State (OBCS) category, the cutoff percentiles were quite competitive, generally falling between 95.29% and 95.88% in the first round.

Beyond percentiles, All India Ranks (AIR) offer a more granular view of admission prospects. These ranks are particularly useful for gauging your position relative to other candidates. ## Interpreting Your Chances

If your MHT CET All India Rank is close to or better than the figures listed above for your desired branch and category, your chances of securing a seat in PCCOE Ravet are good, especially in earlier rounds. However, it's important to remember that cutoffs can vary in subsequent rounds based on seat matrix changes and candidate preferences. Always refer to the official MHT CET counselling portal for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding cutoffs and admission rounds.
