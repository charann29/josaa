# BITSoM MBA Average Package 2025: Placement Report, Highest CTC & Top Recruiters

BITSoM (BITS School of Management) has released its official placement report for the MBA batch of 2023-25. This report provides a comprehensive overview of salaries, top recruiters, and the overall career outcomes for the graduating students.

## Key Takeaways

*   The average package for the BITSoM MBA batch of 2023-25 stood at INR 21 LPA.
*   The highest CTC offered to a student in this batch reached INR 32.50 LPA.
*   A significant 59% of the batch secured offers above INR 20 LPA.
*   Leading companies like McKinsey, BCG, A.D. Little, Unilever, JPMorgan Chase, PwC, and Pidilite were among the top recruiters.

## BITSoM MBA Placement Statistics 2025

The placement report highlights various salary benchmarks for the 2023-25 MBA batch. While the overall average package was INR 21 LPA, it's worth noting that this figure saw a slight adjustment compared to the previous year's average of INR 23.85 LPA.

Here's a detailed breakdown of the salary figures:

| Placement Feature         | Details       |
| :------------------------ | :------------ |
| Highest CTC               | INR 32.50 LPA |
| Average MBA Package       | INR 21.0 LPA  |
| Top 10% Average CTC       | INR 29.11 LPA |
| Top 30% Average CTC       | INR 27.45 LPA |
| Top 50% Average CTC       | INR 25.64 LPA |
| Top 75% Average CTC       | INR 23.35 LPA |
| Median CTC                | INR 20.52 LPA |
| Candidates with CTC > 20L | 59%           |

## Class Profile: MBA Batch 2023-25

The diversity and quality of the student body are crucial factors in placement success. The BITSoM MBA batch of 2023-25 comprised 153 students with varied academic backgrounds and professional experiences.

### Batch Demographics

*   **Batch Strength:** 153 students
*   **Gender Ratio:** 55% Male, 45% Female
*   **Median CAT Percentile:** 94 percentile
*   **Median GRE Score:** 319
*   **Median GMAT Score:** 690
*   **Average Work Experience:** Two years

### Academic Background Distribution

The students came from a wide range of undergraduate disciplines, showcasing a diverse intellectual pool:

*   **Engineering:** 53%
*   **Commerce/Business:** 30%
*   **Arts:** 12%
*   **Science and Medicine:** 5%

### Work Experience Distribution

The average work experience for the batch was two years, with a distribution across different experience levels:

| Years of Experience | Percentage of Candidates |
| :------------------ | :----------------------- |
| Fresher             | 22.7%                    |
| 0-2 Years           | 28.8%                    |
| 2-4 Years           | 43.2%                    |
| 4+ Years            | 5.3%                     |

## Industry and Functional Placements

The 2023-25 batch secured roles across a broad spectrum of industries and functions, reflecting the comprehensive nature of the BITSoM MBA program. Key sectors included Consulting, BFSI, FMCG, IT/ITES, and Manufacturing.

### Top Recruiting Companies

The list of companies that recruited from BITSoM underscores the strong industry connections and the quality of talent graduating from the institution. Some of the prominent names include:

*   McKinsey & Company
*   Boston Consulting Group (BCG)
*   A.D. Little
*   Unilever
*   JPMorgan Chase & Co.
*   PwC
*   Pidilite Industries

This robust placement performance by the BITSoM MBA batch of 2023-25 demonstrates the program's effectiveness in preparing students for successful careers in diverse management roles.
