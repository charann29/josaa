# International Institute of Digital Technologies Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online International Institute of Digital Technologies Courses

The International Institute of Digital Technologies (IIDT) offers specialized postgraduate programs designed to equip professionals with cutting-edge skills in critical digital domains. These residential courses aim to advance the Information Technology and Electronics industry by providing learners with modern facilities and expert instruction.

## Key Takeaways

*   IIDT offers three distinct 11-month residential postgraduate programs: Data Science, Cyber Security, and Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning.
*   These programs are designed to provide in-depth knowledge and practical skills crucial for the modern IT and electronics sectors.
*   Courses are delivered by global experts and industry professionals, utilizing advanced digital tools and techniques.
*   Admission to the Cyber Security program requires an entrance examination, and all programs conclude with term-end assessments and a post-graduate certification.

## About the International Institute of Digital Technologies (IIDT)

IIDT operates under the umbrella of the Andhra Pradesh Electronics & IT Agency (APEITA), with a core mission to foster growth and development within the Information Technology and Electronics industry. The institute focuses on delivering state-of-the-art educational programs that address the evolving needs of the digital landscape. By providing advanced training, IIDT aims to produce highly skilled professionals ready to contribute to both government and private sectors.

## Post Graduate Programme in Data Science

This 11-month residential program is meticulously crafted to instill a comprehensive understanding of data analytics and its pivotal role in the IT industry. The curriculum delves into how data science drives business growth, enhances decision-making processes, and aids in implementing competitive strategies. Learners will engage with modern digital tools and techniques under the guidance of global experts.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Data Science fundamentals
*   Python programming
*   Deep Learning
*   Machine Learning
*   Data Visualization
*   Statistical analysis
*   Excel proficiency
*   Big Data concepts
*   Financial Management insights

The program includes two end-term examinations, leading to a post-graduate certification upon successful completion.

## Post Graduate Programme in Cyber Security

The Cyber Security program is an 11-month residential course dedicated to the essential aspects of protecting digital systems, applications, networks, and data from unauthorized access and threats. It addresses both current challenges and emerging opportunities in safeguarding digital assets across government and private enterprises. This specialized training equips learners with the expertise to investigate and mitigate cybersecurity threats.

**Key Features:**

*   Focus on core IT security principles.
*   Instruction provided by faculty from Gujarat Forensic Science University.
*   An entrance test is required for admission.
*   Assessments are based on term-end examinations.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Comprehensive Cyber Security knowledge

## Post Graduate Programme in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

This advanced 11-month residential program explores the fundamental concepts of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). It highlights their significant impact on automation and their crucial roles within the contemporary Information and Technology industry. Industry experts deliver the curriculum through interactive sessions, employing ultra-modern tools and techniques.

**Skills Covered:**

*   Artificial Intelligence principles
*   Machine Learning techniques
*   Data Science applications
*   Python programming
*   Deep Learning methodologies

Students are evaluated through term-end examinations, receiving a post-graduate certification upon successful completion of the program. The fee for this program is ₹3,00,000.

These programs from IIDT offer a robust pathway for individuals looking to build or enhance their careers in the rapidly evolving digital technology landscape.
