# SDG Academy Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online SDG Academy Courses

The SDG Academy offers a range of online courses designed to educate individuals on the critical aspects of sustainable development. These programs cater to a global audience, providing insights into environmental, social, and economic sustainability challenges and solutions.

## Key Takeaways

*   The SDG Academy offers self-paced online courses covering various facets of sustainable development.
*   Programs are available in both free (audit) and paid modes, with paid options providing unlimited access and a certificate.
*   Courses delve into topics like economic development, planetary boundaries, natural resource management, and macroeconomics.
*   Prominent experts, including university professors and former government officials, lead the instruction.

## The Age of Sustainable Development

This comprehensive program, spanning 14 weeks, is tailored for individuals interested in understanding the core principles of sustainable development. It covers a wide array of topics crucial for a sustainable future, including economic growth, the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs), and the concept of planetary boundaries. Learners will explore human rights, universal health coverage, gender equality, education, sustainable food systems, the development of sustainable cities, and the pressing issue of climate change.

The course aims to equip participants with the knowledge to safeguard biodiversity and appreciate the importance of renewable energy resources. It also emphasizes the significance of gender equality and human rights in achieving a balanced society. By understanding these concepts, individuals can contribute to national flourishing without compromising the environment.

Offered in a self-paced format, participants can choose between a free audit track with limited access or a paid version that grants unlimited access to course materials and a certificate upon completion. The program is considered to be at an intermediate level, making it suitable for those with some foundational knowledge or a strong interest in the subject.

## Macroeconomics for a Sustainable Planet

This 13-week course redefines traditional macroeconomic understanding within the context of global sustainability. It addresses contemporary economic challenges such as inflation, unemployment, fiscal deficits, trade barriers, and protectionism, examining their impact on billions worldwide. The program guides learners on how to pursue economic growth responsibly in an era where planetary health is increasingly fragile.

Participants will explore traditional macroeconomic tools and principles and see how they apply in today's globalized world and the current push for sustainable development. The course features instruction from distinguished experts, including Professor Jeffrey D. Sachs from Columbia University, who also serves as a senior UN advisor, and Felipe Larraín, a former Chilean Minister of Finance.

Designed to be self-paced, the course requires an estimated 2-4 hours of study per week. Similar to other SDG Academy offerings, it is available in both a free audit mode, providing limited access, and a paid mode for full access and certification. This program is also at an intermediate level, suitable for those with a background in economics or related fields.

## Natural Resources for Sustainable Development

Developed in collaboration with the National Resource Governance Institute, this 12-week course provides in-depth guidance on utilizing natural resources responsibly without causing environmental harm. It is particularly relevant for sustainable development practitioners, covering theoretical aspects of resource management in a concise and practical manner.

The program highlights the critical role of communication among industries, governments, and citizens for effective natural resource management. It traces the history and evolution of major natural resources like oil and coal, detailing the entire process from extraction to their use as finished products or components in various industries. The course also touches upon the legal frameworks surrounding natural resource management, offering beneficial insights for participants.

Like other SDG Academy courses, this program offers two enrollment tracks: a free audit mode with limited access and a paid mode for unlimited access to content and a certificate. It is structured for self-paced learning and is considered an intermediate-level course.

These courses empower individuals to become informed advocates and practitioners for a more sustainable future.
