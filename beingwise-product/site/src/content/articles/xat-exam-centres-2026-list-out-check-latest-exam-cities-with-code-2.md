# XAT Exam Centres 2026 List (OUT): Check Latest Exam Cities with Code

The Xavier Aptitude Test (XAT) 2026 will be conducted in numerous cities across India. Candidates applying for XAT can select their preferred examination cities during the application process, with centres assigned based on these choices.

## Key takeaways

*   XAT 2026 will be held in 109 cities, each with at least one examination centre.
*   Applicants can choose up to six preferred cities when filling out the XAT application form.
*   Centre allocation is on a first-come, first-served basis, so early application is advisable.
*   The specific address of your allotted centre will be provided on your XAT 2026 admit card.

## How to Select XAT Test Cities

The option to choose your preferred test cities is an integral part of the XAT 2026 application form. Follow these steps to make your selections:

1.  Register for XAT 2026 on the official XAT website.
2.  Log in to your account to access and fill out the application form.
3.  Navigate to the 'select test city' section within the form.
4.  From the provided list, select up to six cities in your order of preference.
5.  Complete the remaining sections of the application form.
6.  Proceed to make the required fee payment and submit your application.

### Important Considerations for Selecting XAT Exam Cities 2026

Keep the following points in mind when selecting your XAT test cities:

*   You can select up to six cities from the official list.
*   Prioritize your selections carefully; it's recommended to choose your current residence or a nearby city as your first preference.
*   If centres in your first-choice city are fully booked, you will be allocated a centre in your subsequent preferred city.
*   The examination authority reserves the right to change or cancel any test centre.
*   The decision on final centre allotment rests solely with the exam conducting body.
*   Requests for changes to the allotted centre will not be entertained once the XAT 2026 admit card is released. Therefore, choose your cities thoughtfully.
