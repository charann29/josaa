The Andhra Pradesh government regularly announces a wide range of state-level job opportunities for young professionals across various departments. Aspiring candidates can find roles in areas like policing, education, and social welfare, with recruitment notifications typically issued by the Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC).

## Key Takeaways

*   The APPSC is the primary body for most state government job selections in Andhra Pradesh.
*   Opportunities are available for diverse educational backgrounds, from Class 10 to professional degrees.
*   Key sectors with current openings include medical support, clerical roles, and higher education.
*   Candidates should regularly check official portals for the latest updates on application deadlines and processes.

## Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC)

Established in 1956, the Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC) plays a crucial role in the selection process for most government positions within the state. The commission is primarily responsible for recruiting candidates for Group 1, Group 2, and Group 3 services, as well as various technical and specialised roles.

Currently, there are approximately 887 vacancies across different Andhra Pradesh government departments, offering suitable opportunities for individuals with varying educational qualifications. The application process can be either online or offline, depending on the specific recruiting department. It is advisable for interested candidates to visit the official APPSC website to stay informed about important updates regarding these job openings.

## Current Andhra Pradesh Government Job Notifications 2025

Several departments have recently released recruitment notifications, presenting diverse career paths. Here's a look at some of the key opportunities:

### 1. AMC Typist, Technician, and Support Roles

The Andhra Pradesh Medical College (AMC) in Visakhapatnam has issued a notification for 71 vacancies in various support and technical positions. These roles are open to candidates with qualifications ranging from Class 10 to professional degrees such as B.Sc, PGDCA, Diploma, and MS/MD. Available positions include Typist/Data Entry Operator, various Technicians (Anaesthesia, Radiotherapy, OT, C-ARM, EEG), General Duty Attendants, Receptionists, Wardens, Librarians, Prosthetic/Ortho Technicians, and Ambulance Drivers, among others.

### 2. FACT Clerk Vacancy

Fertilizers and Chemicals Travancore Limited (FACT) has announced a recruitment drive for Clerk positions on a fixed-tenure contract basis. Applicants must hold a graduate degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50 percent marks. The maximum age for eligibility is 26 years as of June 30, 2025, with age relaxations provided for candidates from reserved categories. While the exact number of vacancies has not been specified, the application window for these roles will remain open until August 14, 2025. Interested candidates are required to submit hard copies of their applications to the recruitment office. Selected candidates may be posted in various states, including Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, and Odisha.

### 3. IIIT Sri City Faculty Recruitment

The Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Sri City, located in Andhra Pradesh, is inviting applications for 46 faculty vacancies. These openings span various academic ranks, including Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor Grades I-III. Departments with available positions include Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), and Mathematics/Data Science. This is one of the more recent announcements for Andhra Pradesh Government Jobs 2025, with an application deadline of August 7, 2025.

Securing a government job in Andhra Pradesh offers stability and a chance to contribute to public service. Keep a close watch on official announcements for application deadlines and detailed eligibility criteria.
