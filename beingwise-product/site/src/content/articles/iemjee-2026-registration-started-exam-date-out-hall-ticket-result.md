# IEMJEE 2026: Registration (STARTED), Exam Date (OUT), Hall Ticket, Result, Admission

The Institute of Engineering & Management Joint Entrance Examination (IEMJEE) is an annual entrance test for students aspiring to join various undergraduate and postgraduate programs offered by the Institute of Engineering & Management (IEM). This examination serves as a gateway for admissions into fields like engineering, management, computer applications, and more.

## Key Takeaways

*   IEMJEE 2026 registration is currently open online through the official portal, myiemexam.in.
*   The online examination for Phase 2 is ongoing until April 30, 2026, while the offline exam was held on April 3, 2026.
*   Applicants can choose between appearing for the exam online or offline, based on their preference.
*   The exam consists of 90 multiple-choice questions from Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, with a scoring system of +4 for correct answers and -1 for incorrect ones.

## IEMJEE 2026 Important Dates

Staying updated with the IEMJEE 2026 schedule is crucial for all aspiring candidates. Here's a quick overview of the key dates:

| Event                           | Date                   |
| :------------------------------ | :--------------------- |
| IEMJEE 2026 Online Exam (Phase 2) | January 5, 2026 - April 30, 2026 (Ongoing) |
| IEMJEE 2026 Offline Exam        | April 3, 2026          |
| IEMJEE 2026 Offline Admit Card Release | March 27, 2026         |
| IEMJEE 2026 Phase 1 Result Release | December 8, 2025       |

Candidates are advised to regularly check the official IEMJEE portal for any updates or changes to this schedule.

## Programs Offered Through IEMJEE

IEMJEE facilitates admissions to a diverse range of programs. Prospective students can apply for:

*   B.E. / B.Tech
*   BCA, and Integrated BCA+MCA
*   MCA, and Integrated MCA+PhD
*   BBA, and Integrated BBA+MBA
*   MBA, and Integrated MBA+PhD
*   BSc

Each program has specific eligibility criteria, which candidates should review on the official website before applying.

## IEMJEE 2026 Application Process

The application form for IEMJEE 2026 is available exclusively online. There is no application fee. To apply, candidates need to follow these steps:

1.  **Visit the official website:** Go to myiemexam.in.
2.  **Register:** Create an account using your basic details.
3.  **Fill the application form:** Log in and provide all necessary personal, academic, and contact information. Ensure all details are accurate.
4.  **Select exam mode:** Choose your preferred examination mode (online or offline). If opting for the offline exam, you will also need to select your preferred test city.
5.  **Upload documents:** Upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature as per the specifications.
6.  **Submit:** Review all entered information carefully before final submission.

It is important to complete the application before the deadline to be considered for the examination.

## IEMJEE 2026 Exam Pattern and Syllabus

The IEMJEE is designed to assess candidates' fundamental knowledge in core subjects. Here's what to expect:

*   **Total Questions:** 90 multiple-choice questions.
*   **Subjects:** Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
*   **Marking Scheme:** For every correct answer, 4 marks are awarded. For every incorrect answer, 1 mark is deducted. There is no penalty for unattempted questions.

The syllabus generally covers topics from the 10+2 level for undergraduate programs. Detailed syllabus information can be found on the official IEMJEE website.

## Admit Card and Results

**Admit Card:** Registered candidates for the online exam can download their IEMJEE 2026 admit card by logging into the official website using their credentials. For the offline exam, admit cards were released on March 27, 2026.

**Results:** IEMJEE 2026 results will be announced on the official portal. Candidates will need their hall ticket number and Aadhaar number to check their scores. Successful candidates will then be eligible to apply for the subsequent admission and counselling processes.

IEMJEE provides a structured pathway for students to pursue their higher education goals at the Institute of Engineering & Management.
