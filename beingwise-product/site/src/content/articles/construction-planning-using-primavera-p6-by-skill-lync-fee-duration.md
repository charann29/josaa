# Construction Planning using Primavera P6 by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those aspiring to excel in civil engineering and project management, this online course offers a deep dive into construction planning and implementation using the industry-standard Primavera P6 software.

## Key Takeaways

*   A comprehensive 3-month online course focusing on construction planning and management with Primavera P6.
*   It's designed for both new graduates and experienced civil engineers aiming for project management roles.
*   The curriculum covers fundamental planning concepts, project structuring, and hands-on application of Primavera P6.
*   Successful completion leads to a certificate, with special recognition for top performers.

## Course Overview

The Construction Planning using Primavera P6 course is a three-month online program delivered through a combination of video and text materials. It's tailored for individuals interested in the civil engineering sector, providing essential knowledge in project planning, management, and the practical application of Oracle Primavera P6 software. The course aims to equip learners with the skills needed to pursue a career in construction project management.

Upon finishing the program, participants receive a certificate of completion. Additionally, the top 5% of students will be awarded a merit certificate, acknowledging their outstanding performance.

## What You Will Learn

This course is structured to provide a thorough understanding of construction planning and the effective use of Primavera P6. Key learning areas include:

*   **Fundamentals of Project Planning & Management:** Understanding project lifecycle, planning parameters, and basic Primavera P6 concepts.
*   **Project Structuring:** Delving into projects, programs, portfolios, contracts, and organizational structures.
*   **Project Configuration:** Learning to set up projects, calendars, and Work Breakdown Structures (WBS) within Primavera P6.
*   **Activities and Relationships:** Mastering the creation of activities and defining their sequences in construction and demolition projects.
*   **Industrial Applications:** Gaining insights into real-world challenges and practical applications of Primavera P6 in the industry.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is ideal for both fresh civil engineering graduates and seasoned civil engineers who wish to specialize or advance their careers in project management. It's particularly beneficial for those aiming to become successful construction managers or project managers by mastering a crucial industry tool like Primavera P6.

## Course Fees and Support

The program fee for the Construction Planning using Primavera P6 course is Rs. 40,000. This fee covers access to all course materials and support services.

Participants benefit from a range of support options designed to enhance their learning experience:

*   **Expert Instructors:** Guidance from experienced professionals.
*   **Personalized Support:** One-on-one Zoom support sessions, email, and telephone assistance.
*   **Group Learning:** Group Zoom support sessions and a dedicated course-specific forum group.
*   **Practical Experience:** Industry-oriented projects to build a professional portfolio.
*   **Career Assistance:** Course counseling and a one-on-one demo session.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Construction Planning using Primavera P6 course is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official course page.
2.  Select the "Enroll Now" option.
3.  Choose your preferred payment package and provide your name, email ID, and phone number to gain access to the course content.

This course offers a valuable opportunity to enhance your skills and career prospects in the dynamic field of construction project management.
