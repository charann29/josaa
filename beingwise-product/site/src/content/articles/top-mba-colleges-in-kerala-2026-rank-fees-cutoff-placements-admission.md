# Top MBA Colleges in Kerala 2026: Rank, Fees, Cutoff, Placements, Admission

Kerala offers a range of excellent institutions for pursuing a Master of Business Administration (MBA). This guide will help you understand the top colleges, their admission criteria, fee structures, and placement opportunities for the 2026 academic year.

## Key Takeaways

*   IIM Kozhikode consistently holds a top position nationally for its MBA program.
*   Fees for MBA programs in Kerala vary significantly, from approximately ₹67,000 to ₹14 Lakhs.
*   Strong placement records are a highlight, especially at institutions like IIM Kozhikode, with high average salaries.
*   Admission to top MBA colleges typically requires competitive scores in national-level entrance exams like CAT.

## Top MBA Colleges in Kerala

When considering an MBA in Kerala, several institutions stand out based on their academic reputation, infrastructure, and career prospects. The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), an initiative by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, provides a reliable ranking system. Other reputable publishers also release rankings that can guide your decision.

## Admission Process and Eligibility

Admission to MBA programs in Kerala's top colleges is highly competitive. Generally, candidates need a bachelor's degree with a minimum percentage (often 50% or 60%, varying by institution and category). The primary step involves appearing for national-level management entrance exams.

### Entrance Exams

*   **CAT (Common Admission Test):** This is the most widely accepted entrance exam for IIMs and many other premier business schools across India, including those in Kerala. IIM Kozhikode, for instance, primarily uses CAT scores for shortlisting candidates.
*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test):** Conducted by NTA, CMAT scores are accepted by numerous institutions for MBA admissions.
*   **KMAT Kerala (Kerala Management Aptitude Test):** This state-level exam is specifically for admissions to MBA programs offered by universities and colleges within Kerala.

After clearing the entrance exam cutoffs, candidates typically undergo a Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round. Some institutions might also consider academic performance, work experience, and other relevant factors.

## Cutoff Scores

Cutoff scores for MBA programs depend on various factors like the number of applicants, the difficulty of the entrance exam, and the number of available seats. For IIM Kozhikode, the CAT cutoff percentile is usually very high, often above the 90th percentile. Other institutions like CUSAT, NIT Calicut, and Rajagiri Business School will have their specific cutoffs for CAT, CMAT, or KMAT Kerala, which are released after the respective exams.

## Placements and Career Prospects

The placement records of top MBA colleges in Kerala are a strong indicator of their program quality and industry relevance. IIM Kozhikode consistently reports excellent placements, with graduates securing roles in top companies across various sectors like consulting, finance, marketing, and general management. The average salary package at IIM Kozhikode stands at a remarkable ₹27.50 Lakhs per annum.

Other colleges like CUSAT, NIT Calicut, and Rajagiri Business School also have dedicated placement cells that strive to connect students with reputable employers. Their graduates find opportunities in diverse industries, with average salary packages ranging from ₹5.70 Lakhs to ₹7.50 Lakhs per annum, demonstrating good returns on investment for their MBA programs.

Choosing the right MBA program in Kerala can significantly boost your career trajectory. Always research thoroughly and consider your career goals when making your decision.
