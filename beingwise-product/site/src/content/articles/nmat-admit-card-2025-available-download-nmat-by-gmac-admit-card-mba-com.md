# NMAT Admit Card 2025 (Available): Download NMAT by GMAC Admit Card @mba.com

The NMAT by GMAC admit card is an essential document for all candidates appearing for the exam. Issued by the Graduate Management Admission Council (GMAC), this hall ticket confirms your eligibility and provides crucial details about your test. It becomes available after you successfully complete the exam scheduling process.

## Key Takeaways

*   The NMAT admit card is typically released about seven days before your scheduled exam date.
*   You can download your admit card directly from your candidate login dashboard on the official NMAT by GMAC website, mba.com.
*   It is mandatory to carry a printed copy of your admit card along with a valid, original photo identification to the test centre.
*   The admit card includes vital information such as your personal details, exam date, time, venue, and specific instructions for test day.

## How to Download Your NMAT Admit Card

Downloading your NMAT admit card is a straightforward process once your registration and scheduling are complete. Follow these steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Go to the official NMAT by GMAC website, mba.com.
2.  **Log In:** Access your candidate dashboard using your registered credentials.
3.  **Check Application Status:** Ensure your application status shows 'payment successful' or similar confirmation.
4.  **Locate Admit Card:** On your dashboard, you will find a link or section to download your NMAT admit card.
5.  **Download and Print:** Download the PDF and print multiple copies for safety. It's advisable to keep a digital copy as well.

It's important to complete all registration and scheduling steps before attempting to download the admit card. If you encounter any issues, refer to the help section on the official portal.

## Details on Your NMAT Admit Card

The NMAT admit card is a comprehensive document containing all the necessary information for your exam day. Carefully review all the details to ensure accuracy. The admit card will typically include:

| Information Category      | Details Included                                    |
| :------------------------ | :-------------------------------------------------- |
| **Candidate Information** | Your Name, Parent's Name, Gender, Application Number, Date of Birth, Photograph |
| **Identity Proof Details**| Type of Photo ID, ID Number, Your Signature         |
| **NMAT Exam Details**     | Exam Date, Test Shift, Reporting Time, Test Duration, Exam Attempt Number (1st, 2nd, or 3rd) |
| **Test Centre Details**   | Name of the Exam Centre, Complete Address, Exam Day Instructions |

If you find any discrepancies in your admit card details, contact the NMAT by GMAC support team immediately for rectification.

## Required Documents for Exam Day

On the day of your NMAT exam, you must carry specific documents to gain entry to the test centre. Failing to bring these will result in denial of entry.

### Mandatory Documents:

*   **NMAT Admit Card:** A clear printout of your downloaded admit card.
*   **Valid Photo ID Proof (Original):** One of the following government-issued photo identification cards in its original form (expired IDs or photocopies will not be accepted):
    *   Aadhaar Card (UID)
    *   Passport (issued by the Government of India)
    *   PAN Card
    *   Voter's ID Card
    *   Permanent Driver's License

Ensure that the name on your photo ID matches the name on your NMAT admit card exactly. It's recommended to arrive at the test centre well in advance of your scheduled reporting time to complete the verification process smoothly.

Your NMAT admit card is your gateway to the examination hall, so keep it safe and readily accessible. Good luck with your NMAT preparation and exam!
