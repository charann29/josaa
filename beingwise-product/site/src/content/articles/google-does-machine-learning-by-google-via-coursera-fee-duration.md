# How Google does Machine Learning by Google via Coursera: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to understand the practical applications of Machine Learning (ML) in a real-world setting, the "How Google does Machine Learning" course offers a unique perspective directly from Google's experts. This program, delivered through Coursera, unpacks how one of the world's leading tech companies leverages ML to solve complex problems and drive innovation.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn Google's approach to integrating Machine Learning into products and business strategies.
*   Gain practical experience with Google Cloud's Vertex AI platform for ML development.
*   Understand the importance of responsible AI development and addressing biases in models.
*   The course is self-paced, conducted entirely in English, and delivered through video and text-based modules.

## Course Structure and Learning Experience

This program is designed for self-study, allowing you to progress at your own convenience. The learning content is primarily delivered through videos and accompanying text, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of each topic. All instructions and materials are provided in English, making it accessible to a wide audience.

The course begins with an introduction to the series and the concept of being "AI-First," exploring what Machine Learning is and the types of problems it can address. It then dives into Google's specific methodologies, revealing the "secret sauce" behind their ML implementations and how they integrate these technologies into business processes.

## Deep Dive into the Syllabus

The curriculum is structured into several modules, guiding learners from foundational concepts to practical application and ethical considerations:

*   **Module 1: Introduction to Course and Series** - Sets the stage for what to expect.
*   **Module 2: What It Means to Be AI-First** - Explores the essence of ML, problem framing, and infusing apps with ML.
*   **Module 3: How Google Does ML** - Uncovers Google's unique approach, the "ML Surprise," and the path to successful ML implementation.
*   **Module 4: Machine Learning Development with Vertex AI** - Introduces Google Cloud Platform and Vertex AI components, including hands-on labs for training AutoML models.
*   **Module 5: Machine Learning Development with Vertex Notebooks** - Focuses on practical ML development using Vertex Notebooks, with optional labs for model building.
*   **Module 6: Best Practices for Implementing Machine Learning on Vertex AI** - Covers essential best practices for data preprocessing and environment setup.
*   **Module 7: Responsible AI Development** - A crucial module addressing human biases, data biases, evaluating metrics for inclusion, and finding errors in datasets.
*   **Module 8: Summary** - A concluding module consolidating all learnings.

Each module typically includes video lectures, reading materials, and assignments to reinforce learning. Practical labs are integrated, especially in the modules covering Vertex AI, to provide hands-on experience.

## Fees and Certification Details

Access to this course and the ability to earn a certificate comes with a fee. The cost varies depending on the duration for which you wish to access the course materials and complete it. Here's a breakdown of the fees:

| Description | Fee in INR |
| :---------- | :--------- |
| 1 month     | ₹ 4,117    |
| 3 months    | ₹ 8,234    |
| 6 months    | ₹ 12,352   |

Upon successful completion of the course, a certificate is available, issued by Coursera, validating your achievement and understanding of the concepts taught by Google's experts. This certificate can be a valuable addition to your professional profile, demonstrating your commitment to understanding advanced Machine Learning practices.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to learn directly from Google's expertise, providing insights and practical skills essential for navigating the evolving landscape of Machine Learning.
