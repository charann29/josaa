Welcome to an insightful look at the Post Graduate Program in AI and Machine Learning, a collaborative offering designed to equip you with cutting-edge skills in these rapidly evolving fields. This program aims to transform learners into industry-ready professionals, capable of tackling real-world challenges in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program offers a blend of instructor-led online training and self-paced learning, providing flexibility for participants.
*   It emphasizes practical application through numerous hands-on exercises, integrated labs, and industry-based projects.
*   Participants will gain proficiency in core machine learning concepts, including supervised and unsupervised learning, regression, classification, and natural language processing.
*   While no prior AI/ML experience is strictly required, a foundational understanding of college-level mathematics, statistics, and basic Python programming is highly recommended.

## Program Overview and Learning Experience

This Post Graduate Program focuses on developing a comprehensive understanding of machine learning principles and their practical implementation using Python. The curriculum is structured to provide an in-depth exploration of working with real-time data, developing algorithms, and mastering various modeling techniques. Participants will engage with over 25 hands-on exercises and integrated Jupyter notebooks, allowing for immediate practice and reinforcement of concepts learned during sessions.

The program offers a flexible learning environment with 44 hours of instructor-led online training sessions. Beyond the live classes, there are 14 hours of online self-paced learning and an additional 30+ hours of blended learning, ensuring a well-rounded educational experience. This approach caters to different learning styles and paces, making it accessible for a diverse group of students and working professionals. Businesses also have the option to enroll their employees through corporate training.

## Practical Application and Industry Projects

A significant strength of this program lies in its strong emphasis on practical application. Participants will not only learn theoretical concepts but also apply them through a series of practice labs and industry-scale projects. These projects simulate real-world scenarios, requiring learners to design and implement machine learning models for hypothetical companies, drawing inspiration from industry leaders. This hands-on experience is crucial for bridging the gap between academic knowledge and professional competence, ensuring graduates are prepared for immediate contributions in the AI/ML domain.

## Eligibility and Certification

Prospective participants should possess a basic understanding of college-level statistics and mathematics. While not mandatory, it is highly recommended to have some prior exposure to Python programming basics. For those new to Python or needing a refresher, supplementary courses focusing on Python for Data Science and essential statistics and math are available. Successful completion of the program and earning the certification requires active participation in online classes and the submission of at least one completed project. For online bootcamp attendees, this means completing a full batch of machine learning training and submitting a project.

## Program Fees

The fee for the online bootcamp format of this Post Graduate Program in AI and Machine Learning is ₹149,999. This amount is inclusive of GST. It covers all aspects of the program, including instructor-led training, self-paced modules, access to labs, and project-based learning opportunities.

This program presents a robust pathway for individuals looking to build a strong career foundation in the exciting fields of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
