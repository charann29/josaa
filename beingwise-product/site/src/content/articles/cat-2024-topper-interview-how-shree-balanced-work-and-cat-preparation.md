# CAT 2024 Topper Interview: How Shree Balanced Work and CAT Preparation to Achieve 99.88 Percentile

Shree Ahuja, a Computer Science graduate and current Cloud Engineer, demonstrated remarkable dedication by securing an outstanding 99.88 percentile in the CAT 2024 exam. His journey, marked by strategic planning and perseverance, offers valuable lessons for aspiring MBA candidates, especially those juggling professional commitments.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Early Start is Key:** Shree began his preparation in January 2024, giving himself ample time to cover all topics thoroughly.
*   **Strategic Sectional Focus:** He identified and worked on his weaker areas, particularly Verbal Ability, while consistently practicing DILR and strengthening Quant concepts.
*   **Balancing Work and Study:** Despite a demanding job, Shree maintained a consistent study schedule, showing that a full-time role doesn't preclude top CAT scores.
*   **Learning from Previous Attempts:** His first CAT attempt, though not serious, helped him understand the exam pattern, which proved beneficial for his second, successful try.

## About Shree Ahuja

Shree completed his undergraduate studies in Computer Science at Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bengaluru. He currently works as a Cloud Engineer at Sprinklr, applying his skills in the DevOps domain. Beyond his professional and academic pursuits, Shree is a keen sports enthusiast, enjoying badminton and football, and he has a unique hobby of collecting old coins. His family consists of three members, and he is an only child.

## The Journey to 99.88 Percentile

This was Shree's second attempt at the CAT exam. His first attempt was more for familiarization, yielding a respectable 94.99 percentile without serious preparation. For CAT 2024, however, he dedicated himself fully, starting his preparation in January. His hard work paid off with an impressive overall score of 121.79 and sectional scores:

*   DILR: 39.42
*   Verbal Ability (VA): 40.21
*   Quantitative Ability (QA): 42.16

## Section-Wise Preparation Strategy

Shree adopted a tailored approach for each section of the CAT exam:

*   **Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (DILR):** His core strategy was extensive practice. He aimed to solve at least 3-4 DILR sets per week, exposing himself to a wide variety of question types to build proficiency.
*   **Quantitative Ability (QA):** Shree focused on building a strong conceptual foundation. He ensured he understood the basics of each chapter before moving on to solving a range of exercises. He also made it a point to revisit and practice topics where he felt less confident.
*   **Verbal Ability (VA):** Recognizing VA as his weakest area, Shree put in extra effort here. He cultivated a habit of extensive reading, including daily newspapers like The Hindu, and regularly took sectional tests. To improve his accuracy in Reading Comprehension, he even utilized online resources like YouTube videos that offered strategic tips.

## Study Resources and Coaching

Shree primarily relied on the comprehensive study materials provided by his coaching institute. For Quantitative Ability, he also referred to 'Quantum CAT' by Nishit Sinha. He enrolled in offline coaching classes at TIME Bangalore, which provided structured guidance and support for his preparation.

Shree's success underscores the importance of a well-thought-out plan, consistent effort, and the ability to learn and adapt from previous experiences.
