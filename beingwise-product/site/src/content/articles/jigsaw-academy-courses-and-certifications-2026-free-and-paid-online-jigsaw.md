# Jigsaw Academy Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Jigsaw Academy Courses

Jigsaw Academy offers a range of online courses and certifications designed to equip students and professionals with in-demand skills in data science, analytics, and emerging technologies like the Internet of Things (IoT). These programs cater to various learning needs, from foundational knowledge to advanced professional expertise.

## Key takeaways

*   Jigsaw Academy provides specialized certifications in IoT analytics and professional IoT solution development.
*   Many courses feature a self-paced online learning format, allowing flexibility for students.
*   Learning aids often include capstone projects, case studies, and access to online Q&A sessions.
*   A postgraduate diploma in data science is available, offered in collaboration with Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE).

## Internet of Things (IoT) Analyst Certification

The IoT Analyst Certification program focuses on the intersection of IoT devices and data analytics. This course is designed to help professionals understand how to extract, clean, and analyze data from IoT systems to derive business insights. It combines a business-centric approach with analytical techniques.

This certification includes three distinct courses that cover various stages of data processing for IoT, from initial data acquisition and cleaning to exploratory and final analysis. Participants also learn how to apply advanced analytics and machine learning algorithms to IoT data to build sophisticated solutions.

Key features of this program include:

*   Online self-paced learning.
*   Access to learning aids such as a capstone project, online Q&A sessions, and case studies.
*   Provision of an IoT Hardware Kit for practical experience.
*   The course duration is typically 12 months, with a fee of approximately ₹35,200.

## IoT Professional Certification

The IoT Professional Certification aims to develop skills in building IoT solutions from the ground up, with a strong emphasis on practical application. This program uses open-source platforms like Arduino to teach participants how to design, implement, and develop IoT solutions.

Structured into four different courses, the program provides a deep understanding of working with various sensor-connected IoT devices and gateways. It incorporates numerous hands-on exercises and real-world case studies to ensure a comprehensive and practical learning experience within the IoT ecosystem.

Highlights of the IoT Professional Certification:

*   Online self-paced learning for convenience.
*   Culminates in a capstone project to apply acquired knowledge and skills.
*   The typical duration is 12 months, with a course fee around ₹30,800.

## Postgraduate Diploma in Data Science (PGDDS)

Jigsaw Academy, in collaboration with the Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), offers a Postgraduate Diploma in Data Science (PGDDS). This full-time, in-person program is designed to transform students into skilled data science experts.

The curriculum covers essential areas such as Exploratory Data Analysis, Data Visualization, Neural Networks, and Artificial Intelligence. Students gain proficiency in advanced data analytics tools including R, Tableau, Excel, NoSQL, SQL, and Hadoop. The program emphasizes interactive learning and hands-on experience in data research facilities equipped with the latest applications and analytics software.

Key aspects of the PGDDS program:

*   An 11-month full-time, in-person format.
*   Accredited by MAHE with industry collaboration from partners like Equifax.
*   Includes four industry discussions, three assignments, five case studies, and three industry projects.
*   Offers domain-specific options in areas like Unstructured Data Analysis, Marketing & BFSI Analytics, Linear Programming, and Robotic Process Automation.
*   The program fee is approximately ₹4,95,000.

These programs from Jigsaw Academy offer valuable pathways for individuals looking to advance their careers in the dynamic fields of data science and IoT.
