# CEED 2026: Result (DECLARED), Score Card (OUT), Merit List, Counselling Date & Admission Process

The Common Entrance Examination for Design (CEED) 2026 results have been announced, and candidates can now access their scorecards. This national-level examination, conducted by IIT Bombay, is the gateway for admission to Master of Design (M.Des) programs across various prestigious institutions.

## Key takeaways

*   The CEED 2026 result was declared on March 4, 2026, accessible via candidate login credentials on the official CEED portal.
*   Scorecards for CEED 2026 became available for download on March 10, 2026, and will remain valid until July 31, 2026.
*   IIT Gandhinagar has joined the list of participating institutes, offering an M.Des in Integrated Design & Technology.
*   Admission to M.Des programs based on CEED scores involves direct application to participating institutes, followed by their individual selection processes.

## CEED 2026 Result and Scorecard Details

The CEED 2026 examination results were officially released on March 4, 2026. Candidates can view their results by logging into the official CEED website using their registered ID and password. Following the result announcement, the CEED 2026 scorecards were made available for download from March 10, 2026. It's important to note that these scorecards are valid for one year and can be downloaded until July 31, 2026. No physical copies of the scorecards will be dispatched.

For all candidates who appeared for the exam, their Part-A marks are displayed. However, Part-B marks are only shown for those candidates who were shortlisted based on their Part-A performance. All shortlisted candidates receive a merit rank.

## Understanding the CEED Cutoff 2026

The cutoff for CEED 2026 Part-A was announced on February 5, 2026. This cutoff is released in the form of a mean and standard deviation on the official CEED website, which are crucial for calculating Part-A scores. For CEED 2026, the mean for Part-A was 39.35, with a standard deviation of 16.89.

## CEED Participating Institutes and Admission Process

CEED scores are accepted by a range of participating and result-sharing institutes for their M.Des programs. Key participating institutes include IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Guwahati, IIT Hyderabad, IIT Indore, IIT Roorkee, IIITDM Jabalpur, and, new this year, IIT Gandhinagar. Candidates who secure a merit rank in CEED must apply directly to their preferred participating institutions. These institutes will then shortlist applicants based on their CEED merit ranks and may conduct further selection rounds, such as interviews or portfolio reviews, before finalizing their admission lists.

## Post-Result: What's Next?

After the CEED 2026 results are out, the next crucial step for candidates is to apply to the M.Des programs at their desired participating institutes. Each institute will have its own application timeline and specific admission criteria beyond the CEED score, which candidates must meet. It is advisable to regularly check the official websites of the respective IITs and other participating institutions for their individual admission announcements and deadlines.

Congratulations to all candidates on their CEED 2026 results. We wish you the best in your pursuit of design education.
