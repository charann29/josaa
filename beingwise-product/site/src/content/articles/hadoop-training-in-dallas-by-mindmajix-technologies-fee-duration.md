# Hadoop Training in Dallas by Mindmajix Technologies: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to master Big Data technologies, the Hadoop Training in Dallas program offers comprehensive instruction to help you gain expertise and prepare for certification. This online course aims to equip learners with the necessary skills for a career in Big Data.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is delivered entirely online, offering flexibility for various schedules.
*   It provides both self-paced video lessons and live, instructor-led online sessions.
*   The curriculum is designed to be certification-oriented, covering essential Hadoop concepts.
*   Learners receive ongoing support and lifetime access to self-paced video content.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This training delves into various aspects of Hadoop, including Big Data fundamentals, Hadoop storage, processing, and the MapReduce framework. The curriculum also covers Flume, YARN, HDFS, Pig, Hive, advanced HBase, Sqoop, MongoDB, and Spark, among other critical topics. The program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical application, featuring hands-on projects, industry use cases, and real-time examples to solidify understanding.

## Training Formats and Flexibility

The Hadoop Training in Dallas offers three distinct enrollment options to suit different learning preferences and schedules:

*   **Corporate Training:** Tailored for organizational needs.
*   **Self-Paced Training:** Learners can access pre-recorded videos and study at their own convenience.
*   **Instructor-Led Online Live Sessions:** These interactive classes are conducted via platforms like Google Meet or Zoom, allowing for real-time engagement with instructors.

Classes are available on both weekdays and weekends, providing further flexibility.

## Curriculum Highlights

The course syllabus is structured to provide a deep understanding of the Hadoop ecosystem:

### Understanding Big Data and Hadoop

*   Introduction to Big Data and its challenges
*   Overview of Hadoop features and its ecosystem
*   Hadoop 2.x core components: HDFS and MapReduce Framework
*   Different Hadoop distributions

### YARN (Yet another Resource Negotiator)

*   Understanding YARN as the next-generation resource manager
*   Comparison between MapReduce and YARN
*   YARN architecture components: Resource Manager, Application Master, Node Manager

### Hadoop Architecture and HDFS

*   Hadoop 2.x Cluster Architecture, including Federation and High Availability
*   Typical production Hadoop cluster setup and different modes
*   Common Hadoop shell commands
*   Setting up single-node and multi-node clusters

### Hadoop MapReduce Frameworks

*   MapReduce use cases and its significance
*   Hadoop 2.x MapReduce architecture and components
*   YARN MR application execution flow and workflow
*   Input splits and their relation to HDFS blocks
*   MapReduce concepts: Combiner, Partitioner, Sequence Input Format, XML file parsing

### Pig

*   Introduction to Pig and its advantages over MapReduce
*   Pig use cases and programming structure
*   Running modes, components, and execution of Pig Latin programs
*   Data models, data types, and shell commands in Pig
*   Pig Latin relational operators: Group, COGROUP, Joins, Union, Diagnostic Operators, Specialized joins
*   Built-in functions: Eval, Load and Store, Math, String, Date functions

## Support and Certification

Mindmajix Technologies provides comprehensive support to its learners, including one-on-one doubt-clearing sessions, 24/7 lifetime support, and lifetime access to self-paced video content. Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a certificate from Mindmajix Technologies, validating their acquired skills in Hadoop.

This program is ideal for data administrators, Java developers, business analysts, and architects looking to enhance their skills in Big Data technologies.
