# I am getting 140 marks in Part A in UCEED 2025. Can anybody guide if there are any chances of getting IIT?

Scoring 140 marks in Part A of UCEED 2025 is a strong performance, and it naturally leads to questions about your prospects for admission to an IIT. While the final picture depends on your Part B (drawing) score, we can explore your chances based on past trends and the structure of the UCEED exam.

## Key Takeaways

*   A Part A score of 140 is significantly above the average for UCEED 2025, placing you in a strong position.
*   Final IIT admission relies on your combined score from both Part A and Part B.
*   Previous year's cutoffs for IITs indicate high competition, with top ranks securing seats.
*   Cutoffs can fluctuate annually, so past data serves as a guide, not a guarantee.

## Understanding UCEED Scoring and Shortlisting

The UCEED examination is divided into two parts: Part A, which is computer-based, and Part B, which assesses drawing skills. Your raw score in Part A is crucial, and the official UCEED authority uses statistical methods (mean and standard deviation) to determine the cutoff for shortlisting candidates for evaluation of Part B. For UCEED 2025, the mean Part A score was 61.50 with a standard deviation of 25.69, highlighting that 140 is an excellent score well above the average.

Only candidates who clear the Part A cutoff have their Part B evaluated. The final UCEED merit list is then prepared by combining the scores from both Part A and Part B, with specific weightages assigned to each section.

## Historical IIT Cutoffs and Your Prospects

While specific cutoffs vary each year, looking at previous years can offer some insight. For instance, in UCEED 2023, the closing ranks for the General category at various IITs were:

*   **IIT Bombay:** Ranks 1-16
*   **IIT Guwahati:** Ranks 17-59
*   **IIT Hyderabad:** Ranks 21-53
*   **IIITDM Jabalpur:** Ranks 55-93

Given your strong Part A score, if you perform reasonably well in Part B, you could indeed have a good chance of securing a seat in an IIT. Your high Part A score provides a significant advantage, but the final rank will depend on how your combined score compares to other candidates.

## The Importance of Part B (Drawing Section)

It's vital to remember that Part B holds substantial weight in the overall UCEED score. Even with an outstanding Part A score, a less-than-stellar performance in the drawing section could impact your final rank. The drawing section evaluates your creativity, observation skills, and ability to communicate ideas visually, all of which are fundamental to design education.

## What to Do Next?

Stay vigilant for official announcements from the UCEED coordinating body. They will release the final UCEED results, including Part B scores and the combined merit list. Once these are out, you can compare your overall rank with the previous year's closing ranks for your desired IITs. Additionally, keep an eye on the official JoSAA portal for details regarding counselling and seat allocation once the UCEED results are declared.

Your strong Part A score is a fantastic start, and we wish you the best for your UCEED 2025 results and future design aspirations.
