# Secondary: Accountancy (224) by National Institute of Open Schooling via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For students keen to build a strong foundation in accounting principles right from the secondary school level, the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) offers an excellent online course. Delivered through the official Swayam platform, this program, titled "Secondary: Accountancy (224)," provides a comprehensive introduction to the field.

## Key Takeaways

*   This 24-week online course covers fundamental accounting concepts at a secondary school level.
*   It offers 10 academic credit points upon successful completion, making it a valuable addition to your educational profile.
*   The learning experience combines video lectures, textual materials, assignments, and interactive discussions.
*   Upon completing the course and its evaluations, you will receive a certification from NIOS.

## Course Structure and Delivery

Designed for self-study, the "Secondary: Accountancy (224)" course is delivered entirely online. The medium of instruction is English, making it accessible to a wide audience. Learners will engage with the material through a blend of video lectures and specialized reading content. The program spans 24 weeks, providing ample time to grasp each concept thoroughly.

Ms. Anshul Kharbanda from NIOS coordinates this course, ensuring its academic quality and relevance. The interactive nature of the Swayam platform allows for assignments and peer discussion forums, fostering a collaborative learning environment.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is structured to provide a robust understanding of elementary accountancy. Key topics covered include:

*   Basic concepts of financial accounting
*   Understanding journals and subsidiary books
*   Mastering the rules of debit and credit
*   Preparing ledgers and trial balances
*   Identifying and rectifying accounting errors
*   Concepts of depreciation, provisions, and reserves
*   Preparation of financial statements, including income statements and balance sheets
*   An introduction to computerized accounting and software like Tally

This comprehensive syllabus ensures that students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills essential for further studies or entry-level understanding in finance.

## Eligibility and Application Process

This course is open to anyone interested in learning accounting concepts at the secondary level. There are no specific prerequisites mentioned, making it accessible for students from various backgrounds. To enroll in the "Secondary: Accountancy (224)" training, you need to follow a simple online process on the official Swayam portal:

1.  Visit the specific course page on the Swayam platform.
2.  Locate and click on the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  You will then be directed to a new page where you can either sign in with an existing account or create a new one using your username and password.

Once registered, you can begin your learning journey. The course itself does not have a separate fee; participation in Swayam courses and the certification provided by NIOS are generally free.

## Benefits of Certification

Upon successfully completing the course and its evaluation components, learners will be awarded a "Secondary: Accountancy (224)" certification by NIOS. This certificate validates your understanding of fundamental accounting principles and can be a valuable asset for future academic pursuits or career paths. It demonstrates a foundational knowledge in financial management and accounting proficiency, which are crucial skills in today's economic landscape.

This course offers an excellent opportunity to build a solid base in accountancy through a flexible and accessible online format.
