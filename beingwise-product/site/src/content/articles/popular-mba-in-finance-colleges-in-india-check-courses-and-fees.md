# 10 Popular MBA in Finance colleges in India: Check Courses and Fees

For students aiming to build a career in the dynamic world of finance, an MBA with a specialization in Finance can open many doors. This guide explores some of India's leading business schools, beyond the IIMs, that offer excellent MBA in Finance programs.

## Key takeaways

*   An MBA in Finance equips students with skills in financial management, economic forecasting, and investment strategies.
*   Admission to top programs typically requires a bachelor's degree with a minimum percentage and a strong score in an MBA entrance exam.
*   Leading colleges like SPJIMR, NMIMS, and Symbiosis are highly regarded for their finance specializations.
*   Course fees can vary significantly, ranging from approximately INR 8 Lakhs to over INR 20 Lakhs, depending on the institution.

## Understanding MBA in Finance

An MBA in Finance is designed to provide in-depth knowledge about managing, controlling, and reviewing financial resources. The curriculum typically covers areas such as analyzing company financial reports, predicting economic trends, maximizing shareholder value, balancing risk with profitability, and managing investment portfolios. This specialization is ideal for those interested in roles within corporate finance, investment banking, wealth management, and financial analysis.

## Eligibility Criteria for MBA in Finance

To be considered for admission to a top MBA in Finance program, applicants generally need to meet specific academic qualifications and perform well in national-level MBA entrance examinations. While exact requirements can differ by institution, common eligibility criteria include:

*   A bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 50% in graduation for general category candidates, with a relaxation to 45% for reserved categories.
*   Valid scores from accepted entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, NMAT, SNAP, or others as specified by the college.

*   **SPJIMR Mumbai:** This institution, a constituent of Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, is highly regarded and has received accreditation from prestigious bodies like AMBA (UK), NBA, and AACSB International. It consistently ranks well, for instance, placing 29th in Asia by QS Global MBA Ranking 2023.
