## Admission Process and Eligibility for MBA in Assam

While specific admission criteria can vary, the general requirements for MBA programs in Assam are as follows:

*   Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline or an equivalent qualification from a recognized university.
*   Most institutions require a minimum aggregate score of 50% in graduation. For candidates from reserved categories, this minimum score is typically 45%.
*   Applicants must take national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, or ATMA.

## Key MBA Entrance Exams

Admission to MBA programs in Assam primarily depends on scores from major entrance tests. Here are some of the most widely accepted exams:

*   **CAT (Common Admission Test):** This is a national-level exam conducted annually for admission to various MBA/PGDM programs across India. It assesses quantitative ability, verbal ability, and data interpretation & logical reasoning.
*   **XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test):** Conducted by XLRI Jamshedpur, XAT is another prominent national-level management entrance exam. It is known for its challenging nature and includes sections on verbal & logical ability, decision making, and quantitative ability & data interpretation.
*   **MAT (Management Aptitude Test):** Administered by AIMA (All India Management Association), MAT is a widely accepted national-level test for MBA/PGDM admissions.
*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test):** This is a national-level entrance examination facilitated by NTA (National Testing Agency) for admission to management programs.
*   **ATMA (AIMS Test for Management Admissions):** Conducted by the Association of Indian Management Schools (AIMS), ATMA is a single-window entrance test for various management programs.
