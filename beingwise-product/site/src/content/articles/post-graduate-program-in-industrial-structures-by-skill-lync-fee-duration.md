# Post Graduate Program in Industrial Structures by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For civil and structural engineers looking to specialize, the Post Graduate Program in Industrial Structures offers a detailed exploration of designing, analyzing, and constructing robust industrial steel structures. This program is tailored to equip participants with practical skills and in-depth knowledge using industry-standard software.

## Key takeaways

*   The program focuses on real-world application, covering design principles and software tools like STAAD.Pro and STAAD.Foundation.
*   It comprehensively addresses various structural elements and systems, from tension members to pre-engineered buildings and pipe racks.
*   Participants will gain hands-on experience in modelling, analyzing, and designing industrial structures, understanding load calculations and international design codes.
*   The curriculum spans foundational concepts to advanced topics in structural engineering, ensuring a well-rounded learning experience.

## Program Overview and Structure

This postgraduate program is designed to provide a deep dive into the specifics of industrial structural engineering. It covers both theoretical foundations and practical application, ensuring participants are well-prepared for industry demands.

The curriculum is structured over twelve weeks, with each week dedicated to a specific module. This progressive learning approach starts with fundamental concepts and gradually moves to more complex topics and software applications. The program aims to build a strong understanding of steel structures, their behavior under various loads, and the design considerations necessary for safety and efficiency.

## Detailed Course Modules

The program's extensive curriculum covers a wide array of topics crucial for industrial structural design. Here's a breakdown of the weekly modules:

*   **Week 1: Introduction to Industrial Structures** - This module lays the groundwork by introducing steel structures, their properties, types of industrial structures, key design factors, structural systems, common loads, and the general design procedure.
*   **Week 2-5: Design of Structural Elements** - These weeks focus on the detailed design of individual structural components, including tension members, compression members (including built-up sections), various types of beams (considering lateral stability and effective length), and plate girders, utilizing relevant Indian Standards like IS 800:2007.
*   **Week 6: Structural System of Industrial Structure** - This module explores different load-resisting systems, both lateral and vertical, and various flooring systems such as plating grating, deck beams, and composite floor decks.
*   **Week 7-11: Software Application with STAAD.Pro** - A significant portion of the program is dedicated to hands-on training with STAAD.Pro. This includes modelling nodes and beam elements, generating geometry, assigning properties and specifications, applying various loads (self-weight, member loads, floor loads, seismic, wind, and moving loads), performing analysis, interpreting results, and applying design parameters. Specialized applications include analyzing and designing typical steel frame structures, pre-engineered buildings (PEB), and pipe rack structures, considering load combinations from IS 800:2007, AISC ASD, and LRFD.
*   **Week 12: Foundation Design with STAAD.Foundation** - The final module introduces STAAD.Foundation, covering the modelling of supports and loadings, importing data from STAAD.Pro, and designing various foundation types such as isolated footings (including eccentric), continuous footings, pile caps, and both soil-supported and pile-supported mat foundations.

## Learning Methodology

This program emphasizes a practical, application-oriented learning approach. While theoretical concepts are thoroughly explained, a significant focus is placed on using industry-standard software for real-world problem-solving. Through detailed modules on STAAD.Pro and STAAD.Foundation, participants will engage in hands-on exercises, case studies, and design simulations. This ensures that graduates not only understand the principles but can also effectively apply them using the tools prevalent in the industry.

## Who Should Enroll?

This program is ideal for civil engineers, structural engineers, and aspiring professionals who wish to specialize in the design and analysis of industrial steel structures. It is particularly beneficial for those looking to enhance their skills with advanced software tools and gain a comprehensive understanding of international design codes and practical construction considerations.

Embark on this journey to master the intricacies of industrial structural design and elevate your engineering career.
