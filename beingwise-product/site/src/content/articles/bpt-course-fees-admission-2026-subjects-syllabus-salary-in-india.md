# BPT: Course Fees, Admission 2026, Subjects, Syllabus, Salary in India

The Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) is an undergraduate program focused on helping individuals recover from physical injuries, disabilities, and movement disorders through various therapeutic techniques. It's a rewarding field for those passionate about healthcare and improving quality of life.

## Key Takeaways

*   BPT is a 4-year undergraduate degree, often including an internship.
*   Eligibility typically requires a Class 12 Science stream (PCB) with a minimum percentage.
*   Admission is usually based on entrance exams like CUET, KCET, or IPU CET, though NEET scores might also be considered by some institutions.
*   Average course fees generally range from INR 1 Lakh to INR 5 Lakh, depending on the institution.

## BPT Course Details

BPT is a comprehensive program that blends theoretical knowledge with practical clinical experience. Students learn about human anatomy, physiology, biomechanics, and various physiotherapy modalities to diagnose and treat patients. The curriculum prepares graduates to work in diverse settings, from hospitals and rehabilitation centers to sports clinics and private practices.

## BPT Admission 2026

Admission to BPT programs for the 2026 academic year will largely depend on entrance examination scores, followed by counselling. Candidates are advised to check specific college websites for their detailed admission criteria and application timelines.

### Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a BPT program, candidates must have successfully completed their Class 12 (or equivalent) examination with a Science stream, including Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) as core subjects. A minimum aggregate score, often around 50%, is usually required, though this can vary between institutions.

### Important Dates for 2026 Admissions

*   **NEET 2026 Registration:** Expected to close by early March 2026. The exam is tentatively scheduled for early May 2026, as per NTA announcements.
*   **CUET 2026:** This exam is likely to be held between mid-May and end-May 2026, based on past schedules.

Candidates should regularly check the official NTA website and respective university portals for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding application deadlines and exam dates.

## BPT Subjects and Syllabus

The BPT syllabus is designed to provide a strong foundation in medical sciences and specialized physiotherapy techniques. Over the four years, students will cover a wide array of subjects, progressing from foundational knowledge to advanced clinical applications.

Common subjects include:

### Year 1

*   Anatomy
*   Physiology
*   Biochemistry
*   Psychology
*   Sociology
*   Basic Nursing & First Aid

### Year 2

*   Pathology
*   Pharmacology
*   Microbiology
*   Biomechanics & Kinesiology
*   Electrotherapy
*   Exercise Therapy

### Year 3

*   General Medicine
*   General Surgery
*   Orthopaedics & Traumatology
*   Neurology
*   Community Medicine
*   Physiotherapy in Orthopaedics

### Year 4

*   Physiotherapy in Neurology
*   Physiotherapy in Cardio-Respiratory Conditions
*   Sports Physiotherapy
*   Rehabilitation Medicine
*   Research Methodology & Biostatistics
*   Clinical Postings/Internship

## Career Scope and Salary in India

A BPT degree opens doors to a diverse range of career opportunities in the healthcare sector. Physiotherapists play a vital role in patient recovery, rehabilitation, and preventative care.

### Job Roles

*   **Clinical Physiotherapist:** Working in hospitals, clinics, or private practice, treating patients with various conditions.
*   **Sports Physiotherapist:** Specializing in treating and preventing injuries in athletes.
*   **Rehabilitation Specialist:** Helping patients regain function after surgery, injury, or illness.
*   **Home Care Physiotherapist:** Providing therapy services in patients' homes.
*   **Lecturer/Academician:** Teaching in physiotherapy colleges and universities.
*   **Researcher:** Contributing to advancements in physiotherapy techniques and understanding.

### Top Recruiters

Leading healthcare providers and organizations actively seek BPT graduates. Some prominent recruiters include:

*   Apollo Hospitals
*   Fortis Hospital
*   HelpAge India
*   Hosmat Hospital
*   Talwalkers Pantaloon Fitness Pvt Ltd

### Salary Expectations

Salaries for BPT graduates in India can vary significantly based on experience, location, specialization, and the type of employer. Fresh graduates can expect an average starting salary ranging from INR 2 LPA to INR 8 LPA. With experience and specialization, earnings can increase substantially.

Choosing a BPT course means embarking on a fulfilling journey in a dynamic and essential healthcare profession.
