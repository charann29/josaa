# Business Accounting course by Aptech Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a strong foundation in the world of finance and accounting, Aptech Learning offers a specialized Business Accounting course. This program is designed to equip students with essential skills and knowledge, making them job-ready for various roles in the financial sector.

## Key Takeaways

*   This online course spans three months, with classes held three days a week for two hours daily.
*   It covers crucial topics like office automation, advanced presentation techniques, MS Excel, and online business accounting processes.
*   Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate from Aptech Learning.
*   The program is suitable for 10+2 pass-outs, undergraduates, graduates, engineers, and working professionals.

## Course Overview and Structure

Aptech Learning is a well-known educational institution that provides career-focused courses across various domains, including banking, finance, and information technology. Their Business Accounting course has been carefully developed by experienced and certified faculty, taking into account current industry trends and demands.

This course offers a comprehensive introduction to the fundamental principles of business accounting. It aims to help learners acquire practical knowledge and develop relevant skills crucial for a successful career in this field. The curriculum provides process-based training, enabling participants to understand corporate procedures in shared services, finance, banking, and accounting sectors.

Students will gain insights into the various stages and complexities involved in financial transactions and learn to approach them efficiently. The online learning format includes e-books, video tutorials, and workshops, providing flexibility and access to industry experts.

## What You Will Learn

The Business Accounting course is structured to provide participants with a broad range of skills and knowledge. Key areas of learning include:

*   **Office Automation and Presentation:** Mastering essential office tools and advanced presentation techniques.
*   **MS Excel Proficiency:** Developing expertise in using MS Excel for various accounting tasks.
*   **Online Business Accounting:** Understanding the processes involved in online business accounting.
*   **GST Fundamentals:** Gaining a solid understanding of Goods and Services Tax (GST).
*   **Corporate Processes:** Learning about corporate procedures in shared services, finance, banking, and accounting domains.

The syllabus typically covers critical components such as Accounts Receivable & Accounts Payable, Recording & Reporting, Statutory Compliance, and Payroll.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Business Accounting course, candidates must meet specific educational requirements. Applicants should have completed their 10+2 education. The program is also open to undergraduates, graduates, engineers, and working professionals who wish to enhance their accounting skills or transition into the finance sector.

## Course Duration and Fees

The Business Accounting course by Aptech Learning is a concise program designed for efficient learning. It spans a duration of **three months**, with classes scheduled three days a week for two hours each day.

Regarding the financial investment, the course fee ranges from **₹9,800 to ₹12,000**, exclusive of applicable taxes. It's important to note that the exact fee might vary depending on the specific course and the location of the learning center.

## How to Apply

Prospective candidates interested in enrolling in the Business Accounting course can follow a straightforward application process:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official Aptech Learning website.
2.  **Fill the Enquiry Form:** Locate and complete the enquiry form, usually found at the bottom of the page, by providing your details.
3.  **Await Contact:** After submitting the form, Aptech Learning will get in touch with you to guide you through the subsequent admission procedures.

This course offers a valuable opportunity for aspiring professionals to delve deep into the accounting world, enhancing their skills and resume for better career prospects or acceleration in their current roles.
