# Management Essentials by upGrad: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a strong foundation in business management, the Management Essentials program offers a comprehensive overview of core concepts. This program is designed to equip learners with practical knowledge across various critical business functions.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is delivered entirely in English, utilizing a blend of self-study and virtual classroom sessions.
*   Learning materials include both video and text-based content, offering flexibility in how you absorb information.
*   Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate directly from upGrad, validating their newly acquired skills.
*   The curriculum covers fundamental areas like economics, marketing, operations, accounting, and organizational behavior.

## Program Structure and Learning Experience

This management essentials program is structured to provide a well-rounded learning experience. It combines independent study with interactive live sessions, allowing participants to engage with instructors and peers. The medium of instruction is English, ensuring accessibility for a wide range of Indian students and professionals.

The learning is delivered through a mix of video lessons and textual content, catering to different learning preferences. Virtual classroom sessions provide opportunities for real-time interaction, discussions, and clarification of doubts, making the learning process dynamic and engaging.

## What You Will Learn: The Curriculum

The program's curriculum is carefully designed to cover the essential aspects of business management. It begins with an introduction to case-based learning, preparing you for practical application of concepts. Here's a breakdown of the key modules:

*   **Introduction to Case Based Learning:** Includes a live workshop to help you develop analytical skills for real-world business scenarios.
*   **Introduction to MS Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word):** Hands-on live sessions focusing on essential tools for data analysis, presentations, and document creation.
*   **Basics of Business Economics:** Covers microeconomics (demand, supply, consumer/producer theory, market structures) and an introduction to macroeconomics through live sessions.
*   **Basics of Marketing:** Explores fundamental marketing concepts, the marketing process, consumer behavior, and market research in interactive sessions.
*   **Basics of Operations and Supply Chain:** Introduces operations and supply chain management, demand and supply planning, and an overview of logistics and warehousing.
*   **Basics of Accounting:** Delves into accounting fundamentals, financial statements (balance sheet, income statement, cash flow), ratio analysis, working capital, and project evaluation.
*   **Basics of OB and HR:** Covers organizational structures, culture, individual and organizational behavior, and an introduction to Human Resource Management (HRM) and strategic HRM.

Each module incorporates multiple live sessions to deepen understanding and facilitate interactive learning.

## Certification

Upon successful completion of all program requirements, participants will be awarded a certificate by upGrad. This certificate serves as official recognition of your foundational knowledge in management essentials and can be a valuable addition to your professional profile.

## How to Apply

Specific application details, including the application process and any prerequisites, are typically available on the official upGrad program page for Management Essentials. Prospective students should visit the official program page for the most current information regarding enrollment steps and eligibility criteria.

This program offers a robust starting point for anyone aiming to understand the core functions of business and management.
