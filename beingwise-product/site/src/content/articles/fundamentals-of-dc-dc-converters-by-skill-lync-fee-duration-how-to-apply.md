# Fundamentals of DC-DC Converters by Skill Lync: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Understanding how DC-DC converters work is crucial in many engineering fields today, from electric vehicles to power electronics. This Skill Lync certification course offers a deep dive into the analysis, design, and modeling of these essential components using industry-standard software.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a 3-month online certification program focusing on non-isolated DC-DC converters.
*   The curriculum emphasizes practical application through MATLAB and Simulink simulations.
*   Participants will learn to design converters and tackle real-world challenges in power electronics.
*   The course is open to graduates and postgraduates from any stream, including engineering students and Ph.D. scholars.

## Course Overview

The "Fundamentals of DC-DC Converters using MATLAB and Simulink" certification is an online program designed to equip professionals and students with comprehensive knowledge of DC-DC converters. Over three months, participants will explore the theoretical foundations and practical applications of these converters, gaining hands-on experience in modeling and simulation using MATLAB and Simulink. The course aims to clarify why and how DC-DC converters are indispensable for both mechanical and electrical engineers, providing a strong foundation for tackling industry challenges.

## What You Will Learn

This program offers an in-depth exploration of DC-DC converters, covering critical concepts for analysis, design, and modeling. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Comprehensive Understanding:** Grasping the principles of all DC-DC converter types, including their operation, analysis, and design, with a strong emphasis on hands-on simulation.
*   **Control System Basics:** Learning the fundamentals of transfer functions and Bode plots, essential tools for analyzing converter stability and performance.
*   **Software Proficiency:** Gaining practical experience with MATLAB and Simulink for simulating and demonstrating DC-DC converter behavior.
*   **Application-Specific Design:** Developing the ability to design converters tailored for various applications, addressing challenges commonly encountered in the power electronics industry.

## Program Structure and Offerings

The course is delivered entirely online, featuring video and text-based modules for self-paced learning. Participants benefit from:

*   **Flexible Learning:** A self-study model allows students to learn at their own pace.
*   **Dedicated Support:** Access to group video, telephone, forum, and email support, along with technical assistance.
*   **Software Access:** Provision of necessary software for practical exercises and projects.
*   **Professional Development:** Opportunities to build a professional portfolio and work on technical projects.
*   **One-on-One Demos:** Personalized demo sessions to enhance understanding.

## Fees and Certification

The program fee for the "Fundamentals of DC-DC Converters using MATLAB and Simulink" certification is Rs. 40,000. Upon successful completion of projects and exercises, participants will receive a completion certificate from Skill Lync. Additionally, exceptional performers who rank among the top 5% of their cohort may also earn a merit certificate.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the program, prospective students should follow these steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official Skill Lync website.
2.  **Enroll:** Locate and click the 'Enrol Now' button on the course page.
3.  **Account Access:** Either log in to an existing account or create a new one.
4.  **Fee Payment:** Complete the admission process by depositing the program fee.

Upon successful payment, your admission to the course will be confirmed.

This program provides a solid foundation for anyone looking to specialize in power electronics or enhance their understanding of DC-DC converter technology.
