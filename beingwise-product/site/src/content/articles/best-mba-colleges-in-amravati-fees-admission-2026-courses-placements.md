## Admission Process and Entrance Exams for 2026

For prospective students aiming for the 2026 admission cycle, understanding the required entrance exams is crucial. The most commonly accepted entrance examinations for MBA programs in Amravati include:

*   **MAH CET:** A state-level entrance test for admission to management programs in Maharashtra.
*   **CAT (Common Admission Test):** A national-level exam for admission to IIMs and other top B-schools.
*   **MAT (Management Aptitude Test):** A national-level exam accepted by a wide range of B-schools.
*   **XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test):** Conducted by XLRI Jamshedpur, also accepted by many other institutions.
*   **CMAT (Common Management Admission Test):** A national-level exam conducted by NTA.

Applicants should regularly check the official websites of their target colleges and the respective examination bodies for the latest updates on application deadlines and exam schedules for 2026.
