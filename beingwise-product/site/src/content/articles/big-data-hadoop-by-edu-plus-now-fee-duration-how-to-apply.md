# Big Data Hadoop by edu plus now: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone looking to dive into the world of big data, the 'Big Data Hadoop' online certification program offers a structured path. This course, provided by Edu Plus Now, aims to equip students with essential skills in the Hadoop open-source framework and its various tools.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a concise, six-day online certification program focusing on Big Data Hadoop.
*   The course combines recorded lectures with live, interactive sessions for practical learning.
*   Participants will gain hands-on experience with key Hadoop tools like HDFS, YARN, MapReduce, Hive, Pig, Flume, and Sqoop.
*   Career support and a certificate of completion from Edu Plus Now are provided upon successful program completion.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

The 'Big Data Hadoop' certification is designed to build competence in the core concepts of Big Data and the practical application of the Hadoop ecosystem. Collaborating with the Indian Statistical Institute, Edu Plus Now delivers a program that blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience.

The learning format includes both video and text-based content, delivered primarily through self-study and virtual classroom sessions. Live online sessions are scheduled over six days, typically on weekends, ensuring an interactive learning environment. This approach allows students to grasp the principles of Big Data and effectively utilize Hadoop tools, preparing them for roles as Big Data developers or analytics engineers.

## What You Will Learn

The syllabus is carefully structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of Big Data and the Hadoop framework. Key areas of study include:

*   **Introduction to Big Data and Hadoop:** Understanding the role of Hadoop in processing large datasets and its architectural components.
*   **HDFS (Hadoop Distributed File System):** Learning the processes of writing and reading data within HDFS.
*   **Linux and Hadoop Commands:** Familiarity with fundamental Linux commands relevant to Hadoop and specific Hadoop/HDFS commands.
*   **Hadoop Ecosystem Tools:** In-depth knowledge and practical application of tools such as YARN, MapReduce, Hive, Pig, Flume, and Sqoop.

This curriculum is designed to impart both foundational knowledge and practical skills, making participants proficient in managing and analyzing large datasets using industry-standard tools.

## Eligibility and Certification

To qualify for the 'Big Data Hadoop' course certificate, candidates must successfully complete the entire program. The certificate is awarded by Edu Plus Now upon fulfillment of all course requirements. While specific academic prerequisites are not detailed, the course is ideal for individuals and students aspiring to careers as Big Data developers or Big Data Analytics engineers.

## Fees and Application Process

The program fee for the 'Big Data Hadoop' certification is ₹15,000, plus applicable GST. This fee covers all aspects of the online course, including access to learning materials, live sessions, projects, assignments, and career support.

To apply for the course, interested candidates can follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official course page on the Edu Plus Now website.
2.  Locate and click the 'Enroll Now' button.
3.  Complete the registration form by providing necessary details such as your name, phone number, and email address.
4.  Proceed to pay the course fee online to finalize your registration.

Upon successful payment, your enrollment in the program will be confirmed, granting you access to the learning platform.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to gain specialized skills in a rapidly growing field, guided by academic and industry experts.
