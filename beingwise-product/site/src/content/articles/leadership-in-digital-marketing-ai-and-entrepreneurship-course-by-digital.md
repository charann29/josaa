# Leadership in Digital Marketing, AI & Entrepreneurship Course by Digital Academy 360: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This comprehensive program from Digital Academy 360 is designed to equip you with advanced skills in digital marketing, artificial intelligence, and entrepreneurship. It's a fantastic opportunity for anyone looking to make a mark in the rapidly evolving digital world.

## Key Takeaways

*   An intensive 9-month program covering digital marketing, AI, and entrepreneurship.
*   Features extensive hands-on experience with over 15 real-time projects and 20 live agency campaigns.
*   Includes a 3-month integrated internship and leads to more than 22 globally recognized certifications.
*   Suitable for graduates from any background, even without prior digital marketing experience.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Leadership in Digital Marketing, AI & Entrepreneurship program spans nine months, offering a deep dive into crucial aspects of the digital landscape. The curriculum includes 30 in-depth courses and 10 modules, providing comprehensive knowledge across the industry.

A significant part of the learning involves practical application. Students gain hands-on experience through more than 15 real-time projects and participate in over 20 live agency campaigns. These campaigns cover diverse sectors such as education, real estate, e-commerce, banking, and tourism, giving you broad exposure. You'll also execute paid campaigns on major platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google Ads, and Quora, building expertise in performance marketing strategies.

## What You Will Learn

This program is structured to impart a blend of artificial intelligence knowledge, essential digital marketing skills, and entrepreneurial acumen. You will learn to:

*   **Leverage AI for SEO and Content Marketing:** Understand how AI can enhance reach and engagement.
*   **Manage Social Media and Online Reputation with AI:** Develop strategies for brand presence across various platforms.
*   **Execute Paid Advertising Campaigns:** Master advertising on Google, Meta (Facebook), LinkedIn, Twitter, and YouTube.
*   **Plan Media and Analyze Data:** Gain skills in media planning, buying, data analytics, and visualization.
*   **Automate Marketing Processes:** Become proficient in using marketing automation tools.

## Program Highlights and Offerings

The program stands out with several key features designed to maximize your learning and career prospects:

*   **Extensive Case Studies:** Over 50 case studies provide real-world insights.
*   **Hands-on Learning:** Emphasis on practical application and live agency projects.
*   **Expert Guidance:** Learn from more than 12 industry experts and domain-specific trainers.
*   **Integrated Internship:** A valuable 3-month internship with prominent digital marketing agencies.
*   **Certifications:** Earn over 22 globally recognized certifications.
*   **Career Support:** Receive assistance to help you secure roles in the digital marketing field.

The learning format combines self-study with virtual classroom sessions, utilizing video and text-based materials. The medium of instruction is English.

## Who Is This Program For?

This program is ideal for individuals from all academic backgrounds, with no specific degree or prior experience required. It's particularly well-suited for recent graduates looking to launch a career in digital marketing. Upon completion, you will be prepared for various roles, including:

*   Digital Marketing Manager
*   Digital Marketing Analyst
*   SEO Analyst
*   Content Marketing Specialist
*   Social Media Manager
*   Email Marketer
*   Analytics Manager

## Eligibility and Admission Process

The program is open to all backgrounds, and there are no specific academic prerequisites or prior work experience needed. It is particularly beneficial for graduates aiming to enter the digital marketing field.

To apply for admission to the Leadership in Digital Marketing, AI & Entrepreneurship program at Digital Academy 360, prospective students generally begin by selecting the course from the available program list.

## Course and Certification Details

While specific fee details are best obtained directly from Digital Academy 360, the program does offer a certificate upon successful completion. This certificate is provided by Digital Academy 360 itself, validating your acquired skills and knowledge.

This program offers a robust pathway to thrive in the dynamic world of digital marketing, AI, and entrepreneurship.
