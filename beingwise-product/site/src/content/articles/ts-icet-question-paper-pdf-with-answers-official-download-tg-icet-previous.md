## Understanding the Importance of Previous Papers

Engaging with past TS ICET question papers is more than just practice; it's a strategic part of your preparation. By working through these papers, you gain insights into the recurring themes, the difficulty level of questions, and the distribution of marks across different sections. ## Accessing Official TS ICET Question Papers

The Telangana Council of Higher Education (TGCHE) is responsible for conducting the TS ICET examination and subsequently makes the official question papers available. Since the exam is typically held in multiple shifts, you can find shift-wise question papers. These are usually released on the official website, icet.tgche.ac.in, shortly after the examination, often alongside the official answer keys and individual candidate response sheets. Always refer to the official TGCHE portal for the most accurate and up-to-date resources.
