# Best Colleges accepting SNAP Score in India

The Symbiosis National Aptitude (SNAP) Test is a popular entrance examination for postgraduate management programs in India. Over 30 institutions across the country consider SNAP scores for admission to their MBA and PGDM courses, with the vast majority being private colleges.

## Key takeaways

*   More than 30 colleges in India accept SNAP scores for MBA/PGDM admissions.
*   The application window for SNAP 2025 is currently open, with a deadline of November 20, 2025.
*   The SNAP 2025 examination will be conducted on December 6, 14, and 20, 2025.
*   Many Symbiosis International (Deemed University) constituent institutes are among the top choices for SNAP test-takers.

## Understanding the SNAP Exam and Admissions

The SNAP test is a crucial step for aspiring management students aiming for various programs offered by Symbiosis International (Deemed University) and other affiliated institutions. It assesses a candidate's general aptitude, including analytical reasoning, general English, and quantitative abilities. After the exam, candidates typically need to clear a Group Exercise, Personal Interview, and Written Ability Test (GE-PI-WAT) round conducted by the individual institutes to secure admission.

## Top Institutions Accepting SNAP Scores

Several well-regarded institutions accept SNAP scores. These include various Symbiosis institutes known for their specialized management programs. Some prominent names include:

*   Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM)
*   Symbiosis Institute of International Business (SIIB)
*   Symbiosis Institute of Digital and Telecom Management (SIDTM)
*   Symbiosis Institute of Operations Management (SIOM)
*   Symbiosis Centre for Information Technology (SCIT)
*   LM Thapar School of Management

These institutions offer a diverse range of specializations catering to different career aspirations.

## Important Dates for SNAP 2025

Prospective students should note the key dates for the SNAP 2025 examination cycle. The application process for MBA/PGDM courses through SNAP 2025 is already underway. The last date to submit applications is November 20, 2025. The official SNAP Test 2025 will be held on three separate dates: December 6, December 14, and December 20, 2025. Candidates can choose to appear for one or more tests, with their best score considered for admissions.

## Fees and Specializations

The annual fees for MBA programs accepting SNAP scores can vary significantly. While a few colleges might offer programs with fees under INR 1 lakh or between INR 2-5 lakh, the majority of programs, particularly at top-tier private institutions, typically have annual fees exceeding INR 5 lakh. 

Colleges accepting SNAP scores offer a wide array of specializations to prepare students for various roles in the business world. Some of the popular specializations include:

*   Finance
*   Human Resources
*   Sales and Marketing
*   International Business
*   Operations Management
*   Digital Management
*   Telecom Management
*   Information Technology

This variety allows students to choose a program that aligns with their career goals and interests.

## NIRF Rankings and College Selection

When evaluating colleges, the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) rankings, published annually by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, can be a helpful guide. These rankings consider various parameters such as teaching, learning & resources, research and professional practice, graduation outcomes, outreach & inclusivity, and perception. Checking the latest NIRF rankings for management institutions can provide valuable insights into the academic standing and overall quality of colleges accepting SNAP scores.
