# Henry Harvin Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Henry Harvin Courses

Henry Harvin offers a wide array of professional courses and certifications designed to enhance skills across various industries. These programs cater to students and working professionals looking to upskill, reskill, or gain specialized knowledge.

## Key Takeaways

*   Henry Harvin provides diverse certification and postgraduate programs in fields like Data Science, HR, Finance, and Content Writing.
*   Many courses include practical projects, internship assistance, and placement support to help participants gain real-world experience.
*   Programs often come with dual certifications, such as those from Henry Harvin and recognized bodies like NSDC or OTHM UK.
*   Course durations vary significantly, from short-term practitioner courses spanning a few days to extensive postgraduate programs lasting up to 12 months.

## Exploring Popular Henry Harvin Programs

Henry Harvin's offerings span numerous in-demand sectors. Here's a look at some of their prominent courses:

### Data Science & AI Programs

Data science and artificial intelligence are rapidly growing fields, and Henry Harvin offers several programs to help individuals enter or advance within them. The **Diploma in Data Science** is a 288-hour program priced at ₹75,000, providing foundational and advanced knowledge. For a more extensive experience, the **Post Graduate Program in Data Science** requires 192 hours of study. Additionally, an **Advanced Certificate Program in Data Science & AI** is offered in collaboration with IIT Guwahati, also a 192-hour commitment, indicating a strong academic partnership.

### Human Resources & Management

For those aspiring to careers in human resources, the **Certified Human Resource Professional (CHRP) Course** is a comprehensive 288-hour program. Priced at ₹1,20,000, it covers essential HR functions like talent acquisition, compensation, and operations. This course includes project work on recruitment processes and offers internship and placement assistance. Another option is the **Post Graduate Program in Project Management**, a 178-hour course designed to equip individuals with skills for leading and managing projects effectively.

### Finance & Accounting Certifications

Henry Harvin provides several specialized programs in finance and accounting. The **Post Graduate Program in GST** is a 12-month course costing ₹39,000, focusing on Goods and Services Tax regulations. Other notable finance courses include **ACCA Course Training** (900 hours), **US CPA Course | Certified Public Accountant Training** (250 hours), and **ACCA Diploma IFRS Training Course** (75 hours). Shorter, focused programs like the **Certified Financial Analytics Practitioner Course** (4 days, ₹15,000) and **Certified Business Analytics Practitioner** (4 days) are also available.

### Teaching & Content Creation

For educators and aspiring content creators, Henry Harvin offers specialized certifications. The **Professional Certificate in Teaching and Learning Course** is a 10-month program aimed at enhancing teaching skills, incorporating modern techniques and technology. It includes projects on child development and teaching methodologies, offering OTHM UK Level 6 teaching certification along with Henry Harvin and NSDC certifications. This program also provides entrepreneurship mentorship and guaranteed internships. In content creation, options like the **Diploma in Content Writing** (144 hours) and the **Post Graduate Program in Content Writing** (120 hours) are available.
