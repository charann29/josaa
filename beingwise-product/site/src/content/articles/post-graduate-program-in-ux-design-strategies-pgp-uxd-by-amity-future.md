# Post Graduate Program in UX Design Strategies (PGP - UXD) by Amity Future Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Amity Future Academy offers a Post Graduate Program in UX Design Strategies (PGP - UXD) designed to equip aspiring and current professionals with advanced skills in User Experience (UX) architecture and consumer testing. This program aims to enhance your career prospects in the rapidly evolving field of UX design.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a postgraduate-level program focusing on high-demand UX design skills.
*   The course spans 12 months, plus preparatory sessions, requiring about 7-8 hours of study per week.
*   It offers an interactive online learning experience with 24/7 access to materials and live sessions with industry experts.
*   Successful completion earns you a Post Graduate Program in UX Design Strategies certificate from Amity Future Academy and Amity University alumni status.

## Program Overview

The PGP - UXD program is crafted to immerse students in the core principles of User Experience. You will delve into user analysis for design meaning, design policy, and the fundamentals of thesis and architecture, guided by prominent UX design leaders and academics. The curriculum is developed with insights from Berkeley Global and IBM, ensuring a blend of academic rigor and industry relevance. The program aims to bolster your efficiency and intellectual skills, enabling you to apply learned concepts to real-life ventures and case studies.

## What You Will Learn

Through the PGP - UXD training, you will develop a comprehensive understanding of various facets of UX design. Key learning outcomes include:

*   Identifying the production and policy aspects of UX/UI.
*   Comprehending user-centric architecture principles.
*   Executing concept mortgages effectively.
*   Proposing strategies for improved user behavior.
*   Developing robust UX strategies.

This program emphasizes practical application, with assignments, online quizzes, and case studies forming part of the assessment structure.

## Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Post Graduate Program in UX Design Strategies, candidates must meet specific requirements:

*   **Work Experience:** A minimum of one year of professional work experience is necessary.
*   **Education:** Fresh graduates, particularly those with a background in UX design, are encouraged to apply.

To earn the certification, enrolled candidates must achieve a minimum of 60% in the Semester End Exam and 40% in the internal curriculum, which includes quizzes, tasks, and case studies.

## Fees and Duration

The Post Graduate Program in UX Design Strategies is a 12-month program, supplemented by preparatory sessions, designed for flexible learning. It requires a weekly commitment of 7-8 hours. The program fee is ₹185,000, inclusive of all applicable taxes. This payment can be conveniently made using credit cards, debit cards, or net banking.

| Fee Category | Amount in INR |
| :------------------- | :------------------- |
| Programme Fee | ₹185,000 (including taxes) |

Upon successful completion and meeting the qualifying criteria, participants will receive a certificate from Amity Future Academy.

## How to Apply

Interested candidates can begin the admission process by ensuring they meet the eligibility criteria, including the required work experience and educational background. The application typically involves registering and completing the payment for the program fees. Detailed instructions for application are usually available on the official Amity Future Academy portal.

This program offers a valuable opportunity to advance your career in UX design with a comprehensive, industry-aligned curriculum.
