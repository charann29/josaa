# My Son Got CSE in SRM AP, UPES, and GRIET – Which One for Guaranteed Placement?

Choosing the right B.Tech CSE program is a big decision, especially when aiming for strong placement outcomes. Let's break down the options for your son to help you make an informed choice.

## Key Takeaways

*   **SRM AP offers competitive average packages and a high placement rate.** Its reported 100% placement for B.Tech CSE is notable.
*   **UPES provides a good average package, with a strong showing for its top performers.** The focus on specialized CSE branches might appeal to some students.
*   **GRIET, affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad, demonstrates impressive highest packages.** Its strong placement numbers, particularly for a JNTU-affiliated college, are a highlight.
*   **Consider factors beyond just the highest package.** Look at average salaries, the number of companies visiting, and overall student feedback.

## SRM University, Andhra Pradesh

SRM University, AP, has quickly established itself as a significant player in engineering education. For its B.Tech Computer Science Engineering (CSE) program, the university reports a remarkable 100% placement rate. This means all students who sought placements from the CSE batch found opportunities. The highest package offered to a CSE graduate at SRM AP reached an impressive ₹50 LPA, while the average package stood at ₹9.90 LPA. These figures suggest a strong industry connection and a good return on investment for students pursuing CSE here.

## UPES, Dehradun

UPES, located in Dehradun, is known for its industry-focused programs, often offering specialized CSE branches. For its CSE graduates, the highest package reported was ₹33 LPA. The average package for UPES CSE students was ₹8.59 LPA. Interestingly, the university highlights that the top 10% of its placed students achieved average packages ranging from ₹15 LPA to ₹17 LPA. This indicates that high-achieving students at UPES can secure very competitive offers, potentially due to their specialized skill sets or strong academic performance.

## GRIET, Hyderabad (Affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad)

GRIET (Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology) is a private college affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) Hyderabad. Being affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad often means adherence to a well-established curriculum and examination system. GRIET reported a highest package of ₹51.65 LPA, secured through a top tech company. While the lowest package stood at ₹3 LPA, the overall placement data for the 2025 batch (as per their official records) shows 1016 offers made by 86 companies, with 382 students successfully placed. These numbers indicate a robust placement drive and a good number of opportunities for its students.

## Making Your Decision

When comparing these three institutions, consider what truly matters for your son's career path. If a high average package and a near-guaranteed placement rate are top priorities, SRM AP presents a very strong case. If your son is a high-achiever looking for specialized CSE programs and believes he can land in the top percentile, UPES offers good prospects. GRIET, with its affiliation to JNTU Hyderabad and an impressive highest package, also stands out, particularly for students looking for opportunities within a well-established university system. Look beyond just the highest package and consider the average salary, the number of companies that recruit, and the overall campus environment and faculty expertise.

Ultimately, the best choice will align with your son's academic strengths, career aspirations, and what kind of learning environment he will thrive in.
