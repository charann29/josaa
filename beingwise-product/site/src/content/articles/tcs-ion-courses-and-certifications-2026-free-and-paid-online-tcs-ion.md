## TCS iON Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online TCS iON Courses

TCS iON offers a diverse range of online courses and certifications designed to equip students and professionals with industry-relevant skills. These programs, developed in collaboration with academic and industry leaders, aim to bridge the gap between education and employment.

## Key Takeaways

*   TCS iON provides both free and paid online courses, many leading to industry-recognized certifications.
*   Programs often feature experimental learning, including online lectures, practical demonstrations, and industry-oriented assignments.
*   Many courses offer valuable credit points and digital certificates upon successful completion.
*   Expert mentors from leading academic and industrial institutions guide learners throughout their journey.

## Understanding TCS iON Certifications

TCS iON's certification programs are crafted to enhance specific skill sets demanded by various industries. These aren't just theoretical courses; they often incorporate practical, hands-on learning experiences. The focus is on developing competencies that make participants job-ready and competitive in their chosen fields. By collaborating with renowned institutions and industry experts, TCS iON ensures that the curriculum remains current and relevant to evolving market needs.

## Featured Industry Honors Certification Programs

TCS iON offers several specialized 'Industry Honors' certification programs, typically lasting five to six months and costing approximately ₹20,000. These programs are designed for deeper engagement and skill development in critical areas.

### Industrial Mechatronic Systems

This program is tailored for mechanical engineering students. It focuses on developing the knowledge and skills needed to create innovative products and systems for sectors such as communication, healthcare, transportation, energy, space, and defense. The curriculum includes online lectures, tutorials, practical demonstrations, and industry-focused assignments. Successful completion earns participants five credit points and a digital certificate, with potential internship opportunities. Skills covered include automation, robotics, and core engineering principles.

### Solar Energy Technology and its Applications

This certification provides insights into various solar energy technologies and their practical applications. Learners explore solar photovoltaic technology, thermal energy from power plants, hybrid solar systems, solar ponds, and solar cooling for energy storage. The course benefits from academic and industrial expert mentors. Participants may also have the opportunity to attend workshops by the Tata Power Skill Development Institute (TPSDI) for practical industry exposure, subject to specific conditions. Skills gained include knowledge of clean energy, engineering, environmental science, physics, chemistry, and electronics.

### Design for Additive Manufacturing

This course offers a comprehensive understanding of additive manufacturing (AM) techniques and strategies, including 3D printing capabilities, limitations, material resources, and design frameworks for AM regulations. It provides professional insights into design processes and the scope of additive manufacturing. Mentors include academic figures like Dr. Sajan Kapil (Assistant Professor at IIT Guwahati) and industry experts such as Manickam Loganathan and Daniel Arockiam from TCS. The program, conducted in a virtual hands-on environment, awards five credit points and a digital certificate upon completion.
