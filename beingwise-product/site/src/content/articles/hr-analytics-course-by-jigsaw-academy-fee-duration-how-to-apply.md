# HR Analytics Course by Jigsaw Academy: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to merge the human element of HR with the power of data, Jigsaw Academy offers a comprehensive HR Analytics course. This program is designed to equip professionals with the skills to transform HR functions into data-driven strategies.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is a self-paced, online program with a duration of three months.
*   It focuses on making HR professionals adept at using data for better decision-making.
*   The total fee, including taxes, is ₹24,662.
*   Participants need a basic grasp of HR concepts and Excel to enroll.

## Course Overview and Learning Experience

This HR Analytics course is delivered entirely online, allowing for a flexible, self-study approach. The content is presented through a mix of video and text-based modules, accessible via both web and a dedicated mobile app. Designed by industry experts, the curriculum aims to provide a deep understanding of analytical techniques specifically for human resources.

Upon successful completion, participants will receive a certification from Jigsaw Academy. The program also includes practical case studies and offers faculty and technical support to guide learners through the material.

## Who Is This Course For?

This program is particularly well-suited for HR managers and professionals who wish to enhance their analytical capabilities. If your goal is to leverage data to improve workplace efficiency, talent management, and strategic decision-making within an HR context, this course can be highly beneficial.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, moving from foundational concepts to advanced analytical techniques. Key areas of study include:

*   **HR Analytics Fundamentals:** An overview of the field and analytical problem-solving.
*   **Statistics for HR:** Introduction to statistics, advanced statistics, and predictive analytics.
*   **Data Visualization:** Techniques for effectively presenting HR data.
*   **Talent Management Analytics:** Using data for talent management, workforce analytics, and optimizing hiring strategies.
*   **Practical Application:** Numerous case studies, such as understanding correlation using survey data, analyzing compensation data, logistic regression on employee attrition, and assessing the business value of retention.
*   **Implementation:** Guidance on integrating analytics into HR practices.

Through these modules, participants will gain expertise in making data-driven decisions, applying analytical techniques for reporting, and improving measurement maturity within HR.

## Eligibility and Certification

To enroll in the HR Analytics course, candidates should have a fundamental understanding of HR principles and proficiency in Microsoft Excel. The certification of completion is awarded by Jigsaw Academy to participants who successfully finish all sessions of the program.

## Course Fees

The total fee for the HR Analytics course is ₹24,662. This amount is inclusive of all applicable taxes. A breakdown of the fees is provided below:

| Head       | Amount (₹) |
| :--------- | :--------- |
| Course Fee | 20,900     |
| Taxes      | 3,762      |
| Total Fees | 24,662     |

## How to Apply

The application process for the HR Analytics course is straightforward and conducted online:

1.  Visit the official Jigsaw Academy website for HR analytics courses.
2.  Locate and click on the 'Enroll Now' option.
3.  If you have a coupon code, enter it and click 'Apply', then proceed to checkout.
4.  You will need to sign in and verify your identity using a LinkedIn, Google, or Facebook ID.
5.  Complete the payment using the available payment gateway options, which typically include debit cards, credit cards, and net banking.
6.  After payment, it is recommended to email your transaction slip to the officials for confirmation of your enrollment.

This course offers a practical pathway for HR professionals to enhance their skills and contribute to a more data-informed organizational culture.
