## Accessing the Official CMAT 2026 Answer Key

The NTA has made the CMAT 2026 answer key available exclusively online on the official exam website, cmat.nta.nic.in. To download your answer key, question paper, and response sheet, you will need to log in using your CMAT application number and date of birth. This comprehensive package allows you to compare your marked answers with the officially correct ones.

## Understanding the Answer Key Objection Process

Before the final answer key was released, NTA provided a provisional answer key. During that period, candidates had the opportunity to challenge any answers they believed were incorrect. The process involved logging into the official CMAT portal, identifying the specific question and its corresponding option ID, and submitting an objection along with supporting documentation. A non-refundable fee of INR 200 was charged for each question challenged. The NTA then reviewed these objections and, if found valid, incorporated the necessary revisions into the final answer key.
