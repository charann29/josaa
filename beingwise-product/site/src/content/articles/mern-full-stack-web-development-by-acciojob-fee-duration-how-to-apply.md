# MERN Full Stack Web Development by AccioJob: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This BeingWise guide explores AccioJob's MERN Full Stack Web Development certificate program, offered in collaboration with the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). It's designed to equip aspiring developers with a comprehensive understanding of MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js) stack technologies, essential for modern web development.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program is an 8-month certificate course in MERN Full Stack Development.
*   It includes extensive practical training, live sessions, and one-on-one doubt clearing.
*   Participants receive a certificate accredited by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) upon successful completion.
*   AccioJob offers job assistance to eligible candidates after completing the program.

## Course Overview and Structure

The MERN Full Stack Development course is structured to provide a deep dive into all aspects of full-stack web development using the MERN stack. The curriculum covers foundational programming concepts, JavaScript from basics to advanced levels, front-end development with React and Redux, and back-end development with Node.js and Express.js.

The program spans 8 months, with participants gaining access to live learning resources for a full 12 months. Instruction is delivered in English, utilizing a blend of self-study, virtual classrooms, and video and text-based materials.

## What You Will Learn

The comprehensive syllabus is designed to build a strong foundation and advanced skills in web development. Key learning areas include:

*   **Programming Fundamentals:** Covering variables, data types, conditional statements, loops, and functions.
*   **Data Structures and Algorithms:** Introduction to arrays, ArrayLists, sorting techniques, strings, and recursion.
*   **Web Foundations:** HTML and CSS for structuring and styling web pages.
*   **JavaScript:** From fundamentals to advanced concepts, crucial for both front-end and back-end development.
*   **Backend Development:** Building robust server-side applications using Node.js.
*   **Frontend Development:** Creating dynamic user interfaces with React and managing application state with Redux.

## Practical Experience and Support

AccioJob emphasizes practical learning through various components to ensure industry readiness. These include:

*   **Assignments and Projects:** Hands-on tasks and real-world projects to apply learned concepts.
*   **Internships:** Opportunities to gain practical industry experience.
*   **Mock Interviews:** To prepare candidates for job placements.
*   **Doubt-Clearing Sessions:** One-to-one support to address individual queries.
*   **Weekly Contests:** To foster competitive learning and skill enhancement.
*   **Soft Skill Resources:** To develop essential professional skills alongside technical expertise.

Upon successful completion, students receive a certificate accredited by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), validating their skills.

## Eligibility and Admission Process

The MERN Full Stack Course is open to a wide range of individuals, including students, recent graduates, and working professionals looking to upskill or transition into web development. It is particularly beneficial for aspiring software developers and web developers.

To apply for the program, interested candidates should follow these steps:

1.  Visit the official AccioJob website for the MERN Full Stack Development program.
2.  Click on the 'Apply Now for Free' option and provide the required personal details, including a valid mobile number.
3.  Enter the One-Time Password (OTP) sent to the registered mobile number.
4.  Choose a payment option, such as 'Pay After Placement' or 'Semi-Pay After Placement', depending on the available schemes.
5.  Complete the fee payment to begin the program.

This program offers a structured path for individuals aiming to become proficient MERN stack developers, backed by industry-relevant training and placement support.
