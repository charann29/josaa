# Biology XI Part-I by National Council of Educational Research and Training via Swayam: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This online certificate course offers a comprehensive look at Class 11 Biology topics, designed to enhance understanding for students through a self-paced, digital learning experience.

## Key takeaways

*   The course provides a certificate upon successful completion, issued by NCERT and Swayam.
*   It is a 24-week program, delivered entirely online through video lectures and e-text.
*   The content focuses on core Class 11 Biology subjects, including diversity of life, structural organization, and cell biology.
*   Learning is self-directed, with assessments and interactive resources to support student progress.

## Course Overview

Developed by the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) and coordinated by Prof. Dr. Munindra Ruwali from the Department of Education in Science and Mathematics (DESM), NCERT, this program covers essential Class 11 Biology concepts. The curriculum delves into various forms of life, biological classification, and the structural organization of plants and animals. Students will engage with expert-led video lectures and a wealth of digital resources, including supplementary web links, to deepen their knowledge.

## Learning Format and Resources

The Biology XI Part-I course is structured for self-study, allowing students to learn at their own pace. The primary modes of instruction are video lectures and e-text materials, all delivered in English. Participants will have access to an interactive platform featuring reading materials, e-resources, and curated web links to provide a holistic learning experience. Regular self-assessments, including multiple-choice questions, are integrated throughout the course to help students gauge their understanding.

## Duration and Certification

This certificate program spans 24 weeks. Upon successfully completing the course and achieving a score of 70% or higher in the final assessment, students will be awarded a certificate. This certification is jointly provided by NCERT and Swayam, recognizing the learner's achievement in Class 11 Biology.

## Who Should Enroll?

This course is specifically designed for students currently in Class 11 or those who wish to build a strong foundation in Biology at the school level. It's an excellent opportunity for anyone looking to reinforce their understanding of key biological principles through a structured, online format.

## How to Apply

To enroll in the Biology XI Part-I Certification Course, prospective students should follow these simple steps:

1.  Visit the official Swayam platform.
2.  Locate the "Sign-in" or "Register" option, typically found in the top right corner of the page.
3.  Either sign in with an existing account or create a new one using your preferred username and password.
4.  Once logged in, search for the Biology XI Part-I course and follow the instructions to enroll.

## Course Syllabus

The program covers a wide range of topics, structured into chapters:

| Chapter       | Topics Covered                                                                                                           |
| :------------ | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| The Living World | Introduction to the living world                                                                                         |
| Biological Classification | Different systems of classifying living organisms                                                                        |
| Kingdom Plantae | Thallophyta, Bryophyta, Pteridophyta, Gymnosperms, and Angiosperms                                                       |
| Animal Kingdom | Biodiversity and classification of animals                                                                               |
| Plant Morphology | Study of roots, stems, leaves, inflorescence, flowers, seeds, fruits, floral diagrams, and formulae                      |
| Anatomy of Flowering Plants | Tissue systems, anatomy of organs, and secondary growth                                                                  |
| Structural Organization in Animals | Tissue systems in animals and structural organization (e.g., frog)                                                       |
| Cell: The Unit of Life | Detailed study of cell structure and function                                                                            |
| Biomolecules    | Understanding the various biomolecules essential for life                                                                |
| Cell Cycle and Cell Division | Phases of interphase, mitosis, and meiosis                                                                               |

The course concludes with a comprehensive final assessment to evaluate overall learning.

This course offers a valuable pathway for Class 11 students to gain a clearer and deeper understanding of fundamental biological concepts.
