# Alliance University B.Tech. in Civil Engineering Vs Christ University Kengeri Campus: Compare Fees, Placement, Reviews, Cutoffs

Choosing the right B.Tech. Civil Engineering program is a significant decision for any aspiring engineer. This comparison helps you evaluate two prominent institutions in Bangalore: Alliance University and Christ University's Kengeri Campus, focusing on their Civil Engineering programs.

## Key Takeaways

*   Christ University Kengeri Campus generally receives higher student ratings for its B.Tech. Civil Engineering program compared to Alliance University.
*   While Alliance University reports a higher 'highest salary' figure, Christ University's Kengeri Campus has a slightly higher median salary for its B.Tech. graduates.
*   Both universities are private institutions established around the same period, offering full-time, four-year undergraduate degrees.
*   Admission to both programs typically considers scores from national and state-level engineering entrance exams.

## Overview of the Institutions

Alliance University, established in 2010, is a private university in Bangalore. It offers a wide range of courses, including 47 B.Tech programs. Christ University's Kengeri Campus, which began operations in 2007, is also a private institution. Its Kengeri campus offers 21 B.Tech programs among its total of 41 courses.

Both institutions provide a four-year, full-time Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) degree in Civil Engineering, delivered in a traditional classroom setting.

## Student Reviews and Ratings

Student feedback offers valuable insights into the on-campus experience. Based on verified student reviews:

*   **Alliance University's B.Tech. Civil Engineering** program has an overall rating of 3.7 out of 5, based on 4 reviews. Specific aspects were rated as:
    *   Placements: 2.8
    *   Infrastructure: 4.3
    *   Faculty: 4
    *   Crowd & Campus Life: 3.8
    *   Value for Money: 3.5

*   **Christ University Kengeri Campus's B.Tech. Civil Engineering** program holds a higher overall rating of 4.2 out of 5, based on 6 reviews. Its ratings for specific parameters are:
    *   Placements: 3
    *   Infrastructure: 5
    *   Faculty: 4.5
    *   Crowd & Campus Life: 4.7
    *   Value for Money: 3.8

These ratings suggest that Christ University Kengeri Campus is generally perceived better by its students, particularly concerning infrastructure, faculty, and campus environment.

## Placement Opportunities

Placements are a crucial factor for many students. Here’s a comparison of the reported placement statistics:

*   **Alliance University:**
    *   Highest Salary: INR 60.10 Lakh
    *   Median Salary: INR 5.16 Lakh

*   **Christ University Kengeri Campus:**
    *   Highest Salary: INR 21.46 Lakh
    *   Median Salary: INR 5.20 Lakh

While Alliance University reports a significantly higher 'highest salary,' Christ University Kengeri Campus shows a slightly better 'median salary.' Prospective students should consider both these figures, as the median salary often provides a more realistic picture of general placement outcomes.
