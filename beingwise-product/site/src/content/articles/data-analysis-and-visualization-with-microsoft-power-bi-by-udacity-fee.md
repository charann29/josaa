# Data Analysis and Visualization with Microsoft Power BI by Udacity: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This program from Udacity focuses on equipping learners with essential skills in data analysis and visualization using Microsoft Power BI. It's designed to help you transform raw data into actionable insights, a critical skill in today's data-driven world.

## Key takeaways

*   Learn to connect, transform, and model data from various sources using Power BI.
*   Develop expertise in creating effective data visualizations and interactive dashboards.
*   Gain practical experience through real-world projects and dedicated technical mentor support.
*   Receive personalized career services, including resume and LinkedIn profile optimization, upon completion.

## What You Will Learn

The course curriculum is structured to build your proficiency in data analysis and visualization from the ground up. You will begin by understanding the fundamentals of data analysis and its importance in decision-making. A significant portion of the program is dedicated to mastering Power BI, covering how to connect diverse data sources, preprocess and transform data, and apply best practices for data modeling.

Key learning outcomes include:

*   Understanding the Power BI data pipeline.
*   Using DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) to create calculated tables and measures.
*   Identifying appropriate scenarios for data visualization and its potential pitfalls.
*   Designing accessible visualizations for a wide range of users.
*   Implementing navigation features in dashboards to empower users.

## Program Structure and Highlights

This Udacity program is delivered through a self-study model, combining video and text-based content with virtual classroom elements. It emphasizes hands-on learning through real-world projects, ensuring you apply theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios. Throughout your learning journey, you will benefit from:

*   **Dedicated Project Reviewers:** Receive detailed feedback on your project submissions.
*   **Technical Mentor Support:** Get assistance with technical challenges and concepts.
*   **Student Community:** Connect with peers and share insights.
*   **Custom Study Plans:** Tailor your learning pace to fit your schedule.
*   **Workspaces and Quizzes:** Utilize interactive environments and assessments to reinforce learning.
*   **Progress Tracker:** Monitor your advancement through the course material.

## Eligibility Criteria

To get the most out of this program, Udacity recommends that applicants have some basic familiarity with Microsoft Excel. This includes experience in organizing and analyzing data, as well as using fundamental Excel functions. While not mandatory, having knowledge of creating pivot tables in Excel can also be beneficial.

## Fees and Duration

The Data Analysis and Visualization with Microsoft Power BI program is designed to be completed in approximately three months. Udacity offers flexible payment options:

| Payment Plan          | Amount (per month) |
| :-------------------- | :----------------- |
| All Access Monthly    | ₹12,644            |
| All Access Bundle     | ₹10,747            |

Please note that these figures are subject to change, and it's always advisable to check the official Udacity program page for the most current fee information.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Data Analysis and Visualization with Microsoft Power BI program is a straightforward process:

1.  Visit the official program page on the Udacity website.
2.  Either register for a new Udacity account or sign in if you already have one.
3.  Proceed with the fee payment. During this step, you will be asked to fill out a short form providing details such as your city, address, state, PIN code, country, and phone number.
4.  Once the payment is confirmed, you will be officially enrolled in the course and can begin your learning journey.

This program offers a comprehensive pathway for individuals looking to build a career as a data analyst by mastering essential Power BI skills and receiving dedicated career support.
