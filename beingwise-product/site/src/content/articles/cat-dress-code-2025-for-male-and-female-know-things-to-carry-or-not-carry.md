# CAT Dress Code 2025 for Male and Female; Know Things to Carry or Not Carry on Exam Day

Preparing for the Common Admission Test (CAT) involves not just academic readiness but also understanding the guidelines for exam day. Adhering to the official instructions, including the dress code and permitted items, is crucial to ensure a smooth entry and avoid any last-minute complications that could affect your performance.

## Key takeaways

*   Always carry your CAT 2025 admit card and a valid photo ID.
*   Avoid wearing any metal jewellery or accessories.
*   Opt for simple, comfortable clothing without large pockets or elaborate designs.
*   Electronic gadgets, bags, and valuable items are strictly prohibited inside the exam centre.

## The Importance of Following CAT Exam Day Guidelines

The CAT 2025 examination, conducted by IIM Kozhikode, will take place on November 30, 2025, across approximately 400 centres in 170 cities nationwide. The entire process is under strict CCTV surveillance, with rigorous measures in place to prevent malpractices. To ensure fairness and security, the examination authority issues detailed guidelines. Familiarising yourself with these instructions well in advance is essential. This includes understanding the reporting time, the identification process, biometric attendance procedures, and seating arrangements, in addition to knowing what to wear and what to bring.

Your CAT 2025 admit card is a vital document that contains many of these important instructions. It's advisable to download it as soon as it's released and review all the guidelines carefully. This will help you prepare thoroughly for exam day, beyond just your studies.

## CAT 2025 Dress Code: What to Wear

The dress code for the CAT exam is designed to maintain security and prevent any unfair means. Following these instructions precisely is important for a hassle-free entry:

*   **Jewellery and Accessories:** Candidates are strictly not permitted to wear any jewellery, especially items containing metal. This includes rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and anklets. It's best to leave all such items at home.
*   **Footwear:** Closed-toe footwear such as shoes are generally not allowed. It is recommended to wear open-toe footwear like sandals or flip-flops.
*   **Clothing:** Avoid wearing caps, jackets, or clothes with large pockets, elaborate designs, or big buttons. Simple, light, and comfortable clothing is advisable. Plain pullovers, sweaters, or cardigans without pockets are usually permitted if the weather requires them.
*   **Medical Conditions:** If you have a metal implant, pacemaker, or any other medical condition requiring specific items, you must carry a valid medical certificate to present to the invigilators.

## Essential Items to Carry on Exam Day

Ensuring you have the correct documents and permitted items is crucial for entry into the examination hall:

*   **CAT 2025 Admit Card:** This is mandatory. Make sure it is printed clearly and is free from any smudges or damage.
*   **Valid Photo Identification:** You must carry an original, valid photo ID proof. Acceptable IDs typically include an Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving License. A photocopy will not be accepted.
*   **Pen:** A simple blue or black ballpoint pen is required for signing documents and any rough work on the provided scribble pad. Do not bring any other stationery items.
*   **Passport-sized Photographs:** Carry a few recent passport-sized photographs, similar to the one uploaded during your application, as they might be required for attendance sheets or other formalities.
*   **Medical Prescription (if applicable):** Candidates with diabetes or other specific medical conditions may be allowed to carry items like fruits or water, but only if they have a valid medical prescription for it.

## Items Strictly Not Allowed Inside the Exam Centre

To maintain the integrity of the examination, several items are strictly prohibited. Bringing any of these could lead to immediate disqualification:

*   **Electronic Gadgets:** This includes mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators (even non-programmable ones), digital watches, cameras, headphones, Bluetooth devices, and any other electronic communication or storage devices.
*   **Bags and Valuables:** There will be no facility to store bags or valuable items at the test centre. It is strongly advised not to bring them.
*   **Food and Beverages:** Apart from specific medical exceptions with a prescription, no eatables or beverages are allowed inside the exam hall.
*   **Stationery (Other than Pen):** Items like pencils, erasers, sharpeners, geometry boxes, or paper are not allowed. Rough sheets will be provided at the centre.
*   **Watches:** Even simple analog watches are usually not permitted, as the exam hall typically has a clock. It's best to avoid wearing any watch.

By carefully reviewing and adhering to these guidelines, you can ensure a stress-free experience on your CAT 2025 exam day, allowing you to focus solely on your performance.
