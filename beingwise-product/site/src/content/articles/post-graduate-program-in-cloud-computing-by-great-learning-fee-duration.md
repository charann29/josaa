# Post Graduate Program in Cloud Computing by Great Learning: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

Cloud computing is rapidly transforming the technology landscape, and this Post Graduate Program from Great Learning is designed to equip you with the essential skills to thrive in this evolving field. It offers a comprehensive curriculum to help you become proficient in designing, planning, and scaling cloud-based solutions.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an 8-month, certification-level Post Graduate program focused on cloud computing.
*   It requires a Bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks and a minimum of three years of work experience in an IT or technology-related role.
*   The program emphasizes hands-on learning, including lab and industry projects, and offers career assistance.
*   The total fee for the program is ₹1,30,000, plus applicable GST.

## Program Overview and Structure

This program is tailored for IT professionals looking to advance their careers in cloud computing. It aims to provide both a business and practitioner's perspective on fundamental cloud concepts. The curriculum covers core skills needed to build and manage reliable applications on leading cloud platforms.

The learning experience combines self-study with virtual classroom sessions, delivered through video and text-based content. Classes are typically scheduled on weekends, making it accessible for working professionals. The program encompasses over 300 hours of learning content, culminating in a certification from Great Lakes Executive Learning upon successful completion.

## What You Will Learn

The program's syllabus is designed to transform participants into competent cloud developers capable of resolving technical challenges and identifying business risks. Key areas of learning include:

*   **OpenStack Platforms:** You will gain practical knowledge in managing and monitoring remote servers hosted on the internet, focusing on troubleshooting and enhancing business productivity within OpenStack environments.
*   **AWS Platform Skills:** The curriculum covers both foundational and advanced concepts of cloud computing, with a strong emphasis on Amazon Web Services (AWS). You will learn to utilize AWS for advanced functions like Machine Learning (ML), Big Data, Internet of Things (IoT), and DevOps.
*   **Broad Cloud Computing Applications:** The program ensures a wide understanding of various cloud computing applications and their real-world implementation.

Upon completing the program, you will be equipped to handle technical glitches, anticipate future risks in business management, and proficiently work with various cloud technologies.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Post Graduate Program in Cloud Computing, candidates must meet the following requirements:

*   **Educational Qualification:** A Bachelor's degree from a recognized institution with a minimum aggregate score of 50%.
*   **Work Experience:** A minimum of three years of professional experience in an IT or technology-related role. This includes experience in areas such as database administration, quality assurance/testing, or technology infrastructure management.

## Fees and Certification

The total program fee for the Post Graduate Program in Cloud Computing is ₹1,30,000. This amount is exclusive of Goods and Services Tax (GST), which will be applied additionally.

| Fee Component | Amount |
| :---------------- | :----------- |
| Programme Fees | ₹1,30,000 + GST |

Upon successful completion of all program requirements, participants will receive a certification from Great Lakes Executive Learning. This certification acknowledges your expertise in cloud computing gained through the program.

## How to Apply

Interested candidates should visit the official Great Learning website to find detailed application instructions and the application portal. The application process typically involves submitting your academic transcripts, proof of work experience, and completing any required assessments or interviews as specified by the institution.

This program offers a robust pathway for IT professionals to upskill and secure promising careers in the rapidly expanding cloud computing sector.
