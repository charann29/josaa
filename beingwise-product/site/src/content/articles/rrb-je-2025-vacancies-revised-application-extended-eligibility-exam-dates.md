# RRB JE 2025: Vacancies (Revised), Application (Extended), Eligibility, Exam Dates, Syllabus & Pattern

The Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) conduct the Junior Engineer (JE) examination, a highly sought-after opportunity for engineering graduates and diploma holders aiming for a career in the Indian Railways. This page provides essential updates for the 2025 recruitment cycle, including application details, eligibility, and key dates.

## Key Takeaways

*   The application deadline for RRB JE CEN 05/2025 has been extended to December 10, 2025.
*   The total number of vacancies has been slightly increased to 2588.
*   The age relaxation benefit of three years, available in previous cycles, has been removed for the 2025 recruitment.
*   An application correction window will be available from December 13 to December 22, 2025.

## RRB JE 2025 Application Details

The online application process for RRB JE CEN 05/2025 began on October 31, 2025. Candidates interested in these positions should note the revised deadlines and ensure they complete their applications promptly.

*   **Application Start Date:** October 31, 2025
*   **Extended Application Deadline:** December 10, 2025 (until 11:59 PM)
*   **Last Date for Fee Payment:** December 12, 2025
*   **Application Correction Window:** December 13 to December 22, 2025

It is crucial for all applicants to review the official notification thoroughly before submitting their forms to understand all requirements, including the application fee structure. The application fee is INR 500 for most categories, while candidates from SC, ST, Ex-Servicemen, Female, Transgender, Minorities, or Economically Backward Class (EBC) are required to pay INR 250 for CBT 1.

## RRB JE 2025 Vacancies

Good news for aspirants: the Railway Recruitment Boards have revised the total number of vacancies for the Junior Engineer posts. The new total stands at 2588, a slight increase from the previously announced 2569 positions. This adjustment offers more opportunities for candidates across various engineering disciplines.

## Eligibility Criteria and Age Relaxation

Candidates must meet the specific eligibility criteria outlined in the official RRB JE 2025 notification. These typically include educational qualifications (e.g., engineering degree or diploma in relevant fields). A significant change for the 2025 cycle is the removal of the three-year age relaxation that was provided in the previous recruitment. Applicants should carefully check the age limits specified in the official notification to confirm their eligibility.

## Upcoming Exam Dates and Recruitment Phases

While the exact examination dates for RRB JE 2025 have not yet been announced, the recruitment process generally involves multiple stages:

*   **Computer Based Test (CBT 1):** The initial screening test.
*   **Computer Based Test (CBT 2):** A more specialized test for shortlisted candidates.
*   **Document Verification (DV):** For candidates who qualify in CBT 2.
*   **Medical Examination:** The final stage before selection.

For reference, the second round of document verification and medical examination for the previous cycle (CEN 03/2024) was scheduled for November 20, 2025, at the Railway Recruitment Board Office, Chandigarh, with medical examinations on the subsequent working day. Candidates are advised to regularly check the official RRB websites for updates on the 2025 exam schedule.

Prospective candidates are encouraged to stay updated with official announcements from the Railway Recruitment Boards regarding all stages of the RRB JE 2025 recruitment process.
