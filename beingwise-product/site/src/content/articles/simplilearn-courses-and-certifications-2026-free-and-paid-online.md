# Simplilearn Courses & Certifications 2026 - Free & Paid Online Simplilearn Courses

Simplilearn offers a diverse range of online programs, from professional certificates to full-fledged master's degrees, designed to help individuals upskill and advance their careers. These courses cater to various domains, equipping learners with practical knowledge and industry-relevant skills.

## Key takeaways

*   Simplilearn provides both certificate programs and international online master's degrees.
*   Programs often feature a blend of self-paced learning, live instructor-led sessions, and hands-on projects.
*   Collaborations with international universities like IU International University of Applied Sciences (Germany) and London South Bank University (UK) offer globally recognized qualifications.
*   Many courses focus on in-demand fields such as business analytics, AI, machine learning, and digital transformation.

## Master of Business Administration (MBA) Online Programs

Simplilearn facilitates access to international online MBA programs in collaboration with esteemed institutions. One such program, offered jointly by IU International University of Applied Sciences (Germany) and London South Bank University (UK), is a 12-month course designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of global business and management. This MBA focuses on developing leadership qualities, financial acumen, and economic insights essential for the international job market.

Students have the flexibility to pursue this program entirely online or opt for a blended format. The blended option includes an opportunity for an optional second semester on campus in Germany (either Berlin or Bad Honnef), allowing students to gain an accredited German degree while experiencing an international study environment. The curriculum also allows for specialisation in key areas like digital marketing, business analysis, design thinking, and digital transformation.

## Post Graduate Program in Business Analytics

For those looking to delve into the world of data-driven decision-making, the Post Graduate Program in Business Analytics is a valuable offering from Simplilearn. This 11-month certification course integrates self-paced video learning with practical application through industry-based projects, including scenarios from healthcare and e-commerce.

The program covers essential concepts in data and statistical analysis, equipping participants to derive actionable insights from large datasets. A significant component of the training involves Business Intelligence (BI) using Microsoft Power BI, alongside exploring quantitative analysis and predictive modeling techniques. Learners will develop skills in data mining, predictive analytics, and statistical methods, enhancing their problem-solving and decision-making capabilities, particularly through the use of tools like Excel.

## Post Graduate Program in AI and Machine Learning

In collaboration with Purdue University, Simplilearn offers the Post Graduate Program in AI and Machine Learning. This 11-month certificate program is structured to prepare individuals for careers in artificial intelligence and machine learning, with a strong emphasis on practical, industry-ready skills. The curriculum includes interactive learning experiences, often incorporating tools like Jupyter Notebook for immediate practice after sessions.

The course provides an in-depth exploration of working with real-time data, developing algorithms using supervised and unsupervised learning techniques, time series modeling, regression, and classification. It features extensive hands-on exercises (over 25) and 44 hours of instructor-led online training. Furthermore, students engage with industry projects, simulating real-world challenges faced by companies like Amazon, Uber, and IDB, allowing them to design and implement machine learning models at an industry scale.

Simplilearn's diverse course offerings aim to empower individuals with the skills needed to thrive in today's rapidly evolving professional landscape.
