# IBSAT Cut off 2026 (Expected): Check Minimum Cutoff Scores for PI Call

Understanding the expected cutoff scores for IBSAT 2026 is crucial for aspiring MBA students. These scores determine your eligibility for the subsequent selection rounds, including the Micro Presentation and Personal Interview (PI), which are vital steps toward securing admission to your preferred IBS campus.

## Key Takeaways

*   IBSAT cutoff scores are announced shortly after the results are declared, typically in the last week of December 2026.
*   Meeting the cutoff is essential for being shortlisted for the Micro Presentation and Personal Interview (PI) rounds.
*   IBS considers scores from CAT, XAT, GMAT, and NMAT by GMAC in addition to IBSAT for admission.
*   Final selection depends on performance in the entrance exam as well as the Micro Presentation and PI.

## Expected IBSAT Cutoff Scores for PI Calls

The minimum scores required to receive a call for the Micro Presentation and Personal Interview (PI) rounds at IBS Hyderabad are anticipated to be as follows for various entrance exams:

*   **CAT & XAT:** 60 percentile or higher
*   **GMAT:** 500+ score
*   **NMAT by GMAC:** 150+ score

For those aiming for scholarship opportunities, the minimum cutoff scores for eligibility for a INR 2 lakh scholarship are generally higher:

*   **CAT:** Top 75 candidates with a minimum of 90 percentile or higher
*   **XAT:** Top 25 candidates with a minimum of 80 percentile or higher
*   **GMAT:** Top 25 candidates with a minimum of 550 or higher
*   **NMAT by GMAC:** Top 100 candidates with a minimum of 225 or higher

## Factors Influencing IBSAT Cutoff

Several factors contribute to how the IBSAT cutoff scores are determined each year for admission to MBA or PGPM programs across the nine IBS campuses. These include:

*   **Exam Difficulty:** A more challenging exam might lead to lower cutoff scores.
*   **Number of Applicants:** A higher number of candidates appearing for the exam can increase competition and potentially raise cutoffs.
*   **Qualified Candidates:** The total number of candidates who successfully clear the exam also plays a role.
*   **Available Seats:** The number of seats offered in a particular program at a specific IBS campus directly impacts the cutoff.

## IBSAT Selection Process

Once you meet the IBSAT cutoff, you'll proceed to the selection briefing rounds. These stages are critical for your final admission.

### Steps in the Selection Process:

1.  **Shortlisting:** Candidates are shortlisted for selection briefings based on their IBSAT scores and the established cutoffs.
2.  **Selection Briefings:** These briefings typically involve a series of focused seminars designed to provide candidates with deeper insights into the programs and the institution.
3.  **Micro Presentation & Personal Interview (PI):** Shortlisted candidates will participate in these rounds. Performance here is a significant factor in the final selection.
4.  **Merit List & Counselling:** Based on combined performance in the entrance exam and the selection rounds, a merit list is prepared, followed by counselling for final admissions.

Successfully navigating these stages will bring you closer to securing your spot at one of the prestigious IBS campuses. Keep an eye on the official IBS website for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding cutoffs and selection processes.
