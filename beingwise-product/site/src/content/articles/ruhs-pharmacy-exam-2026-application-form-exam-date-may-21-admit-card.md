# RUHS Pharmacy Exam 2026: Application form, Exam Date (May 21), Admit Card, Answer Key, & Result

The application process for the RUHS Pharmacy exam 2026 is entirely online. Prospective students should visit the official RUHS portal during the application window to access and submit the form. It's crucial to complete this process within the specified dates to be eligible for the entrance examination.

## Admit Card

The admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the RUHS Pharmacy entrance exam. It contains vital information such as your roll number, exam center details, and exam timings. For the 2026 exam, the admit card is expected to be released on May 15, 2026. Candidates will be able to download it from the official RUHS website using their login credentials.

## Exam Date and Structure

The RUHS Pharmacy entrance exam for 2026 is tentatively scheduled for May 21, 2026. While the source material primarily discusses Nursing, the general pattern for RUHS entrance exams often includes an offline, OMR-based format. The exam is typically conducted in both English and Hindi, with a duration of two hours.

For detailed information regarding the exam pattern and syllabus specific to Pharmacy courses, candidates should refer to the official RUHS information bulletin when it is released for the 2026 academic year.

## Answer Key and Result

After the examination, RUHS usually releases a provisional answer key, allowing candidates to estimate their scores and raise objections if any. Following this, the final answer key is published, leading to the declaration of results. For the 2026 Pharmacy exam, results are tentatively expected in the first week of June 2026. Candidates can check their results on the official RUHS portal by entering their roll number and other required details.

Stay updated by regularly checking the official RUHS website for the latest announcements and detailed information regarding the Pharmacy entrance examination.
