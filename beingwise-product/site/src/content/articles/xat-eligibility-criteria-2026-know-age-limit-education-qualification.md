# XAT Eligibility Criteria 2026 - Know Age limit, Education Qualification

The XAT examination is a crucial step for admission to MBA/PGDM programs offered by XLRI Jamshedpur, other XAMI member institutions, and numerous other B-Schools across India. Understanding the eligibility requirements for XAT 2026 is essential for all aspiring candidates.

## Key takeaways

*   A Bachelor's degree of at least three years' duration from a recognized university is the primary academic requirement.
*   Final-year students awaiting results are also eligible to apply, provided they submit their degree proof by the admission year's end.
*   There is no upper age limit for appearing in the XAT examination.
*   Specific XLRI programs, such as the General Management Programme, have additional work experience criteria.

## General Eligibility for XAT 2026

To be eligible for XAT 2026, candidates must meet the following general criteria:

*   **Educational Qualification:** Applicants must hold a Bachelor's degree, or an equivalent qualification, of a minimum of three years' duration in any discipline from a university recognized by the government or an equivalent body. 
*   **Final Year Students:** Candidates who are in the final year of their Bachelor's degree program and are awaiting their results are also eligible to apply. However, it is mandatory for them to provide their degree certificate and mark sheets by a specified date, typically by the end of the admission year, as stipulated by XLRI.
*   **Minimum Marks:** While XLRI does not specify a minimum percentage for XAT eligibility, some XAT-accepting colleges might require a minimum of 50% marks in graduation (or 45% for candidates belonging to reserved categories). Candidates should check the specific requirements of their target institutions.

## Age Limit for XAT 2026

One of the notable aspects of XAT eligibility is the absence of an upper age limit. This means that candidates of any age who meet the educational qualifications can apply for the XAT examination.

## XAT Eligibility Criteria for XLRI Courses

While the general XAT eligibility covers most programs, XLRI's specific full-time MBA/PGDM programs have their own distinct requirements:

### Business Management (BM) and Human Resource Management (HRM) Programs

For XLRI's flagship two-year full-time Post Graduate Diploma in Business Management (PGDM-BM) and Post Graduate Diploma in Human Resource Management (PGDM-HRM) programs, the eligibility criteria are:

*   A three-year Bachelor's degree or equivalent in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   Final-year degree students are also eligible to apply.

### General Management Programme (GMP)

The Post Graduate Diploma in General Management (PGDM-GM) at XLRI is designed for experienced professionals. The eligibility for this program includes:

*   A three-year Bachelor's degree or equivalent in any discipline from a recognized university.
*   A minimum of five years of relevant managerial work experience after completing graduation, as of March 31, 2026.

## Applying to XLRI Campuses

When filling out the XAT application form, candidates must carefully select their preferred XLRI programs and campuses (e.g., Jamshedpur or Delhi). It is crucial to make these selections accurately during the application process, as there is typically no option to edit these preferences later. The XAT application form serves as the primary gateway for applying to XLRI's PGDM programs; there isn't a separate application form for these courses.

Meeting the XAT eligibility criteria is the first essential step towards securing admission to your desired management program. Ensure you thoroughly review all requirements before applying for XAT 2026.
