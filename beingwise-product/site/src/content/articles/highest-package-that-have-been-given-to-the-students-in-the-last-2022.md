# What is the Highest Package at AUCEW in 2022?

Understanding placement statistics is a key part of choosing the right engineering college. Let's look at the top salary package offered to students at Andhra University College of Engineering for Women (AUCEW) in 2022.

## Key Takeaways

*   The highest salary package offered at AUCEW in 2022 was ₹14 Lakhs per annum.
*   This top offer was made by Infosys, a leading IT company.
*   Approximately 40% of the students secured placements in various companies in 2022.
*   The overall placement rate at AUCEW typically averages around 62%.

## AUCEW: An Overview

Andhra University College of Engineering for Women (AUCEW) is a government-run engineering college, specifically for female students, and operates as a constituent college of Andhra University, Visakhapatnam. It provides five undergraduate (UG) engineering programs. Admissions to these programs are primarily based on the scores achieved in the AP EAMCET examination.

## Placement Trends at AUCEW

The college has an active placement cell that works to connect students with potential employers. While specific numbers can vary year to year, the institution generally observes an average placement rate of about 62% across all its engineering disciplines.

### Program-Specific Placement Rates

Placement success can differ significantly between various engineering branches. Here's a look at how different programs at AUCEW performed in terms of placement rates:

*   **B.Tech Computer Science and System Engineering:** This program consistently shows the highest placement rate, reaching approximately 77%.
*   **B.Tech Mechanical Engineering:** This program typically has the lowest placement rate, around 46%.

These figures highlight that while some branches enjoy strong placement outcomes, others might require students to be more proactive in their job search or consider further studies.

## The Top Salary Package in 2022

In 2022, the most significant salary package secured by an AUCEW student was ₹14 Lakhs per annum. This impressive offer came from Infosys, a well-known company in the information technology sector. This indicates the potential for students from AUCEW to secure competitive roles in leading organizations.

## Overall Placement Success in 2022

Beyond the highest package, it's also important to consider the broader placement scenario. In 2022, roughly 40% of the students at AUCEW were successfully placed in various reputable companies. This suggests a decent number of opportunities for graduates, with many finding suitable roles after completing their studies.

Understanding these facts can help you make an informed decision about your higher education journey.
