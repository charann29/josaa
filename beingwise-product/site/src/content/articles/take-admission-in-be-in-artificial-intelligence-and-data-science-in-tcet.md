# How to Get Admission in BE Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at TCET Using MHT CET Scores

Securing a Bachelor of Engineering (BE) degree in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science can open doors to exciting career opportunities. If you're aiming for Thakur College of Engineering and Technology (TCET) and plan to use your MHT CET scores, understanding the admission process is key.

## Key Takeaways

*   Admission to BE AI & Data Science at TCET primarily happens through the DTE Maharashtra Centralized Admission Process (CAP).
*   Your MHT CET percentile and score are crucial for eligibility and merit ranking.
*   A score above 93 marks in MHT CET has historically been a competitive benchmark for this program at TCET.
*   Careful registration, document verification, and strategic college preference filling are vital steps.

## Understanding the DTE CAP Process

The Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Maharashtra, conducts a comprehensive Centralized Admission Process (CAP) for engineering admissions across the state. This process is the primary pathway for students seeking admission to various BE programs, including Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at TCET, based on their MHT CET performance. The CAP involves several stages, all managed through the official DTE website.

## MHT CET Scores and Eligibility

Your MHT CET score and the corresponding percentile are fundamental to your admission prospects. These scores determine your merit position among all applicants. While cut-offs can vary each year due to factors like the number of applicants and exam difficulty, past trends indicate that a score above 93 marks in MHT CET has been competitive for securing a seat in the BE Artificial Intelligence and Data Science program at TCET. It's always advisable to check the most recent official DTE information for specific cut-off details once they are released.

## The Application and Preference Filling Stages

Once the DTE CAP portal opens, you will need to register online. This involves submitting your personal and academic details, including your MHT CET scores. Following registration, a critical step is document verification. Ensure all your submitted documents are accurate and complete to avoid any issues. After successful verification, you will be prompted to submit a college preference list. This is where you strategically list your desired colleges and branches in order of priority. For instance, if TCET's BE Artificial Intelligence and Data Science is your top choice, it should be listed first.

## Seat Allotment and Confirmation

Based on your MHT CET merit rank, the preferences you've submitted, and the availability of seats, DTE Maharashtra will conduct the seat allotment rounds. If you are allotted a seat in your desired college and branch, you will then need to confirm your admission by completing the necessary formalities and paying the initial fees within the stipulated timeframe. It's essential to closely follow the DTE Maharashtra schedule and instructions to ensure you don't miss any deadlines.

Navigating the admission process requires attention to detail and timely action, but by understanding each step, you can significantly enhance your chances of joining the BE Artificial Intelligence and Data Science program at TCET.
