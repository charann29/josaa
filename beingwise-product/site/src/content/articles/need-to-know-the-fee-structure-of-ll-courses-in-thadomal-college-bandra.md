# Need to know the fee structure of all courses in Thadomal College Bandra?

If you're considering Thadomal Shahani Engineering College (TSEC) in Bandra for your engineering studies, understanding the fee structure is an important step. This guide breaks down the costs associated with their B.Tech programs.

## Key Takeaways

*   The total fees for all B.Tech programs at TSEC for the first year amount to approximately Rs. 189,103.
*   This includes tuition, college development, university fees, and a one-time insurance charge.
*   Students from boards other than the Maharashtra HSC will incur an additional Mumbai University Eligibility Fee of Rs. 300.
*   The estimated total cost for the full four-year B.Tech degree across all specialisations is around Rs. 7.58 lakhs.

## Understanding Your First-Year Fees

The first year of your B.Tech program at TSEC involves several components that make up the total fee. It's helpful to see these broken down to understand where your money is going. The figures provided are based on information for all B.Tech courses.

Here's a detailed look at the approximate first-year fee components:

| Fee Component                  | Amount (Rs.) |
| :----------------------------- | :----------- |
| Tuition Fees                   | 166,666      |
| College Development Fees       | 18,334       |
| University Enrolment Fee       | 200          |
| Marksheet Verification Fee     | 500          |
| University Other Fee           | 556          |
| B.U. Exam Fees (Sem I & II)    | 2,146        |
| Insurance (one time for 4 years) | 701          |
| **TOTAL FIRST YEAR FEES**      | **189,103**  |


## Additional Fees for Specific Candidates

It's important to note that some students may have an additional charge. If you have completed your Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) or equivalent from a board other than the Maharashtra HSC Board, you will need to pay an extra Mumbai University Eligibility Fee. This fee is Rs. 300 and is in addition to the total first-year fees mentioned above.

## Estimated Total Cost for the B.Tech Degree

For planning purposes, it's useful to have an idea of the overall expenditure for the entire duration of your B.Tech program. Across all engineering courses offered at Thadomal Shahani Engineering College, the approximate total fees for the full four-year degree are estimated to be around **Rs. 7.58 lakhs**.

## Why These Fees Matter

Understanding the fee structure helps you and your family plan your finances effectively for your higher education journey. While these figures provide a clear picture of the academic costs, remember to also factor in other living expenses if you'll be moving to Mumbai, such as accommodation, food, transport, and personal expenses.

We hope this breakdown helps you in your decision-making process for your engineering aspirations at Thadomal College Bandra.
