# Amrita CAT Registration 2026: Direct Link (Available), Fees, How to Apply

Planning to pursue your MBA at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham? The application process for Amrita Common Admission Test (ACAT) 2026 is a crucial first step. This guide will walk you through the registration, eligibility, and important dates.

## Key takeaways

*   The Amrita CAT 2026 application window opened on October 6, 2025.
*   Candidates must meet specific eligibility criteria, including academic percentages and, for some programs, valid test scores from exams like CAT, XAT, MAT, or GMAT.
*   The application fee for Amrita CAT 2026 is INR 1880, payable online only.
*   The last date to apply for MBA Phase 1 admissions is April 15, 2026.

## Amrita CAT 2026 Important Dates

Staying informed about key dates is essential for a smooth application process. Here’s a breakdown of the important schedule for Amrita CAT 2026:

| Event                                 | Date                    |
| :------------------------------------ | :---------------------- |
| Amrita CAT 2026 Application Start     | October 6, 2025         |
| Amrita CAT 2026 Application End       | January 26, 2026        |
| Amrita CAT 2026 Exam Dates            | February 7 & 8, 2026    |
| MBA Phase 1 Application Deadline      | April 15, 2026          |

## Eligibility Criteria for Amrita CAT 2026

Before starting your application, it's vital to ensure you meet the eligibility requirements set by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. Only eligible candidates' applications will be considered.

*   **Academic Qualification:** Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
*   **Minimum Marks:** A minimum aggregate of 50% in Class 10, Class 12, and graduation is required.
*   **Final Year Students:** Those in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply, provided they complete their qualifying examinations by June 30, 2026.
*   **Valid Test Scores:** Candidates with valid scores from national-level management entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, GMAT, or GRE can apply using their test registration number.
*   **KMAT Scores:** For the Department of Management campuses in Kochi and Amritapuri (Kollam), valid KMAT scores are also accepted.
*   **English Proficiency (for MBA-MS Bengaluru):** Applicants for the MBA-MS program at Bengaluru campus must have a minimum IBT-TOEFL score of 79, an IELTS score of 6.5 (with no individual section below 6), or a PTE score of 55 (with no individual section below 50).
*   **Work Experience:** While not mandatory, candidates with relevant work experience may be given preference.

## How to Apply for Amrita CAT 2026

The application process for Amrita CAT 2026 is entirely online. Follow these steps to successfully submit your application:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Go to the official website of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.
2.  **Locate Registration Link:** Look for the Amrita CAT 2026 registration link, or you can directly access the application portal.
3.  **Generate Credentials:** You will need to create a unique User ID and password. These details will also be sent to your registered email address and mobile number via SMS.
4.  **Log In and Fill Form:** Use your newly created User ID and password to log in to the application portal. Carefully fill out all sections of the Amrita CAT 2026 application form.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of necessary documents, such as your photograph and signature, ensuring they meet the specified format and clarity requirements.
6.  **Pay Application Fee:** Proceed to pay the application fee online using a Credit Card, Debit Card, or Net Banking. The fee for Amrita CAT 2026 is INR 1880, which includes GST.

### Important Points to Remember During Application

*   Ensure all details entered in the form are accurate and free from errors.
*   Upload clear and correctly formatted photographs and signatures.
*   The application fee must be paid online; offline payment methods are not accepted.
*   Do not leave any mandatory fields blank to avoid rejection of your application.

## Amrita CAT 2026 Application Fee

The application fee for Amrita CAT 2026 is **INR 1880**, inclusive of GST. This fee is non-refundable and must be paid through online transaction methods. Accepted payment options include:

*   Credit Card
*   Debit Card
*   Internet Banking
*   UPI

Ensure your payment is processed successfully to complete your application.
