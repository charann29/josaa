# IBSAT Login 2026: Registration, Slot Booking, Admit Card, Results

Navigating the IBSAT application process requires understanding how to use your login credentials effectively. From initial registration to checking your results and beyond, your IBSAT login will be your gateway to essential information and actions.

## Key takeaways

*   IBSAT 2026 registration is expected to begin in the first week of July 2026 on the official IBS website.
*   Your IBSAT login is crucial for accessing your application form, paying fees, downloading your admit card, and checking results.
*   Candidates can also use their login to submit scores from other MBA entrance exams like CAT, NMAT, or GMAT, along with graduation and English proficiency scores.
*   The selection process, including Micro Presentation and Personal Interview, will also involve using your login for updates and scheduling.

## IBSAT Important Dates 2026

The ICFAI Business School (IBS) typically releases the official notification for IBSAT, including all important dates, in the first week of July. It's vital to keep track of these dates to ensure you don't miss any deadlines. Below is a tentative schedule for IBSAT 2026:

| Event                                 | Tentative Date              |
| :------------------------------------ | :-------------------------- |
| IBSAT Registrations Begin             | First week of July 2026     |
| IBSAT Registration Last Date          | Third week of December 2026 |
| IBSAT Slot Booking & Admit Card Download | October 2026                |
| IBSAT Exam Window                     | Last week of December 2026  |
| IBSAT Results                         | Last week of December 2026  |
| IBSAT Selection Process               | Mid-February 2027           |

Before creating your login and filling out the application form, always ensure you meet the prescribed eligibility criteria for IBSAT 2026.

## How to Create Your IBSAT 2026 Login

Creating your login for IBSAT 2026 is the first step towards your application. This process typically involves visiting the official IBS website and following the registration link. You will likely need to provide basic personal details, including your name, email address, and mobile number. Once submitted, you will receive your login credentials (username and password) via email or SMS. It's important to keep these details safe as they will be used throughout the entire admission cycle.

### Changing Your IBSAT 2026 Login Password

If you forget your password or wish to change it for security reasons, the official IBSAT portal usually provides a 'Forgot Password' or 'Change Password' option. This generally involves entering your registered email ID or application number, after which you'll receive instructions or a link to reset your password.

## Booking Your IBSAT 2026 Slot

Once you have successfully registered, you will need to book your preferred exam slot. This involves logging into your IBSAT account during the designated slot booking window (expected in October 2026). You'll typically be able to choose your exam date and time based on availability. After booking your slot, your IBSAT admit card will become available for download. The admit card is a mandatory document for appearing in the examination and will contain crucial details like your roll number, exam centre, and reporting time.

## IBSAT Result 2026 Login

After taking the exam, your IBSAT login will be essential to check your results. The IBSAT results are anticipated in the last week of December 2026. By logging into your account on the official website, you can access and download your scorecard. This scorecard will display your performance in the exam and is a vital document for the subsequent selection process. Remember to keep a printout or digital copy of your scorecard for future reference.

Your IBSAT login is a key tool throughout your application journey, so ensure you manage your credentials carefully and stay updated with all official announcements.
