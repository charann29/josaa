## IBPS PO 2025: Interview Call Letter (OUT), Mains Scorecard, Cut Off & Exam Date 2026 Released

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) recently announced the final results for the IBPS PO 2025 examination, along with detailed cut-off marks and the examination calendar for 2026. This is crucial information for aspiring Probationary Officers in public sector banks.

## Key takeaways

*   The IBPS PO Final Result 2025 was declared on January 15, 2026, with the result window closing on February 14, 2026.
*   A total of 6,189 vacancies for Probationary Officers across 11 public sector banks are being filled through the IBPS PO 2025 selection process.
*   The IBPS PO 2026 Preliminary exams are tentatively scheduled for August 22 and 23, 2026, with the Mains exam on October 4, 2026.
*   The Mains scorecard for 2025 was released on January 9, and the Mains result on December 1, 2025.

## IBPS PO Final Result and Cut-Off 2025

IBPS officially released the final results for the Probationary Officer (PO) examination on January 15, 2026, on its official portal. Candidates had until February 14, 2026, to access their results. Alongside the final result, the official cut-off marks for various categories were also published. Earlier, the Mains scorecard was made available on January 9, 2026, following the Mains result declaration on December 1, 2025. The Mains examination for 2025 was conducted on October 12, 2025.

To check their results, candidates need to visit the official IBPS website and log in using their registration number or roll number, along with their date of birth or password.

### How to Access Your IBPS PO Result 2025

Follow these steps to download your result:

1.  Navigate to the official IBPS website.
2.  Locate and click on the 'IBPS PO result' link on the homepage.
3.  On the login page, enter your registration number or roll number and your date of birth or password.
4.  Your IBPS PO result will be displayed on the screen.
5.  Download and save a copy of your result for future reference.

### Details on the IBPS PO Scorecard

The scorecard provides comprehensive details about a candidate's performance, including:

*   Candidate's Name
*   Roll Number
*   Registration Number
*   Examination Date
*   Category
*   Maximum scores (overall and section-wise)
*   IBPS PO cut-off marks
*   Scores obtained in individual sections and overall

## IBPS PO 2026 Examination Schedule

Good news for future aspirants: The IBPS 2026 Calendar was released on January 16, 2026, on the official IBPS portal. Prospective candidates should mark these important dates:

*   **Preliminary Exam:** August 22 and 23, 2026 (tentative)
*   **Mains Exam:** October 4, 2026

The official notification for the IBPS PO 2026 exam, which will include the application form link, is expected to be released in June 2026.

## Understanding the IBPS PO Marking Scheme and Normalisation

Candidates should be aware of the evaluation process for the IBPS PO examination:

*   For every correct answer, candidates are awarded one mark.
*   There is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each incorrect answer.
*   The total score is calculated based on the number of correct and incorrect responses.

Given that the IBPS PO exam is conducted across multiple shifts and days, leading to variations in question paper difficulty, IBPS employs a normalisation method. This process ensures fairness in evaluation by adjusting scores to account for these differences, providing a balanced and rounded-up figure for all candidates.

Stay updated with the official IBPS portal for any further announcements regarding the examination and selection process.
