# CUET Exam 2026: Photo Correction Window (Till April 10), City Slip, Exam Date (Out), Syllabus & Pattern

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) is a crucial exam for students aspiring to join undergraduate programs across India's central and many other universities. This page provides essential updates and details for the CUET UG 2026 examination, helping you stay informed and prepared.

## Key Takeaways

*   The photo correction window for CUET UG 2026 has been extended until April 10, allowing candidates to re-upload necessary documents.
*   The CUET UG 2026 examinations are scheduled to take place from May 11 to May 31.
*   The National Testing Agency (NTA) has introduced syllabus changes for 15 subjects, which candidates should review carefully.
*   The CUET City Intimation Slip for 2026 is expected to be released in the last week of April.

## Important Dates for CUET UG 2026

Staying on top of deadlines is vital for a smooth application and examination process. Here are some key dates for CUET UG 2026:

*   **Application Form Activation:** January 3, 2026
*   **Photo Correction Window:** April 7 to April 10, 2026
*   **City Intimation Slip Release:** Last week of April 2026 (expected)
*   **CUET UG Exam Dates:** May 11 to May 31, 2026

Remember to regularly check the official NTA website for any further announcements or changes.

## Understanding the CUET UG Exam

CUET UG is a single entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate courses. It offers a unified platform for students to gain entry into 47 central universities and over 300 other universities and colleges across India. The exam is conducted in an online, Computer-Based Test (CBT) mode, streamlining the examination process and minimizing logistical challenges.

The exam is offered for 37 subjects, allowing students to choose subjects relevant to their desired undergraduate programs. Each subject paper is typically 1 hour long and consists of 50 questions, with a total of 250 marks per subject. Candidates are encouraged to attempt all questions to maximize their scores.

## Syllabus and Exam Pattern

For CUET UG 2026, the NTA has made specific changes to the syllabi of 15 subjects. It is crucial for all candidates to thoroughly review these updated syllabi and adjust their preparation accordingly. Practicing with the revised content will be key to performing well in the examination.

The exam pattern generally involves multiple-choice questions (MCQs) for each subject. The NTA aims to create a standardized yet fair testing environment for all aspirants.

## The CUET UG Admission Journey

The admission process through CUET UG involves several distinct stages, from application to final university admission:

1.  **Application Form:** The application window for CUET UG 2026 opened on January 3, 2026. Candidates must complete and submit their forms through the official NTA portal.
2.  **CUET Exam:** The examinations will be conducted in CBT mode across 379 cities in India and 26 cities abroad, ensuring wide accessibility for aspirants.
3.  **Answer Key Release:** The NTA first releases provisional answer keys, allowing candidates to raise objections if they find discrepancies. After evaluating these objections, the final answer keys are published.
4.  **Result Declaration:** Following the release of the final answer keys, the NTA declares the CUET results. These results include normalized marks and percentiles for each subject.
5.  **Counselling:** The final stage is counselling, which is managed independently by the various participating universities. The NTA is not responsible for conducting university-specific counselling sessions. Candidates should visit the official websites of their preferred universities for counselling schedules and procedures.

Ensuring you are aware of each step and its associated timelines will significantly help in navigating the admission process for your desired undergraduate program.
