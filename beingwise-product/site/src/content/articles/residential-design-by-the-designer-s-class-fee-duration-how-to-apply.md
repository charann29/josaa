# Residential Design by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For anyone passionate about creating beautiful and functional living spaces, The Designer's Class offers an online certification in Residential Design. This program is designed to guide aspiring designers and professionals through the essentials of residential interiors, taught by renowned designer Gauri Khan.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a beginner-friendly online course focused on residential interior design principles.
*   The program is self-paced, allowing you to learn at your convenience over one year of access.
*   It covers key areas like kitchen, bedroom, and living space design, along with elements like colour, furniture, and lighting.
*   Upon successful completion, including a final assessment, you receive a certificate from The Designer's Class.

## Course Overview

The Residential Design certification is a 24-hour, self-study online program delivered through video and text. It aims to equip learners with fundamental knowledge in designing residential spaces. You'll delve into various aspects, from initial plot planning to detailed interior layouts for specific rooms like bedrooms and kitchens, and even understanding colour palettes. The course material is accessible via mobile phones or PCs, offering flexibility to fit into your schedule.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum for this certification comprehensively covers the spectrum of residential design, a crucial segment of interior design. Key topics include:

*   **Space Planning:** Understanding how to effectively lay out residential areas.
*   **Kitchen Design:** Principles for creating functional and aesthetically pleasing kitchens.
*   **Bedroom Design:** Techniques for designing comfortable and stylish bedrooms.
*   **Living and Drawing Rooms:** Ideas for crafting inviting and elegant communal spaces.
*   **Colour Textures:** Exploring the impact and application of various colour schemes.
*   **Furniture Selection:** Guidance on choosing appropriate furniture for different spaces.
*   **Wardrobe Design:** Concepts for practical and elegant wardrobe solutions.
*   **Plants and Lights:** Integrating natural elements and lighting to enhance interiors.
*   **Space Revamp:** Strategies for transforming existing spaces.

The course also includes an introduction to residential design and concludes with a final assessment.

## Fees and Duration

The fee for the Residential Design certification is ₹2,999. This amount is payable at the time of subscription, granting you one year of access to the course content. The program is estimated to take 24 hours to complete, but as it's self-paced, you can manage your learning speed within the one-year access period.

| Name of the Course | Fee |
| :----------------- | :------ |
| Residential Design | ₹ 2,999 |

## Eligibility and Certification

This program is ideal for individuals interested in interior design, particularly those looking to specialise in residential spaces, such as interior architects and designers. As a beginner-level course, it's suitable for those new to the field or professionals seeking to refine their skills in residential design.

To earn the certificate of completion from The Designer's Class, learners must achieve a minimum score of 70% in the final assessment. This certificate can be a valuable addition to your portfolio, potentially aiding in further studies or career opportunities.

## How to Apply

Enrolling in the Residential Design certification course is a straightforward process. You simply need to visit the official portal for The Designer's Class, select the "Buy now" option for the Residential Design course, and complete the payment. Once the subscription is confirmed, you will gain immediate access to the course materials for one year.

This certification can significantly benefit learners by providing a thorough understanding of how to design various residential spaces, coupled with an official certificate of completion.
