# Certificate in White Hat Hacking by Vskills: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This certification program from Vskills is designed to equip individuals with essential knowledge in ethical hacking and cybersecurity. It offers a comprehensive understanding of various attack types and the countermeasures needed to protect systems, networks, and web applications.

## Key takeaways

*   **Government-backed Certification:** Receive a certificate issued by Vskills, a government initiative, enhancing its credibility.
*   **Flexible Learning:** The course is delivered entirely online, allowing for self-paced study through video and text-based modules.
*   **Industry-Relevant Skills:** The curriculum focuses on practical skills and knowledge demanded by the cybersecurity industry, helping participants become job-ready.
*   **Career Advancement:** Ideal for graduates and professionals looking to enter the IT security field or enhance their existing careers.

## Course Overview

This online program spans approximately 19-20 hours of e-learning content, covering a wide range of topics crucial for an ethical hacker. Participants will delve into the fundamentals of hacking, social engineering tactics, cryptography, footprinting, physical security, and various network attack methodologies. The course is structured to provide a solid foundation in identifying vulnerabilities and implementing robust security measures.

## Who is this course for?

This certification is particularly beneficial for individuals aspiring to become white hat hackers, security professionals in the IT sector, or those looking to advance their IT careers. It's also suitable for existing professionals and graduates who wish to upskill themselves in cybersecurity to improve their employability and broaden their job prospects. Roles that might benefit include:

*   Ethical Hacker
*   Computer Programmer
*   Computer Systems Engineer
*   Computer Systems Administrator
*   System Security Analyst
*   Computer System Analyst

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to impart a thorough understanding of hacking concepts and defensive strategies. Upon completion, participants will have learned:

*   **Ethical Hacking Basics:** Evolution of hacking, different modes, and various stages of a hack.
*   **Cryptography:** Fundamental concepts, data encryption methods, block and stream ciphers, message authentication, and digital signatures.
*   **Social Engineering:** Understanding its concepts, types, and effective countermeasures.
*   **Network Security:** Network scanning and enumeration, and how to defend against attacks targeting firewalls, routers, and switches.
*   **System and Web Hacking:** Various forms of hacking targeting operating systems like Windows and Linux, as well as web-based applications.

## Fees and Certification

The total fee for the Certificate in White Hat Hacking course is ₹3,499 (excluding taxes). This fee covers access to all program offerings, including study materials and practice tests. Upon successful completion of an online exam, participants will receive a government-issued certificate from Vskills.

### Certification Details

To earn the certificate, candidates must pass an online examination. A minimum score of 50% (25 out of total marks) is required to qualify. The exam can be taken online at any time and from any location, offering maximum flexibility.

## How to Apply

Applying for the Certificate in White Hat Hacking program is a straightforward online process. Interested candidates can visit the official Vskills website for the White Hat Hacking certification. From there, they can follow the instructions to fill out the application form and complete the registration process.

This certification can be a valuable step towards a career in the dynamic field of cybersecurity.
