# MAT Exam Centres 2026: Check MAT CBT/PBT Test Centres List

When you're applying for the Management Aptitude Test (MAT), choosing your preferred exam city is an important step. The test centres vary depending on whether you opt for the Paper-Based Test (PBT) or the Computer-Based Test (CBT).

## Key takeaways

*   You must select your preferred test cities carefully during the MAT application process on the AIMA website.
*   Once you submit your application, the chosen test city cannot be changed, so double-check your selection.
*   Both PBT and CBT exams have distinct lists of available cities, and you can generally select up to two preferences for PBT.
*   Always aim to choose a city that is your hometown or closest to your current location for convenience.

## Understanding MAT Test Cities for 2026

The All India Management Association (AIMA) conducts the MAT exam, offering it in two formats: PBT and CBT. Each format has its own set of designated test cities. It's crucial to review these lists before filling out your application form.

## What to Bring to the MAT Exam Centre

Regardless of whether you are taking the PBT or CBT, certain items are mandatory for entry into the MAT exam centre. Make sure you have these prepared well in advance:

*   **Admit Card:** A colour printout of your MAT 2026 admit card. Digital copies are not accepted.
*   **Photo ID:** A valid government-issued photo identification card (e.g., Aadhaar card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License). Digital copies are not accepted.
*   **Passport-size Photos:** Two recent passport-size photographs.
*   **Pen:** A blue or black ballpoint pen (for PBT, and possibly for rough work in CBT).

Ensure all documents are originals and in hard copy format, as digital versions will not be considered valid for verification at the test centre.

Choosing your MAT exam centre wisely is a crucial step in your application journey, so take your time and ensure your selection is accurate and convenient for you.
