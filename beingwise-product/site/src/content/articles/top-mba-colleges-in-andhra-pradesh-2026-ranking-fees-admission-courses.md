## Eligibility and Entrance Exams for MBA Admissions 2026

To secure admission into MBA programs in Andhra Pradesh, candidates must meet specific eligibility criteria and successfully clear relevant entrance examinations. The general requirements are consistent across most institutions, though individual colleges may have additional stipulations.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
*   A minimum aggregate score of 50% in the undergraduate degree is typically required for general category candidates.
*   For reserved category candidates, a minimum aggregate score of 45% is generally accepted.
*   Candidates must also clear one of the accepted management entrance exams.

### Accepted Entrance Exams

Several national and state-level entrance exams are considered for MBA admissions in Andhra Pradesh. The primary exams include:

*   **CAT (Common Admission Test):** A national-level exam primarily for IIMs and other top B-schools.
*   **APICET (Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test):** A state-level exam for admissions into MBA and MCA courses in Andhra Pradesh colleges.
*   **MAT (Management Aptitude Test):** A national-level exam accepted by a wide range of B-schools across India.
