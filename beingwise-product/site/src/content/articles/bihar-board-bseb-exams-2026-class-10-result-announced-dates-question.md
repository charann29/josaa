The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) conducts the annual Class 10 (Matric) and Class 12 (Inter) examinations for students across Bihar. These exams are a crucial step for students moving on to higher education and future careers.

## Key Takeaways

*   The BSEB Class 10 results for 2026 were declared on March 29, 2026, at 1:15 PM.
*   The BSEB Class 12 results for 2026 were announced earlier, on March 23, 2026, around 1:30 PM.
*   Both Class 10 and 12 examinations for 2026 were held in February, conducted in two shifts daily.
*   Students can access their results online using their roll number and roll code on the official BSEB result portals.

## BSEB Exam Dates 2026

The 2026 Bihar Board examinations for both Class 10 and Class 12 followed a structured schedule in February.

*   **Class 10 (Matric) Exams:** These were conducted from February 17 to February 25, 2026.
*   **Class 12 (Inter) Exams:** These took place from February 2 to February 13, 2026.

All examinations were held in two sessions each day: a morning session from 9:30 AM to 12:45 PM and an afternoon session from 2:00 PM to 5:15 PM.

## Checking Your BSEB Results 2026

To check their scores, students need to visit the official BSEB result websites. For Class 12, the results were made available on `interbiharboard.com` and `www.bsebexam.com`. For both Class 10 and Class 12, results can also be found on `biharboardonline.bihar.gov.in` and `www.results.biharboardonline.com`.

Students should keep their admit card handy as they will need their roll number and roll code to log in and view their detailed mark sheet.

## BSEB Syllabus and Exam Pattern

The Bihar School Examination Board sets the curriculum and examination pattern for both Matric and Inter exams. Understanding these is vital for effective preparation. The syllabus covers subjects across Science, Arts, and Commerce streams for Class 12, and various compulsory and optional subjects for Class 10.

The exam pattern typically includes both objective (multiple-choice questions) and subjective (short and long answer questions) components. The exact weightage for each type of question and the overall marking scheme is outlined by the board in advance of the examinations. Students are advised to refer to the official BSEB notifications for the most current information regarding the syllabus and pattern for upcoming exams.

## Question Papers and Preparation

Practicing with previous years' question papers and model papers released by BSEB is an excellent strategy for exam preparation. These resources help students understand the format of the questions, the types of topics emphasized, and the time management required during the actual examination. Staying updated with the official BSEB website for any released model papers or sample questions is highly recommended.

The Bihar School Examination Board plays a significant role in shaping the academic journey of students in Bihar, ensuring a fair and transparent examination process.
