# SSC JE 2025: Tier 1 Answer Key (OUT), Result, Cut Off, Vacancies (Revised) & Syllabus

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the tentative answer key for the Junior Engineer (JE) Tier 1 examination for 2025. This page offers a comprehensive guide to important updates, including exam dates, results, cut-off scores, and the revised vacancy count.

## Key takeaways

*   **Tier 1 Answer Key Released:** The tentative answer key for SSC JE Tier 1 2025 is now available on the official SSC portal.
*   **Objection Window Closed:** Candidates had a specific window from December 19 (6:00 PM) to December 22 (6:00 PM) to challenge the tentative answer key, with a fee of INR 50 per question.
*   **Increased Vacancies:** The total number of vacancies for SSC JE 2025 has been significantly increased from 1,340 to 1,731.
*   **Exam Dates:** The SSC JE Tier 1 exam was conducted across multiple dates: December 3, 6, and 13, 2025.

## SSC JE Tier 1 Answer Key 2025

The Staff Selection Commission has made the tentative answer key for the JE Tier 1 2025 examination available online. Along with the answer key, candidates can also access their individual question papers and response sheets. To view these, applicants need to log in to the official SSC portal using their registration number and password. This allows them to cross-verify their responses and identify any discrepancies.

### How to download the answer key:

*   Visit the official SSC website (ssc.gov.in).
*   Locate and click on the relevant link for the SSC JE Tier 1 2025 answer key.
*   Enter your login credentials (registration number and password).
*   The answer key, along with your response sheet, will be displayed.
*   Download and save it for your records.

Staying informed about the official schedule is crucial for all aspirants. Here are the key dates related to the SSC JE 2025 examination:

| Event                                      | Date              |

## SSC JE 2025 Result: Tier 1 & Tier 2

The Staff Selection Commission will announce the SSC JE Tier 1 2025 results on its official website. The results are typically published in a PDF format, listing the roll numbers of qualified candidates. Those who clear Tier 1 will become eligible to appear for the Tier 2 examination.

### Steps to check your result:

*   Go to the official SSC website (ssc.nic.in).
*   Navigate to the 'Results' section and find the link for SSC JE 2025 Tier 1 result.
*   Clicking the link will open a PDF document.
*   Download the PDF and search for your roll number.

## SSC JE Cut-Off 2025

The cut-off marks for SSC JE 2025 will be released by the Commission separately for different engineering streams (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Quantity Surveying & Contracts) and various categories of candidates. These cut-off scores determine eligibility for the next stage of the selection process. The cut-off details are usually published along with the result PDF, indicating the minimum marks required to qualify.

## SSC JE Vacancy 2025 (Revised)

In a significant update, the Staff Selection Commission revised the total number of vacancies for the Junior Engineer examination on August 5, 2025. The vacancies have been increased from an initial count of 1,340 to a total of 1,731. These positions are available across various government departments for Mechanical Engineers, Civil Engineers, Electrical Engineers, and professionals in Quality and Surveying & Contracts.

## SSC JE Syllabus and Exam Pattern

Candidates preparing for the SSC JE exam should thoroughly review the official syllabus and exam pattern. Understanding the topics covered in both Tier 1 and Tier 2, as well as the marking scheme, is essential for effective preparation. The Tier 1 exam is generally a computer-based test covering General Intelligence & Reasoning, General Awareness, and a specific Engineering discipline. Tier 2 typically focuses on conventional engineering subjects relevant to the chosen discipline.
