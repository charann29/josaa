# Crystal Healing Certification Course by Upskillist: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those intrigued by the ancient wisdom and modern applications of crystal energy, the Crystal Healing Certification Course by Upskillist offers a comprehensive exploration. This program delves into the science and practice of using crystals for well-being, personal growth, and creating positive environments.

## Key Takeaways

*   This is an internationally certified online course, offering flexibility for learners.
*   It combines video lectures, live sessions, and continuous assessments over 16 weeks.
*   Participants gain lifetime access to course materials and can pause their learning at any time.
*   The curriculum covers both the theoretical and practical aspects of crystal healing, from selecting crystals to advanced healing techniques.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Upskillist Crystal Healing Certification Course aims to provide a thorough understanding of how crystals can influence energy, promote balance, and aid in self-rejuvenation. While drawing on historical practices, the course also adopts a rational and scientific approach, explaining the underlying principles behind crystal healing.

The program is delivered through a blend of self-study and virtual classroom components, using video and text-based content. It spans 16 weeks of video lectures, but its flexible online format allows students to learn at their own pace and revisit lessons as needed, thanks to lifetime access to the materials.

## What You Will Learn

This course is designed for individuals interested in alternative healing methods, personal development, or those looking to incorporate crystal practices into their lives. The curriculum is extensive, covering a wide range of topics, including:

*   Techniques for sensing and seeing auras.
*   Methods for making moon water and crystal elixirs.
*   Balancing chakras and cleansing personal and client auras.
*   Guidance on selecting, cleansing, energising, programming, and storing crystals.
*   Enhancing psychic abilities and understanding different types of angels.
*   Practices like dowsing and distance healing.
*   An understanding of the five stages of grieving and how crystals can support healing.
*   Practical advice on assisting clients and handling advanced conditions.

The course also discusses how specific crystals, approximately 32 types, impact the body and mind, offering alternatives to conventional healing procedures.

## Eligibility and Certification

There are no strict prerequisites for enrolling in the Crystal Healing Certification Course. The main requirement for receiving the certification is to complete the course, pay the subscription fee, and pass the final assessment. Upon successful completion, Upskillist, the certifying authority, will issue the certificate.

## Fees and Program Offerings

While specific fee amounts are not detailed, the course operates on a subscription model, which needs to be paid to qualify for certification. The program offers significant value through:

*   Extensive video lectures.
*   Interactive assessments.
*   Live lectures for direct interaction.
*   Flexible online classes tailored to individual schedules.
*   Continuous assessment to track progress.
*   Lifetime access to all course lessons and materials.

## How to Apply

Admission to the Crystal Healing Certification Course is straightforward and can be completed online. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1.  **Start Your Free Trial:** Look for the “Start your free trial” option on the official Upskillist course page for Crystal Healing.
2.  **Answer Initial Questions:** You'll be prompted to answer questions about your biggest learning challenges and why you wish to pursue an online course.
3.  **Set Learning Goals:** Define your learning goals or the number of hours you plan to dedicate to the course.
4.  **Create Student ID:** Provide your mobile number, name, and email ID to generate your unique student ID.
5.  **Confirm Registration:** Complete your registration by setting and confirming your password.
6.  **Personal Details:** Enter your goals, gender, and age, then tap “Complete Registration.”
7.  **Payment Details:** Proceed to enter your payment information for the course subscription.
8.  **Begin Learning:** Once payment is processed, you can immediately begin your studies.

This course provides a valuable opportunity to explore the fascinating world of crystal healing with a structured and supportive learning environment.
