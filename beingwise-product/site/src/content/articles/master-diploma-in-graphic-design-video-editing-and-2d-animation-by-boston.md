# Master Diploma in Graphic Design, Video Editing and 2D Animation by Boston Institute of Analytics: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

This Master Diploma offers a thorough education in graphic design, video editing, and 2D animation, preparing students for creative careers in visual media. It's designed to build practical skills and a strong portfolio.

## Key Takeaways

*   The program combines graphic design, video editing, and 2D animation into a single comprehensive diploma.
*   It spans 10 months and includes 100 hours of learning, culminating in a certification from the Boston Institute of Analytics.
*   Learning modes include self-study, virtual classrooms, and video/text-based content, offering flexibility.
*   No specific academic stream is required; students from Arts, Science, Commerce, and Engineering are all eligible.

## Course Overview and Structure

The Master Diploma in Graphic Design, Video Editing, and 2D Animation is a robust program crafted to equip students with essential skills for the modern creative industry. It focuses on visual storytelling across various digital mediums, offering a well-rounded education.

The curriculum covers a wide array of topics, from foundational graphic design principles to advanced animation techniques. Students will gain hands-on experience with industry-standard software like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects, as well as 3D tools like Maya.

Upon completion, participants will have developed a strong portfolio, showcasing their creativity and technical abilities, which is crucial for entering the professional world.

### Program Highlights

*   **Duration:** 10 months, with 100 hours of dedicated course content.
*   **Certification:** Awarded by the Boston Institute of Analytics.
*   **Learning Approach:** A blend of classroom and online modes, offering live lectures and practical, hands-on learning.
*   **Dual Certification:** The program provides two certifications within one comprehensive diploma.

## What You Will Learn

The syllabus is structured to provide a deep dive into various aspects of design and animation. Key learning areas include:

*   **Graphic Design Fundamentals:** Essential tools, techniques, and principles, including image editing and retouching with Adobe Photoshop, typography, and layout design.
*   **Video Editing and Motion Graphics:** Animation basics and keyframe techniques using Adobe After Effects.
*   **2D Animation:** Introduction to animation principles and character movement.
*   **3D Elements (Introduction):** An overview of modeling, texturing, shading, lighting, and rendering techniques using software like Maya.

Throughout the course, emphasis is placed on practical application, ensuring students can confidently use the software and apply the concepts learned.

## Eligibility and Who Should Apply

### Eligibility Criteria

There are no specific academic stream restrictions for this program. Candidates from all backgrounds – Arts, Science, Commerce, and Engineering – are welcome to apply. The certification is granted upon successful completion of the course.

### Ideal Candidates

This diploma is particularly suited for individuals aspiring to careers in the video and animation industry. It's designed for those interested in roles such as:

*   Animators
*   Multimedia Animators
*   Video Game Designers
*   Film and Video Editors

Whether you are a beginner looking to start a new career path or a professional aiming to enhance your existing skills, this course offers valuable learning opportunities.

## Admission Process

To enroll in the Master Diploma, you will typically follow a straightforward application process:

1.  Visit the official Boston Institute of Analytics website for the specific course page.
2.  Locate and click on the "Enquire Now" option.
3.  Fill out the inquiry form with your contact details.
4.  A representative from the institute will then contact you to discuss further steps and fee payment.
5.  Once the course fees are paid, your enrollment will be confirmed.

This program provides a solid foundation for a creative career, blending theoretical knowledge with practical skills development.
