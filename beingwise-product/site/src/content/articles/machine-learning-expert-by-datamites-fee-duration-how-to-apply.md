# Machine Learning Expert by DataMites: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those looking to build a career in the rapidly growing field of machine learning, DataMites offers a comprehensive Machine Learning Expert certification. This program is designed to equip participants with practical skills and in-depth knowledge, making them ready for industry challenges.

## Key takeaways

*   The program is offered in three flexible learning modes: Live Virtual, Blended Learning, and In-Person Classroom.
*   It includes extensive training, project work, and career guidance, with accreditation from IABAC.
*   Fees vary by learning mode, with discounted rates available for all options.
*   Successful completion leads to an IABAC Machine Learning Expert certification, recognized in the industry.

## Understanding the Program Structure

The Machine Learning Expert program by DataMites is structured to cater to different learning preferences and schedules. The course content is delivered in English across all modes.

### Learning Modes and Duration

*   **Live Virtual:** This mode spans 5 months, featuring 100 hours of live online training complemented by 350 hours of self-paced learning.
*   **Blended Learning:** Ideal for self-starters, this option provides one year of access to program materials for flexible, self-paced study.
*   **In-Person Classroom:** This mode also runs for 5 months, including 48 hours of direct classroom sessions along with 350 hours of additional learning.

All modes emphasize a mix of video and text-based materials to ensure comprehensive understanding.

## What You Will Learn

The curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation and advanced skills in machine learning. Key learning outcomes include:

*   **Data Building:** Understanding the process of constructing and preparing data for machine learning models.
*   **Machine Learning Fundamentals:** Grasping the core principles and essentials of machine learning.
*   **Naive Bayes:** Exploring the concept of Naive Bayes and its practical applications.
*   **Machine Learning Algorithms:** Gaining detailed knowledge of both supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms.
*   **Python with Scikit-Learn:** Mastering machine learning implementation using Python's Scikit-Learn package.
*   **Model Design:** Developing the ability to design machine learning models from scratch to solve real-world business problems.
*   **IABAC Certification Preparation:** Being thoroughly prepared to successfully clear the IABAC certification examination.

## Fees and Certification

Investing in your skills is a crucial step. Here’s a breakdown of the program fees and details about the certification you'll earn.

### Program Fees

The fees for the Machine Learning Expert program vary based on the chosen learning mode, with attractive discounted rates available:

| Mode of Learning | Total Fee (INR) | Discounted Fee (INR) |
| :--------------- | :-------------- | :------------------- |
| Live Virtual     | 60,000          | 37,900               |
| Blended Learning | 36,000          | 22,900               |
| In-Person Classroom | 60,000          | 42,900               |

### Certification Details

Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a certificate of completion from DataMites. More importantly, the program prepares you for the **IABAC Machine Learning Expert certification**. To obtain this, candidates must log in to the official IABAC portal, apply for the final examination, and achieve a passing score after completing the entire program.

## Eligibility and Application Process

To ensure participants can effectively benefit from the program, certain prerequisites are in place. The application process is straightforward.

### Who Should Apply?

This program is ideal for:

*   Individuals aspiring to build a career in data science or machine learning.
*   Graduates or freshers who already possess foundational knowledge in machine learning.
*   Professionals currently pursuing data scientist career paths.

### Eligibility Criteria

Candidates should have:

*   A basic understanding of statistics.
*   Familiarity with Python programming, including libraries like Pandas and NumPy.
*   Completed the Machine Learning Foundation certification course from IABAC or an equivalent machine learning program.

### How to Apply

Applying for the Machine Learning Expert course involves a few simple steps:

1.  **Visit the Course Webpage:** Navigate to the specific course page on the official DataMites website.
2.  **Initiate Inquiry:** Click on the "Enquire now" button prominently displayed on the page.
3.  **Complete Application Form:** Fill in all the required personal and academic details in the application form to proceed with your enrollment.

This certification can be a significant step towards a rewarding career in the field of machine learning.
