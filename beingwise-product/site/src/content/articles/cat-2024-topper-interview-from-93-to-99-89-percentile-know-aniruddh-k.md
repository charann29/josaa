# CAT 2024 Topper Interview: From 93 to 99.89 Percentile, Know Aniruddh K Singh's CAT Success Story

Aniruddh K Singh, a Mechanical Engineering graduate from Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, achieved an outstanding 99.89 percentile in the CAT 2024 exam, building on a previous attempt where he scored in the 93rd percentile. His journey reflects dedication, strategic preparation, and strong family support.

## Key takeaways

*   **Persistence Pays Off:** Aniruddh's significant jump from a 93rd percentile to 99.89 in his second attempt highlights the importance of dedicated preparation and learning from prior experiences.
*   **Balanced Background:** His diverse work experience at PwC and Flipkart, alongside a strong academic record, provided a well-rounded foundation for his MBA aspirations.
*   **Strategic Preparation:** He emphasized focused, section-wise strategies and the critical role of mock tests in improving performance.
*   **Family as a Pillar:** Aniruddh credits his family's unwavering support as a major source of inspiration and strength throughout his preparation.

## Aniruddh's Background and Motivation

Aniruddh completed his early schooling at DPS Mathura and high school at Hillwoods School Gandhinagar in 2019. He then pursued Mechanical Engineering at Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, graduating in 2023. During his undergraduate studies, he gained valuable professional experience, interning at PwC in their Technology Consulting division, which led to a Pre-Placement Offer (PPO). He also completed an SCM internship with Flipkart's Neev Program. These experiences, he notes, fostered significant personal and professional growth.

After working full-time at PwC for a year, Aniruddh decided to fully commit to his CAT preparation, leaving his role in September 2024. He comes from a supportive family; his mother was a Sanskrit teacher, his father works in Gujarat, and his elder brother, an XIMB MBA alumnus, works for an MNC in Bangalore. Aniruddh is also an ardent football fan, drawing lessons of resilience from his favorite team, FC Barcelona.

## The Journey to 99.89 Percentile

This was Aniruddh's second attempt at the CAT. His first attempt in 2022, undertaken without specific preparation, resulted in a 93rd percentile score. For CAT 2024, his focused effort yielded remarkable results:

| Section | Score | Percentile |
| :------ | :---- | :--------- |
| Overall | 122   | 99.89      |
| DILR    | 39.42 | 99.29      |
| VARC    | 45.42 | 99.55      |
| QA      | 37.16 | 99.59      |

He began his dedicated preparation in February, though he took a break for personal reasons during that period.

## Section-Wise Strategy: VARC Focus

Aniruddh identified his reading speed as a primary area for improvement in the Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC) section. While his vocabulary was strong, he realized he read slowly. His initial approach of attempting to force himself to read books proved ineffective. Instead, he shifted his focus to essays and coaching material RCs, a strategy he found more beneficial for improving his speed and comprehension. By late August, he felt a significant improvement in this crucial area.

Aniruddh's success story is a testament to the power of self-assessment, strategic planning, and consistent effort. His journey provides valuable lessons for aspiring CAT candidates.
