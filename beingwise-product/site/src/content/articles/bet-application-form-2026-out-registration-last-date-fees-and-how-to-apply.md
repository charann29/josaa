# BET Application Form 2026 (OUT): Registration Last Date, Fees & How to Apply

The application window for the SDA Bocconi Entrance Test (BET) 2026 is currently open. Aspiring students can complete their registration online through the official SDA Bocconi website, ensuring they submit all necessary details and documents by the deadline.

## Key takeaways

*   The BET 2026 application form is available exclusively online on the SDA Bocconi website.
*   Candidates must register using a valid email ID and mobile number, upload required documents, and pay the application fee.
*   The final deadline for submitting the BET 2026 application is April 15, 2026.
*   A non-refundable application fee of INR 2500 is required to complete the registration process.

## BET 2026 Important Dates and Application Rounds

The BET 2026 application process is structured into several rounds, each with its own submission deadline. Candidates aiming for potential tuition fee waivers should note the final submission date.

Here’s a look at the key dates for BET 2026 applications:

| Dates         | Application Round Deadline         |
| :------------ | :--------------------------------- |
| April 15, 2026 | Round 4 Submission Deadline (Final) |
| March 3, 2026 | Round 3 Submission Deadline        |
| January 13, 2026 | Round 2 Submission Deadline        |
| November 4, 2025 | Round 1 Submission Deadline        |

Applicants who successfully complete their registration will receive their specific BET 2026 exam dates via their registered email address.

## How to Apply for BET 2026

The application process for BET 2026 is straightforward and entirely online. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth registration:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the SDA Bocconi Asia Center's official website.
2.  **Locate the Application Portal:** Look for the 'Programs' section and then 'Bocconi Test'. Alternatively, find an 'Apply Now' link on the My Application Portal section.
3.  **Start Registration:** You will need to provide basic details such as your name, nationality, date of birth, and contact information (email and mobile number).
4.  **Fill the Application Form:** Complete all sections of the application form with accurate personal, academic, and professional details.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Prepare and upload all required documents as specified on the portal. This typically includes academic transcripts, identity proof, and photographs.
6.  **Pay Application Fee:** The final step involves paying the application fee of INR 2500. This fee is non-refundable and essential for completing your registration.
7.  **Submit and Confirm:** After reviewing all details, submit your application. You should receive a confirmation email once your application is successfully processed.

## Documents Required for BET 2026 Application

To complete your BET 2026 application, you will need to prepare and upload several documents. While the specific list might be detailed on the official portal, commonly required documents include:

*   Recent passport-sized photograph
*   Scanned signature
*   Mark sheets and certificates of 10th and 12th standard
*   Undergraduate degree mark sheets and certificate (if applicable)
*   Proof of identity (e.g., Aadhar card, Passport, PAN card)
*   Work experience certificates (if applicable)

Ensure all documents are clear, legible, and in the specified format and size before uploading.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid During BET Registration

To prevent issues with your application, keep the following points in mind:

*   **Incorrect Information:** Double-check all personal and academic details for accuracy. Any discrepancies could lead to rejection.
*   **Late Submission:** Adhere strictly to the application deadlines for each round. Missing a deadline means missing that round.
*   **Unpaid Fees:** Your application is considered incomplete until the INR 2500 application fee is successfully paid.
*   **Improper Document Uploads:** Ensure all uploaded documents meet the specified format, size, and clarity requirements. Blurry or incorrect documents can cause delays.
*   **Using Invalid Contact Details:** Register with an active email ID and mobile number, as all official communication regarding the exam will be sent to these contacts.

By carefully following these guidelines, you can ensure a smooth and successful BET 2026 application process.
