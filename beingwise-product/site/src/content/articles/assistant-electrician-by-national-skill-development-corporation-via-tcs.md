# Assistant Electrician by National Skill Development Corporation via TCS iON: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those interested in the fundamentals of electrical work and aspiring to a career in the construction sector, the Assistant Electrician online course offers a practical introduction. This program is designed to equip learners with essential skills for handling basic electrical installations safely and efficiently.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a concise, one-week, self-paced online course requiring about six hours of effort.
*   It focuses on foundational electrical knowledge, safety, and basic wiring techniques for construction sites.
*   The course is offered by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and is completely free of charge.
*   While the course provides valuable learning, a formal certificate of completion is not issued.

## Course Overview

An assistant electrician plays a crucial role in any construction project, ensuring that all necessary electrical connections are correctly installed according to project specifications. This helps prevent costly redesigns later on. The Assistant Electrician online training is specifically developed for individuals keen on understanding electrical systems and pursuing a path to becoming a skilled electrician.

This program is an initiative of the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), a public-private partnership established to enhance the skills of the Indian workforce. The course aims to transform unskilled and semi-skilled individuals into productive and proficient workers.

## Eligibility and Learning Experience

To enroll in the Assistant Electrician online training, candidates must have completed at least the 10th grade. The course material is delivered through six hours of high-definition video lectures, presented in an easy-to-understand and engaging manner. Learners can access the content in both English and Hindi, and the self-study format allows for flexible learning at their own pace.

The curriculum is designed to provide practical experience in laying cables for low-voltage (LV) single-phase wiring, using both manual and electric tools. This focus on hands-on application ensures learners gain relevant skills for real-world scenarios.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing the Assistant Electrician course, participants will gain insights into various aspects of the construction industry. The curriculum covers important concepts such as Qualification Packs (QP) and National Occupational Standards (NOS), which are frameworks for skill development in India. Learners will also acquire knowledge about common electrical equipment, instruments, and the specific electrical standards and safety protocols that are mandatory on construction sites.

Beyond basic wiring, the course delves into topics like plastering techniques and how to correct common flaws. It also covers different lighting and cabling methods, along with essential testing and quality control procedures to ensure safe and reliable electrical installations.

## Course Structure

The program is divided into several modules, guiding learners through the fundamentals and practical applications of electrical work:

*   **Module 1: Introduction**
*   **Module 2: General Skills**
*   **Module 3: Health and Safety when Doing Electrical Work**
*   **Module 4: Electrical Diagram**
*   **Module 5: Choosing and Using Power Tools and Equipment**
*   **Module 6: Temporary Electrical Work on Construction Site**
*   **Module 7: Wiring in Permanent Structures**
*   **Module 8: Installation and Maintenance of Temporary LV Electrical Panels**
*   **Testing and Quality Checking**

## Fee and Certificate

This Assistant Electrician course is offered completely free of charge, making it accessible to a wide range of aspiring electricians. While the program provides comprehensive training and valuable knowledge, it is important to note that a formal certificate of completion is not issued upon finishing the course.

This program is ideal for individuals looking to enter the electrical trade, providing them with foundational knowledge and practical skills to begin their journey in the construction industry.
