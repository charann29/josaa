# Top Medical Colleges in Delhi: Ranking, Fees, Admission 2026, Courses, Placements, Cutoff

Delhi is home to a select group of highly regarded medical colleges, primarily government institutions, that attract aspiring doctors from across the country. Admission to these colleges is highly competitive and predominantly based on NEET scores.

## Key takeaways

*   There are approximately 7 top medical colleges in Delhi, all government-owned.
*   Admission to MBBS programs in these institutions is primarily through the NEET entrance exam.
*   AIIMS Delhi consistently ranks as the top medical college in India.
*   Annual fees for many of these colleges are typically less than INR 1 lakh.

## Admission Process and Eligibility Criteria

To gain admission to the top medical colleges in Delhi, candidates must meet specific eligibility requirements and perform well in the NEET examination. The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducts NEET annually.

### Eligibility Criteria

*   Candidates must have passed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English as core subjects.
*   Applicants must be at least 17 years old at the time of admission.
*   Qualifying the NEET exam is mandatory for admission to MBBS and BDS courses.

### NEET Exam Overview

NEET is the single national-level entrance examination for admission to MBBS, BDS, BSc Nursing, BAMS, Veterinary, and other medical/paramedical courses across India. It is conducted once a year in offline mode.

Students from these colleges often secure placements in leading hospitals, research institutions, and government health services. The robust clinical training and academic rigor ensure excellent career opportunities and competitive salary packages upon graduation.
