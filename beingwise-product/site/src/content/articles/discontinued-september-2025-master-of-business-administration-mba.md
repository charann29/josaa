# Discontinued (September 2025) Master of Business Administration (MBA) by SASTRA University: Fee, Review, Duration

This page offers a comprehensive look at the Master of Business Administration (MBA) program previously offered by SASTRA University, which is set to be discontinued in September 2025. While this program will no longer be available for new enrollments from that date, understanding its features can still provide valuable insights into SASTRA's academic approach.

## Key Takeaways

*   SASTRA University's MBA program, offered in an online format, will cease to be available for new admissions after September 2025.
*   The program was designed for a duration of 24 months, delivered through online learning.
*   It aimed to equip learners with essential business skills and provide career support.
*   SASTRA University holds a strong reputation, evidenced by its A++ NAAC accreditation and high NIRF rankings.

## Program Overview

SASTRA University, a private institution established in 1984, has been recognized for its academic excellence, holding an A++ accreditation from NAAC. The university's MBA program was structured to provide a robust online learning experience for aspiring business professionals. It was crafted with a curriculum intended to prepare students for a successful journey in various professional domains.

## Learning Experience and Curriculum Focus

The MBA program emphasized a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application. Students were expected to gain structured understanding of contemporary business concepts. The curriculum was designed to foster analytical and critical thinking, crucial for effective problem-solving in managerial roles. Key skills that the program aimed to develop included:

*   Supply Chain Management
*   Relationship Management
*   Marketing
*   Project Management
*   Operations Management
*   Performance Management
*   Portfolio Management
*   Business Analytics
*   Change Management
*   Customer Relationship Management
*   Data Visualization
*   Strategic Management

These areas collectively reflect a broad approach to modern business education, preparing individuals for diverse challenges.

## Who Was This Program For?

The MBA program was targeted at a diverse group of individuals looking to advance their careers or embark on new professional paths. This included:

*   Fresh graduates aiming to enter the corporate world with a contemporary business qualification.
*   Seasoned professionals seeking career progression and enhancement of their managerial skills.
*   Participants with entrepreneurial aspirations, looking to build a strong foundation in business concepts.

## Highlights and University Standing

SASTRA University consistently ranks among India's top educational institutions. According to the NIRF Rankings 2022, SASTRA was positioned at #24 among universities. The MBA program leveraged this strong reputation, offering a credible and recognized degree. It also featured learning from industry professionals through live, interactive sessions and provided personal career support services for a period of six months.
