# Decoding Men's Ethnicwear by The Designer's Class: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For those passionate about fashion and design, understanding the nuances of Indian ethnic wear for men is a valuable skill. This course, developed by renowned designer Kunal Rawal, offers a deep dive into the world of traditional and contemporary men's ethnic fashion.

## Key Takeaways

*   Learn about the history, cultural roots, and modern trends in Indian men's ethnic fashion.
*   The course is self-paced, delivered through video lectures and text, and taught entirely in English.
*   It includes over 11 assignments and 14 downloadable resources to aid learning.
*   Access to the study material is provided for one year from enrollment.

## Course Overview

The "Decoding Men's Ethnicwear" certification program aims to give students a comprehensive understanding of Indian ethnic fashion for men. It covers everything from the historical origins to the characteristics that define modern ethnic couture. The course is an initiative by The Designer's Class, an e-learning platform dedicated to various design disciplines, and is specifically crafted by designer Kunal Rawal.

## What You Will Learn

This program is structured to enhance your fashion design skills and industry knowledge. Upon completion, you will have a stronger grasp of fashion design principles and the factors that shape trends in men's ethnic clothing. The curriculum explores various design elements, traditional Indian craft techniques like weaving, printing, and embroidery, the overall design process, and product development strategies for different garment types, including unstructured, lower, and structured apparel. This knowledge will enable you to create innovative design briefs for ethnic wear projects.

## Course Structure and Content

The course is designed for self-study and is delivered through a combination of video lectures and text-based materials. It offers approximately 1 hour and 53 minutes of detailed video content, supplemented by 11 assignments for practical application and 14 downloadable resources for further reference. The learning medium is English, and students get one year of access to all study materials from their enrollment date.

### Syllabus Highlights

The curriculum covers essential topics for aspiring ethnic wear designers:

*   Introduction to the Designer
*   Introduction to Indian Ethnicwear
*   Indian Ethnic Menswear Designers
*   Elements of Design in Ethnicwear
*   Indian Crafts & Techniques
*   The Design Process
*   Contemporary Trends in Design Process
*   Creative Ideation in Design Process
*   Market-Ready Design Strategies
*   Product Development: Unstructured Garments
*   Product Development: Lower Garments
*   Product Development: Structured Garments
*   Final Assessment

## Fees and Certification

The fee for the entire "Decoding Men's Ethnicwear" online course is ₹2,499. It's important to note that while the course offers valuable learning and assignments, it does not provide a formal certificate upon completion.

## How to Apply

Since this is an online, self-paced course, the application process is generally straightforward. Interested individuals can typically enroll directly through The Designer's Class platform. There are no specific eligibility criteria mentioned, making it accessible to anyone interested in fashion design, particularly those focusing on men's ethnic wear.

This course offers a fantastic opportunity to learn from an industry expert and deepen your understanding of a significant segment of Indian fashion.
