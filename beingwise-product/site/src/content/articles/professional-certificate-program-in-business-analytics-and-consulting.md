# Professional Certificate Program in Business Analytics & Consulting by upGrad: Fee, Duration, How to Apply

For Indian students and professionals keen on mastering the intersection of data and business strategy, the Professional Certificate Program in Business Analytics & Consulting offers a structured online learning path. This program aims to equip participants with the skills to translate complex data into practical business solutions.

## Key takeaways

*   This is a 6-month online program co-developed with PwC India, focusing on real-world business analytics and consulting.
*   The curriculum includes learning 12 tools and working through 30 case studies to provide hands-on experience.
*   Eligibility requires either current college enrollment or a minimum of two years of work experience.
*   The total program fee is ₹62,000, with EMI options available.

## Program Overview and Collaboration

This Professional Certificate Program is designed to bridge the gap between data insights and strategic business decisions. It is a collaborative effort, with the curriculum co-developed by upGrad and PwC India. This collaboration ensures that the content is relevant to industry standards and current business challenges. Participants gain exposure to real business scenarios through live masterclasses, practical projects, and interactive mentorship sessions.

## Learning Experience and Tools

The program is delivered entirely online, using a combination of video and text-based modules. It emphasises practical application, incorporating 30 case studies and training on 12 essential tools. Key analytical concepts covered include exploratory data analysis, statistical inference, hypothesis testing, and optimisation. Participants will also develop proficiency in communicating data-driven insights using powerful visualisation and reporting tools such as Excel, Tableau, SQL dashboards, and Power BI.

## Eligibility and Admissions Process

To be eligible for this program, applicants must either be currently enrolled in college or possess a minimum of two years of professional work experience. The admission process is straightforward:

1.  Visit the official program webpage.
2.  Click on the 'Apply Now' button next to your preferred payment plan.
3.  Complete the application form with your details.
4.  Attempt the scholarship test, if applicable.
5.  Make the required fee payment to secure your enrollment.

## Program Fees and Payment Options

The total fee for the Professional Certificate Program in Business Analytics & Consulting is ₹62,000. For flexibility, an Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) option is available, starting from ₹2,791 per month. The program awards a certificate upon successful completion, provided by upGrad.

## Detailed Syllabus

The program's curriculum is structured to build skills progressively:

### Course 0: Preparatory Content

*   Overview of Business Analytics & Consulting
*   Business Problem Solving

### Course 1: Data Analysis using Excel

*   Slicing and Dicing Data
*   Cell Referencing and Text Functions
*   Creating and Formatting Charts
*   Pivot Table and VLOOKUP
*   What-If Analysis

### Course 2: Business Problem Solving and Storytelling

*   Fundamentals of Management
*   Understanding Problems & Formulating Hypotheses
*   Analysing Patterns and Storytelling
*   Data Analysis and Visualization Using Power BI
*   Data Visualisation with Tableau (Optional)

### Course 3: Basics of Python

*   Python Datatypes and Operations
*   Python Data Structures
*   Python Control Structures
*   Python Libraries - NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn (Optional)

This program is ideal for individuals looking to upskill, transition into analytical roles, or enhance their consulting capabilities.
