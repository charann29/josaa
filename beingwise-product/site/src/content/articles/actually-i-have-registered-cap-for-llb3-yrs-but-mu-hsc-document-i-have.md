## Key takeaways

*   DigiLocker documents are generally accepted as valid and original for CAP registration.

## DigiLocker Documents: Are They Valid?

Yes, documents downloaded from DigiLocker are widely accepted as authentic and original by most educational institutions and admission authorities, including for CAP registration. The government's DigiLocker platform provides digitally verified documents, which means they hold the same validity as their physical counterparts. So, uploading your HSC document from DigiLocker for your 3-year LLB CAP application is generally permissible and should not cause an issue.

## Handling Missing Originals and Re-editing Your Application

It's understandable that your original HSC document might be temporarily misplaced, especially if your college is involved. The fact that it will take about 15 days to retrieve is a common scenario. By re-editing your application form, you've made a proactive and smart move. This allows you to update any relevant information or indicate that your original document is in the process of being obtained.

When re-editing, ensure you do so carefully and well before the application deadline. Double-check all details to make sure they are accurate and consistent with your other submitted information. After making changes, always confirm that all necessary documents, including your DigiLocker HSC certificate, are properly uploaded and clearly visible.

## What Happens When You Get Your Original Document?

Once you receive your original HSC document from your college, it's crucial to update your application form if the portal allows for it. Some admission processes might require you to upload the physical copy once it's available, or present it during a verification stage. Keep an eye on the official CAP portal and your registered email for any specific instructions regarding this.

## Potential Scenarios During Admission

If the admission authority requires a physical copy for in-depth scrutiny, they might:

*   **Provide a deadline:** They may give you a specific date by which you need to submit the physical document.
*   **Provisionally hold your admission:** Your admission might be conditional until the original document is verified. This is usually temporary and resolved once you present the required document.

As long as the details you've provided are correct, and your DigiLocker documents are valid, you should not face significant problems. The key is to be responsive to any communication from the admission authority.

## Staying Informed

The most important step is to consistently monitor the official CAP portal for any announcements, updates, or specific requests related to your application. Also, regularly check your email inbox (including spam/junk folders) for communications from the admission authority. If you have any doubts, the official contact details provided on the CAP portal are your best resource for clarification.
