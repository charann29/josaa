# How much the median salary here

Understanding the salary prospects at any institution is a major factor for students and parents when choosing a higher education path. Let's look at the median salary figures for various programs at JNTUH College of Engineering to give you a clear picture.

## Key Takeaways

*   The median salary for undergraduate B.Tech programs stands at ₹9.42 Lakhs per annum.
*   For postgraduate M.Tech programs, the median salary is slightly higher at ₹9.71 Lakhs per annum.
*   The college boasts an overall placement rate of 73% for its students.
*   Top companies from various sectors, including IT, finance, and product development, recruit from the campus.

## Understanding Median Salary

When we talk about a 'median salary,' it means that half of the placed students earn this amount or more, and the other half earn less. This figure is often a more reliable indicator of typical earnings than an average, as it isn't skewed by a few exceptionally high or low salaries. A median salary in the range of ₹9-10 Lakhs per annum is a strong sign of good placement support and the quality of jobs available to graduates.

## Placement Trends for B.Tech Programs

For students pursuing their Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degrees, the median salary recorded is an encouraging ₹9.42 Lakhs per annum. This figure reflects the strong industry connections and the career preparation support provided by the institution. It shows that B.Tech graduates are securing well-paying roles across various industries upon completing their studies.

## Placement Trends for M.Tech Programs

Postgraduate students undertaking Master of Technology (M.Tech) programs see a median salary of ₹9.71 Lakhs per annum. The slightly higher median for M.Tech graduates often reflects their specialized skills and advanced knowledge, which are highly valued in specific industry roles. This indicates a positive return on investment for students pursuing advanced degrees.

## Top Recruiters and Highest Packages

JNTUH College of Engineering attracts a wide array of reputable companies for campus placements. Prominent recruiters include technology giants like TCS, Wipro, and Amazon, as well as financial services firms like Goldman Sachs, and product companies such as CommVault. Other significant recruiters include Cognizant, among many others. In recent years, the highest reported salary packages have ranged between ₹40 Lakhs and ₹46 Lakhs per annum, showcasing the potential for exceptional career opportunities for high-achieving students.

## Overall Placement Success

The college maintains an impressive overall placement rate of 73%. This percentage indicates that a significant majority of students seeking employment through the college's placement cell successfully secure job offers. Such a high rate underscores the effectiveness of the career services and the strong demand for graduates from JNTUH College of Engineering in the job market.

These figures offer a comprehensive look at the earning potential and placement success for graduates from JNTUH College of Engineering, helping you make an informed decision about your future studies.
