# How to Take Admission in BE in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at TCET Based on MHT CET Marks

For students aspiring to pursue a Bachelor of Engineering (BE) in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Thakur College of Engineering and Technology (TCET), admission primarily depends on your performance in the MHT CET examination and participation in the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) counselling process.

## Key Takeaways

*   Admission to TCET's BE AI & Data Science program is through the DTE Centralized Admission Process (CAP) based on MHT CET scores.
*   A strong MHT CET score, typically above 93 marks (based on past trends), is crucial for a competitive application.
*   Careful registration and document verification on the official DTE website are essential steps.
*   Strategically filling your college preference list during counselling can significantly impact your admission chances.

## Understanding the MHT CET and DTE CAP

The MHT CET (Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test) is the state-level entrance examination for admission to various undergraduate professional courses in engineering and technology in Maharashtra. Your performance in this exam determines your eligibility and ranking for colleges like TCET.

Following the MHT CET results, the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) conducts a Centralized Admission Process (CAP). This online counselling procedure allows eligible candidates to apply for various engineering colleges across Maharashtra. It's through this CAP that you will secure a seat at TCET if you meet the criteria.

## MHT CET Score for TCET AI & Data Science

Based on previous years' admission trends, candidates aiming for the BE Artificial Intelligence and Data Science program at TCET should target a strong MHT CET score. Historically, a score above 93 marks has been considered competitive for securing admission in this popular branch. While cut-offs can vary each year due to factors like the number of applicants and exam difficulty, striving for a score in this range will significantly enhance your prospects.

## The DTE Counselling Process: Step-by-Step

Participating in the DTE CAP is a multi-stage process that requires attention to detail:

1.  **Online Registration:** The first step is to register on the official DTE website. You will need to provide your personal details, academic qualifications, and MHT CET scores.
2.  **Document Verification:** After registration, you must get your documents verified. This can be done online or at designated Facilitation Centers (FCs) as per DTE guidelines. Ensure all submitted documents are accurate and complete to avoid any discrepancies.
3.  **Option Form Submission (College Preference List):** This is a critical stage. You will be asked to submit an 'Option Form' where you list your preferred colleges and courses in order of priority. To apply for TCET's BE AI & Data Science, you must include it in your list. It's wise to research various colleges and branches thoroughly before finalizing your preferences.
4.  **Seat Allotment:** Based on your MHT CET score, your chosen preferences, and seat availability, DTE will allot you a seat in a college and branch. You will be able to view your allotment status on the DTE portal.
5.  **Acceptance and Admission:** If you are allotted a seat you wish to accept, you must confirm your acceptance online and then report to the allotted college to complete the admission formalities and pay the necessary fees within the stipulated timeframe.

## Important Considerations

*   **Stay Updated:** Always refer to the official DTE Maharashtra website for the latest schedules, notifications, and guidelines regarding the MHT CET counselling process.
*   **Document Checklist:** Prepare all necessary documents (academic certificates, MHT CET scorecard, identity proof, caste certificate if applicable, etc.) in advance to ensure a smooth verification process.
*   **Research TCET:** Before filling your option form, thoroughly research TCET, including its facilities, faculty, and specific curriculum for Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. This will help you make an informed decision.

By carefully navigating the MHT CET examination and the DTE counselling process, you can significantly improve your chances of gaining admission to the BE Artificial Intelligence and Data Science program at Thakur College of Engineering and Technology.
