# Is the IIMK BMS Admit Card Released?

For students eagerly awaiting updates on the IIM Kozhikode (IIMK) Bachelor in Management Studies (BMS) Aptitude Test, here's the latest information regarding your admit cards.

## Key Takeaways

*   The IIMK BMS Aptitude Test admit cards are now available.
*   They were released on June 13, 2025.
*   You can download your admit card from the official IIMK BMS webpage.
*   Access requires your login credentials from your application submission.

## Admit Card Availability Confirmed

We understand the anticipation surrounding admit card releases. We can confirm that the admit cards for the IIM Kozhikode BMS Aptitude Test have indeed been made available. This is a crucial step for all registered candidates as it contains vital information about your examination.

## How to Download Your Admit Card

Accessing your admit card is a straightforward process. You will need to visit the official IIMK BMS webpage. On this page, look for the designated login area for applicants. You will be prompted to enter your unique User ID and Password. These are the same credentials you used when you initially submitted your application form for the 2025 intake. Once logged in, you should be able to view and download your admit card.

## Important Information on Your Admit Card

Your admit card is more than just a piece of paper; it's your gateway to the examination. It will contain essential details such as your roll number, the date and time of your aptitude test, the specific examination venue, and important instructions you must follow on the test day. Please ensure you carefully review all the information printed on it. If you find any discrepancies, it's vital to contact the IIMK admissions office immediately for clarification.

## What to Do Before Test Day

Once you've downloaded your admit card, take a printout and keep it safe. It's advisable to print multiple copies. Familiarize yourself with the examination venue location well in advance to avoid any last-minute stress. Also, re-read all the instructions on the admit card regarding permissible items, reporting time, and any prohibited materials. Being well-prepared ensures a smooth experience on the day of your test.

We wish all candidates the very best for their IIMK BMS Aptitude Test!
