# What was the placement for ENTC Branch?

For students considering an Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ENTC) degree, understanding potential career outcomes is crucial. While specific ENTC branch placement data for K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology can be difficult to isolate, we can look at the overall placement trends at the institute to get a good sense of opportunities.

## Key Takeaways

*   K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology saw a significant number of companies participating in its recent placement drives.
*   The institute has recorded a notable highest salary package offered to a student.
*   The average salary package for engineering graduates from the institute falls within a competitive range.
*   Prominent companies from various sectors are regular recruiters at the institute.

## Overall Placement Scenario at K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology

K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology has established a robust placement record. In recent years, the institute attracted over 18 new companies, contributing to a total of 207 companies participating in its placement activities. This broad participation indicates a wide range of opportunities available to engineering graduates, including those from the ENTC branch.

## Salary Packages Offered

The salary packages offered to students at K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology reflect the quality of education and industry demand. The highest salary package secured by a student from the institute was ₹13 lakh per annum. The average salary package for engineering graduates generally ranges between ₹6 lakh and ₹7 lakh per annum. These figures provide a realistic expectation for students pursuing an engineering degree here.

## Leading Recruiters

The institute boasts a strong list of recruiters that regularly hire its graduates. Major companies like Jaro Education, Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Accenture, Pinclick, and Amazon are among the key employers visiting the campus. The presence of such diverse and reputable companies suggests a wide array of career paths for students, including those with an ENTC background, in fields ranging from IT services and consulting to e-commerce and education technology.

## What This Means for ENTC Graduates

While specific ENTC branch data might not always be publicly disaggregated, the overall placement performance of K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology provides a positive outlook. ENTC graduates often find roles in telecommunications, electronics design, embedded systems, software development (especially in communication protocols), and even core IT services. The diverse set of recruiters visiting the campus indicates that skills acquired in an ENTC program are highly valued across various industries.

Considering these overall trends can help prospective students understand the career potential after completing their engineering studies at the institute.
