Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology, often simply called Sathyabama University, is a well-known institution in India. If you're considering a career in aeronautical engineering and wondering about the opportunities for girls at this university, this page will provide clarity.

## Key Takeaways

*   Yes, the B.E./B.Tech Aeronautical Engineering program at Sathyabama University is open to female students.
*   The total course fee for B.E./B.Tech Aeronautical Engineering is approximately ₹70,000.
*   The university maintains a strong placement record, with an average salary package for aeronautical engineering graduates ranging from ₹4-6 lakh per annum.
*   In a recent year, 268 companies visited for placements across various disciplines, resulting in 1827 student placements overall.

## Aeronautical Engineering for Girls

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology offers a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) or Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program in Aeronautical Engineering, and it is fully accessible to female students. The university prides itself on providing a diverse and inclusive learning environment across all its programs, which span engineering, science, technology, architecture, dental science, pharmacy, nursing, management, and arts.

## Fees for Aeronautical Engineering

For students interested in the B.E./B.Tech Aeronautical Engineering program at Sathyabama University, the total fee for the entire course is approximately ₹70,000. It's always advisable to check the official university prospectus or website for the most current and detailed fee structure, as figures can sometimes be subject to change or additional charges.

## University Rankings and Recognition

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology has consistently been recognized in various national rankings. According to the NIRF 2021 Ranking:

*   It was ranked 61st in the overall category.
*   In the engineering category, the institute secured the 55th position.
*   Within the university category, it was ranked 40th in 2021, a slight shift from its 39th rank in 2020.

These rankings reflect the institution's commitment to academic excellence and its standing among other universities in India.

## Placement Opportunities

Sathyabama University has a dedicated placement cell that works to ensure good career opportunities for its graduates. The university has a commendable track record for placements across its various programs. Specifically for Aeronautical Engineering, in a recent academic year:

*   Approximately 268 companies visited the campus for recruitment across various departments.
*   A total of 1827 students from different courses secured placements.
*   The average salary package offered to graduates, including those from Aeronautical Engineering, ranged between ₹4-6 lakh per annum.

These figures demonstrate the university's effectiveness in connecting students with industry opportunities upon graduation.

Sathyabama University offers a robust and inclusive environment for aspiring aeronautical engineers, including female students, backed by strong academic programs and placement support.
