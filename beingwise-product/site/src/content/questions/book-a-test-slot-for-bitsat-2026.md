# How can I book a test slot for BITSAT 2026?

Securing your test slot for BITSAT 2026 is a crucial step in your application journey. This process allows you to choose a convenient date and time for your examination.

## Key takeaways

*   BITSAT slot booking is exclusively done through the official BITS Pilani admissions website.
*   You'll need your BITSAT application number and password to log in and select your slot.
*   Slots are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis, so act quickly once the window opens.
*   Once confirmed, your test slot cannot be changed, so choose carefully.

## Understanding the BITSAT Slot Booking Process

The BITSAT slot booking facility is an online system designed to give candidates flexibility in scheduling their examination. It's a mandatory step for all registered applicants who wish to appear for the BITSAT entrance test. During this period, you will visit the official admissions portal, log in, and then pick your preferred test date and time from the available options. It's essential to understand that without booking a slot, you will not be able to sit for the BITSAT examination.

## Step-by-Step Guide to Booking Your Slot

Booking your BITSAT 2026 test slot is a straightforward process, but it requires careful attention to detail:

1.  **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to the official BITS Pilani admissions website (admissions.bits-pilani.ac.in). This is the only authorized portal for BITSAT-related activities, including slot booking.
2.  **Locate the Slot Booking Link:** Look for the specific link related to BITSAT 2026 slot booking. This link usually becomes active once the booking window opens.
3.  **Log In:** You will be prompted to enter your BITSAT application number and the password you created during the application process. Ensure these details are accurate to avoid login issues.
4.  **Select Your Preferred Slot:** Once logged in, you will see a calendar or a list of available dates and times. Carefully review the options and select the one that best suits your schedule. Remember, availability is dynamic and based on how many candidates have already booked.
5.  **Confirm Your Booking:** After selecting your slot, you will usually be asked to confirm your choice. Double-check all details before finalizing, as changes are generally not permitted once confirmed.
6.  **Print Confirmation:** It's highly advisable to print or save a digital copy of your slot booking confirmation page for your records. This document will contain important details about your exam date, time, and test center.

## Important Dates and Deadlines

The exact dates for BITSAT 2026 slot booking will be announced by BITS Pilani on their official website. It's crucial to regularly check the admissions portal for these announcements. Typically, there is a specific window of a few days during which candidates can book their slots. Missing this deadline will mean you cannot appear for the exam, so mark your calendars and set reminders once the dates are released.

## What to do if you miss the Slot Booking Window?

If, for any reason, you miss the official slot booking window, you will unfortunately not be able to choose your preferred test date and time. In such cases, BITS Pilani may, at their discretion, allot a test slot to you based on availability. However, there is no guarantee that this allotted slot will be convenient for you. Therefore, it is strongly recommended to book your slot as soon as the window opens to secure your preferred option.

Careful planning and timely action are key to a smooth BITSAT experience.
