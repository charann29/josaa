Navigating university admissions can be a bit like solving a puzzle, especially with specific quotas and varied entrance exam scores. Let's break down your chances for Nirma University's B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI-ML) program.

*   Your GUJCET rank of 300 is very promising for Nirma AI-ML admission.
*   The Home State (HS) Female quota significantly boosts your prospects.
*   Previous year's closing ranks suggest a strong possibility of securing a seat.
*   Always monitor the official admission portals for the latest updates and counselling schedules.

## Understanding Nirma University Admissions

Nirma University is a highly regarded institution, and its B.Tech programs, especially in cutting-edge fields like AI-ML, are very popular. Admissions are primarily based on GUJCET ranks for Gujarat students, with a certain percentage of seats reserved for different categories and quotas, including the Home State (HS) Female quota.

## Your GUJCET Rank and Percentile

You've mentioned a GUJCET rank of 300 and an 88th percentile in PCM (GSEB). The GUJCET rank is the more critical factor for Nirma University admissions through the state quota. An 88th percentile in PCM indicates a solid academic foundation, which complements your strong GUJCET performance.

## Previous Year's Cut-offs for AI-ML

Looking at the previous year's admission trends can provide valuable insight. For the B.Tech AI-ML program at Nirma University, the closing ranks in the general category for Round 1 counselling were approximately 307. In subsequent rounds, the ranks extended to around 394 in Round 2 and 399 in Round 3. Your GUJCET rank of 300 places you well within these figures, even before considering any specific quotas.

## The Advantage of the HS Female Quota

The Home State (HS) Female quota is a significant advantage. This reservation means a certain number of seats are specifically set aside for female candidates from Gujarat. While exact cut-offs for this specific quota aren't always publicly detailed in the same way as general ranks, having this quota usually means the closing ranks for these reserved seats are more lenient than the general category. Combined with your already strong GUJCET rank, this quota substantially increases your likelihood of admission.

## Next Steps in the Admission Process

To ensure you don't miss out, it's crucial to participate actively in the counselling process conducted by the Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC). Make sure to register, fill in your choices carefully, and attend all counselling rounds. Keep a close watch on the official ACPC website for schedules, seat matrix, and detailed instructions.

With your excellent GUJCET rank and the benefit of the HS Female quota, you have a very strong chance of getting into Nirma University for B.Tech AI-ML. Good luck with your application!
