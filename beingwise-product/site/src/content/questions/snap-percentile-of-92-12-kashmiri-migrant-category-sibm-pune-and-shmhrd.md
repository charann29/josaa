# Snap Percentile of 92.12: SIBM Pune & SCMHRD Calls Possible for Kashmiri Migrants?

If you're a Kashmiri Migrant candidate with a SNAP percentile of 92.12, you're likely wondering about your prospects for top Symbiosis institutes like SIBM Pune and SCMHRD. This article explores your strong chances and what to expect next.

## Key Takeaways

*   A SNAP percentile of 92.12, combined with the Kashmiri Migrant category, indicates a very strong possibility of receiving interview calls from both SIBM Pune and SCMHRD.
*   Both institutes are highly regarded for their MBA programs and holistic student development.
*   Admission to the flagship MBA programs at these institutes is primarily based on SNAP scores, followed by group exercises and personal interviews.
*   The Kashmiri Migrant category provides specific reservation benefits, significantly enhancing admission chances for eligible candidates.

## Understanding Your Strong Position

Your SNAP percentile of 92.12 is highly competitive. When combined with your eligibility under the Kashmiri Migrant (KM) category, your chances of receiving an interview call from premier Symbiosis institutions like SIBM Pune and SCMHRD are exceptionally high. The KM category offers specific reservation quotas, which can significantly lower the effective cutoff for eligible candidates compared to the general category.

## SIBM Pune: A Hub for Holistic Development

Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM) Pune is renowned for its comprehensive approach to student growth, focusing on both academic excellence and overall personal development. SIBM Pune offers a range of postgraduate management programs, including its flagship Master of Business Administration (MBA), an Executive MBA, and an MBA in Innovation and Entrepreneurship, each with various specializations designed to meet industry demands. The institute consistently ranks among the top business schools in India, attracting a diverse pool of talent.

## SCMHRD Pune: A Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1993, Symbiosis Centre for Management and Human Resource Development (SCMHRD) Pune has built a strong reputation over decades. It is particularly known for its robust MBA programs, especially in Human Resources. Admission to SCMHRD's MBA program is primarily based on the SNAP entrance exam scores. For its Executive MBA program, candidates typically require a valid professional background and work experience. SCMHRD focuses on developing future-ready managers and leaders, emphasizing practical exposure and industry relevance.

## The Admission Process Ahead

Once the SNAP results are out and shortlists are announced, eligible candidates will proceed to the next stages of the admission process. This typically includes a Group Exercise (GE), Written Ability Test (WAT), and Personal Interview (PI). Your strong SNAP score will open the door to these crucial rounds. It's advisable to start preparing for these subsequent stages, focusing on current affairs, general business knowledge, and developing strong communication skills to articulate your aspirations and experiences effectively during the interview.

Your profile presents a very promising outlook for securing a seat at SIBM Pune or SCMHRD. We wish you the best in your admission journey!
