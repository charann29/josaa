# Out of 600 I got 488 and total 802 ts eamcet 14k any chance in BPT? St cat

It's great you're exploring your options for a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) program! Understanding the admission process can sometimes feel complex, especially with different entrance exams and university procedures. Let's clarify your chances for BPT admission in Telangana.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your TS EAMCET rank (14,000) is **not** used for BPT admissions in Telangana.
*   BPT admissions in Telangana are managed by Kaloji Narayana Rao University of Health Sciences (KNRUHS) and are based on your Intermediate Public Examination (IPE, or 12th grade) marks in Biology, Physics, and Chemistry.
*   With 488 out of 600 marks (approximately 81.33%) and being from the ST category, you have a strong academic profile, significantly boosting your chances.
*   You should diligently watch for the KNRUHS paramedical admission notifications, as this is the direct path for BPT.

## TS EAMCET vs. BPT Admissions

Many students understandably get confused about which entrance exam applies to which course. In Telangana, the TS EAMCET (now often called TS EAPCET) is primarily for admissions into Engineering (B.E./B.Tech.), Pharmacy (B.Pharmacy), and Pharm.D programs. It does not govern admissions for medical, dental, or paramedical courses like BPT.

## The KNRUHS Admission Process for BPT

For BPT programs in Telangana, the admissions are handled by Kaloji Narayana Rao University of Health Sciences (KNRUHS). They conduct a separate counselling process for paramedical courses, which includes BPT. This admission is based on your academic performance in your 12th-grade (Intermediate Public Examination - IPE) subjects, specifically Biology, Physics, and Chemistry. Your overall score of 488 out of 600 indicates a very good academic record, which is a significant advantage.

## Your Strong Chances in the ST Category

Given your 12th-grade score of 488 out of 600 (roughly 81.33%) and your Scheduled Tribe (ST) category status, you have a very strong chance of securing a BPT seat. Government seats and convener-quota seats (often referred to as 'free seats') in private colleges are highly sought after, and your profile puts you in a competitive position. While government BPT seats can be limited and highly competitive, your excellent 12th-grade marks, combined with your category, significantly improve your prospects for both government and private college seats under the KNRUHS counselling.

## What to Do Next

Your primary focus should be to regularly check the official KNRUHS website for their paramedical admission notifications. These notifications will provide details on application forms, eligibility criteria, counselling schedules, and required documents. Ensure you complete all steps accurately and on time to maximize your opportunities.

Stay proactive and keep an eye on official announcements from KNRUHS to ensure you don't miss any crucial deadlines for your BPT application.
