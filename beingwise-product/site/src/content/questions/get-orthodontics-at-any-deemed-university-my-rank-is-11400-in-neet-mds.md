# Can I Get Orthodontics at Any Deemed University? My Rank is 11400 in NEET MDS 2021.

Understanding your chances for an MDS Orthodontics seat with a NEET MDS rank of 11400 can feel complex. Let's explore the possibilities, particularly within Deemed Universities, based on past admission trends.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your NEET MDS 2021 rank of 11400 suggests a potential opportunity for clinical MDS seats, including Orthodontics, especially in later counseling rounds.
*   Deemed Universities often have slightly different cutoff trends compared to government colleges, potentially offering more options for ranks like yours.
*   Historical data indicates that some clinical seats have been allotted to candidates with ranks up to 7000-8000 in previous years, and Deemed Universities may extend further.
*   Always refer to the official counseling portals and information bulletins for the most accurate and up-to-date cutoff data and seat matrix.

## Understanding NEET MDS Ranks and Cutoffs

NEET MDS is the gateway to postgraduate dental courses in India. Your rank is a crucial factor in determining your eligibility for various specializations like Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics. While a rank of 11400 might seem challenging for top government colleges, the landscape of Deemed Universities often presents additional avenues.

Cutoffs for MDS programs fluctuate each year due to factors such as the number of applicants, seat availability, and candidate preferences. It's essential to look at past year trends as a guide, but always remember that the current year's actual cutoffs can vary.

## Deemed Universities and Orthodontics Admissions

Deemed Universities play a significant role in postgraduate medical and dental education. They conduct their own counseling for the seats under their management quota, often through the Medical Counseling Committee (MCC). These institutions typically have higher fee structures but can offer more opportunities for candidates whose ranks might not secure a seat in government colleges.

Based on previous years' admission cycles, candidates with ranks in the range of 7000-8000 have secured clinical seats in certain institutions during the second and third rounds of counseling. For Deemed Universities, these cutoffs can sometimes extend further, making your rank of 11400 potentially viable.

Some Deemed Universities that have historically offered MDS Orthodontics programs and might be worth considering include:

*   **Sri Siddhartha Dental College, Tumkur**
*   **DY Patil University School of Dentistry, Navi Mumbai**
*   **SRM Dental College, Kattankulathur**

It is vital to monitor the official MCC counseling portal for the specific year's seat matrix and cutoff information as counseling progresses.

## The Counseling Process for Deemed Universities

Admissions to Deemed Universities for MDS courses are generally handled by the MCC through a centralized counseling process. This usually involves multiple rounds, including a mop-up round and sometimes a stray vacancy round. Your best strategy is to participate in all applicable rounds of counseling, carefully filling your choices based on your preferences and the available seat matrix.

During counseling, pay close attention to the fee structures, bond conditions, and accreditation status of the institutions you are considering. Detailed information is usually provided in the information bulletin released by the counseling authority.

With a rank of 11400 in NEET MDS 2021, securing an Orthodontics seat in a Deemed University is a definite possibility, especially if you participate actively in all counseling rounds and remain flexible with your college choices.
