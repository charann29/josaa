# Can I get direct admission in IMS BHU for BPT course as I had appeared in NEET UG 2025 exam?

Many students aspiring for a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) degree wonder about the admission process at prestigious institutions like the Institute of Medical Sciences (IMS) at Banaras Hindu University (BHU). Let's clarify the pathway to secure a spot in their BPT program.

## Key Takeaways

*   Direct admission to the BPT course at IMS BHU is not available.
*   Admission is based on the CUET-UG exam, not NEET UG scores.
*   You must appear for CUET-UG and meet BHU's specific eligibility and counselling requirements.
*   Always refer to official BHU admission notifications for the most current information.

## Understanding the Admission Process for BPT at IMS BHU

For undergraduate courses, including the BPT program at IMS BHU, admissions are strictly merit-based and follow a well-defined process. Unlike some medical or dental courses, your NEET UG 2025 scores will not be considered for entry into the BPT program at IMS BHU.

## The Role of CUET-UG

The primary entrance examination for BPT admission at IMS BHU is the Common University Entrance Test (CUET-UG). This national-level examination, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), is mandatory for admission to various undergraduate programs across central universities, including BHU. Therefore, to be considered for BPT at IMS BHU, you must have appeared for and achieved a qualifying score in the CUET-UG exam.

## Steps to Secure Your BPT Seat at IMS BHU

To successfully gain admission to the BPT course at IMS BHU, prospective students need to follow these crucial steps:

1.  **Appear for CUET-UG:** Ensure you register for and take the CUET-UG examination for the relevant academic year.
2.  **Achieve Required Scores:** Obtain the necessary scores in CUET-UG as specified by BHU for the BPT program. These cut-offs can vary each year based on competition and other factors.
3.  **Participate in BHU Counselling:** After the CUET-UG results are announced, BHU will conduct its own counselling process. You must register for this counselling, fill in your preferences, and participate actively.
4.  **Meet Eligibility Criteria:** Alongside your CUET-UG performance, you must fulfill all other academic and age-related eligibility criteria set by BHU for the BPT course. These details are typically outlined in the official BHU admission prospectus.

## Where to Find Official Information

It is vital to rely solely on official sources for admission details. Always check the official BHU admission portal and notifications for the latest updates regarding eligibility criteria, application deadlines, CUET-UG subject combinations required for BPT, and counselling schedules. This will ensure you have the most accurate and up-to-date information directly from the university.

While direct admission is not an option, a clear path through CUET-UG and BHU's counselling process awaits aspiring BPT students at IMS BHU.
