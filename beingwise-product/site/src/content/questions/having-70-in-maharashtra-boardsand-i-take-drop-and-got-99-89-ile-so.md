# Can I Get into COEP Pune or VJTI with 70% in Boards and 99.89%ile in MHT CET?

It's fantastic that you've significantly improved your MHT CET score after taking a drop! Let's explore your admission prospects at prestigious institutions like COEP Pune and VJTI Mumbai with your academic profile.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your 99.89 percentile in MHT CET puts you in a very strong position for top engineering colleges in Maharashtra.
*   A 70% score in your Maharashtra Board exams meets the basic eligibility criteria for admission.
*   COEP Pune and VJTI Mumbai primarily admit students through the MHT CET state quota.
*   Strategic participation in the CAP counselling rounds is crucial to secure a seat.

## Understanding MHT CET and Board Marks for Admissions

Maharashtra's engineering admissions primarily rely on the MHT CET score. While your board marks (Class 12) are essential for eligibility, they usually don't carry a direct weight in the final merit calculation for most engineering programs beyond meeting the minimum percentage. For general category candidates, the typical minimum requirement for board exams is 45% or 50%, which your 70% comfortably surpasses. This means your high MHT CET percentile will be the main driver of your admission chances.

## Your Strong Chances at COEP Pune

Given your 99.89 percentile in MHT CET, your prospects for securing a seat at COEP Pune are exceptionally good. COEP Pune is one of Maharashtra's most sought-after engineering colleges, known for its excellent programs. Admissions are predominantly through the MHT CET state quota. Historically, even the most competitive branches like Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, and Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering have cut-offs well within reach of your percentile. Your 70% in the Maharashtra Board exams comfortably meets the eligibility criteria.

### Popular Branches and Expected Cut-offs (MHT CET Percentile)

While specific cut-offs vary each year based on applicant pool and seat availability, your percentile places you very competitively for:

*   **Computer Engineering:** Highly probable
*   **Information Technology:** Highly probable
*   **Artificial Intelligence & Data Science:** Highly probable
*   **Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering:** Highly probable
*   **Mechanical Engineering:** Very high probability

## Your Strong Chances at VJTI Mumbai

Similar to COEP Pune, VJTI Mumbai is another top-tier engineering institute in Maharashtra, and your MHT CET percentile makes you a strong contender here as well. VJTI also primarily admits students through the MHT CET state quota. With a 99.89 percentile, you have an excellent chance for popular branches. Your board percentage will not be an impediment.

### Why Strategic Counselling Matters

To maximise your chances for both COEP Pune and VJTI Mumbai, active and informed participation in the Centralized Admission Process (CAP) counselling rounds is critical. During CAP rounds, you will submit your preferences for colleges and branches. It's advisable to list your desired branches at COEP Pune and VJTI Mumbai at the very top of your preference list. Keep a close eye on the official counselling schedule and guidelines released by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra.

Your dedication to improving your MHT CET score has truly paid off, opening doors to some of the best engineering colleges in the state. Wishing you all the best for your admissions journey!
