# What is the closing JEE Advanced rank in IIT Madras?

Understanding the closing ranks for JEE Advanced at IIT Madras is crucial for any aspiring student. These ranks indicate the competitive scores needed to secure admission into various engineering programs at this prestigious institution.

## Key Takeaways

*   IIT Madras closing ranks vary significantly by engineering program and candidate category.
*   Computer Science and Engineering consistently has the lowest (most competitive) closing ranks.
*   General category ranks are generally much more competitive compared to reserved categories.
*   The official JoSAA portal is the primary source for the most accurate and detailed historical rank data.

## Understanding JEE Advanced Ranks for IIT Madras Admissions

Admission to the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), including IIT Madras, is solely based on the JEE Advanced examination ranks, following qualification in JEE Main. The closing rank for a particular program signifies the rank of the last candidate admitted to that program in a specific admission round. These figures are dynamic and can shift slightly each year based on factors like the number of applicants, the difficulty of the exam, and seat availability.

## Illustrative Closing Ranks for General Category (2025 Trends)

While exact figures can fluctuate, an analysis of recent trends (such as those observed in 2025) provides a good indication of the competitive landscape for general category candidates at IIT Madras. It's important to remember these are approximate and for guidance only. The official JoSAA portal publishes the definitive cut-offs each year.

Here are some approximate closing ranks for various B.Tech programs for the General Category:

*   **Computer Science and Engineering (CSE):** Around 171
*   **AI & Data Analytics:** Approximately 306
*   **Electrical Engineering:** Around 849
*   **Computational Engineering:** Approximately 1300
*   **Engineering Physics:** Around 1440
*   **Mechanical Engineering:** Approximately 2468
*   **Aerospace Engineering:** Around 3211
*   **Chemical Engineering:** Approximately 4116
*   **Civil Engineering:** Around 5639

As you can see, programs like Computer Science and Engineering are highly sought after, reflected in their very low closing ranks. Other branches like Civil or Chemical Engineering, while still competitive, have comparatively higher closing ranks.

## Impact of Category on Closing Ranks

The Indian government's reservation policies play a significant role in IIT admissions. Candidates belonging to reserved categories (SC, ST, OBC-NCL, EWS, and PwD) benefit from separate merit lists and consequently, their closing ranks are generally higher (less competitive) than those for the general category. This ensures equitable access to higher education for various sections of society. For precise category-wise data, always refer to the official JoSAA counselling brochures and seat matrix documents.

## Where to Find Official Rank Information

The most authoritative source for JEE Advanced closing ranks is the official JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority) portal. After each round of counselling, JoSAA releases detailed opening and closing ranks for all IITs, NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs. These documents provide comprehensive data across all programs and categories, which is essential for making informed decisions during the counselling process.

Navigating the JEE Advanced admission process requires careful research and strategic planning. By understanding these rank trends, you can better gauge your chances and make informed choices for your future at IIT Madras.
