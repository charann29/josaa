# My daughter got 3424 AIR in General Category and 970 AIR in OBC Category in JEE Mains. Is it possible to get admission in MNIT Jaipur for Architecture in 2025?

It's natural to wonder about admission possibilities after JEE Main results. Let's explore your daughter's chances for Architecture at MNIT Jaipur, considering her impressive ranks.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your daughter's OBC category rank of 970 significantly boosts her chances for Architecture at MNIT Jaipur.
*   Historical data suggests that an OBC rank around 1500-1600 has secured admission for Architecture at MNIT Jaipur in previous years.
*   Final admission hinges on the specific cutoffs for 2025, the total number of available seats, and the choices she makes during JoSAA counseling.
*   While her General category rank is good, her OBC category rank offers a much stronger advantage for this particular program.

## Understanding JEE Main Ranks for B.Arch

JEE Main Paper 2 is specifically for B.Arch (Bachelor of Architecture) and B.Planning programs. The All India Ranks (AIR) you've mentioned, 3424 in the General category and 970 in the OBC category, are crucial for admission to NITs like MNIT Jaipur. These ranks indicate her position among all candidates nationwide in their respective categories.

## MNIT Jaipur Architecture: OBC Category Advantage

Based on past admission trends, your daughter's OBC category rank of 970 for B.Arch at MNIT Jaipur is very promising. Historically, the closing ranks for the OBC category in the Architecture program at MNIT Jaipur have extended to approximately 1500 to 1600. Her rank of 970 falls comfortably within this range, placing her in a strong position.

It's important to remember that these are historical trends. Actual cutoffs can vary each year due to factors like the total number of applicants, the difficulty level of the exam, and changes in the seat matrix announced by the Ministry of Education.

## The Role of JoSAA Counseling

Admission to NITs, including MNIT Jaipur, is managed through the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) counseling process. This is where your daughter will register, fill in her preferred college and course choices, and participate in the seat allocation rounds. Her strategy in filling choices will be critical. She should list MNIT Jaipur Architecture as a high preference, especially considering her strong OBC rank.

Keep an eye on the official JoSAA portal for the 2025 counseling schedule, detailed information on seat matrix, and previous year's opening and closing ranks. This data, often available after the current year's counseling begins, provides the most accurate insights.

## Factors Influencing Final Admission

Several elements will determine the final outcome:

*   **Yearly Cutoffs:** These are dynamic and change annually based on the overall performance of candidates and the demand for specific programs.
*   **Seat Matrix:** The total number of seats available for Architecture at MNIT Jaipur, particularly for the OBC category, will directly impact admission chances.
*   **Choices Filled in JoSAA:** The order in which your daughter lists her preferences during counseling is extremely important. A well-thought-out preference list can optimize her chances of securing a seat.

In conclusion, with an OBC category rank of 970, your daughter has a very good and realistic chance of securing admission to the Architecture program at MNIT Jaipur for the 2025 academic year. Ensure she prepares diligently for the JoSAA counseling process.
