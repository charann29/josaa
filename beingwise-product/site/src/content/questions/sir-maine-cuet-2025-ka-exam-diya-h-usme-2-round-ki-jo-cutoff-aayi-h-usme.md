# CUET 2025 Counselling: Fee Payment Issues and Solutions

It can be incredibly stressful to see your name on the CUET merit list but face technical difficulties when trying to pay your fees. With deadlines approaching, it's crucial to act quickly and systematically. This guide will help you navigate such a situation effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Act Immediately:** Don't delay, especially if today is the last date for fee payment.
*   **Document Everything:** Keep screenshots and records of all attempts and communications.
*   **Contact Support:** Reach out to both the university and CUET/NTA support via multiple channels.
*   **Try Alternatives:** Explore different browsers, devices, and internet connections.

## Common Reasons for Payment Issues

Sometimes, technical glitches prevent the fee payment option from appearing. This could be due to a few reasons:

*   **Server Overload:** Many students trying to pay at once can slow down or crash the portal.
*   **Browser Compatibility:** Your current web browser might not be fully compatible with the payment gateway.
*   **Temporary Maintenance:** The portal might be undergoing a brief update or maintenance.
*   **Payment Gateway Issues:** The integrated payment system itself might be experiencing a temporary problem.

## Immediate Steps to Take

Given that today is the last date, swift action is essential. Follow these steps without delay:

1.  **Try Different Browsers and Devices:** If you're using Chrome, try Firefox, Edge, or Safari. If you're on a laptop, try a desktop computer or even a different mobile device. Sometimes, clearing your browser's cache and cookies can also help.
2.  **Check Internet Connection:** Ensure you have a stable and strong internet connection. A fluctuating connection can interrupt the loading of payment options.
3.  **Verify Portal Status:** While less common during active counselling, quickly check if the official CUET or university admission portal has any announcements regarding maintenance or technical issues.

## Contacting Support Channels

This is perhaps the most critical step. You need to inform the authorities about your predicament immediately.

*   **University Helpline/Admissions Office:** Your chosen university will have a dedicated helpline number and an admissions email address. Call them repeatedly if needed, and also send a detailed email explaining the issue. Include your application number, CUET roll number, and a clear description of the problem.
*   **CUET/NTA Support:** The National Testing Agency (NTA), which conducts CUET, also has a helpdesk. Look for their official contact details on the NTA website or the CUET (UG) portal. Reach out to them via phone and email as well.

## Documenting Your Efforts

Proof of your attempts to pay and contact support is vital, especially if you miss the deadline due to a technical error. Make sure to:

*   **Take Screenshots:** Capture screenshots of the counselling portal where the payment option is missing. Include the date and time in the screenshot if possible. This serves as undeniable evidence.
*   **Record Call Details:** Note down the date, time, and name of the person you spoke to (if provided) during phone calls to the helpline.
*   **Save Email Correspondence:** Keep all sent emails to the university and CUET support as proof of your communication.

By following these steps, you demonstrate proactive engagement and create a strong case for yourself should the deadline pass without resolution. Remember, staying calm and methodical will help you navigate this challenge effectively.
