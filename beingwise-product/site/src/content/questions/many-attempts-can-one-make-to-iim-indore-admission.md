# How Many Attempts Can One Make for IIM Indore Admission?

Many aspiring students wonder about the number of chances they have to gain admission into the prestigious Indian Institute of Management Indore. The good news is that for both their flagship management programs, there isn't a strict limit on how many times you can apply, provided you meet the essential eligibility criteria.

## Key Takeaways

*   There is no official cap on the number of attempts for the CAT exam, which is crucial for MBA (PGP) admission.
*   Similarly, there's no fixed limit on how many times you can take the IPMAT for the Integrated Program in Management (IPM).
*   The primary restrictions are related to age and academic qualifications, not the number of attempts.
*   As long as you fulfill the program-specific eligibility, you can reapply.

## Understanding the MBA (PGP) Application Process

For those aiming for the Master of Business Administration (MBA), also known as the Post Graduate Program (PGP) at IIM Indore, the Common Admission Test (CAT) is the gateway. The official body conducting the CAT exam does not impose any limit on how many times a candidate can appear for it. This means you can take the CAT multiple times in different years to try and achieve your desired score.

The main requirement here is to satisfy the academic eligibility criteria, which typically involves holding a bachelor's degree with a minimum percentage of marks. As long as you meet these foundational requirements, you are welcome to attempt the CAT exam and apply to IIM Indore's PGP as many times as you wish.

## Navigating the IPM Application Process

The Integrated Program in Management (IPM) at IIM Indore is a unique five-year program for students after Class 12. Admission to this program is through the IPM Aptitude Test (IPMAT). Similar to the CAT, there isn't an official limit on the number of times you can attempt the IPMAT.

However, the IPM program does have a crucial age restriction. For candidates in the General, Other Backward Classes (OBC), and Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) categories, the upper age limit is 20 years as of July 31st of the admission year. For candidates belonging to Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST), and Persons with Disabilities (PwD) categories, there is a relaxation of five years, extending the limit to 25 years as of July 31st of the admission year.

Therefore, while you can take the IPMAT multiple times, your eligibility to apply will cease once you exceed the specified age limit for your category.

## Focus on Eligibility, Not Attempts

The core message from IIM Indore regarding admissions for both the PGP and IPM programs is clear: the focus is on meeting the set eligibility criteria rather than limiting the number of attempts. This approach offers aspiring students multiple opportunities to prepare thoroughly, improve their scores, and strengthen their applications.

It's important to regularly check the official IIM Indore admissions portal for the most current and detailed eligibility requirements for both the PGP and IPM programs, as these can be subject to minor changes from year to year.

In essence, if you meet the age and academic qualifications, you are free to pursue your dream of studying at IIM Indore as many times as it takes.
