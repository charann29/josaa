# What is the Criteria to Get Admission in IIT BHU?

Aspiring to study at IIT (BHU) Varanasi is a fantastic goal, known for its rich history and strong academic programs. Gaining admission requires successfully navigating a competitive process primarily centered around the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Advanced.

## Key Takeaways

*   Admission to undergraduate programs at IIT (BHU) Varanasi is primarily through JEE Advanced.
*   Candidates must meet specific academic eligibility criteria for their 10+2 examinations.
*   The Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) manages the counseling and seat allocation process.
*   Admissions for postgraduate and doctoral programs follow different selection procedures, often involving entrance exams and interviews.

## Undergraduate B.Tech Admissions: The JEE Advanced Path

The main gateway for Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) programs at IIT (BHU) Varanasi is the JEE Advanced examination. This highly competitive national-level test assesses a candidate's aptitude in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

### JEE Main Qualification

Before you can even appear for JEE Advanced, you must first qualify in the JEE Main examination. The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducts JEE Main, which serves as a screening test. Only the top candidates from JEE Main are eligible to register for JEE Advanced.

### JEE Advanced Performance

Your rank in JEE Advanced is crucial. Admission to specific B.Tech branches at IIT (BHU) Varanasi depends on your performance in this exam, combined with the availability of seats and the choices you make during counseling. Each program will have a cut-off rank, which varies year to year based on various factors like the number of applicants and their scores.

## Academic Eligibility for 10+2

Beyond the entrance exams, there are specific requirements for your 10+2 (or equivalent) qualifying examination. To be eligible for admission to IIT (BHU) Varanasi, candidates must have:

*   Passed their 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
*   Secured at least 75% aggregate marks in the 10+2 examination. For candidates belonging to SC, ST, and PwD categories, the minimum aggregate marks required are 65%.
*   Alternatively, candidates can meet the criteria by being in the top 20 percentile of successful candidates in their respective qualifying examination boards. This flexibility ensures that students from various board examination grading systems can still be considered.

It's important to check the official JoSAA portal and the JEE Advanced information brochure for the most up-to-date and detailed eligibility criteria each year.

## The Counseling and Seat Allocation Process (JoSAA)

Once JEE Advanced results are declared, the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) takes over. JoSAA is responsible for managing the joint seat allocation process for admissions to all IITs, NITs, IIITs, and other GFTIs. During this process, you will register, fill in your choices of institutes and courses based on your JEE Advanced rank, and participate in multiple rounds of counseling. Seats are allocated based on your rank, preferences, and category.

## Other Programs: M.Tech, Ph.D., and More

While B.Tech admissions are primarily through JEE Advanced, IIT (BHU) Varanasi offers a range of other programs, each with its own specific admission criteria:

*   **M.Tech Programs:** Admission usually requires a valid Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) score, followed by an interview or a written test conducted by the department.
*   **Ph.D. Programs:** Candidates typically need a master's degree in a relevant discipline, often with a valid GATE/NET score, and must clear a departmental written test and/or interview.
*   **Integrated Dual Degree (IDD) Programs:** These programs combine B.Tech and M.Tech degrees and typically follow the JEE Advanced admission route for the undergraduate component.

Always refer to the official admission brochures published by IIT (BHU) Varanasi for the specific program you are interested in, as criteria can vary.

Embarking on your journey to IIT (BHU) Varanasi requires dedication, hard work, and a clear understanding of the admission process.
