# I got 15112 rank bce category in tgeapcet. I got cse in mgit in 1st phase. Can i get cse/csm/csd in G Narayanamma in 2nd phase?

It's understandable to wonder about your chances for a preferred institute like G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology & Science (GNITS) after securing a seat in the first phase of TS EAPCET counselling. Let's explore what your rank of 15112 in the BCE category might mean for your aspirations.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your TS EAPCET BCE rank of 15112 is a good achievement, securing you a CSE seat in MGIT in Phase 1.
*   GNITS is a highly sought-after institution, especially for Computer Science-related branches.
*   Securing core CSE at GNITS with your rank might be very challenging due to high demand and past cutoff trends.
*   You might have a slim chance for branches like Computer Science and Machine Learning (CSM) or Computer Science and Data Science (CSD) if specific circumstances align in Phase 2.

## Understanding GNITS Demand and Cutoffs

G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology & Science (GNITS) consistently ranks among the top choices for engineering aspirants in Telangana, particularly for its Computer Science Engineering (CSE) program and its related specializations like CSM and CSD. This high demand translates into very competitive cutoff ranks during the TS EAPCET counselling process.

Historically, admission to CSE, CSM, and CSD at GNITS typically closes at significantly higher ranks than your current 15112 in the BCE category. For these popular branches, closing ranks often fall within the range of 6,000 to 12,000, depending on the specific branch and the category of the applicant. This indicates that students with substantially better ranks usually fill these seats.

## Your Chances for CSE at GNITS

Given the historical trends and the intense competition for CSE at GNITS, securing a core CSE seat with a BCE category rank of 15112 in the second phase of counselling would be quite difficult. CSE is the most preferred branch, and its cutoff ranks are usually the lowest (meaning the highest competition) compared to other branches.

While counselling involves many variables, and some movement can occur between phases, a significant jump in rank for core CSE at a top college like GNITS is uncommon. It's important to have realistic expectations based on past admission data.

## Exploring CSM and CSD Options

Your chances for Computer Science and Machine Learning (CSM) or Computer Science and Data Science (CSD) at GNITS might be slightly better than for core CSE, but still remain low. These specializations are also very popular and have competitive cutoffs, though sometimes they might be marginally higher than core CSE's.

If there is any possibility of securing a seat in CSM or CSD in the second phase, it would likely depend on a few factors:

*   **Vacant Seats:** A sufficient number of students who were allotted seats in CSM or CSD in Phase 1 might surrender them or opt for other colleges/branches, creating vacancies.
*   **Cutoff Fluctuations:** While unlikely to be drastic, there could be slight upward movements in cutoff ranks for these branches in Phase 2, potentially making them accessible to a slightly wider rank range.

However, it's crucial to understand that even with these possibilities, the probability remains low for a rank of 15112. You should definitely include these options in your web counselling choices for Phase 2, but it is wise to also consider backup plans.

## Strategic Counselling for Phase 2

Since you already have a CSE seat in MGIT, you are in a relatively comfortable position. When participating in the second phase of TS EAPCET web counselling, you can exercise your options strategically:

1.  **Prioritize GNITS:** List CSE, CSM, and CSD at GNITS at the top of your preference list if they are your absolute priority.
2.  **Include Backup Options:** Ensure you also list other good colleges and branches where you have a strong chance of admission, including your current MGIT CSE seat, to avoid losing out on a good opportunity if GNITS doesn't work out.
3.  **Stay Informed:** Keep a close watch on the official TS EAPCET portal for any announcements regarding seat matrix updates or changes in counselling procedures.

While securing a seat in CSE, CSM, or CSD at GNITS with your rank is a long shot, it doesn't hurt to try in the second phase while keeping your current MGIT CSE seat as a solid backup.
