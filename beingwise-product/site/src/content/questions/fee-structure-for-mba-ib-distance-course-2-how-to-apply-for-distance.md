This page provides essential information about the fee structure and application process for the MBA in International Business (IB) distance learning program at the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT). Understanding these details is crucial for prospective students planning their higher education.

## Key Takeaways

*   The total fee for IIFT's MBA IB distance program is ₹10,50,000, including an alumni fee.
*   This fee can be conveniently paid in six installments.
*   Significant tuition fee concessions are available for SC, ST, and PWD candidates.
*   Applications for the 2025-27 batch are currently closed, with the next opportunity likely in the upcoming academic cycle.

## Understanding the Fee Structure for MBA IB Distance Learning at IIFT

When considering a distance learning MBA IB, it's important to look at the overall cost. While general distance MBA IB programs in India might have fees ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹2,00,000, the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT) offers a distinct program with a specific fee structure.

The total fee for the MBA IB distance course at IIFT is ₹10,50,000. This comprehensive amount includes a ₹10,000 alumni fee, which helps connect you with the institute's extensive network of former students.

## Payment Options and Concessions

IIFT understands the need for flexible payment options. To make the program more accessible, the total fee can be paid in six convenient installments. This allows students to manage their finances more effectively over the course of their study.

Furthermore, IIFT is committed to supporting students from diverse backgrounds. Candidates belonging to the Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), and Persons with Disabilities (PWD) categories are eligible for a substantial 50% concession on the tuition fee component of the program. This initiative aims to promote inclusivity and provide opportunities for a wider range of students.

## How to Apply for IIFT's MBA IB Distance Course

Applying for the MBA IB distance learning program at IIFT involves a specific application window. It's crucial to stay informed about these dates to avoid missing out on the opportunity.

For instance, the application period for the 2025-27 batch has already concluded, with the window closing in January. If you are interested in this program, you should plan to apply in the next academic year. Keep an eye on the official IIFT website for announcements regarding the application timeline for future batches. Typically, the application process will involve submitting an online form, along with any required documents and application fees, as specified by the institute.

Embarking on a distance MBA IB journey with IIFT can be a rewarding experience, offering specialized knowledge in international business and enhancing your career prospects.
