# CAT 2025 Payment Showing Failed Despite Successful Debit? Here's What To Do

It can be quite concerning when you've successfully paid for your CAT 2025 application, the money has left your account, but the official portal still shows the payment as failed. This situation, while frustrating, is not uncommon and usually points to a technical delay.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Don't Panic:** System updates can sometimes lag, leading to temporary discrepancies.
*   **Gather Proof:** Keep all evidence of your successful payment readily available.
*   **Reach Out Again:** Follow up with the CAT helpdesk, providing clear details.
*   **Consider Escalation:** If initial attempts don't yield results, there are further steps you can take.

## Understanding the Delay

When your bank statement confirms a debit to the IIM Kozhikode ICICI Bank account, but the CAT application portal shows the transaction as 'failed,' it most often indicates a synchronization delay between the banking system and the CAT registration portal. These systems aren't always instantly updated, and it can take more than 48 hours for the status to reflect correctly. This doesn't necessarily mean your payment hasn't gone through; it just means the portal hasn't registered it yet.

## Essential Steps to Take

### 1. Secure Your Payment Proof

This is perhaps the most crucial step. Immediately gather and save all documentation related to your payment. This includes:

*   Your bank statement showing the successful debit.
*   The transaction ID or reference number provided by your bank.
*   Any digital receipts or confirmation messages you received from your bank or payment gateway.

Having these details will be vital when communicating with the helpdesk or IIM Kozhikode.

### 2. Contact the CAT Helpdesk Again

Since you've already reached out, it's time for a follow-up. When you contact the CAT helpdesk this time, provide them with comprehensive information:

*   Your CAT application number.
*   The exact date and time of the transaction.
*   The transaction ID from your bank.
*   The amount debited.
*   A clear description of the issue (payment successful, portal failed).
*   Attach screenshots of your bank statement showing the debit, if possible.

Keep a record of when and how you contacted them, along with any ticket numbers or reference IDs they provide. Also, diligently check your registered email address, including spam folders, for any automated responses or updates from the helpdesk.

### 3. Consider Escalating the Issue

If you've waited a reasonable time (a few more business days) after your second contact with the helpdesk and still haven't received a satisfactory response or resolution, it's appropriate to escalate. You can do this by directly contacting the admissions office of IIM Kozhikode, which is the conducting IIM for CAT 2025. Look for official contact details (phone numbers or email addresses) on the official CAT website or IIM Kozhikode's official website. When you contact them, be sure to provide all the details and proofs you've gathered, explaining the timeline of your attempts to resolve the issue with the helpdesk.

By following these steps, you can systematically address the payment discrepancy and ensure your CAT 2025 application is processed smoothly.
