It's completely normal to have questions when filling out university application forms, especially when it comes to academic details. Let's clarify how to accurately enter your graduation scores for your Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI) application.

## Key Takeaways

*   Always provide your *aggregate* score from all semesters, not just the final one.
*   Look for a grand total or overall CGPA on your Mumbai University marksheet.
*   Convert CGPA to a percentage using your university's official conversion formula if required.
*   Accuracy in academic details is crucial for successful application processing.

## Understanding JMI's Score Requirement

When applying for postgraduate programs at Jamia Millia Islamia, the university, like most others, requires a comprehensive overview of your academic performance throughout your bachelor's degree. This means they are interested in your *overall* achievement, not just the results from your final semester. Therefore, you should always enter the cumulative percentage or CGPA that reflects your performance across all six semesters of your Psychology degree from Mumbai University.

## Finding Your Aggregate Score

Your Mumbai University marksheet should typically display a grand total or a final Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) for your entire degree. This is the figure you need to locate and use for your JMI application. Avoid calculating it yourself by averaging individual semester scores unless explicitly instructed, as universities often have specific weighting or calculation methods for the final aggregate.

## CGPA to Percentage Conversion

If your Mumbai University marksheet provides your final result in CGPA, and the JMI application portal asks for a percentage, it's essential to convert it correctly. Do *not* use a generic conversion formula. Instead, refer to the official conversion formula provided by Mumbai University. This formula is often printed on your marksheet, degree certificate, or available on the university's official website. Using the correct, official conversion ensures that your academic details are accurately represented to JMI.

## Why Overall Scores Matter

Universities like Jamia Millia Islamia use the aggregate score to verify that applicants meet the overall eligibility criteria for their chosen course. This cumulative score provides a consistent and standardized way to assess an applicant's academic standing over the entire duration of their undergraduate study, ensuring a fair evaluation process for all candidates.

Ensuring all details, especially your academic scores, are accurate and complete is a vital step in your university application journey. Good luck with your application to Jamia Millia Islamia!
