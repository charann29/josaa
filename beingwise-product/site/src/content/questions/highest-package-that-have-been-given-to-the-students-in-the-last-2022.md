# What is the Highest Salary Package at AUCEW?

For students considering their higher education options, understanding potential career outcomes is a key factor. Let's delve into the top salary offered at Andhra University College of Engineering for Women (AUCEW) in the recent past.

## Key Takeaways

*   The top salary package secured by an AUCEW student in the last year was ₹14 Lakhs per annum.
*   This notable offer came from Infosys, a leading IT services and consulting company.
*   Approximately 40% of the students from AUCEW found placements in various companies during the recent placement cycle.
*   AUCEW is a government-run constituent college of Andhra University, exclusively for women.

## Understanding AUCEW Placements

Andhra University College of Engineering for Women (AUCEW) maintains an active placement cell dedicated to connecting its students with employment opportunities. While the overall placement rate is considered moderate, the college strives to provide valuable career pathways for its graduates. The most recent data indicates that the highest package offered to a student was ₹14 Lakhs per annum.

## The Top Offer: ₹14 Lakhs from Infosys

The most impressive salary package recently secured by an AUCEW student stood at ₹14 Lakhs per annum. This offer was extended by Infosys, a globally recognized company known for its significant presence in the technology sector. Such a package highlights the potential for graduates from AUCEW to secure competitive positions in the industry.

## Overall Placement Scenario

Looking at the broader picture, AUCEW reported an average placement rate of around 62%. This indicates that more than half of the graduating students typically find employment through the college's placement efforts. Specifically, the B.Tech Computer Science and System Engineering program showed the highest placement rate, reaching approximately 77%. In contrast, the B.Tech Mechanical Engineering program had the lowest placement percentage, at around 46%.

## Academic Programs and Admissions

AUCEW offers five undergraduate (UG) engineering programs. Admission to these programs is determined based on the scores obtained in the AP EAMCET entrance examination. As a constituent college of Andhra University, Visakhapatnam, AUCEW operates under a government framework, providing a structured educational environment for its female students.

Understanding these figures can help prospective students and their families make informed decisions about their engineering education at AUCEW.
