# I have got 11009 rank in TG EAPCET 2025: Is there any chance of getting Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in Keshav Memorial Institute of Technology, Narayanguda?

Securing a specific branch like Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML) at a popular institution like Keshav Memorial Institute of Technology (KMIT), Narayanguda, with a TG EAPCET 2025 rank of 11,009 requires careful consideration of various factors.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your rank of 11,009 makes direct admission to AI & ML at KMIT challenging under the general category, based on past trends.
*   Reserved categories (SC, ST, BC) offer better prospects, as their cutoffs are typically different.
*   Exploring related branches or considering other colleges could significantly improve your chances.
*   Participating in all counseling rounds and considering the management quota are also options.

## Understanding Previous Year Trends for KMIT AI & ML

Based on historical data for KMIT, Narayanguda, the closing ranks for the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning branch have generally been lower than 11,009 for the general (Open Category) quota. In previous years, these ranks often closed around the 6,000 mark. While specific cutoffs can vary each year, this indicates that securing a seat in AI & ML at KMIT with your current rank under the general category might be difficult.

However, it's important to note that cutoffs are dynamic and depend on factors like the total number of applicants, seat availability, and the preferences of higher-ranked students.

## The Role of Reserved Categories

If you belong to a reserved category such as Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), or Backward Class (BC), your admission prospects can significantly improve. Each reserved category has its own set of cutoffs, which are often different from the general category. For example, some BC categories might have closing ranks closer to or even above 10,000 in certain years, offering a potential pathway for your rank.

It is highly recommended to check the official TG EAPCET counseling information specific to your category to understand the exact cutoff trends and possibilities.

## Exploring Alternative Branches and Colleges

If AI & ML at KMIT proves challenging, consider broadening your options:

*   **Related Branches at KMIT:** Look into other sought-after branches at KMIT like Information Technology (IT) or Data Science. These branches are closely related to AI & ML and sometimes have slightly higher closing ranks, which might align better with your rank.
*   **Other Engineering Colleges in Telangana:** Telangana has numerous reputable private engineering colleges that offer AI & ML. Many of these institutions might have higher cutoff ranges compared to KMIT, making admission more feasible with your rank. Researching these colleges and their past cutoff data will be beneficial.

## The Counseling Process and Management Quota

Your active participation in the TG EAPCET counseling process is crucial. Make sure to:

*   **Attend All Rounds:** Don't miss any counseling rounds, including subsequent phases and spot rounds. Seat availability can change, and cutoffs might shift as counseling progresses.
*   **Fill Multiple Options:** When filling your choices, include a wide range of colleges and branches. Prioritize KMIT and AI & ML, but also list other preferred branches at KMIT and AI & ML at other suitable colleges where your rank has a better chance.

**Management Quota:** Another avenue to consider is the management quota. Colleges reserve a certain percentage of seats under this quota, which are typically filled based on direct applications and often involve higher fee structures. If conventional admission through counseling doesn't work out, this could be an alternative, though it comes with a different financial commitment.
