Welcome to BeingWise! If you're a PCB student planning to take the MHT-CET only in its second session (typically in May), understanding the correct option to select during the application process is crucial. Let's clarify how to navigate this to ensure you're registered for the right exam.

## Key Takeaways

*   If you intend to appear only in the second session (May) of MHT-CET, you should typically select "First Attempt" during the application, assuming the system treats each session chronologically.
*   Ensure you specifically choose the PCB group for your examination.
*   The application fee for the PCB group (General Category) for a single attempt is generally around INR 1300.
*   Selecting "First Attempt" for the May session means you will not be registered for the earlier (April) session.

## Understanding MHT-CET Sessions

The MHT-CET is often conducted in multiple sessions over a period, giving students flexibility. For PCB students, these sessions typically occur in two main windows, with the second session usually taking place in May. It's important to remember that even if you're only appearing in the second window, for registration purposes, you might still select an option that implies it's your first *attempt* within that particular application cycle, especially if the system doesn't offer a direct "Session 2 only" choice.

## Choosing the Right Option for Session 2

When filling out the MHT-CET application form, if you only wish to appear for the second session (May), look for options that allow you to specify this. Often, the system might present choices like "First Attempt" or "Both Attempts." If you select "First Attempt" and the subsequent options allow you to pick your preferred session month (e.g., May), then this is the correct path. If the form explicitly asks "Which session do you wish to appear for?" and lists April and May, you would simply select May.

*   **If the form asks "First Attempt" or "Both Attempts":** Choose "First Attempt." This will register you for a single session, and you'll then specify or be automatically assigned to the later session if that's how the system is configured for single-session registrations.
*   **If the form asks to select specific months:** Choose "May" (or whichever month the second session is scheduled for).

Always refer to the official MHT-CET information brochure for the most precise instructions regarding session selection for the current year.

## Selecting Your Group and Fees

As a PCB student, it's vital to correctly select the PCB group during your application. This ensures you receive the correct question paper focusing on Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. The application fee varies by category and the number of attempts. For a single attempt in the PCB group, the fee for the General Category is typically around INR 1300. Make sure to double-check the exact fee structure on the official MHT-CET portal for the current academic year.

## What Happens After Selection?

Once you select "First Attempt" (for the May session) and complete your payment, your registration will be solely for that specific session. You will not be issued an admit card or be eligible to appear for the earlier (April) session. This streamlines the process and ensures you're only registered for the exam you intend to take.

Carefully reviewing the official MHT-CET instructions during your application is always recommended to avoid any misunderstandings.
