# Hindustan University Chennai: Boys' Hostel Fees and Facilities

Considering higher education often involves thinking about where you'll live. For students eyeing Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science (HITS), also known as Hindustan University, understanding the accommodation options and costs is a key part of planning.

## Key Takeaways

*   Hindustan University offers on-campus hostel facilities for male students.
*   The approximate annual fee for boys' accommodation is around ₹65,000.
*   Hostels are designed to be centers for academic and social life within the campus.
*   These facilities cater to both undergraduate and postgraduate male students.

## On-Campus Accommodation at HITS

Hindustan University provides dedicated residential facilities directly on its campus. These hostels are designed to offer a supportive environment that complements the academic journey. They serve as more than just a place to sleep; they are hubs for students to interact, study together, and participate in various campus activities.

## Boys' Hostel Fees

For male students, the approximate annual fee for staying in the on-campus hostel at Hindustan University is around ₹65,000. It's important for prospective students and their families to factor this cost into their overall budget planning for their education.

## Capacity and Student Intake

The university's residential facilities for male students can accommodate approximately 200 undergraduate and postgraduate students. This capacity ensures that a significant number of male students have the option to live on campus, fostering a close-knit community and easy access to university resources.

## Life in the Hostel

Living in the hostel at Hindustan University places students at the heart of campus life. It's an environment where academic discussions often extend beyond the classroom, and social bonds are formed through shared experiences. The hostels aim to provide a comfortable and conducive setting for students to focus on their studies while also enjoying a vibrant student life.

We hope this information assists you in making informed decisions about your stay at Hindustan University.
