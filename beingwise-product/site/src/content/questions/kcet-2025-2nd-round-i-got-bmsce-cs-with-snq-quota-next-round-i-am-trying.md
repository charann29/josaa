## Understanding SNQ Quota in KCET 2025: Your Next Steps

It's great that you've secured a seat at BMSCE CS under the SNQ quota in the second round of KCET 2025. As you consider upgrading to PES RR CS (AI/ML) in the subsequent round, understanding how your SNQ quota applies is crucial.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your SNQ (Supernumerary Quota) status, once granted, generally carries forward through all subsequent rounds of KCET counselling.
*   If you are allotted a new seat in a later round, such as PES RR CS (AI/ML), it will also be considered under your existing SNQ quota.
*   You do not need to reapply or re-qualify for the SNQ quota in each round; its applicability is continuous.
*   Always verify the specific rules for seat upgradation and quota application as outlined in the official KCET information bulletin for 2025.

## How SNQ Quota Works Across Rounds

The Supernumerary Quota (SNQ) is a special provision designed to support students from economically weaker sections, offering a fee concession. Once you've been deemed eligible for and allotted a seat under the SNQ quota in an earlier round of KCET counselling, this status typically remains active for all subsequent rounds within the same counselling process. This means that if you participate in further rounds and are offered an upgraded seat, that new seat will also automatically fall under your existing SNQ eligibility.

## Upgrading Your Seat with SNQ

When you opt for an upgrade in a later round, the system generally recognizes your existing SNQ status. So, if you manage to secure a seat in PES RR CS (AI/ML) in the next round, that allotment will be made with the SNQ quota applied. This is a significant advantage, as it ensures the financial benefits associated with SNQ continue to apply to your new, preferred institution and branch, provided you meet all other eligibility criteria for the upgrade.

## Important Considerations for KCET 2025

While the general rule is that SNQ status is retained, it's always wise to consult the official KCET 2025 information bulletin published by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA). This document is the definitive source for all rules regarding counselling, seat allocation, quota application, and upgradation procedures. Pay close attention to sections detailing the process for seat surrender, upgradation, and how quotas are handled across different rounds to ensure there are no unforeseen changes or specific conditions for the 2025 academic year.

## Next Steps: Confirming Your Upgrade

Once the next round of counselling results are out, and if you are indeed allotted PES RR CS (AI/ML), ensure you follow all the prescribed steps for confirming your upgraded seat. This usually involves reporting to the allotted college, completing admission formalities, and paying the revised fees (if any, after SNQ benefits) within the stipulated deadlines. Missing any deadline could lead to forfeiture of your seat.
