# XAT 75th Percentile: Your Chances for HRM at XISS Ranchi (General, Non-Engineer Female)

Scoring 75th percentile in XAT is a good achievement, and many students wonder what this means for their admission prospects at various institutions. Let's explore your chances for the PGDM-HRM program at XISS Ranchi as a general, non-engineering female candidate.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your XAT 75th percentile score is well above the general category cutoff for XISS Ranchi's PGDM-HRM program.
*   Admission involves more than just XAT scores, including academic record, work experience, and performance in subsequent selection rounds.
*   XISS Ranchi has a strong reputation for its HRM program, attracting diverse candidates.
*   Being a non-engineer female can sometimes be an advantage in creating a diverse cohort.

## Understanding XISS Ranchi's Cutoff

According to the official admission information released by XISS Ranchi, the typical XAT cutoff for the Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) in Human Resource Management (HRM) for the unreserved (general) category is around the 60th percentile. With a 75th percentile, your score comfortably exceeds this threshold, indicating that you meet the initial academic requirement for consideration.

## Beyond the XAT Score: A Holistic View

While your XAT score is a crucial first step, it's important to remember that it's just one component of the overall admission process. XISS Ranchi, like many other reputable institutions, employs a holistic evaluation approach. This means they look at a range of factors to assess a candidate's suitability for their programs. These often include:

*   **Academic Performance:** Your undergraduate and, if applicable, postgraduate academic records are thoroughly reviewed.
*   **Work Experience:** Any relevant professional experience can significantly strengthen your application.
*   **Diversity Factors:** Institutions often aim for a diverse student body, considering factors like academic background, gender, and regional representation.
*   **Performance in Selection Rounds:** Shortlisted candidates typically undergo a Group Discussion (GD) and/or Personal Interview (PI) round, which are critical for final selection.

Your profile as a non-engineering female candidate could potentially contribute to the diversity of the incoming batch, which some institutions factor into their selection process.

## The PGDM-HRM Program at XISS Ranchi

Xavier Institute of Social Service (XISS) Ranchi is particularly well-regarded for its Human Resource Management program. It has a long-standing history of producing skilled HR professionals. The curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of HR principles and practices, preparing students for various roles in the corporate world. The institute's focus on practical exposure and industry relevance makes it a popular choice for aspiring HR managers. For the most precise details regarding the program structure, fees, and placement statistics, always refer to the official XISS Ranchi website.

## Next Steps in the Application Process

Given your strong XAT percentile, your next crucial step is to pay close attention to the application deadlines and procedures outlined on the official XISS Ranchi website. Ensure all required documents are submitted accurately and on time. If you are shortlisted, begin preparing diligently for the subsequent selection rounds, which will likely involve a Group Discussion and a Personal Interview. These rounds are your opportunity to demonstrate your communication skills, leadership potential, and genuine interest in the HRM field.

With your XAT score, you have a solid foundation; now, focus on presenting a well-rounded profile in every aspect of your application.
