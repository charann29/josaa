# If I gave 12th this year and scored 70% and took a drop for giving jee next year and cbse improvement exam next year through cbse private candidate....if I am able to improve by score by 90%...will this improved 12th marks be considered in Application rating of top IIM A,B,C???

Many students consider taking a gap year to enhance their academic profile, whether it's for JEE preparation or improving Class 12 scores. This strategy can indeed be beneficial, especially when aiming for top management institutes.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your most recent and improved Class 12 marks will be considered by IIMs.
*   Taking a CBSE improvement exam as a private candidate is a valid way to update your scores.
*   A significant improvement from 70% to 90% can positively impact your IIM application rating.
*   Always ensure you follow official CBSE procedures for improvement exams and retain all valid documents.

## Understanding the CBSE Improvement Exam

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) offers an improvement examination for students who wish to better their Class 12 scores. You can appear for this exam as a private candidate, meaning you are not enrolled in a regular school for that academic year. If you successfully improve your marks, CBSE issues a new marksheet reflecting these higher scores. This new marksheet then becomes your official and valid Class 12 result.

## How IIMs Evaluate Academic Scores

When you apply to premier management institutes like IIM Ahmedabad, Bangalore, or Calcutta, your past academic performance plays a crucial role in the initial shortlisting process, often referred to as the Application Rating (AR) or Academic Profile score. These institutes typically consider your final, official Class 10 and Class 12 scores, along with your undergraduate degree performance.

If you take a CBSE improvement exam and achieve a higher percentage, the IIMs will consider this updated score. They are interested in your best academic achievement at the Class 12 level. Therefore, if you improve your score from 70% to 90%, it is the 90% that will be factored into your application rating.

## The Impact of Improved Scores on Your Application

An improvement from 70% to 90% in your Class 12 board exams is a substantial jump and will undoubtedly strengthen your academic profile. Top IIMs have high academic cut-offs and a strong emphasis on consistent performance. A higher Class 12 percentage not only boosts your raw academic score in their evaluation matrix but also demonstrates your commitment to academic excellence and your ability to improve. This can be a significant advantage in a highly competitive application pool.

## Documentation and Official Procedures

It is critical to ensure that you follow all official CBSE guidelines when appearing for the improvement exam as a private candidate. Keep all your marksheets, admit cards, and other relevant documents safe. When you apply to IIMs, you will need to upload or submit your latest and most accurate Class 12 marksheet. Always refer to the official CBSE website for the most up-to-date information on improvement exam procedures and timelines.

By carefully planning your gap year and successfully improving your Class 12 scores, you can significantly enhance your chances of admission to top-tier IIMs.
