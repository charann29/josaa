# I am from NC OBC Category with 85.77%ile with a 9/9/8 profile I've completed my graduation in Horticulture stream. Do i have any chance of getting calls from IIMA FABM or IIML ABM

Navigating the admissions landscape for top-tier IIM programs can be intricate, especially for specialized courses like Agri-Business Management. With your NC-OBC category, 85.77 percentile in CAT, and a strong academic background in Horticulture, let's explore your prospects for IIM Ahmedabad's FABM and IIM Lucknow's ABM programs.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your 85.77 CAT percentile makes an IIM Ahmedabad FABM call unlikely, even with your strong academics and NC-OBC status.
*   IIM Lucknow's ABM program presents a more realistic opportunity for you, given your horticulture background and academic profile.
*   Meeting sectional CAT cutoffs is crucial for any IIM shortlist, regardless of your overall percentile.
*   If shortlisted, your performance in the Personal Interview (PI) and your demonstrated understanding of the agri-business sector will be key to converting the call.

## Understanding IIM Ahmedabad FABM Admissions

IIM Ahmedabad's Faculty of Agri-Business Management (FABM) is highly competitive, known for its rigorous selection process. Even for candidates from reserved categories like NC-OBC, the CAT percentile requirements are typically very high. In recent admission cycles, successful NC-OBC applicants for FABM often had CAT percentiles in the range of 88 to 92 and above, alongside strong scores in individual sections.

While your academic record (9/9/8) and relevant horticulture degree are definite advantages, a CAT percentile of 85.77 is generally considered to be on the lower side for IIM Ahmedabad's FABM shortlisting. Therefore, securing an interview call from IIMA FABM at this percentile is improbable, unless there are exceptionally unusual circumstances such as a significant drop in cutoffs or a highly favorable normalization of scores.

## Your Prospects for IIM Lucknow ABM

In contrast, IIM Lucknow's Agri-Business Management (ABM) program offers a more achievable pathway for a profile like yours. IIM Lucknow places considerable emphasis on consistent academic performance, a relevant agricultural background, and category reservations. Historically, ABM shortlists for NC-OBC candidates have seen slightly lower CAT cutoffs compared to the institute's flagship PGP program, particularly for those with degrees in agriculture, horticulture, forestry, and related fields.

With your 85.77 percentile, an excellent 9/9/8 academic profile, and a core degree in Horticulture, you have a reasonable chance of receiving an interview call from IIM Lucknow ABM. The crucial condition, of course, is that you also meet the specific sectional cutoffs mandated by the institute.

## The Role of Personal Interview and Domain Knowledge

Should you receive a shortlist for IIM Lucknow ABM, your final selection will depend significantly on your performance in the Personal Interview (PI). The admissions committee will assess your academic relevance, any work experience you might have, and, most importantly, the clarity of your career goals within the agri-business domain. Candidates who can articulate a strong understanding of the sector and demonstrate how their background aligns with the program's objectives often succeed in converting their ABM calls, even if their CAT percentile was relatively lower than those admitted to general MBA programs.

## Expanding Your Options

While IIM Ahmedabad FABM might be a long shot, IIM Lucknow ABM is a realistic and promising option for you. It's also advisable to broaden your application strategy to include other reputable ABM programs. Consider applying to IIM Kashipur (ABM) and IIM Sambalpur (ABM), as well as other well-regarded agri-focused management institutes. Your profile, with its strong academic foundation and specialized horticulture background, would be highly competitive for these programs.

Your journey into agri-business management is well-supported by your academic background, and with strategic applications, you can certainly find a program that fits your aspirations.
