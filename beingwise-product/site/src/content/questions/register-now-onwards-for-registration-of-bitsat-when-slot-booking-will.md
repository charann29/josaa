# Can we register now onwards for registration of BITSAT? When slot booking will be started for BITSAT?

Understanding the BITSAT application process, especially registration and slot booking, is crucial for aspiring students. This guide clarifies the key timelines and steps based on official information.

## Key takeaways

*   BITSAT application forms are typically released early in the year, often in January.
*   The registration window usually extends for several weeks, concluding around late March.
*   A correction window is provided after the main registration period for applicants to make necessary changes.
*   Slot booking for the examination generally opens in April, allowing candidates to select their preferred test date and time.

## BITSAT Application Form Availability

Historically, the application process for BITSAT begins quite early in the year. For instance, the BITSAT 2020 application form, accompanied by the detailed prospectus, was made available by the conducting authority on January 11th of that year. This early release gives candidates ample time to review the requirements and prepare their applications.

## Registration Deadline and Correction Window

Once the application forms are out, candidates have a significant period to complete their online registration. For BITSAT 2020, the final date for submitting applications was March 31st. It's important for all applicants to adhere to this deadline, as late submissions are generally not accepted.

Following the primary registration phase, a dedicated correction window is usually opened. This allows candidates who may have made errors during their initial application to rectify them. For BITSAT 2020, this correction window was scheduled from April 1st to April 6th. Utilizing this window ensures that all details on your application are accurate before the next stages.

## BITSAT Slot Booking Process

After successful registration, the next critical step is slot booking. This is where you get to choose your preferred date and time to appear for the BITSAT examination. The slot booking process is conducted online through the official BITS Pilani online test booking system portal. For BITSAT 2020, candidates were able to reserve their test dates and book slots between April 10th and April 18th.

### What if you miss slot booking?

It is highly recommended to book your slot as soon as the window opens to secure your preferred timing. However, if a candidate happens to miss the slot booking period, the examination conductors generally assign an available slot. While this ensures you still get to take the exam, you might not get your first choice of date or time.

## Admit Card Generation

Once your registration is complete and you have successfully booked your slot, an admit card will be generated. This admit card is a mandatory document that you must carry to the examination center. It contains vital information such as your roll number, exam date, time, and venue. Always ensure you download and print your admit card well in advance of the exam date.

Staying informed about these crucial dates and processes directly from official BITSAT channels is key to a smooth application journey.
