# Real honest review of JAGSom PGDM?

JAGSoM (Jain Academy of Global Studies) offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) that many students consider as they explore their higher education options. Let's take a closer look at what the program entails and if it might be the right fit for your career aspirations.

## Key Takeaways

*   JAGSoM can be a good choice, especially if you're looking for a strong management program and haven't secured admission to a top-tier institution.
*   The PGDM program has a fee of INR 17.50 Lakhs.
*   The highest domestic salary reported for the 2025 batch was INR 20.68 LPA.
*   The average CTC for the 2025 batch stood at INR 10.32 LPA.

## Understanding the PGDM Program at JAGSoM

The PGDM at JAGSoM is designed to equip students with essential management skills and a global perspective. It focuses on a practical, industry-relevant curriculum that aims to prepare graduates for various roles in the corporate world. The program structure often includes a mix of classroom learning, case studies, live projects, and internships, providing a holistic educational experience.

## Fees and Financial Commitment

One of the first things prospective students and their families consider is the financial investment. The total fee for the PGDM program at JAGSoM is INR 17.50 Lakhs. This amount typically covers tuition, study materials, and other academic expenses. It's always advisable to confirm the exact breakdown of fees and any additional costs, such as hostel charges or examination fees, directly with the institution's admissions office.

## Placement Overview: What to Expect

Placements are a crucial factor for many students when choosing a management program. JAGSoM provides placement support to its students, helping them transition into their professional careers. For the batch graduating in 2025, the placement statistics offer a clear picture:

*   **Highest Domestic Salary:** The top domestic package secured was INR 20.68 Lakhs per annum (LPA).
*   **Average CTC:** The average cost to company (CTC) for the batch was INR 10.32 LPA.

These figures indicate the range of opportunities available to JAGSoM graduates and can help you gauge potential career outcomes. It's important to remember that individual placements can vary based on skills, specialization, and market demand.

## Is JAGSoM the Right Fit for You?

Deciding on a B-school is a personal journey. JAGSoM presents itself as a viable option, particularly if you are seeking a strong management education and perhaps haven't secured a spot in one of the traditionally top-ranked Tier-1 colleges. Its curriculum and placement record suggest a commitment to preparing students for the professional world. Consider your career goals, the program's offerings, and the overall learning environment to determine if JAGSoM aligns with your aspirations.

Choosing the right PGDM program is a significant decision that can shape your future career path. We hope this overview helps you make an informed choice.
