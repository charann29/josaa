# I have 3 years of bachelor degree and 2 years of post graduate degree in B.Com. Can I apply for SJMSOM IIT Mumbai for MBA?

It's great that you're considering an MBA from a prestigious institution like SJMSOM IIT Bombay. Let's explore whether your academic background aligns with their eligibility requirements.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your 3-year bachelor's and 2-year postgraduate degrees in B.Com generally meet the academic qualification for SJMSOM's MBA.
*   A minimum percentage or CPI in your qualifying degree is crucial for eligibility.
*   Candidates in their final year of study can also apply, provided they meet other criteria.
*   Always refer to the official SJMSOM IIT Bombay website for the most current and detailed eligibility guidelines.

## Understanding SJMSOM MBA Eligibility

SJMSOM, the Shailesh J. Mehta School of Management at IIT Bombay, is highly regarded for its MBA program. When evaluating applicants, they consider several factors, with academic qualifications being a primary one. The key requirement typically revolves around the completion of a bachelor's or master's degree.

From the information available on the official SJMSOM IIT Bombay portal, candidates generally need to hold either a bachelor's degree or a master's degree. Given your 3-year bachelor's and 2-year postgraduate (master's) degree in B.Com, you would likely satisfy the fundamental degree requirement.

## The Importance of Marks or CPI

While possessing the correct type of degree is essential, the marks or Cumulative Performance Index (CPI) achieved in that degree are equally vital. SJMSOM specifies a minimum academic performance threshold. For general category candidates, this usually means securing at least 60% marks or a minimum CPI of 6.5 out of 10 in their qualifying degree. For candidates belonging to the SC/ST/PwD categories, there is a relaxation in these percentages, typically requiring at least 55% marks or a CPI of 6.0 out of 10.

Therefore, to confirm your eligibility, you'll need to check your final aggregate percentage or CPI from your bachelor's or master's degree and ensure it meets these cut-offs.

## Applying While Awaiting Final Results

SJMSOM also accommodates students who are in the final year of their qualifying degree. If you were still pursuing your postgraduate degree, you would have been eligible to apply, provided you were awaiting your final results. This provision allows a wider pool of candidates to apply without having to wait for their degree completion certificate.

## Next Steps: Verify on the Official Website

While your academic profile appears to align with the general requirements, the most accurate and up-to-date information will always be found on the official SJMSOM IIT Bombay website. Eligibility criteria can sometimes be updated, so it's crucial to cross-reference your details with their latest admission brochure or guidelines for the specific academic year you intend to apply.

By carefully reviewing these points, you can confidently assess your eligibility for the SJMSOM IIT Bombay MBA program.
