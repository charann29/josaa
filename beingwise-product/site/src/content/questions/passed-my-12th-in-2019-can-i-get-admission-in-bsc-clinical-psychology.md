# I Passed My 12th in 2019: Can I Get Admission in BSc Clinical Psychology at SGT University, Gurgaon?

If you completed your 12th standard in 2019 and are now considering a Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Clinical Psychology at SGT University, Gurgaon, it's a great step towards a fulfilling career. Let's explore the key aspects of admission for your situation.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Eligibility is Crucial:** You generally need to have passed your 12th grade with a science background (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and at least 50% marks.
*   **Aptitude Test Possible:** SGT University might conduct an aptitude test as part of its admission process.
*   **Gap Year Consideration:** As you passed in 2019, you will likely need to provide a gap certificate explaining the time between your 12th completion and your application.
*   **Direct Application:** Admissions are typically handled directly by the university.

## Understanding the Eligibility Criteria

For a BSc in Clinical Psychology, SGT University, like many other institutions, sets specific academic requirements. The primary criteria usually involve your performance in your 12th-grade examinations. You are generally expected to have completed your 12th standard from a recognized board or university. A crucial point for this particular course is often the subject combination: candidates are typically required to have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) in their 12th grade. Furthermore, a minimum aggregate score, often around 50%, is usually necessary to be considered for admission.

### Your 2019 Completion Date

Since you passed your 12th in 2019, you have a gap of a few years between your schooling and your potential university application. This is not uncommon, and universities are generally prepared to consider such applications. However, it's highly probable that SGT University will ask you to furnish a 'gap certificate.' This document formally explains the activities or reasons for the period between your 12th-grade completion and your application for the BSc program. Common reasons include preparing for competitive exams, gaining work experience, or personal circumstances. It's important to be transparent and provide a valid explanation.

## The Admission Process: Beyond Academics

While your 12th-grade marks and subject combination are foundational, the admission process for a specialized course like Clinical Psychology can involve more. SGT University may decide to conduct an aptitude test. This test is designed to assess your suitability for the program, potentially evaluating your reasoning abilities, general knowledge, or even basic psychological concepts. It's a way for the university to gauge your potential and interest beyond just academic scores. Always check the official SGT University admissions portal for the most current details on their selection process.

## How to Apply

To apply for the BSc Clinical Psychology program at SGT University, you will typically need to follow their direct application process. This usually involves visiting the official SGT University website, filling out an online application form, uploading necessary documents (including your 12th mark sheet, proof of date of birth, identity proof, and potentially your gap certificate), and paying the application fee. It's always advisable to review the university's official admissions brochure or contact their admissions office directly for precise instructions, deadlines, and any specific requirements for candidates with a gap year.

Considering your academic background and the university's potential requirements, pursuing a BSc in Clinical Psychology at SGT University after passing your 12th in 2019 is certainly a possibility, provided you meet all eligibility criteria and prepare for the specific admission steps.
