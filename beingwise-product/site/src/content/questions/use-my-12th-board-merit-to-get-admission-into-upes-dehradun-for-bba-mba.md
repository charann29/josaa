# Can I Use My 12th Board Merit to Get Admission into UPES Dehradun for BBA-MBA Integrated Course?

Yes, your performance in the Class 12 board examinations can indeed play a role in gaining admission to the integrated BBA-MBA program at UPES Dehradun, provided you meet specific academic benchmarks.

## Key Takeaways

*   A minimum of 50% marks in both Class 10 and Class 12 is essential for eligibility.
*   Direct admission based on board merit is possible if you achieve 75% or higher in both Class 10 and Class 12.
*   Alternative admission routes include scores from entrance exams like UPESMET-UG, UGAT, or CUET.
*   The application process is completed online via the official UPES admissions portal.

## Understanding the BBA-MBA Integrated Program at UPES

The integrated BBA-MBA course at UPES Dehradun is a comprehensive five-year program designed for students aspiring to build a strong foundation in business administration followed by advanced management studies. This program offers various specializations, including Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, and Operations, allowing students to tailor their learning journey to their career interests.

## Eligibility and Selection Criteria

To be considered for this program, all applicants must fulfill the basic academic requirements set by UPES. This includes securing at least 50% aggregate marks in both their Class 10 and Class 12 board examinations from a recognized educational board. Meeting this minimum threshold is the first step towards your application being reviewed.

### Board Merit as an Admission Pathway

UPES Dehradun offers a specific pathway for admission based purely on academic merit from your board exams. If you have achieved an impressive 75% or more in both your Class 10 and Class 12 examinations, you may be eligible for direct admission through this merit-based route. This option recognizes and rewards strong academic performance throughout your schooling.

### Entrance Exams: An Alternative Route

For those who may not meet the higher board merit percentage, or who wish to enhance their application, UPES also accepts scores from various entrance examinations. These include the university's own UPESMET-UG, as well as national-level tests like UGAT (Undergraduate Aptitude Test) and CUET (Common University Entrance Test). Performance in these exams can significantly bolster your chances of admission.

## The Application and Interview Process

All applications for the BBA-MBA integrated program must be submitted online through the official UPES admissions portal. After submitting your application and relevant scores (either board merit or entrance exam results), shortlisted candidates are typically invited for further rounds. These may include a group discussion followed by a personal interview, which assesses your communication skills, aptitude, and suitability for the program. Successful candidates will then proceed to document verification and fee payment to secure their seat.

Your academic record from Class 12, especially if strong, can certainly open doors to the integrated BBA-MBA program at UPES Dehradun, alongside other competitive entrance options.
