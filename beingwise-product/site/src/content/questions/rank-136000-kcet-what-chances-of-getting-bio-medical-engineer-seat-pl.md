## Understanding KCET Ranks and Admissions

The Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) rank is crucial for admissions into various undergraduate engineering programs across Karnataka. Your rank of 136,000 indicates your position among all the candidates who appeared for the exam. Colleges typically release cutoff ranks for different branches, which represent the last rank admitted in the previous academic year. These cutoffs can fluctuate based on factors like the number of applicants, seat availability, and the overall performance of candidates in a given year.
