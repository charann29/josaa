It sounds like you're interested in the free digital marketing course offered by Punjab University through the Swayam platform, but are encountering some difficulties with enrollment. This guide will help you understand how to navigate the process and what to do if the enrollment option isn't visible.

## Key takeaways

*   The free digital marketing course from Punjab University is hosted on the Swayam platform.
*   You must have a Swayam account and be logged in to enroll.
*   If the enrollment button isn't visible or the link doesn't work, it usually means the current session's enrollment period has closed.
*   You'll need to check the Swayam platform periodically for updates on future course sessions.

## Understanding Swayam Enrollment

Swayam is India's national online education platform, offering a wide range of courses from various universities and institutions. For any course on Swayam, including those from Punjab University, the enrollment process is fairly standard. You first need to create an account on the official Swayam portal or log in if you already have one. Once logged in, you can search for the specific course you're interested in, such as the digital marketing course.

## The Enrollment Button Explained

When you find the course page on Swayam, you'll typically see an 'Enroll' or 'Join' button if the enrollment period for the current session is open. This button is your gateway to joining the course. Clicking it will usually lead you through a few simple steps to confirm your registration. Once completed, the course materials will become accessible from your Swayam dashboard.

## What if Enrollment Isn't Available?

If you're unable to find an enrollment button, or if a link you're trying to use isn't working, it almost always signifies that the enrollment window for that particular course session has closed. Swayam courses run in specific cycles, and enrollment is only open for a limited time before a session begins. Universities like Punjab University schedule these courses, and once the start date passes or a certain number of students enroll, the option to join is temporarily removed.

## Next Steps: Checking for Future Sessions

Your best approach is to periodically revisit the course page on the Swayam platform. Universities often announce new sessions for popular courses. Keep an eye out for updates on the course page itself or within the general announcements section of Swayam. There isn't a fixed schedule for when new sessions will open, so regular checks are recommended.

We hope this clarifies the enrollment process for the free digital marketing course by Punjab University on Swayam and helps you plan for future opportunities.
