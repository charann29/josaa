# I Got 90.5%ile in JEE Mains Session 1, I am from Gujarat are there any chances of getting admission colleges like NIRMA, PDPU, JAYPEE NOIDA AND ETC. with reputated branches like CSE, ECE etc.

Achieving a 90.5 percentile in JEE Main Session 1 is a commendable start! For students from Gujarat, this score, combined with the state quota benefits, opens up several promising avenues for engineering admissions, particularly in sought-after branches like Computer Science Engineering (CSE) and Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE).

## Key Takeaways

*   A 90.5 percentile is a strong foundation, especially for Gujarat home-state quota seats.
*   Nirma University and Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDPU) are realistic targets for ECE and other core engineering branches with this percentile.
*   CSE admissions at top private colleges can be highly competitive, often requiring a higher percentile.
*   Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT) Noida, while a good option, does not offer a home-state advantage for Gujarat students.

## Understanding Your JEE Main Percentile

Your 90.5 percentile in JEE Main Session 1 indicates that you performed better than 90.5% of the candidates who appeared for that session. While the exact All India Rank (AIR) depends on the total number of unique candidates across both sessions, a 90.5 percentile typically translates to an approximate rank in the range of 95,000 to 1,00,000 or slightly higher. This rank is a crucial factor in determining your eligibility for various engineering programs.

## Gujarat Home State Quota Advantage

One of the most significant advantages for you is being a resident of Gujarat. Many prominent engineering colleges in the state, including Nirma University and PDPU, reserve a substantial percentage of their seats for candidates who qualify under the home state quota. This reservation significantly improves your chances of securing admission compared to the All India Quota (AIQ) seats, where competition is much broader. Always check the specific admission brochures for each institution to understand their exact quota distributions and eligibility criteria.

## Nirma University and PDPU for ECE and Core Branches

With a 90.5 percentile and the Gujarat home state quota, Nirma University and PDPU are indeed viable options for ECE and other core engineering branches such as Mechanical, Civil, or Electrical Engineering. Historically, cut-offs for ECE at these institutions for home-state candidates have often been accessible within this percentile range. CSE, being the most in-demand branch, tends to have higher cut-offs, but it's always worth checking the previous year's closing ranks for home-state students on the official admission portals or through the Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC) in Gujarat.

## Exploring Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT) Noida

Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT) Noida is another well-regarded private engineering college, particularly known for its CSE and ECE programs. However, it's important to note that JIIT Noida does not offer a home state quota for students from Gujarat. Admissions here are primarily based on All India Ranks. While your 90.5 percentile is good, securing CSE or ECE at JIIT Noida might be more challenging due to the higher competition in the All India category. It's advisable to compare their previous year's closing ranks for AIQ with your likely AIR to gauge your chances accurately.

## Beyond Specific Colleges: Other Considerations

When evaluating your options, consider looking at other reputable private and state-funded engineering colleges within Gujarat that also offer strong CSE and ECE programs. The official ACPC website is an excellent resource for understanding the admission process, participating colleges, and past cut-offs for various branches under the Gujarat quota. Additionally, remember to factor in other aspects like campus facilities, faculty experience, placement records, and overall academic environment when making your final decision.

Your JEE Main Session 1 performance provides a solid foundation, and by strategically utilizing your home state advantage, you have good prospects for securing admission into quality engineering programs.
