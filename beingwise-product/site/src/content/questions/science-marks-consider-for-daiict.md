# Do Your 12th Marks Matter for DAIICT B.Tech Admissions?

When aiming for a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree at DAIICT (Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology), it's natural to wonder how much your Class 12 science scores count. While your JEE Main performance is undoubtedly crucial, your intermediate grades play a specific role in meeting the eligibility criteria.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your Class 12 marks are essential for fulfilling DAIICT's B.Tech eligibility requirements.
*   You must have studied Physics, Mathematics, and one additional science subject in Class 12.
*   There is no specific minimum percentage required for Class 12 for All India category applicants, but you must pass.
*   JEE Main scores are the primary basis for merit-based admissions.

## The Role of Class 12 Subjects

To be considered for DAIICT's B.Tech programs, your academic background in Class 12 must align with their stipulated subject combinations. Specifically, you must have successfully completed your Class 12 (or equivalent) examination with a focus on science subjects. The core requirement is that you should have studied:

*   Physics
*   Mathematics
*   And one of the following subjects:
    *   Chemistry
    *   Computer Science
    *   Biology
    *   Biotechnology

This ensures that prospective students have a foundational understanding of the scientific principles relevant to engineering and technology studies.

## Minimum Percentage Requirements

Many students and parents worry about a high minimum percentage cut-off for Class 12. For applicants under the All India category, DAIICT does not specify a minimum percentage for your Class 12 board examination. The crucial point is that you must have successfully passed your Class 12 exams. This emphasizes that while a strong academic record is always beneficial, the primary gatekeeper for admission merit is your performance in the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main.

## JEE Main: The Main Admission Criterion

While Class 12 marks establish your eligibility, your rank in the JEE Main examination is the predominant factor determining your admission to DAIICT's B.Tech programs. Admissions are primarily conducted through the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) counselling process, which allocates seats based on your JEE Main All India Rank. Therefore, a strong performance in JEE Main should be your main focus for securing a spot.

## Counselling and Seat Allocation

Once you meet the Class 12 subject and passing criteria, and have a valid JEE Main rank, you will participate in the JoSAA counselling process. During this process, you will declare your preferences for institutes and courses. Seats are then allocated based on your JEE Main rank, category, and the availability of seats. The official JoSAA portal provides detailed information regarding the counselling schedule and procedures each year.

In essence, your Class 12 science marks open the door to eligibility, but your JEE Main rank is what truly drives your admission prospects at DAIICT.
