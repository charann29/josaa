# What is the fee structure of VIT Vellore?

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT Vellore) offers a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, and their fee structure varies significantly depending on the course and, for B.Tech programs, the category assigned based on entrance exam ranks. Understanding these costs is crucial for prospective students and their families.

## Key takeaways

*   B.Tech fees at VIT Vellore are determined by your rank in the VIT Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE), falling into different fee categories.
*   Undergraduate programs like B.Arch, B.Des, BCA, BBA, and various B.Sc courses have distinct fee structures.
*   Postgraduate programs such as M.Tech, MBA, and MCA also have their own specific annual tuition fees.
*   A refundable caution deposit is typically required once at the time of admission for most programs.

## B.Tech Program Fees: A Category-Based System

For its popular B.Tech programs, VIT Vellore employs a unique fee structure tied to a student's performance in the VITEEE. The better your rank, the lower the fee category you qualify for, resulting in lower annual tuition.

Here's an overview of the B.Tech fee categories for the academic year 2024-2025, as per official information:

| Fee Category | Annual Tuition Fee (approx.) |
| :----------- | :--------------------------- |
| Category 1   | ₹1,73,000                    |
| Category 2   | ₹2,32,000                    |
| Category 3   | ₹3,40,000                    |
| Category 4   | ₹3,85,000                    |
| Category 5   | ₹4,48,000                    |

Additionally, there's a one-time refundable caution deposit of ₹3,000 for B.Tech admissions. It's important to note that these figures are for tuition only and do not include hostel, mess, or other miscellaneous charges.

## Other Undergraduate Program Fees

Beyond B.Tech, VIT Vellore offers several other compelling undergraduate degrees, each with its own fee details:

*   **B.Arch (Bachelor of Architecture):** The annual tuition fee is approximately ₹3,10,000. Including a ₹3,000 refundable caution deposit, the total first-year fee is around ₹3,13,000.
*   **B.Des (Bachelor of Design in Industrial Design):** The annual tuition for this program is about ₹1,73,000. With the ₹3,000 caution deposit, the first-year total is approximately ₹1,76,000.
*   **B.Sc. (Computer Science), BCA, B.Com, BBA:** Fees for these programs generally range from ₹55,000 to ₹90,000 per annum, depending on the specific course. A refundable caution deposit of ₹2,000 is also applicable.

For certain B.Tech specializations, VIT Vellore categorizes them into Group A and Group B, which affects the base tuition fee before category-based ranking adjustments. For example, specializations like Bioengineering, Biotechnology, Civil, Chemical, Electrical & Electronics, and Fashion Technology might fall into Group A, with a tuition fee around ₹1,73,000 per annum. Group B specializations, which often include Computer Science and Engineering (various specializations), Aerospace, Information Technology, and Mechanical Engineering, might have a tuition fee closer to ₹1,95,000 per annum, plus the caution deposit.

## Postgraduate Program Fees

VIT Vellore also offers a range of postgraduate programs, with varying fee structures:

*   **M.Tech Programs:** Tuition fees for M.Tech specializations (such as Biomedical Engineering, CAD/CAM, Construction Technology, etc.) typically range from ₹1,50,000 to ₹1,90,000 per annum. A ₹3,000 refundable caution deposit is also part of the initial payment.
*   **MBA Program:** The annual tuition fee for the Master of Business Administration (MBA) program is approximately ₹3,48,500. With the ₹3,000 refundable caution deposit, the first-year total comes to about ₹3,51,500.
*   **MCA Program:** The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program has an annual tuition fee of around ₹1,37,000, along with a ₹3,000 refundable caution deposit.

## Additional Costs to Consider

Beyond tuition fees and the caution deposit, students should budget for other expenses. These commonly include:

*   **Hostel Fees:** VIT Vellore provides various hostel options, and their fees differ based on accommodation type (e.g., single room, shared room, AC/non-AC). These are separate from tuition.
*   **Mess Fees:** Charges for food services are also distinct from tuition and hostel fees.
*   **Examination Fees:** Specific fees for examinations will be applicable as per university guidelines.
*   **Miscellaneous Expenses:** This can include costs for books, study materials, personal expenses, and travel.

It is always advisable to refer to the official VIT Vellore admissions brochure or website for the most current and detailed fee breakdown for your chosen program, as figures can be updated annually.
