# How good is executive general management program at IIMB?

IIM Bangalore (IIMB) is consistently ranked among India's premier business schools, offering a range of programs for aspiring leaders. Its Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP) is a one-year, full-time residential program designed for professionals aiming to accelerate their careers and transition into senior management roles.

## Key takeaways

*   IIMB's EPGP is a highly respected one-year executive MBA program, often considered a top choice for experienced professionals.
*   It features a comprehensive curriculum delivered over four terms, including an international immersion module.
*   Graduates are sought after by employers who value their blend of practical experience and advanced management training.
*   The program has a strong track record of high average salaries for its alumni.

## Understanding the EPGP at IIM Bangalore

The Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP) at IIM Bangalore is tailored for mid-career professionals looking to enhance their leadership capabilities and gain a global perspective on business. This intensive, full-time program packs a robust curriculum into a single year, making it an efficient way to acquire advanced management skills.

The program structure typically involves four distinct terms. The final term often includes an international immersion component, providing participants with exposure to global business environments and diverse cultural contexts. This integrated approach ensures that graduates are well-equipped to tackle complex challenges in today's interconnected world.

## Why employers value executive MBA graduates

In today's competitive job market, companies increasingly seek candidates who bring both significant work experience and formal management education. Executive MBA (EMBA) programs like IIMB's EPGP are designed to cultivate this unique blend.

Employers often find that EPGP graduates possess a maturity and practical understanding that complements their academic training. This makes them highly valuable assets, capable of contributing strategically from day one. Their prior professional experience, combined with the rigorous curriculum, positions them for leadership roles and enables them to command competitive compensation packages.

## Eligibility for the EPGP

Admission to the EPGP at IIM Bangalore is highly selective, reflecting the program's prestige. Prospective applicants must meet several key criteria:

*   **Educational Qualification:** A bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks or an equivalent Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA). For candidates from Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), and Persons with Disability (PwD) categories, the minimum requirement is 45% marks or an equivalent CGPA. Importantly, individuals who have completed CA, ICWA, or CS after Class XII are also eligible, provided they meet the minimum percentage/CGPA requirements.
*   **Work Experience:** A minimum of 5 years of post-graduation work experience is required. While there isn't a strict upper limit, candidates typically have less than 12 years of experience by March 31st of the application year.
*   **Standardized Test Scores:** Applicants must submit a valid GMAT or GRE score.

## Career prospects and salaries

The EPGP at IIM Bangalore has a strong reputation for enhancing career trajectories. Graduates often move into leadership positions across various industries, including consulting, technology, finance, and general management.

While specific figures can vary year by year, the program consistently reports impressive average salaries for its alumni. For instance, in 2018, the mean salary for EPGP graduates from IIM Bangalore was officially reported as Rs. 23.5 lakh per annum, highlighting the significant return on investment this program can offer.

IIM Bangalore's EPGP offers a transformative experience for experienced professionals ready to elevate their careers and make a substantial impact in the business world.
