# Is BK School of Management accept MAT score?

If you're considering a Master of Business Administration (MBA) at the BK School of Business Management and wondering about entrance exams, it's important to know their specific requirements. This guide clarifies which scores they accept for their flagship MBA program.

## Key Takeaways

*   The BK School of Business Management does not accept MAT scores for MBA admissions.
*   Admission to their two-year, full-time MBA program requires a valid CMAT score.
*   CMAT is conducted by AICTE, New Delhi, and is the mandatory entrance exam for this institution.
*   Applicants also need a Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for SC/ST candidates).

## Understanding the Entrance Exam Requirement

For aspiring MBA students eyeing the BK School of Business Management, the crucial piece of information is that the Management Aptitude Test (MAT) score is not considered for admissions. The institution has a clear policy regarding the accepted entrance examination for its prestigious Master of Business Administration program.

### CMAT: The Mandatory Exam

The only national-level entrance exam accepted by the BK School of Business Management for its full-time MBA program is the Common Management Admission Test (CMAT). This exam is conducted by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. Therefore, to be eligible for admission, all candidates must appear for and obtain a valid score in the CMAT.

## Eligibility Criteria Beyond Entrance Scores

While a CMAT score is essential, it's just one part of the admission puzzle. The BK School of Business Management also has specific academic requirements that applicants must meet. These criteria ensure that admitted students possess a foundational academic background suitable for the rigorous MBA curriculum.

### Academic Qualifications

To be considered for the MBA program, candidates must hold a Bachelor's degree from a university recognized by official bodies. Furthermore, there's a minimum percentage requirement for the qualifying examination:

*   **General Category:** Candidates must secure at least 50% aggregate marks in their Bachelor's degree.
*   **SC/ST Category:** Candidates belonging to the Scheduled Caste (SC) or Scheduled Tribe (ST) categories receive a concession and must obtain a minimum of 45% aggregate marks in their Bachelor's degree.

## Why CMAT is Preferred

The preference for CMAT at institutions like BK School of Business Management often stems from the exam's comprehensive nature and its widespread acceptance across AICTE-approved management programs in India. CMAT assesses various skills, including quantitative techniques, logical reasoning, language comprehension, and general awareness, providing a holistic evaluation of a candidate's aptitude for management studies.

In summary, if the BK School of Business Management is on your radar for an MBA, focus your preparation on the CMAT, as MAT scores are not part of their admission process.
