I have scored 82.826 percentile(subject wise score) and 79.266 percentile(Overall) in TANCET ME/Mtech 2021 examination. Can i get into PSG tech or Anna university in BC category?

Navigating TANCET scores and admission possibilities can be a bit complex. Let's break down your chances for ME/M.Tech admission at PSG Tech and Anna University based on your TANCET 2021 percentiles and BC category.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your overall percentile of 79.266 is a strong score for TANCET ME/M.Tech.
*   Admission to top colleges like PSG Tech and Anna University is highly competitive and depends on various factors beyond just percentile.
*   Cut-offs vary significantly by college, specific M.Tech program, and reservation category each year.
*   While your score is competitive, final admission depends on the official counselling process and seat availability.

## Understanding TANCET Cut-offs

Cut-off percentiles are the minimum scores required for admission to a particular course and college. These are determined by the Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE) Tamil Nadu during the counselling process and can fluctuate year to year based on the number of applicants, their scores, and the availability of seats. For reserved categories like BC, the cut-offs are generally lower than for the General category.

### PSG Tech Cut-offs for ME/M.Tech

For PSG College of Technology (PSG Tech), a highly regarded institution, admission is very competitive. Historically, general category cut-offs for ME/M.Tech programs have ranged from 75-80 percentile. For backward classes (including BC), cut-offs are considerably lower. While specific figures for the BC category aren't always publicly generalized, they typically fall well below the general category range. Your 79.266 overall percentile is quite strong and could place you in a good position to be considered for various ME/M.Tech specializations at PSG Tech under the BC category.

### Anna University Cut-offs for ME/M.Tech

Anna University, being a premier institution in Tamil Nadu, also has high demand for its ME/M.Tech programs. Generally, the cut-offs for admission to Anna University's main campus and its constituent colleges are above 65 percentile for most programs. Given your overall percentile of 79.266, you certainly meet and exceed this general threshold. For BC category candidates, the cut-offs would again be more favourable than for the general category, further enhancing your prospects.

## The Role of Counselling

Admission to ME/M.Tech programs through TANCET is managed through a centralised counselling process conducted by DoTE Tamil Nadu. During counselling, candidates are allotted seats based on their TANCET percentile, preferred colleges and courses, and their reservation category. Even with a good score, the final allocation depends on how many candidates with higher scores opt for the same programs and colleges, especially in your BC category.

## Factors Beyond Percentile

While your TANCET percentile is crucial, remember that other factors might subtly influence your admission, such as:

*   **Specific Program Demand:** Some M.Tech specializations are more sought after than others, leading to higher cut-offs for those particular programs.
*   **Seat Matrix:** The number of seats available for each program and category at a college also plays a significant role.
*   **Academic Background:** Your undergraduate academic record, while not directly part of the TANCET score, can sometimes be a tie-breaker if multiple candidates have similar TANCET scores.

## What to Do Next

With your TANCET 2021 percentile, you have a strong chance for both PSG Tech and Anna University in the BC category. Your next step should be to actively participate in the official TANCET counselling process. Make sure to research the specific ME/M.Tech programs you are interested in at both institutions and list them strategically during choice filling.

We wish you the very best for your admissions!
