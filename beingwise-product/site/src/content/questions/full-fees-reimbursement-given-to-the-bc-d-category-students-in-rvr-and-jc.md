# Is Full Fees Reimbursement Given to BC-D Category Students in RVR & JC College by Government?

Many students and parents from the BC-D category often wonder about fee reimbursement opportunities at institutions like RVR & JC College of Engineering, Guntur. This article clarifies the government's fee reimbursement scheme for eligible students pursuing higher education.

## Key Takeaways

*   Full fee reimbursement for BC-D students in Andhra Pradesh generally requires an AP EAMCET rank below 10,000.
*   Partial reimbursement may be available for ranks above 10,000, provided other criteria are met.
*   Parental income and employment status are critical factors in determining eligibility.
*   Students must apply for scholarships through the official government channels to avail these benefits.

## Understanding Fee Reimbursement in Andhra Pradesh

The Government of Andhra Pradesh offers fee reimbursement schemes to support students from various categories, including BC-D, in pursuing professional courses. These schemes aim to make higher education more accessible by covering a significant portion, or sometimes the entirety, of the tuition fees. Institutions recognized by the Andhra Pradesh government and participating in AP EAMCET counseling are typically included in these programs.

## Eligibility Criteria for BC-D Category Students

For students belonging to the BC-D category, specific criteria must be met to qualify for fee reimbursement. The primary factors considered are:

*   **AP EAMCET Rank:** To be eligible for full fee reimbursement, BC-D candidates generally need to secure an AP EAMCET rank below 10,000. If your rank is above this threshold, you might still qualify for partial fee reimbursement, assuming other conditions are satisfied.
*   **Parental Income:** The annual income of your parents is a crucial determinant. Typically, their combined annual income should not exceed Rs. 2,00,000. This threshold is set to ensure that the benefits reach economically disadvantaged families.
*   **Parental Employment Status:** If either of your parents is a government employee, you might not be eligible for these specific fee reimbursement benefits, as the scheme targets families without government employment support.

## How Reimbursement Works at RVR & JC College of Engineering

RVR & JC College of Engineering, Guntur, like other colleges recognized by the Government of Andhra Pradesh and participating in AP EAMCET counseling, facilitates government fee reimbursement for eligible students. If you meet the stipulated criteria, you can receive either full or partial fee reimbursement directly from the government. It's important to remember that this is not a college-specific scholarship but a government initiative that the college helps process.

## Application Process for Reimbursement

To avail of fee reimbursement, eligible students must actively apply for the scholarship through the official government portal designated for such schemes. This usually involves submitting necessary documents, including caste certificates, income certificates, and AP EAMCET rank cards, within the specified deadlines. It is crucial to stay updated with announcements from the Department of Collegiate Education or the relevant scholarship authority in Andhra Pradesh for application timelines and procedures.

Securing higher education can be made significantly more affordable with the right understanding of government support programs, and RVR & JC College of Engineering participates in these to benefit eligible students.
