## Current Program Landscape at IIM Indore

IIM Indore is renowned for its comprehensive management education. Its flagship offerings include the Post Graduate Program in Management (PGP), which is equivalent to an MBA, and the Integrated Program in Management (IPM), a unique five-year program for students after Class 12. The institute also provides various executive education programs designed for working professionals, along with doctoral programs in management.

These programs consistently emphasize core management principles, leadership development, and strategic thinking across diverse business functions. The institute's focus has historically been on nurturing future business leaders through rigorous academic and practical exposure in management disciplines.
