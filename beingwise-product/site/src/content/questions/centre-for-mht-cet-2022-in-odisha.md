# Is there any centre for MHT CET 2022 in Odisha?

Many students from outside Maharashtra consider taking the MHT CET for admission to various professional courses. A common question revolves around the availability of exam centers in their home states. This article clarifies the situation regarding MHT CET exam centers in Odisha for the 2022 examination.

## Key takeaways

*   The MHT CET is a state-level entrance examination primarily for admissions within Maharashtra.
*   For the 2022 MHT CET, there were no designated examination centers located in Odisha.
*   While centers were available in Maharashtra's 36 districts, select other states also hosted examination centers.
*   Students from Odisha wishing to take the MHT CET would need to travel to an exam center in one of the approved states.

## MHT CET: A Maharashtra State Examination

The MHT CET, or Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test, is a crucial entrance examination conducted by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra. Its primary purpose is to facilitate admissions to undergraduate professional courses like Engineering, Pharmacy, Agriculture, and Health Sciences offered by institutions within the state of Maharashtra. As a state-level exam, its focus naturally remains on providing ample testing facilities within Maharashtra itself.

## Exam Center Availability for MHT CET 2022

For the MHT CET 2022, examination centers were predominantly located across all 36 districts of Maharashtra. This ensured that students within the state had convenient access to appear for the test. However, recognizing the interest from candidates in neighboring or other Indian states, the examination authorities also established centers in a limited number of locations outside Maharashtra.

## States with MHT CET 2022 Exam Centers Outside Maharashtra

While Odisha was not among the states hosting MHT CET 2022 examination centers, several other states did provide this facility. Candidates from these regions, or those willing to travel, could select centers in the following states:

*   Bihar
*   Chhattisgarh
*   Delhi-NCR
*   Gujarat
*   Jharkhand
*   Karnataka
*   Madhya Pradesh
*   Rajasthan
*   Telangana
*   Uttar Pradesh

This list indicates that candidates from Odisha interested in the MHT CET 2022 would have needed to make arrangements to appear for the exam in one of these specified states or within Maharashtra itself.

## Planning for MHT CET from Odisha

For students in Odisha considering the MHT CET in future years, it's essential to check the official information bulletin released by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, for the most current details on exam centers. While the 2022 examination did not have centers in Odisha, policies can sometimes change. Always refer to the official MHT CET portal for the latest updates on examination cities and other crucial information before applying.

Understanding the geographical spread of MHT CET exam centers is vital for effective planning, especially for students residing outside Maharashtra.
