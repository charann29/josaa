# I belong to OBC category and wish to apply for the same in Neet mds 2026. Will I be asked to upload Non Creamy Layer Certificate during registration process? Or is it only required during Councelling?

Navigating the NEET MDS application process, especially for reserved categories like OBC-NCL, can bring up questions about document submission timelines. Understanding when and how to provide your Non-Creamy Layer (NCL) certificate is crucial for a smooth application.

## Key Takeaways

*   You will indicate your OBC-NCL status during the NEET MDS 2026 application form filling.
*   While an upload might be requested during registration, the primary verification of your NCL certificate occurs during the counseling phase.
*   Ensure your OBC-NCL certificate is valid and issued within the stipulated timeframe before counseling begins.
*   The Medical Counselling Committee (MCC) is responsible for verifying documents for All India Quota seats.

## Declaring Your OBC-NCL Status During Application

When you fill out the NEET MDS 2026 application form, you will be prompted to select your category. If you belong to the Other Backward Classes (OBC) and qualify under the Non-Creamy Layer criteria, you should specifically choose the 'OBC-NCL' option. This declaration is a vital step in ensuring you are considered for the appropriate reservation benefits.

## Certificate Submission: Registration vs. Counseling

While the application form allows you to declare your category, the actual submission and verification of your OBC-NCL certificate generally take place at a later stage. It's possible that the National Board of Examinations in Medical Sciences (NBEMS), which conducts NEET MDS, might ask for a provisional upload or a self-declaration during the initial registration process. However, the definitive scrutiny of your NCL certificate, along with other essential documents, is typically reserved for the counseling rounds.

## The Role of Counseling in Document Verification

The Medical Counselling Committee (MCC) is the body responsible for conducting counseling for All India Quota (AIQ) seats in NEET MDS. During the document verification phase of counseling, candidates are required to present all original documents, including their valid OBC-NCL certificate. This is the stage where your eligibility under the OBC-NCL category is officially confirmed. If your certificate is not in order or does not meet the requirements, your claim for reservation might be rejected, and you could be considered under the General category.

## Validity of Your OBC-NCL Certificate

It is imperative to ensure that your OBC-NCL certificate is current and valid as per the guidelines issued by the competent authorities. Typically, these certificates have a validity period, and you might need to obtain a new one if your existing certificate is nearing its expiry or has already expired before the counseling process begins. Always refer to the latest information provided by the MCC or NTA regarding the specific dates of issuance required for the certificate.

Being prepared with all necessary documents, including your OBC-NCL certificate, well in advance will help you navigate the NEET MDS 2026 application and counseling processes with confidence.
