# What were the least CAT scores that saw selection in IIFT (Delhi & Kolkata)

Aspiring for an MBA in International Business from a prestigious institution like the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT)? Understanding the minimum CAT scores required for admission to its Delhi and Kolkata campuses is a crucial first step in your preparation journey.

## Key takeaways

*   Since 2024, IIFT Delhi has been using CAT scores for its MBA (IB) admissions, replacing its own entrance exam.
*   While CAT percentiles are a significant factor, IIFT's selection process is holistic, considering various aspects beyond just the entrance exam.
*   Cutoffs can fluctuate each year based on factors like the number of applicants, CAT exam difficulty, and the overall quality of the applicant pool.
*   Always refer to the official IIFT website for the most current and accurate admission criteria and cutoff information.

## Understanding IIFT Delhi's CAT Cutoffs

For those aiming for the IIFT Delhi campus, which is highly sought after, the CAT percentile requirements are quite competitive. For the 2024-26 academic batch, based on CAT 2023 scores, the expected minimum percentiles for shortlisting were:

*   **General Category:** Candidates typically needed a CAT percentile in the range of 96.7 to 98 or higher. It's important to note that specific sectional cutoffs also apply, and a strong overall profile (e.g., non-engineering background, work experience, female candidates) could sometimes lead to shortlisting at slightly lower percentiles.
*   **General-EWS/OBC-NCL Categories:** For these categories, the expected shortlisting percentiles generally ranged from 90 to 97.
*   **SC/ST/PwD Categories:** Candidates in these reserved categories could expect shortlisting with CAT percentiles between 75 and 95.

## IIFT Kolkata's CAT Score Expectations

IIFT Kolkata also offers an excellent MBA (IB) program. While the cutoffs are still competitive, they tend to be slightly less stringent than for the Delhi campus. The expected ranges for shortlisting for the 2024-26 batch were broadly:

*   **General Category:** A CAT percentile between 85 and 95 was generally considered for shortlisting.
*   **OBC/SC/ST Categories:** For reserved categories, the percentile requirements could vary more widely, typically falling between 50 and 95, depending on the specific category and the competitiveness of the applicant pool.

## The Holistic Selection Process at IIFT

It's vital to remember that a high CAT score is just one part of IIFT's comprehensive admission process. The institute employs a multi-stage evaluation to select well-rounded candidates. The CAT score usually carries a weightage of approximately 45-48% in the final selection. Beyond the CAT, other components include:

*   **Written Ability Test (WAT):** An assessment of your writing and analytical skills.
*   **Group Discussion (GD):** Evaluates your communication, teamwork, and leadership potential.
*   **Personal Interview (PI):** A direct interaction to gauge your personality, motivation, and suitability for the program.
*   **Academic Consistency:** Your past academic record plays a role.
*   **Work Experience:** Relevant professional experience is highly valued.
*   **Diversity Factors:** IIFT also considers factors like gender and academic background diversity.

## Factors Influencing Cutoffs

IIFT cutoffs are not static; they change annually. Several factors contribute to these variations:

*   **Number of Applicants:** A higher number of applicants can push cutoffs upwards.
*   **CAT Exam Difficulty:** A tougher CAT exam might result in slightly lower percentile cutoffs, and vice-versa.
*   **Overall Applicant Quality:** The strength of the applicant pool in a given year also impacts the required percentiles.
*   **Seat Availability:** The number of available seats for each program and category influences the cutoff thresholds.

Therefore, while the figures provided offer a good indication, they should be viewed as estimates. Always refer to the official IIFT website for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding admission criteria for the current academic year.

Aiming for IIFT requires not just a strong CAT performance but also a well-rounded profile and excellent performance in the subsequent selection rounds. Plan your preparation strategically and keep an eye on official announcements.
