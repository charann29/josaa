## Understanding AP ECET Lateral Entry Admissions

AP ECET (Andhra Pradesh Engineering Common Entrance Test) is the pathway for diploma holders and B.Sc. degree holders to enter the second year of B.E./B.Tech programs in Andhra Pradesh. Admissions are managed through a centralized counselling process, where seats are allocated based on your rank, preferred college, and branch availability. The cut-off ranks, which are the last ranks admitted into a particular program, are determined by factors like the total number of applicants, the difficulty of the exam, and the number of seats available in each institution.
