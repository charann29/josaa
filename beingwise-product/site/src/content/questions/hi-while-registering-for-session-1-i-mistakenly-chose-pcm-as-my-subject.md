# Hi, while registering for session 1 I mistakenly chose pcm as my subject combination, however i'm aiming for pharma and only want to give pcb in bitsat. Can i change my details while registering for the 2nd attempt or during the correction window of the 2nd attempt if i do not give the first one?

It's a common concern to realise you've made a mistake on an exam application, especially when it comes to subject combinations. Let's look at how you might be able to correct your BITSAT subject choice from PCM to PCB, particularly if you're aiming for pharmacy programmes.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Correction Window:** You might be able to change your subject combination during the official correction window for the session you registered for.
*   **Session 2 Registration:** If you register for BITSAT Session 2 separately, you can enter the correct PCB details at that time.
*   **Official Guidelines:** Always refer to the official BITSAT information bulletin and website for the most accurate and up-to-date rules on application changes.
*   **Pharmacy Focus:** For pharmacy (B.Pharm) programmes at BITS, the PCB subject combination is typically required.

## Understanding BITSAT Subject Combinations

BITSAT offers different subject combinations to cater to various engineering and pharmacy programmes. For most engineering courses, Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) is the standard combination. However, for Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) degrees, the requirement is generally Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB).

It's crucial to select the correct combination during registration, as this determines the sections you will attempt in the BITSAT exam. An incorrect choice could mean you're tested on subjects not relevant to your desired course, or worse, you might not meet the eligibility criteria for admission.

## Correcting Your Session 1 Application

If you've already registered for BITSAT Session 1 and mistakenly chose PCM instead of PCB, there might be a chance to rectify this. The exam conducting authority typically opens a 'correction window' after the initial registration period closes. During this window, candidates are often allowed to edit certain details in their application forms.

However, whether the subject combination is an editable field during this window is entirely at the discretion of the BITSAT authorities. You must regularly check the official BITSAT website for announcements regarding the correction window dates and the specific fields that can be modified. If the option to change subjects is available, ensure you make the switch from PCM to PCB promptly.

## Registering for BITSAT Session 2

Even if you registered for Session 1 and didn't attempt the exam or couldn't correct your details, you have another opportunity with Session 2. BITSAT allows candidates to apply for both sessions independently. If you choose to register for Session 2, you will fill out a fresh application form.

This means you can accurately select the PCB subject combination during your Session 2 registration. This is a straightforward way to ensure your application reflects your intention to pursue pharmacy and sit for the correct subject paper. Remember that applying for Session 2 will involve a separate application process and fee.

## Important Considerations

*   **Eligibility for Pharmacy:** Double-check the specific eligibility criteria for B.Pharm programmes at BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses. While PCB is standard, confirming this directly from the official BITSAT information brochure is always best.
*   **No First Attempt Impact:** Not appearing for Session 1 typically does not affect your ability to register and appear for Session 2, provided you meet all other eligibility requirements for the second session.
*   **Stay Informed:** The most reliable information will always come directly from the official BITSAT website. Avoid relying on unverified sources for critical application details.

By carefully managing your registration for Session 2 or making use of any available correction windows, you can ensure your BITSAT application correctly aligns with your pharmacy aspirations.
