# I am not sure if I want to be admitted to MIT-WPU Pune for MBA. One of my friends suggested me to take admission in a good government college. While searching I found IIM Lucknow. what to do? MIT-WPU or IIM Lucknow?

Deciding on the right MBA program is a big step, and it's wise to consider all your options carefully. You're comparing two very different institutions: MIT-WPU Pune and IIM Lucknow. Let's break down the key distinctions to help you make an informed choice.

## Key takeaways

*   IIM Lucknow is a public, 'Institute of National Importance' with a highly competitive admission process, primarily through CAT scores.
*   MIT-WPU is a private university offering a wider range of specialisations and accepts multiple entrance exam scores.
*   While IIM Lucknow has significantly higher fees, it also offers superior placement opportunities.
*   Your career aspirations, financial considerations, and entrance exam performance will be crucial in this decision.

## Understanding the Institutions

MIT-WPU (Vishwanathrao Deshpande University) is a private university located in Pune, offering its MBA through the WPU School of Business. It's known for its diverse programs and modern facilities. On the other hand, IIM Lucknow is a prestigious public institution, established by the Government of India. It holds the esteemed designation of an 'Institute of National Importance,' reflecting its high academic standards and significant contribution to management education in India.

## Admission Process and Exams Accepted

One of the most significant differences lies in the admission gateway. To gain entry into IIM Lucknow's flagship MBA program, you must achieve a high percentile in the Common Admission Test (CAT). Their selection process typically involves the CAT score, followed by a Written Ability Test (WAT) and a Personal Interview (PI).

MIT-WPU, in contrast, offers more flexibility regarding entrance exams. It accepts scores from various national-level tests, including CAT, XAT, NMAT, GMAT, PERA, CMAT, MAH-CET, and MAT. Their selection process generally includes an entrance exam score, followed by a Group Discussion (GD) or Extempore, and a Personal Interaction (PI).

## Specialisations and Program Structure

When it comes to program offerings, MIT-WPU provides a broader spectrum of choices. Their MBA program is available in 11 different specialisations, allowing students to tailor their studies to specific career interests. This could be beneficial if you have a very niche area you wish to specialise in.

IIM Lucknow primarily focuses on its Post Graduate Program (PGP), PGP-Agribusiness Management (PGP-ABM), PGP-Sustainable Management (PGP-SM), and an MBA in Entrepreneurship and Innovation. While these programs are highly regarded, the range of specialisations is more focused compared to MIT-WPU.

## Fees and Financial Commitment

Financial considerations are often a major factor in choosing an MBA program. The annual program fees for MIT-WPU's MBA are approximately Rs. 4,55,000. For IIM Lucknow, the total program fees for its MBA are significantly higher, at around Rs. 20,00,000. It's important to remember that these figures typically cover tuition and may not include living expenses, books, or other miscellaneous costs. Always check the official institution websites for the most current and detailed fee structures.

## Placements and Career Prospects

This is often a deciding factor for many students. Based on general industry reputation and past performance, the placement opportunities at IIM Lucknow are widely considered to be superior to those at MIT-WPU. IIMs consistently attract top recruiters from various sectors, offering higher average salaries and more diverse career roles. While MIT-WPU also has a placement cell that works to secure opportunities for its students, the scale and compensation packages typically associated with IIM Lucknow are significantly higher. You can often find detailed placement reports on the official websites of both institutions.

## Conclusion

Ultimately, IIM Lucknow stands out as the stronger option in terms of academic rigor, national recognition, and career prospects. However, admission is highly competitive, demanding an exceptional CAT percentile. If you are confident in your ability to perform well in CAT and are looking for a top-tier management education with excellent placement outcomes, IIM Lucknow would be an ideal choice. If your CAT score is lower, or if you prefer a wider range of specialisations and a more accessible admission process, MIT-WPU offers a viable alternative in the private university space.
