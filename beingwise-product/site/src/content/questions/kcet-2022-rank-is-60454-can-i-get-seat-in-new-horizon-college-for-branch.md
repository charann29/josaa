## Factors Influencing Cutoffs Annually

It's crucial to remember that KCET cutoffs are not static; they fluctuate every year. Several dynamic factors contribute to these changes:

*   **Total Number of Applicants:** A higher number of candidates appearing for KCET can drive cutoffs up (meaning lower ranks are needed for admission).
*   **Difficulty Level of the Exam:** If the KCET exam is tougher in a given year, candidates might score lower overall, potentially leading to slightly higher closing ranks (meaning your rank might be relatively better).
*   **Number of Available Seats:** Any changes in the sanctioned intake for a particular branch at a college will directly impact the cutoff.
