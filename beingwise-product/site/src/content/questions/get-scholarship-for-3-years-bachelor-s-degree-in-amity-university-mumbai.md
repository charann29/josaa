# Can I get a scholarship for a 3-year bachelor's degree at Amity University, Mumbai? If yes, what is the eligibility?

Amity University, Mumbai, understands that financial assistance can be crucial for students pursuing higher education. They offer a range of scholarships designed to support deserving candidates based on their academic achievements.

## Key Takeaways

*   Amity University, Mumbai, provides scholarships primarily for undergraduate programs.
*   Scholarships are awarded based on Class 12 academic performance.
*   Maintenance of a specific academic score is required for scholarship continuation in subsequent years.
*   Scholarships are typically offered on a first-come, first-served basis, so early application is advisable.

## Understanding Amity University Mumbai Scholarships

Amity University, Mumbai, offers scholarships to students enrolling in their bachelor's degree programs. These scholarships are merit-based, meaning they are awarded to students who have demonstrated excellent academic performance in their Class 12 examinations. It's important to note that these scholarships are generally for the first year of study and are then continued based on academic performance in the university.

## Eligibility Criteria for Undergraduate Scholarships

The primary eligibility for scholarships at Amity University, Mumbai, revolves around your Class 12 board examination results. Here's a breakdown of the typical requirements:

*   **Scholarship Category 1:** Students who have secured 95% or above in their Class 12 CBSE or ICSE board examinations.
*   **Scholarship Category 2:** Students who have scored between 90% and 94.9% in their Class 12 CBSE or ICSE board examinations.
*   **Scholarship Category 3:** Students achieving 90% or above in their Class 12 examinations from a State Board.

While the source mentions graduation percentages, for **bachelor's degrees**, the initial scholarship eligibility is primarily determined by your Class 12 scores. The graduation percentages might relate to scholarships for postgraduate courses or the continuation criteria for undergraduate scholarships, which we'll discuss next.

## Scholarship Continuation Policy

Receiving a scholarship in your first year is a great start. To ensure your scholarship continues for the subsequent years and semesters of your bachelor's degree program, you will need to maintain a certain level of academic performance at Amity University, Mumbai. While specific CGPA or percentage requirements can vary and should always be confirmed with the university's official admissions department, generally, students are expected to maintain a minimum percentage to retain their scholarship. For instance, the source suggests that students initially qualifying with 95% in Class 12 might need to maintain 80% in graduation (referring to their university performance), and those qualifying with 90-94.9% in Class 12 might need to maintain 75% in their university studies. Always check the latest official Amity University, Mumbai scholarship guidelines for precise continuation criteria.

## Application Process and Important Considerations

Scholarships at Amity University, Mumbai, are often awarded on a first-come, first-served basis. This means that applying early for admission and simultaneously for scholarships can significantly increase your chances. When you apply for admission, ensure you provide all necessary academic documents, especially your Class 12 mark sheets, as these are crucial for scholarship assessment. For the most accurate and up-to-date information, always refer to the official Amity University, Mumbai website or contact their admissions office directly.

Exploring scholarship opportunities can significantly ease the financial burden of higher education, allowing you to focus more on your studies and overall university experience.
