# IMS BHU: Does it Offer a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) Course?

It's a common question for aspiring students: does the Institute of Medical Sciences (IMS) at Banaras Hindu University (BHU) offer a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) program? We're here to clarify this for you.

## Key Takeaways

*   Yes, IMS BHU indeed offers a full-time Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) course.
*   Admission to the BPT program is through the CUET UG examination, not NEET.
*   The course spans four years, followed by a mandatory six-month internship.
*   The total fee for the entire BPT program at IMS BHU is approximately Rs. 72,056.

## BPT at IMS BHU: Course Structure and Duration

For those interested in a career in physiotherapy, IMS BHU provides a comprehensive BPT degree. This program is structured as a four-year full-time course, designed to equip students with the necessary theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Following the four years of academic study, students are required to complete a six-month compulsory internship. This internship is crucial for gaining hands-on experience and applying classroom learning in a clinical setting.

## Understanding the Admission Process: CUET UG, Not NEET

One of the main reasons for confusion regarding the BPT course on the IMS BHU website might be the admission process. Unlike medical and dental undergraduate courses like MBBS and BDS, which use the NEET examination for admissions, the BPT program at IMS BHU utilizes the Common University Entrance Test (CUET UG). This distinction is important because the BPT course is typically listed under admissions governed by CUET UG, rather than alongside NEET-affiliated programs. Therefore, when searching for information on the official BHU or IMS BHU portals, it's helpful to look for CUET UG-related admissions.

## Seat Matrix and Fee Details

The BPT program at IMS BHU has a limited number of seats, ensuring focused attention and quality education. There are a total of 25 seats available for the course, allocated across various categories including General, OBC, SC, ST, and EWS, all in accordance with BHU's established guidelines. The fee structure for the BPT program is quite affordable. The approximate total tuition cost for the entire four-year program, as per official information, is Rs. 72,056.

IMS BHU's BPT program offers a valuable pathway for students aspiring to enter the field of physiotherapy.
