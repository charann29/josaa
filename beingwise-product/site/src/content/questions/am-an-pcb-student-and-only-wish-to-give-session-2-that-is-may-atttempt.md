# I am a PCB student and only wish to give session 2 that is May attempt of MHT CET then which option to choose during form filling first attempt or both attempt?

If you're a PCB student planning to appear only for the MHT CET May session (Session 2) and skip the earlier one, it's important to understand how to correctly fill out the application form to reflect your choice.

## Key Takeaways

*   Carefully read all instructions on the official MHT CET application portal.
*   If given options like "First Attempt" and "Both Attempts," choosing "First Attempt" generally implies you are registering for the *next available* or *chosen* session, not necessarily the very first chronological one.
*   Look for specific options to select your preferred session (Session 1 or Session 2) if available.
*   Double-check your selection before final submission to ensure you're registered only for the May session.

## Understanding the MHT CET Application Process

The MHT CET application form is designed to capture your preferences for the examination. For students focusing solely on the May session (Session 2), the key is to ensure your selection aligns with this intention. The phrasing on the form can sometimes be a bit confusing, so close attention is crucial.

## Choosing Your Attempt

When filling out the MHT CET application, if you encounter options like "First Attempt" or "Both Attempts," and your goal is to appear *only* in the May session, selecting "First Attempt" is often the correct path, assuming the system then allows you to specify *which* session you mean. This option usually signifies you're registering for a single, chosen attempt, rather than committing to both. The term "First Attempt" in this context often refers to *your* first or only attempt, not necessarily the earliest chronological session offered by the examination body.

## Session-Specific Selection

Many official examination portals, including those managed by NTA or the State Common Entrance Test Cell, provide clear options for session selection. Look for radio buttons or dropdown menus that explicitly ask you to choose between "Session 1," "Session 2," or "Both Sessions." If such an option is present, you should select "Session 2" directly. This is the most straightforward way to ensure you are registered only for the May attempt.

## Verifying Your Choice

Before you finalize and submit your MHT CET application form, always review all the details you've entered. There is usually a summary page where you can check your selections. Confirm that your registration clearly indicates your intention to appear only for the May session (Session 2). If you have any doubts after reviewing, refer to the official MHT CET information brochure or contact the helpdesk provided on the official MHT CET portal for clarification.

Making the right selection during form filling will ensure a smooth examination experience for your MHT CET May session.
