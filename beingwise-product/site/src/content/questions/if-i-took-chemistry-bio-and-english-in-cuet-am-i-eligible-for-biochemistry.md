Welcome to BeingWise! If you're aiming for a B.Sc. (Hons.) in Biochemistry at Delhi University, understanding the precise subject requirements for the CUET UG exam is crucial. Let's clarify if your chosen subjects — Chemistry, Biology, and English — align with DU's eligibility criteria for this popular program.

## Key takeaways

*   Your current CUET subject combination (Chemistry, Biology, English) is *not* sufficient for B.Sc. (Hons.) Biochemistry at Delhi University.
*   DU mandates four specific subjects for B.Sc. (Hons.) Biochemistry: Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology/Biochemistry, and English.
*   Physics is a compulsory domain subject you would need to include in your CUET application.
*   Always refer to the official Delhi University Undergraduate Admission Bulletin for the most accurate and updated eligibility criteria.

## Understanding DU's Eligibility for B.Sc. (Hons.) Biochemistry

Delhi University has very specific subject combination requirements for admission to its B.Sc. (Hons.) Biochemistry program. These are designed to ensure that students have a strong foundational knowledge in all necessary scientific disciplines before entering such a specialized course.

Based on the official Delhi University guidelines, to be eligible for B.Sc. (Hons.) Biochemistry, candidates must have appeared in the CUET UG with the following subject combination:

*   **Physics:** This is a mandatory domain-specific subject.
*   **Chemistry:** Another mandatory domain-specific subject.
*   **Biology/Biotechnology/Biochemistry:** You must have taken one of these three domain-specific subjects.
*   **English:** This is a mandatory language subject from List A.

## Why Your Current Subjects Are Insufficient

You mentioned taking Chemistry, Biology, and English for your CUET. While these are certainly relevant subjects for Biochemistry, they unfortunately do not meet the complete set of requirements laid out by Delhi University. The critical missing piece in your selection is **Physics**.

DU's admission policy for this program clearly states that Physics is a compulsory domain subject. Without a valid score in Physics from your CUET UG examination, you would not be considered eligible for B.Sc. (Hons.) Biochemistry at Delhi University, even if you perform exceptionally well in Chemistry, Biology, and English.

## Planning Your CUET Subjects for DU

If B.Sc. (Hons.) Biochemistry at Delhi University is your primary goal, it's essential to select your CUET subjects carefully right from the start. When filling out your CUET application, ensure you include all the mandatory subjects as per DU's specific program requirements. For B.Sc. (Hons.) Biochemistry, this means:

1.  Physics
2.  Chemistry
3.  Biology OR Biotechnology OR Biochemistry
4.  English

It is always advisable to consult the latest official Delhi University Undergraduate Admission Bulletin or the NTA CUET information bulletin for the most up-to-date and precise details regarding subject mapping and eligibility criteria.

Making informed choices about your CUET subjects is the first step towards securing your desired spot in Delhi University. Good luck with your preparations!
