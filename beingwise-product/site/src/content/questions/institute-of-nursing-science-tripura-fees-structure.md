# Institute of Nursing Science Tripura Fees Structure

Understanding the financial commitment for higher education is a crucial step for students and parents. This guide provides a clear breakdown of the fees for the B.Sc. Nursing program at the Institute of Nursing Science, Tripura.

## Key Takeaways

*   Total one-time non-refundable fees amount to ₹2,510.
*   A refundable security deposit of ₹25,000 is collected at admission.
*   The annual recurring fee for the B.Sc. Nursing course is ₹1,04,400.
*   The total cost for the first year, including one-time and annual charges (excluding hostel and mess), is ₹1,31,910.

## One-Time Admission Charges (Non-Refundable)

When you first enroll in the B.Sc. Nursing program, there are a few charges that are paid only once and are not refundable. These cover initial administrative costs and student services.

*   Admission Fee: ₹2,000
*   Students Welfare Fund: ₹200
*   Registration Fee: ₹310

**Total Non-Refundable One-Time Charges: ₹2,510**

## Refundable Security Deposits

To ensure the proper use and maintenance of college facilities, the institute collects certain security deposits. These amounts are refundable at the end of your course, subject to the institution's terms and conditions.

*   Laboratory Security Fee: ₹15,000
*   Library Security Fee: ₹10,000

**Total Refundable Security Deposits: ₹25,000**

## Annual Recurring Fees

Each academic year, students are required to pay recurring fees that cover tuition and various operational expenses. These charges are essential for the smooth running of academic and extracurricular activities.

| Fee Type            | Amount (₹) |
| :------------------ | :--------- |
| Tuition Fee         | 99,000     |
| College Magazine    | 300        |
| Examination Fee     | 2,500      |
| Games               | 300        |
| Cultural Activities | 600        |
| Library             | 500        |
| Annual Charge       | 1,200      |

**Total Annual Recurring Fees: ₹1,04,400**

## Total First-Year Fees (Excluding Hostel & Mess)

Combining the one-time charges, refundable deposits, and the annual recurring fees, the total financial outlay for the first year of the B.Sc. Nursing program (before considering accommodation and food) is as follows:

*   Non-Refundable One-Time Charges: ₹2,510
*   Refundable Security Deposits: ₹25,000
*   Annual Recurring Fees: ₹1,04,400

**Grand Total for First Year (excluding hostel and mess): ₹1,31,910**

## Hostel and Mess Charges

For students requiring accommodation, the Institute of Nursing Science also specifies charges for hostel facilities and mess services. These are separate from the academic fees.

*   Hostel Charge: ₹24,000 per year
*   Mess Charge: ₹24,000 per year

These charges are typically paid annually alongside the other recurring fees.

## Payment Information

According to information available, the total tuition fee for the B.Sc. Nursing course can be settled in installments. Each installment is set at ₹60,000, providing flexibility for students and their families. For the most precise and up-to-date payment options and deadlines, it is always best to consult the official admissions office directly.

Planning your finances carefully will help ensure a smooth academic journey at the Institute of Nursing Science, Tripura.
