# I scored 37000 in the COMEDK exam. Can I get admission to SDMCET, Dharwad, CSE course in the 2nd and 3rd rounds?

Understanding your chances for admission to a competitive course like Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at a reputable institution like SDM College of Engineering & Technology (SDMCET), Dharwad, with a COMEDK rank of 37,000 is crucial. Let's explore the possibilities and provide clear guidance.

## Key Takeaways

*   Admission to SDMCET Dharwad's CSE program with a COMEDK rank of 37,000 is highly unlikely, even in later rounds.
*   Historically, CSE closing ranks at SDMCET Dharwad have been significantly lower, typically within the 15,000 to 18,000 range.
*   While later counselling rounds sometimes see minor rank adjustments, they are generally not substantial enough to bridge such a large gap.
*   Consider exploring other engineering branches like ECE or ISE at SDMCET, or CSE at other COMEDK-affiliated colleges with higher closing ranks.

## Understanding COMEDK Ranks and Cutoffs

COMEDK ranks are a reflection of your performance relative to all other candidates who appeared for the exam. Colleges set cutoff ranks for each course, which represent the last rank admitted to that program in a particular counselling round. For highly sought-after branches like CSE, especially in well-regarded colleges, these cutoffs tend to be very competitive (i.e., lower ranks).

Based on historical data for SDMCET, Dharwad, the closing ranks for its CSE program have consistently fallen within the 15,000 to 18,000 range. This means that candidates with ranks above this bracket have generally not secured admission to CSE at this college.

## Chances in Later Counselling Rounds

COMEDK counselling typically proceeds in multiple rounds. While it's true that the second and third rounds can sometimes offer a slight relaxation in cutoff ranks, this usually happens for a few reasons:

*   **Seat Vacancy:** If some candidates admitted in earlier rounds withdraw their admission or don't report to the college.
*   **Branch Preference Shifts:** If candidates opt for a different branch or college, freeing up seats.

However, the extent of this relaxation is generally limited. A rank difference of nearly 20,000 (from 18,000 to 37,000) is quite significant, making it extremely difficult to secure a CSE seat at SDMCET, Dharwad, even in subsequent rounds. While participation is always advised, setting realistic expectations is important.

## Exploring Alternative Options

Given the competitive nature of CSE at SDMCET, Dharwad, with your rank, it would be wise to consider other viable options:

### Other Branches at SDMCET, Dharwad

SDMCET offers other excellent engineering branches that might have higher closing ranks, making them more accessible with your COMEDK score. Branches like Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) or Information Science and Engineering (ISE) are often slightly less competitive than CSE but still offer strong career prospects and quality education. It's worth researching their previous year's closing ranks on the official COMEDK counselling portal.

### Other COMEDK Colleges for CSE

Many other colleges participate in COMEDK counselling. While SDMCET is a strong institution, several other colleges might offer CSE programs with closing ranks that align more closely with your 37,000 score. You should thoroughly research the previous year's cutoffs for CSE in various COMEDK-affiliated colleges to identify suitable alternatives. Look for colleges that consistently admitted students with ranks in the 30,000-40,000 range for CSE.

### State Counselling Quota

If you are eligible for Karnataka state quota seats, remember that admissions through the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) via KCET have different cutoff ranks. It's always beneficial to explore all available counselling avenues if you've appeared for multiple entrance exams.

While securing a CSE seat at SDMCET, Dharwad, with a COMEDK rank of 37,000 appears challenging, a strategic approach to counselling by exploring other branches and colleges can open up promising opportunities for your engineering education.
