# Can You Get an IIT Madras MBA Call with a 71.31 CAT Score?

It's great you're looking into an MBA at IIT Madras! Let's break down your profile and see how it aligns with the admissions criteria for their Department of Management Studies (DoMS).

## Key Takeaways

*   Your academic record from 10th grade through your B.E. is exceptionally strong.
*   A CAT percentile of 71.31 is significantly below the typical cutoff for IIT Madras DoMS.
*   While academic excellence can be a factor, it usually doesn't fully compensate for a much lower CAT score.
*   Retaking the CAT and aiming for a much higher percentile would greatly improve your chances.

## Understanding IIT Madras DoMS Admissions

IIT Madras's Department of Management Studies (DoMS) is a highly sought-after program. Admission to their MBA program primarily hinges on a strong CAT score, followed by an evaluation of academic performance, work experience (if any), and performance in the personal interview (PI) and written ability test (WAT) rounds.

For the general category, the CAT percentile cutoff for IIT Madras DoMS has historically been quite high. Based on past admission cycles, this figure has often been reported in the range of 96-98+ percentile. For instance, in previous years (e.g., 2018 and 2019), it was noted to be around 98.2 percentile for the open category. Your current CAT score of 71.31 is considerably below this benchmark.

## Your Strong Academic Profile

Your academic record is truly impressive:

*   **10th Grade:** 98.6%
*   **12th Grade:** 90.75%
*   **B.E. (up to 7th sem):** CGPA 9.16

These excellent scores demonstrate a consistent track record of academic achievement, which is a significant positive. Top-tier institutions like IIT Madras value strong academics as they indicate a candidate's ability to handle rigorous coursework. However, for MBA admissions at IITs, the CAT percentile often acts as the initial filter.

## The Role of CAT Percentile

The Common Admission Test (CAT) is a crucial component of the selection process for most IIMs and other top management institutes, including the IIT DoMS programs. It serves as an initial screening tool to shortlist candidates for further rounds.

While some institutes might offer slight relaxations or give more weight to academic profiles for candidates with exceptional scores (e.g., above 90% in graduation), even in such cases, there's usually a minimum CAT percentile threshold that must be met. The information available suggests that even with outstanding academic scores, a CAT percentile in the 90s is often still expected to be considered for an interview call at IIT Madras DoMS.

## Next Steps and Recommendations

Given your current profile, securing an interview call from IIT Madras DoMS with a 71.31 CAT percentile is highly unlikely for the upcoming admission cycle. Your academic background is stellar, but the CAT score will likely be a limiting factor.

If IIT Madras DoMS remains a top choice for you, the most effective strategy would be to retake the CAT. With your strong academic foundation, dedicated preparation could very well help you achieve a significantly higher percentile, opening up opportunities at top management programs.
