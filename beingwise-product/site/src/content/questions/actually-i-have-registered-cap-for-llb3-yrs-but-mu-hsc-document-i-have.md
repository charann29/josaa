# Actually I Have Registered CAP for LLB 3 Yrs But My HSC Document I Have Uploaded From DigiLocker as My Original One Is Misplaced My College.. It Will Take 15 Days So I Have Re-edited My Application Form... What Will Happen?

It sounds like you're navigating a common situation during the CAP registration for the 3-year LLB course – dealing with a misplaced original document and using DigiLocker. Let's break down what this means for your application.

## Key Takeaways

*   **DigiLocker documents are generally accepted:** Most official bodies consider documents from DigiLocker valid as they are digitally verified.
*   **Timely updates are crucial:** If you re-edit your form, ensure it's done before the deadline and updated with the original document once you receive it.
*   **Stay vigilant on official channels:** Regularly check the official CAP portal and your email for any communications or requests from the admission authority.
*   **Accuracy is paramount:** As long as your details are correct and documents are valid, you should not face major issues.

## Using DigiLocker Documents for CAP Registration

When applying for the 3-year LLB CAP registration, using your HSC document from DigiLocker is usually acceptable. DigiLocker provides digitally verified documents, which are widely recognized as authentic by most authorities in India. This means that uploading your HSC certificate from DigiLocker, even if your original physical copy is currently unavailable, is generally considered valid.

However, it's always a good practice to ensure the document you've uploaded from DigiLocker is perfectly clear, legible, and accurately reflects all your details. Any discrepancies could lead to questions later in the process.

## Re-editing Your Application Form

You've made a sensible decision by re-editing your application form to indicate the situation regarding your original HSC document. This proactive step shows your awareness and helps keep the admission authority informed. When you re-edit, make sure to:

*   **Review all details carefully:** Double-check every field, not just the document section, to ensure no unintended changes were made.
*   **Upload properly:** Confirm that the DigiLocker document is uploaded correctly and completely.
*   **Adhere to deadlines:** Ensure all edits are finalized and submitted before the official application deadline for the CAP registration.

## What Happens When the Original Document Arrives?

Once you receive your original HSC document, it's important to update your application form immediately if the portal allows for such changes. Even if the DigiLocker version was accepted initially, the admission authority might eventually require the physical original for verification. Keep a close watch on your application portal and emails. The authority might:

*   **Request the physical copy:** They may set a specific deadline for you to submit the original document for physical scrutiny.
*   **Hold your admission:** In some cases, if the original document is a mandatory requirement for final admission and you cannot provide it within their stipulated timeframe, your admission might be put on hold until verification is complete.

## Staying Informed and Proactive

Throughout this process, the most important thing is to stay informed and proactive. Regularly check the official CAP portal for any announcements, updates, or specific instructions related to document verification. Also, monitor your email inbox (including spam folders) for direct communications from the admission authority. If you have any doubts, look for official contact information on the CAP portal to clarify your situation.

By taking these steps, you are demonstrating responsibility and ensuring that your application progresses smoothly despite the temporary absence of your physical original document.
