# What is the Annual Fee for MDS in Orthodontics at KLE Belgaum?

Considering a Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) in Orthodontics at KLE University in Belgaum is a significant step, and understanding the financial commitment is crucial for prospective students and their families.

## Key Takeaways

*   The primary annual tuition for MDS Orthodontics at KLE Belgaum is ₹9,00,000.
*   An additional annual development fee of ₹1,00,000 applies.
*   There's a one-time, non-refundable eligibility fee of ₹2,000.
*   A refundable caution deposit of ₹2,000 is also required.
*   The total initial payment for the first year amounts to ₹10,04,000.
*   The program has a very limited intake of only four students.

## Understanding the Fee Structure

When planning for your MDS in Orthodontics at KLE Belgaum, it's helpful to break down the different components of the fees. The largest part is the annual tuition, which stands at ₹9,00,000. On top of this, there's a development fee of ₹1,00,000 each year, contributing to the institution's infrastructure and resources.

### One-time Charges

Beyond the annual fees, there are a couple of one-time charges to be aware of. An eligibility fee of ₹2,000 is non-refundable and covers the administrative costs associated with verifying your application and qualifications. Additionally, a caution deposit of ₹2,000 is collected. This amount is refundable once you successfully complete your course, provided there are no outstanding dues or damages.

### Total First-Year Outlay

Combining all these figures, the total payment required for the first year of the MDS Orthodontics program at KLE Belgaum is ₹10,04,000. This includes the annual tuition, annual development fee, the non-refundable eligibility fee, and the refundable caution money.

## Program Intake

It's important to note the competitive nature of this program. KLE Belgaum has a very limited intake for its MDS in Orthodontics, with only four seats available. This highlights the need for a strong academic record and thorough preparation for the admissions process.
