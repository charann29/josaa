# Exam of MHT CET will be online or offline?

For students aspiring to pursue engineering and other professional courses in Maharashtra, the MHT CET is a crucial state-level entrance examination. Understanding its format is key to effective preparation. This guide clarifies the MHT CET examination mode and other important details.

## Key Takeaways

*   The MHT CET is consistently conducted in an online, Computer-Based Test (CBT) format.
*   Candidates must appear at designated exam centers to take the online test.
*   The State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra State, is the official body responsible for conducting the exam.
*   Application forms are submitted online via the official MHT CET portal.

## Understanding the MHT CET Exam Mode

The MHT CET examination is held in an **online mode**. This means it is a Computer-Based Test (CBT) where candidates answer questions on a computer screen at an allocated examination center. While the exam is digital, it is not a remote proctored test that can be taken from home; physical presence at a test center is mandatory.

This online format has been consistently adopted for the MHT CET, ensuring a standardized and efficient testing environment for all applicants.

To give you a clearer picture of the MHT CET, here's an overview of its main features:

| Feature                  | Detail                                      |

| Full Exam Name           | Maharashtra Common Entrance Test            |
| Short Exam Name          | MHT CET                                     |
| Conducting Body          | State Common Entrance Test Cell - Maharashtra State |
| Frequency of Conduct     | Once a year                                 |
| Exam Level               | State Level Exam                            |
| Application Mode         | Online                                      |
| Exam Mode                | Online (Computer-Based Test at centers)     |
| Counselling Mode         | Online                                      |

## MHT CET Application Process and Important Dates

The application process for MHT CET is entirely online. Aspiring candidates need to visit the official MHT CET website to register and fill out the application form. For the 2022 admissions cycle, the application window for B.Tech courses opened on February 10. The deadline for submitting the application without a late fee was March 31, with an extended period until April 7 for those applying with a late fee. It is always advisable to check the official MHT CET portal for the most current dates and deadlines for each academic year.

## MHT CET Exam Pattern at a Glance

The structure of the MHT CET is designed to assess a candidate's proficiency in core science and mathematics subjects. Here are the key aspects of the exam pattern:

*   **Total Questions:** 150 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
*   **Sections:** The exam is divided into two main papers:
    *   **Paper 1:** Physics and Chemistry (100 questions total)
    *   **Paper 2:** Mathematics (50 questions total)
*   **Total Duration:** 180 minutes (3 hours)
*   **Marking Scheme:**
    *   Mathematics: 2 marks for each correct answer.
    *   Physics and Chemistry: 1 mark for each correct answer.
*   **Negative Marking:** There is **no negative marking** for incorrect answers, which can be a strategic advantage for test-takers.
*   **Exam Language:** Physics and Chemistry sections are available in English, Marathi, and Urdu. The Mathematics section is available in English only.

## Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the MHT CET, candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination. The qualifying subjects must include Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Specific percentage requirements or other conditions might apply, so always refer to the official information released by the State Common Entrance Test Cell for the most precise and up-to-date eligibility criteria.

Staying informed about these details directly from the official MHT CET portal is the best way to ensure a smooth application and examination experience.
