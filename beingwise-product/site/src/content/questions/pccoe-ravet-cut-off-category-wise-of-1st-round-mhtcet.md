# PCCOE Ravet Cut-Off Category Wise of 1st Round MHT CET

Understanding the MHT CET cut-offs is crucial for aspiring engineering students aiming for admission to top institutions like Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE), Ravet, Pune. This guide provides a detailed look at the Round 1 cut-off percentiles and All India Ranks for various engineering branches and categories.

## Key Takeaways

*   **High Competition:** PCCOE Ravet consistently sees high demand, reflected in its competitive cut-off percentiles and ranks across all branches.
*   **Category-Specific Differences:** Cut-offs vary significantly between the General category and reserved categories like OBC Home State, offering different admission thresholds.
*   **Branch Popularity:** Core branches like Computer Engineering and Information Technology typically have higher cut-offs compared to others, indicating their popularity.
*   **Percentile vs. Rank:** Both MHT CET percentile and All India Rank are vital metrics for assessing your chances of admission.

## Understanding MHT CET Cut-offs

The MHT CET (Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test) is a state-level entrance examination for admission to engineering and pharmacy courses in Maharashtra. The cut-off is the minimum percentile or rank required for admission to a particular college and branch. These cut-offs are determined by factors such as the number of applicants, the difficulty of the exam, and the availability of seats.

For PCCOE Ravet, the MHT CET Round 1 cut-offs provide a clear picture of the competition. For the General category, the MHT CET cut-off percentile typically ranged from approximately 90.59% to 96.99% across different engineering disciplines.

## Category-Wise Percentile Overview

Admission to PCCOE Ravet is highly sought after, and the cut-offs reflect this demand. While the General category has a broad range, reserved categories like OBC Home State (OBCS) also have specific percentile requirements.

For the OBC Home State category, the MHT CET Round 1 cut-off percentile generally fell between 95.29% and 95.88%. This indicates that even within reserved categories, a strong performance in MHT CET is essential for securing a seat.

## All India Ranks for Popular Branches

Beyond percentiles, All India Ranks (AIR) provide a more granular view of admissions. These ranks are crucial for understanding your position relative to other candidates nationwide. Here's a breakdown of the closing All India Ranks for some of the most popular engineering branches at PCCOE Ravet in the first round of admissions:

| Engineering Branch                      | General Category (AIR) | OBC Home State Category (AIR) |
| :-------------------------------------- | :--------------------- | :---------------------------- |
| Computer Engineering                    | Around 10473           | Around 12414                  |
| Information Technology                  | Around 11024           | Around 12276                  |
| Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering | Around 11379           | Around 14354                  |
| Mechanical Engineering                  | Around 17550           | Around 20526                  |
| Civil Engineering                       | Around 32188           | Around 36411                  |

These figures highlight the intense competition for Computer Engineering and Information Technology, which consistently have lower (better) ranks. Branches like Mechanical and Civil Engineering, while still competitive, show relatively higher ranks, indicating a slightly broader admission window.

## What These Ranks Mean for Aspirants

If your MHT CET All India Rank is close to or better than these figures, your chances of securing a seat in PCCOE Ravet are significantly higher. It's important to remember that cut-offs can fluctuate slightly each year based on various factors. Candidates should always refer to the official MHT CET counselling portal for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding cut-offs and admission rounds.

We hope this detailed overview of PCCOE Ravet's MHT CET Round 1 cut-offs helps you in planning your engineering admission journey.
