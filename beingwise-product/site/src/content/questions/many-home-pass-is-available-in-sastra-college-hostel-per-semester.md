This guide provides clarity on the leave policies for female students residing in the Sastra University hostels, helping you understand how often you can visit home during a semester.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Standard Home Leave:** Girls are generally permitted to go home twice per semester.
*   **Medical Exceptions:** An additional third leave may be granted for health-related reasons.
*   **Festival Breaks:** Designated open holidays for major festivals like Diwali and Pongal allow students to go home.
*   **Weekend Outings:** Sunday outings are possible if parents arrange for pick-up and drop-off by 6 PM.
*   **Special Requests:** For urgent needs outside these provisions, students can approach the Dean of Student Affairs.

## Standard Home Leave Policy

Sastra University's hostel policy for female students typically allows for two home visits per academic semester. These are the standard provisions for students to return to their families.

## Provisions for Medical Needs

Understanding that health can be unpredictable, Sastra University makes an allowance for medical emergencies. If a student needs to go home due to a health issue, an additional third leave may be approved within the semester. This provision is designed to support students' well-being when necessary.

## Festival and Open Holidays

Beyond the standard home leaves, the university observes major Indian festivals. During significant holidays such as Diwali and Pongal, the hostels typically have 'open holidays,' which means students are free to go home and spend time with their families. These are separate from the two standard leaves and offer additional opportunities for students to visit home.

## Weekend Outing Options

For those who wish to step out more frequently, Sastra University offers a Sunday outing option. Female students can leave the campus on Sundays, provided their parents personally pick them up and ensure they are dropped back at the hostel by 6:00 PM on the same day. This arrangement allows for regular weekend breaks while maintaining student safety and hostel regulations.

## Special Leave Requests

There might be unforeseen circumstances or compelling personal reasons that require a student to take leave outside the standard provisions. In such cases, Sastra University encourages students to formally approach the Dean of Student Affairs. Students can personally meet the Dean to explain their situation and request special permission for leave, which will be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Understanding these guidelines can help female students and their parents plan effectively for their time at Sastra University's hostels.
