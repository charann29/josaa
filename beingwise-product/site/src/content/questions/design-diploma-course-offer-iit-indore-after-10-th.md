# What Design Courses Does IIT Indore Offer After 10th?

It's common for students to start thinking about their career paths early, even right after Class 10. When it comes to design education at a prestigious institution like IIT Indore, it's important to understand the entry requirements and available programs.

## Key Takeaways

*   IIT Indore does not offer any design diploma courses directly after Class 10.
*   All design programs at IIT Indore are at the undergraduate level, requiring completion of Class 12.
*   The primary undergraduate design program is the Bachelor of Design (B.Des).
*   Admission to the B.Des program typically involves a national-level entrance examination.

## Understanding Design Education Pathways

Many students are eager to jumpstart their design careers, but higher education institutions, particularly the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), structure their programs to build upon a strong foundation of secondary education. Design courses at IIT Indore are designed for students who have completed their Class 12 education, ensuring they have the necessary academic background for the rigorous curriculum.

## Bachelor of Design (B.Des) at IIT Indore

The flagship design program offered at IIT Indore is the Bachelor of Design (B.Des). This is a comprehensive undergraduate degree program that typically spans four years. It's tailored for students who have completed their Class 12 and are looking to delve deep into various aspects of design, including product design, visual communication, interaction design, and more.

## Admission Process for B.Des

To gain admission to the B.Des program at IIT Indore, aspiring designers must first successfully complete their Class 12 examinations. Following this, they need to appear for and qualify in a specific national-level entrance examination. For undergraduate design programs at IITs, this is typically the Undergraduate Common Entrance Exam for Design (UCEED). The official UCEED portal provides all the details regarding eligibility, application dates, and examination structure.

After clearing UCEED, candidates participate in a joint seat allocation process, often managed through platforms like the official JoSAA portal, to secure a seat in their desired IIT based on their ranks and preferences.

## Planning Your Design Journey

If you are currently in Class 10 and aspire to pursue design at IIT Indore, your immediate focus should be on excelling in your Class 11 and 12 studies. This academic preparation is crucial for both meeting the eligibility criteria and performing well in the competitive entrance examinations. Alongside your school studies, you can start exploring different design fields, developing your creative skills, and building a foundational understanding of design principles.

While IIT Indore doesn't offer design diplomas after Class 10, a clear path exists for Class 12 graduates to pursue a rewarding design career there.
