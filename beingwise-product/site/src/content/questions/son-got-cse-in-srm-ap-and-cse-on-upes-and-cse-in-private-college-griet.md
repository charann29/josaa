# My Son Got CSE in SRM AP, UPES, and GRIET – Which One for Guaranteed Placement?

Choosing the right B.Tech CSE program is a significant decision, especially when aiming for strong placement outcomes. Let's break down the options for your son to help you make an informed choice.

## Key Takeaways

*   **SRM University, AP** boasts a 100% placement rate for B.Tech CSE with a high average package.
*   **UPES Dehradun** offers good average placements, with a notable higher average for its top students.
*   **GRIET Hyderabad** shows a very high individual package and a substantial number of offers, but also a wider range in salaries.
*   Beyond just numbers, consider factors like curriculum, faculty, and campus environment.

## SRM University, Andhra Pradesh

SRM University, AP, has quickly established itself as a strong contender in engineering education. For its B.Tech Computer Science Engineering program, the university reports an impressive 100% placement rate. This indicates that all eligible CSE graduates from the latest batch secured job offers.

The highest package achieved by a B.Tech CSE student at SRM AP recently was a remarkable ₹50 LPA. The average salary package for CSE graduates stood at ₹9.90 LPA. These figures suggest a robust placement ecosystem and strong industry connections, providing excellent opportunities for students.

## UPES, Dehradun

UPES, located in Dehradun, is known for its industry-focused programs. For B.Tech CSE, the university reports a highest package of ₹33 LPA. The overall average placement package for CSE students is ₹8.59 LPA. 

It's also worth noting that the top 10% of placed students at UPES secured average packages ranging from ₹15 LPA to ₹17 LPA. This highlights that high-achieving students can attract significantly better offers, indicating a merit-based reward system within their placement process.

## G. Pulla Reddy Engineering College (GRIET), Hyderabad

GRIET, an institution affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad, is a well-regarded private college in the region. The highest package secured by a GRIET student recently was an outstanding ₹51.65 LPA, reportedly through Google. However, it's also important to note that the lowest package recorded, as per their official data, was ₹3 LPA. This indicates a broader spectrum of offers.

In terms of volume, GRIET's placement report for the 2025 batch (which typically refers to students who will graduate in 2025, but are placed in the preceding year) shows 1016 offers made by 86 companies, with 382 students successfully placed. This suggests a high number of companies recruiting from the campus and a good number of students securing positions.

## Making Your Decision

When comparing these three institutions, consider the following:

*   **Placement Rate vs. Package Range:** SRM AP's 100% placement rate is very reassuring, with a strong average. GRIET shows an exceptional highest package but also a wider range, including a lower end. UPES offers a solid average, with top performers achieving significantly more.
*   **Location and Affiliation:** SRM AP and UPES are private universities with their own degree-granting authority. GRIET is affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad, which can be a factor for some students and parents.
*   **Student Feedback:** While we cannot provide specific student feedback, it is always advisable to try and connect with current students or alumni from each college to gather firsthand perspectives on campus life, faculty quality, and overall experience, which can greatly influence a student's success.

Ultimately, the 'guaranteed placement' aspect is complex. While all three colleges demonstrate strong placement records, a student's individual effort, skills, and academic performance will always play the most crucial role in securing a desirable job. Encourage your son to research the specific CSE curriculum, faculty expertise, and opportunities for projects or internships at each institution.
