# Can I get admission into MBA college after doing graduation in Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Open University at Telangana state? I want to do MBA in IIT Bombay... can it possible to do so?

It's a great ambition to pursue an MBA, especially from a prestigious institution like IIT Bombay. Let's break down the possibilities and requirements for students with a degree from Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Open University.

## Key Takeaways

*   A four-year bachelor's degree or a master's degree is generally required for IIT Bombay's MBA program.
*   If your bachelor's degree is three years, a two-year master's degree from a recognized university is essential.
*   Degrees from UGC-recognized open universities are typically valid for further studies.
*   Meeting the minimum percentage criteria (usually 60%, with relaxations for reserved categories) is crucial.

## Understanding IIT Bombay's MBA Eligibility

IIT Bombay's Shailesh J. Mehta School of Management (SJMSOM) has specific eligibility criteria for its Master of Business Administration (MBA) program. The primary requirement is a strong academic background. Generally, candidates need to have completed a four-year bachelor's degree or a master's degree from a university recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

For most applicants, a minimum aggregate of 60% marks is required in their qualifying degree. There's a 5% relaxation in this percentage for candidates belonging to Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), and Persons with Disabilities (PwD) categories, meaning they would need 55%.

## Addressing the Three-Year Degree Scenario

Many open universities, including Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Open University, offer three-year bachelor's degrees. If you hold a three-year bachelor's degree, you will typically need to complete a two-year master's degree from a UGC-recognized university to become eligible for the MBA program at IIT Bombay. It's important that this master's degree also meets the required aggregate percentage for your category.

## Recognition of Degrees from Open Universities

Degrees awarded by Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Open University, as long as the university is recognized by the UGC, are generally considered valid for pursuing higher education, including regular mode MBA programs at top institutions. The key is the recognition status of the university itself. As long as your undergraduate or postgraduate degree comes from a UGC-recognized institution, it should not be an impediment to your application.

## Beyond Academic Eligibility: The CAT Exam

While academic eligibility is the first step, admission to IIT Bombay's MBA program is highly competitive and also requires a strong score in the Common Admission Test (CAT). You will need to appear for the CAT exam and achieve a competitive percentile to be considered for further stages of the admission process, such as Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI).

## Next Steps for Aspiring Candidates

To confirm the most up-to-date and precise eligibility criteria, it is always recommended to refer to the official website of the Shailesh J. Mehta School of Management, IIT Bombay. This will provide you with the exact details for the specific admission cycle you are targeting.

With careful planning and strong academic performance, pursuing an MBA at IIT Bombay is a definite possibility.
