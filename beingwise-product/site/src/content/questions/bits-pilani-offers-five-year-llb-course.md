# Does BITS Pilani Offer a Five-Year LLB Course?

Many aspiring law students wonder if the prestigious BITS Pilani institution offers legal education. While BITS Pilani itself is renowned for engineering and sciences, it's important to understand the offerings of its affiliated law school.

## Key Takeaways

*   BITS Law School, Mumbai, offers five-year integrated BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons) programs.
*   These programs are full-time and designed for undergraduate students.
*   Admission requires a minimum of 45% in Class 12 and a valid score from specific law entrance exams.
*   The law school operates independently but is part of the BITS ecosystem.

## Understanding BITS Law School, Mumbai

It's crucial to distinguish between BITS Pilani and BITS Law School. While BITS Pilani is famous for its engineering and science campuses across India, BITS Law School is a dedicated institution located in Mumbai, focusing specifically on legal education. It is an integral part of the BITS educational framework, bringing the same commitment to excellence in a new domain.

## Integrated Law Programs Offered

BITS Law School, Mumbai, provides two distinct five-year, full-time integrated undergraduate law programs:

*   **BA LLB (Hons):** This program combines a Bachelor of Arts degree with a Bachelor of Laws, offering a broad foundation in humanities alongside legal studies. It's ideal for students interested in a holistic understanding of society and law.
*   **BBA LLB (Hons):** This program integrates a Bachelor of Business Administration with a Bachelor of Laws, suitable for students who wish to combine business acumen with legal expertise. It prepares graduates for roles in corporate law, business consulting, and related fields.

These integrated courses allow students to earn both an undergraduate degree and a law degree over five years, providing a comprehensive and efficient path to a legal career.

## Admission Criteria and Entrance Exams

To be eligible for the BA LLB (Hons) or BBA LLB (Hons) programs at BITS Law School, Mumbai, candidates must meet specific academic and entrance examination requirements:

### Academic Qualification

Applicants need to have successfully completed their Class 12 (or an equivalent examination) from a recognized board. A cumulative score of at least 45% in this examination is mandatory.

### Entrance Examinations

Prospective students must appear for and obtain a valid score in one of the following recognized law entrance tests:

*   BITS Law Admissions Test
*   Common Law Admission Test (CLAT)
*   Symbiosis Law Admission Test (SLAT)
*   Law National Aptitude Test (LNAT)
*   All India Law Entrance Test (AILET)
*   Maharashtra Common Entrance Test for Law (MHCET Law)

The selection process will consider scores from these examinations to assess a candidate's aptitude for legal studies.

## Why Choose BITS Law School?

Choosing BITS Law School means opting for an institution that aims to uphold the academic rigor and innovative spirit associated with the BITS name. The integrated programs are designed to create well-rounded legal professionals equipped with interdisciplinary knowledge, critical thinking skills, and a strong ethical foundation. With its Mumbai location, students also benefit from proximity to major legal and corporate hubs, offering valuable internship and career opportunities.

BITS Law School, Mumbai, offers a compelling pathway for students aspiring to a career in law through its well-structured, five-year integrated programs.
