# Looking for PGD Data Science Fees at Wisdom College for Creativity and Design

Considering a Post Graduate Diploma (PGD) in Data Science can be a great step for your career. If you're exploring this option at Wisdom College for Creativity and Design, understanding the associated costs is naturally a key part of your decision-making.

## Key Takeaways

*   The PGD in Data Science at Wisdom College for Creativity and Design is a 9-month program.
*   Official fee details for this course are not published on the college's website.
*   Based on various reports, the program's fee is estimated to be around INR 1,20,000.
*   It's always best to directly contact the college for the most current and accurate fee information.

## Program Overview: PGD in Data Science

Wisdom College for Creativity and Design offers a Post Graduate Diploma in Data Science. According to their official brochure, this is a concise, focused program designed to equip students with essential data science skills. The duration of the program is 9 months, suggesting an intensive curriculum aimed at fast-tracking students into the field.

## Understanding the Fee Structure

When planning for higher education, fees are a significant consideration. For the PGD in Data Science at Wisdom College for Creativity and Design, the official website does not publicly display the fee structure for any of its courses. This means prospective students won't find this information readily available online.

### Estimated Fees

While the college does not publish its fees, information gathered from various sources indicates that the fee for the PGD Data Science program is approximately INR 1,20,000. It's important to remember that this figure is an estimate based on external reports and not an officially confirmed amount from the college itself.

## How to Get Official Fee Information

Since the official fee structure isn't available on the college's website, the most reliable way to get accurate and up-to-date information is to contact Wisdom College for Creativity and Design directly. You can typically do this through their admissions office or by checking their official contact details provided on their website. They can provide precise details regarding tuition fees, payment schedules, and any other associated costs.

Making an informed decision about your education involves looking at all aspects, including the financial commitment. Always verify fee details directly with the institution.
