# What's your highest package in 2020-21?

Understanding the placement landscape is crucial when choosing an engineering college. Let's delve into the impressive placement performance of CVR College of Engineering, Hyderabad, particularly for the 2020-21 academic year.

## Key Takeaways

*   CVR College of Engineering achieved over 1300 placements in the 2020-21 academic year, a new institutional record.
*   The highest salary package offered during this period was a remarkable Rs. 30.5 Lakhs.
*   More than 120 companies actively recruited from the campus.
*   A significant number of students secured high-value offers, with over 60 students receiving packages above Rs. 8 Lakhs.

## A Record-Breaking Year for Placements

The 2020-21 academic year marked a significant milestone for CVR College of Engineering, Hyderabad. The institution successfully secured over 1300 job placements for its students, setting a new record for the college. This achievement is particularly noteworthy as it represents the highest placement count among its peer institutions.

## Impressive Highest Salary Package

During the 2020-21 placement drive, CVR College of Engineering saw its students receive highly competitive salary offers. The top package reached an outstanding Rs. 30.5 Lakhs per annum. What's even more impressive is that multiple students were able to secure this exceptional offer, demonstrating the high caliber of graduates from the college.

## Broad Spectrum of High-Value Offers

Beyond the highest package, a substantial number of students at CVR College of Engineering secured attractive salary offers. Over 60 students received annual packages exceeding Rs. 8 Lakhs. Furthermore, more than 300 offers were for salaries above Rs. 5 Lakhs per annum, indicating a robust placement scenario across various salary brackets.

## Leading the State in Placements

CVR College of Engineering has established itself as a frontrunner in engineering placements within the state. Its performance in 2020-21, particularly in terms of the number of high-salary offers and the overall placement percentage, positions it as a leading institution. The active participation of over 120 companies in the recruitment process further underscores the industry's confidence in the college's talent pool.

These figures highlight CVR College of Engineering's strong commitment to student career success and its ability to attract top recruiters, offering students excellent opportunities.
