# I Have 71% in 10th, 53% in 12th, and 88% in B.Com. With a 90 Percentile in CAT and SC Category, Can I Get into SJMSOM IIT Bombay or IIT Delhi?

It's natural to aim for top institutions like SJMSOM IIT Bombay or IIT Delhi. Let's break down your profile and explore your chances for admission to these and other reputable MBA programs.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your academic percentages in 10th and 12th standard are a significant factor in MBA admissions, especially for top IITs.
*   While your B.Com score is excellent and your CAT percentile is strong, the overall profile is considered.
*   For SJMSOM IIT Bombay and IIT Delhi, even for SC candidates, the CAT percentile cut-offs are typically very high, often above 92-93 percentile.
*   Many other excellent B-schools offer great MBA/PGDM programs and might be a better fit for your profile.

## Understanding IIT B-School Admissions

IITs offering MBA programs, such as SJMSOM IIT Bombay and DMS IIT Delhi, are highly competitive. Their selection process typically weighs multiple factors, including:

*   **CAT Score:** This is usually the primary shortlisting criterion.
*   **Academic Profile:** Performance in 10th, 12th, and undergraduate degrees carries significant weight. A consistent academic record is often preferred.
*   **Work Experience:** While not always mandatory, relevant work experience can strengthen an application.
*   **Diversity Factors:** Category (like SC), gender, and academic background can play a role, but rarely override significant gaps in other areas for the very top institutions.
*   **Personal Interview (PI) and Written Ability Test (WAT):** These are crucial stages for shortlisted candidates.

### The Role of Academic Scores

For premier institutions, academic consistency is highly valued. While your 88% in B.Com is commendable, your 10th (71%) and particularly your 12th (53%) percentages might pose a challenge for the very top IITs. Many top B-schools have internal cut-offs or weightages for past academic performance, and lower scores in 10th and 12th can sometimes reduce your overall composite score, even with a good CAT percentile.

## CAT Percentile for Top IITs (SC Category)

Based on historical trends, the CAT percentile cut-offs for SJMSOM IIT Bombay and DMS IIT Delhi are extremely high. For general category candidates, it often hovers around 98-99 percentile. While there is a relaxation for SC candidates, the cut-offs are still quite stringent, typically ranging from 92-93 percentile or even higher for SJMSOM. With a 90 percentile, while strong, it falls just short of the usual competitive range for these specific IITs, even with your SC category.

## Exploring Other Excellent MBA/PGDM Options

It's important not to limit your aspirations to just a couple of institutions. Many other highly reputed B-schools across India offer excellent MBA/PGDM programs and could be a great fit for your profile. Your 90 percentile in CAT, combined with your excellent B.Com score and SC category, opens doors to several quality institutions. Consider exploring programs at:

*   **Newer IIMs:** IIM Visakhapatnam, IIM Nagpur, IIM Sambalpur, IIM Sirmaur, IIM Jammu, IIM Amritsar. These IIMs are growing rapidly and offer strong curricula and placements.
*   **Other IIT B-Schools:** DoMS, IIT Roorkee. While still competitive, their cut-offs might be slightly more accessible than SJMSOM or DMS Delhi for some profiles.
*   **Reputable Private B-Schools:** Great Lakes Institute of Management, Chennai, is highly regarded. Also consider institutions like IISWBM Kolkata.

These institutions provide robust management education and can lead to successful careers. It's advisable to research their specific admission criteria, including academic cut-offs and weightages, and their placement reports.

Always ensure you check the official admission brochures of each institution for the most accurate and up-to-date information on their selection process and criteria for your category.
