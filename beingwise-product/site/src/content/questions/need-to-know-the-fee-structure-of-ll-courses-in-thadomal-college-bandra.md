# Need to Know the Fee Structure of All Courses in Thadomal College Bandra?

Understanding the financial commitment for higher education is a crucial step for aspiring students and their families. This guide provides a clear overview of the fee structure at Thadomal Shahani Engineering College (TSEC) in Bandra, Mumbai, helping you plan your academic journey effectively.

## Key Takeaways

*   The total first-year fees for B.Tech courses at Thadomal Shahani Engineering College amount to approximately ₹1,89,103.
*   This includes various components such as tuition, college development, university fees, and a one-time insurance charge.
*   Students from boards other than the Maharashtra State Board (HSC) will need to pay an additional Mumbai University Eligibility Fee.
*   The estimated total fees for the entire four-year B.Tech program are around ₹7.58 lakhs.

## Understanding the First-Year Fee Components

When you enroll in a B.Tech program at Thadomal Shahani Engineering College, the first-year fees are broken down into several parts. These cover various aspects of your education and administrative processes. It's important to be aware of each component to understand the total cost.

Here’s a detailed look at the approximate fees for the first year:

| Fee Component                  | Amount (₹) |
| :----------------------------- | :--------- |
| Tuition Fees                   | 1,66,666   |
| College Development Fees       | 18,334     |
| University Enrolment Fee       | 200        |
| Marksheet Verification Fee     | 500        |
| University Other Fee           | 556        |
| B.U. Exam Fees (Sem I & II)    | 2,146      |
| Insurance (one-time for 4 years) | 701        |
| **Total First-Year Fees**      | **1,89,103** |

## Additional Fees for Specific Candidates

For students who have completed their qualifying examination (e.g., 12th standard) from a board other than the Maharashtra State Board (HSC), there is an additional charge. These candidates are required to pay a Mumbai University Eligibility Fee to be enrolled. This fee is a one-time payment and is separate from the regular first-year fees mentioned above.

*   **Mumbai University Eligibility Fees (for non-HSC candidates):** ₹300

This additional amount will be added to your total first-year payment if applicable.

## Estimated Total Fees for the Four-Year B.Tech Program

While the first-year fees give you an immediate picture, it's helpful to have an estimate of the total cost for the entire duration of your B.Tech degree. Based on the first-year structure, the approximate total fees for all four years of any B.Tech course at Thadomal Shahani Engineering College are around ₹7.58 lakhs. This figure provides a general guide for long-term financial planning.

## Important Considerations

Fee structures can sometimes be subject to minor adjustments by the college or university. While the figures provided here are based on current information, it's always a good practice to confirm the exact amounts directly with the college's admissions or accounts department during your application process. This ensures you have the most up-to-date and accurate financial details.

Planning your finances carefully is an important part of preparing for your engineering education.
