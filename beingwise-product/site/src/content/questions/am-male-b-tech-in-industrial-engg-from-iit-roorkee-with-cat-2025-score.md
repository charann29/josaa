# I Am Male, B.Tech IIT Roorkee (Industrial Engineering), CAT 2025: 98.89 Percentile – What Are My Chances in Top 10 B-Schools?

With a strong academic background from IIT Roorkee and an impressive CAT 2025 score of 98.89 percentile, your profile is well-positioned for many of India's leading business schools. Let's explore your prospects across various institutions.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your IIT background significantly boosts your profile, especially for institutions that consider academic diversity.
*   While 98.89 percentile is excellent, the very top IIMs (A, B, C) often see cut-offs closer to 99+ for general male engineers.
*   You have strong prospects for interview calls from IIM Lucknow, IIM Kozhikode, SPJIMR, MDI Gurgaon, and DMS IIT Delhi.
*   Thorough preparation for the Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds will be crucial for converting calls into admissions.

## Understanding Top IIMs: Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Calcutta (ABC)

For IIM Ahmedabad and IIM Bangalore, the competition for general male engineers is exceptionally high, often requiring CAT percentiles very close to or above 99. Your 98.89 percentile, while outstanding, is just below this typical threshold. However, your IIT Roorkee background in Industrial Engineering is a significant asset that could make your profile stand out during the shortlisting process, potentially leading to a borderline interview call or a waitlist position.

IIM Calcutta, known for its emphasis on academic consistency, might be slightly more flexible. If your Class 10 and Class 12 scores are strong, this could significantly enhance your chances of receiving an interview call, as these scores often carry substantial weight in their shortlisting criteria.

## FMS Delhi, SPJIMR Mumbai, and MDI Gurgaon

Faculty of Management Studies (FMS) Delhi primarily focuses on CAT scores. While they prefer very high percentiles, your score puts you in a competitive position. Your strong sectional scores, particularly in the Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC) section, will be advantageous here.

SPJIMR Mumbai adopts a holistic approach, evaluating not just CAT scores but also academic performance, work experience (if any), and extracurricular activities. Your IIT background is a major plus, and this comprehensive assessment means your slightly lower-than-99 percentile might be offset by other strong elements of your profile, giving you a high chance of an interview call.

MDI Gurgaon is another excellent institution where your profile is very well-suited. With your percentile and academic background, you can expect a very high chance of receiving an interview call.

## Other Top IIMs and DMS IIT Delhi

Your profile aligns very well with the requirements of IIM Lucknow, IIM Kozhikode, and IIM Indore. These institutions consider a blend of CAT scores and academic records. Your IIT degree positions you strongly for interview calls from these IIMs. Similarly, for DMS IIT Delhi, your background is highly competitive, and you have a strong chance of securing an interview.

## The Importance of WAT and PI

Once you receive interview calls, the Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds become paramount. These stages allow admissions committees to assess your communication skills, leadership potential, clarity of thought, and overall personality. Excelling in these rounds is crucial for converting your strong profile and CAT score into a final admission offer.

Your profile is highly competitive and opens doors to many premier business schools. Focus on preparing meticulously for the interview and WAT rounds to maximize your chances of success. Best of luck!
