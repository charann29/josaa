# What's the College Code for GHRCE Nagpur?

If you're exploring engineering colleges in Maharashtra, you might come across G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering (GHRCE) in Nagpur. Understanding its official college code is important for various application processes.

## Key Takeaways

*   GHRCE Nagpur's official college code for admissions is **4116**.
*   This code is crucial for state-level and national-level admission processes.
*   GHRCE is an autonomous institution, offering a range of technical courses.
*   It is located in Nagpur, Maharashtra.

## Understanding the College Code

The college code, **4116**, is a unique identifier assigned to G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur. This code is used by various admission authorities, such as the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) Maharashtra for state-level counselling (like MHT CET CAP rounds) and potentially for national-level processes like JoSAA, depending on the specific program and admission cycle. When filling out application forms or participating in counselling, you'll need to accurately enter this code to select GHRCE as one of your preferred institutions.

## About G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering (GHRCE), Nagpur

GHRCE Nagpur is an autonomous institution that provides technical education to both Indian and international students. Being autonomous means the college has a degree of independence in designing its curriculum, conducting examinations, and awarding degrees, often leading to a more industry-relevant and updated educational experience. The college has consistently been recognized for its contributions to technical education.

## Location and Affiliation

GHRCE is situated in Nagpur, a prominent city in Maharashtra. Its location offers students access to a growing industrial and educational hub. While autonomous, institutions typically maintain an affiliation with a larger university (e.g., Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University in the past, though autonomy status often changes the nature of this relationship for degree awarding). For the most current affiliation details, it's always best to check the official GHRCE website or the DTE Maharashtra portal.

## Why the College Code Matters in Admissions

The college code acts as a digital fingerprint for the institution within the admissions system. Whether you are applying through MHT CET for undergraduate engineering or other state-specific entrance exams, or even if you are using JEE Main scores for admission, this code helps the counselling authority correctly identify your chosen college. Incorrectly entering the code can lead to errors in your application or seat allocation, so always double-check it against official sources like the DTE Maharashtra information brochures or the official GHRCE admissions page.

Knowing the correct college code, **4116**, is your first step towards accurately applying to G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur, through various admission channels.
