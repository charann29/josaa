# 10+2 Marks and Jadavpur University IT Branch Admissions

Jadavpur University is a highly respected institution, and its Information Technology (IT) branch is particularly sought after. Understanding the academic requirements, especially your 10+2 (Class 12) marks, is crucial for aspiring students.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Class 12 Aggregate:** General category students need a minimum of 60% aggregate marks in their Class 12 examinations.
*   **Core Subjects:** This aggregate must include Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
*   **Mathematics Specific:** A distinct requirement is securing at least 60% marks specifically in Mathematics in Class 12.
*   **Reserved Categories:** For candidates belonging to reserved categories, the aggregate Class 12 mark requirement is reduced to 45%.

## Understanding the Academic Requirements

To be eligible for the IT branch at Jadavpur University, your Class 12 performance plays a significant role. Beyond just passing, there are specific percentage thresholds you need to meet. For general category applicants, a minimum overall aggregate of 60% in your Class 12 board exams is mandatory. This aggregate calculation must include your scores in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

However, it's not just about the overall percentage. Jadavpur University places particular emphasis on mathematical proficiency for its engineering programs, especially IT. Therefore, you must also achieve a minimum of 60% marks specifically in your Class 12 Mathematics subject.

## Requirements for Reserved Categories

Jadavpur University adheres to reservation policies as per government norms. For students belonging to reserved categories (such as SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, PWD, etc.), the minimum aggregate mark requirement in Class 12 is relaxed. These candidates need to secure at least 45% aggregate marks in their Class 12 examinations, including Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. The specific Mathematics percentage requirement might also see a corresponding relaxation, which is detailed in the official admission brochure.

## Beyond Class 12 Marks: The Entrance Exam

While your Class 12 marks establish your eligibility, admission to the IT branch at Jadavpur University is primarily determined by your performance in a competitive entrance examination. For most engineering programs in West Bengal, this is the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination (WBJEE). Your rank in WBJEE is the key factor for securing a seat.

After the WBJEE results are declared, counseling is conducted by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB). During this process, seats are allocated based on ranks and choices filled by candidates. The official JoSAA portal also provides information regarding seat allocation for centrally funded technical institutions, but for Jadavpur University, WBJEE counseling is the primary route.

## Cut-offs and Competition

The competition for Jadavpur University's IT branch is consistently high due to its excellent reputation. The cut-off ranks for admission vary each year based on factors like the number of applicants, the difficulty level of the WBJEE exam, and the number of available seats. You can find past year cut-off ranks for Jadavpur University's IT branch on the official WBJEEB website during the counseling period. Reviewing these can give you an idea of the level of performance typically required.

Meeting the Class 12 mark criteria is the first step towards your dream of joining Jadavpur University's IT program; a strong performance in WBJEE will pave the rest of your way.
