# I have BBA and completed my graduation in 2025 wishing to do my post graduation from Lucknow in MCom is it possible to do MCom after BBA

Many students wonder about their postgraduate options after completing a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA). If you're graduating in 2025 with a BBA and are considering an M.Com in Lucknow, you'll be pleased to know that this path is generally very much open to you.

## Key Takeaways

*   **Yes, you can pursue an M.Com after a BBA.** This is a common and accepted academic progression in India.
*   **Subject overlap makes the transition smooth.** Your BBA foundation in areas like finance, accounting, and economics will be highly relevant.
*   **Eligibility typically includes a minimum BBA score.** Most universities require around 50% marks in your undergraduate degree.
*   **Entrance exams might be part of the admission process.** Some institutions conduct their own tests for M.Com admissions.

## Understanding the BBA to M.Com Transition

The BBA program provides a strong general management foundation, covering various aspects of business operations. The Master of Commerce (M.Com) program, on the other hand, delves deeper into specific areas of commerce, such as advanced accounting, financial management, business economics, and taxation. The good news is that the core subjects you studied in your BBA, particularly in areas like financial accounting, corporate finance, and basic economics, form an excellent base for an M.Com.

This synergy means that universities often view BBA graduates as well-prepared candidates for M.Com programs. The curriculum is designed to build upon the knowledge you've already acquired, allowing for a relatively smooth academic transition.

## Eligibility Criteria for M.Com in Lucknow

While specific requirements can vary slightly between universities in Lucknow, there are common eligibility criteria you should be aware of:

*   **Bachelor's Degree:** You must hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. A BBA is widely accepted for M.Com programs.
*   **Minimum Marks:** Most universities mandate a minimum percentage in your bachelor's degree, often around 50% to 55%. It's crucial to check the exact requirement for each university you're interested in.
*   **Entrance Examinations:** Some prominent universities in Lucknow may conduct their own entrance exams for M.Com admissions. These exams typically assess your general aptitude, business awareness, and foundational knowledge in commerce-related subjects. For example, some state universities might follow a common entrance test for postgraduate admissions, while others might have their specific tests.

It's always advisable to consult the official admission brochures or websites of the specific universities in Lucknow you are targeting for the most accurate and up-to-date eligibility details.

## Top Institutions in Lucknow for M.Com

Lucknow offers several reputable institutions where you can pursue your M.Com. While specific rankings can fluctuate, some well-regarded universities include:

*   **University of Lucknow:** A historic and prominent institution offering a well-established M.Com program.
*   **Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University (BBAU):** A central university known for its diverse academic offerings, including M.Com.
*   **Amity University, Lucknow:** A private university with a modern curriculum and infrastructure.

When choosing, consider factors like faculty expertise, course curriculum, campus facilities, and placement support. Reviewing their official websites and, if available, their performance in recognized frameworks like the NIRF rankings can provide valuable insights.
