# Can I Get Shortlisted for IIM Nagpur with a CAT Score of 111.5 and 93.37 Percentile?

It's natural to wonder about your chances for IIM Nagpur with your strong academic background and CAT score. Let's break down your profile to see how it aligns with the institute's typical selection criteria.

## Key Takeaways

*   Your CAT percentile of 93.37% is above the general category cutoff of 90% for IIM Nagpur, which is a strong positive.
*   Your excellent academic record (95% in 10th, 83% in 12th, 89.33% in graduation) significantly boosts your profile.
*   While your 10 months of work experience is valuable, IIMs typically award points for work experience exceeding one year.
*   Overall, you have a good chance of receiving a shortlist for IIM Nagpur's Post Graduate Program (PGP).

## Understanding IIM Nagpur's Selection Process

IIM Nagpur, like other IIMs, uses a multi-stage selection process. The initial stage involves shortlisting candidates for the Personal Interview (PI) and Written Ability Test (WAT) rounds. This shortlisting is based on a composite score that considers your CAT score, academic performance (10th, 12th, and graduation marks), and sometimes work experience and diversity factors (gender, academic background).

## Your CAT Percentile and Cutoff

For the general category, IIM Nagpur typically requires a minimum overall CAT percentile of 90%. Your 93.37% comfortably surpasses this threshold, making you eligible for consideration based on your CAT performance. It's important to remember that this is a minimum and the actual percentile of shortlisted candidates can be higher, but your score is very competitive.

## Academic Profile Assessment

Your academic record is a significant strength. With 95% in 10th grade, 83% in 12th grade, and an impressive 89.33% in your graduation, you demonstrate consistent academic excellence. IIMs place considerable weight on past academic performance as an indicator of a candidate's ability to handle the rigors of their demanding management programs. Your strong percentages will contribute positively to your composite score.

## Impact of Work Experience

You have 10 months of work experience as a general engineer. While this experience provides valuable professional exposure, it's worth noting that many IIMs, including Nagpur, typically award specific points for work experience only when it exceeds a certain duration, often 12 months. However, having work experience, even if it doesn't fetch direct points in the initial shortlisting formula, can be a significant advantage during the Personal Interview, where you can articulate your learning and experiences.

## Overall Chances for Shortlisting

Considering your strong CAT percentile, outstanding academic record, and relevant work experience, you have a very good probability of being shortlisted for the PI and WAT rounds at IIM Nagpur. Your profile aligns well with what the institute typically looks for in its candidates. If shortlisted, focus on preparing thoroughly for the interview and written test to convert the call into a final admission offer.

Best of luck with your application!
