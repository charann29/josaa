## Understanding EAMCET TS Cutoffs

EAMCET TS (Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical Common Entrance Test for Telangana State) cutoffs are the minimum ranks required for admission to various engineering and other professional courses. These cutoffs are determined during the counselling process, which is managed by the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE). They are not fixed and can fluctuate year on year.

Factors influencing cutoffs include:

*   **Overall performance of candidates:** If more students score higher, cutoffs for popular branches might rise.
*   **Number of seats available:** Colleges with fewer seats in a particular branch will naturally have higher (better) cutoff ranks.
*   **Preference of students:** If Mechanical Engineering is highly preferred in a given year, its cutoff ranks will reflect that demand.
