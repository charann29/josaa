# Highest Salary Packages in CSE at SRKR College Bhimavaram

SRKR Engineering College in Bhimavaram is a well-regarded institution, particularly for its Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) program. Many students pursuing CSE at SRKR are keen to understand the career prospects and salary potential upon graduation.

## Key Takeaways

*   The highest salary package recently offered to a CSE student at SRKR College was 11.2 Lakhs Per Annum (LPA).
*   Amazon was noted as a top recruiter, offering this highest package.
*   The average salary package for CSE students at SRKR College stood at 5.6 LPA.
*   A significant number of students, over 1,200 across various disciplines, secured placements, with approximately 75% of eligible students from the current batch already placed.

## Understanding Placement Trends at SRKR College

SRKR Engineering College consistently focuses on providing placement opportunities for its students. While specific numbers for CSE alone are not always fully separated, the overall campus placement data gives a strong indication of the opportunities available. Recent data indicates that a substantial majority of students find placements through the college's dedicated efforts.

## Internship Opportunities

Beyond full-time placements, SRKR College also facilitates internship opportunities for its students. Around 55% of students have secured internships with various companies. Internships are crucial for gaining practical experience and often lead to pre-placement offers, further enhancing a student's career trajectory.

## Salary Packages: Highest, Average, and Lowest

The salary packages offered to SRKR College graduates in CSE demonstrate a range of opportunities. While the highest package reached 11.2 LPA, the average package provides a more realistic expectation for most students. The lowest reported package was 3.6 LPA, indicating that even entry-level roles offer competitive starting salaries. This range reflects the varying roles, company types, and individual student skills.

SRKR College continues to be a strong choice for students aspiring to build a career in Computer Science and Engineering, offering robust placement support and attracting reputable employers.
