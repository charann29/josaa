The TG-EAPCET exam date for 2025 has been decided by the Telangana Council of Higher Education. The exam will be conducted from **April 29 – May 5, 2025.** Candidates aspiring for admission into undergraduate courses like Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy have to appear and qualify for the exam. Over 2 lakh aspirants appear for the exam, making it tougher to secure admission to the top universities. A clear and organised preparation plan can greatly enhance your chances of success, paving the way for a brighter future. This article outlines a comprehensive strategy to help you secure a high score in TS-EAMCET.

## How to Prepare for AP-EAPCET 2025 in 30 Days?

As the [TG-EAPCET](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/), this 30-day preparation greatly helps students who want to secure high scores in the exam. However, students have to make sure that they have gone through the entire syllabus and finished standard sources at least once before following this 30-day preparation plan.

## Tips to Remain Calm And Relaxed During Last 30 Days of  TG-EAPCET 2025

As the TG-EAPCET 2025 approaches, staying calm and focused is essential for success. The final 30 days are critical, so here are some tips to help you stay relaxed and perform your best:

**Practice Relaxation Techniques:** Engage in meditation or deep breathing exercises to reduce anxiety.

**Create a Study Schedule:** Prioritize topics based on strengths and weaknesses.

**Practice Daily Mock Tests:** Simulate exam conditions to improve speed and accuracy.

**Take Short Breaks:** Avoid burnout by taking short, regular breaks to refresh your mind.

**Stay Positive:** Maintain a positive attitude and trust your preparation.

**Eat Well and Stay Hydrated:** Proper nutrition helps maintain focus and energy levels.

**Sleep Well:** Ensure 7-8 hours of sleep each night to keep your mind sharp.

**Avoid Last-Minute Cramming:** Focus on revision instead of learning new topics in the last week.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **How should I prepare for TG-EAPCET 2025?**

Make sure to cover all key topics from the syllabus, take regular mock tests, and consistently revise the concepts. After each mock, analyze your performance and focus on improving your weak areas.

### 2.**Are TG-EAPCET and TS-EAMCET same?**

Yes, they are the same exams as the TS-EAMCET has been renamed as TG-EAPCET.

### 3. **Who are eligible candidates to appear for TG-EAPCET 2025?**

Candidates who have completed their class 12th or equivalent examination from a recognized board or university are eligible to apply for TS-EAMCET 2025.

### 4. **Is 70 a good score in TG-EAPCET?**

Marks around 70 are considered as average score in TG-EAPCET.

### 5. **Is TG-EAPCET easy or tough?**

TG-EAPCET exam is considered moderate to tough compared to other competitive exams like JEE.
