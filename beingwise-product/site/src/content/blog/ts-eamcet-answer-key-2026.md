## TG EAPCET Answer Key Challenge Procedure

Learning about how to raise objections to the TS EAMCET 2026 is a crucial step that allows students to challenge any discrepancy in the official answer key.

The candidates can challenge the answer key if they find any of the answers are wrong in the provisional answer key by providing valid proof in the TS EAMCET Answer Key Challenge.

After the completion of the corrections and objections, TG EAPCET Result 2026 will be prepared, leading to TG EAPCET Result 2026. Refer to the following to know how to raise objections to TS EAMCET 2026.

- Visit the official website of TG EAPCET: eapcet.tgche.ac.in
- Click the “Answer Key Objections
- Enter Hall Ticket Number, Registration Number and Date of Birth to log in.
- Carefully read all instructions and objection terms before proceeding.
- Match the Question ID as mentioned on the Response Sheet with the Question ID in the Master Question Paper.
- From the TS EAMCET Answer Key 2026, choose the questions you wish to challenge.
- Justify each objection with reference to the standard.
- Only upload relevant supporting evidence (PDF/JPEG of textbook/reference material).
- If there is a fee to be paid for each objection (as indicated in the instructions), pay it and submit the form.
- Download or save the confirmation page for future reference.
