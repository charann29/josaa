TG EAPCET 2025 is a state-level examination conducted by JNTUH for admission to various Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy courses. Students who have completed the **10+2 level with 45%** marks are eligible for the entrance examination.

The application form was released on **1st March 2025 and will remain open till 4th April 2025**. TG EAPCET 2025 will be held from 29th April to 5th May 2025. However, **for engineering courses, the exam will be held from 2nd May to 5th May 2025** in two shifts. **Admit card** for **TS EAMCET** will be released on **19th April 2025**.

All the eligible and aspiring candidates who are preparing for the **TS EAMCET exam 2025** need to adhere to some of the exam day guidelines for a seamless and hassle-free exam experience. It will help them to stay calm and focus on their exam.

## Exam Day Guidelines of TG EAPCET 2025

Candidates need to follow certain guidelines and strictly adhere to those on the day of TG EAPCET exam 2025. Given below are some of the [**exam day guidelines**](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/tg-eapcet-examday-guidelines/) that a student can follow.

- Candidates must arrive at the exam hall at least two hours before the exam starts. Failure to do this, the candidates will not be allowed in the **TS EAMCET 2025** exam hall.
- It is mandatory to carry the TG EAPCET admit card to the exam hall. Along with it, you must also carry a black or blue ballpoint pen. Failure to bring the admit card or tampering with it will lead to disqualification of the candidature.
- No candidates should bring calculators, log books, cell phones, smart watches, etc., electronic gadgets to the examination hall. The concerned students will not be allowed to appear for the examination.
- All the candidates will be allowed to leave the exam hall after the stipulated time of the exam set by the officials and not before that.
- Candidates must carefully read all the instructions on the question paper before proceeding for the solutions.

### Dos

- All the candidates must reach the exam centre at least 2 hours before the exam time.
- However, there is no official dress code for the **TS EAMCET 2025** exam, but candidates should wear a simple and formal dress. Flashy and party wear are not recommended.
- Admit Card is mandatory to bring along with a blue/black ballpoint pen.
- Candidate’s photograph will be taken during the registration process at the venue.
- Read the instructions carefully after getting the question paper.
- Ask for a rough sheet for extra calculations.
- Be mindful and stress free and drink plenty of water to keep yourself hydrated.
- Complete your washroom activities before starting the exam so you do not waste your time during the exam.

### TS EAMCET 2025 Guidelines: Exam Login

Check the below steps to log in for TG EAPCET exam 2025.

- After reaching your allocated exam hall and your computer, you can see the name of the candidate with the photograph displayed on the screen.

- You need to enter your Username and Password 10 minutes before the start of the exam.
- Click on “Sign In” button.
- You can raise your hand and contact the invigilator if you need any system related assistance.

### TS EAMCET 2025: Steps to Attempt CBT Exam

Check the steps given below to attempt CBT exam at TG EAPCET 2025.

- Verify your hall ticket number on your allocated system and enter the password.
- Once entered, candidates need to verify the displayed photograph.
- Further, you need to click on the “I have read and understand the instructions” checkbox and click on the “I am ready to begin” button to start the test.
- The duration of the TS EAMCET exam is 3 hours, i.e., 180 minutes.
- You can see the exam link on your computer screen. TG EAPCET 2025 is of 3 hours i.e. 180 minutes. Verify and check everything before solving the problems. Inform the invigilator if any information is missing.
- You can log in 10 minutes prior to the exam.
- You can see the countdown timer on the top right corner of the screen. The exam will end on its own when the timer runs out.
- There is a question palette on the right side of the screen. You can directly get to any number question on the question number and click on it.
- After answering a question, candidates need to click on the “Save & Next” link to submit their responses. If this is not done, the system will not save the response even if the candidates selected the correct option.
- If you want to store your answer and want to review it afterwards, then you must select the “Mark for Review & Next” link.

### Don’ts

- All the candidates are advised not to bring any kind of electronic gadgets to the exam hall, which includes, log tables, cell phones, calculators, smart watches, etc.
- Candidates will not be allowed to leave the exam hall before the end of the examination under any circumstances.
- Do not carry any unfair means to the exam hall to aid your exam. You will be disqualified if found with any questionable items.
- Do not try to talk to other students or try to help them during the TG EAPCET 2025 exam.
- Candidates should not arrive late to the exam venue.
- Do not be anxious and starve yourself before the exam, as it will affect your mental and physical health, and thus your performance in the exam will get affected.
- If candidates arrive even a minute late to the examination center, they will not be allowed to attend the examination, as they must account for thorough checking before entering the examination hall.

## TS EAMCET 2025 Dress Code

Though there is no official dress code for the candidates of TG EAPCET entrance examination. However, you need to follow a dress code for male and female candidates.

### TS EAMCET 2025 Dress Code for Male Candidates

- Any kinds of accessories such as caps, mufflers, rings, bracelets, chains, or masks are not allowed and are advised to avoid while entering the examination hall.
- Male candidates are advised not to wear clothes that have metallic elements. It may cause inconvenience during security checking.
- Try to avoid wearing shirts with folded sleeves, hooded shirts etc. The candidates are advised to wear half sleeve shirts as it will give them much comfort.
- Any kind of electronic gadgets like smart watches, calculator, smart pens, etc are not allowed and candidates should not wear them while entering the hall.

### TS EAMCET 2025 Dress Code for Female Candidates

- Similarly, female candidates are also advised not to wear any metallic jewelry or items which have metals such as belts, underwire bras, etc.
- Candidates are also advised not to wear sarees or any complicated traditional clothes as it will pose a significant challenge during the security check.
- A simple jeans and T shirt or kurta leggings is recommended for the female candidates. You can choose any kind of breathable fabric like khadi or cotton for comfort.
- Avoid wearing jewellery like bracelets, rings, earrings, etc.
- Avide carrying items like scarves, hats, watches, or goggles are prohibited inside the examination hall as these are prohibited.
- Try to avoid any kind of complex hairstyle and keep a pony tale or in straight hairstyle. It will be easier at the time of security checks.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ********************Can I wear traditional clothes during the TG EAMCET 2025 exam?********************

No, traditional clothes are not allowed to wear during the TG EAPCET 2025 exam. Candidates are advised to wear simple clothes.

### 2. ****TS EAMCET 2025 exam is conducted in how many shifts per day?****

TG EAPCET 2025 is conducted in two shifts, one in the forenoon (9 AM to 12 PM) and the other is in the afternoon, i.e. from 3 PM to 6 PM.

### 3. **********How to prepare for the TS EAMCET 2025 exam?**********

Candidates are advised to get knowledge about the syllabus of TS EAMCET and buy books accordingly. After gathering a detailed engineering syllabus along with the books of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, make a timetable and allocate more time to improve your weak areas. Practice mock tests and concentrate on time management. Take help of teachers or experts, if necessary. At last, keep enough time for revision and keep your mind calm. Take a break and sleep well.

### 4. **********Is there a negative marking in TS EAMCET 2025?**********

No, there is no negative marking in TG EAPCET 2025 exam. However, you will be awarded +1 for each right answer.

### 5. **********Am I eligible for TG EAPCET 2025 exam, if I score 40% marks in 10+2 level?**********

If you are from the reserved category, then you are eligible for TG EAPCET 2025 exam with 40% in the 10+2 level. Otherwise, you are not eligible to appear for the examination as for the general candidates, 45% marks is needed at the 10+2 level.

### 6. ****Is wearing accessories like chains or bracelets allowed during TS EAMCET 2025?****

No, it is not advisable to wear any kind of accessories during the TG EAPCET 2025 exam. You will be prohibited from entering the exam hall if you are found with any such accessories.

### 7. ************What is the duration of the TS EAMCET exam?************

TS EAMCET 2025 exam is conducted for 3 hours. At this time, candidates are required to answer all the questions of the respective subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics for admission to the Engineering programs.

### 8.**Where can I find detailed information on the TS EAMCET 2025 Dress Code?**

You can find the detailed information regarding the TS EAMCET 2025 dress code on the admit card. All the candidates are advised to read the guidelines given in the admit card. It is mandatory to follow the guidelines to avoid any inconvenience on the exam day.

### 9. ************What should I carry for the TG EAPCET 2025 exam?************

You must bring the TG EAPCET admit card provided to you by the authority along with the blue/black ball point pen on the exam day.

### 10. ****Can I wear simple blue jeans in the TS EAMCET exam?****

Yes, you can wear simple blue jeans with light coloured tops to the TG EAPCET 2025 exam.
