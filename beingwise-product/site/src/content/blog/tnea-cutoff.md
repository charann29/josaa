## TNEA Cutoff 2025

The TNEA authorities are expected to announce the TNEA cutoff in July or August 2025. The cutoff will be released after each round of counselling. This process enables candidates to score the necessary qualifying marks to secure seat in their desired college and course based on their rank. The ranking cutoff is determined based on the marks obtained in the 12th qualifying examination, particularly in subjects like Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. Candidates must work hard to get good scores in these subjects to enhance their chances of securing admission to a desired college.

## Steps to Check TNEA Cutoff 2025

Once the TNEA 2025 cutoff is released, candidates can follow these steps to check it. They can also use these steps to view the previous year’s cutoff marks and ranks:

**Step 1:** Visit the official website of TNEA 2025 at [tneaonline.org](http://tneaonline.org).

**Step 2:** On the left side of the homepage, candidates can select the “Cutoff Marks” link. Click it

**Step 3:** Now, Select your course year “Marks” or “Rank” cutoff PDF

**Step 4:** The cutoff will be displayed on the screen with the following details:

- College Code
- College Name
- Branch Code
- Branch Name
- Category-wise cutoff marks or ranks

**Step 5:** Candidates can “Download” the PDF for future reference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the TNEA College Cutoff?

TNEA College Cutoff refers to the minimum marks or ranks required for admission to various engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu through the Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) process.

### 2. Where can I find the TNEA College Cutoff?

Candidates can find the TNEA College Cutoff on the official website of the Directorate of Technical Education, Tamil Nadu, or through the TNEA counseling portal.

### 3. How is the TNEA College Cutoff determined?

The TNEA College Cutoff is determined based on various factors, including the number of available seats, the number of applicants, and the overall performance of candidates in the qualifying examination.

### 4. What should I do if my marks/ranks are below the TNEA College Cutoff?

If your marks or ranks are below the TNEA College Cutoff, you may explore other options such as participating in subsequent counseling rounds, considering colleges with lower cutoffs, or seeking alternative educational pathways.

### 5. Can I challenge or dispute the TNEA College Cutoff?

No, TNEA College Cutoff marks/ranks are determined based on predefined criteria and are not subject to dispute or challenge. Candidates are advised to focus on meeting the required criteria for admission.
