## Exam Pattern: CUET UG 2025

The CUET UG 2025 exam is structured into three main sections: Language, Domain-specific Subjects, and General Aptitude, with each subject carrying 250 marks. It evaluates a student’s language skills, subject expertise, and reasoning abilities. Here’s a breakdown of the CUET exam pattern.

|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Section** | **Duration of the Exam** | **Questions Type** | **No of Questions** |
| Section IA- Language | 60 minutes for each subject | GrammarVocabularyReading Comprehension | 50 Questions |
| Section II- Domain Specific | Domain-Specific Questions | 50 Questions |
| Section III- General Test | AptitudeGeneral StudiesReasoning | 50 Questions |
