## How is the Life At CDU Hyderabad, Malla Reddy Vishwavidyapeeth, SMGI, & Aurora University?

Life at CDU Hyderabad, Malla Reddy Vishwavidyapeeth, St. Mary’s Group of Institutions, and Aurora Deemed to be University offers a dynamic blend of academics, campus culture, and opportunities that shape both personal and professional growth. These institutions represent three distinct yet equally vibrant educational environments in Hyderabad, each with its own strengths in teaching, research, and student engagement. From modern infrastructure and active student clubs to strong industry connections and placement ecosystems, they create a well-rounded campus life that encourages innovation, skill development, and community spirit.

**Chaitanya Deemed To Be University**

Chaitanya Deemed To Be University is a private deemed-to-be university established in 1991, located in Hyderabad, Telangana. Chaitanya University Hyderabad is approved by the UGC, AICTE, PCI, and INC, and accredited with an ‘A’ grade by NAAC. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs spanning fields such as Engineering, Management, Science, Pharmacy, Forensic Science, Nursing, and emerging disciplines like AI & Data Sciences, Fintech, and Cyber Forensics.

CDU Hyderabad is recognized for its emphasis on academic quality, technical exposure, and overall student development with courses like B.Tech, BBA, BCA, and MCA to enhance employability. Admissions primarily occur through a university-conducted entrance exam called the Chaitanya Entrance Test (CET), besides accepting scores from national or state-level exams such as TS EAMCET and JEE Main.

**Malla Reddy Vishwavidyapeeth**

Malla Reddy Vishwavidyapeeth was established in 2022 under the sponsorship of the Chandramma Educational Society (CES) by Shri Ch. Malla Reddy. It is a deemed-to-be university located in Hyderabad, Telangana. The university is recognized by the UGC and DSIR and is spread over a campus size of 70+ acres in Suraram, Hyderabad. It has a diverse student body representing more than 10 countries and offers robust infrastructure, including state-of-the-art labs, digital classrooms, libraries, and three hospitals for practical training.

Malla Reddy Vishwavidyapeeth courses provide a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs through its seven constituent institutes. The disciplines cover engineering, medical sciences, dental sciences, pharmacy, nursing, management, agriculture, dental sciences, allied healthcare sciences, and pharmaceutical sciences. Popular courses include B.Tech with specialisations like AI & ML, Data Science, and Cyber Security; B.Sc and M.Sc in various specializations; MBA; and other healthcare-related courses.

Malla Reddy Vishwavidyapeeth fee structure for the Btech course and other UG programs fall between ₹2.4 Lakhs and ₹8 Lakhs.

**Aurora Deemed To Be University**

Aurora Deemed To Be University, officially known as Aurora Higher Education and Research Academy, is a private multidisciplinary university located in Hyderabad, Telangana. It was established as a Deemed to be University in 2022 under section 3 of the UGC Act 1956. The university has sprawling campuses in Uppal, Hyderabad, and Bhongir, Yadadri district. It offers diverse undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines including engineering, management, humanities, science, IT, business, and arts.

Aurora University Hyderabad have 57 courses in 28 specializations such as Civil Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Cyber Security, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and more. The university emphasizes experiential learning, research, innovation, and global exposure to prepare students as industry-ready professionals and responsible global leaders.

The Aurora Engineering College, a constituent unit and flagship institution of the Aurora Educational Society established in 1998, is located at Tilak Road, Abids, Hyderabad. It is recognized as one of the top engineering colleges in Telangana, offering B.Tech courses in major disciplines like Agricultural, Civil, Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics, and Mechanical Engineering. The college has a strong alumni base and continues to grow in student intake and academic stature.

**St. Mary’s Group of Institutions**

St. Mary’s Group of Institutions is a well-known educational group established in 1996, with campuses in Hyderabad, Kolkata, Guntur, and other locations. The institution is recognised for offering industry-focused education across engineering, management, pharmacy, diploma, and postgraduate streams. It has approvals from bodies such as AICTE and affiliations with reputed universities, including JNTU Hyderabad and other state universities. Some campuses are also highlighted with NAAC ‘A’ Grade accreditation, reflecting a focus on academic quality and institutional standards.

The group offers popular courses such as B.Tech, MBA, MCA, M.Tech, B.Pharmacy, M.Pharmacy, Polytechnic, and allied programmes designed to match current career demands. In terms of placements, the institution mentions collaborations with 300+ recruiters, with students placed in companies like TCS, Wipro, Infosys, and HCL. Reported placement highlights include packages up to ₹48 LPA and hundreds of offers for recent graduating batches, making it a preferred choice for students seeking career opportunities alongside academics.
