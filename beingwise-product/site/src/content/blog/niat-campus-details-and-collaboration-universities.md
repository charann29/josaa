## Chaitanya Deemed University

> Chaitanya (Deemed to be University), located in Warangal, Telangana, was established in 1991 and achieved deemed university status in 2019. The university offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, M.Phil, and Ph.D. programs across various disciplines, including Technology, Science, Management, Humanities, and Pharmacy. Notable courses include B.Tech in five specializations, M.Tech in three specializations, B.Sc (Hons) in Agriculture, MBA, B.Pharm, M.Pharm, and integrated B.Sc+M.Sc programs. Admissions are primarily based on the Chaitanya Entrance Test (CET), with some programs also considering national and state-level entrance exams like TS EAMCET

## Yenepoya University (YU)

> Yenepoya (Deemed-to-be University) has expanded its educational footprint with a state-of-the-art campus in Mangalore. This campus offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines, including Computer Science, Management, Allied Health Sciences, Physiotherapy, and Hotel Management. Notable courses include BBA, BCA with specializations in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Cloud Computing, B.Com with ACCA integration, and B.Sc. in Food Technology. The curriculum is designed to meet industry standards, ensuring students are well-prepared for their professional careers.The university emphasizes holistic development by providing soft skills training and maintaining collaborations with multinational companies to facilitate robust placement opportunities.

## Takshashila University (TK)

> Takshashila University, established under the Tamil Nadu State Private University Act of 2019, is located in Ongur, Tindivanam, Villupuram district, Tamil Nadu, India, and has over 25 years of experience in education and research.The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including Engineering, Arts and Sciences, Agriculture, Allied Health Sciences, Physiotherapy, Hotel Management, Pharmacy, Business, and Nursing

## Vivekananda Global University (VGU)

> Vivekananda Global University (VGU), established in 2012, is a private institution located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited with an ‘A+’ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), VGU offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. These include Engineering, Basic and Applied Sciences, Computer Science, Architecture, Humanities and Social Sciences, Law, Management, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Nursing, Hospitality, Agriculture, Journalism and Mass Communication, Design, Physiotherapy, Occupational Therapy, and Paramedical Sciences. The university emphasizes holistic education that aligns with global standards, aiming to nurture intellectual coherence, integrity, and a strong social conscience among its students.

## Sanjay Ghodawat University (SGU)

> Sanjay Ghodawat University (SGU), established in the academic year 2017-18, is a state-private university located in Kolhapur, Maharashtra. The university offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including Technology, Management, Commerce, Science, Liberal Arts, Architecture, Pharmacy, and Computer Applications. The university’s campus spans 150 acres, providing a serene environment amidst idyllic hills and lush green meadows. SGU emphasizes a research-based curriculum and boasts academically oriented faculty, aiming to guide students toward fulfilling careers and lives.

## Alard University

> Alard University is a private institution in Lohegaon, Pune, Maharashtra, India. The university emphasizes innovation and entrepreneurship, offering an environment conducive to developing skills for an ever-evolving world.

## Annamacharya University

> Annamacharya University is a private institution located in Rajampet, Annamayya District, Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 2024, the university is sponsored by the Annamacharya Educational Trust (AET) under the Andhra Pradesh Private Universities (Establishment and Regulation) Act No. 3 of 2016. It is envisioned to become a center of excellence in education, research, and innovation.

## NRI University

> NRI Institute of Technology (NRIIT) is a private institution located in Pothavarappadu village, Agiripalli Mandal, near Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 2007, NRIIT is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK). The institute has achieved autonomous status and is accredited with an ‘A’ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).

## Sharda University

> Sharda University, established in 2009, has quickly become one of India’s leading educational institutions. Recognized as one of South Asia’s top universities by the QS Asia University Rankings 2024, it holds an A+ accreditation from NAAC and ranks 86 on the NIRF list in India. The university promotes an inclusive and multicultural campus, fostering a vibrant community that prepares students for global collaboration. At Sharda, education goes beyond traditional learning, transforming students into future leaders while celebrating diversity and nurturing curiosity.

## Aurora Deemed University

> Aurora Deemed-to-be University, established in 2022, is an emerging multidisciplinary institution in Telangana, rooted in the legacy of the renowned Aurora Group of Colleges. Though new to national rankings, Aurora is rapidly gaining recognition for its innovative, research-driven approach to education. Accredited by NAAC and approved by UGC and AICTE, the university offers a wide array of programs across Engineering, Management, Sciences, Law, Arts, Design, and more. With a focus on inclusivity, experiential learning, and global readiness, Aurora fosters a vibrant, tech-enabled campus environment that prepares students to lead in an evolving world.

## B. S. Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology

> B.S.Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology (formerly Crescent Engineering College) is a prestigious private deemed-to-be university located in Vandalur, Chennai. Founded in 1984 and granted deemed status on December 16, 2008, the institute earned an “A” grade from NAAC in its first accreditation cycle—a noteworthy achievement—and holds NBA accreditation with 15 engineering programs recognized under the Washington Accord. The 50-acre campus features cutting-edge facilities, including smart classrooms, a digital library, labs, sports arenas, a convention center, hostels, and renewable-energy systems.

## Chalapathi Institute of Technology

> Chalapathi Institute of Technology (CIT), located on the NH-16 highway in Lam, Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, was established in 2008. It is a UGC- and AICTE-approved private engineering college, with NBA accreditation for select programs like Computer Science. CIT offers diploma, undergraduate (B.Tech), and postgraduate (M.Tech) programs in areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cyber Security, Electronics, Civil, Mechanical, and Electrical Engineering.

## Noida International University

> **Noida International University (NIU)**, established in 2010 by the Maruti Educational Trust through a UP Legislature Act, is a private university located on a 75-acre campus near the Yamuna Expressway in Greater Noida. Recognized by the UGC and accredited by NAAC, it also holds approvals from AICTE, BCI, COA, INC, and NCTE. NIU comprises over 11 schools offering more than 150 undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs in disciplines such as Engineering, Management, Law, Journalism, Nursing, Architecture, Liberal Arts, Education, and Fine Arts.

## S-VYASA Deemed-to-be University School of Advanced Studies

> **S-VYASA** Deemed-to-be University’s School of Advanced Studies, located in Bengaluru, is a UGC-recognized and AICTE-approved institution, accredited with an “A+” grade by NAAC and rated 4 stars by the Karnataka State Universities Rating Framework. Established in 2002, S-VYASA stands out for its unique integration of traditional Gurukula-based holistic education with modern scientific research and pedagogy.The School offers a diverse range of programs including B.Tech in emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, and Cyber Security.

## AMET University (Academy of Maritime Education & Training)

> AMET University (Academy of Maritime Education & Training), based in Chennai, is India’s first maritime-focused deemed-to-be university. Specializing in marine engineering, nautical science, naval architecture, and shipping management, AMET blends academic excellence with real-world training through its advanced simulators and onboard practical learning. Recognized by the Directorate General of Shipping and accredited with an ‘A’ grade by NAAC, the university is globally connected and consistently ranks among the top maritime institutes in Asia.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Is the NAT the same as a university entrance examination?

No. NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test) is a psychometric evaluation conducted by NxtWave for its upskilling program. It is separate from university entrance exams, and clearing NAT does not guarantee admission to any university.

### 2. Can I choose which partner university I want to attend under NIAT’s upskilling program?

Yes, you can explore and express your preference based on your location, budget, and B.Tech stream. However, final admission is granted solely by the university based on its eligibility criteria. NxtWave only facilitates the process after receiving your consent.

### 3. What B.Tech streams are available across NIAT’s partner universities?

NIAT offers upskilling programs alongside B.Tech streams such as Computer Science Engineering (CSE), CSE with Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, CSE with Data Science, and related specialisations. Availability may vary depending on the university and location.

### 4. What is the eligibility criteria to pursue a B.Tech at a NIAT partner university?

Generally, students must complete 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) from a recognised board. They may also need to take the NAT and participate in counselling. Specific criteria may vary by university.

### 5. Does NIAT provide placement assistance to students?

NIAT focuses on improving employability through industry-aligned training, projects, and skill development. Placement and internship opportunities are typically managed by the universities through their placement cells.

### 6. Are the universities where NIAT conducts its programs government recognised?

Yes, NIAT collaborates with UGC-recognised universities, many of which also have NAAC accreditation and AICTE approval. However, NIAT itself is not a university or degree-granting institution, though it is recognised by industry bodies like NASSCOM and NSDC.

### 7. Can a student from the arts or commerce stream enrol in NIAT’s upskilling program?

B.Tech programs generally require a PCM background in 10+2. Students from arts or commerce streams may not be eligible unless they meet the specific requirements set by the university.

### 8. Is there a free campus visit option before enrolling?

Yes, students can book a free one-day visit to their nearest NIAT upskilling centre to explore the environment, interact with mentors, and understand the program before enrolling.
