## JEE Mains Application Form 2025 Correction Process

Candidates can use the official website for the correction window and make changes or update the required fields.

Follow these steps to access the correction window of JEE Mains 2025.

- Use Official Website: Visit the official JEE Main website, typically hosted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), where you originally registered and submitted your application.
- Log in to your account after providing your valid credentials.
- Access Correction Window: Search for a link mentioning “Application correction” and click through it to access your correction window
- Make Corrections: After carefully reviewing your application, update the required fields that need updating.
- Verify Changes: Carefully review the updated information to ensure there are no mistakes.
- Submit: After reviewing your application, you can submit corrections by providing a security code or password.
- Acknowledgement: Once you submit the corrections, you will usually receive a confirmation message or email indicating that your changes have been successfully updated in your application.

### Corrections Allowed for JEE Mains 2025

The Correction window can be accessed only for a limited time frame so students are advised to make use of this chance. Schedule a timeframe specifically to update or edit your details. Here are the types of corrections allowed for JEE Mains 2025.

- Personal Details: Name, contact details, DOB, etc.
- Academic Details: Academic particulars like Board details, passing year, etc.
- Exam Centre: Exam centre preference can be modified based on your convenience.
- Documents: Incorrectly uploaded documents, like signatures, photographs, and other documents, can be modified.

## Points To Remember for JEE Mains Application Form 2025 Correction

Candidates should keep the following points in mind to avoid any difficulties with the application.

- Providing incorrect information may result in the rejection of the application.
- The JEE Main 2025 application form correction will be available separately for each phase. Applicants must make changes within the specified timeframe.
- Only specific information, such as academic and personal details, can be updated. Students can’t alter all details in the application form.

## What are the documents required for JEE Mains application form 2025?

Before you begin filling out or correcting your JEE Main 2025 application form, gather all necessary documents and details to ensure a smooth process. Having these ready will help prevent mistakes and delays during submission or correction. The required documents for JEE Mains 2025 are listed below.

- Scanned Images:
  - Photograph
  - Signature
- Identity Proof:
  - Aadhar card, bank passbook, ration card, or any government-issued ID.
- Category Certificate (if applicable):
  - For candidates belonging to reserved categories (SC/ST/OBC/EWS).
- Class Certificates:
  - Class 10th and Class 12th mark sheets(only for droppers) or certificates.
- 12th students have to fill in as “APPEARING”.
- Additional Documents (if applicable):
  - PwD certificate for candidates with disabilities.

## Guidelines for Exam Center Selection and Language Medium in JEE Mains 2025

When filling out the JEE Main 2025 application form, you must carefully select your preferred exam centres and language medium. These choices directly impact your exam experience, so it’s important to understand the available options and considerations:

1. **Selecting Exam Centers:**
   - You are required to choose four cities in order of preference as your exam centres.
   - The cities should be selected based on convenience, travel accessibility, and accommodation options.
   - The first city you select will be considered your highest preference, so prioritise accordingly.
   - Review the official list of JEE Main exam centers before making your choices to ensure your selected cities are available for your region.
2. **Order of Preference:**
   - List your most preferred city first, followed by other convenient options.
   - NTA will allot the exam center based on your preferences and availability.
3. **Medium of Question Paper:**
   - You must also select the preferred language medium for the question paper.
   - JEE Main 2025 offers English, Hindi, and 11 regional languages.
   - Choose the medium you are most comfortable with, as it cannot be changed after submission.
4. **Key Points to Remember:**
   - Once submitted, changes to exam center and language medium may only be possible during the official correction window, subject to NTA guidelines.
   - Early selection increases your chances of getting your preferred center, especially in high-demand cities.

When applying for JEE Main 2025, candidates must pay a non-refundable application fee as part of the submission process. The fee amount varies based on category, gender, and exam location.

**Accepted Payment Methods**

You can pay the application fee through any of the following online modes:

- Credit Card (Domestic/International)
- Debit Card
- Net Banking
- UPI
- PAYTM Wallet

**Important Points to Note:**

- All payments must be made online. Offline payment methods such as demand drafts, money orders, or IPOs are not accepted.
- If your payment status is shown as “Not OK,” the transaction is considered cancelled. The amount will be refunded to your original payment method within 7–10 working days, but you will need to make the payment again to complete your application.
- Always ensure the payment status is “OK” before considering your application process complete.
- For any transaction-related issues, candidates can contact the NTA helpdesk.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1.  What is the JEE Mains 2025 Exam Date?

NTA has announced the JEE Main 2025 exam dates, where Session 1 will be held from January 22 to 31, 2025, and Session 2 from April 1 to 8, 2025. [Registration for Session 1](/blog/jee-main/jee-main-exam-dates/) will be closed on November 22, 2024.

### 2. Will Jee mains 2025 be tough?

The difficulty level of JEE 2025 will depend on the NTA’s decision to set the exam as moderate or challenging. Considering the competition candidates need an ample amount of preparation to get through.

### 3. Can IIT give a good future?

Yes. IIT graduates often receive competitive salary packages with perks such as bonuses, health insurance, provident fund, stock options, and allowances for housing and transport, ensuring financial security and a comfortable lifestyle.

### 4.  Can I join MIT after IIT?

Yes, you can pursue studies at MIT after completing your degree at IIT. However, the admission process at MIT is different from IIT, and you will need to fulfill their specific requirements.

### 5. What documents are required for the JEE Main application form?

Documents required for JEE Mains application form 2025, candidates need to have a scanned passport-size photograph, a scanned signature, a class 10 (or equivalent) certificate/marksheet (for DOB proof), identity proof (Aadhaar/other valid ID as per NTA), category/ PwD / EWS certificate (if applicable), and a valid email & mobile number. Official information bulletins list exact formats and sizes — follow the NTA instructions.

### 6. What is required for the JEE Main application?

To fill the application form, the candidate needs a valid ID (Aadhaar/ID proof), personal details, educational details (10th/12th), scanned photo & signature, choice of paper/session, and online payment of the JEE Mains application fee.

### 7. How to get the JEE Main application number?

After you complete the online registration on the JEE Main portal, the JEE Mains application number is displayed on screen and emailed/SMSed to you. If forgotten, use the “Forgot Application Number”/“Recover Application Number” link on the official site (enter name, DOB, and email/mobile number as requested).

### 8. How to know JEE Main application status?

To check the JEE Main application status, log in to the official JEE Main portal using your application number and password; the dashboard shows the payment & form status.

### 9. How to edit the JEE Main application form?

After the initial submission, NTA opens the JEE Mains application form correction window on 26th November 2025. Go to the official website’s correction link to modify allowed fields (some fields may be non-editable). Save and submit the correct changes.

### 10. When will JEE Main application start?

JEE Mains 2025 application was released on 28th October 2024 on the official website of NTA.

### 11. When is the JEE Mains application last date?

JEE Mains application last date was on 22nd November 2024. However, the correction window will be closed on 27th November 2024.
