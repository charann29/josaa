The Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE), Kerala, has issued the KEAM admit card 2026 to the candidates who are to take the Kerala Engineering, Architecture, and Medical entrance test. Students who successfully pass through the application process are in a position to have access to the KEAM admit card download link under the official candidate portal. Aspirants must note that KEAM dates are also revised, and now the exam will be conducted from April 17 to 22, 2026; earlier, it was being conducted from April 17 to 23.

The CEE Kerala KEAM admit card is a document that cannot be forgotten at any cost. The candidates have to carry this card to the test centre because without it, they cannot appear for the test. The KEAM hall ticket 2026 will contain the major information about the examination time, the time when the candidate is to report, the address of the examination centres, and the information about the candidates.

Once the Admit card for KEAM is downloaded, it is important to verify every detail and communicate the differences to the exam authorities as soon as possible in case of any discrepancy in the KEAM admit card. Refer to the following to learn more about the KEAM admit card 2026.

## KEAM 2026 Admit Card Download Steps

Students can follow the steps below to download the KEAM admit card:

**Step 1:** Go to the official KEAM candidate portal.

**Step 2:** On the dashboard, there is a link to the KEAM admit card 2026, which you should press.

**Step 3:** Enter your application number and passwords.

**Step 4:** On the screen, the KEAM Hall ticket 2026 will be shown.

**Step 5:** Verify all the information on the KEAM admit card.

**Step 6:** The file is downloaded from the KEAM admit card download option.

**Step 7:** Have a printed copy of the Admit card to use on exam day in KEAM.

## Discrepancy in KEAM Admit Card?

Once the KEAM admit card download has been completed by the candidates, they must ensure that they verify all the details as indicated in the KEAM admit card 2026. The KEAM 2026 admit card that has been sent by the authorities has essential information in the form of the name of the candidate, the centre of examination, the date of examination, etc.

In case of any error or discrepancy in the KEAM admit card, one must report the same to the exam authorities. The CEE Kerala KEAM admit card must be correct in all respects since it will be verified at the exam centre. The candidates must apply to the Commissioner of Entrance Examinations (CEE) for the same before the exam date mentioned on the KEAM hall ticket 2026.

Once the Admit card for **KEAM 2026** used by the candidates in the exam is ensured to be correct, one will be able to avoid any problems that may occur during the verification of the candidates at the exam centre. Check out the table below for more information on the same.

| **Type of Discrepancy** | **Action to Take** |
| --- | --- |
| Incorrect candidate name | Contact the CEE Kerala helpdesk and request a correction. |
| Wrong photograph or signature | Inform authorities and submit the required identity proof. |
| Incorrect exam centre details | Report the issue to the exam authority immediately. |
| Wrong exam date or time | Verify with the official portal and contact the helpdesk. |
| Incorrect application number | Provide correct application details for verification. |
| Missing or unclear details | Request a corrected KEAM admit card 2026 from the authorities. |

## KEAM 2026 Exam Day Guidelines

Students must take care of all the exam day instructions provided on their respective KEAM admit card 2026. Refer to the following to go through the same.

- Bring a hard copy of the KEAM admit card 2026 and one fresh photo ID document.
- Report to the exam centre at least 30 45 minutes before the reporting time indicated in the KEAM admit card.
- Wear plain and simple clothes and do not use heavy accessories or metallic objects.
- Carry only the necessary documents like KEAM hall ticket 2026, identification documents and a blue or black ball pen.
- Do not carry restricted items such as mobile phones, calculators, smart watches, and other electronics.
- Follow all the directions of the invigilators in the examination.
- Take a seat in the given seat and complete the answer sheet.
- Be disciplined during the exam and do not engage in any form of unfairness.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. When will the KEAM 2026 admit card be released?

The KEAM admit card 2026 has been released on April 9, 2026. The details of this will be updated on the official KEAM admit card release date page, where students need to keep checking.

### 2. How to download the KEAM admit card?

The KEAM admit card can be downloaded through the official KEAM candidate portal by the candidates. Students need to enter their application number and password for the KEAM admit card download.

### **3. What details are required to access the KEAM hall ticket 2026?**

To download the KEAM hall ticket 2026, students need to enter their application number and password. Without these details, it is not possible for them to download their Admit card for KEAM.

### ******4. Is a printed copy of the KEAM admit card mandatory?******

Yes, it is obligatory for the students to carry a printed copy of the KEAM 2026 admit card along with them to be proven.

No, candidates shall not be allowed to be present in the exam hall without carrying the KEAM admit card 2026.

Yes, a person can download the KEAM admit card 2026 multiple times. The person needs to ensure that a neat print is done on the day of the exam.

Any student who has queries related to issues faced in obtaining the CEE Kerala KEAM admit card needs to contact the officials through phone or email. They are able to guide the students accordingly.

### **10. Is it necessary to retain the KEAM admit card after the exam?**

Yes, even after completing the exam, the students need to keep the KEAM 2026 admit card with them.
