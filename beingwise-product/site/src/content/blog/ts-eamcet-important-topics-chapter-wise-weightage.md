## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Which subject is the highest scoring in TS EAMCET 2025?

As per the TS EAMCET/EAPCET, the highest-scoring subject is Mathematics which has a maximum weightage of 80 marks.

### 2. How TS EAMCET 2025 is conducted?

The TS EAMCET/ EAPCET  2025 is conducted in Online mode as a CBT(Computer-based test).

### 3. Who is eligible for TS EAMCET/EAPCET 2025?

Candidates who have secured a minimum aggregate of 45% in their 12th grade or equivalent, for OC and BC categories, and 40% for SC/ST categories are eligible for TS EAMCET 2025.
