**AP EAMCET 2024 Paper Analysis**: The AP EAMCET exam started on May 18th, 2024 for the engineering stream and will last till May 23rd, 2024. The candidates can find the complete paper analysis for every slot here. This paper analysis helps the students understand the exam pattern, important topics, difficulty level, and chapter-wise weightage, offering insights for future AP EAMCET test takers.

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada conducts AP EAMCET, a state-level common entrance test every year under the APSCHE (Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education). The candidates who want admission to engineering courses must undertake this entrance examination.

The AP EAMCET paper analysis is based on the students’ and coaching experts’ feedback and responses. Let’s look into the detailed analysis for each paper below.

### May 18th, 2024 – Shift 1

The slot analysis of AP EAMCET shift 1 held on 18th May 2024 is provided below. According to the students’ feedback, the overall difficulty level was moderate.

Let’s see the subject-wise analysis and important topics asked from every subject below:

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Subject** | **Difficulty Level** | **Important Topics Asked** |

| Physics | Easy to Moderate | Questions were majorly drawn from Motion of straight lines, Thermodynamics, Current electricity, Kinetics, theory of gases, Ray optics, Waves, Alternate current. |
| Chemistry | Easy to Moderate | Questions were majorly drawn from D&F block, Chemical bonding, Atomic structure, Chemical kinetics, Surface chemistry, Biomolecules, Electro chemistry. |
