## How to check TS Inter Results?

Students can check the inter results ts by following the given steps:

- Visit the Telangana State Board of Intermediate Education Website
- Click the [TS Inter Results Link 2026](https://results.cgg.gov.in/tgbieResultsLiveNew2026.do)
- Choose your stream (General/Vocational) and Exam type (Regular/Supplementary)
- Enter the TS Inter hall ticket number in the given field.
- Click on Submit.
- View the TS Inter Result on the screen. It will show Subject-Wise marks, Total marks, & pass/fail status.
- Download the TS Inter Results marks memo and keep a printout for future use.

## What to do after getting your TS Inter Results?

Students are often confused about their next steps after their board results are out. Here’s a simplified step-wise plan to proceed with after the inter ts results are out for the 2nd year students:

- Download the TS Inter Results Marks Memo and verify if the subject names, marks, and personal details are correct. If there is any error, contact the school authorities or TSBIE for correction or reissuing of the marks memo.
- Analyse performance based on your aggregate percentage, pass/fail status, and subject-wise scores. These factors will let you reflect on whether you need improvement or repetition.
- If you failed in 1 or 2 subjects, plan and prepare for the TS Inter supplementary exams.
- Decide which academic stream to choose for your higher studies.
- For 1st year students, enrol in the 2nd year and focus on core subjects. Whereas 2nd year pass students should select their UG degrees which match their stream.
- Students should start preparing for entrance exams and counselling rounds as per their goals.
- Many students opt for a Diploma or other vocational degrees after the 2nd year. Or keep a gap year to improve entrance exam marks.
- Students may also start applying for scholarships and financial aid for college admissions.
