## Why JEE Mains PYQs Is Important?

Practising PYQs of JEE Mains is one of the most effective ways to prepare for the highly competitive JEE Mains exam. These questions give candidates direct insight into the exam pattern, frequently asked topics, and question difficulty level. By solving JEE Mains PYQs chapter-wise, students can strategically focus on important chapters, strengthen weak areas, and enhance problem-solving speed and accuracy. Candidates should collect the JEE Mains PYQs PDF with detailed solutions, making it easy for them to practice extensively offline and track their progress. Incorporating PYQs JEE Mains into preparation not only boosts confidence but also helps develop exam-ready mindset crucial for securing top ranks.

- PYQs of JEE Mains are crucial for understanding the exam pattern, question types, and difficulty level, helping candidates prepare effectively for JEE Mains 2026 by practising questions that have appeared in past exams.​
- Solving JEE Mains PYQs chapter wise allows students to focus on one chapter at a time, understand topic-wise weightage, identify strong and weak areas, and allocate study time according to the importance of each chapter. This method breaks down the vast syllabus into manageable parts.​
- CollegeDost provides year-wise JEE Mains PYQs PDF. These PDFs contain previous years’ questions along with solutions, allowing students to download and practice offline at their convenience.​
- Candidates can also practise PYQs on the **[CollegeDost Mock Test](https://collegedost.in/mock-test/)** Platform. It will give them the exposure to a real-time exam simulation and also practice JEE Mains PYQs simultaneously.
- The use of PYQs JEE Mains has multiple benefits:
  - Familiarization with question formats and difficulty levels.
  - Improved problem-solving speed and accuracy.
  - Ability to benchmark preparation through mock tests and previous year practice.
  - Insights into high-yield chapters based on past weightage trends.
  - Development of strategic approaches to answer different question types.​

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****Is it Worth Practicing JEE Mains PYQs?****

Practicing Previous year’s questions will add value to your exam preparation and help you understand the toughness and difficulty level of the exam.

### 2. **When to Start Practicing PYQs?**

It is advised to practice the previous year’s questions once you complete the major portion of the syllabus as it gives you a clear understanding of where you should shift your focus.

### **3. ******Can I Access any Section While Writing the JEE Mains 2025 Exam?********

Yes. Candidates are allowed to skip the section, go back to the previous section, or navigate between chemistry, physics, and mathematics.

### 4.************I******s 90% a Good Score in JEE Mains?******************

Scoring high in Jee Advanced 2025 will require constant effort toward syllabus completion, practicing mock tests, revision, and working on the weak areas.

### ********5.**** Where Can I get the Previous year’s Question Papers?****

Many online platforms provide free previous years’ question papers, also candidates can refer to the NTA official website for previous year’s question papers.

### 6. How many PYQs are repeated in JEE Mains?

Usually, 2 to 4 questions get repeated each year. Sometimes, the questions are not exactly repeated but a similar, type of question may come. Focusing on recent years’ JEE Mains PYQs helps identify these trends.

### 7. Are PYQs enough for JEE Mains?

PYQs of JEE Mains are an essential part of preparation but not enough alone. They should be supplemented with theory, mock tests, and problem-solving practice.

### 8. Which year PYQs is best for JEE Mains 2025?

The last 5 to 7 years of JEE Mains PYQs are most relevant for the exam, as they reflect current syllabus trends and exam patterns.

### 9. How many PYQs should I solve for JEE Mains?

You should aim to solve at least 5 to 7 years of PYQs to build familiarity and confidence. This includes about 300-400 questions if done chapter-wise.

### 10. How to get PYQs of JEE Mains?

You can get JEE Mains chapter-wise PYQs PDF from official sources or the CollegeDost platform. Along with this you can get PYQs by practising Mock tests and many books also organise PYQs JEE mains chapter-wise.
