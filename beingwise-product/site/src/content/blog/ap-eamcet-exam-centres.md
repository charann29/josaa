The Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (AP EAPCET) 2025, conducted by Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada will release the Examination Centres through the official website in a brochure. There are around 50 examination centers in Andhra Pradesh that conduct the AP EAPCET 2025 exam. While filling out the AP EAPCET 2025 Application Form, the candidates must choose three centers of their preference. The examination center of the candidate is allocated based on their chosen preference and availability.

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada, conducts the AP EAMCET 2025 on behalf of the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE)r. This test is a gateway for admission into different undergraduate courses in the fields of Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy across Andhra Pradesh. The examination authorities scheduled the AP EAPCET 2025 exam from the **21st to the 27th of May** 2025.

## Important Instructions to the Candidates

The important instructions to be followed by the candidate are mentioned below:

1. The AP EAPCET 2025 Hall Ticket issued to the candidate is an important document. They must carry the hall ticket without which they cannot attend their exam.

2. The AP EAPCET Hall Ticket is a non-transferable document. Any alteration of the Hall Ticket will result in the disqualification of the Candidate.

3. The candidates must arrive at the examination center before 2 hours of their examination time.

4. The candidate is not allowed to even be late by one minute from the commencement of the exam.

5. The candidates should also carry the following things to the examination center:

- AP EAMCET 2025 Hall Ticket
- Online Application Form filled out by the candidate
- A valid Photo ID proof of the candidate such as an Aadhar Card, Voter ID, Driving License, or PAN Card
- A Ballpoint Pen (Black or Blue)
- Caste Certificate (for SC/ST Category Candidates only)

6. The candidates are not allowed to carry the following items to the examination hall:

- Text Books
- Study Materials
- Calculators
- Written Notes or Printed Material
- Bits of Papers
- Mobile Phones
- Smart Watches

It’s important to follow the instructions set by the examination authorities. Except for the AP EAPCET Hall Ticket, no other things are allowed in the examination hall. If any prohibited items are found, the candidate’s examination will be cancelled and will also be debarred for future examinations.

## Exam Day Guidelines to the Candidates

Here are some exam Day Guidelines to be followed by the candidates:

- The candidates need to check their details like Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Category, Test Center, Date, and Time of the examination mentioned in the Hall Ticket.
- The candidates must carry all the required documents and stationary things to the examination hall.
- The candidate should reach the examination center at least 2 hours before their examination time.
- Under any circumstances, the candidate will not be allowed to attend the examination after the commencement of the exam.
- The candidate is not allowed to carry any baggage into the examination hall.
- The candidate must listen carefully to the instructions given by the invigilators inside the examination hall.
- In case, any mistakes or glitches occur on the computer during the examination, the candidate must contact the invigilators immediately.
