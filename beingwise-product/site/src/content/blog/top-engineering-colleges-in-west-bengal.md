## Admission to Top Govt Engineering Colleges in West Bengal

Admission to the Top govt engineering colleges in West Bengal is mainly based on the performance of the entrance examinations conducted in the country and in the state. The admission process for the colleges is different from one to another. Below, we have tabulated the mandatory entrance exam required for admission into the top 5 engineering colleges in West Bengal

| **SL No** | **College Name** | **Mandatory Admission Test** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | IIT Kharagpur | JEE Advanced |
| 2 | Jadavpur University | WBJEE |
| 3 | NIT Durgapur | JEE Main |
| 4 | IIEST Shibpur | JEE Main |
| 5 | Kalyani Government Engineering College | WBJEE |

**Key Points to Remember**

- The Top Engineering Colleges in West Bengal, such as IIT Kharagpur, require JEE Main and JEE Advanced examination results, and admission is done through counseling in JoSAA counseling.
- On the other hand, Colleges such as NIT Durgapur and IIEST Shibpur also require JEE Main examination results, and counseling is done in JoSAA counseling.
- Admission to Top government colleges in West Bengal run by the state government, such as Jadavpur University, is done based on the WBJEE examination results, which is conducted every year by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board. After the declaration of the results, counseling is done based on the results.

## Top Private Engineering Colleges in West Bengal
