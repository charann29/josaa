## Exam Pattern: CUET UG 2025

The CUET UG 2025 exam is conducted for 250 marks for each subject, and there are three sections. Candidates are tested for three major skills: language, subject knowledge, and general aptitude. Check the detailed exam pattern below:

|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Section** | **Questions Type** | **No of Questions** | **Duration of the Exam** |
| Section IA- Language | Grammar Vocabulary  Reading Comprehension | 50 Questions | 60 minutes for each subject |
| Section II- Domain Specific | Domain-Specific Questions | 50 Questions |
| Section III- General Test | Aptitude General Studies Reasoning | 50 Questions |

## Student Initial Reaction for CUET Exam: May 30th Shift 1 Exam

Check what students felt after the CUET 2025 shift one exam held on May 30th:

**Ananya (from Delhi):** “The English section was very student-friendly. I found the reading comprehension passages clear, and the vocabulary part was pretty much what we had practised—synonyms, idioms, and para jumbles all felt familiar. I easily managed to finish it before time.”

**Raghav (from Lucknow):** “Economics was smooth for me. The questions on GDP and Perfect Competition were direct, and even the numericals didn’t require much calculation. If you’ve gone through NCERT and mock tests, you’d find it very doable.”

**Meera (from Bhopal):** “Chemistry was mostly from Inorganic and Coordination Compounds. I felt like the questions tested concepts more than memory. Physical Chemistry had a few numerical-based ones, but overall the paper was easy and straightforward.”

**Aarav (from Pune):** “Political Science was exactly like our sample papers—nothing came from outside the syllabus. It focused heavily on Indian politics and recent political events. I didn’t have to guess any answers, which was a relief.”

**Sneha (from Kolkata):** “Geography had very basic questions. I remember questions on WTO and international geography, but everything was covered in our textbooks. It felt more like a recap than a test—definitely the easiest paper of the day for me.”

## CUET UG 2025: Guidelines for the Exam Day

Here are some exam day guidelines set by the National Testing Agency to ensure a smooth examination process. Adhere to the guidelines given below:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Category** | **Details** |
| **Restricted Items** | Calculators, smartwatches, mobile phones, electronic devices, printed materials, etc. |
| **Permitted Items** | Face mask, pen, transparent water bottle |
| **Essential Documents** | Valid ID proof, hard copy of CUET 2025 admit card, recent passport photo, PwD certificate (if applicable) |

| **Technical Guidelines** | Follow all on-screen instructions and directions given by the invigilator |
| **Dress Code Advice** | Wear simple clothing and avoid trousers with bulky or multiple pockets |
