## How to Check GITAM Results 2026?

Once the GITAM GAT Results 2026 are out, students can check the results by following the steps below:

- Visit the GITAM official website.
- Click on the published GITAM Results link.
- Log in with your GITAM results login credentials
- Enter the hall ticket number and the date of birth.
- Click on Submit.
- Your GITAM GAT Results 2026 will be displayed on the screen.
- Download it and print a copy for future records.

Students are given ranks based on the GITAM GAT Exam performance and meeting the eligibility criteria. 25% of the total seats are reserved for meritorious students from Karnataka State for GITAM Bangalore admissions.

## What to do after the GITAM Results are released?

After the Gitam Entrance Test Results are published, students need to proceed with the centralized counselling procedure conducted by GITAM University across its campuses. Check the following section to know the next steps after the GITAM Results are released:

- Register for the GITAM GAT Counselling round and fill in the preferred branch and campus choices.
- Participate in the online counselling round as per the scheduled time and date.
- Attend the personal interview round.
- Keep a check on the registered email ID for counselling call letters.
- Seat allotments are conducted based on student rank, campus & course preference, and availability.
- Download the allotment letter and accept it on the portal.
- Proceed with the document verification process at the allotted campus or online.
- Confirm the admission by paying the tuition fees within the deadline mentioned in the allotment order.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. When are the GITAM supplementary results announced?

GITAM supplementary results are released online after the exams are completed. They are generally announced around the first week of June 2026.

### 2. How can I get the GITAM revaluation results?

Revaluation results apply to end-semester exams. Students must apply through the official website by submitting a request and paying the prescribed fee.

### 3. Where can I check the GITAM GAT Result 2026?

Students can check their results on the official GITAM University results portal.

### 4. Is there any provision for direct admission?

The primary admission route is through GAT and other national/state-level entrance exams. However, students with top ranks in central or state board exams may be eligible for direct admission if they meet all criteria.

### 5. What is the GITAM GAT percentile calculation method?

The percentile calculation method converts individual scores into percentiles based on overall performance, indicating a candidate’s relative ranking among all test-takers.

### 6. What is the tie-breaking criteria for the GITAM GAT exam?

Tie-breaking is done based on subject-wise marks. Higher marks in Mathematics are given priority, followed by Physics and Chemistry. If needed, older candidates are given preference in the final stage.

### 7. What do previous years’ toppers say about GITAM results?

Previous toppers have shared positive feedback, highlighting the accessibility of the exam and the scholarship opportunities, with some students receiving up to 100% scholarships.
