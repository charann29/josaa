## Exam Pattern: CUET UG 2025

The purpose of the CUET UG 2025 exam is to assess a candidate’s proficiency in three key areas: language, topic knowledge, and aptitude. Each subject is given 60 minutes to candidates. Applicants may choose up to five subjects for the CUET UG 2025 test. More information about the CUET exam pattern can be found in the table below.

|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Section IA- Language | 60 minutes for each subject | 50 Questions | Grammar Reading Comprehension Vocabulary |
| Section II- Domain Specific | 50 Questions | Domain-Specific Questions |
| Section III- General Test | 50 Questions | General Studies Reasoning Aptitude |

## Student Initial Reaction for CUET Exam: May 23rd Shift 1 Exam

Here’s what students felt after the CUET exam held on May 23rd, Shift 1:

**Ritika (from Delhi):** “The GAT section felt a bit tougher than usual, with more GK and fewer quant questions. English was easy if your vocabulary is good, but rearrangement was tricky.”

**Aman (from Patna):** Quant had questions on vectors, 3D, and LPP. Mostly formula-based. English RC was easy, but the vocabulary had some tough words. Overall, a balanced paper.”

**Shruti (from Lucknow):** “GK was heavy today—state formation, polity, rivers, and awards came up a lot. Reasoning and Quant were moderate. The chemistry domain was straightforward.”

**Kabir (from Jaipur):** “Lots of GK and history in GAT. Maths had fewer numericals than I expected. English had 3 RCs and idioms like ‘Gift of the Gab’. Vocabulary repeated from earlier shifts.”

**Neha (from Bhopal):** “English felt manageable—basic grammar and vocab like ‘lethargic’, ‘taciturn’ came up again. Quant was time-consuming. Domain subject (Sociology) was simple and scoring.”

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****What is CUET UG 2025?****

CUET UG 2025 is the Common University Entrance Test for undergraduate admissions in central and participating universities across India.

### 2.**********************Is CUET UG 2025 mandatory for all central universities?**********************

Yes, CUET UG 2025 is compulsory for admission to undergraduate programs in all central universities.

### **3. **************************How many subjects can I choose in CUET UG 2025?****************************

You can select up to 10 subjects across three sections: Languages, Domain-Specific Subjects, and General Test.

### **4. ******************What is the exam pattern for CUET UG 2025?********************

The exam comprises multiple-choice questions divided into three sections: Section I (Language), Section II (Domain-Specific Subjects), and Section III (General Test).

### 5. ********Can I apply to multiple universities through CUET UG 2025?********

Yes, CUET UG 2025 allows you to apply to multiple participating universities using a single application.
