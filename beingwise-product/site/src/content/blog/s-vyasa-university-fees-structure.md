## S-Vyasa University Hostel Fees

S-VYASA University fees structure for accommodation is [affordable and hostels](/blog/engineering-colleges/s-vyasa-university-hostels/) are designed to offer a comfortable and secure living environment for students. Each room is furnished with essential amenities such as a cot, bed, pillow, doormat, curtains, bathroom fittings, study table, and chair. Common areas are well-equipped with a TV with a set-top box, CCTV surveillance, a heat pump, a refrigerator, a washing machine, a dining table, a biometric attendance device, and a first aid box, ensuring both convenience and safety.

In addition to physical amenities, the hostel fee also covers several important services. These include 24×7 security, warden supervision, housekeeping, regular maintenance, and access to an on-call doctor along with a 24-hour ambulance facility. Together, these provisions ensure a safe, hygienic, and supportive environment that allows students to focus on their academic and personal growth.
