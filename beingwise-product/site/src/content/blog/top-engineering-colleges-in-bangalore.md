### [JEE Advanced](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-advanced/)

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Advanced is an entrance test for admission to undergraduate programs at the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs). It is considered one of the toughest engineering entrance exams in India and is taken by students who qualify the JEE Main exam.

### COMEDK UGET

The Consortium of Medical, Engineering, and Dental Colleges of Karnataka Under Graduate Entrance Test (COMEDK UGET) is a state-level entrance exam for admission to engineering, medical, and dental courses in various private colleges in Karnataka. It is a popular exam for students seeking engineering admissions in the state.

### [KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test)](https://collegedost.in/blog/kcet/)

KCET is a state-level entrance exam conducted by the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) for admission to undergraduate courses in engineering, architecture, pharmacy, and other fields in Karnataka’s government and **private colleges**.

### PESSAT (PES University Scholastic Aptitude Test)

PESSAT is an entrance exam conducted by PES University, Bangalore, for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and other fields. It is an important exam for students seeking admission to PES University.

### [JEE Main](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/)

JEE Main is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate engineering programs in various colleges, including the National Institutes of Technology (NITs), Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs), and other participating institutions. It is the first step to qualify for JEE Advanced.
