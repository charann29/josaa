## What are the Eligibility Criteria for the AEEE Exam?

To apply for the AEEE entrance exam, students need to fulfil the following eligibility criteria:

- Educational Qualification: Candidates need to have passed Class 12 from any recognised board with 60% marks in aggregate. Must have Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
- Age Requirements: Students’ age should be between July 1, 2005 and June 30, 2010.
- JEE Main-qualified students are eligible to appear for the AEEE entrance exam as well.

## AEEE Exam Login – Step-by-Step Registration & Application Process

 For the AEEE Exam login, firstly, students need to register through the Amrita Official Website. Get the step-by-step registration and application process:

- Open the official website of [Amrita University](http://aeee.amrita.edu).
- Enter your Contact number or Email ID.
- Verify the number and enter the received OTP.
- Once submitted, the AEEE Exam login credentials are generated.
- Keep checking the registered email ID or contact number for the registration confirmation email or message.
- Move forward with the application form filling with the required academic details & personal details.
- Select the 3 preferred cities for the AEEE Exam centre.
- Upload the required documents, like a scanned signature and a photo.
- Pay the required amount of AEEE Exam Fee through Net Banking/debit, or credit card.
- Submit the application form.

### How to book the AEEE Exam slots?

After the applications are done, students need to go ahead with the slot booking for the AEEE Entrance exam. Slot bookings are also done automatically if students do not book them separately. It is generally automatically booked 7 days prior to the exam date. But to do it manually, students need to follow the given steps:

- Use AEEE exam login credentials and Mobile number to log in on the official website.
- Give the OTP and login.
- Choose from the available exam date, time, and centre. The booking is done on first-cum-first-serve basis.
- On confirming the booking, a prompt will be displayed on screen. Students also receive a confirmation message.

The slot booking window for the AEEE Phase 2 exam 2026 will be open from April 10, 2026, to April 20, 2026.

## How can students prepare for the AEEE Exam 2026?

For the upcoming AEEE entrance exam 2026, students need to have a proper understanding of the AEEE exam syllabus and exam pattern. To score better and manage time, students can refer to the following preparation tips:

- Get accustomed to the AEEE exam pattern & AEEE Exam syllabus.
- Plan study time table around the higher weightage sections of each subject. Start with Maths, continue with Physics, Chemistry, and end with English.
- Learn concepts, practice and revise.
- Keep the primary focus on the Class 11 and 12 NCERT syllabus and study materials. Treat it as the foundation of the entire preparation.
- Practice with the AEEE exam previous year papers, the AEEE exam sample paper, and solve full-length mock tests under timed conditions and simulate the exam environment.
- Attempt the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham entrance exam question papers to strengthen the core concepts.
- Take help from the answer keys to analyse errors.
- Solve at least 15-20 full-length mock tests.
- Analyse the errors made, target the weaker areas and study those during weekends.
- For refreshers, maintain short notes or formula sheets for tricky maths or physics formulas, derivations and short tricks.
- Students can refer to some of the preparation resources, like the recommended list of AEEE books.
- Before the exam, only revise and solve 1 mock every 2-3 days. Do not start any new topic.
- For Examday, attempt the easier portion first and leave the uncertain questions for the end.
- On the exam, use the attempt-flag-review approach as the exam is in CBT mode. It will minimise guesswork and negative marking.

As the AEEE Entrance exam is less difficult than JEE Mains, students can prepare accordingly. Remember to take enough breaks and keep consistency in the routine.

## How to download the AEEE Exam Admit Card?

AEEE admit cards are generally available after the slot booking is completed by the students. It is to be downloaded from the official website, and students need to print out a hard copy. The Admit card carries the AEEE exam details such as registration number, exam city, date, time, centre, address, and exam instructions. Students need to follow the given steps to download it online:

- Visit the official Amrita Online Portal
- Log in to the account using the Email ID, phone number, and the received OTP.
- Download the admit card from the given link.
- Keep a printed copy for future use. Students are required to carry it on exam day.

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Entrance Exam Results are published after the exams conclude around May, 2026. It is published on the Amrita University Official website. Download the AEEE Exam results as per the following guidelines:

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1.  Is the AEEE exam easier than the JEE Main?

The AEEE entrance exam is said to be similar to JEE Main but easier in comparison. The AEEE exam difficulty level is moderate.

### 2. Who can appear for the AEEE entrance exam?

Students who have passed the Class 12 exam with 60% and above can appear for the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham exam or the AEEE Entrance exam

### 3. Is the AEEE entrance exam only for Amrita?

Yes, AEEE is the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Entrance Exam for the B.Tech programme admissions. But other universities may also accept AEEE ranks only if they have a separate MOU or tie-up.

### 4. What is the AEEE Exam?

AEEE exam stands for Amrita Entrance Exam – Engineering, organised and conducted by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham for engineering admission. It is conducted across 8 campuses of Amrita.

### 5.  Is there negative marking in the AEEE Entrance exam?

Yes, for each wrong answer, students get a negative marking of -1.

### 6. What is the AEEE Counselling process?

After the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Entrance Exam Results are declared, students need to register for the counselling process. This counselling is conducted by Amrita University and is known as CSAP counselling. During registration, students are required to lock in their campus and branch choices. Seats are then allotted as per their preferences during counselling round based on their rank and seat availability.

### 7. What are the available Scholarship policies under the AEEE Exam 2026?

Students performing well in the AEEE will be awarded merit scholarships. Students with above 80% marks in the last qualifying exam and AEEE will get up to 100% under the Amrita Chancellor scholarship scheme. While the Amrita meritorious scholarship scheme provides 50% scholarship to students scoring 70% to 79% marks in Class 12 exam and AEEE. Selected students need to pay as per the scholarship slab fees structure of Amrita University.
