## **Documents Required for JEE Mains 2025**

According to NTA information bulletin, candidates were required to submit various documents ranging from their academics to personal details. **Scanned copies of their signature** and photographs should be uploaded by the candidates while registering for JEE Mains 2025. Category certificate numbers for candidates from reserved categories (**Gen-EWS/SC/ST/OBC-NCL**) should be provided during the registration process. The table shows the list of important documents required for JEE Mains 2025. Both male and female candidates have to submit the same specified documents for registration. Candidates seeking reservations should submit additional documents for registration.

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Document** | **Specifications** | **Format** |
| Signature | 10 to 50 kb | JPG/JPEG |
| Photo | Photographs should cover 80% of your face. Photos with masks are not allowed. The photo size should be 10 to 300 KB | JPG/JPEG |
| Class X Marksheet | 10 to 50 KB | PDF |
| PwD certificate (If applicable) | 10 to 50 KB | PDF |

## JEE Mains Registration 2025: Steps to Apply

To appear in JEE Mains 2025, candidates were required to fill out the registration form. Following these simple steps below candidates could easily fill out the application form:

1. Go to the official website at **jeemain.nta.nic.in**.
2. On the homepage, look for the link titled **“Registration for JEE Mains 2025 ”** and click on it.
3. You will be redirected to a new page.
4. Create your account and fill out the application form with the required details.
5. Pay the application fee and submit the form.
6. Don’t forget to download and print the confirmation page for future use.

Candidates can directly access the registration link from the website as well.
According to the official notification, **JEE Mains 2025 Session 2 was held from April 1 to April 8, 2025**.

## Tips for Uploading Documents Required for JEE Mains 2025

Uploading documents is an important step during JEE Mains 2025 Registration.  Submitting any wrong/ incomplete information may lead to the cancellation of your candidature. Here are some tips to help you carefully upload the required documents.

- Follow the specified(PDF/JPEG/JPG) formats to upload your documents.
- Follow the specified size and use online tools if you face any problems in resizing.
- Carefully enter all your details and check twice before submitting.
- Provide a working email and mobile number, as any information is communicated to you through these channels.
- Ensure your photograph has a white background, and 80% of your photo should cover your face.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **What Documents are Required for JEE Mains Registration?**

Documents required for JEE Mains 2025 are: Class X mark sheet, photo and signature copies, and PwD certificate if applicable. Also, details about your personal identification are required

### 2. **What Documents are required for the JEE Mains application form for OBC candida**tes?

While most of the documents required are the same for everyone, persons who want to avail of reservation benefits have to submit their category certificate number.

### 3. What is the Application Fee for JEE Mains 2025?

The JEE Mains 2025 application fee for Male candidates is 1000, and for female candidates is 800. Candidates from SC/ST/PwD and third gender have to pay 500 for registration.

### 4.****Do We Need a Category Certificate for JEE Main 2025?****

Yes, a category certificate number and details of the issuing authority are important for JEE Mains 2025 registration.
