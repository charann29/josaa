## NIT Calicut BTech Admission Process

The NIT Calicut BTech admission is strictly based on JEE Main and JoSAA counselling. Refer to the following to go through the complete admission process of NIT Calicut for BTech program.

**Step 1: Appear in JEE Main**

To be admitted to NIT Calicut, candidates are required to register and take JEE Main since it is the main entrance examination for NIT Calicut BTech admissions.

**Step 2: Qualify the Entrance Exam**

To be eligible for NIT Calicut BTech, students must obtain a valid rank in JEE Main for NIT Calicut BTech admission.

**Step 3: JoSAA Counselling registration**

Applicants should undergo JoSAA counselling, which is in charge of seat distribution of the NIT Calicut course.

**Step 4: Choice Filling and Locking**

The applicants will be required to choose the branches of their choice in the counselling process that NIT Calicut courses offered will be providing.

**Step 5: Seat Allotment**

The allocation of seats in the NIT Calicut BTech course is done according to rank, category and availability.

**Step 6: Document Authentication and Fee**

To secure a seat at NIT Calicut, candidates will have to submit documents and pay the admission fee as part of the NIT Calicut courses and fees.
