## Steps to Download TNEA Rank List from the Website

Candidates can follow the steps mentioned below to download the TNEA Rank List 2025:

## TNEA Counselling

The [**TNEA 2025 counselling**](https://collegedost.in/blog/tnea/tnea-counselling/) process is conducted **completely online** through the official website [www.tneaonline.org](https://www.tneaonline.org), where candidates must log in using their User ID and Password to participate. The process is divided into three rounds, each consisting of four stages: Choice Filling, Tentative Allotment, Confirmation of Allotment, and Reporting to the college or TFC (TNEA Facilitation Centre). For assistance, candidates can visit nearby TFCs available in all districts or call the toll-free helpline at 1800-425-0110 or email tneacare@gmail.com. **There is no counselling fee,** but candidates must pay the prescribed fees after allotment to confirm their seats. Special reservation counselling is conducted for categories such as Eminent Sportspersons, Differently Abled, and Wards of Ex-Servicemen, followed by general and vocational counselling. The number of choices a candidate can select is unlimited, and these choices can be reordered or modified until locked. Candidates who forget to fill in their choices in the first round will be moved to the next round. Upon allotment, candidates must choose from the following options: Accept and Join, Accept and Move Upward, Decline and Proceed to Next Round, or Quit. Provisional or Tentative Allotment Orders can be downloaded from the login. Candidates must report to the college or TFC within five days to confirm their seat; failing to do so, the allotment will be cancelled.
