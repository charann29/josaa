## NIT Calicut Admission Eligibility | BTech Program

The following table contains the complete NIT Calicut admission criteria for students looking to pursue BTech program from the institute. Students must note that anyone failing to meet the eligibility criteria will not be able to participate in the NIAT Calicut admission process 2026.

- The candidates should have qualified Class 12 (10+2) from a recognised board.
- Students should have studied Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in Class 12.
- Applicants must also achieve a minimum of 75% marks in Class 12
- In the case of reserved categories (SC/ST), one needs to score 65% marks.
- Applicants must qualify for the JEE Main examination.
- Admission is through JoSAA counselling based on JEE Main rank.
- Students must meet the age criteria as specified for JEE Main.
- Both Indian nationals and eligible foreign candidates can apply.

## NIT Calicut Admission Process for BTech

Students will be mandated to follow the application process mentioned below to successfully secure a seat in NITC.

**Step 1: Check Eligibility Criteria**

Before filling out the application, all applicants must check the NIT Calicut Admission Eligibility for admission to the B.Tech program at NIT Calicut. To be eligible, students have to pass Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics with minimum marks. The first step toward getting admission in NIT Calicut is checking eligibility.

**Step 2: Appear for the Entrance Exam**

Students must take the JEE Main exam in order to gain admission into NIT Calicut for BTech under NIT Calicut btech admission. These tests assess candidates on common subjects and play an essential role in their chances of selection.

**Step 3: Registration and Application**

To apply for the NIT Calicut B.Tech program, students need to register on the exam official portal, which is the NIT Calicut Application Link. Filling in the application form to the fullest is crucial for going forward in the NIT Calicut admission process.

**Step 4: Verify Cutoff and Ranking**

After the declaration of results, candidates must go through the NIT Calicut Cutoff to check their chances of admission. Every year, the cutoff changes. The cutoff change is due to the difficulty level of the exam, the number of candidates, and the availability of seats.

**Step 5: Participate in the Counselling Process**

NIT Calicut Admission Process includes a JoSAA counselling round for eligible candidates only. This procedure consists of registration, filling up of choice and locking.

**Step 6: Process of Allotment of Seats**

The NIT Calicut Selection Criteria include the allocation of seats based on availability, preferences and ranks. Candidates need to keep checking the allotment results and respond within the given time.

**Step 7: Verification of Documents**

Once a seat is allocated, candidates must complete document verification to prove their eligibility for NIT Calicut Admission. All the academic and identity documents should be submitted as per the guidelines.

**Step 8: Course Fee Deposit or Payment**

Candidates must pay the NIT Calicut Admission Fee to secure their seat. Candidate confirmation and payment finalise onboarding after completion of the process.

**Step 9: Final Reporting at the Institute**

Candidates should arrive at the National Institute of Technology Calicut per the schedule. One can be assured that one will not miss any important NIT Calicut Admission Dates through the official notifications.

Students can appear for JEE Main twice in a year for NIT Calicut Admission 2026.

Yes, students must appear for JoSAA counselling as part of the NIT Calicut Admission Process.
