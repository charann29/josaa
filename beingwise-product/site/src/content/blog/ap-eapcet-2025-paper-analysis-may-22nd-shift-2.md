## AP EAMCET 2025 Exam: May 22, 2025, Shift 2

The **AP EAMCET 2025** exam held on **May 22, Shift 2**, was generally perceived as **moderate to tough**, with a difficulty rating of **7.1 out of 10**, making it one of the more challenging shifts of the exam. The **Mathematics** section was particularly demanding, featuring lengthy and complex problems in **Calculus**, **Algebra**, and **Trigonometry**, requiring strong analytical skills and efficient time management. **Physics** was moderately challenging, with questions focusing on **Oscillations**, and **Laws of Motion**, blending conceptual understanding with numerical problem-solving. In contrast, **Chemistry** was considered the most approachable, emphasising **Organic Chemistry**, **Chemical Bonding**, and **Equilibrium**, with questions based mainly on NCERT concepts. Overall, candidates with a solid grasp of fundamentals and effective time management strategies found the paper manageable, though the Mathematics section posed significant challenges.

## AP EAMCET 2025 Mathematics Paper Analysis

The **AP EAMCET 2025 Mathematics** paper for **May 22nd, Shift 2** was considered **moderate to difficult** by most students.
