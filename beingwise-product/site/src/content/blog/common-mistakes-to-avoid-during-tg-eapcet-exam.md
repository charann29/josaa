JNTUH conducts TG EAPCET 2025 and will be held from 29th April to 5th May 2025. Candidates can fill out the application form on the official website from **1st March 2025 till 4th April 2025**. Candidates need to be careful of certain common mistakes to avoid during the TG EAPCET exam. One of the most common areas is the list of documents to bring. Admit card is the foremost important thing to carry in the exam hall. Moreover, extra care should be taken during the preparation of the exam.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid During TG EAPCET Exam Preparation

Most students make many mistakes during the preparation for the **TS EAMCET** exam. Trying to cover all topics at once and not focusing on the strategy are a few among the following. Check below some of the common preparation mistakes and how to avoid it.

**Not having a proper Study Plan:** Creating a structured study plan that allocates sufficient time for each subject should be the priority. You need to categorise the subjects based on your strengths and weaknesses. Allocate more time to your weak subjects and take expert help if necessary. You can also take help from [**TS EAMCET preparation books**](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/ts-eamcet-exam-preparation-books/).

**Not Choosing Quality Study Materials:** It is vital to choose the right **TS EAMCET** Study Material for guidance and learning the proper concepts. Apart from all the books listed below, you can refer to the basic **NCERT textbooks** for all the subjects. Make short notes of the important topics for revision in the last moment.

**Not Able To Filter Important Topics:** Strategic planning is very important to score high in the **TG EAPCET 2025 exam.** You should check the **previous year’s question papers** to filter out the most important topics or frequently occurring topics. Segregate each subject based on the filtered important topics and then start preparing.

**Solving questions directly and not focusing on steps:** Only solving the mock test papers or PYQs will not help if you do not have a clear understanding of the concepts. The first step is to segregate the important topics and understand the concepts by studying the topics. Solving questions will help you in understanding your weaker areas and managing time.

**Not taking enough mock tests:** After you cover the initial syllabus of TG EAPCET exam, you need to take mock tests to check your time management and quick thinking skills.

**Neglecting the PYQs and Mind Mapping:** Creating Mind Maps – Utilize mind-mapping techniques to visually organize key concepts, formulas, and their interrelationships. This approach enhances retention and understanding of complex topics.

**Rote learning and Not Revising:** The most common mistake one makes is learning without understanding anything, and not enough revising. Candidates should understand each concept deeply and practice different questions to prepare themselves. Moreover, ensure to keep enough time for revision.

**Time Management Issues:** To avoid time management issues on the day of the exam, practice enough mock tests and analyze yourself after that. You should try to finish the mock test within 2 hours 30 minutes and keep 30 minutes extra for difficult and unattempted questions. Also during the [**TG EAPCET preparation**](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/preparation-plan-for-tg-eapcet/https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/preparation-plan-for-tg-eapcet/), you can weekwise divide the subjects and important topics.

## How to Stay Healthy and Manage Stress

One of the most common mistakes to avoid during TS EAMCET 2025 exam is to manage your stress and stay healthy. Balanced diet is the most crucial aspect to manage both these factors. You can check the points below to know more about it.

1. **Planning Strategically:** Break down the whole **TG EAPCET syllabus** into sections. Sort out the important topics by checking the PYQs. Make a timetable where you allot time for each subject and set daily, weekly, and monthly goals.

2. **Effective Study Techniques:** Make mind maps, flow charts, flashcards, and pointers to remember the important topics. Practice PYQs and teach your peer to get more clarity on the concepts. Discuss the topics with your peer or take help from any teacher.

3. **Healthy Lifestyle:** Have a balanced diet. Avoid junk food and excessive oily food. Get at least 7 to 8 hours of sleep and stay hydrated. Eat home-cooked food and avoid overeating.

4. **Stress-Relief Activities:** If you are still anxious before the exam day, you are advised to practice meditation and take one topic at a time. Also, take plenty of breaks amidst your study. You should also play outside and engage with your friends for distraction. Too much focus can also cause stress and will lead to low marks. Keep a positive mindset and approach with confidence to ace the exam.

## Time Management During the Exam

You need to practice enough mock test papers to manage time during the TG EAPCET exam. Check some of the pointers that you can keep in your mind while appearing for the exam.

- Attempt the easier problems at first, as this will build confidence and also save time for the tougher ones.
- Learn shortcuts for calculations in Mathematics.
- Solve conceptual questions first, followed by the numerical problems.
- Do not waste time on one problem and skip to the next one. That way you can save time and come back later for the difficult ones.

## Mistakes to Avoid On the Day of TG EAPCET Examination

Check below some of the common mistakes to avoid on the day of the TS EAMCET examination.

**Arriving Late:** One of the most common mistakes which can make any student stress and start the examination on an anxious nature is arriving late to the exam hall. You should reach the exam hall 1 hour in advance of the reporting time to have a smooth start of the examination.

**Inappropriate Attire:** You will be subjected to security checks, and wearing complex and party clothes will be inappropriate and make the checking process more difficult. Though there is no official dress code for the examination. But candidates should adhere to formal and simple[**dress for both male and female**](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/tg-eapcet-dress-code/)**.**

**Missing Documents:** The most important thing to bring to the **TG EAPCET** exam hall is the Admit card. Candidates will not be allowed in the exam hall if they do not bring the admit card.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **********************Can I wear a watch for the TG EAPCET exam?**********************

No, any kind of electronic gadgets, including watches, are not allowed in the exam hall of TS EAMCET 2025.

### 2. ******Is the TG EAPCET Exam Tough?******

TS EAMCET/TG EAPCET exam can be considered moderately difficult. But the exam is easier than the JEE Main exam for the engineering program. However, with the proper preparation and strategy, anyone can crack the exam.

### 3. ************What should be my last minute preparation for TG EAPCET exam?************

For the last minute preparation, revise the topics and do not learn any extra topics. Keep your mind calm and have a positive attitude. Revise all the formulas, drink plenty of water, and eat good food to avoid falling sick. You should avoid getting burned out and being anxious.

### 4. ************Is there any formal dress code for the TS EAMCET exam?************

No, there are no formal dress codes for the TS EAMCET exam. However, candidates are advised to wear simple clothes and not flashy or party wear on the TG EAPCET exam hall.

### 5. **********What happens if I forget to carry my admit card on the day of the TG EAPCET exam?**********

Admit card is the mandatory document to bring on the day of the exam. You will be prohibited from appearing for the exam if you forget to bring the TG EAPCET 2025 admit card.

### 6. ******How can I stay stress-free before the TG EAPCET 2025 exam?******

Plan and cover all the important topics. Take frequent breaks between your studies and stay hydrated. Also, eat a healthy and balanced diet to avoid falling sick.

### 7. **Are calculators allowed in the TG EAPCET exam 2025?**

No, any kind of calculators or log books are not allowed in the TG EAPCET exam.

### 8. ****Can I enter the TG EAPCET exam hall if I am 10 minutes late?****

No, the authorities will not allow any candidate to enter the TS EAMCET exam hall even if they are 1 minute late. Thus, all the candidates are advised to arrive at least 2 hours before the start of the exam.

### 9. **************How many questions will there be in the TS EAMCET exam?**************

There are a total of 160 questions in TG EAPCET exam. 1 mark is awarded for each correct answer, which makes the total marks to be 160. Physics and Chemistry hold 40 marks and Mathematics have 80 marks. There is no negative marking.

### 10. **How much do I need to get to pass the exam?**

You need to score 40 out of 160 marks to pass the TS EAMCET 2025 exam. 25% is the minimum qualifying marks out of the total score.
