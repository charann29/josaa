## JEE Mains 2026 Admit Card Test Instructions: Allowed and Prohibited Items

To prevent disqualification from the JEE Main exam, students need to read the instructions on their admit card carefully and follow them. **Permitted Items in the JEE Main Examinations Centre:**

- Hardcopy of JEE Mains 2026 admit card.
- 1 passport-size photo (the same as the one you uploaded when filling the application form)

- Purses, wallets, bags or handbags.
- Packaged food, drinks or snacks.
- Metal adornments and any accessories.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ******When will the JEE Main 2026 admit card be released?******

The JEE Main 2026 admit card for session 1 has been released on 17th January, 2026. Usually, the hall ticket is published 3 to 4 days before the exam date. ​

### ************2. ********How to download JEE Main admit card?********************

Candidates can download it from the official NTA JEE Main website (jeemain.nta.nic.in.).​

### **3.************Will the JEE Mains admit card 2026 have my exam centre address and date?**************

Yes, the JEE Mains hall ticket contains the applicant’s exam centre address, allotted date and time.

### 4. **********Can I download the JEE Main 2026 admit card for both session 1 and session 2?**********

Each session’s admit card is released separately; those who have registered for both sessions will be able to access both sessions’ admit cards when released.

### 5. **What should I do if there’s a mistake on my admit card?** ?

Students are advised to connect with the concerned authorities (NTA) if there is any discrepancy in the admit card of JEE Mains 2026. ​

### 6. **Is the JEE Main city intimation slip the same as the admit card?**

No, the city intimation slip shows your allotted city, while the admit card contains the complete address. An admit card is mandatory to take the exam, and a city slip is not. ​

### 7. **What happens if I forget my application number for the admit card?**

Students can use the Forgot Application Number option on the website to retrieve it using their personal details.
