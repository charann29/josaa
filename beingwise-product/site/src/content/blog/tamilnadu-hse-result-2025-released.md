The TNDGE is set to release the Higher Secondary Examination (HSE) +1 (Class 11) results for the 2024-25 academic year on **May 16, 2025, at 2 PM**. Over 8 lakh students who appeared for the exams held between March 5 and March 27, 2025, are eagerly awaiting their results. Moreover, **the SSCL Class 10th result will also be released on 16th May, 2025 at 9 AM**.

Students need to visit the official website, <https://tnresults.nic.in/>, to download their marksheet.

## Steps To Check Tamil Nadu HSE+1 Result 2025

Students can check the steps below to know the step-by-step process to download their HSE+1 provisional marksheet.

- Visit the Official Website: <https://tnresults.nic.in/>

- Click on “HSE(+1) Arrears Examination Results March 2025”

- Next, give your registration number and date of birth.
- Click on “Get Marks”.
- The TN Class 11 result will be visible on your screen.

### Alternative Methods to Check Results

**DigiLocker:** Students can access their digital marksheet securely through DigiLocker. You need to first ensure your DigiLocker account is linked with your Aadhaar for smooth access.

**Mobile App:** For checking Class 10 results, students can also download the ‘TN SSLC Result 2025’ app from the Google Play Store. They can use it to receive instant notifications and check their result directly.

### Post-Result Process And Improvement Options

In case of any discrepancies or errors in the provisional marksheet, students should immediately report to the DGE or their school authorities for corrections. Students can take their original marksheet from their respective school teachers. They can also request revaluation or retotaling if they are unhappy with their grades. Students can also apply to the tndge.org application portal for photocopies of answer sheets. A revaluation charge of INR 505 per topic is required.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****When will the Tamil Nadu HSE +1 result be released?****

The Tamil Nadu HSE +1 results will be released on May 16, 2025, at 2:00 PM IST.

### 2. ****Where can I check my Tamil Nadu Class 11 results?****

You can check your result on the official website by giving the registration number and date of birth. Results will also be available via DigiLocker and can be sent to your registered mobile number via SMS.

### 3. ****What details do I need to check in my Class 11 provisional marksheet?****

On receiving the TN HSE+1 provisional marksheet, you must check your name and its spelling, date of birth, registration number, and all the subjects. In case of any discrepancies, you should immediately report it to your school authorities.

### 4. ****When can I expect my original mark sheet?****

You will receive mark sheets from your respective school teachers, about a couple of weeks after the online results are declared. Your school will let you know when the mark sheets are available.
