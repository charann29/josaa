## Continuing Policies and Renewal at Christ University Scholarships

Most scholarships at Christ University are awarded for the first year only. Some are subject to renewal/continuation depending upon certain metrics.

**Common requirements to renew a scholarship are:**

- The scholarship holder must achieve and maintain certain academic metrics (e.g. a specified GPA, class grade, or percentage).
- The scholarship holder must achieve and maintain an attendance quota (e.g. 90% of classes).
- The scholarship holder must not have any backlogs or failed subjects.
- The scholarship holder must not have any disciplinary issues.

**For sports/cultural scholarships:** continued active and satisfactory contribution to university scholarship-related activities.

**Board Approval:** Renewal policies for scholarships are decided by a scholarship board every year. Students may have to submit additional documents or progress reports to facilitate the renewal process.

**Minimum Eligibility Criteria:** If the scholarship holder fails to meet any of the criteria or if the scholarship holder is found to have provided false information, the scholarship may be terminated.

## Christ University Entrance Exam

Christ University Entrance Test (CUET) is the common Christ University Entrance Exam conducted for admissions to Christ University. It provides an opportunity to access different BTech programs through the Christ University exam process. The test evaluates knowledge, aptitude, and analytical abilities based on the course you are applying for. For candidates applying in the next round, the Christ University Entrance Exam 2026 is a significant component of the overall selection process, along with skill evaluation, micro presentation, or personal interview sessions.

### When is the Christ University Entrance Exam Conducted?

The Christ University Entrance Exam is conducted from April to June every year in different phases. It is an online Christ University exam, and candidates can select a preferred slot during the registration process. The dates for the Christ University Entrance Exam 2026 are announced on the official website along with the admission process.

### How to Apply for the Christ University Entrance Exam

Check out the steps below to successfully apply for the Christ University Entrance Test (CUET).

- Visit the official Christ University admissions website to apply for the Christ University Entrance Exam
- Register with a valid email ID and mobile number
- Submit the application form for the course you are applying for under the Christ University exam
- Select the Christ University Entrance Exam 2026 and your desired test date
- Upload required documents, such as a photograph and signature
- Pay the application fee online for the Christ University exam
- Finalise the application form and download the confirmation page
