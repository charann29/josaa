**AP EAMCET Results 2025:** The Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) released the AP EAMCET 2025 Exam Result on June 8th,2025. The examination authorities released the AP EAMCET 2025 Results through the official website. Candidates can check their examination results through the following link: <https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/Eapcet/EAPCET_GetResult.aspx>.

Candidates can also download their Rank Card through the link below. The candidates require their Registration Number, AP EAMCET Hall Ticket Number, and Date of Birth to download their Rank Card.

The AP EAMCET 2025 exam **for the Engineering stream** will be held from **19.05.2025 to 20.05.2025** and the **Agriculture & Pharmacy Stream** examination from **22.05.2025 to 27.05.2025.**

The Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (AP EAPCET) is a state-level entrance test conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada, under the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) every year.

## Steps to check the AP EAPCET 2025 Examination Result

**Step 1:** Visit the AP EAPCET 2025 Official Website, <https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/>

**Step 2:** Click on the AP EAPCET 2025 Results link.

**Step 3:** Log in to the link using the candidate’s Registration Number and Hall Ticket Number.

**Step 4:** The AP EAMCET 2025 Results will be displayed on the screen.

**Step 5:** The candidate can download the AP EAMCET 2025 scorecard and secure it for future reference.
