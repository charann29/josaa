AP-EAPCET is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education(APSCHE). **AP-EAMCET** provides a chance for candidates to secure admission into prestigious colleges for undergraduate courses like Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy. **AP-EAMCET exam** will be held from **May 21st to May 27th, 2025**, and Candidates preparing for the exam might have to go through a hectic exam preparation process. Although it is important to prepare for the exam, there are certain guidelines that candidates have to follow during the exam for a seamless examination process.

The dress code is one of the major confusions among candidates; officials have not mentioned any guidelines for the dress code. Although there is no official dress code prescribed, candidates can stick to some recommended attire advice to avoid inconvenience during the examination process. A simple dress code can reduce distractions and complications during security checks. This article will give you a brief idea of the dress code to appear for

**[AP-EAMCET.](https://collegedost.in/blog/category/ap-eamcet/)**

## AP-EAPCET Dress Code for Male Candidates

Male candidates are advised to wear simple clothing and avoid complex clothes while going to the exam centre. A simple attire will prevent you from multiple security checks and saves you a lot of time. Male candidates can follow the dress code mentioned below:

- **Upper Wear:** Avoid T-shirts/Shirts with heavy-looking images and graphics, wear plain T-shirts/Shirts for the exam. Avoid wearing hooded T-shirts or shirts with large buttons, as they might consume a lot of time during security checks.

- **Lower Wear:** Avoid pants such as cargos and torn jeans, as they can raise security concerns. Wear simple trousers or chinos etc.

- **Accessories:** Avoid wearing jewellery like bracelets, rings, etc, as metallic items are prohibited inside the examination hall.

- **Footwear**: Avoid wearing footwear with thick soles as they might require additional checking. Prefer wearing shoes or sandals.

## AP-EAMCET Dress Code for Female Candidates

A casual attire like kurtas, shirts or T-shirts can ensure comfort and convenience for female candidates. This simple attire helps you avoid multiple security checks, here’s a dress code for female candidates:

- **Upper wear:** Plain kurtas, shirts, or T-shirts are recommended. Avoid heavy embroidery, multiple layers, or frilly dresses to minimize security checks. Also, refrain from wearing clothes with metal elements, such as belts or underwired bras.

- **Lower Wear:** I prefer wearing leggings and jeans. Avoid wearing cargo pants distressed jeans, etc.

- **Accessories:** Maintain a simple hairstyle while appearing for the exam and avoid wearing jewellery like bracelets, hair clips, rings, and necklaces.

- **Footwear**: Opt for simple chappals or sandals. Avoid heels, boots, or any footwear that could draw unnecessary attention or cause discomfort during the exam.

## Guidelines for the AP-EAPCET 2025

Candidates are advised to stick to the following guidelines to avoid discrepancies and ensure a smooth examination experience:

- **Arrive on Time:** Arrive at least 30 minutes before the exam commences, this can help you complete security checks and other formalities without rushing.

- **Documents:** Carry the set of required documents, which include an admit card, passport-sized photograph, photo identity card such as aadhar/PAN/voter ID, etc.

- **Frisking Procedure:** Be prepared to undergo mandatory frisking before entering the examination hall. Cooperate with the security personnel to ensure a smooth and efficient process.

- **Seating arrangement:** Settle in your designated seat after entering the examination hall. Stay calm and wait for the invigilator’s instructions.

- **Follow Instructions:** Pay close attention to all instructions provided by the invigilators and those printed on the question paper and answer sheet.
