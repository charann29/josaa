Over one lakh aspirants apply for the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination (WBJEE), making it one of the most competitive exams. **WBJEE** offers a chance for candidates to get admitted into undergraduate courses like Engineering, Pharmacy, and Architecture in West Bengal. Among the three subjects, Physics, chemistry, and mathematics, Physics and chemistry can often pose a challenge in terms of speed and accuracy. Students have to gain a strong command over problem-solving in order to save time and perform efficiently in exams. In this blog, we’ll discuss some effective strategies to improve your speed and accuracy in WBJEE Physics and Chemistry.

## WBJEE Exam Tips

### 1. Focus on Learning the Basics

Gaining a strong foundation in the basic concepts helps in understanding complex problems. Physics and Chemistry require a deep understanding of fundamental concepts.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Physics** | **Chemistry** |
| Start with basic topics like mechanics, thermodynamics, and electromagnetism.  Focus on understanding advanced concepts like derivations, laws, and principles. Avoid memorization and familiarise yourself with standard equations and formulas. | Learn basic reactions, mechanisms, etc for Organic chemistry. Focus on bonding concepts and coordination compounds as they are usually asked in the exam. Understand key principles such as molarity, normality, and chemical equilibrium for Physical chemistry. |

### 2. Manage your Time

Students are required to answer 155 questions in 4 hours, which makes the exam highly time-bound. Failing to manage your time effectively might lose you marks, which reduces your chances of admission. Physics and Chemistry sections can be lengthy, and students often spend too much time on difficult questions. To avoid this, practice time management during your preparation.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Physics** | **Chemistry** |
| Practice solving problems of varying difficulty levels.  Measure the time you are taking to solve the problems and try to improve consistently. Learn to solve time-consuming problems. Use calculation tricks to solve the problems. | Practice solving problems in each chapter. Set a time and try to solve MCQs and match the following questions in the specified time. Mole concepts or stoichiometry can take longer, so practice them regularly. |

### 3. Solve Previous Year’s Papers and Mock Tests

One of the best ways to boost speed and accuracy is by solving [Previous year’s WBJEE papers](https://collegedost.in/blog/wbjee/wbjee-previous-year-question-papers/). This helps you understand the question pattern and the problems frequently asked in the exam.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Physics** | **Chemistry** |
| Refer to Previous year’s Question papers to understand the types of questions asked. Try techniques like elimination/trial and error for faster calculations. Work on areas where you are weak. | Practice the most frequently asked questions and make a note of them. Simulate real exam-like conditions to get familiar with the exam. |

### 4. Try Different Problem-Solving Techniques

Refer few online sources/[books](https://collegedost.in/blog/wbjee/best-books-for-wbjee-exam-preparation/) to learn efficient problem-solving techniques to increase your accuracy and score high in WBJEE 2025. Here are a few techniques to learn:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Physics** | **Chemistry** |
| Use the process of elimination. Unit conversion Trial and error method. Quick approximations | Learn all the important formulas. Use shortcuts to balance chemical formulas. Quick Calculations |

### 5. Understand the Concepts

The main reason for slow problem-solving and mistakes in both Physics and Chemistry is lack of clarity on concepts. If you’re unsure about a topic, you’ll likely take longer to understand the problem and make errors during calculations.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Physics** | **Chemistry** |
| Focus on frequently asked concepts like laws of motion, Newton’s laws, energy conversion, etc. | In Physical Chemistry, concepts like equilibrium and kinetics can be confusing.  Make sure to understand the underlying principles, as they will help you solve problems faster. |

### 6. Stay Focused

Keeping your senses in control during the exam can improve your speed and accuracy. Stress can cause mistakes and loss of marks.

- **Stay positive:** Confidence in your preparation will reduce anxiety during the exam.

- **Take breaks:** During your study sessions, take breaks to recharge your mind. A rested brain works much faster and with more accuracy.

- **Avoid guesswork:** If you’re unsure about an answer, avoid making random guesses. Eliminate obviously incorrect options and make a smart guess rather than blind guessing.

## Conclusion

To improve speed and accuracy in **[WBJEE Exam](https://collegedost.in/blog/category/wbjee/)**, you need to master the basics, smart techniques, and extensive practice. Make sure to focus on time management, solving past papers, and your problem-solving strategies. Additionally, maintaining mental health and reducing stress is essential for optimal performance. With consistent effort and smart strategies, you’ll be well-prepared to face the WBJEE with confidence and perform at your best.
