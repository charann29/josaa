## How to download MHT CET 2026 Admit Card Online?

The MHT CET 2026 admit card is released by the Maharashtra CET Cell through the official website. Here are some steps that candidates have to follow in order to get the MHT CET 2026 admit card easily.

**Step 1: Visit the Official Website**

The candidates have to visit the official website of the Maharashtra State CET Cell at cetcell.mahacet.org. All the information related to the MHT CET Admit Card 2026 and other details will be provided on the official website.

**Step 2: Find the MHT CET Admit Card Download Link**

Under the “Notifications/Downloads” section on the home page of the website, click on the “MHTCET 2026 Admit Card” link. The candidates interested in appearing for the engineering course need to find the link to the MHT CET 2026 admit card for PCM group.

**Step 3: Get Into the Login Portal**

Once you click on the MHT CET admit card link, there will be another page opened on your screen. You need to log in here using the valid credentials.

**Step 4: Enter Your Login Details**

Enter your registered Email ID, application number, password, and security captcha code in the relevant fields. Ensure that you enter all details correctly to prevent login errors during the download of the MHT CET 2026 PCM Admit Card.

**Step 5: Submit the Entered Details**

After entering all the login details, click the “Submit” button. Once you have done that, the MHT CET Admit Card will be shown on your computer screen.

**Step 6: Verify All Details Mentioned on the Hall Ticket**

Candidates need to check all details mentioned on the admit card for  MHT CET 2026. In case there is any discrepancy on the MHT CET Hall Ticket, candidates must inform the authorities from the Maharashtra CET Cell right away.

**Step 7: Download and Print MHT CET 2026 Admit Card PDF**

Once you have successfully logged in and downloaded the PDF file of the MHT CET Admit Card 2026, print out at least two or three copies of the same.
