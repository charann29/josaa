## **Exam Day Guidelines and Important Instructions For JEE Mains 2025**

To ensure a smooth and fair examination process, candidates must strictly follow the guidelines specified by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for the exam day. Here are the key instructions to keep in mind:

- **Reporting Time:**Arrive at the exam center well before the reporting time mentioned on the admit card. Early arrival helps complete security checks and registration without any rush. Latecomers may not be allowed entry.
- **Entry to Exam Hall:**Entry to the examination hall will be permitted only after proper verification of the admit card and photo ID. Candidates must occupy their allotted seats as per the seating plan.
- **Frisking and Security Checks:**All candidates will undergo mandatory frisking and security checks before entering the exam hall. Metallic items, electronic gadgets, and any prohibited items will not be allowed.
- **Allowed Items:**Only the following items are permitted inside the exam hall:
  - JEE Main Admit Card (printed copy)
  - Valid photo ID (as per registration)
  - Passport size photographs (as specified)
  - Transparent water bottle (for diabetic candidates, fruits/sugar tablets as permitted)
  - Black or blue ballpoint pen
- **Prohibited Items:**Do not carry any of the following:
  - Electronic devices (mobile phones, calculators, smartwatches, earphones, etc.)
  - Stationery items other than permitted pens
  - Bags, wallets, purses, hats, or caps
  - Food items (except as allowed for diabetic candidates)
  - Any textual material, papers, or notes
- **Candidate Conduct:**Maintain silence and discipline at the exam centre. Any form of malpractice, communication with other candidates, or disobedience of the invigilator’s instructions may lead to disqualification.
- **Leaving the Exam Hall:**Candidates are not allowed to leave the examination hall before the end of the examination duration, even if they have finished early.
- **Emergency Procedures:**Follow instructions from the invigilators in case of any emergency or announcement.

Strict adherence to these guidelines is essential. Non-compliance may result in denial of entry or cancellation of candidature.

## **City Intimation Slip for JEE Mains Session 2**

The City Intimation Slip is issued by the National Testing Agency (NTA) a few days before the release of the admit card. This slip contains information about the city where the candidate’s exam center will be located. However, it does not act as an admit card and cannot be used to enter the examination hall.

The main purpose of the City Intimation Slip is to help candidates make travel and accommodation arrangements in advance, especially if their exam center is located in a different city. Candidates can download the City Intimation Slip from the official JEE Mains website (jeemain.nta.nic.in) using their application number and date of birth.

### **JEE Mains Session 2 Admit Card 2025**

The Admit Card is a mandatory document that allows candidates to enter the examination hall and appear for the JEE Mains 2025 exam. It is usually released a few days after the City Intimation Slip is issued. The JEE Mains Session 2 Admit Card contains crucial information such as the exam date, shift timing, exact exam center address, candidate’s photograph, signature, and exam instructions. Candidates must download their Admit Card from the official NTA website and take a clear printout. It is essential to verify all the details mentioned on the admit card and contact the NTA immediately if any discrepancies are found.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****Who can appear for JEE Mains 2025?****

Candidates who have passed the class 12th examination and are appearing for the class 12th examination are eligible to appear for JEE Mains 2025.

### ************2. ******How is the JEE Mains 2025 rank calculated?******************

The JEE Mains 2025 rank will be calculated based on the JEE Mains percentile, and a normalisation procedure will be done to calculate the JEE Mains Rank.

### **3.********When will the JEE Mains 2025 Session 2 admit card be released?**********

The JEE Mains admit card 2025 session 2 was released online by NTA on March 28, 2025, for the exam dates April 2, 3, and 4. Admit cards for the remaining dates, including April 7, 8, and 9 were released on March 29, 2025. Students can download the JEE Main session 2 admit card 2025 from the official website [jeemain.nta.nic.in](https://jeemain.nta.nic.in/).

### 4. ********How can I download the JEE Main session 2 admit card 2025?********

To download the JEE Main 2025 admit card session 2, visit the official NTA JEE Main website, click on the “Download Admit Card” link and log in using your application number, date of birth or password. The admit card will display exam details, including date, shift, and centre. It must be printed and carried to the exam hall for entry.​

### 5. What details are mentioned on the JEE Main 2025 Session 2 admit card?

The admit card contains essential candidate information such as name, application number, exam date and shift, exam centre details, candidate photograph and signature, and important exam instructions. Candidates must carefully review all information and report any errors to the NTA immediately.​

### 6.  Is it mandatory to carry the JEE Main admit card 2025 session 2 to the exam?

Yes, the JEE Main admit card 2025 session 2 is mandatory for entry to the examination hall. Along with the admit card, candidates must carry a valid photo ID proof. Without these documents, candidates will not be allowed to appear for the JEE Main 2025 session 2 exam.​

### 7.  What are the exam shifts for JEE Main 2025 Session 2?

The JEE Mains 2025 session 2 exams are conducted in two shifts daily – Morning shift from 9 AM to 12 noon and Evening shift from 3 PM to 6 PM. Candidates should verify their exact exam shift and time on the admit card.

### 9. Is JEE Main session 2 admit card released?

Yes, the JEE Main 2025 session 2 admit card release date are in phases. Admit cards for exams on April 2, 3, and 4 were released on March 28-29, 2025. For exams on April 7, 8, and 9, 2025, they were released starting April 2, 2025. Candidates can download their admit cards from the official website.

### 10. When JEE Main session 2 admit card 2025 release?

The JEE Mains 2025 session 2 admit card release started three days before each exam date. For the earliest exam dates (April 2, 3, and 4), the admit cards were released on March 28 and 29, 2025. For subsequent exam dates (April 7-9), admit cards were released from April 2, 2025, onwards.
