The KEAM stands for Kerala Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical Entrance Exam (KEAM). It is conducted by the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE). Candidates who have scored a minimum aggregate of 45% in their Higher Secondary Exam are eligible to apply for KEAM 2025. The application window was open from (tentative) March 27th and will be closed on April 19th, 2025. The **KEAM 2025 exam** will be held between **June 1st, 2025 to June 9th, 2025**.

Candidates who registered before the deadline can **upload scanned documents** to the KEAM application form **until April 24th, 2025**. Document uploads can be done through the official website cee.kerala.gov.in.

Additionally, the **facility for addition of course**(s) to the already submitted application will be available on the website [www.cee.kerala.gov.in](http://www.cee.kerala.gov.in) of the Commissioner for Entrance examinations from **23.04.2025, 10 AM to 24.04.2025, 4 PM**.

This entrance exam is for undergraduate programs such as Engineering and Pharmacy. Candidates aspiring to pursue BTech and BPharm are eligible to take the exam.

## Steps to Fill KEAM 2025 Application Form

There are 5 steps to fill out the KEAM 2025 Application form and let’s go through them in detail:

### Step 1: Registration

To register, Candidates must visit the official website [https://www.cee.kerala.gov.in/keamonline2025/](https://www.cee.kerala.gov.in/keamonline2024/). Click on the ‘**Registration Here**’ on the main page.

For registration, candidates should use their own or parents’ email ID and contact number. An OTP for forgotten passwords will be sent to the registered mobile number and email ID. Ensure that the name and date of birth match the corresponding entries on the supporting documents to be uploaded.

### Step 2: Fill the Application

Complete the basic details, including address details, academic details, reservation details, and annual family income.

### Step 3: Pay Application Fee

Candidates can pay the application fee online.

For each category, the application fee is different, Candidates can refer to the table below:

| **S.No** | **Course Opted in Application Form** | **General** | **SC** | **ST** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Engineering only/ B. Pharm only/ both | INR 875 | INR 375 | Nil |
| 2 | Architecture only/ Medical &Allied only/ both | INR 625 | INR 250 | Nil |
| 3 | Both 1 & 2 | INR 1125 | INR 500 | Nil |

### Step 4: Upload Images and Certificates

Candidates are required to upload a photograph and signature. Additionally, upload supporting documents such as proof of date of birth, special reservation, nativity proof, annual income, and communal reservation. All documents must be uploaded within the specified time.

### Step 5: Print Acknowledgement

Upon completing the online application, candidates can print the acknowledgement page for future reference. Do not send the acknowledgement page or supporting documents to the CEE office.

## Prerequisites for Registration

- Candidates should read and be aware of various clauses mentioned in the information brochure.

- Ensure that scanned copies of the photograph and signature are obtained in digital format.
- Make sure to have all the necessary documents ready before filling out the application form.
- Candidates are required to accept the declaration by ticking all the check boxes and then click on ‘Proceed for Registration’.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Who is eligible to apply for KEAM?

Candidates who have passed their higher secondary exam with a minimum aggregate of 45% are eligible to apply for KEAM.

### 2. What programs does KEAM cover?

KEAM is an entrance exam for undergraduate programs such as Engineering and Pharmacy.

### 3. How can I upload documents for the KEAM application form?

Candidates who have registered before the deadline can upload scanned documents to the KEAM application form until April 24th, 2025. Document uploads can be done through the official website cee.kerala.gov.in.

### 4. When is the application window for KEAM open?

The application window for KEAM 2025 is closed now. It was available from March 27th to April 19th, 2025.

### 5. What is the age limit to take KEAM 2025?

There is no specific age limit for appearing in KEAM, but candidates must be at least 17 years old.
