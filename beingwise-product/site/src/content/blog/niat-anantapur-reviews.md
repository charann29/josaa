## What is the NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT)?

Students exploring admissions at Best Innovation University Anantapur often come across references to the NAT examination. It is important to understand that NAT is exclusively related to NIAT’s upskilling program and is separate from the admission process of Bharatiya Engineering Science and Technology Innovation University.

- NAT stands for NxtWave Assessment Test, it is the qualifying test for enrolling in NIAT’s upskilling program.
- NAT is not BESTIU’s entrance examination. It has no direct bearing on your university admission.
- BESTIU independently evaluates students based on its own eligibility criteria (AP EAMCET, BEE, JEE Main, or merit) and admission policy.
- NxtWave can act only as a facilitator, with your written consent, your details may be shared with BESTIU. However, admission is solely BESTIU’s decision.
- Clearing NAT makes you eligible for NIAT’s upskilling program, it does not guarantee a seat in BESTIU B.Tech program.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is NIAT Anantapur Experience Centre?

NIAT (NxtWave Institute of Advanced Technologies) is an upskilling program by NxtWave that operates through a Training Centre provided by BEST Innovation University (BESTIU), Anantapur. It runs alongside the university’s B.Tech program and focuses on industry-aligned skills, project-based learning, and career readiness. NIAT is not a university, college, or academic institution.

### 2. Does NIAT offer a degree or diploma?

No. NIAT does not award any academic degree or diploma. The B.Tech degree is awarded solely by BESTIU. Students who complete NxtWave’s training program receive an IRC (Industry-Ready Certification), which is a skill-based certification and not an academic qualification.

### 3. Will clearing the NAT guarantee me a seat in BESTIU?

No. The NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test) is only the qualifying examination for NIAT’s upskilling program. Admission to BESTIU’s B.Tech program is determined entirely by the university. NxtWave may facilitate the application process, but the final admission decision rests solely with BESTIU.

### 4. Is NIAT NAAC Accredited or AICTE/UGC Approved?

No. NIAT is an upskilling program and not a degree-awarding institution. Therefore, it is not governed by NAAC, AICTE, or UGC regulations. Any applicable regulatory approvals relate to BESTIU as the university offering the B.Tech degree.

### 5. What is the difference between NIAT’s training and BESTIU’s academic program?

BESTIU is responsible for the B.Tech curriculum, academic coursework, examinations, and degree award. NIAT independently delivers industry-focused training, project-based learning, and skill development through NxtWave trainers and mentors. The two programs run in parallel but are separate from each other.

### 6. Who are the trainers at NIAT Anantapur?

NIAT’s training is delivered by NxtWave’s industry-experienced trainers and mentors. These professionals are separate from BESTIU’s faculty members. Students are also assigned Success Coaches who help with goal setting, progress tracking, and career development.

### 7. What does NIAT’s upskilling program cover at BESTIU?

The program covers Full Stack Web Development, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, REST APIs, Data Structures and Algorithms, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning fundamentals, Data Science, Aptitude Training, English Communication, and real-world projects. These modules are part of NxtWave’s training program and not BESTIU’s academic curriculum.

### 8. What fees does NIAT charge?

The total fee for the combined four-year learning experience is generally reported to be in the range of ₹8 lakh to ₹18 lakh, depending on the selected program structure and applicable terms.

### 9. Can I get an internship through NIAT’s program at BESTIU?

Yes. NIAT’s training model is designed to prepare students for internships from the first year itself. Students may gain access to internship opportunities through NxtWave’s hiring network, which includes thousands of partner companies. Internship opportunities and outcomes may vary depending on student performance and market conditions.

### 10. Is NIAT’s upskilling program at BESTIU worth it?

Many students consider NIAT valuable because of its focus on practical skills, project-based learning, industry mentorship, and career preparation alongside a B.Tech degree. Students who actively participate in the training modules and projects are generally positioned to gain the most benefit from the program.
