## PSG College of Technology Fees Structure – B.Tech Fee Components

The fee structure in PSG College of Technology for B.Tech programs varies depending on the admission quota. Students admitted through government-allocated seats via TNEA counselling have a lower fee structure than those admitted through the management quota. The complete breakdown of the PSG College of Technology Fees structure for BTech aspirants is as follows. This includes the tuition fee only, excluding other additional charges.

| Particulars | PSG Fees Under Government Quota | PSG Fees Under Management Quota |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st Year Tuition Fees | INR 55,000 | – |
| 4-Year Tuition Fees | INR 2.20 Lakhs | INR 5,60,000 – INR 5,80,000 |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What are the PSG College placement opportunities for B.Tech?

PSG College offers strong placement opportunities through its Career Development Cell, which manages recruitment drives, training, and career counselling. Top recruiters include HCL Technologies, Google, and others. The highest package offered is INR 52 LPA, while the average package is INR 11 LPA.

### 2. Are there scholarships or financial aid available for B.Tech at PSG College of Technology?

Yes, PSG College provides various scholarships based on merit and family income. These include State and Central Government scholarships, Private NGO scholarships, Alumni scholarships, and the GRD Centenary Scholarship.

### 3. Are hostel and mess charges included in the tuition fee?

No, hostel and mess charges are not included in the tuition fee and are charged separately.

### 4. Are there any one-time fees I should anticipate?

Yes, students need to pay a non-refundable one-time admission fee along with annual examination fees, which are separate from the tuition fee.

### 5. What are the branch-wise B.Tech fees at PSG College of Technology?

The B.Tech fees are almost uniform across all branches, with the total fee being approximately INR 2.20 Lakhs for the program.
