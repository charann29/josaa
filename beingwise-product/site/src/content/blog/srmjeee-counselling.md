## SRMJEEE Counselling Process 2026

Check out the SRMJEEE Counselling procedure below step-by-step:

**1. Log in to the Admission Portal**

The qualified candidates should log in to the official admission portal and enter their credentials. This action opens the Online Admission Portal (OAP), where additional processes of the SRMJEEE counselling process can be done.

**2. Download Rank Card**

The students must download their rank card through the admission portal. This is a necessary document to be involved in the counselling for SRMJEEE and the admission process.

**3. Fill Educational Details**

The students should verify their academic and qualification information correctly in the portal. All details must be accurate to prevent problems when verifying the admission during the SRMJEEE counselling procedure.

**4. Selection of Programmes**

The OAP allows applicants to choose their desired campus and programme. The selection of options should be well done, depending on interest and rank, as part of the SRMJEEE counselling schedule.

**5. Programme Allocation**

The programmes are allotted by the admission committee according to the rank, eligibility, and preferences. The course and campus allocated will be made visible on the admission portal as per the SRMJEEE counselling schedule.

**6. Accept Allotment and Fee Payment**

The students will have to confirm the allotment by paying the necessary registration and counselling fees. This fee is non-refundable and is included in the tuition fee, similar to the SRMJEEE counselling demand draft or online payment mode.

**7. Download Provisional Allotment Letter**

Upon successful payment, the candidates will be able to download their provisional allotment letter and the receipt of the fee. Inability to make payment before the deadline can lead to cancellation of the assigned seat mentioned in the SRMJEEE counselling letter.

**8. Pay the Remaining Fees to Finalise Enrolment**

The applicants will be required to pay the balance of their tuition fee within the stipulated timeline. Once they have paid, they may proceed with the online enrolment process to be assured that they are admitted as per the SRMJEEE counselling availability.

***Note:*** *Before students move on to the formalities of the admission process, they must first download the SRMJEEE counselling call letter. The SRMJEEE counselling procedure also incorporates document verification and payment, which may involve such options as the SRMJEEE counselling demand draft or online fee mode, depending on the guidelines.*

## SRMJEEE Counselling Fees 2026

Once the candidate receives their allotted seat, they have to make the total amount of Rs. 1,10,000 in payments. They have to pay a sum of Rs. 10,000 as the registration fees and another Rs. 1,00,000 as the counselling fee.

It should be noted that once the seat is allotted, the candidate will not get a refund or transfer. The candidate must pay the fees within the stipulated timeframe, failing which they forfeit the allotted seat.

## SRMJEEE Seat Allotment 2026

The results of the allotment of the seats in Phase 1, Round 1, will be declared on 14th May, 2026. Candidates will be allotted seats during every round of counselling, and seats will be allotted based on the candidate’s performance in the entrance test.

The allotted seat should then be confirmed by paying the requisite amount towards the admission fee before the deadline. Candidates who go through counselling are eligible to get seats at different SRM institutes based on their rank.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the mode of SRMJEEE counselling?

SRMJEEE counselling is conducted completely online through the official website, and there is no need to report to the campus during the counselling process.

### 2. Can I change my choices after locking them?

No, once choices are locked, they cannot be changed. Students should carefully review their preferences before final submission.

### 3. Is it necessary to attend all counselling rounds?

No, attending all rounds is optional, but participating in more rounds increases your chances of seat allotment.

### 4. What happens if I miss a counselling deadline?

If you miss a deadline, you may be disqualified from that particular round and will have to wait for the next round.

### 5. Can I get admission without counselling?

No, admission to B.Tech courses is only possible through the official counselling process.

### 6. How is seat allotment decided?

Seat allotment is based on the candidate’s rank, preferences, and availability of seats.

### 7. Is the counselling fee refundable?

The counselling fee is non-refundable and non-transferable, but it is usually adjusted against the tuition fee.

### 8. Is seat upgradation possible?

Yes, seat upgradation may be available in subsequent rounds depending on rank and seat availability.

### 9. Do I need to visit the campus during counselling?

No, counselling is conducted online. However, physical reporting may be required after final admission.

### 10. What if my documents are not verified?

If document verification fails, your admission may be rejected as per the counselling guidelines.
