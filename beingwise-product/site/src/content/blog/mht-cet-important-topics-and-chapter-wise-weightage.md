To score well in MHTCET, candidates must cover the entire syllabus, focusing particularly on important topics to optimize the preparation time. Below are some key chapters that candidates might prioritize while preparing for MHTCET. The exam questions are based on the syllabus provided by the Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell.

The MHTCET exam questions are based on the syllabus from class 11th carrying 20% weightage and from class 12th carrying 80% weightage.

## MHTCET Important Topics

Below are the MHTCET important for each subject:

### Mathematics Important Topics

- Trigonometric functions
- Line and Plane
- Indefinite Integration
- Differential Equation
- Differentiation
- Applications and Derivatives

### Chemistry Important Topics

- Solid state
- Solutions
- Ionic Equilibrium
- Chemical Thermodynamics
- Electrochemistry
- Chemical Kinetics
- Elements of groups – 16,17 and 18
- Transition and inner transition elements
- Halogen Derivatives
- Alcohols, phenols, and ethers
- Aldehydes, Ketones and carboxylic acids
- Biomolecules

### Physics Important Topics

- Wave optics
- Electromagnetic Induction
- AC Circuits
- Structure of Atom & Nuclei
- Current Electricity
- Magnetic Field due to current electricity

### 3. How Can I score 99 percentile in MHTCET 2025?

To score 99 percentile in MHTCET follow these steps:
**–** **Syllabus Analysis:** Analyze the entire syllabus thoroughly, identifying important topics across subjects.
**–** **Focus on Timetable:** Create a study timetable, allocating more time to high-weightage topics in each subject.
**–** **Practice Tests:** Solve problems from various sources, including previous years’ papers and mock tests.
**–** **Revision and Concept Clarity:** Regularly revise concepts and formulas to remember easily. Focus on clarifying doubts to understand better about the topic.
**–** **Stay Updated:** Keep abreast of exam syllabus and paper pattern changes.
**–** **Seek Guidance:** Consult teachers, mentors, or peers for support and guidance.
