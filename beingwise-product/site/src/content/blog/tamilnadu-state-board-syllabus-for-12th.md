## TamilNadu State Board Syllabus for 12th – Subject-Wise Tips To Prepare

Covering the entire State Board of Tamil Nadu syllabus 12th standard can become overwhelming for students appearing for TN HSC. Students need to strategise and make plans according to their strengths and weaknesses. Creating a study routine and allocating time equally amongst the subjects are the stepping stones to the TN 12th Syllabus Preparation. With rigorous mock test practise, solving previous year-question papers and sample papers helps students in identifying their areas of weakness and strength. But to go one step further, focus on the high-scoring areas of each subject under the State Board of Tamil Nadu syllabus 12th standard as per the following subject-wise tips:

- Firstly, students must be allocated more time to high-weightage subjects and topics.
- For Science and Maths, candidates must prioritise numericals, understand concepts, keep flashcards of Formulas and memorise them by heart.
- For Chemistry, Students need to put more focus on balancing equations and learn the elements’ names thoroughly.
- Under the State Board of Tamil Nadu syllabus 12th standard, the English subject requires focus on practising grammar daily and essay or letter writing. Students must give equal importance to the prose and the poem.
- Students who have Commerce need to allocate more time to the journal entries and macro-models.
- Lastly, for Geography or History, students must prepare short notes on historical timelines & Revolution, practice maps, and disasters.
