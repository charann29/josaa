## AP Inter Results 2026: Statistics

This section covers the major numbers from this year’s AP Inter Results 2026. The results are the strongest BIEAP has seen in over a decade across most categories.

### Pass Percentage

The 1st year pass percentage of 77% is a 12-year high. The 2nd year pass rate of 81% is the second-best performance in the same period. Compared to 2025, the 1st year has seen a strong jump from 70%, while the 2nd year stood at 83% last year.

| --- | --- | --- |
| **Category** | **AP Inter Results 2025 Pass (%)** | **AP Inter Results 2026 Pass (%)** |

Government Junior Colleges (GJCs) have shown clear improvement in AP Inter Results 2026. The GJC 1st year pass percentage reached 54%, the highest in 12 years. GJC 2nd year touched 68%, which is the second-highest in a decade.

## How to Check AP Inter Results 2026 Online?

Students can follow these simple steps to check AP Inter Results 2026 on the official website. Keep your hall ticket number ready before you begin.

1. Go to **resultsbie.ap.gov.in**
2. Click on the AP Inter Results 2026 link for 1st year or 2nd year
3. Enter your hall ticket number
4. Click Submit
5. Your AP Intermediate result will appear on the screen
6. Download and save your marks memo as a PDF

## What to Do After AP Inter Results 2026?

The next steps depend on whether you passed or did not pass. Here is a quick guide for both situations.

### If you passed:

- Download and save your online marks memo from resultsbie.ap.gov.in as a PDF
- Collect your original marksheet from your junior college within 10 to 15 days
- Start shortlisting colleges or preparing for entrance exams based on your stream

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Stream** | **Relevant Entrance Exams** |
| MPC | JEE, EAMCET |
| BiPC | NEET, EAMCET |
| CEC | CLAT, EAMCET |
| HEC | EAMCET, other state exams |

### If you did not pass:

- Apply for the AP Inter supply exam 2026 through your junior college before the deadline
- Apply for re-counting or re-verification within 10 days of the AP Inter Results 2026 date
- One failed paper does not close any career option. BIEAP gives every student multiple chances to qualify.
