**MHT CET Answer key 2025**: The Maharashtra **State Council Cell released** the MHT CET Provisional Answer Key on **22nd May** **2025**. Candidates can find the MHT CET provisional answer key through the candidate portal, by using login credentials such as Registered Email ID  and Date of birth.

The CET Cell first releases the Provisional answer key. Candidates unsatisfied with the provisional answer key can raise objections to an open challenge on the official website from **22nd May 2025 to 24th May 2025.** The CET cell reviews the candidates’ submitted challenges and releases the final answer key. Candidates have to pay INR 1000 as a fee to object to the provisional answer key.

The final answer key is **expected to be released in the last week of May 2025.**

## How to Check the MHT CET 2025 Answer Key

The MHT CET Provisional answer key is released on the official website. Candidates can check the MHT CET 2025 answer key by following the steps below:

- Visit the official website of MHT CET 2025: <https://cetcell.mahacet.org/>
- Log in to the candidates’ portal. The candidates can find the respective answer key on their candidate portal.
- Candidates must log in with unique credentials such as Registered Email ID and Date of birth.
- The response sheet with the answer key is displayed.

## How to Challenge MHT CET Answer Key

The exam authorities have released the provisional answer key. Candidates who are not satisfied with it can raise objections. To object, candidates need to visit the official website: <https://cetcell.mahacet.org/>. To object to the provisional key, candidates have to pay a fee of INR 1000.

Candidates who have submitted their challenges along with questions and answers will be reviewed by the Maharashtra State Common Entrance Cell. To raise an objection, candidates must follow the below steps:

- Go to the official website of MHT CET 2025: <https://cetcell.mahacet.org/>.
- Click on the ‘Objection Tracking portal’ on the homepage.
- Log in with unique credentials such as Registered Email ID and Date of Birth.
- You will be shown the question paper with the correct options.
- Now, select the question and answer you want to challenge.
- Pay the challenging fee of Rs . 1000 per objection and submit the form.

## How to Calculate the Score Using MHT CET 2025 Answer Key

Candidates can calculate the score using the MHT CET 2025 Answer key. Here are the steps to calculate the score using the MHT CET answer key:

**Step 1:** Check your answers in the response sheet that you have marked in the exam

**Step 2:** Compare your answers with the answer key released by CET Cell

**Step 3:** For every correct answer to Physics & Chemistry and Botany & Zoology, count 1 mark.

**Step 4:** For every correct answer in Mathematics, count 2 marks.

**Step 5:** Calculate all of them together and you will get your total score in the MHT CET exam.
