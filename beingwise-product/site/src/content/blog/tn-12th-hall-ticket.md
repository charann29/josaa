## How to Download TN 12th Hall Ticket 2026?

It is mandatory for students appearing for the Tamil Nadu 12th Exam to secure the TN 12th hall ticket. Students need to get the admit cards from their respective schools. Students can also download the admit card from the following section:

- Students can visit the [Tamil Nadu Board Exam official website](https://www.dge.tn.gov.in/).
- On the home page, click on the Tamil Nadu HSE +2 Hall Ticket Option or link.
- You are redirected to another window.
- Enter your application number and Date of birth.
- Additionally enter the Captcha Code.
- Once you click on the search option, the admit card appears on the screen.
- Download the hall ticket and save it for use during the result and exam day.

## TN 12th Hall Ticket Details

On the TN 12th Hall ticket, students find the following information. Cross-check the information before appearing for the exam. In case of any mistake or error, report the school authorities.

- The Full Board Name
- Full name of the student according to the registration Card.
- Full name of Student’s Father or Mother’s.
- Student’s Gender
- A Unique 10-digit number also known as the Roll number. It is unique for each and every student.
- Exam Centre code
- Exam name
- Full Address
- Exam Schedule with Dates & Timings
- Students passport size photo
- Signature of Candidates, Principal, and Invigilator
- Instructions on Exams
