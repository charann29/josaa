## NIAT Industry-ready Upskilling Program at Collaborating Universities Enrollment Process

The journey to join the NIAT Industry-ready Upskilling Program at Chaitanya University begins with submitting an online application. Interested candidates can fill out the application form by visiting the official website: <https://www.niatindia.com/>. After submitting the form, applicants are required to take the NxtWave Assessment Test. This test is conducted online, and those who qualify are invited for a one-on-one counselling session with a mentor.

The NIAT Assessment team conducts assessments and counselling sessions to understand your skills, interests, and aptitude through the NxtWave Assessment Test. Based on the performance in NAT and preferences expressed in the one-on-one counselling session, the students will be suggested a university offering NIAT Industry-ready Upskilling Program.

Students should meet the eligibility criteria and admission policies established by the universities. The admission is solely at the discretion of the university, following UGC and AICTE norms.

## Can Diploma Students Join Universities Collaborating with NIAT?

The collaborating Universities where the NIAT upskilling program is offered do not allow diploma holders to enter the second year laterally. It requires all diploma students to begin from the first year itself. This isn’t just a policy—it’s a strategic academic decision! The skill map for the first year is designed to build a strong foundation for the courses they will learn further. Skipping the first year would also make them miss out on the necessary groundwork, practical skills, and internship opportunities. Hence, NIAT doesn’t encourage a lateral entry scheme, and students who want to join Universities collaborating with NIAT after their diploma can safely consider it.

## NxtWave Assessment Test Pattern & Syllabus

NxtWave Assessment Test is a 90-minute online enrollment test into NIAT Upskilling Centre in Hyderabad. A candidate who has qualified for their class 12th or equivalent from a recognized board can appear for NAT. NAT tests include students’ cognitive skills, academic strength, and real-world problem-solving potential for today’s fast-evolving world. NAT comprises three sections, namely, Psychometric, critical thinking, and mathematics sections.

### NAT Syllabus

The NAT 2025 syllabus primarily emphasizes problem-solving abilities and logical reasoning. Key topics include number theory, time and work, ratios, coding-decoding, and blood relations.
