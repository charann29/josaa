As per the official sources, the TS EAMCET 2025 results are expected to be released on **May 11th 2025.** Telangana Chief Minister Mr. Revanth Reddy is scheduled to release the results on May 11th @ 11:00 AM at the press conference. The TS EAMCET 2025 exams are conducted from May 2nd to May 4th across three days. According to official sources, a total of 2,20,327 candidates registered for the engineering exam, and 2,07,190 candidates appeared for the exam. The exam for pharmacy students is held on April 29th and April 30th, with 86,762 students registering for the exam and 81,198 appearing. ## How to Check TS EAMCET 2025 Results?

As per the initial announcement, the announcement of the results is declared to be on May 15th 2025; however, due to the preponement of the results declaration date, the results are being released on May 11th 2025, at 11:00 AM. Check the steps mentioned below to download the TS EAMCET 2025 rank card:

**Step 1:** Visit the official website, i.e. [eapcet.tsche.ac.in](http://eapcet.tsche.ac.in)

**Step 2:** Click on the link showing ‘[TG EAPCET Results 2025](https://eapcet.tgche.ac.in/TGEAPCET/EAPCET_GetResult.aspx)’.

**Step 3:** Provide the required credentials i.e. registration number, hall ticket number and DOB.

**Step 4:** Click on the ‘Submit’ option to view your TS EAMCET Result.

**Step 5:** Download and take a printout for future reference.
