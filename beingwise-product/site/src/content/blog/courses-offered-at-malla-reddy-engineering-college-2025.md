### **2. What is the difference between Malla Reddy Engineering College and Malla Reddy University? Are they the Same?**

Malla Reddy Engineering College (MREC) and Malla Reddy University (MRU) are different institutions. MREC is an autonomous college affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad, while MRU is a private university with its own curriculum and degree-granting powers. Both are part of the Malla Reddy Group but operate separately.
