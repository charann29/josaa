## Joy University Fees Structure 2025

Joy University (Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu) offers a clear and structured fee framework for its 2025‑26 academic year. Undergraduate B.Tech programs cost between ₹1,40,000 and ₹1,80,000 per year (with the average being around ₹1,40,000) depending on the specialization, while an MBA costs ₹1,50,000 per semester. BA and B.Sc courses typically range from ₹50,000 to ₹1,50,000 annually based on the subject. Hostel accommodations include non‑AC and AC options—with attached bathrooms—priced at ₹90,000 and ₹1,20,000 per year respectively, plus a one‑time admission fee of ₹10,000 and refundable caution deposit of ₹15,000. Financial assistance and scholarships are available for meritorious, category‑based, and sports‑quota students. Tuition fee waivers up to 50% are offered under merit-based scholarships for top scorer.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ******What is the tuition fee for B.Tech at Joy University?******

The annual tuition fee for B.Tech programs at Joy University typically ranges between ₹1,00,000 and ₹1,40,000. Core engineering branches like Mechanical, Civil, and Aeronautical are generally priced at ₹1,00,000 per year, while technology-driven specialisations like Electronics and Communication Engineering or Robotics & Automation are ₹1,40,000 per year.

### 2. ********How much does the BBA or BCA programme cost per year?********

For BBA, the annual fee starts from ₹60,000 for the general stream. Industry-aligned BBA programmes like E-Commerce & Digital Marketing or Logistics and SCM can go up to ₹1,20,000. BCA FACE Prep or BCA with specialisations such as Artificial Intelligence and Cyber Security are priced at ₹90,000 per year.

### 3. ************************************Is there a difference in fees between general and specialisation courses?************************************

Yes, specialisation courses typically have a higher tuition fee due to their industry-focused training and career-oriented structure. These programmes often include added learning support like FACE Prep or technology certifications.

### 4. ************What are the hostel and mess charges at Joy University?************

Joy University’s hostel fees range from ₹90,000 to ₹1,40,000 annually, depending on the type of accommodation selected (AC/non-AC, triple or quadruple sharing). A one-time hostel admission fee of ₹10,000 is applicable, along with a refundable caution deposit of ₹15,000. Mess charges are usually included in the annual package.

### 5. ****Are there any one-time or refundable charges to consider?****

Yes, students opting for hostel facilities need to pay a one-time admission fee of ₹10,000 and a refundable caution deposit of ₹15,000. These charges are in addition to the yearly hostel and tuition fees.

### **6. **Does Joy University offer fee instalment options or financial assistance?****

Details on instalment plans or financial assistance are generally updated each academic year. It is best to connect directly with the admissions or finance office through the official website for the latest information.
