## MHT CET Counselling and Admission Process

The MHT CET 2026 Counselling procedure includes three rounds of counselling for admission into engineering and pharmacy programs. Candidates should register themselves online and fill out the choice form via the portal.

- **Registration & Fee Payment:** Candidates should do online registration and also pay the counselling fees. The fees amount to ₹1,000 for general category candidates and ₹800 for candidates belonging to the reserved category.
- **Choice Filling (CAP Rounds):** Candidates must fill and lock their preferred colleges and programs separately for all the CAP rounds.
- **Merit List:** Initially, a provisional merit list is prepared, and finally, the merit list is made public. The merit list is generally published in July after resolving the objections raised.
- **Seat Allotment Procedure:** Allotment of seats is based on a variety of aspects, such as CET Percentile, merit rank, and candidate preferences.

## MHT CET Second Attempt Preparation Tips

The MHT CET paper analysis clearly highlights that scoring well in the second attempt depends not only on your preparation but also on your time management skills. Take some crucial second Attempt Preparation Tips based on the MHT CET paper analysis given below.

### Understand the Latest Paper Pattern & Difficulty

The difficulty level of MHT CET papers based on previous years’ exams has remained moderate overall, with an interesting pattern of Chemistry being easy, Physics moderately difficult, and Mathematics lengthy. Thus, your study plan should concentrate on revising critical chapters while learning to solve questions fast and effectively in the limited time given during the test.

### Revise High-Scoring Topics Based on MHT CET Analysis

Certain chapters are frequently asked questions irrespective of shifts and thus should be revised extensively before the exam. It will be futile to relearn all chapters in depth, and thus you should emphasize high-weightage topics such as calculus in mathematics, modern physics in physics, and organic chemistry from NCERT.

### Improve Your Strategy in Mathematics

The subject that requires the most time in the MHT CET paper analysis is Mathematics, not because the paper is tough, but because of the extensive calculations. Practice such types of questions and learn to skip such questions at first, and attempt them later to increase your attempts.

### Develop an Effective Exam Day Strategy

Students who scored high in the MHT CET Paper analysis followed an effective strategy during their exams. Begin with the Chemistry paper, followed by the Physics and Mathematics paper, to ensure good time allocation. Avoid spending much time on lengthy questions to increase your attempts.
