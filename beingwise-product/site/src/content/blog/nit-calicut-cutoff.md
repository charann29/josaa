## What Factors Affect NIT Calicut JEE Main Cutoff?

The jee main NIT Calicut cutoff is different annually because of several academic and admission factors. Knowledge of these will help in more accurate estimation of the chances by the candidates.

**1. The level of the JEE Main is difficult:** In case the exam is more difficult in a specific year, the overall ranks are altered and this directly affects the NIT Calicut JEE cutoff. The easier papers tend to be more competitive and have a low closing rank.

**2. Number of Applicants:** When the number of students showing up in JEE Main increases, competition increases, and the cutoff of NIT Calicut may be pushed to competitive levels.

**3. Seat Availability:** The number of seats per branch is an important factor. Branches that are less populated such as CSE have a significantly tougher NIT Calicut CSE cutoff than others do.

**4. Popularity of Branches:** Branches like CSE and ECE are in high demand, and these branches are preferred highly, and this makes the NIT Calicut ECE cutoff and branches like this highly competitive compared to other less demanded branches.

**5. Category and Quota:** The overall NIT Calicut cutoffs include cutoffs depending on categories (General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS) and quotas such as Home State and Other State.

**6. Previous Year Trends:** Both the admission authorities and students take into consideration historical data, and thus, NIT Calicut previous year cutoff tends to affect the present tendencies and anticipations.

**7. JoSAA Counselling Rounds:** The cutoffs vary in different rounds of counselling and hence the NIT Calicut round wise cutoff is significant to know how the rank variation takes place up to the last round.
