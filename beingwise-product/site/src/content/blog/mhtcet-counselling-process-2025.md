### What is a CAP Round in MHT CET 2025 Counselling?

The CAP round in MHT CET refers to the Centralised Admission Process round. The MHT CET CAP is a multi-round online counselling process conducted by the Maharashtra CET Cell for seat allotment in engineering and professional courses. It includes registration, document verification, choice filling, and seat allotment based on merit. Candidates can freeze, float, or slide seats to optimise their admission.

- **Freeze:** Accept the allotted seat and confirm admission; you won’t participate in further rounds.
- **Float:** Keep the current seat but stay eligible for higher-preference seats in future rounds.
- **Slide:** Accept the seat but seek a higher-preference course within the same college in the next round.

## Documents Required for MHT CET CAP Round

Document verification is an important procedure before allowing candidates to proceed further with the MHT CET counselling processes. Candidates require a set of their previous academic, and some personal documents to verify their identity. Candidates have to ensure that the documents are clear and recent to avoid discrepancies during the document verification process. Here are the documents required for MHT CET CAP Round 2025:

- **Academic Certificates:** Class X & Class XII th Marksheet, Transfer certificate.
- **Exam-related certificates:** MHT CET Score card & Admit card.
- **Personal Documents:** Aadhar card, Passport-sized photograph, Income certificate.
- **Other Documents:** Domicile certificate, Caste certificate, PwD certificate(If applicable).

### MHT CET CAP Round 1

MHT CET CAP round 1 is the first formal seat allotment process in the counselling process. The MHT CET round 1 seat allotment is often considered more competitive as it involves high cutoff trends and early allotments to the top-tier colleges in Maharashtra. Choice filling for the MHT CET CAP round 1 opens after the document verification is completed. Students are allowed to choose and fill out the colleges they prefer to apply to. Seats are allotted based on merit, category and availability. Once allotted, students have to pay the seat acceptance fee, i.e. ₹1000(Non-refundable) to confirm the allotted seat.

### MHT CET CAP Round 1 Cutoff:

Cutoffs will be released post-allotment. These will be available category-wise, college-wise, and course-wise and serve as a benchmark for future rounds.

**Note: Candidates have the option to freeze, float, or slide once they are allotted the seat. Candidates can participate in the subsequent rounds using the float or slide options.**

### MHT CET CAP Round 2

MHT CET CAP round 2 is the second round of the counselling process intended for students who are not allotted admissions in the MHT CET CAP round 1. Candidates who did not confirm their seats in round 1, or those students who selected the float option, or those students who failed to appear for round 1, are eligible to appear for the MHT CET CAP round 2.

In round 2, Candidates are eligible for the fresh choice filling, rearranging preferences chosen in round 1. The CAP results are expected to be declared in early August. Cutoffs for the MHT CET round 2 are slightly lower compared to the CAP round 1. Students have similar options to confirm or participate in the subsequent rounds.

### MHT CET CAP Round 3

CAP Round 3 is typically the last centralised round before spot admissions. It caters to vacant seats from earlier rounds and welcomes fresh applicants as well as those seeking seat upgradation. This round is open to all registered candidates, including those who did not participate in earlier rounds. Seat allotment is done based on updated preferences and the availability of seats at that stage.

Since most competitive seats are filled by this point, the cutoffs in Round 3 are generally the lowest, making it particularly beneficial for students targeting newer colleges or less competitive branches. Choice filling for this round usually begins in early August, with seat allotment scheduled for mid-August. Candidates are required to report and confirm their admission within 2–3 days of the allotment result. It’s important for students to include realistic and backup options in their preference list to ensure a better chance of securing a seat.

### MHT CET 2025 Spot Round

Spot round is a last resort and is not a guaranteed round that is conducted every year. The Spot admission round is an institute-level round, unlike a centralised process. Only unaided private colleges participate in the spot round, and the seat accepted is final. Students are subjected to document verification at the college and are asked to pay the fees once the seat is allotted.

**Note: Candidates are advised not to rely on this round, since this is not a guaranteed round, unlike the previous CAP rounds.**

## What After MHT CET 2025 Seat Allotment?

Candidates have to ensure that they follow several steps after the MHT CET seat allotment, as failure to follow the steps mentioned below might result in cancellation of the seat.

1. **Step 1:** Log in to their CAP portal account
2. **Step 2:** Accept the seat using “freeze,” “float,” or “slide” options
3. **Step 3:** Pay the seat acceptance fee (₹1000 approx.) online
4. **Step 4:** Report to the allotted college with originals and printouts.
5. **Step 5:** Complete the college admission formalities (fee payment, hostel booking, etc.).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****When will the MHT CET CAP Round start?****

CAP registration and document verification are expected to begin in the first or second week of July 2025.

### 2. ****How many CAP rounds are there in MHT CET?****

There are three main CAP rounds, followed by a possible spot round if seats remain vacant after Round 3.

### 3. ****What is the procedure of CAP rounds in MHT CET?****

It involves registration, document verification, choice filling, seat allotment, result declaration, and college reporting

### 4. ****What is non-CAP round in MHT CET?****

Non-CAP or spot round allows direct college-level admissions for vacant seats, usually in unaided private colleges. However, this non-CAP round is not a guaranteed round unlike the usual CAP rounds.

### 5. ****Can I participate in CAP Round 3 if I skipped Round 1 and 2?****

Yes, CAP Round 3 allows fresh participation for registered candidates who skipped earlier rounds.

### 6. ****Is it possible to get admission without MHT CET score?****

Yes, some seats are allotted based on JEE Main score or management quota in private colleges.
