## Steps to download the CUET UG City Intimation Slip

The **CUET UG City Intimation Slip** is released by the **National Testing Agency (NTA)** to inform candidates in advance about their allotted **exam city, date, and shift**. This slip helps students plan their travel and stay arrangements accordingly. It is important to note that this is **not the admit card**, but a preliminary document released before the actual hall ticket. Here are the steps to download the CUET UG Entrance  exam

## What is CUET UG Entrance Exam?

The **CUET UG (Common University Entrance Test – Undergraduate)** is a national-level entrance examination conducted by the **National Testing Agency (NTA)** for admission to undergraduate programs in various **central, state, and private universities** across India.

It was introduced to provide a **unified platform** for students seeking admission to multiple universities, reducing the burden of appearing in multiple entrance exams. The exam assesses candidates on their language knowledge**, domain-specific subjects**, and **general aptitude**, allowing universities to evaluate applicants through a common standard. CUET UG is typically taken by students who have completed or are in the final year of their **Class 12 education**.

## CUET UG Eligibility Criteria

There is no age limit for appearing in the CUET (UG)—2025 examination. Candidates who have passed the class 12 /equivalent examination or are appearing in 2025 can appear in the CUET (UG)—2025 examination. However, the candidates will be required to fulfil the age criteria (if any) of the University / Institution / Organisation in which they are desirous of admission.

## CUET UG Admit Card

The CUET UG 2025 admit card is an essential document for all candidates appearing for the exam. Issued by the National Testing Agency (NTA), it is expected to be available approximately one week prior to the examination period, which is scheduled from **May 8 to June 1, 2025**. Candidates can access and download their admit cards by visiting the official website:<https://cuet.nta.nic.in>.

To retrieve the admit card, applicants must log in using their application number and date of birth. The document will include vital information such as the candidate’s name, roll number, exam date, reporting time, and examination center details. It is compulsory to carry a printed copy of the admit card along with a valid photo ID on the day of the exam.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****What is the CUET UG 2025 City Intimation Slip?****

The City Intimation Slip informs candidates about the city where their examination center will be located. It helps candidates plan their travel and accommodation in advance. Please note that this is not the admit card.

### **2. ********When will the City Intimation Slip be released?**********

The exact release date for the CUET UG 2025 City Intimation Slip has not been announced yet. Candidates are advised to check the official CUET website:<https://cuet.nta.nic.in> for updates regularly.

### **3. ********How can I download the City Intimation Slip?**********

Once released, candidates can download the City Intimation Slip by visiting the official CUET website and logging in with their application number and date of birth.

### **4. ********Is the City Intimation Slip the same as the Admit Card?**********

No, the City Intimation Slip only provides information about the exam city. The Admit Card, which includes details like exam centre address, date, and time, will be released separately.

### **5. ********What should I do if there is an error in my City Intimation Slip?**********

Suppose you find any discrepancies in your City Intimation Slip. In that case, you should immediately contact the CUET helpdesk or the National Testing Agency (NTA) through the contact information provided on the official website.

### 6. **Can I change my exam city after the City Intimation Slip is released?**

Generally, requests for changing the exam city are not entertained after the City Intimation Slip is released. Candidates should ensure they select their preferred cities carefully during the application process.
