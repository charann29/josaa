## What is NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT) And Syllabus?

NAT is a 90-minute onlineAssessment Test for enrollment to the Upskilling program at NIAT. Further admission to the BTech course to NRI University is based on the rules and regulations of the University. The test focuses on the candidate’s cognitive abilities and potential as a future tech innovator, with 3 sections that test different skills of a student. Candidates need to have a 10+2 level to be eligible for NAT. There are a total of 81 questions divided into Psychometric Tests, Critical Thinking, and Mathematics.
