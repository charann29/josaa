The National Testing Agency has scheduled the exam from **May 13th to June 3rd.** Candidates must carry the admit card to the examination centre to be eligible to enter. ## Steps to Download CUET Admit Card 2025

Follow the steps mentioned below to download the CUET admit card 2025:

**Step 1:** Navigate to the official website: [cuet.nta.nic.in](http://cuet.nta.nic.in).

**Step 2:** Click the ‘Download CUET-UG Admit Card’ link.

**Step 3:** Provide the required credentials to log in to the website(Application ID and Password)

**Step 4:** Click the ‘login’ option and view your CUET Admit card PDF.

**Step 5:** Take a printout of the admit card for future reference.

## Difference Between CUET UG Admit Card and City Intimation Slip

Students often confuse the city intimation slip with the admit card. Here is the table showing how they differ:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **City Intimation Slip** | **Admit Card** |
| Contains the exam centre, date, slot, shift timings, and key instructions. | An important document is required to appear for the exam. |
| Released before the release of the admit card | Released 3-4 days before the exam. |
| Shows subject-wise exam dates, timings, duration, and exam city. | The exam centre, date, slot, shift timings, and key instructions are contained. |
| Not required to appear for the exam. | Compulsory document for the examination. |

## CUET UG 2025 Examday Guidelines

Candidates appearing for the CUET UG 2025 exam have to adhere to the exam day guidelines for a smooth examination process. Here are the exam day guidelines released by the National Testing Agency:

- Arrive at the examination centre at least two hours before the examination time.
- Cooperate with the security checks and frisking process.
- Don’t swap your seats; sit only in the allotted places.
- Double-check your question paper and confirm it with the opted subject.
- Carry all the important documents to the examination centre.
- Don’t carry electronic gadgets to the examination hall.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **When will the CUET 2025 admit card be released?**

The exam admit card from May 13 to May 24 has been released.

### **2. ************Where can I download the CUET 2025 admit card?**************

You can download it from the official website: cuet.nta.nic.in using your Application ID and Password.

### **3. **************Is the city intimation slip the same as the admit card?****************

No. The city intimation slip informs about the exam city, while the admit card is mandatory for entry into the exam centre.

### **4. **************What documents should I carry on CUET exam day?****************

Carry your CUET admit card, self-declaration form, passport-size photo, and valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar, PAN, or School ID.

### 5. ****What are the shift timings for CUET UG 2025?****

CUET UG 2025 will be conducted in three shifts daily:
Slot 1: 9:00 AM – 10:00 AM
Slot 2: 12:00 PM – 1:00 PM
Slot 3: 3:00 PM – 4:00 PM

### 6. ****What to wear for the CUET Examination?****

There is no official dress code for CUET UG 2025. However, candidates are advised to wear simple clothes to feel comfortable during the exam and avoid hectic security checks

### 7. ****What should I do if I face issues downloading the CUET admit card?****

If you have trouble downloading the admit card, contact the NTA helpline immediately or email cuet@nta.ac.in for assistance. You can also clear your cache or try a different browser.
