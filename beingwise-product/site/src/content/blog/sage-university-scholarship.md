## Sage University Scholarship Application Process

Students applying for [SAGE College Scholarships](https://sageuniversity.in/Scholarship/) must appear for the Sage Entrance Exam (SEE) to apply for any scholarship at SAGE University. The step-wise process to apply for the SAGE University Scholarship is given below:

- Visit the SAGE University Official Website
- Fill the admission form of SAGE University by entering all the required details.
- Appear for the Sage University Entrance Exam
- Students who have met the eligibility criteria based on their past academic records do not need the SEE Score to get a scholarship.
- After clearing SEE, students are invited for a counselling session, where they will learn more about the scholarships.
- Once selected for admission, students are automatically awarded a scholarship as per their score.

## SAGE University Entrance Exam – SEE

SAGE Entrance Exam or SEE is the SAGE University Entrance Exam conducted for admission to every program. This mandatory admission test examines the quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, verbal ability, and subject-specific knowledge of all applicants. It is generally conducted in December, and the results are announced within 1-2 weeks.

#### Key Points of the SAGE University Indore Scholarship Application Process

Students must remember the following points during the SAGE University Indore Scholarship Application Process:

- Students who apply for a scholarship based on their Qualifying class results will need to fill out a separate Sage University Scholarship Form.
- Scholarship is applicable for the entire course but is subject to renewal.
- Students need to pay Examination fees, enrollment fees, bus fees, and hostel fees regularly every year.
- Scholarship is only on few seats and for a limited time.

## SAGE University Scholarship Renewal

The following renewal criteria must be kept in mind while applying for the SAGE University Scholarship:

- Students need to consistently keep a high academic performance to meet the pre-defined academic standards for scholarship continuation.
- Scholarship renewals are also based on performance in university examinations, regular attendance, and conduct.
- Sports Scholarship renewals are based on performance in sports in university tournaments.

## SAGE University Admission Criteria For B.Tech Admission

Candidates applying for the SAGE University Admission for engineering courses should fulfil the following criteria:

- The student must have passed the 10+2 level examination or any equivalent exam with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
- A minimum of 50% marks in aggregate must be scored in the 12th Boards.
- Performance in national or state-level entrance exams like JEE Main, MHT CET, and WBJEE is considered for SAGE University admission.
- Students without a valid score in exams like JEE Main need to appear for the SAGE University Entrance exam, called the SAGE Entrance Exam (SEE).
