The WBJEE 2025 exam will be conducted on April 27th, 2025, and candidates preparing for the exam have to know important details like dates, exam guidelines, and instructions to fill out the WBJEE OMR sheet. Knowing these details will help students avoid rushing and solve discrepancies occurring due to these issues. This article will help you with instructions to fill out the OMR sheet and to know how to raise objections against it.

## WBJEE 2025 OMR Sheet

OMR Sheet is a specifically designed sheet to record candidates” responses. Bubbles next to each other are placed based on the question numbers. Candidates have to darken the circle corresponding to the question number. Following the instructions carefully and marking the answers are important as the OMR sheet uses optical recognition technology, and failing to mark the response according to the instructions might lead you to lose marks.

## How to Download WBJEE OMR Sheet 2025

To download the OMR sheet in PDF format, candidates can follow the process mentioned below:

- Access the official website wbjeeb.nic.in
- Select the “OMR Response Sheet” option.
- Login by providing the required credentials like application number and password/DOB.
- OMR sheet will be displayed on the screen.
- Review your responses and raise objections in case of any discrepancies.
- Download and take a hard copy of the OMR sheet for future reference.

## What are all the details mentioned in the OMR Sheet

The OMR sheet in WBJEE consists of a few instructions followed by a name, signature, and exam centre name. Candidates should carefully fill in the particulars with a ballpoint pen. Four circles corresponding to the question number are mentioned. Candidates need to darken the circle according to their preferred option. Candidates need to be careful while marking the correct responses. They are free to leave the question unanswered, but marking an incorrect answer will result in losing marks.

## How to Raise Objections Against OMR Sheet in WBJEE 2025

Candidates who have concerns regarding the machine-read responses of the OMR sheet are allowed to challenge against it. Officials will announce a specified timeframe, and candidates need to pay a non-refundable processing fee of ₹500 for challenging against the OMR sheet.

The official responses will be released by the authorities after reviewing all the challenges received against the OMR sheet. Scores and ranks are calculated based on the final responses, and if candidates are not satisfied with the changes, they can contact the helpdesk within the designated time.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

To ensure that your OMR sheet is filled correctly, avoid these common mistakes:

- **Marking with pencil:** Always use a black or blue ballpoint pen.

- **Not erasing marks properly:** If you make a mistake, erase it thoroughly before filling in the correct answer.

- **Filling multiple bubbles:** Never mark more than one option for any question.

- **Not marking the question set code:** Ensure you mark the correct code for your question paper.

- **Not reviewing the sheet:** Always double-check your OMR sheet before submitting it.
