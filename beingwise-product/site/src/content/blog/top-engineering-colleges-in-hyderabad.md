Engineering is a top career choice for students post-Class 12, with India producing over 15 lakh engineering graduates annually. Hyderabad, home to 68 engineering colleges and over 1.2 lakh seats, is a key destination for aspiring engineers. The city offers a range of B.Tech programs, including Computer Science, Civil, Mechanical, and AI, with admissions based on exams like JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and TS EAMCET.

Top institutions in Hyderabad include IIT Hyderabad, IIIT Hyderabad, University of Hyderabad, and JNTU, providing high-quality education. Fee structures range from Rs 50,000 to Rs 5,00,000 lakh annually, making it an accessible as prestigious location for engineering studies.

### 1. [JEE Main](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/)

JEE Main, conducted by NTA, is a national-level entrance exam for admission to undergraduate engineering programs at NITs, IIITs, and private institutions. It assesses candidates in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. JEE Main is held twice a year, offering multiple attempts for better scores.

### 2. [JEE Advanced](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-advanced/)

JEE Advanced is the second stage of the Joint Entrance Examination, taken by top JEE Main performers to secure admission to prestigious IITs. It is highly competitive and evaluates problem-solving abilities in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Only top 2.5 lakh JEE Main candidates can appear for this exam.

### 3. [TS EAMCET](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/)

TS EAMCET (Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test) is a state-level exam conducted by JNTU Hyderabad for admission to undergraduate engineering courses in Telangana. It tests knowledge in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics and is crucial for securing seats in both private and government engineering colleges.

### 4. UGEE

IIIT Hyderabad conducts UGEE (Undergraduate Entrance Exam) for admission to its BTech programs. The exam evaluates candidates in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English. The selection process also includes past academic performance, and UGEE is held annually for those seeking admission to IIIT Hyderabad.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **********What is the admission process for IIT Hyderabad?**********

IIT Hyderabad admits students based on their performance in JEE Main and JEE Advanced. Candidates need a minimum of 99+ percentile in JEE Main to be considered for admission. The institute offers undergraduate programs like BTech and various postgraduate courses like MTech and PhD.

### 2. ****What is the selection process for IIIT Hyderabad?****

IIIT Hyderabad accepts JEE Main scores and also conducts its own entrance exam called UGEE (Undergraduate Entrance Exam). It assesses candidates in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English. The selection is based on performance in UGEE, past academic records, and sometimes interviews.

### **3.Does IIIT Hyderabad offer any specialized programs?**

Yes, IIIT Hyderabad is particularly renowned for its specialized programs in fields like Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science. The institution offers BTech, MTech, and PhD programs.

JEE Main

IIIT Hyderabad UGEE (for IIIT Hyderabad’s BTech programs)
