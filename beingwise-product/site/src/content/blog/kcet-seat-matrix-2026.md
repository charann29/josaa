## What is KCET Seat Matrix?

Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) seat matrix is one of the most crucial documents published by the Karnataka Examinations Authority for admission to engineering colleges. The KCET Seat Matrix 2026 is helpful for knowing the total number of B.Tech seats available in government, aided, and private engineering colleges in Karnataka.

The KCET Seat Index data gives complete information about the branch-wise seats, category-wise reservations, Hyderabad Karnataka quota seats, rural quota seats, and special category reservations.  You can also use the KCET College Predictor to know your admission chances based on your category and seat reservation criteria.

## KCET Seat Matrix 2026 PDF – How to Download?

KCET Seat Matrix 2026 will be issued in PDF format by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) during KCET counselling. Here is the complete step-by-step process to download the document from the official website

1. Visit the official KEA website at <http://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in>
2. Go to the KCET counseling page on the homepage.
3. Click on the “UGCET 2026 Seat Matrix” tab.
4. Choose the engineering/course-wise seat matrix PDF.
5. The PDF of the KCET seat Intake will appear on the screen.
6. Download and keep the KCET 2026 Seat Matrix PDF for counseling purposes.
7. Now, the seat availability according to college-wise, branch-wise, and category wise mentioned in the document.

## KCET Reservation Criteria 2026

Understanding the reservation system is just as important as understanding the KCET Seat Intake. The KCET Reservation Criteria 2026 explain the category-based seat allocation for engineering and professional courses in Karnataka.

KCET 2026 Reservation criteria contains vertical reservations for SC, ST, OBC, and EWS, as well as horizontal quotas for women, PwD, rural, Kannada medium, and special categories, to assist candidates in understanding their eligibility and chances of admission.

### Quotes applicable in KCET Seat Distribution

| Quota | Details |
| --- | --- |
| General Merit (GM) | Open seats allotted based on KCET rank |
| Hyderabad-Karnataka (371J) Quota | Reservation for Kalyana Karnataka region candidates |
| Rural Quota | Reserved for students who studied in rural areas |
| Kannada Medium Quota | Reservation for Kannada medium students |
| SC/ST Quota | Reserved seats for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes |
| OBC Quota | Reservation for Categories 1, 2A, 2B, 3A, and 3B |
| EWS Quota | Reservation for Economically Weaker Section candidates |
| SNQ Quota | Supernumerary Quota for economically weaker students |
| Defence Quota | Reservation for children of defence personnel |
| NCC/Sports Quota | Reserved seats for NCC and sports achievers |
| Persons with Disabilities (PwD) Quota | Reservation for differently-abled candidates |
| Minority Quota | Seats reserved in minority institutions |
| Government Quota | Seats allotted through KCET counselling at regulated fees |

## How KCET Seat Matrix help in counselling process?

The KCET seat matrix plays an important role in helping candidates analyze their KCET counselling in a better way. Here are a few pointers that explain how the KCET Seat Intake is essential to understand.

- **Understand Seat Availability**

KCET seat matrix shows the distribution of seats branch-wise and college-wise for courses like Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil Engineering, and many other streams. Candidates can know about colleges having maximum seats and good admission chances.

- **Choose Options in a Smart Way**

By analyzing the KCET seat matrix, candidates can prioritize colleges and branches for admission depending upon the seat availability, reservation advantages, and previous year cut-offs. Candidates can smartly enter their options by making ambitious, moderate, and safe options.

- **Understand Category-wise Reservation**

KCET seat matrix also gives details about reserved seats in categories like SC, ST, 2A, 2B, 3A, 3B, Rural, Kannada Medium, and Hyderabad-Karnataka quota. Candidates belonging to reserved categories can analyze their chances of admission from the KCET seat matrix.

- **Estimating the Chance of Admission**

Students can analyze the KCET rank against the number of seats and cut-off marks in previous years to know the chances of getting admission in their favorite colleges and branches.

- **Formulating a Round-wise Counselling Strategy**

KCET seat matrix keeps changing through mock allocation, Round 1, Round 2, and extended rounds based on the vacancy of seats. Students can monitor seat availability and adjust their choices accordingly in the subsequent rounds.

- **Helps in Comparing Colleges and Branches**

Total SEAT intake capacity, Popular engineering branches, Government quota seats, College-wise competition level Comparison will help students select the best option available in accordance with their ranks and choice.

- **Prevents Counselling Errors**

There are numerous cases where students fail to grab admission opportunities owing to poor planning of their choices. By analyzing the KCET seat matrix properly, candidates can prevent any such mistake.

- **Helpful in Selecting Back-up Options**

Students can look out for other alternatives with high seat availability to ensure that they do not miss out on the chance of admission if they cannot get their preferred choice.
