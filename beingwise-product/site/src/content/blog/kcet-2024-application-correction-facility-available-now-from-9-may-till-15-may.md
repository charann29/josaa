The KCET 2024 Application correction facility is available on the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) website. The edit application link is published on 9th May 2024 and will be available up to 15th May. The candidates can start making the corrections in their application form. They can use the below link to correct the mistakes made at the time of filling out the KCET 2024 Application form.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **KCET 2024 Edit Application Link** | [**CLICK HERE**](https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/EDITUGCET2024/FORMS/LOGINPAGE.ASPX) |

The Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) 2024 is a state-level entrance examination, conducted by the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA). The test was conducted on the 18th and 19th of April 2024. The provisional answer key is released on 29.04.2024 through the official website. The KCET 2024 Exam Results will be released on **May 20, 2024,** through the Official Website.

## KCET Edit Application Link

The candidates can correct the mistakes made at the time of filling out the KCET 2024 Application form using the above link. The candidates shall visit the application login portal and enter their Login ID (or) Registration Number and Password to start the process.
