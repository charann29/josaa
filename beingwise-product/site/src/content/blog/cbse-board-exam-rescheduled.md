Given below is the outline of the next steps that students need, from revised preparation timelines and syllabus revisions to mock test strategies and mental health tips.

- Check the Updated Date Sheet: Students should download and examine the revised date sheet for the CBSE 2026 to check the updated dates for all of their exams.
- Adjust Study Schedule: Now, candidates will have to adjust their study plans to fit the new exam dates, and do the same for subjects whose exams have been rescheduled.
