## How to download the JEE Main Admit Card 2026?

To download your NTA JEE Main 2026 Admit Card, follow these steps on the National Testing Agency’s Official Website:

- **Visit the Official Website:** Go to the National Testing Agency’s Official Website at “jeemain.nta.nic.in
- **Locate the Download Link:** On the home page of the National Testing Agency’s Official Website, under the “Latest Announcement” section, click on the “Download Admit Card for JEE (Main) – 2026” link.

- **Select JEE Main Login Option:** On the National Testing Agency’s Official Website, there are generally two options to log in to your account to download your JEE Main Admit Card. These are as follows: Through Application Number and Password, or Through Application Number and Date of Birth.
- **Enter Login Details.** Fill in your Application Number, Password, and Security Pin (CAPTCHA) provided on the screen.

- **Submission:** After filling in your Application Number, Password, and Date of Birth, click on the “Submit” or “Sign In” button. Your Admit Card will appear on the screen.
- **Download the** **JEE Mains Hall Ticket 2026:** Then, click on “Download Admit Card.” Take 2 to 3 prints of your Admit Card. The Admit Card can be either in color or black and white.

## Key Points for Admit Card for JEE Main 2026 Session 2

Here are some key instructions for students to follow when accessing the JEE Main 2026 session 2 admit card.

### What if you forgot the JEE Main Admit Card Login Details?

Forgetting your JEE Main application number is a frustrating situation, especially when you need to use it to download your JEE Main 2026 admit card. However, you need not worry because this situation has been taken care of by NTA. You can easily recover your application number by following a few easy steps:

1. You must first visit the official website of JEE Main.
2. On this website, you will find an option to “Forgot Application Number.”
3. After clicking on this button, you will be required to fill in some basic details such as your name, date of birth, registered email ID, and mobile number.
4. After filling in this information, you will be able to recover your application number. It is important that you make sure that you fill in this information correctly because it has to be similar to the details that you filled in during registration.
5. After recovering your application number, you will be able to download your admit card without any issues.
6. In order to make this situation easier for you, you are advised to save your application number either electronically or manually.
