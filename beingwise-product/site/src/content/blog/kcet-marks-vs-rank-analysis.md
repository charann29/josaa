## KCET Marks Vs Rank Analysis 2025

The factors that determine the KCET 2025 rank are

- The candidate’s subject-wise KCET marks
- The candidate’s category
- The normalization of marks, etc.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **1. What is a decent rank in KCET 2025?**

The rank between 1 to 10,000 is considered a good one. To achieve this rank, the candidate must score more than 75 marks.

### **2. How the KCET rank is calculated?**

The calculation of KCET rank includes the weightage of 50% KCET score and 50% 12th board marks scored by the candidate.

### **3. What information does the candidate require to use the KCET Rank Predictor tool?**

The candidate requires the KCET 2025 score and the marks achieved by the candidate in the 12th board exams for each subject.
