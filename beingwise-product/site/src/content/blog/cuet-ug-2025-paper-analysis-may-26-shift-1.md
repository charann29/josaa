## Exam Pattern: CUET UG 2025

Candidates will have to prove their abilities in three specific domains, namely language, subject knowledge, and aptitude. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ********What is CUET UG 2025, and who conducts it?********

CUET UG 2025 is the Common University Entrance Test for Undergraduate courses, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admissions into central and participating universities.

### 2.**************************How can I download the CUET UG admit card?**************************

You can download the CUET UG admit card from the official NTA CUET website using your application number and password. A printout of the admit card is mandatory on exam day.

### **3. ******************************What is the CUET UG exam pattern for 2025?********************************

The CUET UG exam pattern 2025 consists of three sections: Languages, Domain Subjects, and a General Test. The exam is computer-based and includes multiple-choice questions.

### **4. **********************How many times is the CUET exam 2025 being conducted?************************

The CUET exam 2025 is being conducted in 23 shifts across multiple days to accommodate different subjects and student preferences.

### 5. ************Why is CUET 2025 important for undergraduate admissions?************

CUET 2025 is crucial because it standardises the UG admission process across universities. A strong CUET exam score can significantly improve your chances of admission, as many institutions prioritise it over board marks.
