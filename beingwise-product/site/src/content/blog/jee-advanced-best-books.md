Preparing to get admission to IITs is a journey to cherish. Aspirants preparing for JEE Advanced often require help choosing a standard set of books for physics, maths, and chemistry. Eligible candidates who have cleared JEE Mains will try to prepare for JEE Advanced with the same sources that helped them clear the JEE mains. Although some books do help in their JEE Advanced preparation, there are some books you should include to play your JEE Advanced game right.

The book list plays an important role in your exam preparation as it helps you understand the concepts clearly and practice problems related to those concepts. Books with pictorial representations and flow charts will help you revise the concepts efficiently and save you a lot of valuable time. In this article, we will discuss the best books for maths, physics, and chemistry.

## Best Books for JEE Advanced 2025 Physics

Physics carries a total number of 17 questions in each paper accounting for 61 marks. While considering the moderate difficulty level of this subject, it is important to prioritize this subject and score high in the exam.

Here is the list of best books for physics for JEE Advanced 2025.

## Difference Between JEE Main & JEE Advanced

While JEE Mains and JEE Advanced are the two stages of the exam, there is a difference between them. JEE Mains 2025 involves questions that are of NCERT level while JEE Advanced 2025 involves a higher level of questions that require a deep understanding of concepts. JEE Advanced demands more efforts from students compared to JEE Mains and hence one need a standard set of trusted sources to enhance their preparation.
