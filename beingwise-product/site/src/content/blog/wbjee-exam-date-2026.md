WBJEE exam date 2026 has been officially announced by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board. According to the official notification, **WBJEE 2026 will be conducted on May 24, 2026**.

The registration process for WBJEE 2026 began on **March 10, 2026**, and the application window will remain open until **April 5, 2026**. Candidates who wish to appear for the exam must complete the application process through the official website. WBJEE is a **state-level entrance examination conducted once every year** for admission to undergraduate engineering, technology, and pharmacy programs offered by universities and colleges in West Bengal.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **When will WBJEE 2026 be conducted?**

The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination 2026 will be conducted on May 24, 2026. The exam will be held in two papers on the same day for Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.

### 2. ****Has WBJEE 2026 registration started?****

Yes, the registration for WBJEE 2026 started on March 10, 2026 and the application window will remain open until April 5, 2026, as announced by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board.

### **3. ****When will the WBJEE 2026 admit card be released?******

The WBJEE 2026 admit card is scheduled to be released on May 15, 2026 on the official website. Candidates must download it before appearing for the exam.

### 4.**Is WBJEE conducted twice a year?**

No, WBJEE is conducted once a year by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board for admission to engineering and pharmacy courses in participating institutes across West Bengal.

### ********5. ******What is considered a good score in WBJEE?**************

A score of 140 or above is generally considered a good score in WBJEE, as it can help candidates secure admission to top engineering colleges in the state.
