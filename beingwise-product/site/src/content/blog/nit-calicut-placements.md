## NIT Calicut Placement Process 2026

The NIT Calicut placements are based on a specific and well-organised placement process, which is mandatory to be followed by everyone. Check the complete NIT Calicut placements process below:

**1. Campus (TPO Outreach) Invitation:**

The process begins with an invitation email from the Training and Placement Officer (TPO) to the recruiters. The placement brochure and a Job Description (JD) form is attached to the email. This welcomes businesses to take part in campus placements.

**2. Job Description (JD) Submission:**

Companies fill out the JD form and send it back to the TPO. They include information such as the programs that are available, CGPA qualifications, position and remuneration. This is a clear definition of the job.

**3. Job Creation & Publishing (JNF/INF):**

The TPO develops the job on the recruitment platform based on the JNF/INF. Eligible students are then invited to work in the job. Students are able to see and practice within the deadline.

**4. Sharing Applicant Details:**

The TPO forwards the list of applicants and their resumes to the company after the deadline. This provides the recruiters with a platform of candidates.

**5. Shortlisting of Candidates:**

Recruiters go through resumes and shortlist. They can also administer an online or offline test. This assists in screening the candidates.

**6. Interview Process and Final Selection:**

Interviews are conducted via online or campus interviews of shortlisted candidates. A SPOC assists in handling the process. The final selection list and waitlist are then shared by recruiters.
