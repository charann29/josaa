The Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE) has released the Normalized Scores secured by the candidates in the Engineering/Pharmacy Computer Based (CBT) Entrance Examination, Kerala-2025. The Normalized Score Cards are published on the Official Website, [www.cee.kerala.gov.in](http://www.cee.kerala.gov.in). The candidates can directly download their Normalized Score Card through the below link:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **KEAM 2025 Normalized Score Card** | [**CLICK HERE TO DOWNLOAD**](https://www.cee.kerala.gov.in/keam2024/) |

The Kerala Engineering, Architecture, and Medical (KEAM) is a state-level entrance exam conducted by the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE) every year. This test acts as a gateway for students seeking admission in Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical courses across the state. The candidates must have passed the Higher Secondary Examination, Kerala with a minimum of 45% aggregate marks to be eligible for the KEAM 2025 exam. The KEAM examination dates are revised by the CEE. The Engineering examination will be held from **22.04.2025 to 27.04.2025** from **10:00 AM to 1:00 PM**.

## Steps to Check KEAM 2025 Normalized Score Card

By following the steps, the candidates can check their KEAM 2025 Normalized Score Card:

**Step 1:** Log in to your KEAM home page through the link **‘KEAM-2025 Candidate Portal’,** [https://www.cee.kerala.gov.in/keam2025/](https://www.cee.kerala.gov.in/keam2024/)

**Step 2:** Click the menu **‘Result’** to view the Entrance Examination Normalized score.

**Step 3:** Download and take a printout of the Results for the future reference.

## Details mentioned on the KEAM 2025 Rank Card

The KEAM 2025 Rank Card contains the following details:

- Candidate’s Name
- Candidate’s Application Number
- Candidate’s Roll Number and Date of Birth
- Gender and Category of the Candidate
- Total Marks secured in the KEAM 2025 exam
- Qualifying Marks of KEAM 2025

## KEAM 2025 Rank Lists

The Commissioner of Entrance Examinations (CEE), Kerala will release separate Rank Lists for

1. Engineering Courses

2. Architecture Course

3. Medical & Medical Allied Courses (except BAMS Course)

4. BAMS Course

5. B.Pharm Course

The Rank List for Engineering Courses is based on an equal weightage of 50:50 will be given to normalize the score obtained in the Entrance Exam for Engineering and the marks obtained by the candidate in the final year of the qualifying examination for Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.

If the candidate has not studied Chemistry, the marks obtained in Computer Science shall be considered. If the candidate has not studied Chemistry and Computer Science, the marks obtained in Biotechnology shall be considered. If the candidate has not studied Chemistry, Computer Science, and Biotechnology, the marks obtained in Biology shall be considered.
