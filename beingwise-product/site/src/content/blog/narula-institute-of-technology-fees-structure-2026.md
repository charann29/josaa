Narula Institute of Technology, Agarpara, Kolkata, is a private autonomous engineering college under the JIS Group Educational Initiatives, affiliated with MAKAUT, WB and approved by AICTE. For students shortlisting private engineering colleges in Kolkata, the fee is one of the first numbers they look for.

The Narula Institute of Technology fees structure for B.Tech is charged semester-wise across eight semesters and differs by branch. As per the WBJEEB institute profile, the consolidated total B.Tech fee is INR 6,99,900 across four years. The official branch-wise fee chart published by the institute shows NIT college fees ranging from INR 4,24,700 for branches like Electrical, Civil, and Mechanical Engineering, to INR 7,04,700 for CSE. TFW students pay between INR 1,18,700 and INR 2,20,700, depending on the branch. This article covers the complete Narula Institute of Technology fee structure for Btech.

## Narula Institute of Technology Hostel Fees

As per Narula Institute of Technology Fees Structure, the institute provides separate hostels for boys and girls, with 200 seats each. Hostel accommodation at Narula Institute of Technology covers both food and lodging. The table below shows the Narula Institute of Technology hostel fees as per the institute’s official admission leaflet for 2024-25.

| Fee Component | Amount (INR) |
| --- | --- |
| Food and Lodging (per semester / 6 months) | ₹36,000 (₹6,000 × 6 months) |
| One-Time Hostel Admission Fee | ₹5,000 |
| Total Payable at First Hostel Admission | **₹41,000** |

**Note:**

- The food and lodging fee of INR 36,000 applies per semester; students pay this again for the second half of the year
- The hostel admission fee of INR 5,000 is a one-time charge paid only at the time of first hostel admission
- All hostel payments are made through online mode only

## Narula Institute of Technology Fees Payment

Narula Institute of Technology fees payment is done entirely through online mode. There is no offline payment option. The following portals handle different categories of fee payment:

- **Narula Institute of Technology Fees Structure,  Exam fee for Regular students:** INR 1,200 per examination cycle
- **Narula Institute of Technology Fees Structure, Exam fee for Backlog students:** INR 1,000 per examination cycle

### Narula Institute of Technology Fees Structure: How to Pay NIT Fees?

If you are  willing to take admission at Narula Institute of Technology, you must complete the fee payment procedure by following the simple steps below:

1. Complete enrollment on the exam portal within the notified dates
2. Fill out the exam form within the specified window
3. Pay the exam fee through the fee payment link
4. Download and print the admit card

Note: Entry to the examination hall is strictly prohibited without a valid admit card. For semester fee payment issues, contact the Accounts Section through the institute helpline. Admission-related fees for WBJEE-allotted candidates are processed separately as per counselling instructions.

## Narula Institute of Technology Scholarships

The fee structure of Narula Institute of Technology comes with multiple scholarship options that can reduce the effective tuition cost for eligible students. The institute offers both institute-level and government scholarship schemes. The table below lists all available options as published on the official scholarship page at www.nit.ac.in/institute-scholarship.php.

| Scholarship Name | Criteria |
| --- | --- |
| Sibling Scholarship | 20% on tuition fee for the immediate sibling of an existing or former student |
| Employee Scholarship | For children or immediate siblings of permanent employees on regular JIS Group payroll |
| Alumni Scholarship | 20% on tuition fee for students who completed a degree or diploma from any JIS Group institution and are joining a second programme |
| Sikh Scholarship | 50% freeship on tuition fees for students from the Sikh community |
| WBFS (West Bengal Freeship Scheme) | 50% freeship on tuition fees; student names are sent directly by the Higher Education Department, Govt. of West Bengal |
| Freeship (Financial Hardship) | For students from poor financial backgrounds; applicable from the 2nd semester onwards |
| Tripura Lateral Entry Scholarship | For students from the State of Tripura admitted through Lateral Entry into B.Tech |

**Note:**

- Students who lose their primary earning member during the course are eligible for 50% freeship on tuition fees as per MAKAUT rules
- Freeship cases are reviewed based on academic performance, attendance, and conduct
- Scholarship applications are separate from the main admission process
- Government scholarship schemes for SC/ST/OBC/Minority students are also accepted; full details at www.nit.ac.in/government-scholarship-schemes.php

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the total B.Tech fee at Narula Institute of Technology?

As per official sources, the total B.Tech fee ranges from approximately INR 4,24,700 to INR 7,04,700 depending on the branch. The consolidated fee across four years is around INR 6,99,900.

### 2. Which B.Tech branch has the lowest fee at Narula Institute of Technology?

Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering have the lowest total fee at INR 4,24,700 for General category students. For TFW students, fees can be as low as INR 1,18,700.

### 3. What is the Narula Institute of Technology hostel fee?

The hostel fee is approximately INR 36,000 per semester, covering accommodation and food. A one-time admission fee of INR 5,000 is also charged, making the initial payable amount around INR 41,000.

### 4. Is the fee structure different for TFW students at Narula Institute of Technology?

Yes, TFW students pay significantly lower fees as they are exempt from tuition charges. The total fee ranges between INR 1,18,700 and INR 2,20,700 depending on the branch.

### 5. How is the Narula Institute of Technology fee payment done?

All fees are paid online through the JIS Group ERP portal. Exam fees are paid separately, typically INR 1,200 for regular students and INR 1,000 for backlog students per exam cycle.

### 6. What scholarships are available at Narula Institute of Technology?

Scholarships include sibling and alumni benefits (20% tuition fee reduction), employee scholarships, Sikh community freeship (50%), WBFS (50%), and financial hardship freeships. Government scholarships are also accepted.

### 7. Does the Narula Institute of Technology fee vary by branch?

Yes, fees vary by branch. CSE has the highest fee, while branches like EE, CE, and ME have the lowest. Other branches fall in between depending on specialization.
