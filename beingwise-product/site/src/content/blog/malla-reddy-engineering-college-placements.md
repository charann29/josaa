### Finishing School Program at MREC

To bridge the gap between classroom learning and industry expectations, Malla Reddy Engineering College (MREC) has implemented a comprehensive Finishing School Program for all B.Tech students from the 1st to 4th year. Unlike conventional models offered after graduation, MREC’s Finishing School is integrated throughout the B.Tech journey.

Based on department and recruiter requirements, MREC students undergo specialised tracks tailored to their domain. CSE and IT students receive advanced exposure to Full Stack, Cloud, Testing, Mobile, and Data technologies. ECE and EEE students focus on Embedded Systems and core electronics tools like MATLAB and DSP. Mechanical and Civil engineering students are trained in industry-standard design and simulation tools. This structured and progressive approach ensures students are placement-ready by their final year.
