## PSG College of Technology Placement​ Process

At PSG College Coimbatore, the Office of Placement and Training handles the placement process and provides pre-placement training to pre-final year students. Conducted under the PSG College of Technology placement​ cell, the placement process is structured and student-centric. It is conducted during July to December internships, and during Jan to May.  The step-by-step PSG College of Technology placement​ process for B.Tech is as follows:

- Registered Companies are invited to the placement drive.
- Students meeting the eligibility criteria of scoring 7.0+ CGPA and no backlogs can register through the official website.
- Further, prep-placement talks are conducted by the Recruiters online and offline.
- Students need to appear for the aptitude test, group discussions, technical interviews, and HR rounds.
- At the end of all rounds, Multiple offers are received by students.
- Students need to respond before the deadline.

### Pre-Placement Trainings

PSG College Coimbatore​ offers pre-placement training from the 3rd year to make students job-ready and increase employability. The pre-placement training provided is as follows:

- Workshops and Mock sessions are conducted by top Professionals.
- Soft Skills like Communication and group discussion with alumni and experts.
- Resume Building
- Personal Branding
- Career Counselling
- Industry Visits and guest lectures are arranged as well.
