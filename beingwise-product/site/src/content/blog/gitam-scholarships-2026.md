### Loyalty Scholarship

GITAM offers a fixed 10% tuition fee scholarship under its Loyalty Scholarship scheme to support students who have a prior association with the university. This benefit is available if the student falls under any one of the following categories:

- GITAM alumni enrolling for higher studies
- Children of GITAM alumni
- Real or step-siblings of those currently pursuing Gitam admissions
- Spouse of a GITAM employee
- Children of retired GITAM employees

Only one loyalty category can be applied per student. This scholarship cannot be combined with high-value merit scholarships of 75% or 100%. To continue receiving the benefit each year, students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 7.5.

### Need-based Scholarship

There are need-based scholarships in the Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management, or GITAM University, for students. Such scholarship is provided to those who have received a merit scholarship between 15% to 75%. The eligibility criteria and eligibility slab upgrades for these scholarships are as follows:

- Tier A (program fee ≥ ₹2 lakh/year): family income < ₹12 lakh/year
- Tier B (program fee < ₹2 lakh/year): family income < ₹8 lakh/year

**Eligible slab upgrades:**

- 75% → 100%
- 60% → 75%
- 40% → 60%
- 25% → 40%
- 15% → 25%

**Required Documents:**

- Both parents’/guardians’ Form-16 or ITR

Note: Candidates must note that these GITAM need based scholarship’s continuation depends on income proof and CGPA eligibility of students, and these can be reviewed annually.

## GITAM Entrance Exam

The GITAM Entrance exam, also known as GITAM GAT 2026, is the official exam conducted for students seeking admission into BTech and other undergraduate programmes at GITAM University. This GITAM University Entrance Exam 2026 is conducted across the Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru campuses. The GITAM entrance exam (GAT) covers disciplines such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English and is aligned with the chosen course. Passing the GITAM University Entrance Exam 2026 is a mandatory requirement for applicants aiming to pursue engineering programs through the GITAM Entrance Exam at the university.

**How to Register for GITAM GAT 2026?**

- Visit the official Admissions Gitam Edu​ portal, where you can begin the registration process for the GITAM Entrance Exam.
- Choose the GITAM GAT 2026 registration link related to the GITAM University Entrance Exam 2026.
- Complete the registration using a valid email address and mobile phone number.
- Enter personal, academic, and course preference details required for the GITAM Entrance Exam.
- Upload the necessary documents, including a photograph and signature.
- Make payment of the application fee as per the GITAM University Entrance Exam 2026 guidelines.
- Submit the filled application form and download the confirmation page.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **1. **What exams are considered for GITAM scholarships?****

Scholarships are mainly based on the GITAM Entrance Exam 2026 and other national-level and state-level entrance exams, including JEE Main, EAPCET (for Telangana/AP), and CUET.

### **2. Are GITAM University scholarships available for Hyderabad campus students?**

Yes, GITAM University Hyderabad Scholarships are available to eligible students.

### 3. Is there a scholarship for BTech students after graduation?

Yes, the GITAM Scholarship for BTech graduates supports higher studies.

### 4. **Do scholarships reduce the overall fee structure?**

Yes, scholarships are applied under the GITAM University Fee Structure Scholarship.

### 5. How to get a scholarship at GITAM University?

Perform well in the GITAM University Entrance Exam 2026 (or any exam accepted by the University), meet eligibility criteria, and apply within deadlines.

### 6. Can a student apply for more than one GITAM scholarship at the same time?

Yes, students may be eligible for multiple scholarships, but only after the committee’s approval will they be able to apply for 2 scholarship at the same time.

### 7. Is the GITAM scholarship applicable to all campuses?

Yes, GITAM scholarships are applicable across all campuses, including Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru, subject to campus-specific rules.

### 8. Is the scholarship valid for all years of the BTech program?

Yes, scholarships are renewable every year, provided the student maintains the required CGPA and meets continuation criteria.

### 9. Does GITAM provide hostel fee concessions along with tuition scholarships?

Yes, selected scholarship categories also offer hostel and food fee concessions based on eligibility and performance.

### 10. When should students apply for GITAM scholarships?

Students must apply for scholarships during the GITAM admission and counselling process, not after final admission.
