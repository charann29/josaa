### 2. NIAT Guntur

NIAT Guntur offers a B.Tech program in Computer Science Engineering with AI & ML specialization. It requires 10+2 with at least 50% marks and admission through the NAT entrance exam. The institute collaborates with UGC-approved Chalapathi Institute of Technology and CIET, charges around INR 2.25 Lakhs in fees, and focuses on industry-aligned skill development. Placement details are not publicly reported.

**Admission Process**

1. Submit the NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test) Application Form online and pay the registration fee of INR 1200 (non-refundable).
2. Appear for the NAT, a 90-minute test that evaluates cognitive abilities through three sections: Psychometric Test (personality and emotional traits), Critical Thinking (analytical skills), and Mathematics (problem-solving).
3. Qualify in NAT and attend a one-on-one counselling session to discuss career goals.
4. Based on your NAT performance and counselling preferences, you will be recommended to either Chalapathi Institute of Technology or CIET, collaborating with NIAT, which best fits your aspirations.
5. Apply to the preferred university; admission is processed according to the university’s eligibility criteria and AICTE/UGC norms.

**Documents Required**

- Completed application form for NIAT Guntur admission.
- 12th-grade mark sheet (showing Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics).
- Qualifying exam certificate.
- Identity proof (Aadhaar card, voter ID, or passport).
- Passport size photographs.
- Any other documents required by the specific university during admission processing (may include migration certificate, caste certificate if applicable).

The final admission to NIAT Guntur is at the discretion of the Chalapathi Institute of Technology and CIET, with which NIAT is collaborating, following their policies aligned with AICTE and UGC guidelines.
