### TS EAMCET Counselling 2026

After the TS EAMCET 2026 results are declared, those who qualify for the exam will be able to take part in the TS EAMCET counseling process organized by the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE). The whole TS EAMCET counseling process takes place in the online mode and usually involves various steps.

Candidates have to follow each step carefully in order to get themselves admitted into the college of their choice and desired branch. Candidates are required to keep checking the latest schedule regarding the TS EAMCET counseling process 2026.

### How to check the TS EAMCET Seat Allotment Status?

Students will be able to know their TS EAMCET seat allotment 2026 status by logging onto the official TS EAMCET counselling website and using the following procedure:

- Go to the official TS EAMCET counselling website.
- Locate the link named ‘Seat Allotment Result’ or ‘Phase-wise Allotment’.
- Enter the necessary details like Registration number, Hall ticket number, and Date of birth.
- Now, click on the submit option.
- TS EAMCET seat allotment status will be displayed on the screen.
- Take the printout of your allotment order.
- Proceed to complete the rest of the admission procedures, including Payment of tuition fees, self-reporting online, and reporting at the allotted institute.
