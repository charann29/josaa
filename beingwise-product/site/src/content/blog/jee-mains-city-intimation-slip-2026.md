## What Is JEE Mains City Intimation Slip 2026?

The JEE Main 2026 City Intimation Slip is a document released by the National Testing Agency (NTA) that provides advanced information about the exam city allotted to candidates for the Session 1 examination. This slip is not an admit card but rather serves as a preliminary notification that helps candidates plan their travel and accommodation arrangements. The city slip essentially informs you where your examination centre will be located geographically, though the exact examination center address and other detailed information will be provided later through the actual admit card.​

### Key Differences: JEE Mains Exam City Intimation Slip vs.JEE Main Admit Card

Understanding the distinction between these two documents is crucial for every JEE aspirant. Many candidates often get confused between the city intimation slip and the admit card, leading to unnecessary anxiety and panic.​

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Particulats** | **JEE Main City Intimation Slip** | **JEE Main Exam Admit Card** |
| Purpose | Provides advance notification of exam city and date | Required for entry into the examination hall |
| Contents | Exam city, allotted exam date, shift timing (may be included) | Exact exam center address, roll number, photograph, signature, shift timing |
| Entry to Exam Hall | Cannot be used for examination center entry | Mandatory for entry; must carry original copy |
| Release Timing | Released 10-15 days before examinations | Released 3-4 days before exam date |
| Validity | Limited to planning purposes | An official document is required for exam appearance |

## Step-by-Step Guide: How To Download JEE Mains City Intimation Slip 2026

Downloading the city intimation slip is a straightforward process. Follow these simple steps to access your document without any hassles:​

**Step 1: Visit the Official NTA Website**

Open your web browser and navigate to the official JEE Main portal at jeemain.nta.nic.in. Always ensure you are accessing the genuine website to avoid phishing or fraud attempts. Look for the secure HTTPS connection in your browser’s address bar.​

**Step 2: Locate the City Intimation Slip Link**

On the homepage, scroll down to find the announcements section. Look for the link titled “Advance City Intimation for JEE(Main)-2026 [Session-I]” or “JEE Main 2026 Session 1 City Intimation Slip.” This link is prominently displayed under the latest announcements.​

**Step 3: Click on the Download Link**

Click on the city intimation slip link. You will be redirected to the candidate login page, where you will need to enter your credentials.​

**Step 4: Enter Your Login Credentials**

On the login page, you will be required to provide the following information:​

- Application Number (received during registration)
- Password (set during registration or reset if forgotten)
- Security Pin (displayed on the screen)
- Date of Birth (as registered in your application form)

Carefully enter each credential, paying special attention to avoid typos, as even a single incorrect character will prevent login.​

**Step 5: Complete the Verification**

After entering your credentials, click the “Submit” button. Ensure your internet connection is stable during this process. If the page takes time to load, wait patiently instead of refreshing, as heavy traffic on the NTA website can cause temporary delays.​

**Step 6: View Your JEE Mains City Intimation Slip 2026 Session 1**

Once successfully logged in, your JEE Mains City Intimation Slip 2026 Session 1 will appear on the screen. The document will display:​

- Your allocated examination city
- Your examination date (specific day within the scheduled period)
- Possibly your shift timing information
- Your application number
- Personal details as registered

**Step 7: Download the PDF**

Click on the “Download” button to save the city intimation slip as a PDF file on your device. Choose a secure location on your computer where you can easily retrieve it later.​

**Step 8: Print Multiple Copies**

It is strongly recommended to print at least 2-3 copies of JEE Mains City Intimation Slip 2026 Session 1. Keep these copies safe for reference during your travel arrangements and exam preparation period. While the slip is not required at the examination center, it serves as official proof of your allotted city.​

**Step 9: Verify All Details Carefully**

Before finalising, carefully review all information mentioned on your slip:​

- Name spelling and accuracy
- Allotted city name
- Examination date
- Your registered application number

  - The city slip is not a valid document for exam hall entry.

  - NTA does not authorise sharing these documents online.
  - Keep all exam-related documents confidential and protect login credentials.
- **Mistake 4: Trusting Unofficial or “Leaked” Information**
  - Claims of leaked exam centres or allotments are false and unreliable.
  - NTA never releases information unofficially.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****What is the exact purpose of the JEE Main City Intimation Slip 2026 Session 1?****

The city intimation slip serves as an advance notification to inform candidates about their allotted examination city and scheduled exam date. It is designed to help candidates plan their travel, accommodation, and exam day logistics well in advance. However, it is not a document required for examination center entry.​

### ************2. ******Is the JEE Mains Exam city intimation slip the same as the admit card?******************

No, they are completely different documents. The JEE Mains exam city slip only provides the exam city and date information, while the admit card contains detailed exam centre address, roll number, exact shift timing, and your photograph. Only the admit card is valid for entering the examination hall.​

### **3.********Can I use NTA JEE Mains city intimation slip to enter the examination center?**********

No, absolutely not. JEE Mains city slip session 1 cannot be used for examination center entry. You must carry the official admit card along with a valid government-issued ID to gain entry to the examination hall.​

### 4. ********What should I do if my allotted city is very far from my hometown?********

NTA’s JEE Main city allotment 2026 is final and cannot be changed based on personal preferences. The allotment is done systematically based on your registered location and center availability across India. You must:​
a. Accept the allotment and make travel arrangements accordingly​
b. Book transportation and accommodation in advance to avoid last-minute hassles
c. Plan your journey considering the exam date and travel time and inform your family about the allotted city immediately

### 5. How many times can I download JEE Mains city intimation slip 2026?

You can download your city intimation slip multiple times from the official website. There is no limit to the number of downloads. You can download it whenever needed for reference or planning purposes.​

### 6. Can my exam city or date be changed after the city slip is released?

No, once the city intimation slip is released, your exam city and date allotment is final. NTA does not entertain requests for city changes or exam date changes. You must appear for the exam on your allotted date and city.​

### 7. Is JEE Mains city intimation slip 2026 session 1 required at the exam center?

No, the city intimation slip is not required at the examination center. However, you should carry it as a reference document during your journey to maintain a record of your allotted city and exam date. The only mandatory document at the exam centre is the admit card with a valid government-issued ID.
