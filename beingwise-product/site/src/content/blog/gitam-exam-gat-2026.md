## Who is eligible for the GITAM Exam 2026?

Students need to fulfill the following eligibility criteria for the GITAM University entrance exam for B.Tech:

- Students should be Indian Citizens
- Students must pass the class 10+2 level exam with Physics, Chemistry, and Maths as mandatory subjects
- Must have obtained 60% marks in aggregate in the class 12 exam.

## How to Apply for the GITAM Exam 2026?

Students who are eligible for the GITAM exam need to apply online through the GITAM Admission portal. The application is open till May 14, 2026. The registration and application process for the GITAM Exam 2026:

- Open the [Gitam University Application​ Portal](http://apply.gitam.edu)
- Click on the GITAM application link under the UG admission section.
- Register for the GITAM Admission Test
- Complete registration and create a new account with the required details.
- Once the GITAM student login​ credentials are generated, fill out the GITAM Application Form.
- Enter all the required personal, academic, course, and communication details.
- Upload the scanned copies of the required documents for the GITAM Application.
- Select the GITAM exam centre and review all the filled details.
- Pay the GITAM Application fee of INR 1200 online and submit.
- Download the application confirmation page for future reference and counselling.

## How to Download the GITAM Admit Card?

The GITAM Exam Admit Card is released online shortly before the exam dates. Students need to visit the official website for GITAM admission to download the hall ticket. Check the released admit card link and click on it. Enter the GITAM Application login ID and password to view the admit card. Click on proceed, and the hall ticket will appear on the screen. Check the details on the GITAM hall ticket and download it. Important details like the candidate’s name, ticket number, program applied for, address of the exam centre, photograph, and exam schedule are given on the hall ticket. Remember to carry it to the exam hall on the exam day.

## GITAM GAT Results

GITAM admission test results 2026 are scheduled to be announced on May 17, 2026, on the official website of Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management (GITAM). Students can download the exam result using their GITAM application login credentials from the admission portal. ## GITAM GAT Counselling

After the results and interviews are done, applicants need to attend the GITAM GAT Counselling for the online selection and seat allotment process. Conducted in multiple phases, the counselling process is entirely managed through GITAM’s official portal. The GITAM GAT Counselling is conducted in the following steps:

- GITAM exam-qualified students receive a counselling call letter online.
- Students need to download it from the GITAM admission portal using their login ID and password.
- Register for the online GAT counselling.
- Fill in the preferences for the GITAM campus (Vizag/Bengaluru/Hyderabad) and the course/branch of B.Tech by priority.
- A non-refundable counselling fee payment has to be made through online banking.
- Further, seats are allotted through the online counselling system.
- Seats are allotted based on GAT rank, category, preference and seat availability.
- Students who have been allotted seats need to report to the designated GITAM campus within the given deadline.
- Verify the documents and pay the tuition/admission fee.

### **Check out the GITAM Admission Process in Brief**

Students can take a quick look at the GITAM Admission process:

- Eligible students can apply on the [GITAM official portal](https://apply.gitam.edu/) using a valid email ID and Mobile number.

- Select a GAT Exam centre and fill in the other required details for the application.
- Upload the required documents for GAT and submit the application.
- Appear for the Gitam Entrance Exam 2026 and secure a valid score.
- A Valid score in JEE Main or state-level EAMCET exam is also applicable.

- Select the preferred course and campus of GITAM University.
- Pay the counselling fee.
- Seats are then allotted based on GAT rank, preferences, and availability.
- Once the seats are allotted, students need to report to the allotted campus within the deadline.
- Verify the original documents and pay the first-year tuition fees to confirm admission.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the GITAM University application last date?

The GITAM University application last date is May 14, 2026.

### 2. What is the GITAM admission last date?

The GITAM University admission last date is May 30, 2026.

### 3. What is the GITAM Exam 2026?

GITAM GAT 2026 (GITAM Admission Test) is a university-level entrance exam conducted by GITAM University for admission into B.Tech and other UG/PG programs across its Bangalore, Vizag, and Hyderabad campuses.

### 4. Is the GITAM GAT mandatory for B.Tech admission?

No, GITAM GAT is not mandatory. While it is the primary admission route, students with a valid JEE Main score can also apply for B.Tech programs.

### 5. Where can I apply for GITAM GAT 2026?

Students can apply through the official GITAM admission portal using their GITAM student login credentials.

### 6. Is there a correction window for the GITAM Exam application?

No, GITAM does not provide a correction window or form update option after the application has been submitted.
