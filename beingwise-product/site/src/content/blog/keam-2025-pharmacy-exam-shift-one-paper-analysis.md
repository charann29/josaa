## KEAM 2025 Pharmacy Exam Pattern: April 24th

The KEAM  2025 pharmacy exam pattern  will vary slightly compared to engineering students. **KEAM for Pharmacy exam consists of only two subjects, i.e. Physics and Chemistry.**

Physics comprises 45 questions, and chemistry consists of 30 questions. The marking scheme is the same as that of engineering students. Students will earn four marks for the correct response, and they will effectively lose one mark for an incorrect response. Here is the table for a clearer understanding:

|  |  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Subject** | **No of Questions** | **Marks** | **Marking Scheme** | **Time** |
| Physics | 45 | 180 | +4 Marks for Correct Answer  -1 Mark for Incorrect Answer | 90 Minutes |
| Chemistry | 30 | 120 |
| **Total** | **75** | **400** |

## KEAM Pharmacy Exam Analysis 2025: April 24th Shift 1 Students Review

Here are student reviews for the April 24th KEAM PCM exam:

### **Sreeganga – Kannur**

“The exam was okay and reasonably easy. It felt simpler than Brilliant’s model exams. Physics was moderate, while Chemistry had a mix of Organic and Inorganic questions. Overall, I’d say it was moderate-easy.”

### **Abhinanda – Kottayam**

“I found the exam easy. Chemistry was more comfortable, and there were more questions in it. Brilliant’s prediction videos helped a lot. I finished early and had time to review. It felt easy to moderate.”

### **Basheer – Malappuram**

“It was an easy exam. I was more confident in Physics. Chemistry had direct NCERT-based questions, with some slightly confusing statement types. Many model exam questions were repeated. I had time left to review.”

### **Shruthi – Pathanamthitta**

“Today’s exam was easier than yesterday’s. Chemistry was the easiest for me—both Organic and Inorganic were comfortable. Brilliant’s model papers really helped. I’d say it was an easy-level exam.”

### **Michael – Ernakulam**

“Much easier than yesterday. Chemistry was simpler than Physics. Statement-type questions were common but manageable. Some deleted portions showed up, but they didn’t trouble me. I had enough time to review everything.”
