## Key Anna University Scholarship Schemes For B.Tech

These blends merit, need, and government support to offer students a reduced annual tuition fee. These yearly renewable Anna University scholarships are discussed in detail below:

Mostly, the Merit cum means scholarship is based on the TNEA ranks, Family income, semester-wise performance, and Class 12 academics. ## Anna University Scholarship For B.Tech – Eligibility Criteria

Students applying for the Anna University Scholarship must fulfil the scheme-specific eligibility criteria along with the following general eligibility factors:

- Enrol via TNEA single-window counselling
- No Lateral Entry/Self-Supporting streams are eligible
- No Academic Arrears
- Minimum 7.0 CGPA
- 85%+ Attendance
- Family Income less than INR 1 Lakh to INR 2.5 Lakhs per year.

## Anna University Scholarship For B.Tech – Application Process

To apply for Anna University Scholarship for B.Tech, students need to follow the given steps:

- Collect [Anna University Scholarship Form](https://www.annauniv.edu/act/scholarship/index_files/SC%20ST%20Fresh%20Form.pdf)​ from the CUIC Portal/the Dean’s office
- Or, fill out the Anna University Scholarship Form​ available on the official website.
- Submit the marksheets of Class 12 or the semester, family income certificate, no-arrears certificate, and conduct letter
- Make sure to submit before the deadline.
- Get selected via academic merit or TNEA rank.
- Shortlisted Candidates are called for Verification.

For Government schemes, BC/MBC/SC/ST/First Graduate candidates can register on the Tamil Nadu Scholarship portal using an Aadhar card. Fill out the online form with the required academic details. Upload the community/income certificate, bank passbook and fee receipt. Submit the hard copy to the dean’s office of Anna University campus by the deadlines. The Anna University scholarship amount​ is directly credited to the account.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Does Anna University offer a scholarship for B.Tech 2nd year students?

Yes, Anna University offers scholarships for B.Tech 2nd year students through campus-level financial aid and scholarship schemes.

### 2. What are the main private scholarships for engineering students at Anna University?

The main private scholarships include the Temenos Scholarship, RNTBCI Merit-Cum-Means Scholarship, and the Bank of Tokyo Mitsubishi Scholarship at the UG level.

### 3. Who is eligible for Anna University scholarships for B.Tech?

Students admitted through the TNEA admission process are eligible. Eligibility also depends on Class 12 academic performance, family income, and community category.

### 4. What is the scholarship amount for B.Tech at Anna University?

The scholarship amount varies by scheme, with financial assistance of up to INR 30,000 provided to eligible students.

### 5. Are Anna University scholarships for B.Tech renewable each year?

Yes, scholarships are renewable for up to 4 years if students maintain the required CGPA, meet financial eligibility criteria, and submit renewal applications on time.
