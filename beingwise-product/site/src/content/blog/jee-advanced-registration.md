## JEE Advanced Exam Overview

The JEE Advanced  Exam is Conducted Annually for the students who have qualified through JEE Main. The **JEE Advanced is conducted by IIT Kanpur**, and the exam is conducted in 2 papers. Both the Papers are mandatory to attempt. The JEE Advanced Exam is going to be conducted for 6 Hours a Day. The JEE Advanced is one of the important exams for engineering stream students as it gives admission into premier institutions such as IITs.

## JEE Advanced Application Form

The candidates need to apply for **JEE Advanced** through the official website, and the application form will be released by the Exam Conducting Authority(IIT Kanpur). The Application requires contact information, academic details, and Personal details. The Fee payment can be done through both Online and offline modes.

## Eligibility Criteria for JEE Advanced

Here are the [Eligibility criteria for JEE Advanced](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-advanced/jee-advanced-2025-eligibility-criteria-reduced/) for Indian Citizens.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Category** | **Criteria** |
| **JEE Main Exam** | Candidates must have qualified for the JEE Main Exam and must be among the top 2,50,000 Candidates |
| **Age** | Candidates must have been born on or after October 1st, 2000  to pass the Attempt Limit SC/ST, PwD candidates have a 5-year Relaxation Period |
| **Number of Attempts** | Candidates can attempt the JEE Advanced exam twice. |
| **12th Marks** | For General and OBC Candidates, the 12th percentage required to write JEE Advanced is 75%. For SC/ST candidates, the percentage requirement is 65%. |
| **Eligibility for IITs** | The Candidate should not have been admitted into any IIT before.   The candidates are only eligible  If their seat is canceled  If they withdrew their seat in the last round of JoSAA  If they did not report online |
| **12th exam Appearnce** | The candidates are eligible only if they have appeared for the exam in 2025,2024,2023. |

## Eligibility Criteria for Foreigners

Candidates who are not citizens of India by Birth are considered foreign candidates. Here are the eligibility criteria for foreigners

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Category** | **Criteria** |
| **JEE Main Exam** | Foreign Candidates can attempt the JEE Advanced Exam without attempting the JEE Main Exam. |
| **Candidates** | All the Foreign Candidates will be considered instead of the top 2,50,000 from the JEE Main Exam. |
| **Identity Proof** | The identity proof of the candidate will be required to attempt the exam. The identity proof can be a Foreign passport/citizenship certificate/OCI or PIO Card. |
| **Supernumerary seats** | Foreign Candidates have a limited number of extra seats. These seats do not affect the regular admission process and are not subject to India’s reservation system, and there is no female quota for Foreign candidates. |

## Third Attempt Candidates

The IIT Kanpur has reverted the decision to conduct JEE Advanced three times. However, the Supreme Court has ordered the authorities to **allow the attempts on the aspirants who dropped out of college between November 5th and 18th,2024,** and **students who have appeared for their class 12 examination for the first time in 2023**. For all the other candidates, the number of attempts remains the same, i.e., 2 attempts for 2 consecutive years.

## How to Do Fee Payment for JEE Advanced Exam?

The Fee Payment for JEE Advanced can be done through both online mode and offline mode. Here is the process for fee payment through both online and offline mode.

### Fee Payment Through Online Mode

- The Fee payment can be done through  Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking.
- The  Pay the Registration Fee option will be available after filling out the application form.
- The candidates will be redirected to the State Bank of India Multi-Option Payment System (MOPS) page.
- There will be the following options for the payment; candidates can select any option to pay the exam fee
  - Credit Card
  - Debit Card
  - Net Banking
  - SBI Branch
- After paying the registration fee, candidates will have an application with a JEE Advanced Application Number.

### Fee Payment Through Offline Mode

- The  Pay the Registration Fee option will be available after filling out the application form.
- The candidates will be redirected to the State Bank of India Multi-Option Payment System (MOPS) page.
- For Cash Payment, the Candidates need to select “SBI Branch,”  and an e-challan will be generated.
- Candidates can print this e-challan and visit the SBI Branch and pay the cash at the SBI Branch.
- After the Payment is done, the candidates can download the registration details.

The JEE Advanced Exam is going to be conducted for 360 Marks. It consists of 2 papers, and each paper is conducted for 180 Marks.
