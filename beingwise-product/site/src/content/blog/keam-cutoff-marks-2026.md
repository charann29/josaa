## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. When will the KEAM Cutoff 2026 be released?**

The KEAM Cutoff 2026 will be published during the counselling process. Candidates are advised to regularly check official notifications for updates.

**Q2. Is the KEAM cutoff the same for all colleges and courses?**

No, the KEAM cutoff is different for each college and course. Generally, closing ranks are higher for popular institutes and branches.

**Q3. Does the KEAM cutoff change after each counselling round?**

Yes, after each round of counselling, the KEAM cutoff may vary. Closing ranks are affected by seat vacancies and candidate selections.

**Q4. Are cutoffs different for reservation categories in KEAM?**

Yes, there are separate cutoffs for different reservation categories. Ranks can vary significantly across categories.

**Q5. What happens if I meet the qualifying marks in KEAM?**

If you achieve the qualifying marks, you will be included in the rank list. However, admission depends on the cutoff for specific colleges and branches.
