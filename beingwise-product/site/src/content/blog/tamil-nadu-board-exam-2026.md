The Tamil Nadu Board Exam Time Table can be downloaded via the official website of the Tamil Nadu Board. Students appearing for the HSE+1 or HSE+2 exams can refer to the following steps to download the exam timetable online:

- The Tamil Nadu Board exam timetable 2026 will open.

## Tips to prepare for the Tamil Nadu Board Exam

Students appearing for the Tamil Nadu Board Exams can adhere to the following preparation tips to follow before the exams:

- Firstly, strictly follow the prescribed syllabus and make a proper study plan to move forward with the exam preparations.
- Keep the Tamil Nadu Board Exam Time Table handy and create a realistic day-to-day routine for preparation.
- Allocate time to each subject based on its weightage.
- Make a list of important topics that have to be covered on a weekly or monthly basis. Track the progress at the end of each week.
- Solve Model Papers and Previous Year Question Papers of exams in the Tamil Nadu Board. It helps in recognizing the exam pattern and type of questions.
- After completing the syllabus, keep enough time to revise as per a revision schedule.
- Lastly, be confident in your preparation and keep a calm mind before the exam. Do not stress and keep yourself motivated.

## Tamil Nadu Board Exam Day Guideline – Key Points to Remember

Follow these important guidelines on the exam day:

- Students must carry their Tamil Nadu Board exam Admit card, ID Card, and all the required stationery for the exams.
- To avoid any last-minute mess-up, arrive 30 minutes early at the exam centre.
- Keep the first 10 minutes to read the question paper thoroughly and mentally frame a gist of the answers.
- No electronic devices, scribbing, or leaving early are allowed.
- Ensure to write the exam with fair means and do not engage in any unfair business like cheating.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ********When will the Tamil Nadu Board Exam Admit Card be released?********

The admit card is generally released a few weeks before the Tamil Nadu Board exams are about to start. Students can expect the admit card to be released in February.

### 2.**When are the Tamil Nadu board exam 10th result expected?**

It is expected to be out in May. For the Tamil Nadu Board Exam 2025, the 10th exam results were out on May 10.

### 3. ****How to download the Tamil Nadu Board Marksheet?****

Students need to visit the tnresults.nic.in or dge.tn.gov.in and enter the result link. Enter the Tamil Nadu Board exam registration number, date of birth, and submit it. The result will appear on screen. Simply download and save the result for the future. A proper marksheet will be provided by the respective schools or colleges.
