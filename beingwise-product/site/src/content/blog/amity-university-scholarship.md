## Amity Scholarship | Continuation and Renewal Requirements

To continue or renew the scholarships given by Amity University, students will be required to meet the following eligibility criteria.

**Academic Performance:**

Students must maintain a minimum CGPA or secure a position within the top percentage of their class (e.g., top 5% for 100% scholarships, top 10–15% for partial scholarships). Failure to meet the required academic benchmarks may result in reduction or withdrawal of the scholarship.

**Annual Review:**

Scholarships are reviewed annually. Students must submit updated academic records and, where applicable, performance certificates (such as for sports or co-curricular achievements) each year.

**Conduct and Attendance:**

Maintaining good conduct and satisfactory attendance as per university regulations is mandatory for scholarship renewal.

**Specific Scholarship Policies:**

Some scholarships, such as sports or martyr’s scholarships, may have additional requirements, like continued participation in university teams or verification of eligibility by the relevant committee.

**Documentation:**

All supporting documents for renewal must be submitted by the specified deadline (often October 31st of the academic year) for the scholarship to continue.

## Amity University Entrance Exam

Candidates can apply for Amity Btech courses if they have appeared in JEE Mains/Any State Level Engineering Entrance Exam. If a student has not participated in these national-level or state-level entrance tests, they may choose to appear for the Amity University entrance exam named AMCAT (Amity Common Admission Test). The Amity entrance exam application form is released online on the official portal of Amity University. Students must fill out the application form to take part in the Amity University entrance exam (AMCAT). Refer to the following to learn about how to register for AMCAT 2026 (Amity Entrance Exam):

- Visit the official AMCAT website
- Click on Register or Sign Up
- Sign up using your email ID or social account
- Enter basic details like name, mobile number, email, and education details
- Verify your email/mobile with OTP
- Log in to your AMCAT dashboard
- Select the test date, time slot, and test mode
- Pay the AMCAT registration fee
- Receive test confirmation and exam details

Refer to the following to check out the complete procedure for admissions at Amity University:

Students should have completed their Class 12 Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics from a recognised educational institution as part of Amity University admissions. Minimum percentage eligibility for admission to BTech would differ among campuses and BTech fields.

To continue the Amity University admissions process for the BTech program, visit the Amity University website. You will need to complete the registration with your email address and mobile number.

**Complete Your Application Form**

Your application form for Amity University admissions must include your personal information, academic background, and your campus and BTech program of choice.

**Upload Your Documents**

You will need to upload your Class 10 and 12 mark sheets, your photo, and your signature when applying for Amity University admissions.

**Confirm Your Admission**

Once you are selected for the program (BTech), you will be required to submit valid documents for verification and pay the required Amity University fees.
