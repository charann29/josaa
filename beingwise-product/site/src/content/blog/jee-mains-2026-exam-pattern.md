## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****Has the JEE Mains 2026 exam pattern changed?****

No, the JEE Mains 2026 exam pattern remains the same as last year. Paper 1 still has 75 questions, Paper 2A has 77 questions, and Paper 2B has 100 questions. The revision made last year in Section B, where only 5 numerical questions are asked, remains unchanged.

### ************2. ******How many questions are asked in JEE Mains 2026 Paper 1?******************

JEE Mains 2026 Paper 1 (B.E./B.Tech) has 75 questions in total. Each subject includes 25 questions (20 MCQs + 5 numerical-based questions).

### **3.********What is the marking scheme for JEE Mains 2026?**********

For every correct answer, candidates will get +4 marks, and for every wrong answer, 1 mark will be deducted. There is no negative marking for unattempted questions. This marking scheme applies to both MCQs and numerical value questions.

### 4. ********What is the exam pattern for JEE Mains 2026 BArch (Paper 2A)?********

The BArch paper has 77 questions, including 25 Mathematics questions, 50 Aptitude questions, and 2 Drawing questions. The total marks are 400, with Aptitude carrying the highest weightage (200 marks).

### 5. How many questions are there in JEE Mains 2026 BPlan (Paper 2B)?

JEE Mains Paper 2B includes 100 questions: 25 from Mathematics, 50 from Aptitude and 25 from Planning. The paper carries 400 marks in total, with Aptitude contributing 200 marks.
