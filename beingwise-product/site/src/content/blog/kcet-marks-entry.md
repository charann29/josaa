**KCET Marks Entry 2025**: The KCET Marks Entry facility will be opened, tentatively from **28.05.2025, 5:30 PM** for candidates from CBSE, CICSE, 10+2, IGCSE boards including J&K candidates who have not entered their 12th marks till now. The KEA enabled this portal once again to enter the marks. For admission to the Architecture course, the candidates have to enter their marks obtained in NATA-2025 as per the eligibility.

The Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) 2025 is a state-level entrance examination, conducted by the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA). The test will be conducted on the 16th and 17th of April 2025. The KCET 2025 Exam Results are scheduled to be released on **May 20, 2025,** by the KEA. However, the results are delayed due to undisclosed reasons. The candidates are waiting for the official announcement from the examination authorities.

## KCET 2025 Marks Entry

- The candidates can enter their 12th-standard marks to the students who have passed from CBSE, CICSE, 10+2, IGCSE boards, etc., including J&K.
- Repeater students of Karnataka 2nd PU, CBSE, CICSE, 10+2, IGCSE, and ‘Clause-Y’ claimed students should also enter their marks through online.
- The candidate’s marks details should be entered **before 28-05-2025, 5.30 PM,** and must upload their marks statements in a PDF format.
- Marks details of students who have appeared for Karnataka 2nd PUC Annual 2025 will be obtained directly from the department of PUE.
- For admission to the Architecture course, the candidates should enter their marks obtained in NATA – 2025 (National Aptitude Test in Architecture).

## Steps to Enter Marks

The candidates can follow the below-mentioned steps to enter their respective marks in the KCET Marks Entry portal:

Step 1: Visit the Official Website of KCET, [cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea/](https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea/).

Step 2: Find the UGCET-2025 Marks Entry Link under the Latest Announcements.

Step 3: The candidate needs to enter their Login ID and Password to proceed.

Step 4: After logging in, you should enter 10+2 marks in the required fields.

Step 5: Preview the details and submit them.
