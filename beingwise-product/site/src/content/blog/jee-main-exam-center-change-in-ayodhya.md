NTA has released an Official notification regarding the change in the exam center. Candidates who are scheduled to appear at the Institute for Advanced Computer Technology, 21/03/44/03, Behind Choti Devkali Mandir, Tulsi Nagar Ayodhya, Faizabad, UP-224723, will now have to **appear at SRS Digital Institute, MIG-35 Kaushalpuri Colony, Phase-2, Ayodhya**.

This decision was made by the exam conducting authority based on recommendations from the District Level Committee and the District Administration. As the Mahakumbh is happening in Prayagraj the center has been changed. The NTA has issued Fresh Admit cards for the candidates who are assigned to the Institute for Advanced Computer Technology, 21/03/44/03, Behind Choti Devkali Mandir, Tulsi Nagar Ayodhya, Faizabad, UP-224723 center.

## JEE Main Changed Exam Center Details

NTA has previously changed the center for Sharjah Dubai due to unavoidable circumstances. Now, Because of Mahakumbh in Ayodhya which may lead to a huge congregation of devotees near the exam center area. The decision to change the exam center has been taken.
