The Candidates appearing for AP EAMCET-2024 at **Rajeev Gandhi Memorial College of Engineering & Technology** and **Santhiram Engineering College, Nandyal**, should note that there is a change in their test centre. The candidates are advised to download their revised AP EAMCET Hall Ticket and appear for the test at the revised test centre.

The candidates can download their AP EAMCET 2024 Hall Ticket using the link given below:

The AP EAMCET is a common state-level entrance test conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada under the APSCHE (Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education).

The AP EAMCET 2024 examination is conducted from **16.05.2024 to 17.05.2024 for the Agriculture & Pharmacy** Stream and from **18.05.2024 to 23.05.2024 for the Engineering** Stream.

## Changes in AP EAMCET 2024 Test Centres

- The AP EAMCET authorities have released a notification regarding the test centres through the official website.
- The Candidates appearing for AP EAMCET-2024 at **Rajeev Gandhi Memorial College of Engineering & Technology** and **Santhiram Engineering College, Nandyal**, should note that there is a change in their test centre.
- The candidates are advised to download their revised AP EAMCET Hall Ticket and appear for the test at the revised test centre.
- The steps to download the AP EAMCET 2024 Hall Ticket are given below

## Steps to Download Hall Ticket

The candidates can follow the below-given steps to download their hall ticket:

1. Go to the AP EAMCET 2024 Official Website <https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/>

2. Click on the “Download Hall Ticket” link on the right side of the home page.

3. Candidates must enter the following credentials and click on ‘Proceed’.

- Registration Number (or) Payment Reference ID
- Qualifying Examination Hall Ticket Number
- Date of Birth

4. The candidate’s hall ticket will appear on the screen.

5. The candidates must take the printout of their Admit Card and preserve it. They need to take the hall ticket along with them during the examination day.
