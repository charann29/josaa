## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What should I do after the SRMJEEE result is declared?

After the SRMJEEE result 2026 is declared, you should immediately register for counselling and complete your choice filling. Early participation increases the chances of securing better seats.

### 2. Can I participate in counselling for multiple phases?

Yes, candidates can participate in multiple counselling phases based on eligibility. Seat allotment is conducted separately in each phase of the SRMJEEE admission process.

### 3. Is it necessary to download the SRMJEEE scorecard?

Yes, downloading the SRMJEEE scorecard is important as it is required during counselling and other admission-related procedures.

### 4. What happens if I miss the counselling deadline?

If you miss the counselling deadline, you may lose that round of admission. You can only participate in the next available round, subject to seat availability.

### 5. Can I upgrade my allotted seat later?

Yes, seat upgradation is possible in later counselling rounds depending on availability and your rank.

### 6. Do all campuses have the same cutoff?

No, cutoffs vary across campuses and branches. Popular courses usually have higher cutoff requirements.

### 7. Is the SRMJEEE result valid for the next year?

No, the SRMJEEE result is valid only for the current admission cycle and cannot be carried forward to the next year.

### 8. Can I apply for multiple courses during counselling?

Yes, you can select multiple courses and campuses during counselling to improve your chances of admission.

### 9. What if I forget my login details?

You can use the “Forgot Password” option on the official portal to reset your credentials and access your SRMJEEE result.

### 10. Is there any re-evaluation process for the SRMJEEE result?

No, there is no re-evaluation process. The SRMJEEE result declared by the authority is considered final.
