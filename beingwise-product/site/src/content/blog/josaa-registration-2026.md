JoSAA Registration for the year 2026 has started for candidates seeking admission to IITs, NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs across India. Candidates who have qualified for the JEE Main 2026 & JEE Advanced 2026 exams can now register themselves through the official website of JoSAA.

The Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) is an authority responsible for conducting the centralized JoSAA counseling process for engineering college admissions. Qualified candidates need to complete the JoSAA counselling registration, fill out their choices, and lock their options within the given deadline to apply for seat allocation.

## Step-by-Step Process for JoSAA Counselling Registration 2026

Below, we have shared the step-by-step complete procedure for JoSAA Registration and choice filling.

**Step 1: Online JoSAA Registration of New Candidate**

1. Visit to the JoSAA Counselling Official Portal at https://josaa.nic.in/.
2. Log in to the JoSAA portal and click on the “New Candidate Registration” tab.
3. Enter your JEE Main 2026 credentials on the JoSAA candidate login.
4. Type in your JEE Main 2026 application number, date of birth, candidate’s name, and mother’s name.
5. Confirm the details by entering the OTP received on your mobile number and email ID.
6. Create a password for the JoSAA login.

**Step 2: Log in to the JoSAA Candidate Portal**

1. Upon successful JoSAA registration, log in to the candidate portal of JoSAA using your JEE Main 2026 application number and the created password.

2. Validate the OTP received on your mobile number and email ID.
3. Check your personal, educational, and category information carefully.
4. In case of any discrepancies, correct them with the help of the ‘edit’ facility and confirm the details.

**Step 3: JoSAA Counseling Choice Filling and Choice Locking**

1. Once you complete the registration process, move on to the choice-filling stage.
2. Refrain from making any changes to your details after locking your preferences.
3. Enter your choice for institute type, institute name, and course program from the provided list.
4. Use the “Add” tab to enter your choices.
5. Arrange your entered choices according to your preference.
6. A candidate can make additions to or change their choices until the last date.
7. Lock your choices before June 11, 2026, as it is necessary.

It should be noted that in case a candidate fails to lock his/her choices before the JoSAA last date, the JoSAA will lock the last saved choice of the candidate, which will be used in further counselling sessions.

## JoSAA Counselling Registration Fees

There is no separate JoSAA Counselling registration fee. Candidates will have to pay the JoSAA Seat Acceptance Fee in case they are allotted seats during the counseling process. There is a different JoSAA Counselling fee for the Seat acceptance fee based on the category of candidates as follows

| Category | JoSAA Seat Acceptance Fee |
| --- | --- |
| General / OBC-NCL / EWS | ₹35,000 |
| SC / ST / PwD Candidates | ₹15,000 |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. When does JoSAA counselling start?**

Ans. JoSAA Registration 2026 started on June 2, 2026, at 5:00 PM. Eligible JEE Main and JEE Advanced candidates can register online on the official website.

**2. What is the JoSAA counselling registration last date?**

Ans. JoSAA Registration 2026 and choice filling will continue until June 11, 2026, at 5:00 PM.

**3. Is there any registration fee for JoSAA counselling?**

Ans. No, there is no registration fee for JoSAA counselling. Candidates only need to pay the seat acceptance fee after seat allotment.

**4. What documents are needed for JoSAA counselling?**

Ans. Required documents include Class 10 & 12 mark sheets, JEE Main/JEE Advanced scorecard, category certificate (if applicable), PwD certificate, Aadhaar card, medical certificate, and bank account details.

**5. How can one apply for JoSAA counselling 2026?**

Ans. Candidates need to visit josaa.nic.in, register using JEE credentials, fill and lock their college and course choices, and participate in the seat allocation process.
