### 2. How to download the TS EAPCET Answer key?

### 3. What is the marking scheme for TS EAMCET 2025?

The marking scheme for TS EAMCET is that every correct answer carries one mark and there is no negative making for incorrect answers. The total marks are calculated based on the number of correct answers marked by the candidate.
