The National Testing Agency has issued a Reschedule Notice for a few of the candidates who attempted the Exam on **January 22nd  in the eTalent, (TC code- 40086), No.3, Belmar Estate, Nagasandra Main Rd, Amaravathi Layout, Nagara Bavi, Nalagadderanahalli, Peenya Bengaluru, Karnataka Center**. As Per the Authority, the rescheduling is because of a ’ **technical snag in the examination center**’.

The Authority has mentioned that these candidates can appear for the exam on the **28th or 29th of January 2025**. In the Official Notice, it is also mentioned that the candidates will be issued fresh admit cards. There are **114 candidates affected** by this decision.

## What should the Affected Candidates do Next?

The candidates who are affected by this decision are 114. These are the next steps to be followed for these candidates.

- The Authority will issue **fresh admit cards for the candidates** who were affected by this technical issue.
- As the Admit cards are not yet issued for the candidates who will be appearing on January 28th, and January 29th, 2025. The admit cards will be released simultaneously.
- The Admit Cards will be available on the Official website.
- The Affected Candidates are advised to check the official website to know the availability of admit cards.

## What Should a Candidate do if there is any Technical Issue?

The Candidates can face technical issues while attempting the exam. Here are the instructions on what should be done.

- If there is any technical Snag Immediately inform the Invigilator or the Examination Authorities.
- The candidates will be provided with an online support system during the examination which can be used in case of technical error.
- The NTA Helpline can also be contacted by the candidates in case of a technical issue.
