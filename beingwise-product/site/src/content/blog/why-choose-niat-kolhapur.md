## Does NIAT in Kolhapur Have Any Recognition or Accreditation?

### Q6. Is NIAT in Kolhapur a college or university?

No, NIAT (NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies) is not a college or university. It is an upskilling program that operates from Training Centres provided by collaborating universities like Sanjay Ghodawat University and Sandip University. The degree is awarded by the respective collaborating university, not by NIAT.

### Q7. What is the NAT, and is it an entrance exam for university admission?

NAT stands for NxtWave Assessment Test. It is not an entrance examination conducted by the university. Collaborating Universities independently conduct their own entrance examinations for admission into their degree programs. NAT is specific to NIAT’s upskilling program evaluation process.

### Q8. What certification does a student receive from NIAT in Kolhapur?

Students who complete NIAT’s upskilling program receive an IRC, which validates their hands-on skills and job-market readiness. The academic degree, B.Tech CSE in AI and Data Science, is awarded independently by the collaborating university upon meeting its academic requirements.
