## WBJEE Application Form Correction: How to Edit?

WBJEE Application Form Correction window will open on April 7, 2026. If you notice any mistakes in your application form, the correction window allows you to fix them easily. The process is simple. Here’s how you can access and update your [WBJEE 2026](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-2026/) application form:

- Visit the official WBJEE website
- Look for and click on the “application correction” link available on the homepage
- Log in using your application number and password
- Open your submitted application form and make the required changes
- Review all the updated details carefully before proceeding
- Submit the corrected form

## Common Mistakes Candidates Fix During WBJEE Application Form Correction

From past trends, these are the most frequent errors students correct:

- Uploading blurred or incorrect photographs
- Entering wrong marks or subject combinations
- Typing errors in email ID or phone number
- Incorrect board or passing year details

## WBJEE Form Correction 2026: Things to Remember

Before making changes to your WBJEE application form, it’s important to understand the guidelines set by the exam authority. Keeping these points in mind will help you avoid mistakes during the correction process.

- The form correction facility is available only in online mode; no offline or direct requests to WBJEEB will be accepted.
- Candidates are not required to pay any fee to make changes to the application form.
- Once the corrected details are submitted, no further modifications will be allowed.
- After the correction window closes, the authorities will not accept any requests for changes, so candidates must review their form carefully before final submission.

## WBJEE Registration 2026

[WBJEE 2026 registration process](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-registration-process-2026/) started on March 10, 2026, and is being conducted online through the official website. Candidates could register till April 8, 2026.

### Steps to Complete WBJEE Registration 2026

To fill out the WBJEE application form smoothly, candidates can follow these steps:

1. Visit the official WBJEE website.
2. Click on the “New Candidate Registration” option.
3. Read the instructions carefully and proceed further.
4. Enter basic details such as name, date of birth, parents’ details, contact information, and address.
5. Create a password and submit the registration form.
6. Verify your details using the OTP sent to your registered mobile number and email ID.
7. Log in using your application number and password.
8. Fill in the remaining application details, including academic information and category details.
9. Choose your preferred exam centres.
10. Upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature in the prescribed format.
11. Review the complete form carefully and submit it.
12. Pay the application fee through the available online payment methods.
13. Download and save the confirmation page for future reference.
