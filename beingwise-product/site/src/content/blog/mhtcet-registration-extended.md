The Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell has announced an extension for the registration period of the Maharashtra Common Eligibility Test (MHT CET) 2025. Applicants can now **submit their applications by February 28, 2025,** although **a late fee of Rs 500** will be applied for submissions made after the original deadline.

To register for the MHTCET exam, candidates can visit the official website, ***[cetcell.mahacet.org](http://cetcell.mahacet.org)***.

## Steps to Fill Out the MHTCET Application Form for 2025

Understanding the application process and knowing how to apply for the exam helps candidates apply for the exam without any discrepancies and ensures the application process is smooth. Here are the steps to complete the [MHTCET](https://collegedost.in/blog/mht-cet/) application process:

**Step 1:** Register for MHTCET by providing your email ID and phone number.

**Step 2:** Fill up the application form by providing the necessary details in the designated particulars.

**Step 3:** Provide the details related to your residence, category, etc.

**Step 4:** Provide all your academic details in the given particulars.

**Step 5:** Select your preference for the language of the examination and examination centre.

**Step 6:** Upload the scanned images of your photograph.

**Step 7:** Pay the application fee and take the printout of the application for future reference.

## Documents required for MHTCET Registration

Here is the list of documents that candidates need to upload while registering for the MHTCET exam.

- Marksheets of Class X and XII
- Proof of DOB
- Domicile certificate
- Caste certificate(If applicable)
- Proof of residence
- Scanned Images of photo identity card and photograph.

# MHTCET Registration Day Extension

The registration process for MHTCET **started on December 30th, 2024**. The officials have announced the extended date of the registration process, i.e. **February 23 to 27, 2025.** Candidates who are willing to register for the exam have to apply for the MHTCET exam by **paying a late registration fee of ₹500.**

Candidates must have passed their Class 12th from a recognised university/Institute to be eligible to appear for the MHTCET exam. Candidates can visit the official website, i.e., cetcell.mahacet.org and register for the exam by filling out the application form and paying the application fee after uploading the required documents.

The exam date for MHTCET 2025 is **April 9-17, 2025, for PCB and April 19-27,2025 for PCM.**

## Eligibility Criteria for MHTCET Registration

Candidates aspiring to appear for the MHTCET exam are advised to go through the eligibility criteria, as failing to satisfy the eligibility criteria might lead to disturbances during/after the examination process. Here are the eligibility criteria for the MHTCET exam:

- Candidates should be Indian Nationals.
- There is no age limit to appear for the MHTCET exam.
- Candidates appearing/have passed class 12th with PCM/PCB as majors can apply.
- Candidates must have secured at least 45%(40%) in the qualifying examination.

## MHTCET Exam Pattern

MHTCET syllabus consists of 20% of the exam syllabus from Class XI and 80% of the syllabus from Class XII. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **Has the MHTCET registration process started?**

Yes, the  MHTCET registration process started on December 30th, 2025.

### 2. **What are the registration fees for MHTCET 2025?**

The registration fee for MHTCET is ₹1,000 for General candidates and ₹800 for candidates from OBC/SC/ST/EwS/Transgenders/Orphans.

### 3. **What are all the available payment methods in MHTCET?**

Candidates can pay the application fee through internet banking/credit card/debit card/UPI.

### 4. **What is the exam date of MHTCET 2025?**

MHTCET exam date for 2025 is April 9-17, 2025, for PCB and April 19-27,2025 for PCM.

### 5. **What are all the subjects in MHTCET?**

Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry for PCM and Maths, Physics and Chemistry for PCB.

### **6. Is MHTCET registration extended?**

Yes, the registration date for the MHTCET exam has been extended till February 27th, 2025.

### **7. How to prepare for the MHTCET exam?**

Curate a proper study schedule, divide your syllabus topics based on their weightage, and include revision and mock tests to fill your knowledge gaps to excel in the exam.

### **8. Can I take a calculator for MHTCET exam?**

Calculators and any electronic gadgets are not allowed inside the examination hall.

### **9. Can I edit my application form after submission?**

Yes, MHTCET will allow candidates to edit their application form in the specified dates

### **10. How to download the admit card in MHTCET?**

Candidates can download the MHTCET admit card by visiting the official website and providing the credentials like application ID and password.
