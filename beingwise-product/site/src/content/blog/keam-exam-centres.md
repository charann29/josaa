The KEAM 2026 acts as a gateway for students seeking admission in Engineering, Architecture, and Medical courses across Kerala. The examination authorities opened the KEAM 2026 application on January 5, 2026.

The KEAM exam centres will help participating candidates with their allotted exam centre information and codes. Students are provided with a choice of filling out the KEAM exam centres preference during the registration window.

The examination centre of the candidate is allocated based on preference and availability. The Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE) Kerala follows a standard procedure of finalising the KEAM exam venue for students based on their choices and availability of the exam centres. ## Important Instructions to the Candidates

The important instructions to be followed by the candidate are mentioned below:

1. The KEAM Hall Ticket 2026 is an important document, and they must carry their Admit card without fail.

2. The KEAM 2026 admit card is a non-transferable document. Any alteration of the Hall Ticket will result in the disqualification of the Candidate.

3. The candidates must arrive at the examination center before 2 hours before their examination time.

4. Candidate is not allowed to enter the exam hall if he is late even by a minute from the commencement of the exam.

5. The candidates must carry the following things along with the admit card

- A valid Photo ID proof of the candidate, such as an Aadhar Card, Voter ID, Driving License, or PAN Card
- A Ballpoint Pen (Black or Blue)
- Caste Certificate (for SC/ST Category Candidates only)

6. The candidates are not allowed to carry the following items to the examination hall:

- Text Books
- Study Materials
- Calculators
- Written Notes or Printed Material
- Bits of Papers
- Mobile Phones
- Smart Watches

It’s important to follow the instructions set by the examination authorities. Except for the Hall Ticket and the important documents, no other items are allowed in the examination hall. If any prohibited items are found, the candidate’s examination will be cancelled, and they will also be banned from future examinations.

## Steps to Download KEAM Hall Ticket

To download the admit card of KEAM, the student can follow the steps below:

- The student needs to visit the website of the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations, Kerala.
- On the home page of the website, the link for the candidate’s login is given for the students of KEAM 2026.
- The student needs to enter the required information for the login, i.e., the application number and the password.
- Then the student needs to click on the link for the admit card of KEAM that is given on the dashboard.
- The student then needs to check all the information that is given on the admit card regarding the student, i.e., the student’s information on the KEAM admit card. The student can check the information about the KEAM Exam Centres 2026.
- The student can then obtain the admit card of KEAM and carry it on the day of the exam.

**Note:** Students can check the information regarding the KEAM exam centres in Kerala on the KEAM admit card as well.

### What to Do If There Is an Error in the KEAM Admit Card?

In case the candidates discover any errors on the KEAM admit card, they are supposed to communicate with the Commissioner of Entrance Examinations, Kerala.

They might be required to submit their application information and supporting documents to seek corrections.

The solution to such errors should be done before the date of the KEAM exam to prevent any form of complication during the examination in the KEAM Exam Centre.

**Note:** Some students may require the help of Keam facilitation centres for the examination process. These centres assist candidates who have questions regarding admit cards, exam centres, and other KEAM procedures.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### ****1. How to check my KEAM exam centre code?****

The KEAM admit card contains the KEAM exam centre codes and the venue details.

### ****2. What should I do if my KEAM hall ticket has incorrect centre details?****

Immediately request the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations to correct at the respective KEAM Exam Centre.

### ****3. Are there separate KEAM exam centres for engineering and architecture streams?****

No, there are no differences between the KEAM exam centres in terms of stream, although seating can change within.

### ****4. Can parents accompany students to the KEAM exam centre?****

Parents are not permitted to enter the examination hall of the KEAM exam centres in Kerala.

### ****5. How many students are allocated per KEAM exam centre?****

The centres have different numbers of students based on their locations, based on the KEAM Exam Centres 2026 capacity.

### ****6. Is seating arranged as per roll number in KEAM centres?****

Yes, the seat arrangement will be done in advance as per roll numbers to provide easy behaviour in the KEAM exam centres.

### ****7. Are refreshment facilities available at KEAM exam centres?****

KEAM exam centres do not serve refreshments as a rule; the students should be ready.

### ****8. Can candidates request a different KEAM exam centre for medical courses?****

There are not many cases when the KEAM Exam Centre changes; the candidates need to act according to official prescriptions.
