## Table of Contents

The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) has released the official notification regarding the **documents required for WBJEE registration 2026** on its website. Candidates who wish to appear for the WBJEE exam must prepare all the required documents before filling out the application form online. Keeping the necessary documents ready helps applicants complete the registration process smoothly without technical errors.

**WBJEE 2026 registration process began on March 10, 2026**, and candidates can submit the application form until **April 5, 2026**. During the application process, applicants must upload scanned copies of their photograph and signature and provide details from their academic certificates and identification documents.

Documents such as **Class 10 and Class 12 marksheets, valid ID proof, photograph, signature, category certificate, and domicile certificate** may be required while filling out the application form. Candidates must also ensure that the uploaded images meet the specifications prescribed by WBJEEB. This article provides a complete overview of the **documents required for WBJEE registration 2026, document specifications, application dates, and important guidelines** to help candidates complete the registration successfully.

## List of Documents Required for WBJEE Registration 2026

Before beginning the registration process, candidates should keep the required documents ready in the prescribed format. Having all the documents prepared beforehand can help avoid delays while filling out the online form.

The following documents are generally required for completing [**WBJEE 2026 application form**](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-registration-process-2026/):

- Aadhaar Card or any valid government ID proof
- Class 10 marksheet or certificate
- Class 12 marksheet or certificate (if available)
- A passport-size photograph of the candidate
- Scanned signature of the candidate
- Category certificate (for reserved category candidates)
- Domicile certificate (if applicable)
- Valid email ID and mobile number
- Online payment details such as debit card, credit card, or net banking

## Documents Required for WBJEE Registration: Detailed Breakdown

Academic documents are important to verify a [WBJEE](https://collegedost.in/blog/wbjee/wbjee-2026/)  candidate’s educational qualifications. During the application process, candidates may need to provide details from their **Class 10 and Class 12 certificates**.

### Class 10 Certificate

The Class 10 certificate is generally required as **proof of date of birth**. The details entered in the application form must match the information printed on the certificate.

### Class 12 Marksheet

The Class 12 marksheet is used to verify whether the candidate meets the academic eligibility criteria for engineering admission. Candidates applying for B.Tech programs should have studied the following subjects in Class 12:

- Physics
- Mathematics
- Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology

Students who are appearing for their Class 12 examinations in 2026 are also eligible to apply, provided they fulfil the eligibility requirements during admission.

### Category Certificate Requirements for WBJEE

Candidates belonging to reserved categories must upload valid category certificates to claim reservation benefits during admission.

The commonly accepted category certificates include:

- **SC (Scheduled Caste)** certificate
- **ST (Scheduled Tribe)** certificate
- **OBC-A / OBC-B certificate**
- **EWS (Economically Weaker Section)** certificate

### Domicile Certificate for WBJEE 2026

A domicile certificate may be required to confirm that the candidate is a resident of West Bengal. This document is particularly important for candidates applying for **state quota seats in participating colleges**.

Documents that may serve as domicile proof include:

- Residential certificate issued by a competent authority
- School certificate showing continuous study in West Bengal
- Parent domicile certificate

## Documents Required for WBJEE Registration: Specifications

While filling out the application form, candidates are required to upload scanned copies of their photograph and signature. The files must follow the specifications set by the examination authority. The table below shows the **format, size, and dimensions for uploading documents in the WBJEE application form**:

|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Document** | **Format** | **File Size** | **Dimensions** |
| Photograph | JPG/JPEG | 10 KB – 200 KB | 4 cm × 3 cm |
| Signature | JPG/JPEG | 4 KB – 30 KB | 4 cm × 1.5 cm |

### WBJEE 2026 Photograph Guidelines

Uploading the correct photograph is an important part of the registration process. The photograph will appear on important documents such as the **admit card, result, and counselling records**. Candidates should follow these guidelines while uploading their photograph:

- The photograph should be **recent and passport-sized**.
- A **plain white or light background** is recommended.
- The candidate’s face must be **clearly visible** in the image.
- Avoid wearing sunglasses, caps, or accessories that cover the face.
- The photograph should be taken within the **last few months** before registration.
- Ensure that the image is not blurred or distorted.

### WBJEE 2026 Signature Guidelines

Apart from the photograph, candidates must also upload a scanned copy of their signature while submitting the application form. The following guidelines should be followed while uploading the signature:

- The candidate must sign on **plain white paper using black or blue ink**.
- The signature should be written **clearly and within the specified area**.
- The image should be scanned properly and uploaded in **JPG/JPEG format**.
- The file size must remain within the prescribed limit.
- Avoid uploading digital or typed signatures.

## Steps to Fill WBJEE Registration Form 2026

Candidates can follow the steps below to complete the WBJEE 2026 application process:

1. Visit the official WBJEE website.
2. Click on the **WBJEE 2026 registration link** on the homepage.
3. Create login credentials using personal details such as name, date of birth, and mobile number.
4. Log in using the generated credentials and start filling out the application form.
5. Enter personal details, academic information, and preferred exam city.
6. Upload the required documents in the prescribed format.
7. Pay the application fee using online payment methods.
8. Submit the form and download the confirmation page for future reference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1. **What documents are required for WBJEE registration 2026?**

Candidates need documents such as Class 10 and 12 marksheets, valid ID proof, passport-size photograph, scanned signature, category certificate (if applicable), and a valid email ID and mobile number while filling out the WBJEE application form.

### Q2. **What is the size of photograph required for WBJEE application form?**

The photograph should be uploaded in JPG or JPEG format with a file size between **10 KB and 200 KB** and dimensions around **4 cm × 3 cm**.

### Q3. **Is a domicile certificate required for WBJEE registration?**

A domicile certificate may be required to confirm that the candidate is a resident of West Bengal and to claim state quota seats in participating colleges.

### Q4. **Can I correct mistakes in the WBJEE application form?**

Yes, WBJEEB provides a **correction window** where candidates can modify certain details in the application form.

### Q5. **When will WBJEE 2026 exam be conducted?**

The **WBJEE 2026 examination will be conducted on May 24, 2026**.
