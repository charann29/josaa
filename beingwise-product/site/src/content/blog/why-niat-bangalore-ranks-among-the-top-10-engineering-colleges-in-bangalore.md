## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Is NIAT in Bangalore a college or a university?

Neither. NIAT is an upskilling centre, not a college or university. It operates within collaborating universities like S-VYASA University School of Advanced Studies and St. Peter’s Institute of Higher Education and Research. The B.Tech degree is awarded by the university, not NIAT.

### 2. Why do students choose NIAT over regular engineering colleges?

Students choose NIAT for its industry-focused upskilling program that runs alongside their degree. It offers project-based learning, AI-powered tools, and training designed using inputs from 3,000+ companies and 10,000+ tech professionals.

### 3. Can NIAT guarantee admission into S-VYASA University or St. Peter’s Institute?

No, NIAT cannot guarantee admission. It acts as a facilitator, sharing student details with universities. Admission is solely based on the university’s eligibility criteria and policies.

### 4. What is the difference between the B.Tech degree and NIAT’s IRC?

The B.Tech degree is awarded by the collaborating university and is UGC-recognised and AICTE-approved. The Industry Ready Certificate (IRC) is awarded by NxtWave and validates practical skills and industry readiness.

### 5. Does NIAT Bangalore have its own campus?

No, NIAT does not have its own campus. It operates within infrastructure provided by partner universities, including labs, libraries, hostels, and sports facilities.

### 6. Who teaches at NIAT Bangalore?

NIAT has its own trainers, mentors, and success coaches who are industry professionals. They are different from university faculty and focus on practical, real-world skills.

### 7. What happens if I don’t get admission into the collaborating university?

Admission is handled entirely by the university. If eligibility criteria are not met, NIAT cannot intervene. Students should check university requirements before applying.

### 8. Does NIAT Bangalore support startups or entrepreneurship?

Yes, NIAT provides access to incubation centres, mentorship, and funding support of up to ₹10 Crores, along with connections to angel investors and venture capitalists.

### 9. Does enrolling in NIAT guarantee placement?

No, placement is not guaranteed. NIAT provides strong placement support including resume building, mock interviews, and career guidance, but outcomes depend on individual performance and skill development.
