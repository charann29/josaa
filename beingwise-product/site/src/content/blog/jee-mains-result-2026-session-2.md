## Impact of Tie-Breaking Rule in JEE Mains Merit List

Tie-breaking policies have a vital effect on the JEE Main 2026 merit list because the National Testing Agency uses these guidelines to rank students having the same percentile score. The NTA follows a specific hierarchy to determine the ranking of the students by giving preference to the mathematics section, then physics, and then chemistry. If there is still any similarity between the marks obtained by the students, then the ratio of right and wrong answers is considered.

Thus, tie-breaking policies directly affect the JEE Mains merit list because even students with the same marks get different ranks depending on the scores obtained in various subjects and the number of wrong answers.

## What happens after NTA JEE Mains Result 2026 Session 2 released?

As soon as the JEE Mains Session 2 results are declared by the National Testing Agency, candidates move ahead in the next important phases of the admission process. **As far as the JEE Main result, it will be based on the best percentile obtained in two sessions; thus, candidates will get the All India Rank (AIR).**

### Release of Minimum Marks for JEE Advanced

Candidates who obtain above the JEE Mains Cut off marks become eligible to take the JEE Advanced exam; this exam will help them join any IITs. Besides, the cut-off marks according to different categories are also released, which help in knowing about admission chances.

### JoSAA Counselling & Seat Allotment

In the next phase, the counselling is organised by the Joint Seat Allocation Authority, where students have to register themselves and select their choices regarding the colleges and courses. Students have to participate in various seat allotment rounds depending on the ranks obtained.

### Admission to State & Private Colleges

In addition to this, students can also get admission in many private colleges accepting JEE Main and can participate in other states’ counselling procedures as well. Some of the students who are dissatisfied with their performance in JEE Main result opt for other examinations or decide to prepare well for the next year.
