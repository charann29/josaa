West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination is a state-level examination conducted by **WBJEEB** for entrance into undergraduate courses like engineering, architecture, pharmacy and architecture. Officials announced the exam date to be **April 27, 2025**. Candidates preparing for the exam have to go through several processes, including preparation, application process, and counselling processes. While officials are yet to release the counselling schedule, the tentative dates are given below to help students prepare for the counselling process with the required documents.

## Counselling Dates for WBJEE 2025

Official dates for counselling are yet to be released, and students should check the official website **wbjeeb.nic.in** for updates regarding the counselling process:

### WBJEE Counselling Dates: Round 1

The counselling process for **[WBJEE 2025](https://collegedost.in/blog/category/wbjee/)** typically begins with online registration. Candidates must register by providing personal, academic, and exam-related details on the official portal, followed by fee payment and choice filling for preferred colleges and courses.

### WBJEE Counselling Dates: Round 2

Round 2 of the **WBJEE 2025** counselling process is for candidates who were not allotted a seat in Round 1 or wish to upgrade their allotted seats. The process follows a similar structure as Round 1, with updates on seat allotments and document verification.

Below are the tentative dates for Round 2 of WBJEE 2025 counselling:

|  |  |

### WBJEE Counselling Dates: Mop Up Round

The mop-up round is the final counselling phase conducted to fill any remaining vacant seats after Rounds 1 and 2. This round provides candidates with another opportunity to secure admission if seats are still available.

Below are the tentative dates for the WBJEE 2025 mop-up round:

|  |  |

**Note:** The mop-up round is an additional counselling round that is conducted to fill the vacant seats.

## Documents required for WBJEE 2025

Candidates need to appear for the entrance exam with a set of required documents such as their previous academic documents, hall tickets, etc. Document verification is a vital process, as failing to submit any documents might lead to the cancellation of your admission. Candidates are advised to carry additional sets of required documents during the counselling process.

Here is the list of required documents for WBJEE 2025 counselling.

- Class X marks sheet.
- Class XII mark sheet.
- Class X Hall ticket.
- Domicile certificate
- Category certificate
- PwD certificate (if applicable)
- TfW certificate (if applicable)
- Income certificate
- WBJEE admit card and Rank card.

## WBJEE 2025: Fees for Counselling

Candidates are required to pay INR 500 for the counselling process. This fee is not refundable under any circumstances. Upon getting a seat, candidates are required to pay the fee for admission. Based on the institution and category of the students the fees might vary.

Detailed fee structure is given below:

| --- | --- |
| **Institution** | **Admission Fee** |
| **Tuition fee Waiver(TfW)** | ₹3,000 |
| **Open Category Seats** | ₹5,000 |
