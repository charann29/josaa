# GITAM Admissions 2026 – B.Tech Eligibility & Selection Criteria

GITAM Admissions for B.Tech for the 2026 academic year, the primary admission criteria depend on merit in the entrance exam and the last qualifying exam. The B.Tech eligibility criteria and selection criteria for GITAM University admission are as follows:

### B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

B.Tech applicants must meet the following eligibility criteria to apply for GITAM University Admission:

- **Academic Qualification**: Must have passed the Class 12 exam or any equivalent exam from a recognised board with compulsory subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
- **Minimum Marks**: Students must have a minimum aggregate of 60% or 6.0 CGPA in the 10+2 exam level.
- **Nationality**: Indian Citizens, NRI, and OCI candidates are permitted.
- **Age Limit**: There is no upper age limit for admission.

### B.Tech Selection Criteria

The selection process for GITAM Admissions for B.Tech requires students to go through the standardised criteria as follows:

- **GITAM Entrance Exam**: Students must go through the GITAM Admission Test (GAT) for B.Tech applicants. Other accepted national and state-level entrance exam scores are JEE Mains/ AP EAMCET/ TS EAMCET
- **Merit Ranking**: After qualifying for the GITAM GAT Exam, a merit list is prepared based on the GAT Ranks or other valid entrance scores.
- **Interview Round**: Selected applicants need to appear for the personal interview round and proceed with the counselling round.
- **Counselling & Seat Allotment**: Chosen candidates are then invited to participate in the centralised counselling process and allotted seats based on the candidate’s rank, course preference, campus availability, and merit.

# GITAM University B.Tech Admission Process

GITAM Admissions are conducted in 3 Phases for UG and PG programs. With the beginning of the 3rd phase, the last date of the GITAM application is May 14, 2026. The GITAM Admissions process is an online-led process designed to evaluate candidates through both university-level and recognised national-state-level exams. The Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management course admissions process is as follows:

1. **Online Registration**: Visit the official Admissions GITAM Edu portal to register with a valid Email ID and Mobile number​ and generate GITAM Application login credentials.
2. **GITAM Application Submission**: Login to the GITAM admission portal with your GITAM application login credentials. Complete the application form with personal, academic, and other contact details. Specify the preferred B.Tech branch and campus as well.
3. **Application Fee Payment**: Pay the GITAM application fee of INR 1200 through online netbanking, debit/credit card, or UPI.
4. **Required Documents Upload**: Upload the scanned copies of the required documents for the Class 12 mark sheets, identification proof, and entrance exam score card.
5. **Appear for the GITAM GAT Exam & Interview Round**: Appear for the GITAM GAT Exam 2026, or a valid JEE Mains/ AP EAMCET/ TS EAMCET exam score is accepted as well. Followed by a personal interview round.
6. **Counselling & Merit Selection**: Based on the exam and interview round performance, GITAM University publishes a merit list. Shortlisted candidates are invited for the centralised counselling process.
7. **Document Verification & Admission Confirmation**: Once students are allotted a seat based on their campus and course preference, they must report to the allotted campus for document verification. Further, applicants must pay admission fees as per the GITAM fee structure to confirm admission. ​

As the current 2026 GITAM admissions cycle is ongoing, applicants must apply and complete the admission process before the GITAM admission last date on May 30, 2026. ​

## GITAM Admissions Test – GITAM GAT Exam 2026

GITAM University conducts an entrance exam for admission into various B.Tech programs known as the GITAM Admission Test (GAT). It is a 2-hour duration CBT-based test comprising 100 MCQ-based questions covering Mathematics/Biology, Physics, and Chemistry. It is designed to evaluate aptitude and core subject knowledge. With a total of 200 marks, no negative marking is included.

Students need to apply to the GITAM GAT Exam 2026 through the GITAM Admission Portal and register to create their login credentials. By entering the basic details, students need to complete filling out the online application form and choose the preferred B.Tech program and campus. With the uploading of the documents and payment of the application fee, students can further appear for the GITAM Admissions test.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What are the GITAM CSE fees?

As per the GITAM University fee structure, the total fees for the CSE program is approximately ₹15.76 lakhs for a 4-year duration.

### 2. How are the GITAM University placement outcomes for B.Tech?

GITAM University has strong placement outcomes for B.Tech programs, with top recruiters like Tech Mahindra, ICICI Bank, Dabur, PwC, and Deloitte. The highest package goes up to ₹34 LPA, with an average package of around ₹5.42 LPA.

### 3. Is there a direct GITAM admissions process for B.Tech?

No, there is no direct admission route for B.Tech programs at GITAM University.

### 4. Are GITAM admissions available through lateral entry?

Yes, GITAM offers lateral entry admissions for B.Tech programs. Students must have a diploma in engineering or technology and meet the required eligibility criteria.

### 5. What is the GITAM syllabus & curriculum?

The B.Tech curriculum at GITAM is industry-aligned and includes subjects such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Cyber Security, Computer Graphics & Visualization, Systems & Architecture, and Cyber-Physical Systems.
