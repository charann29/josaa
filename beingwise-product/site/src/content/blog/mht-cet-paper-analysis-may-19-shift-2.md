### MHT CET Answer Key 2026 – How to Raise Objections?

Candidates who feel that any answer is incorrect in the provisional answer key can challenge it by lodging objections online within the deadline. Candidates need to make a non-refundable objection fee payment of Rs 1000 per question. Here is the complete step-by-step process to object to the MHT CET Answer Key.

- Visit the MHT CET Official Portal at cetcell.mahacet.org.
- Click the “Candidate Login” option.
- Now, log in with the required credentials.
- Upon logging in, go to the MHT CET answer key and response sheet area.
- Select the question you want to challenge.
- Select the answer that you feel is right.
- Upload supporting documents or documentation, if needed.
- Pay the objection fee of Rs 1,000 per question.
- Submit the objection form successfully.
- Candidates are encouraged to download the payment receipt and confirmation page for future reference.
