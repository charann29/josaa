Anna University is one of the premier public technical universities in India, formed through the collaboration of 4 leading engineering institutes in Chennai. Established in 1978, it provides top-quality engineering education through its 4 main campuses, 3 regional campuses, and over 500 affiliated colleges. The university departments operate from the main campuses of the College of Engineering Guindy, Madras Institute of Technology, Alagappa College of Technology, and the School of Architecture & Planning.

Anna University Chennai​ is renowned for its rigorous curriculum, strong industry collaborations, and impressive placement records. It offers a wide range of NIRF-ranked B.Tech courses across specializations like specialisations like Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Science & Information Engineering, Technology, and Electronics & Communication Engineering. With students admitted through TNEA counselling based on class 12 PCM marks, this Anna University Chennai overview blog unfolds everything from admissions and fees to campus life for B.Tech applicants.

## Anna University Admission Process For B.Tech

Anna University admission for B.Tech programs is strictly based on Class 12 PCM Merit and TNEA Counselling ranks. No separate entrance exams are conducted for Anna University course admissions. As the B.Tech applications are ongoing, students need to apply through the TNEA counselling website and enrol for the counselling process once their board exam results are announced. Before applying, students need to meet the following eligibility criteria and selection criteria for Anna University admissions:

| Aspect | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **B.Tech Eligibility Criteria** | Passed Class 12 with PCM scoring at least 45% in aggregate |
| **B.Tech Selection Criteria** | Class 12 PCM Normalised Marks through TNEA conversion + TNEA Counselling |

Students fulfilling the Anna University admission criteria for B.Tech enrollment need to complete registration through the TNEA official website. Refer to the following admission and application process to apply to Anna University:

- Register at the [TNEA portal](https://www.tneaonline.org/) and generate a login ID and password.
- Log in to the newly created account and complete the application process.
- Enter the required details regarding academics and personal identity into the application form.
- Scan and upload the TNEA required documents.
- Pay the TNEA application fees and submit the application.
- Selected candidates in the TNEA Rank list need to appear for the choice filling round.
- Fill out the choices regarding college and branch as per priority. Choose Anna University and its campuses.
- Once the seats are allotted, students need to confirm and block the seats before the deadline.
- Book the seat with an initial counselling fee.
- Meanwhile, register on Anna University’s official website as well.
- Upload the required documents and pay the tuition fee online to confirm admission.
- Once done, Students are duly notified regarding the admission dates and reporting dates.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. When are the Anna University results released?

Anna University results for each B.Tech semester are usually released 3–4 months after the exams. Typically, results are declared between November and December.

### 2. What is the total fee for B.Tech?

The total fee for B.Tech programs at Anna University is approximately ₹1.38 lakhs for the 4-year duration. This does not include hostel or additional charges.

### 3. How to get B.Tech admission into Anna University?

Admission to B.Tech programs at Anna University is based on TNEA (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions) counselling, which considers Class 12 marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

### 4. What is the Anna University address?

The main campus of Anna University is located at Sardar Patel Road, Kotturpuram, Chennai, Tamil Nadu – 600025.
