## JEE Main 2026 April 6 Shift 1 Question Paper Review by Students

Knowing the students’ reaction to the JEE Main 2026 April 6 Shift 1 Question Paper is essential. We have talked to the test takers outside the exam center to know their reaction. Some of the student reactions are as follows

- **Aman** considered the paper moderate in the conceptual aspect but very tough in the aspect of execution. As per his perception, the difficulty lies not in attempting the questions but in doing so within the stipulated time and being accurate. According to him, the exam needed a lot of higher-level mathematics, especially for Physics and Mathematics questions. He did not choose long questions of Maths unless he was sure.
- **According to Riya,** the exam paper was well-balanced but highly time-consuming. She could devote more than 2 hours to Physics and Chemistry, but could not manage more than 40-50 minutes for Maths. Even though she was well prepared for the paper, she found it difficult to attempt the Maths questions owing to a lack of time.
- **For Sneha,** Physics was not too difficult conceptually, but extremely calculative. She faced difficulties in the section of Modern Physics, specifically the problems associated with binding energy and unit conversions.  Problems related to rotational motion involved multi-step calculations involving torque and the number of rotations. Physics was highly scoring but highly calculative and time-consuming.
- **Neha said,** Organic and Inorganic Chemistry were very NCERT-based and straightforward. However, the Physical Chemistry calculations were long and tedious in topics such as Chemical Kinetics.
