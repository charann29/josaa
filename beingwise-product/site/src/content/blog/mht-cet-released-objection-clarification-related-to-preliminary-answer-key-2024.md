The MHT CET Cell released a clarification notice regarding objection tracking on 29th May 2024. Candidates who have raised objections against the answer key for particular questions were resolved for each slot which are mentioned below. The CET cell has taken necessary action items on specific objections and resolved all unique question IDs.

On May 21st, 2024, a notice was issued on the website, announcing the availability of objection forms. Candidates were informed that objections were accepted for both PCB and PCM groups. For PCM, Total 1425 objections were raised by candidates, and 232 unique objection IDs were resolved. For PCB, a Total of 17 objections were raised by candidates, and 5 unique objections were resolved. These objections covered a range of subjects including Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology.

The Chief Moderators and Moderators for Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology subjects have thoroughly reviewed the objections raised by candidates and submitted their reports.

MHT CET exams were conducted from 02nd May 2024 to 16th May 2024 for the PCM Group and from 22nd April 2024 to 30th April 2024 for the PCB Group. The MHT CET exams are conducted in 30 sessions.
