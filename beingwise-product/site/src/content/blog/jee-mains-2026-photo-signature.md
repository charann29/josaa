Candidates need to upload a recent passport-size colour photo with their face clearly visible (around 80% of the image), ears shown, and no mask. The background should be white. ## Common Mistakes To Avoid While Uploading JEE Mains 2026 Photo & Signature

Many students face rejection due to issues with their uploaded documents. Make sure you avoid the following mistakes while submitting the JEE Mains 2026 photo & signature.

- **Uploading Old Photographs:** Only recent photos, not older than 6 months, should be uploaded. Older photos may not reflect your current appearance and will be rejected.
- **Using Photos with Masks, Caps, or Sunglasses:** Photos must clearly show your whole face, without any obstructions such as masks, caps, or sunglasses, to ensure proper identification.
- **Uploading Selfies or Group Photos:** A proper passport-size photo of a single person must be submitted. Selfies or group images are invalid and rejected.
- **Submitting Blurred, Pixelated, or Rotated Images:** Ensure your images are clear, high-resolution, correctly oriented, and not distorted or pixelated to avoid upload errors.
- **Incorrect Background in Photos:** The background must be a plain white colour. Coloured, dark, or patterned backgrounds are not acceptable and may result in rejection.
- **Not Following Prescribed Size and Dimension:** Photos should be 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm with a file size between 10 KB and 200 KB. Signatures must be 3.5 cm × 1.5 cm, sized 10 KB to 100 KB.
- **Using Wrong File Formats:** Only JPG or JPEG formats are accepted. PNG, PDF, or other formats will not be allowed.
- **Improper File Naming:** Files must be named exactly “Photograph” for the photo and “Signature” for the signature. Random or incorrect file names may cause technical issues.
- **Signature in Capital Letters or Digital Fonts:** Signatures must be handwritten in blue or black ink on white paper. Typed, digital font, or capitalised signatures are invalid.
- **Using Image Filters or Editing Software:** Avoid any photo editing, filters, brightness adjustments, or software enhancements. The photo and signature must be natural and unaltered for authenticity.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****What are the photo specifications for JEE Mains 2026?****

Candidates must know the JEE Main photo requirements 2026 before filling out the application form and while correcting it. The JEE Mains 2026 photo must be a recent, colored passport-size photograph showing 80% of your face and both ears, with a plain white background. The file should be in JPG/JPEG format, named “Photograph,” sized between 10 KB and 200 KB, and measure 3.5 cm by 4.5 cm. Following these specifications is mandatory to avoid application rejection or exam entry issues.

### ************2. ******Can I correct my photo during the JEE Mains 2026 application correction window?******************

Yes, you can correct the photograph’s dimensions and complete the JEE Mains 2026 photo upload during the application correction window.

### **3.********Are candidates required to submit any documents, including the confirmation Page, to NTA by post, fax, or by hand?**********

No, candidates are not required to send/submit any documents, including the Confirmation Page to NTA through Post/Fax/WhatsApp/Email/by Hand.

### 4. ********When is the JEE Mains admit card 2026 going to be released?********

The JEE Mains 2026 admit card will be released around 3 days before the exam, expected in the third week of January 2026 for Session 1. Candidates must download it from jeemain.nta.nic.in using their application number and date of birth. The admit card is essential for exam entry.

### 5. What documents are required for JEE Mains 2026 registration?

Candidates must have an Aadhaar card for e-KYC verification, a recent photograph with at least 80% of their face visible, a signature, a Class 10 certificate, and, if applicable, PwD/Category certificates. Live photo capture via webcam is mandatory during registration.​

### 6. Does the JEE Main 2026 signature have to be in blue ink only?

Candidates can use either black or blue ink for their JEE Main 2026 signature, made on plain white paper. The signature file should be between 10 KB and 100 KB in JPG or JPEG format.
