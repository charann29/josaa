## What is MHT CET Document Verification?

Verification of the list of documents Required for MHT CET Counselling is the mandatory procedure in the CAP process whereby the State CET Cell ensures that the documents provided by candidates are authentic. Candidates who manage to pass through the MHT CET document verification stage will be eligible for the preparation of merit lists, filling of choices, and admissions through CAP.

The verification exercise of the stated documents Required for MHT CET Counselling can take place both online via the e-Scrutiny portal and in person at designated Facilitation Centers (FCs). During this process, educational certificates, ID proofs, domicile proof, certificates of the category, and other supporting documents provided at the time of registration are cross-checked.

### MHT CET Document Verification Modes

Let’s take a quick guide on how to verify the list of documents required for MHT CET Counselling

#### **1. Online Document Verification (e-Scrutiny)**

The online verification process involves uploading scans of the necessary MMHT CET CAP Documents through the e-Scrutiny portal. The process is done digitally as the officials check the provided documents required for MHT CET CAP Round.

- If the MHT CET admission Documents submitted are found to be correct and comprehensive, the status of the candidate will be updated as “Successfully Registered.”
- In case the document is either unavailable, ambiguous, or incorrect, the status will be indicated as “Unverified.”
- Candidates must then re-upload the corrected documents within the specified timeline.

#### 2. Offline Document Verification (Facilitation Centre)

Candidates choosing offline document verification are required to visit the Facilitation Centre (FC) along with their original and photocopied documents for offline verification.

- Candidates have to choose the FC in the CAP registration itself.
- The process involves checking the candidate’s documents physically by the officers at the center.
- After the MHT CET CAP documents are successfully verified, the candidate gets a confirmation receipt/acknowledgment.

## MHT CET Counselling Process At a Glance

The MHT CET counselling and seat allotment process will be divided into four rounds, as it was last year. Seats are allotted according to the MHT CET merit list 2026. Candidates assigned a seat must confirm their admission online by paying the seat confirmation fees and going to the given institute. The entire MHT CET counselling process is conducted online. Check out all of the steps involved in the MHT CET 2026 counselling below.

### Step 1: Registration

The first step is to register on the official website and provide your personal, communication, and security information. After entering all of the information, a password must be generated. Once the password has been generated, the remaining details must be entered.

After providing the aforesaid information, candidates must register their qualification details (subject-wise and overall marks in 10th and 12th grades, as well as the name of the board) and examination details.

### Step 2: Upload Scanned MHT CET Counselling 2026 Documents

As part of the application procedure, candidates are required to upload scanned copies of the documents required for MHT CET Counselling, along with a recent passport-size photograph and signature.

| Document | File Size Requirement | Dimensions |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Photograph** | 4 KB to 100 KB | 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm |
| **Signature** | 1 KB to 30 KB | 3.5 cm × 1.5 cm |

### Step 3: Payment of Counselling Fees

Finally, candidates must pay the counselling fees online according to their category. The Category-wise MHT CET Counselling fees are given below

- General Category, Outside Maharashtra State Applicant, and Children of Indian Workers in Gulf Countries: Rs 800.
- Reserved Category and Persons with Disability candidates from Maharashtra will be: Rs 600.
- Children of NRI, OCI, PIO, and Foreign National: Rs. 5000.

### Step 4: Merit List Release

The admission committee will release the MHT CET merit list in a 50:50 split between class 12 and CET scores. Candidates will also be able to object to the merit list if they have any concerns about their place on the list.

### Step 5: MHT CET Choice Filling

Candidates must select their preferred college and branch (specialisation). The allocation of seats in the college will be dependent on their merit list scores.

### Step 6: MHT CET Seat Allotment

Candidates will be assigned seats based on their choices and merit position. To confirm their place, candidates must report to the designated college and pay the entrance fee.

## Common Mistakes in Uploading MHT CET Counselling 2026 Documents

In order to prevent any delay or disapproval during the verification process of MHT CET documents, aspirants should be very careful while uploading them. Some common problems that may cause a problem in verification include the following:

- Submitting blurry or unverifiable documents makes it difficult to ascertain the information.
- Submitting inaccurate documents or incorrect documents, such as a wrong mark sheet or certificate.
- Submitting files having an inappropriate file size or format other than required by the prescribed criteria.
- Forgetting to upload mandatory category certificates, including Caste certificate, Caste validity certificate, Non-Creamy Layer certificate, EWS certificate, and so forth.
- Having discrepancies between different uploaded documents, such as personal details like name, date of birth, or category.
- Submission of expired reservation certificates, like Non-Creamy Layer and EWS certificates.
- Uploading an incomplete document in which one or more pages, seals, or signatures are missing.
- Not attending to the correction suggestion by the e-Scrutiny authority and re-uploading within the stipulated time period.

## MHT CET CAP Documents to avail Reservation

Applicants who are availing themselves of reservations on the basis of their categories have to present some more certificates to avail themselves of such reservations in the MHT CET Counselling procedure. These MHT CET Counselling Documents about the category provide the authority with the opportunity to check if the applicant is eligible for these reservations or not.

| Document | Purpose | Applicable Category |

| **EWS Certificate** | Certifies that the candidate belongs to the Economically Weaker Section category. | EWS Candidates |
| **Income Certificate** | May be required to support EWS claims and fee concession benefits. | EWS and Other Eligible Candidates |
| **Domicile Certificate** | Proves that the candidate is a resident of Maharashtra and eligible for state quota reservations. | Maharashtra State Candidates |
| **PwD Certificate** | Confirms disability status for reservation under the Persons with Disabilities category. | PwD Candidates |
| **Defence Service Certificate** | Required to claim a reservation under the Defence quota categories. | Defence Category Candidates |
| **Minority Certificate (if applicable)** | Used for admissions under minority institution quotas. | Minority Category Candidates |

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Q1. What are the mandatory documents for MHT CET Counselling 2026?**
   For MHT CET Counselling 2026, applicants should submit their MHT CET score card, admit card, Class 10 and 12 mark sheets, School Leaving Certificate, nationality proof, photo ID, passport-size photos, and other applicable documents like domicile, caste, EWS, or PwD certificates.
2. **Q2. Is MHT CET document verification process online or offline?**
   Document verification in the MHT CET process is carried out via an online portal called e-Scrutiny. However, if needed, candidates can also opt for verification of documents in the offline mode using the Facilitation Centers (FC).
3. **Q3. What happens if my MHT CET counselling documents are rejected?**
   The status of the document upload will be ‘unverified’ in case of unclear, incomplete, wrong information, or any missing documents. Applicants will be given the chance to correct/upload the documents again within the stipulated time period.
4. **Q4. Are category certificates mandatory for reservation benefits in MHT CET counselling?**
   Yes. Those applying for reservations under categories like SC, ST, OBC, EWS, NT, VJ/DT, SBC, PwD, Defence, or Minority need to upload the respective category certificates.
5. **Q5. What are the photograph and signature requirements for MHT CET Counselling 2026?**
   The passport-size photograph must be in the range of 4 KB to 100 KB in size and dimension 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm, whereas the signature must be in the range of 1 KB to 30 KB and dimensions must be 3.5 cm × 1.5 cm.
6. **Q6. Is a domicile certificate required for MHT CET counselling?**
   Yes, a domicile certificate will be required by students taking admission under the Maharashtra State Quota.
7. **Q7. Do droppers need any additional documents during MHT CET counselling?**
   Yes, those students who have taken a gap year after completing Class 12 can be asked to provide a Gap Certificate.
