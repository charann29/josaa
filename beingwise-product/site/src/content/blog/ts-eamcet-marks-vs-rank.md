The TS EAMCET 2025 rank is determined by the marks obtained in the exam. Candidates can use this article as a reference to estimate their expected rank based on their marks. It’s important to note that higher marks correspond to lower ranks. Securing a lower rank offers a good opportunity for candidates to secure admission at reputable colleges.

Previously, The EAMCET rank is calculated as 25% weightage of IPE marks and 75% weightage of EAMCET marks. But, **this year only EAMCET marks are considered** to calculate the rank.

There is no IPE weightage considered for TS EAMCET 2025.
