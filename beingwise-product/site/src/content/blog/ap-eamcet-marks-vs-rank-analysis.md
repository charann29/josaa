The candidates appearing for the Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (AP EAPCET) 2025 examination conducted by the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE), should be aware that their scores in every subject carry importance in determining their AP EAPCET 2025 rank. The overall rank in the AP EAPCET exam is based on the score you obtained in all subjects.

The Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (EAPCET) is a state-level entrance test conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada, on behalf of the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) once a year. This test is a gateway for admission into different undergraduate courses in the fields of Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy across Andhra Pradesh. According to the official notification, the AP EAPCET 2025 exam will be conducted from the **21st to the 27th of May 2025.**

## AP EAPCET 2025 Marking Scheme

The number of questions for each subject and the marks awarded for them are given below:

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Subject** | **Number of Questions** | **Marks Awarded** |
| Mathematics (or) Biology | 80 Questions | 80 Marks |
| Physics | 40 Questions | 40 Marks |
| Chemistry | 40 Questions | 40 Marks |
| **Total** | **160 Questions** | **160 Marks** |

## AP EAMCET Marks Vs Rank Analysis 2025

The factors that determine the AP EAPCET 2025 rank are

- The candidate’s subject-wise AP EAPCET marks
- The candidate’s **AP EAPCET 2025 rank**
- The candidate’s category
- The normalization of marks, etc

The AP EAPCET 2025 rank is calculated based on both the AP EAPCET 2025 marks and Intermediate marks. The rank is calculated based on the weightage of 25% IPE (Intermediate Public Examination) marks and 75% of the candidate’s AP EAPCET 2025 marks.

## Frequently Asked Questions:

### 1. What is the minimum marks required to qualify for the AP EAPCET 2025 exam?

The candidates must score at least 45% of marks (40% of marks for reserved categories like SC and ST) to qualify for the AP EAPCET 2025 examination.

### 2. For the candidates scoring below 40 marks in the AP EAPCET 2025 exam, What is the expected rank?

If a candidate scores less than 40 marks out of 160 in the AP EAPCET 2025 exam, their rank would be greater than 40,000. 40 marks are the minimum qualifying marks considered to be eligible for the AP EAPCET Counselling process.
