## Student Reviews For AP EAMCET 2025 May 26th Shift 1 Exam

Students described the 26th May Shift 1 paper as challenging overall, with Mathematics being the most difficult and time-consuming section. Many found the Maths questions lengthy, especially in topics like Calculus and Algebra, making time management a significant hurdle. Physics was rated as moderate to tough, featuring conceptual questions and a few tricky numerical problems, while Chemistry was considered mild and scoring, with most questions being direct and NCERT-based, especially from Organic Chemistry. The general feedback suggested that the paper tested problem-solving skills and accuracy, requiring a focused approach across subjects.

### **Mathematics**

**Difficulty Level:** Moderate to tough
**Feedback:** Students found the Mathematics section to be the most time-consuming and challenging. The paper was lengthy, with complex and multi-step problems, especially in Calculus and Algebra. Managing time effectively was a key challenge.

### **Physics**

**Difficulty Level:** Easy to Moderate
**Feedback:** Physics was considered moderate. Topics like Laws of Motion and Current Electricity were concept-heavy, requiring a clear understanding. Some numericals were tricky, but overall, it was manageable for those with strong basics.

### **Chemistry**

**Difficulty Level:**  Easy to Moderate
**Feedback:** Chemistry was relatively easier compared to the other sections. Questions were mostly direct and based on NCERT, with Organic Chemistry (Aldehydes, Ketones, Carboxylic Acids) being the focus. Some students felt it was a scoring section.

## AP EAMCET 2025 Dress Code Guidelines
