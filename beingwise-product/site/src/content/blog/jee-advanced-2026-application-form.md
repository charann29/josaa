## JEE Advanced 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Before starting the JEE Advanced 2026 registration, it is important to note that only the qualified candidates are allowed to take part in the JEE Advanced examination. Make sure you satisfy all the eligibility criteria before applying.

- Candidate should be among the top 2,50,000 rank holders in the BE/BTech paper of JEE Main 2026 to qualify
- International candidates can register directly for JEE Advanced, without taking part in JEE Main
- Candidates should have been born on or after October 1, 2001
- Age relaxation of 5 years is allowed for candidates belonging to SC, ST, and PwD categories
- Maximum two attempts for JEE Advanced in two consecutive years

## How To Fill JEE Advanced Application Form 2026

To fill out the JEE Advanced Application form, aspirants must complete the processes listed below:

Step 1: Go to the official JEE Advanced 2026 website at <https://jeeadv.ac.in/>.

Step 2: Under the important Announcements, click on the registration portal as shown in the image below.

**Step 3:** Candidates must register using their mobile number and email address.

**Step 4:** Candidates must log in to the JEE Advanced portal after verifying their cell number and email address using the URLs sent to their phones and email addresses, respectively.

**Step 5:** On the portal, aspirants must click the application form button and proceed to fill out the JEE Advanced application form 2026. Candidates must fill out all of the details accurately, ensure that they are accurate, and reread them before submitting.

**Step 6:** Once the information is entered, candidates will be taken to the fee payment section, where they must select their preferred payment method.

**Step 7:** After you pay the JEE Advanced Form Fee, the application form will appear on the screen.

**Step 8:** Candidates should print out the JEE Advanced 2026 application form in PDF format for future reference.

The JEE Advanced 2026 Admit card will be released on Monday, May 11, 2026 (10:00 IST). It should be noted that the JEE Advanced Admit card is only issued for those applicants who fulfil the eligibility criteria and submit the application form before the last date.

## JEE Advanced 2026 Result Declaration

**The JEE Advanced 2026 Result will be declared on June 01, 2026, at 10:00 AM**  (IST) on the official website. ## JEE Advanced 2026 Counselling

Following the announcement of the results, the JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority) will handle the counselling procedure. Candidates who qualify must register, select their favourite IITs and courses, and participate in several rounds of seat distribution. The counselling procedure, which involves choice filling, seat allocation, document verification, and reporting to designated institutes, is an important stage in getting admission.

## JEE Advanced 2026 Rank List

The JEE Advanced 2026 rank list, which includes the Common Rank List (CRL) and category-specific rankings, will be announced alongside the results. The ranking is based on the combined marks received in both Paper 1 and Paper 2, and only applicants who match the minimum qualifying conditions are considered.
