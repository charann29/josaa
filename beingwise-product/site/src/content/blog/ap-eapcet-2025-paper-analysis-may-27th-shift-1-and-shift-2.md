## AP EAMCET 2025 Dress Code Guidelines

Candidates taking the **AP EAMCET 2025** exam must follow a well-defined dress code to ensure fairness and prevent malpractice. Clothing should be simple and free of unnecessary embellishments. Below are the guidelines for attire for AP EAMCET 2025.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Category** | **Guidelines** |
| **Upper Wear** | Plain, light-colored shirts, T-shirts, or kurtas without patterns, logos, or embellishments. Avoid heavy embroidery, frills, or layered clothing. |
| **Sleeves** | Only half-sleeves are permitted. Full sleeves are discouraged to facilitate smooth security checks. |
| **Lower Wear** | Simple trousers, pants, or leggings. Avoid cargo pants, jeans with multiple pockets, or any attire with inner pockets. |
| **Footwear** | Open-toe sandals or chappals are recommended. Closed shoes, boots, or footwear with thick soles are discouraged. |
| **Accessories** | Refrain from wearing jewelry such as earrings, rings, bracelets, chains, or any metallic items. |
| **Electronic Devices** | Smartwatches, digital watches, fitness bands, or any electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited inside the exam hall. |
| **Permitted Items** | Admit card, a valid photo ID, a transparent ballpoint pen, and a transparent water bottle. Diabetic candidates may carry necessary items with prior approval. |
| **Prohibited Items** | Bags, wallets, study materials, printed or written materials, calculators, mobile phones, and any other electronic devices. |

### How to Check AP EAMCET Results 2025?

Given below are the steps to check AP EAMCET results 2025.

- Visit the official website: [cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET](http://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET).
- Click on the “Results” tab.
- Enter your registration number and hall ticket number.
- Download and print your rank card for future reference.

### Details Available on the Rank Card

Given below are the details available on the rank card.

- Candidate’s name, gender, and category.
- Hall ticket and registration numbers.
- Stream (Engineering, Agriculture, or Pharmacy).
- Secured marks and qualifying status.
- Intermediate percentage.
- Normalized score and rank.

## AP EAPCET Counselling

Check out the steps of the AP EAPCET counselling process 2025 given below.

**Registration & Processing Fee Payment:** Candidates must register online and pay the processing fee. The fee is INR 1,200 for OC/BC candidates and INR 600 for SC/ST candidates.

**Document Verification:** Eligible candidates will undergo certificate verification. APSCHE has introduced online certificate verification this year, eliminating the need for physical visits to Help Line Centers for many candidates.

**Web Options Entry:** After verification, candidates can log in to the official portal to select their preferred colleges and courses.

**Seat Allotment:** Based on the web options and merit, seats will be allotted. Candidates can download their allotment letters from the official website.

**Reporting to Allotted Colleges:** Candidates must report to the allotted colleges with the necessary documents for final admission.
