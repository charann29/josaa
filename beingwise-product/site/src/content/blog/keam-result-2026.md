## How is the KEAM Mark Calculated?

The ratio used to calculate the KEAM score is based on the student’s performance in the entrance examination, and the marks received in the 12th standard Board exams are given equal weightage. The rank list for KEAM 2026 will be created by the Commissioner of Entrance Examinations, Kerala Commissioner based on the weightage system of 50:50.

It means that the normalised score in the KEAM entrance exam will be given equal weightage, and also marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics of the Class 12 exam. To ensure efficiency across education boards, both are considered.

### Key Highlights About the KEAM Rank Calculation:

Check out the overview of KEAM rank calculation below:

- 50% weightage is assigned to the normalised KEAM entrance test marks.
- 50% weightage is assigned to Class 12 marks obtained in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.
- Normalisation is done in board marks so as to maintain equity in board marks.
- The effect is that the final ranking is done after considering both the normalised marks.
- Ranking determines counselling and seat allocation.

## KEAM Counselling 2026

The KEAM Counselling 2026 will start post declaration of the KEAM Result and Rank List by the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE), Kerala. The qualified candidates will have to go through the Centralised Allotment Process (CAP) for admission to various engineering, architectural and pharmacy courses in the colleges of Kerala.

While counselling, a candidate is required to register for options, choose colleges and courses of their choice and verify documents. Seat allotment will be made as per KEAM rank, category, reservation criteria and availability of seats. Allotment will be offered to the candidates who will be asked to confirm their admission within the stipulated time period.

**KEAM Counselling Process:**

- KEAM Counselling registration (online)
- Choice Filling and College Preferences Submission
- Option Locking Before Deadline
- Trial Allotment Release
- First Seat Allotment Publication
- For any seat allocated, the fee must be paid.
- Downloading Allotment Memo
- Reporting to the Allotted College, a college of the University of Oxford.
- Document Verification Process
- Final Admission Confirmation
- Participation in Further Rounds (if required)

The Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE), Kerala, releases the KEAM Rank List 2026 following the release of the KEAM Result and the uploading of Class 12 scores of candidates.
