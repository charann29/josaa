The JNTU Hyderabad has released the **TS EAMCET exam dates** on the official website: <https://eapcet.tsche.ac.in/>. The exam authorities planned to conduct the TS EAMCET for Agriculture and Pharmacy from **May 4th and 5th, 2026** and for Engineering, the exam is going to be conducted from **May 9th, 10th, 11th** **2026**.

The TS EAMCET(Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test) has been replaced by the TS EAPCET(Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test). Instead of Medical, Pharmacy is replaced. The TS EAMCET 2026 exam for the Engineering Stream will be conducted from **May 9th, 10th, 11th 2026**.

The TS EAMCET 2026 is an entrance exam for students in Telangana who want to study engineering, agriculture, and pharmacy at the undergraduate level. The JNTUH organises the TS EAMCET exam on behalf of TSCHE. Students need to meet eligibility criteria to complete the application process and to appear for the exam on the scheduled date. The exam covers subjects like Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry for engineering aspirants. For medical and agriculture aspirants, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.

The TS EAMCET 2026 acts as a gateway for all aspiring students to pursue their careers in Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy fields.

## TS EAMCET 2026 Admit Card

The conducting authorities will release the TS EAMCET Admit Card on the official website. Candidates can download the TS EAMCET 2026 Admit Cards through the Official Website: <https://eapcet.tsche.ac.in/>.

To download their Hall Ticket, candidates will need some basic credentials such as the TS EAMCET application, registration number, and Date of birth. The TS EAMCET Admit Card is a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall.

## TS EAMCET 2026 Answer Key Date

The Telangana State Council of Higher Education(TSCHE) is expected to publish the TS EAMCET answer key by the end of June 2026 (tentatively) for engineering, agriculture, and pharmacy streams. Candidates can access the answer key on the official website: <https://eapcet.tsche.ac.in/>. To view the details, candidates need to log in using their Hall ticket number, registration number, and date of birth. The answer key will be available in PDF format for easy access and reference.

## TS EAMCET 2026 Result Date

The Telangana State Council of Higher Education(TSCHE) is expected to release the TS EAMCET/EAPCET results in June 2026 (tentatively).  Candidates can check their results by visiting the official website: <https://eapcet.tsche.ac.in/>. To view the results, candidates must log in using their Hall ticket number, Registration number, and Date of birth.

## TS EAMCET (EAPCET) 2026 Counselling Date

The Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE) authorities are expected to start the TS EAMCET/EAPCET counselling process in July 2026 and end by August 2026. Candidates who have qualified in the TS EAMCET 2026  from Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy streams are eligible to participate in the counselling process. The counselling process includes registration, counselling fee payment, document verification, choosing web options, seat allotment and reporting to the college.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. How is the TS EAMCET 2026 exam conducted?

The TS EAMCET 2026 is conducted Online mode as a CBT(Computer-Based Test).

### 2. Is there a negative marking in EAMCET 2026?

There is no negative marking in TS EAMCET 2026. If the candidates have selected the incorrect answers then no marks are deducted from the scored marks. But don’t guess random answers, try to understand the question to answer.

### 3. What is the duration of the TS EAMCET exam?

The duration of the TS EAMCET exam is 3 hours. At this time, candidates are required to answer all the questions within the respective subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics.
