The KEAM correction window 2026 is open now, and students are allowed to access the same till March 21, 2026. The KEAM online correction portal provides the registered candidates with the KEAM correction facility during the registration time period of the KEAM test, enabling them to make some corrections using the KEAM application correction portal. The KEAM application form correction procedure is provided to enable students to revise certain information according to the instructions; not every field can be corrected.

The correction in the KEAM application is made after a few days of the application date, and the same is likely to be followed in the KEAM application correction date 2026. To avoid problems in the admission process later, students must be keen to note the KEAM correction date and make their KEAM registration correction before the stated KEAM application form correction date.

## KEAM Application Form Correction Process

Refer to the following to check out the complete procedure of the KEAM application correction.

- Go to the official site of the KEAM correction window under the Commissioner of Entrance Examinations.
- Click on the link to the Candidate Portal for the KEAM application correction 2026.
- Enter Application Number and Password to access the KEAM online correction portal.
- Click on the correction link on the dashboard of the KEAM application form correction section.
- Read all the information on the screen before proceeding with the KEAM correction.
- Editing the permitted fields according to the correction in the KEAM application guidelines.
- Re-load necessary documents (when needed) in the stipulated format during the KEAM registration correction process.
- Check all the revised details carefully within the KEAM application correction date period and then submit.
- Send the amended application form before the KEAM application form correction date ends.
- Save the confirmation page and use it later as proof of the KEAM correction 2026 submission

## Details Allowed & Restricted to Edit during KEAM Correction Window

Check out the following list to go through the details that are editable and what are not during the form correction window of the KEAM 2026.

**Editable Details:**

- Address
- Contact details
- Academic details
- Photograph
- Signature
- Class 10 certificate
- Reservation documents
- Course preferences

**Non-Editable Details:**

- Name
- Date of Birth
- Application Number
- Login credentials
- Category (without proof)
- Exam centre preference (generally)

## KEAM Defect Rectification & Memo Details Guidelines 2025

Check out the following guidelines regarding the KEAM form correction and memo details.

- The applicants can identify any discrepancy in their uploaded photo, signature, name, and Class 10 certificate by clicking on the memo details option that is located on the Candidate Profile page.
- In case of any defects, the applicants will be required to submit the appropriate picture, signature, or Class 10 certificate before the required deadline (announced by the authorities) for the [**KEAM 2026**](https://www.cee.kerala.gov.in/keamonline2026/) exam.
- The authorities will not accept documents mailed by post, hand delivery, or email.
- Engineering and Pharmacy Entrance Examinations Admit cards will not be given to the candidates who are unable to correct flaws in their photo, signature or Class 10 certificate within the stipulated period of time.
- The information of documents uploaded into claim nativity and reservation benefits will be forwarded to the Candidate Portal upon official scrutiny.
- In case of some flaws that are detected in nativity or reservation documents, then the candidates will be notified via the Candidate Portal, and they are obliged to rectify them within the required time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **Is there any fee for the KEAM application form correction?**

In most cases, KEAM does not impose a correction fee, though the candidates have to verify through the official notification of the KEAM correction date.

### **How many times can I edit my KEAM application during the correction window?**

Only corrections made during the active KEAM correction window can be made, and there may not be any further corrections made after the final submission of the KEAM application correction.

### **Can I update my contact details during correction?**

Changes to the contact details are based on the allowed fields of editing that were noted in the KEAM application form correction notification.

### ****Will corrections affect my KEAM rank?****

There is no direct rank effect in corrections under the KEAM online correction, as far as wrong academic information can reflect in eligibility or rank preparation.

### **Can I make changes after the admit card is issued?**

As a rule, any changes made after the issue of the admit card are not permissible unless otherwise indicated in the KEAM correction guidelines.

### **Whom should I contact for correction-related issues?**

The Commissioner for Entrance Examinations should be contacted by the applicants using the official portal regarding KEAM registration correction.

### **Is the correction facility available for both Engineering and Pharmacy candidates?**

Yes, the correction facility applies to registered candidates of both streams in case of the notification issued for the KEAM application correction.

### **How will I know if there is a defect in my application?**

Any flaws are posted on the Candidate Portal, which is located under the memo or notification details during the correction in the KEAM application process.

### **Can I change my course preference during correction?**

Alteration of course preference can only be made during the KEAM application correction date period.

### **Does KEAM follow the same correction rules as national exams?**

No, KEAM has its own guidelines for state-level correction under the KEAM correction date 2026, which might not be the same as national examinations.
