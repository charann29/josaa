## AP EAMCET 2025 Mathematics Paper Analysis

Students found the Mathematics section to be moderate to difficult, with many describing it as lengthy and time-consuming. Algebra and Coordinate Geometry were heavily featured, requiring extra effort and time from them. Topics like quadratic equations, complex numbers, straight lines, circles, and parabolas appeared frequently. Students also found that Calculus, especially derivatives and their applications, also made an appearance. Students noted several direct formula-based questions in Probability and Statistics, while Vectors, 3D Geometry, and Trigonometry included standard concepts but still demanded careful attention. Overall, the section tested time management and was considered more challenging than other subjects.

## Student Reviews For AP EAMCET 2025 May 22nd Shift 1 Exam

Most students from different states found AP EAMCET 2025 Shift 1, which was held on May 22nd, to be moderate compared to the previous shift. Here is a summary of what students perceived about the exam.

### **Mathematics**

**Difficulty:** Moderate to DIfficult

**Topics Covered:** Calculus, Definite Integrals, Trigonometry, Matrices, Circles.

**Student Feedback:** The Mathematics section was the most challenging, described as lengthy and time-consuming. Questions from Calculus and Trigonometry were particularly tricky. Many students reported spending excessive time on certain questions, leading to difficulty in attempting all questions within the allotted time.

### **Physics**

**Difficulty:** Moderate

Topics Covered: Thermodynamics, Work, Power & Energy, Gravitation, Laws of Motion, Current Electricity.

**Student Feedback:** Students found the Physics section moderately challenging, with a mix of conceptual and numerical questions. Some students reported that the time constraints made it difficult to attempt all questions.

### **Chemistry**

**Difficulty:** Easy

**Topics Covered:** Organic Chemistry (mainly reactions), Chemical Bonding, Atomic Structure, Solutions, States of Matter.

**Student Feedback:** Chemistry was considered the highest-scoring section, with many students finding it straightforward and formula-based, allowing them to save time. The clarity and directness of the questions helped students manage their time effectively.

| --- | --- |

## AP EAPCET Counselling

Check out the steps of AP EAPCET counselling process 2025 given below.

**Registration & Processing Fee Payment:** Candidates must register online and pay the processing fee. The fee is INR 1,200 for OC/BC candidates and INR 600 for SC/ST candidates.

**Document Verification:** Eligible candidates will undergo certificate verification. APSCHE has introduced online certificate verification this year, eliminating the need for physical visits to Help Line Centers for many candidates.

**Web Options Entry:** After verification, candidates can log in to the official portal to select their preferred colleges and courses.

**Seat Allotment:** Based on the web options and merit, seats will be allotted. Candidates can download their allotment letters from the official website.

**Reporting to Allotted Colleges:** Candidates must report to the allotted colleges with the necessary documents for final admission.
