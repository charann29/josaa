Students who successfully registered are now advised to keep a focus on the KEAM admit card date to download the same at the earliest. ## KEAM Registration & Correction Window

As the KEAM registrations are already closed, students will now be provided with an opportunity to make changes in their submitted [**KEAM application forms**](/blog/keam/keam-registration-2026-guide/)**.** Aspirants will be allowed to make changes in certain columns only. Those who wish to make changes in their KEAM registration form can follow the steps listed below once the authorities activate the correction window.

- Go to the KEAM official portal.
- Select the candidate login button.
- Type in your application number and password.
- Open the option of Application Correction.
- Change the necessary information.
- Add corrected documents, where necessary.
- Check all the amendments and make a submission.
- Submit the corrected form.
- Click on save or download the confirmation page to use later.
