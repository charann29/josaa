## Details of The Entrance Examinations and Admission Process

Engineering admissions in Chennai are highly competitive and governed by a mix of national, state, and institute-specific entrance examinations. Aspiring engineers aiming to join prestigious institutions such as IIT Madras, Anna University, SRM Institute, and other top colleges typically prepare for exams like the JEE Main and Advanced at the national level. At the same time, state-level admissions are primarily influenced by the Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) process, which considers Class 12 marks. Many private universities and institutes conduct their own specialized entrance tests, such as SRMJEEE, SAEEE, and NAT, to select candidates based on the criteria.
