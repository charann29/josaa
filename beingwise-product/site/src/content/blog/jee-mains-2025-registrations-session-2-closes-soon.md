### JEE Mains 2025 Registrations Session 2: Registration Procedure for Fresh Candidates

- Visit the official website(jeemain.nta.nic.in)
- Access the Registration Link.
- Click on New Registration
- The Registration Form Tab with Personal details will open Give all details required such as the candidate’s name, father’s name, mother’s name, date of birth, and gender.
- Confirm the personal details and enter your present address, electronic communication address, and permanent address.

  - **Personal details:** Name, category, nationality, place of residence and annual family income

  - **Exam and Center Details:** City Preferences and Guardian Qualification
  - **Qualification Details:** Candidates’ 10th and 12th marks details.
  - **Emergency Contact Details:** Details of the person who should be contacted in case of emergency.
  - **Document Upload:**
    - Aadhar
    - Passport size photograph (Format: JPG/JPEG, File Size:10kb-300kb, Dimensions: 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm)
    - Signature (Format: JPG/JPEG, File Size:10kb-50kb, Dimensions: 3.5 cm x 1.5 cm)

## JEE Mains 2025 Registrations: What’s New?

The Registration procedure for JEE Main 2025 has been changed compared to previous years. Here are the new changes in the JEE Main 2025 registration Process.

- **Aadhar Verification Process:** The NTA has addressed the issues arising from the name Mismatches of 10th certificates and Aadhar card names. If an error occurs during [Aadhar authentication](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/jee-main-2025-updates-nta-rules-and-guidelines-for-aadhar-authentication/) candidates can close the pop-up message and enter the same name as in the Aadhar card.
- **Elimination of Age Criteria:** The Age Criteria have been removed for the JEE Main exam any candidate who meets the qualification can write the examination.
- **Exam centers reduced:** The number of exam centers has been reduced from 300 to 284 for domestic candidates.
- **DigiLocker:** The option to use Digilocker or ABC ID has been removed
- **Photo and Signature Guidelines:** JEE Main photo and signature guidelines have been revised. The size of the uploaded image and PDF has been changed

## JEE Main Application Form Rejection Reasons

Many Applications were rejected by NTA due to errors in application form due to some common mistakes here are some of the common mistakes due to which an [Application can get rejected](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/jee-application-rejection/)

- **Submitting Application Form Twice:** A Candidate can only complete the application form once. If a candidate fills out the application form twice. their application form will be rejected, and their application number will be treated as UFM(Unfair Means).
- **Errors in Non-editable Fields:** Fields such as Email, Phone number, and Identification Number cannot be edited if a candidate makes errors in these fields, their application can be rejected.
- **Not Uploading Documents According to Specifications:** Documents such as the photograph and signature of the candidate need to be uploaded according to the specifications given by the authority failing to do so may lead to application rejection.
- **Payment of Application Fee:** If a candidate fails to pay the application fee, then their application can be rejected.
- **Wrong** **Category Number:** For Reserved Category candidates if they give an incorrect category number, their application can be rejected.
- **Negative Marking for Section B:** Negative marking for Section B has been introduced this year if a candidate makes any error, then there will be -1 negative marking.
