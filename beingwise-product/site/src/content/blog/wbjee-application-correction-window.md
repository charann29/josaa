The application correction window for WBJEE opened on **25th February 2025 and the last date for the correction of particulars for the application is 27th February 2025 .** The Candidates can apply for the correction of particulars on the official WBJEE website(<https://wbjeeb.nic.in/wbjee/>).

Before registering for WBJEE, the candidates are advised to check the official eligibility criteria.

Unregistered candidates will not be considered eligible for the application correction and to write the WBJEE Exam, which will be held on April 27, 2025. After the Correction Window ends, the WBJEE Board will release the WBJEE 2025 admit card next between April 17 to 27.

## How To Avoid Application Rejection

A WBJEE Application can get rejected for the following reasons

- **Incorrect Information:** If the candidate’s information contains a mistake in their name or father’s name or other fields their application can get rejected
- **Incomplete Application Form:** If the candidate’s application form is incomplete or if he left any mandatory fields blank their application can be rejected.
- **Payment of application fee:** If the candidate filled all the details but failed to pay the application fee their Application can get rejected.
- **Late Submission:** If a candidate submits the application form after the registration date, their application can be rejected.
- **Photo and Signature Guidelines:** If a candidate does not fulfill the photo and signature guidelines, their application can be rejected.

## WBJEE Exam Overview

The **[West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination (WBJEE)](https://collegedost.in/blog/category/wbjee/)** is a state-level entrance exam conducted by the **West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB)** for admission to **B.E./B.Tech, B.Pharm, and Architecture** courses in various government and private engineering colleges across **West Bengal**. The exam is typically held once a year in **offline (pen-and-paper) mode** and consists of two papers: **Paper 1 (Mathematics)** and **Paper 2 (Physics & Chemistry combined)**. The total marks for the exam are **200**, with a **negative marking system** for incorrect answers. Eligibility requires candidates to have completed **10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics**, with a minimum percentage specified for different categories.

## Points to Remember While Applying for WBJEE

Here are some points for the candidates to remember to apply for WBJEE

- **Eligibility Criteria:**
  - Candidates must have passed 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  - Candidates must be Indian citizens (OCI candidates are eligible for only specific institutions).
  - The minimum age is 17 years as of December 31 of the exam year; no upper age limit (except for Marine Engineering, which requires a maximum age of 25 years)**.**
- **Application Process:**
  - Ensure all personal details, academic information, and category details are entered correctly.
  - Upload scanned copies of passport-size photographs and signatures in the prescribed format.
  - Double-check all information before final submission, as corrections are limited.
- **Payment Mode:** The Payment Mode for the WBJEE Application fee is through Net Banking/Credit Card/Debit Card
