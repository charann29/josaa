The Average Package of Jadavpur University for 2025 stands at INR 12 LPA. The Highest Package of Jadavpur University this cycle reached INR 85 LPA on the international side and INR 57 LPA on the domestic side, with Google offering the top domestic package to three B.Tech CSE students. This article covers the full Jadavpur University Placement record for B.Tech students. It includes 2025 data first, followed by earlier batch figures, branch-wise analysis, CSE placement details, top recruiters, and salary trends.

## Jadavpur University Placement Process

The Training and Placement Office at Jadavpur University manages the campus recruitment process for B.Tech students. The process follows a structured sequence each year.

1. Students register with the placement cell at the start of their final year.
2. Companies conduct Pre-Placement Talks to outline roles, locations, and packages.
3. Aptitude tests or technical assessments are held as the first screening round.
4. Shortlisted students appear for technical interviews covering core subjects and coding.
5. HR rounds assess communication and professional fit.
6. Selected students receive offer letters.

Jadavpur University Internship drives run through the same office, with companies like PwC, L&T, and ITC participating regularly. Several students convert internships into Pre-Placement Offers, which count toward the final placement figures.

## Conclusion

The Jadavpur University Placement record for the 2025 B.Tech batch is the strongest on record. The Jadavpur Placement rate stands at 90% with 1,230 students placed, a median of INR 14 LPA, and 100+ companies visiting. The Jadavpur University Average Package has grown from INR 8.28 LPA in 2020 to INR 12 LPA in 2025.

The Jadavpur University Packages vary significantly by branch. CSE and IT lead at INR 29.05 LPA and INR 17.99 LPA respectively. Instrumentation, ETCE, and Production Engineering sit in the INR 11 to 16 LPA range. Core branches show lower averages but Mechanical, Instrumentation, and Construction Engineering all achieved 100% placement in 2024. For students appearing in WBJEE and targeting Jadavpur University, the data confirms CSE and IT as the strongest branches for salary outcomes. Non-CSE branches like Electrical, Production, and Metallurgy also deliver competitive figures backed by core engineering and manufacturing recruiters. Students who prepare well in coding and technical subjects have a realistic path to strong Jadavpur Placement outcomes across most branches.
