## How to Download the KEAM Answer Key 2025?

Authorities have released the KEAM Answer Key on the official website, i.e. [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in). Follow the steps mentioned below after the KEAM login to download the answer key for your respective shift:

**Step 1:** Visit the official website [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in).

**Step 2:** Click on the link showing ‘KEAM 2025 Answer Key’

**Step 3:** Provide your credentials if required.

**Step 4:** You’ll be shown the KEAM 2025 answer key on the screen.

**Step 5:** Download and check the answer key PDF.

## KEAM 2025 answer key challenge window

KEAM 2025 Answer Key: The Commissioner for Entrance Examination will stop accepting the key challenges from May 3rd. The shift-wise answer keys are already made available for candidates on the official website cee.kerala.gov.in. Candidates who have objections to the answer key can challenge it by paying Rs. 200 for each question. Candidates can easily challenge the answer key by navigating to the official website and selecting the ‘Answer key challenge’ option. Candidates are required to submit the supporting documents before submitting their objections. Candidates will be provided a refund for the questions that are valid and verified. Challenges submitted after the deadline will not be accepted and are not considered for review.

### KEAM 2025 answer key challenge fee

Candidates are required to pay a fee of**Rs. 200 per question**to challenge the questions in the answer key. The fee will be refunded to the candidate’s account in the same account that they provided during the application process for questions that are valid and verified.

### How to Challenge KEAM 2025 Answer Key

Candidates can challenge the KEAM answer key and based on the responses received, authorities will release the final answer key. Know how to challenge the KEAM answer key:

**Step 1:** Visit the official website.

**Step 2:** Click on the ‘KEAM answer key’ link.

**Step 3:** Login using your credentials.

**Step 4:**You’ll be shown the KEAM answer key, and select the question that you want to challenge.

**Step 5:** Support your question with some supporting documents.

**Step 6:** Pay Rs 200 per question and submit.

## How to Calculate KEAM 2025 Score Using the Answer Key?

Candidates can obtain the probable score using the KEAM answer key by just counting the number of correct and incorrect responses. Use the following formula for calculating your score using the KEAM answer key:

**KEAM Exam Score =**4 x number of correct responses – 1 x number of incorrect responses.

**Follow the steps mentioned below:**

- Download the Answer Key.
- Check the number of correct and incorrect responses.
- Once you’re done, use the above formula to calculate your KEAM Exam Score.

## KEAM Final Answer Key

The KEAM 2025 final answer key will be released after the authorities thoroughly review all the objections raised against the provisional key. This ensures accuracy and fairness in the evaluation process. Once the final key is published, it becomes the basis for calculating each candidate’s score and determining their rank. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****How to download the KEAM provisional answer key 2025?****

You can visit the official website for downloading the KEAM answer key 2025.

### **2. ********What is the difference between KEAM answer key and response sheet?**********

KEAM answer key consists of only the correct answers for the questions asked in the exam while the KEAM response sheet consists of the correct answers along with the candidates response for each question.

### **3. ********Can I challenge the KEAM provisional answer key?**********

Yes, candidates can challenge KEAM provisional key by paying a fee of Rs. 200 per question.

### 4. What is the difference between the Final Answer Key and the Provisional Answer Key in KEAM?

Authorities release the final answer key after clearing all the valid objections raised by the candidates in the provisional answer key.

### **5. ********Can I challenge the KEAM final answer key?**********

No, the KEAM final answer key is not subject to challenge.
