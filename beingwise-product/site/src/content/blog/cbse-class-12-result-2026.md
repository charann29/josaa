## How to Check Class 12 CBSE Result 2026?

Students who have appeared for the CBSE Class 12 Board Exams 2026 can check the results through various mediums apart from the official website. The Central Board of Secondary Education offers many ways for students to check the CBSE Class 12 Results 2026, so that the students can access the results without any difficulties, even during peak hours of website traffic.

Students can check the CBSE Class 12 Results 2026 through any of the mediums by keeping the **roll number, school number, and admit card ID** handy. The step-by-step procedure for checking the CBSE Results in various ways is as follows:

## CBSE Board Result 2026 Class 12 via Official Website

The most convenient way for the students to check the CBSE Class 12 Results 2026 is through the official CBSE result website. The step-by-step procedure for checking the CBSE Results 2026 for Class 12 online is as follows:

**Step 1:** Visit the official website of the CBSE board – cbse.gov.in.

**Step 2:** From the homepage, click on the CBSE Class 12 board Result 2025 link.

**Step 3:** Enter your login details, such as your roll number, school number, and admit card ID.

**Step 4:** After providing the information, click the Submit button.

**Step 5:** Your CBSE Class 12 Result 2026 will be displayed on the screen.

**Step 6:** Download the scorecard and print it for future reference.

## Class 12 Result 2026 CBSE Board through SMS

In case you are not able to cbse.gov.in Result 2026 official website, they can access their scores via the offline SMS facility. Candidates preparing for the forthcoming exam will be able to receive their CBSE board results by SMS, utilising the following steps:

**Step 1:** Open the message or the SMS program on your mobile device.

**Step 2:** Then, enter the following message while inputting your credentials:

**CBSE 12 (roll number) (school number)(center number)**

**Step 3:** Now send this SMS to 7738299899.

**Step 4:** Following this, you will receive your subject-wise CBSE 12th result 2026 on the same number that you sent the message from.

## Digilocker CBSE Board Result 2026 Class 12

The CBSE Board has provided students with the option of receiving their CBSE Class 12 results immediately from their Digilocker accounts. Students can receive CBSE marksheets from the DigiLocker mobile app by following the procedures mentioned here.

Step 1: Get the DigiLocker mobile app from the Play Store or the App Store.

Step 2: Once installed, select the “Access DigiLocker option”.

Step 3: Enter a genuine mobile number registered with the CBSE board. The user will receive an OTP via mobile. Enter the security pin that appears on the screen.

Step 4: After entering the PIN, go to the “Education” page of the Digilocker app. Users must submit extra information, including their registered name and CBSE Board roll number.

Step 5: Finally, students will be able to access and download the CBSE Result 2026 Class 12 Marksheet in PDF format.
