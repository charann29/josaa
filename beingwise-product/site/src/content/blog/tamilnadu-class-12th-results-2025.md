The **Tamil Nadu Class 12th results** are crucial for pursuing higher education in various undergraduate courses. It’s important for students to stay updated with the official notifications from the respective institutions to ensure they meet the eligibility criteria and deadlines for admission. ## Tamil Nadu Class 12th  Score Card

The **Tamil Nadu Class 12th Score Card 2025** is the official marksheet issued by the Directorate of Government Examinations (DGE), Tamil Nadu. It includes essential details such as the student’s name, registration number, subject-wise marks, total score, result status (pass/fail), and division. Students can view and download their provisional scorecard online via the official websites—**tnresults.nic.in** and **dge.tn.gov.in**—or access it through the DigiLocker app. The respective schools will distribute the original scorecards later, and they are required for higher education admissions and verification processes.

## Tamil Nadu Class 12th Exam Pass Criteria

To pass the **Tamil Nadu Class 12th Board Examination**, students must secure a **minimum of 35 marks out of 100 in each subject**. For subjects that include both theory and practical components, students are required to score at least **15 marks in theory** and have a **combined total of 35 marks,** including the practical. It is important to note that there is **no minimum mark required in the practical exam alone**, as long as the overall total for the subject meets the passing requirement. Meeting these criteria is essential for students to be declared as “Pass” and qualify for higher education opportunities.

## Tamil Nadu 12th Result 2025: Re-evaluation

If students are unsatisfied with their TN HSE (+2) results, they can apply for re-evaluation. First, they must request a scanned copy of their answer script (₹275 per subject). After reviewing it, they can request re-evaluation by paying ₹505 per subject. Applications are accepted through the student’s school within the deadline set by the DGE.

### Steps to Apply for Re-evaluation

Below are the steps to apply for re-evaluation

- Visit the official Directorate of Government Examinations (DGE) Tamil Nadu website at [dge.tn.gov.in](https://www.dge.tn.gov.in/) and download the re-evaluation application form
- Fill out the application form with details, including your roll number and the subject(s) you wish to have re-evaluated.
- Submit the completed form to your college along with the required application fee
- The application fee for re-evaluation is ₹505 per subject.
- After submission, the re-evaluation process will be conducted, and the revised marks (if any) will be updated on your mark sheet

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **When will the TNEA 2025 Class 12th results be released?**

### 2. **Where can I check my Tamil Nadu Class 12th results for TNEA?**

### 3. **Can I apply for revaluation or retotalling of TN 12th Result 2025?**

Yes, students who are not satisfied with their marks can apply for revaluation or retotalling through the official DGE Tamil Nadu website within the specified dates after the result declaration.

### 4. **What details will be mentioned in the TN 12th Result 2025 marksheet?**

The marksheet will include the student’s name, registration number, date of birth, subject-wise marks, total marks, grade, result status (pass/fail), and qualifying division.

### 5. **Will the TN 12th Result 2025 be available in a marksheet format?**

Yes, students will be able to download a provisional marksheet online after the results are announced. The official hard copy will be distributed by the respective schools later.

### 6. **What should I do after checking my Class 12th result for TNEA?**

After checking your result, register for TNEA, upload your documents, and wait for the rank list to be published based on your cutoff.

### 7. What are the minimum passing marks for Tamil Nadu class 12 board exam?

To pass the Tamil Nadu Class 12 Board Exam 2025, students must score a minimum of 35 marks out of 100 in each subject. For subjects with practicals, at least 15 out of 70 must be scored in the theory exam, while appearing for the practical is mandatory. For subjects without practicals, students need at least 25 out of 90 in the theory paper to qualify.

### 8. How much is the application fee for TN HSE class 12 re-evaluation?

The application fee for re-evaluating each subject in the Tamil Nadu HSE (Class 12) Board Exam 2025 is **₹505**, which includes ₹500 for revaluation and a ₹5 service charge.

### 9. What should students do if they didn’t pass the regular Tamil Nadu 12th exams?

Students who didn’t pass the regular Tamil Nadu 12th exams can apply for the Tamil Nadu 12th Supplementary Exams 2025 through their respective schools in order to improve their scores in specific subjects.
