## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is NIAT in Chennai, and how is it different from a regular engineering college?

NIAT in Chennai is an upskilling program under NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies. Unlike a regular engineering college, NIAT is not an academic institution; it operates as a training centre within partner universities like AMET University and B.S. Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology. The degree is awarded by the university, while NIAT focuses on industry-ready skills.

### 2. Does NIAT in Chennai award a degree or diploma?

No, NIAT does not award degrees or diplomas. The B.Tech degree is provided by the collaborating universities, while NIAT offers an Industry Ready Certificate (IRC) to validate practical skills.

### 3. Is NIAT Training Centre in Chennai AICTE-approved or UGC-recognised?

NIAT itself is an upskilling program and does not hold AICTE or UGC approvals. However, its partner universities—AMET University and BS Abdur Rahman University- are recognised and accredited, ensuring the validity of the B.Tech degree.

### 4. How does admission work at NIAT in Chennai?

Students must take the NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test). NxtWave then shares student details with partner universities, which independently manage admissions based on their own criteria. NIAT does not guarantee admission.

### 5. Who teaches at NIAT in Chennai?

NIAT uses its own trainers, mentors, and success coaches with industry experience. They are different from university faculty and are trained regularly to stay updated with industry trends.

### 6. What certification do students receive from NIAT Chennai?

Students receive an Industry Ready Certificate (IRC) from NxtWave upon completion, which validates their practical and project-based skills.

### 7. Does NIAT in Chennai have its own campus?

No, NIAT does not have its own campus. Training is conducted within the infrastructure provided by partner universities.

### 8. When does NIAT Chennai start placement preparation?

Placement preparation begins from the first year and includes resume building, aptitude training, mock interviews, career counselling, and salary negotiation support.

### Q9. What is the NIAT College Chennai fees structure?

NIAT fees structure consists of an upskilling fee and a BTech CSE course fee from the partner university. The total NIAT fee is between INR 8-18 Lakhs for 4 years. However, for NIAT entrance exam (NAT qualifying test), students need to pay INR 1200 for NIAT registration.

### Q10. What is the NIAT College Chennai address?

NIAT India in Chennai has collaborated with two partner Universities, one of which is BS Abdur Rahman University. Crescent University Chennai address is Vandalur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu (near the Tambaram area). The second one is AMET University (Academy of Maritime Education & Training), which is located in Kanathur, East Coast Road (ECR), Chennai, Tamil Nadu.
