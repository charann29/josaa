## Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham NIRF Ranking Breakdown For B.Tech

Under Amrita University NIRF Ranking in the B.Tech category, it secured the 23rd position in 2025. With an overall score of 62.46  out of 100, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham NIRF Rankings are evaluated on five key parameter groups as follows:

- Teaching, Learning & Resources (TLR) – This parameter evaluates institutes depending on their strong faculty, student ratio, and infrastructure. It adds a total weightage of 30%.
- Research & Professional Practice (RPC) – With a weightage of 30%, it evaluates publications, patents, and projects.
- Graduation Outcomes (GO) – This factor weighs 20%, evaluating the placements, higher studies, and success.
- Outreach & Inclusivity (OI) – This parameter weighs 10% and evaluates diversity, facilities for the disadvantaged of the institute.
- Peer & Employer Perception (Perception): This parameter has 10% and evaluates reputation among stakeholders.

These metrics underscore the Amrita University Rankings for B.Tech and highlight teaching, research, and positioning Amrita as a top private B.Tech hub. Check the following table for Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham NIRF Rankings in the B.Tech and overall category:

## Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Rankings For B.Tech – Admission Insights & Placement Outcomes
