## Steps to Download JEE Advanced Rank Card for 2025

### Step 1: Access the Official Website

Access the official website jeeadv.ac.in. which is managed by the concerned IIT which is conducting the JEE Advanced for 2025.

### Step 2: Access Rank Card Link

Look for the JEE Advanced exam results link and click on the link which Navigates you to the login page.

### Step 3: Log in

Once you are on the official website, look for the login section. To access your JEE Advanced 2025 rank card, you will need to log in with your JEE Advanced credentials. These credentials are important for security reasons,

This includes:

- **JEE Advanced 2025 Registration Number:** This is the unique number you received after completing your JEE Advanced application process.
- **Date of Birth:** You will need to enter your exact date of birth in the format specified on the website.

### Step 4: Submit

Click on ‘Submit’ once you have filled in all the particulars and you will be redirected to the results page.

### Step 5: Verify Details

Verify the following details mentioned in the scorecard and report if there is any incorrect information.

Check for these details in your Rank card:

- **Candidate Name:** Ensure that your name is spelled correctly.
- **JEE Advanced 2025 Roll Number:** Cross-check your roll number.
- **Category:** The category mentioned (General, OBC, SC, ST, etc.) should be correct.
- **Overall Rank:** Your overall rank will indicate your position in the merit list and determine your eligibility for admission into various IITs.
- **Category Rank:** This is significant for those who belong to reserved categories and may have specific cut-offs for admission.

### Step 6: Download the Rank Card

After verifying the details download the rank card and take a print of the same for future reference.

## How They Prepare JEE Advanced 2025 Results?

Authorities follow the below-mentioned process before declaring the results:

- Based on the score JEE Advanced 2025 ranks are calculated.
- Individual marks of each subject i.e. Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry in paper 1 and paper 2 are calculated and added to obtain the total marks for the subject.
- Aggregate marks are calculated based on the secured by the candidate in each subject.
- Based on the score JEE Advanced 2025 ranks are calculated.
