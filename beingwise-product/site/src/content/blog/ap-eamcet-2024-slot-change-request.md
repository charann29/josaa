The Candidates who are having a clash of the AP EAMCET 2024 examination date with the dates of other competitive examinations, can **submit a representation** along with the **documentary evidence** i.e. Hall Ticket of the other competitive examination for a **change of slot to** [**helpdeskapeapcet@apsche.org**](mailto:helpdeskapeapcet@apsche.org) on or before **12.05.2024**.

The APSCHE (Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education) has released an important exam update for the AP EAMCET 2024. The examination authorities will permit to change the slot of the AP EAMCET examination if the exam clashes with other competitive examinations. The Candidates should write an email to the [helpdeskapeapcet@apsche.org](mailto:helpdeskapeapcet@apsche.org) (AP EAMCET Help Desk). The emails sent by the candidates are considered only up to 12.05.2024. The emails sent after 12th May are not considered.

The AP EAMCET (Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical Common Entrance Test) is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, (JNTU), Kakinada. The AP EAMCET 2024 exam for the **Engineering stream** will be conducted from **18.05.2024 to 23.05.2024** and the **Agriculture & Pharmacy Stream** examination will be held from **16.05.2024 to 17.05.2024.**

The [AP EAMCET 2024 Hall Ticket](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/ap-eamcet-hall-ticket/) is released by the examination authorities on 07.05.2024. The candidates can download their AP EAMCET Hall Ticket as soon as possible and preserve it.
