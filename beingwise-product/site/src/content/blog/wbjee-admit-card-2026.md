## How to Download Admit Card For WBJEE 2026?

West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination Board will release WBJEE Admit Card for all the successfully registered candidates. If you’re wondering **how to download admit card of WBJEE**, follow these simple steps:

1. Visit the official website at wbjeeb.nic.in.
2. Click on the ‘**WBJEE admit card download’** link
3. Enter your application number and date of birth
4. Complete the **WBJEE login admit card** process
5. Your admit card will appear on the screen
6. Download and take a clear printout

## Common Issues Candidates Face with WBJEE Admit Card

Candidates may come across certain issues either after downloading the WBJEE admit card or during the download process itself. Addressing these concerns early helps avoid unnecessary stress on the exam day.

### Admit Card for WBJEE Discrepancy

Any error on the admit card should not be overlooked. In case of a WBJEE admit card discrepancy, candidates should immediately contact the exam authorities to get it corrected. Common issues with WBJEE Admit Card Discrepancy include:

- Incorrect name or date of birth mentioned on the admit card
- Wrong exam centre details were allotted to the candidate
- Blurred or unclear photograph or signature

### Problems While Downloading WBJEE Admit Card

Some candidates may face technical difficulties while completing [WBJEE](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-2026/) admit card download process. These issues are usually temporary and can be resolved with a few simple steps. Candidates can try the following:

- Use a different browser if the page is not loading properly
- Clear cache and cookies before attempting to log in again
- Check the internet connection to ensure it is stable
- Enter the correct login credentials, including application number and date of birth

## Documents Required with Admit Card For WBJEE

On the day of the examination, candidates must carry all the required documents along with the WBJEE admit card for verification at the exam centre. The required documents include:

- A printed copy of the WBJEE admit card
- A valid photo ID proof, such as Aadhaar card, PAN card, voter ID, school ID, or passport
- A recent passport-size photograph (preferably the same as uploaded in the application form)

## WBJEE 2026 Exam Day Guidelines

Candidates should follow a few important instructions to ensure a smooth experience on the exam day. Being prepared in advance can help avoid unnecessary stress at the exam centre. During the exam, you must keep the points below in mind:

- Plan to reach WBJEE exam centres at least one hour before the scheduled start time
- Check the exact location of your exam centre in advance to avoid last-minute confusion
- Carry a printed copy of the WBJEE admit card along with a valid original photo ID proof
- Appear only at the exam centre mentioned on your admit card, as entry to any other centre will not be permitted
- Do not carry electronic items such as mobile phones, calculators, or any other gadgets to the exam hall
