### How Tie Breaking policy implies in BITSAT Merit List?

In the case of a tie between two or more candidates having the same BITSAT score, there will be a tie breaking procedure to make a merit list. The tie is broken considering the following factors in descending order:

- Higher Mathematics/Biology Marks
- Higher Mathematics/Biology marks will be considered first in such cases.
- Higher Physics Marks
- If the tie continues, then Physics marks will be considered.
- Higher Chemistry Marks
- After comparing the Physics marks of candidates, in case of identical scores, chemistry marks will be considered.
- Higher Class 12 PCM/PCB Marks
- When ties remain in all subjects mentioned above, marks secured in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology will be considered.

## What happens after BITSAT Result 2026?

The counselling process begins after the declaration of the BITSAT result by the authority. Once the session one result is declared, the preference form becomes active for eligible candidates.

In order to take part in the counselling process for BITSAT 2026, candidates are supposed to furnish their marks obtained in the qualifying examination of Class 12. To take part in the counselling process, qualified students need to fill their choices in descending order among the three campuses of BITS – Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad

Allotment of seats is done on the basis of several iterations depending on the BITSAT score obtained by the candidate, preference of choice and availability of seats. Candidates who receive an offer for a seat in any programme must pay the required admission fees. Besides, the candidates are also required to complete document verification process. Candidates who complete this procedure have to confirm admission.

**Direct Admission for Board Toppers:** BITS Pilani also provides the option of direct admission for those students who have been declared the first topper in their respective State or Central boards.
