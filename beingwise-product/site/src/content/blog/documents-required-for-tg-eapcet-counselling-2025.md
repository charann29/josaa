## TG EAPCET Counseling Process 2025

TS EAMCET 2025 counseling is conducted in 6 steps. Qualified candidates of TG EAPCET entrance exam can register for the counseling and seat allocation process.

1. **Payment of Counseling Fee and Registration:** The process begins with payment of the counseling fee**.** The fees can be paid through credit/debit card or internet banking of any nationalised bank. The **counseling registration fees for Unreserved candidates are INR 1200**, whereas for **reserved candidates,** **it is INR 600**. Next, candidates need to visit the official website [**TG EAPCET**](https://eapcet.tgche.ac.in/) to complete their registration process.

2. **Verification of Documents:** For the next step, candidates need to visit the TS EAMCET 2025 help centres to verify their documents. After the verification process, an acknowledgement letter will be issued.

3. **Choice Filling:** Candidates must log in to their candidate portal using their registration number and password to exercise web options as the next step of the TG EAPCET Counseling 2025 process. They need to fill in their choices of colleges in order of preference.

4. **Seat Allotment**: Next, the TSCHE will allot seats to candidates within the specified dates based on the scores in TG EAPCET 2025 entrance exam.Candidates need to download their provisional seat allotment letter from the official portal. In case they do not accept the seat, then they can proceed with the next round of seat allotment process.

5. **Payment of Tuition Fees:** After you download the seat allotment letter from the official website, you need to pay the tuition fees of the selected college.

6. **Reporting to the Allotted Institute:** As the final step of the counseling process, you need to report to the institute within the specified date. Candidates need to carry the required documents while reporting to the institutes. Candidates are advised to carry all the necessary documents to the Institute. The admission will be cancelled if the student fails to report to the Institute within the stipulated date and time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **********************If I want to cancel the admission after paying the tuition fee, will I get a full refund?**********************

If you do not want to take admission to the allotted Institute or College after giving the full tuition fee and want to cancel the admission, you will get a full refund.

### 2. ********Is there a reservation for certain categories in TS EAMCET 2025 Counseling?********

TG EAPCET 2025 provides reserved seats for defence personnel, women, sports category, etc. You can check the table below to know the reservation percentage of TG EAMCET. The SC and ST category has 6% and 15% relaxation criteria, respectively. However, the Female category has 33% reservation criteria making it the highest one whereas NCC has 1% criteria.

### 3. **************Can I select more than one college during TG EAPCET counselling process 2025?**************

Yes, you can select more than one college during TS EAMCET 2025 counseling process. Moreover, candidates are advised to select as many as they can based on their preference to increase their chance of admission.

### 4. **********What happens if I miss or forget to register for the TG EAPCET 2025 counseling?**********

If you miss the registration for the TS EAMCET counseling process 2025, then you will not be considered for admission to your preferred college. However, you can check for special rounds or spot counselling if conducted by the authorities.

### 5. ************If I want to cancel the admission after the 1st phase of the TG EAPCET counseling process 2025, will I get a full refund?************

If you want to cancel the admission after the 1st phase of the TG EAPCET counseling process 2025, then you will get the full refund.

### 6. ********During TG EAPCET counselling 2025, can any candidates change their preference after freezing the options?********

No, candidates can not make any changes to their preferences after freezing the options. However, candidates are advised to carefully review their preferences before the final submission.

### 7. ****Can I apply for multiple courses through TG EAPCET?****

All the eligible candidates are allowed to apply for multiple courses through the TS EAMCET exam. Though the application form needs to be filled out separately for each course, and you need to pay the registration fees for each subject.

### 8. ******How are the TS EAMCET 2025 counselling rounds conducted?******

TG EAPCET 2025 counseling rounds are conducted in 6 steps. The first two rounds are paying the counseling fees and registering for it. Next you have to verify all your documents and lock your college preferences. The authorities will release the seat allotment result. Download the provisional seat allotment letter and report to the Institute on the stipulated day and time.

### 9. **********What will happen if any candidate fails to report to the allotted College/Institute within the stipulated date and time?**********

After the candidate accepts the seat, they need to download the seat allotment result and report to the Institute with the necessary documents. In case of the failure of reporting, the admission will be cancelled.

### 10. ****What will be the next process after the candidate accepts the seat allotted to them in TG EAPCET counseling 2025?****

After the candidate accepts the allotted seat, they need to visit the official website to download the provisional seat allotment letter. Next, candidates need to report to the institute within the specified date with the required documents. Failure of reporting can result in cancelling the admission of the candidate.
