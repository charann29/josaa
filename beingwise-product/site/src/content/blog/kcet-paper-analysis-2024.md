## KCET Paper Analysis – **April 18th, 2024**

### Mathematics Subject

The KCET Mathematics 2024 paper difficulty ranges from moderate to difficult level. As expected, students faced **challenges** **with** certain **questions** related to **Integrals and the Binomial Theorem**. Additionally, some candidates found the **paper too lengthy**. Students and experts are concerned about the accuracy of a question concerning determinants in the paper. However, no official notification has been released regarding this matter.

### Biology Subject

The KCET Biology 2024 paper difficulty ranges from moderate to difficult level. Students who appeared for the KCET exam felt that the paper was a bit lengthy. And some of them faced challenges while answering the questions.

## KCET Paper Analysis – April 19th, 2024

### Physics Subject

The difficulty level of the 2024 KCET Physics paper is moderate. As expected, students found questions related to **Moving Charges and Magnetism** to be tough. Candidates can aim for a good score of 45+.

### Chemistry Subject

The KCET Chemistry 2024 paper is scheduled for April 19, 2024, from 2:30 pm to 3:50 pm. The Paper Analysis for Chemistry will be updated after the examination.
