### How to Challenge Responses in the KEAM Answer Key

To challenge the KEAM answer key, candidates must visit the official website of CEE Kerala and log in using their application number and password. Once logged in, they can select the questions they wish to challenge and upload valid supporting documents or references. A prescribed fee per question must be paid online. The challenge must be submitted within the specified deadline. If the challenge is found valid, the answer key will be revised and the fee refunded.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **When will the provisional answer key for KEAM 2025 Pharmacy be released?**

The provisional answer key for KEAM 2025 Pharmacy is expected to be released in May 2025, shortly after all exam sessions are completed.

### **2. ******How can I challenge a response in the KEAM 2025 answer key?********

To challenge a response, log in at the official CEE Kerala website, select the disputed question, upload supporting proof, and pay the required fee per challenge.

### **3. ******What should I do after checking my KEAM 2025 Pharmacy response sheet?********

Once the response sheet is released, calculate your expected score and start preparing documents for counselling. Monitor official updates for the results and counselling dates.

### **4. ******What is the official date of results announcement for KEAM 2025?********

The official date for results announcement is still awaited, however, it is expected that the results will be announced in May or early June 2025.

### **5. ******Will there be negative marking in the KEAM Pharmacy 2025 exam?********

Yes, KEAM Pharmacy 2025 follows a negative marking scheme. For every incorrect answer, one mark will be deducted, while each correct answer carries four marks. Unattempted questions will not affect the score.
