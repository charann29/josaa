## May 18 Shift 1 MHT CET PCM Paper Analysis 2026

The shift 1 of the MHT CET May 18 question paper comprised 150 questions equally distributed into Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. As per the feedback of students, there was no change in the question paper pattern. Here we have provided the subject-wise MHT CET Paper Analysis 2026 and key topics.

### MHT CET Physics Analysis

As per the MHT CET 2026 Exam analysis, the physics section was moderate as per the feedback of the students. The majority of questions were based on formulas and concepts. Students having good conceptual knowledge and practice numericals regularly found this section easy. Check the list of key topics from where questions are asked in this shift

- Current Electricity
- Modern Physics
- Electrostatics
- Oscillations & Waves
- Magnetic Effects of Current
- Semiconductor Electronics

### MHT CET Chemistry Analysis

Following the trends, chemistry was the easiest and scoring section. Many questions were directly asked, and some were theoretical questions. As per the students, preparation from the NCERT/State Books was enough to solve the majority of the questions. Take a look at the key topics where questions are asked in this shift

- Chemical Thermodynamics
- Alcohols, Phenols & Ethers
- Coordination Compounds
- p-Block Elements
- Chemical Kinetics
- Biomolecules

### MHT CET Maths Analysis

As per the students’ feedback, Mathematics was the toughest and longest section in the paper. Some questions needed several calculations, which made the section time-consuming. Weightage for Calculus was high, whereas Algebra and Coordinate Geometry had a number of questions.

Check out the key topics where questions are asked in this shift

- Definite Integration
- Differentiation
- Probability
- Matrices & Determinants
- Vector Algebra
- 3D Geometry
- Complex Numbers

The MHT CET PCM Group Answer key will be released in online mode along with the response sheet. The MHT CET answer key and response sheet can be downloaded by following the process below:
