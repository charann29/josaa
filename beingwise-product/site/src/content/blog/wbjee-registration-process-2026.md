## WBJEE Eligibility 2026

Before applying for [WBJEE 2026](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-2026/)  candidates should carefully review the eligibility criteria. For more details related to [WBJEE Eligibility](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-eligibility-criteria-2026), candidates should refer to the article below:
**Nationality**

Applicants must be citizens of India or Overseas Citizens of India (OCI). OCI candidates are eligible to apply but can only compete for Unreserved seats under the All India quota, subject to approval by the competent authority.

### **Educational Qualification**

Candidates must meet the following academic requirements:

- Must have passed Class 12 (10+2) or equivalent examination before 2026 or be appearing in the 2026 board examination.
- Must have studied Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with Chemistry / Computer Science / Electronics / Information Technology / Biology / Informatics Practices / Biotechnology / Technical Vocational subject / Agriculture / Engineering Graphics / Business Studies / Entrepreneurship.
- Candidates must obtain at least 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, and PwD candidates) in the required three subjects mentioned above.
- Candidates who have passed D.Voc. stream in the same or allied sector are also eligible.
- Must have passed English in Class 12 with at least 30% marks.
- Candidates must pass both theory and practical/project components in the required subjects.

### **Age Limit**

- The minimum age required to appear for WBJEE 2026 is 17 years as of December 31, 2026.
- Candidates must have been born on or before December 31, 2009.
- There is no upper age limit for most courses.

## **How to Fill WBJEE Registration Form 2026**

The WBJEE 2026 application form must be completed online through the official website. The registration process includes five simple steps: registration, filling the application form, uploading documents, paying the fee, and downloading the confirmation page.

### **Step 1: Online Registration**

Candidates first need to create their login credentials through the online registration portal.

- Visit the official WBJEE website.
- Click on “New Candidate Registration.”

- Upon clicking, a page named ‘Instructions and Procedure for Online Submission of Application Form’ will appear. To register, you will have to click on ‘Proceed to Application;

- After clicking on that page, you will be directed to the confirmation page. In this page, you will have to check all the boxes and click on ‘Agree’

- Enter the required personal details such as:
  - Candidate name
  - Father’s name
  - Mother’s name
  - Date of birth
  - Gender
  - Identification type and identity number

Candidates also need to enter their mobile number and email ID under the contact details section. After submitting the details, an OTP verification will be sent to the registered mobile number. Once verified, the system will generate a unique WBJEE application number. During this step, candidates must also create a password and choose a security question with its answer. These login credentials will be required for all future logins.

***Important:*** *The candidate’s name, father’s name, mother’s name, gender, and date of birth must match exactly with the details mentioned in the Class 10 or Class 12 board certificates.*

### **Step 2: Fill the Application Form**

After completing registration, candidates must log in using their application number and password to fill out the remaining details in the application form. In this stage, candidates will need to provide:

- Personal details
- Academic qualification details
- Contact information
- Domicile details
- Category and income details (if applicable)
- Preferred exam city choices

Applicants should carefully review all the information before proceeding to the next step.

### **Step 3: Upload Photograph and Signature**

Candidates must upload scanned copies of their photograph and signature according to the official specifications.

Photograph specifications

- Format: JPG/JPEG
- File size: 10 KB to 200 KB

Signature specifications

- Format: JPG/JPEG
- File size: 4 KB to 30 KB

If the uploaded images do not meet the specified guidelines, the application form may not be accepted.

### **Step 4: Pay WBJEE Application Fee**

Once the documents are uploaded, candidates must pay the application fee through online payment methods. Available payment options include:

After successful payment, the system will generate the acknowledgement page.

Important Note: Candidates can modify their application details before making the fee payment. Once the fee has been paid, most details cannot be edited. If the acknowledgement page is not generated after payment, the transaction will be cancelled and the amount will be refunded automatically.

### **Step 5: Download the Confirmation Page**

After completing the payment, candidates must download the WBJEE confirmation page (acknowledgment page). Candidates should download and print the confirmation page and keep it safe for future reference. The application number mentioned on this page must be used for all future communication related to WBJEE.

#### Important Instructions About Password

During the registration process, candidates are required to create a password along with a security question and answer. Some important points to remember:

- Do not share your password with anyone.
- Always log out after completing your session.
- Passwords can be changed later through the login portal if required.

### How to Reset WBJEE Login Password

If candidates forget their password, they can reset it using any of the following options:

1. Using the security question and answer selected during registration
2. Using an OTP sent to the registered mobile number
3. Using a reset link sent to the registered email address

Candidates should never share OTPs with anyone, as they are confidential.

- Must be a colour photograph with a white background
