## What are the Students’ Reactions to the AP EAPCET 2026 Exam?

Student reactions play an important role in understanding the actual paper pattern and practical difficulty level. The overall AP EAMCET 2026 Exam Analysis clearly reflected mixed reactions because of the highly time-consuming Mathematics section.

**Mathematics:** Students consistently expressed that Mathematics was extremely lengthy and tough. Many students noted that the questions were comparable to JEE-level difficulty, which caused them to get stuck and consume a significant amount of time. Even though some of the underlying concepts might have been manageable, the lengthy nature of the calculations made it the most challenging and time-consuming section of the exam.

**Physics and Chemistry**: In stark contrast to Mathematics, students found Physics and Chemistry to be highly manageable, broadly rating them as Easy to Moderate. The questions in these two sections were straightforward and did not feature a high level of toughness, providing a huge relief to students after dealing with Maths.

Overall, students felt that the paper as a whole was relatively easy to moderate, provided they smartly skipped or delayed the lengthy Maths questions to avoid getting stuck and wasting time
