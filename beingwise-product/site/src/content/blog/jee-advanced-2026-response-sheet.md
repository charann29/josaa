## What is JEE Advanced Response Sheet?

The JEE Advanced Response Sheet is an official document issued by IIT Roorkee consisting of the answers filled in by candidates in JEE Advanced 2026. This document enables candidates to check the responses filled by them in Paper 1 and Paper 2.

With the help of this response sheet and JEE Advanced Answer Key 2026, students can calculate their expected scores in advance, prior to the release of the result announcement. The JEE Advanced 2026 Response Sheet consists of:

- Questions attempted by the candidate

- Recorded responses submitted in the CBT examination
- Section-wise answer details

## Difference Between JEE Adv Response Sheet & Answer Key

Many candidates face confusion between the JEE Advanced Answer Key and the JEE Advanced Response Sheet. These two documents differ from each other and are provided by IIT Roorkee separately for use in result evaluation.

| Aspects | JEE Advanced Response Sheet | JEE Advanced Answer Key |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Meaning | The response sheet contains the answers marked by the candidate during the examination. | The answer key contains the officially correct answers released by IIT Roorkee. |
| Released By | IIT Roorkee | IIT Roorkee |
| Purpose | Helps candidates review their recorded responses. | Helps candidates verify correct answers and calculate scores. |
| Contains | Candidate’s selected options and recorded responses. | Correct answers for all questions in Paper 1 and Paper 2. |
| Usefulness | Used to check what answers were submitted in the exam. | Used to compare responses and estimate probable marks. |
| Release Date 2026 | May 21, 2026 | May 25, 2026 |
| Objection Facility | No objection facility available. | Candidates can challenge the provisional answer key. |
| Login Required | Yes, candidates need login credentials. | Usually available publicly in PDF format. |
