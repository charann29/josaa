## IIT Madras Placements – B.Tech Placement Process

IIT Madras placements offer students placement opportunities and pre-placement training through the Placements and Internship Office. Second -year students get pre-placement training along with career counselling, resume building workshops, mock interviews, and other skill-building activities. Training for Technical interviews, HR rounds, and group discussions is also conducted.

Students satisfying the CPI branch and job profile-specific eligibility criteria can apply for the company interviews. The placement process begins in December when students are required to register on the [IITM placement portal](https://placement.iitm.ac.in/).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Do IIT Madras internship opportunities turn into placement offers?**

Ans. Yes, IIT Madras provides internship opportunities across sectors like software, core engineering, analytics, consulting, finance, and research. Many students participate in paid summer internships (usually 2 months), and these often convert into pre-placement offers.

**2. Is the IIT Madras CSE average package more than that of any other core branches?**

Ans. Yes, the CSE branch at IIT Madras has the highest average package compared to other branches, with an average of around INR 24.2 LPA.

**3. What is the highest package of IIT Madras?**

Ans. IIT Madras recorded the highest international package of approximately INR 4.3 Crore per annum.

**4. Are IIT Madras placements good?**

Ans. Yes, IIT Madras placements are considered top-notch, offering high salary packages, strong placement rates, excellent ROI, and top recruiters. However, core branches may have comparatively fewer opportunities, and the academic workload can be demanding.
