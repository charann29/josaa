## How to Download KEAM Rank List?

Check out the following to learn how to access the KEAM 2026 rank list.

- Take a printout of the same for future reference.

## KEAM Engineering Rank List Preparation

Check out how the KEAM rank list is prepared below:

**Equal Weightage for Entrance and Qualifying Examination Scores:** When preparing the engineering rank list, both the KEAM Engineering Entrance Test scores and the final-year qualifying examination scores are given equal weight. After the required processing of normalisation and standardisation, both scores are treated as 50% of the total score.

**Subjects Considered for Rank Preparation:** From the qualifying examination, the scores of Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry are considered. If a candidate has not studied Chemistry, then the score of Computer Science will be considered. If neither Chemistry nor Computer Science have not been studied, then the score of Biotechnology will be considered. In the absence of Chemistry, Computer Science, and Biotechnology, the score of Biology will be considered.

**Conversion and Standardisation of Qualifying Examination Scores:** The scores of the final-year exams in the respective Education Boards for Mathematics, Physics, and the relevant third subject are first converted to a score of 100. After the process of standardisation, the total score for the three subjects will be 300.

**Standardisation Method Used for Ranking:** The method for standardisation of scores used in ranking has received the approval of an Expert Committee. As a part of this process, the marks of candidates from different Boards are scientifically adjusted, for example, through the Global Mean and the Global Standard Deviation, to maintain equity within the diversity of different education systems.

**Final Subjects Used for Ranking:** For ranking, the final-year qualifying examination scores of Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry (or the officially designated substitutes) are considered.

## How to Submit 10+2 Scores for KEAM Rank List Preparation?

Students may follow the steps below to successfully submit their 10+2 marks for KEAM rank list preparation.

- Go to the official website of the CEE Kerala and access the KEAM Candidate Portal.
- Sign in to the portal by using your application number and password.
- Navigate to the “Mark Submission for Engineering” option in the dashboard.
- Enter your Class 12 (Plus Two) marks for the relevant subjects.
- Verify the entered information very carefully.
- In case you find any discrepancies in the marks shown, make changes where needed.
- Submit your marks before the set deadline.
- Print out the Mark Submission Confirmation Report.
- Make a copy of the Mark Submission Confirmation Report.
- Make sure that your marks have been submitted successfully, because if you fail to do so, you will not get your name in the engineering rank list of the KEAM.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Is the KEAM rank list released separately from the entrance exam result?**

Ans. Yes, the KEAM rank list is released after the entrance exam result. It is prepared by combining entrance scores with qualifying exam marks and is used for counselling and seat allotment.

**2. Can I get admission if my KEAM rank is lower than expected?**

Ans. Yes, admission is possible depending on seat availability, college, and branch preferences. Strategic option entry can improve your chances.

**3. Does the KEAM rank calculator provide the exact rank?**

Ans. No, the KEAM rank calculator only provides an estimated rank based on past data and trends. The official rank may differ.

**4. What is the difference between KEAM marks and rank?**

Ans. KEAM marks represent the candidate’s score in the exam, while rank indicates the candidate’s position among all qualified candidates. Rank is used for admission decisions.

**5. Can the KEAM last rank change every year?**

Ans. Yes, the last rank varies each year based on factors like competition, seat availability, and candidate preferences.

**6. Is the KEAM rank list valid for all participating colleges?**

Ans. Yes, the KEAM rank list is used for admission to participating engineering colleges, although individual eligibility criteria may apply.

**7. How useful is the KEAM last year rank list during counselling?**

Ans. It helps candidates understand past admission trends and make informed choices while filling options during counselling.

**8. Does the KEAM rank predictor with marks guarantee admission chances?**

Ans. No, it only provides an approximate idea. Final admission depends on official rank, seat availability, and counselling outcomes.

**9. Are KEAM college rankings important during option entry?**

Ans. Yes, they help compare colleges and prioritize choices, but candidates should also consider branch preference and career goals.

**10. Where can I check the official KEAM rank list date?**

Ans. Candidates should check the official KEAM admission website and notifications regularly for updates on the rank list release date.
