According to the schedule released by the TSBIE (Telangana Board of Intermediate Education), the Telangana Intermediate Supplementary exams commenced on May 22nd, 2025. The exams are going to be held from May 22nd to May 29th 2025, across 892 different centres in the Telangana state. As per the official reports, a total number of 4.2 Lakh candidates are expected to appear for the exam.

The Telangana Intermediate exam was held from March 6th to March 25th, 2025, and the results were announced on April 22nd, 2025. Those candidates who are not qualified for the exams will have to appear for the Intermediate supplementary exams to qualify for the exam successfully. ### Who can appear for the Telangana intermediate supplementary exam

Understanding who can appear for the Telangana intermediate supplementary exam is important, as students are often confused about the supplementary exam and the improvement exam. The following people are eligible to appear for the Telangana supplementary exam:

- Candidates who failed to attain the minimum marks in the regular exams.
- Candidates who are willing to improve their marks in one or more subjects.

## How to Download Inter Supply Exam Hall Ticket?

Students appearing for the Telangana Intermediate supplementary exams are required to carry their Hall ticket. The hall ticket is an important document without which students are not allowed to write the exam. Check how to download the TS Inter supplementary exam hall ticket:

**Step 1:** Navigate to the official website, i.e.[**tgbie.cgg**](http://tgbie.cgg.gov.in)**[.](http://tgbie.cgg.gov.in)**[**gov.in**](http://tgbie.cgg.gov.in)**.**

## TS Intermediate Supplementary Examination: Guidelines to Follow

Students have to cooperate with the authorities for a smooth examination process without any disturbance. Students are advised to follow the exam day guidelines set by the authorities. Here are some of those:

- Arrive early at the examination centre.
- Candidates can scan the QR code to navigate to the exam centre.
- Items such as Mobile phones, calculators, smart watches, and formula books are prohibited inside the examination hall.
- Candidates should not be involved in any unfair means.
- Students are advised to stay calm during the examination process.

## Documents Required for TS Inter Supply Examination

Here are the documents that students need to carry with them to the examination centre:

- **Hall Ticket/Admit Card:** Students must download their hall tickets from the official website and carry a printed copy to the exam centre.

- **Valid Photo ID:** A valid photo ID, such as an Aadhaar card, PAN card, or voter ID, must be carried for identity verification at the exam centre.

## Final Tips for Students
