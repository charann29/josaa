The Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University released the **Application form for TS EAMCET on  March 1st, 2025.** The Applicants will be able to fill the application form at ***eamcet.tsche.ac.in***.

The candidates can submit the application form only through the Online mode. **Only 12th-passed students or students who have appeared for the 12th Exam** will be able to appear for the TS EAMCET exam.

After completing the registration process on **6th** **April 2025, candidates can access the application correction facility.** Candidates who have made any errors in the application form will be able to correct their details during the application correction window.

## TG-EAPCET Application Form

The **Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (TG-EAPCET) Application Form** is the official registration form for candidates seeking admission to engineering, agriculture, and pharmacy courses in Telangana. Conducted by **Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad**, on behalf of the **Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE)**, the application process is online. Applicants must provide personal details, educational qualifications, and category information, upload necessary documents, and pay the application fee to complete the process. The form is typically released on the official website, and candidates must ensure they meet eligibility criteria before applying. The Application Form was released on March 1st, 2025.

## How to Fill TG-EAPCET Application Form

Here are the steps on how to fill the TG-EAPCET application form and the direct Link to apply for the application form.

- Visit the official website
- The Application form filling will be a 3-step process.
  - Paying registration Fee: [Payment Link](https://eapcet.tgche.ac.in/TGEAPCET/EAPCET_PaymentGatewaypage.aspx)
  - Filling out the Application Form: [Application Form Link](https://eapcet.tgche.ac.in/TGEAPCET/EAPCET_ApplicationForm.aspx)
  - Print the Filled Application Form: [Print Application Form](https://eapcet.tgche.ac.in/TGEAPCET/EAPCET_GetPrintApplication.aspx)

  - Mobile Number

## Reasons for TG EAPCET Application Form Rejection

The Application form should be filled with discretion otherwise, it may lead to the rejection of the application form. Here are a few Potential reasons for the rejection of the application form

- Failure of the Payment
- Unable to completely fill out the application form
- Blurred Images for Photograph and Signature
- Submission of 2 or more application forms
- Incorrect information in the application form
- Submitting the application form after the last date.

## TG EAPCET Admit Card

The [**TG EAPCET Admit Card**](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/ts-eamcet-hall-ticket/) is a crucial document that candidates must carry to the examination center. candidates can download it by entering their **registration number, date of birth, and qualifying exam hall ticket number**. The admit card contains important details such as **candidate’s name, roll number, exam date, time, venue, and instructions**. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ********When is TS EAMCET going to be conducted?********

The TS Eamcet is going to be conducted on 29th to 30th April 2025 for pharmacy students and  2nd May 2025 to 5th May 2025 for Engineering students.

### 2. ********How much is the fee for TG EAPCET Application Correction?********

Students need not pay any money for application corrections.

### 3. ********What is the Mode of TS EAMCET?********

The TS Eamcet is conducted in online CBT(Computer Based Test) Mode

### 4. What if a candidate’s session expired during registration?

If a candidate’s session expired during registration then they can re-login and apply again and the details will be saved in the server.

### 5. ********Is Preparing Last Year’s Papers Necessary?********

Yes, preparing previous year’s question papers can make the candidate score higher in the TG EAPCET Exam.

### 6. ****Can a Candidate Cancel the Submitted Application in TG EAPCET?****

No, Candidates cannot cancel/withdraw the submitted application

### 7. ****How many marks are deducted for skipping a question in TG-EAPCET?****

Marks will not be deducted for the questions missed during the exam in the TG EAPCET.
