## Step-by-Step Guide To Check Class 12 TN Board Results 2026

The Tamil Nadu 12th Results 2026 will be released on the official portal of tn result nic. For the TN 12th marksheet download,​ students need to follow the given steps:

- Open the official TN Board result website or tnresults nic in.
- Click on the [Tamil Nadu 12th Results 2026 Link](http://tnresults.nic.in) on the home page.
- Enter the registration number and DOB written on the TN 12th hall ticket.
- Click on the Submit option.
- The provisional TN Result marksheet appears on the screen.
- Students can see subject-wise scores, total marks, and pass status.
- Download and keep the TN Board Results Marksheet 2026 for Future Reference.

## Class 12 TN Board Results 2026 – Passing Criteria

To pass the Tamil Nadu Class 12th Boards Exams, student to score a minimum of 35 Marks out of 100 per subject. Whether theory or practical exams, this criterion is applied across all the streams for TNEA eligibility. The subject-wise passing marks for Tamil Nadu 12th Results 2026 are as follows:

- **Theory Subjects Pass Marks**: 35/100 Marks
- **Theory + Practical Subject Pass Marks**:
  - Theory Pass Marks: 15/70 Marks
  - Total Pass Marks: 35/100 Marks
  - No separate practical pass marks are required, but attendance is compulsory.
- Internal Subject Pass Marks are not required.

Students who failed to achieve these passing marks may request retotalling or appear in the supplementary exam conducted in June-July.

## Ways to Solve Discrepancies in Your Class 12 TN Board Results

If students find any issues or discrepancies in the Class 12 TN Board result marksheet, such as totalling marks, unchecked answers, and other issues. Students need to act fast and apply to solve the issues within 5 days of the TN results being released. The following are the ways to solve the discrepancies or issues in the Tamil Nadu 12th Results marksheet 2026:

- **Request for Re-totalling**: For Calculation Errors in your TN gov 12th result, request re-totalling in the following manner​:
  - Students can submit an application to the Chief Educational Officer (CEO)
  - To re-add the marks and check the unchecked answers.
  - Students need to pay INR 205 per subject and INR 305 for two-paper subjects.
  - The process is conducted quickly, and students cannot ask for any more retotalling after this result.
- **Self-Check the Photocopy/Scanned Answer Sheet**: To be sure of the marks obtained in the TN Board Results, students can request self-checking of the answer sheets. Apply for the photocopy of the answer sheets in the following manner:
  - Apply via the school website for a digital copy of the answer sheet
  - A Fee of INR 305 Per subject is required to complete the application.
  - If you spot any errors, students can proceed with revaluation or re-totalling.
- **Apply for Revaluation of TN Results**: Revaluation is a detailed re-checking of the student’s answer sheet conducted by a 3-member committee. Check the following criteria for revaluation
  - Students must apply for revaluation after requesting a photocopy or scanned copy to self-check.
  - A fee of INR 505 per subject is payable to access this service.
  - Within 10-15 days, the revaluation results are published.
  - Students must remember that marks may increase or decrease as per revaluation.
