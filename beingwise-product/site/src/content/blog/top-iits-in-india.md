IITs are India’s prestigious institutions offering top-tier education across different branches in undergraduate like Engineering, Architecture, Planning, and some postgraduate and research opportunities. IITs are government-administered institutes and there are 23 IITs in India.

Ministry of Education has recently released the NIRF Engineering ranking for 2024. NIRF Engineering ranking considers 5 parameters to rank the institutions based on these parameters. The parameters include Graduation Outcomes(GO), Outreach & Inclusivity(OI), Research & Professional practices(RPC), Teaching, Learning, and Resources(TLR), and Perception(PER). ## Description of Top 10 IITs in India

### IIT Madras

> **IIT Madras** is located in Chennai and is one of the oldest and most prestigious IITs in India. IIT Madras is known for its academic excellence, offering research programs in diverse fields like engineering, technology, and management. IIT Madras has produced many successful professionals and entrepreneurs. The campus fosters innovation through world-class facilities and collaborations with industries and global universities.

### IIT Delhi

> Established in 1961, **IIT Delhi** is a prestigious institution in India. IIT Delhi is renowned for its high academic standards and research programs. IIT Delhi offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, and sciences. IIT Delhi has graduated more than 60,000 students across various disciplines, including Engineering, Physical Sciences, Management, and Humanities & Social Sciences, since its establishment.

### IIT Bombay

> Located in the center of India’s financial capital, Mumbai, **IIT Bombay** is a leader in technological education and research. Renowned for its faculty, infrastructure, and research facilities, IIT Bombay excels in engineering, computer science, and humanities. Its collaborative environment with industries and its strong emphasis on innovation and entrepreneurship make it one of the top institutes in the world.

### IIT Kanpur

> **IIT Kanpur** was established in 1959 and is one of India’s most respected institutions for education in engineering and technology. With the motto of Tamaso mÄ jyotirgamaya (Lead me from darkness to light) IIT Kanpur has delivered many professionals and entrepreneurs. Spread across 1055 Acres, IIT Kanpur offers world-class amenities for students to cultivate innovation and interdisciplinary learning.

### IIT Kharagpur

> **IIT Kharagpur** was established in 1951 and is known for its academic excellence and excellent research in engineering, science, and technology. With the mission to provide top-tier education and help students foster their professional skills, IIT Kanpur is a hub of innovation and leadership. IIT Kharagpur has a diverse alumni of scientists, entrepreneurs, engineers, etc.

### IIT Roorkee

> **IIT Roorkee** is ranked 6th in the NIRF world rankings and is also ranked 335 in the QS World Rankings. It is one of the oldest technical institutes established in the year 1947. The institute is renowned for its engineering programs, particularly in civil, mechanical, and electrical engineering. IIT Roorkee has a rich legacy of excellence and a research-driven education.

### IIT Guwahati

> **IIT Guwahati** was established in 1994, located in Assam. The institute is known for its focus on engineering, technology, and research in fields like computer science, electronics, and biotechnology. With a beautiful campus along the Brahmaputra River, IIT Guwahati fosters innovation and entrepreneurship, offering a collaborative learning environment with strong industry partnerships.

### IIT Hyderabad

> Established in 2008, IIT Hyderabad is the fastest-growing IIT in India. IIT Hyderabad is recognized for its research excellence and academic programs in various fields of engineering and technology. It focuses on innovative teaching methodologies, research, and industry collaboration. The institute is renowned for its emphasis on entrepreneurship, technological advancements, and creating solutions to real-world challenges.

### IIT Tiruchirapalli

> **IIT Tiruchirapalli** is located in Tamil Nadu and offers high-quality education and research in engineering, technology, and management. The institute is known for its academic excellence and innovative approach to solving real-world problems. With a focus on sustainable development and industry collaboration, IIT Tiruchirapalli nurtures students’ potential through a dynamic learning environment and fosters research initiatives to address global challenges.

### IIT BHU Varanasi

> **IIT BHU Varanasi**, previously known as the Institute of Technology, Banaras Hindu University, is a leader in technical education and research. Located in the cultural and spiritual hub of Varanasi, the institute excels in engineering, technology, and management disciplines. It has a strong tradition of academic excellence, innovation, and research, producing graduates who are leaders in various industries globally.
