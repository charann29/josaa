## IIT Madras B.Tech Admission Eligibility Criteria

IIT Madras B.Tech admissions are conducted based on academic merit and performance in national entrance exams. It follows the admission criteria prescribed by the Joint Admission Board (JAB). Students are required to appear for the JEE Advanced exams and are further allotted seats via the JoSAA counselling process. For IIT Madras Course Admissions, the Eligibility and selection criteria are as follows:

| Program | B.Tech Eligibility Criteria | B.Tech Selection Criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| B.Tech | • Passed 10+2 level exam with minimum 75% aggregate from a recognised board  • Mandatory subjects: Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics | • JEE Advanced Entrance Score  • Seats allotted through JoSAA Counselling |

### IIT Madras Course Admissions Process for B.Tech

IIT Madras course admissions process for B.Tech is conducted in the following steps:

- Eligible students need to appear for the JEE Advanced exams and secure a competitive rank.
- Students with valid ranks can participate in the JoSAA counselling conducted by the Joint Seat Allocation Authority.
- Fill in choices with preferred colleges and branches during the choice-filling round.
- Seats are allotted based on the JEE Advanced ranks, category, preferences, and seat availability.
- Once seats are allotted, students need to complete campus reporting, document verification, and fee payment.

## IIT Madras Campus Life & Facilities

IIT Madras Campus is located in the heart of Chennai, covering a 620-acre area. It is known for its lush green surroundings and excellent academic atmosphere. It offers a perfect balance and provides a mixture of academics, research, and extracurricular activities. IIT Madras campus offers the following facilities to students:

- Modern Classrooms
- The Advanced Research Park, known as IIT Madras Research Park,​ is available to students for various research facilities and labs.
- Digital libraries and the central library
- Innovation centers
- High-speed internet across the IIT Madras campus.
- Technical festivals like Shaastra and Cultural events like Saarang are conducted.
- Hostel Accommodation with dining halls, sports complexes, a gym and medical centres is available for all students.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1. What are the facilities available at IIT Madras hostels?

IIT Madras offers 20 hostels, including 5 for female students and 15 for male students. Facilities include a library, garden, sports facilities, music room, tech room, television room, along with transport, medical, and other basic amenities.

### Q2. What is the IIT Madras QS ranking?

IIT Madras is ranked around 180th position in the QS World University Rankings overall category.

### Q3. Does IIT Madras offer an online B.Tech degree?

No, IIT Madras does not offer an online B.Tech degree.

### Q4. Who are the top recruiters at IIT Madras?

Top recruiters at IIT Madras include Apple, Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and Texas Instruments.
