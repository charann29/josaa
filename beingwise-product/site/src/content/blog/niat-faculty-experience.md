## Who Are the NIAT Mentors and Trainers?

NIAT deploys its own **Trainers, Mentors, and Success Coaches** to deliver the upskilling program at NIAT. These are not university lecturers or academic staff; they are industry-experienced professionals passionate about building job-ready skills in every student they guide.

NIAT mentors and trainers bring strong backgrounds from premier institutions like the IITs and NIITs, combined with real-world experience from leading companies and startups across domains such as software development, data science, AI, and cybersecurity. This blend of academic grounding and hands-on industry exposure means students receive practical, relevant guidance, not just theoretical knowledge.

**What sets NIAT Mentors and Trainers apart:**

- **Industry Experience**: Having worked on real projects at well-known companies and startups, they bring practical knowledge that directly reflects what today’s employers look for.
- **Domain Expertise**: With deep specialisation across areas like Full-Stack Development, AI/ML, Data Science, and Cybersecurity, they ensure training stays sharp and relevant.
- **Skill-Focused Guidance**: Rather than following a fixed academic syllabus, they deliver industry-aligned training modules and project-based learning tailored to the current job market.
- **Mentorship-Driven Support**: Through personalised mentorship and continuous support, Success Coaches ensure every student stays on track with their skilling goals and career readiness.

### Super Mentors

Super mentors at NIAT are from top-tier companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Meta, etc. At NIAT, students learn directly from super mentors and get insights on building an extraordinary career in the tech industry. They go beyond regular mentoring by providing personalised attention, motivation, and expert advice to help students succeed. Each student is assigned one dedicated Super Mentor to ensure focused and personalised guidance. Let’s know more about NIAT Super Mentors.

**Why are they called Super Mentors?**

They are called “Super” mentors because of their deep knowledge, experience, and commitment to going the extra mile. They not only teach but also inspire, solve problems, track progress closely, and help students overcome challenges.

**Day-to-day tasks of a Super Mentor:**

- Guide students through their course content and projects
- Answer questions and clarify doubts promptly
- Monitor each student’s progress and provide feedback
- Motivate students to stay focused and reach their goals
- Help students apply learning to real-world problems
- Organize regular one-on-one or group sessions to discuss challenges

**Who qualifies as a Super Mentor?**

- Experienced professionals from top institutes or industries
- Experts with strong technical skills and teaching ability
- Mentors who are patient, approachable, and proactive in student support

### Success Coaches

NIAT Success Coaches stand beside students every step of the way, offering heartfelt care and understanding. Whether managing daily stress or striving to achieve big dreams, the coaches help set goals, track progress, and overcome roadblocks. They also regularly communicate with parents to update them on their child’s progress and well-being.

The main goal of “Success Coaches” is to help students succeed, not just academically but also personally and professionally. They coach students on time management, goal setting, and problem-solving to make sure they keep moving forward.

**Day-to-day tasks of a Success Coach:**

- Help students set clear, achievable goals
- Track students’ progress and attendance
- Provide motivation and encouragement
- Assist in planning study schedules and managing time
- Identify and address any challenges or barriers
- Conduct regular check-ins to keep students accountable

**Who qualifies as a Success Coach?**

- Professionals with strong communication and mentoring skills
- People experienced in coaching, counselling, or student support
- Those who are empathetic, patient, and motivating

## Conclusion

At NIAT, the quality of guidance students receive goes far beyond conventional classroom instruction. NIAT has dedicated **Trainers, Mentors, Super Mentors, and Success Coaches**, all industry-experienced professionals who are entirely separate from the academic faculty of the collaborating universities. Whether it’s mastering a complex technical concept, navigating career goals, or staying motivated through challenges, every student at NIAT has a support system built around their success. This people-first, skill-focused approach is what makes NIAT’s upskilling program a meaningful complement to your university degree, preparing you not just to graduate, but to truly excel in today’s competitive tech industry.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ********Who teaches at NIAT?********

Students at NIAT are taught by experts in the domain and individuals from tech giants like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, etc.

### 2. ********Can we connect with the NIAT Mentors and Trainers?********

You can connect with NIAT seniors and ask about any queries related to mentors, skill map, etc. Any student can **[fill out the form](https://forms.ccbp.in/register/ama-session-registration)**, book the slot and talk to the seniors of NIAT about the mentors and trainers.

### 3. ********Are mentors at NIAT experienced?********

Mentors at NIAT are experienced in the tech domain and are experienced members of top-tier companies like Amazon, Google, etc.

### 4. ********Are we exposed to practical learning at NIAT?********

Students at NIAT are taught to learn practically with real-world tech problems, ensuring better outcomes for students.

### 5. ****Are the NIAT Mentors and Trainers part of the university’s academic staff?****

No. NxtWave deploys its own Trainers, Mentors, Super Mentors, and Success Coaches exclusively for the upskilling program. They are entirely separate from the collaborating university’s academic faculty and are not involved in delivering university degree courses in any way.

### 6. How are NIAT’s Mentors selected?

NIAT’s mentors are experienced professionals from premier institutions and leading tech companies. They are selected based on their domain expertise, industry experience, teaching ability, and commitment to student success, ensuring every student receives high-quality, practical guidance.

### 7. How does NIAT’s mentorship model differ from a traditional university’s teaching approach?

Traditional universities rely on academic faculty to deliver degree coursework in a lecture-based format. At NIAT, Trainers, Mentors, and Success Coaches focus entirely on skill-building through hands-on projects, personalised mentorship, and continuous support, complementing the university education without replacing it.

### 8. Will I get personalised attention from mentors at NIAT?

Yes. Each student is assigned a dedicated Super Mentor for focused, one-on-one guidance, alongside Success Coaches who provide continuous support throughout the upskilling program ensuring no student is left behind on their journey to becoming industry-ready.
