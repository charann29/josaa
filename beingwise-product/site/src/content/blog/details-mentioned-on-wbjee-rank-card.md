Every candidate who appears for WBJEE wants to know one thing after the result is out: what exactly will their rank card say? The details mentioned on WBJEE rank card are more than just a score. The document carries your identity information, subject-wise performance, merit rank, and category rank, all of which play a direct role in your counselling eligibility and college admission.

Understanding the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card in advance helps you verify your information quickly once it is released and flag any errors before the counselling window opens. This article walks you through every field on the rank card, what each one means, and why it matters.

## WBJEE Rank Card 2026: Overview

Before getting into the specifics, here is a quick look at what the WBJEE rank card is and how it works.

| Particulars | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Issuing Authority | West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) |
| Release Mode | Online only at wbjeeb.nic.in |
| Valid For | 2026–27 academic session only |
| Login Required | Application number and password or date of birth |
| Physical Copy | Not dispatched by post |
| Used For | Counselling, seat allotment, and document verification |

## Full List of Details Mentioned on WBJEE Rank Card

The details mentioned on WBJEE rank card fall into three broad groups: personal information, academic performance, and merit rank data. Here is a section-by-section breakdown.

### Personal and Registration Details

The first section of the rank card covers identity and registration information. These details are pulled directly from the application form submitted during registration.

- Candidate’s full name
- Father’s name
- Date of birth
- Gender
- Category (General, SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, PwD, TFW, EWS)
- Domicile status (Home State or All India)
- Application form number
- Roll number assigned for the exam

These fields must match your original documents exactly. Even a minor spelling difference in the name or a wrong date of birth can create problems during document verification at the allotted institute. If you spot any mismatch in the personal details mentioned on WBJEE rank card, report it to WBJEEB immediately before counselling begins.

### Subject-wise Marks

The academic performance section is one of the most important parts of the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card. WBJEE 2026 was conducted across two papers, and the marks are reported separately for each subject.

| Subject | Paper | Maximum Marks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mathematics | Paper 1 | 100 |
| Physics | Paper 2 | 50 |
| Chemistry | Paper 2 | 50 |
| **Total** | Paper 1 + Paper 2 | **200** |

The marks shown are your final computed score after applying the correct answers from the final answer key and the applicable negative marking rules. These are not raw marks from the OMR sheet. Positive marks, negative marks, and the net subject-wise score are all factored in before the figure appears on the rank card.

### Total Marks Secured

Alongside the individual subject scores, the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card also include the total marks secured out of 200. This combined figure is what WBJEEB uses to prepare the General Merit Rank. A higher total score directly translates to a better rank and wider college options during counselling.

### General Merit Rank (GMR)

The General Merit Rank is the most important figure among all the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card. It is calculated based on your combined performance in Paper 1 (Mathematics) and Paper 2 (Physics and Chemistry).

The GMR determines your position in the overall merit list and is the rank used for:

- Engineering and technology admissions across all WBJEE participating institutes
- Architecture admissions
- Pharmacy admissions at Jadavpur University specifically

A lower GMR number means a better rank. Rank 1 is the highest. The counselling seat allotment process is driven entirely by this rank, your category, and the preferences you fill in.

### Pharmacy Merit Rank (PMR)

The PMR is a separate rank that appears in the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card for eligible candidates. It is calculated based on marks in Paper 2 only, meaning Physics and Chemistry scores alone.

The PMR applies to:

- Admissions to degree-level pharmacy courses at all enlisted pharmacy colleges
- Does not apply to pharmacy at Jadavpur University, where GMR is used instead

Candidates who appeared in both Paper 1 and Paper 2 receive both a GMR and a PMR if they clear the minimum cutoff. Candidates who appeared only in Paper 2 receive a PMR but no GMR. Candidates who appeared only in Paper 1 receive neither rank.

### Category Rank

Category rank is another key element in the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card. It shows your position within your specific reserved category and is listed separately from the GMR.

Category ranks are published for the following groups:

- SC (Scheduled Caste)
- ST (Scheduled Tribe)
- OBC-A (Other Backward Class A)
- OBC-B (Other Backward Class B)
- PwD (Persons with Disability)
- TFW (Tuition Fee Waiver)
- EWS (Economically Weaker Section)

The category rank is used during counselling when seats are being allotted under reserved quotas. It does not replace the GMR but runs alongside it. If your category claim is found to be invalid during document verification, your category rank will be cancelled and you will be considered under the general category only.

### Qualifying Status

The details mentioned on WBJEE rank card also include a qualifying status indicator. This tells you whether you are eligible to participate in counselling or not. Only candidates who have secured a rank appear in the merit list and are eligible for counselling. Candidates who appeared but did not secure any rank will not have a qualifying status on their rank card.

## Why Each Detail on the WBJEE Rank Card Matters

Each field in the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card serves a specific purpose in the admission process. Here is how:

| Detail | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- |
| Name and Date of Birth | Must match marksheets and identity proof at document verification |
| Category | Determines which quota seats you are eligible for |
| Domicile | Decides Home State or All India quota eligibility |
| Subject-wise Marks | Used by some institutes to assess branch-wise eligibility |
| Total Marks | Basis for GMR preparation |
| GMR | Primary rank for engineering and architecture admissions |
| PMR | Used for pharmacy admissions outside Jadavpur University |
| Category Rank | Used for reserved quota seat allotment during counselling |

## Common Errors to Watch for in the Details Mentioned on WBJEE Rank Card

Once the rank card is released, candidates should not simply download it and move on. Verify each field carefully. Common issues that candidates have reported in previous years include:

- Name spelled differently from the Class 10 certificate
- Wrong category listed due to a form-filling error
- Domicile status incorrectly marked
- Mismatch between printed date of birth and actual date of birth
- Category rank missing despite valid category certificate submitted

If any of the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card look incorrect, raise the discrepancy with WBJEEB through the official portal before the counselling registration window opens. Waiting until document verification to flag errors can cost you your seat.

## What to Do Once You Have the WBJEE Rank Card 2026

Once the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card are in front of you, act on them immediately. Here is a simple checklist:

- Verify all personal details against your original documents
- Note your GMR and PMR separately
- Check your category rank if applicable
- Use the rank to shortlist colleges using previous year cutoff data
- Start collecting documents needed for counselling
- Register for counselling on the WBJEEB portal as soon as the window opens

Missing the counselling registration deadline means missing seat allotment for that round. The WBJEE rank card 2026 is only useful if you act on it promptly.

## Conclusion

Knowing the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card before the result is released means you can verify everything quickly and act without delay. The rank card is not just a number. It is a document that carries your complete exam identity, and even a small error in it can disrupt your counselling process if not caught early.

Download it the moment it goes live on wbjeeb.nic.in, check every field against your original documents, and use your GMR or PMR to start planning your college choices. The candidates who are prepared before the rank card drops are the ones who move through counselling without unnecessary stress.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. What are the details mentioned on WBJEE rank card?**

Ans. The WBJEE rank card includes the candidate’s name, roll number, application number, date of birth, gender, category, domicile, subject-wise marks (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry), total marks, General Merit Rank (GMR), Pharmacy Merit Rank (PMR, if applicable), and category rank.

**2. Is the subject-wise score part of the WBJEE rank card details?**

Ans. Yes, subject-wise marks for Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry are clearly mentioned as final computed scores after applying the marking scheme.

**3. Does the WBJEE rank card mention both GMR and PMR?**

Ans. Yes, if applicable. GMR is based on total marks in all subjects, while PMR is based on Physics and Chemistry (Paper 2) marks and is listed separately.

**4. What should I do if there is an error on my WBJEE rank card?**

Ans. You should contact WBJEEB immediately through the official portal and report any errors before the counselling process begins.

**5. Can the WBJEE rank predictor estimate all the details on the rank card?**

Ans. No, the rank predictor only estimates your probable rank range based on expected scores. Actual details like final marks and ranks are available only in the official rank card.

**6. Is category rank included in the WBJEE rank card?**

Ans. Yes, applicable category ranks such as SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, PwD, TFW, and EWS are included for eligible candidates.

**7. Is the qualifying status mentioned on the WBJEE rank card?**

Ans. Yes, the rank card includes a qualifying status indicating whether the candidate is eligible for the counselling process.
