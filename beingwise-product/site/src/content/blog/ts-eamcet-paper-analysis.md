## TG EAPCET 2025 Exam Overview

TG EAPCET is a state-level entrance exam for admission to engineering, agriculture, and pharmacy programs in Telangana. The exam was of a duration of 3 hours and a total of 160 marks. Below is an overview of the TS EAMCET 2025 exam.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Particulars | Details |
| Exam Name | Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (TG EAPCET) |
| TG EAPCET 2025 Exam Date | 2nd May to 4th May 2025 |
| Exam Mode | CBT/Online |
| Exam Duration | 3 hours/ 180 minutes |
| Types of Questions | MCQ |
| Exam Medium | English, Urdu & Telugu |
| Total Marks | 160 marks |
| Number of Questions | Physics: 40 questions Chemistry: 40 questions Mathematics: 80 questions |
| Marking Scheme | 1 mark will be awarded for each correct answer. There is no negative marking. |

### 2. How was the overall difficulty level of TS EAMCET 2025?

The overall difficulty ranged from easy to difficult, depending on the shift, with Mathematics and Physics generally being more challenging, while Chemistry was the easiest and scored the highest.

Yes, the first shift of TS EAMCET 2025 tended to be more difficult and lengthy compared to the second shifts, which were generally more balanced and student-friendly.
