## MHT CET Paper Analysis May 20 Shift 2

The MHT CET Paper Analysis assists candidates in understanding the exam pattern, difficulty level of the exam, and the distribution of marks according to the chapters covered in the examination. The examination will be held for a period of three hours between 2 PM and 5 PM.

According to the MHT CET Paper Analysis based on students’ responses, there were no out-of-syllabus questions or changes in the paper pattern. Chemistry appeared to be the easiest part of the paper, while the questions of Physics were not very hard and included concepts and numerical problems from both Class 11 and Class 12 syllabi.

Based on the May 20 MHT CET 2026 shift 2 Analysis, we can rate the difficulty level as Math>Physics> Chemistry.

## Subject-wise MHT CET PCM Paper Analysis

It becomes necessary to go through Subject-wise MHT CET PCM Paper Analysis in order to know about the difficulty level of the paper, scoring topics, and weightage of the questions that were asked in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

### MHT CET Paper Analysis 2026 for Physics Section

As per the MHT CET Paper analysis, physics was considered a moderate-level paper by the majority of candidates. There were many numerical questions based on formulas. Questions asked in physics were generally from the MHT CET syllabus only. Here are some important physics topics asked:

- Electrostatics
- Current Electricity
- Magnetism
- Modern Physics
- Wave Optics
- Thermodynamics
- Semiconductor
- Kinematics

### MHT CET Paper Analysis for Mathematics Section

As per the feedback received from the candidates appearing for MHT CET 20 Shift 2, Mathematics was the most difficult topic asked in the exam. Students felt that there were many lengthy questions in the paper. Those candidates who were good at concepts of calculus and coordinate geometry performed well. Here are some important mathematics topics asked:

- Integration
- Differential Equations
- Probability
- Vectors and 3D Geometry
- Application of Derivatives
- Matrices
- Pair of Straight Lines
- Complex Numbers

### MHT CET 2026 Exam Analysis for Chemistry Section

The subject Chemistry was relatively easy in comparison to other subjects and aided students in making better attempts. Most of the questions were straight and theoretical, especially related to inorganic and organic chemistry. Look at the important chemistry questions asked:

- Coordination Compounds
- Chemical Bonding
- Electrochemistry
- Amines
- Biomolecules
- Solutions
- p-Block Elements
- Thermodynamics
