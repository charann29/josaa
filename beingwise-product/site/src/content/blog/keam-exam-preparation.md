## KEAM Preparation 2026 | Strategies and Tips

The KEAM Preparation strategies and tips required for the candidates to qualify for the KEAM 2026 exam with a higher rank are as follows:

### 1. Stay Informed: Understanding the KEAM 2026 Exam Pattern

The candidates appearing for the KEAM 2026 exam should be familiar with the latest and updated KEAM exam pattern. It’s important to know every detail of the examination, like the Mode of the exam, Marking Scheme, Types of questions, and Duration of the exam, etc. The candidates can check the table below for the updated KEAM 2026 Exam Pattern:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Total Marks** | 600 (For Engineering) and 300 (For Pharmacy) |
| **Mode of the Exam** | Computer Based Test (CBT) |
| **Medium of the paper** | English |
| **Question Type** | Multiple-choice questions (MCQs) with 5 options and one most appropriate answer |
| **Total Number of Questions** | 150 Questions (For Engineering)75 Questions (For Pharmacy) |
| **Marking Scheme** | +4 Marks are awarded for every correct answer-1  Mark is awarded for every incorrect answer |

### 2. Understanding the KEAM 2026 Syllabus

The candidates shall begin their KEAM preparation by understanding the entire KEAM syllabus 2026 from Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. Being familiar with the syllabus, the candidates can plan a proper study plan and prepare for the examination accordingly.

### 3. Plan a Study Schedule

- After knowing the Exam pattern and understanding the Syllabus, the candidate shall start the KEAM preparation by planning a study schedule effectively.
- Time Management is the Key to success in the KEAM exam. The candidate should design a study schedule by allocating equal time for every subject and topic.
- The KEAM study schedule should also include small breaks between the study hours to maintain productivity.
- The candidate should make sure to follow the plan and avoid distractions.

### 4. Know Important Topics and Chapter Wise Weightage

The candidates must be familiar with the important topics and Chapter Wise Weightage in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. By knowing the important topics and weightage, they will be able to answer the maximum number of questions in their KEAM examination. However, the candidate should not miss out on any topic in all the subjects.

> **Also Read: [KEAM Important Chapters and Topics](/blog/keam/keam-important-chapters-topics/)**

### 5. Practice Regularly

Regular Practice is an important factor in the success of the KEAM 2026 exam. The candidates should make sure to practice the KEAM previous year’s question papers, attend the mock tests, solve sample papers, etc to be thorough with the examination preparation.

> **Checkout: [KEAM Previous Years Question Papers with Solutions](/blog/keam/keam-previous-year-question-papers-solutions-pdfs/)**

### 6. Concentrate on the areas of improvement

While practicing the Previous Year’s Question Papers, and solving the sample papers, the candidates can understand their strengths and weaknesses in every subject. They should pay special attention and concentration to their weaker areas during KEAM preparation.

### 7. Seek Guidance

While concentrating on the weaker areas, the candidates should seek guidance from the subject coaches or teachers in case of any doubts or blockers. This will help them increase their knowledge and they will be able to appear for the exam with confidence.

### 8. Review and Analyze

After attending Mock Tests, and solving the previous year’s question papers, the candidates will know their strengths and weaknesses. This analysis will help the candidates to improve their level of KEAM preparation for the KEAM examination.

The above KEAM preparation strategies and tips will help a candidate to score maximum in their KEAM 2026 examination and be able to get admission to a good university.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### ****1. When will the KEAM 2026 exams be conducted?****

The KEAM 2026 exam will be held between April 17 and April 23, 2026.

### ****2. What is the marking scheme for KEAM 2026?****

All the correct answers award +4 points, and all the wrong answers award -1 point as per the KEAM marking scheme.

### ****3. Which topics have average weightage in KEAM?****

Modern Physics, Coordination Compounds, and Trigonometry are some of the topics that are normally medium-weightage topics.

### ******4. How many questions are in the KEAM engineering exam?******

The engineering exam conducted by the KEAM has 150 questions, wherein Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics are included in the KEAM engineering exam.

### ****5. Can online mock tests help in KEAM preparation?****

Yes, online mock tests assist in time management, accuracy and confidence in the exam.

### ****6. Are previous year question papers useful for KEAM?****

Yes, past years’ question papers help in knowing the patterns of the questions and problem-solving in KEAM previous year papers.

### ****7. How should I divide time between Physics, Chemistry, and Maths?****

Give the same amount of time to each of the three subjects, but spend more time on the KEAM weak areas.

### ****8. Are there any important tips for last-minute KEAM revision?****

The revision should be done at the last-minute on high-weightage topics and formulas as part of the KEAM last-minute revision.

### ****9. Can coaching classes improve KEAM scores?****

Doubts can be explained in coaching classes and weak points reinforced so that they perform better in KEAM coaching classes.

### ****10. What are the best reference books for KEAM Physics?****

The books on Physics that are considered the best are the H.C. Verma Concepts of Physics, Halliday and Resnick Fundamentals of Physics, and DC Pandey Understanding Physics series for KEAM Physics books.
