West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination is a state-level entrance exam for admission to the BTech program in various Engineering colleges. The exam is conducted by WBJEEB. WBJEE 2025 is scheduled on 27th April 2025 in two shifts. Paper 1 (Mathematics) was conducted from 11 AM to 1 PM, and Paper 2 (Physics & Chemistry) was conducted from 2 PM to 4 PM. The admit card has already been released on the official website.

CollegeDost will be providing a comprehensive overview of subject-wise analysis of difficulty levels, types of questions asked, chapter-wise weightage, and student reviews.

## WBJEE Exam Analysis: Student Reviews For April 27th Paper 1 and Paper 2

According to student reviews from the WBJEE  2025, April 27th – Paper 1 & Paper 2, here is a summary of what students perceived about the exam.

### **Paper 1: Mathematics (Shift 1 – Morning Session)**

**Difficulty: Moderate to Difficult**

### **Student Feedback:**

- Many students felt that the Mathematics paper had a long duration and was slightly more difficult than in past years.
- The paper was dominated by topics from Calculus, Coordinate Geometry, and Algebra.
- Some participants mentioned that many of the questions were calculation-based and felt like they took more time.
- A small number of tricky questions appeared in Vectors and 3D Geometry that surprised many students.
- Time was an issue, particularly for average students.

### **Paper 2: Physics & Chemistry (Shift 2 – Afternoon Session)**

**Overall Difficulty Level: Moderate**

### **Student Feedback:**

- Physics was found to be conceptual but well-balanced, with a good balance between numerical and theory-based questions.
- Many of the students liked that Physics had more straightforward formula-based questions, especially from Electrostatics and Modern Physics.
- Chemistry was the easiest section, with questions mainly from the NCERT textbooks.
- Many students made factual statements for Inorganic Chemistry and needed a good understanding of reaction mechanisms for Organic Chemistry.
- Students felt that Paper 2 was easier and more manageable than Paper 1 in terms of timing and accuracy.

## How to check WBJEE Marks 2025

According to authorities, the WBJEE answer key is expected to be released by next Friday, May 2. Candidates will be able to access their results at [**https://wbjeeb.nic.in/**](https://wbjeeb.nic.in/)

### Steps to Download the Answer Key

- Visit the official website of WBJEE
- Next, candidates need to log in to the account using their ID and date of birth.
- To access the answer key, you need to click on the “Submit” button.
- Click on the link for the Answer Key.
- Download the answer key and print it out to calculate your score and for future reference.
- Candidates can also raise objections on the WBJEE answer key, following which the final answer key and result will be announced.

## WBJEE 2025 Answer Key: How To Raise Objections

Candidates can follow the steps below to raise an objection to the answer key of WBJEE 2025.

- Visit the official website of WBJEE:  wbjeeb.nic.in.
- Click on the link for the answer key.
- Log in using the candidate’s application number and password.
- Next, click on the one or more rhan one questions that you want to challenge.
- Pay the fee for challenging the question and submit it.

## WBJEE Scholarships and Financial Aid

WBJEE offers Government and Private scholarships and financial assistance to qualified candidates. Eligible candidates can secure Government scholarships, which are the **Swami Vivekananda Merit-cum-Means Scholarship** and the **Kanyashree Scholarship.**

However, the private scholarships offered to the eligible candidates of WBJEE are the **Reliance Foundation Scholarships** and the **Tata Scholarship Program.**

### 1. ****************What was the difficulty level of WBJEE 2025?****************

The overall difficulty level is reported as moderate. Paper 1 (Mathematics) was said to be much tougher and time-consuming, while Paper 2 (Physics and Chemistry) was more balanced and considered relatively easier.

### 2. ************Which Paper was more difficult in WBJEE 2025 – Paper 1 or Paper 2?************

Paper 1 (Mathematics) was considered to be harder as it had lengthy and tricky questions. Paper 2 (Physics and Chemistry) was easier, especially Chemistry, which had a good number of NCERT-based questions.

### **3. ****Did any sections in WBJEE 2025 have a greater scoring potential than others?******

Yes, Chemistry was considered the most scoring because it had so many direct questions, while Mathematics was the most difficult.

### ********5.******Generally, were there more numerical questions or more theoretical questions in the physics section?**************

A fair combination of numerical and theoretical questions; however, it was slightly more numerical, especially in the areas of Current electricity, Kinematics, and Optics.
