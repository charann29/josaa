## Sathyabama University Anbu Scholarship 2026

The Sathyabama University Anbu Scholarship is a need-based financial assistance scheme offered by Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology to support economically weaker students pursuing undergraduate programmes, mainly in BE and BTech. It helps to offset Sathyabama University’s high tuition fees so that students have easier access to postsecondary education.

The selection of recipients is primarily based on family income and previous academic accomplishments. FN: While all Sathyabama University students interested in applying for Sathyabama University Anbu Scholarships are required to apply for admission to Sathyabama University courses and Anbu Scholarships, all Sathyabama University students applying for admission to Sathyabama University should review Sathyabama University’s Anbu Scholarship eligibility criteria to ensure the inclusion of qualified applicants.

The purpose of Sathyabama University Anbu Scholarships is twofold: to financially support deserving students and to promote good academic standing throughout their educational journey.

## SAEEE Scholarship Scheme

The SAEEE Scholarship Scheme is a financial aid scheme by Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology that is a merit-based scheme. It is designed to assist students who are high performers in academics and wish to pursue their studies at Sathyabama University. Students whose rank is within the 25 top scorers in the SAEEE test receive a 100 per cent scholarship.

The SAEEE entrance exam is administered every year, usually in April or May, to students everywhere in India and is the primary means of securing admission to the engineering and technology undergrad programs of the university. The scholarship is given to the high achievers, reduces the expenses incurred by the meritorious students, and motivates them to pursue their studies in Sathyabama University upon passing the SAEEE test.

Refer to the following to learn about how to pay the Sathyabama University fee.

**Online Payment:**

- Visit the official Sathyabama University portal.
- Log in with your student credentials.
- Select “Fee Payment” and choose your course/semester.
- Pay via net banking, credit card, or debit card.
- Download the fee receipt for records.

**Offline Payment:**

- Visit the university’s finance department.
- Submit a demand draft (DD) or cash for the fee amount.
- Collect the official receipt as proof of payment.
- Important: Make sure to pay before the due date to avoid penalties.

## Sathyabama University Admission

**Check out the following to go through the complete admission process of Sathyabama University Admissions.**

**Entrance Exam:** The Sathyabama All India Engineering Entrance Examination (SAEEE) is the Entrance Examination for Sathyabama University Admission for BTech.

**Eligibility Criteria:** The criteria for eligibility are that the candidate must have completed the level of 10+2 and must have secured a minimum aggregate percentage of 50 in Physics, Mathematics, and one other subject, as is relevant in the present case.

**Apply Online:** The Sathyabama University registration process requires all interested applicants to apply through the official Sathyabama University website, electronically.

**Fill Application Form:** After the registration process, the applicants will be required to fill the application form and will be required to submit supporting documents, a blue certificate of 10th class, a recent photograph, and a signature.

**Pay Fees:** The application fees must be paid during the registration process for the Sathyabama University Admission. The Sathyabama University Fee for application is typically Rs. 500 to Rs. 1200 based on course and academic year.

**Students Selection:** The applicants will be selected on the basis of the SAEEE score or JEE Main scores.

***Note: Those who have qualified JEE Main with a valid rank can directly participate in the Sathyabama University Admission Process. The top scorers, defence officers, and some applicants who have qualified in JEE Main are eligible for scholarships and special consideration provisions.***
