West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination (WBJEEB) is a state-level examination conducted by WBJEEB. It is scheduled for **April 27th, 2025**. Candidates can secure admissions into undergraduate courses like Engineering, Pharmacy, and Architecture. They are advised to look at the participating institutions, which will help them in their admission process. This article will guide you through the various government and private institutions participating in WBJEE.

## WBJEE Participating Colleges

There are numerous colleges in West Bengal that participate in the WBJEE counselling process. These institutions are spread across the state and offer courses in different disciplines such as engineering, pharmacy, and architecture. Let’s take a look at some of the main important colleges in West Bengal that accept [WBJEE](https://collegedost.in/blog/category/wbjee/) scores for admission.
