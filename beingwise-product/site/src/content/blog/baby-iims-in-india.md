## What are These Baby IIMs?

Baby IIMs in India are those IIMs that are established after the older and reputed IIMs. While Baby IIMs are still part of the prestigious IIM family, they are considered “younger” in terms of reputation, infrastructure, and student intake. Baby IIMs in India quickly gained their reputation despite being newer; this happened due to their excellence in delivering world-class education in management and preparing students for future endeavours and shaping them as future managers and capable leaders. Institutes like **IIM Trichy**, **IIM Visakhapatnam**, **IIM** Sirmaur, and **IIM Kashipur** are often referred to as Baby IIMs in India.

## Comparison: New IIMs vs Baby IIMs

The terms New IIMs and Baby IIMs are often used interchangeably, but there are subtle differences. New IIMs were established with the goal of accommodating the growing demand for quality management education in India, while Baby IIMs are typically those that were founded even later, around 2009-2011.

- **New IIMs** tend to have slightly better infrastructure, placements, and alumni networks.
- **Baby IIMs** are still in their growth phase, but are quickly catching up.
