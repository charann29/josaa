## ICFAI University Scholarship Eligibility

ICFAI University offers scholarships to applicants based on the following eligibility criteria:

- Must have scored at least 60% at the 10+2 level exam to be eligible for Merit Scholarships.
- Graduation scores are considered for PG program scholarships.
- A minimum 8 CGPA is necessary to be considered for semester-wise scholarship
- No scholarship is awarded if any candidate has a supplementary exam or disciplinary issues.

Further, students who apply early for admission maximise at the chance of getting the ICFAI University scholarship.

## How to Apply for ICFAI University Scholarship?

To apply for the ICFAI University Scholarship, students need to follow the basic admission procedure, and they get awarded the scholarship automatically based on their eligibility. The following are the steps to apply to ICFAI University:

- Visit the official website of ICFAI University.
- Fill out the online application on the respective campus website.
- Submit the required documents, including the Boards/Graduation marksheet or passing certificates, to claim the merit scholarships.
- Based on the previous academic scores, a merit list is published, and accordingly, students are awarded merit scholarships.

**Key things to remember during ICFAI University Scholarships:**

- Apply early to get early bird registration discounts.
- Candidates belonging to special categories like COVID warriors and student siblings will also receive a certain amount of fee waiver.
- Disbursement of scholarship will reflect in the fee receipts in the form fee waiver.
- Students do not need to fill out any extra form for the scholarship application.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **1. **Is there an ICFAI University entrance exam for scholarship?****

No, students at ICFAI University are provided scholarships based on merit only. No such entrance exam is conducted for the scholarship.

### **2.  **What is the ICFAI University Raipur Scholarship**?**

ICFAI University Raipur Scholarship is a merit scholarship provided based on past academic records and semester-wise performance.

### 3. **What is the ICFAI University Jaipur B.Tech scholarship?**

Students are offered up to INR 1.08 Lakhs fee waiver on ICFAI University Jaipur B.Tech fees as a scholarship. It depends on the previous academic records and semester-wise performance.

### 4. **What is the ICFAI University Scholarship amount?**

Up to INR 1.08 Lakhs scholarship amount is given under the ICFAI University Merit Scholarship, and up to INR 13,500 scholarship amount is given under the semester-wise scholarship

### 5. **Does ICFAI University offers 100% scholarship?**

No, ICFAI University offers fee waivers up to INR 1.08 Lakhs on the total fee amount for engineering branches. It does not guarantee 100% scholarship.

### 6. What are the ICFAI University Raipur Scholarship?

ICFAI University Raipur scholarships are mainly offered based on past qualifying exam merit and semester-wise performance of students.

### 7. Is the ICFAI University Scholarship applicable to all campuses?

Yes, all the campuses offering B.Tech programs under ICFAI University have scholarship offers available.

### 8. What is the Early Bird Scholarship offered by ICFAI University?

ICFAI University offers an Early Bird Scholarship of 10% to students applying early for the admissions.

### 9. What is the minimum CGPA required to secure the ICFAI University scholarship?

For university students, a semester-wise scholarship is provided, where students need to score at least 8.0 CGPA to secure the ICFAI University Scholarship.

### 10. Can students apply for more than one ICFAI University Scholarship?

Yes, students can apply for both merit scholarships at the same time if their semester-wise performance is up to the mark.
