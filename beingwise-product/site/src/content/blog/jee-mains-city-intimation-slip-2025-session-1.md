## **JEE Mains City Intimation Slip 2025 Session 1**

JEE Mains city intimation slip 2025 Session 1 is an official document released by the National Testing Agency (NTA) ahead of the examination. Its primary purpose is to inform candidates about the city where their allotted exam center will be located for JEE Mains 2025. By receiving this information in advance, candidates can plan their travel and accommodation efficiently, reducing last-minute stress and ensuring a timely arrival at the exam venue.

It is important to note that the city intimation slip is **not** the same as the JEE Mains 2025 Admit Card. While the city allotment JEE Mains 2025 reveals the exam city and tentative date, the admit card, which will be released later, contains the complete address of the exam center, exam timings, and additional instructions. Candidates should regularly check the official website for updates and download the city intimation slip as soon as it becomes available.

The JEE Mains 2025 exam city intimation slip is crucial for logistical planning, especially for students travelling from distant locations. It enables candidates to make necessary travel arrangements and prepare for exam day with greater confidence.

## **How to Check JEE Mains City Intimation Slip 2025 Session 1?**

The JEE Mains city intimation slip 2025 session 1 contains the Test city name and Date. The exam centers will be allotted based on the information filled in by the candidate in the application form. The Exam centers that are allotted by the NTA cannot be changed. To download the city intimation slip, the candidate needs to fill in the following information: Application Number, Date of Birth, and Course(B.tech/B.E/B.Arch). The full address of the exam center will be available on the JEE Main [Admit Card](http://blog/jee-main/jee-main-admit-card/).

Here is a step-by-step guide to downloading the city intimation slip.

- Step 1 : Visit the Official Website(<https://jeemain.nta.nic.in/>).
- Step 2 : Link for City Intimation Slip will be available on the official website.
- Step 3 : Click on the Link to check the city details.
- Step 4: The candidate has to enter the Application Number and Date of birth.
- Step 5: Download the City Intimation Slip for Future reference.

## **Common Issues and Solutions With JEE Mains City Intimation Slip 2025 Session 1**

While accessing or interpreting the JEE Main 2025 city intimation slip, candidates may encounter a few common problems. Knowing these issues and their solutions can help you resolve them quickly and avoid unnecessary stress.

1. **Unable to Login or Access the Portal:** Candidates may face difficulty logging in to the portal, often due to entering incorrect application numbers, date of birth, or course details.
**Solution:**Double-check that you are entering the correct application number, date of birth, and course (B.Tech/B.E/B.Arch) exactly as provided during registration. Keep these credentials handy before attempting to log in.

2. **Slow or Unstable Internet Connection:** A slow or unstable internet connection can prevent the slip from loading or cause errors during the download.
**Solution:**Ensure you have a stable and fast internet connection before accessing the portal. Try refreshing the page or switching to a different network if issues persist.

3. **City Intimation Slip Not Displaying/Incomplete Information:** Occasionally, the slip may not display the allotted city or exam date, or the information may appear incomplete.
**Solution:**Wait for a few minutes and try again, as the NTA website may be updating or experiencing high traffic. If the problem continues, clear your browser cache or try accessing the portal from a different device or browser.

4. **Portal Not Responding or Technical Glitches:** The official website may be temporarily down or unresponsive due to heavy traffic after the slip release.
**Solution:**Wait and try to log in later, preferably during off-peak hours. Avoid repeated rapid login attempts, as they may temporarily lock your account.

5. **Forgotten Login Credentials:** If you have forgotten your application number or password, you will not be able to access the slip.
**Solution:**Use the “Forgot Application Number” or “Forgot Password” links on the login page to retrieve your credentials. Follow the instructions provided to reset or recover your details.

6. **Discrepancy or Error in the Slip:** If your slip contains incorrect information (wrong city, date, or personal details), it can cause confusion or travel issues.
**Solution:**Contact the NTA Helpline immediately at 011-40759000 (between 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM) or email the official support address. Keep your application details and supporting documents ready for verification.

7. **Slip Not Available Despite Announcement:** Sometimes, even after the official announcement, your slip may not be visible or available for download.
**Solution:**Check if you have completed all application requirements. If everything is in order, wait a while and try again. If the issue persists, contact the NTA for clarification.

### 1. What does the JEE Mains City Intimation Slip 2025 session 1 contain?

The JEE Mains City Intimation Slip 2025 session 1 contains the Test City name and the Examination Date.

### ************2. ****How to check the complete address of the JEE Mains 2025 exam center?****************

The Candidates can check the complete address of the exam center on the admit card. The Admit Card will contain the center information(including pin code), candidate information, exam details, signatures and photographs, and QR code (for Identification Purposes).

### **3. Does the NTA Provide a Scribe Facility for the JEE Mains 2025 Exam?**

Yes, the NTA provides a Scribe Facility for the JEE Mains 2025 Exam, but the qualification of a scribe must be less than the qualification of the candidate.

### 4. ****What Should A candidate do if He/she is not able to download the Admit card?****

If a candidate is unable to download his/her Admit Card from the website, he/she should approach the NTA Help Line at 011-40759000 between 10.00 A.M. and 05.00 P.M.

### ********5. **Will the JEE Mains 2025 Admit Cards be sent by post?**********

No, The Admit cards for JEE Main will not be sent by post. The Candidates should download the Admit card from the official website and make a Copy for the exam purpose.

### 6. When was the JEE Mains 2025 city intimation slip for Session 1 released?

The JEE Mains 2025 Session 1 city intimation slip released on January 10, 2025, by the NTA. This slip is important as it informs candidates about the city allotted for their exam center, allowing them to plan their travel and accommodation. It can be downloaded from the official website by logging in with the application number and date of birth.

### 7. When is the JEE Mains 2025 Session 1 exam scheduled?

The JEE Mains 2025 Session 1 exam was scheduled from January 22 to January 30, 2025. The exam dates for B.Tech candidates were January 22, 23, 24, 28, and 29, while B.Arch candidates had their exam on January 30, 2025.

### 8. Is the JEE Mains 2025 exam city intimation slip the same as the admit card?

No. The city intimation slip only provides the exam city and schedule. At the same time, the admit card is a compulsory document that includes the full centre address and exam instructions, and you must carry it on exam day.​

### 9. Can I change my exam city after city allotment JEE Mains 2025 is out?

Generally, NTA does not allow changes in exam city after allotment; the city is allotted based on your preferences, availability, and administrative constraints, so you should be prepared to appear in the allotted city.​
