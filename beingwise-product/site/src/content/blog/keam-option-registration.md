**KEAM 2025 Option Registration:** The Centralised Allotment Process (CAP) for Engineering and Pharmacy courses for the 2025-26 academic year has started. During this phase, students can choose their preferred options for Engineering courses in various types of colleges, including Government, Aided, autonomous-aided, Government Cost-Sharing, Private Self-Financing, and Autonomous Self-Financing Engineering Colleges, as well as Government and Self-Financing Pharmacy Colleges.

The candidates who are qualified for admission to Engineering and Pharmacy courses for 2025 can register their choices through the website, [cee.kerala.gov.in](https://www.cee.kerala.gov.in), until **05.08.2025(tentative) 11.00 AM.** Those who do not register within this timeframe will not be considered for allotment under any circumstances.

The candidates can register their options using any computer with internet access within the specified time frame. Those without internet access can use facilities provided for free by the government at various centers. “**Facilitation Centres**” (FC) will be available across the state to help candidates register their options. The list of these centers is on the CEE website. Facilitation Centres will only be operational during the option registration period.

The candidates can find the schedule for the first phase of allotment below:

|  |  |  |

| **1.** | **29.07.2025** | Opening of the website for online option registration |
| **2.** | **05.08.2025, 11.00 AM** | Closure of online option registration facility |
| **3.** | **04.08.2025** | Trial Allotment |
| **4.** | **07.08.2025** | Provisional Allotment Publication |
| **5.** | **08.08.2025** | First Phase Allotment Publication |
| **6.** | **08.08.2025 to 12.08.2025, 4.00 PM** | Candidates who receive an allotment must pay the required fees to the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations, either through online payment or at a Head Post Office. If the fees are not paid by the specified date, candidates will lose their allotment and all current options in that stream. These options will not be available in the next phase. |

The schedule for future allotments will be announced later.
