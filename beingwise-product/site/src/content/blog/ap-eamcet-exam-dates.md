The Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), conducts the AP EAPCET exam every year on behalf of the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE). This test offers admission to Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical courses in various Universities and Colleges across Andhra Pradesh. This article acts as a guide for the candidates appearing for the AP EAPCET 2026 exam. It provides all necessary information regarding the Application Dates, Examination Dates, Releasing of Admit Cards, Answer Key, Results, and Counselling Dates.

The AP EAPCET 2026 authorities have revised the exam dates of the AP EAPCET Exam 2026. The AP EAPCET 2026 exam will be conducted on 12th, 13th, 14th, 15th, 18th, 2026 for Engineering. The exam for the Agriculture and Pharmacy streams will take place on **19th and 20th May 2026**.

## AP EAPCET 2026 Admit Card Date

The AP EAPCET 2026 exam is conducted in the online mode, also known as a Computer-based Test (CBT).

The conducting authorities will issue the Admit Cards from**12th** **May 2026**. The candidates can download the AP EAPCET 2026 Admit Cards through the Official Website, <https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/>. The candidates require some basic credentials like AP EAPCET application or registration number and Date of Birth, to download their Hall Ticket. The AP EAPCET Admit Card is a mandatory document to enter the examination hall.

## AP EAPCET 2026 Answer Key Date

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK) will release the Answer Key for the AP EAPCET 2026 through the Official Website, <https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/>. The tentative release date of the AP EAPCET 2026 Answer Key is 21st May 2026.

A separate answer key will be released for the Agriculture, Engineering, and Medical streams. The key will be released shift-wise of the exams in a PDF format. The candidates can check their respective keys and predict the score of their AP EAPCET 2026 exam.

## AP EAPCET 2026 Results Date

JNTUK will release the Results of the AP EAPCET 2026 through the Official website. The AP EAPCET 2026 result date will be 5th June 2026. The candidates can check their Rank Card by logging in through the official website using their Hall Ticket number and Date of Birth.

## AP EAPCET 2026 Counselling Dates

The Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) will start the Counselling process tentatively in the last week of July or the first week of August 2026. The candidates who are qualified in the AP EAPCET 2026 are eligible to participate in the counselling process.

The Counselling Process includes registration, Counselling Fee, Document Verification, Choosing Web Options, Seat Allotment of the Candidate, and reporting to the College.
