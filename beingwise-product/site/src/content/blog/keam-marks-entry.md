**KEAM Candidate Verification:** The candidates who qualified in the KEAM 2025 were directed to submit the marks obtained in the Second year of Plus Two or equivalent qualifying examination through the website www.cee.kerala.gov.in tentatively up to 03.07.2025, 11.59 PM for the preparation of the **Engineering Rank List 2025.**

Accordingly, the marks data submitted by the candidates have been examined and the data after examination is available on the website [www.cee.kerala.gov.in](http://www.cee.kerala.gov.in) for verification by the candidates. The mark data will be available for verification on the website tentatively until **5.00 pm on 09.07.2025.**

## Steps to verify the submitted Marks data by the candidates

Below are the steps to be followed by the candidates to verify their submitted data:

**Step 1:** Log in to the Candidate home page through **‘KEAM 2025 – Candidate Portal’** on the CEE official website, [www.cee.kerala.gov.in](http://www.cee.kerala.gov.in) by giving your Application Number and Password.

**Step 2:** Find the ‘+2 ‘Marks Verification for Engg’’ Menu to view the marks data submitted by the candidate.

**Step 3:** The candidates can verify their Board of Qualifying Examination, the marks obtained in the second year of the Qualifying Examination for the respective subjects, the Maximum marks, and the Year of Passing to ensure its correctness.

- If the details are correct, click the **‘No Change’** button against each item, and finally click the **‘Verified and Found Correct ’** button.
- If any of the details shown on the web page are incorrect, click the **‘Change Required’** button against the item(s) to be corrected and click the **‘Verified and Found Mismatch ’** button. Such candidates shall have to upload the necessary mark lists as proof for corrections in the submitted marks in a single PDF.
- Those candidates who **edited/entered** the marks on the web page during CEE/152/2025-TA1 I/108219/2025 the submission of marks of the Qualifying Examinations, and have not uploaded all the required Mark lists or who have uploaded defective Mark list will not be able to view and verify their submitted marks. Such candidates shall have to upload all the required mark lists to substantiate the marks submitted by them or have to upload them properly without any defects. Otherwise, the Engineering rank of such candidates will be prepared based on the available mark data, but their rank will be withheld.

## KEAM 2025 Marks Entry

The CEE previously released a notification on 27th June regarding the submission of Plus Two or equivalent qualifying examination marks. The last date for marks submission is extended up to 03.07.2025, 11.59 PM.

Candidates who appeared in the Engineering Entrance Examination 2025 submitted their marks for Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. For those who have not studied Chemistry, marks in Computer Science/Biotechnology/Biology are considered.

Only candidates who qualify for the Entrance Examination and satisfy eligibility conditions will be included in the Engineering Rank List.

## Steps for the Submission of Marks Data

The candidates can follow the below steps to submit their Plus 2 or equivalent qualifying examination marks:

**Step 1:** Log in to the Candidate home page through **‘KEAM 2025 – Candidate Portal’** on the CEE official website, [www.cee.kerala.gov.in](http://www.cee.kerala.gov.in) by giving your Application Number and Password.

**Step 2:** Then, by clicking the Menu **‘Mark Submission for Engg’**, the marks data can be submitted.

The marks submitted by the candidates will be processed after effecting standardization procedure for the preparation of the Engineering Rank List.

## Mark Submission Confirmation Report

After successfully submitting the mark data, the candidates can download the **‘Mark Submission Confirmation Report’**  only after uploading the plus two mark sheet. This confirmation page and Plus Two mark sheet should not be sent to the office of the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations. The engineering rank list will be verified with the original mark list at the time of admission by the admitting authority.
