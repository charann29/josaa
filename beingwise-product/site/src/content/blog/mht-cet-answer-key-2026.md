## How to calculate marks using MHT CET Answer Key?

Following the release of the answer key for MHT CET 2026 by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, students can calculate their score by following the marking scheme. Please note that the score obtained through the MHT CET 2026 answer key will only give you an estimation. There can be some slight difference between the two scores after the official results declaration.

- Download the Official MHT CET Answer Key on the official site
- Match your answers with those in the answer key
- Give yourself 1 mark for every right answer
- There is no negative marking, so marks will not be deducted for wrong answers
- Sum up the marks earned for each right answer to know your estimated score.

## Raise Objection to MHT CET 2026 Provisional Answer Key?

The State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, opens the Objection window facility to challenge the MHT CET provisional answer key after conducting an examination. If any student finds any discrepancies in the MHT CET Provisional Answer Key, they can raise their objection by following the procedure below:

1. Go to the official website of MHT CET (https://mahacet.org/)
2. Click on the “MHT CET Answer Key Challenge” link
3. Sign in with the help of your login ID and password
4. Check the answer key along with your marked answers
5. Choose those questions that you want to challenge and click on the “Challenge” button
6. Add relevant documents in support of their explanation
7. Pay the requisite amount for raising objections against each answer key question
8. Submit your challenge request.
9. After all objections have been examined, the authorities will issue the final MHTCET answer key, along with any required adjustments.
