**KEAM Best Books**: Students preparing for the KEAM 2025 exam must be aware of these best KEAM Preparation books to achieve a maximum score in the examination. The KEAM examination dates are revised by the CEE. The Engineering examination will be held from **05.05.2025 to 09.06.2025** from **10:00 AM to 1:00 PM**.

## Preparation Tips to be followed by the candidate

Below are some preparation tips to be followed by a candidate to score maximum marks in the KEAM 2025 examination:

- Understand the KEAM 2025 examination pattern. The examination authorities have revised the KEAM examination pattern. The candidate can find the new pattern below:

| **Total Marks** | 600 (For Engineering) and  300 (For Pharmacy) |
| --- | --- |
| **Mode** | Computer Based Test (CBT) |
| **Question Type** | Multiple-choice questions (MCQs) with 5 options and one most appropriate answer |
| **Total Number of Questions** | 150 Questions (For Engineering) 75 Questions (For Pharmacy) |
| **Marking Scheme** | +4 Marks are awarded for every correct answer -1 Mark is awarded for every incorrect answer |
| **Exam Duration** | 3 hours (For Engineering)90 min (For Pharmacy) |

- The student should start their exam preparation by knowing the complete [KEAM 2025 Syllabus](https://collegedost.in/blog/keam/keam-syllabus/)
- Plan a proper study schedule based on the [Chapter-wise weightage and important topics](https://collegedost.in/blog/keam/keam-important-chapters-topics/)
- Practice the [Previous Year’s Questions](https://collegedost.in/blog/keam/keam-previous-year-question-papers-solutions-pdfs/) regularly
- Concentrate on the areas of improvement
- Seek Guidance from the experts in case of any doubts.
