The AP EAMCET, now renamed as, Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (EAPCET) is a state-level entrance test conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada, on behalf of the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) every year. This test is a gateway for admission into different undergraduate courses in the fields of Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy across Andhra Pradesh.

## Documents required for AP EAPCET 2025 Application:

Before the **AP EAPCET Application** process starts, the students require the following documents to begin the application process:

- The Hall Ticket Number of Qualifying Examination Number (10+2 or Equivalent).
- Date of Birth Certificate (as mentioned in their SSC).
- Category Certificate (If Applicable).
- Parent’s Income.
- Recent passport-size Photograph (less than 30KB) and Signature (less than 15KB).
- Aadhar Card.
- Email ID and Mobile Number.

All the above documents and details are required for a candidate to start the AP EAPCET 2025 Application process.

## How to Apply for AP EAPCET 2025?

The **AP EAPCET 2025 Application form** can be filled out online through the Official Website. Here is the step-by-step process for filling out the Application form:

**1.** Visit the AP EAPCET Official Website, <https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/>.

**2.** Click on the AP EAPCET 2025 Application Link on the Home page. The application process starts with the Fee Payment.

**Details of Registration Fee:**

|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Stream** | **Registration Fee** | | |
| **OC** | **BC** | **SC/ST** |
| Engineering (E) | Rs. 600 | Rs. 550 | Rs. 500 |
| Agriculture (A) | Rs. 600 | Rs. 550 | Rs. 500 |
| Both E & A | Rs. 1200 | Rs. 1100 | Rs. 1000 |

The Candidate can make the Payment through Net Banking, Credit Card, or Debit Card.

**3.** After completing the Payment, the Candidate needs to start the form-filling process. While filling out the AP EAPCET 2025 Application form they have to provide their personal, academic, and contact details.

**4.** Upload the Candidate’s passport-size photograph and scanned signature in the required format.

**5.** Review all the details, make necessary corrections if needed, and Submit the Application Form.

**6.** After Submission, take a printout of your application form and preserve it carefully.
