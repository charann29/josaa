WBJEE Eligibility Criteria 2026 has been released at wbjeeb.nic.in along with the information brecheure. Candidates planning to appear for **WBJEE 2026** must meet certain eligibility requirements before filling out the application form. These eligibility criteria include academic qualifications, age criteria, domicile requirements, and course-specific eligibility. **As per the eligibility criteria candidates must have passed Class 12 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects**, along with one additional subject such as Chemistry, Computer Science, or Biotechnology. This article discusses the **complete WBJEE eligibility criteria 2026**, including educational qualification, age limit, domicile rules, and reservation policies.

## WBJEE Eligibility Criteria 2026: General Eligibility Criteria

[WBJEE Registration 2026](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-registration-process-2026/) is ongoing. Before proceeding with the application, candidates must satisfy the following basic conditions. For more details related to WBJEE Eligibility Criteria, candidates can refer to the article below:

**Citizenship:** Applicants must be **citizens of India**. Candidates with **OCI (Overseas Citizen of India) status** can also apply, but they will only be eligible for **unreserved seats under the All India quota**.

**Qualifying Examination:** Candidates must have **passed Class 12 (10+2) or an equivalent examination** from a recognised board. Students appearing for the **Class 12 examination in 2026** are also eligible to apply.

**Age Limit:** The **minimum age requirement is 17 years** as of **December 31, 2026**. There is **no upper age limit** for most courses. However, candidates applying for **Marine Engineering** must not exceed **25 years of age as of December 31, 2026**.

## WBJEE Eligibility Criteria 2026: Academic Criteria

Candidates must meet certain academic requirements related to their qualifying examination to be eligible for admission through [WBJEE](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-2026/).

### Minimum Educational Qualification

Candidates must have completed **10+2 or an equivalent examination** with **Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects**.

In addition to these subjects, candidates must have studied one of the following subjects:

- Chemistry
- Computer Science
- Electronics
- Information Technology
- Biology
- Biotechnology
- Informatics Practices
- Technical Vocational Subject
- Agriculture
- Engineering Graphics
- Business Studies or Entrepreneurship

### Minimum Marks Requirement

Candidates must obtain:

- **45% aggregate marks** in Physics, Mathematics, and the third subject
- **40% aggregate marks** for **SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, and PwD candidates**

Candidates must also pass **English with at least 30% marks in Class 12**.

## WBJEE Eligibility Criteria 2026: Course-Wise Criteria

WBJEE Eligibility Criteria 2026 are different across courses. Course-wise, different eligibility criteria have been shared below:

### B.Tech Courses

For admission to engineering programs:

- Candidates must pass **Class 12 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects**.
- They must also have one additional subject, such as Chemistry, Computer Science, Biotechnology, Biology, or a technical vocational subject.
- Minimum marks required:
  - **45% aggregate marks for general category candidates**
  - **40% aggregate marks for reserved category candidates**

Candidates must also pass both **theory and practical examinations** where applicable.

### B.Pharm Courses

For pharmacy courses, candidates must:

- Pass **Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics or Biology**
- Obtain at least:
  - **45% aggregate marks for general category candidates**
  - **40% aggregate marks for reserved category candidates**
- Pass **English with at least 30% marks**

Admission to pharmacy courses is generally based on **WBJEE Physics and Chemistry scores**.

### B.Arch Courses

Candidates applying for architecture courses must:

- Pass **Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics**
- Obtain **50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics combined**
- Secure **50% aggregate marks in Class 12**

Candidates must also **qualify for NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture)** conducted by the Council of Architecture.

### Marine Engineering

For Marine Engineering courses:

- Candidates must secure **60% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics**
- They must also obtain **at least 50% marks in English** either in Class 10 or Class 12
- The **maximum age limit is 25 years** as of December 31, 2026.

## WBJEE  Eligibility Criteria 2026: Domicile Criteria

WBJEE is open to candidates from across India. However, certain seats in West Bengal colleges are reserved for students who qualify as **domiciles of West Bengal**. Meeting the domicile requirement allows candidates to apply for specific state quota and reserved seats during the admission process.

### Conditions to be Considered a West Bengal Domicile

A candidate will be treated as a West Bengal domicile if they meet **any one** of the following conditions:

- The candidate has **lived in West Bengal continuously for at least ten years**, calculated up to **December 31, 2025**.
- The **candidate’s parents are permanent residents of West Bengal** and possess a valid residential address within the state.

### Domicile Certificate Requirement for WBJEE Counselling

Candidates claiming domicile status must present a **valid domicile certificate during the counselling and document verification stage**. The certificate format depends on the basis of eligibility.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Basis of Domicile Eligibility** | **Required Certificate Format** |
| Candidate has stayed in West Bengal for at least 10 consecutive years | Domicile certificate in **Proforma A1 or A2** |
| Parents are permanent residents of West Bengal | Certificate in **Proforma B**, or submission of any **two documents of the parents** such as Voter ID, Aadhaar Card, Passport, or Ration Card |

## WBJEE 2026 Reservation Policy

The reservation policy for WBJEE admissions is mainly applicable to candidates who are domiciled in West Bengal. Students from other states can appear for the examination, but they are generally considered under the General category for seat allotment in most cases. Reservation benefits are implemented in accordance with the rules set by the Government of West Bengal and apply to admissions in government as well as self-financing engineering and professional institutions participating in WBJEE counselling. Candidates claiming reservation must produce valid category certificates issued by the competent authority during the counselling and document verification process. Reserved categories include:

- Scheduled Caste (SC)
- Scheduled Tribe (ST)
- Other Backward Classes (OBC-A and OBC-B)
- Economically Weaker Section (EWS)
- Persons with Disabilities (PwD)
- Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) candidates
- Defence quota seats

Reservation benefits are usually available **only for candidates who are domiciled in West Bengal**. Candidates must provide **valid category certificates issued by the competent authority** during admission.

## WBJEE Eligibility Criteria 2026 for Participating Universities

Apart from the general eligibility requirements set by WBJEEB, some participating universities and institutions have additional academic conditions for admission. These requirements may include higher minimum marks in Class 12, specific subject combinations, or extra qualifying exams for certain courses. Eligibility criteria for some major participating universities have been discussed in details. Let’s have a look:

### **WBJEE Eligibility Criteria for Jadavpur University**

For B.Tech and B.Pharm programs, candidates must:

- Pass Class 12 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
- Obtain minimum aggregate marks in PCM:
  - 60% for General and EWS
  - 54% for OBC-A and OBC-B
  - 45% for SC, ST, and PwD
- Secure the same minimum marks in Mathematics individually according to the category.
- Pass English with at least 30% marks.

For B.Arch courses:

- Candidates must have 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (45% for reserved categories).
- They must also qualify NATA or JEE Main Paper 2, and the score must be valid during admission.

### **WBJEE Eligibility Criteria for Bidhan Chandra Krishi Viswavidyalaya**

For admission to B.Tech in Agricultural Engineering:

- Candidates must pass 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English.
- Individual pass marks are required in both theory and practical examinations.
- Minimum marks required:
  - 50% aggregate for General category
  - 45% for SC, ST, OBC-A, and OBC-B candidates.

### **WBJEE Eligibility Criteria for West Bengal University of Animal and Fishery Sciences**

For B.Tech in Dairy Technology, candidates must:

- Be Indian citizens.
- Be at least 17 years old by December 31 of the admission year.
- Be a domicile of West Bengal.
- Qualify WBJEE 2026.
- Pass 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English with individual pass marks.
- Obtain:
  - 50% aggregate marks in the four subjects
  - 40% for reserved category candidates.

### **WBJEE Eligibility Criteria for Uttar Banga Krishi Viswavidyalaya**

For B.Tech in Agricultural Engineering, candidates must:

- Pass 10+2 with English, Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry or Agriculture.
- Obtain:
  - 45% aggregate marks in the four subjects
  - 40% for SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, and PwD candidates.

### **WBJEE Eligibility Criteria for Aliah University**

Candidates applying for B.Tech programs must:

- Study Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics with at least 100 marks each at the 10+2 level.
- Secure at least 50% marks in PCM combined.
- Pass each subject individually.
- Obtain 60% aggregate marks in Class 12.

### **WBJEE Eligibility Criteria for Techno India University, West Bengal**

For B.Tech admission:

- Candidates must pass 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics or Physics, Mathematics, and Computer Science.
- Minimum 45% marks in the relevant subjects are required.
- Additionally, candidates must secure at least 60% marks in both Class 10 and Class 12 examinations.

### **WBJEE Eligibility Criteria for Adamas University**

Eligibility requirements vary by specialisation. In general:

- Candidates must have Physics and Mathematics along with Chemistry/Biology/Computer Science or related subjects.
- Minimum marks requirement:
  - 60% aggregate for Computer Science and Biotechnology programs
  - 55% aggregate for Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, and Electronics Engineering.

For B.Pharm, candidates must:

- Pass 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology.
- Secure 60% aggregate marks in the qualifying examination.

### **WBJEE Eligibility Criteria for Sister Nivedita University**

For B.Tech programs:

- Candidates must pass 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics along with one additional subject such as Chemistry, Computer Science, Biotechnology, or related fields.
- Minimum marks required:
  - 45% aggregate
  - 40% for reserved category candidates.

For B.Arch courses:

- Candidates must pass 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics.
- Secure 45% aggregate marks (40% for reserved categories).
- They must also qualify NATA or JEE Main Paper 2.

### **WBJEE Eligibility Criteria for Neotia University**

For B.Tech programs:

- Candidates must secure at least 60% marks in Class 12.
- Physics and Mathematics must be mandatory subjects.
- One additional subject, such as Chemistry, Computer Science, Biotechnology, Biology, or Technical Vocational subjects, must also be studied.

Candidates with a three-year diploma in engineering may also be eligible for certain programs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****************What is the WBJEE Eligibility Criteria 2026?****************

The WBJEE Eligibility Criteria 2026 require candidates to pass Class 12 or an equivalent examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with one additional subject such as Chemistry, Computer Science, or Biotechnology. Candidates must also meet the minimum marks requirement, age limit, and other course-specific criteria specified by WBJEEB.

### 2. **What is the minimum age limit for WBJEE 2026?**

As per the WBJEE Eligibility Criteria 2026, candidates must be at least 17 years old as of December 31, 2026. There is no upper age limit for most courses. However, candidates applying for Marine Engineering must not be older than 25 years as of December 31, 2026.

### **3. What are the minimum marks required for WBJEE 2026?**

Candidates must obtain at least 45% aggregate marks in Physics, Mathematics, and the third subject in Class 12. For SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, and PwD candidates, the minimum requirement is 40% aggregate marks.

### 4. ****Is West Bengal domicile mandatory for WBJEE 2026?****

No, candidates from any state in India can appear for WBJEE 2026. However, the domicile requirement applies for certain state quota and reserved seats in West Bengal colleges. Candidates claiming domicile benefits must provide a valid domicile certificate during counselling.

### ********5. **********Can students appearing for Class 12 in 2026 apply for WBJEE 2026?******************

Yes, students who are appearing for the Class 12 examination in 2026 are also eligible to apply for WBJEE 2026. However, they must meet all eligibility requirements and produce the required documents during the admission and counselling process.
