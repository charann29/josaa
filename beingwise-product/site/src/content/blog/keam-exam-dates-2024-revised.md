The KEAM Exam Dates 2024 have been revised by the Commissioner for the Entrance Examinations (CEE). The Engineering examination will be held from **05.05.2024 to 09.06.2024** from **10:00 AM to 1:00 PM**.

KEAM 2024 exam date for the Pharmacy Course has been preponed to **06.06.2024 from 3.30 PM to 5.00 PM.** The candidates appearing for the Pharmacy exam alone should report to the examination venue at 1.00 PM on 6th June.

Earlier, the authorities announced that the examination would be conducted in two slots. Now the slot timings are revised, and the candidates of the engineering examination shall have to report at the examination center at **7:30 AM** and candidates of the pharmacy examination shall have to report at **1:00 PM**.

The Kerala Engineering, Architecture, and Medical (KEAM) is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the Commissioner for the Entrance Examinations (CEE) every year. The Commissioner for Entrance Examination, Kerala has previously announced that the KEAM admit card will be released by their official website CEE Kerala. It will soon be released by the examination authorities. The entrance examination will be conducted as a Computer Based Test (CBT) at selected venues in different sessions as per the given schedule and Time

## KEAM 2024 Admit Card Date

The KEAM 2024 admit card will be available for all registered candidates on the official website [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in). To download the admit cards, candidates must log in to the official website using their application number and password. The KEAM 2024 admit card is scheduled to be released on **May 20th, 2024**.

## KEAM 2024 Answer Key Date

The Kerala CEE releases the answer keys in PDF format for Engineering, Architecture, and Medical exams. Candidates have the option to challenge the answer key by submitting supporting documents along with a fee of 100/- for each question. If accepted, the challenge fee for the questions will be refunded. Following the review of all challenges, the CEE released the final answer key, which candidates can then use to calculate their KEAM results.

## KEAM  Counselling Date

After the KEAM results, candidates can verify their names in the rank list to participate in the KEAM counselling process. Seats are allocated to candidates based on their preferences and the availability of seats in specific colleges and courses. The KEAM 2024 counselling process, known as the Centralized Admission Process, ensures a fair and transparent allocation of seats. While the KEAM Counselling date has not yet been released, once it is available on the official website will be updated here.
