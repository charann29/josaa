The Telangana Engineering Agriculture Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (TS EAPCET) is scheduled to take place from April 29 to May 5, 2025. The officials have announced that the application process for this examination will begin on February 25, 2025. Additionally, the deadline for submitting applications will be April 4, 2025. Candidates need to follow certain exam guidelines to have a smooth experience. One common area of confusion is the dress code. “**There isn’t an official dress code**,” but the following suggestions for both males and females can help you avoid extra security checks and related problems.

## Dress Code Guidelines for Males & Females

Although there is no official guidelines based on the dress code for male and female candidates, candidates can follow a simple dress code mentioned below. Candidates are prohibited to enter the examination hall with metallic items/jewellery. Candidates should review the following guidelines in advance to ensure a smooth exam experience. Below are the detailed dress code instructions for both male and female candidates.

### Dress Code for Males

- Male candidates should avoid wearing clothes that contain metallic elements as they might cause inconvenience during frisking.

- Wearing half-sleeve shirts can give you much comfort while writing exams and try to avoid hooded shirts, shirts with folded sleeves, etc.

- Accessories such as mufflers, caps or masks should be avoided while entering the examination hall.

- Avoid wearing jewellery like rings, bracelets, chains, etc.

- Candidates are not allowed to enter the examination hall with goggles, smart watches, or any similar items.

- Wear slippers or sandals, as wearing shoes can make you prone to regular security checks.

### Dress code for Female

- Female candidates are advised to avoid traditional clothes like sarees as they might waste your time during security checks.

- Female candidates should avoid wearing items containing metal which includes items such as belts, underwired bras, or other similar accessories.

- Opting for a simple T-shirt and jeans, or a top/kurta with leggings, is recommended. Choose clothing made from breathable fabrics like cotton or Khadi for comfort.

- Avoid wearing jewellery like rings, bracelets, earrings, etc.

- Items like hats, scarves, watches, or goggles are prohibited inside the examination hall, so avoid carrying them to the examination hall.

- Pants or shirts with too many pockets should be avoided as it can cause inconvenience during security checks.

- It is advised to avoid Hooded shirts, sweatshirts, and tops with long sleeves.

- Maintain a simple hairstyle and avoid complex hairstyles, as the frisking process may take time during security checks.

## Prohibited Items in Examination Hall for TS-EAMCET 2025

Candidates are prohibited from entering the examination hall with certain exams to ensure a fair examination process and avoid any malpractice and cheating in the examination. Below is the list of items prohibited inside the examination hall:

- Smartwatches
- Water bottle & Eatables
- Papers, Pens, and other stationery items.
- Electronic gadgets like cameras, calculators, etc.
- Metallic Items.

## Exam Day Guidelines for TS-EAMCET 2025

Candidates have to ensure they follow the exam day guidelines for a smooth exam process and avoid any disturbances during the exam:

- Candidates have to reach the examination centre atleast 30 min before the scheduled time.

- Candidates have to carry an ID proof and admit card to the examination hall.

- Candidates must undergo some security checks and are asked to register their fingerprints and photographs.

- Candidates are allotted their seats, and upon sitting, candidates have to log in using the credentials, and in case of any errors, it is advised to follow the invigilator’s advice.

- Any Misbehaviour will not be encouraged and should be avoided which includes conversations, gestures, or disturbances during the examination process.

## TG-EAPCET 2025 Rules and Regulations

- **Arrival:** In order to complete all the security checks, arrive at the exam centre at least 1-2 hours before the scheduled time.

- **Seating:** Follow the instructions of the invigilator and sit in your designated seat. Ensure that changing seats can lead to disqualification.

- **Admit card & ID:** Admit card and Identity proof are to be submitted while entering the examination hall.

- **False Information:** Submitting false information or attempting to participate in multiple sessions will lead to disqualification, and your result will not be announced.

- **Missing the Exam:** The candidate is considered absent if he fails to appear for the exam, and no retest will be conducted for the candidate.

- **Rough work:** Candidates are provided a white paper for rough work, they have to submit the paper before leaving the examination hall.

## Conclusion

Following a simple dress code for TG-EAPCET will ensure a seamless examination process and a smooth examination experience for candidates. Avoid wearing any metallic items and complex clothing. Avoid being anxious and relax after sitting in the designated place. Read the questions carefully and attempt each question with clarity. Avoid carrying items like electronic gadgets to the examination hall.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****Is there any dress code for TG-EAPCET?****

There is no official dress code mentioned for TG-EAPCET.

### 2.****Can I carry a pen and paper for the TG-EAPCET exam?****

No, candidates are not allowed to enter the examination hall with pen and paper. They will be provided for the candidates in the examination hall.

### 3. ****Can I get a free seat with TS-EAMCET?****

Candidates with good scores are eligible for scholarships after TG-EAPCET and can secure a free seat in prestigious colleges.

### 4. ****Can I carry a watch for the TG-EAPCET exam?****

No, candidates are not allowed to carry watches for the TG-EAPCET exam.

### 5. ****Can I take a calculator to the examination centres?****

Electronic gadgets like smartwatches, calculators, and mobile phones are prohibited inside the examination hall.

### 6. What is the TS-EAMCET Exam Date?

The exam date for TS-Eamcet Pharmacy stream students is **April 29th to 30th, 2025** and for Engineering stream students the exam is going to be conducted from **May 2nd to May 5th**.

### 7. Is TS EAMCET Easy?

TS EAMCET is generally considered to be moderately difficult and it is considered to be easier than JEE Main Exam.
