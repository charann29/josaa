The examination process for engineering will commence from **May 21st to May 27th, 2025,** and for **Agriculture/Pharma will begin from April 19th to April 20th.** Candidates can navigate to the official website and register for the exam by paying the application fee.

The tentative dates for the application process are expected to be in **March 2025**. Candidates must go through the application, preparation, and revision stages before the exam. Candidates often feel overwhelmed during the examination process, and candidates are advised to adhere to specific guidelines to ensure a smooth exam experience. This article outlines the necessary exam day instructions, documents required, and instructions to attempt the exam.

## AP-EAPCET Dress Code

Officials have not specified any [Dress code to appear for AP-EAPCET](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/ap-eapcet-dress-code/). However, they are advised to wear simple attire to avoid any disturbances during the security checks. Here are some tips for selecting your dress code:

- Shirts with heavy graphics and designs might attract unnecessary attention, so try to wear plain shirts or T-shirts while appearing for the exam.

- Avoid cargo pants and pants with multiple pockets as they might cause inconvenience during security checks.

- Avoid carrying metallic items or items containing metal as they might cause disturbances while frisking.

- Shoes with thick soles might be subjected to more regular checks, so wear slippers or sandals to avoid those disturbances.

## Exam-day Guidelines for AP-EAPCET

Officials release the examday guidelines which are often instructed for candidates. Candidates have to adhere to these guidelines for the trouble-free examination process. Here are some of the exam day guidelines that candidates can follow:

- Candidates should arrive at the examination centre at least two hours prior to the exam. Latecomers will not be permitted entry into the examination hall.

- Ensure you arrive at the examination center with your admit card and verify all the details on it before proceeding to the exam.

- Candidates have to carry the AP-EAPCET examination hall ticket. Candidates are not allowed to appear for the exam if they fail to provide the hall ticket

- Candidates have to occupy the seats specified for them, and any foul actions, such as trying to change the seats, will lead to termination of the exam and disqualification of the candidate.

- Candidates have to check if the question paper is in English and telugu language.

- Eating, smoking, and any kind of refreshments are prohibited inside the examination hall.

- Make sure to seek the help of the invigilators in case of any technical issues or emergency assistance in the examination hall.

- No candidates are allowed to leave the examination centre without the Superintendent’s or the Invigilator’s permission.

- Candidates must follow the instructions given by the invigilator.

## Exam Instructions for AP-EAPCET

Candidates must adhere to the examination instructions as it might help them avoid any disturbances while recording the responses and technical issues:

- The exam will be commenced strictly at the time mentioned in the hall ticket.

- Candidates have to answer 160 questions divided across three sections i.e. Mathematics-80 questions, Physics & Chemistry 40 questions each in 3 hours.

- Only one correct option is present out of the four choices provided for each question.

- Candidates are provided white papers for rough work and are asked to submit them while leaving the examination hall. Candidates have to write their hall ticket number on the rough paper.

- Any unfair means like malpractice are not encouraged and candidates are punishable as per rules and regulations set by the convener.

- Candidates will be provided with an attendance sheet, which they must sign and provide their left thumb impression in the designated area.
