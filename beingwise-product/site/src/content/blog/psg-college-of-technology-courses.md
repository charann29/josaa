## PSG College of Technology Courses – B.Tech Admission Criteria

PSG College of Technology course admissions for B.Tech are based on Class 12 PCM Marks and TNEA Counselling process. Managed by DoTE Tamil Nadu, students need to apply for the B.Tech Courses available in PSG College of Technology on the [TNEA official website](https://www.tneaonline.org/). Students need to register for the TNEA counselling, and there is no requirement to fill out any separate application form. The B.Tech eligibility criteria and Selection criteria for PSG College of Technology course admissions are as follows:

- **Eligibility Criteria**: Passed Class 12 or any equivalent exam with 50% marks in aggregate with PCM as mandatory subjects.
- **Accepted Entrance Exams**: No Separate Entrance exam is required.
- **Selection Criteria**: Class 12 PCM Marks + TNEA Counselling
- **Seat allotment**: Based on TNEA Ranks, Preferred Branches/College & Categories.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What are the B.Tech courses available at PSG College of Technology?

PSG College of Technology Coimbatore offers B.Tech programs across various specialisations such as Computer Science Engineering (CSE), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Biomedical Engineering, Biotechnology, Electronics, Mechanical Engineering, and several other engineering disciplines.

### 2. What are the B.Tech placement statistics of PSG College of Technology?

PSG College of Technology offers strong placement opportunities for B.Tech students. According to recent placement reports, the highest salary package reached INR 52 LPA, while the average salary package stood at around INR 11 LPA. The placement rate generally ranges between 85% and 90%.

### 3. What are the student reviews and experiences regarding PSG College of Technology courses?

Based on student reviews and experiences, PSG College of Technology B.Tech courses are appreciated for their updated curriculum, supportive faculty members, and excellent placement opportunities. However, some students suggest improvements in older campus infrastructure and mention the academic workload can be demanding.

### 4. Are there diploma courses available at PSG College of Technology for B.Tech students?

No, PSG College of Technology does not offer diploma programs for B.Tech students. The institute mainly offers undergraduate and postgraduate engineering and technology courses.

### 5. What are the campus facilities and infrastructure available at PSG Tech College?

PSG College of Technology provides various campus facilities including departmental laboratories, hostels, a central library, cafeteria, guest rooms, medical facilities, auditoriums, sports infrastructure, and student activity centres.
