## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1.  Can the NTA(National Testing Agency) allot the center manually?

No, the NTA will not allot the centers manually. The center allotment is done based on the exam center choices made by the candidate during the registration process.

### 2. Can a Candidate change the exam center?

Candidates cannot change the exam center after the Admit card is released. However, candidates can correct the exam center selection when the application correction window opens.

### 3. What is JEE Main Practice Center?

NTA has created “NTA JEE Main 2025 Practice centers” to raise awareness among the students for the examination’s CBT mode.
