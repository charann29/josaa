The most awaited TNEA Rank List 2024 has been released by the Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE), Chennai, marking a pivotal moment for aspiring engineers across Tamil Nadu. Published on July 10, 2024, at the official website [www.tneaonline.org](http://www.tneaonline.org), this comprehensive list categorises candidates based on their academic performance in crucial subjects like Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.

For many students, the TNEA Rank List 2024 represents not just a placement but a significant milestone toward their career aspirations in engineering. It opens doors to premier institutions known for their academic excellence and robust engineering programs.

## TNEA Rank List 2024

The TNEA 2024 rank list is prepared using a normalisation method, ensuring fairness in the evaluation process. It converts each candidate’s Class 12th marks into a standardised scale of 200 marks. Mathematics contributes 100 marks, while the scores of Physics and Chemistry are 50 marks each, thereby providing a balanced assessment criterion for all applicants.

## How to Check the TNEA Rank List?

Candidates can check and download their TNEA Rank List 2024 by following these simple steps:

**Step 1:** Visit the Official Website, [www.tneaonline.org](http://www.tneaonline.org)

**Step 2:** Click on the **‘Rank Details’** link on the left side of the homepage.

**Step 3:** Use your registered email ID and password to access the login portal.

**Step 4:** Upon successful login, the TNEA Rank List 2024 in PDF format will be displayed. Download and print multiple copies for future reference.

**Note:** You can also download the TNEA Rank List 2024 without logging in to the official website from below.

## Grievance Redressal and Counselling Process

Candidates who have any concerns or discrepancies regarding their rank in the TNEA Rank List 2024 can raise objections from July 11 to July 18, 2024. This period allows applicants to rectify any issues promptly through the official website.

## Counselling Schedule for TNEA 2024

The [TNEA Counselling](https://collegedost.in/blog/tnea/tnea-counselling/) process for 2024 will commence shortly after the grievance redressal period. It is a crucial phase where candidates will be able to select their preferred colleges and engineering courses based on their ranks and availability of seats. Here’s an overview of the counselling schedule:

**Special Reservation Categories:** Counselling for Differently Abled, Ex-Servicemen, and Sports categories will be held online from July 22 to July 27, 2024.

**General Counselling:** Academic, Government School (7.5% Category), and Vocational counselling will take place online from July 29 to September 3, 2024.

**Supplementary and SC Counselling:** Supplementary counselling is scheduled from September 6 to September 8, 2024, followed by SCA to SC counselling from September 10 to September 11, 2024.
