## **JEE Main 2026 Registration: Why NTA Needs CBSE Registration Number?**

The National Testing Agency introduced this rule to make the **registration and verification process smoother for everyone**. In previous years, many students faced issues because their details in the JEE Main form, board exams, and counselling records didn’t match. These small errors often caused confusion, delays, and extra verification steps.

To fix this issue, **NTA now asks students to enter their Class 11 CBSE Registration Number while filling out the JEE Main 2026 form.** This number **helps create a single verified record that stays the same from Class 11 to JEE Main and final admissions.**

### **How Will This Change Impact JEE Main Registration 2026?**

- It helps **stop duplicate or fake applications** by linking each JEE form with an official CBSE record.
- It ensures that **your details stay consistent** across schools, CBSE, NTA, and JoSAA counselling.
- It makes **document verification quicker and simpler** during the admission process.
- It helps **prevent any clash** in dates between CBSE Class 12 board exams and JEE Main sessions.

As per an official update on October 30, 2025, CBSE stated that NTA added this step to make verification more transparent, accurate, and student-friendly. Schools have also been told to share Class 11 registration numbers with students applying for JEE Main 2026 so they can fill in their details correctly.

## **How to Get CBSE Registration Number?**

You can get your CBSE Registration Number in various school documents. A list of all the documents will be shared below:

- CBSE Class 11 Registration Slip
- Your Class 11 Admit Card
- Your school ID Card

Apart from these, you will also find their class 11 CBSE Registration Number in the school’s online student portal and the school’s office as well.

## **How to Enter CBSE Registration Number in JEE Main 2026 Registration Form?**

Following these simple steps below, you can easily enter your CBSE Registration Number in JEE Main 2026 Registration Form.

- Go to the official JEE Main website at jeemain.nta.ac.in.
- Click on “New Registration” to start filling out the form
- Enter your basic details such as name, date of birth, and parents’ names.
- When asked, type in your Class 11 CBSE Registration Number carefully.
- Fill in your Class 12 academic information.
- Upload the required documents and pay the application fee online.
- Finally, submit the form and download the confirmation page for future reference.

## Steps to apply for JEE Mains 2026 Registration

JEE Main Registration 2026 window is active now. You can fill out the application form following the simple steps below:

- **Step 1: Registration**
  - Visit the official website at jeemain.nta.nic.in.
  - Next, click on ‘New Registration’ and enter the required details and create a password of your choice.
  - After successful registration, you’ll receive a **system-generated JEE Main 2026 application number**.
    [**Direct Link to Register for JEE Main 2026**](https://examinationservices.nic.in/JeeMainx2026/Registration/frmRegistration.aspx)

- **Step 2: Fill Application Form**
  - Log in using your application number and password.
  - Fill in your personal, academic, and contact details carefully.
  - Select your **preferred exam centres**.
  - Capture a **live photo** using your webcam or mobile by scanning the QR code provided.
- **Step 3: Upload Documents**
  - Upload the required documents, including:
    - Live photo
    - Scanned passport-size photograph
    - Scanned signature
    - Scanned copy of ID proof (if you’re not verifying through Aadhaar or DigiLocker)
- **Step 4: Pay Application Fee**
  - Make the payment using **Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, or UPI**.
  - Ensure the payment is successful before proceeding.
- **Step 5: Download Acknowledgement Form**
  - After successful payment, download the **acknowledgement page** generated on the screen.
  - Keep this document safe for **future reference**.

## **Conclusion**

The new requirement of entering the Class 11 CBSE Registration Number in the JEE Main 2026 registration form is to make the entire process smoother for students. By linking school and exam records early, NTA aims to reduce verification delays, mismatch in data, and avoid clashes between CBSE board exams and JEE Main sessions. Students are advised to collect their Class 11 registration number from their schools before starting the form and double-check all details before submission.
