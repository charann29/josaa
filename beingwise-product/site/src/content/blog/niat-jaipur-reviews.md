## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q**1. What makes NIAT different from traditional engineering colleges in Jaipur?**

NIAT is not a standalone university but rather focuses on industry-relevant skills through practical projects, mentorship from professionals at top tech companies, and partnerships with 3000+ hiring companies. It doesn’t focus on heavy theory books and rote learning, which is a major differentiation from traditional learning models.

### Q**2. What is the fee structure for NIAT in Jaipur?**

The NIAT Upskilling Program in Jaipur total fee, which includes upskilling program fee and VGU course fees to be INR 8-18 lakhs total (INR 2-4.5 lakhs per year).

### Q**3. Is the NIAT Upskilling Centre in Jaipur accredited?**

NIAT in Jaipur is not an Institute or a standalone University. However, it is endorsed by NSDC, NASSCOM, and Startup India. Moreover, students receive UGC-recognised, AICTE-approved B.Tech degrees from Vivekananda Global University, along with industry-ready certificates from NIAT.

### Q4. What are the VGU on-campus hostel facilities like?

Students describe VGU on-campus hostels as clean and comfortable with basic amenities, though some note they are located at a distance from the main campus.

### Q5. What does NIAT mean?

NIAT is an acronym that stands for NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies. It is an upskilling program collaborating with 25+ universities across India and widely recognised for its high-quality training, practical learning for specialisations like AI & ML, Data Science, Cyber Security, with hands-on experience. NIAT has received global recognition and collaborated with prestigious institutions and universities all over India. Additionally, NxtWave has earned endorsements from NSDC, NASSCOM, Startup India, and recently collaborated with OpenAI Academy.

### Q6. I’m confused. Do I pay the fees to the Vivekananda Institute or NIAT?

NIAT has collaborated with Vivekananda Global University in Jaipur, which will collect fees for their B.Tech degree programs, which may include tuition fees, examination fees, library fees, and other academic-related fees. The university sets, collects, and manages these fees.
NIAT charges program fees for its high-quality training, practical learning, and industry-relevant skills for specialisations like Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Data Science, Cyber Security, with hands-on experience.

### Q7. How can I get into NIAT Upskilling Program in Jaipur provided by the VGU?

If a student wants to enrol for the upcoming program, they need to qualify for the NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test) followed by a counselling session. Final admission depends on VGU admission criteria. Once all is done, the student will be enrolled in NIAT’s upskilling program in Jaipur along with a BTech CSE degree from VGU.

### Q8. Are students satisfied with the infrastructure provided in Vivekananda Global University?

Yes, most students report positive experiences with infrastructure such as smart classrooms, computer labs, libraries, and campus facilities. However, some reviews mention that certain facilities, like sports areas, may still be improving.

### Q9. What support does NIAT provide besides academics?

Students receive mentorship, soft-skills training, career counselling, coding practice sessions, and access to tech communities and clubs, which help in overall personality and career development.

### Q10. How good is Vivekananda Global University for BTech CSE?

Vivekananda Global University is generally viewed as a reasonable private university choice for BTech CSE, especially for students who want exposure to industry projects and internship opportunities. However, compared to top-ranked institutions, its placement outcomes may not be as strong, and results often depend on individual effort and skills.

### Q11. What is the NIAT Jaipur average package CSE?

NIAT Jaipur average package is around INR 35 LPA. Though there is no information about campus-wise placement data for NIAT. However, the highest package of NIAT recorded is INR 80 LPA.

### Q12. Can I join the NIAT University?

NIAT is not a University or College; it is a Training Centre which provides upskilling programs in collaboration with UGC approved BTech colleges.
