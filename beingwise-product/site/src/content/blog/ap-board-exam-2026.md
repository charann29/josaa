## Table of Contents

AP Board Exam 2026 for AP Intermediate 1st Year and AP
Intermediate 2nd Year (Class XII) is all set to be conducted
from February 2026.  The AP Board exam 2026 is organised
by the Board of Intermediate Education, Andhra Pradesh, or
“BIEAP” annually. The public examinations for the AP Inter
Exam will take place between February and March 2026. The
first year of study will include written examinations from the
23rd of February through to the 24th of March 2026.

And the second year of study will include written examinations
from the 24th of February through the 23rd of March 2026. This
schedule has been published and can be found on the Board of
Intermediate Education Andhra Pradesh (BIEAP) website at
bie.ap.gov.in. Students are required to download their admit
cards from the BIEAP website and prepare for their
examinations by studying the syllabus for AP Inter 2026
provided by BIEAP.

After completing their examinations and when the results of
the AP Intermediate Exam are announced, students will be able
to view their results on the BIEAP website. Students may refer
to the following to check out more information on the 1st and
2nd year exams conducted by the Board of Intermediate
Education Andhra Pradesh.

## Board of Intermediate Education Andhra Pradesh Hall Ticket

The AP Inter Hall Ticket 2026 is an important document that is
published by the Board of Intermediate Education Andhra
Pradesh (BIEAP) regarding all AP Inter 1st Year students as
well as all AP Inter 2nd Year students. It is required to be
in attendance at the Andhra Pradesh Board of Intermediate
Education Year 2 Exam as well as first-year examinations
because your roll number, exam venue, and timetable are
mentioned in this document.

Candidates will be unable to sit in the exam hall without this
document because this document is important because results of
BIEAP are based on this exam. Refer to the following to know
how to download the Board of Intermediate Education, Andhra
Pradesh Hall Ticket.

- Visit the official BIEAP website.
- Click on the AP Inter Hall Ticket 2026 link for 1st or
  2nd-year students.
- Enter your AP Inter 1st Year or AP Inter 2nd Year
  credentials
- Submit the details to view your hall ticket.
- Download and take a printout to carry to the examination
  centre.

## AP Inter Exam Guidelines (11th and 12th)

Refer to the following to check out some of the most important
exam guidelines for the AP Board exam 2026.

- Bring the AP Inter 1st Year or AP Inter 2nd Year hall
  ticket; get the hall ticket by downloading it using the
  Board of Intermediate Education Andhra Pradesh Hall Ticket
  Download link on the official website.
- Applicants must carry the AP Inter Hall Ticket 2026 to enter
  the exam room.
- Follow the AP Board Exam 2026 schedule and check the AP
  Inter Dates 2026 to know the exact day of your exams.
- Arrive at the exam centre 30 to 45 minutes early for
  verification.
- Bring only necessary stationery and your admit card; mobiles
  and electronic devices are not allowed.
- Depending on your year, the exam will be based on the AP
  Board 11th Syllabus 2026 or the AP Board 12th Syllabus.
- Be respectful to the instructions of invigilators and
  strictly follow the rules of the exam hall.
- For any clarifications, visit the BIEAP / Board of
  Intermediate Education Andhra Pradesh BIEAP official
  announcements.
- The AP Inter Results will be published on the Board of
  Intermediate Education Andhra Pradesh results portal after
  the exams.
- Ensure proper discipline during the AP SSC and AP
  Intermediate exams.
