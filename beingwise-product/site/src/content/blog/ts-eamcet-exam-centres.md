The Telangana State Council of Higher Education has released the TS EAMCET 2024 test zones along with the areas on the official website [eapcet.tsche.ac.in](http://eapcet.tsche.ac.in)**.** Candidates planning to appear for EAMCET are advised to thoroughly review the exam centres before filling out the application form.

Candidates will be allotted to those exam centres based on their choice preferred during the TS EAMCET 2024 exam registration. The details regarding the test centres will be mentioned on the TS EAMCET admit card 2024.

The TS EAPCET exam is for candidates seeking admission to Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy programs in Telangana. The EAMCET 2024 exam is conducted by Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTUH) on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE).

The **TS EAMCET 2024 exam** is scheduled to be held from **May 9th to May 12th, 2024**.

## Guidelines for TS EAMCET Exam Centres

Candidates must follow these guidelines before filling out the application form. Here are the instructions provided for TS EAMCET/EAPCET exam centers:

- The exam will be conducted in 21 test zones over Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. It consists of 4 test zones in Hyderabad, 12 test zones in Telangana states, and 5 test zones in Andhra Pradesh.
- Hyderabad is divided into four zones to make it easier for candidates to take the TS EAPCET 2024 in nearby areas without any difficulty.
- Candidates must visit a day before the exam centers. To be aware of the exam centre and to avoid last-moment hurries
- Candidates can check the nearest center from the above-mentioned list to take the exam.

- When filling out the TS EAPCET-2024 Application form online, candidates should choose the zones they prefer. Once chosen, requests to change zones later will not be accepted.
- The Convener may add or remove some test centres from the list of notified zones.
- The Convener has the authority to assign candidates to any zone, regardless of the zone that candidates have chosen.
- Candidates should only submit one application for either the ‘E’ or ‘A & P’ or ‘E & A & P’ category test. If a candidate submits multiple applications for the same category, the Convener may reject all applications or accept only one of them.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. How do I choose an exam center for TS EAMCET/EAPCET 2024?

Candidates can select their preferred exam center for TS EAMCET 2024 while filling out the application form.

### 2. Can I change my TS EAMCET/EAPCET exam center?

No, once the application form is submitted candidates cannot change their TS EAMCET/EAPCET exam center. Therefore, candidates are advised to carefully choose their preferred exam center before submitting the application form.

### 3. What time should I arrive at the TS EAMCET exam center?

Candidates are required to arrive at the TS EAMCET exam center at least 1 hour before the exam begins to avoid any last-minute rush.
