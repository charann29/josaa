## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q**1. What is the total NIAT college fees?**

NIAT is not a college or university, so it doesn’t have a NIAT college fee structure. However, it takes the upskilling fees. So, the total NIAT fees for both the upskilling programs, along with the BTech CSE from the collaborating University, are in the range of INR 8 – 18 Lakhs (INR 2 – 4.5 Lakhs per annum).

### Q**2. What kind of industry exposure can students expect from NIAT in Chennai?**

Students get hands-on projects, internship opportunities, and guidance from mentors with industry experience. There is collaboration with over 3000 companies.

### Q6. What role does the NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT) play?

The NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT) helps assess a student’s readiness for the upskilling program. It is separate from any university admission process. Students need to pay a one-time non-refundable registration fee of INR 1200.

### Q7. Where is the NIAT Upskilling Program in Chennai conducted?

NIAT in Chennai conducts its upskilling programs in training centres or centres of excellence provided by collaborating universities, such as AMET University and BS Abdur Rehman Crescent.

NIAT is an acronym that stands for NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies.
