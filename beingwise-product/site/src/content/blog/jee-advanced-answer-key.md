## JEE Advanced Exam Details

JEE Advanced is the second phase of the exam which is a crucial stage for admissions into IITs. The top 2,50,000 candidates who qualified for JEE Mains are eligible to appear for JEE Advanced. JEE Advanced exam is an undergraduate-level exam offering admissions into various courses like b.tech to architecture. The frequency of the exam is once a year and is conducted in online mode, i.e Computer Based Test.

## How to Raise Objections & Challenge the Answer Key

The authorities will offer the option to challenge the provisional JEE Advanced 2025 answer key through the official website. Candidates who wish to raise objections against any answers must follow the steps provided below.

This process allows candidates to review and contest the accuracy of the answer key.

- Access the official website of JEE advanced (jeeadv.ac.in)
- Access the candidate portal on the official website.
- Provide details like registration number, mobile number and DOB.
- To challenge for the JEE advanced answer key click on the link provided.
- Select the number to be challenged.
- Submit your objections along with relevant supporting documents.
