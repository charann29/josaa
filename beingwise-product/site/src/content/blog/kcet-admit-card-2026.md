## How to Download KCET Admit Card 2026?

The KCET hall ticket download link will be activated on the official website in the second week of April. Candidates can follow the steps below to download their KCET 2026 admit card.

### Steps to Download KCET Hall Ticket 2026

1. Visit the official website at cetonline.karnataka.gov.in.
2. Click on the link for Download KCET Admit Card.
3. Enter your application number and password in the login window.
4. Click on the submit button.
5. The KCET hall ticket will appear on the screen.
6. Download the admit card and take multiple printouts for future reference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. How to check KCET admit card?

Candidates can download the KCET admit card from the official website, cetonline.karnataka.gov.in. First, visit the website and click on the KCET admit card link. Then enter your application number and password in the login window. After submitting the details, the hall ticket will appear on the screen. Download the admit card and take a printout for the exam day.

### 2.   Is  KCET admit card released​?

Yes, the KCET 2026 admit card has been released on April 10, 2026, by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA). Candidates who have successfully completed the KCET registration process will be able to download their hall ticket online from the official website.

### 3. What details are mentioned on the KCET admit card 2026?

The KCET admit card contains important information such as the candidate’s name, registration number, photograph, exam date and time, exam centre address, subject appearing for, and exam day instructions. Candidates must check all details carefully after downloading the hall ticket.

### 4.  What documents should I carry with the KCET admit card?

Candidates must carry a printed copy of the KCET admit card along with a valid photo ID proof such as Aadhaar card, PAN card, or driving licence to the exam centre. Without these documents, candidates may not be allowed to enter the examination hall.

### 5. What should I do if there is an error in my KCET admit card?

If candidates find any mistake in their KCET admit card, such as incorrect personal details or exam centre information, they should immediately contact the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) through the official website to get the issue corrected before the exam.
