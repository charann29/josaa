## Amrita Entrance Exam – Engineering (AEEE Exam)

Amrita Entrance Exam – Engineering, also known as AEEE Exam, is the B.Tech entrance cum scholarship exam conducted by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University. It is a computer-based test featuring 110 MCQ-type questions covering Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English, and Quantitative Aptitude. Students need to answer the 330-mark paper in 2 hours and 30 minutes. Students need to register through the Amrita online portal to register for the exams. Students need to fulfil the general Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham course admissions B.Tech eligibility criteria to appear for this exam. JEE Main qualified students can also apply for the AEEE exam.

## Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Course Admissions For B.Tech – Application Process

To apply for the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Course admissions for B.Tech, students need to visit the online portal of Amrita University. Students appearing for the AEEE Exam or the JEE Main qualified students need to follow the steps given below for Amrita University admissions:

- Register Yourself at the [Amrita University Official Website.](http://aeee.amrita.edu/)
- Use your name, email ID, and phone number For Registration.
- Verify the mobile number and generate login credentials.
- Once credentials are generated, fill out the application form with all the required personal and academic details.
- Students get to select whether they will opt for the AEEE or the JEE Main exam, followed by the AEEE Exam centre choice.
- Submit the application form.
- Upload the required documents for the application.
- Pay the application fees as per the AEEE Exam fee guidelines. JEE Main candidates will also have to pay a certain application fee. Check the following Amrita University admission Application fees:

## Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Course Admissions For B.Tech – Merit Scholarship Schemes & Fees

Students applying for the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Course Admissions for B.Tech can also avail of merit scholarship schemes and need-based scholarship schemes. Even financial aid is available for students. The merit scholarship schemes consist of the Amrita Chancellor Scholarship and the Amrita Meritorious Scholarship. Students are mainly awarded these scholarships based on their academic merit and AEEE Ranks. Let’s check the scholarship criteria for both schemes:

- **Amrita Chancellor Scholarship**: Up to 100% tuition Fee waiver is awarded under this scheme. Students need to secured 80% & above in academic merit and entrance exam ranks. Top AEEE rank holders are also eligible for this waiver.
- **Amrita Meritorious Scholarship**: Up to 50% tuition fee waiver is awarded under the respective merit scholarship. Students scoring 70% to 79% in the last qualifying exam and having a good AEEE rank are eligible.

Both of the schemes are valid for 1 year, and the renewals depend on the semester performance of students. To keep the scholarships, students must score 7.0 CGPA  and have 75% attendance. Backward category students and candidates from low-income families can also opt for need-based scholarships. As per the AEEE ranks, the scholarship fees are also determined. Students with the highest ranks get the Slab 1 Fees, while lower ranks need to scholarship 2 or 3 fees. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **How are the placement statistics of Amrita University?**

Amrita University provides a placement training cell which offers students proper career guidance, CV building sessions, mock interview sessions, and job assistance services. As per the latest campus placement report, the B.Tech department secured INR 80.04 LPA as the highest salary package. While the average package secured is INR 9.08 LPA. Even the placement rates have gone up to 92%.

### 2. **How are B.Tech seats allocated during the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham course admissions?**

Seat Allocation for the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham course admissions for B.Tech is conducted by the CSAP counselling process. Students need to register for the Amrita University CSAP counselling process through the online website.

### 3. **How to get admission in Amrita University for B.Tech?**

Students can get admission at Amrita University for B.Tech only based on merit. The 
Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham admission for B.Tech is conducted through the AEEE Entrance exam or JEE Main entrance. Selected students then need to go through the CSAP counselling process conducted by Amrita University for seat allotment.

### 4. **Is B.Tech lateral entry available for the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Coimbatore Admission?**

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Coimbatore or any other Amrita University campus does not offer B.Tech lateral entry.

### 5. **Can I get direct admission for the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham course admissions for B.Tech?**

No, Direct admissions are also not available for B.Tech applicants at Amrita University.

### 6. **How to get admission in Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham for International candidates?**

International candidates are exempted from the AEEE or JEE Main exam, and only need to have passed the 10+2 level exam with PCM with at least 60% marks in aggregate.
