## Course Overview of the Specialisations Offered at MREC

Malla Reddy Engineering College offers three major specialisations in the Computer Science Domain. Students can check the details of the course below:

### Course Overview of B.Tech CSE

BTech in CSE at Malla Reddy Engineering College is a comprehensive eight-semester program designed to equip students with foundational and advanced knowledge. The CSE course at Malla Reddy Engineering College begins with a focus on fundamentals of programming languages such as C, Python, and hands-on workshops etc. Students will learn the core computing skills with subjects like data structures, Java, computer organisation, etc, in the second year.

In the third and fourth year, the focus will be primarily on the projects and introducing them to the specialised domains such as AI, machine learning, etc. Students will be focusing more on applying the learned concepts and building projects using those concepts. With internships, projects, skill development labs, MREC ensures that students are well-equipped for careers in the tech industry or higher studies.

### Course Overview of B.Tech CSE (AIML)

Malla Reddy Engineering College offers Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning specialisation in CSE. This course follows a typical eight-semester approach and focuses primarily on the fundamentals in the first and second years. With a focus on multi-disciplinary learning, outcome-based education. Third and fourth years are focused on projects and encouraging real-world application of knowledge. The curriculum structure aligns with NEP 2020 and the AICTE model curriculum, giving students the edge to pursue higher education, entrepreneurship, or top-tier placements.

### Course Overview of B.Tech CSE (Data Science)

Malla Reddy Engineering College offers B.Tech in CSE (Data Science), which is designed to take students step-by-step from basics to advanced skills over eight semesters. In the early years, students learn foundation subjects like maths, physics, and basic engineering. As they move forward, they take up more specialised subjects, choose electives based on their interests, and work on mini-projects and internships. Each semester includes a mix of theory, labs, and hands-on activities, with clear weightage in marks and credits. For example, the first semester has 21 credits and 800 marks, while the final semester, which focuses mostly on real-time project work and internships, has 20 credits and 400 marks. The goal is to ensure students don’t just learn concepts but also know how to apply them in the real world.

## Eligibility & Admission Process for BTech in CSE at Malla Reddy Engineering College

Malla Reddy Engineering College offers admissions for students based on the state entrance examination, TS EAPCET, and eligible candidates can also apply for the Category-B/management quota seats. Check the MREC eligibility criteria below:

- Candidates who are allotted admissions for Category-A seats in TG EAPCET counselling are eligible for admission into the four-year B.Tech degree at MREC.

- Candidates who have qualified for class 12th and applied for the MREC admissions are offered admission to the B.Tech courses based on the merit of the candidate.

- Candidates who have completed a diploma are eligible for lateral entry through the ECET exam and counselling processes.
