### 2. NIAT Tirupati

NIAT Tirupati is a collaborative education program offered in partnership with Annamacharya Institute of Technology & Sciences that provides a UGC-recognized and AICTE-approved B.Tech degree in Computer Science Engineering (CSE). Admission eligibility requires passing 10+2 with a minimum of 50% in PCM and clearing the NAT entrance exam. The fee ranges from ₹2.25 Lakhs to ₹3 Lakhs for the course duration.

| AICTE/UGC approved courses | ₹ 2.25 Lakhs – ₹ 3 Lakhs | NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test) | 10+2 with 50% | BTech in CSE | NA |

**Admission Process:**

1. Submit the NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test) Application Form online on the official NIAT website with personal details and pay a non-refundable registration fee of ₹1200.
2. Appear for the NAT exam, a 90-minute test assessing psychometric, critical thinking, and mathematics skills.
3. If qualified, attend a one-on-one counselling session to discuss career goals and preferences.
4. Based on the counselling, apply to Annamacharya University collaborating with NIAT Tirupati, which will process admission as per its eligibility criteria and policies under UGC and AICTE norms.

**Documents Required:**

Admission to NIAT Tirupati is through the Annamacharya University. After qualifying for the counselling round, candidates must carry the specified documents at the time of reporting for admission and document verification. These documents are standard for first-year B.Tech admissions at Annamacharya University, where NIAT upskilling programs are conducted.

- Aadhar card or other ID proof
- Recent passport-size photographs
- Class 10th and 12th marksheets and certificates
- Migration certificate
- Transfer certificate
- Caste certificate (if applicable)
- Entrance exam scorecard (NAT result)
