### How to Prepare For CBSE Class 12 Practical Exam?

To prepare for CBSE practicals effectively for Class 12, follow these key pointers:

- Prepare your lab notebook/record/project file thoroughly, ensuring it is neat, well-organized, and includes clear diagrams with proper labels. Revise this file regularly to gain confidence.
- Practice all the experiments listed in the CBSE syllabus repeatedly. Understand the step-by-step procedures and the scientific concepts behind each experiment rather than just memorizing steps.
- Strengthen your theoretical understanding, especially for viva questions. Be prepared to explain the concepts, objectives, and outcomes of experiments clearly.
- Develop a revision plan for the final week, including mock practical sessions and revising important questions and experimental procedures with peers to boost readiness.
- On the exam day, maintain a calm mind, check that all materials and files are ready, and manage time effectively during the practical exam to reduce anxiety and perform confidently.
- Pay attention to the official exam structure comprising performing experiments/projects, observation and recording in the practical notebook, and viva-voce.
- Avoid common mistakes such as sloppy handwriting, unclear diagrams, and lack of understanding of the underlying theory behind the experiments.
- Seek clarifications from teachers if any doubts arise, collaborate with classmates for discussion and mock practices, and review previous years’ practical exam questions for better preparation.
