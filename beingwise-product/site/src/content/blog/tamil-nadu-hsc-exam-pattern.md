As per the Tamil Nadu HSC Exam pattern, the subject-wise mark pattern is similar for all the core subjects except the vocational subjects. Students are marked on a scale of 100 for all the exams. It includes 70 marks for the theory paper, 20 for the practicals, and 10 marks for the internal assessments.

### 2.  What is the total passing mark of TN HSC?

For TN HSC exams, the total passing mark is 35 out of 100.

### 3. Where can I find the Tamil Nadu HSC Exam pattern?

Students can access the Tamil Nadu 12th exam pattern on the official website of DGE.

### 4.  Is the Tamil Nadu State Board 12th total marks 500 or 600?

Tamil Nadu State Board 12th total marks are 600. Each of the 6 subjects carries 100 marks.

### 5. Am I allowed to take a calculator to the Tamil Nadu HSC Exam?

No, students are not allowed to carry a calculator for the Tamil Nadu 12th Exam.

### 6. What are the dont’s during the Tamil Nadu HSC Exam 2026?

Students appearing for the TN HSC Exams need to avoid using or carrying electronic gadgets and unauthorized materials. Students are to strictly avoid indulging in malpractice or cheating.

### 7. What are the essentials to carry during Tamil Nadu HSC Exam?

Students need to absolutely carry their TN 12th Hall ticket, students id, and stationery items for the Tamil Nadu HSC Exam.
