## Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai  Admission For B.Tech Programs

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai, B.Tech admission for 2026 is ongoing. The admission for B.Tech programs is primarily based on merit through the national-level entrance exams like JEE Main or AEEE. Check the detailed B.Tech eligibility criteria, selection process, and application process of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai B.Tech admission in the following section.

### Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

- Students must have passed the 10+2 level exam from a recognised board with a minimum of 60% aggregate marks.
- Must have Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as mandatory subjects and score not less than 50% marks in each of the subjects.
- Candidates must be born between 1 July 2004 and 30th June 2010.

### Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai B.Tech Selection Criteria

- Students are selected based on the Amrita Entrance Exam – Engineering ranks and JEE Main ranks.
- 70% of the seats are filled through the AEEE Exam selections, and rest 30% students are selected based on JEE Main ranks.
- Selected students are assigned seats through a centralised seat allocation process.
- Direct admissions are not conducted.

### How to Apply for Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai B.Tech Programs?

Students need to follow the steps below to register and apply for the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham B.Tech Admission.

- After checking the eligibility criteria, students need to apply directly on the official website of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.
- Register using the required information of Name, mobile number, Email ID, State, City, and create a password.
- Verify the mobile number with OTP and complete registration.
- Log in to the created account and fill out the application form with personal and academic details.
- Choose the AEEE Exam mode and select the Chennai campus.
- Upload the documents and scanned photo & signature.
- Pay the application fee via online netbanking, UPI, or any other online mode.
- Submit and download the application form.

After completing the application, students need to appear for the AEEE Exam or secure a valid JEE Main rank. For the online AEEE Exam, students need to book their exam slots as per availability. The AEEE Exam is held in two phases; students can appear for any one of them as per their convenience.

After exam results are out, students need to register for the Centralised Seat Allotment Process (CSAP). While registering for CSAP, students need to select their preferred B.Tech branches and campuses. Lock the choices before the deadline and wait for the allotment based on AEEE Rank or JEE Main Rank. Once seats are allotted, students need to report to the campus with the required original documents and pay the admission fees to confirm admission. Classes usually start in August-September.

## Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai Scholarships For B.Tech Aspirants

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai, offers scholarships to B.Tech aspirants based on Merit Scholarship. The following section discusses Amrita Chennai Scholarships for B.Tech in detail.

- Amrita Merit Scholarship – Students who secure high ranks in the AEEE entrance exam or the JEE Main exam, along with good marks in 10+2 level in PCM, are eligible for this merit scholarship. Up to 50% tuition fee waiver is provided to the students.

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai campus of 13.55 Acres, is located in the Vengal Village of the Thiruvallur District. The Chennai campus is approximately 40 kms away from the city.
