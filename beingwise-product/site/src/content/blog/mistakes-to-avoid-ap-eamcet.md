Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy common entrance test is a state-level exam conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh. APSCHE conducts AP EAMCET every year for entrance into undergraduate courses like B.Tech, B.Pharm, and B.Agri, etc. Officials have announced the exam date of AP EAMCET for 2025 to be **May 21st to May 27th.** Candidates can expect the registration process to commence from March 2025. AP EAMCET preparation involves several steps and avoiding some common mistakes can help them enhance their preparation and score high in the exam.

Candidates preparing for the exam have to go through the hectic hours of studies, revision, and practicing multiple mock tests. However, candidates who lack proper guidance might often commit a few mistakes, which might cost them losing marks in the exam. This article will help you with common mistakes that candidates often commit and how to avoid them to secure a good score in the exam.

## Mistakes to Avoid While Preparing for AP EAMCET

Candidates preparing for the exam have to ensure not to commit the mistakes mentioned below, to enhance their preparation and secure good marks in the exam.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Mistakes to Avoid** | **Detail** |
| Ignoring the Syllabus | The [Syllabus](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/preparation-plan-for-ap-eapcet/) gives candidates a blueprint of what the exam consists of. Ignoring and failing to understand the syllabus might lead candidates in a wrong direction. |
| Curating a plan | As often said, “Well planned is half done”, curating a study plan is important to avoid procrastinating and to keep candidates on the right track of preparation. |
| Ignoring Standard Sources | Running after multiple sources for each subject might overburden the students. Questions asked in the AP EAMCET are often from the standard sources of Class 12 and hence it is advised to go through the standard sources rather than running after collecting so many books. |
| Relying only on one source | While it is advised not to run after sources, candidates have to refer to a few online sources or reference materials for a conceptual and practical understanding of the concepts. |
| Ignoring PYQs and Mock tests | [Mock tests](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/ap-eamcet-mock-test/) and [PYQs](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/ap-eapcet-previous-year-papers/) unveil the exam difficulty and help in revising effectively for the exam. Ignoring them might cause candidates to lose an edge over others. |
| Ignoring Revision | Revision helps in retaining the concepts during the exams and helps in effectively storing the information in your memory. Ignoring revision can cause students with difficulty retaining the information during the exam. |
| Avoiding the weak areas | It is important to focus on the weak areas as they increase the chances of securing good marks in the exam. Avoid working on weak areas might not yield the desired results in the exam. |
| Neglecting Mind Mapping | Creating Mindmaps is a great technique to enhance your learning. Mindmaps help you easily remember a topic and help you retain the information better. |

## How to Stay Healthy and Manage Stress

Managing your health is important to sustain the energy over a long run and prepare efficiently for the exam. Candidates are advised to follow the tips mentioned below to stay healthy while preparing for [AP EAMCET 2025 exam](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/):

- Avoid junk food and eat homemade food.
- Stay hydrated, take regular water breaks.
- Take regular breaks to refresh and rejuvenate your mind.
- Focus on some physical activity like running/jogging to stay healthy.
- Meditate often to relax your mind and sustain your focus on studies.

## Time Management During the Exam

Time management is a crucial factor while preparing for AP EAMCET 2025 exam. Here are some tips to manage your time efficiently:

**Before the exam:**

- Practice mocks in exam-like conditions.
- Analyze how much time you are taking for each section and strategize accordingly.
- Avoid spending too much time on one question.
- Learn and use simple calculation tricks to reduce the time taken for calculation.

**After the exam:**

- Strategize your exam by allocating time for each section.
- Avoid spending too much time on one question.
- Focus on easy questions and come to complex problems after answering easy ones.

## Mistakes to Avoid on the Day of the AP EAMCET Exam

Although it is important to focus on avoiding mistakes during the exam, it is also important to learn about the mistakes to avoid during the exam for a smooth examination process. Here are some mistakes to avoid during AP EAMCET 2025 exam:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Mistakes to Avoid** | **Detail** |
| Not managing the Time | AP EAMCET require good time management as candidates have to complete the exam within the set time limit. Plan your exam strategy accordingly. |
| Not following the Instructions | Candidates have to follow certain instructions regarding the method of answering and the marking scheme. Read the instructions carefully to avoid discrepancies during the exam. |
| Arriving Late | Candidates have to arrive early at the examination centre to avoid last minute rush, which can affect their performance and time wastages. |
| Missing Documents | Candidates have to carry all the required documents, as missing documents can lead to disqualification. |
| Inappropriate Attire | Although there is no official attire, Candidates should wear a simple attire while appearing for AP EAMCET 2025 exam. |
| Managing Stress | Managing the stress and anxiety during the exam is important to perform better in the exam. Practice multiple mocks simulating the exam environment to get acquainted with the exam setting. |
| Reviewing Answers | If time permits, cross-check your answers to prevent mistakes. |

Strategize your exam accordingly by answering the easy questions first, avoid wasting time on one question.

Candidates are advised to prioritise their health while appearing for AP EAMCET 2025, as it relaxes the mind and helps in staying healthy.
