The TG-EAPCET If a State Level Examination conducted by Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education(TSCHE). TG-EAPCET Is conducted for entrance into undergraduate courses like engineering, agriculture and pharmacy.  a candidate who has passed their class 12th and Aspiring for admission into undergraduate courses is eligible to appear for the exam by registering on the official website.

The TSCHE(Telangana State Council of Higher Education) will **start accepting applications for TG-EAPCET on February 25th**, 2025, and candidates have to apply for the exam before the **deadline, i.e. April 4th, 2025**. Eligible candidates can apply for the exam after paying the application fee. Officials have announced that the **exam will be held from April 29th to 30th for Agriculture and from May 2nd to May 5th for Engineering.**

Candidates preparing for the exam have to go through the application, preparation, and revision processes. Candidates often feel overwhelmed during the examination process, however, following certain guidelines can ensure a seamless examination process and a smooth exam experience. This article will guide you through the exam day guidelines, documents required and exam instructions.

## TG-EAPCET Dress Code

Although Candidates are not specified any instructions for the [TG-EAPCET Dress code](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/tg-eapcet-dress-code/), candidates are advised to appear for the exam with a simple dress code.

- Avoid wearing shirts with heavy graphics and designs.

- Avoid wearing pants with multiple pockets and hooded shirts, as they might cause inconvenience during security checks.

- Avoid wearing jewellery containing metal as it might cause disturbances while frisking.

- Avoid wearing footwear like shoes and those with thick soles, as you might be subjected to more regular security checks.

## Exam Day Guidelines for TG-EAPCET

Following the exam day guidelines as instructed by officials can help in a smooth and efficient examination process. Here are some exam day guidelines that candidates have to follow:

- Candidates are advised to arrive at the examination centre at least one hour before the exam time, and they are not allowed to enter the examination hall if they arrive late.

- Arrive at the examination centre with an admit card and verify the details mentioned in the admit card before appearing for the exam.

- TG-EAPCET admit card is not transferable and tampering with it might lead candidates to disqualification.

- Candidates must carry an admit card, black or ballpoint pen, the TG-EAPCET application form, and a caste certificate (if applicable).

- Candidates are not allowed to bring log books, calculators, or cell phones to the examination centre. If they are found with those items, they will be sent out of the examination hall.

- Candidates are not allowed to leave the examination centre until completion of time set by officials.

- Candidates are advised to read the exam instructions carefully before attempting the questions.

## Exam Instructions for TG-EAPCET

Following the exam instructions is important, as failing to stick to them might lead candidates to lose marks or disqualification. Here are the exam instructions for candidates appearing for TG-EAPCET 2025:

- Candidates must very the hall ticket number displayed on the monitor before entering the password.

- Candidates have to verify the displayed photograph and inform the officials/invigilators in case of a mismatch.

- Select the ‘I have read and understood the instructions’ checkbox and click on the ‘I am ready to begin’ option to begin the test.

- The exam link will be displayed on the monitor before the examination and candidates are advised to inform the invigilator before commencement of the exam.

- Candidates will have to face severe actions in case they are found performing any unfair actions such as copying answers, etc.

- The timer will be displayed on the screen, and the responses submitted by the candidates are automatically submitted when the timer runs to zero.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ******What are the exam day guidelines for TG-EAPCET?******

Candidates have to reach the exam centre at least 1 hour before the exam time; they are not allowed to carry electronics/log books, etc. Arrive at the exam centre with the required documents and don’t practice any unfair means in the examination.

### 2. ******What is the exam date for TG-EAPCET 2025?******

TG-EAPCET exam for engineering will be held from May 2nd to May 5th, 2025.

### 3. ******Can I wear a hoodie to the exam centre?******

Avoid wearing hooded shirts or sweatshirts, and try to wear plain shirts or T-shirts while appearing for the TG-EAPCET exam.

### 4. ****Are candidates allowed to carry calculators for the TG-EAPCET exam?****

No, candidates are not allowed to carry items such as calculators, cell phones, log books, etc.

### 5. ******What are all the documents to carry for the TG-EAPCET exam?******

Candidates have to carry the admit card, application form, pen and caste certificate(if applicable) to the examination hall.

### 6. **Are we allowed to appear for TG-EAPCET with traditional clothes?**

Candidates are advised to avoid traditional wear as they are subjected to more regular security checks, which might cause inconvenience.

### 7. **What is the difference between EAMCET and EAPCET?**

EAMCET is renamed as EAPCET, but they are the same exams.
