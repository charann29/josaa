TG EAPCET is a state-level entrance exam for admission to various Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy programs. The exam is conducted by JNTUH. TG EAPCET Exam Pattern 2025 for the **Engineering program** includes different topics in **Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics**. However, for Agriculture and Pharmacy programs, the subjects are Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.

Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (TG EAPCET) formerly known as TS EAMCET, is a **3-hour long exam** that has MCQ-type questions. It is a Computer Based Test (CBT) in online mode where the **total mark is 160**. 1 mark is rewarded for each correct answer, whereas there is no negative marking for the wrong answer. Candidates need to **score a minimum of 40 marks out of 160** to pass the exam.

Candidates can start filling out TS EAMCET **application form from 1st March 2025 up to 4th April 2025**. The BTech exam will be held from **2nd May to 5th May 2025**.

### Marking Scheme for Engineering

- TS EAMCET 2025 exam has a total of 160 questions. 1 mark is being allotted to each correct answer.
- There are a total of 160 marks divided into three subjects where: Physics and Chemistry are allotted 40 marks, and Mathematics is allotted 80 marks.

### Minimum Qualifying Marks for TG EAPCET Exam

**25% is the minimum mark that is needed to qualify [TS EAMCET 2025](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/) examination**. However, after you receive the cutoff marks and appear for the counselling process to ultimately get to your desired Engineering College.
