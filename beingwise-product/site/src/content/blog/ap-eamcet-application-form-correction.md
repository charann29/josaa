## How To Make Corrections In AP EAMCET 2025 Application Form?

AP EAMCET application form 2025 has been released on the official website from 6th May to 8th May 2025. Candidates can check the steps below to make corrections in the AP EAMCET 2025 Application Form.

- Visit the official website of AP EAPCET 2025, <https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/Eapcet/EAPCET_HomePage.aspx>.

- Click on “Category – 2 Corrections (only after Submitting Application Form)” blinking on the top right corner of the homepage.

- Next, candidates need to enter the following information in the specified spaces.
  - Enter Payment Reference ID
  - Registration Number
  - Qualifying Examination Hall Ticket No (12th admit card)
  - Enter Mobile Number
  - Date of Birth
- Click on “Proceed to Fill Application”.
- On the application form page, candidates are advised to carefully review all the submitted data and make necessary changes.
- Submit the application form.
- Download the form and take a printout for future reference.

## Last Minute Preparation Tips For AP EAMCET 2025

To succeed on the AP EAMCET entrance Exam, candidates need to create a study and preparation plan. Check out a few last-minute preparation tips to succeed in the exam.

- **Review Smartly:** In the last days of preparation, avoid tackling difficult topics. Instead, focus on reviewing key formulas, definitions, and [**high-weightage chapters for the AP EAMCET**](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/ap-eamcet-important-topics-chapter-wise-weightage/). Utilise your short notes and summary sheets as much as possible during this time, as they are easier to memorise quickly.
- **Practice Mock Tests:** Try to finish either 1-2 mock tests a day in an exam-like situation to start to build speed and stamina.  Go through your tests and mistakes to carefully think about what your weak areas are, and how you might approach your exam strategy.
- **Use flash cards or note sheets:** Keep your flash cards, mind map, and quick revision notes within reach. They will be the best way to do your revision in the last 2-3 days.
- **Look after your wellbeing:** Remember to get lots of sleep, light meals that sit well, and to hydrate. A clear, fresh mind and a healthy body will improve concentration and limit the chance of silly mistakes in the exam hall.

## AP EAMCET 2025: Exam Day Guidelines

Candidates are advised to follow the below given[**exam day guidelines for AP EAMCET 2025**](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/ap-eapcet-exam-guidelines/)carefully. Sticking to these guidelines will help everything go smoothly on the day of the exam.

- **Arrive at the Center Early:** Try to reach your exam center at least two hours before the reporting time so you don’t have to worry about the last-minute rush. This is perfect for you to settle down, verify your details, calm your mind, and prepare yourself for the exam.
- **Take Essential Documents Only:** Keep your AP EAMCET 2025 hall ticket, a valid photo ID, and any other required documents. Do not take gadgets, notes, or any unauthorised items since these are all prohibited inside the exam hall.
- **Time Management:** Don’t get caught up in any difficult questions you can’t answer. Instead, move on and, if time permits, come back to that question after you finish the exam. Just keep an eye on the timer. Make sure that you finish the paper with at least 10 minutes remaining for revision.
- **Stay Calm and Composed:** Do not panic if there are a few questions that you have never seen before. Stay calm, breathe, and try to answer the questions you are sure of first. A calm mind will enable you to make better decisions during the test.
- **Prioritize Your Strengths During The Exam:** When the test begins, complete the easier questions that you’re confident with, which will secure some easy marks. Then move to the difficult sections later.

## AP EAMCET Counselling 2025 Process

After the AP EAMCET 2025 results are declared, eligible candidates can participate in the counselling process. The Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) conducts the AP EAMCET counselling 2025 for admission into engineering, agriculture, and pharmacy courses.

**Step 1: Registration and Counselling Fee Payment:** AP EAMCET 2025 registration for the eligible candidates is through the official website. Click on the “Counselling Registration” link, then give the hall ticket number and date of birth. Next, candidates need to pay the AP EAMCET 2025 counselling processing fee through online mode (credit card, debit card, or net banking).

**Fee Payment Procedure:**

- Visit the official site cets.apsche.ap.gov.in
- Click on the link that says “Processing Fee Payment.”
- Enter your AP EAMCET 2025 Hall Ticket Number and Exam Rank, and click OK.
- Click “Pay Fee Online” to access the payment gateway
- Make payment and note down only the reference number, or print the receipt for your future needs.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Category** | **Counselling Fee** |
| OC/BC | INR 1200 |
| SC/ST | INR 600 |

**NOTE:** Once the payment is successful, the login ID and registration number will be sent to you by SMS.

**Step 2: Certificate Verification:** If your certificates are verified via web services, you can move on to the online process. If not, you will hear from us advising you to come along for offline certificate verification at a Helpline Centre (HLC).

Verification is carried out in rank order. Verified candidates will get a verification slip. Check the details on the slip and ensure there are no errors. Given below are the documents required for verification.

- AP EAMCET 2025 Rank card
- AP EAPCET 2025 Hall Ticket
- Transfer Certificate ( TC)
- Memorandum of Marks (Inter or its equivalent)
- Proof of Date of Birth (SSC or its Equivalent Memo)
- Study Certificate from VI to Intermediate
- EWS certificate
- Andhra Pradesh Residence Certificate of father/mother for 10 years
- Integrated Community Certificate, in the case of BC/ST/SC, issued by the competent authority
- Income certificate of parents or White Ration card
- Local status certificate (if applicable)

**Step 3: Choice Filling**: Candidates may select their preferred colleges and courses. Check the steps below for choice filling. Register by entering your Registration No., Hall Ticket No., Rank, and Date of Birth.

You will have to create a new password and re-sign in by typing the same details.

An OTP will be sent to the mobile number you’ve registered. Enter it to proceed.

The available colleges and courses will reveal themselves. Select and rank your selection based on preference.

Candidates can change their choices until the final locking. Once you lock it, you cannot change it.

**Step 4: Seat Allotment:** The authority allocates seats in the participating institutes based on merit, category, candidate reservations, chosen preferences, and seat availability. The allocated candidates must access their candidate accounts and download their allotment letter.

Once you have the Seat Allotment, then you can:

- Log in and print your Provisional Allotment Letter.
- If you are happy with the options, self-report online, and then report to the college you have been assigned to.
- If you simply self-report online and do not report to the college, you will be disqualified. However, you will still be able to participate in further rounds.
- If you do not self-report online for the rounds or report at the college allocated, you will no longer be allowed to participate in any form of seat allotment in any further rounds.

**Step 5: Reporting to Allotted Institute:** Candidates must report physically to the allotted institute within the given time frame to confirm admission.

- Carry the Provisional Allotment Letter, fee payment receipt, and original documents.
- Failure to report before the deadline will lead to the cancellation of the seat.
- No requests will be entertained after the final reporting date.
