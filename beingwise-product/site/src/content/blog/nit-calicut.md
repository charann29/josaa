## NIT Calicut Admission Process for BTech

Students will be required to go through the complete NIT Calicut admission process below to secure a seat in the institute.

**Step 1: Check Eligibility Requirements:** All candidates should thoroughly verify the NIT Calicut Admission Eligibility before filling out the application form for the B.Tech program. In order to qualify, the students should have completed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics with a minimum of marks.

**Step 2: Pass the Entrance Examination:** To be eligible for NIT Calicut B.Tech admission, applicants need to take the JEE Main examination. This test is taken by students to test them in core subjects, and it has a great impact on determining their selection in the NIT Calicut admission process.

**Step 3: Submission of Form and registration:** To apply for the B.Tech program at NIT Calicut, the applicants must enrol on the official examination portal, which serves as the NIT Calicut Application Link. An application form is compulsory to fill out correctly to proceed with the NIT Calicut admission process.

**Step 4: Check Cutoff and Merit Position:** Upon announcement of results, candidates are advised to check the NIT Calicut Cutoff to determine their chances of admission in NIT Calicut. Cutoff varies annually based on the difficulty of exams, applicant numbers, and availability of seats.

**Step 5: Attend Counselling Process:** The NIT Calicut Admission Process involves taking part in JoSAA counselling among the eligible candidates. The registration, filling choices and locking preferred options are involved in this stage of NIT Calicut admission.

**Step 6: Allocation of Seats Procedure:** According to the NIT Calicut Selection Criteria, the selection and allocation of seats are based on rank, preferences, and availability for NIT Calicut admission. Applicants should consistently review the outcomes of allotment and reply within the stipulated period.

**Step 7: Document Verification Step:** Once the allotment of seats is completed, candidates undergo a document checking exercise to verify their eligibility in NIT Calicut Admission. Any academic and identity documents need to be provided as per the official requirements for NIT Calicut admission.

**Step 8: Payment of Admission Fee:** To secure their seat, the candidates have to pay the NIT Calicut Admission Fee. Admission is officially made when the money is paid for the NIT Calicut admission.

**Step 9: Reporting to the Institute:** The candidates who are shortlisted will report to the National Institute of Technology Calicut according to the official program. It is important to regularly check updates to avoid missing any NIT Calicut Admission Dates for NIT Calicut admission.
