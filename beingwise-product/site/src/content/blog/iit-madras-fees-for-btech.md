## IIT Madras Fees – Hostel & Mess Fees

IIT Madras offers hostel accommodation for both male and female students at the IIT Madras Campus. With all the necessary facilities and amenities, students need to pay an amount of INR 15,750 as the IIT Madras hostel fee per semester. But the total fees of IIT Madras hostels range from **INR 40,102 to INR 40,692.** It includes hostel admission fees, Establishment charges, RO Water charges, Waste Disposal Charges, Utility Charges, and optional events fees. Check the following table for IIT Madras Fees for hostel accommodation.

| Component | Hostel Fees |
| --- | --- |
| Hostel Admission Fees | INR 500 |
| A+B Establishment Charges (For Academic Period of the Semester) | INR 8000 + INR 1500 |
| RO Water Charges | INR 500 |
| Waste Disposal Charges | INR 250 |
| Utility Charges | INR 5000 |
| Optional Fees of Saarang, Shastra, E-Summit | INR 7500 |

## B.Tech Student Feedback on IIT Madras Fees

As per the various student reviews and experiences, IIT Madras fees are recognised as justified due to the strong placement outcomes, academic reputation, research exposure, and infrastructural facilities. Over the years, students have appreciated the following key things about IIT Madras fees.

- Fee Waivers for SC/ST Candidates
- Scholarships for economically weaker candidates
- Accessible IIT Madras fee structure for the toppers.
- Arrangement of educational loans or financial aid support from banks.
- High ROI with average packages varying up to INR 19 Lakhs

However, there are a few mixed reactions regarding the IIT Madras fees. Many complain that the annual B.Tech fee of INR 2 Lakhs and more can be expensive initially. With comparatively reasonable hostel fees, students feel the mess fee is somewhat average.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Ques. What is the IIT Madras B.Tech fee structure for SC candidates?**

Ans. The IIT Madras fees for SC candidates are INR 1.39 Lakhs for 4 years.

**2. Ques. Are there scholarships and financial aid available in the IIT Madras fee structure for B.Tech?**

Ans. Yes, students can avail several scholarships and financial aid in the IIT Madras fee structure for B.Tech. These include the Institute Merit-Cum-Means Scholarship, Institute Free Studentship, Institute Notional Prize, Girish Reddy Scholarship, Indian Women’s Association at Bonn Scholarship, and Post Matric Government Scholarship.

**3. Ques. Can the IIT Madras fee payment be made online?**

Ans. Yes, IIT Madras fee payment can be done online through the IITM integrated fee payment portal.

**4. Ques. Do all students pay the same fees for IIT Madras?**

Ans. No, IIT Madras fees differ based on category, scholarship eligibility, and hostel preferences.

**5. Ques. What is the detailed fee breakup for IIT Madras?**

Ans. The total IIT Madras fees for B.Tech is INR 9.39 Lakhs. This includes components such as tuition fees, examination fees, library fees, and other institutional charges.

**6. Ques. What are the eligibility and fee criteria?**

Ans. The eligibility requirements may affect the applicable fee structure for B.Tech students. Candidates need at least 60% marks in 10+2 for General, EWS, and OBC categories, along with a valid JEE Advanced rank. The fee structure includes non-refundable fees, one-time admission fees, security deposits, tuition fee slabs, and possible tuition fee waivers.
