### What is a Preliminary Answer Key?

The **Preliminary Answer Key** is a *provisional set of answers* released by the exam authorities shortly after the entrance exam. It contains:

- **Correct answers** to all the questions asked in the exam.
- Usually published **stream-wise and shift-wise** (Engineering, Agriculture & Medical).
- Helps students **estimate their scores** before the official results.
- It gives candidates a chance to **raise objections** if they believe any answer is incorrect, backed by a proper explanation or source.

### What is a Final Answer Key?

The **Final Answer Key** is released *after the objection period is over*. It includes:

- **Updated answers** after reviewing all valid objections submitted by candidates.
- Considered the **official and final reference** for calculating scores and preparing results.
- No more changes can be made once the final answer key is out.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **What is the TS EAMCET 2025 response sheet?**

The TS EAMCET 2025 response sheet contains the answers marked by the candidate during the exam, allowing them to match their responses with the official answer key.

### 2. **How can I download the TS EAMCET 2025 response sheet?**

You can download the response sheet from the TS EAMCET official website by logging in with your registration number, hall ticket number, and date of birth.

### 3. **When will the TS EAMCET 2025 response sheet be available?**

The TS  EAMCET 2025 response sheet is available for Agriculture and Pharmacy. It was released on May 4th, 2025, and the engineering stream response sheet was released on May 5th, 2025.

### 4. **Is the TS EAMCET 2025 response sheet different from the answer key?**

Yes, the response sheet shows your marked answers, while the answer key provides the correct answers. Comparing both helps calculate your probable score.

### 5. **Can I challenge or raise objections to the TS EAMCET response sheet?**

You cannot challenge the response sheet, but you can raise objections to the provisional answer key if you find any discrepancies.

### 6. **What details are required to check the TS EAMCET response sheet?**

To access your response sheet, you will need your registration number, hall ticket number, and date of birth.

### 7. **What is the last date to raise objections to the TS EAMCET 2025 answer key?**

The last date to submit objections is May 7th 2025, 5:00 P.M.

### 8. **How can I calculate my score using the response sheet?**

Compare your responses with the official answer key. Award 1 mark for each correct answer. There is no negative marking
