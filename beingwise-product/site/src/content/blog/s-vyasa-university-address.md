## How to Reach S-Vyasa University?

S-Vyasa University campuses, i.e. Global City campus and Prashanti campus, are both easily accessible from the main city. Students can find different modes of transport to reach S-Vyasa University. Check the different modes of transportation through which you can reach S-Vyasa University:

**S-Vyasa University Global City Campus:**

- **Metro:** Individuals can take the metro and navigate to the RajaRajeshwari Nagar metro station, located in the East-West corridor of the purple line.
- **Local Transport:** Candidates can hire an auto rickshaw to directly reach the campus.

**S-Vyasa University Prashanti Campus:**

- **Metro:** Anekal does not have direct metro access; individuals have to reach the Electronic City, and then take a local transport to the S-Vyasa University campus.

- **Local Transport:** Candidates can hire an auto rickshaw to directly reach the campus.
