Malla Reddy Engineering College, located in Maisamguda, Telangana, offers a diverse range of courses across various disciplines like Engineering and Management. Malla Reddy Engineering College fees are relatively affordable compared to most of the private institutions. Known for its excellence in delivering industry-aligned, high-standard education, MREC offers admissions through several entrance exams like EAPCET/ICET and Management Quotas.

Malla Reddy Engineering College is a part of the Malla Reddy Group of Institutions, which consists of Malla Reddy University, MREC, MRECW, etc. MREC offers engineering and management courses at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels, with popular B.Tech branches including Computer Science Engineering (CSE), Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI&ML), Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE), and Mechanical Engineering, among others. At the postgraduate level, it offers M.Tech and MBA programs designed to equip students with advanced technical knowledge and managerial skills. The college is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad, and is approved by AICTE, ensuring academic credibility and standardisation. ## Malla Reddy Engineering College Fees for Engineering Courses

MREC offers a wide range of courses across engineering disciplines at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. Malla Reddy Engineering College offers admissions based on the EAPCET scores(70% Convener quota) and Category-B(30% management quota) for engineering. Students who have completed their diploma in respective branches are eligible for lateral entry admissions based on the ECET scores.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ********What is the fee structure of Malla Reddy Engineering College (MREC)?********

B.Tech fees are ₹1,15,000 per year (convener quota). M.Tech is ₹60,000/year and MBA is ₹60,000/year. Extra charges for hostel and other facilities apply.

### **2. ****What is the management quota fee at Malla Reddy Engineering College?******

Around ₹1.2 lakh to ₹1.6 lakh per year for B.Tech, depending on the branch.

### 3. **********************************What is the fee structure of Malla Reddy Engineering College for Women (MRECW)?**********************************

₹70,000 per year (convener quota); ₹1.1 lakh to ₹1.5 lakh per year (management quota). Hostel charges are separate.

### 4. **************Are there hostel facilities at Malla Reddy Engineering College?**************

Yes, MREC provides students with separate hostels for boys and girls, and maintains hygiene and security for both.

### 5. **********Is Malla Reddy University and Malla Reddy Engineering College the same?**********

No, they are not the same; however, they are both part of the Malla Reddy Group of Institutions.

### 6. **Are the Placement opportunities good at Malla Reddy Engineering College?**

Yes, with a highest package of 22.8 LPA and an average package of 5-6 LPA, Malla Reddy Engineering College provides decent placement opportunities for students.

### 7. **What are the popular courses offered at Malla Reddy Engineering College?**

Popular courses include B.Tech in Computer Science, AI & ML, ECE, IT, and Mechanical. M.Tech and MBA programs are also offered at the postgraduate level.
