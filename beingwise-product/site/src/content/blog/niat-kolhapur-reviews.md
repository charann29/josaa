Moreover, NIAT in Kolhapur is endorsed by NASSCOM, NSDC, and Startup India. NIAT has also collaborated with Sanjay Ghodawat University and Sandip University to get UGC approved BTech CSE degrees.

### Q2. What is the admission process for NIAT in Kolhapur?

The final admission is basically granted by the partner universities.
