## Step-by-Step Process to KEAM Seat Allotment 2025

Here’s a quick recap of the KEAM seat allotment process:

**1. Registration for Counselling:** To participate in the KEAM 2025 counselling, candidates have to register for the counselling process. After registering, candidates have to fill in their preferred list of colleges.

**2. Fee Payment:** Candidates who are allotted colleges have to pay the fees within the specified time window set by the officials.

**3. Admission:** To confirm the admission, candidates have to pay the fees and report to the allotted colleges. Candidates who fail to report to the colleges must remember that they may lose the allotted seat.

**4. Participating in additional rounds:** Candidates who are not satisfied with the allotted colleges can participate in the subsequent KEAM counselling rounds to upgrade their seat.

**5. Mop-up rounds:** Candidates who are not allotted seats can participate in the additional rounds, such as the mop-up round or stray rounds for the KEAM seat allotment.

## How to Check the KEAM Seat Allotment 2025?

Candidates who wish to check the KEAM seat allotment can navigate to the official website and download the KEAM seat allotment list released by the officials. Here’s the step-by-step guide to check the KEAM seat allotment results:

- Navigate to the official website [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in).
- Download the KEAM seat allotment list released by the officials.
- Search for your application number and check the allotted seat in the corresponding column of the PDF.

**Note:** Candidates can also check their seat allotment by logging in to the website using their credentials, including application number, password, and captcha.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Who is eligible for the KEAM Special Reservation?

Students with disabilities (PwD) are eligible for the KEAM special reservation quota and will be allotted seats in specified colleges where seats are available.

### 2. What is the KEAM seat allotment for the stray round?

KEAM seat allotment for the stray rounds is conducted to fill the vacant seats in engineering courses. This is conducted if there are any vacant seats in the participating engineering colleges.

### 3. I was allotted a seat in the KEAM seat allotment. What is the last date for the fee payment?

The last date for fee payment and reporting to the allotted colleges is August 20th, 2025.

### 4. How to check the KEAM seat allotment?

Candidates can check the KEAM seat allotment by navigating to the official website cee.kerala.gov.in.

### 5. What happens if I don’t report to the allotted college on time?

Candidates must report to the allotted colleges on time; those who fail to do so may lose their allotted seat.

### 6. What if a candidate does not receive any allotment in the KEAM counselling stray round 2025?

If the candidate does not receive any seat allotment, then there will be no further chances via KEAM CAP for 2025. However, candidates can take admission through the management quota, which may be available at some institutions.

### 7. What is KEAM Counselling 2025?

KEAM Counselling 2025 is a centralized admission process conducted by the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE), Kerala, for admission to undergraduate programs in engineering, architecture, pharmacy, and medical allied courses across various government and private colleges in Kerala.
