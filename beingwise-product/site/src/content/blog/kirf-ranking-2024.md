## Parameters of KIRF Ranking 2024

The Parameters of KIRF (Kerala Institutional Ranking Framework) Rankings are organized into five broad categories, each with specific sub-parameters that collectively evaluate the overall performance of higher education institutions.

These parameters include:

1. **Teaching, Learning & Resources (TLR):** This covers aspects related to teaching quality, learning effectiveness, and resource availability, including Student Strength, Faculty-Student Ratio, Faculty Qualification and Experience, Online Education, Subscription to e-journals, and Financial Resources and their Utilization.
2. **Knowledge Dissemination and Research Excellence (KDRE):** This measures the institution’s research output and impact through Research Productivity, Research Impact, Intellectual Property Rights, Research Footprint, Consultancy, and Startups.
3. **Graduation Outcome (GO):** This assesses student success and preparedness through Metrics for University Examinations, Entrance Examinations (such as UGC-NET, JRF, and GATE), and Placement and higher studies.
4. **Outreach and Inclusivity (OI):** This evaluates the institution’s efforts in promoting diversity and inclusivity through Regional Diversity, First-Generation Learners, Facilities for Students with Disabilities, and Social Inclusiveness.
5. **Scientific Temper and Secular Outlook (STSO):** This qualitative parameter reflects the institution’s promotion of scientific thinking, secular values, adoption of green technology, and accreditation and rankings.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the KIRF Ranking 2024?

The KIRF Ranking 2024 is a ranking system that evaluates Indian educational institutions, particularly engineering colleges, based on parameters like knowledge output, innovation, research quality, patents, industry collaboration, and societal impact. It is an alternative to the government-backed National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).

### 2. How to participate in KIRF ranking 2024?

KIRF invites higher education institutions interested in participating in the ranking exercise to register on its official portal. Institutions are required to submit their data through an online platform developed specifically for this purpose. To ensure accuracy and reliability, KIRF, in collaboration with KSHEC and other designated partner agencies, also conducts data verification and authentication whenever necessary and feasible. This process guarantees the credibility of the information used for ranking computations.

### 3. Which Engineering College is ranked 1st by the KIRF Ranking 2024?

College of Engineering Trivandrum is rated 1st by the KIRF ranking 2024.

### 4. How is KIRF different from NIRF?

KIRF focuses specifically on institutions in Kerala with additional local metrics, whereas NIRF ranks institutions across India on a broader national scale. KIRF also ranks engineering colleges based on NIRF and state-specific ranking criteria.
