The Telangana State Council of Higher Education announced the TS EAMCET results on May 18, 2024, at 11 am. To access the results, candidates need to visit the official website at [eapcet.tsche.ac.in](http://eapcet.tsche.ac.in). They must log in using their credentials, including the registration number, qualifying hall ticket number, and date of birth. The examination was held from May 9th to 11th, 2024. Upon logging in, candidates can view their results and download the rank card for future reference.

## How to Check TS EAMCET Results?

To check TS EAMCET results, candidates can follow these steps:

**Step 1:** Visit the official website of TS EAMCET 2024 [eapcet.tsche.ac.in](https://eapcet.tsche.ac.in/TSEAPCET/EAPCET_GetResult_2024RS.aspx).

**Step 2:** In the homepage, scroll down and click on “View Results(E, A& P)”

**Step 3:** Now, enter your registration number, qualifying exam hall ticket number, and date of birth.

**Step 4:** After entering the details, click on the “Submit” button.

**Step 5:** Your TS EAMCET result for 2024 should be displayed on the screen.

## How to Download TS EAMCET Rank Card?

To download the TS EAMCET rank card, you can follow these steps:

**Step 1:** Visit the official website of TS EAMCET 2024 at [eapcet.tsche.ac.in](http://eapcet.tsche.ac.in).

**Step 2:** On the  homepage, click on the link  “Download Rank Card(E, A&P)”

**Step 3:** Now, enter your registration number, hall ticket number, and date of birth.

**Step 4:** After entering the details, click on the “View Rank Card” button.

**Step 5:** Your TS EAMCET rank card will be displayed on the screen.

**Step 6:** Print the downloaded result for your records.

## Details Mentioned TS EAMCET Rank Card?

Candidates are required to download a copy of their TS EAMCET results, which include the following details:

- Candidate’s name
- Father’s name
- Mother’s name
- Date of birth
- Total marks secured in the TS EAMCET exam
- Marks secured by candidates in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
- Qualifying status of the candidate
- TS EAMCET hall ticket number
- TS EAMCET registration number
