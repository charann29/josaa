## What Is the WBJEE Rank Card 2026?

The WBJEE rank card 2026 is the official result document released by WBJEEB. It is not a simple pass or fail certificate. The rank card carries each candidate’s subject-wise marks, total marks, and merit rank. Candidates who qualify WBJEE 2026 will need this rank card throughout the counselling and seat allotment process. The rank card is valid only for the 2026–27 academic session.

WBJEEB does not publish a public rank list. The rank card is individual and can only be accessed through the candidate’s login on the official portal.

## How to Download the WBJEE Rank Card 2026?

The WBJEE rank card download process is fully online. No physical copy will be sent by post. Keep your application number and login credentials ready before starting.

- Step 1: Visit the official WBJEEB website at wbjeeb.nic.in
- Step 2: Click on the WBJEE rank card 2026 link on the homepage
- Step 3: Enter your application number and password or date of birth
- Step 4: The rank card will appear on the screen
- Step 5: Download the rank card PDF and take a printout for future reference

Any discrepancy in marks or personal details should be reported to WBJEEB immediately after downloading. Corrections will not be entertained after the deadline.

## Details Mentioned on the WBJEE Rank Card 2026

The WBJEE 2026 rank card is an important document required throughout the admission process. Here is everything that appears on it:

- Candidate’s full name
- Roll number and application form number
- Date of birth
- Category and domicile
- Gender
- Subject-wise marks in Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry
- Total marks secured
- General Merit Rank (GMR)
- Category rank (SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, PwD, TFW, wherever applicable)

Pharmacy Merit Rank (PMR) will also be mentioned for candidates who are eligible for pharmacy admissions based on their Paper II scores.

## WBJEE 2026 Merit List

WBJEEB will prepare a category-wise merit list for WBJEE 2026 based on marks obtained in the entrance examination. The merit list forms the basis for admissions into all WBJEE participating institutes. Category-wise ranks will be published separately for the following groups:

- General Merit Rank (GMR)
- SC rank
- ST rank
- OBC-A rank
- OBC-B rank
- PwD rank
- TFW rank

Candidates are listed in the merit list as per the details submitted in their application form. The WBJEE 2026 merit list is released in online mode only and is not dispatched by post.

## What to Do After Downloading the WBJEE Rank Card 2026

Once you have your WBJEE rank card 2026, the next stage is counselling and seat allotment. Qualified candidates will need to participate in WBJEE counselling 2026 for admission to B.Tech, B.Pharm, and B.Arch courses at WBJEE participating institutes. The counselling process involves the following steps:

- Online registration and choice filling on the official WBJEEB portal
- Payment of counselling registration fee through net banking, debit card, or credit card
- Seat allotment by WBJEEB based on rank, category, preferences, and available seats
- Document verification at the allotted institute
- Admission confirmation and fee payment

Candidates with well-planned preferences during choice filling have better chances of securing their preferred branch and college. Use the WBJEE marks vs rank table and previous year cutoff data to build a realistic preference list before the window opens.
