## What are the eligibility Criteria for the TNEA Application 2026?

The eligibility criteria required to apply for the TNEA counselling application are fixed by the Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE), Tamil Nadu. Check the eligibility criteria for the TNEA online application 2026:

- Must be natives of Tamil Nadu state.
- Students from other states can apply under the Open category with a valid proof of nativity certificate.
- Students from other states can also apply if they have studied classes 8-12 in Tamil Nadu.
- Must be a 10+2 level pass with Physics, Chemistry, & Maths as compulsory subjects.
- General category candidates must have secured 45%, while the SC/ST/OBC/& other reserved categories have 5% relaxation.
- For the TNEA Application, no upper age limit is fixed.
- Only Citizens of India can apply for the TNEA online application.

## What are the documents required for the TNEA Application?

 Students will need an EMIS number, an active contact number, and an email ID for the TNEA Application Login. Before the TNEA Counselling application, students must be ready with the following set of documents required for uploading and certificate verification.

- Class X & XII Score Card or Marksheet
- Class XI Marksheet or Aadhar Card For Date of Birth Proof.
- Transfer Certificate
- Scanned copy of signature
- A passport-sized photo of the candidate
- If Applicable, Community & Nativity certificate for Qouta proof.
- For Fee concession & scholarships, an Income Certificate is required as well.

## How to fill out the TNEA Application Form?

The TNEA Application form filling process starts with the online TNEA Registration. The entire process is conducted online on the official website of TNEA. Post the Class 12 exam announcement, the TNEA Counselling application links are posted on the website by DoTE.

Check the step-by-step TNEA application form filling process:

- TNEA Application Registration Process.
  - Once the registration link is live on the [TNEA website](http://tneaonline.org), visit the page.
  - Select the registration link & fill in the required details of email ID, date of birth, gender, and contact number.
  - Confirm OTP and complete the mobile verification process.
  - Once registration is done, students receive the TNEA Application Login ID.
- TNEA Application Form Filling Process.
  - On the TNEA Website, click on the TNEA application link.
  - Log in with the TNEA Application Login ID and create a password with the given guidelines.
  - Once logged in, fill out the application form step by step.
  - Enter the required personal information of nationality, gender, parents’ names, and service details.
  - Enter the special reservation details if applicable.
- Pay the TNEA Application Fees as per the category requirements through online netbanking or a demand draft under the name of   “Secretary, TNEA” payable at Chennai.
- Fill the rest of the TNEA Application Form.
  - Enter the scholarship information after the TNEA Application fee payment
  - Enter the school details of Classes 7 to 12 for the school of study information section.
  - Save & Proceed.
  - Enter the academic details of the 10+2 level exam.
  - Enter information on the native language, medium of instruction, parents’ profession, and other details.
- Complete the process and click on preview.
- Thoroughly cross-check the details filled in the form.
- After checking and doing the required edits, click on the submit option.
- Download and keep the TNEA Application form printout for the TNEA certification process at the TFC.

## What to do after the TNEA Application?

After the students are done with the TNEA Counselling application, they need to do the following things:

- Monitor the TNEA Application status online.
- Prepare for the TNEA Certificate verification at the TFC or facilitation centre.
- Visit the allotted TFC with the original document required for TNEA Counselling for physical scrutiny. It is required for cross-checking the uploaded documents.
- In case of any issue, address them on the site or report it via the grievance redressal period to avoid rejection.
- Once verification is successful, students need to wait for the TNEA Rank list to be published. The rank list is based on the normalised PCM Marks of class 12.
- Selected students participate in the 3-4 rounds of TNEA Counselling.
- Fill college & branch choices during the choice-filling round online.
- Seats are allotted across all the counselling rounds as students’ priority given in the choice filling round.
- Pay the counselling fees and block the seat.
- Report to the allotted institute and pay the full program fees to confirm admission.

## Tips to Avoid TNEA Application Rejection

To eliminate the chances of application rejection, students must focus on the following tips:

- Verify the TNEA application twice before final submission
- Match the personal information with the uploaded documents.
- Enter the correct PCM Marks as per the marksheet.
- Upload documents in the required format and guidelines.
- Upload all the mandatory files and documents.
- One application per student. Avoid submitting another application for the same candidate.
- Never claim quota and special category under false documents.
- Confirm once the application fee is paid, and keep evidence of the same.
- Always enter an active email ID or contact number in the application form.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **How to edit the TNEA application form?**

Students can fill out the application form after logging into the TNEA online application portal. The edits can be done only before the final submission of the application. Once submitted, no online edits are permitted.

### 2. **Where to find the TNEA Application number?**

The TNEA Application number can be found after logging into the official portal. It is the random 10-digit unique number allotted to each student. It is also referred to as a random number.

### 3. **Can students make changes in the TNEA application correction window?**

No, students cannot make any changes after the final submission of the application form, as there is no TNEA application correction window. Although students can try to report any changes during the grievance redressal window.

### 4. **What are the credentials required for the TNEA Application login?**

Students require the TNEA application login ID and password for application login.

### 5. How to retrieve the TNEA Application login credentials?

Students can retrieve login credentials, such as passwords, through the official portal. Click on the forgot password and a link to set a new password will be sent to the registered email ID. Follow the instructions and change the password.
