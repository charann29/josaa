Once the information posted online has been confirmed, the TS Inter Hall Ticket 2026 can also be downloaded by the candidates directly on the official site of the board by adhering to the following procedure. - Click on the link for the TS Intermediate Hall Ticket 2026
- Select the relevant option for 1st Year or 2nd Year
