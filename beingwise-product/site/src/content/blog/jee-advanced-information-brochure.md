While the exam date has already been declared by the authorities as May 18th, the information clarified the registration dates.

## Details in the JEE Advanced 2025 Information Broucher

JEE Advanced opens the gates for many aspirants for admission into undergraduate courses in prestigious institutions like IITs. IIT Kanpur is conducting JEE Mains & Advanced for 2025 and the information brochure released by IIT Kanpur has the following details which are necessary to help the students effectively plan their preparation.

- **Overview of JEE Advanced:** The JEE Advanced 2025 will be conducted by the seven Zonal Coordinating IITs under the Joint Admission Board (JAB) 2025. It will determine admissions to IITs for Bachelor’s, Integrated Masters, and Dual Degree programs for the 2025-26 academic year. Final decisions will be made by JAB 2025.

- **Exam Schedule:** The exam is held in 2 sessions for paper 1 and paper 2 on May 18th, 2025. Candidates should appear for both papers and the timing for paper 1 will be from 9:00 AM-12:00 PM, for paper 2 will be 2:30 PM-5:30 PM.

- **Registration for the Exam:** To appear for JEE Advanced 2025 candidates must first register for the exam. Registrations will commence on April 23, 2025, and close on May 2nd, 2025.

- **Documents Required for Registration:** Applicants must provide specific documents during the registration process, including proof of identity and academic information. Detailed instructions will be available on the registration portal.

- **JEE Advanced Admit Card 2025:** The admit cards for JEE Advanced 2025 will be available for download from May 11 to May 18, 2025. Candidates must ensure they carry their admit cards to the exam center.

- **Mode of Examination:** Online or Computer Based Test

- **Guidelines:** The information brochure includes the guidelines to follow during the exam day.

- **Registration Fee:** The registration will be based on the location and the applicant’s category.
  - Indian Nationals: Female and SC/ST/PwD – INR 1600; All others-INR 3200
  - Foreign Nationals: Applicants from SAARC Countries – 100 USD; Applicants from Non-SAARC Countries – 200 USD

- **Eligibility Criteria:** Candidates appearing for JEE Advanced 2025 should be among the top 2,50,000 candidates who cleared JEE Mains 2025.
  - **Age limit:** Born on or after October 1, 2000. Age relaxation of five years for candidates from SC, ST, and PwD categories.
  - **Attempts:** Maximum two attempts in consecutive years are accepted.
  - **Class 12th:** Applicants must have appeared for their Class 12th (or equivalent) exam for the first time in 2024 or 2025, with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
