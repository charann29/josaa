## JEE Advanced Eligibility Criteria 2026

Candidates looking to register for the exam must meet the minimum eligibility criteria before registration. Below are some of the mandatory eligibility norms as per the authorities.

- Must qualify JEE Main and be within the top 2.5 lakh candidates.
- Maximum two attempts allowed in consecutive years.
- Date of birth should be on or after October 1, 2000 (5-year relaxation for SC/ST/PwD).
- Must have appeared for Class 12 for the first time in 2024 or 2025.
- Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics are mandatory subjects.
- Candidates previously admitted to an IIT are not eligible.
- Foreign nationals follow separate eligibility rules.

## JEE Advanced Syllabus 2026
