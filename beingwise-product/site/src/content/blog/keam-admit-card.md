**KEAM 2025 Admit Card**: The Commissioner for Entrance Examination, Kerala, released the KEAM 2025 Admit Cards. The candidates who submitted the online application to the CEE for the Kerala Engineering and Pharmacy examination, which will be held from 22nd April to 30th April(23-04-2025 and 25-04-2025 to 29-04-2025 for engineering streams)( 24-04-2025 and 29-04-2025 for Pharmacy Streams)  at different centres in Kerala, Mumbai, New Delhi, and Dubai, can download their admit cards from the link here: [Admit Card Link](https://www.cee.kerala.gov.in/keam2025/)

The Candidates can download their admit cards through the link provided on the CEE official website. The candidates can enter into their respective profile pages by giving the **Application Number** and **Password** of KEAM 2025. By clicking the menu **‘Admit Card’** provided on the Profile page, the admit card can be downloaded. Before downloading the admit card, candidates should ensure that all the details mentioned on the admit card are accurate. The candidates must carry the KEAM 2025 admit card to the examination hall along with any authorized ID cards like School Identity Card/PAN Card/Driving License/Voter ID/Aadhaar Card/E-Aadhaar/Passport/Class 12 Hall Ticket or Admit Card/Bank Passbook with photograph, to prove their identity.

Candidates must carry their admit card to be permitted to the exam hall.

## How to download the KEAM Admit Card 2025?

To obtain the KEAM 2025 admit card, candidates can refer to the following steps:

**Step 1:** Navigate to the official website of KEAM 2025, accessible at <https://cee.kerala.gov.in>

**Step 2:** Select the ‘KEAM Candidate Portal’ option.

**Step 3:** Provide your login details, including application number, password, and access code.

**Step 4:** Then, the  **‘Admit Card’** menu will appear on the screen.

**Step 5:** Now, candidates should download the admit card and secure it for the exam day

## Details Mentioned on KEAM 2025 Admit card

Candidates have to cross-check the below details mentioned on the KEAM 2025 admit card:

- Applicant’s Name
- Application Number
- Roll Number
- Course Name
- Date of Birth
- Gender
- Category
- State of Eligibility
- Candidate’s Photograph & Signature
- KEAM Exam Centre and Centre Code
- KEAM Exam Date and Shift
- Paper (Paper-I or Paper-II)
- Exam Day Guidelines

## Errors to check for in KEAM 2025 Admit Card

Candidates need to verify carefully the following details to avoid errors:

- Name of the candidate
- Paper opted for
- Date of Birth
- Gender
- Test Centre Name
- City
- State Code of Eligibility
- Category

**Note:** In case of any discrepancy in the KEAM Admit card, the candidates should immediately inform the authorities of CEE, Kerala.

## Documents Required to Carry on KEAM 2025 Exam

Candidates need to carry the required documents mentioned below on the day of the KEAM 2025 exam:

- KEAM Admit card
- Valid photo ID proof such as an Aadhar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, Driving License, or Passport
- Passport size photograph

## KEAM 2025 Exam Day Guidelines

Candidates are advised to follow the KEAM 2025 exam day guidelines mentioned below:

1. Candidates must arrive at the exam center at least 1 hour before the scheduled exam time.
2. Candidates are strictly prohibited from leaving the exam hall before the completion of the exam.
3. Candidates must carry their admit card, valid ID proof, and passport-size photos.
4. candidates are not allowed to bring electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited inside the exam hall
5. Any instance of malpractice or misconduct during the exam will result in immediate disqualification.
