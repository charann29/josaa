## What is the Process of Behind Every NIAT placement?

The NIAT placement process is a 4-step system designed to support students at every stage of their career journey. It begins with a skill assessment to evaluate both technical and soft skills, helping identify strengths and areas for improvement. This is followed by company outreach, where 100+ companies visit every month to explore hiring opportunities. Students also receive dedicated interview preparation through mock interviews, guidance, and coaching sessions to build confidence and readiness. Finally, comprehensive placement support is provided, assisting students from receiving the job offer all the way to successful onboarding.

## What Are The Workshops at the Universities collaborating with NIAT India?

As the NIAT placements are focused on collaborating with top tech giants to help students build a better future with them, Workshops are often conducted to give them real-world exposure. Have a look at Workshop projects done during the NIAT upskilling training program:

### [1. Life Saving Drones](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z5Kd__fXJn0)

First-year students, some with no prior experience, learned about drone technology, its components, and its potential applications, such as waste collection in slums and neutralizing toxic gases in mines. The workshop combined theoretical learning with practical, hands-on experience, including building and flying their own drones using mobile controls and programming them through Signas ID.

### [2. Productivity Game in 12 Hours](https://youtu.be/njX9kcVJHoQ?si=G6mniUPZPVL9rGco)

The team has built a gamified productivity app to motivate users to study or be productive by offering rewards and incentives. Users input tasks, and AI calculates completion time, granting XP. Leaderboards rank users by XP. The website will feature hero and monster avatars, where completing quests (quizzes) increases XP and helps defeat the monster. The time taken for a quest influences XP gain. Future plans include an adventure map of completed quests and a coupon system for real-life discounts. The app is being built as a no-coding website using AI tools for design and planning.

### [3. Building a Website in 7 Hours](https://youtu.be/4ktbPmCKEwA?si=6H1aD4PvtoeByznX)

Students from NIAT built a static website for event management. It took them about 3 hours to fully complete the prototype. They were inspired by a health monitoring device idea but focused on the event website. One student had previously built a similar website in 2.5 hours. They view this as a “starting step to building something good”.

### [4. Self-Driving Car](https://youtu.be/S788A1OAOvo?si=L7NtM1FX5QnSjnDw)

The workshop highlighted physical AI, where machines can see and think, a concept emphasised by Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang. This technology has the potential to revolutionise industries like manufacturing and warehousing through automation and robotics. The workshop also included coding, simulations, and live demonstrations of vehicles and parts, providing a clear understanding of AI self-driving car functionality.

## Conclusion

NIAT (NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies) prepares students for the real world through an upskilling program that combines industry-aligned training modules, hands-on project-based learning, and structured mentorship, delivered by NxtWave’s own Trainers, Mentors, and Success Coaches at the Training Centre or Upskilling Centre provided by the collaborating university. From building life-saving drones to developing AI-powered self-driving car simulations and gamified productivity apps, students enrolled in NIAT’s upskilling program get practical exposure to emerging technologies from the very first year.

The collaborating universities further strengthen this journey by maintaining a network of 3000+ companies, offering students access to internships, job placements, resume building, mock interviews, aptitude preparation, and salary negotiation support. It is important to note that placement facilitation is managed independently by each collaborating university; NIAT do not directly manage or guarantee placements. NIAT’s role is to ensure that students are thoroughly skilled and industry-ready by the time they step into the NIAT placement process, giving them a strong foundation to compete confidently in the tech job market.
