## JEE Mains Admit Card 2025: Steps to Download

JEE Mains Admit Card 2025 was available on the official website till the exam date. Candidates could download the admit card by providing their application number and password or their application number and date of birth. A detailed step-by-step procedure to download **JEE Mains 2025 Admit Card** is mentioned below.

- **Access the official website:** Navigate to the official website jeemain.nta.nic.in to download the JEE Mains 2025 admit card.

- **Click the link:**Click on the [JEE Main admit card link,](https://examinationservices.nic.in/JEEMain2025/downloadadmitcard/LoginPWD.aspx?enc=Ei4cajBkK1gZSfgr53ImFVj34FesvYg1WX45sPjGXBpvTjwcqEoJcZ5VnHgmpgmK) which will be displayed on the official website.

- **Details Required:** The Details Required for Accessing the Admit Card are as follows:
  - Application Number
  - Password
  - Captcha Code

- **Consider your preference:** Two options will be displayed where you can choose to download the admit card through the application number and password/application number and date of birth. Select one among those.

- **Sign in:** Sign in after providing all the details and entering the security pin provided in the captcha.

- **Admit Card:** Check for Your admit card as it will be displayed in the candidate portal.

- **Download:** Confirm your details and download the admit card for future reference.

## **Details Mentioned in JEE Mains Admit Card 2025**

JEE Mains Admit Card 2025 contains the following details:

- **Candidate Information:** Name, roll number, photograph, signature, and date of birth.
- **Exam Details:** Exam date, shift, and timing.
- **Center Information:** Address of the exam center with its code.
- **Instructions:** Important guidelines regarding the exam day, including what items are allowed and prohibited inside the exam hall.
- **Signatures and Photographs:** The candidate’s and exam authority’s signatures, along with a passport-sized photograph of the candidate.
- **QR Code:** A unique QR code for identification purposes.

The admit card is usually in PDF format, and candidates need to download and print it before the exam. It must be carried to the exam center along with a valid ID proof.

## JEE Mains Admit Card 2025: How to Correct?

Candidates can often forget their application number and struggle to download the admit card. An application number is mandatory to download your admit card. Follow these steps if you miss your application number. You will receive the application number for the registered email and phone number.

Candidates often find errors in their JEE Mains Admit Card related to personal information or academic details. It is important to correct these mistakes before appearing for the exam. Let’s check how you can apply for correction:

### **How to Correct Errors on JEE Mains 2025 Admit Card**

NTA does not provide any correction facility for the admit card. However, for such issues, candidates should immediately contact the exam authority to resolve them. Refer to the section below to get detailed information on the correction process:

- **Contact the NTA Helpline:** Students should first reach out to the official NTA helpline and clearly describe the error on the admit card. The helpline will provide instructions depending on the type of correction required.
- **Keep Supporting Documents Ready:** Documents such as Aadhaar, school ID, and photographs may be required during the correction process.
