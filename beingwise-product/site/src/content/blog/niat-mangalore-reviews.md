## Can I Join NIAT Upskilling Program in Mangalore With NAT?

Yes, if you qualify for NAT, you can join NIAT’s Upskilling program and will get an Industry Ready certificate. NAT, which stands for NxtWave Assessment Test, is a 90-minute online test that focuses on assessing whether you have the mindset and aptitude for software development careers. After that, you need to attend the mandatory one-on-one counselling session.

However, for admission to the BTech CSE program, you need to take admission to Yenepoya University. In the counselling round, based on your NAT score, 12th merit, and partner University admission criteria, you will be suggested the best fit University. In this case, it will be Yenepoya University. NIAT, then, with the written permission from the student, will share the details with the University. The final admission procedure and the admission letter will be given by Yenepoya University itself. Generally, it will take 2 to 3 weeks for you to get the admission letter from the University, then you will be admitted both for the NIAT’s upskilling program and UGC approved BTech CSE course from the Yenepoya University.
