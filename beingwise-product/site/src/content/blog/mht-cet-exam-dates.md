The Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell has announced revised exam dates for the MHT CET 2025. The scheduled dates for the PCM stream are as follows: April 19 to 27. The PCB exam dates are set from April 9th to April 17th, 2025.

This exam is open to aspiring candidates to pursue undergraduate courses in Maharashtra. The exam pattern typically contains multiple-choice questions (MCQs). The duration for both PCM and PCB exams is 180 minutes. Additionally, the MHT CET exam carries 200 marks. The exam is set to be conducted in a Computer-based test which means online mode.

Candidates preparing for this exam should stay updated with any further announcements or changes by regularly checking the official website of the Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell.

## How to download MHT CET Exam Dates?

To download the revised exam dates, candidates can follow the below steps:

- Visit the official website of MHT CET 2025: <https://cetcell.mahacet.org/>
- Click on the ‘Revised Dates for CETs A.Y. 2025-26’ available on the home page.
- You will be redirected to the page where the exam dates are mentioned
- Choose the ‘Download’ option on the screen
- Now, you can view it offline as well.

## MHT CET Admit Card Date

The Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test (MHT CET) admit cards for the PCM stream is yet to be released. To access their admit cards, candidates should visit the official website at <https://cetcell.mahacet.org/> and log in using their unique credentials, including the registered email ID and password. ## MHT CET Counselling Date

The Maharastra State Council Cell is expected to conduct the MHT CET counselling in June 2025. The complete counselling process will be announced by the CET Cell after the result. There will be 3 rounds of MHT CET counselling and a seat allotment process. The counselling process includes registration, document uploading, and fee payment. The documents of the candidates are verified by the counseling authority. Candidate who are allotted with seat can confirm their admission online by paying college fees and reporting to the college.

## MHT CET Result Date

The Maharashtra State Council Cell is expected to release the MHT CET results in June 2025. Candidates will have the opportunity to view their results by visiting the official website at <https://cetcell.mahacet.org/>. They can do so by logging in with their unique credentials, which typically include their registered email ID and password. Additionally, the CET cell authorities will publish the MHT CET topper list and provide details of all candidates who appeared for the exam.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. When MHT CET exam be Conducted in 2025?

The Maharastra State Council Cell has revised the exam dates of MHT CET due to the election held in Maharastra. So, for the PCM stream the exams are scheduled from April 19th to 27th 2025. And for the PCB stream, the exam is scheduled for April 9th to 17th 2025.

### 2. How to download the MHT CET admit card 2025?

Candidates can obtain their MHT CET admit card by accessing the official website: <https://cetcell.mahacet.org/>. They can do so by utilizing their login credentials, specifically the registered email ID and password. It’s crucial to remember that candidates must bring their admit cards to the examination hall, along with the required documents.

### 3. What is the MHT CET exam pattern for 2025?

The MHT CET exam pattern typically contains Multiple choice questions (MCQs) from subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology. The duration and marking scheme may vary as per the guidelines given by the exam authorities.

### 4. Is there any negative marking in the MHT CET 2025?

There is no negative marking in the MHT CET 2025.

### 5. Who is eligible to take the MHT CET exam?

The candidates who have completed their 10+2 or equivalent from PCM or PCB streams are eligible to take the MHT CET exam.
