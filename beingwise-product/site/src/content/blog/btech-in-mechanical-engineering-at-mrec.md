## B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering at Malla Reddy Engineering College 2025 Overview

Mechanical Engineering Course offered at the Malla Reddy Engineering College is a four-year and 8-semester program designed for students who want to pursue a career in mechanical engineering and its specializations. The course is designed as per the UGC guidelines and to ensure students learn how to apply the concepts in the real world.

The MREC curriculum is designed with a credit-based structure and follows a lecture-tutorial-practical format. The course is designed in a way that students gain a strong base in the foundational subjects and move to the core subjects in their second and third years. Students will be introduced to the practical learning methods through labs, internships, and workshops in their second and pre-final years. In the final years, students will be working on their major project with the help of the project guides and mentors.

In addition to core mechanical subjects, the program offers electives in emerging areas like Automobile Engineering, Robotics, Renewable Energy, and CAD/CAM, allowing students to specialize as per their interests and industry trends. Students can also opt for a Minor in Robotics, adding interdisciplinary value to their profile. B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering at Malla Reddy Engineering College 2025 curriculum ensures continuous internal evaluation (CIE), semester-end exams (SEE), and emphasizes skill development through Engineering Workshops, Machine Drawing, and industry-oriented projects, making graduates job-ready with both theoretical knowledge and practical competence.

## Eligibility & Admission Process for B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering at MREC

Malla Reddy Engineering College offers admissions for students through several state entrance examinations and Category-B quotas. Check the eligibility criteria below:

- Candidates who qualified for the TG EAPCET exam and were allotted a seat in the TG EAPCET counselling are eligible for Category-A admissions.

- Candidates who completed a Diploma and appeared for the TG ECET counselling are eligible for lateral entry admissions to MREC.

- Candidates are eligible for Category B seats based on their merit. Candidates can apply for the Category-B seats through the official website.
