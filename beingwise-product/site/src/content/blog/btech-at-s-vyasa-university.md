## Eligibility Criteria for B.Tech CSE at S-Vyasa University

Students aspiring to pursue B.Tech CSE at S-Vyasa have to satisfy certain eligibility criteria. These eligibility criteria are set by the officials based on the AICTE norms and candidates who fail to satisfy these are not eligible for admissions into the four-year B.Tech CSE course at S-Vyasa University. Check the eligibility criteria for S-Vyasa B.Tech Computer Science Engineering below:

- Aspiring Candidates must have passed their 10+2 or equivalent from a recognised board/university.
- Applicants must have completed studies in Physics and Mathematics as mandatory subjects, along with one additional subject from Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, or a Technical Vocational discipline.
- Candidates must have passed with 45% aggregate(for the general category) or 40% aggregate(for other categories) to be eligible for admission into a four-year B.Tech at S-Vyasa University.
