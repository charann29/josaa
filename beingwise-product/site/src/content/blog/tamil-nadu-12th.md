## Tamil Nadu 12th Exam Last Minute Preparation Strategies

As per the 12th Tamil Nadu Exam Time Table, the TN 12th exams 2026 are currently underway. Students appearing for the same are under exam pressure and stress. To overcome the last-minute exam pressure and score better, here are some tips, strategies, and exam day guidelines in the following section:

- A few days before the exam, focus on balancing the leftover preparation with rest.
- Give priority to revision and do not start with any new chapters.
- Prioritize high scoring topics and chapters.
- Keep flashcards and short notes with formulas for the last-minute glance.
- Revise class notes, diagrams, and the answer sheets of mock tests.
- Before the exam day, try to practice mock tests under strict timing conditions.
- Allocate separate reading and review time before and after answering the TN 12th question paper.
- On the Tamil Nadu 12th exam day, read the question paper thoroughly and attempt easier questions at first.
- Answer with clear numbering, headings, and labelled diagrams.
- For Subjects like Mathematics and Science, attempt all the questions as there are step-by-step marking available.
- Prioritise health and take occasional breaks between study time. Get at least 6-8 hours of sleep and eat balanced meals.
- In case of any doubts and stress, talk to your guardian or teachers.
