Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur has officially announced the JEE Advanced 2025 exam date. According to the official notification released by IIT Kanpur, the JEE Advanced 2025 exam will be conducted throughout India on **May 18.** Candidates have to appear for the exam on May 18th for two sessions: Paper 1 is scheduled from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m., and Paper 2 is scheduled from 2:30 p.m. to 5:30 p.m. [Click Here](https://jeeadv.ac.in/documents/exam_date.pdf) for the official notification.

Candidates are advised to stay updated and keep track of updates or the latest news about JEE Advanced 2025. Staying updated will ensure your preparation strategy and schedule is accurate and avoids exam stress.

## JEE Advanced 2025 Exam Details

JEE Advanced 2025 is the second stage of the exam that candidates will take to secure admissions in top institutes like IITs. Candidates have to clear JEE Mains 2025 in order to appear for JEE Advanced. Approximately 12 – 14 lakh people appear for JEE Mains and only the top 2,50,000 people can appear for JEE Advanced.

## JEE Advanced Exam Dates

The official date for the JEE Advanced 2025 exam has been announced by IIT Kanpur as May 18, 2025, which was confirmed on December 2, 2024. [Collegedost](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-advanced/) has updated this information on its website and will provide further details about the application form once it is released on the official portal. Papers 1 and 2 of the JEE Advanced 2025 will be held on the same day. Paper 1 will take place from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m., while Paper 2 will be conducted from 2:30 p.m. to 5:30 p.m.

## JEE Advanced 2025 Date for Admit Card

JEE Advanced 2025 Admit card will be released by IIT Kanpur on the official website. It is expected that the admit card will be made available to the candidates in May 2025. Candidates can download their admit card once it is released, by providing required details like registration number, DOB, phone number etc. JEE Advanced 2025 admit card will show details of candidates with photograph and examination centre with exam day instructions.

## JEE Advanced 2025 Date for Answer Key

The IIT Kanpur will release the provisional answer keys online, following the examination. These answer keys will be available on the official JEE Advanced website, enabling candidates to access them. Using the provisional answer keys, candidates can estimate their potential scores. If there are any discrepancies, candidates may challenge the answer keys by paying a fee. The final answer key, along with the results, will be published after addressing all such challenges.

## JEE Advanced 2025 Date of Results Announcement

Based on the previous year’s trends, the JEE Advanced 2025 results are expected to be announced in June 2025. Once the results are declared, candidates can check them by logging into the official JEE Advanced website. The results will include the ranks in the Common Rank List (CRL) and All India Rank (AIR) for each category. Candidates who successfully log in will also have the option to download their results for future reference, as no physical marks card will be mailed to them.
