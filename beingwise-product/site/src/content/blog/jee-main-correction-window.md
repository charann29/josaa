## What is JEE Main Correction Window?

The JEE Main Correction Window offers applicants an opportunity to amend or rectify information on their Joint Entrance Exam Main (JEE Main) Application Form via the JEE correction window after having submitted an application.

The JEE Mains 2026 correction window will be open for a short duration after each session (1 & 2) has closed. It is created to assist students in correcting their form-filling errors and, therefore, preventing issues related to students being disqualified from the exam (unqualified to take the exam) or not being able to properly issue an admit card.

## Who can use JEE Main Correction Window?

The JEE Mains 2026 correction window is open to both new and previous JEE Main 2026 applicants. Candidates can use the correction window to change specific fields in their application forms, such as course/paper selection, question paper media, state code of eligibility, examination cities, educational qualifications, gender, category, and fee payment. New candidates who have just applied for Session 2 will have limited options to change their information, such as their name, father’s name, or mother’s name.

## How to JEE Mains Correction Window 2026 Session 2?

The following is a step-by-step process to complete the correction procedure for the JEE Main application form:

Step 1: Navigate to the official JEE Main webpage on the NTA website.

Step 2: Click on the link “Application Form Correction” under JEE Main on the website.

Step 3: From the JEE Main homepage, choose the link “JEE Main Application Form Correction”.

Step 4: Enter your Application No., Password (or Date of Birth), as well as the Security Pin, and click on “Sign in” to log into your Account.

Step 5: Read the guidelines carefully before moving forward with any changes; the only fields that can be edited on the application form are those specifically designated to allow for changes.

Step 6: Double-check to make sure that the revised information provided by you is complete and accurate; specifically, make sure that your names are spelled correctly and that your numbers, as well as the correct selections of options, are

Step 7: If you are making any changes to the application fee(s) (such as changing your category or adding a second paper to the application), be prepared to pay the additional application fee(s) associated with those changes. You will be able to make those payments on the web via debit card/credit card/net banking/UPI.

Step 8: When you have completed making corrections to your application, you should press “Submit” on the Application Page to verify that the corrections have been made.

Step 9: After the correction(s) has been submitted, download and save a copy of your Confirmation Page.

## JEE Main Application Correction Fee

Typically, there are no extra fees required for correction or modifications of information during JEE Main Application Correction, provided that a candidate logs in and corrects an error through the online correction window within the specified period.

Candidates can make corrections, as long as the corrections do not affect the original application fee. It should be noted that additional fees incurred as a result of changing from the reserved to the General/OBC-NCL category, or adding papers, etc., will require payment through any of the payment methods specified in the application form (debit/credit card, net banking, or UPI).

 If no fees are to be charged for upcoming changes, then no additional payment will need to be made by the candidate for any corrections.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **Q1. What details can be changed in the JEE Main Correction window?**

According to NTA norms, candidates can make modifications to certain fields, such as personal details (limited changes), academic details, exam city preference, paper selection, category, gender, and uploaded documents such as photographs and signatures.

### **Q2. What details cannot be modified on the JEE Main 2026 application form?**

Mobile number, email address, permanent address, present address, and emergency contact information are typically non-editable after submitting the application form.

### **Q3. How to use a correction window?**

To use a JEE Main correction window session, log in to the official portal using your credentials between the mentioned dates. Click the “Correction in JEE Main Application Form” link, modify allowed fields (such as personal, academic, or document details), then review, submit, and download the updated confirmation page.

### **Q4. Is jee mains application correction window 2026 necessary?**

The JEE Main Application Form Correction Window helps candidates correct flaws in personal or academic information. It prevents application rejection due to wrong information. The JEE Main Correction window provides candidates with a final opportunity to confirm accuracy before the test procedure begins.
