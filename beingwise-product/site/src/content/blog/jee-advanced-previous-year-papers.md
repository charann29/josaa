The JEE Advanced exam is conducted by NTA every year for admission into prestigious institutions like IITs. The top 2.5 lakh candidates who qualified for the JEE Mains appear for JEE Advanced making it a highly competitive exam. While students will be busy preparing for JEE Advanced and making their personalized preparation plans, one requires a strict routine and effective revision plan to ace the JEE Advanced 2025 exam.

JEE Advanced previous year question papers act as a guide to unveil the exam pattern, toughness of the questions, and topics where you should shift your focus. Including previous years’ papers offers many indispensable benefits in your JEE Advanced exam. Candidates can access the official website jeeadv.ac.in and download the previous year’s JEE Advanced question papers. In this article, we will discuss the importance of JEE Advanced previous years papers and why you should not afford to exclude them in your exam preparation.

## Why it’s important to practice the previous year’s papers?

Although many [mock tests](/blog/jee-advanced/jee-advanced-mock-test/) offer similar types of questions across each of the three subjects, Previous year’s papers are credible sources that you should not afford to ignore. Practicing previous year’s question papers helps you feel confident during the exam. Here are some benefits of practicing the previous year’s questions.

- **Familiarize with exam pattern:** Previous year’s papers help you understand the exam pattern and the toughness of each subject. This helps in curating a perfect strategy to attempt your exam.

- **Time Management:** Practicing JEE Advanced previous year papers reveals the time you are taking for each section and helps in managing your time to finish the exam in the given time frame.

- **Important Topics:** Previous year’s papers are a guide to unveiling the most [important topics](/blog/jee-advanced/tips-to-crack-jee-advanced-2025-exam/) and those topics that are not necessarily important.

- **Boosts Confidence:** The habit of daily solving the previous year’s papers will help you feel confident in yourself.

- **Reveals Weak Areas:** By attempting various questions, you can identify areas where you lack understanding or where you tend to make mistakes, allowing you to focus on those weaknesses.

- **Helps in revising:** Previous year’s papers act as a great revision tool, helping you apply the learned concepts.

### 2. ********What Should I Wear for JEE Advanced 2025?********

Although candidates are free to follow their own [dress code](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/jee-main-dress-code-for-males-and-females/), it’s advised to wear casual like jeans and T-shirts for boys and kurtas for girls. Avoid wearing heavy or traditional clothing as it can consume a lot of time during security checks.
