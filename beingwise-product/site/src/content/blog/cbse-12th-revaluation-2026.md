The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) opened the window for CBSE Class 12 revaluation 2026 on June 2. The revaluation process for the CBSE Class 12th paper is done completely online via the official website at https://postresult.cbseit.in/pvr/

Students who have a copy of their answer sheet may apply for CBSE 12th revaluation before the last date. As per the official notification, the **last date to** **apply for revaluation CBSE 12th paper** **is June 6, 2026.**

Read the full blog to know how to apply revaluation for CBSE 12th Answer sheet, CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026  date, and fees.

## What is the Revaluation Process for CBSE Class 12th?

The process of the CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026  process is an after-result process initiated by CBSE for the correction of any mistakes in the evaluation of the answer papers of the candidate. Under the revaluation process for CBSE Class 12th, candidates can get a re-check of the selected answers after getting a photocopy of their evaluated answer papers.

### Stage-wise Revaluation Process for CBSE Class 12th Answer Sheet

There are three stages under the process of  CBSE Revaluation 2026 12th

**1. Verification of Marks**

In the first step, CBSE verifies if all answers have been evaluated, correct marks are obtained, and those are recorded in the system accurately. The marks are corrected according to the mistakes identified.

**2. Requesting a Scanned Photocopy of the Answer Book**

In the second stage, the scanned or photocopied version of the answer paper, evaluated by the examiner, can be requested from the authority to analyze the marking of the answers.

**3. Re-evaluation of the Answer Book**

In the final stage, students can apply for CBSE revaluation 12th of some selective questions to be marked again by the examiners to check if those were not evaluated or had any mistakes.

Followed the CBSE Class 12th Revialustion 2026 window closes on June 6, the board examines the answer sheets as per request and declares the revised marks, if any revision is needed after re-evaluation.

It is important to remember that the marks can increase or decrease depending on the outcome of the re-evaluation. After declaring the new marks, it becomes final and will also be included in the updated mark sheet of CBSE.

## How to Apply for CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026?

CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026  can be made by the candidates who are not satisfied with their marks through the application procedure at the official website of CBSE post result services.

Candidates should first get a Photocopy/Scanned Copy of the evaluated answer book of the candidate before applying for CBSE Revaluation 2026 12th of their answer sheets.

 It should be noted that the request for the CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026 can be made only in online mode and must be submitted before the last date. The direct link to access the CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026 portal is given below.

[**Direct link to access the CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026 Portal**](https://www.cbse.gov.in/newsite_old/rchk.html)

### Step-by-step process Procedure for revaluation of CBSE 12th Paper

For a better understanding, the detailed procedure for how to apply for revaluation in CBSE 12th Paper is described below:

**Step 1:** Visit the official site of CBSE and login into Post Result Service / Verification and Re-evaluation Service.

**Step 2:** Enter your required credentials on the portal. Candidates will also require the Aadhar card to log in.

**Step 3:** You are also required to enter the Roll No., School No., Center No., and Admit card ID to log in.

**Step 4:** Those candidates whose answer script photocopied have already been taken should only make an application for CBSE Re-evaluation 12th.

**Step 5:** Now select the option for:

- Verification of Marks
- Re-evaluation
- Verification of Marks & Re-evaluation
- Select the concerned subjects for which you want to make an application.

**Step 6:** If the candidate has applied for re-evaluation of marks, select the question number for which you want re-evaluation.

**Step 7:** Make sure that all the information provided in the form is correct.

**Step 8:** Pay the required CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026 application fee online.

**Step 9:** Submit the online application for revaluation of CBSE 12th Paper after making the payment.

## CBSE Class 12th Revaluation 2026 Application Fee

Students applying for CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026  are required to make the applicable payment online through the CBSE post-results services portal. The submission of applications and payment will be done only online.

**The application fee for the Verification of Marks process is INR 100 for each subject, while the application fee for the Re-Evaluation process is INR 100 for each question selected by candidates after getting a copy of their evaluated answer script.**

## CBSE Class 12th Revaluation Result 2026

The CBSE Class 12 Revaluation Result 2026 will be declared online after the completion of the entire procedure of re-evaluation. Students applying for CBSE Class 12th Revaluation 2026 can access the results of their revaluation through the official CBSE post-results services portal.

Students can view their marks for Revaluation CBSE Class 12 by logging into the official CBSE website using their Roll Number, School Number, Centre Number, and Admit Card Number. After logging in, they can view their revised CBSE Class 12th Result.

The CBSE 12th Revaluation 2026 procedure enables students to have their answer sheets checked in case they think an error was made during the evaluation process. Candidates who have already received the photocopy of their evaluated answer book can make applications for re-evaluation through the CBSE post-result services portal until June 6, 2026.

After completing the revaluation procedure, CBSE will declare the results for Revaluation CBSE 12th Result 2026 online. Students are encouraged to complete the application procedure within the specified deadline and check the CBSE website for further details related to revaluation results and post-result services.
