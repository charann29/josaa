## Jadavpur University B.Tech Fees 2026: Branch-Wise

**Jadavpur University B.Tech fees** are government-subsidised, keeping tuition low across almost all engineering branches. The total cost for the entire four-year B.Tech programme ranges from ₹9,600 to ₹1,26,000, as per the official fee structure at jadavpuruniversity.in.

For the vast majority of branches, the total four-year tuition is ₹20,840. The B.E. in Information Technology (Day) is the only branch with a significantly higher fee, totalling approximately ₹1,21,000. The BTech Instrumentation (3-year) and BTech Food Technology and Biochemical Engineering programmes carry a total fee of ₹15,630. ## Jadavpur University Admission Fees 2026

**Jadavpur University admission fees** are minimal. At the time of joining, candidates pay a one-time charge covering admission and associated costs. Per-semester examination fees are charged separately throughout the programme.

| Fee Component | Amount |

## Jadavpur University Hostel Fees 2026

**Jadavpur University hostel fees** are exceptionally low by any standard. The monthly room rent is ₹25. At the time of hostel admission, a one-time deposit of ₹3,000 is collected. Mess charges are billed separately and typically range from ₹1,500 to ₹3,000 per month depending on consumption.

| --- | --- |
| Monthly room rent | ₹25/month |

## Jadavpur University Entrance Exam and Cutoff 2025

Admission to all B.Tech and BE programmes at Jadavpur University is through WBJEE. Candidates must have cleared Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, with a minimum 60% aggregate (55% for SC/ST).

**Jadavpur University cutoff** ranks are among the most competitive in West Bengal. The table below shows verified WBJEE 2025 closing ranks for major branches under the General category, Home State (HS) quota — the most relevant category for most applicants.

**Jadavpur University cutoff 2026** will be published after WBJEE 2026 results are declared. Candidates can use 2025 figures as a reference benchmark.

## Jadavpur University Campus

The **Jadavpur University campus** spans two locations in Kolkata. The main campus on Raja S.C. Mallick Road in Jadavpur covers 60 acres and houses most of the engineering departments, along with the Faculties of Science and Arts. The Salt Lake campus in Sector III covers 26 acres and accommodates select engineering and interdisciplinary departments.

Both campuses have dedicated libraries. The main campus library holds over 10,000 physical volumes with a digital collection of 50,000+ titles. Labs, workshops, canteens, and sports facilities are available across both locations. Annual fests include Sanskriti (cultural) and Srijan (technical).

## Scholarships and Jadavpur University Fees Concessions

Given the already low tuition base, scholarships at JU can further reduce the financial burden for eligible students.

- **Swami Vivekananda Merit-Cum-Means Scholarship (SVMCM):** The primary state government scholarship for West Bengal students. Covers a portion of living and academic costs for students meeting the income threshold.
- **Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW):** Students from families with an annual income below ₹2.5 lakhs may be eligible for full or partial tuition waiver under state government schemes. JU’s WBJEE 2025 data also shows a dedicated TFW category in seat allotment, with CSE TFW closing rank at 352 (HS quota).

## Conclusion

Jadavpur University remains one of the very few institutions in India where a top-20 NIRF engineering education can be accessed at a total tuition cost of under ₹21,000. The fee structure has historically been kept low through state government funding, and that holds true for the 2026 admission cycle across all branches except Information Technology.

For WBJEE 2026 aspirants, the financial case for targeting JU is straightforward. The real challenge is the cutoff CSE and IT require ranks well under 500 in the General (HS) category, and even the broader branches like Mechanical and Electrical close around 800. Candidates should use the 2025 cutoff data as a planning benchmark while the official WBJEE 2026 results and counselling schedule are awaited.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. What are the total Jadavpur University B.Tech fees for 4 years?**

Ans. For most branches, the total tuition fee is ₹20,840. For BE in Information Technology (Day), it is approximately ₹1,21,000. B.Tech Instrumentation and Food Technology have a total fee of ₹15,630.

**2. What are the Jadavpur B.Tech fees per year?**

Ans. For branches with a total fee of ₹20,840, the annual tuition is approximately ₹5,210. For IT (Day), it is around ₹30,250 per year.

**3. What are the Jadavpur University hostel fees per month?**

Ans. Hostel room rent is ₹25 per month. A one-time refundable deposit of ₹3,000 is required at admission. Mess charges are separate and usually range from ₹1,500 to ₹3,000 per month.

**4. What is the Jadavpur University admission fee?**

Ans. Around ₹360 is charged as admission and related fees at the time of joining. Additionally, a per-semester examination fee of approximately ₹60–₹70 is applicable.

**5. Which entrance exam is required for Jadavpur University B.Tech?**

Ans. WBJEE is the primary entrance exam required for admission to all BE and B.Tech programs at Jadavpur University.

**6. What is the Jadavpur University cutoff for CSE in 2025?**

Ans. The WBJEE 2025 closing rank for BE in Computer Science and Engineering was 238 for the General category under Home State quota. For the All India quota, the closing rank was 78.

**7. Is the Jadavpur University fee structure the same for all branches?**

Ans. No, most branches have a total fee of ₹20,840, but IT (Day) has a higher fee of approximately ₹1,21,000. Instrumentation and Food Technology have a total fee of ₹15,630.
