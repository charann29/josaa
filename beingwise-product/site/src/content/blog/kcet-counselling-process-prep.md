## Step-Wise KCET Counselling Process

Get an overview of the KCET Counselling process from the following section:

1. **KCET Counseling Registration:**

- Open the [KEA Counselling portal](https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea/) once the registration begins.
- Log in using the KCET Application Number & Password/OTP.
- Complete the registration process by filling in the required academic and personal details.

2. **KCET Document Verification Round**:

- Attend the KCET verification 2026 round at the designated KEA centres.
- Here, the academic documents, category and reservation certificates are verified.
- Once done, students get a KCET verification slip and eligibility confirmation.

3. **Option Entry or Choice Filling Round**:

- Once this round begins, students can choose their preferred Colleges/Branches.
- These choices should be made in order of preference.
- Students opt for multiple choices for better allotment chances.

4. **Mock Seat Allotment**:

- Based on the entered options and preferences, a mock allotment result list is released.
- Helps in understanding probable seat allotments.
- Students get access to modify, reorder, add, or delete options after the KCET seat allotment.

5. **First Round KCET seat allotment**:

- KEA releases the Round 1 KCET Seat allotment results online.
- Seats are allotted based on KCET rank, Reservation Category, seat availability, and option preferences.

6. **Seat Acceptance & KCET Counselling Fees Payment**:

- After the KCET Seat allotment results, students need to accept the allotted seats.
- They also have the option of upgrading to the next round or rejecting the seat.

7. **Report to the allotted College and pay fees**:

- Students who have the seat allotment need to download the allotment order.
- KCET Counselling Fees need to be paid before that to block the seat.
- Report to the allotted college within the given deadline.
- Submit the original documents to confirm admission.

Vacant seats are filled in Round 2 of the KCET Seat Allotment Round. In this round, students can get better college/branch options. Extended and Mop-up Rounds are conducted for the remaining vacant seats.

## KCET Document Verification 2026

KCET document verification is a mandatory step before counselling. Candidates must get their original documents verified at designated KEA centres as per their eligibility clause. Without a valid verification slip, candidates cannot participate in seat allotment.

| Event | Date |
| --- | --- |
| Slot booking for document verification | May 7, 2026 |
| Document verification (clauses a1, b1, b to o) | May 11 to May 22, 2026 |
| Verification venue | KEA, 18th Cross, Malleshwaram, Bangalore 560012 |

### Who Needs to Attend Document Verification?

- Clause A candidates verified via SATS: auto-verified, no visit required
- Clause A candidates who studied in Kannada medium/Rural/Religious/Linguistic reservations: must get documents verified by Principal of their PUC/Class 12 college or nearest Govt PU college
- Clauses B, C, D, T, J, K, L, M, N: offline verification at KEA centre
- Clauses E, F, G, H, O: academic verification at PUC/Class 12 college; Defence/CAPF-related documents to be submitted separately at KEA

## Understand KCET Option Entry

Students preparing for the KCET Counselling process need to understand the Opting Entry round to get the best option of colleges and desired branches. In the KCET Option Entry, students get to choose their preferred engineering college, courses/branches, and preferred order of admission. It is more of a choice-filling round conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA). Students need to do the KCET Option entry through the official KEA website. Keep the following tips in mind for the KCET option Entry:

- Arrange options from Highest to lowest preference
- Include a mix of Dream colleges, safe colleges, and backup options.
- Check the previous year cutoffs before filling in options
- Do not limit to a few choices
- The option of modifying choices after mock KCET seat allotment is available.

## Mistakes To Avoid During the KCET Counselling Process

Candidates preparing for the KCET Counselling process need to avoid making the following mistakes, which may lead to application cancellation.

- Filling a lesser option during option entry.
- Arranging colleges in the wrong preference order.
- Choosing a college without referring to the previous year’s cutoffs.
- Ignoring to give priority to a safe college option
- Choosing a college based on its brand value and ignoring branch interest.
- Missing the KCET counselling process deadlines
- Uploading the documents incorrectly.
- Forgetting to lock in the options.
- Failing to report to the allotted college

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Where to go for KCET document verification?

Students must visit the designated KEA verification centres to complete KCET document verification during the counselling process.

### 2. When will the KCET provisional seat allotment begin?

The KCET provisional seat allotment is expected to begin around July 2026 as per the counselling schedule.

### 3. What is the KCET spot rank?

KCET spot rank is a special rank assigned to students whose ranks were initially withheld due to missing or incorrect information in their application or qualifying exam details.

### 4. What is the KCET verification last date 2026?

The KCET document verification is expected to be conducted between May 11 and May 22, 2026.

### 5. What is the KCET counselling fees for B.Tech?

The official counselling fees for 2026 are yet to be announced, but it is expected to be around INR 33,810 for general category students in government colleges.

### 6. When are the additional counselling rounds conducted?

Additional rounds such as second, extended, or special rounds are conducted for candidates who do not secure a seat in the initial counselling rounds.
