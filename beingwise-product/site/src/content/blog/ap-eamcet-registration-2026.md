## Table of Contents

AP EAMCET (also called AP EAPCET 2026) is Andhra Pradesh’s state-level entrance test for admission to B.E/BTech, B.Pharmacy, and Agriculture courses. The exam authority will open AP EAMCET 2026 application form correction facility on April 11. Candidates can make edits till April 13, 2026.
JNTU Kakinada conducts AP EAMCET 2026 on behalf of APSCHE. The application window without late fees closed on **March 24, 2026** at cets.apsche.ap.gov.in.. The engineering exam is scheduled for **May 12–15 & 18, 2026**, and the Agriculture/Pharmacy exam for **May 19 to May 20, 2026**. Results are going to be released tentatively on 1st June 2026.

To help students and parents navigate the process, here is a summary of **important dates**, a **step-by-step registration guide**, the **documents you must prepare**, and **common mistakes to avoid**.

## Step-by-Step Registration Process of AP EAPCET 2026

Given below is the step-by-step process for AP EAMCET registration 2026.

- **Check eligibility & pay the application fee.** Before applying, ensure you meet the eligibility (e.g. you should have passed or be appearing in 12th grade with the required subjects). Also note the minimum age: engineering and pharmacy candidates must be **at least 16 years old by Dec 31, 2026**.
- Next, visit the **official website** (cets.apsche.ap.gov.in) and go to the AP EAPCET registration page. Pay the AP EAMCET application fee online of **INR 800 for OC**, **INR 700 for SC/ST**, and **INR 750 for BC** candidates. using Debit/Credit card, Net Banking or AP/TS Online. Keep the payment reference ID handy for the next steps.

- **Fill in personal and academic details.** After payment, fill out the online application form. Enter your personal details (name, date of birth, contact information) and educational details. You will need your **Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (Intermediate) hall ticket numbers** on hand, along with details like caste, income category and Aadhaar number. Also, provide information on any special reservations (PH, NCC, sports, etc.) if applicable.

- **Upload a photo and a signature.** The form will ask you to upload a recent passport-sized **photograph** and your **signature** in the prescribed format. Use a clear, scanned colour photo and a legible signature image. Ensure the file sizes and formats meet the instructions. Also, upload any required certificates in the form (e.g. caste or income certificate) if asked.
- **Verify details and submit.** Carefully review all entries. Once everything is correct, click the **Submit** button. After submission, a confirmation page will show your **registration number** and filled-in details. Download or print this confirmation.
- **Print the application form (for exam day).** After submission, take a printout of the filled application form (which includes your registration number and details). Affix a recent colour photograph on this printout and get it attested by a Gazetted Officer or the Principal of your college. Carry this printed and signed form along with you to the examination centre as proof of registration.

### Documents Required

Keep the following documents ready before you apply. These are needed for filling in details in the form and for admission later (as listed in official sources):

- **Class 10 (SSC) marks memo** – for date of birth and school records
- **Class 12 (Intermediate) marks memo or hall ticket**
- **Study certificates** from Class 6 to 12 (for local status verification)
- **Transfer Certificate (TC)** from your previous institution
- **Caste certificate** (if applicable for SC/ST/BC candidates)
- **Income certificate** (especially if applying for fee reimbursement)
- **Aadhaar card** (or Aadhaar enrollment slip) for identity verification
- **Passport-size photograph (scanned)** for upload
- **Signature image (scanned)** for upload
- **Residence or local status certificate** (or **Ration Card** or study certificate) for local area verification
- **Parents’ income details** (for scholarships or fee waiver)

Ensure all certificate details (name, number, validity) match exactly what you enter online. For example, enter your SSC hall ticket number and date of birth exactly as on your marks memo. Double-check spellings of names.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid During AP EAMCET Registration 2026

Candidates should be very careful while filling out the AP EAPCET application form. Check out some of the common mistakes that candidates may make while filling out the registration form.

- **Waiting until the last minute:** Do not leave registration to the final day. Server traffic or payment delays can cause issues.
- **Entering incorrect information:** A common error is mistyping hall ticket numbers, date of birth or contact details. Always double-check these fields.
- **Uploading wrong or poor-quality images:** Ensure your photo and signature meet the size and format requirements. Blurry or improperly scanned images can lead to rejections.
- **Ignoring confirmation:** After submitting, make sure you actually receive or download the application confirmation. If you don’t see the confirmation page, do not assume the form was submitted.
- **Missing documents:** Have all required certificates ready before you start. For example, if you belong to a reserved category, ensure you have your valid caste certificate at hand.
- **Skipping payment status check:** If the transaction fails or the website disconnects during payment, use the “Know Your Payment Status” option on the site to verify before retrying.

## AP EAMCET 2026 Exam Pattern & Syllabus

The AP EAMCET 2026 exam assesses candidates on subjects relevant to their chosen stream (Engineering or Agriculture & Pharmacy). Understanding the exam pattern and syllabus is essential for effective preparation.

### **Exam Pattern**

- **Mode:** Online (Computer-Based Test)
- **Duration:** 3 hours (180 minutes)
- **Type of Questions:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
- **Total Questions:** 160
- **Total Marks:** 160 (1 mark per question)
- **Marking Scheme:** No negative marking for incorrect answers
- **Medium:** English & Telugu (Urdu available at select centres)

**Subject-wise Distribution:**

Stream Subjects & Questions Engineering Mathematics: 80
Physics: 40
Chemistry: 40 Agriculture & Pharmacy Botany: 40
Zoology: 40
Physics: 40
Chemistry: 40

### **Syllabus**

The syllabus for AP EAMCET 2026 is based on the 10+2 level curriculum as prescribed by the Board of Intermediate Education, Andhra Pradesh. It covers the following subjects and major topics:

**Engineering Stream:**

- **Mathematics:** Algebra, Trigonometry, Vector Algebra, Probability, Calculus, Coordinate Geometry, etc.
- **Physics:** Units and Measurements, Laws of Motion, Thermodynamics, Waves, Optics, Modern Physics, etc.

## How to Edit AP EAMCET 2026 Application Form? (Correction Window, Steps & Editable Fields)

AP EAMCET 2026 application form correction allows candidates to edit selected details online during the official correction window. Students must log in using their Application ID and password, update allowed fields such as contact details or category, and submit the corrected form before the deadline.

The AP EAMCET correction facility allows candidates to edit mistakes made during registration. This is a **one-time opportunity**, so use it carefully.

### How to Make Corrections

- Visit the official website: [cets.apsche.ap.gov.in](http://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in).
- Click on “Application Correction”
- Enter your Application ID, Password, and Date of Birth
- Make the allowed changes
- Submit and download the corrected form

## How to Retrieve AP EAMCET 2026 Application ID and Password? (Step-by-Step Guide)

Candidates who forget their AP EAMCET 2026 Application ID or password can recover them through the official website using the “Forgot Application ID” or “Forgot Password” options by entering their registered mobile number, date of birth, and receiving an OTP for verification.

If you forget your **Application ID or Password**, don’t panic. The official portal provides an easy recovery option.

### Steps to Retrieve Application ID

- Go to the AP EAMCET official website
- Click on “Forgot Application ID”
- Enter:
  - Candidate’s name
  - Father’s name
  - Date of birth
  - Registered mobile number
- Your Application ID will be displayed on the screen

### Steps to Reset Password

- Click on “Forgot Password”
- Enter your:
  - Application ID
  - Registered mobile number
  - Date of birth
- An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number
- Set a new password and log in again

## **AP EAMCET 2026 Answer Key, Result, and Rank Card**

After the AP EAMCET 2026 exam is conducted, the next major steps for candidates are the release of the answer key, declaration of results, and downloading the rank card. Understanding these steps helps you check your performance, challenge any discrepancies, and prepare for counselling.

### **AP EAMCET 2026 Answer Key**

- **Provisional Answer Key Release:** The exam authority (JNTU Kakinada) will publish the provisional AP EAMCET 2026 answer key on the official website ([cets.apsche.ap.gov.in](http://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/)) a few days after the exam.
- **Purpose:** The answer key allows candidates to compare their responses and estimate their probable scores before the official results.
- **Raising Objections:** If you find any discrepancies in the provisional answer key, you can submit objections online within the specified window. You’ll need to provide supporting documents or explanations for each objection.
- **Final Answer Key:** After reviewing all objections, the final answer key is released. This key is used for result calculation.

### **AP EAMCET 2026 Result Declaration**

- **Result Date:** The AP EAMCET 2026 results are expected to be announced around **June 1, 2026** (tentative).
- **How to Check:** Results will be available online. Log in to the official website using your **Application ID** and **Hall Ticket Number**.
- **Details Provided:** The result will display your total marks, subject-wise scores, qualifying status, and (if released) your normalised score.

### **AP EAMCET 2026 Rank Card**

- **Rank Card Release:** Along with or soon after the results, the AP EAMCET 2026 **rank card** will be available for download from the official portal.
- **How to Download:** Use your Application ID and Hall Ticket Number to access and download the rank card PDF.
- **Information on Rank Card:** Your overall rank, marks obtained, and category rank (if applicable) will be mentioned. The rank card is essential for the counselling and admission process.
- **Marks vs Rank:** The authority may publish a **marks vs rank** table to help candidates understand their standing among all test-takers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1. Is AP EAMCET counselling registration started?

No, AP EAMCET counselling registration has not started yet for 2026. The counselling registration for AP EAPCET will follow after the exams are over.

### Q2. What is the AP EAMCET 2026 registration date and last date to apply?

The AP EAMCET 2026 registration date started on February 4, 2026. The last date without a late fee is March 7, 2026, while applications with a late fee are accepted until March 26, 2026.

### Q3. Can I edit mistakes after submitting the AP EAMCET registration form?

Yes, candidates can correct limited details during the official correction window. However, critical fields like name and date of birth usually cannot be edited online.

### Q4. Is AP EAMCET counselling registration separate from exam registration?

Yes, AP EAMCET counselling registration is conducted separately after the results are declared. Candidates must complete counselling registration to participate in seat allotment.

### Q5. When will AP EAMCET 2026 exam be conducted?

As per the schedule, the AP EAPCET 2026 exam date for Engineering is May 12–15 & 18, 2026, and for Agriculture/Pharmacy it is May 19 to May 20, 2026.

### Q6. What documents are required for AP EAMCET registration details verification?

Students typically need Class 10 and 12 hall ticket numbers, Aadhaar details, caste/income certificates (if applicable), photograph, signature, and study certificates.

### Q7. Is AP EAMCET 2025 registration similar to AP EAMCET 2026?

Yes, the process remains mostly the same. Students familiar with AP EAMCET registration 2025 or AP EAMCET 2025 registration details will find the 2026 process similar, with minor updates in dates or fees.
