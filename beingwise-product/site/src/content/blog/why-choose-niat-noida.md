## What is Recognition and Accreditation of NIAT Upskilling Centre in Noida?

NIAT in Noida is an upskilling program and not a recognised institution, college, or university. As such, NIAT does not hold any independent approvals or accreditations from bodies such as AICTE, UGC, or NAAC. However, the university, collaborating with NIAT – Noida International University, holds its own established recognitions, affiliations, and regulatory approvals, ensuring that the degree programs it offers meet national academic and quality standards.

The credibility of the degree earned by students comes entirely from Noida International University, which independently awards degrees that are valid for higher education pursuits and employment opportunities both in India and abroad. NIAT’s role is focused on delivering industry-aligned training modules, project-based learning, and skill-building through its Training Centre, helping students develop competencies that are aligned with real-world job market demands.

### What is NIAT?

NIAT, which stands for **NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies**, is an upskilling program launched in 2023 that trains students on cutting-edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science. Rather than being a university or college, NIAT is a skill-focused program that runs alongside a university B.Tech degree — with students receiving their degree from NIAT’s collaborating universities while building industry-ready competencies through NIAT’s training modules, project-based learning, and structured skill-map.

#### NIAT Fee Structure

The **NIAT fee** for the upskilling program ranges between **INR 8 Lakhs to INR 18 Lakhs** for the full four-year program, depending on the Training Centre and collaborating university. The collaboration of **NIAT college fee structure** varies across locations.

#### NIAT Across India: NIAT Delhi/NIAT Noida

NIAT operates Training Centres across multiple cities in India through collaborations with reputed universities. **NIAT Delhi**/NIAT Noida is among the expanding locations where NIAT conducts its upskilling programs, bringing the same industry-aligned training modules, experienced Trainers, and placement support to students in the Delhi region. Each Training Centre maintains a consistent standard of upskilling, ensuring students across all locations receive an equally high-quality learning experience.

### Q9. Is the degree earned through the collaboration of NIAT with NIU in Noida valid for higher studies and global opportunities?

NIAT does not award degrees of its own. The B.Tech CSE degree is independently awarded by Noida International University (NIU), which is UGC-recognised and NAAC A+ accredited. Since the degree comes directly from NIU, it carries the full academic credibility of a university-awarded qualification and is valid for higher education pursuits and employment opportunities both in India and abroad.
Additionally, students who complete NIAT’s upskilling program receive an Industry Ready Certificate (IRC) from NxtWave, which validates their hands-on, job-market-ready skills.

### Q10. How can I get started with NIAT in Noida and the collaborating university admission process?

Start by visiting the official NIAT website and expressing your interest in the upskilling program. Candidates are required to appear for the NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test). Following the NAT, a counselling process will walk you through the program details, fee structure, and university offerings. With your written consent, NxtWave will then share your details with Noida International University as part of the facilitation process. Please note that final admission to the B.Tech degree program is governed entirely by NIU’s own eligibility criteria and admission policy, and enrolling with NIAT does not guarantee a university seat.
