## AP Inter Results: Passing Criteria

The AP Intermediate passing marks for 2025 are set at 35% in each subject. Students must achieve the minimum marks in both theory and practical/project components separately to qualify. For subjects without practicals, internal assessments or projects are considered, and the same 35% rule applies. For example, in a subject with 100 marks, students need at least 35 marks; for 75 marks, 26 marks; and for 60 marks, 21 marks are required. Failing to meet these criteria results in a failed subject. However, students can still pass the year by appearing for supplementary exams and clearing the failed subjects.
