**AP EAMCET Preparation Books**: Students preparing for the AP EAMCET 2025 examination can find the AP EAMCET 2025 Preparation Books for MPC & BiPC here. The student must be aware of the complete AP EAMCET Syllabus to start their preparation. The preparation books help the students understand the topics from every subject and thorough practice results in achieving the maximum score in the examination.

Additionally, the students can consider the high weightage and important topics and practice the Previous Year’s Question Papers and mock Tests for a clear understanding of their level of preparation.

The AP EAMCET is a common state-level entrance test conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada under the APSCHE (Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education). The AP EAMCET 2025 examination is expected to be conducted from **May 2025 for the Agriculture & Pharmacy** Stream and from **May 2025 for the Engineering** Stream.

## AP EAMCET Preparation Books

Below are the important preparation books for Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology subjects. Follow them for optimal preparation.

### Mathematics Best Books

- Mathematics (VOL – 1A, 2A, 1B, 2B)  by Deepthi / Arihant Publications
- Handbook of Mathematics by Arihant Publications
- EAMCET Mathematics Chapterwise 25yrs Solutions (By Arihant Publications)
- Class XI & XII by R.D. Sharma
- NCERT Solutions for Mathematics

### Physics Best Books

- Text Book for Intermediate (First Year & Second Year)
- Physics I and II Question Bank (Telugu Academy)
- Chapterwise Solutions (By Arihant Publications)
- Handbook of Physics (By Arihant Publications)
- NCERT Solutions for Physics

> Also Check: [AP EAMCET 2025 Mock Test](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/ap-eamcet-mock-test/)

### Chemistry Best Books

- Text Book for Intermediate (First Year & Second Year) by Telugu Academy
- EAMCET Chemistry Text Book (VOL – I & II) by Deepthi Publications
- EAMCET Chemistry Chapterwise 25yrs Solutions (By Arihant Publications)
- NCERT Solutions for Chemistry
- Handbook of Chemistry by Arihant Publications

### Biology Best Books

- All in one Biology Text Book (By Arihant Publications)
- Trueman’s Elementary Biology (By KN Bhatia and MP Tyagi)
- Biology 10yrs papers (By Arihant Publications)
- NCERT Biology Text Books (Both 11 and 12)
