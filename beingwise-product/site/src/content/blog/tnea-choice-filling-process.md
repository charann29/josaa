## Key Features of TNEA Choice Filling 2026

TNEA Choice Filling is an important part of the TNEA counselling process. It is an online process where shortlisted candidates select and align their preferred colleges and branches. This process starts once the TNEA rank list is published, where students’ normalized Class 12 PCM marks form the basis of selection. Students can conduct the choice filling in TNEA through the online TNEA website. Some of the key features of the TNEA Choice Filling are as follows:

- Access to multiple rounds
- Students get to add up to 100 choices from 440+ colleges.
- Smart search filters let students narrow down options by district, course, category, college type, community, and more.
- Students can review choices, reorder via drag-drop, and confirm allotment.

## Step-Wise TNEA Choice Filling 2026 Process

Choice filling for TNEA is conducted via the official website of TNEA. The TNEA Choice filling process is carried out in a structured way through the TNEA login. Check step-wise how to choice filling in TNEA:

- Visit the [TNEA official website](http://tneaonline.org) and log in to the portal.
- Use the TNEA login credentials of your registered Email ID and password.
- Once successfully logged in, navigate to the “Choice Filling” section.
- Apply the required filters to narrow down options by district, college name/code, branch, or category.
- Add the preferred Choice of College and branch combination from top-to-bottom by priority.
- Students can choose up to 100 options for their list.
- Reorder preference if required.
- Review the choices selected and save.
- Before the deadline, lock Choices manually, or it automatically gets locked after the deadline.

### Important Things to Keep in Mind:

While the Choice filling for TNEA students needs to keep the following things in mind:

- Must fill 50-100+ choices based on your rank.
- Keep the 60% options as realistic as possible, whereas 20% safe options.
- Research on the previous year’s closing cutoffs of the preferred colleges.
- Use the [Collegedost TNEA College Predictor](http://college-predictor/tnea-college-predictor/) Tools to predict colleges based on ranks and branch choice.
- Use the drag-and-drop options to reorder choices according to preference and priority.
- Manually lock the choices before the TNEA choice filling last date
- Verify college codes carefully before making confirming the choices.
- Editing is not allowed once options are locked.

## Mistakes to Avoid in TNEA Choice Filling

During the choice filling in TNEA, students need to avoid common mistakes that can cost them their desired college or branch seat. Check the following mistakes to avoid getting better seat allotment outcomes:

- Filling choices under 50 options can reduce the possibility of securing the preferred seat despite good ranks.
- Skipping the previous year’s cutoff research for the colleges leads to unrealistic top picks
- Missing the realistic college or branch choice due to a lack of practical research.
- Poor ordering of preferred choices is the biggest reason to get less desired colleges.
- Forgetting to lock the choices manually before the TNEA choice filling last date.
- Entering the wrong college codes
- Making choices depending on the popularity and prestige factor more than the placement and branch factor.

## Steps to take after TNEA Choice Filling

After the TNEA online choice filling is done, candidates need to proceed with the following steps promptly:

- Check the online TNEA Website for provisional TNEA seat allotment, which is generally conducted after locking of choices.
- After provisional seat allotment based on rank and choices, students need to confirm seat options.
- Confirm by accepting and finalising the seat, or selecting the accept & upward option to get better seats. Followed by other options of Decline & Upward for better seats, or decline & quit the process of TNEA counselling.
- Make sure to confirm before the given deadline.
- Once seats are allotted, students need to download the allotment order.
- Pay the initial fees to book seats and report to the allotted college campus to pay the remaining tuition fees and document verification.
- Students must carry all the required documents for TNEA counselling like 10th and 12th marksheets, TNEA application form 2026, community certificate, category certificate, nativity certificate and valid ID proof.
- Students who have opted for mobility must check the other round seat allotments and repeat the process.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Where to do choice filling in TNEA for reserved categories?

TNEA Choice filling for all candidates is carried out in the online website of TNEA.

### 2. What is choice filling in TNEA counselling?

Choice filling in TNEA counselling is the process of selecting and ordering the desired college and branch options by preference and locking in before the deadline to get the desired Tamil Nadu engineering college seat.

### 3. When does TNEA round 2 choice filling start?

TNEA round 2 choice filling starts around late July 2026 for those who target ranks like 39146 to 137710. The TNEA round 2 choice filling last date is anounced post the round 2 begins.

### 4. Is there any payment required in TNEA Choice Filling?

Initial payment of TNEA registration and booking the seats is required after the TNEA Choice filling is done.

### 5. What if I forget to lock my choices?

During the TNEA choice filling process, choices get auto-locked if not locked manually before the deadline.

### 6. How are TNEA seats allotted?

For TNEA seat allotment, students need to have the required eligibility criteria to apply for TNEA. But the main seat allotment is done based on rank order, category reservation, and choice sequence.
