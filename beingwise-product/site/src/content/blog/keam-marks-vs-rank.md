## How is the KEAM 2025 Result Prepared?

The KEAM result is prepared through two main components, including:

### KEAM 2026 Scorecard

The scorecard is made available on the official website at the candidate login, where the students can check the total marks and the marks obtained in the KEAM 2026 exam.

### KEAM 2026 Engineering Rank List

The rank list for engineering is prepared based on the addition of the marks obtained in the engineering [**KEAM 2026 entrance exam**](https://cee.kerala.gov.in/keamonline2026/) and the qualifying exam, where some weightage is provided to both the exams while preparing the final rank list.

## KEAM Normalisation Process 2026

The KEAM admission process and result declaration are based on the normalisation process, as this makes it fair in ranking.

The first stage is normalisation of the entrance exam scores across study sessions to eliminate differences in difficulty. Standardisation of Class 12 marks in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry is also done to take into consideration variations in boards.

Lastly, a rank list of the engineers is drawn by a 50:50 combination of the standardised qualifying exam marks and normalised KEAM score.
