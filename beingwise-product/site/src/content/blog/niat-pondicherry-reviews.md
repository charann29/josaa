## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q**1. What courses are offered at NIAT Training Centre in Pondicherry?**

At NIAT (NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies) Pondicherry, no degree courses are offered. Instead, NIAT provides industry-focused upskilling programs in areas like software development, AI, ML, and Data Science through structured learning modules and project-based training. These programs are designed to build practical skills, while any formal degree, such as B.Tech CSE, is offered separately by the Takshashila University (Collaborating University) based on its own admission criteria.

### Q**2. How is the learning experience at NIAT Upskilling Program in Pondicherry?**

Students report a practical, hands-on learning approach that focuses on real-world projects and industry applications, rather than just theoretical knowledge.

### Q**3. Are there any early internship opportunities at NIAT in Pondicherry?**

Yes, students typically get internship offers as early as their first year, with many securing paid internships through NIAT’s industry collaborations.

### Q4. What are the eligibility criteria and enrolment process for NIAT Skilling Centre in Pondicherry?

Admission is based on the NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT). Eligibility requires passing Class 12 or an equivalent qualification, accompanied by relevant documents at the time of admission.

### Q5. **What is the infrastructure like at the Takshashila University campus?**

The Takshashila University campus features modern infrastructure, including well-equipped classrooms, robust internet connectivity, state-of-the-art labs, and recreational facilities.

### Q6. What exactly does NIAT Skilling Centre offer in Pondicherry?

NIAT offers industry-focused upskilling programs in Pondicherry, which are designed to build practical tech skills through training modules, projects, and mentorship. It does not offer degree programs; those are provided separately by the collaborating university (Takshashila University).

### Q7. Will I get a B.Tech CSE degree directly from NIAT?

No, NIAT does not provide any degree or diploma. A B.Tech CSE degree is awarded by the collaborating university, such as Takshashila University in Pondicherry, subject to their admission criteria.

### Q8. Where are the NIAT programs conducted in Pondicherry?

The programs are conducted in training centres or centres of excellence provided by the collaborating university, not in standalone campuses owned by NIAT. In Pondicherry, NIAT conducts the upskilling program in the space provided by Takshashila University.

### Q9. What makes NIAT different from traditional engineering colleges in Pondicherry?

Unlike traditional colleges, NIAT focuses purely on industry-relevant skills, hands-on projects, and job readiness, complementing a university degree rather than replacing it.

### Q10. Do students get certificates after completing the NIAT program?

Yes, students receive an Industry Ready Certificate (IRC) from NIAT for completing the upskilling program, which highlights their practical skills.
