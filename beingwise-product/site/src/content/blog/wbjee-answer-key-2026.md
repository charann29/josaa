## What Is the WBJEE 2026 Answer Key?

The WBJEE Answer Key 2026 is the official document released by WBJEEB that lists the correct answers to all questions in the exam. It is released in two stages:

- **Provisional or model answer key:** Released first for candidates to review and raise objections.
- **Final answer key:** Released after WBJEEB reviews all valid objections submitted by candidates.

Using the provisional WBJEE 2026 Answer Key, candidates can calculate their estimated scores before the official result is announced. However, scores calculated from the provisional key may differ from the final result, since corrections can happen after the objection review. The final answer key released by WBJEEB is considered the last word, and no objections are entertained after that point.

## How to Calculate Your Score Using WBJEE Answer Key 2026

Before you start calculating, understand how the marking scheme works. [WBJEE](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-2026/) does not follow a single uniform rule for all questions. The marks and negative marking differ by paper, category, and subject. Here is a full breakdown.

### Paper and Category Structure

The WBJEE 2026 exam carries a total of 200 marks. The question paper is divided into three categories: I, II, and III. Category I questions carry 1 mark each, while Categories II and III carry 2 marks each.

- Category I: 1 mark per correct answer
- Category II: 2 marks per correct answer
- Category III: 2 marks per correct answer

### Subject-wise Marks Distribution

For Paper 1 (Mathematics), Category I carries 50 marks, Category II carries 30 marks, and Category III carries 20 marks. For Paper 2, Physics and Chemistry carry 50 marks each.

| Paper | Subject | Total Marks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Paper 1 | Mathematics | 100 |
| Paper 2 | Physics | 50 |
| Paper 2 | Chemistry | 50 |
| Total |  | 200 |

### Negative Marking Rules

This is where most candidates lose marks unknowingly. Negative marking differs by subject: one-fourth deduction applies for Physics, one-half deduction for Chemistry, and there is no negative marking for Mathematics.

- Mathematics: No negative marking
- Physics: −¼ mark for each wrong answer
- Chemistry: −½ mark for each wrong answer

### How to Apply This While Calculating

Use this simple approach with your WBJEE answer sheet 2026:

- Go question by question for each subject separately
- Mark each response as correct, wrong, or unattempted
- Add full marks for correct answers as per their category
- Subtract the applicable negative marks for wrong answers only
- Do not apply any deduction for unattempted questions
- Add all three subject totals to get your estimated score out of 200

## How to Raise Objections Against WBJEE Answer Key 2026

If you believe a particular answer in the WBJEE Answer Key 2026 is incorrect, you have the option to file a challenge. The WBJEE official answer key objection window is open until May 29, 2026, at 11:59 PM.

Candidates can challenge one or multiple questions by submitting valid proof and paying the prescribed fee. Objections without proper supporting documents or incomplete fee transactions will not be accepted. Here is how to raise an objection against the WBJEE Answer Key 2026:

- Step 1: Visit the official WBJEEB portal at wbjeeb.nic.in.
- Step 2: Log in using your application number and password.
- Step 3: Select the question ID or IDs that you want to challenge.
- Step 4: Upload supporting documents or proof that backs your answer.
- Step 5: Pay the objection fee online.
- Step 6: Submit the challenge form and note your submission confirmation.

## WBJEE 2026 Objection Fee: Details

Candidates must pay a non-refundable processing fee of ₹500 per question challenged. Payment can be made using net banking, debit card, credit card, or UPI. Objections submitted without successful fee payment or valid supporting documents will not be considered. A few things to keep in mind before filing:

- The ₹500 fee is non-refundable regardless of whether the objection is accepted.
- Only challenges submitted with proper evidence and completed payment will be reviewed.
- Raising a baseless challenge wastes your fee, so file only when you have strong supporting proof.

## What Happens After the WBJEE Final Answer Key 2026?

Once the objection window closes on May 29, WBJEEB will hand over all valid challenges to subject experts for review. Based on their findings, the board will release the WBJEE final answer key 2026. The WBJEE 2026 result will be prepared on the basis of the final answer key. Here is what follows:

- **WBJEE Final Answer Key:** Released after expert review of all valid objections.
- **[WBJEE Result 2026](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-result-2026/):** Candidates can expect to download their results in the first week of August 2026 by logging in to the official website.
- **WBJEE Rank Card 2026:** Along with the result, candidates receive their WBJEE rank card. This shows the paper-wise and overall marks, total rank, and category rank — all figures relevant for counselling.
- **WBJEE Counselling 2026:** The counselling process includes registration, choice filling, seat assignment, and document checking, with at least two rounds of seat allotment and a mop-up round.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. When was the WBJEE Answer Key 2026 released?**

**2. Where can I download the WBJEE Answer Key 2026?**

**3. What is the last date to raise objections against WBJEE Answer Key 2026?**

**4. How much is the objection fee for WBJEE Answer Key 2026?**

Ans. The fee is ₹500 per question and is non-refundable.

**5. Will the WBJEE Answer Key 2026 scores match the final result?**

Ans. Not necessarily. Scores calculated using the provisional answer key may change after the final answer key is released post objection review.

**6. When will the WBJEE 2026 result be declared?**

Ans. The WBJEE 2026 result is expected to be declared by August 2026 on the official website.

**7. Is there negative marking in WBJEE 2026?**

Ans.Yes, but it depends on the subject and category. Physics and Chemistry Category I carries −¼ per wrong answer. Category II across Physics and Chemistry carries −½ per wrong answer. Mathematics has no negative marking at all. Category III has no negative marking for any subject.

**8. What is Category III in WBJEE 2026, and how is it scored?**

Ans. Category III questions allow one or more correct answer options. A fully correct response earns 2 marks. A partially correct response — where at least one correct option is selected and no wrong option is selected — earns 1 mark. There is no negative marking for Category III. An incorrect selection in Category III does not result in a deduction.
