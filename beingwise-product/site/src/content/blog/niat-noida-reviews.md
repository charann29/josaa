## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What are the NIAT fees in Noida?

Since NIAT doesn’t offer degrees or diplomas. As it is not a college, NIAT college fees are not applicable. NIAT can’t charge for University Seat Reservation Fee, Seat Confirmation Fee, Admission Fee, Tuition fee, Semester fee, B.Tech Fee, etc. All these are handled by NIU. However, NIAT entrance exam fees for the NAT are INR 1200, and the upskilling program fee is charged separately.
The NIAT BTech fees from the collaborating University and Upskilling programs are INR 8-18 lakhs total (INR 2-4.5 lakhs per year).

### 2. Are the Mentors at NIAT in Noida Qualified?

Yes, Mentors at NIAT Noida are professionals with relevant industry experience and individuals from the top institutes like IIT Bombay.

### 3. Are there any negative reviews about the NIAT Noida?

Reviews about the NIAT Noida are mostly positive across social media platforms, with most of the students mentioning the excellent academic experience and placement opportunities. However, some mentioned the distance between their residency and college is long.

### 4. NIAT Noida has a collaboration with which university?

NIAT Noida has collaborated with the Noida International University to provide students with a world-class academic experience with state-of-the-art infrastructural facilities, experienced mentors and better placement opportunities.

### 5. Do NIAT in Noida provide better placement opportunities?

Yes, NIAT Noida provides better placement opportunities for students with a wide pool of network companies. NIAT has helped more than 200 students to get an internship with INR 30k per month in the very first year.

### 6. How does the NIAT Collaboration work with Partner University?

NIAT has collaborated with Noida International University. This partnership benefits students by providing a dual degree. NIU awards the 4-year B.Tech CSE degree, while NIAT provides IRC at the end of 4 years. The partner university manages admissions, academics, and awards the degree, while NIAT provides practical training, mentorship, and skill-based learning to make students job-ready.

### 7. What do students say about the campus infrastructure and facilities at Noida International University

Students give mixed feedback for NIU: many praise the large campus, Wi-Fi connectivity, and classrooms, while some point out issues with hostel locations and network problems.

### 8. What are students’ opinions about the teaching style and mentorship at NIAT Noida?

Many students mention that the teaching approach is practical and application-focused. Mentors are often described as supportive, especially during projects and doubt-clearing sessions.

### 9. What is NAT? How much is the NAT exam fee for 2026?

NAT stands for the NxtWave Assessment Test, which is the online entrance exam you must take to apply for the upskilling program with NIAT. It checks your problem-solving, reasoning, and cognitive skills in areas like critical thinking and maths.
For 2026, the NAT registration fee is INR 1,200 (non-refundable), and this covers the exam and related counselling process.

### 10. What do NIAT in Noida reviews say about the balance between theory and practical learning?

Many students appreciate that the focus is more on hands-on training rather than only theory, especially in tech-related specializations.

### 11. What is the NIAT admission process?

As NIAT is not a University or a college, it does not give any admission. However, students can enrol in the NIAT upskilling program and take admission to the BTech CSE degree in the partner university, which is NIU.
