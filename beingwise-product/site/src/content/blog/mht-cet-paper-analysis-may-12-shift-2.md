## MHT CET Paper​ Analysis May 12 Shift 2

The Shift 2 of the MHT CET Exam is held from 02:00 PM to 5:00 PM. Based on the reviews received from those candidates who appeared for the MHT CET 2026 May 12 Shift 2 exam, the overall difficulty level of the paper was moderate.

## Subject-wise MHT CET PCM Paper Analysis 2026

MHT CET PCM Analysis 2026 is helpful for students to comprehend the entire pattern, level of difficulty, and significant chapters that will be covered in the entrance examination. ### MHT CET 2026 Exam Analysis for Physics Section

As per the MHT CET 2026 Exam Analysis, the physics section entailed more conceptual understanding apart from formula application. The questions took longer to understand and solve, thereby making it difficult for the candidates to manage time as compared to the chemistry section.

**Take a look at the key physics topics asked in the shift**

- High weightage was provided for Electrostatics and Current Electricity.
- Waves, Oscillations, and SHM were repeated topics.
- Significant weightage was given to Modern Physics and Electromagnetic Induction.
- Physics comprised both numerical and conceptual questions.

### MHT CET 2026 Exam Analysis for Maths Section

The mathematics section in the MHT CET 2026 May 12 Shift 2 test was relatively easy to moderate but lengthy. Though the questions were not complex, it involved many calculations and hence took much time to solve. Generally, mathematics requires proficiency in speedy calculations.

Take a look at the key maths topics asked in the shift

- High weightage was given to integration, with numerous questions being asked.
- Repetition of Vector 3D and Line & Plane was observed.
- Most weightage was given to Calculus, particularly Differentiation.
- Questions on Probability and Matrices were asked in a moderate number.

## MHT CET Paper Analysis for Chemistry Section

Chemistry was found to be the simplest and easiest section in the MHT CET 2026 May 12 Shift 2 exam. Numerical questions were also given in the physical chemistry topic, where practicing PYQ helped a lot. In general, chemistry was helpful since it saved time for the remaining two subjects.

Take a look at the key Chemistry topics asked in the shift

- Organic Chemistry received a considerable amount of weightage.
- Numerous questions on Coordination Compounds and p-Block Elements were asked.
- Numerical questions on Physical Chemistry were straightforward and based on formulas.
- Many theory questions came directly from textbook concepts and reactions.
