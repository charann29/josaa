The NTA has released an intimation regarding the change in the **JEE Main 2025** exam center for Sharjah UAE. The JEE Main exam will be conducted in Various cities across India and 15 Cities from outside the country. The [Exam](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/jee-main-exam-dates/) is conducted from Jan 22nd, 2025, to Jan 29th, 2025, for Paper 1 and Jan 30th, 2025.

The Center Number, Which will see a change in the Exam Center, is **ZZ1401**. The Old Center Details was Scholars Training Institute, 909-903 Belresheed Tower, I, MNC Healthcare Building, Buhaira Corniche, Sharjah, United Arab Emirates, 4472  is now changed to Scholar Training Institute, 1501-1502, The First Tower, Al  Majaz-3, Sharjah, United Arab Emirates, Pincode- 50001. The Candidates Assigned to the Old Center will now appear for the new center, Which is intimated by the NTA.
