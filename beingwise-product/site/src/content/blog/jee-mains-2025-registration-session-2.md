### JEE Mains 2025 Registration Session 2: Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must check the eligibility criteria before registering for the exam, as they cannot appear for the exam without satisfying them. Here are the general guidelines for eligibility:

- Candidates who have completed their 10+2 in 2023 or 2024, or are appearing for their exams in 2025, are eligible to apply.
- Physics and Mathematics should be compulsory subjects in class 12, along with one of the following: Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, or a Technical Vocational subject.
- No minimum age criteria to apply for **[JEE Mains Session 2](/blog/jee-main/)**.

## JEE Mains 2025 Registration Session 2: Steps to Register

Candidates have to follow the steps mentioned below to apply for JEE Mains 2025 Session 2:

- Visit the official website jeemain.nta.nic.in and click on the official link.
- Select the preferred mode for login and registration.
- Register using your email ID and password to create your JEE Main login credentials.
- Complete the application form by providing the necessary personal and educational details.
- Upload the scanned copies of your signature and photograph as per the specified guidelines.
- Pay the registration fee.
- Candidates should download and save the confirmation page for future reference.

## JEE Mains 2025 Session 2 Registration: Documents Required for Registration

Candidates require the following documents for JEE Mains 2025 Registration Session 2:

- Valid Email ID and phone number.
- Class X certificate
- Valid ID proof (AADHAR/ Voter ID/PAN)
- Category Certificate
