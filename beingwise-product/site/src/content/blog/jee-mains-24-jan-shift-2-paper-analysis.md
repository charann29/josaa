## How do students rate the JEE Main Jan 24 Shift 2 Question paper?

Just like the previous shifts, we do conversion with the students who are just coming out of the examination hall to know their Point of view on JEE Mains 2026 24 Jan shift 2 paper analysis. The JEE Mains 2026 paper analysis for the January 24th second shift examination highlighted different student perceptions on course difficulty.

- Ridhi, who attempted the 40-45 questions, described the JEE Main Paper as considerably easier than the previous day’s exam. She stated that, while her questions went well and she felt she might have tried more, and intend to refine their tactics for Session 2.
- Ajay, who attempted 46 questions adid, said JEE Mains 24 Jan shift 2 paper was categorised as simple to moderate but lengthy. They found Chemistry to be extremely conceptual, with considerable calculations that took longer than expected (30-40 minutes), reducing the time available for Mathematics

He also described Physics as calculative; nonetheless, they deemed Mathematics feasible.
