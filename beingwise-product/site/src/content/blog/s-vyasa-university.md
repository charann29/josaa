Established in the year 2002 in Bengaluru, Karnataka, Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana: S‑VYASA University is a UGC-recognised, AICTE‑approved deemed‑to‑be university. S-VYASA University is one of India’s leading A+ NAAC-accredited and AICTE-approved institutions, offering a transformative educational journey that blends ancient wisdom with modern science. From undergraduate to Ph.D. programs, students gain practical skills, industry exposure, and research experience in a supportive and innovative environment. What truly sets S-VYASA apart is its unique integration of Yoga as a way of life, fostering life training and character building through a Gurukula-inspired system rooted in scientific methodology, preparing students not just for careers, but for life.

S‑VYASA University courses are offered across diverse disciplines such as undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral levels. These span fields such as B.Tech., M.Sc, MBA, Yoga Therapy, Yoga & Vedic Science, Naturopathy, Clinical Psychology, Physiotherapy, Occupational Therapy, Ayurveda science, as well as contemporary disciplines like MCA. S-Vyasa University’s curriculum emphasises practical life training through Yoga as a way of life, research-driven, and interdisciplinary learning.

## S-Vyasa University Admissions

Candidates who have completed their class 10+2 or equivalent degree from a recognized board are eligible for S-Vyasa University admissions to its undergraduate programs. Students must have studied Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with one additional subject from Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, or a Technical Vocational stream for S-Vyasa University Admissions. Additionally, candidates from the General category must secure a minimum of 45% aggregate marks in the subjects mentioned above, and candidates from SC/ST/OBC/PwD categories require at least 40% aggregate.
