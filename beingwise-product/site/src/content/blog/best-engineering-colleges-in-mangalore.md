### Entrance Examinations

Here are the entrance examinations accepted by the engineering colleges in Mangalore:

- **KCET:** The **KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test)** is a state-level exam conducted by KEA for admission into engineering, pharmacy, and other professional courses in Karnataka. It tests students on Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology based on the 11th and 12th syllabi.
- **COMEDK:** COMEDK is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the Consortium of Medical, Engineering, and Dental Colleges of Karnataka for admission to engineering, architecture, and dental courses. It serves as a gateway to many top private colleges in Karnataka and is open to students from all over India.
- **JEE Main:** JEE Main is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the NTA for admission to engineering, architecture, and planning courses. It serves as a gateway to top institutes like NITs, IIITs, and acts as a qualifier for JEE Advanced. The exam tests students on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
- **KRLMPCA:** KRLMPCA (Karnataka Religious & Linguistic Minority Professional Colleges Association) conducts an entrance exam for admission to engineering and other professional courses. It is mainly for students belonging to religious and linguistic minority communities. The exam offers seats in reputed minority colleges across Karnataka.

**Institute Specific Entrance Examinations:**

- **NUCAT:** NUCAT (Nitte University Common Admission Test) is an entrance exam conducted by Nitte University for admission to its engineering and other undergraduate programs. It assesses students’ knowledge in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. The exam serves as a gateway to various UG courses offered by Nitte institutions.
- **NAT:** The **NxtWave Admission Test** is an entrance assessment for enrolling in NxtWave’s tech programs like CCBP 4.0 and Intensive 3.0. It evaluates candidates on aptitude, logical reasoning, and basic programming or domain knowledge.

### Admission Process for the Best Engineering Colleges in Mangalore

Admissions to the Top 10 Engineering Colleges in Mangalore are based on the entrance examinations followed by the counselling process. Seats are filled through the entrance examinations and the management quota. Here are the details about the admissions process:

- Students who appeared for the JEE Main, KCET, COMEDK, and KRLMPCA have to appear for the counselling process conducted by the respective authorities. Based on their rank and their preferences, the colleges are allotted.
- Students who appear for the institute-specific entrance examinations have to qualify for the same in order to secure admission.

The admission process for NIAT Mangalore follows several steps as shown below:

5. Yenepoya University collaborates with NIAT, adhering to its established criteria and policies, as well as the norms set by the UGC and AICTE.
