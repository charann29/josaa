### 9. How can I apply for NIAT’s upskilling program?

Students interested in NIAT’s upskilling program can apply through the official NIAT website and appear for the NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test). NIAT acts only as a facilitator, sharing interested student details with VGU Jaipur with the student’s written consent. Admission is granted solely at the university’s discretion and is subject to its own verification, counselling, and confirmation process.
