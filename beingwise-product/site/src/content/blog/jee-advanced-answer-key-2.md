## JEE Advanced Provisional Answer Key 2026 Objection Window

Along with the released provisional answer key for JEE Advanced 2026, the objection window is also opened on the same day.  Those candidates who are dissatisfied with the provisional answers key can raise objections against the same on the official portal.

As per the schedule, the objection window is open till May 26, 2026, at 5:00 PM. Candidates can raise objections during the above-said time frame against the answers that they are not satisfied with. It should be noted here that objections have to be raised strictly online through the candidate login portal of JEE Advanced.

### Important Details of JEE Advanced 2026 Answer Key Objections

- The objection window will remain open from May 25 to May 26, 2026.
- Candidates will have to support their challenges with valid proof or an explanation.
- There may be a fee for every objection raised by the candidate.
- Objections raised after the objection deadline will not be entertained.
- Experts selected by IITs will review objections raised.
- All objections, after going through the review process, will culminate in the publication of the final answer key of JEE Advanced 2026.

### How to Challenge the JEE Advanced Provisional Answer Key?

Any candidate who finds an error in the JEE Advanced provisional answer key 2026 can raise his/her objection by logging into the official website during the designated time period.

1. Go to the official website of JEE Advanced.
2. Scroll down the home page, and look at the Important announcement section.
3. Under this section, you will find the notification mentioning the **JEE (Advanced) 2026 Feedback on Provisional Answer Keys**
4. Click on the link ‘Provisional Answer Key Objection’.
5. Log in with your registration number, date of birth, and password.

6. Choose the question number against which you have to object.
7. Attach relevant documents for the objection raised.
8. Pay the objection fee, if necessary.
9. Submit the objection form and keep the receipt for future reference.
