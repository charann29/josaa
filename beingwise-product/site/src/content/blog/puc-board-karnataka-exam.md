- Students need to be at the exam centre at the time indicated in the Karnataka 1st PUC Timetable or the Karnataka PUC 2nd year Timetable issued by the Karnataka 2nd PUC Board.
- The students must ensure that they verify the subject dates, particularly any revision related to the 2nd puc time table 2026 Karnataka Board announced for KSEAB Class 12 under the Karnataka 12th Board 2026.

- Write down the answer booklets properly and mention the roll number clearly to avoid any issues during evaluation by the Karnataka PUC Board.
