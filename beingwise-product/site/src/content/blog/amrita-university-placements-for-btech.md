## Amrita University Placements Cell & Pre-Placement Training

The Amrita University Placements procedure is conducted by the Directorate of Corporate & Industry Relations (CIR). The Amrita University placements cell for B.Tech provides students with pre-placement training, career guidance, and counselling support.

Further, it provides students with a structured recruitment process and coordinates with other Amrita University campuses. Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Placement Cell offers aptitude and coding test preparation, career pathway mapping, mock interviews, group discussions, resume building, and soft skills training.

## What is the Amrita University Placements Procedure For B.Tech?

Amrita University Placements start from the 3rd year (6th semester) for B.Tech. Before the 3rd Year, students need to register with CIR. Students appearing for the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Placement drive for B.Tech need to go through the following procedure:

- Eligible Candidates register for each company’s drive through the placement cell
- Placement cell forwards the eligible candidates’ list to recruiters
- Visiting companies connect with candidates to explain job roles and requirements.
- Training and grooming are conducted by the  Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Placement Cell for students.
- Once Students are shortlisted, they need to go through a written or Online Test, a group discussion, a technical interview, and an HR interview.
- The final offer is given to students, and students must accept or decline within the given time limit.

The Centralised Amrita University placements drive is conducted across Amrita University campuses. The B.Tech placement drive is generally conducted from August to January.

## Amrita University Placements Internship and Pre-placement Opportunities

Amrita University Placements Cell provides B.Tech students with paid internship opportunities. Almost 90% to 100% students secure internships across top recruiting companies across all the campuses. The internship program emphasises practical exposure and skill-building to boost employability.

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University placement cell offers a summer and winter internship program of 6-8 weeks in the pre-final year. The Amrita Summer Research Internship Program is also one such internship that focuses on hands-on projects in Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and other areas. Top recruiting companies include Oracle, Microsoft, Bosch, and Google, which offer internship programs. The latest internship reports suggest that 20% to 30% students secured paid internships with monthly stipends of INR 5000 to INR 15,000 per month.  Often, internship opportunities convert to pre-placement offers (PPOs). It offers direct full-time roles post-graduation.
