**KCET 2024 Engineering Fee Structure:** The KEA released the KCET 2024 Fee Structure of Government/University/Private Aided/Private Unaided Engineering and Architect Colleges through the official website. The fee structure may vary for different institutes and categories.

The candidates must pay the fee while accepting the seat allotted to them in the Counselling process. The candidates can find the details of the Engineering Fee Structure below on this page.

The [KCET 2024 Counselling](https://collegedost.in/blog/kcet/kcet-counselling/) procedure started, the candidates must check the Final Seat Matrix and download the Verification slip to start the Option Entry process.

## Fee Structure of Engineering/Architecture: Based on the College Type

The candidates can find the fee structure of Government/University/Private Aided/Private Unaided Engineering and Architect Colleges provided by KEA in the below table:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **College Type** | **Fee Structure** |
| Government Colleges | Rs. 40,860 |
| Aided Colleges | Rs. 40,860 |
| VTU Regional Colleges | Rs. 45,750 |
| Type-1Un-aided colleges including Minority and Un-aided courses In Aided colleges | Rs. 96,547 |
| Type-2 Un-aided colleges including Minority and Un-Aided courses In Aided colleges | Rs. 1,04,265 |
| Deemed / Private Universities | Rs. 96,547 |
| For private colleges affiliated with VTU Belgaum, the fee for the remaining 50% of UG course seats under the government quota | at least Rs. 76,135 + an optional skill fee |
| For seats allocated under the COMEDK quota | Rs. 2,61,477 |
