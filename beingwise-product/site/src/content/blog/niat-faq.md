###### **Q10. Does the NIAT collaborating Universities offer placement opportunities?**

Ans. Yes, the Universities where the NIAT upskilling programs take place have a dedicated placement cell that helps the students connect with the top companies in the Industry. It also prepares students by giving soft-skill training, mock interviews, etc.

###### **Q11. How can I get into the Universities offering NIAT upskilling programs?**

Ans. You can get admission to the Universities offering NIAT upskilling programs by registering through the official website and paying a non-refundable INR 1200 fee. Fill out the application form, and the eligible students who have Class 12 degree can appear in NAT. After qualifying the NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT), you need to appear in 1 on 1 counselling session.

###### **Q12. If I have PCM in my 10+2 level and my score is 45%, can I appear for NAT and get admission to the upskilling programs at NIAT collaborating Universities?**

Ans. No, you cannot be admitted to NIAT collaborating Universities or appear for NAT 2026 with 10+2. You need to have 10+2 level to get into the universities collaborating with NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies.

###### **Q14. When did NIAT Upskilling Programs start?**

Ans. NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies (NIAT) was established in 2023, with its primary location at Kapil Kavuri Hub, Nanakramguda, Financial District, Hyderabad. Additionally, NIAT operates from another facility in Hyderabad, situated at Chaitanya Deemed University (CDU) on AHTC Road, near Chilukuri Balaji Temple, Himayatnagar. NIAT offers industry-ready upskilling programs. NIAT has also collaborated with several UGC approved Universities to offer the NIAT upskilling program.

###### **Q15. Does the NIAT collaborating University accept JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and TG EAPCET scores for admission to the Institute?**

Ans. No,  the Universities where NIAT upskilling programs are offered do not accept JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and TG EAPCET scores for admission. Admission for the upskilling program with collaborating universities in 2026 is through Nxtwave Assessment Test (NAT) 2026, followed by 1 on 1 counselling process.

###### **Q16. Are the NxtWave upskilling programs free?**

Ans. No, the collaborating Universities of NIAT conduct NAT and 1:1 counselling that helps students choose the right university that offers the upskilling program. The program is not free of cost. You have to pay the degree course fee at the time of admission to the University, and a separate upskilling program fee to the NIAT.

###### **Q17. What is NAT?**

Ans. Nxtwave Assessment Test (NAT) is the **assessment test accepted by the collaborating Universities with NIAT**. It is a 90-minute online test for admission to the upskilling program offered at universities collaborating with NIAT in Hyderabad. The test focuses on the candidate’s cognitive abilities and potential as a future tech innovator. 3 sections test different skills of a student. It evaluates the student based on Personality and emotional traits, Analytical and adaptive skills, and Practical and problem-solving.
