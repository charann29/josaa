## **AP EAMCET 2025 Exam: May 23, 2025, Shift 2**

The Mathematics section of the AP EAPCET 2025 exam, held on May 23rd (Shift 2), was of moderate to difficult level. It featured a combination of conceptual questions and those based on standard formulas. Important topics included Algebra, Calculus, and Trigonometry. While a few questions were relatively direct, many required strong analytical skills, making effective time management essential. Compared to Shift 1, this section was slightly less challenging but still required a thorough understanding of the fundamental concepts to perform well.

The Physics section in the May 23rd Shift 2 AP EAPCET 2025 exam was moderately difficult, presenting a blend of conceptual and numerical problems from topics like Thermodynamics, Oscillations, and Laws of Motion. The questions were largely direct, assessing students’ understanding of fundamental concepts and their ability to apply formulas accurately. This section was generally more manageable compared to Shift 1, giving students a chance to approach it with greater confidence. Meanwhile, Chemistry stood out as the most scoring section, with questions primarily from Organic Chemistry, Chemical Bonding, and Equilibrium, closely following the NCERT syllabus. The questions were straightforward, offering an advantage to students who had a clear grasp of basic concepts. In contrast to Shift 1, Chemistry in Shift 2 had a stronger focus on reaction-based questions, underscoring the need for a firm understanding of fundamental principles

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ********What is considered a good number of attempts to secure a top rank in May 23rd Shift 2?********

Generally, attempting around 110-120 questions with high accuracy across all subjects can boost your chances of ranking well. Focus on accuracy over quantity.

### 2. ****************Which subjects should I prioritise for maximising my score and improving rank?****************

Chemistry tends to be the scoring subject, so aim for full marks there. Strengthen your problem-solving speed in Mathematics and ensure conceptual clarity in Physics to maximize overall scores.

### 3. ******How important is time management in this shift’s exam?******

Extremely important. Mathematics was lengthy and challenging, so allocate time wisely—don’t spend too long on a single question. Efficient time distribution between subjects can improve accuracy and ranking.

### 4. ************Are there any specific topics in Mathematics or Physics that carry more weight in this shift?************

### 5. ******How can I improve my chances of ranking high if I find the Mathematics section difficult?******
