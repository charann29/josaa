With the JEE Main 2026 registration window now active, candidates should keep the **documents required for JEE Mains 2026** ready to avoid any last-minute issues. NTA has published the complete list of JEE Mains 2026 registration documents at **jeemain.nta.nic.in**. You need to keep your academic, personal, and identity details ready before filling out the JEE Main application form. This year, NTA introduced a streamlined verification process, mainly through the Aadhaar card. So, you need to update your Aadhaar details for smooth registration.

Having all the documents required for JEE Mains 2026 in the proper format and size beforehand helps in completing the application smoothly. The registration window for JEE Mains 2026 is going to close on November 27, 2025. Hence, preparing all the documents beforehand will help to avoid any last-minute technical glitches.

## **Documents Required for JEE Main 2026 Registration: A Complete List**

During registration, aspirants are required to keep the following documents ready:

- UDID card (for PwD candidates)
- Signature of the candidate
- Aadhar Card
- Photograph of the examinee
- Caste certificate (if applicable)
- Class 10th marksheet
- Income certificate (if applicable)

The list above includes all the main documents you **may need while filling out the JEE Main form.** Make sure to fill in the correct details so your JEE Application doesn’t get rejected. You **don’t have to upload all these documents;** general category students can skip most of them. Still, it’s best to keep them nearby to check your details and avoid any mistakes or confusion at the last minute.

## **Documents Required for JEE Mains 2026 Registration: How to Upload?**

Following the simple steps below, you can easily upload all JEE Mains 2026 Registration Documents:
**1. Scan Your Documents**

- Use a scanner or mobile camera to scan documents.
- Ensure they are clear and taken in good lighting.

**2. Save in Correct Format**

- Save your photograph and signature in **JPG/JPEG** format.
- Save certificates (like Class 10 or PwD) as **PDFs**.

**3. Resize as per NTA Guidelines**

- Follow NTA’s file size and dimension limits.
- Use tools like **ilovepdf.com** or **compressjpeg.com** to resize files if needed.

**4. Name and Organize Files**

- Save and check all files before uploading.
- Use simple names like:
  - *Photograph.jpg*
  - *Signature.jpg*
  - *ClassXcertificate.pdf*
- Make sure each file opens correctly.

**5. Upload During JEE Main Registration**

- Log in to the **JEE Main registration portal**.
- Upload files one by one in their respective sections.
- Wait for the “**Upload Successful**” message before moving to the next step.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****What are the documents required for the JEE Mains application form?****

For the JEE Mains application form, you will require an Aadhar Card, UDID card, photograph, signature, etc. Reserved category candidates may also need a caste/category certificate during their JEE Mains 2026 Registration.

### ************2. ******Do I need to upload all the documents during JEE Mains 2026 registration?******************

No. You must upload only the scanned copy of your photograph, signature, and Class 10 certificate. Other documents, like category or PwD certificates, are required only if applicable.

### **3.********What should be the format and size of the scanned documents for JEE Mains 2026?**********

Photograph and signature should be in JPG/JPEG format, while certificates must be in PDF format. File size and dimensions must match NTA guidelines mentioned in the brochure.

### 4. Is the Aadhaar card mandatory for filling the JEE Main 2026 application form?

Yes. Aadhaar-based authentication will automatically fetch your name, date of birth, gender and address. Therefore, it is necessary to ensure your Aadhaar details match the Class 10 certificate.
