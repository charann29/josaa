Located in Vadakangulam (near Kanyakumari) in Tirunelveli district, JOY University is a private multidisciplinary university established in 2019 under the Tamil Nadu Private Universities Act, 2019. It is officially recognised by UGC and holds accreditation from NAAC and NBA, with approval from all relevant regulatory bodies.
Operated under the Rajas Group, the university delivers world‑class engineering and technology programs aiming to blend ethics, innovation, and global citizenship.

BTech CSE at Joy University (especially with a focus on AI/ML, IoT, and Cyber Security) is well-suited for students aiming for industry-oriented, future-focused, and ethically grounded technology education. With affordable fees, strong faculty support, industry tracks, and active placement assistance, it’s a compelling option for ambitious engineering aspirants.

## **BTech Programmes: Overview**

JOY University offers **four-year, full‑time BTech degrees** through two main schools:

- **School of Engineering & Technology**
- **School of Computational Intelligence**

A total of **over 28 specialisations** are available, including:

- **Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)** with specialisations in AI & Data Science, AI & ML, AI & IoT, AI & Robotics, Cyber Security, Blockchain, etc.
- Core fields such as **Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering (incl. AI/Robotics), Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechatronics**
- Emerging technological areas: **Biotechnology, Biomedical Engineering, Agricultural Engineering, Dairy Technology**

The total undergraduate engineering seat intake is approximately **990 seats**, with around **60 seats per specialisation** (for core and most CSE streams).

## **Key Highlights of the BTech CSE Programme**

**Core CSE Track**

- Offers both foundational and advanced Computer Science education
- Emphasises **ethical**, secure, sustainable computing
- Encourages **interdisciplinary applications** in healthcare, sustainability, and socio-economic development.

**Specialised CSE Tracks**

- Includes industry‑collaborative tracks like **Intel, NEC & Edulateral Foundation** for CSE (AI & ML) and CSE (AI & IoT) with tuition around **INR 2.20 L/year**.
- These tracks feature a **15-credit Intel Capstone project** in the final semester to foster hands‑on innovation and research integration.

**Core Specialisations Fee Structure (Annual)**

- **Standard** streams (CSE, AI & DS, AI & ML, AI & IoT, Cyber Security, Blockchain, etc.): approx. **INR 1,40,000/year**
- **Industry‑tied tracks** (Intel‑NEC collaboration): **INR 2,20,000/year**.
- Other engineering fields, such as **Civil, Mechanical, Biotechnology, Agricultural Engineering**, and more: mostly **INR 1,00,000 – INR 1,40,000/year** depending on specialization.

**Sample Curriculum**

- Core subjects: Python, Raspberry Pi, AI, ML, Deep Learning, Data Structures & Algorithms, Operating Systems, Databases, Compiler Design, Cryptography & Security.
- Elective options include **Blockchain, Quantum Computing, Computational Biology**, plus high‑credit capstone and electives in later semesters (especially in specialised tracks).

### **Eligibility & Admissions**

**Minimum Eligibility**

- Completion of **10+2 or equivalent** with **Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics**, and **English**
- **At least 60% aggregate in PCM**, with no subject less than 50% (reservation norms may apply)

**Entrance Tests Accepted**

- **JEE Main**, **CUET (UG)**, and **JOYAT** (internal test)
- Direct admission eligibility: **JEE Main percentile of 80+** in some cases.
- Admissions also consider **state-level exams** like **TNEA,** depending on the program.

## B.Tech CSE at Joy University Application Process

Applying to the BTech CSE program at Joy University is through the official website. Whether you’re applying through JEE Main or JOYAT, the university offers multiple pathways to admission based on your academic performance.

- Online Registration on Joy University’s official portal.
- Fill the application form, upload documents, and pay the INR 1,000 application fee.
- Take the JOYAT exam, if applicable.
- Receive an offer letter based on JOYAT score or JEE Main percentile.
- Document verification (e.g., mark sheets, ID) and admission fee payment finalize enrollment.
- Direct JEE offer holders still complete formality and fee deadlines.
