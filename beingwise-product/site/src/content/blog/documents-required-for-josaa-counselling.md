JoSAA Counselling 2026 is a centralised admission process for applicants with JEE Mains and JEE Advanced scores who want to study at IITs, NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs. **Verification of documents required for JoSAA counselling 2026 becomes one of the crucial processes during the whole process**. Before registration, you must be aware of the list of documents required for JoSAA counselling 2026.

Students need to upload and verify a number of documents to prove that they fall under the required eligibility criteria and the mentioned category. In case of not uploading documents required for JoSAA counselling 2026 within the given time period, there will be cancellation of the allotted seat. With this complete guide, here you can know all about what documents are required for JoSAA counselling 2026.

## About JoSAA Counselling 2026

JoSAA offers a single online platform to process admissions to IITs, NITs, IIITs, IIEST Shibpur, and other Government Funded Technical Institutes (GFTIs). This includes 23 IITs, 31 NITs, 26 IIITs, IIEST Shibpur, and 40 additional GFTIs. **As per the tentative schedule, the registration for the JoSAA Counselling 2026 has started from June 02, 2026.**

All those candidates who have qualified and performed exceptionally well in JEE Advanced can register themselves for JoSAA Counselling after the announcement of JEE Mains & JEE Advanced results 2026. The counselling process will be held over six rounds, with different cutoff marks for each session. To complete the admission procedure and seat allotment, JoSAA counselling 2026 requires copies of all essential papers.

## Documents required for JoSAA Counselling 2026

In JoSAA Counselling 2026, all candidates need to go through the verification process of the required documents for JoSAA counselling 2026, which is usually conducted at the allotted IITs and NITs. It ensures that the documents you have submitted are authentic and fulfill all requirements. Make sure that there are no discrepancies in the required documents for JoSAA counselling 2026 both in copies and originals. Here is a list of documents required for JoSAA counselling

- Three passport-sized pictures (the same as uploaded in the JEE Main 2026 application).
- JoSAA issued a provisional seat allotment letter
- Candidate’s undertaking (according to the format offered during counselling)
- Seat acceptance fee receipt (downloaded after payment through SBI net banking or e-challan)
- Class 10 mark sheet or document with date of birth
- Class 12 mark sheet and passing certificate.
- A valid photo ID (such as an Aadhaar card, school ID, or PAN card)
- Certificate of physical fitness (as shared by JoSAA)
- Caste or category certificate (for SC/ST/OBC/EWS candidates)
- PwD Certificate (if relevant)
- OCI or PIO card (where applicable)
- Printout of the selections submitted during JoSAA counselling.
- Medical certificate (required for admission, in the format specified by JoSAA)

## Why JoSAA Document Verification is Crucial?

The JoSAA document verification process is a crucial stage for the admission process of IITs, NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs. After obtaining seats through counseling, admission at these institutes will be confirmed only after completing the JoSAA document verification process successfully in order to confirm their eligibility for admission.

- **Checks the Eligibility and Authenticity of Information**

It is a process by which the information given by candidates about their academic qualifications and other details is verified by the verification officer.

- **Checks for Entitlement to Reservations**

If candidates avail the benefits of any reserved category, such as SC, ST, OBC-NCL, EWS, or PwD reservation, they have to produce valid certificates. Otherwise, the privilege of availing these facilities will be lost due to the expiry or invalidity of these certificates.

- **Facilitates Confirmation of Seat Allocation**

Documents required for seat allotment in JoSAA and documents required for the JoSAA admission process play an important role in verifying the eligibility of candidates and confirming their allotted seat.

- **Eliminates Problems in the Admission Process**

During JoSAA verification, issues related to missing or invalid documents are found, and candidates get an opportunity to submit them. It saves candidates from losing seats because of any error in the process.

- **Helps Maintain Transparency in Admissions**

Since the whole process of verification is conducted according to the guidelines set by JoSAA, all the admissions done are done in a fair manner, ensuring credibility and fairness throughout the counselling process.

- **Prevents Seat Cancellation**

Failure to respond to verification queries within the designated timeframe can result in cancellation of seats and disqualification from the other rounds of counselling. As such, candidates are expected to continuously check their notifications on the JoSAA website while undergoing the verification process.

### If category certificate documents for JoSAA counselling are found Invalid?

The JoSAA conducts verification of all categories and reservation certificates provided by the candidate. In case of any invalid certificate, expiry date, improper format, or lack of certificate, the reservation category may be changed according to the guidelines of JoSAA.

For example:

- In case of candidates belonging to the GEN-EWS category who have provided an invalid EWS certificate, such candidates will be categorized as General category.
- An OBC-NCL candidate who provides an invalid certificate would lose out on reservations and be placed in the General category.
- SC or ST category candidates who provide invalid certificates would be considered as General category candidates.
- For candidates who are claiming both category and PwD reservations, their category depends on whether their certificate was valid or not.

Hence, the submitted category certificate documents for JoSAA counselling were found invalid the candidates may lose out on the reservation benefits.

- JoSAA seat allocation documents are verified at the time of online reporting.
- Documents required for the JoSAA admission process could be verified at the allotted institute again through physical reporting.
- Candidates should keep checking their accounts on the JoSAA portal.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. What documents are required for JoSAA counselling 2026?**

Ans. Required documents include Class 10 & 12 mark sheets, JEE Main/JEE Advanced admit card, provisional seat allotment letter, photo ID proof, passport-size photographs, medical certificate, and category certificates (if applicable). Both original and scanned copies should be kept ready.

**2. Are original documents required for JoSAA counselling?**

Ans. Yes, original documents are required during physical reporting. For online reporting, scanned copies must be uploaded.

**3. Do I need the Class 10 marksheet for JoSAA counselling?**

Ans. Yes, the Class 10 mark sheet is mandatory as proof of date of birth and is required during document verification.

**4. Is a category certificate mandatory in JoSAA counselling?**

Ans. Category certificates are required only for candidates claiming reservation under SC/ST/OBC-NCL/EWS/PwD. General category candidates do not need to submit them.

**5. What happens if documents are missing in JoSAA counselling?**

Ans. Missing documents may lead to queries from the verification authority. Failure to submit required documents on time can result in cancellation of the allotted seat.

**6. Can I upload scanned copies in JoSAA counselling?**

Ans. Yes, candidates must upload scanned copies of all required documents during online reporting for verification.

**7. How many documents are required for JoSAA counselling?**

Ans. The number of documents varies based on category and institute, but generally around 8–12 documents are required.
