## Types of Presidency University Bangalore Scholarships Schemes

There are four scholarship schemes available during the Presidency University Bangalore admission process. The Presidency University Bangalore Scholarship details can be categorised into the following types of schemes:

- Merit-Based Scholarships – It is awarded to applicants admitted under the management category based on their previous academic performance.
- Entrance Examinations Merit-based Scholarships – Students who have a valid score in JEE Mains/Karnataka CET/PG CET are eligible for this scholarship.
- Special Scholarships – Students with unique personal or social backgrounds are eligible for the special scholarships.
- Sports Scholarship- Student-athletes who have achieved in international/national/state competitions are eligible for this scholarship.

## Presidency University Bangalore Scholarships Application Process

Students can apply online or offline for the **[Presidency University Bangalore Scholarship](https://presidencyuniversity.in/scholarships)**. Although eligible students will automatically get the deserving scholarship upon admission. However, to apply for a scholarship, students need to follow the given steps:

- Visit the online website of Presidency University Bangalore.
- Search for the scholarship circular and click on the scholarship form link.
- Students need to fill in the required details for the application.
- Submit with required academic and family income documents.
- A hard copy of the documents needs to be submitted to the registrar’s office.

### Key Scholarship Guidelines

- Students need to visit www.presidency university Scholarship.in application form.
- Students can opt for one scholarship at a time based on the scholarship benefits.
- Students admitted under the Management Category can only avail the Merit Scholarships.
- The renewal of scholarships is based on students’ performance in the first and subsequent years.
- NRI Students are eligible for a 15% fee concession across all programs.

## Presidency University Bangalore Admission Criteria For B.Tech Programs

B.Tech aspirants applying for Presidency University B.Tech admission are considered as per the below given admission criteria:

- Students must have passed the 10+2 level exam with Physics/Mathematics as compulsory subjects with at least 45% marks in aggregate.
- As a third subject option, students must choose from Chemistry/Computer Science/Electronics/IT/Biology/Informatics Practices/ Biotechnology/Technical Vocational Subject/ Agriculture/ Engineering Graphics/ Business Studies subjects.
- Eligible candidates need to appear for the entrance exams, like JEE Mains/COMEDK UGET/KCET. A valid score in any one of the exams is required for Presidency University Bangalore admission.
- Selected students are then required to appear for an interview conducted by Presidency University Bangalore.

No, scholarships are available only for tuition fees at Presidency University Bangalore.

Up to 100% concession on tuition fee can be expected through the Presidency University scholarship schemes. Through merit scholarship and sports scholarships, Engineering students can secure these scholarships.

No, there are mandatory entrance exams conducted by Presidency University, Bangalore, for scholarships. But UG and PG students must appear for state or national-level competitive exams for admission. Students who have scored valid marks in these exams can get upto 100% scholarship at Presidency University Bangalore.

### 5. **Can I get a scholarship at Presidency University Bangalore with 50% marks?**

No, students need at least a score of 80% or above to secure a merit scholarship at Presidency University Bangalore.

### 6. When to apply for Presidency University Bangalore Scholarship?

Students need to apply for the scholarship during the Presidency University Bangalore admission process or immediately after enrollment at Presidency University Bangalore.

### 7. What is the procedure for the scholarship grant during the Presidency University Bangalore admission?

The Presidency University scholarship is automatically granted once admission is done or the controller of examinations has announced the results.

### 8. When is the Presidency University Bangalore Scholarship withdrawn?

Under a few circumstances, the scholarship issued by the Presidency University Bangalore, can be withdrawn. For instance, when students fail to secure a rank as specified in the admission notification or even pass the exam in first attempt. The scholarship may also be withdrawn if the student is found to have engaged in any misconduct or unfair business.
