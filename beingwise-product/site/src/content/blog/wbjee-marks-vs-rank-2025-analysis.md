### How WBJEE Marks vs Rank Works: The Basics

Before looking at the numbers, it helps to understand how WBJEE marks vs rank is calculated. WBJEE 2026 was conducted on May 24, 2026 by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB). The total paper carries 200 marks across two papers. Paper I covers Mathematics (100 marks, 75 questions). Paper II covers Physics and Chemistry (50 marks each, 40 questions each).

Marks from both papers are added together to produce a total score out of 200. WBJEEB then arranges all candidates in descending order of this total score. The candidate with the highest marks gets Rank 1. Lower scores produce higher rank numbers. That descending arrangement is the basis for the WBJEE marks vs rank system. The official WBJEE 2026 result along with rank cards is expected between July and August 2026 at wbjeeb.nic.in.

### WBJEE 2026 Marking Scheme: How Scores Are Built

The marks vs rank in WBJEE depend not just on how many questions a candidate attempts, but on the category of questions answered correctly and incorrectly. Understanding the marking scheme is important before using any WBJEE marks vs rank predictor. WBJEE 2026 divides questions into three categories across both papers:

| Question Category | Marks per Correct Answer | Negative Marking | Answer Type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Category I** | 1 mark | −¼ per wrong answer | Single correct option |
| **Category II** | 2 marks | −½ per wrong answer | Single correct option |
| **Category III** | 2 marks | **No negative marking** | One or more correct options |

### What Is GMR Rank in WBJEE?

Many candidates who check their result for the first time see two rank numbers and ask: what is GMR rank in WBJEE, and how is it different from the other rank on the card?

The GMR rank in WBJEE stands for General Merit Rank. WBJEEB calculates the GMR rank in WBJEE by combining the total marks a candidate scores in both Paper I (Mathematics) and Paper II (Physics and Chemistry) together, out of 200. Every candidate who appears for both papers and meets the minimum qualifying cutoff receives a GMR.

The GMR rank is used for admission to all B.Tech, B.E., architecture, and the pharmacy programme at Jadavpur University specifically. Seat allotment during WBJEE counselling is driven entirely by the GMR. Your category rank appears on the rank card alongside your GMR, but the actual counselling seat allocation process uses the GMR as the operative rank.

### What Is PMR Rank in WBJEE?

PMR rank in WBJEE stands for Pharmacy Merit Rank. The PMR rank in WBJEE is calculated differently from GMR. It is based only on Paper II marks, meaning Physics and Chemistry scores combined out of 100.

Candidates who appeared for both Paper I and Paper II receive both a GMR and a PMR rank. Candidates who appeared only in Paper II receive a PMR but no GMR. Candidates who only appeared for Paper I receive no rank.

The PMR rank is used for admission to degree-level pharmacy courses at all enlisted pharmacy colleges across West Bengal, except Jadavpur University. Pharmacy admission at Jadavpur University uses the GMR instead of PMR. This distinction matters a great deal for the WBJEE pharmacy rank vs college analysis covered later in this article.

### What Is TFW Rank in WBJEE? Category Breakdown

Several candidates also ask: What is TFW rank in WBJEE and how is it assigned? **TFW stands for Tuition Fee Waiver.** The TFW scheme provides full tuition fee exemption at participating colleges to economically weaker candidates who do not fall under SC, ST, or OBC reservation but cannot afford engineering fees.

 TFW rank in WBJEE is not a separate merit rank calculated independently. A candidate with a strong GMR is eligible to claim the TFW quota during counselling if they meet the income and eligibility criteria set by the state government. The TFW closing rank at top colleges tends to be higher (i.e., more competitive) than general open seats in the same branch because TFW seats are limited.

At Jadavpur University, the TFW closing rank for popular branches has historically been in the 600 to 730 range based on 2024 counselling data. This is considerably more relaxed than the General closing rank for the same branches, which often closes within 100 to 120. For candidates eligible under TFW, this is one of the most financially valuable reservations available in WBJEE.

### How WBJEE Rank Decides Counselling: Tie-Breaking Rules

Two candidates with the same total marks in WBJEE can end up with different GMR ranks. WBJEEB applies a defined tie-breaking sequence to resolve identical scores. Let’s have a look:

- For GMR tie-breaking, marks in Mathematics (Paper I) are compared first. The candidate with higher Maths marks gets the better rank. If still equal, marks in Physics are compared. If still equal, marks in Chemistry are compared. If all three subjects are identical, the candidate with fewer negative marks across the paper gets preference. If the tie persists after all the above, the older candidate receives the better rank.
- For PMR tie-breaking, marks in Physics (Paper II) are compared first. If equal, marks in Chemistry decide the rank. Remaining tie-breaking rules follow a similar sequence to GMR.

These rules explain why two students with the same total out of 200 can end up with ranks hundreds of positions apart in the WBJEE marks vs rank system.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. What is the difference between WBJEE marks vs rank for General and SC category candidates?**
For a score of around 120 to 130 marks, a General category candidate can expect a GMR around 700 to 1000. An SC category candidate with the same score typically gets an SC category rank below 300. At Jadavpur University CSE, the General closing rank was around 120 while the SC category closing rank was around 900 in 2025.

**Q2. What is GMR rank in WBJEE and how is it calculated?**
The GMR rank in WBJEE is the General Merit Rank. It is calculated by adding marks from Paper I (Mathematics) and Paper II (Physics and Chemistry) to get a total out of 200. All candidates are then ranked in descending order. The highest scorer gets Rank 1.

**Q3. What is PMR rank in WBJEE and who gets it?**
PMR rank in WBJEE stands for Pharmacy Merit Rank. It is calculated using Paper II marks only (Physics and Chemistry, out of 100). Candidates who appear for both papers receive both a GMR and a PMR. Candidates who appear only for Paper II get only a PMR. PMR is used for B.Pharma admissions at all pharmacy colleges except Jadavpur University.

**Q4. What is TFW rank in WBJEE?**
The TFW rank in WBJEE is assigned to candidates eligible under the Tuition Fee Waiver scheme. It is not an independent merit rank. TFW seats are filled using the GMR of candidates who qualify for TFW. The TFW closing rank at top colleges like Jadavpur University is typically 5 to 6 times more relaxed than the General open seat closing rank.

**Q5. How many marks are needed for Jadavpur University through WBJEE?**
Based on 2025 trends, a score of around 160 to 170 marks (General category) is needed to target CSE or ECE at Jadavpur University. A score above 130 marks can still access some branches at JU such as Chemical Engineering or Civil Engineering. SC and ST candidates can access JU with significantly lower scores through their category ranks.

**Q6. Can I get a government engineering college with 80 marks in WBJEE?**
Yes. A score of 80 to 90 marks in WBJEE can get a GMR in the 6,000 to 12,000 range for General category candidates. Kalyani Government Engineering College, Haldia Institute of Technology, and some branches at Netaji Subhas Engineering College are options in this rank range. SC and ST candidates may access better options with the same score through category reservation.

**Q7. What rank is needed for Heritage Institute of Technology WBJEE?**
Heritage Institute of Technology has an opening rank of around 390 and a closing rank of around 3,340 for its most competitive branches based on 2025 trends. For non-CSE branches, the closing rank extends beyond 5,000.

**Q8. What is the WBJEE pharmacy rank vs college for top pharmacy institutions?**
Based on 2025 data, the Institute of Pharmacy, Jalpaiguri closes at around PMR rank 3,600. Guru Nanak Institute of Pharmaceutical Science and Technology closes around PMR rank 8,400. NSHM Knowledge Campus and Brainware University are available at PMR ranks beyond 10,000.

**Q9. How does WBJEE tie-breaking work if two candidates score the same marks?**
WBJEEB first compares Maths marks. If equal, Physics marks are compared. If still tied, Chemistry marks decide. If all subjects are identical, fewer negative marks is the next criterion. If the tie still persists, the older candidate receives the better rank.

**Q10. What are the minimum marks to qualify for WBJEE counselling?**
General and EWS candidates need around 45 to 50 marks out of 200 to be eligible for the GMR rank list. SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, and PwD candidates can qualify with around 40 to 45 marks. Qualifying the cutoff only makes a candidate eligible for the rank list; it does not guarantee admission to a specific college.
