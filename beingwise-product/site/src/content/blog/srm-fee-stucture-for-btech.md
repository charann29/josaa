## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the SRM fee for a 4-year B.Tech program with hostel?

The total SRM fee for a 4-year B.Tech program including hostel accommodation generally ranges between ₹15.82 lakh and ₹23.82 lakh, depending on the campus, branch, hostel type, and other applicable charges.

### 2. Is the SRM CSE fee higher than the ECE fee?

Yes, the B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) program generally has a higher fee structure than Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE). The estimated CSE fee is around ₹19.1 lakh, while the ECE fee is approximately ₹11.1 lakh.

### 3. What is the SRM management quota fee?

The SRM management quota fee is typically higher than the regular admission fee. For CSE-related branches, the additional amount is generally estimated to range between ₹6 lakh and ₹8 lakh, subject to admission policies and seat availability.

### 4. Are SRM fees the same across all campuses?

No, the SRM fee structure differs across campuses. Tuition fees, hostel charges, and other academic expenses may vary depending on the campus and program selected.

### 5. What B.Tech scholarships are available at SRM University?

SRM University offers several scholarship and fee-waiver schemes for B.Tech students. These include merit-based scholarships, SRMJEEE rank-based scholarships, sports scholarships, and financial assistance programs. Eligible students may receive tuition fee waivers of up to 100% under selected scholarship schemes such as the Founder’s Scholarship and SRM Merit Scholarship.
