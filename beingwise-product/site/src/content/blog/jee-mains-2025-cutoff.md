## Factors JEE Mains 2025 Cutoff

There are several factors that affect JEE Mains Expected Cutoff 2025, and hence it fluctuates every year based on the following factors:

- **Competition:** The cut-off might be affected by the number of candidates who are appearing for the exam. The higher the competition, the higher the cutoff for JEE Mains 2025.
- **Exam Toughness:**Exam toughness can also decide the cutoff. If the toughness of the exam is lower, many candidates will score more marks, which leads to an increase in the exam cutoff.
- **Performance of candidates:** Based on the performance of all the candidates who appeared for the exam, the cutoff will be affected.
- **Vacant Seats:**The number of vacant seats ready to be filled will also affect the exam cutoff, as fewer seats mean greater competition, which in turn raises the cutoff.
- **Previous Year’s Trends:** Past JEE Main cutoffs serve as a benchmark for the current year’s cutoffs. Analysing trends of earlier years can provide insights into potential outcomes for this year.

## **JEE Mains 2025 Cutoff: Subject-Wise Marks Required**

**JEE Main does not have any subject-wise cutoff**. NTA shortlists candidates based solely on their **total score**, after **[normalisation process](/blog/jee-main/normalization-in-jee-main/)**, and then generates the **NTA Score (Percentile)**.

## **JEE Mains Expected Cutoff 2025: Difference Between Qualifying Cutoff and Admission Cutoff**

When preparing for JEE Main, many students get confused between the qualifying cutoff and the admission cutoff. While both are important, they serve different purposes and are set by different authorities. Knowing the difference helps you plan better for JEE Advanced and admissions in top institutes.

### **JEE Mains 2025 Cutoff: Qualifying Cutoff (NTA Qualifying Cutoff)**

NTA sets JEE Mains 2025 qualifying cutoff. It represents the minimum percentile or score a candidate must achieve to become eligible for JEE Advanced. But meeting this cutoff does not guarantee admission. It only allows you to register for the next stage. The cutoff is determined by the total number of candidates, exam difficulty, and the number of seats available for JEE Advanced.

### **JEE Mains 2025 Cutoff: Admission Cutoff (JoSAA Admission Cutoff)**

The admission cutoff is set by JoSAA and shows the minimum rank or score required to secure admission in a particular institute and branch. Unlike the qualifying cutoff, admission cutoffs vary across institutes, availability of seats, branches, categories, etc.
