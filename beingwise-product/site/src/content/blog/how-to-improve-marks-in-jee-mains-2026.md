## How to Improve Marks in JEE Mains 2026

Refer to the following to learn how to increase the JEE rank. Before moving to any of the advanced preparation strategies, students are advised to be familiar with the JEE Mains syllabus 2026.

### Develop Excellent Conceptual clarity

To achieve better grades in JEE Mains 2026, it is important to start with a proper grasp of the basics in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Rather than memorising formulas, derivations, and applications, emphasise comprehension of formulas and derivations. Good basics will enable one to answer both direct and application-based questions correctly.

### Adhere to a planned study

An effective and well-planned study programme assists in the completion of the whole syllabus in a systematic manner. Divide time on each subject according to level of difficulty and individual competence, and make sure that revision and practice are taking place on a daily basis.

### Do Practice in terms of Mock Tests

Practising the [**JEE Main Mock Tests**](/mock-test/jee-mains) will also enable you to know the exam pattern and time management. By comparing performance on mock tests when you first attempt, you can identify what mistakes you made and develop a strategy to keep you out of those the next time you take the actual test.

**Note:** Students can access the JEE Main free mock test by clicking on the above link.

### Examine and Enhance the Weaknesses

The post-test analysis is important to realise those issues, in which the mistakes are common. Take an additional time in revising such areas and finding out solutions to similar questions to enhance your understanding and improve your accuracy.

### Improve Speed and Accuracy

Train to answer questions more quickly without making more mistakes. Topics-based tests and timer practice assist in the compromise of speed and accuracy, which is vital in scoring better in JEE Mains.

### Revise Consistently

Constant revision will guarantee long-term memory of the concepts and formulas. The use of short notes, formula sheets and speedy revisions prior to mock tests is useful to bolster and minimise the stress associated with the end of the time.

### Getting Learning to ask Smart Questions

The correct selection of questions in the test can greatly improve your grades. You should start with easy and known questions to ensure that you are guaranteed a mark, and then proceed to moderately challenging ones.

### Be Consistent and Confident

Hard work, non-relaxing training and positive attitude are crucial to performance. Being confident and calm in the process of preparation and on the exam day is beneficial in the application of the knowledge and overall marks in JEE Mains 2026.

## Subject-wise Tips to Improve Marks in JEE Mains 2026

Check out the following to learn about how to improve marks in JEE Mains 2026. The following list suggests subject-wise JEE Main preparation tips to help students modify their strategies based on the subject.

### Physics

- Focus on learning physics concepts and formulas, not memorisation.
- Place emphasis on practising numeric problems and performing dimensional analysis.
- Review Modern Physics, Current Electricity and Electrostatics frequently.
- Practice solving previous year problems to increase your ability to solve problems that require applying concepts.
- Improve accuracy when solving numeric problems by solving fewer problems, but have a clear understanding of what you are doing with the math.

### Chemistry

- Split your preparation into three sections: Physical Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, and Inorganic Chemistry.
- In Inorganic Chemistry, remember key reactions, exceptions, and period trends, including the ones that are most important for exams.
- Regularly practice numerics in Physical Chemistry to improve your speed at calculating numeric problems.
- Understand the mechanism of the reactions, rather than memorise them in Organic Chemistry.
- Thoroughly review the NCERTs for Inorganic Chemistry topics.

### Mathematics

- Regularly practice exam questions to build speed and confidence in problem-solving ability.
- Concentrate on Calculus, Algebra and Coordinate Geometry, as they have the highest representation on examinations.
- Know the most common mathematical formulas and shortcuts to allow you to perform calculations more accurately.
- When practising, do not spend excessive time on lengthy problems.
- Use mock tests as tools to learn how to manage your time effectively and how to choose the problems to solve during the actual exams.

## Final Preparation Tips Before the JEE Mains 2026 Exam

Check out the following to go through last-minute preparation tips to learn how to increase the JEE rank.

- Regular modernisation of formulas, primary responses, and pertinent ideas obtained on the basis of brief notes is likely to be recommended.
