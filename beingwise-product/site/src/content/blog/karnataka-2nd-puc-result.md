## How to Download Karnataka 2nd PUC Result?

Refer to the following to know how to download the Karnataka 2nd PUC 2026 results.

- Visit the official website of the Karnataka board – kseab.karnataka.gov.in
- Tap on the link for “2nd PUC Result 2026”.
- Provide registration number and date of birth in the login column.
- Click on Submit.
- The 2nd PUC result will appear on the device.
- Download the result and print it for future reference.
