The MHT CET exam scheduled for May 10th, 2024 shift 2 has been postponed. The timings for the Second shift are 2 PM to 5 PM. According to the information from candidates, the shift 1 exam was delayed. Due to this, the authorities have cancelled the exam for 250 students on the second shift.

The affected examination centre is **RR Info Borivali Centre**. The CET cell assures candidates that rescheduled dates will be announced shortly. Students are advised to regularly check the official website [cetcell.mahacet.org](http://cetcell.mahacet.org), for updates on the rescheduled dates.

The MHT CET Shift 1 exam conducted on May 10th, 2024 was delayed, but candidates were still provided with the full allotted time of 3 hours for the examination. However, due to the delay in the completion of the Shift 1 exam, the authorities have decided to postpone the Shift 2 exam scheduled for the same day.

Candidates can also access the postponed notification released on the official website: [cetcell.mahacet.org](https://cetcell.mahacet.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Notice_exam-extend.pdf) for further details and instructions regarding the rescheduling of the exam.

The CET Cell authorities will release the rescheduled dates shortly after the completion of the scheduled dates. Candidates can expect the new exam dates for MHT CET RR Info Borivali Centre. To stay updated regularly check the official website for rescheduled dates. The expected dates to conduct the exam are May 18th to May 21st, 2024.
