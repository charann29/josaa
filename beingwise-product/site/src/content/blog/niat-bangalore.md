## How to Apply for the Vyasa Deemed to be University Collaborated with NIAT Industry-ready Upskilling Program?

To apply for the S-VYASA University and SPIHER Collaborated with the NIAT Industry-ready Upskilling Program, candidates must visit the official website and register for NAT. The final enrollment is based on a one-on-one counselling session. NIAT will send the names of the students (with their written consent) to the Universities. The final admission to the BTech course is based on the rules of S VYASA University and SPIHER.

### NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT) And Syllabus

**NAT Eligibility Criteria:** 10+2 (PCM) + NAT + S-VYASA and SPIHER University Eligibility Criteria

All the eligible and interested students can apply for the NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT). The exam is conducted for enrollment in the Upskilling program in the NIAT Training Centre. The admission to the BTech course is as per the rules and regulations set by S-VYASA University and SPIHER.

### NAT Syllabus

**1. Logical Reasoning & Problem Solving**

- **Number Theory:** Concepts of divisibility, prime numbers, factors, and remainders.
- **Ratios & Proportions:** Solving problems involving ratios, proportions, and percentages.
- **Time & Work:** Calculating work done, time taken, and efficiency.
- **Coding & Decoding**: Encoding and decoding using alphabet and number patterns.
- **Blood Relations:** Understanding and solving problems based on family relationships.

**2. Quantitative Aptitude**

- **Sets:** Set operations, Venn diagrams, and related applications.
- **Polynomials:** Types of polynomials, operations, and solving polynomial equations.
- **Coordinate Geometry:** Concepts involving points, lines, and shapes on a coordinate plane.
- **Probability:** Basic probability, outcomes, and event-based problem solving.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Where can I download NIAT Brochure?

Go to the official website of NIAT: https://www.niatindia.com/. Click on “Download NIAT brochure”, fill out the form with the necessary details like name, mobile number, language, state, etc. Click on Submit, and you will get the NIAT brochure.

### 2. How do I apply for a NAT?

Visit the official website [niatindia.com](http://niatindia.com/). And register for the NAT by paying the registration fees.

### 3. What are the NIAT Industry-ready Upskilling Program at Vyasa Engineering College Bangalore fees?

The NIAT Industry-ready Upskilling Program at S-VYASA University’s fee structure is INR 8 Lakhs to INR 18 Lakhs for a 4-year duration. However, the annual NIAT college fee structure for CSE ranges from INR 2 Lakhs to INR 4.5 Lakhs. However, it is important to note that NIAT is not a college; rather, it is an upskilling centre that collaborated with S Vyasa School of Advanced Studies and SPIHER to deliver the CSE subject.

### 4. What if I fail to attend the NIAT one-on-one counselling session after qualifying for NAT?

If you fail to attend the NIAT Nxtwave counselling session even after qualifying for NAT, then your candidature will be cancelled, and you will not be enrolled in the Institute. The counselling session is vital to confirm your enrollment in NIAT.

### 5. Are there any hostel and accommodation costs at the University campus of NIAT S-Vyasa University School of Advanced Studies?

Yes, hostel costs are separate and, on average, range from INR 1 to INR 1.5 lakh per year based on the room type (AC/non-AC) and the amenities included in the facilities (Wi-Fi, meals, laundry).

### 6. How does the NIAT skill-map stay relevant to industry needs?

The skill-map is continuously refined using insights from thousands of companies and evolving tech trends, ensuring alignment with real-world job requirements.

### 7. How is learning in NIAT Training Centre in Bangalore different from traditional classroom-based approaches?

The focus is on hands-on, project-based learning and real-world application rather than purely theoretical instruction.

### 8. Are the projects in the NIAT program based on real-world scenarios?

Yes, the learning modules focus on real-world problem-solving, helping students build practical, portfolio-ready projects.

### 9. Does participating in the NAT exam guarantee admission to a university?

No, participating in the NxtWave Assessment Test is only part of the process. Final admission depends on meeting the eligibility criteria set by the S Vyasa University and SPIHER.

### 10. What happens after completing the NIAT upskilling program?

Students receive an Industry Ready Certificate (IRC) for their participation and achievements in the program, while their degree is awarded separately by the university based on its criteria.

### 11. What is the NIAT College in Bangalore address?

The NIAT, NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies, is not a college in Bangalore. The NIAT upskilling sessions in Bangalore are conducted at Training Centres provided by the collaborating university. The address of S-Vyasa Deemed to be University is in Sattva Global City, Mysore Road, Remco Housing Society, Rajarajeshwari Nagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka 5600. The address of the SPIHER (St. Peter’s Institute of Higher Education and Research) campus, which offers the NIAT upskilling program, is in Chandapura, Bengaluru, Karnataka.
