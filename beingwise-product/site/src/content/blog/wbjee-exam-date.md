West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination is a state-level entrance examination into various colleges in West Bengal. The exam is conducted by WBJEEB every year for entrance into courses like undergraduate courses like Engineering & Technology, Pharmacy, and Architecture. The officials announced **April 27 th, 2025** to be the date for conducting the WBJEE exam across the state. While the application and registration process for WBJEE is expected to be in the month of December, The officials still have to confirm the official dates for other phases of the exam.

Candidates are required to fill out the registration form and submit their application in order to appear for the WBJEE 2025 exam. Candidates are advised to fill out and submit this application form before the deadline which is yet to be released by the authorities. The eligibility criteria for WBJEE are made available for candidates to check their eligibility. Candidates can access the official website wbjeeb.nic.in and check for further updates which will be announced soon. The date of release of the admit card and announcement of results will soon be announced in the official website.
