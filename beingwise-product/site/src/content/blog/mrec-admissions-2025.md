## MREC Admissions 2025 For BTech Course

Candidates seeking admission to the B.Tech program at Malla Reddy Engineering College (MREC) must have secured at least 50% marks in their 10+2 examination (with 45% for SC/ST candidates). The qualifying subjects must include Physics and Mathematics as compulsory, along with one of the following: Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, or a Technical Vocational subject. Additionally, candidates are required to have qualified in either the JEE Main or the TS EAMCET entrance examination.

The selection criteria for B.Tech admission at MREC Telangana are based on a combination of 10+2 marks and entrance exam scores (JEE Main or TS EAMCET). A merit list will be prepared category-wise, detailing the All India Ranks (AIR) of the applicants.

To participate in the admission process, candidates must register for online counselling. Based on the cutoff ranks set by MREC Telangana, seats will be allotted accordingly during the counselling rounds.

## MREC Admissions 2025: Application Process

The seats to be allotted in professional institutions for admission of candidates are classified into the following categories:

### **1. Category A Seats**

- Comprises **70%** of the sanctioned intake in each course.
- Filled with **eligible candidates** based on the **rank obtained in EAMCET** (Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical Common Entrance Test).

### **2. Category B Seats**

- Comprises **30%** of the total intake in each course.
- Open for admission to **all eligible candidates** on a **merit basis**.
- Includes candidates from:
  - Other **States**
  - **Union Territories** of India
  - **NRIs** (Non-Resident Indians)

Students who meet the eligibility criteria can apply for different courses at Malla Reddy Engineering College using the online application system. Below is the step-by-step application process.

- Visit the official MREC website and head to the “Admissions” section to start the registration process.
- Fill out the application form with correct personal and academic information.
- Once completed, submit the form and pay the application fee of INR 500 for General applicants and INR 250 for SC/ST candidates using a debit/credit card or net banking.
- After you’ve successfully submitted the form, make sure to download or print a copy for your reference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****Can I switch my branch after getting MREC Admissions 2025?****

Branch change requests are considered **only after 1st year**, subject to academic performance and seat availability.

### **2. **********Does MREC have a centralized counseling system?************

Yes, for **convener quota seats**, admissions are done through **TS EAMCET/ICET counseling**. Management quota admissions are handled **directly by the college**.

### 3. ****************************************Can students from outside Telangana apply for MREC?****************************************

Yes. **Open category seats are available for all Indian nationals**. Some quotas (state-subsidized) may be limited to Telangana domicile candidates.

### 4. ********************What support does MREC offer for newly admitted students?********************

MREC provides a **student orientation program**, academic mentorship, and bridge courses in math, coding, and communication skills to help students transition smoothly

### 5. ****************Can I apply for MREC Admissions 2025 if my qualifying exam results are pending?****************

Yes, for MREC Admissions 2025 provisional applications are accepted. However, final admission will depend on the submission of original marksheets before the counseling date.
