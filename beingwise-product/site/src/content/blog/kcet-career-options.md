**KCET Career Options** **2025**: A student’s decision after their 12th is a life changer. They can pursue different courses after their 12th class by attempting CET (Common Entrance Test) examinations. The students can have many different career options after their KCET examination. Let’s see all the options in detail in this article.

The KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test) is a state-level entrance test conducted by the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) every year. This test is a gateway for students to get admission into various courses and colleges.

The KCET 2025 examination will be expected to be conducted on18.04.2025 and 19.04.2025. The students are now waiting for the KCET Results which will be expected released on **25.05.2025.**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the procedure to get admission to an Architecture course?

To get admission to an Architecture course, the student has to take NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture). In addition to qualifying for the NATA exam, the candidate must register for KCET 2025, fill out the application, and pay the admission fee to become eligible to participate in Counselling.

### 2. What is the procedure for admission to B.P.T., B.Sc. Allied Health Sciences, BPO courses based on PU marks?

For the candidates seeking admission to B.P.T., B.Sc. Allied Health Sciences, BPO Courses based on PU marks, there is no Entrance Exam but they must compulsorily register for CET-2025, fill out the application, and pay the fees to become eligible to participate in the counseling.
