## JEE Main 2025 Dress Code: For Male and Female

Although the NTA has not yet released specific JEE Main 2025 Dress Code. The dress code primarily restricts the wearing of caps, scarves/stoles, and jewellery/metallic items. Students are also encouraged to dress according to the weather and season. It is important for candidates to familiarise themselves with these guidelines before the exam to ensure a smooth examination process. Detailed instructions regarding JEE Main 2025 Dress Code for both male and female candidates are outlined below.

### **JEE Main Dress Code for Male 2025**

- Male candidates are prohibited from wearing any clothing that contains metallic elements as they can cause inconvenience during frisking.

- It is recommended to wear half-sleeves or comfortable clothing while writing the exam and clothes with drapes should be avoided.

- Students are not allowed to wear accessories covering their heads like mufflers, or caps.

- Wearing jewelry or ornaments is strictly prohibited.

- Students are prohibited from entering the exam hall with goggles, rings, bracelets, or any similar items.

- Candidates wearing shoes will undergo more rigorous checks. Therefore, it is recommended to wear slippers or sandals.

### **JEE Main Dress Code for Female 2025**

- Female candidates should wear comfortable clothing considering the season and weather on the exam day.

- Candidates must avoid wearing metallic items or items containing metal, such as belts, underwired bras, and similar accessories.

- Wearing a simple T-shirt and jeans, or a top/kurta with leggings, would be more suitable. It is advised to choose clothing made from cotton, Khadi, or other breathable fabrics.

- Female candidates are prohibited from wearing rings, jewelry, bracelets, etc.

- Any fancy items like hats, scarves, watches, or sunglasses must be removed before entering the examination hall.

- Clothing with too many pockets should be avoided.

- Hoodies, sweatshirts, and tops with long sleeves should be avoided.
- Avoid complex hairstyles like bun hairstyles as the frisking process may take time.

## **JEE Mains 2025 Dress Code: Exceptions for Medical and Religious Reasons**

JEE Mains 2025 Dress code needs to be followed strictly. But, NTA allows certain relaxations for candidates with **medical needs** or **religious requirements**. These exceptions ensure comfort and inclusivity, without compromising exam security.

### **JEE Mains 2025 Dress Code: Medical Exceptions**

Candidates who need to wear or carry anything due to a medical condition must follow the points below:

- Carry a valid medical document (doctor’s certificate or prescription).
- Items allowed only when medically required include:
  - Full-sleeve or compression garments
  - Medical devices or essential medicines
- Inform the exam authorities in advance if possible.
- Be prepared for additional frisking at the entry gate.

### **JEE Mains 2025 Dress Code: Religious Exceptions**

Students who wear specific attire due to religious beliefs will be allowed entry, provided security rules are respected.

- Items may include turbans, hijabs, headscarves, burqas, etc.
- Reach the exam centre early for extra verification time.
- Cooperate fully during frisking and identification.
- Religious clothing should not have metal, hidden pockets, or heavy accessories.

***Note*****:** Exceptions are approved only when supported by **genuine medical documents** or **religious requirements**.

## **JEE Main 2025 Dress Code: Prohibited Items**

Students are prohibited from carrying certain items into the examination hall to ensure that the exam is conducted without any discrepancies. Here is the list of items that you should not carry with you to the exam hall.

- Water bottle & eatables
- Smartwatches
- Notebooks, papers, and other stationery items
- Electronic gadgets like mobile phones, cameras, calculators etc.
- Metallic Objects

## JEE Mains 2025 Rules and Regulations

- **Arrival:** Reach the exam center at least 1 or 2 hours before the scheduled time to complete all the security checks.

- **Seating:** Take your seat and listen to the instructions carefully. Only sit in your designated seat as changing seats can result in disqualification.

- **Admit card & ID:** Carry your original ID along with your admit card as you are not allowed to write the exam without these.

- **Confirmation:** Confirm details on the screen match with your admit card, and inform the invigilator incase of a mismatch.

- **False Information:** Providing false information or attempting to attend multiple sessions will result in disqualification, and your result will not be declared.

- **Missing the Exam:** There will be no retest in case you miss the exam.

- **Rough work:** Rough work should only be done on the paper provided and submit the paper after completing the exam.

## Exam Day Guidelines for JEE Mains 2025

Here are the set of guidelines students need to follow during the exam day.

- Candidates should stay away from any kind of malpractice and follow the code of conduct during exams.

- Candidates without original proof of their ID card, photograph, and admit card are not allowed to appear for the exam.

- Candidates are advised to appear for the exam at least one hour before the set time to avoid last minute rush.

- Candidates are advised to visit the exam venue a day prior to the exam to familiarize themselves with the route and travel time, ensuring a smooth and stress-free experience on the day of the exam.

- Any Misbehaviour should be avoided which includes Conversations, disturbances, or gestures.
- NTA will not be liable to conduct a re-test for a candidate under any circumstances.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **Is there a strict JEE Main 2025 Dress Code?**

Yes, NTA has issued clear dress code guidelines. Candidates must avoid accessories like caps, scarves, jewellery, or any metallic items inside the exam centre. The regulations differ slightly for male and female candidates.

### 2.  **What should I do if I need to wear specific attire for medical reasons that is not mentioned in JEE Mains 2025 Dress Code?**

If you require special clothing such as a full sleeve shirt or need to carry medication/devices, bring valid medical documentation (for example, a doctor’s prescription or certificate). It is strongly advised to inform the exam authorities in advance and present your documents at the entry gate for verification.

### 3. Does NTA allow religious attire under JEE Mains 2025 Dress Code?

Yes, attire such as a burqa, hijab, turban, kirpan, or headscarf is generally permitted. However, you should arrive early at the exam centre to allow extra time for frisking and inform the authorities beforehand.

### 4. **What happens if I wear prohibited items or do not follow JEE Mains 2025 Dress Code?**

Non-compliance with the dress code may result in denial of entry or disqualification from the exam. Always check your admit card and official NTA notifications for the latest guidelines.

### 5.  **What should I do if I have questions or concerns about JEE Main 2025 Dress Code on exam day?**

If you have any doubts, approach the exam centre authorities as early as possible. Cooperation during checks and timely communication can help avoid last-minute issues.

### 6. **Is there a color restriction for clothing under JEE Main 2025 Dress Code?**

No, there is no restriction on clothing color, but light and comfortable clothing is recommended to speed up security checks.
