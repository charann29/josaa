## Student Reaction on MHT CET May 12 question paper Shift 1

When we talked to test takers coming out of the exam centre to know the MHT CET 2026 difficulty level. Based on the MHT CET Paper Analysis by students, we have shared their reaction for all 3 sections, Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, individually below.

- **Aarav Patil** reviewed that Physics was formula-based and easy to answer. Chemistry was the easiest section, as most of the questions were directly from the textbook. Mathematics was the most difficult section, as the Calculus and Integration questions consumed most of my time.
- **Sneha Kulkarni** said that the Physics section had an equal proportion of conceptual and numerical questions. Chemistry was scoring and easy to answer. The mathematics section had easy concepts, but answering lengthy calculation problems in 90 minutes was tough.
- **Rohan Deshmukh** rated the Physics section as moderate, as some questions were quite challenging. Chemistry was easy and had good theoretical concepts. The Mathematics section was time-consuming and required lengthy calculations.
- **Priya Sharma** reviewed that the Physics section was easier as several questions were formula-based. The chemistry section was easy with the appropriate preparation of the NCERT textbooks. The mathematics section had a heavy portion of Calculus and Vectors.”
