Securing SRM University Scholarships makes aiming for a top B.Tech seat at SRM IST dramatically easier and affordable for aspirants. Students with high SRMJEEE ranks, or exceptional academic or sports performance, are rewarded with a scholarship at SRM University. It offers up to 100% fee waiver to 10% scholarship to deserving candidates. Mostly, the [SRM University](https://www.srmist.edu.in/) for b tech is provided based on merit, but other special category scholarships are available for economically weaker, differently abled, and other specified categories of students. ## SRM University Scholarship​ For B Tech – Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the SRM University Scholarship for B tech, students need to fulfill the basic admission requirements listed below:

- Fulfill the admission criteria for the B.Tech 1st year application.
- Appear in the SRMJEEE exam and counselling round for scholarship consideration.
- Students from the economically weaker section.
- Students performing well in the JEE Main entrance exam or the 12th standard exam.
- Students with exceptional contributions in the sports field are also considered.

## SRM University Scholarship​ For B Tech – Key Scholarship Schemes

Some of the top SRM University Scholarships are offered for B tech students to reward students’ academic achievements, entrance exam preference, sports achievements and financial need.  These merit scholarships reduce tuition fees to a large extent, and in some cases, the hostel charges are covered as well. Some of the top SRM University Scholarship Details are as follows:

| SRM University Scholarship Scheme | Eligibility | Benefits |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Founder’s Scholarship** | Only exceptional performers in SRMJEEE or a national-level exam | 100% tuition fee waiver & hostel fee waiver |
| **SRM Merit Scholarship** | Students securing top ranks in SRMJEEE | Up to 100% tuition fee waiver |
| **SRMJEEE Rank-Based Scholarships** | Students with top to mid-tier SRMJEEE ranks | 100% to 10% tuition fee waiver |
| **Sports Scholarships** | Students with achievements in recognized sporting events at state, national or international levels | Tuition fee concession & access to sports facilities |

## SRM University Scholarship​ For B Tech – Renewal Criteria

To retain the SRM University Scholarship for b tech students need to satisfy the specific renewal conditions every academic year. The exact SRM University Scholarship renewal requirements are as follows:

- Must have a 7.0 CGPA or more as per the prescribed scholarship category every year.
- Apply for scholarship renewal within the specified deadline every year.
- There should not be any arrears or backlogs.
- Should maintain conduct and disciplinary actions during the academic year.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **How to apply for scholarship in SRM University?**
   SRM University Scholarships mainly do not require a separate application process. It is auto-allocated during the SRMJEEE counselling round. But a few special categories require students to apply separately.
2. **How much fee waiver can I get through SRM University Scholarships?**
   Students get from 10% to 100% scholarship through various merit scholarships under SRM University Scholarships.
3. **Can Class 12 board marks help me get a scholarship at SRM University?**
   Yes, there are a few scholarship schemes which are given to students who secure top marks in Class 12 board exams.
4. **Can a scholarship be cancelled after admission?**
   Yes, the scholarship may be withdrawn or cancelled if a student fails to meet the renewal criteria.
5. **Is attendance required to retain the scholarships?**
   Yes, students need to have around 75% attendance to retain the SRM University Scholarships.
