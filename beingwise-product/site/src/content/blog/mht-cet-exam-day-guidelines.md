The State Common Entrance Test (CET) Cell administered the MHT CET 2026 exam for session 2 from May 10 to 21. The MHT CET exam day guidelines outline the crucial procedures that must be followed while taking the examination.  Before taking the MHT CET 2026 2nd attempt, students should review the exam day guidelines

The MHT CET exam day guidelines include a code of behaviour, what to bring, directions, and more. Any student who does not comply with the guidelines will not be allowed to appear in the exam. You can read the MHT CET exam day instructions printed on the [MHT CET admit card](/blog/mht-cet/mht-cet-2026-admit-card/).

## MHT CET Exam Day Guidelines 2026

MHT CET Exam Day Guidelines contains several sections like dress code, important do’s and don’ts, documents required at the exam centre, instructions for reporting time, prohibited items, rules for biometric verification, and exam hall conduct guidelines that candidates need to follow diligently on the examination day.

## MHT CET 2026 Exam Day Instructions on AI cheating warning

The authority has imposed stringent instructions concerning unfair means during the MHT CET 2026 exam. Students are warned against resorting to the use of any form of artificial intelligence technology, smart devices, or any other hidden electronic gadgets inside the examination venue.

Students should bear in mind that the use of smart watches, Bluetooth devices, earphones, mobile phones, or any other gadget with an AI component is strictly forbidden during the test. Any candidate found guilty of cheating via the use of technology is likely to face severe consequences. In extreme cases, students can also be banned from taking future exams or even subjected to legal proceedings by the authorities.

## MHT CET 2026 Shift Timings

The MHT CET 2026 test is conducted in different shifts on various exam days. Candidates are advised to check their allotted shift time on the MHT CET admit card. It is recommended that the student reach the exam centre one hour before the commencement of the exam for an easy completion of frisking, document verification, and attendance processes.

 Once the gates are closed for the entrance, late entry may not be entertained. Take a look at the MHT CET 2026 Shift Timings given below.

| Shift | Reporting Time | Exam Timing |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Morning Shift | 7:30 AM | 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM |
| Afternoon Shift | 12:30 PM | 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM |

## MHT CET 2026 Reporting Time

The aspirants who are appearing for MHT CET 2026 need to report at the exam center much earlier than the reporting time mentioned in their admit card. There are various security measures that have to be taken before entering the examination hall, which is why candidates are advised to come early.

- Candidates should report to the exam center at least one hour before the start of the examination.
- The entrance gates get closed about 30 minutes before the commencement of the examination, and there are no chances of entry for those who are late.
- Candidates are recommended to make arrangements beforehand and not come late.
- Strict adherence to the reporting schedule will enable students to verify themselves without any difficulty.

## MHT CET 2026 Dress Code

Students writing for the MHT CET 2026 exam are required to abide by the MHT CET dress code and follow all instructions to ensure there are no problems with frisking and verification at the exam centre. Though no formal announcement regarding the same has been made by the State CET Cell, the students are requested to wear comfortable and light clothes.

MHT CET 2026 dress code helps to facilitate the process of frisking without any problems and maintain the fairness of the test.  MHT CET exam day guidelines for Male and females regarding the dress code are given below,

### MHT CET Dress Code for Girls

Girls who will be appearing for the MHT CET 2026 exam shall opt for simple and comfortable clothes so that there will be no difficulty during frisking at the time of entering the examination hall.

- Candidates must wear simple and lightweight clothes with no heavy embroidery or metallic work.
- They should not wear jewelry items like rings, necklaces, bracelets, chains, earrings, or nose pins.
- No wearing high-heeled or heavy footwear inside the exam center.
- The girls must wear comfortable slippers and sandals during the exam.
- No use of handbags, smart watches, or any other electronic gadgets.
- Avoid wearing jackets, caps, or any fashionable accessories during the examination.

### MHT CET Dress Code for Boys

Boys who will be giving the MHT CET 2026 test should abide by the MHT CET dress code 2026 instructions to prevent any delay during the security clearance procedure.

- They should prefer to wear simple and comfortable T-shirts or shirts without large buttons and metal work.
- No wearing jackets, hoodies, or caps inside the exam center.
- Avoid using accessories like chains, bracelets, watches, or smart watches.
- It is better to wear slippers or sandals during the test.
- Avoid bringing any electronic devices or other prohibited items inside the examination hall.

## Documents Required on MHT CET Exam Day

Make sure you bring just about every document you need with you to the exam centre or else you risk not even being allowed to take the exam. The following documents are absolutely crucial on the MHT CET exam day :

- a printed copy of your MHT CET Admit Card – don’t even think about trying to get in without it\
- any government-approved photo ID like an Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence, or School or College ID Card. It’s up to you which one you choose to bring, but you must bring one.

You need to make sure that all the documents you bring are original and up-to-date, & also that all the details on them are easy to see. It’s also a good idea to make a photocopy of your admit card in case the one you brought gets damaged.

## What to Bring to the MHT CET Exam Hall?

You are only allowed to bring a tiny handful of things with you into the exam hall, so be realistic and only bring what you absolutely need. Permitted items include

- Printed MHT CET admit card
- Valid photo ID proof
- Transparent water bottle
- Transparent pen

## MHT CET 2026 Prohibited Items List

There is a long list of things you are not allowed to bring into the exam hall – the exam authorities are serious about maintaining fairness & security. Any of the following will get you turned away from the exam centre on the day

- digital or analogue watches\
- calculators\
- mobile phones\
- any types of earphones or Bluetooth devices\
- any smart gadget\
- any kind of notes, books, or written papers\
- any electronic device of any type

## MHT CET Admit Card instructions

There are certain MHT CET exam day instructions for the admit card that students need to follow to avoid any uneasy situation.

- Candidates must download the MHT CET Admit Card from the official site of the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra.
- Check all details printed on the hall ticket, such as the candidate’s name, roll number, application number, test date, timings of the shift, and exam centre address.
- Confirm whether the photograph and signature on the hall ticket are legible or not.
- Print out two to three copies of the hall ticket as a backup measure.
- Keep one copy of the hall ticket handy at the time of appearing for the examination.
- The candidate cannot enter the examination hall without a valid hall ticket.
- The candidate should carry an identification proof along with the hall ticket.
- Go through all the MHT CET Exam day guidelines provided in the hall ticket before taking the examination.
- Keep the hall ticket safe even after appearing in the exam for further processes like counselling.

## MHT CET Exam Centre Instructions

Candidates taking the MHT CET Exam should strictly adhere to the set MHT CET 2026 exam day rules issued by the State CET Cell. Following the above guidelines for the exam day and MHT CET exam hall guidelines will assist the candidate in avoiding unnecessary problems during the exam.

- Adhere to discipline and maintain complete silence in the exam hall during the whole duration of the exam.
- Candidates should not communicate with other candidates at any point during the examination process.
- Read all instructions shown on the computer screen before starting the exam.
- Candidates should avoid opening any unauthorized tab, software, or windows on the computer system.
- Follow all MHT CET exam centre instructions related to seating arrangements and use of computers.
- Unfair practices like cheating and any kind of misconduct may result in disqualification from the exam.
- Candidates should cooperate with the invigilator during the verification process.
- Candidates are required to stay in their seats until the end of the exam.
