## Entrance Exams & Admission Process For BTech CSE Engineering College in Hyderabad

The admission process for B.Tech CSE in Hyderabad involves qualifying for entrance exams, such as JEE Main, TS EAMCET, or institute-specific tests like BITSAT. Candidates must meet the eligibility criteria, which include a minimum of 60% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics at the 12th-grade level. After exams, a counseling process allocates seats based on merit and preferences. Finally, selected students complete document verification and payment of the fee to confirm their admission. Some colleges also consider merit-based admissions or conduct additional rounds, such as interviews or group discussions.

### Entrance Exam Accepted

The primary entrance examinations accepted by BTech CSE colleges in Hyderabad include:

- **TS EAMCET** (Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture & Medical Common Entrance Test): Most of the colleges accept this score
- **JEE Main**: Accepted by premier institutions like IIT Hyderabad, IIIT Hyderabad, and BITS Pilani
- **JEE Advanced**: Required for IIT Hyderabad admission
- University-specific exams: Some colleges conduct their own entrance tests
  - **Aurora Deemed University:** Conducts its own entrance exam named AUET (Aurora University Entrance Test). Students can also appear for the NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT) to secure admission to the courses offered at NIAT in collaboration with Aurora University.
  - **Mahindra University:** Conducts MUAT (Mahindra University Admission Test)
  - **Chaitanya Deemed to be University**: Conducts CUET (Chaitanya University Entrance Test). Students can also appear for the NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT) to secure admission to the courses offered at NIAT, in collaboration with CDU.
  - **BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus**: Conducts BITSAT (Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test)

### Admission Process

For BTech in Computer Science Engineering: Candidates must have completed their Class 12 education from a recognized board, with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. Additionally, they are generally required to have a minimum aggregate score of 60% across these subjects.

Admission is based on performance in national-level entrance exams, such as JEE Main, TS EAMCET, and other institution-specific tests, in combination with Class 12 results.
