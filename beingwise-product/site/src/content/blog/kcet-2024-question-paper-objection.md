The Karnataka Authority has released the notification of the KCET 2024 **question paper objection facility** at the official website kea.kar.nic.in. Candidates who appeared for the KCET exam can raise objections with a valid reason to KEA email **keaucet24@gmail.com** till **April** **27th, 2024**.

While objecting to any question of KCET 2024, Candidates need to provide specific details such as the subject, version code, and question number accurately. It’s crucial to submit objections before the deadline, otherwise the examination authority won’t consider them.

## How to Raise Objections in the KCET 2024 Question Paper?

Candidates can follow the below steps to raise objections against the question paper:

- Carefully examine the KCET question papers to identify any questions to raise objections.
- Note down the version code, subject, and question number for each question you want to object.
- Send to the email ID keaucet24@gmail.com before the deadline.
- Use a clear and concise subject line indicating that you’re raising objections to specific questions in the KCET question paper.
- If you have any supporting evidence, such as reference materials or documentation, attach them to the email to strengthen your objections.
- Before sending the email, review all the information provided to ensure accuracy.

## Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ’s)

### 1. What is the objection facility in the KCET 2024 question paper?

The objection facility allows candidates to raise concerns or objections regarding specific questions in the KCET 2024 question paper if they believe there are errors, discrepancies, or unclear words.

### 2. How do I raise objections to questions in the KCET 2024 question paper?

Candidates can raise objections by carefully reviewing the question paper, noting details such as version code, subject, and question number for each objection, and then submitting them through the email ID [keaucet24@gmail.com](mailto:keaucet24@gmail.com) provided by the examination authorities.

### 3. What kind of issues can I raise objections for?

You can raise objections for various issues, including incorrect information, wording, or any other discrepancies that you identify in the questions.

### 4. Is there a deadline for raising objections?

Yes, the last date and time to raise objections is **April 27th, 2024**, at 5:30 pm. Candidates must ensure they submit their objections before the deadline to be considered by the examination authorities.

### 5. What happens after I submit my objections?

After you submit your objections, the examination authorities will review them thoroughly. If they find the objections valid, they may make necessary corrections to the question paper accordingly.

### 6. Will I receive a response to my objections?

Yes, candidates will receive a response from the examination authorities regarding the status of their objections after they have been reviewed.

### 7. Is there any fee for raising objections?

The examination authorities do not charge any fee for raising objections.

### 8. Can I raise objections after the deadline?

No, objections raised after the deadline of 27th April 2024 will not be entertained under any circumstances. It is essential to adhere to the deadline for submitting objections.
