**TS EAMCET Application 2025**: The Telangana State Council of Higher Education will close the TS EAMCET 2025 application process on 24th April 2025. Candidates who haven’t applied can apply for the exam on the official website.

The **last date for submission** of TS EAMCET 2025 Online Applications with the late fee of Rs. 5,000/- **is 24-04-2025**.

The TS EAMCET exam for the Engineering stream is scheduled from May 2nd  to 5th, 2025. For Agriculture & Pharmacy Stream, the exam is scheduled from April 29th to 30th, 2025.

The Exam is conducted in online mode as a Computer-Based Test.

Candidates can **download the hall ticket** from the official website [eapcet.tsche.ac.in](https://eapcet.tsche.ac.in/).  For Agriculture & Pharmacy,and the Engineering stream, the downloading service will be available from April **19th, 2025.**
