## Entrance Exams & Admission Process For B.Tech CSE in Mangalore

The top engineering colleges in Mangalore often follow a similar admission process for engineering admissions. Most of the colleges admit students based on the entrance examinations, such as Karantara Common Entrance Test (KCET), and COMED-K for entrance into engineering colleges in Karnataka. A few per cent of seats are also reserved for the Management quota admissions. Students must have passed their class 12th or equivalent examination from a recognised board to be eligible to apply for the engineering admissions in Mangalore. Upon appearing for the counselling processes after qualifying for the entrance examinations, candidates are allotted colleges.

### Entrance Exam Accepted by Engineering Colleges in Mangalore

Most of the Colleges in Mangalore consider the state entrance examination KCET; however, check the entrance examinations accepted by the colleges in Mangalore from below:

- **JEE Main:** National Institute of Technology Karnataka accepts students through the JOSAA counselling. The counselling is conducted for the students who qualified JEE Mains.
- **KCET:** Karnataka Common Entrance Test is a state entrance test conducted for engineering admissions across the state. Most of the colleges fill 40%-45% of the seats based on the KCET.
- **COMED-K:** COMEDK (Consortium of Medical, Engineering, and Dental Colleges of Karnataka) is a state-level entrance exam for admissions into private engineering colleges in Karnataka, offering diverse courses and career opportunities.
- **NAT** (NxtWave Assessment Test): For admission to NIAT Mangalore in Collaboration with Yenepoya Institute of Technology.
- **KRLMPCA:** KRLMPCA (Karnataka Religious & Linguistic Minority Professional Colleges Association) conducts entrance exams for admissions into minority engineering colleges in Karnataka, ensuring opportunities for students from religious and linguistic minority communities.

### Admission Process for Engineering Colleges in Mangalore

Almost all the colleges in Mangalore follow a similar admission process of offering admissions for students based on the entrance examination and counselling process. Students, upon qualifying the entrance examination, have to appear for the counselling process, which is done in a phased manner. After allotting a seat, students have to report to the college physically, by submitting the required set of academic and personal documents. Each college has a reserved set of seats for entrance examination-based admissions and management quota admissions. Candidates are advised to visit the college’s official website to understand the admission criteria for each category.

## Parameters While Choosing Engineering Colleges in Mangalore for CSE

Choosing to join engineering is a crucial decision, so it is better to understand what to consider and what not to consider while choosing an engineering college. Joining engineering colleges that won’t deliver and upskill students can make you regret your decision. It is important to consider the following factors while choosing to join an engineering college in Mangalore.

- **Rankings, Accreditation & Curriculum:** While choosing an engineering college, ensure the college is accredited from from the govt bodies like NAAC, AICTE, etc. This can help you understand the quality and standards offered by the institute/college.

- **Faculty:** Consider the faculty experience in the related domain, as an experienced faculty member can help you understand the concepts better and guide you in the right direction with valuable insights.

- **Resources:** Check for the infrastructural facilities in the college. Well-equipped labs, libraries, and workshops can enhance your learning and ensure you practically understand the concepts.

- **Placements:** Look for the industry connections and placement statistics of the college. Placement stats help you understand the college’s commitment to providing students with better opportunities.

- **Extracurriculars:** Check for the extracurricular activities and how regularly the events and fests were conducted. It ensures that the college is focused on developing students across all spheres.

- **Student Life:** An active student life with workshops, research, hackathons, etc, can help you improve yourself every day. Talk to your peers about student life.
