Narula Institute of Technology, Agarpara, Kolkata is a private engineering college in Agarpara, North Kolkata. It is one of the well-known private engineering colleges in West Bengal for B.Tech programmes. The institute has been running since 2001 under the JIS Group Educational Initiatives. It is affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, West Bengal (MAKAUT, WB). All its technical programmes are approved by AICTE.

The institute holds NAAC ‘A’ accreditation and has been ranked under the NIRF framework for six years from 2017 to 2022, and again in 2024. It was the first private engineering college in Eastern India to receive a QS International Rating. For students looking for engineering colleges with open admission in Kolkata, Narula Institute of Technology is a commonly considered option. The total B.Tech fee across four years is approximately INR 7 lakhs. The campus placement rate has stayed near 90% for three consecutive years.

## B.Tech Admission Process at Narula Institute of Technology

Admission to B.Tech at Narula Institute of Technology is based on entrance exam scores. The institute does not conduct any separate entrance test. The entire process is completed online through the official website. Following the simple process below, candidates can easily refer to the section below

1. Appear for WBJEE, JEE Main, or CEE-AMPAI and obtain a valid rank or score.
2. Visit www.nit.ac.in and click on the Online Admission Application Form.
3. Click on New Registration. Enter your name, mobile number, and email ID.
4. An OTP is sent to your registered number and email. Enter it to proceed.
5. Fill in all required sections, including course details, personal details, family details, and academic details.
6. Upload the required documents in the specified format and size. Submit the form.
7. The institute verifies your documents and sends an admission offer to your email.
8. Log in again to accept the offer and contact the admission cell.
9. Once the payment option is activated, pay Narula Institute of Technology admission fees online.

Note: For any help during Narula Institute of Technology admission, candidates can call 8902496651, 6291977702, or 8100192401.

### Narula Institute of Technology Direct Admission

Students without a WBJEE or JEE Main score can apply through CEE-AMPAI. This is the Common Entrance Examination conducted by AMPAI. The institute accepts this as an alternate route for B.Tech admission. It is the main direct admission option available at Narula Institute of Technology. There is no management quota or offline direct admission process for Indian nationals.

## Narula Institute of Technology B.Tech Syllabus

Narula Institute of Technology follows an autonomous curriculum under MAKAUT, WB. The syllabus is aligned with NEP 2020 guidelines. The current R25 syllabus is effective from the 2025-26 admission batch. The programme runs across eight semesters with theory papers, practicals, workshops, and a final-year project. All branches share a common first year. Group A covers CSE, AIML, IT, and allied branches. Group B covers ECE, EE, ECS, CE, and ME. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Is Narula Institute of Technology a private or government college?

Narula Institute of Technology is a private engineering college. It operates under the JIS Group and is affiliated to MAKAUT, West Bengal. It has NAAC ‘A’ accreditation and AICTE approval but is not a government institution.

### 2. Is Narula Institute of Technology under JIS University?

No, Narula Institute of Technology and JIS University are separate institutions. Both come under the JIS Group but operate independently with different affiliations.

### 3. Is Narula Institute of Technology a Tier 2 college?

Yes, it is generally considered a Tier 2 private engineering college in West Bengal. It is a good option among private colleges but does not rank at the level of top government institutions.

### 4. What entrance exam does Narula Institute of Technology accept for B.Tech?

The institute accepts WBJEE, JEE Main, and CEE-AMPAI for B.Tech admissions. WBJEE is the primary route, while the others serve as alternative pathways.

### 5. Where can I find the Narula Institute of Technology placement PDF?

Placement details and official PDFs are available on the institute’s official website under the placement section. Year-wise statistics are also shared in the WBJEEB institute profile.

### 6. What is the Narula Institute of Technology syllabus for B.Tech?

The institute follows an autonomous curriculum under MAKAUT based on NEP 2020. The R25 syllabus is applicable from the 2025–26 batch, with branch-wise syllabus details available on the official website.

### 7. What is the exam schedule for 2025–26 at Narula Institute of Technology?

As per the official schedule, 6th and 8th semester exams are held from 25th May to 5th June 2026, while 2nd and 4th semester exams are conducted from 12th June to 27th June 2026. The exam fee is ₹1,200 for regular students and ₹1,000 for backlog students.
