## Reasons for KEAM Application Rejections

The following are some of the common reasons that may lead to the rejection of the KEAM 2026 application:

**1. If the uploaded photograph of the candidate does not follow the specifications given below:**

- To apply online, the candidates must have a digital (or scanned image of their photograph.
- The photograph must be in colour with a light-coloured background (White is preferable). A photo taken by a mobile phone/tablet is not acceptable.
- The photograph must be in a passport-size format. The front view of the full face and shoulder portion of the candidate is to be seen clearly in the photograph.
- The face of the candidate should be straight and at the center.
- Photos, wearing a mask, cap, or dark glasses, will be rejected.
- The scanned image file should be in jpg format.
- The dimensions of the photograph should be 150 pixels in width and 200 pixels in height.
- The image file should be between 15kb and 100kb full size.

**2. If the Applicant’s uploaded signature is not clear or genuine, the application will be rejected.**

**3. If authentic proof of Nativity, Date of Birth, and Class 10th certificate are not uploaded in the application portal, the application will be rejected.**

**4. Suppose the required application fee meant for each category is not paid through the proper channel (The application fee should be paid in online mode). In that case, the authorities will reject the candidate’s application.**

**Note:** These are some of the most common KEAM application rejection reasons, and students must avoid it to successfully participate in the exam.

## Steps to Correct a Rejected KEAM Application

In case your application is rejected for KEAM 2026, then the first step should be to determine the reasons for rejection, which are communicated through the notification received by the CEE Kerala. Depending on the reasons for rejection, the steps to be taken are:

**Photo or Signature Issue:** Re-upload the correct photo or Signature as per the following specifications.

**Wrong or Lack of Documents:** Uploading the correct documents, such as Nativity, Date of Birth, and Class 10th Marksheet.

**Mistake in fee payment:** It is important to ensure that the correct fee is paid for the application through the online mode.

***Note:** After taking the appropriate action for the reasons for rejection, again check the status through the official KEAM portal.*

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can a candidate submit the KEAM Application form multiple times?

A candidate shall submit only one application for all the courses in KEAM. Submitting multiple KEAM application forms may lead to the cancellation of the candidature.

### Is there an option to pay the KEAM Application fee offline?

No, the candidates must pay the KEAM Application fee only in online mode.

### What is the KEAM Helpline number to contact for any application-related issue?

The candidates can contact the following Helpline numbers for any further help
0471-2332120, 0471-2338487, 155300, and 0471-2115054 (All Networks).
**Email: [ceekinfo.cee@kerala.gov.in](mailto:ceekinfo.cee@kerala.gov.in)**

### What are the common KEAM application rejection reasons?

Some of the most frequent KEAM application rejection reasons are wrong photo, signature, missing documents, or wrong payment of fees.

### Can I correct a rejected KEAM application?

The KEAM rejected application can be corrected by the candidates as per the instructions of the CEE Kerala KEAM notification.

### How do I re-upload documents for a rejected application?

A certificate, such as proof of Nativity, Date of Birth, and Class 10th certificate, can be re-uploaded through the official KEAM application portal.

### Who should I contact for KEAM application issues?

Get in touch with the KEAM helpdesk through emails or the helpline numbers offered by CEE Kerala.

### Can a rejected application affect my KEAM exam eligibility?

When rectified early, the application that is rejected will not have an influence on your eligibility for KEAM 2026.

### Is there a deadline to fix a rejected KEAM application?

Yes, the applicants have to fix the problems and reapply within the KEAM application deadline announced by CEE Kerala.

### Can submitting multiple applications be KEAM application rejection reasons?

Yes, candidature can be cancelled, and rejection can come in case several KEAM applications are submitted.
