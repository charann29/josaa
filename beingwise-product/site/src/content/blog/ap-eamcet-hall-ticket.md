## How to Download AP EAMCET Admit Card 2025?

The **AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2025** will be released by the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) on 12th May 2025. Below are the steps to be followed to download the Admit Card:

**1.** Go to the AP EAPCET 2025 Official Website, <https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET>

**2.** Find the “Download Hall Ticket” section on the right side of the home page.

**3.** After clicking on the Download Hall Ticket button, the candidates need to enter the following credentials:

- Registration Number (or) Payment Reference ID
- Qualifying Examination Hall Ticket Number
- Date of Birth

**4.** After entering the required details, click on “Proceed”.

**5.** The candidate’s hall ticket will appear on the screen.

**6.** The candidates must take a printout of their Admit Card and preserve it. They need to take the hall ticket along with them during the examination day.

## Key Details to Verify on AP EAPCET Admit Card

The candidates need to make sure to check the following details in their Admit Card:

- Candidate’s Full Name
- Candidate’s Date of Birth
- Candidate’s Father’s Name
- Registration Number of the Candidate
- Photograph and Signature of the Candidate
- Roll Number of the Candidate
- Date and Time of **AP EAPCET 2025 Exam**
- Full address of the Candidate’s Examination Center
- Important Instructions to be followed by the Candidate

## Important Things to carry along with your Admit Card

Along with your AP EAPCET 2025 Admit Card, you need to carry the following things to your examination hall:

**1.** The AP EAPCET 2025 Application Form printout.

**2.** A valid Photo ID proof such as an Aadhar Card, Voter ID, Driving License, or PAN Card.

**3.** Required stationery items like a Pencil, eraser, and ballpoint pen (Blue or Black).

**4.** The candidate’s passport-size photograph (Same as uploaded in the application form).

**5.** Caste Certificate (If Applicable).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **1. What to do in case there is any wrong information printed on the AP EAMCET admit card?**

The candidate needs to review the admit card thoroughly after downloading it. If they find any mistakes in their admit card, contact the AP EAPCET examination authorities immediately to fix their errors.

### **2. What if the candidate forgets to bring the hall ticket for the examination?**

The candidate must bring the AP EAPCET Admit Card to enter the examination hall. If they forget it, they might not be allowed to enter the examination hall. So, make sure to carry your hall ticket before you go to the examination hall.
