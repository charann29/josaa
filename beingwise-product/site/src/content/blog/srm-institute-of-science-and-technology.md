## SRM Institute of Science And Technology B.Tech Admission Criteria

SRM Admissions for B.Tech are conducted strictly based on merit in the institute entrance exam and in-house counselling. SRM Kattankulathur conducts the SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination (SRMJEEE) for B.Tech admissions. Direct admissions are also conducted, where students do not need to appear for the entrance exam under the management quota. With the ongoing Phase 2 SRM admissions applications, candidates need to fulfil the following admission criteria:

| Program | B.Tech Eligibility Criteria | B.Tech Selection Criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| B.Tech | Passed Class 12 exam with 60% aggregate from a recognised board.   Mandatory subjects: Physics & Mathematics.   Optional subjects: Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology / IT / CS / Informatics Practices / Engineering Graphics / Agriculture / Technical Vocational Subject / Business Studies. | SRMJEEE / JEE Main entrance score.   Seats are allotted through Online SRMJEEE Counselling. |

### How To Apply for the SRM Admission Process?

Students applying for SRM Admissions for B.Tech need to appear for the SRMJEEE entrance exam or have a valid JEE Mains score. To apply for the [SRMJEEE](https://collegedost.in/blog/engineering-colleges/srmjeee-exam/), students must follow the given steps:

- Visit the [applications.srmist.edu.in/btech](http://applications.srmist.edu.in/btech)
- Register for SRMJEEE Exam with a valid email ID.
- Verify your email ID and check your inbox.
- Fill the online SRMJEEE application with academic and personal details.
- Pay the application fees of INR 1400 through netbanking/credit card/debit card.
- Upload the required scanned documents, including photos and signature.
- Book the SRMJEEE exam date slot and time online.
- Appear for the online exam from home and secure valid ranks.
- Selected students need to register for online counselling.
- Choose the preferred B.Tech branch and SRM IST campus in the choice filling round.
- The seat allotment list is released based on SRMJEEE ranks, chosen branch, campus, and seat availability.
- Once seats are allotted, students need to report to the allotted SRM campus and verify documents.
- Pay the admission fees to confirm the seats at SRM IST.
