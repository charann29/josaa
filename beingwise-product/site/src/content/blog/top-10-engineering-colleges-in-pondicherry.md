### Entrance Examinations

- **JEE Mains:** JEE Main is a national-level entrance exam for admission into top engineering colleges like NITs, IIITs, and other central institutions. Some colleges in Pondicherry accept admissions based on the JEE Main scores.
- **CENTAC:** CENTAC (Centralised Admission Committee) is a state-level counselling process for admission into engineering, medical, and other professional courses in Puducherry. It offers merit-based admissions to government and private colleges without any separate entrance exam.
- **NAT (Private Entrance Test):** NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test) is an online test conducted by NIAT to assess students’ aptitude and logical skills for entry into its upskilling programs. Based on performance, students are offered admission.

### Admission Process

The admission process for engineering colleges in **Pondicherry** is managed by **CENTAC (Centralised Admission Committee)**, which handles admissions for both government and private institutions. Candidates need to apply online through the CENTAC portal, where selections are based on Class 12 marks or JEE Main scores. The counselling process allows students to choose colleges and courses according to their preferences. Seat allotment is done based on merit, reservation policies, and seat availability. Apart from this, some private institutions also conduct direct or management quota admissions.

- **Government Colleges:** Admissions are purely based on CENTAC merit lists derived from Class 12 or JEE Main scores.
- **Private Colleges:** Admissions are done through CENTAC or via management quota for eligible candidates.
