## MHT CET Admit Card Download: Step-by-Step Process

Follow these simple steps to complete the MHT CET admit card download:

- Visit the official website: cetcell.mahacet.org.
- On the homepage, click on the “MHTCET 2026 Admit Card” link under the Notifications/Downloads section.
- A new login page will open.

- Enter your registered Email ID and Password.
- Click the Submit button.
- The MHT CET hall ticket 2026 will appear on the screen.
- Carefully verify all details, name, roll number, exam date, shift, and exam centre.
- Download and take a colour printout of the admit card.
- Keep it safe until the admission process is complete.

**What should I do if I forgot my password?**

If a candidate has forgotten their login password, they can click on “Forgot Password” or “Reset Password using OTP” on the login page, reset their credentials using a registered mobile number or email, and then log in again to download the hall ticket.

### Documents To Carry on MHT CET 2026 Exam Day

On exam day, every candidate must carry:

- Printed copy of the MHTCET 2026 admit card (colour printout recommended)
- One valid Photo Identity Proof (any one of the following):
  - Aadhaar Card
  - PAN Card
  - Passport
  - School/College Identity Card
  - Voter ID Card
  - Photo identity proof issued by a People’s Representative on official letterhead

**Note:** Entry into the exam hall will be denied without both documents. Students are advised to reach the exam centre at least 30 minutes before the reporting time.

## Items NOT Allowed Inside the MHT CET Exam Hall

The following items are strictly prohibited inside the examination centre:

- Mobile phones, smartwatches, or any electronic devices
- Calculators, books, or any printed material
- Earphones or Bluetooth devices
- Wallets, handbags, or pouches (except transparent ones)
- Any stationery items (pens, pencils, geometry boxes)
- Food items or water bottles (unless medically required)

## What to Do If There Is an Error on the MHT CET Admit Card?

If a candidate notices any discrepancy in their MHT CET hall ticket 2026, such as incorrect name, date of birth, exam date, or photograph, they must:

1. Do not make corrections on the admit card yourself. Any manual changes will render it invalid.
2. Immediately contact the CET Cell helpdesk.
3. Raise the issue through the official website or the helpdesk email/phone number available on cetcell.mahacet.org.
4. Get the issue rectified well before the exam date.

## Last Minute Preparation for MHT CET 2026

With the MHTCET 2026 exam just days away, it is time to shift from studying new topics to consolidating what has already been covered. With 7.6 lakh students competing in the PCM group alone, smart last-minute preparation can make a real difference to the final score. Here are the two most effective strategies to use in the days before the exam.

### 1. Practice MHT CET Mock Test 2026

Practising the [**MHT CET mock test**](/mock-test/mhtcet) series regularly is one of the most effective ways to prepare for the final days.

- **CBT Interface Familiarity**: Get comfortable with the Computer-Based Test layout before exam day, so there are no surprises.
- **Auto-Close Section Awareness**: Physics and Chemistry automatically close after 90 minutes. Mock tests help students practise working within this hard cutoff.
- **Time Management**: Three hours feel very different in a real exam. Timed mock tests build the pacing instinct needed to attempt all questions.
- **Identify Weak Chapters**: Each mock test reveals exactly which topics need last-minute revision.
- **Score Estimation**: Regular mock attempts give a realistic picture of where the candidate stands before the actual exam.

### 2. Revising and Practising MHTC ET PYQ

Solving [**MHT CET PYQ**](/blog/mht-cet/mht-cet-previous-year-question-papers-solutions/) is highly recommended for students in the final days of the exam. Attempt at least 5 years of MHTCET PYQ papers under full exam conditions before the April session. After each attempt, analyse mistakes and immediately revise the weak topics identified.

- **Spot Recurring Topics**: Previous year papers clearly show which chapters appear most frequently and carry the most weight.
- **Understand Question Types**: PYQs give a feel for how questions are framed, which is different from textbook problems.
- **Build Exam Stamina**: Solving MHTCET PYQ under timed, exam-like conditions prepares both mind and pace for the real thing.
- **Free and Accessible**: Past papers are freely available on the official CET Cell website and most coaching platforms.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1. Does MHT CET have negative marking?

No. There is no negative marking in MHT CET 2026. All students should attempt every question.

### Q2. What is the MHT CET exam date for PCM in Session 1?

The MHT CET date for PCM Session 1 is April 11–20, 2026 (no exam on April 12 due to national exam clashes).

### Q3. Can I download the MHT CET hall ticket 2026 without a password?

No. To complete the MHT CET admit card download, candidates must log in using their registered Email ID and Password on the official portal cetcell.mahacet.org. If the password has been forgotten, candidates can reset it using the “Forgot Password” or “Reset Password using OTP” option available on the login page.

### Q4. Will the MHT CET admit card 2026 be sent by post or email?

No. The admit card MHT CET 2026 will not be dispatched by post or email under any circumstances. Candidates must download the MHT CET admit card themselves by logging into the official CET examination portal at cetcell.mahacet.org.

### Q5. What is the difference between City Slip and Final Admit Card?

The city intimation slip only mentions the exam city and session details. The final MHT CET 2026 admit card includes the complete exam centre address, which is required for entry. Students must download the final admit card, not just the city slip.

### Q6. What should I do if my photo is wrong on the MHT CET PCM admit card?

If there is any discrepancy in the MHT CET PCM admit card, including an incorrect photograph, wrong name, or incorrect date of birth, candidates must not make any corrections on the document themselves. They should immediately contact the CET Cell helpdesk through the official website cetcell.mahacet.org and get the error rectified well before the exam date.

### Q7. Why is there no MHTCET exam on April 12, 2026?

The MHTCET date of April 12, 2026, was removed from the PCM Session 1 exam schedule due to a clash with other national-level competitive exams, specifically NDA and NATA. The CET Cell revised the schedule to allow students appearing for these exams to attend without conflict. Candidates should verify their updated exam date on their MHTCET admit card PCM.

### Q8. Will MHTCET 2026 be held in two sessions?

MHTCET 2026 is being conducted in two sessions. Session 1 for PCM will be conducted from April 11 to 20, 2026, and Session 2 will be from May 12 to 16, 2026. A separate MHT CET hall Ticket will be issued for each session.

### Q9. Which score will be used for MHT CET 2026 admissions, Session 1 or Session 2?

The best score obtained across both sessions will be used for the MHT CET 2026 admissions process. This means candidates who appear in both Session 1 and Session 2 will automatically have their higher percentile score considered. The MHT CET 2026 admit card for Session 2 will be released separately closer to the May exam dates.

### Q10. In how many languages is the MHT CET 2026 paper available?

The MHT CET 2026 question paper is available in three languages: English, Marathi, and Urdu. Candidates can select their preferred medium at the time of filling out the application form. The medium selected during registration will be reflected on the MHT CET admit card 2026 and cannot be changed after submission.
