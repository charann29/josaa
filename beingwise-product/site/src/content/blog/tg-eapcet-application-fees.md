JNTUH conducts TG EAPCET for admission to various Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy courses. TG EAPCET application fees are divided into **two categories: General and Reserved Category**. However, candidates can also fill out the form for two programs and need to pay the registration fee separately for each program. The **General or unreserved candidates need to pay the application fee of INR 900,** and the **Reserved candidates (SC/ST/PH) need to pay INR 500** for one program. However, **for two subjects, General and Reserved candidates need to pay INR 1800 and INR 1000,** respectively.

Candidates will be able to fill out the TS EAMCET **application form** from **1st March 2025 till 4th April 2025**. The **admit card** will be available from **19th April 2025**. It is mandatory to bring the admit card to the test centre, otherwise, you will be prohibited from appearing for the exam. The 3-hour CBT-based test has MCQ-type questions. You need to score a minimum of 40 marks out of 160 to pass the exam.

## How to Pay Registration Fee for TG EAPCET Exam 2025?

The registration fee for TG EAPCET is divided into General and Reserved candidates. You can check the steps below to pay the registration fee for the TS EAMCET exam.

- Go to the official website: https://eapcet.tgche.ac.in/
- Candidates need to click on the “Pay Registration Fee” link.

- Next, you will directly go to the Payment Verification page.
- Carefully fill out all the details and double check to ensure their correctness.
- Click on “Proceed to Payment.”.
- Next, the payment is in either NET Banking / Debit Card / Credit Card. Choose your payment method and enter all the required details.
- Finally, complete your payment by clicking on the “PAY” button.
- Upon successful payment, you will get a “Payment Reference ID”.
- Save the ID number and proceed to fill out the application form.

- Then go to the “Online Application” tab and read the user guide manual.
- Pay the Registration fees through NET Banking/Debit/Credit Card.
- Next, proceed to the Application Form.’
- Carefully fill the form and upload all the necessary documents.
- Submit the form.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ******************How to check payment status for TG EAPCET application form?******************

Candidates can check the below steps to check the payment status for TS EAMCET 2025.
Go to the official website of TG EAPCET.
Click “Know Your Payment Status” on the home page.
Next, you need to enter the required details (Qualifying Exam, Hall Ticket Number, Mobile Number, Date of Birth, and Stream).
Click on “Check Payment Status”.
The payment details and the status will be shown to you.

### 2. ****I have a ST certificate, how much registration fee do I need to pay for TG EAPCET 2025 exam?****

All the reserved category students (including candidates having ST certificate) need to pay INR 500 application fees for each subject. In case, if you are opting for two programs then you need to pay INR 1000 as the application fee.

### 3. **********Is the registration fee payment in online or offline mode for TG EAPCET Exam 2025?**********

For the TS EAMCET 2025 exam, the application fee is in online mode. You need to visit the official website to make the payment.

### 4. ******Do I need to pay separate registration fee for Engineering and Agriculture program in TG EAPCET 2025 exam?******

Yes, you need to pay INR 900 for each Engineering and Agriculture program. However, for the reserved category, INR 500 needs to be paid.

### 5. ******What is the TG EAPCET exam?******

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad on behalf of Telangana Council of Higher Education (TGCHE) conducts The Telangana Engineering, Agriculture & Pharmacy Common Entrance Test 2025 (TG EAPCET 2025). TS EAMCET entrance exam is a state level test for admission to Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy programs.

### 7. ********What is TG EAPCET full form?********

The full form of TG EAPCET is Telangana Engineering, Agriculture & Pharmacy Common Entrance Test 2025 and is conducted by JNTUH.

### 8. ******What are the documents required to fill TG EAPCET application form?******

You can check all the documents required to fill out the application form of TG EAPCET 2025.
1. Qualifying Examination Latest 2nd Year Hall Ticket Number/10+2.
2. Personal Email Id.
3. Personal Mobile Number
4. Hall Ticket Number of S.S.C. or Equivalent
5. Date of Birth
6. Caste in case of SC/ST/BC candidates (Caste Certificate Application number for SC/ST only)
7. PH, CAP, NCC, Sports, EWS etc.
8. Income below 1 Lakh or above 1 lakh and below 2 Lakhs or 2 Lakhs and above (Rupees)
9. Study or Residence or relevant certificate for proof of local status (last 12 years)

### 9.****How to get a free seat in TS EAMCET?****

You can get a “free seat” in the TS EAMCET 2025 exam, if you secure a high rank in the exam. Candidates ranking within the top 10,000 ranks, and belonging to a category are eligible for fee reimbursement under the Telangana government’s E-pass scheme. Moreover, the family of candidates having low income and a valid ration card are also eligible for free seats in the TG EAPCET exam.

### 10. **Who are eligible candidates to appear for TG EAPCET 2025 exam?**

Candidates having 10+2 with 45% (40% for the reserved candidates) with PCM for the Engineering programs are eligible for TS EAMCET exam. Moreover, candidates also need to be of Indian Nationality and from the Telangana state to be eligible for the exam.
