## How to Make Corrections in TS EAMCET Application Form 2025:

Here, are some steps to correct TS EAMCET 2025 Application Form:

**Step 1:** First, Go to the official website of ‘TS EAPCET 2025’ or <https://eapcet.tsche.ac.in/>

**Step 2:** Scroll down, and you’ll get the details like **Information, Online Application, and Registration fee.**

**Step 3:** In the Online Application section, Click on the ‘Correction of Online Application Data(Already Filled by Candidate)’. For a better understanding, see the reference image below.

**Step 4:** Now, Enter all the required details like Registration number, Payment reference ID, Qualifying Examination (Latest) Hall Ticket No. , Mobile Number, and Date of Birth.

**Step 5:** Then, Click on ‘**Proceed to Fill Application**’.

**Step 6:** You’ll be redirected to the application form for editing.

**Step 7:** Enter all the details correctly and make the necessary changes. After that, carefully review all the details you have provided in the application form.

**Step 8:** Now, save and submit the application form.

## TS EAMCET/EAPCET Application form Corrections that Can be edited

To make necessary changes in the application form, Candidates must log in to the official website of TS EAMCET 2025. Only certain details can be edited in the form. Candidates who want to make changes can only edit the following:

- Personal Details
- Education Details
- Candidate’s Address
- Can upload Pending documents

## TS EAMCET/EAPCET Application form Correction that can’t be edited

Candidates can’t make some of the changes in the application form. So, it is crucial to ensure that candidates should review all the details before submitting their application form. To modify the details, candidates must write a mail with the application form and supportive documents to the authorities at [helpdeskeamcet@gmail.com](mailto:helpdeskeamcet@gmail.com).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. How can I change my TS EAMCET exam Centre?

No, you cannot change the TS EAMCET exam center. Candidates are advised to select an exam center that is most convenient and near for them.

### 2. Can I change my phone number and email address in the TS EAMCET application?

No, you cannot change your phone number and email address in the TS EAMCET application. Before submitting, candidates should check all the details.
