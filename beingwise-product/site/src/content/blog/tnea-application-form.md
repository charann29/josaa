**TNEA Application registration** process for the Tamil Nadu Engineering Admission **started on May 7th, 2025, and will conclude on June 6th, 2025.** The entire TNEA registration process is conducted online by the Directorate of Technical Education, Tamil Nadu.

Candidates who have completed their 10+2 education are eligible to apply for TNEA. The application form has been made available on the official website, where candidates can apply by visiting <http://tneaonline.org>.

TNEA, which stands for Tamil Nadu Engineering Admission, selection process is based on the marks obtained in subjects such as Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. This admission procedure is open to aspiring students who want to pursue engineering studies in Tamil Nadu. The total marks considered from these subjects is 200 marks. Candidates should note that TNEA does not conduct a state-level entrance exam.

## How to fill out the TNEA Application Form 2025?

Candidates should follow the steps below to fill out the TNEA Application Form 2025:

**Step 1 –** To begin the registration process, candidates should visit the official website at <http://tneaonline.org>

**Step 2 –** Fill in the essential details such as their name, registered mobile number, email ID, date of birth, gender, etc. Upon completing this step, an OTP will be sent to the candidate’s registered mobile number. They must enter this OTP to receive their unique user ID.

**Step 3 –** Upon receiving their user ID and password, candidates must log in to enter details in the application form.

**Step 4 –** In the online application form, candidates must provide personal details, special reservation information, scholarship information, and academic information.

**Step 5 –** Candidates will be shown a preview of the filled application form. After verifying all the mistakes, they must submit it.

**Step 6 –** Candidates are required to pay the application fees online only, using debit/net banking/credit card.

**Step 7 –** After submitting all the details, candidates must take a printout of the application form for further admission processes. This printout should be kept safe until the admission process is complete.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Who can apply for TNEA?

Candidates who have completed their 10+2 education in Tamilnadu are eligible to apply for TNEA.

### 2. When does the TNEA registration start and end?

TNEA registration commenced on May 7, 2025, and will conclude on June 6, 2025.

### 3. How can I register for TNEA?

To register for TNEA, visit the official website at <http://tneaonline.org> and follow the instructions provided on this page.

### 4. How can I pay the application fee for TNEA?

The application fee for TNEA can typically be paid online using methods such as debit card, credit card, or net banking.

### 5. What is the selection criteria for TNEA?

TNEA selection is based on the marks obtained in subjects like Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry in the qualifying examination (10+2). There is no separate entrance exam for TNEA.

### 6. Can I edit my TNEA application after submission?

No, candidates cannot edit their TNEA application after submission. It is crucial to review all details carefully before submitting your final work.

### 7. When will the TNEA counselling process start?

The counselling process for TNEA typically begins after the completion of the application process. Candidates should stay updated with announcements on the official website for counselling dates and procedures.
