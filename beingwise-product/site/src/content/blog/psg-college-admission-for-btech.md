## How to Apply For PSG College Admission For B.Tech?

To apply for B.Tech admissions at [PSG College Coimbatore](http://psgtech.edu), students go through the centralised Tamil Nadu Engineering Admission (TNEA) process and single window counselling managed by the Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE), Chennai. As it does not require filling out any PSG College of Technology application form 2026, students need to apply through the TNEA official website. The selection of students depends entirely on TNEA counselling performance. Check the following PSG College online application​ process and PSG College admission process for B.Tech:

- Visit the TNEA Official website and click on the New Registration link.
- Complete registration with the required details and generate the TNEA Login credentials.
- Fill out the online TNEA application form before the deadline and upload the required documents.
- Pay the application fees and wait for the TNEA rank list to be published. The TNEA Rank list is prepared based on the Class 12 PCM Marks. It is normalised to a scale of 200 to ensure equality and fairness among all the boards.
- Once the TNEA rank list is prepared and released, selected students need to appear for the online TNEA Choice Filling round.
- Select the preferred branch and PSG College Coimbatore in the choice filling round. Remember to select PSG College counselling code​.
- Seat allotment is conducted post-choice filling round based on TNEA rank, preference, category and availability.
- Once the seats are allotted, students need to accept the allotment order and accept or decline the seat.
- If accepted, students need to report to the PSGTech ECampus or the allotted college to verify the documents and pay the tuition fees to confirm admission.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What are the B.Tech scholarships provided for PSG College admission?

PSG College offers various scholarships including State and Central Government scholarships, private NGO scholarships, alumni scholarships, and the GRD Centenary Scholarship. These are generally awarded based on merit and financial background.

### 2. How are the placement statistics at PSG College Coimbatore?

As per recent placement data, the highest package offered is around INR 52 LPA. The average package is approximately INR 11 LPA, while the median package stands at INR 7.5 LPA, with over 1200 students placed.

### 3. What documents are required for PSG College admission?

Required documents include 10th and 12th mark sheets, school study certificates, community/caste certificate, and proof of family income.

### 4. What is the admission process for NRI candidates at PSG College?

NRI admissions are handled under a separate category for Foreign Nationals, PIO, OCI, and Gulf workers’ children. These candidates can apply directly through the college website without going through TNEA counselling.

### 5. Can students get admission through the management quota?

Yes, management quota admissions are available, but students must meet eligibility criteria. These seats typically have higher fees compared to regular admissions.

### 6. What are the rankings and accreditations of PSG College?

PSG College is ranked 67th in the engineering category by NIRF. It holds NAAC Grade A accreditation and is approved by AICTE.

### 7. How are the campus facilities and infrastructure at PSG College?

Students report that PSG College offers excellent campus facilities, infrastructure, and amenities. The overall experience, including academics and campus life, is considered positive and better compared to many other institutions.
