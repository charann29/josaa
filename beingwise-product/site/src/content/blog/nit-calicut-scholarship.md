NIT Calicut has a range of financial aids to help the BTech students to pursue their academic activities under the NIT Calicut Scholarship. Scholarships for NIT Calicut focus on helping not only the talented but also the economically disadvantaged students to do engineering without the worries of financial constraints as part of the NIT Calicut Scholarship 2026.

Eligible candidates can benefit from various programs that include the Merit cum Means, Central sector scholarships and tuition fee waivers available through the NIT Calicut Scholarship 2026 program, based on the NIT Calicut scholarship selection criteria. The amount of the scholarship at NIT Calicut could either take the form of monthly allowances ranging from 3000 to 10,000 or total fee waivers, depending on the eligibility status of the candidate, reflecting the NIT Calicut Scholarship Amount.

## NIT Calicut Scholarship Amount 2026

The NIT Calicut Scholarship Amount 2026 varies depending on the scheme offered to BTech students, including merit-based and need-based financial aid. Students can receive benefits ranging from monthly stipends to full or partial tuition fee waivers, helping reduce the overall cost of engineering education at NIT Calicut. | **Scholarship** | **Amount** |
| --- | --- |

## NIT Calicut Scholarship Eligibility

Students will be required to meet the minimum eligibility criteria to avail the NIT Calicut scholarship 2026. The following table contains the complete NIT Calicut scholarship eligibility requirements.

| **Scholarship** | **Key Requirements** |
| --- | --- |
| NIT Calicut Merit-Cum-Means Scholarship | Minimum 50% marks in Class 12, no backlogs |
| Central Sector Top Class Scholarship Scheme | Family income below INR 600,000, top 20% meritorious students in that academic year |
| E-Grantz | Income limits vary by category, full-time enrolment |
| Tuition Fee Waiver | Valid category certificate, any income level |
| Engineers India Limited Scholarships | Full-time BTech, income under INR 300,000 |
| NITC Aspire Scholarship | Family income below INR 500,000, good academic record |
| SILA Scholarship | Merit-based, specific to the department |

## NIT Calicut Scholarship Application Process 2026

The scholarship application process at the National Institute of Technology Calicut for the 2026 academic session begins after students complete their admission formalities.

**Step 1: Check Eligibility**

The eligibility requirements of each scholarship scheme should be thoroughly reviewed by students on the official NIT Calicut site or the National Scholarship Portal. All the academic, income and category requirements should be satisfied to continue with the application.

**Step 2: Gather Documents**

It is required that the applicants be ready with all the required documents. These usually consist of income certificates, caste/category certificates (where applicable), academic mark sheets, and other supporting documents to be verified.

**Step 3: Online Registration**

Students must apply at the right site; either the National Scholarship Portal or the official scholarship site of the institute. It is a process of registering a login id and password via the portal like scholarships.gov.in or nitc.ac.in.

**Step 4: Fill Application Form**

Qualified students are supposed to complete the application form with the correct personal, academic and financial information. Rejection of the application can be a result of any incorrect or incomplete information.

**Step 5: Upload Supporting Files**

The candidates must post scanned versions of all the documents within the required format and size. These are the necessary documents to verify and approve the scholarship.

**Step 6: Print and submit.**

Upon submitting all the information, the students are expected to fill in the application form online. It is recommended to save and print a copy of the form submitted to use it in future and keep a record.

## Continuance of NIT Calicut Scholarship Award

The continuance of the scholarship will be in accordance with the authorities’ instructions. Refer to the following to learn about the same.

- Scholarships granted from year 2 onwards are dependent upon satisfactory academic performance and conduct according to the NIT Calicut Scholarship rules.
- The scholarship will continue to be paid every year until the completion of studies, according to the NIT Calicut Scholarship 2026.
- Renewal of scholarship is contingent upon promotion to the subsequent semester/session/year.
- Students who fail in any subject or score less than 55% aggregate marks (equivalent CGPA/CPI) will not get the scholarship benefit, which may impact the NIT Calicut Scholarship Amount.
- The scholarship will be resumed only if the student completes all backlog papers by securing 55% or equivalent CGPA/CPI marks.
- In order to renew, the student is required to submit a Progress Report within 30 days of announcing results, as per the NIT Calicut Scholarship eligibility.
- The progress report should be authorised and recommended by the Head of the Institution.
- Failure to complete the course within the stipulated time may result in the discontinuation of the scholarship according to the NIT Calicut Scholarship.
- Participation in any misconduct activity might cause disqualification based on the NIT Calicut scholarship selection criteria.
- Concluding decisions on continuance or withdrawal depend on the provisions set out in the NIT Scholarship 2026 rules.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### ******Q1. Who can apply for NIT Calicut scholarships?******

BTech students who qualify on a merit basis, financial need and categories are entitled to avail themselves of scholarships under the NIT Calicut Scholarship policy. There are specific eligibility criteria for all schemes.

### **Q2. Is there any income limit for scholarships at NIT Calicut?**

Yes, many scholarships have fixed income requirements for applicants to get financial assistance. These requirements will depend upon the scholarship schemes and the NIT Calicut Scholarship eligibility.

### Q3. Are scholarships available for all BTech branches?

Yes, there are scholarships available for different BTech branches and students. But, it also depends on the terms of particular scholarships under the NIT Calicut Scholarship 2026.

### Q4. Can a student apply for multiple scholarships at NIT Calicut?

Multiple scholarships can be availed by students, provided they satisfy the eligibility criteria. The number of scholarships that can be availed together may depend on regulations.

### Q5. When does the scholarship application process usually begin?

The process to apply for scholarships starts after admission, which normally takes place in the month of July.

### Q6. Is it mandatory to apply through the National Scholarship Portal?

Students need to apply for most scholarships on the national scholarship portal only. Other scholarships have different application processes.

### Q7. What happens if a student fails in a semester?

In case of failure in any BTech semester, the scholarship may be suspended until the backlog is cleared with specific marks, which can affect the NIT Calicut Scholarship Amount.

### Q8. Are scholarships renewed automatically every year?

No, scholarships are not automatically renewed for students. There are certain criteria to be met before renewal of scholarships under the NIT Calicut Scholarship.

### Q9. Do scholarships cover full tuition fees at NIT Calicut?

Many scholarships are completely tuition-free, while others are partially free. There are other scholarships with monthly stipends as well under the NIT Calicut Scholarship 2026.

### Q10. Is maintaining a minimum CGPA necessary for continuation?

Yes, it is necessary to maintain a minimum CGPA for continuing scholarships, as per the NIT Calicut scholarship selection criteria.
