## What is SRMJEEE?

The SRMJEEE is a popular university-level entrance test conducted for admission into engineering programs offered by SRM Institute of Science and Technology. SRMJEEE stands for SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination.

The purpose of taking this test is to gauge the performance of a candidate in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English. The SRMJEEE exam is held in several phases throughout the year, thereby allowing candidates to take their test according to their convenience. Candidates must be updated about the SRMJEEE dates for preparation purposes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1.What is SRMJEEE exam?

SRMJEEE is an entrance test that is administered to secure a place in SRM Institute of Science and Technology. It tests candidates in English, Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in the SRMJEEE exam.

### 2.How to prepare for SRMJEEE?

The SRMJEEE preparation syllabus, which includes taking practice tests, solving SRMJEEE sample papers, and understanding the syllabus, is all part of the preparation. It is also significant to revise and manage time regularly in SRMJEEE preparation.

### 3.Does SRMJEEE have negative marking?

No, SRMJEEE is not negatively marked. The applicants are advised to give all questions a go without being punished as a result of misinterpreting questions in SRMJEEE.

### 4.What is a good score in SRMJEEE?

A high score in SRMJEEE varies according to the branch, with a better ranking required in competitive courses such as CSE. A good score, however, enhances preferred admission in the SRMJEEE cutoff.

### 5.How to check the SRMJEEE admit card?

After slot booking with the help of the login credentials, the SRMJEEE admit card can be downloaded via the official portal. It should be brought on the exam day for the Admit card for SRMJEEE.

### 6.What is SRMJEEE slot booking?

SRMJEEE slot booking is the procedure of choosing the exam date and time. It is conducted online in the SRMJEEE slot booking portal on a first-come, first-served basis in the slot booking process.

### 7.What are the SRMJEEE eligibility criteria?

SRMJEEE eligibility demands that a candidate must have completed or be taking Class 12 with Physics, Mathematics plus one more science subject under the eligibility criteria.

### 8.How is SRMJEEE counselling done?

SRMJEEE counselling is done online on the basis of rank. Students choose their preferences, and seats are assigned based on the same.

### 9. What is the SRMJEEE cutoff rank?

SRMJEEE cutoff rank is the lowest rank needed to get admission to a particular course. It depends on the branch and campus in the SRMJEEE cutoff rank.

### 10. Is SRMJEEE easy?

The level of SRMJEEE is based on the level of preparation. It can be handled with expertise in the SRMJEEE pattern and the practice of the SRM question paper pattern.
