## Eligibility Criteria for B.Tech CSE(Data Science)

Candidates aspiring for admission must satisfy certain eligibility criteria to be eligible to secure admission into the four-year B.Tech CSE(Data Science) course. Check the eligibility criteria below:

- Candidate must have passed with a minimum aggregate of 40%(for all other categories) and 45%(for General category)
