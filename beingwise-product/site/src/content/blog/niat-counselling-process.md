NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies has collaborated with 20+ Universities to provide upskilling programs. To get industry-ready and learn the skills, candidates need to take the Nxtwave Assessment Test NAT for 2026 and appear in a 1:1 counselling session conducted by NIAT. The counselling session will help students choose the right university that offers the upskilling program.

Following the NAT exam, students need to appear for the NIAT counselling process for a better understanding of the industry-ready skills that they are going to be trained in, the types of mentorship they will get, and project-based learning.

After the declaration of NAT results, soon after the exam, both the students and parents can book a campus visit to the collaborating Universities free of cost from the official website. They need to give a preferred date and time for the campus visit.

On the visit, both the students and parents will attend the final step of a one-on-one counselling session conducted by NIAT. The session will evaluate students’ cognitive abilities, including shared passions, goals, and dreams. The parents will assess the learning environment, the security of their child, and the upskilling program structure through the counselling session. This information helps NIAT understand you better and thus helps you navigate the program smoothly. Admission decisions will be made by the respective Universities based on your performance in the NIAT counselling session.

## What is NIAT 1:1 Counselling 2026?

After qualifying for **NAT**, students will have to attend a **one-on-one counselling session**. There will be multiple counselling sessions. Students and Parents will have separate sessions, along with the students having counselling sessions with parents. These sessions will help NIAT in understanding the goals and the vision of the students and parents. It will help them in knowing their expectations and thus discussing how it aligns with the **NIAT NxtWave’s Skill Map.** The students and parents will be evaluated under various factors in the NIAT counselling process 2026; some of the pointers are given below.

## What Are the Points on Which Students Will Be Evaluated?

Check some of the points on which students will be evaluated during the NIAT 1:1 counselling session.

**Their short-term goals:** During the NIAT Counselling Process 2026, the students will be asked about their short-term goals, such as the projects they wish to undertake and the topics they want to explore. For example, why are you interested in this upskilling program? What do you hope to achieve with the program?

**Long-term goals:** Students must prepare and reflect on their post-program plans. The counselling session will help them understand their long-term goals that align with their skills and experiences. For example, do you want to be an entrepreneur? What top tech careers excite you the most?

**Dream Job and Company:** The counsellor will ask you about your Dream Company and why you want to join the company. This answer will give a clear picture of your goal in life and the type of company and work culture you are looking for.

**Hobbies and Other Activities:** The counselling session will help in understanding the hobbies of students and what clubs they want to join. This will help them develop soft skills like being a team player, leadership skills, etc, among the students.

**Alignment with NIAT Culture:** Finally, the session will discuss the **program and the culture of NIAT**. What the Institute is offering and how it aligns with their career goal.

**Students will undergo an Expectation analysis during the NIAT Counselling Process 2026**: Their goals, the skills they have, and how they can get better. What type of co-curricular activities do they prefer? What Clubs do they want to join?

## What Are the Points to Be Evaluated by Parents?

Parents of NIATians can also have their queries answered and be clear on the vision and future of their children during the NIAT 1:1 Counselling session.

**The Program Expectation:** The sessions for the NIAT counselling process 2026 provide an opportunity to share the parents’ expectations and evaluate how [**NIAT’s Skill Map**](/blog/engineering-colleges/niat-comprehensive-curriculum/) aligns with their child’s educational needs. This conversation will help NIAT in understanding the support a student will need in fulfilling their upskilling journey

**The Safety of Students:** The student’s safety is of the utmost importance. The counselling session will give the parents an insight into the comprehensive security measures on the NIAT Collaborative University campus, including 90 surveillance cameras designed to maintain a 24/7 secure learning environment.

**Alignment with NIAT.** Finally, the session will also help parents assess whether **NIAT’s vision** and educational approach align with their long-term goals for their child. A shared understanding among students, parents, and NIAT ensures a smooth academic experience.

## Conclusion

The NIAT 1:1 counselling process is a thoughtful and structured step that follows the NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT). It is designed to help NIAT understand each student’s goals, aspirations, and expectations and to help students and parents understand what NIAT’s upskilling program has to offer. The 1:1 counselling sessions, conducted separately for students and parents, ensure that every individual’s concerns and ambitions are addressed clearly. Students are evaluated on their short-term and long-term goals, hobbies, dream careers, and alignment with NIAT’s skill-focused approach.

Parents, on the other hand, get a clear picture of the upskilling program structure, safety measures at the collaborating university’s campus, and how the program aligns with their child’s future. It is important to understand that admission to any collaborating university is decided solely by that university based on its own eligibility criteria. NxtWave acts only as a facilitator in this process, helping connect interested students with the right collaborating university, with the student’s written consent.

## Frequently Asked Questions

#### 1. When did the NIAT start?

NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies (NIAT) was established in the year 2023. NIAT offers upskilling programs by collaborating with various Universities across India.

#### 2. Is the NIAT 1:1 counselling session the same as a university admission interview?

No. The NIAT counselling session is conducted by NxtWave and is focused on understanding the student’s goals, expectations, and alignment with NIAT’s upskilling program. It is entirely separate from any admission interview or selection process conducted independently by the collaborating university.

#### 3. How to apply for the NIAT upskilling programs offered by various collaborating Universities?

To apply for the upskilling programs offered by various NIAT-collaborating universities, students must qualify for NAT. Register for the test by paying the non-refundable INR 1200 registration fee. However, admission to the partner University will be in accordance with that University’s rules and guidelines.

#### 4. What are the fees of NIAT?

The NIAT collaborating universities offer B.Tech degree programs with fees that vary based on the institution. The 1st-year fee ranges from INR 2 Lakhs to INR 4.5 Lakhs, and the total 4-year fee ranges from INR 8 Lakhs to INR 18 Lakhs.

#### 5. What is NAT?

Nxtwave Assessment Test is designed to evaluate how you think, solve problems, and adapt. The students are not required to memorise anything. After qualifying for the exam, students need to appear for the NIAT counselling process 2026 to understand the skill map to get industry-ready better.

#### 6. Is the NIAT 1:1 counselling session the same as a university admission interview?

No. The NIAT counselling session is conducted by NxtWave and is focused on understanding the student’s goals, expectations, and alignment with NIAT’s upskilling program. It is entirely separate from any admission interview or selection process conducted independently by the collaborating university.

#### 7. Do both students and parents need to attend the counselling session?

Yes. The NIAT counselling process includes separate sessions for students and parents, as well as a joint session. The student’s session focuses on goals, interests, and career aspirations, while the parents’ session covers program expectations, safety at the collaborating university’s campus, and alignment with their student’s long-term vision.

#### 8. Does completing the NIAT 1:1 counselling process mean I am enrolled in the upskilling program?

NIAT counselling session is part of the overall process. Enrollment into NIAT’s upskilling program and admission to the collaborating university are two separate steps, each governed by its respective processes and terms.

#### 9. What happens after the NIAT 1:1 counselling session is completed?

After the NIAT counselling session, admission decisions are made independently by the respective collaborating university based on the student’s performance and their own eligibility criteria. NxtWave facilitates the connection but does not make the university’s admission decision.

#### 10. Will the NIAT 1:1 counselling session determine which university I get admitted to?

Not entirely. The counselling session helps NIAT understand your goals and helps match you with a suitable collaborating university. However, the final admission decision rests solely with that university, governed by its own admission policy and eligibility requirements.
