## TG-EAPCET 2025 List of Examination Centres

The Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH) has announced that the [TG-EAPCET examination](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/) will be conducted in different test centres across different test zones. Here is the detailed list of **TG-EAPCET** examination centres:

### Important Facts Related TS EAMCET 2025 Exam Centres

- TS EAMCET 2025 will be held in 23 Test Zones, including 6 in Hyderabad, 12 in the rest of Telangana, and 5 in Andhra Pradesh.
- During the **TS EAMCET 2025** Application Form submission, candidates can choose their preferred test zone. Once selected, requests for changes will not be entertained.
- The Convener reserves the right to add or remove test zones as needed.
- The Convener also reserves the right to allot candidates to a test zone different from the one they chose due to technical reasons.
- Candidates are not allowed to submit more than one application for the same category (‘E’, ‘AM’, or ‘E & AM’). If multiple applications are submitted, the Convener may either accept one application or reject all submissions.

JNTUH releases the **TG-EAPCET** exam centres list on its official website. Candidates can correct their preferred city zone by visiting the official website. Only those candidates who have completed their application process are eligible to choose their preferred exam centres.

## TS EAMCET 2025 Exam Center Instructions

Candidates are advised to adhere to the [Instructions](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/tg-eapcet-examday-guidelines/) for a fair and smooth examination process:

- **Material to bring on the exam day:** Hall Ticket, filled Online Application Form with a recent passport-size photo, and left-hand thumb impression (done in advance, at least a day before). Submit the signed form to the invigilator.
- **Other Important Instructions:**
  - Trying to tamper with the hall ticket might lead to disqualification.
  - Candidates have to arrive at the exam centre atleast 90 minutes before the exam.
  - Candidates are not allowed inside the examination hall after the commencement of exam.
  - **Candidates must bring the following to the exam hall:** i) Hall Ticket
    ii) Ball Point Pen (Blue/Black)
    iii) Filled Online Application Form with photo and thumb impression
    iv) Attested copy of Caste certificate
  - Candidates should read the instructions before answering the questions.
  - Candidates should not leave the examination until the invigilator advises.

### Prohibited Items at TG EAMCET Exam Centres 2025

## TG-EAPCET 2025 Exam Pattern

Telangana Engineering Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test is conducted in online mode. 160 marks are divided across three sections i.e. Mathematics-80 marks, Chemistry-40 marks and Physics-40 marks. Here is an overview of the exam pattern:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Section** | **Details** |
| Mode of Examination | Computer Based Test |
| Duration of TG-EAPCET exam | 3 hours i.e. 180 minutes |
| Medium of the examination | Telugu, English, and Urdu |
| Sections | Mathematics: 80 Marks Physics: 40 Marks Chemistry: 40 Marks |
| No of Questions | 160 Questions |
| Types of Questions Asked | Multiple Choice Questions |
| Marking Scheme | +1 Mark for every correct answer. No Negative marking |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ********What is the exam date for TG-EAPCET 2025?********

TG-EAPCET exam date for engineering will be from May 2nd to May 5th, 2025.

### 2. ********Will my IPE marks considered for TG-EAPCET 2025?********

No, there is no such criteria for considering IPE marks for TG-EAPCET.

### 3. ********What is the TG-EAPCET exam?********

TG-EAPCET exam is an entrance examination for admission into undergraduate courses like engineering, agriculture, and pharmacy.

### 4. ******Is TG-EAPCET and TS-EAMCET same?******

TS-EAMCET is renamed as TG-EAPCET but they both are the same exams.

### 5. ********What is the age limit to appear for TG-EAPCET?********

Although there is no upper age limit to appear for TG-EAPCET, the minimum age limit to appear for the exam is 16 years.

### 6. ****How many times can I attempt TG-EAPCET?****

Authorities have not set any limits on the number of attempts, and candidates can appear for the exam any number of times.

### 7. ****Is there any negative marking in TG-EAPCET?****

No, there is no negative marking in the TG-EAPCET 2025 exam.
