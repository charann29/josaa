## KEAM Exam Analysis 2025: April 25th Shift Students Review

Here are student reviews for the April 25th KEAM PCM exam:

**Annmariya (from Malappuram):** *“I felt the exam was easy. Physics and Chemistry were comparatively okay, but I personally found Maths a bit difficult. The pattern of questions in Physics and Chemistry was similar to what I had practised, so they felt familiar and not tough.”*

**Sarangi (from Wayanad):** *“The exam was sufficiently easy. The question models were similar to those I saw in the live sessions, so they felt very familiar. Chemistry was okay, with basic, NCERT-based questions and theory everywhere.”*

**Ananya (from Thrissur):** *“The exam was sufficiently easy — actually, quite easy. The questions were simpler than those in the mock exams and question series I practised at Brilliant. I noticed there were fewer questions from Organic Chemistry.”*

**Shivapriya (from Kannur):** *“I found Physics and Chemistry to be sufficiently simple. The exam was much easier compared to the ones we usually wrote at Brilliant. Physics questions were mostly theory-based with very few calculations — if the concepts were clear, they were simple to answer.”*

**Sreelakshmi (from Palakkad):** *“The exam was easy overall. Some Physics questions required more thought and were more concept-oriented. In Chemistry, too, a few questions went a bit deeper than usual.”*

## KEAM Examday Guidelines 2025

Officials have released some exam day guidelines, and students should strictly adhere to them for a smooth examination process. Check the exam day guidelines below:

- Candidates appearing for the exam have to submit a valid ID proof along with the admit card.

- Arrive early at the examination centre.

- Report in advance to the examination centre to complete all the security checks and enter the examination hall.

- Occupy the designated positions and don’t try to change your seats as it might lead to disqualification and severe disciplinary actions.

- Don’t engage in any unfair means in the examination hall.

- Don’t carry any electronic gadgets like mobile phones, smart watches, etc.

- Write your name and roll number on the rough sheets provided in the examination hall and submit them before leaving.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****What are the KEAM exam dates for Pharmacy?****

The KEAM Pharmacy exam will be held on April 24th and April 29th, 2025.

### **2. ********How many shifts are held in one day for the KEAM exam?**********

Except on April 24th, the KEAM exam will be held only in one shift on the other dates.

### **3. **When will the officials release KEAM results?****

KEAM results are announced on 14th May 2025.

### **4. ********What are the prohibited items inside the examination hall?**********

Candidates are not allowed to carry electronic gadgets to the examination hall.

### **5. ****Is there any negative marking in KEAM?******

Yes, one mark will be deducted for every incorrect answer, and four marks will be earned for every correct answer.
