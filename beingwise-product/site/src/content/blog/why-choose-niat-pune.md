## How NIAT Training Centre in Pune Get Its Recognition and Accreditation?

NIAT Training Centre in Pune is an upskilling program and does not independently hold AICTE, UGC, or NAAC accreditations, as these apply exclusively to universities and academic institutions. Instead, NIAT in Pune derives its credibility through its strong upskilling collaborations with Alard University, which holds accreditations such as NAAC, UGC recognition, and AICTE approval.

Since NIAT conducts its upskilling programs within the spaces and ecosystem provided by these collaborating universities, students benefit indirectly from the universities’ accreditation standing, earning a UGC-recognised and AICTE-approved B.Tech degree from the collaborating university, while also receiving an IRC from NxtWave upon successful completion of the upskilling program.
