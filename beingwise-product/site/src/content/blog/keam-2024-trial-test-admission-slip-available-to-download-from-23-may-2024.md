KEAM 2024 Trial Test: The KEAM 2024 trial test admission slip is now available. The candidates can download the trial test admission slip starting from **23-05-2024, 11 AM**. The candidates who have submitted their willingness through the application for KEAM 2024 to participate in the trial test can attend the test on **25.05.2024 from 10.00 AM to 1.00 PM.** The trial test will be conducted in Kerala only.

The Computer-Based (CBT) Engineering and Pharmacy Entrance Examination for admission to B.Tech and B.Pharm courses will be conducted from **05.06.2024 to 09.06.2024** at selected centers in the state, Delhi, Mumbai, and Dubai.

## How to download the KEAM 2024 trial test admission slip?

Here are the steps to be followed to download the trial test admission slip:

Step 1: Visit the KEAM official website, [www.cee.kerala.gov.in.](http://www.cee.kerala.gov.in)

Step 2: Log in to the Candidate’s login portal using Login ID and Password.

Step 3: Find the “**KEAM 2024 Trial Test**” link. The candidates can download the trial test admission slip by clicking the menu.

## Important Instructions

Below are the important instructions to remember:

- The selection of the candidates for the trial test is strictly on a first come, first served basis, in the application of KEAM 2024.
- The applicant should report for the trial test at the allotted examination center at the time given in the admission slip.
- The trial test is conducted on the same pattern as the actual examination. So, the candidates must strictly follow the guidelines given in connection with the KEAM examination.
- No trial test will be conducted in Delhi, Mumbai, and Dubai centres.
- In case of any help, the candidates can contact the Helpline Number, 0471- 2525300.
