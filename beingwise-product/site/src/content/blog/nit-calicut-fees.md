## How to Pay NIT Calicut Fees?

Students can follow the steps below to submit the NIT Calicut BTech fees:

- Visit the institute’s official website and navigate to the NIT Calicut fees 2026 payment portal
- Enter your student credentials, i.e., ID and password, and click on the submit button for NIT Calicut fees access
- Select the semester and type of fees, i.e., tuition fees, hostel fees, etc., as per the NIT Calicut fee structure
- Carefully check your fees detail and amount before proceeding further under fees in NIT Calicut
- Select your preferred mode of payment, i.e., net banking, debit card, credit card, UPI, etc., for fees at NIT Calicut
- Finish your transaction through the online payment gateway for the NIT Calicut Fee Structure for btech
- Save your receipt for future use as proof of NIT Calicut courses and fees payment
- In case of hostel admission, hostel fees and mess fees will be separate, if applicable, under NIT Calicut hostel fees.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **1. What is the mode of paying NIT Calicut fees?**

Students can also make the payment using online modes such as net banking, UPI, debit cards, or credit cards for NIT Calicut fees 2026.

### **2. Are NIT Calicut fees the same for all B.Tech branches?**

Yes, the NIT Calicut fees are almost the same for all B.Tech courses. There might be some small variations depending on the category or additional charges.

### **3. Is the hostel fee included in the NIT Calicut fees?**

No, the hostel fees are generally different from the academic fees under the NIT Calicut fee structure. Students have to make an additional payment for the hostel fees.

### **4. Can students pay NIT Calicut fees in instalments?**

Usually, the payment is done semester-wise rather than in full instalments for fees in NIT Calicut. Students must adhere to the payment schedule.

### **5. Is there any fee waiver available at NIT Calicut?**

Yes, some categories of students, such as SC/ST and economically weaker sections, are eligible to get some waiver on the fees at NIT Calicut.

### **6. What happens if I miss the fee payment deadline?**

If the deadline is missed, some penalties or late fees might be imposed in the NIT Calicut Fee Structure for btech. Students might also be restricted from attending classes.

### **7. Are one-time fees refundable at NIT Calicut?**

Some one-time payment charges, such as the caution deposit, might be refundable under NIT Calicut courses and fees. However, other charges are non-refundable.

### **8. Do fees change every year at NIT Calicut?**

Yes, some changes are made to the fees each year in NIT Calicut mtech fees. Students must stay updated with the latest announcements.

### **9. Is the mess fee included in hostel charges?**

No, mess charges are different from hostel seat rent under the NIT Calicut hostel fees. Students have to make an additional payment for mess charges depending on their usage.

### **10. How can I confirm successful fee payment?**

Students can also confirm the payment using the receipt generated after the transaction for the NIT Calicut fees 2026. It is recommended to download the receipt for future reference.
