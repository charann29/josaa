## What is JEE Main Answer Key Challenge Window?

The JEE Main answer key challenge enables students to challenge provisional answers by entering into the NTA portal (jeemain.nta.ac.in), selecting questions, and paying a non-refundable cost of ₹200 per question. Follow the steps below to challenge the tentative JEE Main answer key 2026.

1. Visit the official website at jeemain.nta.nic.in.
2. Click the link labelled as ‘Challenges Regarding JEE Mains Answer Key 2026’.
3. Candidates must log in using their application number and password.
4. The JEE Main answer key must include the question ID and the ID with the correct option for both examinations 1 and 2.
5. In the event of an error, candidates may challenge the question by selecting one or more IDs.
6. Now, select ‘Save your claim’ and then Next.
7. The question ID so challenged will display on the screen.
8. Candidates must provide the essential papers to substantiate their claim.
9. They must upload the document in pdf format and then click the ‘Choose file’ option on the screen.
10. After objecting to a question, candidates must pay a processing charge of Rs 200.
11. It should be noted that the payment can be made via credit card, debit card, or net banking.

## NTA Guidelines on Dress Code for JEE Mains

The National Testing Agency has issued special guidelines on the JEE Mains 2026 dress code guidelines for the upcoming shifts, which are as follows
