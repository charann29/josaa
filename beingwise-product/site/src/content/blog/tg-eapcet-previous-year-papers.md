## TS-EAMCET Exam Overview 2025

The TG-EAPCET exam is conducted for a total of 160 marks, distributed across three subjects: 80 marks for Mathematics and 40 marks each for Physics and Chemistry. The exam duration is 3 hours (180 minutes), and there is no negative marking.
