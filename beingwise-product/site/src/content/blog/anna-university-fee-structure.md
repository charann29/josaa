The Anna University Fee structure for B.Tech offers structure, balance, and affordability along with quality engineering education to B.Tech aspirants.  Tamil Nadu Students and students from other states can apply for the 4-year B.Tech courses available at INR 1.38 Lakhs across its 4 main campuses of CEG, MIT, and ACTECH campus in Chennai. There are regular and self-supporting programs across specialisations like Information Technology, Chemical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and others. Admission to these programs is primarily based on merit and TNEA counselling.

The Anna University Chennai Fee structure for B.Tech includes One-time Admission Fees, a caution deposit, and semester-wise tuition fees. Other miscellaneous expenses, like semester exam fees and hostel fees, are charged separately. Although Scholarships and government financial aid help make the Anna University Fee structure more accessible. ## Anna University Fee Structure For B.Tech Campus-Wise

The Anna University Chennai Fee structure for B.Tech is mainly standardised and uniform across its 4 main Chennai Campuses. For the 2026 academic year, the Anna University b tech fees for regular programs or self-supporting programs have minor variations. Core Campuses like College of Engineering Guindy, Madras Institute of Technology, and Alagappa College of Technology charge around INR 32,000 to INR 35,500 for general category Tamil Nadu and Other state candidates. Admitted through TNEA counselling, B.Tech students need to pay INR 1.38 Lakhs for 4-year B.Tech programs.

The Anna University Chennai Fee structure across its main campuses follows the uniform one-time admission fee, caution deposit, and semester fee. Other additional expenses like hostel fee, travel expenses, counselling fee, and Anna University exam fees vary a bit. Although the First graduate fee structure for Tamil Nadu candidates is a bit lower than the general category ones. ## Anna University Hostel Fees For Engineering Students

Anna University hostel fees​ for B.Tech students at the ACTECH and CEG Campus are INR 42, 625 for the 2026 academic year. It covers one-time fees, initial semester fees, and yearly amenities charges. Room rent, electricity, water, maintenance and amenities, with separate mess charges, are under this total Anna University hostel fees​ as well. For both Male and female students, the hostel fees are similar. Check the following table for Anna University hostel fees​ for B.Tech students:

| **Criteria** | **Fees** |

| Payable Every Year | INR 3675 |

| Total Anna University Hostel Fees | INR 42,625 |

Anna University houses a placement cell that handles the top recruiters, placement drives, and pre-placement training. Yes, Scholarships and financial aids like merit-cum-means scholarships are offered on the Anna University Fee structure. Schemes like the Temenos Scholarship and Renault Nissan Scholarship offer INR 30,000 per year to B.Tech students.

### **3. Does the Anna University Chennai fee structure vary from one campus to another?**

Anna University Fees do not vary much from one campus to another. It’s mostly uniform and across its 4 main campuses for Anna University.

### **4. What are the additional fees required, except for the Anna University Fee structure?**

The additional charges required are one-time admission fees, Anna University counselling fees​, exam fees, caution deposit, and other personal expenses that need to be considered even after paying the Anna University Fees.

### **5. What is the Anna University fee structure with hostel​ fees for Engineering students**

The total Anna University fee structure for B.Tech is INR 1.38 Lakhs, and the total hostel fee is INR 42,625 across its 4 main campuses.
