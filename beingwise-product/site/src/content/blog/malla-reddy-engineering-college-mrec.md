Malla Reddy Engineering College (MREC), Telangana, is a prominent autonomous institution located in Hyderabad. Founded in 2004, it is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH). The college has earned NAAC’s A++ grade accreditation, and several of its undergraduate programs are accredited by NBA. The MREC NIRF ranking 2024 for the BTech program is 201-300 rank band.

MREC offers B.Tech. and M.Tech. programs across multiple specializations, including Computer Science, AI/ML, Data Science, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering. Admissions to the B.Tech courses are primarily through TS EAMCET and JEE Main scores. The first-year tuition fee for B.Tech is approximately ranges from INR 1 Lakh to INR 2.2 Lakhs, while the M.Tech program costs around INR 60K annually.

Spread over a 10-acre campus on Medchal Road, Malla Reddy Engineering College is known for its modern infrastructure, strong industry connections, and comprehensive academic offerings at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. While student reviews highlight its robust placement ecosystem and campus facilities, opinions on faculty and value for money are varied.

## MREC Admission Process 2025

MREC Admission Process 2025 is divided into two categories. Category A seats constitute 70% of the sanctioned intake in each course and are allotted to eligible candidates based on their rank in the EAMCET (Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical Common Entrance Test). Category B seats make up the remaining 30% and are open to all eligible candidates on a merit basis, including those from other states, Union Territories, and Non-Resident Indians (NRIs).

Eligible students can apply for various programs at Malla Reddy Engineering College through the **online application process**. Entrance exam registration must be completed separately on the respective official portals. Here’s a step-by-step guide for applying:

- Go to the **official MREC website**.
- Navigate to the **“Admissions” section** and begin the registration process.
- Complete the application form carefully with accurate personal and academic details.
- Submit the form and proceed to pay the **application fee** of **INR 500 for General category** and **INR 250 for SC/ST candidates**, payable via debit/credit card or net banking.
- After successful submission, **download or print a copy** of the filled form for your records.

## Campus Life at MREC

Malla Reddy Engineering College offers a wholesome campus life, featuring active cultural, sports, and academic activities, complemented by clean, secure, and well-equipped hostel accommodations. While student opinions on mess food may vary slightly, the overall sentiment highlights satisfactory facilities and an environment conducive to both growth and comfort.

- **Vibrant student ecosystem**: The college encourages active participation through seminars, workshops, student activity centers, mentoring programs, and focus groups.
- **Robust co-curricular culture**: Multiple clubs and societies (such as SAC, NCC, NSS, cultural and sports clubs) organize events, fests (like Akshara, Visesh), sports tournaments, and cultural programs.
- **Infrastructure**: The campus features digital classrooms, well-equipped labs, a spacious auditorium (1000+ capacity), seminar halls, canteen, sports courts (cricket, football, volleyball, basketball, tennis, kabaddi), and a gym.
- **Libraries**: Dedicated libraries with rich collections, e-resources, and reading rooms are available both on campus and in hostels.
- **Wi‑Fi & computers**: The campus and hostels have Wi‑Fi and computer facilities for academic and research needs.

### Hostel Facilities at MREC

- **Separate hostels** for boys and girls, located within the campus, each maintained to high hygiene and safety standards.
- **Modern & eco-friendly** infrastructure with well-ventilated rooms housing 3–5 students each, equipped with furniture like beds, study tables, and lockers.
- **Power and utilities**: 24×7 clean water, electricity backup, purified/mineral water, and solar-heated hot water.
- **Security & safety**: Around-the-clock security and CCTV surveillance; strict anti-ragging measures; centralized warden and housekeeping oversight
- **Medical support**: On-call doctor, first-aid kit, and ambulance services available 24×7
- **Amenities & recreation**: Common rooms with TV, magazines, newspapers, and indoor game areas (table tennis, carrom). Gym and yoga facilities are available within the hostel premises.
- **Dining services**: Hygienic mess providing vegetarian and non-vegetarian meal plans; students report generally good—though occasionally variable—food quality.
- **Internet access**: Wi‑Fi connectivity throughout the hostel campus
- **Fees**: On-campus hostel fees range from INR 55,000 to INR 90,000 per annum, typically split into two installments.
