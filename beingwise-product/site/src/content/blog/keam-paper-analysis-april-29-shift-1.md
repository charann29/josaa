## Exam Overview: KEAM, April 29th Shift 1

The pharmacy exam pattern varies slightly compared to the engineering exam pattern. The KEAM exam for pharmacy students only consists of two subjects, i.e. Physics and Chemistry. The exam is held for 90 Minutes and candidates will earn 4 marks for the correct response while they lose 1 mark for an incorrect response. Check the following table for details:

|  |  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Subject** | **No of Questions** | **Marks** | **Marking Scheme** | **Time** |
| Physics | 45 | 180 | -1 Mark for Incorrect Answer +4 Marks for Correct Answer | 90 Minutes |
| Chemistry | 30 | 120 |
| **Total** | **75** | **300** |

## Response Sheet & Answer Key: KEAM 2025

The authorities have released KEAM answer key on the official website i.e. [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in). Candidates can visit our page to download the shift-wise answer key PDFs. Candidates can challenge the answer key if the face any issues with the questions, however candidates have to also submit the supporting documents inorder to challenge the question. They have to pay ₹200 per question to raise the objection against the answer key.

Officials will release the final answer key after evaluating the received responses, the response sheet of the candidates is also made available in the official website. The response sheet contain the correct answer for the question along with the option marked by the candidate. ## April 29th KEAM 2025 Exam Analysis

Students reviews for KEAM 29th exam is given below:

**Aarav Menon (from Ernakulam):**
“I found the KEAM Pharmacy exam quite easy. In Physics, there were direct questions from topics like Laws of Motion and Current Electricity. Chemistry was very NCERT-focused, especially from Chemical Bonding and States of Matter. Both sections were manageable, and I had time to revise.”

**Sahana Reddy (from Thrissur):**
“The paper was easier than most of my mock tests. Chemistry was quick to finish — questions on Hydrocarbons and The p-Block Elements were very straightforward. Physics questions from Work, Energy & Power and Thermodynamics were clearly framed. It felt like a well-structured and stress-free exam.”

**Devraj (from Kollam):**
“Honestly, the exam felt very doable. Chemistry had several familiar questions from Equilibrium and Basic Principles of Organic Chemistry. Physics included some conceptual but simple problems from Kinematics and Gravitation. Nothing came out of the blue, so overall it was a comfortable experience.”

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ************Was the KEAM 2025 Pharmacy paper on April 29th easy?************

Yes, most students reported the paper as easy, especially with familiar questions from NCERT-based Physics and Chemistry topics.

### **2. ********Which topics were most asked in Physics on April 29?**********

Physics questions were largely from **Current Electricity**, **Gravitation**, **Work, Energy & Power**, and **Thermal Physics**.

### **3. ****How many questions were there in KEAM Pharmacy 2025?******

There were a total of **75 questions** — 45 from Physics and 30 from Chemistry.

### **4. **************What is the marking scheme for KEAM Pharmacy exam?****************

Each correct answer gives **+4 marks**, and **1 mark is deducted** for every incorrect response.
