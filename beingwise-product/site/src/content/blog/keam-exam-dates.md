The KEAM registration is yet to begin. To be eligible for KEAM 2025, candidates must have passed class 12th with a minimum aggregate of 45%. The Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE) conducting KEAM 2025 has scheduled the KEAM exam from  **April 24th to April 28th 2025**. Candidates preparing for KEAM 2025 can begin their preparation by familiarizing themselves with the detailed syllabus and exam pattern. The exam is held in Online mode (Computer based test).

Candidates who want to pursue engineering, architecture, and medicine courses in Kerala are required to take the KEAM 2025 exam.

## KEAM 2025 Admit Card Date

The KEAM 2025 admit card will be available for all registered candidates on the official website [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in). To download the admit cards, candidates must log in to the official website using their application number and password. The KEAM 2025 Admit card is yet to be released

## KEAM 2025 Answer Key Date

The Kerala CEE releases the answer keys in PDF format for Engineering, Architecture and Medical exams. Candidates have the option to challenge the answer key by submitting supporting documents along with a fee of 100/- for each question. If the challenges are accepted, the challenge fee for the questions will be refunded. Following the review of all challenges, the CEE released the final answer key, which candidates can then use to calculate their KEAM results.

## KEAM  Counselling Date

After the KEAM results, candidates can verify their names in the rank list to participate in the KEAM counselling process. Seats are allocated to candidates based on their preferences and the availability of seats in specific colleges and courses. The KEAM 2025 counselling process, known as the Centralised Admission Process, ensures a fair and transparent allocation of seats. While the KEAM Counselling date has not yet been released, Once it is available on the official website will be updated here.
