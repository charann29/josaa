## IIT Madras Course Admissions – B.Tech Admission Criteria

For IIT Madras Admissions, B.Tech applicants are admitted through national entrance examinations and a counselling process conducted by the National Test Agency (NTA). To apply for the B.Tech courses offered in IIT Madras, students must fulfil the following eligibility criteria and selection criteria for IIT Madras course admissions:

| Criteria | B.Tech Eligibility Criteria | B.Tech Selection Criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| B.Tech Programs | Must have passed Class 12 Physics, Chemistry, & Mathematics from a recognised board | Valid JEE Advanced Ranks + Seats are allotted through JoSAA Counselling across 6 rounds |
| Must have obtained 75% Marks in Aggregate |
| Must appear for JEE Mains & JEE Advanced |

## IIT Madras Course Admissions – B.Tech Admission Process

IIT Madras Admission Process for B.Tech is conducted solely through the JEE Advanced and JoSAA Counselling process. No institute-specific entrance test for B.Tech is conducted. Students need to apply online through the NTA website for the JEE Advanced exams and secure a valid All India Rank. Students need to further register for JoSAA counselling from June 2, 2026, after the JEE Advanced results are announced. Check the following to know how to join IIT Madras and the detailed IIT Madras Course Admissions Process for B.Tech applicants:

| Step | Action | Key Details |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Register for the JoSAA Counselling process | On josaa.nic.in, register with all the academic and personal details required. |
| 2 | Pay Registration Fees | Once the registration is done, pay the required amount of fees and complete the registration process. |
| 3 | Fill preferred IIT and Branch | Select the preferred B.Tech branch and institute in order of choice during the choice filling round. |
| 4 | Lock the choices | Students need to lock the preferences before the deadline. |
| 5 | Seat Allotment | Based on JEE Advanced ranks, category, and preferences, seats are allotted across 6 rounds. |
| 6 | Pay Counselling Fee | Students need to pay the counselling fee to confirm/lock the seat. |
| 7 | Document Verification & Admission | Report to the allotted college, verify documents, and pay admission fees to confirm admission. |
