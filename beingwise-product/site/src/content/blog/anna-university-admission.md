## Anna University Admission Criteria For B.Tech

Anna University admission process for B.Tech is entirely based on the student’s merit in the last qualifying exam and TNEA Counselling. Candidates’ Class 12 or equivalent exams, Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics marks are accumulated and normalised through the TNEA formula to scale it down to a 200 mark scheme. The normalised class 12 marks are then used for TNEA rank formulation to implement a fair marking system across all candidates from various boards.

The Final selection is then based on the Class 12 normalised PCM marks, TNEA ranks, student category, and college/branch preference. Check the following Anna University Admission B.Tech eligibility criteria and selection criteria:

| **Criteria** | **B.Tech Eligibility Criteria** | **B.Tech Selection Criteria** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| B.Tech Programs | Must have passed HSC or equivalent exam from a recognised board | Selected based on Class 12/HSC/equivalent exam merit + TNEA Counselling (Class 12 PCM Marks are later normalised using a 200 mark scheme used for TNEA Rank list) |
| Physics, Chemistry, & Mathematics are included as mandatory subjects | Seats are allotted based on TNEA Rank, Class 12 Normalised PCM Marks, & Preference. |
| Minimum aggregate of 45% for the General Category & 45% for the reserved category |

## How to Get Admission in Anna University For B.Tech?

Students can get admission based on academic merit and the TNEA Counselling process for B.Tech. But students need to apply online through the respective counselling official website. Check the following Anna University b tech admission process​ in detail:

| **Anna University Admission Stages** | **For Tamil Nadu Students (TNEA Route)** |
| --- | --- |
| Application Portal | Apply through the [TNEA official porta](https://www.tneaonline.org/)l |
| Registration | Create a TNEA Account  by entering personal, academic and category details |
| Application Fee | Pay the online application fee of INR 500 for the General Category & INR 250 for the Reserved Category |
| Document Upload | Upload the Class 10th & 12th marksheet, community, nativity and income certificate. Along with the Aadhaar card, Photos, and signature. |
| Certificate Verification | Online verification by TNEA authorities at TFC |
| Merit List | The normalised Class 12 PCM Marks-based TNEA rank list is published. Each student gets a TNEA rank |
| Choice Filling round | Log in to the TNEA account and fill in the preferred colleges & Branches |
| Seat Allotment | Multiple rounds of TNEA seat allotment are conducted based on rank, choices, and category reservation |
| Reporting & Verification | Report to the allotted colleges with the required documents, original copy and Pay Tuition fees Or, Register on the Anna University portal and pay the fees. |
| Final Admission | Admission is finalised once all the procedures and formalities are done. |

Check: [TNEA Login](/blog/tnea/tnea-login-steps/)

For other state students or NRI students, admission is done through the OS and NRI categories. The application process for Anna University Admission for other state candidates is as follows:

- Apply online through the Anna University Web portal
- Register with a valid Email ID and mobile number.
- Upload the required documents and a scanned copy of the photos.
- Pay INR 800 + 18% GST as registration fee through Credit Card or Debit Card.
- Further, they need to appear for the Anna University counselling online once the merit list is published.
