Candidates who have completed their application form and paid the registration fee are eligible to upload documents. **The deadline for document uploads is June 9, 2025.** The application process started on May 7, 2025, and will conclude on June 6, 2025**.**

To participate in TNEA counselling, candidates must ensure all required documents are uploaded by the specified deadline. This can be done by visiting the official website <http://tneaonline.org>. Uploaded documents undergo further verification at TFC centres.

To be eligible for the TNEA Counselling, candidates must meet the specified cut-off marks. This counselling process is for aspiring students to pursue engineering programs in Tamil Nadu. Eligible candidates must have successfully completed their 10+2 education.

The cut-off marks are determined based on subjects such as Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry, with a total score of 200. Mathematics carries a weightage of 100 marks, while Physics and Chemistry each carry 50 marks. As admission to top colleges is highly competitive, candidates must dedicate themselves to studying the depth of these subjects. The Directorate of Technical Education, Tamil Nadu, prepares a ranking list based on these marks.

## TNEA Documents Upload Instructions

Here, are the document upload instructions given by the TNEA, candidates must upload all the documents that are mentioned below.

1. Only candidates who have completed registration and paid the fee can upload certificates.
2. Candidates need to log in to their [www.tneaonline.org](http://www.tneaonline.org) account on their scheduled dates.
3. After logging in, candidates should click on the “upload certificates” link.
4. Candidates should read and follow the instructions provided on the upload page.
5. Certificates must be in digital format (PDF or Image).
6. The size of certificates should be between 150 KB and 1 MB. The photo and signature should be between 20 KB and 50 KB.
7. Candidates must upload mandatory documents such as 10th, 11th (for Tamil Nadu State Board students), 12th mark sheets, and community certificates (except for OC candidates).
8. Optional/additional certificates can also be uploaded based on specific criteria.

- Transfer Certificate (optional)
- Nativity Certificate (If Native Tamil Nadu candidates have studied in other states in any one of the classes from 8th std to 12th std)
- Parent’s occupation Certificate (Other state candidates whose parents are Central/State government employee in Tamil Nadu for the past five years)
- Srilankan Refugee Certificate (For Srilankan refugees)
- Ex-servicemen certificate (Special Reservation – Ex-servicemen)
- Differently Abled certificate(if applicable)

9. Certificates should be uploaded one by one by clicking the “upload” button under each certificate.
10. Candidates can check the validity and visibility of uploaded certificates by clicking the “download” icon. They can also delete and upload new ones if needed.
11. Once all certificates are uploaded, candidates should click the “Freeze Upload” button. They need to confirm the declaration in the popup window to finalize the upload.
12. Once certificates are frozen, no further changes can be made, but candidates can view and download them.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. In what format the required documents are uploaded?

The required documents for all certificates should be uploaded in JPG/JPEG/PDF format only.

### 2. What is the last date to upload documents for the TNEA application?

The last date to upload documents for the TNEA application is 9th June 2025.

### 3. What is the registration fee for the TNEA Application?

The registration fee for the TNEA Application is INR 500 for OC candidates and INR 250 for SC/ST candidates.

### 4. Who is eligible to upload the documents for TNEA registration?

Only candidates who have completed the application form and paid the registration fee are eligible to upload documents for TNEA registration.

### 5. Who can fill out the TNEA Application form?

Candidates who have completed their 10+2 education in Tamil Nadu are eligible to fill out the TNEA application form
