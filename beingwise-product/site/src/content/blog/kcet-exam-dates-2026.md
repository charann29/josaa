## KCET Exam Eligibility 2026

Refer to the following to check out the KCET eligibility criteria 2026. The eligibility norms are the same as per the KCET application form 2025 requirements.

- Should be an Indian citizen to apply.
- Had to have successfully taken Class 12/PUC (or due in 2026) with a recognised board.
- Class 12 must have Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
- Should possess Chemistry, Biology, Biotechnology, Electronics or Computer Science as an optional subject in addition to English.
- Nativity: The candidates need to meet Karnataka study/domicile requirements (i.e. a minimum number of years in Karnataka schools) as required by KEA in government seats.
- Students who took Class 12 and passed in the Karnataka Open University are not eligible.
- Minimum qualifying marks: 45% in case of the General category; 40% in case of SC/ST (depending on the course).
