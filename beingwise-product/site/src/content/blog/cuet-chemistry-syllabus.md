## CUET Chemistry Syllabus 2026

The CUET Chemistry Syllabus 2026 is of great importance for candidates aspiring for a B.Tech degree through CUET UG, since it forms the foundation for the basic concepts used in the field of engineering. According to the exam pattern, **the chemistry exam comprises 50 questions in multiple-choice format, which candidates have to answer within 60  minutes in Computer-Based Test (CBT) mode.**

It becomes imperative for candidates aspiring for B.Tech to pay particular attention to topics in Chemistry CUET Syllabus 2026 that carry high weightage, such as electrochemistry, chemical kinetics, solutions, and organic reaction mechanisms. These topics in the CUET Chemistry Syllabus lay the groundwork for entrance into engineering.

## Unit-wise Breakdown of CUET 2026 Chemistry Syllabus​

The CUET UG Chemistry Syllabus is broadly divided into ten units covering crucial areas of organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry. Take a look at the key topics and unit-wise breakdown of the Chemistry CUET Syllabus 2026 below.

| Unit | Unit Name | Key Topics |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Unit I | Solutions | Types of solutions, concentration terms, solubility (solid & gas), Henry’s Law, Raoult’s Law, ideal & non-ideal solutions, azeotropes, colligative properties (boiling point, freezing point, osmotic pressure), reverse osmosis, Van’t Hoff factor |
| Unit II | Electrochemistry | Electrochemical & galvanic cells, EMF, electrode potential, Nernst equation, Gibbs energy relation, conductivity (specific & molar), Kohlrausch law, electrolysis, Faraday’s laws, batteries, fuel cells, corrosion |
| Unit III | Chemical Kinetics | Rate of reaction, factors affecting rate, rate law, order & molecularity, zero & first order reactions, half-life, Arrhenius equation, activation energy, collision theory |
| Unit IV | d & f Block Elements | Electronic configuration, periodic trends, lanthanide contraction, oxidation states, magnetic & colour properties, complex formation, catalytic properties, important compounds (K₂Cr₂O₇, KMnO₄), lanthanoids & actinoids |
| Unit V | Coordination Compounds | Werner’s theory, ligands & coordination number, nomenclature, isomerism, bonding (VBT & CFT), magnetic properties, spectrochemical series, metal carbonyls, applications |
| Unit VI | Haloalkanes & Haloarenes | Classification, nomenclature, C–X bond nature, preparation methods, physical & chemical properties, SN reactions, stereochemistry, important compounds (CHCl₃, CCl₄, DDT, freons) |
| Unit VII | Alcohols, Phenols & Ethers | Classification & structure, preparation, physical properties, reactions of alcohols, phenols & ethers, important compounds (methanol, ethanol) |
| Unit VIII | Aldehydes, Ketones & Carboxylic Acids | Carbonyl structure, nomenclature, preparation, physical properties, chemical reactions, uses, carboxylic acids (structure & reactions) |
| Unit IX | Amines | Classification, nomenclature, preparation, physical properties, basic nature, reactions, diazonium salts & their importance |
| Unit X | Biomolecules | Carbohydrates (glucose, fructose), proteins & amino acids, enzymes, vitamins, nucleic acids (DNA/RNA), hormones |

## How to prepare for the CUET Chemistry Exam

Given the huge size and theoretical nature of the CUET UG Chemistry Syllabus 2026, sticking to the proper preparation techniques will definitely help you score well. Here are a few useful guidelines to ace the CUET 2026 Chemistry Syllabus.

- **Give Priority to Weightage Chapters**

When preparing for the CUET 2026 Chemistry Syllabus, begin with topics that hold higher importance. Some of the most critical topics include Alcohols, Phenols & Ethers, Electrochemistry, Aldehydes, Ketones & Carboxylic Acids, d- & f-Block Elements, and Chemical Kinetics. Concentrating on these will ensure optimal use of time and maximum marks.

- **Understand All Concepts of NCERT Class 12**

The CUET Chemistry Syllabus 2026 PDF is basically compiled from the NCERT Class 12 Chemistry book. You must go through each chapter and solve all the in-text and exercise problems. It is necessary to have an in-depth knowledge of NCERT concepts for effective coverage of the CUET Chemistry Syllabus.

- **Practice Numerical Questions Regularly**

Numerical questions constitute a major portion of the CUET UG Chemistry Syllabus 2026, particularly in Physical Chemistry. Consistent practice will enhance your speed and accuracy in solving numerical problems. Prepare short notes on formulas, SI units, periodic trends, and vital reaction mechanisms for quick revision.

- **Solve Previous Year Questions (PYQs)**

Solving PYQs will assist you in understanding the trend of the Chemistry CUET Syllabus 2026 Exam. This method can give you information on important topics to be prepared in advance.

- **Attempt Full-Length Mock Tests**

Taking full-length mock tests is essential to prepare yourself well in terms of time management and understanding the CUET 2026 Chemistry Syllabus.
