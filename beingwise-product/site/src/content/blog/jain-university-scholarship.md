## Jain University Scholarships

Arka Jain University Scholarship can be largely categorised into the following types of scholarships based on different criteria.

### Jain Deemed To Be University JEE Main-Based Scholarship Criteria

JEE Main-based Scholarship in Jain deemed to be University is offered to B.Tech applicants by the Faculty of Engineering and Technology department. They will be responsible for granting the scholarships as well. Up to 25% fee waiver is offered on the Jain University fees under this B.Tech scholarship scheme. Students who are offered this scholarship should meet the following criteria:

- Must be an Indian Citizen
- Must have appeared for the JEE Main entrance exam
- Must have secured 60% or above in the 10+2  level exam or any other equivalent exam prescribed as per AICTE and UGC.

#### JEE Main-Based Scholarship Process

 Students who have applied for a scholarship will be shortlisted as per the eligibility criteria by the scholarship committee.

- After the JEE Main results are announced, a merit list is created and published on the university website.
- Shortlisted candidates need to appear for the Scholarship Counselling Round (SCR).
- An email is sent to the candidate’s registered email address about the details of the counselling session.
- A final Merit-cum-means list of candidates selected for the scholarship is published on the University’s website.
- Students need to complete the provisional admission formalities within the given time in the Provisional Admission/Scholarship Form. If students fail to complete it on time, the scholarship offer will be withdrawn.

### Jain Deemed To Be University Sports Scholarship Criteria

Jain University Bangalore Scholarships for B.Tech students who have achieved in the sports field can apply for this scholarship. Students need to fulfil the following criteria:

- Candidates who have won or participated at the National Level in Super-A Games or B-Games during the 5 years of basic education in India.
- Students who have participated or won trophies/Medals in Super-A and B-Games while studying from Class 8 to 12 (Five Years)
- Scholarship Committee approvals or decisions are final.

### Jain Deemed To Be University Defence Scholarship Criteria

Jain University offers defence personnel wards with this specific scholarship under the following eligibility criteria:

- Students who are wards of Serving/Ex-Armed Forces Personnel can apply for this scholarship.
- Must have a Defence Certificate (In-Service)
- Must have a defence certificate issued by the Joint Director/Deputy Director (Ex-Defence Personnel)

### Jain Deemed To Be University SC/ST Category Scholarship Criteria

Candidates from the reserved categories can opt for the SC/ST Category Jain University Scholarship. SC/ST category students need to produce the following documents to apply for the scholarship:

- A Caste Certificate/ Caste income certificate issued by the concerned Jurisdictional Tahsildar as per Government Regulations.
- Form D needs to be submitted to the scholarship committee.

### Jain Deemed To Be University Eklavya Scholarship Criteria

Jain University offers a Sports scholarship named Eklavya under the following criteria:

- Students must have passed the qualifying examination.
- Must be a Citizen of India
- Students must be between 17 and 21 years of age.

[Jain University Scholarship](https://www.jainuniversity.ac.in/academics/scholarships-offered-in-india) provides the Eklavya scholarship for a period of 3 years. The renewal of this scholarship is based on the performances of the players or their achievements in the State level/ national level/All India Ranking Tournaments. Based on the performance each respective year, the scholarships will be renewed. Scholarship termination is conducted by the Jain deemed-to-be-university scholarship committee (JUSC).

## Jain University Scholarship – Application, Selection & Disbursement Process

The application and selection process for the Jain University Bangalore Scholarship is as follows:

- Visit the Jain University admission office to obtain the scholarship application.
- Fill the scholarship form with the required details.
- Submit it to the admission office on campus.
- Submitted applications are presented to the Jain University Scholarship Committee.
- After scrutinising, selected applications received a scholarship offer letter along with a scholarship acceptance form.
- Within the indicated timeline on the acceptance form, students must accept or decline the scholarship offer.

After accepting the offer, Jain University Bangalore will inform the details of the date, time, and place of awarding the scholarship to the students.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **1. **Is there any Jain Minority Scholarship?****

No, there are no scholarships available for the Minority community at Jain University Bangalore.

### **2. Do students need to appear for the Jain University Scholarship Test?**

There is no specific Jain University entrance exam to qualify for the Jain University Scholarship. But B.Tech scholarships are presented based on JEE Main entrance exam results. Moreover, B.Tech applicants who have not appeared for any state or national-level entrance exam need to appear for the Jet Exam Jain University.

### 3. **Can I apply online for the Jain University Scholarship?**

No, students need to apply offline at the admission office of Jain University Bangalore campus.

### 4. **How to apply for the Jain University Bangalore Scholarship?**

Students applying for the Jain University Scholarship need to first collect the scholarship form from the admission office and fill it out with the required details. Submit the scholarship form to the office with the required documents.

### 5. **Are there any scholarships for international students at Jain University?**

Yes, Jain University Bangalore Scholarships are also offered to NRI Students under the Study in India Scholarship by MHRD. A 100% scholarship is provided on Tuition fees, hostel food, and Stay Waiver.

### 6. What are the Jain University Fees for B.Tech CSE?

Jain University Bangalore Fee for B.Tech Computer & Science Engineering is INR 3,25,000 for one year. An additional one-time registration fee of INR 30,000 has to be paid as well. For Jain University B.Tech CSE (AI & ML), the fee is INR 4,00,000.

### 7. What is the Jain deemed to be University Admission Process for B.Tech?

The Jain University Bangalore admission process for B.Tech programs requires students to have passed 10+2 level examination with atleast 45% marks with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. Further, students need to have a valid JEE Main/COMEDK/UNIGAUGE score to be eligible for Jain University B.Tech admission.
But candidates without a valid JEE Main/COMEDK/UNIGAUGE score need to appear for the JAIN Entrance Test (JET). Shortlisted students need to appear for the counselling round to block the Jain University Seats. To confirm admission, applicants are required to accept the admission offer letter and pay the Jain University Fees.

### 8. How is the Jain University Bangalore Scholarship amount given to the students?

Students who have received the scholarships are not paid the scholarship amount directly by Jain University. It is deducted from the term fees.

### 9. When are the Jain University Scholarship applications rejected?

Jain University Scholarships are rejected based on any misinformation provided by the candidate during the program. Students will need to pay the entire amount immediately after that.

### 10. What are the other Jain University Scholarships provided?

Apart from Jain University Bangalore B.Tech Scholarships, Jain University offers the Medha Scholarship for B.Com applicants as well.
