## CUET UG 2025 GAT Paper Analysis

The General Aptitude Test (GAT) paper in CUET UG 2025 placed greater emphasis on General Knowledge (GK) and Quantitative Ability, with these two sections carrying the most weightage. Reasoning questions accounted for around 10 to 15 items, providing a balanced challenge for the students without being overly difficult. Notably, the General Knowledge and General Studies (GK/GS) section had more questions in the evening shift. Overall, Students found the GAT paper was rated as easy to moderate in difficulty. The mix of straightforward GK questions combined with moderate quantitative problems made the paper engaging but fair, requiring both conceptual clarity and quick thinking to manage time effectively.

## CUET UG 2025 History Paper Analysis

In the History paper of CUET UG 2025, students found that the maximum number of questions, from the Harappan Civilization. They found questions from the Mauryan Period focused on the order of events, especially during that time. Questions about the framing of the Constitution were mostly about dates and years, and students found them a bit difficult. There was a question about Ashoka’s Dhamma policy, specifically about Dhamma Mahamatta. Students also pointed out 2-3 questions from the chapter on Akbar’s reign, including the Ain-i-Akbari. Questions about the Vijayanagara Empire asked about Raya and its capital city. The 1857 Revolt was mentioned as a possible topic, but was not confirmed in the exam. Chronological order questions were common and required students to arrange events correctly. A question from the Partition chapter was also asked. Many students found that reviewing Previous Year Questions (PYQs) helped because similar questions often come up. Overall, students felt the exam covered many important history topics with a mix of easy and challenging questions.

## General Preparation Advice Based on the CUET May 20 Shift 2 Analysis

- **Focus on PYQs:** PYQs are repeating across subjects and are highly recommended for practice and review.
- **Adhere to NCERT:** For domain subjects, NCERT is the primary and most important source. Avoid relying on isolated topics.
- **Practice GAT:** Especially for Math/Quant and numerical questions in Physics, practice is crucial for success.
- **Time Management:** It is important to manage time during the exam to ensure all questions are attempted and mistakes are avoided.
- **Mock Tests:** Taking mock tests is recommended for practice and getting an exam-like feeling. Mock tests can provide a large pool of questions for practice.
- **Review GK:** Reviewing past GK classes and marathon sessions is advised for the GK section. Do not focus on new current affairs; revise existing GK.

## Student Reviews For CUET UG 2025 May 20th Shift 2 Exam

Most students from different states found CUET UG 2025 Shift 2, which was held on May 20th, was moderate. Here is a summary of what students perceived about the exam.

**One student found the General Aptitude Test (GAT)** section was of moderate difficulty, with most questions focused on reasoning and some quantitative problems. Many students felt time management was important, as the last few questions were slightly tricky.

Another student felt **the English section** had lengthy comprehension passages but was generally straightforward. Vocabulary and grammar questions were rated easy to moderate, making it manageable for students with decent language skills.

**History** questions mainly covered modern Indian history, along with some world history topics. The mix of factual and analytical questions made it a fair challenge, though a few students found questions related to dates and events a bit tougher.

Students found the **Physics** difficulty level was moderate, with application-based questions primarily from mechanics and electricity. Some conceptual problems required a deeper understanding, making this section a bit challenging for some students.

**Biology** was comparatively easier, with questions mostly aligned with the NCERT syllabus. Topics like Genetics and Ecology were emphasized, and many students felt more confident answering Biology than Physics.

## CUET UG 2025 Exam Day Guidelines

Given below are a few CUET UG 2025 exam day guidelines that students need to follow.

- **Carry Only Required Items:** Only take your printed admit card (with passport-size photograph), your valid photo ID (Aadhaar or PAN card), a transparent ballpoint pen, and a transparent water bottle. Candidates can check all the CUET exam details on the admit card and are advised to adhere to the instructions.
- **Be On Time:** Report to the exam center well in advance relative to your reporting time (7:00 AM for Shift 1 and 1:00 PM for Shift 2). If you are late, you will not be allowed to enter.
- **Prohibited Items:** Do not bring mobile phones, smart watches, electronic devices, bags, books, or food if not medically required (with documentation).
- **Maintain Discipline:** Be seated in your assigned seat, only do rough work on the sheets provided, and do not perform any unfair practice or make any noise in the exam hall.
- **Time Management:** It is a critical challenge across subjects, especially with lengthy sections like RC or Quant. It’s advised to tackle easy questions first.
- **The Normalisation Factor:** This factor will account for variations in difficulty across shifts. The focus should be on attempting as many questions with 100% accuracy.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ************************What subjects were covered in the May 20th Shift 2 CUET UG exam?************************

The exam included General Aptitude Test (GAT), English, History, Physics, and Biology.

### 2. ****************How difficult was the General Aptitude Test (GAT) section?****************

It was moderately difficult with reasoning and quantitative questions, requiring good time management.

### **3. ******Were the English questions easy in CUET UG 2025?********

Yes, the English section had manageable comprehension passages and easy-to-moderate vocabulary and grammar questions. Some students faced difficulties with Antonym/Synonym questions involving new words.

### **4. ************What topics were emphasized in the History section of CUET UG 2025?**************

Mainly modern Indian history and some world history topics, with a mix of factual and analytical questions. The topics emphasised were Mauryan Period, Framing the Constitution, Ashoka (Dhamma Policy), Peasants, Zamindars, and the State (Akbar’s reign), Vijayanagara Empire (Raya’s and Capital), and Harappan Civilization.

### 5. **What is the CUET exam syllabus?**

CUET exam syllabus has a language subject which has various types of passages, such as factual, literary, and narrative, etc. 5 domain subjects, and a section of GAT which assesses General Knowledge, Current Affairs, General Mental Ability, Numerical Ability, etc.
