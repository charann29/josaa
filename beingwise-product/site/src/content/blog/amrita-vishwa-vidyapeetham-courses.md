## Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Courses – B.Tech Admission Process & Eligibility Criteria

Students willing to pursue Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Courses for B.Tech are admitted only based on merit. The admission process is conducted entirely based on merit in the previous qualifying exam and at the entrance level exam. Check the following section to get details on the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Course Admissions process and criteria for B.Tech aspirants:

**Eligibility Criteria**: Passed Class 12 or any equivalent exam with 60% marks in aggregate with PCM as mandatory subjects.

**Accepted Entrance Exams**: Amrita Entrance Exam – Engineering (AEEE)/JEE Main

**Selection Criteria**: AEEE/JEE Main Ranks, followed by CSAP Counselling.
**Seat allotment**: 70% seats filled through AEEE & 30% through JEE Main.

Eligible students need to appear for the AEEE exam or have a valid JEE Main rank. Post the AEEE result announcement, shortlisted candidates are required to register for CSAP counselling conducted by Amrita University. During the counselling, students need to choose their preferred branches and campus locations, based on seat allocations. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1.  **What are the B.Tech specializations offered under Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham courses?**

The B.Tech specializations offered under the Amrita University courses are CSE, AI, Data Science, Cyber Security, EEE, ECE, Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, Civil Engineering, and others.

### 2. **What is the B.Tech fee range for Amrita University Courses?**

The B.Tech fee range for Amrita University courses varies from one campus to another. For the main campus, the annual B.Tech fee range varies from INR 3 Lakhs to INR 6 Lakhs.

### 3. **What is the duration of the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham courses for B.Tech?**

The duration for Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham courses for B.Tech is 4 years.

### 4. **Does Amrita University offer scholarships for B.Tech students?**

Amrita University offers merit scholarships under two scholarship schemes: the Amrita Chancellor Scholarship and the Amrita Meritorious Scholarship. Up to 100% scholarship is available for students who score above 80%. Need-based scholarships are offered to students based on the student’s economic background and category.

### 5. **Are Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham courses for B.Tech offered across all Amrita Campuses?**

Yes, almost all the 8 Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham campuses offer B.Tech courses. Although the number of B.Tech courses varies from one campus to another.

### 6. **How is the student life after opting for Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Courses for B.Tech?**

As per student reviews and experiences, the campus life, faculty, and academic environment are disciplined, and the frequent social events and fests make the experience more enjoyable for students. But students report having limited campus access and restrictions regarding campus life.
