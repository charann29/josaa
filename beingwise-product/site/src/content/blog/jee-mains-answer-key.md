## How to Challenge NTA JEE Main Answer Key Session 2

The complete process for the JEE Mains 2026 Answer Key challenge is given below.

**Step 1:** Visit the NTA Official website https://jeemain.nta.nic.in/

**Step 2:** Under the latest announcement, click ‘Challenge (s) regarding Answer Key’.

**Step 3:** Once you click, you are redirected to a login page.

**Step 4:** Now, log in with your Application Number and Password, enter the Captcha Code as displayed, and Submit.

**Step 5:** After logging into the Dashboard, click ‘Challenge (s) regarding Answer Key’.

**Step 6:** You will see the Question IDs in the order below for JEE (Main) – 2026 Session 2 (April 2026)

**Step 7:** The ID next to the question in the ‘Correct Option’ column is the NTA’s most relevant JEE Mains Answer Key.

**Step 8:** If you wish to dispute this choice, you may use any one or more of the choice IDs listed in the next four columns and click the check box.

**Step 9:** You can upload supporting documents by selecting ‘Choose File and Upload’ (all documents will be in a single PDF file).

**Step 10:** After selecting your selected option, scroll down to ‘Save your claim’ and go to the next screen.

**Step 11:** You will get a list of all the Option IDs you challenged in the JEE Main Session 2 Answer Key PDF

**Step 12**: Select ‘Save your claim and pay the fee eventually’.

**Step 13:** Choose the mode of payment and pay a non-refundable processing charge of Rs. 200/- for each question questioned. Make payment using Debit Card, Credit Card, or Net Banking.
