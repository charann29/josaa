## KCET Option Entry

The facility of Option Entry for the first round has started. The candidates who qualified for KCET will be able to start filling their preferred options of institutes/colleges and courses from **23-07-2025.** The candidates must be careful while filling in their options because the seat allotment is based on the choices they enter during the option entry process. The candidates can find the direct link below to the KCET First Round option entry:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **KCET 2025 Option Entry Link** | [**CLICK HERE TO START**](https://keaonline.karnataka.gov.in/ugcet_opt_entry_2024/main/index.php) |

After the first round of the option entry process, the examination authorities will release the mock seat allotment results. Candidates who are unsatisfied with their mock allotment results will be allowed to change their preferences.

## KCET 2025 Option Entry Procedure:

Here is the procedure to be followed by the candidates for the KCET 2025 Option Entry:

**Step 1:** Visit the KCET Option Entry Link, <https://keaonline.karnataka.gov.in/ugcet_opt_entry_2024/main/index.php>

**Step 2:** After clicking on the link, enter your CET Number (which will be available on your online verification slip) and the captcha to submit the form

**Step 3:** The list of colleges and courses will be displayed on the screen. Select your preferred options of colleges and courses.

**Step 4:** After submitting your choices of colleges and courses, take a printout for future reference

## KCET 2025 Counselling Process

The procedure of the KCET 2025 Counselling will be done in four main steps. The detailed process is as follows:

### **1. Document Verification and Registration:**

- After the release of the KCET 2025 results, all the qualified candidates will undergo the Document Verification process.
- The qualified candidates must visit the helpline centres (scheduled by the authorities) to start the document verification process.
- The candidates must carry all the required original documents for verification along with two or three copies of the original documents.
- All the documents will be verified by the authorities. After verification, they will provide an acknowledgement card or a verification slip to the candidate.
- The candidate must check all the details mentioned in the verification slip or acknowledgement card before leaving the helpline centre.
- No changes will be made to the verification slip after leaving the venue.

#### **Required documents for the Counselling Process:**

Below are the documents required for the KCET 2025 Document Verification process:

1. KCET 2025 Application Form printout
2. KCET 2025 Admit Card
3. 10th Class (SSC) Marks sheet
4. 12th Class (Second PUC) Mark sheet
5. Study Certificates of the candidate (Past 7 years)
6. Two recent passport-size photographs of the candidate
7. Kannada Medium Certificate of the candidate (If Applicable)
8. Caste and Income Certificates (If Applicable)

### **2. KCET 2025 Choice Filling and Locking:**

- The candidates who completed the KCET 2025 document verification process can start filling out the college options through the official website.
- The candidate can log in to the official website with the required login credentials and find the choice-filling link.
- After logging in, the students can fill in the college and course options based on their preferences and ranks achieved.
- The candidates can also check the KCET 2025 Seat Matrix at the time of Choice Filling through the official website.
- The candidates can also be able to modify the selected college and course options in the KCET 2025 Choice Filling.

### **3. KCET 2025 Seat Allotment:**

- After filling in the choices of colleges and courses and the locking process, the KCET 2025 Seat Allotment will be released through the official website.
- Based on the candidate’s KCET 2025 rank, category, colleges, and courses they prefer, the seat will be allotted to the candidate.
- The candidates who are satisfied with the allotted seats will have to confirm the seats by paying the fee and downloading the seat allotment order slip. The fee payment will confirm the admission of the candidate.

### **4. Reporting to the respective Colleges:**

- After the seat allotment process, the candidates should report to their respective colleges.
- The candidates must carry the required documents along with the KCET 2025 Allotment order slip.
- To confirm the admission, the candidates must pay the tuition or admission fee to the respective colleges.

## Frequently Asked Questions:

### **1. What is the meaning of Supernumerary Quota in KCET Counselling? How many seats are reserved for this Quota?**

In KCET Counselling, the supernumerary Quota is for students whose family income is below 6 lakhs, no matter which category they belong to. Around 5% of the seats in KCET Counselling are reserved for these students.

### **2. Is it possible to alter my preferences after registering for the KCET Counselling 2025?**

Yes, it is possible. The Karnataka Examinations Authority(KEA) will provide a window for the candidates to adjust their options after the release of the KCET Mock Seat Allotment. During this period, you can modify your preferences based on your scores and the availability of the seats.

### **3. What factors will determine the KCET 2025 Seat Allotment?**

The KCET 2025 Seat Allotment is based on the following factors:
– The candidate’s KCET Merit
– The preferences as chosen by the candidate
– The availability of the seats
