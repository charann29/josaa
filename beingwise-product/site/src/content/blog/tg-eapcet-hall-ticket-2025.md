**TS EAMCET Hall Ticket 2025 will be released by TSCHE on 19th April 2025.** Candidates can download the TS EAMCET 2025 Hall Ticket directly from the official website. However, the application is ongoing and the **last date to submit the form is 4th April 2025**.

TS EAMCET is an important entrance exam for 12th MPC students in Telangana. It’s organized by the JNTUH on behalf of the TSCHE. The exam allows students to apply to various undergraduate professional courses offered by colleges and universities in Telangana.

To download the hall ticket, candidates need to login with their credentials such as application number, Qualifying Examination Hall Ticket Number (12th Hall ticket number), and Date of Birth. It’s important to note that the TS EAMCET Admit card is a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall on the day of the exam.

**TS EAMCET 2025 exam will be held from 29th April to 5th May 2025.** It will cover subjects like Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry for Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy aspirants. Candidates must meet the specific eligibility criteria to attempt the exam.

## How to Download TS EAMCET Admit Card 2025?

Candidates can download their **TS EAMCET Admit Card 2025** by following these steps:

Step 1: Go to the official website of TS EAPCET 2025 or [https://eapcet.tsche.ac.in/](https://eapcet.tsche.ac.in/TSEAPCET/EAPCET2024_GetHallTicket2024.aspx)

Step 2: Click on the ‘**Download Hall Ticket**’ link

Step 3: Log in using your credentials such as Application number, Qualifying Examination Hall Ticket Number (12th Hall ticket number) and Date of birth.

Step 4: Once logged in, review all the details mentioned on the admit card, correct or not.

Step 5: Find the ‘Download’ option below to take a printout of it.

## Exam Day Guidelines of TG EAPCET 2025

Candidates can get all the instructions and guidelines on the **TS EAMCET admit card**. You need to follow the guidelines and strictly adhere to those on the day of the **TG EAPCET exam 2025.** Given below are some of the [**exam day guidelines**](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/tg-eapcet-examday-guidelines/) that a candidate can follow.

- All the candidates should arrive at the exam hall at least two hours before the exam starts. Candidates will not be allowed in the TS EAMCET 2025 exam hall if they are late.
- Bringing the **TG EAPCET admit card** is mandatory for every candidate.
- Any electronic gadgets like calculators, log books, cell phones, smart watches, etc. are not allowed in the examination hall.
- Candidates can not leave the exam hall before the stipulated time of the exam set by the officials.
- Candidates must carefully read all the instructions on the question paper before proceeding with the exam.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ************************What to do in case there is any wrong information printed on the TS EAMCET admit card?************************

If any incorrect information is printed on the TS EAMCET admit card, students should report it to the examination conducting authority. They can contact the exam body through email ID and helpline number provided on the official website.

### 2. ******What if a candidate forgets to bring the hall ticket for the examination?******

If you forget to bring your admit card, you are not allowed to attempt the TS EAMCET exam. However, the test center might still allow you in after verifying your identity through other details. This could involve showing another ID or confirming your details in their records.

### 3. ****************Who releases the TS EAMCET 2025 Admit card?****************

The Telangana State Council of Higher Education(TSCHE) will release the TS EAMCET 2025 admit cards on the official website on 19th April 2025

### 4. ************Is it necessary to carry the TS EAMCET Admit card to the Examination hall?************

Yes, Candidates are advised to carry a hard copy of the TS EAMCET Admit card to the examination center.

### 5. **************What are the documents to be carried along with the TS EAMCET Admit Card?**************

Candidates are required to bring valid ID proof and 2 photographs along with the Admit card.

### 6. **********How to address issues during the TG EAPCET 2025 exam hall ticket download?**********

If you face any issue downloading the TG EAPCET hall ticket 2025, you can directly contact the authorities to solve the issue and for assistance. However, you can also try again after a short interval or after 24 hours to download the admit card.

### 7. ******Will the authority speed post the TG EAPCET hall ticket 2025 to the candidate’s home?******

No, candidates will receive the TS EAMCET admit card in online mode. Candidates need to visit the official website to download the hall ticket.

### 8. **Can I download the TG EAPCET 2025 admit card without the registration number?**

No, you cannot download the TG EAMCET Hall Ticket 2025 without the registration number. The necessary credentials include the qualifying exam hall ticket, registration number, and date of birth.

### 9. ************When is the TG EAPCET exam for admission to the Engineering courses?************

TG EAPCET 2025 exam for the Engineering courses will be held from 2nd May to 5th May 2025.

### 10. ******Can I wear any clothes during the TG EAMCET 2025 exam?******

No, you cannot wear any clothes or traditional clothes to the TG EAPCET 2025 exam. Candidates are advised to wear simple and formal clothes.
