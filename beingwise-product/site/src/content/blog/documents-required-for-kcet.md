## Mandatory Documents Required For KCET Counselling

Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) has prescribed a list of mandatory documents required for KCET counselling. The original copy of these documents must be carried along with photocopies by students during the KCET Document verification process. Check the following list of mandatory documents required for KCET verification:

- KCET 2026 Admit Card
- KCET 2026 Rank Card
- KCET Application Form Printout
- Marksheet of Class 10 & Class 12
- Study Certificate
- Aadhaar Card or any other ID proof
- Passport-size photo
- Transfer certificate
- Character/Conduct Certificate

## KCET Document Verification Process

After getting the KCET ranks and registering for the counselling process, students need to appear for the KCET document verification process. Students need to appear with the original documents required for KCET verification. Check the following KCET Document Verification Process:

- Book a KCET verification slot through the official KEA Portal.
- Visit the assigned verification centre on the scheduled date.
- Carry the original documents required for KCET along with photocopies.
- Submit the documents for Verification.
- KEA Official verify candidate details.
- Biometric/photo verification is conducted as well.
- After successful verification,  KEA issues a KCET verification slip and secret key.

With the completion of KCET Verification round, students need to keep the secret key to access option entry and counselling login. Also, keep the verification slip safely for future counselling stages and admission.

### Instructions to Follow During the KCET Document Verification Process

Refer to the following instructions to keep in mind during the KCET Document verification process:

- Reach the verification centre early.
- Carry both the original and scanned photocopies of the documents required for KCET.
- Ensure the validity of the certificates.
- KCET Application Form details should match with details in documents.
- Missing any document may delay the whole process, so be very careful.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Where to go for KCET document verification?

Students need to visit KEA Centres for the KCET document verification process.

### 2. What are the documents required for KCET counselling for SC category students?

The basic mandatory documents required include caste certificate, domicile certificate, and income certificate for SC category students.

### 3. What are the documents required for KCET document verification?

Basic documents required include KCET rank card, KCET admit card, study certificates, 10th & 12th PUC marksheets, Aadhaar Card, or another photo ID proof.

### 4. Is the KCET document upload necessary?

Yes, KCET document upload is necessary during the online verification stage for application submission, counselling registration, and verification processes.

### 5. Is the Aadhaar Card mandatory for KCET?

Yes, Aadhaar Card is one of the mandatory documents required for KCET as a key identity proof.

### 6. Are documents for Foreign, OCI, and PIO candidates necessary for KCET?

Yes, specific documents are required for OCI, PIO, and foreign candidates. These include study certificates countersigned by BEO and DDPI authorities.

### 7. What are the required documents for reservation and special categories?

Additional documents include caste certificate, income certificate, rural quota certificate, Kannada medium certificate, SNQ certificate, and other special quota documents.
