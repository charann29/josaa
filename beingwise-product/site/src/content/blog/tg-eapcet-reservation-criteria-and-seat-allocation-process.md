TG EAPCET reservation criteria and seat allocation process will commence after the entrance exam and result declaration. The TS EAMCET for the Engineering program will be held from **2nd May to 5th May 2025**.

TS EAMCET 2025 offers seats for various categories, which include physically disabled candidates, women, children of Army personnel, etc. Moreover, **85% seats are reserved for the Locals** of Telangana and **15% seats are reserved for non-local** candidates. However, after the results are out, the qualified candidates can apply for the counselling process. Based on the cutoff marks, the candidates will get their seats in the respective colleges. They can either accept the seat by paying fees or can opt for the next round.

## TG EAPCET Seat Allocation Process

All eligible candidates who have qualified for the TS EAMCET 2025 exam can register for the counseling and seat allocation process. You can check the steps below for the TG EAPCET seat allocation process.

**Payment of the Counseling Fees:**

Candidates must pay the counseling fees online as the first step in the registration process. They can pay the fees using a credit/debit card or internet banking of any nationalized bank. **Unreserved candidates need to pay INR 1200**, whereas r**eserved candidates need to pay INR 600** for the counseling registration fees.

**TG EAPCET 2025 Counseling Registration:**

Candidates must visit the official website after the TS EAMCET entrance exam to register for the 2025 counseling process. Complete the registration process by paying the fees and proceed with certificate verification.

**Certificate Verification:**

Next, you must verify your certificates with the authority. After successful verification, an acknowledgment letter will be issued. Candidates must complete the TG EAPCET web option entry following the certificate verification.

**Documents Required:**

– TS EAMCET 2025 rank card

– TS EAMCET hall ticket 2025

– Aadhaar Card

– Mark Sheets of all classes (from class 6 onwards to the qualifying degree)

– Transfer Certificate (T.C)

– Intermediate or equivalent Memo-cum-Pass Certificate

– Income Certificate issued after 01.01.2025 (if applicable)

– Caste Certificate (if applicable)

– Residence certificate of either parent in Telangana for 10 years for Non-Local candidates.

– Physically Challenged (PH)/Children of Armed Personnel (CAP)/NCC/Sports/Minority certificate (if applicable).

– Residence certificate if the candidate has no institutionalized education.

**TG EAPCET 2025 Seat Allotment:**

After successfully registering for the counseling process and paying the fees, you need to exercise the web option process. Candidates must log in to the portal using the registration number and password.

All the candidates are required to fill out their college preferences by following the given steps.

- Open the official portal of TG EAPCET 2025 @ tseamcet.nic.in
- Click on the “Candidate Login” link
- Log in using your registration number, hall ticket,  password, and date of birth.
- Enter the OTP which was sent to your mobile number and click on the “Click Here for Option Entry” button.
- Select the district in which the college is located.
- Click on the “Display Option Entry Form” button.
- Enter your desired colleges in order of preference.

After completing the above-mentioned step, candidates need to save their options which will be locked for seat allotment. Next you will receive the seat allotment result online. Candidates can check the seat allotment result by logging into the portal. Carefully enter the application number, ROC form number, hall ticket number, and date of birth to check your seat allotment status.

Candidates can either accept the seat or choose to participate in the next round of the seat allotment process. If a candidate accepts the seat, they must download the provisional seat allotment letter from the official website.

**Reporting to the Allotted Institute:**

The final step requires candidates to report to the institute within the specified date. Candidates must carry the required documents when reporting to the institutes. Admission will be canceled if the student fails to report to the Institute within the stipulated date and time.

### How Can I Check the Seat Allotment Result of TG EAPCET 2025?

TS EAMCET 2025 result will be released online. After successful registration and fees payment of the counseling process, you need to lock your seat allotment choices. Candidates can check the steps below to download the [**TG EAPCET**](https://eapcet.tgche.ac.in/) seat allotment result 2025.

- Visit the official website of TSCHE.
- Click on the login option available on the homepage
- Enter the given information:
  - ROC Number
  - TS EAMCET Hall Ticket Number
  - Date of Birth
  - Password
- Click on the submit button
- The seat allotment result will be displayed on the screen
- If you are satisfied, accept the seat and proceed to download the seat allotment letter.

### How to Download TG EAPCET Seat Allotment Letter?

After you successfully accept the seat, check the steps below to download the TS EAMCET 2025 seat allotment letter.

- Go to tseamcet.nic.in
- Click the TG EAPCET seat allotment result link.
- Candidates need to enter their login details.
- The seat allotment letter will appear on the screen.
- Next, you need to download the letter and take a printout for future reference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ******************What should I do, if I do not want the seat allocated to me during TS EAMCET 2025 counseling?******************

You have an option to reject the seat and go to the next round. During the counseling process of TG EAPCET 2025, you can choose to participate in the next round of seat allotment process.

### 2. **What documents are required for TS EAMCET verification and reporting?**

You can check the documents required for TS EAMCET verification and reporting given below.
Adhaar card
SSC Mark Sheet
Intermediate Mark Sheet
Income Certificate
Caste Certificate
Transfer Certificate
TS EAMCET 2025 Hall Ticket
TS EAMCET 2025 Rank Card

### 3. **********What will be the next process after I accept the seat allotted to me in TG EAPCET counseling 2025?**********

After you accept the allotted seat, you need to visit the official website to download the provisional seat allotment letter. Next, candidates need to report to the institute within the specified date with the required documents.

### 4. ********What percentage of seats are there for Local and Non-Local Reservation criteria of Telangana in TG EAPCET 2025?********

85% of the seats are given to the Local candidates of Telangana state. However, 15% of seats are there for non-local candidates.

### 5. ********What will happen if I fail to report to the allotted College/Institute within the stipulated date and time?********

After you accept the seat, you need to download the seat allotment result and report to the Institute with the necessary documents. In case of the failure of reporting, the admission will be cancelled.

### 6. **How to get a free seat in TS EAMCET?**

Candidates ranking within the top 10,000 ranks can get a “free seat” in the TS EAMCET 2025 exam. Moreover, belonging to a category can also get a fee reimbursement under the Telangana government’s E-pass scheme. Low family income and a valid ration card, candidates are also eligible for free seats in the TG EAPCET exam.

### 7. **********When will the TG EAPCET 2025 result be released?**********

The TG EAPCET 2025 result is expected to be released in June 2025.

### 8. ******How can I apply for the TS EAMCET 2025 exam?******

You need to visit the official website of TG EAPCET to apply for the entrance test. The application form was released on 1st March 2025 and the deadline is 4th April 2025.

### 9. **********Can I apply for multiple courses through TG EAPCET?**********

Yes, all the eligible candidates can apply for multiple courses through the TS EAMCET exam. Though you have to separately fill the application form for each course and pay the registration fees.

### 10. **Is there a different registration fee for the reserved category?**

Yes, all the reserved category students need to pay INR 500 application fees for each subject. In case you are opting for two programs, then you need to pay INR 1000 as the application fee.
