## **JEE Mains 2025 Preparation Tips: 5 Strategies to Follow in Last 45 Days**

The last 45 days are considered the final stage of JEE preparation, yet very crucial. In this final phase, you need to prioritise subjects based on your concept and their weightage in the exam. To help you out, we have prepared a list. Let’s check:

### 1. Prioritize Time

Students still have approximately 45 days to prepare for JEE Mains 2025 and this is an ample amount of time to revise and go through the syllabus all over once again. Prioritize your time and divide it into 2 slots: 30 days for syllabus completion and another 15 days for revision and practice.

### 2. Target a Score

Scores are an important factor in deciding your future. Setting a target score and chasing it can motivate you to study hard. Breakdown subjects identify your strong and weak areas and calculate how much you can score in each subject to achieve your desired score. Here is the breakdown of scores and percentiles.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Marks** | **Percentile** |
| **100 – 150** | 90% – 95% |
| **160 – 190** | 96% – 99% |
| **200+** | 99%+ |

### 3. Make a Detailed Plan

Prepare a strategic plan for 45 days to avoid confusion on what to study on a particular day. Prepare a day-wise plan and include all three subjects on a day. Try to finish at least 1 chapter from each subject. Pareto principle can help you while making this plan, as it states that 80% of your efforts on 20% of the most important things will yield you maximum results. So focus on important topics in each chapter.

### 4. Daily Targets

Maintaining daily targets and constantly achieving them leads to a dopamine hit and will ensure you study more daily. Also, daily targets help us focus on important things at a time without worrying about syllabus completion.

### 5. Revision

Completing the syllabus is important but revision helps the concepts stay fresh in our mind during the exam. Revise frequently and identify the knowledge gaps to focus more on those topics. Flashcards are one of the efficient ways to revise regularly. Some digital applications provide flashcards for each topic, you can also use them to save time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **What Should be the Key Focus During JEE Main 2025 Revision?**

Focus on important concepts, high-weightage topics, and solving previous years’ papers and mock tests.

### 2. **How Much Time Should I Dedicate to Revision Before JEE Main?**

Dedicate the last 2-3 weeks for focused revision, prioritizing weak areas and practice.

### 3. **Should I Focus more on Theory or Problem-Solving During Revision?**

Start with theory and key concepts, then focus more on problem-solving and practicing questions.

### 4. **How Can I Effectively Manage Time During JEE Main Revision?**

Create a daily study schedule, prioritize topics, and balance between subjects and practice.

### 5. **How can Mock Tests Aid in JEE Main Revision?**

Mock tests improve time management, build exam confidence, and help identify weak areas for improvement.
