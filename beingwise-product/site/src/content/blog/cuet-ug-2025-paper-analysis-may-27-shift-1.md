## Student Initial Reaction for CUET Exam: May 27th Shift 1 Exam

Know what students after the CUET UG 2025 shift 1 exam held on May 27th 2025:

**1. Riya Sharma (GAT & English):**
“The General Aptitude section was mostly reasoning-based and manageable, but the GK questions were unexpectedly tough. English was straightforward, though some vocabulary questions were tricky.”

**2. Arjun Mehta (Mathematics):**
“Math felt easier compared to previous shifts. Calculus and matrices were prominent, with direct questions. Time management was easier due to fewer lengthy problems.”

**3. Sneha Verma (Political Science):**
“The paper was balanced, focusing on post-Cold War topics and India’s foreign relations. Match-the-following and chronological order questions were common, aligning well with NCERT content.”

**4. Aman Gupta (Hindi):**
“Hindi was straightforward with familiar topics like Sandhi, Samas, and Alankar. The passages were clear, and the questions matched our preparations.”

**5. Priya Nair (English):**
“English was mostly easy, but one-word substitutions and idioms required extra thought. The reading passages were engaging, and sentence rearrangement questions were simple.”

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ********What was the overall difficulty level of the CUET UG 2025 May 27 Shift 1 exam?********

Most students found the exam balanced and manageable, with reasoning-based GAT and easy Mathematics. English and Political Science were easy to moderate.

### 2.**************************Were there any major changes in the CUET UG 2025 exam pattern this year?**************************

No, the exam followed the standard pattern with three sections: Language, Domain-Specific, and General Test, each with 50 questions in 60 minutes.

### **3. ******************************What documents were required on the CUET UG 2025 exam day?********************************

Students had to carry their admit card, a valid photo ID, a passport-size photo, and a PwD certificate (if applicable).

### **4. **********************Were there any technical issues reported during the May 27 Shift 1 exam?************************

No significant technical glitches were reported in Shift 1. The exam was conducted smoothly at most centres.

### 5. ************What items were allowed and prohibited in the CUET UG exam hall?************

Allowed items included a printed admit card, valid ID, pen, transparent water bottle, and face mask (if permitted). Electronic gadgets, notes, and metal accessories were not allowed.
