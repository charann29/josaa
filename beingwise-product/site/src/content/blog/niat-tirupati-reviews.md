## Frequently Asked Questions

### **1. Where is NIAT in Tirupati Located?**

NIAT has collaborated with the Annamacharya University in Tirupati and is located in Boyanapalli, Andhra Pradesh.

### **2. What are the fees for NIAT Upskilling Program in Tirupati?**

The fees for NIAT Upskilling Program and the BTech CSE in Collaborating University in Tirupati range from INR 2 Lakhs to INR 4.5 lakhs per annum. However, the total fee is INR 8 to 18 Lakhs for 4 years duration.

### **4. NIAT is affiliated with which University in Tirupati?**

NIAT has a collaboration with the Annamacharya University in Tirupati.

### **5. What is the course duration at NIAT Tirupati?**

**NIAT Training Centre in Tirupati doesn’t offer any course as it is not a standalone University.** It offers Industry Ready Upskilling program. However, students can receive UGC approved BTech degree from the collaborating University (Annamacharya University). The total duration for the upskilling program along with the BTech CSE is 4 years.

### 6. What is NAT? How much is the registration fee for NAT?

NAT stands for NxtWave Assessment Test which is an enrollment exam for upskilling program at NIAT. However, students need to attend a mandatory 1:1 counselling session after NAT. Based on that, NIAT will send the names of the qualified students (with their written consent) to Annamacharya University (Collaborating University). Admission to the BTech CSE course in the Annamacharya University according to the rules and regulations of the University.
The one time, non-refundable registratin fee for NAT is INR 1200.
