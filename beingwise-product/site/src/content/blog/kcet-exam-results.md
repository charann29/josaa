**KCET 2025 Exam Results**: The KCET Exam Results are released on 24.05.2025 by the Karnataka Examination Authorities (KEA). Candidates can check the results by visiting the official website of KEA, [kea.kar.nic.in](https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea/)

The KEA will release a Spot Rank link on its official website on May 26th, 2025(Tentative). The Spot rank is for candidates whose results were withheld for not entering their 12th Class Marks (Qualifying exam) previously in the marks entry portal.

Candidates must enter their CET Number and Date of Birth to check their Spot Rank. To do so, visit the spot rank portal link provided below.

The Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) 2025 is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA). It was held on April 16th and 17th, 2025. Biology and Mathematics were held on April 18th, whereas Physics and Chemistry were held on April 19th. Candidates can download their results from the official website.

## KCET 2025 Results: Steps to Download the Score Card

**Step 1:** Visit the KCET 2025 Official Website: [kea.kar.nic.in](https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in/kea/)

**Step 2:** The KCET 2025 Results Link will be activated on the Home Page. Click on the link to proceed.

**Step 3:** The candidate needs to enter their login credentials like the Registration Number and the first four characters of their name, and click on the ‘Submit’ button.

**Step 4:** The candidate’s KCET 2025 Results will be displayed on the screen.

**Step 5:** Take a printout of the Rank Card and preserve it for future reference.

## Details mentioned on the KCET 2025 Results Card

After downloading the KCET Results, the candidates should check the following details mentioned on the KCET 2025 Results Card:

- Name of the Candidate
- Roll Number of the Candidate
- The candidate’s subject-wise scores
- Total Marks obtained in the KCET 2025 exam
- Secured rank by the candidate

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. How is the KCET rank calculated?

The calculation of the KCET rank includes the weightage of 50% KCET score and 50% 12th board marks scored by the candidate.

### 2. On what basis a candidate’s KCET Rank is calculated?

A candidate’s KCET Rank is calculated based on the following factors:
– The Candidate’s KCET Marks
– The candidate’s 12th Board Marks
– The candidate’s Category
– Normalization of Marks

### 3. What details do the candidates require to check their KCET 2025 Results?

Candidates must enter their login credentials, such as their Registration Number and the first four characters of their Name.
