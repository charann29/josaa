## **Difference between CAT and GMAT for IIM Admissions**

IIMs’ steadfast dedication to accepting GMAT shows their push toward student diversity and support for experienced professionals and international candidates in their prestigious programmes. The difference between these exams helps candidates make better choices:

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Aspect** | **GMAT** | **CAT** |
| Validity | Scores are valid for 5 years | Scores are valid for 1 year |
| Difficulty Level | Considered easier; even the toughest GMAT questions are often easier than CAT’s medium | Considered harder; questions are tougher and more complex |
| Testing Flexibility | Year-round testing slots, ideal for working professionals | Held once a year on a fixed date |
| Recognition | Accepted by 7,000+ business programmes globally (incl. IIMs, many top global schools) | Recognised mainly in India, primarily by IIMs and some B-schools |
| Application Focus | Best for candidates with international MBA/career goals | Best for those aiming to study and work in India |

## **How to Apply to IIMs with a GMAT Score**

Getting into IIMs through GMAT needs careful planning and precise execution. A well-executed application process can boost your chances of getting into these prestigious institutions.

### **Step-by-step application process**

The IIM application process happens in several stages:

1. Take the GMAT exam – Book your test early since Pearson VUE must report scores directly
2. Research programme options – Pick programmes that match your career goals and qualifications
3. Complete online application – Use the application portal on each IIM’s official website
4. Pay application fees – You’ll need to pay non-refundable fees ranging from ₹17,461.60 for IIM Mumbai to USD 150 for international candidates at IIM Calcutta
5. Submit required documents – Send all needed documentation
6. Await shortlisting – Selected candidates get interview invitations
7. Attend interviews – These usually have a Personal Interview (PI) and sometimes an Analytical Writing Test (AWT)
8. Receive final decision – Schools announce admission offers 4-6 weeks after interviews

IIM Ahmedabad’s PGPX programme runs three application rounds with specific deadlines and interview schedules.

### **Tips for a strong application**

A compelling application needs attention to detail and good planning:

- Start early – Give yourself 4-8 weeks minimum to create a quality application
- Craft a cohesive narrative – Your resume, essays, and interview answers should tell one story
- Be authentic – Show your real self rather than trying to impress
- Seek feedback – Ask mentors or professional consultants to review before you submit
- Prepare for interviews – You should expect questions about current affairs and your future plans
- Track deadlines – Early round applications have better admission chances

Many candidates don’t realise how much time they need to build a strong application. Starting early gives you enough time to handle unexpected challenges.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Can I get into IIM with GMAT?

Yes, you can get into several IIMs with a GMAT score. IIMs accept GMAT scores, such as IIM Ahmedabad, IIM Bangalore, IIM Calcutta, IIM Lucknow, IIM Kozhikode, and others for their executive management programs and, in some cases, for international or non-resident Indian applicants to their flagship MBA courses. The required GMAT scores are typically competitive, often above 700, and candidates must also meet work experience and academic requirements. Therefore, if you have a strong GMAT score and fulfil the additional criteria, you can pursue admission to top IIMs, as many IIMs accept GMAT as a valid entrance test for specific programs.

### 2. What is the GMAT score validity period in IIMs?

Most IIMs accept GMAT scores, which are valid for five years from the application deadline. IIM Calcutta’s PGPEX wants scores from within 36 months of application. Many IIMs now accept both traditional GMAT and new GMAT Focus Edition scores. Top IIMs usually look for 700+ scores in their executive programmes.

### 3. Which IIMs accept GMAT scores for admission in 2025?

Several IIMs accept GMAT scores, primarily for executive MBA programmes and international applicants. These include IIM Ahmedabad, IIM Bangalore, IIM Calcutta, IIM Lucknow, IIM Indore, and IIM Kozhikode, among others.

### 4.  How does work experience factor into IIM admissions via GMAT?

Work experience requirements vary by programme. Executive MBA programmes typically require 4-5 years of post-graduation work experience, with some accepting candidates with up to 12 years of experience. Regular MBA programmes may have minimal or no work experience requirements for international applicants.

### 5. What are the key differences between CAT and GMAT for IIM admissions?

MAT scores are valid for 5 years compared to CAT’s 1-year validity. GMAT offers year-round testing and is generally considered easier than CAT. While CAT is primarily recognised within India, GMAT opens doors to international business schools as well.
