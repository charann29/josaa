TG EAPCET 2025 also known as TS EAMCET is a state level entrance examination conducted by JNTUH on behalf of TGCHE for admission to Engineering, Agriculture & Pharmacy programs. You need to qualify TG EAPCET eligibility criteria 2025 to appear for the entrance examination.

**The application process for TS EAMCET will begin on 1st March 2025**. Application is in online mode with a registration fee of INR 900 for General candidates and INR 500 for Reserved candidates. The **registration deadline for TS EAMCET 2025 is 4th April 2025**. and the exam for the UG Engineering courses will be held from 2nd May 2025 to 5th May 2025 in two shifts.

## Eligibility Criteria Based on Educational Qualification

Candidates applying for various **Engineering courses** must have passed 10+2 from the Board of Intermediate Education, Telangana with PCM- Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry or PCB- Biology, Physics, Chemistry for Pharma course. The minimum marks required is 45% for the unreserved category and 40% for the reserved category.

Students enrolled from the academic year 2000-2002 onwards must also pass bridge courses administered by the conducting body or any other recognized equivalent examination.

## Based on Nationality and Age Limit

Candidates need to check their nationality and age limit to be eligible for TG EAMCET exam 2025. Check the below detailed eligible criteria based for the entrance examination.

### TG EAPCET Eligibility Criteria 2025: Nationality

To be eligible for the TS EAMCET 2025 entrance examination, you need to be of Indian Nationality or a Person of Indian Origin (PIO) / Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) Card Holder.

### Domicile

Candidates must be from the state of Telangana and meet the local or non-local status requirements as specified in the Telangana Education Institutions (Regulations of Admission) Order, 1974, and its subsequent amendments.

#### Local Candidate Definition

Candidates who have resided or attended an educational institute of Telangana for a minimum of four continuous years up to their qualifying exam will be considered local candidates.

### TG EAPCET Eligibility Criteria 2025: Age Limit

For Engineering and Pharmacy courses, you need to complete 16 years of age, as of 31st December of the year of admission. However, there is **no upper age limit is specified for Engineering**.

Moreover, for the DPharma course, the age limit needs to be 17 years as on 31st December at the year of admission.

The upper age limit for candidates from reserved categories seeking admission in B.Tech programs such as Dairy Technology, Agricultural Engineering, or Food Technology is 25 years.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **********What is the application timeline for TG EAPCET 2025 entrance examination?**********

TG EAPCET 2025 application timeline is from 1st March 2025 to 4th April 2025. However, you can also submit the application form after the deadline with late fee till 24th April 2025.

### 2. **********Who is eligible for the TS EAMCET 2025 exam?**********

Students of Indian Nationality and residing in Telangana are eligible for TG EAPCET 2025 entrance examination. However, students need to have 10+2 with 45% marks in PCM to be eligible and 40% marks are required for reserved category.

### 3. **********What is the TG EAPCET exam?**********

Telangana Engineering, Agriculture & Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (TG EAPCET 2025 is a state level entrance examination conducted by JNTUH. It is a perquisite for admission to various Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy programs in different Private and Government colleges in Telangana.

### 4. **Can I appear in TS EAMCET if I belong from West Bengal in the UG Engineering courses?**

No, only students residing in Telangana are eligible to appear in TS EAMCET/TG EAPCET entrance examination. However, students belonging from West Bengal can appear for JEE Main or WBJEE entrance examinations for BTech programs in colleges all over India and in West Bengal, respectively.

### 5. **Who is the conducting body of TG EAPCET entrance examination?**

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH) on behalf of Telangana Council of Higher Education (TGCHE) conducts TG EAPCET/TS EAMCET 2025 entrance examination for admission to various Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy programs in different Private and Government colleges.

### 6. ******Can I apply for multiple courses through TG EAPCET?******

Yes, you can apply for multiple courses through the TG EAPCET exam after qualifying the eligibility criteria. Though you have to separately fill the application form for each course and pay the registration fees.

### 7. **Where can I check TS EAMCET eligibility criteria 2025?**

### 8. **What is the minimum age limit of B.Tech admissions through TS EAMCET 2025?**

Candidates need to be 16 years of age as of 31st December of the year of admission for the Engineering courses through TS EAMCET 2025. However, no upper age limit is specified.

### 9. **What are the domicile requirements for TG EAPCET 2025?**

Candidates need to be of Indian Nationality or Persons of Indian Origin (PIO) / Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) Card Holders to be eligible for TS EAMCET 2025. Moreover, the eligibility criteria also required the candidates to be from the state of Telangana and meet the local or non-local status requirements.

### 10. **Who qualifies as a local candidate under the TS EAMCET 2025 eligibility requirements?**

Local candidates are those who have resided or attended an educational institute of Telangana for a minimum of four continuous years up to their qualifying exam.
