## **Is NIAT in Mangalore Recognised or Accredited?**

NIAT (NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies) Mangalore is an upskilling program, not a technical institute, college, or university. As such, NIAT does not hold any independent recognition or accreditation from bodies such as NAAC, UGC, or AICTE, nor is it required to.

### **Where Does the Accreditation Come From?**

The academic credibility comes entirely from NIAT’s collaborating university, Yenepoya University, a NAAC A+ accredited deemed-to-be university. Yenepoya University independently confers the UGC-recognised B.Tech degree in Computer Science Engineering with a specialisation in Artificial Intelligence to students who meet their academic eligibility criteria.

### **What Does the NIAT Training Centre in Mangalore Contribute?**

While Yenepoya University provides the academic degree and campus infrastructure, NIAT delivers a comprehensive upskilling program through its Training Centre on the university campus in Mangalore. This includes regularly updated skill maps and training modules built around the latest technological trends, hands-on project-based learning, and industry-ready competencies, all aimed at making students job-market-ready upon graduation.
