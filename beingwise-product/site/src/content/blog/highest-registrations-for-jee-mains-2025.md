The National Testing Agency has accepted the applications for JEE Mains 2025 from **October 28th, 2025** to **November 22nd, 2025**. Registrations for the January session of JEE Mains 2025 have surged compared to the previous year’s registrations for January sessions. The report stated that 13.8 lakh aspirants have registered to take the exam in the January session. However, the official data of the total number of registered candidates will be announced officially by the concerned authority. You can check the previous year’s trends below.

## Total Number of Registrations for JEE Mains 2025

NTA opened the application window for candidates on October 28th, 2025, and accepted applications from the candidates till **November 26th, 2024**. According to sources approximately 13.8 lakh students have registered to take the Jee mains 2025 exam in January. This is an exceptional surge of almost 1.6 lakh students compared to last year’s trends.

## JEE Mains 2025 Previous Year’s Trends

There is a surge in almost 1.6 lakh candidates compared to last year as this year’s January session received **13.8 lakh** **applications** compared to last year’s 12.1 lakh registrations. These trends signify the increasing demands for quality education and to get into prestigious institutes like IITs.

- Scores are widely accepted across other institutes in India.
