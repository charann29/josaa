### 4. Arunachala College of Engineering for Women

Arunachala College of Engineering for Women, located in Manavilai, Vellichanthai, stands out as one of Tamil Nadu’s leading women’s engineering colleges. Accredited with a NAAC ‘A’ grade and an impressive CGPA of 3.24, the college offers six B.E. programs including ECE, CSE, EEE, Civil, IT, and the recently introduced AI & Data Science course.

Known for its strong academic foundation and consistent placement success, the college provides a supportive environment through dedicated hostels, advanced labs, and structured mentorship, making it a preferred choice for aspiring female engineers in the region.

### 6. Ponjesly College of Engineering

Ponjesly College of Engineering, located in Neerody near Nagercoil, is a prominent private engineering institution established in 1998 and affiliated with Anna University. It stands out for its solid placement performance and active industry tie-ups. The college has earned NAAC A+ accreditation and boasts NBA accreditation for key programs like Mechanical and Electronics & Communication Engineering.

In the 2023–24 placement season, approximately 68% of B.Tech students (157 out of 256) secured job offers, with a median salary package of ₹3.0 LPA. Spread across a 20-acre campus, Ponjesly is equipped with advanced labs in automation and computing, extensive sports infrastructure, a well-stocked library, and separate hostel facilities—positioning it among the top engineering choices in Kanyakumari.

### 9. Noorul Islam Centre for Higher Education

Noorul Islam Centre for Higher Education (NICHE), situated in Kumaracoil, Kanyakumari, is a private deemed-to-be university known for its academic excellence in engineering and technology. Originally established in 1989 and granted deemed university status in 2008, NICHE is accredited with an ‘A’ grade by NAAC. It has earned national recognition for its academic and research contributions, including a notable 66th rank in the NIRF Engineering category in 2016.

The 47-acre campus is equipped with advanced research laboratories in fields like aeronautics and bioengineering, a spacious central library, and residential hostels. NICHE graduates enjoy promising placement opportunities, particularly in core engineering sectors, underscoring the institution’s focus on quality and innovation.
