## How to Prepare for AP-EAPCET 2025 in 30 Days?

Students are advised to cover all the topics listed in the syllabus before sticking to this 30-day preparation plan. This 30-day preparation plan helps you thoroughly cover all the important topics and revise them through mock tests and regular revision methods. The schedule given below is ideal to cover all the subjects and to keep students ahead of the competition. Make sure to modify the preparation plan at your convenience.

## Tips to Remain Calm And Relaxed During Last 30 Days of  AP-EAPCET 2025

As the AP-EAPCET 2025 draws near, it’s crucial to remain calm and focused for success. The last 30 days are crucial, so here are some strategies to help you stay relaxed and perform at your best:

- **Create a Study Plan:** Organize your study time by focusing on both strong and weak areas.
- **Take Mock Tests Daily:** Replicate exam conditions to enhance your speed and accuracy.
- **Incorporate Short Breaks**: Refresh your mind by taking regular, short breaks to prevent burnout.
- **Maintain a Positive Mindset:** Stay confident and trust in the preparation you’ve done so far.
- **Eat Nutritious Meals and Stay Hydrated:** Good nutrition will keep your energy levels high and support concentration.
- **Avoid Last-Minute Studying:** Focus on reviewing rather than learning new topics in the final days.
- **Practice Stress-Relief Techniques:** Use meditation or deep breathing exercises to ease anxiety and stay calm.

### 1. **How should I prepare for AP-EAPCET 2025?**

Cover all the important topics listed out in the syllabus, give regular mocks and revise all the concepts regularly. Analyse mocks and work on your weak areas.

### 2. **What is the exam date for AP-EAPCET 2025?**

AP-EAPCET exam for 2025 will be conducted from May 21st to 27th.

### 3. **Who are eligible candidates to appear for AP-EAMCET 2025?**

Candidates who passed their class 12th or equivalent exam from a recognised board or university are eligible to appear for AP-EAPCET 2025.

### 4. **What is the exam pattern for AP-EAPCET 2025?**

AP-EAPCET exam will be conducted for 160 marks divided across three subjects, i.e. Maths – 80 marks, Physics – 40 Marks, and Chemistry – 40 Marks. There will be no negative marking in AP-EAPCET.

### 5. **What is the full form of AP-EAMCET?**

AP-EAPCET stands for Andhra Pradesh Engineering Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test.
