## VITEEE 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Students preparing for the Vellore Institute of Technology entrance exam are required to satisfy the following eligibility criteria to appear for the VITEEE 2026 exams:

- Indian Residents, Non-Resident Indians (NRI), PIOs, and OCI candidates can apply for the VITEEE exams
- Candidates must be born on or after July 1, 2004.
- Must have passed Class 12 exam or equivalent exams with mandatory subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Maths/Biology.
- General Category students must have obtained 60% aggregate in the 10+2 level exam.
- Reserved Category students must have obtained 50% aggregate.

## VITEEE 2026 Application Process

The VITEEE application process is conducted online via the official VITEEE website. The application window starts in October and ends in April. For VITEEE 2026, the application started on October 24, 2026 and ended on April 12, 2026. Students need to pay INR 1,350 as the VITEEE 2026 application fee via online netbanking. Read the following steps to know more about the VIT Entrance Exam 2026 Application process:

- Open [viteee.vit.ac.in](http://viteee.vit.ac.in) and click on the New User Register here link.
- Complete registration by entering name, DOB, gender, nationality, email, and mobile number for registration.
- Once the registration is done, save the generated application number.
- Log in with your saved application number and password.
- Enter all the required personal and academic details.
- Select the preferred VITEEE exam centre, and choose an option from Maths or Biology
- Upload the scanned required documents, Photo, and Signature for the VIT Entrance Exam 2026.
- Pay the application fee online and submit the application.
- Download the confirmed page and save it for future reference.

After submitting the application form, proceed with the VITEEE exam slot booking in early April to confirm the VITEEE exam dates.

## VITEEE Exam Slot Booking

VITEEE slot booking dates for VITEEE 2026 exams were scheduled from April 17 (6:00 PM) to April 19 (5:00 PM) . Candidates ​who registered for the VIT Entrance Exam 2026 needed to book their exam slots through the VITEEE online test booking system portal. It is conducted on first-cum, first-served basis. VITEEE Exam Slot booking process allows candidates to select their preferred exam dates, time, and cities after submitting their application. Post this booking, the VITEEE hall ticket is released 48 hours before the chosen slot. Check the following steps on how to book an exam slot for the VIT Entrance exam 2026:

- Visit the [official website](https://viteee.vit.ac.in/) of the VIT entrance exam
- Log in using the VITEEE application number, password, and registered email ID.
- Choose from the available options of exam city, date, and time slot.
- Confirm the selected options and book the exam slot.
- A confirmation email will be sent.
- Download the admit card from the OTBS portal two days before the selected exam slot.

Students are not allowed to make any changes after the booking. Candidates are advised to book early to secure preferred exam slots.
