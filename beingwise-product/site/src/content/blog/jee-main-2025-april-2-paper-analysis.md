**The JEE Mains Session 2 is scheduled from 2nd April 2025 to 8th April 2025**. The Admit Cards for the students appearing for the exam from 2nd,3rd, and 4th April 2025 have been released. The candidates can download the admit card from the official website.

The [JEE Main 2025 exam](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/), to be conducted on April 2nd, is expected to have varying difficulty levels across different shifts, as is typical for the exam. **Based on past trends, the Mathematics section was expected to be lengthy and moderately complex,** with questions requiring strong conceptual understanding and problem-solving skills. Physics generally had a mix of theoretical and numerically based questions, with some demanding the application of multiple concepts. Chemistry was likely the easiest among the three subjects, with NCERT-based questions dominating, especially in Inorganic Chemistry.

## JEE Main Dress Code and Guidelines

The **[JEE Main 2025 dress code and guidelines](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/jee-main-dress-code-for-males-and-females/)** are set by the **National Testing Agency (NTA)** to ensure a smooth and fair examination process. Candidates are advised to follow the following guidelines.

- **Wear light-coloured clothes with half sleeves.**
- Avoid outfits with large buttons or multiple pockets, which may lead to security concerns.
- Avoid wearing **shoes with thick soles. Clothing with metallic elements** is not allowed, so it is recommended that you wear **open-toed sandals or slippers**.
- Accessories like **watches, jewellery, and electronic devices** are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall.
- Candidates must carry their **JEE Main admit card, a valid photo ID, and a passport-size photograph,** as instructed.
