## How to Fill Out the MHT CET 2026 Registration Form?

Refer to the following to check out how to register for the MHT CET 2026.

**Step 1: Eligibility Check.**

Candidates must check the MHT CET eligibility criteria before proceeding with the 2026 registration process. Applicants must satisfy these criteria as specified by the MHT CET Cell.

For engineering courses, the engineering eligibility criteria include Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class 12. Candidates who do not satisfy the eligibility criteria may not be permitted to register.

**Step 2: Visit the Official Portal.**

To start the online registration process, visit the official MHT CET website and access the MHT CET login page. New registrants need to provide basic personal information first.

After completing the registration process, a MHT CET registration number is generated, which will be used for all subsequent logins and MHT CET processes.

**Step 3: Fill the Registration Form.**

After successfully logging in with the new MHT CET login details, fill out the registration form with the correct personal, academic, and contact information.

Candidates also need to upload scanned copies of documents as per the instructions during the 2026 registration process. Verify all the details before proceeding to the next step.

**Step 4: Upload Documents and Pay Fees.**

After entering all the details, upload all the necessary documents in the prescribed format. Pay the MHT CET 2026 registration fees after successfully uploading the documents.

The registration fee differs according to the category of the candidate. The registration is completed after the successful payment of the fees.

**Step 5: Review and Submit**

Candidates must read carefully all the details logged in before submitting the final application. The registered MHT CET registration form has to be submitted by the MHT CET registration last date.

The latest MHT CET 2026 is of great significance because the applications received after the latest date might not be accepted. Once this is done, save and/or print the confirmation page and retain their MHT CET registration number somewhere safe to use in the future.

## How to Prepare for MHT CET 2026?

Aspirants may check out the subject-wise MHT CET preparation tips given below:
**Physics**

- Pay less attention to heavy theory and more to formulas and simple concepts.
- Solve numerical problems on a daily basis, particularly in Class 12 subjects.
- Figure out significant formulas, update them on a regular basis and create a brief formula journal.
- Practice solving MHT CET previous year question papers to know the pattern.

**Chemistry**

- Preparation will be divided into Physical, Organic and Inorganic Chemistry.
- In the case of Physical Chemistry, have practice numbers and equations.
- Clarity in reactions and mechanisms in Organic Chemistry.
- In the case of Inorganic Chemistry, either revise the NCERT facts and tables several times over.

**Mathematics**

- The most important thing is to practice solving problems.
- Speed and accuracy: Maths scores more points per question.
- Correct formulas often and enlist shortcuts.
- Take mock tests and practice tests frequently.

**Biology (for PCB students)**

- Read NCERT step by step, particularly diagrams and definitions.
- Pay attention to significant words, procedures, and illustrations.
- Study MCQs every day to enhance memory.
- Enhance every now and then, since Biology is theory-prolific.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **Q1. What is the MHT CET registration last date for 2026?**

### ****Q2. Where can I complete MHT CET online registration?****

You can complete MHT CET registration on the MHT CET official website managed by the MHT CET Cell.

### **Q3. What documents are required for MHT CET registration?**

Candidates need scanned copies of photographs, signatures, and academic documents as per the registration guidelines.

### Q4. How can I download the MHT CET admit card?

The admit card can be downloaded through the MHT CET login using your registration number and password.

### Q5. What are the eligibility criteria for MHT CET engineering courses?

As per the MHT CET eligibility criteria for engineering, candidates must have passed Class 12 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.

### Q6. Will MHT CET be conducted twice in 2026?

Yes, from 2026, MHT CET Cell will conduct the exam in two sessions in a year. Students are allowed to appear in both sessions, and the best score will be considered for admission.

### Q7. Is there any negative marking in MHT CET 2026?

No, there is no negative marking in MHT CET as per the latest exam pattern released by the MHT CET Cell.

### Q8. What is the exam mode of MHT CET 2026?

MHT CET 2026 is conducted as an online computer-based test (CBT) at allotted exam centres.

### Q9. How many attempts are allowed for MHT CET?

There is no limit on the number of attempts for MHT CET. If a candidate meets the eligibility criteria, he/she can attempt the exam as many times as wanted.

### Q10. Is MHT CET based on the Class 12 syllabus?

The MHT CET syllabus is based on both Class 11 and Class 12 Maharashtra State Board syllabus, with more weightage given to Class 12 topics.
