Joy University is a multidisciplinary private institution situated in the Kanyakumari district of Tamil Nadu. Established under the Tamil Nadu Private Universities Act, 2019, the university operates under the aegis of the Selvam Educational and Charitable Trust. This article presents a detailed overview of the educational programs offered by Joy University at the undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels.

## **Programme Duration & Eligibility**

- B.Tech: 4 years (Full time)
  - Eligibility: 10+2 (HSE/CBSE/ICSE or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, Maths, and English
  - Minimum Aggregate: 60% in PCM; at least 50% in each subject
  - Entrance: JOYAT (university test) or valid JEE Main/TNEA/CUET score; direct admission available for JEE Main scores >80 percentile
- M.Tech: 2 years
  - Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech degree with minimum 60% in relevant field; entrance required (JOYAT/GATE).
