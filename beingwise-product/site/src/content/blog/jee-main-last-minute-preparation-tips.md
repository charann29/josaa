JEE Mains and JEE Advanced are two important stages of the exam to pave the way for aspirants’ IIT dreams. Almost 10 to 14 lakh students attend Jee Mains annually to seek admission into prestigious institutions like IITs and NITs. Considering the difficulty and [JEE new exam pattern changes](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/jee-mains-major-exam-pattern-changes-announced-2025/) students require the right amount of preparation and resources to crack these two stages. Although coaching for JEE Mains is helpful, however, it is important to follow some tips to ensure smooth preparation and success in exams. This article will provide some important tips and tricks for aspirants preparing for JEE mains 2025.

The JEE Main is an objective-type exam conducted online, consisting of 75 questions in total. These are distributed equally across three subjects: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, with 25 questions in each subject. Of these, 20 questions are Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs), and 5 are numerical-based questions. Follow these tips as taking small steps will make a huge difference in your preparation and significantly boost your score in the exam.

## Prepare for JEE Main This Way

- **Draft a Study Plan:** Preparing for JEE mains 2025 is a sprint and setting small, realistic targets to achieve can help maintain focus. Manageable targets will help maintain momentum in your preparation.

- **Prioritize Self-study:** Effective self-study enhances understanding by allowing you to explore topics at your own pace, raise questions, and clarify doubts. A distraction-free environment is key to maximizing productivity.

- **Manage your Time:** Plan your daily activities wisely as time management is crucial for balancing academics and personal life. It helps you complete the syllabus on time while ensuring enough rest and leisure.

- **Standardized Reference Materials:** Selecting the right study materials is crucial. Focus on books that improve comprehension and application, rather than rote memorization, to save time and boost your preparation.

- **Mock Tests:** Practicing JEE Main past papers and mock tests helps in understanding the exam format, and improving speed, accuracy, and confidence. It also helps identify areas for improvement.
- **Revise Regularly:** Prioritising Revision will ensure the content stays fresh in your mind. Revise important concepts regularly and practice numerals to sharpen your concepts.

## Tips for Last-Minute Preparation For Jee Main

Last-minute preparation for Jee Mains has to differ from a generalized preparation plan so as to ensure that your key concepts are clear. Avoid learning new concepts and focus on sharpening those concepts that you have already learned.

- **Clear All Your Doubts:** Always seek clarification from instructors or classmates when in doubt. A clear understanding of complex concepts helps you tackle similar problems more effectively during exams. Asking questions also strengthens your learning and improves retention.

- **Prioritise Revision:** Given the extensive syllabus, regular revision is essential to keep all topics fresh. Consistently review key concepts and practice problems to enhance your recall and understanding.
- **Stay Positive:** Maintaining a positive attitude is essential. Avoid stress, as it can hinder performance. Stay motivated, visualize success, and keep moving forward, even when facing challenges.

## Essential Exam Day Tips for JEE Main 2025

Optimizing your performance on your test day is important as it will get you the desired results for all your hard work. However, students often feel overwhelmed to perform on the exam because of stress and other factors. Here are some important tips to consider on your test day.

- **Focus on Health:** Feeling sick during the exam can be daunting. Focus on your health, prefer home food over junk foods a day before your exam.

- **Be on Time:** Plan your travel to ensure you will be there at the exam center at least 30 minutes before the test time.

- **Quick Review:** Glance your question paper quickly to help you strategize your time so as to perform efficiently.

- **Avoid Guesswork:** Although Guesswork will help you at times, the Jee Mains 2025 exam has negative markings for wrong answers so avoid guesswork. Relay on knowledge more than luck.

- **Perform your Best:** Give your best effort on every question your preparation and hard work are the keys to JEE Mains’s success.
