Students with a firm grasp of fundamentals and practised solving papers under time constraints found the paper manageable. Those who focused more on chemistry were able to boost their scores during this shift.

## **AP EAMCET 2025 Exam: May 21, 2025, Shift 2**

The **AP EAMCET 2025** exam held on **May 21, Shift 2**, was generally perceived as **moderate** in difficulty. In contrast, the **Chemistry** section was considered the easiest among the three, with direct and concept-based questions, particularly from **S-block** and **P-block elements**, allowing students to score efficiently. ## AP EAMCET 2025 Dress Code Guidelines

To maintain a fair examination environment and avoid malpractice, candidates appearing for the AP EAMCET 2025 exam must strictly follow the prescribed dress code. The attire should be simple, without any patterns, logos, or decorative elements. Specifically, candidates are advised to wear plain, light-colored shirts, T-shirts, or kurtas. Half-sleeved tops are mandatory, as full sleeves may complicate security procedures. Simple pants, trousers, or leggings are recommended for bottoms, while cargo pants or jeans with multiple or hidden pockets should be avoided. Footwear should be open sandals or chappals; closed shoes or thick-soled footwear are discouraged. Jewellery and metallic accessories like rings, bracelets, earrings, or chains are prohibited. Additionally, using smartwatches, digital or fitness bands, and other electronic gadgets inside the exam hall is strictly prohibited. Candidates can bring their admit card, a valid photo ID, a transparent ballpoint pen, and a clear water bottle. Diabetic students may carry necessary medical supplies with prior approval. Items such as bags, wallets, notes, calculators, mobile phones, or

Electronic devices must not be brought into the exam room.

## AP EAMCET 2025 Result Details

The AP EAMCET 2025 exam results are expected to be declared in June 2025 after the engineering exams conclude between May 21 and May 27, 2025. Before the final result, the preliminary answer key will be released on May 28, offering candidates a chance to challenge any discrepancies until June 1. The final answer key is anticipated to be published by June 5. Once the results are declared, candidates can check their scores and download their rank cards from the official AP EAMCET website by entering their hall ticket and registration details. The rank card will provide detailed information, including the candidate’s name, subject-wise marks, total marks, qualifying status, overall rank, and category. Following the announcement of results, qualified candidates will be invited to participate in the counselling process, expected to begin in July 2025. The counselling involves verifying documents and selecting preferred courses and colleges, with seat allotments based on merit, category, and availability.

## AP EAMCET 2025 Counselling Process

The counselling for AP EAMCET 2025 is organised online by APSCHE and facilitates admission into various engineering, agriculture, and pharmacy courses offered by participating colleges across Andhra Pradesh. After the results are out, eligible candidates must register online on the counselling portal and pay the required counselling fee. Next, candidates must upload scanned copies of necessary documents such as the AP EAMCET rank card, hall ticket, marksheets for 10th and 12th standards, caste certificate (if applicable), and income certificate for verification purposes. Once the documents are verified, candidates can fill in their choices of colleges and courses in order of preference during the web options entry phase. After the option submission deadline, the authorities will allot seats based on the candidates’ rank, category, and choices made. To complete the admission formalities, candidates must then download the seat allotment order and report to the assigned college within the stipulated timeframe.
