## Branch-Wise Details of Malla Reddy Engineering College Curriculum

The Malla Reddy Engineering College curriculum stands out because of its extensive focus on academic depth and industry alignment. This ensures students graduate not just with degrees but with the skills required to thrive in the industry. By focusing on the building fundamentals in the first year, students will gain primary exposure to what they are going to learn, and they will build a strong foundation in their fundamentals.

Subjects in the second year are designed to ensure students gain a strong foundation in their core domain. In the third and fourth years, students will have to shift their focus towards the projects to learn and apply concepts practically. Malla Reddy Engineering College curriculum is designed by focusing completely on Outcome-based education, Choice-based credit system, and project-based learning. Let’s have a look at the branch-wise curriculum details at Malla Reddy Engineering College.

## Final Year Project and Internship Structure at Malla Reddy Engineering College

Internships are a mandatory aspect at Malla Reddy Engineering College. Through mandatory internships in the third and fourth years, students at Malla Reddy Engineering College are ensured to gain a real-world exposure, practical experience related to their core engineering fields. Students work closely with the startups, research organizations, etc and are made to understand the industry workflows, tools, and expectations.

In the fourth year, students will primarily work on the major project which serves as a culminatio of all their academic learning. Students are deployed into teams and are assigned a mentor/guide to support incase of any requirement.  Students are encouraged to solve real-world problems, innovate using technologies like AI, IoT, or civil simulations (based on branch), and document their work in detail. The final evaluation includes project submission, demo, and viva voce. Many students use this opportunity to publish research papers or build standout portfolios for placements or higher studies.
