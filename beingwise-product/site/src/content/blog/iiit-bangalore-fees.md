**IIIT Bangalore fees** for B.Tech are among the higher-end in the private deemed university segment. For the academic year 2026-27, the total tuition fee for the B.Tech programme is **₹23,82,000** across four years. This article covers the complete **IIIT Bangalore fee structure** for B.Tech, including semester-wise tuition, hostel charges, deposits, scholarships, cutoff ranks, and everything else a candidate needs to plan for the 2026 admission cycle.

## IIIT Bangalore Fees: Overview

The International Institute of Information Technology Bangalore is a Deemed to be University established in 1998. It is located in Electronic City Phase 1, Bengaluru, Karnataka. The table below summarises key institutional details.

| Parameter | Details |
| --- | --- |
| University Type | Deemed to be University (Private) |
| Established | 1998 |
| Location | Electronic City, Bengaluru, Karnataka |
| NAAC Accreditation | A+ |
| NIRF 2025 – Engineering | 69 |
| B.Tech Admission | JEE Main / JEE Advanced |
| Official Website | iiitb.ac.in |

**Note:** IIIT Bangalore admissions are NOT done through JoSAA. Candidates must apply separately through the official admissions portal at admissions.iiitb.net.

## IIIT Bangalore B.Tech Fees 2026: Semester-Wise Tuition

The **IIIT Bangalore B.Tech fees** follow a tiered structure. The tuition per semester increases as students progress through the programme. The fee structure below is as published on the official IIIT Bangalore website for the 2026-27 academic year.

| Year | Tuition Fee Per Semester |
| --- | --- |
| Year 1 | ₹2,76,000 |
| Year 2 | ₹2,76,000 |
| Year 3 | ₹3,04,000 |
| Year 4 | ₹3,35,000 |

The total tuition for the entire four-year B.Tech programme is **₹23,82,000**. This covers tuition only. Hostel, mess, and other charges are billed separately.

The fees of IIIT Bangalore are subject to revision from time to time, as stated in the official admissions notice. Candidates should verify the current figures directly at iiitb.ac.in before making payment decisions.

## IIIT Bangalore B.Tech Fee Structure: Branch-Wise

The **fee structure of IIIT Bangalore** is the same across all three B.Tech specialisations. There is no branch-wise fee difference for the 4-year B.Tech programme.

| Branch | Duration | Total Tuition Fee | Seats |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| B.Tech – Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) | 4 Years | ₹23,82,000 | 120 |
| B.Tech – Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) | 4 Years | ₹23,82,000 | 30 |
| B.Tech – Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (AI&DS) | 4 Years | ₹23,82,000 | 90 |

**Note on Data Science fees:** The **IIIT Bangalore data science course fees** refer to the B.Tech in AI&DS (Artificial Intelligence and Data Science). This programme carries the same total tuition of ₹23,82,000 as the other B.Tech branches. There is no separate standalone B.Tech in Machine Learning at IIIT Bangalore. Candidates searching for **IIIT Bangalore machine learning course fees** should note that ML is covered within the AI&DS curriculum under the same fee structure.

## IIIT Bangalore Hostel Fees 2026

Both the B.Tech and Integrated M.Tech programmes at IIIT Bangalore are **residential and compulsory**. Students must stay on campus. The hostel is not optional.

The **IIIT Bangalore hostel fees** for the B.Tech programme are as follows.

| Hostel Fee Component | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| Hostel and Campus Facility Fee | ₹39,000 per semester |
| Catering and Food Services (Mess) | ₹28,800 per semester |
| Medical Insurance | ₹3,000 per year |
| Library Deposit (One-Time, Refundable) | ₹4,000 |
| Hostel Caution Deposit (One-Time, Refundable) | ₹15,000 |

The library deposit and hostel caution deposit are paid once at the time of admission. Both are refunded at the time of graduation.

## IIIT Bangalore Admission: Entrance Exam and Cutoff

Admission to all B.Tech and Integrated M.Tech programmes at IIIT Bangalore is based on JEE Main or JEE Advanced. Candidates must have completed 10+2 with Mathematics and secured a first-class grade. Students with valid IMOTC or IOITC Olympiad camp selection may also be considered for up to 5 seats across programmes.

**IIIT Bangalore cutoff ranks** for 2025 admissions are given below.

| Programme | 2025 JEE Closing Rank (CRL) |
| --- | --- |
| B.Tech – CSE | 6,942 |
| B.Tech – AI&DS | 8,432 |
| B.Tech – ECE | 8,844 |
| Integrated M.Tech – CSE | 7,357 |
| Integrated M.Tech – ECE | 9,101 |

The **IIIT Bangalore ranking** in NIRF 2025 stands at #69 in the Engineering category. The **IIIT Bangalore NIRF ranking** has shown an improving trend over recent years, moving from #74 in 2024 to #69 in 2025. The institute is accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade.

IIIT Bangalore 2026 cutoffs will be published after the JEE Main 2026 results and counselling rounds. Candidates can use 2025 figures as a planning benchmark.

## IIIT Bangalore Campus

The **IIIT Bangalore campus** is located at Electronic City Phase 1 on Hosur Road, Bengaluru. The campus is situated opposite Infosys Gate 10. A new extension campus at Hosa Road has been set up to accommodate B.Tech and Integrated M.Tech 2026 students.

The main **IIIT Bangalore campus** houses laboratories, libraries, a sports complex, a medical centre, ATMs, a cafeteria, and student activity areas. The campus is fully residential for B.Tech and I.M.Tech students, with hostel accommodation mandatory for all enrolled students.

Research centres on campus include the Machine Intelligence and Robotics CoE (MINRO), the E-Health Research Centre (EHRC), and the Centre for IT and Public Policy (CITAPP), among others.

## Scholarships at IIIT Bangalore

Given the relatively high **IIIT Bangalore fees**, the institute offers several scholarship schemes to reduce the financial burden for eligible students.

**Utkarsha Scholarship:** This is the flagship merit scholarship. It is awarded to the top-ranked student admitted through JEE Main or JEE Advanced with an NTA CRL rank between 1 and 999. For B.Tech, it covers 100% tuition waiver for the first three years. For Integrated M.Tech, it covers the first four years.

**Dean’s Merit List Scholarship:** All students with a CGPA of 3.6 or above are eligible. The top 10% of students by CGPA receive this scholarship each year. No separate application is needed.

**Prerna Scholarship (for Girl Students):** All girl students admitted to B.Tech and Integrated M.Tech in CSE, ECE, and AI&DS are eligible. For 2026-27, 36 scholarships are available across all programmes. Selection is based on JEE rank within each programme.

**Student Benevolent Fund (SBF):** This is a need-based scholarship for students facing genuine financial hardship. Students whose parents’ annual income is below ₹11 lakhs and who are unable to source a student loan are eligible to apply.

**Corporate Scholarships:** IIIT Bangalore facilitates applications to corporate scholarships from companies like Reliance Foundation, Tata Foundation, Micron, Intel, HGS, and CGI when announced.

**AICTE Scholarship:** Applicable to Integrated M.Tech students who qualify GATE in their final year, as per AICTE norms.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What are the total IIIT Bangalore B.Tech fees for 4 years?

The total tuition fee for the 4-year B.Tech programme is ₹23,82,000 as per the 2026–27 fee structure. Including mandatory hostel and mess charges, the overall cost is approximately ₹29.55 lakhs.

### 2. What is the IIIT Bangalore B.Tech fee per semester?

The tuition fee per semester is ₹2,76,000 for Years 1 and 2, ₹3,04,000 for Year 3, and ₹3,35,000 for Year 4.

### 3. Is hostel compulsory at IIIT Bangalore?

Yes. Both the B.Tech and Integrated M.Tech programmes are fully residential. Students are required to stay in the hostel. The hostel fee is ₹39,000 per semester, while mess charges are ₹28,800 per semester.

### 4. What are the IIIT Bangalore Data Science course fees?

The Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AI&DS) programme at IIIT Bangalore has the same fee structure as the other B.Tech branches. The total tuition fee for the 4-year programme is ₹23,82,000.

### 5. What entrance exam is required for IIIT Bangalore B.Tech admission?

Admission to the B.Tech programme is based on JEE Main or JEE Advanced scores. IIIT Bangalore does not participate in JoSAA counselling, and candidates must apply directly through the institute’s admission portal.

### 6. What is the IIIT Bangalore NIRF ranking?

According to NIRF 2025, IIIT Bangalore is ranked 69th in the Engineering category. The institute is also accredited with an A+ grade by NAAC.

### 7. Are there any scholarships to reduce IIIT Bangalore fees?

Yes. IIIT Bangalore offers several scholarships, including the Utkarsha Scholarship for top JEE rank holders, the Prerna Scholarship for girl students, the Dean’s Merit List Scholarship for high CGPA performers, and financial assistance through the Student Benevolent Fund for eligible students.

### 8. Is the fee structure the same for CSE, ECE, and AI&DS at IIIT Bangalore?

Yes. The B.Tech fee structure is identical across Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), and Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI&DS). The total tuition fee for each programme is ₹23,82,000.
