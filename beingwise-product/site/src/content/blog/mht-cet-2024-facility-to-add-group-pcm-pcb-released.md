The Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell has issued a notification regarding the opportunity to add either the PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) or PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) group. The window for this facility is **open on the 27th  from 12:00 AM to 11:59 PM**. Only registered candidates are eligible to utilize this feature to add the alternative group. Candidates must pay applicable fees to add the group of their choice.

Registered Candidates can visit the official website of MHT CET 2024 at [cetcell.mahacet.org](http://cetcell.mahacet.org) to avail this opportunity.

The PCM exam are scheduled from May 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 9th, 10th, 11th, 15th, 16th, and 17th, 2024. The PCB exam is ongoing and it will be completed by April 30th, 2024.

The MHT CET exam is open to aspiring candidates seeking admission to undergraduate courses in Maharashtra. The exam pattern typically consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs). The duration for both the PCM and PCB exams is 180 minutes. The MHT CET exam consists of 200 marks. The exam will be conducted in a computer-based test as online.

Candidates preparing for this exam should stay updated with any further announcements by regularly checking the official website of the Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell.

## How to Add Group PCM/ PCB?

To add the PCM/PCB group, candidates can follow these step-by-step process:

- Go to the official website of MHT CET 2024 at [cetcell.mahacet.org](http://cetcell.mahacet.org).
- Log in using your credentials, such as your “Application Number” and “Password.”
- After logging in, you will be directed to the Application form page. On the top menu, you will be able to see options like “Home” and “Application form.”
- Click on “Home” to access additional options such as “Important Links”.
- Choose the option “Add Subject Group” or “Change Subject Group” to either add a subject or switch groups.
- Complete the process by paying the application fee and then click on “Submit.”
