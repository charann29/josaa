The MHT CET 2025 Syllabus has been released by the Maharastra State Common Entrance Test Cell. The MHT CET exam is based on subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology.  For the PCM stream, the subjects are physics, chemistry, and  Mathematics. And for the PCB stream the subjects are physics, chemistry, and Biology. The MHT CET 2025 Syllabus for both PCM and PCB streams is available in PDF format only. The Syllabus of the MHT CET exam is based on the class 11th and 12th syllabus. Candidates can download the MHT CET syllabus 2025 pdf by visiting the official website cetcell.mahacet.org.

The exam contains approximately 20% weightage from the class 11th syllabus and 80% weightage from the class 12th syllabus. There is no negative marking in the MHT CET exam. The paper difficulty for PCM will be the same as the JEE Main exam and for PCB will be the same as the NEET.

The Maharastra State Common Entrance Test Cell has announced the exam dates for both Engineering and Pharmacy courses. The MHT CET exam is tentatively from **April 22, 2025 to May 17, 2025**.

## Syllabus for MHT CET 2025

The questions will be based on:

1. The complete syllabus of class 12th of 2024 -25 of Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics subjects prescribed by the State Council of Education Research and Training, Maharashtra.
2. The syllabus of standard 11th of 2022-23 prescribed by the State Council of Education Research and Training, Maharashtra is mentioned below.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Subject** | **Chapter or Units of Standard 11th Syllabus** |
| Physics | Motion in a plane, Laws of Motion, Gravitation, Thermal properties of Matter, Sound, Optics, Electrostatics, Semiconductors |
| Chemistry | Some basic concepts of Chemistry, Structure of atoms, Chemical bonding, Redox reactions, Elements of groups 1 and 2, States of matter(Gaseous and Liquids), Adsorption and colloids(Surface chemistry), Hydrocarbons, Basic principles of organic chemistry |
| Mathematics | Trigonometry II, Straight line, Circle, Measures of dispersion, Probability, Complex numbers, Permutations and combinations, Functions, Limits, Continuity |
| Biology | Some basic concepts of Chemistry, Structure of atom, Chemical bonding, Redox reactions, Elements of groups 1 and 2, States of matter(Gaseous and Liquids), Adsorption and colloids(Surface chemistry), Hydrocarbons, Basic principles of organic chemistry |

### 1. What is the MHT CET exam syllabus based on?

The MHT CET exam syllabus is based on the Maharastra State Board of Secondary and  Higher Secondary Education(MSBSHSE) curriculum for classes 11th and class 12th.

### 2. How can I access the detailed syllabus for MHT CET?

You can access the detailed syllabus for  MHT CET on the official website of the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra.

### 3. Are there any specific changes or updates in the MHT CET syllabus for the current year?

If any changes or updates in the MHT CET syllabus then it is officially announced by the state common entrance test cell on the official website with a notification.

### 4. Are there any specific topics that are frequently asked in the MHT CET exam?

There is no specific list, but try to focus on some important topics like mechanics, electrostatics, organic chemistry, calculus, genetics Etc.
