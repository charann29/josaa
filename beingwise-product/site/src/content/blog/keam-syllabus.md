**KEAM 2025 Syllabus** for Engineering and Pharmacy courses. In the KEAM syllabus, some of the units were removed from the Physics and Chemistry subjects. The Candidates can find the updated syllabus in this article below.

The Kerala Engineering, Architecture, and Medical (KEAM) is a state-level entrance exam conducted by the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE) every year. This test acts as a gateway for students seeking admission in Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical courses across the state.

The candidates must have passed the Higher Secondary Examination, Kerala with a minimum of 45% aggregate marks to be eligible for the KEAM 2025 exam. The examination authorities opened the KEAM 2025 application on March 27, 2025. The KEAM 2025 examination updates will be given through the official website, [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in).

The KEAM examination dates are revised by the CEE. The Engineering examination will be held from **22.04.2025 to 27.04.2025** from **10:00 AM to 1:00 PM**. The candidates can find the KEAM 2025 syllabus and paper pattern to have a clear roadmap.
