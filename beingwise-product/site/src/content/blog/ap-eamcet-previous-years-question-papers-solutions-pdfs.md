## Frequently asked questions

### 1. What is the importance of the previous year’s question papers?

Below are the importance of practicing the previous year’s questions:
– The candidates can be familiar with the examination pattern.
– They can calculate the time required for every section and question and prepare accordingly for the AP EAMCET 2024 exam.
– They can understand the key topics which will save the candidate’s time. 
– The students can clarify their doubts priorly by solving the previous questions.

### 2. How many questions are included in each segment of the AP EAMCET 2024?

The AP EAMCET 2024 contains 40 questions each in Chemistry and Physics, while Mathematics and Biology contains 80 questions.

### 3. Where are the mock tests for AP EAMCET 2024 available?

The AP EAMCET 2024 mock tests are available on the official website, [https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/Eapcet](https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/Eapcet/EAPCET_HomePage.aspx)[/](https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/Eapcet/EAPCET_HomePage.aspx)[EAPCET\_HomePage.aspx](https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/Eapcet/EAPCET_HomePage.aspx) for both the Engineering Stream and Agriculture & Pharmacy Stream separately.

### 4. Is practising the AP EAMCET Previous Year’s Questions alone sufficient for the exam?

No, practising the AP EAMCET previous questions alone is not sufficient for the exam. The candidates need to go through the entire AP EAMCET 2024 Syllabus, Practice the previous year’s question papers, and attempt mock tests to check their knowledge, which would be the ideal way to prepare for the exam.

### 5. Can we expect the previous year’s questions to be repeated in the AP EAMCET 2024 exam?

No, the exact previous questions will not be repeated in the AP EAMCET 2024 exam. But the candidates can expect the same pattern of the questions from the previous exams.
