JEE Mains Answer Key 2025 B.Arch/B.Plan was issued on May 13, 2025. Candidates could download the answer key from **jeemain.nta.nic.in** and raise objections between May 14 and May 16, 2025 (up to 11:50 PM). ## **How to Download JEE Mains Answer Key 2025?**

Following the simple steps below, candidates can easily download JEE Mains Answer Key 2025:

- Visit the official NTA website at jeemain.nta.nic.in.
- On the homepage, locate and click on the link “JEE Mains Answer Key 2025.”
- Enter your JEE Main registration number and date of birth in the login window.
- Click on Submit / Login.
- The JEE Main Session 2 Answer Key 2025 will appear on your screen.
- Check the Question ID and Correct Option ID displayed for your paper.
- Click on the Download PDF option to save the answer key.
- Compare your responses with the official answer key to estimate your probable score.

## **Steps to Raise Objections for JEE Mains Answer Key 2025**

The provisional key was released on the official website for [**JEE Mains 2025** **Session 2**](/blog/jee-main/) on April 18**, 2025.** Candidates were allowed to raise objections till April 13. Candidates could raise objections following the simple steps below:

- Navigate to the official website, i.e. jeemain.nta.ac.in.
- Select Challenge (s) regarding the [Answer Key](https://examinationservices.nic.in/JeeMain2025/root/Home.aspx?enc=Ei4cajBkK1gZSfgr53ImFVj34FesvYg1WX45sPjGXBpvTjwcqEoJcZ5VnHgmpgmK)

- Enter your application number and Password. Carefully enter the security pin provided in the dashboard, then click on ‘Submit.’

- Select ‘on Answer Key’.

- Select on Challenge the Answer Key

- The Question IDs for JEE (Main) – 2025 Session 1 (January 2025) will be displayed in the following sequential order.

- The ID listed next to the question under the “Correct Option” column represents the most accurate Answer Key provided by NTA.

- If you wish to challenge this option, you can select one or more of the Option IDs provided in the next four columns by ticking the corresponding checkbox.

- You can upload supporting documents by selecting ‘Choose File and Upload’ (ensure all documents are combined into a single PDF file).

- After selecting your desired option for Mathematics/Physics/Chemistry in Paper 1 (B.E./B.Tech.), Mathematics/Aptitude Test in Paper 2A (B.Arch.), or Mathematics/Aptitude Test/Planning in Paper 2B (B.Planning), scroll down and click on ‘Save Your Claim’ to proceed to the next screen.
- A summary of all the Option IDs you have challenged will be displayed.
- Click on ‘Save Your Claim and Pay Fee Finally.
- Choose your preferred payment method and pay a non-refundable processing fee of INR 200/- for each question.

### 4. **When was the JEE Main 2025 Answer Key Released?**

For Session-1 of the 2025 exam, the final answer key for Paper 1 (B.E./B.Tech) was released on **10 February 2025** by the National Testing Agency (NTA). For Session 2, the final answer key was initially released on **17 April 2025**, then removed and subsequently re-uploaded on **18 April 2025**.

### 5. **How to Download JEE Main 2025 Answer Key?**

To check and download the answer key, first visit the official website **jeemain.nta.nic.in**. Then look for the link labelled “JEE (Main) 2025 Answer Key” (or “Final Answer Key”) on the homepage and click it. Next, log in using your application number and date of birth, or your password, as required. Once logged in, the answer key PDF (along with your recorded responses, if available) will appear on the screen. Finally, download the PDF and save it for your records and evaluation of your probable score.
