**AP EAMCET Last-Minute preparation** tips and strategies will help the candidate to score high marks in their examination. The exam preparation strategies and tips to attempt the EAMCET examination are explained here in this article.

The AP EAMCET is a common state-level entrance test conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada under the APSCHE (Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education) once a year. The AP EAMCET 2024 examination is scheduled from **16.05.2024 to 17.05.2024 for the Agriculture & Pharmacy** Stream and from **18.05.2024 to 23.05.2024 for the Engineering** Stream.

The AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2024 is out on **07.05.2024.** The candidates can start downloading their Hall Ticket through the Official Website.

## AP EAMCET Last-Minute Preparation Tips

The Last-Minute preparation tips to be followed by the candidates are mentioned below:

1. The students are advised not to start new topics before their AP EAMCET examination.
2. The students need to concentrate more on the complete formulae present in Mathematics and Physics. In Physics, the basic-level formulas will help the students.
3. Whereas in Chemistry, the students can focus more on the topics related to the Periodic Table and Organic Chemistry to answer a maximum number of questions in the examination.
4. Revision of the already completed syllabus will help the students to remember better.
5. The students must practice the [Previous Year’s Question papers](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/ap-eamcet-previous-years-question-papers-solutions-pdfs/) for a thorough understanding of the examination pattern.
6. Attending the [AP EAMCET Mock Test](https://collegedost.in/blog/ap-eamcet/ap-eamcet-mock-test/) is mandatory for students to understand their level of preparation.

## Tips to attempt the AP EAMCET Question Paper

Here are the tips to be followed by a student to attempt the AP EAMCET Question paper within the given time.

- Manage your time efficiently during your examination. Don’t spend more than a minute on each question.
- If you can get the answer by spending the extra time on a question, ‘Mark for review’ these questions.
- Solve the ‘Mark for review’ questions at the end of the examination after solving all your known questions.
- While solving the ‘Mark for review’ questions, try to follow the Elimination, and Value substitution processes.
- Consider the following answer options first for unknown questions: All of the above, None of the above. And try to eliminate irrelevant answers.
- If the candidate faces any issues with the system provided, don’t panic. Contact your invigilator immediately.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Which documents should a candidate carry to the AP EAMCET 2024 test center?

The candidates must carry the following documents to the AP EAMCET 2024 test center:
– AP EAMCET 2024 Hall Ticket
– Black/Blue Ballpoint pen
– Filled-in Online Application form with a copy of caste certificate (in case of SC/ST category only)

### 2. Where and When will I get the login credentials to the AP EAMCET CBT test?

The candidate’s login ID and password will be announced 15 minutes before the commencement of the examination in the test center.

### 3. What are the exam duration and timings?

The exam duration and timings are as follows:
**Exam Duration**: 3 hours
**Exam Timings**: FN session: 9:00 AM to 12:00 Noon
                          AN session: 2:30 PM to 5:30 PM
