The **AP EAPCET 2025 Preliminary Key** is released on**28th May for the Engineering Branch** through the official website. The key for every slot, final key dates and information related to Objections will be available for the candidates to check!

The candidates can check the preliminary key by logging in through the Official Website, <https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/>. The preliminary key helps the candidates predict their marks before the release of the final key and result.

## AP EAMCET 2025 Final Answer Key

The Final Answer Key will be released on **5th June 2025.** Just before the **AP EAPCET 2025 Result** after all the considerations of the objections received for the preliminary key. The candidates cannot raise objections to the final answer key. This key helps the students to estimate their rank.

The final key will be released on June 5th 2025. The candidates can check the final key through the official website.

## Objections to the AP EAPCET 2025 Answer Key

The conducting authorities of the AP EAPCET 2025 provided an opportunity to raise objections to the Preliminary Answer Key. The candidates must send an email to object to the Answer Key. The date for raising objections to the key is 1st June 2025. No objections are considered after the end date set by the authorities.

Below are the details required by the candidate to raise objections:

- Candidate’s Hall Ticket Number
- Candidate’s Stream (Engineering / Agriculture / Pharmacy)
- The exam date of the candidate
- Exam session of the candidate (Forenoon / Afternoon)
- The answer given in the Key provided by the authorities
- The suggested answer by the candidate
- Justification for the answer given by the candidate (Along with reference book, chapter name, and page number)

### How to raise Objections to the AP EAPCET 2025 Answer Key?

Here is the step-by-step process to raise objections:

Step 1: Visit the official Website, <https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/>.

Step 2: Find the Key objections registration link and register by entering the required credentials like Registration Number, Hall Ticket Number, and Date of Birth of the candidate.

Objections Link: [Click Here](https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/Eapcet/EAPCET_KeyObjections.aspx)

Step 3: After the OTP verification, Select the question number to be challenged.

Step 4: Upload the supporting documents(Textbook details, page numbers, correct solution, etc. ) to the respective question and click on the Submit button.
