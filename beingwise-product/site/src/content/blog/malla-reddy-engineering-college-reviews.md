Malla Reddy Engineering College is an autonomous institute located in Maisamguda, Hyderabad. Malla Reddy College is a prestigious institution in Telangana and is committed to providing high-quality technical education. Malla Reddy Engineering College is a part of the Malla Reddy Group of Institutions, which includes Malla Reddy University, Malla Reddy Engineering College, etc. MREC offers a diverse range of B.Tech and M.Tech programs in various disciplines.

MREC is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH) and is accredited by the AICTE. Malla Reddy Engineering College has received Grade ‘A++’ from the NAAC. Undergraduate and Postgraduate courses at MREC are accredited by the NBA, which ensures students learn exactly what is required for the present days industry needs.
