## JEE Mains 2025 Registration Session 1: How to Apply?

To apply for JEE Main 2025, Candidates must follow the steps below:

- Filling of JEE Mains 2025 Registration Form
- Filling of Application Form
- Fee Payment

### Filling of JEE Mains 2025 Registration Session 1 Form

**Step 1:** Visit the official Website([jeemain.nta.nic.in](http://jeemain.nta.nic.in)).

**Step 2**: Click on the  “Online Application Form for JEE Main 2025-Session-1” link.

**Step 3:**  Select the New Registration option.

**Step 4:** The candidates have to enter their details according to the 10th Marksheet.

**Step 5:** The candidate needs to choose a password and the security question and answer. For Subsequent logins, the candidate will get a system-generated application number and password. The password should contain upper case letters, lower case letters, and special characters such as ‘\_’, and ‘@’ and the password should be 8-12 characters long.

**Step 6:** The Applicants can review the registration form and then submit it.

### Details Need to Fill in JEE Mains 2025 Registration Session 1 Form

In the Application Form, candidates need to fill out the below details:

- Personal Details
- Identity Details
- Exam and Center Details
- Qualification Details
- Additional Details
- Emergency Contact Details
- Document Upload

**Personal Details**

**Identity details**

In the Identity details section, candidates need to enter their Aadhar details. Before filling out the Aadhar details check the [new NTA Guidelines for Aadhar authentication](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/jee-mains-2025-registration/).

**Exam and Center Details**

In the exam and center details section, candidates can choose their preferred paper (1A/2A/2B) and an exam center.

**Qualification Details**

In this section, candidates need to enter education details such as 10th and 12th Marks.

**Emergency Details**

You must provide the contact information of a person who can be reached in case of any unforeseen circumstances during the exam.

**Document Upload**

Ensure that all documents are uploaded as per the prescribed formats (JPEG/PDF) and file size limits. The required documents include:

- Aadhar
- Passport size photograph (Format: JPG/JPEG, File Size:10kb-300kb, Dimensions:3.5c.mX 4.5c.m)
- Signature (Format: JPG/JPEG, File Size:10kb-50kb, Dimensions:3.5c.mX 1.5c.m)
- Caste certificate
- PwD certificate(If Applicable)
- Class 10 Certificate or Birth certificate
- Class 12 Certificate

### JEE Mains 2025 Registration Session 1: Fee Payment

After filling out the application form, you will be directed to the payment window, where you need to pay the registration fee based on your category.  Payment can be made through various methods such as credit cards, debit cards, or net banking. Ensure you keep the payment receipt or transaction ID for future reference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the Medium of JEE Main Examination?

The JEE Main examination will be conducted in English and 13 other regional Languages. If the applicant selects English as the medium, the exam paper will be presented in English. If the candidate chooses a regional language the question paper will be visible in their regional language.

### 2. Can a Candidate Apply for two sessions in JEE Mains?

Yes, a candidate can apply for 2 sessions. Candidates who are willing to apply for session 2 need to wait until the application window for session 2 opens.

### 3. What should be done if a Candidate Wants to Change the Medium of Examination in JEE Mains 2025?

Candidates who selected English as the medium but want to appear for the exam in their regional language can choose their preferred Language during the examination.

### 4.  For JEE Mains 2025 Session 1 exam, how many candidates registered?

Nearly **13.8 lakh** candidates registered for JEE Mains 2025 Session 1 exam
