The AP EAMCET 2025 is scheduled to be conducted from **16th to 22nd May** 2025. As the exam date is near, the students have to focus on completing the entire syllabus. The important topics in each subject can help the students achieve the maximum score in the AP EAPCET 2025 examination.

The Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (EAPCET) is a state-level entrance test conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada, on behalf of the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) once a year. This test is a gateway for admission into different undergraduate courses in the fields of Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy across Andhra Pradesh.
