Joy University, located in Vadakangulam near Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu, is part of the esteemed Selvam Educational Trust, established in 1984, and recognized under the Tamil Nadu State Private Universities Act (2019). Sprawled across more than 104 acres, this modern campus hosts over 140 programs spanning engineering, law, design, nursing, management, and more. Its standout features include UGC approval, NAAC and NBA accreditation, smart classrooms, digital libraries, moot courts, sports complexes, and a vibrant student activity center.

## **Joy University State-of-the-Art Campus Infrastructure**

**Smart Classrooms & Academic Blocks**

The campus houses contemporary academic blocks equipped with interactive whiteboards and collaborative learning environments.

**World-Class Labs**

Students gain hands-on experience in well-equipped labs, technology, science, and creative arts are all supported with cutting-edge equipment.

**Library & Student Activity Center**

The central library offers a treasure trove of books, journals, and digital resources, with cozy nooks ideal for focused study. Meanwhile, the Student Activity Center (on-campus mall) includes a mini‑theatre, tuck shop, salon, photocopy services, indoor gaming, food court, gym, and more.

**Sports & Recreation**

Joy University encourages holistic wellness with a cricket ground, basketball courts, turf court, swimming pool, and a fully equipped gymnasium. Extensive **green innovation zones** add to the tranquil, eco-friendly ambiance.

## Joy University Hostel For Boys and Girls

Joy University Hostels for Boys and Girls provide a safe, comfortable, and enriching living environment that supports both academic success and personal development. Designed to cater to the unique needs of male and female students, the hostels offer modern amenities, a disciplined atmosphere, and a strong sense of community. With dedicated wardens, well-structured routines, and facilities that promote learning and well-being, Joy University ensures that every student feels at home while staying focused on their goals. Also, check some Joy University hostel photos given below.

### **Joy University Boys Hostels**

**St. Joseph Boys Hostel:** St. Joseph Boys Hostel offers a secure and disciplined environment for male students. With spacious rooms, a well-maintained mess, and a focus on academics and personal growth, it encourages a balanced lifestyle. Regular prayer gatherings and study groups help foster a sense of community.

**Sophia Boys Hostel:** Sophia Boys Hostel is known for its modern facilities and student-friendly atmosphere. It promotes independence while ensuring safety through 24/7 supervision and strict entry protocols. The hostel also provides recreational spaces and internet access to support both academic and personal development.

### **Joy University** **Girls Hostels**

**St. Devasahayam Girls Hostel:** St. Devasahayam Girls Hostel provides a nurturing and peaceful environment tailored for female students. The hostel emphasizes safety, comfort, and cleanliness, and has a curfew system in place. It offers well-ventilated rooms, study areas, and dedicated wardens to ensure the well-being of all residents.

**St. Alphonsa Hostel:** St. Alphonsa Hostel blends spiritual guidance with academic focus. Named after the first Indian woman saint, it upholds values of discipline and modesty. The hostel features secure premises, nutritious meals, and regular counseling sessions, supporting girls in their academic journey and personal growth.

## **Joy University Hostel Accommodation**

Joy University offers thoughtfully designed hostel accommodation that ensures comfort, safety, and a vibrant community experience for students from all backgrounds.

**Multiple Hostels, Thoughtful Design**

Joy University offers several hostels for boys and girls, St. Joseph, St. Alphonsa, Sophia, St. Devasahayam, and St. Theresa, each built for safety, privacy, and community living.

**Joy University Hostel Fees and Room Type**

**2025–26 food & accommodation fees**:

- Non‑Premium (4‑sharing): INR 90,000/year
- Non‑Premium (3‑sharing): INR 110,000/year
- Non‑Premium (2‑sharing): INR 140,000/year
- Premium (4‑sharing): INR 100,000/year
- Premium AC (3‑sharing): INR 125,000/year

Additional charges include a **one‑time, non‑refundable admission fee** of INR 10,000 and a **refundable caution deposit** of INR 15,000.

**Room Amenities**

- Attached washrooms, beds, study tables, chairs, wardrobes
- Options for AC or non‑AC
- 24/7 unlimited Wi‑Fi for academic and recreational use.

**Facilities & Services**

- Daily mess offering North & South Indian meals curated by the Student Council
- Laundry vendor, water coolers, common rooms, indoor gaming zones, TV lounges, cable/SAT, salon & medical store within hostel premises
- Onsite ATM, general store, vendor services, dedicated support staff, CCTV surveillance, and round-the-clock security

**Rules, Safety & Community**

From evening registers at 9 pm and guest-notification protocols, to noise restrictions (10 pm–6 am), safety from ragging, fire safety, appliance restrictions, and a structured disciplinary system, the hostel policy is designed for both comfort and discipline.

**Refund, Maintenance & Discipline**

Fee refunds are cautious; hostel admission fee is non-refundable; room refunds follow guidelines with cancellation charges. Maintenance issues are handled via a Hostel Committee chaired by the Vice‑Chancellor. Any damage or breach may lead to fines or disciplinary action, with ragging carrying zero tolerance and strict sanctions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **What makes Joy University hostel and campus stand out compared to other private universities?**

Joy University emphasizes eco-friendly infrastructure, tech-integrated learning spaces, and student-centric design. The campus includes collaborative zones, wellness areas, and nature trails that promote holistic learning.

### 2. ****Are there any unique cultural or student-led events hosted regularly on campus?****

Yes! Joy University hosts an annual student festival called “JoyVibe”, where cultural fusion meets innovation. There’s also a quarterly “Midnight Minds” session for student entrepreneurs and creatives to pitch ideas in a TEDx-style format.

### 3. ******************************Is there a mentorship system tied to Joy University hostel life?******************************

Absolutely. Each hostel block has assigned senior mentors or Resident Advisors (RAs) who guide juniors in academics, career planning, and adjusting to hostel life. There’s also an optional peer-mentorship platform called “JoyCircles.”

### 4. ********How are hostels designed to support student mental wellness?********

Hostels are equipped with quiet zones, meditation corners, and peer support networks. Each hostel wing has a “Wellness Liaison” who conducts check-ins and facilitates stress-relief activities like yoga or game nights.

### 5. ****Can students customize their Joy University hostel rooms?****

Yes, students can customize their Joy University hostel rooms within certain guidelines. Students are allowed to personalize their space with photos, fairy lights, pin boards, indoor plants, and furniture as long as it doesn’t damage infrastructure or violate safety norms.

### 6. Are there late-night study lounges or co-working spaces available on campus?

Yes, the “24×7 Knowledge Hub” is a popular space with Wi-Fi, ergonomic seating, snack vending, and a small library. It’s perfect for late-night study or group collaboration.

### 7. How is safety and privacy maintained in Joy University hostel?

Joy University Hostel have biometric access, CCTV-monitored common areas (excluding private spaces), and gender-specific floors. Privacy is a priority, with policies against unauthorized room entry and an anonymous complaint mechanism.

### 8. What are the dining options like? Is food customizable for dietary needs?

The campus dining offers a multi-cuisine rotating menu with provisions for vegan, Jain, gluten-free, and low-carb meals. Students can pre-select meal preferences through the “JoyDine” app.

### 9. Is there a campus shuttle or transport system?

Yes. A smart shuttle system operates across campus, especially helpful during rains or for students with accessibility needs. There’s also a bicycle-sharing system available.
