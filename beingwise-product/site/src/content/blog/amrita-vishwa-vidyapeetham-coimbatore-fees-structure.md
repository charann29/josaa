## Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Coimbatore Fees Structure – B.Tech Scholarship Fees

The fee structure of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Coimbatore for B.Tech courses is heavily influenced by the scholarships offered. Under Amrita University Coimbatore scholarships, students with high ranks in the AEEE exam or JEE Main exam can avail merit scholarships of up to 100% on tuition fees.

Two kinds of merit scholarships are available for B.Tech aspirants, mainly, the Amrita Chancellor Scholarship & Amrita Meritorious Scholarship. Students with more than 80% marks in the entrance exam and last qualifying exam are awarded with 100% tuition fee waiver under the Amrita Chancellor Scholarship Scheme. While the Amrita Meritorious Scholarship scheme offers up to 50% tuition fee waiver for students who have scored 70% to 79% in the entrance exam & 10+2 exam.

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Coimbatore fee structure for B.Tech under different scholarship slabs is based on the combined ranks in the Class 12 exam and AEEE/JEE Main exam. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the total Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham BTech Fees at the Coimbatore campus?

The total  Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham BTech Fees structure for the Coimbatore campus varies from INR 10.1 Lakhs to INR 24.1 Lakhs for 4 years.

### 2.   Does scholarship reduce Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham fees for btech?

Yes, the Amrita chancellor and Amrita Meritorious scholarship schemes offer up to 100% tuition fee waiver on the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Coimbatore Fees Structure.

### 3. What is the AEEE Fees structure?

AEEE fees structure is the scholarship slab fee structure of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. It depends on the ranks achieved in the AEEE exam. It is only applicable to the tuition fees.

### 4.  Is the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University Fee Structure for B.Tech payable in instalments?

Yes, students can pay the B.Tech Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Fee Structure in 2-4 instalments.

### 5.   Is there any Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham application fee?

Yes, Students applying through the AEEE entrance exam need to pay INR 1300, JEE Main rank holders need to pay INR 500 as the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham application fee.

### 6. Are there any additional costs other than the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham B Tech fee structure?

Yes, additional charges include semester exam fees, a one-time admission fee, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham application fee, and other miscellaneous costs.

### 7. What is the refund policy on the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham fee structure for B.Tech?

As per UGC norms, the refund policy on the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham fee structure for B.Tech is levied. For up to 45 days of admission withdrawal, the student will get refunds. Students withdrawing their application before the classes start will get 100% refund. But others’ refunds may vary from 90% to 50% based on the timeline of the admission withdrawal.

### 8. What are the admission and management quota fees?

At Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Coimbatore campus, the admission fees vary from INR 10.1 Lakhs to INR 24 Lakhs for B.Tech. While the management quota fees are not fixed, as they are mostly unofficial.
