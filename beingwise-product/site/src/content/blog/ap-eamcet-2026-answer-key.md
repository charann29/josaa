### How to Download AP EAMCET Answer Key 2026

The AP EAMCET 2026 answer key response sheet is released altogether in PDF format. Follow the steps below to download the AP EAMCET Answer key.

1. Go to the official APSCHE EAPCET site at: cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET
2. On this site, click on “Master Question Papers with Preliminary Keys” displayed on its homepage.
3. Then choose your date and shift to appear in the AP EAMCET exam. You will be able to view the AP EAMCET Answer Key PDF on your computer screen.
4. Further, click on “Download AP EAMCET  Response Sheet” from the links given on its homepage.
5. On the login window, fill in your Registration Number and AP EAMCET Hall Ticket Number.
6. Your individual AP EAMCET 2026 Response Sheet will now be visible on your computer screen.

## Raise Objection to AP EAMCET Answer Key 2026

Along with the AP EAMCET 2026 Answer key response sheet release, the board enables the objection facility for the same. Candidates who find discrepancies in the provisional answer key can contest their answers using the official objection portal set up by APSCHE. The objection facility allows candidates to challenge incorrect answers, wrong question numbers, or wrong matching of options before releasing the final answer key.

[**Direct link to access the AP EAMCET Answer Key Objection Window**](https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/Eapcet/EAPCET_KeyObjections.aspx)

### How to Raise Objections to AP EAMCET Preliminary Answer Key

Follow the step-by-step process to object to the AP EAMCET 2026 Answer key.

- Go to the official website of AP EAMCET:
- From the homepage, click on “Key Objection” or “Submit Objections on Preliminary Key.”
- Log in using Registration Number, Hall Ticket Number, and other required credentials.

- Select your examination stream, exam date, and session.
- Pick the question for which you wish to file a challenge.
- Provide the correct option of the answer along with an appropriate explanation.
- Attach any proof/document in case of need.
- Go through all the information carefully before submitting the objections.
- Finally, click on the “Submit” button.
- Take the Acknowledgement Page after successfully objecting to the answer key.
