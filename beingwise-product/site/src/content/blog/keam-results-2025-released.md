The Commissioner for Entrance Examinations, Kerala, has announced the **KEAM Results 2025** **on May 14,** **2025. The authorities have made them** available; candidates can download their scorecard from the official website, [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in). Candidates are required to provide their application number and password to download their rank card from the official website.

The KEAM exam was held from April 23rd to April 29th for engineering and pharmacy aspirants. You can check the [**KEAM 2025 detailed exam analysis.**](https://collegedost.in/blog/category/keam/)

Follow the detailed guide mentioned below on how to check your scores, and more here.

## Here’s how to Check your KEAM Results 2025

Candidates can check their scores available on the official website, i.e. [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in). Students can check their scores by providing their credentials, such as their application number, on the official website. Follow the steps mentioned below to check your KEAM 2025 exam scores:

**Step 1:** Navigate to the official website, i.e. [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in).

**Step 2:** Log in to the official website using your application number and password.

**Step 3:** Your results will be displayed on the screen.

**Step 4:** Download your results and take a printout for future reference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ******Who is eligible to appear for the KEAM 2025 exam?******

You can visit the official website to download the KEAM answer key 2025.

### 2. **When will the KEAM 2025 results be released?**

KEAM 2025 results are released on May 14th 2025.

### **3. **********What should I do if I find any mistake in my KEAM 2025 results?************

Candidates can reach out to the officials in case of any discrepancies with their results.

### 4. **How to check the KEAM 2025 results?**

KEAM 2025 results can be accessed from the KEAM official website, i.e. [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in).

### **5. **********Will KEAM 2025 results be sent via SMS or post?************

No, KEAM results will only be available online on the official website.

### 6. **Is the KEAM 2025 scorecard enough for counselling?**

No, you will also need other documents like rank list, Class 12 marksheets, and ID proof for the counselling process.

### 7. **Can I request a revaluation of the KEAM 2025 results?**

No revaluation is permitted. However, if the scorecard shows a discrepancy, you may file a complaint.
