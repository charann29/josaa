The KEAM 2025 Applicants can verify their Name, Photo, and Signature, which they submitted along with the online applications to the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations to Engineering (or) Pharmacy streams. The candidates can log in through the link ‘KEAM 2025 Candidate Portal’ provided on the website, [https://cee.kerala.gov.in/keam2025/](https://cee.kerala.gov.in/keam2024/) by entering their Application Number and Password.

The candidates who have differences in their Name, Photo, and signature shown on their profile page with their actual Name, photo, and signature can inform the matter through e-mail (ceekinfo.cee@kerala.gov.in) and documents must be uploaded through the Candidate portal if required. The last date is up to **15.05.2025 03.00 PM.** Detailed instructions regarding rectifications are given below.

The Kerala Engineering, Architecture, and Medical (KEAM) is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the Commissioner for the Entrance Examinations (CEE) annually. The conducting bodies have revised the KEAM examination dates.

The Engineering examination will be held from **22.04.2025 to 30.04.2025** from **10:00 AM to 1:00 PM**.The Commissioner for Entrance Examination, Kerala has previously announced that the KEAM admit card will be released on May 20, 2025. But, the KEAM Admit card is yet to be released by the CEE. It will soon be released by the examination authorities.

## Rectification Methods

The candidates can follow the below instructions to rectify their defects:

- Candidates can view their defects, if any, in the photo, signature, name, and class 10th certificate uploaded to the online application by clicking the menu **‘Memo Details’** provided on the profile page.
- Candidates can rectify the defects in their name, photo, signature, and class 10th certificate by uploading the correct photo/signature/class 10th certificate before **3.00 PM on 15-05-2025.**
- Documents received by post/by hand/email will not be considered under any circumstances.
- Admit cards for Engineering/Pharmacy Entrance Examinations will not be issued to those candidates who have not rectified the defects in their Photo/Signature/10th certificate within the stipulated time.
- Details regarding the documents uploaded by the candidates to prove nativity and various reservations will be made available in the ‘candidate portal’ for verification after scrutiny.
- If any defects in the above-mentioned documents, the candidates must be intimated through the ‘Candidate Portal’ in the given time to rectify the defects.
