Established in 1994, Amrita University, also known as Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University, operates under Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi. Amrita University Coimbatore stands as the flagship premium engineering university in India, accredited by NAAC with an A++ Grade and UGC approval. It provides B.Tech programs across its multiple campuses in various cities like Chennai, Amritapuri, Bangalore and others. B.Tech Core and emerging specialisations like CSE, AI, Data Science, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and others are available at the Coimbatore Campus. Applicants need to pay a total fee of INR 24.10 Lakhs for top branches like B.Tech CSE.

The Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University courses for B.Tech emphasise hands-on learning, research-oriented academics and industry-relevant curriculum. With an 8th Overall NIRF Ranking in 2025, Amrita University admissions are based on the national entrance exam ranks, followed by CSAP counselling. AEEE Rank holders and top performers can avail merit scholarships as well. Read the following blog to know in detail about the Amrita University Coimbatore for B.Tech applicants.

### How to Apply for Amrita University Admission for B.Tech?

Students need to apply online on the Amrita Online Application Portal for B.Tech admission and the AEEE Exam application at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University. Refer to the following steps for the Amrita University Application for B.Tech:

- Visit the official website of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University or [aeee.amrita.edu](http://aeee.amrita.edu)
- Register with the required details and generate Login credentials.
- Verify the contact number with the received OTP.
- Once the login is complete, proceed to fill out the application form.
- Fill out the Amrita University Application form with your personal and academic details.
- Upload the required scanned documents in the given format.
- Students appearing for the AEEE Exam need to book exam slots and centres as well. JEE Main qualified students can opt for the AEEE Exams as well.
- After submitting, Students need to pay the Amrita University Application fee or AEEE registration fee of INR 1300. But JEE Main qualified students need to pay INR 500.
- Preview the application form and submit it. Keep a printout copy for future use.

Post application submission, students appear for the CBT-based AEEE exam and secure valid ranks. While the JEE Main qualified students wait for the merit list. After the results are announced, students can register for CSAP counselling conducted by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University. Candidates are required to choose their preferred branches and campus. Based on students’ entrance ranks, choice filling, and availability, seats are allotted through CSAP.

## Amrita University Scholarship & Financial Aid For B.Tech Applicants

Various Scholarship schemes and financial aid are provided by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University to B.Tech applicants. It can be categorised into merit scholarship and Need-based Scholarship. Under Merit scholarships, schemes like the Amrita Meritorious Scholarship and the Amrita Chancellor Scholarship schemes are available. The merit scholarship is awarded to students based on their ranks in the AEEE exam or JEE Main scores, along with performance in the previous qualifying exam. These scholarships are provided through a tuition fee waiver in slabs, which is allotted during the CSAP Counselling. Check the following table for Merit scholarship eligibility Criteria required by each student:

| **Amrita Chancellor Scholarship** | **Amrita Meritorious Scholarship** |
| --- | --- |
| 100% Tuition fee waiver | 50% Tuition Fee Waiver |
| 80% Above in 10+2 level exam + AEEE Ranks/JEE Main Ranks | 70%-79% in 10+2 level exam + AEEE Ranks/JEE Main Ranks |

To maintain the merit scholarships, students are required to score 7.0 CGPA annually in university exams. Scholarships are only given to 70% of the seats. Need-based scholarships and government financial aid schemes are also available for students from the reserved category or low-income families. Merit scholarships are auto-allocated in CSAP and do not need any separate application.

The main campus, Amrita University Coimbatore, is spread across 400 acres of green campus with multiple infrastructural and academic facilities. Some of the key Amrita University Campus Facilities are as follows:

- Smart Classrooms with highly advanced teaching technologies and air-conditioning.
- Access to the library and study room for Project works and assignments
- Advanced laboratories and equipment for each B.Tech branch.
- Indoor Sports Complex, Swimming Pool, and Sports ground for athletes.
- Wifi connectivity across the Amrita University campus.
- Amrita University Hostel accommodation for both male and female students is also provided. Students get to choose from Single, double-sharing, triple-sharing and 4-sharing rooms. All the basic amenities, mess facilities and other student requirements are provided.

Students pursuing B.Tech at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University lead a life with a unique blend of academics, cultural and community services. Annual cultural events and festivals like Amrita Kalotsavam, Onam, and other festivals are conducted at the campus. Student clubs and associations for Robotics, astronomy, photography, dance, music, Saahiti, and others are available as well. Sports Clubs are also organised to promote athletics, Karate, and Inter-campus games.
