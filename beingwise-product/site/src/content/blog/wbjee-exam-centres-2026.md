## How to Choose WBJEE Exam Centres 2026?

Candidates can select their preferred WBJEE exam centres while filling out the application form by following the steps below:

- Visit the official WBJEE website
- Complete the registration using basic details like name, email ID, and mobile number
- Fill in personal and academic information carefully
- Select three preferred exam zones in order of preference
- Review all details and submit the application form

## WBJEE Application Form 2026

WBJEE Application form 2026 is available at wbjeeb.nic.in. You can apply till April 5, 2026. Following the simple steps below, you can easily access [WBJEE Registration Form 2026](/blog/wbjee/wbjee-registration-process-2026/):

- Visit the official WBJEE website and click on “New Candidate Registration”
- Enter basic details like name, email ID, and mobile number, then submit
- Verify your registration using the OTP sent to your mobile number
- After successful registration, note down your application number
- Log in and fill in the application form with the required details, such as:
  - Nationality, domicile, category
  - Qualification details (passing year, school details, etc.)
  - Contact details (present and permanent address)
  - Select three preferred exam centres
- Upload a scanned photograph and signature as per the specified guidelines
- Pay the application fee online using net banking, debit card, or credit card
- Finally, review all details and submit the application form

## WBJEE Admit Card 2026

The final allotted exam centre will be mentioned in the WBJEE admit card. Candidates must check the following details:

- Exam centre name and address
- Reporting time
- Candidate details
- Exam day instructions

## WBJEE Exam Centres: Exam Day Instructions

Candidates should follow some basic instructions at the exam centre to avoid any problems on the exam day. Being prepared in advance will help you stay calm and focused during the exam.

- Reach the exam centre at least one hour before the exam starts
- Check the exact location of your centre beforehand so you don’t get confused on the day
- Carry your WBJEE admit card and a valid original ID proof
- You will be allowed to take the exam only at your allotted centre
- Do not carry mobile phones, calculators, or any electronic devices
- Once the exam centre is allotted, change requests are usually not accepted

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Where can I check the WBJEE exam centre list 2026?

Candidates can check the complete WBJEE exam centre list 2026 on the official website or in the information bulletin. It includes all zones along with district names and codes.

### 2. How can I find a WBJEE exam centre near me?

While filling out the application form, candidates can choose exam centres based on their location. It is recommended to select centres that are closest to your home for convenience.

### 3. How many WBJEE exam centres are there in Kolkata?

There are multiple WBJEE exam zones in Kolkata, including Central, North, South, Salt Lake/New Town, and West Kolkata.

### 4. Are WBJEE exam centres available for candidates from other states?

Yes, candidates from other states can appear for WBJEE, but they can only choose from limited centres in West Bengal such as Kolkata, Howrah, Asansol, Durgapur, and Kharagpur.

### 5. Are there WBJEE exam centres in Bihar?

No, WBJEE exam centres are not available in Bihar. Candidates from Bihar have to choose centres in West Bengal.

### 6.  Are WBJEE exam centres available in Uttar Pradesh (UP)?

No, WBJEE is not conducted in Uttar Pradesh. Candidates need to select centres from the available zones in West Bengal.
