The National Testing Agency will release the JEE Mains 2025 application form in April 2025. Candidates registering for JEE Advanced 2025 have to ensure they are prepared with all the necessary documents to avoid last minute rush. Scanned copies of photographs and signatures are required in specified formats and candidates are advised to look for size and format specifications. Candidates with special needs have to ensure they have the necessary documents from medical authorities.

Candidates can refer to the official website jeeadv.ac.in for any information about JEE Advanced 2025 and are advised to check the latest updates about the exam. This article will guide you through the important documents required for JEE Advanced 2025. Applying for JEE Advanced is comparatively different from the application process for JEE Mains as candidates from India are not required to submit any photograph or signature again. The same details of JEE Mains 2025 will be used and accessed from the database for JEE Advanced 2025.

## Required Documents for JEE Advanced for Indian Nationals

Indian Nationals have to upload several documents while some documents and some information are used from the database as you have applied for JEE Mains 2025. Scanned copies of photographs and signatures are not required and below are some of the important documents required to apply for JEE Advanced for Indian nationals.

|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Required Certificate** | **Format** | **Document Size** | **Remarks** |
| **Birth certificate/ Class X certificate** | PDF | 50 kb – 300 kb | All candidates have to submit. |
| **Class XII or equivalent certificate** | PDF | 50 kb – 300 kb | Mandatory for all candidates except for those who are appearing for Class 12th for the first time. |
| **Category Certificate** | PDF | 50 kb – 300 kb | Required for candidates from (SC /ST /OBC-NCL/GEN-EWS) |
| **PwD (If applicable)** | PDF | 50 kb – 300 kb | For persons who opted ‘Yes’ for PwD. |
| **Persons with Disabilities Facing Difficulty in Writing.** | PDF | 50 kb – 300 kb | For persons facing difficulty in writing. |

## Required Documents for JEE Advanced 2025 for Foreign Nationals

Foreign nationals applying for [JEE Advanced 2025](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-advanced/) may have to submit additional documents compared to Indian nationals. They are advised to keep a list of important documents and organize them for a smooth application process.

Here is the list of important documents required for foreign nationals to apply for JEE Advanced 2025.

- A birth certificate is required as a proof of age.
- The Class XII (or equivalent) certificate is required for those who appeared for the examination for the first time in 2024 or whose Board declared the result after September 21, 2023. If the Class XII (or equivalent) mark sheet for 2025 is available, it must be uploaded.
- Identity proof: Foreign Passport/OCI/Citizenship certificate/PIO Card.
