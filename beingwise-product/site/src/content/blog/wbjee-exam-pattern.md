## The Exam Pattern of Question Papers in WBJEE 2025

Questions in the WBJEE 2025 exam will be in the form of Multiple Choice Questions. Each questions are followed by four options and there will be three categories of questions in the exam. The following table will give you a brief of the maximum marks for each section and the category-wise marks.

- Questions from Category 1 carry **One mark** for each correct answer and -¼ for each incorrect response.
- Questions from Category 2 carry **Two marks** for each correct answer and -½ for each incorrect response.
- Questions from Category 3 carry **Two marks** for each correct answer and No negative marks for incorrect response.
