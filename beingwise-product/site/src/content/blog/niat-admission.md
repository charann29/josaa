## Does NIAT Offer Admission?

No, the NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies (NIAT) does not offer admission to universities or degree programs. NIAT is an upskilling program that provides industry-aligned training modules. It can facilitate the process by sharing a student’s details with collaborating universities (with the student’s consent), but admission is granted solely by the respective university based on its own eligibility criteria and admission process.

## How To Apply For NAT?

NIAT enrolment process is based on the NxtWave Assessment Test, for their upskilling program, followed by a personal counselling session. The application is in online mode. You need to pay INR 1200 to apply for NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies enrollment in 2026. You can check the steps below to apply for registration at NIAT.

**NOTE: The NIAT Application Process is the same as the NAT Registration Process.**

**Students can check the steps below to register and apply for the NIAT enrolment process 2026.**

**Step 1:** Visit the official website of NIAT: [NIAT India](https://api.whatsapp.com/send?phone=919281014900&text=CUET+PYQ&utm_source=College-Dost&utm_medium=CD-Blog&utm_campaign=CUET-PYQ)

**Step 2:** Click on “Apply Now” at the top of the homepage. The Apply Now option will be redirected to the Application page.

OR

Students can also click on the “Assessment Test” and then click on the “Apply Now” blinking on the top right corner of the page. It will be redirected to the Application page.

**Step 3:** Give your mobile number and verify it with an OTP to proceed with the registration process. Make sure you give a valid mobile number and that you are within the network area to successfully receive the OTP.

**Now you will have two options in front of you!**

**Step 4: For Students:** Add your name, address, phone, and mail ID, along with your academic information. The students also need to add their parents’ details, which include names, family income, phone number, and occupation category.

(Or)

**For Parents:** If you are a parent filling out the registration form, then you need to add your name, phone number, family income, and occupation category, along with all the student details.

**NOTE: Make sure every personal detail you add is correct, and there are no mistakes.**

**Step 5:** After carefully filling out all the details, students can proceed to book a 1:1 counselling session slot as part of their upskilling journey through the NAT slot. Here, the student can opt whether they want to appear for the test “ Right Now” or “Later”. If later, then they can select the date and time to take the test.

(NAT is the only entrance test required for joining NIAT 2026.)

**Step 6:** Congratulations on the successful registration. The next step for joining NIAT 2026 involves payment of the application fee.

**Step 7:** Please pay an application fee of INR 1200 by clicking on “Continue to Pay.” This fee will not be refunded even if the student does not appear for the test. So make sure to appear for the test!!

**Step 8:** The payment can be made by card, UPI mode, and NetBanking through ICICI and Axis Bank. Students can also use Wallets like MobiKwik, Ola Money, etc., to make the payment. After selecting the method to pay the registration fee, add your card or UPI ID.

**Step 9:** Voila, the payment is successful, and your NAT slot is booked. Now, you need to prepare for the NAT to get into the NIAT’s upskilling program.

**Step 10:** After the test, students need to attend a 1:1 counselling session for a deeper understanding of their goals and interests.

Confirm your Joining at the universities collaborating with NIAT  in 2026 and pay the course fee to begin your incredible tech journey.

## NxtWave Assessment Test (NAT)

NAT is a 90-minute online assessment test for joining the universities where NIAT’s upskilling program is offered. The test focuses on the candidate’s cognitive abilities and potential as a future tech innovator. There are four sections that test the different skills of a student. Candidates need to have 50% in the 10+2 level to be eligible for NAT.

## What are the Fees of the Universities Collaborating With NIAT?

The fees range for the NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies upskilling program and Collaborating Universities is INR 8-18 lakhs total (INR 2-4.5 lakhs per year). NIAT offers an industry-ready upskilling program in collaboration with the universities.

Students, after completing 4 years, will get an IRC from NIAT and UGC approved BTech degree from the collaborating Universities.

## Frequently Asked Questions

#### 1. What are the courses offered at the Universities where NIAT’s upskilling program is offered?

Universities collaborating with NIAT offer BTech courses with specialisations in Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science. However, students will also get to learn the Upskilling Program from NIAT.

#### 2. What are the eligibility criteria for NIAT?

For NIAT enrollment 2026, students need to pass 10+2. However, to get admission to the university, collaborating with NIAT, students should meet the eligibility criteria and follow the admission policies established by the university.

#### 3. What is NAT?

NxtWave Assessment Test is a 90-minute online test to join the universities where NIAT’s upskilling program is offered. The test focuses on the candidate’s cognitive abilities and potential as a future tech innovator. Three sections test different skills and test the student for the NIAT enrollment. It evaluates the student based on Personality and emotional traits, Analytical and adaptive skills, and Practical and problem-solving.

#### 4. How can I apply for the NIAT 2026?

You need to visit the official website to fill out the application form and pay the registration fee of INR 1200 for NIAT enrollment 2026.

#### 5. If I take PCM in my 10+2 level, can I join NIAT?

Yes, if you have PCM in 10+2, then you are eligible to appear for NAT and NIAT upskilling programs. You will also need to qualify for the NAT exam and meet the specific admission criteria of the partner university.

#### 6. Is NIAT more skill-focused than traditional B.Tech colleges?

Yes, NIAT programs emphasise practical, job-ready skills like coding, AI tools, and real-world projects, whereas traditional colleges may focus more on theory.

#### 7. When is the NAT?

The Phase 1 enrollment for the NAT is currently open. To check the registration deadlines and reserve your slot for the NxtWave Assessment Test, visit the official website of NIAT. The NAT is conducted in an online format. After booking your slot, you will receive complete details about the exam schedule and platform at your registered email ID.

#### 8. Is NAT harder than JEE or other entrance exams?

No, NAT is generally aptitude and skill-based, not heavily focused on PCM theory like JEE.

#### 9. How much is the NAT exam fee for 2026?

The fee for the NxtWave Assessment Test, NAT exam for 2026, is INR 1200. NAT is a 90-minute test designed to evaluate your cognitive abilities and potential as a future tech innovator.

#### 10. Does the NAT syllabus cover JEE-level Physics, Chemistry and Maths?

The NIAT exam syllabus does not specifically cover JEE-level Physics, Chemistry, and Maths, but you don’t need any extra preparation if you’ve studied for JEE, EAMCET, MHT-CET, KCET, or similar exams; you’re already prepared. The NAT exam has three sections: a Psychometric Test, Critical Thinking & Learnability, and Mathematics, designed to assess behaviour, reasoning skills, and quantitative ability. Focuses on core math concepts from high school and competitive exams. Topics include Sets, Polynomials, Progressions, Coordinate Geometry, Trigonometry, Probability, Permutations & Combinations.
