The KEAM 2024 Participating Colleges, Courses offered, and total number of seats are released by the KEAM official website, [www.cee.kerala.gov.in/keamonline2024/](https://www.cee.kerala.gov.in/keamonline2024). The colleges under different streams are listed below in this article. The admissions to different participating colleges will be made based on the candidate’s KEAM 2024 examination results and some other factors.

The Kerala Engineering, Architecture, and Medical (KEAM) is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the Commissioner for the Entrance Examinations (CEE) every year. The conducting bodies have revised the KEAM examination dates.

The Engineering examination will be held from **05.05.2024 to 09.06.2024** from **10:00 AM to 1:00 PM**. The Pharmacy test will be held on **09.06.2024 from 3:30 PM to 5:00 PM.**

The Commissioner for Entrance Examination, Kerala has previously announced that the KEAM admit card will be released on May 20, 2024. But, the KEAM Admit card is yet to be released by the CEE. It will soon be released by the examination authorities.
