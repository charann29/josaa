## What Is MHT CET Exam?

MHT CET exam is a state-level entrance examination that is administered by the Maharashtra State CET Cell for admission in engineering, pharmacy and allied courses. Maharashtra Common Entrance Test is the MHT CET exam full form. To apply to the MHT CET, the candidate has to fill out the MHT CET exam form online and pay the necessary MHT CET exam fees. The test is conducted in various MHT CET exam centres within the state.

The MHT CET syllabus is based on the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education curriculum, and practising to score better is done using MHT CET PYQ. The MHT CET exam result is announced after the test and then the MHT CET cutoff, also known as MHTCET cutoff, used to obtain counselling is released. The MHT CET exam date 2025 was scheduled in various shifts for both the PCM and PCB groups.

## MHT CET Exam Pattern

Check out the following to go through the MHT CET exam pattern 2026 as per the latest question paper designed and released by the authorities.

- Computer-Based Test (CBT) format
- Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) only
- PCM group: 150 questions: 50 each from Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
- PCB group: 200 questions: 50 each from Physics and Chemistry, 100 from Biology
- Total marks: 200 for both PCM and PCB papers
- Duration: 180 minutes (3 hours) for each group
- Marking scheme:
- Physics, Chemistry, Biology: 1 mark per correct answer
- Mathematics: 2 marks per correct answer
- No negative marking for wrong answers
- Medium of exam available in English, Marathi, and Urdu (Mathematics typically in English only)

### How to Fill Out the Application Form for MHT CET Exam?

Refer to the following to check out how to fill out the MHT CET registration form for the 2026 academic year.

- As the first step, students are mandated to go to the official MHT CET portal and click on the Registration Link.
- After that, one has to create a user account by entering basic personal information and an operating e-mail address.
- Once registered, the applicant needs to authenticate by logging in with the newly created credentials.
- The next one is to fill in all the fields of the MHT CET application form, including personal details, academic details, and the targeted examination group (e.g., PCM/PCB).
- At this point, photographs and signatures should be uploaded, scanned and in a formatted form in compliance with the required specifications.
- They should then choose the examination centre that they would like, as well as the language in which the examination will be carried out.
- The application fee will be paid through the specified online payment system of MHT CET.
- A thorough examination of all information input must be carried out to guarantee that all data is accurate before final submission.
- Lastly, a form is to be filled in, and a confirmation page should be downloaded for future reference.
