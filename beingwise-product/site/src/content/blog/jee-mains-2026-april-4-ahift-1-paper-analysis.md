## Advice for upcoming Shifts Based on JEE Mains April analysis

Based on student feedback and expert insights from the JEE Mains 4 April Shift 1 Analysis, candidates for the forthcoming shifts should adjust their strategy with a more intelligent and adaptable approach.

- **Manage Your Time Properly:** Many students found it difficult to finish the paper, mostly owing to the long hours required for solving Mathematics questions. In order to avoid such an issue, it is advisable to first ensure scoring in Physics and Chemistry, which require comparatively fewer hours, before moving on to Mathematics.

- **Intelligent Question Selection** Rather than answering each question in the sequence they appear in the paper, allocate 10-15 minutes for going through the whole paper. This will allow you to pick easier and known questions from all sections. \
