The institute has maintained a campus placement rate close to 90% for B.Tech students for three consecutive years. In 2024, the highest NIT package stood at INR 47.88 LPA. The average NIT package was INR 6.25 LPA. Companies like SAP Labs, Juspay, Deloitte, EY, PwC, IBM, LTI MindTree, Capgemini, TCS, Cognizant, Wipro, and Tech Mahindra have recruited from the campus in recent placement seasons.

This article covers the full Narula Institute of Technology placement record. It includes year-wise statistics, sector-wise analysis, branch-wise data, company list, CSE placement details, and salary figures.

The average NIT package has grown every year. It was INR 4.45 LPA in 2022, INR 5 LPA in 2023, and INR 6.25 LPA in 2024. All three figures are from the official WBJEEB institute profile.

## Narula Institute of Technology Placement Process

The Training and Placement Cell at Narula Institute of Technology manages the end-to-end campus recruitment process. Students from the 7th and 8th semesters are eligible for placement.

The process generally follows these steps:

1. Students register with the placement cell at the start of their final year.
2. Companies visit the campus for Pre-Placement Talks to explain roles and packages.
3. An aptitude or technical test is conducted as the first round.
4. Shortlisted students attend technical interviews covering subject knowledge and coding.
5. HR interviews assess communication skills and professional fit.
6. Offer letters are issued to selected candidates.

The placement cell also runs training sessions through the year. These cover aptitude preparation, coding, communication, and mock interviews. Students from both the 2025 and 2026 batches have received multiple offers from different companies, as confirmed in the official offered candidates list.
