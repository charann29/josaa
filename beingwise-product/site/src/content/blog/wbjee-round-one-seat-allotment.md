## WBJEE Results Announcement 2025

The **WBJEEB Results** for Round 1 seat allotment are expected to be released in the third week of July. Candidates can access the seat allotment list by visiting the official website, wbjeeb.nic.in. To view the result, they need to log in with their roll number and password. Seats are allotted based on the candidate’s rank, preferred institution choice, and seat availability. Candidates who are satisfied with their round 1 results and college admission must pay the seat acceptance fee to confirm their admission.

## Steps to Check WBJEE 2025 Round 1 Seat Allotment Results

Students often find it difficult to check their seat allotment results. Here are a few steps that you can follow to check your round 1 seat allotment results:

1. Visit the official website wbjeeb.nic.in.
2. Check for the WBJEE round 1 seat allotment link on the home screen and select it.
3. You will be redirected to the login page.
4. Enter your credentials and log in.
5. Your seat allotment with the allotted college name is displayed.
6. Download and save it for future reference.

## What to Do After WBJEE Round One Results Announcement

Candidates who are allotted seats in round 1 can report to the colleges directly with the required set of documents. If candidates are not satisfied with their allotted seat and want to upgrade their choice, they can apply for round 2 by paying an additional fee. Candidates who are not allotted any seat will have to wait for the Mop-up round, which is an additional round to fill the vacancies in colleges.

## Counselling Fee For WBJEE

Candidates must pay a non-refundable counselling fee of INR 500. After being allotted a seat, they will need to pay the admission fee, which varies depending on the institution and the student’s category.
