## VITEEE Counselling Process 2026

Students must follow the complete procedure to successfully get a seat through the VITEEE online counselling. Check out the complete counselling process below:

**1. VITEEE Result and Rank:** Once the VITEEE result is released, the candidates need to check their VITEEE rank. This rank helps in participating in the counselling process, and candidates are called for counselling in phases.

**2. Online Registration:** Applicants have to register for counselling through the portal. They will have to provide their basic information and the courses and campuses they are interested in.

**3. Choice Filling:** Once they register, students must indicate their preferred campuses and branches. These choices are vital for seat allocation and need to be made wisely.

**4. Document Verification:** Applicants should submit or bring certain documents for verification. This is to verify the candidate’s eligibility for admission.

**5. Seat Allotment:** This is done according to the candidate’s rank in VITEEE 2026, choice and availability of seats. The VITEEE counselling result​ is published online for each counselling round.

**6. Payment of Fees and Confirmation:** After seat allocation, candidates need to pay the counselling and admission fees to secure their seats. Not doing so can result in a seat cancellation.

*Note: In summary, the counselling is online and phase-based, and provides a fair and transparent admission process for all candidates.*

## VITEEE Seat Allotment Process 2026

The VITEEE seat allotment is an online process, which depends on the candidate’s rank, choices made during counselling and the number of seats available. Those with higher ranks are preferred, and their course and campus preferences are crucial to the allotment process. It is done in several phases, and candidates are eligible to participate based on their rank.

After the allotment, candidates can view their VITEEE counselling results on the counselling portal by entering their login details. Candidates allotted a seat need to accept it by paying the admission fee before the deadline. Failure to make the payment may result in the seat being cancelled and re-allotted to other candidates.

## VITEEE Counselling Fees Structure 2026

Those who have participated in the VITEEE counselling process should ensure they carry the prescribed DD amount for their allotted phase.

There is an inbuilt counselling fee in the total DD amount, which is non-refundable but will be adjusted against the tuition fee if the student takes admission at VIT. Fee criteria vary between students of Phase I and Phase II, and hostellers need to pay extra.

**VITEEE Counselling Fee Amount:**

- First round counselling (Rank 1 to 10,000): DD of ₹50,000 in favour of “Vellore Institute of Technology”, payable at Vellore
- Phase II Counselling (Up to Rank 1,10,000): DD of ₹2,00,000 in favour of ‘Vellore Institute of Technology’, payable at Vellore
- Counselling Fee (Part of above amounts): ₹11,800 (inc. GST) – non-refundable but can be adjusted in fees
- Hostel Fee (if applicable): ₹1,00,000 in another separate Demand Draft in favour of ‘Vellore Institute of Technology’, payable at Vellore

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the mode of VITEEE counselling 2026?

The counselling process is conducted online through VIT online counselling, and all steps are completed digitally.

### 2. When will VITEEE counselling 2026 begin?

VITEEE counselling 2026 is expected to begin in May 2026 after the announcement of results.

### 3. How can I participate in VIT counselling?

Candidates need to register online, fill in their preferences, and pay the counselling fee within the given timeline.

### 4. Is the VIT counselling fee refundable?

No, the counselling fee is non-refundable but will be adjusted against the tuition fee.

### 5. How are seats allotted in VIT counselling?

Seat allotment is based on the candidate’s rank, preferences, and seat availability. Higher-ranked candidates are given priority.

### 6. Can I change my choices after submission?

Yes, candidates usually get a chance to modify their preferences before final locking. Once locked, no changes are allowed.

### 7. What happens if I miss the counselling deadline?

Missing the deadline may result in exclusion from that counselling round, and candidates may have to wait for the next round.

### 8. Is counselling conducted separately for each VIT campus?

No, VIT follows a centralised counselling process for all its campuses.

### 9. Do I need to be physically present for counselling?

No, the entire counselling process is conducted online and does not require physical presence.

### 10. What if I am not allotted a seat in the first round?

Candidates can participate in subsequent counselling rounds until all seats are filled.
