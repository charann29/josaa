## **Best Books for JEE Mains 2025: How to Prepare?**

Without the right books, it’s impossible for students to prepare for a competitive exam like JEE Mains. The list below contains some preparation tips that students can follow during their JEE Mains Preparation. Let’s check:

- You need to check [JEE Mains Syllabus 2025.](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/best-books-for-jee-mains/) Checking it before starting your preparation gives you an idea of what to study. After that, you need to pick your study material, starting with NCERT and then moving to the **best books for JEE Mains** for detailed practice. A simple timetable helps a lot, with fixed study time for Physics, Chemistry, and Maths every day, plus one hour for revision.
- Toppers study one topic at a time. They read the theory, look at solved examples, and then practice questions. Keeping that in mind, you also need regular practice to keep your concept clear. It also help with accuracy.
- [Previous year question papers](/blog/jee-main/jee-main-pyq/) help students understand the exam pattern and the type of questions asked. Once a chapter is completed, many students take chapter-wise mock tests. After finishing the full syllabus, they shift to full-length mock tests to build exam speed and confidence.
- Revision continues throughout preparation so that older topics are not forgotten. Students clear their doubts quickly instead of letting them pile up. Along with studies, proper sleep, breaks, and a relaxed mind also play an important role in JEE success.
