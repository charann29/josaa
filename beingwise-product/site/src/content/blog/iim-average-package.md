## Table of Contents

The Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) continue to set benchmarks in management education and placements, attracting top recruiters year after year. The IIM average package 2025 reflects not only the strength of these premier institutes but also their adaptability to shifting market dynamics.

The average package at IIM Ahmedabad stood at around INR 34–37 LPA, closely followed by IIM Bangalore and IIM Calcutta, both of which reported averages in the INR 32–34 LPA range. Younger IIMs, such as IIM Lucknow and IIM Indore, also delivered strong outcomes, with average packages ranging from INR 25 to 28 LPA, highlighting a consistent upward trend in placements across campuses. On the other hand, the IIM highest package 2025 reached nearly INR 1 crore (international offer) while domestic offers crossed the INR 65–75 LPA mark in selected campuses, proving yet again the global appeal and prestige of IIM graduates.

## Which IIM has the highest package?

IIM Ahmedabad had the highest package in 2025, with the highest reported offer being around INR 1.46 crore per annum (146 LPA), followed closely by IIM Calcutta, which had international offers going up to INR 1.45 crore per annum. These are the highest packages among all the IIMs in 2025.

- The highest package reported during the MBA Post Graduate Programme (PGP) placements in 2023 was INR 1.46 crore per annum (146 LPA).
- For the PGPX (executive MBA) batch of 2025, the highest package was INR 76.5 LPA with an average package of INR 37.25 LPA.
- The PGP batch of 2024-26 achieved 100% summer placement success in 2025, with 394 offers accepted, including 12 international offers.
- Sectors like Consumer Tech saw an 800% rise in offers, with a significant increase in Financial Advisory and Consulting sectors as well.
- The top recruiters for IIM Ahmedabad in 2025 included Boston Consulting Group (BCG), with 35 offers, and Accenture Strategy, with 30 offers, among others.
- The placement process for the MBA PGP batch was conducted in clusters, with sector- and cohort-based recruitment, allowing for focused opportunities.
- The average package for the MBA PGP batch was reported to be approximately INR 37.25 LPA.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the average package of IIMs?

The IIM average package 2025 ranges between INR 25 lakh and INR 37 lakh per annum. For example, IIM Ahmedabad, Bangalore, and Calcutta offer average packages around INR 34-37 LPA, while newer IIMs have averages starting from around 18-20 LPA and up.

### 2. What is the highest package offered at IIMs?

The highest packages at premier IIMs, such as Ahmedabad, Calcutta, and Bangalore, can exceed INR 1 crore per annum. In 2025, IIM Ahmedabad’s highest package reached approximately INR 146 LPA, while IIM Calcutta and others also reported packages close to or above INR 1 crore.

### 3. Which IIM has the highest average package?

IIM Bangalore and IIM Ahmedabad often lead with the highest average packages, ranging from INR 34-35 lakh per annum, making them some of the most lucrative options among all IIMs.

### 4. What emerging sectors are expected to influence future IIM placements?

Sustainability, ESG roles, healthcare technology, and fintech are emerging as promising sectors. Additionally, AI and analytics roles are on the rise across industries, reshaping the placement landscape at IIMs.

### 5. Is the IIM highest package only domestic or includes international offers too?

The highest package figures typically represent domestic offers, but some IIMs also report very high international packages that can exceed INR 1 crore annually, particularly at IIM Calcutta.
