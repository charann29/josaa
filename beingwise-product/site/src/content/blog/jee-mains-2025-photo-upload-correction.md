## JEE Mains 2025 Photo Upload Correction Process: Step-by-Step Guide

If you have received a notification from NTA regarding an error in your uploaded photograph, you must correct it within the specified window to avoid rejection of your application. Here’s how to complete the photo correction process:

1. **Check Notification**:
   Candidates who have uploaded an incorrect photograph will receive a message or email from NTA at their registered mobile number and email ID.
2. **Log in to the Portal**:
   Visit the official JEE Main application portal and log in using your application number and password.
3. **Access the Correction Link**:
   After logging in, click on the link provided for photograph correction. This link is visible only to those candidates who NTA has notified.
4. **Upload Correct Photograph**:
   Prepare a recent color passport-size photograph that meets the following specifications: – File size: 10 KB to 300 KB – Format: JPG/JPEG – Dimensions: 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm – Background: White – 80% face (without mask) visible, including ears – File should be named as ‘Photograph’ – Spectacles allowed only if worn regularly – No Polaroid or computer-generated images – No filters, editing, or fabricated images
5. **Submit and Save**:
   After uploading the correct photograph, review the image for clarity and compliance with all guidelines. Submit the correction and save the updated confirmation page for your records.
6. **Keep Copies**:
   Keep 6 to 8 passport-size colour photographs (matching the uploaded ones) with you for future use during counselling and admissions.

## **JEE Mains 2025 Photo Signature Guidelines And Required Documents**

When filling out the JEE Mains 2025 application form, candidates must upload several official documents and ensure their signature meets the specified criteria. Submitting these documents correctly is essential for the successful acceptance of the application.

**Documents to Upload**

- **Recent Passport-Size Photograph**Must meet NTA specifications (see below for details).
- **Scanned Signature of the Candidate**Signature should be clear and meet the prescribed format and size.
- **Class 10 Mark Sheet or Birth Certificate**Required as proof of age and education.
- **PwD Certificate (if applicable)**For candidates claiming a reservation under the Persons with Disabilities category. The certificate must be in PDF format.
- **Caste Certificate (if applicable)**For candidates applying under reserved categories.

### **JEE Mains 2025 Photo Signature Guidelines**

- The signature must be done on white paper with a black or blue pen.
- Scan the signature in JPG/JPEG format.
- File size should be between 10 KB and 50 KB.
- Dimensions: 3.5 cm x 1.5 cm.
- The background of the scanned signature image must be white.
- The signature should be clear and not pixelated or blurred.

### **File Format and Size Specifications**

|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Document** | **Format** | **Dimensions** | **Size** |
| Photograph | JPG/JPEG | 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm | 10 KB – 300 KB |
| Signature | JPG/JPEG | 3.5 cm x 1.5 cm | 10 KB – 50 KB |
| Class 10/Birth Certificate | PDF | – | 10 KB – 300 KB |
| PwD/Caste Certificate (if needed) | PDF | – | 10 KB – 300 KB |

**Notes:**

- Do not upload Polaroid or computer-generated images.
- Do not use filters or edit the photograph or signature.
- Upload only your own documents and signature. Using someone else’s will result in application rejection under Unfair Means (UFM) guidelines.
