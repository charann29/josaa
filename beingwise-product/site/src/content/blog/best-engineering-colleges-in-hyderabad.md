**Admission Process:**

The admission process for NIAT Hyderabad starts with submitting the NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test) application form online and paying the registration fee. Candidates then appear for the NAT exam; shortlisted students attend a one-on-one counselling session.

Based on test performance and preferences, students are matched with a collaborating university (such as Chaitanya or Aurora) and proceed with the university’s admission formalities, guided by UGC and AICTE norms. Eligibility requires 10+2 (PCM) with at least 50%, completed in 2022, 2023, or 2024.

## How to Choose the Best Engineering College in Hyderabad?

With a massive pool of engineering colleges, students often find it difficult to choose a college that gives them better career prospects and education. It is a crucial decision that shapes a 40-year career ahead. There are several factors that you can consider while choosing a best engineering college in Hyderabad that helps you the best to achieve your dreams. **1. Resources to Support your Learning:** Engineering is all about solving real-world problems by applying the concepts practically. It is important to consider the resources available in the college to ensure you get a comprehensive and practical understanding of the concepts.

**2. Placement Record:** A consistent placement record will help you understand the college’s tie-ups with the industries and its commitment to bringing better opportunities for students. Some of the best engineering colleges in Hyderabad provide excellent placement opportunities for students.

**5. Alumni Network:** A strong alumni network can help in better networking opportunities and career guidance. It also showcases the college’s excellence in producing capable individuals.
