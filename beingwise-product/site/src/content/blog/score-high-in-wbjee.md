West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination is a state-level entrance examination for undergraduate courses like B.Tech, Pharmacy, and Architecture. Approximately 1 lakh students apply for the exam every year and the exam frequency is once a year. The WBJEE exam for 2025 is scheduled to happen on **April 27th 2025**.

The exam pattern consists of two papers with three subjects **mathematics**, **physics** and **chemistry**. Mathematics accounts for 100 marks and the other two subjects for 50 marks each. Candidates prepare for the exam and often miss the cutoff by a few marks. It is important to know how to score high in WBJEE to have a competitive edge and secure admission to the top colleges. This article will help you with how to score high in WBJEE.

## Tips to Score High in the WBJEE 2025 Exam

Obtaining a safe score of 150+ should be the aim of every student. Although this might come with its own difficulties, achieving it is not an impossible feat. A clear strategy to ace the exam can help you score high and secure admission to your dream university/institute.

Here are some tips to score high in [WBJEE 2025](https://collegedost.in/blog/category/wbjee/).

### Understand the Exam Pattern

Analyzing the examination process of WBJEE like the exam mode, frequency, exam pattern, and marking scheme helps you curate a plan personalized for you. You can navigate to the official website for the syllabus document.

### Curate a Plan

After understanding the syllabus and exam pattern, make a plan based on the exam date to complete the syllabus on time. Also, include an effective revision plan and make time for mock tests and to analyze the previous year’s question papers.

### Start Studying

As you have already made a plan begin your preparation and consistently achieve the set targets. Don’t skip any topics and don’t try to skim through any topics as it is advised to complete the syllabus at least once. Make notes while reading as it is difficult to read through textbooks while revising.

### Attempt Mock Tests

Attempt mock tests as they help you revise your concepts and help you analyze your performance. Mock tests are a wonderful source to analyze the areas where you need to improve. Give as many mocks and analyze your performance for each performance.

### Revise Regularly

Students often ignore revision and face difficulty while trying to recollect the concepts during the exam. Hence, regular revision is important to make concepts stay fresh in your mind. Try revising with flashcards and short notes to save time.

### Time Management is Key

Managing your time is the key to ace your exam. Stay away from social media and other distractions to keep yourself focused. Use techniques like Pomodoroo or online tools to increase your productivity.

### Join Test Series

Many institutes offer test series and it is advised to join any test series as they curate the tests by simulating the exam toughness. This helps you familiarize yourself with the examination process and helps you get rid of exam stress.
