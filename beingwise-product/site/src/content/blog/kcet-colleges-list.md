## Factors Affecting KCET Cutoff Ranks

The KCET cutoff ranks are subject to variations from year to year, depending on a number of crucial factors that are associated with the admission process and seat availability. The number of students appearing for the exam, the difficulty level of the question paper, and the overall performance of the students greatly influence the cutoff trends. Check out more below:

- Number of students applying for KCET
- The difficulty of KCET
- Overall performance of students in the examination
- Number of seats available in engineering colleges
- Popularity of various B.Tech programs
- Cutoff scores in the previous years’ KCET
- Various reservation criteria and their impact
- Reputation of colleges for placements
- Number of colleges taking part in the counselling process
- Increased/decreased popularity of innovative fields such as Artificial Intelligence
- Modifications in seat allocation rounds during counselling
- Choice filling patterns of students during option entry

## Mistakes to Avoid During KCET Option Entry

Check out what mistakes to avoid during the KCET option entry below:

- Filling only a few options from the KCET colleges list instead of exploring more suitable colleges.
- Not following the past year’s cut off trends to choose colleges under KCET.
- Not selecting colleges based on preference but popularity alone.
- Filling unrealistic options without verifying the expected ranks by using a KCET rank predictor.
- The absence of a reliable KCET college predictor before making the final option entry choice.
- Placing the college options in the wrong priority sequence.
- Not considering the fee structure, hostel facilities and placements while choosing KCET colleges.
- Failure to check to ensure that branch codes and college information have been double-checked before final submission.
- Delaying the KCET option entry till the last date.
- Failure to follow official information regarding colleges accepting KCET.
