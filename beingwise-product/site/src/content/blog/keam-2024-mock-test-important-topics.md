The official KEAM 2024 Mock Test is conducted just 15 minutes before the actual KEAM examination by the examination authorities. This test is conducted at the test center. This test is conducted to familiarize the candidates before the actual examination.

The Kerala Engineering, Architecture, and Medical (KEAM) is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the Commissioner for the Entrance Examinations (CEE) every year. The KEAM examination dates have been revised. The Engineering and Pharmacy examinations will be held between **05.06.2024 and 09.06.2024**.

The Commissioner for Entrance Examination, Kerala has previously announced that the KEAM admit card will be released on May 20, 2024. But, the KEAM Admit card is yet to be released by the CEE. It will soon be released by the examination authorities.

The candidates should start practicing the [Previous Year’s Questions](https://collegedost.in/blog/keam/keam-previous-year-question-papers-solutions-pdfs/) and sample tests to understand their level of preparation and improve accordingly. To familiarize themselves before the KEAM 2024 actual examination, the examination authorities will conduct a Mock Test. The complete details and process of attempting the Mock Test are explained below.

## KEAM 2024 Mock Test

The mock test starts just 15 minutes before the actual examination. The candidates can practice and familiarize themselves before the actual examination starts. The steps to write the mock test are as follows.

## Steps to Attempt the KEAM 2024 Mock Test

The steps to attempt the KEAM 2024 Mock Test are as follows:

**Step 1:** After login to the Computer Based Test (CBT), there will be a familiarization session (Mock Test) prior to the start of the examination. The familiarization time starts 15 minutes before the actual start of the examination.

**Step 2:** A countdown timer will be visible in the top right corner, indicating the time left to start the Mock Test.

**Step 3:** After the timer hits zero, the page will get redirected to the Mock Test page automatically.

**Step 4:** On the Mock Test page, when the timer hits zero, the page will redirect to the actual examination page automatically.

After the scheduled Mock Test time, the candidates will be automatically redirected to the Examination Screen, where the candidates can attend the actual test

## Important Topics to be covered

Important and [high-weightage chapters](https://collegedost.in/blog/keam/keam-important-chapters-topics/) must be the priority for the students preparing for the KEAM 2024 examination. This will help the candidates to achieve a maximum score in the examination which will result in admission to a good college. The important topics in every subject are mentioned below:

### Mathematics Important Topics

The important topics in Mathematics are listed below:

- Differential Calculus

### Physics Important Topics

The important topics in Physics are listed below:

- Electric charges and fields

- d & f – block elements
