## SRM Admission – B.Tech Admission Process

SRM University admission process for B.Tech aspirants is entirely conducted online on the official website. Based on performance in SRM entrance exam 2026 for B.Tech, also known as SRMJEEE, students are selected. SRM Direct admissions are also conducted for B.Tech programs under management quota. These students do not require to appear for SRM Entrance Exam. Students applying for SRM University b tech admission need to follow the given application and admission process:

| Step No. | SRM Admission Process | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Register Online | Visit the official SRM Admission portal to set up an account using a valid email ID and mobile number. |
| 2 | Fill Out the Application Form | Fill out the online SRM Application Form with personal, academic, and communication details. Select the preferred SRM campus and upload the required documents. |
| 3 | Pay Application Fee | Complete the SRM admission application by paying the required application fee. |
| 4 | Appear for SRMJEEE | Complete SRM slot booking, schedule the exam date, download the SRMJEEE admit card, and appear for the examination. |
| 5 | Check SRMJEEE Results & Rank | After the examination, check the SRMJEEE result online. The obtained rank determines eligibility for counselling and seat allotment. |
| 6 | Attend Online Counselling | Selected candidates must register for the SRM counselling process and fill in their preferred campus and branch choices. |
| 7 | Seat Allotment Process | Seats are allotted based on SRMJEEE rank, seat availability, and chosen specialisation. Pay the tuition fee within the specified deadline after allotment. |
| 8 | Document Verification | Students must submit original documents for verification at the SRM University campus. |
| 9 | Admission Confirmation | Admission is confirmed after successful document verification and fee payment. |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is SRMJEEE?

SRMJEEE (SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination) is the online entrance test conducted by SRM Institute of Science and Technology for admission to its B.Tech programs. The exam is typically conducted in a home-proctored mode and includes questions from Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English, and Aptitude.

### 2. Are SRM placements good?

Yes, SRM placements are considered strong based on placement reports and student reviews. Top recruiters include Amazon, Cognizant, Dell, Ernst & Young, Flipkart, SAP, IBM, Infosys, and Wipro. The highest packages have reached up to ₹65 LPA, while the average package is reported to be around ₹7.88 LPA. Computer Science-related branches generally record higher placement packages than core engineering branches.

### 3. Does SRM University offer scholarships for B.Tech students?

Yes, SRM Institute of Science and Technology offers various scholarships, including merit-based scholarships, SRMJEEE rank-based fee waivers, and special scholarships for outstanding academic and sports achievements.

### 4. Is SRMJEEE mandatory for SRM B.Tech admissions?

Yes, SRMJEEE is the primary admission route for B.Tech programs offered by SRM Institute of Science and Technology. Candidates are generally required to appear for and qualify in the entrance examination.

### 5. What documents are required for SRM admissions?

Candidates typically need their Class 10 and Class 12 marksheets, SRMJEEE rank card, transfer certificate, passport-size photographs, valid photo ID proof, and any other certificates specified during the admission process.
