## JEE Mains Exam Day Instructions

The JEE Mains 2026 admit card mentions a few key guidelines that must be followed by each test taker on the exam day. The key exam day instructions are mentioned below.

1. Visit your JEE Main exam centre the day before the exam to become acquainted with the place and consider aspects such as time and distance.
2. Make sure you arrive at the test centre at least an hour before the reporting time specified on your JEE Main 2026 Admit Card.
3. Candidates who arrive after the gate closes will not be permitted to enter the test venue.
4. Candidates will be undertaking a variety of mandatory processes; thus, being on time is critical.
5. Candidates must write their name and roll number at the top of the rough sheets and submit them to the Invigilator, along with their admit card, before leaving the exam hall.
6. Fill out the attendance form in legible handwriting, sign it, make a left thumb impression, and paste the photograph where it is needed. The left-hand thumb impression is clean and not smeared.
7. JEE Main candidates will be expected to submit the necessary information to an attendance sheet provided by the invigilator.
8. Candidates must also include a coloured passport-sized photograph that matches the one on the application form.
