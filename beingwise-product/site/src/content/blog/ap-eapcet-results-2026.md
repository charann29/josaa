## AP EAPCET Qualifying Marks & Normalization Process

Understanding the AP EAPCET 2026 qualifying criteria and normalization process is essential for candidates aiming to secure a rank and participate in the counselling process. While the qualifying marks determine eligibility for ranking, the normalization process ensures fairness by adjusting scores across different exam sessions, allowing all candidates to be assessed on an equal basis.

### Qualifying Marks for AP EAPCET 2026

To be eligible for a rank in AP EAPCET 2026, candidates must secure a minimum score. Candidates who meet the qualifying criteria are considered for the ranking and subsequent counselling process.

- General/OBC/EWS candidates: Minimum 25% of the total marks, i.e., 40 out of 160.
- SC/ST candidates: No minimum qualifying mark is required. However, admission for these categories is subject to seat availability in their respective quotas.

### What is the AP EAMCET Normalization Process?

AP EAPCET is conducted in multiple sessions with different question papers. To ensure fairness and eliminate any advantage or disadvantage due to varying difficulty levels, the exam authority uses a normalization process. This process adjusts scores so that all candidates are evaluated on a level playing field, regardless of the session attended.

- The normalized score is used for ranking, not the raw score.
- The normalization formula compares the average and top scores across all sessions to adjust each candidate’s marks.
- This ensures that students in a “tough” session are not at a disadvantage compared to those in an “easy” session.

## AP EAPCET Counselling Results vs Seat Allotment: Key Difference

Many candidates confuse AP EAMCET counselling results with AP EAMCET seat allotment results. The distinction is as follows:

- AP EAMCET counselling results indicate successful registration, verification, and eligibility for counselling participation.
- AP EAMCET seat allotment results specify the actual college and course allocated after processing web options.
