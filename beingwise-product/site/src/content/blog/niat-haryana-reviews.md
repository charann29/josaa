## How Students Describe Sushant University? The Collaborating University for NIAT Haryana

Sushant University, formerly known as Ansal University Gurgaon, is a UGC-recognised private university in Haryana established in 2012. Located on Golf Course Road, Gurugram, Sushant University Gurgaon has emerged as one of the top colleges in Gurgaon for engineering, design, architecture, management, and law education.

Sushant University offers programs across eight schools, including Engineering and Technology, Business, Law, Sushant School of Art and Architecture, Sushant School of Architecture, Sushant School of Design, Health Sciences, Hospitality, and Liberal Arts. It has been consistently ranked among the top private universities in North India, notably ranked 1 in Private Architecture Schools in India by Outlook Magazine and ranked 27th in the A++ category by IIRF Rankings 2024.

NIAT conducts its industry-ready upskilling program at the Training Centre/Centre of Excellence provided by Sushant University. - **Project-Based Learning:** From the very first semester, students work on real-world projects across domains including healthcare, defence, and agriculture. Over 4 years, students are expected to complete 50+ projects that form a strong technical portfolio.

- **AI-Powered Learning Platform:** NIAT uses an AI-powered learning platform where students code in sandboxed environments with real-time feedback. This accelerates skill-building and prepares students for technical hiring processes.
- **Hackathons, Workshops, and Masterclasses:** Students participate in regular hackathons, industry workshops, and masterclasses conducted by CXOs and senior professionals from top tech companies. These sessions help students stay connected with the latest industry trends and build their professional network from an early stage.
- **Aptitude and Communication Training:** Alongside technical upskilling, the NIAT program dedicates structured time to aptitude training, English communication, and soft skills, all critical for placement interviews.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **What is NIAT at Sushant University?**
   NIAT (NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies) is an industry-led upskilling program that runs alongside the 4-year B.Tech CSE program at Sushant University. The B.Tech degree is awarded by Sushant University; the upskilling program is delivered by NxtWave’s Trainers, Mentors, and Success Coaches at the Training Centre provided by the university.
2. **Does NIAT give a degree?**
   No. NIAT is an upskilling program and does not award degrees or diplomas. The B.Tech degree is awarded solely by Sushant University. NxtWave provides an Industry Ready Certificate (IRC) upon successful completion.
3. **Is Sushant University recognised by UGC?**
   Yes. Sushant University is a UGC-recognised private university established under the Haryana Private Universities Act, 2006. The degree is valid for jobs and higher studies.
4. **What is the NAT (NxtWave Assessment Test)?**
   NAT is a 90-minute online test by NxtWave to evaluate cognitive ability and aptitude. It is not a university entrance exam. University admission is handled separately by Sushant University.
5. **Does qualifying for NAT guarantee admission?**
   No. NxtWave only facilitates. Final admission depends on Sushant University’s eligibility criteria and policies.
6. **What are NIAT College fees?**
   NIAT is a Training Centre, not a college. The total program cost (including B.Tech) ranges from INR 8–18 lakhs.
7. **Who delivers the upskilling sessions?**
   NxtWave Trainers, Mentors, and Success Coaches handle upskilling. Sushant University faculty manage the academic degree separately.
8. **Is a non-coding background student eligible?**
   Yes. Training starts from basics like HTML, CSS, Python, and SQL. Students with 10+2 PCM can apply.
9. **Is Ansal University and Sushant University the same?**
   Yes. Ansal University was rebranded as Sushant University in August 2020. All recognitions remain valid.
10. **What is the Sushant University Gurgaon Address?**
    Sector 55, Golf Course Road, Gurugram, Haryana – 122003.
11. **What is the NIAT Sushant University Fees Structure?**
    University tuition is separate. NIAT charges additional upskilling fees. Total cost is typically INR 8–18 lakhs over 4 years.
12. **What is the Sushant University NIRF Ranking?**
    It falls under the 151–200 overall band.
13. **What is NIAT Full Form?**
    NIAT stands for NxtWave of Innovation in Advanced Technologies.
14. **Is NIAT Private or Government?**
    NIAT is a private industry-led upskilling program, not a government institution or degree-awarding body.
