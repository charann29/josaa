## Information About NITs in India

### NIT Rourkela

> **NIT Rourkela** was established in 1961 and is one of the oldest NITs in India. NIT Rourkela is established in Odisha and is a renowned institution for its academic excellence and research programs. At NIT Rourkela, technology transfer and innovation are key priorities, making it a hub for interdisciplinary research and industry collaborations. For the academic year 2023-2024, the institute offers a total of 1,058 seats across all courses. The number of students admitted to both undergraduate and graduate programs is based on the seat matrix for each course.

### NIT Karnataka

> **NIT Karnataka** was founded in 1960 and is located in Mangalore, Karnataka. NIT Karnataka has an excellent track record of ranking continuously in NIRF rankings. Focusing on the holistic development of students, NIT Karnataka has an alumni of industry and advanced technology. NIT Karnataka offers 956 seats.

### NIT Warangal

> Established in 1959 **NIT Warangal** is located in Warangal, Telangana. With the vision to become a global knowledge hub, NIT Rourkela is committed to continuously pursuing excellence in education, research, entrepreneurship, and technological services for the betterment of society. NIT Warangal focuses on the all-round development of the candidates and offers 989 seats every year.

### NIT Trichy

> **NIT Trichy**, formerly known as Regional Engineering College, Tiruchirappalli, is one of the most esteemed NITs in India. Established in 1964, the institute has a rich legacy of producing exceptional engineers. The campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, libraries, and sports centers. NIT Trichy’s alumni have made remarkable contributions across various industries, further solidifying its reputation as one of India’s top NITs. The institute offers a total of 1,007 seats across its diverse programs, allocated into different categories.

### NIT Calicut

> With a mission to encourage high-quality technical excellence and personnel, technical and managerial skills, and advanced research capabilities. **NIT Calicut** was established in 1961, boasting a diverse student community. NIT Calicut has diverse Alumni across different fields from business to technology. NIT Calicut offers 1224 seats every year.

### NIT Jaipur

> **NIT Jaipur**, officially known as Malaviya National Institute of Technology, Jaipur, was established in 1963 and is located in the vibrant city of Jaipur, Rajasthan. The institute’s faculty is deeply involved in cutting-edge projects, with a strong emphasis on research and innovation. Known for its beautiful campus and dynamic student community, NIT Jaipur is a preferred choice for aspiring engineers. The institute offers 888 seats across its various courses.

### NIT Durgapur

> **NIT Durgapur** was established in 1960, with the aim of setting the pace of engineering education. NIT Durgapur is a premier institution offering top-tier technical education. NIT Durgapur was ranked 44th among engineering colleges in India. NIT Durgapur offers 969 seats every year.

### NIT Silchar

> **NIT Silchar**, established in 1967, is renowned for its picturesque campus and high-quality education. The institute is committed to research and has strong collaborations with leading organizations and industries. NIT Silchar’s alumni have excelled in diverse fields, including entrepreneurship, business, and academia, contributing to its prestigious reputation.

### NIT Nagpur

> With the mission to cater high-quality technical education for students, to meet the industries and society requirements, **NIT Nagpur** was established in 1960. NIT Nagpur offers excellent infrastructure for candidates and has diverse alumni across different fields.

### NIT Allahabad

> **NIT Allahabad**, now known as **MNNIT Allahabad**, was established in 1961. Located in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, it is a leading institute offering high-quality education in engineering, technology, and management. With strong industry ties and a focus on research and innovation, MNNIT Allahabad has a distinguished reputation and accomplished alumni.
