**AP EAMCET 2025 Application**: The AP EAPCET 2025 Application Form submission without a late fee will be closed on 24th April 2025. However, **candidates** **can submit** their AP EAMCET 2025 **application** **with the late fee of Rs.1000/- by 01/05/2025.**

The **final date** for submitting the AP EAPCET 2025 applications **with a late fee of Rs. 10,000/-** is **16th May** **2025**. Candidates can find all the important exam dates mentioned in the article below.

The Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (EAPCET) is a state-level entrance test conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada, on behalf of the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) once a year. This test is a gateway for admission into different undergraduate courses in Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy across Andhra Pradesh. The authorities scheduled the AP EAPCET 2025 exam from the **21st to the 27th of May** 2025.

## How to Apply for AP EAPCET 2025?

The AP EAPCET 2025 Application form can be filled out online through the Official Website. Here is a step-wise process to fill out the Application Form:

1. Visit the AP EAPCET Official Website, [https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/](https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/).

2**.** Click on Step 1 of the AP EAPCET 2025 Application Link on the Home page. The application process starts with the Fee Payment.

### **Details of Registration Fee**

|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Stream** | **Registration Fee** | | |
| **OC** | **BC** | **SC/ST** |
| Engineering (E) | Rs. 600 | Rs. 550 | Rs. 500 |
| Agriculture (A) | Rs. 600 | Rs. 550 | Rs. 500 |
| Both E & A | Rs. 1200 | Rs. 1100 | Rs. 1000 |

The Candidate can make the Payment through either a Credit Card, Debit Card, or Net Banking.

3**.** After completing the Payment, the Candidate needs to start the form-filling process. While filling out the AP EAPCET 2025 Application form, they have to provide their personal, academic, and contact details.

4**.** Upload the Candidate’s passport-size photograph and scanned signature in the required format.

5**.** Review all the details, make necessary corrections if needed, and Submit the Application Form.

6**.** After Submission, take a printout of your application form and preserve it carefully.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### ****1. What are the documents required for the AP EAPCET Application 2025?****

All the documents required for the AP EAPCET Application 2025 are mentioned in the below:
– The Hall Ticket Number of Qualifying Examination Number (10+2 or Equivalent).
– Date of Birth Certificate (as mentioned in their SSC).
– Category Certificate (If Applicable).
– Parent’s Income.
– Recent passport-size Photograph (less than 30KB) and Signature (less than 15KB).
– Aadhar Card.
– Email ID and Mobile Number.

### **2. What is the exam pattern of AP EAPCET 2025?**

The AP EAPCET 2025 exam pattern is as follows:
– The AP EAPCET 2025 is a computer-based online test.
– The total marks in AP EAPCET 2025 are 160, with 40 marks each allocated for Physics and Chemistry, and 80 marks allocated for Mathematics.
– Each question carries 1 mark. 1 mark is awarded for a correct answer and 0 mark is awarded for an incorrect and unanswered question.

### **3. What are the minimum marks required to qualify for the AP EAMCET 2025 exam?**

The candidates must score at least 45% of marks (40% of marks for reserved categories like SC and ST) to qualify for the AP EAPCET 2025 examination.
