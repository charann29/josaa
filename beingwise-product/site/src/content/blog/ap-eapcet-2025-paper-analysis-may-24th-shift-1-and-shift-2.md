## AP EAMCET 2025 Mathematics Paper Analysis

Students found the Mathematics section similar to previous shifts, but more time-consuming and slightly difficult. Many questions were lengthy, and the options were confusing, with close values like 0.1 and 0.2, making accuracy tricky. Despite this, most students attempted all 160 questions due to the absence of negative marking. Some faced time issues, and a few reported system shutdowns in the last 10–20 minutes, which led to skipped questions. Students with upcoming shifts were advised to review feedback from this session.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Aspect** | **Details** |
| Overall Comparison | Similar to previous shifts |
| Question Length | Questions were often lengthy |
| Difficulty Level | Leaned towards difficult |
| Option Confusion | Very similar options (e.g., 0.1, 0.2, 1.0, 2.0) made it hard to pick the correct one |
| Attempt Rate | Many candidates attempted all 160 questions due to no negative marking |
| Time Management | Some candidates faced time management issues |

## AP EAMCET 2025 Dress Code Guidelines

Candidates taking the **AP EAMCET 2025** exam must follow a well-defined dress code to ensure fairness and prevent malpractice. Clothing should be simple and free of unnecessary embellishments. Below are the guidelines for attire for AP EAMCET 2025.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Category** | **Guidelines** |

| **Lower Wear** | Simple trousers, pants, or leggings. Avoid cargo pants, jeans with multiple pockets, or any attire with inner pockets. |

## AP EAPCET Counselling

Check out the steps of the AP EAPCET counselling process 2025 given below.

**Registration & Processing Fee Payment:** Candidates must register online and pay the processing fee. The fee is INR 1,200 for OC/BC candidates and INR 600 for SC/ST candidates.

**Document Verification:** Eligible candidates will undergo certificate verification. APSCHE has introduced online certificate verification this year, eliminating the need for physical visits to Help Line Centers for many candidates.

**Web Options Entry:** After verification, candidates can log in to the official portal to select their preferred colleges and courses.

**Seat Allotment:** Based on the web options and merit, seats will be allotted. Candidates can download their allotment letters from the official website.

**Reporting to Allotted Colleges:** Candidates must report to the allotted colleges with the necessary documents for final admission.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. **How was the difficulty level of the May 24th Shift 1 exam of AP EAMCET 2025 across different subjects?**

As there is only one shift conducted on 24th May of AP EAMCET 2025, it was generally perceived as moderately difficult, especially in Mathematics and Physics, with a mix of straightforward and tricky questions.

### 2. ******************Which topics were most frequently covered in the May 24th Shift 1 exam?******************

In Shift 1, key topics included Algebra, Calculus, and Mechanics for Math and Physics during AP EAPCET 2025 exam.

### 3. ********Were there any unexpected or new types of questions in the AP EAMCET exam of May 24th?********

Some students reported a few application-based and reasoning questions that were new compared to previous years, especially in the Shift 1 Chemistry sections.

### 4. **************How balanced was the question distribution between theory and application in both shifts?**************

Both shifts maintained a balanced distribution, with approximately equal weightage given to theoretical concepts and practical application, ensuring a fair evaluation of student understanding.

### 5. ********Did students from both shifts report similar time management challenges?********

Yes, students mentioned that managing time was crucial, especially in sections with numerical problems. Shift 1 candidates felt slightly more pressed due to the higher application-based question load.
