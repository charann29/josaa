## Joy University School of Arts & Natural Sciences Admissions 2025

## How to Apply for Joy University?

Applying to Joy University is a straightforward online process. Candidates must first register on the official website using their mobile number or email ID. After verifying their identity through an OTP, they can proceed to fill out the application form with personal and academic details. Once the application is submitted and the fee is paid, candidates may be required to appear for JOYAT (Joy University Admission Test). Finally, applicants must upload their documents to complete the process. Once all steps are done, their application will be reviewed for further steps.

### **Step-by-Step Guide to Apply for Joy University Admissions 2025**

**JOYAT (Joy University Admission Test)** is an internal entrance exam conducted by **Joy University** for admission into various undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

## Joy University Scholarships

**Joy University** offers a wide range of scholarships to encourage academic excellence and provide financial support to deserving students. These include **merit-based**, **need-based**, **sports**, and **special category** scholarships. The **Chancellor Scholarship** and **Merit Scholarship** are awarded to students with top scores in **JEE Main, CUET, or JOYAT**, offering tuition waivers ranging from **60% to 100%**. For example, JEE ranks below 50,000 or CUET percentiles above 90% can qualify for a full tuition fee waiver. Likewise, students with high scores in Class 12 PCM/PCB and top JOYAT ranks may receive up to **85% fee waivers**.

Additionally, **economically disadvantaged students**, SC/ST candidates, and those with physical disabilities can apply for the **JU Economical (CSR) Scholarship**, which offers up to **75% tuition support**. Special scholarships are also available for **sports achievers** at district, state, and national levels, as well as students from the **North East**, **Jammu & Kashmir**, and **differently abled** backgrounds — with waivers up to 100% in certain categories.

Applicants can also take the **JU-SES (Scholastic Excellence Scholarship Exam)**, which offers up to **50% tuition waivers**, independent of other exams or categories. All scholarships are awarded after verifying documents and a possible personal interview, and are renewable yearly based on maintaining academic performance (minimum CGPA 6.5 to 8.0) and good conduct.
