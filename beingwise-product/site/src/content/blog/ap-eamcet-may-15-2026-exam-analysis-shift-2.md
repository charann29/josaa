## Takeaway For Upcoming AP EAMCET Aspirants

Based on the AP EAMCET May 15 2026 Exam Analysis Shift 2, students who have yet to appear for the AP EAMCET 2026 Exams should keep the following tips in mind. These can also be treated as exam day guidelines for aspirants.

- Focus more on speed and accuracy, as the key to securing a good AP EAMCET Score is time management.
- Answer the difficult maths section last and answer the easier chemistry section, following the two-pass method.
- Practice EAMCET previous papers with solutions pdf download, previous year AP EAMCET question paper​, memory-based mock papers, and EAMCET exam model papers regularly.
- Practice Lengthy and calculation-based questions regularly.
- Keep the high AP EAMCET chapter wise weightage topics handy and focus on the high-weightage chapters like Calculus, Coordinate Geometry, Algebra, Modern Physics, Organic Chemistry.
- Revise NCERT topics and formula-based questions for the Chemistry section.
- Solve the EAMCET previous papers AP and AP EAMCET Question papers to understand the exam trend and difficulty level.
- Solve Daily AP EAMCET Mock Tests, EAMCET exam model papers, and EAMCET important questions under timed conditions. This will improve the time management skills of students.
- Students need to learn shortcut methods and smart calculation methods for solving difficult, lengthy calculations.
- Do not spend too much time on one question; skip it and return to it later.
