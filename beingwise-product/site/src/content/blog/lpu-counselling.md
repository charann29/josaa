## LPUNEST Counselling Process 2026

Student can check the complete LPU Counselling or [**LPU admission process**](http://blog/engineering-colleges/lpunest-2026/) below:

**1. Registration for Counselling**

To participate in the LPU counselling, the LPUNEST qualified candidates need to register on the official portal. This process typically starts immediately after the exam result and is online.

**2. Filling of Choices and Seat Allotment**

Once logged in, candidates are required to make their choices for BTech branches and specialisations through LPU online seat preference. This is crucial as LPU seat allotment is done on the basis of preferences and seats.

**3. Seat Allotment Process**

LPUNEST rank and preference choices are used by the university to determine allotment of seats. Allotments are done in a series of rounds such as LPU counselling round 1 based on seat availability and preference.

**4. Payments of Counselling Fee (Initial)**

A fee for counselling in LPU must be paid to secure the seat. This is essential to confirm the seat and move ahead in the admission process.

**5. Submission and Verification of Documents**

Applicants will be required submit and upload documents like marksheet and ID proof. These are then cross-checked with originals for verification by the authorities.

**6. Payment of Remaining Fee and Confirmation of Admission**

Following verification, the candidate needs to pay the remaining course fee within the allotted time. This finalises the admission to the BTech course at LPU.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is LPU counselling for BTech 2026?

LPU counselling is the process through which seats are allocated to students based on their LPUNEST performance. It includes registration, choice filling, seat allotment, and admission.

### 2. How many rounds are there in LPU counselling?

LPU conducts multiple counselling rounds depending on seat availability. Students may get a chance to upgrade their allotted seat in subsequent rounds.

### 3. Do I need to take LPUNEST to get admission in LPU?

Yes, LPUNEST is mandatory for B.Tech admissions and is also used for determining scholarships.

### 4. How are seats allocated in LPU?

Seat allotment is based on LPUNEST rank and the preferences filled by the candidate. Higher-ranked candidates have better chances of getting their preferred branch.

### 5. Is branch change allowed?

Branch change is allowed in subsequent counselling rounds if seats are available, and it depends on merit and vacancy.

### 6. What documents are required for counselling?

Students need to submit academic certificates, ID proof, photographs, and other required counselling documents during the admission process.

### 7. Will LPU counselling be online?

Yes, the entire counselling process is conducted online through the LPU admission portal and e-Connect platform.

### 8. What if I miss counselling dates?

If you miss the counselling schedule, you may lose your chance to participate in that round or secure a seat. It is important to follow the official timeline.

### 9. How are LPU scholarships decided?

Scholarships are based on LPUNEST scores and academic performance. Higher scores generally lead to higher scholarship benefits.

### 10. How to check LPU counselling updates?

Students can check updates on the official LPU admission portal and stay informed about counselling rounds and notifications.
