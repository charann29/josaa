### MHT CET Paper Review April 15 shift 1 Physics Section

The Physics section in MHT CET 2026 April 15 Shift 1 was considered moderately tough. The paper was formula-based and required a conceptual understanding to answer the questions correctly. Some of the questions required a lot of calculations, which increased the time required to answer them properly.

A major portion of the paper consisted of direct application-type questions and was based on various sections, as follows.

- Laws of Motion
- Work, Power & Energy
- Current Electricity
- Optics
- Modern Physics

### MHTCET Paper Analysis April 15 shift 1 Chemistry Section

As per the MHT CET paper analysis, chemistry emerged as the easiest of all three sections. Questions were mainly theoretical, direct, and NCERT-based, making this section very scoring. A large chunk of the total questions was related to Organic and Inorganic Chemistry, and some were from Physical Chemistry as well. This made it easier to score well in this section and improved overall attempts.

**Key Chemistry Topics Asked in the April 15 Shift 1 MHT CET Question Paper**

- Organic Chemistry (Reactions, Mechanisms, Named Reactions)
- Inorganic Chemistry (Periodic Table, Chemical Bonding, Coordination Compounds)
- Physical Chemistry (Mole Concept, Thermodynamics, Chemical Kinetics)
