The JEE Mains 2025 exam is scheduled to take place from **January 22nd to January 30th**. Candidates appearing for Shift 1 of the JEE Mains 2025 have already finished their exam. Candidates who are yet to appear for other shifts wait eagerly for the exam **paper analysis** of shift 2. This analysis will set an expectation on the types of questions asked, the difficulty level of the examination, and the varying difficulty level of the examination across each subject. This article will unveil the difficulty level of the JEE Mains 2025 Shift 2 exam and the difficulty level of each subject.

The NTA will conduct the [JEE Main 2025](https://collegedost.in/blog/jee-main/) Session 1 exam on **January 22, 23, 24, 28, and 29, 2025**. Candidates can review the previous year’s JEE Main paper analysis to gauge the difficulty level of past exams. In JEE Main 2024, both Session 1 and Session 2 were of moderate difficulty. Session 2 had a greater emphasis on Class 11 topics, while Session 1 focused more on Class 12 chapters. Subject-wise, Physics ranged from moderate to difficult, Chemistry was relatively easy, and Mathematics was challenging.
