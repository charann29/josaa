## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1. What makes NIAT different from traditional engineering colleges in Guntur?

NIAT in Guntur focuses on industry-relevant skills through practical projects, early internships from the first year, and mentorship from industry professionals rather than traditional theoretical teaching methods.

### Q2. Is the degree from NIAT Training Centre in Guntur recognised?

NIAT Guntur doesn’t offer any degrees as it is not a university. The B.Tech degree is awarded by Chalapathi Institute of Technology and Chalapathi Institute of Engineering and Technology, which is UGC approved and has collaborated with NIAT. Students receive an Industry Ready Certificate from NIAT.

### Q3. Are the reviews about NIAT Guntur good?

NIAT Guntur reviews on different social media platforms reflect a largely positive student experience, with many highlighting aspects such as supportive mentors and good placement opportunities. However, there are concerns of students related to long-standing placement data and the alumni network.

### Q4. Can I Join NIAT Guntur?

Yes, if you want to learn about the coming edge AI and Technologies with hands-on learning, and receiving Industry Ready Certificate at the end of 4 years, then NIAT Guntur is an excellent option for students. Students can also receive UGC approved BTech degree from the collaborating University.

### Q5. What kind of companies visit for placements at NIAT Guntur?

NIAT has partnerships with over 3000 companies, including top tech giants like Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Deloitte, Infosys, and many other leading organisations.

### Q6. Does NIAT Guntur offer specialisations in Computer Science Engineering?

NIAT is not a standalone University or College that can offer BTech CSE with specialisations. However, it offers an Industry Ready Upskilling program. It also has collaborated with various UGC approved Universitites which offer BTech CSE with specialisations in AI, ML, and Data Science.

### Q7. Who can enroll to the program at NIAT Guntur?

Students who have completed their 12th grade with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics are eligible to apply to NIAT Guntur. Admission is usually based on the NxtWave Assessment Test. However, final admission will be given by CIET and CIT, the partnering universities of NIAT.

### Q8. Are there extracurricular activities available for students at NIAT Guntur?

Yes, NIAT Guntur offers several student clubs and activities, including coding clubs, entrepreneurship groups, public speaking clubs, sports activities, and hackathons that help students develop leadership, teamwork, and innovation skills.

### Q9. What facilities are available on the chalapathi institute of engineering and technology campus?

The CIET campus provides modern infrastructure such as smart classrooms, advanced computer labs, high-speed internet connectivity, a digital library, sports facilities, and various student clubs that support technical and personal development.

### Q10. What kind of learning approach is followed at NIAT Guntur?

NIAT Guntur follows a project-based and practical learning approach. Students work on real-world projects, coding exercises, and hackathons from the first year to build strong technical and problem-solving skills.

### Q11. Is NIAT good?

Yes, NIAT can be a good option if you want to build practical, industry-relevant technology skills along with your engineering degree.
