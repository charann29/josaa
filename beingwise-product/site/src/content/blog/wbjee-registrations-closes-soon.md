The Registrations for WBJEE are going to close soon **the last date for applying for the exam is 23rd February 2025.** The Candidates can apply for the examination on the official website. **The registration window is active till 23rd February 2025** and the **exam is scheduled for 27th April 2025**.

The Online Application form correction will be available from 25th February 2025  to 27th February 2025. The candidates are advised to check the details and fill out the application form in case of any errors in the application candidates can change the details during application form correction.

## Steps to Correct Details in an Application Form

If there are any errors in the application form submitted by the candidate they will be notified via SMS or email. If a candidate receives the mail or SMS they can apply for application correction through the official website. Here is a detailed guide on application correction.

- Visit official website(<https://wbjeeb.nic.in/wbjee/>)
- Click on the Application form correction Link
- Enter your Application Number and date of birth
- Candidates can check all the details filled  by them
- In case of any errors, they can change the details and click on submit.

## About WBJEE 2025

The examination is scheduled for April 27. Paper I, which focuses on Mathematics, will take place from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM, while Paper II, covering Physics and Chemistry, will occur from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM. Candidates will be able to download their admit cards starting April 17, 2025.

## WBJEE 2025: Exam pattern

The WBJEE Exam is conducted as 2 Papers Paper-1 is Mathematics and Paper-2 is Physics and Chemistry. Here is the overview of the WBJEE exam pattern

|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Subject** | **Number of Questions** | **Number of Marks** | **Negative Marking** |
| **Mathematics** | 75 | 100 | **Category 1:** One Mark for the Correct Answer and -¼ for Wrong Response |
| **Physics** | 40 | 50 | **Category 2:** Two Marks for the Correct Answer and -½for Wrong Response |
| **Chemistry** | 40 | 50 | **Category 3:**Two Marks for the Correct Answer and No Negative Marks for Wrong Response |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ************What is the WBJEE Exam Date?************

The Exam Date for WBJEE is April 27th, 2025 Paper -1 timing is 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM and Paper-2 Timing is 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM.

### 2. ********Which Authority Conducts WBJEE?********

The WBJEE exam is Conducted by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination Board.

### **3. Can a Candidate Attend the Examination in a different Exam Center?**

No, the candidate cannot attend the exam in a different center other than the allotted exam center.

### 4. ****What is a Good Score in WBJEE?****

For WBJEE a score above 120+  can be considered a good score.

### ********5.**Can a candidate attempt an exam in Bengali?**********

Yes, the candidates can attempt the exam in Bengali. Two Language options can be available for writing the WBJEE Exam.

### 6. How Many Questions are there in WBJEE?

The WBJEE Exam will contain 155 Questions.

### 7. What are the Total Marks in the WBJEE?

The WBJEE is conducted for  200 Marks.WBJEE Mathematics section is conducted for 100 Marks and Physics and Chemistry Paper is conducted for 50 Marks Each.
