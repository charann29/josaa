## How MHT CET 2025 Result is Calculated

The MHT CET 2025 result is based on a percentile score rather than raw marks, as the exam is conducted in multiple sessions with varying difficulty levels. To ensure fairness, a normalisation process is used before calculating the final result. Here’s how the result is calculated:

### How MHT CET 2025 Results Are Calculated:

**Step 1: Raw Score Calculation:**

Candidates first receive a raw score based on the number of correct answers. There is no negative marking in MHT CET.

**Step 2. Normalization of Marks**:

Since the exam is held in different shifts, raw scores are normalised to adjust for varying levels of difficulty between sessions.

**Step 3: Percentile Formula**:

The percentile is calculated using the formula:

This percentile reflects how many candidates scored equal to or below your score.

**Step 4. Final Result**

Candidates receive subject-wise and overall percentiles. Admissions are based on these percentile scores, not the raw marks.

## MHT CET Results 2025: Tie Breaking process

In some situations where two or more candidates achieve the same scores in the exam, it can create uncertainty about their ranks. To overcome this, CET authorities have decided to implement tie-breaking procedures for MHT CET 2025 results.

### Tie-breaking Process

In case of a tie in MHT CET scores, the following criteria are used to break the tie:

- Candidates with higher scores in Mathematics are given preference.
- If the tie continues, candidates with higher scores in Physics are prioritized.
- If the tie persists, candidates with higher scores in chemistry are prioritized.
- If the tie still continues, the candidate older in age is given preference.
- If all previous criteria fail to resolve the tie, a random selection process is applied to break the tie.

## What to Do After MHT CET 2025 Results?

After the MHT CET 2025 results are declared, the first step is to **download your scorecard** from the official website and check your **overall and subject-wise percentile**. Based on your percentile, compare previous year closing ranks to get a rough idea of the colleges and branches you may be eligible for. Also, verify your personal details and category information on the result.

Next, prepare for the **counselling process** conducted by the Maharashtra State CET Cell. This includes **online registration**, **document verification**, and **filling web options** for preferred colleges and branches. It’s important to research thoroughly and create a list of realistic and aspirational options. Keep all necessary documents ready and regularly check cetcell.mahacet.org for counselling dates and updates.

## MHT CET 2025 Counselling

The MHT CET 2025 counselling will be conducted through the Centralized Admission Process (CAP) by the State CET Cell. After the results are declared, eligible candidates must register online, upload necessary documents, and get them verified either online or through designated e-scrutiny centers. A provisional merit list will be published based on academic performance and examination results.

Following this, candidates can fill in their preferred colleges and branches by submitting their web options. The counselling is held in multiple rounds, and seat allotments are released after each round. Once a seat is allotted, candidates can confirm it by paying the admission fee and reporting to the respective institute, or choose to upgrade their options in the next round.
