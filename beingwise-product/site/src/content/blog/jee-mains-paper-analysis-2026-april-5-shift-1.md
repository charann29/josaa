## JEE Mains Chemistry Paper Analysis

The JEE Mains Chemistry Analysis suggests that the chemistry paper was the easiest and the highest-scoring. The portion was easy, as it took comparatively less time than other portions to complete.

Based on the analysis, High weightage chapters for JEE Mains Chemistry are as follows
