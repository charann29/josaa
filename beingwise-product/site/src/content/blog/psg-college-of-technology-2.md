Established in 1951, PSG College of Technology, situated in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, is renowned for its industry-aligned B.Tech programs tailored for TNEA Aspirants. Affiliated with Anna University, it offers UG students diverse engineering specialisations in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Mechanical Engineering, Biotechnology, Fashion Technology, and others.

Being a government-aided autonomous institute, PSG College Coimbatore​ was founded by PSG & Sons’ Charities Trust. It has been accredited with an NAAC A grade and ranked 67th by NIRF in the engineering category. With more than 8000 students studying, it offers a flexible choice-based credit system (CBCS), rigorous academics, strong placement outcomes, and hands-on industry exposure. This blog simplifies the PSG Tech College B.Tech offerings, campus life, placements, and more.

### PSG College Admission​ Process For B.Tech

PSG College of Technology Coimbatore follows a merit-based admission process, followed by the TNEA counselling process. Students need to apply through the TNEA online portal around the 2nd week of May 2026, when TNEA counselling applications open. Applicants need to follow the PSG College admission process and steps to apply, given below:

- **Register for TNEA**: On the official TNEA website, students need to register for the counselling and generate [TNEA login](/blog/engineering-colleges/anna-university-admission/) credentials. Fill out the TNEA Application form by providing all the required information and uploading the required documents.
- **Document verification**: Verify the uploaded documents at the nearest TNEA facilitation centre (TFC).
- **TNEA Rank List Preparation**: Based on the normalised Class 12 PCM Marks, the TNEA rank list is prepared and published.
- **TNEA Counselling Rounds**: Shortlisted students participate in the TNEA choice filling round to select the preferred course and college.
- **Seat Allotment & Campus Reporting**: Once the seats are allotted, students need to accept or decline the allotment order. If accepted, then students need to report to the allotted college campus, verify the documents and pay the fees to confirm admission.

## PSG College of Technology Campus Life & Facilities

The 45-Acre PSG College of Technology Coimbatore campus is located about 8 Kms away from the Coimbatore railway station and 5 Km from the airport. The PSG Tech College comprises college buildings, hostels, staff quarters, a playground and greenery. The key campus facilities offered by PSG College Coimbatore are as follows:

- 18 Departmental Labs and smart classrooms with advanced teaching technologies.
- A central library with volumes of books, digital access to e-books, and learning materials.
- Extensive wifi connection across campus
- Social Events and Fests are held at the Seminar halls and auditoriums
- Health centres and gymnasiums are available/
- Hostel accommodation for both Girls and boys is offered as well. PSG College hostel rooms​ , along with dining halls, an activity room, a TV room, indoor games and medical services are available.
- Sports facilities are available for tennis, cricket, and other sports. Playgrounds and cricket grounds are available as well.

Students’ life at PSG College of Technology Coimbatore, and PSGtech Ecampus, buzzes with interactive clubs for tech, drama, and production. Annual events like intercultural fests, sports day, and other events are celebrated as well.
