## Step By Step Guide to Register for TG EAPCET 2025

TS EAMCET registration process will begin from 1st March 2025. You can check the below steps to apply for TG EAPCET exam 2025.

- Visit the official website of TS EAMCET: https://eapcet.tgche.ac.in/.
- Click on the “Pay Registration Fee” link.

- You will be transferred to the payment verification page.
- Carefully fill out the form which includes:
  - Group Subjects at Qualifying Examination
  - Name of the Board
  - Qualifying Exam Board Hall Ticket Number
  - Candidate’s Name
  - Date of Birth
  - Student’s Mobile Number
  - Email ID
  - Category: SC/ST/OC/ etc.
- Select the payment type: Net Banking / Debit Card / Credit Card.
- Click on the “Proceed to Payment” button.
- After the successful payment, you will get a payment reference ID. Keep a note of the ID for future reference.
- Click on “Proceed to Fill Application” to go to the Form.

### TG EAPCET Application Process 2025

After successful completion of the registration and payment of the fees, you can proceed to fill out the application form.

- Visit the official website of TG EAPCET and click on the “Fill Online application”.

- On the next page, fill out the Payment reference ID, mobile number, date of birth, and qualifying exam latest 2nd year hall ticket number.
- After that, click on “Proceed to Fill Application”.

- Carefully fill out the application form with all the details and upload the necessary documents.
- Submit the application form and take a printout for future reference.

Moreover, you can also correct the given points and make changes in the application form from 6th April to 8th April 2025.

- Personal Details
- Education Details
- Candidate’s Address
- Upload Pending documents

**NOTE:** Check [**TS EAMCET Application Form 2025**](https://collegedost.in/blog/ts-eamcet/ts-eamcet-application-form/)for detailed steps to fill out the form.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****************Where can I register for TG EAPCET exam 2025?****************

You need to visit the official website of TS EAMCET to register for the exam. Candidates need to pay INR 900 for the general candidates and INR 500 for the reserved candidates. The registration form will be released by JNTUH on 1st March 2025.

### 2. **After the payment of the registration fee, how can I check the payment status?**

You need to visit the official website of TS EAMCET and click on “Know Your Payment Status” on the home page. After entering all the required details you need to click on “Check Payment Status” and you will get to know your payment status.

### 3. ********What is the last date for TG EAPCET registration?********

The application/registration deadline for the TS EAMCET entrance examination is 4th April 2025.

### 4. ******What are the documents required to fill out the TS EAMCET application form?******

You can check all the documents required to fill out the application form of TG EAPCET 2025.
Qualifying Examination Latest 2nd Year Hall Ticket Number/10+2.
Personal Email Id.
Personal Mobile Number
Hall Ticket Number of S.S.C. or Equivalent
Date of Birth
Caste in case of SC/ST/BC candidates (Caste Certificate Application number for SC/ST only)
PH, CAP, NCC, Sports, EWS etc.
Income below 1 Lakh or above 1 lakh and below 2 Lakhs or 2 Lakhs and above (Rupees)
Study or Residence or relevant certificate for proof of local status (last 12 years)

### 5. ******How many marks do I need to get 3000 rank in TS EAMCET 2025?******

To get 3000 rank in TG EAPCET exam, you need to secure 100 marks in the exam and 585–590 in group totals of PCM (physics, chemistry, maths). At times when the question paper is tough, you can get a rank below 3000 even if you get a 90+ score.

### 6. ************How many times can we appear for the TG EAPCET exam?************

You can appear for the TS EAMCET exam as many times as you want. There is no limit to the number of times you can appear in the entrance exam conducted by JNTUH.

### 7. ********How to apply for TS EAMCET counselling?********

After the TG EAPCET entrance exam, you need to appear for the counselling process to get your desired Engineering college. The counselling process involves verification of your documents which includes TG EAPCET rank cards, educational certificates, caste Certificate (if applicable), etc.

### 8. ****How can I download the TG EAPCET admit card 2025?****

To download the admit card on 19th April 2025, candidates need to visit the official website of TS EAMCET. Click on “Download the admit card” by providing all the necessary information which includes: registration number, date of birth, the hall ticket number. It is mandatory to bring the admit card to the exam centre.

### 9. ********What is the exam date for the Engineering program of TG EAPCET entrance test 2025?********

THE TS EAMCET/TG EAPCET 2025 entrance exam for the BTech program will be held from 2nd May to 5th May 2025.
