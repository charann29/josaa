KCET Result 2026 has been declared. KEA released the KCET 2026 result on June 6, 2026. The result link went live at 2 PM on the official website at cetonline.karnataka.gov.in. A press conference by KEA was held at 12 PM ahead of the release.

Over 3.3 lakh students who appeared for the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) 2026 can now download their rank cards using their application number and the first four letters of their name. The exam was conducted on April 23 and 24, 2026, in offline mode across multiple exam centres, in two shifts each day.

The KCET 2026 scorecard includes subject-wise marks in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology, the total marks secured, stream-specific ranks for Engineering, Pharmacy, Agriculture, and Veterinary courses, and the candidate’s qualification status. The result was delayed from its earlier expected date due to the rescheduling of agriculture and veterinary practical exams at the Hiriyur centre.

Qualified candidates will now proceed to the KCET 2026 Counselling process. KEA will release the counselling schedule, option entry dates, and seat allotment details on the official portal. Candidates can check the official website at cetonline.karnataka.gov.in or karresults.nic.in for all counselling-related updates.

## How to check KCET Result 2026?

If you have any difficulty while checking the kea kar nic in KCET Result 2026, follow the steps mentioned below

1. Visit the official website of Karnataka Examinations Authority.
2. Click on the ‘KCET Result 2026 Link’ option found on the homepage.
3. On the login page, enter your application number and other required details.

4. After providing all the details, click on the ‘Submit’ option.
5. The KCET 2026 result Scorecard will display on the screen.
6. Download and take a print of the KCET scorecard for the counselling day use

### KCET 2026 Scorecard Details

The Karnataka Common Entrance Test 2026 scorecard consists of details regarding the candidate’s performance in the entrance examination. It consists of the name of the candidate, roll number, application number, marks obtained in individual subjects, total marks secured, rank in KCET, eligibility status of the candidate, and other category-related information.

Following downloading the KCET Scorecard, carefully cross-check all the details mentioned on the same. Keep the KEA KCET Scorecard in a safe place till the counselling gets over.

## What happens after KCET Results Announced?

After the announcement of the result of the Karnataka Common Entrance Test 2026, the candidate needs to undergo the counselling process and get admission into a suitable college. After the result declaration, the candidate will need to go through various processes mentioned below in a step-by-step manner.

| Stage | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Document Verification | Candidates must attend the KCET document verification at the designated centres with original certificates. |
| Verification Slip Download | After successful verification, KEA issues a verification slip required for counselling. |
| Option Entry Process | Candidates can select their preferred colleges and courses online. |
| Mock Allotment | KEA releases a mock seat allotment so students can modify choices if required. |
| Final Seat Allotment | Seats are allotted based on rank, category, preferences, and seat availability. |
| Fee Payment | Selected candidates must pay the admission fee within the specified deadline. |
| Reporting to College | Candidates need to report to the allotted institute with the required documents for admission confirmation. |
