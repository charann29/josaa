## Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

### 1. How are the MHT CET Marks calculated?

The MHT CET marks are calculated based on the performance of candidates in the entrance test. The marks can vary each year depending on the paper and difficulty level.

### 2. What is a percentile in MHT CET?

The MHT CET results are provided in the form of percentiles. The percentile indicates the percentage of the candidate who scored equal or below the particular score. For example, if a candidate secured 80 marks or equal then he/she scored higher than 80% of candidates who appeared for the exam.

### 3. How can I improve my percentile in MHT CET?

To improve your percentile in MHT CET, you need to focus on thorough preparation, understand the exam pattern, practice previous years’ papers, and take mock tests. Additionally, take guidance from experts, and mentors or watch YouTube videos.

### 4. What is a good percentile in MHT CET?

To secure a good percentile in MHT CET, you need to prepare at a competitive level, aware of the difficulty level, and the specific requirements of colleges or programs. Generally, a percentile above 70 or 80 is considered a good one to secure admission at reputable colleges.
