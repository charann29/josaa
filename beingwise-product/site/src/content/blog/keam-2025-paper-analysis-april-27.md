## Answer Key & Response Sheet: KEAM 2025

Officials have released the answer key for KEAM 2025 on the official website, [cee.kerala.gov.in](http://cee.kerala.gov.in). Candidates can also refer to [our page](https://collegedost.in/blog/keam/keam-answer-key-download/) for downloading the shift-wise answer key PDFS. Candidates can challenge the answer key by paying Rs. 200 per question. Based on the challenges received, authorities will release the final answer key at a later stage.

The response sheet of the candidates is a PDF containing the response marked by the candidate for each question, along with the correct response for the question. Candidates can download the response sheet from the official website.

## April 27th KEAM 2025 Exam Analysis

Here are student reviews for the April 27th shift of the KEAM exam:

**Nehan (from Kozhikode):**
“The exam was very easy. Physics, which usually takes time, was surprisingly quick—I finished it in about 45 minutes. Maths was generally easy too, and Chemistry had some repeated questions from the practice tests. I even had 20 minutes left, which I used to recheck my answers. That definitely helped improve my score.”

**Muhammad Aswin (from Kozhikode):**
“I found the exam easy. Physics had very simple statements, Chemistry was straightforward, and Maths was manageable if you kept track of time. I expected more numbers in Chemistry, but there weren’t many. Overall, the questions were like the model exams we practised. I feel confident about my performance.”

**Hridhya (from Trivandrum):**
“The exam felt easy, especially compared to the model exams by Brilliant. In fact, the real exam was easier. Solving tough questions in practice made it easier to stay calm during the actual test. Chemistry was simple if you had read the NCERT properly. Physics had both problems and statement-based questions, but nothing was too time-consuming.”

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ********Was the KEAM 2025 April 27th paper difficult?********

No, most students described it as easy. Many found the questions direct and similar to mock tests.

### **2. ****Which subject was the easiest in the April 27th KEAM exam?******

All three, Physics, Chemistry, and Maths, were rated easy, but Chemistry was considered the most straightforward by many.

### **3. **Where can I download the KEAM 2025 answer key?****

You can download it from the official website [cee.kerala.gov.in](https://cee.kerala.gov.in) or refer to our shift-wise PDFS.

### **4. **********Can I challenge the KEAM 2025 answer key?************

Yes, candidates can challenge the provisional answer key by paying ₹200 per question within the stipulated period.

### **5. ****How important is the NCERT for KEAM preparation?******

Extremely important. Most students noted that KEAM 2025 questions were directly based on NCERT content, especially in Chemistry.
