## CUET UG 2025 Exam Pattern

Candidates are tested across three skills through the CUET exam pattern. Language, aptitude, and domain-specific skills of the candidate are tested, and those who qualify for the exam are asked to appear for the CUET counselling process. Check the CUET exam pattern from the following table:

|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Section** | **Duration of the Exam** | **No of Questions** | **Questions Type** |
| Section IA- Language | 60 Minutes for Each Subject | 50 Questions | Candidate’s language proficiency is tested based on the type of questions, such as reading comprehension, vocabulary, and grammar. |
| Section II- Domain Specific | 50 Questions | Candidate’s subject knowledge is tested through the subject-specific questions. |

## NTA CUET UG 2025 Mathematics Preparation Tips

Preparing for CUET requires a lot of energy, and it becomes easier if someone guides you on how to effectively get the results. Lack of proper guidance often can lead to time leakages and compromising on the marks. Check the preparation tips given below to score high in the CUET mathematics:

### Tips for Conceptual Clarity and Problem Solving

- **Daily Revision Strategy:**Devise a revision strategy, as it is important to revise the concepts in order to retain them for a longer time.

- **Solve Examples before Exercises:** Solving the examples before solving the exercises can actually help students solve similar types of questions and save a lot of time.

- **Understand theorems and Proofs:** Understand the theorems and proofs instead of rote learning; this helps you remember the formulas better.

### CUET UG 2025 Practice Strategy

- **Mock Tests:** Prepare a weekly Mock test schedule and analyse your progress based on performance in the Mock tests.

- **Time management tips:** Practice mocks in an exam-like setting and track your progress on your time management. Understand where you are taking a lot of time and devise a strategy to improve on it.

- **Track your performance:** Track your performance based on your mock test results and improve on it gradually.

### Tips to Improve Speed in CUET UG 2025

- **Use Mental Math Techniques:** Learn some mental math techniques to solve the problems effectively and save time.

- **Reasoning:** Practice a lot of reasoning techniques as they offer you an easy way to score high in the exam.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. ****What is the syllabus for CUET UG 2025 Mathematics?****

The CUET UG 2025 Mathematics syllabus includes Algebra, Calculus, Probability, Linear Programming, Vectors, 3D Geometry, and Differential Equations. It covers both Class 11 and 12 topics aligned with the NCERT curriculum.

### **2. **************Is Class 11 and 12 NCERT enough for CUET UG Mathematics?****************

Yes, NCERT Mathematics books from Class 11 and 12 are the foundation for CUET. However, practicing CUET-specific guides and previous year papers is essential for better performance.

### **3. ****************How many questions are asked in the CUET UG Mathematics paper?******************

The CUET UG Mathematics section has 50 questions to be answered in 60 minutes. Each correct answer carries +5 marks, and there’s a -1 mark penalty for each wrong answer.

### **4. What are the most important topics in CUET Maths 2025?**

Key topics include Relations & Functions, Calculus, Probability Distributions, Vectors & 3D Geometry, Linear Programming, and Differential Equations. Prioritise these based on question trends in CUET PYQs.

### 5. ****What is the best way to prepare for CUET UG 2025 Maths in 60 days?****

Start with NCERT concepts, then move to reference books like RD Sharma and Arihant CUET Guide. Follow a structured 60-day plan, revise daily, and take regular mock tests and PYQs.
