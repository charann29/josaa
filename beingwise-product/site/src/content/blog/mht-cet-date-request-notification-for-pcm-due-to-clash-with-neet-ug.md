The Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell is conducting the MHT CET 2024 PCM exams from May 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 9th, 10th, 11th, 15th, 16th, and 17th. The CET Cell has released a notification regarding date requests for PCM.

Candidates who have the NEET UG exam on May 5th, 2024, and wish to change their PCM exam date can send an email to **mhtcetpcm2024@gmail.com.** The admit cards are expected to be released by the next week, and candidates can download them from the official website mahacet.org.

The MHT CET 2024 exam is based on the syllabus of class 11 and class 12. The PCM subject comprises two sections, each section carry 100 marks, with a total of 200 marks. The first section covers Mathematics, while the second section includes Physics and Chemistry. The duration of the MHT CET PCM exam is 180 minutes. The exam pattern typically consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs), and the exam is conducted online as a computer-based test.

## Notification for MHT CET 2024 Date Request

The MHT CET 2024 has released a notification for Date request to candidates who are having the NEET UG 2024 exam on May 5th, 2024, it’s important to note that the MHT CET PCM examination isn’t scheduled for the same day.

However, candidates who have received their admit cards and find the need to change their MHT CET PCM exam date due to personal reasons can do so by sending a request along with a copy of their NEET UG 2024 Admit Card to the email address **mhtcetpcm2024@gmail.com**.

Please be aware that requests for date changes will only accepted until April 26th, 2024.
