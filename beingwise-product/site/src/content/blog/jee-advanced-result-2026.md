## What happens after the JEE Advanced Result is released

Upon announcement of JEE Advanced Result 2026, those candidates who clear the qualifying cut-off will be eligible to attend JoSAA Counselling 2026 for admission in the IITs. The counselling procedure takes place online, where candidates have to register themselves, select the IITs and programmes that they prefer, and undergo the seat allocation rounds. Seat allotment is based on the rank obtained by the candidates in the JEE Advanced exam, along with the choice preference filled by them.

Those candidates who get allotted a seat are supposed to undergo the document verification process, deposit the seat acceptance fee, and confirm their admission well before time. For a hassle-free process of counselling and admission, it is advised to keep all the important documents handy in advance.

## Stages of IIT JEE Advanced Counselling Process

There are several stages in the counselling process of the IIT JEE Advanced examination. An overview of the important stages is presented below:

| Step | IIT JEE Advanced Counselling Process Details |
| --- | --- |
| 1. Registration | Qualified candidates must register for JoSAA Counselling 2026 using their JEE Advanced login credentials on the official counselling portal. |
| 2. Choice Filling | Candidates need to select their preferred IITs, courses, and programs in order of preference. |
| 3. Choice Locking | After filling choices, candidates must lock their preferences before the deadline. |
| 4. Mock Seat Allotment | JoSAA releases mock allotments to help candidates understand their admission chances based on the choices filled. |
| 5. Seat Allotment | Seats are allotted based on JEE Advanced rank, category, seat availability, and candidate preferences. |
| 6. Seat Acceptance Fee Payment | Candidates allotted a seat must pay the seat acceptance fee to confirm participation in further counselling rounds. |
| 7. Document Verification | Candidates are required to upload and verify documents such as JEE Advanced scorecard, Class 10 & 12 certificates, category certificate, and identity proof. |
| 8. Freeze, Float, or Slide Option | Candidates can choose Freeze (accept seat), Float (seek better institute), or Slide (seek better course in the same institute). |
| 9. Further Counselling Rounds | Candidates may participate in subsequent JoSAA rounds for better seat allotment opportunities. |
| 10. Final Seat Allotment | After completion of all rounds, the final allotted institute and branch are confirmed. |
| 11. Reporting to the Institute | Candidates must report to the allotted IIT for admission formalities, fee payment, and academic registration. |
