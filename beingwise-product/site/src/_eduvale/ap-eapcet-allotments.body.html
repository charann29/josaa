Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the closing rank for a branch?

Fetch results for your college and branch, then click the Rank column header to sort descending. The highest rank number shown is effectively the closing rank for that branch in the displayed phase. Filter by a specific phase for phase-wise closing ranks.

What is the difference between "Category" and "Allotted Category"?

Category is the reservation category you belong to (OC, BC-A, SC, etc.). Allotted Category is the specific quota under which your seat was allotted — for example, an OC candidate allotted under OC-Open, or a BC-A candidate allotted under BC-A reserved quota.

Can I see allotments across all phases at once?

Yes — the table shows all available phases together. Use the Phase column to sort or filter by a specific counselling round. This lets you track how seats moved between phases as candidates upgraded or exited.

Why is my name not appearing in the results?

Possible reasons: the allotment for your phase hasn't been released yet, you did not confirm/report your seat, or the selected college/branch combination is incorrect. Also ensure you're checking the right branch code — some branches have multiple specialisations with separate codes.

How often is the data updated?

The database is updated after each official APSCHE phase publication. We process and load the official allotment PDFs as soon as they are released, so data reflects the most recently announced phase.
