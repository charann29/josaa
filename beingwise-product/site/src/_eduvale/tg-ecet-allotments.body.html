About TG ECET 2025 College Wise Branch Wise Allotment Data

The TG ECET 2025 seat allotment (formerly TS ECET) is conducted by Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE) for lateral entry admissions into B.E. / B.Tech second year across all government, aided, and private unaided colleges in Telangana. This explorer aggregates official allotment data so candidates can quickly look up closing ranks by college and branch, compare seat availability across categories (OC, BC-A, BC-B, BC-C, BC-D, BC-E, SC, ST, EWS), and understand local vs. non-local seat distribution.

Use the filters below to search TG ECET branch wise allotment details for any engineering discipline — Computer Science, Civil, Mechanical, ECE, EEE, Chemical, Metallurgy, and more. The closing rank shown is the highest rank allotted in that college–branch–category combination and serves as a reliable cutoff reference for TG ECET 2026 planning.

How to Check TG ECET 2025 College Wise Branch Wise Allotment

  1. Select your target college name from the first dropdown (all Telangana ECET colleges are listed).
  2. Choose the branch / course from the second dropdown (e.g., CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil).
  3. Click "View Allotment Details" to load the full allotment table.
  4. The table shows every candidate allotted along with their TG ECET rank, category, and seat type.
  5. The summary cards above the charts display the opening rank, closing rank (cutoff), and total seats for that college–branch combination.
