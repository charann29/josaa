Know Your Seat

TG EAPCET 2026 Category-wise Reservations

Understanding your category's seat share helps you plan realistically during web options filling.

Horizontal Reservations

Over and above vertical reservations: Women – 33% within each category, Physically Handicapped – 3%, NCC/Sports – 1% each.

Local Area Seats

85% of seats are reserved for local area candidates (OU/AU/SVU region). The remaining 15% are open to all (UR – Unreserved).

Step-by-Step Guide

TG EAPCET 2026 Counselling Process

Conducted by TSCHE (Telangana State Council of Higher Education). Know every stage so you don't miss a deadline.

Official Portal

All counselling activities happen on tsche.ac.in. Bookmark it now.

May 2026
Results & Rank Card
Download rank card from tsche.ac.in using registration number and date of birth. Check your rank, marks and stream.
June 2026
Certificate Verification
Report to the designated help line centre for document verification. Carry originals + 2 sets of photocopies.
June 2026
Online Registration & Fee
Register on the TSCHE counselling portal, pay registration fee (₹600–₹1,200 depending on category).
June–July 2026
Web Options Entry
Enter preferred college-branch combinations in order of priority. You can enter 200+ options. More options = better allotment.
July 2026
Phase 1 Seat Allotment
Provisional allotment based on rank, category, and options. Download allotment letter and self-report or report to college.
July–Aug 2026
Phase 2 / Sliding / Upgradation
Slide to a better option if available. Spot admissions for remaining vacant seats.
August 2026
Admission & Fee Payment
Pay tuition fee at the allotted college within the stipulated time to confirm your seat.
Be Prepared

Documents Required for TG EAPCET 2026 Counselling

Missing a single document can block your admission. Keep these ready before heading to the help line centre.

  • TG EAPCET 2026 Rank Card (downloaded from tsche.ac.in)
  • TG EAPCET Hall Ticket / Admit Card
  • Class 10 (SSC) Certificate & Marks Memo (original)
  • Class 12 (Intermediate / +2) Certificate & Marks Memo
  • Study Certificate (proof of local area study for OU/AU/SVU)
  • Caste Certificate (for BC/SC/ST/EWS) – issued by competent authority
  • Income Certificate (required for EWS and some scholarship eligibility)
  • Aadhaar Card (original + photocopy)
  • Recent Passport-size Photographs (6 copies minimum)
  • Transfer Certificate from last attended institution
  • NCC / Sports / CAP / PH certificate (if applying under special quota)
  • Bank passbook / account details for fee payment
