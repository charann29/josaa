FAQs

Frequently asked questions

AP EAPCET (Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test) is the state-level entrance exam for engineering admissions in Andhra Pradesh. This predictor is designed for AP EAPCET 2026 aspirants who have received their rank and want to explore which colleges and branches they are eligible for before counseling begins.
Our predictions are based on officially released AP EAPCET 2025 allotment cutoff data. Historically, cutoffs shift by a small margin year-on-year depending on the number of applicants and seat availability. We recommend using the predictions as a strong planning reference while keeping a ±5% buffer for any variation in the 2026 cutoffs.
AU (Andhra University), SVU (Sri Venkateswara University), and OU (Osmania University) are the three university zones in AP that determine regional seat eligibility. UR (Unreserved Region) refers to seats open to candidates from any region. Select your home region to see the colleges where you are regionally eligible. If you are from outside AP or prefer to see all seats, select UR.

You can always run the prediction multiple times with different filter combinations.

If you are eligible for a special quota — Children of Armed Personnel (CAP), National Cadet Corps (NCC), Persons with Disability (PWD), or Sports & Games (SCG) — select it from the Special Quota dropdown. The predictor will include seats reserved under that quota in your results alongside your regular category eligibility. If you hold no special quota, leave it as "No Special Quota."
