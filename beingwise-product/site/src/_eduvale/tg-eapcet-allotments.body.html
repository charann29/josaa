About TG EAPCET (Formerly TS EAMCET) Seat Allotment

TG EAPCET (Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture & Pharmacy Common Entrance Test), formerly known as TS EAMCET, is the gateway to engineering admissions across Telangana. After the entrance exam, TSCHE conducts centralised web counselling where seats are allotted based on rank, category, region (OU/AU/SVU), and seat type (convener/management/NRI).

This tool lets you explore the full allotment list for any college and branch - useful for gauging realistic cutoffs, understanding category-wise competition, analysing trends over years, and planning your counselling strategy. Data shown is sourced from official TSCHE allotment orders.
